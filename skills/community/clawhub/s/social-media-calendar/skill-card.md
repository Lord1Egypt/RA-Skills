## Description: <br>
Build weekly or monthly social media content calendars for ecommerce brands, aligned with product launches, holidays, seasonal promotions, flash sales, and platform-specific trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce brand owners, social media managers, and marketing teams use this skill to plan executable weekly or monthly social calendars across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest, and X. It helps align content pillars, launch moments, platform formats, post briefs, hashtags, visual direction, and measurement cadence. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Calendar planning may use customer analytics or UGC that contains personal or unauthorized material. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data and UGC the user is authorized to process, prefer aggregated metrics, and redact unnecessary personal details before providing inputs. <br>
Risk: The listing includes crypto and purchase capability tags even though the artifact is a non-executable planning guide. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those capability tags as a listing mismatch unless an installer explicitly asks for matching permissions. <br>
