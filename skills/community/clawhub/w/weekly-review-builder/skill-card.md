## Description: <br>
Build a weekly review that refreshes project phase, bottleneck, and next steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sunbinnju-star](https://clawhub.ai/user/sunbinnju-star) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, project leads, and agents use this skill to compress weekly project logs, decisions, outputs, stale tasks, and blockers into an updated project state and focused next-week plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Suggested project-memory updates may preserve unsupported phase changes, bottleneck choices, stale-task decisions, or sensitive project details. <br>
Mitigation: Review the weekly logs and decisions before writing the skill output into long-term project memory or task tracking. <br>
