## Description: <br>
Creates simple, professional freelance proposals with essential sections for quick client work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kambrosgroup](https://clawhub.ai/user/kambrosgroup) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External freelancers use this skill to draft basic client proposals with a cover letter, project summary, timeline, single investment figure, and next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals may contain inaccurate pricing, payment terms, client details, or legal or business commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit each proposal for accuracy and business suitability before sending it to a client. <br>
