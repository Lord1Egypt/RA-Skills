## Description: <br>
Writes decision-oriented competitor-analysis sections for research reports, strategy decks, and product or brand studies, including benchmarking, marketing campaign analysis, product positioning, launch analysis, and brand upgrade analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[draco-kzn](https://clawhub.ai/user/draco-kzn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users use this skill to turn competitor information into actionable analysis for reports, memos, strategy decks, and product or brand studies. It is intended for questions about what to learn, avoid, or do next from comparable competitors, benchmarks, substitutes, campaigns, launches, positioning, or brand upgrades. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor-analysis recommendations can become misleading if source facts or business assumptions are inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify sources, facts, and business assumptions before relying on the generated recommendations. <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for competitor and benchmarking analysis, so unrelated prompts may produce poorly framed strategic guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when the request involves competitor analysis, benchmarking, campaign analysis, positioning, launch analysis, or brand upgrade analysis. <br>
