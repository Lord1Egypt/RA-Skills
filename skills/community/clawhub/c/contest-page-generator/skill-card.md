## Description: <br>
Creates, optimizes, and audits giveaway, contest, sweepstakes, promo, and viral campaign pages for campaign goals such as signups, engagement, referrals, and sharing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External teams, marketers, and developers use this skill to plan or audit contest and giveaway campaign pages, including prize copy, entry actions, rules summaries, legal checklists, and tool integration notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local project context files may contain campaign details or internal goals that the skill asks the agent to read. <br>
Mitigation: Review `.claude/project-context.md` or `.cursor/project-context.md` before use and avoid exposing sensitive campaign information in shared outputs. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms such as promo may activate the skill during general marketing requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants giveaway, contest, sweepstakes, or viral campaign page guidance before applying the skill. <br>
Risk: Contest and sweepstakes pages can require jurisdiction-specific official rules, eligibility terms, disclosures, and no-purchase language. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated legal checklist and rules copy as drafting support and have qualified reviewers approve final campaign terms. <br>
