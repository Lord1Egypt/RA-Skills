## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agent orchestration can delegate work through powerful operator workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege credentials, review the task graph before execution, and avoid external review fallbacks for private material unless explicitly approved. <br>
