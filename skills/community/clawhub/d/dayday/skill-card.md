## Description: <br>
Dayday helps agents explain MeiRiYiLian product positioning and turn learning goals into AI Teach, AI Learn, AI Practice, and daily practice workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lyhiving](https://clawhub.ai/user/lyhiving) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and learning-focused agents use Dayday to understand MeiRiYiLian, choose a learning mode, and produce small daily practice plans for exam prep, subject learning, or habit building. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may apply Dayday or MeiRiYiLian product framing to general study requests. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for neutral study advice when product framing is not desired. <br>
Risk: Product access or pricing details may be overstated if the agent goes beyond public information. <br>
Mitigation: Ground access answers in the documented public status and avoid pricing claims unless public evidence is available. <br>
