## Description: <br>
Guides users through Ruankao system architect essay preparation, from project material planning and prompt analysis to outlines, abstracts, drafting, and final checks using SCQA, Pyramid Principle, and reusable material libraries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nieen](https://clawhub.ai/user/nieen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External Ruankao system architect exam candidates and coaches use this skill to prepare project material, analyze essay prompts, build outlines and abstracts, draft essays, and check common issues. It is intended for essay-writing support, not essay scoring. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation phrases could apply exam-oriented guidance to unrelated academic writing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for Ruankao system architect exam essays and switch to a more appropriate skill for unrelated academic papers. <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive project details or draft content while preparing essay material. <br>
Mitigation: Redact confidential names, identifiers, and production data before sharing project or draft details with the agent. <br>
