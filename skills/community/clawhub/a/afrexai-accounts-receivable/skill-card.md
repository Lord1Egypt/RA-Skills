## Description: <br>
Automates accounts receivable workflows including aging reports, collection prioritization, payment reminder drafting, cash application matching, DSO tracking, and bad debt forecasting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Finance and operations teams use this skill to analyze open invoices, prioritize collections, draft payment reminders, assist cash application review, track DSO, and estimate bad debt exposure. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Incorrect payment postings, credits, write-offs, collections referrals, or customer communications could affect financial records or customer relationships. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve generated postings, adjustments, referrals, and emails before they are recorded or sent. <br>
Risk: Accounts receivable workflows may expose sensitive invoice, customer, or payment information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the invoice and customer payment data needed for the requested analysis or draft. <br>
