## Description: <br>
Generate user personas, pain points, journey maps, and UX recommendations without conducting interviews. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, product teams, designers, and developers use this skill to generate synthetic UX research deliverables for products or concepts. It supports persona generation, pain point analysis, journey mapping, heuristic evaluation, competitive UX analysis, and prioritized recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can remember product names, research notes, output preferences, and activation preferences in local files. <br>
Mitigation: Review or delete ~/ux-researcher/memory.md and related research notes when working with confidential product strategy. <br>
Risk: Proactive UX suggestions may be intrusive in normal product work. <br>
Mitigation: During setup, prefer explicit per-task activation unless proactive research suggestions are desired. <br>
Risk: Generated UX research is synthetic and may be mistaken for validated user evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Validate important personas, pain points, and recommendations with real users before making major product decisions. <br>
