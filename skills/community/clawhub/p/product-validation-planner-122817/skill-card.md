## Description: <br>
Helps users turn an Ask HN product-help request into a practical workflow, artifact, checklist, analysis, or implementation support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and communicators use this skill to clarify product-help requests, define success criteria, produce actionable deliverables, and check assumptions and follow-up work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may invoke the skill for generic terms such as ask, product, very, high, or quality. <br>
Mitigation: Install it when product-validation help is desired and tighten or disable implicit invocation when the agent platform supports that. <br>
