## Description: <br>
Turn articles, transcripts, long posts, and raw text into a self-contained illustrated HTML explainer with simple language, memorable structure, source-grounded extraction, and explanatory SVG metaphors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nefas11](https://clawhub.ai/user/nefas11) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Readers, analysts, educators, and knowledge workers use this skill to turn source text such as articles, transcripts, long posts, or raw notes into a self-contained illustrated HTML explainer that stays grounded in the provided source. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Implicit invocation may route broad article, transcript, or text-summary requests through this skill, including requests that contain sensitive private content. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit invocation or review the selected skill before processing sensitive private content. <br>
