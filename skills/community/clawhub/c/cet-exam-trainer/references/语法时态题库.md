# 语法时态题库

> 针对四六级常考时态设计的填空练习题，包含答案和简短解析。

---

## 一、一般现在时

**用法：** 表示经常性动作、客观事实或真理。第三人称单数动词加 -s/-es。

### 题目

1. She _____ (go) to school by bus every day.
2. The sun _____ (rise) in the east.
3. My father _____ (not/smoke) cigarettes.
4. _____ you _____ (speak) English?
5. Water _____ (boil) at 100 degrees Celsius.

### 答案与解析

1. **goes** - 第三人称单数，动词加 -es
2. **rises** - 客观事实，第三人称单数
3. **does not smoke / doesn't smoke** - 否定句用 does not + 动词原形
4. **Do...speak** - 疑问句用 Do 开头，动词用原形
5. **boils** - 客观事实，第三人称单数

---

## 二、一般过去时

**用法：** 表示过去某个时间发生的动作或状态。动词用过去式（-ed 或不规则变化）。

### 题目

1. I _____ (visit) Beijing last summer.
2. She _____ (not/come) to the party yesterday.
3. _____ they _____ (watch) the movie last night?
4. He _____ (buy) a new car two days ago.
5. We _____ (be) very tired after the long journey.

### 答案与解析

1. **visited** - 规则动词加 -ed
2. **did not come / didn't come** - 否定句用 did not + 动词原形
3. **Did...watch** - 疑问句用 Did 开头，动词用原形
4. **bought** - 不规则动词，buy → bought
5. **were** - be 动词过去式，we 对应用 were

---

## 三、一般将来时

**用法：** 表示将来某个时间要发生的动作。用 will + 动词原形 或 be going to + 动词原形。

### 题目

1. I _____ (meet) him at the airport tomorrow.
2. Look at the clouds! It _____ (rain) soon.
3. She _____ (be) twenty years old next month.
4. We _____ (not/attend) the meeting tomorrow.
5. _____ you _____ (join) us for dinner tonight?

### 答案与解析

1. **will meet / am going to meet** - 将来的计划或决定
2. **is going to rain** - 根据现有迹象推断，用 be going to
3. **will be** - 自然规律或既定事实
4. **will not attend / won't attend** - 否定形式
5. **Will...join** - 疑问句用 Will 开头

---

## 四、现在完成时

**用法：** 表示过去发生的动作对现在造成的影响，或从过去持续到现在的动作。用 have/has + 过去分词。

### 题目

1. I _____ (finish) my homework already.
2. She _____ (live) in Shanghai for ten years.
3. They _____ (not/see) each other since 2020.
4. _____ you ever _____ (be) to Japan?
5. He _____ just _____ (leave) the office.

### 答案与解析

1. **have finished** - I 用 have，finished 为过去分词
2. **has lived** - 第三人称单数用 has，表示持续状态
3. **have not seen / haven't seen** - 否定形式，see → seen
4. **Have...been** - 疑问句，be 的过去分词是 been
5. **has...left** - just 表示刚刚完成，leave → left

---

## 五、过去完成时

**用法：** 表示在过去某个时间点之前已经完成的动作。用 had + 过去分词。

### 题目

1. When I arrived, the train _____ already _____ (leave).
2. She said she _____ (see) the movie before.
3. By the time he got home, his wife _____ (cook) dinner.
4. They _____ (not/finish) the work by yesterday.
5. I realized I _____ (forget) my keys at home.

### 答案与解析

1. **had...left** - 火车离开发生在到达之前
2. **had seen** - 他说之前已经看过
3. **had cooked** - 做饭发生在到家之前
4. **had not finished / hadn't finished** - 否定形式
5. **had forgotten** - 忘记发生在意识到之前，forget → forgotten

---

## 六、现在进行时

**用法：** 表示正在进行的动作。用 be (am/is/are) + v-ing。

### 题目

1. Look! The children _____ (play) in the garden.
2. She _____ (read) a book in her room now.
3. I _____ (not/listen) to music at the moment.
4. _____ you _____ (wait) for someone?
5. The economy _____ (grow) rapidly these years.

### 答案与解析

1. **are playing** - Look! 提示正在发生，children 是复数
2. **is reading** - 第三人称单数用 is
3. **am not listening** - I 用 am，否定形式
4. **Are...waiting** - 疑问句，you 用 are
5. **is growing** - 表示近期持续变化，第三人称单数

---

## 七、过去进行时

**用法：** 表示过去某个时间点正在进行的动作。用 was/were + v-ing。

### 题目

1. I _____ (study) when you called me last night.
2. They _____ (not/watch) TV at 8 o'clock yesterday.
3. What _____ you _____ (do) when the phone rang?
4. While she _____ (cook), he _____ (clean) the house.
5. It _____ (rain) heavily when I left home.

### 答案与解析

1. **was studying** - I 用 was，过去正在进行的动作
2. **were not watching / weren't watching** - they 用 were，否定形式
3. **were...doing** - 疑问句，you 用 were
4. **was cooking...was cleaning** - 同时进行的两个动作
5. **was raining** - 过去某个时刻正在下雨

---

## 八、将来进行时

**用法：** 表示将来某个时间点正在进行的动作。用 will be + v-ing。

### 题目

1. This time tomorrow, I _____ (fly) to London.
2. At 10 o'clock tonight, she _____ (study) in the library.
3. We _____ (wait) for you at the station when you arrive.
4. Don't call him at 3 pm; he _____ (have) a meeting.
5. By this time next year, they _____ (work) on the new project.

### 答案与解析

1. **will be flying** - 将来某时刻正在进行
2. **will be studying** - 将来某时刻正在学习
3. **will be waiting** - 将来到达时正在等待
4. **will be having** - 将来某时刻正在开会
5. **will be working** - 将来某时刻正在工作

## 综合练习

### 题目

1. By the end of next month, I _____ (complete) the course.
2. He usually _____ (get) up at 7 o'clock, but yesterday he _____ (get) up at 6.
3. I _____ (write) emails when the boss came in.
4. She _____ (never/be) to Europe, but she hopes to go next year.
5. Look at those dark clouds! It _____ (rain).

### 答案与解析

1. **will have completed** - 将来完成时，表示将来某时间前完成
2. **gets...got** - 前半句一般现在时（习惯），后半句一般过去时
3. **was writing** - 过去进行时，老板进来时正在写
4. **has never been** - 现在完成时，否定形式
5. **is going to rain** - 根据迹象推断将来
