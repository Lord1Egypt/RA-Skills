Designers, founders, developers, and brand teams use this skill to plan, prompt, review, refine, and package logos, app icons, and brand marks for digital and print use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide confidential brand strategy, unreleased product details, or third-party trademark material while generating logo prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use only information intended for logo generation, avoid unnecessary confidential details, and review trademark or rights-sensitive inputs before sharing them with an agent or image model. <br>
Risk: AI-generated logo output may be unsuitable as a final asset without inspection, cleanup, vectorization, or rights review. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the skill's visual review loop, test small-size readability, refine text manually, vectorize final marks, and verify ownership or licensing before production use. <br>
