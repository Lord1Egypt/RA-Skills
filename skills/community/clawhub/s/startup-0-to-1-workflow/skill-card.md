## Description: <br>
Complete startup workflow from 0 to 1, integrating founder, business-model-canvas, market-research-reports, competitive-analysis, business-plan, pitch-deck-visuals and other skills. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[earthwalking](https://clawhub.ai/user/earthwalking) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, startup teams, and startup advisors use this skill to plan a staged path from idea validation through market research, business planning, MVP work, product-market-fit validation, and fundraising preparation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate during broad startup or founder discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when structured startup planning is intended, and review outputs before treating them as operational guidance. <br>
Risk: The workflow recommends related skills that may receive sensitive business, market, fundraising, or founder data. <br>
Mitigation: Review those skills separately before sharing confidential information with them. <br>
