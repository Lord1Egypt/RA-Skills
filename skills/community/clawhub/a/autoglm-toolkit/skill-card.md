## Description: <br>
AutoGLM Toolkit is a Zhipu AI-powered agent skill for browser automation, deep research, web search, image generation, image search, and web page content extraction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external users can use this skill to delegate Chinese internet research, browser tasks, search, page reading, and image workflows to AutoGLM and Zhipu AI services. It is suited to agent-assisted browsing, structured research reports, API-backed search, and generated image outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can act through logged-in browser sessions and may reuse those sessions. <br>
Mitigation: Use narrow task descriptions, avoid sensitive accounts or private/internal URLs, and clear or log out of browser sessions after use. <br>
Risk: Browser automation can affect postings, form submissions, purchases, account changes, or document edits. <br>
Mitigation: Keep auto_approve disabled and manually confirm any sensitive operation before it proceeds. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on the AutoGLM/Zhipu provider and browser extension for automation. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when that provider and extension are trusted for the intended workflow. <br>
