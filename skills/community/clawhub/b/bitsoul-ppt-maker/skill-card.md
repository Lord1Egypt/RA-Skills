## Use Case: <br>
Creators, presenters, and agents use this skill to turn Chinese source notes or scripts into a vertical, minimalist technology-style PPT presentation script and single-file HTML slide deck. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated HTML may load CDN scripts for TailwindCSS or Vue3. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML and external script dependencies before sharing, presenting, or running it in restricted environments. <br>
Risk: Condensing user source material into a presentation may change emphasis or omit important details. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated slide text against the original content before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Ambiguous requests may produce Chinese, vertical, minimalist PPT-style output when another language, ratio, or style was intended. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify language, aspect ratio, and visual style expectations before generation. <br>
