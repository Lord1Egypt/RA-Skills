## Use Case: <br>
External content teams, founders, and marketers use this skill to draft SEO-focused blog posts with meta titles, descriptions, URL slugs, structured headings, and image suggestions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Mitigation: Review generated drafts for accuracy, originality, source quality, and brand fit before publication. <br>
