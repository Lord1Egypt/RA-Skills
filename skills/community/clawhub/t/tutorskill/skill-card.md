## Description: <br>
Helps students prepare for exams by extracting exam-ready definitions, key points, keywords, diagram notes, answer sentences, and practice questions from user-provided study material and a syllabus. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ayushsaklani-min](https://clawhub.ai/user/ayushsaklani-min) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students use this skill to turn their own notes or PDFs and syllabus topics into concise exam-prep material. It supports written and MCQ preparation, prioritizes topics when time is limited, and stays within the provided source material. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Study files may contain sensitive personal or academic information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid uploading sensitive information unless comfortable with the platform handling that content. <br>
Risk: Exam-prep output may omit, over-compress, or misstate important source material. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important answers against the original notes, PDF, and syllabus before relying on them. <br>
