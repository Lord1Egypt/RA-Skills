## Description: <br>
专业的简历筛选与评估技能，按照“先硬后软，先筛后读”的漏斗式流程，从基础匹配度、能力匹配度、动机稳定匹配度三个维度对候选人简历进行量化评估，筛选出最合适且最真实的候选人。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Recruiters, hiring managers, and HR reviewers use this skill to organize resume screening, compare candidates, and produce interview, hold, and rejection recommendations. It is decision support and should not be used as an automatic final hiring decision system. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume screening guidance can influence hiring outcomes and may amplify unfair bias in criteria such as school pedigree or job history. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output to organize human review, manually verify recommendations, and check criteria against applicable employment-law and fairness obligations. <br>
Risk: Resumes may contain sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Handle resumes according to privacy obligations and limit sharing of candidate data to appropriate reviewers. <br>
