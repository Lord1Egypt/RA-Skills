## Use Case: <br>
External business leaders, consultants, and strategy teams use this skill to analyze enterprise challenges with Huawei management frameworks, explain methodology choices, and generate structured strategy recommendations or HTML reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business strategy guidance may be applied without enough organization-specific context or expert review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as methodology guidance and have qualified stakeholders review recommendations before making strategic decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may enter highly confidential company details into the agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive business data unless the deployment environment and access controls are appropriate. <br>
Risk: Generated HTML reports may reference Chart.js from a CDN. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML and replace CDN dependencies with approved internal assets when working in restricted environments. <br>
