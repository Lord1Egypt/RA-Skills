## Description: <br>
Plan, schedule, and track content across channels, including newsletters, social media, blog posts, and videos, while managing pipeline stages, publishing cadence, and repurposing opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Clawdssen](https://clawhub.ai/user/Clawdssen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
CC-BY-NC-4.0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and solopreneurs use this skill to maintain a local content calendar, identify overdue or at-risk pieces, plan weekly publishing work, and find opportunities to repurpose published content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The local content folder may contain sensitive client, revenue, or unpublished strategy details that an agent can read while managing the calendar. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive details out of the content workspace unless agent access is intended, and review generated briefs or file changes before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Optional recurring heartbeat reviews may cause the agent to revisit content-planning files on a schedule. <br>
Mitigation: Enable recurring reviews only after deciding the cadence and scope, and keep the HEARTBEAT.md instructions limited to the content files the agent should inspect. <br>
