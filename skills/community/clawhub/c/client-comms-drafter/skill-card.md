## Description: <br>
Draft professional client emails and messages for freelancers, including project updates, scope changes, payment reminders, feedback requests, difficult conversations, and project wrap-ups. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers use this skill to draft concise, send-ready client communications for updates, feedback requests, scope changes, overdue invoices, difficult conversations, outreach, and project wrap-up messages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated client communications may contain inaccurate invoice numbers, contract terms, deadlines, payment amounts, or late-payment-law wording if the prompt is incomplete or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Review every draft before sending and verify invoice details, contract terms, dates, amounts, and legal wording against authoritative records. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include sensitive client or commercial information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the context needed to draft the message and omit unnecessary confidential client data. <br>
