## Description: <br>
Guide users to VideoAny AI Video Dubbing tool to dub video or audio into a target language. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to get concise guidance for dubbing authorized video or audio through VideoAny, including media input options, target language codes, and responsible-use reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may upload media or paste direct media URLs into an external web service without confirming rights, data handling, pricing, or retention terms. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized media, review VideoAny's handling and pricing terms before upload, and treat the local helper as workflow guidance rather than an offline dubbing engine. <br>
Risk: Dubbed speech can mislead viewers about speaker identity or speech attribution. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid deceptive impersonation, clearly label localized or dubbed outputs when needed, and follow applicable laws and platform policy. <br>
