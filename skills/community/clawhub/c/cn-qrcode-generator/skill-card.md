## Description: <br>
Generates PNG QR codes from user-provided URLs or text. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[freedompixels](https://clawhub.ai/user/freedompixels) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to turn URLs or short text into QR code image output for sharing, embedding, or download. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: QR text or URLs are sent to qrserver.com rather than generated locally. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use this skill for secrets, private internal links, password-reset URLs, tokens, or sensitive personal data; use a local QR generator for sensitive content. <br>
