## Description: <br>
Maps complete customer journeys for overseas markets with localized touchpoints, cultural buying triggers, conversion barriers, pain points, opportunities, and channel recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, marketing, customer experience, and growth teams use this skill to map international customer funnels, identify localized trust barriers and friction points, and plan channel, messaging, retention, and measurement improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Journey maps may be mistaken for validated market research or confirmed customer behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning hypotheses and validate market-specific claims with customer research, analytics data, and local evidence before major product or marketing investments. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include sensitive customer, funnel, or market-entry information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary and approved context, and anonymize confidential customer or business data where possible. <br>
