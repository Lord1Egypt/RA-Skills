## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and agent publishers use this skill to browse agent service categories, compare pricing models, search for service examples, and draft a service listing template for the agent-to-agent marketplace. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shell helpers accept user-provided arguments and print marketplace guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review command arguments before running the scripts and use the skill as informational guidance rather than as an automated authority. <br>
Risk: Agent marketplace pricing and service suggestions may become stale or may not fit a specific transaction. <br>
Mitigation: Validate current pricing, counterparties, payment methods, and service terms before relying on the guidance for commercial decisions. <br>
