## Description: <br>
Data Analyst Agent helps track book performance, evaluate operating metrics and ROI, analyze reader feedback, generate reports and dashboards, and raise anomaly alerts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, publishing operators, and content teams use this skill to collect supported book-platform metrics, analyze reader engagement and monetization, produce recurring reports, and surface abnormal trends. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review notes recurring collection, storage, and sharing of potentially private platform revenue and reader data without clear access boundaries or user controls. <br>
Mitigation: Define allowed platforms and data sources before use, avoid publishing account credentials, and provide private revenue data only after consent, storage, deletion, schedule, and report-sharing controls are documented. <br>
Risk: Automated reports and alerts can rely on incomplete or stale platform data. <br>
Mitigation: Require reports to cite the source and time range for each conclusion and label revenue estimates as estimates. <br>
