## Description: <br>
Review HH.ru application packets for clarity, consent, and public-safe wording before a human submits them. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zack-dev-cm](https://clawhub.ai/user/zack-dev-cm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job applicants and their helpers use this skill to review HH.ru application packets before manual submission. It helps refine role summaries, cover letters, attachment checklists, submit/no-submit decisions, and remaining questions for the human. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Application wording could include inaccurate role references, invented experience, hidden workflow notes, or private rationale. <br>
Mitigation: Review employer-facing text against the vacancy and user-provided draft, remove hidden notes, and keep applicant-facing wording limited to what the employer should see. <br>
Risk: Users may try to turn packet review into automated account access or live submission. <br>
Mitigation: Keep final submission manual and do not share HH.ru passwords, session cookies, 2FA or recovery data, or private employer chat content. <br>
