## Description: <br>
Plans food-driven travel by recommending destinations for dishes or cuisines, building city food maps, and creating food-centric itineraries with flights, hotels, restaurants, attractions, and booking links. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel-planning agents use this skill to plan trips where meals are the primary anchor, including destination selection, restaurant routing, lodging, attractions, flights, booking links, and budget estimates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External booking links, image links, restaurant search results, and travel lookup results may come from third-party providers. <br>
Mitigation: Review links and provider results before clicking, booking, or sharing them with end users. <br>
Risk: Travel planning may request personal travel details such as origin, dates, route preferences, or identity-related booking information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the information needed for the specific lookup and avoid providing unnecessary sensitive travel or identity details. <br>
Risk: Restaurant and travel availability, prices, hours, and booking options can change after search results are retrieved. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details with authoritative restaurant, hotel, flight, attraction, or booking sources before relying on the itinerary. <br>
