## Description: <br>
Ai Photonics is a Chinese-language guide for agents answering questions about photonics, including light fundamentals, lasers, fiber-optic communication, nonlinear optics, photonic crystals, metamaterials, optical imaging, optoelectronic devices, and quantum optics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, learners, and technical users can use this skill to get concise Chinese-language explanations of photonics concepts, devices, and application scenarios. It is suited for educational and exploratory guidance across optics, communications, laser processing, display, LiDAR, and related fields. <br>
