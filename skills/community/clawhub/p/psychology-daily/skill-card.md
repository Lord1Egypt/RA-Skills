## Description: <br>
Searches public psychology and neuroscience sources for current research findings and news, then returns ten structured Chinese-language results from a mix of Chinese and international sources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[2025biophilia-coder](https://clawhub.ai/user/2025biophilia-coder) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and researchers use this skill to ask for daily psychology and neuroscience developments and receive a concise, source-linked Chinese summary of recent academic and news items. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill evidence notes a stale hard-coded date that may prevent true daily results. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the current date before use and update date-bound prompts or search terms when generating daily summaries. <br>
Risk: Psychology and neuroscience summaries may be incomplete or unsuitable for health or research decisions without review. <br>
Mitigation: Check cited sources directly and avoid treating summaries as medical advice or final research conclusions. <br>
