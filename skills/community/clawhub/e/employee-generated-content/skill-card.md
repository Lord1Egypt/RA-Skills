## Description: <br>
Plans, implements, and optimizes employee-generated content (EGC) and employee advocacy programs for AI/SaaS products. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, content strategists, and founders use this skill to plan employee-generated content and employee advocacy programs, including platform selection, employee participation tiers, governance, and measurement. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional project context files may contain secrets or sensitive internal information. <br>
Mitigation: Review `.claude/project-context.md` and `.cursor/project-context.md` before use and remove sensitive information that should not be included in generated recommendations. <br>
Risk: Employee advocacy recommendations may create misleading or non-compliant public messaging if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated briefs and posts against brand, disclosure, and approval guidelines before publication. <br>
