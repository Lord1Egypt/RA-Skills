## Description: <br>
Cn Emoji Tool helps agents query Chinese meanings for emojis, search emojis by Chinese keywords, and convert matching Chinese text terms into emojis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[freedompixels](https://clawhub.ai/user/freedompixels) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to look up emoji meanings in Chinese, search for emojis by Chinese keyword, and add emojis to short Chinese text where built-in keyword matches apply. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automatic text-to-emoji replacement can alter short text in ways the user did not intend. <br>
Mitigation: Review converted text before using it in user-facing or business communication. <br>
