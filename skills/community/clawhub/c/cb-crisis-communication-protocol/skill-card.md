## Description: <br>
Handle PR crises in any market with culturally appropriate response protocols, including press statements, social media responses, internal communications templates, and stakeholder updates localized for the target market. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, PR teams, customer support leads, legal or compliance coordinators, and market managers use this skill to create market-aware crisis communication plans, response templates, escalation workflows, and post-crisis review checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crisis response drafts may affect legal, regulatory, safety, or liability-sensitive communications. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the target market and output language, then have qualified legal, regulatory, safety, or liability reviewers approve sensitive statements before use. <br>
Risk: Market localization guidance can be incomplete, unclear, or culturally overgeneralized. <br>
Mitigation: Treat cultural observations as hypotheses, validate current local expectations with reliable sources and local reviewers, and revise unclear examples before publication. <br>
