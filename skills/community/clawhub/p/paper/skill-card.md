## Description: <br>
Provides static Samsung Electronics shopping guidance for Galaxy smartphones, TVs, home appliances, wearables, monitors, support channels, and related product information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and shopping agents use this skill to browse Samsung product categories, compare high-level options, and find official buying or support channels. It is informational only and should not be used as a payment-processing or checkout authority. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The marketplace name may imply credit-card or payment capability even though the security evidence describes a static Samsung shopping-information skill. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as unofficial product guidance only; complete purchases through trusted Samsung or retailer checkout pages. <br>
Risk: Users may expose payment-card details to an agent based on the marketplace title. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide raw payment-card details to the agent based on this skill alone. <br>
Risk: Product availability, pricing, promotions, and support terms can change after the static skill content was published. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current offers, warranty terms, and service options through Samsung or an authorized retailer before purchasing. <br>
