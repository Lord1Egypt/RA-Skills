## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: DataForSEO requests route through ClawLink and may consume DataForSEO credits. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm large crawls, bulk requests, and other high-volume actions before running them, and check account balance, rate limits, and pricing where appropriate. <br>
Risk: Queries may expose sensitive URLs, confidential research terms, regulated data, or internal-only targets to external services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive or regulated inputs unless the user's organization has approved that use. <br>
