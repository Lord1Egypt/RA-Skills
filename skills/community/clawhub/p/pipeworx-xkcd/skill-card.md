## Description: <br>
Fetch xkcd comics by number, latest release, or random selection, returning title, alt text, image, date, transcript, and permalink. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[brucegutman](https://clawhub.ai/user/brucegutman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and technical users use this skill to fetch xkcd comic metadata for citations, examples, documentation, chat responses, or lightweight references. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Comic lookup requests are sent through the disclosed Pipeworx gateway. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for non-sensitive xkcd lookups and confirm that routing requests through the gateway fits the deployment environment. <br>
