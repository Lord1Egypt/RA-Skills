## Description: <br>
Academic paper summarization with dynamic SOP selection based on paper topic classification, supporting method, dataset, multimodal, and other paper types with rigorous analysis templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nomorecoding](https://clawhub.ai/user/nomorecoding) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and developers use this skill to turn paper metadata, abstracts, and topic classifications into structured academic summaries with methodology critique, experiment assessment, strengths, weaknesses, and author questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local summary and prompt files may contain confidential unpublished work, private reviewer notes, or proprietary research context. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with material you are allowed to store locally, review generated files, and delete sensitive summaries or prompts when they are no longer needed. <br>
