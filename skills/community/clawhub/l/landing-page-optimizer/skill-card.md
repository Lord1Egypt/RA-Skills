## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and growth practitioners use this skill to optimize paid-traffic landing pages by setting objectives, assigning channel roles, planning budget and bidding, designing test matrices, and defining scale or stop rules. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Budget, bidding, and growth recommendations could be incorrect or unsuitable for a specific advertising account. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed changes against current account data, constraints, and business goals before applying them. <br>
Risk: Missing objectives, budget frame, or channel scope can lead to weak or overly broad recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Collect the required inputs before use, or ask for only the minimum missing fields before producing a plan. <br>
Risk: Unverified platform policy, billing, or tracking assumptions could create operational risk. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate high-risk policy, billing, or tracking issues with a structured handoff and avoid fabricating metrics or policy outcomes. <br>
