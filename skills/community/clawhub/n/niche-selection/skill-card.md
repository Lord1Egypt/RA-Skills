## Description: <br>
Niche Selection helps solopreneurs generate, score, validate, and position a focused business niche before committing to a market. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JK-0001](https://clawhub.ai/user/JK-0001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Solopreneurs and founders use this skill to narrow broad business ideas into focused niches, compare candidates with weighted criteria, and validate the strongest options through market, community, competitor, pricing, and customer checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may suggest market research, outreach, or business positioning that affects real customers or spending decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggested research steps and outreach messages before acting, and verify current market data with primary sources. <br>
Risk: The skill relies on user-provided judgment when scoring niche candidates, which can produce overconfident or biased conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate top candidates with real customer conversations, competitor checks, and pricing evidence before committing resources. <br>
