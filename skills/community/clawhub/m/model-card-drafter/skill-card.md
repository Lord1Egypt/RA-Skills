## Description: <br>
Drafts a structured Model Card for machine-learning and AI models, covering intended use, training data, evaluation metrics, limitations, and ethical considerations for engineering and governance review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
ML engineers, data scientists, MLOps teams, and responsible-AI leads use this skill to draft Model Cards before deployment, publication, audit, or governance review. The draft helps capture model identity, intended and out-of-scope use, data and evaluation details, subgroup performance, limitations, privacy risks, and reviewer sign-off needs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generated Model Card may be incomplete or unsuitable for compliance decisions if model details, training data, evaluation data, subgroup performance, privacy risks, or sensitive-use context are missing. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as a draft for engineering, governance, and legal review, and resolve all documentation gaps before publication, regulatory filing, or external use. <br>
Risk: A draft Model Card could be mistaken for proof that a model is compliant, safe, or unbiased. <br>
Mitigation: Require supporting evaluation evidence and reviewer sign-off before using the document as authoritative model documentation. <br>
