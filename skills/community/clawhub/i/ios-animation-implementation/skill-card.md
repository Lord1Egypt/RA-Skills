## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated Swift animation changes may be incorrect, misleading, or unsuitable for the target app. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated Swift changes before use and confirm the selected animation API fits the task. <br>
Risk: Performance-sensitive animation suggestions may introduce frame drops or heavy continuous motion. <br>
Mitigation: Profile heavy or continuous animations with Instruments and simplify the pattern when performance issues appear. <br>
Risk: Custom motion can reduce accessibility if Reduce Motion and nonvisual feedback are not considered. <br>
Mitigation: Check accessibility behavior, including Reduce Motion fallbacks, before relying on custom animation guidance. <br>
