## Description: <br>
Provides French social-center management guidance for CAF accreditation, SENACS reporting, governance, financing, indicators, and core planning documents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thibautnext](https://clawhub.ai/user/thibautnext) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Professionals, advisers, and teams supporting French centres sociaux use this skill to plan CAF accreditation, structure social projects, prepare SENACS inputs, define pilotage indicators, and draft annual activity or governance documents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
France <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include names, family situations, volunteer details, identifiable testimonials, or HR documents when drafting social-center records. <br>
Mitigation: Use aggregated statistics, anonymized examples, redacted documents, and local CAF or legal review before sharing or publishing outputs. <br>
