## Description: <br>
Generate multiple game design solution paths before committing to one direction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and agent users use this skill to expand a game feature, live-ops, UX, economy, or design problem into several practical solution paths with tradeoffs before choosing a direction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated design options and recommendations may be incomplete or misaligned if important player, business, implementation, or strategic constraints are omitted. <br>
Mitigation: Review the option set and recommendation against the actual game context and constraints before committing to a design direction. <br>
