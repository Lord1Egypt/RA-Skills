## Description: <br>
Basic programmatic SEO audit - analyze page templates, crawl budget issues, and indexing health. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[apex-stack-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/apex-stack-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External site owners, SEO practitioners, and content teams use this skill to perform a basic programmatic SEO audit for a supplied URL or domain, focusing on page templates, crawl budget issues, indexing health, and content quality. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce incorrect or incomplete SEO recommendations if the provided URL, domain context, or site signals are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review audit findings against first-party analytics, search console data, crawl data, and business constraints before taking action. <br>
Risk: Security evidence marks the artifact clean but advises review before installation and deployment. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when this SEO audit workflow is desired, and review the skill behavior before enabling it for agents. <br>
