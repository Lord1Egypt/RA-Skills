## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Affiliate recommendations may be published without clear disclosure of paid relationships. <br>
Mitigation: Clearly disclose affiliate links, sponsorships, and commission relationships before publishing recommendations. <br>
Risk: Generated product claims or comparisons may be inaccurate, outdated, or too promotional. <br>
Mitigation: Verify product claims, limitations, pricing, and customer experience against current sources before publishing. <br>
Risk: Affiliate content may violate platform rules or consumer-protection requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Check applicable platform policies and advertising, endorsement, and consumer-protection rules before using generated content. <br>
