## Description: <br>
Manages a medical-domain LLM wiki by structuring patient consultation records, clinical Q&A pages, intervention plans, and evidence or concept pages for precise retrieval and reasoning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhangyingf](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhangyingf) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Clinicians, medical knowledge-base maintainers, and developers use this skill to organize authorized patient consultation materials into a structured private wiki. It is intended to preserve clinical details, link questions to answers and interventions, and support later retrieval with source-page traceability. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can persistently store and index sensitive patient details without clear privacy, consent, retention, or deletion safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in authorized medical-data workflows in a controlled private workspace; prefer synthetic or de-identified cases, restrict access to wiki files, define retention and deletion rules, and review generated pages before reuse or sharing. <br>
