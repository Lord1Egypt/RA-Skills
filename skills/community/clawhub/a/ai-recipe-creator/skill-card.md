## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recipe and nutrition suggestions may need ordinary human judgment for allergies, dietary restrictions, food safety, and medical nutrition needs. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated recipes before use, confirm ingredients against personal restrictions, and verify food safety or medical nutrition questions with an appropriate trusted source. <br>
