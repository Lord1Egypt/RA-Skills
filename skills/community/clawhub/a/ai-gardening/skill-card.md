## Description: <br>
AI Gardening provides practical gardening guidance for balcony, indoor, courtyard, rooftop, vegetable, herb, flower, soil, watering, fertilizing, pest, pruning, and seasonal planting needs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and gardening assistants use this skill to answer practical plant-care, garden-planning, soil, watering, fertilizing, pest-control, pruning, and seasonal planting questions. It also supports user-supplied plant photos when the user wants help identifying symptoms or care issues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded plant photos may include personal, household, or location context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat plant photos like other shared images and avoid uploading sensitive visual information. <br>
Risk: Pesticide, fertilizer, and treatment suggestions may be inappropriate for a local product, crop, or regulation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify any pesticide or fertilizer recommendation against local product labels and applicable regulations before applying it. <br>
