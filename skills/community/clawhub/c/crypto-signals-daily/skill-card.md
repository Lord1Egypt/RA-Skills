## Description: <br>
Generates a daily crypto briefing with technical analysis signals across 20 Binance pairs, macro regime context, sentiment indicators, and three trade ideas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Crypto market participants and agents use this skill to prepare daily market briefings, summarize signal conditions, and frame possible trade ideas for further review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto market briefings and trade ideas may be incomplete, incorrect, or unsuitable for a user's financial situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational, verify signals independently, and review any separate exchange, automation, or trading-execution tools before connecting them. <br>
