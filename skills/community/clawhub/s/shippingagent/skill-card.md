## Description: <br>
Dewell Bot helps freight forwarders query ocean container routes, port fees, freight rates, booking details, and cargo tracking guidance across major global ports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freight forwarders and logistics operators use this skill to compare ocean container service options, estimate charges, submit booking details, and understand operational risks before coordinating shipment execution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Freight rates, availability, order numbers, and tracking status may be stale or unconfirmed without a trusted freight-system integration. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm quotes, schedules, bookings, and tracking statuses in an authoritative carrier, freight, or internal operations system before acting. <br>
Risk: Freight workflows can involve sensitive customer, cargo, routing, and commercial details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary shipment details with known recipients and follow internal data-handling policies. <br>
Risk: Special cargo, dangerous goods, port rules, customs requirements, weather, and holiday disruptions can change shipment feasibility or cost. <br>
Mitigation: Validate special-cargo requirements, customs documentation, port rules, and disruption notices with the carrier or responsible operations team before booking. <br>
