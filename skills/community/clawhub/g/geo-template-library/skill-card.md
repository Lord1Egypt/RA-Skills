## Description: <br>
Standardized GEO template library for AI-citable content that helps agents create, reuse, and standardize templates for GEO-optimized pages such as FAQs, definitions, comparisons, how-to guides, statistics roundups, landing pages, and related content formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[geoly-geo](https://clawhub.ai/user/geoly-geo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, content teams, marketers, and developers use this skill to select and generate reusable GEO-aware Markdown templates for AI-citable web content, including FAQ pages, definition articles, comparison guides, tutorials, statistics roundups, product pages, and long-form GEO articles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for ambiguous requests that mention templates but do not clearly require GEO or AI-citable web content. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke it explicitly for GEO or AI-citable content work, and clarify the intended content type when the request is broad. <br>
Risk: Filled templates can introduce inaccurate, unsupported, or over-claimed content in sections intended for AI citation. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims, statistics, sources, and GEO-critical sections before publishing or passing the content to optimization skills. <br>
