## Use Case: <br>
External users use CLAWP through the ClawPad/OpenClaw workflow to turn a short memecoin idea into a structured launch blueprint. The skill provides naming, narrative, visual direction, logo-prompt, launch, and buyback/burn guidance while stating that it does not provide financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto token launch workflows can cause financial loss or irreversible on-chain activity when transaction steps, payment destination, fees, custody, confirmations, or automation controls are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Review the exact platform transaction steps, payment destination, fees, custody model, confirmations, and stop or reversal controls before using the skill for any real launch. <br>
Risk: Launch timing, buyback, or burn suggestions could be mistaken for investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep mandatory disclaimers, avoid buy or sell recommendations, price predictions, and profit promises, and require human review before acting on launch outputs. <br>
