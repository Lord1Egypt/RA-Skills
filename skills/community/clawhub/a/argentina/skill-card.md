## Description: <br>
Plan Argentina trips with region-specific routing, money strategy, seasonal timing, and practical travel logistics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to build practical Argentina itineraries, compare regional routing options, and account for entry, money, transport, weather, safety, accessibility, and booking constraints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local trip memory may retain personal travel preferences, immigration status, identity-related notes, mobility constraints, family details, or booking context after the trip. <br>
Mitigation: Review where ~/argentina/memory.md is written, avoid storing unnecessary sensitive details, and delete or edit the file after the trip if retention is not desired. <br>
Risk: Travel plans can become incorrect when entry rules, border requirements, park access, transport schedules, or weather conditions change. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's official-source references to re-check time-sensitive requirements before booking non-refundable travel or crossing borders. <br>
