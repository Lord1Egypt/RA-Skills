This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
AI agents and their users use Mayu to run structured self-reflection exercises, check behavior against defined boundaries, and distill interaction principles for safer, clearer responses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Always-on global behavior can broadly shape assistant responses while the skill is enabled. <br>
Mitigation: Enable Mayu only where its reflection framework is intended; prefer explicit /mayu invocation or disable always-on/global behavior when the client supports it. <br>
