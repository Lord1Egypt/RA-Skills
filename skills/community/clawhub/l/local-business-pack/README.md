## What this skill does

Four automation workflows for local service businesses — the tasks that eat hours every week and directly impact revenue:

1. **Review Response Engine** — Professional responses to Google/Yelp/TripAdvisor reviews in seconds. Positive, neutral, or negative. On-brand, human-sounding, never robotic.

2. **Appointment Follow-Up Sequence** — 3-message SMS/email sequences to send after every appointment. Drives rebooking without being pushy.

3. **Customer Reactivation Campaign** — Re-engage customers who haven't visited in 60–180 days. 3-message arc that leads with value, not guilt.

4. **Competitor Intelligence Report** — Research what nearby competitors are doing right and wrong, and translate that into your specific opportunities.
