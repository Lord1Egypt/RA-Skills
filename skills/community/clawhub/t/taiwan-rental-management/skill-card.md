## Description: <br>
Rental Management provides Taiwan-focused SOP guidance for landlord rental operations, including tenant sourcing, screening, lease signing, inspections, maintenance, rent collection, dispute handling, and renewal or termination workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tsaitepiao-alt](https://clawhub.ai/user/tsaitepiao-alt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External landlords and property managers use this skill to organize Taiwan rental-property operations across self-managed and agency-managed models. It also supports property-manager training, maintenance planning, dispute workflows, and rental return calculations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Taiwan <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tenant-screening guidance may introduce housing-discrimination and privacy risks. <br>
Mitigation: Revise tenant-screening SOPs before use to remove nationality-based screening and apply lawful, consistent identity and financial checks to all applicants. <br>
Risk: The artifact asks users to collect sensitive applicant identity, financial, and photo records. <br>
Mitigation: Add consent, secure storage, retention, deletion, and local legal-compliance controls for all sensitive applicant data. <br>
Risk: Operational landlord guidance may be mistaken for legal or compliance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Review procedures against current Taiwan rental, privacy, housing, and consumer-protection requirements before relying on them. <br>
