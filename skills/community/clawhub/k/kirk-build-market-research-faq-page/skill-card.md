## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common market research questions. Use when working on FAQ content for analysts, founders, strategy teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, founders, and strategy teams use this skill to draft customer-facing FAQ answers for market research pages, with prompts to clarify audience, goals, constraints, and deliverables before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated market research FAQ answers may contain inaccurate or unsupported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims, sources, and wording before publishing externally or using the content in automated workflows. <br>
Risk: Sensitive proprietary research could be shared through chat or web search workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential market research unless the team has approved those tools for that material. <br>
