## Use Case: <br>
Individuals, employees, and workplace wellness programs use this skill for structured self-help exercises, mood reflection, guided mindfulness scripts, and escalation to crisis resources when severe distress indicators appear. It is supportive guidance and not a replacement for licensed mental health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat wellness guidance as professional diagnosis, therapy, or crisis counseling. <br>
Mitigation: Keep disclaimers visible, route severe distress to crisis resources, and encourage contact with licensed professionals. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose sensitive health information while using mood tracking or journaling workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Advise users not to share protected health information and keep storage local or minimal as described by the artifact. <br>
Risk: Generated mood patterns or burnout insights may be inaccurate or overinterpreted. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as reflective self-help signals and require human review before clinical, workplace, or safety decisions. <br>
