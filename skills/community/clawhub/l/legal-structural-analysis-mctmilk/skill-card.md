## Description: <br>
对法条进行结构化拆解，分析规范类型、适用主体、构成要件、法律效果等七个维度。当律师或法律学习者需要"拆解"法条、理解法条骨架时使用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mctmilk](https://clawhub.ai/user/mctmilk) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External lawyers, legal learners, and drafting reviewers use this skill to break a legal provision into normative type, applicable subjects, constitutive elements, legal effects, exceptions, and ambiguous concepts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal analysis output may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable as legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as study or drafting support, and verify important conclusions with authoritative sources or a qualified professional. <br>
Risk: Long legal provisions may be analyzed too broadly if the user does not identify a focus. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the user which part of a long provision they want to prioritize before producing the structured table. <br>
