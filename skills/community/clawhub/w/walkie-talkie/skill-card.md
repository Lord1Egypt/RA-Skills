## Description: <br>
Handles voice-to-voice conversations on WhatsApp by transcribing incoming audio and responding with both text and local TTS audio. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rubenfb23](https://clawhub.ai/user/rubenfb23) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to hold WhatsApp conversations by voice instead of typing. The agent transcribes incoming audio prompts, processes them normally, and returns a voice-note response with text for clarity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Spoken content may be transcribed into temporary local files during WhatsApp voice processing. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when local voice-message processing is intended, manage temporary files appropriately, and inform conversation participants before using automated voice replies. <br>
