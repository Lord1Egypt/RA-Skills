## Description: <br>
Remember mistakes across sessions. Append one-liner learnings, review before complex tasks, promote important ones. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wubotvix](https://clawhub.ai/user/wubotvix) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to keep a concise learning log that helps avoid repeated mistakes across sessions. It is intended for lightweight notes about project conventions, corrected assumptions, tool limitations, and follow-up cleanup. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Learning entries may capture secrets or untrusted instructions if logged without review. <br>
Mitigation: Do not record secrets or untrusted instructions, and review entries before using or promoting them into long-term memory. <br>
Risk: Outdated or inaccurate lessons can mislead future work. <br>
Mitigation: Periodically delete stale entries, merge duplicates, and promote only confirmed high-impact lessons. <br>
