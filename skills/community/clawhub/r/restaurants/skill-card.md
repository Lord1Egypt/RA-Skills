## Description: <br>
Build a personal restaurant system for tracking places to try, favorites, and dining memories. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to help an agent keep a local restaurant tracker with places to try, favorites, visited meals, and dining notes. The skill helps surface recommendations from the user's saved restaurant history before relying on general knowledge. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Restaurant notes and preferences are stored locally in ~/restaurants/ and may include personal dining history or dietary preferences. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to confirm before saving entries and avoid recording sensitive details unless you explicitly want them stored. <br>
