## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is a high-authority DevOps assistant that may propose broad terminal, file, deployment, or production-system actions. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when that authority is intended, supervise commands before execution, and define which credentials, repositories, and production systems are in scope. <br>
Risk: Bundled project-specific context and persistent memory instructions can carry assumptions that are not appropriate for a new environment. <br>
Mitigation: Remove or parameterize the bundled BeijiXing-AI context, keep repository-local rule files subordinate to platform and user rules, and require redaction or deletion controls for checkpoints, logs, and archives. <br>
