## Description: <br>
Huo15 Wvi guides HR interviewers through a five-step Work Values Inventory conversation, then produces employee value-priority insights, satisfaction gaps, and attrition-risk guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhaobod1](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhaobod1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
HR teams and managers use this skill to run structured employee value interviews, summarize core work values, and identify satisfaction gaps that may need follow-up. Results should support conversations and retention planning, not standalone employment decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate sensitive employee profiling and attrition-risk reports. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as sensitive HR data, limit access to authorized reviewers, avoid unnecessary identifiers, and define retention and deletion rules before use. <br>
Risk: Employees may not understand how their interview responses and generated reports will be used. <br>
Mitigation: Provide an informed-consent notice before the interview and explain who will see the results and how they may inform follow-up conversations. <br>
Risk: Generated risk labels could be overused as standalone employment-decision evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as conversation support and combine them with human review and other relevant context before any HR action. <br>
