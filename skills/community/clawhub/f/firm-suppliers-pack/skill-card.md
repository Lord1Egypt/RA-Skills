## Description: <br>
Procurement and supplier management pack for supplier sourcing, multi-criteria evaluation, TCO analysis, contract management, and supply chain risk monitoring. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[romainsantoli-web](https://clawhub.ai/user/romainsantoli-web) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Procurement, finance, legal, technology, and operations teams use this skill to source suppliers, compare vendors, analyze total cost of ownership, review contract clauses, and monitor supplier risk. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Supplier, contract, or watch-list data may be stored or retained by the backing OpenClaw tools. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm storage location, retention behavior, and removal workflow before submitting sensitive procurement or contract data. <br>
Risk: Risk monitoring and supplier scoring may influence vendor, budget, legal, or operational decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Have procurement, finance, legal, or operations reviewers validate outputs before relying on them for business decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on mcp-openclaw-extensions >= 3.2.0. <br>
Mitigation: Install only trusted versions of the extension and review its data-handling behavior before use. <br>
