## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users can use this skill to answer Buenos Aires travel, relocation, work, study, and startup questions. It provides practical guidance on neighborhoods, costs, visas, transport, safety, food, healthcare, education, culture, and local services. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Visa, tax, work-authorization, and currency-exchange guidance may become outdated or may not apply to a specific user. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those sections as general background and verify current rules with official Argentine sources or qualified professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: The skill is a static local reference and cannot refresh prices, exchange rates, safety conditions, or legal requirements at answer time. <br>
Mitigation: Check time-sensitive details against current official or reputable local sources before making travel, housing, immigration, financial, or business decisions. <br>
