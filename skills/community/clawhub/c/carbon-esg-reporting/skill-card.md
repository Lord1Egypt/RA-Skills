## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Carbon factors, ESG standards, tax rules, and market requirements can change, which may make generated calculations or compliance guidance stale. <br>
Mitigation: Verify outputs against current official emissions factors, reporting standards, carbon market rules, and local regulatory guidance before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Generated ESG reports, green finance guidance, and carbon neutrality plans may influence formal disclosures, financing decisions, or compliance commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified sustainability, legal, finance, and assurance reviewers validate outputs before publication, filing, issuance, or external use. <br>
