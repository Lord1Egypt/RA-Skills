## Description: <br>
Plan, organize, and track content with smart rotation - never publish the same format twice in a row. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Crispyangles](https://clawhub.ai/user/Crispyangles) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content creators, marketers, and publishing teams use this skill to plan recurring content, rotate formats, manage draft states, and maintain JSON trackers without granting publishing authority. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Content trackers and archived drafts may preserve confidential client, company, or personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep trackers in trusted storage and review drafts and performance notes for private information before publishing or archiving. <br>
Risk: Over-posting or repeated content formats may reduce reach or trigger platform throttling. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's maxPerDay control and rotation rule to avoid publishing the same format back to back or exceeding sustainable cadence limits. <br>
