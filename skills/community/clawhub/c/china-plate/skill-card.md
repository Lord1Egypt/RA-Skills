## Description: <br>
Parses and validates Chinese vehicle license plate numbers offline, identifying province, city, and plate type across regular, new energy, military, police, and learner plates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ToBeWin](https://clawhub.ai/user/ToBeWin) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to parse Chinese vehicle license plate numbers, identify registration province and city, classify common plate types, and validate plate formats offline. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Plate rules and city code assignments can change after publication, so results may become outdated for newly introduced or reassigned number ranges. <br>
Mitigation: Verify consequential plate interpretation against current public security traffic management notices or authoritative registration records. <br>
