# Market Stack Reference

Starter table for 20 markets. Numbers and methods shift; verify locally before publishing a plan.

| Market | Dominant marketplaces | Top 3 payments | Logistics norm | Returns expectation | Search behavior |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| US | Amazon, eBay, Walmart | Cards, PayPal, Buy-now-pay-later | UPS/USPS/FedEx; 2-5 day | 30 days, paid by seller (DTC) | Google + Amazon |
| UK | Amazon, eBay | Cards, PayPal, Klarna | Royal Mail / DPD | 14-30 days, paid by seller | Google + Amazon |
| Germany | Amazon, Otto, Zalando | Klarna, SEPA, PayPal, Cards | DHL | 14 days legal min, often free | Google + Idealo |
| France | Amazon, Cdiscount, Fnac | Cards, PayPal, BNPL | La Poste, Colissimo | 14 days legal min | Google + Amazon |
| Netherlands | Bol.com, Amazon | iDEAL, Klarna, Cards | PostNL | 14 days legal min | Google + Bol |
| Italy | Amazon, eBay | Cards, PayPal, Postepay | Poste Italiane | 14 days legal min | Google |
| Spain | Amazon, El Corte Inglés | Cards, Bizum, PayPal | Correos, MRW | 14 days legal min | Google + Amazon |
| Japan | Amazon JP, Rakuten, Yahoo Shopping | Cards, Konbini, Rakuten Pay, PayPay | Yamato, Sagawa | Strict; high standards | Yahoo + Google + Rakuten |
| Korea | Naver Smart Store, Coupang, Gmarket | KakaoPay, Naver Pay, Cards | CJ Logistics | Short, fast turnaround | Naver dominates |
| China | Tmall, Taobao, Douyin, Pinduoduo | Alipay, WeChat Pay | SF Express, JD Logistics | Short | Tmall/Douyin search |
| Australia | Amazon AU, eBay AU | Cards, PayPal, Afterpay | Australia Post | 30 days common | Google + Amazon |
| Brazil | Mercado Livre, Amazon BR | PIX, Boleto, Cards w/ parcelado | Correios | Variable | Google + Mercado |
| Mexico | Amazon MX, Mercado Libre | Cards, OXXO, SPEI | Estafeta, FedEx | Variable | Google + Amazon |
| Canada | Amazon CA | Cards, PayPal | Canada Post | 30 days common | Google + Amazon |
| Singapore | Lazada, Shopee, Amazon SG | Cards, PayNow, GrabPay | SingPost, Ninja Van | 7-14 days | Google + Shopee |
| India | Amazon IN, Flipkart | UPI, Cards, Cash on delivery | Ekart, BlueDart | 7-10 days; COD high | Google + Flipkart |
| UAE | Noon, Amazon AE | Cards, Cash on delivery | Aramex | Variable | Google + Noon |
| Saudi Arabia | Noon, Amazon SA | Mada, Cards, COD | SMSA | Variable | Google + Noon |
| Sweden | Amazon, CDON | Klarna, Swish, Cards | PostNord | 14 days legal min | Google + Prisjakt |
| Poland | Allegro, Amazon | BLIK, Cards, Cash on delivery | InPost lockers | 14 days legal min | Google + Allegro |

Use this as a sanity check, not a substitute for in-market research. Payment share, courier reliability, and marketplace dominance can swing significantly inside a year.
