## The Problem Every Other AI Tool Creates

Every AI marketing tool — ChatGPT, Gemini, Jasper, Copy.ai — generates addiction treatment marketing that contains multiple federal violations:

- **Google/Meta won't run your ads.** Both platforms require LegitScript certification to run ANY addiction treatment advertising. No AI tool knows this. The content it generates is ad spend that produces a suspended account.
- **"85% sobriety rate" is FTC false advertising.** Outcome statistics in addiction treatment marketing are prohibited unless backed by peer-reviewed research following SAMHSA methodology. No AI tool knows this.
- **"We coordinate with your doctor" violates 42 CFR Part 2.** SUD patient records are more protected than HIPAA. Inter-provider communication without Part 2-specific consent is a federal violation. No AI tool knows this.
- **"Call us — immediate admission available" may be patient brokering.** The FTC's most-targeted healthcare category. Criminal exposure when state lines are crossed.
- **"Certified counselors on staff" may be unlicensed advertising.** Nevada CADC credentials are distinct from LADC clinical licensure. Claiming "licensed" when staff hold CADC ≠ clinical license is misrepresentation.

This skill blocks every one of these violations before a single word of content is generated.
