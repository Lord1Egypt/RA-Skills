## Description: <br>
Information-feed ad video script assistant that turns a product name, selling points, and target duration into a structured shooting script with visuals, voiceover, subtitles, and timing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[suwangsen](https://clawhub.ai/user/suwangsen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and creative production teams use this skill to draft short-form information-feed advertising scripts for products. It helps structure scripts by duration, hook, pain point, selling point, product display, call to action, captions, and shooting notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated advertising copy may include unsupported product claims or language that conflicts with platform advertising policies. <br>
Mitigation: Review scripts for truthfulness, substantiation, platform policy compliance, and regulated-product restrictions before publishing. <br>
Risk: Inputs may contain confidential product plans or campaign details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the product information needed for script drafting and avoid unnecessary confidential details. <br>
