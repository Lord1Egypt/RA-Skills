## Description: <br>
Generates structured short-video IP strategy plans, topic libraries, and scripts from a Chinese daily-content methodology for founders, teams, and individual creators. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, founders, marketing teams, and employees use this skill to plan short-video positioning, daily publishing schedules, debate-oriented topic angles, and reusable speaking scripts. It is most applicable to business, technology, strategy, and product-led content workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may encourage aggressive controversy-based content that can create brand, platform, or audience-trust risks. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated topics and scripts before publication, remove unsupported claims or personal attacks, and align controversy level with brand policy and platform rules. <br>
Risk: The workflow can save user-specific business plans and may include confidential company, employee, customer, or product information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive information unless storage and reuse are acceptable; redact confidential details in prompts and generated plan files. <br>
Risk: Adjacent scraping, messaging, or publishing workflows can act on third-party platforms or send content to recipients. <br>
Mitigation: Use those workflows only after confirming authorization, platform terms, exact destinations, and a final human-reviewed preview. <br>
