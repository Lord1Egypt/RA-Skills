## Description: <br>
Creates, optimizes, and audits blog index or listing page structure, content strategy, SEO metadata, and internal linking guidance for blog pages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, marketers, and content teams use this skill to plan blog index pages, content hubs, topic clusters, SEO metadata, and internal linking for product or editorial sites. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use local project context files if they exist, which could include sensitive or irrelevant information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive or unrelated information out of `.claude/project-context.md` and `.cursor/project-context.md`, or review those files before invoking the skill. <br>
Risk: SEO and content strategy recommendations may be inaccurate for a specific market, audience, or site architecture. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current business goals, analytics, search intent, and site constraints before implementation. <br>
