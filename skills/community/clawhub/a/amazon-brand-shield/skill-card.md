## Description: <br>
Amazon brand protection toolkit for detecting hijackers, counterfeits, unauthorized sellers, MAP violations, and trademark abuse, with templates for Brand Registry reporting and evidence collection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce brand owners and marketplace operators use this skill to review Amazon listing risk, identify unauthorized sellers or counterfeit signals, and draft complaint or evidence templates for follow-up action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release is flagged as requiring sensitive credentials and capable of account actions or purchases. <br>
Mitigation: Keep credentials scoped to the user's role and require explicit confirmation before purchases, marketplace submissions, or other account-impacting actions. <br>
Risk: Generated complaint, legal, and marketplace-report templates may contain incomplete or inaccurate claims if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated reports and templates against collected evidence and applicable legal or marketplace requirements before sending or submitting them. <br>
