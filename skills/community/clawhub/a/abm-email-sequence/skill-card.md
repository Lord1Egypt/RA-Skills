## Description: <br>
Creates and optimizes multi-email automated flows such as email sequences, drip campaigns, nurture sequences, onboarding emails, welcome series, re-engagement emails, and lifecycle email programs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mariokarras](https://clawhub.ai/user/mariokarras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, lifecycle, and growth teams use this skill to plan automated email flows with sequence goals, triggers, timing, subject lines, preview text, email bodies, CTAs, segmentation conditions, and metrics. It supports welcome, onboarding, lead nurture, re-engagement, post-purchase, event-based, educational, and sales sequences. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include sensitive customer data, secrets, or confidential business details in local product-marketing context used by the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review local context files before use and remove secrets, unnecessary personal data, and sensitive customer information. <br>
Risk: Generated segmentation, trigger, unsubscribe, or consent recommendations may be inappropriate for a user's marketing, privacy, or compliance obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed email flows against internal policies and applicable marketing and privacy rules before deploying them. <br>
