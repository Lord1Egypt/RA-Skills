## Use Case: <br>
Quality managers, auditors, consultants, and operations teams use this skill to plan ISO 9001:2015 QMS implementation, run gap assessments and internal audits, classify nonconformities, and structure CAPA investigations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: QMS work can involve confidential audit evidence, employee records, supplier data, customer complaints, and proprietary documents. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive organizational records in the agent environment unless the user is comfortable processing that information there. <br>
Risk: ISO 9001 guidance can be misapplied if treated as a substitute for the current standard, auditor judgment, or certification-body requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Validate outputs against the applicable ISO 9001:2015 requirements, internal procedures, and qualified quality or audit personnel before relying on them. <br>
