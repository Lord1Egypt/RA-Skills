# Energy Transition & Carbon

## Overview
A specialized AI agent skill for sustainability managers, energy analysts, and ESG consultants. Enables data-driven energy transition planning and carbon management.

## Capabilities

### 1. Carbon Accounting
- Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions calculation per GHG Protocol
- Product carbon footprint (PCF) lifecycle analysis
- Supply chain emissions estimation using EEIO models
- Automated emission factor lookup (EPA, DEFRA, IPCC)

### 2. Renewable Energy Assessment
- Solar PV potential analysis using geospatial data
- Wind farm feasibility with wind resource mapping
- Energy storage sizing and optimization (BESS)
- PPA (Power Purchase Agreement) financial modeling

### 3. ESG Reporting
- Automated report generation (GRI, SASB, TCFD, ISSB, CSRD)
- ESG rating prediction and improvement roadmap
- Materiality assessment with stakeholder mapping
- Biodiversity impact screening (TNFD framework)

### 4. Carbon Markets
- Carbon credit portfolio management
- EU ETS / CCA allowance price forecasting
- Voluntary carbon market project evaluation (VERRA, Gold Standard)
- Carbon offset strategy optimization

### 5. Net-Zero Pathway
- Science-Based Targets (SBTi) alignment assessment
- Marginal abatement cost curve (MACC) generation
- Technology transition roadmapping (electrification, hydrogen, CCUS)
- Scenario analysis (NGFS, IEA scenarios)

## Usage Examples
- "Calculate Scope 3 emissions for my manufacturing supply chain"
- "Assess solar potential for 3 warehouse rooftops in Arizona"
- "Generate TCFD-aligned climate risk disclosure"
- "Evaluate 5 carbon offset projects for portfolio inclusion"
- "Model net-zero pathway for a steel manufacturer by 2040"

## Data Sources
EPA eGRID, EU ETS registry, NREL solar data, IEA statistics, CDP disclosures, and satellite imagery.
