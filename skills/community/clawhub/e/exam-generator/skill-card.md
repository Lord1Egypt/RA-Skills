## Description: <br>
Generates printable exam papers and answer keys for Chinese primary and middle school subjects, using web search to check current knowledge and answers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobewin](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobewin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Teachers and education-content creators use this skill to draft Chinese primary and middle school exams by subject, grade, knowledge point, question type, difficulty, and duration. It can produce formatted question papers and answer material for human review before classroom use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Web search use may expose student personal information, confidential school materials, or unpublished exam content if those details are included in prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include sensitive student or institutional information in requests, and keep unpublished exam content out of search-backed prompts. <br>
Risk: Generated questions, answers, or curriculum alignment may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review all generated questions and answers against current curriculum standards before classroom use. <br>
