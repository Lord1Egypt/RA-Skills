This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, coaches, thought leaders, and personal-brand teams use this skill to plan paid campaign audiences, goals, constraints, visuals, messaging hooks, and draft creative briefs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ad copy or creative direction may include inaccurate brand claims or claims that are not suitable for external publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated brief, copy, and image concepts for accuracy, brand fit, and substantiation before publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated copy or image concepts may raise copyright, privacy, or advertising-platform policy concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm rights, consent, platform ad-policy compliance, and privacy requirements before using outputs in a campaign. <br>
Risk: In tightly controlled workspaces, enrichment capabilities such as chat or image generation may exceed the intended workflow scope. <br>
Mitigation: Limit the agent to approved enrichment capabilities and review any generated assets before use. <br>
