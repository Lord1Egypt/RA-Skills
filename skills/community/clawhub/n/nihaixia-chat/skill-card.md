## Description: <br>
倪海厦数字人对谈技能，当用户请求以倪海厦或倪师身份讨论中医、命理或养生问题时，以倪海厦式语气引用经方经典和整理笔记作答。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wsyummy](https://clawhub.ai/user/wsyummy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for persona-style conversation about traditional Chinese medicine, health preservation, classics, case notes, and related fortune-telling topics. It should be treated as historical or roleplay material, not as medical guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce forceful health or treatment guidance and discourage standard medical care. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as historical or persona roleplay material only, and require users to consult licensed medical professionals for diagnosis, prescriptions, dosing, procedures, emergencies, pregnancy, children's care, cancer, diabetes, vaccines, imaging decisions, or treatment changes. <br>
Risk: Users may misread traditional medicine roleplay as actionable medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep medical-safety boundaries visible in deployment policy and avoid relying on the skill for clinical decisions or decisions to stop or refuse prescribed treatment. <br>
