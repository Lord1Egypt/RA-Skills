## Description: <br>
Guides structured help for level design reviews using clear templates, checks, and safe defaults. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abdullah944](https://clawhub.ai/user/abdullah944) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game creators and level designers use this skill to review platformer, top-down, puzzle, combat, tutorial, and small indie game levels before building, testing, or publishing them. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share private game assets or unreleased project details while asking for review help. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive project material unless it is appropriate for the agent conversation. <br>
Risk: Checklist advice may overfit a level before it has been playtested. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer small playable improvements and validate changes through playtesting before larger redesigns. <br>
