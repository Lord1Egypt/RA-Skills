## Description: <br>
Aggregates and summarizes the latest AI news from multiple sources including AI news websites and web search, providing concise news briefs with direct links to original articles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nicky1108](https://clawhub.ai/user/nicky1108) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to gather current AI news, filter duplicate or low-value items, and receive a categorized daily briefing with source links. It supports broad updates and focused follow-ups by topic, company, timeframe, depth, or format. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill browses current AI news sources and can produce time-sensitive summaries that depend on source availability and article accuracy. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when current AI news is desired, review source links, and ask the agent not to use the news workflow for conceptual or non-news AI discussion. <br>
Risk: News aggregation can include duplicate stories, stale items, paywalled excerpts, or summaries that need verification. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer original sources, validate links and timestamps, deduplicate overlapping coverage, and note limitations when full article content is unavailable. <br>
