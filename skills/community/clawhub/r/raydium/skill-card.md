## Description: <br>
Audit Raydium liquidity positions before capital is deployed by analyzing depth, concentration, liquidity quality, and structural pool risks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Duclawbot](https://clawhub.ai/user/Duclawbot) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External liquidity providers, DeFi users, and operators use this skill to screen Raydium pools before adding capital, moving size, or relying on a pool's apparent liquidity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pool assessments may be incomplete or misleading if current on-chain depth, concentration, token quality, or parameter data is missing. <br>
Mitigation: Use current pool data and independent verification before deploying capital; do not treat the output as financial advice or a guarantee of pool safety. <br>
