## Description: <br>
Survival protocol for when income drops and debts pile up, covering payment priority, creditor negotiation, debt collector response, validation letters, statutes of limitation, settlement strategies, and likely consequences of nonpayment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and assisting agents use this skill when income loss, delinquency, debt collection, or debt lawsuits require a practical payment triage plan. It helps prioritize essential bills, draft creditor and collector communications, track deadlines, and identify when legal aid or crisis support should be escalated. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with jurisdiction-specific verification required for legal rights, debt collection rules, credit reporting, bankruptcy, and limitation periods. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save sensitive financial and legal details, including debt records, account references, draft letters, collector contacts, and deadline information. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, avoid full account numbers unless necessary, prefer in-session-only workflows for sensitive matters, and delete generated files, reminders, and agent state when the debt matter is resolved. <br>
Risk: The skill includes US-specific debt collection and credit reporting concepts that may be wrong or incomplete for users in other jurisdictions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's country or region before jurisdiction-specific guidance, cite local legal sources, and warn users to verify local rights and procedures before taking legal action. <br>
