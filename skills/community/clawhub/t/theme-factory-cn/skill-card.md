## Description: <br>
为幻灯片、文档、报告和 HTML 页面提供主题配色、字体建议、CSS 变量和版式指导。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[paudyyin](https://clawhub.ai/user/paudyyin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Chinese-speaking employees, external users, and creators use this skill to choose or generate coordinated color palettes and font pairings for presentations, documents, reports, and HTML pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Theme suggestions may be unsuitable for important documents when the user request is broad or underspecified. <br>
Mitigation: Review the proposed palette, fonts, and layout guidance before applying them to important presentations, documents, reports, or pages. <br>
Risk: Generated visual guidance may not fully satisfy accessibility or brand requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Check text/background contrast, brand fit, and audience expectations before final use. <br>
