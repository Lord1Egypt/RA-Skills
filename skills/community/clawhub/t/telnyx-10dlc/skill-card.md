## Description: <br>
Register for 10DLC as a sole proprietor to enable SMS messaging in the USA. Use when setting up A2P SMS, registering brands/campaigns, or assigning phone numbers for compliant US messaging. Requires Telnyx CLI. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill to guide Telnyx 10DLC sole proprietor registration, campaign setup, status checks, and US phone number assignment for compliant A2P SMS messaging. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses the Telnyx CLI/API key to perform 10DLC registration, campaign, and number-assignment work. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when Telnyx 10DLC work is intended, use the narrowest practical Telnyx API key, and review all brand details, campaign IDs, and phone numbers before confirming actions. <br>
Risk: Incorrect campaign details, sample messages, or phone number assignments can delay approval or affect compliant SMS messaging. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the business context, use case, opt-out language, campaign ID, and US phone number before submitting or assigning through the scripts. <br>
