## Description: <br>
Email Assistant helps agents classify user-provided emails, draft professional replies, extract tasks and deadlines, summarize long messages, and prioritize follow-up. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[crazypatient0](https://clawhub.ai/user/crazypatient0) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users and professionals paste or describe email content so the agent can triage messages, draft responses, summarize long threads, and extract action items for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Email content can contain confidential or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only selected email text needed for the task and avoid pasting confidential messages unnecessarily. <br>
Risk: Drafted replies, extracted tasks, summaries, or priorities may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outputs before sending replies or acting on extracted tasks. <br>
