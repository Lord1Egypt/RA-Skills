## Description: <br>
技能诊所 helps agents diagnose, score, optimize, and create skill files using a structured Gene-style workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fslong520](https://clawhub.ai/user/fslong520) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and skill authors use this skill to review existing skills for structure, trigger quality, concision, and operational usefulness, then receive a score and improvement suggestions. They can also use it to create new skill files from collected requirements and built-in templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated or edited skill triggers may activate too broadly. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target directory and review generated trigger metadata before allowing file changes. <br>
Risk: Diagnostic recommendations may introduce incorrect or misleading guidance into skill files. <br>
Mitigation: Review the report and scan changed skills before deployment. <br>
