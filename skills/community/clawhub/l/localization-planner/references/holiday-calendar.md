# Local Promotional Calendar Starter

Top 10 markets. Dates fluctuate; pull a current calendar before publishing a plan.

## United States
- Memorial Day (late May): outdoor / grilling peak
- Independence Day (July 4): apparel + outdoor
- Back to School (Aug-Sep)
- Black Friday + Cyber 5 (late Nov)
- Christmas (Dec 25), gift-buying window Nov-Dec 22

## United Kingdom
- Mother's Day (4th Sunday of Lent, March-ish)
- Boxing Day + Jan sales (Dec 26 onward)
- Black Friday (imported, growing)
- Bank Holidays (May / Aug): weekend peaks

## Germany
- Easter (Frühlingsfest categories)
- Spargelzeit (asparagus season, late April-June): outdoor/food
- Oktoberfest (Sep-Oct): regional
- Black Friday (growing; under US scale)
- Weihnachten (Dec 24-26): primary gifting

## France
- Soldes (state-mandated sales windows, winter and summer)
- Fête des Mères (late May / early June)
- Christmas + Réveillon

## Japan
- Golden Week (late April / early May)
- Obon (mid August)
- Rakuten Super Sale (multiple per year)
- Year-end / oseibo gifting (December)

## China
- Singles' Day / 11.11 (Nov 11): largest single sales day globally
- 6.18 (June 18): JD-led mid-year sales
- Chinese New Year (Jan-Feb): commerce slowdown
- Qixi (Aug): romantic gifting
- Double 12 (Dec 12)

## Brazil
- Carnaval (Feb): commerce soft for some categories
- Mother's Day (2nd Sunday of May): bigger than Valentine's
- Black Friday (huge in BR)
- Christmas

## India
- Diwali (Oct-Nov): single largest commerce peak
- Big Billion Days (Flipkart) / Great Indian Sale (Amazon IN) around Diwali
- Holi (March)
- Raksha Bandhan (Aug)

## Saudi Arabia / UAE
- Ramadan + Eid al-Fitr (shifts each year): commerce reshapes around iftar timing
- Eid al-Adha
- White Friday (late Nov, regional equivalent)
- National Day campaigns

## Australia
- EOFY sales (June 30)
- Christmas (summer in AU, gift category mix differs from northern hemisphere)
- Boxing Day sales (Dec 26)
- Click Frenzy (multiple per year)

Holiday timing affects creative lead time, inventory pre-positioning, and ad-budget allocation. Plan creative and stock 6-8 weeks before each major peak.
