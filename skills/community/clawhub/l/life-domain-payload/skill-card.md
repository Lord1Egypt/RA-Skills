## Description: <br>
Life Domain Payload is a Chinese-language life-planning skill that organizes 534 tasks across 12 domains, including health, wealth, education, career, family, social relationships, legal rights, technology, creative work, civic responsibility, spiritual growth, and life operations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill as a structured Chinese-language planning framework for life-domain tasks. It helps route requests to domain-specific catalogs, requirements, and exemplars for draft plans, reports, suggestions, and workflow templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers medical, financial, legal, and mental-health-adjacent decisions without consistently strong professional-review and crisis safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning or educational drafts and obtain licensed medical, legal, tax, financial, or crisis-support review before acting on high-stakes guidance. <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive personal records while asking for life-domain planning help. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing unnecessary sensitive records, credentials, account details, or private health, financial, legal, and family information. <br>
