## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can help post or schedule content to real social media accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval of the caption, media, target platforms, connected accounts, and schedule before creating any post. <br>
Risk: The Post Bridge API key can grant access to connected posting workflows if exposed. <br>
Mitigation: Keep POST_BRIDGE_API_KEY private, store it outside committed files, and verify that only intended accounts are connected. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing content or schedules may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for a platform. <br>
Mitigation: Review captions, media, hashtags, calls to action, and scheduling choices before publishing. <br>
