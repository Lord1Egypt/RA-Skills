## Description: <br>
Translate strategy into channel-specific execution plans for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Amazon Ads, Shopify Ads, and DSP/programmatic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Growth marketers, media buyers, and operators use this skill to turn advertising strategy into channel-specific execution plans, setup checklists, budget and bidding plans, test matrices, and scale or stop rules across major ad platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ad execution guidance may affect real advertising spend if acted on without review. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human review recommendations, assumptions, and stop-loss conditions before changing live budgets or campaigns. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad business-growth requests when the user wants general strategy instead of ad execution planning. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify whether the request is for advertising execution, and ask for objective, budget frame, and channel scope when those inputs are missing. <br>
