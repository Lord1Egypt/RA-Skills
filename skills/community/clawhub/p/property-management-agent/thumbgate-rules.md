# ThumbGate Prevention Rules – Property Management

1. **Emergency Verification** — Block any 'Emergency' dispatch unless the transcript contains clear evidence of water, fire, or safety risk.
2. **Spending Limit** — Block authorized repair work where the estimated cost exceeds 00.
3. **Tenant Privacy** — Block any response that reveals PII (e.g., unit door codes or owner phone numbers) to unverified callers.
4. **Outcome Management** — Block promises of "Same Day" or "Immediate" service for routine maintenance.
