## Description: <br>
Startup growth consulting agent specializing in Product Hunt launches, open-source marketing, and Go-to-Market audits. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup teams use this skill to review Product Hunt launch assets, open-source project growth mechanics, and go-to-market positioning, then turn feedback into prioritized two-week growth sprints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential launch plans, repository details, positioning, or growth strategy while seeking advice. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information intended for analysis and remove sensitive business details before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Growth recommendations may influence public launch, messaging, or channel decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as strategy input and review them before changing campaigns, launch plans, or public messaging. <br>
