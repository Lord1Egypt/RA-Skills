# Known Chinglish Test Cases

## Pattern A: Subject Dangling
For chip design, competition is fierce among all players. Regarding the ASIC market, it is growing rapidly.

## Pattern B: Redundant "As For"
As for the energy cost, it is becoming the main constraint. When it comes to data centers, power efficiency matters.

## Pattern C: Passive Chain Stacking
It can be seen that the market is being reshaped by export controls. It should be noted that NVIDIA is being challenged by AMD.

## Pattern D: "Make + abstract noun"
This makes the competition harder to win. We need to make a comparison between two architectures. Let's have a discussion about the impact.

## Pattern E: "Not only...but also"
Not only did NVIDIA dominate hardware, but also it built a software ecosystem that competitors cannot match.

## Pattern G: "Should" false obligation
China should develop its own chip industry to reduce dependency. Companies should strengthen their supply chains.

## Pattern H: "The" overuse for generic
The computing power is the most important resource in the modern era. The supply chain must be resilient.

## Pattern I: Chinese filler expressions
To a certain extent, competition benefits consumers. In the process of building data centers, power is critical. As we all know, AI requires massive compute.

## Pattern J: "This is because" structure
This is because the technological barriers are high. This is why NVIDIA dominates.

## Pattern K: Redundant modifier
Companies should actively strengthen their supply chain resilience. Engineers continuously improve the design.

## Pattern L: Weak verb + nominalization
The team conducted research on GPU design and performed analysis on power consumption.

## Pattern M: Emphasis inversion
It is this gap that explains the performance difference. It was the policy shift that triggered the redesign.

## Pattern N: List cadence (aggregated)
First, the market is growing. Second, competition is intensifying. Finally, consolidation is expected.

## Pattern O: Concessive cluster
Although the market is large, but competition is fierce. Though the technology is promising, but adoption is slow.

## Pattern P: Topic-comment fronting
As for NVIDIA, it dominates the AI market. Speaking of AMD, it is still catching up.

## Pattern Q: False inclusive "We"
We can see that the landscape is changing rapidly. We should note that this is an unprecedented development.

## Pattern R: Logical sawtooth
On one hand, the market is growing. On the other hand, competition is intense.

## Pattern S: List summarizer
All these factors contribute to the market growth. These challenges make the environment difficult.

## Clean text (should score 5.0)
NVIDIA dominates the AI chip market because it built a decade-long software ecosystem around CUDA. No competitor has replicated this moat. AMD attempts to close the gap with its ROCm platform, but adoption remains low.
