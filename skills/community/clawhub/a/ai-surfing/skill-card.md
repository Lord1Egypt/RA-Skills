## Description: <br>
Ai Surfing provides surfing guidance for board selection, paddling and pop-up fundamentals, advanced maneuvers, surf forecast interpretation, global surf spots, surf culture, and ocean-safety basics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and surfing learners use this skill to ask practical questions about choosing surfboards, learning core techniques, reading surf reports, planning surf trips, and understanding common ocean-safety issues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded surfing videos may reveal location, identity, minors, bystanders, or other private details. <br>
Mitigation: Use text-only questions when video is not needed, and review footage for private details before uploading. <br>
Risk: Surfing guidance can be incomplete for local, real-time ocean conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat skill output as general education and check local surf reports, posted warnings, lifeguards, or qualified instructors before entering the water. <br>
