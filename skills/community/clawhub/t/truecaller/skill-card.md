## Description: <br>
Organizes public Truecaller product pages, feature descriptions, download entry points, permissions notes, and help information into clear summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to summarize public Truecaller feature pages, platform-specific download/version entry points, privacy and permissions explanations, and help or FAQ content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may summarize outdated or unofficial Truecaller information if the source pages are not checked carefully. <br>
Mitigation: Use public Truecaller pages and help documentation as the source of record, and keep platform-specific differences explicit. <br>
Risk: Users may provide private contacts, account sessions, secrets, or local files that are not needed for the skill's public-information task. <br>
Mitigation: Do not request or use private data or credentials; rely only on publicly available Truecaller information unless a future version clearly discloses a need. <br>
