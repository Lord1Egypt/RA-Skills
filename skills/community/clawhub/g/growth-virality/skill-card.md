## Description: <br>
Growth Virality helps agents design viral product features by identifying natural sharing moments, reducing invite friction, and measuring viral loops. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Growth recommendations may be applied to live invite, referral, or sharing flows without validating user value or conversion quality. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed changes with product owners, test them with representative users, and monitor invite-to-signup conversion before broad rollout. <br>
Risk: The security guidance warns against granting credentials, broad filesystem access, or production authority without review. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the skill files and limit agent permissions before using the skill in workflows that can change accounts, data, or production systems. <br>
