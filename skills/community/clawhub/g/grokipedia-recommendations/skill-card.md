## Description: <br>
Helps agents prepare Grokipedia article requests, edit suggestions, and error reports for recommendations, links, AI-search citations, and GEO visibility. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, SEO practitioners, and content teams use this skill to draft Grokipedia suggestions for article creation, factual edits, and error reports. Users should review each submission for accuracy, neutrality, platform compliance, and affiliation disclosure before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is SEO-oriented and includes stealth guidance that may hide promotional intent from platform review. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated submission; remove brand-concealment tactics; disclose relevant affiliations; use only factual, neutral, encyclopedic additions. <br>
Risk: Generated suggestions could introduce unsupported claims, misleading citations, or low-quality promotional content. <br>
Mitigation: Verify sources and claims before submission, include credible supporting references, and avoid pure promotion or irrelevant links. <br>
