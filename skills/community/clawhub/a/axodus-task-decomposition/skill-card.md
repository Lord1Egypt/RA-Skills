## Description: <br>
Break complex tasks into executable, dependency-aware steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mzfshark](https://clawhub.ai/user/mzfshark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to turn vague or multi-part requests into ordered plans with dependencies, acceptance criteria, open questions, and validation steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Placeholder trigger metadata may cause the skill to run less predictably than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Replace the placeholder trigger with clear activation phrases or conditions before relying on automatic invocation. <br>
Risk: A generated plan may propose incorrect, incomplete, or destructive steps if the original task is underspecified. <br>
Mitigation: Review open questions, assumptions, risk tags, and validation checks before executing the plan; require explicit confirmation for destructive actions. <br>
