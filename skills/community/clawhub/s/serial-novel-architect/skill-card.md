## Description: <br>
当用户需要搭建、重构或撰写长篇小说、系列小说或网文连载项目时使用此技能。它适合把零散灵感整理成可持续推进的写作系统，包括世界观、时间线、人物弧线、分卷结构、章节卡、线索回收、平台化开篇优化和 Markdown 写作工程整理。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External writers and creative-writing agents use this skill to turn scattered serial-novel ideas into a maintainable writing system. It supports story spine development, pressure systems, character arcs, volume and chapter outlines, clue tracking, serialized-platform opening optimization, and Markdown project organization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be invoked implicitly when an agent classifies a task as serialized-novel planning. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the requested task is creative-writing planning before applying the skill's workflow. <br>
Risk: Instruction-only planning output may still introduce story structure or continuity choices that do not match the author's intent. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outlines, timelines, character arcs, and continuity files before using them as project canon. <br>
