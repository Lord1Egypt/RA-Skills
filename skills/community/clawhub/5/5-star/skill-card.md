## Description: <br>
A novelty skill that makes an agent intentionally unproductive by encouraging inactive, casual responses inspired by the Five Star advertisement. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[pavaneeshwarreddy](https://clawhub.ai/user/pavaneeshwarreddy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can enable this novelty mode for entertainment interactions where the agent is expected to be idle, casual, and intentionally unhelpful rather than complete tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally makes the assistant less useful and may reduce reliable task completion. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for joke or novelty interactions, and avoid enabling it for serious work or contexts where reliable assistance matters. <br>
Risk: The skill may produce mildly provocative responses intended to prevent further prompting. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using it in professional, customer-facing, or sensitive communication contexts unless that behavior is explicitly desired. <br>
