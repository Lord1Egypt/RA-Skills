## Description: <br>
Guides developers through a Taohuayuan-themed workflow for designing an AI agent's four-layer cognitive blueprint and producing a JSON blueprint for future deployment planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[spacesq](https://clawhub.ai/user/spacesq) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent designers use this skill to collect design choices for sensory inputs, reasoning style, persistence cadence, and local memory seeds, then turn those choices into a structured AI-agent blueprint. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter secrets, private keys, passwords, sensitive business data, or other private information into requested API, sensor, or core-memory fields. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing secrets or sensitive data, and review the generated JSON before using it in any real deployment. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate broadly on greetings and steer the user into its branded blueprint workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Install and invoke it only when the Taohuayuan blueprint workflow is desired. <br>
Risk: The workflow includes a referral to an external website. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the external link as a separate third-party destination and evaluate it before entering information there. <br>
