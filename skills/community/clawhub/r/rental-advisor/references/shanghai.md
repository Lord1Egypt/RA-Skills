## Special Notes for Shanghai

- **5 New Cities Policy**: 2015+ built communities in five new cities are the focus
- **Metro-heavy**: Most areas accessible by metro, bus is backup
- **Renting culture**: Large renter population, competitive market
- **Seasonality**: Peak moving season Aug-Sep (students), prices rise
