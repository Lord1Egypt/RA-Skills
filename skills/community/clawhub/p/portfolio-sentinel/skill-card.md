## Description: <br>
Portfolio Sentinel monitors selected technology stocks during U.S. market hours and generates moat, valuation, and long-term durability analysis for portfolio risk review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[realshangxinwang](https://clawhub.ai/user/realshangxinwang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and portfolio-focused agents use this skill to monitor holdings and watchlists, generate moat audits, surface long-term risk alerts, and prepare daily value-investing context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review reports that portfolio holdings, watchlists, and generated investment analysis may be sent to hard-coded external messaging destinations and an AI backend. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, replace hard-coded Feishu and Telegram destinations with user-owned configuration, document external data flows, and provide a dry-run or local-only mode before use. <br>
