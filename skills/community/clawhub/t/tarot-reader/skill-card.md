## Description: <br>
Provides single-card and three-card Rider-Waite tarot readings when users ask about tarot, drawing cards, or divination, using upright and reversed interpretations in a warm but grounded style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to receive reflective tarot guidance through a single-card reading or a past-present-future spread tied to their own question. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tarot questions and saved readings may contain sensitive personal relationship, health, financial, or work details. <br>
Mitigation: Treat readings as personal conversation content, ask before saving records, and avoid storing sensitive details unless storage and deletion expectations are clear. <br>
