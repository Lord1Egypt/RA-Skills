# Flying Stars Feng Shui (玄空飞星)

The Xuankong Flying Stars system (玄空飞星) is a time-sensitive Feng Shui system that analyzes the movement of energy (qi) through a building over different time periods.

---

## 1. Core Concepts

### The Three Stars (三大星)

| Star | Represents | Source |
|:----:|:-----------|:-------|
| **运星 (Period Star)** | The dominant energy of the current time period | Always placed in center, determined by current Period |
| **山星 (Mountain Star)** | The energy of the sitting direction — affects people, health, family | Derived from the sitting direction relative to the Period Star |
| **向星 (Water Star)** | The energy of the facing direction — affects wealth, career | Derived from the facing direction relative to the Period Star |

### The Nine Periods (三元九运)

Time is divided into 20-year periods within a 180-year cycle:

| Period | Years | Element | Character |
|:------:|:------|:-------:|:----------|
| 上元一运 | 1864-1883 | 水 | White |
| 上元二运 | 1884-1903 | 土 | Black |
| 上元三运 | 1904-1923 | 木 | Jade |
| 中元四运 | 1924-1943 | 木 | Green |
| 中元五运 | 1944-1963 | 土 | Yellow |
| 中元六运 | 1964-1983 | 金 | White |
| **下元七运** | 1984-2003 | 金 | Red |
| **下元八运** | 2004-2023 | 土 | White |
| **下元九运** | 2024-2043 | 火 | Purple |

**Current Period (as of 2026):** 下元九运 (Period 9) — Fire element, Purple Star. The next period shift will be in 2044.

## 3. The Flying Star Chart (飞星盘)

### Step 1: Determine the Period (运)
Current period determines the central star of the Period Star chart.
- If house was built 2004-2023 → Period 8 chart
- If house was built 2024-2043 → Period 9 chart

### Step 2: Period Star Central Number
- Period 8 → central number is 8
- Period 9 → central number is 9

### Step 3: Forward Direction of the Period Star

The order of star placement follows the 洛书 (Luo Shu) magic square:

```
4    9    2
3    5    7
8    1    6
```

**Forward direction (顺飞):** For yang years and yang directions — 5→6→7→8→9→1→2→3→4
**Reverse direction (逆飞):** For yin years and yin directions — 5→4→3→2→1→9→8→7→6

### Step 4: Complete the Mountain and Water Star Charts

The Period Star chart is the base. Mountain Stars and Water Stars are placed by:
1. Identify the sitting and facing directions in the Period Star chart
2. Determine whether the sitting and facing stars fly forward (顺) or reverse (逆)
3. Place accordingly in each of the 9 squares

## 4. Interpreting the Flying Star Chart (断盘)

### Each square (宫) contains 3 numbers:
```
[Period Star]
[Mountain Star]  [Water Star]
```

### Auspicious combinations (吉格):

| Combination | Meaning |
|:-----------:|:--------|
| **8-8-8** | Triple 8 White — immense wealth (rare) |
| **1-6-8** | Three auspicious stars — best combination |
| **9 + 1** | Fire + Water → harmony, balance |
| **9 + 4** | Fire + Wood → academic and literary success |
| **8 + 1** | Earth + Water → wealth |

### Inauspicious combinations (凶格):

| Combination | Meaning |
|:-----------:|:--------|
| **5-2-5** | Sickness star combination — serious health issues |
| **2-2-2** | Triple sickness star |
| **7-7-7** | Triple robbery star — financial loss |
| **5 + 2** | "二五交加" — unfortunate combination |
| **3 + 7** | "三七同宫" — armed robbery, violence |
| **2 + 3** | "斗牛煞" — argument and conflict |

### Location influence:
- **Front (向首)** — the facing direction square — most important for wealth
- **Sitting (坐山)** — the sitting direction square — affects people's health
- **Center (中宫)** — the central square — overall energy of the house
- **Kitchen (灶位)** — stove location must avoid 5 Yellow and 2 Black
- **Bedroom (卧房)** — master bedroom favorable with auspicious star combination
- **Door (大门)** — front door location activates the star energy at that palace
