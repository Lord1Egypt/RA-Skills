## Description: <br>
Creates and resumes Chinese xuanhuan, xianxia, and cultivation novel projects by coordinating outline discussion, drafting, review, revision, and final TXT formatting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kongzichixiangjiao](https://clawhub.ai/user/kongzichixiangjiao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers and creative agents use this skill to generate long-form Chinese fantasy novel materials, including direction notes, roundtable outline drafts, integrated outlines, chapter drafts, review reports, revisions, and a final formatted TXT manuscript. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may create many draft, review, revision, and final manuscript files in its named workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in a dedicated workspace and review generated output paths before re-running if overwrites would matter. <br>
Risk: The workflow resumes from prior files in its workspace and may continue from existing content without follow-up questions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep unrelated private files out of the workspace and review existing project files before starting or resuming a run. <br>
