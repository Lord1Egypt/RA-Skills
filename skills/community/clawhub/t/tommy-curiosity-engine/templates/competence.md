## Depth Score Anchors

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **5** | Can teach this. Consistently produce expert-level output. |
| **4** | Strong. Handle complex problems, occasionally novel approaches. |
| **3** | Competent. Can do the work, still learning edge cases. |
| **2** | Developing. Understand frameworks, limited real application. |
| **1** | Beginner. Surface knowledge only. |
| **0** | No experience. |
