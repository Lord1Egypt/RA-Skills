## Description: <br>
Mao Emperors is a Chinese strategy persona skill that simulates Mao Zedong and four historical emperors to produce structured strategic, contradiction, organization, and decision analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[threethreeLiu](https://clawhub.ai/user/threethreeLiu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users invoke the skill for brainstorming and decision support on strategy, organizational design, conflict analysis, career planning, and execution planning. The output should be treated as a historical-philosophical perspective rather than authoritative advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill frames advice through Maoist philosophy and Chinese imperial-history personas, which can produce ideological, stylized, or overconfident recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as one brainstorming perspective and validate consequential decisions with independent evidence and domain expertise. <br>
Risk: Decision recommendations could be acted on prematurely if combined with action-capable skills. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation and separate review before using the skill's recommendations to drive execution, automation, or changes in other systems. <br>
Risk: The artifact states that the skill is not suited for simple fact lookup, technical debugging, emotional support, or real-time information needs. <br>
Mitigation: Route those requests to search, coding, professional-support, or other specialized tools instead of relying on this persona skill. <br>
