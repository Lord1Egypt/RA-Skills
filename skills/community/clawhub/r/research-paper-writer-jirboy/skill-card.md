## Description: <br>
Generates formal academic research paper content using standard paper structures and IEEE, ACM, ASCE, and Elsevier formatting guidance, while this release serves as a compatibility layer for the unified paper skill. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, students, and researchers use this skill to draft research paper sections and apply academic structure and formatting conventions. It is especially relevant for IEEE, ACM, ASCE, and Elsevier-style academic writing workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release points users toward a separate unified paper skill that is not covered by this evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review the separate paper skill before relying on forwarded or migrated behavior. <br>
Risk: Academic writing assistance can produce content that needs factual, citation, and formatting review before use. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer verify claims, references, and target publication formatting before submission. <br>
