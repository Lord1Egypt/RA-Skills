## Description: <br>
Chen Humanizer rewrites AI-sounding prose into more natural human-written text by detecting common AI writing patterns and preserving the intended meaning and voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cs995279497-byte](https://clawhub.ai/user/cs995279497-byte) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and content reviewers use this skill to revise selected passages or documents so they read less like AI-generated text while preserving the original meaning, tone, and factual claims. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting can change factual accuracy, tone, or the author's intended voice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill on selected passages or known document files and review the output for factual accuracy, tone, and unintended voice changes before accepting edits. <br>
Risk: Document-editing permissions can modify user-provided files. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed edits or file diffs before keeping changes. <br>
