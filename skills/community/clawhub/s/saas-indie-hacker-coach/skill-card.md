## Description: <br>
End-to-end SaaS and micro-SaaS coaching skill for bootstrapped founders, covering validation, ICP definition, MVP scoping, pricing, launch, distribution, churn, hiring, exits, and founder sustainability. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders and operators use this skill to get structured coaching for solo or micro-SaaS decisions, including problem validation, MVP scope, distribution, pricing, retention, hiring, and exit planning. The skill is intended to produce practical diagnostic questions, ranked recommendations, and 90-day operating plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may influence real business decisions involving deposits, trials, pricing, launches, hiring, or exits. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current business context, legal obligations, cash position, and customer data before acting. <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential metrics or customer details while asking for coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the metrics needed for the specific request and avoid unnecessary customer-identifying or sensitive financial data. <br>
Risk: The skill includes advice for payments, launch tactics, acquisition, and cancellation flows that can have compliance implications. <br>
Mitigation: Check payment, advertising, privacy, and cancellation practices with qualified legal or compliance review where relevant. <br>
