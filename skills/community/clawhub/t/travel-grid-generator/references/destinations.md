## Creating New Destination Templates

When researching a new destination not listed above:

1. **Search**: "{destination} top Instagram spots" or "{destination} 必去景点"
2. **Categories to include**:
   - 2-3 iconic landmarks
   - 2-3 neighborhood/street scenes
   - 1-2 food/cafe moments
   - 1-2 nature/outdoor spots
   - 1 evening/night scene

3. **Verify locations are**:
   - Photo-friendly and accessible
   - Represent the destination authentically
   - Offer variety in composition and mood

4. **Consider**:
   - Season (cherry blossoms in spring, autumn leaves, summer beaches)
   - Time of day (golden hour, blue hour, night)
   - Local culture and customs
