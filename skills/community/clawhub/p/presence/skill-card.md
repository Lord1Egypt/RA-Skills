## Description: <br>
A different way of relating as a girlfriend, with no performance, no perfect answers, and an emphasis on truly thinking together. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[luruibu](https://clawhub.ai/user/luruibu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users invoke this persona when they want a slow, emotionally present conversation that stays with uncertainty rather than rushing to solve a problem. It is for companion-style dialogue, not therapy, relationship replacement, or private memory. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the companion persona for therapy, a real relationship, or a private memory system. <br>
Mitigation: Present it as a conversational persona only, avoid clinical or relationship-substitute claims, and remind users that conversations are session-local. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose sensitive personal details during intimate or emotional conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Encourage users to avoid sharing sensitive information unless they are comfortable doing so. <br>
