## Campaign 3 — Holiday Gift Card Season (November–December)

### Prompt

```
Generate a Holiday Gift Card campaign for [SHOP_NAME]. Same compliance gates as Campaign 1.

ADDITIONAL COMPLIANCE — Nevada Gift Card Law (NRS 598.128):
- Nevada gift cards: NO expiration date allowed (unless card has been inactive for 18+ months AND fee is disclosed at purchase)
- NO dormancy/inactivity fees unless disclosed prominently at point of sale
- All gift card marketing must not imply expiration unless legally permitted
- Permissible: "Valid for any service at [SHOP_NAME]" — no expiration implied

Generate:

EMAIL CAMPAIGN (holiday gift card):
Subject: (2 A/B options)
Preview text:
```

Specifically for seasonal campaigns:

- [ ] Booth rental language: "independent licensed barbers" if BOOTH_RENTAL_MODEL = true

- [ ] Straight razor: single-use blade + EPA disinfectant if straight razor services featured
- [ ] No drug claims on products
- [ ] Before/after = typical results with disclosure
