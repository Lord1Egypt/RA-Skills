## Description: <br>
Save travel inspiration from YouTube videos, Instagram reels, blog URLs, tweets, or plain text like "want to visit Hampi". <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[swayamg20](https://clawhub.ai/user/swayamg20) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and trip-planning users use this skill to save destinations from URLs or short notes, tag them by attributes such as vibe, region, budget, and source, and recall saved places later. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may fetch saved links and retain trip notes or URLs in memory. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving sensitive personal itineraries, private booking links, or URLs containing tokens unless long-term memory storage is acceptable. <br>
