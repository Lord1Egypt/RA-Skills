## Description: <br>
Guides forward deployed engineers through industrial AI scenario diagnosis, data governance, solution design, POC planning, scale-up, and ROI framing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Forward deployed engineers, industrial AI teams, and manufacturing stakeholders use this skill to identify practical factory AI opportunities, plan POCs, evaluate data readiness, and frame measurable deployment outcomes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Factory AI planning can expose sensitive production details, credentials, VPN access, or proprietary operational data. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only within an authorized project scope and avoid sharing production credentials, proprietary data, or operational system details unless approved. <br>
Risk: Industrial AI recommendations may be unsuitable for a specific production line without local validation. <br>
Mitigation: Validate guidance with plant operators, engineering stakeholders, current-state KPI baselines, and controlled POC or A/B testing before relying on it in operations. <br>
