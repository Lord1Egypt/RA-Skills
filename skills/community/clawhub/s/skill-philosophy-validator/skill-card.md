## Description: <br>
Validates agent skill design against five core principles and provides prioritized feedback after a SKILL.md file is created, changed, or explicitly requested for review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kangyishuai](https://clawhub.ai/user/kangyishuai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Skill authors and agent engineers use this skill to review SKILL.md files against progressive disclosure, knowledge delta, prompt-extension architecture, gotchas quality, and trigger-description quality before or after edits. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad triggers may cause the skill to offer design feedback during skill edits where the advice is not relevant. <br>
Mitigation: Review its guidance before applying changes, especially when editing the validator itself or unrelated skill metadata. <br>
