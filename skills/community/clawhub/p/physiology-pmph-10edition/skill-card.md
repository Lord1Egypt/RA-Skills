## Description: <br>
Systematic physiology skill collection based on PMPH Physiology, 10th Edition, covering 165 structured skills across nervous, endocrine, cardiovascular, respiratory, renal, digestive, blood, metabolic, cellular, reproductive, integrative, and methodology topics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Medical students, clinicians, physiology educators, and researchers use this skill collection for structured physiology explanations, mechanism analysis, calculations, and study-oriented clinical assessment drafts. Outputs should be treated as educational references or clinician-review materials rather than diagnosis, treatment, emergency triage, fertility timing, medication dosing, or unsupervised clinical testing advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Clinical or procedural physiology guidance may be mistaken for independent medical diagnosis, treatment, emergency triage, fertility timing, medication dosing, or unsupervised clinical testing. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only as study material or clinician-review drafts, and verify any medical recommendation with qualified clinicians and current guidelines. <br>
Risk: Some included skills provide clinical assessment examples without enough local safety limits. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated clinical interpretations before use and keep the skill scoped to education and supervised review. <br>
Risk: Rendered README pages may load third-party images. <br>
Mitigation: Review external media loading behavior before deploying in restricted or privacy-sensitive environments. <br>
