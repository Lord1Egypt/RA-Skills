## Description: <br>
Generate fully SEO-optimized blog posts and articles with keyword research, competitor analysis, and SERP-aware content. Use when creating SEO content, blog posts, articles, or content for clients. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[seanwyngaard](https://clawhub.ai/user/seanwyngaard) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content marketers, SEO practitioners, and client-service teams use this skill to turn a keyword or batch of keywords into SERP-aware article drafts, on-page SEO metadata, linking suggestions, and publishing-ready content files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Web research and generated article drafts may include outdated, incorrect, or unsupported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify statistics, sources, and any claimed firsthand experience before publishing. <br>
Risk: Batch mode writes generated article files and could overwrite existing work if paths are reused. <br>
Mitigation: Review output paths before running batch mode and keep generated files in a dedicated workspace. <br>
