# Pre-Order Communication Calendar — Templates

Full email, SMS, and social templates for a 4-week pre-order campaign.

---

## Announcement Week (Days 1–7)

### Day 1 — Waitlist Launch

**Email subject**: We're making something new. Here's your first look.

> Hey [first name],
>
> We've been working on something for [X months/years], and we're finally ready to show you.
>
> [Product Name] is [one-sentence description].
>
> We're not selling it yet. But you can join the waitlist today and be first in line when we do — with exclusive early access and a [founding customer reward] that won't be available after launch.
>
> [JOIN THE WAITLIST →]
>
> Expected ship date: [specific date or tight range].

**SMS**:
> [Brand]: We're launching [Product Name] on [date]. Join the waitlist for early access + exclusive rewards: [link]

**Social caption**:
> We've been quiet for a reason. Introducing [Product Name]. Waitlist is open — link in bio. Ships [date].

---

### Day 3 — Brand Story

**Email subject**: Why we made this (and why it took this long)

> [First name],
>
> When we started [Brand Name], the problem we kept running into was [specific pain point].
>
> [Product Name] is the result of [X] months of [testing / formulating / designing]. Here's what makes it different:
>
> - [Differentiator 1]
> - [Differentiator 2]
> - [Differentiator 3]
>
> [JOIN THE WAITLIST if you haven't yet →]

---

### Day 7 — Waitlist Counter Update

**Email subject**: [N] people are already on the waitlist

> [First name],
>
> Quick update: [N] people have joined the waitlist for [Product Name] in the first week.
>
> We set aside [X] Founding Customer spots — [N] are already claimed. [remaining] left.
>
> [PRE-ORDER AS A FOUNDING CUSTOMER →]

---

## Mid-Campaign (Days 8–21)

### Day 14 — Midpoint Urgency

**Email subject**: 2 weeks left + what we've been doing in the factory

> [First name],
>
> We're halfway through the pre-order window. Here's where things stand:
>
> - [N] Founding Customer orders placed
> - [X] Founding Customer spots remaining
> - Expected ship date: [date] — still on track
>
> [PRE-ORDER NOW — [X]% OFF →]

### Day 21 — Final Week Warning

**Email subject**: Last 7 days to pre-order at [X]% off

> [First name],
>
> The pre-order discount disappears in 7 days.
>
> Pre-order now for $[discounted price] and guaranteed stock the day we ship.
>
> [PRE-ORDER AT [X]% OFF →]
>
> Ship date: [specific date]. Full refund guaranteed if we can't deliver by [date + buffer].

## Launch Week

### 24 Hours Before — Waitlist Exclusive Access

**Email subject**: Your early access link is inside (waitlist exclusive)

> [First name],
>
> You're on the waitlist. That means you get access **24 hours before everyone else**.
>
> [Product Name] is live at your exclusive pre-order price: $[price].
>
> [YOUR EXCLUSIVE EARLY ACCESS LINK →]
>
> This link expires at [time/date]. General public access opens [date+1].

### Launch Day — General Announcement

**Email subject**: [Product Name] is now available

## Post-Order Sequence

### Week 2 Post-Order — Production Update

**Subject**: Quick update on your [Product Name] order

> [First name],
>
> We're [X]% through production. Everything is on track for our [ship date] target.
>
> You'll receive a shipping confirmation email as soon as your order leaves our facility.

### Ship Day

**Subject**: Your order is on its way!

### 7 Days Post-Delivery

**Subject**: How's your [Product Name]?

> [First name],
>
> It's been about a week since your [Product Name] arrived. We'd love to know what you think.
>
> If you have 60 seconds, a quick review means the world to a small brand like ours.
>
> [LEAVE A REVIEW →]
>
> And if something isn't right — just reply to this email. We'll make it right.
