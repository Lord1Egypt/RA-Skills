## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and operations teams use this skill to plan command-driven batch spreadsheet workflows such as merging files, splitting large workbooks, converting Excel and CSV files, cleaning data, deduplicating rows, and filling formulas across files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad batch operations or wildcard patterns can unintentionally change many spreadsheets. <br>
Mitigation: Work on copies or backups, write outputs to a separate directory, avoid broad patterns in important folders, and verify results before replacing original files. <br>
Risk: Skipped errors during batch processing can hide incomplete or partial spreadsheet updates. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outputs and skipped-error reports before relying on the processed files. <br>
