## Description: <br>
Generate day-wise travel itineraries with timings, food spots, activities, and transport. Handles "plan my trip", "make an itinerary", revisions like "swap days" or "make it cheaper", and adjusts plans when user has existing flights or trains. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and planning assistants use this skill to create realistic day-wise itineraries from a destination, schedule, budget, group details, and existing flight or train timings. It also supports concise itinerary revisions such as changing days, lowering cost, adding activities, or extending the trip. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Itinerary details such as prices, opening hours, transport times, and local safety conditions can be inaccurate or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current prices, schedules, availability, and safety conditions before relying on the generated itinerary. <br>
Risk: Trip planning may use destinations, dates, budget, and preferences in web searches. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive booking, identity, or payment details unless they are needed for the planning task. <br>
