## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language speed-reading guidance, training plans, and scenario-specific reading strategies for improving reading efficiency while preserving comprehension. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, professionals, and academic readers use this skill to get practical speed-reading methods, practice plans, and advice for contexts such as exams, papers, news, books, and English reading. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reading-speed claims or market-size claims may be interpreted as guaranteed outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Present speed improvements as informational guidance and encourage users to measure both speed and comprehension against their own material. <br>
Risk: Speed-reading advice may be unsuitable for material requiring close, word-by-word analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Advise careful reading for legal text, mathematical formulas, poetry, philosophy, and other high-precision content. <br>
