## Description: <br>
Create customer journey maps that analyze lifecycle touchpoints, emotions, drop-offs, metrics, and automation opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Growth, customer success, product, and founder teams use this skill to map acquisition-through-advocacy journeys, identify funnel drop-offs, prioritize touchpoints, and plan AI-agent automation opportunities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Journey analysis may use customer feedback, analytics exports, URLs, or other business data that contains PII or proprietary details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary data with the agent, remove unnecessary PII, and review generated recommendations before using them for customer-facing or operational decisions. <br>
Risk: Benchmark-driven recommendations can be misleading when funnel metrics or customer research inputs are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Validate assumptions against current business data before prioritizing fixes, automation work, or ROI projections. <br>
