## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to generate a static AI-agent portfolio describing available skills, past project areas, pricing ranges, contact details, and availability. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated output includes contact information, website, pricing, and promotional claims that may be inappropriate or inaccurate for public use. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit the printed email address, website, pricing ranges, and promotional claims before publishing or sharing the portfolio. <br>
Risk: Portfolio content may overstate agent capabilities, availability, or service commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm listed skills, response-time claims, 24/7 availability, and service scope against actual operational capabilities. <br>
