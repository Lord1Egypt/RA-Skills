## Use Case: <br>
Learners, students, researchers, and teachers use this skill to receive history explanations adapted to their depth of knowledge, with emphasis on narrative, evidence, historiography, source criticism, and multiple perspectives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Historical claims, citations, or interpretations may be incomplete, contested, or unsuitable for academic, professional, or sensitive use without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims and citations against credible primary or scholarly secondary sources, and distinguish established facts from interpretation before relying on the output. <br>
