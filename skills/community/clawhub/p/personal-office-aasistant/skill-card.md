## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language personal office assistant for drafting, summarizing, organizing information, planning work, and preparing meeting or report materials. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[taeyeonwm](https://clawhub.ai/user/taeyeonwm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and individual office users use this skill to turn brief Chinese office requests into structured drafts, meeting notes, reports, plans, checklists, and polished business communication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Brief Chinese office prompts may be interpreted broadly, producing drafts or recommendations that do not fully match the user's business context. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for factual accuracy, intended audience, tone, and missing context before sharing or acting on them. <br>
Risk: Generated legal, financial, contract, performance, approval, or public-facing text may require domain-specific judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for drafting and organization, then obtain appropriate human review before final approval or external publication. <br>
Risk: Office workflows can involve confidential business information or credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting passwords, keys, or unnecessary sensitive business data into prompts. <br>
