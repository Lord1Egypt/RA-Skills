## Description: <br>
Guides founders through startup idea validation frameworks, customer discovery interviews, demand testing, and go/no-go decision criteria. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[C0ldSmi1e](https://clawhub.ai/user/C0ldSmi1e) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, and startup advisors use this skill to evaluate startup ideas before investing significant time or money. It helps structure discovery questions, validation experiments, interview scripts, demand tests, and decision thresholds. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business outreach, public demand tests, and pre-sell suggestions may affect prospects or public perception if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt messages before contacting prospects, publishing public tests, or accepting pre-sales. <br>
Risk: Validation guidance may be treated as a substitute for business, legal, or market due diligence. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a planning aid and confirm important business decisions with domain experts and direct customer evidence. <br>
