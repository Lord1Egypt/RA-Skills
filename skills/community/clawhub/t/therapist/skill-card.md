## Use Case: <br>
People and agents use this skill to structure supportive mental-health conversations with CBT, ACT, mindfulness, grounding, reframing, and behavior-change techniques. It is for supportive guidance and should not replace licensed therapeutic, medical, or crisis care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat supportive mental-health guidance as a substitute for licensed therapeutic, medical, or crisis care. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as supportive conversation guidance, and seek immediate human professional help for suicidal ideation, crisis situations, complex trauma, medication needs, or other clinical concerns. <br>
Risk: Mental-health conversations can involve sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing unnecessary sensitive personal information and review outputs before relying on them. <br>
