## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language fiction-writing coaching skill for Fanqie-style short stories, covering platform strategy, title and opening hooks, premise generation, AI-assisted drafting, polishing, monetization, and overseas adaptation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xinmeili0612](https://clawhub.ai/user/xinmeili0612) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and writing coaches use this skill to learn, generate, diagnose, and polish Fanqie-style short fiction assets such as titles, openings, outlines, and revision guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes publishing, monetization, platform-policy, tax, and payment guidance that may be incomplete or change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify copyright, tax, payment, and platform-policy advice before acting on it. <br>
Risk: Drafts, manuscripts, or unpublished story ideas may be exposed when pasted into external AI or publishing platforms. <br>
Mitigation: Review and minimize sensitive content before sharing it with external AI tools or publishing services. <br>
