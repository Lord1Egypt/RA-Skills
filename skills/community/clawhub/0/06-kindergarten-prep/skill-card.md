## Description: <br>
Miki Kindergarten Prep helps assess kindergarten readiness for children ages 2-3 across social ability, self-care, and emotional-social readiness, then suggests preparation steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nic-yuan](https://clawhub.ai/user/nic-yuan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Parents and caregivers use this skill to structure kindergarten-readiness conversations for 2-3 year old children, evaluate common readiness dimensions, and receive practical preparation plans. The output is guidance, not a medical or developmental diagnosis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Caregivers may share unnecessary identifying details about a young child while seeking readiness guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for only the child age, readiness observations, planned timing, and concerns needed for the assessment; avoid names, addresses, school names, and other identifying details. <br>
Risk: Users may treat readiness guidance as a medical or developmental diagnosis. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as practical parenting guidance and recommend qualified professional support when concerns persist or the child's needs appear outside ordinary kindergarten preparation. <br>
