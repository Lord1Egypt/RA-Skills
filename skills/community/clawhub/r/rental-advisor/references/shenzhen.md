## Shenzhen-Specific Notes

- **Newest city**: Most buildings are post-2000, less "old" stock
- **Very metro-dependent**: Traffic is severe, metro is essential
- **Qianhai**: Special economic zone, new developments, growing
- **Rental market**: Large transient population, competitive
- **Hong Kong proximity**: Some rent near border for cross-border commuters
