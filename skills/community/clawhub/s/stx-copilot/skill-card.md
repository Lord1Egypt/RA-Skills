## Description: <br>
STX Copilot provides IBM Transformation Extender (WTX/ITX/Sterling TX) development guidance for type trees, map rules, execution, debugging, deployment, and financial-message transformations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Operational examples may affect IBM ITX, ACE, or related integration environments if followed without confirming the target system. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the intended environment and obtain approval before applying administrative changes or running operational commands. <br>
Risk: Sample credential patterns in guidance could be copied into real systems. <br>
Mitigation: Do not reuse sample password patterns; follow the organization's credential and secret-management requirements. <br>
Risk: Verbose tracing or WTX_DUMP_DATA can expose sensitive input data in logs. <br>
Mitigation: Enable tracing only temporarily in controlled environments, restrict log access, and clean up generated logs after diagnosis. <br>
