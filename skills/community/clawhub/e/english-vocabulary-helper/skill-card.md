## Description: <br>
A bilingual English vocabulary tutor that helps native Chinese speakers understand, analyze, memorize, and review English words. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gratefulheart](https://clawhub.ai/user/gratefulheart) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners and educators use this skill to generate bilingual vocabulary study materials for individual English words, including definitions, examples, word roots, affixes, memory techniques, review cards, and quizzes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated HTML study cards may include content derived from unusual or untrusted prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML before opening it when prompt content is untrusted or unusual. <br>
Risk: The skill saves local study files and auto-opens generated HTML cards. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when local file creation under ~/.qclaw/workspace/vocabulary/words/ and browser opening are acceptable. <br>
