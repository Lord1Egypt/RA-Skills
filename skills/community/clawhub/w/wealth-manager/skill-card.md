## Description: <br>
Wealth Manager provides a four-stage wealth-management framework for accumulation, growth, preservation, and succession, with supporting trading, risk-control, life-strategy references, and calculator tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to structure wealth-management conversations across accumulation, investment growth, asset preservation, and succession planning. It is best treated as educational framing and calculation support, not as financial, legal, tax, immigration, trading, or investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes high-risk trading, offshore, tax, cross-border asset-transfer, and asset-protection guidance without sufficient legal or risk guardrails. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the material as educational only and require qualified financial, legal, tax, immigration, and investment professionals before acting on any plan. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake calculator outputs or wealth-growth scenarios for reliable forecasts. <br>
Mitigation: Use calculator results only as scenario illustrations and review assumptions, downside exposure, jurisdictional constraints, and liquidity needs before relying on them. <br>
