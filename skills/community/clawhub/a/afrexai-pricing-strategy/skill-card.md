## Description: <br>
Analyze and optimize product or service pricing using value-based, cost-plus, competitive, and tiered models with actionable recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, agency owners, product managers, and pricing teams use this skill to analyze product or service pricing, compare competitors, model pricing tiers, estimate willingness to pay, and generate actionable pricing recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the skill may involve sharing confidential pricing, cost, revenue, customer segment, and competitor information with an agent. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved agent environments, redact sensitive details when possible, and follow internal confidentiality controls before sharing business data. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes promotional external AfrexAI resource links. <br>
Mitigation: Review external resources separately before following links, relying on their content, or purchasing related offerings. <br>
Risk: Pricing recommendations and revenue impact estimates may be inaccurate if source inputs are incomplete or market conditions change. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current market data, finance review, and controlled pricing tests before acting on them. <br>
