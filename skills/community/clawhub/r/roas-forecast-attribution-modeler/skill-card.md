## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Spend recommendations can be misleading when baseline metrics, attribution windows, or assumptions are incomplete or uncertain. <br>
Mitigation: Use real baseline metrics, separate observed facts from assumptions, widen forecast intervals when uncertainty is high, and include rollback or stop-loss conditions before changing spend. <br>
Risk: The broad trigger wording may cause the skill to appear in general marketing conversations where ROAS forecasting is not actually needed. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking for ROAS, CPA, revenue, attribution sensitivity, or budget-scenario modeling before applying the skill. <br>
