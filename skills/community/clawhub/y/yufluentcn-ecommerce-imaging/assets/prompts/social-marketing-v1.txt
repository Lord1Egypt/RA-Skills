# 社媒 / 营销场景 Prompt 模板 v1
# 适用于 TikTok / Instagram / 广告素材；不含文字叠加（no text）

## unboxing（开箱）
A {product} in unboxing moment, partially opened premium packaging, hands gently revealing product, clean tabletop, soft natural window light, authentic UGC-meets-brand quality, TikTok/Reels aesthetic

## gift_set（礼盒套装）
A {product} arranged as gift set presentation, ribbon and tissue paper props, warm celebratory lighting, ready-to-gift composition, holiday or birthday marketing photography

## influencer_flatlay（达人平铺）
A {product} in curated influencer flat lay, complementary lifestyle props (coffee cup, notebook, plant), Instagram aesthetic, overhead angle, cohesive color palette, aspirational but authentic

## flash_sale（限时抢购视觉）
A {product} in high-energy flash sale visual, dynamic diagonal composition, bold color splash backdrop, limited-time offer mood without text overlay, social ad creative photography, eye-catching and urgent
