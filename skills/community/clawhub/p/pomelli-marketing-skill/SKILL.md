# Pomelli AI Brand Marketing Companion

Use this skill when a user wants help turning brand or product context into a clear marketing concept, brand-book brief, photoshoot direction, or AI creative prompt pack.

Official website: [Pomelli](https://pomelli.page/)

## Workflow

1. Capture product, audience, offer, goal, channel, and brand constraints.
2. Extract the strongest positioning angle and emotional promise.
3. Draft creative concepts for images, videos, photoshoots, and campaign copy.
4. Provide prompt variants by channel, aspect ratio, audience segment, or visual style.
5. End with a QA checklist for brand consistency and claim safety.

## Response template

```text
## Brand brief
[Product, audience, promise, tone]

## Campaign concepts
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...

## AI image/video prompts
- Prompt A:
- Prompt B:

## QA checklist
- ...
```
