## Description: <br>
Review affiliate video campaign plans, disclosures, claims, creative notes, and platform captions before a human decides what to create or publish. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zack-dev-cm](https://clawhub.ai/user/zack-dev-cm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Affiliate marketers, creators, and campaign reviewers use this skill to check draft video or carousel affiliate campaigns for disclosure placement, claim support, creative rights notes, platform fit, and final human-review readiness before publication or spend. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Affiliate campaign reviews may influence publishing or spending decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep final publishing, scheduling, promotion, and spending decisions under human control. <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive account, payment, analytics, customer, or session data while preparing campaign reviews. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide account credentials, payment information, session cookies, private customer data, or confidential analytics exports. <br>
Risk: Unsupported claims about revenue, rankings, health, legal, financial, safety, or other outcomes can create compliance and consumer-protection exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Require evidence for factual claims and remove unsupported promises before a human decides whether to publish. <br>
