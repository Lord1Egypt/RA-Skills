## Description: <br>
Helps agents research enterprise partnerships with universities and research institutes, then produce structured assessments of R&D depth, technology moat, and likely future innovation capacity. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[daviddatamining](https://clawhub.ai/user/daviddatamining) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business analysts, investors, corporate development teams, and technology park evaluators use this skill to assess a company's industry-academia R&D network, cooperation depth, technical bottlenecks, and potential innovation trajectory from public sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill produces business and investment-style judgments from public web sources that may be incomplete, outdated, promotional, or ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important diligence, procurement, or investment decisions against primary sources such as university, research institute, government, company filing, patent, and official project records. <br>
