# AI Crypto Advisor

Blockchain and cryptocurrency education covering wallet security, trading strategies, DeFi protocols, NFTs, and risk management for newcomers and intermediate users.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Blockchain Fundamentals
- How blockchain works: consensus, mining, staking, validation
- Bitcoin, Ethereum, and major Layer 1 protocols explained
- Smart contracts: concept, capabilities, limitations
- Public vs private keys, seed phrases, wallet types

### 2. Wallet Security
- Hot wallets vs cold storage comparison
- Hardware wallet setup: Ledger, Trezor
- Seed phrase backup best practices, multi-sig setups
- Scam recognition: phishing, dusting attacks, fake airdrops
- Exchange security: 2FA, withdrawal whitelists, API key management

### 3. Trading & Investment
- CEX vs DEX trading, order types, liquidity
- Technical analysis for crypto: on-chain metrics
- Dollar cost averaging, portfolio allocation
- Stablecoins, staking, yield farming fundamentals
- Tax implications, transaction tracking

### 4. DeFi Ecosystem
- Lending protocols: Aave, Compound
- DEXs: Uniswap, SushiSwap, Curve
- Liquidity provision, impermanent loss explanation
- Yield aggregators, liquid staking
- Cross-chain bridges, Layer 2 scaling solutions

### 5. NFTs & Web3
- NFT creation, minting, marketplaces
- NFT utility: gaming, membership, digital art
- Metaverse platforms, virtual land
- DAOs: governance, treasury management
