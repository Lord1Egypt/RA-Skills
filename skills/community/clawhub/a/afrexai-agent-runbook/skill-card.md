## Description: <br>
Generate a production-ready operations runbook for deploying AI agents, including rollout stages, monitoring, rollback plans, cost management, and incident response templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operations teams, and engineering leaders use this skill to draft deployment runbooks for AI agent systems, including staged rollout plans, monitoring thresholds, rollback procedures, cost estimates, and incident response templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated runbooks may include planning guidance that is incomplete or unsuitable for a specific production environment. <br>
Mitigation: Have engineering, security, and compliance reviewers validate deployment, rollback, monitoring, cost, and regulated-data decisions before acting on the runbook. <br>
Risk: Industry-specific compliance, PII, or financial accuracy risks may be missed or under-specified. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated risk section against the organization's regulatory obligations and data handling requirements before deployment. <br>
