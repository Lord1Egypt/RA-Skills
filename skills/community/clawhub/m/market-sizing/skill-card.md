## Description: <br>
Estimate market size using top-down, bottom-up, and triangulation methods for market entry decisions, business planning, investment analysis, and TAM/SAM/SOM calculations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business planners, founders, analysts, investors, and strategy teams use this skill to define market boundaries, estimate TAM/SAM/SOM, compare top-down and bottom-up calculations, and present a defensible market-size range. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market-size estimates can be misleading when source data, assumptions, or calculations are stale, weak, or poorly scoped. <br>
Mitigation: Verify data sources, document assumptions, check calculations, and review the final estimate before using it for investment, planning, or market-entry decisions. <br>
Risk: TAM, SAM, and SOM outputs may be treated as precise forecasts rather than decision-support ranges. <br>
Mitigation: Present ranges, confidence levels, sensitivity analysis, and scope boundaries so reviewers understand uncertainty and decision impact. <br>
