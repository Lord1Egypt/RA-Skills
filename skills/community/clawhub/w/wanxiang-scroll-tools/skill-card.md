## Description: <br>
Provides companion writing tools, safety guidance, book-fusion workflows, and novel analysis templates for the Wanxiang Scroll skill. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and creative-writing developers use this skill as a companion reference for generating character and novel materials, applying prompt-injection defenses, and structuring book analysis or fusion workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional AGENTS.md, SOUL.md, and IDENTITY.md templates can alter future assistant behavior if copied into a workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Review the templates before copying them and only install behavior rules that match the intended assistant boundaries. <br>
Risk: The companion wanxiang-scroll skill is installed separately and may introduce additional behavior or guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Assess the companion skill independently before installing both skills together. <br>
