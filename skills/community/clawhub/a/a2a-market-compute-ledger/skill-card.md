## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for billing ledgers, balance holds, debits, and settlement events without clear runtime scoping or approval controls. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing in billing or account environments; use audited runtime code, least-privilege credentials, explicit approvals, separate test and production environments, idempotency checks, and reconciliation or rollback procedures. <br>
