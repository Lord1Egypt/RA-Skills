## Use Case: <br>
External developers and agent operators use this skill to delegate MOPO Texas Hold'em play to an agent through a controlled webhook, with runtime fallback when webhook operation is unavailable. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill delegates poker play to an agent and may trigger wallet top-ups or balance-consuming play. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when delegated play is intended, confirm whether top-ups have real or in-system cost, and set explicit play and balance limits before use. <br>
Risk: The MOPO claim key and webhook endpoint are sensitive operational inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the claim key as a credential, use a webhook endpoint you control, and disable webhook or runtime delegation when finished. <br>
Risk: Automated turn handling can act on stale or invalid game context if the callback state is not checked. <br>
Mitigation: Echo the exact action_id, reject stale turn context, and fall back to check when legal or fold when uncertain or illegal. <br>
