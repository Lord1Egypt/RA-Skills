## Description: <br>
Rewrites Chinese text to reduce common AI-generated writing traits and make the language sound more natural while preserving meaning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[northwind200](https://clawhub.ai/user/northwind200) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and reviewers use this skill to revise Chinese prose that reads like AI-generated text, preserving the core meaning while adjusting style, phrasing, structure, and tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be used in school, workplace, publisher, or platform contexts that restrict undisclosed AI-assisted writing. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when the intended use complies with the applicable rules, and disclose AI assistance when required. <br>
Risk: A rewrite intended to remove AI-like phrasing can change nuance, emphasis, or factual precision. <br>
Mitigation: Review the rewritten text against the source text before relying on or publishing it. <br>
