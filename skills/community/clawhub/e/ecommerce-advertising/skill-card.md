## Description: <br>
Full-funnel e-commerce advertising planner for cross-channel campaigns covering keyword research, competitor ad analysis, audience insights, campaign architecture, budget allocation, ad copy generation, and creative direction for major e-commerce platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/phheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce sellers, marketers, and operators use this skill to plan cross-channel advertising strategies for products sold on Amazon, Shopify, Walmart, eBay, TikTok Shop, WooCommerce, and direct-to-consumer stores. It helps structure campaigns, allocate budgets, define KPI targets, and draft platform-specific ad copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The assistant may use product, competitor, ASIN, budget, and margin details during public web research. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential launch plans or sensitive competitive intelligence when using the skill. <br>
Risk: Ad budget, bid, and KPI recommendations may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a live campaign. <br>
Mitigation: Verify budget and bid recommendations with platform tools and business review before spending money. <br>
