## Description: <br>
Makes the strategic and moral case for AI augmentation over pure automation, providing leaders with frameworks for job redesign, creativity-driven AI deployment, and long-term workforce investment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Leaders, founders, and strategy teams use this skill to evaluate AI automation choices, redesign roles around human-AI collaboration, and prepare augmentation-focused workforce and board guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business examples, statistics, or claims in generated strategy guidance may be inaccurate or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited examples, statistics, and assumptions before using the output for board materials, workforce plans, or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Prompts about internal AI deployments or workforce redesign may expose sensitive company details to an unapproved model or runtime. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential organizational details unless the selected model and runtime are approved for that data. <br>
