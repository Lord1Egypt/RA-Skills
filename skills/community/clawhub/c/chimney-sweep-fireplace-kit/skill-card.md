## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-oriented marketing copy for certified chimney sweep and fireplace service contractors, including seasonal campaigns, service pages, reputation sequences, digital ads, and JSON-LD schema. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Home services marketers, agencies, and chimney or fireplace contractors use this skill to generate local marketing assets that reflect credential, inspection-level, review-request, and advertising compliance constraints. It is especially oriented toward U.S. chimney sweep, fireplace service, real estate referral, and local SEO workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States, with Southern Nevada-specific burn-advisory disclosures when applicable. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated public marketing assets may contain inaccurate review-count, star-rating, certification, licensing, or local burn-advisory claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all factual business, credential, licensing, review, and jurisdiction-specific claims against the contractor's records before publishing ads, service pages, or referral letters. <br>
Risk: Compliance-oriented copy may be mistaken for authoritative legal or regulatory advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer validate NFPA, FTC, EPA, OSHA, state contractor-license, and local air-quality statements before using the outputs in public campaigns. <br>
