## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and agents use this skill to coordinate a complete WeChat public-account article workflow: strategy, HTML drafting, independent AI-writing risk review, image research or confirmation, style review, revision gates, and final preview or copy-ready HTML delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automatic mode can proceed through drafting, review, image research, style checks, and final delivery without step-by-step user confirmation. <br>
Mitigation: Use automatic mode deliberately and pause when credentials, missing files, compliance limits, fact checks, or high-risk content boundaries require explicit review. <br>
Risk: Generated article content may contain incorrect facts, weak wording, or unverified claims before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated facts and wording before publishing, especially for time-sensitive, legal, financial, medical, policy, or disputed topics. <br>
Risk: Images may have unclear rights, attribution, local-path handling, or contextual suitability for final HTML. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm image rights, attribution, and local-image handling before including images in copy-ready WeChat HTML. <br>
