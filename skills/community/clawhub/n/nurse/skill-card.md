## Description: <br>
Clinical support system for nurses and frontline healthcare workers that helps draft documentation, patient communication, care plans, handover notes, medication-related queries, and incident reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Duclawbot](https://clawhub.ai/user/Duclawbot) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Nurses and frontline healthcare workers use this skill to draft and structure clinical documentation, patient and family communication, care plans, handovers, incident reports, and professional support materials. It supports clinical judgment but does not replace clinician review, local policy, or escalation workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Clinical documentation or medication-related output could be incorrect, incomplete, or unsuitable for the local care context. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated note, medication-related response, handover, and patient explanation against clinical judgment, local policy, and the patient record before use. <br>
Risk: Patient information may be sensitive or regulated. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize patient identifiers and confirm the organization permits entering patient information into the agent before use. <br>
Risk: The skill could be mistaken for clinical decision support in diagnosis, prescribing, emergency decisions, or scope-of-practice questions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as a nursing documentation drafting helper and escalate to appropriate clinical, pharmacy, or policy resources when decisions require professional authority. <br>
