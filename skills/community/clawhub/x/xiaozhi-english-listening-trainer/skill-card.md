## Description: <br>
Generates personalized English listening practice materials matched to a learner's vocabulary profile, interests, level, and listening breakdown points. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners and tutors use this skill to generate level-matched English listening passages, guide a four-step listening practice flow, diagnose comprehension breakdowns, and add new listening vocabulary to a learner profile. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for a broad range of English-listening requests and steer interactions toward Chinese or English. <br>
Mitigation: Review trigger language before deployment and narrow, disable, or localize the skill when users need a different language or tighter routing. <br>
