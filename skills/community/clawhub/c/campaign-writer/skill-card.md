## Description: <br>
Write compelling crowdfunding and fundraising campaign copy including titles, descriptions, story narratives, and social media posts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to draft and improve fundraising campaign titles, narratives, donation appeals, and social media posts for crowdfunding or charity drives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated fundraising copy can mislead donors if names, amounts, deadlines, medical or hardship claims, beneficiary consent, or permission to share personal details are inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify these details before publishing campaign copy and confirm the beneficiary has consented to the shared story and personal information. <br>
