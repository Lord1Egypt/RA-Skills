## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese insurance marketing copy, AI image prompts, and draft compliance reviews through natural conversation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gechengling](https://clawhub.ai/user/gechengling) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Insurance agents, brokers, and marketing operations teams use this skill to draft Chinese promotional copy, match insurance products to topical campaigns, generate image prompts, and perform first-pass compliance checks before human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Compliance checks and regulatory updates may be incomplete or unsuitable as final approval for regulated insurance marketing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for drafting support only and have qualified compliance staff review final public materials before publication. <br>
Risk: Generated insurance marketing copy may require product-specific disclaimers, risk notices, and substantiation before use. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm final copy against current product terms, required disclosures, and applicable advertising or insurance rules. <br>
