## Description: <br>
Generates personalized learning paths from a learner's goal, current level, constraints, and available time, including phased plans, resource suggestions, milestones, and progress checkpoints. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners, coaches, and education-focused agents use this skill to turn learning goals into structured study plans with stage objectives, time budgets, resource categories, assessments, milestones, and next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security guidance notes that resource recommendations are generic and simulated rather than live education-platform results. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommended resources as starting points, verify availability and quality before relying on them, and tailor choices to the learner's budget, language, and preferred format. <br>
Risk: The security guidance notes that the skill may activate on broad learning-plan or learning-resource requests. <br>
Mitigation: Use clear task scoping when invoking the skill and review the generated plan before presenting it as the final learning recommendation. <br>
Risk: Artifact documentation states that progress tracking depends on continued learner updates. <br>
Mitigation: Ask learners to update progress regularly and revise schedules, milestones, and difficulty as their actual completion data changes. <br>
