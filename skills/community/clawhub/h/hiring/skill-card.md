## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and agents use this skill to plan hiring or delegation of work to freelancers, contractors, physical taskers, or specialized AI agents. It helps compare platforms, define scopes, vet candidates, structure contracts and payments, and manage records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Autonomous hiring, dispatch, or payment decisions can spend money, share sensitive details, or initiate real-world work without fresh human approval. <br>
Mitigation: Set explicit budget limits, approved platforms, allowed locations, data-sharing boundaries, and confirmation requirements before allowing an agent to hire, dispatch, or pay anyone. <br>
Risk: Contractor records, task proof, credentials, tax forms, and payment records can contain sensitive personal or business information. <br>
Mitigation: Define storage location, access controls, retention periods, and deletion procedures before collecting or sharing hiring records. <br>
Risk: Hiring humans may create worker-classification, tax, IP ownership, confidentiality, or jurisdiction-specific obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Require human legal or operations review for ambiguous classification, high-value work, unfamiliar jurisdictions, unusual terms, or regulated tasks. <br>
