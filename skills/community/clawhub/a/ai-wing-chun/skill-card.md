## Description: <br>
AI Wing Chun coaching skill for guidance on Siu Nim Tao, Chum Kiu, Biu Jee, Chi Sao, wooden dummy training, Wing Chun theory, lineage context, practical application, and user-provided practice questions or videos. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External Wing Chun learners and martial arts enthusiasts use this skill to ask for training guidance, technique explanations, lineage context, and feedback on practice descriptions or uploaded practice videos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Practice videos may expose bystanders, private locations, identifiable details, or sensitive audio. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid uploading sensitive videos; use text descriptions when privacy matters. <br>
Risk: Martial arts coaching can be misunderstood as a substitute for in-person instruction or safe supervised practice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as general coaching guidance and verify techniques with a qualified instructor before intensive partner practice. <br>
