## Description: <br>
Drafts structured, severity-ranked smart contract audit finding reports for Solidity/EVM protocols from raw vulnerability notes, including exploit narratives, impact analysis, proof-of-concept material, remediation guidance, and severity rationale. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Smart contract auditors, bug bounty participants, and security engineers use this skill to turn verified vulnerability notes into submission-ready finding reports for DeFi audits and contest platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated exploit narratives or proof-of-concept material could be misused against systems the user is not authorized to test. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for authorized audits, contests, or reports, and review generated exploit material before sharing or running it. <br>
Risk: Severity, impact, remediation, or proof-of-concept details may be wrong when the supplied vulnerability notes are incomplete or unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Manually verify the exploit path, affected components, impact claims, and code examples before submission. <br>
