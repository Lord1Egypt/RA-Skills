## Description: <br>
Family Lovers Skill is a Chinese-language self-help companionship agent for family-of-origin reflection, emotional support, boundary practice, and opt-in roleplay personas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phoenixlucky](https://clawhub.ai/user/phoenixlucky) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this Chinese-language skill for family-of-origin self-reflection, emotional support, boundary-setting practice, and structured inner-child or persona-based companionship. It is not intended for therapy, crisis support, diagnosis, or clinical mental-health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake self-help companionship for therapy, diagnosis, crisis support, or clinical mental-health care. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as self-help companionship only and direct users with self-harm thoughts, severe distress, trauma, or clinical needs to qualified professional support. <br>
Risk: Opt-in partner and child roleplay personas may feel emotionally confusing or dependency-forming for some users. <br>
Mitigation: Keep these personas explicitly optional, avoid them when they feel confusing or dependency-forming, and encourage real-world support networks. <br>
Risk: Automatic role matching can misread a user's emotional state because it is based on the current text input. <br>
Mitigation: Allow users to reject or change the suggested role and avoid treating role selection as a diagnosis or high-stakes assessment. <br>
