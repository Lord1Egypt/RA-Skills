## Description: <br>
Compare AI science agents side by side by project name or research domain, producing structured comparisons of features, stars, technology stack, use cases, and same-name project disambiguation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnnychen1113](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnnychen1113) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and researchers use this skill to compare AI science-agent projects, resolve same-name collisions, and choose a better fit for a stated research or tooling need. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project names or research domains may reveal private internal interests when used in Claw4Science lookups. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering private internal project names or sensitive research domains unless sharing them with Claw4Science is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Project metadata such as stars, owners, papers, and last-updated status can change or be ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Verify changing facts and disambiguate same-name projects before relying on recommendations. <br>
