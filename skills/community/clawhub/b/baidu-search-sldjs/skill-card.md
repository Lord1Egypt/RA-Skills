## Description: <br>
Open browser and search for keywords. Use when user wants to search online. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sldjs](https://clawhub.ai/user/sldjs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to open Baidu search results for a query in the local default browser. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search queries are sent to Baidu and may contain sensitive or confidential text. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter passwords, private documents, personal data, or confidential business text as search queries. <br>
