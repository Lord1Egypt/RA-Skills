## Description: <br>
Provides summaries of public Tongcheng Travel hotel and flight pages, including prices, ratings, schedules, and lightweight trip budget estimates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mike47512](https://clawhub.ai/user/mike47512) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travel planners, analysts, and agents use this skill to summarize public Tongcheng hotel and flight listings for price comparison, rating review, schedule comparison, and itinerary budget estimation. It is intended for low-frequency public-page analysis and reminders, not ordering, account-specific workflows, API reverse engineering, or bulk collection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tongcheng prices, availability, and schedules can change after a page is summarized. <br>
Mitigation: Include the collection time in summaries and verify prices and availability directly on Tongcheng before making travel decisions. <br>
Risk: Using the skill on checkout, account-specific, private itinerary, or high-volume collection workflows could exceed the intended public-page summary boundary. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to low-frequency summaries of public hotel and flight pages and avoid checkout, private account pages, and bulk collection. <br>
