## Description: <br>
Web Novel Creation Engine helps agents plan and draft web novels through genre and market analysis, character development, outlines, opening chapters, plot points, pacing, and platform-specific recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External authors, writing teams, and creative-writing agents use this skill to structure web novels from concept through opening chapters. It supports genre selection, character design, chapter outlines, pacing, and platform-specific recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger keywords may activate the skill during general writing or character discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill when web novel planning is intended and review generated guidance before applying it to a manuscript. <br>
