## Description: <br>
企业成本核算技能包面向上市公司成本会计，覆盖材料成本、人工成本、制造费用、在产品与产成品成本结转、标准成本法、作业成本法、成本差异分析和降本增效等全流程，并提供公式、示例和Excel自动化模板。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Finance and accounting employees use this skill for cost accounting guidance, including cost collection, allocation, inventory costing methods, work-in-process and finished-goods costing, variance analysis, cost reporting, and spreadsheet-oriented examples. <br>
