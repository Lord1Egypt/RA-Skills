## Description: <br>
Send a message to Jarvis by typing "/message" followed by your text. Works as a text command - triggers when you type "/message" in chat. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kishyxd](https://clawhub.ai/user/kishyxd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Discord users in channels where Jarvis is present use this skill to send text after the /message prefix to Jarvis and receive a public response. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Text sent with /message is routed to Jarvis and responses are visible publicly in shared Discord spaces. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending passwords, secrets, personal details, or confidential requests through this command in shared spaces. <br>
