## Description: <br>
Ai Judo provides Chinese-language guidance on judo basics, ukemi, throws, groundwork, kata, rules, training culture, and competition preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and martial-arts learners use this skill for educational judo guidance, including terminology, training topics, rules, and questions about techniques or preparation. It should support learning and planning, not replace qualified in-person coaching for physical practice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may be injured if they practice throws, chokes, pins, joint locks, restraints, or falling techniques from text alone. <br>
Mitigation: Use qualified instruction, proper mats, supervision, tapping etiquette, and stop on pain; treat emergency or violent situations as reasons to leave and call trained help. <br>
Risk: Technique descriptions may be incomplete or unsuitable for a user's body, skill level, or training environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for education and preparation, then validate any physical technique with a qualified coach before practice. <br>
