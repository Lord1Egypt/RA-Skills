## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agricultural, compliance, recall, and market outputs may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable as authoritative decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support and verify compliance, recall, treatment, and market decisions against current primary sources and qualified experts. <br>
Risk: Inputs may include proprietary farm, supplier, traceability, or food-safety records. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only data that is permitted under the user's data handling policies, and redact or minimize sensitive records before use. <br>
Risk: Photo-based pest or disease identification and soil or yield recommendations may be uncertain. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm high-impact agronomic recommendations with qualified agronomists, laboratory results, or field inspection before taking action. <br>
