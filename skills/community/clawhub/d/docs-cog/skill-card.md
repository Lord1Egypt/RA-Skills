## Description: <br>
AI document generation powered by CellCog for PDF and DOCX resumes, contracts, reports, proposals, invoices, certificates, NDAs, letters, brochures, legal documents, cover letters, and other professional documents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to ask CellCog to create professional business, career, legal, finance, education, marketing, event, and certificate documents as polished PDFs or editable DOCX files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, uploaded files, generated documents, and CELLCOG_API_KEY are sensitive because the skill relies on an external CellCog service. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when you trust CellCog with the provided content, keep CELLCOG_API_KEY in an environment variable or secret manager, and avoid including unrelated secrets in prompts or source documents. <br>
Risk: Generated resumes, contracts, invoices, legal documents, and confidential reports may contain inaccurate or unsuitable content. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated documents before sharing, signing, filing, sending, or using them for legal, financial, employment, or customer-facing decisions. <br>
Risk: The service may have privacy, retention, or billing practices that affect sensitive or regulated document workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Review CellCog privacy, retention, and billing practices before using the skill with confidential, legal, finance, or personal data. <br>
