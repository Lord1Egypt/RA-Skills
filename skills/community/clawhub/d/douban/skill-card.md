## Description: <br>
Douban is a Chinese-language book, film, and media assistant for writing reviews, analyzing ratings, and planning watchlists or reading lists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhangifonly](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhangifonly) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to draft Douban-style film and book reviews, analyze rating patterns, identify suspicious scoring behavior, and plan themed movie or book lists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reviews or rating analysis may contain outdated Douban scores, release details, or factual claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current Douban information and factual claims before posting. <br>
Risk: Generated comments could mislead readers or violate community rules if posted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit comments for accuracy, originality, spoiler handling, and Douban community-rule compliance. <br>
