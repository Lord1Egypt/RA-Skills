## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: HR, labor-law, termination, compensation, and dispute guidance may be incomplete or incorrect for a specific jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafting support and verify them with qualified local HR, legal, or compliance reviewers before use. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad people-management wording and provide HR guidance in contexts where the user expected narrower assistance. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the request is an HR or people-management scenario before relying on the generated response. <br>
