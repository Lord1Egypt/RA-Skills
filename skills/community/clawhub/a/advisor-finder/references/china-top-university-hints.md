# China Top University School Hints

Use this file as a navigation reference when the target is a major Chinese university and the user wants economics/management, computer science, software, or data science related schools.

Important:
- These are official-looking entry hints, not guaranteed final faculty directories.
- School naming differs by university. Some use School, College, Department, Institute, or Academy.
- Always verify the final school page and faculty page live before using it as authority.

## Tsinghua University
- Main: https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/
- School of Economics and Management: https://www.sem.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/
- Department of Computer Science and Technology: https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/csen/
- School of Software: https://www.thss.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/
- Data science related direction can appear under SIGS or data/information institutes, not always a standalone school.

## Peking University
- Main: https://www.pku.edu.cn/
- Guanghua School of Management: https://www.gsm.pku.edu.cn/
- School of Economics (English): https://econ.pku.edu.cn/english/
- School of Computer Science: https://cs.pku.edu.cn/
- Data science related faculty may be split across CS, AI, statistics, or interdisciplinary institutes.

## Zhejiang University
- Main: https://www.zju.edu.cn/
- Personal page portal: https://person.zju.edu.cn/
- Computer Science related schools may be split across software, computer science, control, CAD/CG, AI, or labs.
- Treat personal pages as partial evidence because direct scraping can be flaky.

## Fudan University
- Main: https://www.fudan.edu.cn/
- Management-related units may sit under School of Management rather than a combined econ-management school.
- Computer and data-related units may sit under computer science, big data, AI, or engineering schools.

## Shanghai Jiao Tong University
- Main: https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/
- Antai College of Economics and Management: https://acem.sjtu.edu.cn/
- Computer Science: https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/
- Software or data science may appear under separate institutes or graduate schools.

## University of Science and Technology of China
- Main: https://www.ustc.edu.cn/
- Management/econ units may not mirror the naming style of comprehensive universities.
- CS, software, AI, and data science may be distributed across multiple schools or institutes.

## Nanjing University
- Main: https://www.nju.edu.cn/
- Business / management and CS schools are usually distinct.
- Data science may appear as an institute, center, or program rather than a standalone school.

## Wuhan University
- Main: https://www.whu.edu.cn/
- Economics and management often appear as separate schools.
- Computer, software, and remote sensing / data related units may cross over.

## Harbin Institute of Technology
- Main: https://www.hit.edu.cn/
- CS/software/AI faculty can be distributed across departments and research centers.

## Xi'an Jiaotong University
- Main: https://www.xjtu.edu.cn/
- Economics and management often appear under a management school and economics programs.
- CS/software/data science may span several schools.

## How to use this file
1. Lock the main university domain first.
2. Prefer the exact school page when the user names the school.
3. If the school name is fuzzy, search within the official domain for likely units: management, economics, computer, software, data, AI.
4. When no standalone software school exists, fall back to CS or software engineering institutes.
5. When no standalone data science school exists, look for data science programs, institutes, AI schools, or statistics-related units.

## Hard rule
Do not claim a school exists just because the pattern is common at another university. Verify it on the official domain.
