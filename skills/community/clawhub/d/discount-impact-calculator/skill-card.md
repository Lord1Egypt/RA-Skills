## Description: <br>
Calculate how discounts affect revenue, margin, conversion assumptions, and allowable acquisition cost so teams can see whether a promotion is actually worth running. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce, growth, and merchandising teams use this skill to pressure-test discount, coupon, bundle, and upsell scenarios before launch. It helps compare baseline and promotional economics, including margin, break-even, refund, conversion, AOV, and CAC assumptions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated calculations or scripts may be wrong or may expose sensitive pricing, margin, refund, or acquisition-cost assumptions if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated analysis and Python script before running or sharing it, and avoid entering confidential commercial data unless the execution environment is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: The skill may ask initial clarification questions in Chinese, which can reduce accessibility for some users. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to continue in the user's preferred language before providing business inputs. <br>
