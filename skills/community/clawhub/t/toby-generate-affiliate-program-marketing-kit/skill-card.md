## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated affiliate marketing claims, disclosures, tracking guidance, or brand language may be inaccurate, noncompliant, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated copy, claims, legal disclosures, tracking links, and brand guidance before external use. <br>
Risk: Generated or suggested image assets may carry brand, licensing, or rights concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm image rights and brand approval before publishing banners or other visual assets. <br>
Risk: Automatic publishing or affiliate account-management permissions could increase operational impact beyond the reviewed instruction-only behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Do not grant separate publishing or account-management permissions without an additional security and workflow review. <br>
