## Description: <br>
Assess IR35 employment status for UK contractors using HMRC criteria and case law factors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jamespatrickthom2003-star](https://clawhub.ai/user/jamespatrickthom2003-star) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Contractors, recruitment agencies, end clients, accountants, and tax advisers use this skill to assess UK IR35 employment status, review contracts and working practices, generate risk ratings, recommend contract amendments, and draft Status Determination Statements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United Kingdom <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste unredacted contracts or personal details into the assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Redact names, addresses, signatures, rates, account details, and confidential client terms unless they are essential to the assessment. <br>
Risk: The output could be mistaken for legal or tax advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the assessment as guidance and have a qualified professional review real determinations, especially borderline or complex cases. <br>
