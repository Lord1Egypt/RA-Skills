## Description: <br>
Creates well-structured agile user stories with acceptance criteria for product development. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huajianjiu000](https://clawhub.ai/user/huajianjiu000) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, product owners, and development teams use this skill to turn feature descriptions, personas, priorities, and constraints into agile user stories with acceptance criteria, story point estimates, technical considerations, and dependencies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential roadmap, customer, or product strategy details into the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide sensitive planning data when the workspace policy allows that data to be processed. <br>
Risk: Generated stories, estimates, or acceptance criteria may be incomplete or inaccurate for the product context. <br>
Mitigation: Review and refine generated stories with product, engineering, and stakeholder input before treating them as final requirements. <br>
