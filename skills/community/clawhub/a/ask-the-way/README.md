# Ask the Way (问道)

> **Ancient wisdom for modern minds. 中英双语。**

Turn 2,500-year-old Daoist philosophy from the *Dao De Jing* (道德经) into practical, actionable insights for today's real-life challenges.

## What It Does

1. You describe a situation — career dilemma, leadership struggle, creative block, life uncertainty
2. The Skill matches your concern to the most relevant chapter of the *Dao De Jing*
3. You receive: the original passage (Classical Chinese + English) + modern interpretation + 3 actionable insights + a mind-flipping perspective shift

## Use Cases

- ✅ Career crossroads and life decisions
- ✅ Leadership and team management struggles
- ✅ Creative blocks and mental ruts
- ✅ Relationship friction (work or personal)
- ✅ Existential uncertainty and anxiety
- ✅ Just want to stretch your thinking (preventive reflection)

## Example

**You say:** *"I'm leading a team and people keep pushing back on my decisions."*

**Ask the Way returns:**
> **Chapter 17** — "The best leader — those below barely know they exist."
> Insight 1: The harder you grip, the more resistance you create.
> Insight 2: Build a system that runs itself; invest in shared ownership.
> Mind Flip: "Nobody follows my plan" is a symptom, not the problem.

## Methodology

The *Dao De Jing* (c. 6th–4th century BCE) has 81 chapters. Each chapter is a dense, poetic teaching. This Skill extracts their **modern-life relevance** through a structured 5-step process: concern → match → passage → interpretation → actionable insights.
