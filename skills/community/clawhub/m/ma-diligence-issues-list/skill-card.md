## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a corporate-development associate, M&A attorney, or PE deal team needs to turn data-room artifacts into a structured diligence issues list. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Corporate development teams, M&A counsel, private-equity deal teams, and banking analysts use this skill to convert data-room excerpts into a workstream-tagged diligence issues package for licensed-counsel and deal-partner review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow may process confidential target-company deal materials. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when users are comfortable providing sensitive deal materials to their agent, and keep target names, deal terms, counterparties, financials, and personnel details confidential. <br>
Risk: Outputs could be mistaken for legal, tax, accounting, valuation, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as a structured draft for licensed-counsel and deal-partner review, not as a binding diligence opinion or go/no-go decision. <br>
