## Description: <br>
Generate a full week of social media content for any topic. Outputs platform-optimized posts for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Instagram with hashtags and posting times. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[claudiodrusus](https://clawhub.ai/user/claudiodrusus) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and small teams use this skill to generate a weekly draft content calendar for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Instagram from a topic and optional audience. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Running the generator replaces content-calendar.md in the current directory. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in a workspace where replacing that file is acceptable, or move any existing calendar first. <br>
Risk: Generated social posts are drafts and may not match brand voice, facts, or platform policy needs. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit the calendar before scheduling or publishing posts. <br>
