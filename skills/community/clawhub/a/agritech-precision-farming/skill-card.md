## Description: <br>
AgriTech provides AI-driven precision farming decision support for crop monitoring, soil nutrient analysis, pest and disease guidance, weather risk assessment, irrigation planning, yield forecasting, and agricultural price trend analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External agricultural users, advisors, and developers use this skill to turn field observations, soil reports, crop images, weather context, remote-sensing indicators, and market signals into farm management recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agricultural treatment, pesticide, fertilizer, irrigation, and yield recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for local field conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as decision support, verify recommendations against local agronomy guidance, product labels, residue limits, safety intervals, and qualified agricultural experts before acting. <br>
Risk: Remote-sensing, image-based diagnosis, weather, and market-price analysis can be limited by input quality, local availability, and changing conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Require current location-specific data, clearly label estimates, and confirm high-impact disease, weather, or financial decisions with authoritative local sources. <br>
