## Description: <br>
Applies Anthropic's official brand colors and typography to artifacts that need Anthropic-style visual formatting or company design standards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[moyu0032](https://clawhub.ai/user/moyu0032) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and agents use this skill to apply Anthropic-inspired colors, typography, and visual styling to artifacts that need consistent brand presentation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill presents styling guidance as Anthropic's official brand look and feel. <br>
Mitigation: Before using it for public, commercial, or trademark-sensitive materials, verify current Anthropic brand rules and whether this package is authorized to present them as official. <br>
Risk: Typography output may vary when Poppins or Lora are unavailable in the target environment. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target environment has the intended fonts installed, or review the Arial and Georgia fallbacks before publishing styled artifacts. <br>
