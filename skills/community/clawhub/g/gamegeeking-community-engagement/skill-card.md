## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through GameGeeking community posting or commenting with account-source confirmation, content-source confirmation, and browser-based login, registration, submission, and result reporting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External agents and operators use this skill to perform browser-based posts or comments in the GameGeeking game community. It covers account selection or registration, content drafting, submission, and completion reporting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles GameGeeking credentials and can register accounts or edit public profiles. <br>
Mitigation: Use only a dedicated GameGeeking account, do not share a primary password, and require explicit approval for every profile edit. <br>
Risk: The skill automates public posting or commenting and may make automated participation look like ordinary user activity. <br>
Mitigation: Require approval for the exact post or comment before publication and avoid disguised automated, sponsored, or coordinated activity. <br>
