## Description: <br>
Researches companies with Exa web search, covering company overview, products, funding, team, recent news, and related company intelligence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mariokarras](https://clawhub.ai/user/mariokarras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to gather structured company intelligence for competitive research, partnership evaluation, prospect qualification, and general company overview tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Company names, target details, or local product-marketing context may be sent to Exa as search queries. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the local Exa CLI helper is trusted and avoid confidential strategy or target details unless sending those terms to Exa is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Web search results may be outdated, incomplete, or unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-reference multiple sources and flag findings that appear outdated or unverified. <br>
