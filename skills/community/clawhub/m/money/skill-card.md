## Use Case: <br>
External users and assistants use this skill to frame personal finance guidance around debt, emergency funds, investing, tax awareness, insurance, automation, and common warning signs. It should gather context such as country, debt, and income stability before giving general guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat educational finance guidance as personalized financial, tax, or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present guidance as general education, ask for relevant context, and direct users to qualified professionals for decisions that depend on their personal situation. <br>
Risk: The skill may ask for personal financial context such as debts, income stability, or country of residence. <br>
Mitigation: Request only the details needed for the guidance, avoid unnecessary sensitive information, and do not persist or share user financial context. <br>
