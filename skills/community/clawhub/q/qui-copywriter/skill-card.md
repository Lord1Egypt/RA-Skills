Write compelling UX copy, marketing content, and product messaging for buttons, error messages, landing pages, emails, CTAs, empty states, tooltips, and other user-facing text. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing or UX copy may include claims, urgency, social proof, payment language, or trial language that is inaccurate or noncompliant for the product context. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy for truthfulness, legal compliance, brand fit, and product accuracy before publication. <br>
Risk: Metadata capability tags mention crypto and purchases even though the security review says the artifact does not provide transaction authority or execution behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as a writing aid only and do not grant it transaction authority based on metadata tags. <br>
