## Description: <br>
Accountability coaching behavior for nudge: psychology, messaging, verification, and strictness for task creation, reminders, completion, failure, deadline negotiation, cancellation, and consequence handling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to compose firm accountability coaching messages for Nudge tasks, including reminders, completion checks, failure handling, extensions, cancellation requests, and consequence enforcement. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Proof of completion, failure reasons, task details, and motivation fields may be shared with configured accountability targets. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive health, employment, location, relationship, document links, screenshots, private repository details, or other private information unless the user is comfortable with the configured targets seeing it. <br>
