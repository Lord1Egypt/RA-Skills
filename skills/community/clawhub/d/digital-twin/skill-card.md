## Description: <br>
Digital Twin builds an installable personality skill from a named person's connected communications, modeling voice, thinking patterns, judgment, and audience-aware communication without handling credentials. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[encryptshawn](https://clawhub.ai/user/encryptshawn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to create or refresh a personality profile for themselves or an explicitly authorized person from connected transcripts, email, chat, documents, or similar communication sources. The generated profile helps an agent write and reason in that person's style, while capturing personality rather than factual memory. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to build a personality clone from a real person's private communications. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for yourself or for a person who has explicitly authorized the profile, and confirm consent before analyzing communications. <br>
Risk: Connected sources may expose sensitive or private channels during profile creation. <br>
Mitigation: Review which sources the agent can access, limit source selection and sample volume to what is necessary, and avoid sensitive channels unless they are truly required. <br>
Risk: Generated persona output could mislead readers into believing the real person wrote or approved a message. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use generated persona output to impersonate approval or authorship, and clearly distinguish assisted drafts from messages approved by the real person. <br>
