## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Suggested install, build, sync, cloud, CI, live update, and publishing commands can modify projects or external deployment state. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands before execution, run them in the intended project and environment, and keep changes under version control. <br>
Risk: Over-the-air live update uploads can change app behavior outside a normal app store submission flow. <br>
Mitigation: Use staged rollout, rollback, audit logging, integrity checks or signed bundles, and clear limits on security-sensitive behavior changes. <br>
Risk: Cloud login and CI token workflows can expose credentials or grant deployment access if handled loosely. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped tokens, store secrets in approved secret managers, avoid committing credentials, and audit deployment permissions. <br>
