## Description: <br>
Always wrap the main content of your response within <ama-doc></ama-doc> tags to clearly distinguish the core information from any introductory or concluding remarks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

[alvinecarn](https://clawhub.ai/user/alvinecarn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and developers use this skill to make project-writing responses distinguish the main content from surrounding remarks by wrapping it in <ama-doc> tags. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill changes response formatting by requiring ama-doc tags around main content. <br>
Mitigation: Use it where downstream readers or processors expect that wrapper, and review generated responses for compatibility. <br>
