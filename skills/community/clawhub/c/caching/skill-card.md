## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Remote GitHub-based installation or global copy commands can make the skill available more broadly than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Trust the referenced source before installation and prefer project-local installation when broad availability is not needed. <br>
Risk: Applying caching guidance without an invalidation, TTL, or sensitive-data review can produce stale data or expose cached secrets and personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before use, define TTL and invalidation behavior, and avoid caching sensitive data unless it is explicitly protected. <br>
