## Description: <br>
Football English helps agents run a Telegram-style bilingual football quiz that teaches English vocabulary with Russian translations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[russianoracle](https://clawhub.ai/user/russianoracle) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to test football knowledge and learn English football vocabulary through button-only quiz flows in Telegram-style chats. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users who cannot use inline buttons or who need non-Russian language support may not be able to complete the intended quiz flow. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target chat platform supports inline buttons and that English-Russian bilingual content matches the intended audience before deployment. <br>
