## Use Case: <br>
Developers and creators use this skill to guide an agent through a design-philosophy step and then produce polished static visual artwork such as posters, art pieces, PDFs, or PNG images with minimal text. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External font downloads may introduce unreviewed third-party assets or licensing concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Approve the font source before download, prefer bundled or well-known font providers, and review generated files before publishing or sharing. <br>
Risk: Generated visual outputs may need quality, layout, or rights review before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated .md, .png, or .pdf artifacts for visual quality, text placement, and suitability before distributing them. <br>
