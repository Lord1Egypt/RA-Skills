## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Parkour and freerunning activities can cause injury, especially advanced moves such as flips, wall flips, wall spins, Double Kong, narrow precision jumps, and drops. <br>
Mitigation: Practice advanced moves only with qualified supervision, appropriate mats or gym equipment, and within the user's current ability. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on text-only coaching for physical movement decisions that depend on environment, fitness, fatigue, and weather. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as general training guidance and perform local risk assessment before attempting movements. <br>
