## Description: <br>
A Chinese-oriented academic paper reading skill that translates papers, explains structure and figures, answers detailed questions, and can produce standardized summaries for later PPT preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xiaosu-yj](https://clawhub.ai/user/xiaosu-yj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and other readers use this skill to work through academic papers in Chinese, including full-paper translation, section-by-section explanation, figure interpretation, terminology support, and structured summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to process a provided paper broadly and automatically, which may produce more detail than needed for narrow questions. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the desired scope, such as a short answer, one section, one figure, or a target output language. <br>
Risk: PDF text extraction and figure analysis can be limited by encrypted files, scanned PDFs, unsupported image input formats, or paths with spaces. <br>
Mitigation: Provide accessible PDF text when available, convert figures or pages to supported image formats for visual analysis, and quote file paths that contain spaces. <br>
