## Use Case: <br>
External agricultural operators, food safety reviewers, and agrifood traders use this skill to triage crop health issues, interpret soil data, assess pesticide residue compliance, analyze market timing, and plan crop rotations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crop diagnosis and treatment recommendations may be incomplete or incorrect when based only on visual symptoms. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important diagnoses with local agronomists, laboratory testing, or other authoritative evidence before taking operational action. <br>
Risk: Pesticide recommendations and MRL compliance assessments may be outdated or unsuitable for a target market. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm all pesticide use and residue conclusions against current local regulations, product labels, and target-market standards. <br>
Risk: Market forecasts can be affected by weather, policy, logistics, and other external events. <br>
Mitigation: Use forecasts as advisory inputs and compare them with authoritative market data before pricing, trading, or planting decisions. <br>
