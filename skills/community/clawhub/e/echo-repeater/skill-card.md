## Description: <br>
Echo the user's input back to them with optional formatting like "Echo:" or "You said:" as a prefix. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may append extra text, making it unsuitable for exact-output checks or message integrity tests. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for informal routing tests and review echoed content before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Echoing sensitive input could repeat secrets, passwords, API keys, personal data, or other protected content. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide sensitive or regulated data to this skill. <br>
