## Description: <br>
Automates short-video storyboard script generation by opening DeepSeek in Chrome, enabling Deep Thinking and Smart Search, and submitting user-provided narration for scene-by-scene output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[infiniteask](https://clawhub.ai/user/infiniteask) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to convert narration text into a segmented short-video storyboard with visual scene descriptions and matching narration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Narration text is submitted to DeepSeek through Chrome. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential, personal, regulated, or proprietary narration unless sending it to DeepSeek is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Browser automation may run in a Chrome profile with sensitive active sessions. <br>
Mitigation: Use a browser profile without sensitive logged-in sessions when running the skill. <br>
Risk: Generated storyboard text may need editorial review before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review the returned scene descriptions and narration alignment before using the storyboard. <br>
