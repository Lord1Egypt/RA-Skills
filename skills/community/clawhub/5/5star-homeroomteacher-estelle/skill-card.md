## Description: <br>
This skill coaches Chinese-speaking homeroom teachers through a 66-day classroom-management path with guidance for student relationships, rule-setting, parent communication, emotional resilience, and scenario planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad stress-related wording outside a school-management context. <br>
Mitigation: For non-school stress or mental-health support, ask the agent to use a more appropriate skill or context. <br>
Risk: Teacher-coaching guidance can be misapplied to sensitive student, parent, or classroom situations. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated guidance against school policy and local safeguarding expectations before acting on it. <br>
