## Description: <br>
Build a personal meeting system for capturing notes, preparing agendas, and never missing follow-ups. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and professionals use this skill to turn meeting transcripts or quick notes into structured records, agendas, attendee context, and follow-up tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting transcripts, attendee notes, communication preferences, and action items may contain confidential or personal information stored under ~/meetings/. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm with the user before writing or retaining sensitive meeting content, and use the skill only when local storage in ~/meetings/ is acceptable. <br>
