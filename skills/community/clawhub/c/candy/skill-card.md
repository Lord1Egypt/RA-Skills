## Description: <br>
Candy helps agents summarize public Candy Crush Saga updates, events, competitions, help content, and community rules without handling private account data or cheating guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CodeKungfu](https://clawhub.ai/user/CodeKungfu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use Candy to turn public Candy Crush Saga game pages, announcements, events, competitions, help pages, and rule notices into dated summaries, tables, and checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be asked to process credentials, private player data, payment details, cheating requests, or account services. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with public pages and decline requests involving private data, payments, cheating, bypasses, boosting, or account services. <br>
Risk: Candy Crush Saga events, rewards, and version details can change frequently. <br>
Mitigation: Keep dates and source links in outputs so users can verify time-sensitive information against the current public source. <br>
