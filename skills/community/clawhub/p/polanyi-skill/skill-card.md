## Description: <br>
Polanyi Skill helps an agent analyze tacit knowledge, skill acquisition, knowledge transfer, apprenticeship, knowledge management, learning methods, AI/tool substitution boundaries, scientific communities, and post-critical philosophy through a Michael Polanyi-inspired perspective. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[enzyme2013](https://clawhub.ai/user/enzyme2013) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, knowledge workers, educators, and organizational practitioners use this skill to reason about tacit knowledge, SOP transfer, mentorship, learning, knowledge management, and the limits of AI/tool substitution. The skill frames responses through Polanyi's concepts such as tacit knowledge, indwelling, subsidiary-focal awareness, fiduciary commitment, post-critical philosophy, and the republic of science. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can nudge responses into a fixed mixed Chinese/English Polanyi-style voice that may not match language, accessibility, or tone requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Restate the desired language, tone, and accessibility constraints when activating the skill, and ask for a neutral summary when role voice is not appropriate. <br>
Risk: Persona-based analysis of topics after Polanyi's 1976 death may be mistaken for direct historical claims or forecasts by Polanyi. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the skill's disclaimer for post-1976 topics and request source-grounded factual support when historical accuracy matters. <br>
