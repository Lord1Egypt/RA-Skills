## Description: <br>
Plan, execute, and measure business events including conferences, webinars, workshops, product launches, networking events, trade shows, and corporate gatherings across the complete lifecycle from concept to post-event ROI analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external event teams, and business operators use this skill to create event strategies, budgets, timelines, registration and marketing plans, sponsorship materials, run-of-show documents, surveys, and ROI analyses for business events. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Event registration, sponsor, survey, lead, and attendee-list workflows can involve personal or business-sensitive data. <br>
Mitigation: Collect only necessary fields, share attendee or lead data only with explicit opt-in consent, restrict access to exports and printouts, delete copies when no longer needed, and follow applicable privacy laws. <br>
