## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-26
Initial release. Guides a planning consultant, zoning attorney, or property-owner applicant through drafting a zoning-variance application narrative — capturing parcel and ordinance section, variance type (area / dimensional vs. use), the unique physical conditions of the property that create unnecessary hardship (not self-created), the no-substantial-detriment-to-the-neighborhood and not-contrary-to-the-public-good analyses, and proposed Findings of Fact mapped to the jurisdiction's adopted variance standards (Euclidean, Münzer, NJ "C-1 / C-2", NY "practical difficulty / unnecessary hardship", or AHJ-specific) — and produces a DRAFT narrative packet with a self-created-hardship audit, a "what NOT to argue" red-flag list, an evidence index (survey, photos, neighbor consents, expert reports), and a hearing-ready Q&A prep block for ZBA / BZA presentation.
