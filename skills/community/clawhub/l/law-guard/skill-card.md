## Description: <br>
面向一人公司和小微团队提供日常法务支持，帮助用户进行合同审查、劳动用工、知识产权、公司治理与合规体检，并用风险分级和行动清单输出可执行建议。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Small-company founders, operators, and team leads use this skill for first-pass legal triage, risk identification, drafting support, and decision checklists for common business law questions. It is intended to organize issues and escalation triggers, not to replace advice from a licensed lawyer. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat general legal triage as formal legal representation or a definitive legal opinion. <br>
Mitigation: State that outputs are informational, verify current local rules, and escalate lawsuits, regulatory action, criminal exposure, major transactions, and high-value disputes to a licensed lawyer. <br>
Risk: Legal questions can include sensitive personal, employee, client, or dispute details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to share only the minimum necessary facts and redact sensitive identifiers where possible. <br>
Risk: Labor and compliance rules can vary by city or region and may change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm jurisdiction before localized answers and flag uncertain local requirements for official-channel or lawyer verification. <br>
