## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language cognitive-bias advisor that helps users examine decisions, learning, action, communication, influence, and management situations and produce bias-aware strategies and scripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jacksu](https://clawhub.ai/user/jacksu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as an interactive Chinese-language coach for decision review, learning habits, action planning, communication, influence, and team management. It guides users through diagnostic questions, bias checks, strategy lists, and reusable wording. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate the skill during ordinary decision, learning, communication, or management conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only where automatic Chinese-language cognitive-bias coaching is desired, and disable or avoid the skill when broad conversational activation would be disruptive. <br>
Risk: Influence and persuasion guidance could be misused to pressure or manipulate other people. <br>
Mitigation: Apply influence scripts transparently, avoid deceptive or coercive use, and keep the skill's stated principles of non-harm, transparency, and reciprocal self-checking in view. <br>
Risk: Bias diagnostics and suggested actions can be incomplete or misleading if treated as final decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as decision support, review important recommendations before acting, and seek domain-specific advice for high-impact financial, legal, medical, employment, or interpersonal decisions. <br>
