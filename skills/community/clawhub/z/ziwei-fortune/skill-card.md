## Description: <br>
Generates Zi Wei Dou Shu charts from lunar birth date, birth time, and gender, then returns deterministic chart calculations with interpretive Markdown for entertainment and self-reflection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[eamanc-lab](https://clawhub.ai/user/eamanc-lab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to calculate and interpret Zi Wei Dou Shu birth charts from lunar birth details. It supports full chart analysis, single-palace queries, major-cycle readings, and main-star lookups for entertainment and self-exploration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save lunar birth date, birth time, gender, and chart details in a local MEMORY.md file. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with consent to local retention, and review or delete MEMORY.md when retained profile data is not desired. <br>
Risk: Fortune-chart interpretations may be mistaken for medical, legal, financial, or other professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present results as entertainment and self-reflection, and direct major life decisions to qualified professional advice. <br>
Risk: Astrological or fortune-telling language can become absolute, frightening, or deterministic. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the artifact's prohibited-expression rules: avoid death predictions, absolute claims, fear-based wording, and paid remedy recommendations. <br>
