## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese-oriented company research, due diligence, and competitor analysis reports covering business model, competitive position, SWOT, market size, management assessment, and key risks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[olina1ye](https://clawhub.ai/user/olina1ye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Investment managers, strategy analysts, business development teams, financial advisers, and industry researchers use this skill to draft Chinese company research, pre-investment due diligence, competitor analysis, business briefing, supplier background check, and industry research outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated due diligence, financial, legal, market, or investment claims may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and independently verify claims against authoritative sources before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The skill is Chinese-oriented, so outputs may be in Chinese unless the user requests another language. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the desired output language when prompting the skill. <br>
