## Description: <br>
Find the best social media promotion spots for any product and generate platform-tailored engagement copy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xjouska](https://clawhub.ai/user/xjouska) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and marketing operators use this skill to analyze a product URL or name, identify ranked social media promotion opportunities, and draft platform-tailored engagement copy for manual review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends product names or URLs to AI-CMO's third-party service with the user's API key. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit confidential launches, internal URLs, customer data, secrets, or regulated information unless AI-CMO's data handling has been approved. <br>
Risk: Generated engagement copy could violate platform rules or appear spammy if posted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated comments as drafts, review every post manually, follow each platform's rules, and avoid stealth promotion or spam. <br>
