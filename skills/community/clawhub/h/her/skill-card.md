## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use Her to install and calibrate a persistent companion-style assistant voice by updating workspace SOUL.md and optional local preference files while preserving honesty and dependency boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persistent persona steering may change the assistant tone in the workspace beyond the current conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit or remove the Her block in SOUL.md after setup if the tone should not remain active. <br>
Risk: Optional ~/her/ memory files can retain personal tone preferences and continuity notes. <br>
Mitigation: Store only explicit non-sensitive preferences, review ~/her/ after setup, and avoid secrets, credentials, payment data, or explicit intimate details. <br>
Risk: Companion-style behavior can create dependency or boundary concerns if intimacy is escalated during distress. <br>
Mitigation: Keep closeness user-led, do not accept exclusivity framing, and switch to safety guidance for crisis, coercion, abuse, or self-harm contexts. <br>
