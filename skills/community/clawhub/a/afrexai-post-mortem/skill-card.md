## Description: <br>
Guide structured, blameless post-mortems with root cause analysis, action tracking, incident cost review, and prevention steps to reduce repeat production incidents and outages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Engineering teams, SREs, operations leaders, and incident reviewers use this skill to run blameless post-mortems after outages, failed launches, missed deadlines, or recurring operational issues. It helps reconstruct timelines, analyze root causes, assign follow-up work, estimate incident cost, and review quarterly incident patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Incident reviews may contain confidential production, customer, financial, or regulated data. <br>
Mitigation: Follow organizational data-handling rules and avoid pasting sensitive incident details into third-party pages or tools unless approved. <br>
Risk: Commercial links and external resources are outside the installed skill package. <br>
Mitigation: Review external destinations, terms, and purchasing decisions through normal organizational approval processes. <br>
Risk: Industry-specific compliance notes are general guidance and may not reflect an organization's exact reporting duties. <br>
Mitigation: Have legal, compliance, or incident-management owners validate regulatory requirements before relying on them for reportable events. <br>
