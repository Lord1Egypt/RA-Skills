## Description: <br>
Generates AI short-video scripts, shot breakdowns, visual prompts, subtitle copy, and compact prompts for Yijia, Sora, Grok, text-to-video, and image-to-video testing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shiftshen](https://clawhub.ai/user/shiftshen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, AI video producers, content marketers, and developers use this skill to turn a topic or keyword into a structured video plan with scenes, narration, subtitles, and generation-ready prompts. It is especially useful for short-form video planning and Yijia-oriented prompt testing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional Yijia workflow commands may reference local scripts, account targets, API keys, quotas, or paid generation resources. <br>
Mitigation: Review the local scripts and command arguments, replace hard-coded paths and target values, and confirm the intended account, API key, quota, and cost exposure before running them. <br>
