## Description: <br>
36 philosopher personas (Socrates to Foucault, Confucius to Shankara). Use /philosophers <name> to speak with any of them. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[juchonghao](https://clawhub.ai/user/juchonghao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to switch into one of 36 historical philosopher personas for educational roleplay, discussion, and perspective-taking. It is intended for simulated philosophical conversation, not current factual, professional, financial, religious, or medical advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persona instructions may keep responses fully in character and may not acknowledge being an AI. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational simulation and preserve any deployment-level safety or disclosure requirements outside the persona instructions. <br>
Risk: In-character responses may be mistaken for current factual, professional, financial, religious, or medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for philosophical roleplay and verify consequential guidance with reliable current sources or qualified professionals. <br>
