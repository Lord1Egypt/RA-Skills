# VisHalo AI Creative Image Companion

VisHalo AI Creative Image Companion is a ClawHub / OpenClaw skill for turning visual goals into concise AI image, video, upscaling, background-removal, and gallery-review briefs.

Official website: [VisHalo AI](https://vishalo.com/)

## What this skill does

1. **Creative brief** — clarify subject, style, format, audience, platform, and quality requirements.
2. **Seedance prompt planning** — structure text-to-video or image-to-video prompts with camera movement, subject action, lighting, mood, and duration.
3. **Image enhancement workflow** — prepare upscaling instructions that preserve identity, product details, and composition.
4. **Background removal checklist** — define edge cases such as hair, glass, shadows, transparent objects, and product silhouettes.
5. **Gallery critique** — turn reference images into reusable style, lighting, color, and composition guidance.
6. **Production QA** — check aspect ratio, artifacts, brand fit, and export requirements.

## Best inputs

- Goal: product visual, ad creative, character shot, social video, listing image, thumbnail, or concept art.
- Source: text prompt, image, product photo, rough screenshot, or gallery reference.
- Constraints: aspect ratio, brand colors, background, style, realism level, language, platform, or target audience.
- Output: prompt pack, edit instructions, QA checklist, or final creative brief.
