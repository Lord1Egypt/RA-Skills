## Description: <br>
Structured GO/NO-GO framework for validating content topics before scripting or recording through audience segmentation, adoption research, saturation checks, and competitive differentiation analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[pingukim225](https://clawhub.ai/user/pingukim225) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and channel operators use this skill to decide whether a content topic is worth producing and what differentiated angle to take before investing in scripting or recording. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may trigger a more extensive research workflow than expected for broad content-planning prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to skip, shorten, or scope the workflow when only brainstorming, scripting, or quick advice is needed. <br>
