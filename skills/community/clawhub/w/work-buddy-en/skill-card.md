## Description: <br>
Work Buddy provides brief, context-aware workday check-ins, useful micro-briefings, and low-pressure reminders while respecting the user's attention. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Julia-XD](https://clawhub.ai/user/Julia-XD) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and individual users use this skill when they want a calm assistant presence during work sessions that can offer short check-ins, useful nudges, micro-briefings, and loop-closing updates without demanding a reply. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Proactive check-ins may arrive more often than some users expect during work sessions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the activation wording before use, and disable or narrow the skill if explicit invocation is preferred. <br>
