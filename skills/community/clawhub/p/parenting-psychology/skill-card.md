## Description: <br>
Emotional and developmental parenting guidance for ages 0-18. Use when someone is struggling with child behavior, needs age-appropriate expectations, wants discipline strategies that work, or is overwhelmed by parenting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to provide age-aware parenting guidance, emotional-development context, discipline strategies, parent-stress triage, and escalation guidance for serious child or caregiver safety concerns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with local adaptation for discipline laws, education systems, and mental-health or crisis resources. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive family details, child behavior, caregiver stress, or follow-up schedules could be retained longer than necessary by a host agent. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize retained family details, avoid unnecessary follow-up storage, and review any memory or scheduling behavior before deployment. <br>
Risk: Users may treat parenting, medical, mental-health, legal, or crisis guidance as a substitute for qualified professional or emergency help. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as supportive guidance, escalate persistent or dangerous behavior to professionals, and route self-harm or caregiver-harm concerns to emergency or crisis resources. <br>
Risk: Parenting norms, corporal-punishment laws, school supports, and mental-health resources vary by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt advice to local legal and service contexts while continuing to prefer evidence-based, non-physical discipline alternatives. <br>
