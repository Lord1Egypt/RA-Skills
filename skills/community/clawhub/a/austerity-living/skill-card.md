## Description: <br>
Austerity Living helps people respond to major income loss by prioritizing essential bills, cutting large expenses, negotiating obligations, and finding assistance resources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People facing severe income reductions use this skill to build a financial survival plan, decide which bills to pay first, identify major expense cuts, and prepare creditor or assistance calls. Agents can use it to produce prioritized plans, scripts, checklists, and reminder suggestions for bill negotiation and monthly reviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may disclose sensitive financial details while creating budget state or planning creditor calls. <br>
Mitigation: Record only the minimum budgeting details needed and avoid storing account numbers, credentials, or unnecessary personal financial data. <br>
Risk: Calendar reminders or filesystem state could preserve inaccurate, outdated, or unwanted bill and assistance information. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed calendar and file changes before enabling them, and update or remove saved state after calls, payments, or applications change. <br>
Risk: Assistance-program guidance may not match a user's eligibility, location, or current program rules. <br>
Mitigation: Verify eligibility and application requirements through the listed official resources or local assistance providers before acting. <br>
