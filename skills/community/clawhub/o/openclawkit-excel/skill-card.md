## Description: <br>
openclawkit-excel helps agents create, read, clean, merge, and generate reports from local Excel spreadsheets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wanghao20150901](https://clawhub.ai/user/wanghao20150901) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and agents use this skill to automate local spreadsheet workflows such as reading, cleaning, merging, and writing Excel files and generating simple reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Spreadsheet outputs can overwrite files or sheets selected by the user during normal local operations. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit input and output paths, keep backups of important spreadsheets, and avoid writing outputs over originals unless replacement is intended. <br>
