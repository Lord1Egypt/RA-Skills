## Description: <br>
Aggregates public data from multiple ecommerce and social platforms to provide normalized competitor pricing, sales, and market analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yayale-deng](https://clawhub.ai/user/yayale-deng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Market analysts, ecommerce operators, and sourcing teams use this skill to compare product prices, sales signals, ratings, and market distribution across AliExpress, 1688, TikTok, Amazon, and target regions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
