## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Craft guidance may involve sharp tools, heat, adhesives, resin, woodworking equipment, or materials that require safe handling. <br>
Mitigation: Review safety steps, product labels, ventilation needs, and tool instructions before following project guidance. <br>
Risk: Material quantities, purchase suggestions, pricing, or project suitability may be incomplete or inaccurate for a user's location or skill level. <br>
Mitigation: Verify materials, costs, local availability, and difficulty before buying supplies or starting a project. <br>
