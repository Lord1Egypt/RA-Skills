## Description: <br>
Diagnose the root causes of product returns and build a concrete reduction plan targeting the highest-impact changes — covering listing accuracy, size guidance, packaging improvements, and post-purchase education that cut return rates by 20–50%. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators, catalog managers, customer experience teams, and analysts use this skill to diagnose high-return products, quantify the cost of returns, and produce a prioritized return-reduction plan for listings, size guidance, packaging, post-purchase education, and return policy changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Return exports, customer order records, shipping labels, and return photos may contain sensitive customer or business data. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize collected data, redact shipping labels and identifiers, and store materials according to the user's privacy and retention practices. <br>
Risk: Recommendations based on incomplete return reason tagging or missing cost inputs can misprioritize return-reduction work. <br>
Mitigation: Establish baseline metrics, use a specific return reason taxonomy, and review results after implementation before treating projected savings as final. <br>
