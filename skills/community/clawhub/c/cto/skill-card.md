## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineering leaders, and startup operators use this skill for CTO-style decision support across technical strategy, architecture, hiring, engineering operations, build-vs-buy choices, and technical debt planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CTO-style recommendations may be mistaken for automatic authority on hiring, firing, vendor commitments, security incidents, or major architecture choices. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for decision support only, and require accountable human review before organizational, contractual, security, or major technical commitments. <br>
Risk: Strategic guidance can be incomplete or misaligned when the agent lacks current business context, team constraints, or system details. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current company stage, reversibility, team capability, business timeline, and scale requirements before acting. <br>
