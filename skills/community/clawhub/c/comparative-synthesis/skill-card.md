## Description: <br>
Compare and synthesize findings across multiple completed DeepScan reports for cross-run analysis, trend comparison, or a unified summary from several research sessions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[papersareflowing](https://clawhub.ai/user/papersareflowing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, analysts, and developers use this skill to compare completed DeepScan reports, synthesize recurring findings, identify divergences and gaps, and create comparison plots when existing report data is sufficient. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can resurface information from prior DeepScan reports in a combined synthesis. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only where those reports are appropriate to combine and share; review synthesized outputs before broader distribution. <br>
Risk: Cross-report summaries may overstate agreement or miss nuance when condensed evidence is sparse. <br>
Mitigation: Retrieve full named reports for specific comparisons and call out sparse data instead of plotting or generalizing from it. <br>
