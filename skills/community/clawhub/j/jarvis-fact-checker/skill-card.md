## Description: <br>
Jarvis Fact Checker guides agents to verify legal references, dates, numbers, citations, cases, and external-source claims before sending information to users. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[claudedamir-art](https://clawhub.ai/user/claudedamir-art) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and developers use this skill to make responses more cautious about factual and legal claims, especially when citing laws, regulations, court cases, dates, metrics, or external sources. It directs the agent to disclose when a source cannot be checked instead of inventing plausible details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may make an agent more conservative when reliable sources are unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Have the agent clearly state that a source cannot be verified and offer an alternate verification path instead of inventing details. <br>
