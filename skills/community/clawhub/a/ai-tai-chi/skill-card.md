## Description: <br>
AI tai chi guidance covering the Chen, Yang, Wu, Wu/Hao, and Sun styles; 24-form simplified tai chi; standing practice; silk-reeling energy; push hands; form analysis; tai chi principles; health-oriented practice; and tai chi philosophy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Risk: Users may over-rely on tai chi health or pain guidance as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present health-related responses as general exercise education and advise users with significant or persistent symptoms to consult a qualified clinician or physical therapist. <br>
