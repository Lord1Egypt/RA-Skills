## Description: <br>
Create a minimalist marketing plan focused on building an audience through content, not ads. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[carollili](https://clawhub.ai/user/carollili) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, and business advisors use this skill to build a content-led marketing plan after early product-market fit. It helps choose a primary content platform, plan educate/inspire/entertain content, build an email list, decide what to share publicly, and identify when paid advertising may be appropriate. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
