## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated HTML may not be suitable for direct publication under every CMS or site security policy. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML before publishing and remove inline event handlers when the target CMS or security policy disallows inline JavaScript. <br>
Risk: The skill can activate on broad product-review, affiliate-content, or React-build prompts where the user did not intend to use this specific content system. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the requested target site, content format, and framework before applying the FurrBudd or AI Money Mastery templates. <br>
Risk: Generated affiliate or editorial content may contain misleading claims or unsuitable recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review product claims, affiliate disclosures, links, and editorial conclusions before placing generated content on a live site. <br>
