## Description: <br>
基于产业规模、创新能力、企业实力、资本活跃度、生态成熟度及发展潜力等维度，生成区域产业竞争力排名和对标分析报告。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External analysts, economic development teams, industry planners, and investment-promotion teams use this skill to benchmark a target industry across regions, identify leading and emerging areas, diagnose local gaps, and draft catch-up or招商路径 recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reports may contain stale, incomplete, or weakly sourced public data because the skill relies on live web research and public-source synthesis. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited sources, publication dates, and scoring assumptions before using outputs for strategy, investment promotion, or policy decisions. <br>
Risk: Quantitative rankings may appear more precise than the available public data supports. <br>
Mitigation: Review the weighting model, mark missing data, and treat scores as decision support rather than authoritative rankings. <br>
