## Description: <br>
文献全文结构一键拆分 helps an agent split user-provided academic paper text into a structured section-by-section outline with each section's purpose, author intent, and concise content summary. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[11xinshou](https://clawhub.ai/user/11xinshou) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and academic reviewers use this skill to turn pasted abstracts or full paper text into a structured review of the paper's sections, including purpose, author intent, and a short content summary for each section. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided academic papers may contain confidential, sensitive, or restricted full text. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in an agent environment approved for the document's data sensitivity and avoid pasting restricted material into unapproved systems. <br>
Risk: The generated structure may omit nuance or misclassify non-standard paper sections. <br>
Mitigation: Review the outline against the source text before relying on it for academic, publication, or research decisions. <br>
