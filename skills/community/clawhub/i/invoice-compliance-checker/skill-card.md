## Description: <br>
Checks invoices for compliance with regulatory requirements and internal policies, producing structured analysis, templates, and recommendations without executing transactions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Finance, accounts payable, tax, and operations teams use this skill to assess invoice templates or vendor invoices against regulatory requirements and internal purchase-order controls. It returns checklists, exception reports, remediation steps, and self-check workflows for human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Invoice compliance guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for legal, tax, or financial decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as an informational checklist and have qualified legal, tax, finance, or compliance professionals review decisions before acting. <br>
Risk: Broad finance-oriented trigger wording may activate the skill for prompts outside invoice compliance. <br>
Mitigation: Keep usage focused on invoice compliance, invoice verification, purchase-order controls, and related review workflows. <br>
Risk: The skill does not independently verify live regulatory requirements, invoice authenticity, or external records. <br>
Mitigation: Validate results against authoritative government portals, current regulations, internal policies, and source invoice documents. <br>
