## Description: <br>
根据学科、年级生成个性化学习助理，支持数学、物理、化学、生物、语文、英语、历史、地理、计算机科学、工程技术、医学、经济学、文学、管理学、艺术学、农学等学科；当用户需要创建学科学习助手、定制教学内容或设计学习路径时使用 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and educators use this skill to create Chinese-language, subject-specific learning assistants for a target discipline, grade level, and learning objective. It helps structure assistant roles, teaching strategies, knowledge frameworks, learning paths, resource suggestions, and interaction examples. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Educational outputs for high-stakes subjects such as medicine, engineering, finance, or current technical topics may be incomplete, outdated, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational drafts and verify them with qualified sources or professionals before relying on them. <br>
