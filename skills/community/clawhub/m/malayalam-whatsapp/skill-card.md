## Description: <br>
Understands Malayalam and Manglish WhatsApp messages and replies politely using culturally appropriate language in matching script style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Babuperumana](https://clawhub.ai/user/Babuperumana) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users and agents use this skill to interpret Malayalam-script or Manglish WhatsApp messages, draft respectful replies, and handle common Malayalam translations while matching the user's script style. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Message content shared with the agent may contain personal or sensitive conversation details. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide WhatsApp message content that the user is comfortable having the agent process. <br>
Risk: Generated replies may miss cultural nuance or use an inappropriate tone for the relationship context. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adjust replies before sending, especially for formal, sensitive, or emotionally charged conversations. <br>
