## Description: <br>
Organize voice message transcripts into a structured, searchable knowledge base with tags, links, and progressive note-taking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People who capture voice messages use this skill after platform transcription to save raw transcript text, organize processed notes, tag related ideas, and link evolving content in a local ~/voice-notes knowledge base. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Raw transcripts and derived notes may contain sensitive personal or business information retained under ~/voice-notes/. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid dictating passwords, tokens, or highly sensitive information, and periodically review or remove transcript and memory entries that should not be retained. <br>
Risk: Processed notes may lose nuance or link ideas incorrectly if stream-of-consciousness transcripts are over-condensed. <br>
Mitigation: Save raw transcript text before processing, preserve user intent and examples, and use transcript references for verification before reorganizing or archiving notes. <br>
