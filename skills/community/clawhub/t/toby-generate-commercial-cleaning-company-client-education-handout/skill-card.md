This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Risk: Generated handouts may include inaccurate or unsupported claims about cleaning methods, pricing, compliance, or health and safety guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated handout before publication and verify business-specific claims against the company's approved policies and service details. <br>
