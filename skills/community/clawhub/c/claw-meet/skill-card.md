## Description: <br>
Claw Meet AI meeting assistant. Use when the user wants to prepare for meetings, generate agendas, take smart notes, extract action items, write meeting summaries, or run more effective meetings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and teams use this skill to prepare meeting agendas, structure notes, extract action items, and draft concise post-meeting summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad meeting-related triggers may invoke the skill for ordinary meeting requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants meeting support before applying Claw Meet templates or guidance. <br>
Risk: Meeting notes and transcripts can contain highly confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing highly confidential transcripts unless the assistant is approved to process that content. <br>
Risk: Generated summaries or action items can omit context, misstate decisions, or assign ownership incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Review summaries and action-item tables with meeting participants before sharing or using them as records. <br>
