## Description: <br>
Analyzes Taobao and Tmall product reviews to extract customer feedback, quality issues, selling points, sentiment, keywords, and competitive comparison insights. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce sellers, product researchers, and market analysts use this skill to summarize Taobao/Tmall review data, identify common complaints, and surface product strengths for listing optimization and competitive research. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scraping or API commands may conflict with site login requirements, rate limits, or usage restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands before running them and confirm they only access permitted public review data. <br>
Risk: Review summaries may be incomplete or misleading if source reviews are sampled, stale, filtered, or unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the analysis as decision support and verify important findings against the underlying product reviews before business use. <br>
