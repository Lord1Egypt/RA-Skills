## Description: <br>
Analyze a Twitter/X account's posting style and generate authentic posts that match their voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gravyxbt](https://clawhub.ai/user/gravyxbt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, brand operators, and agents use this skill to analyze an authorized X/Twitter account's posting patterns and draft style-matched posts from a voice profile. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can closely mimic real X/Twitter accounts without built-in consent checks. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for accounts you own, accounts you are authorized to represent, or clearly disclosed parody or assisted drafting. <br>
Risk: Analyzed tweet samples, generated prompts, and temporary voice-profile files may expose account style details or source content. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts and profile files before sharing them, and avoid storing sensitive samples longer than needed. <br>
