## Description: <br>
Helps subscription and recurring-billing businesses diagnose churn paths and build lifecycle playbooks for onboarding, renewal, cancel-save, win-back, experiments, and retention metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External business operators, lifecycle marketers, and retention teams use this skill to diagnose subscriber churn and create practical 4-8 week lifecycle plans, including onboarding, pre-renewal messaging, cancel-flow alternatives, win-back campaigns, experiments, and metrics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer-facing billing, cancellation, dunning, or win-back recommendations may create legal, transparency, or brand-fit issues if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with the relevant legal, compliance, and brand stakeholders before using them in production. <br>
