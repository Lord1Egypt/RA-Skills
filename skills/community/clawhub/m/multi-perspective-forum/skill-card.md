## Description: <br>
Run a structured multi-perspective debate in a single LLM call. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dodge1218](https://clawhub.ai/user/dodge1218) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, operators, and founders use this skill to pressure-test strategic decisions, leverage audits, and weekly retros through five defined perspectives before accepting a recommendation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recurring audit prompts may send business metrics, customer data, logs, or cost information without clear approval or scoping controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use the forum manually by default; before enabling scheduled audits, define approved data sources, redact sensitive data, and document how to disable the schedule. <br>
