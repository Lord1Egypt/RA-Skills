## Description: <br>
Transforms Chinese web-novel chapters, outlines, or scene notes into cinematic storyboard-style prose with stronger pacing, suspense, and genre-specific satisfaction beats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Writers and editors use this skill to convert Chinese web-novel chapters, outlines, or rough scene descriptions into vivid storyboard scripts. It is intended for cinematic rewriting, suspense design, pacing improvement, and genre-specific爽感 optimization rather than neutral proofreading. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is tuned for Chinese web-novel storyboarding and may over-transform text when the user only wants proofreading, summarization, translation, or neutral editing. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the requested task is genre-specific storyboarding, pacing, suspense, or爽感 optimization before applying the skill. <br>
Risk: Cinematic rewriting can change emphasis, tone, or narrative structure beyond the source material. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated scene beats against the original chapter or outline before using them as final prose. <br>
