## Description: <br>
Generates a structured curriculum with learning objectives, lesson content, presentation slides, and assessment questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Educators, course creators, and corporate trainers use this skill to draft online course curricula with lesson structure, slide content, and assessment questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated educational content may contain inaccuracies or may not match the intended audience, learning goals, or institutional requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review and revise lesson content, slides, and assessments before publication or learner use. <br>
Risk: Course material may include sensitive proprietary training information if the user supplies it to connected chat or presentation services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive proprietary material unless those services are approved for the user's environment. <br>
