## Description: <br>
Militia Military Training Assistant based on the classic Militia Military Training Manual, covering anti-aircraft defense, NBC defense, shooting, combat tactics, combat service support, and field survival. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to produce structured educational reference, prompt templates, checklists, and safety-bounded guidance related to militia-style military training topics. Use should remain limited to lawful, authorized, high-level educational or safety contexts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can steer an AI toward operational militia, firearms, and combat-planning guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to lawful, authorized, high-level educational or safety contexts, and avoid real-world weapons employment, target engagement, ambush or raid planning, patrol execution, or medical treatment decisions. <br>
Risk: Generated outputs may include safety-sensitive military, weapons, NBC, or medical guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the included safety gates, keep NBC content defensive and protective, include lawful-use disclaimers, and refer medical decisions to appropriate professionals. <br>
