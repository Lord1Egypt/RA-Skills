# Agentic Development Proposal Lifecycle

## Flow

1. Discovery recap
   - Summarize workflow, pain, systems, constraints, stakeholders, and decision criteria.

2. Recommended pilot
   - Translate discovery into a bounded first project with clear deliverables.

3. Proposal or SOW
   - Define scope, assumptions, exclusions, timeline, acceptance, pricing model, client responsibilities, and next steps.

4. Buyer approval
   - Provide executive summary or procurement-ready scope summary when needed.

5. Contract handoff
   - Convert approved scope into contract/SOW terms and acceptance criteria.

6. Invoice handoff
   - Use deposit, milestone, retainer, or change-order billing based on proposal model.

## Proposal Gates

Before finalizing, verify:

- named workflow
- buyer pain and desired outcome
- systems/tools involved
- data access expectations
- human approval points
- deliverables
- timeline
- pricing model
- assumptions and exclusions
- acceptance criteria
- client responsibilities
- next step

## Handoff to Other Skills

- Use `agentic-email-skill` before proposal for outreach, follow-up, discovery scheduling, and proposal-send emails.
- Use `agentic-contract-skill` after proposal approval for agreement generation.
- Use `agentic-invoice-skill` after commercial approval for deposits, milestones, change orders, retainers, and final balances.
- Use `agentic-certificate-skill` only when the proposal includes training or attendance certificates.
