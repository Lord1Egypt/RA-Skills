## Description: <br>
Falsification-oriented market thesis challenger for A-shares and U.S. markets. Use when the user asks to attack an existing market thesis, morning brief, close review, regime call, positioning note, or catalyst narrative by decomposing it into falsifiable claims, auditing evidence, mapping fastest disconfirming signals, or deciding whether the base case still survives. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Portfolio managers and market analysts use this skill to challenge an existing market thesis by decomposing it into testable claims, auditing news, data, and price action, and identifying the fastest falsifiers. It supports sizing, timing, and confidence decisions without replacing the user's final judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market challenges can be misleading when the supporting news, data, or price action is stale, incomplete, or unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the underlying market data sources and explicitly mark unavailable evidence before relying on the analysis. <br>
Risk: The skill produces analytical support for sizing and timing decisions, not trading authority. <br>
Mitigation: Keep final trading, position-sizing, and risk decisions under human control. <br>
