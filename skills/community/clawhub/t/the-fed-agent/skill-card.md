## Description: <br>
Analyzes public macroeconomic and central-bank policy news through integrated frameworks to assess policy stance, inflation outlook, market implications, and likely scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rafimchmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/rafimchmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to turn public macroeconomic or central-bank news URLs into structured policy, inflation, market, and scenario analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated macroeconomic data, scenario probabilities, and trading implications may be incorrect, stale, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Use public news URLs only and independently verify economic data, probabilities, and trading implications before acting on the analysis. <br>
