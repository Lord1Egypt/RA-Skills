## Description: <br>
A Simplified Chinese parenting-coach skill that helps caregivers navigate 0-15 year-old child and teen scenarios across emotion regulation, behavior guidance, learning support, social issues, adolescence communication, and parent self-support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gglinux](https://clawhub.ai/user/gglinux) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and caregivers use this skill for practical, age-adapted parenting guidance in everyday family challenges, repair conversations, routine-building, and safety-aware escalation decisions. It is intended as general support and not as a substitute for medical, mental-health, legal, or emergency advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad family or child-related discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only where parenting-focused guidance is expected, and review activation behavior against the target agent's user population. <br>
Risk: Parenting guidance could be mistaken for professional medical, mental-health, legal, or emergency advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as general support, preserve the skill's red-risk escalation boundaries, and route urgent safety, health, legal, or abuse concerns to qualified professionals or emergency services. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to respond primarily in Simplified Chinese, which may not fit all deployments. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm language requirements before deployment and add explicit localization instructions if the agent must support other languages. <br>
