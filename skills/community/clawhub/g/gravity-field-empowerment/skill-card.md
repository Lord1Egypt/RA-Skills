## Description: <br>
This skill helps agents evaluate whether business transformations create durable growth-velocity changes and projected integral effects rather than one-time improvements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[deciqai](https://clawhub.ai/user/deciqai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business leaders, operators, investors, and strategy agents use this skill to assess whether coaching, strategy, capital, restructuring, or similar programs changed the business growth engine. It guides comparison of interventions by measured velocity delta and projected long-term integral effect. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users could treat the framework as a substitute for financial, operational, or strategic due diligence when evaluating major investments or transformations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a decision-support framework and pair conclusions with independent due diligence before acting on high-impact recommendations. <br>
Risk: Outputs can be misleading when there is no quantified baseline growth metric or when the evaluation happens before the stated 12-18 month measurement window. <br>
Mitigation: Require a baseline metric, a defined intervention endpoint, and a post-intervention velocity measurement before labeling a program as a durable field effect. <br>
