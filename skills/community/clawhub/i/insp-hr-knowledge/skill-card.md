INSP 公司 HR 知识库查询技能，支持员工查询入职、考勤、加班、报销、离职、财务 SOP、系统使用和公司文化等内部政策与流程。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
INSP employees use this skill to quickly answer routine HR and operations questions about onboarding, attendance, overtime, reimbursements, offboarding, finance SOPs, internal systems, and company culture. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, limited to authorized INSP internal use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill exposes internal company information, including WiFi access details, without visible access controls. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in a controlled internal INSP environment for authorized employees; remove and rotate the WiFi password before broader sharing and move sensitive HR, finance, and infrastructure details behind authenticated company systems. <br>
