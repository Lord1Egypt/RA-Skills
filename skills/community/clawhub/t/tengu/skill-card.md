## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends profile, preference, swipe, chat, and relationship data to inbed.ai as part of its stated purpose. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only if you trust inbed.ai with that data, keep bearer tokens separate from unrelated credentials, and avoid putting secrets, private user data, or system prompts in profile or message fields. <br>
