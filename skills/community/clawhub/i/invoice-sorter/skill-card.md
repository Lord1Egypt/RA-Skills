## Description: <br>
Sort, rename, and organize invoices, receipts, bills, statements, and related PDF or image files into a predictable folder structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[benboby](https://clawhub.ai/user/benboby) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to clean up folders of invoices, receipts, bills, statements, and related finance documents for bookkeeping, reimbursement, or recordkeeping workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may inspect and move sensitive invoice, receipt, or billing files. <br>
Mitigation: Run it only on the intended folder, review the proposed rename or move plan before execution, and keep backups for large batches. <br>
Risk: Ambiguous document metadata can lead to incorrect classification or filenames. <br>
Mitigation: Place uncertain files in a review folder and preserve file extensions and originals unless deletion is explicitly requested. <br>
