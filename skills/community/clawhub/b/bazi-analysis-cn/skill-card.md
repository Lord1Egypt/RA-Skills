## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to collect birth details and run a local Bazi analysis script for traditional Chinese astrology-style readings. The skill supports Gregorian and lunar dates, optional birth hour input, gender-specific fortune-cycle calculation, and focused readings for general, children, marriage, career, or wealth topics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for birth date, optional birth time, gender, and a personal analysis topic. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing exact birth details in public or shared chats, and only provide the minimum information needed for the requested reading. <br>
Risk: The skill installs and runs a Python dependency to perform local Bazi calculations. <br>
Mitigation: Use an isolated Python virtual environment before installing the dependency or running the analysis script. <br>
Risk: The output is traditional fortune-telling analysis and may be mistaken for factual prediction or professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as cultural or entertainment-oriented guidance and avoid relying on them for consequential personal, financial, medical, or legal decisions. <br>
