## Description: <br>
Chinese-language skill for mapping enterprise, innovation, platform, talent, and carrier resources across an industry chain to identify resource clusters, gaps, and investment-attraction opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[daviddatamining](https://clawhub.ai/user/daviddatamining) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Strategy, industry development, and investment-attraction teams use this skill to generate Chinese-language industry resource distribution reports from public web research for a target industry and optional local region. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may contain incorrect, stale, or incomplete conclusions because the skill depends on public web research. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited public sources and key facts before using the report for business, investment, or policy decisions. <br>
Risk: Inputs or outputs may include confidential strategy or proprietary business data if users provide it during analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential strategy, proprietary business data, or sensitive internal plans unless they are approved for use in the agent workflow. <br>
