## Description: <br>
caseclaw helps legal teams manage filing deadlines, conflict checks, court rules, matter intake, and billable time from a terminal workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External legal professionals, firm staff, and in-house legal teams use this skill to operate caseclaw for deadline tracking, conflict checks, rules review, matter management, and time capture. The skill is oriented around terminal commands and configuration guidance for a legal workflow CLI. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill supports legal deadline, conflict, and filing workflows where incorrect outputs could materially affect a matter. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify deadlines, conflict results, and filing requirements against authoritative court rules and firm procedures before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The workflow may involve confidential legal data and optional external integrations. <br>
Mitigation: Test with non-confidential data first and confirm what Pro/Firm features, alert integrations, and API calls transmit before use with client information. <br>
Risk: Installation depends on a third-party Homebrew formula and CLI binary. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the Homebrew formula, vendor, and installed binary before running the tool in a production legal environment. <br>
