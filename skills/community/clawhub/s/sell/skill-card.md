## Use Case: <br>
People selling personal, handmade, collectible, or used items use this skill to price items, choose sales channels, draft listings, handle offers, and reduce scam exposure. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace fees, platform policies, and local selling conditions can change after publication. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current marketplace fees, platform rules, comparable sales, and local requirements before listing or accepting payment. <br>
Risk: Generated pricing, negotiation, payment, shipping, or meetup advice may not fit a specific item, buyer, or safety context. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggested listings, prices, payment methods, shipping plans, and meetup arrangements before acting. <br>
