## Description: <br>
写大众点评风格的本地生活评论。当用户发餐厅/菜品照片说"写点评"、"帮我评价"、"写个大众点评"、"生成点评文案"时使用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to draft natural, conversational Dianping-style local restaurant reviews from restaurant details or food photos. The skill asks for missing context, chooses a few salient review elements, and returns several candidate review drafts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may infer dish type, restaurant type, or price from limited user-provided details or photos. <br>
Mitigation: Verify factual details such as dishes, location, price range, and service notes before posting. <br>
Risk: Generated restaurant reviews could be used to fabricate experiences. <br>
Mitigation: Use drafts only for genuine dining experiences and edit them to reflect what actually happened. <br>
