# Pre-Sale Communication Sequence Templates

Use these templates for the 72-hour lead-up to any flash sale event. Adapt tone to brand voice.

---

## T-72 Hours — Teaser

**Email subject**: Something big is coming Wednesday…

**Body**:
> Hey [first name],
>
> We're not allowed to tell you everything yet. But we *can* say: Wednesday at 11am EST, something's going live that we've never done before.
>
> Mark your calendar. Tell a friend. Set an alarm.
>
> – [Brand Name]

**SMS** (160 char max):
> Heads up: Something BIG drops Wednesday 11am EST. You'll want to be ready. -[Brand]

**TikTok/Instagram caption**:
> POV: you're about to find out what we've been working on Wednesday. 11am. Set your alarm. #ComingSoon #[BrandName]

---

## T-24 Hours — Full Reveal

**Email subject**: Tomorrow 11am EST — [X]% off [Product Name]. 6 hours only.

**Body**:
> [First name],
>
> Tomorrow at 11am EST, [Product Name] drops to **$[price]** — that's [X]% off retail, for exactly 6 hours.
>
> We have [N] units set aside for this event. When they're gone, they're gone.
>
> [SHOP TOMORROW'S FLASH SALE →]
>
> Not set up with an account yet? Create one now so checkout is instant tomorrow.
>
> See you at 11.

**SMS**:
> [Brand]: Flash Sale TOMORROW 11am–5pm EST. [Product] drops to $[price] ([X]% off). Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

---

## T-1 Hour — Final Reminder

**Push notification title**: ⏰ 1 hour left to prepare
**Body**: [Product Name] flash sale starts at 11am. Tap to go straight to the page.

**SMS** (subscribers who opened T-24h email only):
> [Brand]: 1 HOUR. $[price] for [Product]. 11am EST. Link: [short URL]

---

## T-0 — Launch

**Email subject**: 🔴 LIVE NOW — [Product Name] at $[price]. Ends 5pm.

**Body**:
> [First name], it's live.
>
> **[Product Name] — $[price]** ([X]% off, normally $[retail])
>
> [SHOP NOW →]
>
> This price disappears at 5pm EST today. The countdown is running.

**Social caption (Instagram/TikTok)**:
> WE'RE LIVE. [Product] is $[price] until 5pm today only. Link in bio. Don't say we didn't warn you. 🔴

---

## Mid-Event Update (T+2 Hours, if stock is moving)

**Email subject**: [X] units sold in 2 hours — don't wait

**Body**:
> [First name], we're halfway through the flash sale and [X] units have already gone.
>
> If you've been thinking about it — now's the time.
>
> [SHOP BEFORE IT'S GONE →]
>
> Sale ends at 5pm EST.

---

## T-30 Min Before Close — Final Push

**Email subject**: 30 minutes left (final reminder)

**Body**:
> This is the last email. [Product Name] at $[price] ends in 30 minutes.
>
> After 5pm, it goes back to $[retail].
>
> [LAST CHANCE →]

**SMS** (send only to non-purchasers):
> FINAL CALL: [Product] flash sale ends in 30 min. $[price] → $[retail] at 5pm. [link]

---

## Post-Event — Event Closed

**Email subject**: The sale has ended (but here's what's next)

**Body**:
> [First name],
>
> Today's flash sale has officially closed. [X] units were sold in [N] hours — thank you to everyone who joined.
>
> If you missed it, you're not alone. Join our waitlist below to be the first to know when we do this again.
>
> [JOIN THE WAITLIST →]
>
> For those who got their order in — look for your shipping confirmation soon. We appreciate you.

---

## Buyer Follow-Up (Day 3)

**Email subject**: Your order + a small favor

**Body**:
> Hey [first name],
>
> Your [product] is on its way! We hope you love it.
>
> If you do, we'd love to see your photos or video reaction — tag us at [@handle] or leave a quick review.
>
> Every piece of feedback helps us make the next event even better.
>
> Thank you for being part of this.
