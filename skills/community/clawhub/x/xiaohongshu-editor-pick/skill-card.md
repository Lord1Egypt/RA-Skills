## Description: <br>
Provides a clean, structured, editorial Xiaohongshu writing style for recommendation-style post text used with text-to-image cards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and social media teams use this skill to shape Xiaohongshu post copy into concise editorial recommendations, with short sections, optional bilingual titles, Chinese numbering, and no emoji-heavy or overtly promotional language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can influence tone and formatting of generated Xiaohongshu content, including what language is excluded. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy before publishing, especially when combining this style guide with separate formatter, card-generation, or writing-pipeline skills. <br>
