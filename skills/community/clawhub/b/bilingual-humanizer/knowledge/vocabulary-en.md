# AI Vocabulary in English (Knowledge Base)

This document contains lists of terms and phrases that the engine detects as signals of artificial writing in English.

## 1. Tier 1: High Confidence (Always Flag)
*Terms that are highly distinctive of LLM outputs.*

- delve
- tapestry
- landscape (figurative)
- seamless
- multifaceted
- resonate
- testament
- underscores
- elevating
- foster
- enhance
- embark
- navigate

## 2. Tier 2: Significance Inflation
*Words used to puff up the importance of mundane topics.*

- pivotal
- crucial
- vital
- groundbreaking
- game-changer
- state-of-the-art
- transformative
- unparalleled
- visionary

## 3. Chatbot Artifacts (Communication)
*Persona-driven phrases that should be removed.*

- "I hope this helps!"
- "Let me know if you have any other questions."
- "Great observation!"
- "I understand that..."
- "As an AI model..."
- "Here is an overview..."
