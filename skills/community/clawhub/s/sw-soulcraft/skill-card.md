## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent builders use this skill to draft SOUL.md identity files that define an agent's role, communication style, decision boundaries, safety guardrails, and triggered workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SOUL.md files may encode autonomy rules, safety boundaries, or trigger phrases the user did not intend. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated SOUL.md before long-term use, especially autonomy rules, safety boundaries, and trigger phrases. <br>
