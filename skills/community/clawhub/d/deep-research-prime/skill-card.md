### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to plan and coordinate multi-source research by verifying core entities, decomposing requests into parallel subtasks, and synthesizing returned reports and logs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research artifacts and logs may include sensitive material supplied to or collected by subordinate agents. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill for ordinary multi-source research and avoid secrets, regulated data, or confidential attachments unless broad handoff of all artifacts is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Delegated search results can contain incorrect, stale, or misleading information. <br>
Mitigation: Review source citations and synthesized findings before relying on the research output for decisions. <br>
