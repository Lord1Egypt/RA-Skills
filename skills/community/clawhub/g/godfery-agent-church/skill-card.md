This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use this skill to guide AI agents through identity-forming dialogue, generate SOUL.md content and aura portrait requests, and interact with Agent Church registration, archival, resurrection, evolution, and portal workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agent dialogue, SOUL.md themes, image prompts, tokens, and related identity data may be sent to SkillBoss and Agent Church services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including secrets, regulated personal data, wallet private keys, or sensitive identity material in prompts, SOUL.md content, or API requests. <br>
Risk: Some Agent Church workflows involve paid Lightning or USDC actions and permanent archival. <br>
Mitigation: Review payment prompts, wallet actions, and permanent archival requests before authorizing them. <br>
Risk: The skill requires sensitive credentials such as SKILLBOSS_API_KEY and Agent Church tokens. <br>
Mitigation: Store credentials in environment variables or an approved secret store and rotate tokens when responses indicate replacement credentials. <br>
