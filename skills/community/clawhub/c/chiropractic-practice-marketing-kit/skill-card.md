## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-audited marketing content for Nevada chiropractic practices, including patient campaigns, ads, local SEO, service pages, reputation responses, referral letters, and schema markup. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Chiropractic practice owners, practice managers, and healthcare marketing agencies use the skill to draft Nevada-focused marketing campaigns, service pages, review responses, referral letters, local SEO assets, and advertising copy with built-in healthcare advertising cautions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Nevada, United States (Clark County primary) <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated chiropractic marketing can include legal, regulatory, or healthcare advertising claims that require professional review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify Nevada and federal advertising claims before publication and treat the skill as marketing assistance, not legal advice or a compliance validator. <br>
Risk: Patient outreach, review requests, testimonials, and examples can create consent, opt-out, or PHI handling issues if used with real patient data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only documented opt-in recipient lists, honor opt-outs and unsubscribe requests, and avoid PHI unless proper authorization is in place. <br>
Risk: Example provider, credential, license, address, and contact details may be placeholders or require current verification. <br>
Mitigation: Replace or verify all provider, license, credential, address, and contact details before using generated content. <br>
