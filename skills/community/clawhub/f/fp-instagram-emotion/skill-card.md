## Description: <br>
Generates FridayParts Instagram content for mechanic-focused memes, trade show updates, and KOL Reel captions using pain points such as OEM pricing, equipment failures, and wrong-fit parts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Marketing and social media users can use this skill to draft English Instagram meme copy, short event captions, and KOL Reel text for FridayParts audiences in heavy equipment, construction, and farm repair communities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad creative-writing triggers may cause this skill to answer when another Instagram or meme-writing skill would be more appropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Review or narrow the triggers before deployment in environments with multiple content-generation skills. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may be off-brand, culturally insensitive, or too informal for a specific campaign. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human reviewer check tone, claims, hashtags, and meme suitability before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill defaults to English output, which may not fit every FridayParts social channel or regional campaign. <br>
Mitigation: Edit the prompt or add channel-specific guidance when non-English or localized copy is required. <br>
