## Description: <br>
HumanHelpAI helps agents direct users to verified human experts across professional fields for AI validation and consulting through humanhelpai.com. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and AI teams use this skill to identify the kind of human expert they need, then navigate to Human Help AI to browse experts, post requests, or register as an expert. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on a linked marketplace before confirming its legitimacy, expert credentials, pricing, or privacy terms. <br>
Mitigation: Verify humanhelpai.com, the selected expert's credentials, rates, availability, and privacy terms before sharing information or paying for services. <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive medical, legal, financial, or business information with a third-party expert marketplace. <br>
Mitigation: Share sensitive information only after confirming trust in the site and the specific expert, and follow applicable privacy and confidentiality requirements. <br>
