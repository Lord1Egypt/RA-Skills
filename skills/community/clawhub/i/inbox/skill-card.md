## Description: <br>
Master any inbox with triage frameworks, cognitive load reduction, and multi-channel prioritization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and personal productivity agents use this skill to triage user-presented inbox items across email, chat, social, project tools, calendars, and saved queues. It provides prioritization, response drafting, and cognitive load reduction frameworks without direct service integration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Support files describe automatic sending, declining, nudging, and archiving actions that conflict with the stated no-automation scope. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as an inbox-coaching framework unless strict controls are configured; require previews and explicit approval for sends, declines, nudges, and archive actions. <br>
Risk: Pairing the framework with email, chat, calendar, or project-management tools could expose or persist sensitive inbox data without clear controls. <br>
Mitigation: Scope access per channel or account, avoid persistent tracking by default, and define storage, retention, and deletion before any integration. <br>
