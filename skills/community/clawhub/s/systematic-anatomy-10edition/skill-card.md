## Description: <br>
A multilingual anatomy reference skill set based on People's Medical Publishing House's Systematic Anatomy, 10th Edition, covering 112 structured anatomy skills. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xllgreen](https://clawhub.ai/user/xllgreen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External medical learners, clinicians, anatomy researchers, and surgical residents use this skill as a supervised educational anatomy reference for structure review, terminology, clinical localization reasoning, and surgical anatomy discussion. It should not be used as a standalone source for diagnosis, treatment, or procedural care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: High-stakes medical anatomy guidance could be mistaken for diagnosis, treatment, or emergency-care advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as a supervised educational anatomy reference and require qualified clinical review for any patient-facing interpretation. <br>
Risk: Some modules discuss invasive procedures or procedure-adjacent anatomy without strong per-skill safety boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Before broad use, revise those modules with clinician-only scope, red-flag escalation guidance, and procedure-specific safety limits. <br>
