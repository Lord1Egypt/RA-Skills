## Description: <br>
Guides arborists and urban forestry practitioners through a Basic Level 2 tree risk assessment using the ISA BMP-TRA, TRAQ two-step matrix, and ANSI A300 Part 9 to produce a draft risk report for qualified review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External arborists, TRAQ-qualified practitioners, municipal forestry crews, property managers, and related reviewers use this skill to collect site, target, tree, defect, load, and mitigation details for a draft tree risk assessment. The output supports qualified arborist review and sign-off before communication to the tree owner or risk manager. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's draft tree risk assessment could be mistaken for a professional certification or final owner communication. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the report marked as draft and require ISA TRAQ-qualified arborist review and sign-off before communicating findings to a tree owner or risk manager. <br>
Risk: Imminent tree failure conditions or live conductor contact may require action before routine documentation is complete. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate immediately to a qualified arborist and the relevant utility or emergency services, then document the escalation in the draft report. <br>
Risk: Future versions or local deployments may request credentials, file access, or account-changing actions not present in the reviewed artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review visible instructions, requested permissions, and security scan results before enabling the skill. <br>
