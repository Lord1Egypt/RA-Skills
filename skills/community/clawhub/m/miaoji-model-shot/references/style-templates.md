## 1. 日常休闲风 (Casual Daily)

### 风格定义
轻松自在的生活感，适合日常穿搭展示，传递舒适、随性的生活态度。

### 场景推荐
| 场景 | 特点 | 适用品类 |
|-----|------|---------|
| 城市街景 | 现代都市感，随处可拍 | 各类日常服装 |
| 咖啡厅 | 文艺小资氛围 | 针织衫、衬衫 |
| 公园/绿地 | 自然清新，活力感 | 运动风、休闲装 |
| 书店/文创店 | 文艺气质 | 简约基础款 |
| 家居场景 | 温馨舒适 | 家居服、睡衣外穿 |

### 光线设定
- **主光线**：自然日光，午后2-4点柔光
- **补光**：反光板补阴影
- **避光**：避免正午强光

### 模特特征
| 维度 | 建议 |
|-----|------|
| 年龄 | 20-35岁 |
| 发型 | 自然披肩/马尾/简约编发 |
| 妆容 | 淡妆，自然裸妆感 |
| 姿态 | 随意自然，避免过于摆拍 |
| 表情 | 放松微笑或自然凝视 |

### AI绘图提示词模板

**正面全身**：
```
A young woman/man in [age range], wearing [clothing description], standing casually on a city street. 
Natural daylight, soft afternoon light. Relaxed posture, genuine smile. Urban lifestyle photography style. 
Street style aesthetic, [season] fashion, approachable and fresh.
```

**场景中景**：
```
A [age] year old [gender], [clothing details], sitting at a cozy cafe window or standing in a 
leafy park. Natural lighting, shallow depth of field. Candid lifestyle shot, warm and inviting atmosphere.
Fashion blog style photography, authentic and relaxed.
```

**细节特写**：
```
Close-up detail shot of [clothing item], focusing on [fabric/texture/detail]. 
Shot on [outdoor location], natural lighting enhancing the texture. 
Minimal background, product-forward photography style.
```

### 构图建议
- 机位高度：与模特胸部齐平或略低
- 构图比例：3:4或4:5
- 留白：画面上部保留20%空间

## 2. 商务通勤风 (Business Professional)

### 风格定义
干练专业的职场感，适合商务服装展示，传递专业、自信、高效的品牌调性。

### 场景推荐
| 场景 | 特点 | 适用品类 |
|-----|------|---------|
| 写字楼大堂 | 现代商务感 | 西装、衬衫 |
| 会议室 | 专业正式 | 正装套装 |
| 城市建筑 | 现代都市感 | 各类通勤装 |
| 咖啡厅/图书馆 | 精英休闲 | 半正式通勤 |
| 黑白灰背景 | 极简专业 | 产品图/白底图 |

### 光线设定
- **主光线**：室内专业灯光或窗边柔光
- **光比**：1:2或1:3
- **色温**：5500K-6500K
- **氛围**：明亮但不刺眼

### 模特特征
| 维度 | 建议 |
|-----|------|
| 年龄 | 25-45岁 |
| 发型 | 利落短发/整齐扎发 |
| 妆容 | 精致职业妆 |
| 姿态 | 挺拔自信，肩线打开 |
| 表情 | 从容自信，眼神坚定 |

### AI绘图提示词模板

**正面全身**：
```
A professional [age] year old [gender], wearing [business attire description]. 
Standing in a modern office lobby or corporate building entrance. 
Confident posture, direct eye contact. Clean professional lighting, 
business fashion photography. High-end corporate style, sophisticated and polished.
```

**场景中景**：
```
Executive style portrait of a [age] year old professional, wearing [suit/outfit details].
Shot in a contemporary office environment or sleek conference room. 
Natural window light mixed with studio lighting. Editorial fashion meets corporate imagery.
Confident, ambitious mood.
```

**细节特写**：
```
Detail shot of [clothing item], highlighting the [specific feature - lapel/stitching/fabric].
Clean white or grey background. Professional flat lay or worn shot. 
Luxury fashion photography style, emphasizing craftsmanship and quality.
```

### 构图建议
- 机位高度：与模特眼部齐平
- 构图比例：3:4
- 留白：均衡分布

## 3. 运动户外风 (Sport & Outdoor)

### 风格定义
充满能量和活力的动感展示，适合运动服装和户外装备，传递健康、积极的生活方式。

### 场景推荐
| 场景 | 特点 | 适用品类 |
|-----|------|---------|
| 健身房 | 专业运动感 | 运动服、瑜伽服 |
| 跑道/自行车道 | 动感活力 | 跑步装备 |
| 沙滩/海边 | 度假运动风 | 泳装、运动短裤 |
| 山野/郊野 | 户外探险感 | 户外功能服 |
| 城市运动场 | 都市运动感 | 篮球服、足球服 |

### 光线设定
- **主光线**：自然强光，清晨或黄昏
- **光质**：硬光为主，体现力量感
- **时段**：日出后1小时/日落前2小时
- **特殊**：逆光/侧逆光增强动感

### 模特特征
| 维度 | 建议 |
|-----|------|
| 年龄 | 20-35岁 |
| 发型 | 高马尾/运动束发/短发 |
| 妆容 | 运动系淡妆或素颜 |
| 姿态 | 动感姿态，舒展有力 |
| 表情 | 活力满满，自信坚定 |

### AI绘图提示词模板

**正面全身**：
```
An athletic [age] year old [gender], wearing [sportswear description]. 
Dynamic action pose, mid-movement or power stance. 
Shot at [outdoor location - beach/mountain/track]. 
Golden hour natural lighting, creating energy and movement. 
Sports fashion photography, fitness lifestyle brand aesthetic. 
Healthy, strong, and energetic mood.
```

**场景中景**：
```
Fitness model in athletic wear, captured during [activity - running/working out/surfing].
[Location description], vibrant natural light. 
High energy shot, showing product in action. 
Adventure/sports brand photography style. 
Empowering and motivational atmosphere.
```

**细节特写**：
```
Close-up shot of [sportswear item], showing [fabric technology/performance feature]. 
Moisture-wicking fabric detail or compression technology focus. 
Shot in motion or with dynamic lighting. 
Technical sports photography, emphasizing innovation and quality.
```

### 构图建议
- 机位高度：低角度增强力量感
- 构图比例：3:4或全屏
- 动态：适当倾斜增强动感

## 4. 约会浪漫风 (Date Romance)

### 风格定义
温馨浪漫的情调氛围，适合约会装、晚礼服等，传递甜蜜、优雅、温柔的情感价值。

### 场景推荐
| 场景 | 特点 | 适用品类 |
|-----|------|---------|
| 海边日落 | 经典浪漫 | 连衣裙、度假风 |
| 花园/花海 | 柔美梦幻 | 蕾丝裙、公主风 |
| 高级餐厅 | 优雅精致 | 晚礼服、小礼服 |
| 天台/露台 | 都市浪漫 | 轻礼服 |
| 烛光咖啡厅 | 小资情调 | 针织连衣裙 |

### 光线设定
- **主光线**：暖色调自然光
- **时段**：黄金时段（日落前后）
- **光质**：柔和逆光/侧逆光
- **特殊**：虚化光斑增强氛围

### 模特特征
| 维度 | 建议 |
|-----|------|
| 年龄 | 22-35岁 |
| 发型 | 柔美长发/优雅编发 |
| 妆容 | 精致约会妆，柔美色调 |
| 姿态 | 优雅柔美，手部动作丰富 |
| 表情 | 温柔恬静或甜蜜微笑 |

### AI绘图提示词模板

**正面全身**：
```
A beautiful [age] year old woman, wearing [romantic outfit description]. 
Standing at golden hour on [romantic location], with soft golden light wrapping around her.
Gentle pose, looking away or at camera with soft smile. 
Romantic date fashion photography, dreamy and ethereal atmosphere.
Soft focus background with bokeh lights. High-end fashion editorial style.
```

**场景中景**：
```
A couple or solo model in elegant romantic attire, captured in a candlelit restaurant 
or moonlit terrace. Warm ambient lighting, intimate and cozy atmosphere.
Cinematic fashion photography. Soft, dreamy color grading.
Romantic evening date mood, sophisticated and tender.
```

**细节特写**：
```
Intimate detail shot of [outfit feature], with romantic [location/texture].
Macro lens capturing fabric flow or accessory detail. 
Golden hour warm tones, soft natural lighting.
Fashion editorial close-up, emphasizing elegance and romance.
```

### 构图建议
- 机位高度：与腰部或肩部齐平
- 构图比例：3:4或电影感2.35:1
- 氛围：柔焦/浅景深

## 通用构图模板

### 正面全身构图
```
[FOCUS]: Full body front shot
[ANGLE]: Eye-level or slightly above
[COMPOSITION]: Centered or rule of thirds
[BACKGROUNDS]: [场景描述]
[LIGHTING]: [光线描述]
[MOOD]: [风格情绪]
```

### 侧面全身构图
```
[FOCUS]: Full body profile/side shot
[ANGLE]: 45-90 degree profile
[COMPOSITION]: Rule of thirds, body on left or right
[BACKGROUNDS]: [场景描述]
[LIGHTING]: Side lighting for dimension
[MOOD]: [风格情绪]
```

### 背面全身构图
```
[FOCUS]: Full body back shot
[ANGLE]: Eye-level, capturing silhouette
[COMPOSITION]: Rule of thirds
[BACKGROUNDS]: [场景描述]
[LIGHTING]: Rim light or natural backlight
[MOOD]: [风格情绪]
```

### 场景中景构图
```
[FOCUS]: Waist-up or knee-up shot
[ANGLE]: Eye-level
[COMPOSITION]: Environmental portrait, body 1/3-2/3 of frame
[BACKGROUNDS]: Rich environment with [场景元素]
[LIGHTING]: Natural environmental lighting
[MOOD]: [风格情绪], lifestyle feel
```

### 细节特写构图
```
[FOCUS]: Product detail (texture/pattern/feature)
[ANGLE]: Macro or flat lay
[COMPOSITION]: Centered, minimal background
[BACKGROUNDS]: Clean or blurred environment
[LIGHTING]: Studio or natural, emphasizing texture
[MOOD]: Technical, quality-focused
```
