## Description: <br>
Chinese 28 Star Mansions (二十八星宿) expert system for personality analysis, relationship compatibility (合盘), social media content creation, and comment replies for star mansion content accounts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[luoboask](https://clawhub.ai/user/luoboask) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to look up Chinese 28 Star Mansions from lunar birthdays, draft personality and relationship compatibility readings, and produce short-form social content or comment replies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Astrology-style personality or compatibility readings may be overinterpreted as deterministic relationship advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as tendencies, avoid absolute predictions, and preserve the skill's stated reminder that star mansions do not determine outcomes. <br>
Risk: Birth details and saved content-account preferences can expose personal or business information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the minimum needed lunar month and day, avoid collecting full identifying birth details, and review saved EXTEND.md preferences before reuse or sharing. <br>
Risk: Platform-specific social content guidance may depend on a separate aura-content-strategist skill that is not part of this release artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review the separate skill before relying on it for formatting or publishing guidance. <br>
