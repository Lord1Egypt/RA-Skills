## Description: <br>
Extracts and analyzes competitors' ads from ad libraries such as Facebook and LinkedIn to identify working messaging, pain points, creative patterns, and campaign insights. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hsuyungfeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/hsuyungfeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and growth practitioners use this skill to gather public competitor ads, analyze messaging and creative patterns, and turn the findings into campaign ideas and positioning research. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Collecting competitor ads may implicate ad-library terms, intellectual property, or restricted-content boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized public sources, keep research requests narrow, respect platform terms, and use findings as inspiration rather than copying ads directly. <br>
Risk: The skill can save screenshots, reports, CSVs, and related ad research artifacts locally. <br>
Mitigation: Store archives only in approved locations and delete or relocate saved competitor-ad files when they are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Claims about what messaging or creative patterns are working are inferential and may be incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat analysis as research input, review findings before use, and validate campaign decisions with your own testing and performance data. <br>
