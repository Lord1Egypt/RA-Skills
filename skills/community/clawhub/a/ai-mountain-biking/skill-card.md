Ai Mountain Biking provides Chinese-language mountain biking guidance on bike types, gear selection, riding techniques, maintenance, safety, events, and brands. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Mountain biking beginners, trail riders, gear shoppers, and event enthusiasts use this skill for Chinese-language explanations of MTB categories, equipment choices, riding posture, trail techniques, basic maintenance, safety checks, and community context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Educational riding, safety, and equipment guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a rider's terrain, bike condition, or skill level. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as explanatory assistance, verify choices with qualified mechanics or coaches, and follow local trail rules and safety practices. <br>
Risk: The skill is text-only educational content, so outputs can still be outdated or inaccurate even though no executable code or sensitive access is indicated. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated guidance before relying on it and cross-check current event, brand, maintenance, and safety details against authoritative sources. <br>
