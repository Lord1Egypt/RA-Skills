This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, executives, product leaders, and go-to-market teams at AI startups use this skill to classify market signals, assess competitor moves, score opportunities and threats, and produce strategic intelligence briefs for revenue and positioning decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Strategic intelligence outputs may include incorrect or outdated market, competitor, technology, or regulatory claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims against trusted primary or current sources before using the output for business decisions. <br>
Risk: Inputs may contain confidential business, product, customer, or competitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential business data unless the user is comfortable with the agent using it for analysis. <br>
