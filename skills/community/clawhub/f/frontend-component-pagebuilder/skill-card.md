## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Referenced local project guidance may be stale, incomplete, or untrusted. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the referenced Weline project guidance is trusted and current before applying implementation instructions. <br>
Risk: Visitor-tracking markup or download hooks can create privacy or duplicate-reporting concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Review tracking behavior for privacy compliance and confirm hooks do not duplicate page-level event dispatch. <br>
Risk: Generated frontend components may affect global page behavior if styles or scripts are not scoped. <br>
Mitigation: Keep CSS and JavaScript scoped to the component root and validate behavior on the rendered page. <br>
