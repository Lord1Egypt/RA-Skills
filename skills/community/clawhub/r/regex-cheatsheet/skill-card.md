## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Regex patterns may not behave identically across engines or escaping contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Test selected patterns with the target language, engine, and representative input before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Some regexes can be slow or vulnerable to ReDoS on large or adversarial inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Review pattern complexity, bound input size, and prefer safer parsers for structured formats such as HTML. <br>
Risk: Validation patterns for emails, passwords, or identifiers may be incomplete for real-world policy needs. <br>
Mitigation: Use these patterns as starting points and pair them with domain-specific validation or verification workflows. <br>
