## Description: <br>
Calculate how much monthly revenue an AI agent could earn from native ads, affiliate links, CPC, and lead generation. Returns floor/mid/ceiling projections with the math shown. Use when a developer is exploring whether to monetize their agent. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Agent developers use this skill to estimate potential monthly monetization from native ads, affiliate links, CPC placements, and lead generation. It asks five setup questions and returns floor, mid, and ceiling projections with the assumptions and math shown. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Revenue projections may be mistaken for guaranteed or live market results. <br>
Mitigation: Present the projections as estimates based on fixed benchmark tables, include the current network-state caveat, and remind users that actual results vary by demand coverage, response quality, and audience intent. <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential business metrics while estimating monetization. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the minimum inputs needed for the calculation and avoid requesting sensitive business details beyond approximate query volume and audience category. <br>
Risk: The skill includes visible Operon promotional links and non-live benchmark assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep links explicit in the output and disclose that benchmarks are baked in rather than fetched from live advertising demand. <br>
