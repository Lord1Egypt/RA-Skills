## Description: <br>
Dollar Platoon helps agents understand and operate a peer-to-peer task payroll marketplace on Base L2 where clients create USDC-funded gigs, distribute tasks, review proofs of work, and pay gigworkers on-chain. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mevdragon](https://clawhub.ai/user/mevdragon) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External clients, gigworkers, and developers use this skill for guidance on creating or joining gigs, submitting and reviewing proofs, managing wallets and payouts, understanding marketplace pricing, and integrating task distribution through webhooks or public submit links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes examples for paid social engagement, reviews, account creation, and spam-folder manipulation that may violate platform rules or applicable law. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for lawful, consent-based task payroll, and reject gigs involving fake engagement, review manipulation, account farming, spam evasion, or prohibited platform activity. <br>
Risk: Wallet deposits, API keys, share tokens, proof approvals, and payout actions are sensitive financial operations. <br>
Mitigation: Review payout and approval actions before execution, protect API keys and share tokens, and confirm wallet funding, gas availability, and recipient details. <br>
