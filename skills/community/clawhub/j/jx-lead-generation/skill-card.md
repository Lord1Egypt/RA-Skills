## Description: <br>
Lead Generation finds high-intent buyers in live Twitter, Instagram, and Reddit conversations, researches a product, generates targeted search queries, and discovers people actively looking for related solutions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, growth, and marketing users use this skill to research a product, search social conversations for likely prospects, score lead fit, and draft outreach for human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product details and prospecting queries are sent to SkillBoss API Hub. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when SkillBoss is trusted for that data, and avoid sending confidential strategy, secrets, private customer data, or regulated personal data. <br>
Risk: Lead research and outreach drafts may be saved locally and could contain sensitive or regulated information. <br>
Mitigation: Review locally saved lead files before use, apply appropriate retention controls, and avoid storing private customer data unless approved. <br>
Risk: Suggested outreach may be inaccurate, inappropriate, or non-compliant if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outreach as a draft-only output and have a human review affiliation disclosure, claims, tone, and compliance before sending. <br>
