## Description: <br>
Discovers and scrapes public Facebook pages and groups by location and category using browser simulation, then exports page or group data in JSON or CSV formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ArulmozhiV](https://clawhub.ai/user/ArulmozhiV) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill to discover Facebook pages or public groups for a location and category, scrape public business or group details, and export the results for downstream review or analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill promotes stealth scraping, proxy-based ban avoidance, and account use for collecting Facebook data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with clear authorization, review the actual scraper implementation before installation, and prefer official APIs or narrowly scoped compliant workflows. <br>
Risk: The skill can collect and export contact, location, engagement, and profile or group metadata. <br>
Mitigation: Keep exported data tightly controlled, restrict access to output files, and avoid placing primary Facebook accounts or sensitive proxy credentials in the workflow. <br>
