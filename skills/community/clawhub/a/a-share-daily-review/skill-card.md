## Description: <br>
Generates Markdown templates for daily A-share market reviews across seven review styles, including sentiment, leading-stock, trend, quantitative, personal, top-trader, and complete professional formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[laojun509](https://clawhub.ai/user/laojun509) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External traders and analysts use this skill to create structured daily A-share review Markdown files for documenting market conditions, trading plans, and personal lessons. The generated content is a review framework and should not be treated as financial advice or live market data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A custom output path may overwrite an existing local file. <br>
Mitigation: Choose a new or reviewed output path before generation, especially when using --output. <br>
Risk: Generated review templates may be mistaken for financial advice or live market analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a personal review framework and fill it with independently verified market data and judgment. <br>
