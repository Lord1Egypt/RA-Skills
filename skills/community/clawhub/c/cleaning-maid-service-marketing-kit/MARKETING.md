## Key Differentiators

1. **FTC + OSHA compliance at generation time** — "eco-friendly" and "chemical-free" claims are the #1 FTC risk for cleaning companies; this skill enforces correct language automatically
2. **Airbnb host cold email system** — no competitor at any price generates structured outreach to Airbnb/VRBO hosts; this is the highest-LTV customer segment for cleaning companies
3. **LSA + schema markup** — Google Local Services Ads + LocalBusiness JSON-LD in one skill; competitors omit one or both
4. **Referral program with FTC-compliant structure** — Sparkle Referral Club uses fixed-dollar, non-conditional incentives (compliant vs. illegal "leave a review, get $X" models)
5. **Move-out cleaning legal language** — "meet your landlord's cleaning standards" vs. illegal "get your deposit back guaranteed"
