## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automated crypto trading guidance can lead to financial loss if deployed directly against live markets. <br>
Mitigation: Start with paper or sandbox trading, require explicit human review before live trading, and validate strategy behavior before deployment. <br>
Risk: Exchange API credentials used for trading bots can expose funds if over-permissioned or mishandled. <br>
Mitigation: Never provide seed phrases or unrestricted exchange keys, disable withdrawal permissions, and limit credentials to the minimum trading permissions needed. <br>
