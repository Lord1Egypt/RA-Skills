## Description: <br>
Removes AI-style writing traces to make text sound naturally written by a real author, primarily in Chinese-language contexts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nanbingxyz](https://clawhub.ai/user/nanbingxyz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content editors use this skill to rewrite Chinese-language passages so they read more naturally while preserving the original meaning and facts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The rewritten text may be used to obscure AI assistance or authorship where disclosure is expected. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before use and follow applicable authorship, disclosure, and editorial policies. <br>
Risk: Rewriting important documents can unintentionally change nuance or factual meaning. <br>
Mitigation: Compare the revised text against the source material before accepting changes. <br>
