# Pomelli AI Brand Marketing Companion

Pomelli AI Brand Marketing Companion is a ClawHub / OpenClaw skill for converting product context into brand-book inputs, campaign ideas, photoshoot directions, and AI image/video prompt packs.

Official website: [Pomelli](https://pomelli.page/)

## What this skill does

1. **Brand intake** — summarize product, audience, offer, tone, visual cues, and constraints.
2. **Campaign angle planning** — create hooks, value propositions, and channel-specific concepts.
3. **Brand-book preparation** — organize voice, colors, imagery, typography direction, and usage rules.
4. **Photoshoot direction** — define scene, props, lighting, composition, and shot list.
5. **AI image/video prompt pack** — create prompts for ad creatives, product visuals, social posts, and short videos.
6. **QA checklist** — flag mismatched claims, weak positioning, off-brand visuals, or missing CTA.

## Best inputs

- Product or service description.
- Target audience and use case.
- Brand personality: premium, playful, technical, clean, bold, warm, etc.
- Channels: landing page, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, ads, email, marketplace, or pitch deck.
- Constraints: brand colors, claims to avoid, aspect ratio, locale, budget, or creative style.
