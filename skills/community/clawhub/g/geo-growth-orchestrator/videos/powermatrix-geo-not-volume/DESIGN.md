# PowerMatrix GEO Not Volume - Visual System

## Style Prompt

A restrained enterprise AI explainer with a dark graphite canvas, warm readable typography, structured product UI panels, and precise data-flow motion. The video should feel like an executive-grade workflow diagnosis, not a loud social ad: confident, sharp, and credible, with motion used to reveal logic and causality.

## Colors

- #0F1412 - graphite background, used consistently across scenes
- #F4F0E6 - warm ivory foreground text
- #2FE09B - AI understanding / positive accent
- #F2B84B - operational warning / attention accent
- #F05D43 - visibility gap / brand missing accent
- #A8DADC - secondary data / calm interface accent

## Typography

- Primary: PingFang SC, Microsoft YaHei, Noto Sans CJK SC, system sans-serif
- Use heavy, compact Chinese headlines for key claims, lighter body text for narration and interface labels.

## Motion

- Entrances are quick and directional: 0.35-0.6s, mostly y, x, scale, and opacity.
- Workflow motion should be sequential and causal: node by node, arrow by arrow.
- Background motion stays subtle: slow grid drift, pulse lines, and data traces.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon purple-blue gradients or generic cyberpunk styling.
- Do not make the whole video a pile of identical cards.
- Do not make unsupported ranking or traffic claims.
- Do not use pure black or pure white.
- Do not let decorative UI overpower the core sentence on each beat.
