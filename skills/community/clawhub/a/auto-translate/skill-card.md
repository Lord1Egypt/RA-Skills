## Description: <br>
Auto Translate makes the assistant answer in Chinese and append English and Japanese translations when multilingual output is requested or the skill is active. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[vegetablesh](https://clawhub.ai/user/vegetablesh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill when they need responses presented first in Chinese, then translated into English and Japanese. It is useful for trilingual communication, translated answers, and consistent multilingual response formatting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Replies may become longer than expected because English and Japanese translations are appended. <br>
Mitigation: Enable the skill only for workflows that need trilingual output or where longer responses are acceptable. <br>
Risk: Sensitive text included in a response may be repeated in multiple languages. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid enabling the skill globally for conversations that require concise, single-language, or sensitive responses. <br>
