## D3-06 批量生成参数配置

### 范本：膳食纤维视频批量配置

**YAML配置示例**：
```yaml
encoding:
  codec: H.264
  profile: Main
  level: 4.1
  crf: 18
  fps: 30
  audio_codec: AAC
  audio_bitrate: 128kbps

formats:
  douyin: {width: 1080, height: 1920, bitrate: 8M, max_size: 100MB}
  bilibili: {width: 1920, height: 1080, bitrate: 12M, max_size: 500MB}
  wechat: {width: 1080, height: 1080, bitrate: 6M, max_size: 50MB}

naming:
  pattern: "{brand}_{type}_{platform}_{size}_{date}.mp4"

schedule:
  frequency: weekly
  batch_size: 5
  delivery: {format: zip, notification: dingtalk}
```
