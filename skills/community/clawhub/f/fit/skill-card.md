## Description: <br>
Fit helps agents assess fitness goals, constraints, training history, recovery, nutrition, and disruptions to produce adaptable fitness coaching guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGImodel](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGImodel) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use Fit to guide fitness coaching conversations for goals such as fat loss, muscle gain, performance, strength, recovery, and general movement quality. The skill structures assessment, training design, nutrition targets, disruption handling, and progress tracking around the user's real constraints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake injury, pain, rehabilitation, or health-adjacent fitness guidance for medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on this skill for medical diagnosis, treatment, acute injuries, severe or persistent pain, swelling, loss of function, neurological symptoms, eating disorders, or clinician-directed rehabilitation; consult a qualified clinician for those cases. <br>
Risk: Personal fitness guidance may require sensitive information such as weight, training history, sleep, energy, and injuries. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when the user is comfortable sharing fitness and health-adjacent details with the agent. <br>
