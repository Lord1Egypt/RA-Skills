## Description: <br>
Builds focused literature and citation briefings from PapersFlow for paper search, citation verification, related-paper discovery, and citation graph exploration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[papersareflowing](https://clawhub.ai/user/papersareflowing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers, developers, and analysts use this skill to find papers, verify citations, discover related work, and summarize citation neighborhoods from PapersFlow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Queries may include confidential research ideas, unpublished manuscript details, or private citation data sent to the hosted PapersFlow service. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive or unpublished research details unless the user trusts the hosted PapersFlow service and the data-sharing context. <br>
Risk: Citation graph or neighbor results can be noisy and may overstate relationships between papers. <br>
Mitigation: Label noisy graph neighborhoods clearly and distinguish references, later citations, and similar papers in the briefing. <br>
