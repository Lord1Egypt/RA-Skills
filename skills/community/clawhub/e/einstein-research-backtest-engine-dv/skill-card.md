## Description: <br>
Programmatic backtesting framework for trading strategies that runs historical-data backtests from yfinance or CSV inputs, supports built-in and custom strategies, and generates performance metrics and reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and agents use this skill to run quantitative trading strategy backtests, compare strategy performance against benchmarks, and produce JSON or Markdown performance reports for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Custom strategy files are executable Python and may run arbitrary local code. <br>
Mitigation: Use built-in strategies or reviewed CSV/Yahoo Finance inputs by default, run in a virtual environment, and review any third-party --strategy-file before execution. <br>
Risk: Backtest results may be misread as live trading recommendations or proof of future profitability. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as historical analysis only, review assumptions and transaction costs, and perform out-of-sample or walk-forward validation before relying on results. <br>
