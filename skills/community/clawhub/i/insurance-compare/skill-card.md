## Description: <br>
Compare insurance products side by side on key parameters including premium, coverage, exclusions, deductibles, and insurer ratings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill when comparing insurance plans, policy tiers, or insurers and need a side-by-side evaluation of costs, coverage, exclusions, deductibles, claim process, waiting periods, and insurer reputation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Insurance comparisons may be incomplete or become outdated, especially for premiums, exclusions, deductibles, waiting periods, and insurer ratings. <br>
Mitigation: Verify plan details directly with the insurer or a qualified advisor before buying coverage. <br>
Risk: Users may share more personal, medical, or financial information than is needed for a comparison. <br>
Mitigation: Request only the minimum details needed to compare plans and avoid collecting unnecessary sensitive information. <br>
