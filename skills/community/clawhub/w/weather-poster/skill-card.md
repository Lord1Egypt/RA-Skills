## Description: <br>
Generate weather and outfit recommendation posters as SVG for a specified city, defaulting to Wuhan, China. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ll21121](https://clawhub.ai/user/ll21121) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to create an SVG weather poster that combines current weather details with outfit suggestions for a chosen city. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Weather data and outfit recommendations may be inaccurate, stale, or unavailable because the skill depends on current weather lookup behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Review the weather details and recommendations before relying on or sharing the generated poster. <br>
