## Description: <br>
Guides an agent to help users diagnose curiosity decline, distinguish reversible stagnation from terminal domain fit, and design a 30-day reactivation experiment or transition plan. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[deciqai](https://clawhub.ai/user/deciqai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, coaches, and agents use this skill when a person reports learning stagnation, professional autopilot, or loss of curiosity in an established domain. It produces a structured curiosity audit, reversible-or-terminal diagnosis, and either a 30-day reactivation experiment or domain transition plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat reflective coaching about burnout, relevance, or leaving a domain as medical, mental-health, or career advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as reflective coaching, encourage human judgment, and avoid presenting the skill as a substitute for professional medical, mental-health, or career guidance. <br>
Risk: The skill may recommend a domain transition when stagnation is caused by burnout, structural stress, or another condition outside the curiosity framework. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the documented exclusion checks first, especially burnout, capability gaps, financial stress, and bad environment signals, before using the reactivation or transition process. <br>
