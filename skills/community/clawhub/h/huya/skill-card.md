## Description: <br>
Provides guidance for organizing public Huya livestream categories, event information, creator rules, help center content, and safety notices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codenova58](https://clawhub.ai/user/codenova58) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to summarize and structure public Huya livestreaming pages, events, creator rules, help content, and safety guidance. It is intended for public information organization, not account access, private data, automation abuse, or policy evasion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Huya livestream activities, rules, and help pages can change frequently, so summaries may become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify timestamps and source links against the current public Huya page before relying on the output. <br>
