## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to inspect, build, debug, refactor, review, or generate HarmonyOS and OpenHarmony projects that use ArkTS, ArkUI, DevEco Studio, Stage model apps, build tooling, permissions, packaging, and signing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Suggested dependency, build, signing, or device commands could affect a local project, signing materials, or connected devices. <br>
Mitigation: Review each proposed command and its target project before execution, especially commands involving ohpm, hvigorw, hdc, certificates, permissions, or release signing. <br>
Risk: HarmonyOS permission or signing guidance can lead to overbroad app privileges or mishandled certificate files if applied without project review. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm requested permissions and signing configuration against the app's actual requirements, and keep certificate and key material protected outside public source control. <br>
