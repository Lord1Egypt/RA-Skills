## L01: Pathogenesis of Sensitive Skin

**File**: `C:\Users\19699\Desktop\论文依据\特护文献1\010_特护霜2.0_2024上市支持文献\7【基础研究】（核心）敏感性皮肤的发病机制.pdf`

**Authors**: Shu Hong, He Li (First Affiliated Hospital of Kunming Medical University / Yunnan Dermatology Hospital)

**Key Findings**:
> The pathogenesis of sensitive skin primarily involves:
> 1. **Barrier Dysfunction**
>    - Reduced CLDN5 tight junction protein expression → easier penetration by external irritants
>    - Decreased ceramide content in the epidermis → incomplete stratum corneum structure
> 2. **Neural Dysregulation**
>    - TRPV-1 receptor overexpression → positively correlated with sensitivity severity
>    - Lowered electrical threshold of Aδ and C fibers → sensory hypersensitivity
> 3. **Vascular Hyperreactivity**
>    - Superficial microvasculature closer to the epidermis → easily triggered redness and flushing
> 4. **Immune-Inflammatory Response**
>    - Penetration of chemical, environmental, and microbial agents → triggers sensitivity reactions

**Citation Format**:
