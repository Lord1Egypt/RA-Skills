## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to search for transmission service providers, check availability, and create bookings through Lokuli. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External booking actions may share contact details and create appointments through Lokuli. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the provider, service, time, contact details, price, and cancellation terms before allowing create_booking to run. <br>
