## Description: <br>
Generates high-signal senior-level backend interview questions by topic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cngvc](https://clawhub.ai/user/cngvc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineering leaders, and interviewers use this skill to generate one senior-level backend interview question for a selected topic, with a concise note about what the question evaluates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated interview questions may be incomplete, misleading, or poorly matched to the role or seniority level. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated questions for quality, relevance, and expected evaluation criteria before using them in an interview process. <br>
