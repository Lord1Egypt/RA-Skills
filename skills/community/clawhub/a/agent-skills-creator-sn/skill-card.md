## Description: <br>
Agent-Skills-Creator-SN helps users create or refactor OpenClaw skills through a structured six-step workflow covering material collection, safety review, requirements clarification, draft generation, final review, and self-test validation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CCConan](https://clawhub.ai/user/CCConan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and skill authors use this skill to design, revise, and review OpenClaw skills using a repeatable workflow. It is especially suited for turning natural-language requirements or existing skill material into a SKILL.md draft with safety checks and deployment-oriented output options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The YAML frontmatter may require verification before installation. <br>
Mitigation: Check and fix the frontmatter against OpenClaw requirements before publishing or installing the skill. <br>
Risk: SN verification marks could be mistaken for an independent security guarantee. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the marks as workflow labels and perform a separate security review before deployment. <br>
Risk: Broad triggers and instructions to search or review external material can expose users to untrusted content. <br>
Mitigation: Use official OpenClaw documentation or trusted user-provided URLs when gathering source material. <br>
