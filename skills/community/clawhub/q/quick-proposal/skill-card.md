## Description: <br>
Generates project proposal drafts with requirements analysis, timeline and cost estimates, risk assessment, and reusable proposal sections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers, agencies, and developers use this skill to draft project proposals from client requirements, including solution scope, schedule, budget, payment terms, team roles, risks, and next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals may include inaccurate prices, schedules, payment terms, experience claims, maintenance promises, or client-specific commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Review and customize each proposal before sending it to a client. <br>
