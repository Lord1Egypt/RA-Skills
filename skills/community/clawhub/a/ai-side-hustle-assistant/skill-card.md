## Description: <br>
Chinese-language assistant that helps users explore AI side-hustle paths, compare monetization platforms, estimate effort and income potential, and create weekly action plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[marson2016](https://clawhub.ai/user/marson2016) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to evaluate AI side-hustle options such as skill publishing, content creation, tool packaging, and automation services. It provides practical recommendations, platform comparisons, risk notes, and week-by-week action plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Income estimates and platform recommendations may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a user's location, skills, or market conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the guidance as planning support and verify estimates, platform terms, and legal or tax implications independently before acting. <br>
Risk: The platform reference includes authentication and publish commands that mention tokens. <br>
Mitigation: Enter tokens only in official tools and environments when intentionally authenticating or publishing. <br>
