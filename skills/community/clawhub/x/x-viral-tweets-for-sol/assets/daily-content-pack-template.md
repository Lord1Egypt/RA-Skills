Exact output shape for `/daily-content-pack`. Fill, ship. No deviations to structure unless user requests.

## Rules for generation

1. **Cover at least 4 different hook patterns** across the 5 viral ideas (see viral-hooks.md). Do not produce 5 numeric-specificity hooks.

2. **Tones across the 5 viral ideas must vary.** Don't ship 5 founder-tone posts. Mix.

3. **Reply bait must actually filter.** Vague "thoughts?" prompts are forbidden. Each reply bait should be answerable in <15 words by anyone qualified to reply.

4. **Hot take and controversial opinion must be different.** Hot take = sharp opinion about the ecosystem. Controversial = an opinion the user would defend in a room.

5. **Meme concepts must use 3 different formats.** Three wojaks isn't variety.

6. **Narrative forecast requires fresh data.** Web-search before generating unless the user is offline or explicitly says "from memory."

7. **Account context check.** If positioning isn't in memory and the conversation hasn't established it, ask once before generating the pack. Don't generate generic packs — they're worse than no pack.
