## Description: <br>
Assist recruiters by generating clear, structured job descriptions with responsibilities, qualifications, and key skills based on job title and requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Recruiters, hiring managers, and HR teams use this skill to draft professional job descriptions from a role title and recruitment requirements. It structures the response into responsibilities, qualifications, and key skills. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad hiring terms and is intended for drafting job descriptions, not adjacent hiring decisions or legal hiring policy. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for JD drafting and choose a more specific skill or clarify the request for interview planning, candidate evaluation, or legal policy tasks. <br>
Risk: Generated job descriptions can include incorrect, incomplete, or unsuitable role requirements if the input is vague. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated responsibilities, qualifications, and key skills before publishing or using them in a hiring workflow. <br>
