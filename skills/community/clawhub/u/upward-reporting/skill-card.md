## Description: <br>
Routes Chinese-language workplace reporting requests to planning, postmortem, review, kickoff, and OKR writing workflows, with shared guidance for audience adaptation, style, and oral presentation preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sopaco](https://clawhub.ai/user/sopaco) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and managers use this skill to clarify the audience and scenario for Chinese workplace reports, then draft or structure planning reports, incident postmortems, performance reviews, cross-team kickoff documents, OKRs, and oral reporting preparation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generic writing requests may route into this skill before the workplace scenario, audience, or reporting purpose is clear. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify the intended reader, occasion, and reader expectations before applying a sub-skill or template. <br>
Risk: Workplace reports can contain confidential business, incident, performance, or planning information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential details unless they are approved for use in the active agent environment. <br>
