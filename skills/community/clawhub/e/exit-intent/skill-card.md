## Description: <br>
Design exit-intent popup strategies for ecommerce sites including trigger rules, offer types, copy variants, and A/B testing plans that recover leaving visitors without degrading UX or brand perception. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators, marketers, and implementation teams use this skill to design exit-intent popup strategies that balance recovery offers, trigger timing, mobile behavior, accessibility, performance, and A/B testing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Exit-intent recommendations may affect cookie/localStorage use, analytics tracking, and visitor consent requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review consent, privacy, and analytics requirements before implementing tracking, frequency caps, or audience segmentation. <br>
Risk: Popup, discount, or storefront changes can affect checkout flow, accessibility, brand trust, and commercial margin. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before applying live-store changes, keep popups dismissible and accessible, exclude checkout, and validate mobile behavior before launch. <br>
