## Description: <br>
Map ecommerce keywords to a 12-month demand curve and produce a month-by-month plan for listings, ads, and content timing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators, marketers, and content planners use this skill to classify keywords by seasonality, identify early-mover windows, and plan listing, advertising, content, and inventory actions across a 12-month calendar. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Seasonal keyword recommendations can be wrong if the market, hemisphere, or current demand data is missing or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the target region and validate the plan against current keyword, sales, or platform data before using it for ad budget or inventory decisions. <br>
Risk: Assumption-based demand peaks may lead to mistimed listing, content, or advertising work. <br>
Mitigation: Label each curve as evidenced or assumed and prioritize multi-year search data, platform performance data, and seller history where available. <br>
