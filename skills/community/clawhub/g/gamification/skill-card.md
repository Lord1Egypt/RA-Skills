## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language game rule designer skill that turns a topic or workflow into gamification mechanisms, goal-rule-feedback loops, participation drivers, and ready-to-use gamification plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to design gamification approaches for products, teams, processes, or behavior goals. It helps structure game mechanisms, objectives, rules, feedback loops, participation drivers, validation steps, optimization, and delivery reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms may activate the skill for loosely related gamification requests. <br>
Mitigation: Review activation terms before deployment in environments that require explicit skill invocation. <br>
Risk: Generated gamification plans may include examples, claims, or data points that need verification before operational use. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the skill's factual-discipline rule: use verified facts and mark uncertain examples, cases, or data as requiring verification. <br>
