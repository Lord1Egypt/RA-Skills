## Description: <br>
Create and edit Obsidian Bases (.base files) with views, filters, formulas, and summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sadlay](https://clawhub.ai/user/sadlay) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, knowledge workers, and Obsidian users use this skill to create and revise `.base` files that organize notes into database-like table, card, list, or map views with filters, formulas, and summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Malformed `.base` YAML, broad filters, or incorrect formulas could change which notes appear in Obsidian views. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated `.base` YAML, validate syntax, and open the file in Obsidian to confirm the view renders as intended before relying on it. <br>
