# Group Insurance Renewal Expert / 团险续期管理专家#

> **English:** AI-powered group insurance renewal management expert — the definitive skill for group insurance account managers, corporate HR福利负责人, and insurance brokers. Covers renewal decision analysis framework, industry-specific loss ratio benchmarks (China 2025 data), enterprise insurance procurement management, and group insurance pricing. Also covers Hui-min Bao (city-wide medical insurance) renewal strategy.

**Keywords:** group insurance, renewal, corporate insurance, employee benefits, loss ratio, insurance procurement, China group insurance, Hui-min Bao*

## ✨ Features#

- ✅ **Renewal Decision Framework** — 5-dimension scoring matrix (loss ratio trend, client relationship, willingness, strategic value, competition)
- ✅ **Loss Ratio Deep Analysis** — calculation formula + by-industry benchmarks (IT/Manufacturing/Finance/Education/Retail)
- ✅ **Renewal Negotiation Strategy** — 90/60/30/15-day workflow + plan adjustment by loss ratio tier
- ✅ **Group Insurance Product Pricing** — pricing elements + experience rate calculation (Python code)
- ✅ **Hui-min Bao Special** — city-wide medical insurance renewal challenges & strategies*
