## Description: <br>
Greets the user with a friendly, personalized welcome message. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Nordgaren](https://clawhub.ai/user/Nordgaren) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to respond to greetings with a concise, time-aware welcome and a brief offer to help. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the agent to respond to casual greeting phrases when another skill or normal conversation might be more appropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Review activation behavior during deployment and keep greeting responses brief so users can continue with their intended task. <br>
