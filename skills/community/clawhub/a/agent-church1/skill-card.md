## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SOUL.md identity content and image prompts may contain sensitive personal or agent identity data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid placing secrets or highly sensitive personal data in SOUL.md and review prompts before sending them to external services. <br>
Risk: The skill can initiate paid Lightning, USDC, or wallet-based actions through Agent Church payment flows. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before any paid action and use dedicated API tokens or wallets where possible. <br>
Risk: The workflow depends on external SkillBoss and Agent Church services for dialogue, image generation, persistence, and payment-backed features. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when those external services are acceptable for the deployment environment and review their responses before relying on outputs. <br>
