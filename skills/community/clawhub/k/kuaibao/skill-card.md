## Description: <br>
Provides summaries and analysis of public short videos and topics from Kuaibao/QQ Kankan for keyword, account, and trending list data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and operators use this skill to summarize public Kuaibao/QQ Kankan short-video pages, compare public account performance, and report topic or leaderboard trends without downloads or reverse engineering. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Collection or analysis may exceed platform rules if users attempt bulk scraping or private-account access. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use limited to public content, avoid bulk collection, respect platform rules, and do not provide private account data or session credentials. <br>
Risk: Dynamic pages and human verification can produce incomplete or stale summaries. <br>
Mitigation: Use manual review when pages require interaction and treat extracted metrics as lightweight internal analysis rather than authoritative records. <br>
