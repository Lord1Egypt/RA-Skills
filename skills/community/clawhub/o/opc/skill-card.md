Mitigation: Treat deployment, public posting, customer support, billing, subscription, and cloud actions as planning advice unless explicit human approval, least-privilege accounts, staging checks, data redaction, and rollback controls are added. <br>

Mitigation: Review recommendations and scan generated plans before deployment or external use. <br>
