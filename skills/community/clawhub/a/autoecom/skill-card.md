## Description: <br>
Turn any ecommerce store into daily Instagram and TikTok product carousels by extracting brand signals, selecting bestsellers, generating product slide images, composing branded carousel assets, and publishing through Upload-Post after approval. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators and their agents use this skill to create a recurring social-commerce content workflow: identify brand style, choose a product, generate carousel slides, review the content, publish to Instagram and TikTok, and learn from post metrics over time. <br>
