### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill presents as an embedding-search helper but can route an agent through a broad third-party, pay-as-you-go API gateway. <br>
Mitigation: Review the SkillBoss setup guide before execution, use a restricted or low-limit key when available, and set billing alerts. <br>
Risk: Prompts, documents, or other user data may be sent to SkillBoss or downstream providers through the configured API key. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending sensitive data unless the relevant privacy, retention, and provider terms meet the deployment requirements. <br>
