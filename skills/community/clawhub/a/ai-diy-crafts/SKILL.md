# AI DIY Crafts

Handmade project guides covering paper crafts, textile arts, woodworking, jewelry making, and upcycling for crafters of all skill levels.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Paper Crafts
- Origami: crane, lotus, modular, tessellation, complex models
- Card making: pop-up, quilling, stamping, embossing
- Scrapbooking: layouts, journaling, photo preservation
- Paper flowers, garlands, pinatas, lanterns

### 2. Textile Arts
- Sewing: machine basics, hand stitching, pattern reading
- Embroidery: stitches guide, hoop art, clothing embellishment
- Knitting and crochet: cast on, basic stitches, pattern reading
- Macrame, weaving, tie-dye, fabric painting

### 3. Jewelry Making
- Beading: stringing, wire wrapping, loom, peyote stitch
- Metal: basic soldering, hammering, stamping, patina
- Polymer clay: conditioning, caning, mokume gane
- Resin: casting, inclusions, UV resin jewelry

### 4. Home Decor DIY
- Candle making: container, pillar, soy vs beeswax
- Soap making: cold process, melt and pour, additives
- Furniture refinishing, chalk painting, decoupage
- Terrariums, succulent arrangements, pressed flower art

### 5. Upcycling & Sustainability
- Clothing refashion: thrift flip, visible mending
- Pallet furniture, jar and bottle repurposing
- Zero-waste crafting: scrap fabric projects
- Material sourcing: thrift stores, estate sales, swaps
