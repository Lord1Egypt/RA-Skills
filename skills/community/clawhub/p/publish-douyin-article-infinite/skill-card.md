## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through drafting a Douyin creator article in OpenClaw, including the title, summary, body, cover image, topics, and music, then saves the result as a draft for user review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[infiniteask](https://clawhub.ai/user/infiniteask) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, social media operators, and agents acting for them use this skill to prepare a Douyin image-and-text article draft with required publishing fields. The workflow leaves final review and publishing to the user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, subject to Douyin account availability and local platform terms. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent operates a Douyin creator page and may draft public-facing content for a personal, business, or brand account. <br>
Mitigation: Review the saved draft manually before publishing, especially for business or brand accounts. <br>
Risk: Browser automation can interact with account state, uploads, topics, cover generation, and music selection. <br>
Mitigation: Use the OpenClaw browser profile described by the skill and keep final publishing as a user-controlled action. <br>
