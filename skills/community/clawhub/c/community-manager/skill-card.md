## Description: <br>
Manage online communities with engagement strategies, content planning, and audience growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Community teams and operators use this skill to plan engagement, content calendars, onboarding, moderation guidelines, and health metrics for Discord, Slack, Telegram, or forum communities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local community notes may contain private contacts, unnecessary PII, or sensitive moderation details if the user records them. <br>
Mitigation: Keep secrets and unnecessary personal data out of ~/community-manager/ and review stored notes before relying on or sharing them. <br>
Risk: Community plans, metrics, and moderation guidance can become stale or mismatched to a platform's norms. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans and local memory before posting, moderating, or making community decisions. <br>
