## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto bridge, fee, withdrawal, and protocol-status guidance can become outdated or differ by transaction context. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify bridge URLs, network settings, fees, withdrawal timing, and current protocol status before moving funds. <br>
Risk: Third-party bridges can add liquidity, counterparty, and fee risk compared with the native bridge. <br>
Mitigation: Use the official bridge for large transfers when appropriate, and review third-party bridge fees and risk disclosures before transferring funds. <br>
Risk: Wrong-network wallet configuration can cause confusing balances, failed transactions, or complex recovery workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the Arbitrum One Chain ID, RPC endpoint, wallet network, and available ETH for gas before sending transactions. <br>
