## Description: <br>
Provides an advisory banking credit risk management workflow covering industry rule generation, large-exposure management, credit policy analysis, risk collaboration, risk information extraction, post-loan monitoring, post-loan management, and VLM validation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Bank credit risk employees and reviewers use this skill to structure enterprise credit risk materials, generate risk review frameworks, analyze credit policy context, monitor post-loan risk, and prepare advisory risk reports that require qualified human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive banking, customer, and credit-report data may be processed without sufficient operational controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in an approved banking environment with explicit permission, documented redaction rules, access controls, encryption, and a retention policy. <br>
Risk: The manifest presents an empty tool list and no-storage posture while the workflow describes file inputs, API or web-search sources, scripts, and long-term audit logs. <br>
Mitigation: Require a corrected manifest and documented file, API, search, audit-log, storage, and retention permissions before operational use. <br>
Risk: Advisory outputs could be mistaken for lending decisions or professional financial, legal, or insurance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Require qualified human review and keep outputs clearly scoped as reference material, not approval decisions or professional advice. <br>
