## Description: <br>
Create multi-criteria comparison charts using traffic lights or Harvey balls for option evaluation, competitive comparison, and executive dashboards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[linuszz](https://clawhub.ai/user/linuszz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users, analysts, and decision makers use this skill to compare options across weighted criteria, visualize status or trade-offs, and summarize recommendations in a concise chart. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Criteria, weights, ratings, or source data can be subjective or incomplete, leading to misleading recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review the criteria, weights, ratings, and supporting data before relying on the chart for business decisions. <br>
