## Description: <br>
AI 应用工程师专属的每日资讯日报技能，收集 AI 行业动态（AI 龙头公司官方博客/权威来源），按六大板块组织内容，面向 AI 应用开发工程师，重点关注能力落地和技术实用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[robusth](https://clawhub.ai/user/robusth) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
AI application engineers and developers use this skill to generate a Chinese daily briefing on AI industry updates, model capability boundaries, production feedback, engineering patterns, developer tools, safety and compliance, and practical learning directions. The skill emphasizes source-linked items from official company blogs, authoritative technology media, technical communities, and arXiv. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can activate on broad daily-report requests and produce an AI news report when the user intended a different kind of report. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke it with explicit wording such as “AI 应用工程师日报” or “AI 日报” when precise activation matters. <br>
Risk: Daily news summaries may include stale, incomplete, or source-dependent information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep each item linked to its source URL and review the cited source before acting on time-sensitive or high-impact information. <br>
