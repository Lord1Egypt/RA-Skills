## Description: <br>
萤火虫空压机官方节能诊断助手，帮助用户评估空压机系统节能潜力、计算变频改造 ROI，并生成能效审计报告。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and industrial energy teams use this skill to collect compressor operating data, estimate electricity savings, compare retrofit options, calculate payback, and prepare Markdown or branded HTML audit reports for customer proposals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global; built-in benchmarks, GB 19153 references, pricing assumptions, and contact details are China-focused. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review found no unsafe behavior, but noted that the skill may force Chinese responses for users who need another language. <br>
Mitigation: Use or publish wording that respects the user's language preference when multilingual support is required. <br>
Risk: Energy savings, ROI, and emissions outputs are estimates that can be misleading if inputs are incomplete or site conditions differ from assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated recommendations with qualified personnel and confirm important decisions with on-site measurements before relying on them. <br>
