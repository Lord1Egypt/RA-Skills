## Description: <br>
Transforms comments, DMs, and FAQ threads into prioritized content ideas and script-ready angles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content strategists, creators, and marketing teams use this skill to turn audience comments, DMs, objections, and FAQ snippets into ranked intent clusters and script-ready content ideas. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private comments or DMs may contain personal or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Remove personal details from source comments and DMs before using them with the skill. <br>
Risk: Generated content ideas may carry incorrect priorities or publish-ready claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review prioritized clusters, hooks, and script angles before storing them in a backlog or publishing derived content. <br>
Risk: Commercial licensing is unclear because server metadata and artifact text conflict. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the authoritative license and any separate commercial-use permission before commercial deployment. <br>
