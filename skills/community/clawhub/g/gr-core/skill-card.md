## Description: <br>
Gr Core routes overseas growth questions to the appropriate Gingiris sub-skill for SEO, blogging, Product Hunt launches, OSS marketing, B2B growth, ASO, user interviews, competitor analysis, social distribution, AI citation, or backlink work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, marketers, and growth teams use this skill to classify a growth problem and choose one focused Gingiris playbook instead of manually selecting among sub-skills. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may guide an agent toward shared API-key locations such as environment variables, local files, or MEMORY.md. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit per-task approval before reading credential locations, and configure each downstream sub-skill with only the keys it needs. <br>
