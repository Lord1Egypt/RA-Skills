## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Incorrect network selection, contract addresses, bridge URLs, or transaction details can cause irreversible loss of funds. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify the network, contract address, bridge URL, and full transaction details before moving funds. <br>
Risk: Crypto ecosystem guidance can become stale as networks, bridges, validators, and token contracts change. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check current official sources and block explorer data before acting on the guidance. <br>
Risk: Unknown airdrops, fake sites, malicious approvals, and unaudited tokens may expose users to scams or asset loss. <br>
Mitigation: Use trusted URLs, verify contracts, avoid unknown tokens, revoke unused approvals, and use hardware wallets for large balances. <br>
