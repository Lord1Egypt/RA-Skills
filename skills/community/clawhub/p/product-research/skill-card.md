## Description: <br>
Identifies e-commerce product opportunities by combining social trend signals, regional demand checks, marketplace competition data, sourcing checks, and store-draft preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[h4gen](https://clawhub.ai/user/h4gen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and e-commerce operators use this skill to scout product opportunities, validate demand and competition signals, compare sourcing options, and prepare store drafts and ad scripts with explicit data gates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use API keys and connected marketplace or store accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when comfortable with the required credentials, verify missing-key reports, and confirm account connections before deployment. <br>
Risk: User-provided affiliate or coupon links may appear in publishable output. <br>
Mitigation: Verify any affiliate or coupon URLs before sharing or publishing outputs. <br>
Risk: Market, supplier, or deployment recommendations can be incomplete when required providers or connections are unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's data gates, partial-status reporting, and manual review before committing ad spend or publishing store drafts. <br>
