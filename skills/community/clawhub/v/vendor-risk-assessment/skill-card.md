## Description: <br>
Assesses third-party vendor risk for AI and SaaS products across security posture, data handling, compliance, financial stability, operational resilience, and contractual terms, then produces a scored report with mitigation recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Procurement, security, compliance, and vendor management teams use this skill to evaluate SaaS or AI vendors during onboarding, annual reviews, partnerships, acquisitions, or build-vs-buy decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Public web research or vendor-provided material may be incomplete, outdated, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited sources and corroborate high-impact findings before using the assessment in procurement decisions. <br>
Risk: Vendor documents may contain confidential or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only documents intended for agent analysis and follow internal data-handling requirements. <br>
Risk: The skill supports decision-making but does not replace legal, security, or procurement approval. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified staff review final go/no-go decisions and mitigation requirements before acting on them. <br>
