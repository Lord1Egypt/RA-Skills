## Description: <br>
Practical end-of-life preparation and philosophical mortality awareness for users creating wills, advance directives, funeral preferences, digital legacy plans, and family conversation scripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to organize practical end-of-life planning, including advance directives, wills, life insurance review, digital legacy, funeral preferences, and a death file. It also helps users prepare direct conversations with family while acknowledging grief, cultural context, and jurisdiction-specific requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with local verification required for legal, medical, tax, estate, organ donation, and funeral requirements. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: End-of-life legal, medical, tax, estate, organ donation, and funeral requirements vary by jurisdiction and may change. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as planning guidance, verify current local forms and rules, and consult qualified legal, medical, tax, or financial professionals for binding decisions. <br>
Risk: Digital legacy planning can expose passwords, wallet keys, seed phrases, account recovery data, or other private account details. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste raw secrets into chat; store credentials in a password manager or another secure location and document access procedures for a trusted person. <br>
Risk: The topic can be harmful or counterproductive for users in acute grief, persistent anxiety, panic, depression, or crisis. <br>
Mitigation: Defer paperwork when a user is not ready, keep responses supportive, and direct crisis cases to appropriate emergency or mental health support. <br>
