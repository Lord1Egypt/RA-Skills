## Description: <br>
Design user onboarding flows that maximize activation and time-to-value. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, growth, and design teams use this skill to plan concise onboarding flows, define activation, measure funnel drop-off, structure signup and segmentation, and improve early retention. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Onboarding recommendations involve signup fields, emails, retention metrics, and segmentation that can affect privacy, consent, and user communications. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the recommendations according to the adopting organization's privacy, consent, and user communication policies. <br>
Risk: Activation and funnel advice can be misleading if teams use an unvalidated activation definition or incomplete metrics. <br>
Mitigation: Define activation before implementation and review funnel and drop-off metrics before changing onboarding flows. <br>
