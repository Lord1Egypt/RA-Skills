## Description: <br>
Pitch development and presentation coaching for founders and salespeople. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIstack](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIstack) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, salespeople, and business teams use this skill to shape value propositions, tailor investor or customer pitches, prepare for objections, draft follow-up communications, and receive delivery coaching while retaining human judgment for business decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated investor-facing pitch content may include fabricated or unverified traction, market, team, or fundraising claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated pitches carefully and replace traction, market, team, and fundraising claims with verified facts before use. <br>
Risk: Pitch foundations, objections, and meeting follow-up metadata are kept on local disk. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if local storage under ~/.openclaw/workspace/memory/pitch is acceptable, and periodically review or delete those files. <br>
