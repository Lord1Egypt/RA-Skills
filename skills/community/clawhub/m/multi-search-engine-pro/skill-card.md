## Description: <br>
Build auditable search URLs across Chinese and global engines with region/language filters, advanced operators, time scopes, privacy-first options, compare mode, and no API keys. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[52YuanChangXing](https://clawhub.ai/user/52YuanChangXing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and agents use this skill to compose auditable search URLs, choose engines by region or privacy posture, and compare search entry points before opening result pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Opening a generated search URL shares the query text with the selected search provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid putting secrets, private identifiers, customer data, or confidential research terms into generated queries. <br>
