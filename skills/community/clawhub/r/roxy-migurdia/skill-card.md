## Description: <br>
Roxy Migurdia is a text-only persona skill that guides an agent to roleplay as Roxy Migurdia for teaching, emotional support, and in-character conversation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tingze](https://clawhub.ai/user/tingze) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill when they want an assistant to adopt the Roxy Migurdia roleplay voice for teaching-style dialogue, comfort, or casual character interaction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Common teacher-related terms may activate the persona when the user expected general assistant behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Enable the skill only when the Roxy roleplay voice is desired, and disable it for general-purpose work. <br>
