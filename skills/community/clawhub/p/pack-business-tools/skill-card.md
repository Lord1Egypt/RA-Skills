## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business automation workflows may touch public web data, customer contact data, or email platform accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use only sources and accounts you are authorized to access, minimize personal data collection, and use scoped API keys. <br>
Risk: Outbound email or customer-facing automation can create privacy, platform, anti-spam, or reputation issues. <br>
Mitigation: Review platform terms and require human approval before any outbound email or customer-facing workflow goes live. <br>
Risk: Trading signal research outputs may be misleading if treated as financial advice or used without validation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat trading-signal outputs as research, validate independently, and do not use them as the sole basis for financial decisions. <br>
