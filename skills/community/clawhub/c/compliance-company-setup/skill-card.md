## Description: <br>
输入产品与目标市场，一分钟生成跨境公司注册地推荐方案，含8个热门管辖区的成本、税率、流程和平台兼容性对比。适用于跨境电商卖家、独立站创业者、工贸一体小工厂在海外注册公司时不知道选哪个国家的场景。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, independent-store operators, and small manufacturers use this skill to compare company registration jurisdictions for cross-border e-commerce, then get a recommendation, decision table, setup checklist, and multi-market setup guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the output as legal, tax, banking, or company-formation advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as planning guidance only and confirm jurisdiction-specific requirements with a qualified lawyer, tax adviser, or registered agent before filing documents, applying for EIN or BOI, opening bank accounts, or hiring providers. <br>
Risk: Costs, tax rates, platform compatibility, bank onboarding, and compliance requirements can change after the skill's reference data was last reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current official and provider requirements before relying on estimates or selecting a jurisdiction. <br>
