## Description: <br>
Creative Genius guides an agent through structured creative-thinking workflows that combine cross-domain connection, mental simulation, simplification, prolific iteration, and reflection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to give an AI agent a repeatable process for ideation, creative problem solving, overcoming blocks, and reflecting on completed creative work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can shape an agent's response style and creative workflow even though it does not execute code. <br>
Mitigation: Review the SKILL.md before installation to confirm that its thinking style is appropriate for the intended agent and audience. <br>
Risk: Creative guidance may produce speculative, overly broad, or poorly grounded ideas if used without human judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Have users review generated ideas for factual accuracy, fit, and feasibility before acting on them. <br>
