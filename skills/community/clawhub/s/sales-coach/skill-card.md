## Description: <br>
Provides real-time sales coaching during live meetings, including objection handling, talking points, buying signals, and negotiation tactics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shawnshenopeninterx](https://clawhub.ai/user/shawnshenopeninterx) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales employees use this skill during live meetings, transcript reviews, and meeting preparation to identify coaching moments and generate concise coaching cards with signals, recommended actions, and suggested phrases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive CRM records, email threads, and meeting notes may be used with weak scoping or unclear user control. <br>
Mitigation: Before use, confirm which accounts and records the agent may access and limit retrieval to the current meeting or deal. <br>
Risk: Broad sales-related language could trigger coaching unintentionally in conversations where it is not wanted. <br>
Mitigation: Enable the skill only for intended sales workflows and avoid using it in unrelated conversations. <br>
