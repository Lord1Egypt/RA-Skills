## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad trigger phrase "llm" may invoke the skill unintentionally during ordinary AI or LLM discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only when this specific weekly-report behavior is desired, and prefer a more specific trigger such as the full report title when configuring or invoking it. <br>
Risk: The skill writes a local KnowledgeBase entry when run. <br>
Mitigation: Use it in environments where this local KnowledgeBase behavior is expected and review stored entries if the skill was triggered unintentionally. <br>
