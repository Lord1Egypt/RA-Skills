## Description: <br>
Analyzes partnership meeting notes to identify real needs, key stakeholders, deal stage, close probability, next actions, follow-up wording, and risks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhangxiaopeng-zgci](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhangxiaopeng-zgci) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business development, university-industry partnership, and commercialization teams use this skill after meetings to turn notes into deal-stage judgments, action plans, follow-up messages, and risk flags. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting notes may contain sensitive names, budgets, negotiation positions, and strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Review and minimize sensitive input before use, and keep generated analysis within approved access boundaries. <br>
Risk: Generated follow-up messages may affect pricing, contracts, or high-value partnership discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer approve follow-up messages before sending them externally. <br>
