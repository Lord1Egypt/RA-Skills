## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and other users use this skill to identify repetitive manual tasks, design automation workflows, and draft scripts or configuration to execute and test those workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may encourage an agent to write or run scripts, schedule jobs, send messages, or change files, tools, or accounts without clear approval. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before executing scripts, scheduling automations, sending messages, collecting data, or changing files, tools, or accounts. <br>
Risk: Generated automations may act on an incorrectly identified repetitive workflow or produce incorrect outputs at scale. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated workflows and scripts, test them on a limited scope first, and monitor the results before relying on recurring execution. <br>
