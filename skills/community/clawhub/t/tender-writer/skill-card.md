## Description: <br>
Generates Word-format technical tender proposals for photovoltaic and intelligent weak-current projects from tender documents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shendingyi](https://clawhub.ai/user/shendingyi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and proposal teams use this skill to analyze photovoltaic EPC, photovoltaic EMC, photovoltaic operations and maintenance, or intelligent weak-current tender files and draft structured technical proposals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential bid documents or generated tender content could be sent through Feishu without appropriate authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the recipient account and whether the bid allows external messaging before Feishu delivery; keep generated Word files local in tender/ if not confirmed. <br>
Risk: Generated proposal content could miss tender-specific scoring priorities, brand requirements, qualifications, or schedule constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review the tender file, scoring rules, equipment requirements, staffing qualifications, and delivery schedule before submitting the proposal. <br>
