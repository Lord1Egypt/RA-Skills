## Description: <br>
Generates 5-slide TikTok slideshow drafts for an under-eye dark-circles app, including fresh hooks, slide text, Nano Banana image prompts, and generated image paths for slides 1-4. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Social media marketers and content creators use this skill to draft under-eye dark-circles TikTok slideshows with short-form hooks, consistent character visuals, and a CTA for an app. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
