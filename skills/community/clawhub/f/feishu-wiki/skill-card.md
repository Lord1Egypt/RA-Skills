## Description: <br>
Creates Feishu Wiki spaces and page nodes for organizing team documentation through the Feishu Wiki v2 API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sunnyyao2222-eng](https://clawhub.ai/user/sunnyyao2222-eng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, automation builders, and team knowledge managers use this skill to create, organize, move, and maintain Feishu Wiki spaces and page nodes for shared documentation workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create, move, edit, rename, or delete shared Feishu Wiki content, and the documentation does not clearly scope or gate those changes. <br>
Mitigation: Restrict bot permissions to specific wiki spaces where possible, require explicit confirmation before content-changing actions, and keep an audit trail or backup for important shared pages. <br>
