# Example Questions

Use these for demos, listing copy, tests, and onboarding.

## Small Appliances

- How can others reduce fan noise without sacrificing airflow?
- How have similar products improved heating speed without causing local overheating?
- How can a handheld vacuum reduce weight while maintaining structural strength?

## Automotive

- How have others improved battery pack heat dissipation without increasing volume and pipe complexity?
- How can an automotive seat reduce weight while maintaining crash strength?
- What cross-industry cases are relevant to reducing HVAC noise while maintaining airflow?

## Semiconductor

- How have others improved semiconductor package heat dissipation while controlling warpage?
- How can wafer cleaning improve particle removal while reducing surface damage?
- How have precision positioning systems improved accuracy without reducing throughput?

## Mechanical Manufacturing

- How can a fixture achieve high positioning accuracy and fast changeover at the same time?
- How have others increased machining efficiency while reducing tool wear?
- How can an automated production line improve flexibility without adding too much mechanical complexity?

## Cross-Industry

- Can automotive thermal management cases be transferred to small appliances?
- Can precision positioning cases from semiconductor equipment be applied to mechanical fixtures?
- Can small-appliance noise reduction patterns be transferred to automotive air duct design?
