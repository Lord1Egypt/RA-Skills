## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompt troubleshooting guidance could be misused to weaken legitimate model safety refusals in safety-sensitive environments. <br>
Mitigation: Review or revise the refusal-troubleshooting material before deployment, and do not use fictional, academic, or professional framing to bypass valid safety refusals. <br>
Risk: Prompt recommendations may produce ineffective, inconsistent, or misleading agent behavior if adopted without testing. <br>
Mitigation: Validate revised prompts with representative test cases, edge cases, and human review before production use. <br>
