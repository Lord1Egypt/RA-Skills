## Description: <br>
Log workouts, plan routines, track progress, and get intelligent coaching for any fitness level. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan gym routines, adapt workouts to restrictions or equipment, track workouts and personal records, and receive nutrition and progression guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store workout logs, measurements, restrictions, and other fitness details in local files under ~/gym/. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if local fitness record storage is acceptable, avoid unnecessary medical details, and review or delete those files when privacy matters. <br>
Risk: Fitness, injury, pain, nutrition, or unfamiliar high-risk lift guidance may be inappropriate for a user's medical condition or training context. <br>
Mitigation: Consult a qualified professional for injuries, pain, medical conditions, unfamiliar high-risk lifts, or individualized nutrition and training decisions. <br>
