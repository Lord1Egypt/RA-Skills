## Description: <br>
Generate executive-ready monthly AWS FinOps reports with team-level chargeback and savings opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[anmolnagpal](https://clawhub.ai/user/anmolnagpal) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Finance, FinOps, and engineering teams use this skill to turn AWS billing data into monthly cost reports, team chargeback summaries, savings opportunities, and executive-ready finance narratives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AWS billing and chargeback data can contain sensitive financial and organizational information. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege AWS cost-reporting access, avoid broad account-admin credentials, and review generated finance reports before external sharing. <br>
Risk: Savings opportunities, budget variances, or team chargeback summaries may be wrong if billing history, budgets, or ownership mappings are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Validate report inputs and have finance or FinOps reviewers confirm key numbers before acting on recommendations. <br>
