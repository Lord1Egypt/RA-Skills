## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to ask for Korean crypto market snapshots, including kimchi premium direction, Korean BTC volume movement, and a short market read. Paid Orion ACP access is described for users who want detailed signals, top movers, swap suggestions, or structured JSON output. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto market reads and trading claims may be inaccurate, stale, or unsuitable as financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational, verify prices and volume independently, and require explicit human approval before any trade or swap. <br>
Risk: The skill routes users to a paid Orion ACP service for expanded signals. <br>
Mitigation: Review the Orion ACP service and pricing before paying or sharing prompts. <br>
