## Description: <br>
Write in the user's authentic voice using ToneClone to generate emails, messages, social posts, and other content with trained personas and knowledge cards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jfox85](https://clawhub.ai/user/jfox85) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to set up and run the ToneClone CLI for drafting content in a trained personal writing voice. It helps agents compose prompts, choose personas, add knowledge-card context, and check CLI setup state. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, personas, and knowledge cards may include personal, confidential, regulated, or otherwise sensitive information that is processed by ToneClone. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending secrets, regulated data, confidential business information, or unnecessary personal details, and review generated text before sending or publishing it. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on a third-party CLI and Homebrew tap. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only when the ToneClone CLI, its Homebrew tap, and the publisher are trusted for the intended environment. <br>
