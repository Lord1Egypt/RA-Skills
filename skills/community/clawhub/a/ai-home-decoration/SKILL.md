# AI Home Decoration

Interior design guidance covering style selection, furniture layout, color theory, lighting design, and spatial optimization for apartments, houses, and studios.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Style Discovery
- Modern, minimalist, Scandinavian, industrial, bohemian, farmhouse, mid-century, japandi
- Style quiz and personality matching
- Mix-and-match style fusion guidance

### 2. Room-by-Room Design
- Living room, bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, home office, entryway
- Furniture layout optimization for traffic flow and focal points
- Space-saving solutions for small apartments
- Open-plan zoning strategies

### 3. Color & Material
- Color wheel theory, palette generation, accent walls
- Material pairing: wood, metal, glass, stone, textiles
- Paint selection, wallpaper patterns, texture layering

### 4. Lighting Design
- Ambient, task, accent lighting layers
- Fixture selection: chandeliers, pendants, sconces, floor lamps
- Natural light optimization, window treatments

### 5. Decor & Accessories
- Art placement, gallery walls, mirror positioning
- Rugs, curtains, cushions, throws coordination
- Plants and greenery integration
- Seasonal decor rotation

### 6. Budget Planning
- Cost estimation per room, DIY vs professional
- Thrift and vintage sourcing strategies
- High-impact low-cost upgrades
