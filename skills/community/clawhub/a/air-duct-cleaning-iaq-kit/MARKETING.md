"Duct cleaning improves your air quality" — the default output from every AI tool, every copywriter, and every marketing agency that writes for duct cleaning contractors.

Writing that claim = FTC deceptive practices exposure. Every session.

**The 7 moats in Skill #194:**

1. **NADCA ACR 2021 gate** — "EPA certified duct cleaner" permanently blocked (EPA doesn't certify anyone). NADCA member # required in all marketing.

2. **EPA IAQ accuracy gate** — "improves air quality," "eliminates allergens," "prevents illness" permanently blocked without pre/post IAQ test data. The honest positioning is duct cleanliness — not health outcomes.

3. **Source removal method gate** — brush-and-blow marketed as NADCA-compliant permanently blocked. Negative air machine + point-of-contact agitation = the ACR 2021 standard.

4. **FIFRA biocide gate** — "anti-microbial treatment kills 99.9% of germs" permanently blocked without EPA FIFRA Reg #. Applying unregistered biocide to a duct system = federal pesticide law violation. Informed customer consent required per NADCA policy.

5. **Mold claim gate** — "we found mold in your ducts" without documented inspection evidence permanently blocked. Clark County, NV: mold remediation >10 sq ft requires NRS 624 licensed contractor.

6. **C-21 license gate** — "we'll add a UV light / seal your ducts while we're in there" permanently blocked without NRS 624 C-21 HVAC-R license. Cleaning only = no license. Any modification = C-21 required.

7. **FTC 2023 + TCPA** — "HVAC warranty requires duct cleaning every 2 years" permanently blocked (no manufacturer requires this — it's a commonly fabricated sales claim). MERV-13 ≠ HEPA (99.97% at 0.3 microns — permanently distinguished).
