## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prisma migrations, raw SQL, and database write guidance can affect real data if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated migrations, raw SQL, and write queries in a non-production environment before applying them to real data. <br>
Risk: Query and performance suggestions may still be inefficient or unsafe for a specific schema, dataset, or database configuration. <br>
Mitigation: Check query logs, indexes, pagination behavior, and database plans before deploying changes. <br>
