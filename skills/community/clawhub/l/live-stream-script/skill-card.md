## Description: <br>
Generates reusable live stream scripts and templates for e-commerce, entertainment, and educational streams, including warmups, openings, product pitches, closing prompts, and interaction prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, livestream hosts, and commerce teams use this skill to draft opening, warmup, product presentation, closing, and interaction scripts for livestream sessions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-entered topics and drafts may be saved to a local plaintext history file. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential campaign plans, product details, or unpublished scripts unless local plaintext history logging is acceptable. <br>
Risk: The installed command surface may expose different helper behavior across included scripts. <br>
Mitigation: Verify which executable is installed and review the command help before using the skill in a production workflow. <br>
