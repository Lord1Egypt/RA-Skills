## Description: <br>
Generates a custom AI automation playbook identifying workflows to automate, recommended tools, SOPs, and ROI projections for small businesses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Small business owners, operations managers, consultants, and solopreneurs use this skill to turn business workflow inputs into an AI automation plan with tool recommendations, AI-integrated SOPs, and ROI projections. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated tool recommendations may encourage software purchases or paid integrations that are not appropriate for the business. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations, pricing, and integration requirements before buying tools or changing subscriptions. <br>
Risk: Business workflow prompts can involve sensitive credentials, customer records, financial details, or confidential operational data. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste API keys, passwords, customer records, or confidential financial details into prompts unless the selected AI provider and company policy allow it. <br>
Risk: Generated SOPs, public responses, or business-record changes may be inaccurate or off-brand if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep human approval checkpoints for public posts, customer communications, financial projections, and business-record updates. <br>
