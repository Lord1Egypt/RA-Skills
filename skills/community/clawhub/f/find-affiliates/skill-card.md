## Description: <br>
Find affiliates, publishers, review partners, content partners, and performance-based promotion opportunities for ecommerce offers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, and ecommerce operators use this skill to identify and prioritize affiliate, publisher, review, and content partners by audience fit, content fit, promotion relevance, and commercial intent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary sensitive business details while describing affiliate goals. <br>
Mitigation: Limit inputs to the offer, audience, market, and partner criteria needed for affiliate research. <br>
Risk: Affiliate recommendations may include incomplete or outdated public signals. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify suggested affiliates before outreach or partnership decisions. <br>
