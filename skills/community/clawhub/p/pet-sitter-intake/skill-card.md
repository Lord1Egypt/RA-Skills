## Description: <br>
Generate professional, fillable PDF client intake forms for pet sitting businesses with custom branding, color themes, multi-pet support, and service-specific sections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[basilanathan](https://clawhub.ai/user/basilanathan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Pet sitters, dog walkers, pet boarders, and pet care businesses use this skill to generate branded client onboarding forms, pet questionnaires, and service-specific intake paperwork as local PDFs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Completed intake PDFs can contain sensitive client, pet, and home-access details such as keys, alarm codes, WiFi, and property instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the home-access section only when needed, omit it when possible, store completed PDFs securely, limit sharing, and redact or delete access details when they are no longer required. <br>
