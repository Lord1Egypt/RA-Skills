## Description: <br>
Set up product analytics and define metrics that matter for product decisions, including tracking plans, KPIs, funnels, retention, dashboards, and experimentation setup. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, product teams, and developers use this skill to define actionable product metrics, create tracking plans, implement analytics events, and design dashboards that support growth, activation, retention, and revenue decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Analytics snippets and tracking plans could be used without adequate privacy, consent, or retention controls. <br>
Mitigation: Obtain required consent, document what data is collected, configure retention and anonymization, and review privacy requirements before production use. <br>
Risk: Event properties could accidentally include PII, secrets, or overly identifying user data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending PII or secrets in event properties, prefer pseudonymous identifiers, and review analytics payloads before deployment. <br>
