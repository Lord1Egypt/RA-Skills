## Description: <br>
Routes agent work to task-appropriate models for multimodal inputs, coding, reasoning, writing, long-context, Office document, and subagent workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to guide automatic model selection for multimodal, coding, reasoning, writing, translation, long-context, and document tasks in OpenClaw-style workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automatic model-routing guidance may influence normal chats and subagent work in ways that are unsuitable for sensitive or high-cost tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the selected model before sensitive, regulated, or high-cost work. <br>
Risk: The included monitor script claims background service behavior that may not work as an actual background service in every OpenClaw setup. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the monitor helper as optional until it is independently tested in the target environment. <br>
