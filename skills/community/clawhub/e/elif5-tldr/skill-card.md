## Description: <br>
Forces offering ELI5 or TLDR options after complex responses and always presents simplified alternatives. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Tooled-app](https://clawhub.ai/user/Tooled-app) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and users use this skill to make complex responses easier to skim or simplify by offering ELI5 and TLDR follow-up options after long or technical answers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may append ELI5/TLDR options where exact JSON, code-only output, concise answers, or no extra appended text is required. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid or disable it for workflows that require exact structured output, code-only responses, strict brevity, or no appended options. <br>
