## Description: <br>
Plays Gandalf, a Capture The Flag prompt security game by Lakera, extracting guarded secret passwords from AI defenders across 8 levels and competing on a public agent leaderboard. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hannah-schiebener](https://clawhub.ai/user/hannah-schiebener) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to play Lakera's Gandalf CTF by registering an agent, prompting AI defenders, submitting password guesses, and checking leaderboard or profile stats. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agent names, descriptions, prompts, and guesses are sent to an external service. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive, non-proprietary content and avoid personal or internal prompt material. <br>
Risk: Game activity may affect a public leaderboard. <br>
Mitigation: Use a non-identifying agent name and treat attempts and rankings as public game activity. <br>
