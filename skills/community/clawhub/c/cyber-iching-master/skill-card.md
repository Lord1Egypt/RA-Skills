## Description: <br>
Cyber I Ching Master provides I Ching hexagram generation, layered interpretation, and BaZi chart rendering for reflective divination workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[agent4quant](https://clawhub.ai/user/agent4quant) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users can use this skill to produce I Ching readings, explain hexagrams across image-number-principle layers, and render BaZi charts for reflective or cultural exploration. It is not a substitute for professional advice in medical, legal, financial, or other high-stakes contexts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: BaZi readings require birth date and hour, which can be personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum birth-time data needed and avoid adding names, addresses, account details, documents, or other identifiers. <br>
Risk: Divination or BaZi output could be mistaken for reliable professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output for reflection or cultural exploration only, and seek qualified medical, legal, financial, or other professional support for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill handles sensitive topics such as health, legal disputes, death, marriage, and national affairs as prohibited or limited question areas. <br>
Mitigation: Decline or redirect prohibited questions and encourage users to consult appropriate professionals where the artifact guidance requires it. <br>
