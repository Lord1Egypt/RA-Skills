## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Frontend snippets may depend on third-party libraries or font assets whose licenses, integrity, or availability vary by project. <br>
Mitigation: Review third-party library and font licenses before production use, and pin or self-host dependencies where appropriate. <br>
Risk: Heavy animation, custom cursors, smooth scrolling, and WebGL effects can affect accessibility and performance on real devices. <br>
Mitigation: Test performance, keyboard navigation, color contrast, screen reader behavior, and reduced-motion behavior before release. <br>
