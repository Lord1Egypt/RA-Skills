# Prompt 03 — Reputation Management & Referral Outreach
## Auto Body & Collision Repair Marketing Kit

**PAID TIER**

## How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt below into Claude. Fill in the bracketed fields. Output includes 20 FTC-compliant review request templates, 15 GBP response templates, and 6 B2B referral outreach letters targeting dealerships, rental agencies, fleet managers, and insurance adjusters.

## Notes

**Review gating is an FTC violation.** Sending review requests only to customers you believe are happy, and routing unhappy customers to a private complaint form, is "review gating" — a deceptive practice the FTC has specifically prohibited since 2022. All review requests must go to all customers. This prompt generates templates that comply with this requirement.

**GBP response privacy:** Never include accident date, location, other parties, or insurance claim number in a public GBP response. Even if the customer included these details in their review, your response should not repeat them.

**B2B referral fees:** In Nevada, paying referral fees to insurance adjusters is prohibited. Paying referral fees to dealerships or fleet managers is generally permissible if disclosed, but should be reviewed against specific business arrangement structure. This prompt generates referral arrangements based on mutual business benefit (not financial kickbacks) to avoid this issue.
