## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto questions are sent to Claudius' servers for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include wallet secrets, exchange API keys, account details, private financial information, or other sensitive data in prompts. <br>
Risk: Market analysis and investment recommendations may be incomplete, incorrect, or unsuitable for a user's financial situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational, verify important market claims independently, and do not rely on the skill as financial advice. <br>
