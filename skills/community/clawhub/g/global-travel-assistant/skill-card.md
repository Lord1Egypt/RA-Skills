## Description: <br>
Global Travel Assistant provides travel planning tools for flights, hotels, visa checks, destination safety, plugs, tax refunds, exchange rates, flight baggage and seats, room policies, and emergency contacts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and travel-support agents use this skill to retrieve itinerary, booking, destination, emergency, visa, safety, tax refund, and exchange-rate information for international trips. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Flight, hotel, room, baggage, seat, and exchange-rate lookups may send travel query details to external services. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use the skill only where sending those lookup details to external services is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Booking links may include commission and require the user to complete payment outside the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Review prices, supplier terms, refund policies, and payment details directly with the booking provider before purchase. <br>
Risk: Visa, safety, tax refund, emergency, and exchange-rate information can change or be incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify critical travel requirements and emergency actions against official government, embassy, airline, hotel, tax authority, or financial sources before travel. <br>
