## Description: <br>
Coaches Fiverr and gig-marketplace sellers on gig titles, descriptions, packaging, pricing, visuals, marketplace search, level progression, and buyer conversion. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External freelancers and marketplace sellers use this skill to diagnose funnel bottlenecks across impressions, clicks, orders, reviews, and repeat buyers. It produces actionable guidance for Fiverr-style gig SEO, page copy, pricing tiers, visuals, package structure, and retention planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Advice about off-platform billing, CRM or email-list use, and moving buyers away from a marketplace may conflict with Fiverr or other marketplace rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current marketplace terms and no-circumvention policies before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Dispute-avoidance, cancellation, and partial-refund suggestions may create compliance or customer-service risk if applied automatically. <br>
Mitigation: Treat conflict-handling suggestions as business advice that requires human review and policy checks before use. <br>
