## Description: <br>
A GTM planning helper for generating sales materials, competitive messaging, PR drafts, and customer-facing communications from new feature descriptions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[winsaney](https://clawhub.ai/user/winsaney) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, sales, PR, and customer-facing teams use this skill to turn new feature descriptions into GTM materials such as sales FAQs, competitive talking points, customer Q&A, scripts, and launch copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated GTM or customer-facing claims may be inaccurate or unsuitable for external use. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated claims against product facts and approval requirements before using them externally. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may apply the skill to adjacent launch-writing or customer communication requests. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill intentionally for marketing, PR, sales, or customer communication work. <br>
