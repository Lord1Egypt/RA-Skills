## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to receive personalized wellness habit guidance that emphasizes uncertainty, individual baselines, and referral to healthcare professionals for medical concerns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake wellness guidance for medical diagnosis, treatment, or prescribing. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses framed as general wellness support, acknowledge uncertainty, and refer users to healthcare professionals for medical concerns or concerning symptom patterns. <br>
Risk: Personalization may involve sensitive health conditions, medication, mood, sleep, stress, or routine details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for context the user is comfortable sharing and avoid retaining or expanding sensitive details beyond what is needed for the current guidance. <br>
