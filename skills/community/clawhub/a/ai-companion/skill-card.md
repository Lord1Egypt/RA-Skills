## Description: <br>
高情商聊天伴侣，支持角色扮演、情感陪伴、职场沟通、社交话术等多种场景，可模拟男友、女友、亲人、朋友、职场同事和老年人等角色并生成中英文聊天文案与话术模板。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill for emotional companionship, role-play, relationship advice, workplace communication, social conversation, apology, comfort, and reply-writing scenarios in Chinese or English. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate the skill for generic reply-writing or social communication requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants social coaching, role-play, or communication drafting before applying the skill to ambiguous requests. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce advice for workplace, relationship, emotional-support, and mental-health-adjacent situations where generic wording may be inappropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for context, boundaries, and sensitivity; avoid presenting role-play or social coaching as professional legal, medical, or mental-health advice. <br>
