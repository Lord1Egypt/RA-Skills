## Description: <br>
Helps creators clearly credit collaborators, tools, and partners in a way platforms understand. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[OtherPowers](https://clawhub.ai/user/OtherPowers) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and content teams use this skill to draft platform-aware attribution, disclosure, and credit language before publishing. It helps organize visible credits, AI-use labels, branded-content reminders, and internal reference IDs without making legal or ownership determinations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Attribution and disclosure drafts may be mistaken for legal advice or proof of compliance. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the first-turn disclaimer visible and require users to verify legal, ownership, and compliance questions outside the skill. <br>
Risk: Platform-specific attribution and disclosure expectations may change after the skill is published. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current official platform guidance before publishing content, especially for AI labels and branded-content tools. <br>
Risk: Output could imply ownership, revenue split, enforcement outcome, or risk score if used beyond its documented scope. <br>
Mitigation: Constrain responses to organizational attribution support and avoid ownership determinations, enforcement guarantees, bypass instructions, and risk scoring. <br>
