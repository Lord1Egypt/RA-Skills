## Description: <br>
Review TikTok Shop seller and creator promotional content compliance using official TikTok Shop Seller University guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[LangYou](https://clawhub.ai/user/LangYou) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, creators, and agents use this skill to review TikTok Shop promotional videos, LIVEs, scripts, captions, listing images, product claims, giveaways, and AI-generated ads for permitted, risky, prohibited, or unclear content. It also helps revise flagged content with policy-grounded safer wording or scene direction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: TikTok Shop policy guidance can change and may be market-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify live TikTok Shop policy pages for current, market-specific rules before acting on compliance-sensitive advice. <br>
Risk: Reviewing videos, images, scripts, or listing materials can expose private or sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the media and text needed for review, and avoid sharing unnecessary private or sensitive details. <br>
Risk: The skill provides platform-policy review support, not legal or regulatory advice. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate legal, FDA, FTC, or similar regulatory questions to qualified reviewers before relying on the output. <br>
