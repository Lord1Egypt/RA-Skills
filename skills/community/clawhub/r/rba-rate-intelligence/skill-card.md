## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to review Australian RBA cash rate context, meeting timing, CPI signals, and illustrative mortgage repayment impacts. It supports market monitoring and general financial planning workflows, with current figures verified against official sources before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: RBA, CPI, mortgage repayment, and market-rate figures are static and may become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current rates and economic data with official RBA, ABS, lender, or adviser sources before making financial decisions. <br>
