## Description: <br>
Deep competitor audits with market positioning, gap analysis, and actionable insights for winning strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, product teams, and strategy teams use this skill to research competitors in a niche, compare market positioning, identify gaps, and turn findings into strategic recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor research notes and business context may persist locally under ~/competitor-research/. <br>
Mitigation: Review or delete the local folder when it is no longer needed, and avoid saving confidential strategy unless persistence is intended. <br>
Risk: Pricing, positioning, reviews, and market signals can become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Date findings, cite sources, and refresh important sections before making business decisions. <br>
