## Description: <br>
Dan Koe Writer helps agents analyze writing samples into reusable creative building blocks, generate content ideas, and draft posts using Dan Koe-inspired writing frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alleliu](https://clawhub.ai/user/alleliu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to decompose article examples into hooks, paradoxes, transformation arcs, core problems, phrases, structures, and perspectives, then recombine those materials into new article prompts or drafts for platforms such as WeChat, Xiaohongshu, Twitter, and newsletters. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided article files or URLs may contain sensitive private content that is included in generated prompts or context. <br>
Mitigation: Review source material and generated prompt/context before model use, and avoid sensitive private documents or untrusted pages. <br>
Risk: The skill is based on Dan Koe-inspired public writing methods and bundled examples, but it is not officially affiliated with Dan Koe. <br>
Mitigation: Use the examples for learning and inspiration, rewrite outputs in an original voice, and verify rights before publishing derived content. <br>
