## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Aggressive short-term trading guidance can lead to financial loss if followed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep final buy and sell decisions under explicit human control and treat the skill as decision support. <br>
Risk: Market data or news inputs may be stale, incomplete, or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market data independently before making any trade. <br>
Risk: Connecting the guidance to automated brokerage actions could execute unintended trades. <br>
Mitigation: Do not connect the skill to automated brokerage actions without separate review. <br>
