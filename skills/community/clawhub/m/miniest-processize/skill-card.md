## Description: <br>
Processize helps users turn a product idea into a manual delivery workflow they can validate before writing code. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ethan-china](https://clawhub.ai/user/ethan-china) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders and product builders use this skill to validate product ideas by defining a manual service workflow, initial pricing, candidate customers, and the first step to automate after validation. <br>

Global <br>
