## Description: <br>
Generates ESG and sustainability reports, calculates Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 carbon footprints, benchmarks ESG ratings, and supports climate-risk and compliance analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users such as corporate CSR teams, sustainability consultants, and investors use this skill to draft ESG reports, calculate carbon emissions, compare against ESG rating frameworks, and review sustainability disclosure readiness. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: ESG workflows may involve sensitive employee or business data. <br>
Mitigation: Use aggregated or anonymized employee metrics where possible, avoid unnecessary personal records, and ensure any sensitive data is authorized for use. <br>
Risk: Generated ESG reports, emissions calculations, or compliance checks may be incomplete or misleading if source data or boundaries are wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before external sharing, confirm data sources and calculation boundaries, and validate carbon accounting against the applicable GHG Protocol and disclosure standards. <br>
Risk: ESG rating benchmarks could be mistaken for investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat MSCI, Sustainalytics, CDP, and similar benchmark comparisons as reference material only and obtain qualified review before investment or regulatory use. <br>
