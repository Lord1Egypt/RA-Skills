## Description: <br>
AI-powered banking regulatory reporting assistant - generate CBIRC/NRA/SAFE reports, statistical submissions, and compliance documentation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Bank compliance officers, data analysts, IT systems teams, and regulatory affairs staff use this skill to draft Chinese banking regulatory reports and compliance documentation, including 1104 statistics, NPA, AML, capital adequacy, liquidity risk, green credit, SME credit, and real estate finance reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste production customer, account, transaction, AML, or supervisory information into an unapproved environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved organizational environments and data handling processes; redact sensitive data where possible. <br>
Risk: Regulatory report drafts may contain incorrect, incomplete, or outdated guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Have authorized compliance staff verify outputs against official requirements before use or submission. <br>
Risk: AI-generated reports may be mistaken for final official submissions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and keep final filing decisions with the responsible compliance function. <br>
