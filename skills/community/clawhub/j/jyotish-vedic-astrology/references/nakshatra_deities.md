# Nakshatra Deities and Symbolism - 星宿神祇与象征

## 27 Nakshatras Complete Guide

### Ashwini (अश्विनी) - 0°00' - 13°20' Aries
- **Symbol**: Horse's head (head of the Ashwini Kumaras)
- **Deity**: Ashwini Kumaras (Divine twins, physicians of gods)
- **Planet**: Ketu (South Node)
- **Nature**: Dharma (Charitable)
- **Gana**: Deva (Divine)
- **Guna**: Tamas (Inertia)
- **Element**: Earth
- **Quality**: Swift (Kshipra)
- **Animal**: Male Horse
- **Cast**: Shudra (Service)
- **Power**: Speed of action (Shidhra vyapani shakti)
- **Life Themes**: Healing, medicine, quick action, initiative, beginnings
- **Spiritual Lesson**: Divine healing energy, service to others
- **Favorable For**: Starting new ventures, healing work, transportation, sports

- **Life Themes**: Transformation, endurance, facing challenges, death/rebirth
- **Spiritual Lesson**: Accepting life's cycles, facing death as transformation
- **Favorable For**: Facing difficult truths, transformation work, overcoming obstacles

## Nakshatra Qualities (Gunas)

1. **Sattva (Purity)**: Pushya, Hasta, Uttara Phalguni, Uttara Ashadha, Revati
   - Spiritual, pure, wise

2. **Rajas (Passion)**: Bharani, Rohini, Ardra, Punarvasu, Ashlesha, Magha, Purva Phalguni, Swati, Vishakha, Jyeshtha, Mula, Purva Ashadha, Shatabhisha, Purva Bhadrapada
   - Active, passionate, dynamic

3. **Tamas (Inertia)**: Krittika, Mrigashira, Chitra, Anuradha, Shravana, Dhanishta, Uttara Bhadrapada
   - Stable, grounded, sometimes stagnant

## Nakshatra Nature (Prakriti)

1. **Dharma (Charitable)**: Ashwini, Punarvasu, Pushya, Hasta, Anuradha, Shravana, Dhanishta, Revati
2. **Raja (Royal)**: Krittika, Magha, Ashlesha, Purva Phalguni, Vishakha, Jyeshtha
3. **Manushya (Human)**: Rohini, Mrigashira, Purva Phalguni, Uttara Phalguni, Hasta, Swati, Ardra, Punarvasu, Uttara Ashadha, Purva Ashadha
4. **Chara (Movable)**: Swati, Mula, Shatabhisha, Punarvasu

## Nakshatra Animal Symbolism

Animals represent the temperament and nature of the Nakshatra:
- **Horse (Ashwini)**: Speed, action, energy
- **Elephant (Bharani, Magha, Uttara Phalguni)**: Power, royalty, memory
- **Goat (Pushya)**: Nurturing, agility, sure-footedness
- **Cat (Punarvasu, Ashlesha)**: Independence, grace, mystery
- **Serpent (Rohini, Mrigashira)**: Wisdom, transformation
- **Dog (Ardra, Mula)**: Loyalty, protection, intuition
- **Buffalo (Hasta, Swati)**: Strength, stubbornness
- **Rat (Magha, Purva Phalguni)**: Resourcefulness, survival
- **Bull (Uttara Phalguni)**: Stability, strength, fertility
- **Tiger (Chitra, Vishakha)**: Power, beauty, ferocity
- **Monkey (Purva Ashadha, Shravana)**: Intelligence, agility
- **Mongoose (Uttara Ashadha)**: Alertness, wisdom
- **Lion (Dhanishta, Purva Bhadrapada)**: Power, majesty, courage
- **Cow (Uttara Bhadrapada)**: Nurturing, gentle, abundance

## Remedial Measures by Nakshatra

### Fire Nakshatras (Agni Tattva)
- Krittika, Vishakha, Purva Ashadha, Purva Bhadrapada
- Remedies: Fire rituals (Homa), offering to fire, lighting lamps

### Water Nakshatras (Jala Tattva)
- Rohini, Hasta, Shravana, Shatabhisha, Revati
- Remedies: Water offerings, bathing in sacred rivers, donating water

### Air Nakshatras (Vayu Tattva)
- Ardra, Swati, Purva Bhadrapada
- Remedies: Air-related offerings, incense, ventilation, traveling

### Earth Nakshatras (Prithvi Tattva)
- Ashwini, Bharani, Rohini, Mrigashira, Ashlesha, Pushya, Magha, Purva Phalguni, Uttara Phalguni, Anuradha, Uttara Ashadha, Uttara Bhadrapada, Revati
- Remedies: Earth-related offerings, gardening, grounding practices
