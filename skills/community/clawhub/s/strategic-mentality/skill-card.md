This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External business operators and agent users use this skill to evaluate opportunities, plan execution sprints, score leads, handle sales objections, structure outreach, and make pricing or positioning decisions with named strategic frameworks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release metadata lists crypto and purchase-related capabilities that are not needed for the strategy coaching behavior described in the artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Do not grant crypto, purchasing, payment, or similar financial authority unless a separate workflow clearly requires it and the user approves each external action. <br>
Risk: The recurring Telegram accountability flow can send business metrics and store scores without clear opt-in, retention, or disable controls. <br>
Mitigation: Enable the heartbeat only after confirming the destination chat, metrics to send, score storage location, retention period, and pause or deletion process. <br>
Risk: Lead scoring and sales workflows may involve scraping, CRM updates, email follow-up, or calendar actions around prospect data. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before granting scraping, CRM, email-sending, or calendar authority, and keep prospect data handling consistent with the user's confirmed workflow. <br>
