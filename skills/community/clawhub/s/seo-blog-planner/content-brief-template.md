## Content Guidelines

### Tone and Voice
- **Audience**: [Specific reader persona — experience level, needs, concerns]
- **Tone**: [Authoritative but approachable / Technical and precise / Friendly and conversational]
- **Reading level**: [Grade level target, typically 8th-10th grade for consumer, higher for B2B technical]
- **Perspective**: [First person plural (we) / Second person (you) / Third person]

### What to Avoid
- No thin filler paragraphs — every paragraph should add value
- No keyword stuffing — keyword density should feel natural when read aloud
- No unsubstantiated claims — cite sources for statistics and data points
- No competitor brand mentions without strategic purpose
- No external links to competitor stores or marketplaces

### Content Quality Standards
- Every claim backed by data, expert source, or first-hand experience
- Original insights or analysis, not just regurgitated existing content
- Practical, actionable advice the reader can implement immediately
- Visual aids (images, tables, charts) to break up text every 300-400 words

## Publication Checklist

### Pre-Publish
- [ ] All H2/H3 sections from brief are completed
- [ ] Word count meets target range
- [ ] Primary and secondary keywords placed per checklist
- [ ] All internal links are inserted and working
- [ ] Meta title and description are set
- [ ] URL slug follows convention
- [ ] Featured image has keyword-rich alt text
- [ ] Schema markup is configured
- [ ] Content has been proofread for grammar and clarity
- [ ] Mobile formatting checked (tables render, images resize)

### Post-Publish (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Article appears in sitemap
- [ ] Submit URL to Google Search Console for indexing
- [ ] Verify internal links from other articles point to new post
- [ ] Social media distribution scheduled
- [ ] Email newsletter inclusion scheduled (if applicable)
- [ ] Initial rank tracking keyword added to monitoring tool
