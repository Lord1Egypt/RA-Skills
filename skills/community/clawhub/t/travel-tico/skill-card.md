## Description: <br>
tico-travel helps an agent produce research-backed, PDF-ready travel guides with verified travel logistics, modular regional options, safety notes, and recommendation directories. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jovicuz](https://clawhub.ai/user/jovicuz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and planners use this skill to generate destination-specific travel planning guides tailored to passport nationality, travel style, duration, and year. The output supports planning decisions around entry requirements, health and safety, local logistics, budgets, neighborhoods, activities, lodging, food, and low-cost options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release metadata declares crypto and purchase capabilities that are not explained or scoped by the travel-guide instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a research and planning assistant only; do not grant purchase, payment, account, or crypto tool access unless the publisher provides a clear need and operating scope. <br>
Risk: Travel rules, prices, health alerts, and safety conditions can change or may be difficult to verify. <br>
Mitigation: Verify critical travel facts with official government, health, airline, border-control, embassy, and local sources before relying on the guide. <br>
