Mitigation: Review conclusions against the skill's statistical rigor checklist, require effect sizes and confidence intervals where applicable, and state limitations before acting. <br>
Risk: Source provenance is unavailable for this release, which limits traceability for users who require exact publisher or import history. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the ClawHub listing, publisher profile, version, license, and file hashes before installing in provenance-sensitive environments. <br>
