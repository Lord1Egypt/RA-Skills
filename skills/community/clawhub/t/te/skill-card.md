## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: API keys and stream keys can expose control of the agent's Lobster.fun stream if shared or committed. <br>
Mitigation: Store credentials securely, avoid pasting them into public channels or repositories, and rotate them if exposure is suspected. <br>
Risk: Viewer input and media search terms can influence visible avatar actions, GIFs, YouTube videos, and chat-facing content. <br>
Mitigation: Use stream and chat moderation, review media choices before display when possible, and keep avatar actions appropriate for the audience. <br>
Risk: The listing slug is "te" while the skill content describes the Lobster.fun skill. <br>
Mitigation: Verify before installation that the ClawHub listing is the intended Lobster.fun streaming skill. <br>
