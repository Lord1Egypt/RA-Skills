## Description: <br>
Guides agents through web performance audits, bottleneck diagnosis, optimization playbooks, performance budgets, and monitoring workflows for websites and web applications. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and site operators use this skill to audit website speed, identify Core Web Vitals bottlenecks, and generate targeted remediation guidance for server, rendering, JavaScript, image, layout, mobile, and third-party script issues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Target URLs may be sent to external performance or search services during audits. <br>
Mitigation: Use third-party checks only for public URLs or when the user is comfortable with external service logging; rely on local or manual inspection for sensitive sites. <br>
