## Description: <br>
Generates structured paper trading plans with entry, stop loss, take profit, position size, and failure conditions for SPX, indices, and US equity options. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and trading-plan builders use this skill to generate local educational paper-trading plans for session preparation, including entry, stop, target, position size, and failure-condition checks. It supports planning only and does not execute trades or connect to brokerage accounts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated plans may contain incorrect financial assumptions or may be mistaken for investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat output as educational planning information, independently verify all assumptions, and avoid using it as a sole basis for trading decisions. <br>
Risk: A user may assume the skill executes trades or manages brokerage accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as a local paper-trading planner; evidence shows no network access, credential use, persistence, or trading execution. <br>
