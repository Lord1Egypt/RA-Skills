## Description: <br>
Q Erp answers Qianyi ERP management questions, including business summaries, sales performance, growth potential, product rankings, and multi-day sales trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ljqdh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ljqdh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ERP users and business operators use this skill to ask read-only management questions about Qianyi ERP sales, product performance, operating trends, and follow-up diagnostics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can access ERP business data through its configured query path. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only in environments where q-claw access to ERP business data is intended. <br>
Risk: Short replies such as "ok," "continue," or numbered choices may continue a previous ERP query context. <br>
Mitigation: Users should confirm the conversation context before sending short follow-up replies in sensitive business conversations. <br>
