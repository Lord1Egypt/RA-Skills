## Description: <br>
Humanize Thai ecommerce copy to make it sound more natural, local, and less machine-written while preserving selling intent. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce sellers and marketing teams use this skill to make Thai product pages, ads, captions, selling scripts, and customer replies sound more natural while preserving the offer, proof direction, and call-to-action intent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Humanized sales copy can preserve or amplify unsupported product claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy for truthfulness, platform policy compliance, and banned wording before publishing. <br>
Risk: Making AI-written copy sound human may conflict with a publisher's disclosure practices. <br>
Mitigation: Use the rewritten copy only in contexts where the disclosure approach has been approved by the business or campaign owner. <br>
Risk: The skill is scoped to Thai ecommerce copy and is not a legal, medical, regulated compliance, or translation review tool. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for Thai ecommerce copy refinement and route regulated claims or translation needs to appropriate review workflows. <br>
