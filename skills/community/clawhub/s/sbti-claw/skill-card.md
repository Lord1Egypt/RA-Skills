## Description: <br>
SBTI runs a Chinese joke personality quiz by asking playful questions, scoring four comic dimensions, and generating a humorous personality result card. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[enihsago](https://clawhub.ai/user/enihsago) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for a lighthearted Chinese SBTI-style personality quiz that returns an intentionally unserious personality type and short interpretation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The quiz can activate on broad personality-test or test-related phrases. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when the user clearly wants an SBTI-style joke quiz or agrees to continue after the opening prompt. <br>
Risk: The generated result is intentionally teasing and has no scientific basis. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the tone light, avoid serious psychological claims, and preserve the disclaimer that results are for reference only. <br>
