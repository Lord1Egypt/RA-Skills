## Description: <br>
Use this skill when the user needs public business email leads from Google Maps through an Apify actor, including emails, email details, websites, phones, social profiles, ratings, addresses, coordinates, Google Place IDs, CIDs, and Google Maps URLs from keywords, locations, Google Maps URLs, Place IDs, categories, or map areas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to build and run Apify Google Maps Email Extractor payloads for public business email lead generation, CRM enrichment, spreadsheets, outreach workflows, and related local business research. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can send lead-search inputs and extracted contact data to a paid third-party Apify actor. <br>
Mitigation: Use a budget limit, keep the niche and geography tightly scoped, and run it only when sharing this data with Apify is acceptable. <br>
Risk: The actor can collect person-like contact data from public websites. <br>
Mitigation: Disable personal data unless there is a lawful and appropriate reason to collect it. <br>
