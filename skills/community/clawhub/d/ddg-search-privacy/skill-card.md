## Description: <br>
DuckDuckGo Search helps agents perform privacy-focused web, image, news, and instant-answer searches without requiring an API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to retrieve web information, quick facts, URLs, images, and news results through DuckDuckGo. It is suited for research and fact-finding workflows where users prefer not to rely on tracked or personalized search. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms are sent to DuckDuckGo over the network. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using passwords, tokens, private personal details, or confidential business information in search queries. <br>
Risk: Search results and snippets may contain untrusted, inaccurate, or misleading web content. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims against authoritative sources before using results in downstream work. <br>
