## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Budget or optimization recommendations could be applied before the underlying campaign metrics are reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before making changes in an advertising platform, and use the skill's stop-loss or rollback conditions when spend risk exists. <br>
Risk: Unnecessary campaign data could be shared when asking for analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the campaign metrics needed for the specific question or reporting goal. <br>
