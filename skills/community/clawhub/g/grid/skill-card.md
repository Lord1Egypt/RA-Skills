## Description: <br>
Guides agents in designing, optimizing, and auditing responsive grid layouts for equal-hierarchy content display. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, developers, and agents use this skill to decide when a grid is appropriate and to produce responsive grid-layout guidance for products, templates, tools, features, blog indexes, and galleries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Infinite-scroll grids can keep SEO-critical content from being accessible to crawlers. <br>
Mitigation: Use paginated component pages for SEO-critical content and reserve infinite scroll for content that does not need crawler discovery. <br>
Risk: Grid guidance can be misapplied to dense or text-heavy content where list layouts are more scannable. <br>
Mitigation: Choose list layouts for dense text or title-first scanning, and review responsive behavior before relying on the generated guidance. <br>
