## Use Case: <br>
Developers, strategists, and business users can use this skill as a roleplay-based thinking aid for questions about M&A integration, technology absorption, brand portfolios, platform strategy, globalization, industrial ecosystems, and long-term automotive strategy. Mitigation: Independently verify facts and consult qualified professionals before using outputs for high-stakes decisions. <br>
