## Description: <br>
Takes an existing LinkedIn draft and returns an optimized version with a change log explaining each decision. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zepoldani](https://clawhub.ai/user/zepoldani) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and professionals use this skill to improve an existing LinkedIn draft for hook strength, mobile readability, structure, call to action, and hashtag discipline while preserving the author's voice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pasted drafts may contain private company details, personal data, or unreleased announcements. <br>
Mitigation: Remove sensitive details before asking an agent to optimize the draft. <br>
Risk: The skill improves structure, formatting, and hook strength but cannot guarantee LinkedIn engagement. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as editorial guidance and review the final post before publishing. <br>
