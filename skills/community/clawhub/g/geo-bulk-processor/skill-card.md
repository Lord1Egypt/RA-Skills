## Use Case: <br>
Developers, content strategists, and SEO/GEO operators use this skill to plan repeatable bulk optimization workflows for large websites, content libraries, sitemaps, URL lists, spreadsheets, and CMS exports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided corpora may contain business-sensitive content that should not be shared with an agent. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the corpus is intended to be shared before analysis and avoid including content outside the approved scope. <br>
Risk: Bulk GEO recommendations can affect many pages if applied directly to a CMS or site. <br>
Mitigation: Keep final CMS or site changes behind normal review, staging, sampling, and rollback controls. <br>
