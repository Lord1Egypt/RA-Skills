Mitigation: Verify current platform rules, commission details, and target-market requirements before publishing generated content. <br>
Risk: Generated product claims or affiliate disclosures could be inaccurate or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review product claims, refresh expired links, and clearly disclose promotional or affiliate links before publication. <br>
