## Description: <br>
Generate complaint letters, 12315 filings, and escalation paths for consumer disputes in China. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users with consumer disputes in China use this skill to turn dispute facts into complaint letters, platform complaints, 12315 filings, evidence checklists, escalation steps, and risk notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Consumer-rights guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or mismatched to the user's facts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated documents and cited legal provisions against current official sources before filing or relying on outcome estimates. <br>
Risk: Dispute materials may include personal, payment, merchant, or order details. <br>
Mitigation: Include only information needed for the complaint and share generated documents only with intended merchants, platforms, regulators, or legal advisers. <br>
