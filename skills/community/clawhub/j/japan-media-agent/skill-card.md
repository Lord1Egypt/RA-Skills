## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may help manage connected social-media publishing accounts and move content from draft to live states. <br>
Mitigation: Keep API keys and account credentials local, and review the exact account, copy, media, schedule, and draft/live state before approving publication. <br>
Risk: Deletes, queue cancellation, and platform setting changes can have high-impact effects on connected accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Require separate confirmation for destructive or settings-changing actions, and prefer reversible draft or staging workflows before live changes. <br>
Risk: Generated Japanese copy and platform strategy may include inaccurate market, timing, or tone assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer check local relevance, brand voice, seasonality, hashtags, and platform constraints before publishing. <br>
