## Description: <br>
Social Worker helps agents provide guidance on social services, welfare resources, community support programs, and advocacy for vulnerable individuals and families. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and support agents use this skill to identify relevant social services, welfare benefits, community resources, and advocacy steps for vulnerable individuals or families. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Social-service guidance can be jurisdiction-specific, incomplete, or inappropriate for urgent, legal, medical, or crisis situations. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the user's location, keep guidance informational, and direct urgent, legal, medical, or crisis needs to trusted local authorities, hotlines, or professionals. <br>
Risk: Users seeking assistance may share unnecessary sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Request only the information needed to identify relevant resources and avoid collecting unnecessary sensitive personal data. <br>
