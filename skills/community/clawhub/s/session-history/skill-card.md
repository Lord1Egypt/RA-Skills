## Description: <br>
Search and browse past conversation history across all sessions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[amor71](https://clawhub.ai/user/amor71) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to find prior OpenClaw conversations, resume dropped threads, and summarize relevant past decisions without dumping raw transcripts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Searching past session transcripts can expose sensitive or private conversation history. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only after an explicit request to look up prior chats, avoid shared or logged terminals, and review results for secrets or private content before reusing or sharing them. <br>
