## Description: <br>
Search for, research, and verify non-tech founders on LinkedIn to identify high-value prospects for technology services (Web Dev, ERP, CRM, App Dev, SEO, AI). Generates professional PDF reports. Use when asked to find potential clients or leads. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harshhmaniya](https://clawhub.ai/user/harshhmaniya) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, business development, and agency teams use this skill to find non-technical founders on LinkedIn, assess their company websites, and prepare qualified prospect reports for technology services outreach. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can collect and compile prospect information that may include sensitive personal or business data. <br>
Mitigation: Set clear limits for geography, lead count, acceptable sources, and personal contact details before use; review or delete generated reports when they contain sensitive prospect data. <br>
Risk: LinkedIn-oriented prospect research can produce outreach assumptions that are incomplete, stale, or inappropriate for a specific prospect. <br>
Mitigation: Review each lead, website analysis, and pitch idea before outreach, and verify prospect details against approved sources. <br>
