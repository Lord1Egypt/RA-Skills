## Description: <br>
Generate a pack of professional or aesthetic photos from a single reference image while preserving the exact identity of the person. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Anil-matcha](https://clawhub.ai/user/Anil-matcha) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to generate themed professional, lifestyle, or aesthetic photo packs from a reference image while keeping the subject's identity consistent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded face images are sensitive and are processed through the platform's media-generation service. <br>
Mitigation: Use only photos the user owns or has consent to process, and avoid highly sensitive face images unless the provider's privacy and retention terms are understood. <br>
Risk: Generated professional or lifestyle images may be mistaken for unedited photographs. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated images before use and avoid presenting synthetic images in contexts where authenticity, credentials, or identity proof are required. <br>
