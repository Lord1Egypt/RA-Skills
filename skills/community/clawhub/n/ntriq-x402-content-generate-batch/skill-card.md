## Description: <br>
Batch AI content generation for up to 500 prompts with flat $6.00 USDC payment via x402. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ntriq-gh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ntriq-gh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External developers and content teams use this skill to make paid x402 requests for batch generation of blog, email, social, product, report, or ad copy across up to 500 prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompt text is sent to Ntriq's remote service for generation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential or regulated prompts unless the provider and its privacy practices are approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Each batch request requires a $6.00 USDC x402 payment authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Review payment headers, wallet/network settings, and spending approval before making requests. <br>
