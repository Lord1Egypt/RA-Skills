## Description: <br>
Turn rough project notes into polished portfolio case studies with metrics, visuals checklist, and interviewer talking points. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[52YuanChangXing](https://clawhub.ai/user/52YuanChangXing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Portfolio creators, designers, developers, and job candidates use this skill to turn rough project notes into structured case study drafts, visuals checklists, interview talking points, and FAQ material. It helps organize evidence and mark assumptions without fabricating metrics, credentials, or outcomes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project notes, screenshots, or links may contain confidential or sensitive portfolio material. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the intended audience and sharing scope before including sensitive details in drafts or checklists. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may route unrelated portfolio or interview requests to this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants a portfolio case study workflow before proceeding when the trigger is ambiguous. <br>
Risk: The helper script writes a scaffold file to the selected output path. <br>
Mitigation: Choose an explicit output filename and review whether an existing file could be overwritten before running the script. <br>
