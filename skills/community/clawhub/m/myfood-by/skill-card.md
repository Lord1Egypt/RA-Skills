## Description: <br>
A food inventory and meal recommendation skill that records pantry items and optional expiration dates, asks for dietary needs at recommendation time, prioritizes existing ingredients, marks inventory ingredients in recipes, and highlights items nearing expiration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[BiyaoShang](https://clawhub.ai/user/BiyaoShang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to maintain a local food inventory, check item freshness, and get meal suggestions that prioritize ingredients they already have. The skill is intended for food planning and pantry management, not health record keeping or medical advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill keeps a local food inventory file and may update or remove entries when the user asks it to manage pantry items. <br>
Mitigation: Review the inventory file periodically and confirm item names or expiration dates before relying on recommendations or deleting records. <br>
Risk: Dietary restrictions, allergies, medical conditions, or other sensitive health details could be entered into persistent inventory notes by mistake. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive health details out of the inventory file and provide dietary constraints only in the current conversation when requesting recommendations. <br>
Risk: Meal suggestions are generated by the host model and may be incomplete, unsuitable, or inconsistent with a user's real dietary or medical needs. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as planning guidance, verify ingredients and nutrition-sensitive choices, and consult qualified professionals for medical or allergy-critical decisions. <br>
