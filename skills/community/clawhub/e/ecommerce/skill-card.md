## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal, fiscal, pricing, platform-cost, and marketing benchmark guidance may be outdated or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current platform costs, tax rules, legal requirements, and benchmark assumptions with authoritative sources before relying on them for a live store. <br>
Risk: Payment, webhook, inventory, and checkout code examples may need adaptation before production use. <br>
Mitigation: Review, test, and security-check production payment code, including webhook signatures, idempotency, server-side pricing, and stock updates. <br>
