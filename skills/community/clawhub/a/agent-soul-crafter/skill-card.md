## Description: <br>
Design compelling AI agent personalities with structured SOUL.md templates covering tone, rules, expertise, and response behavior. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[neal-collab](https://clawhub.ai/user/neal-collab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent builders use this skill to draft and review SOUL.md templates that define an AI agent's identity, personality traits, expertise, communication style, response length, and safety boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A generated SOUL.md could give a downstream agent broad tool, memory, posting, financial, health-related, or sensitive-data authority without enough review. <br>
Mitigation: Review each SOUL.md before deployment and restrict downstream agent permissions to the intended domain and authority level. <br>
Risk: A personality template may omit clear boundaries or routing rules, causing inconsistent or overbroad agent behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included quality checklist to verify identity, response length, domain limits, routing, and hard safety rules before use. <br>
