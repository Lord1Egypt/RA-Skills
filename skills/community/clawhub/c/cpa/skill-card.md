## Description: <br>
Prepare for the CPA exam with section-order strategy, 18-month window tracking, score analysis, and state eligibility guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External CPA exam candidates, retakers, working professionals, international candidates, tutors, and supporting family members use this skill to plan section order, track pass windows and NTS deadlines, analyze scores, and maintain study progress records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CPA study records in ~/cpa/ may contain exam progress, scores, NTS deadlines, or other personal study information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep ~/cpa/ private and avoid saving unnecessary score reports or personal identifiers. <br>
Risk: Calendar access or shared progress summaries could expose candidate schedule or student-progress data. <br>
Mitigation: Approve calendar access only when needed and require explicit candidate consent before sharing progress with parents, tutors, or others. <br>
