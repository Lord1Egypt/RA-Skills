## Description: <br>
Generates detailed IT disaster recovery plans with RTO/RPO targets, failover procedures, backup strategies, communication plans, testing schedules, cost modeling, and compliance mapping. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Operations, infrastructure, security, and compliance teams use this skill to create actionable disaster recovery plans for business-critical systems. It helps document recovery tiers, backup and failover procedures, incident communications, testing cadence, cost expectations, and compliance mappings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated failover, backup, DNS, or cloud CLI examples may not match the user's actual environment or change-control requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review and test all generated procedures in a controlled environment before using them in production. <br>
Risk: Planning prompts for disaster recovery can accidentally include secrets, credentials, or sensitive infrastructure details. <br>
Mitigation: Remove live credentials and secrets from prompts, and provide only the minimum environment details needed for planning. <br>
