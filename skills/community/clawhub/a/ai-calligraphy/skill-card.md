## Description: <br>
Ai Calligraphy provides Chinese calligraphy guidance for brush and hard-pen practice, covering major scripts, posture, strokes, structure, composition, copybook study, tools, inscriptions, seals, exhibitions, and competitions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, instructors, and calligraphy enthusiasts use this skill to receive guidance on Chinese calligraphy fundamentals, five major scripts, brush and hard-pen practice, materials, composition, and preparation for exhibitions or competitions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Calligraphy guidance may be incorrect or mismatched to a learner's hand, tools, paper, or competition requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against trusted teachers, copybooks, exhibition rules, and the user's actual practice context before relying on them. <br>
