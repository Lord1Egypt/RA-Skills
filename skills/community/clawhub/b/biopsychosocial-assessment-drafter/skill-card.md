## Description: <br>
Drafts a biopsychosocial-spiritual assessment for licensed social work review, covering intake domains, strengths, safety screening, provisional DSM-5-TR category prompts, and service recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External social work practitioners, interns, case managers, and supervisors use this skill to turn intake notes, forms, or conversation summaries into a structured draft BPSS assessment. The output is intended for licensed social worker review and signature before clinical, billing, or legal use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles sensitive social work intake information that may include PHI. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in authorized intake documentation workflows, avoid unnecessary direct identifiers, and follow organizational HIPAA and privacy rules. <br>
Risk: Drafted content could be mistaken for a final clinical assessment, diagnosis, billing record, or legal document. <br>
Mitigation: Treat every output as a draft requiring licensed clinical review, editing, signature, and professional judgment before any clinical, billing, or legal use. <br>
Risk: Safety risk or mandatory reporting information may require urgent professional action. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate positive safety screens and abuse or neglect concerns to a licensed clinician immediately before relying on other draft content. <br>
