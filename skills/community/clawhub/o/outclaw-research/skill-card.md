## Description: <br>
Deeply researches a specific person or organization for B2B outreach by using available outreach and web research tools and writing a sourced, persistent profile into the OutClaw knowledge base. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[milstan](https://clawhub.ai/user/milstan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, recruiting, and business-development users use this skill to research named people, companies, or prospects and persist sourced outreach profiles. It is intended to support lead discovery, enrichment, relationship context, and follow-up planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can search private messages and notes, extract contact details, and store person or company profiles persistently. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in an approved sales or recruiting environment with clear consent, retention, deletion, and access-control rules. <br>
Risk: Broad tool access can expose personal messaging or notes data that may not be needed for a given research task. <br>
Mitigation: Restrict the active tool inventory to approved business sources and disable personal messaging and notes access unless explicitly required. <br>
Risk: Lead enrichment and contact discovery can involve purchases or spending through connected services. <br>
Mitigation: Define spending rules before deployment and review helper scripts and workflows that perform enrichment or batch lead processing. <br>
