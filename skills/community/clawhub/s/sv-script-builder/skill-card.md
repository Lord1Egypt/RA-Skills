## Description: <br>
Creates timed, structured short-video scripts using proven frameworks with hooks, visual notes, and CTA options for content creators and marketers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, educators, and scriptwriters use this skill to turn topics, drafts, or long-form material into short-video scripts with hooks, timing, visual cues, and calls to action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may apply a short-video script-builder format to broad video-writing prompts when another workflow is intended. <br>
Mitigation: Ask explicitly for a different workflow or review and revise the generated structure before use. <br>
Risk: Generated scripts may be inaccurate, unsuitable for the audience, or off-brand if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and have the creator review facts, tone, claims, and calls to action before publishing. <br>
