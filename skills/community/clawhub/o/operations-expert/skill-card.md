## Description: <br>
Cross-platform content strategy and operations skill for planning topics, structuring briefs, drafting or rewriting publish-ready posts, adapting content across major platforms, building editorial calendars, and reviewing performance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content teams, brand operators, merchants, and creators use this skill to turn messy business context into content briefs, platform-ready copy, calendars, cross-platform adaptations, and content performance recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may contain unsupported claims if the source brief includes assumptions or incomplete facts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content for factual grounding, product claims, customer proof, and performance claims before publishing. <br>
Risk: Helper scripts read local input files selected by the user for brief preparation or output validation. <br>
Mitigation: Run helper scripts only on files intended for content brief processing and avoid passing sensitive or unrelated local files. <br>
