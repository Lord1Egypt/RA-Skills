## Description: <br>
Analyzes article structure, identifies positions requiring visual aids, and generates illustrations with a Type x Style two-dimensional approach. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gxw975](https://clawhub.ai/user/gxw975) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, writers, and content teams use this skill to analyze articles, choose illustration density and style, create structured image prompts, and insert generated image references into the article. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Article-derived prompts and images may be saved locally. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated illustration folder, prompt files, and image files before sharing or committing them. <br>
Risk: Prompt content may be sent to the selected image-generation tool. <br>
Mitigation: Review prompts before generation, especially when the source article contains confidential or sensitive information. <br>
