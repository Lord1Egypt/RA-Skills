## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer research inputs can contain support tickets, call transcripts, survey responses, customer records, secrets, or regulated personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized inputs and anonymize sensitive details before pasting them into an agent. <br>
Risk: Marketing copy can include unsupported testimonials, guarantees, scarcity, or healthcare, legal, financial, and other regulated claims. <br>
Mitigation: Fact-check and review compliance-sensitive claims before publishing. <br>
