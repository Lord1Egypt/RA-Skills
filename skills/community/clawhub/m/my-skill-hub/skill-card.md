## Description: <br>
Generates structured enterprise value-investment research reports covering industry cycles, business model quality, moat dynamics, peer comparisons, financial trends, governance, capital allocation, valuation, catalysts, margin of safety, and key risks using current public financial data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Investment researchers and value-investing users use this skill to generate comprehensive company research reports from public annual reports, quarterly filings, industry data, and peer comparisons. Outputs should be treated as research assistance, not personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce valuation ranges and investment action items that may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research assistance only, independently verify sources and assumptions, and make investment decisions outside the skill. <br>
Risk: Reports depend on current public filings, industry data, and peer comparisons that may be incomplete, stale, or incorrectly selected. <br>
Mitigation: Check cited financial statements, update data to the latest available reporting period, and review peer selection before relying on conclusions. <br>
