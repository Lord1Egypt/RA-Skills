## Description: <br>
Designs AR/VR/WebAR/3D virtual showroom and immersive shopping plans for high-visual, high-AOV product stores, including strategy, asset specifications, on-site paths, content scripts, KPI tracking, experiments, and a Rijoy feedback loop. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketing, ecommerce, growth, 3D production, and implementation teams use this skill to plan immersive shopping experiences for premium visual products and connect those experiences to measurable conversion, lead, return-rate, feedback, and loyalty workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing plans may include customer tagging, follow-up incentives, or loyalty workflows that process customer behavior or feedback data. <br>
Mitigation: Add clear notice, obtain consent where required, minimize collected data, define retention and deletion rules, and complete legal review before implementation. <br>
Risk: Immersive shopping guidance can overstate business lift if teams treat AR/3D engagement as proof of incremental revenue. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's event definitions, dashboard metrics, and A/B experiment plans to validate conversion, lead quality, and return-rate impact before scaling. <br>
Risk: The asset manifest validator checks fields and naming only, not 3D geometry quality, real-world scale accuracy, material fidelity, load performance, or AR placement behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Pair manifest validation with asset QA for scale, PBR materials, mobile load performance, interaction stability, and AR placement sign-off. <br>
