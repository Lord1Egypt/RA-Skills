# App Store Listing Generator — Marketing & Revenue Analysis

## Market Opportunity

- **6.5M+ apps** across iOS App Store (2M+) and Google Play (3.5M+)
- **30M+ registered Apple developers**, 10M+ Android developers
- Every app needs listings — and every app update is a chance to optimize
- App store search drives **65% of downloads** (Apple data) — ASO is not optional
- Top-ranked apps convert at **35%+**; unoptimized apps convert at **1-3%**

## Target Buyers

1. **Indie developers** — built an app, don't know how to write copy ($0 budget, but need this)
2. **Mobile app studios** — manage 5-50 apps, need templates for volume ($29 is trivial)
3. **Growth marketers** — running ASO for clients, need repeatable process
4. **Digital agencies** — adding ASO to service menu, need tools
5. **SaaS founders going mobile** — desktop product, first mobile app (high willingness to pay)
