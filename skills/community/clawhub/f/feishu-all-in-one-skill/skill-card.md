## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad Feishu workspace permissions can expose or change sensitive documents, messages, calendars, approvals, attendance records, files, and meeting content. <br>
Mitigation: Install only from a trusted publisher, grant the minimum required workspace scopes, and prefer read-only permissions unless a workflow truly requires write access. <br>
Risk: The skill advertises modifying actions such as updating documents, sending messages, creating calendar items, moving files, changing approvals or attendance records, and recording or transcribing meetings. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before each modifying action and confirm targets, recipients, files, dates, records, and meeting details before execution. <br>
Risk: The artifact does not clearly define user-facing safety boundaries for high-impact workplace actions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill in trusted workspaces with review or audit procedures for sensitive operations, especially enterprise-wide or private workspace data. <br>
