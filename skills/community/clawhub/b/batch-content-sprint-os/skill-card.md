## Description: <br>
Plan and run weekly batch content production for creators with clear priorities, time blocks, and output targets. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, content teams, and marketing operators use this skill to turn weekly goals, team bandwidth, and channel priorities into an executable content sprint with time blocks, buffers, guardrails, and carryover notes. <br>
