## Description: <br>
Scans and categorizes files in a directory, detects duplicates, and reorganizes them into a clear folder structure with safety features and rollback options. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[underbench2-gif](https://clawhub.ai/user/underbench2-gif) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and end users can use this skill to scan cluttered directories, classify files, identify duplicates, and reorganize files into a traceable folder structure without deleting data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Incorrect classification or organization rules can move files into unexpected locations and disrupt workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Review the dry-run plan before applying changes, start with a non-critical folder, and keep backups for important directories. <br>
Risk: Duplicate handling may group files that still need manual review before cleanup. <br>
Mitigation: Keep duplicates in the dedicated duplicates folder for manual inspection rather than deleting them automatically. <br>
