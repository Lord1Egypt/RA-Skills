## Description: <br>
AI fencing guidance for foil, epee, and sabre fundamentals, rules, equipment, fitness, tactics, and video-based coaching support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and fencing learners use this skill for general fencing guidance across foil, epee, and sabre, including technique, rules, equipment, tactics, fitness training, and optional video analysis prompts. Its advice should be treated as support rather than a substitute for a qualified coach, current federation rules, or safety supervision. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fencing technique, rules, equipment, or training advice may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific athlete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat guidance as general support and confirm important decisions with a qualified coach, current federation rules, and appropriate safety supervision. <br>
Risk: Uploaded fencing videos may contain personal or sensitive imagery. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only videos the user is comfortable sharing with the agent for analysis. <br>
