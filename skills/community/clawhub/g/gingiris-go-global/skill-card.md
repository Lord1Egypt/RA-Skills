## Description: <br>
Gingiris Go Global is a full-lifecycle go-to-market playbook for AI product and SaaS teams expanding internationally, from market validation and positioning through launch channels, conversion, and monetization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders, growth operators, product managers, and agents use this skill to plan and review international expansion for AI products and SaaS businesses. It provides structured guidance for market validation, competitor research, positioning, first-user acquisition, launch execution, SEO/GEO, and monetization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad GTM or launch-related prompts may activate the playbook when a narrower skill would be more appropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Review whether the user is asking for full international expansion strategy before applying the playbook. <br>
Risk: Platform-promotion tactics can violate community rules or become spammy if followed mechanically. <br>
Mitigation: Check each platform's rules and adapt outreach, posting, and launch tactics before acting on the guidance. <br>
