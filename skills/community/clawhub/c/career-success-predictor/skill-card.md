## Description: <br>
Career fit assessment through 10 occupation-specific questions that estimates how likely a user is to succeed in a target career and produces a short narrative report. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tonytan10086](https://clawhub.ai/user/tonytan10086) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to run a structured self-assessment for a specific target career, career switch, or job fit question. It asks tailored multiple-choice questions, separates fit from readiness, and turns the user's answers into a concise career-planning report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary personal identifiers or sensitive career context during the assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing unnecessary personal identifiers and keep answers limited to information needed for career fit assessment. <br>
Risk: The skill can create local reports or conversation-log files that contain personal career information. <br>
Mitigation: Review any generated local report or conversation-log files before storing or sharing them. <br>
Risk: Broad activation could start a career assessment when the user only intended a casual career discussion. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to confirm before starting the assessment workflow. <br>
