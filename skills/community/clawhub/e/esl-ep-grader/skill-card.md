## Description: <br>
Batch-grade English written homework for Chinese elementary students in Grades 3-6, with rubric-based scoring, error annotations, and age-appropriate feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Teachers, tutors, parents, and learning-support agents use this skill to grade elementary ESL written work, including sentences, short paragraphs, dialogues, reading comprehension answers, and workbook exercises. It supports configurable grade level, assignment type, max score, rubric, and feedback language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Student homework submissions may include unnecessary personal details about children. <br>
Mitigation: Remove names, contact details, school identifiers, and other unnecessary personal information before submitting work for grading. <br>
Risk: Feedback may be produced in Chinese by default when another language is expected. <br>
Mitigation: Set feedback_lang to en or bilingual when English or bilingual feedback is required. <br>
