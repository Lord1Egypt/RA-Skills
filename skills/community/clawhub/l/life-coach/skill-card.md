## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language life-coaching skill that responds with emotional support first, then uses GROW-style reflection to help users clarify situations, decisions, goals, and personal blockers without acting as crisis intervention, diagnosis, therapy, legal advice, or financial advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhangyuqi98](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhangyuqi98) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Chinese-language reflective coaching, emotional support, goal clarification, decision framing, and lightweight planning. It is intended for ordinary life and productivity concerns, not urgent safety situations or regulated professional advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat reflective coaching as crisis, medical, psychological, legal, or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill limited to ordinary life coaching and redirect urgent, self-harm, violence, diagnosis, therapy, legal, or financial situations to emergency services or qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Life-coaching responses can still produce misleading or overconfident personal guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for appropriate boundaries, user autonomy, and non-diagnostic language before relying on them for sensitive decisions. <br>
