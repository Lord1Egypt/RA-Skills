## Description: <br>
Guides an agent to write Chinese deep commentary in an observer style that combines rational restraint, sharp irony, interdisciplinary analysis, and concrete analogies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[frankski818](https://clawhub.ai/user/frankski818) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, writers, and content teams use this skill to guide Chinese analytical commentary, reading notes, and cross-domain essays with a structured observer voice. It is intended for deep commentary on topics such as football, economics, technology, and social observation, not news reporting, technical documentation, marketing copy, or academic papers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The style can make generated writing more opinionated, rhetorically sharp, or analogy-heavy. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for balance, factual accuracy, and tone before publication. <br>
Risk: The artifact defines unsuitable scenarios including news reporting, technical documentation, marketing copy, and academic papers. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for deep commentary and analysis rather than genres where neutral reporting or formal precision is required. <br>
