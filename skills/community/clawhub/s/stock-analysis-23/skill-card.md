## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through educational stock technical analysis using 23 Tongdaxin-style indicators, multi-indicator scoring, signal resonance checks, and risk-control guidance. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to ask an agent for educational technical-analysis reports, screening logic, buy/sell signal interpretation, and risk-control considerations for A-share, Hong Kong, and U.S. stocks. Outputs should be treated as research support rather than investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading-style suggestions may be inaccurate, stale, or unsuitable for a user's financial situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational research support, independently verify market data and calculations, and do not use the skill as a substitute for qualified financial advice. <br>
Risk: The supplied security guidance notes that parts of the reference implementation are placeholders while the skill describes a complete 23-indicator system. <br>
Mitigation: Validate the implemented indicators, formulas, data sources, and scoring behavior before relying on results for analysis workflows. <br>
Risk: Technical indicators can lag current market conditions and historical patterns may not predict future performance. <br>
Mitigation: Combine any signal with current market data, broader research, risk limits, and human review before making decisions. <br>
