## Description: <br>
Use when a recruiter wants to batch-evaluate resumes, run general resume quality checks, optionally match resumes to a JD and company background, and produce evidence-backed Markdown and CSV review reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[debtvc2022](https://clawhub.ai/user/debtvc2022) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Recruiters and HR reviewers use this skill to batch-process local resumes, optionally compare them against a job description and company background, and generate review reports for human decision support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Candidate resumes, JD text, and company background may be sent to the configured external model provider. <br>
Mitigation: Use only organization-approved model endpoints and API keys, confirm provider retention terms, and redact candidate PII before processing real applicants. <br>
Risk: Raw JSON output may retain sensitive candidate details when enabled. <br>
Mitigation: Review config/evaluation.yaml before use and disable raw JSON output when retention of model response details is not required. <br>
Risk: Resume scores and buckets could be misread as final hiring or rejection decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the reports only as recruiter review aids and require human review before any employment decision. <br>
