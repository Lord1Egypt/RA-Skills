## Description: <br>
Produces a sermon outline from a scripture passage or theme, ready for preaching. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cheniOS](https://clawhub.ai/user/cheniOS) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External pastors, preachers, and ministry workers use this skill to turn a scripture passage or theme into a preach-ready sermon outline with introduction, main points, applications, conclusion, and scripture references. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Default ESV and Evangelical assumptions may not match the user's preferred Bible translation or church tradition. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the desired translation and theological tradition when invoking the skill. <br>
Risk: Generated sermon outlines may need pastoral and theological review before preaching. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited passages, applications, and doctrinal framing against the passage context and local ministry standards before use. <br>
