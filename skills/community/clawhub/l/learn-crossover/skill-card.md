## Description: <br>
Helps agents explain unfamiliar concepts by first asking what the user already knows, then mapping the new concept to confirmed prior knowledge, structural analogies, and recurring meta-patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[li-evan](https://clawhub.ai/user/li-evan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners and agents use this skill when a user is trying to understand an unfamiliar concept, technology, algorithm, or field. It structures the explanation around user-confirmed prior knowledge, field-level analogy tables, and a short statement of what remains genuinely new. <br>
