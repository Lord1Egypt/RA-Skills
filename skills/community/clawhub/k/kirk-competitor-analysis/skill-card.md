## Description: <br>
Analyzes competitor SEO and GEO strategies, including ranking keywords, content approaches, backlink profiles, and AI citation patterns, to identify opportunities to outperform competitors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, SEO practitioners, content strategists, and growth teams use this skill to compare their site against competitors, analyze keyword, content, backlink, technical SEO, and AI citation patterns, and produce actionable competitive intelligence. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Connected SEO, analytics, Search Console, or AI-monitoring integrations may expose sensitive business, site-performance, or competitor-analysis data. <br>
Mitigation: Use manual-data mode when connector access is not needed, and grant only the minimum integration access required for the analysis. <br>
Risk: Broad prompts such as "who ranks for" can trigger analysis without enough market, site, or search-ranking context. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's site, target market, competitor set, analysis scope, and data sources before producing recommendations. <br>
