# 字体合集

字体资源合集：免费商用字体库、自托管方案、字体配对工具与字体在用案例。更多顶选见 [SKILL.md](../SKILL.md)。

## 免费字体库

- [Font Squirrel](https://www.fontsquirrel.com/) — 100% free for commercial use
- [DaFont](https://www.dafont.com/) — Archive of freely downloadable fonts
- [1001 Free Fonts](https://www.1001freefonts.com/) — Large free font archive
- [Befonts](https://befonts.com/) — High-quality fonts for free

## 精选开源字体

- [Open Foundry](https://open-foundry.com) — Curated open-source typefaces
- [The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/) — Open-source type foundry
- [Use & Modify](https://usemodify.com/) — Curated free and open-source typefaces
- [Bunny Fonts](https://fonts.bunny.net/) — Privacy-focused Google Fonts alternative (GDPR)
- [Lexend](https://www.lexend.com/) — Variable font shown to improve reading proficiency

## 配对与灵感

- [FontPair](https://fontpair.co/) — Helps you pair Google Fonts together
- [Fontjoy](https://fontjoy.com/) — Generate font pairings in one click
- [Typewolf](https://www.typewolf.com/) — What's trending in type
- [Fonts In Use](https://fontsinuse.com/) — Public archive of typography in use
