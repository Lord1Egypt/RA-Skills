# Tweet Writer: 8 Hook Frameworks

Source: [@stijnnoorman](https://x.com/stijnnoorman/status/2018349578672611520) (50K+ followers)

---

## 1. Extreme Hook

Social media rewards extremes. Use superlatives to grab attention, then overdeliver.

**Keywords:** best, biggest, smartest, worst, fastest, easiest

**Examples:**
- "The best productivity system I've ever used"
- "The worst mistake founders make"
- "The fastest way to get 1000 followers"

---

## 2. AIDA Hook

**A**ttention → **I**nterest → **D**esire → **A**ction

People care about what's in it for them. Get them interested first, then deliver value.

**Structure:**
1. Grab attention (bold claim)
2. Build interest (what they'll learn)
3. Create desire (the outcome)
4. Call to action (keep reading)

**Example:**
"I 10x'd my productivity in 30 days. Here's the exact system I used (steal it):"

---

## 3. PAS Hook

**P**roblem → **A**gitate → **S**olution

Make them aware of a problem they have. Agitate the pain. Then solve it.

**Structure:**
1. State the problem
2. Twist the knife (why it hurts)
3. Offer the solution

**Example:**
"You're losing 2 hours a day to email. That's 730 hours a year. Here's how I got it to 15 minutes:"

---

## 4. BAB Hook

**B**efore → **A**fter → **B**ridge

People love transformations. Show where you were, where you are now, and how you got there.

**Structure:**
1. Before (the struggle)
2. After (the result)
3. Bridge (how you transformed)

**Example:**
"6 months ago: Drowning in tasks, working 12hr days.
Today: 4hr workdays, 3x the output.
The bridge? A single system change:"

---

## 5. Emotional Hook

Emotions keep attention. Write about emotional events — struggles, wins, fears, breakthroughs.

**Emotions that work:**
- Frustration → Relief
- Fear → Confidence
- Failure → Comeback
- Doubt → Certainty

**Example:**
"I almost gave up on my startup last Tuesday. Then a stranger's DM changed everything:"

---

## 6. Results Hook

If you achieved their goals, write about it. Numbers and specifics build authority.

**Structure:**
1. State the result (specific numbers)
2. Timeframe
3. How you did it

**Example:**
"$47,000 MRR in 8 months. No funding. No team. Just these 5 systems:"

---

## 7. Client Hook

Social proof > self-promotion. If you helped someone else, write about it.

**Structure:**
1. Client's before state
2. What you did together
3. Their results
4. The lesson

**Example:**
"My client went from 200 to 15,000 followers in 90 days. Here's exactly what we changed:"

---

## 8. Idea Hook

One-liners that hit. If a tweet does well, save it — use it later as a hook for that topic.

**Process:**
1. Post one-liner observations
2. Track which ones hit
3. Expand winning one-liners into threads
4. Use the one-liner as the hook

**Example one-liners:**
- "Your calendar is a to-do list with time slots."
- "Most 'productivity tips' are just procrastination with extra steps."
- "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough."

## Quick Reference

| Hook | Best For | Key Element |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Extreme | Bold claims, listicles | Superlatives |
| AIDA | Educational content | Self-interest |
| PAS | Problem-solving posts | Pain points |
| BAB | Transformation stories | Before/after contrast |
| Emotional | Personal stories | Feelings |
| Results | Authority building | Specific numbers |
| Client | Social proof | Third-party success |
| Idea | Testing topics | Punchy one-liners |

---

## Combining Hooks

Best tweets often combine 2+ frameworks:

**Extreme + Results:**
"The best investment I ever made: $99 course → $47K MRR"

**PAS + BAB:**
"You're wasting 10hrs/week on tasks AI can do.
I was too. Now I spend 30 minutes.
Here's the before/after:"

**Emotional + Client:**
"A founder DM'd me in tears last month. Her startup was dying.
Today she just hit $10K MRR.
What changed:"

**Extreme + AIDA:**
"The fastest way to 10K followers (that nobody talks about). Here's the exact playbook:"

**Results + Client:**
"Helped 3 founders hit $10K MRR this month. The common thread? This one system:"

## Hook Selection Guide

**Choose based on your content:**

| Content Type | Primary Hook | Secondary Hook |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| Tutorial/How-to | AIDA | Extreme |
| Case study | Client | Results |
| Personal story | Emotional | BAB |
| Listicle | Extreme | AIDA |
| Controversial take | Extreme | PAS |
| Transformation | BAB | Results |
| Quick tip | Idea | Extreme |
| Problem-solving | PAS | AIDA |
