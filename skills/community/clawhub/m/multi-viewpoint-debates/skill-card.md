## Description: <br>
Spawns isolated sub-agents representing distinct worldviews to debate decisions, expose blind spots, and archive outputs for future reference. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[LatentFreedom](https://clawhub.ai/user/LatentFreedom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to stress-test decisions by collecting independent perspectives from multiple persona-based sub-agents, then saving the results as searchable Markdown debate records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Debate topics, supporting context, and transcript excerpts may include sensitive information that is shared with multiple sub-agents or retained in local archives. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid secrets, credentials, customer data, regulated information, and sensitive internal URLs; redact sensitive excerpts before archiving or sharing. <br>
Risk: Archived debate records can retain outdated or sensitive decision context longer than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Delete old debate archives when they are no longer needed and review archive contents before sharing them. <br>
