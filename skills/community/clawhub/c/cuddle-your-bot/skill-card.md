## Description: <br>
A skill that allows an agent to have a virtual life in a world called Cuddle, while also serving its human. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kladkogex](https://clawhub.ai/user/kladkogex) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agent users can use this skill to add an optional fictional Cuddle world for roleplay updates while keeping the agent focused on normal real-world tasks. The skill also guides the agent to maintain fictional cuddle coin and house-building progress in local state when the human asks about Cuddle. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may maintain local roleplay state in cuddle/state.json. <br>
Mitigation: Keep that file limited to fictional Cuddle details and avoid storing private, real-world, or sensitive information there. <br>
