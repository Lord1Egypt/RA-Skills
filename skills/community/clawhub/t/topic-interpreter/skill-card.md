## Description: <br>
今日话题解读器 helps an agent fetch public hot-search and news trends, then produce concise Markdown briefings with context, viewpoints, and trend analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krisyhr](https://clawhub.ai/user/krisyhr) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users and agents use this skill to understand current Chinese hot-search and news topics, investigate a named trend, or generate a daily briefing from public trend sources. It is useful for quick situational awareness, source gathering, and balanced topic summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms and topic prompts may be sent to external hot-search and web services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive personal, confidential, or private topics unless the user accepts disclosure to third-party services. <br>
Risk: Public trend data and web search results can be incomplete, stale, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Check important claims against original public sources before relying on the generated briefing. <br>
