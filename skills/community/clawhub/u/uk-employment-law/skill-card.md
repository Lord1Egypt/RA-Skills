## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill gives high-stakes UK HR and employment-law calculations or letters that may affect employment decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafting aids only and verify every rate, deadline, entitlement, and letter with GOV.UK, ACAS, or a qualified UK employment adviser before acting. <br>
Risk: Broad triggers and weak safeguards may cause the skill to answer outside a confirmed UK employment context. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the matter is UK-specific before relying on the output, and decline or redirect requests for binding legal advice. <br>
Risk: Employee scenarios may involve sensitive personal, health, immigration, or disciplinary information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering unnecessary identifiers or sensitive details, and redact facts that are not needed for the calculation or letter. <br>
Risk: The security summary flags current-law claims that appear stale and capability metadata unrelated to the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review the release metadata and update legal rates, dates, and platform capability tags before deployment. <br>
