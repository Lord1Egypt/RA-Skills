## The Problem With Generic Chiropractic Marketing

Every AI marketing tool in existence generates the same chiropractic violations:

- **"Board certified chiropractor"** — without naming the certifying board (ACBSP? ACNB? ACCO? There's no generic "chiropractic board certification")
- **"Cured my herniated disc after 8 visits"** — patient testimonial violating FTC 2023 endorsement rules (outcome claims require typical-result disclosure)
- **"Medicare accepted — we cover everything"** — Medicare Part B covers *one* CPT code category for chiropractic. Everything else (massage, TENS, orthotics, maintenance care) is out-of-pocket
- **"We treat back pain, neck pain, disc herniation, and sciatica"** — legitimate chiropractic scope, but paired with medical diagnosis language that creates unauthorized practice exposure
- **"No upfront cost — your settlement covers it"** — PI lien advertising with no disclosure that the lien attaches to settlement proceeds

This skill blocks all of it permanently.

## The 7 Compliance Moats

| Moat | Standard | What It Blocks |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **1. NRS 634 Scope & Title Gate** [ANCHOR] | Nevada Revised Statutes Chapter 634 | "Chiropractic physician," "chiropractic medicine," prescribing claims, surgery claims, systemic disease cure claims |
| **2. NVBCE Advertising Rule Gate** | NAC 634.445–.450 | "Nevada's #1 chiropractor," "board certified" without board named, outcome guarantees, state licensing as quality signal |
| **3. FTC 2023 Testimonial Gate** | 16 CFR Part 255 (revised Oct 2023) | Cure claims, permanent elimination claims, incentivized reviews, extraordinary outcomes without typical-result disclosure |
| **4. HIPAA PHI Gate** | 45 CFR §164.508 | Patient testimonials with identifiable info, before/after photos with faces, case studies that could identify individuals |
| **5. Medicare Coverage Accuracy Gate** | Medicare Part B; OIG Compliance | "Medicare covers everything," maintenance care billing, X-ray coverage claims, non-covered service billing |
| **6. Credential Accuracy Gate** | CCSP/CCEP/DACNB/FICC/ART/Webster | "Board certified" without board named, lapsed credentials, fake specialty titles, unsettled dry needling claims |
| **7. PI/Workers' Comp Lien Advertising Gate** | Nevada NRS 7; FTC Act §5; OIG | Settlement guarantees, attorney relationship implications, Workers' Comp "always covered" claims, Medicare/Medicaid copay waivers |
