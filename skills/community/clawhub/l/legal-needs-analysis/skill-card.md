## Description: <br>
将客户的日常语言陈述转化为结构化法律事实和问题，帮助律师快速识别案件性质及处理方向。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zaj7373](https://clawhub.ai/user/zaj7373) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Chinese-speaking legal professionals use this skill to turn client narratives, chat records, or transcript-style statements into a structured intake analysis. It helps identify parties, timelines, legal issues, evidence leads, missing information, and risks for lawyer review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Client narratives may contain confidential or personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering unnecessary personal data, handle narratives under applicable confidentiality obligations, and check the host platform's retention settings. <br>
Risk: The generated analysis may omit important facts, misclassify a legal issue, or overstate an initial assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the report as a drafting and triage aid; have a qualified lawyer verify facts, evidence, law, and conclusions before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Legal relevance can depend on jurisdiction, current law, limitation periods, and procedural rules. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the applicable jurisdiction and current legal requirements during lawyer review, especially where the client statement is incomplete. <br>
