## Description: <br>
Ai Official Documents helps users draft, revise, and structure Chinese official documents, including notices, reports, requests, approvals, meeting minutes, speeches, work summaries, and research reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users who prepare Chinese official or organizational documents use this skill to generate outlines, drafts, polished language, and format-aware writing guidance for government, public institution, state-owned enterprise, and related workplace scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated official-document content may be incorrect, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific policy, legal, or organizational context. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer verify facts, requirements, formatting, and approvals before using generated content as an official document. <br>
Risk: Users may include confidential government, business, or personal data while asking for document drafting help. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in an agent environment approved for the sensitivity of the information being processed, and avoid unnecessary sensitive details. <br>
