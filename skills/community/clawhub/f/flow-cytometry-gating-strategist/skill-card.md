## Description: <br>
Recommend optimal flow cytometry gating strategies for specific cell types and fluorophores. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AIPOCH-AI](https://clawhub.ai/user/AIPOCH-AI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers, lab analysts, and developers use this skill to generate advisory flow cytometry gating strategies, fluorophore compatibility notes, panel optimization suggestions, compensation notes, and quality-control reminders for specified cell types and fluorophores. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Biological recommendations may be mistaken for validated clinical or experimental protocol decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as advisory research guidance and review it against lab protocols, instrument configuration, and expert judgment before use. <br>
Risk: The script can write JSON output to a path selected with --output. <br>
Mitigation: Choose output paths deliberately and review generated files before using them in downstream workflows. <br>
