## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal, finance, career, and car-ownership content can become inaccurate or misleading if generated claims, policies, statistics, or examples are not verified. <br>
Mitigation: Review all outputs for factual accuracy and confirm claims against official documents, authoritative reports, or other reliable sources before using them in a WeChat draft or publication workflow. <br>
Risk: Publication-oriented writing guidance can produce content that is unsuitable for a specific WeChat account, audience, platform policy, or local compliance requirement. <br>
Mitigation: Apply human editorial review before publication, avoid sensitive topics identified by the skill, and keep the required personal-opinion and reference-only disclaimer where appropriate. <br>
Risk: High-engagement title and hook patterns can encourage overstated claims or fabricated scenarios if used without restraint. <br>
Mitigation: Keep hooks and titles accurate, avoid fictional cases and made-up statistics, and preserve the artifact's requirement to cite real data sources. <br>
