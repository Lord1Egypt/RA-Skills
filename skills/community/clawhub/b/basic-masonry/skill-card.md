## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes practical masonry and concrete advice that can affect structural safety, permits, and user health. <br>
Mitigation: Check local codes and permit requirements, and involve a structural engineer or qualified contractor for structural cracks, tall retaining walls, or permit-sensitive work. <br>
Risk: The artifact mentions used motor oil as a form-release option, and the security evidence identifies this as a safety issue. <br>
Mitigation: Use a commercial concrete form-release product or a manufacturer-approved safer alternative. <br>
Risk: Cutting, grinding, chipping, or demolishing concrete can create silica dust and flying debris. <br>
Mitigation: Use proper silica dust controls, eye protection, and a properly fitted respirator; seek professional help when the work exceeds safe DIY scope. <br>
