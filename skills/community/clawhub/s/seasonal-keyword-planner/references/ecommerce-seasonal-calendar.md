# Ecommerce Seasonal Calendar

The major recurring demand moments for US/Western ecommerce, with the prep window that precedes each. Use as an anchor, then validate against real volume data for the specific keywords and market.

## Month-by-month (Northern Hemisphere / US default)

- **January:** New-Year resolutions (fitness, organization, wellness, planners); winter clearance; "new year new me." *Prep: early December.*
- **February:** Valentine's Day (gifts, jewelry, flowers, lingerie) peaks ~Feb 1–13. *Prep: early-mid January.*
- **March:** Spring transition (rain gear, gardening, allergy, cleaning); Easter (varies Mar–Apr). *Prep: February.*
- **April:** Spring peak; Easter; tax-season spend (US). *Prep: March.*
- **May:** Mother's Day; wedding season ramp; outdoor/grilling begins. *Prep: April.*
- **June:** Father's Day; weddings; summer travel and outdoor gear; Pride. *Prep: May.*
- **July:** Amazon Prime Day (mid-July, big traffic spike across categories); summer peak; back-to-school early search begins. *Prep: June.*
- **August:** Back-to-school peak (supplies, dorm, apparel, electronics). *Prep: July.*
- **September:** Fall transition (jackets, boots); back-to-trail outdoor; Q4 ramp begins. *Prep: August.*
- **October:** Halloween (costumes, decor) peaks; cold-weather apparel climbs; pre-holiday research. *Prep: September.*
- **November:** Black Friday / Cyber Monday — the single largest demand event; deal-intent keywords spike. *Prep: September–October.*
- **December:** Holiday gifting peak (early–mid Dec); last-minute + digital gifts late Dec; New-Year terms begin. *Prep: October–November.*

## Category timing notes

- **Apparel:** weather-led; cold-weather peaks Oct–Dec, swim/summer Apr–Jun.
- **Fitness/wellness:** strong Jan spike; secondary "summer body" Apr–May.
- **Home/decor:** Q4 gifting + Jan organization; spring refresh Mar–Apr.
- **Beauty:** gifting Nov–Dec; Valentine's; Mother's Day; "summer ready" May–Jun.
- **Electronics/toys:** Prime Day + BFCM + December dominate.
- **Outdoor:** spring (Mar–May) and fall (Sep–Oct) shoulders are strongest.

## Major commercial events (validate exact dates each year)

- **Amazon Prime Day** — mid-July (sometimes a second event in Oct).
- **Black Friday / Cyber Monday** — day after US Thanksgiving through the following Monday.
- **Singles' Day (11.11)** — Nov 11, huge in China/APAC.
- **Boxing Day** — Dec 26, strong in UK/AU/CA.
- **Back-to-school** — late July through early September (US).

## Southern Hemisphere adjustment

Flip the weather-driven seasons: cold-weather demand peaks **Jun–Aug**, summer/swim peaks **Dec–Feb**. Holiday gifting (Dec) and New-Year fitness (Jan) still hold, but layer the inverted weather curve on top.
