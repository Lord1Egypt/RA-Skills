## Description: <br>
Geo Metrics Tracker helps teams design ongoing GEO metrics monitoring and alerting systems for AIGVR, Share of Model, citation volume, coverage, dashboards, schemas, and runbooks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[geoly-geo](https://clawhub.ai/user/geoly-geo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, GEO/SEO, analytics, and engineering teams use this skill to turn static GEO reports and AI visibility samples into tracking schemas, dashboards, alert rules, and operational routines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated sampling workflows may lead teams to store raw AI answers, internal logs, or other sensitive source data. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer derived metrics or redacted excerpts, define retention and access controls, and review platform terms and privacy requirements before implementation. <br>
