## Description: <br>
Converts medical text between academic and patient-friendly language while preserving clinical accuracy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Healthcare writers, clinicians, researchers, and developers use this skill to rewrite medical content for academic or patient-friendly audiences, translate jargon, and assess readability while preserving clinical meaning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medical text may contain protected health information. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in approved environments and de-identify patient details where possible. <br>
Risk: Patient-facing rewrites may omit nuance or introduce medical inaccuracies. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified person verify medical accuracy before using outputs with patients. <br>
