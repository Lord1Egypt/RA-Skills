## Description: <br>
Precisely extracts product names from user-provided text while ignoring unrelated details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users apply this skill when they need an agent to identify product names in provided text and return only those names. It is useful for extracting product mentions from descriptions, articles, lists, or conversations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided text may contain sensitive or confidential information processed in the current agent session. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only text appropriate for the current agent session and avoid submitting content that should not be processed there. <br>

## Skill Output: <br>
**Output Type(s):** [text] <br>
**Output Format:** [Plain text with one product name per line, or the exact phrase "No product names detected".] <br>
**Output Parameters:** [1D] <br>
**Other Properties Related to Output:** [Output should contain only product names, with no explanations, titles, or extra formatting.] <br>
