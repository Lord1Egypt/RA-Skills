## Description: <br>
Optimizes LinkedIn profiles for doctors, physicians, nurses, healthcare professionals, and medical researchers by drafting headlines, summaries, healthcare keywords, and personal branding content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External healthcare professionals and agents supporting them use this skill to draft LinkedIn headlines, About sections, experience bullets, keywords, and profile structure while keeping profile claims reviewable. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
