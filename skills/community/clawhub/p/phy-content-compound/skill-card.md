## Description: <br>
Content atom library builder for social media creators that scans markdown and text files, extracts reusable claims, data points, anecdotes, frameworks, contrarian takes, and questions, tags them with source context, and retrieves relevant atoms for new post outlines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and content teams use this skill to scan their own writing archives, build a reusable library of content atoms, and retrieve relevant material when drafting posts on a new topic. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill scans local writing folders and may echo private, unpublished, or client-sensitive content in terminal or JSON output. <br>
Mitigation: Run it only on a dedicated content folder that excludes private journals, client notes, credentials, and other material that should not be summarized or printed. <br>
