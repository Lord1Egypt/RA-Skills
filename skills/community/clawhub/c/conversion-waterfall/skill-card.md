### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Funnel inputs may contain confidential business metrics or customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymized or aggregated metrics where possible and avoid including secrets, personal data, or customer-identifying details. <br>
Risk: Recommendations may rely on incomplete funnel context, unsupported root-cause assumptions, or unverified benchmark comparisons. <br>
Mitigation: Review findings against trusted analytics data, document assumptions, and validate changes with incremental tests before acting on high-impact recommendations. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases such as conversion, funnel, drop-off, or customer journey may activate the skill unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow trigger phrases or invocation rules if accidental activation creates noise in the user's workflow. <br>
