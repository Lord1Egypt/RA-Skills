## Use Case: <br>
Developers, QA leads, and delivery teams use this skill to design risk-based validation plans across unit, HTTP, E2E, WLS, and documentation checks before acceptance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: WLS validation can affect the wrong runtime if default-port or shared-instance commands are used. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed commands, use an isolated test instance on a non-default port, and stop it after testing. <br>
Risk: A generated QA plan can misclassify change risk or omit a needed validation layer. <br>
Mitigation: Review the risk-to-test mapping against changed components and require evidence for mandatory checks before acceptance. <br>
