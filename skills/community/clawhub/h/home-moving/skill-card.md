## Description: <br>
Provides guidance for cross-city moving, local hauling, bulky item packing, and transparent booking estimates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to compare and request moving, hauling, packing, and booking guidance, including service filters, labor estimates, provider credentials, insurance coverage, and booking considerations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate the skill for requests that are not clearly about moving, hauling, packing, or booking services. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the request is about moving-related services before applying the skill's guidance. <br>
Risk: In-home service coordination can involve property access, valuables, and insurance or credential uncertainty. <br>
Mitigation: Advise users to keep someone present during service, secure valuables, and confirm provider credentials and insurance coverage before booking. <br>
