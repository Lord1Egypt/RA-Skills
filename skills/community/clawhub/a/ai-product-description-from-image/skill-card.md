## Description: <br>
Convert local product images (JPG/PNG/WEBP) to descriptions using Grok Vision AI. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[loutai0307-prog](https://clawhub.ai/user/loutai0307-prog) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, sellers, and e-commerce teams use this skill to generate product descriptions from local image files or folders for listings and product copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Selected product images are sent to x.ai for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Use only images that are approved for external processing, and avoid confidential, regulated, or unreleased product images unless that use is allowed. <br>
Risk: XAI_API_KEY authorizes API requests. <br>
Mitigation: Store the key securely, avoid committing or sharing it, and rotate it if exposure is suspected. <br>
