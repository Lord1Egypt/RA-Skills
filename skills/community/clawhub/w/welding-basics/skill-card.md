## Description: <br>
Guides agents through planning, safety checks, equipment selection, practice tracking, and project documentation for entry-level welding and metal fabrication tasks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to have an agent create welding intake questions, safety checklists, shopping lists, practice plans, project blueprints, progress logs, and supplier communications. It is intended for learning, practice, documentation, shopping lists, and non-critical fabrication support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce welding plans for safety-critical vehicle, trailer, structural, pressure-vessel, confined-space, or code-required work. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for learning, practice, documentation, shopping lists, and non-critical fabrication unless a qualified welding professional reviews the work. <br>
Risk: Generated welding settings, supplier information, or research results may be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable for the user's equipment and materials. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm settings against manufacturer instructions, applicable standards, local rules, and any network research or supplier outreach before acting. <br>
Risk: Filesystem logs may contain personal project details, location information, photos descriptions, or supplier communications. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive personal details in generated welding logs and review files before sharing them. <br>
