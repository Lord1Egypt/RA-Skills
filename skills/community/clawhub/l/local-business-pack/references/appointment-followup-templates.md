# Appointment Follow-Up Templates by Business Type

All SMS examples are under 160 characters. Placeholders: {first_name}, {business_name}, {service}

---

## Hair Salon / Beauty

**Day of (2hrs after):**
"Hey {first_name}! Hope you're loving your new look 💇 Let us know if you have any questions. – {business_name}"

**Day 3:**
"Hi {first_name}, just checking in! Ready to book your next appointment? We're filling up fast for next month. – {business_name}"

**Day 14:**
"Hey {first_name}! Time flies — it's been 2 weeks since your visit. Book now and lock in your spot before we fill up. [link] – {business_name}"

---

## Dental / Medical

**Day of:**
"Hi {first_name}, thanks for coming in today! Any questions about your visit, just reply here. – {business_name}"

**Day 3:**
"Hi {first_name}, hope you're feeling great after your {service}! Your next recommended visit is in [X months]. Want to pre-book? – {business_name}"

**Day 14:**
"Hi {first_name}, just a friendly reminder that staying on schedule with your care makes a big difference. Ready to book your next visit? – {business_name}"

---

## Restaurant / Cafe

## Email format additions (when not SMS)

For email, add:
- Subject line: "How did your [service] go, {first_name}?"
- Opening line: "Hi {first_name},"
- Closing: "[Owner first name] + [Business name] + [phone/website]"
- Keep body under 100 words — local business emails should feel personal, not like a newsletter
