## Description: <br>
Intelligent route planning assistant that compares route options and presents transport tables, recommended schemes, and important timing reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chandler0714](https://clawhub.ai/user/chandler0714) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to compare driving, transit, taxi, and walking routes, then receive recommended travel schemes and timing reminders. It is especially oriented toward Chinese or bilingual route requests that can be answered with Amap route data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Route queries may include private home, workplace, medical, or other sensitive itinerary locations. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive locations unless sharing them with the route-planning provider is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Route responses may depend on Amap processing and may work best for Chinese or bilingual requests. <br>
Mitigation: Review route suggestions before use and provide clear origin, destination, and travel-time details. <br>
