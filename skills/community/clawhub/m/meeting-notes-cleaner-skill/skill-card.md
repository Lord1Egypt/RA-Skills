## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn meeting notes and writing goals into concise plans, numbered checklists, next actions, and optional draft copy while avoiding secrets and unnecessary data collection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting notes may contain secrets or sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting passwords, OTPs, private keys, wallets, seeds, session secrets, or sensitive meeting content unless you are comfortable with the agent processing it. <br>
Risk: Generated note cleanup guidance may be incorrect or miss important context. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans, checklists, next actions, and draft copy before using them in a workflow or sharing them. <br>
