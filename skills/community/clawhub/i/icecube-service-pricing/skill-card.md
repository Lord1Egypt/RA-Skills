## Description: <br>
Helps agents draft standardized pricing, quotations, payment workflows, and monthly revenue tracking for IceCube/OpenClaw service work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ares521521-design](https://clawhub.ai/user/ares521521-design) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and service operators use this skill to draft RMB service pricing tables, quotation templates, payment reminders, and simple monthly revenue summaries for OpenClaw-related consulting and implementation work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer names, contact details, payment information, account identifiers, and receivables records may be captured in local or shared tracking files. <br>
Mitigation: Keep real customer and payment details out of public or shared skill files; apply appropriate retention, access controls, and redaction for monthly service records. <br>
Risk: Generated quotes, payment reminders, and revenue summaries may not match the user's actual commercial terms or accounting records. <br>
Mitigation: Review all pricing, payment terms, and revenue summaries against the current agreement and internal records before sending or relying on them. <br>
