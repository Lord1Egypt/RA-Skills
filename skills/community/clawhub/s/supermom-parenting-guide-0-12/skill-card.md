## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated parenting content may be published without adequate review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy and image prompts before publishing, especially content that could be interpreted as medical, safety, or developmental advice. <br>
Risk: The skill writes generated files and may use a default output folder. <br>
Mitigation: Specify and review the output folder before running the skill, then inspect generated files before distribution. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases and default persona choices may produce content for a wider audience or style than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Specify target language, locale, platform, persona, age range, and topic before use. <br>
