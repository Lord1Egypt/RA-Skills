## Description: <br>
Provides detailed, multi-source insights on global disease outbreaks, clinical trials, drug pipelines, health systems, regulations, and biomedical research. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, researchers, product teams, and health-policy readers use this skill to gather public health and biotech intelligence, compare evidence across sources, and produce structured summaries for outbreaks, trials, drug pipelines, health systems, regulation, reimbursement, and biomedical literature. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Health, regulatory, and investment-sensitive conclusions may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for direct decision-making. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important conclusions against cited primary sources and qualified professional advice, and include source dates and data vintage in generated outputs. <br>
Risk: Bundled reference data and public health sources can become stale as outbreaks, trials, approvals, and policies change. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer fresh official sources for time-sensitive topics and clearly distinguish peer-reviewed consensus, preprints, forecasts, and uncertain evidence. <br>
