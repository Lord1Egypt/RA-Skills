## Description: <br>
Bilingual Buddy reformats user inputs and AI replies into a four-layer bilingual learning format with Chinese text, Pinyin, English translation, and IPA transcription, with optional layer skipping and multi-language extension support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External language learners, educators, and families use this skill to practice bilingual Chinese-English conversation, pronunciation, translation, and reading-aloud formats. It is best suited to language-learning exchanges rather than code, debugging, data analysis, finance, or table-heavy tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to repeat and expand user input, which can expose sensitive text in the conversation transcript. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using the skill with passwords, personal records, private school or child information, or other sensitive content. <br>
Risk: Pronunciation, translation, Pinyin, or IPA output may be inaccurate or misaligned for some phrases. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated language-learning content before relying on it for teaching, assessment, or publication. <br>
