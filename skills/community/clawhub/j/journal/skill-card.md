## Description: <br>
Build a personal journaling practice with prompts, reflection, and pattern discovery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals use this skill to keep a local journal, receive optional writing prompts, organize dated entries, and reflect on themes or patterns across past writing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private journal entries may be saved locally and later searched or resurfaced. <br>
Mitigation: Keep journal files under appropriate filesystem permissions, use encryption for sensitive entries, handle backups carefully, and enable reminders or resurfacing only when comfortable with private content appearing in conversation. <br>
