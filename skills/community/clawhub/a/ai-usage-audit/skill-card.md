## Description: <br>
Monthly AI usage retrospective and insights that reviews recent conversation history, analyzes collaboration patterns, and produces a polished HTML report with actionable improvement recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jjliu6](https://clawhub.ai/user/jjliu6) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals and teams use this skill to review recent AI conversations, understand how their AI usage is distributed, identify friction or repeated work, and plan concrete improvements for the next month. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill reviews recent AI conversations and the resulting report may contain sensitive work patterns or private discussion details. <br>
Mitigation: Use a narrow date range or project scope when possible, avoid running it over confidential chats, and review the saved HTML report before sharing or retaining it. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on chat history or memory access, so it cannot provide a complete retrospective when those capabilities are unavailable or scoped to only part of the user's workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the available history scope before analysis and clearly state any project or product limitations in the report. <br>
