AI Astrology introduces astrology basics, including zodiac signs, planets, houses, aspects, birth-chart interpretation, rising and moon signs, transits, progressions, solar returns, synastry, astrology history, and ethical boundaries against fear-based, medical, or investment advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to learn astrology concepts and request plain-language guidance about birth charts, zodiac placements, transits, progressions, solar returns, and relationship synastry. It is framed as cultural exploration and self-reflection, not as professional medical, financial, or deterministic advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat astrology guidance as professional or deterministic advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs framed as cultural exploration, entertainment, and self-reflection; decline medical or investment advice in line with the skill's ethics section. <br>
Risk: Birth-chart discussion may involve birth date, exact birth time, and birthplace. <br>
Mitigation: Request only details needed for the user's task and avoid storing or sharing personal information. <br>
Risk: Unnecessary credentials or account access could be granted during installation or use. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the security guidance: grant credentials or account access only when the visible instructions match the intended task. <br>
