## Description: <br>
根据DXF户型图和设计师历史报价自动生成符合本地市场的家装或工装预算Excel，并保留原模板格式。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Interior designers and renovation teams use this skill to turn DXF floor-plan information and a local pricing library into an editable budget workbook for residential or commercial projects. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated quantities, rates, totals, and omitted line items may be inaccurate because the workbook is produced from DXF text extraction and local estimation rules. <br>
Mitigation: Manually verify quantities, rates, totals, and omissions before sharing any generated workbook with a client. <br>
Risk: The script depends on a local pricing JSON path, so an incorrect or stale library can produce unsuitable budget lines. <br>
Mitigation: Review and update the local pricing library path before running the skill, preferably inside a virtual environment with project data kept local. <br>
