## Why This Skill Converts

1. **High-urgency buyer** — authors with a book idea are emotionally invested. They've been sitting on the idea for months or years. When they find a tool that can help them move, they buy.
2. **Clear ROI** — a book deal can generate $25,000-$500,000+ in advances alone, before speaking fees, course revenue, and consulting uplift. $29 is a rounding error.
3. **No DIY alternative** — you can't just Google "book proposal template" and get agent-quality output. The skill does something that genuinely requires expertise.
4. **DFY upsell is natural** — after seeing the free output, buyers who want "done for me" have an obvious path to $197. The gap between "here's the framework" and "here's your actual proposal" is clear.
