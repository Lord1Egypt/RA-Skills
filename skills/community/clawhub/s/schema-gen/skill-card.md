## Description: <br>
Generate Schema.org JSON-LD structured data markup for Person, Organization, Product, FAQ, HowTo, Article, LocalBusiness, Event, WebSite, BreadcrumbList, VideoObject, and SoftwareApplication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, site owners, and content authors use this skill to generate Schema.org JSON-LD markup for common page and content types so it can be inserted into HTML and checked with rich result tooling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated structured data may expose inaccurate or unintended personal, business, product, event, pricing, or contact details if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated JSON-LD before publishing and confirm that all included fields are accurate and intended for public exposure. <br>
