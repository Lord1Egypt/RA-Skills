## Eight Principles (八纲辨证)

The most fundamental framework — all patterns can be classified along four pairs of opposites.

| Pair | Principle | Key Indicators |
|:----:|-----------|----------------|
| **阴阳** | Yin / Yang | Yin: cold body, pale tongue, slow pulse, prefers warmth. Yang: fever, red face, restlessness, rapid pulse |
| **表里** | Exterior / Interior | Exterior: chills/fever, body aches, thin tongue coating, floating pulse. Interior: high fever or no fever, organ symptoms, deep pulse |
| **寒热** | Cold / Heat | Cold: aversion to cold, cold limbs, clear thin secretions, pale tongue. Heat: fever, thirst, red face, yellow thick coating, rapid pulse |
| **虚实** | Deficiency / Excess | Deficiency: weak constitution, tired, soft pulse. Excess: strong constitution, acute onset, forceful pulse, pain aggravated by pressure |

**Combined patterns (常见兼夹):**
- 表寒证 (Exterior Cold): Mahuang Tang
- 表热证 (Exterior Heat): Yinqiao San
- 里寒证 (Interior Cold): Lizhong Tang / Sini Tang
- 里热证 (Interior Heat): Baihu Tang / Chengqi Tang
- 表虚证 (Exterior Deficiency): Guizhi Tang
- 表实证 (Exterior Excess): Mahuang Tang
- 虚寒证 (Deficiency Cold): Fuzi Lizhong Tang
- 实热证 (Excess Heat): Da Chengqi Tang
- 上热下寒 (Upper Heat, Lower Cold): Banxia Xiexin Tang
- 真寒假热 (True Cold, False Heat): Sini Tang (careful!)
- 真热假寒 (True Heat, False Cold): Baihu Tang (careful!)

## Six Meridian Differentiation (六经辨证)

Originating from Shanghan Lun (伤寒论) — the framework for classifying the progression of externally-contracted diseases.

### Taiyang Disease (太阳病) — Exterior

| Type | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|:----:|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 中风 (Wind strike) | Aversion to cold, fever, sweating, neck/shoulder stiffness | Thin white coat | Floating, moderate (浮缓) | **Guizhi Tang** (桂枝汤) |
| 伤寒 (Cold damage) | Aversion to cold, fever, no sweating, body pain, dyspnea | Thin white coat | Floating, tight (浮紧) | **Mahuang Tang** (麻黄汤) |
| 蓄水 (Water retention) | Fever, thirst, dysuria, vomiting after drinking | Pale, white coat | Floating | **Wuling San** (五苓散) |
| 蓄血 (Blood accumulation) | Lower abdominal pain/fullness, mental disturbance, dark stools | Dark tongue | Deep, rough or knotted | **Taohu Chengqi Tang** (桃核承气汤) |

### Yangming Disease (阳明病) — Interior Heat/Excess

| Type | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|:----:|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 经证 (Meridian pattern) | High fever, profuse sweating, extreme thirst, red face | Red tongue, dry yellow coat | Surging, large (洪大) | **Baihu Tang** (白虎汤) |
| 腑证 (Bowel pattern) | Tidal fever, constipation, abdominal pain/rigidity, delirium | Dry yellow/dark coat | Deep, forceful (沉实) | **Chengqi Tang** series (承气汤类) |

### Shaoyang Disease (少阳病) — Half-Exterior, Half-Interior

| Type | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|:----:|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 经证 | Alternate chills/fever (寒热往来), chest/rib fullness, bitter taste, dry throat, blurred vision | Thin white coat | String-like (弦) | **Xiao Chaihu Tang** (小柴胡汤) |
| 兼夹 | Shaoyang + Yangming: full chest/abdomen, constipation | Yellow coating | String, full | **Da Chaihu Tang** (大柴胡汤) |

### Taiyin Disease (太阴病) — Spleen Deficiency Cold

| Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| Abdominal fullness, vomiting, poor appetite, diarrhea, intermittent abdominal pain, prefers warmth/pressure | Pale, white greasy coat | Slow, weak (缓弱) | **Lizhong Tang** (理中汤) |

### Shaoyin Disease (少阴病) — Heart-Kidney Deficiency

| Type | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|:----:|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 寒化 (Cold transformation) | Aversion to cold, curled-up sleeping, cold limbs, diarrhea with undigested food, lethargy | Pale, moist | Deep, thin (沉微) | **Sini Tang** (四逆汤) |
| 热化 (Heat transformation) | Restlessness, insomnia, dry mouth/throat, red tip | Red tongue, little coating | Thin, rapid (细数) | **Huanglian Ejiao Tang** (黄连阿胶汤) |

### Jueyin Disease (厥阴病) — Complex Cold-Heat Mix

| Type | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|:----:|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 寒热错杂 (Mixed cold-heat) | Thirst, Qi rushing to chest, heat sensation, hunger but cannot eat, vomiting roundworms | Mixed coating | String (弦) | **Wumei Wan** (乌梅丸) |
| 厥逆 (Cold reversal) | Cold hands/feet or heat in hands/feet | — | — | Various based on pattern |

## Zang-Fu Pattern Differentiation (脏腑辨证)

### Heart (心) Patterns

| Pattern | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|---------|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 心气虚 | Palpitations, shortness of breath on exertion, fatigue, pale face | Pale | Weak (弱) | Yangxin Tang (养心汤) |
| 心血虚 | Palpitations, dizziness, pale complexion, insomnia, poor memory | Pale, thin | Thin, weak (细弱) | Guipi Tang (归脾汤) |
| 心阴虚 | Palpitations, insomnia, low-grade fever, night sweats, dry mouth | Red, little coating | Thin, rapid (细数) | Tianwang Buxin Dan (天王补心丹) |
| 心阳虚 | Palpitations, chest stuffiness, cold limbs, pale face, breathlessness | Pale, dark | Deep, thin, or knotted | Guizhi Gancao Longgu Muli Tang |
| 心血瘀阻 | Chest pain (stabbing), purple lips, palpitations | Dark, purple spots | Knotted or choppy (结/涩) | Xuefu Zhuyu Tang (血府逐瘀汤) |
| 痰火扰心 | Mania, laughing/crying, insomnia, delirium, red face | Red, yellow greasy | Slippery, rapid (滑数) | Wen Dan Tang (温胆汤) / Goun Teng Yin |
| 心火亢盛 | Mouth/tongue sores, restlessness, insomnia, dark urine | Red tip | Rapid (数) | Daochi San (导赤散) / Xiexin Tang (泻心汤) |

### Liver (肝) Patterns

| Pattern | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|---------|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 肝气郁结 | Rib pain, sighing, irritability, depression, breast distension | Thin white | String (弦) | Chaihu Shugan San (柴胡疏肝散) |
| 肝火上炎 | Headache, red face/eyes, tinnitus, bitter taste, irritability | Red, yellow | String, rapid (弦数) | Longdan Xiegan Tang (龙胆泻肝汤) |
| 肝阳上亢 | Dizziness, headache, tinnitus, irritability, insomnia | Red | String, forceful (弦有力) | Tianma Gouteng Yin (天麻钩藤饮) |
| 肝风内动 | Tremors, dizziness, convulsions | Varies | Varies | Various (镇肝熄风汤等) |
| 肝血虚 | Dizziness, numbness, pale nails, blurred vision, pale face | Pale, thin | String, thin (弦细) | Siwu Tang (四物汤) + Bu Gan Tang |
| 寒滞肝脉 | Lower abdominal pain referred to testicles, cold sensation | Pale, white | Deep, string (沉弦) | Nuan Gan Jian (暖肝煎) |

### Spleen (脾) Patterns

| Pattern | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|---------|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 脾气虚 | Fatigue, poor appetite, loose stools, pale face | Pale, white coat | Slow, weak (缓弱) | Sijunzi Tang (四君子汤) |
| 脾阳虚 | Spleen qi deficiency + cold limbs, abdominal pain prefers warmth | Pale, moist | Deep, slow (沉迟) | Lizhong Tang (理中汤) |
| 中气下陷 | Spleen qi deficiency + prolapse, bearing-down sensation | Pale | Weak | Buzhong Yiqi Tang (补中益气汤) |
| 脾不统血 | Spleen qi deficiency + bleeding (purpura, menorrhagia) | Pale | Thin, weak | Guipi Tang (归脾汤) |
| 寒湿困脾 | Heavy sensation, epigastric fullness, nausea, loose stools | Pale, white greasy | Soft, slow (濡缓) | Pingwei San (平胃散) |
| 湿热蕴脾 | Epigastric fullness, nausea, bitter taste, yellow skin | Red, yellow greasy | Soft, rapid (濡数) | Yinchenhao Tang (茵陈蒿汤) |

|---------|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|

| 痰热壅肺 | Cough, yellow thick phlegm, dyspnea, chest pain | Red, yellow greasy | Slippery, rapid | Qingqi Huatan Wan (清气化痰丸) / Maxingshigan Tang (麻杏石甘汤) |

## Wei-Qi-Ying-Xue Differentiation (卫气营血辨证)

For warm diseases (温病) — febrile infectious diseases.

| Level | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|:-----:|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 卫分 (Wei/Defensive) | Fever, slight chills, headache, sore throat, thin sweating | Thin white at edges, red tip | Floating, rapid | Yinqiao San (银翘散) / Sangju Yin (桑菊饮) |
| 气分 (Qi/Energy) | High fever, no chills, profuse sweating, extreme thirst, dark urine | Yellow, dry | Surging, rapid (洪数) | Baihu Tang (白虎汤) |
| 营分 (Ying/Nutritive) | Fever worse at night, restless, faint rash, dry mouth with no desire to drink | Deep red | Thin, rapid (细数) | Qingying Tang (清营汤) |
| 血分 (Blood) | High fever, mania, convulsions, purple skin spots, bleeding | Deep red/crimson | Thin, rapid (细数) | Xijiao Dihuang Tang (犀角地黄汤) |

## Qi-Blood-Fluid Differentiation (气血津液辨证)

| Pattern | Key Symptoms | Tongue | Pulse | Formula |
|---------|-------------|:------:|:-----:|---------|
| 气虚 (Qi deficiency) | Fatigue, shortness of breath, weak voice, spontaneous sweating | Pale | Weak | Sijunzi Tang / Buzhong Yiqi Tang |

| 血虚 (Blood deficiency) | Dizziness, pale complexion, numbness, insomnia | Pale, thin | Thin, weak | Siwu Tang / Danggui Buxue Tang |
| 血瘀 (Blood stasis) | Fixed stabbing pain, masses, purple lips/nails | Dark, purple spots | Choppy (涩) / Knotted (结) | Xuefu Zhuyu Tang / Taohong Siwu Tang |
| 血热 (Blood heat) | Skin eruptions, bleeding, fever, restlessness | Red/crimson | Rapid | Xijiao Dihuang Tang |
| 痰 (Phlegm) | Cough with phlegm, chest tightness, nausea, dizziness | Greasy coating | Slippery (滑) | Erchen Tang (general) |
| 饮 (Fluid retention) | Cough with profuse thin phlegm, edema, chest/abdominal distension | Pale, slippery | String (弦) | Lingguizhugan Tang / Xiao Qinglong Tang |
| 水湿 (Dampness) | Heavy sensation, edema, scanty urine, diarrhea | Pale, greasy | Soft (濡) | Wuling San / Fangji Huangqi Tang |
