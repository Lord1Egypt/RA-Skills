## Description: <br>
JACK helps users get real-time weather, temperature, and clothing guidance for a specified city using search-backed weather information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wanglingchao-2025](https://clawhub.ai/user/wanglingchao-2025) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer weather, temperature, and clothing-advice questions. If no city is provided, the skill asks for a location before searching for current weather information. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: City or location details may be sent to a search or weather provider while retrieving current weather. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing exact private addresses unless necessary; ask for city-level location details when possible. <br>
