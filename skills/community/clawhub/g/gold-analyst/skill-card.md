## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language gold investment analysis agent that searches current gold prices, recent trends, market drivers, and ETF data to produce a structured report with buy or hold guidance and risk notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[SakuraLeanne](https://clawhub.ai/user/SakuraLeanne) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to ask Chinese-language questions about gold prices, market trends, and gold ETFs and receive a structured investment analysis report. The output should be treated as educational analysis and checked against official financial sources before making decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce buy, hold, and ETF ranking guidance that may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational analysis, verify prices and fund data from official financial sources, and do not rely on the report as personalized investment advice. <br>
Risk: The report depends on live market and ETF data that may be delayed, stale, or unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Check timestamps, cite data sources in the report, and mark unavailable values clearly rather than filling them from unsupported assumptions. <br>
