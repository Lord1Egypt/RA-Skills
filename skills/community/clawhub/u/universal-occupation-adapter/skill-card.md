## Description: <br>
Generates occupation-specific cognition skill drafts from a profession name using a five-dimension model and preset templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kingofzhao](https://clawhub.ai/user/kingofzhao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and skill authors use this skill to draft occupation-specific cognition skills from an occupation name, optional custom dimensions, and reusable templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can encourage broad generation and auto-publishing of new skills without enough review or user control. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a local drafting aid, disable or ignore auto-publish behavior, and inspect every generated skill before sharing it. <br>
Risk: Generated occupation guidance may be inappropriate for medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical use cases. <br>
Mitigation: Require expert review before using generated content as authoritative guidance in regulated or safety-critical domains. <br>
