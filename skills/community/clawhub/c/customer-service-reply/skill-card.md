## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese customer service reply templates for pre-sale questions, complaints, bad reviews, refunds, escalations, FAQs, and satisfaction recovery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Customer support and e-commerce teams use this skill to draft customer-facing replies, escalation language, FAQ answers, and satisfaction recovery guidance for common service scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer inputs and command arguments may be retained in local data or history files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets or regulated personal data, set a controlled data directory when needed, and review or delete local records after use. <br>
Risk: Generated replies may include inaccurate promises, unsuitable compensation, or guidance that conflicts with company policy. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt each reply before sending it to customers, especially for refunds, complaints, escalations, and satisfaction recovery. <br>
