## Description: <br>
This skill helps users create structured industry research reports, market analyses, business trend analyses, and professional research documents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chaoliuzhu](https://clawhub.ai/user/chaoliuzhu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business analysts, strategy teams, researchers, and other users can use this skill to scope, draft, and review market research reports with structured sections, credible-source guidance, and markdown formatting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated market research may contain outdated, unsupported, or misleading claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market data, assumptions, and citations against credible current sources before relying on the report. <br>
Risk: Research prompts or source material may include confidential business information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only trusted agent environments and avoid sharing sensitive information unless approved. <br>
