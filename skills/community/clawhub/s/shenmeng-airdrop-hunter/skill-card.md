## Description: <br>
Airdrop Hunter helps users research cryptocurrency airdrop opportunities, follow interaction guides, evaluate risks, manage multi-account strategies, and query current airdrop information across Layer 1/2 chains, DeFi, AI, and RWA projects. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is a paid crypto-focused release that may contact an external billing service and includes charge-capable payment helper code. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if the payment model is acceptable, verify expected charges before use, and avoid providing billing credentials unless paid use is intended. <br>
Risk: The skill includes multi-account, proxy, fingerprint-browser, and behavior-variation guidance that could be used to bypass project rules or anti-abuse controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use the guidance only within each project's terms and do not use it to evade anti-abuse, anti-Sybil, or eligibility rules. <br>
Risk: Wallet transactions, private keys, seed phrases, bridges, swaps, staking, and contract deployments can cause irreversible loss. <br>
Mitigation: Verify official URLs independently, use small test amounts, keep private keys and seed phrases out of agent workflows, and manually review every transaction before signing. <br>
Risk: Airdrop eligibility, token value, and project timelines are uncertain and may change without notice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research support rather than investment advice, validate current project announcements, and avoid committing funds solely on the skill's recommendations. <br>
