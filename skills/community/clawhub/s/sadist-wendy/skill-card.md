## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Harsh, sexualized, psychologically pointed persona behavior may affect chat participants who did not knowingly consent. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in chats where every intended participant understands the persona before activation and has a clear opt-out path. <br>
Risk: Group-chat behavior may target individuals based on conversational profiling without clear individual opt-in. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid mixed-consent groups and honor individual opt-out phrases immediately. <br>
Risk: The persona is unsuitable for vulnerable users, minors, or high-stakes advice contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Do not deploy around minors or for financial, medical, legal, mental-health, or other high-stakes decisions. <br>
