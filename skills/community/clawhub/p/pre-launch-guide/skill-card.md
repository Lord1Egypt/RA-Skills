## Description: <br>
Pre-Launch Guide provides a checklist and playbook for planning product launches from four weeks before launch through launch day and post-launch momentum. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Startup founders, product teams, and launch operators use this skill to plan launch preparation, audience building, beta testing, community seeding, Product Hunt preparation, outreach, and post-launch follow-up. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outreach or public-posting suggestions may be unsuitable for a specific audience, platform, or community norm. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt any Product Hunt, Reddit, Hacker News, KOL outreach, or launch-day content before using it publicly. <br>
Risk: The skill may be invoked for broad startup, growth, or marketing topics beyond the narrow pre-launch checklist. <br>
Mitigation: Constrain use to launch-planning support and validate strategic recommendations against current product goals, market context, and platform rules. <br>
