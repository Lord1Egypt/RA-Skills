## Description: <br>
Generates ideal customer profiles, lead acquisition strategies, LinkedIn and Google search formulas, structured lead lists, and cold outreach connection tips for B2B sales prospecting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, SDRs, account executives, growth marketers, consultants, and agencies use this skill to create B2B prospecting playbooks, ideal customer profiles, search formulas, lead-list templates, and outreach angles from target-market inputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat generated lead templates or search formulas as verified contact data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify prospect identity, company fit, and contact information before outreach; the skill does not scrape or validate live contact data. <br>
Risk: Prospecting workflows can create privacy, consent, platform-terms, or outreach-compliance obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Review GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM, opt-out, data-minimization, LinkedIn, and data-provider requirements before collecting or using prospect data. <br>
