## Description: <br>
Analyzes customer behavior, communications, and transaction data to segment customers, predict likely deals, and suggest personalized follow-up and marketing actions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales, customer success, and marketing teams use this skill to analyze authorized customer chat, email, and CRM records, identify high-value or at-risk customers, forecast conversion opportunities, and plan timely follow-up actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process private customer chats, email, and CRM data without explicit privacy or storage boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Use only customer data the operator is authorized to process, redact unnecessary personal or confidential details, and decide where local outputs or summaries are stored and deleted before use. <br>
Risk: Customer prioritization, deal predictions, or marketing recommendations may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a specific account. <br>
Mitigation: Use a deliberate invocation and confirmation step, and review recommendations before acting on them with real customer records. <br>
