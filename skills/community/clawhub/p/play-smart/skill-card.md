## Description: <br>
Play Smart lets an agent intentionally answer simple questions in an overcomplicated, academic, or intellectualized style, with selectable personas and smartness levels while falling back to plain answers when users are confused or need simplicity. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dox012](https://clawhub.ai/user/dox012) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to switch an assistant into a playful high-intellect style for entertainment, creative tone control, or persona experimentation. It is not intended for factual, academic, medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical answers unless the user asks for plain language and source verification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can make entertainment answers sound overly authoritative or fact-like, even when they are intentionally exaggerated. <br>
Mitigation: Use plain-answer mode and verify sources for factual, academic, medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical questions. <br>
Risk: The overcomplicated style can reduce clarity when users are confused, distressed, or asking for a concise answer. <br>
Mitigation: Honor the documented fallback triggers such as requests for simple language, confusion signals, urgent scenarios, or emotional distress. <br>
