# Discovery Call Debrief

**Platforms:** Claude · Codex
**Domain:** Sales

## Purpose

Turns a sales rep's raw discovery call notes or transcript into a complete, structured debrief. Saves 15–20 minutes per call of manual note-structuring, CRM updating, and follow-up drafting.

## When to Use

- After a B2B discovery or qualification call
- When a rep needs to assess deal qualification before advancing the opportunity stage
- When structured notes are required for manager review or deal inspection
- When a follow-up email must reflect the actual conversation, not a generic template

## What It Does

**Phase 1: Intake**
1. Collects call notes or transcript, company name, and rep details

**Phase 2: Extraction and Scoring**
2. Extracts key signals: pain points, stakeholders, timeline, budget signals, decision process, competitors, and champion indicators — marking each as Confirmed, Implied, or Unknown
3. Scores the deal against the MEDDIC qualification framework and assigns a deal tier (Qualified / Developing / At Risk)

**Phase 3: Output Generation**
4. Produces a CRM-ready field update block (copy-paste usable)
5. Drafts a concise, personalized follow-up email in the rep's voice

## Qualification Frameworks

Defaults to MEDDIC. If the user requests BANT, SPICED, or SPIN, the skill applies that framework instead and produces an equivalent scorecard.

## Notes

Call transcripts may contain personal information (names, job titles, budget figures). The skill does not store, log, or transmit data beyond the current session.
