## Description: <br>
Track the latest Iran war situation with concise 5-8 headline summaries, prioritize breaking and major events, include source links, and append a three-column verification table comparing US claims, Iran claims, and third-party verifiable facts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to request concise Chinese-language Iran conflict updates, battle damage tracking, source links, and claim verification tables. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fast-moving conflict news can include uncertain or conflicting claims. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for current reputable sources and treat claims as uncertain unless independently verified. <br>
Risk: Battle damage or casualty claims may be overstated by official or partisan sources. <br>
Mitigation: Separate event occurrence from damage scale, prefer quantifiable evidence, and use confidence labels for third-party verification. <br>
