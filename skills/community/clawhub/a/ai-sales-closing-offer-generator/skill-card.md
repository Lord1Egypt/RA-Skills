## Description: <br>
Generates closing messages, objection handling responses, pricing strategies, offer designs, and upsell scripts to help salespeople convert prospects into paying customers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated sales copy may include false urgency, unsupported ROI claims, or persuasion that does not match the actual offer. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit every generated message before sending; keep urgency, guarantees, discounts, and ROI claims grounded in facts the seller can support. <br>
Risk: Users may include more customer or deal information than needed when asking for tailored sales copy. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the customer context needed to write the response and avoid unnecessary confidential customer information. <br>
Risk: Copy-ready messages may sound final even though the skill does not send messages automatically. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and personalize them for the relationship, sales channel, and current deal state before use. <br>
