## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common recruiting questions for recruiters, hiring managers, and talent teams working on FAQ content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Recruiters, hiring managers, and talent teams use this skill to draft customer-facing recruiting FAQ answers and refine them for accuracy, readability, and actionability. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated recruiting FAQ content may be inaccurate, biased, or sensitive under employment-law review. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated content for accuracy, fairness, and employment-law sensitivity before publishing or using it in recruiting workflows. <br>
Risk: Candidate, employee, or other confidential recruiting information could be exposed to the agent or web-search environment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential candidate or employee information unless the user is comfortable with that environment handling it. <br>
