## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad trigger phrase "now" may invoke the skill when the user did not specifically ask for the time. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer narrower trigger phrasing such as "current time" or "what time is it" when configuring or reviewing activation behavior. <br>
