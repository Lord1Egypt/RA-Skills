## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese-language Carl Jung perspective for analytical psychology reflection, self-exploration, dream discussion, symbolic interpretation, and personality integration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[baodirewolf](https://clawhub.ai/user/baodirewolf) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ClawHub users can use this skill to have an agent respond from a Chinese Jungian roleplay and reflection frame for psychology-themed exploration, dream discussion, symbolic interpretation, and self-reflection. It should not be used as professional mental-health diagnosis or treatment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake Jungian roleplay and reflection for professional mental-health diagnosis or treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for reflective exploration only and rely on qualified professionals for mental-health diagnosis, treatment, or crisis support. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate broadly in psychology, dream, or unconscious-related conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only when this Chinese-language Jungian perspective is desired, and manually narrow or disable activation when ordinary psychology discussion should remain non-roleplay. <br>
