## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Conversion-focused copy may be unsuitable for legal, medical, financial, safety-critical, HR, or policy communications without compliance review. <br>
Mitigation: Use human review and adapt wording to applicable rules before publishing in regulated or high-stakes contexts. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing claims can be inaccurate, exaggerated, or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims, statistics, testimonials, pricing, guarantees, and product capabilities against authoritative sources before use. <br>
