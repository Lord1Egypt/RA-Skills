## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad SaaS-growth acronyms or general business strategy prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Install if you want a multilingual B2B SaaS growth playbook. Be aware it may activate on general SaaS-growth acronyms, so use it when the conversation is actually about PLG/SLG, pricing, ARR/NRR, PMF, or enterprise sales strategy. <br>
Risk: Strategic growth guidance can be misapplied when the team has not reached the stated readiness point or when metrics differ from the playbook assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the playbook as decision support and validate recommendations against current customer evidence, pricing data, sales-cycle length, and retention metrics before acting. <br>
