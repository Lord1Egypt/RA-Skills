## Description: <br>
A Chinese study-assistant skill for learning and applying Chapter 10, "The Future of Job Analysis," from Brannick, Levine, and Morgeson's Job and Work Analysis textbook. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, instructors, and employees use this skill to review, discuss, and apply textbook concepts about future job analysis, work analysis, flexible work, AI and automation, electronic monitoring, job crafting, analysis units, and work-analysis information management. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake textbook-oriented guidance for current labor-market facts or authoritative citations. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current facts, citations, and labor-market claims against the textbook or up-to-date authoritative sources before relying on them. <br>
