## Use Case: <br>
External users, site owners, and content teams use this skill to draft or structure privacy policy pages, including privacy notices, cookie disclosures, rights sections, data-use descriptions, and legal-review reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft privacy-policy content may not match the user's actual data practices, cookies, vendors, retention periods, or applicable laws. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated content as a starting point and have a qualified reviewer confirm it before publishing. <br>
