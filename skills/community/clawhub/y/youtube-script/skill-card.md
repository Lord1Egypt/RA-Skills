## Description: <br>
YouTube script writer with title testing, thumbnail copy, SEO optimization, hooks, chapter markers, and related creator workflow support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and content teams use this skill to draft and refine YouTube scripts, hooks, calls to action, titles, outlines, hashtags, translations, and upload schedules while keeping a searchable local activity history. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Entered creator notes, scripts, titles, schedules, and related workflow text are retained on local disk and can later be searched or exported. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets or highly confidential material, and delete ~/.local/share/youtube-script when retained history should be purged. <br>
