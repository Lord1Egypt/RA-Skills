## Use Case: <br>
Founders, sales teams, professionals, procurement teams, and other external users use this skill to prepare for salary talks, vendor contracts, client deals, partnerships, and high-stakes negotiations. The agent provides structured preparation briefs, live coaching prompts, objection-handling tactics, and post-negotiation review guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat negotiation coaching as legal, financial, or HR advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as coaching and have qualified legal, financial, HR, or business reviewers validate high-stakes decisions before acting. <br>
Risk: Negotiation preparation may involve confidential salary, contract, vendor, or deal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential details unless the user is comfortable exposing them to the agent environment. <br>
