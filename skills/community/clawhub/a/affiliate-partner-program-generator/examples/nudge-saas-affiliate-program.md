## Prompt 1 Output: Program Architecture

### Program Type Recommendation
**Hybrid RevShare (recurring % + one-time bonus):** At $59/month with an 18-month LTV, a pure CPA would require a high upfront payout to be competitive ($50–$90 flat). A RevShare model (30% of each payment for 12 months) creates compounding income for top affiliates — the #1 driver of affiliate loyalty. We add a one-time $10 activation bonus on the first conversion to reward effort immediately.

### Commission Structure
- **Base commission:** 30% recurring for 12 months per referred customer ($17.70/month per active customer)
- **Average affiliate earnings per conversion:** $212.40 (12 × $17.70) — competitive with $50–$90 CPA programs
- **Cookie window:** 60 days (B2B SaaS with a 14-day trial — buyers research for 2–6 weeks before converting)
- **Payout:** Monthly, net-30 after each billing cycle confirms
- **Minimum payout threshold:** $50 (prevents micro-payouts while keeping low earners engaged)
- **Platform:** Rewardful (1.5% platform fee on commissions — include in payout structure)

**Commission sustainability check:**
- Commission per conversion: $212.40 over 12 months
- LTV: $1,062
- Commission as % of LTV: 20% — within the healthy 15–25% range ✓
- Contribution margin retained: $849.60 per referred customer ✓

### Top 10 Program Rules
1. **No self-referral.** Affiliates may not refer their own accounts or accounts controlled by them. Violations result in immediate commission reversal and program termination.
2. **No brand bidding.** Affiliates may not run paid search ads bidding on "NudgePay," "Nudge Pay," or any NudgePay brand terms. Google Shopping exclusions apply.
3. **No coupon/deal site posting without approval.** Affiliates may not post to Honey, RetailMeNot, or similar sites without written permission from partner@nudgepay.com.
4. **Accurate claims only.** Affiliates must not fabricate testimonials, invent features, or exaggerate results. Approved claims are listed in the co-branding guidelines.
5. **No cookie stuffing.** Affiliates may not use any technique to force affiliate cookie placement without genuine user intent (clicking a link).
6. **No unsolicited email / spam.** Affiliates sending bulk cold email to purchased lists will be terminated and commissions forfeited.
7. **FTC disclosure required.** All promotional content (posts, emails, videos, reviews) must include: "Disclosure: This content includes affiliate links. I may earn a commission if you purchase through my link at no extra cost to you."
8. **Commission reversal on refunds.** If a referred customer cancels within 30 days and receives a full refund, the corresponding commission will be reversed on the next payout.
9. **Chargeback policy.** Commissions on accounts that initiate chargebacks are reversed and the referring affiliate is flagged for review.
10. **Dispute resolution.** Commission disputes must be submitted to partner@nudgepay.com within 60 days of the disputed payout date. Decisions are final after 30-day investigation period.
