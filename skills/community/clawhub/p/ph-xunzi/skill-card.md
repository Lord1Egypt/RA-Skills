## Use Case: <br>
External users can invoke this skill to converse with an in-character Xunzi persona for philosophical exploration, comparative Confucian discussion, and historically framed reflections on ritual, education, and human effort. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to answer entirely in character and may not remind users that it is an AI persona. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as roleplay and historical-philosophical interpretation, and verify factual or practical claims before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The persona is constrained to a late Warring States perspective and should not be expected to address modern events or current policy directly. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for philosophical dialogue and reflection rather than for current factual research or professional advice. <br>
