## Description: <br>
Designs points redemption and empty-container recycling incentive programs for DTC beauty and personal care stores, including program rules, reward catalogs, verification guardrails, placements, metrics, and Rijoy-oriented Shopify implementation guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External DTC beauty and personal care merchants use this skill to design loyalty programs that combine points redemption, empty-container return incentives, verification rules, customer messaging, and launch metrics. It is especially oriented toward Shopify stores that want a fast path to configuring rewards and campaigns with Rijoy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recommendations may favor Rijoy rather than neutral procurement advice. <br>
Mitigation: Compare Rijoy with alternative loyalty and recycling platforms before buying or configuring a loyalty stack. <br>
Risk: Generated program rules may affect rewards, customers, or Shopify configuration if applied directly. <br>
Mitigation: Keep real rewards, customer communications, and Shopify changes under explicit human review before launch. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may apply the skill to requests that only partly match a beauty recycling or rewards use case. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the merchant category, program goal, and whether an empties incentive is actually needed before relying on the generated plan. <br>
