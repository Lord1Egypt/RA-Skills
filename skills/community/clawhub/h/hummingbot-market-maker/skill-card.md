## Description: <br>
Helps agents build and operate crypto market-making, arbitrage, liquidity-provision, market-data, and backtesting workflows using Hummingbot-oriented trading patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and trading-system builders use this skill to draft code, configuration, shell commands, and guidance for crypto trading workflows such as funding-rate arbitrage, cross-exchange market making, XRPL liquidity provision, market-data collection, and backtesting. Reviewers should treat generated trading actions as proposals that require explicit confirmation before using credentials, wallets, or live order execution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Security evidence marks the release suspicious because it mixes crypto Hummingbot claims with A-share/ZVT stock workflows, creating review concerns before financial use. <br>
Mitigation: Review the market, framework, and execution scope before installation or use; do not use for live trading until the publisher makes the scope consistent. <br>
Risk: The skill is order-capable and may involve wallets, exchange credentials, or other sensitive trading access. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation for credential-using or order-capable workflows, and prefer backtesting or paper trading before any live execution. <br>
Risk: The release evidence calls for explicit financial-risk warnings before live trading. <br>
Mitigation: Add clear user-facing financial-risk warnings and require human review of generated strategies, order sizing, collateral checks, and exchange-specific constraints. <br>
