## Use Case: <br>
Developers, database administrators, and SREs use this skill to analyze slow queries, execution plans, indexes, database configuration, replication, caching, and resource bottlenecks, then produce optimization recommendations and implementation steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Database optimization guidance can affect live data systems, availability, performance, or rollback safety if applied directly in production. <br>
Mitigation: Start with read-only diagnostics, require explicit approval before index, schema, configuration, replication, or caching changes, test in staging when possible, and keep backups or rollback plans for production systems. <br>
