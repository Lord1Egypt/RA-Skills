## Description: <br>
Free market research toolkit for competitor audits, positioning matrices, evidence grading, and customer interview scripts, with an optional Company Brain enhancement. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[certainlogicai](https://clawhub.ai/user/certainlogicai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, product teams, and market researchers use this skill to structure early market research, compare competitors, grade evidence quality, run customer interviews, and draft positioning statements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional Company Brain enhancement may use stored company notes as context in generated market-research prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Review and trust the separate Company Brain installation before enabling it, and avoid storing sensitive company notes there unless that use is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Market-research conclusions can be misleading if based on weak or unverifiable evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included evidence grading workflow and require source-backed claims before using conclusions in external materials or strategic decisions. <br>
