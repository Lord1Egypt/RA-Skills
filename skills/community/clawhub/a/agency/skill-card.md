## Use Case: <br>
Agency operators and service-business teams use this skill to structure client onboarding, pricing, project tracking, deliverable workflows, team coordination, and feedback loops. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agency records may include sensitive client, project, pricing, or team information stored under ~/agency/. <br>
Mitigation: Limit stored data, avoid secrets and unnecessary personal data, protect access to the directory, and periodically delete stale records. <br>
Risk: Draft proposals or client communications may be inaccurate, premature, or commercially sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the built-in human-approval rule before sending proposals or communicating with clients. <br>
