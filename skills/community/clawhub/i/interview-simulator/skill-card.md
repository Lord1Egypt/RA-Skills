## Description: <br>
Simulates mock interviews for any role and experience level with tailored technical, behavioral, case, and role-play questions plus feedback and scoring. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Wscats](https://clawhub.ai/user/Wscats) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Candidates and interview coaches use this skill to run realistic practice interviews for a chosen role, seniority level, focus area, and duration. It asks one question at a time, adapts difficulty, and provides scoring, model-answer guidance, and an end-of-session scorecard. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste resumes or work histories containing unnecessary personal, employer, client, or contact information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact unnecessary contact details, addresses, identifiers, references, and confidential employer or client information before using resume-based tailoring. <br>
