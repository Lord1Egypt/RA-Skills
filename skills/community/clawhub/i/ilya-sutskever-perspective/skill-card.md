## Description: <br>
Provides an Ilya Sutskever-inspired thinking framework and response style for analyzing AI technical direction, safety strategy, and research taste as a simulated perspective based on public material. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to request an Ilya Sutskever-inspired analytical lens for AI strategy, safety, research direction, and technical judgment. Outputs should be treated as roleplay or perspective-taking, not as authentic statements from Ilya Sutskever. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake persona outputs for authentic statements, endorsement, or advice from Ilya Sutskever. <br>
Mitigation: State that the skill is a simulation based on public material and avoid presenting outputs as real statements from Ilya Sutskever. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on the simulated perspective for AI safety, business strategy, investment, or other high-stakes decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as an analytical lens only and require independent expert review before using them for high-stakes decisions. <br>
