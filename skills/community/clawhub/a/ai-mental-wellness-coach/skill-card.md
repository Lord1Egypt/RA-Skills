## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat mental-wellness coaching as professional diagnosis or treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as supportive education and direct severe distress, self-harm risk, diagnosis, and treatment decisions to licensed professionals or emergency services. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose sensitive personal or mental-health information during conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Advise users not to share information they would not want processed by their agent. <br>
Risk: Crisis or self-harm situations require timely human support. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate self-harm risk, severe distress, or emergency needs to local crisis resources, emergency services, or licensed care. <br>
