## Description: <br>
Chat as 大司马 (Da Sima / Ma Laoshi), the legendary Douyu game streamer, using a forceful entertainment persona with self-deprecating humor, exaggerated confidence, League of Legends catchphrases, and a coach-turned-entertainer speaking style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to make an assistant respond in Da Sima's comedic game-streamer persona for casual chat, gaming-style commentary, and light entertainment. It is not intended for neutral professional advice where precision and tone control matter. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The forceful entertainment persona may be unsuitable for professional, medical, legal, security, or other sensitive conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for casual entertainment or persona chat, and disable it when a neutral, precise assistant style is required. <br>
Risk: The skill instructs the assistant to stay in character across topics, which can reduce clarity for serious or high-stakes user requests. <br>
Mitigation: Route serious workflows to a non-persona assistant or require a human/operator check before relying on the response. <br>
