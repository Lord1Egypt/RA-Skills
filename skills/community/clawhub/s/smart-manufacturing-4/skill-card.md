## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Safety, OSHA/ISO compliance, maintenance timing, or production recommendations may be incomplete for a specific facility or regulatory context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support and validate them against internal procedures, qualified engineers, and current regulations before acting. <br>
Risk: Factory data sources such as PLC, MES, OPC-UA, MQTT, CSV, or JSON exports may contain sensitive operational information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary data and follow site data-handling policies before providing production, equipment, or factory-system exports to an agent. <br>
