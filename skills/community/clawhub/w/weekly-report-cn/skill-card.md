## Description: <br>
Automatically generate professional Chinese weekly or daily work reports from bullet-point inputs, turning raw task notes into polished reports with structured sections, highlighted achievements, and suggested next steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[goldath](https://clawhub.ai/user/goldath) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and teams use this skill to turn raw Chinese bullet notes into polished daily or weekly reports for technical, business, management, or cross-team updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reports may include inaccurate percentages, counts, dates, or business claims when source notes are incomplete or imprecise. <br>
Mitigation: Provide exact metrics in the input and review the generated report for factual accuracy before sharing. <br>
Risk: Work-report inputs may contain confidential workplace information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential data unless that is appropriate for the user's environment and policies. <br>
