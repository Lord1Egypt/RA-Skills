## Description: <br>
A high-school pastoral-care and adolescent education assistant that helps school staff and families reason through student management, risk grading, phone management, warning-letter follow-up, positive points, motivation rebuilding, and home-school communication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
School leaders, homeroom teachers, subject teachers, dorm staff, and parents use this skill for structured guidance on pastoral-care situations involving discipline, adolescent psychology, home-school communication, student records, and escalation decisions. It is advisory support and should be paired with local school policy, safeguarding procedures, privacy review, and trained human judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill gives guidance about minors, emergencies, discipline, dorm issues, device checks, and possible searches or confiscation workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as advisory support; require local school policy, safeguarding leadership, legal and privacy review, guardian and student rights procedures, and trained human judgment before acting. <br>
Risk: The security review marks the release suspicious because some guidance is under-safeguarded for school settings involving minors and emergencies. <br>
Mitigation: Review the skill carefully before installation, define escalation procedures for crisis situations, and avoid relying on it as a medical, legal, psychological, or disciplinary authority. <br>
