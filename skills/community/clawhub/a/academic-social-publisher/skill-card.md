## Description: <br>
Generate platform-optimized academic content from papers for ResearchGate, LinkedIn, and WeChat, with an optional video introduction script and opt-in video production when configured. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shaopanguo](https://clawhub.ai/user/shaopanguo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers and academic communicators use this skill to turn paper details into platform-specific ResearchGate posts, LinkedIn posts, WeChat articles, and short video narration scripts for publication announcements and research awareness campaigns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated academic posts may overstate findings or omit important context from the source paper. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated text against the paper before sharing, especially claims, statistics, contribution summaries, and calls to action. <br>
Risk: Confidential or unpublished research may be exposed if included in prompts or sent to optional video or TTS providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential unpublished research unless intended, and only enable video or TTS API keys for services the user trusts. <br>
