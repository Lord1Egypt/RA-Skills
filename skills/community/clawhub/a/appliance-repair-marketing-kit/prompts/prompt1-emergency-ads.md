## Instructions

Copy the prompt below into Claude (any version). Fill in every `[BRACKET]` field before submitting.

## The Prompt

```
You are a direct-response copywriter specializing in home services emergency marketing.

Generate an emergency appliance repair hook ad + 3 Google Local Services Ads for this business:

BUSINESS INFO:
- Business name: [BUSINESS NAME]
- City/State: [CITY, STATE]
- Brands serviced: [LIST BRANDS — e.g., GE, Whirlpool, LG, Samsung, Bosch, Maytag]
- Authorized service center: [YES/NO — if yes, list brands]
- Response time: [e.g., same-day, next-day, 24-hour]
- Repair warranty: [e.g., 90-day parts & labor, 1-year parts]
- EPA 608 certified: [YES/NO — required for refrigerator/freezer work]
- Phone number: [PHONE]
- Service area: [CITIES OR ZIP CODES]

OUTPUT 1: EMERGENCY HOOK AD (for Google/Meta/Nextdoor)
- Headline (30 chars max): urgent, problem-focused
- Subheadline (90 chars max): response time + brands + warranty
- Body (3 sentences): emergency scenario, solution, trust signals
- CTA: phone call or booking link
- 2 headline variants for A/B testing

OUTPUT 2: 3 GOOGLE LOCAL SERVICES ADS
Write one LSA for each top appliance category:
- LSA #1: Washer & Dryer Repair
- LSA #2: Refrigerator Repair
- LSA #3: Dishwasher & Oven Repair

For each LSA:
- Headline (30 chars): appliance type + city
- Description line 1 (90 chars): service specifics + response time
- Description line 2 (90 chars): trust signals + warranty
- Call-only extension: yes/no recommendation
- Note: Flag if EPA 608 required (refrigerator LSA)

COMPLIANCE RULES (follow strictly):
1. Do NOT use "authorized by [Brand]" unless authorized service center status is confirmed YES
2. Do NOT claim "guaranteed repair" — appliances have inherent diagnostic uncertainty
3. Refrigerator/freezer ads: only include refrigerant work if EPA 608 = YES
4. Urgency language (same-day, emergency): only if response time confirms this capability
5. Do NOT use superlatives ("best", "#1") without verifiable substantiation
6. Warranty claims must match the warranty provided — do not inflate

Output only the requested ad copy. No filler, no commentary.
```

---

## What You Get

- 1 emergency hook ad + 2 A/B variants
- 3 Google LSAs (washer/dryer, refrigerator, dishwasher/oven)
- Character counts validated for Google specs
- Compliance flags for EPA 608 and brand authorization

## Pro Tip

Run the LSAs exactly as written. Google LSAs for appliance repair average $25–45/lead — get the copy right from the start. The brand-specific ad (refrigerator) is your highest-converting LSA because refrigerator failures are emergencies and customers call immediately.
