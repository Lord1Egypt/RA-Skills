## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-25
Initial release. Adds an AICPA SSFS No.1- and Daubert-aligned economic damages workflow that captures engagement scope (role, matter type, jurisdiction, governing law, trier of fact, deadlines), pleading-anchored cause-of-action and damages theory framing, loss period (date of breach / injury, but-for period, mitigation cut-off), method selection (before-and-after, yardstick / benchmark, market-share / market-model, sales-projection, cost-plus, hypothetical negotiation for IP, life-care plan and earnings-loss for personal injury), incremental-cost analysis (variable vs fixed; avoided costs as offsets), mitigation and other offsets, discount rate and prejudgment-interest treatment, tax treatment (gross-up, lost-net-of-tax, Norfolk & Western), source-document index, Daubert / Kumho reliability self-check (relevance, fit, methodology, error rate, peer review, general acceptance, reasonable-certainty), assumption and limitation register, sensitivity scenarios, and a DRAFT calculation memo with an unsigned engagement-partner sign-off block — for engagement-partner and counsel review, never an executed expert report, never legal advice.
