## Description: <br>
AI-powered research advisor for graduate students - provides research assessment, proposal generation, literature analysis, advisor matching, and publication guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ZhenStaff](https://clawhub.ai/user/ZhenStaff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Graduate students and researchers use this skill to assess research ideas, prepare proposals, analyze literature, identify advisor matches, plan papers, and find academic resources. It provides structured guidance and draft materials that should be reviewed and customized by the user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The reviewed bundle only includes SKILL.md and README.md, while the documentation references installable Python code. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the actual Python package or repository before installation and before running setup commands. <br>
Risk: Generated proposals, literature-review text, and academic guidance may be inaccurate or inappropriate to submit unchanged. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated academic material as drafts, templates, or feedback, and review it for accuracy, citation quality, and academic-integrity requirements. <br>
Risk: The skill may offer to export generated materials to files. <br>
Mitigation: Choose the exact destination and confirm overwrites before allowing file exports. <br>
