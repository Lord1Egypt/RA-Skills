## Description: <br>
Guides podcast creators from show concept through positioning, production, platform operations, growth, and monetization for China's audio market. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External podcast creators, marketers, and agency teams use this skill to plan Chinese-market podcasts, define audience positioning, create episode roadmaps, manage production quality, distribute across podcast platforms, and evaluate growth or monetization options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Advice about ads, affiliates, paid content, or third-party podcast platforms could be mistaken for a binding business decision. <br>
Mitigation: Treat monetization and platform recommendations as planning guidance and review them against the user's business, legal, and platform requirements before publishing or purchasing. <br>
Risk: Podcast planning may involve unpublished guest information, private audio, credentials, or account access. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide passwords, API keys, private audio, or unpublished guest information unless the user intends the agent to process that material. <br>
