## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to generate recurring charisma, communication, influence, and social-dynamics briefs with practical daily experiments. It is intended for scheduled email and calendar delivery of concise insight sets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can maintain a persistent local insight ledger and send or update email and calendar items without clear approval or destination limits. <br>
Mitigation: Configure an allowed recipient, target calendar, and event update rule before use, and require preview and approval before any email is sent or calendar item is created or changed. <br>
