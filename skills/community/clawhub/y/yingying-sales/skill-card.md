## Description: <br>
Activates the Chinese-language "Yingying" sales negotiation coach for sales strategy, negotiation tactics, customer objections, pricing, deal strategy, scripts, channel cooperation, and customer relationship scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales teams, founders, account owners, and customer-facing agents use this skill to plan negotiations, structure offers, respond to objections, and draft practical sales scripts for deal conversations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sales guidance may include scarcity, pressure, or persuasion tactics that are inappropriate for a specific customer, jurisdiction, company policy, or ethical standard. <br>
Mitigation: Review negotiation tactics before use and adapt them to applicable law, customer commitments, and company sales policy. <br>
Risk: Users may enter sensitive customer personal data while seeking negotiation coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive customer personal data unless there is permission and a valid business need. <br>
Risk: The broad activation description may trigger on sales-adjacent requests where negotiation coaching is not the intended behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants sales or negotiation assistance before applying the skill's tactics. <br>
