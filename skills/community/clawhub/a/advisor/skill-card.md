## Description: <br>
Trusted advisor providing balanced, objective guidance on important decisions across business, personal finance, career, and strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and decision-makers use this skill as a sounding board for major business, finance, career, strategy, partnership, or life decisions. It helps users explore assumptions, compare tradeoffs, surface risks, and form a recommendation while leaving the final decision with the user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on conversational advice for consequential decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a sounding board and verify investment, legal, medical, employment, safety, or other consequential decisions with qualified experts before acting. <br>
Risk: A recommendation may miss context, assumptions, or user-specific constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Ask clarifying questions, surface assumptions and blind spots, compare multiple angles, and keep the final decision with the user. <br>
