## Use Case: <br>
HR, recruiting, and hiring teams use this skill to draft and optimize job descriptions, parse and rank resumes, generate interview questions, manage recruiting pipelines, and prepare candidate-facing recruiting content. Human reviewers remain responsible for screening, scoring, outreach, and hiring decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive HR and candidate data. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with candidate data the operator is authorized to process and verify compliance with applicable employment and privacy rules. <br>
Risk: Automated screening, scoring, matching, or outreach may influence hiring outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Keep human review over screening, scoring, outreach, and final hiring decisions. <br>
Risk: Resume parsing, match scores, interview questions, or pipeline analysis may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated reports and recommendations before acting on them. <br>
