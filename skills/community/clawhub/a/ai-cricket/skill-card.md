## Description: <br>
Ai Cricket is a Chinese-language cricket guide covering formats such as Test, ODI, and T20, field setup, bowling, batting, scoring, dismissals, equipment, major events, and cricket culture. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners and cricket viewers use this skill to get simplified Chinese explanations of cricket rules, match formats, scoring, dismissals, terminology, and major competitions. It is suited for beginners, IPL and T20 viewers, and people learning about Commonwealth cricket culture. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is an educational cricket guide and may not reflect current match schedules, live scores, or later rule updates. <br>
Mitigation: Verify time-sensitive cricket information against official competition, team, or governing-body sources before relying on it. <br>
