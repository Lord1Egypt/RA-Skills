## Use Case: <br>
External users and business analysts use this skill to generate persona-framed guidance for evaluating investment questions, commercial decisions, and media or technology trends through a Xiong Xiaoge-inspired perspective. The output should be treated as public-information-based perspective taking, not as the real person's views or specific investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may confuse persona-style business commentary with real investment advice or the real person's views. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the disclaimer visible, use explicit invocation, and independently verify current facts before relying on the output for business or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill is not suitable for specific stock picks, short-term investment calls, private views, or current factual claims. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for perspective taking and decision framing only, and route regulated or high-stakes investment questions to qualified human review. <br>
