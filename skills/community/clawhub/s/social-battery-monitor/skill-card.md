## Description: <br>
Helps assess social energy, evaluate upcoming events' impact, and create realistic plans with boundaries and recovery to manage social overload. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People planning social commitments use this skill to assess current social energy, estimate the cost and value of upcoming events, set boundaries, and reserve recovery time. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat reflective social-energy planning as medical or mental-health care. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as personal planning support and seek appropriate professional help for medical or mental-health concerns. <br>
Risk: Sharing sensitive personal details may expose information to the host agent or runtime. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing details that are not needed for the plan or that the user is not comfortable having processed. <br>
