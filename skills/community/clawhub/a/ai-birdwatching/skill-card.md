## Description: <br>
Ai Birdwatching provides Chinese-language birdwatching guidance for bird identification, birdsong recognition, optics and camera selection, China birding destinations, migration planning, bird photography, and conservation topics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and birdwatching enthusiasts use this skill to get Chinese-language guidance on identifying birds, recognizing bird calls, choosing equipment, planning birding trips in China, improving bird photography, and understanding bird conservation practices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded bird photos or detailed trip plans may expose personal information or sensitive location context. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal details, private locations, or identifying metadata unless the user is comfortable providing that context to the agent. <br>
Risk: Birdwatching advice could be misused in ways that disturb birds or habitats. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the skill's low-disturbance birdwatching ethics, including avoiding nest disturbance, excessive call playback, and intrusive photography practices. <br>
