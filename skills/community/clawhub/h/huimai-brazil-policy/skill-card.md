## Description: <br>
Policy Brazil helps agents answer Brazil market policy questions about import tariffs, ANVISA certification, and INPI intellectual property status. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Trade, logistics, and compliance teams use this skill to research Brazil import duties, certification paths, and intellectual property status. Important business or legal decisions should be verified against official Brazilian sources or a qualified professional. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Brazil tariff, certification, ANVISA, and INPI guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for final business or legal decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important conclusions against official Brazilian sources or a qualified professional before acting. <br>
Risk: Brazilian state ICMS rates vary, and the artifact states that results may use an average rate. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the destination state when ICMS matters and confirm state-level rates with official or professional sources. <br>
Risk: ANVISA approval timelines and status guidance are estimates. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current process status, timing, and requirements through ANVISA or a qualified local representative. <br>
