## Description: <br>
Assists Chinese-first learners studying Chapter 9, "Doing a Job Analysis Study," by explaining job analysis project flow, purpose-method fit, data collection, data quality, and reporting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students and learners use this skill as a Chinese-first study assistant for coursework on practical job analysis studies. It helps them connect job analysis purposes to methods, collection plans, data-quality checks, and reporting decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal or quasi-legal job analysis topics may be mistaken for current compliance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for textbook learning and verify current laws, regulations, and professional standards before applying guidance to real HR decisions. <br>
Risk: Statistical and research-design explanations may be overapplied outside the chapter study context. <br>
Mitigation: For production research, confirm the data structure, sample, scales, and analysis plan with qualified HR research or statistics expertise. <br>
