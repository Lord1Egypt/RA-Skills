## Description: <br>
Use KaspaCom DeFi MCP or CLI to query and transact across KaspaCom DEX, Lending, and LFG Launchpad on IGRA and Kasplex mainnet/testnet. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and DeFi operators use this skill to connect an agent to KaspaCom DEX, Lending, and LFG Launchpad workflows for market queries, position checks, swaps, liquidity changes, borrowing, repayment, and launch token actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide wallet-backed mainnet DeFi actions such as swaps, borrowing, liquidity changes, and launchpad purchases, which may be irreversible and move funds. <br>
Mitigation: Use testnet first, provide only a dedicated low-balance wallet key, and manually confirm every transaction before submission. <br>
