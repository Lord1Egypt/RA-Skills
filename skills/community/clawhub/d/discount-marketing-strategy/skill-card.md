## Description: <br>
Helps agents plan, implement, and optimize discount and promotional pricing strategies for SaaS, e-commerce, and tool products. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, product, and founder teams use this skill to structure promotional pricing, coupon, lifetime deal, and seasonal campaign recommendations with practical financial guardrails. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project context files used for discount planning may contain secrets or private business information. <br>
Mitigation: Review project context before use and remove secrets or private information that should not influence marketing advice. <br>
Risk: Broad discount-related triggers may activate the skill when the user intended a narrower pricing or page-generation workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill manually or tighten routing when stricter activation is needed. <br>
