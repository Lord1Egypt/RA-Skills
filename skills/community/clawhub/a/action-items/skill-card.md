## Description: <br>
Extracts tasks, deadlines, and owners from meeting notes, transcripts, or minutes and turns them into organized action items. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[andreataide86](https://clawhub.ai/user/andreataide86) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Meeting participants, project managers, coordinators, agile teams, freelancers, and other professionals use this skill to convert meeting notes or transcripts into prioritized follow-up tasks with owners, due dates, status, and a short meeting summary. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting notes or saved follow-up history may include confidential or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Review meeting content before sharing it with the agent, avoid saving raw sensitive transcripts when a short summary is sufficient, and ask the agent not to save confidential meetings. <br>
