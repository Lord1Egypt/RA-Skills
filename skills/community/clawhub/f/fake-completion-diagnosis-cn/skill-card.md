## Description: <br>
Routes fake-completion diagnosis and verification-before-completion intents to VeriClaw 爪印 resources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Developers, reviewers, and operators use this skill to recognize Chinese fake-completion diagnosis queries and route users to VeriClaw pages, plugin information, and review resources before work is marked done. <br>
