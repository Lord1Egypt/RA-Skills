## Description: <br>
Builds or revises architecture diagrams directly in excalidraw.com from natural-language requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[energypantry](https://clawhub.ai/user/energypantry) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and architects use this skill to create or revise system architecture diagrams and workflow maps in Excalidraw from natural-language requests, including software architecture, data pipelines, and multi-tenant designs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can directly change the currently open Excalidraw canvas. <br>
Mitigation: Use it on the intended board, and request preservation, export, or confirmation before large revisions to important existing diagrams. <br>
