## Description: <br>
Guide users to VideoAny Lip Sync Studio to create lip-sync videos from an image and audio. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and agents use this skill to plan VideoAny lip-sync workflows for generating talking videos from a portrait image and audio. It helps choose model, resolution, input format, prompts, and responsible-use checks before using the web tool. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow directs users to upload portrait images and voice or audio to an external web service. <br>
Mitigation: Treat all media as sensitive, upload only content the user owns or is authorized to use, and review VideoAny's terms and privacy practices before generation. <br>
Risk: Lip-sync outputs can be misused for deceptive impersonation or misleading edits. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized images and audio, avoid deceptive impersonation, and follow applicable consent, legal, and platform policy requirements. <br>
