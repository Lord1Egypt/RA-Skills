## Description: <br>
Pattern Converter helps an agent analyze the underlying structures and principles of an existing thing, extract transferable abstract patterns, and apply them to a new thing to generate concrete proposals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to structure pattern-transfer work: analyze a source example, extract abstract patterns, assess a target situation, apply the patterns, and produce a validated proposal. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms may cause the skill to be applied to tasks where pattern transfer is not the right method. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants pattern-transfer analysis before applying the workflow, especially for sensitive or highly factual tasks. <br>
Risk: Pattern transfer can produce speculative or misleading proposals when source facts, target constraints, or cited examples are uncertain. <br>
Mitigation: Use verified facts, show the reasoning basis, and mark uncertain cases or supporting claims as requiring verification. <br>
