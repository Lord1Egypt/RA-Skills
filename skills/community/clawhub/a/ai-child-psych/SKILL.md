# AI Child Psychology

Child development guidance from infancy through adolescence. Covers cognitive, social-emotional, and behavioral development with evidence-based parenting strategies.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Developmental Milestones
- Infant (0-12m): attachment, sensory development, motor skills
- Toddler (1-3y): language explosion, autonomy, social referencing
- Preschool (3-5y): theory of mind, pretend play, emotional vocabulary
- School age (6-12y): concrete operations, peer relationships, self-concept
- Adolescent (13-18y): identity formation, abstract reasoning, independence

### 2. Behavior Management
- Positive discipline strategies, natural/logical consequences
- Token economy, behavior charts, reward systems
- Time-in vs time-out approaches
- Managing tantrums, defiance, sibling rivalry
- Screen time boundaries and digital wellness

### 3. Emotional Intelligence
- Emotion coaching: labeling, validating, regulating
- Growth mindset cultivation, resilience building
- Self-esteem development, coping skills
- Anxiety management, mindfulness for children

### 4. Learning & Education
- Learning styles identification: visual, auditory, kinesthetic
- ADHD, dyslexia, autism spectrum support strategies
- Homework routines, study skills, motivation techniques
- Gifted children: acceleration, enrichment, social challenges

### 5. Family Dynamics
- Co-parenting strategies, blended family adjustments
- New sibling preparation, birth order considerations
- Family meetings, communication rituals
- Cultural and values-based parenting approaches
