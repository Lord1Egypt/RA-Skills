## Description: <br>
Amazon Aplus Generator helps Amazon sellers draft A+ content module plans, scenario-based copy, multilingual localization notes, and compliance self-checks from product category, selling points, and target language. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Amazon sellers and ecommerce content teams use this skill to turn product categories, selling points, and target marketplace languages into Amazon A+ module plans, localized copy guidance, visual recommendations, and compliance review checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with artifact guidance for DE, JP, FR, ES, IT, US, and UK marketplaces. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated product claims, comparisons, certification references, or marketplace compliance language may be inaccurate or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated content against product evidence, Amazon marketplace rules, and certification records before publishing. <br>
Risk: Broad keyword triggers may route generic product-description requests to this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the request is specifically for Amazon A+ content before relying on the output. <br>
