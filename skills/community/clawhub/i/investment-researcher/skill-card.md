## Use Case: <br>
Investment analysts and portfolio teams use this skill to structure A-share and Hong Kong equity research, including fundamental analysis, due diligence, valuation work, risk scenarios, and monitoring triggers. It supports research workflows but does not replace verification against primary filings, current market data, or professional investment judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Investment research output may be inaccurate, outdated, incomplete, or mistaken for final financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Verify conclusions against primary filings, current market data, official disclosures, and qualified human review before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may process confidential portfolio positions, proprietary research notes, or sensitive diligence material supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential information unless the agent runtime and memory settings are approved for that data. <br>
