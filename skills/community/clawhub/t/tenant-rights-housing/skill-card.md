## Description: <br>
Tenant rights knowledge and actionable defense templates for renters dealing with repairs, eviction, security deposits, mold, pests, habitability issues, or other landlord disputes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External renters and tenant advocates use this skill to identify jurisdiction-specific tenant rights, document housing problems, draft repair or deposit demand letters, and prepare eviction-defense next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with jurisdiction-specific legal localization required. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Housing and eviction rules vary by jurisdiction, and incorrect advice about withholding rent, filing claims, or responding to eviction notices can create legal or financial harm. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's state, city, or country rules before giving specific guidance, and direct users to legal aid or official tenant resources for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: Filesystem access could expose sensitive leases, photos, correspondence, or draft legal letters if users provide unrelated files. <br>
Mitigation: Use filesystem access only for files the user intentionally provides and remind users to review sensitive personal information before sharing documents. <br>
