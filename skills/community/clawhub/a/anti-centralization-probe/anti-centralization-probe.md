# SKILL: anti-centralization-probe

## Purpose
Identify single points of control and dependency creation; propose decentralization alternatives.

## Inputs
- `proposal` (actors, permissions, critical paths), `policy`

## Steps
1. Map control points and dependencies.
2. Flag single points; rate risk.
3. Output alternatives (multisig, timelock, separation).

## Output
- `centralization_probe_result`

## Safety Rules
- Structural critique only; no abuse instructions.
