## Description: <br>
Business English Coach helps Vietnamese tech professionals improve workplace English for Slack messages, email, standups, code reviews, meetings, 1-on-1s, status updates, and client communication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cngvc](https://clawhub.ai/user/cngvc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, developers, and technical professionals use this skill to rewrite, practice, and improve workplace English for common business and engineering communication scenarios. It provides concise corrections, tone guidance, phrase patterns, and practice prompts tailored to Vietnamese speakers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential company, client, legal, HR, or security-sensitive workplace text for language coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive text unless organizational policy allows it; redact private details before requesting revisions. <br>
Risk: Broad workplace routing language could trigger the skill for non-language workplace advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for English coaching and communication phrasing; seek qualified guidance for legal, HR, security, or business decisions. <br>
