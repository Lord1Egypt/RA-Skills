## Description: <br>
Break problems to fundamentals, rebuild from truth, and eliminate hidden assumptions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, developers, and external users use this skill to analyze complex or stuck problems by decomposing assumptions, identifying fundamental constraints, and rebuilding a solution from verified truths. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: First-principles analysis can overstate confidence if assumptions are incomplete or valid constraints are misclassified. <br>
Mitigation: Review the reasoning, verify constraints against domain evidence, and involve relevant subject-matter experts before acting on high-impact recommendations. <br>
