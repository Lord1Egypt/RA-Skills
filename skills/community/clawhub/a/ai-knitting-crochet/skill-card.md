## Description: <br>
Helps knitting and crochet users choose tools and yarn, learn stitch techniques, read pattern diagrams, plan garments and accessories, and troubleshoot common project issues. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External craft users and hobbyists use this skill for knitting and crochet guidance, including beginner learning, advanced stitch help, yarn and tool selection, pattern interpretation, garment sizing, amigurumi, and project troubleshooting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded pattern images, diagrams, or project files may contain copyrighted or personal materials. <br>
Mitigation: Only share materials the user is comfortable processing in the chat environment, and avoid uploading copyrighted or sensitive project files unless permitted. <br>
Risk: Craft instructions, sizing calculations, or pattern interpretation may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific yarn, gauge, or garment fit. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated guidance before use, make a swatch, and verify measurements and pattern changes before committing to a full project. <br>
