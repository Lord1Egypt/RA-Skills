## Use Case: <br>
Developers, writers, and AI safety communicators can use this skill to frame Xiaobai-style thought experiments, outreach narratives, and evaluation ideas for AI safety awareness. It is most useful as a manually reviewed writing and strategy reference. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill discusses collecting youth opinion data and archiving Gen-Z social-media discussion data without stated privacy limits. <br>
Mitigation: Do not connect it to social-media credentials, scraping tools, analytics pipelines, or identifiable youth data archives without explicit consent, aggregation-only handling, age protections, retention limits, and legal or ethics review. <br>
Risk: AI safety messaging and strategy outputs may be misleading or inappropriate if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a writing and strategy reference only, and have qualified reviewers check claims, audience fit, and safety framing before publication. <br>
