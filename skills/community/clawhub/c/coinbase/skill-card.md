## Description: <br>
Helps users think about digital assets like an institutional allocator by analyzing custody, risk isolation, exposure design, recordkeeping, and tax-aware operational discipline for long-term crypto asset management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users such as high-net-worth crypto holders, family-office-style operators, and treasury-minded allocators use this skill for conservative digital-asset governance, custody risk review, risk isolation, and tax-aware operating discipline. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake this unaffiliated educational guidance for official Coinbase support or regulated financial, legal, or tax advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general crypto custody and governance guidance, and use qualified professional review for legal, tax, compliance, or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose wallet secrets, exchange passwords, API keys, seed phrases, or other sensitive credentials while discussing custody structure. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide secrets or credentials; describe custody arrangements at a high level without exposing access material. <br>
Risk: Governance recommendations may be incomplete without jurisdiction, entity, custody, tax, and recordkeeping context. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for missing non-sensitive context before assessing the setup, and avoid claiming jurisdiction-specific certainty. <br>
