## Description: <br>
Jd Writer Zh helps HR and recruiting teams generate structured Chinese job descriptions from a role title and key requirements, including responsibilities, qualifications, and compensation or benefits details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[olina1ye](https://clawhub.ai/user/olina1ye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
HR teams, recruiters, and hiring managers use this skill to draft Chinese job descriptions for roles across industries such as internet, manufacturing, finance, and education. It turns role names and hiring requirements into recruiting copy with sections for responsibilities, qualifications, and compensation or benefits. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated job descriptions may contain inaccurate, incomplete, unfair, or non-compliant hiring language. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for factual accuracy, fairness, and alignment with organizational hiring policies before publication. <br>
Risk: User-provided hiring details may be included in the generated recruiting copy. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only role, compensation, and hiring details that are approved for inclusion in job postings. <br>
