## Description: <br>
Create compelling marketing hooks and content structures using the Puzzle-Driven Model for social media posts, newsletters, video scripts, and other marketing content that needs to capture attention. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, creators, and other external users use this skill to draft, structure, and evaluate attention-grabbing hooks for social posts, newsletters, video scripts, and related marketing content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing hooks can include unverified numbers, income claims, screenshots, timelines, or social-proof statements. <br>
Mitigation: Verify factual claims and supporting evidence before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill encourages extreme-case framing that can drift into misleading clickbait if unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Pair hooks with accurate reasoning and evidence, and avoid publishing claims that cannot be substantiated. <br>
