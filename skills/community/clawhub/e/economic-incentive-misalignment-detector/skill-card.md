## Description: <br>
Helps identify when marketplace incentives favor publishing volume over safety quality, creating structural pressure toward unsafe or lower-quality skills. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[andyxinweiminicloud](https://clawhub.ai/user/andyxinweiminicloud) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketplace operators, reviewers, and agent users use this skill to assess whether marketplace policies, publisher metrics, or public signals indicate systemic incentive misalignment around quality and safety. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's findings are economic analysis, not proof of wrongdoing or intent. <br>
Mitigation: Use the report as an analytical aid and review claims against the underlying marketplace data, policies, and public records. <br>
Risk: The skill may use incomplete marketplace-level data or public proxy signals. <br>
Mitigation: Treat conclusions as conditional when revenue figures, enforcement actions, review capacity, or investment data are unavailable. <br>
Risk: The skill declares curl and python3 as available tools. <br>
Mitigation: Supervise agent use of these tools and provide only marketplace data or policy documents intended for analysis. <br>
