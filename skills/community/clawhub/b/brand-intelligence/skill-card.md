## Description: <br>
Search, compare, and analyze companies using AI visibility scores, knowledge graphs, and competitive intelligence from geo.sig.ai. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sigaihealth](https://clawhub.ai/user/sigaihealth) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to research brands, compare competitors, summarize watchlists, find alternatives, and inspect market landscapes across industry verticals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Brand names, company lists, comparison targets, watchlists, and capability queries may be sent to geo.sig.ai or a configured API endpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential strategy or undisclosed watchlists unless the organization approves the external service. <br>
Risk: The optional API key is a sensitive credential. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the API key private and configure it only through approved credential handling. <br>
