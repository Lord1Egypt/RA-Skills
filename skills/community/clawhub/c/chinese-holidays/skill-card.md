## Description: <br>
Query Chinese statutory holidays, check if a date is a working day or holiday, and get holiday schedules. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to answer questions about Chinese statutory holidays, adjusted working days, weekends, and holiday schedules for supported years. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Holiday answers can become outdated because the skill relies on bundled static holiday data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the holiday data for the relevant year against official State Council announcements before relying on it for scheduling or business decisions. <br>
