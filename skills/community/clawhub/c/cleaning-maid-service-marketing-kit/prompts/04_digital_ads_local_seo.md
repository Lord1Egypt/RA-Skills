```
You are a performance marketing specialist and local SEO expert for home services. Generate a complete digital marketing system for a cleaning business.

- Business name: [NAME]

1. Google LSA: cleaning business category = "House Cleaning"; do NOT select "Junk Removal" or "Handyman" (separate license requirements)
2. Facebook before/after ads: reminder note required — Facebook policy requires real photos; staged/AI-generated before/after may trigger ad rejection

4. Airbnb outreach: do NOT reference "Airbnb Partner Program" or imply any formal Airbnb affiliation — use "experienced with Airbnb turnover" instead

6. Competitor mention in ads: no comparison ads naming competitors without verifiable proof claim
7. Google LSA budget: recommend minimum $300/month (under-budgeted LSA accounts get deprioritized in lead distribution)

GENERATE:

**SECTION 1: GOOGLE LOCAL SERVICES ADS (LSA)**
- Category selection: confirm "House Cleaning" + any qualifying subcategories
- Business description (150 chars max for LSA profile)
- 5 services to highlight in LSA profile
- Recommended background check/license/insurance setup notes for LSA verification
- Budget recommendation with explanation
- Bidding strategy note (cost-per-lead vs. max budget)

**SECTION 2: GOOGLE ADS — 5 AD GROUPS**

For each ad group, generate:
- Ad group name + primary keyword theme
- 15 headlines (max 30 chars each — count and list character counts)
- 4 descriptions (max 90 chars each)
- 5 keyword match types (broad match modifier, phrase, exact)
- Negative keywords for that group (5–10)

Ad Group 1: Recurring Maid Service / Weekly/Biweekly Cleaning
Ad Group 2: Move-Out / Move-In Cleaning
Ad Group 3: Deep Cleaning Service
Ad Group 4: Airbnb / Vacation Rental Turnover Cleaning
Ad Group 5: Commercial Office Cleaning

**SECTION 3: FACEBOOK LEAD GEN CAMPAIGN**

3-ad carousel campaign:
- Campaign objective: Lead Generation
- Audience: homeowners 28–60 in [city] + surrounding zips, interests: home improvement, Houzz, real estate
- Ad 1 (Acquisition): hook on time savings, before/after format

  - ⚠️ Note: real before/after photos only — staged photos may violate Meta policy
- Ad 2 (Trust): social proof angle, review count + rating
- Ad 3 (Airbnb segment): custom audience of Airbnb hosts (interest targeting + Facebook Marketplace rentals behavior)

Lead form fields to include:
- Full name, email, phone
- "What service are you looking for?" (dropdown: recurring, deep clean, move-out, Airbnb)
- "How many bedrooms?" (dropdown: 1, 2, 3, 4+)
- "Preferred start date" (date picker)

**SECTION 4: 30-DAY GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE POST CALENDAR**

Generate 30 posts (one per day), alternating types:
- Offer posts (5): seasonal deals, referral promos
- Educational posts (8): cleaning tips, product safety info
- Behind-the-scenes posts (5): team photos, before/after (with photo description)
- Review spotlight posts (5): paraphrase a 5-star review (do not copy verbatim without permission)
- Service feature posts (4): spotlight one service per post
- Seasonal/event tie-in posts (3): Las Vegas events, local holidays

Each post: max 300 characters. Include 1 hashtag suggestion.

**SECTION 5: LOCAL SEO KEYWORD CLUSTERS**

Generate 15 keyword clusters, each with:
- Primary keyword (head term)
- 5 long-tail variants
- Search intent (informational / transactional / navigational)
- Recommended page type (home, service page, blog, FAQ)
- Estimated monthly search volume range (low/medium/high)

Focus areas: house cleaning [city], maid service [city/neighborhood], deep cleaning [city], Airbnb cleaning service [city], move out cleaning [city], office cleaning [city], recurring cleaning service [city]

**SECTION 6: AIRBNB/VRBO HOST OUTREACH**

3 cold email templates for Airbnb/VRBO hosts:

Email A — Cold outreach to host with multiple listings (found on Airbnb listing page):
- Subject line (5 variants)
- Body (200 words): value prop for consistent 5-star turnover
- CTA: schedule a test clean

Email B — Referral introduction (sent by mutual contact):
- Subject + body (150 words)
- Softer tone, trust-based

Email C — Property manager outreach (manages 5+ Airbnb units):
- Subject + body (250 words)
- B2B frame: cost-per-turnover pricing, reliability, same-day availability
- Compliance: CAN-SPAM footer required

**SECTION 7: COMMERCIAL CLEANING COLD OUTREACH**

3 email templates:

Template 1: Office manager at small-to-medium office (5–50 employees)
Template 2: Property manager (commercial building)
Template 3: HOA manager (common area cleaning)

For each: subject line, 200-word body, CAN-SPAM footer (physical address + unsubscribe).
Angle: reliability + compliance + same team every visit.
```
