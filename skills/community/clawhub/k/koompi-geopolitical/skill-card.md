## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is broadly scoped for geopolitical and OSINT analysis, which can lead to overcollection or inclusion of unrelated sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the task scope explicit, avoid providing unrelated private data or secrets, and state trusted source requirements when provenance matters. <br>
Risk: Geopolitical analysis can be misleading if sources are biased, stale, or weakly corroborated. <br>
Mitigation: Require source reliability ratings, confidence levels, corroboration notes, assumptions, and intelligence gaps in generated briefings. <br>
