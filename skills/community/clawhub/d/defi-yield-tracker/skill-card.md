## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Wallet addresses and portfolio scans can reveal holdings and cross-chain activity. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer manual wallet-address entry or read-only wallet connections, and avoid sharing portfolio details beyond the analysis needed. <br>
Risk: Requests involving wallet access or DeFi positions may expose sensitive credentials or encourage unsafe transaction handling. <br>
Mitigation: Never provide seed phrases or private keys; review all recommendations manually before connecting wallets or executing transactions. <br>
Risk: Yield, APY, liquidity, audit, and gas data can change quickly and may be incomplete or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check live protocol, explorer, and market data before relying on any output for financial decisions. <br>
