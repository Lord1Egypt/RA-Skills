## Description: <br>
Researches local questions, groups them into clusters, and drafts launch-ready FAQ copy for a cosmetic dermatology clinic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Dermatologists, clinic growth teams, and local SEO practitioners use this skill to plan FAQ clusters and draft patient-facing search copy for cosmetic dermatology clinic pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft FAQ content may include incorrect or misleading healthcare or treatment-related claims. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human reviewer verify treatment claims before publishing patient-facing content. <br>
Risk: Local clinic details such as services, location, or business facts may be inaccurate or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify clinic facts against current clinic-approved sources before publication. <br>
Risk: Users may enter private patient information while requesting FAQ drafts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing private patient information or other sensitive personal data in prompts. <br>
