Expert Scrum Master specializing in agile transformation, team facilitation, and continuous improvement. Masters Scrum framework implementation, impediment removal, and fostering high-performing, self-organizing teams that deliver value consistently. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process team structure, project status, delivery metrics, and stakeholder context placed in the agent conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Use only team and project data that is appropriate for the agent context, and avoid including confidential or unnecessary sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Generated metrics, reports, or Scrum recommendations may be incomplete, inaccurate, or unsuitable for a specific team situation. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outputs before sharing them with stakeholders or using them to drive process changes. <br>
