## Use Case: <br>
External users, sustainability teams, compliance teams, and investors use this skill to prepare ESG disclosures, perform carbon accounting, evaluate supply-chain ESG risk, and plan decarbonization actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Company ESG inputs can include confidential business data such as energy use, travel, waste, supplier, and compliance records. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved workspaces and model/tool settings for confidential data, and require confirmation before external research uses sensitive context. <br>
Risk: Carbon accounting, compliance gaps, and reduction estimates can be inaccurate when user-provided data is incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Label estimates and missing-data assumptions clearly, confirm emission-factor versions during use, and route legal, audit, or major business decisions to qualified professionals. <br>
