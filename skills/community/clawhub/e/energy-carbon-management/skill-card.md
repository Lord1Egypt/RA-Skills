## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Inputs may include sensitive energy bills, fleet records, facility details, or supply-chain invoices. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved data handling practices, limit unnecessary sensitive details, and review generated summaries before sharing. <br>
Risk: Emissions factors, regulatory claims, renewable cost assumptions, and carbon market prices may be outdated or unsuitable for a specific jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify generated calculations, compliance statements, and market pricing against current authoritative sources before using them for reporting, compliance, or financial decisions. <br>
