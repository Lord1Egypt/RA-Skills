## Description: <br>
Acts as the user's goal-setting and accountability partner in the Goals topic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[GekaCross](https://clawhub.ai/user/GekaCross) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
OpenClaw users use this skill in personal goal conversations to set SMART goals, break them into milestones, track progress, update deadlines, and maintain a local goals record. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores personal goals and progress in knowledge/personal/goals.md, which may include sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing highly sensitive details, and periodically review or delete the local goals file when the information is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Progress updates and deadlines may become inaccurate if the local goals file is not reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed updates before relying on them, especially when goals affect commitments, finances, health, or career decisions. <br>
