## Use Case: <br>
External AI consultants, healthcare IT teams, and clinic-system builders use this skill to draft Macau-focused AI clinic proposals, including medical-record assistance, appointment automation, billing/CRM digitization, pricing, and technology stack options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Macau <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Healthcare deployments may expose patient information or affect clinical records without appropriate privacy, consent, and compliance controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use only as planning guidance unless the deployment has patient consent where required, data minimization, encryption, access control, audit logs, retention limits, vendor/security review, and Macau legal review. <br>
Risk: AI-generated records, summaries, or clinical codes may be incorrect or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Require clinician review and approval before AI-generated records, summaries, or codes are saved or used operationally. <br>
