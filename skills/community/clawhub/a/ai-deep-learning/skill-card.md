## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through systematic deep learning of a chosen domain using citation-network literature triage, a focused knowledge base, and multi-AI cross-examination. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Learners, researchers, and professionals use this skill to study a new field, survey academic literature, build a structured knowledge base, and prepare for deeper technical discussion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploading confidential, proprietary, personal, regulated, or paywalled materials to third-party AI or RAG services can expose data or violate usage rights. <br>
Mitigation: Use only materials you are allowed to process, avoid sensitive content unless policies permit it, and review the data policies of each AI or RAG service before uploading. <br>
Risk: AI-recommended papers, founders, or summaries can be incomplete or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check candidate literature against source databases, compare multiple AI suggestions, and ground final conclusions in the uploaded source documents. <br>
Risk: A saved personal knowledge base may retain documents or notes that should not be redistributed. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the knowledge base private unless redistribution is permitted, and periodically remove outdated, sensitive, or unauthorized source material. <br>
