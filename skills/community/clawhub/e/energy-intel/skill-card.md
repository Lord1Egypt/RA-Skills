## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, developers, and business users use this skill to research energy markets, commodity trends, supply chains, policy developments, and geopolitical risks across global energy and natural resources domains. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Energy market data and commodity prices can become stale quickly or vary across providers. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current figures against primary sources, include the latest release date, and cross-check material divergences across sources such as IEA, EIA, OPEC, and exchange data. <br>
Risk: Some referenced market intelligence sources may require separate commercial access or may not be available to every user. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm source availability before relying on restricted data and substitute accessible primary or public data sources when needed. <br>
