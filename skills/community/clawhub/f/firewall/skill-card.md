## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill for practical firewall configuration guidance across servers and cloud-provider firewalls, including SSH access, default-deny posture, service ports, Docker exposure, IPv6, and debugging. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Applying firewall changes without preserving access can lock administrators out of remote systems. <br>
Mitigation: Verify required ports first, keep a working remote session open, test access from a second session, and confirm provider-console recovery is available before applying changes. <br>
