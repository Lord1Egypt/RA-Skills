## [1.0.0] — 2026-04-12

### Added
- Initial release
- Dual-mode book writing companion: organizational backbone and creative writing partner
- Support for both fiction and nonfiction projects with automatic detection from context
- Multi-project tracking with separate workspaces per book
- Character bibles, plot arcs, world-building notes for fiction
- Argument structure, research notes, and source tracking for nonfiction
- Chapter outlines, continuity tracking, and progress tracking across all project types
- Brainstorming, drafting passages, and writer's block support on demand
