## Description: <br>
Research any topic across Reddit, X/Twitter, and the web from the last 30 days and synthesize findings into actionable insights or copy-paste prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dylanbaker24](https://clawhub.ai/user/dylanbaker24) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and content creators use this skill to gather recent web, Reddit, and X/Twitter discussions about a topic and turn them into patterns, cautions, source lists, and ready-to-use prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recent-topic searches may send user topics to Brave/web search, Reddit, and possibly X/Twitter through the configured bird session. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential topics, confirm which X/Twitter account or cookies bird uses, and disable the bird step when authenticated X/Twitter searches are not desired. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on recent public search results and social posts, which can include low-quality, misleading, or engagement-driven claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources before acting on findings and prefer explicit source lists when using generated insights or prompts. <br>
