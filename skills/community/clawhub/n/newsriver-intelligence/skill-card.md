## Description: <br>
NewsRiver Global Intelligence helps agents access quantitative news-price intelligence and DeFi execution workflows, including swaps, bridging, yield actions, AI chat, and wallet-backed API operations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External developers and agent operators use this skill to connect agents to market intelligence, historical news-price analysis, DeFi swaps, cross-chain bridging, wallet operations, paid proxy actions, and API-backed chat workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agents may initiate high-impact DeFi, wallet, bridge, or payment actions without enough stated user controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated low-balance wallet and require explicit human approval for every transaction, bridge, wallet action, and payment. <br>
Risk: Messaging and scraping proxy actions may create privacy, compliance, or unintended automation exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval for email, SMS, and scrape requests, and confirm provider logging, privacy, revocation, and spending-limit practices before use. <br>
