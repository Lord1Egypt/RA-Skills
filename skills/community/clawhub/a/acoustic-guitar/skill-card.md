## Description: <br>
Acoustic guitar practice strategies, fingerpicking, strumming, and progress tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners and instructors use this agent skill for acoustic guitar practice guidance, technique troubleshooting, fingerpicking or strumming exercises, and optional progress logging. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Practice discussions may be saved as local progress notes. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm what the agent writes to ~/acoustic-guitar/ and avoid including unrelated personal information in practice logs. <br>
