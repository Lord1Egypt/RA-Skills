## Description: <br>
Helps product managers turn early product requirements into a standardized feature-list table with module hierarchy, user stories, and dependency notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kira2red](https://clawhub.ai/user/kira2red) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, and delivery teams use this skill during product initiation or release planning to convert rough business needs, workflows, or ideas into a MECE feature list for engineering planning and project management. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill processes product requirements supplied in chat, which may include confidential business details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential requirements unless that is acceptable for the workspace. <br>
Risk: Generated feature lists may contain incomplete or misleading planning guidance if the source requirements are ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Have the product owner review module boundaries, dependencies, priorities, and version targets before using the table for delivery planning. <br>
