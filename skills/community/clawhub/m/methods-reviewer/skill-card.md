## Description: <br>
Review the structure, tense, voice, and details of the Methods section in academic papers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qiuxin-bit](https://clawhub.ai/user/qiuxin-bit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External authors, researchers, and academic writing assistants use this skill to review Methods sections for structural completeness, tense, voice, detail sufficiency, justification, and problem handling. It produces structured revision guidance, a sentence-by-sentence analysis table, a completeness score, and revised text. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The reviewer uses heuristic pattern matching, so its Methods-section feedback can be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the report as editorial guidance and have a qualified author or reviewer confirm revisions before submission. <br>
Risk: Suggested revised text may change scientific meaning if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Compare suggested revisions against the original experiment, protocol, and manuscript intent before incorporating them. <br>
