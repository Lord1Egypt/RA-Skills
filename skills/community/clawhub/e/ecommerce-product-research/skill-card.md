## Use Case: <br>
External users, ecommerce operators, and market researchers use this skill to compare product, ad, advertiser, and store signals from PipiAds before choosing niches or evaluating competitive positioning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Installing and running a third-party MCP package can execute code outside the skill text. <br>
Mitigation: Verify trust in pipiads-mcp-server before installation and prefer a dedicated PipiAds API key. <br>
Risk: Broad searches can consume paid PipiAds credits quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Scope requests by category, region, platform, and price band before running list or search tools. <br>
