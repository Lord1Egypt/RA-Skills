## Description: <br>
Manage contract and subscription renewals with a structured pipeline for reducing churn, optimizing pricing, forecasting revenue, and negotiating vendor renewals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Customer success, account management, operations, finance, and SaaS leadership teams use this skill to structure renewal workflows, price increases, revenue forecasts, churn prevention, save offers, and buy-side vendor renewal negotiations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: An agent connected to CRM, billing, email, document-signature, or customer-account tools could make customer-facing or financial changes based on renewal guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit human approval before contacting customers, changing prices, retrying payments, sending invoices, suspending accounts, cancelling service, or exporting customer data. <br>
Risk: Pricing, discount, churn-prevention, and contract-renewal recommendations may not fit a specific customer contract, market, or legal obligation. <br>
Mitigation: Have account, finance, and legal owners review proposals and contract actions before they are sent or executed. <br>
