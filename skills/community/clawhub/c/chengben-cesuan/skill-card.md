## Description: <br>
Construction Cost Calculator helps construction businesses estimate labor, material, equipment, management, profit, and tax components for project cost analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Construction cost teams, project managers, estimators, and finance staff use this skill to structure project cost estimates, compare target and actual costs, and draft quotation strategy guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes example labor, material, management-fee, profit, and tax rates that may be outdated or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify pricing, tax rates, material costs, and quotation assumptions against current local rules, market data, and project records before relying on outputs. <br>
Risk: Broad cost or budget trigger phrases may activate the skill before enough project details are available. <br>
Mitigation: Collect project type, location, area, schedule, structure, construction difficulty, and owner requirements before presenting detailed estimates. <br>
