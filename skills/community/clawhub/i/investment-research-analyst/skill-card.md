## Description: <br>
A multi-agent investment research framework for comprehensive stock analysis across fundamentals, news, sentiment, technical signals, bull and bear arguments, risk assessment, and fact-checking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gudai1972](https://clawhub.ai/user/gudai1972) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to gather public market information, compare bullish and bearish views, assess investment risks, fact-check key claims, and produce structured equity research reports and dashboards. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may create or deploy an interactive dashboard without clearly confirming user intent first. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before writing files, creating deployable assets, or deploying a dashboard, and confirm whether any deployment should be public or private. <br>
Risk: Investment reports can contain inaccurate, stale, or misleading market interpretations. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research assistance, verify cited data and assumptions against trusted sources, and avoid using the output as sole investment advice. <br>
Risk: Private portfolio, client, account, or proprietary research information could be exposed through data gathering or dashboard publication. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive financial information unless the destination is controlled and access restrictions are confirmed. <br>
