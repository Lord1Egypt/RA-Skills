## Description: <br>
Physical presence and delivery skills for public speaking and presentations. Use when someone needs to present at work, speak at an event, lead a meeting, or wants to develop commanding physical presence in group settings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external speakers, and meeting leaders use this skill to practice body-based public speaking mechanics such as stance, breath, eye contact, gesture, projection, recovery from blanking, and Q&A handling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat anxiety-management coaching as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use this as a public-speaking coaching aid only; consult a licensed clinician or pharmacist for severe anxiety or medication questions. <br>
Risk: Public speaking norms for eye contact, gesture, volume, and formality vary by culture and audience. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt delivery advice to the audience's cultural context and local expectations before applying it. <br>
