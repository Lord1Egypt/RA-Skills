## Description: <br>
Audit an X growth system. Use when reviewing positioning, content mix, reply strategy, profile conversion, analytics, and fit with public algorithm signals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and operators use this skill to review an X account, growth plan, or recent week of activity and identify practical improvements across positioning, content, replies, profile conversion, and analytics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include private analytics, unreleased business plans, or credentials in the workspace while asking for a growth review. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for the current agent workspace and omit credentials or sensitive business details. <br>
Risk: Growth recommendations based on public algorithm signals may be incomplete or misleading for a specific account. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as a review checklist and validate changes against actual account goals, analytics, and brand constraints before acting. <br>
