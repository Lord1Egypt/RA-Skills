## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to track intermittent fasting, extended fasts, eating windows, symptoms, and preferences in a style that adapts to their fasting protocol and experience level. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save sensitive fasting, symptom, preference, and schedule notes locally in ~/fasting/memory.md. <br>
Mitigation: Use on a trusted machine, avoid shared systems for sensitive tracking, and review or delete the local memory file when needed. <br>
Risk: Fasting guidance can be unsafe for some users, including people who are pregnant, underweight, diabetic, taking relevant medications, or have a current or past eating disorder. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as educational tracking support rather than medical advice, follow its contraindication and stop-condition guidance, and consult a qualified clinician for medical concerns. <br>
Risk: Extended or dry fasts can increase dehydration, electrolyte imbalance, hypoglycemia, and refeeding risks. <br>
Mitigation: Keep extended-fast safety checks, electrolyte and hydration reminders, stop-condition prompts, and conservative break-fast guidance in place. <br>
