## Description: <br>
Helps users review writing setbacks, clarify content positioning, and create a practical writing restart plan through a structured reflective conversation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and writing coaches use this skill to run a structured Chinese-language reflection about stalled writing, then turn the user's real experiences into audience positioning, content directions, and restart actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may ask users to discuss personal achievements, regrets, motivations, and writing goals. <br>
Mitigation: Users should share only what they are comfortable discussing and avoid sensitive personal details that are not needed for writing planning. <br>
Risk: A user in emotional crisis may need support beyond a writing restart review. <br>
Mitigation: Pause the structured review flow for crisis-like situations and keep use of the skill limited to writing reflection and planning. <br>
