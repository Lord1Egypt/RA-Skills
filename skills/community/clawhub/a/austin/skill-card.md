## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may keep a small local memory of Austin-related user preferences. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if comfortable with that local memory behavior, and use the disclosed ~/.austin/memory.md structure to inspect or manage stored context. <br>
Risk: Business, tax, immigration, housing, school, and safety guidance can be incomplete, outdated, or situation-dependent. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important decisions with official sources or qualified professionals before relying on the guidance. <br>
Risk: Alcohol, riding, or driving advice could create safety risk if followed uncritically. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on any advice that suggests riding or driving after drinking. <br>
