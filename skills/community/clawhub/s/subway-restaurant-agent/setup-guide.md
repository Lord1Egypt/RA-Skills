**5-Step Setup (Under 20 Minutes)**

1. Install ThumbGate (if not already): `npx thumbgate init --agent openclaw`
2. Create the Google Sheet above and share it with your OpenClaw service account.
3. **Recommended Voice Provider:** Sign up for [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/affiliates) (22% recurring commission for creators) to power the voice ordering features.
4. Copy the SKILL.md above into `~/OpenClaw/skills/subway-restaurant-agent/SKILL.md`
5. Run: `openclaw skill load subway-restaurant-agent`
6. Connect your WhatsApp number in OpenClaw settings.

Test with your own phone. Record a 45-second Loom demo showing it taking an order and doing an upsell.
