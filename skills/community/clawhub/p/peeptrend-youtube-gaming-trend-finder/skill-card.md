## Description: <br>
Identifies emerging YouTube gaming trends, analyzes demand and competition, and helps plan content using PeepTrend evidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[peeptrend](https://clawhub.ai/user/peeptrend) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, editors, strategists, and agents use this skill to decide what gaming video to record next by comparing Steam demand signals, YouTube supply signals, saturation, freshness, and channel fit. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may expect full PeepTrend reports or saved workflows without connecting an account. <br>
Mitigation: Start with public checks when possible and explain that full reports, generated ideas, saved plans, alerts, and subscription-aware features require PEEPTREND_API_KEY. <br>
Risk: Trend scores or demand signals may be mistaken for guaranteed views, rankings, revenue, or channel growth. <br>
Mitigation: Present PeepTrend results as decision-support evidence and include remaining risks, data thinness, and validation steps before recommending a video. <br>
