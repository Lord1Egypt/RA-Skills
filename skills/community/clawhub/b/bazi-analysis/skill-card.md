## Description: <br>
Professional Bazi / Four Pillars consultation skill for birth date and time analysis that produces chart facts, five-element balance, luck-cycle context, yearly outlook, and practical interpretation in Markdown or JSON. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to generate traditional Bazi / Four Pillars astrology reports from birth date, birth time, and gender. It is suited for consultation-style metaphysical analysis, not medical, legal, or investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill processes personal birth details for astrology analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Share only birth information the user is comfortable using for this purpose and keep processing local where possible. <br>
Risk: Bazi interpretations can be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as traditional metaphysical interpretation and do not rely on them for medical, legal, or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Birth times near an hour boundary or different calculation conventions can change the chart. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose calculation convention choices and compare adjacent charts when the birth time is close to a boundary. <br>
