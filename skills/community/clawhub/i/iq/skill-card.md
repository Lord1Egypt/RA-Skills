## Description: <br>
Generates IQ tests, brain teasers, logical puzzles, riddles, cognitive training challenges, interactive quizzes, brain games, and performance feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tools](https://clawhub.ai/user/tools) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to generate informal IQ-style assessments, cognitive practice questions, brain-training games, interactive HTML quizzes, answer explanations, and improvement guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated quiz or HTML files may overwrite user content if filenames are not controlled. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to confirm output filenames before writing files and avoid overwriting important files. <br>
Risk: Generated browser quiz files may be shared before their content is reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML and quiz content before sharing or publishing it. <br>
Risk: IQ ranges and performance labels can be mistaken for validated psychological assessment results. <br>
Mitigation: Treat scores and labels as informal practice feedback, not clinical or validated IQ measurements. <br>
