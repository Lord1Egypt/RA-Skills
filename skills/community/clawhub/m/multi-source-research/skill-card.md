## Description: <br>
A multi-source research assistant that gathers web, academic, social media, and news information, then deduplicates and classifies findings by source and credibility. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and researchers use this skill to collect, compare, deduplicate, and summarize information across search engines, academic sources, social platforms, and news outlets for research reports or topic monitoring. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad research queries can expose confidential, personal, or proprietary topics to external search engines, academic services, news sites, or social platforms. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for public or low-sensitivity topics, and ask the agent to confirm before collecting sensitive topics or monitoring social media. <br>
Risk: Social media and news results can be time-sensitive, duplicated, or unreliable. <br>
Mitigation: Record collection time, deduplicate similar results, classify sources by credibility, and flag uncertain claims for cross-checking. <br>
