## Description: <br>
Track prices, detect deals and manipulation, time purchases, and make informed buying decisions as a consumer or business buyer. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Consumers and business buyers use this skill to assess whether a listed price is fair, compare historical and total cost signals, detect manipulation, and decide whether to buy now, wait, or set a price alert. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases could cause the skill to add or track items the user did not intend to monitor. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for explicit confirmation before adding an item to a watchlist and periodically review stored watchlist entries with the user. <br>
