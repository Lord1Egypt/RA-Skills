## Description: <br>
Helps screen strong leading stocks using three moving averages, price-volume confirmation, breakout patterns, buy signals, and risk controls. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Traders and market researchers use this skill to structure stock-screening prompts around MA5, MA10, and MA20 bullish alignment, breakout behavior, volume confirmation, and position-risk rules. Outputs should be treated as research prompts rather than personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce actionable stock trade-planning guidance that may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research prompts, independently verify market data, and act only after confirming the plan fits the user's risk tolerance and account constraints. <br>
Risk: Screening signals based on price, volume, and moving averages can be stale, incomplete, or unsuitable for current market conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm prices, volumes, trading limits, market trend, and sector context with reliable market data before relying on any generated plan. <br>
