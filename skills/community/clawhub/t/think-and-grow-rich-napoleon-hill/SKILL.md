## Philosophy — 4 rules to remember

1. **Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve.** Your thoughts are the starting point of all achievement. The first step to becoming rich is thinking like a rich person.
2. **Desire is the starting point of all achievement.** Not a wish, not a hope — a burning, obsessive desire backed by a definite plan and a willingness to pay the price.
3. **Faith is the head chemist of the mind.** When you mix faith with your desire, your subconscious mind translates it into reality. Faith is the only antidote to failure.
4. **Persistence is the one quality that guarantees success.** Most people give up one step before their breakthrough. Those who persist are the ones who succeed.

## Core Framework Quick Reference

- **The 13 Steps to Riches** — 1) Burning Desire, 2) Faith, 3) Autosuggestion, 4) Specialized Knowledge, 5) Imagination, 6) Organized Planning, 7) Decision, 8) Persistence, 9) Mastermind, 10) Sex Transmutation, 11) Subconscious Mind, 12) The Brain, 13) The Sixth Sense.
- **Burning Desire** — The starting point. Not "I wish" but "I will." A desire so strong that failure is unthinkable. Must be backed by a definite plan and willingness to pay the price.
- **Faith** — Visualization and belief that your desire is already realized. Faith turns thought vibrations into their spiritual equivalent.
- **The Mastermind Alliance** — The coordination of knowledge and effort between two or more people who work toward a definite purpose in a spirit of harmony.
- **Persistence** — The sustained effort required to turn desire into its monetary equivalent. Persistence is a state of mind that can be cultivated.
- **The Six Basic Fears** — Fear of Poverty, Fear of Criticism, Fear of Ill Health, Fear of Loss of Love, Fear of Old Age, Fear of Death. Every fear is a form of these six.

## Key Principles

1. **Define your burning desire with absolute clarity.** Write it down. Be specific about exactly how much money you want and by when. A vague desire produces vague results.
2. **Believe you have already received what you ask for.** Faith is not hope — it is the conviction that your desire is already on its way. Act as if it is already yours.
3. **Use autosuggestion to reprogram your subconscious.** Read your written desire aloud morning and night. Your subconscious accepts what you repeat with emotion.
4. **Acquire specialized knowledge, not general knowledge.** General knowledge is useless for wealth-building. Specialized knowledge, organized and intelligently directed, is power.
5. **Make decisions quickly and firmly. Change them slowly.** Successful people decide quickly and change slowly. Unsuccessful people decide slowly and change frequently.
6. **Form a mastermind alliance.** No individual has enough experience, education, or knowledge to ensure success without the cooperation of others.
7. **Master fear by analyzing it.** Most fears are not real — they are imagined. Analyze what you fear and what would happen if your fear came true. Nine times out of ten, nothing happens.

## Anti-Pattern Summary

The book's core correction: Most people fail to achieve wealth because they think of it as something that happens to others — they wish for it but don't pursue it with burning desire. They give up at the first obstacle. They are governed by fear instead of faith. Hill's framework replaces wishful thinking with definite purpose, fear with faith, and quitting with persistence.
