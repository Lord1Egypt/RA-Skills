## Description: <br>
Analyze text documents for word count, reading time, readability scores, keyword density, sentence statistics, and multilingual text counts with no external dependencies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zacjiang](https://clawhub.ai/user/zacjiang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, content teams, SEO practitioners, and developers use this skill to analyze local text files for document statistics, readability, reading time, and keyword frequency. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Terminal or chat output may expose filenames and frequent words from analyzed documents. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill only on files intended for analysis and review outputs before sharing them outside the intended audience. <br>
