## Description: <br>
Generates a structured marketing audit report from aggregated data using a single GPT-4.1-mini API call with six predefined sections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AdarshVMore](https://clawhub.ai/user/AdarshVMore) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and marketing teams use this skill to turn aggregated audit data from Instagram, Meta Ads, keyword, competitor, and website sources into a structured marketing audit report. It is intended for agents that need a concise markdown report and retained raw data from one OpenAI model call. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audit data sent to OpenAI may include secrets, private customer data, or internal-only business details. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the data is approved for OpenAI processing and redact unnecessary sensitive information before running the skill. <br>
