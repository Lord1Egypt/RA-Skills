## Description: <br>
Converts LibreOffice-supported office documents, presentations, and spreadsheets to PDF. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[minchoCoin](https://clawhub.ai/user/minchoCoin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and automation agents use this skill to normalize user-chosen Office documents, presentations, and spreadsheets into PDF files with LibreOffice headless commands. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Untrusted Office files can expose document converters to malformed-file risks. <br>
Mitigation: Install LibreOffice only from trusted package sources and convert only documents and output folders the user intends to process. <br>
