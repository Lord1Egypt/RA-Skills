## Description: <br>
Helps independent teachers create weekly schedules, detect scheduling conflicts, manage lesson-hour ledgers, and prepare leave, makeup, rescheduling, and renewal-warning workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External independent teachers use this skill to plan weekly lesson schedules, check teacher and student availability conflicts, maintain lesson-hour package records, and prepare confirmed leave, makeup, rescheduling, and renewal-warning actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Student schedules and lesson-hour records may contain sensitive learner or family information. <br>
Mitigation: Use pseudonyms, avoid real names and parent contact details, and keep scheduling records limited to the fields needed for lesson planning. <br>
Risk: Schedule changes, write-backs, or parent-facing renewal messages could be incorrect if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Require teacher confirmation before writing schedule changes or sending parent-facing messages. <br>
