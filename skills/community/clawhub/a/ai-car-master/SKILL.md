# AI Car Master

Comprehensive automotive guidance covering car buying, maintenance, repair diagnostics, driving techniques, and ownership optimization for all vehicle types.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Car Buying
- New vs used evaluation: depreciation, warranty, financing
- Vehicle history report reading: Carfax, AutoCheck
- Test drive checklist: engine, transmission, suspension, electronics
- Price negotiation strategies, dealer vs private party
- EV and hybrid specific buying considerations

### 2. Maintenance
- Scheduled maintenance by mileage: oil changes, filters, fluids
- Tire care: rotation, pressure, tread depth, seasonal swaps
- Brake system: pads, rotors, fluid flush intervals
- Battery, alternator, starter diagnostics
- Air conditioning, heating system maintenance

### 3. Repair Diagnostics
- Check engine light: OBD-II code reading and interpretation
- Common symptoms: noises, vibrations, smells, leaks diagnosis
- DIY repair feasibility assessment
- Finding reliable mechanics, understanding estimates
- Parts sourcing: OEM vs aftermarket comparison

### 4. Driving Skills
- Manual transmission: clutch control, rev matching, hill starts
- Winter driving: snow chains, traction control, skid recovery
- Performance driving: heel-toe, trail braking, racing line
- Off-road basics: 4WD engagement, airing down, recovery
- Eco-driving: hypermiling techniques, regenerative braking

### 5. Ownership
- Insurance: coverage types, deductibles, claims process
- Registration, title transfer procedures
- Resale value optimization, trade-in timing
- Modification guidance: performance, aesthetic, legal compliance
