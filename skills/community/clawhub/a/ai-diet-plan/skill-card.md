## Description: <br>
Provides personalized nutrition plans tailored for weight loss, muscle gain, medical conditions, and lifestyle goals, based on nutritional science. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to draft personalized diet plans, meal-prep guidance, grocery lists, macro targets, and nutrition suggestions for common lifestyle and fitness goals. Medical nutrition guidance should be treated as general information unless reviewed by a qualified clinician. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Diet guidance may be inappropriate for pregnancy, children, eating disorder recovery, kidney disease, diabetes, hypertension, or other medical nutrition needs. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as general planning support and consult a qualified clinician for medical or high-risk nutrition decisions. <br>
