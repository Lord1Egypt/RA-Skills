## Description: <br>
Learn English through serialized fiction with naturally embedded vocabulary, grammar spotlights, comprehension checks, and A2-B2 story episodes across mystery, city life, and sci-fi series. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners and educators use this skill to generate serialized English-reading episodes, vocabulary review, quizzes, and study guidance at A2-B2 levels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided state or vocabulary terms may be echoed into generated prompts and outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste passwords, API keys, or sensitive personal information into --state or --words. <br>
Risk: Generated learning content may contain mistakes or level mismatches. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated episodes, vocabulary definitions, and grammar explanations before relying on them for formal study or teaching. <br>
