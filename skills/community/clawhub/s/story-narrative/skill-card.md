## Description: <br>
Story Narrative helps agents transform ideas or scenarios into structured narratives, three-act hero journeys, extracted solutions, and reality-mapped reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to guide an agent through story-based ideation: reconstructing a topic as a narrative, converting it into a three-act hero journey, extracting solution ideas, and mapping story elements back to practical scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms may invoke narrative framing in writing or planning tasks where it is not desired. <br>
Mitigation: Disable or avoid the skill for tasks that should not use narrative framing. <br>
Risk: Generated narratives and solution reports can include unverified cases, data, or references when the source facts are uncertain. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the artifact's factual discipline: use verifiable information and label uncertain cases, data, or references as pending verification. <br>
