## Description: <br>
Evaluates AI side-hustle projects, courses, and earning claims with heuristic scoring, red-flag detection, and practical cautionary guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[freedompixels](https://clawhub.ai/user/freedompixels) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to screen AI side-hustle opportunities, training courses, and promotional claims before committing time or money. It produces an informal credibility assessment with risk signals, realistic expectations, and avoidance guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake heuristic side-hustle scores for financial or business advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reports as informal screening guidance and independently verify any opportunity before paying, investing, or sharing personal information. <br>
Risk: Documentation links to external AISoBrand pages outside the local skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review external sites separately before entering data or relying on claims made outside the skill artifact. <br>
Risk: Pattern-based checks can miss novel scams or over-penalize legitimate but aggressively marketed offers. <br>
Mitigation: Use the report as one input alongside human review of evidence, refund terms, seller history, and realistic effort requirements. <br>
