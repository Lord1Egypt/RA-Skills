## Description: <br>
Systematic competitor research and intelligence gathering skill. Covers how to find competitor fanpages, analyze their ad strategies, extract key insights, and build competitive advantage for Boss Store Vietnam in the cosmetics/health retail sector. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and business operators use this skill to find active Facebook ad competitors, analyze public competitor pages and ad patterns, and turn the findings into competitive reports and response proposals for Boss Store Vietnam. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Vietnam <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor search terms and Facebook page URLs may be sent to external research tools. <br>
Mitigation: Review searches and target URLs before tool use, and avoid submitting sensitive internal strategy or customer data. <br>
Risk: Saved competitor notes can become outdated or no longer appropriate to retain. <br>
Mitigation: Review, update, or clear saved competitor records on a regular schedule. <br>
