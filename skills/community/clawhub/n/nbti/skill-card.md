## Description: <br>
NBTI is a Chinese entertainment quiz that asks 8 absurd questions and generates a humorous mental-state personality result. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[enihsago](https://clawhub.ai/user/enihsago) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users run this skill as an entertainment-only Chinese meme personality quiz. The agent asks 8 questions, scores N/B/T/I dimensions, and returns a shareable humorous result card with a clear non-medical posture. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake humorous NBTI results for a real mental-health or personality assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Present the quiz as entertainment only and keep the non-medical disclaimer visible in generated results. <br>
Risk: Broad NBTI or mental-state mentions may activate the skill in contexts where the user is expressing real distress. <br>
Mitigation: Use the quiz only for clear entertainment intent, and avoid applying it to serious mental-health disclosures. <br>
Risk: The skill uses Chinese-only prompts and edgy mental-health humor. <br>
Mitigation: Set expectations that the experience is Chinese-language meme content and stop or reframe if the tone is inappropriate for the user. <br>
