## Description: <br>
AI Compliance helps agents assess AI tools and use cases against EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, GDPR, OECD, financial services regulations, and related governance frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[OpieTaylor911](https://clawhub.ai/user/OpieTaylor911) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Compliance, security, legal, risk, and governance teams use this skill to generate AI compliance checklists, risk classifications, gap analyses, vendor assessments, acceptable use policy drafts, remediation steps, training guidance, and jurisdiction-specific AI regulatory reviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The bundled references include company-specific security and compliance telemetry that may be inappropriate for other organizations. <br>
Mitigation: Review the references before installation and remove or rewrite organization-specific sections as placeholders unless they are intended and authorized for the deployment context. <br>
Risk: The skill can guide sensitive compliance and security workflows involving prompts, DLP logs, webhook events, or privileged remediation commands. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval of scope, redaction, and local paths before allowing an agent to inspect raw operational data or propose privileged remediation actions. <br>
