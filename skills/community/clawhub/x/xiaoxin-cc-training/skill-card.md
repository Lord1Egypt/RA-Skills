## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for educational explanations of A-share ultra-short-term trading concepts, including leading-stock tactics, market emotion cycles, call-auction signals, post-market review, and trading discipline. Its guidance should be treated as general education rather than personalized financial advice. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill discusses speculative A-share short-term trading tactics that may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as general education; require users to consult qualified financial guidance and make independent decisions before real trades. <br>
Risk: Buy, sell, stop-loss, and risk-control examples may be applied mechanically outside the user's circumstances or current market conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Present trading cues as examples, add clear disclaimers, and encourage review against current market data and personal risk tolerance. <br>
