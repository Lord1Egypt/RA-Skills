## Description: <br>
Drafts HACCP and FSMA Preventive Controls food-safety plans with hazard analysis, CCP/preventive-control determination, monitoring, corrective-action, verification, and recordkeeping sections for PCQI or HACCP-team review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Food-safety employees, consultants, and external quality teams use this skill to collect facility, product, process, and hazard evidence and draft HACCP or FSMA Preventive Controls plans for qualified review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the skill may require sharing sensitive facility, recipe, supplier, customer, or process information with the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the organization's data-handling policy before entering proprietary information and avoid sharing confidential details with unapproved services. <br>
Risk: A generated HACCP or food-safety plan could be incomplete, incorrect, or unsuitable if treated as final. <br>
Mitigation: Treat every output as a draft; a qualified PCQI or HACCP team should validate, review, sign, and approve the plan before operational, regulatory, or audit use. <br>
Risk: Unsupported hazards, critical limits, monitoring frequencies, or process parameters could create misleading food-safety guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Require documented sources for hazards and limits, flag unknown or unvalidated values, and route them to PCQI, HACCP-team, or process-authority review. <br>
