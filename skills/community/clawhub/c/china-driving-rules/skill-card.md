## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may provide inaccurate legal exam or traffic-rule content. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as an informal study aid and verify exam answers, penalties, and legal rules with official traffic-management sources. <br>
Risk: The skill includes medical, first-aid, emergency, insurance, and driving-safety guidance that users could rely on in safety-critical situations. <br>
Mitigation: Use emergency services, licensed clinicians, police determinations, and insurer policy terms for real incidents, injuries, health conditions, medications, and fitness-to-drive decisions. <br>
