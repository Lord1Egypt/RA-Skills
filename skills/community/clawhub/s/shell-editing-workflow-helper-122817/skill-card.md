## Description: <br>
Helps maintainers and users create practical workflows, artifacts, checklists, analysis, implementation support, and verification notes for shell and file-editing safety work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, maintainers, and users use this skill to turn shell and file-editing safety requests into actionable plans, deliverables, checklists, analysis, code-change support, and verification notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be invoked broadly for edit, write, and shell-related requests without clear safety boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, narrow triggers or disable implicit invocation if broad activation is not desired, and review generated shell or file-editing guidance before execution. <br>
