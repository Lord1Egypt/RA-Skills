## Use Case: <br>
Developers, job candidates, and technical interviewers use this skill to organize interview preparation across recent question discovery, coding practice, system-design mock interviews, review reports, and mobile-friendly study cards. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow can create GitHub Issues while organizing an interview question bank. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target repository and preview issue content before running any automated issue creation. <br>
Risk: Interview questions gathered from external search or trend sources may be stale, incomplete, or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review sourced questions and generated study materials before relying on them for preparation or interviewer use. <br>
