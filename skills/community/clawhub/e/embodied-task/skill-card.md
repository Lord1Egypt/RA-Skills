## Description: <br>
Helps an agent execute ordinary tasks with more presence by considering who is involved and why the moment matters. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TTtael](https://clawhub.ai/user/TTtael) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill when they want an assistant to add a brief reflective pause and a more present tone to functional tasks such as searching, fetching, writing, or execution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can make assistant responses more reflective or personal, which may be inappropriate for high-stakes or strictly factual work. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for ordinary tasks and prefer neutral, evidence-first responses for medical, legal, financial, safety-critical, or factual decisions. <br>
