## Description: <br>
Provides a local command-line options calculator that estimates call and put delta plus a simple score from user-provided price, strike, expiry, and volatility inputs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kingaiwork](https://clawhub.ai/user/kingaiwork) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents can use this skill to run a simple local options analysis helper for exploratory calculations. It should not be treated as a trading platform or relied on for trading decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
