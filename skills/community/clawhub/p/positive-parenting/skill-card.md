## Description: <br>
Positive Parenting helps agents provide Chinese-language parenting coaching for families with children ages 0-15, covering emotional regulation, behavior guidance, learning support, social challenges, adolescent communication, and parent self-support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gglinux](https://clawhub.ai/user/gglinux) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Parents, caregivers, and family-support agents use this skill to turn a parenting situation into age-aware, practical coaching. It prioritizes stabilizing the parent first, then offers scripts, immediate actions, child-facing content when appropriate, and longer-term relationship or habit guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Parenting conversations can include sensitive family and child details. <br>
Mitigation: Use host applications with appropriate chat storage and privacy controls for sensitive family information. <br>
Risk: The skill provides supportive coaching but is not medical, legal, psychological, or emergency help. <br>
Mitigation: For safety risks or urgent concerns, contact appropriate local professionals or emergency services. <br>
