# Prompt 1: Workflow Audit & AI Opportunity Map

**Tier:** FREE  
**Time to complete:** 5–10 minutes  
**Output:** Full workflow audit with AI opportunity rankings and time savings estimates

---

## Setup Instructions

Copy the prompt below into Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI assistant. Fill in the [BRACKETS] with your business information before sending.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are an AI business operations consultant specializing in workflow automation for small and mid-size businesses.

My business details:
- Business type: [e.g., boutique hotel, marketing agency, dental practice, e-commerce store]
- Industry: [industry]
- Team size: [number of employees or "solo"]
- Annual revenue: [range or "prefer not to say"]
- Main revenue activities: [what actually makes you money — e.g., client projects, selling products, bookings]

My biggest time drains (what eats the most hours per week):
1. [Time drain #1 — be specific, e.g., "responding to customer inquiries, about 8 hours/week"]
2. [Time drain #2]
3. [Time drain #3]
4. [Time drain #4 — optional]
5. [Time drain #5 — optional]

Other recurring work:
[List any other regular tasks: scheduling, invoicing, reporting, content creation, social media, HR admin, etc.]

Please generate a complete Workflow Audit & AI Opportunity Map with:

1. FULL WORKFLOW INVENTORY
   - List every workflow I mentioned (and any you identify I likely have based on my business type)
   - For each: current estimated hours per week
   - Automation difficulty: Easy (under 4 hrs to set up) / Medium (4–16 hrs) / Complex (16+ hrs)

2. AI OPPORTUNITY SCORE
   - Rate each workflow 1–10 for AI automation potential
   - Scoring criteria: 10 = repetitive, rule-based, high volume; 1 = requires human judgment, creativity, or relationship
   - Include one-line rationale for each score

3. TOP 5 HIGHEST-ROI OPPORTUNITIES
   - Ranked by (hours saved × ease of implementation)
   - For each: current hours/week, projected hours/week after automation, net weekly time savings

4. QUICK WINS (implement in under 2 hours)
   - List any automations that can be set up today with minimal tech skill
   - Name the specific AI tool or approach for each

5. STRATEGIC WINS (2–4 weeks to implement, high long-term payoff)
   - List automations worth the investment despite setup time
   - Expected monthly time savings once running

6. WHAT NOT TO AUTOMATE
   - Flag any workflows where AI would reduce quality or damage relationships
   - Brief explanation for each

Format the output as a structured report I can share with my team or use to brief a VA or consultant.
```

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Be specific about time drains — "email" is too vague; "responding to customer inquiries about booking changes" is actionable
- Include tasks you do yourself AND tasks your team does
- Don't pre-filter — even tasks that seem "too small" to automate are worth listing; they add up
