## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The bundle gives agents broad engineering automation abilities, including command execution, code mutation, and local testing. <br>
Mitigation: Use disposable or sandboxed repositories, review commands before execution, and keep production credentials unavailable to the agent. <br>
Risk: Skill testing and security-auditing workflows may inspect or execute untrusted skill content. <br>
Mitigation: Do not run skill tests against untrusted skills on a normal host; isolate the workspace and review generated reports before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Generated migrations, infrastructure changes, and release plans may be incomplete or unsuitable for production. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated artifacts as drafts and require expert review, test coverage, and normal change-management checks before deployment. <br>
