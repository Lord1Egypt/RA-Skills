## Description: <br>
魔鬼代言人 — 反附和批判性评审 Skill。当用户需要方案评审、决策质疑、批判性反馈、红队思考、避免群体思维时使用。触发场景包括：用户在提出方案/想法/决策后需要外部挑战、想听不同意见、需要多视角审查、做重大决策前想要魔鬼代言人把关。自动召唤5个虚拟利益相关方角色进行尖锐质询，输出结构化改进建议。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to challenge plans, decisions, and product ideas with adversarial, multi-stakeholder critique. It produces structured questions and prioritized improvement guidance when the user wants to avoid agreement bias or groupthink. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may provide deliberately blunt or confrontational critique and may trigger more broadly than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when adversarial review is desired, and ask explicitly for balanced or non-adversarial feedback when a softer posture is needed. <br>
Risk: The critique style may be inappropriate as the sole input for sensitive decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat its output as one review perspective and avoid using it for sensitive decisions unless a confrontational review style is intentionally requested. <br>
