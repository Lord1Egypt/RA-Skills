## Description: <br>
Generates privacy policies in Markdown covering GDPR, CCPA, and APPI from business, data collection, and third-party service details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[noahcraft-open](https://clawhub.ai/user/noahcraft-open) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, founders, and developers use this skill to draft a privacy policy for a website or application based on their business details, collected data, cookies, third-party services, and applicable privacy regimes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated privacy policies may omit jurisdiction-specific requirements or business-specific data practices. <br>
Mitigation: Have the drafted policy reviewed against the actual data flows, third-party services, and applicable legal obligations before publishing it. <br>
