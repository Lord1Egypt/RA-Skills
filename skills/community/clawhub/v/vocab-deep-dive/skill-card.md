## Description: <br>
深度解析英语单词的全方位学习信息，包括释义、用法、搭配、例句、同反义词、词根词缀、易错点和雅思考点。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
English learners and IELTS candidates use this skill to request concise, structured vocabulary study notes for a supplied word, including meanings, usage patterns, example sentences, related words, common mistakes, and exam-oriented guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional external dictionary verification may send the checked word to dictionaryapi.dev. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid external lookup for private or sensitive terms unless the user explicitly accepts that disclosure. <br>
Risk: Vocabulary explanations and IELTS guidance may be incomplete or imprecise for obscure, specialized, or ambiguous words. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the intended context and verify uncertain meanings before relying on the output for assessment or publication. <br>
