## Description: <br>
Financial Astrology analyzes sidereal planetary positions, lunar phases, Nakshatra, houses, aspects, combustion, and market sentiment for astrology-themed commentary on gold, Bitcoin, and other financial markets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kimminhpro](https://clawhub.ai/user/kimminhpro) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and market-analysis users can use this skill to generate offline, astrology-themed market commentary for gold, Bitcoin, stocks, and short-term trading scenarios. The output is supplementary commentary and should not be treated as professional financial advice or an automated trading signal. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat astrology-themed market commentary, stop-loss language, natal-chart output, or Dasha output as professional or personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as supplementary commentary only, require independent financial judgment, and avoid using its output as the sole basis for trading decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill includes trading-style signals that could be connected to automated trading without an appropriate review. <br>
Mitigation: Do not connect the skill to automated trading or account actions unless that automation receives a separate financial, safety, and operational review. <br>
