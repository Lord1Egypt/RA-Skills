## Description: <br>
SCI journal selection assistant for meta-analysis and systematic review papers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wenhan9739](https://clawhub.ai/user/wenhan9739) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers and academic authors use this skill to choose SCI journals for meta-analysis or systematic review manuscripts. It gathers manuscript context, checks pre-submission readiness, compares journals by JCR and CAS metrics, and returns a tiered Reach/Target/Safety submission strategy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Journal metrics, acceptance rates, APCs, and submission requirements can change after the bundled data is published. <br>
Mitigation: Verify journal metrics and requirements against current publisher or indexing sites before acting on a recommendation. <br>
Risk: Manuscript details may include confidential research information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the manuscript details needed for selection and redact confidential content when possible. <br>
Risk: The activation wording may be broader than ideal for generic journal-selection requests. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for meta-analysis and systematic review journal selection, and confirm the manuscript type before applying its recommendations. <br>
