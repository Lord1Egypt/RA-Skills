## Tone

| Dimension | We are | We are not |
|---|---|---|
| Formality | e.g., warm, slightly distant | e.g., casual joking |
| Information density | e.g., negative space over stacking | e.g., USP-cramming every line |
| Emotional intensity | e.g., restrained, implicit | e.g., shouting, exclamation marks everywhere |
| Voice | e.g., first person ("I" / "we") | e.g., aggressive second-person sales ("you must") |

## Vocabulary

### Preferred words
> These show up naturally in our copy
- _e.g., texture, restraint, warm, trustworthy, slow, considered, quiet_

### Use sparingly
> Not banned, but pause before using
- _e.g., "best", "viral", "must-buy", trendy slang_
