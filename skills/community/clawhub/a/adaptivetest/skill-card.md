## Description: <br>
Design and implement computerized adaptive testing systems using Item Response Theory, including ability estimation, item calibration, test algorithms, item selection, stopping rules, and implementation patterns for K-12, certification, placement, and diagnostic assessments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, assessment designers, and psychometric teams use this skill to design, calibrate, simulate, and implement computerized adaptive tests with IRT. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the examples with real learners can involve sensitive student identifiers, answers, scores, or ability estimates. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymous or pseudonymous identifiers, restrict access, define retention limits, and follow applicable education privacy requirements. <br>
