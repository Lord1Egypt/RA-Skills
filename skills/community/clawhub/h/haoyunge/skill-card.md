## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for educational roleplay and perspective-taking around Haoyunge-style A-share short-term trading commentary. Outputs should be treated as simulated commentary and learning material, not as official statements or personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake simulated Haoyunge-style trading commentary for personalized financial advice or instructions to trade. <br>
Mitigation: Present responses as educational or roleplay commentary and ask for neutral risk analysis before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The persona style may sound like an official statement from Haoyunge or affiliated accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify that the skill is a simulation based on public material and not an official statement from the person or associated publishers. <br>
