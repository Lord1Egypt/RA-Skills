## Description: <br>
Prosperity Investing Framework helps an agent apply a sector-cycle investing framework to classify industries and stocks, screen candidates, structure research notes, and reason about buying, holding, and exit discipline. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[pcchris1995](https://clawhub.ai/user/pcchris1995) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and investment researchers use this skill to maintain an investment-research notebook, evaluate industry cycle position, classify stocks by prosperity profile, and produce structured research outputs. Its outputs should be treated as research prompts rather than personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce stock timing, valuation, and buy or sell discipline guidance that may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research prompts, verify assumptions against current market data and qualified advice, and require the user to make final investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill can update persistent investment research notes and tracking files. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation before writing or updating investment files, and restrict use to intentionally provided research materials. <br>
Risk: Broad report and file inputs can lead to stale, incomplete, or misleading research conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the user to supply current source materials, identify assumptions in the output, and avoid relying on unsourced or outdated inputs. <br>
