## Description: <br>
Run natural-language data query workflows for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Amazon Ads, and Shopify Ads reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[danyangliu-sandwichlab](https://clawhub.ai/user/danyangliu-sandwichlab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operators, growth teams, and agents use this skill to turn advertising performance questions into metric pulls, concise findings, prioritized campaign actions, guardrails, and handoff payloads across Meta, Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Amazon Ads, and Shopify Ads. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Campaign, budget, bidding, or handoff recommendations could be applied without enough account context. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before applying them in an ad account and require validation checkpoints for budget, bidding, campaign, or handoff changes. <br>
Risk: Advertising performance data may include confidential business information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the confidential performance data needed for the requested analysis. <br>
