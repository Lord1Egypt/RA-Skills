## Description: <br>
Optimize Etsy titles, tags, and descriptions using keyword research and competitor analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ncreighton](https://clawhub.ai/user/ncreighton) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External Etsy sellers and their agents use this skill to improve listing search visibility and conversion quality through keyword research, competitor analysis, title and tag optimization, description rewrites, and batch export workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can use Etsy and keyword-research credentials for listing optimization. <br>
Mitigation: Use limited-scope credentials where possible, keep secrets out of prompts and generated files, and rotate keys if they are exposed. <br>
Risk: Generated titles, tags, descriptions, and projected impact estimates may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a seller's brand or marketplace rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review all recommendations and generated listing changes before publishing them to Etsy. <br>
Risk: Competitor analysis and optional exports can send business data to third-party services such as Google Sheets, Slack, SEMrush, or marketplace endpoints. <br>
Mitigation: Enable only the integrations needed for the workflow and avoid exporting sensitive shop, customer, or competitive data unless the destination is approved. <br>
Risk: Automated competitor scraping may be inappropriate for some environments or service terms. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that any competitor data collection complies with applicable platform terms and local policy before using it. <br>
