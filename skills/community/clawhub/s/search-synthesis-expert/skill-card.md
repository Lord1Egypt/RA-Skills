## Description: <br>
Uses sequential-thinking to decompose tasks and formulate search strategies, browser automation (Playwright) for multi-source information search and collection, and final review of synthesized results. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, analysts, developers, and fact-checkers use this skill to plan, collect, and synthesize multi-source web research into a traceable report. It is suited for deep research, competitive analysis, technical investigation, and fact-checking where source comparison and gap marking matter. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
