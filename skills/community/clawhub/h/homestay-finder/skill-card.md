## Description: <br>
特色民宿 searches and recommends specialty homestays, inns, and other non-standard lodging using structured filters and natural-language recommendation requests. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[travel-skills](https://clawhub.ai/user/travel-skills) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel-planning agents use this skill to find specialty homestays by destination, keyword, point of interest, dates, price, and sort order, or to request AI-assisted lodging recommendations in natural language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel destinations, dates, points of interest, keywords, and free-form recommendation text are sent to the skill's cloud proxy services and onward to travel platform APIs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering highly sensitive personal details in search fields or recommendation prompts. <br>
Risk: Prices, availability, and booking links may change after the skill returns results. <br>
Mitigation: Verify final price, availability, policies, and booking details on the travel platform before purchase. <br>
