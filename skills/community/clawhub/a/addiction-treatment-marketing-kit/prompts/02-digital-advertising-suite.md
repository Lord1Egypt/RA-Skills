## LOCAL SEO CONTENT (Campaign Type C)

Generate one 800-1,200 word locally optimized article per primary keyword cluster. Articles are organic content — no LegitScript gate.

**Target keyword clusters (generate one article per cluster):**
- "Addiction treatment [city] Nevada"
- "Drug rehab [city] Nevada"
- "Alcohol treatment center [county] Nevada"
- "Opioid treatment program Henderson Las Vegas"
- "Dual diagnosis treatment Nevada"
- "PHP IOP outpatient treatment [city]"

**Article structure:**
1. H1: Include primary keyword naturally
2. Introduction: 100-150 words — problem awareness, local context (do NOT open with outcome statistics)
3. What to look for in a Nevada addiction treatment program: ASAM levels of care, DPBH licensing, accreditation (CARF/Joint Commission), clinical staff credentials, MAT availability, insurance acceptance
4. Facility presentation: DPBH license, ASAM service lines, clinical staff credentials (Medical Director, Clinical Director), accreditation, LegitScript certification (for paid advertising transparency)
5. Nevada-specific resources: DPBH behavioral health resources, SAMHSA 988 Helpline, Nevada Crisis Call Center
6. Insurance and access: how to verify benefits, SAMHSA NDATSS (Nevada Drug and Alcohol Treatment Service System) for publicly-funded options
7. FAQ: 5-7 questions addressing common local search intent
8. CTA: "Contact our clinical intake team" — phone number with clinical intake hours
9. Schema ready: mark structured data insertion points for JSON-LD (see Prompt 3)

**SEO requirements:**
- Primary keyword in H1, first paragraph, one H2, meta description
- ZERO outcome statistics
- ZERO "best rehab in Nevada" superlatives without citation
- Include SAMHSA 988 Helpline prominently (Google YMYL quality signal for healthcare)
- Internal link opportunities to service line pages
- Author attribution: if possible, attribute to Medical Director or Clinical Director for E-E-A-T (expertise, experience, authoritativeness, trustworthiness)

**Q&A responses (GBP questions answered by facility):**

A: "Yes, [FACILITY_NAME] is in-network with [LIST VERIFIED PAYERS]. For a complete benefits verification specific to your plan and service needs, please call [PHONE]. A member of our intake team will verify your benefits at no charge."
[NEVER CONFIRM SUD TREATMENT FOR A SPECIFIC INDIVIDUAL IN A PUBLIC GBP RESPONSE]

A: "[FACILITY_NAME] follows SAMHSA evidence-based treatment protocols and is [CARF/Joint Commission] accredited. Recovery is an individual journey — our clinical team matches each patient to the appropriate ASAM level of care based on clinical assessment. We cannot publish aggregate outcome statistics, as recovery outcomes depend on many individual factors."

Q: "Is [specific person's name] a patient at your facility?"
A: "Federal law (42 CFR Part 2) prohibits us from confirming or denying whether any individual is receiving services at our facility. If you have a concern about someone's welfare, please call 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline) or 911 in an emergency."
