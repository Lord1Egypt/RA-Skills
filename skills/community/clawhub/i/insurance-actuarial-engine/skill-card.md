## Description: <br>
AI-driven insurance underwriting, claims processing, and actuarial modeling for policy comparison, risk assessment, premium calculation, fraud detection, and regulatory compliance across life, health, property, and casualty insurance lines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External insurance professionals, actuarial analysts, and insurtech teams use this skill to assess underwriting risk, model reserves and pricing, process claims, compare policies, and support regulatory analysis across insurance lines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Underwriting, claims, health, financial, and policy data may include sensitive or regulated information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm data is authorized for use, redact or minimize sensitive fields where possible, and follow organizational privacy, retention, and compliance requirements. <br>
Risk: Insurance, actuarial, and regulatory outputs may affect high-stakes underwriting, claims, pricing, reserving, or compliance decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Require qualified human review before operational use and verify outputs against current rules, source data, and approved actuarial or compliance methods. <br>
