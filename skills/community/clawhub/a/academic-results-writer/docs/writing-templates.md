## 7. Chinese Academic Writing Templates

Default style: professional, restrained, accurate, logical, consistent with Chinese psychology paper conventions.

### 7.1 Overall Trend
- "总体来看，结果显示……"
- "从图 X 可以看出，……"
- "与预期一致，……"
- "与假设不同，……"
- "值得注意的是，……"

### 7.2 Figure/Table Invitation
- "图 X 展示了……"
- "表 X 汇总了……"
- "如图 X 所示，……"
- "相关统计结果见表 X。"

### 7.3 Key Result
- "进一步分析显示……"
- "简单效应分析表明……"
- "事后比较显示……"
- "该效应在控制……后仍然显著。"

### 7.4 Non-significant Result
- "该差异未达到显著水平。"
- "未观察到可靠的组间差异。"
- "该结果不支持……的预期。"
- "需要注意的是，非显著结果不能被解释为两组完全等同。"

### 7.5 Marginal Significance
- "该结果接近传统显著性水平，但未达到 p < .05 的标准。"
- "因此，该趋势只能作为探索性结果理解。"

### 7.6 Limited Implication
- "这一模式提示……"
- "该结果初步表明……"
- "这些结果与……解释一致，但仍需在 Discussion 中进一步讨论。"

### 7.7 Variable Translation Fidelity Rule
- **self-esteem** → "自尊" (not "自我肯定感" or "自信")
- **perceived social support** → "领悟社会支持" or "感知社会支持" (consistent throughout)
- **depressive symptoms** → "抑郁症状" (not "抑郁" or "抑郁症")
- All mediator/moderator/outcome translations must be consistent throughout
- Use standard psychology field translations; do not invent
- When uncertain: keep original English name and prompt user to confirm

## 8. English Academic Writing Templates

### Overall Pattern
> Overall, the results showed that …

### Figure/Table Invitation
> As shown in Fig. 1, … / Table 1 summarizes the descriptive statistics and pairwise comparisons.

### Main Effect
> A significant main effect of [factor] was observed, F(df1, df2) = xx, p = xx, ηp² = xx.

### Interaction
> The interaction between [factor A] and [factor B] was significant, F(df1, df2) = xx, p = xx, ηp² = xx. Simple effects analysis further indicated that …

### Null Finding
> The effect did not reach statistical significance, t(df) = xx, p = xx, suggesting that the present data do not provide sufficient evidence for a reliable difference between conditions.

### Marginal Finding
> The effect approached but did not reach the conventional significance threshold, p = .0xx, and should therefore be interpreted as exploratory.

### Mediation
> The indirect effect of X on Y through M was statistically significant, indirect effect = xx, 95% CI [xx, xx]. It is important to note that these analyses are based on cross-sectional data and do not establish temporal precedence or causality.

### Regression
> After controlling for [covariates], X remained a significant predictor of Y, β = xx, SE = xx, t = xx, p = xx, ΔR² = xx.

### Meta-Analysis
> Under a random-effects model, the pooled effect size was small to moderate, Hedges' g = xx, 95% CI [xx, xx], p = xx, with moderate heterogeneity, I² = xx%, τ² = xx.

### Qualitative Theme
> Three primary themes emerged from the interview data regarding [topic]. First, participants described … as captured by one participant: "…".

### Correlational Reminder
> These findings should be interpreted as correlational because all variables were measured at the same time point.
