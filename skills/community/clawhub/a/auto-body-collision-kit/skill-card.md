## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-audited marketing content for Nevada auto body and collision repair shops, including campaigns, service pages, ads, reputation workflows, and local SEO copy screened against stated OSHA, EPA, FTC, and Nevada advertising concerns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Auto body shop owners, collision repair managers, and marketing agencies use this skill to draft Nevada-focused campaigns, service pages, ads, review responses, referral copy, and local SEO assets with prompts for compliance review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Nevada, United States (Clark County primary) <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A gift-card template says cards can be applied toward deductibles, which the server security guidance flags as unsafe for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Remove deductible-application language from gift-card copy before use and keep deductible-related claims aligned with insurer and Nevada requirements. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing could be mistaken for legal or regulatory advice in a heavily regulated repair context. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated copy against current Nevada, FTC, OSHA, EPA, warranty, SMS, insurance, and local advertising rules, plus the shop's actual records and certifications. <br>
