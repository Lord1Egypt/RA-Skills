## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language perspective skill that simulates Luo Yonghao's public thinking frameworks and communication style for analysis, feedback, and decision review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[binyuli](https://clawhub.ai/user/binyuli) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can ask for a clearly simulated Lao Luo perspective when they want critique, decision framing, or product and business feedback in his public communication style. The skill should keep the simulation labeled and should not be treated as actual statements from Luo Yonghao. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may be mistaken for actual statements from Luo Yonghao. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses clearly labeled as a simulation based on public information and avoid presenting generated content as his real views. <br>
Risk: Automatic roleplay triggers may surprise users who did not intend to enter a simulated persona. <br>
Mitigation: Require or preserve explicit user intent before using the simulated perspective, especially in shared or professional contexts. <br>
Risk: Factual, business, or high-stakes claims may be incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify important claims and decisions with authoritative sources before relying on the output. <br>
