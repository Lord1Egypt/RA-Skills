## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce sellers use this skill to turn product details into marketplace-ready listing copy for Taobao, Tmall, Xianyu, and Pinduoduo, including titles, image copy, detail-page text, and keyword tags. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated listing copy can include unsupported claims about rankings, warranties, refunds, reviews, or marketplace rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated copy before publishing so claims are accurate and comply with the target marketplace. <br>
Risk: Product prompts may include unnecessary sensitive business or personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the product details needed to draft the listing and omit sensitive personal or business information. <br>
