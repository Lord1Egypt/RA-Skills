## Description: <br>
Leap Of Faith helps users analyze major decisions under irreducible uncertainty by combining known, unknown, and unknowable factors with tacit-knowledge reflection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[clarkchenkai](https://clawhub.ai/user/clarkchenkai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a structured reflection aid for high-uncertainty decisions across business, relationships, health, finance, identity, creative work, ethics, and life direction. It guides the agent to separate known facts, researchable unknowns, and irreducible uncertainty before producing a leap, do-not-leap, or defer recommendation with conditions and stop-loss boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can strongly influence sensitive medical, mental-health, financial, legal, emergency, or irreversible life decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as a reflection aid and direct users to qualified professionals or emergency resources for licensed, urgent, or high-stakes advice. <br>
Risk: The skill gives clear leap, do-not-leap, or defer recommendations that may overstate confidence in uncertain personal situations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep recommendations conditional, preserve residual uncertainty, and include stop-loss boundaries before encouraging action. <br>
