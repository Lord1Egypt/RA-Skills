## Description: <br>
Helps agents debug commands, tools, automations, integrations, and unclear failures through a structured root-cause process instead of guesswork. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jisang1000](https://clawhub.ai/user/jisang1000) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and agent operators use this skill to narrow failure symptoms, isolate likely layers, test one hypothesis at a time, confirm root cause, and report the fix or next blocker. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The debugging checklist mentions anti-bot and authentication troubleshooting, which can be misused outside authorized systems. <br>
Mitigation: Use those steps only for services the user owns or is authorized to test, and prefer official APIs or explicit user-approved access paths when authentication or automation defenses are involved. <br>
