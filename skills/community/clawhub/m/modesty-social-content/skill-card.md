## Description: <br>
Helps create, schedule, and optimize social media content for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and other platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, and content teams use this skill to plan social content strategy, draft platform-specific posts, build content calendars, repurpose existing content, and optimize engagement. Scheduling and scraping-related workflows require a SkillBoss API key and should be reviewed before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires a sensitive SkillBoss API key for scheduling-related workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Install only where use of that API key is approved, keep the key out of generated content, and restrict the key to the minimum access needed. <br>
Risk: Broad publishing or scheduling workflows could post to the wrong account, platform, content, or time without clear approval. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated posts and schedules as drafts until the user explicitly approves the content, destination account, platform, and scheduled time. <br>
Risk: Scraping guidance may collect content that is restricted by platform rules, privacy expectations, or copyright obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm permission and platform-policy compliance before collecting target content or using scraped material in generated social content. <br>
