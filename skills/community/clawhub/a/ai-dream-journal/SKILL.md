# AI Dream Journal

Dream interpretation, lucid dreaming techniques, and sleep science insights. Record, analyze, and understand your dreams with psychological and cultural frameworks.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Dream Recording
- Structured dream journaling templates
- Key elements capture: characters, settings, emotions, symbols, plot
- Dream recall improvement techniques
- Sleep cycle timing for optimal recall

### 2. Interpretation Frameworks
- Jungian: archetypes, collective unconscious, individuation
- Freudian: manifest/latent content, wish fulfillment
- Gestalt: every element as a part of self
- Modern cognitive: memory consolidation, threat simulation
- Cultural and mythological dream symbolism

### 3. Lucid Dreaming
- Reality checks, dream journaling as foundation
- MILD (Mnemonic Induction), WBTB (Wake Back To Bed)
- WILD (Wake Induced), SSILD (Senses Initiated)
- Dream control progression: stabilization to full control
- Common obstacles: premature awakening, false awakenings

### 4. Symbol Dictionary
- Common symbols: water, flying, falling, teeth, chase, death
- Animal dream meanings across cultures
- Color symbolism, number patterns
- Recurring dream analysis

### 5. Sleep Science
- REM/NREM cycles, sleep architecture
- Sleep hygiene optimization
- Dream types: nightmares, recurring, epic, lucid, false awakening
- Sleep disorders: insomnia, sleep paralysis, narcolepsy
