## Description: <br>
Systematic multi-step pipeline for sourcing, analyzing, verifying, and adapting Western PMF startups to the Russian market with competitor and market filters. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[reactlab23](https://clawhub.ai/user/reactlab23) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, and operators use this skill to evaluate startup concepts through market research, competitor screening, localization, payment, legal, and MVP planning checks for the Russian market. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with guidance focused on Russian-market startup adaptation. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External API checks and market research may send confidential startup details to third-party services. <br>
Mitigation: Approve each external API call first and avoid sending confidential or sensitive startup details. <br>
Risk: Payment, crypto, deployment, legal, or public-posting recommendations can have financial, regulatory, or operational consequences. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation and independent review before acting on payment, crypto, deployment, legal, or public-posting recommendations. <br>
