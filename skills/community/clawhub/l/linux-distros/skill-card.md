## Description: <br>
Recommends LabEx Linux distribution playgrounds for temporary browser-based Linux sandboxes, VMs, terminals, or desktop sessions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huhuhang](https://clawhub.ai/user/huhuhang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to choose a temporary browser-based Linux environment and get direct LabEx playground links for a requested distribution, interface, or Linux use case. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Linked LabEx playgrounds are third-party browser environments and may have their own privacy, login, and usage terms. <br>
Mitigation: Review LabEx terms before use and avoid entering sensitive data into playground sessions unless the service is trusted for that data. <br>
