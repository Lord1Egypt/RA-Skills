## Description: <br>
Create high-converting TikTok ad scripts for Shopify and dropshipping products, including viral hooks, UGC-style scripts, A/B test variations, conversion-focused CTAs, and production notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External Shopify merchants, e-commerce marketers, and creative teams use this skill to generate TikTok ad script packages for product campaigns. It helps produce hook banks, main ad scripts, UGC-style versions, CTAs, A/B testing plans, and production guidance from product and audience details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate authentic-looking UGC ad copy that may imply personal experience, endorsement, or sponsorship without adequate disclosure. <br>
Mitigation: Review each script before publication, remove fake personal claims, and add required advertising or sponsorship disclosures. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing claims may overstate product benefits or include unsupported performance, health, beauty, or savings claims. <br>
Mitigation: Check all claims against product evidence, legal requirements, and platform advertising policies before using the copy. <br>
