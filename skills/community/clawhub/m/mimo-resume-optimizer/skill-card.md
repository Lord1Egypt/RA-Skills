## Description: <br>
Resume Optimizer helps users diagnose and improve Chinese or English resumes by quantifying achievements, tuning ATS keywords, adapting structure, and preparing optional LinkedIn, cover letter, and application-strategy guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qqyougitcom](https://clawhub.ai/user/qqyougitcom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External job seekers and career-support agents use this skill to analyze a resume against a target role, improve wording and structure, increase ATS compatibility, and produce a concise optimization report with suggested revisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume drafts and job-search materials can contain personal contact details or other sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize or redact private messages, account identifiers, contact details, customer records, and other personal data before use. <br>
Risk: Resume optimization can drift into overstated or fabricated experience. <br>
Mitigation: Keep all rewritten content grounded in the user's real experience and review recommendations before submission. <br>
