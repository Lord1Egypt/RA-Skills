# YouTube Keyword Research Assistant

## Purpose

YouTube Keyword Research Assistant helps content creators discover search-driven video opportunities on YouTube. The skill analyzes a topic, niche, product, or audience segment and generates keyword recommendations that can be used for video titles, descriptions, tags, playlists, and content planning.

The goal of this skill is to help creators identify topics with audience demand while uncovering long-tail keyword opportunities that may have lower competition.

## What This Skill Does

When a user provides a topic, the skill:

1. Generates primary keyword ideas.
2. Creates long-tail keyword variations.
3. Identifies informational, commercial, and tutorial search intent.
4. Suggests video title ideas optimized for click-through rate.
5. Recommends YouTube tags.
6. Creates content cluster recommendations.
7. Suggests related video topics for channel growth.
8. Recommends external keyword validation tools.

## Keyword Research Methodology

### Primary Keywords

Core search terms that directly describe the topic.

### Long-Tail Keywords

More specific phrases with clearer intent and audience targeting.

### Search Intent Analysis

Each keyword is classified according to likely intent:
- Informational
- Educational
- Commercial
- Comparison
- Tutorial

## Content Cluster Generation

The skill recommends related videos that can be grouped into playlists or content series to improve channel authority and audience retention.

## Video Title Generation

The skill creates title suggestions using proven YouTube title patterns focused on relevance, clarity, and click potential.

## Limitations

The skill provides research ideas and recommendations. Search volume and competition should be validated with external tools when necessary.
