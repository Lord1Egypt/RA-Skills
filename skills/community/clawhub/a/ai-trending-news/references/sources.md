# Source tiers for AI trending news

Use these sources as the default intake list. Prefer official sources first, then editorial coverage, then research, then community trend signals.

## Tier 1: Official sources

These are the most reliable for product launches, model releases, and policy changes.

- OpenAI News / blog
- Anthropic News / blog
- Google AI Blog / DeepMind Blog
- Microsoft AI Blog
- Meta AI Blog
- Hugging Face Blog
- NVIDIA AI Blog
- AWS AI / ML Blog
- Apple Machine Learning Research

## Tier 2: Editorial technology news

Use these for broader industry coverage and verification.

- TechCrunch AI
- The Verge AI
- MIT Technology Review AI
- Ars Technica AI
- VentureBeat AI
- WIRED AI
- Bloomberg technology coverage when accessible
- Reuters technology coverage when accessible

## Tier 3: Research and technical feeds

Use these to catch frontier work and preprint activity.

- arXiv cs.AI
- arXiv cs.LG
- Papers with Code updates
- Semantic Scholar alerts when configured
- conference and lab publication pages

## Tier 4: Community trend signals

Use these only as trend indicators or discovery sources.

- Hacker News
- GitHub Trending
- Reddit r/MachineLearning
- Reddit r/LocalLLaMA
- Product Hunt AI

## Source handling rules

- Prefer original announcements over reposts.
- Prefer the earliest authoritative publication when multiple sources cover the same story.
- When community sources surface a story first, wait for at least one reliable corroborating source before presenting it as a major item unless the story is explicitly labeled as unverified.
- Do not treat a high upvote count as proof of factual accuracy.
