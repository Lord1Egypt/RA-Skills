## Description: <br>
Write contracts from scratch with guided intake, multi-party negotiation, risk analysis, and version control for lawyers, freelancers, and individuals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, lawyers, freelancers, and individuals use this skill to draft new contracts through guided intake, clause drafting, risk review, negotiation notes, and versioned contract files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Contract drafts, intake answers, parties, and negotiation notes may contain sensitive legal or financial details stored on the user's machine. <br>
Mitigation: Enter only necessary details, protect the ~/contracts/ folder, and avoid storing unnecessary sensitive information. <br>
Risk: AI-generated contract language can be incomplete, misleading, or unsuitable for the governing jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Have important contracts reviewed by a licensed attorney in the applicable jurisdiction before signing or relying on them. <br>
Risk: High-stakes or regulated matters such as securities, labor disputes, government contracts, patents, M&A, healthcare, banking, and complex real estate can require specialized legal review. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate those matters to a qualified lawyer and use the skill only to prepare background materials or a review draft. <br>
