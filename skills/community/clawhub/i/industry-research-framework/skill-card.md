## Description: <br>
Supports agents in producing industry research reports using a five-dimension framework, a three-step research workflow, quantitative anchors, global benchmarking, and source-rating practices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and business analysts use this skill to structure industry, market, company, competitive, regulatory, and trend analysis into concise briefs, data tables, benchmarking reports, or longer research reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market data, citations, or trend claims can be outdated, incomplete, or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Require Tier 1 or Tier 2 sources for cited facts, label source confidence, and state uncertainty instead of inventing numbers. <br>
Risk: Outputs may influence investment, business, or regulatory decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reports as analytical support, independently verify material claims, and review conclusions before acting on them. <br>
