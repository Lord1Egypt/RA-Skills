## Description: <br>
Fxtwitter Extract uses the FxTwitter API to retrieve public X/Twitter posts, threads, and profiles, including text, media, polls, and quote data without an API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nicoxia](https://clawhub.ai/user/nicoxia) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and social media analysts use this skill to fetch public X/Twitter post, thread, and profile data through FxTwitter from an ID, handle, or URL. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Requested X/Twitter URLs, tweet IDs, or handles are sent to FxTwitter. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when that third-party exposure is acceptable, and avoid sensitive investigations unless the privacy tradeoff has been reviewed. <br>
