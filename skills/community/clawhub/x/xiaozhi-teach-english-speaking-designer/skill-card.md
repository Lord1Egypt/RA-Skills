## Description: <br>
Designs task-based English speaking lessons for teachers, including activity flows, scaffolds, feedback strategies, and speaking-profile templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External English teachers use this skill to turn speaking practice into structured, task-based classroom activities with input preparation, role play, discussion, feedback, and speaking-profile artifacts. Use with real students should follow the security guidance for consent, pseudonyms, secure storage, limited retention, and controlled sharing of student recordings or speaking profiles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
