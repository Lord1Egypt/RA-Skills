## Description: <br>
Shopify/DTC brand protection toolkit. Detect counterfeit stores, unauthorized resellers, and trademark violations. Includes DMCA takedown templates, domain monitoring, and social media infringement detection. No API key required. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce brand owners and operators use this skill to run manual brand-protection checks, generate risk reports, and draft takedown or complaint templates for suspected counterfeits, unauthorized resellers, trademark misuse, and domain or social media misuse. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release is marketed for Shopify/DTC brand protection while the scripts and templates mostly cover Amazon workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Verify that the workflow and generated templates match the target platform before using them for Shopify, Amazon, or another marketplace. <br>
Risk: Complaint, cease-and-desist, MAP, and test-buy outputs may contain legal or platform-policy claims that are incomplete or incorrect for a specific case. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated notices as drafts and verify facts, platform rules, evidence, and legal claims before sending them to a seller, platform, hosting provider, or other third party. <br>
