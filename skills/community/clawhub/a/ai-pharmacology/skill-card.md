## Use Case: <br>
External users and learners use this skill for educational pharmacology explanations across PK/PD, drug classes, interactions, dose concepts, personalized medicine, drug development, and toxicology. It should not be used as a substitute for clinician or pharmacist judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may ask real medication, interaction, dose, pregnancy, pediatric, overdose, or urgent clinical questions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as educational only and require qualified clinician or pharmacist review for diagnosis, prescribing, dose changes, urgent interactions, and special-population decisions. <br>
Risk: General pharmacology explanations may be incomplete or inappropriate for individual patient factors. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid individualized recommendations without clinical oversight and use current authoritative medical references when discussing medication-specific issues. <br>
