## Description: <br>
AI Recommendation Engine Optimization (AEO/GEO) expert. Audit brand visibility on platforms such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. Analyze why competitors are cited and provide content optimization strategies to improve AI citation rates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, content, and growth teams use this skill to audit brand visibility across AI recommendation engines, compare competitor citation patterns, and produce prioritized content fixes to improve citation likelihood. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Brand strategy, competitor lists, customer profiles, or generated prompts may be sensitive when used with external AI platforms. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the data is approved for use with the target AI platforms and keep project notes or memory scoped to the engagement. <br>
Risk: AI citation audits are point-in-time snapshots and may overstate repeatability if presented as guaranteed outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Report baselines, tested platforms, prompt sets, and recheck dates, and describe recommendations as ways to improve citation likelihood rather than guarantees. <br>
