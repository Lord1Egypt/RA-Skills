## Description: <br>
Professional backtesting framework for trading strategies that tests SMA crossover, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, and custom strategies and generates equity curves, drawdown analysis, and performance metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, traders, and research users can invoke this skill to generate trading-strategy backtest summaries and example command-line output. Security review notes that its reported performance is canned sample output, so results should not be used for financial decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may present canned trading performance metrics as if they came from a real data-backed backtest. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as illustrative only and require transparent calculations, labeled sample data, or independent validation before using results in financial decisions. <br>
