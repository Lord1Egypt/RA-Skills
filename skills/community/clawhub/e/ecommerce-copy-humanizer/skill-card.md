## Description: <br>
Humanize English ecommerce copy to remove obvious AI tone while preserving selling intent, channel fit, and safer claim boundaries for product pages, ads, captions, and customer-facing copy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce teams and copywriters use this skill to rewrite English product pages, ads, captions, and customer-facing copy so it sounds more natural while preserving commercial intent and safer claim boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ecommerce copy could include incorrect, unsupported, or regulated product claims if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy before publishing, especially claims about discounts, guarantees, health, finance, or other legally sensitive topics. <br>
Risk: The skill is not intended to provide legal, medical, regulated compliance, translation, or localization signoff. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for English ecommerce copy humanization and route regulated or multilingual publishing decisions to qualified reviewers. <br>
