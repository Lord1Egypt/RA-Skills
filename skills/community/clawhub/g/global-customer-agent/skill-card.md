## Description: <br>
Global Customer Agent provides 24/7 multilingual customer reception for cross-border commerce by understanding customer needs, recommending products, and drafting quotes in multiple languages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangm-a3](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangm-a3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External customer-service and cross-border commerce teams use this skill to draft multilingual inquiry responses, product recommendations, quote details, and customer lifecycle follow-up guidance. Human operators should review customer-facing messages before sending. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer-facing replies may contain incorrect pricing, shipping, payment terms, or contractual language. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval for pricing, shipping, payment terms, and contractual language before sending responses to customers. <br>
Risk: Automatic language detection or localized phrasing may not match the customer's intended language or tone. <br>
Mitigation: Let operators confirm or override the detected customer language and review localized messages before delivery. <br>
Risk: Real customer quotes or sensitive commercial details may be sent without sufficient review. <br>
Mitigation: Review quote drafts and customer-specific details before using them in a live customer channel. <br>
