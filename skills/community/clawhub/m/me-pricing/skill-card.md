## Description: <br>
Helps users choose, explain, and revisit pricing for a product or service using minimalist entrepreneur principles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[carollili](https://clawhub.ai/user/carollili) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, product builders, and small-business operators use this skill to compare cost-based and value-based pricing, choose an initial price, plan tiers, and estimate how many customers are needed for sustainability. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat pricing recommendations as accounting, legal, or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as business guidance and validate decisions with appropriate financial, legal, or accounting review when needed. <br>
Risk: Pricing discussions can involve confidential customer, revenue, or cost information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the business context needed for the task and avoid entering confidential records unless the environment is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags are broader than the skill behavior described by the security evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Do not authorize purchases, crypto activity, or other external actions based solely on this skill's guidance. <br>
