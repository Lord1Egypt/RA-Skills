## Description: <br>
Automates financial file analysis, ratio calculations, forecasts, visualizations, and report generation for business and investment workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business owners, financial analysts, accountants, e-commerce entrepreneurs, investors, and founders use this skill to analyze CSV or Excel financial data, calculate ratios, forecast performance, create visualizations, and generate financial reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The documentation advertises bank, accounting, and investment integrations without clear credential scope, privacy, or revocation details. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as a local CSV/Excel analyzer unless reviewed integration code and credential-handling documentation are provided. <br>
Risk: Financial input files and generated reports can contain sensitive business or personal financial information. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill in an isolated environment and protect generated reports, charts, and exported files with appropriate access controls. <br>
