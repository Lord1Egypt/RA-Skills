## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally biases Croatian output toward casual, warm phrasing that may be inappropriate for formal, legal, business, or highly neutral contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated Croatian and override the style when the task requires formal register, neutral tone, or precise professional wording. <br>
