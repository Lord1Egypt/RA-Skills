## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>

Mitigation: Use the skill as a reference aid only, and have qualified personnel verify scanner settings, coordinate systems, quality thresholds, and deliverables before field work or operational use. <br>
Risk: The skill includes an executable shell script, even though the reviewed behavior only prints static information. <br>
Mitigation: Review the script before installation or execution and keep execution limited to trusted local environments. <br>
