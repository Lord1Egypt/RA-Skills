## Description: <br>
Score product detail pages on image quality, copy effectiveness, SEO, mobile layout, and trust elements with improvement priorities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce sellers, marketers, and operators use this skill to audit product detail pages and prioritize fixes across imagery, copy, SEO, mobile experience, and trust signals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audience-matching guidance could be misused for protected-characteristic targeting or exclusionary marketing decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the guidance as a content-quality aid, require inclusive non-discriminatory language, and review recommendations before using them for marketing decisions. <br>
Risk: Audit scores and conversion benchmarks can be mistaken for guaranteed business outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Use the report as prioritization guidance and validate material changes with platform data, user research, or controlled experiments. <br>
