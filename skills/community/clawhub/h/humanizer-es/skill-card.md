## Description: <br>
Elimina rastros de texto generado por IA en espanol y ayuda a reescribirlo para que suene mas natural, preciso y humano. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mattc95](https://clawhub.ai/user/mattc95) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, writers, editors, and developers use this skill to review Spanish text, identify common signs of AI-generated writing, and rewrite passages while preserving meaning and tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting may unintentionally change meaning, emphasis, attribution, or the author's intended voice. <br>
Mitigation: Review the edited text against the original and verify factual claims, citations, names, dates, and quoted material before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill can propose or make file edits when used with writable agent tools. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect proposed changes before accepting them and keep a recoverable copy or version history for important documents. <br>
