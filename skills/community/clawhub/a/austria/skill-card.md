## Description: <br>
Plan Austria trips with rail and road logic, alpine season timing, region-specific routing, and practical local execution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and planning agents use this skill to build practical Austria itineraries covering entry requirements, route design, rail and car tradeoffs, alpine season timing, accommodations, costs, family needs, accessibility, and safety. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional Austria trip notes may retain dates, budget, mobility, family, or route constraints locally in ~/austria/memory.md. <br>
Mitigation: Review or delete ~/austria/memory.md when those planning details should not persist. <br>
