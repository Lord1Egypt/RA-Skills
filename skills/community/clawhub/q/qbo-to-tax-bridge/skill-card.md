## Description: <br>
Maps QuickBooks Online bookkeeping data to IRS tax schedules and generates tax-ready workpapers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[samledger67-dotcom](https://clawhub.ai/user/samledger67-dotcom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Bookkeepers, tax preparers, and finance operators use this skill to map QBO chart-of-accounts and transaction data to federal IRS schedules, identify reclassifications, and prepare review-ready workpapers. It supports preparation workflows only and does not file returns or replace qualified professional review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be used with sensitive QuickBooks and tax records. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when authorized to process the relevant financial records and apply normal confidentiality controls for client tax data. <br>
Risk: Workpaper mappings or tax positions may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific filer. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as preparation support and require review by a qualified tax professional before filing or relying on a tax position. <br>
Risk: The artifact is a text guide rather than live QBO automation. <br>
Mitigation: Do not grant QBO credentials or expect direct API access; use exported reports and review generated workpapers manually. <br>
