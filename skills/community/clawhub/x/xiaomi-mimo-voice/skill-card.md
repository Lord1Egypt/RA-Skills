## Description: <br>
Converts Chinese or English text into Xiaomi MiMo V2 text-to-speech audio with selectable voices and styles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[luhuiwang](https://clawhub.ai/user/luhuiwang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to synthesize spoken audio from text for Chinese or English prompts, narration, role-play, dialect, and emotion styles through the MiMo API. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Submitted text and optional context are sent to Xiaomi's MiMo service for synthesis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid secrets, private messages, proprietary material, or regulated data unless the provider's data handling terms have been reviewed and accepted. <br>
