## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, content teams, and site operators use this skill to audit websites, plan keyword and content work, improve technical SEO, structure local SEO, and prepare schema and link recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations applied directly to live sites, Search Console, Google Business Profile, or published content could harm rankings, indexing, or business outcomes if they are incorrect for the site context. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with the site owner or SEO lead, validate against current site data, stage technical changes where possible, and monitor Search Console and traffic metrics after release. <br>
Risk: The optional ~/seo workspace can retain site profiles, audit history, keyword tracking, draft content, and backlink opportunities. <br>
Mitigation: Create the workspace only when persistent history is desired, limit stored client or competitive information, and apply normal file access controls for the environment. <br>
Risk: Link-building, review, and schema guidance can become policy-violating if used for paid links, fake reviews, hidden content, or misleading structured data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only compliant link outreach, genuine reviews, visible page content, and accurate structured data, and manually review tactics before publishing or submitting changes. <br>
