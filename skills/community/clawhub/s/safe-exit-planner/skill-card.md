## Description: <br>
Safety planning resources and procedures for leaving dangerous living situations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and support agents use this skill to provide domestic violence safety planning resources, crisis contacts, secure communication guidance, and exit-planning checklists for people who feel unsafe at home or need to leave a dangerous situation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with US-specific hotline and legal resources that must be localized for non-US users. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers high-stakes domestic violence safety planning, and incorrect or outdated hotline, shelter, or legal information could increase user risk. <br>
Mitigation: Verify hotline numbers, local legal procedures, and shelter resources during live use, especially outside the United States. <br>
Risk: Users may be monitored through shared devices, browser history, location sharing, or compromised accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use a safe device or phone when possible, avoid storing sensitive conversation records, and prioritize immediate crisis resources before planning. <br>
Risk: The skill includes US-specific legal and hotline resources that may not apply internationally. <br>
Mitigation: Determine the user's location and substitute local crisis, legal aid, and shelter resources before giving jurisdiction-specific guidance. <br>
