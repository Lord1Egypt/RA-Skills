## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Operations managers, supply chain directors, logistics coordinators, ecommerce fulfillment teams, 3PL operators, and warehouse managers use this skill to assess routing, warehouse efficiency, transportation spend, inventory positioning, carrier performance, returns, and logistics KPIs. <br>

Mitigation: Review recommendations against current carrier contracts, cost data, service levels, and operational constraints before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes visible paid product links. <br>
Mitigation: Verify external links, pricing, and vendor legitimacy independently before making purchases. <br>
