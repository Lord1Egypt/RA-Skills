# Newsletter & Creator Pack

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Publisher:** drew2operations

## What this skill does

Four automation workflows for newsletter writers and content creators:

1. **Content Repurposing Engine** — Turn one newsletter issue into 7 platform-ready assets (Twitter thread, LinkedIn post, short video script, 5 subject lines, Instagram caption, quote graphics, community post)

2. **Sponsorship Outreach Generator** — Research prospects and write personalized cold emails to potential sponsors. Includes follow-up sequences.

3. **Subscriber Reactivation Campaign** — 3-email sequence to re-engage lapsed subscribers. Includes the "Should I remove you?" email that consistently recovers 5–15% of dormant readers.

4. **Content Performance Analyst** — Paste your metrics, get an actionable report: what's working, what's not, and exactly what to do this week.

## Who it's for
- Solo newsletter operators
- Podcasters and YouTubers
- Content creators monetizing an audience
- Anyone who publishes regularly and wants to do more with less
