## Description: <br>
Xueersi Idiom Chain Game guides an assistant to run an interactive Chinese chengyu chain game with idiom validation, explanations, difficulty levels, and vocabulary learning support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners, educators, and families use this skill to practice Chinese idioms through a conversational chain game with meanings, examples, difficulty settings, and score summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
