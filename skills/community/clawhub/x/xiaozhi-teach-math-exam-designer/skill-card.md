## Description: <br>
Helps math teachers design structured assessments with goals, two-dimensional specification tables, item-selection boundaries, difficulty gradients, implementation guidance, and result-analysis templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Teachers use this skill to turn math test planning into a structured diagnostic workflow covering assessment purpose, blueprint design, item sourcing, difficulty balance, delivery rules, and post-assessment teaching interventions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Assessment planning and result-analysis workflows can involve student performance data, rankings, cross-skill sharing, and stored assessment records. <br>
Mitigation: Use pseudonyms instead of real student names, remove ranking fields from reports unless explicitly justified, confirm before writing results to other skills or archives, and apply a clear retention policy for assessment records. <br>
