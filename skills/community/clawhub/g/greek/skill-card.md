## Use Case: <br>
Writers and agents use this skill to draft or revise Greek text so it reads more casual, warm, and native-like rather than overly formal or robotic. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally makes Greek output more casual and expressive, which can introduce tone, profanity, or formality mismatches in professional or sensitive contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated Greek before use and adjust register, politeness, and expressive language for the audience and setting. <br>
