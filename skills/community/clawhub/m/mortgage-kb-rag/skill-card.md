## Description: <br>
Instant answers to mortgage, credit, and home-buying questions from a curated knowledge base, with sourced answers and knowledge-gap handling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Mortgage loan originators, real estate agents, home buyers, and investors use this skill to ask plain-language mortgage, credit, loan program, and home-buying questions and receive grounded answers with citations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Mortgage, credit, and loan-program guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific borrower or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important answers against current lender, regulator, and licensed professional guidance before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The security classification is low confidence because the scan evidence says the target artifact was not available for direct review during adjudication. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the ClawHub listing files and metadata match the skill's stated mortgage knowledge-base purpose, and review the artifact before use. <br>
