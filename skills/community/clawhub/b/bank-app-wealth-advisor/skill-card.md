## Description: <br>
AI-powered wealth advisory assistant for bank mobile applications - provides product recommendations, investment consultation, and compliance-compliant customer interaction. <br>

This skill is for demonstration purposes and not for production usage. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gechengling](https://clawhub.ai/user/gechengling) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Digital banking teams and wealth management staff use this skill as an educational reference for bank app wealth-advisory flows, product-matching examples, investment consultation scripts, and compliance checklist language. It is not a substitute for licensed financial, legal, insurance, or investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: High-stakes financial-domain outputs could be mistaken for financial, legal, insurance, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only as educational templates and require review by qualified licensed professionals before any real customer recommendation or product sale. <br>
Risk: Product-matching examples or consultation scripts could be reused in regulated banking workflows without proper controls. <br>
Mitigation: Handle real recommendations through official regulated bank systems and apply compliance review, suitability checks, and prohibited-language controls before use. <br>
