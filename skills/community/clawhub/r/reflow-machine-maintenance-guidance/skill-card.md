## Description: <br>
This skill helps answer reflow machine maintenance questions and provide detailed guidance from thermocouple data, MES data, defect data, and reflow technical handbooks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Manufacturing and process engineers use this skill to answer reflow oven maintenance questions, calculate thermocouple and MES-derived process metrics, and cross-check results against reflow technical handbooks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Maintenance recommendations or calculated limits may be incorrect if the supplied handbook or datasets are unauthorized, stale, incomplete, or not applicable to the target equipment. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized datasets and validate calculated limits and maintenance recommendations against the official process handbook before applying them to real equipment. <br>
