## Description: <br>
Manages vendor insurance compliance through collection, validation, scoring, and remediation of certificates of insurance with alerts and action steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flynndavid](https://clawhub.ai/user/flynndavid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Operations, procurement, vendor management, and compliance teams use this skill to track vendor certificates of insurance, validate coverage against category minimums, score compliance, and plan remediation for gaps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat COI workflow guidance as legal or insurance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a compliance checklist and have qualified legal, insurance, or risk staff review coverage requirements and enforcement decisions. <br>
Risk: COIs and vendor compliance registries can contain sensitive business and insurance information. <br>
Mitigation: Store COIs and registry data in restricted systems with appropriate access controls. <br>
Risk: Formal notices, purchase-order holds, work pauses, or vendor termination can create business or contractual impact. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before sending formal notices or taking operational enforcement actions. <br>
