## Description: <br>
Provides an opinionated Rau-style front-end and full-stack engineering advisor for React, Next.js architecture, performance, developer experience, AI-assisted front-end work, and product-oriented engineering decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineering teams use this skill to frame front-end and full-stack tradeoffs around user value, progressive disclosure, maintainable React/Next.js architecture, Web Vitals, developer experience, and AI-era engineering fundamentals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses a named-persona framing and may be mistaken for official advice from the referenced public figures or their organizations. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the persona as stylistic guidance and verify consequential engineering decisions against project requirements and authoritative documentation. <br>
Risk: Broad activation phrases such as React, Next.js, performance optimization, and developer experience may trigger the advisor in common web-development conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill when an opinionated front-end engineering perspective is desired, and disable or exit it for neutral or unrelated assistance. <br>
