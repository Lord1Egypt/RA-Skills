## Description: <br>
Manage Mailchimp audiences, campaigns, automations, e-commerce, and analytics. Create and send email campaigns, manage subscribers, configure automations, handle e-commerce stores, and track performance with detailed reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, operators, and developers use this skill to manage Mailchimp email marketing workflows through an agent, including audiences, campaigns, automations, e-commerce data, reports, and account configuration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can read and manage a connected Mailchimp account, including account data, audiences, subscribers, campaigns, e-commerce records, and webhooks. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the user is comfortable connecting Mailchimp through ClawLink, and keep access scoped to the intended Mailchimp account. <br>
Risk: High-impact writes can send campaigns, delete lists or subscribers, export account data, change e-commerce records, or create webhooks. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation for writes and review the target resource and intended effect before execution. <br>
