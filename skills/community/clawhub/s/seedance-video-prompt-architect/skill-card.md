## Description: <br>
Turn rough creative briefs into structured Seedance 2.0 video prompt packs, tighter variants, and debugging plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aitools101](https://clawhub.ai/user/aitools101) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and developers use this skill to turn rough short-form video ideas into structured Seedance 2.0 prompt packs with variants and revision guidance for text-to-video, image-to-video, or video-to-video workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated prompt workflows may expose confidential campaign details, personal data, or private footage descriptions if users paste sensitive material into the request. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including confidential, personal, or private footage details in prompts; redact sensitive context before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Implicit invocation may apply the prompt-design workflow when a user only wanted a general response. <br>
Mitigation: Disable implicit invocation when the skill should be called only by explicit user request. <br>
Risk: Unsupported Seedance settings or over-specified prompts can mislead users or reduce generation quality. <br>
Mitigation: Keep settings to documented fields and use the skill's revision loop to simplify subject, action, camera, and constraint choices. <br>
