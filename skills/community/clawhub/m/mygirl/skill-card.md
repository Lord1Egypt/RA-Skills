## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for affectionate roleplay, casual companionship, emotional support, and daily wellness-style check-ins. It is entertainment and support-oriented, not a substitute for professional mental-health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the companion persona as professional mental-health care. <br>
Mitigation: Present it as entertainment and emotional support, and direct users to qualified professionals or emergency resources for clinical or crisis needs. <br>
Risk: Personal details shared for conversational memory may create privacy concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal information unless the user is comfortable with it being used for personalization. <br>
