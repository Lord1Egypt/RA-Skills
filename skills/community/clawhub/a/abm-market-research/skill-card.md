## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, marketers, and business teams use this skill to gather and synthesize market intelligence, including market size, trends, key players, opportunities, and risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may perform web research and selective scraping through local Exa and Firecrawl helper tools. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when web research is expected, and confirm the local helper tools are trusted before use. <br>
Risk: Market sizing and forecast data from public sources can be stale, incomplete, or inconsistent. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-reference important figures across credible sources and preserve confidence levels in the final report. <br>
Risk: Local product marketing context files can influence research scope and conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Review any .agents/product-marketing-context.md or .claude/product-marketing-context.md file before relying on the output. <br>
