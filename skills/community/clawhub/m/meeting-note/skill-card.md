## Description: <br>
Meeting Note helps users turn discussion or decision meetings into structured notes with conclusions, consensus and dissent, decision trails, assumptions, risks, action items, and Zettelkasten links. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mikonos](https://clawhub.ai/user/mikonos) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users use this skill to convert multi-party discussions and decision meetings into actionable meeting notes, including decisions, disagreements, risks, follow-up tasks, and knowledge-network links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting notes may include confidential or personal-sensitive details, especially if saved or indexed into a knowledge base. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm target files before saving or indexing output, and remove confidential or personal-sensitive details before use. <br>
Risk: Inferred hidden assumptions, power dynamics, or risks may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Treat inferred analysis as review material rather than fact, preserve the supporting basis, and confirm sensitive conclusions before acting on them. <br>
