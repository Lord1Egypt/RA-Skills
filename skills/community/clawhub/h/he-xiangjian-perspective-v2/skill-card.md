## Description: <br>
He Xiangjian Perspective V2 provides Chinese-language business management guidance framed around He Xiangjian's public management themes, including delegation, professional managers, steady operations, governance, and succession. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and business operators can use this skill to explore enterprise governance, authorization systems, professional-manager structures, family-business succession, and organization change from a simulated He Xiangjian perspective. Outputs should be reviewed as roleplay-based guidance, not as statements from He Xiangjian or professional business advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: First-person persona responses may be mistaken for statements from He Xiangjian or for professional business advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as simulated roleplay based on public information and review them before using them in business decisions. <br>
Risk: Generic business terms such as delegation or professional manager may trigger the persona unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill deliberately and confirm that the perspective is appropriate before relying on the response. <br>
Risk: The skill is based on public information and cannot predict how He Xiangjian would respond to new situations. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as one analytical viewpoint and validate recommendations against current facts, domain expertise, and organizational constraints. <br>
