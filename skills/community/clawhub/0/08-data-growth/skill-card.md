## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Embedded Python calculator snippets may be copied into a local environment and run without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review calculator snippets before execution and run them only in an appropriate local environment. <br>
Risk: Real customer, order, or business performance data may be used with the skill's analytics workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the organization's privacy, access-control, and data-handling policies before using production data. <br>
Risk: Business benchmarks and decision guidance may be outdated or mismatched to a user's market. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current business data and local market context before acting on them. <br>
