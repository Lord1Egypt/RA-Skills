Senior automotive parts quality analysis expert skill for analyzing quality issues, failure modes, 8D reports, SPC data, PPAP documents, and automotive quality work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Automotive quality engineers, supplier quality teams, and related reviewers use this skill to structure investigations, review 8D reports, analyze SPC, PPAP, FMEA, and MSA evidence, and draft quality analysis outputs for automotive components. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Quality analysis outputs may be mistaken for final safety, warranty, regulatory, or supplier approval decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as engineering support and have qualified reviewers validate evidence, assumptions, and approvals before action. <br>
Risk: Automotive investigations may involve confidential supplier, production, or customer complaint data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data approved for the workflow and redact sensitive supplier, production, or customer information when appropriate. <br>
