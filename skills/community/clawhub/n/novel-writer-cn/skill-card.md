## Use Case: <br>
External writers and creators use this skill to generate structured fiction-planning frameworks with character profiles, relationship maps, three-act plot development, alternative endings, and writing suggestions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated story frameworks may steer users toward a fixed planning template when they only need general writing assistance. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for fiction outlining workflows and adjust or replace the template when a narrower writing task is needed. <br>
