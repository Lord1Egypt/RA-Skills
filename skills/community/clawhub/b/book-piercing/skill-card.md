## Description: <br>
Book piercing services through Lokuli MCP. Use when user needs to find and book piercing. Triggers on requests like "book a piercing", "find piercing near me", or any piercing service request. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[edwardrodriguez703-design](https://clawhub.ai/user/edwardrodriguez703-design) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to search for piercing services through Lokuli, check provider availability, and prepare booking requests with customer contact details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Booking requests may send personal contact information to Lokuli and the selected provider. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the provider, service, date, time, name, email, and phone number before allowing create_booking, and avoid sharing unnecessary personal information. <br>
