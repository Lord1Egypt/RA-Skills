## Description: <br>
Generate thumbnail candidates from video frames so creators, ecommerce teams, and operators can choose cleaner covers for social, storefronts, and internal sharing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, ecommerce teams, operators, and external users use this skill to extract and compare strong still-frame thumbnail candidates from source videos for social, storefront, ad, gallery, or internal review use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided videos may contain private, sensitive, or unpublished content. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide videos you are comfortable having the agent inspect, and review selected frames before publishing or sharing them. <br>
Risk: Weak source footage can lead to blurry, awkward, or misleading frame-based thumbnails. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer clear, focused frames and disclose limitations when the video is too low quality to produce strong stills. <br>
