## Description: <br>
HR ArchSystem helps HR teams design organization structures, job level systems, competency dictionaries, and evidence-based role-level evaluations with AI-era considerations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
HR practitioners, people operations teams, and managers use this skill to structure HR architecture work across organization design, job levels, competency modeling, and role-level evaluation. It guides users toward structured Markdown outputs such as architecture notes, job level tables, competency dictionaries, and evaluation reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be used with sensitive employee or candidate information during evaluation, promotion, pay, ranking, or discipline workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data the organization is authorized to process, minimize personal details, keep reports in approved HR systems, define retention and access controls, and require human HR or manager review before acting on outputs. <br>
Risk: Evaluation guidance can be mistaken for a final employment decision when applied to real employees. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support only and calibrate them against documented competency standards, evidence quality, and applicable internal HR review processes. <br>
