# 案例 2：学术段落审查

## 原文

In recent years, more and more researches have been done on the topic of artificial intelligence. According to the actual situation, many scholars play an important role in this field. Based on the above analysis, we can conclude that it is worth mentioning that the data shows a significant number of people are affected. It is necessary to further optimize the actual implementation of the criterias. As far as I am concerned, I think we should pay attention to this phenomenons.

---

## 审查报告

### 🔴 必须修改（硬错误）

1. "more and more researches" → "more and more research" 或 "an increasing number of studies" | research 不可数
2. "the criterias" → "the criteria" | criteria 已是复数（单数是 criterion）
3. "this phenomenons" → "these phenomena" | phenomena 是复数，this→these
4. "the data shows" → "the data show" | 学术用法中 data 为复数
5. "pay attention to this phenomenons" → "note these phenomena" | 双重语法错误

### 🟡 建议修改（翻译腔/不地道）

1. "According to the actual situation" → "In practice" 或删除 | 中式直译，英语不这么说
2. "play an important role in this field" → "have made significant contributions" | 搭配不当，play a role 通常用于事物/因素，不是人
3. "Based on the above analysis, we can conclude that" → "Thus" 或 "Therefore" | 学术写作中过于啰嗦
4. "it is worth mentioning that" → "Notably" | 冗余套话
5. "a significant number of people" → "73% (n=1,303)" | 学术写作需要具体数据
6. "As far as I am concerned, I think" → "We suggest" | 双重主观表达 + 冗余

### 🟢 优化建议

1. "it is necessary to further optimize" → "we recommend improving" | further optimize 是冗余搭配
2. 全文无具体数据支撑 → 建议补充数字/百分比
3. 段落结构不符合 PEEL 原则 → 缺少 Link 句连接上下文

### 📊 整体评估

- **当前水平**：中级可理解
- **主要问题类型**：不可数名词误用 / 学术套话堆砌 / 缺乏数据支撑
- **风格判断**：形式上像学术写作，但充斥中式表达模板，第一人称过多

## 修改后版本

In recent years, an increasing number of studies have examined artificial intelligence. In practice, many scholars have made significant contributions to this field. The data show that a substantial proportion of the population is affected [具体数据待补充]. We recommend improving the implementation criteria. We suggest further investigation of these phenomena.

---

## 为什么这样改

原文 82 词，修改后 54 词。核心改动：
1. 修复 5 处语法硬错误（不可数名词/复数形式/data主谓一致）
2. 删除 4 处学术套话（it is worth mentioning / based on the above analysis 等）
3. 将口语化主观表达转为学术客观表达（As far as I am concerned → We suggest）
4. 标注需要补充具体数据的位置，符合学术写作精确性要求
