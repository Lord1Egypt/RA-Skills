## The Revenue Argument

### Why Cabinet Marketing Needs This Skill

Cabinet and kitchen remodeling has the highest average ticket ($15K–$65K for full remodel) of any residential trade — and the highest litigation rate. The FTC receives more complaints about home improvement contractors than any other category.

The specific claims this skill blocks are not theoretical risks — they're active enforcement targets:

1. **"Solid wood" fraud** — FTC has pursued action against cabinet retailers and contractors misrepresenting MDF/veneer as solid wood. California AG settled with multiple cabinet companies for this claim in 2022-2023.
2. **EPA RRP non-compliance** — EPA fines average $37,500 per violation; lead paint exposure in kitchen renovation of pre-1978 homes is the most common RRP enforcement trigger.
3. **CARB Phase 2** — California AG has fined cabinet manufacturers and contractors for selling non-compliant composite wood products with "eco-friendly" marketing claims.
4. **Soft-close warranty fraud** — BBB arbitration cases involving "lifetime" soft-close warranties with unlisted brand hardware increased 40% in 2023-2024.

No generic AI marketing tool enforces any of these standards. This skill is the only one that does.
