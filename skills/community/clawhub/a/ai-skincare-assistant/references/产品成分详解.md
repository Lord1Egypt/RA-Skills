## V. Safety Notes

### Sensitization Risk Alerts
- Contains **Prinsepia Utilis Royle Oil** — nut allergy sufferers should patch test first
- Contains **Portulaca Oleracea Extract** — rare individuals may react
- Recommended: test on the inner ear area for 24 hours before full use

### Pregnancy / Breastfeeding Considerations
- Soothing sensitive-skin range: **Safe to use** (no retinol or other restricted ingredients)
- AOXMED anti-aging range: Confirm retinoid content; consult a physician if uncertain
- Tripollar RF beauty devices: **Contraindicated during pregnancy**
