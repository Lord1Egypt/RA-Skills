# Overview

This skill helps a resident salaried individual in India understand:
- employer tax declaration and proof-submission lifecycle
- ITR filing basics and consequences of missing it
- common forms and documents
- common taxed assets/income add-ons like FD/RD, dividends, stocks, MFs, and capital gains
- common deduction-linked items like student loan and house loan

It is designed to be:
- conversational, not bureaucratic
- conservative, not overconfident
- calculator-backed when numbers matter
- FY-specific when rules change
