## Description: <br>
Provides guitar instruction on chords, scales, techniques, song learning, music theory, and gear guidance for acoustic and electric players from beginner to advanced. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Guitar learners and instructors use this skill to generate structured lessons, practice guidance, technique explanations, music theory support, song-learning help, and gear advice across beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Music instruction may be incomplete or unsuitable for a learner's instrument, tuning, physical limits, or goals. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated practice plans and technique advice before relying on them, and adapt lessons to the learner's context or defer to a qualified instructor for physical technique concerns. <br>
