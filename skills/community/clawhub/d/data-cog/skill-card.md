This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and data analysts use this skill to send datasets to CellCog for data cleaning, exploratory analysis, visualization, statistical analysis, reporting, and machine-learning evaluation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded datasets may be processed by an external CellCog service. <br>
Mitigation: Do not upload secrets, credentials, personal data, regulated datasets, or confidential business data unless CellCog's data-handling, retention, and privacy terms have been reviewed and accepted. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a sensitive CELLCOG_API_KEY credential. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the API key through the agent environment or secret manager and avoid embedding it in prompts, source files, or generated artifacts. <br>
