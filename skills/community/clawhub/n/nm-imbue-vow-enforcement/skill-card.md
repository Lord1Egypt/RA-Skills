## Description: <br>
Classifies and enforces constraints via soft vows, hard vows, and Nen Court layers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineering teams use this skill to classify project rules by enforcement strength, decide when to promote soft guidance into hooks or validator gates, and audit enforcement gaps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad enforcement guidance could lead teams to add hooks or validator gates that block legitimate work. <br>
Mitigation: Scope, test, and review enforcement mechanisms before deployment, and preserve explicit override or revision paths. <br>
