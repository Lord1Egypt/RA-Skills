## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Flower-care, preservation, and business recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific location, species, supplier, event, or shop context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as general guidance and verify important care, pricing, safety, and business decisions against local expertise and current conditions. <br>
Risk: User-uploaded flower arrangement images may contain private locations, people, messages, or other sensitive visual details. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only images the user is comfortable sharing with the agent and remove sensitive details before analysis. <br>
