## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share birth details, birthplace, or optional room descriptions or photos. <br>
Mitigation: Use only if comfortable sharing those details, and avoid providing unnecessary personal or sensitive information. <br>
Risk: Astrology-style output can be mistaken for medical, legal, financial, or guaranteed life advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as reflective entertainment or self-reflection, and do not make major decisions solely from the reading. <br>
Risk: Exact pillar-level claims can be inaccurate when no trusted BaZi chart calculator is used. <br>
Mitigation: Use a proper BaZi calculator for strict pillar audits; the skill instructs the agent not to fabricate stems, branches, element counts, or luck-pillar ages. <br>
