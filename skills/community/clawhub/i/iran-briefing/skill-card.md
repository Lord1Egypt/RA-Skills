## Description: <br>
Real-time geopolitical intelligence for Iran crisis briefings, Trump Truth Social posts, prediction markets, and tracked entities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dachein](https://clawhub.ai/user/dachein) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to request concise geopolitical briefings, investigate Iran-related events, check Trump Truth Social posts, compare prediction-market signals, and assess source credibility. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: High-impact geopolitical claims may be incomplete, time-sensitive, or based on limited source coverage. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims with independent sources before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Query filters or prompts may reveal confidential plans or private context to the public external service. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for non-sensitive research and avoid including confidential or private details in requests. <br>
Risk: Returned briefings may include Chinese translations that are not useful for every workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Request English output when Chinese translations are not helpful. <br>
