## Description: <br>
Generate customized English practice booklets with diverse question types for grades 3-9 using varied difficulty and content versions, exportable as PDFs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Teachers, tutors, and education-focused developers use this skill to generate English practice worksheets and booklets for grades 3-9, including student PDFs and teacher answer versions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The documented answer-key endpoint could expose teacher-only answer material if a real service is deployed without access controls. <br>
Mitigation: Do not expose answer-key endpoints to students or the public unless authentication and teacher or administrator authorization are in place. <br>
Risk: The artifact describes Python scripts and local question-bank files that are not included in the release package. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan any separately obtained scripts or question-bank files before running them. <br>
