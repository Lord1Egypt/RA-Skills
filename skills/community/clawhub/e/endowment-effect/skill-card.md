## Description: <br>
Activates for valuation gaps caused by ownership attachment, helping agents analyze when sellers, founders, homeowners, teams, or trial users overvalue what they already own and when the effect is weak because transparent market prices or genuine information asymmetry explain the gap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and business operators use this skill to structure negotiation, pricing, product-trial, M&A, real-estate, and product-decision guidance around endowment-effect dynamics. It helps isolate the ownership premium, choose whether to leverage or counteract it, and propose ethical bridges such as reference prices, earnouts, or exchange framing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personalization, free-trial, or cancellation-flow recommendations could be used to create manipulative retention patterns. <br>
Mitigation: Require clear user consent, salient renewal terms, easy data deletion or export, and cancellation that is no harder than signup. <br>
Risk: Endowment-effect framing can overstate a psychological explanation when a valuation gap is actually caused by genuine information asymmetry or transparent market pricing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only after comparing external reference prices and separating factual differences from the ownership premium. <br>
