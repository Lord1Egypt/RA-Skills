## Use Case: <br>
Developers and evaluators use this skill to design weighted rubrics, scoring systems, quality gates, and threshold-based decisions for artifacts, proposals, submissions, and review workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases such as quality, metrics, and evaluation may activate the skill in contexts where a narrower rubric is desired. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adjust trigger phrases before installation when narrower activation is preferred. <br>
Risk: Weighted scoring can produce misleading decisions if criteria, weights, or thresholds are copied without domain calibration. <br>
Mitigation: Customize criteria, weights, and thresholds for the target domain and review results before relying on automated gates. <br>
