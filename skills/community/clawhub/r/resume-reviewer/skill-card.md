## Description: <br>
Analyze resumes for target roles, identify weak bullets, missing keywords, ATS gaps, and provide actionable rewrite suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Andrewgufx](https://clawhub.ai/user/Andrewgufx) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, recent graduates, and early-career candidates use this skill to get practical resume feedback for target roles, especially analytics, data science, product analytics, business analyst, and related applications. It helps identify weak bullets, missing technical or business-impact signals, ATS gaps, and priority revisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume text can contain sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to omit unnecessary sensitive details such as home address, phone number, government IDs, and private account information unless they intentionally want that content reviewed. <br>
Risk: Resume advice may overfit to assumptions when target role, company, region, or resume context is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions clearly and treat the review as guidance for the user to verify against the actual job posting and their own experience. <br>
