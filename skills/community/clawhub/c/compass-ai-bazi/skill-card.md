## Description: <br>
Chinese Bazi (Four Pillars of Destiny) calculation and basic interpretation for agents that need to compute and read a birth chart using traditional Zi-Ping methodology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Judyzj](https://clawhub.ai/user/Judyzj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to calculate a Bazi chart from birth date, birth time, gender, and birth city, then provide a lightweight Chinese astrology interpretation covering pillars, Five Elements, Day Master strength, favorable elements, and major fortune cycles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users for birth details, which can be personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for an astrology reading and review any separate privacy terms before using the linked Compass AI website. <br>
Risk: Astrology interpretations may be mistaken for reliable advice about important life decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as entertainment or cultural interpretation, not as medical, legal, financial, or other high-stakes guidance. <br>
