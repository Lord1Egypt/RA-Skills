## 1. The Five Elements in Feng Shui (五行在风水中的应用)

| Element | Shapes | Colors | Seasons | Enhances | Weakens |
|:-------:|:------:|:------:|:-------:|:--------:|:-------:|
| **木 (Wood)** | Tall, rectangular | Green, teal | Spring | Fire | Metal, Earth |
| **火 (Fire)** | Pointed, triangular | Red, purple, orange | Summer | Earth | Water, Metal |
| **土 (Earth)** | Flat, square, solid | Yellow, brown, beige | Late summer | Metal | Wood, Water |
| **金 (Metal)** | Round, arched, domed | White, silver, gold | Autumn | Water | Fire, Wood |
| **水 (Water)** | Wavy, flowing, irregular | Black, dark blue, deep grey | Winter | Wood | Earth, Fire |

**Productive Cycle (相生):** Wood → Fire → Earth → Metal → Water → Wood
**Destructive Cycle (相克):** Wood → Earth → Water → Fire → Metal → Wood

### Room-by-room element recommendations:

| Room / Area | Dominant Element | Enhancement |
|:------------|:----------------:|:------------|
| Living room (客厅) | Earth (gathering) | Square furniture, warm earth tones |
| Bedroom (卧室) | Yin — Wood/Water | Soft colors, rounded edges |
| Kitchen (厨房) | Fire | Good ventilation, no fire-water clash |
| Study (书房) | Wood/Metal | Green + white, good lighting |
| Bathroom (厕所) | Water | Keep clean, well-ventilated, door closed |
| Home office (办公室) | Metal/Wood | North or West facing desk |
| Front door area (玄关) | Depends on direction | See eight-mansions.md |

## 3. Interior Feng Shui Essentials (室内风水要领)

### Entry Door (大门):
- Main door should be the largest door in the house
- Door should open inward to welcome qi
- No mirror directly facing the door (it pushes qi out)
- Door should not be directly aligned with the back door (qi goes straight through)
- Avoid multiple doors in a straight line (qi accelerates — "arrow qi")

### Bedroom (卧房):
- Bed position: should see the door but not be directly in line with it (command position)
- No beam over the bed
- No mirror facing the bed
- Head against a solid wall (support)
- Minimal electronics — they create yang disturbance
- Soft, warm colors for restful energy

### Kitchen (厨房):
- Stove should not face the door
- Stove and sink should not be directly opposite (Fire vs Water conflict)
- Keep stove clean and in good working order
- Kitchen visible from the front door may cause wealth leakage

### Living Room (客厅):
- Should be bright and welcoming — the first impression of the home
- Sofa should have back support (solid wall behind)
- Avoid sharp corners pointing at seating
- Round or oval coffee table is best (harmonious flow)
- TV should not dominate the room

### Study/Office (书房):
- Desk in command position — facing the door, not directly aligned
- Back should have support (wall or high-back chair)
- Avoid sitting with your back to a window
- Good natural lighting from the side
- Bookshelves should look tidy, organized

## 4. Common Feng Shui Cures & Enhancers (常用化煞与催旺)

| Issue | Solution | Technique |
|:------|:---------|:----------|
| **Shooting arrow qi (路冲/尖角)** | Block or deflect | Screen, plants, wind chime, convex mirror |
| **Lack of support (无靠山)** | Create support | Tall bookcase, solid wall behind, mountain painting |
| **Stagnant qi (气滞)** | Improve flow | Wind chime, bright lights, live plants |
| **Negative star activated** | Suppress with element | Metal for 2/5 Black; Water for 7 Red; Fire for 3 Jade |
| **Wealth leakage** | Gather qi | Wind chime at entry, water feature facing inside |
| **Poison arrow (煞气)** | Intercept | Plant between source and door, screen |
| **Lack of brightness (阴气重)** | Add yang | Bright lights, warm colors, mirrors |
| **Door facing stairs down** | Stop qi from flowing out | Wind chime or plant, threshold higher |

### Bagua Mirror Directions:
| Type | Use | Caution |
|:----:|:----|:--------|
| **凸镜 (Convex)** | Deflects negative sha qi from outside | Never point at neighbor's door |
| **凹镜 (Concave)** | Draws in and gathers qi | Rarely used |
| **八卦镜 (Octagonal)** | Powerful protection tool | Never use indoors |

### Plants for Feng Shui:
| Plant | Element | Best Location |
|:------|:-------:|:--------------|
| Lucky bamboo (富贵竹) | Wood | Southeast (wealth corner) |
| Money plant (发财树) | Wood | Living room |
| Orchid (兰花) | Wood | Study, living room |
| Pothos (绿萝) | Water | Bathroom, indoor |
| Cactus (仙人掌) | Fire | **Only outside** — to deflect sha |
| Peace lily (白掌) | Water | Bedroom (purifying) |

## 5. Fast Feng Shui Assessment Checklist

### External Assessment:
- [ ] Surroundings: Are there friendly shapes or sharp corners?
- [ ] Roads: Are they curving gently or rushing toward the house?
- [ ] Water: Any water features or rivers — direction of flow?
- [ ] Neighbors: Are the neighboring buildings of balanced height/shape?
- [ ] Front door: Is it protected and easy to find?

### Internal Assessment:
- [ ] Main door: Can qi enter easily and gather?
- [ ] Living room: Is it bright and welcoming?
- [ ] Kitchen: Stove in good position? No fire-water clash?
- [ ] Bedrooms: Command position for beds? No beams?
- [ ] Bathroom: Door kept closed, well-ventilated?
- [ ] Clutter: Is there excessive clutter blocking qi flow?
- [ ] Color balance: Are the five elements balanced throughout?
