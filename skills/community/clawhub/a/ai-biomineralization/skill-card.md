## Description: <br>
A Chinese educational agent skill that explains biomineralization across shells, bone, teeth, pearls, magnetosomes, biogenic silica, pathological mineralization, and biomimetic applications. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, educators, and practitioners in materials science, biomimetics, chemistry, biology, medicine, and dentistry use this skill for concise explanations of biomineral systems and related applications. It is suited for general learning and topic exploration, not clinical diagnosis or treatment decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Biomedical and dental content may be mistaken for professional medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as general learning material and consult qualified professionals for clinical decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill content is primarily Chinese and may respond in Chinese by default. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the preferred response language when another language is required. <br>
