## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cocktail, spirits, and bar operations guidance may be inaccurate, unsuitable for a user's health constraints, or affected by local alcohol laws. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before use, follow local alcohol laws and responsible service practices, and avoid treating the skill as medical or legal advice. <br>
Risk: Advanced molecular techniques such as liquid nitrogen, carbonation, smoke, gels, foams, or additives can create safety risks when attempted without training or proper equipment. <br>
Mitigation: Use trained supervision, appropriate protective equipment, food-safe ingredients, and manufacturer safety instructions before attempting advanced techniques. <br>
