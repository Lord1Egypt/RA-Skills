## Description: <br>
Extracts case details and evidence from chat records to help draft standardized Chinese civil complaints and supporting evidence lists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xuleff](https://clawhub.ai/user/xuleff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to organize civil dispute facts, party information, claims, and evidence into a court-style complaint draft. It is intended to support drafting and review, not to replace professional legal advice or filing decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for personal and case details that may include sensitive party, dispute, or third-party information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only information needed for the draft, redact unnecessary third-party details where possible, and review privacy handling before sharing or filing the complaint. <br>
Risk: Legal-document drafts may contain incomplete facts, unsuitable claims, jurisdiction issues, or language that is not appropriate for a specific court or case. <br>
Mitigation: Review the final complaint carefully and consult a qualified legal professional when the matter is complex, high value, or jurisdiction-sensitive. <br>
