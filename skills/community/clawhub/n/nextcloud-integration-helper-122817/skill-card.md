## Description: <br>
Helps maintainers and users plan, implement, and verify Nextcloud support for software projects. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, maintainers, and users use this skill to turn a Nextcloud support enhancement request into practical plans, checklists, implementation guidance, and verification steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may cause implicit invocation for generic requests such as add or support. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly for Nextcloud support work or narrow trigger wording when many skills are installed. <br>
Risk: Planning or implementation guidance may be incomplete for a specific project. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans, code, and commands against project constraints and run the stated verification steps before applying changes. <br>
