## The Compliance Gap — Why Generic AI Fails Here

Auto dealer advertising is governed by more regulations than almost any other industry:

| Regulation | What It Covers | Generic AI Failure |
|---|---|---|
| FTC Used Car Rule | Buyers Guide on all pre-owned | Omits completely |
| Reg Z | APR disclosure requirements | "1.9% APR" without required terms |
| FTC Green Guides | EV/hybrid range claims | "310-mile range" without EPA qualifier |
| NADA Guidelines | Prohibited comparative claims | "Best price in town" violations |
| State DMV Rules | Expiration dates, stock numbers | Missing required identifiers |
| CAN-SPAM/TCPA | Email/SMS opt-out | No footer, no opt-out language |

One non-compliant campaign = FTC complaint, state DMV investigation, or NADA review. This skill generates compliant copy by default — competitors don't.
