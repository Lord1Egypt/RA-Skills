## Description: <br>
Routes users seeking MBTI-based personal growth guidance to type identification questions, type-specific coaching skills, and follow-on MBTI strategy guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lj22503](https://clawhub.ai/user/lj22503) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as an MBTI coaching entry point to identify or confirm a personality type, see common growth patterns, and choose a matching coach skill or advanced guide. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat MBTI coaching as professional mental-health, career, or diagnostic advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as personality-coaching content only, and review stronger recommendations in any related child coach skills before deployment. <br>
Risk: Type-based guidance can become overly deterministic or misleading if presented as fixed identity or guaranteed advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame MBTI type as a starting point for reflection, ask preference questions when uncertain, and encourage users to validate guidance against lived behavior. <br>
