## Description: <br>
Help the user draft, review, and polish blog posts in Markdown, keeping the original languages and informal tone. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[j3ffyang](https://clawhub.ai/user/j3ffyang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and writers use this skill to draft, review, and lightly polish Markdown blog posts while preserving original languages, informal tone, and existing Markdown structure. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Suggested edits may unintentionally change meaning or introduce misleading phrasing into a blog draft. <br>
Mitigation: Review wording before publishing, especially edits marked with [CHANGED] or any structural suggestions. <br>
Risk: Drafts that touch legal, medical, or other professional topics may need expertise beyond writing assistance. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for wording only in those areas and get qualified review for substantive claims or advice. <br>
