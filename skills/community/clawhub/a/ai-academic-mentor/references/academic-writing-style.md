# 学术写作风格指南

本文档提供英文学术写作的常用规范与表达建议，供润色时参考。

## 常见语法问题

### 1. 主谓一致
- ❌ The data shows that...
- ✅ The data show that... (data 是复数)

### 2. 时态使用
- **研究过程**：过去时 (We conducted an experiment...)
- **图表描述**：现在时 (Figure 1 shows...)
- **文献引用**：现在时 (Smith (2020) argues that...)

### 3. 冠词使用
- 泛指可数名词单数需加冠词
- 专有名词通常不加冠词
- 抽象名词视语境而定

## 学术表达替换表

| 口语化表达 | 学术表达 |
|-----------|---------|
| a lot of | numerous, substantial |
| big | significant, considerable |
| get | obtain, acquire |
| look at | examine, investigate |
| use | utilize, employ |
| think | argue, propose, suggest |
| show | demonstrate, indicate, reveal |
| good | effective, efficient, optimal |

## 常用连接词

### 表示递进
- Furthermore, Moreover, In addition, Additionally

### 表示对比
- However, Nevertheless, In contrast, Conversely

### 表示因果
- Therefore, Consequently, As a result, Hence

### 表示举例
- For instance, For example, Specifically, Notably

## 常见论文结构表达

### 引言部分
- This paper aims to...
- The purpose of this study is to...
- This research addresses the question of...

### 方法部分
- The experiment was conducted using...
- Data were collected from...
- We employed... to analyze...

### 结果部分
- The results indicate that...
- As shown in Figure 1...
- A significant difference was observed between...

### 讨论部分
- These findings suggest that...
- This is consistent with previous research...
- One possible explanation is...

### 结论部分
- In conclusion, this study demonstrates...
- Future research could explore...
- The main contribution of this work is...

## 避免的表达

1. **I think / I believe** → 改用客观表达
   - ❌ I think this method is better.
   - ✅ This method demonstrates superior performance.

2. **Very / Really** → 使用具体程度词
   - ❌ This is very important.
   - ✅ This is critically important / of paramount importance.

3. **缩写形式** → 学术写作中避免
   - ❌ don't, can't, it's
   - ✅ do not, cannot, it is

4. **模糊表达** → 使用具体数据
   - ❌ Many studies have shown...
   - ✅ Several studies (Smith, 2019; Jones, 2020) have shown...

## 常见错误示例

### 冗余表达
- ❌ In order to analyze the data
- ✅ To analyze the data

### 词性误用
- ❌ The affect of temperature
- ✅ The effect of temperature (名词用 effect)

### 平行结构
- ❌ The study collected, analyzed, and was interpreting data
- ✅ The study collected, analyzed, and interpreted data
