## Description: <br>
Logo design principles and AI image generation best practices for creating logos, covering logo types, prompting techniques, scalability rules, and iteration workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and brand builders use this skill to guide AI-assisted logo concept generation, evaluate logo suitability, and produce practical prompts and CLI commands for iteration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow uses an external image-generation CLI with provider billing and data-handling implications. <br>
Mitigation: Review the inference.sh installer or use the manual checksum path, confirm provider trust and billing policies, and avoid uploading confidential brand assets unless that external use is acceptable. <br>
Risk: AI image generators may produce distorted or misspelled text in logo concepts. <br>
Mitigation: Generate icon or symbol concepts with AI, then add text or wordmarks manually in a design tool. <br>
