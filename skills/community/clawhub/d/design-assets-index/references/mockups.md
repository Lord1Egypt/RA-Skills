# Mockup 合集

Mockup 合集：设备/浏览器外框生成、3D Mockup、截图转 Mockup 与在线产品样机。更多顶选见 [SKILL.md](../SKILL.md)。

## 设备外框与截图转 Mockup

- [Shotsnapp](https://shotsnapp.com/) — Device mockup images for app/website designs
- [Screenshot.rocks](https://screenshot.rocks/) — Browser & mobile mockups in seconds
- [Screen Peak](https://screenpeek.io/) — Create a mockup from a URL
- [Device Shots](https://deviceshots.com/) — High-res device mockups for social media
- [Clean Mock](https://cleanmock.com/) — Mockups with latest device frames, in-browser
- [Pika](https://pika.style) — Browser mockups and beautiful marketing images

## 3D 与设备文件

- [Threed.io](https://threed.io) — Generate 3D mockups in the browser
- [Facebook Devices](https://facebook.design/devices) — Sketch files of popular devices

## 在线产品样机

- [Mockuphone](https://mockuphone.com/) — 100% free mockups for all devices
- [MockupBro](https://mockupbro.com/) — Online product mockup generator, no watermark
- [Animockup](https://github.com/alyssaxuu/animockup) — Create animated mockups in the browser
- [Mock.Video](https://www.mock.video/) — Add device frames to your videos
- [Lunacy](https://icons8.com/lunacy) — Create mockups from scratch, free
