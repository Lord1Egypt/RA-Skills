## Description: <br>
Build or restyle interfaces with a curated library of bundled DESIGN.md references extracted from public websites. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cheng-zuguang](https://clawhub.ai/user/cheng-zuguang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and designers use this skill to translate brand, vibe, or product references into implementation-ready UI direction for landing pages, dashboards, design systems, redesigns, and frontend polish. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill expects bundled catalog and DESIGN.md reference files; if they are missing or incomplete, generated design guidance may be under-supported. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the installed package contains the expected catalog and DESIGN.md reference files before relying on reference-specific output. <br>
Risk: Reference-based UI guidance may copy inaccessible patterns or project-inappropriate visual choices. <br>
Mitigation: Review selected references for licensing, accessibility, contrast, touch targets, and fit with the target product before implementation. <br>
