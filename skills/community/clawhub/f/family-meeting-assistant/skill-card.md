## Description: <br>
家语 (Jiayu) helps families record memories, organize weekly, monthly, and yearly summaries, track family-member growth, interview elders, and support calmer household discussions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[perrykono-debug](https://clawhub.ai/user/perrykono-debug) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and families use this skill to keep local family memory records, generate recurring family journals and reports, preserve elder stories, track children and adult family-member growth, and structure family conversations without replacing human decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store sensitive family history, household details, and child-related information. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only where participating adults consent, guardians approve minors' data use, and retention, deletion, and per-member visibility controls are confirmed before recording real family data. <br>
Risk: Optional WeCom or Tencent Docs sharing could expose private family records outside the intended household. <br>
Mitigation: Keep records local by default and enable external sharing only after explicit family approval and a review of destination access controls. <br>
