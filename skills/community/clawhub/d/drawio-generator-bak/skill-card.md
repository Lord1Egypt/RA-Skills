## Description: <br>
Generate editable draw.io diagrams from Mermaid, XML, or CSV code and return a Markdown link that opens the diagram in diagrams.net. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizha](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizha) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and other diagram authors use this skill to turn requested visuals such as flowcharts, UML diagrams, ERDs, architecture diagrams, org charts, network topologies, wireframes, and BPMN diagrams into editable draw.io links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Diagram content may be embedded in a diagrams.net link and shared with that external service when opened. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including secrets or confidential architecture details unless sharing that information with diagrams.net is acceptable. <br>
