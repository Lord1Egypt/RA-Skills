## Description: <br>
Provides standardized, role-based AI agents for multi-agent systems with clear responsibilities across strategy, creative, technical, and management domains. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xiulipan](https://clawhub.ai/user/xiulipan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and teams use this skill to design multi-agent systems with clear role boundaries, responsibilities, communication patterns, and reusable role templates for business, creative, technical, and operational workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad role routing or unclear agent boundaries could send tasks or data to the wrong specialist agent. <br>
Mitigation: Define narrow routing rules, explicit role boundaries, and limits on what data each agent may receive or share. <br>
Risk: Examples for sensitive domains could be adopted without appropriate oversight. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval for sensitive domains before turning the templates into an operational multi-agent system. <br>
