## Description: <br>
Access diverse jokes by category, language, and type with filters for safe content using the free JokeAPI without registration or an API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Djttt](https://clawhub.ai/user/Djttt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to fetch random or filtered jokes from JokeAPI, including category, language, joke type, safe-mode, blacklist, and keyword filters. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms and filters are sent to the public JokeAPI service. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use private or sensitive text in search terms or filters. <br>
Risk: Returned jokes can be offensive or unsuitable for professional or public settings. <br>
Mitigation: Use safe-mode and blacklist filters before displaying jokes in shared contexts. <br>
Risk: Server evidence reports limited provenance for this release. <br>
Mitigation: Review the small helper scripts before deployment. <br>
