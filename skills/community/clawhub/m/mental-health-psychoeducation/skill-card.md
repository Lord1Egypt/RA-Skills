## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake mental health psychoeducation for diagnosis, treatment, medication advice, therapy, or crisis support. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as a general learning aid; consult a licensed mental health professional for diagnosis, treatment planning, medication decisions, therapy, or persistent symptoms. <br>
Risk: Urgent self-harm, psychosis, or inability-to-care-for-self situations require immediate support beyond an agent skill. <br>
Mitigation: For urgent risk, use local emergency services, an emergency department, or an appropriate crisis line for the user's country. <br>
