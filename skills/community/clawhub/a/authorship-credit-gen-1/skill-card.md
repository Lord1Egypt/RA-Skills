## Description: <br>
Drafts CRediT contributor statements and structured authorship-credit guidance for research manuscripts using documented author roles and contribution inputs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Research teams, publication contributors, and academic writing reviewers use this skill to draft CRediT contributor statements, map author roles, and structure authorship-credit discussions. It should support transparent documentation rather than replace project-level authorship decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may overstate its ability to make academic authorship decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as drafting or formatting assistance and require human review by the research team before applying authorship recommendations. <br>
Risk: Execution guidance and examples may be inconsistent or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm inputs, file paths, supported CLI options, and journal or institutional authorship policies before relying on generated output. <br>
