## Description: <br>
Ai Archaeology Heritage assists with cultural heritage questions, including artifact interpretation, archaeological dating concepts, site context, museum curation, and conservation guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, museum professionals, cultural heritage workers, students, collectors, and enthusiasts use this skill to ask about artifacts, archaeological dating methods, important sites, exhibit planning, and conservation practices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Artifact photos can contain geotags, identifying metadata, or restricted museum and site information. <br>
Mitigation: Remove geotags and identifying metadata before upload, and avoid sharing restricted museum or archaeological site images. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misused to authenticate, market, hide, or legitimize looted or illegally obtained artifacts. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use the skill for illicit artifact handling, sales, provenance laundering, or ownership claims. <br>
Risk: Artifact interpretation and conservation guidance can be incomplete without expert examination and provenance context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational guidance and consult qualified archaeologists, conservators, or museum professionals for consequential decisions. <br>
