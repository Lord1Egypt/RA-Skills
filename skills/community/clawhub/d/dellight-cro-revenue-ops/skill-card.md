## Use Case: <br>
Revenue leaders and operators use this skill to evaluate sales pipeline decisions, pricing options, go-to-market plans, and ROI for commercial activities. It is tailored to DELLIGHT.AI's revenue operations and startup growth planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
UAE and GCC initially, with planned expansion to EU and US markets. <br>

Risk: Revenue, pricing, and outreach recommendations may affect commercial commitments if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Have an accountable business owner review generated sales, pricing, and outreach recommendations before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The skill text references helper scripts that are not included in this release. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included ROI calculator as the available local script, and review any later-supplied helper scripts before running them. <br>
