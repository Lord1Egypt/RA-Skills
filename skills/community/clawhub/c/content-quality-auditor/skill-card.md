## Description: <br>
Use when auditing content quality, E-E-A-T, publish readiness, or CORE-EEAT scoring with veto checks and a fix plan. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aaron-he-zhu](https://clawhub.ai/user/aaron-he-zhu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content teams, SEO practitioners, and agents use this skill to audit draft or published content against CORE-EEAT criteria, produce a publish-readiness verdict, and prioritize concrete fixes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can save audit blockers and improvement priorities into persistent memory files without asking the user first. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing for private drafts, client work, unpublished business content, or sensitive URLs, and require explicit approval before writes to memory paths. <br>
