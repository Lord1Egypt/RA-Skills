## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to log meals, plan food choices, check nutrition patterns, and get healthy meal suggestions or quick swaps while keeping food data local. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meal logs, wellness notes, and hydration or nutrition details can contain private personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep logs local, avoid entering sensitive medical, allergy, eating-disorder, or treatment details unless comfortable retaining them, and remove private details before sharing outputs. <br>
Risk: Healthy eating suggestions may be mistaken for medical or nutrition treatment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for general habit support and consult a qualified professional for medical conditions, allergies, eating disorders, or individualized treatment guidance. <br>
