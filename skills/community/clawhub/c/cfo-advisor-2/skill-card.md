## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial recommendations or model outputs may be incomplete or incorrect if company context, assumptions, or referenced scripts are not independently verified. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as advisory finance guidance, verify calculations and models separately, and review material decisions with qualified finance or legal reviewers. <br>
Risk: Company context can contain sensitive operating, fundraising, or financial information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep company-context.md limited to information the user is comfortable allowing the agent to use. <br>
