## Description: <br>
Axiomata Skill Evaluator En evaluates OpenClaw skill directories with bundled Axioma 5-dimension scoring and ISO 25010-style structural checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kofna3369](https://clawhub.ai/user/kofna3369) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and skill publishers use this skill to evaluate OpenClaw skill directories before publication, during improvement work, or during quality audits. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The evaluator reads target skill files and may inspect private content if pointed at broad private directories. <br>
Mitigation: Run it only against intended skill directories and avoid providing credentials or broad private paths unless that inspection is intentional. <br>
