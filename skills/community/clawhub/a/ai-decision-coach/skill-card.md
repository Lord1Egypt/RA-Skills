## Description: <br>
AI决策助手。帮用户分析决策困境，权衡利弊，给出建议。不是替用户做决定，而是帮用户看清局面。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for structured decision coaching when they are weighing options, risks, opportunity costs, emotional preferences, and next steps. It supports reflection and recommendation framing while leaving the final decision with the user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the decision analysis as professional legal, medical, financial, or crisis advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present the output as general decision support and direct users to qualified professionals or emergency resources for regulated or crisis decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad indecision language and produce structured advice where the user only wanted casual discussion. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the decision question, available options, and stakes before giving recommendations. <br>
Risk: Recommendations may overfit the provided prompt context or miss facts that materially change the decision. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for missing constraints, deadlines, reversibility, and downside tolerance before presenting next steps. <br>
