## Description: <br>
Generates technical solution documents from requirements, including architecture design, technology selection, risk assessment, schedule estimates, and API design. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[imgolye](https://clawhub.ai/user/imgolye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, product teams, and technical reviewers use this skill to draft structured technical solution documents before requirements reviews, project kickoff, collaboration, or technical sharing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated architecture, schedule, and risk sections may be incomplete or misleading when requirements are vague or the target system is regulated or security-sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts, provide complete requirements, and have qualified reviewers validate architecture, security, compliance, and delivery estimates before use. <br>
