## Description: <br>
End-to-end podcast monetization and growth coach for sponsorship strategy, pricing, media kits, sponsor outreach, audience growth, premium feeds, affiliate revenue, ad networks, live events, courses, branded podcasts, and exits. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Podcasters, podcast operators, and creators use this skill to diagnose their audience, revenue stage, and constraints, then choose monetization tactics such as sponsorships, premium feeds, affiliates, branded shows, live events, courses, or sale preparation. It helps the agent produce practical business guidance while refusing unethical tactics such as fake listenership, undisclosed paid promotions, and pyramid affiliate schemes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share business-sensitive podcast metrics such as downloads, revenue, sponsor history, and audience demographics. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information the user is comfortable discussing and avoid providing unnecessary sensitive business details. <br>
Risk: The skill can advise on contracts, platform signups, affiliate arrangements, and paid-service decisions that may affect revenue or legal obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Review contracts, affiliate terms, platform terms, and paid-service choices separately before acting. <br>
Risk: Podcast monetization guidance can be misused for undisclosed promotions, fake listenership, inflated CPM claims, or pyramid affiliate schemes. <br>
Mitigation: Refuse unethical monetization tactics and redirect to honest sponsorship disclosure, real audience growth, and compliant affiliate practices. <br>
