## Description: <br>
Humanizer 中文学术版 helps an agent polish Chinese academic writing by identifying AI-like patterns, preserving meaning, and producing more natural scholarly prose. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cangtianhuang](https://clawhub.ai/user/cangtianhuang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, students, researchers, and editors use this skill to revise Chinese academic passages so the wording is more natural, specific, and consistent with scholarly writing norms. The skill focuses on text rewriting and editorial guidance rather than factual research or citation generation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be used to make AI-generated academic text harder to detect, which may violate school, publisher, employer, or disclosure rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for legitimate editing of your own work and confirm that this type of rewriting is allowed by the relevant academic or publication policy. <br>
Risk: The README includes remote installation commands that fetch or run content outside the local artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the remote content and source repository before running npx, git, or curl installation commands. <br>
Risk: Rewriting academic prose can unintentionally change meaning, weaken citations, or obscure responsibility for claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review the revised text against the original sources and verify that claims, terminology, and citations remain accurate. <br>
