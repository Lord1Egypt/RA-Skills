## Use Case: <br>
External Amazon sellers and marketplace operators use this skill to prepare Seller Central listings, GTIN exemption applications, FBA shipment plans, image checklists, product content, and inventory-related guidance. It is not intended for legal, tax, trademark, patent, account reinstatement, or autonomous advertising campaign work. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Seller Central listing, pricing, inventory, FBA, removal, or order actions may affect live commercial operations if followed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep control of the browser session and review all proposed submissions or account actions before confirming them. <br>
Risk: Supplement claims, disclaimers, ingredients, warnings, and examples may be incomplete or stale for current Amazon policy or applicable regulations. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify supplement content against current Amazon policy and applicable regulatory requirements before publishing. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes advertising-related example material even though the skill is not intended for Amazon Advertising work. <br>
Mitigation: Ignore advertising examples unless the user intentionally requests advertising help and validates the result separately. <br>
