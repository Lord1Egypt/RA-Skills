## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to apply a Charlie Munger-style educational reasoning lens to investing, business judgment, risk, psychology of misjudgment, and multidisciplinary decision-making prompts. It is intended for mental-model guidance, not personalized financial, tax, or legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake investing-oriented mental-model guidance for personalized financial, tax, or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as educational reasoning support and require independent professional review before acting on financial, tax, or legal decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad phrases related to Munger, Berkshire Hathaway, investing, or mental models. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit prompts and review whether the Charlie Munger-style lens is appropriate for the user request before relying on the response. <br>
Risk: Persona-style responses can overstate confidence or compress nuanced historical and investment context. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for sources, assumptions, and alternatives when the output informs consequential business or investment analysis. <br>
