## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bundled sourcing data includes supplier email contacts and may be used for business outreach. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify supplier names, certifications, capacities, and contact details before use, and do not use the contacts for bulk outreach or unsolicited campaigns. <br>
Risk: Industry and supplier reference data can become stale or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm sourcing decisions against current official sources, supplier documentation, audits, and direct due diligence before committing business activity. <br>
