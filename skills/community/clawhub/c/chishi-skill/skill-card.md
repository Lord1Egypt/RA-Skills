## Description: <br>
Produces Chinese writing in the 赤石TV style: pseudo-serious, absurd, deadpan internet prose for short copy, comments, and micro essays. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[akai-shuuichi](https://clawhub.ai/user/akai-shuuichi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to draft Chinese social commentary, account diagnoses, short comments, and micro essays in a dry, absurd, internet-native voice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The intended style can become judgmental when describing real people or accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated text before publishing and avoid harassment, protected-class targeting, doxxing, and fabricated allegations. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed for absurd internet commentary, which can drift into explicit abuse if used carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs weird rather than malicious, following the artifact boundary that forbids explicit abuse. <br>
