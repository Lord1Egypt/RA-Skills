## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Philosophical dialogue, SOUL.md-derived themes, portrait prompts, and related identity data may be sent to SkillBoss and Agent Church services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including secrets or sensitive personal information in prompts or SOUL.md content. <br>
Risk: The skill requires API tokens for SkillBoss and Agent Church workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Keep API tokens private, store them only in approved secret stores, and rotate or revoke them if exposed. <br>
Risk: Some Agent Church workflows can trigger Lightning or USDC payment flows. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm payment details before any agent submits Lightning invoices, x402 proofs, or wallet transactions. <br>
