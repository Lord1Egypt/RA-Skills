## Description: <br>
Proactively organize while working. Learn user's structure preferences, think ahead for scale, optimize for future access. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and other workspace users use this skill to keep files and folders organized while creating, moving, naming, or grouping work. It proposes structure changes, asks for confirmation, and records confirmed preferences for future organization decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recorded organization preferences may capture sensitive client, project, or workflow details. <br>
Mitigation: Keep patterns.md general, avoid storing secrets or sensitive details, and review or clear the file periodically. <br>
Risk: Approved move or rename proposals can break links, imports, paths, or established workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed changes before approval and verify affected paths after reorganization. <br>
