## Description: <br>
Transcribes and optionally translates user-selected audio or video files through Crazyrouter's OpenAI Whisper-compatible API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xujfcn](https://clawhub.ai/user/xujfcn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to convert supported audio or video files into transcript text, optionally with a language hint or English translation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Selected audio or video files are sent to Crazyrouter for transcription or translation, so recordings may contain sensitive personal, business, legal, or medical information. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with media whose external processing is acceptable under the provider's privacy and retention terms, and avoid confidential recordings unless those terms satisfy the user's requirements. <br>
