## Use Case: <br>
Creative, brand, and marketing teams use this agent to decompose creative challenges, coordinate multi-model ideation, maintain brainstorming minutes, and produce a final creative proposal. Review and redact sensitive strategy, regulated data, and personal information before using workflows that record or share notes with configured LLM tools. <br>
