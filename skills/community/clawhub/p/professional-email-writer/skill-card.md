## Description: <br>
Craft professional emails for business situations with structured drafts, subject line suggestions, and customizable tone for follow-ups, introductions, requests, apologies, and thank-you notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users use this skill to draft and refine professional business emails for introductions, requests, follow-ups, apologies, thank-you notes, and proposals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated email drafts can include inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate wording if the prompt omits important context or includes sensitive data. <br>
Mitigation: Review and customize each draft before sending, and avoid entering secrets, passwords, private keys, or highly sensitive business data in prompts. <br>
Risk: Future versions could materially change risk if they add mailbox access, sending automation, credentials, or network integrations. <br>
Mitigation: Re-evaluate the security posture before installing upgraded versions that add account access, automation, credentials, or network behavior. <br>
