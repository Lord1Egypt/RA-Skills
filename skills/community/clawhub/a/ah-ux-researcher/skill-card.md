## Description: <br>
Expert UX researcher specializing in user insights, usability testing, and data-driven design decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, designers, researchers, and developers use this skill to plan and synthesize UX research, including interviews, usability tests, surveys, analytics review, journey mapping, accessibility research, and actionable design recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: UX research work can involve participant records, recordings, demographics, or other sensitive user data. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm consent and retention rules before use, avoid sharing raw participant records unless necessary, and de-identify data where possible. <br>
Risk: Research summaries can overstate study size, participant counts, or measured outcomes if example language is treated as real evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Require reported study counts, participant counts, and outcomes to come only from actual project evidence. <br>
