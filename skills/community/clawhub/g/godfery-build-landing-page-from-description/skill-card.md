## Description: <br>
Generates a complete landing page with copy, layout, hero image, and responsive design from a text description. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, solopreneurs, and agencies use this skill to turn a product brief into a complete landing-page draft with copy, layout, visual direction, and responsive design. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated landing-page copy, layout, or images may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated content for accuracy, licensing, brand fit, and publication readiness before external use. <br>
Risk: A landing-page brief could expose secrets, proprietary credentials, or sensitive private information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include secrets, credentials, or sensitive proprietary details in the brief. <br>
