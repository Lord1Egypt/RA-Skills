## Description: <br>
Multi-platform infographic card generator for Xiaohongshu, Pinterest, Instagram, YouTube, and related social media visuals, with style, layout, aspect-ratio, and prompt-template guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and agents use this skill to plan social media card series and assemble image-generation prompts with platform-specific ratios, safe zones, visual styles, and layouts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad image-related wording such as "draw," even when the user only needs lightweight visual guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's intent and target platform before preparing a full card-series workflow or image prompt set. <br>
Risk: Image prompts or source content may be sent to a third-party image-generation provider selected by the user or agent. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive private content in prompts unless the chosen provider and workflow are approved for that content. <br>
