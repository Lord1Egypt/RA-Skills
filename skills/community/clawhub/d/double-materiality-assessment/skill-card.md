## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a sustainability lead, ESG analyst, or reporting manager needs to run a CSRD/ESRS-compliant double materiality assessment, covering impact and financial materiality separately, stakeholder-engagement logging, and a ranked material-topic list with audit trail for assurance sign-off. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sustainability leads, ESG analysts, reporting managers, and assurance preparers use this skill to draft a CSRD/ESRS double materiality assessment with separate impact and financial materiality scoring, stakeholder evidence, thresholds, and an audit-ready assessment record. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The assessment may collect sensitive ESG, workforce, supplier, financial, and stakeholder information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep confidential inputs inside the assessment record, avoid sharing them with external tools or searches, and limit access to authorized reviewers. <br>
Risk: The skill produces a draft assessment record, not a legal, audit, assurance, or board decision. <br>
Mitigation: Require review by the responsible ESG lead and appropriate legal or assurance professionals before using the output for CSRD disclosure or assurance work. <br>
