Risk: Uploaded photos may contain personal or sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Share only images you are comfortable having the agent process, and remove sensitive content when possible. <br>
Risk: Equipment-buying, drone-safety, and legal guidance may be incorrect or incomplete for the user's location. <br>
Mitigation: Verify purchase decisions, drone rules, and legal requirements with authoritative local sources before acting. <br>
