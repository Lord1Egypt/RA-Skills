## Description: <br>
Structured framework for deep philosophical and psychological conversations, guiding agents through clarification, framework application, dialectical challenge, and synthesis across Stoicism, Existentialism, Socratic Method, CBT, ACT, and Jungian psychology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to guide reflective conversations about personal issues, existential questions, meaning-making, belief examination, and psychological patterns without treating the skill as therapy or crisis support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake reflective philosophical or psychological dialogue for mental health care, diagnosis, crisis support, or emergency help. <br>
Mitigation: Position the skill as reflective conversation only; route self-harm risk, abuse, psychosis, severe depression, trauma crisis, acute distress, and emergencies to professional or emergency resources. <br>
Risk: The artifact README includes an unpinned git clone installation command. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the source and install only from a reviewed version or pinned commit before deployment. <br>
