## Description: <br>
Turn raw property details into an MLS-ready listing description plus matching Instagram, Facebook, short-form video, and email-blast variants. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zepoldani](https://clawhub.ai/user/zepoldani) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External real estate agents and brokerages use this skill to turn property specifications into MLS, social media, short-form video, and email marketing copy while preserving Fair Housing guardrails. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated listing copy may include factual errors or unsupported property claims if the supplied details are incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review all copy against the source property details before publication and remove claims that are not supported by the listing record. <br>
Risk: Real estate marketing copy may need to comply with Fair Housing, local MLS, brokerage, and advertising rules. <br>
Mitigation: Have an agent, broker, or compliance reviewer approve the final copy before publishing it to MLS, social, or email channels. <br>
Risk: User-provided property and agent details may contain unnecessary personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the information needed for the listing package and omit unrelated personal data from prompts and published drafts. <br>
