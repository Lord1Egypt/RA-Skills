## Description: <br>
Help non-English-first users hit the right technical answer when docs, memory, configs, skills, and runbooks are stored under English-heavy names. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chrix-goh](https://clawhub.ai/user/chrix-goh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use this skill to bridge a user's original phrasing, canonical intent, and English-heavy technical terminology so retrieval and routing are more likely to reach the right docs, memory, configs, skills, or runbooks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persisting learned terminology mappings can retain private user phrasing in agent memory. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm memory settings and retention policy before saving mappings, and avoid persisting sensitive phrasing unless retention is allowed. <br>
