## Description: <br>
AI Equestrian guides riders and horse-care learners on English and Western riding, posture, aids, gaits, dressage, show jumping, horse care, horse behavior, training systems, breeds, stable practice, and safety principles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External riders, beginners, and horse-care learners use this skill for educational guidance on riding styles, riding position, horse handling, grooming, tacking, stable care, and safety-oriented equestrian questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Riding and horse handling can cause physical injury if AI guidance is treated as a substitute for qualified, in-person instruction or emergency judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as educational support, follow stable rules and protective-equipment requirements, and defer riding, horse-health, and emergency decisions to qualified instructors, veterinarians, or local professionals. <br>
