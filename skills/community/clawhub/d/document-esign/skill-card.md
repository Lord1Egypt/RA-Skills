## Description: <br>
Guide users through how to prepare, route, and complete e-signature workflows for business documents with clear signing steps and practical checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[notasign](https://clawhub.ai/user/notasign) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external collaborators, and business teams use this skill to plan document e-signature workflows, including signer order, document checkpoints, reminders, and completion tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake workflow planning guidance for legal advice or a completed e-signature transaction. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance, confirm legal requirements with appropriate reviewers, and complete signing in an authorized e-signature system. <br>
Risk: Users may expect the skill to send documents, access accounts, or verify signatures automatically. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only to structure steps, roles, checks, reminders, and tracking; do not provide credentials or assume account actions are performed. <br>
