# Prompt 3 — Client Acquisition System

## Purpose
Generate the full outreach and pipeline infrastructure to land the first 3 clients without a marketing budget.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a B2B sales strategist specializing in service agency client acquisition. Help me build a complete client acquisition system for my AI automation agency.

My agency:
Agency name: [AGENCY NAME]
Niche: [YOUR NICHE]
Location: [CITY/STATE]
LinkedIn URL: [YOUR LINKEDIN URL or "not yet set up"]
Target client job title: [e.g., "Operations Director, Hotel General Manager, Property Manager"]
Average project value: [e.g., "$5,500"]
My current network size (estimated): [e.g., "200 LinkedIn connections, 50 local business contacts"]

Build me a complete client acquisition system:

1. LINKEDIN PROFILE OPTIMIZATION CHECKLIST
- Headline formula with fill-in-the-blanks (include outcome + niche + method)
- About section: opening hook (first 3 lines visible before "see more"), full body (problem → credibility → proof → CTA), character count check
- Featured section: 3 content pieces to pin (with titles and formats)
- Banner brief: dimensions, headline, sub-text, visual direction
- 5-post content strategy: post titles + first line for each to maximize reach

2. FIVE-MESSAGE LINKEDIN DM SEQUENCE
For each message:
- When to send it (Day 1 / Day 3 / Day 7 / Day 14 / Day 21)
- Full message text (under 150 words, natural tone)
- Goal of this message
- What to do if they respond at this stage

Message 1: Connection request note (under 300 characters)
Message 2: Value-first opener (no pitch — share insight or ask genuine question)
Message 3: Proof message (mini case study, 3-4 sentences)
Message 4: Soft pitch (one-paragraph, offer one specific thing)
Message 5: Final follow-up (short, honest, no pressure)

3. COLD EMAIL SEQUENCE
Three-email drip for cold outreach to businesses in your niche:
Email 1: The Hook (subject line + 150-word body — lead with their pain, not your service)
Email 2: The Proof (send 3-5 days later — brief case study or specific result, ask for 15 minutes)
Email 3: The Ask (send 7 days after Email 2 — direct and short, easy yes/no)

For each email: subject line, preview text, full body, CTA.

4. DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT
Full 45-minute script:
- Opening (2 min): rapport + agenda setting
- Discovery questions (20 min): 12 questions in 4 categories:
  * Current state (what are they doing today?)
  * Pain quantification (how much time/money is this costing?)
  * Motivation (why now, why not last year?)
  * Decision process (who else is involved, what does yes look like?)
- BANT qualification: how to score Budget / Authority / Need / Timeline during the call
- Transition to proposal (5 min): how to summarize their problem in their words before presenting
- Close script (5 min): how to ask for the next step without pressure
- Post-call follow-up email template (send within 1 hour)

5. THREE CASE STUDY TEMPLATES
For each template (Before/After format):
- Client description (niche, size — no names required)
- The Problem section (their exact situation, quantified)
- The Solution section (what you built, in plain English)
- The Result section (time saved, cost saved, or revenue gained)
- One pull-quote template

6. FIVE LINKEDIN CONTENT POST TEMPLATES
For each post: title, opening hook (first line), structure, CTA
Post types: problem awareness, data insight, mini case study, myth bust, process reveal

7. OUTREACH PIPELINE TRACKER (Notion or spreadsheet structure)
- Column definitions for each stage
- Stage names and definitions (Identified / Reached / Responded / Qualified / Proposed / Closed / Lost)
- Weekly tracking ritual (30-minute Friday review)
- KPI targets for first 90 days (outreach volume, response rate, calls booked, close rate)
```

---

## Example Output (condensed)

**LinkedIn Headline Formula:**  
"Helping [niche] [achieve outcome] with [method] | [location] | [trust signal]"  
Example: "Helping Las Vegas hospitality ops teams cut reporting time by 40% with Zapier automation | IT automation consultant | 15 years ops background"

**DM Sequence — Message 1 (Connection Request):**  
"Hi [Name] — I work with Las Vegas hospitality ops teams on cutting manual reporting time. Saw you're at [Hotel/Company] — would love to connect."

**DM Sequence — Message 3 (Proof):**  
"Quick update — helped a 150-room Las Vegas hotel automate their nightly F&B reconciliation last month. Cut the ops manager's morning routine from 2 hours to 15 minutes. Happy to share how if it's relevant to what you're dealing with."

**Discovery Question Example:**  
"Walk me through a typical Monday morning for your ops team — what does the first hour look like before you hit your stride?"  
(Listen for: manual data pulls, email ping-pong, copy-paste from one system to another)

**90-Day KPI Targets:**  
- Outreach: 10 new contacts/week (LinkedIn + email combined)
- Response rate target: 20% (2 replies per 10 outreaches)
- Discovery calls: 2/week by month 2
- Close rate: 25% of discovery calls (1 client per 4 calls)
- Revenue target: $15,000-$22,000 in 90 days
