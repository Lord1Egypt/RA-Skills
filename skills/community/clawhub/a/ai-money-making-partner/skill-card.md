## Description: <br>
Sidekick Os Pro V2.0 helps an agent collect a user's side-business profile, analyze monetization options, and produce a concrete execution plan with follow-up guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to turn a user's skills, available time, budget, and income goals into a personalized side-income plan, including market direction, first-week actions, milestones, and ongoing adjustments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may collect business, budget, income-goal, schedule, and other personal profiling details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum details needed for planning, and avoid entering sensitive financial records, credentials, or private client data. <br>
Risk: The security summary notes under-disclosed local persistence behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Review any local files created during use and confirm storage, deletion, and retention expectations before relying on the skill. <br>
Risk: The security summary notes under-disclosed market-search behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether external search or browsing is enabled in the agent environment and review generated market claims before acting on them. <br>
