## Description: <br>
Creates structured Seedance 2.0 video-generation prompts with asset mapping, timed scene beats, negative constraints, and generation settings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wild-river2016](https://clawhub.ai/user/wild-river2016) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and prompt engineers use this skill to turn Seedance video ideas, reference assets, and scene requirements into concise English prompts for video generation. It is especially focused on multimodal reference mapping, short video timelines, continuation workflows, product videos, dialogue scenes, and original character concepts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes guidance for disguising brand or character references to get past platform review. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for fully original, licensed, or public-domain concepts; do not use it to imitate protected characters, brands, logos, celebrities, or platform-blocked material. <br>
Risk: Generated prompts may be used directly in a video-generation workflow without checking legality, platform policy, or factual fit. <br>
Mitigation: Review each output before use, especially negative constraints, asset references, real-person likenesses, and any brand or character references. <br>
