## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional Postiz automation can send content, media URLs, schedule times, and platform destinations to an external scheduling service. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the exact post content, media URLs, schedule, and connected accounts before using automation. <br>
Risk: Postiz scheduling guidance relies on POSTIZ_API_KEY. <br>
Mitigation: Store POSTIZ_API_KEY only in a secure environment variable or secret store and avoid pasting it into prompts or shared documents. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing guidance may include review campaigns, customer permissions, or public performance claims that need local compliance review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify customer permissions, review-contest rules, and public marketing claims before posting or running campaigns. <br>
