## Description: <br>
Travel Assistant is a personal travel planning advisor that organizes trip details and proactively flags overlooked needs such as visas, weather, cultural considerations, packing lists, and destination-specific precautions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[heidi1998](https://clawhub.ai/user/heidi1998) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan personal travel, organize itinerary details, build checklists, and receive reminders about common travel preparation gaps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel details may be sent to search providers when the skill uses web search for current weather, visa, safety, and destination information. <br>
Mitigation: Use general search terms where possible and avoid entering passport numbers, full booking confirmations, home address, or other unnecessary personal details. <br>
Risk: Visa, entry, weather, safety, and local-policy guidance can change or be incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify time-sensitive travel requirements and safety information with official sources before departure. <br>
