## Description: <br>
Repurposes a published blog post into platform-specific drafts for an X thread, Xiaohongshu note, LinkedIn carousel, and dev.to or Zenn cross-post. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris-1031](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris-1031) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, growth teams, and developers use this skill to turn one published blog post or source file into channel-ready social distribution drafts without writing a new article from scratch. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafts may include built-in example handles, links, or branding. <br>
Mitigation: Review every output before posting and replace @WeiYipei, Gingiris, and example URLs with the publisher's own identity and links. <br>
Risk: Platform-specific publishing defaults can reduce reach or create duplicate-content issues if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm channel rules such as dev.to canonical settings, tag limits, link placement, and posting cadence before publication. <br>
Risk: Zenn guidance defaults toward Japanese output. <br>
Mitigation: State the desired Zenn language explicitly when Japanese is not appropriate. <br>
