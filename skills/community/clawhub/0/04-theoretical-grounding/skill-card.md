## Description: <br>
Use when the user has a research problem but needs theoretical foundations, conceptual lineage, or research traditions that support the problem framing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[snake-fan](https://clawhub.ai/user/snake-fan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers and writers use this skill to turn a checked research problem into claim-centered theoretical grounding, including theory hooks, candidate traditions, selected traditions, and a final grounding report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent reads and updates local research notes in the selected workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the workspace root before use and review generated or modified Markdown before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Targeted theory searches may surface weak, mismatched, or provisional theoretical support. <br>
Mitigation: Verify representative sources and keep unsupported or insufficiently sourced traditions marked as provisional. <br>
Risk: Theoretical grounding can make a weak research problem appear better supported than it is. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve Problem Reality Check verdicts, unresolved fragilities, and evidence needs in the final grounding. <br>
