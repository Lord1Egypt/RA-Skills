## Description: <br>
Automated SEO prospect research and outreach for web designers, agencies, and freelancers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[MrMooseyMan](https://clawhub.ai/user/MrMooseyMan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
Proprietary <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External web designers, agencies, freelancers, and SEO consultants use this skill to research local business prospects, audit web presence, generate prospect reports and outreach drafts, and track a lead pipeline. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow contacts prospect websites and third-party APIs when configured. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only where external lookups are acceptable, configure API access deliberately, and avoid including sensitive client strategy in searches or reports. <br>
Risk: Lead, prospect, and outreach data is saved locally. <br>
Mitigation: Review stored data periodically and delete old prospect records that are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Generated outreach may be inaccurate, too aggressive, or unsuitable for a prospect. <br>
Mitigation: Review every report and outreach draft before sending, and verify prospect details before recording or contacting the business. <br>
