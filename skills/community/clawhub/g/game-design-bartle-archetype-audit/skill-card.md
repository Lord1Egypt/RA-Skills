## Description: <br>
Audit a game, feature, event, progression system, social system, live-ops loop, or onboarding flow through the lens of Bartle player archetypes: Achievers, Explorers, Socializers, and Killers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and live-ops reviewers use this skill to evaluate how a design motivates, neglects, or frustrates different Bartle player archetypes. It helps turn archetype-by-archetype findings into imbalance diagnoses and design recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Game documents or examples supplied for review may include unrelated private information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the game design material needed for the audit and remove unrelated sensitive details before prompting the agent. <br>
Risk: Archetype audit findings are qualitative and can overstate player motivation patterns if treated as definitive user research. <br>
Mitigation: Use the audit as design guidance and validate major product decisions with playtest data, telemetry, or player research. <br>
