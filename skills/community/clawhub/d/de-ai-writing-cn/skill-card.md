## Description: <br>
去模板化写作技能，用于降低 AI 味、提升中文表达自然度与专业感。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
中文内容作者、编辑和 agent 用户使用此技能改写已有文本，减少模板化语气、重复句式和过度列表化表达，同时保留事实、数字、时间、链接和关键名词。 <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can alter tone and emphasis in Chinese writing, which may unintentionally change the author's intended nuance. <br>
Mitigation: Review revised text against the original facts, key entities, dates, numbers, and intended voice before publishing. <br>
