## Description: <br>
Generate personalized cold email sequences with follow-ups, framework selection, A/B variants, and merge-field personalization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tktk-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/tktk-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales and marketing users use this skill to draft B2B cold outreach campaigns, including initial emails, timed follow-ups, personalization fields, A/B variants, and deliverability notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outreach may contain inaccurate claims, deceptive framing, or compliance gaps. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated messages for truthful claims, lawful prospect sourcing, unsubscribe handling, and CAN-SPAM/GDPR compliance before use. <br>
Risk: Email drafts may use spam-triggering wording or patterns that reduce deliverability. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the included deliverability checks and review subject lines, links, formatting, and sender-domain readiness before sending. <br>
