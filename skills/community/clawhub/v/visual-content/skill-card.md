## Description: <br>
Plans, creates, and repurposes visual content such as images, infographics, and social post visuals across website, social, email, and other channels. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content marketers, designers, and developers use this skill to decide what visuals are needed for a content item, choose channel-specific specs, and map one core visual into platform-ready adaptations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local project context may influence visual recommendations if brand guidance files are present. <br>
Mitigation: Keep local visual identity guidance accurate and avoid storing secrets in project context files. <br>
Risk: Instruction-only guidance can still produce incorrect or outdated platform image specifications. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated specs before publication, especially for channel-specific dimensions and cropping constraints. <br>
