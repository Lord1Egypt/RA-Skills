## Description: <br>
ANDG helps users turn website ideas into clear site structure, copy, UX direction, technology suggestions, and build plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Andrewgufx](https://clawhub.ai/user/Andrewgufx) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, and teams use this skill to turn vague website goals into practical page structures, content direction, UX guidance, and implementation plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated strategy, copy, UX direction, or technology recommendations may not fit the user's actual project constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt the generated plan before using it for a real website build. <br>
