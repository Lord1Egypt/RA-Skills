## Description: <br>
Analyzes resume content and produces a structured audit report with scoring, strengths, weaknesses, and improvement suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lemons-niit](https://clawhub.ai/user/lemons-niit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Resume reviewers, job candidates, and career-support agents use this skill to evaluate resume completeness, structure, professional wording, technical depth, and project descriptions. It returns scoring details and concrete recommendations that can guide resume revision. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume text may contain personal contact details, home addresses, government identifiers, or employer-confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Mask or remove unnecessary personal and confidential details before passing resume content to an AI agent. <br>
Risk: AI-generated resume scores and recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific hiring context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the report as review guidance and have a human reviewer confirm important edits and decisions. <br>
