## Description: <br>
Curated image generation assistant covering four categories: characters, scenes, products, and style transformation for generating high-quality stylized 3D images. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ericn26-star](https://clawhub.ai/user/ericn26-star) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to turn character, scene, product, logo, meme, city, landmark, and style-transformation requests into structured image-generation prompts and generated image outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reference images or image prompts may be analyzed by platform image tools. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing sensitive personal, confidential, or regulated images unless the user is comfortable with platform image processing. <br>
Risk: Broad image-related trigger words may activate the skill for ambiguous requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the intended image-generation task when the request is vague or could match multiple styles. <br>
