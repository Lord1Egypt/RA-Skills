## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market figures, industry trends, supplier examples, and procurement advice may be incomplete or outdated for a specific purchase decision. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify market data, certifications, supplier status, pricing, payment terms, and compliance requirements before committing spend. <br>
Risk: The skill provides cluster-level sourcing intelligence rather than verified individual factory contacts. <br>
Mitigation: Use the guidance to narrow regions and subsectors, then perform supplier-specific qualification, audits, and reference checks separately. <br>
