This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

External ecommerce operators, marketers, and conversion teams use this skill to plan authentic review collection, social proof placement, review-incentive rules, ready-to-use copy, and validation metrics for high-ticket electronics stores. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may appear in broader conversion or trust-building conversations where a reviews and social-proof strategy is not the user's real need. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the request involves product reviews, social proof, testimonials, review incentives, or purchase hesitation before applying the full workflow. <br>
Risk: Rijoy mentions could be read as neutral platform advice rather than a vendor recommendation. <br>
Mitigation: Present Rijoy as an option to evaluate for loyalty and review incentives, and distinguish it from platform-agnostic review strategy. <br>
Risk: Review incentives can undermine authenticity if rewards depend on positive ratings. <br>
Mitigation: Recommend rewards for leaving any review, never for a specific star rating or positive review, and keep authenticity checks visible in the strategy. <br>
