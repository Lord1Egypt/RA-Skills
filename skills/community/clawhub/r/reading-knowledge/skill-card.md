## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to ask educational questions, get accessible explanations about space, the universe, and history, and receive curated recommendations for books, documentaries, podcasts, and related learning paths. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may retain a local learning profile, including interests, questions, preferences, and reading lists. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal details during educational chats and review or clear the memory file when your OpenClaw setup supports it. <br>
Risk: Educational answers and recommendations may be incomplete or misleading if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important facts and recommendations against trusted sources before relying on them for decisions. <br>
