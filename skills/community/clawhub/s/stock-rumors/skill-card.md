## Description: <br>
Rumor Scanner - find early signals including M&A rumors, insider activity, analyst upgrades/downgrades, social whispers, and SEC/regulatory activity via the AIsa API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bibaofeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/bibaofeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to scan for stock-market rumor signals across M&A activity, insider transactions, analyst actions, social or news whispers, and regulatory events. It ranks the top signals by impact score and presents market-intelligence notes for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends requests to AIsa or a configured compatible API endpoint using AISA_API_KEY. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the publisher and configured API base URL are trusted, and protect AISA_API_KEY as a sensitive credential. <br>
Risk: Generated rumor reports are unverified market intelligence and may be incomplete, inaccurate, or time-sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as informational only, verify signals against primary sources, and do not rely on the output as financial advice. <br>
