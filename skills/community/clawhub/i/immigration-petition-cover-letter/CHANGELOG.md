## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-24
Initial release. Drafts a USCIS petition or application cover letter (I-129, I-140 EB-1A / EB-1B / EB-1C / EB-2 / EB-2 NIW / EB-3, I-130, I-485, I-589, I-918 / I-914 / I-360, I-526E, N-400) for a single filing — walks the named statutory and regulatory eligibility criteria (e.g., EB-1A 8 CFR § 204.5(h)(3) ten-criterion plus Kazarian final-merits, EB-2 NIW Matter of Dhanasar three-prong, O-1A 8 CFR § 214.2(o)(3)(iii)), maps each exhibit to the criterion it supports, flags missing critical evidence and orphan exhibits, computes filing fees including Asylum Program Fee and Premium Processing I-907, routes to the correct Lockbox / Service Center / online channel, and produces a DRAFT cover letter, tabbed exhibit list, and filing checklist for attorney-of-record review and signature before filing.
