## Description: <br>
Art Philosophy adapts to a user's visual language and creative values to provide personalized art analysis, critique, palette reflection, challenges, references, and philosophy guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External artists, designers, and creative practitioners use this skill to receive adaptive visual-art feedback, critique, creative exercises, and art-philosophy discussion shaped by their stated preferences and prior art-related conversations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personalized art coaching may reflect sensitive creative preferences or personal details shared during art-related conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal details unless they should shape future feedback, and use the agent platform's memory review, reset, or disable controls where available. <br>
