## Description: <br>
Convert any PDF to Markdown, JSON, and HTML using OpenDataLoader. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[adelpro](https://clawhub.ai/user/adelpro) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and document automation users use this skill to convert PDFs into readable Markdown, structured JSON, or HTML for search, RAG pipelines, accessibility, and content migration workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional remote installer executes a script fetched over the network. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect and trust the installer before running it, or install manually with pip where possible. <br>
Risk: Generated Markdown, JSON, and HTML may contain sensitive text or metadata from the source PDF. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outputs before sharing or publishing them. <br>
