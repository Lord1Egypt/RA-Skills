## Description: <br>
Strategic coach for Decrypto (截码战) that helps players practice clue-giving, analyze interceptions, learn strategy, and review completed rounds. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[enihsago](https://clawhub.ai/user/enihsago) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External board-game players use this skill to improve Decrypto clue-giving, interception analysis, multi-round strategy, and post-round review in English or Chinese. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated clue recommendations or interception analysis may be strategically incorrect or reveal patterns opponents can exploit. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggestions against the current game state and choose clues that fit the team's shared context. <br>
Risk: The skill discusses misdirection and secret clue systems as gameplay tactics. <br>
Mitigation: Use those tactics only within consensual Decrypto play and avoid applying them to sensitive or real-world deception contexts. <br>
