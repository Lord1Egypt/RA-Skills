## Description: <br>
Provides NSFC grant application guidance for youth and general projects, including proposal structure, abstract review, logic checks, format review, and representative-paper selection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wylcasia](https://clawhub.ai/user/wylcasia) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers applying for NSFC youth or general projects use this skill to structure proposals, review abstracts and logic, check submission formatting, and plan representative-paper selection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential, classified, or institutionally restricted research material while seeking proposal help. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive material unless organizational policy permits it, and redact project details when possible. <br>
Risk: Grant guidance or revised wording may be inaccurate, outdated, or misaligned with current NSFC and institutional rules. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations against official NSFC guidance and institutional requirements before submission. <br>
Risk: AI-assisted wording, contribution statements, or author-role descriptions may create disclosure or research-integrity issues if used unreviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated or revised wording for factual accuracy and disclose AI assistance where applicable. <br>
Risk: Optional shell checklist scripts prompt for application details and execute locally. <br>
Mitigation: Review scripts before running them and use a trusted local environment. <br>
