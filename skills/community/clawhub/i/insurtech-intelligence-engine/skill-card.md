## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Insurance workflows may involve personal identifiers, medical details, claims records, and other sensitive policyholder data. <br>
Mitigation: Redact unnecessary identifiers, share only the minimum required data, and confirm consent and lawful basis before using sensitive information with the skill. <br>
Risk: Underwriting, claims, solvency, IFRS 17, and regulatory outputs may be incomplete or incorrect if treated as final decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified insurance, actuarial, claims, legal, or compliance professionals verify outputs before acting on them. <br>
