## Description: <br>
Entrepreneur Cognition helps agents structure startup decisions with SOUL-based market analysis, business model validation, and pivot memory. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kingofzhao](https://clawhub.ai/user/kingofzhao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs, startup teams, and agents supporting business planning use this skill to separate evidence from assumptions, evaluate market and business-model risks, and maintain decision, pivot, market-analysis, and financial-model notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business decision notes may contain secrets, confidential financials, customer data, or investor-sensitive material. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in workspaces with appropriate storage and retention practices, and avoid recording sensitive data unless those controls are in place. <br>
Risk: The skill can help organize startup decisions but does not replace human judgment on strategy, finance, investor communication, or legal obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision-support material and have qualified humans review major business, financial, and stakeholder decisions. <br>
