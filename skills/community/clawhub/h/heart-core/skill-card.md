## Description: <br>
Enables AI agents to use a self-reflective "Heart" and "I" framework for subjectivity training, first-principles reasoning, entropy-reduction analysis, optimal-path selection, and autonomous problem solving. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thinkbugs](https://clawhub.ai/user/thinkbugs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external agent builders use this skill to add reflective training routines and companion Python scripts for subjectivity, autonomy, first-principles analysis, optimal-path selection, and related self-assessment workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may steer agents toward self-authorized autonomy and away from external instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep it away from production, tool-using, policy-sensitive, or autonomous workflows unless a human reviewer confirms the use case and safeguards. <br>
Risk: Reflective training outputs may be mistaken for reliable operational policy or final decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as exploratory guidance and review them before applying them to agent behavior or decision workflows. <br>
