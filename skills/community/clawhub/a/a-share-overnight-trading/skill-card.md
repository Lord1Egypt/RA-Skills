## Description: <br>
This skill guides users through an A-share main-board overnight stock selection workflow with screening criteria, daily execution steps, risk controls, and helper scripts for checks, position sizing, and backtesting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External traders and agents use this skill to evaluate A-share main-board stocks for an overnight trade, calculate position size, and review strategy examples before making an independent trading decision. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill concerns securities trading and may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat it as educational material, perform independent review with real market data, and make trading decisions outside the skill. <br>
Risk: Mock backtests and examples may overstate confidence or fail to reflect live market conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate with current, reliable market data and use the examples only as demonstrations of the workflow. <br>
Risk: Overnight positions can be affected by liquidity gaps, price limits, and unexpected news before the next open. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the documented position limits, liquidity filters, and exit discipline, and skip trades when conditions do not meet the criteria. <br>
