## Description: <br>
Formats agent messages for WhatsApp by applying platform-specific styling syntax and avoiding markdown that would render poorly on mobile. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rubenfb23](https://clawhub.ai/user/rubenfb23) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to adapt WhatsApp-targeted messages to WhatsApp's lightweight formatting syntax for clean mobile reading. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Messages may include unsupported markdown or overly complex structure if the agent ignores the formatting rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review WhatsApp-bound output for single-asterisk bold, simple lists, and absence of headers, tables, and horizontal rules before sending. <br>
