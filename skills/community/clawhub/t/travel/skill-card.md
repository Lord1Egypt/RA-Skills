## Description: <br>
Build a personal travel system for dream destinations, trip planning, and travel memories. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to organize personal travel ideas, planned trips, bookings, packing lists, budgets, documents, and completed-trip memories in a local travel workspace. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive travel details may be stored in local files under ~/travel/, including passport, insurance, confirmation, emergency contact, and loyalty account information. <br>
Mitigation: Decide what sensitive details are acceptable to store before use; prefer partial references over full secret numbers unless intentionally saving them locally. <br>
