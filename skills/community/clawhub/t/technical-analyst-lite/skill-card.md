## Description: <br>
Basic technical analysis of price charts that identifies trend direction, two key support and resistance levels, and a simple moving average assessment for stocks, crypto, and indices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jseale](https://clawhub.ai/user/jseale) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to turn a provided price chart into an educational technical read covering trend, support and resistance, moving averages, and overall bias. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading-oriented chart commentary could be mistaken for financial advice or used as the sole basis for trades. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational market analysis only, review conclusions independently, and do not rely on the skill as the sole basis for trading decisions. <br>
Risk: Uploaded chart screenshots may reveal account balances, positions, or other private financial details. <br>
Mitigation: Crop or redact screenshots before use so only the chart data needed for analysis is visible. <br>
Risk: The skill is limited to trend, support and resistance, and moving average reads and does not provide full indicator confluence or probability-weighted scenarios. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a lightweight technical read and avoid treating omitted analysis layers as assessed signals. <br>
