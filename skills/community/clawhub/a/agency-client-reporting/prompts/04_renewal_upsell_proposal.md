# Prompt 4: Renewal & Upsell Proposal

## Prompt

You are a senior account strategist writing a renewal and upsell proposal for a valuable retainer client. The proposal must make renewal feel like the obvious choice — not a sales pitch — by leading with results, framing the investment as ROI, and offering clear options that anchor the client toward the recommended tier.

**Current Contract:**
- Client Name: [CLIENT NAME]
- Current scope: [e.g., SEO + Content Marketing (12 blog posts/month)]
- Current term: [e.g., 6-month retainer ending May 31, 2026]
- Current monthly fee: [e.g., $3,500/month]
- Contract start date: [e.g., December 1, 2025]

**Performance Summary (for the ROI section):**
[Key results from the contract period — be specific]
- Example: "Organic traffic grew from 4,200 to 11,800 sessions/month (+181%)"
- Example: "Generated 312 inbound leads at an average CPL of $11.22"
- Example: "Ranked #1 for 7 target keywords (was 0 at start)"

**Client's Goals for Next Year:**
[What does the client want to achieve in the next 6-12 months?]

**Proposed Renewal:**
- Renewal term: [e.g., 12-month contract]
- Option 1 (maintain): [same scope, same price or slight increase]
- Option 2 (expand): [add 1-2 services, higher price]
- Option 3 (premium): [full-service or priority tier, highest price]

**Proposed Upsell Service(s):**
- Service: [e.g., Google Ads Management]
- Why now: [e.g., "They've maxed organic lead volume for their budget — paid search is the logical next channel"]
- Estimated add-on cost: [e.g., $1,500/month + $800 ad spend]

---

Generate the renewal proposal with:

1. **Cover Letter** (1 page, warm and results-focused)
   - Open with a genuine relationship acknowledgment (not sycophantic)
   - Summarize the partnership in 2 sentences
   - Transition to results ("Here's what we built together...")
   - Close with confidence ("We'd love to keep the momentum going")

2. **ROI Summary: Value Delivered**
   Table format:
   | Investment | Return | Multiple |
   - Total fees paid: $X
   - Revenue/leads/traffic attributable value: $Y
   - ROI multiple: Xх

3. **Renewal Options** (3-tier table with decoy pricing psychology)
   | Option | What's Included | Monthly Investment | Best For |
   - Option A: Maintain current (lowest — anchor)
   - Option B: Growth tier — recommended (middle — highlight this one)
   - Option C: Full-service premium (highest — makes B look reasonable)

4. **Why We Recommend Option B** (3 bullet points — connect to their stated goals for next year)

5. **Upsell Section: The Logical Next Step**
   - What we're recommending: [service name]
   - Why now (3 reasons tied to their business context)
   - What it adds to their current results
   - Investment breakdown (add-on cost framed as cost-per-result)

6. **Testimonial Request** (template for the agency to send to the client asking for a quote)
   - Short, easy-to-respond-to email asking for 1-2 sentences about their experience

7. **Next Steps**
   - Action 1: [Client reviews proposal by X date]
   - Action 2: [30-min call to discuss options — link to book]
   - Action 3: [Contract sent within 24 hours of verbal agreement]
   - Urgency note: renewing 30 days early locks current pricing + guarantees team continuity

**Tone:** Confident, warm, partner-to-partner. Not a sales pitch — a natural next chapter. The client should feel pulled forward, not pushed.
