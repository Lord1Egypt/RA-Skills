## Description: <br>
Create a transparent, sourced "Top 3-5" AI builder signal digest from curated links or a local signals log, with why-care and maturity notes for each item. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and AI agent builders use this skill to produce concise, post-ready digests that explain what each source is, why builders should care, and how mature or adopted it appears to be. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated digests may include sensitive or unsuitable material from a local signals log. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the input log is safe to quote and review the generated Markdown before publishing. <br>
Risk: The bundled script can overwrite a file when --out points at an existing path. <br>
Mitigation: Choose a deliberate output path and avoid pointing --out at important files unless replacement is intended. <br>
Risk: External GitHub or arXiv lookups can fail because of rate limits or network blocking. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the item, disclose unavailable GitHub stats, and avoid guessing maturity or adoption. <br>
