## Description: <br>
Create effective flashcards, optimize retention, and master spaced repetition with card design, deck organization, and study diagnostics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and learners use this skill to create, audit, organize, and improve Anki flashcards from study material they explicitly provide. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent may use sensitive study notes, PDFs, deck exports, or statistics if the user shares them. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the materials intended for flashcard creation or analysis, and exclude private or unnecessary content before asking the agent to process it. <br>
Risk: Flashcards or study settings may be ineffective if generated from incomplete context or a mismatched language preference. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the subject area, current Anki experience level, immediate study goal, and preferred language before using generated cards or settings. <br>
