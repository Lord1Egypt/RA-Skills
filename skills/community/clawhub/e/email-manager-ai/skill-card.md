## Description: <br>
Email Manager helps draft, refine, template, and track professional emails, including replies, follow-ups, cold outreach, subject lines, tone checks, batch emails, and reminders, while keeping data local and not connecting to an inbox or email provider. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, freelancers, and other external users use this skill to compose, improve, and organize email text before manually sending it through their own email tools. It is intended for drafting and local follow-up tracking, not for inbox access or automated email delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local settings, templates, follow-ups, and draft history may contain sensitive email content. <br>
Mitigation: Install only where local retention is acceptable, avoid saving highly sensitive email text, and periodically review or delete files under ~/.openclaw/email-manager/. <br>
