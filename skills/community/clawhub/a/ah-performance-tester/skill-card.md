## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Load, stress, or spike tests can disrupt systems or violate policy when run against production or unauthorized targets. <br>
Mitigation: Run generated tests only against systems you own or are explicitly authorized to test, preferably in staging or isolated environments. <br>
Risk: Generated checkout, authentication, database, or CI examples can touch sensitive flows, write data, or run repeatedly if copied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Use synthetic data, sandbox payment and checkout paths, and review production targets, concurrency, schedules, database writes, and cleanup before execution. <br>
