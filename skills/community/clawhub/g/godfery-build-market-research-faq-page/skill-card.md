## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Web-search-assisted FAQ drafting can include inaccurate or outdated factual claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review market claims, sources, and customer-facing language before publication. <br>
Risk: Prompts may contain confidential business or strategy information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including confidential business data in prompts that may be used with chat or web search. <br>
