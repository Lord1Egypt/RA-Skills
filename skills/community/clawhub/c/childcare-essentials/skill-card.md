## Description: <br>
Practical physical childcare skills for ages 0-5. Use when someone is a new parent, babysitter, grandparent, or anyone suddenly responsible for a young child and needs immediate practical guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External caregivers use this skill for immediate, practical guidance on caring for children ages 0-5, including safe sleep, feeding, bathing, choking response, fever rules, and home childproofing. It is intended to help users recognize when professional medical care, emergency services, Poison Control, or certified CPR training is needed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat high-stakes childcare and emergency-preparedness guidance as a substitute for professional medical advice or emergency services. <br>
Mitigation: Direct users to verify urgent health and safety decisions with pediatric professionals, emergency services, Poison Control, certified CPR training, and local pediatric guidance. <br>
Risk: Childcare safety standards, emergency numbers, poison-control contacts, car-seat laws, vaccination schedules, and formula-preparation guidance vary by country or region. <br>
Mitigation: Apply local emergency numbers, local health authority guidance, and jurisdiction-specific safety requirements when using the skill. <br>
Risk: The declared filesystem permission is not needed for the skill's text-only behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Review requested permissions before deployment and avoid granting filesystem access unless the deployment environment has a separate documented need. <br>
