## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers and proposal writers use this skill to draft, optimize, and analyze platform-specific proposals for Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer, and similar marketplaces. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals may include inaccurate or misleading claims about experience, pricing, timelines, or client requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review each proposal before sending and verify case studies, prices, delivery commitments, and project assumptions. <br>
Risk: Reusable profile details can expose confidential personal, client, or financial information if stored too broadly. <br>
Mitigation: Keep TOOLS.md limited to reusable professional details and avoid confidential client, financial, or account information unless necessary. <br>
Risk: Broad activation phrases can trigger the skill in proposal-adjacent conversations where generation is not intended. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target platform, project brief, and desired output before producing a proposal. <br>
