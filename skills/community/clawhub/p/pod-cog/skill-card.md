## Description: <br>
Pod Cog helps agents use CellCog to create podcast scripts, show notes, interview prep, audiograms, and full multi-voice podcast episodes with intro/outro music and finished MP3 output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nitishgargiitd](https://clawhub.ai/user/nitishgargiitd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, podcast teams, and agents use Pod Cog to draft, plan, and produce podcast materials through CellCog. It supports pre-production, production, post-production, and promotion tasks including full audio episodes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Podcast prompts, scripts, guest research, show notes, and production requests are processed through CellCog. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when CellCog's data handling terms are acceptable for the material being submitted. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a CellCog API key and may run long audio-production jobs. <br>
Mitigation: Use an appropriate API key, keep it out of shared artifacts, and monitor account usage for long-running jobs. <br>
