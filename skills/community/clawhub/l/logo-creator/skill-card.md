## Description: <br>
Engineer professional-grade brand logos using geometric primitives and negative space, generating minimalist, scalable vector-style marks via muapi.ai. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Anil-matcha](https://clawhub.ai/user/Anil-matcha) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to turn a brand name, concept, style, and palette into a structured logo-generation brief and invoke an image-generation helper for a centered, flat, vector-style logo. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Brand names, concepts, and style details are sent through the configured image-generation provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential launch plans, customer data, credentials, or regulated information in logo prompts. <br>
Risk: The script depends on the expected OpenClaw core media image-generation helper. <br>
Mitigation: Install and run the skill only in a trusted OpenClaw environment where that helper is expected and configured. <br>
