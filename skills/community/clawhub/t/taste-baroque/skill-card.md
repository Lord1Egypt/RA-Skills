## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Creative outputs may over-apply Baroque stylistic direction where a simpler or more neutral aesthetic is required. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated writing, UI, or branding outputs against the target audience, brand constraints, and the skill's must-avoid criteria before use. <br>
