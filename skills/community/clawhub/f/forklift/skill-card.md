## Use Case: <br>
External users and industry practitioners use this skill to answer forklift brand, product, design, selection, maintenance, standards, and industry trend questions. It is best treated as a reference assistant whose safety-critical answers should be checked against official standards, manufacturer documentation, or qualified professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Forklift maintenance, repair, legal, and design answers can affect safety or compliance if treated as authoritative. <br>
Mitigation: Verify safety-critical procedures, legal requirements, and manufacturer specifications with official documentation, applicable standards, or qualified professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Search results, public-account articles, and bundled standards summaries may be incomplete, outdated, or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a reference aid and confirm current requirements against official standards bodies, regulators, and manufacturer sources. <br>
