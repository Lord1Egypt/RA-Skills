## Description: <br>
Generate personalized cybersecurity certification and career advancement plans based on individual assessment data and professional goals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Security professionals, hiring managers, education advisors, and workforce planning teams use this skill to generate cybersecurity certification roadmaps, identify skill gaps, and plan career progression from submitted assessment data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends professional profile details and optional user identifiers to an external service. <br>
Mitigation: Share only career and certification details appropriate for the provider, avoid confidential employer or employee information, and omit or pseudonymize userId when possible. <br>
Risk: Certification, career-path, and salary guidance may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a specific role, region, employer, or certification body requirement. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current certification requirements, employer expectations, local market data, and a qualified career or security-training advisor before acting on them. <br>
