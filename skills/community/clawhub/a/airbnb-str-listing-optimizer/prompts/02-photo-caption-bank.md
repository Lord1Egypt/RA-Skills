# Prompt 2: Photo Caption Bank

**What it produces:** 15–20 photo captions (one per photo/space), each combining an emotional hook with a conversion-driving detail. Guests read these before booking — great captions close the deal.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a short-term rental conversion specialist. Photo captions are often overlooked — most hosts write "kitchen" or "master bedroom." Your captions tell a story that converts browsers into bookers.

Write a photo caption bank for this property:

**Property name / vibe:** [e.g., "The Desert Oasis" / sleek modern condo / rustic mountain retreat]
**Primary guest emotion to evoke:** [e.g., relaxation and luxury / adventure and fun / romance / family togetherness]
**Target guest type:** [e.g., couples / families / groups / business travelers / bachelorette parties]

**Photos to caption (list each):**
1. [e.g., Exterior / building entrance]
2. [e.g., Living room — main seating area]
3. [e.g., Kitchen — full kitchen with island]
4. [e.g., Primary bedroom — king bed]
5. [e.g., Primary bathroom — walk-in shower]
6. [e.g., Second bedroom — queen bed]
7. [e.g., Second bathroom]
8. [e.g., Balcony / patio]
9. [e.g., Pool area / hot tub]
10. [e.g., Dining area]
11. [e.g., Workspace / desk setup]
12. [e.g., Smart TV / entertainment setup]
13. [e.g., Welcome basket / coffee station]
14. [e.g., View from unit — city/mountain/ocean]
15. [e.g., Parking / garage]
[Add more as needed]

---

OUTPUT FORMAT:

For each photo, write:

**Photo [#] — [Subject]**
Caption: [15–25 words. Lead with an experience or feeling, end with a specific detail that proves the claim.]

---

RULES:
- Each caption is 15–25 words max. Airbnb displays only 2 lines.
- Start with an action or emotion ("Wake up to...", "Unwind in...", "Cook together...") — not a noun.
- Include one specific sensory or quantifiable detail per caption (thread count, sq ft, TV size, etc.) where natural.
- Never repeat the same opener across captions.
- Last caption (or whichever is best for a closing image): end with a soft call to action or forward-looking statement ("Your next trip starts here").
```

---

## Usage Notes

- Run this after Prompt 1. Your photos + captions work together as a visual narrative.
- Upload photos in order of the guest journey: exterior → living space → kitchen → bedrooms → bathrooms → amenities → views → neighborhood.
- For Airbnb: the first photo is your cover image. Its caption influences click-through rate from search results — make it your strongest one.
- Update captions seasonally if your property has seasonal appeal (pool season, ski season, holiday decor).
