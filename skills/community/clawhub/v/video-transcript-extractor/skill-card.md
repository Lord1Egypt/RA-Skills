## Description: <br>
Extracts text from video and audio links across platforms including TikTok, YouTube, Bilibili, and Douyin using subtitle retrieval, speech recognition, and optional AI correction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, content creators, market researchers, and developers use this skill to extract transcripts, timelines, and background music details from single or batch video or audio URLs for analysis, notes, and content workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Video URLs, subtitles, audio, metadata, and transcripts may be handled by external video, transcription, or AI services. <br>
Mitigation: Use only media you are comfortable processing through third-party services, and avoid private, confidential, regulated, or copyrighted media unless you have permission and have reviewed the services involved. <br>
Risk: Speech recognition and AI correction can introduce incorrect words, quotes, timestamps, or punctuation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important quotes and timestamps against the source media, prefer native subtitles when available, and review AI-corrected transcript sections before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Platform links, APIs, or long videos may fail or produce incomplete results. <br>
Mitigation: Retry failed extractions, provide subtitle files when available, and segment long videos before extraction. <br>
