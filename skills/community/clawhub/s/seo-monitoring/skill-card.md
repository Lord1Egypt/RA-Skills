## Description: <br>
Guides agents in building SEO monitoring outputs for indexing, traffic, keywords, backlinks, benchmarks, article tracking, penalty recovery, and work document management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
SEO practitioners, marketers, developers, and site operators use this skill to structure SEO monitoring reports, dashboards, benchmarks, and action plans from analytics and search data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Privacy-sensitive analytics identifiers may be mishandled when following GA4 User ID guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use only pseudonymous identifiers, avoid sending PII, confirm consent and disclosure requirements, and grant only the analytics access needed for the task. <br>
Risk: Search Console or analytics access may expose sensitive site performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Apply least-privilege access and review any generated monitoring plan before connecting dashboards or automation. <br>
