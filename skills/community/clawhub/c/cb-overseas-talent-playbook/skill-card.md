Risk: Employment, contractor, tax, payroll, immigration, compensation, and country-specific compliance guidance can be jurisdiction-specific or become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning support only and verify current local requirements with qualified legal, tax, payroll, immigration, or employment professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Market and cultural observations may overgeneralize local expectations or miss important context. <br>
Mitigation: Validate assumptions with local professionals, candidates, employees, customers, and current reliable market sources. <br>
