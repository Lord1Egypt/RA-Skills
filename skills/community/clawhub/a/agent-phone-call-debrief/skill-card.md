## Description: <br>
Debrief after an AI agent phone call by comparing the outcome to the goal, capturing commitments, flagging escalations, and producing follow-up tasks and CRM-ready notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ruti3](https://clawhub.ai/user/ruti3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer-service, booking, and operations users use this skill after inbound, outbound, or callback calls to turn a transcript, summary, or recap into a grounded debrief with commitments, risks, follow-up tasks, and a copy-paste CRM or ticket note. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad phone-call recap triggers may activate the skill when a user only wanted a simple summary. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated debrief, follow-up tasks, and CRM note before using them in customer records or sending them to others. <br>
Risk: Post-call summaries can contain uncertain transcript details, sensitive information, or commitments that were not clearly confirmed. <br>
Mitigation: Keep uncertain extractions marked for verification, avoid adding unsupported facts, and route medical, legal, financial, or compliance-sensitive calls to human review. <br>
