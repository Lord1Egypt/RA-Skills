## Description: <br>
Generate AI-illustrated comic-style educational guides from documentation, source code, or technical content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hongzhe4](https://clawhub.ai/user/hongzhe4) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, educators, and technical writers use this skill to turn documents, source code, or technical topics into comic-style learning materials with analysis notes, storyboards, image prompts, and PNG comic images. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Install-time supply-chain exposure from piping a remote script into a shell. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer ClawHub or manual installation; inspect any install script and destination paths before running it. <br>
Risk: Commercial outputs may resemble protected franchise characters or styles. <br>
Mitigation: Use original or properly licensed characters and visual styles for public or commercial releases. <br>
