## Description: <br>
Brand Video Maker is an AI tool for replacing logos, packaging, product shots, and brand elements inside existing videos without reshooting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[imwyvern](https://clawhub.ai/user/imwyvern) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams and content producers use this skill to plan brand element replacement in existing videos, including logos, packaging, product shots, text overlays, and localized campaign variants. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be used to alter video footage, brand assets, or advertising content without proper rights. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with source footage and brand assets the user owns or has permission to modify. <br>
Risk: Materially altered advertising or influencer content may mislead viewers if changes are not disclosed. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated videos and disclose materially altered commercial content where required. <br>
Risk: External AI video editing providers may process source footage or brand assets under their own privacy terms. <br>
Mitigation: Review provider privacy terms before sending sensitive footage, personal data, or confidential brand assets. <br>
