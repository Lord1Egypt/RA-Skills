## Description: <br>
Automates access to FT.com articles and produces structured bilingual English-Chinese summaries of selected Financial Times content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhouziyue233](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhouziyue233) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Readers, researchers, and professionals use this skill to select Financial Times articles they can access and receive concise bilingual reports covering the title, core opinion, arguments, and conclusion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Stored FT credentials and a persistent browser session can expose account or session data if reused or shared. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated FT account or explicitly approved session, avoid placing real passwords in SKILL.md, isolate the browser profile, and clear the session when finished. <br>
