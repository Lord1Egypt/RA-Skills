# 多角度展示 Prompt 模板 v1
# 需配合 source_image（产品实拍）+ img2img，prompt_strength ≈ 0.58
# 占位符 {product} = 产品名称

## front_view（正视图）
The same {product}, straight-on front view, eye-level camera, symmetrical composition, studio product photography, sharp focus on product details, e-commerce gallery shot

## side_view（侧视图）
The same {product}, pure side profile view, 90-degree lateral angle, clean studio lighting, product silhouette and thickness clearly visible

## top_view（俯视图）
The same {product}, overhead flat lay, top-down 90-degree bird's eye view, product centered on clean surface, even studio lighting

## back_view（背视图）
The same {product}, rear view showing back side, same studio setup and lighting as reference, product design consistency maintained

## three_quarter_view（45° 三维视角）
The same {product}, three-quarter angle view, 45-degree perspective showing depth and dimension, professional e-commerce product photography

## other_angles（其他角度）
The same {product}, alternative gallery angle, slightly rotated perspective (about 30 degrees), complementary product shot for listing carousel

## detail_closeup（细节特写）
The same {product}, macro close-up detail shot highlighting material texture, stitching, buttons or logo area, shallow depth of field, studio lighting

## img2img 一致性后缀（自动追加）
, maintain exact same product design, color, material, proportions and branding as reference image
