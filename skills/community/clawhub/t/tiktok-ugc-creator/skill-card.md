## Description: <br>
Hire TikTok users to create authentic user-generated content (UGC), testimonials, and brand storytelling videos that build trust and drive engagement through genuine customer voices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketers and agent operators use this skill to find TikTok UGC creators, draft paid campaign tasks, review creator submissions, and manage authentic testimonial or brand storytelling workflows through PingHuman. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill describes authenticated workflows that can create paid UGC campaigns. <br>
Mitigation: Use a scoped PingHuman token where available and require explicit human approval before creating, approving, rating, or funding any paid task. <br>
Risk: Campaign workflows may involve budgets, deadlines, products, and creator compensation. <br>
Mitigation: Set a campaign budget, deadline, and approval threshold before the agent posts or modifies tasks. <br>
Risk: Product fulfillment can require creator shipping addresses or other personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Treat creator contact and shipping details as personal data, minimize retention, and avoid placing them in prompts, logs, or unrelated tools. <br>
Risk: UGC output may be paid or sponsored content and may have reuse restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Require clear paid or sponsored content disclosure and verify usage rights before repurposing creator content. <br>
