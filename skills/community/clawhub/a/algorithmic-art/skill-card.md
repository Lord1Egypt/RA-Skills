## Description: <br>
Creates original algorithmic art with p5.js by first drafting a generative-art philosophy and then expressing it as seeded, parameterized interactive code. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and creative agents use this skill to turn a user prompt into a concise algorithmic-art philosophy and a self-contained p5.js generative artwork with seed controls and interactive parameters. <br>
