## Description: <br>
Website SEO Audit Optimizer analyzes HTML/CSS websites for SEO issues, generates audit reports, and helps implement metadata, structured data, sitemaps, and robots.txt guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yesong-hue](https://clawhub.ai/user/yesong-hue) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and site maintainers use this skill to audit static HTML/CSS websites, prioritize SEO fixes, generate sitemaps, and apply search and social metadata improvements before publishing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated metadata, robots.txt directives, or sitemap entries can affect how search engines crawl and present a website. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated meta tags, robots.txt, and sitemap changes before publishing them. <br>
Risk: Applying SEO changes directly to a live production site can introduce incorrect page metadata or crawl restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before deploying generated SEO assets or edits to production. <br>
