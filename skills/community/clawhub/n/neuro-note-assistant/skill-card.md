## Use Case: <br>
Neurology clinicians, outpatient nurses, and medical learners use this skill to turn raw clinic note text into structured note sections and draft diagnosis, examination, and treatment-plan suggestions for qualified clinical review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medical records may contain patient-identifiable or sensitive health information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in an authorized privacy environment and de-identify patient records where possible. <br>
Risk: Diagnosis or treatment suggestions may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated suggestions as drafting support and require review by a qualified clinician before clinical use. <br>
