## Description: <br>
Take a tolerance break or quit cannabis with streak tracking and craving support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to start a cannabis tolerance break or quit plan, track progress, handle cravings, and review milestone-based expectations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Streaks, goals, and craving notes can reveal sensitive cannabis-use information when saved locally. <br>
Mitigation: Record only details the user is comfortable storing and review the OpenClaw environment's controls for viewing or deleting saved data. <br>
Risk: A general emotional-support request could accidentally activate cannabis-specific guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify whether the user wants cannabis break or quitting support before applying this skill. <br>
