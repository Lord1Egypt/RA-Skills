This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to ask an agent for Paul Graham-inspired perspective-taking, critique, and decision framing for entrepreneurship, writing, products, and personal choices. The skill should be treated as roleplay based on public material, not as genuine attribution or professional advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake persona-style output for genuine Paul Graham attribution. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as roleplay based on public material and keep the first-use transparency caveat intact. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on framework-based advice for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as a thinking aid only and require qualified review for legal, medical, financial, or other professional decisions. <br>
Risk: The persona may continue when the user wants normal assistant behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Honor explicit exit requests such as exiting the role or switching back to normal mode. <br>
