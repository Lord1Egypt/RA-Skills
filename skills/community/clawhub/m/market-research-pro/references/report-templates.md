# Market Research Report Templates

## Template A: Executive Summary (1-Pager)

```markdown
# [Market Name] Market Research — Executive Summary
**Date**: | **Prepared by**: | **Scope**: [Geography, Segment]

## Market Snapshot
- Market size (current): $X (Source, Year)
- Growth rate (CAGR): X% (20XX–20XX)
- Key drivers: [1], [2], [3]
- Key risks: [1], [2]

## Opportunity Assessment
- TAM: $X | SAM: $X | SOM (Year 3): $X
- Whitespace: [describe the gap]
- Recommended entry point: [segment / use case]

## Competitive Summary
- Market leaders: [Co A (est. X% share)], [Co B], [Co C]
- Notable challengers: [Co D], [Co E]
- Differentiation opportunity: [brief description]

## Verdict
[2–3 sentences on whether to pursue, with key conditions]
```

## Template C: Investor-Ready Market Slide (Pitch Deck)

Use for slide decks — distill to 5 key numbers:

```
Slide title: "$X Billion Market Growing at X% CAGR"

① Total Addressable Market (TAM): $XB
   Source: [Research firm, Year]

② Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): $XB
   [Our target segment description]

③ Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM): $XM by Year 3
   [Based on X% market share, Y conversion rate]

④ Market Tailwind: [1 sentence on macro driver]
   Example: "Remote work adoption driving 2x demand for async tools"

⑤ Why Now: [1 sentence on timing catalyst]
   Example: "New regulation effective Jan 2026 creates mandatory compliance need"
```

## Checklist: Before Submitting Research

- [ ] All market size figures have sources + year
- [ ] Assumptions are clearly labeled [ASSUMPTION]
- [ ] TAM/SAM/SOM are logically consistent
- [ ] Competitive landscape includes ≥ 3 direct competitors
- [ ] PESTEL covers at least 3 relevant dimensions
- [ ] Customer segments have estimated sizes
- [ ] Report includes at least 3 actionable recommendations
- [ ] Data is ≤ 2 years old (or noted as older)
- [ ] Executive summary can stand alone (≤ 200 words)
