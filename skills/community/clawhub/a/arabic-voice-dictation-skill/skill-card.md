## Description: <br>
Capture Arabic voice notes, transcribe them safely, clean dialect/MSA text, preserve meaning, and prepare bilingual summaries or action items. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abdullah944](https://clawhub.ai/user/abdullah944) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Arabic-speaking users and agents use this skill to process authorized Arabic or mixed Arabic/English voice notes into cleaned transcripts, summaries, action lists, message drafts, social captions, reports, or bilingual handoff copy. It is optimized for Gulf/Kuwaiti dialect handling, MSA cleanup, RTL formatting, and privacy-aware summarization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Unauthorized or sensitive audio could expose private personal or business information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only audio or notes the user owns or is authorized to process, avoid secrets and identity documents, and minimize private details in outputs. <br>
Risk: Cleaned transcripts or drafts can misstate unclear speech, names, numbers, dates, or commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Mark unclear words, preserve source facts, review RTL punctuation and key details, and require explicit approval before sending, publishing, or uploading prepared text. <br>
