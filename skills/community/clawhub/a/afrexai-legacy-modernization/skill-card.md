## Description: <br>
Comprehensive legacy system modernization from assessment and strategy to monolith decomposition and cloud migration for any tech stack and scale. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineering leaders, and modernization teams use this skill to assess legacy systems, select a modernization strategy, and produce planning artifacts such as technical debt inventories, migration sequences, testing plans, risk registers, and status reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may activate this skill on general modernization requests when a user has multiple similar planning skills installed. <br>
Mitigation: Review the trigger wording and confirm the selected skill before relying on its output. <br>
Risk: Modernization guidance may be mistaken for an automated migration or system-changing tool. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as planning guidance and require human engineering review before making system changes. <br>
