## Description: <br>
Reviews UI details, screenshots, or descriptions against Nielsen Norman's 10 usability heuristics and produces a prioritized UX evaluation report. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[PHY041](https://clawhub.ai/user/PHY041) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product designers, product managers, and developers use this skill to evaluate screens, flows, screenshots, or UI descriptions against Nielsen Norman usability heuristics and prioritize UX fixes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided screenshots, UI details, or product descriptions may include sensitive product or customer information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary UI evidence and remove sensitive details before using the skill. <br>
Risk: UX recommendations may be incomplete or need product-context review before implementation. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with the relevant product, design, or engineering owners before applying changes. <br>
