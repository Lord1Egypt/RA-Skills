## Description: <br>
Daily Inspiration sends twice-daily Feishu messages with curated spiritual practice, I Ching, Chinese medicine, Daoist philosophy, and Buddhist meditation content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Gabriel-ZZ](https://clawhub.ai/user/Gabriel-ZZ) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to receive scheduled Feishu inspiration messages about spiritual practice, traditional Chinese medicine, Daoist thought, I Ching, and Buddhist meditation. The skill chooses morning and evening themes and formats the selected content for message delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Scheduled Feishu messages may be sent to an unintended recipient or channel. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the Feishu recipient and twice-daily schedule before installation, test in a low-risk channel, and keep a clear way to pause or remove the scheduled task. <br>
