## Description: <br>
Generates and optimizes Douyin short-video marketing scripts using brand memory, user preferences, image-extracted brand briefs, compliance rules, and script formula templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, creators, and brand teams use this skill to draft, optimize, and rewrite Douyin seeding scripts for consumer products while applying stored brand preferences and compliance constraints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may retain brand briefs, product details, preferences, and image-extracted information across conversations without clear opt-in or deletion controls. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential marketing plans, customer data, unreleased product material, or personal information unless retention controls are available and acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated advertising scripts may include sensitive claims or prohibited promotional language if prompts conflict with the compliance guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review scripts against the included compliance rules and applicable platform, advertising, food, skincare, health, and product-category requirements before publication. <br>
