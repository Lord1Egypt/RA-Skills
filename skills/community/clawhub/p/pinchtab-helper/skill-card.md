## Description: <br>
Pinchtab Helper lets an agent control a local browser through PinchTab for navigation, page snapshots, clicking, form filling, form submission, and screenshots. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[adminlove520](https://clawhub.ai/user/adminlove520) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users can use this skill when they intentionally want an agent to operate a browser with PinchTab, including opening pages, collecting snapshots, interacting with elements, entering text, submitting forms, and taking screenshots. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Live browser control can let an agent navigate, click, type, or submit data with unclear confirmation boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when browser control is intended, review actions before submitting forms or changing accounts, and avoid entering sensitive data unless the publisher is trusted. <br>
Risk: Browser automation may expose active sessions or personal data from a normal browsing profile. <br>
Mitigation: Use a separate browser profile or test accounts where possible, and revoke access or credentials after use. <br>
