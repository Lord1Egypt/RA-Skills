## Description: <br>
Suggest optimal academic venues for paper submission based on paper content, novelty level, and author's goals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shaopanguo](https://clawhub.ai/user/shaopanguo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers and authors use this skill to compare publication venues for academic papers from a draft, abstract, or contribution summary. It returns ranked journal or conference recommendations with fit, risk, review-speed, and positioning guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Unpublished drafts may contain confidential or sensitive research content. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential unpublished drafts unless the user is comfortable with the platform and model handling that content. <br>
Risk: Venue statistics, deadlines, scope, and review timelines may be outdated or approximate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify venue details against official venue pages before deciding where to submit. <br>
