## Description: <br>
Human-readable cron job templates for OpenClaw, with pre-built schedules for daily reports, hourly checks, weekly summaries, and custom intervals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TheShadowRose](https://clawhub.ai/user/TheShadowRose) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use Cron Forge to choose plain-English cron schedules and adapt OpenClaw cron commands for recurring automation tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may create recurring OpenClaw jobs from the example commands without fully reviewing the schedule or prompt. <br>
Mitigation: Review each cron expression and prompt before running commands, use narrow permissions for recurring jobs, prefer isolated sessions where appropriate, and remove jobs that are no longer needed. <br>
