## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated product descriptions may contain inaccurate claims, unsupported benefits, or marketplace-noncompliant wording. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy for factual accuracy, compliant claims, and platform-specific rules before publishing listings. <br>
Risk: The artifact references optional CLI helpers, but no scripts were included in the reviewed artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Treat any separately downloaded helper scripts as unreviewed until they are inspected and scanned. <br>
