## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Copy-pasted examples may use HTTP or private-IP service endpoints. <br>
Mitigation: Replace sample endpoints with approved HTTPS services before using generated code. <br>
Risk: Marker and popup examples may include raw HTML. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer safe text or DOM APIs, and sanitize any user-provided HTML before rendering. <br>
Risk: Telemetry or precise location features may affect privacy. <br>
Mitigation: Govern telemetry explicitly and enable precise location tracking only with clear user consent and a visible stop control. <br>
