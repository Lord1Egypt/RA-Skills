## Description: <br>
Generates gentle, restrained Moon Lovers-style romantic chat replies for fictional character roleplay using character profiles and soft 白月光 tone guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phoenixlucky](https://clawhub.ai/user/phoenixlucky) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, roleplay writers, and agent operators use this skill to draft, rewrite, or analyze romantic chat replies in a gentle 白月光 style. It is best suited for fictional character roleplay, ambiguous pre-relationship dialogue, tone calibration, and concise message variants. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Romantic roleplay and reply-writing outputs may be inappropriate for some deployments or relationship contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Install and enable the skill only for intended fictional roleplay or consensual message-writing use cases, and review generated replies before sending. <br>
Risk: Some bundled reference profiles may not fit every audience or policy posture. <br>
Mitigation: Review the included profile references before deployment, especially where youth-coded fictional character styles or persona self-references would be unsuitable. <br>
Risk: The skill may recommend other skills or services when a request falls outside romantic chat reply writing. <br>
Mitigation: Keep any additional skill or service installation under explicit user or operator control. <br>
