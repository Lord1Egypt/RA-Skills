## Description: <br>
从代码或技术方案出发，撰写符合 CNIPA 规范的中国专利申请文件，支持代码特征提取、权利要求布局以及发明专利和实用新型草稿生成。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, inventors, and patent drafting teams use this skill to turn source code or technical solution descriptions into CNIPA-style Chinese patent application drafts. It helps identify potentially patentable technical features, structure claims, draft the specification, and produce review checklists for qualified legal review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated patent drafts may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for direct filing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a reference draft only and require review by a qualified patent agent or attorney before filing. <br>
Risk: Patent drafting may involve sensitive, undisclosed invention details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential invention details in untrusted environments and follow the user's confidentiality and legal-review process. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the skill to be invoked outside Chinese patent drafting work. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly for Chinese patent drafting tasks and verify that its CNIPA-oriented guidance fits the intended jurisdiction. <br>
