## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and operators use this skill to manage FoxReach outreach data and workflows, including lead records, campaign setup, email sequences, inbox handling, and analytics review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: API keys can grant access to live FoxReach outreach data and actions. <br>
Mitigation: Use environment-based secrets, avoid storing real keys in saved snippets, and limit key access to trusted agent sessions. <br>
Risk: The skill can help perform live outreach operations such as deletes, bulk changes, email-account removal, and campaign starts. <br>
Mitigation: Require an explicit user preview and confirmation before destructive, bulk, account-removal, or campaign-start actions. <br>
