Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated citations, marketing claims, or platform-rule-sensitive statements may be inaccurate or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review claims, citations, banned terms, and platform rules before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill could draft content for the wrong platform, tone, or word count if the request is underspecified. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm topic, target platform, style, word count, and image needs before generation. <br>
Risk: User-facing drafts could imply absolute claims or fake personal experiences. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid absolute claims and require verifiable support for reviews, experiences, and data-backed statements. <br>
