## Description: <br>
Track business income and expenses in structured CSV format. Categorize transactions, monitor cash flow, generate P&L summaries, and spot spending trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers, solo founders, and small business owners use this skill to log transactions, summarize profit and loss, monitor cash flow, analyze expenses, and prepare tax-oriented summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive business transaction details while tracking finances. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the transaction details needed for the task, avoid bank passwords, account tokens, and unnecessary personal information, and review generated tax estimates before relying on them. <br>
