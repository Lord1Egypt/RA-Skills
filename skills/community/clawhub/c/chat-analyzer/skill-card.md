## Description: <br>
Chat Analyzer is an AI conversation analysis assistant that reviews message flow, tone, reciprocity, emotional shifts, effort imbalance, and conversation health across relationships, work chats, and group threads. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[imwyvern](https://clawhub.ai/user/imwyvern) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to analyze single messages or conversation threads for tone, intent, reciprocity, turning points, confidence, and suggested response direction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive private conversations for analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing highly sensitive chats unless needed, and remove unnecessary personal or identifying details before analysis. <br>
Risk: The skill may infer intent or relationship dynamics from limited text. <br>
Mitigation: Treat intent readings as possibilities rather than facts, and use the provided confidence level to avoid over-reading ambiguous messages. <br>
Risk: The artifact links to an external service for conversation tracking with memory. <br>
Mitigation: Review the external service's privacy practices before using memory or tracking features. <br>
