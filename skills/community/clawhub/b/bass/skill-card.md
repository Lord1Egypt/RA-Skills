## Use Case: <br>
External users and musicians use this skill for bass practice coaching, groove development, technique troubleshooting, and local practice progress tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may retain practice notes about songs, sessions, techniques, groove exercises, and goals in a local ~/bass/ folder. <br>
Mitigation: Review or delete the ~/bass/ folder if those records should not be retained. <br>
