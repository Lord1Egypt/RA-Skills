## Description: <br>
Challenges a user's assumptions by extracting core decision anchors, inverting one of them, and returning probing questions for deeper reflection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tuobadaidai](https://clawhub.ai/user/tuobadaidai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, strategists, and decision makers can use this skill before non-urgent planning decisions to surface hidden assumptions, generate a coherent counter-model, and prompt human judgment before proceeding. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intentionally confrontational and may create discomfort or slow decision making. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for non-urgent decisions and skip it when assumption-challenging feedback is not wanted. <br>
Risk: Repeated inversion can contribute to analysis paralysis. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as a bounded reflection aid and keep final decisions under human judgment. <br>
