## Description: <br>
Provides manual prompts for triggering a meta-cognition system's knowledge collection, deep analysis, knowledge-base queries, and skill integration workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Individuals, teams, and enterprise users use this skill to manually prompt a separate meta-cognition workflow for immediate information gathering, topic analysis, knowledge-base checks, and periodic insight integration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Manual runs may duplicate scheduled Cron/meta-cognition jobs or operate on sensitive topics without clear data boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm configured data sources, storage location, and schedule overlap before using the skill with sensitive topics. <br>
Risk: The artifact describes a manual wrapper and contains placeholder code, so users may assume more automation exists than is implemented. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as prompt-level guidance unless the separate meta-cognition system is installed and verified. <br>
