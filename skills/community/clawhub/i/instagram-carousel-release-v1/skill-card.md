## Description: <br>
Generate export-ready Instagram carousel HTML with fresh data, safe 4:5 slide layouts, and simple language. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[blablainvest](https://clawhub.ai/user/blablainvest) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and social media teams use this skill to turn a topic and brand inputs into a self-contained Instagram carousel with export-ready slides, current statistics, and clear copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to research current statistics, and cited numbers may be incorrect or stale if not checked before publishing. <br>
Mitigation: Review every cited statistic, source name, and year before posting the carousel. <br>
Risk: The skill defaults to simple Russian copy, which may not match every intended audience. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the target language and tone during the kickoff questions when a different audience is required. <br>
Risk: Generated HTML may need visual review to confirm slide overflow, spacing, and export readiness. <br>
Mitigation: Preview each slide at the target 4:5 size and adjust dense content before exporting images. <br>
