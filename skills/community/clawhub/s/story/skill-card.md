## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to keep terminal-based story drafts, edits, optimization notes, schedules, and related content activity organized locally. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Story drafts, prompts, schedules, edits, and exported files are retained locally under ~/.local/share/story and may appear in recent activity, search, or export output. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets or highly sensitive unpublished material unless local retention is acceptable, and delete ~/.local/share/story to clear stored history. <br>
