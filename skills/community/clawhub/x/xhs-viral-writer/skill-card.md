## Description: <br>
Generates Xiaohongshu-style post drafts with titles, body copy, tags, and image suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[duoduoks](https://clawhub.ai/user/duoduoks) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and agents use this skill to draft Xiaohongshu-style promotional, review, or tutorial notes from a topic or product description. It helps produce a structured draft that can be reviewed and edited before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided topics and writing details are sent to DeepSeek for generation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive, confidential, regulated, or client-proprietary material unless external AI processing is approved. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on a local DeepSeek API key. <br>
Mitigation: Store the key in an approved environment or local configuration, restrict access, and rotate it if exposed. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may be inaccurate, misleading, or unsuitable for a specific brand or compliance context. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit the draft before publishing or using it in customer-facing material. <br>
