Risk: Crypto wallet guidance can become outdated or lead to mistaken actions if used without verification. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify wallet sites, addresses, fees, network details, and platform instructions before acting. <br>
Risk: Users may expose seed phrases, private keys, wallet passwords, or signing prompts while applying wallet guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Never share seed phrases, private keys, wallet passwords, or signing prompts with any tool or agent. <br>
