## Description: <br>
Calculate and compare total selling fees across major e-commerce marketplaces (Amazon, eBay, Etsy, Walmart, Shopify) including referral fees, fulfillment costs, subscription tiers, and hidden charges to optimize channel profitability. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, marketplace operators, and commerce analysts use this skill to estimate per-channel selling costs, compare net profit, and choose marketplace channels for a product or SKU. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The server security evidence marks this release as suspicious because SKILL.md contains an unexplained binary tail. <br>
Mitigation: Review the artifact before installation or request a clean republish of SKILL.md without the binary tail. <br>

Mitigation: Verify current rates in each marketplace seller portal before relying on the calculation. <br>
Risk: Fee analysis may involve sensitive supplier invoices, COGS, and private sales-volume data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive cost or supplier data unless it is necessary for the calculation and approved for the agent environment. <br>
