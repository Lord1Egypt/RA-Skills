## Description: <br>
Paste a paper abstract or methods section to get matched AI agent tools that can reproduce the analysis by mapping methodology keywords to curated Claw4Science science agents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnnychen1113](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnnychen1113) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, developers, and science-agent users use this skill to map paper methods, abstracts, or analysis pipelines to relevant AI agents for reproducing or extending scientific analyses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recommended third-party agents may have their own permissions and data-handling practices. <br>
Mitigation: Review each recommended agent before installing or running it. <br>
Risk: Pasted research methods may include sensitive patient, proprietary, or unpublished research details. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in agent environments approved for that data and redact sensitive details when possible. <br>
