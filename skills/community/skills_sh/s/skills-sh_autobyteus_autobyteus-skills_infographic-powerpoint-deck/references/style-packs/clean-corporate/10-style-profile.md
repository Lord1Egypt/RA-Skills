Style Pack: clean-corporate (clean corporate)
- Favor split, framework, comparison, and didactic board layouts; title or transition slides may use restrained full-bleed overlay when the text is short.
- Use a white or light gray base with brand-blue or cyan accents, and keep body text dark and high-contrast.
- Typography attitude: disciplined corporate sans hierarchy with strong section headers, crisp bullets, and restrained microcopy.
- Surface/material cues: matte white panels, low-reflection glass, clean neutral board fields, and precise divider rules.
- Composition attitude: clean and disciplined, with structure often carried by alignment, dividers, and repeated modules. On structured slides, light panels or cards are acceptable when they improve clarity, but they are not mandatory if softer grouping reads better. On full-bleed slides, keep the text direct and restrained with no cinematic caption box.
- Keep the lighting even and avoid dramatic volumetric effects.
- Favor minimal scenes or geometric abstraction over heavy cinematic storytelling.
