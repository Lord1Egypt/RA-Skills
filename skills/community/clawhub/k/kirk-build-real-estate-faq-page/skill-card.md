This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External real estate teams, brokers, and listing marketers use this skill to draft and refine customer-facing FAQ answers for property marketing and client education. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ content may include inaccurate or outdated real estate information. <br>
Mitigation: Review all FAQ answers for factual accuracy before publication. <br>
Risk: Generated content may conflict with local real estate law or fair-housing requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified reviewers check jurisdiction-specific legal and fair-housing compliance before external use. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include sensitive client or transaction details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive personal, client, or transaction data unless the agent and search settings are approved for that data. <br>
