## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and growth teams use this skill to diagnose paid traffic conversion funnels, identify stage-level bottlenecks, and plan prioritized experiments tied to ROAS or CPA goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Funnel diagnostics may require marketing, checkout, or form data that can contain personal identifiers or unnecessary raw event details. <br>
Mitigation: Use aggregated funnel metrics where possible and remove personal identifiers before sharing inputs with an agent. <br>
Risk: Optimization recommendations can be misleading when stage definitions are inconsistent or source data is sparse. <br>
Mitigation: Normalize funnel definitions first and run diagnostic experiments before making major redesigns. <br>
