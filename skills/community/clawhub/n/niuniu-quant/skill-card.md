## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use the skill to explore stock data query flows, simple backtest examples, stock screening output, and trading-signal style reports. Outputs should be treated as simulated examples rather than investment advice or live market analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill presents hard-coded stock data and buy signals in a professional quant-trading format, which could mislead users making financial decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat all outputs as simulated examples, not investment advice or live market analysis; verify market data and analysis independently before making any financial decision. <br>
Risk: The skill references package installs, public URLs, and market-data credentials for experimentation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify packages and URLs independently, and use only limited-purpose market-data credentials if experimenting with the skill. <br>
