## Description: <br>
Helps agents read A-share financial reports by interpreting income statements, balance sheets, and cash-flow statements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lanyasheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/lanyasheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, investors, and research agents use this skill to fetch A-share report summaries through a trusted AKShare helper and structure earnings reviews across profitability, balance-sheet health, cash flow, valuation, and risks. Outputs are research support and not investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill points to a separate akshare-finance helper script that is not bundled with this artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the helper script on the user's machine comes from a trusted source before running the example commands. <br>
Risk: Financial analysis output may be mistaken for investment advice or relied on without verification. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as research support, validate source data and assumptions, and avoid using it as the sole basis for trading decisions. <br>
