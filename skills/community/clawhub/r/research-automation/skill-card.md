## Description: <br>
Automated web research for peptides, biohacking protocols, longevity science, and trending health topics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[biohackerrrrrr](https://clawhub.ai/user/biohackerrrrrr) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content teams use this skill to run recurring or on-demand web research on health-related topics, curate findings, and generate markdown notes and content angles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may surface health, dosing, or protocol claims from web research that are incomplete or medically inappropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify health-related claims against original medical or scientific sources before using them for decisions or publication. <br>
Risk: Heartbeat scheduling can trigger recurring searches and markdown file creation in the workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Enable scheduled runs only when automatic research and file creation are intended, and review generated files before reuse. <br>
