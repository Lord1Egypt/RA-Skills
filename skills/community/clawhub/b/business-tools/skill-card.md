## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Email automation workflows could send unreviewed messages to real customer lists. <br>
Mitigation: Keep email workflows in draft or sandbox mode until reviewed, and require explicit approval before sending to live recipients. <br>
Risk: Customer research or scraping workflows could collect data from sources without appropriate permission or lawful basis. <br>
Mitigation: Collect data only from sources where the user has permission and a lawful reason to process it. <br>
Risk: Related tools may connect to external business accounts or APIs. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm which accounts each workflow connects to before installation or use, and limit credentials to the minimum required access. <br>
