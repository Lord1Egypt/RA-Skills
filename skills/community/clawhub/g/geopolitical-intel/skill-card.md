## Description: <br>
Provides geopolitical risk and global governance intelligence workflows using public sources for conflict monitoring, sanctions tracking, governance assessment, military comparison, flashpoint analysis, cyber threats, and scenario planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, policy teams, risk teams, and compliance users can use this skill to structure geopolitical assessments, sanctions checks, flashpoint monitoring, military comparisons, and scenario planning from public source material. It is intended to produce source-cited analytical guidance and tables, not final legal, investment, or compliance determinations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sanctions, conflict, cyber threat, and risk-scoring outputs may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable as the sole basis for legal, compliance, safety, or business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important conclusions against official sources and seek qualified legal or compliance advice before acting on sanctions-related or high-impact outputs. <br>
Risk: Scenario probabilities and geopolitical scorecards can imply more precision than the underlying public evidence supports. <br>
Mitigation: Use source dates, uncertainty ranges, and multi-source comparison, and clearly distinguish documented facts from assessed probability or consensus views. <br>
