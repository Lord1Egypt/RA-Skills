营销引流工具包通过分析热点在微博或 X 上自动发文，支持热点发现、内容生成、浏览器发布和效果分析。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operators and creators can use this skill to identify X or Weibo trends, draft promotional posts, publish them through a browser workflow, and review posting outcomes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can automatically publish to X or Weibo on a schedule, which may post content without enough human review. <br>
Mitigation: Use draft-only mode or require explicit approval for each post unless unattended publishing is deliberately enabled. <br>
Risk: Frequent or repeated posting can trigger platform limits or account enforcement. <br>
Mitigation: Define platform limits, rate limits, account-risk tolerance, rollback expectations, and a stop procedure before use. <br>
