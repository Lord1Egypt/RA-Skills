This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Developers and optimization practitioners use this skill to turn final Python solutions from FaMou or related evolutionary optimization work into locally generated visual HTML pages for review and presentation. <br>

Global <br>

Risk: Generated visualization pages load third-party scripts and fonts when opened. <br>
Mitigation: For sensitive solution data or offline use, replace CDN scripts and fonts with trusted local copies before opening the generated HTML. <br>
