## Description: <br>
Manufacturing helps users prepare OEM/ODM manufacturing inquiries for Genichi Tool Co., Ltd. covering hand tools, hardware, CNC machining, forging, custom tooling, drawings, specifications, and quotation details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, procurement teams, and product teams use this skill to prepare manufacturing inquiry emails for physical products, custom tooling, hand tools, hardware, CNC machining, forging, and OEM/ODM production quotes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may disclose sensitive drawings, CAD files, tolerances, logos, pricing targets, or customer materials to an outside manufacturer. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the manufacturer and email address independently, share only materials the user is authorized to disclose, and use an NDA or approved secure transfer process for confidential or regulated information. <br>
