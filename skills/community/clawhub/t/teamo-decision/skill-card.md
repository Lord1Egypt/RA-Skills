## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may delegate to external research, data-analysis, knowledge-base, and work-log tools that could process sensitive business or private content. <br>
Mitigation: Verify tool permissions, retention behavior, and data-handling boundaries before using the skill with sensitive material. <br>
Risk: The skill includes broad delegation behavior and weak transparency around its own workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review delegated outputs and require human approval before relying on recommendations for business, financial, or operational decisions. <br>
Risk: The security verdict is suspicious due to overreaching secrecy and operational behavior, even though no malicious findings were reported. <br>
Mitigation: Scan and review the skill before deployment, and limit use to environments where its delegation behavior is acceptable. <br>
