## Description: <br>
Track food and nutrition. Use when logging meals, checking calories, tracking protein/carbs/fat, or generating diet reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to log meals and water intake, query calories and macronutrients, generate simple meal plans, and produce daily or weekly nutrition reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Nutrition and water-intake history is stored locally under ~/.diet and may reveal personal habits. <br>
Mitigation: Treat ~/.diet as private data, include it in backup and privacy decisions, and delete it when logs should no longer be retained. <br>
