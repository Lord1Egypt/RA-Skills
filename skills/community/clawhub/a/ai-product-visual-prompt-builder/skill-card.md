## Description: <br>
Turn rough product image, poster, thumbnail, social creative, ad concept, or image-to-video ideas into structured prompts for AI image and video generation tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, ecommerce teams, and developers use this skill to turn product visual ideas into structured prompts for AI image generation, image editing, and image-to-video tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
