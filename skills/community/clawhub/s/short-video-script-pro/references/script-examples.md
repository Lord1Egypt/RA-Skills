## Script Timing Guide

### Word Count by Duration
- **15 seconds**: 35–40 words
- **30 seconds**: 70–80 words  
- **60 seconds**: 140–160 words
- **90 seconds**: 210–240 words

### Pacing Rules
- Speak at 140–150 words per minute
- Leave 2–3 seconds total for pauses/emphasis
- Each "beat" (hook/highlight/core/CTA) needs transition time
- Factor in text overlay reading time (add 1–2 seconds per key phrase)

### Visual Cuts
- Change visual every 3–5 seconds minimum
- Zoom in/out counts as a cut
- Text appearing/disappearing counts as a cut
- Hand gestures and movement help retention between cuts
