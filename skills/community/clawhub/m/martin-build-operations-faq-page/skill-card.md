## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common operations questions. Use when working on FAQ content for operators, agencies, business teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Operators, agencies, and business teams use this skill to draft and refine customer-facing FAQ answers for business operations topics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer-facing FAQ answers may contain inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated operational guidance, especially when informed by web search. <br>
Mitigation: Review and fact-check generated answers before publishing them to customers. <br>
Risk: Drafting operations FAQs can involve confidential business details. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only approved business context and confirm agent and search settings before sharing sensitive information. <br>
