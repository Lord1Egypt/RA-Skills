## Description: <br>
Create a polished explainer handout with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for a pediatric dental clinic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Pediatric dentists and dental office managers use this skill to draft client education handouts with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for families. Patient-facing clinical content should be reviewed by qualified staff before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requests broad Bash and Read access that the handout-generation purpose does not explain. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, grant permissions only if acceptable for the environment, and ask the publisher to remove or document narrower permission use. <br>
Risk: Generated patient education content may include incorrect or regulated medical claims. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified clinic staff review all patient-facing content before sending or relying on it. <br>
