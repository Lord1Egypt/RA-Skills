## Description: <br>
Analyze competitor product listings, pricing strategies, and promotional tactics to identify gaps and opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators and marketplace sellers use this skill to turn public competitor observations into structured intelligence reports and prioritized actions for listing, pricing, reviews, promotions, search visibility, and brand positioning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor research may expose confidential margins, internal analytics, or private strategy details if the user provides them. <br>
Mitigation: Use public competitor observations where possible, and provide sensitive internal figures only when the user is comfortable including them in the analysis. <br>
Risk: Stale, estimated, or fabricated marketplace data can lead to misleading competitive conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Timestamp every data point, label evidence as observed, inferred, or estimated, flag data older than 7 days, and avoid inventing sales velocity, conversion rate, ad spend, BSR, or price-history claims. <br>
Risk: Pricing recommendations can breach margin constraints or provoke unwanted competitive responses. <br>
Mitigation: Provide a margin floor before analysis and review pricing actions against margin impact, effort, urgency, and likely competitor response before acting. <br>
