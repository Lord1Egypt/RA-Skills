## Description: <br>
Infj Coach helps users who identify as INFJ work through growth, career, study, focus, and analysis-paralysis questions using reflective coaching or direct action plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees who identify as INFJ use this skill for growth, career, learning, and self-management coaching. The skill supports both reflective question-led coaching and direct, concrete guidance when the user asks for action steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The package mixes INFJ and INTP scope across documentation and reference files, which can produce inconsistent targeting or advice. <br>
Mitigation: Review the package before installing and align README, keywords, and reference material to the intended INFJ scope, or make any cross-type behavior explicit. <br>
Risk: The skill describes retaining coaching records that may include sensitive personal, career, or emotional context without clear user controls. <br>
Mitigation: Make any memory or saved coaching records explicit, opt-in, reviewable, and deletable before deployment. <br>
Risk: Users may treat growth or emotional coaching as professional mental health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the artifact boundary that the skill does not replace professional counseling and direct severe mental health concerns to qualified professionals. <br>
