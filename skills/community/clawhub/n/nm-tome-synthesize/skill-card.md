## Description: <br>
Merges, dedupes, ranks, and formats research findings into a report after research agents return results from multiple channels. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[athola](https://clawhub.ai/user/athola) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and research agents use this skill after a multi-channel research session to merge findings, rank them, group them by theme, and produce a final report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger words such as merge, rank, format, and report may activate the skill during unrelated requests. <br>
Mitigation: Tighten or disable generic triggers in environments where unrelated requests commonly use those terms. <br>
