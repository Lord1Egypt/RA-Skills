## Description: <br>
Helps users create or update a reusable product marketing context document for products, services, or hybrid offerings across B2B and B2C scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mariokarras](https://clawhub.ai/user/mariokarras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing practitioners, founders, consultants, and agents use this skill to establish shared offering, audience, positioning, persona, objection, proof, and brand-voice context before downstream marketing tasks. It can auto-draft from repository materials or guide a conversational intake, then centralizes the result in `.agents/product-marketing-context.md`. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generated marketing context may contain confidential strategy, customer details, credentials, or private internal notes that other marketing skills can read and reuse. <br>
Mitigation: Review `.agents/product-marketing-context.md` before relying on it, and do not store sensitive information there unless it is appropriate for reuse by other marketing skills. <br>
