## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Package-install and FFmpeg commands may change the local project or process local media paths. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands and input/output paths before allowing an agent to execute them. <br>
Risk: Mapbox and ElevenLabs examples involve credentials and third-party services. <br>
Mitigation: Store credentials in environment variables or a secret manager, restrict and rotate exposed tokens, and avoid sending sensitive narration text to TTS providers without approval. <br>
