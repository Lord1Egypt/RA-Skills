# Prompt 1: Program Architecture & Commission Structure

## Purpose
Design your complete affiliate/partner program structure before recruiting anyone. This prompt outputs a program blueprint that prevents the #1 reason programs fail: undefined structure that creates partner confusion and commission disputes.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a partner marketing strategist who has built affiliate programs for B2B SaaS, e-commerce, and digital products. Design a complete affiliate program architecture for the following business.

**Business Details:**
- Company name: [NAME]
- Product/service: [DESCRIBE IN 1–2 SENTENCES]
- Price point: [MONTHLY/ONE-TIME PRICE]
- Customer LTV (average): [$ OR "unknown"]
- Customer acquisition cost (current): [$ OR "unknown"]
- Target customer: [ICP — who buys this?]
- Affiliate recruitment goal: [e.g., 50 affiliates in 90 days]
- Platform for tracking (if chosen): [e.g., Rewardful, Impact, Tapfiliate, or "TBD"]

**Generate the following:**

## 1. Program Type Recommendation
Recommend one of: CPA (cost per acquisition), RevShare (% of revenue), Hybrid (upfront + recurring), or Tiered (performance-based escalation). Explain why this model fits the business based on price point and LTV.

## 2. Commission Structure
Using this formula: recommended_commission = MIN(LTV × 0.20, price × 1.5), output:
- Base commission rate (% or flat $)
- Recurring vs. one-time payout logic
- Minimum payout threshold
- Payout frequency (monthly recommended)
- Cookie window recommendation (7 / 30 / 90 days — explain your choice based on sales cycle)

## 3. Three-Tier Partner System
Design three levels with:
| Tier | Name | Threshold to qualify | Commission rate | Perks |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [e.g., Affiliate] | [e.g., 1+ conversion/month] | [base rate] | [basic portal access + swipe copy] |
| 2 | [e.g., Partner] | [e.g., 5+ conversions/month] | [+5% boost] | [co-marketing opportunities + priority support] |
| 3 | [e.g., Strategic Partner] | [e.g., 15+ conversions/month OR $5K MRR referred] | [+10% boost or custom] | [rev share escalation + dedicated partner manager + co-sell opportunities] |

## 4. Top 10 Program Rules
List 10 rules affiliates must follow. Include:
- Self-referral policy (usually prohibited)
- Paid search / brand bidding policy
- Coupon/deal site policy
- Social proof accuracy (no fabricated claims)
- Cookie stuffing prohibition
- Spam / cold outreach policy
- Disclosure requirements (FTC compliance)
- Termination triggers
- Commission reversal policy (for refunds/chargebacks)
- Dispute resolution process

## 5. KPI Dashboard (Targets for Month 3)
Provide benchmark targets:
| KPI | Month 1 Target | Month 3 Target | What to do if below target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Active affiliate rate | 20% of total | 35% of total | [action] |
| Affiliate conversion rate | 2–5% | 4–8% | [action] |
| EPC (earnings per click) | $0.50+ | $1.00+ | [action] |
| Program-attributed revenue | 5% of total | 15% of total | [action] |
| New affiliate applications/month | [target] | [target] | [action] |

## 6. Program Launch Checklist
10 items to complete before accepting the first affiliate application.
```

---

## Example Input

- Company: NudgePay
- Product: B2B invoicing and payment follow-up SaaS
- Price: $59/month per seat
- LTV: $1,062 (18-month average retention)
- CAC: $210 (current blended)
- ICP: Freelancers and agencies billing $5K–$50K/month
- Goal: 30 affiliates in 60 days
- Platform: Rewardful (already installed)

---

## Tips for Best Results

- If LTV is unknown, estimate: average months retained × monthly price
- For e-commerce, use AOV × 3 as a rough LTV proxy
- For courses/one-time products, use price × 1.2 as a conservative LTV estimate
- RevShare models (%) work better for SaaS; flat CPA works better for e-commerce
- Cookie windows should match your sales cycle: impulse buy = 7 days; B2B SaaS trial = 30–90 days
