## Description: <br>
Ai Glaciology is a Chinese-language educational guide that helps agents answer questions about glaciers, ice sheets, sea-level rise, permafrost, ice cores, glacial landforms, and cryosphere hazards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, students, climate science readers, geography and geomorphology users, coastal planning teams, and environmental policy users can use this skill to ask plain-language questions about glaciology and cryosphere science. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Climate and cryosphere facts, measurements, and projections may be time-sensitive or unsuitable as the sole basis for research, policy, or safety-critical decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important statistics and projections against current authoritative climate science sources before relying on them. <br>
