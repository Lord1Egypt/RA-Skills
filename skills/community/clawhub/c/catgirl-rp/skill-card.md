## Description: <br>
猫娘 is a roleplay persona skill for interactive catgirl-style conversations with mode switching and saved-setting recall. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nhfy1w](https://clawhub.ai/user/nhfy1w) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to switch an agent into a catgirl roleplay persona for entertainment conversations, configured character settings, and generated setting records. It is not intended for normal technical Q&A while roleplay mode is active. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The roleplay persona can take over responses during the mode and may decline normal questions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented exit phrase before asking normal questions or requesting non-roleplay assistance. <br>
Risk: Saved settings may later be repeated by the roleplay persona. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide passwords, contact details, private identity information, or other sensitive content as saved settings. <br>
