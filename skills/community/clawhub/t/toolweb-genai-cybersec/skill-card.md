## Description: <br>
Generate personalized cybersecurity transformation roadmaps based on Microsoft's 5-point blueprint for GenAI-driven cyber defense. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Security leaders, CISOs, and enterprise teams use this skill to generate phased GenAI cybersecurity transformation roadmaps from organizational posture, challenges, goals, timeline, budget, and contact context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Submitted organization posture, budget, concerns, contact details, and user/session metadata may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering credentials, confidential incident details, regulated data, or internal architecture secrets unless the provider's privacy and security terms have been separately verified. <br>
Risk: Generated cybersecurity roadmaps may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific environment. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified security leaders review the roadmap, success metrics, resource assumptions, and risk mitigation plan before using them for operational or budget decisions. <br>
