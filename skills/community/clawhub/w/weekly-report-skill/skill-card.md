## Description: <br>
Generates polished Chinese R&D weekly reports from recent work logs, chat history, commit notes, or development notes while filtering deployment, environment, operations, installation, and troubleshooting details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineering staff use this skill to convert work logs, chat history, meeting notes, commit summaries, issue notes, or loose bullet points into manager-ready Chinese R&D weekly reports. It is especially suited to R&D records about tender agents, RAG, prompts, skills, document parsing, table parsing, scoring standards, content generation, export flows, and Agent work. <br>
