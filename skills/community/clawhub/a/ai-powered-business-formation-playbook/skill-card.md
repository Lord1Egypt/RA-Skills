## Description: <br>
Generate business formation documents for C-Corps and LLCs from a configuration, with references and templates focused on Nevada and Delaware. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[juankisugar-creator](https://clawhub.ai/user/juankisugar-creator) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, business operators, and agents use this skill to gather entity details and produce formation document packages, filing checklists, and post-filing guidance for supported C-Corp and LLC scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may overstate its supported jurisdictions and entity types. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for the explicitly supported Nevada or Delaware C-Corp and LLC scenarios unless a qualified professional reviews any other use. <br>
Risk: Generated formation documents may be mistaken for legal or tax advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as document drafts and filing aids, and have legal or tax professionals review complex structures, tax elections, and filing obligations. <br>
Risk: Configuration and output files can contain sensitive personal, ownership, tax, and business records. <br>
Mitigation: Limit access to configs and generated files, avoid unnecessary sharing, and check cloud folder permissions before upload. <br>
