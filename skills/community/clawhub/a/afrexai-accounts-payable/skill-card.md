## Use Case: <br>
Finance, accounting, and operations teams use this skill to turn current accounts-payable workflows, vendor relationships, invoice volumes, and pain points into a tailored AP automation framework with controls, routing, benchmarks, and ROI guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Accounts-payable workflows can involve sensitive invoices, bank details, tax IDs, vendor credentials, and production accounting data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering real financial records or credentials unless the workflow has been reviewed and approved for the organization's data-handling requirements. <br>
Risk: Generated AP recommendations may affect payment timing, vendor setup, fraud controls, or compliance processes if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Have finance, accounting, and compliance owners review the generated framework before adopting it in production workflows. <br>
Risk: Vendor bank-change guidance and payment-process changes can create fraud exposure if verification steps are skipped. <br>
Mitigation: Use callback verification to known vendor numbers, segregation of duties, positive pay, ACH filters, and periodic vendor-employee match reports. <br>
