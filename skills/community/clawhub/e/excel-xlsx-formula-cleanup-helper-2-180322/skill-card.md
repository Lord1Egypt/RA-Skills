## Description: <br>
Build and troubleshoot cross-Office automation pipelines that move content between Word, Excel, and PowerPoint using Open XML, VBA, python-docx, openpyxl, python-pptx, templates, and batch conversion. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, consultants, and operations teams use this skill to design dependable workflows that move data and content across Word, Excel, and PowerPoint while preserving Office file relationships, formatting, templates, and macros. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scripts or automation steps could modify real Office documents, especially macro-enabled workbooks or workflows that use desktop Office automation. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated scripts before running them, keep backups of source files, and test on copies before applying changes to production documents. <br>
