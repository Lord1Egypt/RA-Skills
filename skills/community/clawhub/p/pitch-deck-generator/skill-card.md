## Description: <br>
Generate professional pitch deck outlines using the $1B pitch formula based on startup data and company information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders, startup teams, accelerator programs, and pitch coaches use this skill to turn startup data into investor-ready pitch deck outlines covering problem, solution, market, business model, traction, competition, team, funding ask, and roadmap. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Startup data submitted to the pitch deck API can include sensitive revenue, traction, roadmap, team, funding, and user identifier details. <br>
Mitigation: Use only information you are comfortable sending to a third-party API, generalize sensitive business details where practical, and avoid sending a real userId unless it is needed. <br>
