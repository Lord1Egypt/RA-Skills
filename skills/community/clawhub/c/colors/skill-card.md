## Description: <br>
Build accessible color palettes with proper contrast ratios and semantic tokens. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, developers, and agents use this skill to choose accessible UI color palettes, contrast thresholds, semantic color tokens, and color-independent state cues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Color recommendations may be applied without validating contrast in the final UI context. <br>
Mitigation: Check contrast ratios and verify that state or status information is also conveyed with text, icons, or another non-color cue before release. <br>
