## Description: <br>
Food expiration calculator. Use when user wants to check if a food product has expired based on production/purchase date and shelf life. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[goog](https://clawhub.ai/user/goog) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Consumers and household helpers use this skill to estimate whether a food item is fresh, expiring soon, or expired from a product or purchase date and shelf-life value. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the calculated status as a food-safety guarantee. <br>
Mitigation: Present results as estimates and advise users to follow packaging labels, storage guidance, and food-safety judgement. <br>
Risk: Month and year shelf-life values are approximate because the script uses 30-day months and 365-day years. <br>
Mitigation: State the approximation when reporting results, especially for date-sensitive foods. <br>
