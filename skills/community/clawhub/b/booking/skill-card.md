## Description: <br>
Search, compare, and book accommodation across platforms with real pricing, user preferences, and end-to-end execution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and travel-support agents use this skill to search live accommodation options, compare total costs across platforms, shortlist properties, and complete bookings after confirming terms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use local booking memory for traveler preferences, history, and alerts. <br>
Mitigation: Review local booking memory before use and avoid storing sensitive travel, identity, or payment details unless necessary. <br>
Risk: Accommodation prices, fees, availability, and cancellation terms can change before checkout. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the exact property, dates, guests, total price, payment terms, and cancellation deadline immediately before any reservation. <br>
Risk: Executing a requested booking can create a financial commitment. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation after presenting the selected property, full cost breakdown, and cancellation policy. <br>
