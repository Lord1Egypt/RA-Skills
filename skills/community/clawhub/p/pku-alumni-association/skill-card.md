## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer questions about Peking University Alumni Association information, including association background, chapters, events, services, contact information, and donation channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad Peking University or alumni-related mentions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking for alumni-association information before presenting results. <br>
Risk: Contact or donation details in the local lookup data may be incomplete, outdated, or examples. <br>
Mitigation: Verify donation and contact information against official PKU websites before acting. <br>
