## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cryptocurrency transfers are irreversible and mistaken addresses or unsupported exchange flows can cause permanent loss. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recipient addresses, exchange requirements, fees, and confirmation policies before acting on the guidance. <br>
Risk: Wallet downloads, seed phrases, and private keys are common targets for phishing and theft. <br>
Mitigation: Use official wallet sources, never share seed phrases or private keys, and prefer hardware or cold storage for larger balances. <br>
Risk: Network fees, wallet support, exchange minimums, and confirmation requirements can change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current requirements with the wallet, exchange, or official Dogecoin network resources before making a transaction. <br>
