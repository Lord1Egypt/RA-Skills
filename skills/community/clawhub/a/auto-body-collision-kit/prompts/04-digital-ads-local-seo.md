# Prompt 04 — Digital Ads & Local SEO
## Auto Body & Collision Repair Marketing Kit v1.0

**Purpose:** Generate Google LSA checklist, RSA ad groups, Facebook/Instagram ad briefs, TikTok calendar, keyword clusters, and GBP 30-day calendar for auto body shops.

**All 7 compliance moats active — ad copy screened for:**
- OSHA: no "fume-free," "non-toxic paint," or false safety claims
- EPA: no "eco-friendly" without documentation reference
- Credentials: I-CAR Gold Class (year), ASE by technician name/designation, OEM by manufacturer program
- Parts: OEM stated only when all-OEM sourcing is verified
- Warranty: "lifetime guarantee" not used without written terms reference
- NRS 487: no "like new" claims on rebuilt-title restoration marketing

## GOOGLE LSA CHECKLIST — Auto Body (Screened Category)

Auto body / collision repair is a Google Local Services Ads screened service category. The following items must be in order before running LSA:

**Business license:** Clark County or applicable county business license — current.
**Insurance:** General liability minimum $1M per occurrence. Upload COI to Google LSA portal.
**Background check:** Owner/principal background check through Google's LSA verification process.
**License verification (if applicable):** Any applicable Nevada contractor or specialized license — upload.

**LSA profile required fields:**
- Business name: [Shop Name]
- Service area: [Henderson/Las Vegas/North Las Vegas/Summerlin — use zip codes, not "all of Clark County"]
- Services offered: Collision repair, auto body repair, dent repair, auto painting, frame repair
- Business hours: [Accurate hours — mismatch between LSA and GBP causes quality score issues]
- Phone number: [Tracking number recommended for LSA calls]

**What NOT to say in your LSA headline:**
- "ASE-certified shop" — shops can't hold ASE certs, individual techs do
- "I-CAR certified" — use "I-CAR Gold Class [year]" if current
- "Eco-friendly shop" — not documented in LSA context
- "Fume-free painting" — OSHA compliance; this is blocked

**LSA headline examples (compliant):**
- "I-CAR Gold Class Collision Repair — Written Estimate First"
- "Certified Technicians | OEM & Aftermarket Parts"
- "Henderson's Auto Body — All Insurers — Written Estimate"
