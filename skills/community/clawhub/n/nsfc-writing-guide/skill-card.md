## Description: <br>
Provides guidance and collaboration support for writing National Natural Science Foundation of China grant proposals, while directing users toward the integrated nsfc entry-point skill. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and grant-writing collaborators use this skill for structured NSFC proposal drafting guidance, migration guidance, and quality checks across common proposal sections. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential grant drafts or unpublished research while using the writing workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with content permitted by the user's organization and avoid providing sensitive draft material unless approved. <br>
Risk: Proposal text may include incorrect, unsupported, or unverified literature references. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify cited literature and supporting methods before relying on generated or revised proposal content. <br>
