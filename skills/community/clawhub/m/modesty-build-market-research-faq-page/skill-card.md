## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>

Mitigation: Verify market claims and cited facts against trusted sources before publishing FAQ content. <br>
Risk: Sensitive customer or business information could be exposed if included in prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing secrets or sensitive customer data when using the skill. <br>
