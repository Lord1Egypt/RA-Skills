## Description: <br>
Formats WeChat public account articles by improving paragraph breaks, heading structure, readability, and selectable formatting styles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External content creators, operators, and developers use this skill to analyze and reformat WeChat public account articles into cleaner Markdown using minimal, professional, or casual styles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The formatter can overwrite the output file path provided by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Run it on sample files first and choose output paths deliberately, preferably writing to a new file before replacing originals. <br>
Risk: Formatting options can remove images or emoji and may change how published content reads. <br>
Mitigation: Select the intended style and review the formatted article before publishing it to WeChat. <br>
