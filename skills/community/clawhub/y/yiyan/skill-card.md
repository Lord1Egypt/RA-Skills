## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill could be asked to process private account information even though it is intended for public product and documentation pages. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to public Baidu Wenxin Yiyan pages and do not provide private account data or request account-level actions. <br>
Risk: Summaries of pricing, quotas, or service status can become stale as public pages change. <br>
Mitigation: Re-check the relevant public pages before relying on summarized pricing, quota, or availability information. <br>
