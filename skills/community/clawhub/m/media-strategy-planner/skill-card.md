## Description: <br>
Plan channel mix and media strategy for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Amazon Ads, Shopify Ads, and DSP/programmatic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and growth teams use this skill to turn advertising objectives, budgets, and channel scope into actionable media plans. It supports channel role definition, budget and bidding plans, test matrices, and scale or kill rules across major ad platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Budget and bidding recommendations could affect advertising spend if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before acting, validate assumptions against current account data, and use stop-loss conditions for spend risk. <br>
Risk: The skill may trigger during broad business discussions that are not clearly advertising-focused. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the objective, budget frame, and channel scope before producing or applying a media plan. <br>
