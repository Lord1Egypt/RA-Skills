### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow involves Meta Ads credentials and account identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Use a least-privileged Meta access token where possible and avoid sharing credentials outside the intended agent environment. <br>
Risk: Campaign, budget, targeting, creative, or account changes can affect live advertising spend and performance. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit human review before activating campaigns or applying budget, targeting, creative, or account changes. <br>
