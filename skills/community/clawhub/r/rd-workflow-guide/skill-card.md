## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language guidance for the full food product R&D workflow, including staged reminders, previous-step summaries, and practical cautions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Food product R&D teams use this skill to navigate stages from market research through launch, track required outputs, and reuse project and stage-review templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Food-safety, labeling, and regulatory guidance may be incomplete or outdated for a specific product or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified food-safety, labeling, and regulatory personnel verify conclusions before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The generic workflow may not match a company's internal quality system, approval gates, or document-control requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt the templates and stage checks to the organization's approved SOPs before operational use. <br>
