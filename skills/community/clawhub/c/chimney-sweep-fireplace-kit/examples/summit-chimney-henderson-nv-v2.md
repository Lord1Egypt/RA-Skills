**Summit Chimney & Fireplace** serves Henderson, Las Vegas, and Clark County area realtors, home inspectors, and homeowners with NFPA 211 Level II pre-listing inspections signed by CSIA Certified Chimney Sweep Daniel Reyes, **#CCS-4471** — verifiable at csia.org/find-a-sweep.

### What's Included in Our NFPA 211 Level II Inspection

**Exterior assessment:**
- Chimney cap: condition, sizing, proper installation
- Chimney crown: cracks, mortar integrity, drainage slope
- Flashing: roof-to-chimney seal, step flashing condition
- Masonry: spalling, mortar joint deterioration, efflorescence

**Interior assessment:**
- Firebox: firebrick condition, mortar, smoke chamber
- Damper: operation, seating, throat condition
- Liner: accessible sections; clay tile crack check; metal liner condition if applicable
- Smoke chamber: parging condition, corbeling

**Level II adds — attic/crawl space/basement access:**
- Chimney chase framing condition
- Clearance to combustibles (chase framing, attic insulation near flue)
- Any structural concerns visible from accessible areas

**Written Report:**
- Typed PDF findings report, delivered digitally within 48 hours
- Creosote grade classification (Grade 1: dusty/routine; Grade 2: tar-like/removal required before use; Grade 3: glazed/do not use — relining assessment required)
- Compatible with e-disclosure packages for MLS upload
- Signed by Daniel Reyes, CCS #CCS-4471

### Who Needs a Level II Inspection

Per NFPA 211 Section 14.2, Level II is required when:
- **Real estate transaction:** any change of occupancy or ownership
- **Operational problem:** after a chimney fire, CO event, smoke rollback
- **System modification:** new appliance, new liner, fuel type change
- **External event:** earthquake, adjacent building fire, severe weather impact

"Chimney inspection" or "pre-listing inspection" without specifying Level II does not meet the NFPA 211 requirement.
