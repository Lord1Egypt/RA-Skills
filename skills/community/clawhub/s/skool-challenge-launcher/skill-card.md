## Description: <br>
Designs public-safe 7-day Skool challenges that invite member action, collect comments, produce visible proof, test demand, and turn repeated tasks into reusable templates or course modules without promising results. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External Skool community operators use this skill to plan a 7-day challenge with public-safe prompts, redacted proof, reminder comments, a free-course trust CTA, success metrics, and follow-up product signals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated prompts could ask members to share private community content, personal details, credentials, payment data, or account information. <br>
Mitigation: Review prompts before posting and require public-safe, member-owned, redacted examples or synthetic samples. <br>
Risk: Challenge copy could imply income, growth, conversion, rank, time saved, health, financial, legal, or education outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Frame metrics as self-reported baselines or product signals, and remove outcome promises before use. <br>
Risk: The skill is a content-planning helper and may produce unsuitable guidance for payment, crypto, legal, financial, health, or education-result contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid those use cases and keep the challenge focused on public community participation and redacted proof. <br>
