## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Engineering guidance can affect physical safety or legal compliance if applied directly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify real-world designs, code requirements, and safety-critical decisions with qualified professionals or the relevant authority. <br>
Risk: Standards, code sections, and jurisdictional requirements may vary by edition or authority. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current standards, applicable amendments, and Authority Having Jurisdiction requirements before using guidance for compliance. <br>
