## Description: <br>
Guides an agent to produce Perplexity-style web search answers with Brave Search, source citations, date awareness, causal analysis, and personal action checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wudi488](https://clawhub.ai/user/wudi488) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to make an agent structure current-event, market, technical, and geopolitical answers around search verification, source reliability, time context, causal links, and actionable next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search-driven answers can cite stale, low-quality, or insufficiently corroborated sources for fast-changing topics. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources, publication dates, and source reliability before relying on outputs, especially for financial, geopolitical, or technical decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill mentions optional reference files or integrations that may not be bundled with the release. <br>
Mitigation: Treat missing references or integrations as optional and verify their availability before asking the agent to depend on them. <br>
