## Description: <br>
Comprehensive spreadsheet creation, editing, and analysis with support for formulas, formatting, data analysis, visualization, and formula recalculation for Excel and tabular files. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to create, edit, analyze, format, and recalculate spreadsheet files while preserving formulas and workbook conventions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The recalculation helper can modify local workbooks and install a persistent LibreOffice Basic macro in the user's profile. <br>
Mitigation: Use copies of important workbooks, avoid untrusted spreadsheets, and review or back up existing LibreOffice macros before running the recalculation script. <br>
