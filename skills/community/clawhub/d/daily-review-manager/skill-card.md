## Description: <br>
Daily Review Manager helps users record, organize, and summarize daily reflections from text or voice into structured Markdown. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hongyegege](https://clawhub.ai/user/hongyegege) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals and productivity-focused users can use this skill to capture daily reflections, preserve personal journaling context, and generate weekly or monthly Markdown summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can save personal reflections, transcriptions, moods, and summaries in a workspace Markdown file. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for material you are comfortable storing in the workspace, review generated entries, and avoid recording sensitive information without explicit intent. <br>
Risk: Message-platform access or scheduled reminders may continue collecting or prompting for review content after setup. <br>
Mitigation: Enable those integrations only when needed and keep clear instructions for turning off reminders or platform access. <br>
