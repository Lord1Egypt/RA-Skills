## Use Case: <br>
Investors or analysts use this skill to structure A-share capital-flow reviews, summarize market and sector rotation signals, diagnose positions, and draft decision-oriented trading guidance. Outputs should be treated as educational analysis and checked against current market data before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial analysis outputs may be incorrect, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific investor. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market data, assumptions, and conclusions independently before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce trading suggestions that may be mistaken for personalized investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as educational analysis only and apply qualified human review for account-specific decisions. <br>
Risk: Cron-style recurring reports can amplify stale assumptions if configured without oversight. <br>
Mitigation: Enable recurring reports only deliberately and review data sources, prompts, and conclusions on each use. <br>
