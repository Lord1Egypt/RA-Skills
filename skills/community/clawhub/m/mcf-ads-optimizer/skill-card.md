## Description: <br>
AI-powered ad campaign management with performance audits, optimization analysis, A/B testing, and anomaly detection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External agents and paid-media teams use this skill to configure ad campaigns, audit performance, request optimization recommendations, run A/B tests, and detect spend or performance anomalies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create or update live advertising campaigns, budgets, targeting, and creative tests. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before campaign creation, budget changes, targeting changes, creative tests, or other live ad mutations. <br>
Risk: Live ad accounts and payment-enabled API calls can incur unexpected spend. <br>
Mitigation: Use accounts, wallets, and API access with strict spend limits and monitor x402 payment activity. <br>
Risk: Client campaign data may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending sensitive campaign data unless the provider is trusted and its data-handling terms are understood. <br>
