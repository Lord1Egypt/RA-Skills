## Description: <br>
AI副业真假鉴别器 helps users evaluate AI side-hustle projects, courses, and income claims for credibility, common scam signals, and realistic expectations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to review AI side-hustle opportunities or paid courses and receive a rule-based credibility score, risk signals, realistic earning expectations, and avoidance guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's credibility score and scam labels are heuristic and may be incomplete or wrong for a specific business opportunity. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as advisory triage only and require independent financial, legal, and business due diligence before acting. <br>
