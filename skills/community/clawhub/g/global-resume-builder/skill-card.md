## Description: <br>
Creates ATS-friendly, professionally formatted resumes and CVs tailored for US, UK, EU, and APAC job markets in tech, finance, corporate, and creative roles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jochoonhwan](https://clawhub.ai/user/jochoonhwan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career-support agents use this skill to draft hiring-manager-ready resumes and CVs for international applications. It helps adapt structure, wording, action verbs, and ATS formatting to the target region and industry. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume inputs can contain personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the details needed for the target application and review generated content before sharing it. <br>
Risk: Generated resume claims may be inaccurate or overstate experience. <br>
Mitigation: Verify every achievement, metric, date, role, and certification against the candidate's actual history. <br>
Risk: Resume conventions vary by country and employer. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the target country, employer expectations, and job posting requirements before generating or finalizing the resume. <br>
