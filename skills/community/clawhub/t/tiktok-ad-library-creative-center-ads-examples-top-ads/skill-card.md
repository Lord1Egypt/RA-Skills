## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to research TikTok-first ad examples, top ads, winning hooks, advertiser patterns, promoted products, stores, landing pages, visual styles, and offer angles through PipiAds discovery tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires sharing PIPIADS_API_KEY with the installed pipiads-mcp-server package. <br>
Mitigation: Install and run it only when PipiAds access is intended, keep the key in the environment variable rather than in prompts or files, and rotate the key if exposure is suspected. <br>
Risk: Broad ad searches can consume paid PipiAds credits quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the niche, region, and time range before large searches, and prefer narrow queries before expanding result counts. <br>
