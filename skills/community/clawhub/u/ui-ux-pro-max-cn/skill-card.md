## Description: <br>
中文UI/UX设计智能 provides localized UI/UX design guidance for Chinese internet products, including design systems, component standards, interaction guidance, color palettes, Chinese font choices, and frontend implementation patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and agents use this skill to generate Chinese-market UI/UX design systems, component guidance, localized color and font recommendations, and frontend implementation guidance for ecommerce, education, finance, SaaS, and public-service products. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global; guidance is targeted to Chinese-language and China-market products. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad triggers may apply Chinese-localized UI/UX assumptions to ordinary UI or frontend requests where they are not desired. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill when Chinese-market defaults are intended, and review generated recommendations for global or non-Chinese products before implementation. <br>
