## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to convert a country or nation-themed brief into a confirmed WeryAI video generation request for short vertical geopolitics hook videos. It is suited for text-to-video and supported image-to-video workflows that need timed English captions, map-driven visuals, and a final risk twist. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, image URLs, and explicitly approved local images may be sent to WeryAI for video generation. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the full expanded prompt and any image inputs before submission; prefer public HTTPS image URLs and require explicit consent before any local file upload path is used. <br>
Risk: WeryAI generation can consume paid credits after confirmation. <br>
Mitigation: Show the model, duration, audio, image mode, and full request payload before running a paid wait or submit command. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a WERYAI_API_KEY secret. <br>
Mitigation: Use a scoped or short-lived API key when possible and never write the key value into skill files, prompts, logs, or generated artifacts. <br>
Risk: Generated geopolitical hook videos may be misleading if treated as factual analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the output positioned as entertainment or opinion content, avoid incitement or hateful targeting, and review country-specific claims before publication. <br>
