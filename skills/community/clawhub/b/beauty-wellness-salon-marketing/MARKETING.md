# Marketing Brief: Beauty & Wellness Salon Marketing Kit

## Market Opportunity

**US Beauty Industry Stats:**
- 80,000+ hair salons in the US (IBISWorld)
- 60,000+ nail salons
- 10,000+ medical spas (American Med Spa Association)
- 340,000+ licensed massage therapists
- 560,000+ estheticians
- Total addressable market: 1M+ beauty/wellness businesses in the US

**The Pain:**
- 30-40% of new salon clients never rebook after their first visit
- Average salon loses $150-300 per client who doesn't rebook
- 90% of salon owners cite "no time for marketing" as their #1 business problem
- Instagram posting inconsistency causes follower drop-off — most post fewer than 4x/month
- FTC testimonial violations: the FTC has issued warning letters to beauty businesses for non-compliant before/after content

**The Opportunity:**
- Las Vegas / Henderson: 1,200+ licensed salons + 400+ nail studios + 85+ med spas in Clark County
- Summer prime season: wedding season (April-June), back-to-school (July-August), holiday (Nov-Dec) — 3 high-demand windows per year
- Med spa market growing 12% annually — fastest-growing segment in beauty
