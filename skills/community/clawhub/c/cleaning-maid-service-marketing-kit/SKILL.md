## What This Skill Does

Generates compliance-aware, conversion-optimized marketing content for residential cleaning, commercial janitorial, move-in/move-out, and vacation rental/Airbnb turnover cleaning businesses.

Every output enforces:
- OSHA HazCom 2012 (GHS SDS references for cleaning products — no unverified "chemical-free" claims)
- EPA Safer Choice program accuracy (only cite if EPA Safer Choice certified)
- FTC Green Guides 2012 (enforced): "eco-friendly," "green," "natural" require substantiation
- FTC 2023 Endorsement Guides (review request language)
- TCPA/CAN-SPAM compliance in all SMS/email templates
- Bonding & insurance disclosure accuracy (separate general liability vs. bonding vs. workers' comp)
- IRS worker classification language (avoid implying employees are independent contractors in copy)
- State contractor license where required (CA HIC, TX, FL DBPR, NV)

### Prompt 1 — Seasonal Campaign + Brand Positioning (FREE)
**File:** `prompts/01_seasonal_campaign.md`

Generates:
- Spring cleaning / move-in/move-out / holiday campaign copy
- Google Ad (RSA: 15 headlines × 4 descriptions, character-count enforced)
- Facebook/Instagram ad (primary text 125 chars, headline 40 chars, 3 audiences)
- Nextdoor sponsored post (neighbor-tone)
- 3 SMS messages (TCPA-compliant, under 160 chars, opt-out included)

**Compliance enforced:**

- "Eco-friendly" only with EPA Safer Choice or equivalent certification

- "Satisfaction guaranteed" tied to specific re-clean policy, not open-ended

### Prompt 2 — Service Pages + Compliance + JSON-LD Schema (PAID)
**File:** `prompts/02_service_pages.md`

Generates:
- Main cleaning services page (800–1,200 words, H1/H2/H3 structure)

**Compliance enforced:**

- FTC Green Guides: "green cleaning" → must name certified products
- Insurance accuracy: distinguishes general liability, bonding, and workers' comp
- IRS safe language: "our cleaning professionals" not "our contractors/employees" (avoids misclassification exposure)

### Prompt 3 — Review, Reputation & Referral System (PAID)
**File:** `prompts/03_review_reputation_referral.md`

Generates:
- 20 review request templates (5 stages: day-of, 24hr, 3-day, 7-day, 30-day)
- 15 review response templates (5-star appreciation, 4-star thanks, 3-star service recovery, 1-star resolution, competitor mention)
- Sparkle Referral Club program (two-sided: $25 both ways, or first clean discount — anti-kickback compliant)
- Review removal request script (Google/Yelp — for fake reviews only)
- Social proof display copy for website, ads, and flyers

**Compliance enforced:**
- FTC 2023 Endorsement Guides: no "incentivized review" language, no conditional review requests ("leave a 5-star review to get..."), incentive disclosed in any review that mentions the discount
- Referral program uses fixed-dollar off (not percentage — cleaner math for disclosure)
- "Verified customer" tag usage: only usable when booking software confirms the appointment
