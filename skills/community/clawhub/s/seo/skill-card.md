## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional local note storage may persist site profiles, audit history, keywords, or content drafts under ~/seo. <br>
Mitigation: Create the optional workspace only when persistent local tracking is desired, and avoid storing sensitive site or client data unless the environment is appropriate. <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations can lead to public site changes, Search Console uploads, redirects, schema edits, or publishing steps that affect visibility and ranking. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed Search Console uploads, publishing steps, and site changes before applying them, and test technical changes on the target site. <br>
