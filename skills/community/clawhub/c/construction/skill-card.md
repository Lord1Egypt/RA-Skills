## Description: <br>
Construction helps agents manage construction project workflows, including schedules, daily site reports, subcontractor coordination, procurement, budgets, change orders, safety, permits, RFIs, punch lists, quality control, weather impacts, and client communication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Construction project managers, superintendents, and project teams use this skill to structure and review construction operations from pre-construction through closeout, including schedule, cost, safety, quality, procurement, and owner communication workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may generate recommendations for pay applications, purchase orders, change orders, or owner-facing communications. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before committing, purchasing, approving payment, changing contract scope, or sending owner-facing communications. <br>
Risk: Construction workflows can involve sensitive budgets, site photos, safety incidents, and subcontractor records. <br>
Mitigation: Connect only necessary project systems, limit write permissions, and apply project data access controls before using it on real projects. <br>
