## Description: <br>
Provides structured buy-side and sell-side M&A guidance for strategy, target screening, valuation, due diligence, deal structuring, integration planning, and exit readiness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, CFOs, corporate development teams, and M&A advisors use this skill to evaluate acquisition targets, prepare companies for sale, structure deal terms, run diligence checklists, and plan post-acquisition integration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat general M&A checklists as professional financial, legal, tax, or valuation advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the playbook as planning guidance and validate deal, valuation, legal, and tax assumptions with qualified advisors. <br>
Risk: M&A workflows may involve highly confidential deal data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive transaction details unless the user is comfortable with platform handling and applicable confidentiality controls. <br>
Risk: Valuation ranges, resource links, and assumptions may be incomplete or context-dependent. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify valuation assumptions, comparable transactions, and external resources before relying on them. <br>
