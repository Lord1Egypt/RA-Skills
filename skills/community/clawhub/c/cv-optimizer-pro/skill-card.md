## Description: <br>
CV Optimizer Pro helps users improve resumes and CVs, rewrite work experience, match content to target job descriptions, generate cover letters, and prepare interview answers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[awdasfdada](https://clawhub.ai/user/awdasfdada) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career coaches use this skill to improve resume content, align experience with a target job description, draft cover letters, and prepare interview responses across multiple industries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Security guidance flags possible URL submission to toui.io and optional API-key use. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm this behavior is intended before installing; if REST access is used, provide only the minimum-scope TOUI_API_KEY needed. <br>
Risk: Resume and job-search materials may contain sensitive personal or employment information. <br>
Mitigation: Review inputs before sharing them with an agent and remove personal data that is not needed for the requested optimization. <br>
