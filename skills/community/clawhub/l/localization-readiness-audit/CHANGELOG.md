## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-24
Initial release. Audits a single product / codebase / content surface against eight i18n readiness dimensions (Unicode and encoding, string externalization, plural and gender, dates / numbers / currency / units, locale-aware sorting and search, RTL and bidi, UI expansion and layout, content-localization process and legal-regulatory) before engaging a translation vendor or launching in a new locale, and produces a DRAFT audit with severity-rated findings, Top-10 blockers, Ship / Fix-then-ship / Hold verdict, owner-tagged remediation plan, and an unresolved-information list — for product / engineering / globalization-lead review.
