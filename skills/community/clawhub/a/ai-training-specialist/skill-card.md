## Description: <br>
Provides guidance for AI training and annotation work, including response evaluation, RLHF review, hallucination checks, platform qualification preparation, and workflow strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
AI trainers, annotators, and people preparing for AI training platform work use this skill to understand evaluation and annotation task types, apply quality criteria, and prepare for qualification assessments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share platform passwords, private qualification materials, confidential client data, or other sensitive information while seeking task-specific help. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as public guidance and provide only information intentionally needed for the task; avoid sharing credentials, private assessments, or confidential client data. <br>
Risk: Guidance about AI training platform work may not match a specific platform's current policies, pay terms, or qualification rules. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm platform-specific requirements and policies directly with the platform before relying on the guidance for paid work or qualifier preparation. <br>
