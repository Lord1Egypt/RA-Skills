## Description: <br>
Mock Interview is a conversational agent skill that acts as an interviewer, runs multi-round interview practice from the user's target role and experience, and produces a structured debrief with scores and improvement suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers use this skill to practice realistic interview Q&A for a target role using their provided job description, resume, and experience details. The skill asks one interview question per round, adapts follow-up questions to the user's answers, and produces a markdown debrief when practice ends. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive resume, employment, education, or career details during mock interview practice. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the resume, job description, and experience details that are appropriate to place in the chat, and avoid unnecessary personal or confidential information. <br>
Risk: Interview debriefs or suggested answer improvements could become misleading if the agent invents facts, metrics, tools, or accomplishments. <br>
Mitigation: Keep feedback and rewritten examples grounded in the user's provided information, using placeholders for missing metrics instead of fabricating specific results. <br>
