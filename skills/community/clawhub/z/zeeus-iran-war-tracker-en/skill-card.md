## Description: <br>
A specialized skill for tracking the US-Iran conflict in English, focused on intelligence published within the last 6 hours for tactical situation reports on military actions, official statements, and international reactions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to gather, verify, and synthesize near-real-time English-language situation reports about US-Iran conflict developments. It emphasizes source triangulation, explicit attribution, and a rolling 6-hour timeline. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Conflict reporting can amplify unverified or competing claims from interested parties. <br>
Mitigation: Triangulate claims with opposing-side sources or reputable neutral agencies, and clearly label uncorroborated claims with attribution. <br>
Risk: Near-real-time intelligence can become stale quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the skill's 6-hour time filter, maintain timestamps, and call out intelligence gaps when current verified information is limited. <br>
Risk: Security evidence is clean but advises review before granting sensitive production access or staff permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for reporting guidance without granting unnecessary credentials or privileged staff capabilities. <br>
