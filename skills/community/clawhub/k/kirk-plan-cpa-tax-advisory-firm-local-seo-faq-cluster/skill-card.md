## Description: <br>
Researches local questions, groups them into clusters, and drafts launch-ready FAQ copy for a CPA tax advisory firm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and local SEO practitioners use this skill to plan CPA tax advisory FAQ clusters, draft location-aware FAQ copy, and refine content before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ copy may include inaccurate tax-related claims or marketing assertions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a draft and require review by a qualified tax or content reviewer before publication. <br>
Risk: Prompts or drafts could include client PII or confidential tax details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing sensitive client information and remove confidential details before using the skill. <br>
