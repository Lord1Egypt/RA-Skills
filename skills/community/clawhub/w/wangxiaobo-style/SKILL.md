# 王小波风格写作助手

## Overview

This skill enables writing and rewriting in the style of Wang Xiaobo (王小波), one of China's most influential essayists. His style is defined by three interlocking layers: rigorous logic (三段论推演、归谬法), intellectual playfulness (知识密度、场景化写作), and deadpan dark humor (逻辑尽头的荒诞). The skill is based on a deep analysis of his essay collection 《我的精神家园》(45 essays), extracting seven reproducible techniques.

## When to Use

Activate this skill when the user:

- Explicitly requests "王小波风格"、"小波风格"、"王小波式" or "wangxiaobo style"
- Wants to transform academic/professional Chinese text into engaging, accessible prose
- Needs to write popular science (科普) articles with logical rigor and humor
- Asks to analyze whether a piece of text exhibits 王小波-style characteristics
- Requests rewriting with "归谬法"、"逻辑推演"、"花剌子模信使" or any technique from the reference

## Core Workflow

### Step 1: Load the reference

- Before/after examples of how to transform text
- Templates for each of the seven techniques

### Step 2: Understand the target content

Identify the core message the user wants to convey. 王小波风格 never sacrifices logical rigor for humor — humor is a byproduct of rigorous thinking, not an additive decoration.

### Step 3: Apply the seven techniques

Choose the most appropriate techniques from the seven below. Not all techniques are needed for every piece — select based on the content and purpose.

#### Technique 1: 三段论推演 (Syllogistic Deduction)

Structure a paragraph as: premise → strict deduction → absurd/counterintuitive conclusion. The conclusion itself becomes the satire.

Template:
```
[日常现象/常识] → 按这个逻辑推下去 → [推导出反常识结论] → 所以，[常识可能是错的]
```

#### Technique 2: 归谬法 (Reductio ad Absurdum)

Push the opponent's logic to its extreme until it self-destructs. Each step must be independently verifiable.

Template:
```
如果A，那就要B。如果B，那就要C。如果C……最后发现：要么接受荒谬的Z，要么承认A是错的。
```

#### Technique 3: 花剌子模信使模型 (Abstract Model Creation)

Create a reusable story-model (person + place + mechanism) that encapsulates a complex social or intellectual pattern. Once created, the model can be referenced by name in future writing.

Key: The model must be simple enough to explain in one sentence, yet rich enough to reuse.

#### Technique 4: 抽象→具体→回弹 (Concrete Scene Replacement)

Never argue at the abstract level. Instead: translate an abstract concept into a concrete, sensory scene → observe how it operates → derive a conclusion deeper than abstract debate could reach.

#### Technique 5: 个人经验替代引证 (Personal Experience over Citation)

Use personal experience as evidence rather than citing authorities. Examples from 王小波: his 插队 (rustication) experiences, studying abroad in America, factory work, childhood memories. Personal experience is unrefutable — readers can only choose to trust or distrust, and trust is built on perceived honesty.

#### Technique 6: 精确数字的异物感 (Precise Numbers as Foreign Bodies)

Insert a precise number or quantitative comparison into an otherwise literary/emotional passage. This creates a "scientist's presence" that signals the writer is calculating, not just emoting.

Example: Instead of "北京城墙很宽", write "假如有两个帕瓦罗蒂那样的人在上面并肩行走，罗马城的城墙就得掉下来一个".

#### Technique 7: 结尾往下沉 (Downward Ending)

Never end with a grand summary or uplifting升华. Instead, end by sinking downward — returning to a specific, personal, almost mundane image or feeling. This downward movement paradoxically makes the weight of the essay land fully on the reader.

### Step 4: Layer the three depths

The best 王小波-style writing has three layers visible to the careful reader:

1. **Surface (趣味性)**: Engaging, readable, full of interesting knowledge and stories
2. **Middle (逻辑性)**: A complete logical argument hidden beneath the stories — premise, reasoning, counterargument, conclusion
3. **Deep (诚实)**: An unshakable commitment to intellectual honesty. The core belief: "人可以傻，但不能骗自己" (One can be foolish, but one must not deceive oneself)

### Step 5: Self-check

After writing, verify:

- [ ] Is every humorous moment a *consequence of logic*, not a standalone joke?
- [ ] Are abstract concepts replaced with concrete scenes?
- [ ] Does the ending sink downward rather than rise upward?
- [ ] Is there at least one moment of honest self-reflection?
- [ ] Could the reader follow the logical chain without being told what to think?

## Key Prohibitions

Never do the following when writing in 王小波 style:

- **No 抖机灵 (cheap wit)**: Humor must emerge from logic, not from puns or clever one-liners
- **No 排比句煽情 (parallel-structure emotional manipulation)**: 王小波 never uses rhetorical parallelism to stir emotion
- **No 引经据典 (excessive citation)**: Rarely cite authorities; use personal experience as evidence
- **No 拔高升华 (uplifting ending)**: Never conclude with "前景光明" or similar grand statements
- **No 抽象辩论 (abstract debate)**: Always ground arguments in concrete, sensory detail

## Quick Reference: Style Transformation

For rapid transformation of academic text into 王小波 style:

| Academic/Formal | 王小波 Style |
|----------------|-------------|
| 抽象概念定义 | 具体场景描述 + 从场景中推导 |
| "某某指出……" | "我读到……有一段让我看了三遍" |
| 排比论证 | 三段论推演或归谬链 |
| "综上所述"总结 | 下沉到个人感受收尾 |
| 宏大叙事 | 物理细节碾压（精确数字、感官体验） |
| 权威引证 | 个人经验 |

## Examples

### Before (academic):
> 经络是血管体网络的涌现现象，穴位是这种涌现现象在体表的可操作节点。

### After (王小波 style):
> 你身上有几百个皮穿支血管穿出筋膜的小洞。古人没有显微镜，但他们靠手摸到了。他们给每个洞起了名字，叫做穴位。这件事让我想了很久——两千年前的人是怎么做到的？后来我想通了：他们不需要知道血管，他们只需要知道按下去有感觉。
