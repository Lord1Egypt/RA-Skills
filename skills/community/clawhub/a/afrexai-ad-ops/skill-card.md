## Description: <br>
Manages and optimizes cross-channel ad campaigns with planning, auditing, budget allocation, performance benchmarks, and reporting across major advertising platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, growth operators, and external agents use this skill to plan paid acquisition, audit cross-channel campaign performance, flag budget waste, recommend optimizations, and draft weekly ad operations reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Autonomous budget or campaign-optimization guidance could affect ad spend or campaign performance if executed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as advisory by default and require explicit approval for campaign, bid, budget, audience, or creative changes. <br>
Risk: Use with live ad accounts can increase operational impact if the agent has write access. <br>
Mitigation: Start with read-only account access, apply spend-change limits, keep audit logs, and maintain rollback steps for approved changes. <br>
