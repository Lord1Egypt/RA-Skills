## Description: <br>
Generate structured social media content calendars with platform-specific posts, hashtags, and scheduling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[seanwyngaard](https://clawhub.ai/user/seanwyngaard) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Social media managers, marketers, and creators use this skill to turn a brand or niche brief into a scheduled content calendar with platform-adapted posts, hashtags, image prompts, and import-ready planning files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create or edit local planning files, especially under output/content-calendar/. <br>
Mitigation: Use it in a workspace where those generated files are expected, and review existing files before allowing overwrites. <br>
Risk: Generated social media posts, hashtags, schedules, and image prompts may be inaccurate, stale, or unsuitable for a brand or regulated audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review the calendar, platform adaptations, accessibility text, and CSV export before publishing or importing into scheduling tools. <br>
