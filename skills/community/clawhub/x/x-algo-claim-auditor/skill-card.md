Review public claims about xai-org/x-algorithm, the X For You algorithm, Phoenix, Grox, Home Mixer, engagement weights, filters, ads blending, or viral algorithm narratives. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to review public X algorithm claims and produce cautious, source-backed verdicts with boundary notes and safer public wording. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat public repository evidence as proof of X's live production ranking behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Require boundary notes that state what public evidence does and does not prove. <br>
Risk: Users may provide private account data, authenticated pages, or session access while asking for claim review. <br>
Mitigation: Limit review to public-source evidence and avoid requesting or using private account data, authenticated pages, or session access. <br>
Risk: Users may ask the skill to predict reach, score draft posts, or diagnose account-specific boosting or suppression. <br>
Mitigation: Return an evidence review rather than growth advice, reach prediction, or account-specific diagnosis. <br>
