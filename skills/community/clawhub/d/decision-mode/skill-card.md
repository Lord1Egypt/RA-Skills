## Description: <br>
Activate when the user asks a question that requires judgment, choice, or decision-making. This skill helps provide structured decision support by analyzing from both AI perspective and user's perspective, with confidence levels and confidence ratings to help users assess the certainty of conclusions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[PatrtioX](https://clawhub.ai/user/PatrtioX) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People using an agent use this skill to evaluate choices, trade-offs, predictions, and risk assessments with dual objective and user-context perspectives. It structures recommendations with confidence scores, evidence quality notes, caveats, and next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate broadly on everyday advice questions and produce recommendations when user context is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review the stated assumptions, provide missing constraints, and treat recommendations as one input to the final decision. <br>
Risk: Time-sensitive or high-stakes decisions may depend on changing external information or professional standards. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources for current topics and seek qualified professional advice for legal, medical, financial, or other high-stakes choices. <br>
