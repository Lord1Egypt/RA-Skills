## Description: <br>
为小红书、X、知乎等平台生成“去 AI 味”的内容重写与新稿产出技能，用于让草稿更像真人表达、减少模板腔、提升口语感和观点力度，并生成平台适配版本。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and social media operators use this skill to diagnose AI-sounding drafts, rewrite them with clearer stance and concrete details, and adapt output for Xiaohongshu, X, or Zhihu. It also supports pre-publication checks for source traceability, uncertainty labels, and platform fit. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated rewrites may add personal details, numbers, sources, or claimed experiences that are not true. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all added details before publication and mark uncertain claims as pending verification. <br>
Risk: AI-assisted content may require disclosure under platform rules or audience expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose AI assistance when required by the publishing platform, client policy, or audience context. <br>
