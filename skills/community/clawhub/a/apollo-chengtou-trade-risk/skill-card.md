## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language trade risk-control assistant that helps screen municipal investment company trade contracts, counterparty background issues, and policy-compliance concerns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nic-yuan](https://clawhub.ai/user/nic-yuan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external risk, legal, or business teams at municipal investment and state-owned companies use this skill for preliminary screening of trade contracts, counterparty due-diligence needs, financing-trade indicators, and state-asset policy questions. It produces structured risk-control reports and practical follow-up checks, not final legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce incomplete or outdated trade-risk, policy, company, judicial, or reputation assessments. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as preliminary screening and verify current policy and source data through lawful, authorized channels before acting. <br>
Risk: Users may treat risk-control output as legal advice for contracts or transactions. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate material contracts, high-risk counterparties, and transaction decisions to qualified legal and compliance reviewers. <br>
Risk: Contract and due-diligence prompts may contain confidential business data or third-party personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Submit only necessary information, apply internal data-handling rules, and do not retain material for feedback or training without permission. <br>
