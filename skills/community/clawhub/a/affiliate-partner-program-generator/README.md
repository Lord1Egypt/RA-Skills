# Partnership & Affiliate Program Generator

**Generate a complete affiliate and partner program — from commission structure to partner toolkit — in one afternoon.**

Most affiliate programs fail not because the product is bad, but because founders launch with no structure, vague commissions, and zero content for partners. This skill fixes all of that.

1. **Program Architecture & Commission Structure** — design tier system, set commission rates using LTV-based formula, define cookie windows, payout schedule, and program rules
2. **Partner Recruitment Kit** — cold outreach emails, LinkedIn DM sequence, partner one-pager, application form, and welcome email
3. **Partner Marketing Toolkit** — email swipe copy (5 variants), social post templates (10), FAQ doc, co-branding guidelines, and product comparison sheet for reviews
4. **Performance Report & Optimization Framework** — monthly report template, partner health scorecard (0–100), intervention playbook, 90-day optimization checklist, partner newsletter
