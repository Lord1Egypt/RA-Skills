## Description: <br>
Use when building a manual stock watchlist, defining alert thresholds, or documenting a repeatable monitoring routine with public market data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xuxuclassmate](https://clawhub.ai/user/xuxuclassmate) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to create manual stock monitoring routines, including watchlists, threshold rules, review cadence, and follow-up notes based on public market data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat delayed or vendor-dependent public quotes as authoritative real-time market data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify quote freshness and source terms before acting on monitoring notes or thresholds. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the manual workflow for an automated alerting or trading system. <br>
Mitigation: State that the skill only produces monitoring plans and does not send alerts, fetch quotes, or execute trades. <br>
Risk: Future versions that add APIs, credentials, automation, notifications, or trading actions would change the risk profile. <br>
Mitigation: Review and rescan future releases before installation or deployment. <br>
