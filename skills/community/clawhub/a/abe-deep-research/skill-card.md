## Description: <br>
Conduct exhaustive multi-source investigation with methodology tracking, source evaluation, and iterative depth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill when they need a structured research process that scopes a question, searches across sources, evaluates credibility, synthesizes findings, and documents methodology. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research queries and source logs may reveal sensitive topics to external search tools or APIs used by the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including secrets, personal data, or confidential project details in research queries; review source logs before sharing reports. <br>
