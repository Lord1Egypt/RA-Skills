## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to register for and navigate a guided drifts.bot meditation journey, including starting the experience, continuing through reflections, checking status, and leaving a review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The drifts.bot account token can continue journeys and post reviews on the user's account if exposed. <br>
Mitigation: Keep YOUR_TOKEN private and avoid sharing it in prompts, logs, or public artifacts. <br>
Risk: Profile details and reflection text are sent to drifts.bot and may contain sensitive personal or health information. <br>
Mitigation: Use the minimum registration details needed and avoid entering sensitive personal or health information. <br>
