## Description: <br>
Write Lithuanian that sounds human. Not formal, not robotic, not AI-generated. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to have an agent produce Lithuanian writing that feels casual, direct, and natural instead of overly formal or robotic. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill favors casual Lithuanian and may be a poor fit for formal, elderly, or professional audiences. <br>
Mitigation: Review the requested audience and ask the agent to use a formal register when professional Lithuanian is required. <br>
Risk: The style guidance includes expressive and slang-like wording that may be inappropriate in sensitive contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated Lithuanian before publication and constrain tone for customer-facing, legal, medical, or workplace communications. <br>
