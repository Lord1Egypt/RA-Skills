## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to analyze companies through a Buffett-style value investing lens, summarize Berkshire meeting lessons, and structure educational investment research. It is an educational aid and should not be treated as personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake educational Buffett-style analysis for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as general information, require user review against personal risk tolerance and jurisdiction, and recommend consultation with a qualified financial advisor before acting. <br>
Risk: Formulas, valuation checklists, and examples may be applied with stale or incomplete market data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all company fundamentals, prices, interest rates, and assumptions against current market data before making any investment decision. <br>
