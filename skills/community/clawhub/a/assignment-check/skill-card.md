## Description: <br>
Checks a local assignment folder, reads assignment PDF instructions, and reports deadlines, approximate completion time, and suggested start dates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[B1llySh1](https://clawhub.ai/user/B1llySh1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students or academic users can use this skill to inspect a local course-resource folder for assignment instruction PDFs and summarize deadline planning information. The skill is intended for local assignment triage and scheduling support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to read local assignment PDFs from a hard-coded personal folder, which may include unrelated course documents. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target folder before use and scope file access to the specific assignment or course folder needed for the task. <br>
