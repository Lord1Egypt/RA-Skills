## Description: <br>
Writing Assistant Pro is a Chinese-language writing assistant for content creation, rewriting, headline generation, and topic ideation using a three-layer identity, operations, and knowledge structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huamu668](https://clawhub.ai/user/huamu668) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to draft Chinese-language marketing and social content, improve existing copy, generate headline options, and plan content topics for channels such as Xiaohongshu and WeChat public accounts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local memory and log files may retain writing preferences or draft-related context. <br>
Mitigation: Review or clear memory and log files before working with sensitive material. <br>
Risk: The skill may be selected unintentionally when many writing skills are installed. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit prompts that name the intended writing task, such as drafting, rewriting, headline generation, or topic ideation. <br>
