## Description: <br>
A startup growth playbook that helps teams move from zero users to a self-sustaining growth engine using atomic network theory, manual first-user acquisition tactics, marketplace seeding, community bootstrapping, and tipping point metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cold outreach, Reddit posting, and marketplace seeding advice can become spammy or deceptive if applied carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Follow platform rules, personalize outreach, contribute genuine value before promotion, and review messaging for accuracy and consent. <br>
Risk: Growth tactics and benchmark claims may not fit every market, audience, or product stage. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the playbook as planning guidance, validate tactics with small tests, and track retention, referral, and organic signup metrics before scaling. <br>
