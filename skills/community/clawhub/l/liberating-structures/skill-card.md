## Description: <br>
Helps facilitators, leaders, and teams choose and apply the right Liberating Structures (33 microstructures) based on context like group size, available time, purpose, and facilitator experience. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alex-ht](https://clawhub.ai/user/alex-ht) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external facilitators, leaders, and teams use this skill to select suitable Liberating Structures, plan workshop sequences, and get grounded facilitation guidance for meetings, strategy sessions, team development, and organizational change. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Workshop recommendations may be incomplete or mismatched for sensitive group dynamics, novice facilitators, or high-conflict settings. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before use, prefer simpler structures for low-trust or novice contexts, and adapt guidance to the actual participants and facilitation constraints. <br>
Risk: Workshop activities that photograph, video, record, or retain participant outputs can create privacy and consent concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Get explicit participant consent, explain how recordings or artifacts will be used and retained, and offer a non-recorded option. <br>
