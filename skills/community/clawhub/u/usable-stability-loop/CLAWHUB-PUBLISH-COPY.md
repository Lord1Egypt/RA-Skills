## Display Name
Usable Stability Loop · The 99/80 Loop

## One-line pitch
A practical skill for agents serving non-technical but goal-clear users: build fast, deploy efficiently, stay stable, and evolve only through real usage.

## Short description
Help agents avoid over-engineering by prioritizing usability and stability first, converging once the solution is truly usable, and improving the remaining gap only through real-world feedback. Best for fast development, efficient deployment, and continuous evolution.

## Target audience
- Non-technical users with clear goals
- Builders who want fast development and efficient deployment
- Agent operators who prefer continuous evolution over speculative polishing

## Problem solved
Most agent systems fail because they either never become truly usable, or they keep adding complexity after the real need is already solved. This skill gives agents a durable rule for fast development, efficient deployment, and continuous evolution without product vanity.
