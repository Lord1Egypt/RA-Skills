## Description: <br>
Promotional Generation is a Chinese-language skill for planning, generating, formatting, and saving promotional materials such as flyers, brochures, posters, and promotional video scripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to create structured Chinese promotional materials by moving from requirements analysis through design rules, content generation, formatting, and file output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated promotional material may be saved to files and could overwrite existing workspace content if the output path is ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Choose an explicit output folder in the workspace and confirm before overwriting existing files. <br>
Risk: Broad Chinese promotional trigger phrases may activate the skill in more situations than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants promotional-material generation before producing or saving output. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy or design guidance may be inaccurate or unsuitable for the target audience, brand, or channel. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content for factual accuracy, brand fit, audience fit, and channel requirements before publication or printing. <br>
