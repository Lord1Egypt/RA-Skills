## Description: <br>
Provides academic paper writing style guidance for geology, geochemistry, lunar science, and planetary science, including Chinese and English writing conventions, journal formatting patterns, phrasing examples, and manuscript revision support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and technical writers use this skill to draft, revise, and polish Chinese or English academic manuscripts in geoscience, geochemistry, lunar science, and planetary science. It helps compare journal expectations, choose phrasing, prepare review responses, and run pre-submission checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Journal requirements and style expectations may change after the reference material was written. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current submission rules with the target journal before relying on formatting, word-count, or checklist guidance. <br>
Risk: Example sentences may be copied too closely into manuscripts. <br>
Mitigation: Use examples as style models and rewrite them for the manuscript's own data, claims, and citation context. <br>
