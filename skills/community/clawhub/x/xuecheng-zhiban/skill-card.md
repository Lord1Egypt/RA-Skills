## Description: <br>
学程智伴 is a Chinese-language student planning and focus assistant for coursework planning, career exploration, non-clinical emotional support, and time management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[smallkeyboy](https://clawhub.ai/user/smallkeyboy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Chinese-speaking undergraduate and graduate students use this skill to plan semesters, choose study strategies, explore career options, run focus sessions, and receive supportive non-clinical coaching. It is intended for everyday academic planning and wellness support, not emergency or professional mental-health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the emotional-support behavior as emergency or clinical mental-health help. <br>
Mitigation: Present it as non-clinical support and direct emergencies, self-harm, suicidal thoughts, or severe distress to local emergency services, crisis hotlines, campus counseling, or a trusted person. <br>
Risk: Academic goals or personal stressors shared during use may be retained by the host agent if memory is enabled. <br>
Mitigation: Review and configure memory settings before use when students do not want academic plans or personal stressors stored. <br>
Risk: Study plans, career guidance, and focus schedules may not match a student's actual program requirements or constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans against official course requirements, advisor guidance, deadlines, and personal capacity before acting on them. <br>
