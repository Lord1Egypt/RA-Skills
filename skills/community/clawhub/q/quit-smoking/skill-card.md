## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for quit-smoking support, including smoke-free progress tracking, craving coping strategies, health recovery milestones, and money-saved estimates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Quit-smoking progress, cravings, and milestones may be remembered locally on the user's device. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when local device storage is acceptable, and avoid entering unnecessary sensitive health details. <br>
Risk: Health recovery timelines and craving support are general guidance, not individualized medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as supportive behavior-change assistance and consult a healthcare professional for medical concerns or treatment decisions. <br>
