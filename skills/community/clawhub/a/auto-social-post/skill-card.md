## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Always-on social posting may publish public content without clear user-control safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Use test or low-risk social accounts first, require human review before live posting, and set explicit posting limits. <br>
Risk: Connected social account credentials could be exposed or misused if the workflow is configured unsafely. <br>
Mitigation: Use revocable credentials and confirm that account access can be withdrawn quickly before connecting live accounts. <br>
