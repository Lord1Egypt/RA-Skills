# Compliant Messaging Templates

Every template here avoids rating direction, avoids incentive entanglement, references the product by name, and includes a negative-path option.

## Template A — In-package insert
Headline: Thanks for choosing the [PRODUCT NAME].
Body: Future buyers rely on real customer stories. If you have a moment after using it, scan the code and share your experience. If anything's not right, email care@[BRAND].com first so we can help.
QR → /review-redirect (auto-routes to Amazon, Shopify reviews, or other based on order source).

## Template B — First-time buyer email
Subject: How's the [PRODUCT NAME] working out?
Body:
Hi [FIRST NAME],

Your [PRODUCT NAME] arrived [DAYS AGO]. Now that you've had time to use it, we'd love to hear what stood out — the good, the surprising, even the part we should improve.

Sharing your experience helps other shoppers decide whether this is right for them.

[Leave a review] → [platform review URL]

If something's wrong with your order or the product isn't what you expected, hit reply or email care@[BRAND].com first and we'll make it right.

— The [BRAND] team

Could you take a minute to share what's kept you loyal? Even one line helps.

## Banned language — never use
- "Please leave 5 stars"
- "Help us hit [number] reviews to unlock [something]"
- "Honest 5-star review"
- "If you don't love it, just contact us before reviewing" (this is gating)
- "Free [thing] for a review"
- Any phrasing that ties anything of value to the act of reviewing
