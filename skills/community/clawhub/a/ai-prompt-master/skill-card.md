## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to convert rough ideas into structured prompts with context, role definition, output format, constraints, and examples for common AI tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include passwords, API keys, private documents, or sensitive personal data in prompts that are later sent to external AI services. <br>
Mitigation: Review prompts before use and remove secrets, private documents, and sensitive personal data before submitting them to any external AI service. <br>
Risk: Optimized prompts can still produce inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable AI outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts and resulting model outputs for correctness, suitability, and policy fit before relying on them. <br>
