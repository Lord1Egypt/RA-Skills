## Description: <br>
AI skateboarding instruction guide covering board selection, beginner fundamentals, flatground tricks, street and bowl techniques, longboard skills, protective gear, safety, maintenance, etiquette, and skate culture. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer skateboarding questions, suggest board and gear choices, explain trick progression, and provide general coaching and safety guidance for uploaded skateboarding scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Skateboarding coaching and injury guidance may be incomplete or inappropriate for a user's physical condition or environment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as general coaching, use protective equipment and safe practice areas, and seek qualified medical or in-person instruction for injuries or high-risk techniques. <br>
Risk: Uploaded skateboarding videos can reveal personal, location, or bystander information. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only videos the user is comfortable sharing with the agent environment and avoid sharing unnecessary identifying details. <br>
