Developers, creators, educators, business users, and other external users use this skill to create structured prompts and reusable prompt templates for AI tools, including Flutter app and game development tasks. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated prompts may include sensitive information if users provide passwords, API keys, customer data, or proprietary code as input. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include sensitive information in prompts and review generated prompts before using them with external AI tools. <br>
Risk: Prompt templates may produce guidance that is incomplete, ambiguous, or unsuitable for a specific target AI tool or workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt generated prompts for the intended use case, model, and operational constraints before relying on the output. <br>
