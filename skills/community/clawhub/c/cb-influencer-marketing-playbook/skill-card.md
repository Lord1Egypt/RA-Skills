## Use Case: <br>
Growth marketers, brand managers, social media leads, and overseas campaign operators use this skill to build market-specific creator partnership playbooks. It helps map platform ecosystems, select creator tiers, vet authenticity and fraud risk, prepare briefs, design compensation models, and measure campaign outcomes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pricing, contract, disclosure, legal, and tax guidance can vary by market and may become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning aids and verify campaign terms, disclosure requirements, payment terms, and tax treatment against current local rules and qualified advisors before launch. <br>
Risk: Creator vetting and performance recommendations may be incomplete for a specific product, market, platform, or campaign objective. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated playbook against market-specific platform data, brand-safety requirements, and campaign performance evidence before committing budget. <br>
