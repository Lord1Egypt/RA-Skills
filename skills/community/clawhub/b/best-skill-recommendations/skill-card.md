## Description: <br>
Based on user goals, comprehensively evaluates candidate skill capabilities and conflict risks with installed skills, then delivers the best install recommendation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhaowh3613](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhaowh3613) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to compare ClawHub skill candidates, check compatibility with installed skills, and decide whether to install, replace, or coexist with explicit confirmation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Installing or uninstalling skills can change future agent behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Review the skill name, source, version, risk signals, and replace/coexist plan before explicitly approving any install or uninstall. <br>
Risk: Recommendations may misjudge overlap or compatibility with already installed skills. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's installed-skill compatibility review and confirm boundaries before adopting its recommendation. <br>
