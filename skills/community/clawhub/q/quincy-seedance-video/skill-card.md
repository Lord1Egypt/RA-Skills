## Description: <br>
Generates AI videos from text prompts or images with Seedance 2.0 on SkillBoss for short ads, product demos, launch clips, and social videos. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[quincygunter](https://clawhub.ai/user/quincygunter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to generate short-form Seedance videos through SkillBoss from text prompts, first-frame images, or reference images. It is suited for ads, product demos, launch clips, and social video formats. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts and reference image URLs are sent to SkillBoss for video generation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive, private, or rights-restricted material unless you have permission and understand SkillBoss handling. <br>
Risk: Generated video output may incur pay-as-you-go charges through SkillBoss. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the intended prompt, duration, aspect ratio, and API key use before making generation requests. <br>
