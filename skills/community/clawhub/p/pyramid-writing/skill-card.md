## Description: <br>
金字塔原理（Pyramid Principle）写作与结构化表达工作流。用于把散乱内容改写成“先结论、后论据”的可汇报文稿（方案评审、周报、邮件、PRD 段落、演讲稿、技术传播），输出关键结论、分组论点（MECE）、证据与行动项，并对照目标做最终评估。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external contributors, and developers use this skill to turn scattered notes into conclusion-first Chinese writing for reviews, reports, emails, PRD sections, presentations, and technical communication. It helps organize material into key messages, MECE arguments, evidence, risks, actions, and a goal-aligned final evaluation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on generic requests such as "structure this" and apply a heavier pyramid-writing workflow than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for or specify the desired language, format, audience, and whether the user wants the full pyramid workflow or a light rewrite. <br>
Risk: Restructured writing can make weak or unsupported claims appear more polished. <br>
Mitigation: Keep evidence gaps and assumptions visible as confirmation items, and review the final output before using it for decisions or external communication. <br>
