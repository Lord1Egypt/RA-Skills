## Description: <br>
Ai Gardening Advisor helps users choose and care for flowers, succulents, balcony vegetables, bonsai, landscape plants, and indoor greenery, including pest and disease guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Gardeners, plant hobbyists, and home growers use this skill for plant selection, routine care, balcony vegetable growing, bonsai shaping, garden planning, and general pest or disease troubleshooting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Plant photos may reveal private home, garden, or location details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid uploading images that include addresses, faces, documents, or other identifying details; remove sensitive image metadata when possible. <br>
Risk: Pesticide, pest-control, and plant-health advice may not reflect local labels, regulations, or site-specific safety needs. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as general guidance and verify them against product labels, local rules, and qualified experts before applying treatments. <br>
