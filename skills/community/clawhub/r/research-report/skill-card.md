## Description: <br>
Researches technical projects and papers and generates comprehensive Markdown or PDF reports in lite or full modes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huaruoji](https://clawhub.ai/user/huaruoji) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and technical researchers use this skill to analyze papers or code projects and produce structured research documentation, including iterative reports and optional PDF exports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Report, log, and memory files may persist research metadata or local project details. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated workspace, review generated files before sharing, and avoid broad or sensitive project paths. <br>
Risk: Full mode can involve environment setup, dependency installation, and experiment execution. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer lite mode unless experiments are required, and run full mode only in an isolated environment. <br>
Risk: The skill documentation describes Telegram delivery without clear opt-in. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enable Telegram or other external delivery unless the report contents are approved for sharing. <br>
