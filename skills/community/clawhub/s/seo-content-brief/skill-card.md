## Description: <br>
Creates SEO content briefs with keyword research, search intent analysis, SERP review, heading hierarchy, word-count targets, and internal linking strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[okaris](https://clawhub.ai/user/okaris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content marketers, SEO specialists, and writers use this skill to plan search-optimized articles and briefs before drafting. It guides keyword targeting, intent matching, competitor analysis, outline structure, word-count targets, and internal linking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill recommends installing an external CLI through a remote shell command. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer the documented manual download and checksum verification path before running the CLI. <br>
Risk: SEO research workflows may send confidential client strategy, unreleased product names, or sensitive internal URLs to external search and extraction apps. <br>
Mitigation: Remove confidential terms and sensitive URLs before using the external apps, or avoid those apps for restricted content. <br>
