## Description: <br>
Book notary services through Lokuli MCP. Use when user needs to find and book notary. Triggers on requests like "book a notary", "find notary near me", or any notary service request. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to search for notary services, check provider availability, and create bookings through Lokuli. Users should confirm appointment details and contact information before a booking is created. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Creating a booking may send appointment details and customer name, email, and phone number to Lokuli. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for explicit notary search or booking requests, and confirm provider, service, time, and contact details before creating an appointment. <br>
