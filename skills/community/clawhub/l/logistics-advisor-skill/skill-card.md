## Description: <br>
Provides EU logistics, tariff, customs-clearance, and cost-optimization guidance for Chinese cross-border e-commerce sellers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[luongkim20222022-crypto](https://clawhub.ai/user/luongkim20222022-crypto) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Cross-border e-commerce sellers and logistics operators use this skill to compare EU shipping channels, estimate tariff and VAT costs, prepare customs documents, and choose DDP, DDU, FBA, or overseas warehouse options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shipping, VAT, tariff, and customs conclusions may be incomplete or outdated for a specific shipment. <br>
Mitigation: Verify tax, VAT, and customs decisions against official sources or a qualified professional before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Shipment details can include sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the shipment details needed for the estimate and avoid unnecessary sensitive business data. <br>
