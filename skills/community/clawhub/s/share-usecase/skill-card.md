## Description: <br>
Share your OpenClaw use case to clawusecase.com by analyzing recent work and drafting a community submission for review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[josephl37](https://clawhub.ai/user/josephl37) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
OpenClaw users use this skill to turn recent assistant work into a reviewed use-case submission for clawusecase.com. It helps draft the title, hook, problem, solution, category, tools, requirements, and optional public attribution before publishing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recent chat context may contain secrets, client names, internal URLs, unreleased plans, or private business details that should not become public. <br>
Mitigation: Review and redact the generated use-case draft before submission; choose anonymous submission if public attribution is not desired. <br>
Risk: OAuth attribution attaches a public Twitter/X or GitHub identity to the published use case. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's attribution choice before submitting and use anonymous submission when the user does not want a public profile attached. <br>
