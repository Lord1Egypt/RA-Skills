## Description: <br>
Deal Genie Enhanced helps e-commerce operators turn a product screenshot or description into a full promotion workflow covering product scoring, positioning, compliance review, copywriting, creator matching, launch execution, follow-up conversion, data review, and optional Feishu sharing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[user-zyj](https://clawhub.ai/user/user-zyj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce operators, affiliate teams, and creator-commerce marketers use this skill to decide whether to promote a product, generate channel-specific materials, coordinate creator outreach and sample tracking, run launch-day execution, and review sales performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional Feishu sharing can expose product, sales, ROI, customer-feedback, and influencer-operation details to the configured group or document. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the webhook destination and audience before enabling Feishu, and avoid including regulated, confidential, or personal data unless that sharing is approved. <br>
