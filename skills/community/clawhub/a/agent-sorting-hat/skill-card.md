## Description: <br>
Diagnose agent role drift, team-structure problems, operating issues, and role misalignment; then redesign roles, boundaries, and team placement before persona refinement. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[child2d](https://clawhub.ai/user/child2d) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent-team designers use this skill to diagnose role drift, unclear boundaries, and team-structure problems before editing persona or role files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Role restructuring guidance may be applied too broadly or overwrite useful existing team boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Review the diagnosis, role map, handoffs, and change plan before applying edits to team or persona files. <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to inspect role, persona, team, and planning context, which can expose unrelated workspace content if reads are too broad. <br>
Mitigation: Limit file access to the relevant role files, team documents, registry or current-state documents, and a small number of comparison examples. <br>
