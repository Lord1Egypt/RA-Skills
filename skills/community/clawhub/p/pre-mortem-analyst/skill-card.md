## Description: <br>
Pre-Mortem Analyst helps users imagine that a project has already failed, then work backward to identify likely causes, mitigations, and warning signs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[artyomx33](https://clawhub.ai/user/artyomx33) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Project leads, founders, product teams, and operators use this skill before launch or major commitments to surface likely failure modes and convert the highest risks into prevention actions and monitoring signals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Example material contains named business scenarios that may bias a generic pre-mortem toward those contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt the examples before relying on the skill for unrelated projects. <br>
Risk: Pre-mortem outputs are speculative planning guidance and may overstate or miss important risks. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a discussion input and have relevant stakeholders validate the priority risks and prevention actions. <br>
