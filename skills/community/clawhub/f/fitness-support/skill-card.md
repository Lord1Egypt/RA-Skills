## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: MEMORY.md is prefilled with a named profile and may retain workout, injury, nutrition, sleep, stress, or preference details over time. <br>
Mitigation: Review or reset MEMORY.md before use, and only store personal fitness and lifestyle details that the user is comfortable retaining for future coaching. <br>
Risk: Workout and recovery guidance may be incomplete if the user's injuries, limitations, equipment, or medical context are not provided. <br>
Mitigation: Ask clarifying questions, adapt plans to stated limits, stop exercises that cause pain, and use qualified professional advice for injuries or medical concerns. <br>
