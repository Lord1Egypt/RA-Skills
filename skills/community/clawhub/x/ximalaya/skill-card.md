## Description: <br>
Provides lightweight summaries and analysis of public Ximalaya programs, albums, hosts, search results, leaderboards, and visible engagement metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and content teams use this skill to inspect public Ximalaya pages, summarize visible engagement metrics, compare host performance, and produce lightweight leaderboard or topic distribution summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the skill for login-only content, audio downloads, bulk scraping, API reverse engineering, or bypassing platform protections would exceed its stated boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to visible public pages, keep request rates low, and avoid downloads, hidden APIs, and bypass attempts. <br>
Risk: Dynamic rendering and human verification checks can make automated extraction incomplete, delayed, or inconsistent. <br>
Mitigation: Open pages manually when needed, capture source links and collection time, and treat extracted metrics as page-visible snapshots. <br>
