## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce sellers and marketing teams use this skill to draft product titles, detail-page copy, selling-point summaries, promotion copy, and competitor-copy analysis for major Chinese ecommerce platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may include unsupported claims, restricted advertising terms, or platform-specific compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Review copy against the included platform rules and substantiate claims such as sales volume, discounts, authenticity, health effects, and rankings before publication. <br>
Risk: Generated copy may misrepresent a product when the input product details are incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify final copy against product specifications, authorization status, pricing records, and promotion terms before use. <br>
