## Description: <br>
Substance use and behavioral addiction self-assessment and harm reduction guidance for people questioning their use, seeking an honest self-check, or looking for affordable treatment options. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and support agents use this skill to guide addiction and substance-use self-assessment, discuss harm reduction, identify urgent withdrawal risks, and find support resources without presenting the result as a diagnosis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may lead an agent to discuss or retain sensitive substance-use or behavioral-addiction details. <br>
Mitigation: Review filesystem and memory permissions before use, and avoid retaining sensitive details unless the user explicitly wants that context preserved. <br>
Risk: Self-assessment guidance can be mistaken for diagnosis or medical care. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for screening and harm-reduction support only, and direct users to qualified medical care for diagnosis, treatment decisions, dangerous withdrawal, overdose symptoms, or suicidal thoughts. <br>
Risk: Treatment resources, helplines, legal status, and cost guidance vary by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Localize resources to the user's country or region and avoid assuming US-specific drug scheduling, insurance, or treatment availability. <br>
