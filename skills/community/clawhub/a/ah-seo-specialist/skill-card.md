## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations can be misleading or harmful when based on incomplete ranking, analytics, site architecture, content, competitor, or backlink context. <br>
Mitigation: Require SEO context gathering before recommendations and validate proposed changes against current analytics, technical implementation, and measurable SEO metrics. <br>
Risk: Optimization work could encourage manipulative or low-quality SEO practices if outputs are applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the artifact's white-hat, user-first quality standards and review recommendations before deployment. <br>
