## Description: <br>
Helps users set up a hyperlocal mutual aid network with neighbors for community resilience, resource sharing, and emergency preparedness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan and launch a small neighborhood mutual aid network, including neighbor outreach, resource surveys, contact sheets, exchange systems, and lightweight ongoing coordination. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may prompt collection and circulation of sensitive neighbor contact, vulnerability, resource, and key-access information. <br>
Mitigation: Get explicit consent before collecting or sharing information, collect only what participants agree to share, and keep emergency needs and key or lockbox details off broadly shared sheets. <br>
Risk: Neighborhood contact and resource sheets could be distributed beyond intended participants or retained after information becomes outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Limit distribution to participants, store files securely, and delete or update records when someone opts out or moves. <br>
