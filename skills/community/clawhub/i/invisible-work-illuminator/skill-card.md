## Description: <br>
Turn messy effort, coordination, analysis, cleanup, and behind-the-scenes work into visible achievement language. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nickzhangai](https://clawhub.ai/user/nickzhangai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, managers, job seekers, and contributors use this skill to convert hidden coordination, analysis, cleanup, and ambiguity-reduction work into honest achievement language for weekly updates, manager syncs, performance reviews, project recaps, and resume bullets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated achievement language could overstate or misrepresent work when the user's source details are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review each statement against the actual work performed and keep the skill's no-fabricated-metrics rule in place. <br>
Risk: The default structure uses Chinese headings and examples, which may not fit every audience. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for English or another target language when preparing resume, status-update, or review language for that audience. <br>
