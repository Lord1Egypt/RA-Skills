## Description: <br>
Provides analysis and comparison of global AI regulations, safety incidents, model evaluations, standards, and international governance signals for AI governance and safety questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Policymakers, compliance officers, researchers, and governance teams use this skill to compare AI rules, track compliance milestones, analyze safety incidents, and frame governance or risk assessments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI regulation changes quickly, so compliance answers may become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important compliance conclusions against primary official sources and current effective dates before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The skill is an informational research aid and should not be treated as legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it to structure research and questions, then consult qualified counsel or authoritative regulators for binding compliance decisions. <br>
Risk: AI incident datasets may have reporting gaps or coverage bias. <br>
Mitigation: Treat incident trends as directional evidence and disclose likely underreporting or source coverage limits in analysis. <br>
