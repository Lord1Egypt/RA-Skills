## Description: <br>
Research venture capital firms, angel investors, and funding sources for startup fundraising and generate structured investor research reports with firm profiles, investment theses, portfolios, contact strategies, and warm-introduction paths. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wulaosiji](https://clawhub.ai/user/wulaosiji) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders and startup operators use this skill to identify, evaluate, prioritize, and approach relevant VC firms, angel investors, accelerators, and funding sources for a fundraising round. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Investor reports may contain confidential fundraising details if generated in a shared or sensitive directory. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the output path before running the skill and avoid shared directories for confidential company or fundraising information. <br>
Risk: Investor data, check sizes, partner names, and warm-introduction paths may be outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify investor details against current sources before outreach and mark uncertain information as needing confirmation. <br>
