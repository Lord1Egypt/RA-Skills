## Description: <br>
Make rapid pattern-based judgments without explicit reasoning, using Recognition-Primed Decision techniques and System 1 response patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to get rapid, pattern-based first impressions and concise judgments, with analytical mode reserved for explicit requests or low-confidence situations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fast, confident pattern-matching can be misleading in medical, legal, financial, security, or major business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to analyze carefully, compare options, explain uncertainty, and treat the intuitive read as a prompt for review rather than a decision. <br>
Risk: Calibration notes or outcome tracking could retain sensitive context if stored without a clear retention location. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing calibration notes unless users know where they are kept and have approved that retention. <br>
Risk: Pattern-based judgments can reflect bias or overfit unfamiliar situations. <br>
Mitigation: Switch to explicit analysis for novel domains, demographic or cultural judgments, and situations with conflicting signals. <br>
