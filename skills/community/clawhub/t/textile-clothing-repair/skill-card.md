## Description: <br>
Basic clothing repair and maintenance skills for fixing torn clothing, replacing buttons, hemming pants, patching holes, removing stains, ironing, and hand-washing delicates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Consumers and assistants use this skill to guide basic clothing repair and maintenance decisions, including sewing on buttons, fixing split seams, patching holes, hemming garments, managing zipper issues, treating stains, ironing, and hand-washing delicates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may damage garments or create safety hazards by applying stain removal, ironing, or hand-washing steps to unsuitable fabrics. <br>
Mitigation: Check the care label, test on a hidden area, avoid mixing cleaners unless compatibility is known, keep alcohol away from heat, and use a professional cleaner or tailor for valuable or uncertain garments. <br>
Risk: The skill gives text-only repair guidance and cannot inspect the garment directly. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for garment, fabric, damage, and stain details before selecting a method, and redirect complex, formal, leather, suede, or high-value repairs to a professional. <br>
