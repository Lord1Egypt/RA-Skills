## Description: <br>
软考系统架构设计师论文评分与诊断。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nieen](https://clawhub.ai/user/nieen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners and exam-preparation users use this skill to score and diagnose Ruankao system architect essays against a five-dimension rubric, including fit to prompt, depth, practicality, expression, and structure. It produces a structured score report and concrete improvement suggestions after confirming the essay topic when needed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Essay submissions may contain confidential or proprietary project details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive content unless the user is comfortable sharing it with the agent; keep file edits and rewrites explicitly user-directed. <br>
