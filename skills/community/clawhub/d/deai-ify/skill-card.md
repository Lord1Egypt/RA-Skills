## Description: <br>
Remove signs of AI-generated writing from text and revise writing to sound more natural and human-written. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hazy2go](https://clawhub.ai/user/hazy2go) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and reviewers use this skill to identify common AI-writing patterns, revise affected passages, and preserve the intended meaning and tone. It should be used transparently and not to evade authorship, academic, hiring, publishing, compliance, or disclosure rules. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be used to make AI-written text appear human-written, creating misrepresentation risk. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for transparent editing and do not use it to bypass AI-detection, authorship, academic, hiring, publishing, compliance, or disclosure rules. <br>
