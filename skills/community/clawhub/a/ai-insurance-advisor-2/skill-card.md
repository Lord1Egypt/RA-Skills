## Use Case: <br>
External users and insurance shoppers use this skill to compare insurance products, interpret policy clauses, prepare claim materials, estimate premiums, and plan family coverage. It provides informational assistance and should be checked against insurer terms before decisions are made. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Insurance guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or inconsistent with an insurer's official terms. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational support and verify product terms, health disclosures, exclusions, and claim requirements with official insurer documents before acting. <br>
Risk: The security guidance notes that maintainer-oriented workflows can affect production data or accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill only in an appropriate workspace with reviewed permissions and avoid granting access to production data unless the action has been explicitly approved. <br>
