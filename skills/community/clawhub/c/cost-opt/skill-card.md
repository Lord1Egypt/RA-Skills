## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cost recommendations such as rightsizing, reservation purchases, or guardrail changes can affect reliability or spend if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before acting, use small measurable changes, include rollback points where impact is high, and validate savings and reliability after each change. <br>
Risk: Missing environment, scale, deadline, or constraint details can lead to generic or incorrect optimization advice. <br>
Mitigation: Ask targeted context questions before strong recommendations and state trade-offs and assumptions explicitly. <br>
