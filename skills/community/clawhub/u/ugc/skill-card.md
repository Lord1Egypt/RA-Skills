## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and creators use this skill to plan UGC-style short-form marketing content, including hooks, creator briefs, platform-specific formats, calls to action, testing strategy, and performance metrics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing guidance could be used to make inaccurate or misleading product claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review all claims against product evidence and campaign requirements before publishing. <br>
Risk: UGC campaigns may misuse creator likeness, content rights, or endorsements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm creator consent, usage rights, sponsorship disclosures, and approval workflows before launch. <br>
Risk: Platform-specific content may violate advertising disclosure or platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Check each platform's advertising and disclosure requirements before posting or scaling content. <br>
