## Description: <br>
All-Market Financial Data Hub queries Eastmoney financial data across equities, funds, bonds, issuers, and related markets from natural-language prompts, then writes structured Excel and Markdown results. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Investors, analysts, researchers, and agents use this skill to answer natural-language financial data questions and collect market, valuation, company, and financial-statement tables for research, monitoring, review, and portfolio analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires an Eastmoney EM_API_KEY, which could be exposed through code, logs, prompts, or shared output files. <br>
Mitigation: Store the key only in the environment, rotate it if exposed, and avoid copying it into prompts, generated files, repositories, or logs. <br>
Risk: Financial responses are third-party market data and may be incomplete, delayed, or unsuitable as the sole basis for investment or compliance decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated tables against authoritative data sources before making financial, trading, audit, or credit decisions. <br>
Risk: Large multi-entity requests can produce partial results or large local Excel and Markdown files. <br>
Mitigation: Limit indicators and date ranges, split large entity lists into batches of no more than 500 entities, and review output files before sharing. <br>
