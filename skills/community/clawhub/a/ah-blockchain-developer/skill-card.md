## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Blockchain guidance can affect high-value assets or irreversible transactions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not share seed phrases or private keys, test on testnets before mainnet, independently review smart contracts, and only sign wallet transactions you understand. <br>
Risk: Generated smart contract examples or deployment guidance may contain defects or incomplete security assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Use comprehensive tests, external review, audit preparation, and security tools before production deployment. <br>
