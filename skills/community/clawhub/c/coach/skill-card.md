## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake coaching-style support for therapy, medical advice, legal advice, or crisis help. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for conversational coaching and action planning only; refer therapy, medical, legal, trauma, or crisis topics to qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Coaching conversations can involve sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal information unless the user is comfortable with how the agent context handles it. <br>
