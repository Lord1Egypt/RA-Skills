## Description: <br>
Design post-first-purchase onboarding sequences that educate customers, reduce returns, and drive repeat orders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, lifecycle, and ecommerce teams use this skill to create post-purchase onboarding playbooks with sequenced touchpoints, channel guidance, content briefs, KPIs, and testing plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated onboarding plans may recommend email, SMS, push, or marketing messages that are inappropriate for a jurisdiction, customer consent state, or privacy requirement. <br>
Mitigation: Review campaigns for applicable consent, unsubscribe, quiet-hour, privacy, marketing-law, and claims-substantiation requirements before launch. <br>
Risk: The release metadata includes crypto and purchase capability tags that are not supported by the Markdown-only artifact behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those capability tags as metadata noise unless the publisher provides separate evidence that the skill requires them. <br>
