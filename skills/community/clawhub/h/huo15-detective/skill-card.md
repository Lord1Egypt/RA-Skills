## Description: <br>
Guides agents through company and personnel background investigations, including platform selection, risk checks, relationship analysis, and report structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business users and agents use this skill for authorized company due diligence and personnel background checks, with structured steps for gathering registry data, identifying legal, operational, and capital risks, and preparing an investigation report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, subject to applicable privacy, employment screening, and investigative-use laws. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide collection and packaging of sensitive personal or business investigation details. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for authorized business due diligence or lawful investigations, confirm consent or another clear legal basis for private-person checks, and redact unnecessary personal identifiers before sharing reports. <br>
Risk: Background-check outputs may be incomplete, stale, or misleading if based on unverified registry or third-party platform data. <br>
Mitigation: Record source and access time for each data point, verify important findings against authoritative records or requested documents, and require human review before making decisions. <br>
