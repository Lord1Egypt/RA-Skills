## Description: <br>
Audits startup strategy analyses and red-team reports for unsupported claims, contradictions, hallucinated data, and distorted reasoning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[reactlab23](https://clawhub.ai/user/reactlab23) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Startup founders, product teams, and reviewers use this skill to stress-test strategy writeups before acting on them. It produces a deliberately skeptical second opinion that checks factual support, unit economics, legal assumptions, timelines, competition, and red-team distortions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intentionally harsh and pessimistic, so its output may overstate weaknesses or reject viable startup ideas. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the result as adversarial critique and compare it with independent evidence, balanced business analysis, and human judgment before making decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact metadata includes broad capability tags, but the security review describes the release as text-only and not able to spend money, access accounts, or handle crypto. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on the tags as functional capability claims; review the generated critique as text guidance only. <br>
