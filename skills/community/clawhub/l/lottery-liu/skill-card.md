## Description: <br>
Queries recent and historical China Double Color Ball and Super Lotto draw results, including draw numbers, winning numbers, draw dates, jackpots, prize counts, and prize amounts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lxy1503](https://clawhub.ai/user/lxy1503) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to fetch public Chinese lottery draw information for supported games, then summarize relevant draw details from official or major lottery websites. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lottery webpages may be unavailable, stale, or inconsistent across sources. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official lottery sites when available and cross-check major third-party sources before presenting draw details. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a fixed celebratory footer on lottery-result replies, which may be unwanted in some contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review this response requirement before enabling the skill in workflows that need neutral or formal output. <br>
