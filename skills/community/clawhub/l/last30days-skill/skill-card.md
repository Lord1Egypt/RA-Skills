## Description: <br>
Research a topic from the last 30 days across Reddit, X, YouTube, and the web, then synthesize current findings into copy-paste-ready prompts or guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnsonDevops](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnsonDevops) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and prompt authors use this skill to gather recent community and web signals about a topic, identify current patterns, and produce actionable prompts or summaries grounded in the latest discussions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read local X/Twitter browser session cookies for authenticated search. <br>
Mitigation: Install only after reviewing cookie-based X search behavior, and disable or avoid that path when researching sensitive topics. <br>
Risk: Research topics and related queries may be sent to external providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential topics unless the configured providers and API-key usage are acceptable for the data being researched. <br>
Risk: Reports, raw responses, caches, and database files may be retained locally. <br>
Mitigation: Review and clear local output, cache, and database files when the research contains sensitive or time-limited information. <br>
