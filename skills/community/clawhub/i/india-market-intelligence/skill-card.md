## Description: <br>
Comprehensive India-focused market intelligence tool that fetches real-time data on NIFTY, SENSEX, global indices, market news, geopolitical events, and provides AI-powered analysis with probability predictions, risk assessment, and trading decisions for Indian stock market. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and market analysts use this skill to fetch public India-focused and global market data, aggregate financial news, and generate informational market reports with technical indicators, risk scores, and decision-support guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill makes outbound requests to public finance and news providers, including ticker symbols or watchlist queries. <br>
Mitigation: Install and run it only where outbound requests to those providers are acceptable, and avoid submitting sensitive portfolio or watchlist data. <br>
Risk: Trading signals, risk scores, and recommendations may be incomplete, delayed, or misleading if treated as investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as educational market commentary, verify critical data with official sources, and consult qualified financial advisors before making investment decisions. <br>
