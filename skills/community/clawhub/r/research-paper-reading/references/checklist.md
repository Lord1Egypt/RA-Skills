# Paper Reading Checklist

## Pre-Reading Checklist
- [ ] Title clear and informative?
- [ ] Authors known in field?
- [ ] Publication venue reputable?
- [ ] Abstract well-structured?
- [ ] Appears relevant to my interests?

## First Pass Checklist
- [ ] Research question clearly stated?
- [ ] Gap in literature identified?
- [ ] Contributions explicitly listed?
- [ ] Methods appropriate?
- [ ] Results support conclusions?
- [ ] Figures/tables clear?

## Deep Reading Checklist
- [ ] Mathematical derivations understood?
- [ ] Experimental setup complete?
- [ ] Reproducibility considerations?
- [ ] Statistical significance assessed?
- [ ] Assumptions explicitly stated?
- [ ] Limitations acknowledged?

## Critical Analysis Checklist
- [ ] Novelty of contribution?
- [ ] Technical correctness?
- [ ] Comparison with prior work?
- [ ] Practical significance?
- [ ] Potential applications?
- [ ] Reproducible?

## Synthesis Checklist
- [ ] Connection to my research?
- [ ] Methods to adopt?
- [ ] Follow-up experiments?
- [ ] Citation for my work?
