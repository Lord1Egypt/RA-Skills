## Description: <br>
Trader Simulator helps users practice stock-market discussion and analysis by simulating multiple investor personas and synthesizing their views with market data and search results. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lucasxing1](https://clawhub.ai/user/lucasxing1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for educational trading practice, simulated stock discussion, and comparison of multiple investor-style perspectives across A-shares, Hong Kong stocks, U.S. stocks, and crypto-related topics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can use financial-data dependencies, web or social lookups, and stock-monitor integrations in ways that may affect user decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation before browser or social search, alert, watchlist, monitor setup, or new persona creation. <br>
Risk: Retrieved social content and simulated investment suggestions may be incomplete, misleading, or mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational material, present uncertainty and risk reminders, and have users independently verify information before making financial decisions. <br>
