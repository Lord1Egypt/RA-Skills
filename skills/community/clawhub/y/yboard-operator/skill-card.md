## Description: <br>
Operate yboard.online to navigate scenes, edit text fields, switch modes, and add clear, text-free hero visuals following YBoard illustration rules. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zonder](https://clawhub.ai/user/zonder) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and agents use this skill to operate a YBoard project by selecting scenes, switching editing modes, updating scene text fields, and adding or replacing simple readable illustrations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can change content in the active YBoard tab, including scene text and images. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with the intended project and scene selected, then review the resulting text and images before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Scene illustrations may become hard to read if generated visuals include overlapping or unnecessary text. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer text-free hero visuals and check that the selected scene, title, attached image, and visual readability are correct. <br>
