## Use Case: <br>
External shoppers use this skill to evaluate Temu listings, compare prices, analyze reviews, spot scam patterns, and plan shipping or returns before buying. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users could share Temu passwords, payment card details, or unnecessary private order information while asking for shopping help. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide credentials, full payment details, or private order data to the agent; use only the listing, review, pricing, and shipping details needed for the analysis. <br>
Risk: Shopping guidance may be wrong or incomplete when product listings, prices, reviews, shipping times, or return policies change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current listing details, seller policies, buyer photos, total costs, and dispute deadlines directly before purchasing. <br>
Risk: The skill can help identify scam patterns but cannot guarantee product authenticity, safety, delivery, or refund outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid high-risk categories and safety-critical purchases when authenticity or compliance matters, and keep documentation for any dispute. <br>
