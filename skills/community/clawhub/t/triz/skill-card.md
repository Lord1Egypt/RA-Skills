## Description: <br>
Helps engineers and product managers systematically analyze technical problems, define innovation directions from user experience insights, and generate TRIZ Five Bridges solution reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[homerzhu](https://clawhub.ai/user/homerzhu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and product managers use this skill to structure technical problem solving with TRIZ, classify contradictions, select relevant Five Bridges analyses, and produce actionable innovation recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scores, rankings, and solution labels may be mistaken for real engineering validation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as brainstorming and decision-support material that requires review by qualified domain experts before implementation. <br>
Risk: Installation adds a local triz command under the user's home directory. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in environments where a local TRIZ CLI helper is intended and review installed files before use. <br>
