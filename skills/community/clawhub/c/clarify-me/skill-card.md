## Description: <br>
Analyze user's conversation to identify unclear parts and suggest better questions. Helps users express their problems more clearly. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JDChi](https://clawhub.ai/user/JDChi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users invoke this skill to review recent conversation context, identify ambiguous wording or missing details, and get clearer ways to ask a question. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may quote recent chat text when explicitly invoked, which could repeat sensitive details from the conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid invoking it in conversations containing sensitive details that should not be repeated in the response. <br>
