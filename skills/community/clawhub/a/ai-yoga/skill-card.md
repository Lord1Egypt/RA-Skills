## Description: <br>
Ai Yoga provides general yoga guidance for styles, asanas, sequencing, pranayama, meditation, philosophy, alignment, props, pregnancy and postpartum practice, and yoga therapy topics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and yoga practitioners use this skill for general yoga education, pose guidance, sequence ideas, breathing practices, meditation, and wellness-oriented yoga therapy information. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat injury, pregnancy, breathing, pain, or recovery guidance as medical care. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as general wellness education and consult a qualified clinician or certified yoga therapist for medical, pregnancy, injury, breathing, blood pressure, balance, or post-surgery concerns. <br>
Risk: Yoga pose, breathing, or therapy suggestions may be unsuitable for a user's body, experience level, or environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review guidance before practice, adapt intensity conservatively, stop if discomfort occurs, and seek in-person instruction for advanced, inverted, therapeutic, or high-risk practices. <br>
