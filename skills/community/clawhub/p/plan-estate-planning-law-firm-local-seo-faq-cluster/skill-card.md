## Description: <br>
Researches local questions, groups them into clusters, and drafts launch-ready FAQ copy for an estate planning law firm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Estate planning attorneys, firm operators, and local SEO practitioners use this skill to research local search questions, organize FAQ clusters, and draft FAQ copy for estate planning law firm pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Firm and location research prompts may be sent to an external web search service. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include confidential client facts or sensitive firm information in prompts; use generalized business and location inputs. <br>
Risk: FAQ copy for legal-topic marketing can be incomplete, inaccurate, or mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human review all output before publishing it or sending it to clients. <br>
