## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to draft and adapt consensual romantic chat messages, date invitations, affectionate check-ins, humorous replies, and response options for dating conversations. It is intended for appropriate romantic contexts where the user reviews and personalizes the suggested wording before sending. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Romantic or flirtatious wording can be inappropriate or unwanted when used outside a consensual dating context. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for appropriate dating conversations, review messages before sending, avoid workplace, professional, minor, or clearly non-romantic contexts, and stop after clear refusal. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause romantic guidance to appear in ambiguous conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the relationship stage and romantic intent before applying the skill, and choose lower-risk, respectful responses when context is unclear. <br>
