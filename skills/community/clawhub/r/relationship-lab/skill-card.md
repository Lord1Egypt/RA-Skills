## Description: <br>
Support users with interpersonal communication practice, social scenario rehearsal, and relationship problem framing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to prepare for difficult conversations, rehearse social interactions, draft respectful scripts, and frame relationship or workplace communication challenges. It supports practice and communication planning, not therapy, diagnosis, crisis counseling, or guaranteed relationship outcomes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat communication rehearsal as therapy, diagnosis, crisis counseling, or a guarantee of relationship outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses framed as practice and communication support, and redirect high-risk safety or mental-health situations toward trusted people or relevant professional resources. <br>
Risk: Conversation scripts could be used to pressure, deceive, emotionally control, harass, or coerce another person. <br>
Mitigation: Refuse manipulative or coercive requests and keep guidance clear, respectful, consent-aware, and boundary-focused. <br>
Risk: Advice may miss context in emotionally intense or abuse-related situations. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only necessary clarifying questions, avoid overconfident conclusions, and prioritize safety concerns over ordinary communication coaching. <br>
