## Description: <br>
Autonomous Chinese tomb-adventure novel creation skill that coordinates outline development, drafting, review, revision, continuation, and final TXT formatting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kongzichixiangjiao](https://clawhub.ai/user/kongzichixiangjiao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creative-writing agents use this skill to generate Chinese tomb-adventure novel concepts, outlines, drafts, review notes, revisions, and a final TXT manuscript. It can continue from existing outlines or partial chapter files by detecting prior outputs and resuming the workflow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to make creative decisions without asking for clarification. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated outline and final manuscript before relying on or publishing the work. <br>
Risk: The skill can write multiple creative output files to a target directory. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in a dedicated workspace or output folder and avoid pointing it at sensitive directories. <br>
