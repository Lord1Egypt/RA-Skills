## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Deeper patent search can require a third-party Zhiyuya API key and may send search queries to that service. <br>
Mitigation: Keep API keys in environment variables or an untracked config file, and review third-party service terms before submitting sensitive search queries. <br>
Risk: Generated patent, trademark, or software copyright materials may be incomplete or unsuitable for filing as-is. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated materials as drafts and have a qualified reviewer check them before submission. <br>
