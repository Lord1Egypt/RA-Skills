## Description: <br>
Automatically performs paged web searches with progress updates, temporary intermediate-result handling, resume support, and final result merging. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhaokkkl](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhaokkkl) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and end users use this skill to run larger web-search tasks that need paged collection, intermediate progress handling, and merged search findings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Temporary search results may be written during processing, which can expose sensitive queries or result content. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive private searches unless the publisher clarifies the temporary file location, deletion behavior, and local temp-content handling. <br>
