## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese listed-company compliance guidance, checklists, and document templates for governance, disclosure, restructuring, shareholder reduction, internal control, audit oversight, ESG reporting, related-party transactions, and fundraising management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Board secretaries, securities affairs representatives, compliance teams, audit teams, and advisors use this skill to review Chinese listed-company compliance duties and draft supporting governance or disclosure documents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Compliance drafts and completed templates can contain sensitive listed-company records or personal identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize or mask personal identifiers where allowed, store drafts only in approved company systems, and restrict access to authorized personnel. <br>
Risk: Regulatory requirements may change after the markdown rule notes were prepared. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified legal or compliance staff verify the latest Chinese securities rules before relying on final documents. <br>
Risk: Template output could be mistaken for legal advice or a final filing-ready document. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafting support and require professional review before submission, disclosure, or board use. <br>
