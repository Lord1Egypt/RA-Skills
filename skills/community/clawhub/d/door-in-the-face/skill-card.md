## Description: <br>
Helps an agent recognize, design, and defend against door-in-the-face negotiation patterns based on reciprocal concession. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[deciqai](https://clawhub.ai/user/deciqai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers can use this skill to coach negotiation, fundraising, sales, or defensive decision-making scenarios where a large initial ask is followed by a smaller target request. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce persuasive negotiation guidance that may be used manipulatively. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for transparent, voluntary negotiation support and evaluate any proposed second ask on its own merits before acting. <br>
Risk: Overconfident application can confuse door-in-the-face patterns with ordinary anchoring or single-offer negotiation. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that there are two requests with a refusal between them before applying the technique or its defenses. <br>
