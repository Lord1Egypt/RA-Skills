## Description: <br>
Provides advisory WhatsApp private-domain operations planning under Meta compliance constraints, including account health diagnosis, user segmentation, lifecycle strategy, compliant message templates, and self-check reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
WhatsApp private-domain operators, cross-border ecommerce teams, outbound marketing teams, and SCRM practitioners use this skill to plan compliant account-health reviews, user segmentation, customer journeys, message templates, and operational self-checks before human execution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Operational plans could be mistaken for legal advice or Meta approval. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as advisory and verify Meta policy, consent records, opt-out handling, message category rules, sending volume, and local privacy law before use. <br>
Risk: Message cadence or template suggestions could be applied without adequate consent or human review. <br>
Mitigation: Require confirmed opt-in records and human approval before executing any outreach plan. <br>
