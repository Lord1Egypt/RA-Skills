Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated training material may contain inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading security guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review each lesson for technical accuracy, policy fit, and audience appropriateness before publishing or using it in training. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include credentials, employee personal data, confidential incidents, or unreleased business information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter sensitive information unless that use is approved in the deployment environment. <br>
