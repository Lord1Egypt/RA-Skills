## Description: <br>
Guide first-time PC builders through use case, CPU/GPU budget split, RAM, PSU wattage, cooler TDP, motherboard tier, and case airflow to get a complete, compatible component spec list -- brand-neutral, no sales bias. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to guide first-time PC builders or upgraders through component selection, compatibility checks, and practical purchasing-ready specifications. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may influence purchasing decisions for PC components and can produce unsuitable recommendations if the user's region, display target, thermal constraints, or reused parts are missing or wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Require the critical consultation inputs identified by the skill before finalizing a build, and have the user verify prices, availability, warranties, and compatibility with retailer or manufacturer specifications before purchase. <br>
Risk: The security guidance flags high-impact moderation and autoreview workflows in the reviewed release evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review the release evidence and operational scope before deployment, and avoid granting broad local or administrative access unless those workflows are intended and controlled. <br>
