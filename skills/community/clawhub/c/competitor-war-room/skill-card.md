## Description: <br>
Real-time competitive intelligence dashboard. Monitors competitor moves, pricing changes, feature launches, hiring signals, and market positioning across 20+ sectors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business, product, and strategy teams use this skill to monitor competitors, pricing, product launches, hiring signals, funding events, and market positioning. It can produce competitive intelligence summaries, SWOT-style comparisons, battle cards, alert severity guidance, and counter-strategy recommendations. <br>

Risk: The security summary says the skill routes prompts and payment credentials through unencrypted HTTP. <br>
