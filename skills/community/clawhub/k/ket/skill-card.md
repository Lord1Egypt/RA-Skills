### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Learner progress, mistakes, recordings, or speech-evaluation results could expose personal or educational data if an app built from this guidance stores them without clear controls. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose stored learner data, obtain consent before recording or AI-evaluating speech, minimize retention, and provide learner or guardian review and deletion controls. <br>
