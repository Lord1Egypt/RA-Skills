## Description: <br>
多智能体联合研究框架 Skill supports multi-domain expert agents collaborating on complex research projects, including task allocation, progress tracking, quality control, and result synchronization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lxg-bot](https://clawhub.ai/user/lxg-bot) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, analysts, and project teams use this skill to coordinate multi-agent research workflows for industry reports, feasibility analysis, market research, policy analysis, and multidimensional assessment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research results may be synchronized to a cloud storage location and could include sensitive files, notes, or source materials. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the storage destination before use, avoid uploading sensitive research unless approved, and keep sensitive work local or disable synchronization where possible. <br>
