## Description: <br>
An AI-powered Audiogram Creator that helps transform podcasts, interviews, and voice notes into visual audio content for social media. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mtsatryan](https://clawhub.ai/user/mtsatryan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, podcast teams, and developers use this skill to plan audiogram generation workflows, captions, audio processing, branding, templates, and platform-specific social video exports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audiogram workflows may involve private recordings, transcripts, or brand assets. <br>
Mitigation: Review upload destinations, retention behavior, and access controls before connecting any renderer, transcription service, scheduler, or social posting integration. <br>
Risk: Generated clips, captions, or social post plans may misrepresent the original audio if timestamps, transcript text, or context are wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human review captions, timing, selected clips, and final exports before publication. <br>
