## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading-related workflows may be mistaken for production or live-trading authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the requested action, credentials, account scope, and whether the workflow is research-only, paper trading, or production before executing live trading, deployment, or external-data steps. <br>
Risk: Backtests, factor analysis, and AI strategy workflows can overfit historical data or produce misleading investment conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Use out-of-sample testing and causal validation before relying on results, and keep final investment or trading decisions under qualified human review. <br>
