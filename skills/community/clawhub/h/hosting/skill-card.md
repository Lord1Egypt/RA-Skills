## Use Case: <br>
Website owners, developers, and small teams use this skill to choose hosting options, understand platform tradeoffs, plan DNS and backups, and avoid common hosting mistakes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Hosting provider terms, prices, limits, and service availability may change after the guidance is written. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current provider terms, pricing, usage limits, and backup options before purchasing or migrating hosting. <br>
Risk: Hosting configuration may involve secrets such as environment variables, API keys, DNS credentials, or database credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Keep secrets out of repositories and configure them only through trusted provider dashboards or secret-management systems. <br>
