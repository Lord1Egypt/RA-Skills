## Description: <br>
Generate a daily or weekly cybersecurity threat briefing from open sources. Covers new vulnerabilities, active exploits, ransomware campaigns, APT activity, and industry-relevant threats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[solomonneas](https://clawhub.ai/user/solomonneas) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External security practitioners and teams use this skill to generate concise daily or weekly cybersecurity briefings covering vulnerabilities, active exploitation, ransomware, APT activity, and prioritized recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Threat-intelligence summaries can be incomplete, outdated, or wrong if source material is not verified. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited threat-intelligence sources before taking operational action. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate the skill on general security-news prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants a structured threat briefing before applying the full briefing format. <br>
