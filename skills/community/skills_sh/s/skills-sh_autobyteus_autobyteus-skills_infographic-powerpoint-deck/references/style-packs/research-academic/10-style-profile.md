Style Pack: research-academic (research academic)
- Favor structured split, framework, comparison, and didactic board layouts; restrained full-bleed slides are acceptable for short section openers or quote transitions.
- Use a neutral gray-white base with deep-blue accents and avoid emotional high-saturation color.
- Typography attitude: academic sans or restrained editorial-serif headline with disciplined body text, exact labels, and evidence-friendly hierarchy.
- Surface/material cues: paper-white poster fields, matte seminar-room surfaces, precise divider rules, and clean diagram backdrops.
- Composition attitude: strongly structured and evidence-friendly, but not necessarily boxed. On dense slides, use crisp zones, dividers, and modules; panels or cards are acceptable when helpful, but implied grouping is equally valid when it reads better. On full-bleed slides, place short text directly on the image with minimal tonal support and no obvious caption box.
- Keep the lighting even and natural, with minimal atmosphere effects.
- Favor libraries, laboratories, seminar rooms, or abstract infographic layers.
