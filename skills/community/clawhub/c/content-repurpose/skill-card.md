## Description: <br>
Transforms one long-form source into platform-tailored drafts for X/Twitter, LinkedIn, blogs, short videos, and newsletters while preserving the source's voice, claims, and intent. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and agents use this skill to turn pasted text or plain-text files into copy-ready social and marketing variants while preserving source claims and style. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private or sensitive information in source material could be carried into generated posts. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide content the agent is allowed to read, avoid private PII, and review generated drafts before publishing. <br>
Risk: Copyrighted or paywalled material may be repurposed without sufficient rights. <br>
Mitigation: Use source material the user owns or is authorized to reuse, or limit use to confirmed fair-use commentary. <br>
Risk: Generated platform drafts could misstate the source's claims or imply authorship. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against the source, preserve claim boundaries, and do not publish drafts that impersonate the source author. <br>
