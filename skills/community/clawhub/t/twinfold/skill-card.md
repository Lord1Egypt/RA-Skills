## Description: <br>
Create, adapt, schedule, publish, and analyze AI-generated social media content across 10 platforms in 13 languages using Twinfold's API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lpbaril](https://clawhub.ai/user/lpbaril) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to operate a Twinfold account from an agent: creating drafts, adapting posts by platform and language, scheduling or publishing content, managing autopilot review, and checking credits, notifications, trends, and analytics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can publish AI-generated content to connected public social accounts and perform account-changing actions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval after showing the exact content, target platforms, accounts, timing, and credit cost before publishing, scheduling, autopilot approval, deletion, brand changes, or knowledge updates. <br>
Risk: Content generation and autopilot workflows may consume Twinfold credits. <br>
Mitigation: Check available credits before heavy operations such as post, article, image, hook, brand guide, brand voice, or Twin query generation. <br>
