## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for explicitly Christian, Bible-based spiritual comfort, prayers, Bible questions, and emotional companionship. It is intended for pastoral-style encouragement and devotional support, not for emergency, medical, or mental health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake Christian comfort responses for emergency, psychiatric, medical, or serious-illness care. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for spiritual encouragement and route emergencies, self-harm thoughts, acute psychiatric distress, medical decisions, or serious illness to local emergency services, crisis resources, licensed clinicians, or trusted pastoral care. <br>
Risk: The skill is explicitly Christian and likely Chinese-language, which may be unsuitable for users seeking secular or non-Christian support. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only in contexts where Bible-based Christian comfort, prayer, and devotional guidance are desired and clearly disclosed. <br>
