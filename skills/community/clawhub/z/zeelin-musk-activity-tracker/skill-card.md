## Description: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to gather and structure recent public information about Elon Musk's X activity, company announcements, and media appearances. It is intended to produce a clear, topic-grouped activity report from current sources. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is focused on recent public activity, so stale search results or weak sources can produce misleading summaries. <br>
Mitigation: Constrain searches to the requested 10-day window, include dates, and prioritize X posts, company channels, and reputable wire or financial news sources. <br>
Risk: Security evidence marks the release as suspicious because related maintainer-oriented workflows may bypass sandbox or approval controls. <br>
Mitigation: Review the exact artifact before use and avoid any sandbox-bypass or automatic local command workflows unless the repository and commands are trusted. <br>
