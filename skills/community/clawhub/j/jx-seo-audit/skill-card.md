## Description: <br>
SEO specialist agent with site audits, content writing, keyword research, technical fixes, link building, and ranking strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and teams use this skill to plan and review SEO work across site audits, on-page improvements, content strategy, keyword research, local SEO, schema markup, and link strategy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations can be incorrect, outdated, or harmful if applied without checking site-specific evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against Search Console data, site analytics, current search results, and the site's business goals before implementation. <br>
Risk: Optional local files under ~/seo/ may contain client, site, ranking, backlink, or content-planning data. <br>
Mitigation: Store only necessary SEO notes, avoid sensitive credentials in the workspace, and grant browser, Search Console, or website access only for a specific task. <br>
