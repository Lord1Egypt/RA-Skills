## Use Case: <br>
Developers and Home Assistant users use this skill for quick reference guidance across setup, patterns, troubleshooting, performance, security, migration, and common workflows. Treat it as general reference material rather than a complete Home Assistant integration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the generic reference output as a full Home Assistant integration or authoritative operational procedure. <br>
Mitigation: Review the guidance against the target Home Assistant environment and authoritative project documentation before applying it to production systems. <br>
