## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-27
Initial release. Six-phase commercial appraisal narrative workflow aligned to the USPAP 2026–2027 edition (effective January 1, 2026) covering engagement intake (client, intended user, intended use, type of value, effective date of value, property rights appraised, jurisdictional exception); subject identification and inspection log; market and neighborhood analysis; highest-and-best-use four-tests analysis (legally permissible, physically possible, financially feasible, maximally productive) as-vacant and as-improved with the explicit reconciliation when the conclusions differ; Sales Comparison Approach (comparable selection, adjustment grid, reconciliation to value indication); Income Capitalization Approach with direct capitalization (PGI, V&C, EGI, OpEx, NOI, OAR, capitalized value) and DCF (lease-by-lease rollover, market rent, V&C trend, OpEx growth, discount rate, terminal cap rate, IRR, NPV); Cost Approach (site value, RCN, depreciation by source, value indication) or an explicit Standards-Rule 2-2(a)(viii) exclusion justification; reconciliation of value indications to the final value opinion; USPAP-compliant certification with the appraiser's signature block (UNSIGNED in DRAFT); assumptions and limiting conditions; and addenda for comps, photos, surveys, leases, expense statements, qualifications, and the engagement letter. DRAFT only, for the Certified General appraiser to verify, certify, and sign.
