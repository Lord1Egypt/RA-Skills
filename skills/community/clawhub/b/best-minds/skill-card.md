## Description: <br>
Guides the agent to answer by identifying the most knowledgeable people for a question and simulating their publicly grounded perspectives. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ru63num07](https://clawhub.ai/user/ru63num07) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can invoke this skill when they want an answer framed through the best available experts for a topic, grounded in those experts' public writing and statements rather than a generic assistant opinion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill encourages simulated expert views and quoted attributions that may be inaccurate or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Verify any quotes, citations, and attributions before relying on the output, especially for important decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording can cause top-expert framing to be applied to a wide range of prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Install or enable the skill only when that answer style is desired for broad expert or world-class-view prompts. <br>
