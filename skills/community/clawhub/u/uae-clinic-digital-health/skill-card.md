## Use Case: <br>
External clinic operators, healthcare consultants, and implementation teams use this skill to structure UAE-specific digital health guidance for EMR, telemedicine, AI workflow, patient portal, and health information exchange decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United Arab Emirates (UAE) <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: UAE healthcare regulations and vendor certifications can change by emirate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify regulatory and vendor claims with current official sources before acting on procurement, launch, or clinical workflow guidance. <br>
Risk: Clinic and patient data is sensitive health information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing real patient data unless intentionally required, and follow clinic privacy, consent, and access-control procedures. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate the skill in general clinic-software conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user needs UAE clinic digital-health guidance before applying UAE-specific recommendations. <br>
