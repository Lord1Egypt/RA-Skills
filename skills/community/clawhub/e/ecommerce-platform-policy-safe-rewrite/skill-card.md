## Description: <br>
Rewrites ecommerce ads, creator scripts, landing page lines, and product claims into safer platform-friendly language while preserving the selling angle. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Growth teams, ecommerce marketers, and creators use this skill to rewrite risky ads, landing page copy, creator scripts, and product claims into safer platform-friendly variants while preserving the selling angle. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat safer marketing copy as legal or regulatory approval. <br>
Mitigation: Present rewrites as marketing risk-reduction guidance and require formal review for legal, regulatory, or high-stakes claims. <br>
Risk: Platform rules and claim standards may change or vary by channel, product category, and geography. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important product claims against real evidence and current platform rules before publishing. <br>
Risk: Weak source evidence can still leave rewritten claims misleading or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Surface remaining watch-outs, soften claims where evidence is weak, and avoid implying legal certainty. <br>
