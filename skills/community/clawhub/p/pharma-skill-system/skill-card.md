## Description: <br>
Pharma Skill System is a markdown reference skill for pharmaceutical workflows across intelligence, content production, events, analytics, knowledge management, compliance, KOL operations, workflow coordination, regulatory affairs, and supply chain production. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Pharma employees, consultants, and agents use this skill as a structured workflow reference for planning and drafting regulated pharmaceutical tasks, including market intelligence, medical content, events, analytics, compliance, regulatory submissions, KOL engagement, and supply chain processes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers medical, pharmacovigilance, regulatory, compliance, HR, financial, and external-publication work where incorrect drafts could create business or regulatory harm. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated outputs as drafts requiring review by qualified human experts before operational, medical, regulatory, or external use. <br>
Risk: The workflows may involve patient-identifiable data, adverse-event reports, HCP contact details, employee data, CRM exports, confidential regulatory materials, contracts, or budgets. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in an approved environment and do not provide sensitive or confidential data unless organizational policy permits that AI use. <br>
