## Description: <br>
Scan for bounties, gigs, freelance opportunities, and revenue lanes across platforms like ClawHub, GitHub, X, and web. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and independent operators use this skill to find, triage, and track revenue opportunities such as bounties, gigs, freelance work, and publishable ClawHub skills. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad autonomy wording could lead an assistant to search across too many sources or pursue opportunities outside the user's intent. <br>
Mitigation: Set clear source, platform, value, effort, and fit limits before running the skill. <br>
Risk: The skill may suggest posting, publishing, applying to opportunities, contacting people, or maintaining an ongoing tracked list. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before outreach, publication, applications, or persistent tracking. <br>
