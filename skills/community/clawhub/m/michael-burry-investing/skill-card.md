## Description: <br>
A Michael Burry-inspired contrarian value investing skill that helps agents reason about bubbles, margin of safety, and concentrated bets against consensus. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[talentedleo](https://clawhub.ai/user/talentedleo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill as an educational investing lens for analyzing market bubbles, value-investing questions, and contrarian theses in a Michael Burry-inspired style. It should not be treated as personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Investing outputs may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational only and verify decisions with current sources and appropriate financial professionals. <br>
Risk: Market claims and Burry-related references may be stale or incomplete for current decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Check current market data, filings, and primary sources before relying on any analysis. <br>
Risk: Broad finance triggers may invoke this persona in ordinary finance discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill when the user wants a Michael Burry-inspired or contrarian value-investing lens. <br>
