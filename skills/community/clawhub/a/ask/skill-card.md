## Description: <br>
Ask helps agents ask better clarifying, reframing, assumption-surfacing, decision-forcing, and root-cause questions before offering advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGImodel](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGImodel) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People using an agent for decisions, problem exploration, or critical conversations use this skill to surface the real question, test assumptions, and decide whether to act or analyze further. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may slow direct problem solving by asking structured follow-up questions before giving advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for reflective decisions and problem exploration, and switch to direct assistance when immediate action is required. <br>
