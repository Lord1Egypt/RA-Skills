## Description: <br>
Generates draft privacy policy text for web or app use cases, including GDPR- and CCPA-oriented variants. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain-lab](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain-lab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill to generate draft privacy policy language for websites or apps, including GDPR and CCPA variants. Generated policies require legal and operational review before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated privacy policies may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific organization, jurisdiction, or data-processing practice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated output as a draft and have qualified legal and operational reviewers verify it before use. <br>
Risk: The release bundles an unrelated security-tool script that can give misleading results and stores user inputs locally. <br>
Mitigation: Review which bundled script the agent will execute, avoid passing secrets or confidential details, and inspect local data writes before installation. <br>
