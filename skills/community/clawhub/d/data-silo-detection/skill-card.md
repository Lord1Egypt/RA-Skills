## Description: <br>
Detect and map data silos in construction organizations, including disconnected data sources, duplicate data, and integration opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[datadrivenconstruction](https://clawhub.ai/user/datadrivenconstruction) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Construction data and technology teams use this skill to analyze organization data sources, identify isolated systems and duplicated entities, and plan integration improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reports may expose internal system names, owners, users, and integration gaps. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the local files or project data intended for analysis, and handle generated reports as sensitive internal material. <br>
Risk: Recommendations can affect construction data integration plans if adopted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review findings with responsible construction data, security, and operations stakeholders before using them to change systems or processes. <br>
