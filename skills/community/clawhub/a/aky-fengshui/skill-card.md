## Description: <br>
Provides Feng Shui-style analysis for homes, offices, directions, and layouts using Eight Mansions, Flying Stars, and Forms School references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can request traditional Feng Shui-style assessments for homes, offices, directions, floor plans, and room layouts. The skill structures cultural guidance using Eight Mansions, Flying Stars, Forms School, and interior layout references. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recommendations may be mistaken for factual or professional building, medical, legal, or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as cultural guidance and ask users to consult qualified professionals for safety, health, legal, financial, or building decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may frame general interior-layout questions as Feng Shui analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify when a user wants Feng Shui-style interpretation before applying the full analysis workflow. <br>
