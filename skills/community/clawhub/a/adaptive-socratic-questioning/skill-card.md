## Description: <br>
Uses adaptive Socratic follow-up questions to deepen student reasoning, uncover misconceptions, and scaffold research or academic problem-solving. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[perpetualhui](https://clawhub.ai/user/perpetualhui) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Educators, researchers, and learning-focused agent users use this skill to turn a learner's answer or research idea into adaptive follow-up questions, misconception checks, reasoning paths, and teacher guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may invoke Socratic questioning where a direct answer is more appropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for learning, academic, research, and reasoning-development contexts; avoid urgent, safety-sensitive, counseling, debugging, or straightforward factual tasks. <br>
Risk: Generated question chains may be incomplete or misaligned without domain context. <br>
Mitigation: Have a teacher or subject-matter reviewer check questions, misconception flags, and guidance before relying on them with learners. <br>
