This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, ecommerce sellers, and social media operators use this skill to generate batches of Xiaohongshu/RED-style Chinese titles from a topic, audience, quantity, and style preference. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on generic title-generation prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants Xiaohongshu/RED-style title generation before applying the workflow. <br>
Risk: Marketing-title inputs may include private or sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to omit or anonymize sensitive details before generating titles. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing titles can become misleading or overclaim results if not reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against the artifact's quality checks for prohibited absolute claims, keyword fit, length, and clickbait risk. <br>
