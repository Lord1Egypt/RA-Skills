## Description: <br>
Design a practical contract-signing workflow with Nota Sign for approvals, signer roles, signing order, recipient verification, templates, and audit trail planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[notasign](https://clawhub.ai/user/notasign) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users and operations teams use this skill to design practical signing workflows for contracts, NDAs, offers, procurement files, and other business agreements. It helps organize approvers, signers, verification needs, templates, recordkeeping, and pre-send checks without providing legal advice or executing contracts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat workflow suggestions as legal advice for important agreements. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as workflow guidance only and have legal counsel review important agreements before sending or signing. <br>
Risk: Contract text or personal data may be shared in prompts without appropriate approval. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential contract text or personal data unless workspace policies allow it. <br>
Risk: Incorrect signer roles, signing order, or verification choices could delay or weaken a signing process. <br>
Mitigation: Review the proposed workflow, role table, checklist, and verification recommendations before using them operationally. <br>
