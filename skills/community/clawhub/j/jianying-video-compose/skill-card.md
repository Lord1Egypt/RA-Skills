## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Chosen media and text may be sent to disclosed Eastmoney/Jianying proxy endpoints. <br>
Mitigation: Use only content appropriate for that service until the service owner, access controls, retention and deletion policy, and HTTPS media URL support are verified. <br>
