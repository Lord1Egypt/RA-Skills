# Content Strategy

Use this file for positioning, funnel logic, and turning business context into content direction.

## Start with three anchors
1. Audience: who should care
2. Offer: what value is actually being sold
3. Goal: what business result the content should support

## Common content goals
- Reach: increase qualified awareness
- Saves: create reusable value
- Replies: trigger discussion or inbound interest
- Leads: move people toward inquiry or demo
- Conversion: push a clear next action

## Translate offer into content
Map the offer into one or more of these angles:
- pain point
- misconception
- method/framework
- before/after contrast
- case pattern
- founder/operator lesson
- checklist or template

## Funnel mapping
- Top of funnel: pain awareness, myths, patterns, broad relevance
- Middle of funnel: process explanation, comparisons, use cases, trust-building
- Bottom of funnel: proof, fit, objections, CTA, decision support

## Positioning checks
Before generating content, ask:
- What should this audience remember?
- What should they believe after reading?
- What action should become more likely?
- What must remain consistent with brand positioning?
