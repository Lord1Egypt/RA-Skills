Plan Paraguay trips with border-savvy routing, river-city priorities, and practical guidance for Asuncion, Encarnacion, Ciudad del Este, and the Chaco. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to plan Paraguay itineraries, choose routes and bases, and handle practical logistics for cities, border crossings, payments, transport, food, family travel, and seasonality. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local trip memory may retain travel preferences, constraints, and trip history in ~/paraguay/memory.md. <br>
Mitigation: Review or delete ~/paraguay/memory.md if those notes should not be retained locally. <br>
Risk: Travel conditions, opening hours, border rules, and customs requirements can change after the skill content is released. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm time-sensitive logistics and legal requirements against current official or local sources before finalizing plans. <br>
