## Description: <br>
Calculates, benchmarks, and diagnoses B2B SaaS metrics from user-provided business numbers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, CFOs, and SaaS operators use this skill to turn raw business inputs such as MRR, churn, CAC, gross margin, headcount, and burn into benchmarked metrics, health flags, and prioritized action items. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users to provide confidential business metrics such as revenue, churn, burn, CAC, and gross margin. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data approved for agent processing, avoid sharing unnecessary sensitive details, and review generated guidance before relying on it for business decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact links to external AfrexAI products that were outside this skill review. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate linked products separately before installing, purchasing, or using them in business workflows. <br>
