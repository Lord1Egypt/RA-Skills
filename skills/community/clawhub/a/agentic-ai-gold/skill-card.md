Mitigation: Treat those claims as unverified until the publisher supplies implementation code, tests, logs, and reproducible validation evidence. <br>

Mitigation: Evaluate in an isolated environment, pin and review dependencies, and confirm installation results before granting sensitive data or production tool access. <br>

Mitigation: Require explicit approval, least-privilege credentials, logging, rollback controls, and human review before enabling unattended write or tool privileges. <br>
