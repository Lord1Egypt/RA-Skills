## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language travel planning assistant that helps users plan trips, evaluate destinations, check travel conditions, recommend attractions, transportation, lodging, food, safety considerations, and packing lists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhuyifeiruichuang](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhuyifeiruichuang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to generate personalized travel plans for China-focused travel, including destination analysis, itinerary guidance, transportation and lodging recommendations, food suggestions, health and safety notes, and packing checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for sensitive health and personal details as part of travel planning. <br>
Mitigation: Collect only details necessary for the specific trip, explain why they are needed, and avoid storing or repeating sensitive information beyond the planning context. <br>
Risk: Travel health, medication, airline, and destination-entry advice may be incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Tell users to verify medical, medication, airline, and destination-entry requirements with qualified professionals or official sources before relying on the plan. <br>
