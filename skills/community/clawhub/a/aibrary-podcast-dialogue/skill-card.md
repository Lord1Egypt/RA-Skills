## Description: <br>
Generates two-speaker podcast scripts where a host and guest expert discuss a book's ideas in natural dialogue. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[asoiso](https://clawhub.ai/user/asoiso) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to turn a book title, author, focus areas, and speaker preferences into a conversational podcast script. It is intended for book discussions in a host-and-guest format rather than single-narrator summaries or debate scripts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated dialogue may include inaccurate or unsupported claims about a book. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims before publication, especially for unfamiliar or ambiguous books. <br>
Risk: Private or unpublished book content provided as prompt input may be exposed to the agent runtime. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid supplying sensitive manuscripts or private notes unless the user is comfortable using them as prompt input. <br>
