## Description: <br>
AI fashion styling assistant for outfit guidance, color coordination, body-shape styling, beauty advice, wardrobe organization, shopping recommendations, and trend-aware style support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to get fashion and beauty guidance for personal style diagnosis, outfit planning, wardrobe management, and shopping decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share identifiable face or full-body photos while requesting fashion advice. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer text descriptions, measurements, and clothing lists; crop backgrounds and identifying details when photos are necessary. <br>
