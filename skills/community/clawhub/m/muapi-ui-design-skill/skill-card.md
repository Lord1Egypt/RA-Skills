## Description: <br>
Generate high-fidelity UI/UX mockups for mobile and web apps using Atomic Design principles, including wireframes and design systems via muapi.ai. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Anil-matcha](https://clawhub.ai/user/Anil-matcha) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and product teams use this skill to turn product briefs into structured UI mockup prompts for mobile or web interfaces. It helps specify layout patterns, design tokens, accessibility constraints, and image-generation settings for high-fidelity interface concepts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Design briefs may contain confidential product plans, customer details, credentials, or other sensitive information that could be sent to an image-generation provider. <br>
Mitigation: Review and remove sensitive or unreleased information from the brief before invoking the image-generation helper. <br>
Risk: Generated UI mockups may include inaccurate, inaccessible, or misleading visual details. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human reviewer check the output for accessibility, brand fit, and product accuracy before using it in design or stakeholder workflows. <br>
