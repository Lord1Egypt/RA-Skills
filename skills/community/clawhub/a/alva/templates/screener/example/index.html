README

Overview in Plain English

This strategy screens the US mid-cap universe (market cap $5B–$50B) for companies showing fundamental inflection: positive revenue growth, four+ quarters of gross-margin data, and gross-margin acceleration in at least two of the last four quarters.

The remaining companies are scored using Growth (revenue, 40%) + Margin (gross-margin acceleration, 40%) + Safety (operating margin + current ratio, 20%) to produce a composite score from 0–100. The top 40 are output with red flag annotations.

20%
growthScore = normalize( revenue growth YoY ) // Higher = better
marginScore = normalize( avg GM Δ over last 4 quarters ) // Higher = accelerating
safetyScore = 0.5 × norm(opMargin) + 0.5 × norm(currentRatio) // Stability
composite = 0.40 × growthScore + 0.40 × marginScore + 0.20 × safetyScore
score = clamp(0, 100, round( 45 + 50 × (composite − min) / (max − min) ))
