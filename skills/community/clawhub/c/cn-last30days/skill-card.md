## Description: <br>
Researches recent discussion across Xiaohongshu, Douyin, and WeChat Official Accounts, then produces cross-platform Chinese social-media trend and sentiment reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[redfox-data](https://clawhub.ai/user/redfox-data) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, brand operators, and market researchers use this skill to study Chinese social-media topics, compare brands or entities, and summarize high-engagement discussion from the last 30 days. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research topics, brand names, or competitor queries may be sent to RedFox and web search providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential or personal data in prompts and use a revocable personal REDFOX_API_KEY. <br>
Risk: The skill writes local JSON and HTML reports and may open the HTML report automatically. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in an appropriate workspace, review generated files before sharing, and treat report paths as local artifacts. <br>
