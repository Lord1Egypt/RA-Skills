## Description: <br>
Researches markets with sizing, segmentation, competitor mapping, pricing checks, and demand validation to turn early market questions into decision-ready evidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[subaru0573](https://clawhub.ai/user/subaru0573) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, operators, analysts, and investors use this skill to size markets, compare competitors, validate demand, assess pricing, and decide whether to enter, launch, expand, or avoid a market opportunity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bundled metadata and registry metadata do not fully match. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the publisher, listing, slug, and version before relying on the release. <br>
Risk: Market research conclusions can become misleading when based on stale external data or unsupervised outreach. <br>
Mitigation: Require current sources for live market claims, supervise real-world outreach, and keep confidence labels and assumptions attached to major conclusions. <br>
