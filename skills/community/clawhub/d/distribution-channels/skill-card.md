## Description: <br>
Guides product distribution planning across third-party marketplaces, app stores, and platform storefronts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, founders, marketers, and developers use this skill to choose marketplace and app-store distribution channels, optimize listings, and map product types to suitable third-party platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace sizing, procurement, and platform-fit guidance may become outdated or may not match a specific product. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market data, listing rules, pricing options, and partner requirements with the target marketplace before acting. <br>
Risk: Pinterest Product Pins require Product Schema or Pinterest Catalog support before the channel is viable. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the product site or feed supports the required schema or catalog setup before prioritizing Pinterest. <br>
