## Description: <br>
Carbon Tracker Pro helps individuals and businesses estimate carbon emissions, plan reductions, track ESG metrics, and prepare sustainability reporting guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals, sustainability teams, and small or midsize businesses use this skill to estimate Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions, review energy use, plan carbon reduction steps, and draft ESG or climate disclosure guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide unnecessary personal or business-sensitive utility, travel, energy, or procurement details while seeking carbon calculations. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum data needed for estimation, aggregate sensitive records where possible, and avoid entering confidential identifiers. <br>
Risk: Emissions estimates, ESG disclosures, or offset recommendations may be inaccurate if inputs, factors, or assumptions are outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify emission factors, assumptions, offset project quality, and formal disclosures with qualified reviewers or third-party assurance before reporting. <br>
