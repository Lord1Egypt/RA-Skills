## Description: <br>
Transforms short-term rental details into optimized Airbnb and VRBO listing copy, photo captions, house rules, FAQs, and automated guest message templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Short-term rental hosts, property managers, co-hosts, real estate investors, and hospitality operators use this skill to turn property details into publication-ready Airbnb or VRBO listing materials and scheduled guest communication templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Guest communication outputs may include real WiFi credentials, lockbox codes, smart-lock details, parking instructions, or other property access information. <br>
Mitigation: Review and replace sensitive access details before publishing or installing templates; share check-in details only through private, confirmed-guest channels and rotate any exposed credentials. <br>
