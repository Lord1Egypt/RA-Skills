## Description: <br>
Build an agent-powered organization by mapping functions to skills and iterating on structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business owners and operators use this skill to plan an agent-assisted organization: identify business functions, map them to existing or custom skills, sequence rollout, and define human oversight checkpoints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat organizational planning advice as approval to automate sensitive finance, legal, HR, customer-facing, or public workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval for sensitive workflows and review any separate tools, integrations, and credentials before connecting them. <br>
Risk: Delegating too much work immediately can create unclear ownership, missed edge cases, or scope creep. <br>
Mitigation: Pilot one function at a time with human review, track overrides and errors, and reduce oversight only after the function is stable. <br>
