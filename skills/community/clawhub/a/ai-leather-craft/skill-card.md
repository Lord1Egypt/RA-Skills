## Description: <br>
Ai Leather Craft provides Chinese-language guidance for selecting leather, choosing tools, hand-stitching, edge finishing, dyeing, carving, and making bags, wallets, belts, straps, and notebooks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
