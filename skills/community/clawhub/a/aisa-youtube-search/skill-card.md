## Description: <br>
YouTube SERP Scout for agents. Search top-ranking videos, channels, and trends for content research and competitor tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AIsaPay](https://clawhub.ai/user/AIsaPay) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External developers and agents use this skill to search YouTube results for content research, competitor tracking, trend discovery, keyword research, and audience research. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: YouTube search terms and API use are sent to AIsa's external API, so sensitive search text or confidential plans could be shared outside the user's environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill only with data approved for external AIsa processing, keep AISA_API_KEY in a secret environment variable, and avoid submitting secrets, private customer data, or confidential business plans as search terms. <br>
