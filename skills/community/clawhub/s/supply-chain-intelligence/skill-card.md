## Use Case: <br>
Procurement managers, logistics coordinators, and supply chain analysts use this skill to monitor supplier risk, optimize logistics, forecast inventory demand, and prepare procurement recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Supplier, shipment, procurement, and ERP/TMS data may be sensitive when shared with an agent or configured external services. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm organizational data-handling approval before using real company data, and limit inputs to information permitted for the configured agent and services. <br>
Risk: Forecasts, supplier risk scores, logistics recommendations, and procurement suggestions may be incomplete, stale, or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against authoritative ERP, TMS, freight, compliance, and procurement sources before making operational or purchasing decisions. <br>
