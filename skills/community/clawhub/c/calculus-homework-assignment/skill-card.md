## Description: <br>
基于学生能力画像，智能生成高等数学个性化自适应作业，支持多模态题目和定时发布管理。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[daigxok](https://clawhub.ai/user/daigxok) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Educators and academic support teams use this skill to generate adaptive higher-mathematics homework from student ability profiles, assemble multimodal question sets, analyze learner strengths and weaknesses, and schedule class releases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can involve student records, ability profiles, class identifiers, deadlines, and learner notifications. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with authorization, limit the student data analyzed, confirm recipient lists before reminders are sent, and follow institutional privacy and retention rules. <br>
Risk: Automatically generated assignments or profile summaries may be incorrect, poorly calibrated, or inappropriate for a class. <br>
Mitigation: Have an educator review generated homework, difficulty distribution, deadlines, and learner-facing messages before publishing. <br>
