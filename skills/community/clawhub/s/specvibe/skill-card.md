## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineering teams use SpecVibe to guide AI coding assistants through a spec-driven lifecycle for planning, testing, implementing, reviewing, documenting, and deploying production-ready applications. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated architecture, authentication, secrets handling, infrastructure, or deployment guidance may be unsuitable for a specific production or regulated system. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human review those outputs against project requirements before implementation or release. <br>
Risk: The skill asks agents to create or modify development artifacts across the full software lifecycle, which can affect application behavior and deployment posture. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included quality gates, security checks, tests, and pre-deployment checklist before merging or deploying generated work. <br>
