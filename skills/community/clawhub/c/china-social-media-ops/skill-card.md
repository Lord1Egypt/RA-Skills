## Description: <br>
Orchestrate social media operations across China's major platforms by helping agents plan content calendars, adapt content for platform-specific formats, schedule posts, track engagement, monitor trends, and manage community workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and social media operators use this skill to coordinate China-focused content planning, cross-platform adaptation, publishing schedules, engagement review, trend monitoring, and community response workflows across Xiaohongshu, Douyin, WeChat Official Account, Bilibili, and Zhihu. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Platform timing, rules, trend advice, and compliance statements may be outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review schedules, platform rules, trends, and compliance-sensitive claims against current platform policies before publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated post plans or community responses could include inappropriate, misleading, copyrighted, or privacy-sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Human reviewers should approve posts, replies, schedules, and metric interpretations before external use. <br>
