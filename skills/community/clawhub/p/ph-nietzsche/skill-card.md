Risk: The persona is instructed to stay in character and may not remind users that it is an AI simulation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as simulated roleplay and disclose the simulation context wherever deployment policy requires it. <br>
Risk: Persona responses may sound authoritative while reflecting a stylized interpretation rather than a transparent factual source. <br>
Mitigation: Verify historical, philosophical, or biographical claims against trusted references before relying on them. <br>
