## Description: <br>
Accesses and summarizes publicly available Dedao courses, columns, and topic pages without bypassing paywalls or downloading content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codenova58](https://clawhub.ai/user/codenova58) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to retrieve and summarize public Dedao course, column, and topic pages for internal analysis, reminders, and topic lists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent may attempt to use logged-in, paid, downloadable, or copyrighted Dedao material beyond the skill's intended public-page scope. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use limited to public pages, do not bypass paywalls or platform restrictions, and review summaries for copyright-sensitive reuse. <br>
Risk: Automated browsing of dynamic Dedao pages may create excessive requests or incomplete extraction. <br>
Mitigation: Use rate limits, wait for public page content to load before summarizing, and retain source links so users can verify extracted details. <br>
