## Description: <br>
Reviews, critiques, and refines Chinese classical poetry with attention to prosody, rhyme, imagery, diction, structure, and genre conventions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ilovecode2018](https://clawhub.ai/user/ilovecode2018) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Poetry writers, reviewers, and learners use this skill to analyze Chinese classical poems, check metrical and rhyme patterns, receive revision suggestions, and generate polished rewrite examples. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional Sou-Yun checker can submit poem text to an external website. <br>
Mitigation: Use the bundled local references for private drafts, and only run the optional checker for poems the user is comfortable sharing externally. <br>
