# Market Entry Brief Template

Complete this before producing a localization plan. A weak brief produces a weak plan.

## Why this market
- What demand signal triggered the consideration? (organic traffic, marketplace requests, partner inbound)
- What is the addressable revenue in this category?
- Who are the top 3 incumbent brands?
- Where do they win and where do they leave gaps?

## Who the buyer is
- Age band
- Income band
- Urban / suburban / rural mix
- Devices and dominant browser
- Trusted channels (search, marketplace, social, friend)
- Trust signals they look for (reviews, certifications, founder story, lab data)

## What we will be in this market
- Premium / mass / value
- DTC only / marketplace only / hybrid
- Categories at launch
- Categories explicitly excluded at launch

## Competitive frame
- Direct competitors (local + global)
- Indirect substitutes
- Common buyer objection in this market that's different from home

## Constraints
- Capital budget for the launch
- Headcount available
- Compliance items that may be long-pole
- Inventory pre-positioning required

## Decision criteria for go / no-go
- Phase 1 success: [metric + threshold]
- Phase 1 fail / kill: [metric + threshold]
- Review cadence: [weekly / monthly]
- Decision-maker: [name + role]

A complete brief makes the rest of the workflow much faster. An incomplete brief means the plan ends up generic.
