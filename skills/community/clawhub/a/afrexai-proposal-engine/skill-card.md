## Description: <br>
Proposal Engine helps agents turn discovery notes into qualified, value-priced proposals with follow-up and objection-handling guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, consultants, agencies, and business operators use this skill to qualify sales opportunities, structure discovery findings, draft proposals, plan pricing options, and review proposal quality before sending client-facing work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals can include inaccurate ROI numbers, pricing, deadlines, payment terms, or proof claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review all client-facing numbers, terms, deadlines, and evidence before sending generated work. <br>
Risk: Persuasion-oriented proposal and follow-up wording may be too aggressive or misaligned with the user's business practices. <br>
Mitigation: Adjust tone, urgency, scarcity language, and objection responses for accuracy, fairness, and client fit. <br>
