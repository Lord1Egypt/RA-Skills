## Description: <br>
Conduct AI-led research with autonomous literature review, hypothesis generation, analysis, and synthesis while human provides vision. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, analysts, and developers use this skill to direct an agent through literature review, gap analysis, hypothesis generation, methodology design, analysis, and synthesis. The human provides the research question, constraints, and validation while the agent drafts structured, cited research outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent may produce unsupported or misleading research claims. <br>
Mitigation: Require citations for factual claims, cross-check important findings across multiple sources, flag confidence levels, and keep human review before publication or use. <br>
Risk: Research methodology or conclusions may be weak without domain oversight. <br>
Mitigation: Use human checkpoints for methodology, citation quality, final conclusions, and high-stakes or consequential results. <br>
Risk: The workflow can be too heavy for simple fact lookup. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill for full research tasks and choose a shorter answer path for simple factual questions. <br>
