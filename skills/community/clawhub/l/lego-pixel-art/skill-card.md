## Description: <br>
Convert any image into LEGO brick pixel art, generating a complete building plan and material purchase list. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
LEGO enthusiasts, craft creators, and educators use this skill to convert an uploaded image into a LEGO-style pixel-art preview, row-by-row building guide, and material list. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generated material list could be incomplete or mismatched for a real purchase. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review colors, counts, and LEGO color codes before buying parts. <br>
Risk: The tool processes user-supplied images in an embedded page. <br>
Mitigation: Use images the user is comfortable opening in the local browser tool. <br>
