## Description: <br>
Ai Calligraphy Master helps users learn brush and hard-pen Chinese calligraphy, copy classical models, plan layouts, explore script history, start seal carving, and receive artwork critique. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, calligraphy hobbyists, exam or competition candidates, and traditional culture enthusiasts use this skill for structured calligraphy study, practice planning, work critique, layout guidance, and seal-carving basics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may upload handwriting or artwork images that they do not want shared with the agent or model provider. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only handwriting or artwork images that are appropriate to share with the configured agent and model. <br>
Risk: Calligraphy critique and practice guidance may be incomplete or inaccurate for formal grading, competition, or expert instruction. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as learning support and review important submissions or exam preparation with a qualified instructor. <br>
