## Description: <br>
Exports selected pages from PPT, PPTX, or ODP presentations to cropped PDF figures through ppt2fig. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[elliottzheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/elliottzheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and document authors use this skill to convert selected presentation slides into paper-ready PDF figures and choose an export backend. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Running a local or downloaded ppt2fig executable on presentation files can execute an untrusted binary. <br>
Mitigation: Use a trusted or verified ppt2fig executable and avoid running the helper from directories that contain untrusted dist/ppt2fig-cli.exe files. <br>
Risk: The install metadata downloads the latest Windows release, so the exact binary may change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Pin or verify the downloaded release before use in controlled environments. <br>
