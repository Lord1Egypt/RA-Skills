## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: PocketBase databases, auth tokens, uploaded files, and admin credentials are sensitive application data. <br>
Mitigation: Handle those assets as sensitive data and avoid exposing them in prompts, logs, generated examples, or shared artifacts. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on a local pocketbase binary. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the pocketbase binary source before using the skill for local PocketBase workflows. <br>
