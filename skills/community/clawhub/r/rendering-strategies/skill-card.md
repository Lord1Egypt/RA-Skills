## Description: <br>
Helps agents choose and optimize rendering strategies for SEO, crawler visibility, and content availability in initial HTML. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and SEO practitioners use this skill to evaluate SSG, SSR, ISR, CSR, and dynamic rendering choices for public web content. It helps check whether page data, metadata, navigation, tabs, carousels, and other critical content are visible to search and AI crawlers without requiring client-side JavaScript. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad rendering terms such as SSR, CSR, or prerendering when the user needs a more specific crawlability or performance workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use a more specific crawlability, performance, or component-focused skill when the task is about adjacent topics rather than rendering strategy selection. <br>
