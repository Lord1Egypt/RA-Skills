## Description: <br>
Provides expert guidance on pet selection, training, nutrition, health care, and enrichment for dogs, cats, and other common pets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Pet owners, prospective adopters, and animal-care helpers use this skill to get practical guidance on choosing, training, feeding, monitoring, and enriching common pets. It is informational and should not replace veterinary care for medical concerns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat informational pet-care guidance as a substitute for veterinary care during illness, poisoning, breathing problems, seizures, severe bleeding, suspected fractures, or other urgent symptoms. <br>
Mitigation: Present health guidance as informational and direct users to contact a licensed veterinarian or emergency clinic for medical concerns and urgent symptoms. <br>
