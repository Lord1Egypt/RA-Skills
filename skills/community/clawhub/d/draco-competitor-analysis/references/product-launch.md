# Product Launch / GTM Competitor Analysis

Use when the task is to study how a product went from 0→1 or 1→10 in the market.

## Focus areas

### 1) Launch rhythm
- Was it slow-burn, explosive, or long-tail?
- What happened in preheat, ignition, and sustain phases?

### 2) Budget allocation
- How was spend distributed over time?
- Across channels?
- Across content directions or audiences?

### 3) Channel mix
- How did online and offline work together?
- What role did each channel play?
- Was there a clear funnel logic?

### 4) Content strategy
- How did they explain the product?
- What concept or framing did they use?
- How was the concept translated into concrete content?

### 5) Paid distribution
- Was there obvious paid amplification?
- Did the spend structure appear reasonable?
- What can we infer about efficiency?

### 6) Commercial result
- Did it work?
- What were the standout strengths?
- What blocked scale?
- Which parts are replicable?

## What to conclude

Your final section should identify:
- the launch model used
- the decisive variable in scaling
- which pieces are context-specific vs reusable
- what launch sequence we should adopt or avoid
