## Domestic Platforms (China)

### Taobao / Tmall
- **7-day no-reason return**: Applies to most physical goods. Buyer bears return shipping.
- **Quality issue return**: Seller bears return shipping. 15-day window from receipt.
- **Exchange**: Supported for quality issues within 15 days; size/color exchange at seller discretion.
- **Refund timeline**: Seller must respond within 48h; platform auto-approves after timeout.

### JD (京东)
- **7-day no-reason return**: Same as Taobao. JD self-operated items often include free return shipping.
- **30-day quality guarantee**: Electronics category; repair → replace → refund escalation.
- **Exchange**: 15-day window for quality issues; 7-day for size/color on apparel.

### Pinduoduo (拼多多)
- **7-day no-reason return**: Available but stricter enforcement — buyer must provide photos.
- **Quality dispute**: Platform often sides with buyer for documented issues.
- **Refund only (no return)**: Available for items under ¥50 at platform discretion.

### Douyin E-commerce (抖音电商)
- **15-day no-reason return**: Live-stream purchased goods get extended 15-day window.
- **Quality issue**: Same 15-day window; seller must respond within 24h.
- **Special note**: Influencer/anchor guarantees may override standard policy.

## Cross-border Platforms

### Shopee
- **Return window**: 7 days from receipt (varies by market).
- **Free return**: Only for damaged/wrong items. Buyer pays for change-of-mind returns.
- **Refund only**: Available for low-value items at Shopee discretion.

### Lazada
- **Return window**: 7-15 days depending on category and market.
- **Quality issue**: Full refund + seller bears shipping.
- **Change of mind**: Buyer pays return shipping; some categories excluded.

### Amazon
- **30-day return**: Standard for most items.
- **Free return**: Many categories; prepaid label provided.
- **Refund timing**: 3-5 business days after item received at warehouse.

## Category Restrictions

Items generally **excluded** from no-reason return across platforms:

- Customized / personalized products
- Perishable goods (food, flowers)
- Underwear / swimwear (hygiene)
- Digital products (software, e-books, virtual items)
- Unsealed cosmetics (varies)
- Bulky furniture (delivery-only return)

## Freight / Shipping Rules

| Scenario | Who Pays Return Shipping |
|---|---|
| 7-day no-reason (buyer's preference) | Buyer |
| Quality issue / defective | Seller |
| Wrong item sent | Seller |
| Damaged in transit | Seller (then claims from courier) |
| Size exchange (no quality issue) | Negotiable — state clearly in reply |

## Dispute Escalation

1. **Seller response timeout**: Platform auto-approves buyer request.
   - Taobao: 48h
   - JD: 24h (self-operated), 48h (third-party)
   - PDD: 36h
   - Douyin: 24h

2. **Platform intervention**: Buyer can request after seller rejection. Platform reviews evidence.

3. **Escalation wording**: Never discourage a buyer from seeking platform help — it's their right. Use phrasing like: "如果对处理结果不满意，您也可以申请平台介入协助处理。"
