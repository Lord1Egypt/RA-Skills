## Description: <br>
Biz Reporter generates daily, weekly, monthly, and ad hoc business intelligence reports by pulling metrics from analytics, revenue, social, CRM, spreadsheet, and custom data sources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, marketers, founders, and analytics teams use this skill to combine business metrics into KPI snapshots, marketing reports, monthly reviews, and natural-language answers about performance trends. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may require broad access to sensitive business analytics, revenue, CRM, and social metrics. <br>
Mitigation: Use read-only credentials where possible, connect only the sources needed for the report, and avoid exposing individual user data or other unnecessary sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Reports can be sent or scheduled to external destinations without enough scoping safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the exact date range, report contents, and destination before delivery, and enable recurring schedules only after the user knows how to review and remove them. <br>
