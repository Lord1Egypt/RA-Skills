## Description: <br>
Generate comprehensive 30-40 page academic research papers with full citations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[roboticresults](https://clawhub.ai/user/roboticresults) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, researchers, analysts, and developers use this skill to plan, source, draft, and quality-check long-form academic research papers across multiple domains. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research topics and derived queries may be sent to third-party websites, public APIs, and search tools. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use confidential project names, unpublished research, personal data, or regulated information as topics unless that external disclosure is approved. <br>
Risk: Generated research may contain unsupported, outdated, or weakly sourced claims despite the workflow's verification steps. <br>
Mitigation: Review the verification log, source list, DOI and URL resolution, and citation integrity before relying on the paper. <br>
Risk: Some referenced source categories may require subscriptions, institutional access, or rate-limit handling. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer open, authoritative sources where possible and document access limitations in the methodology or QA report. <br>
