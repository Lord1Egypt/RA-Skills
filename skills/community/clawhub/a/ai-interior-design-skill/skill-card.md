## Description: <br>
Room redesign planning companion that turns interior design goals, constraints, and photos or text notes into palettes, layouts, lighting plans, AI image prompts, and implementation checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[eavemma5-tech](https://clawhub.ai/user/eavemma5-tech) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn room notes, photos, constraints, and design preferences into practical interior redesign plans, AI image prompts, negative prompts, layout ideas, lighting plans, and implementation checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad home-decor or interior-design phrasing. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants interior design planning before applying the skill's workflow. <br>
Risk: Room photos or home details can reveal personal or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for information needed for the design request and avoid requesting sensitive home photos or personal details unless necessary. <br>
Risk: The skill provides inspirational planning, not professional architectural, engineering, electrical, plumbing, construction, or code-compliance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Refer structural changes, wiring, plumbing, mold, asbestos, lead paint, and building-code questions to qualified professionals. <br>
