## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted LinkedIn posts or replies could be published before human review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before publishing public posts, replies, or campaign content. <br>
Risk: Voice or likeness generation could be used without proper authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm authorization before using Arthur's cloned voice or likeness in any generated media. <br>
Risk: Confidential company or customer material could be sent to third-party AI providers during content production. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential material with third-party AI providers unless policy explicitly allows it. <br>
