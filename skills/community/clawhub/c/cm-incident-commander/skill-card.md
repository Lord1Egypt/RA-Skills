## Description: <br>
Guide incident response with structured communication, timeline tracking, severity assessment, stakeholder updates, and post-incident review for on-call teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
On-call engineers, SRE teams, and incident leads use this skill to coordinate active incidents, assess severity, assign response roles, draft stakeholder updates, maintain timelines, and facilitate post-incident reviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated stakeholder updates, timelines, severity assessments, or runbook suggestions may be incomplete or inaccurate during a live incident. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and have the human incident lead review them before posting, escalating, or acting on recommendations. <br>
Risk: Broad incident-response phrasing could activate the workflow when the user did not intend structured incident management. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill with explicit incident-response requests and confirm the desired workflow before relying on generated guidance. <br>
