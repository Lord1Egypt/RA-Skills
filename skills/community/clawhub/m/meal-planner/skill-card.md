## Description: <br>
Plan meals with weekly menus, shopping lists, batch cooking, budget tracking, dietary preferences, and recipe management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and household meal planners use this skill to organize weekly meals, shopping lists, local recipe notes, food budgets, dietary preferences, and inventory-aware meal prep. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meal plans and memory files may contain household food preferences, allergies, restrictions, ages, and other sensitive family or health-adjacent details. <br>
Mitigation: Keep saved details minimal, avoid full names and exact ages, record allergies and restrictions only as specifically as needed, and delete ~/meal-planner/ to remove local data. <br>
Risk: Proactive meal-planning reminders may be unwanted for users who prefer the skill to respond only when invoked. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for reminder preferences during setup and let users decline or disable proactive reminders. <br>
