## Description: <br>
Autonomous 6-agent pipeline that researches industries, finds businesses, and generates personalized outreach -- one API call. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cameron48](https://clawhub.ai/user/cameron48) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
AI sales, marketing, consulting, and orchestration agents use this skill to find and research business prospects, qualify leads, match solutions, and draft personalized outreach for a chosen industry and location. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses a paid third-party lead-generation API. <br>
Mitigation: Set spending controls and confirm payment behavior before automated or high-volume use. <br>
Risk: Lead research and generated cold-email content may involve personal data, inaccurate contacts, consent issues, privacy obligations, or anti-spam compliance requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting confidential data, and human-review returned contacts and email drafts for accuracy, consent, privacy, and anti-spam compliance before use. <br>
