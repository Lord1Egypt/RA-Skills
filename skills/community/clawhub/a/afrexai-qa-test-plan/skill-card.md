## Description: <br>
Generates QA test plans with coverage matrices, prioritized test cases, automation ROI, release readiness checklists, and metrics dashboards for engineering teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Engineering managers, QA leads, CTOs, and startup teams use this skill to generate structured QA plans, coverage targets, test cases, automation investment analysis, and release readiness criteria. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include confidential product details, customer data, secrets, or unreleased business information while asking for QA plans. <br>
Mitigation: Share only data approved for the workspace and model provider; redact secrets, customer data, and sensitive business information before use. <br>
Risk: Generated QA plans and release criteria may be incomplete or inaccurate for a specific product or compliance environment. <br>
Mitigation: Have QA and engineering owners review generated cases, thresholds, and go/no-go criteria against the actual system and applicable obligations. <br>
