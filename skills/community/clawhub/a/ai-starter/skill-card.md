## Description: <br>
AI-powered idea generation and brainstorming assistant for what-if scenarios, concept exploration, and creative idea generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill to generate, reframe, deepen, and challenge ideas with structured brainstorming prompts and templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad example prompts may cause the skill to activate more often than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit invocation or manual routing when tighter control is required. <br>
Risk: Brainstormed outputs can be incomplete or misleading if treated as final decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review and validate generated ideas before using them for planning, publication, or deployment. <br>
