## Description: <br>
Amazon brand protection toolkit. Detect hijackers, counterfeits, and unauthorized sellers. Includes MAP violation monitoring, trademark abuse detection, complaint templates for Brand Registry, and test buy evidence collection guides. No API key required. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External brand owners, marketplace operators, and e-commerce teams use this skill to identify Amazon listing risks, prepare evidence, and draft Brand Registry, MAP, cease-and-desist, and test-buy materials for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated complaints, MAP notices, and cease-and-desist letters may contain incomplete or incorrect legal assertions if submitted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat all generated materials as drafts and verify seller identity, evidence, trademark rights, and legal basis before sending them or submitting them to Amazon. <br>
Risk: The skill includes a global install command and local scripts that should be trusted before execution. <br>
Mitigation: Review the source and security scan evidence before installing or running the scripts in an agent environment. <br>
