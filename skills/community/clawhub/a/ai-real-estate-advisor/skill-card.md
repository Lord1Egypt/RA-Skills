## Description: <br>
Ai Real Estate Advisor provides Chinese-language support for home-buying decisions, property valuation, rental analysis, mortgage and tax calculations, regional assessment, policy interpretation, renovation budgeting, and real estate investment analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to structure Chinese-language real estate questions and receive informational guidance on purchases, valuation, rentals, loans, taxes, renovation budgets, policies, and investment tradeoffs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global; content focuses on China real estate scenarios. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Real estate, legal, tax, mortgage, and policy guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important decisions with current local sources, professional advisers, and qualified real estate, legal, tax, or mortgage experts before acting. <br>
