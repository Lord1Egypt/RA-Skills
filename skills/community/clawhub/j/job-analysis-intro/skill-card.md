## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may give incomplete or misleading educational guidance if used outside its Chapter 1 job-analysis scope. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use focused on introductory job-analysis concepts and review answers against the bundled reference notes or course materials for graded or professional work. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on the skill for employment, legal, or HR compliance decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use its output as study guidance only and route real HR, selection, compensation, or compliance decisions through qualified reviewers. <br>
