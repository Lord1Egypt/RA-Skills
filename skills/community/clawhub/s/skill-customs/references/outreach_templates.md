# 开发信模板库

## 一、展会开发信

### 模板1：展会现场即时触达
```
Subject: Great meeting you at Canton Fair - [Company Name] outdoor furniture

Hi [Name],

It was a pleasure meeting you at Canton Fair [届数] today! 

I really enjoyed our conversation about [讨论话题]. As mentioned, I'm sending over our latest catalog featuring [产品类别].

[简短价值主张 - 1-2句话]

Would love to continue our conversation. Would you be available for a quick call next week to discuss your requirements in more detail?

Looking forward to hearing from you!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company]
[WhatsApp/WeChat]
```

### 模板2：展会后跟进
```
Subject: Following up from Canton Fair - [Product] solutions for [Country] market

Hi [Name],

It was great connecting with you at Canton Fair [届数]! I hope your time in Guangzhou has been productive.

[核心卖点 - 1-2句话]
[具体数据或案例]

I've attached our latest price list and product brochure for your reference. Would you like me to arrange samples for your evaluation?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## 二、主动开发信

### 模板1：海关数据客户开发
```
Subject: [竞品替代] Can we help [Company] reduce [成本/风险]?

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] has been importing [产品类别] from [竞品名称], with annual import volume around [金额].

We've been helping similar companies in [国家] achieve:
- [具体好处1，如：15-20% cost reduction]
- [具体好处2，如：Faster delivery]
- [具体好处3，如：Better quality control]

Would you be open to a brief call to discuss how we might create similar value for [Company]?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## 三、报价跟进信

### 模板1：报价后跟进
```
Subject: Re: Quotation for [产品] - Any questions?

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on the quotation I sent last [时间] for [产品].

Have you had a chance to review the pricing? I'd be happy to:
- Clarify any specifications
- Discuss volume discounts
- Adjust terms if needed

Looking forward to your feedback!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

```
Subject: Samples shipped - Tracking & next steps

Hi [Name],

Great news! The samples you requested have been shipped today.

Tracking number: [运单号]
Estimated delivery: [日期]

Once you receive them, I'd love to schedule a call to discuss:
- Your feedback on quality
- Any adjustments needed
- Next steps for a trial order

Please let me know when the samples arrive!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```
