## Description: <br>
Coordinates multiple agents by collecting, grouping, summarizing, and forwarding their outputs as a structured report. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnsmithfan](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnsmithfan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and teams use this skill to coordinate multi-agent work: dispatch related tasks, collect reports, remove duplication, and summarize decisions or open items for a CEO-style reviewer. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Aggregated reports can expose sensitive information across agents or to the final recipient. <br>
Mitigation: Use only where sharing combined agent outputs with the coordinator and CEO-style recipient is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Coordinator summaries may omit nuance or change the emphasis of source agent reports. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve source agent conclusions, record submission status and times, and attach or reference original reports when available. <br>
