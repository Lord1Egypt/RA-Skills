## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language supplier audit support by classifying onsite findings, suggesting severity ratings and scoring impacts, analyzing photo evidence, and drafting audit reports across multiple product categories and quality standards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mxp0125-rgb](https://clawhub.ai/user/mxp0125-rgb) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Supplier quality teams, auditors, procurement teams, and compliance reviewers use this skill to turn onsite notes, photos, certificates, records, and scoring data into structured finding classifications, severity recommendations, corrective-action notes, and Chinese audit reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Regulatory, audit-platform, and supplier-qualification guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific legal or commercial decision. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support and have qualified reviewers verify findings, scoring, legal requirements, and supplier decisions against current standards and contracts. <br>
Risk: Audit photos, certificates, supplier records, and business documents can contain sensitive or restricted information. <br>
Mitigation: Process only records the user is authorized to share, minimize unnecessary personal or confidential data, and follow the organization's document-handling rules. <br>
