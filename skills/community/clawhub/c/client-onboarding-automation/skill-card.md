## Description: <br>
Automates client onboarding by processing intake forms, collecting documents, sending welcome email sequences, creating CRM records, assigning tasks, and sending follow-up reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[merjua14](https://clawhub.ai/user/merjua14) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Service providers, SaaS teams, agencies, and client operations teams use this skill to turn new-client intake into document requests, welcome messages, CRM entries, assigned onboarding tasks, and reminder workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles sensitive client documents and onboarding data. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to clients and records covered by explicit consent, retention, access-control, and compliance requirements; avoid payment data, IDs, healthcare, or insurance records unless those requirements are already handled. <br>
Risk: The skill can send emails and write CRM records with external credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege email and CRM credentials, scope access to specific clients or sheets, and require human approval before sending messages or writing records. <br>
