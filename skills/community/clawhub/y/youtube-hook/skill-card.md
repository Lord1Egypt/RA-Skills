## Description: <br>
Generate high-performing YouTube video scripts, titles, thumbnails, descriptions, and chapter markers from any topic or content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[markusmikely](https://clawhub.ai/user/markusmikely) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and content teams use this skill to turn a topic, article, product, or niche into a YouTube planning package with titles, thumbnail direction, script structure, chapters, descriptions, SEO keywords, and upload strategy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional trend lookup can send topic queries to Tavily when TAVILY_API_KEY is configured. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using confidential drafts, private launch plans, or sensitive personal topics with trend lookup unless sharing the query with Tavily is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated hooks, titles, and strategy guidance may overstate performance or create misleading framing if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated claims, SEO framing, and audience promises before publishing. <br>
