## Description: <br>
Napoleon Hill's "Think and Grow Rich" skill helps an agent provide book-grounded coaching on goal setting, wealth mindset, fear, persistence, decision-making, and mastermind planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to apply the Think and Grow Rich framework to personal goals, confidence building, persistence, fear analysis, decision-making, and wealth-oriented planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill has broad activation language and may respond when users mention wealth, success, persistence, or related self-improvement topics. <br>
Mitigation: Review and narrow activation triggers, or require confirmation before the skill responds outside explicit Think and Grow Rich or Napoleon Hill requests. <br>
