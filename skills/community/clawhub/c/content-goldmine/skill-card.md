## Description: <br>
Content Goldmine helps agents deconstruct high-performing articles into reusable writing building blocks, including structure, hooks, quotable lines, transformation arcs, paradoxes, persuasion patterns, rhythm, and reusable elements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liujuntao123](https://clawhub.ai/user/liujuntao123) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to analyze an article they provide and turn observed writing patterns into structured building-block cards for reuse in their own writing workflow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save generated building-block cards into a user material library or Obsidian vault. <br>
Mitigation: Have the user choose the destination, review the filename and generated note, and approve the save before writing files. <br>
Risk: Article analysis can include confidential, private, or copyrighted source material. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid retaining sensitive or copyrighted source content unless the user intentionally provides it for that purpose and approves storage. <br>
