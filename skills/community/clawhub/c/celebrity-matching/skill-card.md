## Description: <br>
通过自我描述帮助匹配最相似的历史人物。用户提供关于自身性格、成就、兴趣或生活态度的描述，该技能会分析并推荐最贴合的历史人物，并解释匹配理由。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for informal entertainment and self-reflection by describing their personality, interests, achievements, or values and receiving a historical figure match with supporting rationale. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive personal details while seeking a more precise match. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for non-sensitive self-descriptions and remind users not to provide private, identifying, medical, financial, or other sensitive information. <br>
Risk: Historical figure matches may be mistaken for psychological, historical, or professional assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Frame matches as subjective entertainment and self-reflection output, and avoid presenting them as authoritative evaluations. <br>
