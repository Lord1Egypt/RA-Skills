## Prompt

You are an SEO content strategist and reputation management specialist for Nevada home services contractors. Generate a local SEO blog post and review response templates for an asphalt driveway sealing contractor.

**Business Information:**
- Company name: [COMPANY NAME]
- Primary service area: [CITY/METRO — e.g., "Las Vegas Valley / Clark County"]
- License: [LICENSE TYPE + NUMBER]
- Sealer used: [ASPHALT EMULSION / ACRYLIC — specify brand if desired]

**Compliance Rules:**
1. Never present coal tar sealer as acceptable or superior
2. All environmental/safety claims must be substantiated and sealer-specific
3. All warranty claims limited to 3–5 years unless manufacturer documentation supports longer
4. All "licensed" references must include license type and number

---

**Generate:**

**A) SEO Blog Post (1,200–1,400 words)**

**Title:** "Coal Tar vs. Asphalt Emulsion Driveway Sealer — What Every [CITY] Homeowner Needs to Know Before Hiring a Contractor"

**SEO Target Keywords:** [city] driveway sealing, best driveway sealer [city], is coal tar sealer safe, asphalt emulsion vs coal tar, driveway sealing near me [city]

**Blog Structure:**
1. **Hook:** Homeowner gets quote with "professional-grade coal tar" — learns post-project it's restricted in 7 states
2. **What is coal tar sealer? Why is it restricted?** — PAH content, 7-state restrictions, EPA USGS research (cite accurately), Clark County SNHD storm water connection
3. **What is asphalt emulsion sealer?** — safer PAH profile, biodegradable carrier, preferred by Nevada contractors who understand storm drain compliance
4. **The 5 questions to ask any Las Vegas driveway sealing contractor before hiring** (include license number check, sealer type, HOA approval process, SWPPP compliance, weather window policy)
5. **How [COMPANY NAME] handles each of these 5 questions** — natural soft pitch
6. **CTA:** Free estimate, mention license number, mention HOA coordination

**B) 15 Review Response Templates**

Generate in three groups:

**5-Star Reviews (5 templates):**
- Vary length (2 short/1 medium/2 longer)
- Reference specific service when mentioned
- Always include license in at least 1 template
- Include HOA satisfaction mention in at least 1

**Neutral Reviews / 3–4 Star with Minor Complaint (5 templates):**
- LAER framework: Listen → Acknowledge → Explore → Respond
- Address specific concern without admitting liability
- Offer resolution path
- Keep compliance-safe: never promise outcomes you can't guarantee

**Negative Reviews / 1–2 Star (5 templates):**
- Open with empathy, not defense
- Take offline promptly
- Never admit fault in public response
- Include: "We'd like to make this right — please call [NUMBER] and ask for [MANAGER NAME]"
- Include warranty/workmanship language only if sealer type and weather conditions are documented

**C) Local Pack Optimization Checklist (10 items)**
Specific to Clark County / Las Vegas asphalt sealing contractors. Include: NRS 624 license posting on GBP, service area radius settings, seasonal photo update schedule, HOA community keyword list for posts.

## Output Format

Return A, B (three groups clearly labeled), and C. Total: 1,800–2,200 words.
