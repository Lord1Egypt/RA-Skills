## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, growth, and operations teams use this skill to plan viral-growth campaigns, define SOPs and metrics, run small pilots, and capture reusable campaign templates. It also reminds users to check platform rules, personal-information protections, and data minimization requirements before execution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with campaign execution subject to applicable local laws and platform rules, including Chinese personal-information and marketing compliance where relevant. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Campaign guidance may be applied in a jurisdiction or platform context with stricter outreach, privacy, or consent requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm applicable laws, platform rules, personal-data handling requirements, and approval workflows before using the plan in a real campaign. <br>
Risk: Growth experiments may collect more personal data than necessary. <br>
Mitigation: Apply data minimization, define metric collection scope before the pilot, and review dashboards for unnecessary personal-data exposure. <br>
