## Description: <br>
Scores the viral potential of social-media titles or copy across TikTok, Twitter/X, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram and provides platform-specific optimization suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Social media teams, creators, advertisers, and brand marketers use this skill to estimate virality potential before publishing or testing content and to get platform-specific copy improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Submitted titles or marketing copy are sent to a third-party server over unencrypted HTTP. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit confidential drafts, client data, credentials, or regulated information; use only when the provider and transport risk are acceptable. <br>
Risk: Each API request may incur a CNY 0.50 charge. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm billing expectations before use and avoid automated high-volume calls without explicit approval. <br>
