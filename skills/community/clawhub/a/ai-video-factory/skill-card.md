## Description: <br>
AI短视频工厂 helps agents plan short-video production from a topic, including scripts, media selection, voiceover, subtitles, BGM, platform adaptation, quality checks, and export guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qqyougitcom](https://clawhub.ai/user/qqyougitcom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and agent users use this skill to produce short-form video plans and deliverable packages for platforms such as Douyin, Kuaishou, TikTok, YouTube, Bilibili, Xiaohongshu, and WeChat Channels. It is intended for short videos up to five minutes and includes batch-production guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad video-making trigger phrases may activate the workflow for generic video requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants automated short-video generation before proceeding, especially for batch work or paid AI/media services. <br>
Risk: The workflow proposes use of media, music, AI-generated assets, and platform-specific publishing guidance that may have copyright or policy implications. <br>
Mitigation: Use licensed or free-to-use assets, retain source and license records, and review target platform rules before publishing. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes batch-production guidance that could amplify errors, unsuitable content, or policy issues across many videos. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated scripts, sources, subtitles, and quality reports before batch export or distribution. <br>
