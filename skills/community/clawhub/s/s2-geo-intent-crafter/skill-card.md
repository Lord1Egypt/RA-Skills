## Description: <br>
品牌 GEO 降维提炼师。运用品牌生态学理论，将传统品牌宣发文案转化为大模型原生可读的“树冠/树根”结构与“买家意图”生态位标签。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Brand, marketing, GEO, agency, and research users apply this skill to convert long-form Chinese brand copy into structured brand-ecology summaries and buyer-intent niche tags for LLM-oriented brand positioning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing and positioning advice may be incomplete or misleading if the supplied brand copy omits patents, ESG data, privacy practices, or other hard evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated GEO report against authoritative brand, legal, technical, and ESG materials before using it in public-facing systems. <br>
Risk: User-provided brand plans or unpublished market strategy may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential internal brand plans unless the user is comfortable processing that text through their OpenClaw agent. <br>
