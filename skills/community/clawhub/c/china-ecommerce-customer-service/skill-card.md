## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese customer-service reply scripts for Taobao, JD, and Pinduoduo across inquiries, complaints, after-sales service, and review responses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobewin](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobewin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators, support teams, and agents use this skill to draft Chinese customer-service responses for common Taobao, JD, and Pinduoduo scenarios such as order questions, complaints, refunds, after-sales service, and review replies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated customer replies may be inaccurate, overpromise refunds or guarantees, or conflict with marketplace policies or legal requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review replies before sending them to customers, especially for refunds, guarantees, legal claims, and platform-policy compliance. <br>
