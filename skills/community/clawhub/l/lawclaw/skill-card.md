## Description: <br>
Drop a contract, get answers. lawclaw rips through PDFs, spots risky clauses, diffs redlines, checks citations, and searches thousands of discovery docs locally, so nothing leaves your machine. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Attorneys, paralegals, and legal operations teams use this skill for local contract review, litigation support, due diligence, e-discovery, citation checks, and redline workflows over legal documents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated text, diff, CSV, log, and index files may contain confidential client or case material. <br>
Mitigation: Keep generated files in a dedicated case folder and protect or delete them under the same confidentiality rules as the originals. <br>
Risk: Batch document commands can modify or create many case files at once. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands before execution and keep backups before running batch operations. <br>
Risk: Combining local workflows with cloud-connected tools may cause client material to leave the machine. <br>
Mitigation: Use the workflow as local-only unless appropriate consent and data-handling review are in place. <br>
