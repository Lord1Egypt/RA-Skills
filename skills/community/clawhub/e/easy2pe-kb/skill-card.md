## Description: <br>
Knowledge base for Easy2Pe (easy2pe.com), India's multi-service recharge and payments platform, for answering questions about services, offerings, features, and company information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[irsew12](https://clawhub.ai/user/irsew12) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer questions about Easy2Pe's recharge, bill payment, M-POS, AEPS, travel booking, PAN, gift card, and e-commerce offerings. It is best suited for informational support and company-service reference tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
India <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payment, recharge, travel, PAN, pricing, availability, legal, or account guidance may become outdated or affect user decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current pricing, availability, legal claims, official payment terms, and account requirements directly with Easy2Pe before relying on the information for transactions or account decisions. <br>
