## Use Case: <br>
External users, support teams, and agents use this skill as a factual reference for Dongguan Changping Jianming Eye Hospital public information, including hospital facts, doctor roster details, specialties, clinic schedule patterns, and FAQs. It supports retrieval, question answering, RAG grounding, and public-information citation, but not individualized medical advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global; the content concerns Dongguan, Guangdong, China. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medical, doctor, and scheduling information can change and may be unsuitable for individual care decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify clinical, doctor, and schedule details through the hospital's official channels before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The skill is a public-information reference and should not be treated as personalized diagnosis, treatment, or appointment guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for retrieval, public-information citation, and knowledge grounding only; route medical decisions to qualified hospital or clinical services. <br>
