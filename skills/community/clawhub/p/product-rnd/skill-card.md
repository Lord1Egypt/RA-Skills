## Description: <br>
End-to-end Product Innovation R&D workflow for inspiration gathering, research, and professional report generation for product concepts and investor or stakeholder audiences. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[owenrao](https://clawhub.ai/user/owenrao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, strategy, and innovation teams use this skill to turn an early product idea into a structured product innovation report with market research notes, product benefits, packaging strategy, competitive analysis, and stakeholder-facing recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may attempt recurring daily trend searches or research automation without clear opt-in and removal guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before creating any cron or scheduler job, and document how to disable or remove any recurring task. <br>
Risk: Generated market claims and product recommendations may be inaccurate or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated research notes, cited sources, and final report claims before using the output for business decisions. <br>
Risk: Generated charts or data visuals from image tools can misrepresent facts because image generation cannot verify data sources. <br>
Mitigation: Use image generation only for product or packaging concept visuals, and create any charts or tables from verified research data. <br>
