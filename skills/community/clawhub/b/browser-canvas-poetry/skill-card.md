## Description: <br>
Transforms poetry, literary imagery, and visual art concepts into browser-native interactive art prompts and project guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zcr3344](https://clawhub.ai/user/zcr3344) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, creative coders, and artists use this skill to turn poems, mood keywords, art styles, and interaction ideas into browser-native generative art prompts, BCPP manifests, technical hints, and validation guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated demos may ask for microphone, camera, file upload, external CDN/API, registry, wallet, payment, or other sensitive access. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated demos before running them, grant browser permissions only when needed, and do not provide wallet, payment, or sensitive credentials unless a future version clearly explains why they are required. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce runnable frontend code and shell-facing project guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect generated code and dependencies before execution, especially examples that load external scripts or contact third-party APIs. <br>
