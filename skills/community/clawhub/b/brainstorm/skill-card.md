## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill to quickly generate, organize, and expand ideas for naming, decision-making, unblocking, and exploratory thinking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may choose a brainstorming style that does not match the user's intent. <br>
Mitigation: Ask explicitly for rapid fire, structured, deep dive, provocative, or collaborative mode when a specific style is needed. <br>
Risk: Generated ideas may be incomplete, impractical, or misleading without domain-specific constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review ideas before acting on them and provide constraints when accuracy or fit matters. <br>
