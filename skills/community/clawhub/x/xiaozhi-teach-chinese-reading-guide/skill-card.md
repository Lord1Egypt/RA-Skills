## Description: <br>
Helps Chinese language teachers design systematic reading instruction using text analysis, reading-strategy practice, staged reading tasks, group-text design, and student reading-progress records. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Chinese language teachers use this skill to turn reading prompts, lesson-planning needs, or student reading difficulties into structured reading-teaching plans, strategy practice, group-text activities, and reading-progress assessment notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reading-progress records may include student information. <br>
Mitigation: Use pseudonyms, avoid unnecessary personal details, keep records in approved school systems, and confirm before syncing assessment data to student-analysis, resource-library, parent-communication, or student-facing tools. <br>
Risk: Reading lessons may rely on copyrighted textbook excerpts or modern works. <br>
Mitigation: Record the copyright status for selected texts, use public-domain, authorized, or Creative Commons material where appropriate, and avoid copying complete textbooks or unlicensed works. <br>
