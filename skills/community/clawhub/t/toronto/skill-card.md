## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Immigration, fees, eligibility, and processing-time guidance may become outdated or may not fit an individual's legal situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those details as general information and verify them with official Canadian or Ontario sources or a qualified professional before making legal, work, study, or residency decisions. <br>
Risk: City costs, transit fares, rental conditions, and local rules can change after the skill release. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm time-sensitive local details against current official or primary sources before relying on them for travel, housing, business, or relocation decisions. <br>
