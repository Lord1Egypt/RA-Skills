## Description: <br>
Provides daily accountability coaching with either supportive encouragement or roast-style motivational feedback to help users stay consistent with habits and quests. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ryudi84](https://clawhub.ai/user/ryudi84) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for daily habit accountability, morning check-ins, and evening reviews with a chosen supportive or sharp comedic tone. It is intended for users who explicitly want motivational coaching with configurable intensity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Roast-style feedback or proactive check-ins may be unwelcome for some users. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the user wants accountability messages; review mode, intensity, and check-in times before use. <br>
Risk: High-intensity accountability language could feel discouraging if a user is struggling. <br>
Mitigation: Use lower intensity or supportive mode when sharp coaching is unwelcome; the artifact directs the coach to tone down feedback during repeated missed days. <br>
