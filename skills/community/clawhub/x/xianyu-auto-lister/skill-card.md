## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may assume the skill automatically publishes Xianyu listings because the artifact claims auto-posting. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as draft listing content only and manually post after review. <br>
Risk: Generated listing details may contain inaccurate prices, warranty terms, authenticity claims, return promises, discounts, or contact and payment information. <br>
Mitigation: Review and correct all commercial claims and transaction details before publishing a listing. <br>
