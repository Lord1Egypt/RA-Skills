## Description: <br>
Web3 AI Agent 项目专业筛选评估工具。输入项目名称或描述，通过11维度专业框架进行深度评估，输出可投资级别评级、核心逻辑、风险提示及综合建议。适用于三年以上价值投资视角的项目筛选。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to screen Web3 AI Agent projects with an 11-dimension investment-style evaluation framework. It produces a structured project report with ratings, rationale, risks, data-source notes, and a long-term investment view. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill produces investment-style analysis that may include incorrect, stale, or incomplete market and project claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify financial, liquidity, TVL, revenue, exchange-listing, and investor claims against primary sources before making decisions. <br>
Risk: Project names or portfolio details used in web searches may be logged by search providers or tools. <br>
Mitigation: Use public project names and avoid confidential portfolio, deal, or strategy information in prompts and search queries. <br>
