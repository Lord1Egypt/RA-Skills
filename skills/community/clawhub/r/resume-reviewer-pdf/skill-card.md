## Description: <br>
Comprehensive review and analysis of software engineering resumes in PDF format, covering technical content quality, career progression, ATS compatibility, grammar, structure, and actionable improvements based on current industry trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and job seekers use this skill to review software engineering resume PDFs for technical positioning, writing quality, structure, ATS compatibility, and prioritized improvement actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume PDFs can contain personal contact details and employment history that may be exposed in the agent context. <br>
Mitigation: Use only resumes the user is comfortable sharing for analysis, and redact unnecessary phone numbers, addresses, or other personal details before review. <br>
Risk: PDF text extraction depends on the local Python environment and may fail if pdfplumber is unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Install pdfplumber before running the extraction script, or report the extraction failure clearly before continuing analysis. <br>
