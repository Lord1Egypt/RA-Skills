## Description: <br>
Guides an AI assistant to redesign SaaS onboarding flows using the Activation Path Method, helping users define their aha moment, map time-to-value, eliminate friction, and produce a concrete minimum viable activation path. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flynndavid](https://clawhub.ai/user/flynndavid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External SaaS founders, growth product managers, and onboarding teams use this skill to diagnose onboarding drop-off and produce a concrete activation redesign plan. It helps define the aha moment, audit time-to-value, design a minimum viable activation path, and draft trigger-based first-week emails. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive customer data, proprietary metrics, or confidential roadmap details while requesting onboarding analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing sensitive or confidential data unless appropriate for the environment and replace private details with representative summaries where possible. <br>
Risk: Generated onboarding emails, UX recommendations, or activation projections may be incorrect, overbroad, or misaligned with the product's actual users. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before implementation, validate assumptions with product data, and test major onboarding changes before broad rollout. <br>
