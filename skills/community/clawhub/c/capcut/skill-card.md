## Description: <br>
Helps agents summarize public CapCut pages about templates, editing features, export specifications, copyright guidance, help content, and pricing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mikeclaw007](https://clawhub.ai/user/mikeclaw007) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to gather concise summaries of public CapCut product, template, help, pricing, copyright, and commercial-use information from official sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be asked to handle CapCut credentials, private projects, unpublished assets, or account actions that are outside its public-information scope. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide credentials or private assets, and restrict use to summarizing public CapCut pages and help information. <br>
Risk: Summaries of copyright, commercial-use boundaries, paid resources, or platform rules may be incomplete or misapplied. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve source links for copyright and commercial-use claims, review official CapCut terms before relying on them, and do not use the skill to bypass paid access or platform rules. <br>
