## Description: <br>
Tutur Gayo produces, adapts, or reviews Bahasa Gayo text with an offline-first, source-aware workflow that avoids claims of native-level or official fluency. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ramaaditya49](https://clawhub.ai/user/ramaaditya49) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, writers, educators, and developers use this skill to draft, adapt, or review ordinary Bahasa Gayo text while preserving uncertainty. Public, legal, ritual, educational, official, or dialect-sensitive wording should receive competent speaker or official review before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce incorrect or overconfident Bahasa Gayo wording when source confidence is limited. <br>
Mitigation: Mark uncertain wording, preserve Indonesian terms when no verified Gayo equivalent is available, and check KBDA, dictionary, or speaker sources. <br>
Risk: Public, legal, ritual, educational, official, or dialect-sensitive text may require local authority or community-specific wording. <br>
Mitigation: Require competent speaker, Balai Bahasa, or relevant local review before publication or formal use. <br>
Risk: The skill could confuse Bahasa Gayo with Bahasa Aceh or overgeneralize a single Gayo variety. <br>
Mitigation: Keep Gayo and Aceh language choices separate, ask for target community context, and use user-provided samples when dialect matters. <br>
