## PROMPT 1 OUTPUT: Customer Story Narrative

**How CloudPath Solutions Recovered 8 Hours a Week — and Landed 3 New Clients**

Every Friday at CloudPath Solutions, VP of Operations Sarah Chen blocked her calendar. Not for strategy, not for client calls — for reporting. Eight hours of copy-pasting numbers from Google Analytics, HubSpot, and Stripe into PowerPoint slides for 22 active client accounts. By the time Monday arrived, she was already behind.

**About CloudPath Solutions**

CloudPath Solutions is a 45-person web development and digital marketing agency based in Austin, Texas. Founded in 2018, the firm manages digital strategy, development, and paid media for mid-market B2B companies across the Southwest. With $4.2M in annual recurring revenue and a roster of 22 active retainer clients, CloudPath competes on the quality of its thinking — not its headcount.

**The Challenge**

The problem wasn't that reporting was hard. It was that it was the same hard every single week.

"Every client wants their data formatted differently," Chen said. "One wants a three-page executive summary. Another wants a spreadsheet they can share with their CFO. We had 22 formats for 22 clients, and none of it was automated."

The manual process consumed roughly 8 hours of Chen's time each week — the equivalent of a full working day. That didn't include the two junior analysts also involved in the process, who each spent 3–4 hours pulling raw exports. The total weekly cost: 14–16 hours across the team, or approximately $2,400/month in blended labor costs. And it was growing. Each new client added 30–45 minutes to the Friday ritual.

Worse, the reports were always slightly late. Clients received them Monday morning instead of Friday afternoon. "We had one client escalate directly to our CEO about it," Chen recalled. "That was the moment I knew we had to fix this."

**Why They Chose ReportLab**

CloudPath evaluated three reporting platforms before selecting ReportLab. Two were eliminated early — one required manual data entry, defeating the purpose, and the other lacked HubSpot integration, which managed CloudPath's entire client contact pipeline.

ReportLab won on two points: it connected natively to all five of CloudPath's data sources (GA4, HubSpot, Stripe, Meta Ads, and Google Search Console), and it supported per-client template customization. Chen's team could maintain their 22 custom report formats without starting from scratch.

"I'll be honest — we were skeptical about the setup time," Chen said. "We'd been burned by software that promised 'easy setup' and took six months to actually work. ReportLab was different. We were pulling real client data in day two."

**Implementation**

The rollout took three weeks. In week one, ReportLab's onboarding team connected all five data source integrations and migrated six of CloudPath's highest-volume client templates. Week two extended the migration to the remaining 16 clients, with Chen's team handling the template customization using ReportLab's drag-and-drop editor. By week three, the system was generating reports automatically every Thursday night — landing in client inboxes by Friday 8 AM.

The first sign it was working came early. "I showed up to work on a Friday and had nothing to do in reporting," Chen said. "I actually thought something had broken."

**The Transformation**

The change wasn't just about time. It was about what CloudPath did with the time it recovered.

In the first month after full deployment, Chen reallocated 6 of the recovered 8 hours to new business development — something she hadn't had bandwidth for in over a year. Within eight weeks, CloudPath had signed three new retainer clients that had been stuck in the pipeline. "Those three clients alone more than paid for the platform for the year," she said.

The reporting quality improved too. Automated pulls eliminated the copy-paste errors that occasionally crept into manual reports. One client specifically commented on the improvement. "They said it was the cleanest data they'd seen from any vendor," Chen noted. "That's not something we expected as a side effect."

**Looking Ahead**

Today, CloudPath uses ReportLab for all 22 client accounts and recently expanded to include its internal operations dashboard. Chen plans to add two more junior account managers in Q3 without increasing the reporting overhead — something that would have been impossible under the old system.

"If you're an agency and you're still doing reports manually," she said, "you're not just losing time. You're losing the ability to grow."
