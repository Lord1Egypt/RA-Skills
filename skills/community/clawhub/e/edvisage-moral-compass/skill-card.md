## Description: <br>
A text-only ethics and reflection skill that helps agents examine uncertain actions, identify manipulation patterns, practice answerability, and store values or reflection notes in memory. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[edvisage](https://clawhub.ai/user/edvisage) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent developers use this skill to give an OpenClaw agent structured prompts for ethical self-reflection, manipulation resistance, and transparent decision review before or after uncertain actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill encourages storing values statements and reflections in long-term memory, which may bias future behavior if saved unreviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review any proposed memory text before saving it, make clear that it cannot override system, developer, or owner instructions, and revise or delete it if it creates unwanted behavioral bias. <br>
Risk: Ethical self-reflection prompts can be mistaken for higher-priority policy or authority. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as reflective guidance only; keep platform, system, developer, and owner instructions authoritative. <br>
