## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may contain licensing, EPA 608, warranty, review incentive, anti-spam, or platform-term mistakes if business inputs are incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify certifications, state licensing, brand authorization, warranty terms, review incentives, unsubscribe language, and ad platform requirements before publishing or sending generated materials. <br>
Risk: Brand authorization and OEM parts claims can mislead customers if the business cannot document those claims. <br>
Mitigation: Use authorized-service and genuine-OEM language only when the business has confirmed authorization and parts sourcing records; otherwise use neutral specialization or quality replacement parts language. <br>
Risk: Outbound lead generation or scraping-based outreach can violate law, platform terms, or recipient expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Use only lawful, terms-compliant lead sources and include required consent, identification, opt-out, and disclosure language for email, SMS, referral, and co-marketing campaigns. <br>
