## Description: <br>
Coordinate family schedules, household tasks, school logistics, care routines, and private-versus-shared memory with a structured family system. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Household members and caregivers use this skill to coordinate schedules, school logistics, chores, meals, care routines, handoffs, and shared local household memory while keeping private details separate. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store sensitive household, child, dependent-care, medication, contact, incident, or document-location details on the local device. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only on an appropriate device, keep stored details minimal, confirm writes before creating or updating ~/family/ files, and review or delete details when they are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Private family details could be promoted into shared household notes too broadly. <br>
Mitigation: Keep new details private by default, share only the minimum needed for coordination, and ask before making unclear boundaries part of shared household memory. <br>
