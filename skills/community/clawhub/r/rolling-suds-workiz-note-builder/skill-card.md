## Description: <br>
Builds concise internal Workiz notes for Rolling Suds exterior-cleaning sales and estimating workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mwdearing](https://clawhub.ai/user/mwdearing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales and office users use this skill to turn quote-intake output, estimator output, appointment details, and messy job notes into short internal Workiz notes. It supports appointment, estimate, comparison, and follow-up notes while preserving operational details and important pricing logic. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated internal notes may omit or misstate appointment, scope, pricing, or follow-up details if the input context is incomplete or messy. <br>
Mitigation: Review the proposed note against the original intake, estimate, and appointment details before pasting it into Workiz. <br>
Risk: The skill may process customer or job details that are intended only for internal sales and office workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs internal-facing and include only the customer and job details needed for the Workiz note. <br>
