## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can run this skill to generate a Qimen Dunjia chart and cultural fortune-style guidance for timing, questions, daily outlooks, directions, and colors. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional HTML interface loads third-party browser libraries and a Google font from the network, creating supply-chain and privacy caveats. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented local Node command for local-only behavior, or prefer a bundled/offline browser version when network loading is not acceptable. <br>
Risk: The skill produces fortune-style scores and recommendations that may be mistaken for decision advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as entertainment or cultural reference only, and do not rely on them as the sole basis for financial, career, relationship, or other major decisions. <br>
