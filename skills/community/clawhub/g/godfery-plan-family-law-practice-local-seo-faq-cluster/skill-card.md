## Description: <br>
Research local questions, group them into clusters, and draft launch-ready FAQ copy for a family law practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External attorneys, family law practice marketers, and SEO content teams use this skill to research local search questions, organize them into FAQ clusters, and draft search-optimized FAQ copy for human legal review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated family-law FAQ copy may be inaccurate for a jurisdiction, ethics rule, or client matter. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human reviewer verify legal accuracy, ethics compliance, and client suitability before publishing or sending copy to clients. <br>
Risk: Manual installation references an external GitHub source that is not backed by server-resolved provenance for this release. <br>
Mitigation: Use the ClawHub release as the source of record, or independently verify the external source before manual installation. <br>
