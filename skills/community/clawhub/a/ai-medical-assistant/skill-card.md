## Use Case: <br>
External users and health-focused agents use this skill to provide Chinese-language medical information support for symptom analysis, medication lookup, report interpretation, medical literature summaries, triage guidance, and health management. It is for health reference and education, not diagnosis, prescribing, emergency care, or treatment decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce broad medical information that users may mistake for diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, dosage changes, or medication-adjustment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only as health reference material and require review by a licensed clinician for diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, dosage, or medication decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may rely on the assistant during urgent symptoms or medical emergencies. <br>
Mitigation: For emergency symptoms, direct users to emergency services or the nearest emergency department instead of continuing normal assistant guidance. <br>
Risk: Health prompts and connected tools may expose personal medical details. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize personal health information shared with the skill and any connected model or tool provider. <br>
