## Description: <br>
Comprehensive apparel and accessories industry suppliers guide for international buyers - provides detailed information about China's garment, footwear, bag, and fashion accessory manufacturing clusters, supply chain structure, regional specializations, and industry trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
International buyers, sourcing teams, and agents use this skill to understand China's apparel and accessories manufacturing clusters, supply chain structure, subsector strengths, and supplier evaluation practices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The bundled dataset includes individual factory contact emails even though the skill description says it does not include individual supplier contacts. <br>
Mitigation: Review contact data before use, verify suppliers independently, and avoid automated or unsolicited outreach. <br>
