## Description: <br>
Funding guides agents through fundraising strategy, pitch materials, investor or donor psychology, and closing workflows for startups, nonprofits, crowdfunding, and personal campaigns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, nonprofit teams, crowdfunders, and individuals use this skill to plan capital raises, shape investor or donor messaging, draft fundraising materials, and review closing considerations before outreach. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated fundraising materials may include inaccurate projections, traction claims, donor-impact statements, investor-interest references, legal terms, or risk disclosures. <br>
Mitigation: Fact-check all claims and have qualified financial, legal, or domain reviewers approve materials before sending them to investors, donors, crowdfunding platforms, or the public. <br>
Risk: The skill provides advisory drafting support and may not reflect the user's specific jurisdiction, transaction structure, or fundraising obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as planning and drafting assistance only, and tailor final materials with professional review for the applicable fundraising context. <br>
