## Description: <br>
A universal business skill framework for structuring task-centered AI pipeline workflows across intelligence, content, operations, analytics, knowledge, compliance, customer, workflow, qualification, and supply-chain functions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, consultants, and developers use this skill to map business goals into reusable capability units, industry-calibrated workflows, and Markdown pipeline outputs for planning, operations, analysis, compliance, customer work, and supply-chain coordination. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business workflow use may involve sensitive business, personal, regulated, financial, customer, or confidential data. <br>
Mitigation: Use authorized, minimum-necessary data and ensure consent or another lawful basis, access controls, and a retention plan before use. <br>
Risk: Generated workflow, CRM, compliance, financial, customer-facing, public, or profiling outputs may be incomplete or misleading if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review outputs before relying on them for operational decisions, external distribution, regulated activity, or customer records. <br>
