## Description: <br>
Produces 2-4 distinct video script variants from one brief, using audience-specific labels, interleaved scene writing, word count targets, and a handoff checklist. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nissan](https://clawhub.ai/user/nissan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content teams and agents use this skill to draft multiple differentiated demo-video scripts from a single product brief. It is intended for workflows that need technical, general, and developer-oriented variants prepared together for review and production handoff. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scripts may retain project-specific handoff names or approval language that is not appropriate for another production process. <br>
Mitigation: Review each script before external sharing and replace project-specific names, approval steps, and handoff notes for the target workflow. <br>
Risk: Variants can drift toward the same message, reuse openings, leak jargon across audiences, or exceed the intended narration length. <br>
Mitigation: Run the differentiation test and final checklist across all variants, including unique openings, audience-specific jargon checks, and explicit word counts. <br>
