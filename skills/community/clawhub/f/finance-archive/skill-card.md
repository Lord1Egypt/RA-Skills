## Description: <br>
A finance archiving skill for organizing vouchers, contracts, attachments, financial records, and audit-support materials into standardized archive structures. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aolikeji](https://clawhub.ai/user/aolikeji) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Finance, accounting, and audit-support teams use this skill to classify, name, retain, deduplicate, and package finance documents such as vouchers, contracts, invoices, bank records, tax materials, and audit workpapers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Running adapted examples on production finance folders could rename, rewrite, compress, or archive local documents. <br>
Mitigation: Work on copies or controlled folders first, and review file paths and outputs before applying changes to live records. <br>
Risk: Retention and destruction guidance may not match an organization's legal, accounting, or records-management obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Verify retention and destruction policies with the organization's legal, accounting, and records-management owners before use. <br>
