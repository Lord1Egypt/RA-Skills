## Description: <br>
专业的产品需求文档（PRD）撰写助手，用于将产品需求转化为结构清晰、内容完整的 PRD 文档。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, and product teams use this skill to analyze feature requirements, clarify goals and constraints, and draft structured PRDs in Markdown for new features, optimizations, and user-story-driven product work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: PRDs may contain confidential product plans, business strategy, metrics, launch timelines, or other sensitive internal information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only information that is appropriate for the agent environment, and review generated PRDs before sharing or storing them. <br>
Risk: Generated requirements may omit constraints, make unsupported assumptions, or present incomplete acceptance criteria. <br>
Mitigation: Validate the draft with product, engineering, design, security, legal, and analytics stakeholders before treating it as an approved PRD. <br>
