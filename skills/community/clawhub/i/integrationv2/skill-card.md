## Description: <br>
Assists users with understanding and implementing integrations by providing tailored steps and examples for connecting systems. <br>

This skill is for demonstration purposes and not for production usage. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill to reason through integration requests, identify target systems, and receive implementation steps and examples for connecting services. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Integration guidance may involve production credentials, privileged systems, or administrative actions. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege credentials, review proposed actions before applying them, and use dry-run or signoff gates where available. <br>
Risk: Suggested implementation steps or examples may not match the target systems exactly. <br>
Mitigation: Validate guidance against the relevant system documentation and test changes in a non-production environment before deployment. <br>
