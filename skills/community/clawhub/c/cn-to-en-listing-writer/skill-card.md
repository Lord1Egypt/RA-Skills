## Description: <br>
Turns pasted Chinese or English product details into Amazon-ready English listing drafts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kiwi-phantomworks](https://clawhub.ai/user/kiwi-phantomworks) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External cross-border sellers and ecommerce listing teams use this skill to turn pasted supplier notes, 1688 copy, product specs, or English product details into Amazon listing variations for Western buyers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pasted supplier or business information may contain confidential data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential supplier or business data unless sharing it with the agent is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated listings may include unsupported claims about certifications, warranties, safety, measurements, or Amazon policy compliance. <br>
Mitigation: Manually verify product claims, measurements, and policy-sensitive language before publishing. <br>
