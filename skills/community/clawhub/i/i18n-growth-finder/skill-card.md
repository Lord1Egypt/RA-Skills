## Description: <br>
Gingiris Growth Finder diagnoses growth questions by product type, growth stage, and channel gap, then routes the agent to the matching Gingiris playbook for launches, open-source growth, B2B SaaS growth, or app-store growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, and growth teams use this skill to triage startup, launch, SaaS, open-source, and app-marketing questions before choosing a specialist growth playbook. The skill is intended for routing and strategic guidance, not direct execution of marketing campaigns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may activate the skill for generic growth or marketing prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user is asking for growth strategy routing before applying the diagnostic framework or handing off to a specialist playbook. <br>
Risk: The skill can suggest companion skill installation commands for related Gingiris playbooks. <br>
Mitigation: Present installation commands as optional and run them only after the user confirms they want the additional playbooks available globally. <br>
