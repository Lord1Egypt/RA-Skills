## Description: <br>
Office Hours guides users through YC-style product discovery by testing needs, challenging assumptions, and producing a design document before implementation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[LawrencePage](https://clawhub.ai/user/LawrencePage) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, product teams, and builders use this skill to pressure-test product or feature ideas before writing code. It asks one question at a time, validates demand evidence, compares implementation paths, and ends with a design document and next action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product-planning guidance may over-challenge assumptions or steer users away from implementation before enough context is gathered. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated design document against user goals and available evidence before treating its recommendation as the implementation plan. <br>
