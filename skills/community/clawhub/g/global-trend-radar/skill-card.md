## Description: <br>
Global Trend Radar monitors and analyzes emerging trends across technology, economics, geopolitics, climate, demographics, and innovation using multi-source web intelligence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Decision-makers, researchers, strategists, and analysts use this skill to identify, compare, and explain global trends with source-backed forecasts, regional comparisons, and actionable implications. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trend forecasts and market interpretations may be incomplete, stale, or speculative. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research assistance, verify important claims independently, and preserve confidence levels and uncertainty notes. <br>
Risk: The bundled source catalog may contain encoding or formatting issues. <br>
Mitigation: Check source names, keyword mappings, and URLs before relying on the catalog for multilingual or region-specific research. <br>
