## Description: <br>
Extracts clean, LLM-optimized content from one or more static or JavaScript-rendered web pages using Firecrawl. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[eohmig](https://clawhub.ai/user/eohmig) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to scrape provided URLs into clean markdown or related formats for reading, extraction, and content workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: URLs, private page content, or sensitive query parameters may be processed by Firecrawl or saved in local .firecrawl output files. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved public URLs when possible, avoid embedding secrets in URLs, and review or remove saved .firecrawl files that may contain sensitive content. <br>
