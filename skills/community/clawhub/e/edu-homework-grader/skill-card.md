## Description: <br>
Grades K-12 homework for Chinese, Math, English, Physics, and Chemistry, combining deterministic scoring for objective items with rubric-based feedback for subjective answers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[boboy-j](https://clawhub.ai/user/boboy-j) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Teachers, tutors, and learners use this skill to score submitted homework against an answer key or rubric and produce feedback reports, common-error analysis, and improvement suggestions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can process sensitive student information in homework, rosters, or generated reports. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize or anonymize names and IDs, restrict access to generated files, confirm approved OCR or assistant providers before using real student data, and set a deletion schedule. <br>
Risk: Automated grading can produce incorrect or incomplete scores, especially for subjective answers. <br>
Mitigation: Require teacher review before using outputs as official grades and keep rubrics and answer keys auditable. <br>
