## Description: <br>
谋道 is an AI planning skill that turns ideas into actionable plans using the 道法术器 framework, with research, quick-plan, deep-planning, and diagnostic modes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to research an idea, generate quick or deep plans, and diagnose execution problems for projects, careers, learning paths, startups, and personal goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided planning details are sent to the configured LLM provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets, confidential business strategy, or sensitive personal details unless the provider and deployment are approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Generated plans may include incorrect or unsuitable high-impact recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify recommendations before using them for business, legal, medical, financial, or other consequential decisions. <br>
