## Description: <br>
Create conversion-focused product marketing videos with Remotion + React using the AIDA framework for launch videos, paid social ads, app promos, SaaS explainers, and product teasers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, marketing teams, and content teams use this skill to turn product briefs into AIDA-structured product marketing videos. It helps create script drafts, scene plan JSON, customized Remotion project files, asset manifests, and final render commands. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bundled server and cloud rendering examples may be reused without production access controls or resource limits. <br>
Mitigation: Treat these snippets as examples and add authentication, authorization, rate limits, input validation, and resource controls before exposing render endpoints publicly. <br>
Risk: Generated ancillary media can weaken or replace the required AIDA narrative if used as the whole advertisement. <br>
Mitigation: Keep Remotion as the final editor and timeline authority, use generated assets only to support scenes, and verify that the video still establishes the problem, introduces the solution, shows use cases, and ends with an incentivized CTA. <br>
