## Description: <br>
Ai Astrochemistry provides Chinese-language guidance on astrochemistry topics including interstellar molecules, dust-grain chemistry, comets, prebiotic chemistry, and radio-astronomy observations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad molecule, comet, or life-origin questions and may answer in Chinese when another language is expected. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for astrochemistry questions and set the host agent's desired response language when needed. <br>
Risk: Educational reference material can contain incomplete, disputed, or outdated scientific claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review important answers against current astrochemistry sources before using them for research, teaching, or publication. <br>
