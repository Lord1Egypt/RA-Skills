# Review Channel Playbook

Each channel has a different cost, conversion rate, and compliance surface. Use them in combination, not in isolation.

## In-package insert
- **Cost:** Negligible (printed during fulfillment).
- **Compliance:** Always allowed; never offer incentives tied to reviews.
- **Best for:** Setting expectation and capturing buyers who never open marketing email.
- **Design:** Short, branded card. QR code to a landing page that redirects to the correct review surface based on order source. One headline ("Help us help future cooks like you"), one line of negative-path support email, no rating direction.
- **Conversion rate:** 0.5-1.5% by itself; amplifies the rate of other channels by 10-20%.

## Platform-native request
- **Amazon Request a Review:** Fires within Amazon's allowed window (5-30 days post-delivery). One per order. No customization. Free.
- **Shopify post-purchase emails:** Built-in or via Shop app. Customizable to a point.
- **Etsy:** Built-in convo with order. Limited.
- **eBay:** Feedback request tool, limited.
- **Compliance:** Safe because the platform owns the message.
- **Conversion rate:** 1-4%.
- **Best for:** Baseline coverage. Always on.

## Branded email
- **Cost:** Whatever your ESP charges per send.
- **Compliance:** Cannot direct rating, cannot offer incentive, must include unsubscribe. Subject line and body should reference the product, not just "review."
- **Best for:** Direct-channel buyers; subscription renewals; higher-AOV cohorts that warrant personalization.
- **Conversion rate:** 2-6% when timed well and segmented.
- **Cadence:** Initial ask + one reminder 5-7 days later, then stop.

## SMS
- **Cost:** Per-message; significant at volume.
- **Compliance:** Opt-in required. TCPA in US. Cannot direct rating. Keep messages short and identifiable.
- **Best for:** Younger demographics, mobile-heavy categories, urgent windows.
- **Conversion rate:** 5-12% when used for buyers who didn't respond to email.
- **Cadence:** One only. Don't stack with email reminder.

## WhatsApp / Messenger
- **Cost:** Per-message; varies by country.
- **Compliance:** Opt-in via business platform. Strict templates.
- **Best for:** Markets where messaging dominates email.
- **Conversion rate:** 8-15%.

## Channel-stack rules
1. No customer receives more than two asks in any 7-day window.
2. The platform-native request always runs (free + safe).
3. If you use both email and SMS, separate by at least 5 days.
4. Negative-path link appears on every channel.
5. Drop any channel that doesn't add at least 0.5 percentage points to overall review rate in a 4-week test.
