## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, AI product leads, and builders use this skill to structure AI product work from discovery through launch evaluation. It produces product-analysis artifacts, PRD templates, AI capability designs, cost estimates, and iteration plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad triggers may cause the skill to activate during general product-management discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when the task is clearly about AI product management, PRDs, Prompt design, Agent workflows, RAG design, evaluation, or cost estimation. <br>
Risk: The prompt pattern reference includes a chain-of-thought style that may be inappropriate for production assistants. <br>
Mitigation: Use concise rationale summaries or structured decision notes instead of asking production assistants to expose detailed private reasoning. <br>
Risk: Bundled cost calculations use reference model prices and assumptions that may become stale. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current provider pricing and workload assumptions before using generated cost estimates for planning. <br>
