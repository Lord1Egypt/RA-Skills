## Description: <br>
AI GIF generation powered by CellCog for reaction GIFs, product loops, cinemagraphs, and social media animations created from scratch rather than converted from existing video. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, creative teams, marketers, and other external users use this skill to ask CellCog to generate optimized GIFs and companion MP4 sources for reactions, product showcases, cinemagraphs, UI demos, and social media content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires a CellCog API key and may send prompts, task context, or media requests to CellCog. <br>
Mitigation: Use a revocable CELLCOG_API_KEY and avoid sending secrets, regulated data, private customer content, or proprietary media unless CellCog policies and organizational rules allow it. <br>
