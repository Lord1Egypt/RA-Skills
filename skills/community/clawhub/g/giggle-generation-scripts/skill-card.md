## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese screenplay drafts with structured synopses, character bios, scene outlines, and dialogue-driven scene scripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[patches429](https://clawhub.ai/user/patches429) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and creative agents use this skill to draft stylized Chinese screenplay material, including synopsis, character design, scene outlines, and serial scene scripts. It is best suited to requests that need dense conflict, black humor, subtext-heavy dialogue, and controlled revision rounds. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is highly opinionated and may be a poor fit for general writing prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for Chinese screenplay drafting tasks where this style is desired, and apply the skill's conflict-handling and assumption-stating steps when inputs are incomplete or inconsistent. <br>
Risk: Requests to imitate existing films could lead to overly similar scenes or lines. <br>
Mitigation: Keep plots, characters, and dialogue original, and follow the skill's explicit quality check to avoid copying specific film scenes or lines. <br>
