## Description: <br>
Generates quality daily, weekly, or monthly reports from key figures and events, with Excel and PPT outputs or equivalent structured content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lams001](https://clawhub.ai/user/lams001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Quality teams, production quality staff, and managers use this skill to draft daily, weekly, or monthly quality reports with KPI summaries, issues, actions, charts, and next-period focus. It can create complete sample-data reports as templates when verified operational data is not available. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reports may include realistic mock figures when users do not provide operational data. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the simulated-data notice visible and replace mock figures with verified data before using the report for decisions or sharing it as an operational record. <br>
Risk: Generated Excel or PPT files may contain inaccurate summaries, charts, or formatting defects. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated files and chart data before distribution, especially when used for management, customer, or quality-system reporting. <br>
Risk: Company-specific quality data or report formats may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving confidential company report contents as reusable templates unless they have been approved for reuse. <br>
