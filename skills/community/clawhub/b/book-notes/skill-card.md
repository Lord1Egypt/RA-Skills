## Description: <br>
Capture reading notes through conversation, organize by book, generate summaries, track reading list. Never lose an insight from a book again. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[theshadowrose](https://clawhub.ai/user/theshadowrose) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Readers, researchers, and developers use this skill to capture book notes conversationally, organize notes by book and chapter, maintain reading-list status, and create summaries or cross-references from local notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local reading notes and tracking files may be changed or lost if the skill is run against an important notes directory. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated books directory and keep independent backups of important notes. <br>
Risk: Reading notes may contain sensitive personal information that could be reused later for summaries or cross-references. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive personal reading notes unless you are comfortable with later reuse by the agent. <br>
