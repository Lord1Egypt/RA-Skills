## Description: <br>
专业级合同风险审查工具，自动识别 20+ 类常见风险条款，给出修改建议和法律依据 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[amaxclaw](https://clawhub.ai/user/amaxclaw) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users such as founders, legal staff, and freelancers use this skill to review Chinese contract text, identify common risk clauses, and receive ranked revision suggestions with cited legal bases. It is intended as a risk-reference workflow and does not replace formal legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill gives legal-risk guidance and clause language that may be incomplete, jurisdiction-specific, or unsuitable for a particular contract. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a review aid only and have significant contracts checked by a qualified lawyer before signing. <br>
Risk: Contract text can contain sensitive business, personal, or confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Review data-handling practices before use and avoid submitting sensitive text unless the workspace and provider are trusted. <br>
Risk: The release advertises paid usage and purchase capability tags. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm pricing, billing intent, and authorization before enabling paid workflows. <br>
