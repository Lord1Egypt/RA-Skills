### Marketplace Description (250 words)

Every B2B company has customer wins. Most of them stay inside Slack threads and onboarding calls — never turned into the content that closes deals.

The Case Study Generator fixes that. Four prompts take you from raw customer details to a complete case study package: a full narrative, a metrics-heavy results section, five pull quotes mapped to every stage of the buying journey, and four ready-to-publish formats (blog post, sales one-pager, LinkedIn story, email snippet).

**What you get:**

- **Prompt 1: Customer Story Narrative** — 700–1,000-word case study with structure that makes buyers say "that's exactly my problem"
- **Prompt 2: Results & Metrics Section** — ROI table, before/after comparisons, data visualization recommendations — the section CFOs actually read
- **Prompt 3: Pull Quotes & Testimonial** — 5 quotes targeting awareness, consideration, decision, results, and advocacy. Plus a 3-sentence email testimonial and 3 one-liners for ad copy
- **Prompt 4: Multi-Format Package** — SEO blog post, PDF-ready sales one-pager, LinkedIn story post, and cold outreach email snippet

Run once, get content for your website, your sales team, your social feeds, and your email sequences — all consistent, all traceable to real numbers.

**Includes** a full worked example: CloudPath Solutions, a 45-person agency that recovered $2,220/month and signed 3 new clients after automating client reporting. All 4 prompts run end-to-end.

One case study that closes one deal will pay for this skill 100x over.
