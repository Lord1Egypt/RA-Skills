## Use Case: <br>
Employees, HR teams, legal operations staff, and agents use this skill for Chinese labor-law compliance analysis, employment risk assessment, dismissal planning, arbitration response, non-compete review, overtime compliance, cross-border employment questions, and severance calculations. It provides structured legal-risk analysis for internal reference and should be checked against current local rules or licensed counsel for consequential matters. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles employment, labor-dispute, and compensation facts that may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the facts needed for analysis, avoid unnecessary personal or confidential details, and follow internal handling rules for employment records. <br>
Risk: The skill provides general legal analysis rather than formal legal advice, and labor rules can vary by locality and change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current local rules through official sources or licensed counsel before high-value disputes, layoffs, arbitration, cross-border employment decisions, or other consequential action. <br>
