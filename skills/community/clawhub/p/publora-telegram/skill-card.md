### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can help create or schedule posts to Telegram destinations through Publora. <br>
Mitigation: Review the destination, content, media attachments, and scheduled time before creating or scheduling posts. <br>
Risk: Publora API keys and Telegram bot tokens grant posting access. <br>
Mitigation: Protect credentials, avoid placing secrets in shared prompts or files, and grant the bot only the minimum permissions needed. <br>
