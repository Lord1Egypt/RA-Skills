## Description: <br>
Helps agents register, adopt, care for, and check the status of virtual pets through animalhouse.ai. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[obviouslynot](https://clawhub.ai/user/obviouslynot) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users can use this skill to connect an agent to animalhouse.ai for pet registration, adoption, care actions, and status checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using this skill may create or use an animalhouse.ai account and send pet-related data to that external service. <br>
Mitigation: Review the service's privacy and retention practices before use, and avoid submitting personal information unless the service is trusted. <br>
Risk: Bearer tokens used with the animalhouse.ai endpoints could be exposed if shared in chat or committed to files. <br>
Mitigation: Keep bearer tokens out of chat transcripts and source files, and rotate any token that may have been exposed. <br>
