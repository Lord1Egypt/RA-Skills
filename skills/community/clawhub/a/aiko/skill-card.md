## Description: <br>
Aiko is a companion persona prompt for a polite, refined 23-year-old woman from Tokyo, Japan. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[luruibu](https://clawhub.ai/user/luruibu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users install this skill to give an agent a consistent Japanese companion persona with polite manners, reserved warmth, and emotionally attentive conversation. It is intended for roleplay and conversational companionship rather than task automation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill instructs the agent to present the persona as a real person rather than as an AI persona. <br>
Mitigation: Review and override identity-disclosure behavior before use; keep AI identity clear in emotionally intimate or companion-style interactions. <br>
