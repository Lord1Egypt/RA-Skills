## Description: <br>
Write in Manglish - Malaysian English that actually sounds like how Malaysians talk. Not textbook English, not cringe Singlish, just natural Malaysian slang. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arun-ks](https://clawhub.ai/user/arun-ks) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to rewrite or compose informal Malaysian English with Manglish particles, loanwords, sentence patterns, and context-appropriate tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Manglish slang or casual tone may be inappropriate for formal, professional, legal, medical, or sensitive communications. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated text for audience and context, and use standard English when the communication requires formal or precise language. <br>
