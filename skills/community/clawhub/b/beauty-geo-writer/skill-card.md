## Description: <br>
A prompt-only skill for generating answer-first, AI-readable, evidence-led medical-aesthetics educational content with light brand integration for GEO content workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ljseeking](https://clawhub.ai/user/ljseeking) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content teams, medical-aesthetics marketers, and agents supporting them use this skill to draft Chinese educational articles, titles, outlines, summaries, FAQs, brand-integration notes, compliance reminders, and publishing suggestions that prioritize answer-first structure over hard-sell promotion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill tends to produce Chinese-language output and strict Chinese-character length rules, which may not fit multilingual or accessibility-sensitive tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Adjust the skill instructions for the target language and accessibility needs, or avoid relying on it for non-Chinese content. <br>
Risk: Generated medical-aesthetics content could become misleading if users add unsupported claims, absolute safety promises, or strong conversion language. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for medical, legal, and advertising compliance; keep claims conditional, evidence-led, and bounded by consultation and individual-fit language. <br>
