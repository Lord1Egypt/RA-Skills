## Description: <br>
A four-round deep research protocol for major architectural decisions, technology choices, and strategic questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[NakedoShadow](https://clawhub.ai/user/NakedoShadow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and technical decision makers use this skill to structure deep analysis of complex architecture, technology, and strategy decisions into landscape mapping, stress testing, multi-perspective synthesis, and a recommendation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad analysis wording can invoke a full four-round research process when a shorter response would be sufficient. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill for major architecture, technology, and strategy decisions, and avoid invoking it for simple factual questions or quick fixes. <br>
Risk: Research queries may be sent through the agent's normal search provider when search is available. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive or private research topics unless the agent's search path is approved, and review cited sources before relying on the final recommendation. <br>
