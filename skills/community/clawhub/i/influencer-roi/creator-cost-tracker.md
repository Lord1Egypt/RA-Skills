## Benchmark: Typical Cost Ranges by Creator Tier

| Tier | Followers | Typical Flat Fee | Seeding Cost | Licensing (Paid Ads) | Expected ROAS Range |
|------|-----------|-----------------|-------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Nano | 1K–10K | $50–$300 | $50–$150 | Rarely required | 8–25x |
| Micro | 10K–100K | $200–$2,000 | $100–$300 | $200–$800/quarter | 5–15x |
| Mid-tier | 100K–500K | $1,000–$10,000 | $200–$500 | $500–$2,500/quarter | 3–8x |
| Macro | 500K–1M | $5,000–$25,000 | $300–$1,000 | $1,500–$7,500/quarter | 2–5x |
| Mega / Celebrity | 1M+ | $20,000–$500,000+ | $500–$2,000 | Negotiate separately | 1–3x (awareness play) |

*Note: ROAS ranges assume direct-response intent and discount code attribution. Brand awareness campaigns will skew lower on direct ROAS.*

## Red Flags in Cost Negotiations

- **Creator asks for licensing rights in perpetuity** → Cap to 12 months with renewal option.
- **Agency charges >25% management fee** → Industry standard is 15–20%. Negotiate down.
- **No content approval step in contract** → Always include 48h review period before posting.
- **Commission-only deal with a macro creator** → Without a minimum guarantee, top creators won't prioritize your product. Set a base fee + commission.
- **No exclusivity clause for competitor brands** → Request 30–60 day category exclusivity for direct-response campaigns.
