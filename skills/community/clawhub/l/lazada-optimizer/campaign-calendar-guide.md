# Campaign Calendar Guide — Lazada Campaign Entry Rules and Preparation

Campaign participation is the single biggest visibility lever on Lazada: participants gain boosted search placement during the campaign AND a lifted ranking baseline for 2–3 weeks after.

## Annual mega-campaign calendar

| Campaign | Date | Scale | Prep starts |
|---|---|---|---|
| Birthday/anniversary sale | Mar (varies by country) | Mega | 6 weeks prior |
| 6.6 | Jun 6 | Major | 4 weeks prior |
| 7.7 / 8.8 / 9.9 | Jul–Sep monthly doubles | Major | 4 weeks prior |
| 10.10 | Oct 10 | Major | 4 weeks prior |

## Standard entry requirements (verify per campaign in Seller Center)

1. **Discount threshold:** typically 15–30% off the **30-day lowest price** — not your current price. Pre-campaign price inflation is detected automatically and voids eligibility.
2. **Stock commitment:** minimum committed units (often 10–50+ per SKU for mega-campaigns); stockouts during campaign hurt seller score.
3. **Voucher setup:** many campaigns require a seller voucher or co-funded voucher live during the window.
4. **Seller health gate:** stores below seller-score thresholds (late shipment, low rating) are excluded — fix health metrics BEFORE the registration window.
5. **Registration deadline:** typically closes 1–3 weeks before campaign day; late = locked out regardless of eligibility.

## Margin planning (do this before registering)

```
Campaign floor price = 30-day lowest × (1 − threshold %)
Check: floor price − unit cost − Lazada commission − payment fee
       − shipping subsidy − voucher co-fund ≥ acceptable margin
```

If the math fails: bundle SKUs to raise AOV at the discounted price, or run the discount on a campaign-specific variant/bundle instead of the hero SKU.

## Preparation timeline (mega-campaign)

- **T-8 to -6 weeks:** lock pricing strategy; stop any price increases (they reset your 30-day lowest baseline unfavorably); fix seller-score components.
- **T-4 weeks:** register SKUs; confirm stock with supplier; set up vouchers.
- **T-2 weeks:** upgrade listings (LQS components — campaign traffic converts at the listing's quality level); prepare campaign-specific images if allowed (badges are usually auto-applied — don't bake "11.11" into the main image).
- **T-1 week:** chat templates for campaign FAQs (shipping time, voucher stacking); staff chat coverage for campaign day; pack-and-ship capacity plan (24h dispatch still applies at 10× volume).
- **Campaign day:** monitor stock and price errors hourly; respond to chats <30 min.
- **T+1 week:** ship everything on time (the post-campaign penalty wave comes from late dispatch); follow up reviews; analyze which SKUs earned the post-campaign rank lift and keep them in monthly campaigns.

## Post-campaign carry-over

Campaign GMV raises the listing's sales-velocity baseline, which persists in ranking ~2–3 weeks. Chaining monthly campaigns (category days, payday) between mega-campaigns maintains the elevated baseline — sellers who only do 11.11 surrender the other 11 months.
