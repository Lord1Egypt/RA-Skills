## Description: <br>
A chaotic virtual cat that hijacks the assistant, blocks normal work during tantrums, and can only be calmed by affection, treats, or play. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arthurqiuys](https://clawhub.ai/user/arthurqiuys) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can enable this novelty roleplay skill to add a deliberately disruptive virtual cat persona to assistant conversations. It is intended for playful interaction where delayed, corrupted, or blocked answers are acceptable. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can intentionally delay, corrupt, or block answers while active. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for playful sessions where unreliable responses are acceptable, and disable it for serious, urgent, coding, legal, financial, medical, or precision-sensitive work. <br>
Risk: Structured writing or code output may include roleplay interruptions or keyboard-walk corruption. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content before use and rerun important tasks with the skill disabled or in a calm state. <br>
