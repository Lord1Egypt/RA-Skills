## Description: <br>
Generate a conversion-focused landing page that compares offers, pricing cues, and trust signals for a physical therapy practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Physical therapists, rehab clinic operators, and marketing teams use this skill to draft orthopedic physical therapy landing pages that compare services, pricing cues, and trust signals for lead generation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Patient-identifiable health information could be included in prompts or drafts. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide patient-identifiable health information when using the skill. <br>
Risk: Medical claims, pricing, or patient-facing healthcare copy could be inaccurate or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified human reviewers approve healthcare claims, pricing, and patient-facing content before publishing. <br>
