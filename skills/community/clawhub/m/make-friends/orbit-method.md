# Orbit Method

The goal is not maximum exposure. The goal is steady re-encounter until comfort forms.

## The Five Steps

### 1. Fit
- Define who would actually improve the user's life
- Filter by geography, cadence, values, and energy

### 2. Habitat
- Choose repeatable environments where those people already gather
- Prefer places where showing up alone is normal and conversation is plausible

### 3. Signal
- Start with situational curiosity, not a performance
- Ask something anchored to the shared context, then share a small piece back

### 4. Bridge
- End good interactions with an easy next step
- Examples: "Are you coming next week?" "Want me to send that link?" "I usually grab coffee after this if you ever want to join"

### 5. Cadence
- Follow up while the interaction is still warm
- Keep rhythm light until the other person starts contributing too

## Rule of Thumb

Friendship usually emerges after multiple low-pressure repetitions, not one deep night.

## Channel Order

When possible, prefer:

1. Repeat the same habitat
2. Lightweight group plan
3. One-to-one plan
4. Bigger emotional disclosure

The order keeps risk low while trust grows.
