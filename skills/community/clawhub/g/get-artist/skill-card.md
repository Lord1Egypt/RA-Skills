## Description: <br>
Look up an Art Blocks artist and their body of work using artblocks-mcp. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ryley-o](https://clawhub.ai/user/ryley-o) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to find an Art Blocks artist by name, slug, or wallet address and review the artist's project portfolio, metadata, floor prices, tags, and project links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on the configured artblocks-mcp service for artist lookup results. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use it only when the configured artblocks-mcp service is trusted. <br>
Risk: Wallet addresses or other artist identifiers submitted for lookup may be sensitive to the user. <br>
Mitigation: Use public identifiers where possible and avoid submitting wallet addresses or identifiers considered private. <br>
