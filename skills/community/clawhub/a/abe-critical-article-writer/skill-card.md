## Description: <br>
Generate draft articles, outlines, and editorial content matching a distinctive analytical, skeptical voice with sharp critical commentary, conversational tone, and strategic humor. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abeltennyson](https://clawhub.ai/user/abeltennyson) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers, analysts, and content teams use this skill to draft skeptical but fair technology critique articles, outlines, social threads, quick takes, and research-backed opinion pieces. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, drafts, and related context may be sent to SkillBoss API Hub and downstream model providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting secrets, regulated personal data, or confidential business material unless the service has been approved by the user's organization. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a SkillBoss API key. <br>
Mitigation: Store the API key in an approved secret manager or environment variable and do not include it in prompts, drafts, or shared documents. <br>
Risk: Generated critical commentary can be misleading if claims, statistics, or source links are not verified. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated drafts against the provided evidence, confirm major claims and links, and add counterarguments or uncertainty where warranted. <br>
