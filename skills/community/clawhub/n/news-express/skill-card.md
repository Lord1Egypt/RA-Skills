## Description: <br>
Use this skill when users ask for news updates, daily briefings, or what's happening in the world. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use News Express to fetch public RSS feeds and receive concise domestic and international news briefings by category. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad news-related trigger phrases may activate this skill when the user intended project or personal updates. <br>
Mitigation: Review the user request and narrow trigger wording when overlapping skills are installed. <br>
Risk: RSS feed availability or network access can affect coverage and freshness. <br>
Mitigation: Use backup sources when a feed fails and treat RSS timestamps as source freshness indicators. <br>
