## Use Case: <br>
External users and relationship-support agents use this skill to analyze Chinese-language relationship messages for manipulative patterns, explain likely tactics, and produce defensive response guidance. It is not a substitute for professional advice in abuse, legal, or mental-health situations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill contains dual-use examples of manipulative relationship tactics. <br>
Mitigation: Treat examples as red flags to recognize and defend against, not scripts to use. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on the skill for serious relationship, abuse, legal, or mental-health decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as pattern-recognition support only and seek qualified professional help for serious situations. <br>
Risk: The framework is based on Chinese-language source material and includes gendered assumptions from the source corpus. <br>
Mitigation: Apply findings as context-specific communication analysis rather than diagnosis or universal judgment. <br>
