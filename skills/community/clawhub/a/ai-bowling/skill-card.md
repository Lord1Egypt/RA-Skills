## Description: <br>
Ai Bowling provides a Chinese-language bowling guide for lane basics, equipment selection, four-step approach, ball release, targeting, spare systems, oil patterns, scoring, and strike or spare strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External bowling learners and recreational players use this skill to ask for Chinese-language guidance on fundamentals, equipment, throwing styles, targeting, spare conversion, scoring, and oil-pattern adjustments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bowling technique, equipment, or fit guidance may not match a user's body mechanics, health constraints, lane conditions, or local coaching standards. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as general guidance, confirm ball fit and drilling with a qualified pro shop or coach, and stop any technique that causes pain or discomfort. <br>
Risk: Rules, scoring explanations, and oil-pattern strategy can be incomplete or stale for a specific league, tournament, or venue. <br>
Mitigation: Check the current event, league, or venue rules before relying on the guidance for competition decisions. <br>
