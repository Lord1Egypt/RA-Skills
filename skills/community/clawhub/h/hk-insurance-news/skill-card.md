## Description: <br>
Generates a weekly WeChat Official Account draft that summarizes important Hong Kong insurance market news from the previous week. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[castlewang](https://clawhub.ai/user/castlewang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Insurance analysts, marketers, and editors use this skill to collect Hong Kong insurance industry news from the prior week and draft a categorized public-facing weekly article with summaries, source links, and image references. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted news summaries may be inaccurate, stale, or based on weak sources. <br>
Mitigation: Review facts, source reliability, and cited links before publishing. <br>
Risk: Included images or remote image links may raise rights, relevance, or safety issues. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm image rights and inspect image links before using them in a public article. <br>
Risk: Adding automatic publishing credentials would expand the skill's impact beyond drafting. <br>
Mitigation: Keep publishing behind a separate explicit approval step and avoid storing posting credentials in the skill. <br>
