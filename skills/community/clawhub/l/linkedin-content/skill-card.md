## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes broad infsh CLI authority and references a pipe-to-shell installer. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when inference.sh is trusted; prefer manual download and checksum verification before using the CLI. <br>
Risk: The skill includes an example that can post to a public social platform from a logged-in account. <br>
Mitigation: Keep publish-capable accounts under explicit user control and require human review before any command posts externally. <br>
Risk: Generated social content can be inaccurate, misleading, or unsuitable for a brand voice. <br>
Mitigation: Review drafts for factual accuracy, tone, attribution, and platform policy fit before publication. <br>
