## Description: <br>
Novel Studio is a Chinese web-novel writing studio with specialized agents for market analysis, worldbuilding, character design, outlining, drafting, editing, continuity management, platform compliance, and genre-specific support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

[weihfei](https://clawhub.ai/user/weihfei) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Authors and writing assistants use this skill to plan, draft, revise, and maintain continuity for Chinese web novels across common genres and publishing platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill reads and writes manuscript project files, which can alter planning, continuity, and chapter documents. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in a dedicated novel-writing workspace with backups or version control, as recommended by the security guidance. <br>
Risk: Compliance and copyright reports may be incomplete for a specific publishing platform or legal situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those reports as drafting guidance and verify platform rules or legal questions with authoritative sources before publication. <br>
Risk: Generated drafts and continuity updates can introduce story, character, or style inconsistencies. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against project.md, character cards, style guidance, and the fact database before accepting manuscript changes. <br>
