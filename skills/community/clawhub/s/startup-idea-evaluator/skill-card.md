## Description: <br>
通过结构化框架评估创业想法的可行性与市场潜力，输出包含量化评分、市场分析、风险识别和行动建议的完整评估报告。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs and startup operators use this skill to evaluate early-stage business ideas, compare options, and identify market, resource, timing, and personal-fit risks before committing effort or budget. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can influence real startup planning and resource allocation decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated recommendations as decision support, validate key market and financial assumptions independently, and review any suggested actions before committing funds or operational changes. <br>
Risk: Incomplete user input can lead to overconfident scoring or weak risk analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Provide detailed market, customer, resource, and founder-background information, and use the skill's follow-up questions to fill critical gaps before relying on the report. <br>
Risk: Evidence security guidance advises review before running commands or using workflow authority. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands and high-impact actions before execution, and reserve repository, moderation, migration, proof-publishing, or account-level actions for authorized environments. <br>
