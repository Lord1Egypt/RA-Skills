# 科创信息监控源清单

> **公开信息声明**
> 以下所有 URL 均为可公开访问的政府门户、行业媒体或企业数据平台。本技能仅采集这些网站上公开可获取的信息，
> 不访问任何需要登录或授权的非公开内容。企业数据平台（企查查、天眼查等）仅作为参考来源列出，
> 用户应遵守各自平台的服务条款。

> **Public Information Only Notice**
> All URLs listed are publicly accessible government portals, industry media, or enterprise data
> platforms. This skill only collects publicly available information and does not access any
> login-restricted data. Enterprise data platforms (qcc.com, tianyancha.com, etc.) are listed for
> reference only; users should comply with their respective terms of service.
