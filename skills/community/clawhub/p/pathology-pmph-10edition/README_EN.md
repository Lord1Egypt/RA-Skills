## About This Project

This project systematically integrates core pathology theory, disease mechanism analysis, tissue diagnostic criteria, molecular subtyping strategies, and clinical translation applications, covering **145 key skills** across 7 major categories.

**Target Audience**: Medical students, pathologists, clinical researchers, public health workers

**Reference Textbook**: People's Medical Publishing House "Pathology" 10th Edition (July 2024)

**⚠️Risk⚠️**: This skill covers pathological diagnosis, disease mechanism analysis, molecular subtyping, and treatment decision support — content that may be misused as independent medical advice.

Mitigation: Use output solely as reference material for education or clinician review, and verify recommendations against current official guidelines, local protocols, and qualified pathology experts.

### Query Examples

#### 1. Concept
Question:
```bash
Explain the pathogenesis of atherosclerosis
```

#### 2. Case Analysis
Question:
```bash
A 45-year-old woman presents with bilateral lower extremity edema and foamy urine for 2 months. Urinalysis: protein (+++), occult blood (+). 24h urine protein 4.2g. Serum albumin 28g/L. What is the most likely diagnosis?
```

#### 3. Molecular Typing

