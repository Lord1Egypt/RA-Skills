## Description: <br>
Ai Mycology is a Chinese-language educational mycology guide covering fungal taxonomy, structure, reproduction, mycorrhizae, edible and toxic mushrooms, medicinal and economic fungi, fungal ecology, and fungal diseases. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, naturalists, ecology and agriculture practitioners, and food or fermentation enthusiasts use this skill for educational explanations about fungi, mushrooms, mycorrhizal networks, fungal ecology, and common fungal risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat educational mushroom-identification guidance as proof that a wild mushroom is edible or safe. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as an educational mycology reference; get in-person confirmation from a qualified local expert and contact emergency services or poison control after suspected exposure. <br>
