## Description: <br>
Agent assistant pour entrepreneurs et freelances francophones qui gere les emails professionnels, genere des devis et factures, relance les clients, prepare des briefs, et automatise la prospection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yerrochdi](https://clawhub.ai/user/yerrochdi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs and freelancers serving French-speaking clients use this skill to draft professional emails, quotes, invoices, payment reminders, client briefs, and prospecting messages in French. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business, client, bank, or personal details may be stored in persistent memory if users follow the configuration guidance without minimizing sensitive data. <br>
Mitigation: Store only the minimum details needed, avoid broad memory storage for IBAN or client personal data unless appropriate, and review generated invoices, legal wording, emails, and prospecting messages before use. <br>
