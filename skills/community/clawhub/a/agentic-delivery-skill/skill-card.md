## Description: <br>
Create CompleteTech LLC delivery execution artifacts for approved agentic development engagements, including kickoff agendas, access checklists, project plans, milestone trackers, status updates, decision logs, risk/issue logs, change request intake, prototype review, evaluation reports, acceptance packets, launch readiness, monitoring, support, handoff, runbooks, quickstarts, closeout, post-launch review, and escalation procedures. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[completetech](https://clawhub.ai/user/completetech) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and delivery teams use this skill after proposal, SOW, or contract approval to draft bounded delivery artifacts for agentic workflow engagements. It supports kickoff, access, planning, evaluation, acceptance, launch readiness, support, handoff, and closeout while preserving human approval gates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated acceptance, launch, support, and handoff artifacts may contain unverified facts or approval-sensitive language. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts until a human verifies the facts, approvals, readiness, and client-facing language. <br>
Risk: The skill writes local delivery artifacts to selected output paths. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit output paths and review generated files before sharing, storing, or treating them as delivery evidence. <br>
