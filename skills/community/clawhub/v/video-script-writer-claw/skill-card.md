## Description: <br>
视频脚本主笔虾 generates short-video shot scripts, podcast outlines, and series content plans from an account style profile and topic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tujinsama](https://clawhub.ai/user/tujinsama) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and podcast producers use this skill to create platform-ready video scripts, podcast segment outlines, and multi-episode content plans with consistent account voice and production notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scripts may include inaccurate claims, unsuitable platform guidance, or copyright-sensitive material. <br>
Mitigation: Review scripts for factual accuracy, copyright concerns, and platform rules before publishing. <br>
Risk: Account style notes or research prompts may contain sensitive personal or business information. <br>
Mitigation: Remove confidential details from style guides and prompts before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Generated content may drift from the creator's voice or brand restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Check the draft against the account style guide and prohibited words before production. <br>
