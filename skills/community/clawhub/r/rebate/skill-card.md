## Description: <br>
Multi-platform rebate management for tracking rebate order status, payout progress, analytics, and savings reports across shopping platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to organize rebate activity across shopping platforms, monitor pending or settled amounts, and generate monthly or annual savings reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may handle account-linked rebate and order data without clearly disclosed privacy and consent boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Before use, confirm which platforms and order or account fields it accesses, whether data is stored or shared, and require explicit confirmation before any sync or report generation. <br>
