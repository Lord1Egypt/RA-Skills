## Description: <br>
Create professional cinematic scripts for AI video generation with character consistency, cinematography guidance, prompt generation, anachronism checking, and Google Drive or local project saving. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[praveenspeaks](https://clawhub.ai/user/praveenspeaks) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, creators, and developers use this skill to create cinematic scripts, story contexts, character and voice consistency guides, AI image or video prompts, cinematography recommendations, and YouTube metadata. It can export generated projects as Markdown, JSON, or text and save organized project files to Google Drive or local storage. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scripts, prompts, character references, metadata, or project files may contain private or unpublished creative material. <br>
Mitigation: Decide what content can be stored before connecting Google Drive or local storage, use a dedicated project folder, and review generated Drive links before sharing. <br>
Risk: Saving to broad local paths can expose or mix generated project files with unrelated personal files. <br>
Mitigation: Use a narrow, dedicated local folder for project storage and avoid broad paths such as the home directory. <br>
