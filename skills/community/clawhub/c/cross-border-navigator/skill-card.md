## Description: <br>
Cross Border Navigator helps agents produce cross-border market entry intelligence, including regulatory, tax, cultural, geopolitical, sanctions, and operational risk analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External business users, global strategists, exporters, investors, supply chain managers, and legal or compliance teams use this skill to structure market entry reports, compliance checks, cultural playbooks, risk matrices, and action plans for international operations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Regulatory, sanctions, tax, and market-entry information can become outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims against current official sources and qualified local counsel before making binding legal, tax, compliance, or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may produce business intelligence that could be mistaken for professional legal, tax, compliance, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as decision-support research, include appropriate caveats, and require expert review for high-impact decisions. <br>
