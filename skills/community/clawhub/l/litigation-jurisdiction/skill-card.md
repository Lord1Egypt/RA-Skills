## Description: <br>
Helps users determine the appropriate court for domestic Chinese civil litigation, including level, territorial, exclusive, and special-case jurisdiction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xuleff](https://clawhub.ai/user/xuleff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and legal-support agents use this skill to gather relevant case facts and reason through the likely court jurisdiction for domestic Chinese civil litigation. It supports preliminary analysis of level jurisdiction, territorial jurisdiction, exclusive jurisdiction, agreed jurisdiction, and special case categories. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Jurisdiction guidance may be incorrect or outdated for a specific court, case posture, or filing rule. <br>
Mitigation: Verify conclusions against current court rules, official filing requirements, or a qualified lawyer before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive personal or case facts while seeking jurisdiction analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the facts needed to identify jurisdiction and avoid unnecessary personal details. <br>
