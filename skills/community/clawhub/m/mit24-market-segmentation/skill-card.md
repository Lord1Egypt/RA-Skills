## Description: <br>
Guides founders through the first five MIT 24 Steps tasks for market segmentation, beachhead selection, persona development, TAM/SAM/SOM sizing, and customer decision analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[earthwalking](https://clawhub.ai/user/earthwalking) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders, startup teams, and venture builders use this skill to structure early market research into a market segmentation and customer insight report. It is useful when narrowing a broad opportunity into priority segments, a beachhead market, personas, market-size assumptions, and a customer decision journey. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter confidential customer lists, proprietary financials, or sensitive market research while building segmentation and market-size outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential or sensitive business data unless the agent environment is approved for that information. <br>
Risk: Market size, persona, and beachhead recommendations can be misleading if based on unverified assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate outputs against customer interviews, cited data sources, and explicit TAM/SAM/SOM calculation assumptions before business decisions. <br>
Risk: The source skill content is primarily written in Chinese, which may reduce usability for non-Chinese readers. <br>
Mitigation: Translate or localize the workflow before use with teams that need another working language. <br>
