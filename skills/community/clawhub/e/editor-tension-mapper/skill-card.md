## Description: <br>
Maps screenplay tension curves, detects pacing issues such as flat segments, whipsaw shifts, midpoint collapse, and missing breather scenes, and returns quantitative diagnostics with editorial recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huangliujiao-tal](https://clawhub.ai/user/huangliujiao-tal) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Screenwriters, editors, and story-development agents use this skill after beat-sheet generation or story-impact review to score scene-by-scene tension, identify pacing weaknesses, and decide where to add transitions, breathers, or escalation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce strong editorial recommendations that may overfit a story to numeric pacing rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review the diagnostics as creative guidance and keep story logic, tone, and author intent as final decision criteria. <br>
