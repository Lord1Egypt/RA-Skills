## Description: <br>
Create recurring focus time blocks on Google Calendar to protect deep work hours. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[googleworkspace-bot](https://clawhub.ai/user/googleworkspace-bot) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People who manage Google Calendar use this recipe to create recurring busy focus-time blocks and verify that the protected time appears on their agenda. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The recurring event could block the wrong calendar, date, time, timezone, recurrence days, or end date. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the calendar account and schedule details before running the command, then edit or delete the recurring event if it blocks the wrong hours. <br>
