## Description: <br>
Generate visualizations for cement production heat balance analysis including process flow diagrams, energy distribution charts, and temperature profiles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[luozhengrongyn](https://clawhub.ai/user/luozhengrongyn) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and process engineers use this skill to turn cement heat-balance data into browser-viewable technical diagrams and reports for analysis, presentations, or documentation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided heat-balance JSON or output paths could be incorrect, untrusted, or unsuitable for the target environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use deliberate input and output paths, avoid untrusted JSON, and review generated files before relying on or sharing them. <br>
Risk: Generated HTML may contain misleading process values or presentation details if the source data is incomplete or wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated visualization and validate heat-balance values against source process data before using it in reports or sensitive environments. <br>
