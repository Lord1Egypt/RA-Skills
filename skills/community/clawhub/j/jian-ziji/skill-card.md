## Description: <br>
生动有趣的自我探索梳理技能，通过对话式问答引导用户深度探索自我，生成“AI眼中的你”分析报告，并基于分析结果创作第一人称公众号风格文章。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for guided self-reflection, life review, and personal narrative development through a bounded conversational interview. The skill turns the conversation into a structured self-understanding report and a first-person article suitable for review, revision, or personal publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can collect intimate self-reflection details during guided conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with information the user is comfortable sharing with an agent, and avoid providing details that should not be saved or reused. <br>
Risk: The skill may automatically transform personal disclosures into a publishable first-person article without a separate consent checkpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Tell the agent upfront whether the desired output is conversation only, a report, or an article, and review or redact generated text before sharing it. <br>
