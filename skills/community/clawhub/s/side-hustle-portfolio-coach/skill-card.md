## Description: <br>
Coach for people running multiple side hustles in parallel who need to decide what to keep, kill, or scale, and how to reduce day-job dependency without burning out. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users with three or more active income streams use this skill to audit portfolio health, compare revenue per hour and trajectory, sequence 30/60/90 day actions, and decide whether to focus, sunset, or scale specific side hustles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill discusses tax, entity, employment, benefits, and quit-your-job decisions that may be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general coaching only and verify legal, tax, financial, employment, benefits, and jurisdiction-specific decisions with qualified professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Portfolio recommendations depend on self-reported revenue, costs, hours, trajectory, runway, and constraints, so weak or incomplete inputs can produce misleading prioritization. <br>
Mitigation: Gather the diagnostic inputs requested by the skill, use verified financial records where possible, and review recommendations before making irreversible changes such as closing streams or leaving employment. <br>
