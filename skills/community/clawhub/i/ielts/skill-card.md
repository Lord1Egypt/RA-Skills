## Description: <br>
Prepare for IELTS Academic or General Training with progress tracking, weak area analysis, band score targeting, and immigration pathway guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External IELTS test takers, including students, immigration applicants, working professionals, and professional-registration candidates, use this skill to diagnose weaknesses, plan study, practice IELTS sections, evaluate writing and speaking, and track progress toward target bands. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local IELTS records may contain goals, exam dates, scores, essays, transcripts, recordings, and feedback corrections. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing unnecessary sensitive details, and review or delete the ~/ielts/ folder when the history is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Band estimates, target planning, immigration-pathway guidance, and professional-registration guidance are advisory and may not reflect a user's official requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Verify final score requirements, deadlines, and eligibility decisions against the relevant IELTS, university, immigration, or registration authority before acting. <br>
