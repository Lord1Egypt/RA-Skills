## Description: <br>
Guides Kjeldahl protein determination for livestock and poultry meat under GB 5009.5-2025, including reagent preparation, digestion, distillation, titration, calculation, and precision checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Laboratory analysts and quality teams use this skill to plan Kjeldahl protein testing for meat samples and calculate or validate results from titration data. It is intended for trained personnel working in an equipped lab. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The procedure uses concentrated acids, strong base, hot digestion, and distillation. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in an equipped laboratory with trained personnel, PPE, a fume hood, spill and first-aid procedures, and proper chemical waste handling. <br>
Risk: The public catalog name appears outdated compared with the current protein-testing content. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the skill content matches the intended protein-testing workflow before installing or relying on it. <br>
