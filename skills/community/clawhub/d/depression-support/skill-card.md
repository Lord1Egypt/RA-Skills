## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for daily depression support through mood check-ins, small behavioral activation tasks, self-care prompts, and personal pattern tracking. It is self-help support and not a substitute for professional or emergency care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the skill as medical care during crisis, self-harm, or severe symptoms. <br>
Mitigation: Frame responses as self-help support, remind users it is not a substitute for professional care, and direct crisis or self-harm situations to emergency or professional resources. <br>
Risk: A broad trigger such as "mental health check" may invoke depression-focused guidance when a user intended a different kind of mental health support. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's intent when the request is broad and keep guidance scoped to depression support unless the user asks for something else. <br>
