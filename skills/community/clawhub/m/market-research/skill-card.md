## Description: <br>
Research markets with sizing, segmentation, competitor mapping, pricing checks, and demand validation that turn fuzzy ideas into decision-ready evidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, investors, and product teams use this skill to size opportunities, map competitors and substitutes, validate demand, check pricing, and turn market uncertainty into a decision-ready recommendation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Live market research or outreach can involve privacy, consent, platform-rule, or paywall concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Review sources, target lists, and drafted messages before use, and avoid restricted or non-consensual data collection. <br>
Risk: Market recommendations can be misleading when based on stale pricing, weak survey enthusiasm, or a single source type. <br>
Mitigation: Use multiple evidence families, label confidence, and include assumptions and invalidation criteria for major conclusions. <br>
