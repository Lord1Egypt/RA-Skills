## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto guidance may become outdated or misleading as bridge URLs, contract addresses, chain IDs, token names, gas requirements, and withdrawal timing change. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify current Polygon documentation and wallet or bridge prompts before moving funds. <br>
Risk: Using the wrong Polygon network or destination chain can lead to stuck or lost funds. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the selected network, chain ID, asset type, destination address, and required gas token before submitting any transaction. <br>
Risk: Third-party bridges and token approvals can introduce smart contract and approval risk. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official or carefully vetted services, review token approvals, and revoke unused approvals after use. <br>
