## Description: <br>
Helps conduct social science academic research and scholarly writing, especially for music, education, and music education studies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[afanmusic](https://clawhub.ai/user/afanmusic) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers, educators, and students use this skill to synthesize user-provided music education corpora, notes, classroom observations, and policy texts into literature reviews, theory models, research gap matrices, and traceable evidence chains. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs could overstate academic certainty when the supplied corpus is incomplete or weak. <br>
Mitigation: Require evidence labels, mark unverified items, and list missing literature instead of filling gaps with unsupported prose. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misapplied to materials outside the intended research corpus. <br>
Mitigation: Keep analysis limited to user-authorized iMA exports or an explicit local research_corpus folder. <br>
