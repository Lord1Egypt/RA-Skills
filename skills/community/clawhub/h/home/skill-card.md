## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Home guidance can involve safety-critical areas such as gas, main electrical, or structural work. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for planning and decision support only; rely on licensed professionals for gas, main electrical, and structural work. <br>
Risk: Photos, dimensions, and household details shared for better recommendations may reveal private information about a living space. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the home details needed for the task and only when comfortable. <br>
