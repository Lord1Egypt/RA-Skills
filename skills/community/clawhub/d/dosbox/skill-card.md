Launch, configure, and troubleshoot DOSBox-X first, with fallback to classic DOSBox, for DOS games and software. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and retro-computing users use this skill to prepare reproducible DOSBox or DOSBox-X launch commands, reusable configuration files, and focused troubleshooting steps for classic DOS games and software. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated launch commands or .conf files may reference unexpected local paths or output locations. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated commands and configuration files before running them, and confirm paths point to intended folders, images, and output files. <br>
Risk: DOS games, installers, or ISO files from untrusted sources may introduce unsafe or unwanted behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Only run DOS programs and disk images from trusted sources. <br>
