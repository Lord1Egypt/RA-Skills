## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language advisory skill that applies Naval Ravikant-style lenses for leverage, specific knowledge, desire management, freedom, career choices, and opportunity decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cellinlab](https://clawhub.ai/user/cellinlab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to frame career, wealth, freedom, desire, and opportunity questions through Naval-style concepts such as leverage, specific knowledge, constraints, and system redesign. It is intended as a perspective aid, not as financial, legal, medical, mental-health, or current-facts advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may provide persuasive life, career, wealth, or desire-management framing that users could over-rely on. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as perspective only; verify current facts separately and do not rely on the skill for financial, legal, medical, or mental-health advice. <br>
Risk: Naval-style reasoning can underweight starting conditions, resource differences, emotional factors, and complex mental-health contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill's stated blind spots visible and review whether its framing fits the user's real constraints before acting on it. <br>
