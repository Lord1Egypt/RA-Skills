## Description: <br>
Theme Factory helps agents apply curated color palettes and typography pairings to slides, documents, reports, landing pages, and custom brand themes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wpank](https://clawhub.ai/user/wpank) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, developers, and content creators use this skill to choose or create cohesive visual themes for artifacts such as slides, documents, reports, and landing pages. It provides bundled palettes, font pairings, application guidance, and contrast checks for consistent styling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Installing a third-party skill globally may place untrusted guidance in future agent sessions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the publisher profile and intended source before installation, especially for global use. <br>
Risk: Theme application can reduce readability if colors are used without sufficient contrast. <br>
Mitigation: Check text, large text, and UI contrast against the WCAG thresholds documented by the skill before finalizing artifacts. <br>
