This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup teams use this skill to plan early paid acquisition campaigns, choose suitable ad platforms, allocate budget, and define measurement and launch steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for sensitive business metrics such as budget, CAC, LTV, and prior campaign results. <br>
Mitigation: Share approximate or non-confidential ranges unless disclosure is acceptable for the working environment. <br>
Risk: Campaign recommendations could lead to real advertising spend based on incomplete or inaccurate assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current business goals and validate with small tests before scaling spend. <br>
Risk: The skill links to external marketing resources that may change over time or reflect third-party opinions. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate external links independently before relying on them for campaign setup or spend decisions. <br>
