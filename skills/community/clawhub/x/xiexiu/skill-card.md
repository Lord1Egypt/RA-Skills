## Description: <br>
Teaches unconventional problem-solving methodology. Use when users mention 邪修, xiexiu, or need help developing lateral thinking, first principles reasoning, or creative approaches to problems. Focuses on teaching HOW to think, not WHAT to do. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yuexiaoliang](https://clawhub.ai/user/yuexiaoliang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to guide low-stakes brainstorming with the xiexiu five-step framework for lateral thinking, first-principles reasoning, and rapid experimentation. It is intended for learning how to think through unconventional approaches, not for replacing expert judgment or required procedures. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can encourage shortcut-seeking that bypasses rules, procedures, approvals, or responsibilities. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for low-stakes brainstorming, and require human review before applying any suggestion in real-world contexts. <br>
Risk: The skill may be inappropriate for high-stakes decisions involving workplace, legal, medical, financial, school, compliance, relationship-sensitive, or safety-related matters. <br>
Mitigation: Route high-stakes questions to qualified domain experts or established procedures instead of relying on this skill. <br>
