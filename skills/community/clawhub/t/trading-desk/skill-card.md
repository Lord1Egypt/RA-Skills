## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Options strategy guidance may be mistaken for financial advice or used for real-money trading without independent review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as educational only and require independent financial review before acting on any trade idea. <br>
Risk: Users may expose sensitive brokerage or account information while asking for portfolio or position guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide brokerage credentials, full account access details, or other sensitive financial secrets to the agent. <br>
Risk: The artifact links to paid off-platform materials, so the public preview may not contain the complete advertised kit. <br>
Mitigation: Review the linked offer and local artifact contents before relying on features that are described as part of the full kit. <br>
