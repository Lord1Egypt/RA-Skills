## Use Case: <br>
Individuals, freelancers, and household finance users use this skill to redesign expense categories, map spending into budget and tax-tracking structures, and produce checklists for follow-up review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial, tax, pricing, or compliance suggestions may be incomplete or unsuitable for a user's circumstances. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general information and verify important decisions with a qualified professional. <br>
Risk: Expense details can include sensitive personal or business financial information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the details needed for categorization and avoid providing account credentials or unnecessary identifiers. <br>
