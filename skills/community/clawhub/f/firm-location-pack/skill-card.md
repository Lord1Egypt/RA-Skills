## Description: <br>
Location strategy and site selection pack for geo-economic analysis, real estate intelligence, site scoring, tax incentive optimization, and TCO simulation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Executives, finance, HR, legal, operations, and real estate teams use this skill to compare candidate locations, assess market and workforce factors, identify incentives, and model occupancy costs for location strategy decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial, tax, and real estate outputs may be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable for high-impact business decisions without review. <br>
Mitigation: Validate generated analyses against current authoritative data and qualified human judgment before using them for location strategy or budgeting decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on mcp-openclaw-extensions for tool access. <br>
Mitigation: Review or trust mcp-openclaw-extensions before granting the agent access to those tools. <br>
