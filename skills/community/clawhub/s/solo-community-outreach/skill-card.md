## Description: <br>
Finds relevant Reddit, Hacker News, and Product Hunt threads, drafts value-first community responses, and prepares a Product Hunt launch checklist. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fortunto2](https://clawhub.ai/user/fortunto2) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External builders and marketing teams use this skill to find relevant community discussions, plan transparent outreach, draft helpful responses, and prepare a Product Hunt launch checklist. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted community responses may be inaccurate, overly promotional, or inconsistent with community rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review each draft before posting, verify factual claims, disclose builder involvement, and check the target community's rules. <br>
Risk: Project context may be used in public searches or configured MCP search services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including confidential project details and use only approved search or MCP services. <br>
Risk: The skill writes a local outreach plan file that may contain product positioning and draft responses. <br>
Mitigation: Review docs/outreach-plan.md before sharing, committing, or acting on the generated plan. <br>
