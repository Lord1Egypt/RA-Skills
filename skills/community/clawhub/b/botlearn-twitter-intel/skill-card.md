## Description: <br>
Monitor Twitter/X to identify key opinion leaders, track emerging trends, analyze sentiment, detect bot activity, and deliver verified real-time intelligence reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[calvinxhk](https://clawhub.ai/user/calvinxhk) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and operators use this skill to scope Twitter/X monitoring requests, track KOLs and emerging narratives, assess sentiment and bot amplification, and produce attributed intelligence briefings for bounded topics and time windows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation can produce over-scoped or noisy social-media monitoring. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit topics, account sets, languages, geography, and time windows before starting analysis. <br>
Risk: Twitter/X-only evidence can misrepresent credibility, urgency, or public consensus. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims against primary sources and avoid acting on Twitter-only crisis intelligence. <br>
Risk: External Twitter/X access may require credentials or API permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege, read-oriented API credentials and respect platform terms and rate limits. <br>
