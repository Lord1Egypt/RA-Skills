## Description: <br>
Guides startup founders and builders through YC-style office hours, premise challenges, alternatives, and a saved design document before implementation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[loocor](https://clawhub.ai/user/loocor) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, and builders use this skill to evaluate product or project ideas before coding, select a startup or builder exploration mode, challenge premises, compare implementation approaches, and save an approved design document. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may inspect repository context and reflect sensitive project details in generated design documents. <br>
Mitigation: Run it only in repositories where this context is acceptable to review, and inspect generated design documents before sharing them. <br>
Risk: The skill may create local design artifacts and browser-based UI sketches. <br>
Mitigation: Use it in a workspace where local Markdown, HTML, and screenshot artifacts are expected, and review paths before keeping or sharing outputs. <br>
Risk: The workflow includes a promotional YC/GStack referral handoff. <br>
Mitigation: Treat referral content as promotional and decide independently whether to follow external application links. <br>
