## Description: <br>
用数据判断市场处于"亢奋/分歧/冰点/修复"哪个阶段，用于A股短线交易中辅助判断仓位和交易策略。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[haohanyang92](https://clawhub.ai/user/haohanyang92) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to frame A-share short-term market sentiment from limit-up count, limit-down count, failed-board rate, and consecutive-board height, then translate the result into cautious position-sizing guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may provide position-sizing suggestions that users could mistake for authoritative financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support, verify market data independently, and do not treat the skill as an instruction to trade. <br>
