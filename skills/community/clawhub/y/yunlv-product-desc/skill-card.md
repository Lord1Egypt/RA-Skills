## Description: <br>
Generates differentiated, SEO-optimized, multilingual product descriptions and listing content for foreign-trade products across B2B, e-commerce, catalog, and exhibition channels. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangm-a3](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangm-a3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External business users and product-content teams use this skill to turn product specifications, competitor notes, target market details, and platform requirements into differentiated product listings, SEO titles, selling points, specification tables, FAQs, and localized descriptions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product specifications, draft listings, pricing claims, and optional competitor comparisons may be sent to Yunlv/TradeGPT. <br>
Mitigation: Use a revocable API key and avoid sending unreleased, confidential, or sensitive commercial data unless the provider terms are acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated selling points, certification claims, SEO language, or competitor comparisons may be inaccurate, exaggerated, or unsuitable for a target platform. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated product claims against source data, certificates, and platform rules before publishing. <br>
