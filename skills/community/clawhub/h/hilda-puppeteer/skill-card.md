## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security scan marked this skill suspicious because it hides persistent workflow notes and includes guidance for avoiding automation detection. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, require confirmation before npm installs, and ask the agent to disclose, review, and let you delete anything saved under ~/puppeteer/. <br>
Risk: Browser automation can affect sites or accounts where automation is not permitted or where private data may be exposed. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for sites and accounts where automation is allowed, and avoid storing credentials, private account data, sensitive selectors, or authenticated browser profiles unless intentionally needed. <br>
