## Description: <br>
Generates personalized B2B cold outreach messages for WeChat, email, SMS, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn using recipient context, channel, seller role, target outcome, tone, and quantity. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales teams, recruiters, consultants, and advisors use this skill to draft channel-specific cold outreach, follow-up sequences, and reply-rate optimization tips. It helps adapt messaging to the prospect profile, seller identity, offer, and desired call to action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Saved sales preferences and outreach history may contain sensitive prospect or business data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing unnecessary personal details, clear memory files when retention is not needed, and limit shared context to the minimum needed for message generation. <br>
Risk: Outbound cold outreach may be subject to anti-spam, privacy, and platform-specific messaging rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated messages for consent, opt-out language, jurisdictional requirements, and platform policy compliance before sending. <br>
