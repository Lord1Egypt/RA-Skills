## Description: <br>
Generates a bilingual daily briefing on global insurance AI news, covering regional updates, investment, regulation, key companies, and AI talent movement, then publishes the report to Feishu. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[suiclaw](https://clawhub.ai/user/suiclaw) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 License <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and insurance or insurtech analysts use this skill to monitor current AI developments across global insurance markets and produce a structured bilingual morning briefing with source links. The skill is also useful for teams that want the report synchronized into a Feishu document for daily distribution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create a recurring daily report and publish it into a Feishu workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the Feishu account or app permissions before use, and verify that the daily schedule can be disabled when automated reporting is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Briefings depend on current web search results and may miss, duplicate, or misdate insurance AI news. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated briefing before distribution and keep source links with each item so readers can verify the underlying publication. <br>
