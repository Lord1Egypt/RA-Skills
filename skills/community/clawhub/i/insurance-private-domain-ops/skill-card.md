## Description: <br>
Provides insurance CRM operations guidance for China's WeChat ecosystem, including RFM and lifecycle segmentation, churn reactivation SOPs, renewal workflows, WeCom operations, personalization, and PIPL-aware data practices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Insurance operations, CRM, and agency teams use this skill to segment existing customers, reactivate lapsed policyholders, plan renewal outreach, and create WeChat or WeCom customer operations materials for the China insurance market. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill supports privacy-sensitive customer segmentation and outreach workflows for insurance customers. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with authorization to handle customer data, and verify consent, opt-out lists, lawful basis, data minimization, and applicable insurance, privacy, telecom, and marketing rules before applying templates to real customers. <br>
Risk: Regulatory and compliance references in operational scripts can become outdated or be misapplied to a specific insurance product or channel. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outreach, renewal, and product recommendation materials against current official rules and internal compliance guidance before deployment. <br>
