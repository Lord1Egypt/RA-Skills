## Description: <br>
Book window-cleaning services through Lokuli MCP by searching providers, checking availability, and creating bookings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to find window-cleaning providers, check appointment availability, and book a selected service through Lokuli's MCP endpoint. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can send contact details and booking requests to Lokuli's external MCP service. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the provider, date, time, and contact details before creating a booking, and avoid sharing unnecessary information. <br>
