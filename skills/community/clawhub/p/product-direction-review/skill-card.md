## Description: <br>
Product Direction Review helps agents evaluate product directions and business models through structured product, user psychology, strategy, investment, and business-model lenses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[woai61878](https://clawhub.ai/user/woai61878) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, founders, and reviewers use this skill to examine a product idea or business model, identify blind spots, and receive direct, actionable recommendations across multiple product and business perspectives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow is opinionated and may produce overconfident product direction advice when user context is thin. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for missing product, user, market, and business-model facts before applying the framework to high-impact decisions. <br>
Risk: The perspectives are interpretive summaries of public product and investment thinking, not endorsements by the named people. <br>
Mitigation: Present results as review lenses and validate recommendations against the user's own evidence and constraints. <br>
Risk: The artifact is primarily Chinese-language and may not match the user's preferred language in ambiguous conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the preferred language before delivering the review when the conversation language is unclear. <br>
