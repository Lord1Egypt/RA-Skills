## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-aware marketing campaigns, service pages, review templates, ad copy, local SEO assets, and outreach content for residential, commercial, move-out, and short-term rental cleaning businesses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Cleaning business owners, local service marketers, and agencies use this skill to draft publishable marketing assets for cleaning and maid service businesses. It helps create campaign copy, service-page content, review and referral workflows, local SEO material, JSON-LD schema, and outreach templates while prompting users to verify regulated claims before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SMS, email, advertising, review, referral, insurance, and certification language may be inaccurate or non-compliant if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify TCPA, CAN-SPAM, FTC endorsement, review/referral, insurance, certification, and local licensing claims before publishing or sending any generated content. <br>
Risk: Business owners may paste customer lists, policy numbers, contact details, or other sensitive business data into an AI provider while filling prompt inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data that the business has a clear consent and privacy basis to process, and omit or redact sensitive identifiers unless they are necessary and approved for use. <br>
Risk: The skill's compliance language is drafting support and may not reflect the user's specific jurisdiction, platform policy, or business facts. <br>
Mitigation: Treat compliance text as a starting point, confirm local rules and platform requirements, and have qualified counsel or a responsible business reviewer approve high-risk claims. <br>
