## Description: <br>
Generates promo video plans with a 30-45 second script, shot-by-shot storyboard, and optional Remotion or Montage-tool configuration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fortunto2](https://clawhub.ai/user/fortunto2) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product builders, and marketing teams use this skill from a project workspace to turn product docs and UI evidence into a short-form promo video plan, including script, storyboard, asset checklist, and tooling notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may inspect private project materials through local reads or project-search MCP tools while planning a promo video. <br>
Mitigation: Run it only from the intended project workspace and review any project-search use on private codebases. <br>
Risk: The generated video plan may contain inaccurate product claims, asset requirements, or production guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review docs/video-promo.md before committing, sharing, or using it for production assets. <br>
