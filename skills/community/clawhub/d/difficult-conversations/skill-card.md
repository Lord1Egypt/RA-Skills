## Description: <br>
Frameworks and scripts for having hard conversations, including confronting a boss, asking for a raise, setting boundaries with family, talking about money with a partner, giving difficult feedback, or addressing situations the user has been avoiding. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to prepare for difficult interpersonal, workplace, family, partner, landlord, or medical conversations by structuring the topic, practicing scripts, and planning responses. It is coaching support, not professional legal, medical, employment, or safety advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may apply conversation coaching in workplace, legal, medical, abuse, or safety-related situations where incorrect guidance could have serious consequences. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as coaching only and use qualified professional help or emergency resources when threats, retaliation, legal disputes, medical concerns, or abuse are involved. <br>
Risk: Conversation scripts may escalate conflict if used without considering timing, safety, or the other person's likely reaction. <br>
Mitigation: Plan logistics, practice the opening, prioritize safety planning for threatening situations, and pause or seek support when the conversation becomes unsafe. <br>
