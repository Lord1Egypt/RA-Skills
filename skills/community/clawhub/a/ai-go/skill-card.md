## Description: <br>
Ai Go is a Chinese-language Go and weiqi knowledge skill covering rules, tactics, openings, joseki, life-and-death practice, endgame concepts, AI analysis, game review, and rule differences. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Go learners, amateur players, and study-focused agents use this skill to explain Go concepts, answer training questions, support life-and-death or tesuji practice, and discuss AI-assisted game analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share personal study notes or game records while asking for Go guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide game records or study material that users are comfortable exposing to their agent environment. <br>
Risk: The skill provides educational Go guidance rather than verified professional coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as study support and verify important training conclusions against trusted teachers, books, or analysis tools. <br>
