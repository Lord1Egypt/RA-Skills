## Description: <br>
Edu Assistant helps learners from K12 through lifelong learning plan courses, choose learning paths, organize knowledge, prepare for exams, get academic writing guidance, and find online learning resources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners use this skill to create study plans, map knowledge areas, prepare for exams, receive academic writing support, and assemble online resource lists across education stages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Study plans, citations, exam guidance, or paper advice may be inaccurate or misleading if treated as authoritative. <br>
Mitigation: Review important outputs against trusted course materials, official exam guidance, and source documents before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Learners may include private student records or sensitive academic information in prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting private student data unless the active agent environment is approved to process it. <br>
