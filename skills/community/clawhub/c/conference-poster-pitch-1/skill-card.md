## Description: <br>
Use conference poster pitch for academic writing workflows that need structured execution, explicit assumptions, and clear output boundaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and academic writers use this skill to draft short poster-session elevator pitches from a poster title and selected duration. It supports bounded academic writing workflows that call out inputs, assumptions, limits, and next checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Unpublished or sensitive poster details may be echoed in terminal output or conversation logs. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive titles or anonymized wording when drafting early-stage research pitches. <br>
Risk: The packaged script produces lightweight template text rather than a complete academic writing workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit the generated pitch for factual accuracy, audience fit, and research-specific nuance before use. <br>
