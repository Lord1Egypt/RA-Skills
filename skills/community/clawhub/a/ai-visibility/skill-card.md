## Description: <br>
Track and analyze brand visibility across AI platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini). Check mention rates, sentiment, competitors, and get improvement recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sharozdawa](https://clawhub.ai/user/sharozdawa) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and teams use this skill to assess how often a brand appears across AI answer platforms, compare competitors, evaluate sentiment, and identify actions to improve AI visibility. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Visibility checks can involve brand strategy, launch plans, customer information, or competitive details being sent to external AI or search services. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved services for these checks and remove confidential, customer, or sensitive competitive information from prompts. <br>
Risk: AI visibility scores, sentiment, and recommendations may be incomplete or misleading if based on limited prompts or platform responses. <br>
Mitigation: Review results before business use, repeat checks across representative queries, and corroborate recommendations with trusted market and search data. <br>
