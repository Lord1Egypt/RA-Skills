## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to intensify emotional response, which can over-amplify anxiety or target vulnerable audiences if used carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Review public or commercial copy before publication, avoid targeting vulnerable audiences, and keep posting under explicit user control. <br>
