## Description: <br>
Ai Study Abroad helps students and families plan international study applications, including school selection, application essays, visas, scholarships, exams, pre-departure preparation, and credential recognition. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External prospective international students, applicants, and families use this skill for study-abroad planning guidance across program selection, application documents, visa preparation, scholarships, exams, travel preparation, and return credential recognition. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Study-abroad, visa, scholarship, safety, and credential-recognition requirements can change by country, institution, and date. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance and verify decisions against official university, scholarship, and government immigration sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Application essays, resumes, recommendation strategy, and student profiles may contain sensitive personal or academic information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary details, remove unnecessary personal data, and review any generated application material before submission. <br>
