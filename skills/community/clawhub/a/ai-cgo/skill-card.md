## Description: <br>
AI Chief Growth Officer helps users diagnose growth bottlenecks, design AI-driven growth workflows, and optimize funnels with metric-linked recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, founders, growth teams, and consultants use this skill to convert business context and growth challenges into diagnosis, AI workflow designs, automation plans, KPI systems, and optimization experiments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad growth and business trigger phrases may activate the skill when a more specialized skill would fit better. <br>
Mitigation: Review the routed mode and clarify requests involving branding, social media operations, ad creative, or unrelated technical consultation. <br>
Risk: Learning-file updates may retain business context, assumptions, or reusable patterns from conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Approve knowledge-base or router-signal updates only after removing confidential or sensitive business details. <br>
