## Description: <br>
Analyze and write LOFTER-style fanfiction, fandom posts, and high-click title/opening formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and fandom-focused agents use this skill to turn an IP, pairing, trope, or rough idea into LOFTER-native fanfiction packaging, outlines, drafts, review notes, and revisions. It is intended for high-click Chinese-language fandom posts that preserve fandom fit, pacing, and emotional payoff density. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses bundled fandom samples and may produce work that resembles platform conventions or source examples too closely. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated text for originality and avoid copying sample wording, scene order, or distinctive phrasing before posting. <br>
Risk: Generated fanfiction may cross platform, rating, content-boundary, or fandom-norm expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Check outputs against platform rules, rating boundaries, fandom norms, and any user-provided limits before publication. <br>
