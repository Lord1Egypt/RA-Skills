## Description: <br>
Founder Mindset helps agents coach early-stage founders through a structured audit of runway, avoided hard actions, premature scaling, unscalable work, and founder stress patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[deciqai](https://clawhub.ai/user/deciqai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders and startup operators use this skill when they want an agent to run a founder-mode coaching audit for early-stage decisions such as runway, hiring, firing, pivoting, fundraising, cutting burn, or returning to customer-facing unscalable work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may ask for confidential startup metrics such as cash, burn, runway, revenue growth, hiring decisions, or personnel issues. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in contexts where sharing that business information with the agent is acceptable, and omit unnecessary sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Founder coaching output can influence consequential business decisions and may be incomplete or wrong for the user's specific company. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the audit as advisory, review the reasoning before acting, and validate major financial, legal, or personnel decisions with appropriate human judgment. <br>
