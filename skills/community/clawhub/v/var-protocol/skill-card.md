## Description: <br>
Enforce Versioning, Archiving, and Rollback discipline for long-lived assets and multi-step delivery work that needs safe iteration, backup-first handling, semantic version naming, changelogs, rollback anchors, or non-destructive updates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wewehg](https://clawhub.ai/user/wewehg) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, creators, and agent operators use this skill to preserve recoverability while editing codebases, websites, documents, decks, prompts, configs, and other long-lived project artifacts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the skill to influence ordinary editing workflows more often than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow activation to important, long-lived, multi-step, or high-impact edits where backup, versioning, and rollback discipline are warranted. <br>
