### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: General VPN advice can lead to connectivity loss or unintended traffic exposure if applied without matching the user's device, network, and VPN provider. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as reference material, follow device and provider documentation for actual changes, and test DNS routing, kill switch behavior, and split tunneling before relying on a configuration. <br>
Risk: Privacy expectations may be overstated if users assume a VPN provides anonymity or removes trust from all intermediaries. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's privacy caveats when advising users, including that VPNs shift trust to the provider and do not prevent identification through accounts, payments, or browser fingerprinting. <br>
