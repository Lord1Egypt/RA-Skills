## Description: <br>
Build and maintain an isolated local personal fitness context for body weight, fat loss, diet, meals, calories, protein, hunger, fasting, food options near work or home, delivery choices, exercise preferences, workouts, sleep, body signals, daily logs, weekly reviews, and lightweight editable diet/exercise plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tinywatermonster](https://clawhub.ai/user/tinywatermonster) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals use this skill to keep a private local fitness record, update diet and exercise plans, and receive practical recommendations based on their own meals, workouts, constraints, and goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores sensitive fitness and health-adjacent history locally, including weight, meals, hunger, sleep, workouts, goals, and body signals. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if local retention is acceptable, and review or delete ~/.openclaw/private/personal-fit-coach/ when those records are no longer wanted. <br>
