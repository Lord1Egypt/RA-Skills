## Description: <br>
Track and analyze global economic data releases, central bank meetings, and key financial events across major economies with impact ratings, market expectations, and trading strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to build daily or weekly economic-calendar summaries, compare expected and prior macroeconomic values, and discuss possible market impact before major data releases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Economic-calendar data and market expectations can become stale or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Verify time-sensitive economic data against authoritative sources before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Trading tips and market-impact discussion may be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as informational context only and apply independent financial review before trading decisions. <br>
