## Description: <br>
ADHD Support helps agents identify overwhelm, planning, focus, transition, reflection, and brain-dump states and respond with concise executive-function support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[betancur](https://clawhub.ai/user/betancur) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill for conversational ADHD and executive-function support, including task initiation, planning, focus setup, transitions, reflection, and brain dumps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad productivity, focus, or overwhelm language. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when conversational executive-function support is desired, and review responses for fit when the user only asked for ordinary productivity help. <br>
Risk: Crisis Mode could be mistaken for medical, mental-health, or emergency support. <br>
Mitigation: Treat Crisis Mode as task-support coaching only and direct users to qualified professionals or emergency services for medical, mental-health, or urgent safety needs. <br>
