## Description: <br>
Parse client URLs and requirements to generate ad proposals, ROI estimates, persuasion logic, and CRM-based close probability forecasting for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Amazon Ads, and Shopify Ads services. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales and advertising teams use this skill to turn prospect URLs, requirements, and CRM signals into ad-service proposals, auditable ROI estimates, objection handling, close-probability forecasts, and follow-up reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ROI estimates, close probabilities, or CRM recommendations may be inaccurate or overconfident. <br>
Mitigation: Review assumptions, CRM inputs, and forecast ranges before using the output for client commitments or commercial decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad business or sales terms outside a specific advertising-planning task. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke it intentionally for advertising or sales-planning work and check that the output matches the intended deal context. <br>
