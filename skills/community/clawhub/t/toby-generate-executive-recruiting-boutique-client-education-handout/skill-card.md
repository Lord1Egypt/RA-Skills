## Description: <br>
Create polished client education handouts with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps tailored for executive recruiting boutique firms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Executive recruiters and talent partners use this skill to draft client education handouts, FAQ guides, and visual explainers for recruiting boutique services. The generated content should be reviewed before client delivery, especially where employment-related claims or recommendations are involved. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security scan reports broad shell and local file-read access without a clear need in the skill documentation. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in an agent environment that can restrict tool use, and approve local reads or shell commands only for specific files and actions. <br>
Risk: Generated recruiting education content may be mistaken for validated employment or screening advice. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review for accuracy, fairness, and employment-related claims before sharing output with clients. <br>
