## Description: <br>
制定针对新项目或在研产品的灰度与推广计划，涵盖阶段推进、渠道策略、话术、数据指标及风险预案。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product, operations, channel, and sales teams use this skill to draft Chinese-language gray-release and promotion plans, including phased rollout gates, channel execution, sales messaging, metrics, and risk plans. It is also useful for preparing internal strategy summaries for VP, channel, sales, or team reviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can activate for adjacent operations or marketing planning tasks because its trigger wording is broad. <br>
Mitigation: Review the activation scope and use it only when the user is asking for gray-release, promotion, operating strategy, or related internal planning help. <br>
Risk: Promotion plans may be inaccurate when product definitions, product status, or target merchant segments are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm uncertain product details, rollout status, and target merchant scope before drafting or finalizing a plan. <br>
Risk: Internal execution plans may accidentally reuse outward-facing product narrative where decision makers need operating detail. <br>
Mitigation: Keep internal reports focused on rollout path, responsibilities, blockers, metrics, risks, and decisions needed. <br>
