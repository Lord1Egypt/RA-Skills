## Description: <br>
Removes signs of AI-generated writing from text and rewrites it to sound more natural and human-written. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jazzyong2023](https://clawhub.ai/user/jazzyong2023) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and agents use this skill to revise text that sounds AI-generated while preserving the intended meaning and tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can make AI-assisted writing less apparent, which may be inappropriate where disclosure or authorship rules apply. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only on documents you are allowed to edit, follow any disclosure requirements, and review the rewritten text before sharing. <br>
Risk: Rewriting may alter tone, emphasis, or meaning in ways the user did not intend. <br>
Mitigation: Compare the output against the source text and keep changes that preserve the original intent. <br>
