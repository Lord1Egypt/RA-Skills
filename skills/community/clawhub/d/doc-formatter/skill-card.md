## Description: <br>
Automates generation of standardized Chinese official documents per GB/T 9704, offering 18 template types with formal wording and layout guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cryptocxf](https://clawhub.ai/user/cryptocxf) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and business users use this skill to draft standardized Chinese official documents such as notices, reports, requests, letters, meeting minutes, work plans, and summaries. Developers and skill maintainers can also use the included template, phrase, and formatting references to customize document generation behavior. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated official documents may contain incorrect wording, missing approvals, or unsuitable factual claims for formal issuance. <br>
Mitigation: Review each draft manually, verify facts and approval paths, and adapt wording to the issuing organization's requirements before stamping or distribution. <br>
Risk: Classified or confidential material could be exposed if users provide sensitive content to a non-approved drafting workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering classified or confidential content and use the organization's approved secure channels for sensitive documents. <br>
