## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, and product teams use this skill to prepare Firefly Air Compressor product media and pages, including background removal, OCR-assisted product details, video edits, voiceovers, 3D displays, and responsive bilingual HTML detail pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided text, images, or media may be processed by external AI, TTS, or image services when those workflows are used. <br>
Mitigation: Review provider settings before use and avoid submitting sensitive or confidential content. <br>
Risk: Optional dependency installation and local media tools can change the runtime environment or process local files. <br>
Mitigation: Use a controlled dependency environment, run the documented check-only command first, and review generated files before publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated product pages, OCR results, voiceover scripts, or marketing media may contain inaccurate product information. <br>
Mitigation: Have a knowledgeable reviewer verify product specifications, bilingual labels, and generated media before release. <br>
