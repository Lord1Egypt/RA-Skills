## Description: <br>
SEO Optimization Suite helps agents produce SEO audits and action plans covering technical SEO, content strategy, competitor analysis, link building, and Baidu plus Google optimization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External site owners, marketers, developers, and SEO practitioners use this skill to diagnose website SEO issues, compare competitors, plan keywords and content, and prioritize optimization work. It is intended to produce reviewable SEO recommendations and timelines rather than guaranteed ranking outcomes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be activated by broad SEO, keyword, ranking, or website optimization discussions that may include private business plans or site analytics. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user intends to invoke this skill before applying it to sensitive SEO discussions. <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations and ranking timelines may be incorrect or misleading if treated as guaranteed outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed changes against site analytics, search-console data, and current search-engine guidance; treat ranking projections as estimates. <br>
