## Description: <br>
Full-stack B2B marketing execution skill for defining ICP and brand messaging, generating multi-platform content, writing cold email sequences, planning SEO, creating competitor battle cards, and producing marketing reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketing teams, and agent developers use this skill to plan and execute B2B marketing workflows, including strategy, content repurposing, outbound email, SEO, competitor monitoring, publishing calendars, and ROI reporting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts and source documents may be sent to an external LLM provider when an API key is configured. <br>
Mitigation: Use a controlled Python environment and a dedicated API key, and avoid confidential or regulated data unless approved. <br>
Risk: Generated cold email and social content may create legal, platform-policy, bias, or brand-safety concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated marketing content before use and confirm compliance with applicable laws, platform rules, and brand standards. <br>
