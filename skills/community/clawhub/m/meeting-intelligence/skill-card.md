## Description: <br>
AI-powered meeting tool that summarizes discussions, extracts prioritized action items with owners and deadlines, and drafts clear follow-up emails. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[di5cip1e](https://clawhub.ai/user/di5cip1e) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, managers, and customer-facing teams use this skill to turn meeting transcripts or notes into concise summaries, decisions, open questions, prioritized action items, and follow-up emails. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting transcripts can contain secrets, sensitive personal information, legal, HR, client, or regulated data. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive content unless authorized to process it, and follow applicable data-handling policies. <br>
Risk: Summaries, action items, owners, deadlines, or drafted follow-up emails may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated notes and email drafts before sharing or sending. <br>
