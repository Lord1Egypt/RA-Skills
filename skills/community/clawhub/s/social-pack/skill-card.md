## Description: <br>
Generate platform-specific social posts from a single brief for Twitter/X threads, LinkedIn, Instagram, Reddit, and Mastodon. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TheShadowRose](https://clawhub.ai/user/TheShadowRose) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to turn one social media brief into platform-specific post text, including threads, captions, hashtags, and Reddit-style title/body output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated posts may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for a target platform or audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated posts before publishing and apply brand, moderation, and platform-account safeguards outside the skill. <br>
