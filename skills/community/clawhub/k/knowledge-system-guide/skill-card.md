## Description: <br>
帮助用户理解知识体系的重要性，并用拆解、归纳、联系、提炼四步方法与外接知识库流程构建系统化知识结构。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Individuals, knowledge workers, and agents use this skill to turn fragmented learning material into a goal-oriented personal knowledge system. It guides AI-assisted organization of raw materials into structured notes while keeping human review in the loop. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI-generated summaries or synthesized notes may be treated as verified knowledge too early. <br>
Mitigation: Preview generated notes and require human review before accepting them into the knowledge base. <br>
Risk: A local write-back workflow could modify unintended knowledge-base files if the workspace scope is unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Define the exact raw/ and wiki/ folders an agent may read or modify before allowing write-back. <br>
