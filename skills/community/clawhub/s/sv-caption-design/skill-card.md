## Description: <br>
Covers caption strategy, styling, placement, timing, accessibility, and engagement for short video subtitles and on-screen text. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Video editors, content creators, social media managers, and accessibility-focused creators use this skill to design readable short-video captions, adapt placement for platform overlays, and check accessibility considerations before publishing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Caption guidance may still result in inaccurate, poorly timed, or inaccessible captions if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify caption accuracy, translation quality, timing, contrast, readability, and platform safe-zone placement before publishing. <br>
