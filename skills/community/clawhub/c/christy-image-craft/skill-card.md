## Description: <br>
Curated image generation assistant covering four categories: characters, scenes, products, and style transformation, for generating high-quality stylized 3D images. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ericn26-star](https://clawhub.ai/user/ericn26-star) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use Image Craft to turn photos, brand or product inputs, city or scene descriptions, and style requests into stylized 3D image outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-supplied private photos, confidential product concepts, or sensitive logos may be processed by image tools or used with public web lookup for context. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive inputs unless the deployment environment and image-generation tools are approved for that data. <br>
