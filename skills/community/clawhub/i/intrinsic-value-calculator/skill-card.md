## Description: <br>
Calculates company intrinsic value and margin of safety using Graham formula, asset value, earnings value, DCF, weighted comparisons, and sensitivity analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lj22503](https://clawhub.ai/user/lj22503) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to structure stock valuation work, compare multiple valuation methods, calculate margin of safety, and present sensitivity analysis. It should be treated as educational valuation assistance rather than personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on valuation outputs or recommendation labels as personalized investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as educational estimates, review all assumptions such as growth rate, discount rate, and PE multiple, and require independent research before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Intrinsic value estimates are highly sensitive to user-supplied assumptions and can imply false precision. <br>
Mitigation: Use scenario and sensitivity analysis, conservative inputs, and value ranges instead of treating a single calculated figure as definitive. <br>
