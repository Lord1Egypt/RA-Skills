## Description: <br>
Screens unadapted novels from platforms such as Fanqie, Qidian, and Qimao for short-drama or AI comic adaptation potential and produces a scored TOP5 ranking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[elevenzhou](https://clawhub.ai/user/elevenzhou) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content development, IP sourcing, and market research teams use this skill to compare novels for adaptation into short dramas or AI comics. It helps rank candidates by story pacing, cast and scene suitability, audience fit, topic novelty, episode fit, and production cost. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rankings, platform metrics, and adaptation-rights status may be incomplete, outdated, or estimated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify rankings, novel metadata, and adaptation-rights status manually before making business or production decisions. <br>
