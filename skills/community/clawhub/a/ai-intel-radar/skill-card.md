## Description: <br>
Build a high-signal AI intelligence brief from fresh web sources, with separate daily and weekly formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[YIKAILucas](https://clawhub.ai/user/YIKAILucas) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and business decision makers use this skill to monitor recent AI news, technical changes, market signals, and practical actions in daily or weekly brief formats. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Current-event summaries and business recommendations can be incomplete, outdated, or misleading if source freshness is weak. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important business or market decisions against the cited primary sources before acting. <br>
Risk: AI news items may contain rumors, repeated commentary, or uncertain claims. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official and primary sources, remove duplicate event chains, and mark uncertain claims as pending verification. <br>
Risk: Generated briefs could include fabricated or unreliable links. <br>
Mitigation: Require at least one source URL per item and do not fabricate links when reliable updates are unavailable. <br>
