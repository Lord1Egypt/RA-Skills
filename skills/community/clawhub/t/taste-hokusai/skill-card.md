## Description: <br>
Taste Hokusai is an aesthetic skill that guides agents toward Hokusai-inspired writing, UI design, and branding using ukiyo-e precision, dynamic natural forces, bold outlines, and a Prussian blue palette. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents, creators, designers, and developers use this skill to apply Hokusai-inspired aesthetic constraints to prose, interface direction, and brand language. It is best suited for creative work where the desired style emphasizes ukiyo-e precision, natural forces, human scale, and flat color planes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Installing this skill intentionally changes an agent's stylistic guidance toward a specific Hokusai-inspired aesthetic. <br>
Mitigation: Review the artistic constraints before installation and use the skill only when that style is desired. <br>
