## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to find acupuncture services, check appointment availability, and create bookings through Lokuli. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Booking workflows can send appointment search details and customer contact information to Lokuli. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if Lokuli is an acceptable external booking service, and confirm the name, email, phone number, provider, service, and appointment time before creating a booking. <br>
Risk: Users may book the wrong provider, time, service, or accept unexpected price or cancellation terms. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the provider, service, appointment time, price, and cancellation terms before submitting a booking. <br>
