## Description: <br>
Generates bride and groom horoscopes then produces a marriage compatibility report with PDF outputs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, astrology consultants, and matrimonial platform developers use this skill to submit bride and groom birth details, generate individual horoscope PDFs, and produce a marriage compatibility report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow sends sensitive personal and birth details for two people to an external service. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with explicit consent from both people, minimize unnecessary phone or family details where possible, and confirm the provider's privacy policy, retention period, and deletion process before use. <br>
Risk: Generated PDF links are exposed without documented access controls in the available evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Verify download-link protections before sharing reports and avoid distributing links beyond the intended recipients. <br>
