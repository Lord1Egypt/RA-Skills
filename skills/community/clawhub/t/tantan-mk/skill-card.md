## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese-language market intelligence assistant for industry trends, competitor analysis, customer profiles, market sizing, and opportunity assessment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business users, founders, product teams, and strategy teams use this skill to request structured market research briefs, competitor breakdowns, customer profiles, and market opportunity assessments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the skill to respond to many market, competitor, trend, and report-related prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Review the trigger scope before deployment and narrow activation wording if users need more explicit opt-in behavior. <br>
Risk: Market intelligence outputs may include estimates, stale public information, or unsupported inferences if sources are not checked. <br>
Mitigation: Require source review, clear fact-versus-estimate labeling, and human validation before relying on outputs for business decisions. <br>
