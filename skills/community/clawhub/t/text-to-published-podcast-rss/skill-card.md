## Description: <br>
Helps an agent or app turn text into audio podcast episodes, with one API call creating an episode through TTS and auto-publishing it to an RSS feed. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cast0](https://clawhub.ai/user/cast0) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external users with a Cast0 podcast use this skill to create podcast episodes from text, poll generation status, list episodes, and publish completed audio through a public RSS feed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Text submitted through the skill is uploaded to Cast0 and may become part of a public podcast feed. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the content is appropriate for publication before creating an episode, and do not submit secrets, confidential documents, personal data, or material without the required rights and approvals. <br>
