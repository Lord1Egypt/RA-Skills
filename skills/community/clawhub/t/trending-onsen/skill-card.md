## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel-planning agents use this skill to find popular hot spring venues, compare highlights, and draft visit routes by city, theme, budget, or time window. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel details such as prices, opening hours, booking rules, queue times, and venue restrictions may change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details with official venue or booking sources before making travel plans. <br>
Risk: The skill provides popularity-based recommendations that may be incomplete or subjective. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as a planning aid and compare recommendations against current reviews, maps, and local guidance. <br>
