## Use Case: <br>
External shoppers and deal reviewers use this skill to calculate final purchase prices after stacked discounts, coupons, rebates, red packets, and subsidies. It helps compare single-item, cart-building, and promotion-plan scenarios before purchase. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad shopping price questions and produce purchase-related calculations from incomplete or retailer-specific discount rules. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm retailer rebate terms, eligibility, stacking order, and arithmetic before relying on the result for a purchase. <br>
