## Description: <br>
Guides agents through implementing and configuring animation-style toon and cel shaders, including outlines, rim lighting, MatCap, emission, dissolve, hatching, and stylized effects across common Unity shader libraries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Yuki001](https://clawhub.ai/user/Yuki001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and technical artists use this skill to map animation-style shader goals to presets, feature sets, and relevant bundled references before producing shader configuration guidance or code. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shader guidance may be incomplete or differ from upstream shader behavior in production projects. <br>
Mitigation: Verify technical shader details against upstream shader documentation before production use. <br>
Risk: Generated shader code or material settings can affect rendering performance or compatibility. <br>
Mitigation: Review and test generated shader changes in the target Unity version, render pipeline, and content workflow. <br>
