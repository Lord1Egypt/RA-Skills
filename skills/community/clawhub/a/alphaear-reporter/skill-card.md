## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may perform outbound finance, news, search, and external model-provider requests. <br>
Mitigation: Run it only in an approved network environment and avoid proprietary research targets or API keys unless those data flows are intended. <br>
Risk: The skill can write local databases and generated model or chart files. <br>
Mitigation: Use an isolated workspace and review generated files before sharing or relying on them. <br>
Risk: Financial reports and prediction workflows may produce incorrect or misleading market analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Require analyst review before using outputs for investment, trading, or customer-facing decisions. <br>
