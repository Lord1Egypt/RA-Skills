## Description: <br>
Professional AI + IoT Engineering Career Roadmap Platform that generates personalized learning paths and specialization guidance for aspiring and current engineers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, career planners, and technical managers use this skill to generate AIoT career roadmaps, inspect available specializations, and review structured learning paths based on assessment data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Roadmap personalization may require profile, session, or user identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending confidential employer details, secrets, or unnecessary personal identifiers; treat sessionId and userId values as linkable activity data. <br>
