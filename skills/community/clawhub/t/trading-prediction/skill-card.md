## Description: <br>
A crypto research skill that guides agents to analyze BTC, ETH, and AVAX direction signals using MACD trend tracking, multi-timeframe confirmation, and crossover checks. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hyjaixiao](https://clawhub.ai/user/hyjaixiao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill for crypto market direction research, including MACD-based signal review, multi-asset screening, and backtest context for BTC, ETH, and AVAX. It is not investment advice and should not be used to authorize trading actions on its own. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading performance claims or Sharpe ratios may be misread as guaranteed future results. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the figures as research context only and independently validate assumptions, data, and results before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Agent output could be used as a direct trading signal without human review. <br>
Mitigation: Do not authorize automatic trading or account actions based only on this skill; require human review and separate risk controls. <br>
