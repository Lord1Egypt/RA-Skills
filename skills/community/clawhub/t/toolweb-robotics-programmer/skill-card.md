## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, robotics learners, and technical training teams use this skill to request personalized robotics and automation career roadmaps based on experience, skills, and goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Roadmap requests may include personal career details, proprietary project context, or other unnecessary identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit secrets, confidential company information, proprietary project details, or unnecessary personal identifiers. <br>
Risk: Generated roadmap guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific role, organization, or career decision. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before using them for training plans, hiring criteria, or career commitments. <br>
