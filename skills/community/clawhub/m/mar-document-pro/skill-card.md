## Description: <br>
Document Pro helps an agent read, extract, convert, and summarize PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, text, and Markdown documents, with optional AI analysis through SkillBoss API Hub. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[marjoriebroad](https://clawhub.ai/user/marjoriebroad) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to process uploaded documents, extract text and tables, summarize reports or papers, identify keywords, and present document insights in Chinese. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Document text may be sent to SkillBoss API Hub for AI analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with documents you are permitted to send to that provider, avoid confidential or regulated content unless approved, and prefer a dedicated API key with limited access. <br>
Risk: Scanned PDFs, complex formatting, images, and charts may be incomplete or require OCR before analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Use OCR where needed and review extracted text, tables, and summaries against the source document before relying on results. <br>
