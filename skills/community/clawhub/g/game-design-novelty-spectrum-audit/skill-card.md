## Description: <br>
Evaluate a game design, feature proposal, system concept, pitch, or prototype on the novelty spectrum between too familiar and too novel. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and developers use this skill to critique game concepts, feature proposals, pitches, and prototypes for whether their novelty is commercially legible, differentiated, and aligned with player expectations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may receive confidential unreleased game concepts, product plans, or business strategy during critique. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in trusted agent environments and avoid pasting sensitive details unless the publisher and runtime are acceptable for that information. <br>
Risk: Novelty-spectrum judgments are qualitative and may be mistaken for validated market evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as design critique inputs and validate important decisions with prototypes, player research, or stakeholder review. <br>
