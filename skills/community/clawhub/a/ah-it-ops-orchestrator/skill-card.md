## Description: <br>
IT operations meta-orchestrator specializing in routing tasks across PowerShell, .NET, infrastructure, Azure, and M365 subagents, with PowerShell-based automation as the default implementation language. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
IT operators, administrators, and developers use this skill to triage cross-domain operations requests and route work across PowerShell, .NET, Windows infrastructure, Azure, M365, and security specialists. It is useful when a task needs coordinated planning, safety review, and a unified implementation approach. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scripts or infrastructure changes for sensitive administration environments could have unintended operational impact. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm scope, approvals, and rollback plans before applying generated changes. <br>
Risk: IT operations requests may involve credentials, tenant details, or other secrets. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing secrets unless absolutely necessary and prefer least-privilege workflows. <br>
