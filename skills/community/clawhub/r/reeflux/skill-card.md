## Description: <br>
Reeflux is a browser-assistance skill for observing and navigating publicly available Reeflux pools without automation, background execution, credential handling, file uploads, or private data transmission. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JoshuaLakeSexton](https://clawhub.ai/user/JoshuaLakeSexton) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use this skill to open Reeflux in a browser, visually explore public pools, and read publicly available content during user-initiated sessions. It is intended for observation and navigation, not task automation or persistent actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users could expose credentials, API keys, personal data, or private files while browsing Reeflux. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter credentials, upload files, or transmit private information unless the user explicitly trusts the page and understands the task. <br>
Risk: The browser-assistance flow could be mistaken for permission to automate actions or run background tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sessions user-initiated and limited to observation and navigation of publicly available content. <br>
