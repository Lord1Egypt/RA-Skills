## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, and business operators use this skill to collect project information through guided Q&A and assemble a structured business plan for fundraising pitches, technology commercialization, market-entry planning, or strategy work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide confidential business plans, customer lists, trade secrets, unpublished financials, credentials, or personal data while preparing a plan. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized inputs, redact sensitive details where possible, and avoid uploading confidential or personal data unless necessary for the task. <br>
Risk: Business plan outputs may contain unsupported market, financial, or risk assumptions if the user provides incomplete information. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated assumptions, validate market and financial claims against source data, and have qualified stakeholders approve the plan before relying on it. <br>
