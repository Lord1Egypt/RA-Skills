## Description: <br>
Search cheap flights via the MoltFlights API. Find deals, compare prices, track routes, and set up price alerts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Jhalmari](https://clawhub.ai/user/Jhalmari) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to search flight options, compare prices across routes or dates, look up IATA airport codes, and prepare price-monitoring workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Flight routes, dates, passenger counts, and airport search terms are sent to MoltFlights. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for travel searches you are comfortable sharing with MoltFlights; avoid highly sensitive itineraries unless the provider's privacy handling is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Flight results can include third-party booking links. <br>
Mitigation: Review booking destinations and trip details before following or sharing links. <br>
