## Description: <br>
Guides teacher-student startup teams through launch and validation work, including commercialization assessment, team formation, technical validation, market validation, MVP testing, and customer interviews. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, faculty, and startup mentors use this skill to plan early teacher-student venture formation and validate whether a research outcome is ready for commercialization. It produces structured planning guidance, checklists, scoring criteria, and document outputs for launch and validation stages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
