## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Diagnostic output, account details, timestamps, and exact addresses may expose sensitive service or location information. <br>
Mitigation: Run diagnostics only when troubleshooting, share an exact address only when needed for coverage checks, and avoid posting full diagnostic output publicly. <br>
Risk: Provider, roaming, eSIM, router, or QoS changes can create costs, compatibility issues, throttling, or service disruption if applied without verification. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the device, plan, destination coverage, current contract terms, router model, and original settings before making changes. <br>
