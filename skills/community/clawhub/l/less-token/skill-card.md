## Description: <br>
Less Token converts verbose summarization prompts into compact I-Lang instructions for token-efficient text-to-text workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[adsorgcn](https://clawhub.ai/user/adsorgcn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, writers, analysts, and other agent users use this skill to translate verbose summarization requests into shorter structured I-Lang prompts while preserving the intended summary format, tone, and focus. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shortening summarization instructions can remove nuance or change emphasis in important summaries. <br>
Mitigation: Review the compressed prompt before relying on it, especially for business-critical or high-stakes summaries. <br>
