YouTube SERP Scout for agents. Search top-ranking videos, channels, and trends for content research and competitor tracking. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content researchers, marketing teams, and agents use this skill to search YouTube results for ranking videos, channels, and trends. It supports competitor tracking, keyword research, regional audience research, and content planning with country and language filters. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search queries and usage details are transmitted to api.aisa.one and may reveal confidential research topics if sensitive terms are submitted. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending secrets, personal data, or confidential business research terms in queries. <br>
Risk: The AISA_API_KEY authorizes paid API usage and could be misused if shared or exposed. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated, revocable API key and monitor usage or credits. <br>
