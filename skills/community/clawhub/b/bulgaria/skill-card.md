### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can retain trip preferences and notes in ~/bulgaria/memory.md, which may include sensitive itinerary or preference details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving details that are not needed for planning, use an only-when-asked activation posture if preferred, and delete the local memory file when the trip context is no longer useful. <br>
Risk: Travel guidance can become stale for prices, hours, transport schedules, weather, and safety conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm time-sensitive details with current official or operator sources before booking, traveling, hiking, or making safety decisions. <br>
