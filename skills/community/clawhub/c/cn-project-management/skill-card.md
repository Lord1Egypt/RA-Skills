## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese construction project management guidance and templates for planning, schedule control, earned value analysis, quality, safety, contract, information, and risk management based on GB/T 50326 and related construction standards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Construction project managers, owner-side representatives, delegated construction managers, and engineering consultants use this skill to draft project management plans, progress reports, risk registers, earned value analyses, quality checks, safety controls, and contract management artifacts for Chinese construction projects. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Construction standards, legal references, and safety guidance may be outdated or may not match local project requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Verify outputs against current applicable rules, contract terms, and qualified professional judgment before use. <br>
Risk: Generated project management templates and calculations may be incomplete or incorrect for a specific construction project. <br>
Mitigation: Review schedule, earned value, risk, quality, safety, and contract outputs with responsible project professionals before relying on them. <br>
