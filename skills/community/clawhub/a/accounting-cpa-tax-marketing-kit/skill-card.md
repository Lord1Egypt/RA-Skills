## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-focused marketing content for accounting, CPA, enrolled agent, and tax preparation firms with required disclosures and no prohibited claims. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Accounting, CPA, enrolled agent, and tax preparation firms use this skill to draft marketing campaigns, referral programs, review requests, and advisory-service outreach. It is intended for marketing templates and general educational content, not client-specific tax advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing may still need jurisdiction-specific review before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Verify state board, IRS, AICPA, FTC, TCPA, and CAN-SPAM requirements with a qualified professional. <br>
Risk: Users could expose confidential client or taxpayer information while generating campaigns. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter confidential client or taxpayer information into prompts or examples. <br>
