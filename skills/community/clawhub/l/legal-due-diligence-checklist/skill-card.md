## Description: <br>
Generates tailored legal due diligence checklists and templates to support transaction review and risk assessment for corporate and investment professionals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Corporate attorneys, M&A teams, startup counsel, investment teams, legal operations teams, and paralegals use this skill to structure due diligence requests, issue trackers, red-flag summaries, document indexes, and related transaction planning aids. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Checklist output may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific jurisdiction, deadline, filing requirement, confidentiality obligation, or transaction strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as legal workflow aids and verify jurisdiction-specific law, facts, deadlines, filings, ethics duties, confidentiality obligations, and strategy with qualified counsel before relying on them. <br>
