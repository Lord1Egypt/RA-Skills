## Description: <br>
Baidu Hot Cn helps agents produce Baidu trending-search lists and trend summaries, with the current helper script returning simulated topic data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[guohongbin-git](https://clawhub.ai/user/guohongbin-git) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to ask an agent for Baidu trending-topic snapshots, keyword hotness checks, and Chinese search trend summaries. Current outputs should be treated as simulated examples unless the publisher supplies verified live Baidu data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The current helper script returns simulated Baidu hot topics rather than verified live Baidu data. <br>
Mitigation: Label outputs as mock or simulated, avoid using rankings or counts for current-events or business decisions, and verify against authoritative sources before relying on trend claims. <br>
