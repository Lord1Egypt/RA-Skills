## Description: <br>
B2B LinkedIn ghostwriting for strategic interviews, hook engineering, and post body drafting when a user wants to turn a story, result, or insight into LinkedIn content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[samber](https://clawhub.ai/user/samber) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, executives, and B2B marketers use this skill to turn business stories, results, and insights into LinkedIn post workflows. The skill interviews for quantified context, proposes hooks, and drafts concise post copy with directive calls to action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may ask for business metrics, customer context, and marketing claims that could be sensitive or not yet public. <br>
Mitigation: Share only details approved for marketing use and remove confidential, customer-identifying, or non-public information before drafting. <br>
Risk: Drafted posts may include quantified claims that are inaccurate, outdated, or not publicly supportable. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all numbers, examples, and claims against approved source material before publishing. <br>
Risk: The workflow may route a completed draft through a separate humanizer-style polishing skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review any downstream polishing output to ensure the hook, facts, tone, and disclosure requirements remain intact. <br>
