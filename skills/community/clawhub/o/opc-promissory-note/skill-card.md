## Description: <br>
即期本票全流程技能，帮助用户掌握票据法合规要求、票据知识、真伪鉴别、诈骗识别、银行验证、佣金审查和审核决策。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and financial-review practitioners use this skill as a promissory-note review checklist for compliance checks, authenticity review, fraud-signal screening, bank verification, commission review, and decision support. It is a reference aid and should not be used by itself to execute or approve transactions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on the skill to approve or execute high-value promissory-note or cross-border financing transactions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as a reference checklist and require qualified legal, banking, and compliance review before any real financial action. <br>
Risk: SBLC, green-channel, and project-specific financing sections may be under-scoped or unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those sections as prompts for further diligence rather than authoritative transaction guidance. <br>
