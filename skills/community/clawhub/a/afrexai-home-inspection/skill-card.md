## Description: <br>
Helps agents provide home inspection business operations guidance covering pricing, licensing, insurance, equipment, reporting workflows, KPIs, and growth planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Home inspectors, inspection firm owners, and agents supporting home inspection businesses use this skill to answer operational questions, plan pricing and growth, and summarize compliance, insurance, equipment, reporting, and workflow considerations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Licensing, insurance, pricing, and compliance details may be incomplete or vary by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify state-specific requirements and business obligations with official boards, insurers, or qualified advisors before relying on the guidance. <br>
