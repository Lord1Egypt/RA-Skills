## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese-language shortcut list of China Postal Savings Bank and related service website entry points, including personal banking, corporate banking, investor relations, wealth management, procurement, recruitment, and rural finance pages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to retrieve PSBC-related official website addresses for common banking, corporate, investor, recruitment, procurement, and service-platform needs. It is a reference aid and should not be treated as independent verification before sensitive banking activity. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on a shortcut list as independent verification before sensitive banking activity. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm destinations through PSBC official channels before logging in, transferring money, applying for products, or entering personal data. <br>
Risk: Banking links may become outdated or mislabeled over time. <br>
Mitigation: Review listed URLs against PSBC's current official channels before deploying or using the skill in sensitive workflows. <br>
