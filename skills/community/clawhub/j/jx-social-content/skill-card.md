## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, creators, and social media operators use this skill to plan platform-specific posts, repurpose existing content, build content calendars, analyze performance patterns, and prepare scheduled social campaigns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires SKILLBOSS_API_KEY and describes publishing or scheduling social content through an external service. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide the API key when the publisher is trusted, the key can be revoked, and the connected social accounts and permissions are understood. Approve the exact content, platform, account, and scheduled time before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill describes collecting large numbers of social posts through a scraping workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use scraping only when authorized and when the workflow complies with the relevant platform rules and data-use expectations. <br>
Risk: Generated social content or optimization advice may be inaccurate, off-brand, or misleading if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and review claims, tone, links, calls to action, and compliance requirements before posting or scheduling. <br>
