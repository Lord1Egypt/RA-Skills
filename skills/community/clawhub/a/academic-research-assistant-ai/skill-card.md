## Description: <br>
Provides academic research and paper-writing support across literature review, study design, data analysis, manuscript drafting, journal matching, review responses, and grant writing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers, students, and academic writers use this skill to plan literature searches, structure reviews, design experiments, analyze results, draft manuscripts, select journals, respond to reviewers, and prepare grant materials. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Academic literature, journal metrics, funding rules, statistics, ethics, and AI-disclosure guidance may be incomplete or out of date. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important decisions against current authoritative sources before using the output in research, publication, funding, or compliance workflows. <br>
Risk: Draft manuscript, review-response, and grant-writing text may not satisfy discipline-specific, institutional, journal, or funder requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified human reviewers check final materials and confirm required disclosures, approvals, citations, and formatting before submission. <br>
