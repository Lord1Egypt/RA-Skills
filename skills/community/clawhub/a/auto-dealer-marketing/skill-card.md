## Description: <br>
Helps agents create, optimize, and plan automotive dealership marketing across inventory merchandising, VDP copy, seasonal campaigns, conquest ads, service retention, OEM co-op creative, local SEO, database mining, and BDC scripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External dealership marketing teams and agents use this skill to draft campaign creative, VDP copy, BDC outreach, local SEO plans, fixed-ops promotions, and strategic notes for automotive retail marketing. It is intended as a marketing copy and planning aid, not as legal or compliance authority. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing outputs may omit, misstate, or oversimplify legal, OEM co-op, pricing, consent, opt-out, or state advertising requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Have dealership counsel, OEM co-op reviewers, or qualified compliance staff verify disclosures, consent, opt-outs, pricing claims, and campaign rules before publishing or sending. <br>
Risk: CRM and outreach workflows may involve customer PII or regulated communication channels. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide CRM exports, customer PII, credentials, or campaign-system access unless necessary and authorized. <br>
