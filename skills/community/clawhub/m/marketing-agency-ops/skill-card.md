## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process agency operations context, including client names, retainer amounts, reporting details, billing notes, and team information. <br>
Mitigation: Use client aliases or minimal details where possible and avoid unnecessary confidential or regulated information. <br>
Risk: Generated reminders, reports, billing notes, and client-facing drafts may be incomplete or inaccurate if inputs are wrong or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm details before using outputs for reminders, reports, billing notes, or client-facing drafts. <br>
