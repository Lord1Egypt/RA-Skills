## Description: <br>
Cross-material knowledge synthesis session for OpenAlgernon that checks for at least two reviewed materials, surfaces conceptual bridges across materials, asks synthesis questions, and ends with a production scenario challenge. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AntonioVFranco](https://clawhub.ai/user/AntonioVFranco) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners and developers use this skill to connect concepts across studied OpenAlgernon materials and test whether they can apply those connections to production AI system scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill reads a local study database and may save session summaries to Notion and a daily conversation log, which can expose study context in shared workspaces or logs. <br>
Mitigation: Before running it, confirm the database path, notion-cli account, target Notion page, and log destination match the intended private or shared workspace. <br>
