## Description: <br>
Plan, research, and prepare for tattoos with artist discovery, design exploration, cultural research, and aftercare guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan tattoo decisions, research cultural context, vet artists, prepare for appointments, and follow general aftercare guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tattoo planning and aftercare guidance can be mistaken for professional medical or artist-specific advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the material as general guidance, follow the tattoo artist's instructions, and seek medical care for signs of infection or allergic reaction. <br>
Risk: Cultural-symbol guidance may be incomplete for a specific community, symbol, or personal context. <br>
Mitigation: Consult primary cultural sources, cultural organizations, or knowledgeable community representatives before committing to a design. <br>
