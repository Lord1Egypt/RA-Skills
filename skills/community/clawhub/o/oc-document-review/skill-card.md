## Description: <br>
Review messy specs, policies, and handoff notes with source-linked findings instead of vague rewrite advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiepu110](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiepu110) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external reviewers, and developers use this skill to review specs, policies, and handoff notes against explicit criteria, producing source-linked findings, suggested fixes, and open questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Document reviews may surface incorrect or overconfident findings if the input document or review criteria are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill on documents you intend the agent to read, provide explicit review criteria when possible, and review suggested edits or findings before publication or execution. <br>
Risk: The skill may be asked to review legal, tax, medical, or investment content where professional advice would be required. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the output to document-review observations and source-linked questions, and route professional advice decisions to qualified reviewers. <br>
