## Description: <br>
Grades English written homework for elementary students in grades 3-6, including spelling, fill-in-the-blank, sentence writing, short paragraphs, and grammar exercises. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, parents, tutors, and educators use this skill to score and correct elementary English homework with age-appropriate, encouraging feedback. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A review helper associated with the skill may request broader local access than typical grading workflows need. <br>
Mitigation: Install only from a trusted publisher and disable broad-access autoreview behavior unless that access is intentional. <br>
Risk: Homework grading feedback may be incorrect or may not match a teacher's rubric. <br>
Mitigation: Review scores and corrections before sharing them with a student, and provide a rubric override when specific classroom rules apply. <br>
