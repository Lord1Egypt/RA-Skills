## Description: <br>
Track calories and macros conversationally. Auto-adapts to your goals and style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to log meals from text or photos, estimate calories and macros, adapt tracking to weight, muscle, maintenance, or casual goals, and maintain a local library of repeated foods. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill locally retains personal food, goal, preference, and saved-food history. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if comfortable with local retention in ~/calories/memory.md, and review or delete that file to reset or remove retained nutrition history. <br>
Risk: Calorie and macro estimates may be approximate and may be inappropriate for contraindicated or medically supervised nutrition contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Screen for contraindications before tracking, avoid use for listed higher-risk populations without professional oversight, and present estimates as ranges rather than exact values. <br>
