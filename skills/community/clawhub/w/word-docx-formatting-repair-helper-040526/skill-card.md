## Use Case: <br>
Office workers, legal teams, students, documentation maintainers, and operations teams use this skill to plan and verify DOCX formatting repairs, style normalization, safe find-and-replace, numbering fixes, table cleanup, and conversion workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad terms such as formatting, styles, or tables even when the task is not about Word or DOCX work. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's task involves Word, DOCX, or Word-like document repair before applying the skill's workflow. <br>
Risk: Batch find-and-replace or formatting repairs can unintentionally alter protected content, fields, links, numbering, or tracked changes. <br>
Mitigation: Use backups or dry-run reports before destructive edits, apply changes in small reversible stages, and verify page flow, styles, numbering, tables, links, and conversion fidelity afterward. <br>
