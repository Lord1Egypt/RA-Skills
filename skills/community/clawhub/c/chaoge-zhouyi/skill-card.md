## Description: <br>
中国最古老的周易占卜 is a Chinese traditional divination skill for entertainment-oriented I Ching hexagram casting, Meihua Yishu interpretation, calendar/time calculations, and related classical fortune-telling references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to generate entertainment-only Chinese divination readings, consult traditional reference material, and produce interpretive guidance for I Ching, Meihua Yishu, Bazi, Zi Wei Dou Shu, Qimen Dunjia, and related folklore systems. It should not be used for medical, safety, financial, legal, mental-health, or non-consensual personal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes ritual, exorcism, and illness-related reference material that could mislead users in medical or vulnerable situations. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as folklore or entertainment only, direct users with health symptoms or mental distress to qualified professionals, and avoid presenting rituals or divination as treatment. <br>
Risk: Divination workflows may ask for names, birth dates, times, or details about other people. <br>
Mitigation: Use pseudonyms when real names are unnecessary, minimize personal data, and avoid advice about other people without consent. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on predictions or auspicious timing for financial, safety, legal, or other consequential decisions. <br>
Mitigation: State uncertainty clearly and avoid using the skill as a basis for consequential decisions; recommend appropriate professional advice for high-stakes topics. <br>
