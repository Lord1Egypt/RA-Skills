## Description: <br>
Automatically collects shared links from group chats, fetches their content, creates Feishu documents, and updates a knowledge base table. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wuhongchen](https://clawhub.ai/user/wuhongchen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users and teams use this skill to archive useful links from chats into a Feishu knowledge base with standardized documents, table records, and index updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can silently copy shared links and full content into a broadly visible Feishu knowledge base without tight consent or access limits. <br>
Mitigation: Install only after disabling silent mode or requiring confirmation, restricting approved users, chats, and domains, verifying the Feishu account and scopes used, and defining visibility, retention, audit, and deletion rules for archived content. <br>
