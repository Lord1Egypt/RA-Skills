## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product teams, and AI users use this skill to turn vague tasks or problematic prompts into structured Chinese prompt guidance, templates, and troubleshooting advice across GPT, Claude, Gemini, and similar models. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompt templates may be applied to sensitive or high-stakes tasks without enough domain review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts and resulting model outputs for factuality, safety, privacy, and policy fit before use. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed for Chinese-language prompt guidance and may not fit workflows that require another language or locale. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to adapt the guidance for the target language and verify terminology before deployment. <br>
