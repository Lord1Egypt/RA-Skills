## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agent activity on public platforms can change real accounts, affect reputation, or violate community rules. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review before posting and check platform and community rules before each engagement. <br>
Risk: Automation or account actions may require OAuth tokens, purchases, or platform-specific permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Use official platform APIs and labels where available, limit token scope, and confirm any purchase or account-changing action before execution. <br>
Risk: Moderation, production auth/deploy, migration, PR publishing, or autoreview full-access paths can change real services or expose repository diffs to external tools. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the release security guidance by pausing for explicit approval before sensitive operational paths and reviewing affected data before external submission. <br>
