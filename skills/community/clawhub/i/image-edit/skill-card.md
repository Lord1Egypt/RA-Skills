## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Some examples send selected images to third-party AI services, which can create privacy, consent, retention, and billing concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm each image is safe to upload, review provider privacy and retention terms, check consent and billing requirements, and prefer local tools for confidential photos, IDs, business images, or regulated content. <br>
Risk: Generative edits, upscaling, and restoration may introduce artifacts or alter important image details such as faces, edges, or backgrounds. <br>
Mitigation: Keep original files, use non-destructive editing, inspect outputs closely, and manually touch up or rerun edits when results are inaccurate. <br>
