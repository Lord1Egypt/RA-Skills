## Description: <br>
Access a detailed exercise database with muscle groups, equipment information, and instructions for building workout routines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[brucegutman](https://clawhub.ai/user/brucegutman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents use this skill to answer exercise lookup questions and help build workout routines using exercise, muscle, and equipment data from the wger MCP gateway. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Exercise and workout-related queries are sent to the external gateway at gateway.pipeworx.io. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending sensitive health details unless the user trusts that service. <br>
