## Description: <br>
Amazon keyword research and market opportunity analysis for sellers across supported Amazon marketplaces. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/phheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External Amazon sellers and ecommerce researchers use this skill to gather long-tail keyword suggestions, compare keywords, assess competitor landscape and seasonality, and form market opportunity recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product keywords may be sent to public Amazon autocomplete, Google Trends, or web search endpoints during research. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for explicit Amazon market research tasks and avoid submitting sensitive product plans unless external-service sharing is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Public autocomplete, search, and trend data can be incomplete, rate-limited, or misleading for business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated reports as research guidance and verify important conclusions against trusted sales, advertising, or marketplace analytics before acting. <br>
