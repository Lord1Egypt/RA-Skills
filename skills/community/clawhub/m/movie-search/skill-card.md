## Description: <br>
Searches for movie and TV title resource links, grouping download, streaming, and subtitle search targets for Chinese or English queries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Placeboy](https://clawhub.ai/user/Placeboy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to find organized movie or TV search links by title, year, and media type. It is intended for resource discovery across streaming platforms, subtitle sites, and third-party download indexes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may direct agents toward torrent and third-party download sites that can be illegal or unsafe. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official streaming, rental, purchase, or public-domain sources and verify copyright legality in the user's jurisdiction. <br>
Risk: Unknown torrent or download pages may expose users to unsafe files or misleading links. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated links before use and avoid downloading files from untrusted pages. <br>
