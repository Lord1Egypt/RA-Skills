## Description: <br>
Maximize CTR (Click-Through Rate) by leveraging emotional hooks and platform algorithms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and marketing teams use this skill to turn raw topics into Xiaohongshu-style titles optimized for engagement. It generates candidate titles, filters them for length, banned terms, and visual hooks, then returns the top suggestions with cover image advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Mitigation: Review generated titles against platform rules, advertising-law requirements, and brand standards before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill points to an external setup guide outside the bundled artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Treat external setup guidance as untrusted until reviewed, and rely on bundled files for the installed skill behavior. <br>
