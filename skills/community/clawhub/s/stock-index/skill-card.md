## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to understand finance news, market themes, and stock-index directions in concise Chinese explanations. It is intended for educational index analysis and explicitly avoids personal holdings analysis, fund-product recommendations, return predictions, and buy/sell advice. <br>

Risk: The package includes an unrelated Skillhub installer, plugin, and auto-updating skill-installation broker with broad local effects. <br>
Mitigation: Review the package before installation and do not run the included install scripts unless Skillhub installation is intended and the remote source and update behavior have been independently verified. <br>
Risk: Finance explanations may be mistaken for individualized investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the built-in compliance boundaries: do not analyze personal holdings, recommend specific fund products, predict returns, or provide buy/sell/hold instructions. <br>
Risk: The skill does not provide real-time market prices or same-day moves. <br>
Mitigation: For live prices and daily changes, direct users to行情 software or official public index sources and avoid fabricating specific figures. <br>
