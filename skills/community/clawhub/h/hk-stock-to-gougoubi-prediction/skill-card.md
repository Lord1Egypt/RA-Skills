## Description: <br>
Turn Hong Kong stock research into Gougoubi-ready prediction proposals with deterministic market wording, deadline selection, resolution rules, and tags. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chinasong](https://clawhub.ai/user/chinasong) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external users use this skill to convert Hong Kong stock analysis or themes into one objectively resolvable Gougoubi prediction-market proposal with deadline, rules, tags, and optional creation input. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A market proposal could be published with an incorrect name, deadline, rules, tags, or transaction confirmation. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated Gougoubi fields and any publication confirmation before asking the agent to submit or create the market. <br>
Risk: Installing from a GitHub or local repository instead of the reviewed ClawHub artifact could introduce unreviewed installer behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the referenced installer script before using a non-ClawHub installation source. <br>
