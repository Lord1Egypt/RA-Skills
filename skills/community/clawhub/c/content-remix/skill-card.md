## Description: <br>
Chinese content remix tool with API-powered platform rules that transforms one piece of content into platform-native versions for Xiaohongshu, Douyin, WeChat Official Account, Zhihu, Weibo, and WeChat Video with compliance checks for Chinese advertising law. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and content operators use this skill to adapt Chinese-language source content for multiple Chinese social platforms. It produces platform-specific copy, hashtags or topics, compliance findings, and distribution guidance while preserving the source message. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Source drafts, unreleased campaigns, or regulated content may be sent to a third-party API when using the bundled script or API-backed rule lookup. <br>
Mitigation: Review content sensitivity before use and avoid submitting confidential, unreleased, or regulated material unless third-party handling is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated compliance checks and advertising-law replacements may miss context-specific legal or platform policy issues. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review compliance findings, especially for regulated products, advertising claims, financial, medical, education, or other high-risk content. <br>
