## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate advice for covert persuasion, control tactics, or pressure in sensitive interpersonal contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for legitimate strategy coaching, avoid intimate relationships, vulnerable people, workplace power imbalances, political persuasion, deception, dependency creation, and covert pressure, and review outputs before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The skill may steer negotiation or communication choices in manipulative ways even though it is text-only and does not execute code. <br>
Mitigation: Keep a human reviewer responsible for ethical boundaries, reject fraudulent or harmful requests, and prefer transparent, consent-aware communication. <br>
