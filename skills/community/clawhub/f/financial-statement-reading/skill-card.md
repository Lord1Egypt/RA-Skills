## Use Case: <br>
Employees, analysts, and external users can use this skill to structure educational financial statement review, compare financial ratios, analyze cash flow, and flag common financial-reporting warning signs. It is intended to support understanding and reporting, not to replace professional accounting, legal, or investment judgment. <br>
