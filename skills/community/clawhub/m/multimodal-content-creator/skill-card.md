## Description: <br>
End-to-end multimodal content creation workflow: receive WhatsApp requests by text or voice, transcribe audio with Whisper, generate images with DALL-E 3, and reply automatically. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External freelance creators and content automation operators use this skill to process customer WhatsApp requests, turn text or voice prompts into image prompts, generate images, and send results back to the requester. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires sensitive credentials, including an OpenAI API key and a WhatsApp-style token stored in local configuration. <br>
Mitigation: Use environment or secret management for the OpenAI key, restrict permissions on ~/.wacli/config.json, delete unused local tokens, and rotate any credential that may have been exposed. <br>
Risk: Customer text, voice notes, and generated prompts may be sent to OpenAI services and saved in local output files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid processing sensitive audio or text unless consent is clear, minimize customer data in prompts, and remove generated transcripts or images when they are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: The workflow can automatically send generated images back to WhatsApp recipients. <br>
Mitigation: Add message approval, recipient allowlists, or recipient limits before production use, especially for customer-facing accounts. <br>
