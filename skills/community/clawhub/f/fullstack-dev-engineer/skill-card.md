## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated authentication or deployment examples may be unsafe if copied into real systems without review. <br>
Mitigation: Replace placeholders, avoid long-lived browser-stored tokens, use least-privilege secrets, and require human review before production use. <br>
Risk: Generated CI/CD or Kubernetes commands and configuration can affect the wrong environment. <br>
Mitigation: Verify Kubernetes context, namespace, target environment, and secret scope before applying changes. <br>
