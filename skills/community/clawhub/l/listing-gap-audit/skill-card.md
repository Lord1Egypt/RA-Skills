## Description: <br>
Audits listing competitiveness by comparing a PDP or listing against top competitors and surfacing high-impact copy and proof gaps for rewrite prioritization. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, ecommerce, and conversion teams use this skill to benchmark a product listing against competitors, identify copy, proof, offer, and objection-handling gaps, and plan prioritized listing improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The audit may produce inaccurate or misleading listing recommendations if supplied listing text or browser evidence is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Provide complete listing text or URLs and label any partial browser evidence before relying on the recommendations. <br>
Risk: Competitor benchmarking can lead to copying competitor claims or adopting claims that are not supported by the product. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as rewrite guidance only, avoid verbatim competitor claims, and keep recommendations within current product constraints. <br>
Risk: Compliance-sensitive product claims may be surfaced during the comparison. <br>
Mitigation: Flag sensitive claims for review and confirm claim support before publishing revised listing copy. <br>
Risk: The artifact states non-commercial license terms that may not fit commercial reuse without separate permission. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm license permissions before using or adapting the skill in a commercial context. <br>
