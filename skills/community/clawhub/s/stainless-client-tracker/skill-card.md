## Description: <br>
Tracks stainless steel industry customers, follow-up stages, visit records, opportunity funnels, reminders, reports, and CRM integrations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales and business-development teams in the stainless steel industry use this skill to organize customer profiles, follow-up activity, opportunity pipeline status, and recurring reports. It can also guide agents in drafting tracker data structures and integration plans for Feishu, WeChat, email, and calendars. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer records and contact details may be synced with Feishu, WeChat, email, or calendar integrations without clear data-scope limits. <br>
Mitigation: Before connecting integrations, confirm which customer fields may be synced and use limited business accounts or scopes. <br>
Risk: Automated customer-facing messages, quotes, contracts, or reminders may be sent without adequate review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before any customer-facing message, document, or calendar action is sent or created. <br>
