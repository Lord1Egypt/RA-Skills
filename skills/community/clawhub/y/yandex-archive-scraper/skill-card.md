## Description: <br>
Search and extract data from Yandex.Archive metric books, newspapers, and directories using Scrapling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flobo3](https://clawhub.ai/user/flobo3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to search Yandex.Archive indexes for archival records and extract document titles, snippets, viewer links, thumbnail links, and original image links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automating access to Yandex.Archive may violate terms of service or trigger legal, account, IP blocking, or rate-limit consequences. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when authorized, prefer official or permissioned access methods where available, and keep request volume within allowed limits. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on scraping and bot-protection bypass behavior that can fail or change without notice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as best-effort search results, review failures before relying on them, and isolate the scraping dependencies from sensitive environments. <br>
