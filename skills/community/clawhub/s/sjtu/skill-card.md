## Description: <br>
Provides step-by-step guidance for browsing, posting, replying, searching, and managing notifications on Shanghai Jiao Tong University's Shuiyuan Discourse forum. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, alumni, and other Shuiyuan users can use this skill as a quick guide for navigating the forum, participating in discussions after login, using search and notifications, and understanding common Discourse API endpoints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Authenticated forum actions such as posting, replying, or viewing private notifications may expose account activity or private information. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation before authenticated actions and use normal browser login or a revocable limited token; do not provide raw passwords or session cookies. <br>
