## Description: <br>
Reusable Chinese academic writing guidance for drafting, revising, and evaluating C-journal-style papers in journalism, education governance, and public administration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shiiyyo](https://clawhub.ai/user/shiiyyo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers, students, and academic writers use this skill to structure Chinese C-journal-style papers, develop research questions, synthesize literature reviews, build analytical frameworks, and polish mechanism-focused drafts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Academic writing assistance can introduce fabricated citations, unsupported facts, or misleading claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify every citation, fact, dataset claim, and case description against authoritative sources before use. <br>
Risk: Generated paper structure or prose may conflict with institutional academic-integrity requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as writing and revision support only, and follow the author's institution, journal, and disclosure rules. <br>
Risk: Mechanism-focused drafts may overstate empirical support when the available material is typified rather than statistical. <br>
Mitigation: Keep method boundaries explicit and avoid claiming case libraries, statistical findings, or empirical tests that were not actually performed. <br>
