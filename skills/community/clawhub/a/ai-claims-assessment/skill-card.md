## Description: <br>
Assesses public healthcare-AI vendor documents to identify gaps between AI use claims, legal language, and HIPAA rules on PHI handling and model training. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dangsllc](https://clawhub.ai/user/dangsllc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External healthcare compliance, vendor review, and procurement teams use this skill to compare public product, Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy language for healthcare-AI vendors. It produces cited public-documentation gaps that a covered entity may need to resolve during vendor review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Security evidence notes powerful repo, GitHub, Convex, and moderation workflows in the scanned skill set. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with intended credentials and targets; confirm the target, environment, and credentials before running helper workflows. <br>
Risk: The assessment is limited to public documents and could be mistaken for proof of actual PHI handling or a legal determination. <br>
Mitigation: Keep findings framed as public-documentation gaps and route material vendor-review decisions through qualified compliance or legal reviewers. <br>
