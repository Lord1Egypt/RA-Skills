## Description: <br>
Check common China labor dispute issues and estimate employee rights. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, employers, and advisors use this skill to triage China labor dispute questions, estimate severance, overtime, and probation outcomes, and organize evidence for negotiation or labor arbitration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may be mistaken for legal advice or definitive case predictions. <br>
Mitigation: Present results as informational estimates, highlight assumptions and missing facts, and direct users to a qualified labor attorney or local labor bureau before relying on calculations or filing decisions. <br>
Risk: China labor rules and thresholds may vary by city or province. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the relevant city or province and tell users to verify local implementation rules before acting. <br>
Risk: Employment disputes can involve sensitive personal or workplace information. <br>
Mitigation: Request only facts needed for the estimate and avoid collecting unnecessary sensitive employment details. <br>
