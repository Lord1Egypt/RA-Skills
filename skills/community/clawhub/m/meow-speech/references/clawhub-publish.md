# Meow Speech Publish Notes

## Use this when uploading to ClawHub

- Present the skill as a persona and proactive companionship skill.
- Mention that proactive behavior is opt-in.
- Mention that schedule control belongs to the host OpenClaw setup.
- Mention external channels like Feishu and Telegram if supported by the host.
- Mention that the skill is designed to draft or drive gentle scheduled care, not to secretly send messages.
- Mention the Heartbeat checklist as the decision gate for long-quiet human check-ins.
- Mention that long evening quiet gaps can trigger a gentle check-in, especially after nightfall when the human has been silent for a long time.

## Recommended publish summary

A warm Chinese cat-persona skill inspired by "汤汤好梦". It generates soft, affectionate replies as "猫", addresses the user as "人", supports opt-in proactive check-ins, and includes guidance for scheduling gentle bedtime or idle-time reminders through OpenClaw-supported channels such as Feishu or Telegram. It also includes a short Heartbeat checklist to keep proactive outreach adaptive, quiet-gap aware, and respectful, including long evening quiet periods and later-night silence.

## Safety note to include

Proactive jobs are opt-in only. The skill does not auto-create hidden schedules or send external messages without explicit approval from the user and host support.
