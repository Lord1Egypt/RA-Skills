## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to choose AI music providers, craft music prompts, and generate songs, instrumentals, background music, or sound effects through web tools, APIs, or local models. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, lyrics, uploaded audio, generated tracks, and API keys may be shared with third-party music providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential or unreleased material unless the selected provider has been vetted, and use separate scoped API keys where possible. <br>
Risk: Commercial rights and provider terms vary across Suno, Udio, Stable Audio, MusicGen, Mubert, Soundraw, Riffusion, and Replicate-hosted models. <br>
Mitigation: Check the current provider license and plan terms before using generated music in commercial releases. <br>
Risk: Unofficial Suno or Udio API wrappers may introduce additional reliability, privacy, and terms-of-use risk. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official web or API access when available and vet wrapper services before sending prompts, files, or credentials. <br>
