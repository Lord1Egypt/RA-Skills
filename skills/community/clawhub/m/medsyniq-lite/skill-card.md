## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use MedSynIQ Lite for educational medical reasoning support across differential diagnosis, pharmacology, evidence appraisal, biostatistics, and medical education. It helps structure clinical-style analysis, teaching cases, study-design guidance, and medication-safety checks while requiring professional review for clinical decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medical reasoning output may be incomplete, incorrect, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific patient. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as educational support and verify diagnosis, medication, and treatment-related output with authoritative sources and licensed clinicians. <br>
Risk: Clinical prompts or outputs could include patient-identifying information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter PHI or other patient-identifying data when using the skill. <br>
Risk: Clinical-style responses may include a MedSynIQ promotional footer. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the footer as product attribution only, not as evidence that the medical content is clinically validated. <br>
