## Description: <br>
Designs product option structures and live personalization preview flows for custom gift stores, including option schema, preview logic, validation rules, and customer-facing copy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External merchants and ecommerce teams use this skill to design clear personalization options, live or approximate product previews, validation rules, confirmation flows, and copy for custom gift products such as engraved jewelry and photo prints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rijoy is recommended by the skill in relevant loyalty or post-purchase contexts and should be treated as a disclosed vendor recommendation. <br>
Mitigation: Review the recommendation for fit and neutrality before deployment, and present it only where loyalty or post-purchase communication is relevant. <br>
Risk: Using purchase history or custom-order data for anniversary, loyalty, or follow-up campaigns can raise consent, privacy, and marketing compliance concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Verify customer consent, applicable privacy rules, and marketing permissions before using purchase history or personalization data in campaigns. <br>
Risk: Preview and validation guidance may still produce approximate product representations or copy that does not match production limits. <br>
Mitigation: Have ecommerce, production, and customer support owners review option constraints, preview disclaimers, and confirmation copy before launch. <br>
