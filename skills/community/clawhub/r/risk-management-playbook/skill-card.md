## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The README includes a global install command with --yes, which can reduce review before installation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the publisher and repository, review the skill files, and scan the package before installing it globally. <br>
Risk: The skill produces broad risk, regulatory, insurance, sanctions, AML, security-incident, and crisis guidance that could be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a general playbook and validate recommendations against the relevant jurisdiction, industry requirements, and qualified experts before acting. <br>
Risk: The broad trigger can apply the playbook to situations that require domain-specific incident response or legal escalation. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for operational context, identify when specialist review is required, and avoid treating the output as an authoritative incident or compliance decision. <br>
