## Description: <br>
Generates chemical process diagrams, including PFD, P&ID, system architecture, and data-flow diagrams with industry symbols, temperature color gradients, and Chinese font support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xx235300](https://clawhub.ai/user/xx235300) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, process engineers, and technical teams use this skill to create presentation-ready process, piping and instrumentation, system architecture, and data-flow diagrams for chemical, pharmaceutical, food, water-treatment, and related process-industry workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional browser-automation guidance may navigate pages, click controls, fill forms, capture screenshots, or extract text when used for research. <br>
Mitigation: Use agent-browser only when needed, avoid logged-in or sensitive pages, and review navigation, clicks, form fills, screenshots, and extracted text before allowing them. <br>
Risk: Generated process diagrams may be useful for presentations but may not replace engineering-grade design or CAD review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated diagrams with qualified domain experts before using them for engineering, safety, procurement, or operational decisions. <br>
