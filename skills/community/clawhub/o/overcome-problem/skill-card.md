## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, employees, and other users use this skill to turn complex challenges into clear constraints, solution options, sequenced tasks, blockers, and progress updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad problem-solving prompts may activate the skill when the user only needs a narrow answer. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants structured decomposition before producing a full action plan. <br>
Risk: Problem statements may include sensitive or confidential details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive details unless they are appropriate for the user's normal agent environment. <br>
