## Description: <br>
Safe chainsaw operation and basic tree felling techniques for users who need to fell a tree, cut firewood, clear storm damage, or choose their first chainsaw. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to screen chainsaw and tree-work situations, enforce PPE and professional-arborist escalation checks, and provide cautious guidance for basic felling, limbing, bucking, storm cleanup, saw selection, and maintenance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat written chainsaw guidance as a substitute for hands-on training or certified arborist judgment. <br>
Mitigation: The skill requires screening for hazardous conditions, directs users to certified professionals for high-risk work, and presents the guidance as general safety support rather than a replacement for training. <br>
Risk: Chainsaw and tree-work mistakes can cause severe injury, property damage, or death, especially near power lines, structures, storm damage, large trees, or uncertain lean and tension conditions. <br>
Mitigation: The skill emphasizes mandatory PPE, refusal to proceed without safety checks, explicit professional-escalation criteria, and conservative limits for beginners. <br>
