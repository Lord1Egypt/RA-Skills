## Description: <br>
Provides a startup investor relations playbook for investor updates, shareholder letters, meeting preparation, cap table planning, investor-facing KPIs, crisis communication, and fundraise readiness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, CFOs, and chiefs of staff use this skill to create repeatable investor communication workflows, prepare updates and meetings, track investor-facing metrics, and assess fundraise readiness. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Linked external resources may involve purchases, separate setup, or entry of confidential company or investor data. <br>
Mitigation: Review each linked resource independently before purchasing, installing anything, or entering confidential data. <br>
Risk: Investor relations templates and scoring rubrics may not match a company's legal, financing, or disclosure obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Have company leadership, counsel, or finance owners review investor-facing materials before distribution. <br>
