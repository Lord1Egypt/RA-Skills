## Description: <br>
中级会计师考试AI备考导师，独创五大应试方法论+八大执行引擎+六大应试资源库，覆盖会计实务、财务管理、经济法三科全部核心考点，从知识点罗列升级为系统化得分体系，帮助考生提高备考效率。 <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners preparing for China's Intermediate Accounting Professional Qualification exam use this skill for structured tutoring, practice questions, mistake review, formulas, accounting entries, legal-rule memorization, and study planning across accounting practice, financial management, and economic law. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Wrong-answer logs and personalized study planning may contain personal study data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive personal information, confirm where any study history is stored, and clear logs when they are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Accounting, tax, legal, and exam-policy content can become outdated or may not match the user's jurisdiction or exam year. <br>
Mitigation: Verify high-stakes answers against current official exam materials and authoritative accounting, tax, and legal references before relying on them. <br>
