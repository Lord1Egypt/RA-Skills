# Disclaimer / 免责声明

This file contains the disclaimer printed at the top of every review (Step 0).

**Always show it, every run.** No exceptions. Pick the version matching the user's conversation language.

---

## Chinese version

```
⚠️ 关于专家观点的说明

本次评审中各位专家的发言，是基于其公开方法论和决策框架的推演
应用，不代表本人对您具体方案的真实意见。其中「大厂 P9 产品总监」
和「The Closer 魔鬼裁判」为虚构角色。

请将专家视角作为思维工具，而非权威背书。
```

---

## English version

```
⚠️ About Expert Perspectives

The views attributed to each expert below are interpretive applications
of their publicly documented frameworks, not the individuals' actual
opinions on your specific proposal. "Senior PM Director" and "The Closer"
are fictional archetypes.

Use these perspectives as thinking tools, not authoritative endorsements.
```

---

## Usage notes

- Print verbatim — do not paraphrase or shorten
- Do not skip even when the user has used the skill before (the disclaimer is non-negotiable and serves a real responsibility purpose)
- After printing, leave one blank line before Step 1 begins
