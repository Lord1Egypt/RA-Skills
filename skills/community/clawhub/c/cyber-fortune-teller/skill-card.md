## Description: <br>
Provides entertainment-oriented Chinese fortune-telling readings from birth details and optional face or palm images, including Bazi, astrology, physiognomy, palmistry, and cross-check summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for entertainment fortune-telling and traditional-culture readings based on birth date, birth time, birth city, gender, and optional face or palm photos. It produces Bazi charts, horoscope-style readings, face or palm interpretations, daily fortune summaries, and cross-validation notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users for sensitive birth details and optional face or palm images. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when users intentionally choose an entertainment fortune-telling interaction, and avoid collecting or sharing unnecessary personal details or images. <br>
Risk: The skill may produce health, lifespan, relationship, fertility, wealth, status, or character claims that sound authoritative. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as entertainment and traditional-culture interpretation only, and do not use them to make decisions about yourself or other people. <br>
Risk: Appearance-based face and palm readings can create misleading or unfair judgments. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid treating appearance-based conclusions as factual assessments of ability, character, health, or future outcomes. <br>
