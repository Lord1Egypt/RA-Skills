## Description: <br>
Guides off-page SEO link-building strategy for acquiring quality backlinks through outreach, guest posting, broken-link replacement, unlinked brand mentions, directory submissions, partnerships, and digital PR. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
SEO practitioners, founders, marketers, and site owners use this skill to plan off-page SEO link acquisition, identify suitable backlink strategies, and produce linkable asset ideas, outreach templates, target-site categories, and tracking metrics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project context files may contain sensitive business details that the skill can use when drafting link-building strategy or outreach templates. <br>
Mitigation: Review `.claude/project-context.md` and `.cursor/project-context.md` before use and remove sensitive details that should not be reflected in outputs. <br>
Risk: Link-building recommendations and outreach drafts can become spammy or low-quality if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for relevance, personalization, accurate claims, and appropriate outreach cadence before sending or publishing. <br>
