## Description: <br>
A comprehensive AI agent skill for anyone facing criminal charges, civil lawsuits, or regulatory investigations that helps users understand what they are facing, protect their rights from the first moment of contact, work effectively with defense counsel, evaluate options at every stage, and make informed decisions when the stakes are highest. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIstack](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIstack) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users facing criminal charges, civil lawsuits, or regulatory investigations use this skill to organize facts, understand defense process steps, preserve rights and evidence, prepare questions for counsel, and evaluate plea or settlement options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill concerns serious legal matters and may be mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it to organize facts and prepare questions for qualified counsel in the relevant jurisdiction. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms such as "defense" may activate the skill in unrelated conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is discussing a criminal, civil, regulatory, or administrative defense situation before applying the guidance. <br>
