## Description: <br>
Anonymous first-person crypto confession engine. Generates raw, specific, honest loss stories that teach through experience. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[0xzahra](https://clawhub.ai/user/0xzahra) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content creators and educators use this skill to draft anonymized first-person crypto loss stories that illustrate trading psychology, risk, and regret without identifying real people. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated confession drafts could expose personal details, wallet addresses, specific dates, or project identifiers if the user provides them. <br>
Mitigation: Strip identifying details before generation and review the final text before publishing. <br>
Risk: Crypto-loss narratives may be mistaken for financial advice or may normalize reckless trading behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the output focused on educational reflection and avoid glorifying losses, pitching products, or recommending trades. <br>
