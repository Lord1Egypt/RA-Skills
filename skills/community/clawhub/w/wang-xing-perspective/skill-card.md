## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to frame startup decisions, strategy planning, competitive analysis, and business model questions through a Wang Xing-inspired analytical perspective. It should be treated as role-played analysis based on public information, not as actual advice from Wang Xing or verified factual research. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the role-played perspective for Wang Xing's actual advice or for verified factual research. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as Wang Xing-inspired analysis based on public information, and require users to verify strategic or factual claims before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases could activate the persona when a user only mentions Wang Xing or related concepts in passing. <br>
Mitigation: Use narrow trigger phrases or explicit invocation when deploying the skill in environments where accidental activation would be disruptive. <br>
Risk: The artifact notes limited first-hand sources and some public-knowledge inference, which can reduce fidelity to the real person's views. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as a decision-framing aid, not an authoritative representation, and supplement important analysis with current primary sources. <br>
