## Description: <br>
Convert persona docs into detailed feature specifications with acceptance criteria. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wpank](https://clawhub.ai/user/wpank) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product managers, and QA teams use this skill to turn persona documentation, user feedback, and stakeholder needs into implementable feature specifications with user stories, acceptance criteria, dependencies, and scope boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated specifications may encode incomplete persona assumptions or ambiguous stakeholder requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review the specification against source persona documents and confirm priority, scope, dependencies, edge cases, and acceptance criteria with stakeholders before development. <br>
Risk: Installing through a repository URL can depend on an unpinned or unverified source. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer the ClawHub install command; when using a repository URL, verify the source and pin a trusted commit or release. <br>
