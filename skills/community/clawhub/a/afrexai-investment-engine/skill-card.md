This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to structure investment research, portfolio health checks, position sizing, and risk review across stocks, crypto, ETFs, bonds, and alternatives. Outputs should be treated as educational and non-personalized analysis rather than financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can influence financial decisions even though it does not execute trades or request credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market data independently and require explicit human approval before using any separate broker or trading tool to place real orders. <br>
Risk: Tax and investment conclusions may not match the user's jurisdiction, objectives, or risk tolerance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational and non-personalized, and check tax or financial guidance with qualified local professionals before acting. <br>
