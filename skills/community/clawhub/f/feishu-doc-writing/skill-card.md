## Description: <br>
Organizes group discussions, meetings, and research into clear, complete Feishu documents with structured headings, visuals, emphasis, authorship, permissions, and redaction guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[guoqunabc](https://clawhub.ai/user/guoqunabc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, employees, and agents use this skill to turn chat threads, meeting notes, and research material into polished Feishu documents that are readable by people who did not participate in the original discussion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A Feishu document could be shared with unintended recipients or overly broad permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm intended recipients, who counts as the boss or group members, and required access levels before creating or sharing the document. <br>
Risk: Internal sensitive information could appear in a generated document, especially in external-facing versions. <br>
Mitigation: Redact API keys, internal system addresses, unpublished data, and any other sensitive details before publication. <br>
