## Description: <br>
Simulates a Ni Haixia-style traditional Chinese medicine consultation that asks symptom follow-ups, performs pattern differentiation, and suggests classical formulas and lifestyle guidance for study reference. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[william22820785-cmyk](https://clawhub.ai/user/william22820785-cmyk) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and learners use this skill to explore Ni Haixia-style TCM reasoning, including symptom intake, six-channel and organ pattern analysis, formula suggestions, and study-oriented lifestyle guidance. It should not be used for self-diagnosis, treatment, or medication decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce confident medical-style recommendations, herbal formulas, and dosage-style guidance that users could mistake for treatment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for study or historical discussion, keep medical disclaimers visible, avoid self-diagnosis or treatment decisions, and consult a licensed clinician for symptoms, pregnancy or postpartum issues, bleeding, severe pain, fever, cancer, psychiatric symptoms, or medication and herb use. <br>
