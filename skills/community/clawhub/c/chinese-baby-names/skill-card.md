## Description: <br>
Generates meaningful Chinese baby name suggestions from family preferences such as surname, gender, style, intended meaning, generational requirements, and characters to avoid. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xixinglong](https://clawhub.ai/user/xixinglong) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to gather naming preferences and receive Chinese baby name recommendations with pinyin, meaning explanations, and optional ratings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may disclose family naming details while using the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Only share surname, generational naming details, and preferences that users are comfortable including in a chat. <br>
Risk: Suggested names may be personally, culturally, or dialectically unsuitable despite following the requested naming criteria. <br>
Mitigation: Review candidate names with family members or fluent Chinese speakers before making a final naming decision. <br>
