## Description: <br>
Growth Guardian helps parents keep structured child growth records across an eight-dimension development model, analyze conversations for wellbeing signals, and generate weekly, monthly, and quarterly reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[duzhilei951](https://clawhub.ai/user/duzhilei951) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Parents and caregivers use this skill to organize child development observations, maintain per-child growth archives, identify motivation or relationship signals, and produce recurring summaries for reflection and planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create long-term records containing sensitive information about children and family life. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated private folder, avoid real names or school and medical details where possible, and periodically delete or redact old records. <br>
Risk: Conversation transcripts or recordings may expose sensitive information about minors. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide recordings or transcripts when comfortable with that processing, and prefer minimal, relevant excerpts for analysis. <br>
