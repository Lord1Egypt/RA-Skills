## Description: <br>
MktClaw is a Chinese demand-to-delivery AI marketing agency skill that helps agents clarify briefs, route work across 10 agency roles, and draft brand plans, campaigns, media strategies, livestream scripts, crisis PR materials, and marketing analyses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, brand, growth, media, content, livestream, KOL, data, and crisis-PR practitioners can use this skill to turn a client brief into structured Chinese-language agency deliverables. It is especially suited for first-draft planning artifacts that still need human review before client delivery or publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may receive marketing briefs or Campaign Vault context that includes PII, customer-level data, credentials, wallet details, or sensitive financial records. <br>
Mitigation: Use only local, aggregated, non-sensitive data and avoid entering credentials, wallet details, raw customer records, or sensitive financial information. <br>
Risk: Draft marketing claims for medical-aesthetic, finance, education, or other regulated sectors may be inaccurate, noncompliant, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified professional, legal, or compliance reviewers approve regulated claims before using them externally. <br>
Risk: Crisis PR outputs can affect reputation, legal exposure, or public communication during sensitive incidents. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review and approve crisis PR statements, media Q&A, and response plans before publication. <br>
Risk: Bundled documentation is described by security evidence as stale and overbroad. <br>
Mitigation: Check current platform rules, advertising law, and market facts before relying on generated campaign or media recommendations. <br>
