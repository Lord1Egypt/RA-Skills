## Description: <br>
Generate UK/EU GDPR compliance documents, including privacy policies, cookie policies, DPIAs, ROPA, DSAR responses, data breach notifications, and consent forms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
UK and EU businesses, freelancers, SMEs, DPOs, and consultants use this skill to draft tailored GDPR compliance documents from business and processing details. Outputs are intended as implementation-ready starting points that require professional legal or data-protection review before adoption. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United Kingdom and European Union <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated GDPR documents may be incomplete or unsuitable if treated as legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the outputs as drafts and have a qualified solicitor, DPO, or data protection professional review lawful bases, PECR and cookie positions, transfer mechanisms, sector obligations, and retention periods before implementation. <br>
Risk: Incorrect or missing business details can lead to inaccurate lawful bases, retention periods, processor terms, or breach-response wording. <br>
Mitigation: Collect the business type, personal-data categories, required document set, and special circumstances before generation, and preserve placeholders for facts the user must confirm. <br>
Risk: The skill is scoped to UK and EU GDPR materials and may not cover other privacy regimes or sector rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for UK/EU GDPR drafting unless a qualified reviewer adapts the documents for other jurisdictions or additional sector-specific obligations. <br>
