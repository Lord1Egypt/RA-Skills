## Description: <br>
Helps Chinese learners prepare for CET-4 and CET-6 with exam-style writing, translation, reading, listening, feedback, and study planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liuxiangjian-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/liuxiangjian-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students preparing for China's CET-4 or CET-6 use this skill to choose a level and section, complete exam-style practice, receive diagnostic feedback, and build a targeted study plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat simulated CET practice as an official exam source or as a guarantee of future test content. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as an educational tutor and verify official exam requirements and schedules through authoritative CET sources. <br>
Risk: Personalized study planning can lead users to share more personal information than needed. <br>
Mitigation: Share only study-relevant details such as target level, weak section, available study time, and exam preparation window. <br>
