# Xiaohongshu Writing Templates

> Reference: proven note structures used by top Xiaohongshu creators.
> Each template follows a specific psychological arc to maximize engagement.

---

## Template 1: 痛点 → 发现 → 惊喜 (Pain → Discovery → Delight)

Best for: beauty, skincare, lifestyle products

```
[Pain expression] 😭 + [situation description]
BUT THEN I discovered [product]!
[Specific sensory detail: texture/smell/feel]
[Quantifiable result: time × effect]
[Skeptic-proof detail: skin type, allergies]
[CTA: try it, tag me, comment your experience]
```

**Example arc:** 熬夜脸暗沉 → 用了玫瑰水 → 拍两遍就润 → 一周后亮了 → 敏感肌可用 → 快来试试

---

## Template 2: 反差对比 (Before/After Contrast)

Best for: weight loss, skin transformation, room makeover

```
Before: [Old state] 😩
After:  [New state] 🤩
Key change: [1-2 things that made the difference]
Details: [honest timeline, struggles, what actually worked]
Verdict: [Honest opinion — would/wouldn't repurchase]
```

---

## Template 3: 探店攻略 (Venue Review Guide)

Best for: food, travel, cafés, hotels

```
📍 [Location / landmark]
💰 [Price range]
⭐ [Rating / why it's worth it]
Must-order: [1-3 dishes or highlights]
⚠️ Pitfall alert: [what to avoid]
🍽️ [Overall verdict + who should go]
```

## Template 4: 清单/合集 (Curated List)

Best for: product roundups, gift guides, shopping hauls

```

2️⃣ [Item B] — [1-line why, price] ✅
```

## Punctuation Patterns

- **Exclamation marks**: Use 2-3 per body (‼️ for emphasis is popular on XHS)
- **Ellipsis**: 2-3 dots for suspense ("还没完…") — not 6 dots
- **Line breaks**: Every 20-40 Chinese chars for mobile readability
- **Spacing**: Single space after emoji; no space before punctuation
- **Highlight markers**: **bold** or 【】 for key benefits
