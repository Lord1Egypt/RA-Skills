## Description: <br>
Give AI agents creative judgment and design taste for creating, reviewing, improving, and quality-gating visual content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rosalie-gill](https://clawhub.ai/user/rosalie-gill) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents that create visual content, merch, social posts, ads, product photography, or brand materials use this skill to study references, compare drafts, generate more intentionally, and evaluate creative quality before publishing. It also helps teams set up brand guardrails, reference libraries, style profiles, and pre-publish review workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Strict creative gates may be treated as mandatory publishing blockers without an explicit release policy. <br>
Mitigation: Decide before live publishing whether each gate is mandatory or advisory, and document that policy in the workflow. <br>
Risk: Study logs, reference libraries, and style profiles can preserve confidential brand material for later reuse. <br>
Mitigation: Keep those workspace files free of confidential material unless the team intends and approves reuse by the agent. <br>
