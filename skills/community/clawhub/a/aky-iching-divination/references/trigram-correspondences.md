# Trigram Correspondences (八卦万物类象)

The eight trigrams (八卦) are the building blocks of both Yijing and Plum Blossom divination.

---

## 1. Trigram Basic Properties

| Trigram | Symbol | Nature | Element | Direction | Family | Body | Season |
|:-------:|:------:|:------:|:-------:|:---------:|:------:|:----:|:------:|
| 乾 (Qián) | ☰ | Heaven | Metal | Northwest | Father | Head | Late autumn |
| 坤 (Kūn) | ☷ | Earth | Earth | Southwest | Mother | Abdomen | Late summer |
| 震 (Zhèn) | ☳ | Thunder | Wood | East | Eldest son | Feet | Spring |
| 巽 (Xùn) | ☴ | Wind | Wood | Southeast | Eldest daughter | Thighs | Early summer |
| 坎 (Kǎn) | ☵ | Water | Water | North | Middle son | Ears | Winter |
| 离 (Lí) | ☲ | Fire | Fire | South | Middle daughter | Eyes | Summer |
| 艮 (Gèn) | ☶ | Mountain | Earth | Northeast | Youngest son | Hands | Late winter |
| 兑 (Duì) | ☱ | Lake | Metal | West | Youngest daughter | Mouth | Autumn |

## 3. Five Element Interactions (五行生克)

| Relationship | Meaning | Application in Divination |
|:-----------:|---------|--------------------------|
| **相生** | Generating cycle | Wood→Fire→Earth→Metal→Water→Wood |
| **相克** | Controlling cycle | Wood→Earth→Water→Fire→Metal→Wood |
| **生我** | Mother-son (give life) | The element that generates me = support |
| **我生** | Son-mother (receive life) | The element I generate = expenditure |
| **克我** | Restraining other | The element that controls me = obstacle |
| **我克** | Being restrained | The element I control = manageable thing |

**In Plum Blossom divination:**
- 体 (Body/Subject) = the lower trigram = the person asking
- 用 (Function/Object) = the upper trigram = the matter/question
- 体生用 = subject generates object → subject puts in effort (耗)
- 用生体 = object generates subject → favorable outcome (吉)
- 体克用 = subject controls object → achievable but effort (可成)
- 用克体 = object controls subject → unfavorable (凶)
- 体用比和 = same element → harmony, ease (大吉)
