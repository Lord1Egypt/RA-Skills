## Description: <br>
cloud-architect helps agents design cloud architectures across AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure, including cost optimization, security, disaster recovery, migration planning, and infrastructure-as-code examples. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mtsatryan](https://clawhub.ai/user/mtsatryan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
For agents assisting with cloud architecture planning, technology selection, cost and resilience tradeoffs, security considerations, migration strategies, and draft Terraform or CloudFormation templates for AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure environments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated Terraform, CloudFormation, or configuration examples may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific production environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review, test, and adapt generated infrastructure artifacts against account-specific requirements before applying them. <br>
Risk: Cost estimates and security recommendations may be generic or miss organization-specific constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations with cloud provider tooling, current pricing, security teams, and applicable compliance requirements. <br>
