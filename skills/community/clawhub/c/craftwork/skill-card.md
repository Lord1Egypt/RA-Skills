## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may guide agents toward commits, pushes, merges, or subagent dispatch in real repositories. <br>
Mitigation: Decide allowed agent actions before use, keep branch protection enabled, and require human diff review before merge. <br>
Risk: Workflow guidance can still produce incorrect plans, reviews, or fixes if applied without project-specific verification. <br>
Mitigation: Require fresh verification commands, tests, and review evidence before accepting completion claims. <br>
