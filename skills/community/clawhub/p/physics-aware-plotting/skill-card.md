## Description: <br>
Physics-aware scientific plotting for research repositories. Use before making or updating Matplotlib figures to identify axis meanings, the comparison the figure supports, physically meaningful axis ranges, appropriate linear/log scaling, and publication-style visual choices inspired by ApJ/AAS figures. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and data scientists use this skill before creating or updating Matplotlib figures so plots communicate the intended physical comparison with appropriate units, axis ranges, scaling, and publication-ready styling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automated cleanup of slide assets can move or remove a figure variant that is still needed. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm which slide or manuscript figure is current before accepting cleanup, and review generated figure code and outputs before relying on them. <br>
