## Description: <br>
Researches local questions, groups them into clusters, and drafts launch-ready FAQ copy for an executive recruiting firm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, recruiting firm operators, and content teams use this skill to plan local SEO FAQ clusters for executive recruiting services and draft review-ready FAQ copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated HR-adjacent FAQ copy may be inaccurate, biased, or sensitive in an employment context. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated copy for accuracy, bias, and employment-law sensitivity before publishing or using it with clients or candidates. <br>
Risk: Prompts may expose confidential client or candidate information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential client, candidate, or search data into prompts. <br>
