## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a civil engineer, QSD, QSP, or erosion-control designer needs to convert a construction project's site data, BMP selections, and receiving-water context into a DRAFT Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan (SWPPP) aligned to the EPA 2022 CGP or a named state CGP, producing a BMP matrix, inspection schedule, and open-questions list. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Civil engineers, QSDs, QSPs, erosion-control designers, environmental consultants, and contractors use this skill to draft SWPPP narratives, BMP matrices, inspection schedules, signatory blocks, recordkeeping frameworks, and open-question lists for qualified professional review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process project-specific site, operator, parcel, coordinate, and contact details. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with information the user is comfortable sharing with the agent, and avoid unnecessary personal or sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Draft SWPPP content can be wrong if the governing permit, project facts, receiving-water status, or BMP selections are incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the governing permit, source every project fact, and have a qualified professional review, sign, seal, and submit any required documents. <br>
