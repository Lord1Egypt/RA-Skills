## Description: <br>
Social Media Scheduler helps users plan, draft, rewrite, schedule, and organize social media content while keeping generated drafts and planning data local. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mkpareek0315](https://clawhub.ai/user/mkpareek0315) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to generate social media captions, hashtags, post ideas, content calendars, drafts, bios, and platform-specific content strategies for manual posting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may create or update local planning and draft files under ~/.openclaw/social-media-scheduler. <br>
Mitigation: Review saved drafts and delete ~/.openclaw/social-media-scheduler to reset stored local data. <br>
Risk: Generated social media content, hashtags, trends, or performance guidance may be inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable for a brand. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated content before publishing it manually on any social platform. <br>
