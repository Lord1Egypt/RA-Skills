## Description: <br>
Turn long documents, reports, proposals, and email threads into decision-ready memos with key points, risks, open questions, and next steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ATWatcher](https://clawhub.ai/user/ATWatcher) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill to turn long documents, proposals, contracts, reports, and email threads into practical decision memos, comparison memos, risk briefs, executive summaries, and next-step recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce an overconfident recommendation when source material is incomplete or ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: It instructs the agent to distinguish facts from interpretation, note uncertainty, include open questions, and state a confidence level based on source completeness. <br>
Risk: Contract, policy, legal, medical, or financial material could be summarized in a way that sounds more authoritative than the source supports. <br>
Mitigation: It instructs the agent to avoid legal, medical, or financial certainty beyond the source and to flag terms or issues that may need expert review. <br>
