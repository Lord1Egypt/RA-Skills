## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide publishing workflows and may affect social accounts if used with connected scheduling or publishing tools. <br>
Mitigation: Review every post preview, destination account, and publishing time before approving publication. <br>
Risk: The skill includes large-scale scraping guidance that could collect private, protected, or unauthorized content. <br>
Mitigation: Limit collection to public or explicitly authorized sources and confirm consent, platform terms, and scope before scraping. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a sensitive SKILLBOSS_API_KEY that may grant access to account or scraper functions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the minimum-privilege key available, keep it out of prompts and shared logs, and provide it only after understanding what services it can access. <br>
