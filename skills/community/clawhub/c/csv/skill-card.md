## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CSV fields opened in spreadsheets can trigger formula injection when values begin with formula characters. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated CSV before sharing and prefix risky spreadsheet values as described by the skill guidance. <br>
