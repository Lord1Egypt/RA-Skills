## Use Case: <br>
External users and ecommerce operators use this skill to find, compare, and shortlist suppliers, manufacturers, service providers, fulfillment partners, and other sourcing options before outreach. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Vendor shortlist entries may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a user's constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as research leads and verify vendor legitimacy, certifications, pricing, capacity, and contract terms before outreach or purchase. <br>
