## Description: <br>
Create a polished explainer handout with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for a private K-12 school. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
School administrators and enrollment marketers use this skill to draft client education handouts for private K-12 schools, including explanatory copy, FAQs, visuals, and next steps for families or clients. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security scan reports that the skill may ask an agent to approve broad OpenClaw device permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Install only after reviewing the exact requested permissions, and do not run approval commands unless the operator and secrets-related permissions are acceptable. <br>
Risk: The security scan reports subagent creation behavior that is more powerful than the stated education-handout purpose requires. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when subagent creation is intentional, and monitor the agent's actions before allowing execution. <br>
Risk: Generated handouts and visuals may be used externally with families or clients. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for accuracy, readability, school-specific constraints, and publication readiness before sharing. <br>
