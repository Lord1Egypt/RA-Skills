## Description: <br>
Provides Twitter templates, deployment scripts, AI agent DM lists, and guides for promoting and monetizing OpenClaw skills efficiently. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[achilles2200ai-sys](https://clawhub.ai/user/achilles2200ai-sys) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external OpenClaw skill builders use this kit to prepare promotional X thread drafts, outreach lists, publishing commands, and bounty-hunting guidance for ClawHub skill releases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The included publishing script can publish a skill to ClawHub using the user's active account. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect scripts/publish-to-clawhub.sh, confirm the target skill directory and release arguments, and run it only when ready to publish. <br>
Risk: The outreach templates and handle list could be used for spammy or unsolicited bulk messages. <br>
Mitigation: Customize outreach, contact relevant recipients only, and follow platform rules and consent expectations. <br>
