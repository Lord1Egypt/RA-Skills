## Description: <br>
Uploads product documents, FAQs, or historical conversations and generates a structured customer-service knowledge base with intent classification, response optimization, sensitive-word checks, dialogue flows, and coverage analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[amaxclaw](https://clawhub.ai/user/amaxclaw) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer service teams and product managers use this skill to turn existing support material into a reviewable knowledge base. It helps organize intents, draft response templates, design multi-turn flows, and identify content gaps before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Input documents or chat logs may contain personal data, credentials, regulated records, or confidential customer details. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive information before use and only provide documents that are appropriate for the agent runtime. <br>
Risk: Generated knowledge-base entries, response templates, and dialogue flows may be inaccurate or unsuitable for production use. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review generated content before publishing and update the knowledge base based on user feedback. <br>
