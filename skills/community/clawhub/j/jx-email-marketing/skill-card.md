### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the guidance for real campaigns without confirming consent, unsubscribe handling, or legal requirements could create compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm consent records, unsubscribe behavior, data deletion processes, and applicable email marketing obligations before sending campaigns. <br>
Risk: Connecting broad email platform access while applying the guidance could expose subscriber data or enable unintended sends. <br>
Mitigation: Use narrowly scoped email platform permissions, review campaign settings, and test on limited segments before broad sends. <br>
