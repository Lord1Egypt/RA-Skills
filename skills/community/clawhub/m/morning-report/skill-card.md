## Description: <br>
Generates a daily SLG mobile game industry morning report covering AI and gaming news, competitor monitoring, player sentiment, SLG tests, Steam strategy releases, and market data, then formats it for DingTalk. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automatic DingTalk delivery may post the report to an unintended channel. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that DingTalk target 2735046220840628 is intended before scheduling and use a narrowly permissioned bot or account. <br>
Risk: Fresh web-sourced rankings, sentiment, and advertising benchmarks can be incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Require source links for report claims and mark unavailable data explicitly rather than filling gaps. <br>
