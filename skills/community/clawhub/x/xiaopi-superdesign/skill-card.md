## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated frontend guidance may recommend external CDN scripts, placeholder image services, or implementation choices that are unsuitable for a sensitive or production environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated dependencies, asset URLs, and code before use; pin approved packages and replace prototype CDN or placeholder assets with organization-approved alternatives. <br>
Risk: Design advice may be incomplete for a specific product's accessibility, brand, localization, or compliance requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Validate outputs against the target product's design system, accessibility standards, and legal or brand review requirements before release. <br>
