## Description: <br>
Conducts comprehensive autonomous research by finding information, evaluating sources, synthesizing findings, and creating detailed cited reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobisamaa](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobisamaa) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and external users use this skill to conduct deep research, evaluate source credibility, synthesize findings, and produce structured reports with citations and recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research prompts may be sent to external search or network-backed tools when web research is used. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential, regulated, or proprietary details unless they are intended for external search, and provide clear limits for topic, depth, sources, and output format. <br>
Risk: Research outputs can include incomplete, outdated, or misleading information from external sources. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources, cross-check important claims, and validate findings before relying on the report for decisions. <br>
