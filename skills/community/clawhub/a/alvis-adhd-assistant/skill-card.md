## Description: <br>
ADHD-friendly life management assistant that helps users plan, break down tasks, manage time, prioritize work, use body doubling, regulate motivation, and maintain routines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alvisdunlop](https://clawhub.ai/user/alvisdunlop) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals using agent assistance for ADHD-framed planning and executive-function support use this skill to create realistic daily plans, break work into small next actions, recover from overwhelm or time blindness, and set up check-ins, reminders, and reviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Memory-based personalization may capture health-adjacent details such as ADHD status, treatment context, therapy details, medication context, or crisis history. <br>
Mitigation: Keep saved memory limited to practical preferences and patterns unless the user explicitly understands how SkillBoss memory can be reviewed and deleted. <br>
Risk: Plans, reviews, or summaries may be written to files through host integrations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm target files and review generated content before allowing the agent to write plans, summaries, or task lists. <br>
Risk: Users may treat ADHD-framed productivity support as diagnosis, medical advice, therapy, or crisis support. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for organization and executive-function scaffolding only, and direct diagnosis, medication, therapy, or crisis needs to appropriate professionals or regional crisis resources. <br>
