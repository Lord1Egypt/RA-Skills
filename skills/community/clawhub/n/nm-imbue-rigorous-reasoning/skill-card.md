## Description: <br>
Applies anti-sycophancy checklist to override agreement bias. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[athola](https://clawhub.ai/user/athola) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and agent operators use this skill to make an agent evaluate contested claims, interpersonal conflicts, corrections, and analytical debates through explicit evidence and validity checks before agreeing, challenging, or revising conclusions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation triggers such as "analysis" may make the direct reasoning style appear in more conversations than expected. <br>
Mitigation: Scope activation to conversations where evidence-focused reasoning is desired, and override or disable the skill when a warmer support posture is required. <br>
Risk: The skill's direct, anti-sycophancy posture can be inappropriate in sensitive personal, crisis, medical, legal, or abuse-related situations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep applicable safety, empathy, and domain-specific policies primary, and avoid using the skill to replace professional, emergency, legal, or medical guidance. <br>
