## Templates

### 1. The "Observational" Opener

**Best for:** First contact, showing you did research

```
Hi {name},

I was looking at {company}'s recent {project/initiative} and noticed {specific observation}.

That made me think — are you currently {related challenge}?

If yes, I have a {specific result} that might help.

Worth a 5-minute conversation?

{signature}
```

**Example:**
```
Hi Sarah,

I was looking at Acme Corp's recent expansion into APAC and noticed your team grew from 12 to 34 people in 6 months.

That made me think — are you currently onboarding faster than your systems can handle?

If yes, we just reduced onboarding ramp time from 90 to 14 days for a similar-stage company.

Worth a 5-minute conversation?

— Anton, CertainLogic
```

---

### 2. The "Value First" Opener

**Best for:** Busy executives who screen aggressively

```
{name} — {specific result} without {common objection}.

That's what we just did for {similar company}.

Want the 2-minute breakdown?

{signature}
```

**Example:**
```
James — 3x faster agent responses without hiring more engineers.

That's what we just did for TechCorp.

Want the 2-minute breakdown?

— Anton, CertainLogic
```

---

### 3. The "Question" Opener

**Best for:** Starting conversations, not pushing demos

```
Hi {name},

Quick question: How does {company} currently handle {specific pain point}?

I ask because we built {solution} for {similar company} and they saw {specific result}.

Not pitching — genuinely curious about your approach.

{signature}
```

**Example:**
```
Hi Marcus,

Quick question: How does DataFlow currently handle agent hallucinations in production?

I ask because we built a verification layer for StreamCo and they cut false-positives by 92%.

Not pitching — genuinely curious about your approach.

— Anton, CertainLogic
```

---

### 4. The "Follow-Up" (Ghosted After 1st Email)

**Best for:** Day 4-7 after no response

```
Hi {name},

Wanted to float this to the top of your inbox.

Last week I mentioned {specific result} for {similar company}.

If {pain point} isn't a priority right now, totally understand.

If it is, here's a 90-second video explaining how it works: {link}

{signature}
```

---

### 5. The "Break-Up" (Final Email)

**Best for:** Day 14, last attempt

```
Hi {name},

I haven't heard back, so I'm going to assume {pain point} isn't a priority for {company} right now.

Fair enough — timing is everything.

I'll check back in a few months. If things change, feel free to reach out.

{signature}

P.S. — Here's a free resource on {related topic} that helped 3 companies similar to yours: {link}
```
