Mitigation: Check official HarmonyOS documentation before relying on guidance for important API, security-sensitive, or release-blocking decisions. <br>
Risk: The reference library is large, and broad reads can produce irrelevant or incomplete context. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented lookup path through KITS.md, TASK_MAP.md, and INDEX.md before reading specific reference material. <br>
