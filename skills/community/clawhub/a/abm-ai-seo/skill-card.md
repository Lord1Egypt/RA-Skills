## Description: <br>
Helps users optimize content for AI search engines, LLM citations, and AI-generated answers across platforms such as Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, content, SEO, and growth teams use this skill to audit and improve content so AI search systems can discover, extract, and cite it. It produces practical guidance for content structure, authority signals, schema strategy, AI crawler access, platform-specific optimization, and monitoring. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read local product-marketing context files and use them to tailor recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review local marketing context before use and avoid including sensitive positioning, customer, or launch information that should not influence generated guidance. <br>
Risk: Robots.txt recommendations for AI crawlers can affect whether public content is crawled, reused, trained on, or cited by AI systems. <br>
Mitigation: Treat AI crawler access as a business and policy decision, and confirm crawler allow or block rules with the site owner before applying them. <br>
Risk: AI search platform behavior and citation factors can change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current platform results, analytics, and crawler behavior before making high-impact SEO changes. <br>
