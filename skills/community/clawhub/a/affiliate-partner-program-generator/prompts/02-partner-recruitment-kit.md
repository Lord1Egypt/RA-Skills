# Prompt 2: Partner Recruitment Kit

## Purpose
Generate outreach materials to recruit affiliates. Most programs stall at 5–10 partners because founders don't have compelling recruitment content. This prompt outputs everything needed to go from 0 to 50 active affiliates.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a partner marketing manager writing recruitment materials for an affiliate program. Generate a complete partner recruitment kit for the following program.

**Program Details:**
- Company name: [NAME]
- Product/service: [DESCRIBE]
- Commission offer: [e.g., 30% recurring | $50 flat per conversion]
- Cookie window: [e.g., 30 days]
- Payout schedule: [e.g., monthly, net-30]
- Minimum payout: [e.g., $50]
- Target affiliate types: [e.g., freelance designers, Shopify app reviewers, SaaS bloggers]
- Program platform/portal URL (if live): [URL or "TBD"]
- One compelling proof point: [e.g., "top affiliates earn $2,000/month" or "82% trial-to-paid conversion rate"]

**Generate the following:**

## 1. Ideal Partner Profile (IPP)
List 5 audience segments who should promote this product, with:
| Segment | Why they convert | Where to find them | Estimated audience size |
|---|---|---|---|
| [e.g., SaaS review bloggers] | [their audience is evaluating tools] | [G2, Capterra, Product Hunt communities] | [10K–100K readers] |

## 2. Cold Outreach Email — Blogger/Influencer Angle
Write a cold pitch email for bloggers, YouTubers, or content creators who already cover this topic.
- Subject line (3 variants)
- Email body (150–200 words): lead with their audience benefit, not your commission
- Call to action: link to application form or reply to this email

## 3. Cold Outreach Email — Industry Peer / Complementary Service Angle
Write a cold pitch for a complementary service provider (e.g., pitching a bookkeeper on promoting an invoicing tool).
- Subject line (3 variants)
- Email body (150–200 words): position as a value-add for their clients, not just a commission opportunity
- Call to action: reply or schedule a 15-min call

## 4. Cold Outreach Email — Complementary SaaS / Tool Angle
Write a cold pitch for a non-competing SaaS or tool that serves the same ICP.
- Subject line (3 variants)
- Email body (150–200 words): frame as co-marketing / cross-referral, not just affiliate recruitment
- Call to action: partner intro call

## 5. LinkedIn DM Sequence (3 messages)
Write 3 LinkedIn messages for recruiting partners:
- Message 1 (connection request note, max 300 chars): establish context
- Message 2 (follow-up 3 days after connection): deliver value before the ask
- Message 3 (soft pitch 4 days after message 2): introduce the program without being pushy

## 6. Partner Program One-Pager
Write a 1-page "Why Partner With Us" document with:
- Headline: [why partners should care]
- 3 reasons to partner (bullet points with proof)
- Commission structure summary (clear and scannable)
- Who makes the best partners (ideal partner description)
- "Here's what you'll get" — list of portal assets, support, and resources
- Social proof (if available): [e.g., "Our top affiliate earns $2,200/month"]
- CTA: Apply at [URL] or reply to this email

## 7. Partner Application Form Questions
Write 10 questions for a partner application form that qualify applicants without being off-putting:
- Audience size / reach (soft — don't require a number)
- Content channels (blog, YouTube, LinkedIn, podcast, email list, etc.)
- How they plan to promote (editorial, paid, reviews, tutorials)
- Whether they're already using the product or a competitor
- Any existing affiliate or referral partnerships
- Agreement to program terms

## 8. Partner Welcome Email (for approved applicants)
Write the welcome email sent after approval:
- Congratulate them on acceptance
- Share their affiliate link, portal login, and dashboard overview
- List the top 3 assets in the toolkit (link to the 3 most useful)
- Share the single most important statistic about your product (conversion rate, trial rate, or bestseller signal)
- Set expectations: first commission payout timeline, support contact
- One-sentence CTA: "Your first step is..."
```

---

## Example Input

- Company: NudgePay
- Product: B2B invoicing SaaS ($59/month)
- Commission: 30% recurring commission (up to month 12 per referred customer)
- Cookie window: 60 days
- Payout: Monthly, net-30, minimum $50
- Target affiliates: freelance accountants, bookkeepers, Shopify app review YouTubers, SaaS founders with newsletters
- Portal: Rewardful (partner.nudgepay.com)
- Proof point: "Average referred customer stays 19 months — top affiliates earn $1,100+/month"

---

## Tips for Best Results

- For the one-pager, lead with EPC (earnings per click) or average partner earnings if you have data — this is the #1 thing potential partners want to know
- For B2B products, the "complementary service" angle (Prompt 3) often converts better than pure affiliate outreach — it's a trusted peer recommending, not a sponsored post
- Don't mention your commission in the LinkedIn connection request note — lead with something relevant to them first
