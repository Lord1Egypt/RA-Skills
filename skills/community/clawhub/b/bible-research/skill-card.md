## Description: <br>
Bible Research helps agents answer Bible study questions from a disclosed Jehovah's Witness perspective using scripture context, Watchtower/JW references, and study-ready response formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Marvel1click](https://clawhub.ai/user/Marvel1click) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to research Bible topics, explain scriptures in context, prepare study or ministry answers, and compare non-JW views with a Jehovah's Witness conclusion when requested. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the skill's answers for neutral or interfaith Bible research. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose the Jehovah's Witness perspective and ask explicitly for side-by-side translation or viewpoint comparisons when needed. <br>
Risk: Scripture, publication, title, date, or link errors could mislead religious study. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the artifact guardrails: do not invent sources, distinguish Bible text from Watchtower explanation and plain reasoning, and state when evidence is limited. <br>
Risk: Questions requiring web lookup may expose private personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including private personal details in prompts, especially when asking for searches of external Bible or publication resources. <br>
