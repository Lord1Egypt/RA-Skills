## Description: <br>
Travel Guide is a bilingual travel-planning skill that helps users choose destinations, plan itineraries, compare travel styles, and avoid common travel pitfalls for major Chinese holidays, Western holidays, Chinese destinations, and international destinations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and agent users use this skill to get personalized travel recommendations, destination overviews, itinerary suggestions, transportation and accommodation guidance, food and shopping tips, and pitfall-avoidance advice. It is intended for conversational travel planning across budget, luxury, family, solo, food, photography, and holiday travel scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may ask about travel dates, budget, party details, and preferences while planning a trip. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive identifiers such as passport numbers, government ID numbers, payment details, or exact private addresses in the conversation. <br>
Risk: Travel facts such as visa rules, safety advisories, weather, transport schedules, prices, and opening hours can change quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify time-sensitive details with authoritative sources before booking or traveling. <br>
Risk: Broad travel routing keywords may activate the skill for general travel questions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the resulting recommendations for relevance and ask the agent to narrow the destination, date range, budget, or travel style when needed. <br>
