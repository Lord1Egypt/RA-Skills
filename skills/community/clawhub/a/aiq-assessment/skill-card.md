## Description: <br>
AIQ Assessment evaluates individuals or teams against a three-layer, 18-ability AI collaboration framework and produces structured scores, key findings, scenario-weighted analysis, and improvement suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ravenlionx](https://clawhub.ai/user/ravenlionx) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals, teams, recruiters, and organization leaders use this skill to assess AI collaboration effectiveness through self-assessment, peer assessment, or quick scans. It is suited for self-diagnosis, hiring support, team development, and AI maturity audits when scores are treated as advisory evidence rather than sole decision criteria. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The assessment may collect sensitive personal, workplace, chat, or hiring evaluation details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to omit secrets, private chats, and unnecessary HR details; only collect information needed for the intended assessment. <br>
Risk: AIQ scores can be overused as a basis for hiring, promotion, or personnel decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present scores as advisory evidence and require human review with additional evaluation signals before personnel decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may save or attach a local report containing assessment information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the filename and storage location before writing or attaching the report. <br>
