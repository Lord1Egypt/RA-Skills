## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a tax or transfer-pricing professional wants to draft or review an OECD TPG benchmarking memo for one intercompany transaction, covering FAR analysis, tested-party and method selection, comparables search logs, adjustments, interquartile-range conclusions, and reviewer sign-off boundaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Transfer-pricing professionals use this skill to draft or review an OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines-aligned benchmarking memo for a single intercompany transaction. It structures the analysis for BEPS Action 13 Local File work while preserving professional review and sign-off boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may handle sensitive transfer-pricing, financial, agreement, and comparables data. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in a workspace appropriate for confidential tax work and avoid sending confidential transaction or comparables data to external tools or web searches. <br>
Risk: Draft memo content could be mistaken for legal or tax advice or relied on without adequate review. <br>
Mitigation: Require qualified professional review and sign-off before taking a return position, filing documentation, or using the memo in APA, MAP, or audit contexts. <br>
