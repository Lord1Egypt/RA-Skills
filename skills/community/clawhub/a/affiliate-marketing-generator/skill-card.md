## Description: <br>
Generates SEO-optimized affiliate reviews, comparison posts, email sequences, and social scripts to promote products and monetize content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, affiliate marketers, newsletter operators, and freelance marketers use this skill to draft product review posts, comparison articles, email promotions, and short-form social scripts with affiliate disclosures. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated affiliate copy may create misleading urgency or unsupported proof points. <br>
Mitigation: Use real deadlines, real prices, substantiated claims, and clear affiliate disclosures before publication. <br>
Risk: Comparison content may bias recommendations toward higher-commission products. <br>
Mitigation: Order and recommend products based on reader fit, objective criteria, and documented limitations rather than commission size. <br>
