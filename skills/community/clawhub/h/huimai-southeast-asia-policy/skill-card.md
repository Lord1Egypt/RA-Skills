This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External cross-border sellers, trade operators, and market-entry teams use the skill to get policy-oriented guidance for Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, the Philippines, and Singapore. The skill supports tariff, certification, licensing, FTA origin-rule, and multi-country comparison questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global use; policy coverage focuses on Southeast Asia. <br>
