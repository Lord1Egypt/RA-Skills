## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and social media operators use this skill to ideate, generate, adapt, upload, and schedule posts across platforms connected through Postiz. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can publish or schedule content to real connected social media accounts through Postiz. <br>
Mitigation: Require manual review and approval before publishing, and prefer draft-only workflows until content and target platforms are confirmed. <br>
Risk: A Postiz API key can upload media, list integrations, and create or schedule posts. <br>
Mitigation: Use the least-privilege Postiz key available, keep it out of logs and chat history, and rotate it if exposure is suspected. <br>
