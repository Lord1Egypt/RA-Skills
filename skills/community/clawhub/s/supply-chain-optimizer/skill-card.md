## Description: <br>
Supply Chain Optimizer helps agents produce demand forecasts, inventory plans, route optimization outputs, supplier evaluations, and what-if logistics scenario analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Supply chain, logistics, procurement, and operations teams use this skill to analyze historical sales, inventory, logistics network, and supplier data, then generate planning recommendations for inventory, replenishment, delivery routes, supplier selection, and disruption scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sales, inventory, logistics, supplier, pricing, or customer-linked datasets may contain sensitive business or personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum fields needed, redact personal data and confidential supplier terms where possible, and review recommendations before applying them operationally. <br>
