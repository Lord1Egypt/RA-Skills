### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: An OAuth example passes a login token through a URL, which can expose credentials through browser history, logs, referrers, or analytics. <br>
Mitigation: Use server-side session cookies or an authorization-code/PKCE-style exchange, and review generated authentication code before deployment. <br>
