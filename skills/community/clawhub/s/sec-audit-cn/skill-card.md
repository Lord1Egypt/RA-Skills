## Description: <br>
Sec Audit Cn helps agents review code and configuration for application security issues in China and similar regions, including OWASP Top 10, authentication, secrets, CORS/CSP, input validation, dependencies, and prioritized remediation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and security reviewers use this skill to audit web/API backends, mobile backend services, mini-program servers, and business logic involving personal data or payment callbacks. It produces prioritized findings and practical remediation guidance aligned with OWASP categories and regional compliance considerations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill has broad security-related activation keywords and may appear during general security conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it deliberately for security-review tasks and avoid pasting real secrets unless they are intentionally being reviewed in the local agent context. <br>
Risk: Compliance notes are development guidance and not legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Consult legal or compliance reviewers for decisions involving cybersecurity, data security, personal information protection, or other regulatory obligations. <br>
