## Description: <br>
Helps agents design or improve web, app, extension, landing-page, media, and policy-page UIs with a DESIGN.md-backed design system, responsive visual checks, accessibility checks, conversion-oriented copy, and optional generated raster imagery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and design-focused agents use this skill to create or refine DESIGN.md design systems, public product pages, extension UIs, marketing landings, media assets, and screenshot-verified UI changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill has a broad design scope and may activate for general UI or design-related requests. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when design or UI help is intended, confirm the requested scope, and review generated files before applying them to an existing project. <br>
Risk: Generated UI changes, copy, or public-page guidance could introduce inaccurate claims, accessibility issues, broken links, or responsive layout regressions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify rendered screenshots across mobile, tablet, and desktop widths, check accessibility and links, and ensure visible claims match real product behavior before deployment. <br>
Risk: Generated imagery or design explorations could be mistaken for real product evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Use real screenshots for product evidence and treat generated imagery as illustrative support or design direction only. <br>
