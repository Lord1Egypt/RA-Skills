## Why This Kit Is Different

Every other appliance repair marketing template ignores the landmines:

- **OEM vs. aftermarket parts** — FTC Act Section 5 requires disclosure. Call a non-OEM part "genuine" and you're exposed. This kit enforces the right language automatically.
- **Authorized service claims** — "GE Authorized" without authorization is trademark misuse. Kit enforces "authorized" vs. "specializes in" distinction.
- **EPA 608 refrigerant** — Fridge and freezer marketing that implies refrigerant handling without 608 certification exposes you to liability. Kit flags this in every relevant prompt.
- **Manufacturer warranty** — Non-authorized repairs can void warranty. Kit includes opt-in disclosure language that builds trust instead of hiding the issue.
