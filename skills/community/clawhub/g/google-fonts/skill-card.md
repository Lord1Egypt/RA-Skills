## Use Case: <br>
Developers and designers use this skill to choose Google Fonts, load only the required weights and subsets, and apply performance-conscious fallback and self-hosting practices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Loading Google Fonts from Google servers can expose visitor IP addresses or create privacy concerns on public or privacy-sensitive websites. <br>
Mitigation: Review privacy requirements before deployment and self-host fonts when Google-hosted font requests are not acceptable. <br>
Risk: Following font-loading guidance without review could introduce unnecessary font weights or subsets that slow page loads. <br>
Mitigation: Load only the weights and character subsets used by the site, and confirm font-display and fallback stacks during implementation review. <br>
