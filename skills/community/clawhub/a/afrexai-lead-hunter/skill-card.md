## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Autonomous prospecting can collect or enrich business contact data from unapproved sources or without a documented legal basis. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved prospecting sources, document the legal basis for processing, apply retention and deletion rules, and keep audit logs for collected lead data. <br>
Risk: Autonomous outreach and CRM updates can send unwanted messages, ignore suppression lists, or change records without review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before outbound messages or CRM writes, enforce unsubscribe and suppression lists, set strict sending limits, and define stop controls before enabling scheduled routines. <br>
