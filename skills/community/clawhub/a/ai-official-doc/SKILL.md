# AI Official Documents

Government forms assistance, official letter writing, administrative procedure guidance, and document templates for bureaucratic processes.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Government Forms
- Tax forms: W-2, W-4, 1040, Schedule C, 1099 explanations
- Identity documents: passport application, driver's license renewal
- Immigration: visa applications, green card, citizenship forms
- Social Security, Medicare/Medicaid enrollment

### 2. Official Correspondence
- Formal letter structure: header, salutation, body, closing
- Complaint letters to government agencies, companies
- Request letters: FOIA, records, information, reconsideration
- Appeal letters: benefits denial, parking tickets, insurance claims
- Notification letters: address change, name change, power of attorney

### 3. Business Documentation
- Business registration: LLC, corporation, DBA filing
- Permits and licenses by industry and location
- Tax ID (EIN) application, business bank account setup
- Contracts: service agreements, NDAs, partnership agreements

### 4. Personal Records
- Vital records: birth certificate, marriage license, death certificate
- Name change process, gender marker updates
- Property records: deed, title, mortgage documents
- Medical records requests, advance directives

### 5. Administrative Navigation
- Agency identification for specific needs
- Form-filling guidance: avoiding common rejection reasons
- Document authentication: notarization, apostille, certification
- Record-keeping: what to keep, how long, digital backups
