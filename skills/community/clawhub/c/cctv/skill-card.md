## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill could be used beyond its stated boundaries for downloads, bulk scraping, API reverse engineering, or bypassing platform controls. <br>
Mitigation: Keep usage to lightweight summaries of public CCTV pages and follow the server security guidance. <br>
Risk: Dynamic pages and human verification checks can make automated extraction incomplete or unreliable. <br>
Mitigation: Open pages manually when needed, trigger parsing after visible content loads, and review summaries before acting on them. <br>
