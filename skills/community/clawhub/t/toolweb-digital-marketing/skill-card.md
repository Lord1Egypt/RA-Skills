## Description: <br>
Professional career roadmap platform that generates personalized digital marketing learning paths based on user experience, skills, and goals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, career coaches, education platforms, HR teams, and agent developers use this skill to request personalized digital marketing career roadmaps, specialization options, and learning paths from an API. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Career history, skills, goals, session IDs, timestamps, and user IDs may be personal data when sent to the API provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending sensitive personal or employment information until the provider, endpoint, TLS configuration, and privacy or retention terms have been reviewed. <br>
Risk: Generated roadmaps and certification recommendations may not match a user's current job market, employer expectations, or training constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review API output before acting on it and adapt recommendations to the user's verified goals, context, and constraints. <br>
