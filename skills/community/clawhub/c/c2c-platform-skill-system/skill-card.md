## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and advisors for C2C local-services marketplaces use this skill to route platform operations work into specialized capability clusters and reusable pipelines. It supports planning and producing operational reports, policies, process designs, analytics guidance, growth plans, compliance workflows, and infrastructure recommendations. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Operational data, user data, financial data, or cloud/payment/SMS secrets could be exposed when using the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymized or minimized exports, never paste live secrets into the agent, and apply access control, retention, audit logging, and deletion rules before using real data. <br>
Risk: Generated recommendations may affect user accounts, payouts, withdrawals, freezes, bans, or other fund-related decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Require authorized human approval before executing account-impacting or fund-impacting decisions. <br>
Risk: Compliance, privacy, and marketplace governance outputs may be incomplete for a specific jurisdiction or platform model. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as operational guidance and have qualified reviewers confirm consent, opt-out, regulatory, and policy requirements before deployment. <br>
