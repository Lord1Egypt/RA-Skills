## Description: <br>
Plan United States trips with region-specific routing, verified entry rules, transport logistics, and practical tourist safety. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to plan U.S. tourism routes, entry/document readiness, transportation choices, costs, seasonal risks, and practical safety details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trip dates, budget, mobility needs, entry status, and other travel preferences may be retained in ~/united-states/memory.md. <br>
Mitigation: Review, edit, or delete ~/united-states/memory.md when those details should not remain on the local machine. <br>
