# Output Template

Deliver each policy as a standalone, publishable page using the structure below. Use plain second-person language, scannable headings, concrete numbers, an effective date, and a contact path.

## Returns & Refunds Policy

```
# Returns & Refunds

## Return window
You can return [eligible items] within [N] days of [delivery date].

## Condition requirements
Items must be [unused / unworn / unwashed / tags attached / original packaging].

## Who pays return shipping
- Defective or our error: we pay.
- Change of mind: [customer pays / flat $X deducted / free].

## How refunds are issued
Refunds go to the [original payment method] within [N–M business days] of us receiving and inspecting the return.

## Exchanges
[Process, if offered.]

## Exclusions (non-returnable)
[Final-sale, hygiene, custom, perishable — itemized.]

## How to start a return
[Channel] with [what to include]. We respond within [timeframe].

Effective date: [date]
```

## Shipping & Delivery Policy

```
# Shipping & Delivery

## Processing time
Orders are processed within [N business days].

## Shipping methods & estimates
[Carrier/service] — [estimated delivery window] — [cost or free threshold].

## Domestic vs international
[Regions served, customs/duties responsibility, restrictions.]

## Tracking
[How/when tracking is provided.]

## Lost, delayed, or damaged shipments
[What the customer should do and what you'll do.]

Effective date: [date]
```

## Privacy Policy

```
# Privacy Policy

## What we collect
[Provided data; automatically collected data via cookies/analytics.]

## Why we collect it
[Fulfillment, support, fraud prevention, marketing with consent.]

## Who we share it with
[Processors: payments, shipping, analytics, advertising. State if you do NOT sell data.]

## Your rights
[GDPR rights for EU/UK; CCPA/CPRA rights for California; how to exercise them.]

## Cookies
[Banner behavior; essential vs non-essential.]

## Data retention & security
[How long, how protected.]

## Contact
[Privacy contact + effective date.]
```

## Cross-policy consistency

- Same business name, contact channels, and effective date across all four.
- Numbers in Returns must match Shipping (e.g. processing time, carriers).
- Voice and formatting consistent across documents.
