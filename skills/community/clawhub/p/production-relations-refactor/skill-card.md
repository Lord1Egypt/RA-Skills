## Description: <br>
Production Relations Refactor helps agents apply a structured method for redesigning organizational production relationships into AI-assisted, single-person workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and organizational designers use this skill to analyze teams, enterprises, communities, platforms, or other collaborative structures and produce restructuring guidance. It guides users through relationship identification, justification analysis, compensation relationship removal, collaboration primitive design, validation, and execution-form selection. <br>
