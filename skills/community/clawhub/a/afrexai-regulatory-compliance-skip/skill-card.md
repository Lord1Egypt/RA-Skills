## Use Case: <br>
Founders, compliance leads, operations teams, and agents use this skill to structure regulatory compliance audits, identify applicable frameworks, score control gaps, and produce remediation roadmaps and board-ready summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat generated compliance conclusions as legal advice or as definitive regulatory interpretation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify framework applicability, deadlines, costs, and regulatory interpretations with qualified counsel or compliance professionals before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Users may include sensitive customer data, PHI, payment card data, secrets, privileged legal material, or employee records in audit prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use sanitized summaries and avoid entering raw sensitive records or secrets. <br>
Risk: Compliance checklists, cost benchmarks, and regulatory examples can become stale or may not match a specific organization. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as a manual checklist and report structure, then validate requirements against current applicable frameworks and organization-specific facts. <br>
