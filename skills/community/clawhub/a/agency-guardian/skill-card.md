## Description: <br>
Gentle reminders to stay human while using AI. Reflection, not restriction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aranej](https://clawhub.ai/user/aranej) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users install Agency Guardian to receive periodic self-reflection prompts and gentle boundary reminders while using an AI assistant, especially around health, finances, and relationships. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reflective prompts around health, finances, and relationships could be distracting or unwelcome in sensitive conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when occasional reflective prompts are desired, and disable boundary nudges if they are unhelpful. <br>
Risk: The skill provides reflective guidance rather than professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat its reminders as prompts for personal judgment and seek qualified professional support for health, financial, or relationship decisions when needed. <br>
