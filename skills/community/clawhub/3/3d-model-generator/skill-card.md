## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through using Meshy.ai to convert text prompts or reference images into textured 3D model files such as STL or OBJ. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rioo-maker](https://clawhub.ai/user/rioo-maker) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and 3D creators use this skill to run Meshy.ai text-to-3D or image-to-3D workflows, including prompt optimization, texture application, and export of STL or OBJ files for 3D printing, games, or visualization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, private reference images, or confidential designs may be uploaded to Meshy.ai during model generation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive inputs unless Meshy.ai privacy, billing, and workspace terms have been reviewed and approved for the intended use. <br>
