## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on crypto backtest results or live trading signals as financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational trading analysis, verify independently, and do not use the signals as the sole basis for trades. <br>
Risk: Telegram interactions may expose usernames, chat activity, or subscription interactions to Telegram and the bot operator. <br>
Mitigation: Use Telegram with privacy expectations appropriate for a third-party paid signal service and avoid sharing sensitive account or wallet information. <br>
Risk: The skill describes paid subscription payment over crypto rails, which can be difficult to reverse if the destination is wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the publisher handle, bot identity, subscription terms, and payment destination independently before sending funds. <br>
