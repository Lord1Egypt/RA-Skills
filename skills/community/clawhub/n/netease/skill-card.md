## Description: <br>
Netease Video helps agents summarize and analyze public Netease video and topic pages, including video performance, account timelines, and leaderboard distributions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CodeKungfu](https://clawhub.ai/user/CodeKungfu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to organize public Netease short-video and topic-page information into lightweight summaries, comparisons, and distribution statistics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automated or high-volume access to Netease pages could violate platform rules or trigger anti-automation controls. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use lightweight, prefer manual page access when needed, avoid batch scraping, and follow the platform's rules for automated access. <br>
Risk: Entering credentials or private data could expose information beyond the public pages this skill is intended to analyze. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with publicly available pages and avoid entering credentials, private account data, or confidential information. <br>
Risk: Downloads, private API calls, or interface reverse engineering would go beyond the documented scope of the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Do not download videos, call private interfaces, reverse engineer platform APIs, or attempt to bypass access controls. <br>
