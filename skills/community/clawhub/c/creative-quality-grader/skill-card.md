## Description: <br>
Score ad creatives before launch for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Google Ads, and Amazon Ads policy and quality fit. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operators, growth teams, and creative reviewers use this skill to score advertising creatives before launch, generate revision notes, and decide whether assets should launch, be reworked, or be rejected. It supports channel-aware review across Meta, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Google Ads, Amazon Ads, and related paid media workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Advertising launch, spend, and policy recommendations may be treated as final operational decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as planning support and verify platform policy, budget, billing, and tracking decisions before acting. <br>
Risk: Missing or uncertain campaign inputs can produce low-confidence creative scoring or channel guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the minimum required fields, separate observed facts from assumptions, mark low confidence explicitly, and provide a validation checklist. <br>
Risk: High-risk issues such as policy concerns, billing problems, or tracking breakage can affect campaign performance or compliance. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate those issues with a structured handoff payload and include rollback or stop-loss conditions when spend risk exists. <br>
