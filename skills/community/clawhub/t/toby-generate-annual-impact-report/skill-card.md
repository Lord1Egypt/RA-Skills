## Description: <br>
Create a visual impact report from program data for nonprofit, NGO, and fundraising reporting work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobeyrebecca](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobeyrebecca) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External nonprofit, NGO, and fundraising teams use this skill to turn program data into an annual impact report draft with narrative, presentation, and visual asset support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reports may include inaccurate program outcomes, financial details, or impact claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify metrics, source data, and claims with the nonprofit's responsible reviewers before publication. <br>
Risk: Report drafting may involve donor records, beneficiary personal data, or unpublished financial details. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data approved for the underlying AI services, and redact confidential or personal information when approval is unclear. <br>
