## Description: <br>
Generates standardized after-sales service SOPs for returns, complaints, escalation, service recovery, compensation, quality monitoring, and team training. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Customer support and operations teams use this skill to turn business policies, team structure, common support issues, service standards, and escalation rules into practical after-sales workflows and guidelines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated refund, compensation, escalation, or service-recovery procedures may conflict with company policy, local law, or approval limits. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated SOPs against internal policies, legal requirements, and financial authorization thresholds before adopting them. <br>
