## Description: <br>
Calculates and benchmarks true customer acquisition cost by channel and cohort, identifies savings opportunities, and builds data-driven CAC reduction plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, finance teams, growth leaders, and agents supporting them use this skill to calculate fully loaded CAC, benchmark channel and cohort performance, and produce prioritized CAC reduction plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CAC analysis may involve sensitive sales, marketing, revenue, payroll, and channel-performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only information appropriate for the normal agent environment and remove confidential details that are not needed for the analysis. <br>
Risk: Recommendations can be misleading when acquisition costs, customer counts, margins, or retention data are incomplete or not comparable to the benchmark tables. <br>
Mitigation: Review inputs, assumptions, and resulting recommendations before using the analysis for budget or channel decisions. <br>
