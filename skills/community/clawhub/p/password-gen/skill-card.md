## Description: <br>
Generates secure passwords and passphrases locally and analyzes password strength with configurable character sets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ouyangAbel](https://clawhub.ai/user/ouyangAbel) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and end users use this skill to generate local random passwords or memorable passphrases and to review password strength before storing credentials securely. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated passwords and analyzed passwords are displayed in plain text in the terminal. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill in a private terminal, avoid analyzing real existing passwords through command-line arguments, and store generated passwords in a password manager. <br>
