## Use Case: <br>
Developers, SREs, and platform engineers use this skill to design and implement observability practices, dashboards, alerting rules, instrumentation, log management, tracing, synthetic monitoring, and SLI/SLO workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Example monitoring configurations and snippets may expose sensitive telemetry, identifiers, URLs, traces, screenshots, or alert destinations if copied into production unchanged. <br>
Mitigation: Review and sanitize examples before use; rely on secret-managed low-privilege synthetic accounts, redact sensitive observability data, and route alerts only to approved internal destinations. <br>
