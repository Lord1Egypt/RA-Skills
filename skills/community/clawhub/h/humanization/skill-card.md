## Description: <br>
Check an essay's AI score on ZeroGPT and automatically rewrite it to bring the score below 70%, while preserving meaning and maintaining a professional, academic tone suitable for a learning journal. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[puppetjellyfish](https://clawhub.ai/user/puppetjellyfish) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to check essays with ZeroGPT and iteratively rewrite drafts to lower AI-detection scores while preserving meaning and academic tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Essay text is submitted to a third-party website for AI-detection scoring. <br>
Mitigation: Use only when the user is comfortable sharing the essay with ZeroGPT and the relevant institution or publisher permits that use. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to rewrite essays against AI-detection thresholds, which can create academic-integrity concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer ordinary writing support for clarity, grammar, citations, and structure when detector-targeted rewriting is not explicitly permitted. <br>
