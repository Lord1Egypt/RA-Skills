## Description: <br>
Turn messy weekly ecommerce performance data into an operator-ready report with KPI trends, winners, risks, and next-step recommendations. Use when teams need a concise weekly business review instead of raw spreadsheets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees and ecommerce operators use this skill to turn weekly sales, advertising, refund, and profit data into a concise business review with KPI changes, drivers, risks, and next-week actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded ecommerce performance data may contain confidential business information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only data needed for the weekly report and handle sales, advertising, refund, and profit data as confidential. <br>
Risk: KPI calculations or driver explanations may be wrong if source data is incomplete or inconsistent. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the reporting period, comparison basis, data scope, and key calculations before using the report for financial or operational decisions. <br>
Risk: The report is intended for operating review, not audit-grade financial validation. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output for weekly business discussion and action planning, and escalate audit or accounting decisions to appropriate financial review. <br>
