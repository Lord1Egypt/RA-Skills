## Description: <br>
Rewrites Chinese articles through iterative drafting and critique to reduce common AI-writing patterns and make the text read more like human-authored prose. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[moxunjinmu](https://clawhub.ai/user/moxunjinmu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and agents use this skill to revise Chinese prose that feels formulaic or AI-generated. It produces iterative rewritten drafts, diagnostics, and revision decisions until the text reaches the skill's humanized-writing threshold or a three-round cap. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can overwrite a user-provided file path after rewriting the content. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer pasted text, request the rewritten result in the chat, or ask the agent to create a backup or write to a new file before applying changes. <br>
