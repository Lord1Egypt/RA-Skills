## Differentiator Notes

**Why compliance moats matter in appliance repair marketing specifically:**

1. LG, Samsung, Whirlpool are aggressive about protecting "Authorized Service Center" trademark. False authorization claims = C&D letters and Google review warnings from brand monitors.

2. Refrigerant claims without EPA 608 = potential Clean Air Act violation ($44,539/day fine per EPA). No generic template addresses this.

3. FTC review gating (pre-screening customers before sending to review link) is now an enforcement priority. The 2023 Endorsement Guide updates make this clear. Most appliance repair shops still use pre-screening. This kit doesn't.

These aren't edge cases — they're common mistakes in appliance repair marketing. The compliance moats are the product.
