## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-22
Initial release. Five-phase workflow covering pilot intake (PIC role, certificates, currency, IMSAFE), aircraft intake (type, MEL/CDL, fuel, weight & balance), enVironment intake (weather, terrain, density altitude, alternates, day/night, VFR/IFR), External pressures intake (passenger expectations, schedule, get-home-itis), scoring each PAVE category with rationale and Green/Yellow/Red mapping, mitigation planning per identified hazard, and dispatch-authority recommendation, producing a DRAFT FRAT log with re-FRAT triggers — final go/no-go remains the pilot-in-command's decision.
