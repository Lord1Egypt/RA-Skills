## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese-language automotive export market-analysis assistant for global market screening, competitive analysis, field-research planning, and risk warnings using a three-layer funnel model. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Automotive export teams and market analysts use this skill to structure overseas market-entry decisions for Chinese vehicle brands, including country prioritization, channel planning, product-fit analysis, and risk checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market, tariff, regulatory, and foreign-exchange guidance may become outdated or may be incomplete for a specific transaction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify business-critical claims against current authoritative sources before making market-entry, pricing, or compliance decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may receive confidential commercial details such as inventory pressure, sales targets, market plans, or channel strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential business data unless the current agent environment and chat context are approved for that information. <br>
