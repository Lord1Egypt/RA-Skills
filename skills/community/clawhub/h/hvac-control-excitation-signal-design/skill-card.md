## Use Case: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Step-test guidance may be applied to real equipment where unsafe input levels, actuator saturation, or insufficient supervision could create operational or physical safety risk. <br>
Mitigation: Review equipment limits, actuator saturation behavior, supervision, and emergency-stop procedures before running tests on real systems. <br>
