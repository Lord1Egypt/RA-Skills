## Description: <br>
Design and implement no-code automation workflows that help solopreneurs identify repetitive tasks, choose tools, build trigger/action workflows, test them, and maintain them. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Solopreneurs and operators use this skill to find high-value manual processes, map workflow triggers and actions, select Zapier, Make, or n8n, and plan testing and maintenance for business automations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automation workflows may request OAuth scopes or credentials that grant broad access to business systems. <br>
Mitigation: Review requested scopes before enabling a workflow, use least-privilege accounts, and revoke access when an automation is retired. <br>
Risk: A misconfigured workflow could create incorrect records, send unintended messages, or trigger purchases or payment-related actions. <br>
Mitigation: Test with non-production or low-risk data first, verify every trigger and action mapping, and keep a clear disable or rollback path. <br>
Risk: Silent failures in no-code automations can delay business processes or leave data out of sync. <br>
Mitigation: Configure error notifications, review workflow logs regularly, and document each automation's purpose, connected apps, and troubleshooting steps. <br>
