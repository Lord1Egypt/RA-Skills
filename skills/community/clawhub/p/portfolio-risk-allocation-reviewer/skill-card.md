## Description: <br>
Helps investors, advisors, analysts, and family-office operators review portfolio allocation, concentration risk, fees, rebalancing needs, and investment policy notes from brokerage exports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individual investors, financial advisors, analysts, and family-office operators use this skill to turn portfolio exports or brokerage statements into practical allocation reviews, risk checks, fee notes, rebalancing workflows, and plain-language investment policy support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat portfolio allocation and rebalancing output as professional financial, tax, or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame results as decision support, surface assumptions and limitations, and recommend qualified professional review for consequential decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad implicit invocation could make the skill appear in loosely related finance conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill when the user asks for portfolio allocation, risk review, brokerage export analysis, rebalancing, fees, or investment policy workflow support. <br>
Risk: Portfolio exports and statements may contain sensitive account or personal financial details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to omit credentials and unnecessary personal data, and prefer local workflows that use only the fields required for the review. <br>
