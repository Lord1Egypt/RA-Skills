# Prompt 1: Seasonal Campaign + Brand Positioning (FREE)

## Instructions

Copy this prompt into Claude (or any AI). Fill in the `[BRACKETS]` with your business details.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a direct-response copywriter specializing in home services marketing. Generate a seasonal marketing campaign for a cleaning business using the exact specifications below.

BUSINESS DETAILS:
- Business name: [NAME]
- City/area: [CITY, STATE]
- Services: [e.g., residential maid service, deep cleaning, move-out, Airbnb turnover]
- Campaign season/focus: [e.g., Spring Cleaning, Summer Airbnb, Fall Move-Out, Holiday Prep]
- Unique selling point: [e.g., eco-certified products, same-day booking, satisfaction guarantee with free re-clean]
- Bonded and insured: [Yes/No — if yes, specify: general liability $X, bonded $X, workers' comp Yes/No]
- Certifications: [e.g., EPA Safer Choice certified products, ARCSI member, BBB Accredited — or "none"]
- Review rating: [e.g., 4.9★ 287 reviews on Google]
- Average first-clean price range: [e.g., $120–$180 for 2BR/2BA]
- Offer for this campaign: [e.g., $25 off first clean, free oven cleaning with spring deep clean]
- Phone/website: [optional]

COMPLIANCE RULES — FOLLOW EXACTLY:
1. No "chemical-free" or "toxin-free" unless EPA Safer Choice certified products are confirmed
2. "Eco-friendly" or "green" requires the certification name (EPA Safer Choice, EcoLogo, etc.) — if not certified, use "plant-based" or "professional-grade" only
3. Bonding: describe as "bonded & insured" — do NOT say "licensed" (cleaning is not state-licensed in most states)
4. "Satisfaction guaranteed" must be tied to a specific re-clean policy (e.g., "we re-clean any missed area free within 24 hours") — not open-ended
5. No "best" or "top-rated" unless verifiable
6. SMS messages must be under 160 characters and include "Reply STOP to opt out"

GENERATE:

**1. GOOGLE RSA AD**
- 15 unique headlines (max 30 chars each — count every character including spaces)
- 4 unique descriptions (max 90 chars each)
- List character count after each headline in brackets: e.g., "Spring Deep Clean Special [26]"

**2. FACEBOOK/INSTAGRAM AD**
- Primary text: max 125 characters
- Headline: max 40 characters
- Description: max 30 characters
- 3 target audience suggestions with interest targeting notes
- Image description (for stock photo search or AI image generation)

**3. NEXTDOOR SPONSORED POST**
- 150–200 words, neighbor-to-neighbor tone
- No marketing buzzwords — conversational, specific to the neighborhood/city

**4. 3 SMS MESSAGES** (campaign sequence)
- Message 1: Initial offer (launch day)
- Message 2: Urgency reminder (day 5)
- Message 3: Last chance (day 10)
- Each under 160 characters, TCPA-compliant opt-out included

**5. GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE POST**
- 250–300 characters, includes offer + call to action

**6. YELP PROMO**
- Headline: max 100 characters
- Offer description: max 200 characters

After each section, flag any compliance issues with ⚠️ and explain what to verify before publishing.
```
