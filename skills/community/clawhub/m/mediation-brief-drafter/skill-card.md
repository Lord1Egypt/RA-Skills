## Description: <br>
Drafts a two-part civil mediation brief package with a shared brief, a confidential mediator-only brief, BATNA/WATNA/ZOPA analysis, settlement-authority worksheet, redaction checklist, and licensed-counsel review banner. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Litigators, in-house counsel, ADR specialists, and mediation advocates use this skill to prepare a draft shared mediation brief and a confidential mediator-only strategy brief for a scheduled civil mediation session. It structures intake, authority planning, BATNA/WATNA/ZOPA analysis, and do-not-share review before licensed counsel approves any exchange. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles sensitive civil-mediation facts, settlement positions, privileged strategy, and attorney work-product. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in an approved workspace, limit inputs to information counsel is authorized to process, and require licensed counsel review before any exchange or submission. <br>
Risk: Confidential mediator-only strategy could be exposed in the shared brief if the draft is not reviewed carefully. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the skill's dual-audience separation audit and keep BATNA, WATNA, ZOPA, settlement authority, leverage, and weakness analysis in the confidential brief only. <br>
