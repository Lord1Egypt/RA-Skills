## Description: <br>
Professional AI companion for foreign travelers in China, covering pre-arrival preparation, visa and payment guidance, transportation, translation support, itinerary planning, and emergency assistance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External travelers, backpackers, long-term visitors, and trip planners use this skill to prepare for and navigate travel in China. It helps with visa checks, payment setup, transport choices, translation tasks, route recommendations, cultural etiquette, and emergency reference workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with guidance focused on travel to China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Visa, hospital, emergency, payment-limit, attraction-booking, and transport details may become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Treat embedded guidance as a starting point and verify critical details with official sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Translation tasks may involve menus, signs, medicine labels, or documents that contain sensitive personal or travel information. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only the specific content needed for the travel task. <br>
