## Description: <br>
Rewrite X posts for algorithm-relevant actions. Use when drafting posts to maximize dwell, replies, reposts, shares, profile clicks, follows, and low negative feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators and marketers use this skill to turn rough ideas into X posts optimized for a chosen engagement signal while checking negative-feedback risk. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated posts may be inaccurate, off-tone, misaligned with platform policy, or carry reputational risk when optimized for engagement. <br>
Mitigation: Review each post for accuracy, tone, platform policy fit, and reputational impact before publishing. <br>
Risk: Engagement optimization can drift toward controversy or negative feedback if the signal target is applied without judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's operating principle to prioritize useful positive actions and avoid rage bait unless the user explicitly requests controversy. <br>
