## Description: <br>
Plan lean product validation for founders and product teams. Use when the user needs to test a product idea, define assumptions, choose validation experiments, write interview prompts, build a prototype brief, or decide whether to keep building. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders, product managers, makers, and small teams use this skill to turn rough product ideas into falsifiable validation plans before spending significant time or money. It helps prioritize assumptions, choose lean experiments, define pass/fail thresholds, and draft interview scripts, prototype briefs, landing-page copy, or go/no-go recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be invoked automatically for broad product or startup-related requests. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit invocation or narrower routing when tighter control is required. <br>
Risk: Validation advice can be misleading if users rely on vanity metrics, biased interview questions, or experiments that cannot change the decision. <br>
Mitigation: Require behavior-based evidence, predefine pass/fail thresholds, and flag tests that cannot support a go/no-go decision. <br>
