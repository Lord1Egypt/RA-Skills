## Description: <br>
Manage residential rental properties, including tenant screening, lease tracking, maintenance coordination, rent collection monitoring, vacancy optimization, and compliance reminders for small landlords and lightweight property managers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users such as small landlords, self-managing residential property owners, and lightweight property managers use this skill to organize rental portfolios, tenant and lease workflows, maintenance planning, rent tracking, vacancy turnover, and compliance reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States, with included reference material focused on Ohio landlord-tenant law <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release requests purchase and crypto-related capability tags that are not explained by the rental-management instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Review requested capabilities before installing and do not grant purchase, payment, crypto, or broad account authority unless there is a clear need. <br>
Risk: Rental management work can involve sensitive tenant, lease, payment, and property information. <br>
Mitigation: Store only necessary tenant information, secure the workspace, and limit optional integrations to the access required for the workflow. <br>
Risk: Generated legal notices and compliance reminders may be incomplete or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify legal notices, timelines, and compliance guidance against official sources or local counsel before relying on them. <br>
