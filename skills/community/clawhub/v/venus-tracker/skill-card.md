## Description: <br>
Venus Tracker helps maintain a Feishu Bitable tracking system for the Venus kindergarten ERP project, including daily reports, task status, project progress, roster sync, and morning/evening follow-up. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Project leads and development teams use this skill to synchronize and summarize Feishu Bitable records for Venus ERP project tracking, daily reporting, task follow-up, and launch-stage risk visibility. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide user-directed commands that create or modify Feishu Bitable tables, group tabs, roster data, and project-tracking records. <br>
Mitigation: Before running commands, confirm the intended Venus ERP and Feishu workspace, and review the referenced local script and configuration. <br>
Risk: Using the skill without the Venus project management context may produce incomplete or misleading project follow-up. <br>
Mitigation: Load the required project management skill and planning file before relying on tracking outputs. <br>
