## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The persona may activate unexpectedly when broad Spinoza-related trigger text appears. <br>
Mitigation: Use the explicit /spinoza trigger when clear control is needed, and avoid broad trigger terms in contexts where persona switching is undesirable. <br>
Risk: The persona is instructed not to know events after November 1677, so it may be unsuitable for current factual questions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for historical or philosophical dialogue, and route current factual questions to a non-persona workflow. <br>
