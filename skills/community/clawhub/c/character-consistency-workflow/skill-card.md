## Description: <br>
Guides creators through reference asset preparation, consistency tokens, cross-shot prompt construction, and quality checks to reduce character drift in AI-generated short films and video sequences. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[beermanzz](https://clawhub.ai/user/beermanzz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, media teams, and developers use this skill to keep AI-generated characters visually consistent across shots by preparing reference images, maintaining reusable character tokens, and checking outputs for drift. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow may involve uploading character reference images to AI media tools. <br>
Mitigation: Use only images with appropriate rights and consent, and avoid sensitive personal imagery unless that use is intentional and approved. <br>
Risk: Prompts and reference notes may accidentally include API keys or service credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Keep credentials out of prompts, token files, and reference material before using the workflow with external tools. <br>
