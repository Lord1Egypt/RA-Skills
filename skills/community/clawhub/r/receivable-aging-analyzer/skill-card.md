## Description: <br>
Analyzes accounts receivable aging and suggests prioritization strategies for collections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business owners, finance teams, and advisors use this skill to review accounts-receivable aging, prioritize collection activity, diagnose DSO increases, and draft practical credit-control workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broader finance prompts than receivable aging. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user is asking for accounts-receivable, collections, DSO, or credit-control guidance before applying its recommendations. <br>
Risk: Collections and legal escalation advice may affect financial or legal decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational planning aids and verify collection or legal steps with qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Receivables analysis can involve sensitive customer and financial details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the minimum necessary data and avoid entering sensitive financial details unless needed for the requested analysis. <br>
