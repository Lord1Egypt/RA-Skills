## Description: <br>
Shopify/DTC profit margin calculator for sellers. Calculate cost breakdowns including ad spend, CAC, payment processing fees, and 3PL costs. Includes LTV/CAC analysis and DTC-specific benchmarks. No API key required. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security evidence reports that the skill is advertised for Shopify/DTC profit calculations while the runnable script uses Amazon/FBA-style fees, which can produce misleading business results. <br>
Mitigation: Review the calculation model before installing or relying on outputs; confirm whether Shopify/DTC or Amazon/FBA assumptions are intended and correct the code or documentation before business use. <br>
