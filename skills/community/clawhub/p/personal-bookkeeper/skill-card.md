## Description: <br>
Record double-entry bookkeeping for personal finances, including transactions, categories, account balances, spending trends, budget forecasts, reports, and tax notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals and freelancers use this skill to keep local plain-text personal finance logs, review spending and budget notes, export records, and search recent financial activity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial notes, exports, and history are stored as local plain-text files and may contain sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Protect the data directory and exported files, avoid entering bank credentials or full account numbers, and delete exports when no longer needed. <br>
