## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto contract addresses and market data can influence financial decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify links, token addresses, and live market data before trading or transferring funds. <br>
Risk: Market data retrieved from CoinGecko may be unavailable, stale, or changed after retrieval. <br>
Mitigation: Present the source and retrieval context when reporting live price, volume, market cap, or circulating supply. <br>
