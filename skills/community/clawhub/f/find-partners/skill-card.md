## Description: <br>
Finds business partners, channel partners, distribution partners, collaboration targets, and strategic allies for ecommerce growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External business users and growth teams use this skill to identify, compare, and prioritize potential partnership targets by niche, market, channel, geography, and collaboration model. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Partner recommendations may rely on incomplete or outdated public information. <br>
Mitigation: Review the cited signals, validate current partner fit, and confirm assumptions before outreach. <br>
Risk: A shortlist can overstate strategic fit when the user's goals, exclusions, or commercial model are underspecified. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify must-have criteria and treat weak assumptions as validation items before acting on recommendations. <br>
