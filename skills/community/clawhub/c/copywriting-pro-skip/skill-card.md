This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, sales teams, and external agents use this skill to draft, improve, rewrite, and review conversion-focused copy. It is intended for human-reviewed commercial messaging where claims, urgency, scarcity, and sensitive-topic framing must be checked before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce persuasive marketing language that may overstate claims, urgency, scarcity, or proof if the user supplies weak or unverified facts. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated copy against source evidence, product facts, actual availability, real deadlines, and substantiated proof before publication. <br>
Risk: The skill's persuasion guidance can be inappropriate for sensitive topics such as health, money, shame, or fear. <br>
Mitigation: Apply additional human review and domain-specific compliance checks before using outputs in sensitive or regulated contexts. <br>
