## Description: <br>
Coach real-estate agents and FSBO sellers through listing optimization: pricing strategy, MLS description writing, photo direction, staging priorities, marketing channel mix, open-house planning, and negotiation preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Real-estate agents and FSBO sellers use this skill to prepare new listings, rescue stale listings, write MLS-ready descriptions, plan open houses, structure price reductions, critique listings, and prepare buyer offers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Open-house follow-up can involve collecting buyer contact information. <br>
Mitigation: Use clear consent language, collect only necessary contact details, honor opt-outs, and verify local privacy, telemarketing, MLS, Fair Housing, and real-estate rules. <br>
Risk: Listing, pricing, commission, and offer guidance can be affected by local rules and market conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as coaching output and verify final decisions against current local MLS requirements, Fair Housing obligations, and licensed real-estate or legal advice where applicable. <br>
