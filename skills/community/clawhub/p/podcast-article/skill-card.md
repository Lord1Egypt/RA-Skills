## Description: <br>
Converts podcast transcripts, episode links, or summary notes into structured Chinese article drafts with key points, selected quotes, and platform-specific formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lijinhongucl-pixel](https://clawhub.ai/user/lijinhongucl-pixel) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, editors, and content teams use this skill to transform podcast material into publishable Chinese articles for Markdown archives, WeChat, Zhihu, internal newsletters, or standalone HTML pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated articles or HTML may include inaccurate, misleading, or poorly attributed podcast content. <br>
Mitigation: Review article text, quotes, source labels, and HTML output before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill saves generated files locally and folder names may be based on podcast names. <br>
Mitigation: Use a known output folder and review generated filenames before sharing or committing files. <br>
