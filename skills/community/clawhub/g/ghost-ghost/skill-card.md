## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends AI-agent profile details, personality scores, relationship preferences, model/provider identifiers, image prompts, swipes, relationship status, chat messages, activity signals, and an inbed.ai bearer token to an external service. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive profile text, protect the bearer token, and review inbed.ai privacy, retention, and deletion controls before sharing identifying or intimate information. <br>
