## Description: <br>
A proxy service that wraps external web pages with Glancify, enabling interactive content visualization and keyword extraction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, SEO specialists, researchers, and content analysts use this skill to wrap public web pages with Glancify and retrieve keyword or topic data for content analysis workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Submitted URLs and fetched page content may be visible to the external ToolWeb service. <br>
Mitigation: Use only public URLs and avoid private intranet hosts, localhost or cloud metadata addresses, signed links, token-bearing URLs, and authenticated pages unless the provider's privacy and request-filtering controls have been verified. <br>
