## Use Case: <br>
External users and ecommerce operators use this skill to explore static Taobao product category ideas, pricing guidance, and seasonal shopping trends for market research, personal shopping, dropshipping, or store operations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can present static shopping advice as if it reflects current Taobao prices, sales volume, or trends. <br>
Mitigation: Verify prices, sales volume, and trend claims against live marketplace data before using the output for purchasing, dropshipping, or business decisions. <br>
