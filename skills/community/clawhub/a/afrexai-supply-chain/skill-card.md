## Description: <br>
Analyze supplier risks, map dependencies, score vulnerabilities, and generate data-driven mitigation plans with risk dashboards and scenario modeling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Operations leaders, procurement teams, CFOs, and vendor-risk stakeholders use this skill to assess supplier dependency risk, model disruption exposure, and produce prioritized mitigation plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Supply-chain assessments may include confidential supplier, financial, or operational details. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only data appropriate for the agent environment and redact sensitive details when possible. <br>
Risk: Generated mitigation plans and disruption scenarios may influence procurement, inventory, or sourcing decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with qualified business owners before making operational changes. <br>
