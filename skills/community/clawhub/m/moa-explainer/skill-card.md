## Description: <br>
Generate 3D animation scripts and lay explanations for drug mechanisms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to generate drug mechanism-of-action storyboards, voiceover scripts, visual concepts, and simplified explanations for medical education, sales training, investor presentations, or patient education. Outputs should be reviewed for scientific and medical accuracy before sharing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A user-provided output path can overwrite an existing local file. <br>
Mitigation: Use project-local output paths, avoid important existing files, and review the target path before running with --output. <br>
Risk: Generated medical or scientific explanations may be inaccurate or incomplete if shared without review. <br>
Mitigation: Independently review the explanation for scientific and medical accuracy before publishing or presenting it. <br>
