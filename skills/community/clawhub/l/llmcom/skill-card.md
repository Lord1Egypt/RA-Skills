This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and prompt writers use this skill to compress prompts for Claude Code CLI, Cursor agent, or LLM APIs by removing filler words, applying abbreviations, and formatting prompts as compact pipe-separated instructions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompt compression can remove words or abbreviate terms in ways that change intent or reduce clarity. <br>
Mitigation: Review compressed prompts before sending them to an agent or LLM API. <br>
