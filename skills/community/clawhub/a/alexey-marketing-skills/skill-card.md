This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, growth, product, and web teams use this skill to draft and review conversion, SEO, analytics, advertising, launch, pricing, email, referral, and social-content work. It is best used for advisory outputs, draft copy, structured plans, and implementation checklists that a human reviews before publishing or changing connected accounts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Some modules can imply publishing social posts, changing ad campaigns, editing targeting, or changing budgets. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the agent in advisory or draft mode unless a human explicitly approves each publish, schedule, campaign edit, targeting change, and budget change. <br>
Risk: Analytics, tracking, and scraping-oriented guidance may involve privacy-sensitive data collection or platform-policy constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review privacy, consent, platform terms, and scraping rules before implementing any tracking or data-collection tactic. <br>
