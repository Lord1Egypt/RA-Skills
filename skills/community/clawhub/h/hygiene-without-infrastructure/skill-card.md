## Description: <br>
Maintains hygiene and sanitation when normal plumbing, water service, refrigeration, or field facilities are unavailable. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and assisting agents use this skill for emergency hygiene, safe water handling, waste management, food safety, personal hygiene, and basic wound hygiene when ordinary infrastructure is disrupted. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
