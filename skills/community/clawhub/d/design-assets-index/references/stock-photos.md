# 图库素材合集

免费图库与照片素材合集：通用摄影、聚合站、人脸头像、特定主题（美食/复古/创业）等。更多顶选见 [SKILL.md](../SKILL.md)。

## 通用免费图库

- [The Stocks](http://thestocks.im/) — Aggregates free photos, videos, icons, fonts and colors
- [AllTheFreeStock](http://allthefreestock.com/) — Index of free stock photos, videos, icons and music
- [500px](https://web.500px.com/) — 15M+ photographers community
- [LIFE OF PIX](http://www.lifeofpix.com/) — Free high-res photos, no copyright restrictions
- [picjumbo](https://picjumbo.com/) — Free stock photos for personal & commercial use
- [Magdeleine](https://magdeleine.co/browse/) — Hand-picked free photos for inspiration
- [SplitShire](https://www.splitshire.com/) — Free stock photos for commercial use
- [BARNIMAGES](https://barnimages.com/) — Free high-resolution images for everyone

## 特定主题

- [UI Faces](https://uifaces.co/) — Aggregated avatars for personas and placeholders
- [Gratisography](http://www.gratisography.com/) — Quirky free high-resolution stock images
- [FoodiesFeed](https://www.foodiesfeed.com/) — Free natural-looking food photos
- [New Old Stock Photos](http://nos.twnsnd.co/) — Vintage photos from public archives
- [Startup Stock Photos](http://startupstockphotos.com/) — Free photos for startups
