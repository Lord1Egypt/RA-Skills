## Description: <br>
Ultra-fast speech transcription using the iFLYTEK Speed Transcription API for long audio files, with support for Chinese, English, Chinese dialects, and automatic language detection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external users use this skill to transcribe permitted MP3 audio recordings into text or structured JSON through the iFLYTEK Speed Transcription API. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Selected audio files are uploaded to iFLYTEK for transcription. <br>
Mitigation: Use only recordings approved for third-party processing, and avoid confidential, regulated, or third-party recordings without permission and organizational approval. <br>
