## Description: <br>
Generates and updates product test cases from PRDs and existing case libraries, producing separate Excel workbooks for new and updated test cases. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[melissa-lu](https://clawhub.ai/user/melissa-lu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
QA engineers, product teams, and developers use this skill to analyze PRDs, compare them with existing product test-case libraries, and generate reviewable Excel files for new or updated test cases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Configured testcase libraries may be read from or updated at local workbook paths, including in-place update workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Verify configured workbook paths before use, keep backups of source libraries, and manually review update workbooks before applying changes. <br>
