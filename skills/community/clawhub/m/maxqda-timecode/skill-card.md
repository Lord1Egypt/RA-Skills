## Description: <br>
Convert transcript timecodes from MM:SS format to MAXQDA-compatible [hh:mm:ss] format for qualitative research data import. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Kiriny1999](https://clawhub.ai/user/Kiriny1999) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers and analysts use this skill to convert interview transcript lines from Speaker MM:SS format into MAXQDA-compatible [hh:mm:ss]Speaker lines before import. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The conversion commands overwrite transcript files and remove the first line. <br>
Mitigation: Keep a backup and test the conversion on one copied transcript before running a batch command on a directory. <br>
