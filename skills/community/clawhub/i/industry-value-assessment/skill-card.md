## Description: <br>
Builds a structured Chinese-language industry value assessment report using market, policy, capital, competitive, value-chain, and regional-fit evidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[daviddatamining](https://clawhub.ai/user/daviddatamining) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Government planners, regional economic development teams, industry strategy consultants, and investment decision-makers use this skill to assess one or more industries for development value, regional fit, policy support, market potential, and prioritization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market forecasts, financing activity, and public industry data can be incomplete, outdated, or inconsistent. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources, verify current figures, and treat conclusions as decision support rather than final investment or policy determinations. <br>
Risk: Regional fit analysis may be unreliable when local industrial base, talent, land, capital, or policy data is missing. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm local assumptions with current regional data and identify unavailable evidence explicitly before acting on recommendations. <br>
