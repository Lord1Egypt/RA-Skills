## Description: <br>
Create natural, high-retention hooks and opening text for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and slideshow/carousel-style short-form content in English and German. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wotaso-dev](https://clawhub.ai/user/wotaso-dev) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and content teams use this skill to draft and audit hooks, opening copy, visual opens, continuations, and A/B test sets for short-form video and slideshow content in English and German. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated hooks could overstate outcomes, imply unsupported proof, or make sensitive medical or financial claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review hooks against the supplied source material and keep the skill's restrictions against fake proof, guaranteed results, exploitative fear, medical certainty, and financial certainty. <br>
Risk: Bilingual output may lose platform-native tone if German hooks are literal translations of English hooks. <br>
Mitigation: Review German hooks for natural spoken German and rewrite them independently when the audience or context requires it. <br>
