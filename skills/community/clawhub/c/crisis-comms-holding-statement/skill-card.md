## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a PR, comms, or executive team must publish a holding statement within the first hour of a crisis, producing tiered internal, customer, press, and social statements plus a spokesperson Q&A briefing and stakeholder notification timeline. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
PR, communications, legal, and executive teams use this skill to draft first-hour crisis holding statements and align internal, customer, press, and social messaging while facts are still incomplete. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crisis facts may include sensitive incident details or personally identifiable information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved agent environments for sensitive inputs and avoid entering PII unless the environment is cleared for it. <br>
Risk: Draft public statements could create legal, regulatory, or reputational exposure if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Route external statements through legal and executive review before publication. <br>
