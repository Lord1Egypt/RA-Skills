## Description: <br>
Remove signs of AI-generated writing from text and rewrite drafts to sound more natural and human-written. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jzOcb](https://clawhub.ai/user/jzOcb) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and developers use this skill to review pasted text or draft files for common AI-writing patterns, then produce a more natural rewrite while preserving the original meaning and tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read or edit draft files when invoked. <br>
Mitigation: Use it on specific pasted text or named files, and review diffs before accepting edits. <br>
Risk: The skill can make AI-assisted writing appear human-written in contexts where disclosure, authorship, academic, or workplace rules may apply. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only where rewriting is allowed and disclose AI assistance when required by the applicable policy. <br>
