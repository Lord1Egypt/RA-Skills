## Description: <br>
Helps agents explain basic hand and power tools, choose the right tool for a job, and guide budget toolkit building. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to identify appropriate tools and fasteners, learn basic operation and safety practices, and build a functional home-repair toolkit on a budget. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tool-use guidance may be unsafe if applied to electrical, gas, plumbing, structural, load-bearing, or code-governed work without additional review. <br>
Mitigation: Follow manufacturer instructions, local code requirements, and qualified professional advice when mistakes could cause injury or property damage. <br>
Risk: Users may treat general educational guidance as sufficient for hazardous repair work. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm safety equipment, secure the work area, and escalate high-risk projects to a qualified professional before starting. <br>
