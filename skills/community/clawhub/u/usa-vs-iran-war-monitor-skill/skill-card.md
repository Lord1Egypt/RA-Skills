## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Unverified conflict claims can mislead readers or encourage panic sharing. <br>
Mitigation: Require source verification, label uncertain claims clearly, and wait for official or major-wire confirmation before sharing confident alerts. <br>
Risk: War-risk updates could be overused for financial, legal, military, or emergency decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for situational awareness only and defer high-stakes decisions to official advisories, qualified professionals, and emergency authorities. <br>
