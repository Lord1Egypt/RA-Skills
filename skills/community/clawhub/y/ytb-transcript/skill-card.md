## Description: <br>
Fetch YouTube video transcripts, subtitles, and generate summaries for research, lecture notes, content analysis, or processing long videos without watching them. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Rqcker](https://clawhub.ai/user/Rqcker) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, researchers, students, and analysts use this skill to extract YouTube transcripts or subtitles from a URL or video ID and summarize the resulting video content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Referenced implementation files are missing from the provided artifact, so the skill may not fetch transcripts in every environment. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the skill works with representative YouTube URLs before relying on it for important work. <br>
Risk: Videos without available subtitles may produce incomplete or metadata-based summaries. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for missing captions and confirm key claims against the original video when subtitles are unavailable. <br>
