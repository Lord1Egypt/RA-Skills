## Description: <br>
Yinyo Liuyao guides agents through Liuyao divination readings using Yehe-style rules from Zengshan Buyi, including hexagram input checks, setup, useful-spirit selection, relationship analysis, and timing guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1yihui](https://clawhub.ai/user/1yihui) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill when a user provides, or needs help completing, Liuyao hexagram details for a single divination question. Outputs should be framed as uncertain interpretive guidance and not used as medical, legal, financial, or safety advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive personal context while asking about a divination question. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the hexagram and question details needed for the reading, and advise users not to include sensitive personal information. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on divination output for medical, legal, financial, or safety decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present readings as uncertain interpretive guidance and direct users to qualified professionals for high-stakes decisions. <br>
