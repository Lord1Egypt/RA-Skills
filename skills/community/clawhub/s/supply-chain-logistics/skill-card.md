## Description: <br>
Supply Chain & Logistics Optimizer helps agents plan demand forecasting, inventory management, transportation routing, procurement, customs compliance, warehouse layout, and carbon tracking workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Supply chain, logistics, procurement, and operations teams can use this skill to structure optimization work for forecasting, replenishment, routing, supplier evaluation, trade compliance, warehouse planning, and emissions tracking. Agents can use it to produce planning guidance, model-selection recommendations, and structured configuration examples for logistics workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optimization workflows may involve ERP records, supplier data, customs information, map APIs, or business spreadsheets that contain sensitive operational data. <br>
Mitigation: Review integrations before connecting live systems, use approved data-handling controls, and avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive fields. <br>
Risk: Customs, trade compliance, carbon accounting, and supplier-risk outputs may be incomplete or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified reviewers validate outputs before filings, contracts, procurement decisions, or operational commitments. <br>
