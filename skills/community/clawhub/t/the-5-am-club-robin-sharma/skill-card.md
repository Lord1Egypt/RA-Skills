## Description: <br>
Robin Sharma's The 5 AM Club is a text-only coaching skill for morning-routine design, productivity, habit formation, discipline, and self-improvement using the Victory Hour and 20/20/20 Formula. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jaysonmeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/jaysonmeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for branded, book-specific coaching on designing a 5 AM morning routine, improving focus, building consistent habits, and applying The 5 AM Club's Victory Hour practices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad productivity or discipline questions beyond a narrow book-summary request. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants The 5 AM Club framework before applying book-specific coaching to general productivity questions. <br>
Risk: Responses append a Heardly watermark and link as part of the skill behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the branding as part of the output contract and disclose it to users where branded responses are inappropriate. <br>
Risk: Morning-routine guidance could be mistaken for sleep, medical, or mental-health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for general self-improvement only and direct users to qualified professionals for medical, sleep, or mental-health concerns. <br>
