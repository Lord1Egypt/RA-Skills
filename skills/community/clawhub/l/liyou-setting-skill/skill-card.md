## Description: <br>
Supports querying, modifying, adding, and deleting Liyou worldbuilding settings and novel content, with automatic chapter saving and character-setting synchronization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jay0609](https://clawhub.ai/user/jay0609) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and worldbuilding authors use this skill to maintain Liyou setting notes, character or creature entries, and novel chapters while drafting in ClawHub. It guides the agent to persist requested updates and synchronize new story entities into the setting system. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to automatically save or update worldbuilding notes and novel chapters, so brainstorming text could be persisted unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when persistence is desired, explicitly tell the agent when content should not be saved, and keep backups or version history for important drafts. <br>
