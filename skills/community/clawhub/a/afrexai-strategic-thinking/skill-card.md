## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, investors, leaders, and other external users use this skill to structure important decisions, select suitable mental models, score decision quality, and produce decision records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be triggered by broad decision-making phrases during ordinary conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Treat it as an optional structured aid and confirm that the user wants a formal decision framework before applying detailed analysis. <br>
Risk: Business, investing, pricing, hiring, product, and personal recommendations may be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the outputs as frameworks for review, require human judgment before acting, and seek qualified professional advice for consequential legal, financial, medical, or employment decisions. <br>
