## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Kjeldahl testing uses corrosive acids, strong bases, heating, distillation, and potentially hazardous vapors. <br>
Mitigation: Perform the laboratory procedure only under trained supervision with appropriate PPE, ventilation, validated standards, and applicable laboratory SOPs. <br>
Risk: Incorrect sample measurements, titration values, or conversion factors can produce misleading protein results. <br>
Mitigation: Review inputs, run blank and parallel determinations, and verify calculations against the applicable GB 5009.5-2025 procedure before reporting results. <br>
