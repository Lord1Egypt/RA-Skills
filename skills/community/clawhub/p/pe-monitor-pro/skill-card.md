## Description: <br>
PE Monitor Pro helps private equity teams monitor portfolio companies across personnel changes, equity events, financing, financial status, legal matters, government relationships, partnerships, public-opinion risk, competitors, technology, product releases, and IPO progress. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Investment and portfolio operations teams use this skill to track post-investment developments for named Chinese portfolio companies and generate monitoring or risk-analysis reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External company and news lookups may expose API keys, provider logs, billing data, or confidential portfolio research context. <br>
Mitigation: Use limited-purpose Tianyancha and news API keys, monitor provider usage, and avoid submitting confidential investment notes unless external lookups are approved. <br>
Risk: Portfolio monitoring reports can include incomplete or outdated business, legal, financing, or public-opinion information. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated reports as research support and verify material findings against approved primary sources before investment or governance decisions. <br>
