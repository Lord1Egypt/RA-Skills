## Description: <br>
Conducts deep multi-source research and synthesis with structured output using MiniMax CLI for complex investigation and fact verification tasks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ariffazil](https://clawhub.ai/user/ariffazil) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to run web-backed research workflows with MiniMax CLI, synthesize findings across sources, structure outputs as JSON, tables, briefs, reports, or verdicts, and cross-check claims. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad research prompts may be sent to the external MiniMax CLI service. <br>
Mitigation: Verify that the mmx CLI is from a trusted source, use a revocable API key where possible, and avoid sending secrets, personal data, or confidential business material unless external MiniMax processing is acceptable. <br>
