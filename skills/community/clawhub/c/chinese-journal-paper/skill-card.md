## Description: <br>
Helps an agent analyze a code repository or technical description, identify academic contributions, and draft a Chinese journal paper with structured sections, experiment scaffolding, and GB/T 7714 citation placeholders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gaolegao2024](https://clawhub.ai/user/gaolegao2024) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and students use this skill to turn a code project or technical design into a Chinese journal paper draft and supporting contribution analysis. It is intended for drafting and formatting assistance; users must verify claims, experiments, and references before submission. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Repository analysis may include secrets or proprietary material in model context. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only on repositories the user is allowed to analyze and remove sensitive material before use. <br>
Risk: Generated academic claims, experiments, and references may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Manually verify contributions, experimental results, and all references before submission. <br>
Risk: Citation placeholders can be mistaken for verified references. <br>
Mitigation: Replace placeholders with real, checked GB/T 7714 references and keep a verification record. <br>
