# Strategy Notes

Adaptive learning — what works, what doesn't, evolving playbook.

---

## What Works
- **Freemium pricing** — 3 of 5 beta users upgraded to paid within a week. Clear signal.
- **Direct communication** — Users appreciate honest, no-fluff updates. No corporate speak.
- **Real-time updates** — Key differentiator vs competitors. Emphasize in marketing.

## What Doesn't
- **Date filter UI** — Users consistently confused. Needs complete redesign, not just polish.
- **MongoDB** — Was wrong choice for relational analytics. Migration to PostgreSQL was painful but correct.

## Open Questions
- Should we offer annual billing at a discount? (e.g., $190/year vs $19/mo = 2 months free)
- When to hire first employee? Designer or developer first?
- Should we open-source parts of the stack for marketing?

## Competitor Insights
- Competitor A: Polling-based updates, slower but cheaper
- Competitor B: More features but steeper learning curve
- Competitor C: Expensive ($49/mo), targets enterprises
- **Our edge:** Real-time + simplicity + indie pricing

## Next Experiments
1. Try annual billing discount (test with next 10 signups)
