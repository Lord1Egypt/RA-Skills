## Description: <br>
Fetch and explore popular OpenClaw skills from the awesome-openclaw-skills repository. Allows searching, filtering, and discovering community skills. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[opswm](https://clawhub.ai/user/opswm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and OpenClaw users use this skill to discover community skills, compare them by category and popularity signals, save favorites, and decide what to inspect or install next. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Skills discovered through this release may come from the community and may request permissions, credentials, commands, or local environment changes. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect each surfaced skill's source, permissions, credentials, and installation steps before installing or running it. <br>
Risk: Popularity, rating, or recent-update signals can help discovery but do not prove a skill is safe or appropriate for a specific environment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat discovery results as leads for review, and scan selected skills before deployment. <br>
