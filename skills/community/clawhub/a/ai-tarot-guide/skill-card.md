## Description: <br>
AI塔罗指引帮助用户进行塔罗解读与学习，覆盖78张牌、经典牌阵、主要塔罗体系、读牌方法、心理学视角和职业伦理，并可生成交互式HTML报告。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for tarot-themed reflection, entertainment, and learning. It supports complete tarot readings, single-card meaning lookup, tarot theory questions, and HTML report generation while keeping medical, legal, financial, crisis, and life-critical topics out of scope. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on tarot output for medical, legal, financial, crisis, or life-critical decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as entertainment, reflection, or learning, and redirect high-stakes topics to qualified professionals or crisis resources. <br>
Risk: Generated HTML reports are active browser content. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated HTML before opening, publishing, or sharing it. <br>
Risk: Broad divination wording may activate the skill unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's intent before providing a full tarot reading when the request is ambiguous. <br>
