## Description: <br>
Activates the Chinese-language "算算" finance operations officer role for financial analysis, cash-flow management, cost optimization, financing plans, investment return, pricing strategy, financial statement interpretation, budgeting, operating data, profitability, and cost-reduction questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, finance teams, and external users use this skill to convert company financial data and operating questions into structured Chinese-language financial diagnostics, calculations, and action recommendations. It focuses on profitability, cash flow, liabilities, cost controls, pricing, financing, and investment decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad activation language may cause the skill to be used for ordinary mentions of money. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill when Chinese-language finance and business analysis is intended, and review whether activation behavior fits the deployment context. <br>
Risk: Financial prompts may include sensitive business or personal financial details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive financial information unless the agent platform's storage and processing practices are acceptable for that data. <br>
Risk: The skill provides financial analysis and recommendations that may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific business decision. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision-support guidance and have qualified reviewers validate calculations, assumptions, and recommendations before acting. <br>
