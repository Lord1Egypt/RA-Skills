## Description: <br>
Extracts learner questions from enterprise WeChat group messages, classifies them by course stage, and records them in a structured Markdown table. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jackzhenguo](https://clawhub.ai/user/jackzhenguo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Course operators and instructors use this skill to collect bot-mentioned learner questions from enterprise WeChat groups, categorize them by course stage, and maintain a question record. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Learner questions and nicknames from WeChat course groups may be recorded in a local Markdown file. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in intended course groups, inform participants that bot-mentioned questions may be recorded, restrict access to the record file, and define retention or deletion rules. <br>
