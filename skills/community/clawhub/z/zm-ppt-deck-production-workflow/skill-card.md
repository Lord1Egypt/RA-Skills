## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Deck production may involve likenesses, voices, screenshots, customer conversations, logos, or business data without adequate rights or consent. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm usage rights, consent, public-sharing boundaries, and retention expectations before including these materials in slides or delivery packages. <br>
Risk: Generated visuals may be mistaken for real evidence, official brand assets, product interfaces, QR codes, people, or business results. <br>
Mitigation: Use real or official source assets for evidence-bearing material, keep placeholders clearly marked, and remove placeholder notes before final delivery. <br>
Risk: Knowledge-base uploads or external sharing can expose sensitive customer, account, or unpublished business information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the upload destination, version strategy, file name, token or link, and verification result, and avoid uploading sensitive data without an approved sharing plan. <br>
Risk: Compressed or incomplete deck packages may be confused with the final presentation version. <br>
Mitigation: Label compressed versions by purpose and verify the delivery package contains final PPTX files, source visuals, previews, production notes, review records, upload records, and a manifest. <br>
