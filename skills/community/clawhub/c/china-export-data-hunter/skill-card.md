## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External trade-data research may involve API credentials, scraping, or rate-limited services. <br>
Mitigation: Use dedicated API keys where possible, avoid sharing credentials in chats, respect third-party terms and rate limits, and keep automation bounded. <br>
Risk: Export-data conclusions can be misleading when HS codes change, re-exports are included, trade modes differ, or seasonal effects distort trends. <br>
Mitigation: Validate HS codes and assumptions, cross-check sources, and label confidence before acting on competitive intelligence. <br>
