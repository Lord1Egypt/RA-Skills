# Quality Checklist

Pre-submission verification checklist for ungating applications. Complete all applicable sections.

## A. Account Readiness (5 items)

- [ ] 1. Professional seller account active
- [ ] 2. ODR below 1%, valid tracking above 95%, late shipment below 4%
- [ ] 3. No active policy violations or IP complaints (past 90 days)
- [ ] 4. No other ungating applications currently pending
- [ ] 5. Account age meets category minimum (typically 3+ months)

## B. Invoice Content (7 items)

- [ ] 6. Buyer name matches Seller Central legal entity exactly
- [ ] 7. Invoice dated within 180 days of submission
- [ ] 8. Minimum 10 units per ASIN shown
- [ ] 9. Supplier name, address, and phone number included
- [ ] 10. Supplier phone is verifiable (connects to their business)
- [ ] 11. Product descriptions match target ASINs (brand, name, size)
- [ ] 12. Unique invoice number visible

## C. Invoice Format (5 items)

- [ ] 13. Saved as PDF with selectable text
- [ ] 14. No editing artifacts or signs of modification
- [ ] 15. All text legible, not cut off by margins
- [ ] 16. File named: `Invoice_SupplierName_YYYYMMDD.pdf`
- [ ] 17. If redacted, only pricing obscured (descriptions and dates visible)

## D. Product Photography (8 items)

- [ ] 18. White or light neutral background
- [ ] 19. Even, diffused lighting without shadows or glare
- [ ] 20. Resolution at least 1000x1000px, product fills 80%+ of frame
- [ ] 21. Front label captured, all text legible
- [ ] 22. Back label captured, all text legible
- [ ] 23. Ingredients/materials panel fully visible
- [ ] 24. UPC/EAN barcode fully visible and scannable
- [ ] 25. Lot number and expiration date legible

## E. Brand and Safety Documents (6 items)

- [ ] 26. Brand auth letter on official letterhead (if brand-gated)
- [ ] 27. Letter names seller's legal entity and lists authorized products
- [ ] 28. Letter signed, dated, with verifiable brand contact info
- [ ] 29. Safety certs reference specific product by name, ASIN, or UPC
- [ ] 30. Test reports from ISO 17025 or CPSC-accepted lab
- [ ] 31. All certifications are current (not expired)

## F. ASIN Verification (4 items)

- [ ] 32. Invoice descriptions mappable to target ASIN catalog listings
- [ ] 33. Target ASIN is active and not suppressed
- [ ] 34. No additional brand-level gates on target ASIN
- [ ] 35. Product condition matches the condition gate applied for

## G. Appeal Items (3 items)

- [ ] 36. Appeal letter addresses the specific rejection reason
- [ ] 37. Corrected documents differ from previously rejected ones
- [ ] 38. All corrective actions in the letter supported by attached docs

---

**Ready**: All applicable items checked. **Review**: 1-2 gaps. **Not ready**: 3+ gaps -- resolve before submitting. Mark non-applicable items N/A.
