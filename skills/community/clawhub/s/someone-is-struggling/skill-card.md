## Description: <br>
How to talk to someone you're worried about -- depression, substance abuse, self-harm, or suicidal thoughts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill when they are worried about a friend, family member, partner, or coworker and need practical conversation support, crisis-response steps, and referral options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill gives high-stakes mental-health support guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as conversation support rather than professional care, and verify current local emergency or crisis resources when someone may be in immediate danger. <br>
Risk: A user may describe suicidal ideation, self-harm, or immediate danger. <br>
Mitigation: Provide crisis resources promptly and escalate to local emergency services or qualified professionals when risk appears immediate. <br>
