## Description: <br>
Organizes raw meeting transcripts or notes into structured summaries with clear decisions, action items, owners, deadlines, and follow-up items. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[goldath](https://clawhub.ai/user/goldath) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and teams use this skill after meetings to turn Chinese or bilingual transcripts, bullet notes, or email summaries into shareable meeting minutes and action-item tables. It is useful for standups, project reviews, sprint meetings, kick-offs, and follow-up tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting transcripts and notes can contain confidential business information, personal data, customer details, or secrets when pasted into AI, transcription, Jira, Notion, Lark, or similar tools. <br>
Mitigation: Check organizational data policy, obtain required participant consent, and redact secrets and sensitive personal or customer information before using external tools. <br>
Risk: The skill may infer owners, deadlines, priorities, and action items incorrectly from ambiguous meeting notes. <br>
Mitigation: Review inferred owners, deadlines, priorities, and action items with meeting participants before sharing notes or creating project-management tickets. <br>
