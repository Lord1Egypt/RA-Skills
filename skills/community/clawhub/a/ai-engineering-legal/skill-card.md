Provides construction cost and legal workflow guidance for contract risk review, change-claim analysis, settlement dispute diagnosis, evidence-chain analysis, and judicial appraisal preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Construction cost professionals, legal advisors, project managers, auditors, and appraisal practitioners use this skill to structure construction-cost and legal analysis, prepare checklists, draft review prompts, and organize dispute or appraisal materials. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat generated construction-law or appraisal guidance as authoritative legal, appraisal, or jurisdiction-specific advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as drafting and workflow support only; have qualified professionals review contracts, filings, appraisal opinions, evidence strategy, and major financial or litigation decisions. <br>
Risk: Knowledge-base citations, standards, cases, or laws may be incomplete or outdated for a specific dispute. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited authorities and factual assumptions against current authoritative sources before relying on an output. <br>
