## Description: <br>
Patent Expert supports patent mining, portfolio planning, prior-art search, invention disclosure drafting, claim analysis, and patent drawing guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lingmoon96-dev](https://clawhub.ai/user/lingmoon96-dev) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Patent professionals, inventors, and technical teams use this skill to identify patentable technical points, plan patent portfolios, search prior art, draft invention disclosures, analyze claims, and prepare patent drawing guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated patent guidance or legal conclusions may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Verify legal conclusions, patentability assessments, and filing strategy with a qualified patent professional before relying on them. <br>
Risk: User-provided invention details may include confidential or proprietary information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive invention details unless the user is comfortable placing them in the agent context. <br>
Risk: Suggested Python or Graphviz drawing scripts may require local execution. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated scripts and confirm the local Python, Graphviz, font, and output-path configuration before running them. <br>
