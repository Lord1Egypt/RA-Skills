## Description: <br>
Leadership Prompts provides 25+ prompt templates for engineering leaders covering 1-on-1s, team health, incident retrospectives, technical strategy, hiring, career development, and stakeholder communication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Engineering managers, tech leads, VPs of Engineering, and CTOs use this skill to browse and apply structured prompt templates for recurring leadership situations such as difficult 1-on-1s, team health checks, incident retrospectives, technical strategy, hiring, career development, and stakeholder communication. <br>
