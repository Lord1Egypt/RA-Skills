## Description: <br>
Use when user needs scalping or day trading analysis for IDX (Bursa Efek Indonesia) stocks, including entry points, cut loss levels, and target sell levels based on momentum, volume, and short-term M5/M15 timeframes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to analyze IDX intraday scalping and day-trading setups from user-provided Stockbit OHLC, volume, or screenshot data. It produces structured technical-analysis guidance with entry, cut loss, target sell, risk-reward, and volume confirmation details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, for analysis of IDX-listed stocks <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading-analysis outputs may be mistaken for financial advice or automated trade instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational trading opinions, confirm IDX context and risk assumptions, and make independent financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Signals depend on user-provided Stockbit OHLC, volume, or screenshot data that may be incomplete, stale, or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for current Stockbit data before analysis and verify entry, stop-loss, target, liquidity, and timeframe assumptions before relying on the output. <br>
