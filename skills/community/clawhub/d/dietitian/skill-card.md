## Description: <br>
Structured meal planning — calorie targets, macro calculations, meal timing, and goal-specific diet protocols. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to draft calorie targets, macro splits, meal timing, food swaps, and meal prep guidance for goal-oriented nutrition planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Diet guidance may be mistaken for personalized medical care. <br>
Mitigation: Users with medical conditions, eating disorder history, pregnancy, diabetes, kidney disease, or medication-related diet concerns should consult a physician or registered dietitian before following calorie deficits, fasting, ketogenic, or high-protein protocols. <br>
