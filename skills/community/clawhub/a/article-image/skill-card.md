## Description: <br>
Recommends article image sources, search keywords, visual styles, sizes, and copyright checks based on the article topic and intended image use. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[regexl](https://clawhub.ai/user/regexl) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, marketers, and content creators use this skill to plan suitable cover images or inline illustrations for articles. It provides source suggestions, bilingual search keywords, style direction, recommended dimensions, and licensing reminders while leaving final image selection and rights review to the user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Image-source suggestions can be mistaken for confirmed usage rights. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the license, attribution requirements, and permitted use for each selected image before public or commercial use. <br>
Risk: Recommended keywords, sources, or styles may not match the article's context or audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against the article goals and choose or revise image searches before using them. <br>
