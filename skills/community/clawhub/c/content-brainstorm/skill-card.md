## Description: <br>
Social media content ideation engine that generates a 7-day topic plan with titles, angles, SEO keywords, and trend scores for any niche and platform. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alexandrali1989-cell](https://clawhub.ai/user/alexandrali1989-cell) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and marketers use this skill to plan Chinese social media topics across platforms such as Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, WeChat public accounts, Douyin, and Twitter. It helps turn a niche, platform, and account positioning into a structured weekly content calendar. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated claims, citations, competitor references, or marketing language may be inaccurate or unsuitable for the target platform. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims, citations, references, marketing language, and platform compliance before publishing. <br>
Risk: Trend-focused topics may be time-sensitive and lose relevance quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target platform, language, and current trend context before using time-sensitive topics. <br>
