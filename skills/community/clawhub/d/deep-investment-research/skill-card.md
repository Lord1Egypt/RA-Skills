## Description: <br>
Deep Investment Research provides reusable investment research methodology, topic-specific frameworks, report templates, and Python data collection helpers for macro, industry, policy, company, stock, feasibility, risk, and investment planning research. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and investment research agents use this skill to structure deep research workflows, gather public financial and policy source material, and produce evidence-backed research reports. It is for research support only and does not perform trading, trade execution, position monitoring, or automated buy/sell signaling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Investment topics, tickers, watchlists, and research queries may be sent to external search and data providers. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when that disclosure is acceptable, and avoid confidential positions, proprietary research topics, or non-public strategy details. <br>
Risk: The skill can read API keys from environment variables and uses an optional SEARCH_SKILL_PATH helper. <br>
Mitigation: Keep credentials in normal environment variables, rotate them as needed, and set SEARCH_SKILL_PATH only to a trusted local search helper. <br>
Risk: Research reports and fetched data are written to local output directories. <br>
Mitigation: Set RESEARCH_OUTPUT_DIR and RESEARCH_DATA_ROOT to dedicated, non-sensitive directories before running data collection or report generation. <br>
Risk: Research output may be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable as direct trading advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated results as research input, verify material claims against primary sources, and keep final investment decisions under human control. <br>
