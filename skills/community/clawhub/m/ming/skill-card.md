## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on metaphysics output for health, legal, financial, career, marriage, or other major decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as cultural or entertainment reference only and direct users to qualified professionals for consequential decisions. <br>
Risk: Birth dates, birth times, and names can be personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the minimum information needed for the requested analysis and avoid storing or sharing it. <br>
Risk: Simplified calculations may be inaccurate for lunar conversion, solar-term boundaries, local time, true solar time, or full fortune-cycle analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose the simplified solar-calendar assumptions and avoid presenting results as precise traditional charting. <br>
