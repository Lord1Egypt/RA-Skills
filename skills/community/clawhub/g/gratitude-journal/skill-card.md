## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to maintain a local gratitude practice by logging daily entries, receiving reflection prompts, tracking streaks, reviewing past entries, and summarizing gratitude patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal gratitude entries may include sensitive journal-like details stored locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid recording details you would not want stored locally, and review the host environment's storage and deletion options when privacy matters. <br>
Risk: Theme and pattern insights may be incomplete or may overstate trends in a small set of entries. <br>
Mitigation: Treat insights as reflective prompts rather than factual psychological assessment, and review underlying entries before acting on conclusions. <br>
