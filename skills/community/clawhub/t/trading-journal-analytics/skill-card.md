## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading journal entries can contain sensitive account size, P&L, screenshots, and emotion notes. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm where journal data is stored before use, and avoid entering broker credentials, private keys, bank details, or sensitive account identifiers. <br>
Risk: Cloud backup, sync, TradingView, calendar, and collaboration features may share trading data outside the local environment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat integrations and sync features as external sharing surfaces, and require clear privacy, consent, retention, and deletion controls before using them with real trade data. <br>
