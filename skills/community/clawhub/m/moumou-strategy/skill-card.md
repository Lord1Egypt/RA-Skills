## Description: <br>
Activates a Chinese-language strategic decision officer mode for major business decisions such as strategic planning, market entry, financing, mergers and acquisitions, transformation, annual planning, and competitive strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business owners, executives, and strategy teams use this skill to structure major corporate direction decisions, compare strategic options, choose a recommended path, and identify execution milestones and risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Strategic recommendations for financing, market entry, transformation, or M&A may be incomplete or misleading without current business data and human review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as advisory guidance and verify important decisions with current market, financial, legal, and operational evidence before acting. <br>
