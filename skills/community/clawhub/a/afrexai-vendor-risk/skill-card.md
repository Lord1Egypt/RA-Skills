## Description: <br>
Evaluate and score vendors on security, financial stability, compliance, operations, and data handling to classify risk and manage remediation plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Procurement teams, CISOs, and operations leaders use this skill to assess critical vendors, classify risk tiers, plan remediation, and maintain a portfolio-level vendor risk view. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter confidential vendor contracts, financials, security questionnaires, or other sensitive third-party information into their agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use redacted or approved data unless the organization is comfortable sharing that information with the active agent environment. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes external promotional links that are not required to perform the assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat external links as optional resources and review them separately before relying on them. <br>
