## Description: <br>
Track Shopify store sales and monitor store performance using PPSPY sales monitoring tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fanyanggod](https://clawhub.ai/user/fanyanggod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, ecommerce operators, and analysts use this skill to create and manage PPSPY Shopify monitoring tasks, inspect hourly and daily sales, and review monitored store performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shopify store URLs, monitoring targets, and task metadata provided to the skill may be sent to PPSPY. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with stores and monitoring targets that are appropriate to share with PPSPY. <br>
Risk: Create, edit, stop, and delete actions may alter PPSPY monitoring tasks or consume account quota. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed task changes before execution and monitor PPSPY account quota and billing. <br>
