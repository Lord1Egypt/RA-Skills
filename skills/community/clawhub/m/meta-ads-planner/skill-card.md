## Description: <br>
Plan profitable Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns for ecommerce businesses with structured audience segmentation, creative strategy, placement optimization, bidding configuration, and budget allocation across funnel stages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators, media buyers, and marketing managers use this skill to plan Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns across funnel stages, audiences, creatives, placements, bidding, budgets, and measurement. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audience planning can involve privacy-sensitive Pixel, Conversions API, retargeting, lookalike, or Customer List workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm user consent, privacy-policy coverage, customer-list permissions, and applicable Meta policy and legal requirements before using the guidance. <br>
Risk: Budgeting and bidding recommendations may influence paid ad spend. <br>
Mitigation: Review campaign plans against business margins, budget limits, and account performance data before launching or scaling campaigns. <br>
