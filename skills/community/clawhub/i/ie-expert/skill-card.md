## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Operational recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific production environment. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations with qualified process owners and pilot changes before applying them in production. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include trade secrets, confidential production data, or sensitive supplier or customer details. <br>
Mitigation: Omit or anonymize sensitive operational and commercial data unless it is approved for agent use. <br>
Risk: Industry regulations or major investment decisions may exceed the skill's advisory scope. <br>
Mitigation: Consult appropriate specialists and perform a full risk review before relying on the advice for regulated or high-value decisions. <br>
