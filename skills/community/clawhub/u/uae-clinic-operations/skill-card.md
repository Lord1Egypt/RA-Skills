## Description: <br>
Provides UAE clinic operations guidance for scheduling, patient flow, SOPs, KPIs, urgent transfer protocols, and daily opening or closing checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Clinic managers, front desk teams, and operations staff use this skill to draft practical workflows, checklists, SOP outlines, and performance metrics for private clinic operations in the UAE. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United Arab Emirates <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Healthcare policy guidance could treat patients differently based on nationality, residency status, VIP status, or referral source. <br>
Mitigation: Before deployment, rewrite no-show, cancellation, fee, prepayment, exception, and scheduling-flexibility policies around neutral documented criteria such as appointment history, medical urgency, hardship, insurance rules, or compliance-approved policy. <br>
