## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User interviews and screen shares can expose personal, customer, product, or business-sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Use an explicit consent script, require recording opt-in, redact screenshots and screen shares, and define retention and deletion rules before running interviews. <br>
Risk: Cold outreach and repeated follow-up can create compliance, trust, or reputational issues if applied without limits. <br>
Mitigation: Set clear limits on outreach frequency and channels, honor opt-outs, and adapt messaging to the audience and applicable policies. <br>
Risk: The playbook provides operational guidance that may not fit every product, market, or research context. <br>
Mitigation: Review interview plans, beta-test tasks, and user grading criteria with the responsible product, research, legal, or privacy stakeholders before deployment. <br>
