## Description: <br>
Personal travel assistant for Atlas - dream destinations, planning, history, documents, budget, checklists and reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[donmeusi](https://clawhub.ai/user/donmeusi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to plan and organize travel, including destination wishlists, trip workspaces, budgets, packing lists, document checklists, and renewal reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may encourage storing sensitive travel, identity, medical, insurance, device, and booking details in ordinary local Markdown files. <br>
Mitigation: Use redacted values in Markdown files, keep full identifiers in a password manager or encrypted notes, and confirm before the agent creates or updates document files. <br>
