# Traffic Masters

16 especialistas em paid traffic para campanhas de alto desempenho.

- **traffic-chief** - Orchestrator
- **scale-optimizer** - Scaling campaigns profitably
- **fiscal** - Budget management, ROAS
- **performance-analyst** - Data analysis

- create-ad-strategy - Create ad strategy
- audit-ad-account - Audit ad account
- create-ad-creative - Create ad creative
- setup-tracking - Setup tracking/pixels
- scale-campaign - Scale campaign profitably
- manage-budget - Budget management
- analyze-performance - Analyze performance

```

"Preciso de criativos que convertem"
```
