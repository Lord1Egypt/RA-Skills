## Description: <br>
高考报名+志愿填报全能辅助智能体，帮助考生、家长、复读生和社会考生理解高考报名政策、流程、材料审核，并规划志愿填报、院校推荐、位次换算、冲稳保方案和专业就业分析。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External students, parents, repeat candidates, and social candidates use this skill to organize Gaokao registration questions, prepare application materials, compare score and rank data, and draft volunteer-application plans. The guidance is planning support and should be checked against official provincial education exam notices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Gaokao registration deadlines, eligibility rules, score/rank tables, and admission policies can change by province and year. <br>
Mitigation: Verify deadlines, eligibility, score/rank tables, and admission rules on the relevant official provincial education exam site before acting. <br>
Risk: Registration troubleshooting can involve sensitive student identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing full ID card numbers, admission-ticket numbers, or other unnecessary private identifiers; redact personal data when asking for help. <br>
Risk: 志愿填报 recommendations can be mistaken for guaranteed admissions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat school lists and 冲稳保 plans as planning analysis only, not as admission guarantees. <br>
