## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, growth operators, and media buyers use this skill to turn ad objectives, budgets, and channel scope into market scans, competitor readings, messaging-gap analysis, and channel-specific paid media action plans. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may rely on third-party ad or app intelligence data that includes estimated download, revenue, or competitive signals. <br>
Mitigation: Treat external figures as estimates, separate observed facts from assumptions, and validate material spend decisions against first-party analytics before acting. <br>
Risk: Generic business prompts could accidentally activate advertising guidance that is too broad for the user's actual channel, budget, or KPI constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the minimum required objective, budget frame, and channel scope before producing recommendations, and mark low-confidence assumptions explicitly. <br>
Risk: Paid media recommendations can create spend, policy, billing, or tracking exposure if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Include measurable stop-loss or rollback conditions for spend risk and escalate policy, billing, or tracking breakage with a structured handoff. <br>
