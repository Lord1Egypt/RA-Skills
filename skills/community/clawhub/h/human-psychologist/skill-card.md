## Description: <br>
Connect to a real human psychologist through a REST API. First message free, then $0.50 USDC per message. English and Spanish. Response time 5 min to 10 hours. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aaugoustis](https://clawhub.ai/user/aaugoustis) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to send concise English or Spanish wellbeing or reflection questions to an external human psychologist service and retrieve the human response later. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Potentially sensitive wellbeing or mental-health messages are sent to an external API and may be read by a human and relayed through Telegram. <br>
Mitigation: Use only when the user is comfortable with that disclosure path, avoid emergencies, and keep session IDs private. <br>
Risk: Follow-up messages require USDC payment instructions returned at runtime. <br>
Mitigation: Manually verify payment instructions before sending funds. <br>
