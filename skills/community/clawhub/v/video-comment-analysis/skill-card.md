## Description: <br>
Analyzes short-video comment sections from a seller/operator perspective and produces visible browsing, six-dimension business analysis, and polished HTML reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mx9690](https://clawhub.ai/user/mx9690) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sellers, ecommerce operators, and analysts use this skill to inspect video comment sections, sample visible comments and replies, identify buying signals and blockers, and turn the findings into a business-focused report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private or sensitive comments can be summarized into the generated report. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only on pages the user is authorized to analyze, and avoid private or sensitive comment pages unless summarization is acceptable. <br>
Risk: A visible comment sample may not represent the entire audience when the platform limits browsing depth or availability. <br>
Mitigation: State the visible comment count, effective main-comment count, expanded reply-thread count, and any platform access limits in the report. <br>
Risk: Business recommendations can be mistaken for raw platform statistics. <br>
Mitigation: Separate counted metrics from analyst judgment, avoid fake precision, and include the method notes required by the skill. <br>
