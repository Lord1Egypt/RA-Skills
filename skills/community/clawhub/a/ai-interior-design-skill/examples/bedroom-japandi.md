# Example: Japandi Bedroom

## Input

Room: bedroom  
Style: Japandi  
Mood: warm calm  
Constraints: renter-friendly, low budget

## Prompt

Small bedroom interior redesign in Japandi style, warm calm atmosphere, oak platform bed, linen bedding, soft beige walls, paper pendant light, woven rug, minimal decor, balanced layout, realistic proportions, natural daylight, editorial architectural photography.

## Negative prompt

clutter, oversized furniture, glossy plastic, harsh ceiling light, distorted windows, unrealistic proportions, overdecorated shelves.
