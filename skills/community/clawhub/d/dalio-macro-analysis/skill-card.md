## Description: <br>
This skill applies Ray Dalio's macro analysis framework to analyze global economic conditions, investment allocation, and geopolitical risks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to structure macroeconomic, debt-cycle, geopolitical, and asset-allocation analysis using a Dalio-inspired five-forces framework. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market and portfolio guidance can be misleading if embedded assumptions or dated macro claims are inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify facts independently and treat outputs as an analytical framework, not personalized investment advice. <br>
Risk: Suggested allocations may be inappropriate for a user's objectives, constraints, or regulatory context. <br>
Mitigation: Review conclusions with qualified financial or compliance professionals before acting on them. <br>
