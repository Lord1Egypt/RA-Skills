## Description: <br>
Professional brand identity designer for SVG logos, icons, brandmarks, and complete visual identity systems. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[perplexingperfectionist](https://clawhub.ai/user/perplexingperfectionist) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to create SVG brand identity assets such as logos, icons, favicons, app icons, monograms, emblems, badges, and icon packs. It guides the agent through discovery, strategy, concept generation, SVG asset creation, icon simplification, and quality review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may infer brand details from a user prompt and include those details in design rationale or SVG output. <br>
Mitigation: Review prompts and generated rationale before sharing externally, especially when working with confidential brand strategy or unreleased product names. <br>
Risk: Server capability tags include crypto, wallet, and sensitive-credential requirements, but the security evidence says these tags are unsupported by the artifacts. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide secrets, wallet credentials, seed phrases, API keys, or other sensitive credentials when using this design-only skill. <br>
Risk: Generated SVG brand assets may be visually generic, too complex at small sizes, or unsuitable for the intended brand context. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's quality review, favicon-size checks, and human brand review before adopting generated assets in production. <br>
