## Description: <br>
Random Driven helps an agent use random-association brainstorming to extract core concepts, introduce unrelated concepts, combine them, preserve interesting absurd connections, and develop them into complete proposals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill for structured creative ideation, concept recombination, and proposal development when they want deliberately unexpected associations. It is best suited for brainstorming and draft generation rather than authoritative factual, legal, medical, financial, or operational decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Random-association brainstorming can produce misleading or unsupported ideas if treated as factual output. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill intentionally for ideation and verify any factual, legal, medical, financial, or operational claims before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Generated proposals may preserve absurd connections that are interesting but not practical. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for relevance, feasibility, and user goals before turning them into plans or decisions. <br>
