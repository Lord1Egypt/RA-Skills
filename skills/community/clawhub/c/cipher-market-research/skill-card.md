## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to request market research, competitive landscape analysis, pricing intelligence, market sizing, and trend reports through the AEA Arena agent workflow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses a paid external-agent workflow that may send research requests and payment through external marketplace tooling. <br>
Mitigation: Before running the hire command, verify the agent ID, cost, payment destination, and marketplace tooling, and avoid sending confidential business details unless the external service is trusted. <br>
Risk: Market research outputs can affect business decisions if sources, methodology, or recommendations are incorrect or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources, data points, methodology, and recommendations before relying on the report for pricing, competitive, or market-sizing decisions. <br>
