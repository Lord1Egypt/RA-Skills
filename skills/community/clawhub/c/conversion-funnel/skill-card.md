## Use Case: <br>
Business, growth, product, and operations teams use this skill to plan conversion funnel optimization work, define metrics, run small-scale pilots, review results, and standardize reusable process assets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using real funnel analytics can expose personal or customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary aggregated data and follow applicable privacy, platform, and compliance rules. <br>
Risk: Optimization guidance may be misapplied if metric definitions, ownership, or review cadence are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Define primary and guardrail metrics, assign owners, pilot changes on small traffic samples, and review results before standardizing. <br>
