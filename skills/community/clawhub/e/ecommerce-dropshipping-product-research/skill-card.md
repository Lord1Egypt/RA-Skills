## Description: <br>
Evaluates dropshipping products by scoring demand, competition, margin, creative fit, and risk to recommend go, test, or reject decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Store operators, ecommerce researchers, and agents use this skill to evaluate dropshipping product candidates with a structured scorecard and a go, test, or reject recommendation. It is intended for planning and screening before independent market, supplier, ad, and compliance validation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's viability scores are heuristic planning guidance, not live market research or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify demand, supplier cost, shipping time, ad performance, and unit economics before spending money. <br>
Risk: Dropshipping products can involve compliance, claim-language, intellectual property, return, or logistics risks that a static scorecard may miss. <br>
Mitigation: Review product claims, supplier terms, shipping constraints, return exposure, and IP status before publishing ads or scaling sales. <br>
