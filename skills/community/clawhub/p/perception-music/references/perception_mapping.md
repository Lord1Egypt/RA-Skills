# Perception → Music Mapping

## Brightness → Carrier Frequency

| Brightness (0-255) | Frequency (Hz) | Note | Feeling |
|-------------------|----------------|------|---------|
| 0-30 | 65 (C2) | Deep bass | Night, darkness |
| 30-60 | 110 (A2) | Low | Dusk, overcast |
| 60-100 | 165 (E3) | Low-mid | Cloudy, indoor |
| 100-150 | 220 (A3) | Mid | Overcast-bright |
| 150-200 | 440 (A4) | Mid-high | Clear, sunny |
| 200-255 | 880-1760 (A5-A6) | High | Intense light |

## RMS → Modulation Index

| RMS | Index I | Timbre | Scene |
|-----|---------|--------|-------|
| 0-10 | 0.5 | Pure sine | Silent night |
| 10-30 | 1.5 | Warm | Ambient, wind |
| 30-80 | 3.0 | Bright | Rain, activity |
| 80-200 | 5.0 | Harsh | Heavy rain, thunder |
| 200+ | 8.0 | Metal | Extreme events |

## Phase → Rhythm

| Phase | Density | Pattern |
|-------|---------|---------|
| night | 0.1-0.2 | Isolated tones, long decay |
| dawn | 0.2-0.4 | Slow pulses, gradual |
| morning | 0.4-0.6 | Regular rhythm |
| afternoon | 0.6-0.8 | Dense, active |
| evening | 0.4-0.6 | Settling, moderate |
| dusk | 0.2-0.4 | Sparse, transitional |

| Temperature (°C) | Ratio fc/fm | Spectrum |

| 15-25 | 1.5 | Slightly inharmonic |
