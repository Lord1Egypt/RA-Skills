Mitigation: Verify current marketplace state and platform policies before relying on recommendations. <br>
Risk: Legal, tax, purchasing, listing, and account-risk guidance may affect real-world transactions or account standing. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill's advice as guidance to verify and require explicit user approval before any transaction or account change. <br>
