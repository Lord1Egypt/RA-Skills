## Description: <br>
Professional-grade Claude prompt optimization engine that restructures raw prompts for optimization, refinement, token efficiency, and output precision. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, writers, product managers, and designers use this skill to turn raw or ambiguous prompts into structured Claude-ready prompts with roles, tasks, constraints, and output format guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation language may trigger prompt optimization on general requests to optimize, refine, restructure, or save tokens. <br>
Mitigation: Review or narrow the trigger wording before deployment if the skill should only run after explicit opt-in. <br>
Risk: Prompt restructuring can change emphasis or omit details from ambiguous source prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Review optimized prompts before use when exact wording, constraints, or domain-specific nuance matters. <br>
