## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive legal data can be exposed if the skill is installed in a broad or unrelated workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in a workspace intended for sensitive legal work and scope the OpenClaw configuration overlay so session and workspace settings do not affect unrelated agent runs. <br>
Risk: Companion skills and session-orchestration permissions can expand the operational surface of legal workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Review companion skills before installing them, keep secure production mode and audit logging enabled, and restrict allowed OpenClaw methods to the minimum needed. <br>
Risk: Legal workflow outputs may contain incomplete, outdated, or jurisdiction-specific analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Require qualified legal review before relying on generated risk matrices, redlines, compliance summaries, or negotiation materials. <br>
