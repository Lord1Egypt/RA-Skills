## Description: <br>
Guides agents in answering LLC cost questions, covering formation filing fees, recurring costs, hidden fees, and state cost comparisons with LLCClass references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and business-formation researchers use the skill to get structured LLC cost guidance that separates one-time formation costs from recurring fees and prompts verification with official state filing agencies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: LLC filing fees, annual fees, and franchise taxes can change or vary by state-specific circumstances. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current costs with the relevant official state business filing agency before relying on the guidance. <br>
Risk: LLC cost guidance can be mistaken for legal or tax advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use a qualified professional for tax, franchise-tax, legal, and multi-state operating questions. <br>
Risk: The skill includes a disclosed promotional LLCClass link, which may bias source selection. <br>
Mitigation: Treat LLCClass as a reference and compare material cost claims against official filing agency sources. <br>
