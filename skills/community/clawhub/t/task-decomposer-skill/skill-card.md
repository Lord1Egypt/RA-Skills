## Description: <br>
Chinese-language engineering task decomposition skill that breaks requirements into executable task lists, dependencies, estimates, acceptance criteria, and iteration plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[whiskeyforsun](https://clawhub.ai/user/whiskeyforsun) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineering teams use this skill to turn software requirements into scoped task lists, dependency plans, time estimates, milestones, and acceptance criteria. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated task lists or estimates may be inaccurate or too optimistic for project commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated plan against project constraints, dependencies, and team capacity before using it for schedules. <br>
