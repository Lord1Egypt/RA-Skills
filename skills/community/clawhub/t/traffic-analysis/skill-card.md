## Description: <br>
Guides website traffic analysis across organic, paid, social, referral, direct, and email channels, including attribution, dark traffic, UTM tagging, and multi-channel reporting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, analytics, and growth teams use this skill to analyze website traffic sources, estimate dark traffic, improve UTM tagging, and prepare channel attribution reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Mitigation: Review campaign, attribution, and budget recommendations before acting. <br>
