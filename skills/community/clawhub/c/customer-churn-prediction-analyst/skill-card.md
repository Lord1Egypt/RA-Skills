## Description: <br>
Analyze customer behavior patterns and predict churn risk across Stripe, Shopify, and SaaS platforms. Identify at-risk accounts, generate personalized intervention recommendations, and track win-back success. Use when the user needs to prevent customer attrition, prioritize retention efforts, or create targeted recovery campaigns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Customer success, retention, ecommerce, and SaaS operations teams use this skill to analyze customer behavior, rank churn risk, and prepare retention playbooks, campaign copy, and win-back recommendations. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill works with sensitive business and customer information from churn-analysis data sources. <br>
Mitigation: Use restricted or read-only API keys where possible, minimize or mask PII, and confirm legal basis and platform permissions before use. <br>
Risk: Generated emails, discounts, Slack alerts, or win-back actions could affect customers if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review recommendations and outreach before deployment, especially for high-value accounts or automated campaign workflows. <br>
