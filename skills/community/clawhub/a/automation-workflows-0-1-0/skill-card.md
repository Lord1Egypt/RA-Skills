### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automation workflows may read or change sensitive business data if connected with broad account permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Review each automation's data access, use the narrowest available permissions, and test with sample or sandbox data before enabling it. <br>
Risk: Customer-facing, payment, or business-record automations can cause incorrect messages, transactions, or updates if mappings are wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Verify field mappings, error handling, and rollback steps before enabling workflows that contact customers, move payment data, or update records. <br>
