## Description: <br>
Helps users plan and draft government digitalization PPTs with structured slide outlines, copy-ready page text, speaker notes, and visual guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[boboy-j](https://clawhub.ai/user/boboy-j) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
For sales consultants, solution architects, project managers, and presentation authors who need copy-ready PPT content for government digitalization solution pitches, planning proposals, and project status reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide confidential government, customer, or company materials while requesting copy-ready presentation content. <br>
Mitigation: Use trusted agent platforms and avoid submitting confidential materials unless the deployment environment is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Generated policy references, factual claims, figures, or compliance language may be inaccurate or unsuitable for formal presentations. <br>
Mitigation: Verify policy citations, numbers, security claims, and compliance wording with authoritative sources before external or official use. <br>
