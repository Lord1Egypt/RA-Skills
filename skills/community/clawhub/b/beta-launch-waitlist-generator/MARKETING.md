**Title:** Beta Launch & Waitlist Campaign Generator  
**Tagline:** Land on Product Hunt. Fill your waitlist. Convert beta users to paid — before launch day.  
**Category:** Product Marketing / Launch Strategy  
**Price:** $29 one-time | $19.99/month  

### Marketplace Description

Every product launch follows the same failure pattern: months of building, one quiet launch day, a few signups from friends, then silence.

The fix is boring and ignored: build an audience before you launch.

This skill gives you the complete pre-launch system:

**Prompt 1 (Free) — Waitlist Landing Page**  
6 headline variants (outcome, curiosity, pain, social proof, FOMO, bold claim) + benefit bullets + FAQ + email opt-in microcopy + meta title/description. Everything you need to launch a coming-soon page today.

**Prompt 2 — 6-Email Countdown Campaign**  
Day 0 confirmation → Day 3 problem → Day 7 behind-the-scenes → Day 14 social proof → Day 21 founder story → Launch day conversion. 3 subject line variants per email. A/B test guide included.

**Prompt 3 — Product Hunt Launch Kit**  
5 tagline variants with analysis. PH description (3 options). First comment (maker comment, 300-400 words). Hunter outreach email + DM. Upvote ask messages for warm, cold, and email audiences. Twitter/X launch thread (10 tweets). 24-hour war room playbook with hourly checklist and comment response scripts.

**Prompt 4 — Early Access Nurture + Upgrade Sequence**  
Day 0 activation email → Day 2 quick win → Day 7 power user tip → Day 14 upgrade ask (3 variants: outcome, social proof, urgency) → Day 30 anniversary + testimonial request + referral ask. Churn rescue sequence (2 emails) for users who go dark.

**Worked example included:** SnapFlow no-code workflow automation — all 4 prompts run end-to-end for a real product scenario.

## Target Buyer Personas

### Persona 1: Indie Hacker / Solo SaaS Founder
- Building in public on Twitter/X and Indie Hackers
- Has product almost ready, knows they need to "build in public" but hasn't built an audience
- Has seen Product Hunt launches and wants one, but doesn't know the playbook
- Price sensitivity: high — will pay $29, not $500
- **Trigger:** "How do I get my first 100 users?" / "How do I do a Product Hunt launch?"

### Persona 2: Startup Founder (Pre-Seed / Bootstrapped)
- 1-5 person team, launching in the next 30-90 days
- Needs a professional-looking launch but doesn't have budget for an agency
- Wants the Product Hunt playbook specifically
- **Trigger:** "We're launching next month and I don't have a plan"

### Persona 3: Course Creator / Community Builder
- Opening enrollment for a cohort course or paid community
- Already has an email list but doesn't have a launch sequence
- Needs the countdown campaign and early-access nurture — not the PH kit
- **Trigger:** "I'm opening enrollment in 6 weeks — how do I build excitement?"

### Persona 4: Agency Account Manager
- Runs product launches for SaaS clients
- Needs a templated system to deliver fast and consistently
- $29 is a rounding error vs. agency billing rate
- **Trigger:** Client says "we're launching in 6 weeks, can you handle it?"
