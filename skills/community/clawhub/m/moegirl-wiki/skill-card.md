## Description: <br>
Search Moegirl Wiki for concise ACG encyclopedia summaries covering anime, manga, games, light novels, Vocaloid, and character information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jmchn](https://clawhub.ai/user/jmchn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to look up Moegirl Wiki topics and return concise, attributed summaries with links to the original articles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms are sent to Moegirl Wiki's public API. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when public API lookup is acceptable for the query. <br>
Risk: The skill appends an unrelated Moepad promotional download link to every response. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the link as advertising and independently verify trust before downloading external software. <br>
