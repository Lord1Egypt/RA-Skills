## Description: <br>
Provides backend architecture guidance, examples, and scaffolds for Clean Architecture, Hexagonal Architecture, and Domain-Driven Design. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wpank](https://clawhub.ai/user/wpank) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and backend engineers use this skill to choose, explain, and apply architecture patterns when designing new services, refactoring monoliths, setting team standards, or creating testable backend codebases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payment and credential-related template snippets could be copied into a real service without production safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Treat these snippets as teaching templates; add test-mode defaults, credential handling, operational safeguards, and review before adapting them to production code. <br>
Risk: Architecture patterns can be over-applied to simple systems. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included decision framework and avoid Clean Architecture, Hexagonal Architecture, or DDD where a simpler CRUD structure is sufficient. <br>
