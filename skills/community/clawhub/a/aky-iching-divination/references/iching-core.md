# I Ching 64 Hexagrams (周易六十四卦)

The 64 hexagrams (卦) are the core of the Zhouyi (周易). Each consists of:
- **卦名** (Hexagram name)
- **卦象** (Hexagram symbol — upper/lower trigrams)
- **卦辞** (Judgment — text attributed to King Wen)
- **彖传** (Commentary on the judgment — part of the Ten Wings)
- **象传** (Image commentary)
- **核心含义** (Core meaning)

---

## Upper Canon (上经) — 30 Hexagrams

### 1. ䷀ 乾 (Qián) — The Creative, Heaven
- **卦辞:** 元亨利贞 (Supreme success, favorable through perseverance)
- **象:** 天行健，君子以自强不息 (Heaven moves with strength; the noble person strives unceasingly)
- **核心:** Pure yang, creativity, strength, leadership, initiative
- **体用定位:** 体健用刚，主导地位

### 2. ䷁ 坤 (Kūn) — The Receptive, Earth
- **卦辞:** 元亨，利牝马之贞 (Supreme success, favorable through the perseverance of a mare)
- **象:** 地势坤，君子以厚德载物 (Earth's condition is receptive; the noble person carries all things with deep virtue)
- **核心:** Pure yin, receptivity, devotion, nurturing, support
- **体用定位:** 体柔用顺，配合地位

### 3. ䷂ 屯 (Zhūn) — Difficulty at the Beginning
- **卦辞:** 元亨利贞，勿用有攸往，利建侯 (Supreme success — but do not act recklessly; beneficial to establish helpers)
- **象:** 云雷屯，君子以经纶 (Clouds and thunder — difficulty at beginning; the noble person brings order out of chaos)
- **核心:** Initial difficulty, birth pangs, gathering forces, need patience

### 4. ䷃ 蒙 (Méng) — Youthful Folly / The Young Shoot
- **卦辞:** 亨，匪我求童蒙，童蒙求我 (Success — not I seek the young fool, the young fool seeks me)
- **象:** 山下出泉，蒙；君子以果行育德 (A spring emerges from a mountain — youthful folly; the noble person acts decisively and cultivates virtue)
- **核心:** Inexperience, seeking guidance, education, opening of mind

### 5. ䷄ 需 (Xū) — Waiting / Nourishment
- **卦辞:** 有孚，光亨，贞吉，利涉大川 (With sincerity, success; perseverance brings good fortune; favorable to cross great rivers)
- **象:** 云上于天，需；君子以饮食宴乐 (Clouds rising to heaven — waiting; the noble person eats and drinks and enjoys life)
- **核心:** Patient waiting, timing, preparation, nourishing strength

### 6. ䷅ 讼 (Sòng) — Conflict / Lawsuit
- **卦辞:** 有孚窒，惕中吉，终凶 (With sincerity yet blocked — caution brings good fortune, pressing to the end brings misfortune)
- **象:** 天与水违行，讼；君子以作事谋始 (Heaven and water go in opposite directions — conflict; the noble person plans carefully at the start)
- **核心:** Disagreement, litigation, confrontation, avoid escalation

### 7. ䷆ 师 (Shī) — The Army / Leadership
- **卦辞:** 贞丈人吉，无咎 (Perseverance with a mature leader brings good fortune, no blame)
- **象:** 地中有水，师；君子以容民畜众 (Water in the earth — the army; the noble person is generous toward the people and nurtures the masses)
- **核心:** Collective action, organized force, leadership, authority

### 8. ䷇ 比 (Bǐ) — Union / Holding Together
- **卦辞:** 吉，原筮元永贞，无咎 (Good fortune — consult the oracle again; supreme and lasting perseverance, no blame)
- **象:** 地上有水，比；先王以建万国亲诸侯 (Water on the earth — union; the ancient kings established relationships and alliances)
- **核心:** Alliance, harmony, mutual support, belonging

### 9. ䷈ 小畜 (Xiǎo Chù) — Small Restraint / Taming Power of the Small
- **卦辞:** 亨，密云不雨，自我西郊 (Success — dense clouds but no rain, from the western border)
- **象:** 风行天上，小畜；君子以懿文德 (Wind moving above heaven — small restraint; the noble person cultivates refined virtue)
- **核心:** Temporary restraint, accumulation, preparing, minor obstacles

### 10. ䷉ 履 (Lǚ) — Treading / Conduct
- **卦辞:** 履虎尾，不咥人，亨 (Treading on the tiger's tail — it does not bite; success)
- **象:** 上天下泽，履；君子以辨上下定民志 (Heaven above, lake below — treading; the noble person distinguishes high and low to settle the people's will)
- **核心:** Cautious conduct, treading carefully, proper behavior

### 11. ䷊ 泰 (Tài) — Peace / Prosperity
- **卦辞:** 小往大来，吉亨 (The small departs, the great approaches; good fortune and success)
- **象:** 天地交泰；后以财成天地之道 (Heaven and earth in communion — peace; the ruler shapes the way of heaven and earth)
- **核心:** Harmony, balance, smooth progress, union of opposites
- **体用定位:** 天地交泰，万事通达

### 12. ䷋ 否 (Pǐ) — Standstill / Obstruction
- **卦辞:** 否之匪人，不利君子贞，大往小来 (Obstruction — not favorable for the noble person's perseverance; the great departs, the small approaches)
- **象:** 天地不交，否；君子以俭德辟难 (Heaven and earth not in communion — obstruction; the noble person withdraws inwardly to avoid difficulty)
- **核心:** Stagnation, separation, retreat, wait for change

### 13. ䷌ 同人 (Tóng Rén) — Fellowship / Gathering Among People
- **卦辞:** 同人于野，亨，利涉大川，利君子贞 (Fellowship in the open — success, favorable to cross great rivers, favorable for the noble person)
- **象:** 天与火，同人；君子以类族辨物 (Heaven with fire — fellowship; the noble person organizes groups and distinguishes things)
- **核心:** Unity, cooperation, shared purpose, universal love

### 14. ䷍ 大有 (Dà Yǒu) — Great Possession / Abundance
- **卦辞:** 元亨 (Supreme success)
- **象:** 火在天上，大有；君子以遏恶扬善顺天休命 (Fire in heaven — great possession; the noble person curbs evil and promotes good)
- **核心:** Great abundance, wealth of resources, generosity, enlightenment

### 15. ䷎ 谦 (Qiān) — Modesty
- **卦辞:** 亨，君子有终 (Success — the noble person carries things through to completion)
- **象:** 地中有山，谦；君子以裒多益寡 (Mountain in the earth — modesty; the noble person reduces what is excessive and increases what is insufficient)
- **核心:** Humility, modesty in success, balancing excess and deficiency

### 16. ䷏ 豫 (Yù) — Enthusiasm
- **卦辞:** 利建侯行师 (Favorable to establish helpers and deploy armies)
- **象:** 雷出地奋，豫；先王以作乐崇德 (Thunder comes bursting from the earth — enthusiasm; the ancient kings created music to honor virtue)
- **核心:** Joyful energy, inspired action, timing, preparation

### 17. ䷐ 随 (Suí) — Following
- **卦辞:** 元亨利贞，无咎 (Supreme success; favorable through perseverance; no blame)
- **象:** 泽中有雷，随；君子以向晦入宴息 (Thunder in the lake — following; at dusk the noble person goes indoors to rest)
- **核心:** Adaptability, following the right leader, compliance with natural rhythms

### 18. ䷑ 蛊 (Gǔ) — Decay / Rectifying the Spoiled
- **卦辞:** 元亨，利涉大川；先甲三日，后甲三日 (Supreme success; favorable to cross great rivers; three days before and after the beginning)
- **象:** 山下有风，蛊；君子以振民育德 (Wind at the foot of a mountain — decay; the noble person rouses the people and nourishes virtue)
- **核心:** Correcting corruption, reforming from within, turning decay around

### 19. ䷒ 临 (Lín) — Approach / Overseeing
- **卦辞:** 元亨利贞，至于八月有凶 (Supreme success — but by the eighth month there will be misfortune)
- **象:** 泽上有地，临；君子以教思无穷，容保民无疆 (Earth above the lake — approach; the noble person's teaching and thought are inexhaustible)
- **核心:** Approaching a critical juncture, leadership, timely action

### 20. ䷓ 观 (Guān) — Contemplation / Viewing
- **卦辞:** 盥而不荐，有孚颙若 (Washing hands but not yet offering — sincere and reverent)
- **象:** 风行地上，观；先王以省方观民设教 (Wind moving over the earth — contemplation; the ancient kings inspected the regions and taught the people)
- **核心:** Observation, self-reflection, gaining perspective, assessment

### 21. ䷔ 噬嗑 (Shì Kè) — Biting Through
- **卦辞:** 亨，利用狱 (Success — favorable for legal proceedings)
- **象:** 雷电噬嗑；先王以明罚敕法 (Thunder and lightning — biting through; the ancient kings made penalties clear and enforced the laws)
- **核心:** Removing obstacles, decisive action, judgment, legal matters

### 22. ䷕ 贲 (Bì) — Grace / Adornment
- **卦辞:** 亨，小利有攸往 (Success — small benefit in going forward)
- **象:** 山下有火，贲；君子以明庶政无敢折狱 (Fire at the foot of a mountain — grace; the noble person clarifies administrative matters but dares not decide legal cases)
- **核心:** Beauty, adornment, refinement, but not substance

### 23. ䷖ 剥 (Bō) — Splitting Apart / Peeling
- **卦辞:** 不利有攸往 (Not favorable to go anywhere)
- **象:** 山附于地，剥；上以厚下安宅 (The mountain rests on the earth — splitting apart; those above ensure security for those below)
- **核心:** Decline, decay, the end of a cycle, stripping away the false

### 24. ䷗ 复 (Fù) — Return / Turning Point
- **卦辞:** 亨，出入无疾，朋来无咎 (Success — going out and coming in without error, friends come without blame)
- **象:** 雷在地中，复；先王以至日闭关 (Thunder in the earth — return; the ancient kings closed the passes at the solstice)
- **核心:** Rebirth, renewal, the turning point, the return of light after darkness

### 25. ䷘ 无妄 (Wú Wàng) — Innocence / Unexpected
- **卦辞:** 元亨利贞，其匪正有眚，不利有攸往 (Supreme success — but if not correct there will be calamity, not favorable to go forward)
- **象:** 天下雷行，物与无妄；先王以茂对时育万物 (Thunder moves under heaven — innocence; the ancient kings nurtured all beings in accord with the seasons)
- **核心:** Natural authenticity, unexpected events, acting without ulterior motive

### 26. ䷙ 大畜 (Dà Chù) — Great Restraint / Taming Power of the Great
- **卦辞:** 利贞，不家食吉，利涉大川 (Favorable through perseverance; not eating at home brings good fortune; favorable to cross great rivers)
- **象:** 天在山中，大畜；君子以多识前言往行以畜其德 (Heaven in the mountain — great restraint; the noble person accumulates virtue by studying past deeds)
- **核心:** Accumulation, cultivation, immense potential waiting to be released

### 27. ䷚ 颐 (Yí) — Nourishment / The Corners of the Mouth
- **卦辞:** 贞吉，观颐，自求口实 (Perseverance brings good fortune; watch how you nourish yourself and seek your own food)
- **象:** 山下有雷，颐；君子以慎言语节饮食 (Thunder at the foot of a mountain — nourishment; the noble person is careful with words and moderate in eating)
- **核心:** Self-nurture, seeking sustenance, proper care of body and spirit

### 28. ䷛ 大过 (Dà Guò) — Great Crossing / Excess
- **卦辞:** 栋桡，利有攸往，亨 (The ridgepole sags — favorable to go forward; success)
- **象:** 泽灭木，大过；君子以独立不惧 (The lake rises above the tree — great crossing; the noble person stands alone without fear)
- **核心:** Great pressure, extraordinary measures, crisis requiring bold action

### 29. ䷜ 坎 (Kǎn) — The Abyss / Water Repeated
- **卦辞:** 有孚维心亨，行有尚 (With sincerity in the heart, success; action brings reward)
- **象:** 水洊至，习坎；君子以常德行习教事 (Water flowing continuously — the abyss; the noble person maintains virtue and teaches)
- **核心:** Danger, obstacles, repeated trials, maintaining heart-sincerity through adversity

### 30. ䷝ 离 (Lí) — Fire / Clinging / Radiance
- **卦辞:** 利贞，亨 (Favorable through perseverance; success)
- **象:** 明两作，离；大人以继明照于四方 (Light rising twice — fire; the great person illuminates the four directions)
- **核心:** Clarity, attachment, radiance, dependence on what is illuminating

## Lower Canon (下经) — 34 Hexagrams

### 31. ䷞ 咸 (Xián) — Influence / Mutual Attraction
- **卦辞:** 亨，利贞，取女吉 (Success; favorable through perseverance; taking a maiden brings good fortune)
- **象:** 山上有泽，咸；君子以虚受人 (A lake on a mountain — influence; the noble person receives others with an empty heart)
- **核心:** Attraction, relationship, receptivity, the beginning of courtship

### 32. ䷟ 恒 (Héng) — Duration / Constancy
- **卦辞:** 亨，无咎，利贞，利有攸往 (Success, no blame, favorable through perseverance, favorable to go forward)
- **象:** 雷风，恒；君子以立不易方 (Thunder and wind — duration; the noble person stands firm without changing direction)
- **核心:** Lasting relationships, perseverance through time, stability

### 33. ䷠ 遁 (Dùn) — Retreat
- **卦辞:** 亨，小利贞 (Success — small matters bring favorable perseverance)
- **象:** 天下有山，遁；君子以远小人不恶而严 (Mountain under heaven — retreat; the noble person keeps distance from petty people without showing hostility)
- **核心:** Strategic withdrawal, knowing when to yield, timing of retreat

### 34. ䷡ 大壮 (Dà Zhuàng) — Great Strength
- **卦辞:** 利贞 (Favorable through perseverance)
- **象:** 雷在天上，大壮；君子以非礼弗履 (Thunder in heaven — great strength; the noble person does not tread where there is no propriety)
- **核心:** Power at its peak, strength tempered by righteousness, not abusing power

### 35. ䷢ 晋 (Jìn) — Progress / Advance
- **卦辞:** 康侯用锡马蕃庶，昼日三接 (The noble lord is rewarded with many horses and received three times in a day)
- **象:** 明出地上，晋；君子以自昭明德 (Light rising over the earth — progress; the noble person reveals their own bright virtue)
- **核心:** Advancement, recognition, upward movement, being seen

### 36. ䷣ 明夷 (Míng Yí) — Darkening of the Light
- **卦辞:** 利艰贞 (Favorable through difficulty and perseverance)
- **象:** 明入地中，明夷；君子以莅众用晦而明 (Light hidden in the earth — darkening of light; the noble person governs by not being too obvious)
- **核心:** Adversity, concealment of wisdom, enduring hardship, hidden virtue

### 37. ䷤ 家人 (Jiā Rén) — The Family / Clan
- **卦辞:** 利女贞 (Favorable for the woman's perseverance)
- **象:** 风自火出，家人；君子以言有物而行有恒 (Wind from fire — the family; the noble person speaks with substance and acts with constancy)
- **核心:** Family relationships, domestic order, proper roles, harmony in the home

### 38. ䷥ 睽 (Kuí) — Opposition / Divergence
- **卦辞:** 小事吉 (Small matters bring good fortune)
- **象:** 上火下泽，睽；君子以同而异 (Fire above, lake below — opposition; the noble person unites in common ground while respecting differences)
- **核心:** Disagreement, estrangement, differences, finding unity amid diversity

### 39. ䷦ 蹇 (Jiǎn) — Obstruction / Difficulty
- **卦辞:** 利西南，不利东北，利见大人，贞吉 (Favorable in the southwest, not favorable in the northeast; favorable to see the great person; perseverance brings good fortune)
- **象:** 山上有水，蹇；君子以反身修德 (Water on the mountain — difficulty; the noble person turns inward and cultivates virtue)
- **核心:** Mountainous obstacles, confronting difficulties, self-reflection

### 40. ䷧ 解 (Xiè) — Release / Deliverance
- **卦辞:** 利西南，无所往其来复吉，有攸往夙吉 (Favorable in the southwest; if nothing to do, returning is good; if action needed, early action is good)
- **象:** 雷雨作，解；君子以赦过宥罪 (Thunder and rain — release; the noble person pardons mistakes and forgives offenses)
- **核心:** Release from tension, resolving problems, timely liberation

### 41. ䷨ 损 (Sǔn) — Decrease
- **卦辞:** 有孚，元吉，无咎，可贞，利有攸往 (With sincerity, supreme good fortune, no blame, favorable to go forward)
- **象:** 山下有泽，损；君子以惩忿窒欲 (A lake at the foot of a mountain — decrease; the noble person curbs anger and restrains desire)
- **核心:** Loss, sacrifice, reduction, letting go of the superfluous

### 42. ䷩ 益 (Yì) — Increase
- **卦辞:** 利有攸往，利涉大川 (Favorable to go forward; favorable to cross great rivers)
- **象:** 风雷，益；君子以见善则迁有过则改 (Wind and thunder — increase; the noble person follows every good they see and corrects every fault)
- **核心:** Gain, abundance, benefiting others, growth through improvement

### 43. ䷪ 夬 (Guài) — Breakthrough / Parting
- **卦辞:** 扬于王庭，孚号有厉，告自邑不利即戎 (Manifest in the royal court; sincere proclamation but danger; better to resolve internally than use force)
- **象:** 泽上于天，夬；君子以施禄及下 (The lake rises to heaven — breakthrough; the noble person bestows blessings upon those below)
- **核心:** Decisive action, a critical moment of change, separating from negativity

### 44. ䷫ 姤 (Gòu) — Meeting / Coming to Meet
- **卦辞:** 女壮勿用取女 (A strong woman — do not take her as a wife)
- **象:** 天下有风，姤；后以施命诰四方 (Wind under heaven — meeting; the ruler issues commands and proclaims them to the four quarters)
- **核心:** Unexpected encounter, attraction, cautious engagement with the new

### 45. ䷬ 萃 (Cuì) — Gathering Together
- **卦辞:** 亨，王假有庙，利见大人 (Success; the king approaches the temple; favorable to see the great person)
- **象:** 泽上于地，萃；君子以除戎器戒不虞 (The lake rises above the earth — gathering; the noble person prepares for unexpected events)
- **核心:** Gathering, community, concentration of resources, group purpose

### 46. ䷭ 升 (Shēng) — Ascending / Pushing Upward
- **卦辞:** 元亨，用见大人，勿恤 (Supreme success; use this to see the great person; do not worry)
- **象:** 地中升木，升；君子以顺德积小以高大 (Wood growing from the earth — ascending; the noble person accumulates small virtues to achieve greatness)
- **核心:** Gradual rise, advancement, steady progress upward

### 47. ䷮ 困 (Kùn) — Oppression / Exhaustion
- **卦辞:** 亨，贞大人吉，无咎 (Success; perseverance of the great person brings good fortune, no blame)
- **象:** 泽无水，困；君子以致命遂志 (A lake without water — oppression; the noble person stakes their life on their principles)
- **核心:** Exhaustion, depletion, being trapped, finding inner strength

### 48. ䷯ 井 (Jǐng) — The Well
- **卦辞:** 改邑不改井，无丧无得，往来井井 (Towns change but the well remains — nothing lost, nothing gained; people come and go drawing from the well)
- **象:** 木上有水，井；君子以劳民劝相 (Wood above water — the well; the noble person encourages the people and helps one another)
- **核心:** Source of nourishment, unchanging foundation, community resource

### 49. ䷰ 革 (Gé) — Revolution / Molting
- **卦辞:** 己日乃孚，元亨利贞悔亡 (On your own day, sincerity; supreme success; regret disappears)
- **象:** 泽中有火，革；君子以治历明时 (Fire in the lake — revolution; the noble person regulates the calendar and clarifies the seasons)
- **核心:** Radical change, revolution, timely transformation, the snake sheds its skin

### 50. ䷱ 鼎 (Dǐng) — The Cauldron / Establishing the New
- **卦辞:** 元吉，亨 (Supreme good fortune; success)
- **象:** 木上有火，鼎；君子以正位凝命 (Wood over fire — the cauldron; the noble person takes their proper position and fulfills their destiny)
- **核心:** Establishing what is new, nourishment through civilization, the vessel of culture

### 51. ䷲ 震 (Zhèn) — Arousing / Thunder
- **卦辞:** 亨，震来虩虩笑言哑哑，震惊百里不丧匕鬯 (Success — thunder comes and causes trembling, but later laughter; it shocks a hundred miles but the sacrificial spoon and wine are not lost)
- **象:** 洊雷，震；君子以恐惧修省 (Repeated thunder — arousing; the noble person examines themselves with reverence)
- **核心:** Sudden shock, awakening, crisis, maintaining composure through turmoil

### 52. ䷳ 艮 (Gèn) — Keeping Still / Mountain
- **卦辞:** 艮其背不获其身，行其庭不见其人，无咎 (Keeping still in the back, no awareness of the self; walking in the courtyard, not seeing the person — no blame)
- **象:** 兼山，艮；君子以思不出其位 (Double mountain — keeping still; the noble person does not let their thoughts go beyond their position)
- **核心:** Stillness, meditation, knowing when to stop, inner peace

### 53. ䷴ 渐 (Jiàn) — Gradual Progress / Development
- **卦辞:** 女归吉，利贞 (The maiden's marriage brings good fortune; favorable through perseverance)
- **象:** 山上有木，渐；君子以居贤德善俗 (Wood on a mountain — gradual progress; the noble person cultivates virtue and improves customs)
- **核心:** Gradual advance, organic development, patient growth

### 54. ䷵ 归妹 (Guī Mèi) — Converting the Maiden
- **卦辞:** 征凶，无攸利 (Action brings misfortune; nothing is favorable)
- **象:** 泽上有雷，归妹；君子以永终知敝 (Thunder over the lake — converting the maiden; the noble person understands the end from the beginning)
- **核心:** Mismatched union, forced relationship, acting out of proper order

### 55. ䷶ 丰 (Fēng) — Abundance / Fullness
- **卦辞:** 亨，王假之勿忧，宜日中 (Success; the king reaches it; do not worry; it's good to be at midday)
- **象:** 雷电皆至，丰；君子以折狱致刑 (Thunder and lightning together — abundance; the noble person judges cases and executes penalties)
- **核心:** Fullness of time, prosperity at its peak, the noon sun must decline

### 56. ䷷ 旅 (Lǚ) — The Wanderer / Travel
- **卦辞:** 小亨，旅贞吉 (Small success; the wanderer's perseverance brings good fortune)
- **象:** 山上有火，旅；君子以明慎用刑而不留狱 (Fire on a mountain — the wanderer; the noble person is clear and cautious in judgment)
- **核心:** Traveling, temporary circumstances, life as a sojourner, adaptability

### 57. ䷸ 巽 (Xùn) — Gentle / Wind
- **卦辞:** 小亨，利有攸往，利见大人 (Small success; favorable to go forward; favorable to see the great person)
- **象:** 随风，巽；君子以申命行事 (Wind following wind — gentleness; the noble person spreads commands and carries out affairs)
- **核心:** Gentle influence, penetration, following, adaptability

### 58. ䷹ 兑 (Duì) — Joy / Lake
- **卦辞:** 亨，利贞 (Success; favorable through perseverance)
- **象:** 丽泽，兑；君子以朋友讲习 (Two lakes — joy; the noble person discusses and practices with friends)
- **核心:** Joy, open communication, pleasant exchange, the power of happiness

### 59. ䷺ 涣 (Huàn) — Dispersion / Dissolution
- **卦辞:** 亨，王假有庙，利涉大川利贞 (Success; the king approaches the temple; favorable to cross great rivers; favorable through perseverance)
- **象:** 风行水上，涣；先王以享于帝立庙 (Wind moving over water — dispersion; the ancient kings made offerings and established temples)
- **核心:** Dispersal of what has accumulated, dissolving obstacles, restoring clarity

### 60. ䷻ 节 (Jié) — Limitation / Regulation
- **卦辞:** 亨，苦节不可贞 (Success — but bitter limitation cannot be maintained through perseverance)
- **象:** 泽上有水，节；君子以制数度议德行 (Water over a lake — limitation; the noble person establishes standards and evaluates conduct)
- **核心:** Moderation, limits, discipline, the necessity of boundaries

### 61. ䷼ 中孚 (Zhōng Fú) — Inner Truth / Central Sincerity
- **卦辞:** 豚鱼吉，利涉大川，利贞 (Pigs and fish — good fortune; favorable to cross great rivers; favorable through perseverance)
- **象:** 泽上有风，中孚；君子以议狱缓死 (Wind over a lake — inner truth; the noble person deliberates on cases and delays executions)
- **核心:** Sincerity, integrity, inner truth reaching outward, trust

### 62. ䷽ 小过 (Xiǎo Guò) — Small Excess / Small Crossing
- **卦辞:** 亨，利贞，可小事不可大事 (Success; favorable through perseverance; small matters are favorable, not great matters)
- **象:** 山上有雷，小过；君子以行过乎恭丧过乎哀用过乎俭 (Thunder on a mountain — small excess; the noble person is extra respectful, sorrowful, and frugal)
- **核心:** Small transgressions, exceeding slightly, going beyond in minor ways

### 63. ䷾ 既济 (Jì Jì) — Already Completed / After Completion
- **卦辞:** 亨小，利贞，初吉终乱 (Small success; favorable through perseverance; good at the beginning, disorder at the end)
- **象:** 水在火上，既济；君子以思患而豫防之 (Water over fire — already completed; the noble person anticipates problems and prevents them)
- **核心:** Completion, achievement, but vigilance is needed — the end of one cycle

### 64. ䷿ 未济 (Wèi Jì) — Not Yet Completed / Before Completion
- **卦辞:** 亨，小狐汔济濡其尾，无攸利 (Success — the young fox almost crosses the river but gets its tail wet; nothing favorable)
- **象:** 火在水上，未济；君子以慎辨物居方 (Fire over water — not yet completed; the noble person carefully distinguishes things and places them properly)
- **核心:** Transition, incompletion, the future is still unfolding — the last hexagram signifies new beginnings
