# Claim Preparation Checklist

Use this checklist when preparing claims for Adjudication or Arbitration. Thorough preparation significantly improves outcomes.

---

**Project:** _______________________________ **Claim Type:** _______________

## Phase 1: Strategy & Planning

- [ ] Reviewed the contract dispute resolution clause
- [ ] Completed the 7-Dimension Strategy Worksheet
- [ ] Identified the main heads of claim (EOT, disruption, variation, prolongation, acceleration)
- [ ] Decided whether to submit claims separately or combined

## Phase 2: Evidence Collection

- [ ] Compiled a clear chronology of events
- [ ] Collected and organised all relevant correspondence
- [ ] Gathered all notices with proof of submission
- [ ] Collected programme, progress, and site records
- [ ] Gathered cost and quantum supporting documents
- [ ] Identified and addressed any gaps in records

## Phase 3: Claim Development

- [ ] Prepared a clear summary of the claim
- [ ] Developed Primary and Fallback arguments
- [ ] Prepared quantum calculation with clear methodology
- [ ] Identified and prepared witnesses, if required
- [ ] Engaged experts (delay analyst, quantum expert, technical expert) if needed

## Phase 4: Risk Assessment & Review

- [ ] Conducted honest Risk Assessment of own case
- [ ] Identified likely counter-arguments from the other side
- [ ] Reviewed and strengthened weak areas
- [ ] Finalised disclosure strategy

## Phase 5: Final Preparation & Submission

- [ ] Prepared position paper / submissions
- [ ] Organised document bundle, indexed and paginated
- [ ] Prepared opening statement / summary
- [ ] Conducted internal review
- [ ] Submitted on time

## Adjudication-Specific Items

- [ ] Kept submissions concise and focused
- [ ] Prioritised speed and clarity

## Arbitration-Specific Items

- [ ] Prepared more detailed and comprehensive submissions
- [ ] Arranged witness statements and expert evidence
- [ ] Planned for possible hearings
