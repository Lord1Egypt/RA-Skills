## Description: <br>
Guides display advertising across ad networks, banner formats, programmatic buying, metrics, creative requirements, UTM tagging, and pre-launch checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, growth operators, and agencies use this skill to plan display, banner, native, video pre-roll, rich media, mobile interstitial, and retargeting campaigns before launching them in ad platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Display advertising guidance could be applied to live budgets, targeting, tracking, or platform settings without enough review. <br>
Mitigation: Review campaign budgets, targeting, tracking, creative, and platform actions separately before making real campaign changes. <br>
