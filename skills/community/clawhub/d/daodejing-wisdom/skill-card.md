## Description: <br>
道德经智慧 is a bilingual Chinese and English guide that applies Laozi's Daodejing themes, including wu-wei, softness, dialectical thinking, non-contention, humility, strategy, leadership, and personal reflection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to frame strategy, leadership, conflict, negotiation, and mindset questions through Daodejing principles. It is a reflection and decision-framing aid, not a substitute for technical, legal, medical, emergency, or mental-health support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad philosophy, strategy, management, or mindset wording when the user actually needs specialist help. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a general reflection aid and redirect to technical, legal, medical, emergency, or mental-health support when the request calls for expert or urgent guidance. <br>
Risk: Daodejing-style framing can be mistaken for passivity or a guaranteed practical answer. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision-framing guidance, review recommendations against the user's real constraints, and avoid relying on the skill as the sole basis for consequential decisions. <br>
