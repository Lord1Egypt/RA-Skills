## Description: <br>
Writes personalized cold emails that actually get replies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External and employee users can use this skill to draft concise, personalized cold outreach and follow-up sequences from recipient details, offer context, target pain points, and a single call to action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recipient, company, or personalization details may be sensitive when shared with an agent. <br>
Mitigation: Review any recipient or company details before providing them to the skill. <br>
Risk: Generated outreach may contain inaccurate personalization, unsuitable claims, or messaging that should not be sent as-is. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review and approve each email and follow-up before sending. <br>
Risk: Linked context packs and companion skills are separate resources from this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate linked resources separately before visiting, installing, or using them. <br>
