## Description: <br>
Write detailed, academically rigorous Chinese results chapters or results sections for theses, dissertations, and undergraduate papers from a user's quantitative data, tables, statistical outputs, research questions, hypotheses, qualitative themes, interview excerpts, case summaries, or extra information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and academic writers use this skill to draft, expand, polish, organize, or structure Chinese thesis results chapters from user-supplied quantitative data, qualitative findings, or mixed-methods evidence. It is limited to objective results reporting and should not write literature review, methodology, discussion, recommendations, or conclusion chapters. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research data, interview excerpts, or participant details may contain sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: De-identify sensitive material before use and follow institutional rules for AI-assisted academic writing. <br>
Risk: Generated wording, statistics, or results structure may diverge from the actual source data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify generated prose and all statistics, tables, quotations, themes, and claims against the real findings before submission. <br>
