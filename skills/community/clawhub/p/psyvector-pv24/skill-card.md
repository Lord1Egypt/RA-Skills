## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Common business terms such as cooperation, resources, negotiation, win-win, or interests may activate the persona unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented exit phrases, such as exiting the role or switching back to normal mode, when persona behavior is not desired. <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential business details while seeking negotiation or partnership analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential business information unless the user intentionally wants the agent to use it for negotiation analysis. <br>
Risk: Negotiation recommendations may be incomplete or speculative because the skill cannot know a counterparty's true bottom line. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance and have a qualified human review strategic, legal, or commercial decisions before acting. <br>
