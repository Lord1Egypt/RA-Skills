## Description: <br>
Ai Tattoo Design provides tattoo style, design workflow, body placement, artist selection, hygiene, aftercare, and cover-up guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to explore tattoo styles, plan designs and placements, prepare questions for tattoo artists, and understand general hygiene, aftercare, and cover-up considerations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tattoo hygiene, aftercare, allergy, and medical-condition guidance may be too general for an individual user's health context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the guidance as general information and confirm important health, allergy, aftercare, or legal questions with a qualified tattoo artist or clinician. <br>
