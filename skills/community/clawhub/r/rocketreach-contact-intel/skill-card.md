## Description: <br>
Search people and companies, inspect contact intelligence, and review RocketReach company signals including funding, growth, size, and tech stack. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hith3sh](https://clawhub.ai/user/hith3sh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, sales teams, recruiters, and developers use this skill to research people and companies through a connected RocketReach account, confirm lookup targets, and produce contact-intelligence guidance or tool calls. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: RocketReach lookups may consume API credits or return personal contact data. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm each lookup target, check account state before bulk use, and avoid storing or sharing personal data without consent and a legitimate purpose. <br>
Risk: The skill requires installing and allowing the ClawLink plugin plus connecting a RocketReach account. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the user accepts the ClawLink setup, OpenClaw allow-list change, gateway restart, and RocketReach account connection. <br>
