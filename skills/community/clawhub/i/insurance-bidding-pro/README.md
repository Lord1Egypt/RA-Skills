# Insurance Industry Bidding Expert / 保险行业投标专家#

> **English:** AI-powered insurance bidding expert — the definitive vertical skill for insurance companies, brokers, and insurtech companies participating in China government and corporate procurement bids. Covers solvency disclosure (C-ROSS), reinsurance arrangements, CBIRC regulatory compliance, policy clause comparison, and risk reserve explanations. Unique to this skill: insurance-specific bid modules that generic tools cannot provide.

**Keywords:** insurance bid, group insurance bid, solvency, reinsurance, CBIRC compliance, China insurance, insurance tender

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Insurance-Specific Bid Modules** — solvency disclosure, reinsurance arrangements, claims service network
- ✅ **Policy Clause Comparison** — compares main vs. additional coverage, different companies' products, old vs. new terms
- ✅ **CBIRC Compliance Check** — automated checking of Insurance Law Art.89-118, C-ROSS Rules 1-20, Reinsurance Management Regulations
- ✅ **Group Insurance Specialization** — group health, enterprise annuity, key person insurance, employee benefit design
- ✅ **Elimination Risk Check (Insurance)** — license validity, solvency ratio, reinsurance compliance, policy prohibition clauses
