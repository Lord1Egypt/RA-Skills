## Description: <br>
Wanxiang Scroll Creation helps agents produce Chinese web fiction, including content-generation workflows, platform adaptation, and quality-control guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dandanllab](https://clawhub.ai/user/dandanllab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and agents use this skill to draft, outline, expand, adapt, and edit Chinese web fiction for platforms such as Zhihu, Qidian, and Jinjiang. It also provides quality checks and style guidance for making generated prose read more naturally. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes guidance for making AI-written text appear human and reducing detector confidence. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only where platform rules, academic policies, client requirements, and disclosure obligations allow AI-assisted writing and editing. <br>
Risk: Outputs may be adapted for publication on specific writing platforms and could conflict with those platforms' content or disclosure rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated or edited content against the target platform's current rules before publication. <br>
