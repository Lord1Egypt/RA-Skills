## Description: <br>
Designs personalized resilience-building practices for adversity navigation and psychological recovery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals, teams, parents, and other external users use this skill to assess resilience resources and generate self-reflection plans for stress, adversity, setbacks, burnout, and recovery. It is intended for personal development and educational guidance, not therapy, crisis support, medical advice, legal advice, or other professional care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake resilience guidance for therapy, crisis support, medical advice, legal advice, or other professional care. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill positioned as self-reflection and personal development; direct users who feel unsafe, hopeless, suicidal, unable to cope, or in medical or mental-health crisis to local emergency services, a crisis hotline, or a licensed professional. <br>
