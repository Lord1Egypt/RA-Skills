## Description: <br>
Search and access China wholesale products, factories, suppliers, top rankings, and request quotations on Alibaba with tracking parameter traffic_type=ags_llm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sourcing teams, procurement staff, and agents use this skill to search Alibaba products, inspect supplier information, browse top-ranking products, and prepare RFQ workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use a logged-in Alibaba session to inspect supplier messages and draft or send supplier inquiries. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when that account access is acceptable, and review each outgoing supplier message before approval. <br>
Risk: Supplier conversation notes may contain sensitive business data. <br>
Mitigation: Treat local supplier conversation notes as sensitive procurement data and store or share them according to internal policy. <br>
