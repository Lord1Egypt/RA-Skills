## Description: <br>
Helps project managers and PMO analysts update RAID logs from project inputs and draft stakeholder status reports with delta markers and proposed RAG ratings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Project managers, program managers, and PMO analysts use this skill to triage reporting-period inputs into a current RAID log and draft a sponsor-review status report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential project, vendor, customer, personnel, legal, or regulatory information may be included in source notes or status drafts. <br>
Mitigation: Sanitize sensitive details before pasting inputs, keep personnel issues at role level, and review drafts before stakeholder sharing. <br>
Risk: Draft RAG ratings and issue closures can mislead stakeholders if published without human review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat output as PM and sponsor review material only, require explicit resolution evidence before closing risks or issues, and leave the published RAG decision with the sponsor. <br>
