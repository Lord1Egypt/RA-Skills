# Shot List by Product Category

## Apparel & Footwear

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: Ghost mannequin or flat lay, white background
2. On-model front view (full body or cropped appropriately)
3. On-model back view
4. Close-up: fabric texture / material detail
5. Size chart infographic with model measurement callout ("Model is 5'7", 130lbs, wearing size M")
6. Detail shot: zipper / buttons / seams / logo
7. Outfit styling shot (multiple ways to wear)
8. Color variants: all colors on same consistent background

### Category-specific additions
- Activewear: stretch/movement shot, sweat-wicking infographic
- Footwear: sole shot, side profile, heel height measurement
- Outerwear: lifestyle in weather conditions (rain, cold)
- Swimwear: water/pool setting lifestyle

---

## Kitchen Appliances & Gadgets

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: White background, product assembled/ready-to-use
2. In-use lifestyle: food being prepared, person using the tool
3. Disassembled parts flat lay: shows everything in the box
4. Dimensions infographic with measurements in inches + cm
5. Compatibility shot: on induction/gas/electric stove (if applicable)
6. Close-up: cooking surface, material, any key feature
7. Results shot: finished food/dish (aspirational outcome)
8. Storage shot: folded/stored compact (if applicable)

### Category-specific additions
- Knives: edge close-up, slicing in action, handle grip
- Bakeware: baked result, oven temperature rating callout
- Coffee equipment: brewing process, coffee result

---

## Electronics & Tech Accessories

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: White or gradient background, product at 3/4 angle
2. In-use shot: connected to/used with primary compatible device
3. Compatibility infographic: list of compatible devices
4. Size reference: held in hand, next to common object
5. Feature callout infographic: labeled diagram of buttons/ports
6. Cable/accessories included flat lay
7. Charging or power infographic: battery life, charge time
8. Color/finish variants: same angle, all options

### Category-specific additions
- Audio: frequency response or driver specs infographic
- Phone cases: drop test certification callout, raised edge detail
- Cables: braided material close-up, connector close-up

---

## Beauty & Personal Care

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: Product on white or gradient background, label facing front
2. Texture/formula shot: product dispensed on surface showing consistency
3. Ingredient callout infographic: key active ingredients with benefit
4. Before/after: results on skin/hair (use real model, not digital manipulation)
5. On-skin application step-by-step
6. Full range lifestyle: product among other similar products in routine
7. Close-up: packaging detail, pump/applicator

### Category-specific additions
- Skincare: skin texture close-up (pores, glow), absorption shot
- Haircare: hair before vs. after, hair type suitability callout
- Makeup: swatch shot on different skin tones

---

## Fitness & Sports Equipment

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: Equipment assembled, white or clean background
2. In-use demonstration: target muscle group being worked
3. Dimensions infographic: assembled size and storage/folded size
4. Weight capacity callout: "Supports up to 300 lbs"
5. Exercise guide infographic: 4–6 exercises possible with this equipment
6. Detail shot: grips, resistance bands, adjustment mechanism
7. Storage/compact shot: size comparison when stored
8. Multi-user lifestyle: different body types and fitness levels in use

---

## Baby & Kids

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: White background, product without child
2. Age-appropriate use shot: child in correct age range using product
3. Size reference: relative to child or adult hand
4. Safety certification callout infographic: certifications earned
5. Materials infographic: "BPA-free", "non-toxic" with material labels
6. Assembly/setup shot: if assembly required
7. Color/character variants: all options shown
8. Parent-plus-child lifestyle: parent's experience highlighted

---

## Home Organization & Storage

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: Product assembled, styled but not over-cluttered
2. Capacity demo: show how much fits inside / maximum load
3. Dimensions infographic with interior and exterior measurements
4. Assembly infographic: step-by-step for products requiring assembly
5. Before/after: organizing a real space
6. Multiple-unit arrangement: how units combine or stack
7. Material close-up: finish quality, texture
8. Lifestyle: styled in realistic home setting

---

## Pet Products

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: Product on white, pet not required
2. Pet in use: correct pet type and size for the product
3. Size reference: product next to specific breed/size of pet
4. Materials/safety infographic: non-toxic, machine washable, etc.
5. Adjustment/customization shot: how to size/adjust
6. Multiple pets/breeds: showing versatility (if applicable)
7. Close-up: stitching, clasp, buckle quality detail

---

## Supplements & Vitamins

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: Product on white, label clearly visible
2. Ingredient callout infographic: key ingredients + benefits
3. Serving size / dosage infographic
4. Third-party certification callout: NSF, Informed Sport, GMP
5. Lifestyle: active person using product in context
6. Label close-up: Supplement Facts panel clearly readable
7. Comparison: serving size vs. competitors or serving cost

Note: Before/after claims require substantiation. Avoid before/after body transformation shots unless clinically supported.

---

## Tools & Hardware

### Must-have shots
1. Hero: Tool on white background, assembled/ready to use
2. In-use shot: person using the tool on a project
3. Specs infographic: blade length, torque specs, battery capacity
4. Compatibility callout: compatible with / not compatible with
5. Kit contents flat lay: everything included in the box
6. Size reference: tool in hand showing grip and scale
7. Detail shots: key mechanisms, adjustments, safety features
8. Storage/case: if carrying case included
