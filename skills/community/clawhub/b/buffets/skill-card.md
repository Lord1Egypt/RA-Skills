## Description: <br>
提供关于网红自助餐的推荐清单、打卡路线与指南。用户搜索网红自助餐或规划相关出行时调用。 <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel planners use this skill to find influencer-style buffet recommendations, compare venues by location, budget, cuisine, wait time, reservation options, and plan efficient check-in routes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Restaurant prices, hours, hygiene ratings, reservation availability, and venue rules may change before a visit. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details with the venue or booking platform before visiting. <br>
Risk: Peak wait times, minimum spend, filming restrictions, and venue-specific rules may affect a planned buffet visit. <br>
Mitigation: Check on-site rules, reserve ahead when possible, and include off-peak or alternate venue options. <br>
