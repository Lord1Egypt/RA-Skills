## Description: <br>
AI Public Speaking helps an agent coach users on speech structure, storytelling, stage-fright management, body language, voice control, slide use, impromptu speaking, TED-style talks, pitches, interviews, reports, and ceremonial remarks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill to get public-speaking coaching for speeches, presentations, pitches, rehearsals, interviews, status reports, wedding remarks, and stage-fright preparation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may upload sensitive rehearsal materials, speech drafts, private presentation content, or videos for feedback. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only materials needed for the presentation task and avoid unrelated private or confidential documents. <br>
Risk: Stage-fright guidance could be mistaken for medical or mental-health care. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill's anxiety and stage-fright advice as coaching support, not clinical diagnosis or treatment. <br>
