## Description: <br>
Calculate embodied carbon and lifecycle emissions for construction materials and projects. Support sustainable design decisions with carbon data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[datadrivenconstruction](https://clawhub.ai/user/datadrivenconstruction) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Construction, sustainability, and design teams use this skill to estimate embodied and lifecycle carbon for materials, assemblies, and projects, compare results to building-type benchmarks, and identify carbon reduction opportunities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Included carbon factors and benchmarks may be sample data that do not match current EPDs, local regulations, or project-specific quantities. <br>
Mitigation: Verify factors, benchmarks, and quantities against current EPDs, applicable local requirements, and project records before using outputs for compliance or design decisions. <br>
