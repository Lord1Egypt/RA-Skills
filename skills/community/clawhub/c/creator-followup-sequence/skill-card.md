## Description: <br>
Write short, human follow-up messages for creator outreach when the first message gets ignored, delayed, or half-answered. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creator acquisition, UGC, affiliate, and partnership teams use this skill to recover stalled creator outreach with concise second-touch and third-touch messages. It helps diagnose the conversation state, choose a follow-up angle, draft a short sequence, recommend timing, and define when to stop. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Follow-up messages may pressure creators or feel spammy if they escalate urgency or ignore disinterest. <br>
Mitigation: Keep messages short, add useful context or a smaller ask, avoid guilt or fake urgency, and follow the stop rule when the creator appears uninterested or low fit. <br>
Risk: The artifact states a CC BY-NC-SA non-commercial license while metadata separately reports MIT-0. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the applicable license before commercial use and obtain separate commercial permission from Razestar if required. <br>
Risk: Creator outreach inputs may include sensitive business or creator relationship details. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only outreach details the user is comfortable sharing with the agent. <br>
