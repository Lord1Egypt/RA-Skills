## Why This Skill Exists

Generic AI writes chimney marketing copy with mistakes that a credentialed sweep immediately spots — and that create real liability:

- "CSIA-certified company" (CSIA certifies individuals, not companies — FTC §5 deceptive without individual CCS #)

- "UL-listed liner" without the specific listing number (UL 103 vs. UL 1777 are NOT interchangeable — wrong one voids the warranty)
- "EPA-approved fireplace" (EPA certifies emissions, not appliances — "EPA Phase 2 certified" is correct)
- "Please give us 5 stars" (FTC 2023 Endorsement Guide violation — civil investigative demand territory)

This skill eliminates all of that before a single word of copy is written.

## The 7 Compliance Moats

1. **CSIA Certification Gating** — "CSIA-certified company" permanently blocked; individual CCS # required
2. **NFPA 211 Inspection Level Classification** — Level I/II/III specified; "inspection" alone blocked in real estate copy
3. **UL Liner Listing Accuracy** — UL 103 / UL 103HT / UL 127 / UL 1777 specified by fuel type and application
4. **EPA Phase 2 Wood Heater Rules** — EPA Phase 2 certified units only; "EPA-approved" permanently blocked; Clark County burn advisory integrated
5. **NFI Gas Appliance Credential + NV Gas Line Scope** — CSIA ≠ NFI; gas line work beyond appliance connection requires licensed NV C-1 contractor
6. **OSHA 29 CFR 1926.1053 Ladder Safety** — roof-access OSHA compliance disclosure in all service pages
7. **FTC 2023 Endorsement Guides** — "5-star review" request permanently blocked; incentivized reviews blocked; honest review language enforced
