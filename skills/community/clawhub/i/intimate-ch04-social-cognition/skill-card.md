## Description: <br>
Assists Chinese-language study conversations about Chapter 4, "Social Cognition," from Rowland S. Miller's Intimate Relationships by explaining chapter concepts, examples, and distinctions with progressive disclosure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external learners, and study assistants use this skill to review and apply social-cognition concepts from Intimate Relationships Chapter 4 in Chinese. It supports definitions, exam review, textbook-example explanations, and bounded relationship-scenario analysis without acting as counseling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the study helper as relationship counseling. <br>
Mitigation: Present it as an educational chapter aid and keep relationship scenarios framed as concept analysis rather than professional advice. <br>
Risk: Users may need exact textbook citations or original wording. <br>
Mitigation: Direct users to verify against the original textbook when exact citations, wording, or page-level accuracy matters. <br>
Risk: The server evidence includes an unrelated crypto capability tag. <br>
Mitigation: Do not describe crypto functionality; treat the tag as publisher metadata that should be corrected for clarity. <br>
