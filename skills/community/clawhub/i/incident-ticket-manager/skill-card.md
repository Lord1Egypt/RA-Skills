## Description: <br>
Create and route ad operations incident tickets for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, and multi-platform campaign failures. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ad operations teams and performance marketing operators use this skill to capture incidents, classify severity, route owners, and produce actionable mitigation tickets for Meta, Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, and related campaign failures. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recommended pause, rollback, spend, billing, or platform-setting actions may affect live advertising operations. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review those actions before applying them, especially when high-severity risks or account-health changes are involved. <br>
Risk: Incident triage can involve account IDs, logs, thresholds, and owner contact details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the operational details needed for the specific incident or account-health triage. <br>
Risk: The trigger wording is broader than ideal for a focused incident-ticketing skill. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for actual ad incidents or account-health triage rather than general growth strategy. <br>
