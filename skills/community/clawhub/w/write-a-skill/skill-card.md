## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated skill drafts may be incomplete or mis-scoped if requirements are vague. <br>
Mitigation: Review the draft with the requester, confirm trigger wording and supported workflows, and scan the skill before publishing. <br>
Risk: Suggested scripts or shell commands in a new skill could be unsafe if copied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect generated scripts and commands before approval, especially commands that modify files, install dependencies, or access services. <br>
