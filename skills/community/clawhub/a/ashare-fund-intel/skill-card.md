## Description: <br>
Produce scheduled A-share and fund intelligence reports with bullish and bearish signals, source-backed analysis, and position-adjustment suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[warriorfan](https://clawhub.ai/user/warriorfan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ClawHub users use this skill to produce scheduled China A-share and fund briefs, NAV confirmation reports, and source-backed position adjustment input based on risk preference, watchlist, and holdings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The included portfolio file may reflect another user's holdings and could personalize reports incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Review or replace references/portfolio-current.md before use. <br>
Risk: Generated allocation ranges and position changes could be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational analysis only and keep the report's non-investment-advice disclaimer. <br>
Risk: Market claims may be incomplete or stale if source pages are unavailable, blocked, or paywalled. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims against cited sources and switch to alternate sources when access is blocked. <br>
