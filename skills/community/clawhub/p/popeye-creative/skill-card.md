## Description: <br>
Creative assistant for brainstorming, naming suggestions, and concept design using Chinese-language triggers for brainstorming, naming, and creative design. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiangtaoid](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiangtaoid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to generate creative directions, names, slogans, concept plans, visual directions, campaign ideas, and next-step suggestions for products, brands, activities, and marketing work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated names, slogans, examples, or campaign concepts may be unavailable, legally unsuitable, or commercially inappropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Independently check trademarks, registrations, domain and account availability, legal suitability, and brand fit before commercial use. <br>
Risk: Broad Chinese trigger phrases may activate the skill during general brainstorming or naming requests. <br>
Mitigation: Review whether the skill should respond in the current conversation before relying on its output. <br>
