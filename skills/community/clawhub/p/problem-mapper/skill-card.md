## Description: <br>
Problem Mapper helps users systematically define problems, set success criteria, identify risks, and convert ambiguous decisions or strategic questions into structured action plans using dialogue or waterfall modes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to clarify complex decisions, strategy questions, product plans, interview preparation, and other ambiguous situations before selecting solutions. It produces a structured problem map with refined problem statements, success metrics, risk assessment, solution directions, and an MVP validation plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad Chinese-language activation prompt can apply to general problem-analysis requests and may trigger when a narrower skill would be more appropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Review the skill description and activation scope before installation, and invoke it only when a structured Chinese-language problem-mapping workflow fits the use case. <br>
Risk: The skill produces planning and decision-support guidance that can be incomplete or misleading if the user supplies weak assumptions, especially for finance, career, strategy, or product decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision-support drafts, validate assumptions and metrics with subject-matter experts, and avoid using the generated plan as a substitute for professional consultation or execution. <br>
