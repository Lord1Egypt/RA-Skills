## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language guidance for AI-assisted voice acting, covering vocal training, character voice design, dubbing formats, recording equipment, post-production, and career paths. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, voice actors, and audio producers use this skill to get practical guidance on voice training, character voice design, recording setup, post-production, and voice-acting career workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided voice recordings may contain personal or identifying data. <br>
Mitigation: Only share audio that is appropriate for the current agent environment and that the user is comfortable having analyzed. <br>
Risk: Voice-acting guidance may be applied to commercial recordings without checking project requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review recording, rights, contract, and delivery requirements before using the guidance in a production workflow. <br>
