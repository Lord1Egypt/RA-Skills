## Description: <br>
一步步引导学习算法，不直接给答案。适用于学习 LeetCode 算法题，采用苏格拉底式提问教学法，先引导思考暴力解法，再优化到最优解，每道题完成后自动生成题解文档和代码文件。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xiaodingyang](https://clawhub.ai/user/xiaodingyang) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
学习者和开发者可用它以苏格拉底式提问练习 LeetCode 算法题，从暴力解法逐步优化，并在完成后生成题解、代码文件和 progress.md 记录。 <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may create or update learning files such as progress.md and solution files during its tutoring workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use it in a dedicated algorithm-practice folder, ask the agent to show planned file paths, and confirm before overwriting existing files. <br>
