## Description: <br>
Helps teams achieve WCAG 2.1 AA compliance by auditing and guiding inclusive design for web, mobile, desktop, and other digital products. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, QA teams, product teams, and accessibility leads use this skill to audit digital products against WCAG 2.1 AA, plan remediation, and generate accessibility guidance for components, content, testing, and organizational programs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Accessibility, legal, and compliance guidance may be incomplete or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify formal compliance claims and legal obligations with qualified accessibility, legal, or compliance professionals before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Suggested npm, axe, pa11y, CI, or URL-scanning commands can affect projects or test systems if run in the wrong environment. <br>
Mitigation: Run commands only in projects and against URLs the user is authorized to test, and review generated configuration before applying it. <br>
