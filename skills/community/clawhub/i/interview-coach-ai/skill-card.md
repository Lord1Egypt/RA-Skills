## Description: <br>
Interview Coach helps users prepare for job interviews with mock interviews, role-specific questions, answer scoring, STAR-method practice, salary negotiation coaching, follow-up email drafting, and local progress tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mkpareek0315](https://clawhub.ai/user/mkpareek0315) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and job seekers use this skill to practice interviews, improve answers, prepare for behavioral, technical, HR, panel, virtual, and salary conversations, and track interview-prep progress locally. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save profile details, practice history, scores, weak areas, and saved answers locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive interview or personal details that should not remain on the machine, and review or delete files under ~/.openclaw/interview-coach/ when needed. <br>
Risk: Broad interview-related triggers may activate the skill during general job-search conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit interview-prep prompts when invoking the skill and review generated coaching before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Company-specific preparation is based on general knowledge rather than real-time company research. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current company facts, role requirements, and compensation details with authoritative sources before an interview or negotiation. <br>
