## Description: <br>
AI brand identity design powered by CellCog. Brand kits, color palettes, typography, brand guidelines, logo design, visual identity, social media assets, web design, video - complete brand identity from a single brief. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, designers, founders, and marketing teams use this skill to ask CellCog for brand identity work such as complete brand kits, logos, color palettes, typography recommendations, brand guidelines, social templates, web assets, and video assets from a brief. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, API usage, and brand strategy may be processed by the third-party CellCog service. <br>
Mitigation: Use only when CellCog's privacy and retention terms are acceptable, and avoid sending secrets, regulated data, or confidential strategy unless approved. <br>
Risk: The skill requires CELLCOG_API_KEY and the external cellcog dependency. <br>
Mitigation: Store the API key securely, avoid exposing it in prompts or logs, and verify the cellcog package during setup. <br>
