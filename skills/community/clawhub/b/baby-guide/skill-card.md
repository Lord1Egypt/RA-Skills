## Description: <br>
Baby Guide provides baby-care guidance for developmental milestones, feeding, vaccination reminders, early education, sleep training, and parent-child interaction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Parents and caregivers use this skill as a general baby-care reference for age-based milestones, feeding, vaccines, education, sleep, and common symptom topics. Medical and urgent-care output should be treated as general reference and checked with pediatric professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes pediatric emergency and medication guidance that could be mistaken for medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat emergency and medication output as general reference only and use professional care for urgent symptoms. <br>
Risk: An unrelated helper script stores local command history. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid or remove scripts/script.sh unless the local design-helper behavior and history file are desired. <br>
