## Description: <br>
Generates a personalized message, converts to natural speech, and sends as an SMS notification with audio link. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobeyrebecca](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobeyrebecca) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business owners and operations managers use this skill to draft customer voice notifications, produce natural-speech audio, and send an SMS notification with an audio link. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SMS voice notifications may be sent to an unintended or non-consenting recipient. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the phone number, recipient identity, and consent before sending any SMS or audio link. <br>
Risk: Generated message text, audio links, SMS delivery, TTS usage, and hosted audio retention can create accuracy, cost, or privacy concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated text and audio link before delivery, and confirm applicable SMS/TTS costs and audio retention behavior. <br>
