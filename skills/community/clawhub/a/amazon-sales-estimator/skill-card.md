## Description: <br>
Amazon Sales Estimator helps sellers and product researchers estimate monthly Amazon sales and revenue from BSR, ASIN, or keyword inputs across 12 marketplaces. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/phheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, product researchers, and e-commerce operators use this skill to estimate product sales, size keyword markets, benchmark competitors, and compare revenue opportunities using public Amazon information. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may make public Amazon or search requests for ASIN and keyword analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Use only public product or keyword inputs and avoid providing seller credentials, cookies, private account pages, or sensitive business strategy. <br>
Risk: Sales and revenue estimates can be incomplete or misleading because the skill cannot access actual seller sales, historical BSR data, or PPC conversion metrics. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as directional estimates and validate important business decisions against authoritative analytics or internal sales data. <br>
