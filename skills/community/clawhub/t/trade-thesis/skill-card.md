## Description: <br>
Structured pre-trade thesis builder for bull and bear cases, invalidation criteria, and sizing rationale before live strategy deployment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[superior-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/superior-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and trading agents use this skill to structure pre-trade analysis before committing live capital to a new strategy idea. It guides the agent to produce a two-sided thesis, measurable invalidation criteria, data checks, and position sizing rationale. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill supports trading decisions and may produce position sizing or stoploss rationale that users could mistake for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before using real funds or deploying a live strategy, and present outputs as decision support rather than trading permission. <br>
