## Description: <br>
Find design and AI art inspiration from curated galleries, screenshot libraries, and creative showcases. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, developers, and creative agents use this skill to find targeted visual references for UI patterns, landing pages, AI-generated imagery, colors, typography, icons, and motion ideas. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Linked third-party inspiration sites may track visits, require accounts, or require paid plans. <br>
Mitigation: Review destination sites before use and avoid submitting sensitive information unless the site is approved for the user's context. <br>
Risk: Some AI image galleries may contain mature or unsuitable imagery. <br>
Mitigation: Use available site filters and select sources appropriate to the audience and project requirements. <br>
Risk: Optional preference tracking could store user design preferences locally. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive project, customer, or personal information out of the optional preferences file. <br>
