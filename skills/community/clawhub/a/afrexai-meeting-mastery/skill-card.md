## Description: <br>
Meeting Mastery helps an agent prepare meeting briefs, capture structured notes, generate follow-ups, track action items, maintain contact history, and audit meeting ROI. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users, operators, sales teams, managers, recruiters, and agents use this skill to prepare for meetings, produce structured notes and summaries, track action items, draft follow-ups, and review meeting value over time. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores and reuses detailed contact profiles and meeting history without clear consent, retention, or deletion controls. <br>
Mitigation: Set explicit retention rules before use, periodically delete or edit stored contact cards and meeting files, and avoid storing personal-life details without permission. <br>
Risk: Attendee research can collect personal or irrelevant information beyond the business purpose of the meeting. <br>
Mitigation: Limit attendee research to business-relevant facts and exclude personal details unless they are necessary and permissioned. <br>
Risk: Meeting notes, audits, and relationship memory can include confidential or regulated information. <br>
Mitigation: Exclude confidential or regulated meetings from memory and audits unless approved controls are in place. <br>
Risk: Generated summaries and follow-up drafts may include sensitive, incorrect, or over-specific content. <br>
Mitigation: Review all summaries, reminders, and follow-up drafts before sending them to attendees or third parties. <br>
