## Description: <br>
Extracts behavior-first ideal player personas, anti-personas, motivation and tolerance profiles, and design implications from game, feature, loop, or progression descriptions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and publishing teams use this skill to infer the player profile implied by a game design and translate audience fit into design implications, persona summaries, and optional market-facing hypotheses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential game design, roadmap, or publishing details into the analysis prompt. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved sharing channels, redact sensitive details when possible, and treat pasted design material as input processed by the agent. <br>
Risk: Market-validation requests may trigger public web research for recent audience or demographic data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid placing confidential product facts in search queries, cite public sources, and separate evidence from inference. <br>
Risk: Demographic and market-fit claims can be overstated if they are not grounded in title-specific or genre-specific evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Label demographic outputs as hypotheses or market signals, prefer ranges over false precision, and state confidence and uncertainty. <br>
Risk: Persona recommendations can mislead design decisions when the input design description is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions, anchor conclusions to the supplied systems or play observations, and treat recommendations as design guidance rather than validated market truth. <br>
