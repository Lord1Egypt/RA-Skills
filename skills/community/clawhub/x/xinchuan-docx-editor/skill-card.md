## Description: <br>
Guides an agent in editing Word documents for Xinchuang compliance planning, including replacing legacy deployment and database terminology with target technology-stack language. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hczaihe](https://clawhub.ai/user/hczaihe) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and technical planners use this skill when revising .docx migration, implementation, or technology-planning documents for Xinchuang compliance terminology. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The replacement guidance is sparse and incomplete, which may lead to inaccurate or incomplete Word document edits. <br>
Mitigation: Keep backups of important .docx files and review edited documents carefully before use. <br>
