## Description: <br>
Visualize how changes in variables affect outcomes. Use for risk analysis and decision support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and decision-support teams use this skill to format sensitivity analysis, tornado charts, scenario analysis, variable impact rankings, and related risk mitigations for a provided decision or project. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated financial, scenario, or risk assumptions may be incorrect or misleading if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and validate all assumptions, ranges, probabilities, and outputs before relying on them for business or financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger words may activate the skill for general what-if questions where a full sensitivity analysis is not intended. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that sensitivity analysis or tornado-chart formatting is appropriate before applying the skill's output. <br>
