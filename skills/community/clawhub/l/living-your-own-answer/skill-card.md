## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language reflective coaching skill that helps users journal life events, review patterns, and build personal judgment, boundaries, creativity, and life direction over time. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[web-seeker](https://clawhub.ai/user/web-seeker) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Use this skill when a user wants structured self-reflection around life confusion, relationships, boundaries, emotional processing, personal growth, choices, reviews, reading insights, practical experiments, or long-term pattern recognition. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
No geography restrictions were identified in the submitted evidence; use where ClawHub skills are permitted and local requirements allow reflective coaching content. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat reflective coaching as a substitute for crisis support, therapy, legal, medical, financial, or other professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for journaling and self-reflection only, and seek qualified professional support for crisis, clinical, legal, medical, or financial decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill can invite sensitive personal disclosures while exploring emotions, relationships, life events, and long-term patterns. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the personal details needed for reflection and avoid entering highly sensitive information unless the environment is appropriate for that data. <br>
