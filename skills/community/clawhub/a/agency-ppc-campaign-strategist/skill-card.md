## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, agencies, and PPC operators use this skill to plan, restructure, and optimize paid media campaigns across Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, and Amazon Ads. It supports account architecture, bidding, budgeting, keyword, audience, and cross-platform strategy decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use available ad-platform integrations to inspect or modify live advertising accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use read-only or narrowly scoped credentials for analysis and require manual approval before campaign, bidding, budget, or keyword changes. <br>
Risk: Incorrect PPC recommendations or account changes could affect spend, performance, or reporting. <br>
Mitigation: Use audit logging, change review, and rollback procedures before connecting production ad accounts. <br>
