## Description: <br>
Helps creators and communicators turn an Ask HN product-help request into a practical plan, artifact, checklist, analysis, or implementation support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, communicators, and product builders use this skill to convert a product-help request into a concrete workflow, checklist, analysis, code change, artifact, or decision-support output with visible assumptions and follow-up risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger words may activate the skill for unrelated product, content, or planning requests. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow the trigger terms or disable implicit invocation when precise routing is required. <br>
Risk: Generated product-validation guidance may depend on assumptions when the user's inputs are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions, ask only for missing information that materially changes the output, and validate the result against the user's success criteria. <br>
