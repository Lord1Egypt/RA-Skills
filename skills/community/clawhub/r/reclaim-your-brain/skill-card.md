## Description: <br>
Transforms AI conversations into active learning sessions using Socratic questioning, explain-back verification, progressive disclosure, cognitive load management, and prior knowledge linking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JK-0001](https://clawhub.ai/user/JK-0001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners, developers, and knowledge workers use this skill when they want to understand a concept deeply instead of receiving a quick answer. It guides the assistant to teach through questions, explain-back checks, gradual layering, and links to the user's prior knowledge. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad requests to understand a topic deeply may activate Socratic teaching when the user needs a quick direct answer. <br>
Mitigation: Use this mode intentionally for learning sessions, and disable or avoid the trigger phrases when a concise answer is needed. <br>
