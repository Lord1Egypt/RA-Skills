## Description: <br>
Conducts Amazon product research by analyzing market size, competition, profit margins, competitors, and risks to provide GO/NO-GO recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[beyondbright](https://clawhub.ai/user/beyondbright) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to evaluate Amazon product ideas, estimate market opportunity and profit potential, compare competitors, and receive GO, CAUTION, or NO-GO recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on external market data and SellerSprite-style API access, so missing credentials or stale data can affect the analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Review the required data-layer setup and API access before use, and validate important market or competitor findings against current source data. <br>
Risk: The output is business analysis and may not guarantee financial outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as decision support, not as a guaranteed investment or product-launch decision. <br>
