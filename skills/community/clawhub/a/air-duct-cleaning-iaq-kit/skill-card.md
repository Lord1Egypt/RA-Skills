## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-focused marketing prompts and example outputs for NADCA-certified air duct cleaning and indoor air quality contractors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Air duct cleaning contractors, HVAC service providers, IAQ firms, and marketing agencies use this skill to draft seasonal campaigns, service pages, schema markup, review requests, referral outreach, ads, and local SEO content while checking claims against stated NADCA, EPA, FIFRA, mold, licensing, FTC, and TCPA constraints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States, with Nevada-specific examples and licensing references <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security summary says the skill is prompt-only but has compliance claims and declared financial-capability metadata that do not fully match the templates. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as draft marketing copy, manually review them before publication, and re-check or ignore the financial-authority and purchasing capability tags unless the publisher documents why they are needed. <br>
Risk: Marketing, legal, FTC/TCPA, medical-adjacent, licensing, and contractor compliance claims may be wrong for a specific business or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify NADCA membership, equipment specifications, biocide registration, review-request practices, SMS consent, and applicable local licensing requirements before using generated content. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes Nevada-specific licensing and example content that may not apply outside Nevada. <br>
Mitigation: Replace local references with the contractor's actual service area and confirm state, county, and city rules before publishing. <br>
