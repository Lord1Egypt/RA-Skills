## Description: <br>
Plan a business research study the way a professional consultant would by selecting the right analytical framework, designing the research method, and defining a specific actionable output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[owenrao](https://clawhub.ai/user/owenrao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business, product, marketing, and strategy teams use this skill to turn broad research questions into decision-focused research plans. It helps define the problem, choose an analytical framework such as JTBD, KANO, STP, GE-McKinsey, or journey mapping, and plan desk or user research before analysis begins. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Framework recommendations or research plans may be poorly matched to the user's business context. <br>
Mitigation: Review the selected framework, research method, and proposed output for fit before acting on the plan. <br>
Risk: Research planning may involve confidential strategy, customer, or market information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing sensitive business data unless the user is comfortable sharing that context with the agent. <br>
