## Description: <br>
Rewrite, edit, or review Indonesian text so it sounds natural, human, and context-appropriate. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ramaaditya49](https://clawhub.ai/user/ramaaditya49) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to humanize, rewrite, or review Indonesian writing while preserving facts, intent, and register. It supports formal, professional, casual, sales, academic, and technical Indonesian editing tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Humanized generated writing could be used to misrepresent authorship where disclosure matters. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for editing and register control, and disclose AI assistance when academic, professional, client, or platform rules require it. <br>
Risk: Rewrites can accidentally add unsupported claims, citations, numbers, names, or proof. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the source facts and review the final text against the original before publication. <br>
