## Description: <br>
Cognitive Skill Factory packages 17 cognitive decision-framework skills for analyzing strategy, products, investment questions, risk, learning, career choices, and organizational decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xiejianjun000](https://clawhub.ai/user/xiejianjun000) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
Proprietary <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill bundle to prompt an agent for structured decision guidance using named entrepreneur, investor, product, learning, and risk-management thinking frameworks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional install-all script installs additional skills that persist in the agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review scripts/install-all.sh and the listed skills before running the installer. <br>
Risk: License evidence conflicts between MIT-0, MIT, and proprietary terms. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the controlling license with the publisher before modifying, redistributing, or using the skill commercially. <br>
Risk: The skill provides advisory thinking frameworks that may be mistaken for authoritative business, investment, education, or career advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support and review them against domain expertise and current facts before acting. <br>
