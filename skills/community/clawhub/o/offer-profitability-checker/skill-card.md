## Description: <br>
Checks whether an ecommerce offer is commercially viable after discounts, costs, refunds, and traffic economics are included. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce, growth, and merchandising teams use this skill to pressure-test discounts, bundles, upsells, shipping offers, and other promotions before launch or scale. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Profitability conclusions can be misleading if offer assumptions, costs, refund rates, traffic economics, or conversion inputs are incomplete or wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Verify business assumptions and guardrails before using the analysis for pricing, launch, or scaling decisions. <br>
Risk: A generated Python model may contain formula, default, or scenario assumptions that do not match the team's operating model. <br>
Mitigation: Review any generated Python script, formulas, and defaults before relying on them for commercial decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes Chinese workflow instructions, which may be less accessible for non-Chinese users. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to respond in the user's preferred language when clarity or localization is needed. <br>
