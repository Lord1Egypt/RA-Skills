## Description: <br>
Convert apparel sketches into ecommerce-ready colorized shell-jacket renders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bananooo-zhang](https://clawhub.ai/user/bananooo-zhang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, apparel product teams, and creative agents use this skill to turn garment line art into ecommerce-style shell jacket product renders while preserving key sketch structure. It supports iterative refinement by reusing a previous output as a style reference with focused brief updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Selected apparel sketches, optional reference images, and design briefs are sent to a disclosed remote image-generation endpoint for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when external processing is acceptable, avoid confidential or unreleased designs unless approved, and use a dedicated or restricted API key where possible. <br>
Risk: Generated images may not perfectly preserve garment construction details from the input sketch. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before use and apply focused revision briefs with the preferred prior output as a style reference. <br>
