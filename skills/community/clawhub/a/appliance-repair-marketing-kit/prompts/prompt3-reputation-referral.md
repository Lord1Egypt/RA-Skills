# Prompt 3: Reputation Engine + Referral System

**Skill:** Appliance Repair Marketing Kit  
**Prompt:** 3 of 4  
**Tier:** Paid

---

## Instructions

Copy the prompt below into Claude (any version). Fill in every `[BRACKET]` field before submitting.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a customer retention and reputation marketing specialist for home services businesses.

Generate a complete reputation engine + referral system for this appliance repair business:

BUSINESS INFO:
- Business name: [BUSINESS NAME]
- Owner/technician first name: [NAME — for personal sign-off]
- City/State: [CITY, STATE]
- Brands serviced: [LIST BRANDS]
- Repair warranty: [e.g., 90-day parts & labor, 1-year parts]
- Review platforms: [Google / Yelp / Facebook — list all active]
- Google review link: [URL]
- Yelp review link: [URL or "not applicable"]
- Referral reward: [e.g., $20 off next repair, $25 gift card, 10% off]
- Pricing model: [flat-rate / diagnostic fee]

GENERATE:

SECTION 1: POST-REPAIR REVIEW REQUEST SEQUENCE

1A. SMS Review Request (immediately after job complete)
- Under 160 chars (one SMS)
- Personal tone — from technician, not brand
- Specific to the appliance repaired (use [APPLIANCE] placeholder)
- Direct Google review link
- FTC compliant: no incentive tied to positive review only

1B. Email Review Request (sent same day, 2 hours after SMS)
- Subject line (50 chars max): specific to appliance repaired
- Body: 3 short paragraphs — thanks, specific mention of repair, review ask
- PS: mention warranty reminder
- Unsubscribe footer

1C. Follow-Up SMS (3 days later, if no review)
- Under 160 chars
- Soft follow-up, not pushy
- Different angle: "quick question" framing

SECTION 2: APPLIANCE WARRANTY REGISTRATION FOLLOW-UP
(for customers whose appliance warranty should be registered after repair)

2A. Email (5 days post-repair)
- Subject: "Register your [APPLIANCE] repair warranty — [BUSINESS NAME]"
- Body: warranty details, how to contact if issue recurs, maintenance tip for that appliance type
- Use [APPLIANCE] and [WARRANTY_PERIOD] placeholders
- Secondary CTA: refer a friend + referral reward

SECTION 3: REFERRAL PROGRAM COPY

3A. Referral Program One-Pager
- Program name (short, memorable)
- How it works (3 steps, simple)
- Referral reward details
- Exclusions/limitations (one-time use, new customers only, etc.)
- "Share this" text (pre-written for customer to forward via text)

3B. Technician Leave-Behind Card Copy
- For physical card left after each job (2.5"x3.5" business card size)
- Front: referral hook + reward
- Back: warranty reminder + review ask + QR code placeholder

SECTION 4: REVIEW RESPONSE TEMPLATES

4A. 5-Star Review Response (3 variants — for different appliance types)
4B. 4-Star Review Response (acknowledge, thank, invite back)
4C. 3-Star Review Response (professional, solution-focused, take offline)
4D. 1-2 Star Review Response (de-escalation template, offer to resolve, NEVER defensive)

COMPLIANCE RULES (mandatory):
1. FTC 2023 ENDORSEMENT GUIDES: Review requests must NOT offer incentive conditioned on leaving a positive review. Referral rewards must be disclosed if reviewer is compensated.
   - ALLOWED: "Leave us a review and get $X off your next service" (incentive not tied to star rating)
   - NOT ALLOWED: "Leave us a 5-star review and get $X off" (conditioned on positive review)
2. REVIEW GATING: Do NOT filter customers before review ask (no "are you satisfied?" pre-screen that only forwards happy customers to review link)
3. WARRANTY CLAIMS: only state the actual warranty period provided
4. SMS: include opt-out option in all SMS messages ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe")
5. EMAIL: CAN-SPAM compliant — business name, physical address, unsubscribe link required

Output all sections in order. Use headers. Include all placeholders in [BRACKET] format.
```

---

## What You Get

- 3-step SMS + email review sequence
- Warranty follow-up email (turns warranty into referral opportunity)
- Referral program with leave-behind card copy
- 4 review response templates (5-star through 1-star)
- All copy FTC 2023 + CAN-SPAM + TCPA compliant

## Pro Tip

The 3-day follow-up SMS doubles review conversion rates for appliance repair — customers often mean to leave a review but forget once the crisis is resolved. The warranty follow-up email is where you get your referrals: the customer is happy, the repair held, and they're mentally saying "that guy was great." That's when to ask.
