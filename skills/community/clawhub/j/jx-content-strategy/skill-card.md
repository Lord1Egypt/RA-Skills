## Description: <br>
Build and execute a content marketing strategy for a solopreneur business. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Solopreneurs, founders, and content operators use this skill to plan content goals, audience research, pillars, channels, calendars, distribution, repurposing, and performance measurement. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Content planning may involve customer conversations, support tickets, sales call notes, or private community exports that contain personal or sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved, minimized, and de-identified source material before sharing it with an agent. <br>
Risk: Generated content strategy recommendations may be inaccurate, misaligned with the business, or based on weak assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against business goals, audience evidence, channel constraints, and performance metrics before execution. <br>
