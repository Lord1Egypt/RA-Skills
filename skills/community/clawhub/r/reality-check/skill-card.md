## Description: <br>
Prevent bad strategic decisions by surfacing assumptions, testing feasibility, and exposing critical flaws before execution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mzfshark](https://clawhub.ai/user/mzfshark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to pressure-test plans before commitment by making assumptions explicit, identifying weak links, and choosing whether to proceed, revise, or stop. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad critique-oriented trigger phrases may activate the skill more often than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording when invoking or avoiding a reality-check review, and review trigger behavior before relying on automatic activation. <br>
Risk: Decision-support output may be overread as certainty in high-stakes planning. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the viability score and recommendation as advisory, require supporting evidence, and add human or domain review before execution. <br>
