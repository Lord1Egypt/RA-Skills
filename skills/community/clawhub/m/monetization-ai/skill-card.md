## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outreach workflows can send unwanted or non-compliant messages if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review every outbound message before sending, rate-limit outreach, and respect platform and legal rules. <br>
Risk: Money-impacting actions can create financial or account harm if executed automatically. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the required explicit confirmation step before transfers, orders, payments, or irreversible account actions. <br>
Risk: Connected business accounts may expose sensitive operational data. <br>
Mitigation: Connect only accounts the user controls and keep an audit trail for outbound actions and workflow changes. <br>
