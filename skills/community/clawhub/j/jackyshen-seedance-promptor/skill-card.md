## Description: <br>
This skill generates storyboard prompts for Seedance 2.0. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mebusw](https://clawhub.ai/user/mebusw) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and video production teams use this skill to turn Seedance 2.0 video ideas and reference media into structured storyboard prompts with scene timing, camera movement, reference annotations, and optimization suggestions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated prompts may include confidential media, private faces, or sensitive business material intended for a downstream video service. <br>
Mitigation: Review prompts and reference annotations before submission, and remove sensitive material unless sharing it with the downstream service is approved. <br>
Risk: Storyboard prompts may be unsuitable, inaccurate, or inconsistent with the user's intended brand, story, or reference-media usage. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated scene breakdown, camera movements, and reference explanations before using them in production workflows. <br>
