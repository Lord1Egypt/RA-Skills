AI Smart Agriculture provides agricultural decision support for crop selection, pest and disease diagnosis, soil and fertilizer management, irrigation, weather risk, market analysis, greenhouse operations, orchards, aquaculture, and medicinal crops. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External growers, farm operators, agribusiness teams, and agricultural advisors use this skill to get practical guidance on crop planning, pest and disease response, soil and fertilizer management, irrigation, weather-related agricultural risk, market trends, and controlled-environment production. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agricultural recommendations for pesticide, fertilizer, weather, or market decisions may be incorrect, stale, or inappropriate for a local jurisdiction or production context. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations against local regulations, product labels, extension services, authoritative weather and market sources, or qualified agronomy advice before acting. <br>
Risk: Image-based pest or disease guidance can misidentify crop conditions and lead to ineffective or harmful treatment choices. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's diagnosis as decision support only and confirm important crop-health decisions with field scouting, lab testing, extension services, or qualified agronomy advice. <br>
