## Use Case: <br>
Traders, developers, and automation agents use this skill to analyze BTC or ETH setups and draft code-friendly bot rules for entries, exits, and risk controls. Users should separate the crypto trading workflow from the unrelated OpenClaw administration material present in the artifact. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The artifact is advertised as a crypto trading bot but also includes unrelated OpenClaw host security, skill-authoring, and device-connection instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Use only the crypto trading section for trading-rule generation and remove or ignore unrelated OpenClaw administration sections before installing or invoking the skill. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes guidance to approve the latest OpenClaw device, which could authorize the wrong device if run without verification. <br>
Mitigation: Do not allow device approval commands unless the exact device identity has been verified and the user explicitly requested that action. <br>
