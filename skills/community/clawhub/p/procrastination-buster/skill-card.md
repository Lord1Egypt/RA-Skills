## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a local productivity coach to break down avoided work, choose a small first step, track blockers, and review progress. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Accountability features may record sensitive work, health, financial, or personal commitments locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid logging sensitive details unless the user is comfortable keeping them in local memory for future check-ins. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases such as "just start" or "stop procrastinating" may invoke the skill during casual productivity conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's intent before starting accountability tracking or storing task-related commitments. <br>
