## Description: <br>
Generate customizable secure passwords with options for length, character types, similarity exclusion, strength analysis, and batch creation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The listing includes unrelated financial referral links that are not part of the password workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Ignore promotional crypto or stock links and evaluate only the local password generator behavior. <br>
Risk: Analyzing a real password may echo it in local terminal output. <br>
Mitigation: Use test strings for strength checks unless local terminal exposure is acceptable. <br>
