## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to improve speech-to-text accuracy by recording compact corrections, recurring transcription patterns, domain terms, and STT configuration guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persistent transcription personalization may store confidential names or domain terms. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving sensitive terms unless necessary and periodically review learned corrections. <br>
Risk: STT provider or API-key configuration changes can affect cost and privacy. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm provider, cost, and privacy tradeoffs before applying configuration changes. <br>
Risk: Incorrect learned corrections can degrade future transcription quality. <br>
Mitigation: Use the provided criteria, confirm patterns after repeated occurrences, and keep entries compact and reviewable. <br>
