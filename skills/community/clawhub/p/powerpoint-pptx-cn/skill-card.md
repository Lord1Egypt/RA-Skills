## Description: <br>
Create, inspect, and edit Microsoft PowerPoint presentations and PPTX decks with reliable layouts, templates, placeholders, notes, charts, and visual QA. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and presentation authors use this skill when an agent needs to create, inspect, edit, or QA PowerPoint decks while preserving templates, layouts, notes, charts, and visual fidelity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: PowerPoint decks may contain confidential business content, speaker notes, comments, or linked media. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide decks the user is comfortable having the agent read or edit, and review notes, comments, and linked assets before sharing or publishing output. <br>
Risk: Template, placeholder, chart, font, or aspect-ratio mismatches can create incorrect or visually broken slides. <br>
Mitigation: Inventory the deck before edits and run separate content and visual QA, including rendered-slide inspection, before delivery. <br>
