### 功能描述（英文）

**What does this skill do?**

Automatically monitors and collects tender announcements for IT, security, surveillance, and intelligent infrastructure across China. Integrates 4 major data sources with 90%+ keyword coverage — delivering daily briefings directly to your workspace (Feishu/WeChat/DingTalk) so you never miss a bid deadline.

**Who is it for?**

- **Tech manufacturers** (surveillance, security, networking, IT) with regional sales teams
- **System integrators** bidding on government/enterprise projects
- **IT service providers** targeting public sector opportunities
- **Management** needing market intelligence and competitor insights

**What makes it different?**

- ⏰ **Real-time freshness** — Daily automated collection, tenders delivered within the same day they appear. Never miss a bid deadline.
- 🎯 **90%+ coverage** — Integrates 4 major data sources (Qianlima · Zhongxiang · Jianyu · Gansu CCPC), keyword-driven capture for maximum relevant tenders.
- 🔔 **Proactive push** — Daily briefings sent directly to Feishu, WeChat, or DingTalk — stay informed without checking websites.
- 📊 **Dual perspective** — Sales mode (what should I act on today?) + Management mode (what's the market trend?)
- 🇨🇳 **Nationwide scope** — Keyword-based nationwide search, expanding data sources province by province
