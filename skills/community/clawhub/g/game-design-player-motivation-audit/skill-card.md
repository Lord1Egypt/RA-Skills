## Description: <br>
Audit a game, feature, live-ops system, progression loop, social feature, or monetization surface using a Self-Determination Theory-inspired motivation framework. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and live-ops teams use this skill to audit how a design motivates player behavior and to rebalance systems that feel hollow, coercive, exhausting, or weak over time. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The audit may involve sensitive player data, proprietary telemetry, or confidential business plans. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data approved for the agent environment and avoid pasting confidential material into unapproved contexts. <br>
Risk: Recommendations about retention, rewards, or monetization could affect player wellbeing. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggested changes with product, design, legal, and player-safety stakeholders before implementation. <br>
