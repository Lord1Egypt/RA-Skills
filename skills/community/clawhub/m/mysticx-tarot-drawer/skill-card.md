## Description: <br>
Draw tarot cards from MysticX.ai - one card, daily card, or any of 13 spreads. Browse the full 78-card deck. No API key needed. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shrekuu](https://clawhub.ai/user/shrekuu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to draw MysticX tarot cards, browse the Rider-Waite deck, inspect available spreads, and display API-returned meanings in the user's selected language. The skill is intended for entertainment and exploratory tarot workflows, not for sensitive personal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tarot questions and language preferences may be sent to MysticX.ai. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive health, financial, relationship, identity, contact, or other private details in tarot questions. <br>
Risk: Returned images, links, and card content are third-party content from MysticX.ai. <br>
Mitigation: Treat images and links as third-party content and review them before relying on or sharing them. <br>
Risk: Tarot output could be mistaken for high-stakes personal guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for entertainment or exploratory tarot workflows and do not treat returned card meanings as professional advice. <br>
