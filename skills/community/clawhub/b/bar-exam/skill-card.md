## Description: <br>
Prepare for the US Bar Exam with MBE practice, essay drilling, weak area targeting, and jurisdiction planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users preparing for a US bar exam use this skill to structure study plans, drill MBE subjects, practice MEE and MPT responses, track performance, and adapt preparation by jurisdiction and learner profile. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local study records in ~/bar-exam/ may include personal exam, school, score, or retake-history details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive personal details on shared machines, and periodically review or delete retained study history. <br>
Risk: Bar exam formats, score thresholds, and eligibility requirements vary by jurisdiction and can affect study planning. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm jurisdiction-specific requirements with official bar admission sources before relying on a plan. <br>
