### SHOPLINE Account Overview

> 来源：[https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/40009243248921-SHOPLINE-Account-Overview](https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/40009243248921-SHOPLINE-Account-Overview)

Your eCommerce journey starts with your SHOPLINE account. It provides the foundation for managing your store's settings, user access, and security options. SHOPLINE makes it easy to oversee your store’s account needs.

 

Logging In

Accessing your SHOPLINE account is straightforward with a secure login process. In addition to email and password login, SHOPLINE also supports third-party quick login options, such as logging in with your Google, Facebook, or Apple account.

 

Managing Multiple Stores

By logging in with your account, you can see all the stores you have access to in one place. Easily switch between stores by going to the Stores page, allowing efficient management for multi-store owners.

 

Managing Accounts

Update your password, change login account, configure quick login, manage login device, maintain account details, and manage privacy settings to keep your account secure and organized.

### SHOPLINE Admin Panel Overview

> 来源：[https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/40304238894105-SHOPLINE-Admin-Panel-Overview](https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/40304238894105-SHOPLINE-Admin-Panel-Overview)

After you login with your account, you are in your SHOPLINE admin panel. This is the command center where you build, manage, and operate your ecommerce business. Learn how to navigate it to make smooth and efficient operations.

 

The Left Side Bar

The left side bar organizes the essential modules, including Orders, Products, Customers, and more. Click each to access the management page for detailed configuration and monitoring. It also provides you access to SHOPLINE App Store that helps enhance your store with an extensive ecosystem of apps, and portals to your diverse sales channel, including Facebook, Live Shopping, and others. Lastly, customize critical aspects of your store such as language, checkout and payment, and locations in Settings.

 

The Right Panel

The right panel serves like a dashboard. You can learn your store’s performance with real-time data, and take actions following the suggestion cards when our system intelligently identifies matters that require your attention.

 

The Top Bar

The top bar offers auxiliary features. You can search for features, apps or content with the search bar, switch to another store from the store selector, access SHOPLINE Help Center to learn detailed instructions and guides, stay informed about SHOPLINE updates and feature releases in the notification center, examine your store health, and manage your account details.

### Migration Overview

> 来源：[https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/32597198299801-Migration-Overview](https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/32597198299801-Migration-Overview)

Migrating your existing data from your current store is a crucial step that ensures a smooth transition for your customers and keeps your internal operations running smoothly. SHOPLINE simplifies this process with user-friendly applications designed to transfer data from your current system with ease. Our range of Open APIs enables seamless integration of your existing data into SHOPLINE. You can also choose to upload your data using our templates or work with our third-party partners for comprehensive migration support. 

 

Introduction to Store Migration 

To make the migration smooth and effortless, SHOPLINE provides free migration tools for you to migrate your data . Go to SHOPLINE App Store and install Multi-platform Store Migration, or search for ‘migration’ to see available apps.  

You can use APIs related to products, customers, and orders for your migration by querying the data from the existing system and creating them in the SHOPLINE admin panel.

SHOPLINE also offers CSV templates to help you format your data for imports. 

 

Benefits of Migration

The SHOPLINE migration tool offers significant benefits for merchants, including synchronizing the Feed ID with the advertising channels to retain the original advertising effects. It requires no technical skills or coding; merchants simply provide an API Token. The tool preserves organic traffic by synchronizing SEO data and maintaining original links. The tool accurately migrates comprehensive product, customer, and order information, including product tags, collections, and detailed order data, enhancing the migration experience.

Please make sure you have registered a store before migration. The Orders module serves as the central hub to manage the orders customers placed. With an e-commerce-first design, it streamlines the fulfillment process to ensure your operations are efficient, accurate, and effortless.

### SHOPLINE Affiliate Program Overview

> 来源：[https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/28199912584985-SHOPLINE-Affiliate-Program-Overview](https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/28199912584985-SHOPLINE-Affiliate-Program-Overview)

The SHOPLINE Affiliate Program provides a link-generation tool for global content creators, e-commerce course instructors, and influencers. They can generate links with UTM parameters and showcase these affiliate links across the internet, social media, and offline training. This enables their audience to swiftly create SHOPLINE stores through the affiliate links during their training and education sessions. Simultaneously, they can earn commissions with each successful referral of a new SHOPLINE customer.

With the SHOPLINE Affiliate Program, you can:

Create your exclusive affiliate links
Generate affiliate links with UTM parameters for performance tracking in various scenarios and channels, including sales performance management
Monitor conversion data
Download preset QR codes and posters

Campaign	Each link with UTM parameters is considered as a “campaign”.
Clicks	The “Clicks” for affiliate links includes the total clicks for all campaigns. Each audience member is counted once per day, regardless of the number of clicks on the homepage.

SHOPLINE rewards partners for their contributions to the SHOPLINE ecosystem. All referral earnings are calculated based on the merchant's subscription to SHOPLINE plans or apps. If there are no subscription fees, no earnings will be calculated. Your earnings can come from these four sources:

Referral through your affiliate link: When a customer signs up for a SHOPLINE store plan using your affiliate link and makes a payment.
Successful lead conversion: When a lead you submit successfully converts into a paying customer.
Development store transfer: When a development store you create is transferred to a customer who then subscribes to a SHOPLINE store plan.
App subscription: When a merchant subscribes to an app you've developed.

### Introduction to Leads

> 来源：[https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/28195750448281-Introduction-to-Leads](https://help.shopline.com/hc/en-001/articles/28195750448281-Introduction-to-Leads)

Leads refer to partners contributing leads to SHOPLINE. When potential customers become paid customers of SHOPLINE, partners are eligible for commissions. After submitting a lead, partners can seek additional support from SHOPLINE, such as business introduction materials, product training, and more.

You can create leads in the Partner Portal when you have the following three types of potential customers:

Intention to subscribe to SHOPLINE store packages
Intention to subscribe to SHOPLINE POS packages
Intention to subscribe to SHOPLINE Smartpush packages

 

 

Create Leads

Go to Partner Portal and click on Referrals > Leads.

You can create different types of leads based on the intentions of potential customers. Each type of lead requires filling in customer information and company details. 

You need to provide the Contact details of potential customers as well as Company info, and ensure the authenticity of the contact and customer information.

 

 

Fill in Lead’s Business Information

After you have completed the filling of potential customer contact information and company details, different types of leads will require the input of various business information. You can try to provide as much comprehensive information about the business opportunity as possible, as this will greatly assist SHOPLINE in reviewing the leads and preparing relevant sales and training materials. 

Once all business opportunity details have been filled in, click Submit to complete the lead creation.

 

 

Check Lead’s status

After your leads have been created, SHOPLINE will review the lead based on its validity and uniqueness. Following the lead approval, SHOPLINE will track the customer conversion progress. You can check the status of the lead on the lead list page.


You can click on the business name on the lead list page to view the specific details of the lead.
