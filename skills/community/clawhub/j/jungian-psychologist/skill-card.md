## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for educational Jungian self-reflection, including shadow work, dream exploration, archetypal analysis, active imagination prompts, addiction/recovery reflection, and visual psyche mapping. It is not intended for therapy, diagnosis, crisis support, or replacing qualified human care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake educational Jungian self-reflection for therapy, diagnosis, or crisis support. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for education and self-exploration; seek qualified human support for diagnosis, therapy, suicidality, psychosis, severe dissociation, or other urgent mental-health needs. <br>
Risk: Active imagination, shadow work, or dream exercises can surface overwhelming material. <br>
Mitigation: Stop exercises if overwhelmed and use grounding or professional support before continuing. <br>
Risk: Addiction and recovery content could be misused as a replacement for treatment or emergency care. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use the skill for withdrawal, overdose risk, or replacing addiction treatment; rely on qualified clinical or emergency resources for those situations. <br>
