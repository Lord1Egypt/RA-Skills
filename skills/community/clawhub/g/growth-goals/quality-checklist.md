# Growth Goals Quality Checklist

---

## Goal Foundation

- [ ] Annual GMV target set with margin guardrail
- [ ] All four levers have baselines
- [ ] Lever model validates target is mathematically achievable
- [ ] Channel targets sum to annual total
- [ ] Seasonality-adjusted monthly milestones built

## Ownership

- [ ] Every goal has one named owner
- [ ] Weekly review meeting is on the calendar

## Goal Quality (SMART)

- [ ] Specific (exact number)
- [ ] Measurable (clear metric with data source)
- [ ] Achievable (lever model shows path)
- [ ] Relevant (linked to business priorities)
- [ ] Time-bound (exact end date)

## Review

- [ ] Weekly review time slot protected
- [ ] Action items logged with owner and due date
- [ ] Quarterly recalibration scheduled
