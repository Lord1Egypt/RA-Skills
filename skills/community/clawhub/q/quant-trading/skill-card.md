## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill for educational quantitative trading guidance, including stock analysis templates, strategy explanations, risk metric interpretation, and learning paths. Outputs should be treated as hypothetical guidance rather than personalized investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat stock suggestions or strategy outputs as personalized professional financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as educational and hypothetical, verify market data independently, and consult a licensed financial professional for investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Trading examples and strategy templates may be unsuitable without current data, backtesting, and user-specific risk constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Require independent validation, backtesting, and risk review before applying any strategy or position guidance. <br>
