# MARKETING.md — Coaching & Consulting Business Launch Kit

## Market Analysis

### Market Size
- **60,000+** ICF-certified coaches in the United States
- **800,000+** people self-identify as "coach" or "consultant" in LinkedIn profiles
- **$20B+** US coaching industry, growing 6.7% annually (IBISWorld 2025)
- **73M+ freelancers** in the US — most of whom sell expertise-based services
- **Addressable buyer:** Anyone who monetizes their knowledge and needs to look and sell like a professional

### The Problem
Coaches and consultants universally struggle with three things:
1. **Positioning:** They can describe what they do but not why they're different
2. **Sales conversations:** They give advice for free on discovery calls and wonder why people don't buy
3. **Proof:** They have client wins locked in their heads, not on their website

Generic AI tools don't solve any of these. They produce generic content that sounds like every other coach.

## Differentiation

### What No Competitor at Any Price Offers
1. **Methodology Naming** — the proprietary name for your approach (coaches who name their method charge 2-3x more; competitors skip this or treat it as an afterthought)
2. **Word-for-word objection handling** — Prompt 2 produces scripts, not tips. "Here's how to handle 'I need to think about it'" with actual language, not frameworks.
3. **30-day calendar with specific hooks** — Prompt 3 produces "Here's what I worked in HR for 12 years. I was the person making promotion decisions." Not "write a thought leadership post."
4. **ROI table** in case studies — converts analytics-driven buyers who need to justify the investment to themselves or a partner
