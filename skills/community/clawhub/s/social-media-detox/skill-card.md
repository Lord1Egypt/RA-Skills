## Description: <br>
Break social media addiction with screen-free streaks, urge tracking, and digital wellness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jhillin8](https://clawhub.ai/user/jhillin8) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a digital wellness aid to start social media detox plans, log urges, track screen-free streaks and time reclaimed, and define app or notification boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Urge logs can reveal sensitive emotional patterns and social media habits. <br>
Mitigation: Keep logs local, avoid entering unnecessary sensitive detail, and review content before sharing any summaries. <br>
Risk: App-blocking or notification changes may disrupt expected access to social platforms or notifications. <br>
Mitigation: Review each proposed boundary before applying it and keep a clear reversal path for any device or app setting changes. <br>
