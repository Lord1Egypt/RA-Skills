## Description: <br>
Design rigorous experiments for 3DGS research papers. Recommends datasets, baselines, metrics, ablation matrices. Targets CVPR/ICCV/ECCV/SIGGRAPH/TVCG. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jaccen](https://clawhub.ai/user/jaccen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, developers, and technical authors use this skill to plan rigorous 3D Gaussian Splatting experiments for research papers. It helps select datasets, baselines, metrics, ablations, figures, efficiency analyses, and reviewer-response strategies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fast-moving 3DGS dataset, benchmark, and paper recommendations may become outdated or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommended datasets, baselines, metrics, papers, and venue expectations against current authoritative sources before relying on the plan. <br>
