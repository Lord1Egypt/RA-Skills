## Description: <br>
Translate Indonesian song lyrics into natural-sounding English while preserving singability, rhythm, emotional intent, and cultural nuance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[polacapital](https://clawhub.ai/user/polacapital) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Beralio and collaborators use this skill to adapt Indonesian lyrics into English for international release, sync licensing, or collaboration with English-speaking artists while keeping lines natural and singable. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Translated lyrics may alter meaning, singability, or cultural nuance. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for meaning, rhythm, emotional intent, and suitability before release or collaboration. <br>
Risk: Lyric adaptation may raise copyright, authorship, or disclosure expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm copyright and authorship requirements, and disclose AI assistance when collaborators, publishers, platforms, or licensing partners require it. <br>
