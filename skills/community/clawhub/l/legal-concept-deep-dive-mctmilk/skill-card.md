## Description: <br>
深入分析法条中的不确定法律概念（如"合理期限"、"重大误解"、"明显不当"）的内涵、外延、裁判标准和边界案例。当用户想穿透法条中模糊词汇的具体含义时使用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to analyze ambiguous legal terms in statutes, including legislative intent, mainstream academic interpretation, judicial guidance, practical judgment factors, boundary cases, and common misunderstandings. <br>

Risk: Legal interpretations and example cases may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific dispute. <br>
Mitigation: Verify statutes, judicial interpretations, cases, and legal conclusions against authoritative sources or a qualified legal professional before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Boundary-case examples generated by an agent may not correspond to real or current adjudication outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Check any cited or AI-analyzed cases against authoritative case databases before using them in legal work. <br>
