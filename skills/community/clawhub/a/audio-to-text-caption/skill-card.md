## Description: <br>
Turn creator audio into clean text captions for ecommerce content and reuse. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content, ecommerce, and social-video teams use this skill to convert short creator audio into a cleaned transcript, caption-ready text, and manual review notes for unclear segments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audio may include unclear speech or material the user does not have permission to process. <br>
Mitigation: Use only audio you have permission to process, and review unclear segments before reusing the transcript or captions. <br>
Risk: The artifact states non-commercial CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 terms for reuse and requires a separate paid commercial license from Razestar for commercial use. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the authoritative license terms before distribution or commercial reuse, and obtain the required commercial license when applicable. <br>
