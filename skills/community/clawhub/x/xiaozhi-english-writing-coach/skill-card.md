## Description: <br>
英语写作教练 helps learners improve English writing through targeted grammar, vocabulary, and logic feedback, guided revision prompts, and opt-in progress tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Learners and writing coaches use this skill to review English essays, emails, speeches, and practice scenarios. It provides focused feedback on a few high-impact issues, asks students to revise rather than simply supplying rewritten text, and can summarize progress when the user permits tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may track writing habits or progress across sessions, which can expose sensitive personal writing if users submit private essays, applications, emails, or other confidential material. <br>
Mitigation: Use progress tracking only with clear user consent, avoid submitting sensitive writing, and review or delete retained writing history where the host agent supports it. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad English writing requests, so users may receive coaching behavior when they expected a simple edit. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's goal before using coaching workflows, especially when the user asks only for a quick check or direct rewrite. <br>
