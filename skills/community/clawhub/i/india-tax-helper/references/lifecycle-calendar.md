## Common forget points
- Did not submit declaration in time → higher TDS through the year
- Did not submit proofs in time → employer deducts more TDS in last 2-3 months
- Did not reconcile AIS / TIS / 26AS / broker data → mismatches and notices
- Forgot to file ITR → penalties, loss of carry-forward, refund forfeiture
- Assumed bank/broker TDS already means everything is settled → additional tax may still be payable
- Missed advance tax deadlines → interest u/s 234B and 234C

## Interest on late filing / short payment
- **234A**: Interest on tax shortfall if ITR filed after due date (1% per month)
- **234B**: Interest if advance tax paid is less than 90% of assessed tax (1% per month)
- **234C**: Interest for deferment of advance tax instalments (1% per month for 3 months)
- **234F**: Late filing fee — Rs 5,000 if filed after due date but before 31-Dec; Rs 1,000 if total income <= 5L
