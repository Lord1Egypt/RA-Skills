## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through public competitor research to analyze product features, pricing, reviews, weaknesses, and differentiation opportunities for websites or Mini Programs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qiu-difeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/qiu-difeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, founders, and agents conducting market research use this skill to inspect public competitor pages, pricing, user reviews, traction signals, and recurring complaints, then turn those findings into exploitable product gaps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor research may collect or summarize information outside the user's intended scope, including information whose use could conflict with site terms or privacy expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the competitor name or URL and research scope before use, rely on public sources, and avoid confidential internal materials or prohibited data. <br>
Risk: Public reviews, traffic claims, and technology signals can be incomplete, stale, or noisy, leading to misleading weakness scores or product-gap conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check claims across multiple public sources and treat single complaints as noise unless the skill's stated threshold is met. <br>
