## Description: <br>
Conducts procurement assessments using company inputs to evaluate maturity, identify savings opportunities, prioritize automation, and produce a 90-day improvement roadmap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business operators and procurement teams use this skill to assess procurement maturity, benchmark spend processes, rank cost-saving opportunities, and plan procurement automation work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive procurement information such as spend estimates, supplier lists, contract gaps, and compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for the user's agent environment and review generated procurement recommendations before using them for business decisions. <br>
