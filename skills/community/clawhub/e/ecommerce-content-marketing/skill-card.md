## Description: <br>
E-commerce content marketing strategy planner that generates content calendars, topic ideas, and platform-specific strategies by analyzing customer reviews, trends, competitor content, and SEO opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/phheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce operators, marketers, and content teams use this skill to build or audit content strategies for product launches, social platforms, blog/SEO, and Amazon A+ content. It helps turn public customer reviews, trend research, competitor content, and SEO opportunities into calendars, topic ideas, content pillars, and next-step recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide credentials, private analytics, or non-public account information when asking for an audit. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only public brand, product, website, and social profile information needed for the strategy; avoid credentials and private analytics. <br>
Risk: Public web research can miss private social analytics, exact engagement metrics, paid trend data, or recent platform changes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated calendars and recommendations as planning guidance, then verify visible metrics, current trends, and platform requirements before publishing. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes an npx-based installation command. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the package and install source before running the command in a local agent environment. <br>
