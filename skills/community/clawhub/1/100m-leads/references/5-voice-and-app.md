## 5 Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Zero Leads, Zero Customers, Just Starting
**You have a great product but nobody knows about it.**
- **What to do:** 1) This is a Warm Outreach problem, not a content problem. 2) Open your phone contacts. 3) Count them. 4) Start at 100/day using the ACA framework. 5) Offer your first 5 for free. 6) Get testimonials. 7) Start charging when referrals begin.
- **Book quote:** "This process alone can take you to $100,000+ per year with nothing else. 500 reach outs per week = 5 customers per week → $2,000/wk → $104,000/year."

### Scenario 2: Paid Ads Losing Money
**You're spending money on ads but not getting sales.**
- **What to do:** 1) Check your lead magnet before your ad creative. Is it valuable enough? 2) Check your landing page — does it convert? 3) Test three different headlines. Ogilvy: 80 cents of your ad dollar is the headline. 4) Are you sending them to a sale page or a lead magnet? Lead magnet first, always. 5) Track ROAS (Return on Ad Spend). Hormozi's target: 36:1.
- **Book quote:** "The best strategy I know for scarcity is — reality. If you sold 1000x the customers tomorrow, could you handle it? If not, you have some limit. Don't keep it a secret — advertise it."

### Scenario 3: Building an Audience from Scratch
**You want to grow your social media following but don't know where to start.**
- **What to do:** 1) Accept: there is no secret. The answer is volume. 2) Pick 2 platforms max. 3) Post 3-5x/day on each. 4) Every post needs Hook → Retain → Reward. 5) Use personal stories from Far Past, Recent Past, and Present. 6) 10x your volume for 60 days. Measure the difference.
- **Book quote:** "Volume works. Content works. A growing audience is the result."

### Scenario 4: Building an Affiliate / Referral Program
**You want others to get leads for you.**
- **What to do:** 1) Referrals from customers are easiest — ask every happy customer to send 3 friends. 2) Affiliates need: great product + attractive commission + easy-to-share assets. 3) Agencies: find agencies that serve your customers and partner with them. 4) Employees: hire dedicated lead generation people once you have the budget. 5) Start with referrals before affiliates — they're free.
- **Book insight:** Section IV covers Get Lead Getters: Customer Referrals → Employees → Agencies → Affiliates and Partners. Each builds on the previous.

### Scenario 5: "I've Tried Everything and Nothing Works"
**You've attempted multiple lead gen methods with no results.**
- **Self-diagnosis:** The problem is either (a) not enough volume, (b) not enough skill, or (c) both. Pick ONE method. Do 100/day for 30 days. Track everything. Adjust. If it still doesn't work after 30 days at volume, try a different method. But don't judge a method at 5 attempts.
- **Book quote:** "If you have thousands of nos, you will get your yeses, I promise. I always tell myself: Yeses give me opportunity. Nos give me feedback. Either way, I win."
