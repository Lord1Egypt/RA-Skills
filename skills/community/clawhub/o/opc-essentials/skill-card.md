## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals, payment nudges, renewal plans, or other client-facing messages may include assumptions or tone choices that do not fit the relationship. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt client-facing output before sending, especially invoice, proposal, sales, and renewal language. <br>
Risk: The skill may be used with sensitive client names, invoices, sales notes, renewal details, or business pipeline information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the context needed for the task and follow normal privacy and confidentiality practices for client and payment information. <br>
