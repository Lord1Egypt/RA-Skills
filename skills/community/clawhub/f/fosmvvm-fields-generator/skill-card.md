This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers use this skill to generate reusable FOSMVVM form specifications for Swift projects, including form field protocols, validation message structs, and YAML localization files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated Swift or YAML may encode incorrect validation rules, localization keys, or form behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated files before committing or running them, especially validation and localization output that affects app behavior. <br>
