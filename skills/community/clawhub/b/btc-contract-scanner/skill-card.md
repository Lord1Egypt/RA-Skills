## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Telegram alerts may expose trading signals or strategy information to Telegram, a bot, or chat participants. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending API keys, account balances, open positions, or personal identifiers unless the publisher documents how that data is handled. <br>
Risk: Trading signals and rolling backtest results may be inaccurate, stale, or unsuitable for a user's financial situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational guidance, review signals before trading, and apply independent risk controls. <br>
