## Description: <br>
Audits games, features, progression systems, social loops, monetization surfaces, and onboarding flows against a granular player motivation taxonomy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and researchers use this skill to audit how a game design or feature serves, neglects, or frustrates granular player motivation segments and to turn the findings into design recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process confidential game plans, monetization concepts, or private user research supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide sensitive material when authorized, and redact details that are not needed for the motivation audit. <br>
Risk: The release metadata includes crypto and purchase capability tags despite the security summary describing a documentation-only skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review installation metadata before use and grant only the permissions needed for text-based analysis. <br>
