## Description: <br>
Creates a traditional SEO roadmap for early-stage startups seeking SEO strategy, keyword research, and organic search growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup operators use this skill to answer discovery questions and receive tailored SEO priorities, keyword strategy, technical SEO foundations, content planning, link building ideas, and a 90-day action plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations can be incorrect, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific business context. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggested website, content, and link-building changes before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Discovery answers may include confidential business strategy or competitive details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential strategy details unless they are appropriate for the agent using the skill. <br>
