## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The JustOneAPI token is sent as a URL query parameter, which can expose credentials through logs, browser history, or copied request URLs. <br>
Mitigation: Use a low-scope, revocable token; avoid sharing logs or full request URLs; and rotate the token if it may have been exposed. <br>
