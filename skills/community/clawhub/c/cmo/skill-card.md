## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing recommendations may be unsuitable for high-impact decisions such as major repositioning, crisis communications, large campaign budgets, or public competitive messaging. <br>
Mitigation: Use human review before applying guidance to high-impact marketing, communications, budget, agency, or competitive decisions. <br>
Risk: Strategic guidance may rely on incomplete business context or unvalidated market assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against customer evidence, sales feedback, budget constraints, and current channel performance before execution. <br>
