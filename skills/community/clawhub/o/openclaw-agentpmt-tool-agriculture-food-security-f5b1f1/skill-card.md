## Description: <br>
Find and install the current canonical AgentPMT skills published under the AgentPMT ClawHub org account. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[firef1ie](https://clawhub.ai/user/firef1ie) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this index to choose current AgentPMT setup, overview, payment, and product skills and route users to canonical ClawHub pages and install commands. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Linked AgentPMT skills may involve bearer tokens, x402 payments, or external services. <br>
Mitigation: Review each linked skill's permissions, credential handling, and pricing before installing or using it. <br>
Risk: The index can become stale because it points to current AgentPMT skill listings. <br>
Mitigation: Check the AgentPMT ClawHub publisher page before relying on the index when it is more than seven days past the listed update date. <br>
