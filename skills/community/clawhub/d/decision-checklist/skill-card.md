## Description: <br>
决策清单 helps an agent structure investment decisions with capability-circle checks, cognitive-bias review, reverse thinking, multidisciplinary reasoning, scoring, and action guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and investment-focused agents use this skill to turn an investment idea into a structured checklist that surfaces knowledge limits, cognitive biases, downside scenarios, risk level, scoring, and suggested next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce ratings, investment labels, position-sizing suggestions, and buy-strategy guidance that may be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision-support prompts, verify conclusions independently, and use qualified financial advice where appropriate. <br>
Risk: The skill cannot verify current market facts, prices, company fundamentals, or news before producing an investment checklist. <br>
Mitigation: Pair the checklist with current, independent market research before making or changing an investment position. <br>
