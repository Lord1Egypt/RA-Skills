## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to generate Bazi astrology charts from a birth date, birth hour, optional gender, and optional annual forecast year. It produces traditional Chinese astrology outputs such as four pillars, ten gods, five-element counts, luck cycles, pattern judgment, useful-god selection, and interpretive readings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses personal birth date, birth hour, and gender, which may be sensitive if retained in the local agent session or logs. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide details you are comfortable sharing, avoid entering real details for other people without consent, and manage local logs according to your privacy requirements. <br>
Risk: Fortune-telling, health, financial, relationship, or life-planning interpretations may be inaccurate or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as entertainment or cultural interpretation, not as medical, financial, legal, or life-planning advice. <br>
Risk: The artifact implements simplified calendar and interpretation rules, so generated charts may not match specialized Bazi systems. <br>
Mitigation: Review important results with an appropriate expert or trusted reference before relying on them. <br>
