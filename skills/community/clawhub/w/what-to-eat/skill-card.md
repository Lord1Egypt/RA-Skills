## Description: <br>
随机推荐今天吃什么。支持按菜系、口味、预算、人数筛选。Use when: user asks what to eat, meal recommendations, food suggestions, 吃什么, 今天吃啥, restaurant ideas, or needs help deciding on food. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junweiyuan](https://clawhub.ai/user/junweiyuan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to get casual meal suggestions when deciding what to eat. It can tailor recommendations by cuisine, taste, budget, group size, dietary limits, meal time, and dining scenario. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meal suggestions may be unsuitable for users with allergies, dietary restrictions, budget limits, or language preferences if those constraints are not provided. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the user for allergies, dietary restrictions, budget, preferred language, and other meal constraints before making recommendations when those details matter. <br>
