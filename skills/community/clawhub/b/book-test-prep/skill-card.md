## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to search for test-prep providers, check availability, and create bookings through Lokuli's MCP service. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Booking creation sends customer contact details to an external Lokuli service. <br>
Mitigation: Before creating a booking, confirm the provider, service, time slot, customer name, email, and phone number with the user. <br>
