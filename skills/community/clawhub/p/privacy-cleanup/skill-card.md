## Description: <br>
Guides agents through removing personal data from data brokers and people-search sites, reducing tracking, and handling privacy cleanup for general privacy or stalking-related concerns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to guide an agent through finding exposed personal data, submitting opt-out requests to data brokers, and reducing future data collection. It is also intended to help prioritize address-revealing sites and safety resources when the user is facing stalking or harassment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow may involve sensitive personal details such as names, addresses, phone numbers, and stalking-related context. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive details in files or logs unless the user explicitly needs a record, and minimize personal data shared with the agent. <br>
Risk: Some broker opt-out processes require submitting personal identifiers or verification details. <br>
Mitigation: Review each broker site before submitting data and use a dedicated email address or phone number where possible. <br>
Risk: Users facing stalking or harassment may need safety-sensitive prioritization before routine privacy cleanup. <br>
Mitigation: Prioritize address-revealing sites first and direct users to relevant safety resources or local authorities when appropriate. <br>
