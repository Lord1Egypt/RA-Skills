## Description: <br>
Calculates and benchmarks key unit economics metrics like CAC, LTV, payback period, contribution margin, segment performance, and scenario outcomes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, founders, finance teams, and analysts use this skill to calculate unit economics, compare them with industry benchmarks, identify unprofitable segments, and model changes to pricing, churn, retention, and acquisition cost. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on calculated metrics or benchmarks as financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance and validate assumptions, calculations, and decisions with qualified finance or business reviewers. <br>
Risk: The analysis can involve sensitive business metrics such as revenue, churn, margins, acquisition costs, and customer segment performance. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the metrics needed for the analysis and avoid unnecessary confidential, customer-identifying, or proprietary details. <br>
Risk: The artifact links to promotional external resources and paid context packs. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate external links and purchases separately before relying on or buying related materials. <br>
