## Description: <br>
Writes academically rigorous Abstract/摘要 and Keywords/关键词 sections for theses and academic papers from user-provided research material and requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and academic writers use this skill to draft, polish, shorten, expand, structure, translate, or generate thesis abstracts and keyword lists from supplied thesis content and formatting requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential thesis material into the agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential or sensitive research material unless the user is comfortable with that environment handling it. <br>
Risk: Generated abstracts can imply unsupported methods, findings, or practical implications when source material is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Use only user-supplied thesis evidence, ask for missing method/result details, and review the final abstract for factual accuracy. <br>
Risk: School, journal, or department formatting requirements may differ from the skill defaults. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the required language, word or character limit, abstract structure, and keyword conventions, then verify the output against those requirements. <br>
