## Description: <br>
TikTok Shop profit margin calculator for sellers that estimates cost breakdowns including affiliate commissions, platform fees, FBT costs, and return-rate effects without requiring an API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sellers and ecommerce operators use this skill to estimate per-product profitability, break-even price, and pricing targets for marketplace sales. Review the marketplace assumptions before relying on outputs for pricing or inventory decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Security review reports that the skill is advertised for TikTok Shop while the shipped calculator uses Amazon/FBA-style profitability assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Align the documentation, formulas, input names, and report labels to one clearly stated marketplace before relying on pricing or inventory decisions. <br>
