## Use Case: <br>
Course creators, educators, and teams use this skill to plan Udemy course topics, draft lessons and supporting materials, prepare publishing assets, manage student interactions, and check platform compliance before publishing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated course materials, marketing copy, or Q&A drafts may contain inaccurate, low-quality, or misleading content. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit all generated materials before publication, add instructor expertise and examples, and validate factual claims against authoritative sources. <br>
Risk: Udemy policy or disclosure requirements may change after the skill guidance was written. <br>
Mitigation: Verify Udemy's current instructor terms, GenAI policy, and quality requirements before publishing or updating a course. <br>
Risk: Local notes about students, reviews, revenue, or course performance could expose private operational information if shared carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Keep course management records private and avoid sharing student or revenue details unless they are intentionally prepared for disclosure. <br>
