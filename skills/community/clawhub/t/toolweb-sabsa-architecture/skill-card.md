## Use Case: <br>
Security architects, CISOs, enterprise risk managers, and compliance leaders use this skill to assess security architecture maturity against the SABSA framework, identify capability gaps, and plan security program improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Submitting enterprise architecture assessment data to a third-party service may expose sensitive security details or internal identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the data can be shared with the provider; prefer sanitized or high-level inputs, omit userId when possible, and exclude secrets, production identifiers, regulated data, and unnecessary internal security details. <br>
