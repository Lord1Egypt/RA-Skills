## Description: <br>
End-of-day review to capture progress, insights, and plan tomorrow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ItsFlow](https://clawhub.ai/user/ItsFlow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and individual contributors use this skill to review the day's modified and new notes, summarize progress, capture insights, identify blockers, and prepare tomorrow's priorities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read project notes in the workspace where it is run. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only in workspaces where note review is acceptable. <br>
Risk: The skill may propose edits to daily notes, task archives, project status notes, or flagged items. <br>
Mitigation: Preview proposed note edits, task archives, and status updates before saving them, especially in important or shared projects. <br>
