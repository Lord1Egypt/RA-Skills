## Description: <br>
Style Design Generator helps agents create, mix, extend, and adapt visual style prompt schemes using bundled font-design and infographic reference templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, writers, game creators, and creative agents use this skill to generate style prompts, remix visual attributes, extend style libraries, or adapt a style scheme to domains such as comics, fiction, games, film, product design, typography, and infographics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
