## Description: <br>
Meeting Minutes AI helps users turn meeting transcripts or notes into structured minutes with key decisions, action items, owners, and follow-up details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[awdasfdada](https://clawhub.ai/user/awdasfdada) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external collaborators, and knowledge workers use this skill to convert meeting recordings, transcripts, or notes into structured meeting minutes. It is intended for weekly meetings, reviews, one-on-ones, project kickoffs, retrospectives, and similar business meetings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting transcripts and notes may contain confidential business information or personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide meeting content that is approved for processing in the user's agent environment. <br>
Risk: Generated minutes may omit, misattribute, or overstate decisions and action items from the source meeting content. <br>
Mitigation: Review the minutes against the original transcript or notes before distributing or using them for follow-up. <br>
