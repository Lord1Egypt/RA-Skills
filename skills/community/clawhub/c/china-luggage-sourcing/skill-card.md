## Description: <br>
Comprehensive luggage and bag industry sourcing guide for international buyers - provides detailed information about China's travel luggage, handbags, backpacks, and specialty bag manufacturing clusters, supply chain structure, regional specializations, and industry trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
