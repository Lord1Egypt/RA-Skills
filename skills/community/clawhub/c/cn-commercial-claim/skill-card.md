External vehicle owners and claims-support users in mainland China use this skill for commercial auto insurance claims process guidance, material checklists, payout formula explanations, and refusal-risk reminders. It is informational only and does not replace insurer loss assessment or legal advice. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Claims guidance may be mistaken for final insurer, legal, or professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as an informational checklist only and verify current requirements with the insurer or a qualified professional. <br>
Risk: Claims questions may involve identity details, bank information, medical records, or third-party documents. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting full ID numbers, bank details, medical records, or third-party documents into an agent unless the environment is trusted and sharing is necessary. <br>
