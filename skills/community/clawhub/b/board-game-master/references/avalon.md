# Avalon / 阿瓦隆 — Strategy Guide

## Game Overview / 游戏概述

The Resistance: Avalon is a social deduction game for 5-10 players. Players are divided into Good (亚瑟阵营/Arthur's side) and Evil (莫德雷德阵营/Mordred's side). The game revolves around 5 missions — Good wins by passing 3 missions; Evil wins by failing 3 missions OR by assassinating Merlin at the end.

阿瓦隆是5-10人的社交推理游戏。玩家分为好人（亚瑟阵营）和坏人（莫德雷德阵营）。游戏围绕5个任务——好人通过3个任务获胜；坏人失败3个任务或在最后刺杀梅林获胜。

## Player Count Configurations / 人数配置

| Players / 人数 | Good / 好人 | Evil / 坏人 | Mission Sizes (1-5) / 任务人数 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 5 | 3 | 2 | 2, 3, 2, 3, 3 |
| 6 | 4 | 2 | 2, 3, 4, 3, 4 |
| 7 | 4 | 3 | 2, 3, 3, 4*, 4 |
| 8 | 5 | 3 | 3, 4, 4, 5*, 5 |
| 9 | 6 | 3 | 3, 4, 4, 5*, 5 |
| 10 | 6 | 4 | 3, 4, 4, 5*, 5 |

*Mission 4 requires 2 fails in 7+ player games. 7人以上游戏第4个任务需要2个失败。

## Roles / 角色

### Good Side / 好人阵营

**Merlin / 梅林**
- Knows all evil players (except Mordred)
- Must help good win WITHOUT being identified by evil
- If good wins 3 missions, the Assassin gets one chance to kill Merlin — if correct, evil wins
- 知道所有坏人（莫德雷德除外）。必须帮助好人获胜但不能被坏人识别。

**Percival / 派西维尔**
- Knows who Merlin and Morgana are (but not which is which)
- Shields Merlin by providing cover
- 知道梅林和莫甘娜是谁（但不知道哪个是哪个）。保护梅林。

**Loyal Servants of Arthur / 亚瑟的忠臣**
- No special information
- Must deduce alignment through voting patterns, mission results, and behavior
- 没有特殊信息。必须通过投票模式、任务结果和行为推断阵营。

### Evil Side / 坏人阵营

**Assassin / 刺客**
- Standard evil player
- After good wins 3 missions, gets to attempt Merlin assassination
- 标准坏人。好人赢3个任务后，尝试刺杀梅林。

**Morgana / 莫甘娜**
- Appears as Merlin to Percival
- Creates confusion about who the real Merlin is
- 对派西维尔显示为梅林。制造关于谁是真梅林的混乱。

**Mordred / 莫德雷德**
- Hidden from Merlin
- Merlin doesn't know Mordred is evil — the blind spot
- 对梅林隐藏。梅林不知道莫德雷德是坏人——盲点。

**Oberon / 奥伯伦**
- Evil but unknown to other evil players (and doesn't know them)
- A wild card — often accidentally fails missions that evil wanted to pass
- 坏人但其他坏人不知道（也不知道其他坏人）。外卡——经常意外搞砸坏人的计划。

**Lady of the Lake / 湖中女士**
- Not a role but a game variant token
- Holder can check one player's alignment, then passes the token to them
- Powerful mid-game information tool
- 不是角色而是游戏变体道具。持有者可以检查一个玩家的阵营，然后传递给他们。

## As Merlin / 梅林策略

**The core tension:** You know everything, but revealing too much gets you killed.

核心矛盾：你什么都知道，但暴露太多会被杀。

### Information Drip / 信息滴灌
- Don't reject evil players on team 1 — you shouldn't "know" yet
- Gradually build suspicion over rounds 2-4
- Frame your reads as logical deductions, not certainty: "I think X is suspicious because of their voting pattern"
- 不要在第1轮就拒绝坏人——你不应该"知道"。逐渐在第2-4轮建立怀疑。

### Voting Patterns / 投票模式
- Approve some teams with evil on them early (rounds 1-2) to avoid looking omniscient
- Reject teams later when you have "reasons"
- Mirror what a good reader would naturally do
- 早期通过一些有坏人的队伍以避免看起来全知。后期有"理由"时拒绝。

### Hiding Among Good Readers / 藏在好读者中
- The best Merlin play is when 2-3 good players independently reach similar conclusions
- Nudge others toward correct reads so you're not the only one pointing at evil
- If only you are accusing someone, you're painting a target on yourself
- 最好的梅林打法是2-3个好人独立得出类似结论。引导他人做出正确判断。

### Merlin Don'ts / 梅林禁忌
- Never say "I know X is evil" — there's no good cover for that
- Don't reject every evil-containing team
- Don't avoid eye contact with evil players (body language tells)
- Don't go silent — a quiet player who always votes correctly screams Merlin

## As Percival / 派西维尔策略

### Identifying Real Merlin / 识别真梅林
- Watch who plays like they have information vs who plays like they're faking it
- Merlin's reads will be correct; Morgana's may contradict mission results
- Over 2-3 rounds, Merlin's voting pattern is consistently correct
- 观察谁像有信息 vs 谁像在假装。梅林的判断会正确；莫甘娜的可能与任务结果矛盾。

### Shielding Merlin / 保护梅林
- Once you identify Merlin, make similar accusations yourself
- Be the louder voice so Assassin targets you instead
- If you're confident, take credit for Merlin's reads publicly
- 一旦识别梅林，自己做出类似指控。做更大声的声音让刺客瞄准你。

### The Percival Gambit / 派西维尔赌博
- Claim Merlin openly to draw the assassination attempt
- Extremely risky — only do this if Merlin is obviously about to be identified
- Works best in experienced groups where the bluff is believable
- 公开声称自己是梅林以吸引刺杀。极度冒险——只在梅林明显要被识别时使用。

## As Evil / 坏人策略

### When to Fail vs Pass / 何时失败 vs 通过

**Fail when:**
- You're confident the mission will be blamed on someone else
- It's mission 4 (needs 2 fails in 7+) and both evil are on the team
- Losing this mission would make it 3-0 or 3-1 for good

**Pass when:**
- You're the only evil on a 2-person mission (instant exposure)
- Building trust for a critical later mission
- You need to stay clean for the assassination read

何时失败：确信失败会被怪到别人头上；第4任务两个坏人都在队里；不能让好人3-0或3-1。
何时通过：你是2人任务中唯一的坏人（会暴露）；为后期关键任务建立信任；需要保持干净以判断刺杀目标。

### Reverse Psychology / 反向心理
- Occasionally advocate for rejecting a team you're on (then "reluctantly" accept)
- Accuse a fellow evil player mildly to look like a good reader
- 偶尔主张拒绝你所在的队伍（然后"勉强"接受）。温和地指控同伴坏人以看起来像好的读者。

### Mission 4 with 2 Fails / 第4任务需2个失败
- In 7+ player games, mission 4 requires 2 fails
- Evil needs both evil on the team to fail it
- If only 1 evil is on the team, PASS — a single fail wastes a mission without failing it
- 7人以上游戏第4任务需要2个失败。只有1个坏人在队伍中时应该通过。

### Assassination Targeting / 刺杀目标选择
- Watch who has the best reads throughout the game
- Merlin often votes correctly more than other good players
- Look for the player who "always knew" but never fully committed to saying it
- The quiet, consistently correct voter is usually Merlin
- 观察谁全程判断最准。梅林通常比其他好人投票更正确。安静但始终正确的投票者通常是梅林。

## As Loyal Servants / 忠臣策略

### Vote Analysis / 投票分析
- Track every approve/reject vote in a grid
- Players who approve teams that fail are suspect
- Players who consistently reject teams that pass are likely evil (trying to block good missions)
- 追踪每次通过/拒绝投票。通过失败任务队伍的玩家可疑。

### Team Composition / 队伍组成
- Propose teams that give you information regardless of outcome
- Include yourself + people you're testing
- If a mission fails, you know evil is among your teammates (not you)
- 提出无论结果都能给你信息的队伍。包含自己+你在测试的人。

### Trust Chains / 信任链
- If you went on mission 1 (2 players) and it passed, you and your partner are semi-confirmed
- Build from confirmed pairs
- Be wary of evil passing early missions to build false trust
- 如果你参加了任务1（2人）且通过了，你和搭档半确认。从确认的配对开始构建。

## Advanced Tactics / 高级战术

### Hammer Position / 锤子位置
- The 5th vote rejection forces the mission to go with the current leader's team
- If you're the last voter before the hammer, your leverage is enormous
- Evil can exploit hammer to force bad teams through
- 第5次投票拒绝后任务必须使用当前队长的队伍。如果你是锤子前的最后投票者，你的影响力巨大。

### 5th Mission Tactics / 第5任务战术
- When it's 2-2 in missions, the 5th mission is everything
- Good should minimize team size and include only proven players
- Evil will fight hard to get on this team — watch desperation levels
- 当任务2-2时，第5任务决定一切。好人应最小化队伍大小。

### Lady of the Lake Timing / 湖中女士时机
- Use early (round 2-3) to establish a trust anchor
- Pass to someone you want to verify — not someone you already trust
- Evil holding Lady can lie about results — cross-reference with other info
- 早期使用（第2-3轮）建立信任锚点。传递给你想验证的人。

### Voting Matrix Analysis / 投票矩阵分析

Track votes in a grid:

```
         Team1  Team2  Team3  Team4
PlayerA   ✓      ✗      ✓      ✓
PlayerB   ✓      ✓      ✗      ✓
PlayerC   ✗      ✓      ✓      ✗
```

- Look for voting correlation between players — evil players tend to vote similarly
- Cross-reference votes with mission outcomes
- 寻找玩家间的投票关联——坏人倾向于投票相似。与任务结果交叉验证。

### Meta-Gaming / 元游戏
- Body language, hesitation, and tone of voice are all fair game
- "Table talk" — how someone discusses the game reveals alignment
- Experienced evil players will fake body language tells — look for inconsistency over time
- 肢体语言、犹豫和语气都是公平的判断依据。经验丰富的坏人会伪造肢体语言——寻找长期不一致。

## Common Mistakes / 常见错误

**As Good:**
- Approving every team proposed (gives evil free passes)
- Not tracking votes systematically
- Trusting mission 1 pass too much (evil often passes early)
- Merlin being too obvious with reads
- Percival not providing cover for Merlin

**As Evil:**
- Failing the first mission (instant suspicion)
- Both evil failing on the same mission when only 1 fail needed
- Not preparing an assassination target throughout the game
- Accusing fellow evil too aggressively (looks coordinated when revealed)
- Oberon failing missions randomly without strategy

好人常见错误：通过所有提议的队伍；不系统追踪投票；过度信任任务1通过；梅林太明显。
坏人常见错误：失败第一个任务；两个坏人同时失败；不准备刺杀目标；过度指控同伴。
