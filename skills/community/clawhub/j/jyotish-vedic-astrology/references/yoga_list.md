# Complete Yoga List - 完整瑜伽格局

## Royal Yogas (Raja Yogas - 王侯瑜伽)

### Basic Raja Yoga Formation
- **Definition**: Kendra lord (1, 4, 7, 10) + Trikona lord (1, 5, 9) in Kendra, Trikona, or mutual aspect
- **Strength Factors**:
  - Both lords in own sign or exaltation → strongest Raja Yoga
  - In friendly signs → moderate strength
  - In enemy signs → weak Raja Yoga
  - Conjoined by malefic → diminished strength
  - In 6th, 8th, 12th houses → yoga weak or cancelled

### Specific Raja Yogas
1. **Bhadra Yoga**: Mercury in 1, 4, 7, 10 in own sign or exaltation
2. **Hamsa Yoga**: Jupiter in 1, 4, 7, 10 in own sign or exaltation
3. **Malavya Yoga**: Venus in 1, 4, 7, 10 in own sign or exaltation
4. **Ruchaka Yoga**: Mars in 1, 4, 7, 10 in own sign or exaltation
5. **Shasha Yoga**: Saturn in 1, 4, 7, 10 in own sign or exaltation

## Wealth Yogas (Dhana Yogas - 财富瑜伽)

### Primary Dhana Yogas
- **2nd + 11th Lord Connection**:
  - Lords conjoined in any house
  - Lords in mutual aspect
  - Exchange of signs (Parivartana Yoga)
  - Both in Kendra/Trikona → strongest

- **5th + 9th Lord Connection** (Lakshmi Yoga):
  - Conjunction or aspect between 5th and 9th lords
  - Indicates fortune, blessings, spiritual wealth

### Additional Wealth Indicators
- Benefics in 2nd, 5th, 9th, 11th houses
- 2nd lord in 11th house or 11th lord in 2nd house
- Jupiter + Venus conjunction or mutual aspect
- Mercury in 2nd house in own sign

## Cancellation of Debility Yogas (Neechabhanga Raja Yoga)

### Formation Rules
Planet in debilitation (Neecha) can be cancelled by:

1. **Owner of Debilitation Sign in Kendra/Trikona**
   - Example: Jupiter debilitated in Capricorn; Saturn (Capricorn lord) in 1, 4, 7, 10

2. **Exalted Planet in Kendra from Moon**
   - Any exalted planet in Kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) from Moon Lagna

3. **Planet in Exaltation Navamsa**
   - Neecha planet occupies exalted sign in D9 Navamsa

4. **Exchange with Debilitation Lord**
   - Parivartana between Neecha planet and its debilitation sign lord

5. **Neecha Planet with Exalted Planet**
   - Neecha planet conjoined by exalted planet

**Result**: Person rises from humble beginnings to great heights (rags to riches)

## Moon-Based Yogas

### Gaja Kesari Yoga (Elephant-Lion Yoga)
- **Formation**: Jupiter + Moon in Kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) or mutual aspect
- **Effects**: Wisdom, fame, leadership, spiritual blessings, respected by all
- **Strength**: Stronger in own signs/exaltation, weaker with malefic affliction

### Kemadruma Yoga (Moon Isolation)
- **Formation**: Moon without planets in:
  - Adjacent houses (2nd, 12th)
  - Kendra houses (1, 4, 7, 10)
  - With Jupiter (partial cancellation)
- **Effects**: Emotional insecurity, isolation, struggles in early life
- **Cancellation**: Jupiter in Kendra from Moon, benefics in 2nd/12th

### Sunapha Yoga
- **Formation**: Planet(s) in 2nd from Moon (except Sun)
- **Effects**: Self-made, earning through own efforts

### Anapha Yoga
- **Formation**: Planet(s) in 12th from Moon (except Sun)
- **Effects**: Charismatic, supportive, fortunate

### Durudhara Yoga
- **Formation**: Planets in both 2nd and 12th from Moon
- **Effects**: Wealthy, powerful, influential

## Special Combination Yogas

### Budhaditya Yoga (Mercury + Sun)
- **Formation**: Sun + Mercury conjunction (unless combust)
- **Effects**: Intelligence, eloquence, writing ability, analytical mind

### Shukra-Mangala Yoga (Venus + Mars)
- **Formation**: Venus + Mars conjunction
- **Effects**: Creative energy, artistic talent, romantic nature

### Guru-Chandala Yoga (Jupiter + Rahu)
- **Formation**: Jupiter + Rahu conjunction
- **Effects**: Spiritual challenges, unconventional beliefs, risk of unethical behavior

### Chandra-Mangala Yoga (Moon + Mars)
- **Formation**: Moon + Mars conjunction
- **Effects**: Financial struggles, emotional volatility, maternal health issues

### Guru-Shukra Yoga (Jupiter + Venus)
- **Formation**: Jupiter + Venus conjunction
- **Effects**: Wealth, luxuries, spiritual wisdom, artistic talent

## Negative Yogas

### Daridra Yoga (Poverty Combinations)
- **Formations**:
  - Lords of 2nd, 6th, 8th, 11th conjoined in 6th, 8th, 12th
  - Lagna lord in 8th house with malefics
  - 2nd lord in 6th or 8th, aspected by malefics
- **Effects**: Financial struggles, debt, poverty

### Mangal Dosha (Kuja Dosha - Mars Affliction)
- **Formation**: Mars in 1st, 4th, 7th, 8th, 12th houses
- **Cancellation Factors**:
  - Mars in own sign/exaltation
  - Mars aspected by Jupiter
  - Mars in conjunction with Jupiter
  - Lagna or 7th lord in Kendra
  - Mars in 8th only (partial)
  - Mars in 12th only (partial)
- **Effects**: Marriage delays, marital discord, spouse health issues

### Kaal Sarpa Yoga (Serpent Yoga)
- **Formation**: All planets between Rahu and Ketu (in their nodes)
- **Variations**:
  - **Anant Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 1st, Ketu in 7th
  - **Kulik Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 2nd, Ketu in 8th
  - **Vasuki Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 3rd, Ketu in 9th
  - **Shankhapal Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 4th, Ketu in 10th
  - **Padma Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 5th, Ketu in 11th
  - **Mahapadma Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 6th, Ketu in 12th
  - **Takshak Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 7th, Ketu in 1st
  - **Karkotak Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 8th, Ketu in 2nd
  - **Shankhachood Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 9th, Ketu in 3rd
  - **Ghatak Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 10th, Ketu in 4th
  - **Vishdhar Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 11th, Ketu in 5th
  - **Sheshnag Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu in 12th, Ketu in 6th
- **Effects**: Karmic challenges, ancestral patterns, life struggles, but also spiritual potential
- **Cancellation**: If one planet is outside Rahu-Ketu axis

### Shakata Yoga
- **Formation**: Jupiter in 6th, 8th, or 12th from Moon
- **Effects**: Fluctuating fortunes, struggles, but periodic success

### Papa Kartari Yoga
- **Formation**: Malefics in adjacent houses (2nd and 12th) to a house or planet
- **Effects**: Challenges related to the afflicted house/planet

### Papadrishti Yoga
- **Formation**: Planet or house aspected by malefics (Saturn, Mars, Rahu, Ketu, Sun)
- **Effects**: Challenges, obstacles related to the aspected house/planet

## Strength Assessment Guidelines

### Strong Yoga Indicators
1. Yoga-forming planets in own sign/exaltation
2. Yoga confirmed in multiple Varga charts
3. Yoga activated by current Dasha
4. Yoga reinforced by transits
5. Yoga in Kendra/Trikona houses
6. Yoga planets in good dignity (not combust, not debilitated)
7. Aspected by benefics (Jupiter, Venus, Mercury, waxing Moon)

### Weak Yoga Indicators
1. Yoga planets in enemy signs/debilitation
2. Yoga in 6th, 8th, 12th houses
3. Yoga conjoined/aspected by malefics
4. Yoga not confirmed in D9 or D10
5. Yoga not activated by Dasha
6. Yoga planets combust or weak
7. Yoga in dusthana houses from Lagna

### Yoga Activation Timing
- **Mahadasha lord**: If Dasha lord is yoga-forming planet → major activation
- **Bhukti lord**: Sub-period of yoga planet → specific manifestation
- **Transits**: Jupiter/Saturn transit over yoga planets → activation
- **Pratyantar**: Fine-tuning of timing

## Remedial Measures (Upaya)

For Afflicted Yogas:
- **Neechabhanga**: Strengthen debilitated planet with gemstones, mantras, charity
- **Kaal Sarpa**: Rahu-Ketu remedies, snake worship, charity on Rahu/Ketu day
- **Mangal Dosha**: Mars remedies, wedding rituals, partner compatibility check
- **Daridra Yoga**: Lakshmi worship, charity on Fridays, strengthen 2nd/11th lords
