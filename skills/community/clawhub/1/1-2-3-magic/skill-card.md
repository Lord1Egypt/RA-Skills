## Description: <br>
Provides 1-2-3 Magic parenting discipline guidance for children ages 2-12, covering counting for stop behavior, routines for start behavior, testing responses, and parent-child relationship support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jaysonmeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/jaysonmeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External parents, guardians, and caregivers use this skill to get routine, book-style parenting guidance for discipline, daily routines, sibling conflict, testing behavior, and relationship repair. It is intended for ordinary parenting support, not emergencies or professional medical, legal, abuse, custody, or self-harm intervention. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may ask for help with self-harm threats, abuse, medical issues, legal custody matters, or emergencies that routine parenting guidance cannot address safely. <br>
Mitigation: Direct users to appropriate professional, legal, medical, child-safety, crisis, or emergency support instead of relying on this skill for those cases. <br>
Risk: Book-style discipline suggestions may be over-applied to situations that need emotional support or individualized professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep guidance limited to routine parenting support, distinguish stop behavior from relationship or safety concerns, and encourage professional help when the situation falls outside ordinary discipline. <br>
