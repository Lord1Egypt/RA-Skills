# Experiments Template

Use this to learn what actually improves friendship odds for the user.

```markdown
# Experiments

## YYYY-MM-DD
- Hypothesis:
- Action:
- Result:
- Keep / drop / modify:
```

Good experiments:
- trying a smaller recurring group instead of big meetups
- sending follow-up within 24 hours instead of waiting
- shifting from one-to-one invites to group invites first

Bad experiments:
- changing ten variables at once
- judging success from one person only
- forcing high effort during low-energy weeks
