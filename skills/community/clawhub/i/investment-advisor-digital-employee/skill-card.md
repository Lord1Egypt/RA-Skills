## Description: <br>
Covers A-share hot-topic discovery, comparable-company analysis, fund diagnosis and screening, stock fund-flow analysis, multi-factor stock screening, market technical analysis, shareholder and capital-structure analysis, and deep technical analysis for investment-advisor market research. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External investment advisors and research analysts use this skill to structure market, equity, and fund research reports from public financial data. Outputs are reference material only and require qualified human review before any investment decision. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Declared no-tool and no-data posture conflicts with artifact behavior describing external financial lookups and local report file generation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as capable of using external data services and creating files; review permissions, data flows, and generated outputs before operational use. <br>
Risk: Finance analysis may be mistaken for personalized investment, legal, or insurance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs framed as reference research, include risk disclosures, and require qualified human review before acting on any recommendation-like analysis. <br>
Risk: Sensitive financial or personal information could be exposed if users provide private holdings, credentials, identifiers, or confidential business data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing credentials, account data, portfolio holdings, personal identifiers, or confidential business information unless corrected disclosures and approved controls are in place. <br>
