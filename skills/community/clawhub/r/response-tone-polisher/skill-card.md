## Description: <br>
Polishes response letters by transforming defensive or harsh language into professional, courteous academic prose while preserving the author's scientific position. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ewankeynes](https://clawhub.ai/user/ewankeynes) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, academic authors, and writing assistants use this skill to rewrite draft peer-review responses into diplomatic, professional language. It helps soften refusals, disagreements, and defensive phrasing while keeping the author's intended position intact. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The tool reads user-supplied input files and can write an output file to a user-selected path. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide files intended for polishing and choose output paths carefully to avoid overwriting important content. <br>
Risk: Polished responses may imply manuscript changes, concessions, or softened claims that the author did not intend. <br>
Mitigation: Review the final response before submission to confirm it preserves the scientific position and accurately describes any manuscript changes. <br>
