## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Avatar cloning can misuse a person's likeness or create misleading media. <br>
Mitigation: Use only media you own or are authorized to use, avoid impersonation, and clearly label AI-generated avatar content. <br>
Risk: The workflow may involve sensitive media uploads and vendor retention or privacy exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Review each vendor's privacy and retention policies before uploading photos, videos, or voice assets. <br>
Risk: API keys and paid avatar services can expose credentials or unexpected costs. <br>
Mitigation: Protect API keys, avoid committing secrets, and confirm pricing and usage limits before generating videos. <br>
