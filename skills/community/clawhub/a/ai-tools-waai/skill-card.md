## Description: <br>
AI Tools Box helps agents search and compare a directory of 100+ AI services for writing, image, video, coding, office, search, chat, audio, design, agent, translation, development, learning, and detection tasks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to find AI services by keyword or category, compare available options, and return tool names, descriptions, and URLs for the user to evaluate. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The main risk comes from third-party AI service links, which may have their own privacy, account, payment, and data-retention terms. <br>
Mitigation: Review the destination provider and its terms before use, and avoid entering sensitive data unless the provider is trusted for that use case. <br>
Risk: Directory entries may become outdated or may not match a user's compliance requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the selected provider's current capabilities, pricing, and policy fit before relying on it for production work. <br>
