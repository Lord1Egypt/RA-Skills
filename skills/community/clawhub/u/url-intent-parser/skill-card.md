## Description: <br>
Parse product and landing URLs into an executable ads brief for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Amazon Ads, Shopify Ads, and DSP/programmatic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operators, growth teams, and agents use this skill to turn a product, service, or landing page URL into an ads launch brief with offer extraction, funnel assumptions, channel recommendations, and a structured hand-off payload. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ad briefs may rely on stale, unreachable, or misread landing page facts. <br>
Mitigation: Verify extracted page facts before acting, keep observed facts separate from assumptions, and use the validation checklist when confidence is low. <br>
Risk: Channel and budget recommendations can affect paid campaign spend, tracking, and policy exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Keep campaign changes and budgets under explicit human control, confirm tracking and platform constraints, and include rollback or stop-loss conditions before launch. <br>
