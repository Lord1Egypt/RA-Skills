## Description: <br>
Parallel fetches AI news from multiple sources, summarizes the top items, and delivers a structured daily brief via Telegram. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[warrior156006050](https://clawhub.ai/user/warrior156006050) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users who follow AI industry news use this skill to gather recent items from 36Kr and GeekPark, turn them into concise Markdown briefings, and deliver the brief through Telegram. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generated AI-news brief may be sent to a fixed Telegram recipient without recipient choice or send confirmation. <br>
Mitigation: Review the report locally, confirm the Telegram account or bot and intended recipient, and require explicit approval before sending. <br>
