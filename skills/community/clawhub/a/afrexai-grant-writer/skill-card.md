## Description: <br>
Grant Writer helps an agent draft tailored grant proposals and funding applications for government, foundation, and corporate programs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and organizations use this skill to prepare grant proposal sections, budget justifications, evaluation plans, sustainability plans, support-letter drafts, and rubric-based self-reviews for specific funding opportunities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Grant drafts may require sensitive organizational information, including project plans, budgets, partner details, staffing information, proprietary data, or unpublished research. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information the organization is authorized to provide to the agent or model provider, and remove confidential details unless they are required for the drafting task. <br>
Risk: Generated citations, eligibility claims, funder priorities, budgets, and submission details may be inaccurate or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all citations, eligibility requirements, funder-specific instructions, budget figures, and rubric alignment against the official grant materials before submission. <br>
Risk: Optional AfrexAI links may be treated as required setup or authoritative grant evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Treat linked AfrexAI resources as optional external resources, and rely on the funder's official call text and organizational source documents for submission-critical facts. <br>
