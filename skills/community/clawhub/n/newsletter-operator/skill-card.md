## Description: <br>
Use when the user asks to run, plan, build, write, review, publish, promote, monetize, audit, or improve a newsletter. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Newsletter operators, editors, and marketing teams use this skill to research issues, build drafts, run preflight reviews, create promotion copy, plan growth and monetization work, and prepare handoffs. It is intended for human-reviewed newsletter operations with explicit approval before publishing, subscriber, sponsor, payment, spending, or destructive workspace actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Unreviewed publishing, subscriber, sponsor, payment, spending, or destructive workspace actions could affect readers, revenue, or business relationships. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before sending, scheduling, publishing, changing subscribers, contacting sponsors, collecting payment, committing spend, or making destructive edits. <br>
Risk: Newsletter drafts or recommendations can become misleading if facts, dates, links, quotes, event details, audience metrics, revenue, or sponsor claims are incomplete or unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Separate verified facts from assumptions, preserve source notes, verify core claims and links, and keep unverified items out of polished copy. <br>
Risk: Growth and monetization advice can harm reader trust if sponsor obligations, attribution, or approval constraints are missed. <br>
Mitigation: Check sponsor placements, approved copy, links, tracking, assets, exclusivity, and approvals; use small tests with clear tracking and stop or continue criteria. <br>
