## 6. Important Notes

1. **No colloquial language**: Replace "搞/弄/干" with "开展/推进/落实"
2. **No internet slang**: Avoid informal terms like "给力/点赞/洪荒之力"
3. **Proper abbreviations**: Use full names on first mention, with abbreviation in parentheses
4. **Avoid emotional language**: Maintain objective, neutral, rigorous tone
5. **Accurate data**: Cite sources and time points for all data
6. **Appropriate length**: Requests ≤1500 chars, Reports ≤3000 chars (general documents)
7. **Clear hierarchy**: Headings follow "一、（一）1. (1)" order, no skipping levels
