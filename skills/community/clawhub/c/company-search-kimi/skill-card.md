## Description: <br>
Generates structured public-source company research reports using multi-source search and page fetching for business registration, ownership, financing, legal, operational, intellectual property, procurement, hiring, news, and competitive analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fan31415](https://clawhub.ai/user/fan31415) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, founders, investors, legal reviewers, and business teams use this skill to research companies from public web sources and produce due-diligence style reports with cited evidence and confidence levels. It is suited for company background checks, risk reviews, ownership mapping, financing history, and competitive landscape analysis. <br>
