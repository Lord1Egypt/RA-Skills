## Description: <br>
Extracts action items, decisions, and key insights from meeting transcripts or recordings into structured minutes with owners and deadlines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wulaosiji](https://clawhub.ai/user/wulaosiji) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, operators, and team leads use this skill to turn meeting transcripts or notes into shareable minutes, decision lists, action-item tables, and quick-share follow-ups. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting transcripts can contain confidential or sensitive information, and the helper may write generated minutes to a local Markdown file. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential transcripts unless local file output is acceptable, review generated minutes before sharing, and specify an output path when workspace cleanliness or privacy matters. <br>
