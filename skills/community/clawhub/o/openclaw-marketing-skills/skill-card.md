## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Paid ads, retargeting, and ad-account workflows can create spend, platform-policy, targeting, or permission exposure if executed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before changing campaigns, audiences, budgets, account permissions, or spend limits; verify platform rules and targeting restrictions before launch. <br>
Risk: Analytics, tracking, customer-list uploads, and email automation may involve personal data, consent requirements, or regulated customer information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm privacy requirements, consent state, suppression lists, data minimization, and account permissions before implementation or upload; avoid secrets and regulated customer data in reusable context files unless explicitly approved. <br>
Risk: Voice-cloning and generative ad-creative workflows can create consent, likeness, disclosure, or brand-safety issues. <br>
Mitigation: Use voice or likeness generation only with documented rights and approval, review outputs before publication, and prefer clearly authorized or compensated voice sources. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing recommendations can be inaccurate, misleading, noncompliant, or inconsistent with brand and legal requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review all copy, claims, benchmarks, compliance-sensitive advice, and implementation snippets before publishing, deploying, or sending to customers. <br>
