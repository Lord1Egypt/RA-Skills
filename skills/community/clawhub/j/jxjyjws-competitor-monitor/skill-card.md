## Description: <br>
Helps agents research one or more competitors through recent-activity monitoring and four-dimension competitive analysis, then produce a structured single-file HTML report. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product teams, founders, and analysts use this skill to monitor public competitor activity or create structured competitive analysis across product, pricing, users, and social media signals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may include sensitive strategy, customer, or internal business details if the user provides them for analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential information unless it is intended for the report, and review the generated HTML before sharing. <br>
Risk: The generated report is shareable and may be distributed beyond the original audience. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as a public-facing artifact until reviewed, and remove any details that should not be disclosed. <br>
Risk: Competitor findings depend on public web sources and may be incomplete or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Check cited source links for key claims before relying on the report for business decisions. <br>
