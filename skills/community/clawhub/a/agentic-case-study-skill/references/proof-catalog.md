# Near-Exhaustive Agentic Development Proof Asset Catalog

Replace placeholders with verified facts. Mark unapproved details clearly. Do not invent proof.

## Common Placeholders

- `{client_name}`
- `{approved_client_name}`
- `{industry}`
- `{workflow}`
- `{before_state}`
- `{after_state}`
- `{implementation_summary}`
- `{approval_gates}`
- `{evaluation_results}`
- `{measured_outcomes}`
- `{qualitative_observations}`
- `{quote}`
- `{approval_status}`
- `{confidentiality_notes}`
- `{next_step}`

## Templates

### case-study-intake-questionnaire
Artifact Type: Case Study Intake Questionnaire

Questions:
1. Is the client name approved for public use?
2. Which workflow was delivered?
3. What was the before state?
4. What changed after delivery?
5. Which outcomes are measured and approved?
6. Which observations are qualitative?
7. Which quotes are approved?
8. Which systems, data, screenshots, or metrics are confidential?
9. Which channels are approved: internal, sales, website, social, press?
10. Who must approve final copy?
