挑战杯大挑参赛指导助手。当用户需要参加"挑战杯"课外学术科技作品竞赛、准备参赛项目、获取作品类别建议或制作参赛PPT时使用此技能。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students and competition teams use this skill to choose a Challenge Cup entry category, interpret judging criteria, prepare materials, and request a structured presentation workflow for the 2025 seventeenth competition. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competition guidance may be outdated because the skill is framed around the 2025 seventeenth Challenge Cup. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current rules, eligibility, team limits, deadlines, and judging criteria against official competition materials before relying on advice. <br>
Risk: Presentation workflows can include unpublished research, contact details, or team information. <br>
Mitigation: Review inputs and generated materials before sharing and avoid providing sensitive or unpublished information unless it is intended for inclusion. <br>
Risk: PPT creation depends on a separate pptx skill when the user requests presentation output. <br>
Mitigation: Review the separate pptx skill before using that workflow and inspect generated slides before submission or distribution. <br>
