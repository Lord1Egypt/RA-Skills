## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, customer success, and sales enablement teams use this skill to draft B2B customer case studies, success stories, portfolio pieces, and testimonial-driven marketing content with quantified results and customer approval checkpoints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External CLI installation and app execution can introduce supply-chain and account-usage risk. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the inference.sh installer or use the manual checksum path before installing, and use an appropriate inference.sh account. <br>
Risk: Case study drafts and research prompts may include confidential customer names, quotes, metrics, or unpublished results. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending confidential customer information to external search or execution apps unless approval has been obtained. <br>
Risk: Incorrect or unapproved customer claims can create legal, relationship, or marketing risk. <br>
Mitigation: Review all metrics, quotes, and final case study copy with the customer and relevant internal reviewers before publication. <br>
