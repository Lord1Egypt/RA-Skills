## Description: <br>
Send feedback to the Ravi team - bugs, feature requests, praise, frustrations, suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[raunaksingwi](https://clawhub.ai/user/raunaksingwi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents use this skill to send bug reports, feature requests, praise, frustrations, and workflow suggestions to the Ravi team. It is intended for Ravi product feedback only, not general email sending. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill encourages sending feedback from the user's authenticated Ravi identity without an explicit approval step. <br>
Mitigation: Review the recipient, subject, and body before sending any message, and only include details the user intends to share with Ravi. <br>
Risk: Feedback content may include private workflow details or sensitive context. <br>
Mitigation: Remove confidential, personal, or unnecessary operational details before composing feedback. <br>
