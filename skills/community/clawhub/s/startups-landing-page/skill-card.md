## Description: <br>
Reviews and improves homepage copy, positioning, and messaging for startups. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup teams use this skill to review landing-page messaging, clarify positioning, generate homepage structure, draft headline options, improve copy, and prioritize conversion-focused next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive startup information while requesting positioning or landing-page feedback. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential customer lists, unreleased strategy, private financial metrics, or sensitive testimonial details unless appropriate for the chat context. <br>
