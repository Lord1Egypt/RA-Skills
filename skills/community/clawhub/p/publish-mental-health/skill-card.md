## Description: <br>
Publish Mental Health is an informational advisor for mental-health market research, covering online counseling, digital therapeutics, AI-assisted counseling, mindfulness, enterprise EAP, regulatory trends, and investment analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Mental-health professionals, digital health founders, analysts, and investors use this skill to navigate mental-health industry trends, service models, digital therapeutics, AI-assisted counseling, clinical pathways, regulatory policy, and market comparisons. It is an informational research aid and should not be used as medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or standalone financial diligence. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake mental-health summaries for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational research only and consult qualified medical or mental-health professionals for clinical decisions or crisis situations. <br>
Risk: Users may rely on market or company comparisons as standalone investment diligence. <br>
Mitigation: Verify financial, valuation, and market claims against current authoritative sources before making business or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Regulatory, clinical, and market data can become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Check current guidance from regulators, clinical bodies, and market data providers before relying on time-sensitive claims. <br>
