## Description: <br>
Helps agents proofread Chinese video scripts for continuity, costume, prop, logic, and language issues. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flankechen](https://clawhub.ai/user/flankechen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and video production teams use this skill to review Chinese scripts for continuity errors, costume and prop inconsistencies, logic breaks, and wording issues while preserving the author's intent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local review files may contain confidential or unpublished script details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill in a dedicated folder, check for existing character_check.txt, scene_check.txt, or QUESTION.md before running it, and delete generated review notes when they are no longer needed. <br>
