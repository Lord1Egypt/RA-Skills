## Description: <br>
OpenClaw AI Marketing helps agents run, audit, or improve a ClawLite/OpenClaw AI marketing operating system across research, assets, content packaging, publishing, lifecycle activation, QA, and truth-state management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operators, founders, and agent-team builders use this skill to coordinate daily AI marketing work from audience research through asset updates, social and blog publishing, lifecycle activation, QA, receipts, and final truth-state closure. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can steer agents toward real public posts, blog publishing, lifecycle sends, cohort actions, and writebacks. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in draft or audit mode by default and require explicit approval for every public publish, email send, cohort action, and writeback. <br>
Risk: Marketing operations may involve credentials, customer identifiers, private campaign strategy, or reusable receipts. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege accounts and keep secrets, customer data, private strategy, and reusable receipts out of shared skill files. <br>
Risk: Receipt and truth-state language can create misleading completion claims if work is not verified. <br>
Mitigation: Require live URLs, visibility proof, QA receipts, and Mission Control updates before marking work delivered or passed. <br>
