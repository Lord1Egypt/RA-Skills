## Description: <br>
深度挖掘目标企业近12个月内的实质战略合作，解析合作模式及背后商业意图，输出全面战略生态洞察报告。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business analysts, investors, due diligence teams, and partnership strategists use this skill to research a company's recent strategic cooperation activity, classify partnership depth, and infer commercial intent from public information. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive target names, nonpublic diligence plans, or investigative goals could be exposed through external public search queries. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential deal targets or sensitive research objectives unless the user is comfortable with those terms being used in external searches. <br>
Risk: Public partnership information may be incomplete, promotional, outdated, or limited to high-level framework agreements. <br>
Mitigation: Label limited evidence clearly, prioritize official announcements and reputable financial reporting, and avoid presenting inferred cooperation depth as fact. <br>
Risk: Rumors or unconfirmed cooperation claims could mislead investment, due diligence, or strategy decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Exclude rumors by default; when explicitly included, mark them as unconfirmed and separate them from official disclosures. <br>
