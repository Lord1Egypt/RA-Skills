## Description: <br>
Generates concise English prompts for Seedance 2.0 video creation from Chinese user requirements, including mode selection, asset mapping, timelines, camera language, sound layers, and negative constraints. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wild-river2016](https://clawhub.ai/user/wild-river2016) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to turn Chinese video concepts, reference assets, product-showcase needs, or storyboard ideas into Seedance 2.0-ready English prompts. The skill helps structure prompts with timing, visual direction, audio guidance, asset roles, platform limits, and review checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes guidance for avoiding IP or copyright review blocks, which could be misused to imitate protected characters, brands, or franchise styles. <br>
Mitigation: Use the IP guidance only to create original characters and concepts; do not use it to copy protected works or bypass platform moderation. <br>
Risk: Generated video prompts may still violate copyright, brand, or platform policy boundaries if user input requests protected IP or recognizable marks. <br>
Mitigation: Review prompts before use, remove protected names or distinctive marks, and keep character, brand, and style descriptions original. <br>
