## Description: <br>
Use when turning requirements, screenshots, or other QA inputs into structured test cases and review questions with an AI-assisted workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xuxuclassmate](https://clawhub.ai/user/xuxuclassmate) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
QA engineers, product teams, and developers use this skill to turn requirements, screenshots, specifications, or plain text briefs into structured test scope summaries, module-level test points, detailed cases, and open review questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Requirements, screenshots, PRDs, or QA notes provided to the skill may contain sensitive product information. <br>
Mitigation: Treat supplied inputs as confidential and avoid exposing them outside the intended agent session or review workflow. <br>
Risk: Generated test cases may depend on missing or ambiguous product details. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions clearly and include open questions for product or engineering review before using the cases as execution-ready test work. <br>
