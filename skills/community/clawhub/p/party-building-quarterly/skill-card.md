## Description: <br>
Generates a fixed-format quarterly party-building and government-affairs analysis report from public sources covering recent Xi Jinping speeches, articles, and central meeting themes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[whitetin](https://clawhub.ai/user/whitetin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users use this skill to prepare concise quarterly Markdown reports that summarize public-source party-building and government-affairs materials with source links and trend analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on a separate summarize CLI tool that is not included in this artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Verify and trust the installed summarize tool separately before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Generated citations and political-content claims may be inaccurate or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Independently check source links and quoted claims before relying on or publishing the report. <br>
