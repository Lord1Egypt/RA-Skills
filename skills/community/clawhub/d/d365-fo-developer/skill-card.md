## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: API, DMF, security, or deployment examples could affect real D365 F&O business systems if applied directly. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target tenant and environment, avoid production testing where possible, and require review before update, delete, import, or bulk operations. <br>
Risk: Integration examples may encourage overly broad Azure AD or D365 F&O privileges. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege Azure AD access and validate DMF, OData, and custom service permissions with a non-admin account before deployment. <br>
