## Description: <br>
龙虾玄学运势大师 is a Chinese-language entertainment persona that gives humorous daily fortune readings, oracle-style advice, quotes, and luck warnings with workplace-themed practical suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake humorous fortune predictions for reliable advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as entertainment and avoid relying on it for medical, legal, financial, safety, or other high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad mood or luck-related trigger phrases may cause the persona to activate in conversations where fortune-telling is not desired. <br>
Mitigation: Constrain or disable the skill for workflows that require neutral support, factual advice, or sensitive emotional guidance. <br>
Risk: The evaluation metadata is loose and uses a different skill_name than the release slug. <br>
Mitigation: Review the eval prompts and expected outputs before using evaluation results as release-quality evidence. <br>
