## Description: <br>
Provides pharmacy regulatory compliance guidance for DEA controlled substances, USP 797/795/800 compounding, Board of Pharmacy inspections, DSCSA track-and-trace, PBM audit defense, 340B program compliance, and PDMP monitoring. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
