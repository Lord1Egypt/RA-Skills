## Description: <br>
A pet short-video creation reference skill that guides pet profile building, story design, storyboard scripting, and AI video prompt generation for tools such as Seedance 2.0, Kling, and Veo. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and developers use this skill as a structured reference for pet short-video workflows: pet characterization, story and shot planning, Seedance-style prompts, and sound cues. Full production workflows depend on Universal Task OS; without it, the skill remains a read-only reference. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill declares an automatic Universal Task OS installation path for full execution mode. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that Universal Task OS is trusted and intended before enabling full execution; otherwise use the skill only as a read-only reference. <br>
Risk: Pet video scripts and prompts can drift from the intended pet appearance or platform constraints if inputs are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated storyboards and prompts for role consistency, visible actions, platform duration, aspect ratio, and tool-specific prompt limits before production use. <br>
