## Description: <br>
Generates a four-week JSON marketing content calendar for CraveGo.ph new-user signup promotions, including offer angles, platform cadence, CTAs, and hook ideas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bobneobux](https://clawhub.ai/user/bobneobux) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams and operators use this skill to plan CraveGo.ph signup campaigns for the Philippines. It focuses on safe, generic new-user promotions with friendly Filipino-market social content for FB, IG, and TikTok. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Philippines <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated campaign ideas may include inaccurate offers, unavailable promotions, or claims that do not match platform rules or local advertising and consumer-protection requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review brand accuracy, offer availability, platform rules, and local compliance before publishing; keep offer language soft and avoid guarantees or legal and financial claims. <br>
