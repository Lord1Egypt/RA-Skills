## Description: <br>
Generates a Creem audit-ready compliance pack with legal policies, a preflight checklist, and site-surface punchlists for Merchant of Record onboarding or review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Hamer-2014](https://clawhub.ai/user/Hamer-2014) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, and operators preparing a SaaS or digital product for Creem review use this skill to draft a preflight checklist, legal page pack, site-surface punchlist, and open TODOs for missing product or legal inputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may publish unfinished legal templates or policy text that does not match their actual product, checkout flow, jurisdiction, or refund model. <br>
Mitigation: Fill every placeholder, choose one refund model, remove template notes, align the pages with the live product and checkout flow, and get qualified legal review before publishing. <br>
Risk: Inconsistent support, refund, auto-renewal, pricing, or Merchant of Record language across public pages can create Creem review and customer-compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Run the consistency checks across policy pages, pricing, checkout, account, billing, and footer surfaces before using the generated pack publicly. <br>
