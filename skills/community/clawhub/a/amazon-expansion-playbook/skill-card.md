## Description: <br>
Provides a practical Amazon global expansion playbook for sellers moving from the United States into markets such as North America, Europe, Japan, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia, with market entry, localization, and compliance guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Amazon sellers, ecommerce operators, and marketplace consultants use this skill to evaluate whether and where to expand internationally, compare target markets, and build market-specific action plans for localization, logistics, tax, and product compliance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market-size, VAT, certification, tax, and pricing guidance may become outdated or vary by product category and jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important regulatory, tax, certification, and financial decisions against current authoritative sources or qualified advisors before acting. <br>
Risk: The playbook provides general business guidance and may not account for a seller's product-specific compliance obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the checklists as planning prompts and perform product-level compliance review for each target marketplace. <br>
