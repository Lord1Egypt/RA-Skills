## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may follow incorrect URLs, token addresses, network selections, fee assumptions, or transaction details when moving funds. <br>
Mitigation: Verify official URLs, token addresses, network selection, fees, and transaction details before initiating any transfer or bridge action. <br>
Risk: Third-party bridges and subnet-specific bridges can introduce additional security and operational risk. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official bridges when available and review the bridge, subnet, and validator security assumptions before moving assets. <br>
