Risk: Conversion output may be inappropriate for critical engineering, medical, financial, or legal decisions if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify conversion accuracy independently before relying on results in critical contexts. <br>
Risk: Unsupported units or cross-category conversions can produce conversion errors. <br>
Mitigation: Use the categories and list commands to confirm supported units before converting. <br>
