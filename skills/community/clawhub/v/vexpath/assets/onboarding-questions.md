# VexPath Onboarding Intake

## Instructions

Ask these questions at the start of a new client setup. All 10 are required. Record answers in workspace `TOOLS.md` and use them to configure the instance (see `references/onboarding.md`).

---

## Questions

**1. What is your business name?**

> Determines display names in emails, reports, and automations.

---

**2. What type of business do you run?**

> Agency / Service Business / E-commerce / Local Business / SaaS / Other (describe)

> This determines default workflow templates, triage priorities, and automation recommendations.

---

**3. What is your primary business email address?**

> The inbox VEX will monitor and triage. Include the full email address (e.g., hello@yourbusiness.com).

---

**4. What email service do you use?**

> Gmail / Outlook / Hostinger / Other (specify)

> If using a custom domain, also tell me who hosts your email (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Hostinger, etc.)

---

**5. Do you use a calendar system for appointments or scheduling?**

> Yes / No — If yes: Google Calendar / Outlook Calendar / Calendly / Acuity / Other (specify)

> This enables calendar event creation from scheduling requests and follow-ups.

---

**6. Do you use a CRM or lead tracking tool?**

> Yes / No — If yes, which one? (Airtable / Notion / HubSpot / Google Sheets / other)

> If no CRM yet, VEX can track leads in a Google Sheet or Notion database.

---

**7. What is your biggest operational pain point right now?**

> Choose all that apply:
> - Missing or slow responses to leads
> - Manual scheduling and booking
> - Disorganized inbox / missed emails
> - No follow-up system
> - Manual invoicing / payment tracking
> - No client onboarding process
> - Content / social media not consistent
> - Too much time on admin tasks
> - Disconnected tools that don't talk to each other
> - Other: ___

> This drives the bottleneck audit and first workflow priority.

---

**8. What communication channels do you use for your business?**

> Select all: Email / Phone / SMS / WhatsApp / Slack / Discord / Instagram DMs / Facebook / Other

> VEX routes and responds based on the channels you actually use.

---

**9. Are you comfortable with VEX sending emails on your behalf once you approve a draft?**

> Yes — after I approve the draft, send it automatically
> No — I want to review and send manually every time
> Sometimes — auto-send for certain categories (specify which)

> This sets the approval gate policy for outbound messages.

---

**10. What would make this feel like a success after 30 days?**

> Open-ended. Examples: inbox zero most days, no missed follow-ups, 50% less admin time, first automated workflow running.

> This becomes the benchmark for evaluating VexPath performance.

---

## After Collecting Answers

1. Record all answers in workspace `TOOLS.md`
2. Map business type to profile in `references/onboarding.md`
3. Identify top 1–2 pain points → match to workflow templates
4. Run setup scripts
5. Deliver onboarding summary to client
