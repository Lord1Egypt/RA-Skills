## Description: <br>
Rewrite and optimize content for 8+ platforms with AI-powered tone adjustment, batch conversion, quality scoring, and translation. Powered by evolink.ai <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[evolinkai](https://clawhub.ai/user/evolinkai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, content creators, and developers use this skill to rewrite, adapt, score, and translate text files for social, blog, email, and community platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Selected draft content is sent to api.evolink.ai for cloud processing. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with content approved for EvoLink processing; avoid secrets, regulated data, private business documents, and customer information unless EvoLink terms fit the use case. <br>
Risk: Routine agent use could send text to a third-party service without explicit user intent. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before using AI commands for ordinary editing requests. <br>
