## Description: <br>
Provides guidance on Türkiye monthly and periodic tax declaration dates, SGK notices, KDV, withholding, stamp tax, provisional tax, corporate tax, accrual slips, late-payment context, and monthly business-owner tasks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business owners, accountants, and agents supporting Turkish businesses use this skill to answer tax and SGK calendar questions, prepare monthly document handoff checklists, and explain tax payment artifacts such as tahakkuk fisi. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Türkiye <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tax and SGK deadlines or obligations may change after the bundled reference material was written. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current dates and obligations with official Turkish sources or a qualified accountant before acting. <br>
Risk: Generic tax questions could be answered with Türkiye-specific guidance when the user needs another jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking about Turkish tax or SGK obligations before applying this skill. <br>
Risk: The skill provides reference guidance, not professional tax advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a checklist or explanation aid and defer filing, payment, and compliance decisions to the user, official sources, or a qualified accountant. <br>
