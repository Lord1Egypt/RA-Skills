## Description: <br>
Guides licensed PE/SE structural engineers through ASCE/SEI 41-23 Tier 1 seismic evaluation intake, building-type classification, checklist execution, deficiency aggregation, and memo drafting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Licensed structural engineers and reviewing engineers use this skill to draft peer-reviewable ASCE/SEI 41-23 Tier 1 seismic evaluation memos, including scope intake, checklist results, deficiency lists, and Tier 2 or Tier 3 recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated memo content may be mistaken for a final engineering determination. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as draft documentation under licensed PE/SE supervision, with responsible review, signature, and seal before any final use. <br>
Risk: The workflow may involve building addresses, owner or client names, license numbers, drawings, site observations, or sensitive facility details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information you are authorized to use, minimize sensitive details, and avoid unnecessary contact PII, security-sensitive floor plans, or confidential lease terms. <br>
Risk: A Tier 1 screening may be inappropriate for some building types, heights, seismicity levels, performance objectives, or geologic hazards. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm Tier 1 eligibility before checklist execution and stop with a Tier 2 or Tier 3 recommendation when the building falls outside Tier 1 scope. <br>
