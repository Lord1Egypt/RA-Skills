## Description: <br>
When blocked, report exact blocker + attempts made + minimum user input needed. Enable fast unblock without back-and-forth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Dalomeve](https://clawhub.ai/user/Dalomeve) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to make blocked work actionable by requiring an exact blocker, attempts made, minimum unblock input, and a fallback option. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Blocker reports may expose sensitive project names, paths, or account details through exact error messages. <br>
Mitigation: Review reports before sharing them externally and redact credentials, tokens, keys, and sensitive paths. <br>
