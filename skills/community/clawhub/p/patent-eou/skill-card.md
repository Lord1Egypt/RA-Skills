## Description: <br>
Evidence of Use patent analysis skill for analyzing whether products may infringe patents, generating EOU claim charts and reports, mapping patent claims to product features, and discovering potential infringement leads. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chaochaoSZ](https://clawhub.ai/user/chaochaoSZ) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, patent analysts, and IP teams use this skill to screen patents against public product evidence, identify potential infringement leads, and generate preliminary EOU claim charts and reports. The reports are for reference only and do not replace review by a qualified patent attorney. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Patent and product research may involve external web or database queries that could reveal confidential litigation strategy or unpublished product details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with information appropriate for public research workflows, and avoid confidential strategy or unpublished product details unless external queries are acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated EOU reports may contain errors, omissions, hallucinations, or legal conclusions that are not suitable for direct reliance. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated reports before relying on or sharing them, and consult a qualified patent attorney for legal decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill includes Patsnap attribution and disclaimer requirements that may matter for downstream distribution or branding. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the Patsnap affiliation and preserve the required report disclaimers when that branding or attribution matters. <br>
