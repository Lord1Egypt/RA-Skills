## Use Case: <br>
Dental practice owners, office managers, DSO operators, consultants, and startup teams use this skill to compare practice metrics against benchmarks, improve schedules and collections, manage no-shows, and plan OSHA, HIPAA, billing, and marketing workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste patient PHI, staff records, insurance credentials, or proprietary financial exports while asking for operational help. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved agent environments for sensitive dental practice data, and redact or omit PHI, staff records, credentials, and proprietary exports when approval is not established. <br>
Risk: Compliance, payer, fee, and professional guidance can vary by jurisdiction and change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Verify OSHA, HIPAA, state board, payer, CDT, fee, and professional requirements against current authoritative sources before taking action. <br>
