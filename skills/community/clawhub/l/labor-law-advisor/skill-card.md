## Description: <br>
劳动法律顾问 helps agents answer Chinese labor-law questions about disputes, injury recognition, contracts, overtime pay, social insurance, non-compete issues, severance, arbitration, and related document templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xunx33](https://clawhub.ai/user/xunx33) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, advocates, and agents use this skill to analyze Chinese labor disputes, identify relevant statutes, calculate likely compensation, outline arbitration steps, and draft common labor-dispute documents. It is for legal information and workflow support, not formal legal representation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce Chinese labor-law information that may be incomplete for a user's facts, city, deadlines, or current local rules. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for missing facts, verify current local standards and deadlines, and consult a qualified lawyer for high-value or time-sensitive disputes. <br>
Risk: User facts may include personal identifiers or sensitive employment details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid collecting or sharing unnecessary personal identifiers and keep only the facts needed to evaluate the labor issue. <br>
