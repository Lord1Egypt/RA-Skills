## Description: <br>
Provides guidance for scheduling door-to-door recycling of used appliances, cardboard, and plastic items, including recycling points redemption. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to ask for concise recycling workflow guidance, including pickup scheduling steps, item categories, status considerations, and points redemption cautions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Real pickup booking or points redemption may require address, phone, or authorization details. <br>
Mitigation: Only share personal or authorization details with a recycling provider the user intentionally trusts. <br>
Risk: Some recycling features may depend on third-party services and can vary in availability or stability. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm booking status, supported items, and points redemption terms with the provider before relying on the guidance. <br>
