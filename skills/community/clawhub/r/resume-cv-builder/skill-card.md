## Description: <br>
Create professional resumes and CVs, including ATS-friendly formats, optimized bullet points, job-specific tailoring, and exports to Markdown, HTML, LaTeX, and PDF. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers, career coaches, and professional profile reviewers use this skill to create, improve, tailor, and export resumes or CVs for specific roles and job descriptions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume content can contain sensitive personal information, and generated HTML may reference remote CSS. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid putting private resume data into HTML that loads remote CSS; prefer the local style.css export pattern. <br>
Risk: Server-resolved source provenance is unavailable, and the artifact homepage appears to be a placeholder. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the publisher and source provenance before relying on origin or authorship claims. <br>
