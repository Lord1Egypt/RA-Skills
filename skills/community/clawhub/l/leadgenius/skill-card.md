## Description: <br>
Enrich and score sales leads using AI-powered analysis, data validation, and CRM integration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ncreighton](https://clawhub.ai/user/ncreighton) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, demand generation, and marketing operations teams use this skill to enrich prospect records, validate contact data, score leads, audit data quality, and prepare CRM or workflow updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may update CRM records or send prospect data to external workflow services at scale. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege CRM and enrichment credentials, run a small dry run first, and require a preview before any CRM, Sheets, Slack, or Zapier export. <br>
Risk: Prospect and customer data may be sensitive or regulated. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting production secrets into chat or committed files, and verify privacy, retention, consent, and compliance requirements before processing customer or regulated data. <br>
