## Description: <br>
Automatically generates Xiaohongshu-style lucky wallpapers, wealth avatars, and mood image cards across wealth, lucky, healing, and career themes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Creators and social-media operators use this skill to prepare prompt/configuration files for lucky-themed wallpapers, avatars, and Xiaohongshu-style visual posts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may help operate a logged-in Xiaohongshu or social account. <br>
Mitigation: Use dry-run or review mode first, inspect generated content and destination account, and require explicit confirmation before publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated files, drafts, or reports may persist in the output directory. <br>
Mitigation: Review where files are saved and remove drafts or reports that should not be retained. <br>
