## Description: <br>
Hainan Cost Index helps agents query and analyze Hainan Province construction cost indicators for investment estimates, cost comparisons, and structured project reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ruiyongwang](https://clawhub.ai/user/ruiyongwang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, construction cost analysts, and project planners use this skill to estimate investments, query cost indices, compare projects, and produce advisory reports for Hainan housing, municipal, public, and industrial construction projects. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Construction cost estimates may be stale, incomplete, or unsuitable for important investment decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as advisory and verify important figures against official current sources or a professional construction cost consultant. <br>
Risk: Broad cost-estimation triggers may invoke the skill for requests outside its Hainan construction-cost scope. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow activation to Hainan construction cost queries and confirm region, project type, area, and price period before estimating. <br>
