## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Milan costs, transport rules, visa requirements, healthcare details, taxes, rental practices, and event impacts can change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for planning guidance and verify time-sensitive or legal decisions with official sources or qualified professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Neighborhood, safety, housing, and nightlife guidance may vary by block, season, and user profile. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the user's role, time horizon, budget, commute, and district context before making specific recommendations. <br>
