## Description: <br>
Enhanced Prompt Suite is a broad prompt-template library for generating reusable prompt patterns, domain-specific templates, plans, reports, code, and guidance across many activity domains. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Agents and prompt authors use this skill as a large prompt-template library for selecting or adapting prompt patterns, decomposing domain tasks, and drafting structured outputs such as plans, reports, code, and guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers medical, mental-health, financial, legal, and compliance topics without clear safety limits. <br>
Mitigation: Treat these outputs as drafting or brainstorming only and require qualified human review before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Templates may produce advice for deletion, cancellation, write-off, termination, or other consequential workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Do not allow an agent to take real actions from these outputs without explicit user confirmation and appropriate review. <br>
Risk: The library is very broad, which can make template fit and output quality uneven across domains. <br>
Mitigation: Use close supervision, verify the generated content against domain requirements, and narrow the prompt before operational use. <br>
