## Description: <br>
Driver Receipt Generator helps limo, chauffeur, rideshare, taxi, and delivery drivers create professional receipts, invoices, passenger records, income summaries, and tax-prep records through ReceAI. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bptravel2017](https://clawhub.ai/user/bptravel2017) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External drivers and independent contractors use this skill to collect ride or delivery details and generate professional receipts, emailed receipt links, or monthly income summaries for customer documentation and tax records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Receipt generation or email sending may disclose passenger, route, fare, payment, or contact details to ReceAI or an email recipient. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the passenger email address, route, fare, payment details, and third-party retention expectations before creating or emailing receipts; avoid including unnecessary passenger information. <br>
Risk: Generated receipts or income summaries may contain incorrect financial or trip details if the user provides incomplete information. <br>
Mitigation: Review receipt and summary details before sharing them with passengers, clients, or tax preparers. <br>
