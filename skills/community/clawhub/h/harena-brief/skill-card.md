## Description: <br>
Harena Brief generates Chinese market and news briefs from financial tickers or event keywords using live market and news sources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[allenzhang79](https://clawhub.ai/user/allenzhang79) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and individual investors use this skill to request structured Chinese briefs for public financial symbols or market-moving event keywords. It helps summarize what happened, why it matters, current short- and medium-term views, and catalysts to watch. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ticker symbols and event keywords are sent to a hosted MCP provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering private portfolio details, personal identifiers, non-public company information, or sensitive investment plans. <br>
Risk: Generated market or investment analysis may be incomplete, stale, or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify financial conclusions and underlying data before making decisions. <br>
