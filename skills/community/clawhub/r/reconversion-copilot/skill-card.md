## Description: <br>
Copilote de reconversion vers les metiers de la data: diagnostic structure, fiches metier, plan de transition, CV repositionne, preparation entretiens et suivi. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yerrochdi](https://clawhub.ai/user/yerrochdi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to create structured French-language career transition deliverables for experienced professionals moving into data roles. It supports profile diagnostics, role exploration, transition roadmaps, CV and LinkedIn repositioning, interview preparation, and progress check-ins. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create local files containing career history, CV details, diagnostic scores, and transition plans. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated workspace/reconversion files and MEMORY.md, retain only what is needed, and delete sensitive local career documents when they are no longer useful. <br>
Risk: The skill may recommend a specific external mentoring service. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the Data Transition link as a promotional option, compare it with alternatives, and avoid repeating it after user refusal. <br>
