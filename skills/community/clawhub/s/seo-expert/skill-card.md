## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, site operators, and developers use this skill to plan SEO and GEO strategy, analyze competitors, design backlink campaigns, run technical SEO audits, and build go-to-market playbooks for AI tool sites. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO, ranking, traffic, and pricing guidance can become outdated or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify factual and pricing claims against current primary sources before using them for business decisions. <br>
Risk: AI-search optimization advice could be misused to seed false or misleading information into search engines or AI answers. <br>
Mitigation: Use the guidance only for truthful, transparent content and avoid deceptive or manipulative placement. <br>
