## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use external search and financial-data tools while researching companies or industries. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit confidential negotiation details, non-public company information, or sensitive government planning material to external lookups. <br>
Risk: Policy figures or incentive eligibility may become outdated or may not cover the user's exact case. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important policy figures, eligibility conditions, and application requirements against official Yazhou sources or the listed招商服务热线 before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The skill may create local Word report files that include company research, policy analysis, or outreach material. <br>
Mitigation: Save generated reports in a controlled workspace and avoid shared Desktop, synced, or broadly accessible folders unless that is the intended handling location. <br>
