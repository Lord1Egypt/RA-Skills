## Description: <br>
浦江科德高级中学融合部（Adcote School Zhezhong Campus）对外介绍与咨询 Skill，用于向家长、学生、合作方和教育同行介绍学校基本信息、课程路径、学生支持、校园环境、表达边界和常见问答。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External education and admissions communicators use this skill to prepare school introductions, parent-facing consultation answers, open-day scripts, social media copy, one-page summaries, and bilingual descriptions for Pujiang Kede Senior High School's International Department. It is intended for public-facing explanation and fit assessment, not internal management analysis or student-private cases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad school or admissions prompts when the user does not clearly mean Pujiang Kede Senior High School's International Department. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking about this specific school and department before relying on the skill's prepared content. <br>
Risk: Admissions, fee, scholarship, policy, course, or application claims may become outdated or need official confirmation. <br>
Mitigation: Review public-facing claims against current official school or university information before publishing or using them in consultation. <br>
Risk: The source material excludes internal management, student privacy, sensitive cases, performance, retention, and business-operation analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Decline or redirect internal management and private student requests instead of adapting this public-facing information skill. <br>
