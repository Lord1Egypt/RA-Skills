## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Analytics changes can affect user privacy obligations and site disclosures. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated diff, update privacy notices or cookie banners as needed, and use the consent option where users or jurisdictions require it. <br>
Risk: An incorrect GA4 Measurement ID or unreviewed implementation can produce misleading analytics data. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the Measurement ID, deploy to an appropriate environment, and verify collection in GA4 real-time reports before relying on the data. <br>
