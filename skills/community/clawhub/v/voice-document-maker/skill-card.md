## Description: <br>
Creates MP4 voice-video cards by converting text to TTS speech, generating a background image, and combining the image and audio with FFmpeg for WeChat-style delivery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[interccy-stack](https://clawhub.ai/user/interccy-stack) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to package narration text as short MP4 video cards with synthesized speech and generated background imagery for WeChat video-card delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Narration text and background prompts are processed by external generation services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid secrets, personal data, and regulated content in text or image prompts before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Unsafe output filenames can overwrite files outside the intended output folder. <br>
Mitigation: Use simple filenames rather than paths, and review the output argument handling before deployment. <br>
Risk: The script contains hardcoded Windows paths for FFmpeg and Python. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect and adjust the FFmpeg and Python paths for the target runtime before execution. <br>
