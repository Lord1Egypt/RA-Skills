## Description: <br>
Track urgent appointment calls across multiple providers with a live availability board, call log, openings, callbacks, and confirmation details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to organize urgent scheduling calls across offices or providers, compare the soonest openings, track callbacks and waitlists, and confirm appointment details before relying on a slot. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review reports unsafe healthcare access advice in examples, including portal monitoring and sensitive medical-document handling without adequate safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Review the examples before use; remove scalper or broker suggestions and limit portal or medical-document workflows to explicit consent, official channels, credential, PHI, storage, and retention safeguards. <br>
Risk: Users may treat scheduling guidance as medical, legal, or other professional advice when urgent situations are involved. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use logistics-only, avoid judging whether it is safe to wait, and direct emergencies or immediate risks to emergency services or the appropriate licensed professional channel. <br>
