## Description: <br>
humanizer-cn identifies and rewrites AI-generated patterns in Chinese text so the result reads more natural and human-written. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[0xcjl](https://clawhub.ai/user/0xcjl) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and developers use this skill to revise Chinese prose that sounds AI-generated by detecting common AI-writing patterns and producing a more natural rewrite. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting can change tone, nuance, attribution, or meaning. <br>
Mitigation: Review the revised text against the original before relying on it, especially where wording or attribution matters. <br>
Risk: The skill may be unsuitable for sensitive, legal, academic, compliance, or authorship-critical text without careful review. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when a reviewer can verify every change and preserve required meaning, authorship, and compliance context. <br>
