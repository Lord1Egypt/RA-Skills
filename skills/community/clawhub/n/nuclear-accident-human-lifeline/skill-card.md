## Description: <br>
Identify, pre-connect with, and rapidly activate trusted local humans (neighbors, officials, experts, and everyday residents) who become your real-time network for shared shelter, accurate information, resource pooling, and mutual aid the moment a nuclear accident or fallout event begins. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users preparing for radiation-release emergencies use this skill to identify nearby trusted contacts, build low-pressure mutual-aid agreements, and activate a local check-in network during a nuclear accident or fallout alert. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to keep sensitive notes about real nearby people and their assets. <br>
Mitigation: Get explicit permission before saving contact details or capabilities, minimize sensitive fields such as trust ratings or asset inventories, store files securely, and delete them when no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Emergency coordination guidance could conflict with official nuclear accident instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Follow emergency services and local authorities first, then use the human network only as a supplementary mutual-aid channel. <br>
