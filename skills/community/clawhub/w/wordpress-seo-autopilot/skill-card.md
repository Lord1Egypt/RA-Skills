## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may propose live WordPress edits, bulk metadata updates, schema changes, or internal-link modifications that affect site content and search performance. <br>
Mitigation: Use a staging site first, back up content, process large sites in small batches, and require explicit approval before applying changes to a live site. <br>
Risk: The skill uses WordPress, RankMath, Google Search Console, and SEO service credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege credentials, store secrets outside prompts and source files, rotate credentials when needed, and avoid broad administrator access unless required. <br>
Risk: Automated SEO recommendations can introduce incorrect, misleading, or brand-inconsistent metadata, schema, or linking guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review for critical pages, test changes on lower-risk content first, and monitor outcomes so changes can be rolled back. <br>
