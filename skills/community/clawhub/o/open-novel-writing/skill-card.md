## Description: <br>
open-novel-writing helps Chinese-language novel writers manage settings, outline stories, plan chapters, draft prose, and review quality across a full long-form writing workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[SoftMeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/SoftMeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers and creative teams use this skill to develop Chinese long-form fiction from an initial idea into settings, outlines, chapter specs, draft chapters, and review reports. It can also run batch chapter advancement for repeated planning, drafting, review, and revision steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create or overwrite local novel project files during drafting and batch mode. <br>
Mitigation: Use it in a dedicated project folder, keep backups or version control, and confirm chapter ranges and overwrite expectations before batch runs. <br>
Risk: Generated drafts, chapter specs, and review reports may contain continuity, quality, or style issues. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated files before relying on them and manually revise outputs that do not match the story plan or quality bar. <br>
Risk: Batch chapter advancement can repeatedly plan, draft, review, and revise multiple chapters. <br>
Mitigation: Start with a small chapter count, inspect outputs between runs, and stop or pause the workflow when the direction changes. <br>
