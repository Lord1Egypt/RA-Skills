## Description: <br>
Academic paper writing for 3D vision, computer graphics, CAD, and 3D understanding. Covers NeRF, 3DGS, SLAM, point cloud, 3D shape, CAD modeling. Supports CVPR/ICCV/ECCV/SIGGRAPH venues. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jaccen](https://clawhub.ai/user/jaccen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and technical authors use this skill to draft and revise academic papers in 3D vision, computer graphics, CAD, and 3D understanding. It helps structure abstracts, introductions, related work, methods, experiments, contribution statements, terminology, citation checks, and venue-specific writing style. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated academic writing may include inaccurate citations, venue facts, recent-paper references, or unsupported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify citations, venue requirements, recent-paper status, and all quantitative claims against authoritative sources before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: The skill has a Chinese academic writing default that may not match every target venue or output language. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the desired output language, venue style, and formatting expectations in the request. <br>
