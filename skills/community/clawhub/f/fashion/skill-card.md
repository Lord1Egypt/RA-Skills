## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for outfit advice, wardrobe strategy, shopping guidance, and dress-code problem solving. It adapts recommendations to body proportions, budget, climate, lifestyle, culture, accessibility needs, and event context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fashion advice may involve personal body, budget, cultural, religious, accessibility, or medical-device context. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the personal context needed for styling advice and avoid providing sensitive details beyond the user's comfort level. <br>
Risk: Shopping, sizing, trend, and dress-code guidance can be wrong for a specific brand, region, workplace, or event. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's current context, preferences, constraints, and venue expectations before relying on recommendations. <br>
