## Description: <br>
AI-powered real estate investment decision support for property valuation, mortgage comparison, rental analysis, city assessment, tax planning, and investment return modeling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to produce real estate investment analyses, including valuation reports, loan comparisons, rental cash-flow estimates, city investment scores, tax calculations, and buy/sell risk signals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial, tax, policy, rate, and market outputs may be treated as authoritative investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support only and require users to verify assumptions, current market data, and tax or policy claims before acting. <br>
Risk: The skill may encourage external data fetching or ongoing monitoring for property listings, rates, policies, or alerts. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before enabling external data fetching, monitoring, or alert workflows, and disclose any required dependencies before installation. <br>
