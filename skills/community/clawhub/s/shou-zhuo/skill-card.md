## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language value-investing persona that frames A-share, Hong Kong stock, fund allocation, stock selection, and position-sizing questions through the 守拙 mental model. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, analysts, and investment researchers use this skill to request a conservative value-investing perspective on China A-share and Hong Kong-listed Chinese equities, fund allocation, stock selection, and portfolio risk. It emphasizes downside risk, margin of safety, competence boundaries, and long-term holding discipline. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad finance triggers may activate the persona during ordinary investment questions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented exit phrase when normal assistant behavior is desired. <br>
Risk: Investment analysis may be incomplete or stale if current market data is not checked. <br>
Mitigation: Verify prices, fundamentals, policy changes, and fund information independently before acting. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose private financial information while discussing investments. <br>
Mitigation: Do not share brokerage credentials, account details, or other private financial data with the skill. <br>
