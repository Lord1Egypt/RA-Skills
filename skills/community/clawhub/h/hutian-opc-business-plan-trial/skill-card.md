## Use Case: <br>
External users and business teams use this skill to collect project information through guided Q&A and draft a structured business plan PPT for funding, technology transfer, market entry, or strategic planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business, financial, or fundraising inputs may contain sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Review and minimize sensitive information before sharing it with the agent or including it in generated business plan materials. <br>
Risk: Generated market, financial, and fundraising content may be incomplete or inaccurate without domain review. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified reviewers validate assumptions, figures, and claims before using the output for investor, market-entry, or strategic decisions. <br>
