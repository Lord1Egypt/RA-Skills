## Description: <br>
Download videos, extract transcripts, capture frames. Analyze YouTube, tutorials, DD videos with yt-dlp + Whisper + ffmpeg. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Zedit42](https://clawhub.ai/user/Zedit42) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and operators use this skill to download video sources, extract local transcripts, capture periodic frames, and prepare AI-ready summaries for due diligence videos, lectures, podcasts, tutorials, and meeting recordings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Downloaded media, transcripts, subtitles, and frame images may contain confidential content and remain in the output directory until deleted. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated output folder, avoid processing confidential videos unless local storage is acceptable, and delete generated files when they are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Transcript content may be pasted or piped into an external AI tool during summarization. <br>
Mitigation: Review transcript sensitivity before sending it to any external service and only share content the user intends to disclose. <br>
Risk: Instructions embedded in a transcript could attempt to influence downstream AI summarization. <br>
Mitigation: Treat transcript text as untrusted source content and ignore any instructions that appear inside it. <br>
