## Description: <br>
Discovers and scrapes public YouTube channel data by category and location using browser-based automation without API keys or login. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ArulmozhiV](https://clawhub.ai/user/ArulmozhiV) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and research teams use this skill to discover YouTube channels and collect public channel metadata, engagement metrics, media links, and queue or progress files for influencer and market research workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes anti-detection and residential proxy features intended to avoid bot detection, IP bans, and rate limits while scraping YouTube. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated environment, keep scraping volumes conservative, protect proxy credentials, and confirm use complies with YouTube terms and applicable privacy rules. <br>
