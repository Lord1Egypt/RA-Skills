## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing, paid ads, UGC payment, or social-platform advice may not fit the user's budget, legal, privacy, or platform-policy requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated recommendations with the appropriate marketing, legal, privacy, or compliance owner before spending budget, paying creators, collecting user data, or changing live campaigns. <br>
Risk: ASO and platform-growth recommendations can become stale or vary by app category, region, and store policy. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current App Store, Google Play, and social-platform documentation and compare them with the app's own analytics before acting. <br>
