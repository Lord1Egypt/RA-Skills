External teams, operators, and agents use this skill to audit conversion funnels, design statistically valid A/B tests, interpret results, and propose compliant conversion improvements for websites, SaaS products, and ecommerce flows. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CRO recommendations may affect customer-facing pricing, checkout, tracking, or marketing flows. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before applying recommendations to live experiences, especially pricing, checkout, analytics, session recording, or abandonment messaging. <br>
Risk: Analytics and A/B testing can involve cookies, identifiers, consent choices, or personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Keep tool access least-privilege and verify privacy, consent, opt-out, and regional marketing-law requirements before launch. <br>
Risk: Underpowered tests, early stopping, or multiple uncorrected variants can produce misleading conversion conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Pre-calculate sample size, run tests for their full planned duration, document hypotheses before launch, and apply appropriate statistical thresholds. <br>
