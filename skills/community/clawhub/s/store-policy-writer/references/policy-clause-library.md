# Policy Clause Library

Reusable, fill-in clauses for the decisions that cause most disputes. Pick the variant that matches the business and insert real numbers.

## Return window

- **Standard:** "within 30 days of delivery."
- **Extended (builds trust for higher-ticket):** "within 60 days of delivery."
- **Holiday extension:** "Orders placed Nov 1–Dec 24 may be returned through Jan 31."
- Always anchor to the **delivery date**, not the order date.

## Condition requirements

- Apparel: "unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached."
- Electronics: "in original packaging with all accessories; not activated/registered."
- General: "unused and in resalable condition."

## Who pays return shipping (state per scenario)

- Defect/our error → "we cover return shipping and send a prepaid label."
- Change of mind → choose: "free returns," or "a flat $X return fee is deducted from your refund," or "the customer pays return shipping."
- Restocking fee (use sparingly, disclose clearly): "a X% restocking fee applies to [category]."

## Refund timing & method

- "Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect your return."
- Store credit option: "Choose a refund to your original payment method or store credit (issued within 1 business day)."

## Exchanges

- "We replace defective or incorrect items at no cost. For size/color exchanges, [process]."

## Exclusions (itemize; keep fair)

- Hygiene: underwear, swimwear without liner, pierced jewelry, opened cosmetics.
- Custom/personalized items.
- Perishables.
- Final-sale items (must be clearly marked at checkout).
- Digital goods/gift cards.

## Shipping clauses

- Processing: "Orders ship within 1–2 business days (excluding weekends/holidays)."
- Free-shipping threshold: "Free standard shipping on orders over $X."
- Duties/taxes (international): "International customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes."
- Damaged in transit: "Contact us within 7 days with photos and we'll replace or refund."

## Privacy clauses

- No-sale statement: "We do not sell your personal information."
- Marketing consent: "We only send marketing emails if you opt in, and every email has an unsubscribe link."
- Processor disclosure: "We share data with service providers strictly to operate our store — payment processing, shipping, and analytics."

## Terms clauses

- Pricing errors: "We reserve the right to correct pricing errors and cancel affected orders with a full refund."
- Liability cap: "Our liability is limited to the amount you paid for the product."
- Governing law: "These terms are governed by the laws of [state/country]."

## Contact & change clauses

- "Questions? Email [address] — we reply within 1 business day."
- "We may update these policies; the effective date above reflects the latest version."
