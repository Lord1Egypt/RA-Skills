## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Backup procedures can expose sensitive operational data such as credentials, repository passwords, restore paths, and storage locations. <br>
Mitigation: Store restore procedures and secrets in approved secure systems, restrict access, and review operational details before use. <br>
Risk: Generic backup guidance may not fully cover a specific environment, restore objective, or retention obligation. <br>
Mitigation: Validate backup and retention plans with restore tests, integrity checks, documented recovery timing, and any required compliance review. <br>
