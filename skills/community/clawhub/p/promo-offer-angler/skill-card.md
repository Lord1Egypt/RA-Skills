## Description: <br>
Generate safe signup promo offer angles for CraveGo.ph (Philippines). Use friendly & casual brand voice. Focus ONLY on new-user/sign-up promos and avoid legal/financial claims. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams and social media operators use this skill to generate JSON-formatted new-user signup promo angles for CraveGo.ph posts on FB, IG, and TikTok. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Philippines <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated promotional copy may be inaccurate for the brand, unsuitable for a platform's advertising rules, or misaligned with local Philippines advertising compliance. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated offers for brand accuracy, platform policy fit, and local advertising compliance before publishing. <br>
Risk: Promo wording could imply guarantees or unsupported financial or legal claims if edited after generation. <br>
Mitigation: Keep final copy limited to safe signup-promo phrasing and remove guarantees or financial/legal assertions. <br>
