## Description: <br>
Automatically generate social media posts from articles for Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Threads, and Bluesky. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fly3094](https://clawhub.ai/user/fly3094) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content marketers, solopreneurs, agencies, and social media operators use this skill to repurpose articles into platform-specific posts, captions, short-form video scripts, image prompts, schedules, and A/B variations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated posts may contain inaccurate claims, unsuitable tone, rights issues, or content that conflicts with platform policy. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a drafting aid and review posts for accuracy, rights, tone, and platform policy before scheduling or publishing. <br>
Risk: The artifact discloses npm package install notes for Threads and Bluesky integrations. <br>
Mitigation: Approve package installs or social-account integrations only when the user trusts the packages and intends to use those platforms. <br>
Risk: Scheduling-oriented output can be mistaken for unattended publishing instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep a human approval step before posts are scheduled or published. <br>
