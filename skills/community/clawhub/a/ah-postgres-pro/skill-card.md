## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may recommend high-impact PostgreSQL changes involving schemas, configuration, authentication, replication, backups, failover, vacuum settings, or production systems. <br>
Mitigation: Require a named target environment, a recent verified backup, a rollback plan, and explicit approval for each operational change before execution. <br>
Risk: Database administration advice can affect availability, data integrity, or recovery posture if applied without validation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as advisory by default, test changes incrementally, and verify monitoring, backups, and recovery procedures before applying them to production. <br>
