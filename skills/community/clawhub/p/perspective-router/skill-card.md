## Description: <br>
Nova Perspective Router matches a task description to one to three expert-style perspectives and returns routing guidance for deeper analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[catplus-eric](https://clawhub.ai/user/catplus-eric) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and developers use this skill to route complex, multi-domain prompts toward relevant expert-style perspective skills before composing a final response. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may influence the style and framing of ordinary analysis responses. <br>
Mitigation: Review or narrow trigger phrases when explicit opt-in routing is preferred. <br>
Risk: Keyword-only matching can miss complex intent or select an imperfect perspective. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the routed perspective as advisory and keep final response judgment with the calling agent. <br>
