# Meme Engine Reference

For `/find-memes` and `/generate-meme`. Output meme concepts in this format:

```
FORMAT: [template name]
CAPTION / TEXT: [what goes on the image]
WHY IT HITS: [one-line psychological reason]
```

If the user wants an actual image, suggest the template and they'll generate. This skill does not produce raster images by default.

## Core Solana / CT meme formats

### 1. Two-button / decision panel
Sweaty hand hovering between two buttons.
- Top: a known wrong-but-tempting choice ("ape the new launch")
- Bottom: the obvious-but-painful right choice ("dca and touch grass")
Captures cope, FOMO, the gap between knowing and doing.

### 2. Drake yes/no
- No: the cringe behavior CT mocks
- Yes: the based behavior CT pretends to do
Works because CT is built on identity policing.

### 3. Galaxy brain (expanding brain)
- Tier 1: normie take
- Tier 2: slightly smarter
- Tier 3: midwit cope
- Tier 4 (galaxy): looping back to the normie take but framed as ascended
Best for narrative ladders. Wallet intel: "checking price" → "checking holders" → "checking dev wallet" → "feeling the vibe."

### 4. Wojak family
- **Doomer wojak**: rugs, exit liquidity, late-cycle pain
- **Crying wojak / sold the bottom**: regret content
- **Soyjak / pointing**: ridicule another tribe
- **Chad wojak**: "yes" energy, conviction posts
- **NPC**: criticizing low-information crypto takes
Use sparingly. Wojaks are overused; pick the specific subtype with intent.

### 5. Pepe variants
- **Comfy pepe**: holding through volatility
- **Smug pepe**: "I told you so"
- **Sad pepe**: missed opportunity / underwater
- **Apu**: wholesome ecosystem support

### 6. "How it started / how it's going"
Two screenshots, one transformation. Works for builder arcs, portfolio swings, founder evolutions.

### 7. Screenshot reaction stack
Take a real (or convincingly fake-styled) screenshot of a wallet, chart, tweet, telegram message. Add one sentence.
Pure receipt energy. Solana's strongest meme weapon because everything is on-chain and verifiable.

### 8. POV: tweets
"pov: you're a [solana founder / pump dev / liquidity provider] and..." then a relatable scenario. Casts the reader as the subject. Strong identification.

### 9. The bell curve / IQ chart
- Left (low IQ): simple based take
- Middle (midwit): elaborate cope
- Right (high IQ): same take as left, said differently
Brutal for calling out cycle behavior. "Just buy solana" — left and right agree.

### 10. Lord of the rings council
Four-panel "one does not simply..." — for big claims about the ecosystem. Older but still hits.

### 11. The new format slot
Memes mutate. Always reserve a slot for the format that emerged this week. Search before generating if uncertain. Check pump.fun trending and the larger CT accounts to spot fresh templates.

## Native Solana meme territory

Themes that always work because they're rooted in shared CT experience:

### Pump.fun cycle pain
- buying at the top
- dev rugging at 95k mcap
- the chad who sold at 1m and never came back
- "0.5 sol → 47 sol → 0.3 sol" rollercoaster posts

### Wallet pain
- approving a malicious contract
- sending to wrong address
- losing seed phrase
- "checking my wallet on monday morning"

### ETH vs SOL framing
- museum vs city
- gas fees vs sub-cent transactions
- gm vs gn maxis
Keep it playful. Don't punch down — the tribal war is a meme, not a movement.

### AI agent cycle (current)
- agents that are just prompts
- "autonomous" agents that need 6 humans to operate
- agent tokens that pump on a tweet from one launcher
- the gap between agent demos and agent reality

### Builder vs trader gap
- builder shipping during a bull run vs trader on perps
- "you guys are still trading? I'm building."
- shipping during a bear, watching apes outperform

### Cycle-relative
- "you weren't here in [previous cycle]"
- "this is your first cycle, isn't it"
- "the chart looks exactly like [past cycle], buckle up"

### Cope archetypes
- "fundamentals don't matter in a bear"
- "fundamentals matter in a bull"
- "wait until [arbitrary date] and you'll see"

## Meme psychology — why memes hit

A meme works when it satisfies one of these:

1. **Recognition.** "this is literally me." Identity confirmation.
2. **Tribalism.** "us vs them." In-group reinforcement.
3. **Compression.** A 3-paragraph thought collapsed to one image.
4. **Catharsis.** Names the pain everyone is feeling but not saying.
5. **Schadenfreude.** The other tribe's loss, made visible.
6. **Aspiration / cope.** Either "I am that chad" or "I will be."
7. **Inside joke.** Only people who lived through [event] get it.
8. **Truth no one says out loud.** The forbidden honest take.

When generating, identify which of these the meme is targeting. If it hits zero, kill it.

## Meme lifecycle

| Stage | Signals | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Emerging** | Used by <5 accounts, fresh format | Use early, builds early-adopter cred |
| **Peaking** | Every major account using it, top of feed | Use 1–2 times, then drop |
| **Saturated** | Brand accounts and noobs using it | Stop using or use ironically |
| **Dead** | Last week's saturated format | Avoid except for self-aware "remember this?" |

Solana memes burn faster than general crypto memes. A format that's hot Monday is often dead by Thursday.

## Generation rules

- One concept per output, unless the user asks for a batch. If batch, vary formats (don't give 3 wojak variants).
- Caption length: ≤ 12 words ideal, ≤ 20 hard cap.
- Lowercase unless the format demands caps (impact-font two-line meme, etc.).
- Specific > generic. "Pump.fun dev at 95k mcap" > "memecoin dev."
- If the meme requires a real person's likeness, only use figures who are public CT characters (anons, public founders) and only for non-defamatory humor.
- Never produce a meme that mocks an identifiable real person for personal traits (looks, family, illness). Mocking takes, projects, public positions — fair game.

## "Why it works" template

When labeling a meme's psychological hook, be specific:

- "lands because it names the exact pump.fun cycle pain everyone felt this week"
- "tribalism — sol holders flagging eth holders' midwit takes"
- "recognition — every founder has had this exact friday"

If you can't articulate why it works in one line, the meme isn't ready.
