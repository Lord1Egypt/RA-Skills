## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to call the Spraay gateway for payments, swap quotes, token prices, balance queries, AI inference, ENS resolution, and chain data. It is intended for workflows that need one gateway interface across payment, DeFi, AI, and blockchain operations. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payment, swap, and paid endpoint calls can spend real funds or incur x402 micropayments. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm recipient addresses, token, chain, amounts, slippage, fees, and the configured gateway URL with the user before approving a transaction. <br>
Risk: AI responses are generated by third-party models and may be unsuitable for high-stakes decisions without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content before relying on it and keep human approval in workflows where accuracy or safety matters. <br>
Risk: A misconfigured gateway URL could send requests to an unintended endpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Verify SPRAAY_GATEWAY_URL is the intended Spraay gateway before using paid or financial endpoints. <br>
