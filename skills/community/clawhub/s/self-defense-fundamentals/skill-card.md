## Description: <br>
Personal safety awareness and basic physical self-defense techniques for users who want practical self-defense knowledge without martial arts training. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to provide practical personal-safety guidance for everyday situations such as commuting, working late shifts, solo travel, delivery work, or isolated workplaces. The skill emphasizes awareness, avoidance, de-escalation, escape, and jurisdiction-specific checks before legal or weapon-related advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Physical self-defense guidance can be misapplied or escalated beyond defensive escape. <br>
Mitigation: Keep recommendations escape-focused, prioritize awareness, avoidance, and de-escalation, and frame physical response as a last resort for immediate danger. <br>
Risk: Legal and weapon-related advice varies by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify local law or consult a qualified professional before acting on legal or weapon-related guidance. <br>
