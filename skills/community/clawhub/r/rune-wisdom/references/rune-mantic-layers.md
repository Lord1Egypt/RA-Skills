# Mantic Interpretation Layers: Three Russian Esoteric Schools

> This reference merges three distinct Russian esoteric traditions of runic interpretation into a single comparative resource. **These are three different schools with different epistemologies** — do not blend them into one voice. Each school illuminates a different facet of the runes:
>
> - **Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin):** Practical, domain-specific divinatory meanings (Business, Personal/Love, Health) with position-dependent interpretation. Concrete, grounded, situational.
> - **Kys School (A.N. Kys):** Psychological and advisory layer emphasizing inner processes, self-reflection, and the dynamics underlying rune manifestations. Depth-oriented, introspective.
> - **Shi School (Школа Магии Ши):** Tree correspondences, auxiliary rune meanings, runescript crafting, and the material-energetic dimension. Craft-oriented, practical magic.
>
> For each rune, all three perspectives are presented side by side. Where a school does not cover a specific aspect, that section is omitted rather than interpolated.
>
> **Sources:**
> - Velya/Runava/Sery Angel school (Kaoranah, based on materials by S. Batyushkov and F. Asvin): https://www.velyarunavaangel.org/t1695-topic
> - Kys A.N. psychological/advisory system (posted by Kaoranah): https://www.velyarunavaangel.org/t1611-topic
> - Shi School of Magic (Амон Ра): https://www.velyarunavaangel.org/t509-topic

> **On Position Terminology:** This reference uses "Upright" and "Reversed" following the Velya and Kys school conventions. However, the Elder Futhark runes actually have up to **three possible positions** — Direct (прямое), Mirrored (зеркальное), and Inverted (перевёрнутое). What the Velya school calls "Reversed" (перевернутое) may correspond to either Mirrored or Inverted depending on the rune's graphic symmetry — and these are fundamentally different: Mirrored position reflects a **conscious, deliberate** stance toward the rune's principle, while Inverted position represents an **externally imposed** condition.
>
> **Physical basis:** When a rune tile is drawn from a sack, "Upright" (face up, right way) = Direct. "Reversed" (face up, upside down) = Inverted for vertically-symmetric runes but actually Mirrored for horizontally-symmetric runes. A face-down tile may represent the Mirrored position as a conscious withholding — the rune's principle is hidden, not negated. Bednenko herself acknowledges that the Mirrored position for asymmetrical runes requires intentional inscription rather than emerging from random draw.
>
> The Kys school's "Internal/External Manifestation Principle" (see Thurisaz entry) touches on this distinction but does not fully map it. For the complete three-position system with per-rune graphic logic and physical mechanics, see [`rune-three-positions-bednenko.md`](rune-three-positions-bednenko.md). For an alternative perspective distinguishing mirrored from inverted with practical magical applications for each position, see [`raduga-mirror-inverted.md`](raduga-mirror-inverted.md).
>
> **Cross-reference for element-based combinations:** For a different approach to rune combinations using element classification (Fire/Water/Air/Earth/Time) rather than situational prediction, plus bindrunes and graphic magic systems, see [`rune-combinations-elements.md`](rune-combinations-elements.md).

## SCHOOL OVERVIEWS

### Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin)

The Velya school provides **concrete, practical rune interpretations** for real-world divination. Each rune is read through domain-specific lenses (Business, Personal/Love, Health) with careful attention to position in the spread (initial vs. final). The tradition is grounded in extensive practical experience and emphasizes how surrounding runes modify individual rune meanings. It also includes a systematic approach to person-identification and yes/no divination.

### Kys School (A.N. Kys)

The Kys school adds a **psychological and advisory layer** that complements the Velya school's practical focus. Where Velya tells you what will happen, Kys illuminates the inner dynamics and psychological states that underlie external events. Key principles include the distinction between luck (Ansuz) and success (Jera), the internal/external manifestation principle for reversible runes, and the Perthro Principle ("this cannot be known"). The school also includes a unique Hagalaz Spread Technique for tracing causal connections between events.

### Shi School (Школа Магии Ши)

The Shi school contributes a **crafting and material-energetic dimension** absent from the other two traditions. Its distinctive features include: a polyvalent tree correspondence system (different woods for different aspects of the same rune), the concept of auxiliary rune meanings (functional roles in runescripts), practical runescript construction principles, pigment and coloring guidance, and magical application notes. The Shi school is essential for any runic crafting or operative (as opposed to purely divinatory) work.

## INTERPRETIVE PRINCIPLES

### Velya School Principles

**Position-Dependent Interpretation:** The position of a rune in the spread dramatically changes its interpretation:

1. **Initial Position (Past/Root Cause):** The rune describes what has already been set in motion. Fehu here means investment is required; in final position it means profit will come.
2. **Middle Position (Process/Current State):** The rune describes the active forces at work. Surrounding runes modify each other here most strongly.
3. **Final/Result Position (Outcome):** The rune describes the likely outcome. Pay special attention to repeated appearances of the same rune here across multiple readings — this can indicate external interference (positive or negative).
4. **Repeated Runes (In-Spread):** If the same rune appears more than once within a single spread, apply the two-mode repetition framework:
   - **Same rune, same orientation** (e.g., Fehu direct + Fehu direct) = **meaning amplification**. The rune's force is directly multiplied — two = doubled intensity, three = peak/crisis. This is the principle behind historical inscriptions like the Lindholm amulet (3× Algiz for protection, 3× Tiwaz for victory). Consult the per-rune amplification table in `rune-combinations-elements.md` §Part 1.5.G.
   - **Same rune, different orientations** (e.g., Fehu direct + Fehu inverted) = **attention amplification, NOT meaning amplification**. The rune demands focus, but the different orientations create a nuanced dialogue rather than a simple multiplier. The tension between orientations IS the message. For example, Uruz direct + Uruz inverted is not "double strength" — it is "strength and disempowerment coexist; you are powerful in one area and undermined in another."
5. **Repeated Runes (Cross-Reading Recurrence):** If the same rune appears repeatedly across different readings for the same querent:
   - **Positive runes** (Sowilo, Wunjo, Gebo) repeatedly in result position → genuine ongoing blessing
   - **Delaying runes** (Wunjo reversed, Dagaz) repeatedly in result position → possible professional negative magical interference; someone may be manipulating your event stream
   - **Blocking runes** (Isa, Naudhiz) repeatedly in result position → deliberate obstruction, possibly esoteric in nature
   - **Any rune recurring across different questions** → a core life theme that transcends the specific question. The querent is being asked to engage with this rune's principle at a fundamental level, not merely in the context of the question asked. Suggest meditation, journaling, or carrying the rune daily.
   - **Historical precedent:** In Germanic paganism, repeating runes were used intentionally to multiply their power. The Lindholm amulet (2nd–4th c.) contains three consecutive Tiwaz (invocation of Týr for victory), and eight consecutive Ansuz (invoking the full divine council). Ancient practitioners understood that repetition = multiplication of sacred force.

### Kys School Principles

**1. Luck vs. Success (Ansuz vs. Jera):**
What Ansuz interprets as "luck" (везение — divine favor, providence, gifts from the gods), Jera interprets as "success" (успех — positive results earned through correct, directed action). When Jera appears, the outcome is earned, not given. When Ansuz appears, the outcome is gifted or divinely ordained. This distinction profoundly changes how you advise the querent.

**2. Internal vs. External Manifestation (Thurisaz Principle):**
For reversible runes, the upright position tends toward external, manifest, active expression, while the reversed position tends toward internal, passive, latent expression. This is not a simple "good/bad" distinction — the boundary between meanings is thin, and the same core truth applies in both positions, but the mode of expression differs.

**3. The Perthro Principle: "This Cannot Be Known":**
When Perthro appears, it explicitly signals that the answer to the question cannot be obtained. The reader must then rely on surrounding runes for contextual clues, approximate meanings derived from the rune's name, and intuition (which should always be engaged when working with runes, not only for Perthro).

**4. Potential vs. Realized Energy:**
Several runes (especially Fehu) represent potential energies — conditions are created, but the querent must still act wisely to realize them. A rune indicating favorable conditions is NOT the same as a guaranteed outcome.

### Shi School Principles

**Primary and Auxiliary Meanings:**
Every rune possesses a direct meaning and an auxiliary meaning — a functional role it can play when added to a runescript:

- **Raido**: direct = "path, journey"; auxiliary = "synthesis" (connects and combines elements)
- **Eihwaz**: also serves as a synthesis rune (connecting and harmonizing)
- **Fehu**: direct = "wealth, money"; auxiliary = "providing a runescript with material loading/energetic charge"

**The Principle of Directed Flow:**
When you carve a rune, you are tracing a flow and directing it into the channel you need. A runescript is created to adjust this flow relative to a specific situation or desire.

**Fundamental Rules:**
1. **Never inscribe the current situation.** For example, if you want to protect someone, do not carve Hagalaz to represent their current dangerous situation. Instead, immediately inscribe what you want to achieve. Casting runes gives diagnosis; carving runes allows you to correct the situation based on the prior "case history."
2. **Carve runes beautifully and harmoniously.** Runes must be cut with aesthetic precision — they work more cleanly when well-crafted.
3. **Unexpected runes are meaningful.** If an unplanned rune emerges in a bind-rune composition, it is likely not accidental — it is cause for reflection on what additional force is manifesting.

## RUNESCRIPT TYPES (Shi School)

### Bound Runescript (Вязаный)
A runescript in which an ordered sequence of rune actions is established. A bound rune is like an irrigation system — the flow passes through each rune in a predetermined order. "Weaving differs from tangles by the presence of a pattern."

### Complex Runescript
A runescript in which all runes are bound simultaneously and act as a complex — all forces activate at once rather than in sequence.

### Palindrome Runescript
A runescript with a central rune to which identical runes are attached on both sides. It is read as a single entity from either direction. **Important caution with palindromes:** The central rune's full spectrum applies. For example, a person who wants to become rich through transformation takes Dagaz (transformation). But Dagaz's spectrum is wide — theoretically, the person could die and become a holy martyr. Adding Fehu on both sides of Dagaz clarifies what transformation and what outcome are desired. This can be further refined by adding Uruz on both sides.

**Palindrome combination examples:**
- **Uruz-Dagaz-Fehu** — "get rich quickly" (primal force accelerates transformation into wealth)
- **Fehu-Dagaz-Othala** — "get rid of a relative and receive an inheritance"

In a palindrome, runes can be interwoven not only horizontally but also vertically simultaneously.

## PIGMENTS AND COLORING (Shi School)

The traditional color for painting runes is **red**. The use of blood is a highly personal question, the answer to which depends on your readiness to use it and the meaning it acquires for you.

**Blood options:**
- Your own blood — creates a harder binding between you and the rune; can be considered a sacrifice when you need to achieve something
- Animal blood — also acceptable

**Recommended pigments:**
- **Ochre** — red from iron oxide
- **Hematite** — red from iron content
- Acceptable shades range from **orange to burgundy**

**Naturalness test:** Drop a pinch of the proposed pigment into a glass of water. If after a few hours the substance settles as sediment and the water remains clear, the pigment is natural. If the water remains colored, the pigment is artificial (synthetic dyes are not suitable for runic work).

## TREE CORRESPONDENCE SYSTEM (Shi School)

### Three Universal Trees
Three trees are suitable for carving any rune:

1. **Ash** (ясень) — the world tree, Yggdrasil; universal connection
2. **Yew** (тисс) — the tree of death and immortality; Eihwaz's tree; connects to the lower worlds
3. **Oak** (дуб) — the tree of strength and endurance; Thor's tree

### Secondary Universal Trees
- **Birch** (берёза) — good for most runes; pure, pliable growth energy
- **Hazel** (орешник/лещина) — particularly good for mantic (divinatory) use of runes

### Purpose-Dependent Tree Selection
The Shi school's tree system is **polyvalent** — each rune can be carved on different woods depending on which aspect of the rune's meaning you want to activate. This is a major difference from the English tradition which assigns a single tree per rune.

#### ᚠ Fehu

**Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin):**

**Upright — Business:** Fehu in the **final position** means the enterprise/project will be successful, or a new job will bring good money. Fehu in the **initial position** means you must invest heavily first before seeing profit. The rune marks the difference between "harvest" and "sowing."

**Upright — Personal:** The person being read for is financially secure and has no material problems (at least for the duration of the reading's timeframe).

**Upright — Health:** Even an elderly or ill person with Fehu in the final position is fundamentally healthy — the condition will stabilize and the disease will not progress for some time.

**Reversed — Business:** The enterprise will bring no money at best; at worst, you'll be lucky to break even at zero.

**Reversed — Personal:** Relationships will bring nothing but expenses. In a pre-wedding reading, Fehu reversed may indicate that the couple is a "financial catastrophe" combination for each other — a "person-catastrophe" who is destructive not to everyone, but specifically to this particular partner.

**Reversed — Health:** Expect illness. **With Thurisaz upright: possible surgery or serious injury.**

---

**Kys School:**

**Upright — Psychological Layer:** At the moment of the reading, **creative energy** is available that enables the acquisition of wealth (not necessarily material). All conditions are being created for a new enterprise, for "the start" — but Fehu represents **potential energies**. You must still use them wisely: no impulsive or unconsidered actions. Further actions require deliberation, weighing, and "householder's savvy."

**Upright — Advisory:** May also indicate the need to pay attention to the **physical plane** (especially for people who are perpetually "floating in the clouds"), strengthen connections with loved ones, and concern yourself with foundations and reserves. Acquisitions may not be of a "lasting" character — they may simply indicate that current actions are correct for now/the near future.

**Reversed — Psychological Layer:** Alienation. The person loses property and positions, is "knocked out of the rhythm of life." Connection with society (as a social circle) is disrupted, leading to awkward and ambiguous situations. May also indicate incorrect handling of property (not necessarily material), greed, avarice, or obstruction of the free circulation of property.

**Reversed — Advisory:** Fehu's energy must be both given and received — hoarding or blocking the flow creates the reversal.

- **Combination:** Fehu+Nauthiz — binds a person to what they acquire (creates lasting attachment)

#### ᚢ Uruz

**Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin):**

**Upright — General:** Uncontrollable energy. You cannot control the processes happening around you. Why you cannot — the surrounding runes will tell. Most likely you are facing an irresistible force.

**Upright — Business:** Nothing depends on you. If you're a subordinate, perhaps you're used to this. If you're a leader — think hard about how you ended up in a situation where nothing depends on you. For high-level executives: expect government or other "high" inspections that cannot be argued with.

**Upright — Personal:** Your relationship problems depend not on you but on your partner. You can do nothing about it. If a rune indicating the opposite sex appears nearby, someone is trying to "push you aside" for reasons beyond your control.

**Upright — Health:** Nature is taking its course, and neither you nor the doctors control it. This is more positive than negative — though much depends on surrounding runes.

**Reversed — General:** You not only don't control the situation, you have no strength or even a chance to. Sometimes indicates a person who has "given up" and lets everything drift.

**Reversed — Business:** "You've been had." Try not to lose everything. Let the situation down gently. Often appears when staff has been quietly cheating the boss for years. Recommend casting runes on the culprit — the runes can narrow suspects to 2-3 people.

**Reversed — Personal:** The relationship has exhausted itself and continues by inertia. Psychological incompatibility — they've "had enough" of each other but lack the courage to talk honestly and part.

**Reversed — Health:** The person may survive but will likely need permanent care — the patient will probably remain disabled and "work for the pharmacy" for a long time.

**Kys School:**

**Upright — Psychological Layer:** Symbolizes **personal strength** — both physical and psychic. Can reflect the ability to see things realistically. The sign of a strong person, confident in themselves and in what they want from life.

**Upright — Collective Action Principle:** Uruz specifically recommends **"making a bet on the collective"** — "one in the field is not a warrior." Together, with united efforts, it is far simpler and more effective to achieve what is desired when the entire collective works in the context of the rune. There is no place for egoism here — your ideas must serve not only you but the common good, and when you attain "good for all," it becomes "good for you."

**Upright — Short-Duration Action:** Uruz is a **rune of short-term action** — a single impulse cannot last long. The crowd achieves (or fails to achieve) its goals and eventually disperses home. This temporal limitation is crucial for timing advice.

**Upright — Additional Meanings:** May indicate the need to take risks. Signals an imminent surge of strength. Can show an upcoming struggle for freedom and independence, defending personal space, one's "right to be." Hints at success in this endeavor IF you follow the character of the rune (sober assessment, realistic evaluation of events, determination not to retreat).

**Reversed — Psychological Layer:** Disorganization of Strength, loss of energy, the "engine," the foundation. Most often shows weakness, obsession, dependence on others, on others' opinions, acting on someone else's instructions (not for your own benefit), and the inability — due to your own weakness — to escape this influence.

**Reversed — Collective Context:** Disorganization of connections in a collective. Possibly someone has appeared in the group who destroys it (even unconsciously), thereby making you weak, depriving you of your "foundation."

**Reversed — Advisory:** Advises against risk and urges caution, as your appearance means you are currently too weak. May indicate missed opportunities, failures, and disappointments. The key is to understand WHERE and WHY your Strength is malfunctioning — it's working differently than it should.

#### ᚦ Thurisaz

**Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin):**

**Upright — General:** Obstacles or physical danger. Since Thurisaz means "blade," "edge," "thorn" — be cautious: drive carefully, don't go out for cigarettes at 2 AM, avoid potentially dangerous situations. Also means "gate," "threshold," "obstacle." Concentrate and make no mistakes.

**Upright — Business:** Danger of ruthless competition. Prepare for a war of annihilation. **With Inguz:** either you win and rise to a new level, or you lose everything and start from zero. **With Ansuz:** passage through bureaucratic "purgatory" with document problems.

**Upright — Personal:** There is something to discuss with your partner in a hard, no-nonsense conversation. Like in business, this rune warns of a dangerous competitor and a brutal struggle where only one will survive. Ask yourself: does this person deserve such effort?

**Upright — Health:** Prepare for surgical intervention — it will at least be offered. The case may be urgent. **With Raidho + Fehu reversed: very high probability of serious trauma involving vehicles.** Also possible: criminal injury, even accidental knife cuts.

**Reversed — General:** You will pass "along the blade of a sword" and overcome obstacles, possibly with minimal losses.

**Reversed — Business:** The storm will pass very close but barely touch you. Small losses, but you remain undefeated and may even gain experience or "immunity" to this type of trouble. Could also mean competitors are trying to scare you, but the real problems will be much smaller than the threats.

**Reversed — Personal:** Minor unpleasantness in the relationship needs to be overcome. No global obstacles. May be time to boldly review the relationship and take steps to improve it.

**Reversed — Health:** "The devil is not as scary as he's painted." Surgery may not be needed, health is not under threat — though the condition may still be serious. Breathe easier, but don't fully relax yet.

**Kys School:**

**Upright — Psychological Layer:** Gates, pressure, decisiveness, defense, conflict, breakthrough, clearing of barriers. Recommends waiting — but not passively; rather for the purpose of deliberation and preparation for action, after which you should proceed to act. Makes sense to look back at the past and not drag what has outlived its usefulness behind you.

**Upright — Advisory:** Can be interpreted as conflict with external (possibly unknown) forces. A crisis may arrive, but it will accelerate change. This is NOT a rune of subtle intellectual play — in its sphere of action there is still much that is random and unpredictable.

**Reversed — Psychological Layer:** Blocked creative energies and various unconscious prohibitions that hinder development and self-expression. Surrounding runes can indicate constructive means of overcoming these blocks. Also associated with weakness and compulsion, violation of internal "structure" (moral aspect).

**Reversed — Key Distinction:** The situation is complex and tangled. Actions being taken are not as wise as desired; much now depends not on you, and the inability to "look truth in the eye" only worsens matters. The rune recommends pulling yourself together, turning inward, and identifying your true desires. Warns against betrayal and draws attention to insufficient protection.

**Reversed — Internal/External Principle:** The boundary between meanings is very thin — what is true for one is true for the other. However, in the reversed position, everything has a **passive, internal character**; in the upright, a more **external, manifest** character.

#### ᚨ Ansuz

**Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin):**

**Upright — General:** People are talking about you — and telling the truth. May indicate legal questions. Ansuz recommends talking with someone and discussing details. Seek advice from elders or more experienced people; possibly consult a lawyer.

**Upright — Business:** At the start of a new project: formalize everything legally, study all deal documents thoroughly. In the context of Russian business: conduct all operations legally, "in white." For large contracts — notarize. Review statistical data and consult with someone who has done similar work before.

**Upright — Personal:** Close people should talk to each other and put all the dots over the "i" — honestly, without concealment. People are discussing this couple truthfully — parents, or rivals.

**Upright — Health:** Consult a doctor even if you think everything is fine — but consult a real specialist, not someone who "had the same thing and knows what's what." **With Eihwaz: consider seeing a psychologist — talking about the mind.**

**Reversed — General:** Lies, false rumors, slander, denunciations, illegality.

**Reversed — Business:** You are being deceived, something is being hidden. Check ALL documents, contracts, receipts — from birth certificate to the last gas station receipt. A conspiracy is being prepared against you, possibly involving "legal" structures. Stay silent about your plans. May indicate information leaks or industrial espionage. Check if a case is being prepared against you.

**Reversed — Personal:** Prepare for an information war on the personal front! Verify all information from all acquaintances — or simply ignore it all. Your partner may come with a scandal about your behavior. Even absurd police reports from angry parents have been seen with this rune.

**Reversed — Health:** The true condition of the patient is being hidden from you — it could be better OR worse than claimed. A debtor may fake illness (even producing medical certificates), or a person already discharged may still be used to extract money from you.

**Upright — Psychological Layer:** Receiving a sign or message (both mundane and elevated). Creative thought. Artistic inspiration. Communication and information transfer. Also shows the origins of the current situation in a spread.

**Upright — Levels of Meaning:** These are the highest sources of information, both internal and external. In the simplest case — receiving a gift, unexpected luck. Someone's appearance or news from someone. In a more complex case — receiving a "sign from the gods" or expecting one, a spiritual gift. The rune recommends being sensitive and wise.

**Reversed — Psychological Layer:** Futility, delusion, misunderstanding. You may have missed your "sign" (see upright) — walked right past your luck. The cause of failures is most likely an internal state. Prohibitions and blocks that hinder harmonious development. Your actions are not as wise as you'd like; you focus on trifles and miss what's more important.

**Reversed — Advisory:** You may have become too closed off on mundane questions and literally have no time left for life. First, overcome despondency and the sense of futility, then soberly investigate the situation. The rune speaks of the inevitability and timeliness of the current situation, connected with transitional processes in your life.

**Reversed — Deep Layer:** Can reflect closedness, isolation, confusion, inability to follow one's "divine spark," suppression of the "divine beginning" within, one's own "I."

#### ᚱ Raidho

**Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin):**

**Upright — General:** Wagon, road, journey. In modern terms: automobile or train travel. Also means a successful journey.

**Upright — Business:** The project will require much running around. Work involves business trips — even if not officially stated. The journey/trip will be successful and beneficial. If reading about a new job with promised good pay: the employer may require you to get a driver's license, or it may turn out you need a personal car.

**Upright — Personal:** A shared life journey is assured, at least for the reading's timeframe. If thinking of buying a car/motorcycle — now is the time, you'll be lucky. If planning a trip together — go! The trip will be excellent. Advises ground transport over air travel.

**Upright — Health:** Recommends a recovery resort or change of scenery — dacha, sea, spa — anywhere, but you must go.

**Reversed — General:** Long-distance travel is not recommended. Better to ride the bus — your car will be safer, and you won't pay for bus repairs. Especially dangerous **with Thurisaz upright**.

**Reversed — Business:** Problems on business trips. Upon arrival, you may find the trip was unnecessary. If starting a new project: this project has no road — don't start. Or change some details, add backup plans, and it may work.

**Reversed — Personal:** This relationship has no road. Maybe later, or under different conditions. For family vacation: go separately, change destination, or change dates — but verify each new option with additional rune readings.

**Upright — Psychological Layer:** Path, control, direction, movement, equilibrium, coordination, reunification, journey, decision. #### ᚲ Kenaz

**Velya School (Batyushkov/Asvin):**

**Upright — General:** Control over the situation. Married man, or a domestically-inclined man. In "who did it?" readings: a man who is married or blends into the team, seems quiet, but is "nobody's fool."

**Upright — Business:** You fully control the situation. However, without you nothing will function yet — the business requires your constant presence. **With Othala: you may need to sleep at work.** But Kenaz is very positive — if you set things up well, success will come.

**Upright — Personal:** If a girl reads on a recently-met young man and Kenaz appears: the man is married or permanently living with a woman — **especially if Berkana also appears in the same reading.** Small chance he decided to marry the reader that quickly. If a woman reads on her established partner: the most rosy prospects — such a man doesn't shy from domestic duties.

**Upright — Health:** Recommends home care. If possible, keep the patient at home with a visiting doctor. Home atmosphere literally sustains the patient with hearth energy. If emergency hospitalization is required — go. But if told "you can pick them up tomorrow" and Kenaz appears — take them home.

**Reversed — General:** Complete lack of control due to your own negligence.

**Reversed — Business:** The situation is out of control solely due to your negligence, or your subordinates' negligence which you stopped monitoring. Unlike Uruz (which shows genuinely no strength to control), reversed Kenaz points to YOUR oversight and incompetence. Staff doesn't obey you, nobody respects you — and only you are to blame.

**Reversed — Personal:** The man has a passive life position. He deliberately decides nothing, wants to decide nothing, and prefers someone else do it for him. May also indicate a hapless lazy incompetent.

**Reversed — Health:** It's no longer in your power to control the situation — call the doctor. If the patient is very serious and in hospital: nobody can truly control the situation. If the gods will it — the patient recovers; if not — it's in the gods' hands.

Draw a single rune for a direct yes/no question. The rune's position (upright/reversed) and its conditional advice determine the answer. **Special cases:**
- **Isa:** Always NO (but the process continues without you — you're frozen out)
- **Wyrd:** Conditional YES — "by God's will" — demands further questions

#### Practical Notes on Yes/No Readings

1. **This method is simplistic.** It works for quick checks but should not be the sole method for important decisions. Always follow up with a fuller spread.
2. **Naudhiz nuance:** While listed as "always NO," practitioners note it's more of a "not yet" or "forced pause." A process is underway but hasn't completed. The question itself may be premature.
3. **Hagalaz is definitive NO.** No conditions, no nuance. The answer is simply no.
4. **Beware of wishful thinking.** Uruz upright warns that when you want an answer too badly, you'll twist all rune responses to fit. Approach yes/no readings with genuine neutrality.
5. **When a rune gives conditional YES,** the conditions are prescriptive — they tell the querent what to change or do for the favorable outcome. This makes yes/no readings surprisingly actionable.
6. **For ambiguous runes** (Naudhiz, Isa), consider drawing a second clarifying rune rather than forcing a yes/no interpretation.

### Kys School: Hagalaz Spread Technique

A unique spread method using Hagalaz as a structural framework for tracing connections between events:

**Method 1** (using the first variant of Hagalaz's shape):
- Place two runes: one to the right, one to the left of Hagalaz
- Hagalaz indicates directions of mutual influence between events

**Method 2** (using the second variant — the star/cross shape):
- Place additional runes along the branches/rays of Hagalaz
- Each branch traces a specific event and its connections to others

**Versatility of the technique** — depending on the question's initial conditions, Hagalaz can be used to:
- Trace connections between mutually influencing events
- Identify connections that must be broken
- Find events participation in which should be declined
- Show the base and goal that can be reached, plus paths and means of reaching it

The key insight: Hagalaz doesn't only mean destruction — it can serve as a **structural map** revealing the web of causality between events in the querent's life.

## HUMAN ARCHETYPES IN SPREADS (Raduga)

> When a rune appears in a spread that asks about a person — "What kind of person is X?", "Who is behind this situation?", "Describe my partner" — the rune functions as a **personality archetype**, compressing a whole character sketch into a single symbol. This interpretive layer is distinct from the domain-specific (Business/Personal/Health) or psychological layers: it answers not "what will happen" or "what is the inner state" but **"who is this person?"**
>
> The archetype descriptions below come from Екатерина Радуга (Струны Мира, 2018). They are vivid, colloquial, and deliberately unflattering when the rune is reversed — this is practical divination language, not academic prose. Use these when a rune falls in a position that describes a person rather than an event.
>
> **пп** = перевернутое положение (reversed/inverted position)
>
> **Source:** Радуга, Е. «Характеристика людей в рунических раскладах» — Струны Мира, 01.11.2018

### ᚠ Fehu
**Upright:** Creative person with business acumen — knows how to spend money wisely, not lose it but multiply it.
**пп:** Spendthrift, wastrel — someone who doesn't know the value of money, has lost creative interest in life, a good-for-nothing son sponging off parents.

### ᚢ Uruz
**Upright:** A person full of energy, ready to throw their strength where it finds application.
**пп:** Someone who doesn't know where to apply themselves, wasting energy left and right, often getting angry without cause — venting unused "steam" wherever it goes.

### ᚦ Thurisaz
**Upright:** Aggressive personality who stops at nothing to reach their goal, crushing everything in their path — sometimes useful to society.
**пп:** A damaged person whom everyone kicks around — possibly through their own fault.

### ᚨ Ansuz
**Upright:** Talker, artist, writer, poet, singer, orator, MC, broadcaster, philosopher.
**пп:** Someone who can't string two words together, mute, quiet one — or a liar, deceiver, someone isolated from information.

### ᚱ Raidho
**Upright:** A proper person who knows the rules and lives by them.
**пп:** Someone who has lost their bearings — doesn't know the rules or the direction of movement, seeks easy paths that clash with the laws of society.

### ᚲ Kenaz
**Upright:** Mage, healer, engineer — a specialist who knows what they're doing.
**пп:** Burned-out personality, or someone who was never a light to begin with — dull, ignorant, insensitive, an empty shell.

### ᚷ Gebo
**Upright:** Sweet couple, husband and wife, or business partners — 50/50. Gebo has no reversed position; in person-descriptions it always indicates a paired dynamic.

### ᚹ Wunjo
**Upright:** Kind-hearted person, easy to connect with, who can find joy in small but pleasant things.
**пп:** Phlegmatic who agrees with everything just to be left alone — gets satisfaction not from the work itself but from the fact that it's done.

### ᚺ Hagalaz
**Upright:** A person who destroys everything in their path, often without realizing it — can inadvertently jinx or hex with a single word, without even resorting to magic.
*Note: Hagalaz has no reversed position in the traditional system.*

### ᚾ Naudhiz
**Upright:** A bad person with a heap of personal flaws and problems that bring nothing but harm to society. The key point: fighting this character is useless — only the grave will fix them.
**пп:** A person possessed by their own unconscious urges bubbling up from within — needs that rarely bring benefit but almost always bring trouble.

### ᛁ Isa
**Upright:** A limited personality — in sex, work, friends, creativity. May indicate frigidity, a blonde, a nurse, a thin or simply tall woman.
*Note: Isa has no reversed position; the description itself covers the constrained state.*

### ᛃ Jera
**Upright:** Core qualities are measuredness and constant development — a person always growing while bringing only benefit to those around them.
*Note: Jera has no reversed position; the archetype is inherently constructive.*

### ᛇ Eihwaz
**Upright:** Stubborn but consistent person, ready to endure for a long time to reach their goal — whose stages of development span a lifetime.
*Note: Eihwaz has no reversed position; the archetype describes steadfast endurance.*

### ᛈ Perthro
**Upright:** Person of great potential for whom no barriers to getting what they want exist — a sponge who absorbs and constantly lets in the new. May indicate occult abilities.
**пп:** Conservative traditionalist for whom "New Age" is a dirty word. A hermit who loves solitude. People of this type resist development, live in the past — still reminiscing about sausage at 2.20 and vodka at 3.62.

### ᛉ Algiz
**Upright:** A person with a hundred roads before them, each leading to their cherished goal. For this person, personal development comes first — only the tree that stretches its branches toward the sun receives the energy needed for growth.
**пп:** A dependent person who cannot exist on their own — lacking leadership qualities or the ability to create anything. Someone who cannot stand up for themselves, who would rather endure hardship than tell their boss what they really think of them.

### ᛋ Sowilo
**Upright:** Bright personality in any collective, always visible because they know how to present themselves effectively. If male — leader and motor of the group. If female — beautiful, possibly the "face of the company."
*Note: Sowilo has no reversed position; the archetype is inherently radiant.*

### ᛏ Tiwaz
**Upright:** Honest one, military type — someone who achieves everything through honest means, a law-abiding citizen nearly impossible to knock off their true path.
**пп:** May indicate an elderly man, sometimes a stupid incompetent worker, or a person who has earned retirement.

### ᛒ Berkano
**Upright:** Family woman — children, family, the one who feeds and cares for the household.
**пп:** An older woman who cannot bear children, a bad person, or simply a mistress.

### ᛖ Ehwaz
**Upright:** A person who connects well with others for mutual development — to be useful to society.
**пп:** Someone outside the social game due to polarized interests — a rider who has lost their horse.

### ᛗ Mannaz
**Upright:** Ideal person, role model — always knows how to smooth a situation when necessary. Acts in society's interests.
**пп:** Egoistic personality who isn't troubled about achieving goals through deception — on the contrary, they proclaim: "I think this way, this is my position, so I don't care about anyone, I'm the best."

### ᛚ Laguz
**Upright:** Pleasant young woman, possibly with ties to witchcraft — though her character is fickle as her mood, simultaneously everywhere and nowhere.
**пп:** Older woman, or simply a woman burdened by her own problems that strongly affect her life — where nothing happens and there's no light at the end of the tunnel. Core principle: "I won't do anything, let whatever will be, be — it's God's will."

### ᛝ Ingwaz
**Upright:** A person in constant dynamic motion that leads to the fulfillment of their plans and the realization of all projects.
*Note: Ingwaz has no reversed position; the archetype is inherently generative.*

### ᛞ Dagaz
**Upright:** A person who quickly adapts to new waves — innovator, experimenter, active personality. Transforming their surroundings is their life's goal.
*Note: Dagaz has no reversed position; the archetype is inherently transformative.*

### ᛟ Othala
**Upright:** Master of their own life — knows how and where to set things right so their "HOME" is cozy.
**пп:** A person without roots — someone who finds it hard to discover their "three notes" to play in the right sequence with drive, thereby leaving behind a "melody" as their legacy.
