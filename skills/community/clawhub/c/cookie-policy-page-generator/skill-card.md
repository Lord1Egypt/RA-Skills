## Description: <br>
Guides agents in creating or optimizing cookie policy page content, including cookie types, consent flows, jurisdiction notes, and legal-review disclaimers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and content teams use this skill to draft or improve cookie policy pages with clear cookie categories, consent-management references, and prompts for legal review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cookie policy guidance may be mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep legal-review disclaimers in generated output and have qualified counsel review policy text before publication. <br>
Risk: Broad cookie-related trigger wording may invoke the skill for unrelated cookie requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants website cookie policy or consent guidance before producing policy content. <br>
