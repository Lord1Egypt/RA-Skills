## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Strategy, investment-related, legal, or market recommendations may be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general analysis drafts and review them with qualified business, financial, legal, or domain experts before acting. <br>
Risk: Business analysis prompts may include confidential company information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential company information unless the workspace is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Market assessments can be incorrect or outdated if source data and assumptions are not checked. <br>
Mitigation: Require current source attribution, validate key assumptions, and document uncertainty before using recommendations. <br>
