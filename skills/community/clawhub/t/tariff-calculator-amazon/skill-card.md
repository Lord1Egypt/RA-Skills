## Description: <br>
Universal tariff calculator for Amazon sellers that estimates import duties, landed costs, VAT/GST, Section 301 tariffs, trade agreement rates, and HS code matches for common global trade routes without requiring an API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/phheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce sellers and developers can use the skill to run offline landed-cost and HS-code lookup scenarios for Amazon import routes before validating rates with official customs sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Static tariff, VAT/GST, Section 301, and HS-code estimates can be outdated or incomplete for real customs decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current rates and classifications with official customs or trade sources before making business or legal decisions. <br>
