## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on an external encrypted knowledge-base archive that is unpacked locally without path safety checks. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, use the knowledge-base package only from a trusted source, and prefer a package with published hashes or signatures. <br>
Risk: The assistant provides direct admissions and career advice that may be opinionated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as reference guidance and verify admissions, school, and policy details against official sources before acting. <br>
