## Description: <br>
Creates practical B2B sales collateral such as sales decks, one-pagers, objection handling docs, ROI calculator structures, demo scripts, sales playbooks, proposal templates, and buyer persona cards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mariokarras](https://clawhub.ai/user/mariokarras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, product marketing, and go-to-market teams use this skill to draft deal-stage-specific enablement assets that reps can use with prospects and internal champions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Workspace product-marketing context may contain secrets, credentials, or confidential claims that could be copied into sales collateral. <br>
Mitigation: Review local product-marketing context before use and remove secrets, credentials, and unapproved confidential claims. <br>
Risk: Generated collateral may include ROI figures, proof points, or competitive positioning that are inaccurate or not approved for customer-facing use. <br>
Mitigation: Have sales, product marketing, and legal or compliance reviewers validate claims, metrics, and positioning before sharing externally. <br>
