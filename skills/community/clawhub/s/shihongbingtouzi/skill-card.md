## Description: <br>
Provides a structured investment research framework for analyzing stocks, commodities, ETFs, and thematic ideas across macro conditions, scarcity, company quality, timing, scoring, and risk checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents can use this skill to structure medium- and long-horizon investment research on stocks, commodities, ETFs, themes, and regional allocations. It helps organize evidence, assumptions, scoring, risk reversals, and action labels, but outputs should be treated as research support rather than financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Investment analysis outputs may rely on incomplete, stale, or unverified market information. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market data, filings, and source material independently before relying on any conclusion. <br>
Risk: Action labels such as observe, light position, or medium position may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as research support only and apply human review, suitability checks, and qualified financial advice where appropriate. <br>
