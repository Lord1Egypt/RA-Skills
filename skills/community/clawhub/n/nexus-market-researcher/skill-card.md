## Description: <br>
Strategic market research agent. Conducts competitive analysis, market sizing, pricing intelligence, and identifies business opportunities across EdTech, corporate, healthcare, legal, and HR verticals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Strategy and business users use this skill to research markets, compare competitors, size opportunities, analyze pricing, and shape go-to-market recommendations across defined industry verticals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated market, competitive, or pricing recommendations could be inaccurate or misleading if sources are weak or context is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review source citations, confidence scores, and business assumptions before relying on the output for business decisions. <br>
Risk: Filesystem access may expose or modify local documents if enabled unnecessarily. <br>
Mitigation: Limit filesystem access unless local source documents or saved deliverables are specifically required. <br>
