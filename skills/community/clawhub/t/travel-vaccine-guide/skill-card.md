## Description: <br>
零配置即装即用，查询目的地疫苗和健康风险，支持查询目的地疫苗要求、列出常见旅行疫苗、推荐出行疫苗方案，基于公开API数据。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External travelers use this skill to check destination-specific vaccine requirements, browse common travel vaccines, and generate reference-only vaccination timing suggestions before a trip. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Static travel vaccine guidance may be outdated or incomplete for current entry rules and health risks. <br>
Mitigation: Verify vaccine requirements and health risks with official health agencies or a clinician before travel. <br>
Risk: Users may treat recommendation output as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present the output as reference-only and defer itinerary-specific medical decisions to qualified health professionals. <br>
