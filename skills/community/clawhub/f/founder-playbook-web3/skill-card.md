## Description: <br>
Decision validation and thinking frameworks for startup founders, covering fundraising, customer development, runway management, prioritization, and crypto/web3 challenges. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tenequm](https://clawhub.ai/user/tenequm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders and startup operators use this skill to pressure-test decisions, validate next steps, and structure coaching-style thinking around fundraising, customer development, runway, prioritization, and crypto/web3 strategy. It is a coaching aid and should not be treated as professional legal, financial, mental-health, regulatory, or investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Consequential startup and crypto guidance could be mistaken for professional legal, financial, mental-health, regulatory, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as coaching support, verify consequential claims independently, and consult qualified professionals before acting on high-impact decisions. <br>
Risk: Startup, fundraising, regulatory, and crypto market information can become stale. <br>
Mitigation: Check current sources before relying on time-sensitive claims or ecosystem statistics. <br>
Risk: Wallet secrets, private keys, credentials, or confidential business data could be exposed if shared during coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Do not share wallet secrets, private keys, credentials, or highly confidential business data with the agent. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the skill to influence general founder decisions beyond its best-supported coaching scope. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for founder decision validation and coaching-style reflection, and review outputs before turning them into operational commitments. <br>
