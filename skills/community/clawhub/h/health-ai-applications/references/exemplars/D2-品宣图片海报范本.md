## D2-05 批量生成参数配置

### 范本：膳食纤维批量生成配置

**YAML配置文件示例**：
```yaml
dimensions:
  wechat_cover: {width: 900, height: 383, dpi: 72, color_mode: RGB}
  friend_circle: {width: 1080, height: 1080, dpi: 72, color_mode: RGB}
  xiaohongshu: {width: 1080, height: 1440, dpi: 72, color_mode: RGB}
  douyin: {width: 1080, height: 1920, dpi: 72, color_mode: RGB}

formats:
  screen: {type: JPG, quality: 90, max_size: 5MB}
  print: {type: PDF, color_mode: CMYK, dpi: 300, bleed: 3mm}

naming:
  pattern: "{brand}_{platform}_{date}_{seq}"
  example: "纤维君_小红书_20260615_001"

schedule:
  frequency: weekly
  batch_size: 10
  delivery_format: zip
```
