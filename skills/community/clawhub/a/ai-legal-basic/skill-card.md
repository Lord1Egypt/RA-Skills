## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat general legal information as legal advice for consequential decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as informational and direct users to a licensed attorney for deadlines, disputes, filings, jurisdiction-specific rules, or high-impact decisions. <br>
Risk: Legal rules vary by jurisdiction and may change over time. <br>
Mitigation: State jurisdictional assumptions, avoid unsupported definitive claims, and recommend checking current local requirements or consulting counsel. <br>
