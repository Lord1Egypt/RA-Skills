## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and agents use this skill to produce Chinese digital marketing plans, content calendars, budget allocations, KPI frameworks, and platform-specific growth guidance. It is suited to planning campaigns across Xiaohongshu, Douyin, WeChat, Baidu, and major Chinese e-commerce channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The benchmark script calls an external third-party API. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending confidential campaign details or sensitive marketing copy to the service, and confirm the environment permits outbound network calls before running the script. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing benchmarks and strategy guidance may not match a specific brand's live performance or current platform conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current campaign analytics, platform dashboards, budget constraints, and applicable marketing review processes before execution. <br>
