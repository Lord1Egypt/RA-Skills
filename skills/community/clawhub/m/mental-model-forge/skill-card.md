## Description: <br>
F.A.C.E.T. is a cognitive framework for extracting reusable mental models from classic books and theories. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kedoupi](https://clawhub.ai/user/kedoupi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to turn books, theories, or research material into F.A.C.E.T. analyses with a core mechanism, anchor case, contradiction, boundary conditions, transfer guidance, and a strategic question. It can also produce a structured knowledge-base metadata block for later storage or retrieval. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: USER.md personalization can expose sensitive personal, project, or business context in generated analyses. <br>
Mitigation: Keep secrets out of USER.md and review generated content before sharing it. <br>
Risk: Generated knowledge-base metadata can encode incorrect categories, tags, scenarios, or related model links. <br>
Mitigation: Review the metadata block before saving it into a shared or long-term knowledge base. <br>
