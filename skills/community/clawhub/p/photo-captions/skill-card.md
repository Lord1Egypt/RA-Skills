## Description: <br>
Generates platform-tuned social media captions for photography across Instagram, Flickr, X/Twitter, Glass, Tumblr, Threads, Bluesky, 500px, Reddit, Facebook, VSCO, Pinterest, and Substack. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[pfrederiksen](https://clawhub.ai/user/pfrederiksen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External photographers and social media teams use this skill to turn a shared photo plus optional location, subject, camera, lens, film stock, or mood context into captions tailored for platform-specific posting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Captions may include location, gear, or personal context that the user does not want to publish. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated captions before posting and remove sensitive location, equipment, or personal details. <br>
Risk: Draft captions can misdescribe photo content or imply details that were not provided. <br>
Mitigation: Check each platform draft against the actual photo and supplied context before using it publicly. <br>
