## Description: <br>
Run a silent 21-day diagnostic to determine if your specific role is being phased out or automated in the next 6-18 months, then build a hidden financial and skill buffer while still fully employed and collecting every paycheck. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and individual contributors use this skill to assess possible role automation or phase-out risk and prepare a personal financial, skills, and reference buffer while still employed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may prompt sensitive financial or workplace actions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval and keep the agent from opening accounts, changing payroll, altering calendars, sending messages, or retaining employer emails automatically. <br>
Risk: The skill may collect confidential workplace or financial details. <br>
Mitigation: Use only on a personal device and store notes only in a location the user deliberately chooses as safe. <br>
Risk: Advice about potential job loss can be misleading or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance for user review, not legal, financial, or employment advice. <br>
