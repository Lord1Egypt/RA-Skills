# 跟进序列模板

## 一、LinkedIn连接序列（5步）

| 步骤 | 时间 | 动作 | 内容类型 |
|-----|------|------|---------|
| 1 | Day 0 | 连接请求 | 个性化邀请语（300字符内） |
| 2 | Day 3 | 首封私信 | 价值传递 |
| 3 | Day 10 | 跟进私信 | 案例/数据 |
| 4 | Day 17 | 再次跟进 | 资源分享 |
| 5 | Day 31 | 最终私信 | 保持开放 |

### Step 1: 连接请求
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [观察点]. I'm [你的身份] helping companies like yours [核心价值]. Would love to connect and share some insights.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Step 2: 首封私信（连接通过后）
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I help B2B companies in [行业] [具体成果].

I noticed [观察点]. Would you be open to a quick chat about [价值点]?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Step 3: 跟进私信（Day 10）
```
Hi [Name],

Just following up on my previous message.

We recently helped [类似公司] achieve [具体成果]. Here's the quick case study: [一句话总结].

Happy to share more if helpful!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Step 4: 再次跟进（Day 17）
```
Hi [Name],

I know you're busy — no pressure!

I've attached [资源：白皮书/案例/报告] that might be useful for [他们的挑战].

Feel free to reach out anytime.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Step 5: 最终私信（Day 31）
```
Hi [Name],

Last note from me — don't want to fill up your inbox!

If the timing ever works for a chat about [话题], I'd genuinely love to connect.

Otherwise, wishing you and [公司] a great [季节/时期]!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## 二、邮件跟进序列

### 序列1：冷启动（5封）
1. Day 0: 初始开发信
2. Day 3: 跟进（无回复？）
3. Day 7: 价值强调
4. Day 14: 优惠/限时
5. Day 28: 最终尝试

### 序列2：询盘回复（4封）
1. Day 0: 详细报价
2. Day 5: 确认收到
3. Day 14: 询问进展
4. Day 30: 报价到期提醒

### 序列3：展会跟进（3封）
1. Day 0: 感谢+资料
2. Day 7: 个性化报价
3. Day 21: 继续跟进
