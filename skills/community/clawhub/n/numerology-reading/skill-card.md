## Use Case: <br>
External users ask an assistant for a symbolic numerology snapshot based on a birth date, name, or repeating digits. The skill guides the assistant to show transparent calculations, preserve stated master-number rules, and frame themes as reflective entertainment rather than factual claims or advice. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat numerology themes as factual, scientific, or professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame readings as symbolic entertainment and self-reflection only, and include a clear disclaimer that outputs are not medical, legal, financial, or life advice. <br>
Risk: The skill may process personal inputs such as a name or birth date. <br>
Mitigation: Use only information the user voluntarily provides and avoid requesting unnecessary personal details. <br>
