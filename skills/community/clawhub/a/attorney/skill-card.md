## Description: <br>
A comprehensive AI agent skill for navigating legal situations that require or may require an attorney. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[agenticio](https://clawhub.ai/user/agenticio) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, small business owners, and clients use this skill to assess whether a legal situation may require an attorney, identify the right legal specialty, prepare for consultations, understand fee structures, and work more effectively with legal representation. It provides informational triage and preparation guidance, not jurisdiction-specific legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake informational legal triage for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as preparation and issue-spotting guidance, and direct users to consult a licensed attorney for jurisdiction-specific or high-stakes matters. <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary privileged or sensitive legal details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the facts needed to organize questions and deadlines, and remind users to avoid sharing unnecessary privileged details. <br>
Risk: Legal documents and deadlines may require prompt professional review. <br>
Mitigation: Encourage users to confirm document meaning, response deadlines, and next steps with a qualified attorney before acting. <br>
