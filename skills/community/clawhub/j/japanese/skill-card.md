## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to have an agent write or revise Japanese in a more casual, native-sounding style with natural particles, contractions, fillers, slang, and register choices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally favors casual, slang-heavy, and sometimes blunt wording that may be inappropriate in formal or professional settings. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated Japanese for audience, context, and register before using it in formal, professional, or sensitive communications. <br>
