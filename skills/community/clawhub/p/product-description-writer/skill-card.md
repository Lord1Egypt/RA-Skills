## Description: <br>
Generates SEO-optimized e-commerce product copy from product specs, rough notes, or existing listings, including titles, descriptions, benefit-led bullets, and meta descriptions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce sellers, marketers, and operators use this skill to turn product details into publish-ready product page copy. It supports SEO titles, 300-500 word descriptions, benefit-led bullets, meta descriptions, and optional hooks or mobile formatting notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated product copy can include unsupported certifications, health claims, performance claims, or competitor-derived wording if the input is inaccurate or unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated claims before publishing and verify certifications, regulated claims, performance statements, and competitor references against authoritative product documentation. <br>
Risk: Optional helper scripts read and write local files supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Run helper scripts only against files intended for the skill to read or create. <br>
