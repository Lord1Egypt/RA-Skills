## Description: <br>
Provides Davis-Bacon and prevailing wage compliance guidance for wage determinations, certified payroll, classification conformance, audit preparation, and related federal or state construction requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Contractors, subcontractors, payroll teams, and compliance reviewers use this skill to plan and check Davis-Bacon, Related Acts, IRA, IIJA, and state prevailing wage obligations for construction projects. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Compliance guidance may be outdated or incomplete for a specific project, jurisdiction, wage determination, or employee classification. <br>
Mitigation: Verify wage determinations, classifications, payroll requirements, and state rules against current official sources before relying on output. <br>
Risk: Certified payroll workflows can involve sensitive employee data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering real payroll records, SSNs, or employee data unless the agent environment is approved for that information. <br>
