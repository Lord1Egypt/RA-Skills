## Description: <br>
[Didoo AI] Conducts competitive intelligence for Meta Ads campaigns by analyzing competitor ads, landing pages, and market benchmarks before a new campaign or market entry. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and growth operators use this skill to research public Meta ads, competitor landing pages, funnel patterns, benchmarks, and creative opportunities before launching campaigns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent may browse public ad libraries, search the web, and fetch competitor landing pages during research. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm this browsing scope before use and keep the workflow limited to observation and analysis unless separate approval is given for interactive site actions. <br>
Risk: Marketing benchmarks, spend signals, and ad endurance can be incomplete or outdated when sourced from public pages. <br>
Mitigation: Report only observed data, label estimates clearly, and avoid making up unavailable metrics. <br>
Risk: Competitive analysis could drift into copying competitor ads or misusing branding. <br>
Mitigation: Use findings to adapt general principles, present observations objectively, and avoid direct copying or improper trademark use. <br>
