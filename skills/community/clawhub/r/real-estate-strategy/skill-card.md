## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Web searches may expose confidential parcel names, deal terms, or internal strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Approve search queries before use and avoid confidential project details unless disclosure is acceptable. <br>
Risk: The report script writes to a hard-coded local output directory and has limited filename controls. <br>
Mitigation: Change the output directory before running the script and use simple project names without path characters. <br>
Risk: Market, policy, pricing, and customer-demand data may be incomplete or time-sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important figures against user-provided materials or cited current sources before making investment or product decisions. <br>
