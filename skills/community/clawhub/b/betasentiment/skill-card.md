## Description: <br>
Runs local keyword-based sentiment analysis for social media, news headlines, and financial text. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1477009639zw-blip](https://clawhub.ai/user/1477009639zw-blip) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and external users can run this local command-line helper to quickly triage sentiment in short social, news, or financial text. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sentiment results are heuristic and keyword-based, so they may be misleading if treated as AI-grade analysis or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use results as an initial signal only and review important conclusions before acting on them. <br>
Risk: File input reads local content and prints a short preview of the analyzed text. <br>
Mitigation: Analyze only files whose contents are appropriate to display in terminal output. <br>
