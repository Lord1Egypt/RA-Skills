## Description: <br>
Plan and execute repeatable browser-based workflows such as opening sites, logging in, navigating dashboards, filling forms, exporting data, taking screenshots, and extracting page content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill to plan and run repeatable browser workflows on the local machine, especially fixed multi-step website tasks that may involve navigation, forms, exports, screenshots, or visible page extraction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Browser automation can touch logged-in accounts, exports, payment pages, account settings, MFA, or CAPTCHA prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the exact site and intended outcome before automation starts, and keep manual control of sensitive or account-changing steps. <br>
Risk: Website changes, popups, anti-bot protections, or complex UI interactions may make a scripted browser flow unreliable. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer read-only web tools for public pages, use existing local automation where available, and report where the workflow may break. <br>
