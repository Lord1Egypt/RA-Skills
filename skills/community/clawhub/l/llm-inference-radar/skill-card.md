## Description: <br>
Build and run a Chinese research intelligence radar for LLM inference acceleration, AI infra, model serving, kernels, reproducibility, open-source project signals, company infra updates, horizon transfer opportunities, feedback learning, and a persistent knowledge base. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and infrastructure engineers use this skill to create Chinese daily or weekly research briefs, paper radars, open-source watches, infra watches, transfer-matrix updates, and persistent knowledge-base updates for LLM inference acceleration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is expected to browse current public sources, which can include early or incomplete signals. <br>
Mitigation: Use primary sources where available, label media-only items as early signals, and avoid strong conclusions without benchmark or reproducibility context. <br>
Risk: When asked or when durable feedback is provided, the skill may maintain local research notes and preference records. <br>
Mitigation: Review knowledge-base and feedback updates before retaining or sharing them, especially in workflows where persistent local notes are not desired. <br>
