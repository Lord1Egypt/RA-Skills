## Description: <br>
Web3 Security Auditor helps agents review Solidity and Rust smart contracts for common Web3 vulnerabilities and produce severity-rated remediation guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ssidharhubble](https://clawhub.ai/user/ssidharhubble) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, DeFi teams, blockchain security reviewers, and investors use this skill to triage Ethereum, Solana, and BSC smart contracts and draft audit reports before deployment or investment decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI-generated audit output may miss vulnerabilities or produce false positives. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a preliminary review aid and require expert review and testing for high-value deployments or investments. <br>
Risk: Submitting confidential targets may expose sensitive contract details when external blockchain explorer lookups are acceptable for the workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit confidential targets unless use of Etherscan, Solscan, or other on-chain lookup sources is approved. <br>
