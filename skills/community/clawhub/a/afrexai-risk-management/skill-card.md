## Description: <br>
Helps organizations identify, assess, mitigate, and monitor operational, financial, strategic, compliance, cyber, reputational, people, and external risks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business leaders, risk teams, compliance teams, and advisors use this skill to create risk registers, appetite statements, KRI dashboards, scenario analyses, continuity plans, and board-ready risk reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad prompts about risk, crisis response, or risk culture may activate the skill unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for intended business-risk advisory work and avoid sharing sensitive enterprise details unless the agent is expected to process them. <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive enterprise risk, incident, financial, operational, or compliance context supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Limit inputs to information appropriate for the agent environment and remove confidential details when they are not needed for the risk-management task. <br>
