## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to roleplay a Li Xiang-style perspective when evaluating product definition, strategy, operational efficiency, and family-user market positioning. Treat outputs as simulated decision support, not official statements from Li Xiang or Li Auto. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the roleplay persona for official statements from Li Xiang or Li Auto. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as simulated decision support and verify important claims against authoritative sources. <br>
Risk: Generic business phrases may trigger the persona in contexts where roleplay is not intended. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit activation and exit phrases, and review the active context before relying on responses. <br>
