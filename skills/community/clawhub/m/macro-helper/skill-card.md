## Description: <br>
Excel macro and VBA helper that helps agents generate VBA code, explain macros, debug errors, provide templates, convert formats, and optimize performance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and spreadsheet automation users can use this skill to draft, explain, debug, convert, and optimize Excel VBA macros. It is most useful when an agent needs structured macro guidance plus reusable VBA patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The package includes an unrelated utility that can store command history and user-provided data under a local macro-helper data directory. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid passing sensitive workbook names, business data, credentials, paths, or private notes to utility commands unless local storage is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated or suggested VBA may be incorrect or unsuitable for important workbooks. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated VBA and test it on workbook copies before running it on important files. <br>
