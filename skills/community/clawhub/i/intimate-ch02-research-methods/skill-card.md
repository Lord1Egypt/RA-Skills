## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language tutoring skill that helps learners study Chapter 2, Research Methods, of Rowland S. Miller's Intimate Relationships by using progressive disclosure and chapter-specific reference notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Learners, educators, and study assistants use this skill to answer questions, compare concepts, review examples, and interpret ethics or evidence claims for Chapter 2 research-methods topics in intimate-relationships coursework. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may provide inaccurate textbook details or extend beyond the documented Chapter 2 research-methods scope. <br>
Mitigation: Review answers against the bundled references and the original textbook when precision matters, and keep responses within the stated Chapter 2 boundary. <br>
Risk: Research-methods explanations can be overinterpreted as causal or broadly generalizable claims. <br>
Mitigation: State the relevant design, sampling, measurement, and ethics limits, and distinguish correlation from causation. <br>
Risk: Users may rely on generated study guidance without checking whether it matches their course edition or instructor expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Encourage comparison with assigned course materials and use the skill as a study aid rather than an authoritative replacement for the source text. <br>
