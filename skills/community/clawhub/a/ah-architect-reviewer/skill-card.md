## Description: <br>
Expert architecture reviewer specializing in system design validation, architectural patterns, and technical decision assessment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mtsatryan](https://clawhub.ai/user/mtsatryan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and architecture reviewers use this skill to evaluate system designs, diagrams, technology choices, scalability, maintainability, security architecture, integration patterns, and technical debt, then produce pragmatic improvement recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Architecture documents, diagrams, and system context can contain confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the skill access only to projects and materials approved for architecture review. <br>
Risk: Architecture recommendations may be incomplete or misleading if the provided system context is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with project owners and validate them against current requirements, constraints, and security expectations before acting. <br>
