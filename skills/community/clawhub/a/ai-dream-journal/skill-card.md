Mitigation: Treat journal content as sensitive and avoid sharing details that are not needed for the task. <br>
Risk: Dream interpretation or sleep guidance could be mistaken for medical or mental-health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as general journaling and educational support, and consult qualified professionals for sleep disorders, distressing symptoms, or mental-health concerns. <br>
