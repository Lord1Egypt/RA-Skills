## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide public campaign activity or posting workflows without explicit approval boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Require manual approval for public posts, account access, campaign launch steps, and any distribution plan before execution. <br>
Risk: The skill discusses archiving youth and social-media discussion data, which may create privacy and retention concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Use aggregated public metrics where possible, avoid identifiable data about minors or social-media users, and define retention and deletion rules before collecting data. <br>
Risk: Generated campaign language may not match the user's preferred language or audience expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Review and localize outputs before publication, including tone, terminology, and bilingual content choices. <br>
