## Description: <br>
Import, search, and archive AI conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Clean indexed formats, full-text search, static HTML archive. No server required. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TheShadowRose](https://clawhub.ai/user/TheShadowRose) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and individual users use Chat Lift to import exported ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini conversations, search them locally, and generate portable Markdown, HTML, and JSON archives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated archives may contain sensitive private AI conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Store archives in a private local directory and avoid cloud-synced, public, or web-served folders unless sharing is intentional. <br>
Risk: Published or shared Markdown, HTML, and JSON archives may expose confidential chat content. <br>
Mitigation: Review and redact sensitive conversations before importing, publishing, or sharing generated archive files. <br>
