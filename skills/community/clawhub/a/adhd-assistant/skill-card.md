## Description: <br>
ADHD Assistant helps users plan days, break down tasks, manage time, prioritize work, use body doubling, and maintain routines with ADHD-friendly executive-function support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thinktankmachine](https://clawhub.ai/user/thinktankmachine) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to provide ADHD-friendly planning, task breakdown, time management, accountability, and emotional self-compassion support during productivity workflows. It is not intended to diagnose conditions, provide medical advice, prescribe medications, or replace professional therapy or coaching. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to handle and potentially remember sensitive mental-health, treatment, diagnosis, or emotional-sensitivity details. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit opt-in before storing sensitive details, and provide a clear way for users to inspect, edit, and delete remembered information. <br>
Risk: Users may treat ADHD support as medical, psychiatric, diagnostic, or therapeutic advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses framed as productivity and executive-function support, and direct users to qualified professionals for diagnosis, medication, therapy, crisis support, or persistent distress. <br>
Risk: ADHD-specific framing may not fit every user's needs or comfort level. <br>
Mitigation: Review the skill before installing and use it only when users are comfortable with ADHD-specific language and support patterns. <br>
