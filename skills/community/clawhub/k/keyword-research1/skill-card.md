## Description: <br>
Multi-source keyword intelligence and autocomplete research that fetches real-time suggestions from Google, YouTube, Amazon, DuckDuckGo, and Bing without requiring an API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[brasco05](https://clawhub.ai/user/brasco05) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
SEO professionals, content marketers, agency teams, and developers use this skill to expand seed terms into autocomplete keyword lists for search, content, advertising, and niche research workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Seed keywords are sent to third-party autocomplete services, including Bing by default. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential client names, unpublished products, and sensitive campaign terms; use explicit --sources, --lang, and --region options when tighter control is needed. <br>
