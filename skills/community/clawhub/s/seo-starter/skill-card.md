## Description: <br>
Free SEO toolkit for keyword research, search intent matching, content structure, and E-E-A-T signals, with an optional upgrade path for technical audits and automated analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, founders, and content teams use this skill to plan SEO work, match content to search intent, structure pages for ranking signals, and run manual keyword or competitor gap analysis without paid SEO tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
