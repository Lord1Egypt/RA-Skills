## Description: <br>
Design and evaluate A/B tests for different price points, discount levels, and bundle combinations to find the highest-converting offer structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators, growth teams, and analysts use this skill to plan pricing, discount, bundle, and shipping-threshold A/B tests and decide winners based on profit per visitor with guardrails. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pricing recommendations can be misleading if sample size, stopping rules, or guardrail metrics are ignored. <br>
Mitigation: Pre-register the primary metric, required sample size, duration, and stopping rule; verify assumptions and calculations before changing live prices. <br>
Risk: Discounts, reference prices, and inconsistent price presentation can create legal, platform-policy, or customer-trust issues. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm offers and claimed discounts comply with local laws and platform policies, and keep assigned prices consistent across product, cart, and checkout pages. <br>
Risk: The skill provides advisory business guidance, not an automated control or statistical guarantee. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as reviewable plans and have the responsible operator validate margin inputs, refund and chargeback guardrails, and rollout decisions. <br>
