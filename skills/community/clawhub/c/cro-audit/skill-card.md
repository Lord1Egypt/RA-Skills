## Description: <br>
Suggests CRO experiments and A/B test ideas for startups. Use when someone wants help with conversion rate optimization, A/B testing, or improving website conversions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Startup founders, growth teams, and marketers use this skill to run a guided CRO discovery process, identify funnel leaks, and receive prioritized A/B test ideas, quick wins, testing guidance, and an action plan for improving signups, activations, and conversions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for conversion rates, funnel data, analytics setup, and strategy details that may be commercially sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Share approximate or non-confidential inputs unless the agent environment is approved for that business information. <br>
Risk: CRO and A/B testing recommendations can be misleading if based on incomplete funnel data or too little experiment traffic. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current analytics, sample size, and guardrail metrics before making product or pricing changes. <br>
