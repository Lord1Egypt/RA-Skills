## Description: <br>
学术文献逻辑梳理专家，专注挖掘论文内部章节间的递进关系与论证脉络，输出逻辑链而非结构清单。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[11xinshou](https://clawhub.ai/user/11xinshou) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and knowledge workers use this skill to turn academic papers into concise logic maps that show argument flow, section transitions, innovation points, core findings, and chapter-level takeaways. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Paper text may contain confidential or unpublished material. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide papers or excerpts that the user is comfortable having the agent process. <br>
Risk: Condensed logic summaries can omit nuance or misstate a paper's argument structure. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated logic map against the source paper before using it for citation, publication, or high-stakes decisions. <br>
