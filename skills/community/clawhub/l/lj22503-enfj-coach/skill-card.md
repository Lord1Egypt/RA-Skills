## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language ENFJ coaching guidance for growth, career, and learning situations using coaching questions, consultant-style action plans, and ENFJ-oriented strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users who identify as ENFJ use this skill for growth, career, and learning coaching, especially when they feel stuck in analysis paralysis, unclear direction, or scattered attention. The skill alternates between reflective coaching questions and direct action-oriented advice based on the user's urgency. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Mixed ENFJ and INTP materials may produce inconsistent or misleading coaching guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review and correct the personality-type-specific materials before deployment, and prefer a version with consistent ENFJ content. <br>
Risk: The artifact describes saving personal coaching notes without clear user review or deletion controls. <br>
Mitigation: Disable or tightly control memory use, and instruct the agent not to save personal reflections unless the user explicitly approves. <br>
Risk: Users may treat personality coaching as professional mental health or career advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the artifact's boundary that it does not replace professional counseling and route serious mental health concerns to qualified professionals. <br>
