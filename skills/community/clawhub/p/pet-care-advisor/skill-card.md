## Description: <br>
Pet Care Advisor provides lifecycle pet health and care guidance for cats and dogs, including breed identification, nutrition planning, behavior analysis, early symptom screening, vaccination and deworming reminders, and first-aid guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External pet owners and caretakers use this skill for non-diagnostic pet-care guidance, including routine care planning, nutrition support, behavior training ideas, health tracking, and escalation guidance for urgent symptoms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat pet-care guidance as a veterinary diagnosis or emergency plan. <br>
Mitigation: State that outputs are non-diagnostic and direct users to contact a veterinarian or emergency animal hospital for poisoning, seizures, breathing trouble, bleeding, medication dosing, or other urgent cases. <br>
Risk: Breed, nutrition, behavior, or symptom guidance may be incomplete or inaccurate for a specific animal. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for relevant pet details, present guidance as preliminary, and recommend veterinarian review for persistent symptoms, special diets, chronic conditions, or medication decisions. <br>
