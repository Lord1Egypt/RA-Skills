This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, cloud engineers, and FinOps teams use this skill to review AWS environments for cost-saving opportunities and produce actionable optimization recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cost analysis may require sensitive AWS billing, inventory, or account information. <br>
Mitigation: Use read-only billing and inventory exports, and avoid sharing access keys, secrets, or unnecessary account details. <br>
Risk: Optimization recommendations can be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific workload if based on limited inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with workload owners before changing reservations, savings plans, capacity, or resource configuration. <br>
