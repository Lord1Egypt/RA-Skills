# Page-by-Page Guide — What to Check at Each Funnel Stage

## Homepage (first impression)

- **5-second test:** what they sell, for whom, why here — answerable above the fold.
- **Headline:** benefit-led, ≤8 words. "Welcome to [brand]" is a zero-information headline.
- **Hero:** product in use, optimized weight (image <300KB, no multi-MB autoplay video on mobile).
- **CTA:** one primary action above the fold ("Shop bestsellers" beats three competing buttons).
- **Announcement bar:** concrete (free-shipping threshold, delivery cutoff) beats slogans.
- **Navigation:** ≤6 top-level items; bestsellers/collections reachable in one tap.
- **Social proof teaser:** review count, press logos, or UGC strip within first two scrolls.

## Collection pages

- **Card sufficiency:** price, review stars, variant cues (color swatches) on the card — forcing a PDP visit to learn the price loses browsers.
- **Filters:** required for >12 products; must include price range and the category's decisive attribute (size, material, room).
- **Sort:** bestselling default; "price low-high" available.
- **Image consistency:** same aspect ratio, background style, and crop across cards.
- **Dead ends:** empty filter combinations show alternatives, not a blank grid.
- **Sale display:** compare-at price visible on cards, not only on PDPs.

## Product detail page (PDP)

- **Title/price block:** price adjacent to title; compare-at framing honest; unit price for consumables.
- **Image set:** ≥5 — hero, lifestyle, scale reference, detail close-up, what's-in-the-box; pinch-zoom works on mobile.
- **Description:** benefits before story; scannable bullets; specs in accordion; objection handlers (sizing, materials, compatibility, care).
- **Social proof:** stars + count visible without scrolling (mobile included); reviews with photos surfaced first; worst recent review answered.
- **Shipping/returns:** delivery estimate and return window stated near ATC, not only in footer.
- **Variant picker:** unavailable combinations disabled with restock notify; size guide linked at the picker.
- **ATC:** sticky on mobile; one-tap express option (Shop Pay) shown.
- **Urgency:** only true scarcity ("3 left" from live inventory); no resetting timers.
- **Cross-sell:** below primary info; never between description and ATC.

## Cart

- **Access:** slide-out cart preserves context; full-page cart acceptable for high-AOV consideration purchases.
- **Cost preview:** shipping estimate or free-shipping progress meter IN the cart — the #1 abandonment cause is cost surprise at checkout.
- **Editability:** quantity and variant changes without page reload errors.
- **Add-ons:** one relevant low-price add-on max; cluttered carts depress completion.
- **CTA:** "Checkout" primary; express payment buttons present.

## Checkout (Shopify-locked, but check the inputs)

- **Guest checkout:** enabled (Settings > Customer accounts).
- **Express payments:** Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay toggled on (Settings > Payments).
- **Discount strategy:** automatic discounts during sales so the code field doesn't send buyers off-site.
- **Shipping options:** at least one fast and one free/cheap option; delivery dates not just "5–10 days".
- **Trust:** return policy and support contact linked in checkout footer.
- **Post-purchase:** order confirmation upsell apps audited — one offer max.

## Cross-page

- **Consistency:** shipping promises identical on announcement bar, PDP, and cart.
- **404/search:** site search returns results for misspellings; 404 page links to bestsellers.
- **Policies:** shipping/returns reachable in ≤2 taps from any page.
