# Commission Math Guide — Making Earnings Concrete

The earning sentence is the highest-leverage line in any outreach message. This guide shows how to build it honestly.

## Core formulas

```
Per-sale earnings      = AOV × commission rate
Projected weekly sales = views per video × conversion rate
Weekly earning range   = per-sale earnings × projected sales (low / high scenario)
```

## Realistic conversion assumptions by follower band (TikTok Shop, physical products)

| Follower band | Typical views/video | Conversion (views→orders) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 5K–20K | 1K–10K | 0.3%–1.0% | High variance; niche micro-creators can outperform |
| 20K–100K | 5K–50K | 0.4%–1.2% | Sweet spot for product-fit content |
| 100K–500K | 20K–200K | 0.2%–0.8% | Broader audiences dilute conversion |
| 500K+ | 50K–1M+ | 0.1%–0.5% | Reach play; negotiate flat fee + commission hybrid |

Use the LOW end in outreach messages. An honest "$150–$400 per landing video" outperforms an inflated "$2,000/week potential" — creators discount hype automatically and platforms penalize guaranteed-earnings claims.

## Worked example

Product: $34 chopper, 18% commission → $6.12/sale.
Creator: 60K followers, averages 15K views/video.
Low scenario: 15K × 0.4% = 60 sales → $367.
Conservative pitch line: "creators your size are earning $150–$400 per video that lands."

## Tier design (for the commission structure itself)

- **Flat rate:** simplest to pitch; use when AOV < $50 or program is new.
- **Two-tier:** base + performance tier (e.g., 12% base, 18% above 30 sales/month). Pitch the top tier in outreach to ambitious creators, but show base math too.
- **Reactivation tier:** time-limited boost (60–90 days) for lapsed affiliates — always pair with a *new* product or bundle so the message isn't a repeat pitch.
- **Hybrid (flat fee + commission):** for 500K+ creators only; commission-only pitches get ignored at that tier.

## AOV levers that change the math

- Bundles raise AOV and per-sale earnings — "promote the $68 bundle, not the $42 single" can double the earning sentence.
- Free-shipping thresholds just above AOV push order values up; mention the bundle price in outreach when it's the better math.

## Red lines

1. Never project earnings that require above-band conversion rates.
2. Never say "guaranteed," "passive income," or "easy money" — platform-policy and FTC exposure.
3. Every number in the message must trace to: stated AOV, stated commission, and a conversion assumption you could defend to the creator on a call.
4. If the honest math is weak (<$3/sale), fix the offer (bundle, tier, sample) before writing outreach — copy cannot rescue bad unit economics.
