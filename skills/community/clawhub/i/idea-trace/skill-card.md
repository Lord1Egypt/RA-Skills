## Description: <br>
Don't just save ideas - trace how they mature. Use when a user wants to follow how an important idea was shaped by later conversations, work, projects, and lived experience; decide whether it deserves long-term tracking; classify what changed it; and explain how it became a more mature view. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to trace how important ideas mature across later conversations, work, projects, and lived experience. It helps qualify whether an idea is worth long-term tracking, classify shaping contributions, and produce a concise evolution artifact. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can overstate weak or speculative links between later events and an earlier idea. <br>
Mitigation: Mark confirmed, partial, weak, and speculative connections explicitly, and avoid presenting weak links as established. <br>
Risk: The skill can force a trace for a fleeting task idea or unsupported fragment. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the qualification test before substantive work and clearly decline proposition tracing when the idea does not qualify. <br>
