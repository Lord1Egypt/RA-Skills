## Description: <br>
Mortgage process guidance with affordability calculations and application tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[agenticio](https://clawhub.ai/user/agenticio) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Home buyers and mortgage applicants use this skill to estimate affordability, understand mortgage terms, prepare application document checklists, compare offer details, and track approval milestones. It is educational and organizational support, not mortgage advice or a substitute for licensed professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter mortgage-related financial details into local workspace memory. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only where local workspace storage is acceptable, and delete retained mortgage data when it is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Affordability estimates or comparisons could be mistaken for mortgage advice or lender recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational support and verify loan decisions, lender terms, rates, and eligibility with licensed mortgage or financial professionals. <br>
Risk: Some helper scripts referenced by the skill are not included in this package. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm a referenced script exists before relying on that workflow, and use included functionality only for supported calculations. <br>
