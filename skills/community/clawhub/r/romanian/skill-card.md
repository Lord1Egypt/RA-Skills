## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to produce Romanian text that reads casually and naturally, with guidance on register, address forms, fillers, expressions, reactions, and regional consistency. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may encourage Romanian wording that is too casual for formal, elderly, or professional audiences. <br>
Mitigation: Review the intended audience and switch to a more formal register when the user requests it or the context requires it. <br>
Risk: AI-assisted style changes may be inappropriate where disclosure is required by a platform, institution, or workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose AI assistance where required by the applicable context or platform rules. <br>
