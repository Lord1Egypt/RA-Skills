This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People seeking reflective or entertainment-style tarot use this skill to draw one or three Major Arcana cards and receive gentle, non-predictive prompts for emotional reflection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat reflective tarot output as professional medical, legal, financial, or mental-health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present readings as symbolic reflection only, avoid directives or diagnosis, and stop the tarot flow in favor of safety-focused human support if self-harm intent appears. <br>
