## Description: <br>
Rewrites technical, jargon-heavy marketing drafts into concise, polished, consumer-friendly commercial copy with two style variants and CTA guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[brucey0017-cloud](https://clawhub.ai/user/brucey0017-cloud) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External product, marketing, and documentation teams use this skill to turn technical feature copy into clear, restrained, consumer-facing website copy, product descriptions, and CTA text. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewritten copy may make polished product or security claims that go beyond the underlying product evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Manually verify factual, product, pricing, and security claims before publishing the rewritten copy. <br>
Risk: The built-in tone and blacklist rules may not match a specific brand voice or compliance style. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated wording against the organization's brand, legal, and localization guidelines before release. <br>
