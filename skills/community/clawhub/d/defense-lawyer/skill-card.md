## Description: <br>
AI-powered criminal defense assistant for Chinese legal system - provides case analysis, defense strategy formulation, evidence assessment, and legal document generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ZhenStaff](https://clawhub.ai/user/ZhenStaff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External legal professionals use this skill to draft and review criminal case analysis, defense strategy, evidence assessments, sentencing analysis, legal research, and defense documents for matters under the Chinese legal system. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles sensitive criminal-defense scenarios and may process personal or case-confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering unnecessary personal data, store generated case documents securely, and do not place them in public repositories or automatically synced folders. <br>
Risk: Generated analysis, strategy, citations, or legal documents may be incomplete, incorrect, or unsuitable as formal legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as drafting and analysis support for qualified legal professionals; licensed attorneys should review all outputs and make final legal decisions. <br>
Risk: Installation depends on a referenced Python package outside the reviewed markdown artifacts. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if the publisher and referenced package are trusted in the deployment environment. <br>
