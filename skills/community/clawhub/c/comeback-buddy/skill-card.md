## Description: <br>
Comeback Buddy helps users analyze tense conversations and draft Chinese comeback responses in multiple tones, from calm boundary-setting to high-intensity replies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wxhzzsf](https://clawhub.ai/user/wxhzzsf) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to analyze chat screenshots, conversation transcripts, or described situations where they felt dismissed, bullied, or unable to respond. It produces situation analysis, ready-to-use Chinese comeback options across several tones, and reusable response techniques. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Aggressive or nuclear comeback drafts may escalate workplace, family, relationship, or customer conflicts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's desired intensity, prefer boundary-setting options first, and warn about consequences before providing high-intensity replies. <br>
Risk: One-sided or incomplete conversation context can lead to unfair or misleading analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Assess both sides of the described exchange and state when the user's position appears weak or when more context is needed. <br>
Risk: Screenshots and transcripts may contain sensitive personal or workplace information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only the details needed to analyze the exchange and avoid exposing unnecessary private information in generated replies. <br>
