## Description: <br>
Generates comprehensive partnership agreements, joint venture frameworks, and strategic alliance documents for B2B relationships. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, agencies, legal operations teams, and consultants use this skill to draft first-pass partnership, joint venture, strategic alliance, and revenue-share agreement frameworks. It helps gather business terms, produce clause language, and flag common agreement risks before professional review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated agreement text may omit or misstate jurisdiction-specific legal, tax, liability, or governing-law requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the jurisdiction, parties, governing law, business terms, tax position, and liability structure with qualified counsel before signing or relying on the agreement. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the output for legal advice rather than drafting assistance. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for first-pass drafting and review every generated agreement with a qualified legal professional. <br>
