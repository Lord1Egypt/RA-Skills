## Description: <br>
Clause-by-clause BAA analysis against 45 CFR 164.504(e)(2). Evaluates all 9 required HIPAA provisions with risk scoring and remediation recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dangsllc](https://clawhub.ai/user/dangsllc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Compliance, legal, privacy, and vendor-management reviewers use this skill to assess Business Associate Agreements against HIPAA BAA requirements and receive a structured JSON report with provision-level findings, risk ratings, and remediation recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: BAA review may involve sensitive protected health information, confidential pricing, account details, or other regulated business information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact unnecessary sensitive details and use the skill only in an environment approved for regulated or confidential documents. <br>
Risk: The skill provides compliance analysis and remediation suggestions that may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific legal context. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified legal or compliance personnel review the output before relying on it for HIPAA compliance decisions or contract changes. <br>
