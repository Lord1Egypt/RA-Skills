## Description: <br>
Automatically sends browser screenshots to users through the message tool so visual results reach the conversation channel. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shenhuanjie](https://clawhub.ai/user/shenhuanjie) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and automation agents use this skill after browser screenshot operations to send the resulting image to the user. It is intended for web automation, page verification, debugging, and visual testing workflows that require user-visible screenshots. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Screenshots may expose passwords, private pages, customer data, or confidential work. <br>
Mitigation: Review screenshots before sending them and avoid saving or sharing images in channels where that content does not belong. <br>
