## Use Case: <br>
External business, growth, finance, and executive teams use this skill to convert enterprise targets, market signals, capital constraints, and governance rules into channel allocation plans, scenario forecasts, risk thresholds, and decision memos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Capital allocation guidance could be misleading if input data, market signals, or assumptions are incomplete or low quality. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm data quality, assumptions, governance thresholds, and downside scenarios with finance, marketing, and executive stakeholders before acting. <br>
Risk: Forecasts or scenarios may be mistaken for deterministic outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Present confidence bands, base/upside/downside scenarios, and explicit executive review gates for high-stakes or low-confidence decisions. <br>
