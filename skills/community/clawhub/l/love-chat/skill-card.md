## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may pressure, manipulate, or disrespect another person's boundaries if used carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in respectful, consensual situations and review outputs for tone, consent, and boundary-respecting language before sending. <br>
Risk: Chat logs or relationship details may contain personal or intimate information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact names, contact details, and intimate details before providing conversation history to an agent. <br>
Risk: Relationship advice can be context-sensitive and may be incorrect or inappropriate for the situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated advice as suggestions, not authoritative guidance, and avoid using it for harassment, coercion, or illegal content. <br>
