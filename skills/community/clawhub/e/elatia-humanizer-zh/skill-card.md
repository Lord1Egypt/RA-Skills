## Description: <br>
去除文本中的 AI 生成痕迹，让中文文本更自然、更有人味。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ken0122](https://clawhub.ai/user/ken0122) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and writers use this skill to revise Chinese emails, copy, articles, reports, and other AI-generated drafts so they read more naturally while preserving the intended meaning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may make text less appropriate for formal, legal, academic, policy-governed, or technical contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review edits before use and preserve formal wording where domain requirements or user instructions call for it. <br>
Risk: The skill can edit files when asked, so unintended wording changes may be written to user content. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect file edits or diffs before relying on the revised text. <br>
Risk: Creative-writing enhancement may depend on a separate narrative-voice skill if present in the user's environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review that referenced skill separately before enabling the integration. <br>
