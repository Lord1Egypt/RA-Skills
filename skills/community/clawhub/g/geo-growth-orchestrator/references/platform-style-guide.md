# Platform Style Guide

Use this reference when creating platform-specific tasks, drafts, titles, or publishing checklists.

## Universal Rules

- Human review is mandatory.
- Do not auto-publish.
- Do not click publish buttons.
- Do not read, save, export, or print cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, tokens, or passwords.
- Do not bypass captchas or platform risk controls.
- Do not promise ranking, traffic, indexing, conversion, or model citation.
- Do not invent cases, prices, certifications, awards, reviews, or third-party endorsements.

## Zhihu

Best for:

- decision questions
- industry explanations
- comparison and methodology
- trust-building long answers

Style:

- answer the question first
- analytical and balanced
- low ad feel
- include counterpoints and boundaries
- brand mention should be natural and removable

Outputs:

- question candidates
- long answer
- short answer
- no-ad version
- article version
- titles
- topics/tags
- publish checklist

## Toutiao

Best for:

- local services
- consumer education
- boss-readable business content
- everyday scenarios

Style:

- plain language
- strong real-world scenario
- avoid technical jargon
- no anxiety marketing
- explain what to do next

Outputs:

- article
- title candidates
- summary
- cover prompt ideas
- micro-posts
- keywords/topics
- publish checklist

## CSDN

Best for:

- technical tutorials
- architecture explanations
- implementation guides
- troubleshooting
- developer documentation

Style:

- clear background/problem
- implementation steps
- input/output examples
- pseudo-code or config examples when useful
- explicit boundaries and FAQ

Outputs:

- article
- markdown-ready version
- titles
- summary
- tags
- code/config examples
- publish checklist

## Juejin

Best for:

- developer practice
- engineering review
- prompt/workflow implementation
- lessons learned

Style:

- developer perspective
- practical examples
- honest tradeoffs
- code/prompt/process snippets when useful
- no exaggerated claims

Outputs:

- article
- markdown-ready version
- titles
- summary
- tags
- publish checklist

## Xiaohongshu

Best for:

- consumer experience
- local service trust
- visual education
- before/after and checklist-style content

Style:

- concrete scene
- short paragraphs
- visual hooks
- practical tips
- no fake testimonials

Output a manual content brief unless a dedicated safe drafting workflow exists.

## Douyin

Best for:

- short explainer
- service walkthrough
- comparison in 30-60 seconds
- local trust-building

Style:

- first 3 seconds state the problem
- one message per video
- simple structure: problem, decision point, evidence, next step
- no guaranteed outcome claims

Output script outline, shot list, on-screen text, and compliance notes. Do not automate upload.
