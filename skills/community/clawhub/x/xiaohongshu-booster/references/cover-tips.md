# Xiaohongshu Cover Image Tips

> A great cover can double click-through rate on Xiaohongshu.
> First impressions happen in the 2:3 feed thumbnail.

---

## Rule of Thumb

1. **Resolution**: 1080×1440px (3:4 ratio) — vertical, fills the feed
2. **Text overlay**: 5-8 Chinese characters max, bold, high contrast
3. **Color**: Warm tones (yellow-orange-red) outperform cool tones 2:1 on XHS
4. **Face**: Posts with visible human faces get +30% CTR
5. **Before/After**: Side-by-side transformation images are the highest-CTR format

---

## Cover Templates by Category

### Beauty / Skincare
- **Close-up**: Hand holding product, natural light, clean background
- **Flat lay**: Product + flowers/mirror on marble surface
- **Before/after**: Split face or side-by-side progress
- **Text**: "熬夜脸有救了" / "回购第3瓶" / "敏感肌亲测"

### Fashion / Outfits
- **Full-body mirror**: Natural standing pose, clean background
- **Detail shot**: Fabric texture or accessory closeup
- **Outfit grid**: 3-4 looks in a collage
- **Text**: "梨形身材必买" / "通勤一周穿搭" / "这一条赢了"

### Food / Restaurant
- **Close-up hero**: Food on grill/plate, steam visible, warm lighting
- **Ambiance**: Restaurant interior, candlelight, cozy vibe
- **Menu shot**: User holding menu or dish
- **Text**: "人均200吃和牛" / "排队也要吃" / "上海必吃榜"

### Travel
- **Scenic wide**: Landscape with human silhouette in foreground
- **Selfie destination**: Smiling pose at landmark
- **Detail**: Local food / accommodation / sign
- **Text**: "周末逃离南京" / "国内小众海岛" / "这个民宿绝了"

### Lifestyle / Home
- **Clean aesthetic**: Room corner, organized desk, candle
- **Product focus**: Single hero item on clean surface
- **Before/after**: Makeover transformation
- **Text**: "租房改造" / "桌面布置灵感" / "用了就回不去"

## Text Overlay Guidelines

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Font | Bold sans-serif (PingFang SC, Noto Sans SC) |
| Color | White with drop shadow, or bright yellow #FFD700 |
| Position | Lower-third or center, avoid top (timestamp area) |
| Max length | 8 Chinese chars (2 lines × 4 chars) |
| Emoji on cover | Use sparingly — 1 emoji max, or none |
| Avoid | Tiny text, low-contrast colors, cluttered background |

---

## Photo Sources & Tips

- **Self-shot**: Use portrait mode, f/2.8 aperture for bokeh
- **Flat lay**: Overhead shot at 90°, even lighting
- **Mirror shot**: Clean mirror, phone covers face or is held at chin level
- **AI generation**: OK for product mockups if disclosed
- **Reposting**: Always credit original creator; avoid screenshots with watermarks

## A/B Testing Your Covers

1. Create 3 cover variants per note
2. Post at different times (morning / lunch / evening)
3. After 2 hours, compare:
   - **Tap-through rate** (CTR) = Saves / Impressions
   - The variant with highest CTR in the first 2 hours usually wins long-term
4. Archive low-performing variants and repost the winner after 7 days
