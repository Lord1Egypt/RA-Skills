## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: ESG inputs may include business-sensitive operational, emissions, workforce, and supply-chain data. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only data suitable for the agent environment and avoid sharing confidential source records unless the environment is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Generated ESG report content may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for formal disclosure without review. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified compliance, sustainability, or audit professionals review the report before publication. <br>
