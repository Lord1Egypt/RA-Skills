External users, especially university students and early-career job seekers, use this skill to refine resumes, practice interviews, track applications, and generate structured career research in Chinese. <br>

Risk: Users may share unnecessary personal identifiers, private employer information, or sensitive contact details while using resume and interview workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for information needed for the specific career task and avoid retaining or exposing sensitive details in generated outputs. <br>
