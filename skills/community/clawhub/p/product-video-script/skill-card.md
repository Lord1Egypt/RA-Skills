## Description: <br>
Write engaging product video scripts for short-form platforms like TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, ecommerce marketers, UGC partners, and in-house editors use this skill to turn product briefs into platform-optimized, shot-by-shot short-form video scripts with hooks, visuals, captions, hashtags, and a single CTA. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing scripts may include product claims, scarcity language, testimonials, ratings, health statements, earnings statements, or platform-specific ad content that needs substantiation before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated ads before publishing and verify claims, urgency, testimonials, ratings, health or earnings statements, and platform ad-policy compliance against the product's evidence and campaign requirements. <br>
