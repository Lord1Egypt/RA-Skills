## Description: <br>
Parallax views decisions from user, maintainer, operator, adversary, and sponsor perspectives to reveal blind spots in single-perspective reasoning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jcools1977](https://clawhub.ai/user/jcools1977) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and product decision makers use Parallax to review proposed changes, features, and architecture decisions across five stakeholder lenses before committing to a tradeoff. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can influence how an agent frames decisions, so incomplete context may lead to misleading tradeoff recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated analysis against project-specific requirements and have accountable stakeholders confirm major tradeoffs before implementation. <br>
