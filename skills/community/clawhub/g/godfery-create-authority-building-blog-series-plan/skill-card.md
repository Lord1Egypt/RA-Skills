## Description: <br>
Plan a multi-part content series to establish expertise. Use when working on thought leadership for thought leaders, coaches, personal brand builders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Thought leaders, coaches, and personal brand builders use this skill to plan a multi-part blog series that establishes expertise, clarifies the target audience and goals, and prepares content for review before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Risk: Generated content plans may include inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable publishing guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated drafts before publication and check facts, SEO claims, and audience assumptions. <br>
Risk: User-provided private client or business information may be incorporated into the content plan. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing private client or business information unless it is intended for use in the generated plan. <br>
