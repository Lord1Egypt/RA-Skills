## Description: <br>
Helps non-lawyers review, understand, and assess NDAs, employment agreements, service agreements, and purchase agreements in plain language by explaining key terms, flagging potential problems, and recommending professional review for high-risk clauses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees without a legal background use this skill before signing a contract they do not fully understand, to walk through terms, spot missing or ambiguous clauses, and prepare questions for professional counsel. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the skill's contract explanations or signing posture as legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as educational guidance, avoid final legal decisions, and recommend a qualified lawyer for high-value, employment, IP, non-compete, or dispute-related contracts. <br>
Risk: Contracts can contain sensitive personal, commercial, or legal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting highly sensitive contracts unless the user is comfortable sharing that text with the agent environment; use section-by-section review when appropriate. <br>
Risk: Plain-language review may miss jurisdiction-specific requirements or unusual legal consequences. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for contract context, flag high-risk or ambiguous clauses, and route complex or high-risk issues to professional review. <br>
