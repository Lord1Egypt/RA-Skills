## Description: <br>
Evaluates pricing model, trial structure, and willingness-to-pay for startups. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
SaaS founders, startup operators, and product teams use this skill to evaluate pricing models, price points, trial structure, pricing-page design, and pricing experiments before making monetization decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pricing plans, customer feedback, competitor notes, and willingness-to-pay details can be business-sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Share only details appropriate for a chat context and avoid confidential customer, revenue, contract, or strategy information. <br>
Risk: Pricing recommendations are advisory and may not reflect real customer willingness to pay or market constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations with customer conversations, controlled pricing experiments, and business review before changing prices. <br>
