## Description: <br>
Interprets free-form dream descriptions by grounding Zhougong-style traditional meanings in web sources and separating them from a modern psychology perspective. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Chinese-speaking users and agents use this skill to interpret dreams with concise traditional Zhougong-style context, source-aware uncertainty, and a separate modern psychology reading. It is suited for reflective, cultural, or entertainment-oriented dream interpretation, not diagnosis or prediction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Traditional dream interpretations may be mistaken for factual predictions. <br>
Mitigation: Frame Zhougong-style interpretations as folk or cultural references, identify uncertainty, and avoid presenting them as fact. <br>
Risk: Psychological readings may be over-interpreted as mental health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the psychology view reflective and non-diagnostic, and remind users to rely on real-world judgment for important decisions. <br>
