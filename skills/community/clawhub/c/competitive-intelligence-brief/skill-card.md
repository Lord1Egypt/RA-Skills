## Description: <br>
Drafts a structured competitive intelligence brief from monitoring data, news signals, pricing changes, and win/loss notes, synthesizing recent competitor moves into actionable insights for sales, product, marketing, and executive audiences. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Competitive intelligence analysts, product marketers, strategy professionals, and sales enablement leads use this skill to turn monitoring data, news, pricing changes, product launches, and win/loss notes into an internal draft CI brief for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated competitive claims or inferred strategy may be incomplete, unsourced, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Review sourced claims before distribution and label unsupported areas as coverage gaps rather than filling them with speculation. <br>
Risk: Internal customer names, active deal details, or proprietary win/loss notes could be exposed outside the intended audience. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive deal information internal and verify the distribution label before sharing the brief. <br>
Risk: Draft battlecard counter-messaging could be used externally without appropriate review. <br>
Mitigation: Obtain product marketing and legal sign-off before using battlecard counter-messages in sales or public materials. <br>
