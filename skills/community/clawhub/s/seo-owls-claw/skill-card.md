## Description: <br>
Generates persona-driven SEO content, briefs, content plans, audits, and deploy-ready HTML using brand profiles, locale files, templates, and SEO checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[larya-dot-eu](https://clawhub.ai/user/larya-dot-eu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketers, SEO practitioners, content teams, and site operators use this skill to research search intent, plan content clusters, create SEO briefs, generate page or social content, and produce HTML with structured-data guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SEO guidance or page content may be misleading, inaccurate, or inappropriate for a brand, locale, or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content, SEO claims, locale settings, and active brand profile before publishing. <br>
Risk: Workflows that save output could write files the user did not intend to create or overwrite. <br>
Mitigation: Require the agent to show the exact content and destination path, then wait for explicit confirmation before saving. <br>
Risk: Live SEO checks can target pages outside the user's control. <br>
Mitigation: Use live URL checks only on pages the user owns or is authorized to audit. <br>
