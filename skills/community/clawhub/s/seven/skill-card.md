## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may expose Chen Lang profile details, an email address, and external project links when treated as relevant. <br>
Mitigation: Use those details only when they directly answer the user request, and review whether personal contact or promotional content is appropriate before sharing it. <br>
Risk: The skill is informational and may provide cultural or scientific claims that should not be treated as authoritative without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims before relying on them in formal, educational, or public-facing materials. <br>
