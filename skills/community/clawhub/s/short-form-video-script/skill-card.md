## Description: <br>
Write short-form video scripts (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) for any audience and topic with proper hook, arc, and CTA. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and operators use this skill to draft or improve short-form video scripts, captions, topic angles, and performance-informed follow-up scripts for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A high-retention hook or CTA could overstate the underlying product, topic, or evidence if the user provides incomplete source material. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims, proof points, and the final CTA before publishing, and adjust the hook so the payoff matches the promise. <br>
Risk: Scripts may miss the presenter's voice, audience depth, or platform constraints when those inputs are not supplied. <br>
Mitigation: Provide a voice profile, audience level, runtime, and any character limits before generation, then read the script aloud and revise before production. <br>
