## Description: <br>
The model produces correct-looking output that addresses a different task than the one requested - typically a related but distinct interpretation of an ambiguous prompt. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mvogt99](https://clawhub.ai/user/mvogt99) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and prompt authors use this guidance skill to recognize when an AI answer is coherent but solves a related wrong task, then tighten prompts and acceptance checks around the intended deliverable. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include secrets or sensitive private material when asking an AI to diagnose prompts or outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Remove secrets and unnecessary private details from prompt and output excerpts before using the skill. <br>
