## Description: <br>
Generates and rewrites short-video marketing copy for Douyin, TikTok Japan, and TikTok US, with optional translation and text-to-speech output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[web-seeker](https://clawhub.ai/user/web-seeker) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and marketers use this skill to draft viral-style short-video copy, adapt existing copy into new variations, translate product text, and generate spoken audio for drafts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Translation and text-to-speech features send generated or entered text to third-party services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive, confidential, or regulated content when using translation or voice synthesis. <br>
Risk: Documentation describes local-only storage, which does not cover text sent to MyMemory or tts.wangwangit.com for optional features. <br>
Mitigation: Treat local-only claims as applying to saved browser history only, and review third-party service exposure before commercial use. <br>
