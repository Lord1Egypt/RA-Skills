## Description: <br>
Generates bilingual Chinese and English JIRA ticket descriptions from user input for Bug, Task, Story, and Epic issue types. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[robertsong2019](https://clawhub.ai/user/robertsong2019) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product managers, and project teams use this skill to turn raw task or bug descriptions into copy-ready bilingual JIRA tickets with consistent titles, descriptions, requirements, and acceptance criteria. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive project, customer, or incident details may be carried into generated ticket text. <br>
Mitigation: Review and remove confidential details before sharing the generated ticket or pasting it into JIRA. <br>
