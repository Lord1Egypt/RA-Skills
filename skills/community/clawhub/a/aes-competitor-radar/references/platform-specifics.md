# Platform-Specific Reference

Data points, benchmarks, and analysis nuances for each supported ecommerce platform. Use this reference to calibrate competitor analysis to the platform context.

---

## Amazon

### Key Data Points Available

| Data Point | Where to Find | Reliability |
|---|---|---|
| Price and price history | Product page, CamelCamelCamel, Keepa | High (tools track continuously) |
| Review count and rating | Product page, review section | High (public data) |
| BSR (Best Sellers Rank) | Product detail page, "Product Information" section | High (real-time, but fluctuates hourly) |
| Image count and quality | Product page image gallery | High (directly observable) |
| A+ Content presence | Product page, below bullets | High (directly observable) |
| Fulfillment method | "Ships from and sold by" section | High (directly observable) |
| Coupon availability | Product page, coupon badge | High (directly observable) |
| Subscribe & Save pricing | Product page, S&S option | High (directly observable) |
| Sponsored ad presence | Search results page ("Sponsored" label) | High (observable but varies by search) |
| Backend search terms | Not directly visible | N/A — cannot be observed |
| Conversion rate | Not available | N/A — private seller data |
| Exact sales volume | Not available (BSR is a proxy) | Low — BSR is a rank, not volume |

### Amazon-Specific Benchmarks

- **Images:** Category average is 5-6. Top performers use all 7 slots + video.
- **Review velocity:** 30-50 reviews/month is strong for a mid-range product. 100+/month suggests promotional review programs or very high sales volume.
- **BSR:** Varies dramatically by category. BSR 500 in Kitchen means ~200 units/day. BSR 500 in Industrial means ~5 units/day.
- **A+ Content:** Present on ~60% of Brand Registered listings. Conversion lift of 3-10% reported by Amazon.
- **Coupon clip rate:** Average 5-15% of page viewers clip a coupon.

### Amazon-Specific Analysis Notes

- **Buy Box:** Multiple sellers may compete for the same ASIN. Check if the competitor owns the Buy Box or is a third-party seller on someone else's listing.
- **Variation strategy:** Some competitors create extensive variation families (size, color, flavor) to consolidate reviews across variants. This is a legitimate strategy that inflates apparent review counts.
- **Vine reviews:** Amazon Vine reviews are marked. A product with many Vine reviews early on is using Amazon's review seeding program.
- **Subscribe & Save:** Competitors offering S&S at deeper discounts (15-20%) are trading margin for recurring revenue and customer lock-in.

## Shopee

### Key Data Points Available

| Data Point | Where to Find | Reliability |
|---|---|---|
| Price and discount history | Product page, voucher section | Medium (discounts change frequently) |
| Units sold | Product page ("X sold") | High (public, cumulative) |
| Rating and review count | Product page, review tab | High (public data) |
| Shop rating | Shop page header | High (public data) |
| Response rate and time | Shop page header | High (platform-calculated) |
| Follower count | Shop page header | High (public data) |
| Product count | Shop page | High (public data) |
| Voucher availability | Product page, shop page | High (directly observable) |
| Flash sale participation | Flash deals page | High (observable during events) |
| Shopee Live activity | Live tab | High (observable, but timing-dependent) |
| Shopee Ads placement | Search results ("Ad" label) | High (observable) |
| Exact revenue | Not available | N/A — "units sold" is cumulative, not periodic |

### Shopee-Specific Benchmarks

- **Shop rating:** 4.7+ is competitive. Below 4.5 risks search suppression.
- **Response rate:** 95%+ expected by buyers. Below 90% is a trust signal issue.
- **Response time:** "Within hours" is standard. "Within minutes" is competitive advantage.
- **Units sold:** Interpret carefully — this is cumulative lifetime, not monthly. A product showing "10K+ sold" over 2 years is ~420/month. A product showing "5K+ sold" in 3 months is ~1,700/month.
- **Follower count:** 10K+ followers indicates an established shop. 100K+ indicates a major seller.

### Shopee-Specific Analysis Notes

- **Voucher stacking:** Shopee allows multiple vouchers (shop voucher + platform voucher + coins cashback). The effective price can be 20-40% below listed price. Always calculate the stacked discount.
- **Shopee Live:** Sellers running live sessions 3+ times/week are investing heavily in the platform's social commerce tools. This correlates with higher visibility.
- **Preferred Seller / Mall status:** Shopee Mall sellers have verified brand status and stricter quality standards. Preferred Sellers have met performance thresholds. These badges affect trust and search ranking.
- **Free shipping program:** Shopee's free shipping program is subsidized by the platform. Sellers opting in get a search boost but absorb partial shipping costs.

## TikTok Shop

### Key Data Points Available

| Data Point | Where to Find | Reliability |
|---|---|---|
| Price and promotions | Product page, creator videos | High (directly observable) |
| Units sold | Product page | High (cumulative) |
| Rating and review count | Product page, review section | High (public data) |
| Creator/affiliate content | "Videos about this product" section | High (directly observable) |
| Shop rating | Shop page | High (public data) |
| Live selling activity | Live tab, scheduled lives | Medium (timing-dependent) |
| Ad spend indicators | "Sponsored" labels in feed | Low (hard to quantify) |

### TikTok Shop-Specific Benchmarks

- **Creator content:** Products with 50+ creator videos have strong affiliate presence. Products with 500+ are category leaders.
- **Review count:** TikTok Shop is newer — 100+ reviews is established, 1,000+ is dominant for most categories.
- **Price point:** TikTok Shop skews toward impulse purchases. Products $10-$40 convert best. Premium products ($60+) require strong creator endorsement.
- **Live selling:** Sellers doing daily lives with 100+ concurrent viewers are generating significant volume.

### TikTok Shop-Specific Analysis Notes

- **Affiliate commission rates:** Competitors offering higher commission rates (15-25%) attract more creator content. This is a key competitive lever not visible on the listing itself but observable by the volume and diversity of creator content.
- **Content velocity:** How quickly are new videos being created about the product? 10+ new videos/week indicates an active affiliate program.
- **Viral potential:** Some TikTok Shop products spike due to a single viral video. Check if the competitor's success is sustainable (consistent creator content) or flash-in-the-pan (one viral moment).

|---|---|---|

| Shop rating | Shop page | High |

### Lazada-Specific Analysis Notes

- **LazMall vs Marketplace:** LazMall sellers are brand-authorized with higher trust signals. Marketplace sellers compete more on price.
- **Campaign participation:** Lazada runs major campaigns monthly (Birthday Sale, Mid-Year Sale, 11.11, 12.12). Competitor participation and discount depth during these events is a key indicator.
- **Cross-border sellers:** Some competitors ship from China with lower costs but longer delivery times. Note fulfillment origin in analysis.

## Shopify / DTC

### Key Data Points Available

| Data Point | Where to Find | Reliability |
|---|---|---|
| Price | Product page | High |
| Product reviews | Review app on site (if installed) | Medium (may use Yotpo, Judge.me, etc.) |
| Site traffic estimates | SimilarWeb, SEMrush | Low-Medium (estimates only) |
| SEO rankings | SEMrush, Ahrefs | Medium (tools provide estimates) |
| Social media metrics | Platform profiles | High (public data) |
| Email marketing indicators | Pop-ups, footer subscribe | High (observable) |
| Paid ad presence | Google Ads, Meta Ads Library | High (observable) |
| Tech stack | BuiltWith, Wappalyzer | High (tools identify Shopify apps) |

### Shopify-Specific Analysis Notes

- **Review app choice:** The presence of Yotpo, Judge.me, Loox, or Stamped.io indicates investment in social proof. The number and quality of reviews on these apps is a competitive signal.
- **Subscription offerings:** Competitors using ReCharge or Bold Subscriptions have a recurring revenue model. Analyze subscription pricing vs one-time pricing.
- **Abandoned cart recovery:** Not directly observable, but competitors with sophisticated email flows (check their social ads for retargeting) are likely recovering 5-15% of abandoned carts.
- **Upsell/cross-sell:** Check product pages for "Frequently bought together," "You may also like," and bundle offers. These indicate AOV optimization efforts.
- **Page speed:** Use Google PageSpeed Insights. Score below 50 on mobile is a competitive disadvantage. Score above 80 is strong.
