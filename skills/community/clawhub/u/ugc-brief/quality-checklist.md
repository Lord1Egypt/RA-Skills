# UGC Brief Quality Checklist

---

## Before Sending the Brief

- [ ] Product objective is clearly defined (not "make a good video")
- [ ] Deliverables are fully specified: format, duration, aspect ratio, quantity, audio, revisions
- [ ] Three or more hook options provided (not one mandatory hook)
- [ ] Single hero message identified (not a list of features)
- [ ] Do's are specific behaviors (not attitudes like "be authentic")
- [ ] Don'ts include prohibited claims, competitor mentions, and common pitfalls
- [ ] Usage rights are explicit: platforms, use type, duration, edit rights
- [ ] Full timeline with dates for submission, feedback, revision, and final delivery
- [ ] Payment structure and payment method specified

## Content Quality Gates (when reviewing submissions)

- [ ] Hook appears in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Product is shown in use (not just held up to camera)
- [ ] Hero message is communicated naturally (not read from a prompt)
- [ ] Product name is mentioned at least once
- [ ] CTA is included at end
- [ ] No unapproved claims made (health, medical, comparative)
- [ ] No competitor products visible in frame
- [ ] Audio is clear (no excessive background noise)
- [ ] Lighting is adequate (face and product clearly visible)
- [ ] Video length is within specified range

## Usage Rights Verification

- [ ] Rights grant is documented (in brief, in contract, or in email confirmation)
- [ ] Rights duration is logged with expiration date reminder
- [ ] Any exclusivity terms are logged and monitored
- [ ] Whitelist access (if applicable) is confirmed and active

## Creator Relationship Management

- [ ] Creator has confirmed receipt of brief
- [ ] Q&A deadline has passed without unresolved questions
- [ ] Submission confirmed by deadline (or extension agreed in writing)
- [ ] Feedback delivered within the agreed feedback window
- [ ] Final payment triggered on delivery of final assets
- [ ] Content filed in asset library with rights metadata
