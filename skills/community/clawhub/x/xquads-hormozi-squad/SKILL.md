## Frameworks Principais

### Grand Slam Offer (hormozi-offers)
1. What's the Dream Outcome?
2. What problems do they have?
3. What's the vehicle/solution?
4. Stack the value

### CLOSER Framework (hormozi-closer)
- **C**larify their situation
- **L**abel the problem
- **O**verlap with their story
- **S**how the solution
- **E**xplain the offer
- **R**einforce the decision

### $100M Leads (hormozi-leads)
- Core offer → Lead magnet
- Lead magnet → Core offer
- Lead sources diversification
