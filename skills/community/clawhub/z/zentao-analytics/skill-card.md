## Description: <br>
ZenTao Analytics helps analyze ZenTao task data for task volume, work hours, difficulty, efficiency, and workload saturation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiaolongxue](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiaolongxue) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Team leads, project managers, and delivery managers use this skill to review ZenTao task workload, efficiency, and saturation indicators for team performance, resource allocation, and project monitoring. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release evidence says the skill advertises live employee and task analytics while the included analyzer currently uses hard-coded sample data. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the analysis is based on live ZenTao data before using reports for management decisions. <br>
Risk: Generated reports may contain sensitive personnel and workload information. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated read-only ZenTao token or database account and treat generated reports as sensitive internal data. <br>
