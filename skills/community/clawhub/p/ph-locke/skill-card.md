## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for educational roleplay conversations with a simulated John Locke persona. It is suited for discussing Locke's empiricism, political philosophy, religious toleration, and related historical works in English or Chinese. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad name-based triggers can activate the persona unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly with /locke when possible and review activation settings before deployment. <br>
Risk: In-character roleplay can be mistaken for a neutral assistant or an authoritative historical source. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as an AI simulation of John Locke and verify factual or historical claims against trusted sources. <br>
