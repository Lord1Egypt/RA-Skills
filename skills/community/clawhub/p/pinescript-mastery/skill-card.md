## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated trading strategies may be incorrect, overfit, or misleading if used directly for real-money trading. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated Pine Script, test it in TradingView with realistic fees and slippage, validate it out-of-sample, and avoid live trading or automated alerts without independent review. <br>
Risk: Generated code may include assumptions that do not match the user's market, timeframe, broker constraints, or Pine Script version target. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the asset class, timeframe, entry and exit logic, risk model, and Pine Script version before relying on the output. <br>
