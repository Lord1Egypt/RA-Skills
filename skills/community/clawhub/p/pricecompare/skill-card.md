## Description: <br>
购物省钱宝 helps agents parse JD/Taobao/Pinduoduo shopping links and share codes, search coupons, compare product prices, and convert product links into deal links. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External shoppers and shopping-assistant agents use this skill to process JD.com, Taobao, and Pinduoduo product links, share codes, and product queries, then return coupon, converted-link, and price-comparison results. <br>
