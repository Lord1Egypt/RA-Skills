## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases can cause the assistant to adopt the persona during ordinary business discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer explicit activation phrases and let normal safety, transparency, and user-intent rules take priority over role-play behavior. <br>
Risk: The skill speaks in first person as a public figure-inspired persona, which may be mistaken for the real person's views. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the artifact's disclaimer visible on first activation and clarify that outputs are derived perspective-taking, not statements from Wang Jianlin. <br>
