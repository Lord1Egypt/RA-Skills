## Description: <br>
Weekly meal prep plan from fridge ingredients with nutrition and shopping list. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn available ingredients, dietary goals, and serving counts into a weekly meal prep plan with nutrition estimates, shopping needs, and cooking timelines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Nutrition and macronutrient values may be inaccurate estimates. <br>
Mitigation: Treat nutrition values as planning estimates and verify them against product labels or a trusted nutrition source before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Meal plans may be unsuitable for medical dietary needs or health conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use the skill as medical or dietary advice; consult a qualified professional for condition-specific nutrition requirements. <br>
