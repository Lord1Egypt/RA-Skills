## Description: <br>
Helps agents create, optimize, or audit B2B solutions pages organized around industries, company sizes, teams, outcomes, proof, CTAs, and SEO metadata. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, growth, and web teams use this skill to plan or review solutions pages that communicate measurable business outcomes by industry, company size, team, or outcome. It helps structure page sections, messaging, proof points, internal links, and SEO metadata for solutions-page content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated solutions-page content may include unapproved metrics, customer claims, or business details from project context. <br>
Mitigation: Review project-context inputs and verify all metrics, customer claims, and business details before publishing or sharing externally. <br>
Risk: Outcome-led page recommendations can be directionally useful but may be inaccurate for a specific product, market, or regulated industry. <br>
Mitigation: Have product, marketing, legal, or compliance reviewers confirm positioning, proof points, and industry-specific claims before release. <br>
