## Description: <br>
Generates standardized, friendly e-commerce customer-service replies for return, exchange, refund, shipping-fee, and after-sales policy questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer support agents and commerce operators use this skill to draft clear buyer-facing responses for returns, exchanges, refunds, shipping-fee questions, and after-sales disputes while following the bundled policy rules. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated replies can be wrong if the bundled return policy differs from the merchant's current platform rules or product category exceptions. <br>
Mitigation: Review and update the policy rules before deployment, and require staff verification for uncertain or exception-heavy cases. <br>
Risk: Customer-facing drafts may accidentally overpromise refunds, compensation, or processing outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated replies before sending and keep the skill's constraints against unsupported guarantees in force. <br>
