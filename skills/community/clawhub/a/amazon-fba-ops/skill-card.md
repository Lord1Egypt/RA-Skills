## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may request seller business data such as ASINs, lead times, supplier details, advertising budgets, and campaign targets. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum data needed, use supplier nicknames or internal IDs where possible, and avoid confidential contract or banking details. <br>
Risk: Operational recommendations can affect purchasing, advertising spend, financial planning, competitor analysis, or public seller responses. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review purchase orders, financial calculations, PPC decisions, competitor findings, and response drafts before acting on them. <br>
