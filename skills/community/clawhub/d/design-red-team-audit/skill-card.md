## Description: <br>
Adversarial design audit that stress-tests a game feature, system, pitch, roadmap item, or product idea by assuming failure and identifying the most credible reasons it would fail. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External product, design, and game development teams use this skill to pressure-test concepts before production by identifying credible failure modes, weak assumptions, validation gaps, and fast de-risking moves. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive design documents, product plans, metrics, or proprietary pitches for critique. <br>
Mitigation: Share only material appropriate for model analysis and give explicit constraints for assumptions or topics the audit should avoid. <br>
Risk: Adversarial critique can overstate risks if the input omits target audience, success metrics, or rollout context. <br>
Mitigation: Include the intended audience, business goal, validation plan, and known constraints so the audit can focus on credible failure mechanisms. <br>
