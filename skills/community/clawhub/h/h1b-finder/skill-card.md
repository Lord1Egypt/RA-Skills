## Description: <br>
Find H1B / H-1B sponsor companies, visa sponsorship employers, and sponsor history for US jobs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ewangchong](https://clawhub.ai/user/ewangchong) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External job seekers, international students, OPT candidates, and professionals use this skill to research US employers with historical H1B sponsorship activity by company, role, location, or year. It helps narrow sponsor leads and compare employer filing history before applying. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Queries could include unnecessary personal details during H1B sponsor research. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive personal information; use role, employer, city, state, and year filters instead. <br>
Risk: Historical filing data may be delayed, incomplete, uneven across employers, or not representative of current hiring and sponsorship plans. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as research leads and verify important job, visa, and legal details with current employer or official sources before acting. <br>
