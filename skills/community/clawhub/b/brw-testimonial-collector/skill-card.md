## Description: <br>
Systematically gather and format client testimonials. Use when someone needs social proof, wants to collect feedback, or needs to turn happy clients into public advocates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business owners, marketers, consultants, and sales teams use this skill to ask clients for testimonials, shape responses into short or long social proof, and maintain a testimonial library with permission tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Client names, titles, companies, and quotes may be sensitive business data if collected into a testimonial library. <br>
Mitigation: Store testimonial records only in approved systems and keep documented permission for each quote. <br>
Risk: Published testimonials can become misleading if edits add claims, exaggerate results, or change the client's meaning. <br>
Mitigation: Limit edits to clarity and grammar, preserve the original meaning, and get client approval before publication or reuse. <br>
