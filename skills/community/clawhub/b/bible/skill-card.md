## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Religious study guidance may be mistaken for authoritative doctrine or scholarship. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as study guidance and verify interpretation against trusted texts, translations, and qualified human review. <br>
Risk: Incorrect Bible citations, translation labels, or verse ranges could mislead a reader. <br>
Mitigation: Check cited passages, identify the translation used, and confirm that quoted or summarized ranges match the source text. <br>
