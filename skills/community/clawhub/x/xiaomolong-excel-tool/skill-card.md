## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Spreadsheet edits can unintentionally damage formulas, formatting, data types, workbook structure, or important business data. <br>
Mitigation: Keep backups and review formulas, error cells, data types, formatting, hidden structure, and visual layout before delivering edited workbooks. <br>
Risk: The registry release metadata and bundled artifact metadata are not perfectly aligned. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the ClawHub page, publisher handle, and release version before installation or deployment. <br>
