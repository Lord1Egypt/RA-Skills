## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to draft and refine Chinese social-insight articles with structured argumentation, style templates, and optional local helpers for outlines, phrasing, and style checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Security evidence notes sensitive maintainer workflows may use local credentials or external reviewers when invoked. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when those workflows are intended, and review commands before execution. <br>
Risk: Generated social commentary may include unsupported examples, data, or conclusions if the user does not provide verified source material. <br>
Mitigation: Review facts, citations, and tone before publication, and provide verified data or cases for claims that need evidence. <br>
Risk: Style imitation and automated scoring can overfit surface phrasing while missing accuracy, originality, or audience fit. <br>
Mitigation: Use the style checks as drafting aids, then perform human editorial review for substance, originality, and appropriateness. <br>
