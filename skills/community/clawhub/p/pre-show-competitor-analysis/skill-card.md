## Description: <br>
Analyze competitor exhibitor presence, booth positioning, and messaging before a trade show. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External trade show, marketing, sales, and product teams use this skill before an event to map relevant competitors, score threats, find positioning gaps, and prepare booth and messaging responses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitive analysis can influence business strategy and may be wrong if exhibitor lists, floor plans, or messaging are outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before acting, verify the event edition and sources, and keep inferred, estimated, or unavailable details tagged instead of treating them as facts. <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive company, competitor, or event-planning context while requesting analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only event, competitor, and company context that is appropriate to use for competitive analysis. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed for pre-show intelligence and may not reflect real-time booth activity. <br>
Mitigation: Use it before the event and verify open questions on site with direct observation or a dedicated on-site competitor-radar workflow. <br>
