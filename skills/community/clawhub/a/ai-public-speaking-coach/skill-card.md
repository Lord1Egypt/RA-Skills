## Description: <br>
AI Public Speaking Coach helps users draft, rehearse, and improve speeches for TED-style talks, business presentations, impromptu remarks, debates, wedding speeches, interviews, and related public-speaking scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and professionals use this skill to generate speech drafts, presentation structures, delivery coaching, and feedback for public-speaking situations. It is especially suited for workplace reports, pitch decks, impromptu speaking, Toastmasters practice, weddings, interviews, debates, and online presentations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated speeches or coaching advice may contain inaccurate, unsuitable, or audience-inappropriate content. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt generated material for accuracy, tone, audience, venue, and appropriateness before using it publicly. <br>
