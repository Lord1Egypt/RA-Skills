## Description: <br>
Generates moody dark academia images such as candlelit libraries, vintage portraits, classical statues, and autumn campus scenes using the Neta AI image generation API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[omactiengartelle](https://clawhub.ai/user/omactiengartelle) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and agent users use this skill to generate dark academia aesthetic image URLs from text prompts for moodboards, journal covers, social posts, playlist art, and atmospheric backgrounds. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts and API tokens are sent to the Neta/TalesOfAI service. <br>
Mitigation: Decide whether to trust the service with prompts and tokens before installing; avoid exposing real tokens in shared terminals, CI jobs, recordings, screenshots, or logs. <br>
