## Description: <br>
Xiaozhi Physics Problem Coach guides physics learners through diagram-first problem solving, physical modeling, calculation, and reflection prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students and learning-support agents use this skill to work through physics problems without receiving complete solutions up front. It emphasizes drawing physical situations, choosing models, checking units, and reflecting on errors. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional learning-record features may store progress or physics error patterns for personalization. <br>
Mitigation: Enable long-term learning or error-DNA updates only with informed consent, especially for students or minors, and provide controls to review, disable, or delete stored learning records. <br>
