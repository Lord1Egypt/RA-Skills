## Description: <br>
Comprehensive Pandas toolkit for construction data analysis. Filter, group, aggregate BIM elements, calculate quantities, merge datasets, and generate reports from structured construction data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[datadrivenconstruction](https://clawhub.ai/user/datadrivenconstruction) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Construction analysts, BIM coordinators, estimators, and project teams use this skill to analyze construction spreadsheets or BIM exports with Pandas, calculate quantities and costs, merge datasets, and prepare structured reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read construction spreadsheets or BIM exports from user-provided file paths and may write CSV, Excel, or JSON reports. <br>
Mitigation: Review input and output paths before execution and avoid pointing it at unrelated private folders. <br>
Risk: Analysis results can be misleading if source data has missing, inconsistent, or incorrect columns. <br>
Mitigation: Validate inputs, report errors clearly, and review summary tables before using them for quantity take-off, cost, or schedule decisions. <br>
