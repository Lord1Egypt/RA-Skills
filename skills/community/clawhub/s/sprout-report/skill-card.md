## Description: <br>
发芽报告将录音、转写文本或话题内容转化为多维度分析报告，帮助发现洞察、故事连接和创作角度。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to analyze recordings, transcripts, or topics from multiple disciplinary perspectives and turn the results into structured creative directions. It is suited for deep topic exploration, writing inspiration, and identifying unexpected connections. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recordings or conversations can contain private or participant-sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm participant consent before use and prefer transcripts or redacted text when the content is sensitive. <br>
Risk: The generated analysis can present interpretive claims or creative angles that may not be factually verified. <br>
Mitigation: Review the report before relying on it for publication, education, or decision-making. <br>
