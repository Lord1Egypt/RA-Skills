## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, faculty, and academic teams use this skill to plan an academic brand, increase scholarly and industry visibility, prepare communications for research outputs, and build pathways for industry collaboration or academic entrepreneurship. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share unpublished research, confidential partnership details, or account credentials while seeking branding advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use sanitized inputs and avoid including unpublished research details, confidential commercial information, credentials, or private account data in prompts. <br>
Risk: Strategic branding recommendations may overstate academic results or create misleading public claims if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated positioning, media copy, and outreach plans against the user's actual evidence, institutional policies, and academic ethics before publishing. <br>
