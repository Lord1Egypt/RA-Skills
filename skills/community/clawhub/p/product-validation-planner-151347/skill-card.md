## Description: <br>
Helps founders, product managers, makers, and small teams turn rough product ideas into validated plans, prototypes, user-facing positioning, and actionable validation workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, product managers, makers, and small teams use this skill to test whether a product idea is worth building before spending heavily. It helps clarify outcomes and constraints, convert product uncertainty into checklists, templates, lightweight analyses, prototypes, and decision support, and verify outputs against practical success criteria. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad product and startup keywords may cause the skill to appear in unrelated business-planning conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Explicitly invoke the skill when product validation planning is desired, and ignore or disable it when it appears outside that context. <br>
Risk: Validation plans and product recommendations may be incomplete or misleading if the user provides sparse market, customer, or constraint information. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions, identify missing inputs that materially affect the plan, and validate outputs against the user's success criteria before acting on them. <br>
