## Description: <br>
Smzdm helps agents summarize public SMZDM hot deals, rankings, searches, coupons, and promotions for price alerts, category statistics, and activity reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CodeKungfu](https://clawhub.ai/user/CodeKungfu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to collect concise summaries of public SMZDM deal, discovery, ranking, search, coupon, and event pages. It is intended for lightweight analysis and reminders, not purchasing, account operations, reverse engineering, or bulk collection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The server security scan marked the release suspicious. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if you trust the publisher and need this SMZDM public-page summarization workflow. <br>
Risk: Deal prices, coupons, rankings, and event times can change after collection. <br>
Mitigation: Include the collection time in summaries and verify time-sensitive details on the source page before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Automating public pages can conflict with platform rules or trigger human verification. <br>
Mitigation: Use lightweight public-page access, avoid account operations, ordering, reverse engineering, and bulk collection, and switch to manual review when verification appears. <br>
