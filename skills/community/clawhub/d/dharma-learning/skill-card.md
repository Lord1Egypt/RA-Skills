## Description: <br>
Autonomous Buddhist learning skill with web search, local Dharma database, and Middle Way view, dedicated to the long lives of HH Dalai Lama and Khen Rinpoche Geshe Chonyi of Kopan Monastery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dorjenorbulim](https://clawhub.ai/user/dorjenorbulim) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to ask Buddhist study and practice questions, search local Dharma notes and authoritative web sources, and receive sourced summaries with daily-life applications. It is intended as a learning support and does not replace a teacher, meditation practice, or direct realization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Server security evidence reports undeclared business, prediction-market, purchase, and crypto-related scope in an otherwise Buddhist study skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing; do not grant purchase, payment, wallet, crypto, or financial permissions unless those capabilities are removed or separately reviewed. <br>
Risk: The skill persists and reuses local Dharma notes and learned summaries, which may include sensitive personal practice material. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid placing sensitive personal notes in dharma/ unless local persistence and later reuse are acceptable. <br>
Risk: The security guidance identifies monetization and weather-trading files as out of scope for a Dharma-only release. <br>
Mitigation: Remove or quarantine the monetization and weather-trading files if the release is intended to remain Dharma-only. <br>
