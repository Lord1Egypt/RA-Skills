## 一、自我介绍类 / Self-Introduction

### 1.1 基础自我介绍 / Basic Self-Introduction
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "请做一个简单的自我介绍"
- "介绍一下你自己"
- "用2-3分钟介绍一下你的经历"

<!-- EN -->
- "Please give a brief self-introduction"
- "Tell me about yourself"
- "Introduce your background in 2-3 minutes"

---

### 1.2 英文自我介绍 / English Self-Introduction
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "Please introduce yourself in English"
- "Can you do a self-introduction in English?"

<!-- EN -->
- "Please introduce yourself in English"
- "Can you walk me through your background in English?"

---

### 1.3 针对简历的自我介绍 / Resume-Based Introduction
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "看你的简历，你从XX年到XX年在YY公司，能介绍一下吗？"
- "你之前是做产品运营的，后来转型做商分，为什么？"

<!-- EN -->
- "I see on your resume you were at [Company A] from [Year] to [Year]. Can you tell me about that?"
- "You transitioned from product operations to business analysis. Why?"

## 二、项目经历类 / Project & Experience Questions

### 2.1 项目背景 / Project Background
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "介绍一下你做过的项目"
- "能讲讲你在某头部互联网公司A/某头部互联网公司B的工作吗？"
- "你负责的数据分析工作具体是什么？"

<!-- EN -->
- "Tell me about a project you worked on"
- "Walk me through your work at Company A / Company B"
- "What did your data analytics work involve day-to-day?"

---

### 2.2 项目细节追问 / Project Details Follow-Up
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "这个项目的业务目标是什么？"
- "你具体负责什么？团队有几个人？"
- "项目的周期是多长？"
- "你是主动发起的还是被动接的需求？"

<!-- EN -->
- "What was the business objective of this project?"
- "What was your specific role? How many people were on the team?"
- "How long did the project take?"
- "Did you initiate this project or were you assigned to it?"

---

### 2.3 数据处理相关 / Data Handling Questions
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "数据是怎么取的？从哪个表？"
- "样本量是多少？"
- "数据清洗怎么处理缺失值和异常值？"
- "特征工程是怎么做的？"

<!-- EN -->
- "How did you extract the data? From which tables?"
- "What was the sample size?"
- "How did you handle missing values and outliers in data cleaning?"
- "How was feature engineering done?"

---

### 2.4 方法论追问 / Methodology Follow-Up
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "RFM模型是怎么用的？阈值怎么设定？"
- "DID的原理是什么？怎么排除干扰因素？"
- "PSM怎么匹配？特征怎么选？"
- "K-means聚类的K值怎么确定？"

<!-- EN -->
- "How did you apply the RFM model? How were thresholds set?"
- "What is the logic behind DID? How did you control for confounders?"
- "How did you perform PSM matching? Which features did you use?"
- "How did you determine K in K-means clustering?"

---

### 2.5 项目结果 / Project Outcomes
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "最终效果怎么样？"
- "指标提升了多少？"
- "怎么证明是项目带来的效果而不是自然增长？"
- "业务方认可你的分析吗？落地情况如何？"

<!-- EN -->
- "What were the final results?"
- "How much did the metrics improve?"
- "How do you prove the impact was from your project and not organic growth?"
- "Did stakeholders accept your analysis? Was it implemented?"

---

## 三、方法论类 / Methodology Questions

### 3.1 统计分析 / Statistical Analysis
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "你熟悉哪些统计分析方法？"
- "AB测试怎么做？"
- "小样本情况下怎么做实验？"
- "怎么判断实验结果的显著性？"
- "回归分析和AB测试有什么区别？"

<!-- EN -->
- "What statistical analysis methods are you familiar with?"
- "How do you run an A/B test?"
- "How do you design experiments with small sample sizes?"
- "How do you determine statistical significance?"
- "What's the difference between regression analysis and A/B testing?"

---

### 3.2 机器学习 / Machine Learning
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "你用过哪些机器学习算法？"
- "在什么项目里用过？"
- "深度学习你熟悉吗？"
- "特征工程是怎么做的？"

<!-- EN -->
- "Which ML algorithms have you used?"
- "In what projects did you apply them?"
- "Are you familiar with deep learning?"
- "Walk me through your feature engineering process"

---

### 3.3 数据建模 / Data Modeling
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "你搭建过哪些数据模型？"
- "模型的输入输出是什么？"
- "模型怎么验证效果？"
- "模型的局限性是什么？"

<!-- EN -->
- "What data models have you built?"
- "What are the inputs and outputs of your model?"
- "How do you validate model performance?"
- "What are the limitations of your model?"

---

### 3.4 指标体系 / KPI Framework
<!-- ZH-CN -->
- "你们团队的北极星指标是什么？"
- "怎么拆解一级、二级指标？"
- "指标口径怎么定？"
- "怎么做指标的自动监测和预警？"

<!-- EN -->
- "What is your team's North Star metric?"
- "How do you break it down into L1 and L2 metrics?"
- "How do you define metric calculation logic and scope?"
- "How do you set up automated metric monitoring and alerts?"
