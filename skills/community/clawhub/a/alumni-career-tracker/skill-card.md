## Description: <br>
Analyze laboratory alumni career trajectories and outcomes to provide data-driven career guidance for current students and postdocs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[googolme](https://clawhub.ai/user/googolme) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, lab administrators, mentors, and research program staff use this skill to maintain alumni career data, analyze outcomes, and generate career guidance or aggregate reporting for trainees and program reviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Alumni records can include sensitive personal, salary, location, and career notes. <br>
Mitigation: Use consented data, minimize salary and private notes, protect local data files, and restrict exports to approved recipients. <br>
Risk: Reports or recommendations may expose identifiable details or overstate conclusions from small or biased samples. <br>
Mitigation: Share aggregate reports where possible, anonymize public outputs, include sample-size context, and review recommendations before using them for mentoring or program decisions. <br>
Risk: Unpinned dependencies may change behavior in uncontrolled environments. <br>
Mitigation: Pin and review dependencies in a controlled environment before using the skill with real alumni data. <br>
