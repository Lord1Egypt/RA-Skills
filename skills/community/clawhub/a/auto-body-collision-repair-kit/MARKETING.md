## Target Buyer Segments

### Primary: Independent Shop Owner
- **Pain point:** Competing against DRP shops that have insurer referral pipelines; needs to position quality and customer advocacy
- **Trigger:** Loses a high-value customer who went to the "insurer's preferred shop"
- **Win:** DRP disclosure gate gives independent shops a legal, accurate way to say "we work for you, not your insurer"

### Secondary: High-End / OEM-Certified Shop
- **Pain point:** Spent $20,000-$50,000 on OEM tooling + certification; generic AI copy doesn't mention it
- **Trigger:** Booking luxury vehicles that require OEM documentation (Tesla, BMW, Rivian)
- **Win:** I-CAR Gold Class + OEM certification accuracy gate generates content that matches their actual credentials

### Tertiary: Marketing Agency (collision repair vertical)
- **Pain point:** Client wants "auto body marketing content" — agency uses ChatGPT, generates EPA violations
- **Trigger:** Client gets an FTC complaint or Google ad disapproval for unsubstantiated claim
- **Win:** This skill is the agency's compliance backstop — generates defensible content for every client
