## Description: <br>
Generates crisis communication plans with holding statements, stakeholder messaging, media responses, internal communications templates, social media responses, timelines, and review guides. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business communications, executive, legal, compliance, security, and incident response teams use this skill to draft crisis response materials for incidents such as data breaches, outages, recalls, negative press, regulatory investigations, and financial restatements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crisis messages may be incomplete, inaccurate, legally sensitive, or unsuitable for internal or external release without approval. <br>
Mitigation: Have legal, compliance, security, executive, and communications stakeholders review and approve generated drafts before use. <br>
