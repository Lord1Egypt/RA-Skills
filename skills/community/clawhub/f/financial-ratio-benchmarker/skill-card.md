## Description: <br>
Provides financial ratio benchmarks with structured analysis, actionable recommendations, and next steps without executing code, external calls, or financial transactions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and analysts use this skill to generate financial ratio benchmark reports, gap analysis, recommendations, and next-step checklists for company performance, unit economics, and investor-facing finance questions. It is informational only and does not execute code, call external APIs, or perform financial transactions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial ratio benchmark output may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific investment or business decision. <br>
Mitigation: Treat output as informational guidance, verify benchmarks against current trusted sources, and consult qualified financial professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Generated recommendations could be mistaken for execution-ready financial advice or transactions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill limited to descriptive analysis, recommendations, and checklists; do not use it to execute code, call external services, or perform financial transactions. <br>
