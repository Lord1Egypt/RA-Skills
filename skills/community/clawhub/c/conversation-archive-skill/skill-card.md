## Description: <br>
Use when every conversation needs to be archived to the "历史归档" directory for knowledge management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yanll](https://clawhub.ai/user/yanll) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to append conversation questions, summarized assistant responses, and original assistant replies to daily Markdown archive files in a configured notes repository. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill persistently stores conversation content, which can include sensitive or confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Use a private archive location and avoid using the skill for conversations involving credentials, personal information, legal or medical details, proprietary content, or confidential material. <br>
Risk: The skill does not describe clear opt-out, redaction, or retention controls for archived conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Review archive contents before keeping or sharing them, and establish manual retention and deletion practices for the notes repository. <br>
