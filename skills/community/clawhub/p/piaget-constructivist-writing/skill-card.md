## Description: <br>
皮亚杰建构主义（constructivism）写作与教学表达工作流。用于把知识点写成“读者可建构理解”的文章/讲义/课程脚本：从旧知出发，制造可控认知冲突，同化/顺应逐步搭建概念结构，输出概念地图、学习路径、分层讲解、练习题、误解纠偏与目标达成评估。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Teachers, writers, and learners use this skill to turn a topic into constructivist educational content such as lectures, study notes, course scripts, or explanatory articles. It helps structure material around prior knowledge, controlled cognitive conflict, scaffolding, transfer exercises, misconception correction, and goal evaluation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can steer broad writing requests into a constructivist lesson format even when that is not the user's intended style. <br>
Mitigation: Enable or invoke it selectively for teaching, explanatory writing, lecture, study-note, or beginner-oriented requests. <br>
Risk: Prompts for lesson planning may include real student personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including real student personal information unless the user is comfortable processing it with the agent. <br>
Risk: Generated teaching content can still contain pedagogical gaps, misleading analogies, or unsupported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review the output against the included rubric, anti-pattern guidance, and any domain-specific standards before using it with learners. <br>
