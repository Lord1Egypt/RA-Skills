## Description: <br>
Analyzes learning needs and performance gaps to recommend and blueprint a best-fit instructional strategy with human oversight for corporate training. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mrummler17](https://clawhub.ai/user/mrummler17) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Instructional designers, L&D leads, learning consultants, and capability teams use this skill to diagnose training needs, decide whether training is appropriate, select an instructional framework, and produce a structured strategy blueprint before building learning artifacts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce advisory recommendations that are incomplete, unsuitable, or based on gaps in the provided discovery material. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated recommendations before acting, validate assumptions with stakeholders, and keep human approval points for instructional strategy decisions. <br>
Risk: Training briefs, SME notes, or policy documents may contain confidential business or learner information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential training materials unless the workspace and model provider are approved for that data. <br>
