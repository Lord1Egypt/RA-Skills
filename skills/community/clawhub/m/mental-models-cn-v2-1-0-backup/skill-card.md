## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese mental-model reference library for matching analysis, decision-making, strategy, investment, psychology, and cognitive-science frameworks to user problems. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gmilton09](https://clawhub.ai/user/gmilton09) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill as Chinese-language decision support for choosing and applying mental models to business analysis, investment review, risk assessment, career choices, learning, and interpersonal communication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes investment-oriented mental models that could be mistaken for professional financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational decision support and have qualified humans review investment decisions before acting. <br>
Risk: Documentation refers to a recommendation script that the release changelog says was removed. <br>
Mitigation: Install through the normal ClawHub path and inspect any newly downloaded or restored script before execution. <br>
Risk: Broad triggers may cause the skill to influence general analytical answers. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a reference skill and review generated model recommendations for fit, accuracy, and context. <br>
