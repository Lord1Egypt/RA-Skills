## Description: <br>
Generate a tailored 7-day LinkedIn content plan with pillars, formats, hooks, CTAs, comment targets, and a weekly engagement checklist. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sergebulaev](https://clawhub.ai/user/sergebulaev) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, founders, and marketing teams use this skill to turn a theme, audience, and content-pillar mix into a weekly LinkedIn publishing and engagement plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A generic request such as "plan my week" may trigger the skill unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants a LinkedIn content plan before applying the weekly planning workflow. <br>
Risk: Suggested posts, comments, CTAs, or DMs may be inaccurate or misaligned with the user's brand. <br>
Mitigation: Have the user review and edit all generated social content before publishing or sending it. <br>
