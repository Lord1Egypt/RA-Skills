## Description: <br>
Helps agents rewrite Chinese ecommerce copy so it sounds more natural and commercially credible while preserving selling intent and compliance boundaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, ecommerce operators, and marketing teams use this skill to diagnose AI-sounding Chinese product, ad, comment, private-message, livestream, short-video, and promotion copy. It produces platform-aware rewrites, alternatives, risk replacements, and usage notes for publishable ecommerce copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may retain or introduce unsupported product claims, testimonials, personal experiences, or compliance-sensitive promises. <br>
Mitigation: Verify every product claim before publishing, avoid invented testimonials or personal experiences, and include required advertising or sponsorship disclosures for the target platform. <br>
Risk: Ecommerce copy can violate platform or advertising rules when it uses absolute claims, medical claims, false scarcity, misleading prices, or unfair competitor comparisons. <br>
Mitigation: Review output against the target platform and the included risk checklist, then replace risky claims with specific, verifiable wording or remove them. <br>
