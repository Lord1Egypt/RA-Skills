# Prompt 1: Vehicle Inventory & Promotion Campaigns (Free)

## Use This For
New arrival announcements, weekend sales, trade-in campaigns, and seasonal promotions. Works for new car dealers, used lots, and multi-line stores.

---

## The Prompt

You are an automotive marketing expert who specializes in FTC-compliant, high-converting dealer advertising. I need a complete vehicle inventory & promotion campaign kit.

**Dealership Details:**
- Dealership name: [NAME]
- Location: [CITY, STATE]
- Make(s): [NEW MAKE(S) — e.g., "Toyota" or "Multi-line: Ford + pre-owned"]
- Type: [Franchise new / Independent used / BHPH / Service-only]
- Current campaign focus: [e.g., "spring tax refund season", "year-end clearance", "new 2026 model arrivals"]
- Featured vehicle(s): [e.g., "2026 Toyota Camry XSE starting at $32,495", "Certified Pre-Owned inventory $10K-$25K"]
- Key offer: [e.g., "$0 down, 1.9% APR for 60 months with approved credit", "trade-in bonus up to $2,000", "service credit with purchase"]
- Target customer: [e.g., "First-time buyers / subprime", "families upgrading", "lease conquest (Toyota/Honda expirations)"]
- Unique selling point: [e.g., "300+ vehicles in stock", "lifetime powertrain warranty", "no-haggle pricing"]

**Generate the following campaign assets:**

### 1. New Arrival Social Posts (3 formats)
- **Instagram post** (125 chars max caption + 5 hashtags): visual-first, headline hooks at scroll-stop. Include vehicle year/make/model/trim. Ref: FTC — include "Starting at" if price mentioned; no unverifiable superlatives.
- **Facebook post** (300 chars max): conversational, benefit-led, link to inventory CTA. Include stock number range if featuring multiple units.
- **Nextdoor post** (200 chars): neighbor-tone, local angle ("Henderson families — your next car is already here"). No price claims without expiration date.

### 2. Weekend Sale Email Campaign (3 emails)
- **Email 1 — Thursday (preview)**: Subject line (2 variants), 180-word body. Create urgency without false scarcity. Include "expires Sunday" and full disclosure block.
- **Email 2 — Friday (launch)**: Subject line (2 variants), 200-word body with offer mechanics, CTA. Include Reg Z disclosure for any APR/financing claim.
- **Email 3 — Sunday PM (last chance)**: Subject line (2 variants), 120-word urgency close. Hard deadline. Include full legal footer template.

### 3. Trade-In Campaign (Digital + SMS)
- **Email**: Subject line, 180-word body. Use KBB/Edmunds trade-in hook. "What's your [YEAR] [MAKE MODEL] worth?" framing. CTA: free appraisal. No guaranteed value claims.
- **SMS** (under 160 chars): Compliant trade-in invite with opt-out. Business name required.
- **Facebook ad brief**: Headline (25 chars), body (125 chars), CTA button, targeting note (owners of [competing make], 2-4 year lease expirations).

### 4. Seasonal Campaign: [CAMPAIGN FOCUS]
Tailor the following to the campaign focus provided:
- **Google RSA** (15 headlines × 30 chars, 4 descriptions × 90 chars): FTC-compliant, no "best price" or "lowest price" without substantiation, no unverifiable claims.
- **Radio/Video script** (30 seconds, 75 words): Conversational, local market tone, required disclosure read.
- **Voicemail drop script** (20 seconds): For conquest leads only. CAN-SPAM/TCPA compliant.

### 5. Required Disclosures Checklist
Generate a customized compliance checklist for this dealership's state and campaign type:
- FTC Used Car Rule requirements (if featuring pre-owned)
- Reg Z triggers (any credit/financing mention → required disclosures)
- State DMV advertising rules for [STATE]: expiration dates, stock number requirements, delivery charge disclosure
- FTC Green Guides triggers (if featuring EV/hybrid)
- NADA guidelines: prohibited claims for this campaign type

---

## Output Format
Deliver in clearly labeled sections. All ad copy in quotation marks. Disclosures in [brackets]. Flag any claim I've inputted that may be non-compliant and suggest the compliant alternative.
