## Description: <br>
Package neighborhood recommendations into branded guest content for local experience content workflows for travel agencies, hotels, destination marketers, and tour operators. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External hospitality, travel, and destination marketing teams use this skill to turn local neighborhood recommendations into branded guest guide content. It supports drafting, enriching, and refining local experience content before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local recommendations or venue details may be inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable for guests if generated without review. <br>
Mitigation: Fact-check recommendations before publishing guest-facing content. <br>
Risk: Guest PII or private business data could be exposed if provided to an unapproved agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering guest PII or private business data unless the agent environment is approved for that use. <br>
