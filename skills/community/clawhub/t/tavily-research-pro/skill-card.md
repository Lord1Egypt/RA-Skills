## Description: <br>
Professional AI-powered search and deep research engine. Features multi-dimensional data aggregation, semantic analysis, and automated report generation for structured information gathering. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[airoom-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/airoom-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External developers, researchers, and analysts use this skill to run Tavily-backed web search, public sentiment analysis, webpage extraction, and short research reports from agent-managed shell commands. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided queries and URLs are sent to Tavily for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit secrets, private internal URLs, tokenized links, regulated data, or confidential project details unless Tavily use is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Web search, extraction, and generated summaries can be incomplete, stale, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Review Tavily results and source URLs before using generated findings in research, reporting, or business decisions. <br>
Risk: The packaged _meta.json is malformed and contains script-like content instead of clean JSON metadata. <br>
Mitigation: Treat _meta.json as packaging metadata only and rely on the disclosed script files plus security evidence when reviewing behavior. <br>
