## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Translations for legal, medical, financial, or technical content can lose domain-specific meaning if the target locale, formality, unit handling, or terminology expectations are underspecified. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the target language, locale, desired formality, and whether units, dates, currencies, idioms, or tone should remain literal before using the translated output. <br>
