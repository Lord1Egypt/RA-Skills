## Description: <br>
Ai Go Coach provides Go/weiqi coaching for beginners through amateur 5-dan players, covering rules, joseki, life-and-death training, opening strategy, middlegame fighting, endgame counting, and game review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External Go players and learners use this skill to ask coaching questions, plan training, study standard patterns, practice life-and-death problems, and review SGF game records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI-style Go analysis may be mistaken for results from a connected KataGo or similar engine. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reviews and move suggestions as general coaching unless the agent session is explicitly connected to a real analysis engine. <br>
Risk: Uploaded SGF game records may contain private games, player names, or tournament context. <br>
Mitigation: Share only game records that are appropriate for the agent session, or remove private details before asking for review. <br>
