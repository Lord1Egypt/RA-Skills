## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese shuangwen web-novel chapters from a short option-based prompt and can adapt the chapter into comic-style short-drama storyboard and video prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liuxiangjian-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/liuxiangjian-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, prompt-library builders, and AI writing assistants use this skill to create Chinese web-novel opening chapters with reversal-driven conflict, then optionally convert the chapter into short-form comic-drama storyboard and video-generation prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The default story style emphasizes humiliation, conflict, and reversal, which could drift into hateful, sexual, or overly violent content. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs fictional and follow the skill's safety boundary to avoid hate expression, low-quality sexual content, and detailed violence. <br>
Risk: Medical, legal, or other realistic scenarios may be mistaken for practical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame these scenarios as fiction and avoid real-world medical, legal, or safety instructions. <br>
Risk: Users may include private details or real people in prompts for story or video adaptation. <br>
Mitigation: Use fictionalized characters and omit personal, private, or identifying information. <br>
