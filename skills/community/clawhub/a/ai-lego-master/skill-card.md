## Description: <br>
Ai Lego Master helps users with LEGO set collecting, Technic mechanisms, Ideas and MOC design, BrickLink purchasing, parts-library management, building techniques, lighting, and display. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External LEGO hobbyists, collectors, and builders use this skill to get guidance on set selection, Technic mechanisms, MOC planning, parts sourcing, storage, lighting, display, and community participation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is oriented toward Chinese-language LEGO collecting and building advice, which may not fit users who need another language. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to answer in the user's preferred language and confirm product names, budgets, and regional availability before relying on recommendations. <br>
Risk: The skill covers a broad LEGO hobby scope, so very general prompts may produce advice that is less targeted than needed. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the specific theme, build goal, experience level, parts constraints, and budget when asking for guidance. <br>
