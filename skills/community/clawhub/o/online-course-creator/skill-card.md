## Description: <br>
Generates online course outlines, video scripts, quizzes, assignments, and marketing materials from a course topic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lvjunjie-byte](https://clawhub.ai/user/lvjunjie-byte) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Educators, content creators, knowledge publishers, and training teams use this skill to draft complete online course packages, including lesson structure, teaching scripts, assessments, and promotional copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may include testimonials, urgency claims, guarantees, discounts, certificates, or earnings-style claims that require review before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit promotional materials for accuracy, substantiation, policy compliance, and jurisdiction-specific advertising requirements before using them publicly. <br>
Risk: Running the included example writes a local JSON export file. <br>
Mitigation: Run examples in an appropriate workspace and review generated files before sharing or committing them. <br>
