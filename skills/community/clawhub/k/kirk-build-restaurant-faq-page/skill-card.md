## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common restaurant questions. Use when working on FAQ content for restaurant owners, food delivery businesses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Restaurant owners, food delivery businesses, and marketing teams use this skill to draft customer-facing FAQ answers for common restaurant questions. It supports FAQ content work that should be reviewed for accuracy before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated restaurant FAQ content may contain incorrect or outdated facts about hours, delivery terms, allergens, refunds, or policies. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as a draft and verify business facts before publishing or using it in customer-facing channels. <br>
Risk: An agent with CMS or posting integrations could publish draft FAQ copy without review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep publication user-approved when connecting this skill to external posting or content-management workflows. <br>
