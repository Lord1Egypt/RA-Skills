## Description: <br>
Guides structured help for Cozy Game Planner using clear templates, checks, and safe defaults (category: Game Genres). <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abdullah944](https://clawhub.ai/user/abdullah944) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to turn cozy game planning goals into concise plans, numbered checklists, next actions, and editable draft copy while avoiding secrets and irreversible actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste secrets or private personal data into planning prompts. <br>
Mitigation: The skill instructs users not to provide secrets and recommends keeping personally identifiable information minimal. <br>
Risk: Planning guidance could be mistaken for actions to execute without review. <br>
Mitigation: The skill favors factual, reversible checklists and short next-action lists over irreversible or hidden execution steps. <br>
