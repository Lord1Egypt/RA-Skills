This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
HVAC owners and field service managers use this skill to draft service comparison landing pages with offer positioning, pricing cues, and trust signals. It supports first-draft copy and page-structure decisions that should be reviewed before publication. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated landing-page copy may include inaccurate pricing claims, unsupported trust signals, or images that are not ready for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy, pricing claims, trust signals, and images before publishing or using them in customer-facing campaigns. <br>
Risk: The artifact description is truncated, which may leave some intended scope unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the business goal, audience, constraints, and deliverable before relying on the generated page draft. <br>
