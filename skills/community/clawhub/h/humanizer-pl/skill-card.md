## Description: <br>
Rewrites Polish text to remove common AI-writing patterns while preserving the intended meaning, tone, and voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alexwoo-awso](https://clawhub.ai/user/alexwoo-awso) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and agents use this skill to revise Polish prose that sounds generated, promotional, generic, or overly structured. It is useful when polishing drafts, document edits, and review workflows that need more natural Polish wording. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The rewrite may unintentionally change meaning, emphasis, or tone in important documents. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for a preview or diff and review the revised text before publishing or relying on it. <br>
Risk: Using rewritten text to hide AI assistance may conflict with school, workplace, publication, or platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the applicable disclosure and content policies for the document's context. <br>
Risk: The skill operates on user-provided text, which may contain sensitive or restricted content. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only on text you are allowed to revise and share with the agent environment. <br>
