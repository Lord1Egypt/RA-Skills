## Description: <br>
Gather scattered tax-time paperwork quickly into a clear staging board with a missing-items checklist, folder map, and handoff cover sheet, without giving tax or legal advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
People preparing tax-season paperwork use this skill to organize scattered paper and digital records into a staging board, folder map, missing-items checklist, and handoff cover sheet for self-prep or professional review. It is for document staging only and directs tax, legal, accounting, and investment questions to official guidance or qualified professionals. <br>
