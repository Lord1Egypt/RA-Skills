## Description: <br>
Generates structured investment-attraction daily reports for a specified location using current public web information, with sourced policy, industry opportunity, task, and policy-toolkit sections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[daviddatamining](https://clawhub.ai/user/daviddatamining) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Economic development, investment promotion, and business development teams use this skill to generate daily Markdown briefings for a city or district. The report summarizes current public policy signals, local industry opportunities, concrete outreach tasks, and available policy tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reports may contain outdated, incomplete, or incorrectly interpreted public information. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated facts, source labels, and publication dates before using the report for investment, outreach, or policy decisions. <br>
