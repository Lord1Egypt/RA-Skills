## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated content may include inaccurate claims, unsupported statistics, or misleading competitive assertions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims, statistics, examples, and competitor references against approved sources before publishing. <br>
Risk: Regulated, HR, financial, public-company, or region-specific content may require legal or compliance review. <br>
Mitigation: Route sensitive topics through the appropriate legal, compliance, communications, or regional review process before use. <br>
Risk: Drafts may expose confidential, customer-identifying, or unapproved internal information if that information is provided as input. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymized examples and exclude confidential or customer-identifying data unless sharing is explicitly approved. <br>
