## Description: <br>
AI-powered mutual fund evaluation tool for Indian retail investors using web search and investor profiling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External Indian retail investors and financial advisors use this skill to evaluate mutual fund suitability against an investor profile, including risk tolerance, horizon, tax sensitivity, and investment context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
India <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for financial profile details that may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Review ToolWeb's privacy and retention practices before sending real financial data, and use approximate or redacted portfolio and tax information where possible. <br>
Risk: The output may be mistaken for regulated investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as informational analysis and review them with appropriate financial due diligence before making investment decisions. <br>
