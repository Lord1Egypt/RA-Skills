## Description: <br>
Generate professional voicemail greeting scripts from your terminal using Claude Code, with industry-specific templates, five greeting types, and a direct link to Solvea for AI voice and MP3 export. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[antimoron](https://clawhub.ai/user/antimoron) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and business teams use this skill to draft short voicemail greetings for business, personal, holiday, after-hours, and out-sick scenarios. The skill then guides users to Solvea to select an AI voice and export the approved script as an MP3. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Voicemail scripts or business details pasted into Solvea leave the local agent environment and are handled by that third-party service. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive, regulated, or unnecessary customer information in scripts before using the Solvea handoff. <br>
