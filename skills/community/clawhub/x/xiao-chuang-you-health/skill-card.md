## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language wellness skill that activates for diet, recipes, tea drinks, sleep, daily routines, traditional movement, acupoint self-care, and related general wellness topics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to receive general Chinese-language lifestyle guidance about seasonal eating, recipes, tea drinks, sleep routines, traditional movement, acupoint self-care, and emotional regulation. It is for general wellness support, not medical diagnosis, prescription drug recommendations, or urgent care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: General wellness suggestions may be mistaken for medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for general wellness guidance and consult a clinician for severe, persistent, worsening, pregnancy-related, chronic-condition, medication-related, or unusual symptoms. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad everyday health or food terms. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's intent and personal health assumptions before relying on specific suggestions. <br>
Risk: Traditional diet, acupoint, or movement guidance may not fit every person's condition. <br>
Mitigation: Keep recommendations non-diagnostic, include relevant cautions, and avoid prescription drug recommendations. <br>
