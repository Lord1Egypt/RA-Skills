## Description: <br>
Creates professional resumes from chat conversations, with support for templates, ATS optimization, cover letters, interview prep, and local-only data storage. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mkpareek0315](https://clawhub.ai/user/mkpareek0315) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career-focused users use this skill to build, edit, tailor, review, and export resumes and cover letters while keeping profile data in local files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores resume details, contact information, work history, and cover letters locally. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when local storage under ~/.openclaw/resume-builder is acceptable, and remove or protect those files according to the user's privacy needs. <br>
Risk: Generated resume examples and suggested metrics could become inaccurate claims if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated achievement, metric, and cover-letter claim, and replace examples with numbers and facts the user can substantiate. <br>
