## Description: <br>
Multi-AI gateway. 50+ models: chat, image, video, TTS, music, search. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external users use this skill to list SkillBoss/HeyBoss models, send prompts to selected chat, image, video, TTS, STT, music, and search models, and retrieve text or media URLs from the provider gateway. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts and task inputs are sent to the external SkillBoss/HeyBoss provider gateway. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated, revocable API key and avoid submitting secrets, private files, regulated data, or sensitive prompts unless the provider's data-handling terms have been reviewed. <br>
