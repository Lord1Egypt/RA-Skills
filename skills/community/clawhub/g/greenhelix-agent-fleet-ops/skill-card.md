Mitigation: Review every snippet before use, verify endpoints, run with limited test accounts and scoped API keys, and add dry-run, approval, rollback, and kill-switch controls. <br>

Mitigation: Avoid sending sensitive tool inputs into audit or message records unless they are minimized, approved, and governed by the deployment's data-handling policy. <br>
