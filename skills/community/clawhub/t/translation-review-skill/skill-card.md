## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The trigger wording may activate for broader planning requests than translation review. <br>
Mitigation: Use or revise the skill around source/target translation comparison, terminology, fluency, and fidelity review. <br>
Risk: Users may paste sensitive or personally identifying text into review prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the artifact guidance to avoid secrets and keep personally identifying information minimal. <br>
