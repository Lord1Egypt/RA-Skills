# Labor Dispute Evidence Pack

This reference helps turn a labor dispute check into an evidence plan. It is informational support, not legal advice.

## Universal Proof Goals

Most China labor disputes need evidence for:

1. Employment relationship
2. Work period and contract term
3. Wage base and payment history
4. Employer action or violation
5. Employee response and timeline
6. Loss amount or requested compensation

## Common Evidence Types

Strong evidence:

- Signed labor contract
- Termination notice or written HR communication
- Payslips, bank salary records, tax records
- Attendance records, shift schedules, overtime approval records
- Social insurance and housing fund records

Medium evidence:

- Work email, enterprise chat logs, task systems
- Performance review records
- Meeting notes confirmed by participants
- Screenshots with visible time, sender, and context

Weak or supporting evidence:

- Personal notes
- Witness names without written statement
- Photos without time/context
- Verbal descriptions with no supporting record

## Termination Pack

Collect:

- Start date, end date, contract term
- Termination reason and notice method
- Wage base for the previous 12 months
- HR or manager communication
- Handover records
- Performance warnings or absence of warnings

Output:

- Lawful vs potentially unlawful termination issue
- N, N+1, or 2N discussion with assumptions
- Missing facts that change the result
- Negotiation or arbitration preparation checklist

## Overtime Pack

Collect:

- Date-by-date overtime hours
- Workday/rest day/statutory holiday classification
- Whether compensatory rest was arranged
- Attendance, shift, approval, chat, or task records
- Wage base and pay cycle

Output:

- Formula table by overtime type
- Time-limit warning for older claims
- Evidence gaps that may reduce recovery

## Probation Pack

Collect:

- Contract length
- Probation length
- Probation wage and regular wage
- Hiring criteria or performance criteria
- Termination reason and proof

Output:

- Maximum legal probation comparison
- Wage ratio check
- Termination procedure risk notes
- Back-pay or unlawful termination discussion when applicable

## Arbitration Outline

Use this structure:

1. Applicant and employer information
2. Claims and requested amount
3. Facts and timeline
4. Calculation basis
5. Evidence list
6. Missing evidence and risk notes
7. Filing deadline and local consultation reminder

Never promise a result. Say which facts and evidence would likely matter most.
