## Description: <br>
Helps users optimize content for AI search engines, earn citations from LLMs, and appear in AI-generated answers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Mrhuang09](https://clawhub.ai/user/Mrhuang09) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, content strategists, and site owners use this skill to audit AI visibility, improve extractability and citation-worthiness, and decide how to handle AI crawler access. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local product-marketing context may contain secrets or sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Review local context files before use and avoid exposing credentials, private customer data, or confidential strategy in generated recommendations. <br>
Risk: Suggested robots.txt changes can alter how public content is crawled, trained on, or cited by AI systems. <br>
Mitigation: Have the site owner review crawler access changes and align them with the organization's content protection and AI visibility goals before publishing. <br>
