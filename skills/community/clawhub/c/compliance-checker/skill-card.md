## Description: <br>
Audit ecommerce listings, ad copy, and creator content against TikTok Shop policies to catch violations before they trigger penalties. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators, marketplace teams, and creator managers use this skill to pre-check TikTok Shop listings, promotional copy, creator deliverables, and violation responses before publication or appeal. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce policy-review guidance that may be outdated or incomplete for a specific TikTok Shop market or product category. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current TikTok Shop rules, category requirements, and local advertising law before acting on high-risk findings. <br>
Risk: Users may treat the audit output as legal advice for regulated claims, restricted products, or appeal strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a compliance-review aid and route high-risk categories, substantiation questions, and appeals through qualified legal or marketplace specialists. <br>
