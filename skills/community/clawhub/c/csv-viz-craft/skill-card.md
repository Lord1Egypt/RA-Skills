## Use Case: <br>
Developers and teams use this skill to turn pasted CSV or TSV data into an interactive, shareable HTML table or dashboard with sorting, search, filtering, pagination, summaries, comparison, chart, and print-oriented modes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shared generated HTML files may expose the CSV contents embedded in or rendered by the file. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with data you are allowed to transform and share; redact personal, confidential, or regulated data before distributing generated HTML. <br>
