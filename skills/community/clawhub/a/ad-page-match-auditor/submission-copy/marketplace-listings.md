## Long Description

Ad-Page Match Auditor is a no-code AI agent skill for improving paid ad conversion paths.

Most paid campaigns are reviewed as separate pieces: the ad, the landing page, the form, the booking flow, the creative, and the report. This skill reviews them as one path from paid click to lead, call, quote request, booking, or appointment.

Use it to audit Meta Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads, local-service ads, clinic campaigns, appointment campaigns, and agency landing pages. It identifies message-match gaps, unclear offers, weak proof, CTA mismatch, mobile friction, overlong forms, generic booking flows, and low-confidence measurement plans.

The skill then suggests 2-3 practical variants to test next, with hypotheses, headlines, CTAs, page changes, and measurement targets.

Built by FunnelFinch, the AI-first ad-page optimization company for service businesses.
