## Description: <br>
Supports pre-investment, M&A, and incubation due diligence for cultural and creative businesses by evaluating asset quality, IP value, monetization, compliance, finance, team capability, and related risks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External investors, acquirers, incubators, and business analysts use this skill to structure due diligence on cultural and creative companies such as MCNs, short-drama studios, comics teams, UGC platforms, and content communities. It guides collection, cross-checking, scoring, risk review, and report generation for business decision support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be used with confidential company, financial, contract, platform, or personal records. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when authorized to review the target company, redact unnecessary sensitive identifiers, and store submitted materials securely. <br>
Risk: Generated ratings or investment recommendations can be mistaken for final legal, financial, or business advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat all outputs as draft decision support and require review by qualified legal, financial, and business professionals. <br>
Risk: Incomplete or single-source data can produce misleading due-diligence conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check claims across platform backends, contracts, bank records, interviews, and public data, and mark unavailable data instead of fabricating values. <br>
