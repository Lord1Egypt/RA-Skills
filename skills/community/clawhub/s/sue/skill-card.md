## Description: <br>
A comprehensive AI agent skill for anyone considering or facing a lawsuit that helps evaluate whether suing is worth it, understand litigation from filing to resolution, prepare a case, navigate settlement negotiations, and make informed decisions at each stage of a legal dispute. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIstack](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIstack) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users considering, facing, or enforcing a civil lawsuit use this skill to evaluate whether litigation is worth pursuing, understand litigation stages, organize evidence, assess settlement offers, navigate small claims issues, and think through judgment collection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake general civil lawsuit guidance for jurisdiction-specific legal advice about deadlines, filing requirements, settlement decisions, service rules, or court procedure. <br>
Mitigation: Check official court resources or consult a licensed attorney before relying on the guidance for legal deadlines, filings, settlement decisions, service, or jurisdiction-specific procedure. <br>
Risk: Users may include sensitive legal facts or dispute details in prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive legal details unless comfortable doing so, and keep identifying or privileged information out of prompts when possible. <br>
