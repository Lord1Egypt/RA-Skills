## Description: <br>
Generate comprehensive website style guides and design systems from URLs, screenshots, and existing documentation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TomsTools11](https://clawhub.ai/user/TomsTools11) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, developers, and brand teams use this skill to turn websites, screenshots, and existing documentation into a structured style guide covering brand identity, typography, UI components, layout, accessibility, resources, and changelog content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential brand documents, internal URLs, or sensitive screenshots could be exposed or summarized if supplied as inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Use public or authorized materials unless sensitive processing is appropriate for the workspace. <br>
Risk: The generated PDF may contain inferred design attributes or placeholders when source materials are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated PDF for accuracy and completeness before sharing or adopting it as a design source of truth. <br>
