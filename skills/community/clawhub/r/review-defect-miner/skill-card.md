## Description: <br>
Extract and cluster product defect signals from ecommerce reviews to prioritize quality fixes and address low ratings or negative sentiment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, quality, support, and content teams use this skill to analyze low-star reviews, comments, support tickets, and return notes. It turns recurring dissatisfaction signals into prioritized defect clusters, evidence snippets, messaging mitigations, and a short execution plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Review exports, support tickets, and return notes may contain personal or confidential customer information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized data and redact unnecessary personal or confidential details before analysis. <br>
Risk: License evidence needs review because the server lists MIT-0 while the artifact text states CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 non-commercial terms with separate commercial licensing. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the governing release license and commercial-use rights before publishing or deploying the skill. <br>
