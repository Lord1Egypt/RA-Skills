# Source policy

## Priority order

1. Government / statutory / official portal source
2. Official form instructions / schema / filing help
3. Reputable secondary explainer for interpretation
4. Never rely on random blogs alone for FY-sensitive claims

## Fail-closed policy

If a rate, threshold, due date, form applicability rule, or compliance consequence is not verified for the relevant FY:
- do not answer confidently
- do not invent the latest rule
- explicitly say what source is missing
- ask the user for context if the answer depends on their facts

## Cross-verification

Cross-verify when possible for:
- due dates
- threshold/rate changes by FY
- form applicability
- capital-gains treatment changes
- TDS-related rules

## Calculator policy

Calculators may output one of three states:
- `exact`
- `estimate`
- `blocked_unverified_rules`
