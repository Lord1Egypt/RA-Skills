## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Texas-specific guidance about moving, living, visiting, driving, housing, taxes, weather readiness, schools, healthcare, and state or local logistics. It helps classify the user's Texas mode and produce practical checklists, tradeoffs, and source-backed next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional local memory can retain personal relocation, family, vehicle, health, budget, or timeline context. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm what will be saved before enabling memory, avoid storing precise addresses, identifiers, medical specifics, credentials, or payment information, and delete ~/texas/memory.md when persistent context is no longer wanted. <br>
Risk: Texas rules and local workflows can change or vary by city, county, appraisal district, school district, utility territory, or flood zone. <br>
Mitigation: Use official state or local sources for precise compliance steps and label which guidance is statewide versus locally verified. <br>
