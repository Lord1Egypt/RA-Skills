## Description: <br>
Professional IT Support Career Roadmap Platform that generates personalized learning paths for Desktop Support Engineers based on experience, skills, and career goals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
IT support professionals, team leads, and training planners use this skill to generate personalized Desktop Support Engineer learning roadmaps from experience, skills, certifications, and career goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Career background, skills, goals, timestamps, and session or user identifiers are sent to an external service operator. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymous or minimal identifiers and avoid sending confidential employer details, HR records, or unnecessary personal information. <br>
Risk: Generated learning roadmaps may be incomplete or mismatched to a user's actual workplace requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against organizational role expectations, approved training paths, and current certification requirements before relying on them. <br>
