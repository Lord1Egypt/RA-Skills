## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes startup, crypto, fundraising, treasury, legal, tax, investment, and wellbeing guidance that may be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational coaching only and consult qualified professionals and current sources before making financial, legal, compliance, tax, investment, or mental health decisions. <br>
Risk: Some market, funding, crypto, and ecosystem details are time-sensitive and may become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify time-sensitive claims against current primary sources before relying on them for planning or decisions. <br>
