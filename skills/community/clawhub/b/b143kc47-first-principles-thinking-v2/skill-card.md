## Description: <br>
Reason from fundamentals with in-session claim ledgers, mechanism maps, assumption checks, Fermi estimates, evidence grounding, verification questions, red-team critique, and structured brainstorming. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product teams, researchers, and decision makers use this skill to break complex decisions, diagnoses, critiques, explanations, syntheses, and brainstorming tasks into explicit claims, assumptions, mechanisms, falsifiers, and grounded next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad implicit invocation can add first-principles process to tasks where the user expected a direct mechanical answer. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the documented skip signals, keep Quick depth compact for reversible choices, and honor explicit user requests to skip analysis. <br>
Risk: Reasoning outputs may rely on assumptions, reported claims, or external tools when the task requires evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Keep claims in the in-session ledger, separate verified facts from assumptions and unknowns, and use approved evidence-gathering or calculation tools only when the user's task requires them. <br>
