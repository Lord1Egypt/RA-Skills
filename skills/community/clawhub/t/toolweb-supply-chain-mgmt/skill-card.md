## Description: <br>
Professional platform that generates personalized Supply Chain Management career roadmaps based on user assessment data and experience level. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Supply chain professionals, organizations, and agents use this skill to generate personalized supply chain career roadmaps and retrieve specialization and learning-path catalogs from the documented API. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Roadmap personalization can involve privacy-relevant assessment data, employer details, session identifiers, and optional user IDs. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the provider's privacy practices and avoid sending sensitive employer details, personal identifiers, or stable user IDs unless personalization requires them and the endpoint is trusted. <br>
Risk: The skill documents paid API tiers, so automated or high-volume usage can create cost exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the selected plan, quota, and approval path before making repeated or organization-wide calls. <br>
