## Use Case: <br>
External users can ask for trending bento restaurant shortlists, visit-planning guidance, and route suggestions filtered by city, budget, cuisine, queue tolerance, hygiene score, accessibility, and dining preferences. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Restaurant prices, hours, queue times, hygiene ratings, and popularity can change before a visit. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details with live sources or the venue before making travel or purchase decisions. <br>
Risk: Some venues may enforce photography restrictions, reservations, minimum spend, or other on-site rules. <br>
Mitigation: Check venue rules in advance and follow staff instructions on arrival. <br>
