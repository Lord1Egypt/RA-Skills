## Description: <br>
Audit, track, and optimize an organization's software licenses by identifying waste, compliance risks, renewal timing, and vendor negotiation opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Operations, finance, procurement, and IT teams use this skill to structure software license audits, find unused or duplicate subscriptions, prepare renewal calendars, and draft cost or compliance remediation guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: License audits may expose sensitive business data such as spend, contracts, employee-seat assignments, and usage records. <br>
Mitigation: Decide what data the agent may access before use, provide only the minimum necessary inputs, and keep generated reports within approved business systems. <br>
Risk: Generated cancellation, seat-removal, tier-change, or compliance conclusions may be incomplete or unsuitable for the organization. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as recommendations and require explicit human approval before changing subscriptions, removing seats, changing tiers, or relying on compliance conclusions. <br>
