## Use Case: <br>
External advertisers, growth marketers, and ad operations teams use this skill to triage campaign questions, infer KPI assumptions, and produce prioritized action plans for major ads platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ads recommendations can affect budget, bidding, targeting, and campaign status if acted on without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review assumptions and validate recommendations against account context before making campaign changes. <br>
Risk: Requests may include sensitive account or performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the account and performance details needed for the analysis, especially before passing handoff payloads to downstream execution skills. <br>
Risk: The skill may infer KPIs or platform context when the user provides incomplete inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Mark assumptions clearly and request missing critical fields before final recommendations when uncertainty is material. <br>
