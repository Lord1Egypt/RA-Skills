## Description: <br>
Generates comprehensive interview question sets tailored to specific job roles, including behavioral, technical, and situational questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Hiring managers, recruiters, and HR professionals use this skill to generate role-specific interview question banks with behavioral, situational, technical, culture-fit, scoring, and interview-structure guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated interview questions or scoring guidance may be biased, inaccessible, legally inappropriate, or insufficiently relevant to a specific role. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt generated content against employment-law, accessibility, bias, and role-relevance requirements before using it in real hiring decisions. <br>
