## Description: <br>
Chinese Naming Master (天衡命名宗师) helps generate and analyze culturally rich Chinese personal names using traditional philosophy, I Ching, Five Elements, classical poetry, phonology, and psychology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[longge0516](https://clawhub.ai/user/longge0516) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Chinese personal naming tasks, including newborn naming, adult name changes, name analysis, and generational naming constrained by family genealogy. The skill collects required birth details before producing ranked candidate names with cultural, phonological, and traditional naming analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users for birth date/time and optional family details, which can be personal or sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to share only the minimum details needed, avoid parent names unless required for conflict checks, and treat traditional astrology-style analysis as cultural guidance rather than factual prediction. <br>
