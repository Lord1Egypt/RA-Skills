## Description: <br>
Generates a structured curriculum with learning objectives, lesson content, presentation slides, and assessment questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Educators, course creators, and corporate trainers use this skill to draft complete online courses with modules, learning objectives, lesson content, slide content, and assessment questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated educational content and slides may be inaccurate, unsuitable for the intended audience, or not ready for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review the curriculum, slides, and assessments for accuracy, licensing, and learner suitability before external publication or use. <br>
