## Description: <br>
Provides recommendation lists, check-in routes, and planning guidance for designer-toy shops, pop-ups, and collectible experiences. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mike47512](https://clawhub.ai/user/mike47512) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to find designer-toy and collectible destinations, filter options by style, price, authenticity, availability, services, and photo rules, and plan shopping or check-in routes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broader travel or itinerary requests where the shopping or collectible context is not relevant. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking about designer toys, collectibles, pop-ups, or related shopping before applying its recommendations. <br>
Risk: Venue details such as hours, queues, pricing, reservations, and photo or fitting rules may change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current venue information and on-site rules before visiting, purchasing, or making reservations. <br>
