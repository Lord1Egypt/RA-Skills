## Description: <br>
Email writer and sender for OpenClaw that helps draft, reply to, and send emails through browser-based Gmail, Outlook, QQ Mail, 163 Mail, and 126 Mail sessions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobewin](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobewin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users with an OpenClaw browser session use this skill to compose or reply to email, including setting recipients, subjects, body text, and tone across supported webmail providers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can operate a logged-in webmail session and click Send in real email accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for drafting first, review the account, recipient, subject, and body, and require explicit confirmation before any send action. <br>
