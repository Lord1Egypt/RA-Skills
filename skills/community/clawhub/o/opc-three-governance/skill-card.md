## Use Case: <br>
External users and business operators use this skill to structure governance discussions, match business questions to mentor, executive-board, or expert-committee perspectives, and produce decision recommendations, risk notes, action plans, meeting minutes, decision logs, or skill reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security summary flags under-scoped guidance about using personal government relationships to obtain approvals, public resources, and official support. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with explicit user opt-in for governance analysis, and rewrite or constrain government-relations guidance to require lawful public channels and compliance review. <br>
Risk: Governance analysis may involve personal, confidential, regulated, or trade-secret information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive information unless a protected handling process is in place, and minimize business details to what is needed for the analysis. <br>
