## Description: <br>
Helps map regulatory obligations, controls, owners, evidence, review cadence, verification gaps, and attorney-review triggers for a product, business unit, or jurisdiction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Compliance counsel, in-house legal teams, risk managers, and startup operators use this skill to structure regulatory obligation maps, control trackers, evidence checklists, review calendars, and verification gap lists. It supports compliance planning and issue spotting, not legal advice or jurisdiction-specific legal conclusions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Jurisdiction-specific obligations, deadlines, regulator names, license requirements, or timelines may be incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning drafts and verify every legal authority, filing requirement, deadline, citation, and strategic decision against current authoritative sources with qualified counsel. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake structured compliance workflow output for legal advice or a legal conclusion. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the skill's legal disclaimer, label uncertain items as verification gaps, and route attorney-review triggers to qualified counsel before relying on the output. <br>
