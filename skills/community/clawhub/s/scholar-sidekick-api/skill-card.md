## Description: <br>
Scholar Sidekick resolves scholarly identifiers into formatted citations and bibliography exports, and checks retraction, open-access, and citation-verification status through a documented REST API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mlava](https://clawhub.ai/user/mlava) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and research workflows use this skill when they already have scholarly identifiers or claimed citations and need formatted citations, bibliography exports, retraction checks, open-access status, or citation-verification results. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends scholarly identifiers, bibliography inputs, and claimed citation details to Scholar Sidekick's external API. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting confidential or unpublished research material unless external processing by Scholar Sidekick is acceptable. <br>
