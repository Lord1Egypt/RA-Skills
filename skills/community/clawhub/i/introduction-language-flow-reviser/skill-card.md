## Description: <br>
Revise complete English research-paper Introduction sections using a p3-derived language-and-writing checklist for verb tense choices, sentence linkage, transition signals, passive/active choices, and paragraphing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, editors, and academic writers use this skill to revise complete English Introduction sections while preserving research content, citations, claims, terminology, and evidence strength. It produces a traceable method report and a clean revised Introduction for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill writes Markdown outputs and could overwrite existing files if filenames or output locations are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm exact output paths before running the skill, or request inline output when file creation is not desired. <br>
Risk: Unpublished or sensitive manuscript text may be included in the revision workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential text unless the execution environment is approved for that content, and ask the agent to minimize saved copies when needed. <br>
