## Description: <br>
A privacy-first, consent-based self-improvement skill for AI agents that records learnings, errors, and best practices with sanitization, duplicate detection, and user-confirmed skill draft generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hjfl888](https://clawhub.ai/user/hjfl888) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to capture lessons, errors, best practices, task evaluations, and recurring workflow patterns as local learning records. It can summarize those records and generate reusable skill drafts for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Learning records, exports, or generated skill drafts may contain private project details despite sanitization. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated records, drafts, and exports for secrets or private details before saving, sharing, installing, or publishing them. <br>
Risk: Installing or publishing generated skills goes beyond the skill's stated local learning-record boundary. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation and review or scan generated skill content before installation or publication. <br>
