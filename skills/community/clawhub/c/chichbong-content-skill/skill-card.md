## Description: <br>
Reads one public 24h.chichbong.com product page and produces a compact Vietnamese Zalo Shop product block with a short intro, suggested markup price, safer title, and thumbnail link. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Shop operators and listing writers use this skill to convert a single 24h.chichbong.com product URL into factual Vietnamese product-listing copy, a safer title, a 30-50% marked-up suggested price, and a thumbnail reference. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product facts, thumbnail, or original price may be unavailable or incorrect if the product page cannot be opened or fallback search results are stale. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the product facts, thumbnail, and suggested price markup against the source page and shop or advertising compliance rules before publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated listing copy may overstate product benefits or include sensitive advertising claims when the source page uses risky wording. <br>
Mitigation: Keep only page-supported facts, apply the safety filter, and replace medical, treatment, absolute, or other sensitive claims with neutral ecommerce wording. <br>
