## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to format social content for multiple services and prepare posts or posting commands for MoltX, Twitter/X, Discord, and Telegram. Teams considering paid cross-posting services should review posting authority and approval controls before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks agents to use credentials and account tokens that can grant posting authority on external platforms. <br>
Mitigation: Use only accounts approved for delegated posting, keep credentials out of chat and shell history, and rotate credentials after testing. <br>
Risk: The skill describes registering a paid external posting service that other agents may invoke. <br>
Mitigation: Register the service only after confirming who can invoke it and enforcing explicit approval for every post. <br>
