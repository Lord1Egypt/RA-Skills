## Description: <br>
Provides a JSON data bundle for a parody SBTI personality quiz with 20 questions, 5 joke personality results, and 15 scoring dimensions for custom frontend implementations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and entertainment creators use this data bundle to build custom parody personality quiz frontends and sample scoring logic without adding executable skill code. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crude or insulting joke labels may be inappropriate in public, school, or workplace contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt quiz text and personality labels before public or institutional use. <br>
Risk: Frontend features added around the data bundle, such as analytics, sharing, uploads, or network requests, can introduce separate privacy obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Assess those added features independently and avoid collecting or transmitting answers unless there is a clear user-facing purpose and consent. <br>
