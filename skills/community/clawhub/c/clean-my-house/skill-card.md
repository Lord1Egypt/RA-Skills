## Description: <br>
Helps plan, organize, and guide household cleaning tasks room by room, including quick tidy-ups, deep cleaning, and recurring routines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[know-hub](https://clawhub.ai/user/know-hub) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Household users use this skill to plan quick tidies, standard cleaning sessions, deep cleans, guest preparation, and recurring room-by-room maintenance routines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional helper script is executable code even though evidence indicates it only prints checklists. <br>
Mitigation: Review the script before running it and use it only when text checklist output is desired. <br>
Risk: Cleaning plans may be too broad or physically demanding for a user's time, energy, pets, children, or mobility constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for constraints first and prefer smaller, time-boxed checklists when the user is overwhelmed or has limited capacity. <br>
