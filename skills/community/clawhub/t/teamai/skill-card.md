## Description: <br>
Klaus Team helps WinClaw users assemble and run a configurable AI software-development team with product, design, DevOps, backend, frontend, integration, and QA roles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[klaus-long](https://clawhub.ai/user/klaus-long) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and builders use this skill inside WinClaw to plan, prototype, implement, test, preview, and package software projects through selectable AI specialist roles. It is best suited to multi-step development workflows rather than simple one-off chat tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated development commands, service startup behavior, and packaged ZIP outputs may affect local projects or machines if run without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated DevOps commands, startup steps, service behavior, and ZIP contents before executing them on important repositories or systems. <br>
Risk: Generated plans, code, tests, or review reports may be incomplete or incorrect for the target project. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's pause, inspection, independent role editing, QA, and repair-loop workflow to review outputs before accepting or deploying them. <br>
