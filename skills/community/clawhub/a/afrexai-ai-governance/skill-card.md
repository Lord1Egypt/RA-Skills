This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
CTOs, CISOs, compliance officers, and operations leaders use this skill to draft and review internal AI governance programs, including acceptable use policies, vendor review, data handling controls, regulatory mapping, incident response, and board reporting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Governance and compliance templates may be incomplete, outdated, or mismatched to a specific organization or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt the templates with qualified legal, compliance, security, and business owners before adoption. <br>
Risk: Template thresholds, approval paths, and vendor contract requirements may not match an organization's risk appetite or operating model. <br>
Mitigation: Tailor numeric thresholds, authority levels, data classifications, and contract clauses to approved internal policy before use. <br>
Risk: Some linked resources are promotional or paid. <br>
Mitigation: Treat external links as optional third-party resources and review their terms, cost, and fit before relying on them. <br>
