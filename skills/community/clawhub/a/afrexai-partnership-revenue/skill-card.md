## Description: <br>
Systematic framework to identify, qualify, and scale revenue through integration, referral, reseller, and strategic partnerships. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External business development, partnerships, and revenue teams use this skill to research potential partners, score partner fit, draft outreach, structure partner programs, and manage partnership performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Partner research and outreach can include inaccurate, outdated, or unsupported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify company facts, cite sources where possible, and review generated claims before using them in outreach or partner materials. <br>
Risk: Research briefs and outreach drafts can collect or expose unnecessary personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only lawful, permitted sources and minimize personal data to what is necessary for the partnership workflow. <br>
Risk: Draft emails, deal terms, or agreement checklists can be mistaken for final business or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human review generated emails, deal structures, and agreement language before external use. <br>
