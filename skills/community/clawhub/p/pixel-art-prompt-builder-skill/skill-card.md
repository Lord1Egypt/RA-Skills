## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts or pasted notes could include secrets or sensitive personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide passwords, keys, wallets, OTPs, session tokens, or unnecessary personal data; keep inputs to owned notes and minimal context. <br>
Risk: Generated plans, checklists, or draft copy may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before use, keep actions factual and reversible, and avoid remote installers or chained download-and-run commands. <br>
