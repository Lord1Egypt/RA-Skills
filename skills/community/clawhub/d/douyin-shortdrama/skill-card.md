## Description: <br>
Provides guidance for organizing and summarizing public Douyin short drama channel and episode page information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mike47512](https://clawhub.ai/user/mike47512) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to summarize public Douyin short drama pages, including titles, update status, episode lists, cast, popularity signals, comments, ranking entries, and genre distribution. It is intended for lightweight analysis and notifications from manually reviewed public pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automated or high-volume collection could violate Douyin platform rules or rate limits. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for lightweight manual review and summaries of public pages, control request frequency, and follow Douyin's terms. <br>
Risk: Requests to download media, call private interfaces, reverse engineer APIs, or bypass platform controls would exceed the documented skill boundary. <br>
Mitigation: Limit outputs to summaries and analysis of public page information; decline download, interface-calling, reverse-engineering, and bypass workflows. <br>
