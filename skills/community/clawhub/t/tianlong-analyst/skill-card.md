## Description: <br>
Analyzes requirements with the Tianlong engine to surface root needs, assess complexity, challenge assumptions, and produce structured action recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, developers, and individual decision-makers use this skill to clarify ambiguous needs, evaluate product or technical choices, and turn early ideas into structured analysis and next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business, career, investment, or strategy recommendations may be incomplete or misleading if treated as authoritative advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as decision support and independently verify important conclusions before acting. <br>
Risk: Prompt-only analysis can overstate confidence when the input requirement is vague or missing context. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated assumptions, add missing constraints, and rerun or revise the analysis before using it for planning. <br>
