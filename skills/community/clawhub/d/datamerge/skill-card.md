## Description: <br>
Enrich companies and find B2B contacts using the DataMerge MCP server for firmographic data, validated contact details, lookalike discovery, corporate hierarchy data, and target-account list management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[poolside-ventures](https://clawhub.ai/user/poolside-ventures) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, growth, and business-development teams use this skill to enrich company records, find B2B contacts, discover similar companies, inspect corporate hierarchy, and manage target-account lists through DataMerge. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can retrieve business contact emails and phone numbers, which creates privacy and compliance obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for approved B2B enrichment workflows and confirm privacy, consent, anti-spam, and workplace data policy compliance before requesting contact data. <br>
Risk: The skill can spend DataMerge credits and write list changes to a DataMerge account. <br>
Mitigation: Check credit balance before large jobs, request phone numbers only when explicitly needed, use duplicate controls such as skip_if_exists, and review list changes before writing them. <br>
