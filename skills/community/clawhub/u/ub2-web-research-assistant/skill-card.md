## Description: <br>
Conducts structured web research by querying multiple sources, evaluating credibility, synthesizing findings, and producing a cited summary. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[underbench2-gif](https://clawhub.ai/user/underbench2-gif) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and other ClawHub users use this skill to guide an agent through structured web research, source evaluation, synthesis, citation collection, and confidence assessment for a requested topic. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Web research outputs may rely on low-quality, outdated, or misleading sources. <br>
Mitigation: Review citations, publication dates, and source quality before relying on findings. <br>
Risk: Research prompts may expose private or sensitive information to the agent environment or research tools. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive material unless the deployment environment and tools are trusted for that data. <br>
