# Hébička - Naivní Komiks

Generate comics in the distinctive naive style of **Hébička** (Heebička) - a classic Czech webcomic featuring simple, charming creatures with wholesome adventures.

## Visual Style (Based on Original Comics)

### Art Characteristics (from actual comics)
- **Black and white line drawings**: Simple pen/pencil strokes, no colors!
- **Very naive/childlike**: Truly amateur, as if drawn by a child
- **Thin uneven lines**: Hand-drawn, slightly wobbly, not confident strokes
- **Simple geometric shapes**: Ovals, circles, simple curves
- **No shading or gradients**: Pure line art with minimal hatching
- **White backgrounds**: No complex scenes, just figures and speech bubbles
- **Hand-written text**: Text is handwritten directly in speech bubbles

### Panel Layout
- **Simple rectangular panels**: Often 3x3 or 2x2 grid
- **No fancy borders**: Simple thin lines separating panels
- **Speech bubbles**: Simple ovals with tails pointing to characters
- **Text inside bubbles**: Handwritten Czech, sometimes uppercase

### Creature Design (from actual comics)
All creatures share these traits:
- **Oval/round bodies**: Like eggs or pebbles
- **Large round eyes**: Simple dots or circles with pupils
- **Tiny stick legs**: 2-4 simple lines for legs
- **No complex anatomy**: Simplified to basic shapes
- **Expressive through eyes**: Emotion shown mainly through eye size/placement

## Characters (Tvorečkové) - Detailed from Comics

### Hébička (Main Character) - UPDATED
- **Shape**: Large horizontal oval, slightly flattened bottom
- **Size**: Bigger than other creatures (adult-like)
- **Eyes**: Two large round eyes, side by side, with small dot pupils
- **Mouth**: Simple smile line, sometimes open for speech
- **LEGS**: ONE large leg underneath (not 2-4 stick legs) - like a pedestal!
- **Features**: Sometimes shown with nostrils (two small dots)
- **Expression**: Kind, wise, sometimes worried or surprised
- **Speech**: "KVAK!", "HÉB!", "HÉBÍČKU!"
- **IMPORTANT**: Can be drawn VERY SMALL when next to giant creatures (like Uintatérium)

### Rybík (The Fish/Eel) - UPDATED
- **Shape**: Long wavy eel-like body, NOT simple fish!
- **Body**: Elongated, serpentine, flexible curves
- **Eyes**: Large round eyes on sides of head
- **Fins**: Small simple fins, wavy tail
- **Expression**: Curious, worried, thoughtful
- **Environment**: Water scenes with wavy lines, underwater plants
- **Speech**: "Eee...", questions, contemplative

### Žabík (The Frog Friend / Žabinka)
- **Shape**: Similar to Hébička but smaller, more frog-like
- **Body**: More elongated than Hébička
- **Eyes**: Bulging frog eyes on top of head
- **Legs**: Longer frog legs, sometimes shown sitting
- **Color in text**: Green (implied from name)
- **Personality**: Gentle, follower

### Sviník (The Many-Legged)
- **Shape**: Pillbug/centipede - segmented oval body
- **Body segments**: 5-8 rounded segments
- **Legs**: Multiple pairs of tiny legs underneath
- **Striped pattern**: Segments shown with lines across body
- **Eyes**: Small, simple
- **Personality**: Mysterious, helpful

### Larva (The Colorful Caterpillar)
- **Shape**: Segmented caterpillar/larva body
- **Colors**: Blue and purple (modro-fialová)!
- **Body**: Round segments, can be fuzzy/spiky when scared
- **Transformation**: Can change into beautiful form (butterfly-like)
- **Expression**: Scared, sad, then happy
- **Story**: Sad about being ugly → becomes beautiful → "Děkujeme ti, Hébičko!"

### Uintatérium (The Wise One) - UPDATED
- **Shape**: Tall, dog/horse-like creature OR giant mountain-like being!
- **Size**: ENORMOUS compared to others (like a mountain)
- **Features**: Long snout, simple eyes, four legs
- **Personality**: Wise, ancient, powerful
- **Speech**: Formal, wise, helpful
- **IMPORTANT**: Scale varies - can be giant or normal sized!
- **Expression**: Thoughtful, kind, sometimes confused

### Rourka (The Sad One)
- **Shape**: Tube-shaped, simple cylinder with face
- **Personality**: Depressed, lonely, finds friends through Hébička
- **Story arc**: Sad → finds friends → becomes happy

### Other Creatures
- **Tiny round beings**: Simple circles with eyes (small background creatures)
- **Horse/dog creatures**: Four-legged, various sizes
- **Underwater creatures**: Various fish, shells, aquatic plants
- **Human-like tiny figures**: Small people in some scenes (bathtub scene)

## Drawing Technique

### Line Quality
- Thin black lines
- Some lines are doubled or sketchy (hesitant strokes)
- No clean digital lines - genuinely hand-drawn feel
- Cross-hatching for emphasis (rare)

### Eyes - Critical Detail
- **Large round eyeballs**: White circles with black dot pupils
- **Expression changes**: Eye size shows emotion (big=surprised, small=calm)
- **Eye placement**: Side by side, close together
- **Sometimes**: Just dots for eyes on distant/small creatures

### Text Integration
- Speech bubbles are part of the drawing
- Text is handwritten in block letters or cursive
- Sometimes text is outside bubbles (sound effects)
- Sound effects: "KRUO", "KVAK", "ŤAP ŤAP", "BLÉ BLÉ"

## Storytelling Style

### Panel Flow
- Left to right, top to bottom
- Simple sequential storytelling
- 9-12 panels per page typical
- First panel often establishes scene

### Text Style
- Characters talk to each other directly
- Questions and answers format common
- Sound effects integrated into panels
- Sometimes narrative text at bottom

### Themes (from actual comics)
1. **Meeting strangers**: "Ahoj tvorečku, já jsem Hébička"
2. **Problem solving**: Helping creatures with issues
3. **Scientific explanations**: How things work (universe, physics)
4. **Emotional support**: Comforting sad creatures
5. **Adventures**: Trips, walks, discoveries
6. **Comedy**: Misunderstandings, funny situations
7. **Education**: Learning about world

## Important Notes
- **NO COLORS**: Original comics are black and white line art
- **Simple backgrounds**: Usually just ground line or minimal scene
- **Focus on characters**: Creatures are the main visual element
- **Handwritten feel**: Everything looks hand-drawn, not digital
- **Speech bubbles**: Essential part of the composition

## Story Themes

1. **Friendship & Helpfulness**: Hébička always helps friends
2. **Simple Science**: Explaining physics, universe, dimensions
3. **Overcoming Sadness**: Rourka's story of finding friends
4. **Adventure**: Walks, trips, space travel
5. **Love**: Žabinka falls in love
6. **Problem Solving**: Using brains and friendship
7. **Wholesome Endings**: "All can happily kvak"

## Speech Patterns (Slovník)

### Czech Naive Language
- Diminutives everywhere: "výletíček", "děkováníčko", "chvičku", "nožičky"
- Onomatopoeia: "Kvak!", "Héb, héb!", "Ťap, ťap"
- Childlike grammar: simple sentences, exclamation marks
- Self-deprecating: "podivný človík neuměl moc kreslit"
- Enthusiasm: "Ó jak se to roztomilé!!", "Jé to bude veselé!"
- Double consonants: "kvakavé", "kulaťoučká"

### Typical Phrases
- "Kvak kvak!! :)"
- "Héb, héb!"
- "To je ale hezký příběh!"
- "Všichni budou hezky kvakat!"
- "Jé, to bylo ale snadné."
- "Ó to je ale hezký příběh!"
