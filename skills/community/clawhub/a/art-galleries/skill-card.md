## Use Case: <br>
Travel planners and exhibition visitors use this skill to shortlist popular art galleries, compare visit constraints, and plan routes, ticketing, entry, transportation, dining, and lodging details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ticketing, refunds, seating, venue rules, and event details may be outdated or too broad for a specific visit. <br>
Mitigation: Verify reservation, purchase, refund, entry, photography, and venue-policy details with official venue or ticketing sources before making plans or purchases. <br>
Risk: Popular venues may have long queues, limited availability, minimum spend, or changing photography restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Plan ahead, book or reserve where required, prefer off-peak times when possible, and follow current on-site rules. <br>
