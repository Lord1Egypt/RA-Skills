### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides SAP configuration, migration, OSS note, and accounting-impact advice that may be unsuitable for a specific production system. <br>
Mitigation: Verify production SAP changes against official SAP documentation and authorized SAP staff before applying them. <br>
Risk: Broad SAP keyword auto-activation may invoke the skill in conversations that only loosely match SAP FI/CO support. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the requested task is within SAP FI/CO scope before relying on the response. <br>
Risk: The assistant persona describes consultant credentials that should not be treated as human professional certification. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the consultant profile as an AI role and validate high-impact accounting or migration decisions through normal review channels. <br>
