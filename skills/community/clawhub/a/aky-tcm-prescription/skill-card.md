## Description: <br>
AI-powered Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) prescription assistant based on classical texts: Shanghan Lun (伤寒论), Jingui Yaolue (金匮要略), and Formula Study (方剂学). Supports syndrome differentiation (八纲/六经/脏腑/卫气营血), formula recommendation with composition analysis, herb safety checking (十八反十九畏, 妊娠禁忌), dosage guidance, and formula modification. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and clinicians can use this skill as an educational TCM reference for syndrome differentiation, classical formula analysis, herb safety review, dosage guidance, and formula modification. Outputs require review by a licensed TCM practitioner before any clinical use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce individualized TCM diagnoses, herbal formulas, and dosages without safeguards adequate for lay users or vulnerable medical situations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as an educational or clinician-reviewed TCM reference; require qualified medical review before clinical use. <br>
Risk: Users may apply outputs to self-diagnosis, pregnancy, children, elderly or frail patients, severe symptoms, toxic herbs, prescription-drug interactions, or serious medical conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use the skill for those cases without qualified medical review, and do not use it for self-prescribing. <br>
