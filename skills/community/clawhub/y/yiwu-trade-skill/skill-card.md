## Use Case: <br>
External trade operators, sourcing teams, and agent workflows use this skill to plan and execute Yiwu small-commodity export workflows, including supplier selection, quote preparation, payment-risk review, logistics selection, and compliance checklists. Developers may also use the included scripts and configuration examples to support cost, pricing, and logistics recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CRM profiling, persistent memory, and customer quote files may contain personal or commercially sensitive data. <br>
Mitigation: Deploy with role-based access, defined retention and deletion rules, and protected or expiring links for exported quotes and customer files. <br>
Risk: Bulk WhatsApp outreach and sales automation can create consent, opt-out, and regional messaging-compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit consent, opt-out handling, and human approval before bulk sends or automated customer follow-up. <br>
Risk: External AI and API integrations can share user, customer, or trade data with third-party services. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose Coze, DeepSeek, OpenAI, and Hugobsp data sharing, limit transmitted data to the minimum needed, and review provider terms before deployment. <br>
Risk: Automatic improvement workflows and knowledge-base changes may alter operational guidance without adequate review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval, version review, and security scanning before automatic updates or knowledge-base changes are applied. <br>
