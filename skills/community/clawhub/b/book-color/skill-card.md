## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to find color services, check availability, and create bookings through Lokuli. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Booking requests and customer contact details may be sent to Lokuli. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when that data flow is acceptable, and review contact details before creating a booking. <br>
Risk: The skill can create appointments based on selected service, time, location, and customer information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the service, time, location, and contact information before finalizing any booking. <br>
