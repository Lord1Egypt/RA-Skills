## Use Case: <br>
Chinese-speaking developers and technical content maintainers use this skill as a practical checklist for publishing, editing, and managing original or translated articles on OSCHINA. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow involves logging in to OSCHINA and may expose account credentials or verification codes if delegated too broadly. <br>
Mitigation: Log in directly on oschina.net and do not share passwords, verification codes, or account recovery information with the agent. <br>
Risk: Published articles may violate originality, translation attribution, or commercial-content rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review each article for originality, translation attribution, and OSCHINA commercial-content requirements before publishing. <br>
