# Auto Dealership & Car Service Marketing Kit

**Generate FTC/NADA/state DMV-compliant automotive marketing content that gets results — without the compliance landmines.**

Auto dealer advertising is one of the most regulated marketing categories in the US. Generic AI tools produce copy that violates FTC Used Car Rule, Reg Z, NADA advertising guidelines, and state DMV rules every single time. This skill fixes that — compliance enforced at generation time.

## What You Get

- **Inventory & Promotion Campaigns** (free) — new arrivals, weekend sales, trade-in campaigns, seasonal promotions across email, social, SMS, and search
- **Service Department Marketing** — reminder sequences, advisor upsell scripts (word-for-word), declined service follow-up, Quick Lane differentiation
- **Reputation & Review System** — 20 response templates across all departments, GBP optimization (30-day Posts calendar + 30 Q&As), review request sequences, crisis responses
- **Digital Advertising & Lead Nurture** — Google RSAs, Facebook/Instagram ad briefs, 6-email inquiry-to-showroom sequence, unsold prospect follow-up, conquest campaigns

## Every Output Includes

- FTC Used Car Rule compliance (Buyers Guide reference in all pre-owned ads)
- Reg Z disclosures (all APR/financing claims → required disclosure language)
- FTC Green Guides (EV/hybrid range claims → "EPA-estimated" qualifier)
- NADA guidelines (no "invoice price" without substantiation, no prohibited comparisons)
- State DMV requirements (sale price expiration dates, stock numbers, delivery charge disclosure)
- CAN-SPAM + TCPA compliance on all email and SMS sequences
