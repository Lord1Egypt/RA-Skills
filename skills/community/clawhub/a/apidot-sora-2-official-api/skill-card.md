## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: APIDOT_API_KEY exposure during real integrations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the key server-side in environment variables or a backend secret manager, and avoid placing it in browser code, public repositories, logs, screenshots, or chat output. <br>
Risk: Private prompts, media URLs, generated video URLs, or callback URLs may be exposed through logs or shared transcripts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid logging or echoing private prompts, private image URLs, generated video URLs, callback URLs, or API keys. <br>
Risk: Sora 2 Official model fields, availability, limits, or commercial terms may change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the current APIDot docs and model page before preparing payloads or making live API calls. <br>
