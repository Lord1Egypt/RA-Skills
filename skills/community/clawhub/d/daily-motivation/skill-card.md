## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Motivation prompts may include personal goals, progress details, wins, streaks, or accountability commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive medical, financial, credential, or deeply private information in goals, wins, streaks, or accountability prompts. <br>
Risk: Common phrases such as "motivate me" may activate the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review the active conversation context before adding personal details when using broad motivation prompts. <br>
