# Ratio Definitions

| Ratio | Formula | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Gross Margin | (Revenue − COGS) / Revenue | Profitability |
| Operating Margin | Operating Income / Revenue | Profitability |
| ROA | Net Income / Total Assets | Efficiency |
| ROE | Net Income / Total Equity | Return to shareholders |
| Debt Ratio | Total Liabilities / Total Assets | Leverage |
| OCF / NI | Operating Cash Flow / Net Income | Earnings quality |
