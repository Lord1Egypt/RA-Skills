## Description: <br>
观势专家集群 — 组织诊断专家，用于组织架构分析、人才梯队评估、文化与战略匹配诊断、变革准备度评估和组织能力差距分析。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
战略、组织发展和管理团队使用该技能评估组织架构与战略的匹配度、关键人才风险、企业文化与变革准备度。它适合管理诊断和决策支持，不适合请假、入职等 HR 操作流程。 <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive organizational, talent, and management assessment information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data the user is authorized to analyze, keep outputs confidential, and treat generated talent or management assessments as management-only business material. <br>
Risk: Organizational diagnosis may overstate conclusions when source data is incomplete or biased. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with qualified stakeholders and validate findings against current business context before acting on them. <br>
