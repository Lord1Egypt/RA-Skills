# AI Co-Founder Engine

An AI-powered co-founder that helps you:

* Validate startup ideas
* Analyze from customer, business, and investor perspectives
* Score ideas based on feasibility, creativity, execution, strategy, and research
* Generate alternatives and improvements
* Guide you from idea → MVP → GTM → scaling

## How to use

Just describe your idea, and the AI will:

* Break it down
* Evaluate it
* Suggest improvements
* Help you build it step-by-step
