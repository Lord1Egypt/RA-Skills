# Meeting Types Reference

## brainstorming
- **Goal**: Generate ideas, explore possibilities
- **Output**: Idea list, clusters, themes
- **Template focus**: Discussion Points section expanded
- **Action items**: Usually research/validate tasks

## planning
- **Goal**: Define goals, milestones, assignments
- **Output**: Timeline, responsibilities, deliverables
- **Template focus**: Action Items + Decisions Made
- **Action items**: Concrete deliverables with owners and deadlines

## review
- **Goal**: Assess progress, identify blockers
- **Output**: Status updates, risk assessment, adjustments
- **Template focus**: Key Discussion Points (status per agenda item)
- **Action items**: Blockers to resolve, adjustments to make

## retro
- **Goal**: Reflect on what worked / what didn't
- **Output**: Improvements, experiments to try
- **Template focus**: Decisions Made (improvement decisions)
- **Action items**: Process changes, experiments
