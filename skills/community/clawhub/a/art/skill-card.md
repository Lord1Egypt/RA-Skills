## Use Case: <br>
External users and artists use this skill to get medium-specific art guidance, focused critique, technique exercises, material recommendations, and art-appreciation context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Art advice may be inappropriate when the medium, tools, or materials are unknown. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the medium, hardware or software, and material budget before giving technical recommendations. <br>
Risk: Broad critique can overwhelm or discourage artists. <br>
Mitigation: Focus feedback on one concrete improvement area while also identifying what is already working. <br>
