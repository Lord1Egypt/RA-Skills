**Example notes:**
- Ford ProFirst + I-CAR Gold Class combination is the most accurate credential stack in this market. Most shops in Henderson running Ford ads are NOT Ford ProFirst certified.
- Deductible waiver explicit decline = trust signal to insurance agents. Agents refer more to shops they know won't create audit problems.
- EPA SQG status disclosure in B2B letters differentiates from shops that ignore RCRA entirely. Fleet RFPs often require this.
- ASE by name + designation is immediately more credible than "ASE-certified shop" because it's verifiable. Consumers can look up ASE numbers.
