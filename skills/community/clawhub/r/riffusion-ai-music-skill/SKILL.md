# Riffusion AI Music Workflow

Use this skill when planning a music-generation workflow for songs, vocals, instrumentals, loops, or soundtrack ideas with [Riffusion](https://riffusion.org).

## Product Link

- [Riffusion AI music generator](https://riffusion.org)

## Workflow

1. Define the desired musical style, mood, structure, and target use case.
2. Write a concise prompt that includes genre, instruments, vocal direction, tempo, and emotional tone.
3. Generate variations in Riffusion and compare the best outputs.
4. Export or iterate on the strongest result for the target creative project.

## Prompt Checklist

- Genre or reference style
- Mood and energy level
- Vocal or instrumental preference
- Tempo or rhythmic feel
- Intended use: social video, game loop, ad, podcast intro, or soundtrack cue
