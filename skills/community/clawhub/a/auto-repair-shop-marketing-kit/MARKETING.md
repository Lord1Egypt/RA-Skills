# MARKETING.md — Skill #135: Auto Repair & Auto Body Shop Marketing Kit

## Market Opportunity

- **US auto repair shops:** 162,000+ independent shops (IBISWorld 2024)
- **Annual industry revenue:** $115B
- **Average shop:** 4-8 bays, 3-6 technicians, $500K-$2M annual revenue
- **Marketing pain point:** Most shop owners are technicians first, marketers never — they rely on word of mouth and outdated Yellow Pages thinking
- **Digital gap:** Less than 40% of independent shops have an optimized Google Business Profile; fewer than 20% run structured local SEO
- **Compliance gap:** Nearly all auto repair marketing AI tools lack ASE accuracy, EPA 608 disclosure, and state estimate law knowledge

## Target Customer Profiles

### Primary: Independent Shop Owner
- 1-3 locations, 4-12 bays
- Revenue: $400K-$3M/year
- Pain: No time to write marketing, no budget for an agency
- Willingness to pay: $29 is less than 2 hours of labor at their shop rate
- Find them: NAPA AutoCare network, AAA-approved shops, Carfax Service Network, ASE.com

### Secondary: Shop Marketing Manager (Multi-Location)
- Handles 2-8 locations for a growing chain
- Needs consistent brand voice + compliance across all locations
- Willingness to pay: $29/location or DFY at $79/shop is trivial vs. agency rates

### Tertiary: Marketing Agency (Auto Vertical)
- Agencies serving auto service clients
- Need compliant templates they can customize
- DFY at $79/shop = sell at $500+ to clients (6x margin)
