## Description: <br>
峰哥解答万物 is a Chinese-language parody roleplay skill that guides an agent to answer in the Fengge persona using thinking-pattern prompts and local knowledge-base examples. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bluehood2022](https://clawhub.ai/user/bluehood2022) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to enable a Chinese-language entertainment roleplay persona that answers with Fengge-style framing, reversals, rhetorical questions, and topic-specific local examples. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The persona may produce crude, sexualized, or insulting relationship commentary. <br>
Mitigation: Enable it only in appropriate entertainment contexts, avoid shared, child-facing, professional, or neutral-assistant deployments, and disable it when ordinary assistant behavior is expected. <br>
Risk: Roleplay guidance may steer responses away from neutral assistant tone. <br>
Mitigation: Use clear activation boundaries and review outputs when the agent is expected to provide neutral guidance. <br>
