Competitor Research Playbook gives agents a structured SOP for competitor and market research, including website evolution analysis, social media teardown, growth flywheel scoring, X/Twitter propagation mapping, and ICP/pricing benchmarking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor, traffic, social, or ad research may involve public platform content and third-party analytics data that have usage limits, privacy expectations, or legal constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Use the playbook only for explicit competitor or market-analysis tasks, respect platform rules and applicable law, and avoid collecting or exposing private or restricted data. <br>
Risk: The playbook can produce strategic conclusions from public signals that may be incomplete, stale, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research guidance, verify important claims against current primary sources, and document uncertainty before using findings for business decisions. <br>
