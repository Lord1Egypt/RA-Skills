## Description: <br>
Review contracts for risks, unfair terms, and missing clauses, generating plain-English summaries, risk flags, and negotiation talking points for UK-oriented contract review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jamespatrickthom2003-star](https://clawhub.ai/user/jamespatrickthom2003-star) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, freelancers, sole traders, and SME owners use this skill to review pasted contracts or described agreements for common UK contract risks, missing protections, unfair terms, and negotiation points. It is a risk-flagging aid and explicitly does not provide legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with UK-oriented legal analysis that should be checked against the contract's governing law. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat UK-oriented risk flags as legal advice or as applicable to contracts governed by another jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: State that the output is not legal advice, confirm governing law before relying on findings, and consult a qualified solicitor for binding or high-value decisions. <br>
Risk: Pasted contracts can contain confidential, personal, or commercially sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact unnecessary sensitive details before use and avoid sharing information that is not needed for risk flagging. <br>
