## Description: <br>
伺服电机课程智能体 is a Chinese-language electrical machinery course assistant for knowledge Q&A, quiz practice, and teaching video recommendations across DC machines, transformers, AC windings, asynchronous machines, and synchronous machines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[smallkeyboy](https://clawhub.ai/user/smallkeyboy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students and instructors use this agent to review electrical machinery concepts, generate chapter-focused or randomized practice questions, receive worked explanations, and find course video resources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The packaged interface can silently load remote content that was not fully captured in the reviewed artifact, so visible UI or scripts could change after review. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, prefer a release that packages the UI locally or clearly discloses and pins external content, and re-review any remote content before deployment. <br>
Risk: Electrical experiment guidance may be unsafe if applied outside an appropriate lab setting. <br>
Mitigation: Use experiment guidance only in supervised lab settings with proper institutional safety procedures. <br>
