Use when the user wants a polished Ziwei report from birth date and time, including life palace structure, twelve palaces, four transformations, major luck cycles, yearly triggers, optional chart images, and a clear split between chart facts and traditional interpretation. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill runs a local astrology calculation script and may use personal birth details. <br>
Mitigation: Install only from trusted sources, keep birth details local, and review the generated report before sharing it. <br>
Risk: Optional chart exports can create or overwrite files at user-selected paths. <br>
Mitigation: Choose chart output paths deliberately and review filesystem writes before running export commands. <br>
Risk: Ziwei Doushu output is interpretive and may be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as traditional interpretation and preserve the skill's boundary that it is not medical, legal, or financial advice. <br>
