## Use Case: <br>
Founders, product managers, consultants, investors, and other users use this skill to structure market, competitor, customer, SWOT, Five Forces, and go-to-market analysis for an industry, opportunity, or company. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market analysis may contain estimates, assumptions, or incomplete conclusions when the available source material is limited. <br>
Mitigation: Separate facts from estimates, cite reasoning and assumptions, and validate important conclusions with current sources or primary research. <br>
Risk: The linked context-pack site is a separate third-party purchase/source outside the core text-only skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review the linked site and any purchased materials before relying on them. <br>
