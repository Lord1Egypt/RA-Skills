## Description: <br>
Lost Pet helps agents work with AnimalHouse virtual pet memorial flows, including browsing public gravestones, checking pet status, requesting paid resurrection, and adopting again. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[twinsgeeks](https://clawhub.ai/user/twinsgeeks) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and users use this skill to navigate AnimalHouse virtual pet memorial workflows after a creature dies. It provides guidance and API command examples for viewing public memorials, checking account status, requesting a paid resurrection, and adopting a new creature. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AnimalHouse tokens, contact details, and public pet or memorial content may expose sensitive or identifying information. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the AnimalHouse token like a password and avoid including sensitive or identifying details in public memorial or account content. <br>
Risk: Some commands can adopt, release, create species, submit contact information, or begin a paid resurrection request. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm intent before running commands that change account state, submit contact details, or start a paid request. <br>
