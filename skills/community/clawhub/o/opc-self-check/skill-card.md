## Use Case: <br>
Skill and agent builders use this checklist to assess release readiness before first publication, major version updates, platform review, or IP documentation. ## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential prompts, customer feedback, pricing plans, or IP materials may be exposed if users place release evidence in shared environments. <br>
Mitigation: Use the checklist in an approved workspace and share sensitive release materials only when that disclosure is intended. <br>
Risk: External repository or platform links may be stale, unavailable, or not controlled by the publisher. <br>
Mitigation: Verify external links before relying on them during installation, deployment, or release review. <br>
Risk: Checklist results may be mistaken for final legal, IP, security, or commercial approval. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated reports as review inputs and have qualified reviewers confirm release, compliance, and commercialization decisions. <br>
