## Description: <br>
Scan Freelancer.com for new projects matching your skills, draft personalized bid proposals, and track bidding history. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[onlyloveher](https://clawhub.ai/user/onlyloveher) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers and agents use this skill to find matching Freelancer.com projects, draft tailored bid proposals, suggest bid pricing, and maintain a bid history log. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals or bid price suggestions may be inaccurate, misleading, or unsuitable for a specific client. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit every proposal and price recommendation before use, and require explicit approval before any real bid submission. <br>
Risk: The bids.md log can expose client, budget, or job-search information if stored in a shared workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the log in a private workspace, redact sensitive details, or disable logging when the information should not be retained. <br>
