## Description: <br>
Automates an end-to-end protein mining workflow from literature review through candidate protein prioritization and publication-oriented research outputs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[majiajia0529](https://clawhub.ai/user/majiajia0529) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Bioinformatics researchers and computational biology teams use this skill to plan and run a structured protein discovery workflow covering literature survey, sequence collection, HMM profiling, cross-species search, structure-based search, phylogenetic analysis, and candidate prioritization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow depends on separate bioinformatics tools and any referenced protein-mining command that are not bundled with the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Verify tools such as MAFFT, hmmbuild, and IQ-TREE from trusted sources before installation or use. <br>
Risk: Generated reports, figures, rankings, and candidate proteins may be incomplete or scientifically misleading if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research assistance and require expert review, reproducibility checks, and appropriate validation before relying on results. <br>
