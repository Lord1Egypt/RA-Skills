## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineering managers, SREs, and platform teams use this skill to define or revisit SLOs/SLIs, error-budget policies, alerting, on-call practices, toil reduction, and continuous reliability improvement. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reliability policy guidance can affect production delivery, on-call load, and change management if adopted without local review. <br>
Mitigation: Review SLO, error-budget, change-freeze, alerting, and on-call recommendations with the teams responsible for production reliability before adoption. <br>
Risk: Generic SRE targets may not fit every service tier, dependency profile, or customer commitment. <br>
Mitigation: Tailor SLO targets, windows, and toil expectations to user-visible journeys, measured SLIs, dependency limits, service criticality, and any existing SLAs. <br>
