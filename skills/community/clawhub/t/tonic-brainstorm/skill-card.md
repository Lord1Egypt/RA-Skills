## Description: <br>
Generate ideas fast. Adapt depth and structure to what the user actually needs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to generate and organize brainstorming ideas, names, options, comparisons, and follow-up directions with a response style scaled to the request. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
