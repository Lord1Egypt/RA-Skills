## Description: <br>
Refines an active research session by drilling deeper into a subtopic after tome:research to narrow results to a specific channel or angle. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[athola](https://clawhub.ai/user/athola) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers and developers using the Tome workflow use this skill to refine an existing research session, focus on a subtopic, and update the active report with new findings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read and update an active research session or report. <br>
Mitigation: Review the current session and generated report changes before accepting or sharing the refined findings. <br>
Risk: Targeted searches can add incomplete, stale, or off-topic research findings. <br>
Mitigation: Check source quality and relevance before relying on the added findings. <br>
