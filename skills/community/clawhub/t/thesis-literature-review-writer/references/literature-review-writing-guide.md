## Core Function of Literature Review

A literature review provides the rationale for the study. It supports the statement of the problem, research questions or hypotheses, theoretical framework, method selection, and interpretation of later findings.

A strong literature review should:

- Provide historical background for the problem.
- Describe the current status of research.
- Support the purpose of the study.
- Identify gaps in the literature.
- Clarify variables, concepts, constructs, and theories.
- Identify seminal studies and leading scholars.
- Propose useful theoretical constructs.
- Show appropriate methodological procedures.
- Provide comparative studies useful for later analysis and discussion.

Core rule:

> 文献综述不是文献罗列，而是围绕研究问题对已有研究进行归纳、比较、评价和综合。

## Literature Search and Source Strategy

When the user asks for search guidance or has no references, recommend a broad but focused source strategy.

Useful source types:

- Handbooks and encyclopedias: good starting points for core concepts and classic references.
- Annual reviews: useful models for critical synthesis.
- Review articles: summarize major research trends and debates.
- Abstracting and indexing databases: ERIC, PsycINFO, Sociological Abstracts, Dissertation Abstracts, Social Science Citation Index, CINAHL, Medline, Social Work Abstracts, and field-specific databases.
- Government/statistical documents: useful for policy background and current statistics.
- Public search engines: use cautiously; prioritize university, government, journal, and professional organization sources.
- Professional journals in the user's discipline.

Chinese thesis database examples:

- 中国知网
- 万方
- 维普
- PubMed
- Web of Science
- PsycINFO
- Google Scholar
- ERIC

Source quality rules:

- Prefer peer-reviewed journal articles, academic books, dissertations, official statistics, and recognized reports.
- Prefer primary sources over secondary descriptions.
- Use review articles to understand the field, but do not rely only on them.
- If only a secondary source is available, mark it transparently according to the required citation style.

## Keyword Strategy

Search terms should come from:

- Working title
- Independent and dependent variables
- Key concepts or themes
- Population
- Theory/model
- Method
- Synonyms and related terms
- Keywords listed in relevant abstracts

Example:

For 《大学生睡眠质量与焦虑水平的关系研究》:

- 中文关键词：大学生、睡眠质量、焦虑、心理健康、影响因素、相关研究
- English keywords: college students, sleep quality, anxiety, mental health, correlates, predictors

For qualitative topics:

- 中文关键词：大学生、心理求助、质性研究、访谈、主题分析、影响因素
- English keywords: college students, help-seeking, qualitative study, interview, thematic analysis, barriers

For intervention-scheme topics:

- 中文关键词：大学生、考试焦虑、认知行为疗法、团体辅导、干预方案
- English keywords: test anxiety, cognitive behavioral therapy, group counseling, intervention, college students

When drafting without exact references, add:

> 建议后续以"……""……""……"为关键词，在CNKI、万方、PubMed、Web of Science等数据库中检索近五至十年文献，并补充经典理论文献。

## Recommended Chapter Structure

Default full chapter:

```markdown
第二章 文献综述

2.1 文献检索与综述思路
2.2 核心概念界定
2.3 理论基础
2.4 变量/主题一研究现状
2.5 变量/主题二研究现状
2.6 变量关系/作用机制/干预研究现状
2.7 既有研究述评
2.8 本章小结
```

For many undergraduate papers, simplify to:

```markdown
第二章 文献综述

2.1 核心概念界定
2.2 相关理论基础
2.3 国内外研究现状
2.4 既有研究述评
2.5 本章小结
```

For short "国内外研究现状":

```markdown
一、核心概念及理论基础
二、国内研究现状
三、国外研究现状
四、研究述评
```

## Funnel Organization

Use a funnel structure:

1. General and historical literature.
2. Core concepts and theories.
3. Current research on each variable/concept/theme.
4. Research closest to the user's population, method, setting, and research problem.
5. Gaps and direct lead-in to the user's study.

Coverage rule:

- Highly relevant studies: discuss in detail.
- Moderately relevant studies: discuss briefly.
- Tangential studies: mention only if they support context or theory; otherwise exclude.

Avoid:

- Giving every article the same amount of coverage.
- Switching repeatedly between broad background and narrow evidence.
- Overloading the chapter with background unrelated to the research questions.

## Selective Coverage

For every source, ask:

- Does it explain the theory supporting the study?
- Does it define the current state of research?
- Does it directly concern the topic, population, variables, or method?
- Does it help maintain a coherent argument?
- Does it identify a gap, contradiction, trend, or method issue?
- Does it support the study's research questions, hypotheses, or design?

Preferred style:

> Existing studies can be grouped into three main lines...

Avoid overusing:

> 张三认为……李四认为……王五认为……

Better Chinese pattern:

> 现有研究主要从……、……和……三个方面探讨……。其中，……研究强调……；……研究则更多关注……。总体来看，这些研究为理解……提供了基础，但在……方面仍存在不足。

## Writing the Chapter Opening

The review chapter should begin with a brief introduction that:

- Reintroduces the research problem.
- Names the key variables, concepts, themes, or theories.
- States the scope of the review.
- Provides an advance organizer listing the exact sections of the chapter.

Chinese template:

> 本章围绕……主题展开文献综述，旨在为本研究的问题提出、理论基础和研究设计提供依据。结合本文研究目的，本章主要从……、……、……和……四个方面梳理相关研究：首先，对……进行概念界定；其次，介绍……理论基础；再次，综述……和……的研究现状；最后，对既有研究进行评述，并指出本研究的切入点。

Quality rule:

- The headings announced in the opening should match the actual headings in the chapter.

## Headings and Transitions

Use headings and subheadings to keep the review coherent.

Practical guideline:

- Use at least one heading or subheading for every major topic.
- Long reviews should not run for many paragraphs without a heading.
- Each section should begin with a topic sentence and end with a transition or mini-summary.

Useful transition phrases:

- 在上述研究基础上……
- 与……研究不同，……
- 从研究对象来看……
- 从研究方法来看……
- 尽管已有研究取得了一定进展，但……
- 进一步来看……
- 因此，有必要……
- 综上，……

Transition template:

> 以上研究主要揭示了……，但对于……的讨论仍相对有限。为进一步理解……，有必要继续梳理……方面的研究。

## Writing the Chapter Summary

The chapter should end with a brief summary that:

- Reviews the main theoretical and empirical literature.
- Identifies what is known.
- Identifies what remains unclear.
- Shows how the user's study extends or applies existing knowledge.
- Leads naturally to the research method or research questions.

Chinese template:

> 综上所述，既有研究围绕……已经形成了较为丰富的成果，主要集中在……、……和……等方面。这些研究为本文理解……提供了重要基础。然而，现有研究仍存在以下不足：第一，……；第二，……；第三，……。基于此，本文拟以……为研究对象，围绕……展开研究，以期进一步补充……领域的相关认识。

## Critical Synthesis Strategies

Use one or more of these strategies to avoid simple listing.

### 1. Historical Context

Use when a construct or theory developed over time.

Chinese pattern:

> 关于……的研究经历了从……到……的发展过程。早期研究主要关注……，随后研究者逐渐转向……。近年来，随着……，相关研究进一步拓展到……。

### 2. Literature Gap

Use when the field lacks research on a population, setting, method, or mechanism.

Chinese pattern:

> 尽管已有研究关注……，但关于……群体/情境/机制的研究仍相对不足。尤其是在……方面，现有研究尚未形成充分解释。

### 3. Different Approach

Use when the user's study takes a different angle from prior work.

Chinese pattern:

> 既有研究多从……角度解释……，而较少从……角度展开。因此，本文尝试以……为切入点，对……进行分析。

### 4. Trends and Themes

Use when studies can be grouped into themes.

Chinese pattern:

> 综合已有文献可以发现，相关研究主要形成了三类主题：一是……；二是……；三是……。

### 5. Practical Significance

Use when empirical studies report effect sizes, sample sizes, intervention outcomes, or practical implications.

Chinese pattern:

> 从实践效果来看，相关研究显示……可能对……具有一定作用。但不同研究在样本规模、测量工具和干预周期上存在差异，因此其结论仍需结合具体情境理解。

### 6. Conflicting Theories

Use when theories or findings disagree.

Chinese pattern:

> 关于……的解释，现有研究存在两种不同观点。一种观点认为……；另一种观点则强调……。两种观点分别揭示了……和……，但也存在……方面的局限。

### 7. Summary Table

Use a table when at least 8-10 studies concern the same variable, intervention, instrument, or theme.

Suggested table columns:

| 作者/年份 | 研究对象 | 研究方法 | 主要变量/主题 | 主要发现 | 局限 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

### 8. Inconsistent Findings

Use when results are mixed.

Chinese pattern:

> 关于……与……的关系，现有研究结论并不一致。部分研究发现……，而另一些研究则指出……。造成差异的原因可能与样本特征、测量工具、研究设计和文化情境有关。

### 9. Continuing Line of Inquiry

Use when a classic study or theory generated later studies.

Chinese pattern:

> 自……提出……以来，相关研究不断围绕……展开。一方面，后续研究延续了……的基本思路；另一方面，也在……方面进行了修正和拓展。

## Method-Specific Review Structures

### Quantitative Questionnaire Study

Recommended structure:

1. 核心概念界定
2. 理论基础
3. 变量A研究现状
4. 变量B研究现状
5. 变量A与变量B关系研究
6. 人口学变量差异研究，if relevant
7. 既有研究述评
8. 本章小结

Emphasize:

- Definitions and measures.
- Theoretical rationale for variable relationships.
- Prior findings on correlations, differences, prediction, mediation, or moderation.
- Gaps in population, sample, context, or method.

### Qualitative Study

Recommended structure:

1. 核心概念界定
2. 理论视角或分析框架
3. 目标群体相关研究
4. 研究主题的质性/实证研究现状
5. 影响因素、经验过程或意义建构研究
6. 既有研究述评
7. 本章小结

Emphasize:

- Participants' experiences, meanings, processes, and contexts.
- Existing qualitative themes and gaps.
- Why interviews, focus groups, observations, case study, or text analysis are appropriate.
- Avoid turning qualitative review into variable-correlation logic.

### Mixed Methods Study

Recommended structure:

1. 核心概念与理论基础
2. 定量研究现状
3. 质性研究现状
4. 混合研究的必要性
5. 既有研究述评
6. 本章小结

Emphasize:

- What quantitative studies show.
- What qualitative studies explain.
- Why both are needed.
- How the user's mixed design addresses gaps.

### Literature Review Paper

Recommended structure:

1. 文献检索与筛选
2. 核心概念界定
3. 理论基础
4. 主题一
5. 主题二
6. 主题三
7. 研究不足与未来方向

Emphasize:

- Search scope and inclusion/exclusion criteria.
- Thematic synthesis.
- Critical comparison.
- Future research agenda.

### Intervention-Scheme Design

Recommended structure:

1. 目标问题的概念与表现
2. 目标问题的影响因素和发生机制
3. 相关理论基础
4. 既有干预研究
5. 现有干预的不足
6. 对本研究方案设计的启示

Emphasize:

- Intervention mechanisms.
- Target population needs.
- Evidence for intervention components.
- Limitations of existing interventions.
- The user's scheme as a design, not proven effective.

## Chinese Writing Templates

### Concept Review

> 关于……的概念，学界尚未形成完全一致的界定。已有研究主要从……、……和……三个角度进行解释。综合相关文献，本文将……界定为……，并在后续研究中主要从……方面加以分析。

### Theory Review

> ……理论为解释……提供了重要视角。该理论认为……。在……研究中，该理论常被用于解释……。结合本文研究主题，……理论可以帮助理解……，因此本文将其作为重要理论基础。

### Empirical Review

> 现有关于……的实证研究主要集中在……方面。部分研究发现……，说明……；也有研究指出……，提示……。总体来看，相关研究为理解……提供了经验依据，但在……方面仍存在进一步探讨空间。

### Qualitative Review

> 既有质性研究显示，……群体在……过程中通常呈现出……、……和……等经验主题。这些研究强调了……的重要性，但对于……情境下的具体经验和意义建构仍缺乏充分讨论。

### Research Gap

> 综上，已有研究为本文提供了重要基础，但仍存在以下不足：第一，……；第二，……；第三，……。因此，本文拟在既有研究基础上，以……为对象，围绕……展开进一步研究。

### Chapter Summary

> 本章围绕……进行了文献梳理。首先，本文对……进行了概念界定；其次，介绍了……理论基础；再次，从……、……和……方面综述了相关研究；最后，对既有研究进行了评述。总体来看，现有研究为本文提供了理论和经验依据，但仍需要在……方面进一步拓展。

## Citation and Plagiarism Safeguards

Rules:

- Do not invent citations.
- Do not copy long passages from sources.
- Use quotation marks and citations for exact wording.
- Prefer paraphrase plus citation for most literature.
- Cite ideas, not only direct quotations.
- Use primary sources when possible.
- If a source is secondary, mark it transparently.

When sources are missing:

> （此处补充关于……的近五年实证研究）

When exact data are missing:

> 相关调查显示……（此处补充数据来源）

When a classic theory is mentioned but not sourced:

> 根据……理论，……（此处补充经典文献）

## Revision Checklist

Before finalizing, check:

- Does the chapter begin with an advance organizer?
- Are headings aligned with the user's topic, variables, concepts, or themes?
- Does the review move from broad to specific?
- Are the most relevant studies covered in greater detail?
- Does each subsection include synthesis rather than only summaries?
- Are gaps, contradictions, limitations, or trends clearly identified?
- Does the chapter summary lead directly to the user's study?
- Are citations real or clearly marked as placeholders?
- Are transitions clear between sections?
- Is the prose formal Chinese academic style?
