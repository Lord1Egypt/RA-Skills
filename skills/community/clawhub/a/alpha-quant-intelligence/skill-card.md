## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading signals, win-rate claims, insider-intelligence wording, and daily alpha picks may be mistaken for verified financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational market discussion, independently verify any claim, and avoid making trading decisions solely from this skill. <br>
Risk: Connecting brokerage, payment, or paid signal-stream services can create financial exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Do not connect brokerage or payment APIs unless the service has been separately reviewed, trusted, and approved for the intended use. <br>
