# BossSkill 老板经营秘书

> 给创业者和中小企业老板用的 AI 经营顾问、客户管理助手、团队管理助手和执行闭环秘书。

老板真正缺的，往往不是一个新的管理系统，而是一个每天都能帮你记客户、盯团队、追任务、做复盘、给建议的贴身秘书。

BossSkill 就是为这个场景设计的 AI Skill。它让老板用自然语言完成客户建档、团队画像、任务记录、人脉沉淀、经营诊断、晚间复盘和长期知识积累。

你不用一项一项填表，只要像平时说话一样告诉它：

```text
李总是客户，做餐饮加盟，预算5万，5月12日跟进。
张三是销售，执行力强，但成交话术弱。
王总懂本地生活投流，以后获客问题可以请教他。
今晚帮我追一下今天没有结果的任务。
这个建议有效，客户愿意继续推进。
```

BossSkill 会把这些碎片信息逐步变成客户库、团队库、任务库、人脉库、经营记忆和老板行动建议。

它的目标不是替老板做决定，而是帮助老板：

- 更快看见经营问题
- 更少遗漏重点客户
- 更及时追任务结果
- 更系统训练团队
- 更稳定沉淀经营经验
- 更清楚每天应该先做什么

简单理解：

```text
免费版 = 本地记录工具 + 基础老板简报
授权版 = AI 老板秘书 + 经营顾问 + 执行教练
```

## 为什么老板需要 BossSkill

很多老板每天都很忙，但忙完以后说不清今天真正推进了什么。

客户聊过了，但预算、异议、生日、爱好、下次跟进时间没有沉淀。员工安排了任务，但有没有结果没人追。团队出现问题时，靠感觉判断，缺少长期记录。经营指标变差时，才发现前面已经有很多信号被忽略。

BossSkill 解决的是这些真实痛点：

- 客户信息散落在聊天、脑子和临时笔记里
- 员工能力、短板、训练记录没有长期画像
- 任务创建了，但没有完成、延期、转交、停止的闭环
- 老板每天不知道应该优先盯客户、团队、回款还是风险
- 经营建议执行后，没有记录有效还是无效
- 系统越用越麻烦，老板不愿意专门录入资料

BossSkill 的设计原则是：少填表、多对话、重执行、追结果、能沉淀。

## 核心能力

### 1. 自然语言建档

老板不用打开复杂后台。直接说一句话，系统会判断这是客户、员工、人脉还是任务。

```text
刘总是客户，做建材加盟，预算15万，比较关心回本周期。
```

系统会把它沉淀为客户信息，并在后续跟进、简报和复盘中继续使用。

### 2. 客户跟进管理

BossSkill 可以记录客户状态、行业、预算、需求、异议、生日、爱好、负责人、最近跟进时间和下次跟进时间。

适合：

- 销售客户跟进
- 招商加盟客户管理
- 教育咨询客户管理
- 本地生活客户维护
- 企业服务客户推进
- 私域客户关系维护

### 3. 团队画像和训练

老板可以记录员工岗位、能力、执行力、话术短板、任务表现、训练记录和复盘情况。

```text
张三是销售，执行力不错，但报价后的逼单能力弱。
```

后续系统可以围绕张三生成训练建议、复盘问题和任务追踪。

### 4. 任务闭环和晚间复盘

BossSkill 不只是记录任务，更关注任务有没有结果。

晚间可以直接说：

```text
今晚追结果。
```

系统会提示今天哪些任务没有闭环，并要求把任务变成明确状态：

- 完成
- 延期
- 转交
- 停止

### 5. 人脉资源库

老板不只管理客户和员工，还需要管理朋友、合作方、专家、供应商、投流资源、财税资源、法务资源等社会关系。

```text
王总是朋友，懂本地生活投流，以后获客问题可以请教。
```

以后当老板说“我想找懂投流的人”，系统可以从人脉库中推荐可协助对象。

### 6. 老板偏好学习

授权版支持学习老板偏好。

```text
以后提醒我直接一点，不要说太多。
这个建议有效，因为客户愿意继续推进。
这个建议没用，客户觉得太强势。
```

系统会把反馈沉淀为长期记忆，后续建议更贴近老板风格。

### 7. 自然追问补全资料

系统不会要求老板一次性填完所有资料，而是在日常对话中顺手追问一个最有价值的问题。

比如客户缺少生日、预算、行业、爱好、决策人、下次跟进时间时，系统会在合适场景轻量追问，逐步补全画像。

### 8. 经营诊断和行动建议

授权版会围绕客户、团队、任务、指标、风险、行业打法和知识库，给老板更具体的执行建议。

建议必须尽量包含：

- 动作
- 对象
- 负责人
- 时间
- 指标或验收标准

# BossSkill AI Business Secretary

> An AI executive assistant, startup coach, CRM assistant, team management assistant, and execution follow-up tool for founders and small business owners.

Most business owners do not need another complicated dashboard. They need a practical assistant that helps them remember customers, follow up with the team, track tasks, review results, and turn daily conversations into business memory.

BossSkill is designed for that job.

It helps founders, small business owners, sales leaders, and project managers manage customers, team members, tasks, contacts, business reviews, execution loops, and long-term knowledge through natural language.

You can talk to it like this:

```text
Mr. Li is a customer. He is interested in a restaurant franchise, has a budget of 50,000, and should be followed up on May 12.
Zhang San is a salesperson. He executes well, but his closing script is weak.
Mr. Wang understands local life traffic acquisition. Ask him when we need help with lead generation.
Help me review unfinished tasks tonight.
This suggestion worked because the customer agreed to move forward.
```

BossSkill turns these fragments into customer profiles, team profiles, task records, contact resources, business memory, and actionable recommendations.

## Why BossSkill Exists

Many business owners work hard every day, but still struggle to answer one question at night: what actually moved forward today?

Customer needs are scattered across chat history and memory. Team tasks are assigned, but results are not tracked. Employee strengths and weaknesses are judged by feeling instead of long-term records. Business risks are noticed too late because early signals were never captured.

BossSkill focuses on practical business pain points:

- Customer information is scattered
- Employee capabilities are not tracked over time
- Tasks are created but not closed
- Owners do not know what to prioritize today
- Advice is not calibrated by real outcomes
- Data entry feels too heavy for daily use

The design principle is simple: less manual input, more conversation, stronger execution, better follow-up, and continuous learning.

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Natural Language Data Entry

No complex forms. Just say what happened.

```text
Mr. Liu is a customer in building materials franchising. His budget is 150,000 and he cares about payback period.
```

BossSkill can turn it into a customer record and reuse it in future follow-ups, briefs, and reviews.

### 2. Customer Follow-Up Management

BossSkill can track customer status, industry, budget, needs, objections, birthday, preferences, owner, last follow-up time, and next follow-up time.

Suitable for:

- Sales follow-up
- Franchise lead management
- Education consulting
- Local service businesses
- B2B service sales
- Private domain customer relationship management

### 3. Team Profiles and Training

Record roles, strengths, weaknesses, execution quality, training history, task performance, and review results.

```text
Zhang San is a salesperson. He executes well, but he is weak at closing after quotation.
```

BossSkill can later generate training suggestions, review questions, and follow-up tasks.

### 4. Task Closure and Evening Review

BossSkill is not only about creating tasks. It focuses on whether tasks actually produce results.

You can say:

```text
Review unfinished tasks tonight.
```

The system will push tasks into clear statuses:

- Done
- Delayed
- Transferred
- Stopped

### 5. Contact Resource Library

Business owners also manage friends, partners, suppliers, experts, legal resources, finance resources, traffic resources, and other social connections.

```text
Mr. Wang is a friend who understands local life traffic acquisition. Ask him when we need lead generation advice.
```

When the owner later asks, “Who can help with traffic acquisition?”, BossSkill can recommend relevant contacts.

### 6. Owner Preference Learning

The licensed version can learn how the owner prefers to work.

```text
Remind me more directly in the future.
This suggestion worked because the customer moved forward.
This suggestion did not work because it felt too aggressive.
```

BossSkill stores these signals and improves future recommendations.

### 7. Progressive Profile Completion

BossSkill does not force users to fill out everything at once. It asks one useful follow-up question at the right time.

If a customer profile is missing birthday, budget, industry, preference, decision maker, or next follow-up time, the system can gently ask during normal conversation.

### 8. Business Diagnosis and Action Plans

The licensed version provides more concrete recommendations around customers, team, tasks, metrics, risks, industry playbooks, and knowledge base.

Recommendations should include as many of these as possible:

- Action
- Target
- Owner
- Time
- Metric or acceptance standard

## Privacy and Data

BossSkill stores customers, team members, tasks, and contacts in a local SQLite database by default.

License verification only sends the license key, device identifier, and feature name to the license server. It does not require users to upload their local database.

When using licensed cloud features, the client only sends the parameters required for the current command, such as the current input, command name, and license information. It does not automatically upload the full customer database, team database, task database, or local SQLite file.

Only when the user explicitly requests a public document download link will the selected Word/PDF file be uploaded to `docs.fanfan.la`, and the link is designed to be temporary.
