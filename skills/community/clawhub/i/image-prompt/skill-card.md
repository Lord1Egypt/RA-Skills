## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Saved prompt names, prompt text, and command history are stored locally in plain text. <br>
Mitigation: Use IMAGE_PROMPT_DIR to choose an appropriate storage location and avoid saving confidential client details, secrets, or proprietary concepts unless plain-text local storage is acceptable. <br>
