## Description: <br>
Paper Polisher helps agents polish Chinese and English academic papers by improving language, logic, terminology, and formatting while preserving the author's intended meaning and structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jirboy](https://clawhub.ai/user/jirboy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and academic writers use this skill to revise Chinese core-journal papers, SCI manuscripts, and theses for clearer academic style, grammar, terminology consistency, and change explanations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste unpublished research, confidential manuscripts, reviewer comments, or personal data into the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive manuscript content unless agent processing is acceptable, and review all suggested edits for accuracy before use. <br>
