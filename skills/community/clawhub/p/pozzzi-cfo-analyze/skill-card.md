## Description: <br>
报表官-分析 analyzes 1-6 uploaded Excel/WPS/DingTalk/Tencent Docs/CSV business reports in Chinese and returns conclusions, data attribution, decision guidance, and chart code. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Chinese small and medium business decision-makers and analysts use this skill to ask business questions about uploaded reports and receive structured financial, operations, sales, or HR analysis with recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded spreadsheets and the user's OpenClaw token are sent to the publisher's analysis backend. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill deliberately for confidential reports and avoid regulated or highly sensitive company data unless the publisher's privacy and retention claims meet the user's needs. <br>
Risk: Generated business conclusions and recommendations may be incomplete, low-confidence, or unsuitable for important decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as advisory, review confidence indicators and source columns, and combine the analysis with human business judgment before acting. <br>
