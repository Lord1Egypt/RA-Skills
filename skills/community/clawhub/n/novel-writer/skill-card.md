## Description: <br>
A Chinese web-fiction writing assistant for generating outlines, character profiles, chapter continuations, style-consistent prose, and revision passes across popular web-novel genres. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[n99991](https://clawhub.ai/user/n99991) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and agent users use this skill to plan and draft Chinese web novels, maintain outlines and character notes, continue chapters, and revise prose while keeping genre pacing and style consistent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to write chapter drafts, outlines, character notes, and state metadata under a workspace novels folder. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in a workspace where those novel files may be created or updated, and review changed files before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The default workflow and guidance are optimized for Chinese web-fiction output. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the desired language, genre, style, and audience before drafting if different output is required. <br>
Risk: Chapter progress depends on outline, character, chapter, and state metadata staying consistent. <br>
Mitigation: Check state.json and recent chapter files after writing or editing chapters, especially after manual changes. <br>
