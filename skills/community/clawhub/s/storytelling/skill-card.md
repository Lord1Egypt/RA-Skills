## Description: <br>
Craft clear, emotionally resonant stories with audience-first framing, narrative arc control, and channel-specific rewrites. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, creators, founders, marketers, educators, and other writers use this skill to turn product stories, founder narratives, case studies, speeches, long-form writing, and short-form adaptations into coherent, audience-focused narratives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local storytelling memory under ~/storytelling/ can retain confidential customer details, unreleased business strategy, sensitive personal stories, or credentials if a user intentionally stores them. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive material out of storytelling memory unless retention is intentional, and review or delete local notes before sharing the workspace or releasing derived content. <br>
Risk: Narrative drafts can become misleading if they include fabricated facts, testimonials, or outcomes that are not supported by evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual, legal, and domain-specific claims before use, and require supporting evidence for testimonials, metrics, and outcome claims. <br>
