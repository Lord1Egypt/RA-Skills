This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External crypto researchers, analysts, and developers use this skill to run structured, collaborative crypto market, project, technical, tokenomics, social, and content workflows with human review at each major phase. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may search or fetch public web sources for crypto research, so current-market conclusions can depend on source quality and timeliness. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources and validate important claims against primary project materials, market data, and recent public information before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: The skill may save reports and workspace metadata under workspaces/. <br>
Mitigation: Review saved outputs periodically and avoid placing secrets, private keys, or sensitive personal information in workspace documents. <br>
Risk: Crypto research outputs may include investment recommendations or position-sizing language. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research input rather than financial advice, and require human review before making investment decisions. <br>
