## Description: <br>
输入竞品关键词或网站，自动抓取竞品公开信息并生成支持 JSON/Markdown 导出的结构化竞品分析报告。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jpengcheng523-netizen](https://clawhub.ai/user/jpengcheng523-netizen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
SEO practitioners, operations teams, product managers, and market analysts use this skill to gather public competitor information from keywords or URLs and produce comparison reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill searches DuckDuckGo and crawls public competitor websites, so inputs may expose sensitive project names or URLs and may interact with third-party site policies. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit public competitor-analysis requests, avoid private project names or sensitive URLs, and respect robots.txt and target site terms. <br>
Risk: Exported Markdown or JSON reports may persist collected competitor data locally. <br>
Mitigation: Choose output filenames and storage locations deliberately, and review generated reports before sharing them. <br>
