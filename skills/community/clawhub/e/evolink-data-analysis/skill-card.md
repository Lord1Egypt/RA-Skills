Developers, analysts, and external users use this skill to analyze CSV, Excel, and JSON files for trends, anomalies, KPI questions, experiments, cohorts, funnels, and decision support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends full local file contents and the analysis question to an external EvoLink API. <br>
Mitigation: Use only files approved for transmission to Evolink, and avoid secrets, credentials, regulated data, or private business exports unless explicitly authorized. <br>
Risk: The security evidence reports unclear consent or permission scoping for uploading file contents. <br>
Mitigation: Review the script and operating procedure before installation, and prefer a release that clearly declares permissions, shows exactly what will be uploaded, and requires explicit consent before transmission. <br>
