## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Architecture, permitting, zoning, code, engineering, and liability guidance can be jurisdiction-specific and project-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify decisions with qualified local professionals, local authorities, and project-specific documents before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: The skill may discuss professional practice, code sections, or discipline boundaries in ways that require licensed judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as general support, ask for project location when relevant, and defer structural, MEP, legal, permitting, and liability decisions to qualified professionals. <br>
