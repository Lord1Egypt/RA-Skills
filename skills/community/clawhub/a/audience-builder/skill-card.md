## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer and prospect data used for ad audiences can include personal information and regulated targeting signals. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm lawful basis or consent, minimize exported fields, use platform-approved hashing or protected upload flows, restrict access, and delete CSV exports after use. <br>
Risk: Audience uploads, suppression syncs, budget changes, or campaign launches can affect live advertising spend. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before uploading lists, syncing suppression audiences, changing budgets, or launching campaigns. <br>
Risk: Cross-platform targeting and attribution can double-count conversions or overexpose the same users. <br>
Mitigation: Use suppression lists, frequency monitoring, holdout testing, and documented attribution windows before acting on performance claims. <br>
