## Description: <br>
国网调研报告撰写 helps agents draft State Grid human resources research reports from user-provided policies, reference literature, project materials, and research data, with Word document output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[meifeng1998](https://clawhub.ai/user/meifeng1998) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Consultants, analysts, and enterprise HR teams use this skill to turn authorized State Grid policy files, research references, interview notes, and project data into structured research reports on state-owned enterprise reform, performance assessment, compensation, job management, governance, and incentive mechanisms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided policy, HR, compensation, or interview materials may be confidential or restricted. <br>
Mitigation: Use only documents the user is authorized to process, confirm the output filename and location, and avoid sharing generated reports outside approved channels. <br>
Risk: Generated report text may misstate source documents or overstate findings if source materials are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated .docx against the provided materials, keep uncertain claims marked for verification, and confirm policy citations before relying on the report. <br>
