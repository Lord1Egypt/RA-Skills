## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a local discipline coach to define daily commitments, track streaks, stack habits, and review patterns across sleep, exercise, nutrition, focus, and recovery routines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal habit and health-adjacent details may be stored locally as commitments and streak history. <br>
Mitigation: Decide what details are appropriate to track and use the local OpenClaw environment to view or delete stored commitments and streak history. <br>
