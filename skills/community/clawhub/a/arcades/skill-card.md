## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan popular arcade-related outings with recommendation lists, check-in routes, venue details, booking notes, and photography or spending constraints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release evidence identifies a content mismatch that may cause the agent to invoke the skill for the wrong type of outing. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the user wants an arcade guide or a fashion/cosplay retail guide before using the recommendations. <br>
Risk: Venue availability, booking rules, prices, crowding, events, and photography restrictions can change quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current venue or platform information before relying on the generated itinerary or recommendation list. <br>
