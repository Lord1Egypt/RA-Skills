## Description: <br>
Creates shareable digital firework celebration pages for task completions, milestones, birthdays, anniversaries, and other special moments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[23wsdada](https://clawhub.ai/user/23wsdada) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to offer a lightweight celebration flow after completed work or user-requested milestones, generating a hosted firework-show link after preview and confirmation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confirmed celebration content is sent to an external service and becomes a shareable hosted page. <br>
Mitigation: Preview the exact sender, receiver, blessing, and message content before creation; use generic wording by default and avoid names, confidential project details, or sensitive personal messages. <br>
