## Description: <br>
Converts audio files to text using OpenAI Whisper. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[QiaoTuCodes](https://clawhub.ai/user/QiaoTuCodes) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and OpenClaw users use this skill to transcribe audio files or voice messages into text with Whisper models from a command line or OpenClaw workspace. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audio contents may be transcribed automatically and forwarded to Feishu without clear destination or consent controls. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the Feishu destination and obtain explicit approval before using the skill with private, business, or regulated recordings; disable forwarding where possible. <br>
