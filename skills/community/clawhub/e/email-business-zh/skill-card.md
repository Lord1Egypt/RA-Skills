## Description: <br>
根据用户提供的核心要点，快速生成结构清晰、语气正式得体的中文商务邮件，适用于对外合作洽谈、客户沟通、内部通知、会议邀约、项目跟进、投诉处理等多种职场场景。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external-facing business users use this skill to draft formal Chinese business emails from key points for client communication, internal notices, meeting invitations, project follow-ups, supplier requests, and complaint handling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted business emails may contain incorrect names, dates, obligations, pricing, confidential business details, or tone. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated drafts before sending and verify all factual, contractual, financial, and sensitive details. <br>
