## Description: <br>
Turn OpenClaw into a learning-loop agent with seeded workspace rules, skill promotion, reflective memory, and proactive maintenance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External developers and OpenClaw users use Hermes Agent to add a lightweight local learning loop that retrieves durable operational memory, reflects after significant work, and promotes repeated workflows into stable rules or future skills. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Durable local memory can preserve operational preferences or sensitive notes longer than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Review files under ~/hermes-agent/ periodically and avoid storing credentials, secrets, payment data, health data, sensitive personal information, or copied transcripts. <br>
Risk: Workspace seed edits can change how future OpenClaw sessions retrieve memory and reflect on work. <br>
Mitigation: Approve only seed edits you want, keep changes additive, and patch the smallest relevant AGENTS.md, SOUL.md, or HEARTBEAT.md section. <br>
Risk: Noisy or stale memory can reduce future task quality. <br>
Mitigation: Keep memory.md short and current, distill only reusable lessons, and archive stale or superseded entries. <br>
