## Description: <br>
Provides structured reasoning frameworks for deep analysis, decision-making, strategic planning, and critique. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wanikua](https://clawhub.ai/user/wanikua) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to guide an agent through named thinking frameworks such as critical thinking, first principles, red team analysis, premortems, Bayesian thinking, and systems thinking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad analysis requests, which can make behavior less predictable when the user did not intend a specific framework. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit framework names or command-style prompts when predictable behavior is required. <br>
