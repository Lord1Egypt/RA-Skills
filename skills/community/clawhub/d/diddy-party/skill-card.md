## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The persona may make responses feel more informal than expected for serious or high-stakes work. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented deactivation phrases, such as "normal mode," "plain mode," or "drop persona," to return to a neutral assistant voice. <br>
Risk: The skill changes tone and OpenClaw terminology, which may obscure platform concepts for users who expect standard labels. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated responses for clarity and switch back to neutral mode when precise platform terminology is required. <br>
