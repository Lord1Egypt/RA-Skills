### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive or private research could expose information to sources, APIs, crawlers, or workflows the user did not intend to use. <br>
Mitigation: Set explicit privacy boundaries, allowed sources, API or crawler permissions, and information that must not be shared externally before invoking the skill. <br>
Risk: Broad search guidance can still produce incomplete, outdated, or non-authoritative findings when source constraints are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Require source quality criteria, recency expectations, citation requirements, and verification steps for important findings. <br>
