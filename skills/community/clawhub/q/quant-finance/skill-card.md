Mitigation: Treat outputs as general reference guidance and have qualified professionals review decisions before using them for investment, legal, compliance, or operational action. <br>
Risk: Static reference content can become outdated as markets, regulations, and industry practices change. <br>
Mitigation: Validate important formulas, regulatory points, and due diligence steps against current authoritative sources before relying on them. <br>
