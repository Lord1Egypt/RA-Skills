## Description: <br>
Vault Intelligence guides agents through Paradex vault discovery, comparison, risk scoring, and monitoring by orchestrating Paradex MCP vault tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External Paradex users and agents use this skill to screen vaults, compare performance and risk, inspect positions and account health, and present deposit-oriented vault recommendations with caveats. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may shape financial decisions by ranking and recommending Paradex vaults. <br>
Mitigation: Verify vault data, lockup terms, and risk independently before acting; treat the output as analysis and screening, not investment advice. <br>
