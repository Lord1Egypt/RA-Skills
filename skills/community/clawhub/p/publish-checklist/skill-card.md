## Description: <br>
A pre-publication review checklist for ClawHub skills, GitHub PRs and issues, posts, emails, and other public-facing content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[guoqunabc](https://clawhub.ai/user/guoqunabc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and maintainers use this skill before publishing ClawHub skills, GitHub PRs or issues, posts, emails, and other public content to check for privacy, security, extensibility, and public communication risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The checklist may trigger broadly for PRs, issues, posts, and emails. <br>
Mitigation: Apply it as a review aid for public-facing work and adjust project-specific requirements during human review. <br>
Risk: The checklist may encourage bilingual PR or issue text where a project does not require it. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the target project's contribution norms when bilingual text is unnecessary. <br>
