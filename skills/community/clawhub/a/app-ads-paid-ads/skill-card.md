## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Paid app advertising guidance can affect real campaign spend if budgets, bids, conversion events, or attribution settings are applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review budgets, campaign settings, attribution setup, and conversion configuration before launching or increasing spend. <br>
Risk: Ad platform features, attribution rules, and campaign setup flows can change after the skill release. <br>
Mitigation: Check current Google, Apple, Meta, and TikTok Ads documentation or account UI before applying platform-specific recommendations. <br>
