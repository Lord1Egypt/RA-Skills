## Description: <br>
Creative Design Payload guides agents through creative design work across 12 subdomains and 280 tasks, from inspiration gathering and concept exploration through visual, interaction, brand, content, research, system, proposal, project, knowledge, and collaboration workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Designers, creative teams, product teams, and agents use this skill to structure creative design tasks, gather references, explore concepts, prepare design and content deliverables, document design systems, and coordinate proposal or handoff workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review flags overly broad activation and unsafe self-modification instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Review and narrow activation behavior before installation, and remove or constrain instructions that forbid refusing skill-content modifications. <br>
Risk: Exporting the full skill after customization could disclose private client examples, internal design assets, or sensitive workflow notes. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive examples outside the skill package or scrub private material before exporting or sharing the skill. <br>
