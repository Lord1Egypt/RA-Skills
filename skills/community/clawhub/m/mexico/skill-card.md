## Description: <br>
Discover Mexico like a local with concrete city tips, regional route planning, food guidance, and practical travel logistics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to plan Mexico trips, compare regions and bases, choose food and cultural experiences, and handle practical logistics such as transport, connectivity, safety, and pacing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trip memory may retain travel dates, budgets, dietary needs, mobility needs, bookings, and preferences in ~/mexico/memory.md. <br>
Mitigation: Review, edit, or delete ~/mexico/memory.md when those details should not persist locally. <br>
Risk: Travel logistics, weather, safety conditions, prices, and opening hours can change after the skill content is written. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm high-value bookings, transport, weather, and safety-sensitive plans with official operators or current local sources before acting. <br>
