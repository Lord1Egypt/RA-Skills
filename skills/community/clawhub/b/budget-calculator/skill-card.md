## Description: <br>
装修大包预算计算工具，支持新房和旧房预算计算，提供详细的费用清单。可根据房屋面积和类型自动计算半包施工费、主材材料费以及总预算，并按类别分组展示费用明细。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Homeowners, renovation planners, and agents use this skill to estimate full renovation budgets for new or old homes from area, house type, and optional community name. It returns a budget overview and categorized cost details for labor, materials, and management fees. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Renovation prices are estimates and may differ from actual costs by location, contractor, materials, and project details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the generated budget as planning guidance and verify final costs with local suppliers and contractors before making purchasing or construction decisions. <br>
