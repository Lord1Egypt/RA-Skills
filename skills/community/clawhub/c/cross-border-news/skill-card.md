## Description: <br>
跨境电商资讯查询 Skill，会从多个跨境电商资讯源抓取最新动态，并整理成面向 Shopee、TikTok Shop、Temu、亚马逊、Lazada 等平台的中文 Markdown 简报。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
跨境卖家、运营人员和电商分析人员使用该 skill 获取中文跨境电商资讯日报或按平台、政策、选品、物流支付等主题整理的近期动态。 <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on current public ecommerce news sources, so articles may be incomplete, unavailable, duplicated, or quickly outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Use the linked original sources for important decisions and verify platform policy changes before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The skill contacts public external websites when gathering current cross-border ecommerce information. <br>
Mitigation: Deploy it only where outbound access to those public news and platform sources is acceptable. <br>
