## Description: <br>
Generates Nevada-focused marketing assets for business bankruptcy attorneys, including ads, intake emails, website copy, review responses, referral materials, and JSON-LD snippets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Nevada business bankruptcy attorneys and legal marketing teams use this skill to draft law-firm marketing campaigns, website content, review workflows, and referral materials that account for Nevada advertising, confidentiality, and bankruptcy-law constraints. Outputs should be reviewed by a qualified Nevada bankruptcy attorney before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States (Nevada) <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal-marketing copy may include eligibility thresholds, bankruptcy-law statements, or advertising claims that are outdated, jurisdiction-specific, or unsuitable for a particular firm. <br>
Mitigation: Install and publish outputs only after a Nevada bankruptcy attorney reviews the content and verifies all thresholds, disclosures, and legal claims against current law. <br>
Risk: Public review responses or referral workflows could expose client confidentiality or imply matter-specific facts. <br>
Mitigation: Rewrite public responses to be fully generic, omit financial details and outcomes, and have counsel approve review and referral language before use. <br>
Risk: Review requests could become selective or conditional in ways that create testimonial and advertising compliance concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Send review requests consistently to all clients, ask only for honest feedback, avoid satisfaction-based gating, and disclose any incentives where applicable. <br>
