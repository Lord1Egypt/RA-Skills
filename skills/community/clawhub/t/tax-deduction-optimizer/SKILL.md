# Tax Deduction Optimizer

## Overview

Maps deduction opportunities, record-keeping gaps, and deduction-versus-risk tradeoffs for freelancers, small businesses, and salaried taxpayers. It is a descriptive planning skill, not tax filing software or legal advice.

## Trigger

Use this skill when the user wants to:
- get structured guidance on tax deduction optimizer
- apply best-practice frameworks to their financial situation
- generate templates and checklists for financial planning

### Example prompts
- "What deductions can a freelance designer usually claim?"
- "Should I take the standard deduction or itemize?"
- "How should I organize travel and meal receipts for audit readiness?"

## Usage Scenarios

1. **User input:** "I'm a freelance designer making $90K/year. What deductions am I missing?"
→ **Expected output:** Deduction audit checklist — home office (simplified vs. regular method calc), equipment §179, software subscriptions, health insurance premiums, retirement contributions (SEP IRA vs. Solo 401K), business travel, professional development — with estimated tax savings per deduction.
2. **User input:** "Set up a year-round tax deduction tracking system for my 10-person agency."
→ **Expected output:** Monthly tracking system — digital receipt capture workflow, mileage log template, home-office documentation checklist, quarterly estimated-tax calculator, and year-end deduction-maximization review process with deadline calendar.
3. **User input:** "I'm W-2 employed but have significant unreimbursed business expenses. What can I do?"
→ **Expected output:** W-2 employee tax strategy — accountable plan negotiation with employer, itemized deduction analysis (state-specific), FSA/HSA optimization, and side-business structuring options to convert unreimbursed expenses into deductible business costs.


### Scenario 4: 年终奖和个税怎么少交点
**User input:** "年终奖马上要发了，听说单独计税和合并计税差很多。怎么算能让我少交个税？还有平时的专项附加扣除我是不是漏填了？"
**Expected output:** 个人所得税优化实操指南——第一步：用个人所得税App查看是否有未填报的专项附加扣除（住房贷款/房租/子女教育/继续教育/大病医疗/赡养老人/3岁以下婴幼儿照护，每一项都有额度）；第二步：年终奖使用个税App里的模拟计算功能比较单独计税vs合并计税，选金额低的；第三步：如果快到税率跳档线（14.4/30/42/66/96万），和HR协商把部分年终奖延后或改成绩效分摊；第四步：检查是否有可以追补的往年扣除项。关键工具：个人所得税App+国家税务总局官网。
