## Description: <br>
Creates targeted, high-converting advertising copy with headline, body, call-to-action, and visual guidance for multi-platform digital marketing campaigns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketers, founders, and growth teams use this skill to draft advertising creative for feed, native, social, and display campaigns. It turns campaign inputs into headline options, body copy, CTA options, creative concepts, audience variants, visual direction, and an A/B testing plan. <br>
