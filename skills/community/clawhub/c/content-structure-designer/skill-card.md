## Description: <br>
Helps content creators design clear outlines and logical structures for tutorials, reviews, stories, opinion pieces, lists, and Q&A content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and writers use this skill to choose a content type, assemble a practical outline, improve flow, and check whether a draft has the expected structure, examples, and calls to action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
