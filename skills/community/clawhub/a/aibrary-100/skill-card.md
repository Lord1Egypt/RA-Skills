## Description: <br>
Aibrary 100 helps agents present Aibrary's curated list of must-read books for the AI age, organized by category and tailored to the user's reading purpose. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[asoiso](https://clawhub.ai/user/asoiso) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to get curated book recommendations across critical thinking, creativity, systems thinking, execution, and human connection. It can filter by category, result count, and purpose, then respond in the user's language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may answer broad book-recommendation prompts using the Aibrary framework when another source or style is desired. <br>
Mitigation: Ask explicitly for a different recommendation source, category framing, or response style when the Aibrary list should not be used. <br>
Risk: Book recommendations and descriptions may be incomplete or inaccurate because the skill is instruction-only and does not verify books against an external source. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before relying on them for purchasing, training, or curriculum decisions. <br>
