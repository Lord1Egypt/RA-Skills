## Description: <br>
AI绘画与AI视频制作的提示词生成助手，支持文生图、图生图、文生视频、图生视频、首尾帧视频等多种生成模式的提示词构建。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bozoyan](https://clawhub.ai/user/bozoyan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, designers, and agent users use this skill to turn image or video generation requests into concise Chinese prompts for text-to-image, image-to-image, text-to-video, image-to-video, and keyframe video workflows. It is especially useful for prompts involving character setup, scene design, camera movement, poses, hairstyles, locations, and traditional Chinese visual styles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may treat broad Chinese cultural or historical terms as a request to generate creative image or video prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated prompt when discussing those topics outside a creative-generation workflow, and clarify intent before using the output in an image or video tool. <br>
Risk: Generated prompts can still contain unsuitable, inaccurate, or overly broad creative details for a target tool or audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit prompts before production use, especially for brand, cultural, historical, or user-supplied reference-image requirements. <br>
