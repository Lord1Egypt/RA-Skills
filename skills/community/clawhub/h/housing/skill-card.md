## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Housing, lease, tenant-screening, tax, and record-retention guidance can vary by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify local law and use qualified legal or tax help before taking action. <br>
Risk: Tenant-screening and application workflows may involve sensitive personal or financial documents. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing unredacted IDs, bank statements, tax returns, credit reports, or background-check results unless there is lawful consent and a clear need. <br>
Risk: Real estate market conditions and prices can change quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current local data from reliable market, registry, MLS, or professional sources before relying on specific figures. <br>
