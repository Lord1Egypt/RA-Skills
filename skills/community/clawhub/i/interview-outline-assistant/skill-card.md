## Description: <br>
Helps B2B product teams design interview outlines, write participant invitations, run interview practice, and turn interview notes into structured review reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zp900830-design](https://clawhub.ai/user/zp900830-design) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, designers, and user researchers use this skill to plan B2B customer interviews, screen and invite participants, practice interview technique, and synthesize findings into prioritized reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Interview notes and quotes may contain participant identities, company details, or sensitive customer information. <br>
Mitigation: Use participant IDs where possible, store notes only in approved confidential locations, and redact identifying company or role details before sharing reports. <br>
Risk: Recording or using customer interviews without permission can create consent and authorization issues. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm authorization for any customer or platform data and obtain recording consent before using the skill with real participants. <br>
