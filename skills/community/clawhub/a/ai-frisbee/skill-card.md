## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Technique videos may contain personal or location details. <br>
Mitigation: Review videos before sharing and avoid uploading footage that contains sensitive people, places, or metadata. <br>
Risk: Sports-coaching guidance may be insufficient for significant injuries. <br>
Mitigation: Use qualified medical advice for significant injuries and treat the skill as general coaching information. <br>
Risk: Incorrectly applied training or technique advice could contribute to strain or injury. <br>
Mitigation: Apply guidance gradually, warm up appropriately, and adapt drills to the user's fitness level and playing context. <br>
