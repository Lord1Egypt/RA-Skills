## Description: <br>
Master Teacher helps AI agents create and deliver structured multi-lesson courses with research prep, mastery checks, Socratic questioning, and persistent progress tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jacky-wzj](https://clawhub.ai/user/jacky-wzj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, technical learners, and external users use this skill when they want an agent to research a topic, build a curriculum, teach lessons one at a time, verify understanding, and maintain progress across sessions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill writes local course notes and progress history that may contain sensitive personal learning context or confidential project details. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only in workspaces where persisted course files are acceptable, avoid entering sensitive details into lesson responses or profile files, review USER.md and MEMORY.md for private data, and delete generated course directories when the history is no longer needed. <br>
