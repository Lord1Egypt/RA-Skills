## Description: <br>
为文章规划并生成可读性强的极简强线条手绘配图，先分析文章结构并选择图型，再生成构思卡、提示词和插图交付物。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bettermen](https://clawhub.ai/user/bettermen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, content teams, and agents use this skill to turn article passages into readable hand-drawn explanatory illustrations. It is suited for choosing illustration locations, producing concept cards, writing image prompts, and generating 16:9 article artwork. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Article-derived details may be sent to an image generation tool or saved in local illustration, prompt, and planning outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive article content when using the skill, or ask the agent to show the plan before image generation. <br>
Risk: Source articles may mention real public figures or protected characters that are inappropriate to reproduce directly in generated artwork. <br>
Mitigation: Use symbolic objects, silhouettes, back views, or article-specific non-infringing visual cues instead of recreating faces or copyrighted characters. <br>
