## Description: <br>
Guides users through UX and service design workflows with stage-based methods, templates, and local generators for research, analysis, prototyping, usability testing, and design reporting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kingsunzhang-openclaw](https://clawhub.ai/user/kingsunzhang-openclaw) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, instructors, and design teams use this skill to choose the next step in a UXD or service design process, structure user research and competitor analysis, plan low-fidelity prototypes, run usability tests, and prepare design reports or presentation scripts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: License metadata and artifact documentation do not fully agree, and the README includes separate commercial authorization language. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the governing license and commercial-use terms before using the skill in a commercial workflow. <br>
Risk: Local generator scripts can overwrite a path supplied with --output. <br>
Mitigation: Run scripts in a project or scratch directory and review output paths before execution. <br>
