## Description: <br>
Generate GDPR/CCPA-compliant privacy policies. Use when scanning data practices, auditing consent flows, checking compliance, drafting policy documents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers can use this skill to draft privacy-policy text and compliance checklists for web or app data practices. Generated policies should be reviewed by qualified legal and operational reviewers before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The privacy-policy generator can produce incomplete or misleading legal guidance if treated as proof of GDPR/CCPA compliance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated policy text as a drafting aid and require qualified legal and operational review before publication or deployment. <br>
Risk: An unrelated bundled security-tool script silently logs command arguments locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid passing sensitive company details, secrets, customer data, or policy text to those commands unless the script is removed or its logging behavior is disabled and disclosed. <br>
