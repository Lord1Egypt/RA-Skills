## 1. Exterior-Releasing Formulas (解表剂)

### Mahuang Tang (麻黄汤) — Ephedra Decoction
- **Origin:** Shanghan Lun (伤寒论)
- **Category:** Pungent-warm exterior-releasing (辛温解表)
- **Composition:** Mahuang 9g, Guizhi 6g, Xingren 6g, Gancao 3g
- **Action:** Releases exterior cold, promotes lung qi
- **Indication:** Wind-cold exterior excess (风寒表实证) — aversion to cold, fever, no sweating, body aches, dyspnea, floating tight pulse
- **Key DX:** No sweating + body aches + floating tight pulse
- **配伍结构:** 麻黄+桂枝=相须发汗 (mutual reinforcement for sweating); 麻黄+杏仁=宣降相因 (open-descend synergy); 甘草=调和缓峻
- **方歌:** 麻黄汤中用桂枝，杏仁甘草四般施，发热恶寒头项痛，喘而无汗服之宜
- **Modifications:** +Shengjiang (生姜) +Mahuang; -Guizhi = San'ao Tang (三拗汤) for cough
- **Cautions:** Sweating too profuse → dehydration. Contraindicated in exterior deficiency, Yin deficiency, blood loss

### Guizhi Tang (桂枝汤) — Ramulus Cinnamomi Decoction
- **Origin:** Shanghan Lun (伤寒论)
- **Category:** Pungent-warm exterior-releasing (辛温解表)
- **Composition:** Guizhi 9g, Baishao 9g, Shengjiang 9g, Dazao 4 pieces, Gancao 6g
- **Action:** Releases exterior, harmonizes ying/wei (解肌发表, 调和营卫)
- **Indication:** Wind-cold exterior deficiency (风寒表虚证) — aversion to cold, fever, sweating, weak pulse; also for ying-wei disharmony
- **Key DX:** Sweating + aversion to wind + floating moderate pulse
- **配伍结构:** 桂枝+白芍=散中有收 (disperse with astringe — balance); 生姜+大枣=调和营卫 (harmonize ying-wei); 甘草=调和诸药
- **方歌:** 桂枝汤治太阳风，芍药甘草姜枣同，解肌发表调营卫，表虚自汗此为宗
- **Modifications:** +Gegen = Guizhi Jia Gegen Tang (桂枝加葛根汤) for neck stiffness; +Houpo +Xingren for asthma
- **Cautions:** Contraindicated in interior heat, alcohol intoxication

### Yinqiao San (银翘散) — Lonicera & Forsythia Powder
- **Origin:** Wenbing Tiaobian (温病条辨)
- **Category:** Pungent-cool exterior-releasing (辛凉解表)
- **Composition:** Yinhua (Jinyinhua) 15g, Lianqiao 15g, Jiegeng 6g, Niubangzi 9g, Bohe 6g, Jingjiesui 6g, Douchi 6g, Zhuye 6g, Gancao 5g, Xian Lugen (fresh)
- **Action:** Releases exterior heat, clears toxicity
- **Indication:** Wind-heat exterior (风热表证) — fever, slight chills, headache, sore throat, red tongue tip, thin yellow coating, floating rapid pulse
- **Modifications:** +Xuanfu for severe cough; +Shengma +Banziao for sore throat; -Jingjie +Baimaogen for nosebleed
- **Cautions:** Not for wind-cold exterior patterns

### Sangju Yin (桑菊饮) — Mulberry Leaf & Chrysanthemum Decoction
- **Origin:** Wenbing Tiaobian (温病条辨)
- **Composition:** Sangye 8g, Juhua 6g, Xingren 6g, Lianqiao 6g, Bohe 3g, Jiegeng 6g, Gancao 3g, Lugen 6g
- **Action:** Releases wind-heat, stops cough
- **Indication:** Wind-heat exterior cough — mild fever, cough, slight thirst

### Xiao Qinglong Tang (小青龙汤) — Minor Blue-Green Dragon Decoction
- **Origin:** Shanghan Lun (伤寒论)
- **Composition:** Mahuang 9g, Guizhi 9g, Ganjiang 9g, Xixin 3g, Banxia 9g, Wuweizi 6g, Baishao 9g, Gancao 6g
- **Action:** Releases exterior, warms and transforms phlegm fluids
- **Indication:** Exterior cold + interior fluids (外寒内饮) — aversion to cold, fever, no sweating, cough with white frothy phlegm, dyspnea
- **配伍结构:** 麻+桂=解表散寒; 姜+辛+夏=温化水饮; 五味子+白芍=散中有收 (prevent over-dispersing); 甘草=调和 — **散中有收, 开中有合**
- **方歌:** 小青龙汤麻桂芍，细辛干姜五味草，半夏温肺化寒饮，外寒内饮此方好
- **Modifications:** +Shigao for interior heat

## 2. Interior-Clearing Formulas (清热剂)

### Baihu Tang (白虎汤) — White Tiger Decoction
- **Origin:** Shanghan Lun (伤寒论)
- **Composition:** Shigao 30g, Zhimu 9g, Gancao 3g, Jingmi 9g
- **Action:** Clears qi-level heat
- **Indication:** Yangming meridian heat — high fever, profuse sweating, extreme thirst, surging large pulse (四大症: 大热, 大汗, 大渴, 脉洪大)
- **配伍结构:** 石膏+知母=辛寒折热 (pungent-cold breaks heat); 粳米+甘草=护胃生津 (protect stomach, generate fluids)
- **方歌:** 白虎汤中用石膏，知母甘草粳米熬，大热大汗兼大渴，气分热盛此方高
- **Modifications:** +Renshen = Baihu Jia Renshen Tang for deficiency with heat; +Cangzhu for damp-heat
- **Cautions:** Contraindicated in true cold false heat; contraindicated when pulse is not surging

### Qingying Tang (清营汤) — Clear Nutritive Level Decoction
- **Origin:** Wenbing Tiaobian (温病条辨)
- **Composition:** Shuiniujiao 30g, Shengdi 15g, Xuanshen 9g, Zhuye 3g, Maidong 9g, Danshen 6g, Huanglian 5g, Yinhua 9g, Lianqiao 6g
- **Action:** Clears ying-level heat
- **Indication:** Heat entering ying level — fever worse at night, restless, faint rash, crimson tongue

### Huanglian Jiedu Tang (黄连解毒汤) — Coptis Toxin-Resolving Decoction
- **Composition:** Huanglian 9g, Huangqin 6g, Huangbai 6g, Zhizi 9g
- **Action:** Clears fire and toxicity in all three burners
- **Indication:** Excess heat-toxin pattern — high fever, mania, jaundice, carbuncles, dark urine

### Longdan Xiegan Tang (龙胆泻肝汤) — Gentiana Liver-Draining Decoction
- **Composition:** Longdancao 6g, Huangqin 9g, Zhizi 9g, Zexie 9g, Cheqianzi 6g, Chuanmuxiang 6g, Shengdi 9g, Danggui 3g, Gancao 6g, Chaihu 6g
- **Action:** Drains liver fire, clears damp-heat from liver/gallbladder
- **Indication:** Liver fire upward attack (headache, red eyes, tinnitus); Damp-heat in liver channel (genital sores, yellow vaginal discharge)
- **Cautions:** Cold nature, short-term use only

## 3. Interior-Warming Formulas (温里剂)

### Sini Tang (四逆汤) — Frigid Extremities Decoction

- **Composition:** Fuzi 5-15g (先煎), Ganjiang 6-9g, Gancao 6g

- **Modifications:** +Zhi'bo = Zhibai Dihuang Wan (知柏地黄丸) for yin deficiency with heat; +Juhua +Gouqi = Qi Ju Dihuang Wan (杞菊地黄丸) for yin deficiency affecting eyes
