## Description: <br>
Researches SEO keywords for a topic and generates a Markdown article brief with primary and secondary keywords, competitor gap analysis, article structure, and SEO recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kryzl19](https://clawhub.ai/user/kryzl19) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content marketers, SEO specialists, and developers use this skill to research keyword opportunities from public search results and competitor pages, then draft an article brief for content planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fetched public search results and competitor pages are untrusted and may contain misleading content or prompt-injection text. <br>
Mitigation: Treat fetched pages as untrusted evidence, validate important claims independently, and review the generated brief before using it for publication planning. <br>
Risk: Generated SEO recommendations may be inaccurate, outdated, or poorly matched to the target audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review the brief against current search results, analytics data, and editorial requirements before publishing or assigning content. <br>
