## Description: <br>
Auto Dealer Pro helps new-energy vehicle dealerships create showroom reception scripts, lead-conversion follow-ups, social media copy, promotion templates, and brand-specific sales talk tracks for the Chinese auto market. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External dealership sales and marketing teams use this skill to draft customer-facing scripts, social media posts, event promotions, and lead follow-up workflows for new-energy vehicle retail in China. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated dealership copy may contain outdated or inaccurate prices, discounts, subsidies, warranty terms, availability, performance claims, or competitor comparisons. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all customer-facing claims against current dealership, manufacturer, legal, and market sources before publishing or using them with customers. <br>
Risk: Lead follow-up workflows may involve customer names, phone numbers, or other personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering unnecessary customer personal data, and use the skill only within applicable privacy, consent, and data-handling requirements. <br>
