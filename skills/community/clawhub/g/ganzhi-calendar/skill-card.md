## Description: <br>
Calculates Chinese lunar calendar dates with Heavenly Stems (天干) and Earthly Branches (地支) for weekly tables or individual Gregorian dates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wingchiu](https://clawhub.ai/user/wingchiu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to generate Chinese calendar tables, check Huangli-style date information, or convert Gregorian dates into year, month, and day stem-branch values. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The lunar-calendar calculation is approximate and may be inaccurate for authoritative calendar, holiday, or cultural-use decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as informational guidance and verify important lunar dates against a dedicated lunar-calendar library or authoritative calendar source. <br>
Risk: Users must trust a local Python script before execution. <br>
Mitigation: Review the small script before running it and execute it in a normal least-privilege local environment. <br>
