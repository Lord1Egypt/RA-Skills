# AI Pet Advisor

Comprehensive pet care guidance covering dogs, cats, small mammals, birds, reptiles, and fish. Breed selection, training, nutrition, health monitoring, and behavior solutions.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Pet Selection
- Breed compatibility matching based on lifestyle, living space, activity level
- Adoption vs breeder guidance, rescue organization resources
- First-time owner breed recommendations
- Multi-pet household compatibility assessment

### 2. Training & Behavior
- Puppy/kitten training: housebreaking, crate training, socialization
- Obedience: sit, stay, come, heel, leave it, recall
- Behavior problems: barking, chewing, scratching, aggression, separation anxiety
- Clicker training, positive reinforcement protocols
- Trick training for mental stimulation

### 3. Nutrition
- Life stage feeding: puppy/kitten, adult, senior
- Dry vs wet vs raw diet comparison
- Breed-specific nutritional needs
- Food allergy identification and elimination diets
- Healthy treat alternatives, toxic foods awareness

### 4. Health & Wellness
- Vaccination schedules, parasite prevention
- Common breed health issues and early warning signs
- Dental care, grooming schedules by breed
- Senior pet care, arthritis management
- Emergency first aid, when to see a vet

### 5. Enrichment
- Interactive toys, puzzle feeders, DIY enrichment
- Exercise requirements by breed and age
- Indoor cat environmental enrichment
- Social needs: dog parks, playdates, daycare
