## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional continuity storage can contain sensitive partner details, commission logic, payout concerns, fraud notes, or compliance incidents. <br>
Mitigation: Create local continuity files only with user consent, keep notes minimal, and avoid storing unnecessary customer-level or confidential financial data. <br>
Risk: Affiliate recommendations can affect payouts, attribution disputes, disclosures, trademark use, or regulated promotional claims. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate legal, brand, compliance, and payout-sensitive decisions for review before launch or scale. <br>
Risk: Broken tracking, coupon mapping, or attribution logic can mislead partner reporting and payout decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Run tracking QA before traffic goes live and whenever offers, payouts, links, coupons, or attribution rules change. <br>
