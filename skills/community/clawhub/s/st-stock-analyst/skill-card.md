## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Stock ratings, price ranges, and turnaround interpretations may be incomplete, stale, or misleading if treated as investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research notes only and verify company filings, exchange disclosures, and current market data before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill directs the agent to search public finance websites and create a PDF report in a local downloads path. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that web research and local PDF creation are acceptable before use. <br>
