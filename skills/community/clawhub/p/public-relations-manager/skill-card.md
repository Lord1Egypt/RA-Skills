## Description: <br>
AI-powered assistant for PR professionals, marketers, and founders to match media contacts, draft PR content, and plan public relations campaigns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ZhenStaff](https://clawhub.ai/user/ZhenStaff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
PR professionals, marketing teams, and startup founders use this skill to identify relevant media contacts, generate press releases and personalized pitches, and organize multi-week campaign plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Risk: PR recommendations or generated outreach content may be unsuitable for a brand, audience, journalist, or announcement context. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt generated media matches, pitches, press releases, and campaign plans before publication or outreach. <br>
Risk: Running install, test, or source commands may use local package, account, or repository state that differs from the intended target. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the package source, repository, and local environment before installing dependencies or running commands. <br>
