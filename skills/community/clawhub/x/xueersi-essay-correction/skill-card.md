## Description: <br>
Grades, analyzes, and suggests improvements for Chinese essays with structured feedback on theme, structure, language, content, conventions, and revision examples. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhouzhi-tal](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhouzhi-tal) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, teachers, and education assistants use this skill to produce concise Markdown feedback for Chinese essays, including scoring, strengths, issues, targeted rewrites, comments, and practice suggestions. It is intended to guide revision rather than rewrite the full essay. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Student essays can contain personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting unnecessary personal information and review shared essay text before submitting it to an agent. <br>
Risk: The skill text presents Xueersi branding, but the server evidence does not prove official Xueersi affiliation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the publisher as the server-resolved third-party account zhouzhi-tal unless separate verification confirms an official affiliation. <br>
Risk: Essay feedback may be subjective or may miss context about grade level, assignment requirements, or classroom rubric. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated feedback before relying on it and provide the grade level or rubric when precise assessment is needed. <br>
