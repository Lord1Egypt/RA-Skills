## Description: <br>
Dental health advisor providing oral hygiene guidance, dental condition information, treatment explanations, and pain management advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer dental health questions, explain oral conditions and procedures, assess urgency, and provide oral hygiene or interim pain-management guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat dental guidance as professional diagnosis or emergency care. <br>
Mitigation: Present responses as general information and direct severe pain, facial swelling, fever, trauma, uncontrolled bleeding, or trouble swallowing or breathing to urgent dental or emergency care. <br>
