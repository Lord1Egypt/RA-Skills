## Description: <br>
Analyzes internal link structure, authority flow, orphan pages, anchor text, and topic clusters, then produces a prioritized linking plan with source, target, and anchor recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aaron-he-zhu](https://clawhub.ai/user/aaron-he-zhu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
SEO practitioners, content teams, and site owners use this skill to audit internal link architecture and produce prioritized recommendations for orphan pages, anchor text, topic clusters, navigation, and contextual links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Connected crawler or analytics accounts can expose site data outside the intended analysis scope. <br>
Mitigation: Connect only accounts for sites the user is authorized to analyze, and limit access to the pages and metrics needed for the audit. <br>
Risk: Saved audit summaries may persist site priorities, open issues, URLs, or similar operational details. <br>
Mitigation: Allow memory saves only when persistence is intended, and review the summary before storing it. <br>
Risk: Internal link recommendations can reduce usability or search performance if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed source pages, target pages, anchor text, and priorities before making site changes. <br>
