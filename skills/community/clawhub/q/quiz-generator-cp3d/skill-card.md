## Description: <br>
Quiz Generator helps agents draft psychology tests, personality quizzes, surveys, scoring logic, result-page copy, and shareable quiz content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, content creators, product teams, and developers use this skill to generate quiz or questionnaire drafts with answer options, scoring rules, result categories, and result explanations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated psychology or mental-health quizzes may be mistaken for diagnosis or medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present generated quizzes as entertainment or self-reflection content, avoid diagnostic claims, and direct users with health concerns to qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Generated questions, scoring rules, or result explanations may be misleading, biased, or unsupported for a real assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Review question wording, scoring logic, result categories, and final copy before publication or operational use. <br>
