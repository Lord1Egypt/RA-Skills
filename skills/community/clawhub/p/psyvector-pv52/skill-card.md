## Description: <br>
PsyVector pv52 is a Chinese-language crisis-consultant persona that helps agents classify crises, prioritize risks, plan immediate actions, and structure stakeholder communication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to adopt a calm crisis-consultant role for risk triage, emergency decision framing, stakeholder planning, and crisis response checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to use tools and retrieve unspecified internal data without clear permission boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before web searches or internal data access, and define which internal sources the agent may read before enabling connectors. <br>
Risk: Crisis guidance can be misleading if it is based on incomplete or sensitive incident details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential incident details with search tools and have domain, legal, or compliance reviewers validate high-impact recommendations. <br>
