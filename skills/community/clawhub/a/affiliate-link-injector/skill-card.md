## Description: <br>
Scans content for product mentions and helps insert tracked affiliate links with FTC compliance disclosures. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ncreighton](https://clawhub.ai/user/ncreighton) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content creators, bloggers, ecommerce marketers, and agencies use this skill to find monetization opportunities, add affiliate links, and draft disclosure language for content they provide. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may require publishing or platform credentials and can affect public content. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped, revocable tokens stored outside chat, test on drafts or copies, keep backups, and review changes before publishing. <br>
Risk: Automatically inserted affiliate links or disclosures could be incorrect, incomplete, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Keep AUTO_INJECT disabled unless live edits are intended, and review every proposed link and disclosure for accuracy and compliance. <br>
