## Description: <br>
Generate tailored 7-day social media content calendars with hooks, drafts, hashtags, posting times, and engagement tips for X/Twitter and Moltbook. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Gpunter](https://clawhub.ai/user/Gpunter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, founders, and agent operators use this skill to plan a week of social posts around a topic, product, launch, or narrative. It helps draft platform-specific post angles, copy, hashtags, posting times, and engagement guidance for X/Twitter and Moltbook. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated posts could include private metrics, revenue details, people, brands, or performance claims that are inaccurate or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated posts before publishing, especially claims involving private data, revenue, named people, brands, or expected reach. <br>
Risk: Suggested posting times, content mix, and estimated reach are drafting guidance rather than verified performance forecasts. <br>
Mitigation: Treat planning guidance as a starting point and adjust it using the publisher's own analytics, audience norms, and approval process. <br>
