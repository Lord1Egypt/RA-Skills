## Description: <br>
Log home maintenance tasks, set reminders, and track repair history with checklists for scheduling repairs, tracking appliance upkeep, and inventorying household items. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xueyetianya](https://clawhub.ai/user/xueyetianya) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use Maintenance to record home maintenance tasks, schedule reminders, track appliance inventory and upkeep, log costs, generate reports, and export local records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local maintenance logs and exports may contain appliance details, schedules, costs, or other household information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets or access codes, protect backups of ~/.local/share/maintenance, and delete old logs or export files when they are no longer needed. <br>
