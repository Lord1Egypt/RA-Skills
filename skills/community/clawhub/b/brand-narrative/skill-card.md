## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad business-operations language could be mistaken for legal, financial, compliance, vendor-management, or operational advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as brand and narrative support only; have qualified domain experts review and tailor recommendations before using them for regulated or operational decisions. <br>
Risk: Brand prompts may include confidential business, customer, or strategy information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for the surrounding agent environment and redact sensitive details when possible. <br>
Risk: Incomplete or inaccurate input can lead to generic or poorly targeted brand narratives. <br>
Mitigation: Provide clear objectives, target audience, constraints, and source material, then review outputs for fit before publication. <br>
