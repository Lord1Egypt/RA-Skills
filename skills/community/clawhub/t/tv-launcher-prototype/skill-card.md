## Description: <br>
Generates Smart TV launcher and 10-foot UI frontend prototypes with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, including remote-control navigation, focus animation, scroll-follow behavior, and dark TV interface patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ysredcity](https://clawhub.ai/user/ysredcity) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Frontend developers and designers use this skill to create browser-openable Smart TV and OTT interface prototypes with keyboard-simulated remote control behavior. It is suited for launcher screens, movie detail pages, settings pages, and other large-screen experiences that require visible focus states and TV-style navigation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated TV interfaces are prototypes and may not reflect real device APIs, platform constraints, or production accessibility requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review and test generated HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the target device or emulator before using it in a production TV application. <br>
Risk: The skill is documented primarily in Chinese and may respond in Chinese by default. <br>
Mitigation: State the desired response language when invoking the skill if a different language is required. <br>
