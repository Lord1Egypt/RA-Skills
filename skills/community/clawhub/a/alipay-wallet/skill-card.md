### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill discusses sensitive Alipay financial records, transaction exports, tax details, and account activity. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide passwords, SMS codes, cookies, unrestricted transaction exports, or sensitive account records; keep verification inside the official Alipay app. <br>
Risk: Balances, investment details, tax information, and transfer guidance may be incomplete or misleading if treated as authoritative. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as general guidance only and verify financial decisions, tax details, and transfers through official Alipay records and qualified professional advice when needed. <br>
