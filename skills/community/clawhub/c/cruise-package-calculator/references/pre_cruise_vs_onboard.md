## Embarkation Day Kiosk Strategy

**Critical insight**: On Royal Caribbean, Celebrity, and Carnival, the **embarkation day price** at the cruise terminal kiosk is sometimes 5–10% lower than the pre-cruise online price. This is a price-discrimination tactic for last-minute buyers.

**Recommendation flow**:
1. Lock in pre-cruise price as a "ceiling".
2. Cancel the pre-cruise package within the cancellation window if a better embarkation deal appears.
3. Most cruise lines refund pre-cruise package purchases up to 24–48 hours before sailing.

## Sale Calendar (Recurring)

- **January (Wave Season)**: best overall pricing, biggest pre-cruise drink discounts
- **Black Friday / Cyber Monday**: 25–30% off bundles at Royal, NCL, Carnival
- **September "Cruise Week"**: large industry-wide promo
- **Memorial Day / Labor Day**: 5–10% off, smaller deal
- **Avoid buying at full price between these windows** unless boarding within 14 days
