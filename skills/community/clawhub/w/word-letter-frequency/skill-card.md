## Description: <br>
Counts how many times each letter appears in a word or short phrase. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[simmusjune](https://clawhub.ai/user/simmusjune) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to answer short text-analysis requests by counting per-character frequency in a word or short phrase, with options for case sensitivity, non-alphabetic characters, and JSON output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Letter counts can be misleading if case handling or punctuation filtering is not stated. <br>
Mitigation: State whether default normalization was used or which flags were applied when presenting results. <br>
