## Vendor Selection Criteria

When evaluating packaging vendors, score them across these dimensions:

### Evaluation Scorecard

| Criterion | Weight | Key Questions |
|---|---|---|
| Print and production quality | 25% | Request samples; check color consistency and structural integrity |
| Pricing and volume flexibility | 20% | Get quotes at 3 volume tiers; understand price breaks |
| Minimum order quantities | 15% | MOQs for custom vs. semi-custom options |
| Lead time reliability | 15% | Standard lead time, rush capability, on-time delivery rate |
| Sustainability credentials | 10% | FSC certification, recycled content percentages, carbon programs |
| Material range and innovation | 10% | Sustainable alternatives, new materials, specialty sourcing |
| Customer service | 5% | Response time, dedicated account manager, problem resolution |

Must-haves: MOQs compatible with your volume, 3-6 week lead times, sample capability, regulatory compliance (FDA for food, CPSC for children's products), and documented QC processes.

## Cost Optimization Strategies

### Volume-Based Savings

1. **Consolidate vendors**: Multi-component orders from one vendor unlock bundled pricing.
2. **Annual commitments**: Staggered annual volume reduces per-unit costs 10-25% vs. per-order.
3. **Standard sizes**: Design within standard box and card dimensions to avoid tooling charges.
4. **Gang printing**: Print multiple insert variants together on a single sheet run.

### Design-Based Savings

5. **Reduce print colors**: 2-color printing cuts box print costs 20-35% vs. CMYK.
6. **Stickers over full print**: Kraft box + premium sticker achieves 80% brand impact at 40% cost.
7. **Multi-purpose elements**: Cards that double as bookmarks or coasters eliminate a component.
8. **Simplify structures**: Tuck-flap closure ($0.10) vs. magnetic closure ($1-3) — evaluate ROI.

### Operational Savings

9. **Flat-pack shipping**: Boxes that ship flat save on storage and inbound freight.
10. **Right-size packaging**: 2-3 box sizes covering your product range minimizes void fill waste.
11. **Negotiate freight**: Include inbound freight in vendor contracts.
12. **Seasonal simplification**: Simplified tier for peak seasons maintains brand with fewer labor steps.
