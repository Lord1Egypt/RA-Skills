## Description: <br>
Converts novel chapters into cinematic storyboard scripts with shot numbers, scene headings, visual direction, audio cues, and editing notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zjx15296694073](https://clawhub.ai/user/zjx15296694073) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, filmmakers, and creative teams use this skill to turn pasted or uploaded novel text into a professional shot-by-shot storyboard script for screen adaptation planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded or pasted source material may include unpublished, private, or sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Review the input material before use and avoid sending confidential text to optional external image-generation or API steps unless approved. <br>
Risk: Generated storyboard scripts may contain adaptation choices or paraphrases that do not match the author's intent. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human reviewer check the generated shots, quoted text, and scene structure before production use. <br>
