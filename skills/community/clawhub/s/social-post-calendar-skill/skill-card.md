## Use Case: <br>
External users and marketing operators use this skill to plan reusable social post calendars with concise plans, checklists, next actions, and editable draft copy while avoiding secrets and unnecessary personal data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste secrets, credentials, or unnecessary personal data into marketing planning prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for goals and constraints without passwords, OTPs, private keys, wallets, session secrets, or avoidable PII. <br>
Risk: Draft social content may include material the user does not have rights to use. <br>
Mitigation: Use only notes the user owns or is authorized to adapt, and review draft copy before publication. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes an optional external donation link. <br>
Mitigation: Treat donations as optional and verify the external destination before following or sharing it. <br>
