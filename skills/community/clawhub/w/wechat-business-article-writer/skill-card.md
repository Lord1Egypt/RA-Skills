## Description: <br>
Create WeChat public account style business and technology articles with professional yet approachable tone. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers, marketers, founders, and product teams use this skill to draft WeChat public account articles about business observations, technology reviews, and product thinking in a professional but approachable voice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated articles can contain factual inaccuracies, unoriginal phrasing, or statements that do not comply with WeChat publishing expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Review each article for factual accuracy, originality, tone, and platform compliance before publishing. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include sensitive business details while drafting strategy, product, or market commentary. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including confidential business information unless sharing it with the agent environment is acceptable. <br>
