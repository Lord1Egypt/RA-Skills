Ai Cosmology is a Chinese-language guide for explaining core cosmology topics including the Big Bang, cosmic expansion, dark matter, dark energy, the cosmic microwave background, inflation, large-scale structure, black holes, gravitational waves, LCDM, and observational cosmology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
