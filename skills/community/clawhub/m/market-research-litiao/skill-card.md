## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tavily searches may send market strategy, customer, or competitive research terms to an external research service. <br>
Mitigation: Use a limited or purpose-specific Tavily API key and avoid confidential business plans, customer data, or unreleased strategy in search queries. <br>
Risk: Market forecasts, competitor estimates, and validation results can be outdated, incomplete, or mistaken. <br>
Mitigation: Caveat estimates with methodology and confidence level, verify sources, and escalate regulated industries or statistically valid primary research to qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Market research can be misused as investment advice or overstate certainty. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs framed as probabilistic research, not financial advice or guaranteed forecasts. <br>
