## Description: <br>
nobodybuilt helps users research underbuilt product ideas from text or images, validate gaps across public sources, score concepts, and generate publish-ready project code, README content, and launch copy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kewang0622](https://clawhub.ai/user/kewang0622) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, builders, and product teams use this skill to discover underexplored tool or app ideas, validate demand and competition, then produce a runnable project package with launch materials. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The installer pulls the latest GitHub copy rather than a pinned reviewed release. <br>
Mitigation: Review the fetched source before running it and prefer a pinned, verified revision when repeatability matters. <br>
Risk: GitHub, ClawHub, marketplace, and social publishing steps can create public side effects under the user's account. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review generated files, posts, and exact commands before approving any publishing action. <br>
Risk: Idea validation can produce misleading recommendations if search coverage is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit search evidence for claimed market gaps and treat generated launch advice as a draft for human review. <br>
