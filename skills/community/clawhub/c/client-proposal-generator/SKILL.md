## What This Skill Does

Generates professional, conversion-optimized client proposals for freelancers, consultants, and agencies. Input your service details and client context — get a polished proposal in minutes instead of hours.

Covers 4 core outputs:
1. **Executive Summary** — hook the client with a problem/solution opener
2. **Scope of Work** — clear deliverables, timeline, and exclusions
3. **Pricing & Packages** — tiered options that anchor high and reduce sticker shock
4. **Terms & Close** — next steps, payment terms, and confidence-building guarantee

---

## Prompts

### Prompt 1: Executive Summary
**File:** `prompts/executive-summary.md`  
**Use when:** Opening the proposal — needs to show you understand their problem.  
**Output:** 150–200 word executive summary with problem statement, proposed solution, and 2–3 key outcomes.

### Prompt 2: Scope of Work
**File:** `prompts/scope-of-work.md`  
**Use when:** Defining exactly what you will and won't deliver.  
**Output:** Bulleted scope with deliverables, timeline milestones, revision policy, and out-of-scope clause.

### Prompt 3: Pricing & Packages
**File:** `prompts/pricing-packages.md`  
**Use when:** Presenting investment options.  
**Output:** 3-tier pricing table (Starter / Professional / Premium) with feature comparison and recommended tier callout.

### Prompt 4: Terms & Close
**File:** `prompts/terms-close.md`  
**Use when:** Sealing the deal.  
**Output:** Payment terms, revision rounds, satisfaction guarantee language, and a confident call-to-action close.

---

## Inputs Required

For best results, provide:
- **Your name/company** and service type
- **Client name/company** and their industry
- **The problem** they described (in their words if possible)
- **Your proposed solution** (what you'll do)
- **Rough timeline** (weeks/months)
- **Target price range** (or let the skill suggest based on service type)
- **Your unique angle** — why you vs. anyone else?
