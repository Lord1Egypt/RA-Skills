## Description: <br>
Generates editable WeChat Moments marketing copy from a product or scenario, including emoji layouts, image suggestions, posting times, and engagement prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jessabuivolova-sketch](https://clawhub.ai/user/jessabuivolova-sketch) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, small-business marketers, and private-domain commerce operators use this skill to draft promotional WeChat Moments posts for product promotion, personal branding, community marketing, and time-limited offers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated promotional copy may include false scarcity, unsupported product claims, misleading discounts, pressure language, or unverifiable income stories. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit every generated post before publishing; remove claims that cannot be verified and ensure the offer accurately reflects the product, price, and availability. <br>
