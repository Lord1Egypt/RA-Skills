## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Diagnostic reports or generated recommendations may include logs, headers, request bodies, environment details, or screenshots that contain sensitive project information. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated diagnostic reports and examples before sharing them outside the project. <br>
Risk: Incorrect cache keys, stale-time choices, or optimistic updates can show stale data, mix cached results, or fail to roll back after errors. <br>
Mitigation: Review cache keys, invalidation rules, stale-time settings, and optimistic-update rollback paths before applying the guidance. <br>
