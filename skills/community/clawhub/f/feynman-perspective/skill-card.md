## Description: <br>
这个 skill 让 agent 以费曼启发的思维框架分析问题、审视决策并给出反馈，侧重反自欺、命名与理解区分、货物崇拜检测和具体化解释。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill when they want a Feynman-inspired thinking advisor for testing understanding, detecting cargo-cult behavior, simplifying complex ideas, and challenging weak reasoning. It is intended for reflective analysis and feedback, not as an authoritative representation of Richard Feynman. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses a real-person impersonation style that may be mistaken for Richard Feynman's own statements. <br>
Mitigation: In serious, educational, or public-facing contexts, keep a visible disclaimer that responses use a Feynman-inspired perspective and are not Richard Feynman's actual views. <br>
