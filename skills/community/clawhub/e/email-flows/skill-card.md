## Description: <br>
Designs onboarding, activation, and retention email sequences for SaaS email marketing, drip campaigns, and email automation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, marketers, and operators use this skill to plan SaaS lifecycle email flows for onboarding, activation, trial conversion, retention, and re-engagement. It helps turn product and journey details into email sequences with subject lines, timing, behavioral triggers, metrics, and implementation checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated email copy may contain inaccurate product claims, off-brand positioning, or unsuitable urgency language. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit all generated copy for brand accuracy, truthful claims, and appropriate tone before using it in campaigns. <br>
Risk: Lifecycle email recommendations may omit compliance details such as unsubscribe handling, consent, or region-specific marketing requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Check campaign setup against applicable email marketing and privacy requirements before sending. <br>
Risk: User-provided customer or product data may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing unnecessary customer data and handle any campaign inputs according to the organization's data-handling policies. <br>
