## Description: <br>
A no-code BI analysis skill that cleans CSV/JSON data, calculates business metrics, recommends charts, and generates actionable business insights in Markdown. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[boboy-j](https://clawhub.ai/user/boboy-j) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users, analysts, and developers use this skill to turn CSV/JSON data samples and business context into lightweight BI reports for sales, inventory, user growth, finance, supply chain, marketing, and related operational analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided datasets may contain personal, financial, customer, or confidential business data. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymized or sampled data and avoid pasting sensitive raw records into the agent runtime. <br>
Risk: Generated BI recommendations may be incomplete, misleading, or based on correlation rather than causation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations against business context, source data quality, and domain review before acting on them. <br>
