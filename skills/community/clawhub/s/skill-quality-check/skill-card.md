## Description: <br>
Quality audit for AI Agent Skills; use before installing or after writing any SKILL.md to score five dimensions and get actionable improvements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[webkong](https://clawhub.ai/user/webkong) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, skill authors, and reviewers use this skill to assess AI Agent Skills for trigger quality, progressive disclosure, resource layering, performance impact, and concrete improvement actions before installation or submission. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The audit report could be mistaken for a security approval of a third-party skill. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill as a writing-quality checklist only, and run a separate security review before installing third-party skills. <br>
Risk: Downloaded or reviewed SKILL.md content may be untrusted source text. <br>
Mitigation: Audit only skill files intentionally selected by the user and treat any remote or downloaded skill content as evidence, not instructions to execute. <br>
