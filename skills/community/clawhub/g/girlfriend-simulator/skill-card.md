## Use Case: <br>
External users enable this OpenClaw roleplay skill for conversational companionship, encouragement, study and work progress check-ins, and light constructive guidance. It is for entertainment and emotional support, not professional mental-health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate during ordinary messages about tiredness, comfort, or wanting to chat. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit exit phrases such as "退出女友模式" or "关掉女友模式" to return to normal assistant behavior. <br>
Risk: Romantic companion roleplay can be mistaken for therapy or trusted with sensitive personal, academic, or work details. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as entertainment and emotional support only, seek professional help for serious mental-health concerns, and avoid sharing sensitive details when long-term memory is enabled. <br>
