# Disclaimer

This skill is a trading template and does not guarantee profit.

## Safety rules

- Default to dry-run unless `--live` is explicitly requested.
- Trade only when the market mapping is trustworthy.
- Do not hardcode market IDs.
- Verify the fixture before entering any position.
- Keep public reasoning concise and factual.
- Use conservative sizing and skip thin edges.

The skill is designed to work through ClawHub/Simmer-compatible tooling and should be published only after you have verified the script against a paper-trading or dry-run setup.
