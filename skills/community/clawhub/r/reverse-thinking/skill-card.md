## Description: <br>
Reverse Thinking is a structured reverse-thinking workflow that identifies core assumptions, inverts them, pushes the logic to extremes, and rebuilds multiple feasible solutions into one integrated proposal. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and strategy practitioners use this skill to apply a five-step reverse-thinking process to analysis, planning, and solution design tasks. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate reverse-thinking analysis during ordinary strategy discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly when this analysis style is desired, and review whether its framing fits the task before relying on the output. <br>
