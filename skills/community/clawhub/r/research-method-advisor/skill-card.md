## Description: <br>
研究方法决策助手 helps users choose suitable statistical tests from research goals, data types, group counts, study design, and assumption checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and analysts use this skill to select statistical tests, identify assumptions, find non-parametric alternatives, and locate relevant Python functions for quantitative research planning and analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Statistical recommendations can be inappropriate when the study design, variable types, or assumptions are entered incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify inputs, assumptions, and final method choices against the study context before relying on the recommendation. <br>
Risk: Generated Python examples may not cover all data-cleaning, diagnostic, or reporting requirements for a specific analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Review the suggested functions and supplement them with appropriate diagnostics, effect sizes, corrections, and domain review. <br>
