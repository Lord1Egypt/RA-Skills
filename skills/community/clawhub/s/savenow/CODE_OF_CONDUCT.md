# Code of Conduct

This is a small project, and the rules are simple.

## Be kind

Treat other contributors with respect. Assume good faith. Disagree without being rude.

## Be helpful

When you report a bug, include enough detail that someone else can reproduce it. When you review a PR, focus on the change in front of you rather than the contributor. When you answer a question, link to the answer if you've answered it before.

## Be patient

I maintain this on the side. Replies may take a while. Pinging multiple times doesn't move it up the queue.

## What's not okay

- Personal attacks, slurs, or harassment of any kind toward another contributor.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or trying to game the issue tracker.
- Sharing other contributors' private information without their consent.

## Enforcement

If something here is being broken, open an issue tagged `conduct` or reach out via a private security advisory ([see SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md)) — the same channel works for either.

I'll respond to anything reported here. Possible outcomes: a private conversation, a warning, removing a comment or PR, or banning a contributor from the repo. I'll pick whichever fits.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any direct messages prompted by project activity. It applies to maintainers and contributors equally.
