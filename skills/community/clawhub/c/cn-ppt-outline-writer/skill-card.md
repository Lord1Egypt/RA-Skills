## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external business users use this skill to draft Chinese PPT outlines for workplace reports, business proposals, speeches, product launches, training decks, and annual summaries. It helps collect presentation requirements, choose a narrative framework, generate slide-by-slide structure, draft opening remarks, and suggest visuals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad Chinese workplace presentation terms, which could lead users to share sensitive business details before confirming the task context. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the assistant is helping with a presentation before sharing sensitive business information. <br>
Risk: Generated PPT outlines and suggested data points may include placeholders or unverified business claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated outline and replace suggested figures, comparisons, and examples with verified information before use. <br>
