## Description: <br>
Provides a Sun Tzu-inspired strategic reasoning perspective for planning, competitive analysis, decision-making, leadership, and negotiation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tiger19750107](https://clawhub.ai/user/tiger19750107) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer strategy, competition, negotiation, leadership, and decision questions from a Sun Tzu-inspired perspective. It frames advice through mental models such as winning without fighting, information gathering, initiative control, strength matching, and preparing before action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Strategic guidance may be mistaken for professional legal, ethical, or business-critical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as historical strategy framing and require qualified human review for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes helper scripts that are inert unless run manually and may read or write local input and output paths. <br>
Mitigation: Do not run bundled scripts automatically; inspect the command, inputs, and output paths before any manual execution. <br>
