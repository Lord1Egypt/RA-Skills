## Description: <br>
Guide for disposing specific chemical wastes into the correct colored waste containers, with safety precautions and regulatory compliance notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and lab staff use this skill to look up standard laboratory chemical waste categories, container colors, disposal notes, and safety reminders for specific chemicals or waste types. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Chemical disposal guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for local requirements, mixtures, unknown materials, or regulated waste streams. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as informational and verify against institutional EHS procedures, SDS guidance, and local regulations before disposal. <br>
Risk: The skill is out of scope for emergency spill response, radioactive waste, biological waste, controlled substances, and compliance certification. <br>
Mitigation: Stop rather than guess for out-of-scope requests and refer users to the appropriate EHS, safety, or regulatory process. <br>
