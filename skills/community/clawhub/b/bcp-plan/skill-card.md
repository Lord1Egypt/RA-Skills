## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Operations, risk, compliance, and continuity teams use this skill to draft business continuity plans from business functions, disruption scenarios, recovery objectives, dependencies, current capabilities, and regulatory requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business continuity planning may involve sensitive internal operations, supplier details, recovery objectives, and compliance obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide details appropriate for the agent environment, and remove or generalize sensitive information before sharing generated plans. <br>
Risk: Generated continuity plans may omit organization-specific controls, contacts, regulatory obligations, or recovery constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Have operational, legal, compliance, and security stakeholders review generated plans before using them for preparedness, training, or incident response. <br>
