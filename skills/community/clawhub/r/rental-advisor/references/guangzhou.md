## Guangzhou-Specific Notes

- **Most affordable first-tier**: GZ rents lower than Beijing/Shanghai/Shenzhen
- **"Food city"**: Renting near food streets popular
- **Foshan connection**: Guangfo metro extends to Foshan, cheaper living possible
- **Historical culture**: Liwan/Shamian area has unique character
- **Panyu growth**: Many new communities in Panyu
