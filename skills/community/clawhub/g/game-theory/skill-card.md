## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Governance and MEV attack material is dual-use and could be misapplied to manipulation, exploit planning, or deceptive governance activity. <br>
Mitigation: Use the material for defensive analysis, protocol hardening, and risk review; do not use it to plan manipulation, exploit execution, or deceptive governance activity against real communities or protocols. <br>
Risk: Game-theoretic analysis can be incomplete or misleading if protocol parameters, actor incentives, or market conditions are modeled incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Validate conclusions against current protocol data, threat models, and independent expert review before using them for governance, security, or economic decisions. <br>
