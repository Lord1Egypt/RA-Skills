# Typical Consumption Profiles

Use these baselines when the user does not know their own consumption. Always offer the user a choice — never assume.

## Drink Profiles (per adult, per day)

### Light Drinker
- 1 cocktail or glass of wine at dinner
- 1 specialty coffee in the morning
- 2 sodas / juices throughout the day
- 1 bottled water
- **Estimated à-la-carte cost**: $30–$36/day

### Moderate Drinker (most common cruise persona)
- 3 cocktails or 4 beers across afternoon + dinner
- 1–2 specialty coffees
- 2–3 sodas
- 2 bottled waters
- **Estimated à-la-carte cost**: $58–$68/day

### Heavy Drinker (vacation-mode cruiser)
- 6–8 cocktails / mixed drinks across the day
- 2–3 beers at the pool
- 1 wine at dinner
- 1–2 specialty coffees
- 3 sodas / mixers
- **Estimated à-la-carte cost**: $115–$140/day

### Family with Young Kids
- Adults: 2 cocktails/day each (dinner only)
- Kids: 4 sodas/day each (pool + dinner + dessert)
- 1 bottled water per person
- **Estimated à-la-carte cost (family of 4)**: $90–$110/day

### Couple Celebrating Anniversary
- Champagne brunch: 2 glasses
- Pool drinks: 2 cocktails
- Dinner wine bottle (split)
- After-dinner cocktails: 2
- **Estimated à-la-carte cost (couple)**: $130–$160/day

## Wi-Fi Profiles (per device, per day)

### Casual checker
- 30–60 minutes/day, mostly messaging + email
- Recommended: per-day pass on sea days only

### Social poster
- Posts photos, uses Instagram/TikTok 1–2 hrs/day
- Recommended: streaming-tier package for 5+ night sailings

### Remote worker / digital nomad
- Needs reliable connection 4+ hrs/day
- Recommended: premium streaming, multi-device

### Family with teens
- 2–4 devices, varying use
- Recommended: multi-device package (Royal Caribbean's "The Key" or NCL multi-device add-on)

## Specialty Dining Profiles

### Foodie couple, 7-night cruise
- Wants 4–5 specialty meals
- Almost always: BUY 5-night dining package

### Indifferent diner, 7-night cruise
- Wants maybe 1 specialty meal for an anniversary night
- Almost always: SKIP package, pay à-la-carte

### Family of 4
- Specialty dining adds up fast (4 covers per meal)
- Run the math carefully — 1 specialty dinner for 4 = $140–$200, package for 4 may exceed $400

## Photo Package Profiles

- Most users dramatically overestimate how many photos they will buy à-la-carte.
- Default assumption: average user buys 2–4 photos per cruise without a package.
- Photo packages start to make sense at 5+ photos.
