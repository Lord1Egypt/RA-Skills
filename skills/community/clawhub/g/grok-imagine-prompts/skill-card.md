## Description: <br>
Searches a live YouMind library of community-curated Grok Imagine video generation prompts sourced from X/Twitter creators. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dophinl](https://clawhub.ai/user/dophinl) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to find, browse, and adapt Grok Imagine video prompts from a community prompt library. The agent searches by keyword, summarizes up to three matching prompts, and provides source or gallery links when available. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms are sent to YouMind's live API. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering private, confidential, or sensitive details in prompt searches. <br>
