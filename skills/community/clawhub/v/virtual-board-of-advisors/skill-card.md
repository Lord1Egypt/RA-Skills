## Description: <br>
Virtual Board of Advisors helps users consult documented frameworks from 10 public business thinkers for strategic decisions across growth, pricing, marketing, AI, leadership, culture, and fitness or nutrition product development. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[santaclaw8800](https://clawhub.ai/user/santaclaw8800) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, operators, and business strategists use this skill to get concise advisory synthesis or long-form reports grounded in advisor reference files and the user's business context. It is intended for strategic business guidance, not legal, medical, financial, or licensed professional advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce business recommendations that are incomplete, outdated, or poorly matched to the user's constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as advisory synthesis, require the user's business context, and have users review recommendations before acting. <br>
Risk: Users may seek individualized medical, nutrition, supplement, legal, or financial advice beyond the skill's stated scope. <br>
Mitigation: Keep those topics framed as general information and direct users to qualified professionals for licensed or individualized advice. <br>
Risk: Business context shared with the agent may include sensitive commercial details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for details needed to tailor guidance and advise users to avoid sharing information they would not be comfortable putting into an AI chat. <br>
Risk: Advisor perspectives could be mistaken for impersonation or private opinions. <br>
Mitigation: Use documented public frameworks, keep advisor perspectives distinct, and label extrapolations as inference. <br>
