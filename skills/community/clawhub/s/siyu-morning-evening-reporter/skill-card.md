## Description: <br>
Automatically generates daily morning and evening reports for private communities, combining weather, topical news, benefits or event previews, and styled greetings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Private-community operators, ecommerce teams, brand teams, and paid-community managers use this skill to draft daily morning and evening posts for WeChat or enterprise WeChat groups. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use web or news lookup terms that reveal sensitive community positioning, brand context, or private group details. <br>
Mitigation: Use only non-sensitive inputs for lookup, require approved sources, or provide reviewed news items manually for sensitive communities. <br>
Risk: Generated welfare, event, weather, and news details can be outdated, incorrect, or unsuitable for a specific community. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated report before posting and manually confirm benefits, deadlines, source material, and audience fit. <br>
