## Description: <br>
Transforms AI-generated text into more natural writing using Academic, Casual, Professional, and SEO modes while fixing common AI-writing patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gunybuny](https://clawhub.ai/user/gunybuny) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content teams use this skill to rewrite AI-generated or stiff draft text into natural prose for academic, casual, professional, and SEO contexts while preserving intended meaning, keywords, citations, and tone requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting can introduce incorrect facts, alter meaning, or affect citation accuracy. <br>
Mitigation: Review diffs before accepting edits and verify facts, citations, and preserved keywords after rewriting. <br>
Risk: Humanized output can be misused to bypass academic, workplace, publishing, or disclosure rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only where rewriting is permitted and maintain any required attribution, disclosure, or review process. <br>
