## Description: <br>
Add an emotional safety layer to reminiscence, life review, grief, trauma-adjacent, or otherwise emotionally loaded conversations by detecting distress signals, pausing depth work, grounding the person, and returning control to them. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to add conversational safety behavior for emotionally loaded discussions, especially reminiscence, life review, grief, trauma-adjacent, or sensitive personal topics. The skill helps an agent slow down, avoid probing, ground the person, offer choices, and switch to emergency or professional-help protocols when the situation exceeds conversational support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake conversational de-escalation for therapy or crisis intervention. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as a conversational safety layer and switch to the host system's emergency or professional-help protocols for immediate danger, self-harm risk, medical emergencies, urgent abuse reporting, or severe disorientation. <br>
Risk: Continuing deep questions after distress appears may intensify emotional strain. <br>
Mitigation: Stop depth work, validate the moment, use one simple grounding prompt, offer choices, and resume depth only if the person clearly leads back. <br>
