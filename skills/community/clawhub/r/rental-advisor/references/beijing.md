## Beijing-Specific Notes

- **Hutong culture**: Some rentals in traditional courtyards (平房)
- **North vs South**: North Beijing more desirable (cleaner air historically)
- **Air quality**: Affects rental premiums near parks
- **Work location matters**: Traffic is severe, metro proximity critical
