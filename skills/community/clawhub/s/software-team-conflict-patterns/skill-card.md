## Description: <br>
Helps analyze recurring conflict patterns on software or game projects by identifying collaboration archetypes, behavior patterns, project risks, and practical response strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Software teams, managers, and individual contributors use this skill to reason through recurring collaboration conflicts, identify likely behavior patterns, and choose practical next steps that protect project execution and team trust. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can label coworkers or collaborators too confidently when the situation is ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Treat archetype names as tentative behavior patterns, distinguish observed facts from interpretation, and avoid using the output as a diagnosis or formal HR finding. <br>
Risk: Workplace conflict descriptions may include names, company details, or protected HR information. <br>
Mitigation: Anonymize names, companies, and sensitive employment details before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Users may be facing harassment, retaliation, legal exposure, or employment decisions beyond the skill's advisory scope. <br>
Mitigation: Use official workplace, HR, legal, or safety channels for high-stakes employment matters rather than relying on the skill's output. <br>
