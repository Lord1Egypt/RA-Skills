## Description: <br>
Organizes public Zhangyue/iReader product pages, book library entry points, membership information, help content, activity pages, and copyright notices into compliant summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codenova58](https://clawhub.ai/user/codenova58) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to summarize publicly visible Zhangyue/iReader website information, including book categories, rankings, membership benefits, help center content, download constraints, device support, activity pages, and copyright notices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Membership terms, copyright details, and regional availability may change or vary by location. <br>
Mitigation: Verify summaries against official Zhangyue/iReader pages before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Requests could drift toward private account data, pirated resources, member sharing, or interface bypass guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs limited to publicly visible information and decline private, infringing, or bypass-oriented requests. <br>
