## Description: <br>
Turn complex business goals into navigable strategic maps that identify milestones, resource nodes, risk points, bottlenecks, and route logic so users can see the terrain before they move. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIsearch](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIsearch) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, strategists, and teams planning complex initiatives use this skill to turn ambiguous business goals into strategic route maps with milestones, dependencies, risk zones, bottlenecks, and next-step recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad planning language and produce strategic guidance outside the user's intended scope. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the user wants a strategic map when intent is ambiguous, and ask for missing destination, starting point, or constraint details before producing a detailed map. <br>
Risk: Strategic plans can involve confidential business context and may be mistaken for legal, financial, or operational sign-off. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential details unless appropriate for the agent environment, and require qualified review for legal, financial, operational, or domain-specific decisions. <br>
