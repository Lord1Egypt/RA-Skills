## Description: <br>
Dream Journal records dreams, learns recurring dream imagery, generates poetic titles, and supports keyword search and statistical analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[michealxie001](https://clawhub.ai/user/michealxie001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
OpenClaw users use this skill to keep a local dream journal that records dream entries, learns personal imagery tags, and later searches or summarizes dream patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Dream descriptions and derived imagery tags may contain sensitive personal information and are retained in local OpenClaw workspace memory files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid recording highly sensitive dreams in shared or backed-up workspaces, and review or delete the memory files when long-term retention is not desired. <br>
