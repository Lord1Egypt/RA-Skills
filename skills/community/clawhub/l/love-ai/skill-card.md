## Description: <br>
A Chinese response-style skill that guides an assistant to answer with emotional awareness, classical Chinese quotations, warm empathy, and hopeful closing language. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nitu-ni](https://clawhub.ai/user/nitu-ni) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to make assistant replies consistently warmer, Chinese-language oriented, and grounded in classical quotations and emotional support patterns. It is best suited for opted-in conversational experiences rather than strict technical, legal, medical, crisis, or machine-readable workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can heavily reshape every assistant reply with a warm Chinese style, classical quotations, and hopeful endings. <br>
Mitigation: Enable it only for opted-in Chinese conversational experiences where that tone is desired. <br>
Risk: The response style may conflict with technical work, strict JSON or other fixed output formats, legal or medical contexts, crisis support, or users who did not ask for this tone. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid enabling it globally; scope activation away from high-precision, regulated, crisis, or machine-readable workflows. <br>
