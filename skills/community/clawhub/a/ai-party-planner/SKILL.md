# AI Party Planner

Event planning for birthdays, weddings, holidays, corporate events, and themed parties. Budgeting, venue, catering, entertainment, timeline management.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Event Types
- Birthday: kids, milestone, surprise, adult themes
- Wedding: ceremony, reception, rehearsal dinner, bridal shower
- Holiday: Christmas, Halloween, New Year, Thanksgiving, Diwali, Lunar New Year
- Corporate: team building, holiday party, product launch, gala
- Themed: decades, masquerade, casino night, garden, beach

### 2. Planning & Budgeting
- Budget breakdown: venue, food, drinks, decor, entertainment, contingency
- Guest list management, RSVP tracking, dietary restriction handling
- Timeline creation: 3-month, 1-month, 1-week, day-of schedules
- Vendor research, contract review, deposit management

### 3. Venue & Setup
- Space calculation: seating, dance floor, buffet, bar areas
- Layout design, traffic flow, accessibility considerations
- Lighting: ambient, accent, special effects
- Seating chart strategies, table arrangement

### 4. Food & Beverage
- Menu planning: passed appetizers, buffet, plated, stations
- Dietary accommodations: vegan, gluten-free, allergies, religious
- Bar service: open bar, cash bar, signature cocktails, mocktails
- Cake and dessert table design

### 5. Entertainment
- Music: DJ vs live band, playlist curation, first dance
- Activities: photo booth, games, trivia, karaoke, dance-offs
- Kids entertainment: face painting, magician, craft station
- Speeches, toasts, awards, ceremony scripting
