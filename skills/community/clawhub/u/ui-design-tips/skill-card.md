## Description: <br>
Apply practical UI/UX design principles to review, critique, and improve interfaces, landing pages, web apps, components, accessibility, UX writing, and conversion flows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, designers, developers, and product teams use this skill to evaluate and improve product UI, landing pages, forms, navigation, pricing pages, empty states, mobile UX, accessibility, and UX copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: UI recommendations may be applied without enough accessibility review. <br>
Mitigation: Review implementations for visible keyboard focus, reduced-motion support, adequate contrast, native or proven accessible controls, and RTL or multilingual layout needs. <br>
Risk: Design and conversion guidance may not fit every product, audience, or regulatory context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as design recommendations and validate them with product requirements, user research, accessibility checks, and human review before release. <br>
