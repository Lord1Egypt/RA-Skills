## Description: <br>
A specialized skill for tracking the US-Iran conflict, strictly focusing on intelligence published within the last 6 hours for hyper-current tactical situation reports on military actions, official statements, and international reactions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to structure near-real-time reports on the US-Iran conflict, emphasizing recent source collection, attribution, triangulation, and clear separation of verified facts from competing claims. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Breaking-news claims about the US-Iran conflict may be wrong, incomplete, or intentionally misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify claims with multiple recent sources and clearly separate verified facts from attributed claims. <br>
Risk: Insufficient verified intelligence may make a complete current report difficult. <br>
Mitigation: State intelligence gaps explicitly and use background context only to support, not replace, verified recent information. <br>
