## Description: <br>
Provides SEO guidance for optimizing H1-H6 heading hierarchy and content structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, marketers, and content teams use this skill to assess page heading hierarchy, recommend one clear H1, draft H2-H6 outlines, and fix common heading structure issues for SEO. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use local project context files to infer target keywords, which could expose sensitive planning notes to SEO-heading work. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive notes in .claude/project-context.md or .cursor/project-context.md if they should not be considered during heading optimization. <br>
Risk: Heading recommendations may be inaccurate when page type, primary keyword, or content outline are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review the suggested H1-H6 outline against the actual page intent and content before publishing. <br>
