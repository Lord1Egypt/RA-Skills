## Description: <br>
Generate model replacement prompts based on user-uploaded model images, replacing the character and scene while preserving clothing and shoes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to generate concise image-editing prompts for replacing a model and scene while preserving the original clothing, shoes, photography style, and season cues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill deliberately forces replacement models to be Chinese, which may be unsuitable for neutral or user-directed image editing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when that demographic constraint is intended; otherwise revise or disable the constraint before use. <br>
