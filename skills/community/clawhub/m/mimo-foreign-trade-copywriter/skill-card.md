## Description: <br>
Helps draft localized multilingual foreign-trade copy, including outreach emails, inquiry replies, product descriptions, exhibition invitations, social posts, and quotation follow-ups for B2B export scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qqyougitcom](https://clawhub.ai/user/qqyougitcom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External trade, export, and B2B sales users use this skill to turn product, market, and buyer information into localized outreach, product, social, quotation, and follow-up copy. It also prompts for missing inputs and checks cultural, terminology, and trade-compliance considerations before finalizing copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy can include incorrect business claims, pricing, lead times, certifications, or compliance statements. <br>
Mitigation: Review product facts, commercial terms, certifications, and target-market compliance before sending or publishing copy. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate too broadly for unrelated trade, compliance, or account-operation tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for foreign-trade copywriting, outreach, quotation, and product-copy tasks; avoid or disable it for unrelated operational work. <br>
Risk: Localized copy may miss cultural or legal nuance for a target market. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the skill to surface assumptions and have a qualified reviewer confirm culturally sensitive phrasing and regulated claims. <br>
