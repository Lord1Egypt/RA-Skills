## Description: <br>
根据用户提供的文本或文件，生成结构清晰、内容详实的中文个人发展复盘报告，涵盖职业、人际、健康等多方面分析。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Westwood-Lin](https://clawhub.ai/user/Westwood-Lin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People who keep journals, notes, or personal records use this skill to turn user-provided text or local files into a structured Chinese review of recent career, social, health, finance, and lifestyle patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal review reports may include sensitive details about health, relationships, finances, work, and daily life. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only text or file paths intentionally meant for summarization, and review the generated report before sharing or storing it. <br>
Risk: The skill can read user-provided local files, so an unintended path could expose unrelated private notes. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit, intended file paths and verify the input source before asking the agent to summarize it. <br>
