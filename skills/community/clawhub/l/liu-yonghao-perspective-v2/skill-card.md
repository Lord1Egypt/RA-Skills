## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business, finance, agriculture, and succession guidance may be inaccurate, outdated, or overfit to a public-figure-inspired viewpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify recommendations against current facts, qualified professional advice, and the user's specific context before acting. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases can activate the persona when the user did not intend that framing. <br>
Mitigation: Use narrower trigger phrases or confirm the desired perspective before applying this skill in ambiguous conversations. <br>
