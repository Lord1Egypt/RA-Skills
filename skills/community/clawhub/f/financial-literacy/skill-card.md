## Use Case: <br>
External users, students, educators, finance professionals, and researchers use this skill to adapt financial explanations, analysis framing, and decision-support guidance to the audience's literacy level while avoiding personalized investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial education may be mistaken for personalized investment, tax, legal, or major financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses educational, avoid specific investment recommendations for individual situations, and direct users to qualified professionals for decisions. <br>
Risk: Market, tax, and regulatory information can become outdated quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current authoritative sources, cite source dates, and flag stale or uncertain data. <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary sensitive financial details while seeking help. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for needed context and discourage disclosure of account numbers, full identity details, or other unnecessary sensitive data. <br>
