## Description: <br>
Need Clarifier helps an agent ask a small set of key questions when a request is vague, clarifying goals, audience, context, inputs, outputs, success criteria, constraints, and must-haves before planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[je44](https://clawhub.ai/user/je44) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill when an initial request is incomplete or ambiguous and should be clarified before solution design. It guides the agent to ask only the most important questions and then organize the clarified need into goal, known information, missing information, and suggested additions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Clarifying questions may ask for goals, constraints, audience, and success criteria that could include sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information needed to clarify the task, and avoid secrets or unnecessary sensitive personal information. <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally delays solution design until enough context is available, which may slow direct execution requests. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for vague or incomplete requests and switch to planning or execution once the missing information is resolved. <br>
