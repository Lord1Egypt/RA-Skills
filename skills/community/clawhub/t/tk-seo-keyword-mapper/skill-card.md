## Description: <br>
Generate comprehensive keyword maps for any niche, including clusters, search intent, difficulty estimates, content briefs, and internal linking strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tktk-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/tktk-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content teams, agencies, SaaS companies, e-commerce teams, bloggers, and freelance SEO practitioners use this skill to turn seed keywords into keyword clusters, content briefs, competitor-gap groupings, internal linking plans, and publishing calendars. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search volume, keyword difficulty, ranking timelines, and competitor-gap outputs may be estimates or become stale without current trusted SEO data. <br>
Mitigation: Validate high-impact SEO recommendations against current trusted sources before relying on them for client strategy, budgeting, or publishing decisions. <br>
Risk: Keyword maps and content calendars may include confidential client strategy or commercially sensitive planning details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential client information unless the model and runtime are approved for that data. <br>
