Marketing, growth, and content teams use this skill to plan or improve customer proof pages, including case study structure, story outlines, metric collection, SEO metadata, and internal linking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger language may activate the skill for adjacent marketing-writing requests beyond customer stories or case study pages. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for customer proof and case-study work, and choose a more specific skill for unrelated landing pages, website copy, or standalone testimonial components. <br>
Risk: Generated customer-story copy may include or imply business outcomes that are not supported by supplied metrics. <br>
Mitigation: Review quoted claims, customer permissions, and result metrics before publishing or relying on the output. <br>
