## Description: <br>
AI Marathon Coach helps runners plan training, analyze running form, choose pacing strategies, manage nutrition, reduce injury risk, select gear, and prepare for races from 5K through ultramarathon distances. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External runners and coaches use this skill for marathon and distance-running guidance, including training plans, race pacing, nutrition, gear preparation, and general injury-prevention education. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on training, pain, or injury-prevention guidance as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present injury-related guidance as general education and direct users with persistent pain, acute injury, or concerning symptoms to a licensed clinician. <br>
Risk: Running data, medical details, or identifiable video could expose sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid uploading sensitive medical information or identifiable video unless the user is comfortable sharing it with the agent environment. <br>
