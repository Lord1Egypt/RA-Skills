## Description: <br>
Audit and quality-review AI agent skills (SKILL.md files) against established best practices from the Nevo Systems skill-writing framework. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhangwei8387](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhangwei8387) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and skill authors use this skill to audit SKILL.md files for trigger quality, structure, conciseness, progressive disclosure, bundled resources, and common skill-writing mistakes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad requests such as reviewing a skill, which could be unintended if the user did not mean a SKILL.md quality audit. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target is an agent skill file before relying on the audit output. <br>
Risk: Review findings may be incorrect or incomplete because the skill provides qualitative guidance about skill quality. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human reviewer check the findings before changing or publishing a skill. <br>
