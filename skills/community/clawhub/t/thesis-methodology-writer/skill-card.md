## Description: <br>
Writes academically rigorous Chinese methodology chapters or methodology sections for theses, dissertations, and undergraduate papers after identifying the study type from user-provided research details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External students, researchers, and academic writers use this skill to draft, expand, polish, or structure Chinese methodology chapters and sections. It supports quantitative, qualitative, mixed-methods, literature-review, and intervention-design methodology writing while preserving clear placeholders for missing study details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generated methodology may be used as a substitute for the user's own research design or institutional academic-integrity requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a drafting aid and review it against institutional rules, advisor requirements, and the actual study design. <br>
Risk: User prompts may include identifiable participant data or confidential study records. <br>
Mitigation: Provide anonymized or generalized research details and avoid entering personally identifiable or confidential information. <br>
Risk: A polished methodology draft can still contain inaccurate assumptions about instruments, analyses, ethics, citations, or local formatting requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all methods, placeholders, citations, ethics language, and analysis choices before submitting or publishing the work. <br>
