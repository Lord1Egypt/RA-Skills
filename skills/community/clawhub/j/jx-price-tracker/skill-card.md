## Description: <br>
Monitor product prices across Amazon, eBay, Walmart, and Best Buy to identify arbitrage opportunities and profit margins. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to search product prices, compare marketplace listings, monitor thresholds, and produce arbitrage or price-history reports for retail decision support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Historical price, trend, volatility, and prediction reports may be simulated while appearing decision-grade. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those reports as unverified unless the publisher replaces the mock history with real sourced data or clearly labels it as simulation. <br>
Risk: Current price comparisons are unverified leads from an external API and extraction workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Verify product price, condition, seller reputation, fees, taxes, shipping, inventory, and return terms directly before purchasing or reselling. <br>
Risk: Product lists, sourcing plans, margins, inventory, or unreleased product details may be sent to SkillBoss. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting confidential commercial data unless sharing it with SkillBoss is acceptable. <br>
