## Description: <br>
Transforms project briefs into testable specifications with user stories and acceptance criteria. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[athola](https://clawhub.ai/user/athola) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and project teams use this skill after brainstorming to convert a project brief into a testable specification with user stories, acceptance criteria, validation strategy, and scope boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow may move from specification writing into planning after docs/specification.md is created. <br>
Mitigation: Use --standalone or explicitly ask the agent to stop after the specification if planning should not begin. <br>
