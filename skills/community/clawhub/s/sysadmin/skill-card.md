## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: System administration guidance can affect accounts, packages, services, permissions, storage, or filesystems on a target host. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands before running them, confirm the target host, and take backups before account, package, service, permission, or filesystem changes. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes operational examples that may be inappropriate for a specific distribution, host role, or production environment. <br>
Mitigation: Validate commands against the host operating system, package manager, service manager, and local change-control process before execution. <br>
