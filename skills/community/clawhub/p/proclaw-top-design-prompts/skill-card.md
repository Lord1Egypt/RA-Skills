## Description: <br>
Generates structured, high-end graphic design prompts from concise brand or campaign inputs, covering brand strategy, visual style, composition, color, typography, quality standards, and output constraints. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thinkbugs](https://clawhub.ai/user/thinkbugs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, designers, marketers, and brand teams use this skill to turn minimal campaign or brand information into detailed image-generation and visual-design prompts. It is suited for posters, brand visuals, social media assets, launch visuals, and other graphic design briefs that need explicit style, composition, color, typography, and quality direction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated campaign prompts may include exaggerated authority, false urgency, fabricated popularity, or pressure tactics, especially in sensitive domains or for vulnerable audiences. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before publication for substantiation, audience sensitivity, and compliance with brand, legal, and platform policies. <br>
