## Description: <br>
Ultimate Sichuan cuisine (川菜) knowledge base with 45+ classic recipes spanning cold dishes, hot dishes, soups, snacks, hot pot/maocai/gan guo, and banquet dishes, all 24 flavor profiles, ingredient guidance, and cooking techniques. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and culinary assistants use this skill to answer Sichuan cuisine questions, provide recipes, explain flavor profiles and techniques, recommend ingredients or substitutions, and plan meals with Chengdu-style assumptions when relevant. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recipe guidance may not account for individual allergies, food safety requirements, heat tolerance, or local ingredient differences. <br>
Mitigation: Users should adapt recipes to dietary needs, safe cooking practices, spice tolerance, and available ingredients. <br>
Risk: Some guidance assumes Chengdu-style ingredients, brands, flavor conventions, or market availability. <br>
Mitigation: Treat regional assumptions as defaults and substitute local equivalents when authentic Sichuan ingredients are unavailable. <br>
