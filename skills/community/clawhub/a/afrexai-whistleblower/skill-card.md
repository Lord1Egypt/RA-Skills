## Description: <br>
Helps an agent draft confidential ethics reporting workflows, whistleblower policies, triage matrices, investigation protocols, retaliation-prevention checklists, and board reporting materials. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Compliance officers, HR leaders, legal teams, and governance consultants use this skill to design or audit anonymous reporting programs and produce policy, investigation, escalation, and board-reporting materials for their organization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated legal, compliance, privacy, retention, or anonymity guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific organization or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Have legal, compliance, privacy, and security teams validate generated policies, anonymity claims, metadata protections, retention periods, and reporting workflows before real use. <br>
Risk: Whistleblower reports can contain sensitive or identifying case details. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste real report details into an AI agent unless the environment is approved for that data. <br>
