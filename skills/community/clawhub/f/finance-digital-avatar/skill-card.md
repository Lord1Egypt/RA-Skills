## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Regulatory and compliance notes may be outdated or incomplete for real financial marketing or customer workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Verify official requirements and route customer-facing avatar content through appropriate financial compliance and consumer-protection review. <br>
Risk: Broad avatar and persona trigger terms may activate the skill when the user only needs generic avatar guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants financial digital avatar guidance before applying finance-specific templates or compliance notes. <br>
Risk: Voice cloning and brand persona guidance could be used to create misleading or insufficiently governed customer-facing avatars. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review of identity, disclosure, branding, and governance choices before publishing or deploying avatar outputs. <br>
