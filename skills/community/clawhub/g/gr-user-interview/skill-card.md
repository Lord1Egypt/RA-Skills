## Description: <br>
A user interview framework for discovering product-market fit, covering recruiting, question design, interview execution, insight synthesis, and decision making. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, founders, and researchers use this skill to plan behavior-focused customer discovery interviews, synthesize pains, workarounds, and quotes, and decide whether to continue, pivot, or collect more data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Interview recordings or transcripts may contain personal, sensitive, or confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Get informed consent before recording, explain data use and retention, store materials securely, and redact sensitive details where possible. <br>
Risk: Poorly framed interviews can produce confirmation bias or misleading product-market-fit signals. <br>
Mitigation: Use behavior-focused questions, avoid pitching during interviews, pause after small batches to synthesize patterns, and review conclusions before product decisions. <br>
