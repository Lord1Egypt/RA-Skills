## The Core Problem

Addiction treatment marketing has three layers of enforcement that postdate virtually all AI training data:

1. **Google and Meta banned addiction treatment advertising in 2017** (fraud, patient brokering, consumer harm). Reinstated in 2018 — but ONLY for LegitScript-certified providers. No AI marketing tool knows this. Every Google or Meta ad it generates for a non-certified treatment center produces a suspended account, not a call.

2. **FTC enforcement since 2019** has specifically targeted addiction treatment marketing for: outcome statistics, guarantee language, and patient brokering arrangements. The FTC's most targeted healthcare advertising category, by enforcement action volume.

3. **42 CFR Part 2 is more protective than HIPAA** for substance use disorder records. Any marketing that implies inter-provider communication, alumni identification, or record sharing without Part 2-compliant consent is a federal violation. No AI tool knows this law exists.

Every general AI marketing tool (ChatGPT, Jasper, Copy.ai, Gemini) generates content that violates all three layers simultaneously. This skill blocks them before content is generated.
