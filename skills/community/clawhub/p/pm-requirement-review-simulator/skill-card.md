## Description: <br>
PRD review stress-test simulator where five cross-functional roles challenge requirements across three difficulty levels and produce a scored HTML survival report with a radar chart and meeting script. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chris1wang3](https://clawhub.ai/user/chris1wang3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers and product teams use this skill to rehearse PRD reviews, gather cross-functional pushback, calculate a survival score, and prepare meeting-ready review assets before a formal requirements review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential PRDs, regulated details, or internal business data for review. <br>
Mitigation: Use only content that is appropriate for agent processing, redact sensitive information where possible, and avoid regulated legal, medical, financial, or internal business data unless the processing environment is approved for it. <br>
Risk: Generated compliance and legal review sections may be mistaken for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill's legal and compliance output as advisory review support and consult qualified professionals for regulated or high-stakes decisions. <br>
