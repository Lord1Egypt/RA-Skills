## Description: <br>
Analyze industry competitive dynamics using Porter's Five Forces framework. Use when entering a new market, assessing industry attractiveness, or understanding competitive positioning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business strategists, founders, product teams, and analysts use this skill to draft a Porter's Five Forces assessment for an industry or market, including force ratings, industry attractiveness, and strategic implications. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated competitive analysis can be incomplete or misleading if market facts, competitor data, or assumptions are outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market facts, data sources, force ratings, and strategic conclusions before using the analysis for business decisions. <br>
Risk: Porter's Five Forces is a static framework and may understate dynamic trends, geographic variation, or regulation. <br>
Mitigation: Supplement the output with current market research and explicitly review trends, geography, and regulatory factors when they affect the industry. <br>
