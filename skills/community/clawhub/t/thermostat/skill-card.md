## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Thermostat changes may affect the wrong device, zone, mode, temperature, duration, or automation schedule. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit confirmation of the thermostat or zone, mode, target temperature, duration, and automation or return date before making smart-home control changes. <br>
Risk: Extreme away or vacation settings may contribute to frozen pipes, humidity issues, mold, or uncomfortable recovery times. <br>
Mitigation: Keep extended-away settings within documented guardrails such as 55°F minimum heat protection, 85°F maximum cooling limit, and a clear return date or geofencing plan. <br>
Risk: HVAC fault symptoms may require professional service rather than continued thermostat adjustments. <br>
Mitigation: Recommend HVAC service for persistent short cycling, systems that cannot reach setpoint, leaks, strange smells, uncleared error codes, large bill spikes, or unusual auxiliary heat behavior. <br>
