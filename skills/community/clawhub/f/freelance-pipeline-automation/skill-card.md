## Description: <br>
Automated freelance pipeline manager for AI agents and independent professionals that scores leads, drafts proposals, and tracks pipeline status in local JSON files. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ssidharhubble](https://clawhub.ai/user/ssidharhubble) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External freelancers and AI-agent users use this skill to score and track freelance leads, generate proposal drafts from profile data, and review a digest of qualified local pipeline entries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's marketing claims overstate automatic job discovery on Upwork, Fiverr, LinkedIn, and niche boards. <br>
Mitigation: Treat it as a local JSON-based lead tracker, add or verify leads manually, and keep FREELANCE_PROFILE and FREELANCE_PIPELINE pointed at a dedicated private folder. <br>
