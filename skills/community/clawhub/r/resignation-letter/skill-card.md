## Description: <br>
Generate professional resignation letters from templates. Use when preparing to resign. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xueyetianya](https://clawhub.ai/user/xueyetianya) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users use this skill to draft resignation letters, handover documents, exit checklists, farewell messages, and resignation-timing guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated resignation and handover documents may contain sensitive career plans, employer information, contacts, or operational details. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated drafts before sharing and omit passwords, private employer secrets, and unnecessary personal information. <br>
Risk: The skill creates a local data directory that may remain on shared, synced, or employer-managed machines. <br>
Mitigation: Remove ~/.local/share/resignation-letter/ after use when retained local files are not desired. <br>
