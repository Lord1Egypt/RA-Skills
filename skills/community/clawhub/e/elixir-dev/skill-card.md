## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Development commands or generated examples may affect the wrong project, environment, or database target. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the current project, MIX_ENV, DATABASE_URL, and database target before running mix commands or applying generated code. <br>
Risk: Examples include potentially destructive database, rollback, formatting, dependency update, and deletion workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Review destructive operations before execution and prefer backups, dry runs, or non-production environments where available. <br>
