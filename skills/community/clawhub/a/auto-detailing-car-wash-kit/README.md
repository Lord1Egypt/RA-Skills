## The Problem With Generic AI Detailing Copy

Generic AI generates detailing copy that sounds professional but creates real liability:

- **"10-year ceramic coating"** — unverifiable without manufacturer spec sheets. FTC Section 5 exposure.
- **"Eco-friendly waterless wash"** — some waterless products exceed CA AQMD Rule 1171 VOC limits. "Eco-friendly" requires FTC substantiation.
- **"Safe on all paint finishes"** — legally unverifiable. One exception = false advertising claim.
- **"Paint correction included"** — without specifying 1, 2, or 3-stage correction, this sets expectations you can't guarantee.
- **"Free on-site water"** — in CA, NV, and AZ, mobile car wash operators are legally required to contain or reclaim wash water runoff. "Free water" means nothing if you're sending it down the storm drain.

This skill knows the difference. Every claim it generates is calibrated to what you can actually defend.
