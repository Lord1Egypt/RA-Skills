## Description: <br>
Provides a curated pack of prompts that help digital sellers generate product ideas, sales copy, email launch sequences, Etsy SEO content, Gumroad descriptions, and promotional assets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators and digital sellers use this skill to prompt an agent for marketplace-oriented product validation, listing copy, launch emails, SEO tags, FAQs, upsells, social posts, and short promotional scripts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing, SEO, or sales copy may be inaccurate, overstate product claims, or be unsuitable for a specific marketplace policy. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt generated copy before publication, especially claims, pricing, platform terms, and product-specific details. <br>
Risk: The README points readers to a commercial Gumroad link for a larger paid prompt pack. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate the external offer separately before purchase or use; it is outside the text-only skill package. <br>
