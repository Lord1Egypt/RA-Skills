## Description: <br>
AI resume scoring and optimization skill that reviews an uploaded resume against a target role, produces multidimensional scores, diagnoses concrete issues, and drafts an improved resume version. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yangmanqi2104201431-ship-it](https://clawhub.ai/user/yangmanqi2104201431-ship-it) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career-support agents use this skill to evaluate a resume for a target role or job description, identify the highest-impact improvements, and generate a concise revised resume draft without inventing experience. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resumes can contain sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact unnecessary identifiers such as home address, ID numbers, and unrelated personal details before use; confirm runtime file retention and privacy behavior for confidential resumes. <br>
Risk: Resume rewriting can imply or add facts that the user did not provide. <br>
Mitigation: Keep generated revisions grounded in the supplied resume and job description, ask for missing metrics when needed, and avoid inventing experience. <br>
