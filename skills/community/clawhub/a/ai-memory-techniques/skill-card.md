## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Memory-health and cognitive-performance tips could be mistaken for professional medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat health-related guidance as general education and seek professional medical guidance for memory concerns. <br>
Risk: Password-memory advice could encourage users to rely on memorized password patterns for real accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use a password manager for real passwords and avoid treating mnemonic password tips as a security control. <br>
