## Description: <br>
Scans HN, Lobsters, Reddit, and tech blogs for community experience reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[athola](https://clawhub.ai/user/athola) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and researchers use this skill to gather practitioner opinions and experience reports about a technology or approach from public community channels such as Hacker News, Lobsters, Reddit, and tech blogs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search queries may reveal the technology or approach being researched to external search or content services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive, confidential, or unreleased topics as queries. <br>
Risk: Community discussions can be subjective, incomplete, outdated, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as opinion signals and verify important claims against authoritative sources before acting on them. <br>
