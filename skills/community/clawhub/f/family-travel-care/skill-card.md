## Description: <br>
Itinerary-first flight support for babies, children, elderly passengers, reduced-mobility travelers, and people carrying medication or medical devices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel-support agents use this skill to prepare practical flight plans for families, elderly passengers, reduced-mobility travelers, and passengers carrying medication or medical devices. It turns trip details into timelines, checklists, service request cards, and airport or airline communication scripts in Simplified Chinese. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trip-specific airline, airport, security, and destination-country rules can change or vary by carrier, route, medical device, medication, and traveler condition. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill output as a preparation plan, then confirm requirements directly with the airline, airport security authority, and destination-country rules before travel. <br>
Risk: The skill may need booking, medical, medication, or device details to produce useful request cards and checklists. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the details needed for the specific trip and avoid unnecessary sensitive health or identity information. <br>
