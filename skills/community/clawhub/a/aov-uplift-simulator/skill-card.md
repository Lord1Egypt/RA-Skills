## Description: <br>
Estimate how bundle uptake, upsell acceptance, or cart-building changes could increase average order value while preserving visibility into margin tradeoffs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce growth, merchandising, and finance teams use this skill to compare bundle, upsell, discount, and cart-building scenarios before launch. It helps pressure-test AOV upside, margin impact, break-even pressure, and go / watch / no-go recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may expose sensitive pricing, cost, margin, refund, or traffic assumptions to their agent environment while modeling scenarios. <br>
Mitigation: Use sanitized or approved commercial inputs unless the deployment environment is cleared for confidential business data. <br>
Risk: The included output template appears unrelated to the AOV simulator workflow and may confuse the expected report structure. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the SKILL.md workflow and review or replace the template before using it as a final reporting format. <br>
Risk: Scenario recommendations can be misleading when assumptions are incomplete or optimistic. <br>
Mitigation: Review baseline inputs, sensitivity points, and break-even logic before using recommendations for offer launches or scaling decisions. <br>
