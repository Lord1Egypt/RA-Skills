## Description: <br>
Use when you need a practical testing playbook for turning product or code changes into a clear test strategy, test cases, and execution checklist. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, QA engineers, and product teams use this skill to turn feature changes, bug fixes, refactors, or requirements into a scoped testing plan, coverage review, prioritized test cases, regression checklist, and release questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated test plans may omit product-specific behavior or reflect unconfirmed assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Review plans against requirements and mark assumptions or manual verification items explicitly. <br>
Risk: Prompts or test-case generation could expose secrets or live customer data if supplied as context. <br>
Mitigation: Use synthetic, minimized, or redacted data when requesting test plans. <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags mention crypto and purchases even though the security summary says they appear unrelated. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm or correct capability tags before relying on marketplace risk filters. <br>
