## Description: <br>
Interview Analysis helps agents turn interview transcripts into structured candidate assessments using role-specific expert-routing frameworks and evidence-focused analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mikonos](https://clawhub.ai/user/mikonos) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Hiring teams and interview reviewers use this skill to analyze interview transcripts, reconstruct candidate evidence, identify red flags, and produce structured Markdown assessment cards for decision review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated hiring notes may contain sensitive candidate information. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in workspaces appropriate for confidential hiring material and restrict access to saved candidate notes. <br>
Risk: Generated recommendations may be biased, incomplete, or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for bias and accuracy, and keep humans responsible for employment decisions. <br>
Risk: Candidate names in paths can expose personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Consider using pseudonymous candidate IDs in analysis folder paths. <br>
