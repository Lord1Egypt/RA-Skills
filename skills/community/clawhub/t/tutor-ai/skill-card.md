## Description: <br>
AI Tutor provides interactive subject tutoring for elementary, middle, and high school students, adapting explanations, questions, practice, and review to the learner's grade and feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[armholder](https://clawhub.ai/user/armholder) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students and learning-support agents use this skill for homework help, subject explanations, guided practice, and study planning across elementary, middle, and high school subjects. It is oriented toward tutoring Chinese learners with adaptive pacing, review prompts, and multimodal explanation options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save student learning records locally when the bundled student manager is used. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering student names or unnecessary sensitive details, and review or delete local tutor_data JSON files when learning records should not be retained. <br>
