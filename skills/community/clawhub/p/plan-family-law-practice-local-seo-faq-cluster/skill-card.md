## Description: <br>
Research local questions, group them into clusters, and draft launch-ready FAQ copy for a family law practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Family law attorneys and firm marketers use this skill to research local search questions, cluster them by intent, and draft FAQ content for local SEO. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts or web-search queries could expose confidential family-law case facts, client names, privileged communications, or other sensitive details. <br>
Mitigation: Use generalized or anonymized facts and avoid including client-identifying or privileged information in prompts and search queries. <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ content may be inaccurate, jurisdiction-specific, or mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human reviewer verify factual accuracy, jurisdiction fit, and client-facing wording before publication or client use. <br>
Risk: External setup guides or manually cloned repositories may differ from the scanned release artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan external setup materials or cloned repositories separately before using them. <br>
