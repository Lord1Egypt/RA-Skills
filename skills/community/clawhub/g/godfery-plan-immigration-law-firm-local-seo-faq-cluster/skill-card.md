Research local questions, group them into clusters, and draft launch-ready FAQ copy for an immigration law firm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Immigration attorneys, legal operations teams, and local SEO practitioners use this skill to plan question clusters and draft FAQ content for local search pages. Human review is needed before publishing or treating outputs as legal guidance. <br>
