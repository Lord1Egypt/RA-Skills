## Description: <br>
Guides agents in choosing SEO-friendly domains for new sites, covering brand versus keyword domains, TLD selection, domain length, history checks, and defensive registration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, SEO practitioners, founders, and developers use this skill to choose an initial domain strategy for a website or product. It helps compare branded, partial-match, and exact-match domains; select an appropriate TLD; and apply launch checks before registering a domain. <br>
