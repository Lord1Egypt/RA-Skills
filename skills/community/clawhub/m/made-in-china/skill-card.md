## Description: <br>
Analyze China's export volumes, product categories, and trade destinations using official customs data to reveal trends and summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, researchers, and business users use this skill to request concise summaries of Chinese export volumes, product categories, destination markets, and trends from public customs statistics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trade statistics may be incomplete, outdated, or misread when used for business or policy decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important figures against the General Administration of Customs of China before relying on them. <br>
