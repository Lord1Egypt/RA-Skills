## Description: <br>
Create a structured fundraising plan for organizations or individuals, covering goals, target audience, channel strategy, timeline, and success metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, nonprofits, community projects, and individuals use this skill to turn a fundraising idea into an actionable campaign plan with donor segments, channels, timeline, budget, KPIs, and contingency steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fundraising recommendations may miss legal, tax, platform, or financial compliance requirements for a real campaign. <br>
Mitigation: Use the plan as planning support and verify campaign requirements with appropriate legal, tax, platform, and financial sources before launch. <br>
