## Description: <br>
Anti-sycophancy protocol for Claude: surface real flaws, push back when wrong, and stop validating bad ideas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiajiaoy](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiajiaoy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and teams use this skill when they want direct evaluation of plans, designs, decisions, assumptions, or work in progress rather than validation or encouragement. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally makes feedback more direct and critical, which may be unsuitable for sensitive or highly nuanced decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it intentionally for reviews, plans, and assumption testing, and apply extra human judgment before acting on blunt verdicts in sensitive contexts. <br>
Risk: The skill can surface incorrect or misleading critique if the user's context is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the relevant constraints, evidence, and success criteria before requesting an Honest Critic review. <br>
