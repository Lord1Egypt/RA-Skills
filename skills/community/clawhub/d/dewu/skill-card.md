## Description: <br>
Dewu helps agents organize public Dewu ecommerce platform information, including merchant onboarding requirements, campaign details, marketing tools, and service rules. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mikeclaw007](https://clawhub.ai/user/mikeclaw007) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to organize public Dewu official pages into merchant onboarding summaries, activity timelines, marketing-resource checklists, and service-rule references. It is intended for public information review only, not merchant backend operation or private account workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Dewu public rules, campaign pages, and onboarding details may change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Retain source links and dates in outputs and verify current official pages before using the summary. <br>
Risk: Users could provide merchant login sessions, order details, payment data, customer information, or other private business data outside the skill boundary. <br>
Mitigation: Use only public Dewu pages and do not provide private account sessions, order details, payment data, customer information, or backend access. <br>
