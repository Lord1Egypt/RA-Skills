### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste secrets or unnecessary personal data while asking for portfolio help. <br>
Mitigation: The skill instructs users not to provide passwords, keys, wallets, seeds, session secrets, or excessive personal data. <br>
Risk: Generated portfolio copy could be inaccurate or too broad for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated portfolio copy before publishing, keeping claims factual and based on owned notes. <br>
Risk: A visible PayPal donation link could be mistaken for an automated payment action. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the link as disclosed support information only; the skill does not use it automatically. <br>
