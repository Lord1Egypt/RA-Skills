## Description: <br>
Research local questions, group them into clusters, and draft launch-ready FAQ copy for a fractional CFO firm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, SEO operators, and fractional CFO firms use this skill to research local search questions, organize them into FAQ clusters, and draft publishable FAQ copy for local search pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Web searches may disclose confidential client names, private financials, internal strategy, or other non-public business information to search providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential or non-public business information in search prompts unless sharing it externally is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated SEO FAQ copy may contain inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable claims for a specific fractional CFO firm or market. <br>
Mitigation: Review and refine outputs for accuracy, readability, business fit, and publication readiness before external use. <br>
