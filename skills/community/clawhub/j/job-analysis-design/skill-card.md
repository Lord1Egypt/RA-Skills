## Description: <br>
在对话中辅助学习"工作分析/Job Analysis"第 7 章 HR 应用：job description、performance management and appraisal、job evaluation for compensation、job design/redesign。基于 c7.pdf。当用户讨论岗位说明书、绩效评估量表、绩效管理、薪酬岗位评价、compensable factors、point-factor method、工作再设计、任务分配、人机分工、残障便利或"工作分析如何服务 HR 目的"时使用。使用渐进式披露，按需读取 references/ 下的细节。 <br>

Students and HR learners use this skill as a Chinese-language study aid for Job and Work Analysis Chapter 7, especially to understand job descriptions, performance appraisal, job evaluation for compensation, and job design/redesign.
