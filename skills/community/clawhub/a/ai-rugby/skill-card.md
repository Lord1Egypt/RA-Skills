## Description: <br>
AI Rugby is a Chinese-language rugby guide that explains Rugby Union, Rugby League, NFL differences, scoring, positions, core rules, major events, All Blacks culture, and beginner training concepts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to answer beginner and comparison questions about rugby rules, match viewing, positions, events, and basic training terminology. It is best suited for general sports education rather than live officiating or medical coaching decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rugby training or contact-sport technique guidance may be incomplete for a user's body, age, league, or safety context. <br>
Mitigation: Use responses as introductory education and defer participation, tackling technique, injury, and coaching decisions to qualified coaches, medical professionals, and official competition rules. <br>
Risk: Static event, team, and rule summaries can become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current schedules, laws, disciplinary standards, and competition details with official rugby bodies before relying on them. <br>
