## Description: <br>
Translate text accurately while preserving formatting, handling plurals, and adapting tone per locale. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[evolinkai](https://clawhub.ai/user/evolinkai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and localization teams use this skill to translate text, files, and conversations while preserving placeholders, markup, terminology, and locale-specific tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Translation requests are sent to EvoLink's external API. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending confidential, regulated, or personal data unless EvoLink's privacy terms and the user's data-handling requirements allow it. <br>
Risk: The skill requires an EvoLink API key. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated, revocable API key and rotate or revoke it when access is no longer needed. <br>
