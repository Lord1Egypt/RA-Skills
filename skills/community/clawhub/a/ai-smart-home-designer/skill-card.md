## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Renovation, structural, gas, or electrical recommendations may be unsafe or noncompliant if used as final instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance and verify real work with qualified professionals, property approval processes, and applicable local codes. <br>
Risk: Smart-home device compatibility and installation details can change with vendor firmware, ecosystems, and product documentation. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm compatibility, wiring requirements, and installation steps against current manufacturer documentation before purchasing or installing devices. <br>
Risk: The release declares Python, git, and a requests dependency even though the reviewed artifact contains only Markdown guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review declared dependencies during installation and install them only in environments where they are expected and permitted. <br>
