## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may apply the guidance to sensitive datasets, SQL connections, notebooks, or business information. <br>
Mitigation: Handle all data and credentials under the organization's normal privacy, access-control, and data-governance policies. <br>
Risk: Incorrect assumptions, weak data quality checks, or overstated confidence can lead to misleading analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Validate row counts, date ranges, null rates, joins, source definitions, and assumptions before presenting conclusions. <br>
