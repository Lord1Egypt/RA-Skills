## Description: <br>
Turns Chinese children's stories, fables, classroom reading texts, or original plots into age-appropriate picture-book storyboards, captions, character anchors, and image-generation prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xhuaustc](https://clawhub.ai/user/xhuaustc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Educators, parents, and content creators use this skill to adapt Chinese children's stories into picture-book page plans with readable captions, consistent character descriptions, and illustration prompts for preschool or primary-school readers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Source stories or generated prompts may include private or child-specific classroom details before use with an external image-generation service. <br>
Mitigation: Review and redact source text, captions, and prompts before sharing them with external services. <br>
Risk: Generated storyboards or illustration prompts may need review for age suitability and classroom context. <br>
Mitigation: Have an adult reviewer check captions, scene tone, and prompt wording before publication or classroom use. <br>
