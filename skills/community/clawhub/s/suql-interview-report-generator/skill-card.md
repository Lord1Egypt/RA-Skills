## Use Case: <br>
HR teams, hiring managers, and recruiting operations users use this skill to turn candidate materials, job requirements, and interview notes into structured interview analysis reports. The generated report supports candidate evaluation, hiring recommendations, background-check focus areas, compensation suggestions, and probation planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles sensitive candidate and employment data. <br>
Mitigation: Use authorized candidate materials, minimize unnecessary personal data, and store generated reports only in approved systems. <br>
Risk: Interview reports may influence hiring, compensation, probation, and background-check decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill inside an approved HR process and review generated conclusions before using them for employment decisions. <br>
Risk: OCR or external document-processing services can expose candidate records if used without approval. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid cloud OCR unless the service is approved for candidate records and complies with internal privacy requirements. <br>
Risk: Background checks and reference checks can create consent and legal compliance risk. <br>
Mitigation: Perform checks only with proper candidate consent and legal or HR policy review. <br>
