## Description: <br>
OpenClaw 16 MBTI configures an agent's communication style and task perspective using 16 MBTI-inspired persona profiles for analysis, writing, workflow recommendations, and perspective comparison. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archibald80000-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/archibald80000-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and teams use this skill to draft, analyze, recommend, or compare responses through MBTI-styled communication perspectives. It is intended for communication style and brainstorming support, not clinical diagnosis, hiring, candidate selection, or other high-stakes determinations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-treat MBTI-styled output as neutral professional advice for legal, compliance, HR, financial, or other high-stakes decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as style and brainstorming guidance only, and require appropriate human review before acting on high-stakes outputs. <br>
Risk: MBTI personas can be misused for clinical diagnosis, hiring, candidate selection, or performance judgments. <br>
Mitigation: Keep requests focused on communication style and task perspective; refuse clinical, diagnostic, hiring, selection, and screening uses. <br>
Risk: Some persona styles may produce unusually aggressive or risk-tolerant recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs carefully, especially for ESTP or ENTP personas, and balance them with policy, compliance, and domain expertise before use. <br>
