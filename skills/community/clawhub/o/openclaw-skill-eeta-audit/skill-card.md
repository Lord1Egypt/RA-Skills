## Description: <br>
OpenClaw Skill quality audit based on the CORE-EEAT framework for evaluating AI agent skill utility, security, reliability, veto risks, and improvement priorities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[281862066-a11y](https://clawhub.ai/user/281862066-a11y) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, maintainers, and reviewers use this skill to evaluate OpenClaw skills before installation, release, or maintenance decisions. It produces structured quality, utility, and security audit guidance using the included EEAT benchmark and security checklist. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audit findings may be incomplete or incorrect if treated as a final security guarantee. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as an audit aid and manually confirm security-sensitive conclusions before installing or deploying a reviewed skill. <br>
Risk: Recommendations may propose install, removal, or other high-impact changes to a skill workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before acting on installation, removal, permission, or production-readiness recommendations. <br>
