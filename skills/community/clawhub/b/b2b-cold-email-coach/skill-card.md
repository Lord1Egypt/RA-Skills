## Description: <br>
Coach B2B senders to write, sequence, and deliver cold emails that improve opens and replies without damaging domain reputation or landing in spam. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External B2B founders, SDRs, agencies, and freelancers use this skill to diagnose cold outreach funnels, rewrite emails, plan sequences, improve targeting, and set up domain and mailbox practices for outbound campaigns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cold outreach guidance may be used in ways that ignore consent, opt-out requests, or applicable email laws. <br>
Mitigation: Use human review, honor opt-out requests, and check campaign practices against applicable email laws before sending. <br>
Risk: Advice may be connected to automated sending workflows without adequate controls. <br>
Mitigation: Keep explicit controls and human approval in place before applying recommendations to automated outbound tools. <br>
