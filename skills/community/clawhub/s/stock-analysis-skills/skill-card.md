## Description: <br>
Stock Analysis Skills provides a structured framework for producing equity research reports across macro, technical, fundamental, valuation, risk, ESG, catalyst, and investment-summary modules. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qiuqp](https://clawhub.ai/user/qiuqp) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to structure equity research on publicly traded companies, including data collection, module scoring, investment-thesis development, and final stance articulation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill produces investment-style opinions that could be mistaken for professional financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research assistance, cite current data sources, and have users or qualified reviewers validate conclusions before acting. <br>
Risk: Broad stock or company-analysis prompts may activate the skill, and incomplete or stale market data can affect conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Require recent data, disclose data gaps and assumptions, and avoid unsourced claims. <br>
