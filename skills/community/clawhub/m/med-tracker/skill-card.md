## Description: <br>
Medication tracker for logging prescriptions, dosages, schedules, reminders, missed-dose concerns, and general drug interaction questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People and agents use this skill to maintain a medication list, schedule reminders, review missed-dose concerns, and prepare general interaction questions for pharmacist or doctor verification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medication schedules, missed-dose handling, and interaction answers can affect health decisions if treated as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for tracking and general reminders only; verify dosing changes, missed-dose actions, and interaction questions with a pharmacist or clinician. <br>
Risk: The security review was clean but low-confidence because the available telemetry did not support a full independent coherence review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the artifact before installation and avoid granting sensitive credentials or broad local access unless a future version clearly requires them. <br>
