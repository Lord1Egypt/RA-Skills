## Description: <br>
Perform a structured conversion rate optimization (CRO) audit of a landing page. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flynndavid](https://clawhub.ai/user/flynndavid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders, marketers, and growth teams use this skill to evaluate landing pages from a URL, pasted copy, or screenshot description and get a scored CRO report with prioritized fixes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided landing page URLs, draft pages, internal URLs, or campaign copy may contain confidential marketing content. <br>
Mitigation: Use public or sanitized inputs, or confirm permission before allowing an agent to fetch or analyze private pages and confidential copy. <br>
Risk: The audit may be less reliable when the input omits visual, mobile, speed, or structural details. <br>
Mitigation: Treat scores as directional when inputs are incomplete and preserve the skill's stated limitations in the report rationale. <br>
