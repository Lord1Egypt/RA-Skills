## Description: <br>
Search and discover internet radio stations worldwide by name, genre, or country with direct stream URLs and detailed metadata. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[brucegutman](https://clawhub.ai/user/brucegutman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and builders use this skill to search for internet radio stations, retrieve popular stations, list countries and tags, and obtain stream metadata for music apps, localization workflows, and creative coding projects. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Radio searches are sent to a disclosed third-party service and may return third-party stream URLs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive search terms and review returned stream URLs before using them in user-facing applications. <br>
