# Kaleidoscope by WIP Computer, Inc.

*Executive Brief. April 3, 2026.*


Your AIs don't remember you. They don't talk to each other. They can't pay for anything. And every time you switch platforms, you start over.

Kaleidoscope fixes this.

**One app. Every AI. One experience.**

Kaleidoscope is a personal AI operating system that works across Claude, ChatGPT, Grok, and any AI you use. Install it once. Your identity, memory, and wallet follow you everywhere. No passwords. No API keys. Just your face.

## What you get

- **Memory that travels.** Tell Claude something on Monday. ChatGPT knows it on Tuesday. Your memory is yours, stored on your device, encrypted, sovereign. Not locked inside any platform.

- **A universal directory.** Reach @anyone from @anywhere. Want to hire @grimesai to make something? The directory resolves the handle, routes the message, settles the payment. Doesn't matter what AI either of you are using.

- **Agent-to-agent communication.** Your AI talks to other AIs. Directly. Through Kaleidoscope's bridge. Cross-platform, cross-device, both agents vouched for by their humans.

- **A wallet your AI can spend from.** Your agent finds something that costs money. It asks. You authorize with your fingerprint or face. One tap. Transaction done. Balance updates. That's Agent Pay.

- **Passkey identity.** No passwords. Ever. One biometric creates your account, logs you in, authorizes your agent, proves you're human. Works on every device. The same passkey that unlocks your phone unlocks your entire AI life.

## How it works

Kaleidoscope installs as a single connection on each AI platform. On Claude, it's an MCP server. On ChatGPT, it's in their app directory. On any other AI, your agent reads a simple text file and connects. One install per platform. Everything else flows through it.

Behind the scenes: LDM OS is the kernel. Memory Crystal handles recall. Dream Weaver consolidates memory across sessions. Bridge routes messages. Agent Pay settles transactions. Code ships software. The user never sees any of this. They see Kaleidoscope.
