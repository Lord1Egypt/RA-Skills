## The Problem

Biohazard and crime scene cleanup companies market themselves in life-or-death moments — unattended deaths, homicides, suicides, industrial accidents. The families, property managers, and insurance adjusters calling them are stressed, vulnerable, and making fast decisions. Trust is everything.

Yet 90%+ of biohazard marketing makes claims that are:
- **Regulatory violations:** "OSHA-compliant" without a written Bloodborne Pathogens Exposure Control Plan is a multi-citation OSHA inspection scenario
- **Criminally risky:** "Complete death scene cleanup" without coroner release can be a gross misdemeanor under NRS 451.020
- **Fake credentials:** "IICRC certified crime scene cleaner" — this credential does not exist
- **False licensing:** "Licensed medical waste disposal" without NDEP medical waste transporter registration = unlicensed hazardous waste transport
- **Illegal activity:** "We clean meth labs" without Nevada DPBH certification = practicing unlicensed remediation under NRS 441A.640

Generic AI tools generate this marketing without blinking. They don't know the OSHA BBP standard from a biohazard sticker. This kit does.
