## Description: <br>
Nutrition and hydration guidance for physically demanding occupations, including construction, trades, agriculture, food service, warehousing, and other jobs requiring sustained physical effort. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Workers and agents assisting workers use this skill to estimate fueling needs, plan pre-shift and on-shift meals, adjust hydration for heat or exertion, and prepare recovery meals for physically demanding work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Nutrition and hydration recommendations may be inappropriate for users with medical conditions, heat illness symptoms, persistent fatigue, GI problems, or supplement concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Present the guidance as general nutrition information and advise affected users to verify recommendations with a qualified clinician. <br>
