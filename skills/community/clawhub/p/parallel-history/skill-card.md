## Description: <br>
A conversational, turn-based alternate-history roleplay game where users choose a historical starting point, faction, location, and decisions across a limited political lifetime. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[BulletYuan](https://clawhub.ai/user/BulletYuan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and roleplay players use this skill to run structured alternate-history simulations with staged choices, event outcomes, relationship changes, legacy tracking, and endings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad phrases about starting from a year or changing history may accidentally activate the game during ordinary history discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording when starting the game, and disable or avoid the skill when accidental roleplay activation would disrupt normal conversation. <br>
Risk: Alternate-history narration can be mistaken for factual historical guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as roleplay simulation, and verify historical claims against reliable sources before relying on them outside the game. <br>
