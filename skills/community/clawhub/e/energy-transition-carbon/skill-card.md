## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded ESG, facility, supply-chain, or financial planning files may contain sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Handle uploaded files under the user's data governance controls and remove unnecessary confidential information before analysis. <br>
Risk: Compliance, carbon-market, and forecasting outputs may be inaccurate or unsuitable for regulated reporting or investment decisions without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify results against authoritative standards, source data, and qualified expert review before external reporting or financial use. <br>
