## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for everyday mental wellness self-help, including mood check-ins, stress management, guided meditation, cognitive reframing, and structured journaling. It is scoped to self-awareness and practice guidance, not clinical diagnosis or professional care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake wellness coaching for clinical diagnosis, therapy, or emergency care. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as self-help only, keep safety reminders visible, and direct users to professional care or local emergency and crisis resources for immediate or severe concerns. <br>
Risk: Mood assessments, cognitive reframing, or meditation guidance may be inappropriate for a user's current condition or environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use conservative language, avoid diagnostic claims, recommend safe surroundings for meditation, and encourage professional support when distress persists. <br>
Risk: High-risk self-harm or suicide signals require urgent escalation beyond agent guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Screen for crisis signals first and provide crisis hotline or emergency-service guidance instead of continuing ordinary self-help coaching. <br>
