Here are our hot-selling products:
• {product_1} - {price_1}
• {product_2} - {price_2}
• {product_3} - {price_3}

✓ Factory direct price with MOQ {moq}
✓ Quality guaranteed with SGS/BSCI certification
✓ Sample available before bulk order
✓ 7-15 days production lead time

Would you like me to send you our catalog?

{sales_name}
