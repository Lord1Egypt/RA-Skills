## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive product or business information may be exposed if users upload confidential knowledge bases. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved product information and exclude trade secrets, supplier contracts, personal data, and confidential formulations. <br>
Risk: Generated pet health or product efficacy claims may be inaccurate, unsupported, or noncompliant. <br>
Mitigation: Review posts against substantiated product evidence and required advertising disclosures before publishing. <br>
Risk: Promotional posts or realistic visuals may imply fake testimonials or staged experiences. <br>
Mitigation: Label promotional content appropriately and avoid testimonials or imagery that imply real experiences that did not occur. <br>
