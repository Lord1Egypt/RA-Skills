## Description: <br>
A structured knowledge extraction tool that helps users clarify, organize, and store tacit knowledge through active questioning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ChenChen913](https://clawhub.ai/user/ChenChen913) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals and teams use this skill to turn vague ideas, personal expertise, and domain knowledge into structured notes through focused questioning, periodic synthesis, and final knowledge records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill saves and later reuses reflective notes in local domain Markdown files. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when a persistent local knowledge archive is desired, and review, redact, or delete saved notes when they contain sensitive personal, business, legal, or medical information. <br>
