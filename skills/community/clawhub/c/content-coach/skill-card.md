## Description: <br>
Self-media creator coaching skill that uses creator, content, positioning, and monetization frameworks to guide users from niche selection through content production and commercialization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yushuo-aicoder](https://clawhub.ai/user/yushuo-aicoder) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and creator-focused operators use this skill to diagnose their current content stage, choose a positioning or production framework, and receive concise next actions for content growth or monetization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger language may activate the skill for general content, growth, or monetization questions when the user did not intend creator coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording when invoking or excluding the skill, and review the coaching output before applying strategy recommendations. <br>
