## Description: <br>
AI-curated market analysis and research — what 6 opposing analysts say about ETFs today with source links. Free, no API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fredxyt](https://clawhub.ai/user/fredxyt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to fetch public MoltStreet market research for ETF and market-news questions, then summarize bullish and bearish analyst perspectives with source links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ticker symbols or market search topics are sent to MoltStreet when the skill fetches results. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for queries the user is comfortable sharing with MoltStreet. <br>
Risk: Market summaries may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present results as research context, preserve source links, and avoid treating them as trading instructions. <br>
Risk: AI-generated market analysis can be incomplete, stale, or wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims against cited sources and remind users that the skill does not provide real-time quotes. <br>
