## Description: <br>
Draft TikTok Shop and short-video commerce scripts for hooks, UGC scripts, product demos, livestream cues, captions, and creator briefs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, ecommerce operators, and marketing teams use this skill to turn product facts into marketplace-ready short-video commerce copy, including briefs, hooks, captions, livestream cues, audits, and launch bundles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ecommerce copy may include unsupported, misleading, or marketplace-noncompliant claims if product facts are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated claims against official marketplace policies and source product documentation before publication. <br>
Risk: Confidential business data passed as command-line arguments can be exposed through shell history or process listings. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using sensitive product, pricing, or campaign information in command-line arguments; sanitize local shell history and process visibility where needed. <br>
