## Description: <br>
基于R-O框架（角色&现实情况、对象&输出）优化用户提示词，将普通需求转化为专业、结构化的AI提示词，以激发AI最大潜力。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dukeacco](https://clawhub.ai/user/dukeacco) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to turn vague or simple requests into structured R-O prompts with role, context, audience, and output requirements. It is most useful when improving AI interaction quality, standardizing prompt format, or preparing prompts for creative, analytical, execution, decision, and communication tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optimized prompts can include private or sensitive details entered by the user, and the optional script can print, save, or copy the result. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets, credentials, private business data, or sensitive personal information when using outputs that may be printed, saved, or copied. <br>
Risk: Prompt optimization can reshape a vague request into a more specific instruction that may not fully preserve the user's intended scope. <br>
Mitigation: Review the optimized prompt before use, adjust any role or context assumptions, and use the output only for legitimate safe tasks. <br>
