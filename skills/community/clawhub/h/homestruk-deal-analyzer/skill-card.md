## Description: <br>
Analyze rental property investment deals by calculating key metrics including cap rate, cash-on-cash return, DSCR, GRM, and the 1% rule. Use when evaluating a property purchase, comparing deals, running what-if scenarios on price or financing, or when an owner asks whether a deal makes financial sense. Produces a deal verdict and 5-year projection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External landlords, property owners, and real estate investors use this skill to evaluate rental property purchases, compare deals, and test price, rent, or financing assumptions. It produces investment metrics, a deal verdict, what-if scenarios, and an optional 5-year projection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Property addresses, purchase terms, financing assumptions, and local market details may be sensitive or disclose investment plans when saved or used in lookups. <br>
Mitigation: Review inputs before running searches, avoid unnecessary personal or confidential details, and confirm whether local deal reports should be retained. <br>
Risk: Financial metrics and verdicts can be misleading if rents, taxes, expenses, interest rates, or repair assumptions are incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify assumptions against current source data and treat the output as deal-analysis support rather than professional financial advice. <br>
