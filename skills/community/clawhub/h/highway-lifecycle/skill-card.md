### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill gives safety-critical highway, tunnel, traffic, and maintenance guidance that could be mistaken for approval-ready engineering decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only as decision support; require qualified engineers, field investigation, current codes, instrumentation or lab data where needed, and documented human sign-off before action. <br>
Risk: Model-based event, tunnel, and road-damage analysis may produce incorrect, incomplete, or misleading findings. <br>
Mitigation: Validate findings against source data, inspection records, applicable standards, and independent review before prioritizing repairs, changing tunnel support, or managing traffic events. <br>
