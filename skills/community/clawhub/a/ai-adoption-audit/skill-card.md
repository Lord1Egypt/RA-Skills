## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and business teams use this skill to research a company's public AI adoption signals and produce a structured maturity report for market research or customer-reference planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated company AI adoption reports may be saved locally and reused for up to 7 days. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential strategy, customer details, or sensitive internal notes unless local workspace retention is acceptable, and delete the output folder when stored reports are no longer needed. <br>
