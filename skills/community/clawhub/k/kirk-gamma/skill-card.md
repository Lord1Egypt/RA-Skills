## Description: <br>
Generate AI-powered presentations, documents, and social posts using SkillBoss API Hub. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to generate presentations, pitch decks, documents, and social media carousels from prompt text or provided content through the SkillBoss API Hub. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, documents, styling instructions, and the SKILLBOSS_API_KEY are sent to an external SkillBoss/HeyBossAI service. <br>
Mitigation: Use a revocable API key and avoid confidential customer data, secrets, regulated data, or proprietary decks unless the provider's data-handling terms have been reviewed. <br>
