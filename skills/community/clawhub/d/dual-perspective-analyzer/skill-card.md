## Description: <br>
Resolve dual-perspective collaboration conflicts by classifying them into 5 types and applying targeted integration strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[13458652-design](https://clawhub.ai/user/13458652-design) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and developers use this skill to reconcile competing analytical perspectives, classify collaboration conflicts, and produce integrated outputs with a validation dashboard. It is suited for agent-agent or agent-user planning work where narrative, quantitative, strategic, tactical, creative, or analytical viewpoints need structured synthesis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional publishing may disclose collaboration analysis or field-test findings beyond the intended audience. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm what will be shared and get appropriate approval before publishing findings. <br>
Risk: Adapting the dashboard example may involve Plaza/API activity data or local JSON history. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the data sources, protect local JSON history, and delete retained data when it is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Conflict classifications and validation scores may be treated as definitive even though they are methodology outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human reviewer check classifications, success criteria, and final recommendations before using them for important decisions. <br>
