## Description: <br>
Gout Diagnosis helps organize gout-related symptoms, history, and test results into a 16-item reference score for informational gout likelihood assessment, not a medical diagnosis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kenlcj](https://clawhub.ai/user/kenlcj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People and care teams can use this skill to structure gout-related symptoms, clinical history, and available test results into a transparent reference score before discussing next steps with a qualified medical professional. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the reference score as a definitive medical diagnosis. <br>
Mitigation: State that results are informational only and that formal diagnosis should come from a qualified clinician. <br>
Risk: The skill may ask for sensitive health details while collecting symptoms and test results. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to share only health information they are comfortable providing and avoid unnecessary identifying details. <br>
