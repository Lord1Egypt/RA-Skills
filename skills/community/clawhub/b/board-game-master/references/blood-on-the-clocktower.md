# Blood on the Clocktower / 血染钟楼 — Strategy Guide

## Game Overview / 游戏概述

Blood on the Clocktower is a social deduction game for 5-20 players. A Storyteller (说书人) runs the game — they are not a player but a neutral facilitator who manages night actions and may introduce misinformation through character abilities.

**Key differences from other social deduction games:**
- Dead players still participate — they get one final vote and can still talk
- The Storyteller has discretion in how certain abilities resolve
- Information can be "poisoned" or wrong by design
- Games are longer and more conversational than Werewolf/Mafia

血染钟楼是5-20人的社交推理游戏。说书人负责运营游戏，不是玩家，而是中立的主持人。死亡玩家仍可参与讨论，并有一次最终投票权。

## Editions / 版本

| Edition / 版本 | Complexity / 复杂度 | Best For / 适合 |
|---|---|---|
| Trouble Brewing / 灾祸滋生 | Beginner / 入门 | First games, learning basics |
| Bad Moon Rising / 血月升起 | Intermediate / 中级 | More death, less info |
| Sects and Violets / 梦殒春宵 | Advanced / 高级 | Madness, misinformation chaos |

## Role Categories / 角色分类

| Category / 类别 | Alignment / 阵营 | Role / 职责 |
|---|---|---|
| Townsfolk / 镇民 | Good / 好人 | Information gathering, protection, killing |
| Outsiders / 外来者 | Good / 好人 | Hindrance to good team (but still want good to win) |
| Minions / 爪牙 | Evil / 邪恶 | Support the Demon, spread chaos |
| Demon / 恶魔 | Evil / 邪恶 | Kill at night, must survive to win |

## Trouble Brewing Key Roles / 灾祸滋生关键角色

### Good Side / 好人阵营

**Washerwoman / 洗衣妇**
- Night 1: Learn that one of two players is a specific Townsfolk
- Strategy: Share info early to establish trust. Your info is strong — use it to verify or challenge other claims.
- 策略：尽早分享信息建立信任。你的信息很强——用来验证或质疑其他人的声明。

**Investigator / 调查员**
- Night 1: Learn that one of two players is a specific Minion
- Strategy: This narrows evil suspects immediately. Cross-reference with other info. Be careful — Spy can appear as anyone.
- 策略：立即缩小邪恶嫌疑范围。与其他信息交叉验证。注意——间谍可以显示为任何人。

**Empath / 共情者**
- Each night: Learn how many of your alive neighbors are evil
- Strategy: Track your neighbors as they die/shift. Getting a "1" is powerful — one neighbor is evil. Process of elimination over multiple nights.
- 策略：跟踪邻居的死亡/变化。得到"1"很强——有一个邻居是邪恶的。多个夜晚的排除法。

**Fortune Teller / 占卜师**
- Each night: Choose 2 players, learn if either is the Demon. But one player is a "red herring" who always reads as Demon.
- Strategy: Systematically check pairs. Track your results. Remember the red herring — one false positive is built in. Don't immediately trust a "yes."
- 策略：系统地检查配对。追踪结果。记住红鲱鱼——有一个假阳性。不要立即相信"是"的结果。

**Undertaker / 掘墓人**
- Each night after an execution: Learn the role of the executed player
- Strategy: Crucial for confirming or denying claims. If someone claimed Empath and Undertaker sees they were Empath, trust chain extends.
- 策略：确认或否认声明的关键。如果有人声称是共情者，掘墓人确认了，信任链延伸。

**Monk / 僧侣**
- Each night: Choose a player (not yourself) to protect from the Demon
- Strategy: Protect the most valuable confirmed player. Don't announce who you're protecting — evil will kill someone else. If your target survives, you gain info.
- 策略：保护最有价值的已确认玩家。不要宣布保护对象——邪恶会杀别人。如果目标存活，你获得信息。

**Slayer / 猎手**
- Once per game: Choose a player; if they're the Demon, they die
- Strategy: Wait for strong consensus before using. A wasted shot helps evil. Can also be used to prove yourself — if you shoot someone and they don't die, it confirms they're not the Demon (usually).
- 策略：等待强共识再使用。浪费一枪帮助邪恶。也可用来自证——射击未死说明对方不是恶魔（通常）。

**Mayor / 市长**
- If only 3 players alive and no execution, Good wins. If you die at night, another player might die instead.
- Strategy: Late game power. Push for "no execution" when 3 remain and you're confident about alignment. Risky — if you're wrong about the other two, evil wins.
- 策略：后期强力能力。剩3人时推动"不处决"。有风险——如果你对另外两人的判断错误，邪恶获胜。

### Evil Side / 邪恶阵营

**Imp / 小恶魔**
- Kills one player each night. If the Imp kills themselves, a Minion becomes the new Imp ("star passing").
- Strategy: Star passing is the Imp's superpower. If heat is on you, kill yourself to pass demon to an unsuspected Minion. Coordinate with Minions on who.
- 策略：星星传递是小恶魔的超能力。如果嫌疑指向你，自杀传递恶魔给不受怀疑的爪牙。与爪牙协调传递对象。

**Poisoner / 投毒者**
- Each night: Choose a player to poison. Their ability malfunctions that night and the following day.
- Strategy: Poison the strongest info roles (Empath, Fortune Teller). If you know who the Undertaker is, poison them before a key execution. Poisoning the Monk leaves targets unprotected.
- 策略：毒害最强的信息角色（共情者、占卜师）。如果知道掘墓人是谁，在关键处决前毒害他们。毒害僧侣使目标失去保护。

**Spy / 间谍**
- Sees the Grimoire (all roles). May register as Good or as a specific Townsfolk/Outsider to abilities.
- Strategy: The most powerful Minion. Use your perfect info to craft believable claims. You can claim any role and appear to "confirm" other evil players as good. Feed misinformation through "confirmed" info channels.
- 策略：最强力的爪牙。利用完美信息制作可信声明。你可以声称任何角色并看起来"确认"其他邪恶玩家为好人。

**Scarlet Woman / 红衣女**
- If the Demon dies and 5+ players remain, you become the Demon.
- Strategy: Play aggressively as "good." If the Demon gets caught and executed, the game continues with you as the new Demon. The town thinks they won but hasn't.
- 策略：积极扮演"好人"。如果恶魔被抓住处决，游戏继续由你成为新恶魔。镇民以为赢了但没有。

**Baron / 男爵**
- Adds 2 extra Outsiders to the game (replacing Townsfolk).
- Strategy: Less info in the game benefits evil. Claim a Townsfolk role confidently. The extra Outsiders create confusion — "why are there so many Outsiders?"
- 策略：游戏中信息减少有利于邪恶。自信地声称镇民角色。额外的外来者制造混乱。

## Good Side Strategy / 好人阵营策略

### Information Sharing / 信息分享

**When to share publicly vs privately:**
- Day 1-2: Share privately with 1-2 trusted players first. Build small trust networks.
- Mid-game: Go public when you have corroborating evidence from multiple sources.
- Late game: Everything should be public — no time for secrets.

何时公开 vs 私下分享：
- 第1-2天：先私下和1-2个信任的玩家分享。建立小型信任网络。
- 中期：当你有多个来源的佐证时公开。
- 后期：一切都应公开——没时间保密了。

**The Fishbowl Technique / 鱼缸技巧:**
- Have 2-3 players discuss openly while others watch
- Observers compare their private info against what's being said
- Spot contradictions without revealing your own hand

**Reading Nominations / 解读提名:**
- Who nominates whom and when reveals alignment signals
- Evil players often nominate good info roles early
- Good players who never nominate are often hiding something (or just passive)
- Track who votes together — voting blocks = likely same alignment

### Trust Chains / 信任链
- Start from your strongest confirmed info
- If Washerwoman confirms Player A is the Empath, and Empath says Player B has 0 evil neighbors, Player B gets a trust boost
- Chain breaks if any link is poisoned or lying

### Common Good Mistakes / 好人常见错误
- Sharing all info immediately on Day 1 (gives evil free targeting info)
- Trusting someone just because they claimed first
- Ignoring dead players' input — they often have the clearest perspective
- Not tracking Poisoner — any info role could be giving wrong info
- Tunnel visioning on one suspect while the real Demon sits quietly

## Evil Side Strategy / 邪恶阵营策略

### Bluffing / 虚张声势

**Pick your claim before Day 1.** The Storyteller gives evil players a list of roles not in play — use these for safe claims.

在第1天之前选好你的声明。说书人给邪恶玩家不在场角色列表——用这些来安全声明。

**Bluffing tiers:**
1. **Safe:** Claim a not-in-play role from the bluff list
2. **Moderate:** Claim a Townsfolk whose real holder hasn't spoken yet
3. **Risky:** Double-claim against someone (one of you is lying)

**Coordination with Demon / 与恶魔协调:**
- Minions know who the Demon is; Demon knows who Minions are
- Plan complementary claims (don't both claim info that contradicts)
- One Minion should stay "clean" as long as possible as insurance

**Sacrificing Minions / 牺牲爪牙:**
- A Minion getting executed to prove they're "good" (if Undertaker is poisoned) buys time
- In Trouble Brewing with Scarlet Woman: let the Demon die on purpose to surprise the town

### Poisoner Targets / 投毒目标优先级
1. Fortune Teller (prevents Demon identification)
2. Empath (prevents neighbor-based deduction)
3. Undertaker (prevents execution confirmation)
4. Monk (allows kills on protected targets)

## Advanced Tactics / 高级战术

### Soft vs Hard Claims / 软声明 vs 硬声明
- **Soft claim:** "I have info that suggests Player A might be evil" — deniable, flexible
- **Hard claim:** "I am the Empath. My neighbors are both good." — committed, verifiable
- Good players should hard claim when they have strong info. Evil players prefer soft claims to maintain flexibility.

### The Double Claim Trap / 双重声明陷阱
- If two players claim the same role, one is lying
- Good players: force this situation by claiming publicly. If evil has already claimed your role, the conflict exposes them.
- Evil players: avoid claiming roles that might be double-claimed. If caught, pivot to "I was poisoned/drunk."

### Nomination Order Matters / 提名顺序很重要
- Early nominations burn vote tokens on non-essential targets
- Save your nomination for when you have a strong case
- The last nomination of the day often carries the most weight

### Dead Player Votes / 死亡玩家投票
- Dead players get exactly ONE vote for the rest of the game
- This is precious — save it for a critical execution
- Dead players often have the clearest read because they have nothing to hide
- 死亡玩家只有一次投票——用在关键处决上

### Star Passing (Imp) / 星星传递（小恶魔）
- The Imp kills themselves → a Minion becomes the new Imp
- Best done when the Imp is under heavy suspicion
- The new Imp was previously "confirmed" good → devastating for good team
- Counter: track who the Imp's allies were before death
- 最佳时机：小恶魔被高度怀疑时。新小恶魔之前被"确认"为好人→对好人阵营毁灭性打击。

### Common Mistakes / 常见错误

**Good players:**
- Over-relying on a single info source
- Not considering the Poisoner when evaluating info
- Executing too quickly without discussion
- Ignoring quiet players (quiet ≠ innocent)

**Evil players:**
- Over-defending when accused (looks guilty)
- Not having a prepared bluff
- Minions playing too safely (need to actively mislead)
- Forgetting star pass timing — don't wait until it's too late
