## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel-planning agents use this skill to request Chinese-language trending bar recommendations, filtered venue details, check-in routes, and visit guidance by city, budget, queue time, reservation availability, taste, accessibility, or venue constraints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Venue hours, prices, wait times, reservations, popularity, and restrictions may change before a visit. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details with authoritative or recent sources before making travel, reservation, or spending decisions. <br>
Risk: Some venues may have photography limits, minimum spend, or other on-site rules. <br>
Mitigation: Check the venue's current policies and follow posted or staff-provided rules at the location. <br>
