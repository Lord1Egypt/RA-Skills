## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payment-system examples may omit production safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Review snippets before use and require backend-only secret storage, authenticated webhooks, idempotent entitlement updates, careful receipt/token retention, privacy review, and clear paywall disclosures. <br>
Risk: Client-side validation or entitlement handling can be bypassed or become inconsistent if copied without a backend review. <br>
Mitigation: Use server-side receipt or token verification and treat the skill's implementation examples as sketches until adapted and tested for the target app. <br>
