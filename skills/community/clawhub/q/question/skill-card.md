This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external practitioners, and agents use this skill to improve questioning in research, sales discovery, coaching, journalism, and learning contexts. It helps detect biased questions, generate follow-ups, surface assumptions, and decide when to pause. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive interviews, coaching, sales, or investigations may use recalled prior conversation context without participants understanding that context use. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the agent's memory is enabled and avoid using recalled prior conversations unless participants understand and approve that context use. <br>
Risk: Questioning guidance can still produce biased, leading, or inappropriate prompts if applied without context review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated questions for neutral framing, scope, and participant safety before using them in sensitive conversations. <br>
