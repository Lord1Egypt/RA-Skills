## Description: <br>
Humanizer Academic English edition for polishing academic prose, reducing AI-like wording, and preserving meaning, evidence logic, disciplinary register, and authorial voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[duducoco](https://clawhub.ai/user/duducoco) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, students, researchers, and editors use this skill to rewrite English academic prose so it sounds less formulaic while preserving meaning, citations, field conventions, and scholarly stance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release security verdict is suspicious because the broader skill collection may run privileged review workflows with safeguards disabled by default. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in a trusted ClawHub maintainer environment and avoid full-access review modes unless explicitly needed. <br>
Risk: Fallback review tooling may send diffs to other local AI CLIs. <br>
Mitigation: Disable fallback reviewers when diffs should remain local to a selected reviewer. <br>
Risk: Moderation and PR-publishing workflows can affect external artifacts or repositories. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit targets, reasons, and confirmation before invoking those workflows. <br>
