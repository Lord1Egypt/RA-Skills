## Description: <br>
Analyze basketball games, lineups, players, and practice plans with film-room structure, scouting grids, and possession-based coaching tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Coaches, analysts, scouts, players, and basketball staff use this skill to turn game prep, post-game review, lineup fit, player scouting, and weekly practice planning into coach-ready outputs. It helps structure basketball decisions around available evidence, role context, possession themes, and practical next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local basketball notes may include roster, player, opponent, or practice details. <br>
Mitigation: Enable memory only after user approval, keep notes in ~/basketball/ lean, and avoid saving credentials, private medical details, or raw private material. <br>
Risk: The skill may engage during casual basketball discussion if activation is configured broadly. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit-only activation when the user does not want proactive basketball assistance. <br>
