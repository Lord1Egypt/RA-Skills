## Description: <br>
E-commerce AI Toolkit Pro is a prompt-only toolkit for marketplace sellers to draft product listings, analyze competitors and product opportunities, respond to customers, plan listing launches, generate ads, and review sales data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketplace sellers and e-commerce operators use this skill to turn product, platform, customer-service, advertising, and sales inputs into concise operational prompts and draft content. It is aimed at cross-border platforms such as Amazon, Shopee, Temu, TikTok Shop, Lazada, and independent stores. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The review-request template encourages offering a gift or coupon as an incentive for a positive review, which can create biased feedback and may violate marketplace review policies. <br>
Mitigation: Remove or rewrite that template so review requests ask for honest, unbiased feedback and do not condition gifts, coupons, discounts, or other benefits on ratings or reviews. <br>
Risk: Generated listing, customer-service, advertising, and product-selection drafts may conflict with platform-specific policies or the seller's actual product facts. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against the target marketplace's current rules and the seller's verified product, logistics, warranty, and pricing data before use. <br>
