## Description: <br>
Expert guidance for systematic backtesting of trading strategies. Use when developing, testing, stress-testing, or validating quantitative trading strategies. Covers "beating ideas to death" methodology, parameter robustness testing, slippage modeling, bias prevention, and interpreting backtest results. Applicable when user asks about backtesting, strategy validation, robustness testing, avoiding overfitting, or systematic trading development. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat educational trading methodology as financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as backtesting guidance and require users to make independent financial, compliance, and risk decisions. <br>
Risk: Registry version and bundled metadata version differ slightly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the installed package against the ClawHub release metadata and file hashes before deployment. <br>
Risk: Backtest guidance can still lead to misleading conclusions if data quality, slippage, sample size, or bias controls are ignored. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the skill's recommended robustness checks, including out-of-sample validation, conservative execution costs, and bias audits. <br>
