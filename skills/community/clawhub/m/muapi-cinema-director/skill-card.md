## Description: <br>
Direct high-fidelity cinematic video with AI by translating creative intent into technical cinematographic directives for Veo3, Kling, and Luma video models via muapi.ai. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Anil-matcha](https://clawhub.ai/user/Anil-matcha) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creative agents, video producers, and developers use this skill to turn a scene brief and director intent into cinematographic prompt guidance and shell commands for asynchronous AI video generation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts or media may be sent to an external video-generation provider. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated prompt and avoid submitting confidential or sensitive content unless the provider and project are approved. <br>
Risk: Video generation jobs may incur provider costs. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to show the exact command and estimated cost before submitting a generation job. <br>
