# Dispute Resolver — Output Template

Every dispute deliverable is a package with up to four parts. Parts 1-2 are always present; 3-4 as applicable.

---

## Part 1 — Case Assessment

- **Dispute type:** [INR / INAD / damaged / wrong item / return condition / chargeback / complaint escalation]
- **Stage & deadline:** [first contact / platform claim / appeal / chargeback] — respond by [date+time, platform timezone]
- **Order value / at-stake amount:** 
- **Buyer history:** [orders, prior disputes, account age]
- **Evidence inventory:**

| Evidence | Status | Strength |
|---|---|---|
| e.g., Delivery scan + GPS | Have | Strong |
| Outbound QC photo | Can get (warehouse) | Medium |

- **Strategy:** [full refund / partial / replacement / fight / refund+report] 
- **Why (the math):** refund cost $X vs. fight time-cost + loss risk vs. customer LTV $Y vs. precedent risk — one short paragraph.

## Part 2 — Platform / Evidence Submission

> Written for a reviewer with 90 seconds. No emotion. Every fact dated and tied to an attachment.

**Case summary (1 line):** [e.g., "Tracking, GPS, and delivery photo confirm delivery to the buyer's address on June 3."]

**Facts:**
1. [Fact + evidence reference + attachment #]
2. …

**Policy basis:** [the platform's own rule that decides this case, quoted or referenced]

**Requested outcome:** [deny claim / partial refund of $X / return required before refund / etc.]

**Attachments list:** numbered, matching the facts.

## Part 3 — Buyer Message

> Different audience, different goal: keep the customer (or close respectfully).

- Open: one genuine empathy sentence specific to their situation (not "we apologize for any inconvenience").
- Middle: what you found, factual, no accusations; attach/describe the key evidence in plain language.
- Resolution: exactly what happens next and when (refund timing, replacement tracking, or why the claim outcome rests with the platform).
- Close: forward-looking line.

Length: 60-150 words. Reading level: customer, not legal.

## Part 4 — Prevention & Log Entry

- **Log:** type | SKU | carrier | buyer ID | outcome | cost | date
- **Root-cause flag:** [listing accuracy / packaging / carrier lane / QC / buyer abuse pattern / none]
- **Action:** [e.g., signature confirmation for zip prefix 941xx; add nozzle to QC photo checklist; block-list entry]
- **Separate filings:** [extortion report, fraud report, carrier claim] — each with its own deadline.

---

### Formatting rules

- Platform submission: numbered facts, ≤200 words before attachments.
- Never assert unattached evidence; never editorialize ("clearly lying", "ridiculous claim").
- Buyer message and platform submission are SEPARATE texts — no copy-paste between them.
- All dates absolute (June 3, 14:32), never "last Tuesday".
- State the deadline at the top of the package in bold.
