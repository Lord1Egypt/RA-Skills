## Description: <br>
Professional, non-explicit guidance on intimate wellbeing and sexual performance: communication, stress reduction, sleep, exercise, habits, medical red flags, and relationship health. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nerdcuba](https://clawhub.ai/user/nerdcuba) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for respectful, non-explicit wellbeing guidance about intimacy, communication, lifestyle habits, performance anxiety, and when to seek professional care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may describe sensitive health or intimate wellbeing information. <br>
Mitigation: Encourage users to share only what they are comfortable providing to an AI system. <br>
Risk: The skill may be asked about pain, sudden changes, persistent symptoms, or medical concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Prompt consultation with a qualified clinician for medical red flags and avoid presenting guidance as a diagnosis. <br>
Risk: The topic can drift into explicit sexual content outside the skill scope. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses wellness-focused, respectful, and non-explicit. <br>
