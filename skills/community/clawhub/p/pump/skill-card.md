## Description: <br>
Design launch momentum before a project goes live by optimizing first-day release logic, trigger sequencing, attention concentration, and breakout timing for products, skills, campaigns, and digital launches. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, operators, skill creators, and digital product teams use Pump to diagnose launch momentum gaps and design trigger sequences, first-day curves, and follow-through actions for release events. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad launch-related prompts when the user needs general marketing strategy instead of launch-momentum design. <br>
Mitigation: Use it specifically for release-event momentum planning, and ask whether the user wants launch-momentum design or broader marketing strategy when context is ambiguous. <br>
Risk: Launch recommendations could be mistaken for guaranteed virality or growth outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance and validate them against product quality, audience fit, available channels, launch timing, and operational constraints. <br>
