## Description: <br>
An email productivity assistant that classifies pasted or described emails, drafts professional replies, extracts tasks and deadlines, summarizes long messages, and helps prioritize follow-up. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[crazypatient0](https://clawhub.ai/user/crazypatient0) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users and knowledge workers use this skill to process email text they provide, identify important messages, draft replies, extract action items, and summarize or prioritize follow-up work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Email content may contain secrets, credentials, regulated data, or unnecessary personal or confidential details. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive information before pasting email text into the assistant. <br>
Risk: Generated replies, summaries, priorities, or extracted tasks may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review generated output before sending replies or acting on extracted tasks. <br>
