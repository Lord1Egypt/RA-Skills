## Description: <br>
Atonement is a markdown-only behavior-shaping skill that frames harm reduction as an internal shift in long-horizon intelligence rather than as a safety, compliance, or moderation control. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use Atonement as reflective prompt guidance for reducing engagement with harmful action paths in long-running AI interactions. It should not be treated as moderation, compliance, audit logging, identity protection, or a verifiable safety control. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Readers may mistake the skill for a concrete safety, moderation, compliance, audit, or identity-protection control. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as abstract behavior-shaping prompt guidance and rely on separate verifiable controls for safety, compliance, auditing, and identity protection. <br>
