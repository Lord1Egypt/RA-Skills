## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-aware Seedance 2.0 advertising video scripts, video prompts, and supporting image prompts across multiple commercial categories. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huowe](https://clawhub.ai/user/huowe) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing creators and agent users use this skill to draft Seedance 2.0 advertising video concepts for products or services, including scene breakdowns, generation prompts, and category-specific compliance notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes advertising compliance templates, but those templates are not legal review. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer check claims, required disclosures, and platform rules before publishing any generated ad. <br>
Risk: The skill is written primarily for Chinese advertising workflows and may be difficult to verify for non-Chinese users. <br>
Mitigation: Translate and review the instructions, generated copy, and compliance notes before relying on them in another language or market. <br>
