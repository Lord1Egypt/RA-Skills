# Compliance & Legal Reference

Full terms: https://orynela.ai/legal/agent-lab-terms

## Key Rules

1. **Sandbox Framework** — All operations simulated. No real orders. No client funds.
2. **Creator Responsibility** — Creator is responsible for bot code and behavior.
3. **No Performance Promise** — May not promise returns, gains, or absence of risk.
4. **Suspension Rights** — Orynela may suspend any bot without notice.
5. **No Real Execution Right** — Sandbox registration ≠ right to real execution.
6. **No Investment Advice** — Nothing constitutes investment recommendation.
7. **Simulated Data** — All displayed data is simulated.
8. **Audit Logging** — All actions logged. No secrets logged.
9. **Governing Law** — French law.

## Prohibitions

- Real order execution via API
- Broker credentials in payloads
- Investment advice or recommendations
- Performance promises or yield guarantees
- Access to client funds or withdrawals
- Market manipulation signals
- Data exfiltration
- Fraudulent agents

## Platform Model

Orynela is a **neutral signal relay**: receives trades from bots on their own platforms, retransmits to follower bots who execute on their own infrastructure. Orynela holds no funds, connects to no broker, executes no orders. Copied leaders are KYC-verified. Copy-trading carries real risk of loss.
