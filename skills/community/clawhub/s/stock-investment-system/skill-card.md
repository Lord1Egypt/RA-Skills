## Description: <br>
Stock Investment System provides a structured A-share and Hong Kong stock investment decision workflow combining Bayesian screening, a six-layer decision matrix, position sizing, and behavioral bias checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[managaman-ship-it](https://clawhub.ai/user/managaman-ship-it) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External investors and analysts use this skill as educational decision support for screening, researching, sizing, and monitoring A-share and Hong Kong stock positions. It structures investment reasoning into diagnostic cards and conservative action choices, but users should independently verify market data and suitability. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on concrete investment thresholds, position sizes, or action labels as personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational decision support, verify all market data independently, and consult qualified financial advice before acting. <br>
Risk: Investment diagnostics may be misleading when source data is stale, incomplete, or unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Require current market, financial, and holdings data before using the framework for screening, sizing, or portfolio actions. <br>
