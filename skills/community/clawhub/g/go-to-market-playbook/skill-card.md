## Description: <br>
A reusable go-to-market strategy template for B2B and B2C launches that covers positioning, messaging, ideal customer profiles, channel selection, and competitive analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris-1031](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris-1031) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, marketing, growth, and startup teams use this skill to structure launch planning for B2B or B2C products. It helps organize positioning, ideal customer profile work, messaging, channel-market fit, and competitive analysis into reusable planning outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Planning templates can produce positioning, ICP, channel, or competitive guidance that is incomplete or poorly matched to a specific market. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs with product, marketing, sales, and customer evidence before using them in launch decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill links to related public ClawHub pages and a public X profile. <br>
Mitigation: Verify linked destinations before relying on them or sharing them in a customer-facing workflow. <br>
