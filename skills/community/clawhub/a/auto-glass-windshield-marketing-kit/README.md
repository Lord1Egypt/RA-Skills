## Compliance Moats

**AGRSS Standard** — The Auto Glass Replacement Safety Standard covers installation procedures and certification. The skill gates "AGRSS certified technicians" on your confirmed certification status.

**ADAS Recalibration** — Vehicles with forward-facing cameras (Toyota Safety Sense, Honda Sensing, Subaru EyeSight, GM Super Cruise, etc.) require camera recalibration after windshield replacement. Missing this disclosure is a safety liability. Every windshield replacement page includes this section.

**OEM vs. OEE vs. Aftermarket** — Three different glass tiers with different ADAS compatibility profiles. The skill never implies equivalence and gates "OEM glass" language on your actual inventory.

**Insurance Direct Billing** — "No out-of-pocket" copy always includes "(subject to your deductible)" — FTC-compliant framing that protects you from deceptive advertising claims.

**Rock Chip Repair Limits** — AGRSS standard: chip <1" (25mm) diameter, crack <3" (75mm) long, not in driver's primary view field, not within 2" of the glass edge. Every chip repair page states these limits — sets proper customer expectations and protects you from warranty disputes.
