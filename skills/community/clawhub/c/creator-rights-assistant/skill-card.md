## Description: <br>
Standardize provenance, attribution, and licensing metadata at creation time so content travels cleanly across platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[OtherPowers](https://clawhub.ai/user/OtherPowers) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and rights operations teams use this skill to organize provenance, attribution, licensing scope, and asset lifecycle metadata before publication or distribution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake organized rights metadata for legal advice, ownership validation, or platform outcome prediction. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as an organizational helper only, keep the first-turn disclaimer visible, and verify legal, license, ownership, and attribution questions with qualified review. <br>
Risk: Incorrect provenance, license, attribution, or source-reference inputs could create misleading records that travel with an asset. <br>
Mitigation: Require creators to verify the accuracy and completeness of supplied information before relying on generated metadata. <br>
Risk: Asset metadata collection may include unnecessary personal or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact personal information during ingestion and record only the metadata needed for provenance, attribution, licensing, and internal tracking. <br>
