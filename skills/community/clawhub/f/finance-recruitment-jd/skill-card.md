## Description: <br>
Generates structured finance recruitment job descriptions from a role name, selected capability requirements, weights, and optional organization background. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
HR recruiters and finance leaders use this skill to draft finance role job descriptions with responsibilities, qualifications, experience expectations, and KPIs aligned to selected finance capabilities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated job descriptions may contain inaccurate, biased, or legally non-compliant HR language. <br>
Mitigation: Review each draft for role accuracy, fairness, and applicable HR or legal compliance before use. <br>
Risk: Broad HR and recruitment trigger phrases may activate the skill unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit prompts that name the finance role and intended JD drafting task. <br>
