## Use Case: <br>
External content creators and teams use this skill to draft, optimize, and adapt Chinese social-media articles for WeChat public accounts, Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, and similar platforms. It helps produce titles, article structure, cover and image suggestions, formatting guidance, and more natural human-facing prose. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording could cause the skill to engage during unrelated writing or publication tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for explicit content-writing, optimization, or publication tasks and review generated content before public use. <br>
Risk: Generated article guidance may be inaccurate, misleading, or unsuitable for the intended audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review facts, claims, titles, image suggestions, and final copy before publishing. <br>
