## Description: <br>
Analyzes business contracts to identify risks, flag problematic clauses, check for missing protections, and generate negotiation checklists with an overall risk score. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operations teams, freelancers, agencies, and business reviewers use this skill for first-pass contract analysis before negotiation or legal review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Contracts may contain sensitive business, legal, financial, or personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the contract text or specific file needed for review, and avoid unrelated folders or sensitive extras. <br>
Risk: The skill output is a first-pass contract review and may miss legal nuance or jurisdiction-specific requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Treat findings as review guidance and have qualified counsel evaluate important contracts before signing. <br>
