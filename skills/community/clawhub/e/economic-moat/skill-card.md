## Description: <br>
Helps an agent evaluate whether a business has a durable economic moat by testing structural sources, moat trajectory, and smart-attacker resistance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[deciqai](https://clawhub.ai/user/deciqai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business strategists, investors, founders, and acquisition reviewers use this skill to structure long-horizon analysis of whether a company has defensible returns, what creates the moat, and whether it is widening or narrowing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Investment-related output could be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill's output as analytical framing, verify claims with independent evidence, and use qualified financial judgment before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate during broad business-strategy discussions where moat analysis is not the right tool. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for multi-year defensibility, investment, acquisition, or startup strategy questions, and avoid relying on it for short-term execution or single-quarter decisions. <br>
