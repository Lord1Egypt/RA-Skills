## Description: <br>
Intelligent recruiting assistant for technical talents, featuring multi-platform candidate search, profile analysis, personalized email generation, and candidate pipeline management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[BiancaBian77](https://clawhub.ai/user/BiancaBian77) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Recruiters, hiring managers, and sourcing teams use this skill to find and analyze algorithm and R&D candidates across public technical platforms, draft personalized outreach, and manage a recruiting pipeline. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cross-platform candidate profiling can collect excessive or job-irrelevant personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only lawful, job-relevant recruiting data from approved sources, provide candidate notice or opt-out where required, and minimize retained personal data. <br>
Risk: Continuing after anti-bot or captcha challenges with proxy workarounds can create site-term and compliance risk. <br>
Mitigation: Treat anti-bot or captcha challenges as stop conditions and prefer official APIs or approved manual workflows. <br>
Risk: Recruiting reports, Feishu records, and API credentials may expose candidate data or secrets if stored broadly. <br>
Mitigation: Store exports and Feishu records in restricted locations, keep API tokens out of committed config files, and use approved secret handling. <br>
