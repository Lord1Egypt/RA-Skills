## Description: <br>
Universal skill publishing checklist and rules for ClawHub, GitHub, Claude Code, Hermes, and OpenClaw, covering pre-publish vetting, anti-duplicate checks, naming conventions, and promotion strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and skill publishers use this checklist to prepare AI agent skills for release across ClawHub, GitHub, Claude Code, Hermes, OpenClaw, and similar platforms. It guides pre-release review, naming, metadata, publishing, and post-publish validation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The checklist includes the author's PayPal and GitHub links in template guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Replace or remove those links before reusing the template in another publisher's skill. <br>
Risk: The checklist includes publish, install, and repository commands that may affect local projects or public releases. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands and substitute the correct slug, workspace, and account values before running them. <br>
