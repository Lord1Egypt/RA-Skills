## Description: <br>
Rigorous evidence-based inquiry: decompose fuzzy questions, retrieve & grade evidence (S/A/B/C/D), cross-validate, and output conclusions with confidence intervals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liwenyu2002](https://clawhub.ai/user/liwenyu2002) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to plan and conduct evidence-based research, fact-checking, trend analysis, comparisons, and industry inquiries. It guides agents to confirm scope before searching, grade sources, cross-validate findings, and present conclusions with confidence and uncertainty. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research queries may send sensitive context to external public sources after plan approval. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include secrets or private data in research prompts, and review the proposed plan before approving retrieval. <br>
Risk: The workflow can persist preferences or change future skill behavior when the user explicitly approves updates. <br>
Mitigation: Approve memory or skill updates only when the behavior change is intentional, and review updated skill content before deployment. <br>
