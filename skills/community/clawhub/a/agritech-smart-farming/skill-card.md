## Description: <br>
AgriTech Smart Farming is a Chinese-language smart agriculture assistant for crop pest and disease guidance, soil nutrient and fertilizer analysis, crop price tracking, subsidy policy lookup, machinery matching, and climate-adaptive planting advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Farmers, agricultural cooperatives, agricultural input dealers, and agricultural investors use this skill to generate localized decision-support guidance for Chinese smart farming scenarios. It supports diagnosis and planning across crop health, fertilization, market prices, policy eligibility, machinery sourcing, and weather-related planting risk. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agricultural and pesticide recommendations may be incorrect, incomplete, or unsuitable for a specific farm, crop stage, location, regulation, or treatment scale. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before acting on them and consult qualified agricultural experts for large-scale treatment, pesticide use, or high-impact farm management decisions. <br>
Risk: Market prices and policy details may be delayed or change after the skill generates guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current prices, subsidy eligibility, application deadlines, and regulatory requirements with authoritative local sources before making financial or compliance decisions. <br>
