## Description: <br>
Prime provides a strategic guide for evaluating and maximizing Amazon Prime membership value across shipping, deals, digital entertainment, photo storage, and household sharing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Consumers and account-management users can use this skill to compare Amazon Prime benefits, estimate whether membership is financially worthwhile, and plan use of shipping, deal, streaming, photo-storage, and household-sharing features. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Amazon Household or teen-profile guidance can involve sharing payment methods, content access, and purchase permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Review Amazon account-sharing, payment, content-access, and purchase-permission settings before applying those recommendations. <br>
Risk: Membership pricing, benefit availability, delivery timing, and promotion rules can vary by region and change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current Amazon Prime terms, local availability, and pricing before making purchase or subscription decisions. <br>
Risk: The value calculator is illustrative and may not reflect an individual user's actual costs or savings. <br>
Mitigation: Replace example assumptions with current membership fees, actual shipping spend, subscription costs, and storage needs before relying on the calculation. <br>
