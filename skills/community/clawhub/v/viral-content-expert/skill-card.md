## Description: <br>
Creates secondary viral-content scripts, captions, titles, tags, and visual-support guidance for Douyin, WeChat Channels, and Xiaohongshu by researching current trends, structuring platform-specific angles, and checking factual claims. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and social media operators use this skill to turn current topics into ready-to-publish secondary content tailored for Douyin, WeChat Channels, and Xiaohongshu. It is intended for drafting scripts, captions, titles, tags, interaction prompts, and visual guidance that still require human review before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate unattended scheduled content and save or deploy outputs without clear approval controls. <br>
Mitigation: Keep scheduled runs disabled unless scope and cadence are explicitly configured, and require user confirmation before writing files, downloading images, or deploying content. <br>
Risk: Trend-based social content may contain incorrect, misleading, or non-compliant claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review all outputs before publishing and verify factual claims against authoritative sources. <br>
