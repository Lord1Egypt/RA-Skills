## Description: <br>
心理测评 guides users through PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS-10, RSES, and BFI-10 self-assessments, scores responses, and produces a local visual HTML report with interpretation and support resources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for guided mental-health self-assessment and local report generation. It supports screening and self-reflection, not clinical diagnosis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks sensitive mental-health screening questions and creates a local report. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use it only in a private environment, and delete the generated report on shared devices. <br>
Risk: Self-assessment results can be mistaken for a clinical diagnosis. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as screening information and consult a qualified professional for diagnosis or treatment decisions. <br>
Risk: Severe or self-harm results require immediate attention. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the skill guidance to contact a qualified professional or crisis service when severe symptoms or self-harm indicators appear. <br>
