## Description: <br>
Search jobs, build professional resumes, and get career advice using Workopia's hosted MCP server. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and career-focused agents use this skill to search employer-sourced jobs, improve or generate resumes, and request career advice such as transition planning and skill gap analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Job-search prompts, resume text, and career questions are sent to Workopia's hosted MCP endpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the personal and career information needed for the task, avoid unnecessary sensitive details, and review Workopia's current privacy terms before use. <br>
Risk: Resume PDF generation and high-volume use may depend on Workopia's current quota, retention, and pricing terms. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current Workopia terms before relying on generated PDFs, larger workloads, or custom quota arrangements. <br>
