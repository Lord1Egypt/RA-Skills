## Description: <br>
Evolutionary Iteration helps an agent generate several solution options, iterate through evaluation, selection, crossover, and mutation, then present optimal options and choose a best solution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and other agent users use this skill to structure open-ended problem solving into candidate generation, iterative evaluation, selection, refinement, and final delivery. It is most useful when a task benefits from comparing multiple solution paths before choosing one. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generic trigger phrases may activate the skill during unrelated planning tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Review when the skill activates and disable it if it appears during unrelated work. <br>
Risk: Generated solution options can be misleading if they are not grounded in verifiable task evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's factual-discipline rules: rely on known facts, verify evaluation criteria, and mark uncertain cases for review. <br>
