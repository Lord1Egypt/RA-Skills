## Description: <br>
Helps agents invert goals into likely failure paths, avoidance strategies, and anti-checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[artyomx33](https://clawhub.ai/user/artyomx33) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to analyze plans by asking what would guarantee failure, then turning those failure paths into concrete avoidance strategies and checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personalized business examples may influence later answers in ways that are not appropriate for every user or organization. <br>
Mitigation: Review the bundled examples before installation and remove or edit examples that should not shape future reasoning. <br>
Risk: Broad activation phrases such as "flip it" or "how would this fail" may trigger the skill in general planning conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit invocation or review outputs for relevance when those phrases appear outside a deliberate inversion exercise. <br>
Risk: The skill produces strategic guidance that may be incomplete or misleading if treated as a decision authority. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a reasoning aid and validate recommendations against domain expertise, current constraints, and stakeholder review. <br>
