## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses a paid external CellCog service that receives prompts and media inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with trusted CellCog access, review CellCog terms and billing, and avoid sending secrets, confidential business material, or regulated personal data. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a CellCog API key. <br>
Mitigation: Store CELLCOG_API_KEY securely, avoid pasting credentials into prompts, and rotate the key if exposure is suspected. <br>
Risk: Generated videos may include real people’s likeness or voice. <br>
Mitigation: Use likenesses, voices, and personal media only with appropriate approval and in line with CellCog synthetic-media policies. <br>
Risk: Long-form AI video generation can be unpredictable and may consume credits without producing a usable result. <br>
Mitigation: Start with short, well-scoped prompts, review outputs before publication, and set cost expectations before using the service for production work. <br>
