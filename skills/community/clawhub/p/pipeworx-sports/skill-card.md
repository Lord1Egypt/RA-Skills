## Description: <br>
Provides live scores, standings, team information, and player data across football, basketball, baseball, hockey, and other sports through a Pipeworx-hosted sports MCP gateway. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[brucegutman](https://clawhub.ai/user/brucegutman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to look up current and historical sports data, including team search results, player profiles, league standings, recent results, and upcoming fixtures. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sports-related queries are sent to the Pipeworx MCP gateway. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including personal, confidential, or sensitive information in sports lookup queries. <br>
Risk: Server-resolved GitHub import provenance is unavailable for this release. <br>
Mitigation: Use this release when the ClawHub evidence and disclosed endpoint are sufficient; prefer a provider with public source or documentation when provenance is required. <br>
