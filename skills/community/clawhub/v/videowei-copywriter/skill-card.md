## Description: <br>
生成适合视频号带货的中文口播文案，按 11 个爆点结构组织健康、食材、书籍或话题类内容。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[KtsslyTop](https://clawhub.ai/user/KtsslyTop) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and commerce operators use this skill to draft long-form Chinese Video Account sales scripts, especially for food, health, book, and topic-based promotions that follow a proven dialogue structure. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated health, nutrition, author, research, or product claims may be inaccurate or overstate benefits. <br>
Mitigation: Fact-check claims before publication and avoid medical guarantees. <br>
Risk: Persuasive sales language may be sensitive when aimed at older or health-concerned audiences. <br>
Mitigation: Review tone and calls to action so the final script remains accurate, fair, and appropriate for the intended audience. <br>
