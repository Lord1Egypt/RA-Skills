## Description: <br>
对已初步处理的文本进行二次轻微扰动。用于第一轮重写后需要进一步降低风格一致性特征时使用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhengqizhuang](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhengqizhuang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and writing assistants use this skill to apply a second, subtle style perturbation pass to already rewritten academic text, varying rhythm, wording, references, and local transitions while preserving terminology and avoiding new information. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The rewritten text may blur style signals or subtly affect meaning and local logical flow, especially in academic or attributed writing. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review each output against the source text before use, confirming terminology, meaning, attribution, and logical continuity. <br>
