## Description: <br>
Generates C-ROSS Phase II solvency report drafts, capital adequacy calculations, stress testing material, and risk rating documentation for Chinese insurance finance, actuarial, and compliance teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gechengling](https://clawhub.ai/user/gechengling) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External insurance company CFOs, actuaries, and compliance teams use this skill to draft quarterly or annual C-ROSS solvency reports, assess capital adequacy, and prepare stress testing and risk rating documentation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated regulatory reporting guidance or solvency calculations may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for official filings. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current NFRA requirements, company inputs, and all generated calculations with qualified actuarial, finance, and compliance reviewers before official use. <br>
