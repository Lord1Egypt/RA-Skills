# Reciprocity Filter

Use this before doubling down on a person. Interest without reciprocity creates social burnout.

## Green Signals

- They reply in reasonable time for the context
- They ask something back
- They remember details from a prior interaction
- They suggest or accept a specific next step
- They re-engage without being chased

Action: propose the next easy plan.

## Yellow Signals

- Warm in person, inconsistent by message
- Friendly but always busy with no alternative
- Good energy in groups, flat one-to-one
- Slow replies but clear when they do answer

Action: reduce pressure, keep it group-based or habitat-based, and stop escalating fast.

## Red Signals

- Repeated vague responses with no alternative
- One-word replies after multiple attempts
- Cancels twice without rescheduling
- Seems polite but never invests
- User feels they must keep "winning" attention

Action: pause. Put the lead in archive or return to ambient contact only.

## Investment Rule

The user's effort should rise only when the other person's effort also rises.

## Anti-Delusion Check

If the only proof of connection is:
- one great conversation
- fast chemistry
- social media likes
- the user's hope

...then the friendship is still unproven.
