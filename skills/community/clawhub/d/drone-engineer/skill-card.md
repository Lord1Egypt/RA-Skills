## Description: <br>
Professional entry-level drone and UAV systems engineering career roadmap platform that generates personalized learning paths based on experience and skills assessment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, career changers, engineering students, and technical professionals use this skill to request personalized drone and UAV systems engineering career roadmaps from assessment data about experience, skills, goals, and session metadata. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the roadmap endpoint may send career history, skills, goals, timestamps, and session or user identifiers to the API operator. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the assessment details needed for roadmap generation and avoid confidential employer, project, or personal information. <br>
Risk: Career roadmaps may be incomplete or not aligned with a user's local regulatory, certification, or employment requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated recommendations against authoritative certification bodies, local aviation rules, employer requirements, and professional judgment before acting on them. <br>
