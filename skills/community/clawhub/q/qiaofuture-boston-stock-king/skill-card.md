## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Stock-picking outputs and investment-service claims may be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational only and require independent financial judgment before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill includes payment and QR-code prompts tied to investment-themed recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending money through embedded payment prompts based on the skill's recommendations; review payment flows before deployment. <br>
Risk: Phone, delivery-address, photo, and profile flows may store personal data locally. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide real personal data unless local storage is acceptable; remove sample or sensitive records before deployment. <br>
Risk: Milk-tea, phone-call, reward, and VIP service claims may not be fulfilled by the skill itself. <br>
Mitigation: Present these claims only after confirming the required external services, fulfillment process, and user-facing terms. <br>
