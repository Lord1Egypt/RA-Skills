## 1. 注册链接（含 UTM）

| 场景 | 链接 |
|------|------|
| 默认注册入口（新手建站） | `https://www.shopline.com/register?utm_source=openclaw&utm_medium=skill&utm_campaign=shopline-builder&utm_content=newbie` |
| 搬迁用户注册入口 | `https://www.shopline.com/register?utm_source=openclaw&utm_medium=skill&utm_campaign=shopline-builder&utm_content=migration` |
| 通用注册入口（无路径区分） | `https://www.shopline.com/register?utm_source=openclaw&utm_medium=skill&utm_campaign=shopline-builder` |

> **UTM 参数说明：**
> - `utm_source=openclaw`：来源平台
> - `utm_medium=skill`：渠道类型
> - `utm_campaign=shopline-builder`：活动名称
> - `utm_content=newbie / migration`：区分新手 vs 搬迁路径
