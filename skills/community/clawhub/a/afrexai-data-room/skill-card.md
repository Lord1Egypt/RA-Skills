## Description: <br>
Builds a structured virtual data room folder hierarchy, document checklist, gap analysis, and access-control guidance for fundraising, M&A, or due diligence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, finance teams, operators, and deal teams use this skill to prepare a data room checklist tailored to company stage, industry, transaction type, and investor or acquirer requests. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Data room planning may involve confidential company records, employee compensation, tax records, legal disputes, or investor materials. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting sensitive records unless the agent environment is trusted; use the output as a planning checklist rather than as a secure document repository. <br>
Risk: Checklist output could be mistaken for legal, tax, or deal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified counsel or transaction advisors review sensitive documents and sharing decisions before disclosure. <br>
