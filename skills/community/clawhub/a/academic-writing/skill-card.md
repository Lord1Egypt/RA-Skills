## Description: <br>
Supports scholarly papers, literature reviews, research methodology, and thesis writing with formal academic structure and citation standards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[teamolab](https://clawhub.ai/user/teamolab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, students, researchers, and academic writers use this skill to draft and structure scholarly Markdown content with formal tone, sequential citations, references, tables, and diagrams when appropriate. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Academic writing outputs can contain incorrect citations, unverifiable claims, or material that conflicts with institutional academic integrity policies. <br>
Mitigation: Users should independently verify citations, factual claims, and compliance with their institution's academic integrity policies before using the output. <br>
