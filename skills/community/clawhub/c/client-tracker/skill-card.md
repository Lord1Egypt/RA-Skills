## Description: <br>
Simple CRM for freelancers. Track contacts, projects, invoices, follow-ups, communication history. All through conversation with your agent. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TheShadowRose](https://clawhub.ai/user/TheShadowRose) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and freelancers use this skill to track client contacts, projects, invoices, follow-ups, communication history, pipeline stages, and revenue summaries through an agent conversation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local CRM records may contain sensitive client, invoice, budget, or notes data. <br>
Mitigation: Protect the local clients folder, avoid storing unnecessary sensitive information, keep backups as appropriate, and confirm before creating or updating records. <br>
Risk: Invoice, budget, revenue, and pipeline outputs may be mistaken for professional financial or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as operational tracking information and verify business decisions independently. <br>
