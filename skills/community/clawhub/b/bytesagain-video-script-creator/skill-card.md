## Description: <br>
Generate platform-optimized video scripts with hooks and timed segments for YouTube, TikTok, Douyin, and Bilibili. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and video producers use this skill to draft ready-to-film scripts, timed outlines, hooks, camera directions, calls to action, and platform-specific formatting for short-form and long-form video. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scripts may include generic claims, calls to action, hashtags, or production guidance that do not match the creator's brand, audience, or platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated copy before filming or publishing, especially claims, examples, hashtags, and audience prompts. <br>
Risk: The skill is delivered as a shell script that invokes local Python for generation. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands before running them and execute with least-privilege local permissions. <br>
