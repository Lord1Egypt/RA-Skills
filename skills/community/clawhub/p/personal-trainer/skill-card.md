## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat general fitness guidance as medical care or injury assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Advise users with injuries, pregnancy, chronic conditions, pain, or post-surgery status to consult a qualified professional before following workout advice. <br>
Risk: The agent cannot observe exercise form or physically spot a user during training. <br>
Mitigation: Recommend conservative loading, safety equipment, regression options, and professional form checks when needed. <br>
