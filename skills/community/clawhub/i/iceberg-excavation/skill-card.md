## Description: <br>
Iceberg Excavation guides an agent through layered emotional self-reflection to analyze emotional triggers, subconscious needs, thought patterns, and core operating beliefs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jack-long-2022](https://clawhub.ai/user/jack-long-2022) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to turn an emotional concern into a structured, configurable self-reflection report with optional scoring and system-reconstruction suggestions. It is not medical therapy or diagnosis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can elicit sensitive psychological self-reflection and may make emotional-pattern inferences. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for reflective emotional-pattern analysis, avoid sharing crisis, trauma, or highly private details unless comfortable, and seek qualified professional help for clinical or crisis needs. <br>
Risk: The agent may remember analysis-depth preferences or other details if memory is enabled. <br>
Mitigation: Tell the agent not to save memories unless you explicitly want preferences such as shallow versus deep analysis remembered. <br>
