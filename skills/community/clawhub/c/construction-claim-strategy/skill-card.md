## Description: <br>
Strategic planning framework for construction claim responses that helps users plan direction, scope, arguments, disclosure strategy, response architecture, and risk assessment before selecting clauses or drafting correspondence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[redkiwi1688-prog](https://clawhub.ai/user/redkiwi1688-prog) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Construction contract managers, claims consultants, quantity surveyors, commercial managers, and construction lawyers use this skill to plan claim-response strategy for EOT, delay, disruption, variation, and payment disputes. It supports response scoping, argument ranking, disclosure control, deadline awareness, ADR choices, risk assessment, and report generation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated claim strategy may be incomplete, jurisdiction-specific, or unsuitable for a live dispute if treated as legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified counsel review disclosure decisions, deadlines, dispute submissions, and any reliance on the generated strategy. <br>
Risk: Inputs and generated reports can contain confidential legal or commercial project information. <br>
Mitigation: Run the local tool in an approved environment and handle input files and generated reports as confidential material. <br>
Risk: Unrelated metadata capability tags may imply payment, wallet, or account authority that the security evidence does not support. <br>
Mitigation: Do not grant payment, wallet, or account permissions when installing or running this skill. <br>
