## Description: <br>
Audits a game's FTUE (First Time User Experience) through the lens of the Hero's Journey / monomyth, including onboarding, tutorial flow, first-session retention, early emotional hooks, mentor usage, first meaningful action, and whether the player feels like the hero of the experience. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, UX researchers, product teams, and developers use this skill to audit game onboarding and early-session flows through a Hero's Journey lens. It helps identify emotional hooks, guidance quality, drop risks, psychological-need gaps, and concrete FTUE recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
