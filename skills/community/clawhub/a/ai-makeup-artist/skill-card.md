## Use Case: <br>
External users and beauty-focused agents use this skill to produce makeup guidance, cosmetic technique suggestions, skincare routine ideas, and product-selection advice for everyday, professional, bridal, editorial, and special-event looks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Skincare guidance may be mistaken for medical care. <br>
Mitigation: Treat skincare recommendations as general beauty guidance and consult qualified professionals for medical concerns. <br>
Risk: Product suggestions may influence purchases or require sharing sensitive appearance details. <br>
Mitigation: Review product recommendations before spending money and avoid sharing unnecessary sensitive personal details. <br>
