## Description: <br>
Product changelog and release notes that users actually read, covering categorization, user-facing language, visuals, and distribution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[okaris](https://clawhub.ai/user/okaris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, developer relations teams, founders, and release owners use this skill to turn product changes into readable changelogs, release notes, feature announcements, and update distribution plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes an external CLI installer and optional visual-generation workflows through inference.sh. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when inference.sh and the infsh CLI are trusted, and use the manual checksum-verification path in sensitive environments. <br>
Risk: Visual examples may send app URLs, screenshots, prompts, or product details to an external provider. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use private app URLs, unreleased screenshots, credentials, customer data, or confidential product details unless sharing that material externally is approved. <br>
