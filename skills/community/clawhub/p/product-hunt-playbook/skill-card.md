## Description: <br>
Product Hunt Playbook gives launch teams an hour-by-hour Product Hunt launch plan, ranking-factor guidance, voter mobilization tactics, asset preparation guidance, and post-launch conversion steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris-1031](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris-1031) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders, makers, and go-to-market operators use this skill to plan and coordinate a Product Hunt launch. It helps an agent provide launch-day checklists, outreach guidance, Product Hunt ranking considerations, asset preparation advice, and post-launch follow-up steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing and paid-service promotion may influence the launch advice or outbound links. <br>
Mitigation: Review linked services and distinguish free playbook guidance from paid offers before relying on recommendations. <br>
Risk: The broad "launch day" trigger may activate the skill for launches unrelated to Product Hunt. <br>
Mitigation: Use Product Hunt-specific prompts or narrow activation triggers before deployment. <br>
Risk: Launch tactics and voter mobilization suggestions may need policy review before outreach. <br>
Mitigation: Treat mobilization steps as proposals and review them against the launch team's outreach rules before use. <br>
