## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides broad browser-control guidance for sensitive workflows such as checkout, form submission, account changes, messaging, and public posting. <br>
Mitigation: Keep tasks narrow and require explicit confirmation before submitting forms, completing purchases, changing account settings, sending messages, or posting publicly. <br>
Risk: Screenshots, page HTML, URLs, and visible private data may be captured or sent to a configured vision model. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive logged-in sites unless necessary and review captured browser context before using external vision or automation services. <br>
