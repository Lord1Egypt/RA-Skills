## Description: <br>
Deep design systems workflow-tokens, components, accessibility, documentation, governance, and adoption for scaling UI consistency across teams or evolving a component library with Figma and code parity. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Designers, frontend developers, and product teams use this skill to plan or scale a design system across tokens, components, accessibility, documentation, governance, and adoption. It is most relevant when multiple applications diverge visually, accessibility debt is growing, or Figma and production code are drifting apart. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Design-system recommendations may be incomplete or mismatched to a team's framework, accessibility baseline, or governance needs. <br>
Mitigation: Review the staged guidance with design, engineering, and accessibility owners before adopting it for a production component library. <br>
