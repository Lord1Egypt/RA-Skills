## Description: <br>
Ashin Perspective is a Chinese-language persona skill that applies a research-derived model of Chen Xinhong (Ashin)'s public style to analyze problems, review decisions, and provide feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jlcaiye](https://clawhub.ai/user/jlcaiye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to switch into a stylized Ashin-inspired perspective for reflective analysis, decision feedback, and companion-style communication. It should be treated as an AI-generated roleplay based on public materials, not as statements from the real person. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake stylized persona responses for statements or intent from the real person. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as AI-generated roleplay based on public materials and retain the first-activation disclaimer described by the skill. <br>
Risk: The persona may remain active when the user wants normal assistant behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented exit phrases such as 'exit', 'switch back to normal', or equivalent requests to leave the role. <br>
