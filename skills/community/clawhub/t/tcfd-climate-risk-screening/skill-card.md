## Description: <br>
Guides sustainability, risk, finance, and board users through a structured TCFD-aligned climate risk assessment, including physical and transition risk screening, scenario analysis documentation, financial materiality assessment, and draft disclosure narrative preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
CFOs, sustainability leads, ESG reporting officers, risk managers, board audit committees, climate risk consultants, advisors, and compliance teams use this skill to draft internal climate risk assessments and TCFD-aligned disclosure packages for qualified review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft climate risk assessments or disclosure narratives may be mistaken for final public reporting or regulatory filing material. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and require review by qualified sustainability, legal, audit, or regulatory advisors before external disclosure. <br>
Risk: Physical risk exposure ratings, transition risks, and financial materiality estimates may be incomplete when based only on user-provided or qualitative information. <br>
Mitigation: Label estimates as preliminary management estimates and use asset-level modelling or validated third-party datasets for quantification. <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary confidential asset, financial, or supply-chain details while preparing the assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the minimum information needed for drafting and avoid unnecessary confidential details. <br>
