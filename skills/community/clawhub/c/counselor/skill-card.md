## Description: <br>
Professional counseling assistant for life challenges, career decisions, relationship issues, and personal growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users can use this skill for structured reflection around life challenges, career decisions, relationship issues, academic struggles, and personal growth. It helps users clarify values, compare perspectives, and consider practical next steps while directing serious mental health needs to qualified professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat counseling-style output as therapy, emergency support, or licensed medical or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as a lightweight reflection aid and switch to qualified professionals or emergency resources for serious distress, self-harm, abuse, or regulated decisions. <br>
Risk: Guidance may be incomplete for high-stakes personal, relationship, career, academic, or mental health situations. <br>
Mitigation: Frame responses as decision support, ask clarifying questions, present multiple perspectives, and avoid prescribing a single course of action. <br>
