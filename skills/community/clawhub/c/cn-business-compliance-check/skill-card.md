## Description: <br>
Screens business strategies, side projects, and monetization plans for China-focused compliance risks and produces structured review or warning reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kuanghs](https://clawhub.ai/user/kuanghs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill before pursuing China-focused commercial plans, side projects, or monetization workflows to identify redline, operational, and revenue-model compliance risks. It is a checklist-style aid and does not replace legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat checklist output as legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as general compliance guidance and consult qualified counsel before acting on high-stakes or ambiguous business decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad activation may interrupt ordinary business-planning conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for explicit China business compliance review requests or when the user asks about legality, compliance, redlines, or legal risk. <br>
Risk: Static redline and gray-zone catalogs may miss newer regulatory changes or fact-specific obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited legal areas against current authoritative sources before using the report for operational decisions. <br>
