## Description: <br>
Generate complete undergraduate thesis topic, proposal, opening-report, and writing guidance from a user's research theme, population, variables, influencing factors, or preferred method. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn rough undergraduate thesis ideas into feasible research topics, proposal structures, opening reports, chapter plans, and writing guidance. It is especially focused on psychology, counseling, education, social science, and student-development thesis work in Chinese or English. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Thesis plans may include citation, school-rule, ethics, or mental-health intervention details that require local verification. <br>
Mitigation: Users should verify citations, school requirements, ethics constraints, and mental-health intervention plans with supervisors or qualified reviewers before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Some built-in templates are Chinese-first, so English users may receive less suitable phrasing by default. <br>
Mitigation: English users should explicitly request English output and review terminology for their institution's expected thesis format. <br>
