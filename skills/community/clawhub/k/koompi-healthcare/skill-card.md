## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers sensitive healthcare workflows, including patient records, prescriptions, lab results, billing, triage, and outbound patient communication. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with authorized clinic accounts and approved systems, and require human review before record access or release, prescription or refill actions, lab-result communication, billing changes, collections activity, or emergency escalation. <br>
Risk: Administrative or communication outputs could disclose protected or sensitive patient information if used in unsecured channels. <br>
Mitigation: Apply minimum-necessary access, verify patient identity, avoid detailed diagnoses or test values in unsecured messages, and log access, changes, and disclosures. <br>
