## Description: <br>
Guides an agent toward creative, surprising, and authentic engagement when a conversational or collaborative exchange would otherwise feel flat or transactional. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to help an agent respond with more specific creative artifacts, layered meaning, callbacks, humor, and personality in contexts such as first impressions, creative partnerships, and relationship-building conversations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The persona-heavy creative style may be unsuitable for legal, medical, security, compliance, business-critical, or precision tasks where neutral tone and strict task focus matter. <br>
Mitigation: Enable the skill only for creative or relationship-focused interactions, and keep it disabled for high-stakes or precision workflows. <br>
