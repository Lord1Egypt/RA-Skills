## Description: <br>
Ssq Predictor analyzes local Double Chromosphere lottery history CSV data and produces balanced number recommendations using hot/cold counts, missing-value tracking, and zone-distribution patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to analyze SSQ lottery history data and generate multiple zone-balanced number recommendation sets. Outputs should be treated as entertainment and statistical exploration, not financial advice or a guarantee of winning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lottery recommendations may be misunderstood as financial advice or a promise of winning. <br>
Mitigation: Present predictions as entertainment-only statistical exploration and avoid using them as the basis for financial decisions. <br>
Risk: The scripts read a local SSQ history CSV and results depend on that file's contents. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the intended lottery CSV from a trusted source and review the output before relying on it. <br>
