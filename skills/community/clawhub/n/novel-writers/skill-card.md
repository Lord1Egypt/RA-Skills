### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation phrases may route general writing requests to a genre-specific helper. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke it explicitly for Tomato-style Chinese urban science-fiction work, and choose a different writing skill for other genres, languages, or platform styles. <br>
