## Description: <br>
Triages UK tachograph and WTD infringements, identifies common patterns, and outputs what-to-check-next prompts plus weekly review notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[KOwl64](https://clawhub.ai/user/KOwl64) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Transport managers and compliance reviewers use this skill to turn weekly UK tachograph and WTD infringement records into neutral triage notes, per-driver follow-up actions, and manager-facing review packs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United Kingdom <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide unauthorized, incomplete, or inaccurate tachograph/WTD records or prior RAG history. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm records are authorized, complete, current, and handled under applicable privacy, HR, and compliance policies before using the triage output. <br>
Risk: Triage notes could be treated as final policy, HR, or disciplinary decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as review support only; require human policy review before escalation, investigation, or disciplinary action. <br>
Risk: Incomplete records, missing downloads, or unclear context can lead to unsupported conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Flag data gaps, ask for missing driver lists, periods, and RAG history, and verify findings against the supplied record extract. <br>
