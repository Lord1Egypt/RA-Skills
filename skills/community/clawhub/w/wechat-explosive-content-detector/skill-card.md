## Description: <br>
A data-insight skill for WeChat Official Account content planning that searches recent high-performing articles, filters them by signals such as low-follower/high-read performance, growth, ranking, and originality, and returns reference samples for creators. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yuanyi-github](https://clawhub.ai/user/yuanyi-github) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, editors, WeChat Official Account operators, and MCN or growth teams use this skill to find recent popular article examples, compare topic patterns, and generate up to 10 relevant recommendations for a requested niche or keyword. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The data-fetching script disables HTTPS verification, so returned reports or article links could be altered in transit. <br>
Mitigation: Review the skill before installation, avoid use on untrusted networks, and fix HTTPS verification before relying on generated reports. <br>
Risk: Search keywords and dates are sent to a third-party content-data API and local report files are written. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive searches and confirm that displaying or exporting public-account metadata fits the user's compliance requirements. <br>
