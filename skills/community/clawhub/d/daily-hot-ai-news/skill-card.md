## Description: <br>
Produces concise Chinese AI news briefs with current headlines, analysis, impact notes, and source links when a user asks for AI news or trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chenhao11235813](https://clawhub.ai/user/chenhao11235813) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill on demand to understand recent AI industry news, technical breakthroughs, market events, and public discussion in a Chinese-language brief. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: News summaries may include incomplete, outdated, or misleading claims from search results. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims against the cited source links before relying on them. <br>
Risk: User-provided news queries could reveal private interests or sensitive context through web search. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including private information in prompts for AI news briefs. <br>
