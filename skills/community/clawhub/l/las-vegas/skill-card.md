Risk: Persistent memory may capture personal travel, budget, work, family, and relocation details without clear user-facing consent. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for explicit approval before saving personal details, honor declined or never-ask preferences, and avoid recording sensitive information unless the user approves it. <br>
Risk: The skill covers high-impact topics such as medical, legal, tax, housing, licensing, safety, venue, and pricing decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify current information with authoritative sources and qualified professionals before acting on these recommendations. <br>
