## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Hands-on sharpening can expose users to sharp edges and blade-path injuries. <br>
Mitigation: Use a stable work surface, keep fingers out of the blade path, and prefer paper or food-based sharpness tests unless experienced. <br>
Risk: Tool-specific or workplace rules may impose requirements beyond the skill's general guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Follow local rules, workplace policies, and manufacturer safety instructions before sharpening or using tools. <br>
