## Description: <br>
Collaborative thinking partner for exploring complex problems through questioning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jirboy](https://clawhub.ai/user/jirboy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to explore complex problems through structured questioning, capture emerging insights, surface assumptions, and delay solutioning until the problem is better understood. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can search existing notes or maintain an insight log, which may expose or retain personal context if the agent has broad memory or note access. <br>
Mitigation: Before installing it in an agent connected to private notes or memory, confirm that note access and the running insight log are limited to the intended chat or workspace. <br>
