## Description: <br>
A bilingual B2B sales coaching prompt that helps users reason through enterprise software sales scenarios, write sales messages, handle objections, and plan deal next steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lijian417](https://clawhub.ai/user/lijian417) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales employees, founders, and go-to-market teams use this skill to get structured coaching for enterprise software deals, including discovery, POC, procurement, renewal, follow-up emails, objection handling, and stakeholder next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat coaching responses as verified business intelligence or definitive deal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current customer facts, sales policy, and management judgment before using them with prospects or customers. <br>
Risk: Sales scenarios can include confidential customer, pricing, or deal information. <br>
Mitigation: Anonymize customer names, contacts, contract values, and sensitive commercial details before entering them into the agent. <br>
Risk: The prompt includes a paid-upgrade flow after limited free use. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose the free-tier limit and paid-upgrade behavior to users before deployment in a shared or customer-facing environment. <br>
