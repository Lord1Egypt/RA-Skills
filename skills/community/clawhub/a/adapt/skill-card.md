## Description: <br>
Adapt is a short alias for web-adapt / universal-web-adaptation that guides generic adaptation of unfamiliar public websites before site-specific rules are created. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[allens0104](https://clawhub.ai/user/allens0104) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use Adapt to try a generic website adaptation path on unfamiliar public sites, test common controls such as search inputs, buttons, popups, and new tabs, and decide whether a dedicated site profile is needed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the skill on unfamiliar websites may lead an agent into popups, new tabs, login pages, payment flows, or private-data contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Supervise those actions and review the agent's proposed steps before allowing sensitive navigation or data entry. <br>
