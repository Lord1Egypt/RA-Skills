### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to search for plumbers, check service availability, and request a booking through Lokuli's MCP server. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can share contact information and booking details with an external service. <br>
Mitigation: Use only when the user is comfortable sending those details to Lokuli, and confirm the customer name, email, phone number, service, provider, and time slot before any booking request. <br>
Risk: The skill can create a real appointment without clear consent or confirmation instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before externally searching for providers or creating an appointment. <br>
