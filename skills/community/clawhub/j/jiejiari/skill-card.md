## Description: <br>
Look up official Mainland China public holiday schedules and make-up workdays from bundled annual reference data for 2026, including holiday periods, adjusted workdays, and date status checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer questions about Mainland China 2026 statutory holiday schedules, make-up workdays, and whether a specific date is a holiday, adjusted workday, weekend, or normal workday. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on general holiday or date-related prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording such as Mainland China statutory holidays, adjusted rest days, make-up workdays, State Council notices, or a specific year. <br>
Risk: Answers outside the bundled 2026 Mainland China holiday data could be incomplete or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Limit responses to the included 2026 reference data and state when a requested year or regional arrangement is not included. <br>
