## Description: <br>
Thesis-driven equity and options trading analyst for evaluating trade ideas, options pricing, earnings catalyst plays, losing positions, emotional trading patterns, and pre-trade checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[browndroid](https://clawhub.ai/user/browndroid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Traders and investing-focused users use this skill to structure equity and options trade decisions before entry, compare implied and historical volatility, plan exits, and check for emotional or FOMO-driven behavior. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides financial-analysis structure that could be mistaken for personalized, licensed investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support only, verify assumptions independently, and consult qualified financial professionals when appropriate. <br>
Risk: Trade decisions may rely on stale, incorrect, or incomplete market data supplied by the user or external tools. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm prices, volatility, earnings dates, and risk limits with current trusted sources before placing any trade. <br>
Risk: The linked spreadsheet template may expose sensitive financial information if users enter private data into an external document. <br>
Mitigation: Make a private copy, avoid entering sensitive account details, and review sharing permissions before use. <br>
