## Description: <br>
Book pest-control services through Lokuli MCP. Use when user needs to find and book pest-control. Triggers on requests like "book a pest-control", "find pest-control near me", or any pest-control service request. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[edwardrodriguez703-design](https://clawhub.ai/user/edwardrodriguez703-design) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to search for pest-control providers through Lokuli, check availability, and create a booking after confirming service details and contact information sharing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The booking flow may send customer contact information to Lokuli and the selected service provider. <br>
Mitigation: Before creating a booking, confirm the provider, service, date, time, price or cancellation terms, and user consent to share contact information. <br>
