## Description: <br>
Monitors China refined oil price adjustment windows, supports current price lookups, and helps remind users before expected price changes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tengjunqing](https://clawhub.ai/user/tengjunqing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to check China refined oil price changes, identify upcoming adjustment windows, and prepare reminder-style summaries before expected changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recurring monitoring can trigger repeated public web searches and may record user interest in oil prices. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm user consent before enabling scheduled monitoring or writing any related MEMORY.md note. <br>
Risk: Oil price movement estimates may be predictions rather than confirmed changes and can vary by region. <br>
Mitigation: Label predicted values clearly, prefer official or reputable sources, and state that regional pump prices may differ. <br>
