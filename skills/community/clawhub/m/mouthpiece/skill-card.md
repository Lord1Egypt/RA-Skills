## Use Case: <br>
Employees use this Chinese-language skill to frame workplace concerns, analyze interpersonal dynamics, draft tactful or firm replies, and understand risks before responding to colleagues or managers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personalization may ask for workplace context and financial-status details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only broad context needed for advice, skip financial details unless they are useful, and update or reset the profile when stored context becomes stale or too personal. <br>
Risk: Firm or assertive reply suggestions can affect workplace relationships or escalation paths. <br>
Mitigation: Review the tone before sending, prefer softer templates when relationship risk is high, and seek appropriate workplace, HR, or legal guidance for serious disputes. <br>
