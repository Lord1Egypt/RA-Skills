## Description: <br>
AI job advisor that helps analyze roles, tailor and generate resumes, prepare for interviews, draft HR follow-ups, negotiate compensation, and track job-search progress. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[iamzhukai-png](https://clawhub.ai/user/iamzhukai-png) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers use this skill to decode job descriptions, adapt resumes for target roles, prepare interview and HR follow-up messaging, compare offers, and maintain a job-search progress record. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can store sensitive resume, salary, employer, screenshot, and job-progress details in local memory files. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in a workspace suitable for job-search data, and review generated memory files before keeping or sharing them. <br>
Risk: The skill can reuse prior local job-search records without clear per-session consent. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm which historical records may be read or updated before relying on persistent job-search memory. <br>
