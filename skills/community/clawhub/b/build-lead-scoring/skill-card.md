## Description: <br>
Create a comprehensive lead scoring model with separate Fit and Engagement scores using HubSpot's new Lead Scoring tool. Replaces the deprecated HubSpot Score property. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operations and revenue operations teams use this skill to configure HubSpot Fit and Engagement lead scoring, including score criteria, thresholds, recalculation checks, and verification steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Turning on HubSpot scores can recalculate and change prioritization for many contacts. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with authorization, review existing scores first, and confirm criteria and thresholds with the business owner before enabling changes. <br>
Risk: Incorrect scoring criteria or thresholds can misprioritize sales and marketing follow-up. <br>
Mitigation: Validate the top, bottom, and sample contacts after recalculation, then tune point values against observed conversion data. <br>
