## Description: <br>
Applies the EmmeStudio brand identity to visual and text outputs such as documents, presentations, quotes, letters, one-pagers, reports, and other formatted material. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[silvioandreoli](https://clawhub.ai/user/silvioandreoli) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and agents producing EmmeStudio-facing materials use this skill to keep documents, presentations, quotes, letters, reports, and other formal outputs aligned with the EmmeStudio brand system. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may apply EmmeStudio branding broadly, including to outputs that do not actually need formal brand treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the task is for an EmmeStudio-facing or formal internal output before applying the full brand workflow. <br>
Risk: The skill may direct the agent to fetch templates or fonts from Google Drive. <br>
Mitigation: Before allowing Drive access, confirm that the Google Drive account and folder are legitimate and that the current task needs those official files. <br>
