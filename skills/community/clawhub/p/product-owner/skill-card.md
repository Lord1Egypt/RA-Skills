## Description: <br>
Manage backlogs, write user stories, define acceptance criteria, and maximize product value. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product owners, product managers, and delivery teams use this skill to manage product backlogs, write INVEST-style user stories, define acceptance criteria, prioritize sprint work, and preserve product context in local notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Backlog, roadmap, sprint, stakeholder, and priority notes may contain sensitive product information in local files. <br>
Mitigation: Use opt-in-only assistance for sensitive products and periodically review or clear the local memory files under ~/product-owner/. <br>
Risk: Product-owner guidance can affect priorities, acceptance criteria, and sprint scope. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated backlog changes and prioritization recommendations with the responsible product and delivery stakeholders before treating them as commitments. <br>
