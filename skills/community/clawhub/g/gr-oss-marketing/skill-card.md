## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outreach and posting recommendations can conflict with platform rules or audience language expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outreach and posting user-directed, review each platform's rules, and adapt recommendations to the audience's preferred language. <br>
Risk: External reference material fetched during use may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific launch. <br>
Mitigation: Review any fetched GitHub reference material before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Growth guidance could be misused for artificial engagement or low-quality promotion. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid fake star growth and drive organic launch activity through useful documentation, demos, community participation, and relevant content. <br>
