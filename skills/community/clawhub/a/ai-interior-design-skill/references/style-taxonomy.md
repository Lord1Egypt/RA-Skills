# Interior Style Taxonomy

Use this reference when you need concise style vocabulary for room redesign prompts.

## Calm modern

- Japandi: oak, linen, warm white, low furniture, handmade ceramics, soft indirect light.
- Scandinavian: pale woods, white walls, functional storage, cozy textiles, daylight.
- Minimal modern: hidden storage, clean silhouettes, neutral palette, negative space.

## Warm character

- Organic modern: stone, plaster, walnut, boucle, curved furniture, earth tones.
- Mediterranean: limewash walls, terracotta, arched forms, woven textures, sun-washed colors.
- Farmhouse modern: shaker cabinets, warm wood, matte black accents, practical storage.

## Rich and dramatic

- Mid-century modern: walnut, tapered legs, geometric art, warm leather, vintage lamps.
- Art deco: brass, velvet, curved shapes, deep jewel tones, symmetrical styling.
- Moody contemporary: charcoal, walnut, layered lighting, large art, textured rugs.

## Prompt details that improve realism

- Mention room scale and camera angle.
- Specify natural and artificial lighting.
- Add material words: linen, oak, travertine, plaster, wool, walnut, rattan.
- Avoid too many competing styles in one prompt.
