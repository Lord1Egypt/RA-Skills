## Description: <br>
E-Commerce After-Sales CS is a prompt-only drafting skill that helps customer service staff generate brand-aligned replies for returns, exchanges, logistics exceptions, and compensation negotiations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer support agents use this skill to draft consistent after-sales responses for return or exchange requests, logistics delays or damage, lost packages, and customer compensation discussions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated replies may include incorrect order facts, refund limits, compensation authority, or timing commitments if the input context is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify order details, brand policy limits, compensation authority, and promised timelines before sending a generated response to a customer. <br>
Risk: The artifact prohibits absolute guarantees, but one example uses the wording "一定", which could lead to over-promising if copied. <br>
Mitigation: Revise generated or example-derived replies to remove absolute guarantee language and keep commitments within confirmed policy limits. <br>
