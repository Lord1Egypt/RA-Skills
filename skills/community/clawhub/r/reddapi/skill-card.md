## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Queries and API credentials are sent to reddapi.dev, a third-party provider. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated API key and avoid sending secrets, regulated personal data, customer data, or confidential strategy in queries. <br>
Risk: Lead-generation outputs may be used for outreach or profiling. <br>
Mitigation: Review lead outputs before use and apply appropriate privacy, consent, and outreach policies. <br>
Risk: Separately downloaded CLI scripts could execute local commands. <br>
Mitigation: Review any downloaded CLI script before running it. <br>
