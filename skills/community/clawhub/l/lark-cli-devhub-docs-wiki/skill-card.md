## Use Case: <br>
Developers and teams use this skill to structure Lark or Feishu Docs and Wiki spaces as a development knowledge hub, with Base records acting as the index for long-form project pages, design notes, runbooks, bug retrospectives, and related artifacts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes user-run Lark or Feishu CLI examples that can create or organize Docs and Wiki content with the permissions of the configured account. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the configured lark-cli or feishu-cli setup is trusted and that the Lark or Feishu account has appropriate permissions before running commands. <br>
