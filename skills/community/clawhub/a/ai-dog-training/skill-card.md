This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External dog owners and trainers use this skill to ask for guidance on puppy care, obedience commands, leash manners, behavior issues, canine sports, scent work, and professional training paths. It can also help users discuss walking or training videos while considering privacy before sharing footage. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded pet-training videos can expose identifying details such as faces, children, addresses, license plates, home interiors, or documents. <br>
Mitigation: Review footage before uploading and blur or avoid sharing sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Complex aggression, separation anxiety, injury, pain, or safety-critical behavior can be inappropriate for generic training guidance alone. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as guidance and consult a qualified trainer, veterinarian, or veterinary behavior specialist for high-risk behavior or medical concerns. <br>
