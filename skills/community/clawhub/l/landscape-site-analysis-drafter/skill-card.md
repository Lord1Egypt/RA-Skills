## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a landscape architect, RLA, site designer, or planning consultant needs to draft a site analysis report before beginning design development. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Landscape architects, site designers, planning consultants, and landscape architecture interns use this skill to turn site data and field observations into a draft pre-design site analysis report for registered landscape architect review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the generated report as permit-ready, client-ready, or final professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the output labeled as a draft and require registered landscape architect review, field verification, and AHJ confirmation before presentation or submission. <br>
Risk: Wetlands, geotechnical, accessibility, floodplain, zoning, or easement issues may be misstated if missing data is assumed. <br>
Mitigation: Flag data gaps explicitly and direct users to licensed specialists or current authority records before relying on those findings. <br>
