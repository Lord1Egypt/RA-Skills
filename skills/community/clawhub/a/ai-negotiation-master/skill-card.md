## Description: <br>
Ai Negotiation Master provides negotiation strategy guidance for business, salary, procurement, real-estate, and dispute-resolution scenarios using concepts such as principled negotiation, BATNA analysis, anchoring, and concession planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, job seekers, procurement and sales staff, founders, and everyday consumers use this skill to prepare negotiation strategies, evaluate alternatives and bargaining zones, plan offers and concessions, and draft scenario-specific talking points. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Negotiation guidance may influence employment, commercial, real-estate, or dispute decisions where incomplete facts or local requirements can change the right strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as planning support, verify facts and applicable rules, and have qualified reviewers check final terms before making commitments. <br>
Risk: If the skill is used in a workflow that touches privileged operational systems, mistaken targets or unreviewed writes could create high-impact changes. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit targets, review previews or dry runs, confirm commands before writes, and use accounts with the minimum access needed. <br>
