## Description: <br>
Scans Freelancer.com for new projects matching a user's skills, drafts personalized bid proposals, and tracks bidding history. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[onlyloveher](https://clawhub.ai/user/onlyloveher) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers and agencies use this skill to find relevant Freelancer.com projects, compare opportunities, draft bid proposals, and maintain a workspace bid log. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated bid proposals or pricing suggestions could be inaccurate or misaligned with the user's intentions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep proposals draft-first, review every proposal and bid price, and require explicit confirmation before any submission. <br>
Risk: Bid-history tracking can create or update bids.md in the workspace with project and bidding details. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the log should be created or updated, and review entries before saving them. <br>
