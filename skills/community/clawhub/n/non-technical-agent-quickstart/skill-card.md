## Description: <br>
Helps non-technical founders launch AI agents in 30 minutes without coding by using plain English and ready-made workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flynndavid](https://clawhub.ai/user/flynndavid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Non-technical founders use this skill to set up a first AI agent with plain-English job descriptions, prompts, and no-code workflow patterns. It focuses on email assistance, task management, weekly summaries, and prompt-library habits. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential, regulated, customer, employee, financial, legal, credential, or trade-secret material into AI tools while following the workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive details before use, confirm approval for regulated or confidential data, and check the AI provider's retention and training settings. <br>
