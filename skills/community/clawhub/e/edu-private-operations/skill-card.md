## Description: <br>
Provides private-domain operations guidance for education and training institutions, including enrollment strategy, community operations, parent communication, content planning, activity planning, and data analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Education and training institution operators, enrollment teams, community managers, and course consultants use this skill to produce localized private-domain operating plans, parent-facing scripts, social content calendars, activity plans, SOPs, and metric-based recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Enrollment, marketing, and parent-communication guidance could be misapplied in ways that create false claims, excessive outreach, or local education-policy compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans and scripts against local education advertising rules, platform policies, and the institution's compliance standards before use. <br>
Risk: Operational analysis may involve parent, student, or lead data. <br>
Mitigation: Use aggregated or minimized data where possible, avoid entering sensitive personal information, and keep any local files or API tokens scoped to the intended worktree. <br>
Risk: Benchmarks and recommendations are general planning aids and may not match a specific region, course type, or institution maturity. <br>
Mitigation: Validate benchmark comparisons and suggested actions with current institution data and local market knowledge before making business decisions. <br>
