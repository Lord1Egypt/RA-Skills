## Description: <br>
Ghosted helps users dealing with ghosting, being left on read, or a once-warm conversation going cold decide whether to follow up, let go, or reset the dynamic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[imwyvern](https://clawhub.ai/user/imwyvern) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this conversational coaching skill to analyze silence patterns, last-message tone, and relationship or professional context after someone stops replying. It provides an honest assessment, evidence-based interpretation, a recommended next move, and a dignified follow-up message when appropriate. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The included publish.sh script can push Git changes and publish under the wrong ReplyHer skill identity. <br>
Mitigation: Do not run publish.sh unless that release path is intended; fix or remove the script before treating the package as clean. <br>
Risk: Conversational coaching about being ignored can encourage repeated contact if boundaries are not respected. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill's stated limit of one follow-up, avoid harassment, and reframe situations that may involve abuse or control. <br>
