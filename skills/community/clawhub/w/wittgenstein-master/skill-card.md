## Description: <br>
Wittgenstein Master provides Chinese-language, Wittgenstein-inspired language analysis to identify pseudo-problems, clarify language games, and dissolve conceptual confusion. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ruiyongwang](https://clawhub.ai/user/ruiyongwang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineering consultants, and other users can ask the skill to analyze contract language, cross-disciplinary communication, project-management disputes, or philosophical questions by examining how key terms are used in context. It is intended for language clarification and conceptual analysis rather than quantitative decisions, legal advice, or ethical pronouncements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The trigger phrase may be easy to invoke accidentally. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit invocation and review whether the skill was intentionally activated before relying on its response. <br>
Risk: The skill may respond in Chinese even when that is not the user preferred language. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the desired response language explicitly when invoking the skill. <br>
Risk: The skill focuses on philosophical language analysis and may not provide actionable legal, ethical, or quantitative decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for clarification and problem framing, then consult qualified domain experts or quantitative tools for decisions. <br>
