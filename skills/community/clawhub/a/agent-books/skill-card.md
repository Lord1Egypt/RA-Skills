## Description: <br>
AgentBooks helps AI agents track LLM inference costs, record confirmed income, manage multi-provider crypto wallets, and compute a Financial Health Score. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[NeilJo-GY](https://clawhub.ai/user/NeilJo-GY) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External developers and agent operators use AgentBooks to route an agent's behavior based on financial health, record costs and confirmed income, inspect ledgers and reports, and configure wallet-backed provider funding. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill installs and invokes an external financial and wallet-management CLI at runtime. <br>
Mitigation: Review the agentbooks package and source before installation, then evaluate it first in a sandboxed environment. <br>
Risk: Financial state and wallet-related configuration may affect real funds or sensitive agent accounting data. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated AGENTBOOKS_DATA_PATH and test or throwaway wallets before connecting funded providers or sensitive credentials. <br>
