# FPL Rules Reference

## Squad Structure

- **Budget**: £100.0m initial
- **Squad size**: 15 players
  - 2 Goalkeepers (GKP)
  - 5 Defenders (DEF)
  - 5 Midfielders (MID)
  - 3 Forwards (FWD)
- **Starting XI**: Pick 11 each GW, must include at least 1 GKP, 3 DEF, 2 MID, 1 FWD
- **Valid formations**: 4-4-2, 4-3-3, 3-4-3, 3-5-2, 4-5-1, 5-4-1, 5-3-2, 5-2-3
- **Club limit**: Maximum 3 players from any single club

## Scoring System

### Playing Time
- Appear (1-59 min): 1 pt
- Appear (60+ min): 2 pts
- Did not play: 0 pts

### Goals
- Goal by GKP: 6 pts
- Goal by DEF: 6 pts
- Goal by MID: 5 pts
- Goal by FWD: 4 pts

### Assists
- Any position: 3 pts

### Clean Sheets (played 60+ min)
- GKP: 4 pts
- DEF: 4 pts
- MID: 1 pt
- FWD: 0 pts

### Goals Conceded (per 2 goals, GKP/DEF only, played 60+ min)
- GKP: -1 pt
- DEF: -1 pt

### Cards
- Yellow card: -1 pt
- Red card: -3 pts (plus loss of any clean sheet bonus for that match)

### Other
- Own goal: -2 pts
- Penalty miss: -2 pts
- Penalty save (GKP): 5 pts
- Save per 3 shots (GKP): 1 pt
- Bonus points: 1-3 pts awarded to top performers in each match (via BPS)

## Transfers

- **Free transfers**: 1 per GW (can bank up to a maximum of 2, i.e., don't use 1 and have 2 next GW)
- **Extra transfers**: Each beyond free allocation costs -4 pts
- **Wildcard**: Unlimited free transfers for that GW (1 in GW1-19, 1 in GW20-38)
- **Deadline**: Usually 90 minutes before first kickoff of the GW

## Player Price Changes

- Prices fluctuate based on transfer activity (net in vs out)
- Selling price = purchase price + 50% of any profit (rounded down)
- Minimum selling price = purchase price (you never lose money on sale below buy price)
- Example: Bought at £7.0m, now £7.6m → profit £0.6m → 50% = £0.3m → sell at £7.3m

## Chips

| Chip | Effect | Available |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Wildcard | Unlimited free transfers for 1 GW | 1 per half-season (GW1-19, GW20-38) |
| Free Hit | Unlimited transfers for 1 GW, squad reverts next GW | 1 per season (GW2-38) |
| Bench Boost | All 15 players score points | 1 per season (GW1-19) |
| Triple Captain | Captain scores 3x instead of 2x | 1 per season (GW1-19) |

Note: Only 1 chip can be played per GW. Wildcard cannot be combined with any other chip.

## Captain & Vice-Captain

- Captain: Points doubled (2x)
- Vice-captain: Becomes captain if captain doesn't play at all (0 minutes)
- If both captain and vice-captain don't play, no captain bonus applies

## Substitutes (Auto-Sub)

- Bench players replace starting players who play 0 minutes
- Substitution order follows bench priority (1st, 2nd, 3rd sub)
- Formation constraints must still be maintained after substitution
- A sub only comes on if it doesn't break the minimum formation rules

## Bonus Points System (BPS)

Players earn BPS during matches based on actions. The top 3 BPS earners in each match get bonus FPL points:
- 1st: 3 pts
- 2nd: 2 pts
- 3rd: 1 pt
- Ties are resolved by splitting (e.g., two players tied 1st both get 2.5 → rounds to 3 and 2)

## Cup

- Qualification: Top ranked managers after GW16
- Format: Head-to-head knockout from GW17 onwards
- Tiebreaker: Overall GW score → overall rank
