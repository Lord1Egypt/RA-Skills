## Description: <br>
Generates a traceable MATLAB reproduction package from an academic paper by extracting algorithms, resolving cited operations when needed, creating simulation code, running main scripts when MATLAB is available, and comparing outputs with paper results. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and engineers use this skill to turn a paper file, URL, DOI, arXiv link, or uploaded PDF into MATLAB code, Chinese reproduction documentation, parameter audits, pseudocode, result plots, and similarity reports. <br>
