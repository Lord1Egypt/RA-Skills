## Use Case: <br>
External users and productivity practitioners use this skill to receive conversational coaching in the style of Ye Wubin, focused on time management, energy state assessment, goals, habits, and practical next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill roleplays a named person and may present inferred coaching advice as a persona response. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as roleplay, verify factual claims about credentials or courses, and ask the assistant to exit the role when persona behavior is not intended. <br>
Risk: Productivity and energy-management advice may not fit medical, financial, or unrelated technical decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for personal effectiveness coaching only and seek qualified professional advice for medical, investment, or specialized technical questions. <br>
