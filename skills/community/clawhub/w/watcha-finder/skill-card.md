## Description: <br>
Finds, evaluates, and recommends AI products using watcha.cn product data, user reviews, and web cross-references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Charipoter](https://clawhub.ai/user/Charipoter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to discover, compare, and recommend AI tools for specific needs, including coding, video creation, productivity, and China-accessible products. It helps rank options by fit and evidence quality rather than popularity alone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms and product research may be sent to watcha.cn and general web search providers. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for non-sensitive AI product research and avoid submitting confidential product plans, customer data, or private requirements. <br>
Risk: Recommendations may reflect watcha.cn coverage gaps, Chinese-language query expansion, popularity signals, and community-review bias. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check shortlisted products with official sites and external sources, and treat low-review scores as weak evidence rather than definitive quality signals. <br>
