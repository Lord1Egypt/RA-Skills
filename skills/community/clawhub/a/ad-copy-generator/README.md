## The Problem

Writing ad copy is expensive and slow:
- Freelance copywriter: $75–$300 per ad, 2–5 day turnaround
- Copy.ai or Jasper: $49–$82/month subscription
- ChatGPT (generic): 45 minutes of prompting to get something usable

Most paid tools give you filler. This skill gives you copy built on direct response principles — character limits enforced, platform specs followed, psychological triggers applied.
