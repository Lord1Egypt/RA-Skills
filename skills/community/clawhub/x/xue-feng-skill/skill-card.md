## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Chinese gaokao major selection, college application tradeoff analysis, and early career path planning. It structures recommendations around scores, province, subject track, family context, region, and stated priorities, while requiring current data checks for concrete admissions, salary, and employment claims. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for personal and family context to make education and career recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Collect only the details needed for the recommendation, avoid unnecessary identifiers, and keep sensitive family context out of shared transcripts when possible. <br>
Risk: The skill uses gender-based rules in consequential education and career guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat gender as optional context, present non-stereotyped alternatives, and avoid excluding majors or careers solely on gender. <br>
Risk: Admissions, salary, employment, policy, and industry-cycle claims may be outdated or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Verify concrete claims with official admissions sources, trusted labor-market data, and current policy information before acting on the advice. <br>
