## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Biotechnology guidance can be misapplied to unsafe wet-lab work, dangerous pathogens, select agents, or procedures requiring institutional oversight. <br>
Mitigation: The skill limits dangerous protocol assistance, requires validation by qualified personnel, and reminds users to follow local regulations, institutional review, biosafety training, and appropriate containment. <br>
Risk: Optional memory can preserve confidential research, proprietary project details, or sensitive academic or professional context. <br>
Mitigation: Use memory only when the user wants persistent context and avoid storing confidential or sensitive details unless the user intentionally wants them reused later. <br>
Risk: Biotechnology information changes quickly and responses may reflect outdated or incomplete scientific knowledge. <br>
Mitigation: The skill tells the agent to distinguish established science from frontier work, state knowledge boundaries, verify names and conditions, and suggest literature or database searches for recent information. <br>
