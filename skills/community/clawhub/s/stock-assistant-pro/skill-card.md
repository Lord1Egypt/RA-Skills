## Description: <br>
Stock Assistant Pro helps analyze stocks using five-day moving average, MACD, and fundamental-screening signals, with trading signals, reports, and strategy guidance for A-share investors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to review stock watchlists, evaluate technical and fundamental signals, generate stock reports, and receive informational buy, hold, sell, or watch guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Stock opinions, trading signals, and report conclusions may be inaccurate, stale, or unsuitable for a user's risk tolerance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as informational, verify market data and fundamentals independently, and make decisions based on personal risk limits. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on generated buy, sell, or watch guidance for real trades. <br>
Mitigation: Do not make trades solely based on this skill's output; review signals against independent sources and professional advice where appropriate. <br>
Risk: Requests involving brokerage access or credentials would increase financial-account exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide brokerage credentials or authorize account actions through the skill. <br>
