## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: FinOps recommendations may involve production cloud changes such as deleting, stopping, resizing, auto-terminating, blocking deployments, or changing committed spend. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval, ownership checks, dependency review, rollback planning, and approved change windows before applying those actions. <br>
Risk: Broad cloud account permissions could turn advisory guidance into high-impact operational changes. <br>
Mitigation: Install as a reference skill and prefer read-only billing access for analysis unless a reviewed workflow explicitly requires stronger permissions. <br>
Risk: Cloud billing and allocation data may expose sensitive financial or ownership information. <br>
Mitigation: Limit access to the minimum billing and cost-management data needed for the analysis and follow the organization's data handling policies. <br>
