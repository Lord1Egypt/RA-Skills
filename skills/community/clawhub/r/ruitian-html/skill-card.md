## Description: <br>
Generates enterprise-style HTML documents with a Ruitian purple gradient theme, card layouts, tables, flow diagrams, and responsive Bootstrap styling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lcviva](https://clawhub.ai/user/lcviva) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and business teams use this skill to turn outlines for project delivery reports, technical proposals, training materials, product documents, meeting notes, and work summaries into polished HTML documents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad document-generation requests. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit prompts when requesting or avoiding this HTML template behavior. <br>
Risk: Generated HTML may depend on external Bootstrap CDN resources. <br>
Mitigation: Review dependency use and host assets locally when offline operation or dependency control is required. <br>
Risk: Polished generated documents can make draft or unverified business content look final. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual content, links, and stakeholder-facing claims before distribution. <br>
