### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad business-context prompts may expose customer data, contracts, credentials, pricing strategy, regulated information, or internal plans. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the minimum context needed and redact sensitive business, customer, contract, credential, pricing, regulatory, and internal planning details. <br>
Risk: Sales, compliance, financial, legal, or strategic recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific business situation. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified person review recommendations before using them for legal, compliance, financial, or strategic decisions. <br>
