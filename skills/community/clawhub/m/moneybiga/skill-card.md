## Description: <br>
MoneybigA helps agents analyze A-share, Hong Kong, and U.S. stocks using technical, fundamental, quantitative-factor, and smart-money frameworks, then produce an interactive HTML-style analysis dashboard with scores, signals, risk conditions, stop-loss references, and target-price references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, active traders, researchers, and value investors use this skill to ask an agent for stock, index, financial-report, valuation, sector, and technical-analysis workflows across A-share, Hong Kong, and U.S. markets. The skill is intended for informational analysis and education, not trade execution or personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce buy/sell signals, target prices, and stop-loss levels that may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational analysis only and independently verify market data, assumptions, and conclusions before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Public web market data may be delayed, incomplete, or unavailable for some securities. <br>
Mitigation: Check the report's stated data availability and confirm material figures against trusted market and issuer sources. <br>
