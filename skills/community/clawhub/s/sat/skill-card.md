## Description: <br>
Prepare for the SAT with adaptive practice, score prediction, weak area targeting, and college admissions planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External students, parents, guardians, tutors, and teachers use this skill to plan SAT preparation, target weak areas, track progress, estimate scores, and align prep with college admissions goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local SAT study notes may retain scores, mistakes, target colleges, and study feedback under ~/sat/. <br>
Mitigation: Store only necessary study information, avoid adding sensitive personal details, and review or delete ~/sat/ when the notes are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: SAT strategy, score targets, college policies, and test dates can change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's plans as study guidance and confirm current College Board and college-specific policies before registration or score submission decisions. <br>
