## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: ADB examples may affect the wrong connected device or emulator if run without checking the target. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target device or emulator and APK path before running adb commands. <br>
Risk: Logcat output can expose app or device details if shared broadly. <br>
Mitigation: Review and redact logcat output before sharing it outside the development context. <br>
