## Description: <br>
Ai Ethology is a Chinese-language educational guide that helps agents explain core animal behavior topics, including instinct, learning, communication, social behavior, migration, and cognition. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, educators, developers, and animal-behavior enthusiasts use this skill to ask an agent for concise explanations of ethology concepts and examples across animal communication, social behavior, migration, cognition, and AI-inspired biomimicry. <br>
