## Description: <br>
Reddit Demand Sniffer & MVP Engine helps founders and product builders mine Reddit communities for demand signals, design a 7-day MVP, draft validation posts, and score commercial viability. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders, indie hackers, SaaS builders, cross-border entrepreneurs, and AI tool builders use this skill to turn Reddit market research into prioritized pain points, MVP plans, validation post drafts, and Go/No-Go business scoring. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated Reddit validation posts could conflict with subreddit rules or appear manipulative if posted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and rewrite every post before use, disclose that you are building or validating a product, follow each subreddit's rules, and avoid account-warming or engagement tactics. <br>
Risk: Validation workflows may collect emails, DMs, manuscripts, analytics, or session recordings from potential users. <br>
Mitigation: Add clear privacy terms before collecting user information and limit collection to data needed for validation. <br>
