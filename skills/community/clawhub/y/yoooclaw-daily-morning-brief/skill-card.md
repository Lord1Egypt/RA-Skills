## Description: <br>
Creates a concise morning brief from recent notifications, highlighting key updates, today's tasks, missed items, follow-ups, decisions, and risks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and individual users use this skill to turn recent notification streams into a short morning action brief. It prioritizes important people, topics, apps, groups, deadlines, follow-ups, and decisions so the user can start the day with a focused task list. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad notification searches may expose sensitive personal, work, family, calendar, or email content. <br>
Mitigation: Install only where this access is acceptable, and use explicit requests with narrow time ranges and clear source expectations. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases can cause recent notification history to be searched and summarized. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid vague prompts when notification history should not be searched, and confirm the intended scope before using the skill. <br>
Risk: The brief may omit, merge, or misclassify tasks, deadlines, decisions, or priorities. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated briefs against source notifications before acting on commitments, deadlines, or decisions. <br>
