## Description: <br>
用人类经典哲学思想解答任何现实困惑。不仅限于个人问题，还能分析国际事件、社会现象、科技伦理、历史争议等任何需要深度思考的话题。调取哲学、文学、政治学、经济学经典智慧进行多元解读。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Chinese-language reflective advice on personal questions, social issues, ethics, politics, economics, history, and career topics using classic philosophical and social-science reference material. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers mental-health, legal, financial, military, emergency, and current-events topics where reflective advice can be mistaken for authoritative guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as educational reflection only, verify facts against authoritative sources, and consult qualified professionals for severe distress, self-harm risk, legal disputes, financial decisions, emergency, or safety-critical situations. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes an off-platform WeChat contact note for custom services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal, financial, legal, medical, or confidential information through off-platform contact channels. <br>
Risk: The skill relies on curated quotation files and may produce incomplete, outdated, mistranslated, or contested interpretations. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited quotations and interpretations before relying on them, especially for controversial public issues or decisions with material consequences. <br>
