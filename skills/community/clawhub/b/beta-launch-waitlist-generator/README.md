## Why This Exists

Most products fail before they launch. Not because the product is bad — because no one's waiting when the doors open. Founders skip the pre-launch phase, then wonder why launch day is quiet.

This skill builds your audience while you're still building the product:
- **Waitlist landing page** converts "curious" to "committed"
- **Countdown campaign** keeps them warm for 3-6 weeks
- **Product Hunt kit** makes launch day a coordinated event, not a prayer
- **Early access nurture** converts free users to paying before their trial ends
