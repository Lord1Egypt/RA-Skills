## Use Case: <br>
Developers and business teams use this skill to plan and generate chatbot assets for customer support, sales assistance, and internal knowledge-base assistants across web, WeChat, Slack, Discord, and API channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Knowledge bases, conversation memory, and logs may contain sensitive customer or employee data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved documents and websites, avoid secrets and sensitive personal data, and define retention rules before production use. <br>
Risk: Multi-channel integrations can expose chatbot behavior through Slack, Discord, WeChat, web embeds, or APIs. <br>
Mitigation: Test each integration with limited permissions and staged channels before broad deployment. <br>
Risk: Broad activation triggers or handoff settings can route users incorrectly or delay human review. <br>
Mitigation: Review triggers, handoff thresholds, and escalation flows against real support scenarios before launch. <br>
