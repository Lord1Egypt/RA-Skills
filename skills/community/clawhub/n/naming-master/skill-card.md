## Description: <br>
Professional Chinese naming assistant for generating and evaluating names using Five Elements, Eight Trigrams, I Ching, tonal patterns, and Chinese literary or cultural references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[supershuhasai](https://clawhub.ai/user/supershuhasai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to request Chinese baby names, evaluate existing names, compare candidate names, or learn traditional Chinese naming theory. It can tailor suggestions around surname, gender, style preferences, birth details when provided, pronunciation, and cultural meaning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bazi or Five Elements analysis may require exact birth date or time, which can be sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Share birth details only when that analysis is desired, and omit or generalize them when a name can be generated from surname, style, sound, and cultural preferences alone. <br>
Risk: Traditional Chinese naming methods can introduce cultural, gendered, or non-neutral assumptions that may not match the user's intent. <br>
Mitigation: State desired naming style and ask the agent to avoid traditional, gendered, or Chinese-cultural defaults when a neutral or different naming approach is preferred. <br>
