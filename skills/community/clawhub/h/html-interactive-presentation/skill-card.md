## Description: <br>
Converts a Markdown article into a click-driven 16:9 interactive HTML presentation with a Vite, React, and TypeScript scaffold plus optional generated images and narrated audio. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, technical writers, educators, and product teams use this skill to turn Markdown articles, technical blogs, documentation, or learning notes into interactive static web presentations with chapter navigation, narration scripts, optional media generation, and deployable build output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential Markdown content may be transformed into prompts or narration text sent to MiniMax or another selected media provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using confidential source material unless the selected external media provider is approved for that content. <br>
Risk: The workflow references external scaffold or synthesis scripts that are not included in the release package. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan external scaffold or synthesis scripts before executing them. <br>
