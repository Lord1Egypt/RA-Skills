## Description: <br>
Cn Smart Reply generates canned Chinese reply suggestions for social and messaging scenarios such as thanks, apologies, confirmations, reminders, Zhihu, and Xiaohongshu. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[freedompixels](https://clawhub.ai/user/freedompixels) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators can use this skill to draft short Chinese replies for common social, messaging, and community-posting situations. The generated text is a starting point and should be reviewed before sending. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated replies are canned suggestions and may be unsuitable for sensitive, personal, or brand-critical conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated text before sending it to another person or publishing it. <br>
Risk: The skill documentation includes promotional links that are not part of the reply-generation behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Treat documentation links as publisher-provided references and verify them separately before relying on them. <br>
