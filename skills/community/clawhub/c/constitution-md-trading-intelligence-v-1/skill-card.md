This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External traders and analysts use this skill to structure manual intraday market briefs, session updates, setup alerts, and risk warnings for XAU/USD, EUR/USD, GBP/JPY, USD/JPY, and BTC/USD. It emphasizes capital preservation, verified market inputs, and WAIT decisions when confidence, structure, or macro conditions are unclear. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading-analysis outputs may be incomplete, stale, or incorrect if market, spread, session, or macro-news inputs are missing or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational, independently verify live market and news data, and follow the artifact's WAIT posture when required inputs or confidence are insufficient. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misused as automated trading advice or connected to execution, messaging, or paid-data systems without separate review. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for manual decision support unless broker access, WhatsApp automation, and paid data permissions are separately reviewed and approved. <br>
