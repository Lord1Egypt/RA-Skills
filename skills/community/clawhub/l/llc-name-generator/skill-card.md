## Description: <br>
Generates LLC name ideas by industry, keyword, and tone, with reminders to verify state availability, domain status, trademark risk, and filing requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs, startup operators, and business consultants use this skill to brainstorm LLC names and get practical next-step guidance before independently checking legal availability, domains, trademarks, and state filing rules. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may present LLC availability, trademark, domain, cost, or state-compliance statements as if they were verified checks. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only for brainstorming and verify directly with the relevant Secretary of State database, a domain registrar, USPTO resources, and qualified legal help before filing, reserving, or buying anything. <br>
