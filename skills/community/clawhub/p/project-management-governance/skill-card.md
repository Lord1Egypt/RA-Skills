## Description: <br>
Use this skill for multi-step project work that requires structured intake, context discovery, scope control, environment awareness, evidence-driven debugging, synchronized documentation, and disciplined handoff. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and project contributors use this skill to manage complex, multi-step work with structured intake, evidence-first execution, scope control, validation, documentation synchronization, and handoff. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project-local learning notes may preserve stale assumptions that influence later work. <br>
Mitigation: Review .learnings notes periodically and promote validated conclusions into the project's formal documentation. <br>
Risk: Governance workflows can broaden inspection beyond what a simple task requires. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the skill to multi-step work and keep evidence gathering scoped to files, documentation, and checks relevant to the requested outcome. <br>
