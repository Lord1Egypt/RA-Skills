## Description: <br>
Guides agents in recommending and implementing tab and accordion components for content organization, SEO visibility, UX, accessibility, and schema alignment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, SEO practitioners, and content teams use this skill to decide when to use tabs or accordions and how to keep expandable content accessible, crawlable, and aligned with page intent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO guidance for hidden or expandable content can depend on current crawler behavior and the site's rendering approach. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations against the site's current accessibility, rendering, and search requirements before deployment. <br>
Risk: Incorrect tab or accordion implementation can hide important content from users or crawlers, especially when content is loaded only after interaction. <br>
Mitigation: Keep important content server-rendered in the initial DOM, make primary ranking content visible by default, and review the final component for accessibility and crawler visibility. <br>
