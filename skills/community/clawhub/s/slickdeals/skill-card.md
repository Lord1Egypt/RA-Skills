## Description: <br>
Search Slickdeals.net for deals, coupons, and promo codes. Use when the user asks for deals, discounts, price comparisons, or to find cheap games, electronics, or other products. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to find current Slickdeals offers, coupon codes, and price comparisons for shopping queries. The skill is intended to filter out expired or unavailable deals before presenting results. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shopping search terms are sent to Slickdeals and may be sent to retailer sites during price or availability checks. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive personal information and review the sites contacted before using the results. <br>
Risk: Deals can expire quickly, vary by region, or require store memberships or coupons. <br>
Mitigation: Verify price and availability with the retailer before purchasing, and disclose coupon or membership requirements in the result. <br>
