## Description: <br>
Helps agents draft, revise, diagnose, structure, review, and deliver Chinese project or grant proposals, including Word .docx deliverables with constraint matrices, fact ledgers, compliance checklists, and red-team review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Proposal writers, researchers, institutional research administrators, and agents use this skill to prepare Chinese grant and project applications for programs such as NSFC, national key R&D plans, Shanghai science and technology programs, and talent projects. It supports guide interpretation, proposal drafting, review-style diagnosis, compliance checklists, and final Word document delivery while preserving applicant review responsibility. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be used with confidential proposal details, unpublished research data, personal information, clinical material, business secrets, or cooperation documents. <br>
Mitigation: Redact confidential details where possible, share only information needed for the proposal task, and keep sensitive materials subject to applicant and institutional controls. <br>
Risk: Annual funding guides, templates, budget rules, eligibility rules, attachment requirements, ethics requirements, deadlines, and submission-system fields may change. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer user-uploaded current official guides and templates, verify changing requirements against authoritative funder or institutional sources, and mark unresolved items as needing confirmation. <br>
Risk: Generated proposal text or Word documents may contain placeholders, user-stated facts that lack evidence, or compliance assumptions that should not be submitted as final commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Maintain a fact ledger and missing-facts list, avoid fabricating facts, and require applicant, finance, ethics/data, intellectual-property, research-office, and partner review before submission. <br>
Risk: The README includes an npx install command, but server-resolved provenance for this release is unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the repository or source for the install command before installation or operational use. <br>
