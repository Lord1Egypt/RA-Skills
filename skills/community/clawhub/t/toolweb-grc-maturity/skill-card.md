## Description: <br>
Conducts Governance, Risk & Compliance maturity assessments and returns maturity levels, capability gaps, and prioritized recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Compliance officers, risk managers, internal audit teams, and organizations running regulatory or governance improvement programs use this skill to benchmark GRC maturity, identify gaps, and plan remediation work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Assessment requests may include sensitive GRC posture, session identifiers, timestamps, or user identifiers submitted to an external API. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the API operator, base URL, authentication method, TLS use, privacy policy, retention period, and access controls before using real compliance data. <br>
Risk: Maturity scores, gap analysis, and recommendations may influence governance, risk, compliance, or audit decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified GRC personnel review outputs before using them for regulatory, audit, or management action. <br>
