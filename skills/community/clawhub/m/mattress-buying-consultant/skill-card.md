## Description: <br>
Guides mattress buyers through sleeping position, body weight, and usage questions to determine the exact firmness, type, and size specs they need in an unbiased, brand-neutral way. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arbazex](https://clawhub.ai/user/arbazex) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External mattress buyers use this skill to run a structured buying consultation that turns sleep position, body weight, health context, room constraints, frame type, and region into mattress specifications and product starting points. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The consultation may ask for body weight, health concerns, allergies, and location. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the level of personal detail needed for shopping guidance and avoid sensitive information you are not comfortable disclosing. <br>
Risk: Product suggestions, prices, availability, trials, warranties, and retailer terms can change. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify product details and retailer terms before making a purchase. <br>
Risk: Comfort and pain-related recommendations are shopping guidance, not medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output to compare mattress specs and consult a qualified professional for medical concerns. <br>
