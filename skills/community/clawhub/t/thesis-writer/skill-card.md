## Description: <br>
Thesis Writer is a compatibility skill for academic thesis writing workflows that directs users to the integrated `paper thesis` entrypoint while preserving the legacy `thesis` command. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jirboy](https://clawhub.ai/user/jirboy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students and academic writers use this skill to route thesis drafting, chapter expansion, consistency revision, advisor feedback, and terminology-unification requests into the integrated paper workflow while preserving legacy thesis commands. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The compatibility layer points users toward a separate paper skill that was not included in this artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan the separate paper skill before using it with private thesis drafts, unpublished research, or sensitive academic material. <br>
Risk: Thesis drafting assistance can introduce unsupported experimental data, citations, or terminology if outputs are accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify data, citations, and terminology against source materials and institutional requirements before using generated thesis content. <br>
