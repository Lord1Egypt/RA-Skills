## Description: <br>
Write a comprehensive promotion strategy based on provided product information, including product selling point extraction, multi-channel promotion planning, goal setting, and expected outcome evaluation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, product, and growth teams use this skill to draft structured product promotion strategies from product, audience, competitor, budget, and launch-timing inputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Promotion plans may include confidential budgets, launch details, competitor analysis, or unreleased product information supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential commercial information unless the agent environment running the skill is approved for that information. <br>
Risk: Generated promotion targets, budget guidance, and channel recommendations may be based on assumptions when the user provides incomplete product or market details. <br>
Mitigation: Review assumptions, metrics, and channel choices before using the strategy for business planning or external execution. <br>
