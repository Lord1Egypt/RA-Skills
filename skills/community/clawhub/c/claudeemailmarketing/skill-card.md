## Description: <br>
Helps agents plan, review, implement, audit, or improve Claude-assisted email marketing work for positioning, segmentation, lifecycle content, and campaign strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, lifecycle owners, and agent operators use this skill to turn product evidence, audience segments, and funnel goals into Claude-assisted positioning briefs, lifecycle copy reviews, prompt packs, experiment plans, and campaign recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Email strategy or copy recommendations could overstate urgency, scarcity, outcomes, or proof. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved source material, state which evidence supports each recommendation, and route risky claims to brand, legal, or product reviewers. <br>
Risk: Guidance could affect live email systems if applied directly to sends, contact imports, DNS, suppression rules, or production automations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep recommendations separate from execution and require explicit approval before high-risk live-system actions. <br>
