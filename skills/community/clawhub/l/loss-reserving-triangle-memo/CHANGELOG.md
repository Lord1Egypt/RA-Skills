## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-25
Initial release. Adds a CAS-aligned P&C reserving workflow that captures scope (line of business, segmentation, evaluation date, currency, reinsurance basis, data inventory), reviews triangle data quality (paid losses, case incurred, reported claim counts, exposures), produces age-to-age factors with averaging policy (simple / volume-weighted / latest-N / excluding outliers), develops paid LDF, incurred LDF, Bornhuetter-Ferguson (on paid and on incurred), Cape Cod, and expected-loss-ratio ultimates per accident year and segment, runs diagnostics (paid-to-incurred ratios, calendar-year diagonals, age-to-age variability, ultimate-loss-ratio progression, IBNR-to-case ratio reasonableness), reconciles indicated to selected ultimates and IBNR, captures tail-factor and trend judgments, builds a sensitivity table, and produces a peer-reviewable DRAFT memo with a judgment log — never a signed Statement of Actuarial Opinion.
