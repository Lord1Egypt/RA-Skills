## Description: <br>
China Beauty Sourcing is a beauty-industry sourcing guide for international buyers that covers China's skincare, color cosmetics, hair care, personal care manufacturing clusters, supply-chain structure, regional specializations, and industry trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
International buyers and sourcing teams use this skill to explore China beauty manufacturing clusters, compare subsectors and supply-chain roles, and get supplier evaluation guidance for beauty and personal-care products. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market figures and sourcing guidance may become stale because the skill relies on bundled reference data rather than live authoritative updates. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important supplier, regulatory, and market decisions against current authoritative sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Supplier examples and regional guidance are informational and do not replace commercial due diligence. <br>
Mitigation: Validate supplier credentials, certifications, samples, contracts, and compliance requirements independently. <br>
