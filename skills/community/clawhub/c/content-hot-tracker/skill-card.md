## Description: <br>
Content Hot Tracker helps content creators monitor trending topics across Weibo, Zhihu, Douyin, Xiaohongshu, and Bilibili, analyze trend timing, and generate content angles and headline ideas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and social media operators use this skill to research current platform trends, identify promising angles, and draft topic and title suggestions for timely posts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trend claims may be stale or inaccurate because the skill relies on web search and public trend data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify live platform rankings, source dates, and public evidence before using recommendations for publishing decisions. <br>
Risk: Generated content angles and headline suggestions can overstate popularity or encourage misleading framing. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against factual accuracy, audience expectations, brand standards, and platform policies before publication. <br>
