## Use Case: <br>
Sales professionals and teams use this skill to analyze customer meetings, sales recordings, stakeholder dynamics, objections, next-step planning, and repeatable coaching checklists. It can also produce concise internal meeting, channel cooperation, and investment-related analysis when the source material matches those scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can read sensitive local customer profiles, channel profiles, meeting notes, recordings, stakeholder details, relationship maps, and sales strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Run it only on specific files the user chooses, confirm that the material is appropriate to analyze, and avoid granting broad access to unrelated local knowledge-base folders. <br>
Risk: Saved reports may include customer details, personal stakeholder profiles, relationship maps, and sales recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated reports before sharing, remove unnecessary personal or confidential details, and store reports only in approved locations. <br>
Risk: The skill may broaden from sales review into internal business, channel, or investment analysis based on keywords. <br>
Mitigation: Keep requests scoped to the intended business purpose and verify that any non-sales analysis is authorized before using or distributing it. <br>
