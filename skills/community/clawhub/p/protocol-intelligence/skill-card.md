## Description: <br>
Produces structured research briefs on AI governance, agent protocols, decentralized AI infrastructure, and emerging technology standards for decision-makers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[filipbl4gojevic](https://clawhub.ai/user/filipbl4gojevic) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Builders, buyers, governance teams, and strategy leaders use this skill to turn a topic in AI governance, agent protocols, or decentralized infrastructure into an actionable brief with technical context, competitive positioning, regulatory signals, strategic implications, and cited sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated briefs may contain outdated or incorrect citations, dates, regulatory claims, or strategic recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited sources, dates, and compliance-sensitive claims before using a brief for legal, investment, business, or operational decisions. <br>
Risk: When browsing is enabled, the skill may rely on external standards, policy, or market sources that change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer primary sources and record the date each source was checked in the brief. <br>
