## Description: <br>
Helps an agent guide drop-spindle yarn making by identifying suitable fibers, calculating twist and yardage, scheduling preparation and spinning sessions, tracking tests, and producing inventory or barter templates while the human performs all physical fiber work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and craft practitioners use this skill to plan and track small-batch yarn production from local wool, flax, cotton, or plant fiber. The agent provides fiber assessment, calculations, schedules, logs, safety reminders, and templates while the user makes the yarn manually. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store local logs, recipes, dashboards, reminders, and notes based on user-provided photos or inventory. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only relevant fiber photos, inventory details, and craft notes; avoid unrelated personal files or sensitive photos. <br>
Risk: Drop-spindle spinning involves repetitive hand, wrist, and shoulder motion. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the skill's break reminders and stop if pain occurs before resuming with an ergonomic review. <br>
