## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Vocal-care guidance may be mistaken for medical or speech-language advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for educational music practice only; for throat pain, persistent hoarseness, suspected vocal injury, breathing issues, or other health symptoms, consult qualified medical or speech-language professionals. <br>
