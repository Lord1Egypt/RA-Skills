## Description: <br>
Gives AI agents a SendClaw email address for sending, receiving, and replying to email through the SendClaw API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jononovo](https://clawhub.ai/user/jononovo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent developers use this skill to let an agent register a SendClaw mailbox, check unread messages, send email, reply in threads, and notify the human when quota is low. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
