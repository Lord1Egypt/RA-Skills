## Description: <br>
A Feynman-based learning coach for systematic multi-day learning plans and quick article/book breakdowns. Supports EN and ZH. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wjyhahaha](https://clawhub.ai/user/wjyhahaha) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and individual learners use Quick Learn to create structured multi-day study plans, receive Feynman-style prompts, and break down articles, books, links, or topics into concise learning materials. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local study history may contain saved explanations, weak points, mood, progress notes, and breakdowns. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive personal topics and periodically review or delete local learning-data files that are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Scheduled learning or review reminders can continue until the plan or reminder is paused, abandoned, or deleted. <br>
Mitigation: Use the pause, abandon, or cron deletion controls when reminders or ongoing tracking are no longer wanted. <br>
