## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bearer tokens could be exposed through logs, prompts, or shared command history. <br>
Mitigation: Store tokens securely, avoid logging them, and replace example placeholders before use. <br>
Risk: Adoption, scheduled care, species creation, and pet release actions can change account or pet state. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit confirmation before state-changing actions, especially releasing or deleting a pet. <br>
