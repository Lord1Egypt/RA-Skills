## Description: <br>
Analyzes Reddit and professional forum discussions for machinery communities, clustering weekly hot topics, recurring user pain points, FridayParts content opportunities, and trend signals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and operations teams use this skill to turn community posts from Reddit and forums into prioritized topics, pain points, and concrete content ideas for downstream YouTube, blog, Facebook, and Instagram workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad Chinese trigger phrases may activate the skill for adjacent analysis tasks or produce guidance outside the intended community-insight workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated reports before using them for research, content planning, or publishing decisions. <br>
Risk: Community discussions about DPF delete, EGR delete, ECU cracking, or safety-system bypasses could lead to noncompliant content ideas if handled carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs focused on cleaning, maintenance, correct diagnosis, and technical verification; do not use the skill to create deletion, bypass, or cracking instructions. <br>
