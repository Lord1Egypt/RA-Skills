## How It Works.

**Step 1:** Tell me your problem (any problem)

**Step 2:** I guide you through 8 questions (one by one, can't skip)

**Step 3:** You leave with a complete action plan

10 minutes. Problem → Clarity → Plan → Action.

---

## The 8 Steps.

1. **Find your real problem** (not the 10 fake ones)
2. **Map everyone involved** (who matters, what they want)
3. **Get facts, not opinions** (what's actually happening)
4. **Set a clear target** (SMART goal, 5 elements)
5. **Measure the gap** (where you are vs where you want to be)
6. **Find interference** (knowledge, skill, motivation, environment)
7. **Build action plan** (who, what, when, where, how)
8. **Review and improve** (what worked, what to change)
