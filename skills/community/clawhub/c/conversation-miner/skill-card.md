## Description: <br>
对话价值提取助手 - 从 AI 对话中自动总结、提取待办/想法/决策，支持持续进化 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, knowledge workers, and other ClawHub users can use this skill to summarize AI conversations, extract todos, ideas, decisions, code snippets, and links, and optionally save or export structured conversation records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can inspect recent conversation history and may process sensitive chat content. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only on conversations intended for summarization, and avoid chats containing secrets, confidential work, legal, medical, financial, or personal information. <br>
Risk: The skill can save summaries, extracted items, feedback, preferences, and exported conversation records. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated summaries and exports before saving or sharing them, and choose storage or export destinations carefully. <br>
Risk: Broad natural-language triggers and default history collection can include more context than expected. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer explicit commands or scoped topics when summarizing, and check the output for unintended conversation details. <br>
