## Description: <br>
Automatically creates periodic progress reports for running OpenClaw tasks, including current status, recent work, overall progress, and next steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zenmejiang-commits](https://clawhub.ai/user/zenmejiang-commits) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill to monitor long-running agent tasks by generating recurring markdown summaries of task status, recent activity, progress, and planned next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Task descriptions and recent log activity may be written to local progress-report files. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in workspaces where file permissions and report retention are appropriate for the sensitivity of the task. <br>
Risk: Old progress reports may retain sensitive task context after the work is complete. <br>
Mitigation: Review and clean up generated report files when they are no longer needed. <br>
