## Description: <br>
Practical skills for cooking and hosting groups of 10-50 people, including recipe scaling, menu planning, timing, equipment, food safety, and potluck coordination. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan and execute group meals for events, community gatherings, family reunions, block parties, regular group meals, and potlucks for roughly 10-50 people. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill gives food-safety and large-group cooking guidance where incorrect temperatures, holding times, or allergen handling could harm guests. <br>
Mitigation: Review food-safety guidance against local regulations and trusted food-safety sources before relying on it for an event. <br>
Risk: The release declares filesystem access even though the security summary says the content does not appear to need it. <br>
Mitigation: Install with minimal permissions where possible or ask the publisher to tighten the declared tool metadata. <br>
