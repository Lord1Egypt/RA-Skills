## Description: <br>
AI tutoring and education powered by CellCog for study guides, exam prep, coding tutorials, language learning, math help, science explanations, practice problems, diagrams, analogies, worked examples, and interactive lessons. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, educators, developers, and other learners use this skill to ask CellCog for explanations, study materials, practice questions, coding tutorials, and language-learning support tailored to their level and learning style. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires a CellCog API key and delegates learning prompts to CellCog. <br>
Mitigation: Use a rotatable API key, install only if you trust CellCog, and rotate or revoke the key if exposure is suspected. <br>
Risk: Prompts may contain private student records, confidential documents, proprietary code, or regulated data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive data unless CellCog's privacy and retention terms satisfy the user's requirements. <br>
Risk: Educational responses may be incorrect, incomplete, or unsuitable for the learner's context. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated explanations, study plans, and answers before relying on them for coursework, exams, or professional decisions. <br>
