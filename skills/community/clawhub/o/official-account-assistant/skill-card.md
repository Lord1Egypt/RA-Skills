## Description: <br>
Official Account Assistant helps draft, humanize, format, illustrate, and prepare WeChat Official Account articles for publication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External content creators and social media operators use this skill to draft WeChat Official Account articles, rewrite AI-generated drafts into a more conversational style, suggest titles and images, and prepare publishing workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Publishing actions could affect a real public account because the skill describes upload, scheduling, publishing, and mass-send workflows without clear safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for drafting or editing only unless explicit permission scopes, preview, confirmation, and manual approval are added before upload, scheduling, publication, or mass sending. <br>
Risk: The AI-humanizing feature may be used to conceal AI assistance or fabricate personal experience. <br>
Mitigation: Use rewriting for readability and style only; do not add false personal claims, and follow disclosure expectations for the publishing context. <br>
Risk: Referenced helper scripts for AI rewriting, auto-publishing, and image generation are described but not present as inspectable artifact files. <br>
Mitigation: Do not run or authorize referenced helper scripts until their contents, permission scopes, and external service behavior are reviewed. <br>
Risk: Generated or recommended images may have unclear copyright or platform-policy status. <br>
Mitigation: Verify image licenses, usage rights, and platform compliance before publication. <br>
