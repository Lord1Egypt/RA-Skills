## Description: <br>
Generate a full week of social media content for any topic, with platform-optimized drafts for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Instagram. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[claudiodrusus](https://clawhub.ai/user/claudiodrusus) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and small teams use this skill to create a week-long social media calendar for a chosen topic and audience. It provides daily platform-specific draft sections, hashtags, posting times, and content-mix guidance for later customization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated social media drafts may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for public posting without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and customize content-calendar.md before publishing, including claims, tone, hashtags, and posting times. <br>
Risk: Running the generator overwrites content-calendar.md in the current directory. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in the intended workspace or rename any existing content-calendar.md before generating a new calendar. <br>
