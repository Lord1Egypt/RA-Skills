## Description: <br>
Strictly review any new skill, browser extension, app, community, course, template, or micro-product before building or launching it. Use when the user wants a traffic-source, trust, shareability, free-MVP, go/no-go, or ecosystem-fit review of a product idea or shipped draft. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, creators, and product builders use this skill to review a product idea or shipped draft for traffic source, trust reason, share trigger, free MVP plan, stop criteria, and ecosystem fit before building, packaging, submitting, launching, or monetizing it. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide command execution or repository edits in unfamiliar projects. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect referenced local scripts and review changes to package scripts, validation files, and release checks before allowing execution. <br>
Risk: Product reviews may tempt users to provide private community data, credentials, payment data, customer exports, or raw identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Use public page copy, aggregate counts, creator-owned notes, explicit opt-in feedback, or synthetic examples instead of sensitive data. <br>
Risk: Launch, growth, monetization, legal, financial, health, or education guidance can be mistaken for guaranteed outcomes or professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat verdicts as decision-support guidance and independently verify claims, compliance boundaries, and launch decisions before publication. <br>
