## Description: <br>
Conducts Freedom-to-Operate patent searches and infringement risk analyses from technical descriptions or images, using Google Patents or Patsnap to produce search queries, relevant patent lists, feature comparison tables, and risk conclusions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chaochaoSZ](https://clawhub.ai/user/chaochaoSZ) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product teams, and IP professionals use this skill to structure FTO patent searches before product launch or technical implementation. It helps decompose a technical solution, search granted and pending patents across selected jurisdictions, compare high-relevance claims, and draft a research report that must be reviewed by qualified counsel. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may disclose confidential product plans, invention details, or images to external patent-search services. <br>
Mitigation: Submit only information the user is authorized to disclose, and avoid confidential or sensitive inputs unless disclosure to the selected service is approved. <br>
Risk: Patsnap access may require user authentication. <br>
Mitigation: Log in only through the official Patsnap site and do not provide raw credentials to the agent. <br>
Risk: FTO reports can be incomplete, inaccurate, or mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated reports as research support and have findings reviewed by a qualified patent attorney before acting on them. <br>
