## Description: <br>
Add a support case follow-up. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wxt-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/wxt-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Support teams and operations staff use this skill to convert a support case comment or escalation note into a concise ticket follow-up for controlled support-note drafting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the workbench skill for a complete support-system integration. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for controlled support-note drafting and review generated ticket comments before adding them to real support records. <br>
Risk: The fixed validation marker is suitable for controlled validation, not live case content. <br>
Mitigation: Do not treat validation-marker output as operational support data outside validation workflows. <br>
