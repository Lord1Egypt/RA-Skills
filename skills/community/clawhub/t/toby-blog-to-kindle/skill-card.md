## Description: <br>
Scrapes blogs and essay sites, compiles them into Kindle-friendly EPUBs with AI-generated cover art, and supports requests to download, compile, or send blog archives for Paul Graham, Kevin Kelly, Derek Sivers, Wait But Why, Astral Codex Ten, and custom sites. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External readers and developers use this skill to collect supported blog posts or essays, generate cover art, compile a Kindle-ready EPUB, and send it to a Kindle address. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can email local files through Mail.app to a Kindle address without a confirmation step. <br>
Mitigation: Require the user to enter and confirm the recipient, attachment, and subject before every send, and restrict sending to EPUBs generated by the skill. <br>
Risk: The bundled Kindle delivery script includes a hardcoded default Kindle address. <br>
Mitigation: Replace the default address before use and require an explicit Kindle recipient for each delivery. <br>
Risk: Cover generation sends prompts to an external image provider using SKILLBOSS_API_KEY. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide the API key when external image generation is acceptable, and avoid sensitive content in cover prompts. <br>
Risk: The Mail.app AppleScript flow needs safer escaping for user-controlled values. <br>
Mitigation: Escape AppleScript fields or use a safer mail-sending flow before relying on automated delivery. <br>
