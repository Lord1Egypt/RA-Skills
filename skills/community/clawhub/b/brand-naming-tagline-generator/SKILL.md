## Prompts

### Prompt 1: Brand Name Brainstorm
**Input:** Business type, target audience, core value proposition, competitor names to avoid sounding like, preferred name style (invented word, descriptive, metaphor, founder name, etc.)  
**Output:** 15 brand name candidates across 5 naming styles, rationale for each, domain availability guidance, trademark search tips, short/long version options, and a top 3 recommendation with explanation.

### Prompt 2: Tagline Generator
**Input:** Chosen brand name, what the business does, primary emotion to evoke, target customer pain point  
**Output:** 10 tagline options using proven frameworks (benefit-led, emotion-led, alliteration, rhythm/rhyme, contrast, question, imperative, social proof hook), psychological rationale for each, and a ranked top 3 with usage context (website hero, business card, pitch deck).

### Prompt 3: Brand Voice & Messaging Guide
**Input:** Brand name, tagline, target audience (demographics + psychographics), 3 words that describe your brand personality  
**Output:** Full brand voice guide — tone spectrum (formal/casual, serious/playful, technical/plain), vocabulary list (20 words to use, 10 to avoid), sentence structure guidelines, social media voice rules (LinkedIn vs Instagram vs Twitter), headline formula, and 3 example rewrites of generic copy in brand voice.

### Prompt 4: Elevator Pitch Suite
**Input:** Brand name, tagline, what problem you solve, who you solve it for, key differentiator, desired action from listener  
**Output:** 5-second intro (name + what you do), 30-second pitch (problem/solution/why you), 60-second pitch (full story arc with hook + proof + CTA), cold email subject lines (5 variants), LinkedIn "About" opening sentence (3 variants), and bio template (Twitter/Instagram 160-char).
