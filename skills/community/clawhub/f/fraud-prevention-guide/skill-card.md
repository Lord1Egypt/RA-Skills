## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fraud controls may affect customer data handling, payment compliance, privacy notices, or automated order decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the guidance as a planning reference and confirm processor terms, PCI obligations, privacy requirements, applicable law, and internal approvals before applying it. <br>
Risk: Aggressive screening thresholds can block legitimate customers or create misleading fraud signals. <br>
Mitigation: Calibrate rules with business data, monitor false positives, and require human review before production enforcement. <br>
