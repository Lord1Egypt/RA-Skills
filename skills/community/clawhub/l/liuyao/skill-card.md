## Description: <br>
Liuyao provides traditional Chinese I Ching six-line coin divination with real or virtual coin casting, hexagram analysis, and Confucian and Taoist reference material. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for reflective Liuyao divination readings, learning the six-line coin-casting process, and exploring related I Ching, Confucian, Taoist, and five-elements references. Outputs should be treated as entertainment or personal reflection, not professional financial, medical, legal, or investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Divination readings may be mistaken for professional advice on health, finance, investment, legal, or major life decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present readings as entertainment or reflection and direct users to qualified professionals for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may run local Python helpers and one manually invoked maintenance script can append notes to its own log. <br>
Mitigation: Review helper behavior before execution and keep script use limited to the bundled skill directory. <br>
