## Description: <br>
Shike Gift Advisor helps users choose gifts by relationship, occasion, and budget, with cultural taboo checks and personalized blessing messages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sjj2026](https://clawhub.ai/user/sjj2026) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to generate gift recommendations, explain why each gift fits the recipient and occasion, check culturally sensitive gift taboos, and draft personalized blessing messages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Gift recommendations may include prices or purchase suggestions that are inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable for the recipient. <br>
Mitigation: Verify prices, availability, and recipient suitability before buying or sending a gift. <br>
Risk: Gift planning can involve personal relationship, preference, and occasion details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal information and provide only the details needed for the recommendation. <br>
Risk: The artifact lists email and WeChat contacts for paid services outside the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those contacts as separate third-party channels and evaluate them independently before engaging. <br>
