## Description: <br>
AI Company CTO is a governance agent skill for designing, deploying, and improving AI-agent collaboration systems with MLOps lifecycle practices, security hardening, human-AI operating models, technology portfolio planning, and risk controls. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, technical leaders, and AI operations teams use this skill to structure CTO-style decisions, architecture plans, MLOps governance, agent collaboration practices, and risk assessments for AI-company operating models. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for high-autonomy CTO governance workflows and declares broad automation permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Limit access to approved project files, APIs, and subagents, and require explicit human approval for writes, deletions, deployments, permission changes, production actions, and cross-agent data sharing. <br>
Risk: Architecture or governance recommendations could be applied incorrectly if treated as automatically executable decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated decisions, architecture plans, and risk assessments before implementation, especially for security, compliance, production, and cross-agent coordination changes. <br>
