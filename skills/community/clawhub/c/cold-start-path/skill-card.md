## Description: <br>
Choose the best first growth path for a new product, new shop, or weak early-stage offer based on product shape, margin room, content readiness, and channel constraints. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Growth operators, founders, and marketing teams use this skill to choose the first cold-start channel for a new or stalled offer before spreading effort across search, creators, ads, live selling, and merchant content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use commercially sensitive inputs such as margin room, ad spend, product sheets, store exports, search trend exports, Sheets, or CSV data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the business details needed for the recommendation, redact sensitive fields from optional exports, and do not provide credentials. <br>
Risk: Cold-start recommendations can be misleading when key economics or channel constraints are missing. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for missing product, price, margin, asset, creator, budget, and active-channel details before recommending a path; mark assumptions clearly when fallback guidance is used. <br>
