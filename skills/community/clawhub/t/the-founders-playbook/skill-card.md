## Description: <br>
Converts Anthropic's AI-native startup playbook into reusable, stage-aware agent guidance for validating, building, launching, and scaling a startup across four stages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lloyd-c137](https://clawhub.ai/user/lloyd-c137) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup teams use this skill with an AI agent to identify their current startup stage, pressure-test assumptions, run stage-specific exercises, and decide when to move between Idea, MVP, Launch, and Scale. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation language can cause the agent to enter startup-playbook mode during generic discussions about launch, scale, or build work. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly or ask the agent to confirm before switching into the playbook when the startup stage is ambiguous. <br>
Risk: Stage guidance may be over-applied to business decisions that need current market, legal, security, or financial review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as structured planning support and review its recommendations with appropriate human expertise before acting on high-impact decisions. <br>
