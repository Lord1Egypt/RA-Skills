## Description: <br>
Skill to ensure all messages sent to WhatsApp follow the platform's specific formatting syntax, preventing markdown bloat and ensuring a clean, mobile-first reading experience. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rubenfb23](https://clawhub.ai/user/rubenfb23) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to format outbound WhatsApp messages with WhatsApp-specific text styling while avoiding raw Markdown that may render poorly on mobile. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may install the wrong formatting helper or assume it needs broader access than message styling. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm this is the intended WhatsApp styling helper and that no special credentials or system access are required before installation. <br>
Risk: Incorrect formatting guidance could expose raw Markdown symbols or reduce readability in WhatsApp messages. <br>
Mitigation: Review outbound messages against the skill's WhatsApp syntax rules before sending. <br>
