## Description: <br>
Localize product listings across English and Chinese ecommerce channels. Use when adapting Amazon, Shopify, Taobao, Pinduoduo, TK, or independent-site copy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain-lab](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain-lab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators and localization teams use this skill to turn product facts into channel-ready listing copy, audits, SEO metadata, FAQs, scripts, and launch bundles for English and Chinese commerce channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ecommerce claims can be inaccurate, unsupported, or inconsistent with marketplace policy. <br>
Mitigation: Review official marketplace policies and verify product facts, certifications, dimensions, compatibility, and disclaimers before publishing. <br>
