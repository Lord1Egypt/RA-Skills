## Description: <br>
Plan educational travel experiences including museum visits, university tours, cultural workshops, historical field trips, hands-on learning activities, and related travel bookings powered by Fliggy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel-planning agents use this skill to collect study-tour requirements, run FlyAI travel search commands, and return educational itinerary options with booking links. It is intended for educational trips such as museum tours, field trips, university visits, history tours, and science-focused travel. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill instructs agents to install and run an unpinned global npm package for the FlyAI CLI. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in a sandbox or managed environment, review the package first, and prefer a pinned version approved by the operator. <br>
Risk: Travel prices, availability, booking terms, and booking links may change or carry privacy and purchase implications. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify booking details, terms, and privacy implications before purchasing or sharing personal information. <br>
