## Description: <br>
Provides structured study-abroad application assessment and strategy guidance, including school selection, essay direction, background-building priorities, and optional presentation planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kaylezhao](https://clawhub.ai/user/kaylezhao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, families, and admissions advisors use this skill to assess applicant readiness, plan application strategy across major study-abroad destinations, and draft structured guidance or presentation content for the application process. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Admissions policies, visa conditions, market trends, and school-specific requirements can change after the skill's reference material was prepared. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current admissions policies, deadlines, testing rules, and visa requirements with official school and government sources before acting on recommendations. <br>
Risk: Applicant privacy could be affected if users provide more personal, academic, or family information than needed for advising. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum applicant details needed for assessment and remove unnecessary personally identifiable information from generated materials. <br>
Risk: Generated presentations may include the default WeChat contact from the artifact if exported without review. <br>
Mitigation: Remove or replace the default WeChat contact before exporting, sharing, or presenting any generated PPT materials. <br>
Risk: Application advice may be over-relied on as a substitute for individualized review by schools, qualified advisors, or legal immigration counsel. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance, validate high-impact decisions independently, and seek qualified professional input for school-specific or immigration-sensitive questions. <br>
