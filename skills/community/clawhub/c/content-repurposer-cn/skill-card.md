## Description: <br>
Convert any content into platform-tailored posts for Twitter, LinkedIn, WeChat, Zhihu, Xiaohongshu, Douyin, and Toutiao in one step. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and operators use this skill to turn an article, idea, notes, transcript, keywords, or outline into native drafts for multiple social and publishing platforms. It supports platform-specific tone, language, length, structure, scripts, and posting checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Risk: Generated posts, translations, or platform adaptations may be inaccurate, misleading, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated draft and translation before publishing. <br>
Risk: Private, regulated, or sensitive content provided to the skill may be sent to the user's configured AI provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing private or regulated material unless the user is comfortable sending it to that provider. <br>
Risk: Off-platform support channels could be misused to request API keys or account credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Do not share API keys or account credentials through Telegram or other off-platform support. <br>
