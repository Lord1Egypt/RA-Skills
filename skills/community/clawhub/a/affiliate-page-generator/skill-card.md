## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and developers use this skill to draft, optimize, or audit affiliate program pages that explain commission terms, product fit, affiliate support, FAQs, CTAs, and SEO metadata. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project context may contain confidential product, revenue, customer, or proof-point details that could be reflected in generated affiliate page copy. <br>
Mitigation: Review and sanitize project context before use, and remove confidential details from generated copy before publishing. <br>
Risk: Affiliate copy may include inaccurate claims, commission terms, payout language, or disclosure wording. <br>
Mitigation: Review final claims, commission structure, payout terms, and affiliate disclosures with the responsible business or legal owner before publishing. <br>
