## Description: <br>
Ai-Interview-Skill provides adaptive technical interview practice across AI/ML, Python, Java, Go, C/C++, system design, and algorithms with performance tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to run timed mock technical interviews, receive scored feedback, and maintain ability profiles and session notes for later review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Interview profiles and full Q&A notes may contain personal details, employer-confidential information, or sensitive code stored under ~/.claude/ai-interview. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing secrets, confidential employer material, or unnecessary personal details during sessions; periodically review or delete local profile and session files. <br>
