## Description: <br>
Build and execute a content marketing strategy for a solopreneur business, including content planning, channel selection, calendars, distribution, repurposing, and measurement. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External solopreneurs, consultants, and solo business owners use this skill to plan content goals, research audience needs, select formats and channels, build calendars, distribute and repurpose content, and review performance metrics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may lead users to work with private customer conversations, support tickets, sales-call notes, or community content during audience research. <br>
Mitigation: Use only information you are authorized to use, remove sensitive personal or customer data, and avoid pasting private source material into an agent unless approved. <br>
Risk: Content strategy recommendations can be based on incomplete assumptions about the audience or market. <br>
Mitigation: Validate topics and channel choices with customer conversations, search or community signals, and monthly performance metrics before scaling production. <br>
