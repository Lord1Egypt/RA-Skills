# App Launch — Mobile App Launch Strategy & First 1000 Users

> **Gingiris Mobile Growth Series** | From zero to 10K DAU without paid ads

## The 90-Day App Launch Framework

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (T-60 to T-7)
**Goal: Build a launch audience before the app is public**

#### Week 1-4: Foundation
- [ ] Create a landing page with email waitlist (convert 30%+ of visitors)
- [ ] Set up TestFlight (iOS) + Google Play Beta (Android)
- [ ] Build your micro-influencer list (200+ accounts at 5K-100K followers)
- [ ] Create a demo video that shows core value in under 60 seconds

#### Week 5-8: Community Seeding
- [ ] Onboard 50-200 beta users from waitlist
- [ ] Post in relevant subreddits (soft, helpful — not promotional)
- [ ] Send personalized DMs to 20 micro-influencers with free access
- [ ] Start collecting testimonials and UGC from beta users

### Phase 2: Launch Week (T-0 to T+7)
**Goal: Spike rankings + collect social proof**

#### Launch Day Checklist
```
Morning (T+0):
□ App Store metadata locked (title, subtitle, keywords, screenshots)
□ Product Hunt mobile launch submitted
□ Press kit sent to 10-20 tech media contacts
□ Influencer posts go live (pre-coordinated)

Day 1-3:
□ Respond to every App Store review
□ Reddit AMA or "Show HN" post
□ Creator content starts rolling out
□ Monitor keywords — tweak ASO if needed

Day 4-7:
□ Feature storytelling: "How we built X" post
□ Re-engage beta users for reviews
□ Analyze retention data — find your Day-1 drop-off
```

### Phase 3: Growth Engine (T+8 to T+90)
**Goal: Build repeatable, sustainable daily downloads**

## UGC Creator Program

### Creator Tier Structure
| Tier | Followers | Payment | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | $10-30/video | 1 video, authentic |
| Micro | 10K-50K | $50-100/video | 1 video + story |
| Mid | 50K-200K | $150-300/video | 1 video + negotiated |

### Creator Brief Template
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed your content about [relevant topic] — your audience would 
love [App Name] because [specific reason relevant to their content].

We'd love to offer you:
- Free [premium plan] access
- $[X] creator fee for one authentic video
- No script — just share your genuine experience

Would you be open to trying it?

[Your name]
```

## App Store Launch Optimization

### The "Title + Subtitle" Formula for Downloads
```
Title: [Core Function] — [Key Differentiator]
Subtitle: [Use Case] + [Result] + [Target User]

Example:
Title: "AI Video Maker — HeyGen"
Subtitle: "Talking Avatar for Business Presentations"
```

### Screenshot Sequence (iOS 5 slots)
1. **Hero**: Core value prop in one line + product shot
2. **Feature 1**: Most-used feature that solves biggest pain
3. **Social proof**: User quote + result stat
4. **Feature 2**: Differentiating feature vs. competitors
5. **CTA**: "Download free" + urgency/value summary

## First 1,000 Reviews Strategy

### Week 1: In-App Prompt Timing
- Prompt for review after user achieves first success (not at signup)
- Wait for "positive signal" moment: completed export, shared content, etc.
- iOS: Use `SKStoreReviewAPI` (shown max 3x/year)
- Android: Use In-App Review API

### Responding to Reviews Script
**5-star**: "Thank you [Name]! We love hearing [specific thing they mentioned]. Stay tuned for [upcoming feature]."

**3-star**: "Thanks for the feedback. I'm [founder name] — can you DM me the specific issue? I want to personally fix this."

**1-star**: "Hi [Name], I'm sorry about this. I'm the founder and I'd love to understand what went wrong. Emailing you directly."
