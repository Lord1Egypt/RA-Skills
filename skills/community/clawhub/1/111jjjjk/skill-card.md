## Description: <br>
薇薇发 helps an agent use uploaded images, files, CSV data, and chat or workflow inputs to generate customized text responses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and workflow builders use this skill to route uploaded images, documents, CSV data, and chat history into an LLM or workflow app and draft tailored text responses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release evidence reports exposed service endpoints, user or file identifiers, and an API bearer token without clear privacy or access-control boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing; do not use with sensitive files or images until the publisher removes and rotates exposed credentials, replaces internal endpoints with placeholders, and documents upload access controls and retention. <br>
Risk: The evidence describes internal testing notes for file and image upload workflows, which may not provide enough operational guidance for safe production use. <br>
Mitigation: Require publisher documentation for expected inputs, authorization behavior, upload handling, and retention before deployment. <br>
