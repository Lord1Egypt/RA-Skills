## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Telegram bot tokens or webhook endpoints could be exposed or misused during deployment. <br>
Mitigation: Protect bot tokens, validate webhook traffic, and grant only the permissions required for the bot's intended chats or groups. <br>
Risk: Payment, moderation, or group-management flows could affect users if implemented without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review payment and moderation logic carefully before launch, including permission checks, rate limits, and blocked-user handling. <br>
