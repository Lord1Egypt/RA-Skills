## Description: <br>
Use when analyzing, clarifying, specifying, reviewing, validating, approving, or archiving product and business requirements, including PRDs, AI scenarios, prototypes, acceptance criteria, requirement changes, and development handoffs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, business analysts, and development teams use DemandSpec to turn ideas, meeting notes, existing PRDs, screenshots, and AI scenario concepts into traceable requirements assets that can be reviewed, validated, handed off, and archived. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to create or update workspace requirements files, so ambiguous requests can lead to writes in unintended project paths. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target directory, demand or change identifier, and whether the user wants files written before using it in an important repository. <br>
Risk: Sparse source material can cause assumptions to be mistaken for confirmed requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Keep facts, assumptions, and blockers separate, and require each assumption to include a validation method, owner, and deadline. <br>
