### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel, visa, legal, medical, tax, and business guidance can become outdated or vary by user nationality and situation. <br>
Mitigation: Re-check official sources before booking travel, signing leases, choosing schools, seeking medical coverage, filing taxes, or starting business activity. <br>
Risk: The skill may create ~/thailand/memory.md and retain travel or relocation preferences locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing highly sensitive details unless needed, and review or delete the local memory file when retained context is no longer wanted. <br>
Risk: Region, cost, seasonality, and safety recommendations may not fit users with specialized health, mobility, family, or legal constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify the user's timeline, nationality, budget, health needs, risk tolerance, and intended activities before giving specific recommendations. <br>
