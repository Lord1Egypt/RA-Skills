## Description: <br>
Argus Edge helps agents evaluate prediction-market betting opportunities by comparing technical-analysis probabilities with market odds, applying freshness and consensus guards, and suggesting Kelly-style sizing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JamieRossouw](https://clawhub.ai/user/JamieRossouw) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to structure prediction-market scans, expected-value calculations, and betting-size recommendations for Polymarket-style crypto markets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Betting guidance may be mistaken for a verified money-making system or used for real-money wagers without independent validation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as analysis only, independently validate win-rate and expected-value claims, and do not give an agent account access or permission to place real-money bets unless separate trusted tooling and explicit limits are in place. <br>
