## Description: <br>
Provides content lookup and performance summaries for public Pearvideo short-video and topic pages without downloads or API reverse engineering. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mike47512](https://clawhub.ai/user/mike47512) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external analysts, and agents use this skill to summarize public Pearvideo search results, account pages, topic pages, and ranking entries for lightweight content and engagement analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bulk scraping, high-frequency requests, or bypassing human checks could violate platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use only light, user-directed summaries of public pages, keep request frequency controlled, and do not bypass dynamic rendering or verification checks. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misused to collect non-public content or download media. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to publicly visible page information and do not perform downloads, API calls, reverse engineering, or access to private data. <br>
