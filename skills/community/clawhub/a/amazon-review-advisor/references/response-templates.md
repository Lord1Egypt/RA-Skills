## 一、公开回复模板

### 1.1 质量问题类

**英文版**：
```
Dear Customer,

Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We're truly sorry that [specific issue] did not meet your expectations.

We take product quality very seriously and have already shared your feedback with our quality control team for immediate investigation. [Specific improvement action]

As a next step, we'd like to make this right for you. Please contact us at [email] with your order number so we can discuss options.

We appreciate your honesty and hope to serve you better in the future.

Best regards,
[Store Name] Customer Support
```

**中文参考**：
```
尊敬的买家，

感谢您的反馈。我们对[具体问题]给您带来的不便深表歉意。

为了让您满意，我们愿意提供解决方案。请通过[邮箱]联系我们，提供订单号，我们会尽快处理。

感谢您的反馈，期待为您做得更好。

此致敬礼
[店铺名称] 客服团队
```

We'd like to make this right for you. Could you please email us at [email] with your order number so we can discuss options?

Thank you for giving us the chance to improve.

Best,
[Name]

## 三、评价邀请模板

> ✅ 所有评价邀请通过亚马逊官方 "Request a Review" 按钮发送，对所有买家一视同仁。

### 3.1 标准版（通过官方Request a Review）

```
Hi [Buyer Name],

Thank you for your purchase! We hope you're enjoying [Product Name].

If you have a moment, we'd love to hear your thoughts. You can easily share your feedback by clicking the "Request a Review" button on your order page.

Your review helps other shoppers make informed decisions and helps us improve.

Thank you for your support!

Best regards,
[Store Name]
```

### 3.2 使用后跟进版（订单送达7天后）

```
Hi [Buyer Name],

We hope you've had a chance to try out [Product Name]!

We're curious to know how it's working for you. If you're willing, you can leave a review through the "Request a Review" button on your order page.

Every honest review, positive or constructive, helps us understand how we can do better.

Thanks for being a valued customer!

Best,
[Store Name]
```

## 四、合规申诉材料清单

### 4.1 人身攻击/违规内容申诉

**需要准备的材料**：
- [ ] 评论截图（含日期和评分）
- [ ] 订单号（如有）
- [ ] 具体指出违规内容（如"使用了侮辱性词汇'XXX'"）
- [ ] 说明为何违反评论政策

**申诉话术**：
```
This review contains [type of violation, e.g., "personal insults and offensive language"] which violates Amazon's community guidelines. Specifically, the reviewer used the following inappropriate term: [quote].

We kindly request Amazon to re-evaluate this review per their review content guidelines.

Thank you for your consideration.
```

### 4.2 虚假/无关评论申诉

**需要准备的材料**：
- [ ] 评论截图
- [ ] 证明未购买/产品不符的证据
- [ ] 产品ASIN对比说明

**申诉话术**：
```
This review appears to be about a different product or from a non-purchaser. The reviewer mentions [specific claim] which does not match our product [ASIN].

We kindly request verification of this reviewer's purchase history.

Thank you.
```
