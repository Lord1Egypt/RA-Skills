Administrative teams, legal operations, HR teams, and developers use this skill to plan, implement, and troubleshoot repeatable Word mail merge and DOCX template assembly workflows without breaking document formatting. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be invoked too broadly in some agent setups, which could cause generic document-workflow guidance to be applied to unrelated or sensitive tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the task is actually about Word mail merge or DOCX automation before following generated workflow, code, or configuration guidance. <br>
Risk: Generated Word or DOCX automation plans for legal, HR, or other sensitive documents could preserve incorrect fields, formatting, or source data. <br>
Mitigation: Validate generated documents for field completion, formatting, page count, section breaks, table rows, filenames, and required PDF export before use. <br>
