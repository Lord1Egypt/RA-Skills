## Description: <br>
PDF Math Translate helps translate scientific PDF documents while preserving formulas, charts, tables of contents, and annotations, with support for multiple languages and translation services. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, researchers, students, and developers use this skill to translate scientific PDFs that contain mathematical formulas, figures, tables of contents, and annotations while keeping the original layout. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: PDF content may be sent to online demos or third-party translation providers selected by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Use local or self-hosted translation for confidential, unpublished, regulated, or proprietary PDFs, and review provider privacy and retention terms before use. <br>
