## Description: <br>
Optimize product color, size, and variant offerings based on sales data, market trends, and inventory constraints. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators and merchandising teams use this skill to evaluate product variants, reduce underperforming options, plan inventory allocation, and identify data-supported additions or bundles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Variant recommendations may influence commercial inventory, pricing, discounting, listing, or purchase decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision-support only and require manual approval before acting on purchases, discounts, listing changes, or variant retirements. <br>
Risk: Variant analysis can involve sensitive sales, inventory, customer, vendor, or marketplace performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum business data needed for the analysis and avoid unnecessary customer or vendor identifiers. <br>
