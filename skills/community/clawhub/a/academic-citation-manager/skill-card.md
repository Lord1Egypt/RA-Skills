## Description: <br>
Add real references and standardize citations for research papers and theses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[YouStudyeveryday](https://clawhub.ai/user/YouStudyeveryday) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, academic writers, and developers use this skill to retrieve bibliographic metadata, manage reference libraries, generate in-text citations and bibliographies, convert citation styles, and check citation integrity for papers and theses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bibliographic lookup queries can disclose DOI, ISBN, title, author, or unpublished research information to external metadata services. <br>
Mitigation: Use online lookups only when that disclosure is acceptable, and avoid lookups for confidential or unpublished research. <br>
Risk: Local import and export operations can overwrite or alter bibliography files when output paths are reused. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit output filenames and keep backups of important papers, libraries, and bibliography files. <br>
Risk: Generated or converted citation entries may contain formatting or metadata errors. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated citations and bibliographies against the target style guide and source records before submission. <br>
