## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Craft activities described by the skill may involve blades, wet folding chemicals, LEDs, or laser cutting. <br>
Mitigation: Use appropriate safety practices, supervision, protective equipment, and manufacturer guidance for tools, chemicals, electronics, and laser cutters. <br>
Risk: Commercial use of paper-art designs may raise copyright or permission issues. <br>
Mitigation: Verify rights, licenses, and permissions before selling, publishing, teaching, or adapting paper-art designs commercially. <br>
