## Description: <br>
基于前六步分析结果，为法条生成完整的适用决策流程图（SOP），标注判断节点、证据要求和切换条件。这是法条分析的最终沉淀，形成可复用的实战操作指南。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Legal practitioners, researchers, and students use this Chinese-language skill to turn prior statute analysis into reusable decision-tree SOPs with decision nodes, evidence requirements, burdens of proof, dispute points, and fallback statute paths. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated decision trees may be mistaken for legal advice or applied without jurisdiction-specific review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafting support, verify the analysis against current law and case facts, and have a qualified human review before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Confidential or privileged case facts could be exposed if entered into an untrusted agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential case details unless the surrounding agent environment and data-handling controls are trusted. <br>
Risk: Incomplete prerequisite analysis can produce an oversimplified or misleading legal SOP. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill after completing the relevant statute structure, concept, system, and evidence mapping steps, then review assumptions before reuse. <br>
