## Description: <br>
副业/独立开发项目验证工具，用于分析副业想法、产品 idea 或方向是否值得做，覆盖需求真伪、竞品情况、市场规模、变现可能和实施难度。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[belivar](https://clawhub.ai/user/belivar) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, indie developers, and product builders use this skill to validate side-project and product ideas through a structured five-step workflow covering demand, competition, market size, monetization, and implementation difficulty. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional research scripts may send idea keywords, product names, or market terms to public web services and third-party APIs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using confidential product names, proprietary concepts, or sensitive keywords with the scripts; review the small shell scripts before running them in restricted environments. <br>
