# Social Media Calendar Quality Checklist

Review every calendar against this checklist before finalizing. 55 items across 11 categories.

---

## A. Content Pillars (5 items)

- [ ] **A1.** 4-6 pillars defined with names, descriptions, and target frequencies
- [ ] **A2.** Each pillar has a designated primary platform
- [ ] **A3.** Content mix ratio documented (e.g., 25% educational, 20% product)
- [ ] **A4.** Promotional content does not exceed 30% of total posts
- [ ] **A5.** Pillars cover the full journey: awareness, consideration, conversion, retention

## B. Calendar Structure (7 items)

- [ ] **B1.** Every day has a planned post or deliberate rest day
- [ ] **B2.** Each entry includes: date, platform, pillar, type, topic, caption brief, visual direction, CTA, hashtags, posting time
- [ ] **B3.** All active platforms are covered
- [ ] **B4.** Stories and ephemeral content are planned alongside feed content
- [ ] **B5.** 1-2 flex slots per week reserved for trending/reactive content
- [ ] **B6.** Posting frequency matches platform recommendations
- [ ] **B7.** Calendar spans the full requested planning period

## C. Platform Optimization (7 items)

- [ ] **C1.** Image dimensions match platform specs (1080x1350 IG, 1000x1500 Pinterest, 1080x1920 Reels/TikTok)
- [ ] **C2.** Video lengths respect platform norms (15-45s Reels/TikTok, under 3 min Facebook)
- [ ] **C3.** Hashtag counts appropriate per platform (5-15 IG, 3-5 TikTok, 0-3 FB, 1-2 X)
- [ ] **C4.** Captions front-load hooks within first 125 characters (IG) or 280 characters (X)
- [ ] **C5.** Cross-posting adaptations documented for multi-platform posts
- [ ] **C6.** Pinterest content includes keyword-rich titles and descriptions
- [ ] **C7.** Instagram posts include product tag recommendations where applicable

## D. Commercial Alignment (6 items)

- [ ] **D1.** Product launches have pre-launch, launch-day, and post-launch content
- [ ] **D2.** Sales events have lead-up, live, and follow-up content mapped
- [ ] **D3.** Holidays and seasonal moments planned at least 2 weeks in advance
- [ ] **D4.** Every promo post includes a specific offer (code, free shipping, bundle)
- [ ] **D5.** Landing page URLs or link-in-bio directions specified for traffic posts
- [ ] **D6.** Post-purchase content included (thank you, how-to, UGC encouragement)

## E. Visual Consistency (5 items)

- [ ] **E1.** Visual direction notes included for every post requiring original assets
- [ ] **E2.** Brand color palette referenced in visual notes
- [ ] **E3.** Photography/video style consistent across planned content
- [ ] **E4.** Text overlay fonts and styles aligned with brand guidelines
- [ ] **E5.** UGC reviewed for quality and brand alignment before inclusion

## F. Copy Quality (5 items)

- [ ] **F1.** Every caption brief includes an attention-grabbing hook
- [ ] **F2.** Brand voice consistent across platforms (adjusted for tone, same identity)
- [ ] **F3.** Every post has a single, clear call to action
- [ ] **F4.** Caption lengths vary (mix of short punchy and longer storytelling)
- [ ] **F5.** All copy free of errors and placeholder text

## G. Hashtag Strategy (5 items)

- [ ] **G1.** Hashtag sets prepared in advance (not improvised at posting)
- [ ] **G2.** Each set mixes branded (1-2), niche (5-8), and discovery (3-5) tags
- [ ] **G3.** Sets rotated across posts to avoid repetition penalties
- [ ] **G4.** At least 5 different sets prepared for rotation
- [ ] **G5.** Banned or spammy hashtags checked and removed

## H. Scheduling (4 items)

- [ ] **H1.** Posting times based on audience analytics, not convenience
- [ ] **H2.** Times specified in the audience's timezone
- [ ] **H3.** Time-sensitive content scheduled with appropriate lead time
- [ ] **H4.** Posts staggered across platforms (not all at the same time)

## I. Content Batching (4 items)

- [ ] **I1.** Batching plan included with production session dates
- [ ] **I2.** Photo/video shoot requirements listed with assets and team
- [ ] **I3.** Copywriting sessions scheduled 5+ business days before publication
- [ ] **I4.** All content scheduled 3-5 days before publish date for review

## J. Approval Workflow (3 items)

- [ ] **J1.** Workflow documented with clear roles (creator, reviewer, approver)
- [ ] **J2.** Turnaround times specified per stage
- [ ] **J3.** Each post has an approval status field

## K. Analytics (4 items)

- [ ] **K1.** KPIs defined for each content pillar
- [ ] **K2.** Platform-specific metrics identified (IG saves, TikTok completion, Pinterest clicks)
- [ ] **K3.** Commercial metrics tracked (social revenue, link clicks, code redemptions)
- [ ] **K4.** Weekly review and monthly retrospective cadence established
