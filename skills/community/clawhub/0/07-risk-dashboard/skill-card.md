## Description: <br>
Provides internal control leaders with a full-chain risk dashboard that combines complaint, logistics exception, and supplier score inputs into risk heat maps, alerts, trend forecasts, and decision-ready reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nic-yuan](https://clawhub.ai/user/nic-yuan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Internal control managers and risk analysts use this skill to turn user-provided internal risk data into daily reports, weekly outlooks, text heat maps, anomaly pattern analysis, and prioritized action lists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Internal reports may contain sensitive or audience-limited business data. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only data that the intended audience is allowed to see and define the business scope and time range for each report. <br>
Risk: Risk summaries could be mistaken for official Alibaba enforcement decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as an internal reporting template and require human review before supplier penalties, escalations, or broad push notifications. <br>
