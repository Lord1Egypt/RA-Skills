## Description: <br>
Guides agents in planning or building an interactive dashboard for ClickBank niche rankings, Pinterest search demand estimates, affiliate payout potential, and opportunity scoring. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External developers, builders, and affiliate marketers use this skill to create a Pinterest-focused affiliate trend dashboard that helps compare niches, payouts, competition, and search demand before selecting products or content ideas. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Affiliate-link analytics and click tracking may collect or share user interaction data. <br>
Mitigation: Add clear privacy language covering tracked events, affiliate parameters, third-party data sharing, retention, and opt-out controls before using the dashboard with real users. <br>
Risk: The mockup or implementation may rely on third-party assets, external links, or scripts. <br>
Mitigation: Review third-party assets and scripts before deployment, keep only necessary dependencies, and document the external services used by ClickBank, Pinterest, analytics, or hosting integrations. <br>
