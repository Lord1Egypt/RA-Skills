## Description: <br>
智能伴游AI旅游搭子，提供沉浸式景点与文物讲解服务。当用户要求讲解景点、介绍文物、提供旅游信息、推荐游览路线时使用。支持当年明月幽默风格与孔二狗江湖风格。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[e2e5g](https://clawhub.ai/user/e2e5g) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and cultural-tour users use this skill to get immersive Chinese-language explanations of attractions, artifacts, history, poems, and route suggestions in selectable narrative styles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Time-sensitive travel details such as opening hours, ticket prices, transportation, weather, and local notices may change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current travel details against official venue, transit, weather, or tourism sources before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Personalized recommendations may require location or trip preference details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the location and preference information needed for the requested guidance. <br>
