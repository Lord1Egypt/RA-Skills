## Description: <br>
Provides calm, nonjudgmental money-mindset coaching inspired by The Psychology of Money, helping users reflect on saving, spending, debt, investing emotions, confidence, and habits without giving legal, tax, accounting, or product-specific financial advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golofu](https://clawhub.ai/user/golofu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for reflective conversations about personal money habits, financial anxiety, saving, spending, debt, and investing mindset. It is intended to help users clarify their situation and next steps while redirecting requests for regulated financial, legal, tax, accounting, or product-specific advice to qualified professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake reflective money-mindset coaching for licensed financial, investment, legal, tax, or accounting advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses framed as general coaching, avoid product-specific recommendations or market predictions, and direct regulated advice requests to qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary sensitive financial details during personal money conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for information needed to reason about the user's situation and avoid collecting account numbers, credentials, or other unnecessary sensitive data. <br>
