## Description: <br>
Summarize URLs or files with the summarize CLI (web, PDFs, images, audio, YouTube). <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[pin-alt](https://clawhub.ai/user/pin-alt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and other ClawHub users use this skill to summarize web pages, local files, PDFs, images, audio, and YouTube links through the summarize CLI. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The summarize CLI is installed from a third-party Homebrew tap. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the publisher and steipete Homebrew tap are trusted, and review the installed CLI before sensitive use. <br>
Risk: Summarized local files, private URLs, regulated documents, or confidential media may be sent to configured AI or extraction providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive inputs unless the configured providers and data handling are approved for that content. <br>
