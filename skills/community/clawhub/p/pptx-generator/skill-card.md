## Description: <br>
Pptx Generator helps agents create editable PowerPoint presentations with professional layouts, multiple visual styles, charts, tables, and image slides. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobewin](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobewin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to generate editable .pptx presentations for business reports, academic defenses, and technical talks from user prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated presentation content or layout may be inaccurate or unsuitable for sharing without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated .pptx before distributing or presenting it. <br>
Risk: The skill writes local PowerPoint files and depends on local Python packages. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in a trusted Python environment and specify the intended output filename or directory. <br>
