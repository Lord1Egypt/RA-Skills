## Use Case: <br>
Students and researchers use this skill to draft and structure Chinese liberal-arts and social-science papers from supplied references, research background, and optional chapter-count preferences. It supports title generation, outline creation, chapter-by-chapter drafting, abstracts, keywords, and final plain-text paper assembly. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated academic text may contain inaccurate claims, weak citations, or content that conflicts with academic-integrity rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review and revise generated drafts, verify factual claims and citations against source materials, and follow the user's institutional academic-integrity requirements. <br>
Risk: Research materials provided to the agent may include confidential or sensitive work. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential research unless the user's OpenClaw and model-provider data handling settings are acceptable for that material. <br>
