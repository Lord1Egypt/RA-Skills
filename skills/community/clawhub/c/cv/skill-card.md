Job seekers, career coaches, and agents use this skill to draft or review resumes and CVs with ATS-friendly structure, quantified experience bullets, and industry-specific content guidance. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume or CV content may include incorrect claims, dates, metrics, or formatting. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content for factual accuracy and suitability before submitting applications. <br>
Risk: The skill may process personal career information provided by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only personal information intended for use in a resume or CV. <br>
