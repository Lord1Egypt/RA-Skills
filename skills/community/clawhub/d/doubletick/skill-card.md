## Description: <br>
Send tracked emails via Gmail and check if they were opened. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cseguinlz](https://clawhub.ai/user/cseguinlz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and other users can use this skill to send Gmail messages with read tracking and review whether tracked messages were opened. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tracked email opens can expose recipient behavior and may be privacy-sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Use tracking only when it is lawful and consistent with organizational policy and recipient expectations. <br>
Risk: An agent-accessible email tool can send messages through a Gmail account. <br>
Mitigation: Review OAuth/login prompts and confirm each recipient and message before sending. <br>
