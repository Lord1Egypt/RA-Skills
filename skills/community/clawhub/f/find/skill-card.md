## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to find specific items, sources, or locations when the answer is not already known and the search may require clarification, expansion, and validation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad or underspecified searches can waste effort or return results that do not match the user's intent. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify success criteria and constrain the search by source boundaries, location, budget, timeframe, and acceptable result format before expanding. <br>
Risk: Search results may be unreliable, stale, or only partially aligned with the request. <br>
Mitigation: Validate source reliability and currentness before delivery, and state confidence and caveats when uncertainty remains. <br>
