# Review Rubric

Use this checklist before finalizing important outputs.

## Audience fit
- Is the language native to the intended audience?
- Does the content address a real pain, desire, or decision?

## Platform fit
- Does the format match how people consume on this platform?
- Is the opening strong enough for the feed environment?

## Hook quality
- Is the first line specific?
- Does it create curiosity, tension, or relevance?

## Structure quality
- Is there a clear flow?
- Is the content easy to scan or follow?

## CTA quality
- Is the next action obvious?
- Does the CTA match the user's intent and audience readiness?

## Factual grounding
- Are all product claims and performance claims supported?
- Are placeholders clearly marked where evidence is missing?

## Common failure patterns
- generic opening
- too much brand language, not enough audience language
- identical copy pasted across platforms
- weak CTA
- abstract points with no concrete scenario
- unverified claims presented as proof
