## Description: <br>
Pet Economy provides lifecycle pet-management guidance for breed selection, feeding plans, vaccination and parasite reminders, symptom triage, insurance comparison, behavior training, and memorial services for dogs, cats, and exotic pets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Pet owners, pet shop operators, and pet-industry entrepreneurs use this skill to create pet care plans, compare insurance options, identify symptoms that may require veterinary care, and plan ongoing health and behavior management. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pet health guidance can be mistaken for veterinary diagnosis or emergency care. <br>
Mitigation: Treat symptom guidance as informational and confirm urgent or serious symptoms with a veterinarian. <br>
Risk: Insurance terms, vaccination schedules, and feeding plans may change or may not fit an individual animal. <br>
Mitigation: Verify insurance terms and care plans against current professional advice and the pet's specific health needs. <br>
