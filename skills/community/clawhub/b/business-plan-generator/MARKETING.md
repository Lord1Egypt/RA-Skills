## Marketplace Listing

**Title:** Business Plan Generator — Investor-Ready Plans in Under 1 Hour

**Tagline:** 4 prompts. Executive summary, market analysis, financial model, GTM strategy. No consultants required.

**Description (150 words):**
Writing a business plan is the thing most entrepreneurs avoid until a bank forces them to. It's not because the business isn't real — it's because staring at a blank doc trying to calculate TAM or write a 3-year financial model is genuinely painful.

Business Plan Generator solves this with four targeted prompts that walk Claude through every section investors and lenders want to see. Fill in your business details, run the prompt, get investor-grade output.

Each prompt is purpose-built: the financial projections prompt enforces LTV:CAC ratios and break-even analysis. The market analysis prompt outputs a competitive matrix that puts YOUR business in the table. The GTM prompt produces a 90-day launch plan with specific tactics and success metrics — not vague advice.

Works for SaaS, e-commerce, services, brick-and-mortar, nonprofits, and everything in between.
