## Description: <br>
Zhua Metacognition helps an agent monitor its reasoning process, select cognitive strategies, detect errors, explain decisions, and improve decision quality. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Agents and their operators use this skill to apply a lightweight self-reflection checklist during reasoning and decision tasks. It supports confidence checks, task difficulty assessment, strategy selection, error detection, and post-decision reasoning review. <br>
