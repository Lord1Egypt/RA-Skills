## Description: <br>
Hệ thống phân tích tính cách Thần Cửu độc lập (phát minh bởi Anh Vũ), hỗ trợ tính toán 8 chỉ số cốt lõi và phân tích tương quan mối quan hệ. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Jaskies](https://clawhub.ai/user/Jaskies) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can ask an agent to calculate and explain Thần Cửu personality indicators from a person's legal name and birth date, or compare two people for relationship-oriented interpretation. The skill is best suited for reflective guidance, not factual prediction or professional advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for real names and birth dates, which are personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only details that the user is authorized to provide, avoid entering another person's information without consent, and do not include unnecessary identifiers. <br>
Risk: The skill states that analysis results are saved locally under knowledge/ThanCuu/. <br>
Mitigation: Delete locally retained records when they are no longer needed or when the user does not want them retained. <br>
Risk: Personality and relationship interpretations can be mistaken for predictive or professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as reflective interpretation and avoid treating them as factual predictions, medical advice, legal advice, employment screening, or other high-impact decision support. <br>
