# 案例 3：Chinglish 高频句审查

## 原文

Under the leadership of our team, with the development of the market, we lay a solid foundation for the future. At the same time, we need to strengthen the management of the process. Generally speaking, it goes without saying that we should make a decision to take measures to promote the development of the project. In this regard, there is no doubt that we can achieve our goal.

---

## 审查报告

### 🔴 必须修改（硬错误）

（无语法硬错误，但全段充满中式翻译腔）

### 🟡 建议修改（翻译腔/不地道）

1. "Under the leadership of our team" → "Led by our team" 或 "Our team" | 中式官腔直译
2. "with the development of the market" → "as the market grows" | 中式伴随结构
3. "we lay a solid foundation for the future" → "we have built a strong base" | lay a solid foundation 是中文"打下坚实基础"直译
4. "At the same time" → "Meanwhile" 或直接删除 | 中式连接词滥用
5. "strengthen the management of the process" → "improve process management" | 动词+of+名词 → 形容词+名词
6. "Generally speaking" → 删除 | 空泛开头
7. "it goes without saying that" → 删除 | 冗余套话
8. "make a decision to take measures to" → "take steps to" 或 "decide to" | 三重冗余
9. "promote the development of the project" → "advance the project" | promote the development of 是"促进...发展"直译
10. "In this regard" → "In this respect" 或删除 | 中式过渡词
11. "there is no doubt that" → "we will" | there-be 开头 + 空洞强调

### 🟢 优化建议

1. 整段 78 词，信息量极低，全是套话 → 建议直接用 1-2 句话说清核心观点
2. 缺乏具体行动项和数据支撑

### 📊 整体评估

- **当前水平**：初级翻译腔
- **主要问题类型**：中式官腔套话 / 连接词堆砌 / there-be 滥用 / 三重动词冗余
- **风格判断**：典型中文政府工作报告风格的英文直译，缺乏英文商务写作的简洁性

---

## 修改后版本

Led by our team, we have built a strong market presence. Next, we need to improve process management to advance the project.

---

## 为什么这样改

原文 78 词 → 修改后 22 词，减少 72%。这是最典型的"中式官腔"案例：全段没有一个具体信息，全是套话和连接词。修改后保留两个核心信息：(1) 团队建立了市场基础；(2) 下一步需要改进流程管理推进项目。英文商务写作的核心原则是"one idea per sentence"，原文一个观点写了 4 句话。
