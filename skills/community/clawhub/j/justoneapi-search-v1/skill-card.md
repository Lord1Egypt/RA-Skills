## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The token and search terms are sent to the declared JustOneAPI service, and query-string tokens may appear in service, proxy, or monitoring logs. <br>
Mitigation: Use a scoped or easily rotated token, avoid sensitive private searches unless that sharing is acceptable, and avoid pasting token values into chat messages, screenshots, or logs. <br>
