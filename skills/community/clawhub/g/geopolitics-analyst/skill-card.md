## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, researchers, and operators use this skill to produce structured geopolitics and international affairs analysis across global power relations, security, economics, conflicts, and governance. It supports concise overviews, topic deep dives, event interpretation, and report-style outputs with source labeling. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reports may be written to disk and uploaded to an external service without a clearly documented consent gate. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the upload destination, included content, optionality, and retention or deletion policy before invoking report upload workflows. <br>
Risk: Geopolitical analysis can become misleading if current events are not verified or if facts and forecasts are blurred. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's source-labeling and cross-checking workflow, distinguish verified facts from assessments, and review outputs before distribution. <br>
