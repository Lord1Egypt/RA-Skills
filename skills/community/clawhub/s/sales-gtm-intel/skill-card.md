## Description: <br>
AI-powered B2B sales and go-to-market intelligence engine that builds ICP profiles, generates sales battle cards and outreach sequences, analyzes competitors and prospects, and creates TAM/SAM/SOM market-entry analyses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, revenue, and go-to-market teams use this skill to research markets and accounts, shape ICPs, create battle cards, plan territories, qualify deals, and draft personalized outreach. Generated sales content should be reviewed for accuracy, consent, platform terms, and regional compliance before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated prospecting or outreach content could conflict with privacy rules, opt-outs, anti-spam requirements, or platform terms if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review outbound content and target lists against applicable privacy rules, CAN-SPAM, GDPR, opt-outs, and each platform's terms before use. <br>
Risk: Broad sales and GTM triggers may activate the skill for tasks where sales-intelligence behavior is not intended. <br>
Mitigation: Install or scope the skill for explicit sales, GTM, prospecting, competitor, and outreach workflows. <br>
Risk: Market, competitor, and prospect intelligence may be incomplete or outdated when used for business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate generated analysis against current first-party data, licensed data sources, and human sales review before acting on it. <br>
