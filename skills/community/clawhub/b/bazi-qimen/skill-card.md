## Description: <br>
Bazi (Four Pillars Astrology) and Qi Men Dun Jia chart calculation and interpretation skills. Provides data analysis and cognitive science based calculations and interpretations, serving as an auxiliary decision-making reference rooted in traditional Chinese metaphysics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to calculate Bazi and Qi Men Dun Jia charts and receive structured cultural interpretation for career, wealth, relationships, health, and query-specific divination topics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce career, wealth, relationship, or health readings that users may over-weight as decision advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame readings as cultural reference material, preserve the skill's disclaimer posture, and direct users to qualified professional advice for substantive decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may route vague astrology or divination requests into this skill without enough user intent. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify whether the user wants Bazi, Qi Men, or both before producing an interpretation when the request is ambiguous. <br>
Risk: The bundled calculations use simplified solar-term and luck-start assumptions that can differ from precise calendars. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose simplified calculation limits and avoid presenting chart boundaries or timing-sensitive results as definitive. <br>
