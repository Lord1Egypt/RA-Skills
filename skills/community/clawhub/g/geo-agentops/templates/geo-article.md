# GEO Article Template

> Template: Follow this structure to maximize AI citation rate

---

## Article Title

**Requirements**:
- Include core keywords
- Length: 10–20 words
- Format: Question-based / Numbered / Comparative

**Examples**:
- "How to Reduce B2B Inquiry Costs by 80% Using GEO in 2026?"
- "The Complete GEO Guide for Export Companies: From Zero to AI Citations"
- "GEO vs SEO: Which Strategy Gets You More B2B Inquiries?"

---

## Article Structure

### 1. Introduction (100–150 words)

**Template**:
```
In [industry context], [target users] face [core pain point]. This article explains [solution] and how it helps you achieve [key benefit]. According to [data source], [key statistic].
```

### 2. Body (800–1500 words)

**Subheading structure**:
```
## 1. [Core Concept]
### 1.1 [Sub-topic]
### 1.2 [Sub-topic]

## 2. [Methodology / Steps]
### 2.1 Step One
### 2.2 Step Two

## 3. [Case Study]

## 4. [Data Support]
```

### 3. FAQ Module (200–300 words)

```markdown
## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: [Question]?
**A**: [Answer, 50–100 words]

### Q2: [Question]?
**A**: [Answer, 50–100 words]
```

---

## Content Requirements

### Data Support
- At least 3 data citations
- Data sources must be verifiable
- Format: Per [source], [data point]

### Keyword Layout
- Title contains core keyword
- Body keyword density: 2–3%
- Natural integration, no stuffing

### Internal Links
- 2–3 internal links
- Point to relevant product/service pages
- Diverse anchor text
