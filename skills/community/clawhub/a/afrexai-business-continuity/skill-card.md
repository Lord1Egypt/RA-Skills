## Description: <br>
Builds a practical business continuity and disaster recovery plan by mapping critical functions, recovery objectives, risks, recovery strategies, and communication paths. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Operations leads, CTOs, founders, and audit-preparation teams use this skill to draft a tailored BCP/DR plan for organizational review and testing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The planning workflow may collect sensitive business continuity details, vendor dependencies, incident roles, and contact information. <br>
Mitigation: Treat inputs and generated plans as sensitive business information, avoid credentials and unnecessary personal phone numbers, and store the final plan only in approved locations. <br>
Risk: Disaster recovery or failover tests based on the generated plan can disrupt production systems if run casually. <br>
Mitigation: Run DR, failover, and simulation testing only through normal change-control and approval processes. <br>
