## Description: <br>
Redesign paywall and upgrade screens to maximize free-to-paid conversion. Use when a user wants to improve their SaaS upgrade screen, paywall copy, plan structure, pricing psychology, CTA copy, or overall upgrade flow. Produces a full conversion-optimized upgrade screen spec using the Upgrade Moment Method framework. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External SaaS founders, product managers, growth teams, and marketers use this skill to evaluate and redesign paywall or upgrade screens. It guides the agent to produce conversion-focused copy, plan structure recommendations, trigger placement strategy, risk reversal copy, CTA options, and a ready-to-implement upgrade screen specification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce marketing claims, testimonials, urgency language, guarantees, or savings statements that are not verified for the user's product. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated claims against real product terms, customer evidence, pricing, and legal requirements before publishing. <br>
Risk: Users may provide confidential pricing, customer, or conversion data while seeking tailored recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for the AI session, and redact sensitive customer or business details when possible. <br>
