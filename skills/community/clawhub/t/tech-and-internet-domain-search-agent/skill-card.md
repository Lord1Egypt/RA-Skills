## Description: <br>
AI agent for tech and internet domain search tasks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[urrrich](https://clawhub.ai/user/urrrich) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and other external users use this skill to run structured technology and internet-domain research, save a research log, and produce a cited final report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Saved research logs and final reports may contain sensitive research topics or copied details. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill for non-sensitive web research unless storage and deletion of wiki documents are understood, and review saved documents before sharing. <br>
Risk: Web research can include stale, incomplete, or misleading claims from scraped pages. <br>
Mitigation: Verify key claims across authoritative sources and review citations in the final report before relying on the results. <br>
