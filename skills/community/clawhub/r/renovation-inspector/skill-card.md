## Description: <br>
Renovation Inspector helps homeowners review renovation quotes and contracts, prepare construction-stage inspection checklists, evaluate waterproofing or tile issues from supplied evidence, and draft negotiation language or supplemental agreement clauses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dreammer2408](https://clawhub.ai/user/dreammer2408) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External homeowners and renovation clients use this skill to audit contractor quotes, inspect renovation milestones, preserve evidence, respond to negotiation pressure, and draft practical contract addenda. It is especially oriented toward residential renovation workflows that reference Chinese construction practices and local code verification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Contracts, quotes, renovation photos, and PDFs may contain personal or property details. <br>
Mitigation: Redact unnecessary personal details before sharing documents or images with an agent. <br>
Risk: Building codes, acceptable workmanship standards, and material prices can vary by city, supplier, and date. <br>
Mitigation: Verify local building codes, current market prices, and jurisdiction-specific requirements before relying on the guidance. <br>
Risk: Generated supplemental agreement clauses may be incomplete for a specific dispute or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated clauses as drafting aids and consult a qualified professional for legal advice or major disputes. <br>
