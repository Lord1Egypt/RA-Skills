## Description: <br>
Generates FAQ lists from complex medical policies or protocols for patient education materials and simplified explanations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AIPOCH-AI](https://clawhub.ai/user/AIPOCH-AI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Medical, policy, and education teams can use this skill to draft FAQ-style summaries from medical policies, protocols, or patient-facing documents. Its output should be reviewed before clinical or patient-facing use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated medical FAQs may be misleading because the packaged script returns canned answers instead of grounding each answer in the supplied document. <br>
Mitigation: Treat output as a draft and require a qualified reviewer to check every answer against the original source document before patient-facing or clinical use. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on simplified medical or protocol language for decisions that require professional judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for drafting and education support only, and keep source documents and clinical review as the authority. <br>
