## Description: <br>
Publish content once and sync WordPress, Substack, Medium, and LinkedIn automatically for multi-channel publishing, content distribution, and unified editorial workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketing teams, newsletter publishers, and B2B content teams use this skill to adapt, schedule, publish, and track content across WordPress, Substack, Medium, LinkedIn, Slack, and Google Drive workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad publishing and scheduling permissions could affect multiple third-party accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege tokens only for selected platforms and confirm each destination and scheduled time before publishing. <br>
Risk: Optional notification and backup integrations can expand where content or metadata is sent. <br>
Mitigation: Keep Slack and Google Drive integrations disabled unless needed and review what content is included before enabling them. <br>
Risk: Queued or scheduled cross-platform jobs may publish content later than the initial review moment. <br>
Mitigation: Verify that queued jobs and backups can be reviewed, canceled, or corrected before installation and routine use. <br>
