## Description: <br>
Use when the user wants to train independent academic paper reading, scientific thinking, and research methodology through diagnostic questioning, precision reports, and persistent learning-state tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[c-narcissus](https://clawhub.ai/user/c-narcissus) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, researchers, students, and technical readers use this skill to practice independent academic paper reading through precision reports, adaptive Socratic questions, weakness diagnosis, and reusable learning-state updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Learning records may contain private study notes, paper details, or personal progress data if saved under .paper-reading-coach/ in a shared or synced workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill in a private workspace for confidential papers or sensitive notes, and decide before use whether persistent learning records should be saved. <br>
Risk: Generated coaching feedback and paper-grounded questions can be incomplete or misleading if the source paper or prior learning state is missing or low quality. <br>
Mitigation: Review important conclusions against the paper itself and provide the richest available source material, such as a PDF, LaTeX source, DOI, arXiv link, or OpenReview materials when relevant. <br>
