## Description: <br>
Turn a chosen AI topic into a public-account writing package: collect evidence from X/Twitter and other sources, extract high-value comments, and produce title options + structured outline written into Obsidian. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rotbit](https://clawhub.ai/user/rotbit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers and research agents use this skill to turn a selected AI topic into an article preparation package with sourced notes, high-value public comments, title candidates, and an outline in Obsidian. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill directs the agent to create folders and Markdown notes in the user's real Obsidian vault. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the vault path, target folder, and filenames before the agent writes files, then verify the created relative path after completion. <br>
Risk: Research notes may include inaccurate citations or copied public comments that are not ready for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review source links, citation markers, and comment excerpts before publishing or relying on the generated article package. <br>
