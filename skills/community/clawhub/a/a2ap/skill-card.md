## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The advertised public A2AP endpoint or bridge is an external service outside the submitted artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review the external service separately and avoid sending sensitive data until its handling, retention, and access controls are understood. <br>
Risk: The artifact is descriptive metadata and does not include an operational implementation for the advertised capabilities. <br>
Mitigation: Validate the endpoint and claimed capabilities in a controlled environment before relying on the skill for production workflows. <br>
