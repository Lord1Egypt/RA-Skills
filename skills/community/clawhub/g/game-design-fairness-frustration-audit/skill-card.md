## Description: <br>
Audits game mechanics, failure loops, encounters, rewards, progression walls, and high-variance systems for perceived fairness, player frustration, and retry willingness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, developers, and product teams use this skill to diagnose why a game feature, failure loop, combat encounter, reward system, progression wall, or random mechanic feels unfair or makes players unwilling to retry. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Capability metadata includes crypto and purchase-related tags that the security evidence says appear inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as a Markdown-only game-design analysis aid and verify capability metadata before granting any special permissions. <br>
Risk: The skill may process confidential game plans, unreleased mechanics, or playtest observations supplied by a user. <br>
Mitigation: Use an approved environment for sensitive material and redact confidential details when that processing is not intended. <br>
