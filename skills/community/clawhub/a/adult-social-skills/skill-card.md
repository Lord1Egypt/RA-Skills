## Description: <br>
Building and maintaining social connections as an adult, for use when someone is lonely, has moved to a new city, wants to make friends, struggles in group settings, or needs to rebuild a social life. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to get practical guidance for making and maintaining adult friendships, including finding activity-based groups, joining conversations, hosting gatherings, and converting acquaintances into closer friends. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The artifact declares filesystem access even though the server security summary describes the skill as markdown-only social advice. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when comfortable with the platform permission model and avoid sharing sensitive personal details unless they are needed for the conversation. <br>
Risk: Social-skills guidance may be insufficient for users describing panic, avoidance, or inability to attend social situations. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate to appropriate anxiety support and recommend professional help before continuing with social skill-building. <br>
