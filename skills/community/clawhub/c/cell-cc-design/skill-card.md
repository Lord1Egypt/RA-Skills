## Description: <br>
High-fidelity HTML design and prototype skill for landing pages, slide decks, mobile mockups, interactive prototypes, dashboard explorations, design-direction advising, and design review work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cellinlab](https://clawhub.ai/user/cellinlab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and design-oriented agents use this skill to produce reviewable HTML-based visual artifacts, including landing pages, slide decks, mobile screens, dashboards, interactive prototypes, visual direction explorations, and design reviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated design files or exports may be shared before review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML, DESIGN.md, PDF, PPTX, and other artifacts before publishing or sharing. <br>
Risk: Brand-styled work can unintentionally imitate third-party brands too closely. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm rights or approval before using third-party brand assets or creating close brand imitations. <br>
Risk: Optional local export scripts can inspect local design files and produce derived artifacts. <br>
Mitigation: Run export helpers only in trusted workspaces and inspect outputs before distribution. <br>
