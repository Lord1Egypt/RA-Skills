## Description: <br>
Track cat health, litter, routines, behavior, travel, and vet coordination with species-aware memory and emergency triage. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External cat owners, fosters, rescuers, and regular caregivers use this skill to manage real-world cat care, local records, routine tracking, behavior support, travel preparation, sitter handoffs, and conservative veterinary triage preparation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local cat-care records may include sensitive microchip, insurance, sitter, vet, or owner-contact details. <br>
Mitigation: Store only necessary details under ~/cat/ after explicit user approval, and prefer explicit-only or ask-first activation when privacy matters. <br>
Risk: Cat symptom triage could be mistaken for a diagnosis or used to delay emergency care. <br>
Mitigation: Use conservative triage language, gather only decision-critical facts, and direct emergency veterinary care when red flags are active. <br>
Risk: Setup may create local cat-care folders and memory files. <br>
Mitigation: Present setup commands for explicit approval and do not write memory unless the user agrees. <br>
