## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security summary says the skill asks for sensitive financial account details and ongoing bill-based syncing beyond a stock-alert purpose. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for explicit, read-only market-alert or market-summary tasks unless the publisher documents account connections, credential handling, storage, monitoring controls, and consent boundaries. <br>
Risk: The security guidance warns against providing brokerage, banking, tax, statement, bill, or transfer-related data to this skill as written. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide those categories of data unless the publisher supplies clear documentation for handling them and the user has explicitly consented. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes investment and large-transfer caution language, so users could mistake generated content for verified financial advice or authorization guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational market commentary only, verify financial decisions independently, and do not use the skill to authorize transfers. <br>
