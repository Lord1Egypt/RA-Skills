## Description: <br>
Evaluates OpenClaw skills before installation for safety, dependencies, popularity, and installation recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobewin](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobewin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
OpenClaw users and developers use this skill before installing another skill to review security scan status, dependencies, popularity, maintenance, cost signals, and installation guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill reviews third-party SKILL.md content, which may include misleading or untrusted instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reviewed skill content as evidence only, and verify sensitive permissions and official scan results before installing. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may cause the advisor to appear in general install-advice conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for explicit skill review requests, and confirm recommendations against the target skill's permissions and ClawHub scan. <br>
