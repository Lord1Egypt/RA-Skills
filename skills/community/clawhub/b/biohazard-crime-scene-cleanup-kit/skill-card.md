## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-focused marketing materials for licensed biohazard and crime scene cleanup companies, including acquisition campaigns, service pages, review templates, referral letters, digital ads, local SEO plans, and structured data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Biohazard cleanup operators, restoration companies, and agencies use this skill to produce marketing copy and outreach assets that avoid unsupported claims about credentials, scene access, insurance, and Nevada-specific regulatory requirements. It is intended for businesses that already hold the required credentials and need compliant, human-reviewed marketing outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States, with Nevada and Clark County-specific legal and compliance framing <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing may contain Nevada and Clark County legal, credentialing, insurance, or platform compliance statements that are wrong for a specific operator or have changed since release. <br>
Mitigation: Before publishing, verify the operator's licenses, permits, credentials, insurance language, privacy obligations, platform rules, and current local law with qualified counsel. <br>
Risk: The kit is focused on Nevada and Clark County, so outputs may be unsuitable for operators in other jurisdictions without adaptation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat jurisdiction-specific claims as placeholders for local review and replace them with requirements verified for the operator's actual service area. <br>
Risk: The artifact is a prompt-only marketing kit, so it can guide copy generation but cannot confirm an operator's real-world compliance status. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only to market credentials and processes the operator can document independently. <br>
