## Description: <br>
A comprehensive AI agent skill for estate planning and will preparation. Helps you understand what a will covers, what happens without one, how to inventory your assets, what decisions you need to make, how to prepare for your attorney meeting, and how to keep your estate plan current as your life changes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to understand will preparation, organize estate-planning information, audit beneficiary designations, and prepare focused questions for a qualified estate planning attorney. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may discuss private financial, family, or estate information while using the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary details and avoid account numbers, passwords, Social Security numbers, and other secrets. <br>
Risk: Estate planning is jurisdiction-specific and the skill does not replace legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have final estate documents and decisions reviewed by a qualified attorney in the relevant jurisdiction. <br>
