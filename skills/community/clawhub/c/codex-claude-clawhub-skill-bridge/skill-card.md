## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated adapter notes could be acted on without review for workflows involving accounts, scripts, publishing, installs, browser state, private data, or money-related actions. <br>
Mitigation: Review each generated adapter before acting on it, and require explicit approval before publish, non-local install, hooks, package installers, service restarts, or workspace-wide rewrites. <br>
Risk: Source skill material may contain private names, paths, links, credentials, exports, screenshots, paid content, or unverified public claims. <br>
Mitigation: Replace sensitive or unsupported details with placeholders and keep public claims conservative before drafting or sharing adapter notes. <br>
Risk: Runtime-specific assumptions may be hidden inside the portable core and reduce compatibility across agent environments. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the canonical core runtime-neutral and place Codex, Claude Code, ClawHub, and OpenClaw details in separate adapter notes. <br>
