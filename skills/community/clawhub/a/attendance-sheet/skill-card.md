## Description: <br>
Generate professional attendance sheets in xlsx format from employee work information for HR, team management, and daily attendance tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gykdly](https://clawhub.ai/user/gykdly) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
HR staff, team managers, and operations employees use this skill to turn employee names, date ranges, and attendance statuses into formatted Excel attendance sheets for daily or monthly reporting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Attendance spreadsheets may contain personal or employment data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only attendance data you are authorized to process and store generated spreadsheets in an approved location. <br>
Risk: The generator writes to the requested output path. <br>
Mitigation: Choose the output path carefully to avoid overwriting an existing file. <br>
Risk: The script depends on the openpyxl Python package. <br>
Mitigation: Install dependencies only from trusted Python package sources. <br>
