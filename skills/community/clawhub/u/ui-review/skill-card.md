## Description: <br>
Run a structured UI/UX heuristic review based on Nielsen's 10 usability principles for screenshots, mockups, wireframes, product pages, app screens, and design comps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Dawen-Ye](https://clawhub.ai/user/Dawen-Ye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, designers, developers, and reviewers use this skill to evaluate UI screenshots or design comps against Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics and receive prioritized, actionable recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided screenshots or product context may include unreleased designs, customer data, internal metrics, or proprietary workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Share only screenshots and context that are appropriate for the reviewing agent to analyze, and redact sensitive information before use. <br>
Risk: Usability findings may rely on visible screenshot evidence and stated assumptions rather than the full product flow. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the review as decision support and validate recommendations against user research, analytics, accessibility checks, and product constraints before implementation. <br>
