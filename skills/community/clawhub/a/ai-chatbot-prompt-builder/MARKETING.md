# Marketing & Revenue Strategy: AI Chatbot Prompt & Persona Builder

## ClawHub Marketplace Listing

**Title:** AI Chatbot Prompt & Persona Builder — System Prompts, Guardrails & FAQ Training Data

**Tagline:** Deploy a production-ready AI chatbot in 30 minutes. System prompt, persona, guardrails, and 50-pair training dataset included.

**Description:**
The AI chatbot market is growing 23% per year. Every business is deploying one. Most fail not because the technology is bad — but because the prompts are generic, the guardrails are missing, and the bot has no coherent personality. This skill solves all four failure modes.

Four prompts generate everything: a complete system prompt (role, knowledge, rules, response format, escalation triggers), a persona character sheet with signature phrases and brand voice guide, a guardrails document with topic boundaries and sensitive question playbooks, and 50 Q&A training pairs in JSONL format ready for fine-tuning or RAG.

Works with any platform: ChatGPT, Claude, Intercom Fin, Tidio, Crisp, Voiceflow, or a custom API.

**What you get:**
- System prompt (500-800 words): role, knowledge base, DO/DON'T pairs, response format, 3 sample templates
- Persona sheet: name, archetype, backstory, 10 signature phrases, 10 anti-patterns, 5 demo exchanges
- Guardrails: 10-15 rules, topic boundary matrix, 5 deflection scripts, jailbreak resistance, escalation matrix
- FAQ training data: 50 JSONL pairs, 10 multi-turn conversations, negative examples, metadata tags
