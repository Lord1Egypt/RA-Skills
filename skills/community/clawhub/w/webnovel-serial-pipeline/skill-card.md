## Description: <br>
Build and publish a Quartz-hosted Korean web-novel serial, covering draft creation, cover conversion, episode Markdown, linting, and reviewed publishing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jeong-wooseok](https://clawhub.ai/user/jeong-wooseok) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Authors and operators of Quartz-hosted Korean web-novel sites use this skill to plan serial episodes, prepare draft Markdown, convert cover images to WebP, lint episode style, and publish reviewed drafts into a series folder. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Publishing commands can create files in a user-selected Quartz folder and rewrite the series index. <br>
Mitigation: Review the draft, confirm the Quartz and draft paths, and keep a backup of the target series folder before running publish commands. <br>
