## Description: <br>
智囊团式私董会多专家协作分析框架，支持2-5位专家私董会式动态讨论、正反辩论、红队审查、方案对比和综合决策判断。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[e2e5g](https://clawhub.ai/user/e2e5g) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to structure complex decisions, strategy questions, product reviews, management problems, study plans, and research analysis as a private-board style discussion among selected expert perspectives. It supports comparison, debate, critique, red-team review, synthesis, and action-oriented recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides advisory reasoning and may produce incorrect, incomplete, or overconfident guidance for high-stakes business, legal, medical, financial, or policy decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support, verify factual claims and assumptions, and require qualified human review before acting in regulated or high-stakes contexts. <br>
Risk: The artifact examples refer to expert-thinking while the release slug is brain-trust-private-board, which can create invocation ambiguity. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the intended skill is being invoked before relying on its output. <br>
