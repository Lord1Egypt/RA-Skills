## Description: <br>
Creates AI chatbot system prompts, personas, guardrails, and FAQ training datasets for business chatbot deployments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, agencies, and internal tools teams use this skill to draft chatbot system prompts, brand personas, guardrails, escalation rules, and FAQ training data. It supports business chatbot setup for customer support, pre-sales, onboarding, knowledge base, and service workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated prompts, personas, guardrails, or training examples may be inaccurate or unsuitable for high-stakes production contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs with a qualified human before production use, especially for legal, medical, financial, HR, mental-health, or customer-support contexts. <br>
Risk: Users may paste raw customer records, support tickets, secrets, or regulated data into prompts or datasets. <br>
Mitigation: Redact and approve sensitive information before using it as prompt input or training data. <br>
