## Description: <br>
CryptoRank helps an agent turn public CryptoRank market, funding, public sale, airdrop, and daily brief data into concise Chinese or English Web3 signal summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[0xcii](https://clawhub.ai/user/0xcii) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Use this skill when a user asks for CryptoRank-based market radar, funding radar, upcoming sale tracking, airdrop activity summaries, daily Web3 briefs, or raw public CryptoRank data formatted for downstream agent workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
No geographic restriction was identified in the provided evidence; users should follow their local laws and the terms of the referenced CryptoRank services. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may include airdrop task or claim links related to crypto opportunities. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as informational, verify links independently before connecting a wallet, and avoid entering wallet secrets or sensitive credentials. <br>
Risk: Market, funding, sale, and airdrop summaries can become stale or incomplete because they are fetched from public CryptoRank sources. <br>
Mitigation: Check the referenced CryptoRank pages before making financial, trading, or wallet-connection decisions. <br>
