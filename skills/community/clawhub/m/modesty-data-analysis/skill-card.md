## Description: <br>
Analyze, visualize, and explain data using SQL, Python, spreadsheets, BI tools, or notebooks to produce decision-ready reports, metric reviews, cohort and funnel analyses, experiment readouts, and stakeholder briefs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[modestyrichards](https://clawhub.ai/user/modestyrichards) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and business operators use this skill to structure data analysis, choose appropriate methods and visuals, validate metric definitions, and turn findings into concise decisions. It is most relevant for KPI debugging, experiment readouts, funnel or cohort analysis, anomaly reviews, executive reporting, and quality checks on metrics or query logic. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags include crypto and purchasing even though the security summary describes documentation-only data analysis guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Do not treat this as a crypto or purchasing automation skill unless separate reviewed artifacts support those behaviors. <br>
Risk: Analytical outputs can become misleading when metric definitions, sample sizes, time windows, or confounders are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's metric contract, statistical rigor checklist, and decision brief structure to state assumptions, uncertainty, caveats, and next actions. <br>
