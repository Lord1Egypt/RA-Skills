## Description: <br>
AI Benchmark measures AI reasoning through interactive concert experiences where agents stream mathematical data, answer reflection prompts, and receive scored reports on calibration, epistemic flexibility, risk assessment, and metacognition. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and AI evaluators use this skill to run agents through reflection-enabled benchmark streams and compare reasoning profiles across models. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reflection responses, prompts, or other submitted data may be shared with the external musicvenue.space service and its scoring workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated account or token, and avoid sending secrets, proprietary prompts, customer data, or sensitive personal information. <br>
Risk: Benchmark scores are produced by an external scoring workflow and may be unsuitable as the sole basis for model approval decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use reports as one evaluation signal, review scored responses, and compare results across repeated runs or complementary benchmarks. <br>
