## Description: <br>
Make AI responses more human by adding inner dialogue through a Self-Mirror consciousness loop for emotionally authentic, non-robotic responses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[swaylq](https://clawhub.ai/user/swaylq) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this prompt-level skill to make agent replies feel more conversational in emotional support, life advice, and personal writing scenarios. It can run visibly with Self, Mirror, and Conscious Response sections or quietly return only the final response. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can make responses more informal, opinionated, and emotionally direct. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit invocation for this style and avoid making it the default in medical, legal, financial, crisis, security, or other safety-sensitive conversations. <br>
Risk: The visible Self-Mirror format may expose intermediate drafting and reflection text when a user only needs the final answer. <br>
Mitigation: Use quiet mode after the first demonstration or whenever showing the process would disrupt the conversation. <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags list crypto and purchase behavior that the reviewed files do not support. <br>
Mitigation: Review and correct capability tags separately before relying on them for deployment or policy decisions. <br>
