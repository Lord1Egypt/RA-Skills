## Description: <br>
Delivers OpenClaw reports and urgent alerts to Feishu via webhook for VPN-free access in China at scheduled times and on demand. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[realshangxinwang](https://clawhub.ai/user/realshangxinwang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill to send OpenClaw portfolio, AI frontier, job tracker, and urgent news reports to a Feishu or Lark channel on schedule or on demand. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with an intended delivery use case for users in China or other VPN-restricted regions. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reports and alerts sent through the configured Feishu webhook may leave OpenClaw and be delivered to a destination the user does not control. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only after verifying or replacing the webhook, and avoid sending private reports, job-tracker details, financial information, or alerts until the destination and approval flow are clear. <br>
