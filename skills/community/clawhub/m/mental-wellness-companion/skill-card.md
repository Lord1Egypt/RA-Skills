## Description: <br>
Provides AI-guided mental wellness self-help support, including emotion recognition, CBT/DBT/ACT-informed exercises, mindfulness guidance, stress assessment, and sleep improvement suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for self-help emotional check-ins, stress reduction, mindfulness practice, sleep support, and structured reflection; it is not a substitute for clinical diagnosis or treatment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake self-help assessments or CBT and mindfulness suggestions for professional diagnosis or treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as self-help support only and direct users to professional or emergency help for severe distress, self-harm, or suicide risk. <br>
Risk: The skill may respond to broad mentions of stress, mood, sleep, or emotional difficulty. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses scoped to supportive wellness guidance and avoid clinical conclusions unless reviewed by qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Mental wellness interactions can involve sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Use local processing by default, avoid unnecessary retention or cloud upload, and disclose privacy expectations to users. <br>
