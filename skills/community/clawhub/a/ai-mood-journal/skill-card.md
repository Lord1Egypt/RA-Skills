## Description: <br>
Guides users through daily mood journaling, analyzes emotional trends, triggers, coping strategies, and body responses, and produces supportive insights and suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chenyuan35](https://clawhub.ai/user/chenyuan35) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to record moods, identify recurring emotional patterns, track triggers and coping effectiveness, and receive journaling-oriented suggestions. It should be treated as a reflective journaling aid rather than licensed therapy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may record and reuse sensitive mood, trigger, body-response, and coping-pattern information. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when the user is comfortable storing this information, avoid highly private or clinical details unless retention and deletion controls are clear, and avoid exposing journal outputs to unrelated workflows. <br>
Risk: The skill frames itself as a personal psychological counselor and may be mistaken for licensed clinical support. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as journaling reflections and suggestions, not diagnosis or therapy, and direct crisis or clinical concerns to qualified professionals or emergency resources. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate too broadly for emotional content and create a long-term mood profile without clear user intent. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when the user explicitly asks to record, analyze, or track emotions, and ask before maintaining or updating a long-term profile. <br>
