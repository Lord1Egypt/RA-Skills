## Description: <br>
Evaluate new marketplace opportunities by comparing platform fees, audience fit, competition density, fulfillment requirements, and expected ROI to build a prioritized expansion roadmap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators and marketplace teams use this skill to evaluate candidate sales channels, compare fees and operational fit, model ROI, and produce a phased expansion roadmap with go/no-go recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace fee rates, seller requirements, and compliance obligations can change after the reference material is reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Verify final fee rates, onboarding rules, and compliance requirements against official marketplace and regulatory sources before making launch decisions. <br>
Risk: A planning output could be mistaken for authorization to spend money, alter seller accounts, or commit inventory. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as planning guidance only and require explicit human approval before purchases, account changes, inventory transfers, or marketplace launches. <br>
Risk: Marketplace expansion analysis may involve sensitive business financials, product data, and seller-dashboard access. <br>
Mitigation: Protect financial inputs and credentials, provide seller-dashboard access only under separate explicit controls, and avoid exposing unnecessary confidential data to the agent. <br>
