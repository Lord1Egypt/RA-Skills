## Description: <br>
Chinese Four Pillars (BaZi 八字) chart interpretation from birth data, covering year, month, day, and hour pillars, Heavenly Stems, Earthly Branches, Five Elements, and high-level pattern reading with cultural and reflective framing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and assistants use this skill to structure BaZi-style readings, request the correct birth inputs, explain chart concepts, and present non-fatalistic themes for reflection. It is not intended for medical, legal, financial, or other professional advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: BaZi interpretations can be mistaken for certain predictions or professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame readings as cultural and reflective, avoid deterministic claims, and repeat that outputs are not medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice. <br>
Risk: Precise pillars, true solar time adjustments, and luck-cycle ranges may be inaccurate without a calendar engine or verified source chart. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions clearly and ask the user to provide calculator output or consult a qualified practitioner before interpreting exact timing. <br>
Risk: Birth details can be sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the birth information needed for the requested reading and caution users not to share details they consider private. <br>
