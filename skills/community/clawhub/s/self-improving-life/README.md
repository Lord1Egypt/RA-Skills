# Self-Improving Life

Holistic life audit and balance optimization skill across health, relationships, work, finances, growth, leisure, and personal routines.

## Use Cases

- Monthly or quarterly life wheel review.
- Routine reset after a major life transition.
- Burnout prevention and work-life balance review.
- Turning vague dissatisfaction into a 7-day experiment.

## Privacy

This skill may involve sensitive health, relationship, work, and finance reflections. Keep logs local and avoid storing details the user does not want retained.
