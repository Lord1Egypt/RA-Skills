# Cruise Line Quirks — Add-On Packages

Snapshot of pricing, exclusions, and gotchas for the 8 major cruise lines serving English-speaking EU/US customers. Verify on the cruise line's own site for booking-day accuracy. Last updated: 2026-04.

## Royal Caribbean

**Deluxe Beverage Package**
- Pre-cruise: typically $80–$95/day | Onboard: $105–$120/day
- Gratuity: 18% added
- Excludes: drinks priced over $15 on the menu
- Rule: **all adults 21+ in same stateroom must purchase**
- Includes: cocktails, beer, wine by glass, sodas, premium coffee, bottled water, fresh juice

**Refreshment Package**
- Pre-cruise: $30–$38/day | Onboard: $40–$45/day
- Includes: sodas, mocktails, premium coffees, bottled water, fresh juice
- No alcohol

**The Key (multi-perk bundle)**
- Pre-cruise: $24–$34/day depending on ship/itinerary
- Includes: priority boarding, lunch on embarkation day, surf+stream Wi-Fi, exclusive VOOM events
- Caveat: Wi-Fi alone costs $20–$25/day, so The Key often wins on math

**Specialty Dining Package**
- 3-night package: $99–$129
- 5-night package: $159–$199
- Unlimited package: $209–$259

**Surf+Stream Wi-Fi**
- Pre-cruise: $20–$25/device/day | Onboard: $25–$30/device/day

## Carnival Cruise Line

**Cheers! Beverage Program**
- Pre-cruise: $59.95–$64.95/day | Onboard: $69.95–$74.95/day
- Gratuity: 18% added
- Limit: 15 alcoholic drinks per 24 hours
- Excludes: drinks over $20
- Rule: all adults in stateroom must purchase

**Bottomless Bubbles** (kids' soda package)
- $9.50/day for guests under 21, $9.95/day for 21+
- Pays for itself at ~3 sodas/day

**Wi-Fi packages**
- Social: $9.95/day | Value: $14.95/day | Premium: $18.70/day
- Buy pre-cruise for ~25% off

## Norwegian Cruise Line (NCL)

**Free at Sea promo** complicates this — when active, drinks/Wi-Fi are "free" but gratuity (~$22/day for premium drinks) is charged on the included value. Math the gratuity carefully.

**Premium Beverage Package** (when not bundled)
- ~$109/day + 20% gratuity ≈ $131/day effective
- Excludes drinks over $15

**Premium Plus** (top tier)
- ~$138/day pre-cruise | $158/day onboard
- Includes top-shelf, wine bottles up to $130, specialty coffees

**Wi-Fi**
- Unlimited: $29.99/day | Premium: $34.99/day

## MSC Cruises

**Easy / Easy Plus / Premium Extra**
- Easy: $49–$54/day
- Easy Plus: $69–$75/day
- Premium Extra: $89–$99/day
- Gratuity: included in some EU bookings, added as 18% in US bookings — verify

**Note for US users**: MSC pricing fluctuates wildly with promos; cross-check with TA quote.

## Princess Cruises

**Plus Package** (recommended starting tier)
- $60/day per adult
- Includes: drinks up to $12, Wi-Fi (1 device), gratuities, OceanNow delivery, fitness classes
- Already includes daily gratuity, so EDC = $60 (no 18% on top)

**Premier Package**
- $80/day
- Adds: drinks up to $20, 2-device Wi-Fi, specialty dining (2 visits), photo package

**Note**: Plus and Premier already absorb gratuities and Wi-Fi, so the math heavily favors them for 7+ night sailings even at moderate consumption.

## Celebrity Cruises

**Classic Drinks Package**
- $89/day | Excludes drinks over $10

**Premium Drinks Package**
- $109/day | Excludes drinks over $17
- 20% gratuity added

**Always Included** fares already bundle drinks + Wi-Fi + tips — when active, do not buy separately. Cross-reference fare type before computing.

## Holland America

**Have It All** (bundle)
- ~$50/day adds drinks, Wi-Fi, specialty dining (1+ nights), shore excursion credit
- Often the best deal on HAL — don't price the components separately

**Signature Beverage Package**
- $69.99/day, 18% gratuity added
- $11 drink limit

## Disney Cruise Line

- Disney does NOT sell unlimited drink packages.
- Beer mug refill ($14.99 + $5/refill at most bars) is the closest thing.
- Wine packages: 3-bottle ($110), 5-bottle ($168), 7-bottle ($230)
- Internet: Pay-as-you-go MB pricing — typically buy a 100MB or 1000MB pass, NOT unlimited.

**Disney pricing strategy**: family-sailings rarely benefit from heavy package buying — direct users to per-drink budgeting.

## Universal Hidden Gotchas

1. **Same-stateroom rule** — all adults must buy on Royal Caribbean, Carnival, NCL.
2. **Already-included gratuity** — Princess Plus/Premier and HAL Have It All bundle gratuities; do not double-count.
3. **Pre-cruise vs onboard delta** is largest on Wi-Fi (avg 30% off), smallest on specialty dining (avg 5–10% off).
4. **Embarkation day pricing trap** — some cruise lines (Royal Caribbean, Celebrity) offer the best deal at the cruise terminal kiosk, NOT pre-cruise. Always recommend checking the kiosk before final pre-cruise purchase deadline.
5. **Free at Sea / Always Included** — when active, packages are essentially free. Verify the user's fare class first.
