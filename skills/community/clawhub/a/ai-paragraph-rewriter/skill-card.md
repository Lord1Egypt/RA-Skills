## Description: <br>
Rewrite paragraphs to sound natural and human-written, bypassing AI detectors (GPTZero, Turnitin, Originality.ai). <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and editors use this skill to rewrite paragraphs so they read more naturally while preserving meaning. It should not be used to hide AI assistance in schoolwork, hiring, compliance, plagiarism review, workplace disclosure, or other settings where that would mislead readers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to make AI-written text appear human-written to detector and authenticity-review systems. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using it where hiding AI assistance would mislead readers, violate disclosure rules, or undermine schoolwork, hiring, compliance, plagiarism review, or workplace authenticity checks. <br>
