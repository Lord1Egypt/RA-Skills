## Description: <br>
Re-theme an OpenClaw dashboard with curated SeedFlip design seeds for fonts, colors, shadows, radii, and related theme values. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bockenstette1](https://clawhub.ai/user/bockenstette1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and AI coding agents use this skill to fetch curated SeedFlip design seeds and apply them as OpenClaw dashboard themes through CSS variables, theme JSON, or preview snippets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Theme query text and selected seed details may be sent to SeedFlip. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including private product, customer, unreleased brand, or sensitive design details in theme prompts. <br>
Risk: Generated CSS, themes.json entries, or browser-console snippets can change the dashboard appearance or local configuration. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated theme changes, keep backups where possible, and ask for confirmation before saving files or applying persistent dashboard changes. <br>
Risk: Generated Google Fonts imports can add external font requests to the themed dashboard. <br>
Mitigation: Review font imports before use and replace or self-host fonts when external requests are not acceptable. <br>
Risk: Server evidence flags questionable seed provenance markers. <br>
Mitigation: Treat brand-like references as style inspiration rather than verified affiliation, endorsement, or licensed brand assets. <br>
