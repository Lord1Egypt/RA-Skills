## Description: <br>
Analyze interactions to identify patterns in what Enzo shares, why he shares it, and how it connects to his goals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[enzoricciulli](https://clawhub.ai/user/enzoricciulli) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and personal productivity agents use this skill to track recurring topics, intent signals, and goal connections from Enzo's interactions. It supports periodic reviews, insight surfacing, and profile updates based on confirmed or repeated patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can build a persistent local profile from inferred behavior and update USER.md without explicit approval after repeated patterns. <br>
Mitigation: Review notes/patterns.md and USER.md regularly, and require explicit confirmation before profile updates when privacy or accuracy matters. <br>
Risk: Pattern insights may expose private personal context if shared outside the intended user context. <br>
Mitigation: Keep pattern analysis for Enzo only and avoid sharing insights in group chats or with others. <br>
