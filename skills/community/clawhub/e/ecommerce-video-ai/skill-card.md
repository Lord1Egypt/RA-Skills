## Description: <br>
Ecommerce Video AI is an AI product video assistant for sellers who need scroll-stopping ecommerce videos that explain benefits fast, turning listings, demos, reviews, and product footage into platform-ready clips for Amazon, Shopify, TikTok Shop, and social commerce campaigns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce sellers and marketing teams use this skill to turn product photos, listings, reviews, and campaign briefs into video concepts, scripts, captions, CTAs, and platform-specific production guidance for commerce channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product photos, listings, customer reviews, or campaign plans may contain sensitive commercial information. <br>
Mitigation: Review and minimize inputs before sharing them with an AI workflow. <br>
Risk: The skill links to external MediaClaw sites for related or batch-production services. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate those external sites and any paid-service terms separately before using them. <br>
Risk: Generated ecommerce scripts, captions, CTAs, or demonstrations may be inaccurate or overstate product benefits. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated marketing content against product evidence, platform policies, and applicable advertising requirements before publication. <br>
