## Description: <br>
Comprehensive SEO suite combining audit, content planning, and strategic optimization for diagnosing SEO issues, creating content strategies, planning topic clusters, and prioritizing organic growth actions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
SEO practitioners, marketers, content strategists, and developers use this skill to run SEO audits, diagnose ranking issues, map search intent, plan topic clusters, and produce prioritized organic growth roadmaps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO audits and planning can involve user-provided analytics, rankings, search console reports, or business performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the reports or access needed for the specific audit, scope data to the requested task, and avoid pasting raw credentials. <br>
