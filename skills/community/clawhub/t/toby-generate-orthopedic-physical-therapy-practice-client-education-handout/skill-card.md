## Description: <br>
Create a polished explainer handout with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for a physical therapy practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobeyrebecca](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobeyrebecca) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Physical therapists, rehab clinic operators, and practice staff use this skill to draft patient-facing orthopedic physical therapy education handouts with visuals, FAQs, and next steps. Generated content should be reviewed by a qualified clinician before distribution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requests broad shell-command and local file-read capability beyond what its stated handout-generation purpose explains. <br>
Mitigation: Review permissions before installation and remove or narrow shell and file-read access unless required in the deployment environment. <br>
Risk: Generated orthopedic physical therapy education may contain inaccurate or inappropriate medical guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Require review by a qualified clinician before distributing content to patients or clients. <br>
Risk: Patient-identifying information could be exposed if included in prompts or local files processed by the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include patient-identifying information unless the environment is approved for that data and the workflow meets applicable privacy requirements. <br>
