## Description: <br>
Provides conceptual guidance for designing AI agent constitutions that define mandates, permissions, prohibitions, performance standards, escalation protocols, and succession plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, organizations, and AI agents use this skill to understand constitutional governance concepts and plan constitution structure before creating or managing constitutions through the Umnix web platform. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Constitutions are public by design, so sensitive data placed in them could be exposed. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include passwords, API keys, personal data, financial credentials, trade secrets, or sensitive internal procedures in constitutions. <br>
Risk: Conceptual governance guidance may be mistaken for an enforcement tool. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill as planning guidance and rely on the Umnix platform and human organization admins for constitution creation, amendment, review, and activation. <br>
Risk: Poorly reviewed constitutions could set unsafe autonomy, escalation, or transaction boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Require human organization administrators to review and approve any constitution or amendment before activation. <br>
