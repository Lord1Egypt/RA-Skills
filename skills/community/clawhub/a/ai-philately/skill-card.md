## Description: <br>
Ai Philately is a Chinese-language philately reference skill covering stamp history, classifications, printing methods, perforations, watermarks, errors, authentication, postal history, collecting, and auction context. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Collectors, researchers, and agents helping users with stamp questions use this skill to explain philately concepts, compare stamp types and defects, discuss authentication signals, and provide educational collecting or auction context. Pricing, investment, or auction information should be treated as educational rather than a current financial appraisal. <br>
