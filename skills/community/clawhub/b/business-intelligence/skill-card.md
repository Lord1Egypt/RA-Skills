## Description: <br>
Model business performance, define KPIs, and turn data into decision-ready dashboards, briefings, and operating cadences for teams and executives. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Operators, founders, product leaders, finance leaders, and analysts use this skill to define KPI contracts, metric trees, dashboard specifications, insight briefs, and decision cadences that turn business data into owned actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: KPI definitions, revenue metrics, business objectives, or decision notes may be sensitive if stored in local memory. <br>
Mitigation: Enable memory only when appropriate, keep entries concise, and avoid storing credentials, secrets, raw confidential data, or unrelated personal details. <br>
Risk: Incorrect KPI formulas, source assumptions, or stale data can make dashboards and briefs misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Use KPI contracts, formula versioning, source quality gates, and confidence levels before making high-confidence claims or recommendations. <br>
