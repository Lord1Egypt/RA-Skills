## Description: <br>
Film Lighting Bible is a Chinese-language reference skill that helps agents produce cinematic lighting, color temperature, shadow, and prompt guidance for AI image and video generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[beermanzz](https://clawhub.ai/user/beermanzz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, designers, and prompt-writing agents use this skill to select lighting direction, color temperature, shadow logic, and bilingual cinematography terms for AI-generated images or video. It is most useful when constructing or refining visual prompts that need a coherent cinematic lighting plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
