## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Green-energy engineering and market claims may be too general for real planning, investment, or safety-critical decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims, assumptions, site data, safety constraints, and current market details with qualified sources before relying on the guidance. <br>
Risk: The solar potential code example is a simplified educational estimate and may omit losses, shading, local weather, regulations, and equipment constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Use the calculator only for learning or rough estimation, and validate production designs with site-specific engineering tools and professional review. <br>
