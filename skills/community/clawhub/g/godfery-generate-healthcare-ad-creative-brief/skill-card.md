## Use Case: <br>
Healthcare operators, clinic teams, and patient educators use this skill to draft paid campaign creative briefs, visual concepts, and messaging hooks for healthcare promotions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Healthcare ad concepts may include inaccurate medical claims or miss advertising, legal, or compliance requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified reviewers check medical accuracy, advertising-platform rules, and legal or compliance obligations before use. <br>
Risk: Prompts or outputs could expose protected health information or sensitive patient data. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide protected health information or patient-identifying data; use anonymized campaign context. <br>
Risk: Image concepts for healthcare campaigns may require consent, rights review, or extra care for sensitive scenarios. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm consent, imagery rights, and suitability before using visuals in patient-facing materials. <br>
