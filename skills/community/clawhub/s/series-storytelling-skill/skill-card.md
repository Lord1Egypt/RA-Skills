## Description: <br>
Use this skill to turn a premise, video notes, a one-episode idea, or a long novel into a multi-line long-form series or season outline. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hshgogogo](https://clawhub.ai/user/hshgogogo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, story developers, and creative teams use this skill to expand premises, video notes, one-episode ideas, or long novels into season outlines, episode plans, character arcs, and multi-line story structures. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Because the skill is always enabled, it may influence responses for relevant storytelling or adaptation prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for creative writing and series-development work, and review whether its structure-first guidance is appropriate for the user request. <br>
Risk: Adaptation workflows can accidentally track an existing work too closely. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs to ensure they extract structural methods rather than reproducing existing plots, scripts, videos, or dialogue. <br>
