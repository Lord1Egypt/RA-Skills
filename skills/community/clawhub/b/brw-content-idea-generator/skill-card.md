## Description: <br>
Generates content ideas rooted in positioning for users who need post, blog, LinkedIn, or other content topics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[BrianRWagner](https://clawhub.ai/user/BrianRWagner) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, founders, and consultants use this skill to turn clear positioning into content ideas, hooks, core insights, and platform-fit recommendations for near-term and deeper content planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad content-brainstorming requests may activate opinionated marketing guidance when the user expected a different content workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's positioning, audience, and desired content channel before applying the frameworks. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes a publisher-provided promotional strategy-call link. <br>
Mitigation: Review the link against deployment and disclosure policies before sharing it externally. <br>
