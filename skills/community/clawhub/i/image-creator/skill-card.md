## Description: <br>
Curated image generation assistant covering 17 styles across 4 categories: character figures, scenes, products, and style transforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and creative teams use this skill to choose from curated image-generation styles, provide required references or descriptions, and receive generated image assets for characters, scenes, products, and style transformations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded reference images may include private, personal, or sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Use only images you have consent to process, and avoid uploading private or sensitive photos unless they are necessary for the request. <br>
Risk: City, landmark, weather, or brand requests may involve external lookup and can introduce incorrect or brand-sensitive details. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts and images before public or commercial use, especially for brand, landmark, weather, and advertising content. <br>
Risk: Broad activation terms can route ambiguous image-related requests into generation workflows. <br>
Mitigation: When user intent or required inputs are unclear, ask a concise clarification or present the available style choices before generating. <br>
