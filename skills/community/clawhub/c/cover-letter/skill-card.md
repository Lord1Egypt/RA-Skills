## Description: <br>
Cover Letter helps agents generate and review Chinese and English cover letters with JD matching, scoring diagnostics, role-specific templates, and batch generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xueyetianya](https://clawhub.ai/user/xueyetianya) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and career-support agents use this skill to draft Chinese or English cover letters, compare drafts with job descriptions, score cover-letter quality, and generate multiple role-specific variants. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The helper utility can store user-provided text and command history in a local cover-letter data directory. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive personal details unless local retention is acceptable, and clear the configured cover-letter data directory when retained entries are no longer needed. <br>
