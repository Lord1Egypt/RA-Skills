## Description: <br>
OpenClaw Creator Setup helps content creators check their OpenClaw environment, review Feishu authorization and installed skills, configure publishing platforms, and manage workflow templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rfdiosuao](https://clawhub.ai/user/rfdiosuao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and self-media operators use this skill to check creator-environment readiness, configure publishing platforms, and set up reusable content workflows in OpenClaw. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases such as status checks may invoke creator-environment checks unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants a creator-environment check before allowing account, authorization, or installed-skill status checks. <br>
