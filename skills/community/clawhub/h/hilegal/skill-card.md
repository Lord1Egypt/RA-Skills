## Description: <br>
HiLegal supports bilingual cross-border compliance consultation, foreign law research, corporate credit checks, and global price lookups for international business questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[netllc](https://clawhub.ai/user/netllc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and business teams use this skill to structure cross-border compliance research, legal-adjacent Q&A, and practical risk summaries across trade, data protection, labor, tax, and corporate background topics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill gives broad legal-adjacent compliance, sanctions, credit, tax, and pricing guidance that may be incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current law, sanctions, credit, and pricing data with primary sources or qualified counsel before acting. <br>
Risk: Corporate compliance queries may include confidential or sensitive company information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential company information unless the external lookup provider and platform protections are known and acceptable. <br>
