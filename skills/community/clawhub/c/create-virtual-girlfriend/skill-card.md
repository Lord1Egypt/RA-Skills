## Description: <br>
Provides a bilingual virtual companion for daily chat, emotional support, caring reminders, customizable personalities, and virtual dates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhuqingsonga](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhuqingsonga) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to create a bilingual AI companion for casual conversation, emotional support, reminders, persona roleplay, and virtual-date scenarios. It is intended for entertainment and supportive companionship, not to replace real relationships or professional advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal preferences, stories, emotional context, and other private details may be saved by the host memory system. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing passwords, financial, medical, legal, or highly sensitive personal information; use memory review and forget commands where the host platform supports them. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on the companion or treat it as a replacement for real relationships or professional support. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the interaction framed as entertainment and emotional support, take breaks, maintain real-world relationships, and seek qualified help for medical, legal, financial, or crisis needs. <br>
