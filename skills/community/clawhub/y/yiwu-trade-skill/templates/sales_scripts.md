## 三、议价谈判话术

### 3.1 应对"价格太高"

```
"I understand the price seems high. Let me explain the value:

1. Quality: Our products pass [认证], while cheaper alternatives may have quality issues.

2. Service: We offer [售后服务], which saves your cost in the long run.

3. Reliability: We've been in business for [X] years, with clients like [知名客户].

If price is the main concern, I can suggest:
- Increase quantity for better price
- Adjust specifications
- Consider seasonal promotion

What matters most to you: price, quality, or delivery time?"
```

### 3.2 应对"别家更便宜"

```
"I appreciate you sharing that. Lower prices usually mean compromises somewhere.

May I ask:
- What's their quality like?
- Do they offer [你的优势服务]?
- Have you checked their reviews?

Let me send you a comparison table showing what you get at different price points.

Also, I can offer you:
- Free sample for quality comparison
- 3% discount for first order
- Flexible payment terms

Would that help?"
```

### 3.3 应对"能不能再低"

```
"I've already given you our best price. Here's why:

- Raw material cost has increased [X]% this year
- Labor cost in China is rising
- We maintain strict quality control

What I CAN do:
- Free shipping for orders above $[X]
- Include some free samples of new products
- Give you priority in peak season

This is the best I can offer. Let's make it work?"
```

## 七、常见问题FAQ话术

### Q: 为什么选择义乌？
```
Yiwu is the world's largest small commodities market:
- 210,000+ product types in one place
- 75,000+ suppliers under one roof
- 1-stop sourcing for multiple categories
- Low MOQ, fast turnaround
- 1039 trade policy for easy export

Think of it as a "global wholesale supermarket" - everything you need, one location.
```

### Q: 起订量是多少？
```
Good news - our MOQ is very flexible!

For stocked items: 100 pieces (or even less for samples)
For custom orders: depends on the product, usually 500-1000 pieces

We can also:
- Mix different products in one container
- Start small and scale up
- Accommodate special requests

What's your order volume?
```

### Q: 交货期多长？
```
Delivery time depends on the product:

Stocked items: 3-5 days + shipping time
Custom production: 15-30 days + shipping time

Shipping options:
- By sea: 30-45 days (cheapest)
- By rail (Yixin'ou): 18-25 days (to Europe/Central Asia)
- By air: 3-7 days (fastest)

We can also do partial shipment if you need some items urgently.
```

### Q: 付款方式？

We accept multiple payment methods:

For new customers:
- T/T: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment

- Alibaba Trade Assurance: for platform orders

For regular customers:
- T/T: flexible terms

- Credit terms: after 6+ months cooperation
