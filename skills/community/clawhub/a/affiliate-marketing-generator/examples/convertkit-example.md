**Q: How does ConvertKit compare to Mailchimp for small lists?**
A: For lists under 1,000 subscribers, ConvertKit's free plan is actually less generous (300 vs Mailchimp's 500). But the usability difference is significant. ConvertKit's automations take 10 minutes to set up; Mailchimp's comparable workflow takes 40+.

**Q: Can I sell products with ConvertKit?**
A: Yes. The Creator plan includes digital product sales with a small transaction fee. Creator Pro has 0% transaction fees. You can sell ebooks, courses, templates, or paid newsletter subscriptions directly.

**Q: Is ConvertKit good for cold email or outreach?**
A: No. ConvertKit is strictly for opt-in email marketing. Sending to purchased lists will get your account suspended.

**Q: What's the difference between Creator and Creator Pro?**
A: Creator Pro adds newsletter referral tracking (your readers can earn rewards for referring subscribers), subscriber scoring, and advanced reporting. Worth it at 5K+ subscribers when you're optimizing for growth.
