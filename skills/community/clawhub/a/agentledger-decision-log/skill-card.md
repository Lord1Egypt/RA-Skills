## Description: <br>
AI-powered decision journal for solopreneurs that captures decisions with context, rationale, alternatives, expected outcomes, confidence, and later review notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Clawdssen](https://clawhub.ai/user/Clawdssen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Solopreneurs and independent operators use this skill to maintain a local decision journal, record meaningful business and strategic choices, and review outcomes over time for calibration and pattern analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Decision entries may contain sensitive business, client, financial, or strategy information because the skill is designed to create a persistent local decision record. <br>
Mitigation: Configure automatic triggers narrowly, review entries before saving or sharing them, and avoid recording credentials or sensitive private details unless intentionally required. <br>
Risk: Automated prompts may over-log routine or low-value decisions, making the journal noisy and harder to review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the artifact's threshold guidance to log only material decisions, such as meaningful spending, project starts or stops, client choices, hiring decisions, or choices the user explicitly flags for tracking. <br>
