## 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1: Program Architecture & Commission Structure
Design the full program: structure, tiers, commissions, payouts, rules, and cookie tracking windows.

**Output:**
- Program type (CPA, RevShare, Hybrid, Tiered)
- Commission rate recommendation with rationale (LTV-based formula)
- 3-tier partner system (Affiliate / Partner / Strategic Partner) with thresholds
- Cookie window logic (7/30/90-day recommendations by business type)
- Payout schedule and minimum threshold
- Top 10 program rules (what affiliates can/can't do)
- KPI dashboard: conversion rate targets, EPC benchmarks, partner activation rate

**Best for:** SaaS, e-commerce, courses, consulting, and service businesses

---

### Prompt 2: Partner Recruitment Kit
Generate outreach materials to recruit affiliates, referral partners, and strategic alliances.

**Output:**
- Ideal Partner Profile (IPP): 5 audience segments + why each converts
- 3 cold outreach emails (blogger/influencer, industry peer, complementary SaaS)
- LinkedIn DM sequence (3 messages: opener → value prop → ask)
- Partner program one-pager (the "join us" pitch deck equivalent)
- Partner application form questions (10 qualifying questions)
- Welcome email for approved partners (sets expectations, shares links)

**Best for:** Any business actively recruiting affiliates or referral partners

---

### Prompt 3: Partner Marketing Toolkit
Build the assets partners need to promote effectively — most programs fail because partners don't know what to say.

**Output:**
- 5 email swipe copy blocks (short, medium, long, story-based, promotional)
- 10 social post templates (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram captions)
- 3 promotional angles (problem-aware, solution-aware, product-aware)
- FAQ document for partners (answers 15 most common partner questions)
- Co-branding guidelines (logo usage, approved messaging, what NOT to say)
- Product comparison sheet (your product vs. 3 alternatives — partners use this in reviews)

**Best for:** Any stage of program launch; share directly in partner portal or onboarding email

---

### Prompt 4: Performance Report & Optimization Framework
Track what's working, identify top partners, and systematically improve program performance.

**Output:**
- Monthly partner performance report template (MRR from affiliate channel, top 10 partners, EPC trend)
- Partner health scorecard (0–100 with 5 dimensions: activity, conversion, compliance, engagement, growth)
- Tiered intervention playbook (active partners → nurture, dormant → re-engage, fraudulent → terminate)
- 90-day program optimization checklist (commission calibration, creative refresh, bonus campaigns)
- Partner newsletter template (monthly update to keep affiliates engaged)
- Annual program review framework (what to measure, what to adjust, when to expand)

**Best for:** Programs with 10+ active affiliates or 3+ months of data

---

## How to Use

1. Run **Prompt 1** to design your program architecture before recruiting anyone
2. Run **Prompt 2** to generate outreach and recruitment assets
3. Run **Prompt 3** to build your partner toolkit (add to portal or send in welcome kit)
4. Run **Prompt 4** after your first 30–90 days to optimize performance
