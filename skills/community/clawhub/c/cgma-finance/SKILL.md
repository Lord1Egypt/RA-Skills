## CGMA评分标准 / CGMA Scoring Criteria

### 四大原则详细评分维度 / 4 Principles Detailed Scoring

**1. 沟通产生影响（Communicate to Influence）**
- 财务信息是否驱动行动？/ Does financial info drive action?
- 是否建立有效的利益相关者沟通机制？/ Is an effective stakeholder communication mechanism established?
- 财务BP能力是否到位？/ Is finance BP capability in place?

**2. 信息具有相关性（Inform Decision Making）**
- 信息是否及时、准确、相关？/ Is info timely, accurate, relevant?
- 是否支持各级管理决策？/ Does it support management decisions at all levels?
- 情景规划和竞争分析能力是否具备？/ Are scenario planning & competitive analysis capabilities present?

**3. 分析创造可持续价值（Analyze to Create Value）**
- 是否通过深度分析创造长期价值？/ Does deep analysis create long-term value?
- 估值分析和投资组合管理是否到位？/ Are valuation analysis & portfolio management in place?
- M&A能力是否体系化？/ Is M&A capability systematized?

**4. 管理构建信任（Manage to Build Trust）**
- 财务治理是否透明、合规？/ Is financial governance transparent & compliant?
- 内部控制是否健全？/ Are internal controls sound?
- 税务合规和ESG报告是否完善？/ Are tax compliance & ESG reporting well-established?
