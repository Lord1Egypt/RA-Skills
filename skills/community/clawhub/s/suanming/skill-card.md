## Description: <br>
Suanming provides entertainment-style Liu Yao and Bagua fortune-telling readings from a user's birth date and optional birth time. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Ray1012](https://clawhub.ai/user/Ray1012) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for entertainment-oriented fortune-telling when they ask about Liu Yao, Bagua, destiny, luck, or birth-date analysis. The skill gathers birth date details, produces a hexagram-style personality and fortune reading, and includes an entertainment disclaimer. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat generic fortune-telling output as career, financial, relationship, or health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present readings as entertainment only, preserve the disclaimer, and avoid relying on the output for consequential decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill can activate from broad fortune-telling phrases even when user intent is ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants an entertainment fortune-telling reading before collecting birth date or birth time details. <br>
