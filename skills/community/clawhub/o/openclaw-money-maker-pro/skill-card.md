## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks agents to store platform credentials for multiple services. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped, revocable tokens where possible, protect credentials files, and avoid committing credentials to source control. <br>
Risk: Recurring workflows could post publicly, submit work, claim bounties, publish skills, or change account state without clear approval gates. <br>
Mitigation: Require manual approval before account-affecting actions and constrain any automation to supervised runs. <br>
