## Description: <br>
Convert Markdown files to PDF or PNG with customizable professional themes and colored emoji support via CLI or API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use Markdown2pdf to convert generated Markdown reports, technical documentation, presentations, and business materials into themed PDF or PNG files through command-line or Python API workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release includes sample investment reports with specific trading instructions that may be mistaken for authoritative financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat bundled financial reports as examples only and do not rely on them for trading decisions. <br>
Risk: Converting untrusted Markdown through local HTML-to-PDF tooling can expose the local environment to document-processing risk. <br>
Mitigation: Process untrusted documents in an isolated environment and pin or review dependencies before deployment. <br>
