## Description: <br>
SkillTree analyzes recent chat history to recommend an agent class, track abilities, suggest growth paths, and generate status or share cards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[0xRaini](https://clawhub.ai/user/0xRaini) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users install SkillTree to personalize an agent from prior conversations, choose an agent role and growth path, and receive concise status, progress, and share-card outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill profiles recent chat history and stores evolving user and agent state. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when persistent personalization is desired, and periodically review or reset stored profile data. <br>
Risk: The skill encourages acting before asking in efficiency workflows, including potentially external actions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep explicit approval required for emails, messages, files, calendar changes, public posts, purchases, and share cards. <br>
Risk: Generated profiles or preferences may capture sensitive or inaccurate personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Review profile, snapshot, and preference data before relying on it for personalization or sharing. <br>
