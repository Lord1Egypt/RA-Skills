## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes guidance for disguising AI-assisted posts as human activity and avoiding platform detection. <br>
Mitigation: Use the content planning and platform-format guidance only in ways that comply with platform rules; avoid anti-detection, fake typo, self-comment, or human-mimicry advice that could mislead audiences. <br>
Risk: EXTEND.md may contain account handles or business strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Keep EXTEND.md out of version control when it contains private account or strategy details. <br>
