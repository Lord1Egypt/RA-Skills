Risk: Lead forms may collect names, phone numbers, vehicle interests, store preferences, or other customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Add clear privacy notices and contact-consent language, collect only necessary fields, restrict access to exports, and define retention and deletion rules. <br>
Risk: Campaigns may send customer or lead data through Eqxiu, CRM, DMS, CDP, marketing automation, or private-deployment integrations. <br>
Mitigation: Review Eqxiu platform terms and each integration's data flow, permissions, and security controls before transmitting customer data. <br>
