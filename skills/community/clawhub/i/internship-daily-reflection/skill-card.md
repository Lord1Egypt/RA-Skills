## Description: <br>
将实习中的零散记录、聊天片段和任务笔记整理成可直接粘贴的中文日报或进展汇报。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[npccxx](https://clawhub.ai/user/npccxx) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Interns, employees, and external users use this skill to turn scattered Chinese internship notes, chat fragments, task records, and learning notes into structured daily reports or progress updates for mentors, leaders, or fixed reporting systems. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided internship, company, customer, or chat records may contain confidential information that is included in the model prompt. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with an OpenClaw or model runtime approved for that data, and avoid pasting confidential records into unapproved runtimes. <br>
