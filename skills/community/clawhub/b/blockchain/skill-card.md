## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Adapting wallet or smart-contract examples into live systems can create financial loss if addresses, networks, approvals, signatures, or transaction effects are wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Verify addresses, networks, approvals, and transaction effects; use small test transactions and never expose seed phrases or private keys. <br>
Risk: Educational blockchain guidance may be misapplied as production, legal, investment, or protocol-specific advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a reference aid and have qualified reviewers validate production architecture, compliance obligations, and transaction workflows before deployment. <br>
