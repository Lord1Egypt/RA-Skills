## Description: <br>
Xungen generates playful Shang-dynasty ancestry-style identity cards from a user's surname or hometown, with confidence labels for evidence-based, inferred, and entertainment-only claims. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sumleo](https://clawhub.ai/user/sumleo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for surname-origin exploration, cultural-history entertainment, social icebreakers, and family-gathering discussion prompts. The skill maps a surname or hometown to Shang-dynasty clans, states, totems, official roles, and regions, while labeling each result by confidence level. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat playful ancestry-style interpretations as factual genealogy. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as entertainment and cultural commentary, and preserve the skill's confidence labels for evidence-based, inferred, and entertainment-only claims. <br>
Risk: Surname or hometown mentions may trigger identity-adjacent ancestry-style output unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer explicit /xungen activation when users want deliberate use. <br>
