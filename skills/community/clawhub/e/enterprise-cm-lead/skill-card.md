## Use Case: <br>
Employees, executives, change leads, and delivery teams use this skill to assess enterprise change readiness, diagnose adoption risks, create sponsor action plans, analyze stakeholder impact, and respond to resistance during cybersecurity, cloud, AI, compliance, and operating model initiatives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bash-capable skill execution is broader than necessary for advisory change-management text. <br>
Mitigation: Run only the included local Python commands you intend to use and review future updates for added network, file-write, or persistence behavior. <br>
Risk: Change-management recommendations may be incomplete when the user provides limited initiative, stakeholder, readiness, or adoption evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs with accountable sponsors and validate assumptions against current program evidence before using plans for enterprise decisions. <br>
