## Description: <br>
Proof Engine captures business results across trading, agents, funnels, content, clients, products, media, and partnerships, then organizes them into proof items, dashboards, stories, content briefs, opportunity rankings, and deployment outputs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and external business users use this skill to turn operational results, revenue metrics, testimonials, and milestones into reusable proof assets for dashboards, content, funnels, outreach, and VSL scripts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill scans broad workspace locations that may contain sensitive client, financial, brand, revenue, content, or learning data. <br>
Mitigation: Review the scan scope before installation and avoid placing sensitive data in scanned directories unless it is intended for proof capture. <br>
Risk: When Telegram environment variables are configured, summaries can be sent outside the local workspace through the Telegram Bot API. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated Telegram bot and chat, test notification content first, and avoid including sensitive proof details in messages. <br>
Risk: Generated proof can be deployed into brand, voice, funnel, content, and outreach workflows, which could amplify incorrect or unverified claims. <br>
Mitigation: Keep scheduled syncs disabled until reviewed, verify proof items before deployment, and check cross-skill writes in an isolated workspace. <br>
