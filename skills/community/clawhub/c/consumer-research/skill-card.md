## Use Case: <br>
Product researchers, product managers, and market analysts use this skill to structure consumer insight work, define target audiences, design questionnaires, analyze needs, and produce consumer profile reports for product positioning decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Consumer research workflows may collect identifiable survey responses or sensitive demographic information. <br>
Mitigation: Collect consent before storing real responses, minimize personal data, and define retention and deletion practices before saving files locally. <br>
Risk: Web-based market research can introduce outdated, biased, or weakly sourced consumer insights. <br>
Mitigation: Use reputable sources, compare multiple references, record data sources, and review findings before using them for product positioning decisions. <br>
Risk: Poor sample design or questionnaire quality can produce misleading audience profiles. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the bundled research guidelines for sample sizing, quota control, attention checks, and data cleaning before reporting insights. <br>
