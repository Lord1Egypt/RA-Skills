## Description: <br>
Build private knowledge bases for AI-powered document Q&A with document uploads, smart chunking, vector retrieval, and source citations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huajianjiu000](https://clawhub.ai/user/huajianjiu000) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, support teams, educators, and developers use this skill to build searchable private knowledge bases from uploaded documents and answer questions with citations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded files, extracted text, metadata, and embeddings may contain confidential, regulated, legal, health, or proprietary information. <br>
Mitigation: Before use, define storage location, access controls, model/provider handling, retention, audit requirements, and whether deletion removes both source documents and indexes. <br>
