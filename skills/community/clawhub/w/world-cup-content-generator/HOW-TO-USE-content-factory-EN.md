# How to Use — World Cup 2026 Content Factory

Turn one match into a full content pack — a long-form article, a short-video script, a free prediction post, and headline options — for your football audience, in about two minutes. No writing grind.

---

## What you got
- `SKILL.md` — the engine (a single text file / prompt).
- This guide.

It works in **Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Codex, Cursor**, or any custom GPT / agent builder.

---

## Step 1 — Load the skill (one time)
Open your AI assistant, start a new chat, open `SKILL.md`, copy **everything** inside, paste it in, and send.

(Now the AI is your full-time football content editor.)

> Using Claude Code, Cursor, or a custom GPT? Add `SKILL.md` as a skill / system prompt instead of pasting — same result.

---

## Step 2 — Set up your channel (one time)
Send one short message:

> **Handle / brand:** [e.g., @YourFootballPage] — used for the byline
> **Football or soccer?** [pick the word your audience uses — US → "soccer," most other markets → "football"]

---

## Step 3 — Each match, give it the fixture
The skill auto-detects which mode you need, so just give it what you have:

- **Before kickoff (no score yet)** → you get a **PRE-MATCH pack** (preview + free prediction).
- **After the match (give the real final score + key events)** → you get a **POST-MATCH pack** (recap + reaction).

Either way, one match gets you a full pack:
1. A **long-form article** (1,200–1,800 words — ready for Medium / Substack / your blog, with an ad slot marked)
2. A **short-video script** (15–40s for TikTok / Reels / YouTube Shorts)
3. A **free prediction / discussion post** (X poll, also reusable for Facebook / Reddit / Discord)
4. **5 headline & thumbnail hooks**

---

## Step 4 — Pick, polish, publish (a couple of minutes)
- Glance at the facts (it won't invent scores, but always check).
- Tweak the wording to sound like you.
- Drop your ad or sponsor read into the **[ Sponsor / ad slot ]** marked in the article.
- Publish across your channels.

---

## Quick example

**Before the match, you send:**
> Match: [Team A] vs [Team B], group stage. Give me the full pre-match pack.
> Handle: @YourFootballPage. Audience says "football."

→ You get back a **preview article + short-video script + an X poll + 5 headlines.**

**After the final whistle, you send:**
> Final: [Team A] 2–1 [Team B]. Scorers: [names]. Now the recap pack.

→ You get back a **recap article + short-video script + a reaction post + 5 headlines.**

---

## Tips for the best results
- **Feed the real score and events** for recaps — the skill won't make them up.
- **One match at a time** for a clean pack; to batch a matchday, paste fixtures one after another.
- **Use the ad slot.** The article marks one natural spot for your sponsor or ad — that's your monetization built in.
- **Lean on the free prediction post.** It's a betting-free engagement game — one of the fastest ways to grow and keep a community during the tournament.
- **Repurpose everything:** article → Medium/Substack/blog; script → TikTok/Reels/Shorts; poll → X/Facebook/Reddit/Discord. One match, every channel covered.

---

## Built-in guardrails
- Real facts only — never invents scores, players, or events.
- No betting or gambling content.
- Won't fake official FIFA / World Cup affiliation or use protected logos — keep references generic ("last night's game").
- Disclose ads or partnerships (e.g., #ad) where relevant.

That's everything. Now go cover the tournament like a newsroom of one. ⚽
