## Description: <br>
Email Design helps agents create email marketing layouts, subject lines, templates, and deliverability guidance for welcome, promotional, transactional, and newsletter emails. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[okaris](https://clawhub.ai/user/okaris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, and developers use this skill to draft email campaign structures, subject lines, responsive templates, CTA patterns, and deliverability checklists. The skill can also provide shell commands for generating email header visuals through the inference.sh CLI. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes commands that install and use the third-party inference.sh CLI for email visual generation. <br>
Mitigation: Review the installer before use or follow the checksum-verified manual install path, and run login or image-generation commands only when the user accepts that third-party CLI. <br>
