## Description: <br>
Generates draft construction bid materials, including bid letters, technical proposals, commercial proposals, quotation sheets, and qualification document outlines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Construction companies, bid specialists, project managers, and bid-training users use this skill to draft and organize construction tender packages from project, qualification, schedule, and pricing inputs. Outputs should be treated as draft bid paperwork that requires legal, commercial, engineering, and bid-team review before submission. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft bid documents may contain incorrect figures, credentials, compliance statements, or project commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified legal, commercial, engineering, and bid staff verify all generated content before submission. <br>
Risk: Bid inputs may include confidential company, pricing, identity, qualification, or project documents. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the information needed to draft the bid and avoid providing unnecessary confidential materials. <br>
