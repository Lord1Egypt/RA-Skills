## Description: <br>
Ai Archery provides archery guidance for bow selection, shooting technique, equipment tuning, training, venue etiquette, competition formats, and safety. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and archery learners use this skill to ask for coaching-style guidance on recurve, compound, traditional, and barebow archery, including training plans, equipment setup, competition preparation, and safe range practice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Archery safety, injury prevention, hunting, or equipment advice may be incomplete or unsuitable for a user's body, range, equipment, or local rules. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as informational and defer to qualified instructors, range rules, local laws, equipment manuals, and medical professionals where applicable. <br>
Risk: Technique or tuning guidance can be misapplied without in-person observation, especially for draw weight, release form, and injury symptoms. <br>
Mitigation: Use the guidance as a starting point, progress gradually, stop when pain or unsafe conditions occur, and seek hands-on review from a qualified coach. <br>
