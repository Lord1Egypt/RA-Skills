## Description: <br>
Practical weather observation and environmental awareness skills for outdoor conditions, weather-dependent decisions without phone access, and environmental situational awareness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Outdoor workers, hikers, event planners, and other users spending time outside use this skill to interpret clouds, wind, pressure changes, lightning risk, heat and cold exposure, and cancellation thresholds. It helps an agent provide practical preparation and safety guidance when app access, forecasts, or local connectivity may be limited. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Weather and exposure guidance may be used for safety-sensitive outdoor decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present advice as preparation and situational awareness, prioritize emergency services for severe symptoms or immediate danger, and encourage users to combine sky observations with official alerts when available. <br>
Risk: Optional seasonal reminders or weather-preparation prompts could become unwanted automation. <br>
Mitigation: Keep reminders opt-in and avoid automatic outreach unless the user has clearly requested it. <br>
Risk: Location can be sensitive when used for outdoor condition briefings. <br>
Mitigation: Request only the location detail needed for the task and avoid storing precise location unless necessary. <br>
