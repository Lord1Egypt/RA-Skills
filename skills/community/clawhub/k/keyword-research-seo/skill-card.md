## Description: <br>
Guides SEO keyword research by helping agents find target keywords, assess keyword difficulty, analyze search intent, evaluate long-tail opportunities, and build topical maps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
SEO practitioners, marketers, content teams, and developers use this skill to research keyword opportunities, classify search intent, map keywords to pages, and prepare prioritized SEO content reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use project-context files and keyword, competitor, analytics, or SEO-tool data provided during a task. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only data approved for the SEO task and review generated reports before sharing or implementation. <br>
Risk: Keyword priorities and search-intent recommendations can be inaccurate when source data is stale, incomplete, or not validated against current SERPs. <br>
Mitigation: Validate high-impact recommendations with current SEO tools, analytics, and manual SERP review before execution. <br>
