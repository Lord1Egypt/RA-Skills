## Description: <br>
Learn to cook real meals from basic ingredients on a tight budget, using meal plans, foundational techniques, and a pantry system for people who never learned to cook or rely on takeout. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to help beginners learn practical, low-cost home cooking from pantry setup through weekly meal planning. It is aimed at people relying on takeout, living on tight food budgets, or starting to cook independently. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Budget estimates, nutrition suggestions, and food safety details may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a user's location, diet, health status, or ingredients. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as general cooking reference material and verify current food safety, medical, nutrition, and local cost information when those details matter. <br>
