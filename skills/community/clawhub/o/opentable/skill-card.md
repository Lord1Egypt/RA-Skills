## Description: <br>
Guide OpenTable availability, booking flows, and guest messaging with conversion-focused listing, pacing, and incident response playbooks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Restaurant operators and hospitality teams use this skill to plan OpenTable availability, booking pacing, listing improvements, guest messaging, and incident recovery while keeping reservation changes tied to measurable service goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Operational recommendations could expose sensitive guest details if raw reservation data is copied into long-lived notes. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering credentials or unnecessary sensitive guest details, and store only decision-oriented operational trends. <br>
Risk: Reservation availability, pacing, or messaging changes could harm service quality if applied without business approval or capacity checks. <br>
Mitigation: Keep real reservation changes under normal business approval controls and validate capacity constraints before changing availability or policies. <br>
