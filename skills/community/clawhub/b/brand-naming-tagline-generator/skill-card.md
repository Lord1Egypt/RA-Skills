## Description: <br>
Generates structured brand names, taglines, brand voice guides, and elevator pitches for startups, freelancers, side hustles, agencies, and small businesses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, freelancers, agencies, and small business operators use this skill to turn business details into candidate names, tagline options, voice guidance, and short-form pitch materials. It is intended for branding ideation and marketing draft generation, with separate checks for domains, trademarks, and business suitability. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter confidential client or proprietary business details into an LLM provider while running the prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use an LLM provider you trust and avoid sensitive business details unless sharing them is appropriate. <br>
Risk: Domain and trademark suggestions may be incomplete or unsuitable for a final naming decision. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify domain availability and trademark status before adopting a name. <br>
Risk: The artifact describes paid tiers and a done-for-you offer. <br>
Mitigation: Treat paid upgrades and done-for-you services as optional purchases that require explicit user authorization. <br>
