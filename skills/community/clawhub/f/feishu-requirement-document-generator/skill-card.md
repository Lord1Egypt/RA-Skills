## Description: <br>
从飞书文档、妙记、会议纪要或粘贴文本中提取客户需求，并整理生成结构化需求文档和飞书文档。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, consultants, and product or business analysts use this skill to turn customer communications, Feishu documents, meeting notes, and pasted records into BRD, PRD, TRD, or SRS-style requirement documents. It can create a new Feishu document, write the structured content, and return the document link. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive customer, meeting, or internal requirement content supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with content you are permitted to process, and review the generated document before treating it as an authoritative requirement record. <br>
Risk: Generated Feishu documents may be shared with unintended recipients or with excessive edit permission. <br>
Mitigation: Check the Feishu recipient list and access level before sharing, especially when edit permission is granted. <br>
