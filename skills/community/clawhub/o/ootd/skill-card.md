## Description: <br>
Your personal AI stylist. Suggests an Outfit Of The Day based on real-time weather and your personal style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[heytedward](https://clawhub.ai/user/heytedward) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals use this skill to get daily outfit guidance based on current weather, personal style notes, and optional wardrobe data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use personal style notes, wardrobe details, or current-location context when preparing outfit recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep USER.md and wardrobe.json limited to style and clothing details you are comfortable using, and provide a city manually when you do not want current-location context involved. <br>
Risk: Weather-based outfit suggestions can be unsuitable if weather data, style notes, or wardrobe data are missing or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Review the recommendation against current conditions and update style or wardrobe files before relying on the suggestion. <br>
