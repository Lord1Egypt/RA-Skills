## Description: <br>
Analyzes Polymarket BTC prediction markets to summarize active market sentiment, crowd consensus, and alignment with Orion on-chain signals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and market analysts use this skill to ask an agent for a concise read on BTC prediction-market sentiment and whether it appears aligned or divergent with Orion on-chain signals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading-signal output may be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable for financial decision-making. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational and verify Polymarket odds, BTC market data, and on-chain claims against primary sources before acting. <br>
Risk: The skill routes users toward paid Orion ACP offerings. <br>
Mitigation: Review the Orion/ACP agent identity, offering name, price, and payment approval flow before spending money. <br>
