## Description: <br>
A shared emotional memo for relationships - records emotional moments and gently reminds before old wounds are touched. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zj-zc](https://clawhub.ai/user/zj-zc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill as a supportive relationship notebook that records emotional moments, translates difficult feelings into warmer messages, reviews history, updates healing status, and notices recurring patterns without blame. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive relationship details may be saved locally in data/memo.json and reused in later conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Only record details the users are comfortable retaining, avoid secrets or safety-critical information, and periodically review or delete entries that should no longer be kept. <br>
Risk: Emotional support guidance may be insufficient for abuse, self-harm, persistent despair, or other safety concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's boundary guidance to suggest professional support and avoid diagnoses or taking sides. <br>
