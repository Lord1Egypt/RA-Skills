## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat entertainment-style lottery numbers or fortune language as predictive financial or gambling advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as entertainment only and avoid claiming improved odds or guaranteed outcomes. <br>
Risk: Personalization may use a real birthday or name supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to provide those fields only if they are comfortable sharing them with the local agent context. <br>
