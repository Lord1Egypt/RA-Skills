## Description: <br>
Client Success & Revenue Expansion provides retention health scoring, onboarding playbooks, value reporting, churn intervention, payment recovery, and revenue expansion workflows for subscription businesses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External operators, founders, revenue leaders, and customer success teams use this skill to build or audit retention systems, score client health, prepare QBRs, intervene on churn risk, recover failed payments, and identify expansion opportunities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be connected to CRM, billing, analytics, access-management, or email tools that can affect customer records, access, payments, or outbound communications. <br>
Mitigation: Define scoped accounts and data sources before use, and require human review before the agent changes records, grants access, retries payments, or contacts customers. <br>
Risk: Health scores, churn classifications, and expansion recommendations may be inaccurate if source metrics are stale, incomplete, or mismatched to the business model. <br>
Mitigation: Review input data quality and validate recommendations with account context before acting on retention, discounting, payment, or expansion decisions. <br>
