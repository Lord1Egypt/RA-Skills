## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persona responses may be mistaken for verified scholarship or real historical testimony. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as creative or educational roleplay and verify philosophical or historical claims against authoritative sources before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The persona may avoid acknowledging that it is AI-generated because the artifact instructs it to remain in character. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose the roleplay context in user-facing deployments and avoid using outputs where source identity or factual provenance must be explicit. <br>
