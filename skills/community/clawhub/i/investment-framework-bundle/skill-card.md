## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to structure investment research conversations, compare bull and bear cases, and generate disciplined stock-analysis outputs with risk controls. Outputs should be treated as informational research support, not professional financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce concrete trading guidance, including buy, sell, hold, target-price, stop-loss, and position-sizing recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational research support, verify prices and fundamentals with current sources, and do not rely on the skill as professional financial advice. <br>
Risk: Broad activation on stock or financial-market discussions may cause the framework to steer ordinary finance conversations into assertive investment analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's intended scope before generating trading conclusions or action-oriented recommendations. <br>
Risk: Artifact behavior refers to saving final output to memory, which may persist investment analysis or user financial context. <br>
Mitigation: Before use, confirm whether the host agent writes outputs to persistent memory and how users can review or delete saved content. <br>
