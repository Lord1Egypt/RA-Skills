# High-EQ Communication Learning Toolkit (高情商沟通学习工具包)

An **educational** multilingual toolkit for understanding communication frameworks from Eastern and Western traditions. Designed for **personal growth and self-awareness**, not for manipulation or deceptive persuasion.

> ⚠️ **Ethical Use Policy**: This toolkit is for learning and self-improvement only. All influence frameworks should be used with **transparency, informed consent, and respect for the other party's autonomy**. Never use these techniques to deceive, manipulate, or pressure others.

> 🌐 **11 Languages Supported**: English · 中文 · 日本語 · 한국어 · Bahasa Melayu · हिन्दी · العربية · Español · Deutsch · Français · Italiano

## Core Frameworks (核心框架)

### 1. Nonviolent Communication (NVC / 非暴力沟通)

Marshall Rosenberg's 4-step method:

| Step | English | 中文 | 日本語 | 한국어 | Bahasa Melayu |
|------|---------|------|--------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Observation | 观察 | 観察 | 관찰 | Pemerhatian |
| 2 | Feeling | 感受 | 感情 | 감정 | Perasaan |
| 3 | Need | 需要 | 必要 | 필요 | Keperluan |
| 4 | Request | 请求 | 要求 | 요청 | Permintaan |

### 2. Guiguzi Strategy (鬼谷子谋略)

| Tactic | Chinese | English |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 揣摩 | Chuai Mo | Assess & Empathize |
| 权谋 | Quan Mou | Weigh Options |
| 决策 | Jue Ce | Guide Decision |
| 捭阖 | Bai He | Open & Close |

### 3. Cialdini's Ethical Influence (西奥迪尼伦理影响力)

> 🛡️ **Guardrails**: These principles describe how influence works in practice. Ethical use requires transparency and mutual benefit. Avoid fabricated urgency, false scarcity, or deceptive social proof.

| Principle | English | 中文 | 日本語 |
|-----------|---------|------|--------|
| Reciprocity | Genuine give-first mindset | 真诚互惠 | 真の互恵 |
| Commitment | Consistency through small agreements | 一致性承诺 | 一貫性コミットメント |
| Social Proof | Authentic testimonials | 真实社会认同 | 真の社会的証明 |
| Authority | Demonstrated expertise | 实证权威 | 実証された権威 |
| Liking | Genuine rapport building | 真诚喜好 | 真の好意 |
| Scarcity | Real limited availability | 真实稀缺 | 真の希少性 |

### 4. Sun Tzu (孙子兵法)

| Principle | Chinese | English |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| 知彼知己 | Know yourself & enemy | Research first |
| 以正合以奇胜 | Strategy + surprise | Logic + creativity |
| 投之亡地然后存 | Understand pressure dynamics | Situational awareness |
| 上兵伐谋 | Win through understanding | Understanding > argument |

### 5. Cognitive Bias Awareness (认知偏差觉察)

> 🛡️ **Purpose**: Understanding these biases helps you **recognize when they are used on you** and make more informed decisions. This awareness is for **self-protection and ethical communication**, not for exploiting others.

| Bias | English | 中文 | 日本語 |
|------|---------|------|--------|
| Anchoring | How initial numbers shape perception | 锚定效应 | アンカリング |
| Loss Aversion | Why losses feel bigger than gains | 损失厌恶 | 損失回避 |
| Bandwagon | Why we follow the crowd | 从众效应 | バンドワゴン効果 |
| IKEA Effect | Why we overvalue what we build | 宜家效应 | IKEA効果 |

## Ethical Guidelines (伦理准则)

When using this toolkit, the agent MUST:

1. **Transparency**: Always disclose that you are applying communication frameworks. Never use influence techniques covertly.
2. **Informed Consent**: Before suggesting persuasion strategies, confirm the user wants to learn about ethical influence — not manipulation.
3. **Autonomy Respect**: Frame all suggestions as options, not prescriptions. The other party always has the right to say no.
4. **No Deception**: Never fabricate urgency, false scarcity, fake testimonials, or artificial social pressure.
5. **Mutual Benefit**: All communication strategies should aim for outcomes where both parties benefit.
