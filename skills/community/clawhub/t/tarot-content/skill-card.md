## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tarot or astrology content could be interpreted as medical, financial, legal, or other professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as reflection or entertainment, and avoid health, financial, legal, fear-inducing, or deterministic predictions. <br>
Risk: Using the optional ephemeris helper requires installing an external Python package. <br>
Mitigation: Install pyswisseph from a trusted package source, preferably inside a virtual environment, before running helper code. <br>
