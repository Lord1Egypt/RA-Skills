## Description: <br>
Rewrites rough goals, scattered notes, or vague requests into clear, actionable prompts with explicit objectives, constraints, output format, and labeled assumptions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Codex users and other agent operators use this skill to turn messy task ideas into concise prompts for writing, coding, planning, research, content generation, or delegation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User prompts may contain sensitive information that should not be shared with an agent. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including passwords, API keys, private documents, or other sensitive content unless that processing is acceptable for the environment. <br>
Risk: Low-risk assumptions added during polishing may still change the intended task. <br>
Mitigation: Review the polished prompt, especially the assumptions and follow-up questions, before using it for downstream work. <br>
