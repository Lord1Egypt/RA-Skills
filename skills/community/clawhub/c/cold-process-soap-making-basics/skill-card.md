## Description: <br>
Helps users calculate cold-process soap recipes, safety checks, curing schedules, batch logs, and barter templates from available fats, oils, water, and lye while leaving all physical handling to the human. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn local fats, oils, and lye into cold-process soap through agent-generated recipe math, safety checklists, curing reminders, quality logs, and trade-ready templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cold-process soap making involves caustic lye and can cause burns or inhalation hazards if measurements, protective equipment, ventilation, or supervision are inadequate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recipe and safety guidance against reputable soap-making and chemical-safety references, use accurate 0.1 g measurement, wear PPE, ensure ventilation, and do not physically mix lye unless safe working conditions are present. <br>
Risk: Incorrect curing or testing can leave soap unsafe for skin contact. <br>
Mitigation: Follow curing timelines, pH or zap-test checkpoints, batch logs, and conservative discard or rebatch guidance before use or trade. <br>
