This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, agencies, course creators, and brands use this skill to draft YouTube scripts, hooks, SEO descriptions, tags, thumbnail copy, and series plans optimized for retention. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated scripts may include inaccurate claims, misleading SEO language, or retention tactics that do not match the creator's brand or audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit scripts, titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnail copy before publication. <br>
Risk: Calls to action, affiliate links, or product claims in generated descriptions may require disclosure or compliance review. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm links, disclosures, and claims against the channel's policies and applicable platform or advertising rules. <br>
