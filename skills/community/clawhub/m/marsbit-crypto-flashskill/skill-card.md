## Description: <br>
Crypto-native Web3 news and flash intelligence from MarsBit through a hosted MCP endpoint for L1/L2 ecosystems, DeFi/CeFi, regulation, exchange flows, and market-moving events. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to retrieve and search MarsBit crypto/Web3 news, flash updates, channels, article details, and related coverage for market awareness and narrative tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto/Web3 searches and prompts are sent to MarsBit's hosted MCP endpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include private trading plans, wallet details, credentials, or confidential business information unless the endpoint is trusted for that use. <br>
Risk: News and flash updates may be time-sensitive, incomplete, or unsuitable as the sole basis for financial decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Check publication times and source details, and verify market-moving claims with additional sources before acting. <br>
