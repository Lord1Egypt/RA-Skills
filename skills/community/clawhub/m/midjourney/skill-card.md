## Description: <br>
AI image generation via Midjourney Discord bot: create images from prompts, upscale, create variations, blend images, describe images. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill as a practical reference for generating, varying, upscaling, blending, and describing images with the Midjourney Discord bot. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts or images submitted through Discord or Midjourney may contain confidential, personal, regulated, or proprietary information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive prompts or images unless the user has reviewed Discord and Midjourney processing, retention, visibility, and account settings. <br>
Risk: Generated images may have usage, licensing, pricing, or channel-visibility constraints outside the skill's guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Check current Midjourney terms, pricing, subscription status, and Discord channel visibility before using outputs in a project. <br>
Risk: The skill provides prompt guidance and command examples, so outdated Midjourney options can lead to failed or unexpected generations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm command syntax and model parameters against Midjourney's official documentation before relying on a workflow. <br>
