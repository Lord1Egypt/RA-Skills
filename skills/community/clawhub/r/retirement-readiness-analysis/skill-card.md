## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a CFP professional, financial advisor, or paraplanner needs to produce a SECURE 2.0-aware retirement readiness report for a single client household. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Financial planning professionals use this skill to draft a single-household retirement readiness analysis for licensed-advisor review, including funded ratio, income gap, withdrawal stress, SECURE 2.0 and tax-professional flags, scenario sensitivity, and a prioritized action checklist. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Client financial, tax, and household details may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize or mask PII, treat all client data as confidential, and avoid sending real client details to unnecessary external services. <br>
Risk: Retirement, tax, Social Security, SECURE 2.0, state-tax, or product-specific assumptions may be outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current IRS, SSA, SECURE 2.0, state tax, and product-specific details before using the draft for client action. <br>
Risk: The draft could be mistaken for final investment, tax, or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output only as a draft for licensed advisor review, with tax and estate items reviewed by the appropriate licensed professional. <br>
