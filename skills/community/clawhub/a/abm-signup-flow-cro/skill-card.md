## Description: <br>
Provides structured guidance for auditing and improving signup, registration, account creation, and trial activation flows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mariokarras](https://clawhub.ai/user/mariokarras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, growth, and UX teams use this skill to audit signup or registration funnels, identify conversion friction, and prioritize fixes and experiments for B2B, B2C, waitlist, and ecommerce account flows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read local product marketing context if present, which could contain sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Review any product marketing context file before use and keep secrets, credentials, and highly sensitive business data out of it. <br>
Risk: Conversion recommendations may be incorrect for a specific market, compliance requirement, or customer segment. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against analytics, user research, and controlled experiments before relying on them. <br>
