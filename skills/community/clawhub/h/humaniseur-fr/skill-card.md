## Description: <br>
Humaniseur Fr helps an agent rewrite French text that reads like AI output by detecting common AI-writing patterns while preserving meaning, voice, and register. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[samber](https://clawhub.ai/user/samber) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and agents working in French use this skill to identify AI-writing tells, rewrite problematic passages, preserve the intended register, and produce a final humanized version with a brief anti-AI review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad humanizing or rewriting requests and change tone or emphasis beyond the user's intended scope. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the text, audience, and register before relying on the rewrite, and review the final version for meaning preservation. <br>
Risk: The release metadata includes crypto and purchase capability tags that do not match the French writing-cleanup behavior described by the artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as a French rewriting aid, and review installation policy if only tightly scoped writing skills are allowed. <br>
