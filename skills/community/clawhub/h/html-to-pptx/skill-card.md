## Description: <br>
Convert multi-slide HTML presentations into styled PPTX files for easy sharing and distribution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[manshan-lin](https://clawhub.ai/user/manshan-lin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and presentation authors use this skill to turn LLM-generated HTML slide content into shareable PowerPoint presentations while preserving multi-slide structure, layout, and styling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided HTML may contain confidential slide content that becomes part of the generated PPTX. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide HTML that is appropriate to include in the output presentation, and review the PPTX before sharing. <br>
Risk: HTML-to-PPTX conversion may change layout or styling in ways that affect the final presentation. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated slides for visual fidelity before distribution. <br>
