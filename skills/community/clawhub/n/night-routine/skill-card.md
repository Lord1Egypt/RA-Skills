## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sleep habits, routine adherence, and next-day priorities can be personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the host application's local storage and deletion controls before using tracking features with sensitive data. <br>
Risk: Sleep preparation suggestions are general wellness guidance and may not fit medical or clinical sleep concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for routine planning and consult an appropriate professional for persistent or serious sleep issues. <br>
