## Description: <br>
Create academic literature review presentation slides (PPTX) from research papers or articles, with Chinese translations, preserved citations, figures, tables, and statistical details, and a blue academic visual style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, researchers, students, and presentation authors use this skill to convert academic papers or articles into Chinese-language literature review PPTX slide decks for journal clubs, group meetings, paper reviews, and literature reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill defaults to Chinese-language PPTX output and a specific blue academic slide style, which may not match requests for English, bilingual, source-language, or differently styled presentations. <br>
Mitigation: State the required language and slide style before using the skill when the default Chinese blue academic presentation format is not desired. <br>
Risk: Research-paper summaries can misstate citations, statistics, figures, tables, or conclusions if source details are not checked carefully. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated deck against the original paper, with particular attention to citations, statistical values, figures, tables, and hypothesis-support statements. <br>
