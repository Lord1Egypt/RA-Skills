## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese business email drafts for common scenarios using local templates and user-provided fields. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[freedompixels](https://clawhub.ai/user/freedompixels) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to draft structured Chinese business emails for outreach, follow-up, thanks, quotes, refusals, apologies, meeting invitations, weekly reports, farewell notes, and holiday greetings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated email drafts may contain incorrect tone, recipient details, or sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Review each draft for accuracy, tone, recipient, and sensitive details before sending. <br>
