## Description: <br>
Shop Freshippo (盒马鲜生) in safe decision-assist mode with fresh grocery selection, shopping-list planning, delivery-window strategy, price and freshness checks, and manual order handoff. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to compare Freshippo grocery options, plan fresh-food purchases, choose delivery timing, and prepare a manual shopping list. It keeps login, account changes, coupons, address selection, checkout, order submission, and payment under user control. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake shopping guidance for authorization to place orders or change account state. <br>
Mitigation: Keep checkout, payment, address choice, coupon use, delivery-slot reservation, and logged-in account actions under direct user control. <br>
Risk: Prices, stock, delivery slots, and freshness information can change before the user orders. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the user to manually verify final price, stock, freshness labels, coupons, address, delivery window, and payment details in the Freshippo app. <br>
