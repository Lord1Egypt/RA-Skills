## Use Case: <br>
External users, local business owners, marketers, and operators use this skill to create structured local GEO plans for websites, map listings, reviews, Q&A, structured data, and AI crawler signaling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate broadly for local-business search or discovery conversations and may use location, listing, and review details supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only addresses, service areas, listing URLs, and review details that are appropriate for local GEO planning, and review generated plans before publishing changes. <br>
