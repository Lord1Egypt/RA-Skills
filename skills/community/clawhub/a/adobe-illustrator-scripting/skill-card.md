## Use Case: <br>
Developers and designers use this skill to create, debug, and adapt Adobe Illustrator ExtendScript automation for document manipulation, batch exports, text replacement, color generation, and Illustrator DOM workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated or bundled Illustrator scripts can modify open artwork, replace text, create documents, or export files. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated JSX before execution, keep backups of important artwork, and verify input and output paths before running scripts. <br>
Risk: Startup Scripts and externally launched scripts can run automatically or outside the normal Illustrator menu flow. <br>
Mitigation: Only place scripts in Startup Scripts after they are fully trusted and understood, and prefer explicit sidecar files for required external inputs. <br>
