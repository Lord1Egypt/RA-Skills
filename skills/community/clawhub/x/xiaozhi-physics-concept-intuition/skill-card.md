## Description: <br>
Helps agents tutor physics concepts by building intuition through everyday analogies, imagined experiments, and formula meaning before formal derivation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, tutors, and learning agents use this skill to turn abstract middle-school physics concepts into intuitive explanations, controlled thought experiments, formula derivations, and verification questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
