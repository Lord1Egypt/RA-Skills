## About

This project systematically integrates histology, embryology, and congenital malformation knowledge, covering **124 key skills** across categories including histology fundamentals, epithelium and barriers, connective tissue, muscle and nerve, circulatory and lymphatic systems, blood and immunity, endocrinology, reproduction, urology, digestion, respiration, sensory organs, embryonic development, congenital malformations, and special topics.

**Target Audience**: Medical students, histology educators, researchers, clinical physicians

**Reference Textbook**: People's Medical Publishing House "Histology and Embryology" 10th Edition

**⚠️Risk⚠️**: This skill pack covers histological structure identification, embryonic development mechanisms, congenital malformation classification, and clinical-pathological correlations — content that may be misused as independent medical advice.

**Mitigation**: Use outputs solely as educational references or for clinician review. Verify recommendations against current official guidelines, local protocols, and qualified specialists.
