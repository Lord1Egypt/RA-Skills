## Description: <br>
Generates simulated Gaussian peak plots for chromatography, spectroscopy, or signal-visualization workflows, including single, cluster, merged, and negative peaks with annotations, CSV import/export, and customizable axes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ldxs001](https://clawhub.ai/user/ldxs001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, educators, and lab users can use this skill to configure simulated peak data, generate PNG peak plots, export CSV data, and print sampled Markdown tables for testing, teaching, demonstrations, or signal-visualization workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outputs may contain undisclosed deterministic markers in data or PNG pixels. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before use and remove the fixed-seed noise replacement and PNG pixel post-processing if that behavior is not acceptable. <br>
