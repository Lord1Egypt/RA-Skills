## Description: <br>
Nubaby prompt system for prompt augmentation, routers, dictionaries, dataset captions, prompt tags, compact prompts, video/storyboard prompt shaping, and structured visual tension expansion. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arthurlin1979](https://clawhub.ai/user/arthurlin1979) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, developers, and prompt engineers use this skill to expand short or vague image, video, dataset-caption, tag, and storyboard requests into structured prompts before downstream generation workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Saved prompt recipes can include confidential, proprietary, or untrusted content and may influence future outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Save only trusted non-confidential prompts, and periodically review or remove saved recipes from the skill knowledge JSON. <br>
Risk: Prompt expansion can produce misleading or unsuitable generation guidance if the input request is vague, sensitive, or age-ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts before downstream use and apply the skill's documented adult-only and non-explicit constraints for campus or academy-style aesthetics. <br>
