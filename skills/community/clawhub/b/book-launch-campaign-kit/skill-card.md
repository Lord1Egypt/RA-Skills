## Description: <br>
Create a reusable launch system for an English book or digital guide, including positioning, posters, promo videos, Amazon/Kindle conversion copy, people-holding-book ad concepts, polished campaign videos, and ready-to-publish launch packs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jinhuadeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/jinhuadeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Authors, publishers, and marketing teams use this skill to turn a book cover and core hook into a reusable launch campaign with posters, promo videos, marketplace copy, ad concepts, and ready-to-publish metadata. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The helper script creates folders and starter markdown files in the target path. <br>
Mitigation: Choose and review the target directory before running the scaffold command. <br>
Risk: Generated marketplace copy and ad materials may contain unsupported claims or fail platform requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review public copy against Amazon, Kindle, advertising, rights, and language requirements before publication. <br>
Risk: People-holding-book ad concepts could imply unauthorized celebrity endorsement if interpreted too literally. <br>
Mitigation: Use style and campaign energy without copying a real person's likeness, name, or endorsement unless legally authorized. <br>
