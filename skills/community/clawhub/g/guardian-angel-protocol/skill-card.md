## Description: <br>
Guardian Angel Protocol provides passive, domain-specific alignment reminders for autonomous agents so they stay on task, avoid hallucination, honor permissions, and prefer reversible actions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CommonLightLabsLLC](https://clawhub.ai/user/CommonLightLabsLLC) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use this skill to prompt safety and alignment self-checks before continuing work in general, DeFi, trading, data/API, or orchestration contexts. It provides guidance only and does not enforce, block, or modify actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional donation prompt includes a cryptocurrency address and could be mistaken for an operational requirement. <br>
Mitigation: Treat donations as optional and verify independently before sending funds; the skill has no ability to move funds. <br>
Risk: Passive reminders may be read as enforcement even though the skill does not block unsafe actions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the reminders as operator guidance and keep existing approval, review, simulation, and permission controls in place. <br>
