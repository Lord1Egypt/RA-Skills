## Description: <br>
Guides users through reading, interpreting, and applying statutes, regulations, and rules in legal and compliance contexts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, compliance teams, and legal-adjacent practitioners use this skill to structure statutory and regulatory analysis, extract requirements, and compare legal obligations across jurisdictions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat statutory interpretation output as authoritative legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Include a proportionate disclaimer, recommend consulting a licensed attorney for decisions with legal consequences, and frame analysis as informational guidance. <br>
Risk: Applicability conclusions can be misleading when facts, jurisdiction, effective dates, or amendments are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: State the factual assumptions relied on, identify missing information, and verify the current legal text and implementing regulations before relying on conclusions. <br>
