## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The listing and artifact behavior do not align, so users expecting a Beancount accounting helper may receive ZVT quant trading and backtesting guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the release as a ZVT quant/backtesting assistant unless the publisher aligns the listing, permissions, install steps, and runtime instructions. <br>
Risk: The artifact discusses broker, OAuth, paid-provider, and sensitive credential contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide broker, OAuth, paid-provider, or sensitive credentials unless the publisher and provider need are verified; prefer free or read-only providers where possible. <br>
Risk: Generated package-install, data-fetching, backtesting, or trading workflow commands may affect the local environment or financial decision-making. <br>
Mitigation: Review every command before execution, run in an isolated workspace, and validate strategy assumptions before using any output for live trading or reporting. <br>
