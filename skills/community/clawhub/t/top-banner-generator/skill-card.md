## Description: <br>
Guides agents to add, optimize, or audit top announcement bars and sticky banners for lead capture, promotions, urgency, trust, free shipping, launches, and related calls to action. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, designers, and developers use this skill to plan announcement banner messaging, CTA copy, placement, targeting, and design notes for ecommerce or product sites. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional project context files may influence the generated marketing recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review any .claude/project-context.md or .cursor/project-context.md content before use. <br>
Risk: Banner recommendations may include offer or campaign messaging that needs business review before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed copy, targeting, discounts, and urgency claims against current campaign requirements before deployment. <br>
