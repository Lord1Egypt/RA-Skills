## Description: <br>
Evaluates overseas distributors, agencies, resellers, and ecosystem partners using weighted scorecards, due-diligence checklists, commercial model comparisons, pilot plans, and exit guardrails. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, business development teams, channel managers, and international expansion leaders use this skill to assess overseas local partners, compare commercial models, plan due diligence, and design low-risk pilots before committing to a partnership. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat partnership assessment output as legal, financial, tax, investment, anti-corruption, or compliance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance and verify sensitive decisions with qualified professionals before sharing confidential data or signing agreements. <br>
Risk: Partner claims, market facts, local laws, platform policies, and operating assumptions may be incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Validate current facts, references, local requirements, and evidence gaps with reliable sources and local expertise before acting. <br>
Risk: Market adaptation notes can overgeneralize cultural or regional behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Frame cultural observations as hypotheses and require local validation before using them in partner evaluation or negotiation. <br>
