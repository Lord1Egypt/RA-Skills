## Description: <br>
Progressive insurance skill for comparing coverage, getting quotes, filing claims, and exploring savings tools such as Snapshot, Name Your Price, and multi-product bundling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to understand Progressive insurance products, quote and claims channels, available discounts, and important caveats before interacting directly with Progressive or a licensed agent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter personal, vehicle, policy, claims, or payment information into an unofficial channel. <br>
Mitigation: Use this as general insurance information. Before entering personal, vehicle, policy, claims, or payment information, verify that you are on Progressive's official website or app, calling an official number, or working with a licensed agent. <br>
Risk: Insurance details, discounts, rates, and availability can vary by state and individual circumstances. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as general information and confirm personalized terms directly with Progressive or a licensed agent. <br>
