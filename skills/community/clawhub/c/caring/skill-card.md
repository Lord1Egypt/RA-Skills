## Description: <br>
Apply a caring quality review to any work product - draft, plan, code, report, analysis, or reply - before it ships. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[minerva-care](https://clawhub.ai/user/minerva-care) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to apply a structured quality review to drafts, plans, code, reports, analyses, replies, and other deliverables before release. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad review requests may invoke this skill's intentionally blunt review style when a softer or narrower review was expected. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly only when direct, structured quality feedback is wanted, and review the generated feedback before sharing it with recipients. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce subjective quality judgments that may overstate issues or miss context outside the provided work product. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the review as advisory and compare it against the recipient's goals, audience, and missing context before making final changes. <br>
