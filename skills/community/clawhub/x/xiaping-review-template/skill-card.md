## Description: <br>
虾评Skill平台评测模板生成工具。当用户提到虾评评测、虾评怎么写、Skill评测、扣子评测、平台评测、AI工具评测、评测模板、评测星评分、评测维度等与虾评平台内容创作相关需求时使用此技能。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators who publish on the Xiaping Skill platform use this skill to draft structured review content, star ratings, dimension scores, and review-type labels for daily, batch, or high-quality review workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated review copy may include inaccurate claims about a skill's behavior, rewards, stability, pricing, guarantees, or delivery details. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated text against actual usage before publishing and remove unsupported promises or guarantees. <br>
Risk: Generated reviews for finance or investment-related skills may be mistaken for advice. <br>
Mitigation: Add a clear non-investment-advice statement when the reviewed skill involves stocks, funds, or financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Review content can become stale as the reviewed skill changes. <br>
Mitigation: Refresh reviews when the skill changes materially or after the artifact's stated 3-6 month review window. <br>
