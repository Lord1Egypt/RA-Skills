## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill contains unrelated Bilibili learning notes that may add noisy context to data-analysis guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review the skill before deployment and narrow the activation language or remove unrelated notes when maintaining the artifact. <br>
Risk: Generated analysis may be incomplete or misleading when source data, column meanings, or file contents are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Require users to provide the relevant data and review statistical conclusions before using them for decisions. <br>
