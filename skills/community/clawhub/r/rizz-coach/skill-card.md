## Description: <br>
AI texting and flirting coach that grades messages, improves texts, generates replies, simulates conversations, and creates shareable results. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[vajih](https://clawhub.ai/user/vajih) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to evaluate and improve dating or social text messages, generate tone-specific replies, practice conversations, and create screenshot-ready text summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pasted messages and relationship context may be sent to Anthropic for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets, phone numbers, real names, workplace-confidential details, or private conversations without permission. <br>
Risk: Outputs and share cards are designed to be easy to copy or post. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated text and share cards before sending or publishing them. <br>
