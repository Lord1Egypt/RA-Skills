## Description: <br>
Plan the optimal product reveal order for TikTok Live sessions based on price anchoring, audience warmup, and peak engagement timing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, creators, and live-commerce operators use this skill to turn a product list into a sequenced live shopping session plan with product roles, pacing, stock allocation, engagement beats, and script blocks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated sales scripts may include inaccurate stock, discount, availability, or bonus claims if the operator provides unverified inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm inventory, discounts, cart links, and bonuses before going live, and replace placeholders with truthful values. <br>
Risk: Urgency and scarcity language may conflict with platform policies or consumer-protection obligations if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the lineup and host script for platform policy compliance and applicable advertising or consumer-protection requirements before broadcast. <br>
