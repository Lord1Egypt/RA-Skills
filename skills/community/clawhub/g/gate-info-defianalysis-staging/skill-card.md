## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Yield, reserve, liquidation, and other DeFi market outputs may be mistaken for investment advice or guarantees. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as informational market data, avoid buy/sell advice, and state that APY, reserves, and liquidation levels can change. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on the Gate-Info MCP server for data availability and integrity. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the configured Gate-Info MCP server is trusted before installation and label unavailable tool results instead of fabricating missing data. <br>
