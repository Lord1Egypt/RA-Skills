## Description: <br>
TikTok Shop brand protection toolkit for detecting unauthorized sellers, counterfeit signals, price issues, affiliate abuse, and producing complaint templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Brand owners, marketplace operators, and e-commerce teams use this skill to review seller, listing, pricing, and review data for brand-protection concerns and to draft enforcement follow-up. Users should verify platform-specific rules before relying on generated templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release is marketed as TikTok Shop brand protection, while the reviewed code and templates are mostly Amazon-focused. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on generated enforcement guidance without checking it against the correct TikTok Shop workflow and platform rules. <br>
Risk: Generated complaint and cease-and-desist templates may be treated as legal or compliance documents. <br>
Mitigation: Have appropriate legal or compliance reviewers approve templates before sending them to sellers, platforms, or rights-protection channels. <br>
