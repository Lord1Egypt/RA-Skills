## Description: <br>
Provides woodworking guidance for wood selection, hand tools, joinery, machinery, finishing, furniture projects, and workshop setup. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External woodworking learners, DIY furniture makers, studio users, and joinery enthusiasts use this skill to plan projects, choose materials and tools, troubleshoot joinery, and understand shop setup and finishing options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Woodworking machinery, dust collection, and finishing advice can be unsafe if treated as real-time operational instruction. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for planning and learning only; follow manufacturer manuals, PPE requirements, ventilation guidance, and supervised instruction for actual shop work. <br>
Risk: Generated project, joinery, or material recommendations may be incorrect or incomplete for a specific tool, shop, or workpiece. <br>
Mitigation: Review dimensions, tool setup, material behavior, and safety steps before cutting, machining, finishing, or assembly. <br>
