## Description: <br>
Generate video, images, audio, and music using 40+ AI models via fal.ai. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, creators, and developers use this skill to generate or transform media with fal.ai models, including video, images, voiceovers, music, upscaling, background removal, and lip sync. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts and selected input media may be uploaded to fal.ai for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit confidential, personal, biometric, regulated, or proprietary media unless external processing is approved. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a fal.ai API key and can incur provider usage or charges. <br>
Mitigation: Use a revocable FAL_KEY and monitor fal.ai usage and billing. <br>
