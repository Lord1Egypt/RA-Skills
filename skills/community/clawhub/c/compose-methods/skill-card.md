## Description: <br>
A general content-composition methodology skill that guides agents through checklist-based and sample-based workflows for producing structured outputs such as documents, plans, code, reports, presentations, and business plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to choose and apply a structured content-production workflow: building from a component checklist, imitating a suitable sample, or combining both after user confirmation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sample-based workflows may expose private, copyrighted, or otherwise restricted examples if users provide unsuitable samples. <br>
Mitigation: Use only samples the user is allowed to share, avoid copying protected content directly, and review outputs for privacy and copyright concerns. <br>
Risk: This general methodology may be too broad for specialized legal, medical, financial, security, or other domain-specific work. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer a specialized skill for regulated or expert domains, or explicitly instruct the agent not to use this methodology for those tasks. <br>
