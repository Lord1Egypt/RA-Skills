## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad write, network, and agent-delegation permissions can affect files, sessions, or external communications. <br>
Mitigation: Install in a dedicated workspace, require explicit invocation, and confirm before any write, network, webhook/email, session, resource, budget, or personnel-related action. <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for business, HR, financial, and governance contexts where sensitive data may be exposed. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide sensitive HR, financial, or business data unless the receiving agents and storage locations are approved. <br>
Risk: Budget and personnel boundaries are not consistently scoped. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as proposals and require human approval for budget, personnel, resource, and high-risk governance decisions. <br>
