## Description: <br>
Generates personalized network engineering career roadmaps by assessing experience, skills, and goals to guide skill development and certification planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd](https://clawhub.ai/user/krishnakumarmahadevan-cmd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Network engineers, administrators, and career-development agents use this skill to turn career history, current skills, and target roles into a structured learning roadmap with certifications, milestones, and action items. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends personal career history, goals, and skill details to an external roadmap service without included privacy terms. <br>
Mitigation: Share only career information needed for roadmap generation, avoid confidential employer or network architecture details, and leave the optional userId unset when possible. <br>
