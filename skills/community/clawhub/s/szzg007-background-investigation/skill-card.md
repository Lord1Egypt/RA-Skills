### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill profiles people and may be used without sufficient authorization, consent, or proportionality. <br>
Mitigation: Install and run it only for lawful, authorized, and proportionate investigations with documented purpose and permitted data sources. <br>
Risk: Generated trust scores, risk assessments, or recommendations could affect consequential decisions about a person. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use the reports as the sole basis for hiring, credit, access, account, or reputational decisions; require human review and independent verification. <br>
Risk: Investigation reports may contain sensitive personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize collected data, store outputs only in access-controlled locations, and define retention and deletion rules before use. <br>
