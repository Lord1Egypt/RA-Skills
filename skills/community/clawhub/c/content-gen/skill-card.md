## Description: <br>
Multi-format content generation with built-in neural quality scoring -- every piece is scored against neuromarketing dimensions before delivery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[drivenautoplex1](https://clawhub.ai/user/drivenautoplex1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, real estate and mortgage teams, and content operators use this skill to draft social calendars, video scripts, SEO articles, listing copy, partnership materials, objection handling scripts, and newsletters with scored output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User prompts and notes may be sent to an external LLM when remote generation is used. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting secrets, regulated data, or confidential customer notes unless the provider setup is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: The neural score could be mistaken for independently verified marketing proof. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the score as drafting guidance and review final claims before publishing or delivering content. <br>
