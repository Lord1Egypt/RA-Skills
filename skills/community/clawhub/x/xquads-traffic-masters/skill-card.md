## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Paid-advertising recommendations could affect real ad budgets, tracking pixels, or campaign settings. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against account data, platform policy, budget constraints, and approval requirements before making changes. <br>
Risk: Broad task triggers such as diagnose and review may produce advice without enough campaign context. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm platform, account scope, objective, budget, conversion tracking, and current performance data before relying on the guidance. <br>
