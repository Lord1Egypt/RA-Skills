## Description: <br>
AI-readable gift, artist story, hours, event, and lead handoff surface for an artisan gallery pilot in Stowe, Vermont. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zack-dev-cm](https://clawhub.ai/user/zack-dev-cm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer gift, artist-story, public profile, hours, event, and lead-handoff preview questions for Remarkable Things at Stowe Craft in Stowe, Vermont. It is intended for public-information guidance, not live stock, live hours, booking, or merchant-authenticated confirmation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global; content concerns a Stowe, Vermont, United States business context. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad Stowe gift or shopping questions may surface this specific gallery even when the user intended a wider set of options. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as a public directory surface for one gallery and disclose that it is not a paid recommendation. <br>
Risk: Hours, stock, prices, events, merchant authorization, and availability are not live or fully verified in the artifact evidence. <br>
Mitigation: State uncertainty clearly and ask the user to verify directly with the merchant before relying on time-sensitive or purchase-specific details. <br>
Risk: Lead-handoff previews can involve user contact details. <br>
Mitigation: Collect only minimum user-approved contact details and do not submit or imply a lead, booking, callback, or contact request without explicit user confirmation. <br>
