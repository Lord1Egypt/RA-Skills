## Use Case: <br>
RF engineers and lab technicians use this skill for guidance on operating spectrum analyzers, vector network analyzers, power meters, signal generators, and related RF test accessories for calibration, measurement, troubleshooting, and safety checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: RF measurement guidance can be misapplied to live equipment, creating safety exposure or equipment-damage risk. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm procedures against official instrument manuals, lab safety rules, RF exposure limits, and equipment power ratings before applying the guidance. <br>
Risk: Instrument settings, calibration steps, and example limits may not match a specific lab setup or device under test. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified RF personnel verify calibration state, attenuation, input limits, and measurement assumptions for the specific instrument and DUT. <br>
