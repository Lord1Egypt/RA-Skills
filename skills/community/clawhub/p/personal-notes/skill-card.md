## Description: <br>
Acts as the user's note-taking and journaling assistant in the Notes topic. Captures thoughts, reflections, daily logs, random notes. Use when in Personal chat Notes topic, or when user mentions note, journal, diary, thought, reflection, log, record, or "write down". <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this agent skill as a personal notebook and diary assistant to record, find, review, and tag notes with timestamps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal notes are saved as plaintext in knowledge/personal/notes.md. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing passwords, secrets, health details, or highly sensitive diary entries unless workspace file access and backups are managed appropriately. <br>
Risk: Broad note-taking triggers can cause accidental capture of personal content. <br>
Mitigation: Be explicit when asking the agent to save or retrieve notes, and review new entries before relying on them. <br>
