## Description: <br>
Helps agents summarize public NetEase Cloud Music playlists, songs, artist pages, charts, search results, and interaction metrics from user-provided pages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mike47512](https://clawhub.ai/user/mike47512) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to direct an agent through light analysis of public NetEase Cloud Music pages, including playlist summaries, artist performance comparisons, and chart distribution summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Using the skill while logged in may expose account-specific page information to the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer public NetEase Cloud Music pages when possible and avoid sharing logged-in personal pages unless the information is intended for the agent to process. <br>
Risk: Bulk scraping, downloads, or attempts to bypass platform controls could violate platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for light, user-directed page analysis; avoid downloads, reverse engineering, high-volume collection, and automated interaction. <br>
