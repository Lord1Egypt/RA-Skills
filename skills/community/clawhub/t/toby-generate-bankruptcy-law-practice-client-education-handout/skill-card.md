## Description: <br>
Create a polished explainer handout with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for a bankruptcy law practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Bankruptcy attorneys and legal marketing teams use this skill to draft client-facing educational handouts with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps. It is intended for client education workflows and should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated bankruptcy-law education content may be inaccurate, incomplete, or mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified attorney review and approve the handout before sending it to clients or relying on it for legal guidance. <br>
Risk: Visuals or simplified FAQs may omit important client-specific constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Tailor the draft to the practice's jurisdiction, audience, and engagement terms before publication. <br>
