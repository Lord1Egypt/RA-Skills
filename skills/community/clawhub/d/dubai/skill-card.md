Risk: Static legal, immigration, tax, healthcare, LGBTQ+ safety, education, business setup, safety, and housing guidance may become outdated or may not fit a user's exact circumstances. <br>
Mitigation: Treat these sections as starting points and verify current requirements with official sources or qualified professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Users may rely on city guidance for consequential travel, relocation, employment, schooling, healthcare, or business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for orientation and planning support, then confirm requirements, costs, eligibility, and safety considerations with current authoritative sources. <br>
