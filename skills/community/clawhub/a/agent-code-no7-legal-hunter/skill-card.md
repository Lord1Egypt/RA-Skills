## Description: <br>
Agent Code No.7 Legal Hunter helps agents research Chinese laws, regulations, judicial interpretations, guiding cases, and case law to produce structured legal analysis and legal opinion outlines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[monarchmio](https://clawhub.ai/user/monarchmio) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Legal researchers, compliance teams, and agents use this skill to structure research on Chinese legal questions, retrieve relevant authorities, compare legal arguments, and draft legal research reports or opinion frameworks. Outputs are research aids and should not be treated as formal legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal research output may include outdated, incorrect, incomplete, or non-authoritative statutes, cases, citations, or conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify statutes, case citations, validity status, and conclusions against authoritative sources or consult a qualified lawyer before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the generated research report or opinion framework for formal legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the skill's disclaimer that research is for reference only and does not constitute a formal legal opinion. <br>
