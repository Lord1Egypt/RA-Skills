## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product, marketing, data, and engineering teams use this skill to draft A/B test plans with hypotheses, metrics, variants, sample-size assumptions, duration, implementation notes, and decision criteria. <br>

Risk: Generated sample-size assumptions, business metrics, or decision thresholds may be inappropriate for the user's product context. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated plan's sample-size assumptions, metrics, and thresholds with the responsible product, analytics, or experimentation owner before using it for product or revenue decisions. <br>
