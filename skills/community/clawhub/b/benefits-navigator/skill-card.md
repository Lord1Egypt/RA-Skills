## Description: <br>
Comprehensive guide to major U.S. government assistance programs including SNAP, Medicaid, unemployment, LIHEAP, TANF, WIC, Section 8, Lifeline, SSDI, SSI, and more, with eligibility, application process, processing time, and approval guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and assisting agents use this skill to screen for likely U.S. public benefits, understand program requirements, prepare application documents, track application status, and plan follow-up or recertification steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may involve sensitive benefits, income, household, disability, health, and application-status details. <br>
Mitigation: Keep screening in-session unless the user explicitly asks to save information, avoid storing Social Security numbers, bank details, medical specifics, or confirmation numbers, and delete tracker files when finished. <br>
Risk: Calendar reminders or tracker names could reveal private benefits activity. <br>
Mitigation: Use generic reminder names and confirm with the user before creating reminders or persistent files. <br>
