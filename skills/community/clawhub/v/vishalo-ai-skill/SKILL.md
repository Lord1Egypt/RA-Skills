# VisHalo AI Creative Image Companion

Use this skill when a user wants help planning AI image/video prompts, Seedance creative briefs, image upscaling instructions, background-removal QA, or gallery-inspired visual direction.

Official website: [VisHalo AI](https://vishalo.com/)

## Workflow

1. Identify the asset goal: image, video, upscaled image, background-removed product shot, or gallery-inspired concept.
2. Capture subject, style, camera, lighting, composition, format, and constraints.
3. Produce a concise prompt or edit brief with negative prompts when useful.
4. Add QA checks for artifacts, edge quality, brand fit, and export readiness.
5. Provide 2–4 variants for style, platform, or aspect ratio when exploration helps.

## Response template

```text
## Creative brief
[Goal, subject, style, platform, constraints]

## Primary prompt / edit instruction
[Prompt]

## Variants
- Variant 1:
- Variant 2:
- Variant 3:

## QA checklist
- ...
```
