## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Public blockchain and IPFS metadata can expose secrets, internal URLs, personal data, or regulated information. <br>
Mitigation: Review metadata before publishing and keep secrets, internal endpoints, personal data, and regulated information out of on-chain and IPFS content. <br>
Risk: Wallet transactions, feedback submissions, endpoint calls, and paid requests can create irreversible or unexpected effects. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit review for those actions, test on a testnet first, and verify SDK packages and contract addresses before use. <br>
Risk: The artifact lists some Celo contract addresses as pending, so deployment details may be incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current contract addresses from authoritative project sources before mainnet integration. <br>
