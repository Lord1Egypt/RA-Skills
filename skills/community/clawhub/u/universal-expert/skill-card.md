## Description: <br>
Universal Expert is a Chinese-language analysis skill for research-backed expert analysis, decision support, and information verification across domains. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[azazlf09](https://clawhub.ai/user/azazlf09) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill when they need deeper analysis of complex topics, with explicit source collection, confidence labeling, scenario analysis, and human-intervention prompts for high-stakes decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may perform web research or browser-assisted access before deep analyses, including workflows that could involve logged-in or sensitive-data contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with approved research tools, avoid unnecessary sensitive-data access, and require explicit user confirmation before account-based, third-party-upload, or high-permission browsing. <br>
Risk: The skill's source instructions are primarily Chinese, which may cause ambiguity for non-Chinese users reviewing expected behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify language expectations before relying on the skill for important decisions or governance review. <br>
