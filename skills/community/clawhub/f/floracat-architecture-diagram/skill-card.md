## Description: <br>
Create or revise standalone HTML/SVG architecture diagrams, runtime flow diagrams, sequence diagrams, and PPT-like technical visuals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, technical writers, and product teams use this skill to create or refine publication-ready architecture, runtime flow, and sequence diagrams as standalone HTML files with embedded SVG. It is especially suited for human-readable technical visuals with clear layout, precise arrows, and Chinese labels when needed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Architecture diagrams can reveal sensitive system design, dependencies, or internal implementation details when based on real systems. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the code, docs, or diagrams needed for the visual, and avoid including secrets or sensitive internal details unless your policy allows it. <br>
Risk: Generated or revised diagrams may contain incorrect relationships, ambiguous arrows, overlaps, or misleading hierarchy. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included review checklist to verify node meaning, arrow direction, spacing, overlap handling, readability, and publication fit before sharing. <br>
