## Description: <br>
Generates Dianping review drafts with rating suggestions, photo prompts, and tags for multiple business categories. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[laojun509](https://clawhub.ai/user/laojun509) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to draft Dianping review text, photo suggestions, and tags for restaurants, beauty services, entertainment venues, fitness businesses, and hotels. It should be used only for reviews grounded in real experiences, not to fabricate experiences, bulk-post reviews, or evade platform moderation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated review drafts could be used to fabricate consumer experiences, bulk-post reviews, or evade Dianping moderation. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only to draft reviews based on real visits, do not provide account credentials, and review each output before posting for accuracy and platform compliance. <br>
