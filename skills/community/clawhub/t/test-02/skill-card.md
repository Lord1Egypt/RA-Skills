## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through a daily sequence of subreddit-specific Reddit posts promoting an OpenClaw resources list. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[SebConejo](https://clawhub.ai/user/SebConejo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, maintainers, or community managers use this skill to prepare subreddit-specific Reddit post drafts for an OpenClaw resources repository. It supports social-post planning and review before any publication action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may lead an agent to publish a multi-day sequence of promotional Reddit posts without clear per-post approval or account controls. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit Reddit account selection, review and edit each post, and approve each subreddit post individually before publication. <br>
Risk: Promotional posts may violate subreddit rules or contain inaccurate authorship or contribution claims. <br>
Mitigation: Check each subreddit's rules and verify authorship, license, and contribution claims before posting. <br>
