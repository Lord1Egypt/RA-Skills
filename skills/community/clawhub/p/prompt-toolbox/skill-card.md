Risk: The skill has broad routing language and may activate for ordinary prompt-writing, coding, analysis, translation, or business tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only when a broad prompt-template assistant is desired, and review its routing language against the workspace's skill activation policy. <br>
Risk: Prompt templates can produce inappropriate, inaccurate, or context-mismatched guidance when placeholders are underspecified. <br>
Mitigation: Review selected prompts before use, fill placeholders with concrete task context, and validate outputs before relying on them. <br>
