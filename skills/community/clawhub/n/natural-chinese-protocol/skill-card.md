## Description: <br>
Applies a Natural Chinese Protocol for editing Chinese prose to reduce AI-like writing patterns while preserving the original meaning and context. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[garden-cosmos](https://clawhub.ai/user/garden-cosmos) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Chinese writers, editors, and agents use this skill to rewrite general Chinese text so it sounds more natural, less formulaic, and better matched to the target context. It is intended for prose naturalization where style changes are acceptable and the original meaning must be preserved. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad Chinese prose naturalization can change tone, register, or exact wording. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when style changes are acceptable, and review edited text against the intended meaning and context. <br>
Risk: Applying the skill to legal, compliance, medical, academic, quoted, translation, or brand-sensitive text may undermine wording that must be preserved. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using the skill for text that requires exact wording, dialect, register, or disclosure preservation. <br>
