## Description: <br>
Internal-Medicine-PMPH-10edition is a 432-module internal medicine reference skill based on People's Medical Publishing House's Internal Medicine, 10th Edition, for matching clinical questions to focused diagnostic, risk assessment, treatment, laboratory, imaging, emergency care, and chronic disease management guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Clinicians, medical trainees, and supervised educational users use this skill as a static internal-medicine textbook reference for clinical questions across diagnosis, risk assessment, treatment planning, drug safety, laboratory and imaging interpretation, emergency scenarios, and chronic disease management. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Treatment, procedure, dosage, and emergency-care guidance could be applied without enough clinical context. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as educational support only and require qualified clinician or supervised medical judgment before acting on outputs. <br>
Risk: Static textbook guidance may conflict with current local protocols, institutional policies, or patient-specific contraindications. <br>
Mitigation: Verify plans, thresholds, procedures, and follow-up guidance against current local standards and institutional policies before use. <br>
Risk: The metadata includes an npm install requirement even though security evidence describes no executable package files. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect setup requirements before installation and avoid running unnecessary install commands unless their source and purpose are verified. <br>
