## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common nonprofit questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External-facing nonprofit, NGO, and fundraising teams use this skill to draft FAQ page answers for common audience questions and refine them for accuracy, readability, and actionability before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ answers may contain inaccurate or unverified web-sourced claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review all answers before publication and verify factual claims against trusted organizational or external sources. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include donor-sensitive or confidential organizational data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including donor-sensitive or confidential data when asking the skill to draft FAQ content. <br>
