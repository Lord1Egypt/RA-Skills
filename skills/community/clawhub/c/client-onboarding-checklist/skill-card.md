## Description: <br>
Generate customized client onboarding checklists, welcome emails, and setup task lists for IT service providers, MSPs, and consultants. Covers network setup, software deployment, user provisioning, and documentation handoff. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[josh4hire](https://clawhub.ai/user/josh4hire) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External IT service providers, managed service providers, and consultants use this skill to create client onboarding checklists, welcome emails, setup task lists, and kickoff agendas for new client engagements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Onboarding workflows may involve admin access, passwords, or other sensitive client information. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before sending or saving them, avoid pasting passwords or raw secrets into the agent, and use a password manager or secure sharing process for admin-access transfer. <br>
Risk: Generic onboarding templates may not account for specialized regulatory requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Use the generated checklist as a starting point and consult the appropriate compliance team for specialized industries or regulated environments. <br>
