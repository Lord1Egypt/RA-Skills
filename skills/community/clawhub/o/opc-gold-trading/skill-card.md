跨境黄金交易全流程咨询技能，覆盖合规分析、供货方尽调、交易流程、分账税务、账本管理、风险识别、银行验证、交易审核八大模块，帮助OPC导师为客户提供从金矿到终端的黄金供应链合规咨询，识别风险并设计安全交易结构。 <br>

## Use Case: <br>
OPC mentors and compliance-oriented trade advisors use this skill as a professional checklist for cross-border gold sourcing, supplier due diligence, AML/KYC review, bank verification, transaction workflow review, accounting templates, and risk screening for Laos/Southeast Asia, Hong Kong, China, and related SBLC scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with documented scenarios involving Laos, Southeast Asia, Hong Kong, and China. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can prompt collection or review of sensitive personal, financial, banking, and trade-compliance documents across jurisdictions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with authorized compliance staff or counsel, collect the minimum necessary data, redact nonessential fields, use secure channels, define retention and deletion rules, and verify cross-border data-transfer obligations. <br>
Risk: Cross-border gold trading and SBLC workflows carry legal, AML, fraud, and financial risks if checklist output is treated as definitive advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as professional checklists, not legal or investment advice; verify transaction details through official bank, regulator, and counsel channels before acting. <br>
