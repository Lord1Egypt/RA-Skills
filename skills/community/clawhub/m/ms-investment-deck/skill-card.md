## Description: <br>
Generates Morgan Stanley-style investment presentation PPTX decks for roadshows, pitch books, investment committee memos, stock coverage launches, and quarterly update presentations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External analysts, investment professionals, and developers can use this skill to generate finance-facing PPTX decks for roadshows, pitch books, investment committee materials, coverage initiation, and quarterly updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated investment decks may include inaccurate or misleading financial claims, ratings, targets, or disclaimers. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified reviewers validate all financial content, ratings, targets, and disclaimers before sharing decks with clients, investors, or internal committees. <br>
Risk: Generated decks may include embedded local images or user-provided material that is unsuitable, outdated, or not cleared for distribution. <br>
Mitigation: Review all embedded assets and source materials before distributing the generated PPTX. <br>
