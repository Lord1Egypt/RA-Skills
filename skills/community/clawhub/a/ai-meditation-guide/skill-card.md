## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat meditation or breathwork guidance as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as general wellness guidance and direct users with medical, mental health, pregnancy-related, respiratory, cardiovascular, or neurological concerns to qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Advanced breathwork, breath retention, hyperventilation-style exercises, or cold exposure may be unsafe for some users. <br>
Mitigation: Use caution around advanced practices, avoid encouraging risky intensity, and recommend qualified instruction for those techniques. <br>
