## Use Case: <br>
External users use Chef to get cooking guidance for recipes, techniques, substitutions, dietary adaptations, measurement scaling, troubleshooting, meal planning, and kitchen safety. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cooking guidance can be incorrect or incomplete for food safety, allergies, or medical dietary needs. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify food-safety temperatures, allergen handling, and medical dietary advice before relying on the guidance. <br>
Risk: Dietary substitutions may change flavor, texture, timing, or suitability for severe allergies. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's restrictions and available ingredients, explain tradeoffs, and mention cross-contact concerns when relevant. <br>
