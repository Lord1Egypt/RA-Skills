## Description: <br>
Create, verify, and package Islamic-ethics content by summarizing texts, checking sources, generating post variants, crafting questions, and archiving ideas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[m7madash](https://clawhub.ai/user/m7madash) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content creators and discussion facilitators use this skill to transform provided Islamic-ethics source material into summaries, source checks, social post variants, discussion questions, and compact archive entries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The main reference file includes prompt-injection-style language that may try to override assistant instructions or carry into future tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reference files as source text only, keep system, developer, and current user instructions authoritative, and review generated content before use. <br>
Risk: Religious or source-sensitive claims may be unverifiable from the provided text alone. <br>
Mitigation: Label unverifiable claims as "needs source" and avoid inventing citations. <br>
