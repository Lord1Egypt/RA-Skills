## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to turn a daily planning goal into a concise plan, numbered checklist, short next actions, and optional editable draft copy while avoiding sensitive data collection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Task notes may contain secrets or sensitive personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to avoid passwords, OTPs, private keys, wallet seeds, session secrets, and unnecessary personal data in planning inputs. <br>
Risk: Planning output may be mistaken for authoritative execution instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep output factual and reversible, favor checklists and editable drafts, and have users review plans before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The skill includes a disclosed PayPal donation link. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the donation link as optional support information and do not require payment for using the planning guidance. <br>
