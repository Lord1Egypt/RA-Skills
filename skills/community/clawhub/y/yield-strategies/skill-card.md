This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to discover DeFi yield opportunities, compare strategy filters such as chain, token, APY, and risk level, and guide wallet-backed deposits or withdrawals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide deposits into or withdrawals from DeFi strategies using an authenticated crypto wallet. <br>
Mitigation: Before any transaction, manually verify the chain, protocol, strategy or position ID, token contract, amount, recipient, fees, approvals, and wallet prompt details. <br>
Risk: DeFi protocols can expose users to smart contract, liquidity, market, and APY volatility risks. <br>
Mitigation: Present the strategy risk level and require the human to make the final choice about protocol, strategy, and deposit amount. <br>
