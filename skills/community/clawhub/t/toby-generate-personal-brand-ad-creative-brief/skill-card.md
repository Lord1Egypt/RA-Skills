## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requests broad local Bash and file-read access that is not clearly required for marketing brief creation. <br>
Mitigation: Install only from the ClawHub package, restrict the agent to user-provided files, and require explicit approval before running shell commands. <br>
Risk: Generated ad concepts and hooks may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for publication without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for factual accuracy, brand fit, audience appropriateness, and campaign compliance before external use. <br>
