## Description: <br>
Generate polished corporate PowerPoint decks from provided content using a light corporate template with white backgrounds, red accents, reusable layouts, icon support, QA checks, and scoring helpers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ksfrist](https://clawhub.ai/user/ksfrist) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to turn source material into H3C-style light corporate PowerPoint presentations, revise existing decks, and run local QA and scoring checks before delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated presentations and feedback memory can contain confidential slide content or business details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive content in feedback memory, and periodically review or delete memory/ppt-feedback.md. <br>
Risk: The skill saves generated PowerPoint files locally and relies on separately provided PPTX template and icon assets. <br>
Mitigation: Review the output location and verify PPTX template and icon assets before use. <br>
