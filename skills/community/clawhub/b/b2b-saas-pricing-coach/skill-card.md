## Description: <br>
Coach for B2B SaaS founders on pricing strategy, including value metrics, tier structure, headline prices, free-trial or freemium choices, packaging, discounting, contract terms, and repricing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pricing diagnostics can involve confidential customer names, contracts, renewal dates, health scores, and discount history. <br>
Mitigation: Remove customer names and contract identifiers unless the environment is approved for confidential business data; use aggregated cohorts, price bands, renewal windows, and health segments when sufficient. <br>
Risk: Pricing recommendations can materially affect revenue, churn, and customer relationships if applied without business review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support, validate assumptions with finance, sales, and customer-success stakeholders, and test changes with clear customer communication and churn monitoring. <br>
