## Description: <br>
PetVet helps agents provide AI-assisted dog and cat health support, including symptom triage, vaccine and deworming schedules, nutrition guidance, behavior support, senior pet care, emergency first-aid guidance, and lab report interpretation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External pet owners and pet-care support users use this skill to organize pet health information, understand possible care urgency, prepare questions for veterinarians, and generate structured pet health management reports. It is an assistant for guidance and triage, not a replacement for licensed veterinary diagnosis or treatment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI-assisted pet health triage can be incorrect or incomplete, especially for urgent symptoms. <br>
Mitigation: Keep veterinarian confirmation central, preserve the skill's urgent-care escalation language, and advise immediate veterinary care for severe or worsening symptoms. <br>
Risk: Nutrition, behavior, and chronic-care guidance may not fit an individual pet's medical history. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for species, breed, age, weight, symptoms, current diet, medication history, and veterinary findings before offering tailored guidance, and avoid prescription or dosing instructions. <br>
