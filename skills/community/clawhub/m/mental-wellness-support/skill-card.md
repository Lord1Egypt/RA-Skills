## Description: <br>
MentalWellness provides AI-assisted mental wellness assessment and emotional support, including PHQ-9, GAD-7, and PSS screening, emotion journal analysis, mindfulness guidance, stress and sleep support, and burnout assessment based on positive psychology and CBT frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees can use this skill for supportive wellness check-ins, mood and stress reflection, sleep improvement suggestions, and guided CBT or mindfulness exercises. It is not a substitute for professional diagnosis, treatment, crisis support, or medication advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may handle sensitive mental-health information, including mood journals, symptoms, sleep issues, stressors, and relationship concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as supportive guidance only and avoid sharing more personal detail than needed. <br>
Risk: Mental-health screening and wellness suggestions can be mistaken for professional diagnosis, crisis care, or medication guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Seek qualified professional or emergency help for self-harm thoughts, severe symptoms, crisis situations, or medication questions. <br>
