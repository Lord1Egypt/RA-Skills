## Description: <br>
Generates polite, firm Chinese follow-up email drafts for sales, business development, and customer service teams when customers or partners have not replied. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[olina1ye](https://clawhub.ai/user/olina1ye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, business development, and customer service teams use this skill to draft Chinese follow-up emails for quotes, contract discussions, partnership invitations, and delayed customer replies. It helps adapt tone for first follow-ups, second reminders, and urgent follow-ups while preserving a professional relationship. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated follow-up emails may include inaccurate customer details, pricing, contract references, or urgency cues if the prompt context is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit every generated message before sending, especially when it references customer-specific facts, commercial terms, or deadlines. <br>
