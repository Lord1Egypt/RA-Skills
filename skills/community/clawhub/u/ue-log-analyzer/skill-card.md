## Description: <br>
Analyze Unreal Engine log files for errors, warnings, crashes, and performance issues. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[vincentwilliam](https://clawhub.ai/user/vincentwilliam) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to review Unreal Engine logs and crash information for fatal errors, warnings, rendering failures, Lua/UnLua errors, pak or loading issues, network issues, and likely fixes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Unreal Engine logs can include private paths, project details, and diagnostic information. <br>
Mitigation: Point the agent at specific log or crash files instead of broad folders, and review findings before sharing them outside the project team. <br>
