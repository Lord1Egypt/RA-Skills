## Description: <br>
日语到中文翻译器 helps agents translate Japanese business and legal documents into Chinese while preserving tone, terminology, numbers, and document structure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xiaoxuan1917](https://clawhub.ai/user/xiaoxuan1917) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, business teams, legal reviewers, and agents use this skill to translate Japanese contracts, LOIs, NDAs, emails, meeting notes, and reports into Chinese while preserving formatting and professional terminology. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential business or legal documents may contain sensitive contract, LOI, NDA, or report content. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target language and confirm that the selected agent and model provider are appropriate for handling the document contents before use. <br>
Risk: Incorrect translation of legal terms, dates, amounts, percentages, or party names could change business meaning. <br>
Mitigation: Review translated documents against the Japanese source, with special attention to terminology, numbers, dates, units, signatures, and clause references. <br>
