## Description: <br>
GEO运营助手，帮助运营人员优化内容在生成式搜索引擎中的引用表现，提供关键词拓展、引用源分析、内容优化、发布策略等全流程支持；当用户需要GEO优化、提升大模型引用率、内容权威性分析或发布策略建议时使用 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Mitigation: Use only public or approved webpages, avoid confidential pasted content, and redact personal or proprietary details from reports and validation records. <br>
Risk: The skill can generate citation and publishing recommendations that may be inaccurate or misleading if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review GEO recommendations, source claims, and generated content before publication or operational use. <br>
