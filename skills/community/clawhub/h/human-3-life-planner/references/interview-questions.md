## Mind Quadrant (Personal Mental World)

### Initial Questions (use to detect level)

Ask these first. Listen carefully to HOW the user answers — the reasoning pattern reveals more than the content.

- "当你遇到与你世界观相矛盾的想法时，你的第一反应是什么？"
- "你如何判断什么是真实或有价值的信息？"
- "描述你上一次重大的信念转变——是什么触发的？"

### Level Detection Guide

Based on initial answers, look for these signals. Note: users often STRADDLE levels — Knowledge may be Level 2 while Experience remains Level 1. Record the dominant level AND any gaps between dimensions.

**Level 1 signals:**
- Defers to authority figures, tradition, or "experts" as truth source
- Binary language: right/wrong, good/bad, true/false
- Discomfort with ambiguity; seeks certainty
- Has not questioned inherited beliefs; beliefs are "just how things are"
- Emotional reactivity when beliefs challenged

**Level 2 signals:**
- Rejects external authority; "I think for myself"
- Confident in personal reasoning; sometimes contrarian
- Can articulate why they believe what they believe
- May dismiss traditional perspectives entirely
- Critical thinking present but sometimes rigid in own certainty

**Level 3 signals:**
- Holds multiple perspectives simultaneously
- Uses paradox and nuance naturally
- Can explain why opposing views contain truth
- Aware of the frameworks they use to think
- Chooses when to be narrow vs. broad strategically

**Level 4+ (Evolutionary):** Extremely rare — if responses consistently exceed Level 3 across multiple quadrants, consult knowledge base for Level 4.0 markers. Do not score above 3.0; note distinctive patterns in report. This applies to all quadrants.

### Level-Specific Branches

After detecting level, probe these topics with follow-up questions:

**Level 1 detected** — probe: sources of beliefs, comfort with ambiguity, response to criticism
- "你的核心信念来自哪里？是你自己选择的还是继承的？"
- "面对不确定或模糊的情况时，你感到舒服还是焦虑？"
- "当有人批评你的观点时，你的第一反应是什么？"

**Level 2 detected** — probe: blind spots, integration of opposing views, metacognition practices
- "你有没有发现自己认为对的事情后来被证明是错的？"
- "你如何看待与你完全相反的观点中可能存在的真理？"
- "你是否定期审视自己的思维盲区？如何审视？"

**Level 3 detected** — probe: intentional limitation strategies, construct awareness, teaching/creating
- "你是否有意识地选择在何时展现狭窄或开放的思维？为什么？"
- "你对自己的思维框架有多觉察？能否看到框架本身的局限？"
- "你是否在创造新的思维模式，而不仅仅是使用现有的？"

### Phase Detection

Phase is detected from behavioral patterns in the user's answers, NOT by asking directly. Users at lower levels cannot identify their own phase. After level-specific probing, assess phase based on:

- **Dissonance (x.1):** User expresses boredom, restlessness, or vague dissatisfaction but hasn't started changing. Answers feel stale or repetitive.
- **Uncertainty (x.2):** User is experimenting or questioning but nothing has clicked yet. Answers feel exploratory and unstable.
- **Discovery (x.3):** User describes concrete changes that are sticking. Answers show new patterns that feel solidified.

If needed to confirm phase observation, use ONE indirect question (not "are you in phase X"):
- "最近有什么让你感到比较兴奋或者比较烦的事？" (the affect reveals the phase)

### Trait Assessment

- **Knowledge:** "你能解释但无法实施的概念有哪些？"
- **Experience:** "你实践过但不完全理解的是什么？"
- **Skill:** "什么东西曾经需要努力，现在却自然而然了？"

## Body Quadrant (Personal Physical World)

### Initial Questions (use to detect level)

- "描述你与身体的关系——是盟友、敌人，还是工具？"
- "你的健康/健身决策由什么驱动——外表、表现，还是长寿？"
- "当生活变得混乱时，你的身体实践有多稳定？"

### Level Detection Guide

**Level 1 signals:**
- No conscious health practice; follows what others do or what's easy
- Health = absence of immediate pain; "fine" means not sick
- Diet and exercise are afterthoughts or driven by social pressure
- Disconnected from body signals; eats/moves without awareness
- External motivation needed to start any physical practice

**Level 2 signals:**
- Deliberate optimization; tracks metrics, follows protocols
- Health as performance metric or achievement goal
- Experiments with different methods and diets
- Sometimes obsessive about data while ignoring actual feelings
- Sustainability is a challenge; frequent protocol switching

**Level 3 signals:**
- Intuitive practice; body wisdom overrides rigid plans
- Health integrated naturally with other life domains
- Can teach or guide others from embodied experience
- Movement is joy, not discipline
- Body awareness is automatic, not effortful

### Level-Specific Branches

**Level 1 detected** — probe: basic habits, health literacy, external motivation needs
- "你每天的基本健康习惯是什么？是自主选择还是随大流？"
- "你对营养和运动的基本理解是什么程度？"
- "什么外部因素会促使你开始关注健康？"

**Level 2 detected** — probe: optimization attempts, metric obsessions, sustainability
- "你尝试过哪些优化方案？追踪了哪些指标？"
- "你的健康方案能持续多久？是否频繁切换方法？"
- "你对数字和数据的关注是否有时超过了实际感受？"

**Level 3 detected** — probe: intuitive practices, integration with other quadrants, teaching others
- "你的身体实践是否凭直觉而非严格的计划？"
- "身体训练如何与你的其他生活领域相互促进？"
- "你是否通过自己的身体经验来帮助或指导他人？"

### Phase Detection

Phase is detected from behavioral patterns, NOT by asking directly. Assess based on:
- **Dissonance (x.1):** User knows body needs attention but hasn't started. Complains but no action pattern.
- **Uncertainty (x.2):** User has tried changing but nothing stuck. Pattern of starting and stopping.
- **Discovery (x.3):** User describes a practice that has become automatic. Pattern shows consistency.

### Trait Assessment

- **Knowledge:** "你了解很多健康理论但实际执行的有哪些差距？"
- **Experience:** "你尝试过很多健康方案但不确定科学原理的是什么？"
- **Skill:** "什么健康习惯曾经需要意志力，现在已经自动化了？"

## Spirit Quadrant (Collective Mental World)

### Initial Questions (use to detect level)

- "当一切感觉毫无意义时，你如何找到意义？"
- "描述你与社群的关系——是必要的、可有可无的，还是不可或缺的？"
- "你对绝对真理与相对视角的立场是什么？"

### Level Detection Guide

Note: users often STRADDLE levels — e.g., may intellectually understand multiple perspectives (Knowledge Level 2) but still seek external validation for meaning (Experience Level 1).

**Level 1 signals:**
- Belonging through compliance with group norms
- Meaning derived from external authority or tradition
- God/transcendence as external figure or rigid doctrine
- Relationships based on roles and obligations
- Fear of isolation; community is security structure

**Level 2 signals:**
- Rejection of inherited faith; personal spiritual exploration
- Meaning from personal achievement or self-discovery
- May be militant atheist or spiritual shopper (trying many paths)
- Relationships as self-growth laboratories
- Tendency toward isolation or selectivity

**Level 3 signals:**
- Bridge-builder between different belief systems
- Comfortable with paradox in spiritual matters
- Sacred and secular naturally integrated in daily life
- Relationships as mutual practice, not just self-serving
- Meaning creation capacity independent of circumstance

### Level-Specific Branches

**Level 1 detected** — probe: tradition, authority figures, belonging needs
- "你所属的社群或传统对你的意义来自哪里？"
- "在你的生活中，谁是精神上的权威人物？"
- "你对归属感的需求有多强烈？没有社群时你会怎样？"

**Level 2 detected** — probe: rebellion patterns, spiritual shopping, isolation tendencies
- "你是否曾经完全拒绝过某种信仰体系？现在的态度是什么？"
- "你是否在不同的灵性实践之间切换？为什么？"
- "你是否更倾向于独自探索而非参与集体实践？"

**Level 3 detected** — probe: bridge-building, paradox comfort, sacred/secular integration
- "你如何在不同的信仰体系之间搭建桥梁？"
- "你能否同时持有矛盾的精神观点而不感到不适？"
- "你如何看待神圣与世俗的融合？"

### Phase Detection

Phase is detected from behavioral patterns, NOT by asking directly. Assess based on:
- **Dissonance (x.1):** User feels something is missing in relationships or meaning but hasn't changed behavior.
- **Uncertainty (x.2):** User is questioning inherited beliefs or exploring new communities but nothing has landed.
- **Discovery (x.3):** User has found practices or connections that feel solid and are sustained.

### Trait Assessment

- **Knowledge:** "你理解的精神理念中，哪些你还没有真正体验过？"
- **Experience:** "你经历过但还没有完全理解的精神体验是什么？"
- **Skill:** "什么精神实践曾经需要刻意为之，现在已经自然融入生活？"

## Vocation Quadrant (Collective Physical World)

### Initial Questions (use to detect level)

- "你认为工作是你做的事、你拥有的身份，还是你本身的延伸？"
- "你如何衡量职业成功——薪水、影响力，还是满足感？"
- "如果金钱和地位都不是问题，你会做什么？"

### Level Detection Guide

Note: users often STRADDLE levels — e.g., may have chosen their own career direction (Level 2 autonomy) but still view money purely as security (Level 1). Record the dominant level AND the gap.

**Level 1 signals:**
- Work is "just a job" — necessary evil for survival
- Security and stability are primary career drivers
- Follows career templates: get degree, get job, climb ladder
- Money is scarce resource; success = steady paycheck
- Retirement is the only escape from work
- Completes assigned tasks without questioning purpose

**Level 2 signals:**
- Work as self-expression or achievement vehicle
- Money as scoreboard or measure of differentiation
- Actively creating own path: entrepreneurship, freelancing, side projects
- Financial independence as explicit goal
- Measures success by personal standards, not organizational ones
- May hustle without clear deeper purpose

**Level 3 signals:**
- Work feels like play (Calling realized, not just Career)
- Money as energy for creation, not as status symbol
- Building systems or structures that outlast personal involvement
- Creating new "games" rather than competing in existing ones
- Legacy and generational impact are explicit considerations
- Value creation is natural, not forced

### Level-Specific Branches

**Level 1 detected** — probe: security needs, authority relationships, skill development
- "你的职业选择是基于安全感还是热情？"
- "你对上级或组织的关系是什么样的？"
- "你是否在发展市场需要的技能，还是仅仅完成分配的任务？"

**Level 2 detected** — probe: entrepreneurial attempts, ladder choices, value creation
- "你是否尝试过创业或自由职业？结果如何？"
- "你在职业阶梯上的策略是什么——攀爬还是跳脱？"
- "你如何定义自己创造的价值？"

**Level 3 detected** — probe: system building, legacy thinking, game creation
- "你是否在构建超越个人的系统或组织？"
- "你的工作如何影响未来的几代人？"
- "你是否在设计新的'游戏规则'，而不仅仅在现有规则中竞争？"

### Phase Detection

Phase is detected from behavioral patterns, NOT by asking directly. Assess based on:
- **Dissonance (x.1):** User is dissatisfied with work/career but hasn't made changes. Complains without action.
- **Uncertainty (x.2):** User is considering changes or experimenting but hasn't committed. Exploring options.
- **Discovery (x.3):** User describes concrete career moves that feel right and are producing results.

### Trait Assessment

- **Knowledge:** "你了解的商业/职业理论中，哪些你还没有应用过？"
- **Experience:** "你实际做过但不确定底层逻辑的职业经历是什么？"
- **Skill:** "什么职业技能曾经需要努力，现在变成了直觉？"

## Lifestyle Integration Observation

These are NOT questions to ask the user directly. They are patterns for the ASSESSOR to observe during the four quadrant interviews:

- Which quadrant dominates the user's time and energy allocation (revealed through which answers are most detailed vs. thinnest)
- Which quadrants feel like obligation vs. play (revealed through emotional tone when discussing each)
- Where the user sacrifices one area for another (revealed through "总有事情会影响锻炼" type answers)
- Whether problems in one quadrant actually stem from neglect in another (the assessor identifies this, not the user)
- Whether the user sees connections between their life domains (their ability or inability to see connections IS a level indicator)

### When to probe lifestyle integration

If after all four quadrants the integration pattern is still unclear, ask ONE indirect question:
- "如果下周突然多出十个小时的自由时间，你会用它做什么？" (reveals where energy naturally flows)

## False Transformation Probing (Inline)

Use these INLINE during any quadrant's interview when a user's claim exceeds their demonstrated behavior. Do NOT wait until all quadrants are complete:

- "你提到了[高级概念]——请详细描述你如何在日常中应用它"
- "这听起来很理想——当你未能达到这个标准时会发生什么？"
- "有趣的观点——反对者会指出什么？"
- "你坚持这个实践多长时间了？期间有没有倒退过？"
- "你的知识和你的执行之间的差距有多大？"

## Post-Interview Detection Probes

> After completing all four quadrants, PROACTIVELY ask at least one question from each category below. Do NOT wait for the user to mention these topics.

### Active Channel Detection

Ask proactively even if user hasn't mentioned obsession:

- "你有没有过这样的经历——对某件事着迷到废寝忘食，完全停不下来做别的事？"
- If yes: "告诉我更多关于这种痴迷——你每天投入多少小时？" / "是什么触发了这段高度集中的时期？" / "为了维持这种势头，你牺牲了什么？"

### Regression Detection

Ask proactively:

- "生活中有没有什么方面感觉比两年前更糟了？"
- If yes: "你有哪些能力但目前无法发挥出来？" / "什么压力模式反复将你击倒？"

### Glitch User Detection

Ask proactively — critical to assess foundation before discussing any accelerants:

- "你目前使用AI工具的频率高吗？你觉得它是在增强你的思考还是在替代你的思考？"
- "你有没有做出过或者正在考虑什么极端的生活改变？"
- If Level < 2.5 and user mentions heavy AI/social media use: probe "你如何区分自己的能力和工具的能力？" / "你是否尝试过改变意识的物质或实践？"
