## Description: <br>
Generate database schemas, migrations, and ERD diagrams from plain English descriptions, with support for PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite, and MongoDB indexes and constraints. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[MatrixTrickery](https://clawhub.ai/user/MatrixTrickery) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to turn plain-language data model descriptions into database schema drafts, migration code, ER diagrams, indexes, and optional seed data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated migrations, cascade rules, indexes, or seed data may be incorrect or unsuitable for a production database. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated database changes against application requirements and test them before applying them to real data. <br>
