## Description: <br>
Product strategy session with 6 forcing questions that reframes the problem, challenges premises, generates implementation approaches, and works directly in OpenClaw without a Claude Code session. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[muthukumaran-k-1](https://clawhub.ai/user/muthukumaran-k-1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product builders, and planning teams use this conversational skill to pressure-test a product idea through six focused questions, identify the narrowest useful wedge, compare implementation approaches, and choose a recommended path. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional handoff to Claude Code or another UCTS workflow may expose sensitive project details to a separate workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review that workflow before using it with sensitive project details. <br>
Risk: Product strategy recommendations may be incorrect or too narrow if the user's answers are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as a planning aid and review the resulting problem framing, approaches, and metric before execution. <br>
