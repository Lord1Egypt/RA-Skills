# 外贸合同类型模板参考

## 一、PI（Proforma Invoice）标准模板要素

### 必须包含的要素
1. **头部信息**：PI编号、日期、有效期
2. **买卖双方信息**：公司名称、地址、联系人、电话、邮箱、银行信息
3. **产品明细表**：品名、规格、数量、单价、总价
4. **价格条款**：Incoterms 2020（FOB/CIF/DDP等）
5. **装运信息**：起运港、目的港、最迟装运期
6. **付款条款**：付款方式、比例、时间节点
7. **包装信息**：包装方式、毛重、体积
8. **特殊条款**：质量标准、样品确认、颜色确认等
9. **双方签章栏**：Buyer/Seller签名

### PI特殊保护条款（必须添加）
```
☑ 30% deposit must be paid within [X] days of PI confirmation
☑ Order not confirmed until deposit received in seller's account
☑ Seller reserves right to cancel if deposit not received within [X] days
☑ PI is valid for [X] days from date of issue
☑ Prices are based on current exchange rates and may be adjusted if RMB appreciates >3%
☑ Partial shipment/transshipment: NOT allowed (unless agreed in writing)
```

## 二、销售合同（Sales Contract）核心条款

### 必须谈判的条款及风险优先级

| 条款类别 | 风险等级 | 谈判优先级 | 说明 |
|---------|---------|-----------|------|
| 付款方式 | 🔴 高 | P1 | T/T vs L/C vs D/P vs O/A |
| 货物风险转移点 | 🔴 高 | P1 | FOB船上交货 vs CIF目的地交货 |
| 不可抗力范围 | 🔴 高 | P1 | 必须覆盖物流/政策因素 |
| 违约金上限 | 🟡 中 | P2 | 建议不超过合同金额10-15% |
| 争议解决机构 | 🟡 中 | P2 | CIETAC/ICC/SIAC |
| 适用法律 | 🟡 中 | P2 | 中国法/买方国法/第三方法律 |
| 检验条款 | 🟡 中 | P2 | 装船前检验 vs 到货检验 |
| 知识产权保护 | 🟢 低 | P3 | OEM产品需明确品牌归属 |

### 推荐条款设置

#### 付款条款推荐结构
```
对于$10,000-50,000订单：
30% T/T deposit upon order confirmation
70% T/T against copy of B/L (Bill of Lading)

对于$50,000-200,000订单：
30% T/T deposit upon order confirmation  
70% L/C at sight (irrevocable, issued by top 50 world banks)

对于$200,000+订单：
20% T/T deposit
80% L/C at 30 days (negotiable based on relationship)
+ credit insurance recommended
```

#### 不可抗力条款扩展版（推荐使用）
```
Force Majeure events shall include but not limited to:
- Natural disasters (earthquakes, typhoons, floods)
- War, terrorism, civil unrest
- Government actions, embargoes, sanctions
- Port congestion, shipping line suspension
- Customs delays beyond seller's control
- Epidemics/pandemics declared by WHO/government
- Changes in laws or regulations making performance impossible

Notice requirement: Party affected must notify the other within [5] business days
Documentation: Party affected must provide official certificates from relevant authorities
```

## 三、NDA（保密协议）核心要素

### 关键定义条款
```
"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by 
either party, including but not limited to:
- Technical specifications, designs, and drawings
- Pricing information and commercial terms
- Business plans, customer lists, and supplier information
- Manufacturing processes and know-how
- Any information marked as "Confidential" or obviously confidential

"Exclusions from Confidential Information":
- Information already in public domain
- Information known before disclosure without obligation of confidentiality
- Information independently developed without use of confidential information
- Information received from third party without confidentiality obligation
```

### NDA有效期限
```
Standard term: 3 years from date of signature
Survival clause: Confidentiality obligations survive termination for [2] years
```

## 四、MOU（谅解备忘录）框架结构

### 推荐框架
```
MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

This MOU is entered into on [DATE] between:
Party A: [Company A details]
Party B: [Company B details]

1. PURPOSE
[Description of the business relationship intent]

2. SCOPE OF COOPERATION
[Specific areas of collaboration]

3. OBLIGATIONS OF PARTIES
Party A: [Obligations]
Party B: [Obligations]

4. FINANCIAL TERMS
[Commercial framework - prices, payment, exclusivity if any]

5. EXCLUSIVITY (if applicable)
[Geographic/product exclusivity terms]

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
[Standard confidentiality clause]

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
[IP ownership and usage rights]

8. TERM AND TERMINATION
[Duration and termination conditions]

9. GOVERNING LAW
[Choice of law]

10. NON-BINDING NATURE
This MOU is intended to be a statement of mutual intent and does not 
constitute a legally binding agreement, except for Confidentiality 
(Clause 6) and IP (Clause 7) which are binding upon execution.

Signed:
________________    ________________
Party A            Party B
```
