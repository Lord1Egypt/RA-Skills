## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Used-car valuation and inspection guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific vehicle. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill's output as advisory and verify important decisions with official vehicle records, trusted inspection services, or an in-person inspection. <br>
Risk: Vehicle photos, VINs, contracts, or ownership materials can contain personal or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the vehicle details needed for the question and redact unnecessary personal, financial, or identity information. <br>
Risk: Transfer, insurance, lien, auction, and contract practices can vary by location and transaction type. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm transaction requirements with local authorities, official records, or qualified professionals before payment or transfer. <br>
