## Description: <br>
Generates variable, function, and API names from Chinese or English keywords in common programming naming styles, including full and abbreviated variants. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gmmg55](https://clawhub.ai/user/gmmg55) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers use this skill to turn Chinese or English domain terms into consistent variable, function, API, file, and path-style names, including abbreviation variants. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated names may not match a team's local naming conventions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the suggested names against project style guides before using them in code. <br>
Risk: Registry capability tags mention crypto and purchases even though the artifact is an instruction-only naming helper. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as a text-generation helper and do not grant external service or purchasing permissions based on those tags alone. <br>
