## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated Python and R snippets may read local files, query databases, or save output images. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt snippets before running them, especially where they access files, databases, or write outputs. <br>
Risk: Statistical guidance or report templates can be misapplied to unsuitable data or analysis goals. <br>
Mitigation: Validate data quality, assumptions, method selection, and interpretation before relying on results. <br>
