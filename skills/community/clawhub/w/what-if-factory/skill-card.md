## Description: <br>
Transforms hypothetical prompts into entertaining parallel-universe content with a fictional news article, absurd consequences, life advice, image prompts, meme quotes, and an optional probability rating. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ideasflowai](https://clawhub.ai/user/ideasflowai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill for entertainment, brainstorming, and playful alternate-reality storytelling around explicit hypothetical prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The trigger wording is broad and may activate the full creative parallel-universe format during ordinary hypothetical questions. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording when requesting the format, and ask the agent to skip the skill for factual planning, normal reasoning, or decisions that require grounded advice. <br>
Risk: Fictional news-style output and humorous advice may be mistaken for factual reporting or practical recommendations outside an entertainment context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated articles, probability ratings, consequences, and advice as creative fiction unless separately verified through authoritative sources. <br>
