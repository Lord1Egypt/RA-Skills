# AI Personal Stylist

Personalized fashion guidance covering body type analysis, color theory, wardrobe curation, and style identity development for all genders and occasions.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Body Type Analysis
- Shape identification: hourglass, pear, apple, rectangle, inverted triangle
- Proportion balancing through silhouette, neckline, and hemline
- Petite, tall, plus-size specific styling strategies
- Visual length/layering techniques

### 2. Color Analysis
- Seasonal color theory: spring, summer, autumn, winter palettes
- Undertone determination: warm, cool, neutral
- Contrast levels and color combining rules
- Print and pattern selection: scale, density, placement

### 3. Wardrobe Curation
- Capsule wardrobe building: 30-50 piece foundations
- Wardrobe audit: keep, alter, donate, store framework
- Essential investment pieces identification
- Gap analysis and strategic shopping lists

### 4. Occasion Dressing
- Business formal, business casual, smart casual
- Wedding guest, cocktail, black tie dress codes
- Date night, brunch, vacation capsule recommendations
- Interview attire by industry

### 5. Style Identity
- Style archetypes: classic, romantic, dramatic, natural, gamine
- Personal brand through clothing
- Accessories: bags, shoes, jewelry, scarves, belts
- Grooming and presentation: hair, makeup, fragrance
