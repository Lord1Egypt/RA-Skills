## What It Does

Generates professional client-facing monthly reports, QBR narratives, internal health scorecards, and renewal/upsell proposals — everything an agency needs to retain clients and grow accounts.

## The Problem It Solves

Agencies lose clients not because the work is bad, but because they fail to communicate value. Most agencies:
- Send generic email updates with no narrative
- Skip QBRs entirely (too time-consuming to prepare)
- Miss churn signals until it's too late
- Send renewal emails that feel like invoice reminders

This skill fixes all four with 4 structured prompts that take 15 minutes instead of 4 hours.
