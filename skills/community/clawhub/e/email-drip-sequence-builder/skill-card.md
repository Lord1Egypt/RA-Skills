## Description: <br>
Generate complete email drip sequences from product descriptions with personalization, A/B subject lines, and ESP-ready formatting. Use when the user needs email campaigns, welcome series, product launch sequences, re-engagement flows, or nurture campaigns for any industry. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, operators, and content creators use this skill to draft complete email drip campaigns from a product description or business context, including subject variants, timing, segmentation, calls to action, and ESP-ready copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Campaign prompts or generated drafts may include sensitive customer, subscriber, or business information sent to an AI provider. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data allowed by the organization's policy, minimize personal data in prompts, and confirm AI-provider handling is acceptable before drafting campaigns. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing emails may contain inaccurate claims, weak sender identification, missing consent context, or incomplete compliance placeholders. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review each email for accuracy, unsubscribe links, sender identity, consent basis, physical address placeholders, and applicable email-marketing law before sending. <br>
Risk: The required OPENAI_API_KEY can be exposed if stored or shared improperly. <br>
Mitigation: Store the API key in approved secret-management or environment configuration, avoid committing it to files, and rotate it if exposure is suspected. <br>
