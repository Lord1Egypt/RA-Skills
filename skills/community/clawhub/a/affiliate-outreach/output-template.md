# Output Template — Affiliate Outreach

Produce the output in exactly this structure. Fill every placeholder; never leave bracketed tokens in the final output.

## Section 1: TikTok DM Variant (120–160 words)

```
[Specific opener referencing the creator's recent post, series, or signature format — 1–2 sentences]

[Product introduction with the unique selling hook if available — 1 sentence]

[Earning math: price, commission %, per-sale dollar amount, realistic weekly scenario for this creator's tier — 1–2 sentences]

[Audience-fit line connecting the product to their exact viewer profile — 1 sentence]

[Single CTA, lowest possible friction, ideally free-sample offer with reply trigger — 1–2 sentences]
```

Rules: no links in the first DM; casual register; contractions encouraged; no emoji unless the creator's own captions use them.

## Section 2: Email Variant (200–260 words)

```
Subject: [≤9 words, contains creator's niche or past result, no clickbait]

Hi [Name],

[Opening hook referencing their content or past performance — 2 sentences]

[Value proposition paragraph: product, hook, commission structure, concrete earning math — 3–4 sentences]

[Audience-fit + social proof sentence]

[Single CTA sentence formatted to stand alone on its own line]

[Sign-off with real name and role]
```

Rules: one link maximum; CTA sentence must be copy-paste usable as a button label; no attachments mentioned.

## Section 3: Creator Marketplace Pitch (80–120 words)

```
[Product-audience fit statement — why this product suits creators in this category — 1–2 sentences]

[Commission, free-sample policy, payment schedule — 1–2 sentences]

[Deliverable expectation and support offered (hook sheet, talking points) — 1 sentence]

[Closing availability line — 1 sentence]
```

Formatted for: TikTok Creator Marketplace, Shopee Affiliate, Aspire, LTK.

## Section 4: FAQ Block (5 items, 1–2 sentences each)

1. **Product sending:** [free unit policy, shipping timeline, who pays]
2. **Exclusivity:** [whether competing products are allowed]
3. **Payment:** [schedule, method, minimum threshold]
4. **Content approval:** [pre-approval requirement or freedom, any compliance must-says]
5. **Link setup:** [how the affiliate link/code is generated and tracked]

## Section 5: Follow-up Message (60–100 words)

One follow-up scheduled 3–4 days later with a DIFFERENT lead angle than the original. State which angle was used originally and which is used in the follow-up.

## Quality gates before delivery

- Opener names something only this creator does — would fail if sent to anyone else
- Earning math traces to AOV × commission × stated conversion assumption
- Exactly one CTA per message
- Word counts within range for each variant
- Tone matches the requested brand voice across all three variants
