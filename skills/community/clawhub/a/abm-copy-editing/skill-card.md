## Description: <br>
Helps agents review and improve existing marketing copy through focused editing passes for clarity, voice, customer relevance, proof, specificity, emotional impact, and risk reduction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mariokarras](https://clawhub.ai/user/mariokarras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, growth, sales, and product teams use this skill to tighten and strengthen existing marketing copy while preserving the original message. It is intended for review, polishing, proofreading, copy sweeps, and rewrite suggestions rather than writing new copy from scratch. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use local product marketing or brand voice context files if they exist, which could expose sensitive business or customer information to the editing workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Only keep information in those context files that is intended to guide copy feedback, and remove highly sensitive details before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Suggested edits may unintentionally change claim strength, positioning, or compliance-sensitive wording. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before publishing, especially claims involving results, guarantees, testimonials, pricing, or regulated products. <br>
