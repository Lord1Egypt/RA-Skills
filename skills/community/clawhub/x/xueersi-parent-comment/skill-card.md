## Description: <br>
Generates warm, appropriate parent signature comments for report cards, homework books, and school communication forms based on a child's performance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhouzhi-tal](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhouzhi-tal) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Parents and guardians use this skill to draft concise, constructive school comments in a parent voice for report cards, homework books, and school communication forms. It can produce several tone variants and adapt wording for Traditional Chinese or English when requested. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include unnecessary personal details about a child when requesting a comment. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum school-performance context needed and avoid personal identifiers or sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Generated comments may not fully match the parent's intent, the child's situation, or the school's expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated comments before sending them to a teacher or school. <br>
