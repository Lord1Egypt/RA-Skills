## Prompt 1 Output — Positioning & Niche Selection

**Top 3 Niches:**

1. **Hospitality Operations Automation** (Las Vegas)
   - Demand: 9/10 — 450+ hotels/resorts, nearly all running manual F&B and scheduling
   - Competition: 3/10 — no specialist agencies targeting Las Vegas hospitality ops
   - Avg project: $5,000–$15,000
   - Core pain: Ops managers waste 15-20 hrs/week reconciling PMS, POS, and spreadsheets
   - Marcus's edge: IT ops background + LV network + speaks hotel back-of-house language

2. **Property Management Automation** (Las Vegas/Nevada)
   - Demand: 7/10 — large rental market, most PMs still on manual lease/maintenance workflows
   - Competition: 5/10 — some national players but no local specialist
   - Avg project: $3,000–$8,000

3. **Casino-Adjacent Business Automation** (Gaming support vendors, F&B suppliers)
   - Demand: 8/10 — high-volume transaction businesses with complex reporting needs
   - Competition: 2/10 — very niche, almost no competition
   - Avg project: $8,000–$20,000

**Recommendation:** Hospitality Operations — best combination of demand, margin, and Marcus's background.

**Positioning Statement:**  
"NV Tech Automation helps Las Vegas hotels and resorts cut manual reporting and scheduling work by 40% using Zapier and Google Workspace — without hiring a developer or replacing your existing systems."

**30-Second Cold Intro:**  
"I help hotel operations teams in Las Vegas stop losing 15 hours a week to manual data work. I build automations using the tools your team already has — Zapier, Google Sheets, your PMS — so your ops manager gets their mornings back. I've spent 15 years in IT operations and I know exactly how hotel back-of-house systems talk to each other, or don't."

**Core Services:**
1. Daily Operations Dashboard Automation — automatically pulls nightly numbers from PMS and POS into a live Google Sheets dashboard. GM sees everything at 7AM without anyone touching a keyboard.
2. Staff Scheduling & Call-Out Alerts — automates shift change notifications and fill requests via Slack/SMS. No more phone trees when someone calls out at 5AM.
3. Vendor Invoice Routing — routes incoming invoices to the right approver, tracks status, sends payment reminders. Accounting team stops chasing paper.
