## Description: <br>
Queries and summarizes Europe, Australia, New Zealand, and Canada market policies, regulations, and investment-environment information with configurable data sources and Chinese or English output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business users, analysts, and developers can use this skill to query regional policy categories and generate basic investment-environment summaries for Europe, Australia, New Zealand, and Canada workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Configured data-source or API-key values may be included in returned results. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive test values until the implementation is reviewed or changed to avoid echoing secrets. <br>
Risk: The implementation returns placeholder policy data rather than verified live policy information. <br>
Mitigation: Validate policy outputs against authoritative sources before using them for business or compliance decisions. <br>
