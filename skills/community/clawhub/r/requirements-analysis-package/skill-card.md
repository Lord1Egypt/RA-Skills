## Description: <br>
Help a product or design team turn fuzzy feature requests into structured requirement artifacts for planning and handoff. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, designers, and development teams use this skill to clarify ambiguous feature requests into business goals, user scenarios, functional scope, user stories, acceptance criteria, and prioritization notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad requirements-planning description could lead to use during general product discussion where structured templates are not needed. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill for explicit requirements-planning, handoff, user-story, acceptance-criteria, or prioritization tasks. <br>
Risk: Template-generated requirement artifacts may omit stakeholder context, edge cases, or delivery constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs with product, design, and engineering stakeholders before treating them as handoff-ready requirements. <br>
