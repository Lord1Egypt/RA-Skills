## Description: <br>
Guides users through a Chinese-language coaching workflow for learning by building a minimal prototype, identifying defects, testing improvement hypotheses, and capturing reusable methods. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[li-evan](https://clawhub.ai/user/li-evan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill when they want to make, research, improve, or learn something through an iterative prototype-first coaching process. The agent prompts users to surface their own problems and test improvements rather than directly doing the work for them. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may trigger on broad learning or optimization requests where the user wants another language or a narrower workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use clearer activation rules or confirm the user's preferred workflow and language before applying the coaching process. <br>
Risk: Assuming a user's prior knowledge can make the coaching ineffective or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the user to confirm relevant prior knowledge before tailoring improvement steps. <br>
