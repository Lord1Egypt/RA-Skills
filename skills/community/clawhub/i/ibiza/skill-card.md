## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Visa, tax, transport, healthcare, rental, and venue details may change after publication. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details from official or direct sources before acting on the guidance or spending money. <br>
Risk: Rental decisions can expose identity documents, financial documents, deposits, and payments to misuse or fraud. <br>
Mitigation: Use reputable channels, verify ownership and contract terms, and protect identity and financial documents during rental workflows. <br>
Risk: Nightlife, water, cliff, driving, and boating activities can involve current local safety conditions not captured in static guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Check current local safety conditions and follow official or venue-specific safety instructions before participating. <br>
