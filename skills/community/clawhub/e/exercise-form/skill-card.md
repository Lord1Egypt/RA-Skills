## Description: <br>
Exercise form guide with warmup routines and workout plans. Use when planning workouts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain-lab](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain-lab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to retrieve concise exercise-form cues, muscle-group exercise suggestions, warmups, routines, category lists, and training tips for workout planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Exercise guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a user's health status, ability, or training context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as generic fitness guidance and consult a qualified trainer or medical professional for individualized advice. <br>
Risk: The helper runs local Bash commands and creates a disclosed local data directory. <br>
Mitigation: Review the script before execution in managed environments and confirm local file creation is acceptable. <br>
