## Description: <br>
Helps agents guide contractors, developers, designers, investors, and homeowners through building permit planning, application checklists, plan review issues, inspection sequencing, and fee estimation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to answer building permit questions, classify likely permit needs, prepare residential or commercial application checklists, plan inspection sequences, and estimate typical permit fees. Its outputs should be checked against the local building department or a licensed professional before action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Permit requirements, fees, inspection sequences, and code interpretations vary by jurisdiction and can change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify outputs with the local building department or a licensed professional before filing, scheduling work, or relying on cost estimates. <br>
Risk: Permit applications may involve property records, ownership documents, plans, or other sensitive project details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the information needed for the task and avoid providing unnecessary personal property documents. <br>
