## Description: <br>
Provides a staged A-share profit-taking framework that locks in gains without trying to sell at the absolute peak. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External A-share investors and agents use this skill for general sell-decision support: selling 50% at 10% profit, 30% at 20% profit, and keeping 20% to follow the trend until a breakdown signal. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The staged profit-taking framework could be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general decision support and confirm position size, risk tolerance, and market context before acting. <br>
Risk: Fixed 10%, 20%, and trend-following thresholds may not fit every A-share position or market condition. <br>
Mitigation: Use the thresholds as a discipline framework, not as automatic trading instructions. <br>
