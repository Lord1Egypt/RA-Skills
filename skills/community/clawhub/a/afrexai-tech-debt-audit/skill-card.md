## Description: <br>
Systematically identifies, scores, and prioritizes technical debt across codebases with impact analysis and detailed remediation roadmaps for engineering teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Engineering managers, CTOs, tech leads, and agencies use this skill to audit technical debt, quantify business impact, estimate carrying cost, and plan remediation work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Technical debt audits may involve sensitive architecture, workflow, cost, or proprietary source details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the information needed for the audit and exclude secrets, credentials, customer data, or proprietary code unless that information is intentionally in scope. <br>
