## Description: <br>
Analyzes contract text to extract key clauses, risk indicators, party obligations, compliance notes, and negotiation recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dj-520-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/dj-520-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Consultants, freelancers, small business owners, and analysts use this skill to triage contract language, identify common clause categories, and surface review notes before human legal review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace metadata advertises crypto and purchase capabilities that do not match a contract-review tool. <br>
Mitigation: Review the capability tags before installing and use only if those tags are corrected or verified harmless for the deployment. <br>
Risk: Contract findings may be incomplete because the skill uses simple pattern matching. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a triage aid and route important contracts to qualified legal review. <br>
