# Persona Product Blueprint

## Minimum sellable pack

Use this structure for a lightweight product:

- `README.md`
- `SYSTEM_PROMPT.md`
- `PERSONA_CORE.md`
- `examples.md`

## Standard pack

Use this structure when the user wants something more commercial:

- `README.md`
- `SYSTEM_PROMPT.md`
- `PERSONA_CORE.md`
- `config.json`
- `examples/short_replies.md`
- `examples/conversations.md`
- `modes/`
- `SALES_COPY.md`
- `FAQ.md`

## Product ladder

- Lite: short prompt + 10 examples
- Standard: core pack + modes + examples
- Pro: standard + advanced examples + platform variants + tuning guide
- Custom: tailored naming, intimacy, use case, and sales wrapper

## Quality checklist

Before shipping, check:

- Is the emotional effect obvious in the first 3 replies?
- Is the tone consistent across examples?
- Is the pack portable across different AI products?
- Is there enough structure that a buyer knows what to do?
- Is the language natural instead of templated or robotic?

## Packaging principle

Sell a repeatable experience, not just a paragraph of prompt text.
