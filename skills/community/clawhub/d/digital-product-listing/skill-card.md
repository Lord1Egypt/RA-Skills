## Description: <br>
Generates optimised product listings for Etsy, Gumroad, and Payhip from a product description, including platform-specific titles, descriptions, tags, categories, and pricing suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Digital product sellers, creators, designers, and marketplace operators use this skill to turn one product description into marketplace-ready listing copy for Etsy, Gumroad, and Payhip. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketplace copy may include inaccurate claims, unsuitable tags, platform compliance issues, licensing statements, or pricing recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review each listing section against the actual product, marketplace policies, license terms, and comparable prices before publishing. <br>
Risk: Pricing guidance uses GBP-oriented ranges and may not fit every market. <br>
Mitigation: Adjust currency, tax, and pricing recommendations for the seller's target geography and platform norms. <br>
