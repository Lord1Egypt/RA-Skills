## Description: <br>
Acts as the user's personal care and grooming assistant in the Hygiene topic. Tracks grooming routines, skincare, dental, haircuts, purchases of hygiene products. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[GekaCross](https://clawhub.ai/user/GekaCross) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to track personal hygiene routines, skincare and grooming products, haircut timing, dental visits, and related shopping reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal care routines, dental visit timing, product lists, and similar private details may be stored in the local workspace file. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in workspaces where that information is acceptable to store, and review or delete knowledge/personal/hygiene.md when the workspace is shared, backed up, or no longer needs it. <br>
