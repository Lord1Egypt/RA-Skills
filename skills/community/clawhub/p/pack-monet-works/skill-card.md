## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketing teams, and developers use this content pack to run financial or investment-adjacent drafts through quality, compliance, and humanization checks before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The pack points to referenced skills rather than containing the full remediation logic itself. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect monet-works-content-qa-dv and ogilvy-humanizer-dv before installing or relying on this pack. <br>
Risk: Automated disclaimer, CTA, and wording changes may be inappropriate for financial or regulated content if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated edits as draft assistance, preserve originals where possible, and require qualified review before publication. <br>
