## Description: <br>
Free daily growth assistant for online businesses. Track basic revenue metrics, get daily action priorities, and monitor progress toward goals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[apex-stack-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/apex-stack-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Online business owners and solo developers use this skill to manually review basic revenue metrics, compare current performance with revenue goals, and choose daily growth priorities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share credentials, payment-platform exports, customer data, or sensitive financial details while requesting revenue analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Use manual summary metrics only and avoid sharing credentials, raw exports, customer data, or more financial detail than the analysis requires. <br>
Risk: Revenue recommendations may be incomplete because the Lite version uses one manually entered revenue source and does not read dashboards or detect anomalies. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current business records and treat the output as planning guidance rather than an automated financial control. <br>
Risk: The skill links to an external paid product, which may affect user expectations or purchasing decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the external product terms and capabilities separately before relying on or purchasing the paid version. <br>
