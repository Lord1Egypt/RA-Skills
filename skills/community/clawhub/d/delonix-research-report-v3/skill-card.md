## Description: <br>
Use when the user requests industry research reports, market analysis, business trend analysis, or professional research documents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chaoliuzhu](https://clawhub.ai/user/chaoliuzhu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users, analysts, strategists, and researchers use this skill to structure market analysis, industry research reports, competitive intelligence, investment theses, and technology trend reports in professional Markdown. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market research outputs may rely on current external sources and can become inaccurate or misleading if sources are weak, outdated, or uncited. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources, dates, assumptions, and data quality before using the report for investment, legal, regulatory, or strategic decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce recommendations or outlooks that users may treat as decision-ready analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer validate conclusions, risks, and recommendations against authoritative data and the intended business context. <br>
