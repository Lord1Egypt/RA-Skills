## Description: <br>
Generates 60 high-impact Twitter/X post ideas from reference content, organized by harsh advice, quotes, pain points, counterintuitive truths, key insights, and creative wildcards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[vincentchan](https://clawhub.ai/user/vincentchan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and social media teams use this skill to turn pasted material, draft files, notes, newsletters, scripts, or supplied URLs into categorized Twitter/X post ideas with short rationale and recommendation notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may fetch URLs supplied by the user and analyze their contents. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only URLs the user is authorized to share and review source-derived statements before use. <br>
Risk: Generated persuasive or high-conviction claims can be misleading if the source material is weak, outdated, or sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated tweet ideas for factual accuracy, attribution, privacy, and brand suitability before publishing. <br>
Risk: Derived content is saved locally under tweet-ideas/. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid supplying confidential material unless local derived markdown output is acceptable, and delete generated files when no longer needed. <br>
