## Description: <br>
Explain, summarize, analyze, or adapt the "Republic with No Masters" / Democratic Formalism governance framework when asked to produce content, guidance, critiques, FAQs, or implementation ideas based on the manifesto in principles.md. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, writers, and developers use this skill to produce summaries, explanations, critiques, FAQs, public-facing writing, or implementation ideas grounded in the bundled Democratic Formalism manifesto. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks an agent to treat a specific political-governance manifesto as authoritative within the requested framework. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when intentionally working within that framework, and request neutral comparison or critique when a broader viewpoint is needed. <br>
Risk: Manifesto-grounded proposals could be mistaken for real-world legal, operational, or adoption claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before use and require user-provided evidence before claiming legal enforceability, operational readiness, or real-world adoption. <br>
