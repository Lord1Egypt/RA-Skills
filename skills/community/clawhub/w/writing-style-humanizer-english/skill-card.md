## Description: <br>
Rewrites English text to reduce common AI-generated writing patterns while preserving the original meaning and intended tone. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shing19](https://clawhub.ai/user/shing19) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers, editors, and reviewers use this skill to revise English drafts that sound AI-generated into more natural prose while preserving the author's meaning and intended tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is explicitly built to make AI-written text look unaided and human-written, which can conflict with academic, workplace, publishing, legal, or platform disclosure rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only on text you are allowed to revise, and disclose AI assistance whenever the applicable rules require it. <br>
Risk: Rewrites may introduce unsupported first-person claims, opinions, or changes to the author's actual meaning. <br>
Mitigation: Review the final text against the source, verify factual claims, and reject edits that change the author's intended message. <br>
