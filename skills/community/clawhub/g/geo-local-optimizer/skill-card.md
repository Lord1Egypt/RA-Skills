## Description: <br>
Local business-focused GEO optimization orchestrator for AI-powered local search across store pages, map listings, reviews, and AI answer visibility. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[GEOlyAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/GEOlyAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External business owners, marketers, and local SEO/GEO practitioners use this skill to create structured local optimization plans for shops, clinics, restaurants, service providers, stores, franchises, and service areas. It helps align local pages, map and directory listings, reviews, Q&A, structured data, and crawler signals so AI systems can understand and cite local business information. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive or business-critical location details such as addresses, service areas, opening hours, listings, and review strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for the planning task and verify business details before using them in public listings or pages. <br>
Risk: Marketing, structured data, listing, or review recommendations may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a specific local market or regulated business category. <br>
Mitigation: Review all recommendations before publication and adapt claims, schema, and review practices to the business facts, platform policies, and local requirements. <br>
