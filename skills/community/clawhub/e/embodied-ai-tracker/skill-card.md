## Description: <br>
Tracks frontier embodied AI research, open-source projects, lab updates, and demo-video material, then produces a structured daily report with publication dates, source links, and video-content leads. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jessy-huang](https://clawhub.ai/user/jessy-huang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, researchers, and developers use this skill to monitor recent embodied AI work and collect source links, demo-video leads, and trend notes for daily reporting or video planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requests broad shell and local file access for a workflow that primarily needs public web research. <br>
Mitigation: Run it with web/search-only permissions where possible, or require confirmation before any shell command or local file access. <br>
Risk: The digest depends on current web search results and may include stale, unavailable, or incorrectly summarized links. <br>
Mitigation: Verify key paper, code, and video links before publishing or using the report for external content. <br>
