## Description: <br>
Vision Simulator predicts campaign outcomes, product launches, and market reactions using multi-agent simulation with optional live news, trend, and competitor data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dorongss](https://clawhub.ai/user/dorongss) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External marketers and product teams use this skill to evaluate campaign, launch, and market scenarios. It combines persona-style agent reactions with market context to produce a prediction report and prioritized actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Live data collection may send scenario keywords or launch details to web or search providers. <br>
Mitigation: Use manual or basic inputs, or skip live web collection, for confidential launches and strategy. <br>
Risk: Forecast scores and success probabilities can be mistaken for guaranteed marketing outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat predictions as decision-support estimates and review recommendations before acting on them. <br>
