## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may involve sensitive insurance customer data such as identity numbers, phone numbers, health details, bank information, recordings, and claim data. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit consent, collect only necessary fields, mask or encrypt sensitive data, enforce access controls, and define retention and deletion handling before production use. <br>
Risk: Insurance rules, product terms, and PIPL obligations may change or vary by insurer and jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current regulations, insurer policy terms, and compliance requirements with qualified reviewers before using outputs with customers. <br>
Risk: Automated service flows could collect or process information before the customer intent, language preference, or recording consent is clear. <br>
Mitigation: Add explicit intent confirmation, consent prompts, and language handling before collecting or processing personal, policy, claim, or recording data. <br>
