## The 7 Compliance Moats

| Moat | What It Enforces | Why It Matters |
|------|-----------------|----------------|
| IICRC Certification Level | CTS/CCT/WFR/WRT/AMRT scope gating; Firm cert # in all credentialing claims | Wrong cert level in ads = consumer deception; IICRC ethics complaint exposure |
| IICRC S100 Fiber pH | pH 5–8 for wool, pH 2–10 for nylon, pH 2–11 for olefin — enforced per S100 | Alkaline pre-spray on wool at pH > 8.5 = irreversible fiber damage; warranty void |
| Truck-Mount vs. Portable | Equipment type, temperature (150–212°F), CFM (300–500), vacuum lift (14–16") | Portable units (100°F, 150 CFM) leave 40% more moisture — false drying time claims |
| Drying Time Accuracy | Equipment-spec only + ambient condition qualifier | "Dries in 2 hours guaranteed" = FTC deceptive practice + #1 consumer complaint trigger |
| WoolSafe Certification | Membership required before wool/natural fiber cleaning claims | Shaw, Mohawk Karastan, Couristan wool warranty voided by non-WoolSafe chemistry |
| Health/Allergen Claims | EPA Reg. # required for "kills bacteria"; "allergen-free" blocked | FIFRA violation; FTC Section 5 medical benefit claim without clinical evidence |
| FTC 2023 / TCPA / CAN-SPAM | No review incentives; SMS written consent; email unsubscribe | TCPA: $500–$1,500 per non-consented text; FTC review incentive enforcement active |
