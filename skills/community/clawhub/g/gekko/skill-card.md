## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to request Base DeFi portfolio analysis, yield comparisons, token analysis, market intelligence, and strategy guidance from the Gekko API. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Inputs are sent to Gekko's remote service. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit seed phrases, private keys, API keys, wallet authentication material, confidential trading plans, or nonpublic strategy details. <br>
Risk: DeFi analysis and market recommendations can be incorrect, stale, or unsuitable for a user's financial situation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations independently and review risks before moving funds or changing allocations. <br>
