## Description: <br>
Federation Ethical Hand enforces non-interference, equity, plurality, and cultural sensitivity through a 5-phase ethics pattern for ethically complex requests. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dmater01](https://clawhub.ai/user/dmater01) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and users apply this skill when requests involve fairness, cross-cultural policy, data sovereignty, or ambiguous ethical tradeoffs. It guides responses through a 5-phase ethics pattern that surfaces multiple cultural perspectives and keeps value-laden decisions with the user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can broadly influence answers on ethically sensitive requests. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for context fit, keep cultural lenses explicit, and defer contested value judgments to the user. <br>
Risk: The skill may keep local ethics artifacts or metrics under ~/.openclaw/hands/federation_ethic_hand. <br>
Mitigation: Review, clear, or disable retained local files when local records are not wanted. <br>
