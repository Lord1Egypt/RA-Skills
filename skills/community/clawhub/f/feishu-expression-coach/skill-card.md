## Description: <br>
表达教练分析用户提供的文字或语音转文字内容，指出啰嗦、模糊、逻辑混乱、语气不当等表达问题，并给出优化建议和改写版本。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and writers use this skill to review drafts, message previews, and speech-to-text content for clarity, brevity, logic, tone, and completeness before sending. <br>

Global <br>
