## Description: <br>
Customer Research helps sales agents research customer background and optional competitor or procurement intelligence from public sources, then generate Word research reports and scenario icebreaker presentations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[andyrenxu7255](https://clawhub.ai/user/andyrenxu7255) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales teams and sales agents use this skill to prepare for customer visits by gathering public customer, AI adoption, procurement, and competitor signals. The skill supports local report and presentation generation after user confirmation and before any approved delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Public-source customer or competitor research can be incomplete or misleading if generated without verifying actual search results. <br>
Mitigation: Require actual searches, cite sources, mark unavailable findings clearly, and avoid fabricating procurement records or customer details. <br>
Risk: Generated sales reports or presentations could be sent to unintended recipients or channels. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the customer, output location, recipient, and channel, and require user review before any files are sent. <br>
