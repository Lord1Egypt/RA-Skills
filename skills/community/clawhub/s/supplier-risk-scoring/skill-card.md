## Description: <br>
Generates a 0-100 Supplier Risk Index score across financial, dependency, compliance, performance, and geographic risks with tiered action plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Procurement, operations, finance, legal, and compliance teams use this skill to score supplier risk consistently at onboarding, during periodic reviews, and after material vendor changes. It helps prioritize oversight, contingency planning, escalation, and sourcing decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The framework includes geography-based, sanctions-related, and regulatory scoring guidance that may not match an organization's policies or current legal obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Customize those scoring criteria to approved organizational policies and require legal or compliance review before using the score for procurement decisions. <br>
Risk: Supplier assessments may use sensitive or third-party business information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only supplier information the organization is authorized to review and handle it under the applicable vendor, privacy, and procurement controls. <br>
Risk: A low supplier score could lead to adverse procurement action if applied mechanically. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the score as decision support and require human review before withholding awards, changing contract terms, escalating vendors, or initiating transition plans. <br>
