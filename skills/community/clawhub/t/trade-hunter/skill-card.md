## Description: <br>
B2B foreign-trade customer discovery skill for collecting public company data globally and applying an initial Business Opportunity Index score. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangm-a3](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangm-a3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sales, sourcing, and business-development users use this skill to find potential B2B customers by industry, region, and revenue profile, then review a scored lead list before outreach. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business contact data and outreach workflows can create privacy, marketing compliance, or unsolicited bulk outreach risk. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm a lawful basis before collecting or using contact data, follow applicable email marketing and privacy rules, and avoid bulk unsolicited outreach. <br>
Risk: Lead exports can expose contact information if saved or shared insecurely. <br>
Mitigation: Store and export lead files only in approved secure locations with appropriate access controls. <br>
Risk: Company and contact results from public commercial data may be stale or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review and verify lead details before relying on them for qualification, outreach, or business decisions. <br>
