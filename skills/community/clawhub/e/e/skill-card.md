## Description: <br>
Short alias skill for editing files, code, or configurations. Use when you need to make changes to existing content using text editors or IDE shortcuts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlang-cn](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlang-cn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to choose a suitable text editor or command-line workflow for small, controlled edits to files, code, and configuration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad editing shortcuts can change local files or configuration unexpectedly when used with vague targets or wide replacement commands. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit target paths, keep backups or version control available, and review diffs after edits. <br>
Risk: Bulk replacements or full-directory restore commands can discard intended work. <br>
Mitigation: Search and narrow the scope before replacing text, and avoid whole-directory restore commands unless the diff has been reviewed. <br>
