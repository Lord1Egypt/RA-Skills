## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends product descriptions or URLs and target-audience details to a third-party API. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential launch plans, proprietary positioning, customer lists, sensitive market research, and regulated data unless the API service is trusted for that use. <br>
Risk: Generated persuasion or advertising content could be misleading, inappropriate, or unsuitable for regulated marketing contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review all buyer psychology and ad copy before use, especially for regulated products, sensitive audiences, or claims that require substantiation. <br>
