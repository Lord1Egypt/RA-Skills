## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common legal services questions for legal ops teams, attorneys, and compliance teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Legal ops teams, attorneys, and compliance teams use this skill to draft and refine customer-facing FAQ content for legal services. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted FAQ content may be inaccurate, jurisdiction-specific, or mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified legal staff review client-facing or jurisdiction-specific statements before use. <br>
Risk: Using web search or other external tools with confidential client details could expose sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential client information into web search or other external services. <br>
