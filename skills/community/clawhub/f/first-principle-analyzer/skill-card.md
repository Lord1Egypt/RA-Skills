## Description: <br>
Use this skill when analyzing complex problems through first principles. Provides structured 7-phase analysis framework, assumption identification and challenging, basic truth extraction, and innovative solution generation from fundamentals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, product managers, entrepreneurs, and decision-makers use this skill to structure complex technical, business, product, research, or career questions into assumptions, basic truths, solution options, comparisons, and recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review flags the skill as suspicious because it asks for more local command authority and sharing capability than its decision-analysis purpose clearly justifies. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, grant only the local command authority needed for the intended workflow, and avoid share or export workflows until data handling is independently verified. <br>
Risk: Analysis reports may contain confidential business, research, financial, or personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use share or export workflows for sensitive information unless the destination, access controls, and retention behavior have been verified. <br>
