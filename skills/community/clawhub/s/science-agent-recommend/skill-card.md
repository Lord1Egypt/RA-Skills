## Description: <br>
Get personalized AI agent recommendations for a research domain, task, or workflow from curated science AI projects and skill hubs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnnychen1113](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnnychen1113) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, lab staff, and developers use this skill to narrow a research domain or workflow into AI agent and skill-hub recommendations with links and brief fit rationale. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Linked third-party projects may request credentials, local files, clinical data, or research datasets after the user leaves this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review each linked project separately before installing it or granting access to credentials, local files, clinical data, or research datasets. <br>
