## Description: <br>
Researches public trend signals across Reddit, Hacker News, X, YouTube, and GitHub to generate multi-platform, actionable trend reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aptratcn](https://clawhub.ai/user/aptratcn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and product teams use this skill to collect public trend signals across discussion, code, and video platforms and synthesize an actionable Markdown report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research topics may be sent to external public search or API services. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for topics appropriate for external research services. <br>
Risk: Trend reports can include incomplete, stale, or misleading public-source signals. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited sources and important findings before relying on the report. <br>
