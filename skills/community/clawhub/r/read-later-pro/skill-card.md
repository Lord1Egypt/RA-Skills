## Description: <br>
Read Later Pro helps agents save web articles for offline reading, extract clean article content from URLs, export articles to Markdown, PDF, EPUB, or HTML, and organize reading lists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external users use this skill to capture web articles, clean extracted article text, convert saved content into offline reading formats, and manage a searchable local reading library. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can fetch article URLs and store saved content locally. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use it only where fetching those URLs and retaining local article copies is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Optional Pocket or Instapaper imports may involve credentials or access tokens. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid placing passwords or long-lived tokens directly in shell commands; prefer temporary tokens, environment variables, or secure prompts, and revoke tokens when no longer needed. <br>
