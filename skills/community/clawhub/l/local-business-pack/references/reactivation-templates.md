# Customer Reactivation Templates

For customers who haven't visited in 60–180 days.
Placeholders: {first_name}, {last_service}, {business_name}

Rule: Lead with value. Never lead with absence or guilt.

---

## Hair Salon / Beauty

**Message 1 — "We miss you" (day 0):**
"Hey {first_name}! We've got some exciting new styles and treatments this season. Thought of you! — {business_name}"

**Message 2 — Value add (day 5):**
"Hi {first_name}! Quick tip: {seasonal hair care tip}. We use [product] in-salon if you ever want to try it. — {business_name}"

**Message 3 — Soft offer (day 12):**
"Hi {first_name}, we're doing a 'welcome back' rate for returning clients this month only. Want to grab a spot before we fill up? — {business_name}"

---

## Dental / Medical

**Message 1:**
"Hi {first_name}! Just a heads up — [new service/technology] is now available at {business_name}. Thought you'd want to know!"

**Message 2:**
"Hi {first_name}, did you know [relevant health tip tied to their last service]? Happy to answer any questions when you're ready for your next visit."

**Message 3:**
"Hi {first_name}, it's been a while since your last {last_service}. We have openings this month — want to lock one in? — {business_name}"

---

## Restaurant / Cafe

**Message 1:**
"Hey {first_name}! We just launched [new menu item/seasonal special] and immediately thought of you. Hope you're doing great! — {business_name}"

**Message 2:**
"Hi {first_name}! Quick one: we're now open [new hours / new location / new feature]. Figured you'd want to know. — {business_name}"

**Message 3:**
"Hey {first_name}, we've got a little something for returning guests this week. Come say hi? — {business_name}"

---

## Contractor / Home Services

**Message 1:**
"Hi {first_name}! Heading into [season] — great time to get ahead of [relevant maintenance]. How's everything holding up since your last {last_service}? — {business_name}"

**Message 2:**
"Hi {first_name}, we put together a quick [season] checklist for homeowners that you might find useful. Want me to send it over? — {business_name}"

**Message 3:**
"Hi {first_name}, we're booking [service] appointments for [month] now — slots go fast this time of year. Want to lock one in? — {business_name}"

---

## Writing rules
- Message 1 never mentions they've been absent
- Message 2 is 100% value — no ask
- Message 3 is the only message with a clear CTA
- Time-limit the offer subtly ("this month", "before we fill up") — not fake urgency
- Always sound like a person, not a CRM drip sequence
