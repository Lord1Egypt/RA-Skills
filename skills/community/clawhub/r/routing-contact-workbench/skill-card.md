## Use Case: <br>
Operations or support users use this skill to record the selected recipient for a routine client communication in a controlled recipient-logging workflow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat this narrow validation-style workbench as a general communications or CRM integration. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for controlled recipient-logging workflows and verify the recipient value before relying on the record. <br>
Risk: A recipient value may be logged incorrectly if the prior coordination context is ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the intended recipient before recording it and avoid adding unrelated account details. <br>
