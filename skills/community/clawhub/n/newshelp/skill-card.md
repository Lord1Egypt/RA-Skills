## Description: <br>
NewsHelp helps an agent navigate newshelp.org by using the homepage, tags index, topic pages, and search-engine shortcuts to reach news content efficiently. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nerkn](https://clawhub.ai/user/nerkn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and users use this skill to find current or topic-specific NewsHelp articles faster by navigating direct tag URLs, the tags index, the homepage, or an optional search-engine query. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: NewsHelp content may be incomplete, outdated, or biased as a news source. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as navigation guidance and corroborate important claims with trusted sources before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The optional search-engine shortcut can expose query terms to the chosen search provider. <br>
Mitigation: Use direct NewsHelp URLs or the tags index for sensitive topics, and avoid entering private or confidential terms into external search engines. <br>
