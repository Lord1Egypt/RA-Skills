## Description: <br>
Help users prepare for doctor appointments by organizing symptoms, questions, medication lists, and medical history. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People with upcoming medical visits use this skill to prepare a concise appointment checklist with symptom timelines, questions, medications, supplements, relevant history, and allergies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter private health details while preparing their appointment notes. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid unnecessary personal identifiers and follow applicable privacy practices before sharing sensitive health information in chat. <br>
Risk: Prepared questions and checklists could be mistaken for medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as appointment preparation only and rely on licensed clinicians for diagnosis, treatment, and medication decisions. <br>
