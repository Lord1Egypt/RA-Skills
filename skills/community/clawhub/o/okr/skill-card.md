## Description: <br>
基于流行 OKR 体系管理智能体目标（O）与关键结果（KR）。当用户提到目标管理、季度计划、OKR、关键结果、复盘、评分、经验沉淀时使用，并将信息维护到 memory/okr.md。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
People using OpenClaw use this skill to plan objectives, define measurable key results, track OKR progress, and complete retrospectives with rating labels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: OKR notes are retained in a local memory file and may include sensitive planning details if users provide them. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing passwords, keys, private credentials, or highly sensitive personal or business details, and review ~/.openclaw/workspace/memory/okr.md when needed. <br>
Risk: Progress updates and retrospective ratings may be misleading when they are not grounded in clear key-result evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Base updates on measurable KR values and ask for clarification before closing an objective or assigning a final rating. <br>
