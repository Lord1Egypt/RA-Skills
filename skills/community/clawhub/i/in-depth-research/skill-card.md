## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, analysts, developers, and decision-makers use this skill to plan and conduct structured multi-source investigations, evaluate source credibility, synthesize contradictions, and deliver research reports with confidence, caveats, gaps, and methodology notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research summaries may combine public information of varying credibility, which can mislead high-stakes medical, legal, financial, or business decisions if accepted without verification. <br>
Mitigation: Set clear depth and source requirements, prioritize primary sources, document uncertainty, and independently verify conclusions before relying on them for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad research can stop before all important gaps are resolved, especially when scope or time budget is unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the intended depth and decision context before starting, document stop conditions, and explicitly list unresolved gaps in the final output. <br>
