## Description: <br>
SEO and GEO playbook that helps AI agents research keywords, draft optimized content, create comparison pages, configure structured data and IndexNow, and monitor AI search citations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, founders, content teams, and developer-tool teams use this skill with AI agents to build SEO/GEO strategy, content workflows, structured data, comparison pages, and AI-citation monitoring for websites and SaaS products. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Applying robots.txt or IndexNow examples too broadly could make admin, API, staging, or private URLs discoverable by crawlers and search systems. <br>
Mitigation: Audit the sitemap and submitted URLs first, keep private areas protected by authentication, and submit only pages intended for public search and AI crawler discovery. <br>
