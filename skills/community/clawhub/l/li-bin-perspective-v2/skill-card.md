## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs, product leaders, and strategy teams use this skill to frame decisions through a simulated Li Bin business perspective focused on user enterprise thinking, long-term survival, community operations, and value-chain control. ## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill simulates a public figure's business perspective and may be mistaken for an official statement from Li Bin or NIO. <br>
Mitigation: Label outputs as simulated perspective and avoid presenting generated responses as official statements. <br>
Risk: Generated strategic, factual, financial, or market claims may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims against reliable current sources before using them for business, financial, or strategic decisions. <br>
