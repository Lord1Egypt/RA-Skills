## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill scrapes public Lowyat forum usernames, timestamps, and comments into Excel files, which can create privacy or terms-of-service concerns if stored or shared carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Use only forum.lowyat.net topic URLs, avoid bulk scraping, and consider Lowyat forum terms and privacy implications before storing or sharing outputs. <br>
Risk: The security summary notes scoping and hardening gaps for a public forum search-and-scrape helper. <br>
Mitigation: Review target URLs before execution, limit scraping volume, and inspect generated files before relying on or redistributing the analysis. <br>
