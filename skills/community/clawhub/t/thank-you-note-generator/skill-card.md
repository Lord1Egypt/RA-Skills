## Description: <br>
Write heartfelt and professional thank you notes for any occasion. Perfect for showing gratitude after interviews, gifts, help, or acts of kindness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and professionals use this skill to draft thank you notes for interviews, gifts, personal help, events, and business partnerships. It helps adapt tone, length, and specific details for the occasion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated notes may include inaccurate, overly generic, or sensitive personal, workplace, or business details. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit each note before sending, and avoid including unnecessary sensitive information. <br>
