## Description: <br>
I Ching / Zhouyi assistant for hexagram lookup, coin/number/time divination, changing-line interpretation, and practical reflection. Use bundled scripts for deterministic divination data when available; treat guidance as reflective advice, not prediction, medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical instruction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use Yi to look up I Ching and Zhouyi hexagrams, run coin, number, or time divination workflows, interpret changing lines, and turn symbolic readings into practical reflection. The skill is positioned for reflective or entertainment guidance, not medical, legal, financial, safety-critical, or other high-stakes decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat divination output as a prediction or as advice for medical, legal, financial, safety-critical, or other high-stakes decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present readings as reflective or entertainment guidance, retain user responsibility, and redirect high-stakes decisions to qualified professional advice. <br>
Risk: Bundled hexagram data may be incomplete for some readings. <br>
Mitigation: Quote or summarize only data returned by the bundled scripts and state when classical text or line data is unavailable. <br>
Risk: Simulated coin divination can be mistaken for user-supplied or deterministic evidence. <br>
Mitigation: State when randomness is simulated and record the method and input values used for the reading. <br>
