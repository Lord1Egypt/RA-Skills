## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Geo-blocking rules can incorrectly restrict access or affect users in sensitive regions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the intended regional rules, legal basis, and user impact before applying blocking behavior. <br>
Risk: The skill references the @clawhub/geo-shield npm package, whose dependencies and behavior are not embedded in the documentation artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review the package publisher, version, dependencies, and security posture before installing or deploying it. <br>
