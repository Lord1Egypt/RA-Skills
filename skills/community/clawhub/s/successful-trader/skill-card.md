## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill for trading education, strategy planning, risk-management discussion, technical and fundamental analysis, journaling templates, and performance review. It is not a substitute for personalized financial advice or controlled trade-execution systems. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat broad trading guidance as personalized financial advice or a guarantee of profit. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as educational information, include trading-risk disclaimers, and encourage users to do their own research or consult a licensed financial advisor. <br>
Risk: An agent using this skill could be connected to broker accounts or trade execution workflows without adequate controls. <br>
Mitigation: Do not connect the agent to broker accounts or permit trade execution unless separate, explicit authorization, review, and execution controls are in place. <br>
