## Description: <br>
Executes deep research by performing comprehensive web searches and synthesizing findings into detailed cited reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bird-frank](https://clawhub.ai/user/bird-frank) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and analysts use this skill to execute research plans by searching broadly, analyzing sources, and writing English Markdown reports with citations and an index entry. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad web research can surface unreliable or conflicting sources. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated report and citations before relying on the findings. <br>
Risk: The skill writes report files and updates index.md. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated file changes before preserving, sharing, or committing them. <br>
