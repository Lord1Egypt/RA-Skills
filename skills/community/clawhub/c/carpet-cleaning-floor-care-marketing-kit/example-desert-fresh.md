**How long until my carpet is dry?**

**Is hot water extraction safe for my kids and pets?**
Truck-mount hot water extraction uses heat and suction to physically remove particles from carpet fibers — no chemical residue is left behind in standard cleaning. We use pH-neutral, residue-free solutions. Pets and children can return to cleaned areas once dry (4-6 hours).

**Are you IICRC certified?**
