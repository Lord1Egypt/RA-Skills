## Description: <br>
Use when a compliance officer, security analyst, or auditor needs to assess an organization's controls against a regulatory framework (GDPR, SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, NIST CSF). Guides structured evidence collection per control domain and produces a gap analysis matrix, risk-rated findings, and a prioritized remediation roadmap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Compliance officers, security analysts, and auditors use this skill to run a structured readiness or reassessment interview against a chosen compliance framework and produce stakeholder-ready findings. It is suited to first-time audit preparation, post-audit progress tracking, scoping exercises, and executive reporting on compliance posture. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive compliance, security posture, employee, credential, or customer information during the assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum necessary details, avoid credentials and customer records, and keep sensitive identifiers out of the report unless required. <br>
Risk: The report is based on self-reported controls and may be incomplete or inaccurate without evidence review. <br>
Mitigation: Validate conclusions through internal evidence collection, qualified compliance review, legal review where needed, and third-party audit when appropriate. <br>
Risk: Regulatory or framework interpretations may be wrong or outdated if controls are inferred without checking the official standard. <br>
Mitigation: Verify uncertain requirements against the official published framework and mark ambiguous items for qualified reviewer confirmation. <br>
