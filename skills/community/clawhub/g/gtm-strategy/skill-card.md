Risk: The skill may activate for broad business terms such as ICP or buyer persona. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking for GTM planning before applying the framework. <br>
Risk: GTM planning can involve confidential customer lists, revenue figures, or unreleased strategy. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive business data in the agent session unless approved for that environment. <br>
