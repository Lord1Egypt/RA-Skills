## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal analysis can be incorrect, outdated, or mismatched to the facts of a specific matter. <br>
Mitigation: Require professional review of every analysis step and confirm the jurisdiction, current statutes, and relevant judicial interpretations before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: AI-generated case citations or summaries may be fabricated or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify every cited case through authoritative legal research sources before using it in legal work. <br>
Risk: The workflow depends on seven referenced subskills that are not included in this artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review the referenced subskills before relying on the full chained process. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include sensitive case facts or confidential client information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential facts unless the deployment environment is approved for that information. <br>
