This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, mentors, and competition coaches use this skill to diagnose or design a 国创赛 business model, align content with track-specific judging criteria, draft the business-model portion of a pitch deck, and prepare business-model defense answers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated competition materials may include outdated judging assumptions or market figures. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current competition rules and market data before submitting or presenting generated content. <br>
Risk: Users may share unreleased project details, contracts, private financials, or proprietary IP while drafting business-model materials. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential information unless the deployment environment is approved for that data, and review generated materials before external sharing. <br>
