## Description: <br>
Audits social media draft text for AI-content signature signals across eight linguistic dimensions and returns platform-specific scores, flagged patterns, and suggested fixes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[PHY041](https://clawhub.ai/user/PHY041) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and social media teams use this skill to audit posts before publication, understand which linguistic patterns may be read as AI-generated, and prioritize targeted edits. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be used to tune social posts to avoid AI-content detection, which may enable misrepresentation of authorship or attempts to bypass platform authenticity systems. <br>
Mitigation: Use the results only for transparent editing and compliance with platform rules, and review publication intent before relying on the recommendations. <br>
