A curated collection of battle-tested prompts and prompt engineering frameworks for content creators, crypto founders, and indie hackers.

## Workflow

1. Identify the task category
2. Pull the matching prompt from `references/prompts.md`
3. Customize variables (in brackets `[like this]`)
4. Execute and iterate based on output quality

## Prompt Engineering Principles

- **Be specific**: Vague prompts produce vague results
- **Set the role**: "You are a [ROLE]" dramatically improves output
- **Add constraints**: Format, length, tone, audience — all shape quality
- **Chain prompts**: Break complex tasks into sequential prompts
- **Test and iterate**: Save winning prompts — they're assets

## Variable Convention

Replace anything in `[brackets]` with your specific context. Do not remove the brackets — they mark customizable inputs.
