# 论文表达例句库

本文档收录从2020-2025年发表的高水平论文中提取的经典表达，供撰写论文时参考。

---

## 一、引言部分 (Introduction)

### 1.1 阐述研究背景

**Nature/Science 风格（简洁有力）:**
```
Water abundance in the lunar mantle provides insights into the giant impact formation models for the Moon and plays a crucial role in the crystallization of the lunar magma ocean and subsequent magma-ism and long-lived volcanism.
```

**GRL 风格（条理清晰）:**
```
Recently, China's Chang'E-5 mission targeted a higher latitude on the Moon than previous Apollo and Luna missions, and brought back scooped and drilled samples to the Earth. These new soil samples provide an opportunity to investigate the distribution, abundance, and origin(s) of water in Moon's middle latitude.
```

**中文期刊风格:**
```
为研究太阳风成因水在月表低纬度地区的分布特征，本文对中国科学院地质与地球物理研究所博物馆珍藏的一份Apollo 11月壤样品开展了单颗粒原位纳米离子探针同位素和水含量深度剖面分析。
```

### 1.2 引用前人研究

**经典句式:**
```
In the past two decades, substantial advances in secondary ion mass spectrometry have improved our knowledge of water in the lunar mantle, causing a shift from the once traditional 'bone dry' concept to a relatively 'wet' Moon.
```

```
Extensive studies of various lunar samples derived from the melting of lunar mantle have shown a heterogeneous distribution of water in the lunar interior with abundances ranging from about 1 μg g⁻¹ to 200 μg g⁻¹.
```

### 1.3 指出研究空白

```
However, most of these lunar water estimates derive from Apollo and Luna samples collected from low-latitude regions. Whether or not the solar wind-derived water in lunar soil grains can be preserved beneath the surface remains limited.
```

### 1.4 阐述研究目的

```
Here, we report the NanoSIMS measurements of abundances and D/H ratios on soil grains from three deepest sections of the Chang'E-5 drill core sampled at depths of 0.45–0.8 m.
```

## 二、方法部分 (Methods)

### 2.1 样品描述

```
A total of 578 particles varying from 0.1–1.5 mm in size were sieved and hand-picked from one CE6 soil sample (CE6C0200YJFM001 of 5 g). Mare basalt fragments account for around 28% of the selected particles characterized by scanning electron microscopy and electron probe microanalysis (EPMA).
```

```
The dominant hydrous mineral in mare basalts—apatite and entrapped melt hosted by olivine and ilmenite—were the main targets selected for this study.
```

### 2.2 实验方法

```
High-precision U–Pb dating and trace element analyses of zircon were conducted by LA-ICP-MS at the Institute of Geology and Geophysics, Chinese Academy of Sciences.
```

```
NanoSIMS depth-profiling measurements of H isotope and water content were carried out on Apollo 11 soils that were stored in the Geological Museum Institute of Geology and Geophysics, Chinese Academy of Sciences.
```

### 2.3 数据处理

```
The results show that the surface of all lunar grains (<200 nm) has a high water content, the maximum water content varying from 0.35% to 1.59%.
```

---

## 三、结果部分 (Results)

### 3.1 主要发现

```
The results show that most of the grain rims (topmost ~100 nm) show high abundances of hydrogen (1,116 to 2,516 ppm) with extremely low δD values (−908 to −992‰), implying nearly exclusively a SW origin.
```

```
The hydrogen-content depth distribution in the grain rims is phase-dependent, either bell-shaped for glass or monotonic decrease for mineral grains.
```

### 3.2 数据特征

```
Except for one plagioclase A11-021-1 whose surface H isotope composition (δD) is −262‰, the other grains have very low δD values ranging from −987‰ to −642‰.
```

```
High water contents of 0.13–1.3 wt.% are present on approximately half of the grain surfaces (topmost ∼100 nm).
```

### 3.3 对比分析

```
In contrast to the Apollo samples, the Chang'E-5 soils exhibit significantly higher water content and lower δD values.
```

## 四、讨论部分 (Discussion)

### 4.1 解释现象

```
These characteristics indicate that the high water content in the surface layer of lunar grains is the result of solar-wind implantation rather than terrestrial contamination.
```

```
The different water content and δD values may be caused by H diffusion after initial solar-wind implantation.
```

### 4.2 提出模型/假说

```
We propose that the dynamic equilibrium between implantation and outgassing of SW-hydrogen in soil grains on the lunar surface.
```

```
By comparing with the Apollo missions, both observations and simulations provide constraints on the governing role of temperature (latitude) on hydrogen implantation/migration in lunar soils.
```

### 4.3 意义阐述

```
Our discoveries shed light on complicated space weathering processes on the Moon.
```

```
This new estimate for the lunar farside mantle represents a landmark for estimating the water abundance of the bulk silicate Moon, providing critical constraints on the giant impact origin hypothesis.
```

## 五、结论部分 (Conclusions)

### 5.1 总结主要结论

```
In summary, we have demonstrated that the lunar farside mantle is potentially drier than its nearside counterpart, suggesting that the distribution of water in the interior of the Moon may exhibit a hemispheric dichotomy.
```

```
The results are indicative of homogeneous distribution of SW-derived water in the vertical direction, providing compelling evidence for the well-mixed nature of the lunar regolith.
```

### 5.2 研究价值

```
These findings provide important constraints on the origin and evolution of the Moon.
```

## 七、中文期刊特色表达

### 7.1 摘要中的表达

```
为研究太阳风成因水在月表低纬度地区的分布特征，本文对...开展了单颗粒原位纳米离子探针同位素和水含量深度剖面分析。
```

```
结果表明月壤颗粒表层（＜200 nm）具有较高的水含量（剖面最大水含量变化范围为0.35%～1.59%，平均值为0.82%）。
```

### 7.2 结论性表达

```
该结果与前人对Apollo样品的报导类似。
```

```
这一特征表明月壤颗粒表层的高水含量是太阳风注入的结果，而非陆地污染所致。
```

### 7.3 讨论中的表达

```
不同水含量和δD值可能是太阳风注入后H扩散造成的。
```

```
此外，我们发现橄榄石、斜辉石和斜长石的水含量深度分布特征相似，且随着深度增加逐渐降低。
```
