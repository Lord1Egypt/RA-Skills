## Description: <br>
去除中文文本中的 AI 味、官话味、模板味，让文字更自然、具体、更像真人表达；适用于博客、公众号、邮件、演讲稿、产品文案等中文文本润色。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and writers use this skill to rewrite Chinese drafts so they read more naturally, with less AI-like phrasing, corporate jargon, boilerplate structure, and unsupported authority claims. It is intended for blogs, public posts, emails, speeches, product copy, project descriptions, and similar Chinese prose, with careful review for legal, academic, medical, financial, or compliance material. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewritten text could change meaning, remove necessary formality, or make high-stakes content sound more certain than the source supports. <br>
Mitigation: Review rewritten text before use, especially for legal, academic, medical, financial, or compliance material, and use light editing when strict formality is required. <br>
Risk: Input text may contain unverified sources, data, citations, media claims, or examples. <br>
Mitigation: Flag missing or unverifiable support instead of inventing sources, data, cases, citations, or media coverage. <br>
Risk: Users may paste sensitive private data for rewriting. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in a trusted agent environment and avoid providing sensitive data unless that environment is approved for it. <br>
