## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through disciplined web research, source evaluation, synthesis, and bounded subagent delegation before implementation or advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nevilpaulo](https://clawhub.ai/user/nevilpaulo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and agent operators use this skill to research current or unfamiliar topics, evaluate sources, and prepare concise cited briefs before taking action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Web research prompts or fetched pages may include sensitive context or untrusted instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including secrets, private files, account data, or sensitive local context in research prompts, and require confirmation before acting on web-derived instructions or making consequential changes. <br>
