## Description: <br>
Provides a complete SOP for launching and managing community and ambassador programs, including recruitment, tiering, incentives, events, retention, metrics, and governance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Community, DevRel, and growth teams use this skill to plan, launch, and operate ambassador programs with practical checklists, operating procedures, templates, health metrics, and governance guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the skill to respond to general community-growth or retention prompts where the playbook is only partially relevant. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the prompt is about community or ambassador-program operations before applying the guidance, and adapt recommendations to the user's actual program stage and market. <br>
Risk: Advisory playbook content can be over-applied to organizations whose product maturity, community size, or regional norms differ from the examples. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as operational guidance, validate them against current business constraints, and review incentive, governance, and escalation choices before rollout. <br>
