## Description: <br>
Complete startup fundraising system from pre-seed to Series B, covering investor targeting, pitch deck construction, term sheet negotiation, due diligence preparation, and cap table management. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup operators use this skill to assess fundraising readiness, build investor pipelines, prepare pitch materials, model fundraising assumptions, review term sheets, organize due diligence, and calculate cap table dilution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fundraising, valuation, legal, and investment guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific company or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as business guidance and have qualified legal, finance, or fundraising advisors review term sheets, securities questions, valuation decisions, and investor materials. <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential fundraising materials with an agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary information, redact sensitive bank, tax, customer, cap table, compensation, and investor contact details, and follow organizational data-handling rules. <br>
