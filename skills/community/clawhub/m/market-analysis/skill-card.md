## Use Case: <br>
Market researchers, founders, analysts, and agents can use this skill to request market trend summaries, regional comparisons, and high-level sector indicators for industry research, competitive analysis, and investment decision support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market research keywords and region choices are sent to a remote service over plain HTTP without transport encryption. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive business plans, confidential investment ideas, client names, or private financial details in queries. <br>
