## Description: <br>
Provision and manage on-demand VAST.ai GPU instances, including offer search, rental, SSH connection retrieval, and account balance checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sschepis](https://clawhub.ai/user/sschepis) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
ISC <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to find, rent, connect to, and monitor VAST.ai GPU instances from an agent workflow or CLI. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Renting VAST.ai instances can create ongoing paid usage. <br>
Mitigation: Before approving rental, confirm the offer ID, hourly price, image, expected runtime, and the plan to stop or destroy the instance. <br>
