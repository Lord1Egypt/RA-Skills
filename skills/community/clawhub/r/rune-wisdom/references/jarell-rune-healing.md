## 1. Methodology

### 1.1 Self-Healing vs. Practitioner-Assisted Healing

Jarell distinguishes two fundamental modes of rune healing:

**Self-healing (самооздоровление):**
- Uses body posture (Stðdhr), mantra, mineral/wood supports
- Generates a harmonizing flow that primarily *reduces excess energy* rather than increasing deficient energy
- The harmonizing flow "programs" the etheric body to repeat itself
- The body's own defenses must then strengthen the depleted areas
- Slower and less stable than practitioner-assisted healing

**Practitioner-assisted healing (групповой вариант):**
- Practitioner places patient in the rune posture (generating inward-directed energy)
- Practitioner simultaneously uses the corresponding mudra (generating outward-directed energy toward patient)
- The mudra's outward-directed flow *increases the patient's energy level*
- More reliable and faster than self-healing
- Key principle: **Posture directs energy inward; mudra directs energy outward**

### 1.2 Energy Direction Principles

| Method | Direction | Use Case |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Body posture (Stðdhr) | Inward (into practitioner) | Self-healing |
| Mudra (hand gesture) | Outward (toward patient) | Healing others |
| Combined (posture + mudra) | Both directions | Practitioner-assisted healing |

### 1.3 Disease as Energy Imbalance

Most diseases arise from *energy imbalance*: not simply excess or deficiency of one energy flow, but simultaneous excess of one and deficiency of another. Sometimes such an altered pair disrupts energy flow in adjacent body areas as well.

### 1.4 Amulet Types and Their Strength

| Type | Strength | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Fresh (green) wood rune figure | Very strong (several times base) | Most powerful form |
| Dry wood rune figure | ~2x base | Standard amplification |
| Metal amulet | Strong, pure | Clean impact; may be too strong for unstable etheric bodies |
| Wooden amulet | Moderate | Gentler than metal; preferred for many conditions |
| Mineral/crystal amulet | Moderate | Must be tuned to specific rune |
| Naturally grown rune in wood (except IS) | Exceptional | Occurs where energy concentrates naturally; traditionally hung above doorways as protective charms |

### 1.5 Special Note: Children

Children's organisms tune into energy harmonies faster and better than adults. Therefore, even strong amulets that would be contraindicated for adults may be appropriate for children (e.g., metal amulets for asthenic children).

## 2. Crystal Preparation and Rune-Harmonized Water Protocols

### 2.1 Crystal Cleansing and Tuning

**Step 1 — Cleansing:**

| Method | Procedure | Receptivity Window |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Cold running water** (preferred) | Hold crystal in cold running water for ≥24 hours. Spring/stream/river water is far superior to tap water. | ~1 hour after removal from water |
| **Fire** (clean flame) | Briefly pass crystal through clean flame. | 1–2 minutes only; crystal may not cool in time |
| **Baking in bread** (village method) | Bake crystal inside bread dough. | Up to 30 minutes |

The water method is strongly preferred because the longer receptivity window allows proper tuning.

**Step 2 — Personal Tuning:**
- Hold the cleansed crystal in your palm throughout the receptivity window
- This imprints your personal energy signature onto the crystal

**Step 3 — Rune Attunement:**
- Immediately after the cleansing+personal tuning, perform the mudra of the desired rune *over the crystal for at least 5 minutes*
- Must be done immediately upon removing crystal from water/fire
- The crystal now independently focuses the energy of that specific rune
- Can be used as a healing aid or as a talisman

### 2.2 Rune-Harmonized Water

Water can be charged with rune energy for internal or external use. Key principles:

- **Temperature matters**: Cold water for excess-energy conditions; warm water for deficiency conditions; hot water for specific conditions as noted
- **Drinking protocol**: Sip slowly, in small gulps (especially for UR-harmonized water)
- **External use**: Wash/irrigate affected areas with harmonized water
- **Timing**: Some waters are best taken at sunrise or after sunset (varies by rune and condition)
- **Vessel**: For TIR-harmonized water, best prepared in a vessel made of harmonized iron

### 2.3 Rune-Harmonized Herbal Infusions

Herbs can be prepared as infusions and additionally harmonized with specific rune energies. This dual approach (herbal + runic) amplifies therapeutic effect. Key combinations documented below in Section 6.

## 3. Elemental Diagnosis Framework

### 3.1 The Four Elements in Diagnosis

Jarell uses a four-element diagnostic system (Fire, Air, Water, Earth) to determine treatment approach:

**Element of Fire (Огонь):**
- Excess: Excitement, impulsiveness, emotional outbursts, hysteria
- Deficiency: Sleepiness, lethargy, stiffness, sluggishness

**Element of Air (Воздух):**
- Excess: Spasms, instability, erratic energy, migraines
- Deficiency (in tissue): Predisposition to respiratory disease
- Air conflicts with: Earth (lacks heat and moisture of Air)

**Element of Water (Влага):**
- Imbalance: Affects mucus membranes, fluid regulation, lymph
- Deficiency of moisture: Illnesses run acute with high fever, dry cough
- Adequate moisture: Illnesses run prolonged with loose phlegm

**Element of Earth (Земля):**
- Excess: Deposits, calcification, stagnation
- Conflicts with: Air (deposits from water-earth conflict)

### 3.2 Diagnostic Decision Trees

**For Neurotic Disorders (Rune FA):**
1. Patient sleepy, lethargic, stiff → **Fire deficiency** → Use fresh wood rune + invoke solar fire in affirmation; position in direct sunlight
2. Patient excitable, impulsive, emotional → **Fire excess** → Invoke moon in affirmation; very soft approach; FA amulets absolutely contraindicated; for hysteria use only cooled rune-harmonized water

**For Thyroid Disorders (Rune UR):**
1. Hyperactive, elevated temperature, rapid pulse, bright/shiny eyes → **Elevated thyroid** → Avoid any harsh impact; only gentle UR dance in morning; warm UR-harmonized water wash on neck
2. Slow speech, drowsiness, edema, excessive skin moisture → **Depressed thyroid** → Use fresh wood UR figure + wooden or metal UR amulet

**For Pharyngitis (Rune OS):**
1. Illnesses run acute, high fever, dry cough → **Moisture deficiency** → Perform OS dance at twilight; warm OS-harmonized wild rosemary infusion; wooden HAGAL amulets or metal LAF amulets
2. Illnesses run prolonged, low-grade fever, loose phlegm → **Heat deficiency** → Exercises at noon in open air; supplement with mudras; heat-water procedures (e.g., bathhouse); metal OS amulet

**For Salt Metabolism (Rune THORN):**
- Salt retention → Deposits and elevated blood pressure
- Excessive salt excretion → Arrhythmias

### 3.3 Elemental Conflict in Disease

| Condition | Elemental Conflict | Treatment Implication |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Salt/calcium deposits | Water vs. Earth | Resolved by THORN (cosmic dance force) |
| Migraine | Moisture/Thought instability + Air excess | Calm with OS + IR; avoid cold |
| Pharyngitis | Air vs. Earth | Determine heat vs. moisture deficiency |
| Winter angina | Air excess | Limit air influence during treatment |
| Summer angina | Air deficiency | Strong treatments; open air morning sessions |
| Skin diseases | Internal/external energy excess | Soft harmonizing only; never powerful sessions |

### Rune THORN/TOR (ТОР) — #3

**Conditions covered:** Polyarthritis, salt metabolism disorders, kidney stones, cystitis

#### Polyarthritis

- Inflammatory joint disease causing deformity (distinguished from rheumatism: elderly onset, small joints, persistent rather than "migrating" pain)
- Connected to protein and salt metabolism disruption, uneven etheric body energy density

- **Treatment**:
  - **Soft approach, evening hours**
  - For finger joint arthritis: self-healing with mudras (energy reaches fingers in most harmonious form)
  - **Wash affected areas** with THORN+HAGAL harmonized water (warm, 3–4 times daily)
  - Recovery typically prolonged

- **Dietary protocol**: Complete exclusion of all dietary fats (including vegetable oils)
  - THORN's pure, strong energy conflicts with sluggish fat-energy patterns
  - Fat-free diet + energy treatment typically brings **rapid complete relief (~1 week)**
  - Additionally useful: HAGAL-harmonized water

#### Salt Metabolism Disorders

- Manifestations: calcium deposits in joints/lungs, kidney stones, hypertension, arrhythmia, edema

- **Treatment** (regardless of variant):
  - **Gentle and regular** approach
  - All procedures in **morning or evening hours**
  - Structure: **increase meditation/mantra period; reduce dance figure to 5–10 minutes**
  - Mudra of little help in self-treatment
  - **Pine shoot infusion**, additionally harmonized with THORN energy
  - Amulets not used (wooden rune images as last resort only)

#### Kidney Stones (Urolithiasis)

- Both salt and protein metabolism disrupted
- Often accompanied by gout-like conditions; in advanced cases, hypertension
- Chronic urinary tract infection due to disrupted urine flow and crystal trauma
- **Do not expect rapid cure**: pain can be reduced, general condition improved, stone formation slowed/stopped; but existing stones remain

- **Treatment**:
  - **Midday**, sufficiently intense (amulets and wood figures acceptable)
  - Drink significant amounts (up to **1 liter/day**) of pine shoot infusion harmonized with THORN
  - Additionally provide **IS amulet** (regulates fluid flow)
  - Underlying cause: conflict of Water and Earth elements → resolved by THORN's force

#### Cystitis

- **Acute phase: energy treatment CONTRAINDICATED** — fully mobilized defenses don't need support; additional force may cause serious consequences
- Energy treatment useful as: **prevention** for predisposition, and **supportive therapy** for chronic form

- **Diagnostic notes**:
  - Uncomplicated cystitis: pulling/sharp pains midline below navel, toward pubic bone
  - Pain does NOT radiate to back/flanks
  - Slow pressure then sudden release does NOT worsen pain
  - Often accompanied by fever/chills
  - Urine cloudy with flakes/streaks of mucus/pus, sometimes blood
  - Three-glass test: glass 1 murky = urethra; glass 2 murky = cystitis; all 3 murky = pyelonephritis

- **Treatment** (chronic/preventive only):
  - Avoid amulets and direct wood figure application
  - Do not overuse mudras

  - In younger patients: **HAGAL** energy has greater effect

### Rune RIT (РИТ) — #5

**Conditions covered:** Rheumatism, gout, neurological diseases

#### Rheumatism

- Autoimmune disease: immune system reacts to streptococcus antigens in nasopharynx (triggering angina)
- Antibodies attack proteins found in both joints and heart lining tissue
- **Children**: extremely serious — can cause heart damage and disability
  - ⚠️ **At slightest suspicion: seek medical help immediately; do NOT self-treat!**
- **Adults**: usually limited to joint pain without serious cardiac complications
- Diagnostic sign: **"migrating" pain** — joint sharply painful today, completely healthy tomorrow
- Joints often swollen

- **Treatment** (adults only, after medical clearance):
  - Soft approach, regular sessions
  - Exercises in morning or evening hours
  - Dance figure combined with **oak bark infusion** harmonized with RIT
  - Wooden amulets acceptable; metal amulets contraindicated for children
  - **Dietary note**: avoid foods that exacerbate autoimmune response

#### Gout

- Metabolic disease with uric acid crystal deposition in joints (typically big toe)
- Acute attacks with severe pain, often at night after dietary excess
- Related to disturbed protein metabolism

- **Treatment**:
  - Similar to rheumatism protocol but adjusted for metabolic focus
  - Regular, moderate sessions
  - Emphasis on meditation and mantra over dance
  - Harmonized RIT water
  - Dietary restrictions essential

#### Karmic Context

Feeling separated from our higher self. Inner conflicts lead to physical joint conflicts (inflexibility). We must find unity with our higher nature.
