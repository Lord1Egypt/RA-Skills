## Description: <br>
家庭返利统一管理工具，聚合全家人的购物返利账户，统一追踪、统一提现，帮全家省钱。 <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Families and household account managers use this skill to consolidate shopping rebate accounts, track rebate orders across family members, and prepare shared savings and withdrawal summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Family rebate account aggregation can expose household shopping history, rebate balances, withdrawal status, and account activity without clear consent. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only after each family member explicitly agrees, and define who may view, store, export, or delete shared shopping and rebate history. <br>
Risk: Assisted ordering and unified withdrawals can affect another person's account or funds. <br>
Mitigation: Require separate authorization for each account and explicit confirmation before every order or withdrawal. <br>
