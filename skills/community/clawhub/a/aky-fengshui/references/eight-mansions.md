# Eight Mansions Feng Shui (八宅风水)

The Eight Mansions system (八宅法) classifies people and houses into East Group (东四命/宅) and West Group (西四命/宅) based on their trigram.

---

## 1. Life Trigram Calculation (本命卦)

### For males (男命):
Take the last two digits of the birth year → add digits until single digit → subtract from 10 → result maps to trigram.

**Formula:** `(10 - reduced_sum) → trigram`

If result is 5 → 男坤卦 (Kun for males)

### For females (女命):
Take the last two digits of the birth year → add digits until single digit → add 5 → reduce to single digit → map to trigram.

**Formula:** `(reduced_sum + 5 → reduce) → trigram`

If result is 5 → 女艮卦 (Gen for females)

### Digit-to-Trigram Mapping:
| Number | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 | 9 |
|:------:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
| **Trigram** | 坎 | 坤 | 震 | 巽 | — | 乾 | 兑 | 艮 | 离 |
| **Group** | E | W | E | E | — | W | W | W | E |

**East Group (东四命):** 坎(1), 震(3), 巽(4), 离(9)
**West Group (西四命):** 坤(2), 乾(6), 兑(7), 艮(8)

---

## 2. East/West Life Groups (东四命 / 西四命)

### East Group People (东四命) — Facing East preferred
- Life trigrams: 坎(Water), 离(Fire), 震(Wood), 巽(Wood)
- Favorable directions: East, Southeast, South, North
- Best house orientation: facing East or South

### West Group People (西四命) — Facing West preferred
- Life trigrams: 坤(Earth), 兑(Lake/Metal), 乾(Heaven/Metal), 艮(Mountain/Earth)
- Favorable directions: West, Northwest, Southwest, Northeast
- Best house orientation: facing West or North

### The Core Principle:
A person's life trigram should be **compatible** with the house's sitting/facing direction. East group people in east group houses → harmony. West group people in west group houses → harmony. Mixing east and west → potential disharmony.

## 3. The Eight Directions & Four Auspicious/Four Inauspicious Positions

Each life trigram has 4 good and 4 bad directions:

### For a person with [Kan/坎] Life Trigram (East Group):

| Direction | Quality | Chinese Name | Meaning |
|:---------:|:-------:|:------------:|:--------|
| **South (离)** | 🟢 Best | 生气 (Sheng Qi) | Vitality, wealth, success |
| **Southeast (巽)** | 🟢 2nd | 天医 (Tian Yi) | Health, healing |
| **East (震)** | 🟢 3rd | 延年 (Yan Nian) | Longevity, relationships |
| **North (坎)** | 🟢 4th | 伏位 (Fu Wei) | Stability, foundation |
| **Southwest (坤)** | 🔴 Worst | 绝命 (Jue Ming) | Destruction, loss |
| **West (兑)** | 🔴 2nd worst | 五鬼 (Wu Gui) | Misfortune, conflict |
| **Northeast (艮)** | 🔴 3rd worst | 六煞 (Liu Sha) | Obstacles, gossip |
| **Northwest (乾)** | 🔴 4th worst | 祸害 (Huo Hai) | Arguments, minor harm |

### The four auspicious directions (四吉方):
| Quality | Best For | Element |
|:-------:|:---------|:-------:|
| 生气 Sheng Qi | Career, wealth, front door, master bedroom | Same element + generating |
| 天医 Tian Yi | Health, sick room, elderly room | Generating element |
| 延年 Yan Nian | Marriage, family harmony, study | Supporting element |
| 伏位 Fu Wei | Stable foundation, quiet rooms, storage | Same element |

### The four inauspicious directions (四凶方):
| Quality | Avoid For | Element |
|:-------:|:---------|:-------:|
| 绝命 Jue Ming | Main door, bedroom, stove | Controlling element |
| 五鬼 Wu Gui | Front door, any important room | Excessive generation |
| 六煞 Liu Sha | Bedroom, study | Conflicting element |
| 祸害 Huo Hai | Kitchen, children's room | Weakening element |

## 5. House Sitting-Facing Analysis (宅向分析)

### Determining the house sitting and facing direction:
- **坐 (Sitting)** = the back of the house (where the back door or the quiet side is)
- **向 (Facing)** = the front of the house (where the main door is)

### House classification:
| House Type | Sitting | Facing | Group |
|:----------:|:-------:|:------:|:-----:|
| **震宅** | East (90°) | West (270°) | East |
| **巽宅** | Southeast (135°) | Northwest (315°) | East |
| **离宅** | South (180°) | North (0°/360°) | East |
| **坎宅** | North (0°/360°) | South (180°) | East |
| **乾宅** | Northwest (315°) | Southeast (135°) | West |
| **坤宅** | Southwest (225°) | Northeast (45°) | West |
| **艮宅** | Northeast (45°) | Southwest (225°) | West |
| **兑宅** | West (270°) | East (90°) | West |

### Compatibility check:
- East group person + East group house → 🟢 Good
- West group person + West group house → 🟢 Good
- East group person + West group house → 🔴 Mismatch (can be remedied)

## 6. Room Placement Based on Eight Mansions

| Room | Best Direction | Avoid |
|:----|:--------------|:------|
| **大门 (Front Door)** | 生气 or 延年 | 绝命 or 五鬼 |
| **主卧 (Master Bedroom)** | 生气 or 延年 | 绝命 or 五鬼 |
| **厨房 (Kitchen)** | 天医 or 伏位 | 绝命 or 祸害 |
| **灶位 (Stove)** | Facing auspicious direction | Facing inauspicious |
| **书房/办公室 (Study)** | 生气 or 延年 | 六煞 |
| **厕所 (Bathroom)** | 绝命 or 五鬼 (suppress) | 生气 or 天医 |
| **客厅 (Living Room)** | 生气 or 延年 | 六煞 |
| **神位 (Altar)** | 伏位 or 生气 | 绝命 |
| **楼梯 (Stairs)** | Avoid center of house | — |
| **车库 (Garage)** | 六煞 or 祸害 (acceptable) | 生气 |

**Important principle:** Place important rooms (bedroom, door, kitchen, study) in auspicious directions; place "dirty" rooms (bathroom, storage) in inauspicious directions to suppress the bad energy.
