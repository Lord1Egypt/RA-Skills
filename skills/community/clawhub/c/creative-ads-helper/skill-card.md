## Description: <br>
Generate and evaluate ad creatives for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Google Ads, and Amazon Ads with script generation, performance scoring, fatigue timing, and inspiration references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, growth, and creative teams use this skill to generate ad concepts, score creative assets, diagnose fatigue, and plan replacement tests across Meta, TikTok, YouTube, Google, and Amazon ad placements. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Campaign metrics, brand strategy, and unpublished creative plans may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the minimum data needed for the task and handle all inputs according to the organization's data policy. <br>
Risk: Generated ad claims or creative recommendations may conflict with platform policy or legal requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated claims and campaign recommendations for platform policy, substantiation, and legal compliance before use. <br>
Risk: Creative scoring can be provisional when performance data is missing or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Label recommendations as provisional when data is absent and retest with measurable thresholds before scaling spend. <br>
