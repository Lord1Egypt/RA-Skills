## Description: <br>
Provides a lead-qualification prompt framework that collects budget, decision timeline, and pain point signals before recommending the next sales action. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aineuralagency](https://clawhub.ai/user/aineuralagency) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales teams, agencies, freelancers, and business owners use this skill to guide AI-assisted lead qualification conversations and decide whether to schedule a human appointment, nurture the lead, or discard it. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lead qualification conversations can collect prospect business details and potentially personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Tell prospects how their information will be used and handle collected lead data under the deploying organization's privacy and retention policies. <br>
Risk: The artifact mentions an external promotional website for an automated API-integrated version. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the promotional website separately before entering information or adopting any external integration. <br>
