## Description: <br>
亲子出行助手 helps families traveling with children check child ticket policies, find family-friendly attractions, and generate age-based packing checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[travel-skills](https://clawhub.ai/user/travel-skills) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Families and travel-planning agents use this skill to answer child ticket policy questions, recommend family-friendly attractions, and prepare age-based packing checklists for trips with children. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with travel content focused on China ticket policies and China-based attraction and weather services. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Attraction and weather lookups send city, destination, attraction keywords, and child age to the skill author's proxy services and downstream travel or weather APIs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering precise home addresses, full itineraries, names, contact details, or other sensitive family information unless necessary. <br>
