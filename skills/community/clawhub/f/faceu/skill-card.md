## Description: <br>
FaceU skill organizes publicly available information on templates, editing features, export specs, help docs, and pricing from faceu.com. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to summarize public FaceU website information about templates, editing capabilities, export specifications, help content, copyright terms, and pricing plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pricing, copyright, and commercial-use terms may change or be misunderstood if summarized without source verification. <br>
Mitigation: Verify those terms against the official linked FaceU pages before relying on the output, especially for commercial use. <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for public information only and could be misapplied to private account assets or non-public resources. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to publicly visible FaceU pages and do not request account actions, paid resource access, private project data, or copyright bypasses. <br>
