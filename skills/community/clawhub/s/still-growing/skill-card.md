## Description: <br>
Still Growing / 第二代父母 helps parents, children, and AI-assisted support seekers reflect on intergenerational parenting misalignment, emotional needs, and parent-child repair prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mark-heartflow](https://clawhub.ai/user/mark-heartflow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT No Attribution <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External parents, caregivers, and people reflecting on family relationships use this skill for guided self-reflection, scenario-based dialogue prompts, and parent-child communication repair. It is reflective parenting support, not emergency, clinical, or child-safety advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Parenting guidance may be used in crisis situations involving suicidal thoughts or imminent child-safety danger. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as reflective support only; contact local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately and seek qualified professional help when there is imminent risk. <br>
Risk: The artifact may prompt agents to search local skills, session history, and git history more broadly than needed. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow or disable those context-search instructions before use when local files, conversation history, or repository history may contain sensitive information. <br>
