## Description: <br>
Full-cycle recruiting agent for sourcing, screening, scoring, pipeline management, and hiring workflows using structured frameworks, scorecards, and templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Recruiters, hiring managers, and recruiting operations teams use this skill to structure role intake, sourcing, candidate screening, interview scorecards, pipeline tracking, and offer communications. It is intended to assist hiring workflows while keeping humans responsible for decisions and candidate communications. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recruiting workflows may involve real candidate data and employment decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep a human responsible for decisions and messages, minimize candidate data, secure pipeline files, and follow applicable employment, privacy, retention, and anti-discrimination rules. <br>
Risk: Generated screening scores, outreach, or interview notes may be incomplete, inaccurate, or unsuitable for a specific hiring context. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outputs before use, rely on documented evidence and structured scorecards, and avoid protected-class or sensitive personal information unless legally authorized. <br>
