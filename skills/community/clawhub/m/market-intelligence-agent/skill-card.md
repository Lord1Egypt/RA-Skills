## Description: <br>
Find business opportunities others miss. Mines Reddit, HackerNews, ProductHunt for pain points, validates ideas, and surfaces emerging trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs, product teams, and market researchers use this skill to mine public product and community signals for pain points, validate business ideas, spot trends, analyze competitors, and prepare opportunity briefings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market research prompts may include confidential business ideas or competitive strategy topics. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive proprietary details unless the host agent and connected research tools are approved for that information. <br>
Risk: Opportunity, trend, and competitor analyses may be incomplete or misleading if public sources are stale, biased, or unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research leads and validate important decisions against current primary sources and independent market data. <br>
