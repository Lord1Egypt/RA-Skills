## Description: <br>
Runs fast sentiment analysis on 15-minute Bitcoin price action for short-term Kalshi or trading decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[intheorytv](https://clawhub.ai/user/intheorytv) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to produce a concise second opinion on very short-term BTC direction before deciding whether to place a 15-minute Kalshi bet. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is explicitly focused on short-horizon BTC wagering and may influence real-money betting decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not allow agents to place bets automatically; require independent user review before any wager. <br>
Risk: Live BTC price, social chatter, and Kalshi market conditions can change quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current market data independently before relying on the generated sentiment summary. <br>
Risk: The generated output could be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as decision support only and consider legal, platform, fee, and loss-risk limits before use. <br>
