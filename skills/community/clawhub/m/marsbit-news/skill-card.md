## Description: <br>
Retrieve the latest and searched crypto, blockchain, and Web3 news articles and flash updates from Marsbit, with full article content available on request. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
AI assistants and agents use this skill to retrieve, search, and summarize Marsbit crypto, blockchain, and Web3 news articles and flash updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Searches and article lookups contact Marsbit's public API and may reveal requested topics. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive or private topics in searches and disclose network access before use in sensitive contexts. <br>
