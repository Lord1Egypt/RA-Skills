## Description: <br>
Helps agents plan, ghostwrite, optimize, and distribute Substack newsletter issues and web posts with voice matching, SEO, growth, monetization, and platform formatting guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[samber](https://clawhub.ai/user/samber) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Newsletter writers, founders, marketers, and ghostwriters use this skill to create Substack issues or web posts, adapt existing content, match a client's voice, and prepare growth-oriented distribution assets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Voice samples, recordings, transcripts, internal communications, or client materials may include private or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Scope the agent to specific approved drafts, URLs, and source files; remove sensitive details and confirm permission before providing client or internal materials. <br>
Risk: Ghostwriting and content edits can produce inaccurate voice matching, misleading claims, or unwanted changes to published material. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated drafts and file edits before publication, validate the voice guide with the user or client, and keep final publishing decisions under human control. <br>
Risk: Recording or transcribing conversations without consent can create privacy and compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Get consent before recording or transcribing conversations and decide where any saved voice guide should live and when it should be deleted. <br>
