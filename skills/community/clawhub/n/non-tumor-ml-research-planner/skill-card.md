## Description: <br>
Generates structured, publication-oriented research plans for non-cancer biomedical bioinformatics and machine learning studies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AIPOCH-AI](https://clawhub.ai/user/AIPOCH-AI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers and biomedical analysts use this skill to plan non-tumor bioinformatics and machine learning studies, including diagnostic biomarker discovery, mechanism-focused analyses, GEO dataset workflows, validation planning, and publication upgrade paths. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Biomedical research plans may be mistaken for clinical or medical decision support. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only as research-planning guidance and independently review methods, dataset suitability, and claims before clinical or publication use. <br>
Risk: Dataset availability, cohort size, and platform heterogeneity can make a generated ML plan infeasible or weakly supported. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm GEO dataset availability, sample size, validation cohorts, batch effects, and statistical assumptions before committing to a study design. <br>
Risk: Generated plans can overstate diagnostic or mechanistic evidence if reused without review. <br>
Mitigation: Require external validation where feasible and avoid causal or clinical utility claims from ROC, enrichment, immune correlation, or network analysis alone. <br>
