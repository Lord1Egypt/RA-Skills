# Travel Photography Storyteller
Helps travelers plan and execute meaningful travel photography that tells stories, not just snapshots.
## What it does
- Develops **story concept frameworks** including narrative arcs, chapter-based shot lists, and golden-hour scheduling
- Provides a **shot planning framework** with platform-specific ratios, storyboard templates, and B-roll checklists
- Delivers **ethical photography guidelines** for respectful subject interaction and culturally sensitive shooting
## Example scenarios
**1. 旅行发朋友圈构图困难户**
> 👤 每次出去玩拍几百张照片，回来发朋友圈发现没有一张能看的，全是游客照。教我怎么拍出小红书那种质感照片？
> 🤖 输出朋友圈出片三步法——黄金时刻法（日出后1h/日落前1h、曝光下拉半档）、九宫格法则（网格线+交叉点构图、俯拍/仰拍避开人群）、三张起手式（全景环境+局部特写+人物动作，一起发有故事感），配文用场景+感受+emoji。

**2. Photo essay in Morocco**
> 👤 I'm going to Morocco for 10 days. Help me plan a photo essay that tells a story, not just snapshots.
> 🤖 Story arc planning — 5-chapter narrative (Arrival, Medina Life, Craft Heritage, Sahara, Departure) with shot list per chapter, golden-hour scheduling, location scouting notes, subject-interaction etiquette, and gear-packing checklist optimized for storytelling.

**3. Content creator shot planning**
> 👤 Create a travel photography shot-planning template for social media content creators.
> 🤖 Content-creator shot planner — platform-specific ratio guide (Instagram 4:5, Reels 9:16, YouTube 16:9), storyboard template for 7-day trip, B-roll checklist, location-scouting app workflow, and batch-caption writing tips.
