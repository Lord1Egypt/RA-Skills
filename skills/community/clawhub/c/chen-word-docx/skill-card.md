Developers, editors, and document automation agents use this skill when creating, inspecting, or editing Word/DOCX files where tracked changes, comments, fields, tables, styles, sections, templates, or round-trip compatibility matter. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Word/DOCX edits can unintentionally alter tracked changes, comments, fields, templates, or formatting-sensitive layout. <br>
Mitigation: Work from copies of important documents, make minimal targeted edits, and verify the result in the intended editor before delivery. <br>
Risk: Server-resolved provenance is unavailable for this release. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the ClawHub publisher profile and target version before installing when source provenance matters. <br>
