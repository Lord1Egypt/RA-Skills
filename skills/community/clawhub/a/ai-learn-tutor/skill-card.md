## Description: <br>
AI学习私教 - 搭知识库、规划路径、出题练习、批改讲解、总结输出。学练查写闭环，从零学成高手。支持任意学科：专业、考证、编程、论文等。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Learners, educators, and external users use this agent to build study knowledge bases, plan learning paths, generate practice questions, review mistakes, summarize concepts, and track progress across subjects such as programming, research, exams, and language learning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may upload private study materials, papers, notes, corporate documents, or personal information for tutoring and knowledge-base workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm where materials will be processed or stored before uploading them, and use explicit prompts when asking the agent to work with sensitive content. <br>
Risk: Generated study plans, explanations, quizzes, summaries, or code examples may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Review educational outputs against trusted course materials, instructor guidance, or authoritative references before relying on them. <br>
