## Description: <br>
Produce, adapt, or review text in a source-aware Yogyakarta/Jogja court-inspired Basa Bagongan register without claiming ritual or palace-protocol authenticity. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ramaaditya49](https://clawhub.ai/user/ramaaditya49) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to draft, adapt, or review culturally source-aware Yogyakarta Bagongan-inspired wording for ordinary cultural copy, event language, and explanatory notes. Protocol-sensitive, ceremonial, inscription, royal-address, or public authenticity claims require expert review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may be mistaken for official Yogyakarta palace, ritual, or protocol wording. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as draft cultural-language guidance and seek expert review for ceremonies, inscriptions, royal-address text, or public claims of authenticity. <br>
Risk: Specialized Bagongan wording may be culturally or linguistically inaccurate for high-stakes public use. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for ordinary cultural copy and review exact source phrases, roles, and context with a Yogyakarta court-language expert before publication. <br>
