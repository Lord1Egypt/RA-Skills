## Description: <br>
Guides users through recording life stories, collecting local and web materials, and generating a personal biography-style Life Book. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sunnyyao2222-eng](https://clawhub.ai/user/sunnyyao2222-eng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to capture personal life experiences, organize them into chapters, import supporting materials, and generate a Markdown biography. It is intended for personal biography creation, not simple notes or temporary reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save sensitive personal life details in plaintext on disk. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with explicit approval before saving entries, avoid recording highly sensitive details, and review or delete stored files before sharing or syncing the workspace. <br>
Risk: The skill can collect web materials or import URLs, which involves external network activity. <br>
Mitigation: Treat web searches and URL imports as network activity, review sources before importing them, and avoid importing broad folders or untrusted URLs. <br>
