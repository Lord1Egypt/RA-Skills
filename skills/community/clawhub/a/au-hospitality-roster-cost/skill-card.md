## Description: <br>
Calculate Australian hospitality award pay rates, penalty rates, and labour costs under HIGA MA000009; optimise rosters to reduce wage spend while maintaining Fair Work compliance for hotels, pubs, bars, and accommodation venues. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Australian hospitality employers and operators use this skill to calculate HIGA MA000009 wage costs, penalty rates, overtime, allowances, superannuation, and roster options for hotels, pubs, bars, and accommodation venues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Australia <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payroll calculations may become inaccurate when award rates or Fair Work guidance changes after the embedded 1 July 2025 rate set. <br>
Mitigation: Verify rates against the current Fair Work pay guide before setting wages, processing payroll, or making compliance decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may rely on general award guidance as legal, payroll, or employment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general information and consult the Fair Work Ombudsman or a qualified employment adviser for complex, disputed, or high-risk situations. <br>
Risk: The wrong award may be applied to restaurants, cafes, clubs, fast-food venues, or other non-HIGA workplaces. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm award coverage and venue type before calculating rates, and use Fair Work tools when coverage is uncertain. <br>
