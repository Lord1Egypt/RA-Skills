## Description: <br>
Convert PDF documents to clean Markdown with preserved structure using the MinerU API and mineru-open-api CLI. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[veeicwgy](https://clawhub.ai/user/veeicwgy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, technical writers, and researchers use this skill to convert PDFs such as papers, manuals, and reports into Markdown for documentation, knowledge bases, publishing, and version control. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: PDF content is processed through a third-party MinerU API/CLI service. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential, regulated, or proprietary documents unless the service's privacy, retention, and credential requirements have been reviewed and approved. <br>
Risk: The workflow depends on installing and running the third-party mineru-open-api npm package. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use the package only in environments where that dependency and its command-line behavior are acceptable. <br>
