## Description: <br>
职业考试智能备考工具包。当用户提到备考、考试、刷题、复习、公务员、法考、CPA、注册会计师、教师资格、建造师、考研、职业资格、考试规划、学习计划、高频考点、错题、冲刺、模拟题等任何与职业考试备考相关的需求时使用此技能。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners use this skill to plan and execute Chinese professional-exam preparation across supported exams such as civil service, legal qualification, CPA, teacher certification, and construction engineer exams. It produces day-level study plans, high-frequency topic cards, practice questions with explanations, error remediation, and final review guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill directs users to an off-platform QR-code payment flow for Pro activation. <br>
Mitigation: Proceed only if the user trusts the publisher and is comfortable with an off-platform purchase and license flow; review payment instructions before scanning or paying. <br>
Risk: The skill stores a local activation marker after successful activation. <br>
Mitigation: Review the activation behavior before using Pro features and avoid sharing activation codes or payment screenshots in unrelated contexts. <br>
