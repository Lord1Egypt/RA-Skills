## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to compare product prices across Chinese shopping platforms, including Taobao, JD, and Pinduoduo, and receive a concise buying report. It is intended for interactive shopping research where the user controls browser login, cart, checkout, payment, and account changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent may view logged-in shopping search results and member pricing while helping compare prices. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill interactively, preferably with a dedicated browser profile, and only approve account, cart, checkout, or payment actions when you explicitly intend them. <br>
Risk: Prices, discounts, sales counts, and member benefits can change before purchase. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the report as shopping research and verify final price, seller, shipping, and return terms on the platform before ordering. <br>
