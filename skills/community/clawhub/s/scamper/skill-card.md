## Description: <br>
Apply the SCAMPER creative thinking method to develop ideas, adapt frameworks, generate hackathon concepts, or break through stuck points. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Agents assisting external users use this skill to turn a subject, idea, or blocker into structured SCAMPER prompts and markdown brainstorming output for idea development, framework adaptation, hackathon concepts, or stuck-point analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated brainstorming content may be appended to notes/ideas.md when the user accepts that workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve note additions only when the generated ideas should be saved. <br>
