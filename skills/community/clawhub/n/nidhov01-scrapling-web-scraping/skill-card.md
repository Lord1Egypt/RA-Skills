## Use Case: <br>
Developers and automation engineers use this skill to plan and operate authorized Scrapling-based web extraction workflows, including MCP setup, selector strategies, spider patterns, and anti-bot troubleshooting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers dual-use web scraping features, including stealth, Cloudflare-solving, proxy rotation, login sessions, and large crawls. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for sites you own, are authorized to test, or are explicitly allowed to scrape, and review any stealth, proxy, login, or large-crawl plan before execution. <br>
Risk: Scraped outputs, checkpoints, downloads, and cookies can contain sensitive data or session material. <br>
Mitigation: Keep scraping artifacts in a dedicated workspace and review cleanup commands before running them. <br>
