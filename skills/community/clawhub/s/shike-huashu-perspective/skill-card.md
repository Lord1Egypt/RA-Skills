## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business, product, and AI-coding claims may be persuasive but are informational guidance rather than verified guarantees. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against project constraints, independent evidence, and user requirements before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes contact details and payment links in descriptive text. <br>
Mitigation: Treat contact and payment details as informational and do not require payment or external contact to use the skill. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed as a Chinese persona and may answer in Chinese by default. <br>
Mitigation: Set the agent or conversation language preference explicitly when another language is required. <br>
