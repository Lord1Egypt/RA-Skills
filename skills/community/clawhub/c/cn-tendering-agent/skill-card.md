## Description: <br>
Cn Tendering Agent helps agents draft, review, and organize Chinese construction engineering tendering and procurement materials across planning, bid documents, evaluation, risk, contract, cost, and schedule workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ruiyongwang](https://clawhub.ai/user/ruiyongwang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External procurement and construction tendering practitioners use this skill to prepare tendering plans, bidding documents, bid evaluation materials, risk assessments, contract planning, and project-control guidance for Chinese construction engineering procurement workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tendering and legal templates may be outdated, incomplete, or unsuitable for a specific project or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current Chinese tendering rules, project requirements, and cited legal bases before relying on generated materials. <br>
Risk: Confidential bid, procurement, or project materials could be exposed if pasted into an untrusted agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use only trusted execution environments and avoid sharing confidential bid materials unless appropriate data handling controls are in place. <br>
