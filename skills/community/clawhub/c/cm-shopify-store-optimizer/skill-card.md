## Description: <br>
Audits Shopify stores end to end and produces prioritized recommendations to improve conversion rate, average order value, SEO traffic, speed, product pages, checkout, retention, and app stack quality. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Shopify merchants, ecommerce operators, and consultants use this skill to diagnose revenue leaks and plan practical store improvements. It produces ranked fixes with expected lift, effort estimates, and Shopify-specific implementation notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recommendations involving paid apps, theme changes, checkout or payment settings, and marketing automation can affect store cost and revenue. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before acting, test theme and checkout changes before publishing, and confirm paid app or automation changes with the store owner. <br>
