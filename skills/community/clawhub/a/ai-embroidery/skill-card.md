## Description: <br>
Ai Embroidery helps users learn embroidery styles, stitches, materials, transfer methods, project design, troubleshooting, and image-assisted embroidery planning from beginner cross-stitch through traditional Chinese and couture embroidery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

External users, craft learners, and embroidery practitioners use this skill for embroidery education, project planning, stitch and material selection, design transfer advice, and troubleshooting. <br>
