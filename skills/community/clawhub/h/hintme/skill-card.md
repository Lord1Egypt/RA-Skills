## Description: <br>
Use this skill when the user is stuck in a video game and wants help without spoilers, analyzing screenshots or descriptions to provide progressive hints from gentle nudge to explicit answer. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kewang0622](https://clawhub.ai/user/kewang0622) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and game players use this skill when they are stuck in a video game and want spoiler-light help from a screenshot or short description. The skill guides the agent to identify the situation, provide a first gentle hint, and reveal clearer guidance or the answer only when requested. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases such as 'I'm stuck' or 'what do I do next' could activate the skill outside a gaming context. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke or apply the skill only when the user provides clear game context, such as a title, boss, puzzle, level, quest, or gameplay screenshot. <br>
