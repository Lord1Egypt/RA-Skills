Prospective franchisees, multi-unit operators, franchise consultants, and franchise development teams use this skill to evaluate franchise disclosures, model economics, compare royalty structures, and plan expansion decisions. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Franchise, legal, financial, and compliance conclusions may be incomplete or inaccurate if treated as professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as decision support and verify legal, financial, and compliance conclusions with qualified professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Inputs may include sensitive business plans, personal financial details, or franchise disclosure materials. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the information needed for the analysis and avoid unnecessary sensitive business or personal financial details. <br>
