## Description: <br>
Auto-learns your visual preferences. Adapts to UI, graphics, video, and any creative work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creative practitioners use this skill to help an agent observe, remember, and reuse their visual style preferences across UI, graphics, video, print, and other design work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Mistaken or stale inferred visual preferences may continue influencing future design work. <br>
Mitigation: Periodically review the saved Aesthetic, By Medium, Brands, and Never sections and remove or correct preferences that no longer apply. <br>
Risk: One-off project requirements or client-specific brand choices could be overgeneralized as the user's personal taste. <br>
Mitigation: Only add preferences when the user states them directly, chooses a reference clearly, rejects a design with specific feedback, or shows a repeated pattern. <br>
