## Description: <br>
Scheduler is a command-line productivity utility for creating, tracking, reviewing, exporting, and reporting local scheduling entries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and external users can use this skill to manage scheduler-style notes and productivity records from the command line, including adding items, reviewing recent activity, checking status, and exporting entries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Scheduler entries are stored and exported locally, so sensitive personal or work information could be exposed if files are shared or synced unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing secrets, credentials, or highly sensitive information in entries, and review exported files before sharing or syncing them. <br>
