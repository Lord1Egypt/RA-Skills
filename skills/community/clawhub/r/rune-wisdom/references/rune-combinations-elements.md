# PART 1: ELEMENT COMBINATIONS (SKLYAROVA)

Based on V.A. Sklyarova's "Golden Runes" (Золотые руны, 2018). This system classifies each rune by element and describes how runes interact when they appear together in a reading, based on their elemental affinity.

---

## Element Classification of the Elder Futhark

Each rune belongs to one of five elements. When runes of the same or different elements appear together in a spread, their combined meaning follows systematic patterns.

| Element | Runes | Core Principle |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **Fire** (Огонь) | Fehu, Thurisaz, Raido, Kenaz, Nauthiz, Algiz, Sowilo, Tiwaz, Mannaz | Accomplishment, creative energy, emotions, manifestation |
| **Water** (Вода) | Hagalaz, Eihwaz, Perthro, Laguz, Isa | Sensual aspect, high potential, renewal of feelings, change |
| **Air** (Воздух) | Ansuz, Gebo, Ehwaz | Intentions, possibilities, vibrational field, direction of wind |
| **Earth** (Земля) | Uruz, Wunjo, Berkano, Ingwaz, Othala | Materiality of existence, mercy, recompense, material blessings |
| **Time** (Время) | Jera, Dagaz | Development of processes over time, tendencies, direction, path |

**Note on the element of Time:** In Sklyarova's system, a fifth element — Time — is added to the traditional four. Time-element runes always contextualize the surrounding runes in terms of process development and chronology. When reading combinations with Time runes, the process is "handed over to the great Saturn, the planet of Time, Chronos."

**Note on Isa:** Isa is classified under the element of Ice (a sub-category of Water), reflecting its nature as frozen water — preservation, stillness, and the crystalline quality of ice.

---

## Element Combination Rules

When a rune appears alongside runes of various elements, the following patterns emerge:

- **Same element (self):** The rune's energy is amplified or modified by runes sharing its nature. The result depends on whether the surrounding same-element runes are positive or negative.
- **Fire + any rune:** Brings energy, action, transformation. Can indicate crisis, passion, or sudden change.
- **Air + any rune:** Brings intellect, communication, intention. Indicates mental activity, plans, and possibilities.
- **Water + any rune:** Brings emotion, intuition, renewal. Indicates changes, feelings, and the flow of events.
- **Earth + any rune:** Brings stability, materiality, patience. Indicates concrete outcomes, health, and material benefit.
- **Time + any rune:** Contextualizes the process chronologically. Indicates timing, duration, and the arc of development.

**Reversed runes in combinations:** When any rune in a combination appears reversed, the meaning shifts to the negative aspect of the combination. Use the direct reading but take the worst interpretation.

## Rune Classification by Direction and Function

From Sklyarova's classification system:

### Mirror Runes (symmetric, no reversed position)
Gebo, Isa, Jera, Sowilo, Eihwaz — these runes do not have a reversed reading. Their energy is self-contained and symmetrical. They are the most powerful for talismans and amulets.

### "Bow-tie" Runes (karmic knots)
Mannaz, Ingwaz, Dagaz, Othala — these runes "tie a bow," preserving their inner potential and fixing established situations with karmic bonds. Their appearance signals changes in karmic forces and influences, often transitions to new karmic levels. Consult the combination tables for their multi-variant readings.

### Directional Runes
Fehu, Tiwaz, Laguz — more "material," more tied to the realities of life, more concrete.

### Inner Growth and Creation Runes
Kenaz, Perthro, Berkano — three runes of inner development and building.

### Runes of Overcoming
Uruz, Thurisaz — two runes of struggle and surmounting obstacles.

### Energy Protection Rune
Sowilo — the sole rune of energetic protection in the Futhark.

### Universal Protection Rune
Algiz — the single rune of protection for all occasions. Most frequently used in runic mantic practice.

### Runes of Change for the Worse
Nauthiz, Hagalaz — two runes signaling deterioration. Hagalaz is recognized as the most negative rune of the Futhark.

## Method: Reading Two-Rune Combinations in Spreads

When interpreting a spread, read rune pairs position by position. For example, in a three-position spread:

1. Position 1 + Position 2: Read the combination of the two runes
2. Position 2 + Position 3: Read the next pair
3. Continue through all adjacent positions

**IMPORTANT:** Before reading individual pairs, check all consecutive triplets (positions 1-2-3, 2-3-4, etc.) against Part 1.5 (Three-Rune Combinations). A matched triplet provides a single fused meaning that **overrides** the pair readings for those positions. Only fall back to pair reading if no triplet matches.

For each pair, determine:
- The element of each rune
- Look up the element combination meaning from the tables above
- Check for any named special pairings
- Consider whether either rune is reversed (take the worst aspect)
- Interpret in the context of the question asked

**Note on negative combinations:** The combination tables above provide direct (positive) readings. For reversed/negative readings, take the worst aspect of the direct reading. For example, "financial success" becomes "financial loss," "protection" becomes "vulnerability," etc.

# PART 1.5: THREE-RUNE COMBINATIONS (TRIPLETS)

> This section consolidates all known three-rune combinations (triplets) from across the entire reference corpus into a single lookup table. When three adjacent runes in a spread match any of these triplets, the triplet meaning **overrides** the individual pair readings — the three runes function as a single semantic unit.
>
> **Why triplets matter:** Skírnir's three runes in the Eddic poem (Skírnismál v.37) establish the principle that three runes carved together form the **minimal complete operative unit** — just as the three Norns, three aetts, and three positions (Direct/Mirrored/Inverted) express completeness through triadic structure. A triplet in a spread is not three separate readings but a single pattern the Norns are weaving.
>
> **How to use this section:** When reading a spread of 3+ runes, check ALL consecutive triplets (positions 1-2-3, 2-3-4, 3-4-5, etc.) against the tables below. A matched triplet provides a single fused meaning that replaces the three individual rune interpretations for those positions. If no triplet matches, fall back to the two-rune pair system in Part 1.

## B. Seasonal Triads (Aswynn's Annual Cycle)

These triplets correspond to the eight pagan festivals of the year. When all three runes of a seasonal triad appear in the same reading — regardless of position or order — the reading is **amplified by the seasonal current**. The season's energy is actively supporting or challenging the querent.

If the reading occurs during the corresponding festival period, the triad's power is at maximum; if outside the season, the triad indicates that the querent is experiencing that seasonal energy out of phase — either prematurely or as an echo.

## C. Mantic Palindrome Triplets (Shi School)

These are operative palindrome formulas from the Shi school (Амон Ра) where a central rune is flanked by identical runes, creating a bilateral amplification. When such a pattern appears in a spread — regardless of whether it was consciously constructed — it functions as a **self-reinforcing magical pattern** embedded in the reading.

| Triplet | Elements | Meaning | Source |
|---------|----------|---------|--------|
| ᚢ Uruz + ᛞ Dagaz + ᚠ Fehu | Earth + Time + Fire | Get rich quickly. Primal force accelerates transformation into wealth. The central Dagaz (breakthrough) is driven by Uruz's raw power on one side and Fehu's material manifestation on the other. | `rune-mantic-layers.md` (Shi school Palindrome section) |
| ᚠ Fehu + ᛞ Dagaz + ᛟ Othala | Fire + Time + Earth | Get rid of a relative and receive an inheritance. Transformation of family structure into heritage. The central Dagaz transforms Fehu's movable wealth into Othala's ancestral property. | Same |

**Note:** The Shi school explicitly warns that Dagaz's full spectrum is wide — theoretically, the person could die and become a holy martyr. Adding Fehu on both sides of Dagaz clarifies what transformation and what outcome are desired. When Uruz-Dagaz-Fehu or Fehu-Dagaz-Othala appears unbidden in a divination spread, the Norns are showing a **transformation of material state** — but the querent should be warned that Dagaz-transformations are not always gentle.

### How to Read a Three-Element Synthesis

1. **Identify each rune's element** from the classification table in Part 1.
2. **Match the element sequence** to the synthesis table above (read left-to-right in spread order, or past→present→future in temporal spreads).
3. **Overlay the specific runes' meanings** onto the general element narrative. For example, if the triplet is Fehu (Fire) + Ansuz (Air) + Othala (Earth), the element pattern is Fire→Air→Earth ("vision + strategy = achievement"), and the specific reading is: "Creative energy (Fehu) channeled through divine communication (Ansuz) manifests as ancestral heritage (Othala)."
4. **Check for reversed runes**: If any rune is reversed/inverted/mirrored, the element's contribution shifts to its negative aspect within the narrative structure. For example, Fire→Air→Earth with Fehu inverted becomes: "Financial loss (Fehu inverted) channeled through communication (Ansuz) still manifests as heritage (Othala) — but the heritage may come through loss or inheritance rather than earning."
5. **Check against named triplets first**: Always scan Sections A–E before falling back to this synthesis method. A named triplet always takes precedence over a general element reading.

## I. BINDRUNES: DEFINITION AND THEORY

### What is a Bindrune?
A bindrune (from Old Norse *bandrún*) is several runes combined into a single composite sign — a monogram. Also called "linked runes," "binding runes," or (in Meadows' translation) "unifying runes." Bindrunes can be forged, cast, or bound as jewelry in their completed form.

### Core Principles
- **Same logical basis as runescripts**: a bindrune may encode a word, a name, or a complex of intentional entities
- **Orientation flexibility**: runes within a bindrune may change orientation (mirror-flipped, as when writing right-to-left)
- **HARMONY IS PARAMOUNT**: a bindrune must be visually balanced and harmonious. This is the primary rule, overriding symmetry concerns
- **Multiple compositions possible**: the same set of runes can be arranged in multiple bindrune configurations — test each for harmony
- **Max 5 runes recommended** (traditional): each rune should retain its individuality and not "dissolve" into the complex symbol. Keep requests simple and concrete

### Hidden Runes (Second-Plane Runes)
When runes merge within a bindrune, their overlapping lines naturally form NEW runes that were not intentionally placed. These "hidden runes" or "second-plane runes" are critical:
- They can **enhance** the bindrune's intent (e.g., exam help → hidden Nauthiz + Algiz = persistent effort + protection)
- They can **contradict** the intent, breaking harmony
- **Always analyze the completed bindrune** for accidental rune formations before activation
- Experienced practitioners always diagnose before activating
- Sometimes visual harmony matters more than mathematical precision — aesthetically perfect signs implant more easily into the subconscious and begin working on the subtle plane faster

### The Grounding Line
A technique attributed to Tony Willis: a horizontal line connecting the two lower ends of a bindrune to "ground" its influence — giving materiality, substance, and tangible manifestation to the working.
- Purpose: to anchor the rune's influence in physical reality, add "materiality"
- **NOT applied** to bindrunes "standing on one leg" (those built on a single central vertical axis)
- Functions as a horizontal variant of Isa — fixation, preservation of expected result
- Used to prevent two vertical runes from merging into an unintended form (e.g., double Laguz + Ar would create an overly active Teiwaz — the grounding line separates them)
- Isa + grounding line = preservation of existing relationship (not improvement, specifically preservation)

### Teutonic Linked Runes
A specific technique using two main "benevolent" runes as axial supports:
- **Gebo** (axis of partnership/love)
- **Nauthiz** (axis of need/necessity/coercion)
- Historically linked to love magic ("binding"), but modern understanding: the purpose is to help a timid person express feelings, not to force love
- Other runes are "hung" on the axial rune

## II. RUNESCRIPTS vs. BINDRUNES: DECISION FRAMEWORK

### Runescript (Linear Formula)
- Sequential writing, left to right (or right to left by tradition)
- **Operates in TIME** — cause and effect, linear progression
- First rune launches the process, middle runes transform the situation, final rune locks the result
- Like writing a sentence — each word has its assigned role
- Ideal for phased tasks (e.g., find job: remove obstacles → find opportunities → interview → get position)
- Energy flows sequentially like water through a channel — no skipping stages
- Best for: moving from point A to point B, development, learning, sequential achievements
- Typical carrier: paper, candle (short-term)
- Duration: hours to ~1 month, not renewable

### Bindrune (Woven/Composite Sign)
- Runes overlaid upon each other into a unique new symbol
- **Operates in the MOMENT** — not in time, but as a simultaneous field of tension "here and now"
- Like a loud chord where all notes sound at once — not a sentence but an acoustic event
- Acts as a **seal** or **energy accumulator**
- Does NOT unfold gradually — it changes the STATE of the object or person directly
- No beginning or end — a cyclic, closed circuit of force that constantly broadcasts its program
- Best for: protection amulets, luck talismans, health harmonization, fixing results, character changes
- Typical carrier: wood, stone, metal (long-term wearable)
- Duration: months to indefinitely (for well-constructed bindrunes)

### Quick Decision Guide
| Need | Choose |
|------|--------|
| Sequential process, A→B | Runescript |
| Fix result, set protection | Bindrune |
| Short-term, disposable | Runescript |
| Long-term, wearable amulet | Bindrune |
| Change someone's state | Bindrune |
| Guide a process through steps | Runescript |
| Dynamic, moving energy | Runescript |
| Static, stabilizing energy | Bindrune |

## III. FORMULA CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM

### Tier 1: Rune Formula / Palindrome (Рунная формула)
- 3-5+ runes written in a row
- Fast and direct, works from 1 hour to ~1 month
- NOT renewable — must destroy and recreate
- Best for simple, quick matters

### Tier 2: Galdrastav / Stav (Гальдрастав)
- Runes encrypted into a unified graphic drawing
- Composition IS known to the operator
- Works up to 3 months, sometimes longer
- Renewable with difficulty
- More powerful than linear formulas due to encryption

### Tier 3: Galdramyund (Гальдрамюнд)
- Graphic drawing from runes where composition is UNKNOWN
- The encryption itself amplifies the working — secrecy = power
- Works up to 6 months
- Can be renewed/recharged
- Principle: "encrypted formulas work better and longer than those with known composition"

### Tier 4: Bindrune (Биндруна)
- Runes are so thoroughly encrypted they are not even visible
- Stylized as a tree-like figure with runes "hung" on a central trunk/stem
- Works very long term
- Suitable for amulets and even tattoos
- Highest level of runic encryption

### Key Rule: Less Is More
- The fewer runes in a formula, the faster and stronger it works
- Ideal: 3-5 runes
- Formulas with 20+ repeated runes are "gravitsapas" that won't fly — energy divides among all signs, each gets ~1% instead of ~30%
- Repeated runes count as ONE rune — writing the same rune 10 times doesn't make it 10x stronger
- Heavy, overloaded graphics (entire Futhark + Chinese alphabet on 2 pages) should be avoided

## IV. GRAPHIC MAGIC: THE COMPLETE SYSTEM

### Fundamental Principle
All magic = moving energy from one place to another. Energy is substance — it fills people, objects, entities; moves through channels and streams that collect into egregors. The essence of any magical operation: find fuel, receive it, direct it where needed.

### The Four Types of Graphic Magic

#### 1. Runes and Liters
- Individual "puzzle pieces" with fixed meanings
- Like letters — each has one specific concept
- Cannot invent new meanings for established runes
- Must combine 3+ runes for meaningful programs (one rune = one letter, not a sentence)
- **Duration**: 1-2 months, fast but short-term
- **Recharging**: very difficult, reluctantly — better to destroy and make new formula
- Encrypted/hidden runes work longer than explicit ones

#### 2. Glyphs (Глифы)
- Next complexity level — mini-formulas embedded in a single graphic sign
- 1 glyph ≈ 2-3 runes in semantic weight
- While a rune denotes a concept (e.g., Raido = "movement"), a glyph carries a whole process (e.g., "successful movement in travel with negative line cutoff")
- Several glyphs = full composition with broad program
- **Duration**: longer and more powerful than runes
- **Recharging**: easily recharged multiple times
- **Carrier tolerance**: survives being wiped off body — program continues for ogovored duration
- Can be tattooed (re-ogovor every few months)
- Do NOT depend on Moon phases — work until result, then switch off

#### 3. Sigils (Сигилы)
- Highest level of graphic expression
- Complex semantic fabric with elaborate program for infinite repetition when needed
- Like a QR-code: a small image encodes a large volume of information readable by the subtle plane
- Can work for centuries
- One ogovor, forever — activates/deactivates based on operator attention
- Can be tattooed, worn as amulets
- **Entity seals** are sigils — personal "phone numbers" of entities
  - Each entity has ~50 personal sigils at different trust levels
  - More exclusive sigil = faster response
  - Old famous sigils (e.g., Belial's) often intercepted by lesser entities
  - Fresh personal sigils require direct relationship with entity

#### 4. Calligraphy and Autowriting
- Drawing random-looking marks while thinking of question/person
- Individual cipher — meaning only known to creator
- Impossible to read from outside
- Method for creating personal sigils and encrypted signs
- Can incorporate letters, lines, any visual elements

### Critical Universal Principle: Pantheon Independence
No graphic system is permanently bound to a specific pantheon, egregor, or plane. Runes are UNIVERSAL SCRIPT — you can write an appeal to Shiva or Hecate using runes, and they receive it just fine, often faster than other methods. This means:
- Runes from different systems CAN be mixed (Futhark + Northumbrian + glyphs = valid)
- Innovation is valid — newly created graphic signs work if properly charged
- The "tradition only" mindset is cargo cult, not practical reality

## V. ACTIVATION AND OGOVOR PROTOCOLS

### The Three-Step Working Method
1. **Draw graphics on carrier** — any method, any material, any order
2. **Ogovor (verbalize intent)** — speak the program aloud or as thoughtform
3. **Charge** — happens automatically through your contact (eyes, hands)

### Activation: No Special Ritual Needed
- Graphics activate from ogovor + charging — no separate "activation" step required
- Activation via breath, saliva, blood, fire, elements = harmless but unnecessary addition
- The "Paraguayan national anthem" test: if singing a random song after activation changes nothing, the activation itself was decorative
- A formula that's properly ogovored and charged begins working immediately
- Reaches maximum in 2-3 days
- Achieves result within 1 month (if physically achievable)
- If result is physically impossible — runes switch off, sigils/glyphs retry with more energy

### Energy Management (The "Gasoline" Principle)
- The operator's Qi (life energy) is the fuel
- You will feel slightly worse after charging — this is normal
- Body won't give its reserves, only surplus — 80% of formulas fail from lack of energy, not incorrect procedure
- Multiple formulas divide available energy: 2 formulas = 50% each, 4 = 25% each
- External energy sources possible IF the source consents — you cannot steal energy from "neighbor Vasya"
- Drawing energy directly from space is "unhygienic" (like drinking from a city river)

### Ogovor Structure (80% of Success)
The ogovor is whispered or spoken softly, following this structure:
1. **Rune names** (if known) — activates the runes
2. **Purpose and problem** — detailed description of what you want; hold the approximate action of each rune in mind
3. **Course of action** — briefly describe HOW the runes should work
4. **Clarifications** — without harm to physical/mental health, lifestyle; specific timeframes; any exceptions
5. **Rune names again** (if known) — closing activation

**Important**: For glyphs/sigils/unknown runes — skip name parts, use the general formula name instead.

The ogovor is the program. The same formula can be ogovored for completely different purposes (e.g., a potency formula ogovored for physical stamina; a protective formula ogovored as a curse). It all depends on the ogovor.

## VI. PRACTICAL APPLICATION RULES

### Where to Apply
- On self, another person, photo, water/food/soap/cream, clean paper/material
- **Critical condition**: must be able to ERASE or DESTROY the carrier
- NOT recommended as tattoo or carved into massive stone (runes work in cycles — must be destroyed after result)

### Body Application Rules
- **LEFT side of body**: influencing YOURSELF (money, work, weight loss, healing)
- **RIGHT side of body**: influencing OTHERS from yourself (morok, love spells, influence)
- Specific body part doesn't matter — wherever you can reach
- Left-handedness does NOT reverse the rule

### Material and Method
- Any writing instrument: pen, pencil, pastel, paint, scratch with nail, cross-stitch
- Emergency: finger on foggy glass, stick tracing on water, saliva on wrist
- Medicinal formulas: carve on food, draw on water with toothpick/knife
- Can be applied to bath foam, soap, cream, comb, etc.

### Deactivation
- **Destroy the carrier**: burn, tear to pieces, flush down toilet, throw in river
- **Erase the inscription** if carrier must be preserved
- Runes work in cycles — after reaching maximum positive influence they swing back like a pendulum → destroy formula IMMEDIATELY after result

### Timing
- Moon phase: does NOT affect rune/glyph workings (only individual sensitive people may notice)
- Glyphs and sigils work until result regardless of lunar calendar
- Rune formulas: ogovor "until result" or for specific time (e.g., "4 months")
