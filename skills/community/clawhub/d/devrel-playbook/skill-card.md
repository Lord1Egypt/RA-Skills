## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Manual application of moderation, event ROI, and reporting guidance could be unsuitable for a specific community or organization. <br>
Mitigation: Review the recommendations with appropriate internal stakeholders before using them operationally. <br>
Risk: Users may overestimate the skill's operational reach because it discusses Discord, Slack, events, and reporting workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as planning guidance only; the skill does not configure services, read private data, or take automatic account actions. <br>
