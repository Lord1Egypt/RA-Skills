## Description: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students and student-support agents use this skill to discuss graduation-season employment stress, interview anxiety, career uncertainty, stress-management practices, and appropriate counseling resources. It provides supportive Chinese-language coaching and practical action plans, not licensed mental-health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat supportive coaching as a substitute for licensed mental-health care. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as supportive coaching and refer users to qualified counseling or crisis resources when distress is severe or urgent. <br>
Risk: Crisis hotlines or counseling resources may be region-specific or out of date. <br>
Mitigation: Verify crisis hotlines and counseling resources for the user's location before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Student support conversations can involve sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid requesting or retaining unnecessary personal information. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed for Chinese-language support and may be less appropriate for other language contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use it only where Chinese-language student support is appropriate. <br>
