## Description: <br>
Requirement Sharpening Stone helps product managers clarify ambiguous product requests with Socratic, first-principles, and Occam-style questioning, then produce review-ready PRD v3.x documents with optional Agent-product planning stages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product managers and product teams use this skill to turn unclear product ideas into scoped PRDs, review summaries, decision records, and optional Agent-product interaction or learning-loop plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential PRD content may be written to Feishu documents or local Markdown. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the intended output location before use and restrict sensitive projects to approved storage. <br>
Risk: Optional web search and local project-context reads may expose or mix in unrelated information. <br>
Mitigation: For confidential projects, instruct the agent to avoid web search and to read only explicitly approved local files. <br>
Risk: Local experience-base logs may retain product details from each stage. <br>
Mitigation: Disable, review, or periodically remove local stage logs when retention is not appropriate. <br>
