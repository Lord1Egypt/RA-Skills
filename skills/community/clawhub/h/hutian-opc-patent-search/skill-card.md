## Use Case: <br>
External founders, product teams, and IP practitioners use this skill to prepare IP novelty searches, compare patent and trademark conflicts, and plan application, maintenance, and rights-management steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China-focused; use in other jurisdictions requires local filing and legal review. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive identity, company, source-code, or trade-secret materials while preparing IP filings. <br>
Mitigation: Redact nonessential identifiers and do not upload raw ID cards, business licenses, signatures, authorization letters, confidential source code, or trade-secret material unless using a secure, approved workflow. <br>
Risk: IP filing requirements, fees, and legal strategy may be jurisdiction-specific or change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Verify filing requirements, fee schedules, deadlines, and legal strategy with official sources or a qualified professional before acting. <br>
