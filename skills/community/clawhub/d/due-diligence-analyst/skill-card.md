## Description: <br>
AI-powered due diligence analysis that drafts company profiles, quick due diligence reports, and due diligence guidance for investment decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ZhenStaff](https://clawhub.ai/user/ZhenStaff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Investors, M&A advisors, and corporate development teams use this skill for initial target screening, preliminary risk assessment, due diligence report drafting, and due diligence process guidance. Its outputs should be independently verified before investment, legal, hiring, credit, or partnership decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reports may be mistaken for authoritative due diligence even though the evidence describes this as an early-stage LLM drafting assistant. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only for preliminary screening or drafting, and require independent verification and qualified human review before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The skill references background, credit, legal, and investment analysis domains that may require authorization, current records, and professional judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm authorization and applicable policy before any personal-background or credit-related checks, and validate legal or investment findings through official sources and qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: The artifact states that the current version relies on AI reasoning, public knowledge, and user-provided information rather than real-time enterprise databases. <br>
Mitigation: Treat company facts, ownership details, litigation status, financial data, and risk scores as unverified until checked against official disclosures or trusted data providers. <br>
