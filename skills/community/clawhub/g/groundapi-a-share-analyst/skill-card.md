## Description: <br>
Analyze A-share stocks with 13 data dimensions, macro context for gold and forex, trending sentiment, calendar awareness, realtime quotes, financials, technicals, capital flow, shareholders, management, events, and peer comparison using GroundAPI MCP tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and analysts use this skill to retrieve GroundAPI market data and produce structured A-share stock, index, ETF, and peer-comparison analysis. It supports quick overviews, deeper technical and fundamental reviews, capital-flow checks, shareholder information, recent events, sentiment, and macro context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires a GroundAPI API key and sends stock-analysis queries to GroundAPI's MCP endpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm trust in GroundAPI before installation, use a scoped or revocable API key when available, and review billing or usage limits. <br>
Risk: Generated stock analysis may be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable for investment decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational, preserve the disclaimer, and verify important conclusions against independent market data before acting. <br>
