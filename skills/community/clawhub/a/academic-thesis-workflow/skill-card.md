## Description: <br>
此技能提供一个标准化、可复现的学术论文生成工作流，通过四个有序步骤将论文主题转化为完整学术论文：主题可行性评估、主题转论证骨架加衍生方向、选定方向优化骨架、骨架转完整论文加自动复核。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External researchers, students, and academic writers use this skill to turn a thesis or paper topic into a feasibility assessment, structured argument outline, refined outline, complete draft, and review report. It is intended as a drafting workflow for Chinese academic writing across humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and engineering topics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Academic drafts may contain fabricated citations, inaccurate facts, or references that do not support the claim. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all citations and facts before relying on the output, and mark uncertain sources for follow-up rather than treating them as final. <br>
Risk: Generated thesis content could conflict with institutional academic integrity rules if submitted without appropriate human authorship and review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a drafting aid and follow the user's institution-specific rules for AI-assisted academic work. <br>
