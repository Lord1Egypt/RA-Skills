## Description: <br>
Carbon footprint calculation and ESG reporting assistant that computes Scope 1/2/3 emissions, generates sustainability reports aligned with GRI/SASB/TCFD standards, and provides decarbonization roadmap recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sustainability, finance, operations, and compliance teams use this skill to draft carbon accounting analyses, ESG reports, decarbonization roadmaps, regulatory gap checks, carbon-credit evaluations, and peer benchmarks from operational data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated emissions calculations, regulatory checks, or carbon-credit recommendations may be incomplete or outdated if source factors, rules, or market data have changed. <br>
Mitigation: Verify emission factors, regulatory claims, and carbon-credit recommendations against current official sources before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive operational, supply-chain, or financial data while preparing ESG analyses. <br>
Mitigation: Review and minimize company data before sharing it with the agent, and follow the organization's data-handling requirements. <br>
