## Description: <br>
整理和起草法律文书（庭后意见书、代理词、上诉状、答辩状、反驳意见书、质证意见等）。当用户提供案件素材（庭审笔录、证据清单、法律条文、口头陈述要点）需要整理成结构化法律文书时使用。支持行政诉讼、民事诉讼、消费者权益保护、互联网平台纠纷、合同纠纷等场景。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn case materials, hearing notes, evidence lists, statutes, and argument outlines into structured Chinese legal drafts and case-analysis outputs. It is drafting assistance only and does not replace review by a qualified legal professional. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated drafts or analysis may contain incorrect legal conclusions or unreliable citations. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all citations, legal conclusions, and filing-ready text independently before sharing or submitting. <br>
Risk: Case materials can include sensitive personal or confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive case details before using the skill and review any generated files before distribution. <br>
Risk: The command-line drafting and analysis behavior is more limited than the documentation suggests. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as structured drafting assistance and check that the generated file contains the expected analysis before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Publishing commands run from a directory with private case materials could expose unintended files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid running publish commands from directories that contain private case materials. <br>
