## Description: <br>
Coaches a newly hired Chief of Staff through their first 90 days with guidance on role definition, principal alignment, stakeholder navigation, operating cadence, and high-leverage initiatives. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external operators use this skill to plan and navigate Chief of Staff onboarding for a CEO or founder-facing role. It helps structure the first 30, 60, and 90 days around scope-setting, stakeholder interviews, cadence design, and initiative selection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential board materials, calendars, Slack or email history, or executive performance details while using the coach. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential company materials or sensitive executive details unless the agent environment is approved for that information. <br>
