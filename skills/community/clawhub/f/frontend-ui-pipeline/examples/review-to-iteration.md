# Example: Review to Iteration Plan

## Prompt

"Users say our homepage looks polished but they still cannot understand what the product does. The CTA also feels buried. Help plan the next iteration."

## Recommended Route

- Stage: Iteration Plan
- Optional companion: `web-design-guidelines` for audit depth before prioritization

## Example Output Shape

### What Stays
- Overall visual polish
- Existing brand tone
- Sections with credible proof

### What Changes

#### High Priority
- Rewrite hero hierarchy around the product value
- Move primary CTA into the first screen region
- Clarify product workflow with a short visual sequence

#### Medium Priority
- Reduce competing secondary actions
- Improve section headings for scanability

#### Low Priority
- Refine visual rhythm after hierarchy changes

### Type of Change Needed
- **Strategy problem:** Product value is not clear enough
- **Structure problem:** CTA and explanation are not prominent enough
- **Content problem:** Messaging is too vague

### Next Implementation Step
- Update hero, CTA hierarchy, and product workflow section first.
- Update `DESIGN.md` with the revised strategy and review focus.
