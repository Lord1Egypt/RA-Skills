## Description: <br>
Finds discounted local Groupon deals for services and activities such as oil changes, massages, fitness, dining, and entertainment by city and category. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dejimarquis](https://clawhub.ai/user/dejimarquis) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to search Groupon for local discounts, compare deal options, and present prices, discounts, ratings, locations, and fine print for user review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad discount questions and return deals that are outdated, unavailable, low quality, or restricted by fine print. <br>
Mitigation: Review results before acting and verify current price, availability, merchant quality, expiration, and fine print on Groupon before buying. <br>
