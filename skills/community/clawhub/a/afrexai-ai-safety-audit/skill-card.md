## Description: <br>
Performs a structured AI safety and alignment audit for businesses deploying AI agents, producing a safety scorecard, gap analysis, and 90-day remediation roadmap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Businesses, developers, and compliance or security reviewers use this skill to evaluate production AI models and agents against 30 controls across governance, data protection, output safety, security, monitoring, and organizational readiness. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audit results may include regulatory conclusions or compliance cost estimates that are incomplete or incorrect for a specific organization. <br>
Mitigation: Verify regulatory conclusions, cost estimates, and remediation plans with qualified compliance or security professionals before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Using real system details during an audit may expose unnecessary confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Scope inputs carefully and avoid sharing confidential system details that are not required for the audit. <br>
Risk: Linked paid resources may not be necessary or appropriate for every deployment. <br>
Mitigation: Review linked resources independently before purchase or operational use. <br>
