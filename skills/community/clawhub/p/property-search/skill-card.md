## Description: <br>
Search property.com.au for Australian property research, including valuations, sold history, suburb profiles, and property details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JakeLin](https://clawhub.ai/user/JakeLin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents researching Australian real estate use this skill to construct property.com.au URLs for property profiles, sale history, suburb profiles, listings, street browsing, and school insight pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Australia <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated property links can be wrong or stale if address, suburb, postcode, or property ID inputs are incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Verify generated property links and cross-check property details before relying on them. <br>
Risk: Third-party property websites may request personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering unnecessary personal information and follow the destination site's privacy and account practices. <br>
Risk: Automated fetching may be blocked by the destination site's anti-bot controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use generated browser URLs or manual verification when web fetching is unavailable. <br>
