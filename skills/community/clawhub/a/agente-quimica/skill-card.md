## Description: <br>
Agente Quimica provides conversational chemistry tutoring for FUVEST first-phase preparation, covering core topics such as atomic structure, periodic trends, bonding, stoichiometry, solutions, thermochemistry, electrochemistry, kinetics, equilibrium, and organic chemistry. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners and tutoring agents use this skill to guide FUVEST chemistry study through conceptual prompts, topic checklists, and links to biology, physics, and mathematics where relevant. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad activation trigger may route casual chemistry-related mentions into this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow activation to explicit FUVEST, vestibular, or chemistry-help requests when tighter routing is needed. <br>
Risk: Learners may rely on generated chemistry explanations as authoritative exam preparation. <br>
Mitigation: Review important explanations against trusted curriculum materials and past FUVEST questions before high-stakes use. <br>
