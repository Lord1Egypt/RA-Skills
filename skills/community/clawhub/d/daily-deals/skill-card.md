## Description: <br>
Aggregates online discounts into concise daily deal reports for savings-focused users and resale arbitrage workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gf1023456](https://clawhub.ai/user/gf1023456) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users subscribe to scheduled deal reports, customize categories and discount thresholds, and review prior reports. The skill is oriented toward public e-commerce discount monitoring, savings, and resale opportunity analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recurring WeChat delivery may involve account linking, stored delivery identifiers, user preferences, and unsubscribe handling that are not documented. <br>
Mitigation: Before use, confirm what identifiers are stored, what data is sent to WeChat or any delivery service, how consent is captured, and how users can revoke access or unsubscribe. <br>
Risk: Deal reports may include prices, discounts, and arbitrage suggestions that become outdated quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reported prices as time-sensitive guidance, verify prices on the source platform before purchase or resale decisions, and avoid relying on stale reports. <br>
