## Description: <br>
Analyzes how often a brand appears in AI search results (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude). Identifies citation gaps and content opportunities for GEO (Generative Engine Optimization). <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, brand teams, and content strategists use this skill to audit how often a brand is cited by AI answer engines, compare visibility against competitors, and plan GEO content improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Brand, competitor, or prompt data may be sent to external AI platforms during the audit process. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm which AI platforms and accounts will be used, what information will be shared, and whether platform usage costs apply before running the audit. <br>
Risk: The release declares a python3 binary requirement even though the artifact contains no executable code. <br>
Mitigation: Verify whether python3 is actually needed before installation or remove the requirement in a future release. <br>
Risk: Citation reports and content recommendations can be misleading if query selection, competitor selection, or AI platform responses are not reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review the query set, source responses, competitor assumptions, and recommended roadmap before using the report for business decisions. <br>
