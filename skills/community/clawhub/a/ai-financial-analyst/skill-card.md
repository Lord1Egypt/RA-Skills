This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Personal investors, financial advisers, finance professionals, and business students use this skill to request financial analysis, valuation modeling, portfolio review, and market or industry research. The skill provides analysis support only and does not provide licensed investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial analysis output may be mistaken for licensed investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as analysis support only and require professional review before using outputs for trades or portfolio decisions. <br>
Risk: Market data, valuation assumptions, or portfolio recommendations may be stale, incomplete, or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Verify market data and key assumptions independently before relying on the analysis. <br>
