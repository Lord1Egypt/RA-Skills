## Description: <br>
Coordinates search requests by splitting them into up to eight complementary subtasks and assigning them to suitable search agents, always including General_Search_Agent. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[urrrich0](https://clawhub.ai/user/urrrich0) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to coordinate broad search tasks by decomposing a query into focused subtasks for search agents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search prompts may be passed to delegated search agents. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including private or sensitive information in search prompts unless that sharing is acceptable. <br>
