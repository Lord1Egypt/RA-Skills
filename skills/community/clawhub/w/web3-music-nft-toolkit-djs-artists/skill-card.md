## Description: <br>
Step-by-step Music NFT onboarding agent for DJs and artists with enforced safety rules, copyright protection, wallet security, and NFT minting guide. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External DJs and music artists use this skill to learn Web3 concepts, set up a wallet, choose a minting platform, and prepare music NFTs with wallet-safety and copyright checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Wallet onboarding can expose users to irreversible loss if a secret phrase is shared or stored insecurely. <br>
Mitigation: Warn users never to share a wallet secret phrase with an agent, author, website, or support channel, and to store it offline. <br>
Risk: Minting NFTs or funding a wallet can require spending money, and fees or platform steps may change. <br>
Mitigation: Tell users to verify current fees, network choices, and marketplace steps with official wallet and marketplace documentation before spending money. <br>
Risk: Users may attempt to mint music they do not fully own or have permission to use. <br>
Mitigation: Require an ownership or written-permission check before minting and stop the workflow when the user cannot confirm rights. <br>
Risk: The skill includes promotional X and Discord links. <br>
Mitigation: Treat promotional links as optional advertising and do not require users to visit them to follow the safety guidance. <br>
