## Description: <br>
Practical food growing for complete beginners across apartments, balconies, backyards, and windowsills, with realistic timelines and advice on what to skip. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to create a practical beginner food-growing plan for limited spaces, choose suitable crops, set up containers, and follow simple care routines. It can also support local plan files and gardening reminders when the user wants ongoing help. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save a gardening plan in the user's Documents folder. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to show the plan inline first or confirm the exact file path before writing. <br>
Risk: The skill may set recurring gardening reminders. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm reminder schedules before creating calendar entries. <br>
