## Description: <br>
AI-powered operations assistant for plumbing businesses that helps with job estimates, schedule optimization, parts inventory, customer communication templates, and profitability analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[afrexai-cto](https://clawhub.ai/user/afrexai-cto) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Owner-operators and small plumbing companies use this skill to draft estimates, organize technician schedules, track common inventory needs, prepare customer messages, and review operational performance. It is intended as a planning and drafting aid for teams of roughly 1-15 technicians. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include customer personal information, addresses, employee schedules, financial records, license details, or other sensitive business data in prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Enter only the information needed for the task, omit unnecessary personal or financial details, and review generated customer-facing content before use. <br>
Risk: Generated estimates, permit fees, margin targets, warranty tracking, and compliance reminders may not match local market conditions or legal requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm pricing, permit fees, warranty terms, license renewals, and compliance obligations against company records and local authorities before relying on the output. <br>
