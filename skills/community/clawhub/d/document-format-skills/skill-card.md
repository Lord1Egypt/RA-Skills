## Description: <br>
Document Format Skills helps agents diagnose DOCX formatting issues, fix Chinese/English punctuation, and apply preset document styles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[standman61](https://clawhub.ai/user/standman61) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to clean Chinese DOCX documents by finding formatting issues, correcting punctuation, and applying official, academic, or legal style presets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Input documents may contain confidential workplace details that could be exposed in the current chat or processing environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use only documents appropriate for the current environment and avoid sharing sensitive content unless approved. <br>
Risk: Automated diagnostics and formatting changes may miss context or alter intended document layout. <br>
Mitigation: Keep original backups and review diagnostic reports and generated DOCX files before distribution. <br>
