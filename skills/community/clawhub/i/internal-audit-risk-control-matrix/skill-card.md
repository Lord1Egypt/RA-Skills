## Description: <br>
Use this skill when an internal auditor, SOX analyst, or co-sourced audit team needs to convert an audit engagement's objective, scope, and process documentation into an IIA/COSO 2013-aligned Risk-Control Matrix. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Internal auditors, SOX analysts, and co-sourced audit teams use this skill to turn audit objectives, scope, and process documentation into a draft IIA/COSO 2013-aligned Risk-Control Matrix and audit-program outline for qualified auditor and CAE review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft audit matrices, sample sizes, reliance decisions, or residual-risk judgments may be incomplete or unsuitable if used without professional review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs labeled as drafts and require review, editing, and approval by a qualified internal auditor and CAE before walkthroughs, Test of Design, or Test of Operating Effectiveness begin. <br>
Risk: Audit scoping may involve sensitive company, process, control, or financial information. <br>
Mitigation: Limit unnecessary sensitive company details in prompts and outputs, consistent with the security guidance. <br>
