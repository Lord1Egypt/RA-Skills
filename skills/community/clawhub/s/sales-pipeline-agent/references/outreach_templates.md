# Outreach Templates by Stage

These are starting points. The agent customizes them per deal.

---

## Introduction / Cold Outreach

**Subject:** Quick question about [pain point] at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [specific thing about their company/role]. We help [ICP description] with [core value prop] — typically [specific outcome/metric].

Would it make sense to connect for 15 minutes to see if there's a fit?

[Name]

---

## Follow-Up After No Response (Day 5)

**Subject:** Re: [Original subject]

Hi [Name],

Wanted to bump this up. I know things get busy.

[One sentence reminder of value prop + specific outcome relevant to them]

Worth a quick call this week?

[Name]

---

## Post-Demo / Discovery Follow-Up

**Subject:** Next steps for [Company] + [Your Company]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the time today. A few things I want to make sure land:

- **Your main challenge:** [what they said]
- **How we solve it:** [specific capability]
- **Outcome you'd expect:** [specific metric or result]

I'll put together a proposal based on our conversation. Does [close date] work as a target for making a decision?

[Name]

---

## Proposal Send

**Subject:** [Company] — Proposal + Next Steps

Hi [Name],

Attached is the proposal we discussed. Quick summary:

- **Scope:** [what's included]
- **Investment:** $[amount]
- **Timeline:** [delivery or start date]
- **Expected outcome:** [value metric]

I'd like to walk you through it — are you free for 30 minutes [day/time options]?

[Name]

---

## Negotiation / Close Push

**Subject:** Moving forward — [Company]

Hi [Name],

I want to make sure we can hit your [timeline] start date. To do that, we'd need to finalize by [date].

I'm flexible on [concession area if applicable]. Here's what I'd propose to get this across the line: [specific offer].

Can we get 20 minutes on the calendar this week to finalize?

[Name]

---

## Re-Engagement (Gone Dark)

**Subject:** Still worth connecting?

Hi [Name],

It's been a while since we last spoke. I don't want to assume one way or the other.

If [original pain point] is still on your radar, I'd love to reconnect — we've made some changes that might be relevant.

If the timing just isn't right, no worries — just let me know and I'll leave you alone.

[Name]

---

## Breakup / Last Attempt

**Subject:** Closing the loop

Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times without hearing back. I'm going to assume the timing isn't right and close things out on my end.

If anything changes or [pain point] comes back up, feel free to reach out — I'm happy to reconnect.

Best of luck with [their initiative],
[Name]

---

## Win / Handoff

**Subject:** Welcome to [Your Company] — next steps

Hi [Name],

Thrilled to have [Company] on board. I'm looping in [CS/onboarding contact] who will be your main point of contact from here.

[CS Name] will reach out within [timeframe] to schedule your kickoff.

Don't hesitate to reach out to me directly if anything comes up. Looking forward to [outcome they bought for].

[Name]
