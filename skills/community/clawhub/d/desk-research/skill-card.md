## Description: <br>
Structured desk research workflow for market, company, policy, product, and competitor questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[draco-kzn](https://clawhub.ai/user/draco-kzn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, researchers, and analysts use this skill to plan and produce public-source desk research with scoped questions, source reliability checks, evidence tables, confidence tags, and concise synthesis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research outputs can contain incorrect, stale, or weakly supported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important citations, dates, and source reliability before relying on conclusions. <br>
Risk: Users may include confidential material in desk research prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use only information intended for the resulting analysis and follow the user's confidentiality requirements. <br>
