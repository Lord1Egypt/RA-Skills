## Description: <br>
Revenue Tracker helps autonomous AI agents log income and expenses, summarize financial performance, calculate runway, and track revenue goals using local workspace storage. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Gpunter](https://clawhub.ai/user/Gpunter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to track an agent's revenue, expenses, goals, and financial runway while keeping records in the local workspace. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores sensitive revenue, expense, balance, and goal records in local workspace memory. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid logging unnecessary sensitive details, review the local records periodically, and delete records that are no longer needed. <br>
