## Description: <br>
提供 Khronos 官方 OpenCL 规范、头文件、SDK、CTS 及社区工具的准确下载链接和资源索引。当用户询问 OpenCL 官方文档、API 参考、开发组件获取方式时使用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to find official Khronos OpenCL specifications, API references, headers, SDK components, conformance tests, and clearly attributed community tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional commands may download repositories or documents and may consume network bandwidth, disk space, and local build tools. <br>
Mitigation: Review target URLs before running commands, execute them from a user-owned project directory, and skip or comment out large CTS/source-build steps when they are not needed. <br>
Risk: Community tools could be mistaken for official Khronos resources. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the skill's attribution guidance and clearly label Intel, Google, and other community projects as non-Khronos tools. <br>
