## Description: <br>
Coordinate interior design projects with space verification, supplier localization, and staged decision-making. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to plan interior design work for homes, rentals, offices, staging, and room visualization. It helps verify dimensions, localize supplier recommendations, calculate material quantities, sequence decisions, and maintain local project notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local project notes may retain sensitive home details, including room dimensions, budget, supplier preferences, and staging plans. <br>
Mitigation: Review, archive, or delete ~/interior-design when the project is complete or when the information should no longer be retained. <br>
Risk: Design or product guidance can be unsuitable if dimensions, entry paths, budget tier, region, or availability are unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm measurements, user location, accessible retailers, logistics, and price tier before acting on specific layout or product recommendations. <br>
Risk: Material quantities, ROI estimates, and budget multipliers are planning aids and may not match supplier, contractor, or property-specific conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate calculations and costs with the relevant supplier, contractor, or property manager before purchases or physical work. <br>
