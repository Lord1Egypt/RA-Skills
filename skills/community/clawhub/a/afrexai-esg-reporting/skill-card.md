## Description: <br>
Generate ESG and sustainability reports covering materiality, environmental, social, governance, compliance, scoring, and action recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sustainability officers, finance leaders, compliance teams, investors, board members, and consultants use this skill to prepare ESG reports, scorecards, due diligence responses, and regulatory-readiness materials. It helps organize company ESG metrics into materiality assessments, disclosure tables, benchmarks, RAG statuses, and prioritized recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated regulatory or disclosure guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify regulatory claims and external-use reports with current legal, compliance, or sustainability reporting guidance before publication. <br>
Risk: ESG reporting inputs may include confidential company metrics, workforce data, supplier details, or investor materials. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide company metrics and supporting documents that the user is authorized to share with the agent environment. <br>
Risk: Fixed benchmarks and scoring guidance can be unsuitable for a specific industry, geography, or reporting boundary. <br>
Mitigation: Review benchmarks, materiality assumptions, and scoring outputs against the organization's sector, footprint, and reporting scope. <br>
