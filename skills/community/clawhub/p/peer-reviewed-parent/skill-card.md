## Description: <br>
Evidence-based parenting assistant for children ages 0-24 months that answers infant and toddler care questions with cited research and age-specific context. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[riverventures](https://clawhub.ai/user/riverventures) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External parents and caregivers use this skill to ask about development, feeding, sleep, screen time, discipline, and related questions for children under 2. It is educational support and should not replace pediatric care or emergency guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Infant-care answers may overstate evidence quality or be mistaken for medical guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational support, verify citations and current guidance for sleep safety, feeding, allergens, illness, injury, fever, breathing, or developmental concerns, and consult a pediatrician or emergency resources for medical decisions. <br>
