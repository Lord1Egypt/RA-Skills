## Description: <br>
Audit LinkedIn post drafts for algorithm compliance, AI cues, structure, and length; provides pass/fail results with detailed fixes and posting advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sergebulaev](https://clawhub.ai/user/sergebulaev) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, creators, and social media operators use this skill to review LinkedIn drafts before publishing. It flags blockers, warnings, score estimates, and suggested fixes for structure, style, timing, and AI-like phrasing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The audit uses heuristic writing advice and may be mistaken for verified LinkedIn algorithm data or AI-detector integration. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the report as guidance, review suggested rewrites before publishing, and do not rely on score estimates as verified platform or detector results. <br>
Risk: A user may assume the skill can access prior posts, LinkedIn accounts, or publishing workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Provide past posts manually when comparison is needed and do not grant LinkedIn login, posting, or scraping permissions unless a separate integration discloses that access. <br>
