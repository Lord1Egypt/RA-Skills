## Description: <br>
A language-learning agent skill that builds personalized study plans, teaches vocabulary in context, explains writing and speaking corrections, prepares learners for real conversations, and tracks progress. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[EthAgent](https://clawhub.ai/user/EthAgent) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a personalized language tutor for planning study, learning vocabulary, receiving correction explanations, practicing realistic conversations, and monitoring progress. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personalized tutoring examples may include sensitive personal, workplace, account, medical, legal, or identity details supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing highly sensitive details unless they are necessary and appropriate for the tutoring context. <br>
