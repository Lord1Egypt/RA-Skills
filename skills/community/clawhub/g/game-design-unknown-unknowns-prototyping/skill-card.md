## Description: <br>
Detect unknown unknowns in game design and decide what to prototype before committing to production. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and developers use this skill to map uncertainty in new game feature concepts, choose low-cost prototypes, sequence learning tests, and decide when to move toward production framing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Planning output can include incorrect or misleading prototype recommendations if sensitive project context is omitted or unsuitable project details are shared. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans before using them for production decisions and avoid sharing sensitive internal project details unless appropriate for the workspace and model settings. <br>
