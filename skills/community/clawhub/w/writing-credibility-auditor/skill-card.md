## Description: <br>
Audits user-provided writing for missing citations, unsupported claims, logical fallacies, weasel words, and misleading statistics, then produces a structured credibility report with flagged excerpts, severity ratings, and suggested fixes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arbazex](https://clawhub.ai/user/arbazex) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, journalists, students, editors, and other external users use this skill to review articles, essays, reports, research summaries, or arguments for credibility issues before citing, publishing, or acting on them. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the credibility audit as authoritative live fact-checking. <br>
Mitigation: Present the output as reasoning-structure and language analysis; verify real-world claims with current sources or subject-matter review. <br>
Risk: The skill may miss or overstate credibility issues in highly technical, domain-specific, or non-English text. <br>
Mitigation: Use a domain expert or language-fluent reviewer for specialized claims, non-English material, and publication-critical decisions. <br>
