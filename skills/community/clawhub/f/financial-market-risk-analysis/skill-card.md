### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial-market analysis can be mistaken for investment advice or treated as certain. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as analysis, not financial advice, and verify current prices, rates, and news before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Market data, rates, and news can become stale quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Pull current data when the answer depends on price, news, rates, laws, or market state, and state uncertainty clearly. <br>
