## Description: <br>
A novel-writing agent that generates fiction outlines, drafts chapter prose, checks chapter quality and sensitive terms, supports Chinese and English writing, and manages reusable writing materials. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External writers and publishing teams use this skill to turn planning documents into complete novel outlines, chapter drafts, quality checks, and release-ready writing packages. It is designed for bilingual Chinese and English fiction workflows with platform-specific style guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private manuscripts, unreleased plots, or business-sensitive publishing plans could be exposed through prompts or retained writing materials. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive planning inputs unless the hosting environment's retention and access controls are understood. <br>
Risk: The material-library workflow may accumulate reusable scenes, dialogue templates, plot devices, or drafts across writing sessions. <br>
Mitigation: Review stored materials before reuse and clear low-quality, private, or rights-sensitive entries. <br>
Risk: Generated fiction may still contain sensitive, prohibited, or publication-inappropriate content despite the skill's self-check guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Run human review before publication, especially for sensitive terms, political content, sexual content, violence, originality, and platform policy fit. <br>
