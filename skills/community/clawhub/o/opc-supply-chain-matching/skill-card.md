## Description: <br>
根据项目阶段判断制造能力层级，实现从技术原型到量产的供应链精准匹配。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
OPC community members and project operators use this skill to diagnose manufacturing needs, choose internal collaboration or external supplier sourcing, compare quotes, negotiate costs, and manage supplier onboarding through delivery acceptance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Supplier forms, contracts, payment details, drawings, BOMs, CAD or Gerber files, and supplier records may contain confidential business data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum necessary information, verify supplier legitimacy, and use NDA or confidentiality terms before sending sensitive project materials. <br>
Risk: Procurement templates and supplier recommendations may be incomplete for a specific jurisdiction, supplier, or product category. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated forms, contracts, qualification checks, and acceptance criteria with responsible business, legal, and technical reviewers before use. <br>
