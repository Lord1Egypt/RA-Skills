## Description: <br>
Provides 15 strategic AI advisor personas for board-level and company-defining decisions such as governance, fundraising, exits, risk, scaling, and talent. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rmarji](https://clawhub.ai/user/rmarji) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and business operators use this skill to structure strategic decision-making with simulated board-level advisors. It helps frame questions, surface red flags, and apply strategy frameworks, but it does not replace qualified legal, financial, governance, fundraising, or M&A professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat simulated advisor personas as verified experts for major legal, financial, governance, fundraising, or M&A decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as structured business-strategy brainstorming and consult qualified professionals before acting on high-stakes decisions. <br>
