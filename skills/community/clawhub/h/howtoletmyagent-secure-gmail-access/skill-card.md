## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Gmail OAuth setup can expose sensitive mail data or credentials if broad scopes, OAuth secrets, or tokens are handled carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed Gmail scopes and Google Cloud changes manually, use the narrowest permissions that meet the task, and do not paste OAuth secrets or tokens into chat unless the exposure is understood. <br>
