## Description: <br>
Provides Telegram-based B2B sales guidance, bot command templates, inline keyboard qualification flows, large file sharing practices, and channel-specific sales strategies for SDR workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ipythoning](https://clawhub.ai/user/ipythoning) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, sales operations teams, and SDR builders use this skill to design Telegram bot commands, qualification flows, and file-sharing practices for B2B sales conversations. It is most useful as a template for implementing Telegram outreach and CRM-connected lead handling with appropriate review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Telegram and CRM workflows may collect or store lead data without adequate notice, consent, retention limits, or deletion handling. <br>
Mitigation: Add a privacy notice, obtain appropriate consent for CRM storage and outreach, and define retention and deletion rules before deployment. <br>
Risk: Bot credentials and administrative commands could expose the Telegram bot or sales workflow if handled casually. <br>
Mitigation: Keep bot tokens secret and restrict administrative commands to approved operators. <br>
Risk: Language detection from Telegram profile data may produce incorrect or undesired customer language choices. <br>
Mitigation: Let users view and change their language preference during the conversation. <br>
