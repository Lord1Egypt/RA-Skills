## Description: <br>
Generate personalized donor communication messages including thank-you notes, impact updates, re-engagement emails, and donation acknowledgment letters. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Fundraisers and nonprofit staff use this skill to draft donor thank-you notes, impact updates, re-engagement emails, and formal donation acknowledgments tailored to donor context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts for donor communications may include donor personal data, payment details, or confidential fundraising records. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the donor details needed for the requested message and avoid unnecessary payment or confidential fundraising information. <br>
Risk: Generated donor messages may overstate impact or include inaccurate fundraising claims if source details are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review each draft against verified campaign records before sending it to donors or supporters. <br>
