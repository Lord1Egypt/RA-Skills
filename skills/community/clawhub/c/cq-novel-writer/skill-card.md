## Description: <br>
长篇小说持续创作技能。当需要写小说、写网文、写重生穿越文、每天定期写章节、更新小说大纲、维护人物关系时使用此技能。自动读取故事总纲、前文、范本，确保章节连贯、风格一致。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and writers use this skill to continue long-form Chinese web fiction by reading the story outline, recent chapters, and a style example before drafting new chapters and updating continuity notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may modify local writing files and append generated chapter content. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target paths before use and keep backups of affected novel project files. <br>
Risk: The skill describes optional hourly automation for recurring chapter writing. <br>
Mitigation: Disable or avoid scheduled execution unless recurring automated writing is intentional. <br>
