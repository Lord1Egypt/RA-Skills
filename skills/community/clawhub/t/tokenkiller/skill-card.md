## Description: <br>
Reduces token usage across multi-skill agent workflows using budgets, gating, progressive disclosure, and deduped evidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CodeRangerX](https://clawhub.ai/user/CodeRangerX) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use TokenKiller to reduce token consumption during search, coding, debugging, testing, and documentation workflows while preserving enough evidence to complete the task. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation triggers may cause the skill to shape ordinary conversations where token-saving behavior was not intended. <br>
Mitigation: Disable the skill or narrow activation when concise, budgeted responses interfere with the task. <br>
