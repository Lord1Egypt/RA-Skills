## Description: <br>
Audits how a brand appears in AI-powered search across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[BrianRWagner](https://clawhub.ai/user/BrianRWagner) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, founders, and consultants use this skill to audit how a brand appears in AI search, document recognition and accuracy gaps, compare competitors, and build a prioritized action plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Queries sent to external AI platforms may expose sensitive or unreleased brand, customer, or competitor information. <br>
Mitigation: Use public or non-sensitive brand information when running audit queries on external AI platforms. <br>
Risk: Generated output may include the author's booking link from the source skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review the final output and remove the booking link when promotional material is not desired. <br>
