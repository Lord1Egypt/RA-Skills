## Description: <br>
技术成果转化全流程管理，覆盖从实验室成果到商业产品的转化路径规划与执行。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External technology-transfer consultants, research organizations, universities, public institutions, and enterprise innovation teams use this skill to evaluate early- and mid-stage research outputs, identify resource gaps, design cooperation or transaction paths, and structure return-sharing plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Technology-transfer work can expose personal, proprietary, or IP-sensitive project materials. <br>
Mitigation: Get documented authorization from rights holders, redact unnecessary sensitive details, and require confidentiality terms before sharing detailed materials. <br>
Risk: Contract, valuation, regulatory, or investment outputs may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific transaction. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified legal, IP, regulatory, or investment professionals review contracts, valuations, and transaction plans before use. <br>
