## Description: <br>
深度拆解爆款文章，分析其核心观点、说服策略、情绪触发点、金句及语言风格。适用于分析高流量内容、学习爆款写作技巧以及内容营销研究。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External content teams, marketers, writers, and researchers use this skill to deconstruct high-traffic articles into viewpoints, persuasion methods, emotional triggers, language style, and reusable writing patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce interpretive claims about real people, organizations, or marketing intent that overstate what the source article supports. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against the source article and treat unsupported interpretations as hypotheses rather than facts. <br>
