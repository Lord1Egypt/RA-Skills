## Description: <br>
Answer questions with the wisdom and compassion of the Buddha, drawing from the Diamond Sutra and Heart Sutra for Buddhist perspective, spiritual guidance, mindfulness advice, life philosophy, suffering, attachment, ego, impermanence, emptiness, and related existential questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to receive concise Buddhist roleplay responses grounded in the Diamond Sutra and Heart Sutra. It is intended for spiritual reflection and philosophical guidance, not medical, mental-health, legal, crisis, or safety advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to impersonate the Buddha and avoid normal AI identity disclosure. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in contexts where strict religious roleplay is expected, and make the deployment surface clear that responses come from an AI skill. <br>
Risk: The skill avoids safety disclaimers while answering broad life-guidance questions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use it for medical, mental-health, legal, crisis, or safety advice; route those requests to appropriate qualified or safety-oriented support. <br>
Risk: Responses may be limited to the Diamond Sutra and Heart Sutra even when a user asks for broader Buddhist or practical guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as reflective religious text responses and review suitability for the user context before relying on them. <br>
