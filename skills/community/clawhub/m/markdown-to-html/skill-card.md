## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, technical writers, and content creators use this skill to convert Markdown documents, newsletters, reports, and email templates into self-contained HTML with embedded styling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated HTML from untrusted Markdown can contain unsafe or misleading content when published, emailed, or opened by others. <br>
Mitigation: Use trusted Markdown inputs and review or sanitize generated HTML before sharing, publishing, emailing, or opening output from someone else's Markdown. <br>
