## Description: <br>
Provide grounded knowledge, distinctions, and reflective support around enlightenment-related topics such as 开悟, 觉醒, 见性, 无我, 空性, spiritual insight, and inner transformation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to ask about enlightenment-related concepts, reflect on awakening-like experiences, correct misconceptions, and express subtle inner experiences with grounded, non-dogmatic language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat spiritual or anxiety-related reflection as mental-health care. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as reflective support only, and route ordinary distress or safety-sensitive wellbeing cases to an appropriate wellbeing or safety-aware assistant. <br>
Risk: The skill could over-certify enlightenment or spiritual attainment. <br>
Mitigation: Use cautious language, avoid declaring attainment, and focus on observable grounding, stability, and real-life behavior. <br>
Risk: Destabilized, dissociated, or grandiose states could be spiritualized. <br>
Mitigation: Slow down interpretation, encourage sleep, routine, body grounding, and trusted relationships, and prioritize safety if self-harm or dangerous behavior appears. <br>
