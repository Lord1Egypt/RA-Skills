## Description: <br>
Diagnose spreadsheet structure and recommend practical Excel/WPS-compatible formulas and workflows for cleaning, lookup and matching, summarization, text and date cleanup, and common formula troubleshooting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[winstonice-prog](https://clawhub.ai/user/winstonice-prog) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Spreadsheet users and agents use this skill to inspect table headers, sample rows, and formula problems before choosing a practical Excel or WPS cleanup, lookup, matching, summarization, or troubleshooting workflow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Spreadsheet samples may contain sensitive business or personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the headers and sample rows needed for diagnosis. <br>
Risk: Formula or workflow recommendations may be incorrect if the table structure is incomplete or misunderstood. <br>
Mitigation: Review formulas before applying them and require confirmation before direct workbook changes. <br>
