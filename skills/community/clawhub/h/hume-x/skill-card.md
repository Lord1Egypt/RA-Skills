## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to operate the hume-x CLI for X/Twitter workflows, including reading timelines, searching, posting, engagement actions, account relationship changes, and scheduled tweets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can use browser session cookies or explicit credentials for an X/Twitter account. <br>
Mitigation: Install only from a trusted hume-x package source, prefer explicit credentials over browser-cookie discovery, and consider a dedicated account. <br>
Risk: The skill can post, delete, follow, unfollow, mute, block, pin, retweet, and schedule tweets. <br>
Mitigation: Require manual confirmation before any account-changing or public-posting action. <br>
