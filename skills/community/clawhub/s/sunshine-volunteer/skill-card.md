Mitigation: Avoid entering personal or identifying details, and anonymize people and locations before using generated content publicly. <br>
Risk: Public charity or education content could be inaccurate, lack consent, or frame rural children disrespectfully. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated material for consent, factual accuracy, and respectful language before publishing or sharing. <br>
