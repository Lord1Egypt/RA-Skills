## Description: <br>
Send test or generated images to Telegram chats using the message tool with Picsum.photos URLs or custom media. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[drones277](https://clawhub.ai/user/drones277) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Telegram users and automation builders use this skill to send a selected image or generated image URL to a Telegram chat with an optional caption. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Images or captions may be sent to the wrong Telegram chat or expose private media if the destination and source are not checked. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target chat and media URL or file source before sending, especially for private or user-provided content. <br>
