## Description: <br>
Routes mixed Chinese-English bot correction intents to VeriClaw resources for evidence, diagnosis, intervention, and verification after bot drift or premature completion claims. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sheygoodbai](https://clawhub.ai/user/sheygoodbai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and OpenClaw users use this skill to recognize bot-correction, bot-validation, and bot-drift-recovery requests and route them to VeriClaw guidance and install paths. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may trigger during generic troubleshooting or unrelated verification requests. <br>
Mitigation: Review trigger behavior before installation and narrow or disable triggers if it fires outside bot correction, bot validation, or bot drift recovery contexts. <br>
