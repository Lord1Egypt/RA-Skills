Mitigation: Only provide datasets intended for analysis, prefer copies for sensitive files, specify output locations, and review generated files before sharing. <br>
Risk: Generated analysis, SQL, or statistics may be incorrect or misleading if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed queries, assumptions, generated CSVs, reports, and images before relying on them for decisions. <br>
