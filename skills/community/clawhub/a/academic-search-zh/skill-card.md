## Description: <br>
Academic Search Zh helps users plan Chinese and English academic literature searches, select databases, analyze paper abstracts and methods, and draft structured literature reviews. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[olina1ye](https://clawhub.ai/user/olina1ye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, instructors, and R&D teams use this skill to create literature search strategies, choose databases such as CNKI, Wanfang, Web of Science, Scopus, and Google Scholar, analyze research methods, and organize findings into literature-review structures. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated summaries, citations, and research claims may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify summaries, citations, and claims against the original papers and database records before relying on them. <br>
Risk: User-provided PDFs or documents may contain sensitive or unpublished research content. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide PDFs or extracted text that the user is comfortable having the agent process. <br>
Risk: The skill does not directly access paid journal full text or replace actual database searches. <br>
Mitigation: Use the generated search strings and review structure as support, then perform and verify searches in the relevant academic databases. <br>
