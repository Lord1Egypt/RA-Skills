## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill as an opt-in accountability persona that responds to procrastination or explicit requests for prompting with randomized, escalating motivational messages and brief next-step guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may unexpectedly switch the agent into a sharp accountability persona during ordinary conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Limit activation to explicit opt-in phrases such as “催我一下” or other direct requests for prompting. <br>
Risk: Escalating language can feel too forceful for users who are resting, on leave, or handling incidents. <br>
Mitigation: Honor the skill's stated quiet periods and exceptions, and switch to practical help for urgent production or emergency contexts. <br>
