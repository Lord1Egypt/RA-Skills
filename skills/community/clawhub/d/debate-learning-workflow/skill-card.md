## Description: <br>
Run evidence-backed multi-agent debates with 20-40 rounds, loophole analysis, judge-gated continuation, and actionable lesson extraction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kdylan1010-alt](https://clawhub.ai/user/kdylan1010-alt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and reasoning-focused agent users use this skill to run structured debates that test claims, expose loopholes, and convert outcomes into transferable lessons. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Debate topics and extracted lessons may be saved locally in Desktop Markdown files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive topics unless local note storage is acceptable, and review generated notes before sharing or retaining them. <br>
