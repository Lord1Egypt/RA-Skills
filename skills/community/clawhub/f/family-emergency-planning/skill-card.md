## Description: <br>
Comprehensive family emergency preparedness planning for creating household emergency plans, preparing for natural disasters, building go-bags, and helping families communicate and reunite during a crisis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and households use this skill to build practical emergency plans, including fire escape routes, communication plans, document kits, go-bags, evacuation routes, utility shut-off steps, and special-needs planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users to copy, digitize, store, and share highly sensitive identity, financial, medical, and legal documents. <br>
Mitigation: Advise users to redact unnecessary details, avoid uploading full SSNs, full bank or card numbers, tax returns, or legal records to ordinary cloud folders, and use stronger encryption, two-factor authentication, limited sharing, and a clear recovery plan for any document backups. <br>
