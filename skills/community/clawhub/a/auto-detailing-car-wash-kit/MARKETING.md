## Differentiators
1. **Ceramic coating specificity** — SiO2% disclosure requirement. The only skill that won't write "10-year ceramic" without a product citation. This is the claim that generates the most customer complaints and chargeback disputes in detailing.
2. **Water reclaim compliance** — Mobile detailers in CA, NV, AZ are legally required to contain or reclaim wash water. No other marketing tool knows this. We turn it into a trust differentiator.
3. **Paint correction staging** — 1-stage, 2-stage, 3-stage copy generated separately. Eliminates the #1 customer expectation gap in detailing (customer expects full defect removal, gets a 1-stage polish).
4. **Fleet outreach built in** — Dealer prep + fleet management is the highest-margin, most stable revenue stream in detailing. Generic AI doesn't write dealer pitch decks.
5. **Las Vegas market depth** — Desert heat + UV + casino/hotel luxury vehicles = ideal detailing market. Skill generates NV-specific content (extreme heat, SNWA water context, casino fleet contracts).
