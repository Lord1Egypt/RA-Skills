## Use Case: <br>
External sales and marketing teams use this skill to develop OOH advertising prospects, follow celebrity endorsement signals, prepare media proposals, and adapt sales scripts and case references for customer outreach. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing claims, prices, ROI estimates, availability, and scarcity claims may be outdated or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm claims, prices, expected results, point availability, and success case details before using them in customer-facing outreach or proposals. <br>
Risk: Prospecting workflows and follow-up scripts can create consent, anti-spam, or privacy issues when used with real leads. <br>
Mitigation: Get user approval before contacting people, follow applicable consent and anti-spam rules, and avoid storing unnecessary personal data in lead notes. <br>
