## Description: <br>
Manages a reading list by helping users add articles, track progress, organize notes, generate summaries, recommend related reading, and review reading statistics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yang1002378395-cmyk](https://clawhub.ai/user/yang1002378395-cmyk) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Readers, researchers, content creators, and teams use this skill to maintain a structured reading queue, update reading status, capture notes, summarize source material, and review reading activity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: URLs, titles, notes, progress, and reading habits may persist locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive research topics unless the local file location is acceptable, and preview delete, share, or export actions before confirming them. <br>
