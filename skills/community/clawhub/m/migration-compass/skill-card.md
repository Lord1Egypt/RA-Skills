## Description: <br>
Migration Compass helps developers and engineers plan incremental software migrations with ordered steps, validation gates, rollback points, and parallel-run strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jcools1977](https://clawhub.ai/user/jcools1977) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to plan library, framework, language, database, architecture, and major-version migrations before changing production systems. It produces a step-by-step route with validation checks, rollback points, risk areas, and parallel work opportunities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated migration plans can include dependency removals, route switching, database work, or production deployment steps that may affect running systems if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve each generated checklist before making changes, and validate rollback points and deployment gates against the actual system. <br>
Risk: The skill is a planning aid and may miss project-specific constraints or edge cases. <br>
Mitigation: Compare the plan against codebase evidence, operational metrics, tests, and team ownership boundaries before implementation. <br>
