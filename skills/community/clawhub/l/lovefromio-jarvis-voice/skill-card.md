## Description: <br>
Metallic AI voice persona with TTS and visual transcript styling. Speak responses aloud with a JARVIS-like robotic voice and display transcripts in purple italics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lovefromio](https://clawhub.ai/user/lovefromio) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and assistant users use this skill to add local spoken output, metallic voice processing, and styled transcript formatting to OpenClaw assistant responses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Assistant responses may be spoken aloud locally and expose sensitive information to nearby listeners. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in environments where spoken responses are appropriate, and avoid persistent voice rules in shared or sensitive settings. <br>
Risk: The referenced jarvis helper script is not included in the reviewed package. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the helper script or obtain it from a trusted source before making it executable or using it. <br>
