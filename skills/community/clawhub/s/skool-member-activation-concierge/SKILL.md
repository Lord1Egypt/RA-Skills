# Skool Member Activation Concierge

Use this skill when a Skool community needs early engagement, cleaner onboarding,
or a repeatable path from new member to useful outcome.

## Inputs

Collect or infer:

- community promise,
- new member types,
- first useful action,
- common blocker categories,
- available templates or lessons,
- founder/operator time available each week,
- no-paid-implementation boundary.

## Workflow

1. Define the first member action in one sentence.
2. Write a welcome comment template.
3. Write a "where are you now" poll.
4. Write a blocker poll.
5. Write a first-win prompt.
6. Create a 7-day concierge checklist for the operator.
7. Define escalation rules for free proof reviews and future interest-list requests.

## Output

Return:

- first action,
- welcome post or comment,
- poll copy,
- first-win prompt,
- 7-day concierge checklist,
- trust escalation rules,
- activation metrics.

Do not automate private DMs or scrape member data. Keep outreach consent-first
and based on visible member posts or explicit replies.

## Examples

Good public-safe inputs:

- "Write a welcome comment for members who want to audit one repeated task."
- "Create a blocker poll using only visible replies and aggregate counts."

Avoid inputs that require member lists, DMs, hidden posts, paid lessons,
credentials, private exports, or screenshots with personal/account details.
Use visible posts, explicit replies, synthetic samples, or aggregate counts.

## Guardrails

- Do not scrape private communities, member lists, DMs, paid lessons, or hidden
  pages.
- Do not request, store, transform, or paste credentials, API keys, session
  cookies, payment data, private exports, or account recovery data.
- Do not promise income, growth, conversion, rank, time saved, health,
  financial, legal, or education outcomes.
- Do not automate private DMs or off-platform outreach.
- Keep proof reviews free, consent-first, and based on user-submitted or
  operator-owned artifacts.
