# Ungating Requirements Guide

Category-specific documentation requirements, supplier invoice formatting standards, and approval criteria for Amazon's major gated categories.

---

## Universal Requirements

These requirements apply to all gated category applications regardless of the specific category.

### Seller Account Prerequisites

- **Professional selling plan**: Individual accounts cannot apply for gated categories
- **Account standing**: No active suspensions, warnings, or policy violations
- **Order Defect Rate**: Below 1% (below 0.5% strongly recommended)
- **Valid tracking rate**: Above 95%
- **Late shipment rate**: Below 4%
- **Account age**: Minimum 3 months with sales history preferred; some categories require 6+ months

### Invoice Formatting Standards

1. **Format**: PDF only with selectable text (apply OCR if scanned)
2. **Buyer name**: Must exactly match the legal entity in Seller Central (not DBA or personal name)
3. **Supplier info**: Full legal name, address, phone, and website
4. **Date**: Within 180 days of submission (within 90 days is strongest)
5. **Products**: Descriptions specific enough to match the ASIN catalog (brand, name, size, UPC)
6. **Quantity**: Minimum 10 units per ASIN
7. **Integrity**: Unaltered originals only -- editing artifacts cause rejection and investigation

### Product Photography Standards

White background, even lighting, minimum 1000x1000px resolution, product filling 80%+ of frame, all label text legible at 100% zoom, no props or watermarks. Capture front, back, and all regulatory panels.

## Category-Specific Requirements

### Grocery & Gourmet Food

**Gate type**: Category-level

**Required documents**:
- Wholesale invoices from recognized food distributors (e.g., KeHE, UNFI, DPI Specialty Foods)
- Minimum 10 units per ASIN
- Product images showing: front label, nutrition facts panel, ingredients list, UPC barcode, lot number, best-by or expiration date

**Special requirements**:
- Products must have a remaining shelf life of at least 90 days at time of FBA inbound shipment
- Expiration dates must be visible and legible in photographs
- No home-prepared, repackaged, or unlabeled food items
- FDA facility registration may be requested for certain food subcategories

**Approval timeline**: Often auto-approved within hours if documentation is clean. Manual review may take 1-3 business days.

---

### Topicals (Beauty & Personal Care subcategory)

**Gate type**: Subcategory-level

**Required documents**:
- Wholesale invoices showing exact product variants (size, formula)
- Product images of all label panels including drug facts (if OTC), ingredients, directions for use, and manufacturer information
- Certificate of Analysis (COA) from the product manufacturer or an ISO 17025 accredited lab

**Special requirements**:
- Products classified as OTC drugs (sunscreens, acne treatments, anti-dandruff shampoos) require FDA drug listing numbers
- COA must reference the specific product by name or UPC and test for relevant safety parameters (heavy metals, microbial limits)
- No products with unapproved drug claims
- Packaging must be tamper-evident

**Approval timeline**: Manual review, typically 2-5 business days.

---

### Toys & Games (Holiday season gating)

**Gate type**: Seasonal category-level (typically enforced October through January)

**Required documents**:
- Wholesale invoices from recognized toy distributors
- Minimum 10 units per ASIN
- CPC (Children's Product Certificate) for products intended for children 12 and under
- CPSIA test reports from a CPSC-accepted laboratory

**Special requirements**:
- Sellers must meet elevated performance thresholds during the holiday gate period: ODR below 0.5%, late shipment below 2%
- Account must have been active and selling for at least 90 days prior to the gating period
- Prior year holiday selling history is strongly favorable
- Products must comply with ASTM F963 toy safety standard

**Approval timeline**: Applications should be submitted well before October. During the gating period, review times increase to 3-7 business days.

---

### Watches

**Gate type**: Category-level

**Required documents**:
- Invoices from authorized watch distributors or directly from the brand/manufacturer
- Minimum 10 units per ASIN
- Brand authorization letter (required for most recognizable watch brands)
- Product images showing the watch face, case back with serial number, and branded packaging

**Special requirements**:
- High-counterfeit-risk category; Amazon's verification is more rigorous
- Suppliers must be verifiable through public directories or brand websites
- Serial numbers in photographs may be cross-referenced against manufacturer databases
- Luxury brands (Rolex, Omega, TAG Heuer) may have additional brand-registry-level gates

**Approval timeline**: Manual review, 3-7 business days. Luxury brands may take longer.

---

### Dietary Supplements

**Gate type**: Subcategory-level under Health & Household

**Required documents**:
- Wholesale invoices from supplement distributors or manufacturers
- Product images including supplement facts panel, ingredient list, manufacturer information, and lot/expiration markings
- COA from an ISO 17025 accredited or FDA-registered testing laboratory
- GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) certification for the manufacturing facility

**Special requirements**:
- Products must comply with DSHEA (Dietary Supplement Health and Education Act)
- No products with drug claims or unapproved ingredients
- Labels must include the required FDA disclaimer: "These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA..."
- Products manufactured in facilities not following GMP regulations will be rejected

**Approval timeline**: Manual review, 3-7 business days.

---

## Supplier Verification

Amazon may independently verify suppliers. Ensure the phone number on the invoice reaches the supplier's business, their website is active, and information is consistent across the invoice, website, and public directories. Only manufacturers, authorized distributors, and licensed wholesalers are accepted -- retail stores, Amazon sellers, and eBay sellers are not valid suppliers.
