## Description: <br>
Business Plan Cn helps agents generate Chinese-language business plan materials, including full business plans, lean canvases, SWOT analysis, financial projections, market analysis, and elevator pitches. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain3](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and business users can use this skill to draft structured Chinese-language business planning outputs for startup and project planning workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release includes an unrelated content-writing script that saves entered topics to local plaintext history. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer scripts/bp.sh for business-plan generation and avoid entering confidential startup ideas, business plans, or draft text into scripts/script.sh unless local plaintext history is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated financial projections and business-plan sections are template-based estimates rather than validated business advice. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated outputs and validate financial, market, and operating assumptions before using them for decisions or external sharing. <br>
