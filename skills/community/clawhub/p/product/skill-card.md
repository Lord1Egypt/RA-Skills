## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Validation tactics can mislead customers if unfinished, manual, or experimental features are presented as complete. <br>
Mitigation: Be transparent about prototype status and manually operated workflows when running validation tests. <br>
Risk: Product experiments and marketplace launches can involve privacy, advertising, platform, or consumer-protection obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Check the applicable rules before launch and avoid collecting sensitive information during tests unless there is a clear, compliant need. <br>
Risk: Physical-product guidance may not cover every regulatory requirement for a specific product category or market. <br>
Mitigation: Use the compliance guidance as a planning aid and confirm certification, labeling, safety, and import requirements with qualified reviewers before selling. <br>
