## Use Case: <br>
People and agents use this skill to draft or prepare messages across email, chat, and social platforms while matching tone, choosing the right channel, and escalating high-stakes communication for human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Messages can create commitments, approvals, or relationship consequences if sent without review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval for financial, legal, investor, client escalation, emotional, first-contact, and commitment-bearing messages. <br>
Risk: Tone matching can mislead recipients or over-automate personal communication where disclosure is expected. <br>
Mitigation: Use only the message history needed for context, preserve the user's style without deceptive impersonation, and disclose automation where appropriate. <br>
Risk: Wrong recipient, channel, or timing can expose information or escalate conflict. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recipient and channel before sending, choose the communication channel by urgency and formality, and schedule non-urgent late-night messages for appropriate hours. <br>
