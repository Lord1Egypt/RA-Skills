## Description: <br>
End-to-end workflow for planning, producing, distributing, and repurposing podcast content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, solo podcasters, Web3 project leads, and community builders use this skill to plan episodes, prepare outlines and guest questions, create show notes, publish to podcast platforms, repurpose episodes into social content, and plan audience growth. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for a broad range of podcast-related prompts and produce publishing or marketing guidance when the user intended a narrower task. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the requested module and target platform before applying checklists, publishing steps, or promotional recommendations. <br>
Risk: The workflow discusses uploading, platform publishing, social posting, and account-specific actions that could affect public content or creator accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Keep uploads, account logins, platform posting, and credential entry under direct user control; treat generated titles, descriptions, and publishing steps as drafts for review. <br>
Risk: Podcast assets such as show notes, guest briefs, and social posts may contain incorrect, outdated, or sensitive information if source material is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content against episode recordings, guest-approved details, and platform requirements before publishing. <br>
