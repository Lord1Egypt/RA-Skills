## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese and English email draft generation, reply suggestions, template-based writing, tone adjustment, and basic inbox classification guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers can use this skill to draft leave, business, thank-you, job application, meeting, report, apology, and payment reminder emails. It helps users generate editable subject lines and message bodies with placeholders rather than sending mail automatically. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated email drafts may contain placeholders, incomplete context, or wording that is inappropriate for the recipient. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit every draft before sending it. <br>
Risk: Sensitive email content may be retained in local JSON files in the skill directory. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving sensitive email content unless local storage is acceptable for the user and environment. <br>
