## Description: <br>
Creates professional Hebrew and English prompts for AI image and video tools, with realistic cinematic defaults and guidance for Jewish and Torah-related content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jojobok](https://clawhub.ai/user/jojobok) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, educators, and social media teams use this skill to draft copy-ready Hebrew prompts for AI image and video generation, especially for realistic Jewish, Torah, and biblical-era scenes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's Hebrew language and cinematic realism defaults may not match every user's desired tone, language, or target model. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated prompt before use and ask for English, a different tone, or a different visual style when needed. <br>
Risk: Prompts about biblical or historical scenes can imply unsupported historical details or culturally sensitive portrayals. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts for accuracy and religious or cultural sensitivity, especially before public release. <br>
