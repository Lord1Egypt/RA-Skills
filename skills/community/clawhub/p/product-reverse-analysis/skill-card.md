## Description: <br>
Analyzes product interface screenshots to infer business flows, likely technical architecture, and design rationale. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yz6214589-hash](https://clawhub.ai/user/yz6214589-hash) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, designers, researchers, and developers use this skill to analyze product screenshots, understand interface structure, infer user flows, and produce a structured product reverse-analysis report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Screenshots may contain passwords, API keys, customer data, or confidential business information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive content before use and review the generated Markdown report before sharing or storing it. <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to infer product architecture and business logic from screenshots, so conclusions may be speculative. <br>
Mitigation: Treat inferred flows, architecture, and risks as analysis hypotheses and validate them against product documentation or stakeholder review. <br>
