## Description: <br>
Score and qualify leads using customizable fit, intent, and engagement criteria to prioritize the sales pipeline. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales reps, SDRs, and founders use this skill to score individual or batched leads, qualify them with BANT and MEDDIC, and choose the next sales action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lead scores and qualification guidance may be unreliable when user-provided lead data or scoring criteria are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review scores against sales policy and verify important account decisions before outreach, nurture routing, or disqualification. <br>
Risk: Referenced external context packs and optional install commands are separate resources outside this bundle's clean scan. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve external resources separately before installing or using them. <br>
