## Description: <br>
Smart Daily Assistant helps users manage daily personal tasks through chat, including reminders, notes, briefings, message drafts, quick replies, translations, planning, habits, contacts, expenses, saved links, and local-only data handling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a local personal assistant for everyday organization, lightweight writing help, planning, and tracking. It is suited to users who want reminders, notes, messages, contacts, expenses, habits, and briefings handled through conversational commands. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal assistant data, including reminders, notes, contacts, expenses, and related records, is saved locally under ~/.openclaw/smart-daily-assistant/. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing highly sensitive personal, contact, or financial information, and review or delete the local data folder when retained data is no longer needed. <br>
