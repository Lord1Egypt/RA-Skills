# X Growth Review

Use this to audit an account, plan, or week of activity.

## Review Areas

### Positioning

- Is the target audience specific?
- Does the account repeat recognizable topics?
- Is the follow promise obvious?

### Content

- Does the mix create dwell, replies, reposts, and follows?
- Is there enough proof of work?
- Are posts specific or generic?
- Are opinions useful, not just angry?

### Replies

- Is the user replying under the right accounts?
- Do replies add evidence, frameworks, or sharp questions?
- Do replies create profile-click curiosity?

### Profile

- Does bio match content?
- Is the pinned post strong?
- Would a stranger know why to follow?

### Analytics

- Are winners logged?
- Are winners turned into series?
- Are replies turned into standalone posts?
- Are weak formats stopped?

## Output

Return:

- **Verdict**
- **Scores**: positioning, content, replies, profile, analytics
- **Biggest bottleneck**
- **Prioritized fixes**
- **Next 7 days**
- **3 experiments**

## Operating Principle

Treat the public X algorithm as a signal map, not a cheat code. Optimize for posts and replies that earn useful positive actions (dwell, replies, reposts, shares, profile clicks, follows) while avoiding negative actions (not interested, mute, block, report, fast skips).
