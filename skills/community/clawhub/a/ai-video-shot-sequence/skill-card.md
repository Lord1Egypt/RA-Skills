## Description: <br>
Designs coherent AI video shot sequences from scripts or scene descriptions, organizing shots into narrative arcs with rhythm, emotion, coverage, movement, sound cues, and continuity checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[beermanzz](https://clawhub.ai/user/beermanzz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creative teams, filmmakers, and agents use this skill to turn a script or scene description into a structured shot sequence for AI video planning, including sequence templates, rhythm curves, shot movement, sound-design notes, and continuity checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project inputs may include confidential scripts, reference images, character sheets, production details, or licensed materials. <br>
Mitigation: Provide those materials only when allowed by workspace policy and applicable license terms. <br>
Risk: Shot-sequence guidance may require creative and continuity review before production use. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed shot order, axis continuity, asset anchors, light direction, and sound-design notes before using the plan in an AI video workflow. <br>
