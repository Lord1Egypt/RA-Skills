Risk: Telecom prices and availability may become outdated because recommendations use built-in pricing data. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current roaming, eSIM, local SIM, and WiFi rental pricing with carriers or providers before purchase. <br>
Risk: Advice may not cover every destination or traveler constraint. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as planning guidance and verify destination-specific coverage, device compatibility, and calling needs before travel. <br>
