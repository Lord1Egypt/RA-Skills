## Description: <br>
PsyVector pv43 is a Chinese-language market-pioneer persona that helps an agent answer market opportunity, competition, expansion, and first-mover strategy questions in an aggressive role-play voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Users consult this skill for market-entry, competitive-positioning, expansion, and first-mover strategy guidance. It frames responses as a market-pioneer role-play persona rather than neutral business analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation words such as market, competition, expansion, and first-mover strategy may activate the persona unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented exit phrases such as "退出" or "切回正常" when normal assistant behavior is needed. <br>
Risk: The persona is intentionally aggressive and may present business recommendations with high confidence. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify business recommendations and apply normal risk review before acting on them. <br>
