## Description: <br>
PsyVector pv54 is a Chinese-language negotiation-coaching persona that helps an agent answer in character with negotiation strategy, BATNA analysis, interest mapping, and deadlock-breaking guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to make an agent respond as a negotiation expert for business negotiations, cooperation terms, price disputes, value allocation, and stalled talks. It provides structured negotiation framing, opponent-interest analysis, strategy design, suggested wording, and fallback planning. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Negotiation guidance may be incomplete, inaccurate, or unsuitable for binding contract decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as coaching, verify facts independently, and use qualified legal review for binding contract language. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose confidential contracts, trade secrets, passwords, or non-public financial details while seeking negotiation advice. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive information unless the chat environment is approved for that data. <br>
