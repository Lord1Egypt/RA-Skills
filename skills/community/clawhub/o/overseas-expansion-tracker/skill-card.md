## Use Case: <br>
Business analysts, investment researchers, and corporate strategy teams use this skill to produce evidence-based Chinese-language reports on a company's overseas subsidiaries, factories, capacity shifts, supply-chain relocation, and related geopolitical or commercial risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill produces investment and strategy analysis that could be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as analytical research support and have qualified reviewers validate conclusions before using them for investment or business decisions. <br>
Risk: Public-source company expansion data may be incomplete, stale, or based on unverified reporting. <br>
Mitigation: Verify cited sources, prefer official and high-quality publications, and exclude claims that lack clear dates, locations, concrete actions, and traceable sources. <br>
