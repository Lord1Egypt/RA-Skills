## Description: <br>
AI Product Manager helps product teams draft PRDs, user stories, requirements analyses, competitor research, roadmaps, analytics reviews, and A/B testing plans for consumer, B2B, SaaS, and AI products. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
