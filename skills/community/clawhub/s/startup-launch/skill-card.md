## Description: <br>
Startup Launch Day Playbook gives founders and launch teams an hour-by-hour launch-day checklist covering timezone timing, channel sequencing, crisis management, and post-launch momentum. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders and launch teams use this skill to plan and execute a coordinated product launch from T-12 hours through the 72-hour post-launch sprint. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Launch recommendations may be applied too broadly to live public communications, analytics, payments, or incident response. <br>
Mitigation: Review each checklist item against the team's current launch plan, account permissions, and incident procedures before acting. <br>
Risk: The playbook gives business guidance and does not access accounts or perform launch actions automatically. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a human-reviewed planning aid; keep execution in existing approved tools and workflows. <br>
