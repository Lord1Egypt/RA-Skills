## Description: <br>
AI content brief, script, and outline generator for video, image, and audio production that provides strategic guidance on workflow design, model selection, parameter optimization, and visual consistency. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dai-shuo](https://clawhub.ai/user/dai-shuo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, creators, and agents use this skill to plan multi-step AI media workflows, choose models, optimize parameters, maintain visual consistency, and prepare briefs, scripts, and production outlines before using IMA Studio execution skills. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Model-selection guidance may steer sensitive real-person or sexualized image tasks toward providers perceived as more permissive. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review of sensitive-content workflows and apply the selected provider's safety policy instead of using provider choice to bypass restrictions. <br>
Risk: The skill can influence downstream image, video, and audio generation choices even though it does not call APIs directly. <br>
Mitigation: Review model, parameter, and workflow recommendations before execution, especially for real people, celebrities, children, sexual content, and other restricted content. <br>
