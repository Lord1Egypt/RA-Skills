# UGC Hook Library

40+ proven hook frameworks for ecommerce UGC content. Organized by type. Fill in the [brackets].

---

## Problem Hooks
Draw the viewer in by naming their pain point first.

1. "The reason your [problem] isn't getting better"
2. "Stop wasting money on [bad solution] — here's what actually works"
3. "If you've tried everything for [problem] and nothing works, watch this"
4. "Why [common product category] almost never actually solves [problem]"
5. "I spent [$X] on [product type] that didn't work before finding this"
6. "The [problem] mistake you're probably making right now"
7. "Nobody talks about why [common solution] actually makes [problem] worse"

---

## Result/Transformation Hooks
Lead with the outcome to create aspirational pull.

8. "POV: Your [problem] is completely gone after 2 weeks of this"
9. "I went from [before state] to [after state] in [timeframe]"
10. "This [$X] product did what [$Y expensive alternative] couldn't"
11. "I've tried [number] [product type] — this is the only one that [result]"
12. "Before and after using [product] every day for [timeframe]"
13. "The [result] glow-up I didn't expect from a $[price] product"

---

## Curiosity/Surprise Hooks
Create a pattern interrupt or open loop.

14. "Wait — did you know [counterintuitive fact about your category]?"
15. "Nobody tells you [unexpected benefit of your product]"
16. "I can't believe this [product] is only $[price]"
17. "The [adjective] truth about [product category]"
18. "This thing sounds gimmicky but I tested it and..."
19. "Okay I was skeptical but [product] just [result]"

---

## Social Proof Hooks
Leverage existing trust signals.

20. "[Number] people use [product] every month — here's why"
21. "My [relation: mom/sister/friend] got me into [product] and now I'm obsessed"
22. "This has [X] 5-star reviews and I finally understand why"
23. "I bought this because everyone kept talking about it — verdict:"

---

## POV Hooks
Drop the viewer into a relatable moment.

24. "POV: You finally found [product] that doesn't [common failure mode]"
25. "POV: You're about to [positive experience] for the first time"
26. "POV: [relatable situation] and you're using [product]"
27. "Me, getting ready with [product] in the routine"

---

## Question Hooks
Prompt the viewer to self-identify.

28. "Are you using [product type] wrong?"
29. "Have you ever wondered why [problem] comes back even when you [common solution]?"
30. "What's your morning routine missing? Here's mine:"
31. "Do you actually know what's in your [product category]?"

---

## Comparison/Contrast Hooks
Create value through contrast.

32. "[Expensive alternative] vs. this $[price] option — let's be honest"
33. "What I used to spend $[X] on vs. what I use now"
34. "I thought all [product category] were the same until I tried this"
35. "This vs. [generic category]: the difference is [specific attribute]"

---

## Hook + Transition Templates

Pair with these natural transitions after the hook:
- "Let me show you why..."
- "Here's what changed for me..."
- "So I tested it for [X] days and here's what happened:"
- "The secret is actually really simple:"
- "What you actually need is [product] because..."
