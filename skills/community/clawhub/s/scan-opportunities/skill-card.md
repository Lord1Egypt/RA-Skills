## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External DeFi researchers and developers use this skill to shortlist Uniswap liquidity-pool, arbitrage, and high-yield pool opportunities for further verification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may surface incorrect, stale, or misleading APY, liquidity, token-risk, or arbitrage-feasibility information. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as DeFi research, verify APY, liquidity, token risk, and execution feasibility independently, and do not treat the output as financial advice. <br>
Risk: High-yield pools and arbitrage leads can carry elevated impermanent-loss, low-liquidity, new-token, and time-sensitivity risks. <br>
Mitigation: Review the reported risk rating, TVL, pool age, volume trend, and current market conditions before taking any action. <br>
Risk: Sensitive wallet details could be exposed if shared with an untrusted agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing private keys, seed phrases, credentials, or sensitive wallet details unless the broader agent environment is trusted. <br>
