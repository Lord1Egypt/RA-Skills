## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to structure China mainland legal scenario analysis, identify issues, evidence needs, procedural risks, and possible action paths. It provides legal information and practical analysis support, not formal legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China (mainland legal contexts) <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake generated legal analysis for formal legal advice or a guaranteed case outcome. <br>
Mitigation: State that outputs are legal information and conditional analysis only, and direct high-risk or fact-dependent matters to a licensed lawyer or appropriate authority. <br>
Risk: Incomplete facts, missing evidence, or outdated legal sources can lead to misleading conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Collect key missing facts, preserve original evidence, verify current authoritative legal sources when available, and qualify conclusions where facts or law are uncertain. <br>
Risk: Legal scenarios can involve sensitive personal, business, medical, or family information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to redact unnecessary identifiers and provide only dispute-relevant text or facts needed for analysis. <br>
