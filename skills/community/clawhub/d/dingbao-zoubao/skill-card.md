## Description: <br>
每天定时生成综合、财经、科技、AI、股市等主题的个性化早报，并可推送到飞书或手动返回给用户。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Users who want recurring Chinese news briefings use this skill to generate concise daily updates across general, finance, technology, AI, and stock-market topics. It supports manual requests and scheduled delivery to a configured Feishu chat. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Scheduled briefings could be posted to the wrong Feishu chat or at an unintended time. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target chat, posting schedule, and disable path before enabling scheduled delivery. <br>
Risk: Deduplication history or personalization settings may retain briefing-related data. <br>
Mitigation: Review what data is stored and retained before enabling deduplication or personalized briefings. <br>
