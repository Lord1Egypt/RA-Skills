## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated email campaigns may omit consent, unsubscribe, anti-spam, or deliverability safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Review campaigns against applicable marketing, consent, unsubscribe, and deliverability requirements before sending. <br>
Risk: Conversion-focused copy may overstate urgency, scarcity, claims, or expected outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Check subject lines, calls to action, offers, and performance claims for accuracy and brand compliance before publication. <br>
