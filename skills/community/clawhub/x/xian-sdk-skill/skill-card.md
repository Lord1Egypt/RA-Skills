## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Transaction, approval, swap, send_tx, and submit_contract examples can affect live wallets or networks when executed against production infrastructure. <br>
Mitigation: Use a local node or testnet first, verify addresses and amounts, protect private keys and mnemonics, and require explicit confirmation before broadcasting. <br>
