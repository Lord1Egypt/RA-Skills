## Description: <br>
AI Language Tutor helps learners practice English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, and Chinese with pronunciation feedback, grammar explanations, scenario dialogues, exam preparation, vocabulary work, and personalized study plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners use this skill as a multilingual tutor for guided practice, pronunciation correction, grammar review, situational dialogue, exam preparation, vocabulary building, and study planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill content and examples are primarily written in Chinese, so users may receive Chinese interface text unless they specify another language. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the preferred interface language at the start of the tutoring session or include it in the user's request. <br>
Risk: Broad language-learning requests may activate this prompt-style tutor even when a user intended a narrower answer. <br>
Mitigation: Review the active skill context and disable the skill when general language questions should not use tutoring behavior. <br>
