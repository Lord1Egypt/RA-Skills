## Description: <br>
Barefoot Doctor provides Chinese and traditional Chinese medicine reference guidance for symptom triage, disease-category questions, herbs, acupoints, and emergency first-aid prompts based on the Barefoot Doctor Handbook. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnsmithfan](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnsmithfan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill as an educational Chinese/TCM medical reference assistant for symptom-oriented questions, basic disease-category lookup, herb and acupoint reference, and urgent-care escalation prompts. It is not a substitute for professional diagnosis, medication decisions, emergency care, pregnancy care, pediatric care, or clinician advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat educational medical-reference output as a diagnosis or clinical treatment plan. <br>
Mitigation: Present guidance as educational reference only and direct users to professional medical care for diagnosis, treatment, medication decisions, persistent symptoms, or worsening symptoms. <br>
Risk: Emergency, severe, pregnancy, pediatric, or medication-related situations may require immediate professional care. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate urgent or high-risk scenarios to emergency services or qualified clinicians and avoid substituting the skill's output for clinician judgment. <br>
Risk: Herb, acupoint, and first-aid suggestions may be unsafe when applied without context or professional supervision. <br>
Mitigation: Keep recommendations conservative, include warnings and disclaimers, and advise consultation with qualified clinicians or pharmacists before use. <br>
