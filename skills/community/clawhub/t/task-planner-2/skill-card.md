## Description: <br>
Task Planner helps agents turn vague, multi-step goals into clear plans, confirm the plan with the user, and guide execution step by step. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiangrb](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiangrb) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill when a goal needs planning, task decomposition, risk identification, user confirmation, and guided execution across several dependent steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Planning guidance can be incomplete or misleading for coding or operational work if followed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the proposed plan before execution and keep normal approval boundaries for file edits, shell commands, and other project-changing actions. <br>
