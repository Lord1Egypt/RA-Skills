## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automatic routing may invoke the skill for generic writing requests rather than intentional video-script generation. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target format, platform, and content goal before relying on generated scripts. <br>
Risk: Package metadata declares a Node.js requirement while the bundled helper tool is a Python script. <br>
Mitigation: Check runtime prerequisites during installation and verify that both agent-side skill use and any helper execution work in the deployment environment. <br>
Risk: Generated scripts can include persuasive claims, product messaging, or calls to action that may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a regulated campaign. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims, compliance language, and brand requirements before production or publication. <br>
