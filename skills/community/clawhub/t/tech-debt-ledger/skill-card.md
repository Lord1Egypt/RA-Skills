## Description: <br>
Tracks technical debt as financial debt with principal, interest rates, minimum payments, compound growth, maturity dates, and payoff strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineering leads, and planning teams use this skill to identify, value, prioritize, and plan repayment of technical debt using a financial ledger model. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Risk: The security scan reports suspicious behavior in the bundle, including an autoreview helper that may bypass sandbox or approval controls and send diffs to fallback AI reviewers. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in trusted maintainer environments; for private or untrusted code, run review helpers without yolo mode, disable fallback reviewers when diffs must stay local, and restrict moderation or PR workflows to trusted staff credentials. <br>
