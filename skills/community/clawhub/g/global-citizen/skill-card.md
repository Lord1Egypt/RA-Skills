## Description: <br>
Provides a structured overseas identity planning framework for evaluating golden visas, citizenship by investment, digital nomad visas, residency-by-investment, tax residency, and broker risks with live verification of current requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and advisors use this skill to structure global mobility and overseas identity planning decisions, compare candidate pathways, verify current government requirements, and identify broker or tax-risk issues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security guidance warns that privileged workflows may act with local credentials when explicitly invoked. <br>
Mitigation: Install only for expected workflows and review moderation or autoreview helper behavior before use. <br>
Risk: Immigration requirements, costs, and timelines can become stale or misleading if static reference material is treated as current data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify concrete program details against official government sources at response time and label uncertain data as unverified. <br>
