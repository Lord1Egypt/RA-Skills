## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes Tolstoy-specific guidance that can lead an agent to read local business context files before generating content. <br>
Mitigation: Before use, require the agent to list and confirm any local files it will read, and provide only the scoped files needed for the task. <br>
Risk: The skill describes Webflow publishing actions that could publish generated SEO content publicly. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped Webflow credentials, create drafts or previews first, and require human review before publishing any item. <br>
