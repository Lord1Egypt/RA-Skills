## Description: <br>
Apply GB/T 47041-2026, the Chinese national standard for service quality and assessment of rehabilitation institutions for children with autism. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[julescore](https://clawhub.ai/user/julescore) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, institution operators, evaluators, and compliance reviewers use this skill to interpret GB/T 47041-2026 and produce checklists, evidence matrices, gap analyses, scoring sheets, evaluation report outlines, and improvement recommendations for autism rehabilitation institution service quality. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include identifiable child, family, or patient information while preparing compliance materials. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering identifiable records unless the user controls how the agent stores and shares that data. <br>
Risk: Users may treat generated compliance outputs as official legal, medical, licensing, accreditation, or government certification advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as compliance assistance and verify official filings, accreditation, disputes, and high-stakes decisions against the source standard and competent authority. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on bundled standard text and guidance supplied by a third-party publisher. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if the publisher and bundled standard text are trusted, and verify exact clause wording before high-stakes use. <br>
