## Description: <br>
Generates Chinese-language daily stock briefings that summarize holdings, market news, technical levels, capital flows, and trading suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[awdasfdada](https://clawhub.ai/user/awdasfdada) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Investors and market watchers use this skill to produce concise daily portfolio briefings, single-stock summaries, and informational trading considerations from market, news, and technical-analysis inputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market summaries and trading suggestions may be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable as personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current prices, news, analyst claims, and technical levels with trusted financial sources before making investment decisions. <br>
