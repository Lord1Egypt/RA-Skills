## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel planners use this skill to prepare for language barriers with phrase frameworks, communication toolkits, cultural context notes, and emergency communication plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel, translation, emergency contact, and local requirement details may be outdated or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Verify critical details with official or trusted local sources before relying on the plan. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on informational travel guidance for medical, legal, financial, or safety-sensitive decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for preparation only and consult qualified professionals or official services for high-stakes situations. <br>
Risk: General cultural guidance may not fit every destination, community, or situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat cultural notes as starting points and confirm local norms with current, destination-specific sources. <br>
