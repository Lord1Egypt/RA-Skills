## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a freight-claim analyst, shipper, consignee, 3PL, or transportation counsel needs to draft a cargo loss, damage, or shortage claim under the Carmack Amendment for motor or rail shipments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freight-claim analysts, shippers, consignees, 3PL teams, and transportation counsel use this skill to prepare a draft U.S. interstate Carmack cargo claim packet for claims-team and counsel review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generated claim packet may contain incorrect legal framing, tariff analysis, deadlines, or claim amounts if inputs are incomplete or unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Have claims staff or counsel verify the legal framing, tariffs, deadlines, amounts, and final claim before submission. <br>
Risk: Users may provide privileged carrier materials or information they are not authorized to use. <br>
Mitigation: Use only materials the user is authorized to provide, and exclude privileged or unlawfully obtained carrier documents. <br>
Risk: The workflow can involve confidential shipment, vendor, customer, pricing, and cost details. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only information needed for the claim narrative and avoid exposing internal pricing, customer lists, or cost structure beyond what the claim requires. <br>
