## Description: <br>
Setting and maintaining personal boundaries across all relationships. Use when someone can't say no, feels constantly drained by others' demands, is being guilt-tripped, or needs to establish limits with family, work, friends, or partners. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and assisting agents use this skill to produce practical boundary-setting guidance, refusal scripts, and escalation language for family, work, friends, and partners. It is self-help guidance and should not replace professional support in legal, workplace retaliation, abuse, threat, or emergency situations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may apply boundary scripts in situations involving abuse, threats, workplace retaliation, or other safety-critical issues where self-help guidance is insufficient. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as self-help support and direct users to qualified therapists, attorneys, HR professionals, emergency services, or safety-planning resources when risk escalates. <br>
Risk: Boundary-setting norms and consequences vary across cultures, workplaces, legal systems, and relationship contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt scripts to the user's context and avoid presenting any single script as universally appropriate. <br>
