## Description: <br>
Helps agents optimize post-signup onboarding, user activation, first-run experience, and time-to-value. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External product, growth, and lifecycle teams use this skill to audit post-signup onboarding, define activation moments, design first-run flows, and plan onboarding experiments. <br>
