## Description: <br>
Aggregates domestic and international social, technology, and military news by searching, filtering, and organizing key points. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[httencn](https://clawhub.ai/user/httencn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and analysts can use this skill to gather and structure current social, technology, and military news summaries from named public sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Summaries rely on live public news sources and may reflect source-specific errors, omissions, or bias. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims against the linked original sources before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Military or fast-moving news can change quickly after a summary is generated. <br>
Mitigation: Refresh the search and check publication times when using the output for time-sensitive decisions. <br>
