## Description: <br>
Provides current weather conditions for cities, airport codes, or zip codes using wttr.in without requiring an API key. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ryansorr1](https://clawhub.ai/user/ryansorr1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and developers use this skill to fetch concise weather reports for a city, airport code, or zip code during planning, travel, support, or general information workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Location queries are sent to wttr.in. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using overly precise or sensitive locations when making weather requests. <br>
