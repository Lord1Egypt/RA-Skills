## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local beekeeping ordinances and registration requirements vary by municipality, state, province, and country. <br>
Mitigation: Verify local beekeeping rules, registration requirements, hive-count limits, setbacks, and HOA restrictions before buying equipment or bees. <br>
Risk: Varroa mite treatments have jurisdiction-specific approvals and safety requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm locally approved mite treatments and follow product labels, protective-equipment requirements, and honey-flow restrictions. <br>
Risk: Bee stings can cause severe allergic reactions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat allergy status as a gating safety question, seek medical testing when unsure, and direct severe reaction symptoms to emergency services. <br>
Risk: Reminder and state fields can become stale or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reminders as advisory and confirm inspection dates, treatment dates, hive status, and seasonal conditions before acting. <br>
