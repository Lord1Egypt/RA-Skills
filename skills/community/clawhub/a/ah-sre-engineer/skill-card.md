## Use Case: <br>
Developers, SREs, and platform teams use this skill to assess reliability posture, define SLIs and SLOs, manage error budgets, reduce toil, plan capacity, improve incident response, and design resilient automation for production services. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recommendations may affect production infrastructure, CI/CD workflows, load testing, failure testing, or chaos testing. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped credentials, require human approval for infrastructure or CI/CD changes, and run load, failure, or chaos tests only in approved environments with monitoring and rollback plans. <br>
Risk: Incorrect reliability analysis could lead to misleading SLOs, alerting, automation, or incident-response changes. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against service architecture, reliability requirements, current metrics, toil levels, and incident history before applying them. <br>
