# Example: 神奇的鸡蛋壳, Grade 2

This is only an example for one source story. Use it when the user specifically provides or references `神奇的鸡蛋壳`.

## Character Anchors

- 小青虫: bright green, round face, rosy cheeks, happy eyes, small yellow scarf.
- 小蚂蚁: warm brown, small red backpack, expressive antennae, cheerful smile.
- 小蝴蝶: pastel yellow wings with pink and blue dots, friendly face, playful posture.
- 鸡蛋壳: half white eggshell with pale blue speckles, magical but simple.

## Six-Page Storyboard

| Page | Scene | Caption |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Sunny morning by grass and river; the three friends find the eggshell in the grass and look surprised. | 草丛里，藏着一个神奇的蛋壳！ |
| 2 | They use the eggshell as a little boat crossing a clear river, paddling with tiny hands, water sparkling. | 蛋壳小船摇呀摇，带我们过河喽！ |
| 3 | On a big green meadow, they tie cloth and ropes to the eggshell to make a hot air balloon, excited and busy. | 加上布和绳，蛋壳飞船做好啦！ |
| 4 | The eggshell hot air balloon flies over a stream, green fields, flowers, and soft clouds; the friends wave happily. | 飞过小溪和花园，天空真热闹！ |
| 5 | Afternoon rainstorm; the friends flip the eggshell into a cozy shelter or umbrella, safe under it while rain falls outside. | 哗啦啦下雨了，蛋壳伞来帮忙！ |
| 6 | Night after rain; stars twinkle, and the friends sleep in the eggshell bed with leaves as blankets. | 星星眨眼睛，大家甜甜睡着了。 |

Recommended image setup:

- Six panels in a clean 2x3 grid for quick preview.
- One full-page image per scene for printable pages.
- Soft watercolor and colored pencil texture.
- Cream caption area at the bottom of each page or panel.
