## Description: <br>
Dld Product Search helps agents search and analyze 1688 wholesale products using LinkFox/DianLeiDa product-search data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[linkfox-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/linkfox-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, sourcing professionals, and agents use this skill to find 1688 products, compare wholesale and dropship pricing, evaluate supplier signals, and prepare product-search result summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms, sourcing criteria, and feedback may be sent to LinkFox services. <br>
Mitigation: Review queries before running sensitive sourcing research and protect the LINKFOXAGENT_API_KEY. <br>
Risk: Product and supplier decisions depend on external 1688 and DianLeiDa data returned by the service. <br>
Mitigation: Verify product listings, supplier details, and purchasing terms before acting on sourcing results. <br>
