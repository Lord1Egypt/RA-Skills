## Description: <br>
Coordinates multi-agent editorial production by loading audience and channel strategies, assigning specialist roles, enforcing quality boundaries, and staging review-ready publication files. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chaoyang78](https://clawhub.ai/user/chaoyang78) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content leads and editorial operators use this skill to coordinate article planning, drafting, visual production, review, and Obsidian staging for specific audience and publication channel combinations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow may stage files in the wrong Obsidian location or overwrite existing publication assets if paths and filenames are not confirmed. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the exact vault path, target folder, filenames, and overwrite policy before allowing file writes. <br>
Risk: Review-ready content could be mistaken for approved content and published before final human review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep publishing as a separate manual approval step after the staged article and embedded assets have been reviewed. <br>
