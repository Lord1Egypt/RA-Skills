## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pre-order plans can damage customer trust if ship dates, refund policies, or delay communications are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Use specific ship dates or tight ranges, publish a clear refund policy, add a fulfillment buffer, and prepare delay messages before launch. <br>
Risk: Early-bird incentives can reduce margin if discounts, gifts, packaging, and shipping costs are not modeled before launch. <br>
Mitigation: Calculate incentive COGS and compare pre-order margin against standard launch margin before committing to the incentive tier. <br>
Risk: Platform pre-order rules and payment-capture requirements can vary by sales channel. <br>
Mitigation: Verify platform terms and payment timing before publishing listings, especially for marketplace and social-commerce channels. <br>
