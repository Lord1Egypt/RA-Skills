## Use Case: <br>
Developers, writers, and agents use this skill to localize Hungarian-facing text by applying Hungarian date, number, Forint currency, phone number, and address formatting conventions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Incorrect locale formatting could change the meaning of dates, amounts, phone numbers, or addresses in user-facing Hungarian content. <br>
Mitigation: Review formatted output against the intended Hungarian convention before publishing or using it in transactional content. <br>
