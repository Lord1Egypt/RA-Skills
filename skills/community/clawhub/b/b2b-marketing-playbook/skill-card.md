## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cold email, LinkedIn coordination, website-visit tracking, intent data, and engagement scoring can create privacy, anti-spam, opt-out, internal policy, or platform-terms risk. <br>
Mitigation: Review planned outreach and tracking workflows against applicable privacy laws, anti-spam rules, opt-out requirements, internal data policies, and platform terms before use. <br>
Risk: Marketing playbook recommendations and benchmark targets may be misapplied outside the user's market, customer segment, or sales motion. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the playbook as guidance, validate assumptions against current business data, and have a qualified marketing or compliance owner approve campaigns before launch. <br>
