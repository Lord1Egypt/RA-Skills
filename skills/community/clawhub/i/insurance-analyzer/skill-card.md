## Description: <br>
保险保单分析助手 helps users collect insurance policy and household details, analyze coverage completeness, insured amount adequacy, and cost reasonableness, and produce structured optimization suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to organize insurance policy details, evaluate coverage gaps, check benefit amounts and premium reasonableness, and receive non-product-specific optimization suggestions. It is intended for deliberate policy analysis and includes reminders to rely on policy contracts and consult qualified professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for sensitive health, family, income, debt, and insurance-policy details. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for deliberate policy analysis, provide the minimum necessary information, and redact identifiers such as policy numbers, ID numbers, addresses, phone numbers, claim numbers, and unrelated health or financial details. <br>
Risk: Insurance analysis may be incomplete or misleading if policy terms, exclusions, or personal circumstances are missing. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as reference analysis, verify against the policy contract, and consult an insurance professional for decisions. <br>
