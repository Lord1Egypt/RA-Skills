## Differentiation — The Compliance Moat

Every major competitor (PatientPop, Birdeye, Jasper, Copy.ai) generates healthcare marketing copy without:
1. HIPAA compliance in email subject lines
2. FTC testimonial disclaimers on before/after social posts
3. ACA/APTA ethics rules in ad copy
4. State board advertising restrictions (Nevada: NAC 634)
5. PI attorney referral materials (unique to chiropractic/PT — no general marketing tool covers this)

The PI referral one-pager is the differentiator no competitor touches. Chiropractors who build PI referral networks earn $1,200-$4,000 per case (lien-based). This skill generates the materials to build that network.
