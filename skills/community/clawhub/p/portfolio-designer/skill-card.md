## Description: <br>
组合设计师 helps an agent create educational portfolio allocation plans based on user risk profile, investment goals, time horizon, and a simplified Yale model framework. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lj22503](https://clawhub.ai/user/lj22503) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to draft educational asset allocation plans, fund category suggestions, and rebalancing rules for long-term portfolio design. It is intended to structure portfolio thinking, not to replace qualified financial, tax, or legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Portfolio allocations or ETF examples may be mistaken for personalized financial, tax, or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as educational examples and ask users to consider their jurisdiction, liquidity needs, fees, taxes, risk tolerance, and qualified adviser input before acting. <br>
Risk: A static allocation model may not fit a user's current constraints, local market access, or changing financial situation. <br>
Mitigation: Require users to review assumptions, adapt allocations to their circumstances, and reassess periodically before making investment decisions. <br>
