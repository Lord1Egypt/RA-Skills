## Description: <br>
语音触发一键直达深圳广电官方资讯页面，快速获取深圳本地最新新闻、民生热点与广电节目动态，无需手动输入网址。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, especially Shenzhen local news readers, use this skill to open the official Shenzhen Broadcast & TV information page by voice trigger instead of manually entering or searching for the URL. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill opens an external sztv.com.cn web page, so users are exposed to ordinary web-browsing risks from that destination site. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use the skill only if you are comfortable with the assistant opening that page when the trigger phrase is used, and review the destination page before relying on its content. <br>
