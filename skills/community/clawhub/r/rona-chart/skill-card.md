## Description: <br>
Decompose Return on Net Assets into component ratios. Use for asset efficiency analysis, capital structure review, and business model comparison. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[linuszz](https://clawhub.ai/user/linuszz) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Finance analysts, operators, and business reviewers use this skill to decompose Return on Net Assets into margin and asset turnover drivers, compare performance over time, and frame benchmarking discussions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Company financial inputs may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved data handling channels and avoid submitting confidential figures unless permitted. <br>
Risk: Incorrect source data, formulas, or benchmark assumptions can lead to misleading RONA conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify formulas, financial statements, and benchmark assumptions before using outputs for business decisions. <br>
