### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Host tuning commands, database ALTER SYSTEM statements, index operations, materialized-view changes, and load tests can affect production reliability or data safety. <br>
Mitigation: Use approval, backups, staging validation, rollback steps, and a maintenance window before running those examples on production systems. <br>
Risk: Telemetry and performance monitoring examples can collect operational data that may require privacy review. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize collected data and send telemetry only to an approved analytics destination. <br>
