## Description: <br>
Create Arabic or bilingual Arabic-English flashcards with RTL-safe formatting, pronunciation help, examples, quizzes, and study review loops. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abdullah944](https://clawhub.ai/user/abdullah944) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, educators, and study-tool users use this skill to turn Arabic vocabulary, phrases, grammar points, passages, or class notes into flashcards, Anki-style CSV, printable cards, or chat quizzes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided lesson material may contain private student details or other personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Anonymize names, school IDs, phone numbers, addresses, and family details before creating shareable flashcards. <br>
Risk: User-provided textbook or paid course content may not be authorized for reuse. <br>
Mitigation: Use allowed notes, public examples, or original practice material, and review generated cards before sharing. <br>
Risk: Arabic dialect or pronunciation labels can be wrong when the source is ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for dialect context or label uncertain entries as possible dialect instead of claiming certainty. <br>
