## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Enabled OpenClaw hooks can continue changing future prompt construction until disabled or removed. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated hook JavaScript before enabling it, and disable or remove hooks that no longer match the intended behavior. <br>
