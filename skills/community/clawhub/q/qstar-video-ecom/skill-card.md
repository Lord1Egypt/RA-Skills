## Description: <br>
One-click AI product video generator for Chinese e-commerce, upgraded with advanced creation modes for classic product videos, single-image fast starts, batch variants, and natural-language revisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, marketers, and agents use this skill to collect product details, choose Chinese e-commerce platform and script options, and request short-form product videos or revised variants from Qstar/Guanma services. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product descriptions, public product image URLs, platform-linked user IDs, and optional contact details are sent to an external Qstar/Guanma service. <br>
Mitigation: Do not submit confidential product images, private URLs, sensitive business details, or unnecessary contact information unless the user accepts sending them to that service. <br>
Risk: Generated e-commerce scripts may overstate benefits, guarantees, or claims for regulated or sensitive product categories. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated copy before publication, especially for food, cosmetics, supplements, baby products, elder-care products, and health or safety claims. <br>
Risk: Image-assisted generation requires image URLs that are publicly reachable by the generation backend. <br>
Mitigation: Use only intended public product images and avoid internal-only, private, or access-controlled URLs. <br>
