## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may fetch current market and ETF data and produce financial recommendations that could be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable for a specific portfolio. <br>
Mitigation: Use independent market data and human financial review before acting on any recommendation. <br>
Risk: The artifact references local analysis-log locations that could contain credentials, brokerage session data, private trading strategies, or sensitive portfolio details. <br>
Mitigation: Keep sensitive portfolio and credential data out of logs and review local files before sharing or publishing outputs. <br>
