### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can execute real crypto transactions and move wallet funds. <br>
Mitigation: Use only a dedicated testnet wallet or a wallet containing funds you are prepared to risk. <br>
Risk: Advertised pause, suggest-only, or dashboard controls may not be fully enforced by the code. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on those controls without review; add explicit transaction limits and verify enforcement before running. <br>
Risk: Mainnet use can expose funds to irreversible transaction, contract, and key-management failures. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid mainnet use without an audit, authenticated local APIs, transaction limits, and a safer signer such as a hardware wallet or restricted signer. <br>
