## Description: <br>
Uses AI tools such as Doubao, Kimi, and ChatGPT to research a question, organize the response, and save it as an Obsidian Markdown document. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Alphalpha7](https://clawhub.ai/user/Alphalpha7) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, writers, and developers use this skill to turn AI-assisted web research into structured Obsidian notes with frontmatter, headings, source attribution, and local vault placement. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research prompts may be submitted to third-party AI providers. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the selected provider and avoid submitting sensitive or proprietary questions unless that use is approved. <br>
Risk: Generated Markdown may contain inaccurate content or be saved to the wrong Obsidian vault path. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated note, source attribution, filename, and vault path before saving. <br>
