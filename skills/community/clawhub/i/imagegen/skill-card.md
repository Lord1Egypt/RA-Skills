## Description: <br>
Image Gen guides agents through generating, editing, prompting, validating, and saving raster image assets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zack-dev-cm](https://clawhub.ai/user/zack-dev-cm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and agents use this skill to prepare prompts and workflows for generating or editing bitmap assets such as photos, illustrations, product mockups, sprites, textures, UI mockups, and transparent cutouts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated or edited images can contain rights-sensitive content, misleading evidence, endorsements, trademarks, or brand marks. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated images before use and avoid or remove rights-sensitive content unless the user has confirmed appropriate rights and intent. <br>
Risk: Optional local background-removal helpers or CLI fallback can depend on tooling outside this text-only release. <br>
Mitigation: Use only trusted, approved local helpers or host tools, and confirm the output after post-processing. <br>
