## Description: <br>
Professional skincare advisor for skincare advice and product recommendations across sensitive skin care, anti-aging, acne control, moisturizing, sunscreen, and personalized routines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to receive consumer skincare guidance, ingredient explanations, routine suggestions, and tiered product recommendation criteria based on skin concerns, region, habits, and budget. It is intended for non-diagnostic skincare support and cites available product, ingredient, and paper references when making efficacy claims. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary personal details while answering profile questions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep profile answers optional, ask only for skincare-relevant factors, and avoid collecting more personal detail than needed for general recommendations. <br>
Risk: Skincare or product guidance may be mistaken for medical diagnosis or treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Present recommendations as consumer guidance, do not diagnose conditions, and direct severe acne, eczema, atopic dermatitis, pregnancy-related uncertainty, or adverse reactions to a dermatologist or physician. <br>
Risk: Product recommendations may overstate efficacy or reflect recommendation bias. <br>
Mitigation: Tie claims to cited ingredient, product, NMPA, official website, or research references, and clearly state when supporting data is unavailable. <br>
Risk: Ingredient sensitivity or contraindication risk may be missed. <br>
Mitigation: Advise patch testing for sensitizing ingredients and avoid retinol or RF beauty devices for pregnancy or breastfeeding unless a clinician confirms suitability. <br>
