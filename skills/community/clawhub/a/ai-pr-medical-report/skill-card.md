## Description: <br>
AI 医学报告解读助手 helps users upload medical test-report PDFs, interpret key indicators, search medical literature, and generate structured health explanation reports with optional visual output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zlszhonglongshen](https://clawhub.ai/user/zlszhonglongshen) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn medical test reports into understandable health summaries, abnormal-indicator explanations, literature-backed context, and optional share-ready education content. Outputs are informational and do not replace clinician diagnosis or treatment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medical reports can contain sensitive personal and health information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact names, IDs, hospital numbers, and other personal details before using the skill or sharing outputs. <br>
Risk: Generated explanations may be mistaken for medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational summaries and rely on qualified clinicians for diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up decisions. <br>
Risk: Optional publishing to WeChat or Xiaohongshu could expose private health information. <br>
Mitigation: Review, sanitize, and intentionally approve any generated post before publishing. <br>
