## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Review-gating or negative-review interception could create unfair or non-compliant review collection. <br>
Mitigation: Give all reviewers an equal public submission path, while separately routing dissatisfied customers to support. <br>
Risk: SMS and marketing touchpoints can create consent and unsubscribe compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Require documented opt-in, honor unsubscribe and STOP requests, and separate transactional messages from marketing. <br>
Risk: Post-purchase flows can expose or overuse customer and order data. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize customer data, avoid raw order or customer IDs in URLs, and use explicit rights terms before collecting or reusing customer-generated content. <br>
