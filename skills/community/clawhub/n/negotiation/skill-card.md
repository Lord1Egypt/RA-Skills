## Description: <br>
Tactical negotiation framework based on Chris Voss's "Never Split the Difference." Use when preparing for negotiations, during live negotiation scenarios, analyzing counterpart behavior, crafting responses to difficult conversations, handling objections, salary/contract negotiations, or when asked about negotiation techniques like mirroring, labeling, calibrated questions, or the Ackerman method. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and agents use this skill for lawful, consensual, good-faith negotiation coaching, including preparation, live response drafting, objection handling, and post-conversation analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Negotiation coaching can be misused as pressure or manipulation in sensitive or vulnerable contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for lawful, consensual, good-faith bargaining, and avoid abusive relationships, crises, medical or legal decisions, employment precarity, or conversations involving vulnerable people. <br>
Risk: The skill provides persuasive advice that may be inappropriate without human judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggested language before using it and adapt it to the relationship, context, applicable rules, and ethical constraints. <br>
