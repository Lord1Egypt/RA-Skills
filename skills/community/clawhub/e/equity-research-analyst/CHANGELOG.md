- Real-time market data queries for stocks, cryptocurrencies, forex, futures, and more
- Technical indicator analysis (RSI, MACD, moving averages, etc.)
- Financial statement data retrieval
- Analyst ratings and forecasts
- Economic calendar and event tracking
- News and sentiment analysis
