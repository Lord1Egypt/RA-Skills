## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and other agent users apply this skill when they want more structured analysis for complex tasks such as ambiguous requirements, architectural decisions, debugging sessions, or multi-step trade-off evaluation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may make an agent more verbose or reflective than needed for simple tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for complex or ambiguous work, and skip it when a terse single-step response is sufficient. <br>
Risk: Reflective reasoning guidance could encourage disclosure of private chain-of-thought. <br>
Mitigation: Agents should summarize reasoning and conclusions without exposing private chain-of-thought. <br>
