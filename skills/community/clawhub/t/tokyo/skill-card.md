Mitigation: Verify legal, medical, safety, price, and business decisions with current official sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Static city guidance can become outdated as neighborhoods, services, policies, and seasonal conditions change. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for orientation and planning, then confirm current schedules, prices, closures, and local rules when making specific plans. <br>
