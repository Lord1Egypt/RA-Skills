## Description: <br>
Create, inspect, and edit Microsoft PowerPoint presentations and PPTX decks with reliable layouts, templates, placeholders, notes, charts, and visual QA. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gyzx](https://clawhub.ai/user/gyzx) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, developers, and agents use this skill to create, inspect, edit, and QA PowerPoint or PPTX decks where layouts, placeholders, notes, comments, charts, templates, and visual fidelity matter. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may lead an agent to inspect non-visible PowerPoint content such as speaker notes, comments, media, and linked assets. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with decks whose hidden or linked content the agent is allowed to inspect, and review sensitive notes, comments, and linked assets before sharing outputs. <br>
Risk: Publisher provenance is unavailable for this version. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the ClawHub publisher handle, profile, and release version before deployment when provenance requirements apply. <br>
