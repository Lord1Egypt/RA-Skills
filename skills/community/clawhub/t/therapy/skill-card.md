## Description: <br>
A supportive AI agent skill for mental health awareness and therapy preparation that helps users identify emotions, prepare for therapy sessions, track mood patterns, build coping strategies, and understand therapeutic concepts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIstack](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIstack) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a self-awareness and therapy-preparation aid for exploring emotions, preparing session topics, tracking mood patterns, and considering coping strategies. It is not clinical care and does not replace a licensed mental health professional. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users could mistake supportive mental-health guidance for clinical care or crisis support. <br>
Mitigation: State that the skill does not replace licensed care and direct crisis situations to professional or emergency resources. <br>
Risk: User-provided mood or therapy-preparation details may include sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid requesting unnecessary identifying details and keep prompts focused on self-awareness, preparation, and safety. <br>
