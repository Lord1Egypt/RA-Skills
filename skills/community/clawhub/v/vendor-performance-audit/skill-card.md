## Description: <br>
Vendor Performance Audit helps teams run recurring vendor reviews with weighted KPI scoring, incident review, improvement plans, and renewal or offboarding recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flynndavid](https://clawhub.ai/user/flynndavid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Procurement, operations, and vendor management teams use this skill to evaluate significant vendors before quarterly reviews, incidents, or renewals. It turns vendor evidence into a scored review, tier classification, improvement plan, or offboarding recommendation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Vendor reviews may involve sensitive contracts, spend data, SLA records, incident logs, or compliance details. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only records relevant to the vendor being reviewed and avoid unnecessary confidential or regulated data. <br>
Risk: Incorrect scores or unsupported conclusions could lead to poor renewal, renegotiation, or offboarding decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify each score against source evidence and require authorized approval before vendor communications or business actions. <br>
