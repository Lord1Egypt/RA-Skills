## Description: <br>
A Chinese prose refinement assistant that polishes, rewrites, proofreads, and adjusts the tone of user-provided text within the chat context. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chenkek901](https://clawhub.ai/user/chenkek901) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, employees, and content creators use this skill to refine Chinese text, compare original and revised versions, and understand the main edits. It is intended for text supplied in chat and does not access external systems. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting can change emphasis or introduce unsupported details in fact-sensitive text. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before using them in legal, medical, financial, marketing, or other fact-sensitive contexts. <br>
Risk: Professional-domain text may require domain-specific judgment beyond prose polishing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for wording improvements and have an appropriate professional review final content. <br>
