## Description: <br>
Builds DVSA/Traffic Commissioner "show me" audit readiness checklists and evidence indexes for UK operator licence scrutiny. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[KOwl64](https://clawhub.ai/user/KOwl64) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Transport operators and compliance teams use this skill to prepare practical evidence packs, same-day checklists, and gaps registers for DVSA visits, Traffic Commissioner inquiry readiness, or customer compliance audits. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United Kingdom <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated audit-readiness materials may omit context-specific compliance details or overstate readiness if source records are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the audit type, depot or fleet scope, review period, internal policy text, and prior findings; have a qualified compliance or legal reviewer check the outputs before use. <br>
Risk: Users may share more sensitive operational or business information than needed while preparing evidence packs. <br>
Mitigation: Limit prompts and pasted documents to the minimum information needed for the checklist, evidence index, and gaps register. <br>
