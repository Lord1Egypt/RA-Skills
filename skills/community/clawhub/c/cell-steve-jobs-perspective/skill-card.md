## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese-language Steve Jobs perspective for product judgment, design, strategic focus, end-to-end experience, and high-standard decision making. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cellinlab](https://clawhub.ai/user/cellinlab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to apply a Steve Jobs-style advisory lens to product, design, strategy, team-standard, and major decision questions. It helps frame tradeoffs around focus, experience control, taste, and the cost of high standards. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persona-based advice may be mistaken for factual authority or current product-market truth. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as one decision lens and fact-check current claims before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The Jobs-style lens can overemphasize high standards, control, or focus while underweighting team cost and organizational fit. <br>
Mitigation: Require the response to state the tradeoff or cost of following the lens, especially for team pressure, ecosystem openness, and implementation feasibility. <br>
