## Description: <br>
Helps business users rank channels by ROI, response speed, and deal probability, then produce daily follow-up priorities, checklists, and message templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gdp6539](https://clawhub.ai/user/gdp6539) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business, sales, partnership, and investor-relations teams use this skill to prioritize channel follow-up, generate daily action lists, and draft stage-appropriate follow-up messages from their pipeline data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Contact, pipeline, and deal data can be sensitive if more information is shared than the planning task requires. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the contact and pipeline details needed for the current follow-up plan. <br>
Risk: Generated follow-up messages may be inaccurate, poorly timed, or unsuitable for the recipient if sent without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit messages before sending them. <br>
Risk: The skill is a manual planning assistant and may not reflect the authoritative state of the business pipeline. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the authoritative business records in a system you control and update them after each follow-up. <br>
