# Six Star Astrology (六星占術 / ろくせいせんじゅつ)

A Japanese destiny system created by Hosoki Kazuko (细木数子 / 細木数子) in 1980, rooted in Chinese metaphysics (I-Ching, Four Pillars / 八字).

### 🇨🇳 中文

**六星占術：一个从地狱归来的女人留下的命格密码**

1980年，日本出版了一本奇书。

书的作者叫细木数子。在她之前，占卜在日本要么是寺庙里的抽签，要么是需要精确到分钟的复杂排盘。细木数子说：都不用。给我你的生日，就够了。

这就是六星占術的起源——一种只需要出生日期就能算出你是什么「星型」的命格系统。它脱胎于中国传统的六十甲子（天干地支），但被细木数子重新包装成了一个极其简洁的框架：

你的生日 → 一个1-60的星号 → 对应六颗星（土、金、火、天王、木、水）之一 → 再加上出生年份决定的正负极性 → 12种人格，全部就位。

没有时辰，没有排盘，没有术语壁垒。输入生日，命运的轮廓就出来了。

**这大概是人类历史上最早的命运「轻应用」。**

> 🎬 2026年，Netflix剧集《地狱占星師》用9集篇幅讲述了细木数子本人的传奇与争议——野心、重生、以及建立在命运之上的帝国要付出什么代价。

### 🇬🇧 English

**Six Star Astrology: The Destiny Code Left Behind by a Woman Who Returned from Hell**

In 1980, a remarkable book was published in Japan.

Its author was Hosoki Kazuko. Before her, fortune-telling in Japan was either drawing lots at a temple or constructing complex charts that demanded birth time down to the minute. Hosoki said: none of that. **Just give me your birthday.**

That was the origin of Rokusei — a destiny system that reveals your star type from your birthday alone. Rooted in the Chinese sexagenary cycle (Heavenly Stems + Earthly Branches), it was repackaged by Hosoki into a radically simple framework:

Your birthday → a star number (1–60) → one of six stars (Saturn, Venus, Mars, Uranus, Jupiter, Mercury) → plus polarity (+/−) determined by birth year → 12 personality profiles, fully assembled.

No birth time. No chart construction. No jargon barrier. Enter your birthday, and the outline of your destiny appears.

**This may be the earliest light app for fate in human history.**

> 🎬 In 2026, Netflix's *The Astrologer from Hell* (地狱占星師) told the extraordinary — and controversial — story of Hosoki Kazuko herself across 9 episodes: ambition, reinvention, and the price of building an empire on destiny.

出生時刻也不要。排盤も不要。専門知識の壁もない。誕生日を入力すれば、運命の輪郭が浮かび上がる。

**これはおそらく、人類史上最古の運命の「ライトアプリ」だ。**

## What It Does

Calculate your destiny profile from **birthday only** (no birth time required).

生日即可算命，无需时辰。

誕生日だけで運命を算出。出生時刻は不要。

### Calculation Steps

1. **Birthday → Star Number (1-60)** — via Heavenly Stem + Earthly Branch (天干地支 / 十干十二支)
2. **Birth Year → Polarity (+/-)** — via Earthly Branch
3. **Star Type + Polarity → 12 destiny profiles**
4. **Age mod 12 → Current life cycle phase**
