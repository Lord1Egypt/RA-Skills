## Description: <br>
智能错题本 is an AI-assisted tutoring skill that records student wrong-answer cases, classifies root causes, triggers weak-area alerts, and generates review sets and semester mistake reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, tutors, and learning agents use this skill to turn wrong answers, described mistakes, or question photos into structured mistake records, root-cause analysis, targeted practice, weak-area alerts, and semester-level review reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill maintains detailed student mistake records and learning-profile data. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with clear consent, especially for students or minors, and define review, retention, and deletion expectations before deployment. <br>
Risk: Question photos or mistake records may include personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Crop or remove personal details before use and prefer local-only handling when available. <br>
Risk: The skill shares selected records with related learning-analysis and reminder skills. <br>
Mitigation: Enable cross-skill sharing only when users understand what is shared and have a way to opt out or delete stored records. <br>
