## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers can use this skill to operate a document-processing command-line toolkit for conversion, OCR, batch handling, official document formatting, comparison, extraction, watermarking, and PDF utilities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can perform broad batch file operations such as conversion, renaming, watermarking, encryption, and decryption. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only on selected folders, make backups before batch operations, and review commands before execution. <br>
Risk: Document OCR and extraction workflows may process confidential or regulated content. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive documents unless the operator understands where OCR and extracted content is stored or sent. <br>
