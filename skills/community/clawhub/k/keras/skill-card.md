## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to build Keras or TensorFlow models, choose layer and architecture patterns, diagnose training issues, and prepare model workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can retain Keras workflow preferences, hyperparameters, architecture choices, and hardware constraints in ~/keras/memory.md. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm before storing preferences, keep credentials and model data out of saved notes, and review or delete ~/keras/memory.md when retention is not desired. <br>
Risk: Transfer learning examples may download pretrained weights when weights='imagenet' is used. <br>
Mitigation: Use weights=None for fully offline operation or review network and model-source requirements before running generated code. <br>
