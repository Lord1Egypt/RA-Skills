## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated strategy guidance or trading code could affect financial decisions if used without validation. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review outputs, run backtests, use hard account and position limits, and require explicit human approval before connecting anything to a live brokerage or QMT account. <br>
Risk: Users could introduce account credentials or sensitive trading configuration while adapting examples. <br>
Mitigation: Do not hard-code credentials; keep secrets in approved secure storage and review configuration before execution. <br>
