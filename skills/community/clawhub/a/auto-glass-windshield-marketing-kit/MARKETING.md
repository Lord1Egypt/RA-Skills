## Compliance Moats (Defensible Differentiators)

1. **ADAS Recalibration Disclosure** — 50%+ of replaced windshields now need camera recalibration. Static (in-shop, targets on wall) vs. dynamic (drive at highway speed) calibration. Missing this = safety liability + warranty disputes. No competitor skill generates this disclosure.

2. **AGRSS Certification Gating** — Auto Glass Replacement Safety Standard. Only shops with current AGRSS certification can truthfully say "AGRSS certified technicians." Skill gates this claim.

3. **OEM / OEE / Aftermarket Distinction** — Three tiers: OEM (from original manufacturer), OEE (Original Equipment Equivalent — same factory, different branding), Aftermarket (third-party, may not meet ADAS specs). Generic AI writes "OEM quality" for aftermarket = false advertising risk.

4. **Insurance Billing Compliance** — FTC and state insurance regulations require deductible disclosures alongside "no out-of-pocket" promises. Skill includes "(subject to your deductible)" automatically.

5. **Rock Chip Repair Size Limits** — AGRSS defines repairable chips: <1" (25mm) diameter, cracks <3" (75mm), not in driver's primary view, not within 2" of edge. Communicating these standards protects shops from "you said you could fix it" disputes.
