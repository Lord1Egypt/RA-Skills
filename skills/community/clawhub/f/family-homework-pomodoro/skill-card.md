## Description: <br>
Parent-confirmed homework intake, Pomodoro execution, nightly completion checks, and reward approval. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Parents and family-focused assistants use this skill to record same-day homework, confirm a start time, guide simple Pomodoro work sessions, check completion at night, and keep rewards under parent approval. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shared prompts or published variants could expose child names, school names, private schedules, account IDs, or other sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Keep examples generic and non-identifying, and avoid adding real family, school, account, health, or schedule details to prompts or public variants. <br>
Risk: The assistant could overstep the intended workflow by inventing homework details, deciding start times, declaring completion, or granting rewards without parent confirmation. <br>
Mitigation: Require parent confirmation for start time, completion status, unfinished-work handling, and reward approval; ask short clarification questions instead of guessing missing details. <br>
