## Description: <br>
Create a polished explainer handout with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for cosmetic dermatology clinic client education. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Dermatologists and clinic growth teams use this skill to draft client education handouts that explain cosmetic dermatology topics with FAQs, visuals, and clear next steps. Generated material should be reviewed before patient-facing use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated healthcare education material may contain inaccurate, overstated, or locally noncompliant claims. <br>
Mitigation: Have a licensed clinician and any required compliance reviewer verify claims, wording, consent language, next steps, and local advertising or healthcare requirements before client or patient use. <br>
Risk: Prompts or drafts could include patient-identifiable information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering patient-identifiable information and use only de-identified or generic case details. <br>
Risk: Generated visuals or visual concepts may be misleading for cosmetic dermatology expectations or outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Review all images and visual guidance for clinical accuracy, realistic expectations, and compliance before publishing or distributing. <br>
