## Description: <br>
Fiction prose analysis that helps catch weak verbs, repetition, cliches, passive voice, and other craft issues in manuscripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jpoehnelt](https://clawhub.ai/user/jpoehnelt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, writing assistants, and fiction authors use this skill to run IronProse CLI analysis on manuscript text, compare drafts, inspect schemas, and rate diagnostics for prose-quality feedback. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may send manuscript text and optional feedback context to a remote prose-analysis service without strong privacy notice in the supplied evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential drafts, client work, unpublished manuscripts, or personal material unless the publisher documents retention, logging, and privacy practices or offers a local-only mode. <br>
