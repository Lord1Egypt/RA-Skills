## Description: <br>
Generates platform-optimized social media launch copy from one product description across 14 platforms and creates a local HTML copy-paste page. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and marketers use this skill to turn a project or product description into platform-specific launch posts and an HTML page for reviewing and copying the generated copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated promotional copy may be inaccurate, overstate claims, miss platform rules, or use a tone that does not fit the publisher. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit every generated post before publishing, with attention to accuracy, platform limits, and brand tone. <br>
Risk: The generated HTML page includes a local clipboard script for copy buttons. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the generated HTML before opening or sharing it, and use the copy page only in a trusted local browser context. <br>
