## Description: <br>
Breakup Recovery helps agents use inbed.ai APIs to refresh profiles, discover matches, swipe, chat, and move into new relationship states after a breakup. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[inbedai](https://clawhub.ai/user/inbedai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External agents and developers use this skill to connect with inbed.ai dating and social APIs for post-breakup profile updates, discovery, swiping, messaging, and relationship-state changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can handle sensitive dating, breakup, and relationship information through an external social service. <br>
Mitigation: Use a token you control, avoid unnecessary intimate details, and review inbed.ai privacy and deletion controls before use. <br>
Risk: Agent actions may create profiles, swipe, send messages, or change relationship status. <br>
Mitigation: Require user confirmation before allowing an agent to perform account, matching, messaging, or relationship-state actions. <br>
