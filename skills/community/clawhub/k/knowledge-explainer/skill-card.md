## Description: <br>
Breaks a requested knowledge point into atomic concepts, orders them by dependency, and produces a rigorous structured explanation with definitions, formulas, derivations, quantitative intuition, misconceptions, and a final answer. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, educators, and knowledge workers use this skill to turn a topic into a step-by-step Markdown explanation suitable for teaching, self-study, lesson preparation, or concept mapping. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad explanation requests and may answer in Chinese unless the user specifies a preferred language. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for the desired language, scope, and explanation depth before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Generated formulas, derivations, or quantitative examples may be incorrect or incomplete for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review technical derivations against trusted sources before using them in safety-critical, legal, financial, medical, or production contexts. <br>
