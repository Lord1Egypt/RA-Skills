## Description: <br>
Conduct structured self-evaluations after tasks to analyze efficiency, accuracy, approach quality, and extract patterns for improved future performance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charlie-morrison](https://clawhub.ai/user/charlie-morrison) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and developers use this skill after complex tasks, debugging sessions, or multi-step workflows to produce structured retrospectives and reusable improvement patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Retrospectives may capture sensitive project details, credentials, customer data, or unverified conclusions if users include them in prompts or saved outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Review retrospective inputs and outputs before storing or sharing them, and omit secrets, customer data, private project details, and conclusions that have not been verified. <br>
