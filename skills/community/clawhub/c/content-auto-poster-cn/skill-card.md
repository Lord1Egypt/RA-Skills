## Description: <br>
自动发布内容到多个平台，支持微信公众号、微博、知乎等平台定时发布。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and publishing teams use this skill to prepare multi-platform content posts, scheduled publishing plans, platform-specific formatting, and publication status summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can publish or schedule content on public platforms without clearly documented confirmation, account, or cancellation safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval for each target platform, publish time, and account before publishing or scheduling content. <br>
Risk: Scheduled posts may create compliance or confidentiality exposure if used for private, regulated, or sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid private, regulated, or sensitive content unless storage, cancellation, and audit behavior is understood and accepted. <br>
