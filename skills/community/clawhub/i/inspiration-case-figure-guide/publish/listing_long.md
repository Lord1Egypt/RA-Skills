# Inspiration Case Figure Guide

`inspiration-case-figure-guide` v3.0.0 helps produce publication-ready raster figures that explain why a research paper is needed.

It supports:

- motivating example figures;
- problem-teaser first figures;
- failure and limitation cases;
- before/after contrast panels;
- observation-to-hypothesis diagrams;
- scenario storyboards;
- evidence-to-inspiration boards;
- design-space gap hooks;
- reviewer-facing concern cases.

The skill was generated from `research-paper-figure-skill-factory` v1.0.1 with a full local evidence base: 7,631 processed PDFs, 146,071 extracted figure captions, 119,534 diagram-relevant captions, 93,088 multi-label figure records, and 48,536 inspiration/case-like records.

Every text reply includes current plan, default recommendation, state/output footer, full step position, and next-question prompts. Every multi-option text decision must go through visual candidate-board setup, IMAGE_ONLY candidate image generation, and candidate review before final prompt or caption work.
