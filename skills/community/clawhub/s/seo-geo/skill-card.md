## Description: <br>
Complete SEO and GEO system for SaaS companies that helps agents plan search strategy, research keywords, audit performance, write optimized articles, and improve visibility in Google and AI search engines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[berthelol](https://clawhub.ai/user/berthelol) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External SaaS founders, marketers, and developers use this skill to set up SEO workflows, analyze search performance, build content calendars, and produce SEO/GEO-optimized SaaS articles. It can create and update project SEO files and guide use of Google, GA4, SemRush, DataForSEO, and CMS-specific publishing workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses Google and DataForSEO credentials and may involve paid SEO API usage. <br>
Mitigation: Keep credentials in environment variables or a secrets manager, avoid pasting secrets into chat or committing them, and monitor paid API usage. <br>
Risk: Generated SEO files and article changes could contain incorrect, stale, or misleading business guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated seo/ file changes, article drafts, citations, schema, and publishing recommendations before publishing or committing. <br>
