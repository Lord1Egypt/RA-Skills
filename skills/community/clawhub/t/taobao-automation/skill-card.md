## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce operators and store managers use this skill to analyze Taobao product categories, monitor competitor pages, optimize listings, generate promotional copy, and receive scheduled operational reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recurring reports or monitoring may expose shop metrics to unintended chats, calendars, or recipients. <br>
Mitigation: Decide which metrics may be read and which Feishu, WeChat, or calendar destinations may receive them before enabling scheduled jobs. <br>
Risk: Competitor page checks and web data collection may violate platform or third-party site rules if run too broadly or too often. <br>
Mitigation: Use least-privilege credentials, set conservative job frequency, define allowed pages, and follow Taobao and third-party site rules. <br>
