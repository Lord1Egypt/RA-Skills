# Partner Onboarding Kit

The quality of your partner onboarding determines whether a new affiliate becomes active within 7 days or never posts at all. This kit gives you everything you need to send to a new partner on Day 1.

---

## Welcome Email Template

**Subject:** Welcome to [Brand] Affiliate Program — everything you need to get started

Hi [Creator Name],

Welcome to the [Brand] affiliate program — we're excited to have you on the team!

Here's everything you need to get started:

**Your affiliate link:** [LINK]
**Your discount code (for your audience):** [CODE] — gives your followers [X]% off their first order

**Your commission:**
- Current tier: [Starter/Growth/Elite]
- Rate: [X]% on all confirmed sales
- Attribution window: [X] days after click
- Payment: Net-30 on the 1st of each month (minimum $[X] balance)

**To get your first commission faster:**
We're sending you a product sample to [shipping address] — expected arrival [date]. We'd love for you to try it before your first post.

**What converts well for [Brand]:**
- [Key message 1: specific hook or angle]
- [Key message 2: common customer pain point]
- Authentic "in my routine" or "I've been using this for X weeks" framing

**What to avoid:**
- Medical or clinical efficacy claims
- Mentioning competitor brands

**Content resources:**
- [Link to brand asset folder: images, video clips, brand fonts]
- [Link to approved claims and messaging guide]

Any questions, reply directly to this email or message me at [contact].

Looking forward to your first post!

[Your name]
[Brand]

---

## Partner Quick-Start Checklist (include in onboarding)

- [ ] Affiliate link created and tested (confirm it tracks)
- [ ] Discount code assigned and tested (confirm it applies)
- [ ] Product sample shipped (tracking: [tracking number])
- [ ] Brand asset folder shared ([link])
- [ ] Approved claims guide shared ([link])
- [ ] First post deadline discussed: [suggested date]

---

## Brand Asset Folder Contents

Provide new affiliates with:

1. **Product images** — High-res white background + lifestyle shots
2. **Video clips** — B-roll of product in use, brand story clips
3. **Logo files** — For any co-branded content
4. **Approved claims list** — What you can and can't say
5. **Product fact sheet** — Ingredients, specs, key benefits in plain language
6. **UGC content brief** (optional) — If you want specific content format
