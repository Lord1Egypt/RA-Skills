## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to guide an agent through one-off Grok Imagine image and video generation tasks with the RunAPI CLI, or to identify SDK options for app and backend integrations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can authorize RunAPI image and video generation actions that may consume provider quota or incur cost. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm intended generation commands, RunAPI authentication method, pricing, and rate limits before execution. <br>
Risk: The skill describes both image and video workflows while its name and summary are image-focused. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as capable of image and video generation, and verify the selected action before running the CLI. <br>
