## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Credential or verification secrets could be exposed if users paste passwords, mailbox credentials, one-time codes, reset links, API keys, or recovery details into chat. <br>
Mitigation: Keep credentials in a password manager, complete logins and verifications outside chat, and never record passwords in spreadsheets or markdown trackers. <br>
Risk: Business listings, paid placements, Search Console settings, or public profiles could be changed without proper authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit approval before changing listings, paid placements, Search Console settings, or public profiles. <br>
Risk: The skill is a supervised SEO checklist, so recommendations may be applied incorrectly if business identity and NAP details are not verified first. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm canonical business name, address, phone, website, target services, and service areas before audits, citations, schema work, or content placement. <br>
