## Use Case: <br>
Writers, translators, and agents use this skill to produce Vietnamese phrasing that sounds natural, casual, and relationship-aware rather than overly formal. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Casual Vietnamese, regional phrasing, particles, or texting-style shortcuts may be inappropriate for formal, professional, or audience-sensitive contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated Vietnamese for audience fit, intended register, regional consistency, and exact tone marks before use. <br>
