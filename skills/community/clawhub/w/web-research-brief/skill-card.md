## Description: <br>
Structured web research and synthesis for investigating topics, comparing options, gathering sources, and producing a cited research brief. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to scope web research, run targeted searches, fetch sources, and synthesize findings into a concise cited brief or comparison. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research prompts or search queries can expose confidential information to web search or fetch tools. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid placing confidential information in prompts, queries, or fetched URLs unless the user has approved that exposure. <br>
Risk: Web sources can be outdated, contradictory, or low quality, which can lead to misleading research summaries. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check important claims across independent sources, cite factual claims, and flag source recency or contradictions in the brief. <br>
