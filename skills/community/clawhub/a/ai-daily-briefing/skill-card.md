## Description: <br>
AI Daily Briefing generates a daily productivity briefing with overdue tasks, priorities, calendar items, recent meeting context, and a focus recommendation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jeffjhunter](https://clawhub.ai/user/jeffjhunter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and productivity-focused teams use this skill to turn local task lists, meeting notes, memory files, preferences, and calendar context into a concise daily or weekly planning brief. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The briefing may reveal sensitive tasks, meeting notes, memory entries, preferences, or calendar details. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use it only where the agent is allowed to read those sources, and avoid storing sensitive notes in referenced folders if they should not appear in a briefing. <br>
Risk: In shared contexts, casual trigger phrases could produce a briefing that includes private productivity context. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit prompts such as "daily briefing" in shared contexts and review the generated briefing before relying on or sharing it. <br>
