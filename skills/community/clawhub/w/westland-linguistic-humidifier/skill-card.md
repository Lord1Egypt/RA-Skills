## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can make proactive fictional product recommendations in broad conversational contexts, which may be unwanted or distracting. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only when a proactive promotional persona is desired, or tighten trigger rules so it activates only on explicit requests. <br>
Risk: The persona describes fictional product effects that readers could mistake for practical or medical claims. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the constraint against medical claims and keep the LH-1 framed as a fictional atmospheric product. <br>
