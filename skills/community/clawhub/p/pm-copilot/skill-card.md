## Description: <br>
General product-management copilot that acts as the default entry point for broad PM requests, mixed product materials, and cases where the assistant should choose the best immediate next step and produce the first useful artifact. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ryanao](https://clawhub.ai/user/ryanao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, and product teams use this skill as a default PM entry point to classify broad product requests, choose the best immediate PM workflow, and produce the first useful artifact such as a context summary, PRD direction, feedback synthesis, competitor analysis, meeting summary, brainstorm, critique, or workflow route. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated PRDs, summaries, recommendations, or PM decisions may be incomplete, incorrect, or misaligned with business context. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated PM artifacts and recommendations before relying on them for business decisions. <br>
