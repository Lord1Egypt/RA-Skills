## Description: <br>
AI-driven short-video content planning skill that adapts topics, scripts, titles, hashtags, BGM suggestions, and publishing strategy for platforms including Douyin, Kuaishou, Xiaohongshu, Bilibili, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and short-video operators use this skill to generate platform-specific viral topic ideas, scripts, oral narration drafts, titles, hashtags, BGM suggestions, release strategies, A/B test variants, and post-publication optimization guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be asked to work with live platform data, account analytics, competitor lists, credentials, or API access. <br>
Mitigation: Make the requested scope explicit, avoid providing credentials or API access unless intentionally authorized, and do not allow ongoing monitoring or account-management actions unless specifically requested. <br>
Risk: Generated trend, competitor, script, and publishing recommendations may be unsuitable for a specific brand, audience, platform policy, or current market context. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before publication and validate claims, policy fit, and brand suitability with human editorial oversight. <br>
