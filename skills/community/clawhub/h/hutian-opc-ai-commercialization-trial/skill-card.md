## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential business plans, pricing ideas, or anti-copying strategies while asking for commercialization guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive details unless the execution environment is approved for that information, and redact customer, financial, or private strategy data where possible. <br>
Risk: The output is business guidance and may not account for platform policy, legal, or licensing constraints in a specific market. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated plans as drafts and have qualified business, legal, or platform operations reviewers confirm them before launch. <br>
