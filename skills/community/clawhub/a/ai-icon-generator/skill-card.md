## Description: <br>
Generate custom icons using artificial intelligence from text prompts or descriptions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and product teams use this skill to generate web, mobile, design-system, marketing, and CI/CD icon assets from natural language prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Icon prompts, style choices, and generated assets are sent to an external API provider. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending secrets, unreleased product details, customer data, regulated content, or confidential brand material unless the provider's privacy and retention practices have been reviewed and approved. <br>
