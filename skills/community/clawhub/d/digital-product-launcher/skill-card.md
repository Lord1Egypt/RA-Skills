## Description: <br>
Digital Product Launcher helps solopreneurs and creators plan, package, price, and launch downloadable digital products across ideation, sales copy, funnels, email sequences, and launch checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, solopreneurs, and small business operators use this skill to turn a digital product idea into a launch plan, including positioning, pricing, product page copy, funnel design, platform setup, and post-launch optimization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business, pricing, and platform recommendations may be generic or become outdated for a user's market. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against current platform rules, fees, customer research, and business constraints before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Users could disclose platform passwords, private customer data, or sensitive business information while planning a launch. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering credentials, private customer records, or confidential business data into the assistant. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may trigger the skill for general online-selling or income-generation questions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking for digital product launch guidance before applying the full launch workflow. <br>
