## Description: <br>
Configure these X growth skills for an account. Use when first installing this repo, starting a new X account plan, or defining niche, pillars, cadence, and metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to set up an X account growth brief, define audience and content pillars, choose posting cadence and metrics, and decide where to save account notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may collect and save an X growth brief containing strategy, audience, proof points, cadence, metrics, and voice rules. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive private information unless the user is comfortable storing it in the chosen repo or notes folder. <br>
Risk: Growth recommendations based on public X algorithm signals can become misleading if treated as guaranteed tactics. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the signals as a planning map, review the brief before use, and optimize for useful positive engagement while avoiding spammy or harmful behavior. <br>
