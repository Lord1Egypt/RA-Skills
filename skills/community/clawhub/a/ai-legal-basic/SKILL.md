# AI Legal Basics

Practical legal guidance for everyday situations. Contract review, legal document templates, rights awareness, and compliance checklists for individuals and small businesses.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Contract Essentials
- Common contract types: employment, lease, service, sales, NDA
- Key clauses explained: indemnification, liability, termination, jurisdiction
- Red flag identification in contract language
- Template customization and adaptation guidance

### 2. Consumer Rights
- Product returns, warranties, refunds by jurisdiction
- Consumer protection laws overview
- Dispute resolution: mediation, small claims court
- Scam and fraud recognition, reporting procedures

### 3. Employment Law
- Employment contract review, non-compete analysis
- Workplace rights: discrimination, harassment, wage and hour
- Termination: wrongful dismissal, severance, unemployment benefits
- Freelancer/contractor agreement essentials

### 4. Housing & Property
- Lease agreement review, tenant rights, eviction process
- Property purchase basics, title searches, disclosures
- Homeowner association rules, neighbor disputes
- Security deposit recovery, repair request documentation

### 5. Family & Personal
- Marriage, divorce basics, prenuptial agreements
- Child custody and support framework
- Estate planning: wills, trusts, power of attorney
- Name change, vital records amendment

### 6. Small Business
- Business entity selection: LLC, corporation, sole proprietorship
- IP basics: trademarks, copyrights, patents
- Terms of service and privacy policy drafting
- Regulatory compliance basics by industry
