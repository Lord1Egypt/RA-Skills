## Description: <br>
Simple task intake, reminder, and completion check-in flow for public-safe daily coordination. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[guoma970](https://clawhub.ai/user/guoma970) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and community skill authors use this skill to coordinate same-day task intake, confirmed reminder timing, short task reminders, nightly completion checks, and sender-approved reward or follow-up notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include sensitive personal information, private schedules, school details, health details, account IDs, or real names in the task flow. <br>
Mitigation: Use only generic, public-safe task descriptions and avoid identity-linked or sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Reminder messages may start at the wrong time if the assistant assumes timing. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the start time with the sender before beginning reminders. <br>
Risk: Reward or follow-up messages may be sent without sender approval. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for sender approval after completion before sending any reward or follow-up note. <br>
