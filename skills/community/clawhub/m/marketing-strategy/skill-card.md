## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and agencies use this skill to draft China-focused channel strategies, content calendars, budget allocations, KPI frameworks, and risk-aware growth plans. It is intended for planning support and should be reviewed against current platform rules, brand constraints, and campaign data before execution. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Running the benchmark helper contacts an external API service. <br>
Mitigation: Run the helper only when comfortable contacting that service, and avoid sending confidential campaign copy or business details unless its data handling is trusted. <br>
Risk: Marketing recommendations may become inaccurate as platform policies, ad costs, and audience behavior change. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans against current platform rules, campaign analytics, and brand requirements before use. <br>
