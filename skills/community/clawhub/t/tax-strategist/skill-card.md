## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users can use this agent for China-focused tax planning, compliance review, transfer pricing analysis, and tax memorandum drafting. The skill is best suited to explicit PRC tax questions where outputs will be reviewed by a qualified tax professional. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China and cross-border jurisdictions relevant to PRC tax matters <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad finance or business prompts even when the user did not ask for tax advice. <br>
Mitigation: Install or invoke it only for explicit China or PRC tax questions, and keep general finance workflows routed to a more appropriate skill. <br>
Risk: Tax guidance is high-stakes and may become outdated as laws, policy notices, or local enforcement practices change. <br>
Mitigation: Require review by a qualified tax professional and verify cited positions against current authoritative tax sources before taking action. <br>
Risk: Suggested tax structures could be unsuitable without complete facts about business substance, cash flow, jurisdictions, and related-party arrangements. <br>
Mitigation: Collect transaction facts, document assumptions, quantify uncertainty, and preserve contemporaneous support before implementation. <br>
