### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automations can modify business records, trigger payments, change CRM status, publish public posts, or send customer-facing messages if connected with broad permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Connect only the minimum app permissions needed and add human review for payments, CRM status changes, public posts, and customer-facing messages. <br>
Risk: Incorrect field mappings, untested workflows, or failed automation steps can create inaccurate data or missed actions. <br>
Mitigation: Test with dummy or low-risk data first, review logs, configure error alerts, and keep rollback steps for automations that modify business records. <br>
