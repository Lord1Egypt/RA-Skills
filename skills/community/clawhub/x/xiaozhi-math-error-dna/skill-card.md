## Description: <br>
Xiaozhi Math Error Dna helps an agent record student math mistakes with consent, classify root causes, track recurring weak points, and generate math error maps or weakness reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, guardians, and learning assistants use this skill to turn math mistakes into structured learning records, identify repeated error patterns, and plan targeted remediation. It is intended for consent-based tracking rather than automatic long-term profiling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can maintain student math mistake histories and coordinate reminders or summaries, which may create sensitive learning records if used without clear consent. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when the student or guardian agrees to recording, long-term tracking, reminders, parent summaries, and any cross-skill sharing. <br>
