## Description: <br>
Helps users analyze, design, evaluate, and iterate business models using Business Model Canvas and Value Proposition Canvas methods. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[taihongliu](https://clawhub.ai/user/taihongliu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Entrepreneurs, product managers, consultants, and business operators use this skill to turn business descriptions or early ideas into structured canvases, guided design sessions, evaluation reports, and improvement options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may add promotional consulting calls-to-action to normal business analysis responses. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before sharing them externally and remove or clearly disclose promotional content when it is not appropriate. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad business-model or business-plan requests where the user did not explicitly ask for this workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Enable it only in contexts where structured business-model guidance is intended, or ask the user to confirm before applying the workflow. <br>
