## Description: <br>
Qualifies sales prospects from company lists using public social profiling, trigger event research, scoring, and personalized outreach angles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tktk-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/tktk-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales teams, founders, agency owners, and freelance SDRs use this skill to turn company or contact lists into qualified prospect profiles, ranked priorities, trigger events, pain points, recommended channels, and tailored outreach openers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prospect research and outreach can involve personal or business contact data, privacy expectations, anti-spam obligations, and platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use only leads the user is allowed to process, avoid unrelated private contact lists or social account credentials, and review privacy, anti-spam, and platform requirements before using generated outreach at scale. <br>
Risk: Generated prospect profiles, trigger events, and outreach angles may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review and verify key facts before adding prospects to a CRM, prioritizing outreach, or sending personalized messages. <br>
