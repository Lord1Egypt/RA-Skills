## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated text could misrepresent authorship where disclosure of AI assistance is expected. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for tone and fluency, but disclose AI assistance when required by school, work, platform, legal, or reader expectations. <br>
Risk: Casual phrasing, slang, or profanity may be unsuitable for some audiences. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for audience, region, and setting before sending or publishing. <br>
