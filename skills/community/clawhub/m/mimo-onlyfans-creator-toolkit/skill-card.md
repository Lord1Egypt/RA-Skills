## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language operating plans for subscription-platform creators, covering positioning, pricing, content calendars, fan CRM, DM automation, traffic, and growth metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators and creator-operations teams use this skill to produce Chinese-language subscription-platform growth plans, including 30-day onboarding roadmaps, pricing tiers, content schedules, fan segmentation, DM workflows, and revenue metric targets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides business and marketing guidance that could be mistaken for legal, tax, platform compliance, or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Users should verify platform terms, local law, age requirements, tax obligations, and financial assumptions with qualified sources before acting. <br>
Risk: The skill recommends paid third-party creator tools and pricing benchmarks that may change or may not fit a specific creator's market. <br>
Mitigation: Users should independently evaluate tool pricing, platform compatibility, contractual terms, and current market data before purchase or deployment. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to answer in Chinese, which may limit reviewability for non-Chinese users. <br>
Mitigation: Non-Chinese users should translate and review the output before using it in business operations. <br>
