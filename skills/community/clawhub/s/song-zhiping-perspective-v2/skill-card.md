## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The public-figure-inspired persona may be mistaken for the real person's current views or for authoritative business, legal, financial, regulatory, or transaction advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as a public-information-derived thinking aid and validate consequential decisions with qualified human advisors and current source material. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases could make the skill answer in character when the user did not intend that posture. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit exit phrases or reset the conversation context when persona-style guidance is not desired. <br>
