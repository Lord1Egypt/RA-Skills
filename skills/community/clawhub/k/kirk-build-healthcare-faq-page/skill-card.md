## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common healthcare questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Healthcare operators, clinic teams, and patient educators use this skill to draft customer-facing FAQ content for common healthcare questions. Drafts should be reviewed by qualified humans before publication, especially when they include regulated medical claims. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: FAQ drafts may contain inaccurate, outdated, or regulated medical claims. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified human reviewers check medical and regulated claims before publishing. <br>
Risk: Using patient-specific or sensitive details in prompts could expose PHI or other confidential data. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter PHI or sensitive patient details when using the skill. <br>
Risk: Web-sourced supporting facts may be stale or unreliable. <br>
Mitigation: Verify web-sourced facts against appropriate healthcare, policy, or organizational sources before publication. <br>
