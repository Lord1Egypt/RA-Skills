## Description: <br>
Precise nutrition calculator for Chinese food analysis and goal-oriented macro tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to estimate nutrition for Chinese meals, calculate BMR, TDEE, and goal-based macro targets, and compare meal intake against daily targets. It can also summarize multi-day trends and produce markdown-friendly nutrition reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Nutrition estimates may be wrong when food matching, portion estimation, or fallback dish estimates are inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Treat reports as estimates, review assumptions in the meal breakdown, and do not use the output as medical or dietetic advice. <br>
Risk: The skill can process personal profile fields such as weight, height, age, gender, activity level, and goals. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the personal details needed for the calculation and avoid sharing sensitive health information beyond the task. <br>
