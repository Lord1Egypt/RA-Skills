## Description: <br>
Runs a Chinese-language Oxford Union-style multi-agent debate workflow for any user-provided motion. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fitler-lin](https://clawhub.ai/user/fitler-lin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and debate participants use this skill to test argument strength, prepare formal debates, or run a structured Chinese Oxford Union-style debate on a chosen motion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can steer qualifying requests into a formal multi-round debate when the user expected ordinary discussion. <br>
Mitigation: Ask explicitly for normal discussion or another language when the Oxford Union-style Chinese debate workflow is not desired. <br>
Risk: Generated arguments, scores, and summaries may be persuasive without being factually complete. <br>
Mitigation: Review debate outputs before relying on them for decisions, publications, or formal preparation. <br>
