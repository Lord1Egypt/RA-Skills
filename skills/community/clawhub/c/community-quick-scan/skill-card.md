## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Community operators and project teams use this skill to get fast feedback on Telegram or Discord engagement, spam risk, onboarding friction, and one practical improvement to make next. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may expose private community access or moderation details while requesting a scan. <br>
Mitigation: Share only public or intentionally shared invite links, or provide screenshots, redacted onboarding text, and manual metrics for private communities. <br>
