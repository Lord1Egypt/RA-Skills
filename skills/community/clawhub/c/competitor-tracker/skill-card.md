## Description: <br>
Tracks competitor prices, sales estimates, reviews, promotions, keyword rankings, and inventory for ecommerce monitoring and comparison reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce sellers, market analysts, brand operators, pricing strategists, purchasing managers, and investment analysts use this skill to monitor competitor product activity and generate comparison reports, alerts, and response guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Monitoring ecommerce pages may violate platform terms, anti-bot rules, privacy duties, or data-use restrictions if data collection is not authorized. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm each platform's terms, robots or anti-bot restrictions, and privacy obligations before collecting, storing, or acting on reviews, sales estimates, inventory, or ranking data. <br>
Risk: Optional Python dependencies could affect a shared environment if installed globally. <br>
Mitigation: Install and run any dependencies in an isolated Python environment. <br>
