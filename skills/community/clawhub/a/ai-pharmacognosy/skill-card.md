## Description: <br>
Ai Pharmacognosy provides educational reference guidance on pharmacognosy, natural products, medicinal material identification, processing, quality standards, and active compound separation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, educators, and pharmacy or natural-products practitioners can use the skill for educational explanations of pharmacognosy topics, including medicinal material identification, natural-product chemistry, processing methods, quality control, and pharmacopoeial concepts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Herbal identification, preparation, detoxification, dosing, or consumption content can be mistaken for professional medical or preparation guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as educational reference material only and consult licensed professionals, verified suppliers, and appropriate lab testing before any real-world medicinal herb use. <br>
Risk: Content involving toxic materials such as aconite can create safety risk if acted on directly. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use the skill to prepare, detoxify, dose, or consume toxic medicinal materials. <br>
