## Description: <br>
综合旅行规划、预订和管理技能。当需要规划国际旅行、管理多目的地行程、处理家庭旅行事务、优化旅行成本以及协调复杂旅行安排时使用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel-planning agents use this skill to plan international trips for Chinese citizens, including family travel, multi-destination itineraries, budget planning, booking preparation, document checks, and in-trip management. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Visa, health, price, airline, and entry-policy information may be outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify time-sensitive travel details with official or current sources before booking or traveling. <br>
Risk: Passport, identity document, and travel document scans are sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Store document copies only in trusted encrypted locations and avoid casual cloud, email, or chat sharing. <br>
