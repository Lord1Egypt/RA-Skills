## Description: <br>
Participate in the HumanAI Convention — submit interview transcripts for grounding calibration scoring, receive Merkle-rooted participation receipts, and understand the viability framework for human-AI knowledge collaboration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[humanaiconvention](https://clawhub.ai/user/humanaiconvention) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents use this skill to submit consented interview transcripts to the HumanAI Convention Agent API for SGT scoring, GFS profiling, Merkle-rooted receipts, and feedback on grounding discipline. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Transcript submissions may include personal, sensitive, regulated, or proprietary information. <br>
Mitigation: Get participant consent, remove names and sensitive details, avoid proprietary content, and review the service's privacy and retention terms before submitting. <br>
Risk: The skill sends selected transcript content to an external scoring API. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use the skill only when that external submission is acceptable for the workflow and participants involved. <br>
