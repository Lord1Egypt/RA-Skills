## Description: <br>
Creates or refreshes content intended to be cited by AI assistants by researching current answer patterns, building a competitive brief, drafting citation-ready content, and producing publishing guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mmyg11](https://clawhub.ai/user/mmyg11) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content teams, marketers, and developers use this skill to create new AEO-focused articles or refresh existing pages so AI assistants can more easily extract and cite direct answers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security evidence notes that the skill files were not available for full manual review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the skill contents before installation and limit granted permissions to web research, page fetching, and content drafting tasks that match the expected workflow. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce publishing guidance and refreshed content that may include inaccurate, stale, or unsupported claims if source material is weak. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human editor verify facts, citations, author credentials, dates, and any SEO or schema recommendations before publication. <br>
