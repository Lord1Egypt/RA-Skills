## Description: <br>
Provides realistic career-change guidance for white-collar workers displaced by AI, including transferable skill audits, growing fields, transition timelines, and financial runway planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to evaluate realistic career paths after AI-related job displacement, audit transferable skills, compare income and training timelines, and plan a transition without exhausting savings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may ask users to share sensitive career history, transferable skills, milestones, and rough savings or expense runway. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information needed for planning, review any stored notes or files, and avoid entering details that should not be retained by the agent. <br>
Risk: Recurring career progress and financial runway check-ins could continue longer or more often than the user expects. <br>
Mitigation: Before enabling reminders, confirm the schedule, duration, and how to pause or stop weekly, monthly, or 90-day follow-ups. <br>
