## Description: <br>
Provides a Zhang Yiming-inspired perspective framework for entrepreneurship, product strategy, organizational management, personal growth, and investment questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xiejianjun000](https://clawhub.ai/user/xiejianjun000) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and end users can invoke the skill to have an assistant analyze business, product, organization, personal growth, or investment questions through a Zhang Yiming-inspired lens. Outputs should be treated as perspective prompts, not real statements from Zhang Yiming or authoritative business advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake generated perspective outputs for real statements from Zhang Yiming or authoritative business advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as Zhang Yiming-inspired perspective prompts and review them before making business, product, investment, or management decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the assistant to enter the perspective unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit opt-in trigger phrases, or narrow the trigger phrases during deployment if automatic activation is not desired. <br>
