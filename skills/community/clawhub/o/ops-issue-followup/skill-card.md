## Description: <br>
Guides Codex through structured follow-up for project, system maintenance, and operations issues by clarifying facts, gathering context, investigating evidence, and summarizing findings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operations teams use this skill to keep incident or maintenance follow-up focused on confirmed facts, ownership, project-local evidence, and concrete next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad issue follow-up can lead an agent to inspect unrelated project files, configuration, or logs. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target project or service and scope before broad review, then rely on project-local evidence and ask for missing information when needed. <br>
