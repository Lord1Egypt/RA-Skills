## Description: <br>
Evaluate business decisions through the lens of sustainable, profitable growth. Use when someone is making decisions about spending, hiring, fundraising, or scaling their business. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[carollili](https://clawhub.ai/user/carollili) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, and business advisors use this skill to evaluate spending, hiring, fundraising, and scaling decisions against sustainable profitability principles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business guidance may be mistaken for legal, tax, investment, or accounting advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as startup strategy guidance and consult qualified professionals for regulated or high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose confidential financial records, credentials, or other sensitive business information while seeking advice. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the context needed for the decision and avoid secrets or sensitive records unless the surrounding agent session truly requires them. <br>
