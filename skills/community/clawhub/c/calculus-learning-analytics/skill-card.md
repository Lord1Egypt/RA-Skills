## Description: <br>
基于作业批改数据，提供学生能力画像、班级学情看板和个性化教学建议，帮助高等数学教师分析学习情况并规划教学干预。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Educators and academic support teams use this skill to turn calculus assignment data into class dashboards, individual learner profiles, risk alerts, and teaching recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Student-level analytics may expose identifiable profiles, rankings, and risk flags. <br>
Mitigation: Use only under an approved educational data policy, minimize identifiers, and restrict individual reports to authorized educators. <br>
Risk: Learning-risk predictions and ranking outputs may be misused or shared beyond their intended audience. <br>
Mitigation: Keep risk flags and rankings private, review recommendations before acting on them, and define retention, deletion, export, and audit practices before using real student data. <br>
