## Description: <br>
Manage anxiety with grounding exercises, breathing techniques, and thought reframing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for self-guided anxiety relief, including grounding exercises, breathing techniques, thought reframing, and private anxiety logging. It is intended as self-help guidance and not as a substitute for medical or mental-health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on self-help guidance when professional medical or mental-health care is needed. <br>
Mitigation: State that the skill is self-help guidance only and direct users in crisis or with self-harm thoughts to crisis resources or professional support. <br>
Risk: Anxiety logs may contain sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep logs local and treat them as private information. <br>
