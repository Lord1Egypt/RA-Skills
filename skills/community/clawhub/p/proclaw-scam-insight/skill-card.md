## Description: <br>
基于不确定性与风险转移理论，识别金融骗局模式、分析收割机制、提供反收割策略；当用户需要判断投资是否靠谱、分析骗局原理或学习如何避免被收割时使用 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thinkbugs](https://clawhub.ai/user/thinkbugs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to assess financial or crypto-related scam patterns, analyze risk-transfer mechanisms, and generate anti-scam decision guidance. It also provides local Python scripts for risk scoring, liquidity analysis, Kelly criterion sizing, risk metrics, decision matrices, data processing, and visualization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review flags offensive market-control, social-engineering, insider-information, rule-design, leverage, and market-control material. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for education, scam recognition, and defensive analysis; do not use it to manipulate markets, coordinate deception, exploit insider information, or influence others' investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce investment-analysis and risk-scoring outputs that users may mistake for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational decision support and require independent review before any financial, trading, or investment action. <br>
Risk: Several Python utilities process user-provided data and may generate files such as reports or visualizations. <br>
Mitigation: Review local inputs and generated outputs before sharing or relying on them, and run scripts in a controlled local environment. <br>
