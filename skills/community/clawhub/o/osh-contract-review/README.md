# Contract Review

**Platforms:** Claude · Openclaw · Codex
**Domain:** Legal

## Purpose

A conversational contract guide for non-lawyers. Claude walks through any contract step by step in plain language, identifies risk clauses, and gives an overall fairness score — no legal background required.

## When to Use

- Before signing any contract you don't fully understand
- When you want to know what you're agreeing to
- When you suspect a clause might be unfair or unusual

## What It Does

1. Asks for context (contract type, your role)
2. Walks through the contract block by block in plain language
3. Flags missing or ambiguous clauses
4. Lists the Top 3 risks with concrete modification suggestions
5. Gives an overall score (X/10) for fairness and safety
6. Answers follow-up questions in open Q&A

## Note

This skill helps you understand contracts — it does not provide legal advice. For high-stakes contracts, consult a qualified lawyer.
