## Description: <br>
The final publishing layer for anything you write. Paste messy text and get something clear, strong, and ready to send. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIstack](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIstack) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external writers, and developers use this skill to refine drafts, replies, posts, translated English, and notes into clearer publish-ready text while preserving the user's intended meaning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill improves wording but does not verify facts, citations, legal claims, medical claims, financial claims, employment statements, or public statements. <br>
Mitigation: Review final wording and verify high-stakes claims before sending or publishing. <br>
Risk: Editing may accidentally change nuance or intent in sensitive communication. <br>
Mitigation: Compare the Clean, Strong, and Ready versions against the original intent before use. <br>
