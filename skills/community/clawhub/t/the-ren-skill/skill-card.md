## Description: <br>
The Ren Skill helps business owners generate Chinese-language people-assessment guidance, checklists, scorecards, and decision prompts for employee, customer, partner, and competitor scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xtoyun](https://clawhub.ai/user/xtoyun) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business owners, executives, and founders use this skill as a subjective discussion aid for generating interview questions, evaluation checklists, partner/customer risk prompts, and negotiation guidance. It should not be used as the basis for hiring, promotion, firing, partnership, credit, or customer trust decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill encourages using face and body cues for hiring, firing, partnership, and trust decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as subjective discussion aids only; rely on structured interviews, work samples, references, documented performance, and compliant review processes for employment or business screening. <br>
Risk: Interview photos or appearance-based observations can raise consent, privacy, retention, and anti-discrimination concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid collecting or using interview photos unless there is explicit consent, a lawful purpose, retention limits, and anti-discrimination safeguards. <br>
