## Description: <br>
A Chinese AI trading coach that analyzes user-provided candlestick data or market descriptions with the Qiqiao Linglong v4.0 method and returns structured trading guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to request educational trading analysis from K-line data, screenshots, or market descriptions, including trend/range classification, position sizing suggestions, loss-streak management, and structured trade logs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce risky financial suggestions from user-provided market data. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational suggestions, verify market data and assumptions independently, and do not rely on the skill alone before risking money. <br>
