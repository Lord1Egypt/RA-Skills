## Description: <br>
Generates professional cold outreach emails for sales, partnership, or networking purposes with customizable templates and personalization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sales, partnership, business development, and networking users apply this skill to draft personalized cold outreach messages, subject lines, and tone variations for review before sending. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outreach may contain unsupported claims, incorrect personalization details, or unverifiable metrics. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated messages before use and verify claims, metrics, customer names, and personalization details. <br>
Risk: Cold outreach campaigns may be subject to anti-spam, privacy, or consent requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm campaigns comply with applicable anti-spam and privacy rules before sending. <br>
