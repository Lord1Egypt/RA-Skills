## Description: <br>
Helps agents create, optimize, or audit pricing page content and structure for SaaS, subscription, usage-based, enterprise, API, and special-program pricing pages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, product managers, and developers use this skill to produce pricing-page guidance, page structures, plan comparisons, FAQ topics, CTA copy, objection handling, and SEO metadata. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project-context files can contain product, audience, and pricing details that the agent may read to tailor recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review project-context files before using the skill and remove sensitive pricing, customer, or strategy details that should not be used in generated guidance. <br>
Risk: Pricing-page recommendations may be incorrect or unsuitable for a specific business model or market. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated pricing structure, claims, discounts, and FAQ copy with product, sales, finance, and legal stakeholders before publishing. <br>
