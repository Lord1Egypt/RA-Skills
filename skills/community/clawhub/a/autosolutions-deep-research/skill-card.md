## Description: <br>
Conduct deep multi-phase research using parallel subagents and iterative search. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[autosolutionsai-didac](https://clawhub.ai/user/autosolutionsai-didac) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and research-focused agents use this skill to break complex research questions into parallel workstreams, gather web evidence, and synthesize cited reports for competitive intelligence, market research, and comprehensive topic analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Large research runs may consume significant web search, fetch, and subagent runtime. <br>
Mitigation: Scope the research question before execution and time-box parallel research phases. <br>
Risk: Generated reports may include incorrect, low-quality, copyrighted, or access-restricted source use. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources, verify high-impact claims, and check copyright or access constraints before relying on or sharing the report. <br>
