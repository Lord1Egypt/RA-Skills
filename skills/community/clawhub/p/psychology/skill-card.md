## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat educational psychology explanations as diagnosis, treatment, crisis support, or case-specific clinical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses educational, avoid diagnosis or treatment recommendations, and direct users to qualified professionals or emergency resources for clinical or crisis needs. <br>
Risk: Psychology claims, citations, or statistics may be inaccurate, outdated, or overconfident. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to distinguish evidence levels, avoid fabricated citations, and verify important claims against primary sources before relying on them. <br>
