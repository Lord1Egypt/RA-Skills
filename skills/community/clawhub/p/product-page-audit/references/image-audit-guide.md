# Image Audit Guide — Platform & Category Reference

## Platform-Specific Image Requirements

### Amazon
- **Minimum resolution**: 1600x1600px (enables zoom on desktop)
- **Hero image**: Pure white background (#FFFFFF), product fills 85%+ of frame, no text/badges/watermarks
- **Maximum images**: 9 (7 standard + 2 video slots)
- **File format**: JPEG, PNG, or TIFF; sRGB or CMYK color profile
- **Infographic text**: minimum 18pt equivalent for mobile readability
- **Banned elements**: pricing, promotional text ("sale," "best seller"), Amazon logos

### Shopify / DTC Stores
- **Recommended resolution**: 2048x2048px for high-DPI displays
- **Hero image**: Consistent style across collection (white bg OR lifestyle, not mixed)
- **Maximum images**: Platform unlimited; recommended 7-12 for high-consideration products
- **File format**: WebP preferred (40% smaller), JPEG fallback
- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1 for grid consistency; can vary on PDP if theme supports it
- **Performance note**: Compress to <500KB per image for mobile speed

### TikTok Shop
- **Minimum resolution**: 600x600px (lower than other platforms)
- **Hero image**: Clean background, but lifestyle/in-use images perform better in feeds
- **Maximum images**: 9
- **Key difference**: First image appears in feed — must be scroll-stopping
- **Video priority**: TikTok weighs video content higher; include at least 1 product video

### Walmart Marketplace
- **Minimum resolution**: 1000x1000px
- **Hero image**: White or light gray background, product centered
- **Maximum images**: Up to 10
- **Special requirement**: "Rich Media" module for enhanced content (similar to A+ Content)
- **Compliance**: No competitor references, no unverified claims

## Image Type Evaluation Criteria

### Hero Image (Weight: 20/100)
| Criterion | Score 10 | Score 5 | Score 0 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Background | Clean white/contextual per platform | Slightly cluttered but acceptable | Distracting, off-brand, or non-compliant |
| Product fill | 85-95% of frame | 60-84% of frame | <60% or >95% (cropped) |
| Lighting | Even, professional, true-to-color | Acceptable but minor shadows/hotspots | Poor lighting, color cast, amateur quality |
| Sharpness | Tack sharp at full zoom | Acceptable at normal view | Visible blur or compression artifacts |

### Lifestyle Images (Weight: 20/100)
| Criterion | Score 10 | Score 5 | Score 0 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Demographic match | Model/setting matches target audience | Generic lifestyle, loosely relevant | No lifestyle context OR mismatched demographic |
| Use case clarity | Product clearly shown in use, benefit visible | Product visible but use case unclear | Staged/artificial feeling, product secondary |
| Emotional resonance | Aspirational, creates desire to own | Neutral, informational only | Negative associations or confusing context |
| Variety | Multiple distinct scenarios shown | One scenario, one angle | Repetitive or redundant lifestyle shots |

### Infographic Images (Weight: 20/100)
| Criterion | Score 10 | Score 5 | Score 0 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Text readability | All text readable at 375px width (≥16pt equiv) | Most text readable, minor exceptions | Key text unreadable on mobile |
| Information density | Clear focus on 3-5 key features per infographic | Slightly crowded but digestible | Information overload, wall of text |
| Visual hierarchy | Clear focal point, logical reading flow | Somewhat organized | Confusing layout, no clear hierarchy |
| Data accuracy | All claims verified, specific numbers used | General claims, mostly accurate | Vague or potentially misleading claims |

### Scale/Size Reference (Weight: 15/100)
| Criterion | Score 10 | Score 5 | Score 0 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reference object | Common object (hand, coin, ruler, model for wearables) | Dimensions listed but no visual reference | No size context whatsoever |
| Clarity | Immediately conveys actual size | Requires some interpretation | Misleading or confusing scale |

### Comparison/Before-After (Weight: 10/100)
| Criterion | Score 10 | Score 5 | Score 0 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clarity | Obvious difference shown, same conditions | Difference visible but conditions vary | No comparison imagery when applicable |
| Credibility | Realistic results, disclosures if needed | Somewhat exaggerated but plausible | Clearly misleading or impossible claims |

## Mobile Thumbnail Readability Test

To evaluate if image text is mobile-readable:
1. View image at 375px width (iPhone SE viewport)
2. All text should be legible without pinch-to-zoom
3. Key selling points should be identifiable in under 2 seconds
4. Minimum effective text size: 16pt equivalent at thumbnail scale
5. Maximum text elements per image: 5-7 for mobile scanning

## Image Sequence Best Practices

**Optimal order for most product categories:**
1. Hero product shot (clean, professional)
2. Primary lifestyle/in-use image
3. Key feature infographic
4. Secondary lifestyle (different scenario)
5. Size/dimensions/scale reference
6. Material/texture close-up or ingredient detail
7. Packaging/unboxing (sets delivery expectations)
8. Social proof (UGC, review quotes, awards)
9. Bundle/accessory overview (if applicable)

**Category-specific adjustments:**
- **Fashion/Apparel**: Lead with on-model shots, include fit guide, flat-lay for detail
- **Electronics**: Lead with product hero, include UI screenshots, comparison charts
- **Food/Beverage**: Lead with styled beauty shot, include ingredient close-ups, serving suggestions
- **Home/Furniture**: Lead with room context, include dimension diagrams, material close-ups
- **Beauty/Skincare**: Lead with product + texture swatch, include before/after, ingredient callouts
