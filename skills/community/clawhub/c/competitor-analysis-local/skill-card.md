## Description: <br>
Analyzes local competitors from a Google Maps CSV export by comparing ratings, reviews, digital presence, and market positioning without API calls. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gmapsscraper](https://clawhub.ai/user/gmapsscraper) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users, marketers, and local-market analysts use this skill to turn a business CSV export into a competitor analysis report with rankings, market gaps, and positioning recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be invoked for broad market-analysis requests that are not actually local competitor CSV workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants local competitor analysis from a business CSV before applying the workflow. <br>
Risk: The workflow can involve sharing business data and points users without an existing CSV to gmapsscraper.io. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the user to confirm they are comfortable using the CSV source and sharing the relevant business data before proceeding. <br>
