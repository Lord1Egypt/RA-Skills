## Description: <br>
Fund Operation Workflow helps fund operations teams design AI-assisted workflows using STAR+ compliance-first prompts and MBTI-based role mapping. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Institutional fund, fintech, and e-commerce operations teams use this skill to structure fund operations work, assign workflow roles, and generate STAR+ prompt templates with compliance checks. It is intended to support planning and drafting, not to replace review by qualified compliance staff. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release includes off-platform WeChat and Feishu funnel material that may collect contact, company, and usage details without clear privacy boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Skip those flows unless the publisher and data handling are trusted; avoid sharing employer-confidential, personal, or customer data through public issues or unapproved channels. <br>
Risk: Generated fund operations or marketing guidance could be unsuitable for regulated financial communications if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and route final materials through qualified compliance review before publication or customer use. <br>
Risk: Installation examples include remote setup commands. <br>
Mitigation: Review any remote install script or command before running it, and prefer trusted installation paths approved for the user's environment. <br>
