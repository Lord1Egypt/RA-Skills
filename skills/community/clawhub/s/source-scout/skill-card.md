## Description: <br>
Grounds factual answers with web search, reliable citations, and sourced images or figures when useful. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jkobject](https://clawhub.ai/user/jkobject) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use Source Scout to answer factual knowledge questions with web search, cross-checking, citations, and optional sourced imagery. It is intended for science, medicine, history, geography, current facts, and similar questions where provenance improves trust. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Factual questions may be sent to web search tools, which can expose sensitive private context. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using the skill for sensitive private questions or redact private details before asking it to search. <br>
Risk: Downloaded images are external files and may be untrusted until checked. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official or open-license source pages and verify the file type before attaching or opening downloaded images. <br>
