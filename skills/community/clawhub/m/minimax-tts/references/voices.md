## Other Languages

MiniMax supports 40+ languages. To find voice IDs for other languages (Portuguese, Arabic, Russian, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, Turkish, Dutch, Polish, Italian, Indonesian, Malay, Persian, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Hebrew, Romanian, Greek, Czech, Hungarian, Tamil, Afrikaans, etc.), use the Voice Management API:

```bash
curl -s -H "Authorization: Bearer $MINIMAX_API_KEY" \
  "https://api.minimax.io/v1/voices" | jq '.[] | {voice_id, name, language}'
```

Or visit: https://platform.minimax.io/docs/api-reference/voice-management-get
