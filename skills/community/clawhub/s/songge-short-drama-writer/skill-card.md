## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language writing workflow for short-drama and AI manga scripts, including genre selection, story structure, shot breakdowns, prompt-ready character and scene details, and ViFlow-compatible JSON export. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, writers, and agent users can use this skill to draft short-form drama or AI manga concepts, episode structures, scenes, dialogue, hooks, storyboard shots, and ViFlow-style production JSON. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad plot, storyboard, or informal writing requests and produce a full structured export when a lighter response was intended. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the desired deliverable before producing the full workflow, especially whether the user wants only brainstorming, a script draft, storyboard shots, or ViFlow JSON. <br>
Risk: Market, platform, and performance claims in the writing references may become outdated or may not apply to every distribution channel. <br>
Mitigation: Treat market figures and platform heuristics as creative guidance, and ask the user to verify current platform rules before production or commercial release. <br>
