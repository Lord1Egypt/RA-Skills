## Description: <br>
Use when the user is new to a research direction and wants to scan papers to understand branches, representative works, common methods, benchmarks, metrics, saturated areas, and unclear areas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[snake-fan](https://clawhub.ai/user/snake-fan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and research engineers use this skill to map a new research direction by defining the field boundary, selecting seed papers, expanding the literature set, clustering papers, and identifying evidence-backed research opportunities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Online academic discovery may be incomplete when network access is unavailable or sources are limited. <br>
Mitigation: Record each paper's resource, source verification, and discovery source; prioritize user-provided papers when available. <br>
Risk: The workflow writes durable notes and a static report under a workspace directory. <br>
Mitigation: Use the selected workspace root and review generated artifacts before relying on or sharing them. <br>
Risk: Unsupported speculation could be mistaken for a research opportunity. <br>
Mitigation: Require each research opportunity candidate to link to specific paper records and phrase it as a possible research question. <br>
