## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake informational stock-analysis guidance for licensed financial advice or trading authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as research support, verify market data independently, and avoid brokerage credentials or trade authorization unless a separate, clearly scoped tool provides those controls. <br>
