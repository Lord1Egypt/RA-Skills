## Description: <br>
A proactive personal finance manager for AI agents that tracks income and expenses, monitors subscriptions and bills, detects unusual spending, builds monthly budget reports, reminds users of payment due dates, and helps users understand where their money goes every month. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, families, freelancers, and self-employed people use this skill to track income, expenses, budgets, recurring bills, subscriptions, and savings opportunities through natural-language interaction with an agent. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles sensitive personal finance details including receipts, bills, income, budgets, subscriptions, and spending history. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in a private account or device, avoid forwarding documents with unnecessary account identifiers, and confirm how to review, delete, or reset stored finance records before relying on ongoing reminders. <br>
Risk: Incorrect parsing or categorization of receipts, bills, income, or subscriptions could produce misleading budget reports or reminders. <br>
Mitigation: Review parsed entries, correct ambiguous categories, and confirm important payment details before acting on any reminder or report. <br>
Risk: The skill discusses bills and payment timing but is not intended to execute payments or transfers. <br>
Mitigation: Keep payment execution outside the agent workflow and require the user to make any payment directly after reviewing the details. <br>
