## Description: <br>
An automated email marketing campaign manager that guides an agent to design responsive HTML emails, manage lists, send via Resend, and track analytics on a web dashboard. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Joeyzzyy](https://clawhub.ai/user/Joeyzzyy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operators and developers use this skill to guide an OpenClaw agent through branded email campaign creation, recipient list management, Resend-based sending, and campaign analytics review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow depends on a separate companion plugin that can send email and report campaign analytics. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only after reviewing and trusting the claw-kanban plugin and confirming its API access is appropriate for the workspace. <br>
Risk: Mailing lists, Resend keys, dashboard keys, campaign HTML, and engagement analytics may contain sensitive or regulated data. <br>
Mitigation: Use consented recipient lists, verify legal and compliance requirements, protect keys and local data, and preview the full campaign before approving any send. <br>
