## Description: <br>
Ai Perfume Making helps users learn fragrance families, perfumery materials, scent pyramids, scent training, DIY formulation, and perfume appreciation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External fragrance hobbyists, DIY perfume learners, and scent-appreciation users use this skill to ask for introductory perfume-making guidance, material selection, formula structure, scent identification practice, and perfume recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: DIY perfume recipes may omit practical chemical-safety precautions for fragrance materials, solvents, and skin exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Treat guidance as hobby education, use ventilation and PPE, keep ethanol away from flames, review supplier SDS and IFRA limits, avoid eye contact or ingestion, patch test diluted skin products, and store materials away from children and pets. <br>
