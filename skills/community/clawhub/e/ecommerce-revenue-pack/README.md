# E-commerce Revenue Pack

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Publisher:** drew2operations
**Price:** $29
**Category:** E-commerce / Business Intelligence

## What this skill does

Four intelligence and automation workflows for online sellers:

1. **Competitor Price Monitor** — Track up to 5 competitors per product. Get a clear price position report and recommended action (hold / test lower / increase).

2. **Product Research Engine** — Evaluate any product opportunity with a scored report: demand, competition, margin potential, and differentiation. Includes 1-star review mining for your product improvement roadmap.

3. **Profit Margin Calculator** — True net margin accounting for every cost layer: COGS, FBA fees, ad spend, returns, platform fees, packaging. Runs 3 price scenarios automatically.

4. **Abandoned Cart Recovery Sequences** — Full 3-message email + SMS sequences. Includes subject lines, send timing, and objection-handling built for your product type.

## Who it's for
- Shopify store owners
- Amazon FBA sellers
- DTC brands
- Dropshippers evaluating products
- Anyone selling physical products online
