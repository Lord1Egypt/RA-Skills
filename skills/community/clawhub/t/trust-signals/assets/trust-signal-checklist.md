# Trust Signal Quality Checklist

## Above-the-Fold Signals (8 checks)
- [ ] Star rating visible without scroll on mobile (375px)
- [ ] Review count visible (not hidden behind "read reviews" link)
- [ ] Brand name/logo clearly displayed
- [ ] At least one trust badge visible (secure checkout, free returns, certified)
- [ ] Hero image is professional and shows product in use
- [ ] Price is presented with confidence framing (no excessive discounting language)
- [ ] Primary CTA button ("Add to Cart", "Buy Now") is prominent and clear
- [ ] No broken images or placeholder text visible

## Social Proof (8 checks)
- [ ] 25+ reviews present (platform visibility threshold)
- [ ] Average star rating 4.0+ displayed
- [ ] Photo or video reviews present and displayed early in the review section
- [ ] Most recent review is within 60 days
- [ ] Brand has responded to at least negative reviews
- [ ] "Verified purchase" badges visible (Amazon) or "Verified buyer" (Shopify)
- [ ] UGC or influencer content referenced in listing or gallery
- [ ] Press mention or media logo displayed (if available)

## Guarantee & Returns (6 checks)
- [ ] Guarantee is specific: exact timeframe + refund type + process
- [ ] Guarantee is visible on product page without clicking away
- [ ] Return shipping cost clearly stated (free vs. buyer pays)
- [ ] Return process is simple (one-step: email/form — not "call and wait")
- [ ] Guarantee applies to all customers (no exclusions buried in fine print)
- [ ] Post-purchase: confirmation email references guarantee/return process

## Certifications & Credentials (6 checks)
- [ ] All claimed certifications are genuine and current
- [ ] Certification badges linked to verifiable registry/certificate
- [ ] Certifications appropriate to product category displayed
- [ ] No certifications claimed that are not held
- [ ] Certifications visible in product images and description
- [ ] FDA disclaimer present for supplements/food (if applicable)

## Payment & Security (5 checks)
- [ ] SSL padlock and "secure checkout" visible near CTA
- [ ] Accepted payment logos shown (Visa, MC, PayPal, Apple Pay minimum)
- [ ] BNPL option present for items >$50 (Klarna, Afterpay, Shop Pay)
- [ ] No unexpected fees revealed at checkout (price shown = price paid)
- [ ] 3D Secure / fraud protection indicator shown for high-value items

## Brand Authority (5 checks)
- [ ] About page exists with founding story and team (for DTC)
- [ ] Active social media profiles linked and recently posted
- [ ] Physical address or country of origin visible
- [ ] Customer service contact method listed (email/chat/phone)
- [ ] Business registration / legal entity visible (for high-ticket B2B)

## Mobile Review (4 checks)
- [ ] All trust badges visible on 375px viewport without horizontal scroll
- [ ] Review section accessible within 2 scrolls on mobile
- [ ] Guarantee copy visible within 3 scrolls of page load on mobile
- [ ] Payment logos visible at or near mobile checkout button
