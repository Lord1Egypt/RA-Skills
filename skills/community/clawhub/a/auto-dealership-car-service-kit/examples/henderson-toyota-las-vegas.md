## Compliance Notes (Nevada-Specific)
- Nevada NRS 482.3175: All advertised prices must include all charges except licensing, registration, and taxes. "Plus tax, license, and registration" required on every price mention.
- Nevada DMV: Sale prices require expiration date. Stock numbers required on all specific vehicle advertisements.
- FTC Used Car Rule: Buyers Guide required on all pre-owned vehicles — reference in all CPO advertising.
- Reg Z: APR 1.9% triggers full disclosure: "1.9% APR for 60 months with approved credit, $X down, [LENDER], on select models." Include in email footer and ad descriptions.
- FTC: "No-haggle pricing" is acceptable if consistently applied. Cannot be used for specific vehicles where negotiation occurs.
