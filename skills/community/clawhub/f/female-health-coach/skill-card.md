## Description: <br>
Female Health Coach provides lightweight wellness coaching for office workers covering muscle gain, fat loss, diet planning, short exercise plans, and basic stress support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Office workers use this skill for general wellness planning, including practical high-protein meal ideas, 15-40 minute exercise plans, menstrual-cycle-aware intensity adjustments when provided, and basic stress management guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake general wellness guidance for medical, nutrition, or mental health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general lifestyle guidance and consult a qualified professional for serious physical or mental health concerns. <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive health details while asking for tailored coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive medical details that are not needed for general wellness planning. <br>
