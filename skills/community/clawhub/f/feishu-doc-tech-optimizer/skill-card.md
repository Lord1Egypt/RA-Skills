## Description: <br>
Optimizes Feishu document content in place into a rigorous technical documentation style aligned with RFC, OpenAPI, Alibaba Cloud, and Huawei Cloud documentation conventions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[forrestIsRunning](https://clawhub.ai/user/forrestIsRunning) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Technical writers, product teams, and engineers use this skill to rewrite selected Feishu documents into complete, structured, formal technical documentation. It is intended for direct in-place optimization of existing document content rather than creating new documents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Direct in-place rewriting can replace selected Feishu document content with an unwanted or inaccurate version. <br>
Mitigation: Use Feishu document version history or a separate backup before running the skill, then review the final rewritten content before sharing or relying on it. <br>
