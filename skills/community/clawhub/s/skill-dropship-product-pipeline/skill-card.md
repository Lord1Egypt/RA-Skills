## Description: <br>
End-to-end dropship product lifecycle pipeline from CJ Dropshipping sourcing through margin checks, AI hero image generation, WooCommerce publishing, and CJ supplier mapping. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators and developers use this skill to source dropshipping products, check margins, generate product imagery, publish WooCommerce listings, and prepare CJ supplier mappings for fulfillment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can publish live WooCommerce products using powerful store and supplier credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Use a staging WooCommerce store first, run dry-run before live actions, and use least-privilege or disposable API credentials. <br>
Risk: The actual pipeline implementation is not included in the reviewed artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the pipeline.js implementation before installation or execution, and scan it before connecting production services. <br>
Risk: Incorrect product data, margin calculations, or CJ supplier mappings could affect storefront accuracy and fulfillment. <br>
Mitigation: Manually confirm product details, pricing, margins, and supplier mappings before publishing or enabling auto-fulfillment. <br>
