## Description: <br>
Assistente especializado em Direito Eleitoral brasileiro que responde dúvidas para WhatsApp usando uma base estruturada de documentos de referência. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[iajonathangam2-creator](https://clawhub.ai/user/iajonathangam2-creator) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to ask concise questions about Brazilian electoral law, including candidacy, voting, electoral crimes, party membership, campaign finance, electoral registration, and party procedures. The skill is designed to answer in Portuguese for WhatsApp-style conversations, cite legal bases from its reference set, and include an AI-generated-content disclaimer. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Brazil <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reference files include prompt-style role changes and memory-like directions that could steer the assistant beyond concise legal Q&A. <br>
Mitigation: Review and clean the reference files before deployment so they function as source material only, removing role-changing and persistent-learning instructions. <br>
Risk: Legal answers may overstate authority, rely on outdated electoral rules, or request sensitive voter data. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the AI disclaimer, require users to verify current legislation, and limit collection or repetition of personal electoral data. <br>
