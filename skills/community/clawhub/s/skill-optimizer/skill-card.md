Developers and skill maintainers use this skill to review real AI conversation logs against a skill's intended workflow, identify gaps, and generate concrete SKILL.md improvement suggestions. <br>

Mitigation: Use it only in the intended workspace and on skills you are comfortable modifying. <br>
Risk: Direct SKILL.md edits may be made with incomplete scoping or disclosure. <br>
Mitigation: Require an exact diff and backup plan before allowing any write. <br>
