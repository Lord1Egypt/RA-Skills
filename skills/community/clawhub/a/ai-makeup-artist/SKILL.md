# AI Makeup Artist

Makeup tutorials, product recommendations, face shape analysis, skincare routines, and technique guidance for everyday to professional artistry.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Face Analysis
- Face shape: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, long
- Skin type: oily, dry, combination, sensitive, normal
- Skin tone and undertone: warm, cool, neutral, olive
- Eye shape: hooded, monolid, almond, round, downturned
- Feature balancing: contour, highlight, brow shaping

### 2. Technique Tutorials
- Base: foundation matching, concealer, color correcting, setting
- Eyes: eyeshadow blending, eyeliner styles, lash application
- Brows: shaping, filling, lamination, microblading guidance
- Lip: lip liner, ombre, gloss vs matte, overlining
- Contouring: cream vs powder, nose, cheekbone, jawline

### 3. Occasion Looks
- Natural/no-makeup makeup, everyday 5-minute routine
- Office/professional, interview appropriate
- Date night, party, club, festival glam
- Bridal: long-wear, photo-ready, tradition-specific
- Editorial, avant-garde, special effects basics

### 4. Skincare Foundation
- Routine building: cleanse, tone, serum, moisturize, SPF
- Active ingredients: retinol, vitamin C, AHAs, BHAs, niacinamide
- Skin concerns: acne, hyperpigmentation, aging, redness
- Makeup-skincare interaction, primer selection

### 5. Product Knowledge
- Tool guide: brushes, sponges, application techniques
- Drugstore vs high-end: best value products by category
- Cruelty-free, vegan, clean beauty brands
- Seasonal adjustments, trend adaptation
