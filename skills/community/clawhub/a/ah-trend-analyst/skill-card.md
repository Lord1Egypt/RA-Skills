## Use Case: <br>
Business, strategy, product, and research teams use this skill to scan signals, analyze emerging trends, forecast possible futures, and turn trend insights into actionable strategic recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trend forecasts and strategic recommendations may be inaccurate, incomplete, or overstate confidence if signals are weak or unvalidated. <br>
Mitigation: Review source assumptions, validate important signals with current data, and treat generated forecasts as decision support rather than final authority. <br>
