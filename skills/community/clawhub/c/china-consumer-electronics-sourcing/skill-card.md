## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sourcing and market guidance may become stale or incomplete for a specific purchasing decision. <br>
Mitigation: Verify supplier capabilities, certifications, pricing, export controls, and compliance obligations against current sources before contracting. <br>
Risk: The skill provides cluster-level guidance, not due diligence on individual suppliers. <br>
Mitigation: Use independent audits, sample validation, reference checks, and legal review before selecting or paying a supplier. <br>
