# 薪资谈判策略 / Salary Negotiation Strategies

---

## 一、市场行情了解 / Market Research

### 1.1 了解渠道 / Research Channels

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
1. 招聘网站 — BOSS直聘、拉勾
   看同类岗位的薪资范围

2. 脉脉/LinkedIn
   看同级别同学的薪资，参考同龄人水平

3. 猎头
   可以问猎头市场行情，但要核实可信度

4. 同学/朋友
   同行打听，了解不同公司薪资体系
```

<!-- EN -->
```
1. Job boards — LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Indeed
   Check salary ranges for comparable roles

2. LinkedIn / industry networks
   Compare with peers at similar levels

3. Recruiters
   Ask for market data — verify independently

4. Peers / friends
   Benchmark across different companies
```

---

### 1.2 薪资构成 / Compensation Components

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
常见构成：Base（基本工资）、年终奖（通常2-4个月）、股票/期权、福利

计算：月薪 × 月数 = 年包（如：20K × 16 = 32万年包）

谈判要点：
- 优先谈Base（每年调薪基数）
- 年终奖要看公司历史
- 股票要看归属期和行权价
```

<!-- EN -->
```
Components: Base salary, annual bonus (typically 2–4 months), equity/stock options, benefits

Calculation: Monthly base × months = total annual (e.g., $20K × 16 = $320K annual)

Key negotiation points:
- Prioritize base (adjustment base for annual raises)
- Check company's bonus history
- For equity: check vesting schedule and strike price
```

## 二、谈判时机 / When to Negotiate

### 2.1 最佳时机 / Best Timing

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
✅ 好时机：HR主动问薪资预期时、进入offer阶段时、有多家offer比较时
❌ 不好时机：刚面试完就问、面试过程中主动提、还没到谈offer阶段
```

<!-- EN -->
```
✅ Best: When HR asks for your salary expectation, when entering offer stage, when comparing multiple offers
❌ Avoid: Right after an interview, unprompted during interviews, before offer stage
```

---

### 2.2 谈判顺序 / Negotiation Sequence

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
流程：面试通过 → HR沟通 → 聊岗位内容 → 聊团队 → 聊发展 → 聊薪资 → 拿到offer → 谈判

原则：
- 先聊价值，再谈价格
- 让对方先出价
- 不要第一个开口
```

<!-- EN -->
```
Flow: Pass interview → HR outreach → Discuss role → Discuss team → Discuss growth → Discuss salary → Receive offer → Negotiate

Principles:
- Discuss value first, price second
- Let them make the first offer
- Don't be the first to name a number
```

## 三、报价策略 / How to Quote Your Expectation

### 3.1 报价方式 / Quoting Strategies

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
❌ 错误方式：
- 直接报底价："16K可以接受"
- 报区间太宽："12-30K都行"
- 低于市场："有个工作就行"

✅ 正确方式：
- 报合理区间：有上限的区间
- 锚定高位：参考市场行情报高点
- 给对方留空间：不是非涨不可
```

<!-- EN -->
```
❌ Bad approaches:
- Bottom-dwelling: "I can accept $16K" → signals low confidence
- Too wide a range: "$12–30K works for me" → loses all leverage
- Below market: "I just need a job" → undervalues yourself

✅ Good approaches:
- Reasonable range with a ceiling
- Anchor high based on market data
- Leave room for negotiation
```

---

### 3.2 报价示例 / Example Negotiation Scripts

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
场景：市场行情15-25K，你期望20-25K

HR: "你期望多少？"
你: "看岗位JD和我的匹配度，
     我了解到市场行情在20-25K，
     我期望在22K以上"

HR: "我们最多能给到18K"
你: "18K确实和预期有差距，
     但我对贵公司平台很感兴趣，
     能否在18K基础上，
     年终多1个月或者股票多给一些？"
```

<!-- EN -->
```
Scenario: Market range $15–25K, you expect $20–25K

HR: "What are your salary expectations?"
You: "Based on my experience and the market data I've reviewed ($20–25K for this level),
     I'm targeting $22K or above."

HR: "We can only offer $18K"
You: "There's a gap from my expectation, but I'm genuinely interested in this opportunity.
     Could we close the gap through other means — perhaps an extra month of bonus or additional equity?"
```

## 四、谈判话术 / Negotiation Scripts

### 4.1 报预期 / Stating Your Expectation

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
"基于我的经验（X年）和能力，
以及市场行情，我期望年薪XX万，
或者月薪XXK以上。
当然我也希望能和公司长期发展，
具体可以协商。"
```

<!-- EN -->
```
"Based on my X years of experience, my skill set, and current market rates,
I'm targeting an annual package of $XX, or a monthly base of $XXK+.
I'm very interested in growing with the company, and I'm flexible on the specifics."
```

---

### 4.2 对方给低了 / When They Lowball You

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
方式1（谈价值）：
"我了解到这个岗位的市场价在20K以上，
而且我之前有XX经验，和这个岗位很匹配，
能否在薪资上有所调整？"

方式2（谈package）：
"如果Base有压力的话，
能否在其他方面补充？
- 年终奖多发1个月
- 试用期不打折
- 股票/期权
- 签字费"

方式3（以退为进）：
"18K我需要再考虑一下，
能否给我1-2天时间确认？"
```

<!-- EN -->
```
Script 1 (Anchor on value):
"The market rate for this level is $20K+, and with my XX experience that's highly relevant
to this role, I'd like to revisit the number."

Script 2 (Negotiate package):
"If base is constrained, could we close the gap elsewhere?
— An extra month of bonus
— Full pay during probation
— Additional equity grant
— A signing bonus"

Script 3 (Buy time):
"$18K is below my target — could I take a day or two to think it over?"
(This gives you time to leverage other offers.)
```

---

### 4.3 有多家offer / When You Have Multiple Offers

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
"我目前也在看其他机会，
有XX公司给了XXK的offer，整体待遇不错。
我对贵公司平台更感兴趣，
希望能在薪资上有所体现。"
```

<!-- EN -->
```
"I'm also considering another offer at $XXK with solid overall terms.
I'm genuinely more interested in this opportunity, and I'd love to find a way
to make the numbers work on your end."
```

---

### 4.4 对方坚持 / When They Hold Firm

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
"薪资确实达不到预期，我理解公司有薪酬体系限制。
那能否在其他方面给一些补偿？
- 试用期时长
- 试用期工资
- 晋升时间
- 培训机会
- 签字费"
```

<!-- EN -->
```
"I understand you have a compensation structure to maintain.
If the base can't move, could we explore other forms of value?
— Shorter probation period
— Full probation pay
— Faster review timeline
— Training budget
— Signing bonus"
```

## 五、注意事项 / Key Principles

### 5.1 禁忌事项 / Don'ts

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
❌ 只谈钱 — 不要让HR觉得你只看重薪资，要表达对岗位和公司的兴趣
❌ 没有底气 — 谈判要有筹码，但不要虚报offer威胁
❌ 当场答应/拒绝 — "我考虑一下，明天回复您"
❌ 抱怨前公司 — "上家给得太低了" → 会让人觉得你没价值
```

<!-- EN -->
```
❌ Salary-only focus — show genuine interest in the role and company
❌ Bluffing — have real leverage, never fake competing offers
❌ Yes/no on the spot — "Let me think it over and get back to you tomorrow"
❌ Badmouthing past employers — signals low self-worth
```

---

### 5.2 谈判原则 / Do's

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
✅ 价值导向："基于我的能力和贡献，希望得到相应回报"
✅ 有理有据："市场行情是XX，同级岗位是XX，我有XX年经验"
✅ 留有余地："具体可以协商""可以在package上谈"
✅ 见好就收：差不多就接受，不要贪心过头
```

<!-- EN -->
```
✅ Value-based framing: "Based on my contributions, I expect fair compensation"
✅ Data-backed: "Market is $XX for this level, I have X years of experience"
✅ Flexible: "I'm open to discussing the overall package"
✅ Know when to close: Take the deal when it's good enough — don't overplay
```

## 七、常见问题 / Common Situations

### 7.1 HR问上家薪资 / Asked About Current Salary

<!-- ZH-CN -->
```
✅ 回答："上家月薪XXK，年薪XX万"（可以如实说，HR会做背调）

如果不想说："不方便透露具体的数字，我主要看市场行情和岗位匹配度"
```

<!-- EN -->
```
Answer: "My current base is $XXK/month, total package $XX/year" (be honest — they'll verify)

If you'd rather not: "I prefer to focus on market rates and role fit rather than current salary"
```

---

### 7.2 试用期薪资 / Probation Period Pay

<!-- ZH-CN -->
通常试用期不打折（100%）。如果打折：最多8折。
```
谈判话术："能否试用期不打折？或者试用期短一些？"
```

<!-- EN -->
Probation is typically 100% pay. If discounted: max 20% off.
```
Script: "Can the probation period be at full pay? Or could the probation period be shorter?"
```

---

### 7.3 年终奖谈判 / Negotiating Annual Bonus

<!-- ZH-CN -->
通常年终奖2-4个月。如果有压力可以谈：
- 保底年终（比如3个月），写入offer
- 或者用其他方式补偿
```

<!-- EN -->
Typically 2–4 months bonus. If under pressure:
- Request a guaranteed minimum (e.g., 3 months), written into the offer
- Or compensate with other components
```
