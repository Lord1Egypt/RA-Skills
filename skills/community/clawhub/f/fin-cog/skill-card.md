## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial prompts can contain private account details, tax identifiers, statements, or other sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Redact account numbers, tax identifiers, full statements, and unnecessary personal identifiers before sending prompts to CellCog. <br>
Risk: Generated analysis can be mistaken for licensed financial, tax, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research support and have qualified professionals review high-stakes financial, tax, or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a CellCog API key. <br>
Mitigation: Store CELLCOG_API_KEY securely, avoid logging it, and rotate it if it may have been exposed. <br>
