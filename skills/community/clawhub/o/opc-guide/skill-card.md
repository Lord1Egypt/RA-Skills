## Description: <br>
Guides solo founders through validating startup ideas, building a Business Model Canvas, planning 90-day execution, and defining brand positioning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[peadue1858](https://clawhub.ai/user/peadue1858) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders and operators use this skill as a structured startup coach for pressure-testing ideas, deriving an execution plan, and shaping brand positioning. It produces business documents and frameworks rather than code or project scaffolding. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill saves and reuses local planning documents that may contain sensitive customer, pricing, or strategy details. <br>
Mitigation: Review or delete files in ~/opc-guide/ when they contain sensitive information, and ask the agent to confirm before saving if tighter control is needed. <br>
