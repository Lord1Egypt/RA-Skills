Initial release. Drafts a patient-specific anesthesia plan from signalment, today's measured weight, ASA status, procedure, and comorbidities, with weight-based dose ranges for premedication / induction / maintenance / analgesia, AAHA- and ACVAA-aligned monitoring and recovery plans, a pre-calculated emergency-drug worksheet, and a required-equipment checklist for licensed-veterinarian review and sign-off.
