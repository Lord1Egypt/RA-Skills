## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow can involve sensitive face, voice, and script data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only images, text, and voice IDs that the user is authorized to process, and obtain consent for likeness or voice cloning. <br>
Risk: A real implementation would send media and prompts to third-party services. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped API keys, review provider terms, and avoid submitting confidential or regulated content unless the service is approved for that use. <br>
Risk: The current script simulates success and does not create the promised media output. <br>
Mitigation: Verify service job status and actual output files before treating a generated video as complete. <br>
