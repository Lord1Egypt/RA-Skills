## Description: <br>
Generate daily or on-demand medical research briefs for any medical specialty. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnyquest7](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnyquest7) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Clinicians and medical professionals use this skill to receive specialty-specific research updates with concise takeaways, source links, publication dates, and clinical relevance labels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medical summaries and clinical impact labels may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for patient-specific decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify linked source articles and clinical impact labels before relying on the brief, and do not use the output by itself for patient-specific medical decisions. <br>
Risk: Repeat-avoidance history could retain more information than needed. <br>
Mitigation: Keep repeat-avoidance state limited to article links or identifiers. <br>
