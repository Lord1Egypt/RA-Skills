## Description: <br>
Generates academic technology roadmap diagrams from ten classic templates using a research topic or structured content input. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liuwenqi123123](https://clawhub.ai/user/liuwenqi123123) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and proposal authors use this skill to turn a research topic or structured research plan into an editable academic roadmap diagram. It helps choose a template, generate SVG output, and prepare diagram attachments for review or sharing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated diagrams can include unexpected attribution or unrelated visible text, especially with templates 24 and 27. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated diagrams before sharing and edit or regenerate any output that contains unrelated visible text. <br>
Risk: The generator writes output to a user-selected path and may overwrite an existing file. <br>
Mitigation: Choose a new or disposable output path before running the generation command. <br>
