## Description: <br>
Track lab equipment calibration dates and send maintenance reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AIPOCH-AI](https://clawhub.ai/user/AIPOCH-AI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Lab staff, operations teams, and agents that support equipment upkeep use this skill to record calibration dates, list equipment, and check for overdue or upcoming maintenance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Equipment names and locations can reveal sensitive facility or asset information when stored locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive facility or equipment-location details unless local storage at ~/.openclaw/equipment_log.json is acceptable. <br>
