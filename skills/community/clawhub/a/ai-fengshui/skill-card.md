## Description: <br>
Ai Fengshui provides Chinese feng shui guidance for residential, office, and shop layout using traditional concepts such as bagua, five elements, luopan, eight mansions, and flying stars. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can describe a home, room, office, storefront, or floor-plan concern to receive feng shui-oriented layout and decor guidance. The guidance should be treated as cultural or design advice rather than a substitute for professional safety, legal, medical, or financial judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Feng shui guidance may be mistaken for building safety, legal, medical, or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as cultural and design-oriented guidance, and direct users to qualified professionals for safety, legal, medical, or financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may share more private floor-plan, location, or household context than necessary. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the minimum layout details needed and avoid exposing precise addresses or sensitive personal context. <br>
