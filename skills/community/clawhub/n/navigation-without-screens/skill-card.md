## Description: <br>
Physical navigation skills without digital devices. Use when someone wants to learn map reading, compass use, natural navigation, or needs to navigate when technology fails. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to learn and practice physical navigation, including map reading, compass bearings, terrain association, natural navigation, and lost-person protocols when digital navigation is unavailable. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat training guidance as a substitute for emergency services or professional rescue advice during an active emergency. <br>
Mitigation: Prioritize local emergency services, carry emergency communication, and use the skill's lost-person guidance only as support. <br>
Risk: Navigation details such as magnetic declination, map systems, grid references, and emergency numbers vary by location. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current local map systems, declination, and emergency contacts before applying compass or map instructions outdoors. <br>
Risk: Natural indicators and landmarks can be unreliable when used alone. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check map, compass, terrain, and current conditions rather than relying on a single navigation cue. <br>
