## Description: <br>
Research SEO keywords for any topic and generate a complete article brief with primary keyword, secondary keywords, competition analysis, article structure, and SEO recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kryzl19](https://clawhub.ai/user/kryzl19) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content strategists, marketers, and writers use this skill to research keywords, compare competing pages, cluster search intent, and draft article briefs for SEO-focused content planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations and competitor observations may be incomplete, outdated, or based on transient search results. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated brief against current search results and trusted SEO tooling before publishing or making content decisions. <br>
Risk: Keyword and content-planning prompts may include private business strategy or unpublished content plans. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the business context needed for the SEO task and avoid unnecessary private data, consistent with the security guidance. <br>
