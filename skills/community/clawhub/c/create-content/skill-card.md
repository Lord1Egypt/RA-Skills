## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external creators, and operators use this skill to clarify rough ideas, calibrate voice, and draft platform-optimized posts for X and LinkedIn. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The idea-discovery flow can expose private notes, journals, or prior sessions if the agent is given broad access. <br>
Mitigation: Limit the agent to explicit notes, folders, or time ranges and exclude confidential or highly personal material. <br>
Risk: Generated public posts may include claims, examples, numbers, or tone choices that do not match the user's intent. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit drafts carefully before posting, especially factual claims, examples, and platform-specific framing. <br>
