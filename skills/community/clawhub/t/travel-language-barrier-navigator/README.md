# Travel Language Barrier Navigator
Systematic approach to navigating language barriers — from essential phrase cards to emergency communication protocols.
## What it does
- Builds an **essential phrase framework** with phonetic pronunciation cards for transport, food, and emergency situations
- Curates a **communication tool kit** including offline translation apps, visual aids, and cultural gesture guides
- Creates an **emergency communication plan** with pre-translated medical cards, embassy contacts, and escalation scripts
## Example scenarios
**1. 一句英语都不会去曼谷自由行**
> 👤 一句英语都不会，去曼谷自由行5天，能行吗？有什么工具和技巧？
> 🤖 输出曼谷不精通英语自由行工具箱——翻译工具（Google翻译中泰互译+有道翻译官拍照翻译菜单）、出行（Grab打车支持中文地址）、支付（支付宝/微信在7-11等高普及+现金用于夜市）、住宿（Booking/Agoda中文服务酒店）、安全（中国驻泰使馆电话），结论：曼谷对中国人极其友好，完全没问题。

**2. Rural Japan with zero Japanese**
> 👤 I'm traveling to rural Japan with zero Japanese. How do I prepare?
> 🤖 Communication survival kit — essential phrase cards (transport, food, emergency) with phonetic pronunciation, offline translation app setup (Google Translate + camera mode), visual communication aids (picture menu cards), cultural gesture guide (bowing, business cards), and backup communication plan.

**3. Medical situation abroad**
> 👤 I need to handle a medical situation abroad where no one speaks English.
> 🤖 Medical communication protocol — pre-trip medical history card in local language, symptom-description visual aids, translation app medical-mode setup, embassy medical-assistance contact, and escalation script (pharmacy → clinic → hospital with translator service).
