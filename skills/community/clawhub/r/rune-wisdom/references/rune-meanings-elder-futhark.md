# Elder Futhark Rune Meanings: Comprehensive Reference

> Definitive reference for the 24 Elder Futhark runes.
> Merged from RuneDictionary.com deep-dive and skill compilation sources.
> Includes Anglo-Saxon Rune Poem verses, divinatory meanings, mythology, magical applications, talisman use, rituals, rune pairings, and correspondences.
>
> **On Rune Positions:** This file uses the terms "Upright" and "Reversed" following its source traditions. However, the Elder Futhark runes actually have up to **three possible positions** — Direct (прямое), Mirrored (зеркальное), and Inverted (перевёрнутое) — depending on their graphic symmetry. What many sources call "Reversed" may correspond to either Mirrored (conscious stance toward the rune's principle) or Inverted (externally imposed condition), which are fundamentally different.
>
> **Physical basis:** When a rune tile is drawn from a sack, "Upright" (face up, right way) = Direct. "Reversed" (face up, upside down) = Inverted for vertically-symmetric runes (e.g., Mannaz) but actually Mirrored for horizontally-symmetric runes (e.g., Perthro). A face-down tile — where the carving is hidden — may represent the Mirrored position as a conscious withholding of the rune's principle. Bednenko herself notes that the Mirrored position for asymmetrical runes requires intentional inscription rather than emerging from random draw.
>
> For the complete three-position system with per-rune distinctions and full physical mechanics, see [`rune-three-positions-bednenko.md`](rune-three-positions-bednenko.md). For an alternative perspective that distinguishes mirrored from inverted positions with magical applications, see [`raduga-mirror-inverted.md`](raduga-mirror-inverted.md).

## FREYJA'S AETT (First Eight)
### ᚠ Fehu

**Phonetic Value:** F

**Meaning:** Cattle, wealth, power, prosperity, abundance, vitality, creative energy, mobile wealth

**Aett:** Freyja's

**Position in Futhark:** 1

**Anglo-Saxon Rune Poem:**
> Wealth is a comfort to all men; yet must every man bestow it freely, if he wish to gain honour in the sight of the Lord.

**Mythology:**
Fehu is linked to Freyja, the goddess of fertility, love, and wealth. The rune's association with cattle reflects the Norse measurement of wealth in livestock, and its mobile, circulating nature mirrors Freyja's necklace Brísingamen — treasure won through giving. Fehu embodies the sacred cycle of giving and receiving that sustains both material and spiritual prosperity.

**Divinatory Meanings:**
- *Upright:* Fehu signals the flow of abundance and material prosperity into your life. This is a time when your energy and efforts are attracting rewards, but the rune reminds you that wealth must circulate — hoarding leads to stagnation. Fehu encourages you to invest your resources wisely and share generously, for prosperity grows through movement and exchange.
- *Reversed:* Financial loss, missed opportunities, or greed creating blockages. This position warns against materialism and possessiveness, suggesting that clinging too tightly to resources will cause them to slip away. It advises reevaluating your relationship with money and material possessions, and focusing on what truly nourishes you.

**Magical Applications:**
- Attracting wealth and financial abundance
- Increasing vitality and life force energy
- Enhancing creative power and manifestation
- Blessing new ventures and business endeavors
- Amplifying the energy of other prosperity runes

**Talisman Use:**
- **Purpose:** Enhances wealth, prosperity, and creative vitality
- **Recommended crystals:** Citrine, aventurine, amber
- **Placement:** Wallet, cash register, workspace, or worn as jewelry

**Ritual:**
Purpose: To attract abundance and ensure the healthy flow of prosperity.
Materials: Fehu rune token, green or gold candle, offerings of coins or grain.
Light a green or gold candle and place the Fehu rune before it. Arrange coins or grain as offerings around the rune. Chant: "Fehu, flow of fortune, let abundance fill my life." Visualize golden light streaming from the rune into your hands, filling them with the energy of prosperity and the confidence to use wealth wisely. Let the candle burn itself out safely.

#### Prosperity Ritual

To invoke Fehu’s energy for attracting wealth, perform a simple prosperity ritual. What you’ll need: Drawing or token of the Fehu rune Green candle Coins, seeds, shells, or other representation of wealth. Begin by lighting a green candle and placing the Fehu rune in front of it. Offerings such as coins, seeds, or a small bowl of grain can be placed around the rune. Focus on your intention for prosperity and chant an incantation, such as: “Fehu, bring forth abundance and wealth, let prosperity flow and nourish my path.” You can write your own incantation to be more personalized or specific to your financial goal. Visualize the energy of the rune infusing your life with riches and success. Carry the rune with you as a reminder, renewing the ritual as needed.

#### New Beginnings Ritual

For marking the start of a new venture or phase in life, incorporate Fehu into a new beginnings ritual. What you’ll need: Drawing or token of the Fehu rune White candle Paper & pen Meditate on the Fehu rune, focusing on the opportunities and potential ahead. Write down your goals and aspirations, then place the paper beneath the Fehu rune. Light a white candle to symbolize a fresh start and visualize your goals coming to fruition. Don’t just picture the end result, but imagine yourself accomplishing all of the steps that will get you there. Use this meditation and goal-setting practice to align your intentions with the energy of Fehu.

#### Wealth Circulation Ritual

To ensure the balanced flow of wealth and promote generosity, perform a wealth circulation ritual. What you’ll need: Drawing or token of the Fehu rune One green and one gold candle Journal/notebook & pen Begin by lighting a green and gold candle. Place the Fehu rune between them. Reflect on the importance of sharing your blessings and how generosity can attract more abundance. Use a journal to make a list of things you’re grateful for and ways you plan on paying it forward. Make sure these actions are as detailed as possible. Rather than writing “donate to charity”, be specific and write “I will donate $100 to my local animal shelter by next month”. Sit with your eyes closed and visualize the flow of wealth circulating, enriching your life and the lives of others. Incorporate Fehu into daily practices of gratitude and sharing to reinforce this energy.

**Pennick's Interpretation (Магические алфавиты):**
*Feoh as primordial transition to existence, mobile wealth requiring responsibility.*
Первой руной всех рунических алфавитов является Feoh ( также Fehu), главная руна первого атта. Как первый буквенный знак, эта руна имеет значение «скот», передает звук [f]. Такое же значение имеют первые буквы древнееврейского и греческого алфавитов, но они передают другие звуки. Скот в буквальном смысле относится к движимому имуществу. В обществе кочевого или земледельческого типа количество собственного скота было главным признаком благосостояния отдельной особы, семьи, клана. В древней европейской традиции скот представлял собой имущество, способное быть предметом торговой сделки, — в отличие от усадьбы, которая являлась наследуемым имуществом и не подлежала продаже или обмену. Символически руна скота обозначает имущество, которое может продаваться или обмениваться, — собственность, которой можно торговать. Впоследствии, в современном английском, название этой руны появилось в слове fee — «плата».

Традиционное общество во многом зависело от молока, мяса, кожи и рогов коров. Эта сущность коровьей природы отводит ей основополагающую роль в индоевропейской традиции. Норвежский миф о сотворении мира рассказывает о первобыьной корове, называемой Аудхумла, которая тоже участвовала во всеобщем созидании: она лизала кубик кристаллической соли, содержащий прародителя человечества, первоначальное существо Бури.

Согласно теории рун, Feoh олицетворяет тот первоначальный переход к существованию, которому мы все обязаны своим происхождением. В класической средиземноморсой традиции молодой бык также служит отправной точкой. Для древних египтян рога быка символизировали начало всего. Рассуждения Вергилия о рождении человека начинаются словами: Белый бык с позолоченными рогами открывает год», а поклонники Митры утверждали, что бык был первоначалом созидаемого органического мира.

В материальном плане Feoh олицетворяет накопление могущества. Она обозначает способность управлять. Єта сила двоякого рода: физическая (то есть управление стадом) и

экономическая (как следствие испоьзования стада). Этот символ управления заключает в себе еще и ответственность, связанную с собственностью. Владение и управление скотом, как и любое другое проявление дотатка, требует чтобы владелец демонстрировал чувство ответсвенности. Чтобы не понести потерь, требуется надлежащее руководство. Если человек, управляющий стадом, склонен к расточительству или слишком скуп, то вскоре он познает несчастье и нищету. В современном контексте руна Feoh означает деньги., а также способность и возможность достигнуть мирского успеха и благополучия и умение сохранить его. Старые толкования руны предостеригают нас от скупости и ее социальных последствий. Норвежская руническая поема подчеркнуто предупреждает нас, что все беды богатых людей возникают от жадности и зависти. Эти силы могут вызвать раскол в семье, клане или нации, ведущий к общему разорению: «Богатство вызывает раздоры между родственниками, а тем временем волки скрываются в лесных засадах». Подобным образом, под рубрикой первой руны древнеанглийская руническая проема говорит нам, что «Богатство — всякому в утеху, но каждый должен уметь легко отказываться от него, если хочет заслужить милость в глазах Господа».

### ᚢ Uruz

**Phonetic Value:** U

**Meaning:** Aurochs, strength, energy, physical power, primal force, health, manifestation, wild aurochs

**Aett:** Freyja's

**Position in Futhark:** 2

**Anglo-Saxon Rune Poem:**
> The aurochs is proud and has great horns; it is a very savage beast and fights with its horns; a great ranger of the moors, it is a creature of mettle.

**Mythology:**
Uruz is connected to the aurochs, the massive wild ox that roamed the ancient forests of Europe and symbolized untamed primal power. In Norse myth, the aurochs echoes Audhumla, the primeval cow who nourished Ymir and licked the first god, Buri, from the salt of creation. Uruz represents the raw, formative energy from which all things take shape — the driving force behind manifestation itself.

**Divinatory Meanings:**
- *Upright:* Uruz signals a surge of vital energy and the strength to overcome obstacles. This rune suggests you possess the raw power needed to shape your circumstances, but it must be channeled with discipline. Uruz encourages you to draw on your inner reserves of courage and stamina, trusting that your natural vitality will carry you through challenges.
- *Reversed:* Weakness, illness, or misdirected force. This position warns against squandering energy or allowing stubbornness to override wisdom. It may indicate a period of diminished vitality or a situation where strength is being used destructively rather than constructively. It advises rest, recovery, and recalibration of your efforts.

**Magical Applications:**
- Healing and strengthening physical vitality
- Boosting courage and determination
- Breaking through obstacles and resistance
- Enhancing physical endurance and stamina
- Supporting new beginnings and manifestation

**Talisman Use:**
- **Purpose:** Enhances strength, health, and primal vitality
- **Recommended crystals:** Red jasper, garnet, bloodstone
- **Placement:** Worn as jewelry for vitality, or placed in areas requiring strength and endurance

*Additional guidance (RuneDictionary):*
- Uruz can be used as a talisman to boost strength, vitality, and healing. You can buy a rune stone or other item with Uruz to use as a talisman or create your own. Engrave or draw the Uruz rune on the talisman and perform a simple ritual to charge it with your intention. Carry the talisman with you, wear it as jewelry, or place it in a health-related area – like next to your bathroom mirror or on your vitamin case – to boost the effects. Uruz will serve as a reminder of your inner strength and help attract positive energy into your life.

**Ritual:**
Purpose: To draw upon inner strength and vitality when facing physical or emotional challenges.
Materials: Uruz rune token, red or orange candle, offerings of iron or horn.
Light a red or orange candle and place the Uruz rune before it. Place a piece of iron or horn as an offering beside it. Chant: "Uruz, power of the wild, fill me with strength and resolve." Visualize the aurochs standing firm on a windswept moor — feel that unshakable power flowing into your body, fortifying your bones and muscles with primal, resilient energy. Let the candle burn itself out safely.

#### Strength and Vitality Ritual

Use Uruz to enhance your physical strength and vitality with this ritual. If you’re feeling low-energy or otherwise in a funk, this ritual is a good booster. What you’ll need: Drawing or token of the Uruz rune Red Candle Offerings (water, herbs, iron, other items associated with strength and life) Light the red candle and place the Uruz rune in front of it. Surround the rune with your offerings. Allow the candle to safely burn itself out. Wear your Uruz talisman or tuck it somewhere where you can see it throughout the day, reminding you to remain strong until your goal is achieved.

Своевольная сила Ur убеждает, что не может быть низведена до индивидуального уровня, взята под чье-то единоличное управление. В сфере человеческих устремлений Ur передает мощь коллективной воли, «наше» могущество. Магическое влияние Ur приносит удачу, коллективную силу и личный успех, измеряемый общим благополучием».

### ᚦ Thurisaz

**Phonetic Value:** Th

**Meaning:** Giant, thorn, defensive force, disruption, Thor's hammer, reactive power, small hammer, ettins

**Aett:** Freyja's

**Position in Futhark:** 3

**Anglo-Saxon Rune Poem:**
> The thorn is exceedingly sharp, an evil thing for any knight to touch, uncommonly severe on all who sit among them.

**Mythology:**
Thurisaz is linked to the Thurs (giants) of Jotunheim and to Thor, whose hammer Mjolnir serves as both weapon and protector. Like the thorn that guards the rose, Thurisaz embodies the paradox of protection through sharp defense — it wounds those who reach carelessly, yet shields what is precious within. The rune captures the tension between chaotic giant-force and the controlled power of Thor's lightning.

**Divinatory Meanings:**
- *Upright:* Thurisaz indicates a need for defensive awareness and calculated action. A thorny situation or powerful force is at play — approach with caution rather than impulsiveness. This rune advises you to wait and observe before striking, using your protective instincts to discern friend from foe. When the time is right, act decisively.
- *Reversed:* Vulnerability, danger from hidden threats, or reckless aggression. This position warns against letting your guard down or lashing out without thinking. It may indicate that a defensive barrier has been breached or that you are misusing your power. It advises patience and strategic thinking over brute force.

**Magical Applications:**
- Protection against hostile forces and negative energy
- Breaking through barriers and resistance
- Harnessing reactive power for defense
- Creating boundaries and thorny barriers
- Working with Thor's energy for courage and strength

**Talisman Use:**
- **Purpose:** Enhances protection and defensive awareness
- **Recommended crystals:** Black tourmaline, obsidian, onyx
- **Placement:** Entryways, worn as protective jewelry, or carried during confrontational situations

**Ritual:**
Purpose: To create a protective barrier against harmful influences.
Materials: Thurisaz rune token, black or dark red candle, offerings of thorny branches or iron.
Light a black or dark red candle and place the Thurisaz rune before it. Arrange thorny branches or a piece of iron as offerings around the rune. Chant: "Thurisaz, thorn of protection, shield me from harm and malice." Visualize a dense thicket of thorns surrounding you, impenetrable to any hostile force, while you remain safe within. Let the candle burn itself out safely.

To invoke Thurisaz’s energy for safeguarding yourself or your space, perform a simple protection ritual. To use Thurisaz’s energy for catalyzing change and transformation, perform a transformational ritual. Use this ritual when you’re feeling stuck or stagnant. **Phonetic Value:** G

**Meaning:** Gift, exchange, sacrifice, partnership, balance, reciprocity, gratitude, mutual exchange

**Aett:** Freyja's

**Position in Futhark:** 7

**Anglo-Saxon Rune Poem:**
> Generosity brings credit and honour, which support one's dignity; it furnishes help and subsistence to all broken men who are devoid of aught else.

**Mythology:**
Gebo embodies the sacred principle of gift-giving that underpins Norse society and cosmology. The gods themselves gave humanity önd and ódhr — breath and inspiration — and in return, humans offer devotion and sacrifice. The rune also reflects the sacred hospitality and gift-giving customs of Norse culture, and the meeting point between giver and receiver, heaven and earth. Gebo's X-shape represents the intersection where two forces meet in mutual exchange, reflecting the bond between Odin and the rune vitki, between giver and receiver, and the cosmic law that all gifts demand a gift in return.

**Divinatory Meanings:**
- *Upright:* Gebo is a positive sign indicating harmony, generosity, and successful partnerships. This rune can also suggest that you may be receiving a meaningful gift. Gebo encourages you to embrace generosity and to seek out harmonious interactions in all areas of your life. It signals a time of balanced give-and-take, where your efforts to support others will be reciprocated, leading to strengthened relationships and personal fulfillment.
- *Reversed:* Gebo does not have a reversed position in the traditional sense. Instead, it always emphasizes the importance of balance and exchange. However, if Gebo appears in a challenging context, it may suggest issues related to imbalance, selfishness, or a lack of reciprocity. This position prompts you to reflect on areas where you might be giving too much or too little, and to take steps to restore balance.

**Magical Applications:**
- Strengthening partnerships and relationships
- Enhancing generosity and gratitude
- Restoring balance in one-sided situations
- Sealing agreements and vows
- Creating harmonious exchange in all forms

**Talisman Use:**
- **Purpose:** Enhances generosity, balance, and harmonious relationships
- **Recommended crystals:** Rose quartz, green aventurine, jade
- **Placement:** Carried with you or worn; also helpful in your home or workplace where you seek to enhance balance and harmony

**Ritual:**
Purpose: To boost generosity and clear blockages around giving and receiving.
Materials: Gebo rune token, green or pink candle, offerings to represent appreciation (gifts, gratitude list, etc.).
Begin by lighting a green or pink candle and placing the Gebo rune in front of it. Place offerings of small gifts or tokens of appreciation around the rune. Focus on your intention for generosity and chant: "Gebo, bring forth the spirit of giving, let generosity flow from my heart." Visualize the energy of the rune enhancing your ability to give freely and joyfully, fostering a sense of fulfillment and connection. Let the candle burn itself out safely.

> Bliss he enjoys who knows not suffering, sorrow nor anxiety, and has prosperity and happiness and a good enough house.

**Mythology:**
Wunjo is the rune of wish-fulfillment and the joy that comes when all parts of the self are in alignment. It resonates with Freyr, the god of prosperity and pleasure, whose realm is Alfheim — the home of the light elves and a place of beauty and contentment. Connected to the joy of the mead-hall and the bonds of kinship, and to the Vanir gods and the prosperity and pleasure they bring. Wunjo's shape, like a flag atop a pole, signals victory and the achievement of a goal, echoing the banner carried by those who have found their true purpose.

- *Upright:* Wunjo is a highly positive sign indicating joy, harmony, and successful outcomes. It signals a time of peace, well-being, and positive energy, promoting harmonious relationships and personal fulfillment.

This position suggests the need for self-reflection and the identification of areas where negativity or conflict may be blocking your joy. Reversed Wunjo prompts you to address any sources of discord and to take proactive steps to restore harmony and happiness in your life.

**Magical Applications:**

## HEIMDALL'S AETT (Second Eight)

### ᚺ Hagalaz

**Phonetic Value:** H

**Meaning:** Hail, disruption, transformation, natural forces, crystalline structure, pause, hailstorms, upheaval

**Aett:** Heimdall's

**Position in Futhark:** 9

**Anglo-Saxon Rune Poem:**
> Hail is the whitest of grain; it is whirled from the vault of heaven and is tossed about by gusts of wind and then it melts into water.

**Mythology:**
Hagalaz is the rune of Hel, goddess of the underworld, and of Heimdall, the watchman who sees all disruption before it arrives. Like hail that destroys crops only to melt and water the soil, Hagalaz embodies the cosmic principle that destruction and creation are inseparable. The rune's crystalline structure reflects the pattern within chaos — even in upheaval, there is an underlying order waiting to be discovered. Linked to the Norns who weave fate, and to the concept that destruction is necessary for renewal. Hagalaz is the seed of the world tree, containing the complete pattern of creation within its frozen form.

**Divinatory Meanings:**
- *Upright:* When Hagalaz appears upright in a rune reading, it signifies disruption and the potential for transformation. This rune suggests that you may be facing sudden changes or upheavals in your life. Hagalaz encourages you to see these disruptions as opportunities for growth and transformation. It signals a time to let go of outdated structures and to embrace the new possibilities that arise from the chaos. This rune urges you to remain resilient and adaptable in the face of change. Patience will serve you better than stubbornness.
- *Reversed:* Hagalaz does not traditionally have a reversed position, as its meaning inherently involves disruption and transformation. However, if Hagalaz appears in a challenging context, it may suggest resistance to change or difficulty in navigating upheaval. This position prompts you to reflect on areas where you may be clinging to old patterns or resisting necessary transformation. It advises you to release your attachment to the past and to trust in the transformative power of change.

**Magical Applications:**
- Navigating disruptive life changes with resilience
- Breaking old patterns and destructive habits
- Accessing the creative potential within chaos
- Working with crystalline and elemental energies
- Harnessing the transformative power of destruction

**Talisman Use:**
- **Purpose:** Navigates change and harnesses transformative power; reminds of patience during inevitable changes and delays
- **Recommended crystals:** Obsidian, black tourmaline, smoky quartz
- **Placement:** Especially powerful worn as jewelry; also helpful on a day planner or workspace as a reminder of adaptability

**Ritual:**
Purpose: To break old patterns and make way for new beginnings; especially useful for breaking bad habits.
Materials: Hagalaz rune token, white or silver candle, offerings that represent the pattern you'd like to break.
Light a white or silver candle to represent clarity and new beginnings, and place the Hagalaz rune in front of it. *Haegl as the primal seed-form of cosmic pattern, hail as destructive and creative force.*
В начале второго атта идет Hagal, или Haegl, руна с фонетическим [x]. Название буквально означает hail (град), ту замерзшую воду, которая падет с неба в гранулированной форме. Град является водой, превращенной в лед на короткое время, в течении которого он может падать с неба, уничтожая урожай или имущество. Нанеся подобный ущерб, он тает, основа превращаясь в безобидную или даже благодатную жидкость. Символически Hagal является ледяным первичным зерном структуры и трансформации, смоделированным в соответствии с первоначальной священной геометрией, формы которой заложены в глубинах Вселенной. Кроме того, Hagal, конечно же представляет собой стихию льда — пятую стихию Северной Традиции.

Hagal ассоциируется с божественными покровителями путей, которые связывают нашу Среднюю Землю с Верхним Миром через Радужный мост, Бифрест; и Модгуд — богиня, которая охраняет мост, ведущий в Нижний Мир. Руна также ассоциируется с Урд, старшей норной, «ведающей о том, что было». Эта руна Самхейна — празднества, которое сегодня известно как канун Дня всех святых.

Девятая руна представляет те модели существования, которые, хотя возникли в прошлом, все еще остаются активными и влияют на настоящее положение вещей. Она означает сильное, пусть даже неуловимое влияние, таким образом символизируя силу эволюции в структуре современного существования. Hagal относится к корню вещей, как на физическим, материальном уровне, так и на временном. Это одна из основных рун Вирда: те модели событий в нашей прошлой жизни, которые формируют настоящее сегодняшнего дня.

Гадатели по рунам часто приписывают Hagal плохое значение, усматривая в ней предвестие жизненной катастрофы, когда она попадает в расклад рун. Негативные аспекты руны могут поступать в виде несчастных случаев или, более распространено, в форме обычно непредвиденного «невезения». Но какими бы они ни были, события, обозначаемые этой руной, могут происходить внезапно и неожиданно. К тому же их нельзя избежать, так как они находятся вне контроля и вмешательства человека. Однако они не являются случайными, так как происходят согласно уже установившимся правилам, аналогично суду. В силу этого результат событий будет беспристрастным и неотвратимым, неподвластным человеческим эмоциям и предпочтениям. Этой стороной Hagal обозначает скорее действие механического процесса, чем результат творчества человека.

Hagal является руной бессознательного ума личности и формотворческого процесса мысли. **Pennick's Interpretation (Магические алфавиты):**
*Ethel as the ancestral homeland and inherited estate, the spiritual DNA of lineage and belonging.*
23-я руна Odal (Odil, Ethil, Ethel). Ее фонетическое значение [о]. В буквальном смысле Odal означает землю предков, в более специфичном — собственность семьи, усадьбу. В фризском языке эта руна называется Eeyen-eerde — «своя земля», «свой край». Древнеанглийская руническая поэма называет ее «домом» и говорит нам: «Все люди любят домашний приют, если они могут благоденствовать там в мире и радоваться частым урожаям». Odal воплощает свойства принадлежности, объединенности, родового наследия и не поддающееся определению, но тем не менее реально существующие фамильные качества, которые передаются из поколение в поколение. На менее личностном уровне эта руна обозначает врожденные свойства чего-либо, его материальное и духовное наследие. Она является символом сопротивления против вмешательства произвольного правления государственных властей; она обозначает целостность, разумное и бережливое пользование ресурсами и свободу индивидуума и клана в пределах естественного закона».
