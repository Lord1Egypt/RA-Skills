## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompt recommendations may be incorrect, misleading, or insufficiently tested for a user's domain. <br>
Mitigation: Validate suggested prompts against real use cases and keep human review for critical applications. <br>
Risk: Troubleshooting guidance could be misused to reframe refused or sensitive requests in harmful or manipulative ways. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only to clarify legitimate intent and avoid applying it to bypass safety refusals or request harmful guidance. <br>
