## Description: <br>
Research HubSpot Community profiles, boards, unanswered threads, leaderboards, and Community Champion opportunities to identify visibility patterns and produce non-spam participation plans, reply drafts, and scorecards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[danielsinewe](https://clawhub.ai/user/danielsinewe) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and community managers use this skill to research HubSpot Community visibility patterns, identify high-quality unanswered-thread opportunities, benchmark contributors, and draft participation plans or replies for human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reply drafts or participation plans could be posted without sufficient human review. <br>
Mitigation: Review all drafted content before posting and do not use the skill to automate posting, liking, upvoting, commenting, or other engagement. <br>
Risk: Community participation could violate HubSpot rules, rate limits, or disclosure expectations if used for spammy or undisclosed AI-assisted engagement. <br>
Mitigation: Respect HubSpot rules and rate limits, avoid automated engagement, verify accuracy, and disclose AI assistance when required. <br>
Risk: Some HubSpot Community pages may require login or otherwise be inaccessible. <br>
Mitigation: Use public sources where available, note blocked data explicitly, and continue with the best supported next actions. <br>
