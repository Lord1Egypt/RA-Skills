## Description: <br>
Calculate RSI for any asset. Use when checking overbought/oversold conditions, spotting divergence signals, comparing multi-timeframe RSI, or setting trading entry/exit alerts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill as a local RSI reference for interpreting RSI values, comparing RSI zones and divergence patterns, and reviewing common trading strategies. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: RSI outputs and buy/sell labels may be mistaken for financial advice or real trading analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as an educational RSI reference, verify actual price data independently, and combine any RSI interpretation with price action, volume, and support or resistance analysis. <br>
Risk: The calculate command presents RSI formulas and examples rather than independently fetching or validating market price data. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on the calculate command for trading decisions unless the underlying price data and calculations are independently provided and checked. <br>
