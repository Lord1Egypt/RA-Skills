## Description: <br>
Corrects Chinese sentences with punctuation-focused errors while preserving meaning and making only minimal edits. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[catchFishCat](https://clawhub.ai/user/catchFishCat) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, editors, writers, and developers use this skill to proofread Chinese text for punctuation mistakes, batch correction, or requested explanations while preserving original meaning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Punctuation or small wording changes may alter tone or intended meaning in important Chinese text. <br>
Mitigation: Review corrected output for accuracy before using it in high-stakes or published materials. <br>
Risk: Ambiguous punctuation cases may lead to over-correction. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve original punctuation when confidence is low and request explanation or clarification for ambiguous edits. <br>
