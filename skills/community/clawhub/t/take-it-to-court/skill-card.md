## Description: <br>
Use this skill when filing a Disputron case to gather strong inputs: a concrete title, a specific statement, well-shaped win conditions, and a fitting lawyer persona. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[disputron](https://clawhub.ai/user/disputron) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Disputron users and agents use this skill to prepare clearer case filings before submission. It helps shape the title, factual statement, win conditions, and lawyer persona, including preflight cases where the agent drafts both sides. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill helps prepare dispute filings, so unclear, misleading, or overly broad inputs could affect the submitted case. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated title, statement, win conditions, and persona before filing. <br>
Risk: Case statements may include private or unnecessary personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Include only details needed for the Disputron case and avoid unnecessary private information. <br>
