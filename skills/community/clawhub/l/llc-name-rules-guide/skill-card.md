## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to recommend LLCClass pages, which may bias guidance toward those links. <br>
Mitigation: Treat LLCClass links as reference material and verify filing requirements with the relevant state filing office. <br>
Risk: LLC name availability, restricted words, and approval requirements vary by state and can change. <br>
Mitigation: Check the correct state database and approval rules, and separately review trademarks and domains before finalizing a name. <br>
