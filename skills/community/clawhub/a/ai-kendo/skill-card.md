AI kendo guide covering stances, footwork, suburi, strikes, shinai and bogu, Nippon Kendo Kata, practice methods, match rules, grading, etiquette, kendo philosophy, and related culture. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners and kendo practitioners use this skill for concise Chinese-language guidance on kendo fundamentals, etiquette, equipment care, match rules, grading topics, and cultural context. It is informational support and not a substitute for supervised dojo instruction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Martial-arts technique advice can be misapplied during physical practice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as informational only and practice contact drills, sparring, and tsuki under qualified dojo supervision with proper protective equipment and maintained weapons. <br>
Risk: Users may rely on the guide as a substitute for local federation, dojo, or instructor rules. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm technique, safety, equipment, grading, and match-rule details with a qualified instructor and the applicable dojo or federation rules before practice. <br>
