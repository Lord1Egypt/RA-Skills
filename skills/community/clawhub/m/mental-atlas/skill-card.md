## Description: <br>
Apply the four-layer learning framework to user-provided material, files, URLs, or domain names to extract representations, schemas, mental models, and explanatory frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Learners, analysts, and domain explorers use this skill to turn pasted text, local files, fetched URLs, or short domain prompts into a structured study framework with concepts, schemas, mental models, disputes, test questions, and a concise compression. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read files or fetch URLs that the user explicitly provides, which can expose private documents, internal URLs, or sensitive material within the conversation. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only material intended for processing, and avoid private documents, internal URLs, or sensitive content unless that processing is intentional. <br>
Risk: The generated framework may compress source material into misleading or incomplete mental models if the input is sparse, biased, or outside the model's reliable knowledge. <br>
Mitigation: Review the distilled concepts, models, disputes, and test questions against the original material or a domain expert before relying on them. <br>
