## Description: <br>
This skill should be used when writing blog posts, articles, or long-form content in the writer's distinctive writing style, producing authentic, opinionated content that matches a direct, conversational voice grounded in personal experience. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to draft blog posts, articles, and thought leadership pieces in a distinctive personal writing style. It is intended for long-form content on AI, productivity, sales, marketing, and technology topics, with review and iteration before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill directs drafts and finalized posts to a fixed Notion database without a clear per-draft confirmation step. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the user controls the target Notion workspace and wants blog content sent there; require explicit confirmation before any Notion write. <br>
Risk: Private notes, proprietary research, or client material could be included in drafts that are sent to Notion. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using sensitive material unless the user has permission to publish it to the configured workspace, and ask the publisher for a local-only mode or configurable database ID. <br>
