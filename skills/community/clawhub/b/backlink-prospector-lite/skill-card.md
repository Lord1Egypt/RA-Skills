## Description: <br>
Free version - find guest post opportunities and draft one outreach email. Covers opportunity discovery and basic pitching. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, SEO, and content teams use this skill to identify up to five guest post prospects for a niche and draft one personalized outreach email for a selected target. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outreach can become spammy, inaccurate, or misaligned with a publication's guidelines if sent without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and personalize the draft before sending, verify target details, and follow each publication's submission rules. <br>
Risk: The skill includes an external upsell link to a paid version. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the external paid product as optional and review the destination before purchase or use. <br>
