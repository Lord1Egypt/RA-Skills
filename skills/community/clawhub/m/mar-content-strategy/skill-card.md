## Description: <br>
Build and execute a content marketing strategy for a solopreneur business. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Solopreneurs and small-business operators use this skill to plan content goals, research audience needs, choose formats and channels, build content calendars, distribute and repurpose content, and measure performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste customer tickets, call transcripts, private community posts, or personal data while researching content topics. <br>
Mitigation: Summarize or anonymize sensitive material before using it with the agent. <br>
Risk: Content plans and recommendations may be inaccurate, off-brand, or misleading if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated strategy, calendar, and messaging before posting or sending content publicly. <br>
