## Description: <br>
Audit a game, feature, level sequence, combat loop, progression curve, onboarding path, event structure, or return-player journey through the lens of Flow theory. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and developers use this skill to diagnose where a game experience drifts into boredom, anxiety, frustration, or pacing breaks and to identify concrete changes that restore challenge-skill alignment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Design recommendations may be applied without validating them against playtest evidence or player telemetry. <br>
Mitigation: Review the audit findings with the design team and validate material changes through playtests, telemetry, or controlled rollout before shipping. <br>
