## Description: <br>
Formats review deliverables with consistent structure for comparable findings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[athola](https://clawhub.ai/user/athola) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and reviewers use this skill to turn completed reviews, analyses, release notes, PR descriptions, or incident summaries into consistent, actionable deliverables with findings, recommendations, evidence, and appendices. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generic formatting requests may activate the skill and change the final wording or structure when another task requires an exact output style. <br>
Mitigation: Review the final deliverable for wording, section structure, and evidence fidelity before sharing it or committing it to documentation. <br>
