## Description: <br>
Assists Chinese-language study conversations about Chapter 7, friendship, from Rowland S. Miller's Intimate Relationships, 6th edition. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners and educators use this skill to review friendship concepts, examples, lifespan changes, social support, shyness, and loneliness from the textbook chapter in Chinese study conversations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Learners may treat educational explanations about shyness or loneliness as psychological care. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as study support only and direct serious mental health or self-harm concerns to appropriate safety and professional resources. <br>
Risk: Study notes may contain textbook interpretation or copyright suitability issues if redistributed. <br>
Mitigation: Verify textbook accuracy and redistribution permissions before publishing or reusing the material outside personal study. <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary sensitive personal relationship details while asking for examples. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid requesting sensitive personal details and keep responses focused on chapter concepts. <br>
