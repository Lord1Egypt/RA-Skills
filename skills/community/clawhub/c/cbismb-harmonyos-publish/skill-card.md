## Description: <br>
Automatically publishes articles to the HarmonyOS section of Zhongxiao Qiye IT Network (cbismb.com) when the user asks to publish to cbismb.com or the HarmonyOS developer community. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[itcastWsy](https://clawhub.ai/user/itcastWsy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and content authors use this skill to prepare and publish HarmonyOS articles on cbismb.com, including title, Markdown body, category, tags, originality, privacy agreement, and comment settings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent may publish an article publicly before the user has fully reviewed the destination account, content, category, tags, privacy agreement, and comment settings. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the logged-in cbismb.com account and require user review of all article fields before clicking publish; use save draft or final confirmation when publication is not intended. <br>
