## Use Case: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payment, transfer, red packet, billing, discount, and authorization guidance may be outdated or inaccurate for a specific financial action. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details in official WeChat Pay channels before taking financial action. <br>
Risk: Users may expose personal information or grant sensitive authorization while following payment guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review authorization prompts carefully and share only information needed for the intended transaction. <br>
