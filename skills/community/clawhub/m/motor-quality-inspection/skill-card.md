## Description: <br>
Generates GJB-based military motor quality inspection procedures and report templates across incoming, process, finished-product, and outgoing inspection workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Quality engineers and motor manufacturing teams use this skill to draft inspection procedures, inspection records, and local report-generation commands for military motor quality workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Inspection thresholds or referenced GJB standards may not match an organization's approved quality procedures. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated procedures against approved internal standards before using them for quality decisions. <br>
Risk: The report generator writes to a user-provided local output filename. <br>
Mitigation: Choose a noncritical output path and review the script before execution. <br>
