## Description: <br>
Helps agents interpret biological research findings, evaluate life science evidence, compare biological hypotheses, and design or critique experiments across biology, genetics, genomics, cell biology, immunology, neuroscience, ecology, and related life science domains. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[agents365-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/agents365-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, researchers, and agent developers use this skill to make biology and life science answers more structured, cautious, and evidence-aware. It is most useful when interpreting experiments, omics results, genotype-phenotype claims, model-system relevance, and mechanism-versus-association questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may make biology-related answers sound more authoritative than the underlying evidence supports if users skip the provided evidence-strength checks. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for clear separation of observation, inference, background knowledge, and uncertainty before using them in research or decision workflows. <br>
Risk: Viewing bundled README content in a renderer that loads remote images may contact GitHub-hosted donation image URLs. <br>
Mitigation: Disable remote image loading or review README rendering behavior in environments where outbound image requests are sensitive. <br>
