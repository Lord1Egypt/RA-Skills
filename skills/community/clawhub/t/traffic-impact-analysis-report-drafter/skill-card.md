## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a transportation engineer or planner wants to draft or review a Traffic Impact Analysis for a proposed development. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Transportation engineers and planners use this skill to collect project inputs, draft scoping materials, and prepare a draft Traffic Impact Analysis report for licensed PE or PTOE review, stamp, and submission. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States by default; international equivalents only when the user names the applicable framework. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A draft report could be mistaken for a licensed engineering submission. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the output labeled as draft-only and require a licensed PE or PTOE to verify, stamp, and submit final materials. <br>
Risk: Project data, count data, or agency materials may be confidential or restricted. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only information the user is authorized to share and avoid unnecessary external searches with confidential material. <br>
Risk: Unsupported traffic counts, LOS results, queue lengths, or agency requirements could mislead a project team. <br>
Mitigation: Do not fabricate engineering values; mark missing software-derived results for PE HCM runs and surface unknowns as blockers or assumptions. <br>
