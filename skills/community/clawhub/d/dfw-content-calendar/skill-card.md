## Description: <br>
Generate 7- or 30-day social media calendars with hooks, body copy, CTAs, and hashtags tailored by niche, audience, and platform in JSON or CSV formats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, content creators, and business operators use this skill to draft ready-to-schedule social media calendars for a chosen niche, audience, tone, and platform. It supports review-friendly human output plus JSON or CSV formats for scheduler and automation workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Calendar prompts may be sent to Anthropic when the local model backend is unavailable or not selected. <br>
Mitigation: Use --demo or --compliance-only for no-API modes, set LLM_BACKEND=local when prompts must stay local, and avoid entering confidential customer data or regulated content unless policy allows external processing. <br>
