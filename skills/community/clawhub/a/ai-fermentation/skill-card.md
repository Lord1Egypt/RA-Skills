## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fermentation and home-brewing guidance can be unsafe if users rely on unverified recipes or ignore sanitation, acidity, salt, temperature, pressure, or spoilage controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use verified recipes and food-safety practices, sanitize equipment, control salt, temperature, acidity, and pressure, and discard uncertain spoilage. <br>
Risk: Alcohol-related fermentation may be subject to local legal restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Check and follow applicable local alcohol laws before brewing or fermenting alcoholic products. <br>
