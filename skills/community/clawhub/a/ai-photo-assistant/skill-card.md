## Description: <br>
AI写真助手 is an AI photo studio customer-service assistant that explains the service, answers questions, recommends photo styles, checks the listed price, and guides users toward ordering. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to provide AI photo service information, answer customer questions, recommend photo styles, and direct customers to the AI photo ordering flow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses a DeepSeek API key and sends user questions to DeepSeek. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if DeepSeek credential use is acceptable, and avoid entering sensitive personal information. <br>
Risk: The skill directs users to an external AI photo ordering site for photo upload and payment. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the destination site before uploading photos or paying. <br>
