## Description: <br>
Analyzes three-step checkout abandonment by exit step, classifies shopper resistance, and generates tailored recovery tactics plus a mandatory three-part abandoned-checkout email sequence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rijoyai](https://clawhub.ai/user/rijoyai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce operators, growth marketers, and checkout optimization teams use this skill to diagnose checkout drop-off by step and create step-specific recovery tactics, including compliant abandoned-checkout email skeletons. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated urgency or final-send copy could imply false deadlines, invalid offers, or deceptive scarcity if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated email against actual promotion dates, inventory facts, shipping terms, and internal marketing-compliance rules before sending. <br>
Risk: Abandoned-checkout outreach may omit required unsubscribe, consent, frequency, or localization requirements for a merchant's market. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm opt-in status, unsubscribe or preference language, local legal requirements, and campaign frequency caps before customer use. <br>
