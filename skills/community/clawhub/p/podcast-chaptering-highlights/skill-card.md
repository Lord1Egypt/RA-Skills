## Description: <br>
Create chapters, highlights, and show notes from podcast audio or transcripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codedao12](https://clawhub.ai/user/codedao12) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Podcast producers, editors, and agents use this skill to turn episode audio or transcripts into chapter markers, highlight clip suggestions, show notes, and optional social post drafts without publishing or distribution actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Podcast audio or transcripts may contain private, sensitive, or unpublished content. <br>
Mitigation: Treat audio content as private, avoid sharing raw audio beyond the workspace, and confirm what content leaves the workspace before using external transcription, audio-analysis, webhook, or hosting integrations. <br>
Risk: Generated chapters, highlights, show notes, or social posts could be mistaken for publication-ready material. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for draft-only analysis and require human review before any publishing or distribution step. <br>
Risk: Highlight excerpts may raise rights or consent concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm rights to publish excerpts and avoid use when rights or consent are unclear. <br>
