## Description: <br>
Helps agents explain and optimize for SERP features such as PAA, sitelinks, rich results, zero-click results, and AI search summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, SEO practitioners, and content teams use this skill to assess which SERP features appear for target keywords, estimate zero-click and CTR impact, and prioritize optimization work across schema, content structure, and related SEO workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations could be applied directly to live sites, CMS workflows, schema deployment, or publishing workflows without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations and any related skills before granting CMS access, deploying schema, changing live pages, or publishing content. <br>
Risk: SERP feature availability, AI search behavior, and CTR impact can change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Validate important recommendations against current search results and platform documentation before making material SEO decisions. <br>
