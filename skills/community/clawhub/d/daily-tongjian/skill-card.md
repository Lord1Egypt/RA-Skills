## Description: <br>
Daily Tongjian guides agents through a progressive bilingual reading of Zizhi Tongjian, generating full lecture text, scene art, voice narration, and locally tracked reading progress. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yumyumtum](https://clawhub.ai/user/yumyumtum) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to run a daily historical lecture series for Zizhi Tongjian in Chinese or English. It produces a complete lecture, optional scene imagery and narration, and progress updates for continued reading. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate image and voice deliverables by default when the user has not constrained the requested output. <br>
Mitigation: Ask explicitly for text-only output when image or voice generation is not wanted. <br>
Risk: The skill stores local reading progress and language preference in ~/.openclaw/workspace/daily-tongjian/progress.json. <br>
Mitigation: Review, reset, or delete the progress file when local state should be cleared. <br>
Risk: Lecture text is generated by the agent from its knowledge of Zizhi Tongjian rather than from a bundled full-text source. <br>
Mitigation: Review important quotations, translations, and historical claims against a trusted edition before using them as reference material. <br>
