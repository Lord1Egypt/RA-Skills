## Description: <br>
Turn vague tattoo ideas into stronger AI-ready prompts by combining motif, style, placement, scale, and mood into one clearer instruction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alpha777778](https://clawhub.ai/user/alpha777778) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to refine rough tattoo concepts into renderable prompts for image generation, placement comparison, prompt iteration, or discussion with a tattoo artist. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tattoo ideas can include personal creative input or sensitive symbolism. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid adding unnecessary sensitive details and keep prompts focused on visual elements needed for the tattoo concept. <br>
Risk: Generated tattoo concepts may be visually plausible but unsuitable for permanent body art. <br>
Mitigation: Review any generated concept with a qualified tattoo artist before using it for real tattoo planning. <br>
