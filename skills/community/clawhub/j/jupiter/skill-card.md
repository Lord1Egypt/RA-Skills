## Description: <br>
Compute the best path when multiple choices compete, including vendors, investments, execution options, and strategic decisions where the goal is best-fit path selection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIsearch](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIsearch) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Operators, founders, and decision-makers use Jupiter to compare several viable options, rank routes against objectives and constraints, expose hidden tradeoffs, and choose a best path with a fallback. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation phrases may cause the skill to appear in unrelated best-option or routing conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Review its advice before acting on it, and narrow or disable triggers if it activates outside intended option-comparison workflows. <br>
Risk: Decision rankings can be misleading when objectives, constraints, or risk tolerance are underspecified. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify the objective function and decision constraints before accepting a ranking, and treat unknown inputs as reasons to validate rather than assume. <br>
Risk: The skill can discuss investment, legal, tax, procurement, or fiduciary choices but is not a substitute for regulated professional approval. <br>
Mitigation: Use its output as structured comparison guidance only, and obtain qualified review before acting in regulated or fiduciary contexts. <br>
