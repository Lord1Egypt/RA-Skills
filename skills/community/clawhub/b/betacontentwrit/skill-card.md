## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill generates outline templates rather than complete, sourced content, so readers may overstate the output as researched or final. <br>
Mitigation: Review each outline, add independent sources, and label generated material as draft content before publication. <br>
Risk: Finance and cryptocurrency topics can be mistaken for investment advice if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Apply editorial and compliance review before using market commentary in external communications. <br>
