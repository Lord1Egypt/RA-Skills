## Description: <br>
Intent Translation Skill helps users clarify vague ideas through guided questions and turn them into structured prompts that another AI can execute. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wesley138cn](https://clawhub.ai/user/wesley138cn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
AI users, creatives, learners, and decision-makers use this skill to clarify unclear intentions before asking another AI for help. It is especially suited for Chinese-language prompt preparation across creative, career, communication, equity, and learning scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include private names, financial details, workplace conflicts, relationship details, or other sensitive context while preparing prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive information unless it is appropriate to reuse in another AI service; redact or generalize personal and confidential details before copying generated prompts elsewhere. <br>
Risk: Generated prompts can ask another AI for legal, financial, career, equity, or relationship guidance that may be incomplete or inappropriate for the user's situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as drafting help and review important decisions with qualified professionals or trusted human reviewers before acting. <br>
