## Description: <br>
Find papers and preprints that cite a given URL, especially blogs, docs pages, project pages, or other web content that standard citation indexes often miss. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zjsxply](https://clawhub.ai/user/zjsxply) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and researchers use this skill to find and verify papers or preprints that cite a specific web URL. It is suited for citation reports, DOI or arXiv link collection, BibTeX output, and separating confirmed citations from unverified candidates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Citation reports may be misleading if search results or candidate pages are treated as proof without checking the cited work. <br>
Mitigation: Review the evidence links and rely on citations confirmed in full text, metadata, PDF text, or PDF link annotations before using the report. <br>
