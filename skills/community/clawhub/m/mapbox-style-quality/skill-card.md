## Description: <br>
Expert guidance on validating, optimizing, and ensuring quality of Mapbox styles through validation, accessibility checks, and optimization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mapbox](https://clawhub.ai/user/mapbox) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to prepare Mapbox styles for production, debug style quality issues, validate accessibility and GeoJSON inputs, compare style changes, and optimize style performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may recommend optimized style changes that affect rendering, accessibility, or performance behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Review optimization reports, test optimized styles, and verify changes before committing or deploying. <br>
Risk: The skill names external tools and npm scripts but does not provide their implementations. <br>
Mitigation: Verify any external tool or script before running it in a development, CI, or production environment. <br>
