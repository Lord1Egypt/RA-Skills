## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common customer support questions for support teams and customer success managers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobeyrebecca](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobeyrebecca) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Support teams and customer success managers use this skill to draft and refine customer-facing FAQ answers for support pages and customer help workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ answers may be inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable for customer-facing publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit generated content for accuracy, readability, and policy fit before publishing. <br>
Risk: Prompts or enrichment queries could include customer PII, secrets, or other sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering PII, secrets, or confidential customer details into prompts or search queries. <br>
Risk: Web search enrichment may send search queries to external services. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive search terms and review externally sourced material before including it in FAQ content. <br>
