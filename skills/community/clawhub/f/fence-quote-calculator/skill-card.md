## Description: <br>
Calculate material takeoffs and quotes for wood, vinyl, and aluminum fence projects, including post counts, picket quantities, concrete bags, gate hardware, and total material costs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[reesepj](https://clawhub.ai/user/reesepj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Fence estimators and project operators use this skill to turn project dimensions, fence type, corners, and gate details into material takeoffs for wood, vinyl, and aluminum fence jobs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Material counts or quote assumptions may be inaccurate for local code requirements, supplier specifications, waste factors, or current material prices. <br>
Mitigation: Verify formulas, layout details, local requirements, waste factors, and prices before using the output for a real fence quote. <br>
