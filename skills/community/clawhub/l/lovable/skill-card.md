## Use Case: <br>
External developers and hackathon participants use this skill to plan and build clinical Lovable prototypes such as patient intake forms, assessments, dashboards, and PHI-aware MVP workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Clinical prototypes may expose PHI if real patient data is used in prompts, logs, or unapproved environments. <br>
Mitigation: Use synthetic data unless approved, and keep real data only in a compliant database and runtime with minimal logging. <br>
Risk: Apps handling patient-specific data can expose sensitive records without authentication, access controls, and retention policies. <br>
Mitigation: Add authentication early, apply least-privilege access controls, and define retention before using sensitive data. <br>
