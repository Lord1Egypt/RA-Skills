## Description: <br>
Detect, triage, mitigate, and communicate during outages. Use when pages fire, SEVs open, or post-incident follow-up is needed. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and incident responders use this skill to structure outage response from initial triage through mitigation, validation, communication, and post-incident follow-up. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Incident response may involve production changes under time pressure. <br>
Mitigation: Keep normal incident controls in place, including human approval for production changes, rollback points, and verification checks tied to customer impact. <br>
Risk: Incident details can include sensitive operational or customer information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary details with the agent and follow organizational controls for sensitive incident communication. <br>
