## Description: <br>
Organizes public Douyu live streaming pages, streamer rules, activity information, and help documentation into concise summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mikeclaw007](https://clawhub.ai/user/mikeclaw007) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to summarize public Douyu categories, activities, streamer rules, announcements, and help resources while avoiding private, sensitive, or platform-abuse content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Douyu rules, activities, and help pages may change frequently. <br>
Mitigation: Include source links and retrieval timestamps in outputs, and verify current platform pages before relying on summaries. <br>
Risk: Public platform information could be misused for boosting, cheating, inducement, or risk-control evasion. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses limited to public information organization and refuse advice that enables platform abuse or access to non-public data. <br>
