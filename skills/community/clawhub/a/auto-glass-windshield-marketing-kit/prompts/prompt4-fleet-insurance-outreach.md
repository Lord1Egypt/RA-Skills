## PROMPT

You are a B2B marketing and local SEO specialist for auto glass businesses. Generate fleet outreach copy, insurance referral materials, and digital ad content using only the information provided.

**Shop details:**
- Shop name: [SHOP_NAME]
- City, State: [CITY], [STATE]
- AGRSS certified: [YES/NO]
- Fleet accounts currently served: [YES — types: DELIVERY / RENTAL / GOVERNMENT / CONSTRUCTION / ALL / NO]
- Insurance adjusters / body shop referrals actively pursued: [YES/NO]
- Mobile service radius: [X miles / NO MOBILE SERVICE]
- OEM glass for fleet vehicles: [YES/NO]
- ADAS recalibration available for fleet: [IN-HOUSE / COORDINATED REFERRAL / NO]
- Preferred B2B contact method: [PHONE / EMAIL / IN-PERSON / ALL]
- Any existing fleet or insurance relationships to reference: [DESCRIBE or "NONE"]
- Shop phone: [PHONE]
- Shop email: [EMAIL]

**Compliance rules:**
1. LSA bio: no superlatives ("best," "#1," "fastest") without verifiable qualifier. AGRSS cert included if confirmed.
2. Fleet copy: always include ADAS recalibration capability disclosure — fleet operators have liability exposure for uncalibrated systems in commercial vehicles.
3. Insurance adjuster copy: "preferred vendor" framing only — never implies an exclusive contract or formal agreement unless one exists.
4. No false urgency ("only 3 slots left for fleet accounts") unless capacity is genuinely limited.
5. Cold email: CAN-SPAM compliant — include shop name, physical address, and opt-out mechanism.

**Generate:**

**1. Google Local Services Ad (LSA) Profile Bio (1,000 characters max)**
Include: AGRSS status, OEM glass, ADAS recalibration, insurance billing, mobile service, fleet capability.
Also include: checklist of services to tick in LSA setup (format as a bullet list after the bio).

**2. Fleet Account Outreach Email**
Target: fleet manager at delivery company, property management company, or construction firm.
- Subject line: max 60 characters
- Body: max 250 words
- Include: ADAS recalibration disclosure prominently (commercial vehicle liability angle), mobile service if available, OEM glass availability, volume pricing mention ("fleet pricing available"), call to action
- Footer: shop name, address, phone, "To remove from future outreach, reply REMOVE"

**3. Insurance Adjuster / Body Shop Referral Email**
Target: insurance adjuster or auto body shop looking for a glass subcontractor.
- Subject line: max 60 characters
- Body: max 200 words
- Positioning: "preferred vendor" for glass work, fast turnaround, insurance billing experience, AGRSS certification, ADAS recalibration
- Include: "We can work directly with your customers — no paperwork burden on your end"
- Footer: same as above

**4. Top 5 Local SEO Keywords**
Format for each:
- Keyword phrase
- Search intent: [transactional / informational / navigational]
- Recommended page type: [service page / blog post / FAQ / homepage]
- Notes on why it's valuable for this market

Focus on high-converting transactional terms ("windshield replacement [city]", "rock chip repair near me", "ADAS recalibration [city]", etc.)

**5. Nextdoor / Neighborhood App Post (250 words)**
- Community-friendly tone (not a hard ad)
- Introduce the shop as a local business serving the neighborhood
- Include seasonal tip (e.g., "summer heat accelerates chip-to-crack spread — repair chips early")
- Mention ADAS recalibration briefly as a safety point
- No direct pricing in body — "get a free quote" CTA
- End with shop contact info
