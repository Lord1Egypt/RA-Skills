## Description: <br>
Prepare for India's Joint Entrance Examination with progress tracking, weak area analysis, mock test strategy, and IIT/NIT targeting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External students, parents, guardians, tutors, and coaching teachers use this skill to plan JEE Main and Advanced preparation, track progress, analyze weak areas, tune mock test strategy, and map expected ranks to IIT/NIT options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may retain study goals, category, exam dates, scores, mistake logs, and feedback in local files under ~/jee/. <br>
Mitigation: Store only details you are comfortable retaining locally, review the ~/jee/ folder periodically, and delete it when the records are no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Rank, cutoff, and college-targeting guidance can become stale or vary by category, year, institute, and counseling round. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's targeting output as planning guidance and verify admissions decisions against official current-year exam and JoSAA sources. <br>
Risk: Wellbeing guidance is supportive and may not be sufficient for severe stress or crisis situations. <br>
Mitigation: Pause academic demands and contact parents, counselors, local emergency services, or appropriate helplines when severe distress is present. <br>
