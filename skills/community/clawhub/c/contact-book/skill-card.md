## Description: <br>
Store personal contacts with tags, search, and relationship tracking. Use when adding contacts, searching details, planning follow-ups. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individuals and teams use this skill as a lightweight local contact book for adding contacts, planning follow-ups, tracking interactions, tagging relationships, searching history, and exporting contact activity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Contact details and notes are stored locally in readable plain-text files, and exports create additional readable copies. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for contact information suitable for local plain-text storage, avoid secrets or highly sensitive notes, and manage exported files carefully. <br>
