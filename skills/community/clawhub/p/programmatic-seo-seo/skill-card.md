## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Programmatic pages can become thin, duplicate, or misleading when pages only swap variables or use weak public data. <br>
Mitigation: Require unique evidence blocks, defensible data tiers, selective indexation, deduplication, and pruning of low-value URLs before publishing at scale. <br>
Risk: AI-assisted copy can introduce unverified claims, fabricated numbers, or unsupported citations. <br>
Mitigation: Ground generation in structured inputs, keep the data layer as source of truth, forbid invented metrics, and review or block pages with missing required fields or citations. <br>
Risk: User-generated, customer, partner, or scraped data can create privacy, consent, licensing, or trademark issues. <br>
Mitigation: Use moderated and consented inputs, document provenance and freshness rules, respect partner contract scope, and prefer product-generated or product-derived data where available. <br>
