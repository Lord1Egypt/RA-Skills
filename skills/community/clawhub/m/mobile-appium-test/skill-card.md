## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Appium relaxed-security mode can expand what the test server is allowed to do. <br>
Mitigation: Bind Appium to localhost and avoid relaxed-security mode unless a specific test requires it temporarily in an isolated test environment. <br>
Risk: Device control, screenshots, and logs may expose personal or sensitive app data. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated test device and test apps, avoid Wi-Fi debugging on untrusted networks, and avoid collecting screenshots or logs from personal or sensitive apps. <br>
