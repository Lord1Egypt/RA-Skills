## Description: <br>
Enhance an AI agent with prompt optimization, ROI-based task ranking, self-correction, and action-plan generation for complex goals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to turn a broad goal into optimized prompts, prioritized actions, ROI-filtered execution decisions, and concise lessons from prior task results. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated plans and ROI scores may be incorrect or incomplete for real-world decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed actions, ROI assumptions, and execution decisions before approving them. <br>
Risk: Prompt or task details may be retained in local logs when the helper code is executed. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets or highly sensitive task details, and review local logs before sharing or publishing outputs. <br>
