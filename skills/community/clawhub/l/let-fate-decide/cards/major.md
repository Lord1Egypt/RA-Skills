# Major Arcana

# The Fool

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: 0

## Upright
New beginnings, innocence, and spontaneity. The Fool steps forward without fear, trusting the journey ahead. This card suggests embracing the unknown and taking a leap of faith.

## Reversed
Recklessness, carelessness, and negligence. The reversed Fool rushes in without thinking, ignoring obvious risks. Naivety becomes a liability rather than a strength.

## In Technical Context
Suggests starting fresh — a greenfield approach or unfamiliar technology. Embrace experimentation, but have a safety net.

---

# The Magician

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: I

## Upright
Manifestation, resourcefulness, and power. All tools are at your disposal. The Magician channels raw potential into tangible results through focused will and skill.

## Reversed
Manipulation, poor planning, and untapped talents. Resources are available but misused or squandered. Trickery masquerading as competence.

## In Technical Context
You have everything you need — the tools, the knowledge, the access. Now focus and execute. Don't go shopping for new frameworks; use what's already there.

---

# The High Priestess

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: II

## Upright
Intuition, sacred knowledge, and the subconscious mind. Something important lies beneath the surface. Trust the quiet inner voice that knows before logic catches up.

## Reversed
Secrets, withdrawal, and disconnection from intuition. Over-reliance on logic while ignoring gut feelings. Information is being withheld or overlooked.

## In Technical Context
There's something in the codebase or requirements you haven't noticed yet. Read the docs again. Check the logs. The answer is already there, waiting to be seen.

---

# The Empress

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: III

## Upright
Abundance, nurturing, and creativity. Growth comes naturally when the conditions are right. Focus on creating a fertile environment rather than forcing outcomes.

## Reversed
Creative block, dependence, and neglect. The garden has been left untended. Over-mothering a project stifles its growth.

## In Technical Context
Invest in developer experience, documentation, and tooling. Create conditions where good work grows naturally. Nurture the ecosystem.

---

# The Emperor

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: IV

## Upright
Authority, structure, and stability. Establish order through clear rules and boundaries. Leadership through firm but fair governance.

## Reversed
Tyranny, rigidity, and excessive control. Rules for rules' sake. The structure has become a cage rather than a scaffold.

## In Technical Context
Add structure: types, schemas, validation, linting rules. But don't over-engineer. The goal is stability, not bureaucracy.

---

# The Hierophant

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: V

## Upright
Tradition, conformity, and established wisdom. Follow the well-trodden path. There's a reason the standard approach exists — learn from those who came before.

## Reversed
Personal beliefs, freedom, and challenging the status quo. The traditional way doesn't fit here. Break from convention with intention.

## In Technical Context
Use the standard library. Follow the convention. Read the docs. The boring, established approach is probably correct here — unless reversed, in which case: innovate deliberately.

---

# The Lovers

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: VI

## Upright
Partnerships, alignment, and meaningful choices. A decision must be made between two paths, and both have merit. Choose with your values, not just logic.

## Reversed
Disharmony, imbalance, and misalignment of values. A partnership or integration isn't working. The choice was made for the wrong reasons.

## In Technical Context
Two approaches both look viable. The right choice depends on values: what matters more — speed or correctness? Simplicity or flexibility? Choose deliberately.

---

# The Chariot

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: VII

## Upright
Determination, willpower, and triumph. Push forward with confidence despite obstacles. Control opposing forces through sheer force of will.

## Reversed
Lack of control, aggression, and directionlessness. Charging ahead without a plan. Effort without aim is just motion.

## In Technical Context
Ship it. Stop deliberating. You know enough to move forward — maintain momentum and course-correct along the way.

---

# Strength

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: VIII

## Upright
Courage, patience, and gentle persuasion. True strength is quiet confidence, not brute force. Tame the chaos with compassion and persistence.

## Reversed
Self-doubt, weakness, and raw emotion. Inner turmoil undermines outward action. Trying to force what requires patience.

## In Technical Context
The gentle approach wins here. Incremental refactoring over big rewrites. Patient debugging over frustrated hacking. Steady progress over heroics.

---

# The Hermit

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: IX

## Upright
Soul-searching, introspection, and inner guidance. Step back from the noise to find clarity. The answer comes in solitude and deep thought.

## Reversed
Isolation, loneliness, and withdrawal. Too much solitude becomes avoidance. Refusing to seek or accept help when it's needed.

## In Technical Context
Take time to think before coding. Read the source. Understand the problem deeply before reaching for a solution. Solo deep-work time is needed here.

---

# Wheel of Fortune

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: X

## Upright
Good luck, karma, and life cycles. Change is coming regardless of action. Ride the wave rather than fighting the current.

## Reversed
Bad luck, resistance to change, and breaking cycles. Fighting inevitable change. Clinging to control in an uncontrollable situation.

## In Technical Context
External factors will influence the outcome regardless of your choice. Design for adaptability. What goes up comes down — build for both states.

---

# Justice

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XI

## Upright
Fairness, truth, and cause and effect. Every action has consequences. Make the decision that is objectively correct, even if it's not the easy one.

## Reversed
Unfairness, dishonesty, and lack of accountability. Cutting corners catches up. Technical debt accrues interest.

## In Technical Context
Do it right. Pay the technical debt. Write the tests. Fix the root cause instead of patching symptoms. Future you will thank present you.

---

# The Hanged Man

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XII

## Upright
Surrender, new perspectives, and letting go. Sometimes you need to stop trying and look at the problem upside down. Pause creates insight.

## Reversed
Stalling, needless sacrifice, and resistance to stillness. Stuck not from wisdom but from fear. Refusing to try a different angle.

## In Technical Context
Stop. You're approaching this wrong. Flip your assumptions. What if the constraint you're designing around isn't actually a constraint?

---

# Death

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XIII

## Upright
Endings, transformation, and necessary transition. Death clears away what no longer serves to make room for what comes next. Something must end so something better can begin.

## Reversed
Resistance to change, stagnation, and inability to let go. Clinging to what is already gone, refusing the transformation. Decay accelerates when you won't let the dead thing die.

## In Technical Context
Kill the feature, deprecate the API, or delete the legacy code. It served its purpose and now it's holding you back. The longer you keep it on life support, the more it costs.

---

# Temperance

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XIV

## Upright
Balance, moderation, and patience. Blend opposing elements carefully. The middle path between extremes leads to something greater than either alone.

## Reversed
Imbalance, excess, and lack of long-term vision. Swinging between extremes rather than finding the center. Mixing things that shouldn't be mixed.

## In Technical Context
Find the middle ground. Not the minimal MVP and not the gold-plated solution — the balanced approach that serves the need without overbuilding.

---

# The Devil

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XV

## Upright
Bondage, materialism, and shadow self. You're trapped by something — but the chains are loose. The constraint you feel is more self-imposed than real.

## Reversed
Release, breaking free, and reclaiming power. The chains fall away. A dependency or constraint you assumed was permanent turns out to be optional.

## In Technical Context
That library/framework/tool you think you're locked into? You're not. The migration cost is lower than the ongoing cost of staying. Or reversed: you just realized you can break free.

---

# The Tower

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XVI

## Upright
Sudden upheaval, revelation, and necessary destruction. The foundation was flawed. Better to tear it down now than let it collapse on someone later.

## Reversed
Avoidance of disaster, fear of change, and delayed inevitable. You sense the tower is unstable but keep adding floors. The fall will be worse for waiting.

## In Technical Context
Something fundamental is wrong with the approach. Scrap it and rebuild rather than patching. Yes, really. The sunk cost is already sunk.

---

# The Star

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XVII

## Upright
Hope, faith, and renewed purpose. After destruction comes renewal. Follow the aspirational vision — the ideal solution is within reach.

## Reversed
Lack of faith, despair, and disconnection. Losing sight of the north star. The ideal has become so distant it feels impossible.

## In Technical Context
Aim high. The clean, elegant solution is achievable. Don't settle for the cynical 'good enough' — the star is showing you what's actually possible.

---

# The Moon

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XVIII

## Upright
Illusion, fear, and the unknown. Things are not as they appear. Trust is unreliable right now. Proceed carefully through the fog.

## Reversed
Release of fear, clarity emerging, and facing truth. The fog lifts. What seemed scary or confusing turns out to be manageable once seen clearly.

## In Technical Context
There's a bug or misunderstanding you haven't found yet. Add logging. Write tests. Don't trust that the happy path is the only path.

---

# The Sun

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XIX

## Upright
Positivity, success, and vitality. Everything is working. This is the green-light card — the approach is sound and the outcome will be good.

## Reversed
Inner child blocked, temporary setback, and over-optimism. Success is there but slightly obscured. Don't let minor setbacks derail genuine progress.

## In Technical Context
Full send. The approach is correct, the vibes are right, and it's going to work. Trust the momentum.

---

# Judgement

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XX

## Upright
Reflection, reckoning, and inner calling. Time to evaluate what's been built and decide what to carry forward. A higher purpose calls.

## Reversed
Self-doubt, refusal to learn, and avoiding self-evaluation. Not learning from past mistakes. Repeating patterns without reflection.

## In Technical Context
Review time. Look at what you've built with fresh eyes. What serves the mission and what's just legacy? Time for a retrospective before the next phase.

---

# The World

**Arcana**: Major | **Number**: XXI

## Upright
Completion, integration, and accomplishment. The cycle is complete. Everything has come together into a unified whole.

## Reversed
Incompletion, shortcuts, and seeking closure. Almost there but not quite. Something is preventing the final integration.

## In Technical Context
Ship it — it's ready. Or if reversed: one more thing needs to come together before this is truly done. Don't rush the final integration.
