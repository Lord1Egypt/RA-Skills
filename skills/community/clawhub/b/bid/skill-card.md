## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language bid-document assistant that explains engineering tender documents, gives proposal-writing guidance, and points users to the 标书魔方 service and community links. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Chinese-speaking proposal and procurement users can ask for bid-document interpretation, bid-writing considerations, and concise summaries of core tender information. The skill may direct users to the 标书魔方 website or a WeCom group for related services and discussion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global; responses are intended for Chinese-language bid and tender workflows. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential tender documents, pricing, credentials, client details, or internal project information with the external website, group, or web search. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with organization-approved material, redact sensitive details, and avoid sending confidential information to external services or groups unless approved. <br>
