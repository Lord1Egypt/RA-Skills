## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Assessment requests can include personal career details and user identifiers sent to an external API provider. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymous or minimal identifiers where possible, avoid sensitive personal details, and review the provider's privacy and retention terms before using real profile data at scale. <br>
