## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share resume and career details while using the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information needed for the requested document and avoid unnecessary personal identifiers or sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Generated resume, cover letter, or LinkedIn text may be inaccurate, overstated, or poorly tailored. <br>
Mitigation: Review and verify all generated career materials against the user's real experience before using them professionally. <br>
