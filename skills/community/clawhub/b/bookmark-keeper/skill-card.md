## Description: <br>
Bookmark Keeper saves, organizes, and searches web bookmarks, research links, plans, tasks, and review notes in local command-line logs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and other command-line users use this skill to collect links, tag resources, track plans and reviews, search local activity history, and export entries for later use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-entered bookmark, productivity, or review text is stored in local plain-text logs. <br>
Mitigation: Do not store passwords, API keys, private personal notes, or sensitive business data; periodically inspect or delete ~/.local/share/bookmark-keeper if the saved history is no longer needed. <br>
