## Description: <br>
商家GEO推手 helps small and midsize merchants collect business materials, generate SEO/GEO promotional content for eight Chinese content platforms, and prepare drafts or browser-automation posting steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External merchants and agents assisting them use this skill to turn business identity materials, product photos, and customer examples into platform-adapted promotional drafts, GEO keyword plans, and guided publishing workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow collects sensitive business identity materials such as business licenses, storefront photos, product photos, responsible-person details, and customer cases. <br>
Mitigation: Use only materials the merchant controls, redact unnecessary license or personal details, and keep collected files limited to the content-generation task. <br>
Risk: Browser automation can prepare or submit public posts from logged-in merchant platform accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer draft/export mode, use dedicated work accounts, and require a human review and confirmation before any publish action. <br>
Risk: Generated promotional claims, customer cases, and GEO content may be inaccurate, exaggerated, or unsupported by the supplied materials. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated claim, metric, testimonial, and customer example against merchant-provided evidence before publication. <br>
Risk: Publishing across multiple third-party platforms can expose account access and create policy or moderation issues. <br>
Mitigation: Follow each platform's rules, avoid unnecessary account permissions, and keep screenshots or draft exports for review before final posting. <br>
