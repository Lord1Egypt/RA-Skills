## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to help an agent organize, index, search, and safely manage local photo libraries while preserving originals and reviewing bulk changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Photo libraries can contain private location, identity, and camera metadata. <br>
Mitigation: Grant access only to intended folders and review sharing or metadata-stripping actions before approval. <br>
Risk: Bulk organization, duplicate handling, or conversion steps could move many files at once. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated manifest and planned file actions before allowing bulk operations. <br>
