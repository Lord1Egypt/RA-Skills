## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language writing skill for turning academic papers and research outcomes into structured academic press releases with section guidance, formatting requirements, quality checks, and reusable templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[438061781](https://clawhub.ai/user/438061781) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, researchers, communications staff, and agents use this skill to draft public-facing Chinese academic press releases from papers, research results, team information, and supporting figures. It helps structure the release, preserve factual accuracy, prepare Markdown, and guide Word-document generation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process a user-provided PDF and create extracted images or Word documents. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the PDF is intended for processing and review generated files before sharing them. <br>
Risk: The artifact references an automated figure extraction script and dependencies that are not included in the package. <br>
Mitigation: Check any referenced script and dependencies before using the automated figure or Word-document workflow. <br>
