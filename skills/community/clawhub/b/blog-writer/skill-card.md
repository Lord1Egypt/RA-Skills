## Description: <br>
This skill should be used when writing blog posts, articles, or long-form content in the writer's distinctive writing style. It produces authentic, opinionated content that matches the writer's voice--direct, conversational, and grounded in personal experience. The skill handles the complete workflow from research review through Notion publication. Use this skill for drafting blog posts, thought leadership pieces, or any writing meant to reflect the writer's perspective on AI, productivity, sales, marketing, or technology topics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to draft, revise, and publish blog posts or long-form articles in Tom Panos's direct, conversational writing style. It is aimed at thought leadership and practical writing about AI, productivity, sales, marketing, and technology. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft content can be sent to the configured Notion database as part of the expected workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with content that is appropriate for that Notion workspace, and review drafts before publication when they include sensitive research or private notes. <br>
Risk: Finalized posts can persist as future style examples. <br>
Mitigation: Review finalized examples before saving them and avoid retaining private or sensitive material in the examples library. <br>
