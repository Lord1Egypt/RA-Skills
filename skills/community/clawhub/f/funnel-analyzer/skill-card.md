## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release includes an under-described generic utility that stores local data and command history. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer the intended scripts/funnel.sh workflow and check ~/.local/share/funnel-analyzer for retained data if the generic script has been run. <br>
Risk: Sensitive business metrics or secrets could be retained by the generic funnel-analyzer commands. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets or sensitive business metrics into the generic commands, and review the skill before installing or executing it. <br>
