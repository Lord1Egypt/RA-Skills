## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to run guided breathing exercises, meditation timers, mindfulness prompts, and optional local session tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Session tracking can store meditation dates and durations locally on shared devices. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid the session tracking commands on shared devices or delete ~/.local/share/meditation-guide when you do not want the history retained. <br>
