## Description: <br>
PowerPoint presentation generation skill for creating professional PPTX presentations from scratch with design guidance, templates, and content generation support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to plan, generate, format, and save PowerPoint presentations for business reports, academic talks, product launches, training materials, and similar presentation workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill writes PowerPoint files and may overwrite or expose content if filenames, destinations, or reusable templates are not reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Check the output filename and destination before writing in shared workspaces, avoid overwriting existing files, and sanitize sensitive content before adding it to templates. <br>
Risk: Broad presentation-generation triggers may cause the skill to activate for loosely related requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants a PowerPoint presentation and clarify the expected scope, format, and save location before generating the file. <br>
