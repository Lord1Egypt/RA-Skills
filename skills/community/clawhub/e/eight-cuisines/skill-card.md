## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese eight-cuisine cooking guidance, including classic recipes, seasoning formulas, core techniques, and practical cooking tips for Sichuan, Shandong, Cantonese, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Fujian, Hunan, and Anhui cuisines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and cooking-focused agents use this skill to answer questions about the eight major Chinese cuisines, generate structured recipes, explain regional seasoning patterns, and provide technique-specific cooking guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cooking guidance may involve knives, steam, hot oil, fermented foods, and high-heat wok cooking. <br>
Mitigation: Use standard kitchen precautions, reduce heat if cookware smokes heavily, maintain good ventilation, and adapt recipes for dietary restrictions or safety constraints. <br>
