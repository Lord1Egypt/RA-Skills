## Description: <br>
Manages client onboarding workflows: welcome sequences, document collection, intake forms, kickoff scheduling, progress tracking, multiple onboarding tracks, and stalled-account reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Client success, sales, and implementation teams use this skill to coordinate new-client onboarding, including welcome messages, intake, document collection, kickoff scheduling, progress tracking, and follow-up for stalled accounts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Client-facing messages, document requests, and kickoff scheduling may affect confidential or regulated onboarding data. <br>
Mitigation: Limit agent access to only the required client records, email, document system, and calendar, and require human review before sending messages, requesting documents, or scheduling meetings. <br>
Risk: Progress tracking and stalled-account reminders may become inaccurate if source systems are incomplete or out of date. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated checklists and reminders against the current client record before acting on them. <br>
