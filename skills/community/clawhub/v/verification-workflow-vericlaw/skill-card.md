## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad verification, incident response, or AI supervision phrasing may route to VeriClaw even when the user intended a different workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's intent and review the canonical VeriClaw skill or plugin before installing or relying on the route. <br>
Risk: Users may confuse this alias page with the canonical public install surface. <br>
Mitigation: Use the canonical VeriClaw skill page or `clawhub install vericlaw` when the goal is installation. <br>
