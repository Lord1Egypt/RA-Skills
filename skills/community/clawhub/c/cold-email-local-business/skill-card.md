## Description: <br>
Write hyper-personalized cold emails to local businesses using their Google Maps data (CSV). Generates AIDA sequences with personalization from ratings, reviews, website, and industry context. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gmapsscraper](https://clawhub.ai/user/gmapsscraper) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and sales teams use this skill to turn local-business CSV data into personalized cold outreach. It segments leads by fields such as website presence, rating, review count, address, and category, then drafts a three-email AIDA sequence for each lead. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cold outreach may use contact data that the user is not allowed to process or email. <br>
Mitigation: Use only lawful CSV sources, minimize personal data, and confirm CAN-SPAM, GDPR, platform terms, and local privacy requirements before sending. <br>
Risk: Generated outreach copy may contain inaccurate personalization or claims. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review each exported email sequence before importing it into bulk email tools or sending to recipients. <br>
