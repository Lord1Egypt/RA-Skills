## Description: <br>
Help a beginner or early-stage game team figure out which roles, skills, or disciplines they are missing for a given game concept and target scope. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External game developers, founders, and early-stage teams use this skill to compare a game concept and target milestone against their current role coverage. It identifies missing or weakly covered disciplines, risky role stacking, and the smallest workable team shape. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill output is advisory and may overstate or understate team readiness if the user provides incomplete project, milestone, or skill information. <br>
Mitigation: Review the recommendations against the actual team, budget, timeline, and milestone before making staffing or scope decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential business plans or personal team details while asking for team-gap diagnosis. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the project and staffing details needed for planning, and avoid unnecessary confidential or personal information. <br>
