## Description: <br>
Teaching Plan Writer helps teachers generate structured, personalized lesson plans aligned with China's 2022 compulsory education curriculum standards and teaching-assessment consistency principles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hwx1971](https://clawhub.ai/user/hwx1971) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Teachers and education staff use this skill to draft, revise, and personalize primary and middle school lesson plans from a topic, subject keyword, grade level, or teaching requirement. The skill structures outputs around curriculum-standard analysis, learning objectives, classroom activities, assessment design, resources, and reflection. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lesson topics and revision requests may be used as web-search terms. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include student personal information, confidential school materials, or private classroom details in prompts. <br>
Risk: Generated lesson plans may contain curriculum inaccuracies or unsuitable teaching assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Teachers should review outputs for curriculum alignment, subject accuracy, local classroom context, and age appropriateness before use. <br>
