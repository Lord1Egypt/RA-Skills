## Description: <br>
Buddha Companion is a Chinese-language Buddhist practice and wisdom dialogue assistant for explaining core Buddhist ideas, helping users notice attachment, and offering concise reflective prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TTtael](https://clawhub.ai/user/TTtael) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Buddhist and philosophical reflection, including questions about practice, life difficulties, attachment, non-self, emptiness, and the middle way. It is intended to provide spiritual framing and reflective dialogue, not professional mental health, medical, legal, or financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat Buddhist or philosophical reflection as professional mental health, medical, legal, or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for spiritual reflection only and consult qualified professionals for medical, mental health, legal, financial, or other regulated advice. <br>
Risk: Ordinary life concerns may be framed primarily through Buddhist practice concepts, which may not fit every user's needs or beliefs. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants Buddhist or philosophical framing before applying practice concepts to sensitive personal situations. <br>
