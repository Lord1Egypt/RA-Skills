## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-26
Initial release. Guides a residential HVAC contractor, energy rater, or designer through an ACCA Manual J 8th Edition block + room-by-room load calculation — capturing climate / outdoor design conditions, indoor design conditions, envelope assemblies (walls, ceilings, floors, windows, doors with U-value / SHGC), infiltration, mechanical ventilation, internal gains (people, lighting, appliances), and duct gains / losses — and produces a DRAFT load-calculation packet with sensible / latent heating and cooling loads (BTU/h), room-by-room cooling CFM targets for Manual D, a Manual S equipment-sizing handoff block (95–115% cooling, ≥100% heating), an input-assumption log, and a licensed-designer review block for permit, rebate, and code-compliance use.
