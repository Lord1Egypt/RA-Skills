## Description: <br>
Six Russian-language helper skills for contracts, housing and utility bills, medical test explanations, complaints, government documents, and tax calculations for individual entrepreneurs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aggel008](https://clawhub.ai/user/aggel008) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Russian-speaking external users use this skill pack to draft, explain, or calculate common household, legal, medical, government, and tax-related text tasks. Outputs should be treated as assistance for understanding and drafting, not as professional legal, medical, tax, or government advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal, medical, tax, and government-document outputs may be incomplete, outdated, or inappropriate for a user's specific jurisdiction or situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as drafting and explanation assistance, and have qualified professionals or official sources review high-impact decisions before use. <br>
Risk: Broad Russian-language document prompts can produce confident explanations from incomplete source text. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to provide the relevant document excerpt, dates, amounts, and jurisdiction, and state uncertainty when source details are missing. <br>
