# Personal Ritual Designer
Creates meaningful personal rituals for grounding, intention, and daily transitions.
## What it does
- Assesses your ritual needs (grounding, transition support, meaning-making, celebration) and maps to appropriate ritual architectures
- Designs rituals using a 4-part structure: Preparation, Invocation, Core Practice, and Completion — tailored to your preferences (secular or spiritual)
- Creates sustainability-calibrated rituals for your real life, not your aspirational life — starting with 2-minute practices that grow with consistency
## Example scenarios
**1. 每天早起太难了**
> 👤 每天闹钟响了按掉继续睡，到点了才慌慌张张出门，想建立一个有仪式感的早起习惯。
> 🤖 晨间仪式三步法：1）前一晚准备——把第二天穿的衣服、早餐食材提前备好，减少早上的决策疲劳；2）起床触发——闹钟放够不到的地方，关后立刻拉窗帘或去洗手间，用物理移动打断赖床惯性；3）15分钟仪式——5分钟喝水+拉伸、5分钟写晨间日记、5分钟读一页书，坚持3周形成习惯。

**2. 设计个性化晨间仪式**
> 👤 "I want a morning ritual that's not just 'meditate and journal' — something more personal."
> 🤖 Custom morning ritual design — values audit (what matters: creativity, connection, calm), 3-anchor ritual (sensory wake-up with specific tea/blend, 5-minute free-write to specific prompt, personal mantra creation), time-boxed to 20 minutes, with adaptation guidance for travel days and low-energy mornings.

**3. 下班的心理切换仪式**
> 👤 "Design an end-of-workday transition ritual so I stop carrying stress home."
> 🤖 Work-to-home transition ritual — physical trigger (specific doorway action), cognitive unload (3-line 'parking lot' capture), identity shift (changing clothes + specific playlist), and digital boundary (phone-to-drawer ceremony) — with science-backed rationale and 2-week habit-formation schedule.
