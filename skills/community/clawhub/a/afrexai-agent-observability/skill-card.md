## Description: <br>
Evaluate and monitor AI agent fleets across six key dimensions to score health, identify issues, and optimize performance for ops teams managing 1-100+ agents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ops teams, platform engineers, and CTOs use this skill to assess production AI agent fleets across execution visibility, cost attribution, output quality, failure recovery, security boundaries, and fleet coordination, then prioritize monitoring fixes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: An autonomous agent running the assessment could be given broader production, credential, or account access than the checklist requires. <br>
Mitigation: Grant only explicit read-only access to the monitoring, incident, and billing sources selected for review, and require separate approval before granting broader access. <br>
Risk: Assessment outputs may contain incorrect or incomplete operational recommendations if the provided monitoring, incident, cost, or alerting data is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review the assessment with the responsible operations or platform team before acting on remediation priorities. <br>
