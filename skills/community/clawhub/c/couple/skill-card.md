## Description: <br>
Strengthen relationships with milestone tracking, personalized celebrations, date planning, and shared memory across all relationship stages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to keep consensual relationship notes, remember milestones, and plan dates, gifts, anniversaries, and shared activities. It helps an agent personalize suggestions from user-provided preferences while staying within privacy and consent boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local relationship notes may expose sensitive personal or partner information if the device, home directory, or backups are shared. <br>
Mitigation: Store only consensual, non-sensitive notes; avoid private messages, location or schedule details, financial or health information, intimate details, credentials, and unshared partner data; periodically review or delete files under ~/couple/. <br>
Risk: Relationship-planning assistance can be misused for monitoring, manipulation, or keeping adversarial records about a partner. <br>
Mitigation: Refuse requests to track a partner, monitor communications or social media, manipulate behavior, score a partner, or keep records of mistakes; redirect users toward direct communication, counseling, or safety resources when appropriate. <br>
