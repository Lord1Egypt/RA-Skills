## Description: <br>
Generates consulting-style business slide diagrams as HTML and converts them to PNG with a headless Chrome workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mtdnot](https://clawhub.ai/user/mtdnot) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, consultants, and business users use this skill to draft structured presentation visuals such as problem slides, solution flows, 2x2 matrices, comparison tables, and process flows. It is suited for generating editable HTML slide assets and PNG exports for proposals, business decks, and internal presentation materials. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The templates fetch Tailwind and fonts from external CDNs during rendering. <br>
Mitigation: For confidential, offline, or highly controlled environments, vendor Tailwind and fonts locally or disable outbound network access during rendering. <br>
Risk: HTML-to-PNG rendering can execute or load content embedded in the HTML being converted. <br>
Mitigation: Only convert HTML that was generated by the skill or reviewed before rendering. <br>
