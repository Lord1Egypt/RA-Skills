## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-24
Initial release. Five-phase workflow that turns a voyage-charter party (laytime allowed, demurrage and despatch rates, NOR clause, exception clauses, WWDSHEX / SHINC / SHEX semantics, reversible / non-reversible regime), the port Statement of Facts, and the NOR tender / acceptance record into a clause-by-clause DRAFT laytime statement — covering charter / port / cargo intake, NOR-validity walkthrough (tendered in writing, vessel in all respects ready, at the agreed place, within the agreed hours, to the right party), timesheet construction with exceptions applied (weather, strikes, port closures, breakdowns, "once on demurrage always on demurrage", interrupted laytime), used-vs-allowed comparison, demurrage / despatch computation (half-despatch where the charter so provides), disputed-period flags, an evidence index linking every timesheet row to a SoF entry, and an open-questions list — for chartering, operations, claims-team, and counsel review before any invoice, counter-claim, or arbitration filing.
