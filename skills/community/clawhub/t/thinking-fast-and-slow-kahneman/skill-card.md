## Description: <br>
An educational prompt skill that applies Daniel Kahneman's Thinking, Fast and Slow concepts to decision-making, cognitive bias recognition, risk assessment, judgment improvement, and behavioral economics questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jaysonmeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/jaysonmeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill as a thinking aid for applying Thinking, Fast and Slow concepts to personal, professional, and organizational decisions. It helps users recognize cognitive biases, evaluate risk, slow down important judgments, and choose practical next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on the skill's financial, career, or life-decision guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as a structured thinking aid, not as professional, financial, legal, medical, or career advice. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad bias, decision-making, or risk conversations and appends Heardly branding. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated responses for scope fit and branding suitability before relying on or redistributing them. <br>
