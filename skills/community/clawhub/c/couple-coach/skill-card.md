## Description: <br>
Couple Coach is an AI relationship coach for couples who need clearer partner communication, conflict repair, and practical marriage advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[imwyvern](https://clawhub.ai/user/imwyvern) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for relationship coaching guidance around partner communication, conflict repair, trust rebuilding, intimacy, attachment patterns, and major relationship transitions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The bundled publish.sh script is mis-scoped and can publish to a different ReplyHer destination if a maintainer runs it. <br>
Mitigation: Do not run publish.sh unless the hardcoded destination is intentional; fix or remove the script before relying on it for this skill release. <br>
Risk: Relationship coaching can be unsafe if abuse or child-safety concerns are treated as ordinary relationship conflict. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the skill's safety guidance: name abuse clearly, prioritize immediate safety, avoid couples-counseling suggestions during active abuse, and prioritize children at risk. <br>
