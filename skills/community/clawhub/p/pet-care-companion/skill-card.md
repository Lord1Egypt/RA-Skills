## Description: <br>
Pet Care Companion helps pet owners manage care for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, aquarium pets, and small mammals with health tracking, vaccine schedules, nutrition guidance, behavior training, hospital search, and breed information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External pet owners use this skill to organize routine pet care, compare care needs across animal types, and receive informational guidance for health monitoring, nutrition, training, vaccination planning, nearby hospital search, and breed care research. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts may include pet health details and location data for hospital search. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the details needed for the task and prefer coarse location when searching for nearby care. <br>
Risk: Symptom guidance can be mistaken for professional veterinary diagnosis or treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat symptom analysis as informational triage and consult a qualified veterinarian for diagnosis, urgent symptoms, or treatment decisions. <br>
