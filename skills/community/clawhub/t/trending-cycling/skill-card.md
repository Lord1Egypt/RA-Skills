## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Route guidance may be outdated or incomplete for weather, closures, permits, local rules, or on-site restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current route conditions, weather, access requirements, local rules, and safety details before riding. <br>
Risk: Popular cycling routes can involve crowding, long queues, limited signal coverage, or insufficient supplies. <br>
Mitigation: Plan off-peak timing where possible, identify supply points and exit options, and carry appropriate safety equipment. <br>
