# Platform Fee Schedule Reference

Current as of Q2 2025. Fee structures change frequently — always verify against the platform's current seller documentation before finalizing your analysis.

---

## Amazon

### Referral Fees (Commission)
Referral fees are a percentage of the total sale price (item price + shipping + gift wrap).

| Category | Referral Fee |
|----------|-------------|
| Beauty & Personal Care | 8% (15% for items >$10) |
| Clothing & Accessories | 17% |
| Consumer Electronics | 8% |
| Home & Kitchen | 15% |
| Health & Household | 8% (15% for items >$10) |
| Sports & Outdoors | 15% |
| Toys & Games | 15% |
| Grocery & Gourmet | 8% (15% for items >$15) |
| Pet Supplies | 15% (22% for items ≤$10) |

*Note: Amazon applies a minimum referral fee of $0.30 per unit in most categories.*

### FBA Fulfillment Fees (2025 rates)
Based on product size tier and shipping weight.

| Size Tier | Weight Range | Fee |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| Small standard | ≤ 6 oz | $3.06 |
| Small standard | 6–12 oz | $3.15 |
| Small standard | 12 oz–16 oz | $3.47 |
| Large standard | ≤ 4 oz | $3.68 |
| Large standard | 4–8 oz | $3.98 |
| Large standard | 8–12 oz | $4.25 |

### Storage Fees
| Period | Standard-Size | Oversize |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| Jan–Sep | $0.78/cubic ft/month | $0.56/cubic ft/month |
| Oct–Dec | $2.40/cubic ft/month | $1.40/cubic ft/month |

### Other Amazon Fees
- **Professional seller plan:** $39.99/month (amortize across units sold)
- **Aged inventory surcharge:** Applies to inventory over 181 days — $0.50–$6.90/cubic ft depending on age
- **Return processing fee:** Charged in categories with high return rates (apparel, shoes, etc.)
- **Inbound placement service fee:** $0.21–$0.68/unit depending on number of inbound locations

## Shopify (DTC)

### Subscription Plans
| Plan | Monthly Cost | Transaction Fee (Shopify Payments) |
|------|-------------|-----------------------------------|
| Basic | $39/month | 2.9% + $0.30 |
| Shopify | $105/month | 2.7% + $0.30 |
| Advanced | $399/month | 2.5% + $0.30 |
| Plus | $2,300/month | 2.15% + $0.30 |

*Non-Shopify Payments processors add an additional 0.5–2.0% transaction fee on top.*

### Common App Costs to Amortize
| App Type | Typical Monthly Cost |
|----------|---------------------|
| Email/SMS (Klaviyo) | $45–$350 (based on contacts) |
| Reviews (Judge.me, Yotpo) | $0–$299 |
| Subscriptions (Recharge) | $99–$499 |
| Upsell/cross-sell | $30–$150 |
| Returns management | $50–$350 |
| Analytics | $0–$299 |

**Amortization:** Sum all monthly app costs and divide by monthly units sold to get per-unit app cost.

### Shopify-Specific Considerations
- No commission or referral fee (unlike marketplaces)
- Payment processing is the primary platform cost
- High-volume merchants can negotiate payment processing rates
- Shopify Capital repayments (if used) should be tracked separately as financing cost, not COGS

## TikTok Shop

### Commission Rates
| Category | Commission |
|----------|-----------|
| Fashion | 8% |
| Beauty & Personal Care | 8% |
| Food & Beverage | 5% |
| Health | 8% |
| Home & Garden | 8% |
| Sports & Outdoors | 8% |
| Electronics | 5% |
| Baby & Maternity | 5% |

*New sellers may receive a 90-day commission reduction to 1.8%.*

### Affiliate Program Costs
| Component | Typical Rate |
|-----------|-------------|
| Open collaboration commission | 10–20% of sale price |
| Targeted collaboration commission | 15–30% (negotiated with creator) |
| Sample product cost | Full COGS per sample |
| TikTok Shop affiliate platform fee | 1% of affiliate-driven sales |

**Important:** Affiliate commissions are ON TOP of platform commission. A product with 8% platform commission and 15% affiliate commission loses 23% of the sale price before any other costs.

### Shipping Considerations
- TikTok Shop subsidized shipping programs periodically offer reduced rates
- Shipping subsidies can be clawed back if service level targets are not met
- Self-fulfilled orders require merchant-paid shipping
- Fulfilled by TikTok (FBT) available in limited regions with separate fee structure

| Category | Referral Fee |
|----------|-------------|
| Apparel & Accessories | 15% |
| Beauty | 8% |
| Consumer Electronics | 8% |
| Food & Gourmet | 15% |
| Health & Personal Care | 10% |
| Home & Garden | 15% |
| Sports & Outdoors | 15% |

### Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)
| Weight | Fulfillment Fee |
|--------|----------------|
| ≤ 1 lb | $3.45 |
| 1–2 lb | $4.95 |
| 2–3 lb | $5.45 |
| 3+ lb | $5.45 + $0.40/lb above 3 lb |

**Storage:** $0.75/cubic ft/month

### Walmart-Specific Considerations
- No monthly subscription fee (unlike Amazon Professional)
- Payment processing included in referral fee
- Walmart+ customers drive higher conversion but same fee structure
- WFS items get a "2-Day Delivery" badge, similar to Prime
- Lower competition than Amazon in most categories but also lower traffic volume

## Cross-Platform Fee Comparison

### Quick Reference at $30 ASP (Home & Kitchen category)

| Fee Component | Amazon FBA | Shopify DTC | TikTok Shop | Walmart WFS |
|--------------|-----------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| Commission/referral | $4.50 (15%) | $0.00 | $2.40 (8%) | $4.50 (15%) |

| Storage | $0.18/mo est. | N/A | N/A | $0.10/mo est. |
| Subscription amortized | $0.01–0.10 | $0.01–0.10 | $0.00 | $0.00 |

*DTC has the lowest platform fees but requires merchant-paid shipping and advertising that other channels partially absorb.*
