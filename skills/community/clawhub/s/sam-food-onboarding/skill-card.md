## Use Case: <br>
Food suppliers and business operators use this skill to plan Sam's Club food product onboarding, including demand analysis, compliance preparation, application submission, review follow-up, and launch execution. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides procedural onboarding guidance that users may mistake for official Sam's Club, legal, or food-safety advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a planning aid and verify requirements through official Sam's Club supplier channels and qualified compliance advisors. <br>
Risk: Supplier onboarding may involve sensitive business, banking, tax, licensing, or contract information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting full bank account numbers, tax IDs, license scans, or confidential contracts into chat; upload sensitive documents only through trusted business systems. <br>
