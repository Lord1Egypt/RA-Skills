## Description: <br>
NicheHunter Ultra is a headless App Store market intelligence workflow for OpenClaw that detects underserved niches, analyzes competitors, validates monetization signals, scores opportunities, and generates MVP PRDs optimized for Telegram. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chebyte](https://clawhub.ai/user/chebyte) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External developers and product builders use this skill to research App Store categories, compare competitors, identify market gaps, and turn selected opportunities into Rork-ready MVP PRDs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can make public web searches or HTTP requests while researching markets. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with data that is appropriate for public research and avoid sharing private business plans, credentials, or sensitive internal data. <br>
Risk: Market and revenue estimates may rely on proxy signals when direct revenue data is unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Treat proxy estimates as decision support, require confidence labels, and verify important findings against primary or trusted sources before acting on them. <br>
