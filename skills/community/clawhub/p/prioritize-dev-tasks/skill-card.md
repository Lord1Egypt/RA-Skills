## Description: <br>
Prioritize software project backlogs, bugs, technical debt, sprint tasks, release checks, and requirement pools using business impact, dependency blocking, delivery risk, security and data risk, and implementation cost. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kokidebuff](https://clawhub.ai/user/kokidebuff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineering teams use this skill to classify unfinished software work, identify blockers and high-risk items, choose an execution order, and decide what to defer, merge, or remove. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Task lists can contain sensitive project, incident, security, or customer details. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include secrets, credentials, or private incident details unless the agent is intended to reason over that information. <br>
Risk: The skill provides prioritization guidance that may be based on incomplete project information. <br>
Mitigation: Review the stated assumptions, blockers, and information-to-confirm section before acting on the recommended order. <br>
