## Description: <br>
Daily financial news briefing — stock markets, crypto, macro economy, Fed/PBOC policy, earnings reports. Bilingual EN/CN visual dashboard. Morning push + evening recap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and financial-news workflows use this skill to generate bilingual EN/CN daily market briefings, visual HTML dashboards, morning pushes, and evening recaps covering equities, crypto, macroeconomics, central-bank policy, and earnings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated market summaries may contain delayed, incomplete, or incorrect financial information. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited data sources before relying on the briefing, keep the financial disclaimer visible, and treat the output as informational rather than investment advice. <br>
Risk: Optional scheduled pushes can send recurring finance briefings to external messaging channels. <br>
Mitigation: Enable push delivery only intentionally, and review the userId, channel, morning time, and evening time before scheduling. <br>
