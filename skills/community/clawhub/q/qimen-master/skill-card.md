## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Divination-style readings may be misleading if treated as health, legal, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as cultural or informational guidance only and consult qualified professionals for health, legal, or financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Report-sharing workflows may expose personal questions or chart details. <br>
Mitigation: Share reports only after the user explicitly approves the destination and content. <br>
Risk: Local script execution can create avoidable exposure if run with elevated privileges. <br>
Mitigation: Run the Node.js scripts without elevated privileges. <br>
