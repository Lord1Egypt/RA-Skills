## Description: <br>
用真实案例验证法条分析结果，分析司法实践中的裁判分歧、高频败诉原因和法官审查重点。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Legal practitioners and users analyzing Chinese statutes use this skill to check whether statutory analysis aligns with judicial practice. It prompts for common case types, judgment divergences, guiding cases, frequent loss reasons, judicial review focus, and practical recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI-generated case names, docket numbers, or holdings may be inaccurate or nonexistent. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all cited cases and holdings in authoritative legal databases such as China Judgments Online, PKU Law, or Faxin before relying on the analysis. <br>
Risk: The skill provides a research framework and may be mistaken for a substitute for formal case-law research. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as an analysis checklist and perform independent legal research before making legal or litigation decisions. <br>
