## Description: <br>
Monitor live promotional traffic, conversion efficiency, inventory stress, and ad allocation so merchants can scale winners, hold broken funnels, and redirect traffic during major sale events. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External commerce operators and marketing teams use this skill during active promotional windows to compare traffic, orders, spend, ROAS or MER, and inventory constraints. It helps decide whether to scale ad spend, hold or cut inefficient campaigns, redirect traffic from out-of-stock SKUs, and run landing-page or checkout checks before spending more. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Budget, traffic-routing, or restock recommendations may be wrong if promo metrics are stale, attribution is lagged, inventory state is inaccurate, or merchant risk tolerance is not confirmed. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm source metrics, inventory state, attribution lag, and acceptable risk with the merchant or account owner before changing budgets, pausing ads, redirecting traffic, or restocking. <br>
