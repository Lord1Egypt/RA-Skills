## Description: <br>
Full LP workflow from opportunity scanning to position entry, including opportunity selection, strategy design, risk assessment, token preparation, liquidity entry, and portfolio impact reporting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and DeFi operators use this skill to turn available capital into an end-to-end Uniswap LP workflow with opportunity scanning, strategy selection, risk checks, user-gated swaps, liquidity entry, and post-entry reporting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill prepares real DeFi transactions that can move wallet funds and create exposure to slippage, approvals, gas costs, impermanent loss, and pool-specific market risk. <br>
Mitigation: Use a limited wallet and verify chain, pool, token addresses, amounts, slippage, approvals, gas, and the risk assessment before approving any swap or liquidity-entry confirmation. <br>
Risk: Automated opportunity and strategy recommendations may be incomplete, stale, or unsuitable for the user's risk tolerance. <br>
Mitigation: Review the scan results, selected pool, strategy assumptions, and risk report before accepting a recommendation or proceeding to transaction execution. <br>
