## Description: <br>
SimInTech Coder helps users write, debug, and document SimInTech 2023+ code and modeling patterns for dynamic systems. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[producedbysavant](https://clawhub.ai/user/producedbysavant) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill for Russian-language assistance with SimInTech code examples, block design, model debugging, and documentation. It is focused on SimInTech questions and does not cover MATLAB, licensing bypasses, or undocumented internal product details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SimInTech examples or modeling guidance may be incorrect or unsuitable for a real engineering model. <br>
Mitigation: Review and test generated code and model changes in SimInTech before relying on them in production or engineering decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to prefer Russian-language responses, which may not match every workspace or reviewer expectation. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm language expectations before use and review outputs when sharing them with multilingual teams. <br>
