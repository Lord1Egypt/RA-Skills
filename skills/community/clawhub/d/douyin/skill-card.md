## Description: <br>
Rebuild weak scripts into stronger Douyin-native traffic structures by diagnosing first-3-second hooks, retention friction, replay triggers, and recommendation-friendly pacing for short-form video. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Traffic, retention, and recommendation advice may be mistaken for guaranteed platform performance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as creative script guidance and validate results through normal content review, testing, and platform analytics. <br>
Risk: Current trend context or niche culture may be missing from the user's prompt. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for audience, niche, trend, or goal context before making trend-specific recommendations, and avoid claiming certainty about current platform behavior. <br>
