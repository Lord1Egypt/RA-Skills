# Compliance Policy

The Skill supports recruiter review. It does not make final recruitment decisions.

## Must Not Affect Scores

Age, sex, gender, marriage, pregnancy, fertility, ethnicity, household registration, nationality, health, religion, and other protected or job-irrelevant traits must not affect scoring.

If these appear in a resume, report them under compliance warnings and state that they must not be used for screening decisions.

## Manual Review Required

- Missing or ambiguous hard requirements.
- Low confidence role-family inference.
- Inconsistent timeline or claims.
- Sensitive information in the resume.
- Model output with insufficient evidence.

## Prohibited Labels

Do not use hire, reject, pass, fail, eliminate, admit, 录用, 淘汰, 通过, 不通过 as final candidate decisions.

## Allowed Review Buckets

Use review buckets only: `strong_review`, `review`, `weak_review`, `manual_review`.
