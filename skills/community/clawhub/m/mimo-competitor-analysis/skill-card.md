## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad business-research prompts and produce a competitor-analysis report when the user intended a narrower task. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording when requesting or excluding competitor analysis, especially around market research, product comparisons, SWOT, and pricing prompts. <br>
Risk: Competitor, pricing, user-review, and market data can become outdated or remain unverified if not checked against current public sources. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current data with public sources, cite the source for each major claim, and include a data cutoff date in the report. <br>
Risk: User-provided confidential strategy or product plans could be incorporated into the analysis context. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid supplying confidential strategy unless it is intended to shape the analysis, and review outputs before sharing them externally. <br>
