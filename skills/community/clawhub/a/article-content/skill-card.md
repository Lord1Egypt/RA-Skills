## Description: <br>
Helps agents draft and audit article body content for blog posts, long-form guides, how-to articles, listicles, and SEO-oriented article sections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, content marketers, and writing agents use this skill to produce article body drafts, outlines, TL;DRs, introductions, body sections, conclusions, CTA options, and article-content audits. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated factual, SEO, or statistics claims may be inaccurate, outdated, or unsupported before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review article drafts before publishing, verify facts and statistics against current sources, and add citations where claims rely on external evidence. <br>
