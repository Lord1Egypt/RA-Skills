## Description: <br>
Provide professional ASMR music recommendations and playback services to help users achieve sleep aid, heart rate regulation, and focus enhancement through scientifically designed audio. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ashterry](https://clawhub.ai/user/ashterry) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to receive ASMR and ambient-audio recommendations for sleep routines, relaxation, focus, stress relief, meditation, and optional nightly listening reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat sleep, anxiety, or heart-rate language as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present recommendations as general wellness support and advise users to consult professionals for serious sleep, anxiety, or health concerns. <br>
Risk: Playback links send users to external audio destinations. <br>
Mitigation: Use only the disclosed links from references/asmr_music.md and review links before opening them. <br>
Risk: The optional nightly reminder creates a recurring 10 PM notification. <br>
Mitigation: Enable reminders only after a deliberate user request and provide clear disable guidance. <br>
