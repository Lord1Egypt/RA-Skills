## Description: <br>
AI compute sales negotiation coach that turns customer objections into response scripts and strategy across pricing, competitor, technical, process, and security scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dashiming](https://clawhub.ai/user/dashiming) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales teams and customer-facing employees use this skill to analyze customer objections and generate concise replies, detailed talking points, email drafts, supporting-material suggestions, cautions, and next steps for AI compute deals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may receive sensitive customer or deal details as objection text. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential customer information unless the local agent context and handling practices are acceptable for that data. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms may activate the skill in conversations where a sales-coaching workflow was not intended. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit prompts when requesting objection-handling guidance. <br>
Risk: Generated sales claims and response scripts may be inaccurate for a specific product, region, contract, or compliance posture. <br>
Mitigation: Review claims, certifications, pricing, SLA statements, and legal commitments before sharing them with customers. <br>
