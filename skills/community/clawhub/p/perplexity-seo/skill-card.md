## Description: <br>
Guides agents through Perplexity SEO optimization, including citation-oriented content structure, FAQPage schema, crawler access checks, IndexNow submission, and audit prompts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketers, founders, SEO practitioners, and content teams use this skill to audit and rewrite content so it is easier for Perplexity AI to cite. It provides practical checklists, prompt templates, schema examples, crawler guidance, and submission steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Robots.txt or IndexNow examples could affect real site crawling or submission behavior if applied to a site the user does not control. <br>
Mitigation: Review all robots.txt and IndexNow changes before use, and only submit URLs or keys for sites the user intentionally controls. <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations may be applied without validating whether they fit the user's site, audience, or current search platform behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the generated checklist and prompts as review guidance, then validate schema, crawler access, and content changes before publishing. <br>
