## Description: <br>
Error Rosetta translates cryptic errors, stack traces, and logs into plain-language diagnoses, likely root causes, and concrete fixes for the active codebase. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Developers and engineers use this skill to decode unfamiliar compiler errors, runtime exceptions, stack traces, CI logs, and user-reported failures into actionable debugging guidance. <br>
