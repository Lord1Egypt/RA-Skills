## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires a CellCog API key. <br>
Mitigation: Keep CELLCOG_API_KEY in environment variables or a secret manager, and do not paste it into prompts or logs. <br>
Risk: Creative-writing prompts and manuscripts may be processed by CellCog. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending confidential manuscripts, personal data, regulated data, or secrets unless CellCog is approved for that content. <br>
