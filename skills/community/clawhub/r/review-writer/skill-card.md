## Description: <br>
Data-driven writing and polishing for professional workplace documents, including performance reviews, resumes, work summaries, OKRs, PRDs, bilingual drafts, tone alignment, structure templates, bullet rewrites, and iterative first drafts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ykitty](https://clawhub.ai/user/ykitty) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees and workplace writers use this skill to turn raw professional notes into structured, data-driven drafts for reviews, resumes, reports, OKRs, and PRDs, then iterate them for audience, evidence, and tone. It supports Chinese, English, and bilingual drafting based on the user's request. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide confidential workplace information, personal details, unreleased business metrics, financial figures, or customer names while drafting professional documents. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive details unless sharing them with the agent is acceptable, and use placeholders for confidential names, metrics, systems, and customers when uncertain. <br>
Risk: Generated workplace claims may be overstated, incorrectly quantified, or unsupported by the user's evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Verify facts, metrics, baselines, attribution, and business impact before reusing generated text. <br>
