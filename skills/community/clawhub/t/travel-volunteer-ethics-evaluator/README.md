# Volunteer Tourism Ethics Evaluator
Helps travelers evaluate volunteer tourism opportunities for ethical impact — separating genuine service from commercial "voluntourism."
## What it does
- Provides an **ethical evaluation framework** scoring organizations on transparency, child-protection, local-staff ratio, and long-term impact
- Delivers an **organization assessment checklist** with 20 vetting questions covering mission alignment, community-led design, and economic displacement
- Performs **skill-match analysis** to determine whether your skills genuinely fill a need or if the program mainly wants your money
## Example scenarios
**1. 想去尼泊尔做义工旅行靠谱吗？**
> 👤 看到网上有很多尼泊尔义工旅行的项目，就是那种一边旅游一边做公益的，交几千块报名费，靠谱吗？
> 🤖 输出义工旅行伦理评估——警惕"付费义工"陷阱（真正的NGO不向志愿者收高额费，收费项目本质是商业旅游）、伦理风险（短期孤儿院义工可能造成儿童依恋创伤）、推荐替代（通过UN Volunteers/IVHQ等正规组织，或国内乡村支教如美丽中国、支教中国2.0），建议优先考虑四川/云南短期支教。

**2. Evaluate orphanage program in Cambodia**
> 👤 Evaluate this orphanage volunteer program in Cambodia before I commit $2,000.
> 🤖 Ethical evaluation scorecard — organization transparency, child-protection policy, local-staff ratio, community need vs. volunteer demand, long-term impact evidence, and 'orphanage tourism' red flags — with go/no-go recommendation and alternative suggestions.

**3. Create a vetting framework**
> 👤 I want to volunteer abroad but don't want to do harm. Create a vetting framework.
> 🤖 5-step vetting process — mission alignment check, community-led vs. outsider-imposed assessment, skills-match test (do they need what I offer or just my money?), economic displacement analysis, post-departure sustainability plan — with 20 vetting questions to ask organizations.
