# ArcaScience — Company Context & Positioning

## Company Overview
- **Founded:** 2018
- **Total raised:** $10M (last round: $7M in September 2025)
- **Product:** AI-powered dynamic Benefit-Risk Assessment (BRA) platform
- **Clients:** 20+ blue chip pharmaceutical companies
- **Technology:** 24 small AI models trained by clinicians
- **Data:** 100bn+ data points — the most comprehensive BRE database in the world
- **Key metric:** Reduces BRA project time by 80% (validated by independent BRA experts, 2025)
- **Award:** Best healthtech NLP Startup 2023 (StartUs Insights)
- **Regulatory foundation:** Inspired by ICH Working Group XII and Guideline E2C(R2)

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Dynamic Benefit-Risk Assessment
- Continuously updates drug benefit-risk profiles (not just periodic PBRER/DSUR snapshots)
- Probabilistic approval prediction
- Integrates clinical trial data AND real-world evidence
- 24 specialized AI models for different aspects of B/R evaluation

### 2. Evidence Mapping & Regulatory Strategy
- Cross-indication evidence harmonization (e.g., same molecule across multiple indications)
- Regulatory gap analysis — pre-empt CHMP/PRAC questions before they arise
- Endpoint alignment strategy for coordinated submissions across trials
- Geographic bridging (EU/US/Japan regulatory harmonization)

### 3. Post-Marketing & Lifecycle
- Dynamic safety monitoring at population scale
- Real-world evidence study design
- Signal detection and B/R impact assessment
- Multi-indication lifecycle evidence strategy

### 4. Specialized Applications
- Pediatric evidence bridging (PIP compliance, pediatric-specific B/R)
- Biosimilar evidence strategy (interchangeability, multi-indication extrapolation)
- Re-examination support (reconstructing evidence after CHMP negative opinions)
- Complex modality B/R (cell therapy, gene therapy, ADCs, bispecifics)
- Orphan drug B/R modeling (small populations, limited safety databases)

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already have PV systems" | ArcaScience doesn't replace PV systems — it's the analytical intelligence layer that converts processed safety data into continuous B/R assessment. Works alongside ARTEMIS, IQVIA, Argus, etc. |
| "How is this different from spreadsheets/manual B/R?" | Manual B/R assessment takes months and is static. ArcaScience does it in seconds and continuously updates as new data arrives. 80% time reduction validated by independent experts. |
| "Is this accepted by regulators?" | Built on ICH E2C(R2) guidelines and Working Group XII frameworks. 20+ blue chip pharma clients already using it for regulatory submissions. |
| "AI in safety decisions is risky" | 24 models trained by clinicians, not generic AI. Transparent, explainable outputs. Compatible with RAISE-type responsible AI frameworks (e.g., Sanofi). EU AI Act compliant. |
| "We're too small for this" | Platform scales from single-molecule startups to 100+ product portfolios. Biosimilar companies particularly benefit from multi-indication evidence efficiency. |
