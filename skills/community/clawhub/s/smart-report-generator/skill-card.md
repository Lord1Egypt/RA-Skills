Developers, team leads, and operations teams can use this skill to draft daily or weekly status reports from task data and optionally send them to configured workplace chat webhooks. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Reports and task details can leave the local environment through an AI service or configured chat webhook. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill only where such processing is approved, avoid confidential task details unless permitted, and configure limited-scope webhooks. <br>
Risk: The advertised scheduling and platform support are broader than the included code. <br>
Mitigation: Test the configured workflow and recipient platform before relying on it for scheduled or multi-platform delivery. <br>
