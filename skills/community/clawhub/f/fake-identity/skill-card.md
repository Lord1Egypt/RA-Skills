## Description: <br>
Generates realistic synthetic identity profiles and disposable email account details after collecting country or ethnicity, gender, and explicit user confirmation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to generate a country- or ethnicity-specific synthetic identity profile with formatted personal, contact, address, government-style ID, and occupation fields. The workflow requires the user to choose country or ethnicity and gender, then confirm before any profile or disposable email account details are generated. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to create realistic identity packages with government-style identifiers and working disposable email account details. <br>
Mitigation: Review carefully before installing and prefer clearly fictional test-data workflows that use reserved email domains, invalid official IDs, and no live mailbox creation. <br>
Risk: Generated details could be misused for impersonation, fraud, or account creation if treated as real identity data. <br>
Mitigation: Restrict use to legitimate synthetic-data or testing contexts and require human review before deploying the skill. <br>
