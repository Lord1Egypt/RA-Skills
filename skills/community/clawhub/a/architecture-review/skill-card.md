## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language architecture review checklists, report templates, and design principles for evaluating technical architecture and improvement plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to review architecture proposals, evaluate functionality, performance, availability, scalability, security, maintainability, and cost, and draft architecture review reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Architecture review conclusions may be treated as definitive even though the skill provides advisory checklist and report guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified reviewers validate assumptions, tradeoffs, and recommendations before using outputs for design approval. <br>
Risk: Chinese-language outputs may not fit teams that require English or bilingual review artifacts. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent for English or bilingual output when the review process requires it. <br>
