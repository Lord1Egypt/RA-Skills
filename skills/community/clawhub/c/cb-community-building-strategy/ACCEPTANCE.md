# Acceptance Criteria

## Community Building Strategy

1. Produces a platform strategy with specific channels and a recommended funnel
2. Defines community persona and value proposition per target market
3. Includes a content calendar with at least 30 days of planned content
4. Provides an engagement playbook with response templates and escalation rules
5. Includes growth mechanics and a KPI dashboard with specific targets
6. Produces actionable, market-specific output rather than generic community advice
