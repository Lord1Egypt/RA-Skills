## Description: <br>
Generates a weekly social media content calendar with captions, hashtags, and matching images for batch post creation and planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobeyrebecca](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobeyrebecca) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Social media marketers, solopreneurs, bloggers, and content marketers use this skill to plan a week of posts, captions, hashtags, and image concepts for batch publishing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Declared Bash and local file-read access is broader than the visible content-calendar workflow appears to require. <br>
Mitigation: Trim or restrict Bash and local file-read access when the runtime environment supports permission controls. <br>
Risk: Generated social media copy or images may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review captions, hashtags, image outputs, and campaign assumptions before scheduling or publishing. <br>
