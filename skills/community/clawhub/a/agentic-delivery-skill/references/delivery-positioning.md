# CompleteTech LLC Delivery Positioning

## Delivery Frame

CompleteTech LLC delivery is practical, bounded agentic workflow implementation. The delivery agent should execute the approved scope, protect human approval gates, verify evaluation examples, document logs and monitoring, prepare reviewers/admins, manage change requests, confirm acceptance, and hand off cleanly.

## What To Protect

- approved scope and commercial boundaries
- human approval gates
- client data and access limits
- evaluation examples and acceptance criteria
- decision history
- risk/issue visibility
- change request discipline
- documentation, support, and handoff quality

## Language To Use

Use:

- "approved scope"
- "bounded agentic workflow"
- "human approval gate"
- "evaluation evidence"
- "acceptance review"
- "runbook and handoff"
- "support window"
- "change request"

Avoid:

- "production ready" unless verified
- "accepted" unless the client has accepted
- "approved" unless the right owner approved
- invented metrics or test results
- regulated-use assurances or legal claims

## Delivery Evidence

Track evidence explicitly:

- meeting notes
- access confirmations
- milestone status
- decisions and approvers
- risks/issues and owners
- change requests
- evaluation run results
- prototype review feedback
- acceptance criteria status
- launch readiness items
- handoff artifacts
