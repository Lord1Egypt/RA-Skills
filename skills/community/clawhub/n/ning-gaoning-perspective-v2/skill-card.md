## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake persona responses for real statements from Ning Gaoning or for professional financial, legal, governance, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as simulated management-framework brainstorming and require qualified review before using them for business, legal, financial, governance, or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad business trigger phrases may activate the persona unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly by name when the persona is desired and exit the role when normal assistant behavior is needed. <br>
