## Use Case: <br>
Employees, travel coordinators, and enterprise assistants use this skill to estimate, request, schedule, and manage car-service trips such as ride hailing, airport transfers, and chartered cars. Live booking, cancellation, payment, or account actions require a separately connected provider and explicit user confirmation. <br>
