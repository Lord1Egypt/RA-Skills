## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated social media content may conflict with platform rules or contain unsupported advertising, health, political, or brand claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content for platform rules, claim accuracy, political sensitivity, and brand requirements before publishing. <br>
Risk: Hashtag and trend suggestions may become stale or mismatched to the topic. <br>
Mitigation: Validate current tags and high-engagement examples with platform search before using the final post. <br>
