## Description: <br>
Plan weddings with budget guardrails, guest-list scenarios, vendor scorecards, payment tracking, and deadline-driven coordination. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Couples, family organizers, planners, and coordinators use this skill to structure wedding planning around budgets, guest-count scenarios, vendor comparisons, payment deadlines, timelines, and day-of execution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persistent planning notes may capture sensitive wedding, family, financial, or legal details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask before enabling memory and avoid storing payment card data, full contracts, IDs, or deeply personal family details unless the user explicitly needs them for planning. <br>
Risk: Planning guidance can affect live vendor commitments, deposits, payment timing, or final guest communications. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning support and confirm with the user before recording or acting on anything that affects contracts, deposits, or final communications. <br>
