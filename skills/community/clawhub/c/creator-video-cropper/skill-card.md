## Description: <br>
Crop videos to focus on the most important area for social, ecommerce, ads, and storefront use without making the result look clumsy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, ecommerce teams, advertisers, and storefront operators use this skill to plan tighter static video crops that keep the subject readable and remove distracting frame edges. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A static crop can fail when the subject moves too much, causing faces, hands, products, subtitles, or full objects to leave the crop area. <br>
Mitigation: Review the source video for motion before applying the crop, choose conservative framing when motion is present, and inspect the exported result before use. <br>
Risk: Video files may contain private or sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide videos that are appropriate for the processing environment and review outputs before sharing. <br>
