# Sample Papers for Psychology Thesis Guidance

Use this reference to turn real papers into undergraduate thesis examples. Do not present these papers as templates to copy mechanically. Use them to justify topic feasibility, method choice, intervention structure, outcome measures, and limitations.

## How to Use These Samples

For each user topic, choose 2-4 closest papers and extract:

1. What the paper studied: population, issue, intervention or variables.
2. What the user can imitate: title wording, method label, sample size logic, session structure, measures, evaluation plan.
3. What the undergraduate paper should avoid: overclaiming efficacy, overly complex statistics, clinical-risk samples, or direct replication without permission.

For non-implemented intervention-scheme papers, convert empirical studies into "方案设计依据", not evidence that the proposed untested scheme is already effective.

## Example Conversions

### Example A: CBT Test Anxiety Scheme

User theme:

> 大学生考试焦虑，不想真正做干预，只想设计方案。

Recommended topic:

> 《基于认知行为疗法的大学生考试焦虑团体辅导方案设计》

Literature basis to cite:

- Reiss et al. (2017): CBT group intervention can include relaxation and imagery/behavioral practice.
- Khaira et al. (2023): test anxiety interventions often combine relaxation, coping, and psychological skills, but evidence quality varies.

Method:

> 文献研究法、理论建构法、方案设计法。

Design implication:

> Build a 6-session group program: understand test anxiety, identify automatic thoughts, cognitive restructuring, relaxation training, exam-situation coping, consolidation and relapse prevention.

Limitation:

> This thesis designs a scheme only; it cannot conclude that the scheme reduces test anxiety without future pre-post or controlled evaluation.

### Example B: Mindfulness Stress Management Scheme

User theme:

> 大学生压力管理，想写正念训练方案。

Recommended topic:

> 《正念训练在大学生压力管理中的应用方案设计》

Literature basis to cite:

- de Vibe et al. (2013): 7-week MBSR for medical and psychology students, with distress, study stress, well-being, mindfulness, attendance, and compliance as evaluation indicators.
- Galante et al. (2018): 8-week mindfulness course for university students, with psychological distress during exams as a key outcome.

Method:

> 文献研究法、理论分析法、方案设计法。

Design implication:

> Design 6-8 weekly sessions: mindfulness orientation, breathing, body scan, mindful movement, stress and thought awareness, self-compassion, daily practice planning, review and maintenance.

Evaluation idea:

> Future implementation can use PSS, DASS-21, GAD-7, WHO-5 or subjective well-being scales, plus attendance, practice frequency, and satisfaction.

### Example C: Freshman Adjustment Group Counseling Scheme

User theme:

> 大学新生适应，人际关系、学习适应、情绪管理。

Recommended topic:

> 《团体辅导视角下大学新生适应问题干预方案设计》

Literature basis to cite:

- Xu Kai et al. (2012): Chinese freshman-adaptation intervention used group counseling plus mental health education over 3 months.
- Harris et al. (2003): psychoeducational groups can support first-semester adjustment and stress relief.

Method:

> 文献研究法、理论建构法、方案设计法。

Design implication:

> Design an 8-session developmental group: group building, campus adaptation, interpersonal connection, emotional expression, learning strategies, stress coping, goal setting, growth sharing.

Evaluation idea:

> Future implementation can use college adaptation scales, SCL-90 or DASS-21 where appropriate, social support/self-efficacy scales, attendance, homework completion, and written feedback.

## Cautions

- Prefer open-access or verifiable sources when giving citations to users.
- Check publication details online if the user asks for formal references in a specific style.
- Do not cite low-quality reposts as primary evidence when a journal page, PubMed, ERIC, Springer, ScienceDirect, or PMC page is available.
- For clinical terms such as anxiety disorder, depression, self-harm, or crisis, include screening, referral, and ethics notes.
