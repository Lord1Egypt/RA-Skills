## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI visibility reports may mislead clients if estimated or simulated platform results are presented as measured results. <br>
Mitigation: Run real AI-platform tests when tooling is available; otherwise label results as estimates and identify the missing tests that still need to be run. <br>
Risk: Generated reports, screenshots, and tracking files may persist proprietary brand, competitor, analytics, or CRM data. <br>
Mitigation: Use a workspace-relative output directory, confirm file writes before saving deliverables, and include only data the client has approved for storage. <br>
Risk: Browser-based AI-platform testing can expose personal authenticated sessions or sensitive screenshots. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid personal authenticated AI sessions, use dedicated test sessions where possible, and review screenshots before saving or sharing them. <br>
Risk: Recommendations about AI search visibility can become stale as AI platforms and rankings change. <br>
Mitigation: Include test dates, platform names, methodology notes, and limitations in diagnostic and monitoring deliverables; avoid guaranteeing future ranking or recommendation positions. <br>
