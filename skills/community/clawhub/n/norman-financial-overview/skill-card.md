## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business users and finance operators use this skill to ask for a concise financial status overview across balances, recent cash flow, outstanding invoices, and upcoming tax obligations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can expose sensitive business balances, transactions, invoices, and tax information through the Norman Finance MCP integration. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in trusted environments, use private conversations, and ask for narrower financial answers when a full overview is not needed. <br>
