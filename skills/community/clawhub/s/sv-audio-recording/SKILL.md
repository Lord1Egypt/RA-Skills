## Usage Scenarios

### Scenario 1

**User Input:** "I'm recording a voiceover in my untreated home office. Assess my setup and give me a pre-flight checklist."

**Expected Output:** Environment assessment: room size, hard surfaces, background noise sources identified. Checklist: blanket baffles, mic placement at 6-8 inches, pop filter position, gain-setting walkthrough.

### Scenario 2

**User Input:** "Listen to this 30-second clip. Is the audio quality good enough for a TikTok product review?"

**Expected Output:** Audio quality report: loudness (LUFS) check, noise floor analysis, plosive detection, reverb assessment. Pass/fail against platform best-practice thresholds with specific fix suggestions.

### Scenario 3

**User Input:** "I recorded 12 takes. Rank them by clarity and recommend the best one for final use."

**Expected Output:** Ranking with scores: clarity, background noise, pacing, and emotional tone. Top pick highlighted with timestamps for the strongest sections.


### Scenario 4: 用手机录播客/短视频声音太差
**User input:** "我在B站做知识类视频，用手机录音总是声音嘈杂、有回音。有没有低成本（0预算）的录音改善方案？"
**Expected output:** 手机录音零预算方案——环境：选衣柜挂满衣服的房间（布料吸音最好），关门窗拉窗帘（减少外界噪音和回声）；摆放：手机放在桌上用支架而不是手持（减少摩擦声），收音口对着自己方向但不要正对嘴巴（避免喷麦）；后续：用剪映App的"音频降噪"或格式工厂的音效处理（回音消除+降噪），B站有详细教程；进阶：花15元在拼多多买一个手机用的小麦克风（领夹式），效果立竿见影。关键：环境比设备重要100倍，先解决房间回音再考虑买设备。

## Safety & Limitations

Audio guidance is educational. Does not control recording equipment. Volume safety: guidance on safe listening levels but not a substitute for professional audio engineering.
