## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Remote installer execution for PinchTab may introduce supply-chain or endpoint risk. <br>
Mitigation: Review the installer before running it and prefer a verified or pinned installation method. <br>
Risk: Apify tokens and retained browser sessions can expose accounts or collected data if mishandled. <br>
Mitigation: Use a least-privilege Apify token, keep secrets out of shared folders and commits, rotate exposed tokens, and use a dedicated browser profile or test account for automation. <br>
Risk: Scraping workflows can collect regulated or disallowed data or violate platform requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm each target, platform terms, rate limits, data fields, storage location, and retention period before collecting or exporting data. <br>
