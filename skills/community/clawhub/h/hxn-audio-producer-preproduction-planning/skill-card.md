## Description: <br>
Helps audio producers plan audiobook and multi-role voice production before recording by structuring project initialization, reading notes, source packages, episode planning, role colors, recording responsibilities, script variants, recaps, trial-recording calibration, and Markdown huaben templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jane-huan](https://clawhub.ai/user/jane-huan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External audio producers and production assistants use this skill to turn usable manuscript text into trackable preproduction artifacts for audiobooks, multi-cast narration, and CV collaboration. It is intended for planning before formal recording, including episode boundaries, source-package notes, huaben variants, role and color systems, recaps, and calibration records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process and preserve long manuscript text, project requirements, role notes, CV notes, and production records that are copyrighted or confidential. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in workspaces where storing those materials is acceptable, and avoid adding manuscripts or production details that should not be retained. <br>
Risk: Preproduction outputs can shape episode boundaries, recording scripts, role responsibilities, and production timing. <br>
Mitigation: Have the producer review generated planning artifacts before recording, especially unresolved source-text issues, pending confirmations, and calibration assumptions. <br>
