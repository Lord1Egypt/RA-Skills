## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may expose YouTube credentials, OAuth tokens, API keys, or full account exports while seeking channel advice. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide credentials or complete account exports; share only the analytics and channel details needed for the requested optimization guidance. <br>
Risk: Optimization recommendations may overemphasize click-through tactics and produce misleading packaging if not reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review titles, thumbnails, and hooks for accuracy before publication, keeping curiosity-driven packaging aligned with the actual video content. <br>
