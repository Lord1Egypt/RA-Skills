## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can submit personal contact details and engagement events to a third-party API. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the publisher, API host, privacy practices, and retention practices before use; only provide data you are authorized to share. <br>
Risk: Update and delete operations can change or remove stored business card data. <br>
Mitigation: Review the target slug and requested operation before execution, especially for destructive delete requests. <br>
Risk: The release has no server-resolved GitHub provenance for this version. <br>
Mitigation: Use the ClawHub listing, publisher profile, and server-resolved publisher handle as the release identity evidence; do not assume GitHub source provenance. <br>
