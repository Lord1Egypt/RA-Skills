# GA Insights - Your AI Analytics Co-Pilot

**Stop clicking through confusing dashboards. Just ask.**

## What It Does

GA Insights connects to your Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and answers your questions in plain English:

- "How's my traffic doing?"
- "Which pages have high bounce rate?"
- "Why did visitors drop yesterday?"
- "What's my top traffic source?"
- "How many users converted this week?"

## Why You'll Love It

✅ **No more custom reports** - Just ask questions naturally  
✅ **2-minute setup** - Add our email to GA4, paste your Property ID, done  
✅ **Works in OpenClaw** - Right where you already work  
✅ **Free to start** - Google API is free for standard GA4  

## Commands

```
ga traffic        - 7-day traffic summary
ga sources       - Where your visitors come from
ga realtime      - Current active users on site
ga pages         - Best and worst performing pages
ga bounce        - Bounce rate analysis
ga devices       - Mobile vs desktop breakdown
ga geo           - Traffic by country/city
ga conversions   - Event and goal tracking
ga compare       - This week vs last week
ga drop          - Why did traffic change?
```

Or just ask naturally: "What's my conversion rate?"

## Quick Setup (2 Steps)

1. **Add our service account to your GA4:**
   - Go to Analytics → Admin → Property Access Management
   - Add: `ga-insights@plucky-engine-488015-d4.iam.gserviceaccount.com`
   - Role: Viewer

2. **Tell us your Property ID:**
   - Find it in Analytics → Admin → Property Settings
   - Run: `ga setup` and paste your ID

Done! Start asking questions.
