## Description: <br>
Checks Arabic and bilingual right-to-left layouts for readable ordering, alignment, font support, punctuation, mirroring, and common UI/design mistakes before publishing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abdullah944](https://clawhub.ai/user/abdullah944) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, localization reviewers, marketers, and developers use this skill to review Arabic, bilingual Arabic/English, and RTL layouts before publishing. It helps identify readability, alignment, typography, mirroring, mixed-language, and translation-quality issues in UI screens, websites, posts, PDFs, slides, and documents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Arabic and RTL layout reviews may involve client screenshots, drafts, or unpublished materials. <br>
Mitigation: Share only authorized content needed for the review and remove credentials, secrets, private documents, OTPs, tokens, and unrelated personal data. <br>
Risk: Review guidance can miss context-specific brand, audience, dialect, or publishing requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the intended audience, dialect, fixed brand rules, and target format, then review findings before publishing. <br>
