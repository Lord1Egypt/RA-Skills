## Description: <br>
Explain DAO governance rules and implications clearly; translate constraints into allowed/forbidden actions with consequences. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mzfshark](https://clawhub.ai/user/mzfshark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, DAO operators, and governance reviewers use this skill to interpret provided DAO rules, proposed actions, votes, permissions, and policy constraints. It turns the supplied governance text and situation into practical allowed actions, forbidden actions, prerequisites, and consequences. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate in unrelated conversations because the available security evidence identifies a trigger-precision concern. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when the user is explicitly asking about DAO rules, proposals, votes, permissions, or related governance policy. <br>
Risk: Governance interpretations can be misleading when the provided rule or situation is incomplete or ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Separate explicit governance text from interpretation, ask the minimum clarifying questions, and default to the safer interpretation when ambiguity remains. <br>
