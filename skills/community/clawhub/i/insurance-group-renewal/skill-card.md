Provides group insurance renewal guidance for account managers, HR benefits leaders, and brokers, including renewal decisions, loss-ratio analysis, negotiation strategy, procurement workflows, pricing references, and Huimin Bao renewal planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
