## Description: <br>
Ai Biogeography is a Chinese educational guide for biogeography topics including species distribution patterns, dispersal and isolation, historical and island biogeography, biogeographic realms, invasive species, human impacts, and conservation applications. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, conservation practitioners, policy-oriented readers, and nature enthusiasts use this skill to ask biogeography questions and receive structured study guidance about distribution, dispersal, island theory, historical biogeography, invasions, and conservation planning. <br>
