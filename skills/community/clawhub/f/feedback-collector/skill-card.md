## Description: <br>
Collects and processes user feedback on task completion. Use after important tasks to ask if the result was satisfactory. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wqz666](https://clawhub.ai/user/wqz666) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and assistant operators use this skill to ask for satisfaction feedback after important tasks, capture quick or detailed responses, and inform future behavior improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Feedback entries can create a long-term local record of user satisfaction, preferences, and task details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including secrets or sensitive case details in feedback, and periodically review or delete memory and preference files. <br>
Risk: Automatic feedback prompts after important tasks may interrupt workflows or collect more feedback than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only where post-task satisfaction checks are acceptable and adjust or disable prompts for sensitive or high-volume workflows. <br>
