## Use Case: <br>
External users buying a primary home or investment property use this skill to structure budget decisions, listing comparisons, offer strategy, due diligence, and closing readiness with repeatable guardrails. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local notes may include budget, financing posture, property, offer, and closing checklist details under ~/home-buying/. <br>
Mitigation: Store only decision-grade notes, avoid account numbers, identity documents, lender credentials, and other secrets, and remove ~/home-buying/ when the history is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Home-buying recommendations can affect major financial and contractual decisions when based on incomplete or stale deal information. <br>
Mitigation: Verify budget calculations, inspection issues, lender figures, title items, insurance costs, and offer terms with qualified professionals before committing. <br>
