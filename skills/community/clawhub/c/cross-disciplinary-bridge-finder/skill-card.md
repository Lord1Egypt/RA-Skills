## Description: <br>
Finds cross-disciplinary collaboration opportunities by identifying synergies between scientific domains, matching complementary expertise, translating methodologies, and supporting interdisciplinary grant and team formation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AIPOCH-AI](https://clawhub.ai/user/AIPOCH-AI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, research administrators, and developers use this skill to explore cross-domain research bridges, identify collaboration directions, generate innovation hypotheses, and draft interdisciplinary proposal outlines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Dependency and packaging hygiene issues may cause installation or runtime failures. <br>
Mitigation: Install in an isolated environment, review and pin dependencies before use, and validate command examples against the packaged files. <br>
Risk: The skill can run Bash commands and write output files. <br>
Mitigation: Approve Bash and file-write actions explicitly, and review generated files before using them in research or proposal workflows. <br>
Risk: Generated collaboration matches, bridge scores, and proposal ideas may be incomplete or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research leads, verify candidates and funding opportunities against authoritative sources, and apply domain-expert review before action. <br>
