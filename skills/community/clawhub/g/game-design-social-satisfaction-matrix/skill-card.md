## Description: <br>
Evaluate a game's social features and multiplayer elements by their potential to create social satisfaction versus social dissatisfaction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and community-facing teams use this skill to audit social and multiplayer features, classify them as basic, critical, booster, or secondary, and prioritize changes that improve social satisfaction while managing dissatisfaction risk. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts may include private player data, internal community logs, or sensitive moderation records. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymized or non-sensitive examples unless the user explicitly intends the assistant to process that information. <br>
