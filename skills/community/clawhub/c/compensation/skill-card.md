### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce negotiation guidance that users mistake for legal, tax, or financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general negotiation guidance and consult qualified professionals for legal, tax, or financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Compensation discussions can involve sensitive personal, employer-confidential, or relationship-impacting information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary context, avoid confidential details, and review recommendations before using them in compensation conversations. <br>
