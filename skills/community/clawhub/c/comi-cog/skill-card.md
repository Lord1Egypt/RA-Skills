## Description: <br>
AI comic and manga creation powered by CellCog, including comics, manga, webtoons, graphic novels, comic strips, sequential art, character-consistent panels, and visual storytelling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and creative agents use this skill to send comic and manga generation tasks to CellCog, including page layouts, webtoon episodes, comic strips, graphic novel pages, and character-consistent sequential art prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Comic prompts, story ideas, character descriptions, and referenced creative material are sent to the CellCog service. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if you trust CellCog, avoid submitting sensitive or unlicensed material, and review prompts before sending them. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a CELLCOG_API_KEY, which may grant account access or incur usage costs. <br>
Mitigation: Use a revocable API key, store it outside committed files, rotate it when needed, and monitor account usage or billing. <br>
