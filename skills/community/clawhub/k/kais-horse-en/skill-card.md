## Description: <br>
Kai's Horse provides command-driven exploration of 48 classic thought experiments across philosophy, consciousness, AI ethics, logic paradoxes, and speculative analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wanyview1](https://clawhub.ai/user/wanyview1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users and agents use this skill as a philosophy companion for selecting, explaining, comparing, searching, and narrating classic thought experiments. It is intended for reflective discussion and AI-era reinterpretation of topics such as ethics, consciousness, paradoxes, and alignment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad always-on topic activation may affect ordinary discussions about philosophy, consciousness, ethics, or AI. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer explicit /kh command use or disable the skill when its speculative framing is not desired. <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally applies a cold, literary, and speculative style that may be inappropriate for neutral or sensitive discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for tone and framing before using them in external, educational, or decision-support contexts. <br>
