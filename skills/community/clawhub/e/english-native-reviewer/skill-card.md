## Description: <br>
Reviews English writing for grammar, Chinglish, word choice, and style so drafts read more naturally to native English speakers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tuobadaidai](https://clawhub.ai/user/tuobadaidai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, students, and business users use this skill to polish English text, identify Chinglish and translation-style phrasing, and receive rewritten text plus review notes for business, academic, report, and marketing contexts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Language edits may change nuance or preserve unsupported claims because the skill explicitly does not verify facts, data, or logic. <br>
Mitigation: Review revised text against the source intent and verify factual, numerical, and quoted material before relying on it. <br>
Risk: The review is not a substitute for legal, medical, financial, or other specialized professional editing. <br>
Mitigation: Route regulated or domain-specialized documents through an appropriate subject-matter reviewer before publication or use. <br>
Risk: Default en-US style and Chinese explanations may not match the user's desired audience or language variant. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the target English variant, tone, document type, and explanation language when requesting a review. <br>
