## Marketplace Description (250 words)

Every business starts with a name. A bad name costs you years of trying to make people remember something forgettable. A great name does half your marketing for you.

This skill gives you the exact process professional naming agencies charge $5,000–$50,000 to run — compressed into 4 structured prompts you can run in 40 minutes.

**What you get:**

**Prompt 1 — Brand Name Brainstorm (FREE)**  
15 name candidates across 5 naming styles. Domain availability guidance. Trademark search steps. Top 3 recommendation with rationale.

**Prompt 2 — Tagline Generator**  
10 taglines using 5 psychological frameworks (benefit-led, emotion, contrast, command, alliteration). Scoring matrix. Ranked recommendation with 4 variations.

**Prompt 3 — Brand Voice Guide**  
Vocabulary list (20 words to use, 10 to avoid). Channel-specific rules for LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, email, and website. 3 headline formulas with examples. 3 sample rewrites in your brand's voice.

**Prompt 4 — Elevator Pitch Suite**  
5-second intro. 30-second pitch (2 versions). 60-second script with stage directions. 5 cold email subject lines. LinkedIn About opener (3 variants). Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn bio templates. Your "party answer" one-liner.

**Who this is for:** Startup founders, freelancers, side hustlers, Etsy sellers, service businesses, agency clients.

**Result:** A complete Brand Bible in one Google Doc — name, tagline, voice, and every pitch format you'll ever need.

Stop agonizing. Start building.
