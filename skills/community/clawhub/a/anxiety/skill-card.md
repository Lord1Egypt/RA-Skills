## Description: <br>
Track anxiety episodes, triggers, thoughts, and coping responses with therapy-ready logs, weekly trend reviews, and safety-first escalation cues. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to record anxiety episodes, map triggers and avoidance patterns, plan coping responses, and prepare concise review notes for therapy or self-management. It supports tracking and behavior-change planning, not diagnosis or emergency treatment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can store anxiety-related notes in a local ~/anxiety/ folder, which may include sensitive health information. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those files as private, avoid saving details that should not exist in plaintext, and periodically review or delete old entries. <br>
Risk: The skill is not a substitute for licensed care, diagnosis, or emergency treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Seek licensed or emergency support for severe symptoms, self-harm risk, medical red flags, worsening function, or crisis situations. <br>
Risk: Local note updates could capture more personal detail than the user intended. <br>
Mitigation: Review planned writes before approval and keep entries focused on information needed for future support quality. <br>
