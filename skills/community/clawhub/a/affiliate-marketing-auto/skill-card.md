## Description: <br>
Automates affiliate marketing workflows for product discovery, promotional content generation, tracking link management, and revenue reporting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, content creators, and agent developers use this skill to identify affiliate products, generate promotional content, create tracking links, and analyze revenue performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Click tracking may collect raw visitor metadata such as IP address, user agent, referrer, and location without documented privacy controls. <br>
Mitigation: Add a privacy notice or consent basis, minimize or anonymize visitor metadata, and define retention and deletion controls before deployment on public traffic. <br>
Risk: Product and revenue outputs may be demo-grade unless real affiliate integrations are documented. <br>
Mitigation: Validate affiliate platform integrations, revenue calculations, and generated promotional content before using outputs for business decisions. <br>
