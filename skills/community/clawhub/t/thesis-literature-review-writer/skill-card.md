## Description: <br>
Writes detailed, academically rigorous Chinese literature review chapters or literature review sections for theses, dissertations, and undergraduate papers from a user's topic, research problem, variables, theory, method, population, references, and formatting requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, students, and academic writers use this skill to draft, expand, polish, organize, synthesize, or structure Chinese thesis literature review chapters and related review sections. It is focused on literature review work, including search scope, conceptual review, theoretical review, empirical review, themes, gaps, critique, summaries, and transitions to research questions or study design. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted academic text may contain citation placeholders, incomplete source details, or unsupported claims if the user does not provide references. <br>
Mitigation: Require users to replace placeholders with real sources, verify all source details and findings, and check citation accuracy before submission. <br>
Risk: Generated literature review prose could be misused as final academic work without appropriate review or disclosure. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a drafting aid, revise it against institutional academic-integrity rules, and ensure the final work reflects verified sources and the user's own analysis. <br>
