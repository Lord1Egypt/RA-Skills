## Description: <br>
Narrative Voice helps an agent answer emotional, creative, and reflective conversations with warm, story-like prose and light or deep response depth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ken0122](https://clawhub.ai/user/ken0122) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill when they want conversational responses to personal reflections, creative ideas, or life topics in a warm narrative style rather than terse factual answers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may broadly change conversational tone and language, making it unsuitable for concise factual answers, urgent task handling, or conversations where the current language must be preserved. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for reflective, literary Chinese responses in emotional or creative conversations, and explicitly override or disable it when factual precision, speed, or language preservation matters. <br>
