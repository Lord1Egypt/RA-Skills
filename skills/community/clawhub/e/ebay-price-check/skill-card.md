## Description: <br>
Search eBay for current and sold item prices, then summarize price ranges, averages, median prices, and item availability signals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[KL0nLutiy](https://clawhub.ai/user/KL0nLutiy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators can use this skill to check marketplace prices for a named item and compare active listings with sold-price history before making buying, selling, or valuation decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Item search terms are sent to eBay. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive or confidential search terms and review network access expectations before use. <br>
Risk: Price results may be incomplete or inaccurate because the skill appears to scrape public eBay pages and evidence notes setup and decode reliability issues. <br>
Mitigation: Validate results against eBay directly before making purchasing, pricing, or operational decisions. <br>
