## Description: <br>
Designs referral and affiliate program structure for startups. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
SaaS founders and startup operators use this skill to choose between referral and affiliate programs, then design incentives, mechanics, promotion, and a launch checklist. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for business metrics such as pricing, referral budget, and active-user counts. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for the conversation and avoid confidential customer or financial details unless the environment is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Referral and affiliate recommendations may affect unit economics, customer incentives, and fraud exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed incentives, attribution rules, reward triggers, and anti-fraud controls against your margins and policies before launch. <br>
