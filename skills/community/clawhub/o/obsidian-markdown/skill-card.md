## Description: <br>
Create and edit Obsidian Flavored Markdown with wikilinks, embeds, callouts, properties, and other Obsidian-specific syntax. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sadlay](https://clawhub.ai/user/sadlay) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, writers, researchers, and other Obsidian users use this skill to create or edit notes that rely on Obsidian-specific Markdown features such as wikilinks, embeds, callouts, frontmatter properties, tags, comments, highlighting, math, Mermaid diagrams, and footnotes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated note changes may introduce incorrect Obsidian syntax, broken wikilinks, or misleading content into important vault notes. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed edits and verify rendering in Obsidian reading view before applying or relying on them. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate when generic Markdown concepts such as tags or frontmatter are mentioned. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the task is intended for Obsidian-specific Markdown before applying wikilinks, callouts, embeds, or other Obsidian-only syntax. <br>
