## The Compliance Moat Nobody Else Has

**Why auto glass marketing is higher-risk than it looks:**

1. **AGSC Standard 150** — most shop owners know adhesive cure times matter; none know their marketing creates liability when they say "drive away in 30 minutes" without MDAT verification
2. **ADAS recalibration** — the fastest-growing hidden liability in auto glass; insurance adjusters are starting to require documented recalibration; shops without the equipment are losing insurance network referrals
3. **Nevada NRS 686A.2825** — the DOI has issued 11 enforcement actions in Clark County in the past 3 years for deductible waiver violations; shop owners think it's "just how the industry works" until they get the C&D letter
4. **OEM glass claims** — Safelite's legal team has flagged independent competitors claiming OEM when using aftermarket; FTC complaint pathway is low-cost for larger players to weaponize against independents
