## Description: <br>
Industry-adaptive B2B newsletter creation with stage, role, and geography-aware workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and B2B operators use this skill to plan, draft, refine, and distribute newsletters tailored to industry, company stage, role, geography, approval constraints, and business goals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill has an always-on activation setting, so an agent may consider it outside explicit newsletter requests. <br>
Mitigation: Install it when reusable newsletter workflow guidance is desired, or narrow/remove the always-on trigger when activation should require an explicit request. <br>
Risk: Newsletter drafts for regulated, employee-led, or high-impact contexts may contain claims that require review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's role-based approval workflow, including PR, legal, manager, brand, or stakeholder review when the context calls for it. <br>
