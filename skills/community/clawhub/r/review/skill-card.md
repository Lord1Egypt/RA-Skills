## Description: <br>
Professional reviewer and critic for structured feedback on documents, plans, code, decisions, strategies, designs, pitches, and arguments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[EthAgent](https://clawhub.ai/user/EthAgent) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to get direct, prioritized, actionable feedback on written material, plans, code, decisions, pitches, strategies, designs, and arguments before taking them further. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on casual opinion requests and review material the user did not intend to subject to structured critique. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive or unfinished material unless direct, structured feedback is intended. <br>
Risk: Review feedback can be incorrect, incomplete, or overconfident when source material lacks context or stakes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as proposals and verify important changes against domain expertise, tests, or source evidence before acting. <br>
