## Description: <br>
Provides outreach templates, AI agent DM lists, a bounty guide, and a ClawHub publish helper for promoting OpenClaw skills. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External developers and OpenClaw builders use AgentReach to draft promotional X threads, identify outreach targets, publish skills with a helper script, and plan bounty-driven monetization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The publish helper may upload the selected skill contents to ClawHub. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the script and confirm the target directory, slug, name, version, and changelog before running it. <br>
Risk: Outreach templates and DM lists can be misused for unsolicited or automated bulk messaging. <br>
Mitigation: Use the lists responsibly, customize messages for recipients, and avoid spam or unauthorized automation. <br>
