## Description: <br>
Peter Thiel's "Zero to One" skill helps agents apply startup strategy frameworks for contrarian thinking, monopoly strategy, founding teams, sales, and distribution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders, operators, and startup advisors use this skill to apply Zero to One-style frameworks to startup ideas, market selection, monopoly strategy, founding teams, fundraising, and distribution decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording can cause the skill to activate in unrelated technology or business conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow or remove generic trigger phrases if activation becomes noisy. <br>
