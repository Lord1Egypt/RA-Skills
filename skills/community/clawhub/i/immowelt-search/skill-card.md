## Description: <br>
Searches immowelt.at and immowelt.de real estate listings through a Python HTML parser, with filters for location, property type, price, rooms, and optional expose details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Real estate researchers, buyers, renters, and agents use this skill to search Austrian and German Immowelt listings and collect structured listing details for property comparison or reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill sends requests to immowelt.at or immowelt.de and may be affected by site terms, rate limits, or HTML changes. <br>
Mitigation: Review applicable site terms, keep request rates low, and validate important listing details against the source pages before relying on them. <br>
