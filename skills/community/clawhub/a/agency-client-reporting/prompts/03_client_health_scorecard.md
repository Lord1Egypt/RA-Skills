# Prompt 3: Client Health Scorecard

## Prompt

You are a client success director assessing the health of a client account. Your goal is to produce an objective, actionable health scorecard that helps the internal agency team identify churn risk early and take proactive steps to retain and grow the account.

This is an INTERNAL document — not client-facing. Be honest and direct.

**Client Context:**
- Client Name: [CLIENT NAME]
- Months retained: [e.g., 8 months]
- Monthly contract value: [e.g., $4,500/month]
- Services: [e.g., SEO + Content + Google Ads]
- Account Manager: [Name]
- Last NPS score: [e.g., 8/10, or "Unknown"]

**Score each dimension (answer honestly):**

1. **Payment reliability:** [Always on time / Occasionally late / Consistently late / In arrears]
2. **Service engagement:** [Actively engaged — reviews reports, gives fast feedback / Somewhat engaged — responds within a week / Passively engaged — rarely responds / Disengaged — we rarely hear from them]
3. **Communication responsiveness:** [Same-day / 1-3 days / 1 week / >1 week or no response]
4. **Budget trajectory:** [Increasing / Stable / Declining / Threatening to cut]
5. **Strategic alignment:** [Their goals are clear and our work directly supports them / Somewhat aligned / Goals unclear or drifting / Misaligned — they want things outside our scope]
6. **Relationship depth:** [We know the decision-maker + multiple stakeholders / We know 1-2 contacts / Only know the day-to-day PM / Gatekeeper only — no exec access]
7. **Referrals given:** [Active referrer (sent 1+ leads) / Offered to refer but nothing materialized / Never mentioned referrals / We've asked but they're resistant]
8. **Escalation history:** [Zero escalations / 1 minor escalation, resolved / Multiple escalations / Ongoing escalation or formal complaint]

**Additional context:**
[Any recent wins, concerns, relationship dynamics, or events that should inform the score]

---

Generate the client health scorecard with:

1. **Overall Health Score** (0-100) with letter grade
   - Green Zone (80-100): Healthy — grow the account
   - Yellow Zone (50-79): Watch — proactive retention needed
   - Red Zone (0-49): At risk — escalate immediately

2. **Dimension Breakdown** (table)
   | Dimension | Score (0-10) | Rationale |
   (8 rows, one per dimension above — be specific in the rationale)

3. **Churn Risk Flags** (list only the red/yellow flags with urgency level: 🔴 High / 🟡 Medium)
   - Each flag: what the signal is + what it typically means

4. **Recommended Action Plan**
   - Immediate (this week): 1-2 specific actions
   - Short-term (this month): 2-3 relationship plays
   - If score is Red: escalation protocol (who to involve, what to say)

5. **Internal Note: Retention Play**
   - Based on this profile, which retention play fits best?
   - Options: [Executive sponsor engagement / Surprise value add / Contract restructure / Results showcase / Exit interview + rescue offer / Let them churn cleanly]
   - Recommended play with 2-3 sentence rationale

**Tone:** Internal, direct, no sugarcoating. This is a business assessment, not a report card to share with the client.
