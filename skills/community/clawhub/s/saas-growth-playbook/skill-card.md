## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The playbook recommends exit surveys, session recordings, NPS feedback, and usage-based health scoring, which can involve customer data and privacy-sensitive behavioral records. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm customer notice, consent, retention, access controls, and applicable privacy obligations before collecting or acting on that data. <br>
Risk: Pricing, churn, activation, and expansion recommendations are generic business guidance and may not fit a specific market, contract, or customer segment. <br>
Mitigation: Run small experiments, track conversion and retention impact, and review results before broad rollout. <br>
