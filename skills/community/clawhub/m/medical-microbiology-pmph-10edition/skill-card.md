## Description: <br>
A medical microbiology and infectious disease skill set based on the 10th edition of People's Medical Publishing House Medical Microbiology, covering 153 clinical and laboratory skills across bacteriology, virology, immunology, disinfection and sterilization, microbiome science, and infection control. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Medical students, clinicians, laboratory staff, public health professionals, and infection control teams use this skill as an educational microbiology reference for classification, diagnostic reasoning, laboratory workflow review, infection control concepts, and textbook-aligned study support. It should not be used as standalone medical advice, diagnosis, prescription, emergency-care guidance, or a substitute for qualified clinical, laboratory, biosafety, or institutional review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat microbiology diagnostics, antimicrobial therapy, infection control, or laboratory procedure guidance as standalone medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only as educational reference material and require qualified clinical, laboratory, biosafety, or institutional review for diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, emergency care, and local protocol decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill content may not consistently enforce clinician-only safety boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Deploy with system-level medical safety policies that escalate diagnosis, prescription, dosing, emergency care, self-treatment, and biosafety decisions to qualified professionals. <br>
