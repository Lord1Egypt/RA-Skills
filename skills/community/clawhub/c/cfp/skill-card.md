## Description: <br>
Certified Financial Planner (CFP) style financial planning assistant that helps users with comprehensive financial planning including budgeting, insurance needs analysis, investment planning, retirement planning, tax optimization, and estate planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for general financial education and planning conversations across cash flow, insurance, investments, taxes, retirement, and estate planning. It should not be treated as a substitute for licensed financial, tax, legal, or investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat general financial planning output as personalized licensed financial, tax, legal, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as general financial education and recommend licensed professionals for personalized decisions. <br>
Risk: Financial planning conversations can involve sensitive personal financial details. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to share only the details needed for the conversation and avoid unnecessary sensitive information. <br>
