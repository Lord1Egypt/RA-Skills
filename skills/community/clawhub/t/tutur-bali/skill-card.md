## Description: <br>
Produce, adapt, or review text for Bahasa Bali with an offline-first regional-language workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ramaaditya49](https://clawhub.ai/user/ramaaditya49) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to draft, adapt, or review Bahasa Bali text for ordinary, tourism, community, and public contexts while surfacing speech-level and cultural uncertainty before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bahasa Bali output may be incorrect or inappropriate for temple, ritual, legal, educational, official, or status-sensitive contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Treat output as language assistance and require review by a competent speaker or domain expert before use in sensitive contexts. <br>
Risk: The skill may be asked to invent undocumented vocabulary or flatten speech levels into a generic style. <br>
Mitigation: Use the bundled source and style references, preserve Indonesian terms when no verified Balinese equivalent is known, and ask for audience or register details when needed. <br>
