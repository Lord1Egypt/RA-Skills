## Description: <br>
Create, inspect, and edit Microsoft Word documents and DOCX files with reliable styles, numbering, tracked changes, tables, sections, and compatibility checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and document-focused agents use this skill when working with Microsoft Word or DOCX files, especially when tracked changes, comments, styles, numbering, fields, tables, templates, sections, or round-trip compatibility matter. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive Word documents may be inspected or edited by the agent during DOCX workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Use normal caution with sensitive documents, keep backups of important files, and review final document changes before sharing. <br>
Risk: Tracked changes, comments, fields, tables, headers, and numbering can survive or drift across Word, LibreOffice, Google Docs, and conversion tools. <br>
Mitigation: Use minimal structure-preserving edits, inspect review metadata and field outputs, and verify round-trip compatibility in the target editor before delivery. <br>
