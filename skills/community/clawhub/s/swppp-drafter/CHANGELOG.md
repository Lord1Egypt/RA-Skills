## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-24
Initial release. Turns a construction project's location, disturbed acreage, soils, slopes, receiving waters, sequence of construction, and planned BMP set into a DRAFT EPA 2022 CGP–aligned Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan (SWPPP) with site assessment, BMP selection matrix, inspection-and-corrective-action schedule, training and signatory blocks, and a QSD / QSP / PE open-questions list for review before NOI submittal.
