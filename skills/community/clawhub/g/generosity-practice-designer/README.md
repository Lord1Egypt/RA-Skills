# Generosity Practice Designer
Designs sustainable generosity practices that align personal values with meaningful giving.
## What it does
- Assesses your natural generosity style (time, money, skills, presence, or connection) and maps it to sustainable giving patterns
- Helps distinguish genuine voluntary generosity from guilt-driven or performative giving to unlock the wellbeing benefits of authentic giving
- Generates a personalized generosity architecture with monthly rhythm, specific practices, and boundary-setting to prevent burnout
## Example scenarios
**1. 随份子文化下的慷慨焦虑**
> 👤 每年朋友圈+亲戚群里随份子就花掉我一个月工资，而且每次都是硬着头皮给。有没有既能维持社交关系又不让我觉得被掏空的给法？
> 🤖 中国式随份子减压策略——先定义三类关系（至亲/好友/普通社交），每个级别设置上限；对普通关系的份子钱改送实用小礼物；和几个好友约定互相不收份子钱，改聚餐庆祝；每次随份子后记录感受，逐步优化自己的社交边界。

**2. 设计全方位慷慨练习**
> 👤 "I want to be more generous but not just throw money at problems. Design a holistic giving practice."
> 🤖 Multi-mode generosity plan — time (skill-based volunteering matched to professional expertise), money (giving-budget calculator with percentage-of-income framework), attention (deep-listening practice for relationships), and knowledge (free mentoring/teaching commitment) — with quarterly review ritual and generosity journal.

**3. 建立家庭多代传承的给予传统**
> 👤 "Our family wants to build a multigenerational giving tradition. Design the practice."
> 🤖 Family giving framework — annual family giving summit agenda, children's giving-allowance system (3 jars: save, spend, give), family-foundation or DAF setup guide, cause-selection voting process, and legacy-giving story collection.
