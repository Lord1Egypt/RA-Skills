## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may expose real seed phrases, private keys, wallet files, API keys, or production credentials while planning Solana deployments. <br>
Mitigation: Use placeholders during planning, keep production secrets out of chat, and move credential handling into environment variables, secret managers, or deployment-specific configuration. <br>
Risk: Generated setup, deployment, or transaction commands could affect wallets, clusters, or infrastructure if run without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands before execution, test against localnet or devnet first, and confirm cluster, fee payer, deploy authority, and upgrade authority settings before production use. <br>
