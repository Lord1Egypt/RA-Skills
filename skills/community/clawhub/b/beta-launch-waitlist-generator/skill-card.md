## Description: <br>
Generates complete pre-launch campaigns for SaaS, apps, courses, hardware, and communities, including waitlist landing pages, countdown emails, Product Hunt launch kits, and early-access nurture sequences. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, indie hackers, course creators, community builders, hardware teams, and agencies use this prompt-only skill to generate pre-launch marketing assets that build a waitlist, coordinate launch-day outreach, and convert early-access users. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated campaign copy can include incorrect or misleading claims, testimonials, launch metrics, deadlines, pricing, or urgency. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all factual claims, proof points, deadlines, offers, and pricing before publishing or sending the generated assets. <br>
Risk: Generated outreach or upvote-request copy can conflict with Product Hunt, email, Slack, Discord, Reddit, or community rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review each outreach asset against the target platform's rules and only send messages where the audience has appropriate consent or context. <br>
