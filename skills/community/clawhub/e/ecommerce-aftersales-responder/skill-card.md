## Description: <br>
Generates standardized Chinese after-sales customer-service replies for return or exchange requests, logistics exceptions, and compensation negotiations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer support teams and service agents use this skill to draft consistent Chinese responses for common e-commerce after-sales cases, including returns, exchanges, refunds, logistics issues, and compensation discussions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The drafted response may rely on missing or incorrect order, logistics, refund, or compensation facts. <br>
Mitigation: Verify order records, carrier status, customer evidence, and approved policy limits before sending the response. <br>
Risk: A response could promise refunds, replacements, or compensation beyond the brand's authorized limits. <br>
Mitigation: Keep all remedies within approved refund and compensation rules, and escalate cases that exceed the agent's authority. <br>
Risk: Customer-service drafting may include more personal customer information than needed. <br>
Mitigation: Use only the customer details required to resolve the case and avoid including unnecessary personal data in the response. <br>
