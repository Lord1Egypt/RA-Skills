## Description: <br>
Assess construction data quality using completeness, accuracy, consistency, timeliness, and validity metrics with automated validation patterns, thresholds, and reporting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[datadrivenconstruction](https://clawhub.ai/user/datadrivenconstruction) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Construction data practitioners, developers, and project teams use this skill to assess project datasets for completeness, accuracy, consistency, timeliness, and validity, then review findings and optional exportable reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Construction datasets and generated quality reports may contain sensitive project information. <br>
Mitigation: Review the files provided to the skill and use a trusted local output folder before exporting reports. <br>
Risk: Quality findings depend on user-provided data and validation thresholds. <br>
Mitigation: Review reported issues, thresholds, and validation rules before using the results for project decisions. <br>
