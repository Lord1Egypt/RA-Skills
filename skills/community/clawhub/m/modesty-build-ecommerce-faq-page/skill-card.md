## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common ecommerce questions. Use when working on FAQ content for ecommerce operators, growth teams, merchants. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators, growth teams, and merchants use this skill to draft customer-facing FAQ answers for online stores, then refine them for accuracy and publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ answers may make inaccurate claims about shipping, returns, pricing, warranties, legal obligations, or privacy. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated answers against current store policies before publication. <br>
Risk: Web search or external enrichment may send business context in queries. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive customer, pricing, or proprietary details in prompts or search queries unless approved. <br>
