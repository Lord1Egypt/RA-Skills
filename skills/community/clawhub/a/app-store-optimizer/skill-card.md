## Use Case: <br>
External developers, app marketers, and product teams use this skill to diagnose mobile app discoverability or conversion problems and produce focused ASO deliverables such as keyword strategies, metadata rewrites, visual asset recommendations, competitor analysis, and full strategy reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: ASO recommendations can become misleading if they are published without current store policy checks, factual substantiation, trademark review, or validation against real app performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Verify app store rules, trademark constraints, factual claims, social proof, localized messaging, and competitor observations before shipping listing changes. <br>
Risk: Competitor and market observations may reflect incomplete or outdated information when the user has not provided live ASO tool data. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as hypotheses to test with App Store Product Page Optimization, Google Play Experiments, ranking tracking, and conversion metrics. <br>
