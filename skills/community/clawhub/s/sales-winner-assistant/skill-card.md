## Description: <br>
Sales Winner Assistant is a Chinese-language sales enablement skill for Yonyou sales teams that analyzes target companies, recommends win strategies, and drafts sales talk tracks, follow-up plans, and negotiation guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[areskaks777](https://clawhub.ai/user/areskaks777) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees in Yonyou sales roles use this skill to research prospects, assess opportunities, plan follow-up, and prepare customer-facing sales messaging for enterprise software deals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can turn broad sales requests into web research and recommendations that may contain outdated, incomplete, or misleading customer facts. <br>
Mitigation: Review web-sourced facts and cited sources before using the output in sales decisions or customer communications. <br>
Risk: The skill describes customer-record updates, opportunity-stage changes, task creation, reminders, and colleague notifications without clear approval controls. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill read-only unless an authorized user explicitly approves each CRM update, status change, task, reminder, or notification. <br>
Risk: Sales notes, customer profiles, and contact details may include confidential or personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Enter confidential customer notes or personal contact details only when the organization permits that use and the deployment environment is approved for that data. <br>
