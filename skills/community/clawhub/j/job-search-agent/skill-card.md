## Description: <br>
AI-powered job search assistant that searches multiple job boards, matches opportunities against your CV, and helps you apply faster. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career-support agents use this skill to search job boards, rank opportunities against a CV, draft personalized cover letters, and track applications. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill encourages automated bulk job applications without clear review, consent, or personal-data handling safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for searching, ranking, drafting, and tracking first; require explicit approval for every employer, role, resume, cover letter, and form answer before anything is submitted. <br>
