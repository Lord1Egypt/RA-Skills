## Description: <br>
Autonomous web research agent that performs multi-step searches, follows links, extracts data, and synthesizes findings into structured reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kassimisai](https://clawhub.ai/user/kassimisai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and other users use this skill to plan and conduct web research across multiple sources, compare findings, and produce structured reports with citations, confidence notes, and gaps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Live web research can expose sensitive query terms or selected URLs to search and fetch services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using the skill for private or sensitive topics that should not be sent to external search or fetch tools. <br>
Risk: Research findings can be incomplete, stale, or based on sources with uneven reliability. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources before relying on important claims, and treat the skill's confidence and gap notes as prompts for human verification. <br>
