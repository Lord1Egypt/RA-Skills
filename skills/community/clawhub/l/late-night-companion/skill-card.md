## Description: <br>
Proactively offers gentle, non-judgmental emotional support during local late-night hours without forced positivity or unsolicited advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codenova58](https://clawhub.ai/user/codenova58) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users who are active during local late-night hours use this skill for low-pressure emotional check-ins, deep listening, and quiet session closing without unsolicited advice. It is not a substitute for professional or emergency mental-health support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may initiate sensitive late-night emotional conversations when the user is active or online. <br>
Mitigation: Install only where proactive check-ins are desired, and confirm DND, disable, and proactive-suppression controls are available. <br>
Risk: Saved emotional context or risk flags may expose sensitive personal information if retained longer than needed. <br>
Mitigation: Use memory-clearing controls and keep any remembered stress, emotion, comfort-style, or risk fields lightweight and based only on what the user shared. <br>
Risk: Users could mistake the skill for professional or emergency mental-health support. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the artifact's safety boundary visible: for intent to harm self or others, encourage immediate contact with local emergency services or a crisis helpline. <br>
