## Description: <br>
Helps teams audit organizational knowledge, design knowledge-base structures, create documentation templates, and maintain freshness, contribution, and health practices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Teams, managers, and documentation owners use this skill to assess knowledge-management gaps, capture at-risk expertise, and produce runbooks, ADRs, troubleshooting guides, taxonomies, dashboards, and review workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated documentation may include or request sensitive internal knowledge, credentials, or private operational details. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste raw credentials or secrets into generated docs, and use only Slack, meeting notes, or internal documents approved for the intended audience. <br>
Risk: Knowledge-management recommendations can become misleading if teams adopt them without review against current practices. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated audits, templates, ownership assignments, and freshness schedules with the responsible subject-matter experts before relying on them. <br>
