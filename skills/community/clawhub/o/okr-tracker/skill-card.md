## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Enabled integrations may require sensitive API credentials for tools such as Notion, Jira, or Feishu/Lark. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm which integrations are enabled before installation and use least-privilege tokens where possible. <br>
Risk: OKR reports can expose sensitive enterprise, team, or individual performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated reports as confidential, review export and sharing settings, and apply access controls before distribution. <br>
Risk: Data-flow details for third-party integrations are not fully clarified in the evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Verify whether integrations are read-only, whether reports are exported or shared, and what data is cached locally before connecting work accounts. <br>
