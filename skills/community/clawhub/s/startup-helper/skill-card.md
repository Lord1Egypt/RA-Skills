## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Startup, legal, tax, banking, ICP, and trademark guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify requirements with official agencies, current platform documentation, or qualified legal, tax, banking, and trademark professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Company and identity documents may be sensitive when completing registration, filing, banking, or trademark workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Submit sensitive documents only through trusted official platforms or selected service providers, and avoid sharing them in agent conversations unless required by an approved workflow. <br>
Risk: The bundled checklist generator writes a Markdown checklist file in the skill assets directory. <br>
Mitigation: Run the generator only in an intended workspace and review generated checklist content before using it operationally. <br>
