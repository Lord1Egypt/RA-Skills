# Dropshipping Product Research
Helps beginners and small operators evaluate whether a product is worth testing — structured scoring, risk filtering, and clear go/test/reject decisions.
## What it does
- Produces a **product viability scorecard** across five dimensions: demand, competition, margin, creative fit, and risk
- Performs **market competition analysis** including ad-intensity checks, review-count saturation, and differentiation-gap assessment
- Delivers a **Go/Test/Reject decision framework** with actionable hypotheses and next-step testing recommendations
## Example scenarios
**1. 在多多跨境找爆品的野路子**
> 👤 我在Temu上做店群，但不知道选什么品好。有没有快速找到下一个爆品的方法？
> 🤖 输出Temu选品方法论——第一步关注抖音/小红书/快手带货直播间找新品（搜索量上升但商家数未暴涨的阶段）、第二步1688跨境专供频道按新品排序找现货品、第三步Temu和SHEIN前台按飙升/最新排序找涨势品、第四步Google Trends查目标国搜索趋势（找上升30%以上的品），关键工具：1688跨境专供+神鹰数据+Google Trends+Temu前台。

**2. Score 5 product ideas**
> 👤 Score these 5 product ideas for a US-focused Shopify dropshipping store.
> 🤖 5-product scorecard — demand (Google Trends + Amazon BSR proxy), competition (ad-intensity check, review-count saturation), margin (cost-price vs. market-price with ad-cost breakeven), creative potential (video-ad suitability score), risk (shipping time, return rate, IP infringement check) — ranked with go/test/reject verdict.

**3. Too late to enter?**
> 👤 I found a product on AliExpress with 10,000 orders. Is it too late to enter?
> 🤖 Market-saturation analysis — competition-velocity check, ad-auction heat (CPM trend), differentiation-gap assessment, and "late entrant" strategy options (niche-down, geo-target underserved region, content-first approach).
