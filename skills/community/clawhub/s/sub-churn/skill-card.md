## Description: <br>
Monitor subscription commerce for churn risk by blending account activity signals, payment failure tracking, and retention flows including compensation offers and downgrade paths. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rijoyai](https://clawhub.ai/user/rijoyai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Subscription commerce operators and retention teams use this skill to identify churn risk, plan dunning outreach, and prepare downgrade or pause options for customers at risk of canceling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated dunning, discount, pause, downgrade, or cancellation messaging may not match the merchant's policies or regional subscription rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review all customer-facing copy, offer terms, and cancellation wording before sending. <br>
Risk: Churn exports or examples can contain unnecessary sensitive customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Use the minimum needed fields and remove or anonymize personal data before sharing examples with an agent. <br>
