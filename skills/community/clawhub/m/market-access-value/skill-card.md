## Description: <br>
Drafts structured payer-facing pharmacoeconomic value propositions for market access, HTA, pricing, and reimbursement workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Academic writers, market access teams, and health economics analysts use this skill to prepare structured payer value narratives, ICER discussion, budget impact text, value dossier sections, and payer objection handlers from confirmed drug and market inputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated payer, HTA, or pharmacoeconomic content may be incomplete or inaccurate if inputs are outdated, unsupported, or outside the documented scope. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated material for regulatory, medical, and commercial accuracy before using it externally, and stop when required inputs are missing. <br>
Risk: The local script writes a generated text file to the requested output path. <br>
Mitigation: Choose an output path inside the workspace and inspect the file before sharing or relying on it. <br>
