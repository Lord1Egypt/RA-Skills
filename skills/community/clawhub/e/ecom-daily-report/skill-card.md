每日整合直播电商行业最新资讯，覆盖千川政策、平台动态、爆款素材、竞品动向和行业大盘，支持品类定制与日报保存。 <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Live-commerce operators, media buyers, and merchants use this skill to generate daily industry reports that summarize platform policy changes, advertising updates, competitor movement, trending creative material, and market signals. It can tailor action points by product category and save Markdown or HTML reports locally. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill searches public web sources, so daily report items and recommendations may reflect incomplete, stale, or inaccurate public information. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited sources, dates, and recommended actions before making advertising, operational, or compliance decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill writes report files to a configurable local folder and may read prior reports from that folder for comparison. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated report directory and avoid pointing the save path at sensitive or unrelated folders. <br>
