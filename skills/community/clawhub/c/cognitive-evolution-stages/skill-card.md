## Description: <br>
Helps agents diagnose where a person or team is stuck in a five-stage cognitive development process and produce a stage map, capability gap analysis, and deliberate practice design. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[deciqai](https://clawhub.ai/user/deciqai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, analysts, creators, and teams use this skill to identify whether recent work reflects acquisition, imitation, trade-off understanding, creation, or self-revising evolution. It turns that diagnosis into a concrete next-stage capability gap and four-week deliberate practice plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the stage diagnosis as authoritative rather than advisory coaching. <br>
Mitigation: Review stage labels and practice plans against concrete recent work before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The five-stage framework may steer an agent toward a single developmental interpretation when another explanation is more appropriate. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when the stated activation conditions fit, and avoid it when the user lacks basic domain familiarity or the block is motivational. <br>
