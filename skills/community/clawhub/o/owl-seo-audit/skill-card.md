## Description: <br>
Perform comprehensive SEO audits including technical SEO, content analysis, backlink profiling, and competitive benchmarking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[btizzy](https://clawhub.ai/user/btizzy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, SEO practitioners, and website operators use this skill to structure audits of technical SEO, on-page content, off-page signals, and competitive opportunities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO audit guidance may be incomplete or misleading if applied without checking the target website and current search platform requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated audit findings before acting on them, and verify recommendations against the target site's data and current search guidance. <br>
Risk: Backlink and competitive analysis can involve third-party website data with varying reliability. <br>
Mitigation: Treat backlink and competitor observations as advisory and confirm them with trusted analytics or SEO tooling before making business decisions. <br>
