## Description: <br>
Navigate e-commerce privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, cookie consent, data collection policies, email marketing compliance, and customer data handling to protect your business from fines and build customer trust. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce operators, privacy leads, and business teams use this skill to assess privacy compliance obligations and produce practical implementation plans for data inventory, cookie consent, privacy policies, email marketing, data-subject requests, vendor handling, and breach response. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides privacy compliance guidance that could be mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as operational guidance and have qualified legal or privacy counsel review obligations before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The skill may suggest real business changes such as deleting email lists, changing retention settings, installing paid tools, or altering tracking behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before making purchasing, deletion, retention, cookie, analytics, or marketing configuration changes. <br>
Risk: Privacy laws and enforcement thresholds change over time and vary by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current legal requirements for the relevant jurisdictions before implementing the generated plan. <br>
