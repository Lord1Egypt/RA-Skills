## Description: <br>
Ai Tarot provides Chinese-language tarot learning and interpretation guidance across card meanings, spreads, tarot systems, reading methods, psychology, and ethical boundaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Tarot learners and readers use this skill to study the 78-card deck, compare major tarot systems, interpret common spreads, and frame readings as self-reflection rather than professional advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat tarot readings as medical, legal, financial, or definitive predictive advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present readings as reflective or entertainment guidance and direct users to qualified professionals for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive personal details or private spread images while seeking an interpretation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid requesting sensitive medical, legal, financial, or deeply personal information and remind users to limit what they disclose. <br>
