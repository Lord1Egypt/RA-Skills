## Description: <br>
Plan Pilates sessions, refine form, and track steady progress with equipment-aware modifications, core-focused cues, and weekly reviews. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan short Pilates practices, adapt sessions to equipment and experience, repair one form issue at a time, and review weekly progress. It is intended for movement quality, consistency, posture awareness, and safety-aware habit building, not diagnosis or medical care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat Pilates guidance as medical, rehabilitation, pregnancy, postpartum, or injury advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep scope to movement quality and practice planning, use conservative modifications, stop routine coaching for red flags, and recommend a clinician or qualified instructor for significant pain, recent surgery, neurological symptoms, pregnancy or postpartum concerns, or other medical uncertainty. <br>
Risk: Local notes in ~/pilates/ may contain personal goals, symptoms, safety limits, or session history. <br>
Mitigation: Ask before creating or changing local files, store only details that improve future sessions, keep medical details minimal, and remind users to review stored notes when privacy matters. <br>
