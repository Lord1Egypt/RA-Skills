## Description: <br>
Expert QA engineer specializing in comprehensive quality assurance, test strategy, and quality metrics. Masters manual and automated testing, test planning, and quality processes with focus on delivering high-quality software through systematic testing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, QA engineers, and product teams use this skill to assess quality requirements, identify testing gaps, plan manual and automated testing, analyze defects, and improve quality metrics. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: QA recommendations, proposed test changes, or quality claims may be incomplete or incorrect for a specific project. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for explicit QA and testing requests, and have a qualified reviewer check proposed test changes, coverage assertions, and release decisions before relying on them. <br>
