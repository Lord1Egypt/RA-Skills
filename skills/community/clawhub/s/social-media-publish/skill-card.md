## Description: <br>
社交媒体浏览器自动化发布。支持微信公众号、百家号、小红书等平台。当用户说"发公众号"、"发百家号"、"发布到..."时使用此技能。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lsmonet](https://clawhub.ai/user/lsmonet) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, operators, and social media teams use this skill to guide browser-based publishing of articles or posts to WeChat Official Accounts, Baijiahao, and Xiaohongshu. It helps collect the platform, title, content, and cover-image choices before carrying out the posting flow in a logged-in web dashboard. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill operates logged-in social media publishing dashboards and may publish content publicly or submit it for review. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target platform, title, body, cover image, and whether the action saves a draft or publishes publicly before each posting flow; prefer draft saving where available. <br>
