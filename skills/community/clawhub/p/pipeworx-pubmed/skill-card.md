## Description: <br>
Search PubMed for biomedical articles by keyword, author, or MeSH term, then retrieve article metadata and structured abstracts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[brucegutman](https://clawhub.ai/user/brucegutman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, agents, and biomedical researchers use this skill to find PubMed citations, inspect article metadata, and retrieve abstracts for literature review workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Submitted lookup values are sent to a Pipeworx-hosted remote service. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive private address information unless that data sharing is acceptable for the intended use case. <br>
