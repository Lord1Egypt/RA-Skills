## Description: <br>
Turn rough or incomplete product ideas into polished, runnable interactive demos with a structured intake, planning, prototype review, and production validation pipeline. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stdoses72](https://clawhub.ai/user/stdoses72) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and coding-agent users use this skill to convert vague product concepts into interactive demos, with explicit assumptions, a review gate, and follow-up edge case validation. It is best suited for prototypes, MVP-style mockups, dashboards, workflow demos, and AI tool demos where the user has not fully specified the workflow or implementation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prototype-oriented file changes, dependencies, mock data, or reference research may reflect reasonable assumptions that are not suitable for the final product. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated files, dependencies, mock data, external references, and stated assumptions before using the demo as a production basis. <br>
