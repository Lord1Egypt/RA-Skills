## Description: <br>
Helps create Chinese-language English vocabulary lessons for 11-year-old learners, including story-based word grouping, study schedules, quizzes, spaced review reminders, progress adjustments, and local PNG teaching materials. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tixianxian](https://clawhub.ai/user/tixianxian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users such as parents, tutors, and learners use this skill to turn a vocabulary list, deadline, and available study time into a structured English vocabulary learning plan with story mnemonics, assessment tasks, generated teaching images, and review reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may ask the agent to install Python packages and run generated code to create local teaching images. <br>
Mitigation: Review package-install commands before execution and run image generation only in an approved local environment. <br>
Risk: Generated vocabulary meanings, pronunciation guidance, examples, or quiz scoring may be inaccurate for a learner's curriculum. <br>
Mitigation: Have a parent, tutor, or teacher review lesson content and assessments before relying on them. <br>
