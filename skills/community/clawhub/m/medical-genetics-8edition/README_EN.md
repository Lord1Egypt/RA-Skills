## Project Introduction

This project systematically integrates medical genetics, genomics, metabolic diseases, prenatal diagnosis, gene therapy, and epigenetic regulation, covering **131 key clinical skills**.

**Target Audience**: Clinical geneticists, medical students, genetic counselors, precision medicine researchers, prenatal diagnosis healthcare teams

**Reference Textbook**: *Medical Genetics* 8th Edition

**⚠️ Risk ⚠️**: This skill pack covers genetic diagnosis, risk assessment, prenatal screening, gene therapy strategies, and ethical decision-making — topics that may be misused as standalone medical advice.

**Mitigation**: Use outputs solely as educational or clinician-reviewed reference materials, and verify recommendations against current official guidelines, local protocols, and qualified genetics specialists.


