## Description: <br>
Provides China travel lookup guidance for flights, hotels, trains, attractions, itinerary planning, and price comparison across Qunar, Ctrip, Fliggy, and 12306. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Travel planners, business travelers, and agents use this skill to compare China travel options, plan itineraries, monitor prices, and draft API or script-based lookup workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes third-party API and scraping examples that may require partner access or may violate service terms if used without authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer official travel APIs, confirm access rights before use, and review any separately created or downloaded scripts and packages before execution. <br>
Risk: Travel searches may involve sensitive itinerary, account, or payment information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing account, payment, or sensitive itinerary details unless intentionally using a trusted service. <br>
Risk: Prices and availability can change quickly, so generated travel guidance may become stale. <br>
Mitigation: Verify fares, hotel rates, train availability, and booking terms with the relevant provider before making travel decisions. <br>
