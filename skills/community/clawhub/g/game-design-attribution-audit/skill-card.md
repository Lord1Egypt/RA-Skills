## Description: <br>
Audits game outcomes through attribution theory to assess how players explain success, failure, fairness, control, and learnability. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, UX researchers, and product teams use this skill to review combat deaths, failure loops, loot or economy outcomes, onboarding mistakes, and other game scenarios for perceived fairness, control, and learning clarity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
