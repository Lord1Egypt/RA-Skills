## Use Case: <br>
Business strategists, product leaders, operators, and agent users can use this skill to frame decisions through a strong product, technology, channel, quality, brand, rules, and long-term value lens. Outputs should be treated as a styled analytical perspective, not as Dong Mingzhu's actual views. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill speaks in a strong simulated persona and may be mistaken for the real person's current views. <br>
Mitigation: Label outputs as simulated perspective and avoid presenting them as factual statements from Dong Mingzhu. <br>
Risk: Gender-based leadership generalizations may be inappropriate for workplace decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for strategic framing, and avoid relying on gendered claims when evaluating people, teams, or employment decisions. <br>
Risk: Generic business prompts may unintentionally activate the persona style. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit activation phrasing when the persona is desired and ask for neutral analysis when it is not. <br>
