## Description: <br>
Helps agents design, optimize, or audit website footers for navigation, SEO, accessibility, and conversion. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and marketing site builders use this skill to ask an agent for footer structures, link groups, anchor text suggestions, SEO checks, and accessibility checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Project context files may contain secrets or private details that could be reflected in footer recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review .claude/project-context.md and .cursor/project-context.md before use and remove secrets or sensitive internal details. <br>
Risk: Footer advice can over-link pages or create accessibility issues if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed links for priority and validate contrast, touch targets, keyboard navigation, and screen reader structure before publishing. <br>
