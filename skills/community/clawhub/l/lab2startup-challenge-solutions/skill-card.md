## Description: <br>
Helps users diagnose and address ownership, productization, and market-fit challenges in teacher-student startup collaborations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[earthwalking](https://clawhub.ai/user/earthwalking) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and startup teams use this skill to create a structured response plan for teacher-student co-creation challenges, including role division, technical maturity, productization, market education, and implementation risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Governance, intellectual property, equity, and commercialization recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific venture. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning guidance and review decisions with qualified legal, business, and domain advisors before acting. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose confidential project, research, or startup details while seeking tailored advice. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive details unless the user is comfortable providing them to the agent running the skill. <br>
