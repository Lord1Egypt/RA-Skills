## Description: <br>
Helps physics teachers design structured lesson frameworks that build physics concepts, laws, models, experiments, applications, classroom summaries, and anonymized learner profiles around core physics ideas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External educators and lesson-planning agents use this skill to draft physics lesson frameworks, concept-introduction paths, experiment prompts, model-building activities, application exercises, and classroom summaries. The skill is intended to support teacher review, not replace teacher preparation or classroom judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Student profile templates could receive identifiable learner data. <br>
Mitigation: Use pseudonyms and avoid entering identifiable student data unless the deployment environment has appropriate privacy controls. <br>
Risk: Classroom experiment suggestions could be applied without local safety review. <br>
Mitigation: Review all experiment suggestions for classroom safety, materials, and age appropriateness before use. <br>
Risk: Lesson-plan output could be mistaken for complete teacher preparation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning frameworks and require teacher review before classroom use. <br>
