## Description: <br>
Plans and documents detailed data migrations, including schema mapping, ETL pipeline design, validation tests, rollback strategies, and runbook creation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Engineering teams, CTOs, consultants, and operations teams use this skill to plan migrations between CRM, ERP, database, and SaaS systems and to produce migration runbooks with schema mappings, ETL steps, validation checks, cutover gates, and rollback plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated migration plans can be incomplete or unsafe if applied directly to production systems. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm authorization, backups, tested rollback, non-production validation, downtime approval, and go/no-go criteria before execution. <br>
Risk: Migration guidance may involve sensitive or regulated data. <br>
Mitigation: Complete privacy, compliance, and least-privilege access reviews before using any generated runbook with real datasets. <br>
