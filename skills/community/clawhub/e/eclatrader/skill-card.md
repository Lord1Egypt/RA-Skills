## Description: <br>
Trader Bot is an educational trading mentor that explains price action, technical analysis, and risk management logic for day-trading learners. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cryptofedge](https://clawhub.ai/user/cryptofedge) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners and trading practitioners use this skill to ask for educational explanations of technical indicators, position-sizing calculations, stop-loss framing, and theoretical reviews of trading plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake educational trading analysis or position-sizing math for guaranteed financial advice or trade instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for education and trading-plan review only; independently verify assumptions, apply risk controls, and do not treat responses as an instruction to place trades. <br>
Risk: The skill can mention companion app links when users ask for practice resources. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the publisher and destination before following external links or relying on companion-app materials. <br>
