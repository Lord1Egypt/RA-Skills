## Description: <br>
Provides concise Chinese workplace and career guidance in the persona of the career content creator "爱女孩的面条". <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yuranliu0818](https://clawhub.ai/user/yuranliu0818) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Chinese-language support on workplace relationships, career choices, interviewing, salary negotiation, workplace stress, probation periods, overtime, and job changes. It answers in a direct, empathetic style and uses guiding questions instead of making decisions for the user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad job-related trigger terms may activate the persona during general chat, not only explicit career-coaching requests. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow the trigger terms or disable the skill for general chat contexts when explicit career-advice activation is required. <br>
