## Description: <br>
Manage ad creative libraries and usage rules across Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, and Amazon Ads workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and growth teams use this skill to plan, classify, adapt, test, and review advertising creative assets across major paid media channels. It helps turn product, audience, placement, and constraint inputs into actionable creative objectives, hook variants, asset specifications, test plans, and QA notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ad creative recommendations may affect spend, campaign performance, policy compliance, tracking, or brand claims if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before using them in live ad accounts, verify claims and tracking assumptions, and require human approval for budget or policy-sensitive changes. <br>
Risk: The skill may trigger on broad marketing prompts where a general marketing strategy skill would be a better fit. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the request is specifically about ad creative assets, placements, usage rules, or lifecycle management before relying on this skill's output. <br>
Risk: Platform constraints or creative performance assumptions may be incomplete or conflict across channels. <br>
Mitigation: Separate observed facts from assumptions, document trade-offs, and include validation checks plus rollback or stop-loss conditions when spend risk exists. <br>
