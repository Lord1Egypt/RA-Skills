## Description: <br>
Humanizer edits user-provided text to remove common signs of AI-generated writing and make the prose sound more natural and human-written. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nissan](https://clawhub.ai/user/nissan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and agents use this skill to revise draft text so it sounds less machine-generated while preserving the original meaning and appropriate tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Humanized text can obscure AI assistance or authorship provenance where disclosure matters. <br>
Mitigation: Use the edited output transparently and keep provenance truthful in settings that require human authorship, personal experience, or AI disclosure. <br>
