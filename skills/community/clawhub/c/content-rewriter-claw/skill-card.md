## Description: <br>
流量重组洗稿虾 helps agents rewrite text by preserving viral content patterns, adapting style and platform norms, and producing an originality and compliance assessment. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tujinsama](https://clawhub.ai/user/tujinsama) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and content operators use this skill to adapt existing written content into new account styles, platform formats, and angles while checking duplication, sensitive wording, and copyright-adjacent risk. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Copyright or platform-policy risk may remain when rewriting copyrighted, confidential, or platform-sensitive source material. <br>
Mitigation: Review source permissions, replace distinctive examples or data where needed, cite sources for quoted material, and perform a human policy review before publishing. <br>
Risk: Estimated duplication ranges do not prove originality or legal permission to publish. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's compliance output as a screening aid only, then verify originality and publication rights with appropriate review tools and human judgment. <br>
