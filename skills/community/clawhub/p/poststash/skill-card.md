## Description: <br>
Schedule posts and threads to Threads and X via PostStash. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[juliendsv](https://clawhub.ai/user/juliendsv) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to schedule single posts, threads, drafts, and ready-to-publish content for X and Threads through PostStash, and to review post status and analytics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can send post content to PostStash and publish Ready posts to public social platforms. <br>
Mitigation: Review agent-generated content and scheduling details before allowing Ready posts to publish. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a PostStash API key with access to schedule posts and read related account posts and analytics. <br>
Mitigation: Use a scoped or revocable API key when available and rotate or revoke it if agent access is no longer needed. <br>
