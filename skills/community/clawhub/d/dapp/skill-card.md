## Description: <br>
Dapp helps Web3 users and investors discover, evaluate, and monitor decentralized applications by surfacing on-chain activity anomalies, smart contract risks, emerging protocols, DeFi position context, and tokenomics signals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External Web3 users, investors, and analysts use this skill to discover, evaluate, and monitor dApps, smart contract risks, on-chain activity, DeFi positions, and tokenomics before acting on market signals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financially sensitive Web3 analysis may influence investment, liquidation, or contract-risk decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide seed phrases, private keys, or signing access; independently verify contract-risk, liquidation, and investment conclusions before acting. <br>
Risk: Portfolio details, wallet addresses, or protocol interests can reveal sensitive financial context. <br>
Mitigation: Share wallet addresses or portfolio details only when comfortable exposing that context, and prefer read-only observation. <br>
