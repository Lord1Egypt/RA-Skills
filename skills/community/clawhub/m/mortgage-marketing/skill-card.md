## Description: <br>
Multi-channel mortgage marketing campaign engine -- one brief generates email, social, and ad copy with audience segmentation and A/B variants. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Mortgage marketing teams and agents use this skill to turn a campaign brief into coordinated email, social, advertising, and listing copy with audience-specific variants and compliance notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External model or Telegram usage could expose confidential or regulated mortgage-marketing material. <br>
Mitigation: Use local mode for sensitive content when available, and avoid sending customer PII, lead details, financial information, unpublished strategy, or compliance-sensitive content unless the organization approves that data flow. <br>
Risk: Generated mortgage marketing copy may contain incorrect, incomplete, or jurisdiction-specific compliance language. <br>
Mitigation: Review campaign outputs, disclosures, and compliance flags with qualified marketing or compliance staff before publication. <br>
