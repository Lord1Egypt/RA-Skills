## Description: <br>
Competitive landscape and market intelligence for M&A due diligence — TAM/SAM/SOM, competitor mapping, and industry analysis <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Deal teams, analysts, and diligence researchers use this skill to structure market intelligence for acquisition review, including market sizing, competitor mapping, industry dynamics, customer and channel analysis, and comparable M&A activity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market sizing, competitor revenue, valuation, and transaction benchmark data can be inaccurate, stale, or estimate-based. <br>
Mitigation: Require current citations, date-stamp intelligence, mark estimated values clearly, and cross-reference market-size claims with at least two sources. <br>
Risk: M&A diligence work may involve confidential deal materials or non-public company information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not share confidential deal materials with an agent unless organizational policy and deal confidentiality terms permit that use. <br>
Risk: Research outputs may influence commercial diligence decisions despite uncertainty in source data. <br>
Mitigation: Flag low-confidence assessments explicitly and have qualified analysts review conclusions before using them in transaction decisions. <br>
