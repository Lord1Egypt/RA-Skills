## Description: <br>
Chinese-language writing assistant for title generation, marketing copy, and email drafting, triggered by phrases such as "写标题", "写文案", and "写邮件". <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiangtaoid](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiangtaoid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content creators use this skill to draft Chinese titles, marketing copy, and complete email messages from brief prompts, audience details, platform context, and desired tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Common Chinese writing phrases such as "写文案" or "写邮件" may invoke the skill unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit prompts when requesting or avoiding writing assistance, and review the agent's selected skill before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing claims or email content may be inaccurate, overstated, or inappropriate for the recipient or platform. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated claims, tone, audience fit, and recipient details before sending or publishing. <br>
