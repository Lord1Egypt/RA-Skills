## Description: <br>
Produce daily and weekly performance reports for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, Amazon Ads, and DSP/programmatic campaigns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketers and growth teams use this skill to assemble daily or weekly advertising performance reports, interpret KPI movement across ad platforms, and decide next campaign actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Budget, bidding, or campaign recommendations may materially affect advertising spend if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations, attribution assumptions, and confidence notes before making account changes. <br>
Risk: Reports can be misleading when metric definitions, attribution windows, sample size, or source data are incomplete or inconsistent. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm KPI definitions, date ranges, data sources, and attribution windows before relying on the analysis. <br>
