## Description: <br>
Structured logging, distributed tracing, and metrics collection patterns for building observable systems. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to design or review observability practices for services, including structured JSON logging, OpenTelemetry tracing, RED and USE metrics, alerting, dashboards, and PII or secret scrubbing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Observability examples can expose secrets, personal data, or overly broad telemetry if copied without local controls. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt examples with secret redaction enabled, avoid logging sensitive values, and confirm telemetry endpoints, retention, and access controls against privacy and compliance requirements. <br>
