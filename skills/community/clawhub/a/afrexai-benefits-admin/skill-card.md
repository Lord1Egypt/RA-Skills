## Description: <br>
Assist companies with designing, benchmarking, and managing benefits programs including 401(k), health insurance, PTO, HSA, and compliance across all states. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
HR teams, operations managers, CFOs, and founders at companies with 10-500 employees use this skill to benchmark benefits, design plan options, model costs, and produce compliance-oriented recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Benefits, tax, insurance, and employment-law guidance can be incorrect, outdated, or incomplete for a specific employer or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify IRS limits, ERISA and ACA duties, state leave requirements, insurance pricing, and plan changes with qualified HR, legal, tax, or benefits professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Benefits analysis may involve sensitive employee-level personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing unnecessary employee-level personal data and use the skill for drafting, analysis, and planning rather than final determinations. <br>
