## Description: <br>
Queries real-time domestic flight options in China, including prices, schedules, airlines, direct or connecting routes, and booking links using Fliggy data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[travel-skills](https://clawhub.ai/user/travel-skills) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to search China domestic flights by structured origin, destination, and date fields or by natural-language queries. It helps compare route type, timing, cabin class, price, duration, and booking links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Flight search details are sent to the publisher's cloud proxy and onward to Fliggy. <br>
Mitigation: Use structured origin, destination, and date searches, and avoid names, IDs, trip purpose, or other private details. <br>
Risk: Returned prices and inventory can change before purchase. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm price, availability, and terms on the booking page before buying a ticket. <br>
