## Description: <br>
Generate Instagram marketing content from product URLs, including product extraction, visual briefs, captions, hashtag strategy, and posting guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[godferylindsay](https://clawhub.ai/user/godferylindsay) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, creators, and e-commerce operators use this skill to turn public product URLs into Instagram-ready content packages for feed posts, carousels, stories, or reels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automated extraction sends product URLs and short scraped page excerpts to the SkillBoss/HeyBoss API. <br>
Mitigation: Use public product pages only, avoid private storefronts or commercially sensitive links, and confirm the API domain before configuring credentials. <br>
Risk: Generated captions, hashtags, and posting plans may be unsuitable for a brand, campaign, or current platform trends. <br>
Mitigation: Review the content package before posting and refresh hashtag choices against current Instagram performance signals. <br>
