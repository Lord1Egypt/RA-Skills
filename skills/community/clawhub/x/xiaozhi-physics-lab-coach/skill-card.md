## Description: <br>
物理实验教练 helps students build physics experiment thinking through guided work on experimental methods, variable control, data analysis, error analysis, and experiment evaluation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Students and learning-support agents use this skill to coach middle-school physics experiment questions, especially experimental design, controlled variables, data interpretation, error analysis, and rigorous conclusion writing. <br>
