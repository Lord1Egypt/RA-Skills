## Description: <br>
Rich Dad Poor Dad helps agents provide book-based personal finance guidance on money mindset, assets versus liabilities, passive income, financial education, and entrepreneurship using Robert Kiyosaki's six-lesson framework. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users seeking personal-finance education use this skill to map Rich Dad Poor Dad concepts to questions about cashflow, asset building, passive income, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and teaching money concepts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial, investing, debt, tax, or business-entity suggestions may be mistaken for personalized professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational framing and verify decisions with qualified financial, tax, or legal professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Opinionated guidance about asset building, leverage, and paying yourself first could encourage choices that are unsuitable for a user's finances. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate recommendations against the user's cash reserves, debt costs, risk tolerance, local laws, and current obligations before using them. <br>
