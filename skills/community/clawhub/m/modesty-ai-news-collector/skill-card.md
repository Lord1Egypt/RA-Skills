## Description: <br>
AI News Collector helps an agent collect, cross-check, rank, and summarize recent AI news from newsletters, community forums, product announcements, funding, research, and policy sources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[modestyrichards](https://clawhub.ai/user/modestyrichards) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to ask an agent for Chinese AI news briefings sorted by heat, with source links and coverage notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to perform multiple public searches and fetches, which may expose the user to source quality, paywall, copyright, and site-terms constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review source links before relying on or redistributing summaries, respect paywalls and site terms, and keep attribution links in the final briefing. <br>
Risk: Manual installation references an external GitHub repository and setup-guide link that are not confirmed by server-resolved provenance. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the repository and setup guide independently before using manual installation paths. <br>
