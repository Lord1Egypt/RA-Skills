## Description: <br>
Lifecycle management for autonomous AI agents: birth, forking, succession, migration, retirement, genealogy, reputation inheritance, and identity continuity as agents evolve. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alexfleetcommander](https://clawhub.ai/user/alexfleetcommander) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use this skill to register autonomous agents, track forks and lineage, process retirement or succession, and inspect lifecycle genealogy with reputation and obligation transfer records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill directs users to install and use a separate PyPI package. <br>
Mitigation: Install only after reviewing and trusting the PyPI package source and selected version. <br>
Risk: Lifecycle, reputation, or obligation transfer records could influence real governance decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep lifecycle stores project-local and review records before using them for operational agent governance. <br>
