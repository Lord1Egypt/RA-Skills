## Use Case: <br>
Mobazha sellers use this skill to research competitor listings, reviews, pricing, and market gaps before deciding what to sell, how to price products, or how to differentiate store positioning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor analysis and pricing recommendations can be misleading if based on stale, incomplete, or biased marketplace information. <br>
Mitigation: Verify key prices, reviews, and competitor claims against current source pages before making store, pricing, or inventory decisions. <br>
Risk: The release security review flags that a bundled review helper may run nested Codex with full filesystem access and approval-bypass privileges by default. <br>
Mitigation: Install only after reviewing the security guidance, keep normal sandbox protections enabled, and avoid approval-bypass modes unless the user explicitly accepts that risk. <br>
