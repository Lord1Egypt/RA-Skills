## Description: <br>
An image-prompt engineering skill that helps agents craft structured prompts for image generators using subject, style, lighting, composition, platform, and negative-prompt guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mtsatryan](https://clawhub.ai/user/mtsatryan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, creators, and agent users use this skill to generate and refine image prompts for Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Flux, and similar image-generation tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated prompts may include inappropriate content, copyright-sensitive style references, or terms that conflict with the chosen image platform's policies. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts before use and adjust them for appropriateness, copyright or style-reference concerns, and platform-policy compatibility. <br>
Risk: Prompt advice may not match the current behavior or parameter support of every image-generation platform. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm platform-specific parameters against the target generator and iterate on outputs before production use. <br>
