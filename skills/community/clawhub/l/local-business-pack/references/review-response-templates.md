# Review Response Templates by Business Type

Use these as tone references — not copy-paste. Adapt to the specific review.

---

## Restaurant / Cafe

### Positive
"So glad the pasta primavera hit the spot! Our kitchen team puts a lot of heart into that dish. We'd love to see you back soon — ask your server about our weekly specials. — The [Name] Team"

### Negative
"We're sorry your experience didn't reflect the evening we work hard to create. Please reach out to us directly at [email] so we can make it right. Your feedback matters and we take it seriously. — [Owner name]"

---

## Hair Salon / Beauty

### Positive
"You just made our whole day! [Stylist name] will love hearing this. We look forward to seeing you at your next appointment — you know where to find us. 💇 — [Salon name]"

### Neutral
"Thanks for taking the time to share this. We're glad you enjoyed [specific service] and we hear you on [concern]. We'd love the chance to do better — book your next visit and ask for [name] directly. — [Salon name]"

### Negative
"We're genuinely sorry this wasn't the experience you deserved. Please call us at [number] — we'd like to personally make this right for you. — [Owner name], [Salon name]"

---

## Contractor / Home Services

### Positive
"Really appreciate you taking the time to write this — it means a lot to the team. Jobs like yours are why we do what we do. Don't hesitate to reach out for any future projects. — [Company name]"

### Negative
"This is not the standard we hold ourselves to and we're sorry for the frustration. Please contact me directly at [phone] — I want to understand what happened and fix it. — [Owner name]"

---

## Dental / Medical

### Positive
"Thank you so much — we know a dental visit isn't anyone's favorite outing, so hearing this genuinely makes our day. See you at your next checkup! — [Practice name]"

### Negative
"Patient experience is our top priority and we're sorry this visit fell short. Please call our office at [number] and ask for [name] — we'd like to speak with you directly. — [Practice name]"

---

## General rules reminder
- Never copy these word for word — always reference the actual review
- Vary sentence starters across multiple responses for the same business
- Keep owner/manager signatures human: first name or "The [Name] Team"
- Never mention competitors, discounts, or legal matters in public responses
