## Description: <br>
Social dance basics and movement skills for people who have never danced, including rhythm practice, beginner social dance guidance, movement for stress relief, and body awareness. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill when they want beginner-friendly coaching for social dancing, finding rhythm, preparing for dance events, or using movement as a stress-relief practice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad wellness or stress-relief requests that are not specifically about dance or movement. <br>
Mitigation: Configure or prompt the agent to use this skill only for movement- or dance-related help. <br>
Risk: Users may treat movement-for-stress-relief coaching as a substitute for professional medical or mental health advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses framed as general movement coaching and direct users to qualified professionals for medical, injury, trauma, or mental health concerns. <br>
Risk: Dance guidance can involve physical activity or partner contact that may not fit a user's abilities, culture, or comfort level. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt recommendations to the user's physical limits, local norms, and consent boundaries before suggesting practice or social dance steps. <br>
