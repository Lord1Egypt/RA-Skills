## Description: <br>
Book Pedicure helps agents search for and book pedicure services through Lokuli MCP. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[edwardrodriguez703-design](https://clawhub.ai/user/edwardrodriguez703-design) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill through an agent to find pedicure providers, check availability, and create a booking with their contact details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Booking sends personal contact details to Lokuli. <br>
Mitigation: Before finalizing a booking, confirm the provider, appointment time, cancellation terms, and that the user's name, email, and phone number will be sent to Lokuli. <br>
