## Description: <br>
A comprehensive AI agent skill for building and optimizing marketing and sales funnels. Helps SaaS founders, marketers, and growth teams design conversion paths, diagnose drop-off points, write copy for every funnel stage, set up measurement, run systematic experiments, and turn a leaky funnel into a compounding growth engine. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External SaaS founders, growth marketers, product managers, and marketing teams use Funnel to diagnose conversion drop-offs, build measurement frameworks, write funnel copy, design onboarding, plan experiments, and connect retention metrics to acquisition decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate the skill during unrelated marketing or product conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke it explicitly for funnel, onboarding, activation, retention, or conversion optimization tasks, and confirm the active goal before applying its guidance. <br>
Risk: Funnel recommendations can be misleading when supplied metrics, benchmarks, or attribution assumptions are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Validate user-provided inputs, segment conversion data by channel or cohort where possible, and review recommendations before changing spend, pricing, onboarding, or sales process. <br>
