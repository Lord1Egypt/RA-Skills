# Supply Chain Intelligence

## Overview
A comprehensive AI agent skill for end-to-end supply chain management. Designed for procurement managers, logistics coordinators, and supply chain analysts who need real-time visibility and intelligent decision support.

## Capabilities

### 1. Supplier Risk Monitoring
- Multi-tier supplier mapping and dependency analysis
- Real-time risk scoring based on geopolitical, financial, and operational factors
- Automated alerts for supplier disruptions, sanctions changes, and compliance issues
- Supplier performance benchmarking against industry standards

### 2. Logistics Optimization
- Route optimization across sea, air, rail, and road freight
- Real-time shipment tracking with ETA prediction
- Freight cost comparison and carrier selection
- Customs clearance documentation automation

### 3. Inventory Intelligence
- Demand forecasting using ML models (seasonal, trend, promotional)
- Safety stock optimization with cost-service level trade-off
- ABC classification and SKU rationalization
- Automated replenishment recommendations

### 4. Procurement Assistant
- RFQ generation and supplier bid analysis
- Contract term extraction and comparison
- Purchase order creation and tracking
- Spend analysis and cost-saving opportunity identification

### 5. Supply Chain Analytics
- End-to-end visibility dashboard generation
- Carbon footprint calculation per shipment
- What-if scenario simulation for disruptions
- KPI tracking: OTIF, lead time, inventory turns, cash-to-cash cycle

## Usage Examples
- "Analyze my supplier portfolio for concentration risk"
- "Optimize shipping route from Shanghai to Rotterdam"
- "Forecast Q3 inventory requirements for SKU-4521"
- "Generate RFQ for 5000 units of electronic components"
- "Calculate supply chain carbon footprint for last quarter"

## Data Sources
Integrates with ERP systems (SAP, Oracle), TMS platforms, freight APIs (Freightos, Xeneta), and public trade databases.
