## Description: <br>
维特根斯坦的思维框架与语言分析方法，帮助用户以维特根斯坦视角分析语言困惑、追问本质的有效性，并处理难以命题化表达的领域。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this Chinese-language persona skill as a philosophy and thinking advisor for language analysis, conceptual clarification, and Wittgenstein-style questioning. It is suited to reflective guidance, not authoritative scholarship. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's philosophical summaries and persona responses may be interpretive rather than authoritative scholarship. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as reflective guidance and compare important claims with primary sources or trusted scholarship. <br>
Risk: The skill is primarily Chinese-language and may not match a user's preferred language by default. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent explicitly for another language or translation style when needed. <br>
