## Description: <br>
Personal Insight Engine (PIE) scans local OpenClaw memory logs and extracts three strategic insights using Gemini or Zhipu. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, and builders use this skill to review recent OpenClaw session logs and identify strategic product, business, market, and scaling patterns. It is intended for periodic strategic review rather than automated decision-making. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Recent OpenClaw memory logs are sent to Gemini or Zhipu after best-effort redaction. <br>
Mitigation: Review sensitive logs before running the skill, keep the --days window small, and use a dedicated provider API key. <br>
Risk: Redaction may miss secrets, private details, or context-specific sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on automated redaction as the only privacy control; remove sensitive material from logs before analysis. <br>
Risk: Strategic insights generated by an LLM may be incomplete, incorrect, or overconfident. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the report as review input and validate conclusions against business context before acting on them. <br>
