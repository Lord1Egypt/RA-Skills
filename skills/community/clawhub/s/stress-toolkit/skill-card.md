## Description: <br>
Provide non-clinical stress-relief exercises, grounding prompts, breathing techniques, and crisis-safe referral language without diagnosis or automated crisis detection claims. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users use this skill for everyday, non-clinical stress support, including breathing exercises, grounding, short reflection prompts, sleep wind-down routines, and simple next-step plans. It is not intended for diagnosis, therapy, crisis detection, emergency response, or replacement of professional support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat stress-relief prompts as medical, therapy, or crisis support. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses non-clinical, avoid diagnosis and crisis-detection claims, and direct immediate danger or self-harm intent to emergency services or trusted local support. <br>
Risk: Breathing or relaxation exercises may be uncomfortable for some users. <br>
Mitigation: Offer short opt-in exercises one at a time and stop if the user reports dizziness, discomfort, or immediate danger. <br>
Risk: Multilingual examples may create unclear expectations about supported languages. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify supported language policy during deployment and keep safety boundaries consistent across languages. <br>
