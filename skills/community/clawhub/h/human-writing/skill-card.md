## Description: <br>
Human Writing helps agents draft, edit, and review external-facing content so it reads naturally human and avoids common AI writing patterns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[reighlan](https://clawhub.ai/user/reighlan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External-facing writers, editors, marketers, and publishing teams use this skill to draft, edit, or review content for natural prose, concrete wording, and fewer common AI writing tells. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill could be used to obscure AI-assisted authorship where disclosure or honest authorship labeling is required. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as an editing aid while following any AI-use disclosure and authorship rules that apply to the work. <br>
