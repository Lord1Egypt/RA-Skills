## Description: <br>
Generates a local SRT subtitle output from an audio URL and optional dialogue text. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ghwyever](https://clawhub.ai/user/ghwyever) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and automation agents use this skill to produce an SRT subtitle URL for video or audio workflows when simple local caption output is acceptable. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generated subtitles may be placeholders rather than accurate audio-synced captions. <br>
Mitigation: Use this skill only when simple SRT output is acceptable, and verify caption timing and text before publishing. <br>

**Output Type(s):** [text, files] <br>
