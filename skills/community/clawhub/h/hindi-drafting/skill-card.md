## Description: <br>
Professional Hindi drafting for business documents, government applications, HR, legal, and marketing content for Indian startups and SMEs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to draft or translate professional Hindi content for business, government, HR, legal, marketing, investor, and startup workflows. It provides templates, industry vocabulary, tone guidance, and a self-check process for Devanagari Hindi output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal, HR, loan, investor, or financial drafts may be inaccurate or incomplete for a user's specific situation. <br>
Mitigation: Users should review generated drafts carefully and seek qualified review before relying on them for formal decisions or submissions. <br>
Risk: Drafting requests may include sensitive business, financial, employee, or personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Users should avoid sharing sensitive details unless they are comfortable providing them to the agent using the skill. <br>
Risk: The artifact instructs the agent not to invent financial figures, but incomplete user inputs can still lead to unsupported assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Use clearly marked placeholders for unknown amounts, dates, names, and factual claims, then ask the user to verify or fill them in. <br>
