This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External TCM practitioners, integrated Chinese-Western medicine clinicians, medical students, researchers, and health professionals use this skill to retrieve structured educational TCM reference material for qualified clinical review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat TCM diagnostic, formula, acupuncture, tuina, oncology, pregnancy, bleeding, infection, severe pain, dehydration, neurologic, or emergency content as self-diagnosis or self-treatment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational or clinician-review reference only, and use the skill only with a qualified medical or TCM professional and current local clinical guidance. <br>
Risk: Actionable medical treatment and procedure guidance is not consistently scoped to qualified clinical use. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing and before using outputs; do not use the skill to prescribe herbs, perform acupuncture, or manage urgent or high-risk medical scenarios. <br>
