# Medical Cell Biology — Core Skills Collection

This skill set encompasses **146 core competencies** in Medical Cell Biology, based on the nationally planned textbook **"Medical Cell Biology" (7th Edition, People's Medical Publishing House)**. The skills span cell structure and function, molecular mechanisms, signal transduction, cell cycle regulation, stem cell biology, cell death and aging, organelle dynamics, membrane transport, gene expression regulation, disease mechanisms, and cutting-edge research technologies.

## Usage Scenarios

This skill collection is designed for:

- **Medical students** — systematic learning and exam preparation
- **Researchers** — experimental design and mechanism analysis
- **Clinical practitioners** — understanding disease pathogenesis at the cellular level
- **Educators** — curriculum design and teaching material development
