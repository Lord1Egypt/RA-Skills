## Description: <br>
Identify the single highest-leverage thing to remove from a game design, feature, system, UX flow, pitch, roadmap item, or prototype in order to improve it significantly. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product leads, UX designers, and developers use this skill to get a focused subtractive critique of a game design, feature, system, UX flow, pitch, roadmap item, or prototype. It identifies one high-leverage removal, explains the tradeoff, and proposes a value-preserving alternative when useful. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts may include confidential game plans, unreleased business details, or sensitive user data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential or sensitive material unless the agent environment is approved for that content. <br>
Risk: A subtractive critique may recommend removing a feature that still carries business, retention, accessibility, or player-value goals. <br>
Mitigation: Review the recommendation against product goals and validate material cuts with prototype or playtest evidence before shipping. <br>
