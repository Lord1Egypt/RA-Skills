## Description: <br>
Repurpose long-form video or audio into short-form clip plans with timestamps, hooks, captions, and packaging notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codedao12](https://clawhub.ai/user/codedao12) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, editors, and marketing teams use this skill to convert long videos, podcasts, or streams into draft short-form clip plans, captions, titles, hashtags, thumbnail notes, and editor cut lists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The user may not have rights or consent to repurpose the source media. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm repurposing rights before creating clip plans and avoid private conversations, minors, or sensitive personal data. <br>
Risk: Sensitive recordings could be exposed through unnecessary third-party transcription or processing. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer local processing for sensitive media and use only trusted transcription providers with user-managed keys when needed. <br>
Risk: Generated timestamps, captions, or packaging notes may be inaccurate or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Verify timestamps against the source media and treat all captions, titles, hashtags, and cut lists as drafts for human review. <br>
