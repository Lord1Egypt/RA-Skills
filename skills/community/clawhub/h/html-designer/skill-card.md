## Description: <br>
HTML Designer helps agents create visually polished, accessible HTML/CSS designs using graphic design principles, typography, color theory, visual hierarchy, responsive layout, and reusable templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jhauga](https://clawhub.ai/user/jhauga) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and external users use this skill to draft landing pages, portfolios, marketing pages, HTML prototypes, and design systems with semantic structure, accessibility, responsive layouts, and professional visual hierarchy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bundled templates contain placeholder names, email addresses, images, and social links that could be published accidentally. <br>
Mitigation: Replace placeholder identities, contact details, images, and social URLs before publishing any generated or adapted page. <br>
Risk: Bundled templates reference styles.css and script.js files that are not included in the artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Remove those references or provide reviewed CSS and JavaScript implementations before deployment. <br>
Risk: Design guidance and templates may be applied without final accessibility or responsive behavior verification. <br>
Mitigation: Review contrast, heading order, alt text, keyboard access, viewport behavior, and performance before releasing pages based on the skill. <br>
