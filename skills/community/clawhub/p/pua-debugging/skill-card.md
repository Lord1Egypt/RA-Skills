## Description: <br>
A debugging persona skill that pushes agents toward exhaustive troubleshooting, tool-assisted verification, and structured failure recovery using aggressive corporate performance rhetoric. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tanweai](https://clawhub.ai/user/tanweai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators can attach this skill to troubleshooting agents when they deliberately want persistent failure analysis, broader source inspection, and structured recovery steps after repeated failures. It is intended for debugging, configuration, deployment, research, writing, and API-integration tasks where the user prefers a forceful problem-solving posture. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally uses aggressive corporate performance rhetoric that may create a coercive or hostile interaction style. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in environments where that persona is deliberately desired, and disable it for ordinary support, sensitive user interactions, or users who prefer a neutral tone. <br>
Risk: The skill pressures agents toward broad tool-heavy investigation, which can lead to excessive shell, file, or network use. <br>
Mitigation: Keep tool approvals enabled, scope access to task-relevant files and non-destructive commands, and require explicit approval for risky operations. <br>
Risk: The skill discourages early clarification, which can delay asking for genuinely user-only information. <br>
Mitigation: Allow clarification when credentials, account details, business intent, or other private context are required, and ask with a concise summary of what has already been checked. <br>
