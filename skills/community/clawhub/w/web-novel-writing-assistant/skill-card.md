## Description: <br>
Assists web novel writers with planning, outlining, chapter writing, style consistency, conflict detection, pacing, plot-thread management, foreshadowing, and reader feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers and editors use this skill to structure serialized web novels, maintain continuity across long arcs, diagnose pacing and setting conflicts, and incorporate reader feedback into revision decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Creative guidance and checklists can still lead to continuity, pacing, or logic mistakes if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans and chapters against established setting, character, timeline, foreshadowing, and plot-thread records before publication. <br>
Risk: Reader feedback analysis can overweight small, biased, or emotional feedback samples. <br>
Mitigation: Use feedback grades and decision logs as advisory inputs, prioritize repeated or data-backed signals, and verify the effect of changes over subsequent chapters. <br>
