## Description: <br>
用于撰写结构清晰的中文周报。只要用户提到“周报、weekly report、本周总结、工作复盘、下周计划、给老板汇报”，即使没有明确说“写周报”，也应主动使用此技能来生成可直接发送的周报内容。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, managers, and agents use this skill to turn weekly work notes into a concise Chinese weekly report for progress updates. It organizes completed work, measurable outcomes, issues, risks, next steps, and coordination needs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated weekly reports may include confidential work details or unverified statements. <br>
Mitigation: Review the report before sending and confirm facts, metrics, names, timelines, and sensitive information. <br>
Risk: The skill defaults to Chinese and may not match the desired reporting language or audience tone. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for a different language or tone when needed, and check that the final report is appropriate for its recipients. <br>
Risk: Incomplete input can leave gaps in the report. <br>
Mitigation: Treat placeholders such as "待补充" as required follow-up items before submission. <br>
