## Description: <br>
Generates platform-specific ad copy for Google Search Ads, Facebook and Instagram ads, LinkedIn ads, and retargeting campaigns from product, audience, offer, tone, and campaign details. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, agencies, freelancers, and advertisers use this skill to draft campaign-ready ad copy and testing recommendations across major paid advertising platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ads may include unsupported statistics, testimonials, guarantees, urgency, scarcity, privacy language, or regulated health and weight-related claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims against evidence and applicable platform advertising rules before publishing. <br>
Risk: Campaign publishing or ad spend decisions could be made from generated copy without adequate review. <br>
Mitigation: Keep campaign launch, budget, purchasing, and spend controls under direct human approval. <br>
Risk: Marketing copy can be misleading or noncompliant even when generated by a prompt-only skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review final copy for accuracy, substantiation, audience suitability, and brand or legal requirements. <br>
