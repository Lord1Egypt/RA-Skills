## Description: <br>
Generates customer-facing FAQ answers for travel and hospitality teams, marketers, and planners. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Travel teams, hospitality marketers, and planners use this skill to draft customer-facing FAQ content for travel and hospitality services. It helps clarify audience needs, generate a first draft, and refine answers for accuracy, readability, and publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ answers may contain incorrect or unsupported pricing, refund, booking, accessibility, safety, legal, or policy claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review answers against authoritative business policies and applicable requirements before publishing or operational use. <br>
Risk: Web search results may be stale, incomplete, or unsuitable as source material for customer-facing travel and hospitality content. <br>
Mitigation: Treat web_search output as draft support and verify important claims with official sources before release. <br>
