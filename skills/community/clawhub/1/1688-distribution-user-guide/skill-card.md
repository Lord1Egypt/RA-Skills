## Description: <br>
A 1688 dropshipping guidance skill that helps beginners with store setup, shop binding, product selection, listing, purchasing, after-sales handling, and store growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1688aiinfra](https://clawhub.ai/user/1688aiinfra) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sellers and new 1688 distributors use this skill to get practical, step-by-step guidance for one-piece dropshipping workflows, from choosing a sales platform through shop binding, listing, purchasing, and after-sales handling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may send users to 1688 pages for shop binding, tool authorization, automated ordering, or refund features. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the destination page and agreement before authorizing store tools or enabling automated ordering and refund workflows. <br>
Risk: The skill provides operational guidance for marketplace selling, where platform policies, category restrictions, fees, and prohibited terms can change. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current platform rules and supplier requirements before acting on business, pricing, authorization, or after-sales recommendations. <br>
