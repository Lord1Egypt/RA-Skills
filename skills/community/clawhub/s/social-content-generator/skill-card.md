## Description: <br>
Helps an agent create, schedule, repurpose, and optimize social media content for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and related platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, creators, marketers, and business teams use this skill to draft platform-specific posts, plan content calendars, repurpose existing content, and analyze engagement patterns before publishing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may guide an agent toward public posting, scheduling, direct messages, comments, or other external-account actions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before any external-account action, publication, scheduled post, comment, or direct message. <br>
Risk: The skill includes bulk social content collection and analysis guidance that could be misused for scraping. <br>
Mitigation: Collect platform data only through authorized, compliant methods and respect each platform's terms and access controls. <br>
Risk: The server security summary says the skill under-scopes high-impact posting authority and bulk scraping guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for drafting, planning, and content analysis ideas; review outputs before execution and keep publication authority under human control. <br>
