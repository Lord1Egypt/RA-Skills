## Description: <br>
Analyzes article structure, identifies positions requiring visual aids, and generates illustrations with a Type x Style approach. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nengnengZ](https://clawhub.ai/user/nengnengZ) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, writers, and developers use this skill to analyze articles, plan useful illustration placements, generate prompt files and image assets, and insert Markdown image references into articles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create or modify article, prompt, outline, preference, reference, and image files. <br>
Mitigation: Keep projects under version control or backups and review planned replacements or article edits before execution. <br>
Risk: User-level preferences can affect future runs across projects. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer project-local preferences unless global behavior is intended. <br>
Risk: Generated visuals or image labels can misrepresent article details if prompts are not reviewed. <br>
Mitigation: Review the outline and saved prompt files before image generation and verify article-specific numbers, labels, and terms before insertion. <br>
