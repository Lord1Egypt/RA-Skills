## Description: <br>
AI合同审查大师 helps agents review multilingual contracts across major jurisdictions, identify legal and business risks, and draft structured review guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Legal, procurement, sales, and operations teams can use this skill to spot contract risks, compare versions, review clauses against selected jurisdictions, and prepare negotiation-oriented review reports. It is drafting and risk-spotting support, not a replacement for qualified legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive contracts and related business information. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in an agent environment approved for the contract data being reviewed, and share only documents you are comfortable processing there. <br>
Risk: Contract analysis can be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific jurisdiction or high-value agreement. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafting and risk-spotting support, and have qualified counsel review major, jurisdiction-specific, or final legal decisions. <br>
