## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can permanently erase broad local data if target paths, recursive deletion, or force deletion are used incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Verify exact target paths before use and avoid recursive or force deletion unless necessary. <br>
Risk: The documented interactive mode is not implemented in this version. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on interactive confirmation; review deletion targets before execution. <br>
