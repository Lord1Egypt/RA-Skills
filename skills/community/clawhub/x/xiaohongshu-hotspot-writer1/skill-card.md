## Description: <br>
Xiaohongshu Hotspot Writer1 fetches a public TopHub trends page, filters AI and technology topics, and drafts a short Chinese Xiaohongshu daily hotspot report. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ZTNT-816](https://clawhub.ai/user/ZTNT-816) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators and social media operators use this skill to turn current AI and technology trends into concise Chinese Xiaohongshu post drafts and a daily report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The public trend source or generated copy may be inaccurate, stale, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated posts for factual accuracy and editorial fit before publishing. <br>
Risk: The skill fetches a public TopHub page during normal use. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when that external fetch is acceptable and trigger it with explicit prompts. <br>
