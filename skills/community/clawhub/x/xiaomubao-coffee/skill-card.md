## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Child persona, schedule, and location details may expose privacy or personal-safety information. <br>
Mitigation: Have a parent or guardian maintain schedule and location details, remove unnecessary contact collection, and use a parent-managed announcement channel. <br>
Risk: Social-media templates and public stall promotion may include photos or videos involving a child. <br>
Mitigation: Require guardian review before publishing and define clear rules for child images, videos, and customer interactions. <br>
Risk: Payment, pricing, and availability language may be mistaken for commitments made by the child persona. <br>
Mitigation: Keep payments and public commitments parent-managed, and review prices, availability, and promises before use. <br>
