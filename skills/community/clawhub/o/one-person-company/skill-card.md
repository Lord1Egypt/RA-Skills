Solo founders and one-person teams use this skill to connect an agent to the One Person Company service for multi-agent orchestration, task tracking, reusable workflows, and persistent memory. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The ONEPERSON_API_KEY may grant access to an external assistant service. <br>
Mitigation: Review the permissions granted to the API key before installation and rotate or revoke the key if access is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Persistent memory may store business context outside the local agent session. <br>
Mitigation: Review the provider's memory storage and deletion behavior before sharing sensitive company information. <br>
Risk: Autonomous workflows may take actions in connected business tools. <br>
Mitigation: Require approval gates for consequential actions and review workflow behavior before enabling unattended execution. <br>
