## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language, system-first family and parenting guidance for relationship, parenting, couple coordination, household boundary, and long-term stewardship questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ting2tao](https://clawhub.ai/user/ting2tao) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to get concise Chinese-language guidance on family relationships, parenting by age stage, couple coordination, household rules, and long-term family stewardship. It helps frame practical next steps while keeping medical, mental-health, legal, and investment topics outside scope. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat family and parenting guidance as professional medical, mental-health, legal, financial, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses framed as general guidance, state boundaries when those domains appear, and direct high-stakes issues to qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Family conflict, child safety, self-harm, severe behavioral, developmental, or mental-health situations can exceed the skill's advice-only scope. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate crisis or safety signals to appropriate professional, emergency, or local support instead of relying on the skill's household coaching framework. <br>
