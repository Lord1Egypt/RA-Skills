# Research Guide

Use research only when the agent has tools that can access current public sources.

## Search Targets

Prefer official or platform-native sources:
- TikTok Creative Center
- TikTok Shop / marketplace pages
- Meta Ads Library or platform ad libraries when relevant
- YouTube/TikTok public pages when accessible
- Brand/product official pages for factual claims
- Recent credible trend reports when platform pages are incomplete

## Query Pattern

Combine:
- category: lipstick, sunscreen, snack, coffee, gadget
- platform: TikTok US, Douyin, Xiaohongshu
- commerce intent: shop, haul, review, deal, top ads
- recency: last 7 days, this week, 2026

## Safe Claims

Do:
- Summarize visible content patterns.
- Cite links used.
- Say when exact likes/ranking cannot be verified.
- Use “in the researched sample” instead of “the whole platform” when coverage is incomplete.

Do not:
- Invent creators, likes, rankings, or video links.
- Claim “guaranteed viral” or “guaranteed sales”.
- Copy a creator’s exact script line-by-line. Replicate structure, pacing, and function instead.

## Research Output

For 3-5 references, capture:
- hook type
- opening frame
- product proof/demo
- pacing
- text style
- CTA
- why it works
