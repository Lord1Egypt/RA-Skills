External users, creators, and developers use this skill to choose AI music providers, shape music-generation prompts, and produce songs, instrumentals, background music, or sound effects. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Third-party music-generation services may receive prompts, lyrics, uploaded audio, webhook URLs, or API credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-confidential material, review each provider's terms and privacy practices, and avoid long-lived or broadly scoped API keys. <br>
Risk: Provider licensing, commercial-use rights, pricing, and billing behavior vary across services and plans. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the current provider terms and required subscription or API license before using generated audio commercially. <br>
