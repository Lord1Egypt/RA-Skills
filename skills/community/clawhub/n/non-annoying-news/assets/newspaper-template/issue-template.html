Standard
Lead section

Main headline with a clear claim

Deck explains the news and why it matters without requiring source context.

Lead body starts with the concrete event, claim, or change. It names the source of the information without turning the article into a bibliography.

The middle explains the mechanism: what changed, how it works, which evidence matters, and why a reader should care now.

The final paragraph states the boundary: what is still uncertain, inaccessible, disputed, or dependent on future evidence.
