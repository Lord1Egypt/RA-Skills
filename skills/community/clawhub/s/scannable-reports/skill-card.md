## Description: <br>
Formats agent outputs for mobile chat apps using Unicode progress bars, sparklines, status indicators, and monospace tables. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rmarji](https://clawhub.ai/user/rmarji) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and operators use this skill to turn status updates, dashboards, metrics, and progress reports into compact chat-friendly text for Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp, and similar channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Unicode bars, emoji, and box-drawing characters may not render consistently for every audience or chat client. <br>
Mitigation: Preview reports in the target channel and fall back to simpler ASCII labels or shorter text when compatibility is poor. <br>
