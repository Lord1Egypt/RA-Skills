## Description: <br>
Khazix Writer 卡兹克写作 helps agents draft Chinese public-account long-form articles in Khazix's style using stated writing rules, self-checks, content methodology, and rhetorical examples. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kkkkhazix](https://clawhub.ai/user/kkkkhazix) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, editors, and writing agents use this skill to turn supplied material into Chinese public-account articles or long-form drafts in the Khazix writing style. It is suited for drafting, continuation, structure refinement, and style transfer, with human review for facts and editorial judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafts can contain unsupported factual claims or weak reasoning if source material is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Require source-backed fact checks and human editorial review before publication. <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally applies a distinctive personal writing style that may be inappropriate for generic, sensitive, or brand-controlled writing. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for a neutral tone or required house style when the Khazix style is not desired. <br>
Risk: External GitHub materials referenced by the artifact may change independently of this release. <br>
Mitigation: Review external materials before asking an agent to rely on them. <br>
