## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Wildcard WebSocket CORS examples may be copied into production. <br>
Mitigation: Replace wildcard origins with trusted origins before using WebSocket gateway examples in a real application. <br>
Risk: Gateway examples may need app-specific authentication and authorization controls. <br>
Mitigation: Add and review authentication and authorization checks for gateway connections before deployment. <br>
