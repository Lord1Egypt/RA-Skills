## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may be mistaken for Li Kaifu's actual views or direct quotes. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as a style-inspired analytical persona and do not attribute generated content to Li Kaifu. <br>
Risk: Factual, startup, career, or investment guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a user's situation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify factual claims and seek qualified review before making business, career, or investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Work-health balance guidance may be too general for personal health decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat health-related output as general perspective only and consult appropriate professionals for medical concerns. <br>
