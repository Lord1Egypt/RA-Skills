## Description: <br>
Converts a local audio file to a Feishu-playable Opus voice message, uploads it to Feishu, and sends it as an audio message. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tianyn1990](https://clawhub.ai/user/tianyn1990) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operators use this skill when they need an agent to convert local audio or generated TTS output into Feishu's required voice-message format and send it to a selected Feishu user. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The script uploads selected local audio content to Feishu. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the audio content is intended for Feishu before running the command, especially when the audio was generated by another tool. <br>
Risk: Feishu app credentials are required to request an access token. <br>
Mitigation: Use a least-privilege Feishu app and protect FEISHU_APP_SECRET in the execution environment. <br>
Risk: An incorrect receiver Open ID could send audio to the wrong Feishu user. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the receiver Open ID or FEISHU_RECEIVER value before sending. <br>
