## Description: <br>
Talking Head Production guides agents through creating AI avatar talking-head videos with portraits, generated or prepared voiceover, lipsync, captions, and short-clip production workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[okaris](https://clawhub.ai/user/okaris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, creators, and developers use this skill to plan and run AI talking-head video workflows for spokesperson videos, course content, social media, presentations, and demos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow uses an external CLI installer and remote media-generation services. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect or manually download the installer, verify available checksums or signatures, and review provider terms before using the service. <br>
Risk: The workflow can upload portraits, voices, scripts, and videos that may contain likeness, voice, or sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Use only media and scripts the user has rights and consent to process, and avoid sensitive or regulated content unless provider privacy, retention, deletion, and billing terms are acceptable. <br>
