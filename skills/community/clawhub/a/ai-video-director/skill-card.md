## Description: <br>
AI Video Director helps create AI video storyboards, frame-level prompts, platform-specific prompt sets, parameter recommendations, and post-production guidance for video-generation workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and video producers use this skill to turn a video concept into a shot-by-shot storyboard, platform-specific AI video prompts, generation parameters, and post-production notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated video prompts or storyboards may be unsuitable for the intended platform, language, audience, or public campaign. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the target platform, output language, brand tone, and use context, then review the generated prompts before using them in paid or public video-generation tools. <br>
