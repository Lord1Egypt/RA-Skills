## Description: <br>
Audit product pages for missing or weak trust signals including reviews, guarantees, certifications, and social proof that affect purchase confidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators, marketers, and agents use this skill to audit product pages for trust signals, score gaps in reviews, guarantees, certifications, security indicators, and brand authority, then prioritize conversion-focused fixes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audit outputs may recommend certification, refund, review, or advertising claims that are inaccurate for a specific product or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all certifications, refund terms, customer review statements, and advertising claims before publishing them. <br>
Risk: The workflow discusses using customer photos, videos, and user-generated content in listings and advertising. <br>
Mitigation: Obtain clear written permission before using customer media, and use formal releases for paid advertising placements. <br>
Risk: The release has financial-authority and can-make-purchases capability tags, while the security guidance says the skill should remain advisory. <br>
Mitigation: Do not grant the skill authority to make purchases or modify financial, checkout, or account settings. <br>
