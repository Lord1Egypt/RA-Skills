## Description: <br>
全球选品雷达 helps agents research e-commerce products, supplier options, market trends, profit assumptions, competitor signals, and product-selection risks across global marketplaces. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
E-commerce operators, marketplace sellers, and sourcing teams use this skill to compare product opportunities, estimate demand and ROI, evaluate suppliers, and generate product decision cards with risk notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product, supplier, market, profit, and ROI outputs may be estimates or public-source interpretations that are incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify generated claims against current marketplace data, supplier records, platform fees, logistics costs, and business constraints before purchasing inventory or changing pricing. <br>
