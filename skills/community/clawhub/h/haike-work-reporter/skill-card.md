## Description: <br>
Helps team members organize daily work, generate daily, weekly, and monthly reports, and carry unfinished-task reminders into the next day. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[beargzhaike](https://clawhub.ai/user/beargzhaike) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and team members use this skill to turn work activity logs or manual descriptions into Chinese daily, weekly, and monthly reports, then identify unfinished work for follow-up reminders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Work-activity monitoring may capture sensitive screen contents or personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with explicit opt-in, visible pause controls, and limits for approved work hours and screens. <br>
Risk: Automatically delivered reports may include sensitive or incorrect work details. <br>
Mitigation: Review reports before sending them and redact confidential, personal, or inaccurate content. <br>
Risk: Stored .workbuddy logs may retain sensitive activity history. <br>
Mitigation: Define storage, redaction, retention, and deletion rules before deployment. <br>
