## Description: <br>
Provides disaster preparedness, warning interpretation, first aid, evacuation planning, and recovery guidance for earthquakes, floods, typhoons, fires, geological hazards, and outdoor emergencies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, households, organizations, and emergency-preparedness teams use this skill to ask for disaster preparation, self-rescue, first-aid, evacuation, shelter, and recovery guidance. It supports scenario-based questions for natural disasters, fires, medical emergencies, outdoor safety, and enterprise emergency management. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on general disaster or first-aid guidance during urgent or life-threatening situations. <br>
Mitigation: Contact local emergency services immediately, follow official alerts and responder instructions, and use trained medical or disaster professionals when available. <br>
Risk: Markdown-only guidance can be incomplete or mismatched to local conditions, hazards, laws, infrastructure, or medical needs. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as preparedness and general information support, then verify actions against local emergency authorities, site-specific plans, and qualified responders. <br>
