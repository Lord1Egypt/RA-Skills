## Description: <br>
Uses four AI pattern methods to generate academically scoped, innovative thesis topics across disciplines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and academic writers use this skill to turn a field, research interest, or selected ideation mode into thesis topic candidates with evaluation notes, innovation paths, and optional outlining support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated thesis topics and research directions may be unsuitable, overly broad, or academically unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as brainstorming drafts and verify feasibility, originality, scope, and theoretical grounding before using them. <br>
Risk: Literature suggestions or theory references may be incomplete or uncertain. <br>
Mitigation: Check cited or suggested sources independently and require confirmed, real references before relying on them. <br>
Risk: The skill can activate for broad research-idea requests and may choose ideation modes automatically. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's discipline, constraints, and preferred mode when the request is ambiguous. <br>
