## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Example administration commands may delete files, flush firewall rules, prune Docker resources, change services, or create accounts if run on a real system. <br>
Mitigation: Review commands before execution, prefer a VM or lab environment, and add backups or rollback steps for real systems. <br>
Risk: The skill is documentation-only training material and may provide study guidance or configuration examples that require local validation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as study aid material and verify commands, configurations, and exam-specific assumptions against the target environment or official course requirements. <br>
