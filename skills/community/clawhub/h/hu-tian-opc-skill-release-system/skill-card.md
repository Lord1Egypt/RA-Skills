## Description: <br>
OPC Skill商业化发布体系，含标准化发布流程、版权保护、互引推荐引擎与商业化定价策略。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External skill developers, AI platform operators, paid-knowledge creators, and technology service teams use this skill to structure OPC-style commercial skill releases. It provides publishing checklists, copyright protection patterns, cross-skill recommendation design, pricing guidance, and reusable templates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated release guidance or templates may introduce incorrect or misleading claims into a published skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan generated skill content before deployment or marketplace submission. <br>
Risk: Cross-skill recommendations and pricing labels may steer users toward paid or related offerings. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendation blocks before sharing and disclose paid recommendations where they appear. <br>
Risk: Publishing workflows may involve diligence, pricing, or intellectual-property material that is confidential. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential materials unless the active platform's data-handling rules are acceptable. <br>
