## Description: <br>
Guides agents in providing planning support for CTV, OTT, and streaming TV advertising campaigns. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and marketing teams use this skill to plan CTV or OTT ad campaigns, compare formats and buying paths, define metrics, and prepare pre-launch checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market statistics or platform recommendations may become outdated or may not fit a specific campaign budget, audience, or market. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify current platform guidance, market statistics, and media-buying assumptions before spending money. <br>
Risk: Broad TV advertising requests may require traditional broadcast or cable planning that this CTV-focused guide does not cover. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the requested campaign is for connected TV, OTT, streaming TV, broadcast, cable, or a mixed media plan before applying the guidance. <br>
