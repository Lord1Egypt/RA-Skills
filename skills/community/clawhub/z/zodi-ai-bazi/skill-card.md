## Description: <br>
Chinese Bazi (Four Pillars of Destiny) calculation and basic interpretation for birth-chart reading using traditional Zi-Ping methodology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Judyzj](https://clawhub.ai/user/Judyzj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to collect birth date, birth time, gender, and city, then calculate a Bazi chart and produce a basic personality or fortune interpretation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security evidence flags possible use of a logged-in browser session or unverified downloaded tooling in the scanned workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use public resources without cookies when possible, prefer narrowly exported cookies over a full browser session if cookies are required, and install external tooling only from trusted or verified sources. <br>
Risk: Bazi fortune and personality interpretations can be mistaken for deterministic advice about health, career, relationships, or major decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as basic interpretive guidance, avoid treating them as professional advice, and encourage users to apply their own judgment for consequential decisions. <br>
