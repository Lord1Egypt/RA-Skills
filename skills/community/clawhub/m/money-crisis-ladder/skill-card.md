## Description: <br>
Three-phase money crisis protocol: immediate triage when bills cannot be paid, survival on drastically reduced income, and financial resilience once stable. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to prioritize urgent bills, contact creditors, locate assistance programs, reduce expenses during income loss, and rebuild an emergency fund. It is especially relevant when users face rent, utility, medication, debt, or income-interruption crises. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with localization required for non-US users because Phase 1 and Phase 2 reference US-specific assistance programs, agencies, and laws. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store sensitive financial details such as creditors, expenses, balances, assistance applications, and emergency-fund targets. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's comfort before retaining financial details, keep only information needed for the plan, and avoid exposing those details in shared files or logs. <br>
Risk: Benefits, hotline, banking, debt, and legal guidance can vary by location and date. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current local sources before acting, ask for jurisdiction when unclear, and clearly label any substituted non-US programs. <br>
