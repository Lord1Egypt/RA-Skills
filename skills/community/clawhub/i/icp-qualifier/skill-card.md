## Description: <br>
Qualifies DTC Shopify leads for Visual Dept against ICP criteria using a 100-point scoring rubric. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[visualdeptcreative](https://clawhub.ai/user/visualdeptcreative) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales and growth teams use this skill to evaluate DTC e-commerce prospects, score fit against Visual Dept's ICP, and draft a personalized outreach hook. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global use, with the included ICP rubric aimed at United States leads. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Demographic criteria such as founder gender and age could lead to discriminatory qualification decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use age or gender to qualify leads; treat those fields as removable campaign context requiring human oversight. <br>
Risk: The US-only filter and contact-information research can create privacy and outreach compliance risk. <br>
Mitigation: Treat geography as a configurable campaign constraint, verify public contact details before outreach, and follow applicable privacy and anti-spam requirements. <br>
