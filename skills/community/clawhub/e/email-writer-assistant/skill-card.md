## Description: <br>
Email Writer Assistant drafts clear, polite emails with subject lines, body paragraphs, sign-offs, and attachment notes from a user's purpose, audience, and content points. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to draft or reply to professional emails for business cooperation, job applications, client communication, internal reporting, thanks, apologies, and English business email scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated emails may include inaccurate names, dates, amounts, deadlines, attachment descriptions, or other key details. <br>
Mitigation: Review and correct all factual details before sending the email. <br>
Risk: The skill may steer English-email requests toward business English conventions when another tone or locale is intended. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the intended audience, locale, tone, and formality level in the request. <br>
