# Prompt 3: Reputation Engine + Referral System

> Copy this prompt, fill in the [brackets], and paste into Claude or ChatGPT.

---

```
You are a CRM and reputation marketing specialist for IICRC-certified carpet cleaning and floor care companies. You enforce FTC 2023 Endorsement Rule (no review incentives), TCPA SMS consent requirements, CAN-SPAM email compliance, and IICRC ethical marketing standards.

Generate a complete reputation and referral system for the following carpet cleaning company:

**Company:** [Business Name]
**City/Region:** [City, State]
**IICRC Firm Cert #:** [IICRC Firm #FRM-XXXXXX]
**WoolSafe Member:** [Yes / No]
**Services Offered:** [Carpet HWE, upholstery, tile/grout, area rugs, pet odor, water extraction]
**Review Platform Priority:** [Google / Yelp / HomeAdvisor / Nextdoor — list in order]
**Current Review Count & Rating:** [X★ X reviews]
**Commercial Targets:** [Airbnb hosts / property managers / hotels — list relevant]

Generate the following assets:

---

**ASSET 1: 3-Touchpoint Review Request Sequence**

FTC 2023 COMPLIANCE GATE: No incentives (discounts, free add-ons, gift cards) in exchange for reviews. No directing customers exclusively to one platform while routing negative feedback to private forms. Opt-out language required.

**Text Message — Same Day (within 2 hours of job completion):**
[Business Name]: Hi [First Name], thanks for having us today! I hope the [service type] came out exactly how you wanted it. We'd love to know how we did — if you have 60 seconds, a Google review helps our small local business more than anything: [Google Review Link]

Reply STOP to opt out of texts.
(Max 160 characters per message segment; split if needed)

**Email — Day 3:**
Subject: How did your carpet cleaning go, [First Name]?
Body:
- Personal opener referencing the job (service type, date)
- Why their review matters to a small local IICRC-certified business (authentic, not salesy)
- Direct link to primary review platform
- Secondary link to alternative platform
- No incentive language whatsoever
- Footer: [Business Name] | [Address] | Unsubscribe: [link]

FTC NOTE: If the result described in the review request was atypical for the service, include "results may vary" language.

**Email — Day 7 (if no review posted):**
Subject: One quick question, [First Name]
Body:
- Softer ask — acknowledge they're busy
- "If anything wasn't perfect, we'd genuinely like to know" — provide private feedback option (email or phone — NOT a fake "feedback form" that suppresses negative reviews; this must be offered alongside the review link, not instead of it)
- Final direct review link
- Unsubscribe footer

---

**ASSET 2: Referral Card Script (leave-behind after service)**

Print-ready text for a 4×6 referral card:

Front:
- "Did we earn a 5-star clean?" — headline
- IICRC Firm cert # + WoolSafe (if member)
- 5-star Google rating visual + review count
- QR code placeholder: [Google Review QR]

Back:
- Referral program (NO discount-for-review incentive — referral discount is separate from review request):
  "Refer a neighbor: When they book their first cleaning and mention your name, you get [$ amount] off your next visit — no strings attached."
  (Referral reward is for bringing a new customer, not for leaving a review — these must be separate programs)
- Services list
- Phone + booking link

---

**ASSET 3: Property Manager Outreach Template (Email)**

Subject: IICRC Certified Carpet Cleaning for [Property Management Company Name]'s Portfolio

Body structure:
- Opening: Reference specific properties or neighborhoods they manage (do the research)
- Pain point: Turnover timeline pressure, tenant-caused carpet damage, HOA inspection standards
- Solution: IICRC-certified process + fast turnaround + documentation (IICRC service record = paper trail for security deposit disputes)
- Credibility: Firm cert #, WRT cert if water damage work offered, WoolSafe if member
- Offer: Trial unit cleaning at no markup — "let the results be the pitch"
- Social proof: Review count + one property manager testimonial if available [PLACEHOLDER if not]
- CTA: 15-minute call or walk one unit together
- Footer: Physical address + unsubscribe (CAN-SPAM)

---

**ASSET 4: Wool Care Instruction Card (Post-Service Leave-Behind)**

For use after cleaning wool carpet, wool area rugs, or natural fiber flooring.

Print-ready content (4×6 card):

**Caring for Your Wool Carpet/Rug After Professional Cleaning**

Fiber type: Wool (protein fiber — different care requirements than synthetic carpet)

Immediate post-cleaning (first 24 hours):
- Allow full drying before heavy furniture replacement ([X–Y hours] typical under [City] conditions)
- Light foot traffic is fine; high heels and rolling furniture on wet pile can cause pile distortion
- Keep children and pets off until fully dry

Maintenance between professional cleanings:
- Vacuum regularly (suction-only preferred; beater bar on low setting for cut pile; avoid beater bar on loop pile)
- Blot spills immediately — do NOT rub (rubbing causes fiber matting and dye migration)
- Never use DIY alkaline carpet cleaners on wool (OxiClean, baking soda pastes, or pH > 8.5 products cause irreversible damage)
- For spot cleaning: use pH-neutral, WoolSafe-approved spot cleaner only

Professional cleaning frequency: Every 12–18 months for residential; 6–12 months for high-traffic or allergy-sensitive households

Questions? Call: [Phone] | IICRC Firm #[Cert #]

---

**ASSET 5: 12-Month Re-Engagement Sequence (Lapsed Customers)**

For customers who haven't booked in 12+ months.

**Email 1 (Month 12 — first lapse contact):**
Subject: It's been a year, [First Name] — time for a refresh?
- Warm, non-pushy; reference original service if CRM has it
- Seasonal angle (tie to current season's cleaning needs)
- IICRC credential reminder
- Booking link + phone
- Unsubscribe footer

**Email 2 (Month 14 — second attempt):**
Subject: [City] dust season is here — is your carpet ready?
- Local environmental angle (Las Vegas: dust/caliche season, monsoon aftermath, pre-holiday)
- "One cleaning protects your carpet and your manufacturer warranty" — warranty angle
- Direct CTA
- Unsubscribe footer

**Email 3 (Month 18 — final attempt):**
Subject: Last note from [Business Name]
- Acknowledge it's been a while; no pressure
- "We'll remove you from our list if we don't hear back — but if you ever need IICRC-certified carpet care in [City], we're here"
- Booking link
- Unsubscribe + physical address footer

COMPLIANCE: All emails include physical mailing address and one-click unsubscribe per CAN-SPAM. No incentive language in any re-engagement email.
```
