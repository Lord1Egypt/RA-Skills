## General Formatting Rules

1. **Title**: Centered, bold
2. **Party Information**: Individual: name+gender+ethnicity+DOB+ID No.+address+phone; Legal entity: name+address+unified social credit code+legal representative
3. **Claims/Requests**: Listed separately, specific and clear
4. **Facts and Reasons**: Chronological narrative + legal analysis + legal basis
5. **Closing**: Court name to which addressed + signature/seal + date
6. **Attachments**: Number of copies (defendants + 1), evidence index
7. **Format**: A4 paper, margins 2.54cm top/bottom, 3.17cm left/right

## 1. Civil Complaint

```
CIVIL COMPLAINT

Plaintiff: [Name, gender, ethnicity, DOB, ID No., address, phone]
(If legal entity: [Name, address, unified social credit code])
Legal Representative: [Name, title]
Authorized Agent: [Name, (if attorney, indicate firm)]

Defendant: [Name, gender, ethnicity, DOB, ID No., address, phone]
(If legal entity: [Name, address, unified social credit code])
Legal Representative: [Name, title]

CLAIMS:
1. [Specific claim, e.g., order defendant to pay RMB XXX]
2. [Order defendant to bear litigation costs]
3. [Other claims]

FACTS AND REASONS:
[Narrate facts chronologically, clearly and with emphasis on key points]

[Cite specific legal basis]

In conclusion, pursuant to Article [X] of the Civil Code of the PRC and
Article [X] of the Civil Procedure Law of the PRC, the plaintiff hereby
files this action with your court and respectfully requests support for
all claims.

Sincerely submitted to:
[Court Name]

Plaintiff: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]

Attachments:
1. [X] copies of this complaint
2. [X] items of evidence
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Claims must be specific and enforceable, not "judge according to law"
- Narrate facts chronologically, cross-reference evidence (see evidence page X)
- Cite specific legal provisions with full names
- List service addresses for multiple defendants
- Verify correct jurisdiction

## 2. Civil Answer

```
CIVIL ANSWER

Defendant/Respondent: [Name]
(If legal entity: [Name, address, unified social credit code])
Legal Representative: [Name, title]

Plaintiff/Appellant: [Name]

In response to the plaintiff's complaint in the [cause of action] dispute
(Case No. [XXXX]), the defendant submits this answer as follows:

DEFENSE:

I. [Response to each fact alleged in the complaint]
(Indicate admitted facts and disputed facts)

II. [Response to plaintiff's claims]
(Which are accepted, which are opposed, and why)

III. [Defendant's primary defense grounds]
(Legal basis + factual basis)

In conclusion, [defendant's core position]. Your court is respectfully
requested to dismiss plaintiff's claims in their entirety / render a
just judgment.

Sincerely submitted to:
[Court Name]

Defendant: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]

Attachments: [X] items of evidence
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Respond to complaint point by point, do not omit disputed issues
- Clearly admit undisputed facts to narrow the scope of dispute
- Provide counter-evidence or rebuttal for disputed facts
- Answer deadline: 15 days from receiving complaint (ordinary procedure)

## 3. Appeal

```
CIVIL APPEAL

Appellant (Original [Plaintiff/Defendant/Third Party]): [Name]
Respondent (Original [Plaintiff/Defendant/Third Party]): [Name]

Appellant appeals the civil judgment/ruling ([Year]) [Court] Civil
Case No. [X] rendered by [Court Name] in the [cause of action] dispute
between appellant and respondent.

APPEAL REQUESTS:
1. Vacate paragraph [X] of ([Year]) [Case No.] of [Court Name];
2. Amend to [specific amended judgment sought];
3. Respondent to bear all litigation costs.

GROUNDS FOR APPEAL:

I. The lower court erred in fact-finding
[Identify the specific findings that are wrong and what the correct
findings should be]
[Provide evidence support]

II. The lower court erred in legal application
[The legal provisions applied and why they were wrong]
[The correct legal provisions that should apply]

III. Procedural violations by the lower court (if any)
[Specific procedural violations]

In conclusion, [restate appeal requests]. Your court is respectfully
requested to support appellant's appeal.

Sincerely submitted to:
[Appellate Court Name]

Appellant: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Appeal deadline: 15 days (civil judgment) / 10 days (civil ruling); 10 days (criminal judgment) / 5 days (criminal ruling)
- Distinct from retrial: appeal challenges pre-effective judgment; retrial challenges effective judgment
- Note the "no additional punishment on appeal" principle (criminal, defendant-only appeal)

## 4. Retrial Petition

```
RETRIAL PETITION

Petitioner (Original [Plaintiff/Defendant/Third Party]): [Name]
Respondent (Original [Plaintiff/Defendant/Third Party]): [Name]

Petitioner appeals the civil judgment/ruling ([Year]) [Court] Civil
Case No. [X] rendered by [Court Name] in the [cause of action] dispute
between petitioner and respondent, and petitions for retrial pursuant to
Article 211([X]) of the Civil Procedure Law of the PRC.

RETRIAL REQUESTS:
1. Vacate the judgment/ruling ([Year]) [Case No.] of [Court Name];
2. Amend to [requested amendment] / Remand for retrial;
3. Respondent to bear all litigation costs.

STATUTORY GROUNDS FOR RETRIAL:
[Cite specific subsection of Civil Procedure Law Art. 211]

FACTS AND REASONS:
[Argument focused on the selected retrial grounds]

Sincerely submitted to:
[Court with Jurisdiction]

Petitioner: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]

Attachments:
1. Copy of original judgment/ruling
2. New evidence (if any)
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Retrial deadline: 6 months from judgment becoming effective (《民诉法》Art. 216)
- Must explicitly cite statutory retrial grounds (one of 13 grounds in Art. 211)
- For new evidence, must explain why it was not submitted earlier
- Filing a retrial petition does not stay enforcement (court may order stay)

## 5. Representation Opinion / Defense Statement

```
REPRESENTATION OPINION / DEFENSE STATEMENT

Your Honors (Presiding Judge and Judges):

[Firm Name] accepts the appointment of [Client] and assigns [Attorney Name]
to serve as [representative/defense counsel]. Having reviewed the case file,
conducted investigations, and participated in the trial, counsel now submits
the following opinion:

I. FINDINGS OF FACT
   (A) Undisputed facts — confirmed
   (B) Disputed fact 1 — analysis based on evidence...
   (C) Disputed fact 2 — analysis based on evidence...

II. LEGAL ANALYSIS
   (A) Legal analysis of issue 1...
   (B) Legal analysis of issue 2...

III. CONCLUSION
[Clearly state position on the specific claims and reasons]

Respectfully submitted for the panel's consideration.

[Attorney Name]
[Firm Name]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Opening address: "Your Honors" or "Presiding Judge and Judges"
- Focus on disputed issues identified during trial; do not stray
- Factual arguments must be grounded entirely in evidence, not speculation
- Cite precise legal provisions
- Criminal defense: note "in dubio pro reo" and "favor the defendant" principles
- Civil representation: distinguish factual review from legal review

## 6. Enforcement Application

```
ENFORCEMENT APPLICATION

Applicant/Enforcement Creditor: [Name]
Respondent/Enforcement Debtor: [Name]

The applicant seeks enforcement of the legally effective judgment/ruling/
mediation statement ([Year]) [Court] Civil Case No. [X] rendered by
[Court Name]. The respondent has failed to perform its obligations under
the enforceable instrument. The applicant hereby applies for compulsory
enforcement pursuant to Article 246 of the Civil Procedure Law of the PRC.

Enforceable Instrument: ([Year]) [Case No.] [judgment/ruling/mediation]

ENFORCEMENT TARGET:
1. [Monetary payment: amount and calculation]
2. [Specific performance: description of act]
3. [Late payment interest / enforcement fines]

DEBTOR'S ASSET LEADS (if known):
1. Bank account: [Bank name + account No.]
2. Real property: [Location + certificate No.]
3. Vehicle: [License plate + make/model]
4. Other assets: [Equity/claims/IP, etc.]

Sincerely submitted to:
[Court with Jurisdiction]

Applicant: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Enforcement deadline: 2 years (commences from last installment due date / effective date)
- Jurisdiction: first instance court or property-location court at the same level
- More detailed asset leads = faster enforcement
- May simultaneously request restrictions on high spending and inclusion on dishonesty list

## 7. Asset Preservation Application

```
ASSET PRESERVATION APPLICATION

Applicant: [Name]
Respondent: [Name]

APPLICATION:
Petition to freeze respondent's bank deposits up to RMB XXX, or to seize/
impound other property of equivalent value.

FACTS AND REASONS:
[Briefly state case facts and necessity of preservation]
[Explain why preservation is necessary to prevent judgment from becoming
unenforceable]

SECURITY:
[Cash/bank guarantee/property mortgage/litigation preservation insurance]

Sincerely submitted to:
[Court Name]

Applicant: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Pre-action preservation: full security required, ruling within 48 hours
- In-litigation preservation: court may require or waive security
- Preservation scope must not exceed the claims
- Wrongful preservation causing loss is compensable

## 8. Jurisdictional Objection

```
JURISDICTIONAL OBJECTION

Objector ([Litigation Status]): [Name]

Objector raises a jurisdictional objection regarding the above-captioned
[cause of action] dispute.

GROUNDS:

I. [Why this court lacks jurisdiction]
(e.g., Defendant's domicile is at [location] / Contract performance
location is [location] / Real property is at [location])

II. [Which court should have jurisdiction]

LEGAL BASIS:
Article [X] of the Civil Procedure Law of the PRC

Sincerely submitted to:
[Court Name]

Objector: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Deadline: within 15 days of receiving complaint (during answer period)
- Objection ruling is appealable (10 days)
- Do not abuse to delay proceedings

## 9. Evidence Investigation Application

```
EVIDENCE INVESTIGATION APPLICATION

Applicant: [Name]

APPLICATION:
Petition the court to investigate and collect the following evidence:
[Specific evidence description]

FACTS AND REASONS:
I. [Importance of the evidence to the case]
II. [Reasons applicant cannot collect it independently]
(e.g., held by a government agency / involves state secrets / trade
secrets / objective impossibility)

EVIDENCE LEADS:
[Provide as detailed leads as possible: custodian, document name,
reference number, general content]

Sincerely submitted to:
[Court Name]

Applicant: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Submit within evidence submission period
- Must explain "objective inability to collect independently"
- Provide sufficient detail for the court to locate the evidence
- One reconsideration available if court denies

## 10. Enforcement Objection

```
ENFORCEMENT OBJECTION

Objector (Outsider / Party): [Name]

Objector objects to this court's enforcement of [specific measure/
specific asset] in the enforcement proceedings of [Creditor] v. [Debtor],
[cause of action] dispute.

OBJECTION REQUEST:
Petition to cease/remove enforcement against [specific asset/measure].

FACTS AND REASONS:
I. [Objector's claimed right]
(Outsider: ownership/mortgage/leasehold over subject asset)
(Party: enforcement violates law / exceeds scope / other violations)

II. [Evidence supporting claimed right]
III. [Legal basis]

Sincerely submitted to:
[Enforcement Court]

Objector: [Signature/Seal]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Two types: outsider's objection (substantive right) and party's objection (enforcement conduct)
- Outsider objection: review within 15 days, dissatisfied party may file lawsuit
- Party objection: review within 15 days, dissatisfied party may apply for reconsideration to higher court

## 11. Cross-Examination Opinion

```
CROSS-EXAMINATION OPINION

[Case Name / Case No.]

Regarding Group [X] of evidence submitted by [opposing party/prosecution],
counsel submits the following cross-examination opinion:

┌─────────────────┬──────┬──────┬──────┬────────────────────────┐
│ Evidence Name   │Auth. │Legal │Relv. │Specific Opinion & Basis│
├─────────────────┼──────┼──────┼──────┼────────────────────────┤
│ Evidence 1      │ Y/N  │ Y/N  │ Y/N  │ [Analysis]             │
│ Evidence 2      │ Y/N  │ Y/N  │ Y/N  │ [Analysis]             │
└─────────────────┴──────┴──────┴──────┴────────────────────────┘

OVERALL CROSS-EXAMINATION OPINION:
[Overall assessment of opposing party's evidence system — can it prove their claim?]

Counsel: [Signature]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Analyze all three dimensions separately (authenticity, legality, relevance)
- For authenticity objections: specify (original/copy, signature/seal, timing, etc.)
- For legality objections: specify collection subject/procedure/form violations
- For relevance objections: specify no logical connection to facts to be proved
- Distinguish between evidence with no objection vs. partial objection

## 12. Legal Opinion

```
LEGAL OPINION

To: [Client Name]

Re: [Matter description]

[Firm Name], upon engagement by [Client], issues this legal opinion
regarding [matter]. Having reviewed relevant documents and verified
the facts, counsel renders the following opinion:

I. KEY FACTS
[Chronological summary of key facts]

II. LEGAL ANALYSIS
   (A) Legal analysis of issue 1...
   (B) Legal analysis of issue 2...
   (C) Other legal issues...

III. LEGAL RISK WARNINGS
[Identify risks and possible consequences]

IV. CONCLUSIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS
[Clear legal conclusion and actionable recommendations]

This legal opinion is based solely on information provided by the client
and the laws currently in effect. It is for the client's internal reference
only and not for any other purpose.

[Firm Name] (Seal)
Attorney: [Signature]
[Date]
```

**Drafting Tips:**
- Opening statement: basis and scope of the opinion
- Facts section: must note source of information (based on client-provided materials)
- Legal analysis: cite specific provisions
- Risk warnings: comprehensive (litigation risk + enforcement risk + cost risk)
- Conclusion: clear and definite, not ambiguous
- Include disclaimer
