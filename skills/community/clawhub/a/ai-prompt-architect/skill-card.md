## Description: <br>
Prompt Architect helps agents interview users, design, improve, debug, and evaluate prompts and AI workflows with structured templates, workflow diagrams, examples, and review checklists. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jzw6](https://clawhub.ai/user/jzw6) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, prompt engineers, and teams building AI assistants use this skill to turn rough requirements or existing prompts into production-oriented system prompts, few-shot examples, and agent workflow designs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate prompts or workflow guidance that may be used in customer-facing, regulated, or high-impact settings. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts, examples, and workflow decisions before deployment, especially for compliance, safety boundaries, and domain-specific accuracy. <br>
Risk: The skill defaults to Chinese responses unless the user requests another language. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the desired output language when using the skill in English or multilingual workflows. <br>
