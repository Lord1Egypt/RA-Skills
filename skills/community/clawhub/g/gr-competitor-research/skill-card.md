## Description: <br>
Provides a structured competitor research playbook for website evolution analysis, social media teardown, traffic source review, ad spend review, X/Twitter propagation mapping, and growth flywheel scoring. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Growth teams, founders, product marketers, and market researchers use this skill to organize competitor research and turn external signals into reusable go-to-market, positioning, content, and growth-flywheel insights. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may activate the skill for adjacent market-research requests beyond narrow competitor-teardown workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Review and narrow trigger wording before installation if activation should be limited to specific competitor research workflows. <br>
Risk: Competitive intelligence outputs may be misleading when external traffic, advertising, search, or social data is outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important findings against current source tools and primary evidence before using the analysis for business decisions. <br>
