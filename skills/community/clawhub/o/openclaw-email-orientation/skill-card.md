## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Credential paths and authentication setup are discussed, which could lead to accidental disclosure of credential file contents. <br>
Mitigation: Mention credential paths only; do not print or paste credential file contents into chat. <br>
Risk: Example commands can authenticate Google accounts, send mail, remove accounts, or create calendar events. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target account and permission from the Google account owner before running account-changing gog commands. <br>
