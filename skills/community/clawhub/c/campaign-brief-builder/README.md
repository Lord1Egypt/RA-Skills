# Campaign Brief Builder

**Platforms:** Claude · Codex
**Domain:** Marketing

## Purpose

Turns a rough campaign idea into a complete, review-ready campaign brief so every campaign starts with a measurable objective, a justified channel plan, and a known asset list before kickoff. Replaces inconsistent, manually filled brief templates with a repeatable, rigorous intake.

## When to Use

- At the start of any marketing campaign, before creative or media work begins
- When a stakeholder request is too vague to act on and needs structuring
- When campaigns repeatedly launch without a measurable goal or clear asset list
- When standardizing how briefs are written across a marketing team

## What It Does

**Phase 1: Intake**
1. Runs a focused interview — one question at a time — covering idea, offer, audience, objective, budget, timeline, and constraints

**Phase 2: Strategy**
2. Pressure-tests the objective into a single SMART statement and confirms it
3. Defines the audience, core insight, and single key message
4. Builds a justified channel plan and a measurement plan with a primary KPI

**Phase 3: Output**
5. Generates the brief plus an asset tracker checklist and an assumptions/open-questions list, then offers one revision pass

## Notes

The skill defines what the creative must achieve; it does not produce ad copy or designs. It never invents benchmarks or audience data — unknowns are marked as assumptions or items to confirm.
