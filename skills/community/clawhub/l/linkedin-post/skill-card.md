## Description: <br>
Linkedin Post helps draft, refine, organize, search, and export LinkedIn content such as hooks, hashtags, CTAs, schedules, rewrites, translations, tone notes, headlines, and outlines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, founders, and agents use this skill to manage LinkedIn post ideas and revisions through a local command-line workflow. It is useful for capturing drafts, hooks, hashtags, CTAs, schedules, rewrites, translations, tone adjustments, headlines, outlines, and searchable/exportable activity history. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: LinkedIn drafts, schedules, headlines, comments, and similar entered text are stored locally in plaintext and can later be searched or exported. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential client data, internal metrics, embargoed announcements, or other sensitive material unless you will manage access to and deletion of ~/.local/share/linkedin-post. <br>
