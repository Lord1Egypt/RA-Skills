## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Live-trading examples can affect real accounts if copied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review every passorder call, account identifier, signal file path, duplicate-order control, stop-loss rule, and QMT-side capital or risk limit before using generated or copied code with a real account. <br>
Risk: Incorrect QMT strategy guidance could produce unsafe or unintended trading behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Test strategy logic in backtesting or paper-trading workflows and confirm stop-loss, take-profit, exception handling, and duplicate-order safeguards before live deployment. <br>
