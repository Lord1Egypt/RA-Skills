Mitigation: Treat field guidance as informational; confirm legal permissions, site restrictions, ownership rules, and safety precautions before searching for or handling fossils. <br>
Risk: Educational paleontology answers may simplify scientific topics or contain outdated details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for general learning and verify important scientific claims against current paleontology references or expert sources. <br>
