Risk: Legal, tax, non-compete, and salary-negotiation advice may be incomplete or jurisdiction-dependent. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's responses as general coaching and verify important legal, tax, compensation, or non-compete decisions with appropriate professionals. <br>
