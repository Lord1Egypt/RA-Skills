## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language medical-reference skill that helps an agent answer from a Li Ke-style traditional Chinese medicine perspective using internal case notes, formula references, six-channel differentiation, dose discussions, and false-pattern identification. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jangviktor-web](https://clawhub.ai/user/jangviktor-web) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill for historical or academic study of Li Ke-style traditional Chinese medicine reasoning, case retrieval, formula discussion, and symptom-to-pattern routing. It should not be used for real diagnosis, dosing, emergency care, cancer treatment, pregnancy, pediatric illness, or medication decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce medical treatment, dosing, and emergency-care guidance that users may mistake for professional care. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for historical or academic study; do not rely on it for diagnosis, dosing, medication decisions, or treatment planning. <br>
Risk: The artifact covers acute symptoms, toxic-herb formulas, cancer treatment, pregnancy, pediatric illness, and emergency scenarios. <br>
Mitigation: Route acute or high-risk symptoms to licensed emergency medical care and avoid following formula or preparation instructions as medical advice. <br>
