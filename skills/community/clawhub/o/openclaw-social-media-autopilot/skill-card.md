## Description: <br>
Generate a complete 7-day social media content calendar with Instagram-ready captions, hashtags, optimal posting times, and content mix strategy for any brand. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dreamsarts](https://clawhub.ai/user/dreamsarts) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, creators, and small businesses use this skill to draft a 7-day social media content calendar with platform-ready captions, hashtags, posting times, calls to action, and content-pillar balance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Brand, audience, tone, and campaign details are sent to Gemini for content generation. <br>
Mitigation: Use only with information approved for Gemini processing, avoid confidential campaign details, and review generated copy before use. <br>
Risk: The artifact references a hard-coded local .env path for the Gemini API key. <br>
Mitigation: Replace the local path with the deploying user's own secret-management setup before running the tool. <br>
Risk: Generated posts may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review and explicit approval before scheduling or publishing any generated content. <br>
