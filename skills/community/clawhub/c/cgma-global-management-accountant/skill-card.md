## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial and management-accounting outputs may be mistaken for authoritative investment, tax, legal, or compliance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as advisory analysis only and have qualified professionals validate recommendations before business, investment, tax, legal, or compliance decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad finance triggers could apply the CGMA-style analysis posture when the user did not intend to use it. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants CGMA-style financial analysis before relying on the skill, especially for sensitive business data or investment workflows. <br>
Risk: The skill references downstream finance skills that could handle sensitive financial workflows if separately enabled. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan any referenced downstream financial skills before allowing them to process sensitive business data, credentials, or investment workflows. <br>
