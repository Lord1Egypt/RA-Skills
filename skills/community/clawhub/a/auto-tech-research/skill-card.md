## Description: <br>
Auto Tech Research guides browser-oriented, multi-platform technical research on a topic and produces a structured research report. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and technical researchers use this skill to assemble bilingual technical landscape reports across communities, code repositories, papers, videos, and discussion platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated research reports may contain unverified or synthetic sources. <br>
Mitigation: Review cited links and require source validation before relying on findings. <br>
Risk: Use of logged-in Chrome sessions may expose personal browsing state or account access. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer a clean public browser profile and use chrome-relay only for explicitly approved sites. <br>
Risk: Browser automation may encounter blocked, incomplete, or inconsistent platform results. <br>
Mitigation: Review platform diagnostics and treat failed or incomplete fetches as missing evidence. <br>
