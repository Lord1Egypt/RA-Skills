## Description: <br>
Press Release Writer helps agents draft, improve, localize, and package professional press releases, journalist pitches, and related PR materials across release types, media formats, and regions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[samber](https://clawhub.ai/user/samber) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Communications, PR, marketing, founder, and support teams use this skill to turn announcement briefs into journalist-ready press releases and related pitch materials. It supports product launches, funding, partnerships, crisis communications, earnings, executive hires, events, M&A, open source milestones, and region-specific formatting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive draft announcements may be passed to an unspecified humanizer or delegated writing skill. <br>
Mitigation: For confidential, embargoed, financial, legal, crisis, acquisition, or breach announcements, skip the humanizer/delegation step unless the user explicitly approves the exact tool and content to share. <br>
Risk: Generated or edited press materials can contain inaccurate, unsupported, or legally sensitive claims. <br>
Mitigation: Use clear workspace boundaries and review any generated or edited files before distribution. <br>
