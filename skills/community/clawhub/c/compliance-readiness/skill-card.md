## Description: <br>
AI Compliance Readiness Assessment evaluates how prepared an organization is for AI governance regulations, scores readiness across eight dimensions, and generates an action plan for compliance gaps, audits, or governance roadmaps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Compliance, legal, risk, and governance teams use this skill to assess organizational readiness for AI governance obligations and produce an action-oriented gap report. It is intended for pre-audit preparation, governance roadmap planning, and review of AI systems across regulated jurisdictions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential compliance records, audit details, vendor inventories, or regulated business information while completing the assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential compliance records unless approved for the user's environment, and limit inputs to information appropriate for the agent context. <br>
Risk: The skill's regulatory deadlines and compliance conclusions may be incomplete or outdated for a specific organization or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify regulatory deadlines and legal conclusions with qualified compliance or legal counsel before relying on the assessment. <br>
