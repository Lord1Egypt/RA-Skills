## Description: <br>
Guides agents in writing structured AI video-generation prompts with scene, motion, camera, lighting, style, and platform adaptation guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[heyun9123](https://clawhub.ai/user/heyun9123) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, creators, and prompt engineers use this skill to draft detailed prompts for AI video generation models and adapt wording for different video platforms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompt outputs can still be vague, contradictory, or over-constrained, which may reduce generation quality or create misleading expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Review prompts for clear subject, action, scene, camera movement, lighting, style, duration, and platform constraints before using them. <br>
Risk: Platform-specific model behavior can change after publication. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the platform notes as general guidance and confirm important production choices against current model documentation. <br>
