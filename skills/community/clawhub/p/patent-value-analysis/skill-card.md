## Description: <br>
TG 专利价值分析评估 helps agents draft patent value assessment reports from a user-provided patent name, including patent facts, five-dimensional value scoring, economic-life estimates, valuation ranges, and conversion or financing suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Chase-Zhouchang](https://clawhub.ai/user/Chase-Zhouchang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Patent analysts, IP teams, founders, and agents use this skill to draft structured patent value assessments from patent names. It guides lookup, scoring, valuation-range estimation, and recommendations for commercialization or financing review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce incorrect or incomplete patent status, valuation, legal-strength, or financing conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify findings against authoritative patent databases and qualified legal, valuation, or financial professionals before relying on the report. <br>
Risk: Patent-name searches can expose unpublished inventions, trade secrets, internal codenames, or confidential R&D details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential patent or R&D information unless the user is comfortable with it being used in online searches. <br>
