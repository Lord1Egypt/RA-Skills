## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Branding and naming recommendations can be mistaken for legal, trademark, domain, or competitive clearance. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify trademark registries, domains, social handles, and competitive landscape, and consult qualified counsel before launch decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may trigger on general business-branding prompts where a narrower specialist skill is preferred. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the request is a branding strategy, naming, positioning, messaging, or identity task before relying on the skill output. <br>
