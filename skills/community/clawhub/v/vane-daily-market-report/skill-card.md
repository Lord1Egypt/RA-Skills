## Description: <br>
Automated daily crypto/US stock/A-share report. BTC/ETH/SOL prices, RSI, EMA signals, position P&L, and trading recommendations. Ready for Telegram/Substack publishing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and market analysts use this skill to generate daily market reports covering crypto, US stocks, Chinese A-shares, portfolio P&L, and buy/sell/hold signal summaries for publishing channels such as Telegram or Substack. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading recommendations or indicator summaries may be incorrect, stale, or unsuitable for a user's portfolio. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as informational and verify market data, indicators, and suitability independently before making trading decisions. <br>
Risk: Portfolio or exchange-account data could be exposed if connected through an unsafe implementation. <br>
Mitigation: Use trusted integrations with read-only, narrowly scoped credentials and avoid providing unnecessary account permissions. <br>
