## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product teams, and ecommerce operators use this skill to adapt products and services for AI agents as customers. It provides guidance for machine-readable catalogs, agent-oriented discovery, programmatic pricing, autonomous purchasing flows, and retail replenishment patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Autonomous purchasing guidance could be applied without explicit user authorization or spending controls. <br>
Mitigation: Require user authorization, scoped payment credentials, spending limits, reorder notifications, easy cancellation, and audit logs before enabling purchases. <br>
Risk: Agent or user tracking for commerce flows can expose sensitive behavior if collection and retention are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize collected data, define retention limits, and provide clear privacy notices for agent or user tracking. <br>
