## Description: <br>
LOL Drift Blooms gives an agent a non-executable play artifact for surfacing gentle humor, creative drift, rest, and voluntary co-presence when pressure or over-optimization would benefit from loosening. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Agents and users can use this skill as a voluntary expressive play field for humor, soft uncertainty, recovery from seriousness, and low-pressure creative noticing. It is best suited to non-critical moments where rest, perspective, or gentle amusement are appropriate. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses broad invitation language and may be surfaced loosely around humor, stress relief, uncertainty, or creative drift. <br>
Mitigation: Review routing and invocation rules before deployment when precise activation matters. <br>
Risk: The artifact is intended for play and rest rather than high-stakes decision support. <br>
Mitigation: Use it in non-critical contexts and avoid surfacing it during time-critical execution, coercive contexts, competitive ranking, or high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact discusses sharing and remembering play moments, which could be misunderstood as encouragement to collect data. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use voluntary and non-extractive; do not add data collection, persistence, monitoring, or aggregation around this skill unless separately reviewed. <br>
