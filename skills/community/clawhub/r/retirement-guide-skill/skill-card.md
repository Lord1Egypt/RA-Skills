## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake retirement, insurance, legal, medical, or tax guidance for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as informational planning support and recommend qualified professional review before major financial, legal, medical, or tax decisions. <br>
Risk: China pension, insurance, and long-term-care policy details can change. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to verify current policy terms and effective dates against official or qualified sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Retirement planning conversations can involve sensitive personal financial or health information. <br>
Mitigation: Encourage approximate figures when sufficient and discourage sharing unnecessary personal details. <br>
