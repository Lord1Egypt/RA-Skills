## Description: <br>
Aggregates recent updates from the domestic company website, DXC's global website, core long-term clients, industry policy, bids, competitors, and partnership opportunities into a concise Chinese-first daily business briefing with links and actionable opportunity notes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Rozley](https://clawhub.ai/user/Rozley) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and business development users use this skill to generate daily DXC-focused commercial briefings, monitor official company and customer updates, and identify follow-up opportunities from recent public web sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Briefings may include stale source data, inaccessible links, or commercial conclusions that need validation before business action. <br>
Mitigation: Verify original links, publication dates, and business implications before acting on the briefing. <br>
