## Description: <br>
Turn fuzzy briefs into a stable pre-design workflow for requirement validation, brief clarification, early-stage research, inspiration direction setting, and approved-site inspiration collection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhusunlangzi668](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhusunlangzi668) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Design teams use this skill to turn early or incomplete project briefs into structured pre-design research, direction options, and inspiration references. It is aimed at external and internal design workflows that need brief calibration, research questions, moodboard inputs, and source-aware visual reference gathering. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill defaults to external inspiration sources, which may conflict with workplace approved-source policies. <br>
Mitigation: Review or customize the default source list before deployment and invoke the skill explicitly when external research is appropriate. <br>
Risk: Inspiration, moodboard, and reference links may be mistaken for assets cleared for commercial reuse. <br>
Mitigation: Require users to verify original source pages and license terms before using any referenced material in final design work. <br>
