## Description: <br>
Build surveys, collect responses, and analyze results. Use when creating forms, checking responses, converting data, analyzing trends, generating reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain3](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to create survey-style entries, collect responses, inspect status, search recent activity, and export stored results. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores broad user input locally, and advertised survey, export, or status behavior may not reliably match its claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, test with non-sensitive data first, and avoid personal, customer, employee, research, medical, financial, or confidential survey data unless storage and deletion behavior is clearly documented and fixed. <br>
