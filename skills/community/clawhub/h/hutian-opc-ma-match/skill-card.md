## Description: <br>
Helps agents identify and evaluate A-share and Hong Kong-listed acquisition candidates, shell-company opportunities, buyer profiles, sector fit, and financial feasibility for technology commercialization M&A matching. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External teams, founders, and advisors use this skill to structure M&A target searches, shell-resource screening, buyer motivation analysis, diligence prompts, and matching reports for technology commercialization scenarios. It is most relevant to Mainland China A-share and Hong Kong listed-company contexts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with content focused on Mainland China and Hong Kong securities markets <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shell-company targeting and outreach guidance could be used deceptively or outside legitimate M&A authority. <br>
Mitigation: Use only under legal and compliance supervision, state the real transaction intent, and avoid any request for insider information. <br>
Risk: Contact lists, diligence materials, and prospective transaction data can contain sensitive business or personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Handle materials under privacy, NDA, securities-law, and access-control requirements; store market-data credentials outside public skill files. <br>
Risk: Market, regulatory, and company-status information may become outdated or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations against current official disclosures and qualified financial, legal, and regulatory advice before outreach or transaction decisions. <br>
