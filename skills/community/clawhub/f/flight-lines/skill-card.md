## Description: <br>
Navigate problems along lines of flight by composing operations from arbitrary domains and maintaining parallel work paths as new structure emerges. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill as a creative reasoning framework for low-to-moderate-stakes ideation, especially when conventional domain-bound approaches feel exhausted. It helps map structural constraints and compose operations from unrelated domains into practical problem-solving assemblages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce creative cross-domain guidance that sounds plausible but is not domain-standard advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as brainstorming for low-to-moderate-stakes ideation and rely on qualified expertise or domain-standard guidance for medical, legal, financial, security, or safety-critical decisions. <br>
Risk: Cross-domain analogies can overextend beyond the evidence available for a specific problem. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed operations for concrete executability, structural fit, and real-world constraints before acting on them. <br>
