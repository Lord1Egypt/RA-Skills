## Description: <br>
Workflow Refactor helps agents restructure complex workflows or artifacts into AI-assisted, one-person execution chains by decomposing steps, removing human-limitation compensation layers, and rebuilding an IPO primitive chain. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to analyze traditional workflows, remove handoff and coordination overhead, preserve needed calibration and compliance checks, and reshape the result into an AI-assisted execution plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the agent to apply a workflow-refactoring posture to generic optimization or simplification requests. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when this behavior is wanted, and review recommendations before applying them to real business processes. <br>
Risk: Workflow simplification could remove quality, legal, safety, or compliance checkpoints if the method is applied too aggressively. <br>
Mitigation: Keep human review for compliance and other high-impact decisions, and verify that retained checkpoints satisfy the user's real constraints. <br>
