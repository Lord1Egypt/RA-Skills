## Description: <br>
Crypto-native Web3 news and flash intelligence from MarsBit through hosted MCP for L1/L2 ecosystems, DeFi/CeFi, regulation, exchange flows, and market-moving events. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to retrieve current crypto and Web3 news, flash updates, keyword searches, article details, related stories, and channel listings from MarsBit's hosted MCP endpoint. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Queries and prompt context are sent to MarsBit's hosted MCP service. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include secrets, private trading plans, customer data, or proprietary internal context when using the skill. <br>
Risk: Crypto news and flash updates can be time-sensitive or market-moving. <br>
Mitigation: Verify publication times and source context before using retrieved information for decisions. <br>
