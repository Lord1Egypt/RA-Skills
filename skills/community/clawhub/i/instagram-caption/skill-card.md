## Description: <br>
Instagram Caption helps users draft captions, hashtags, Story and Reels scripts, bio optimization notes, and content calendar entries for Instagram workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and social media operators use this skill to draft, revise, search, schedule, and export Instagram caption workflow notes locally. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Instagram drafts, campaign notes, schedules, hashtags, and exports may be saved in plaintext on the local machine. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter secrets, credentials, confidential client material, or unreleased business-sensitive copy unless local plaintext retention is acceptable. <br>
