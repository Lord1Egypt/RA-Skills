## Description: <br>
Uncover the real "job" customers hire your product to do. Goes beyond features to understand functional, emotional, and social motivations. Use when user says "jobs to be done", "jtbd", "why do customers", "what job", "customer motivation", "what problem", "user needs", "why do people buy". <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[artyomx33](https://clawhub.ai/user/artyomx33) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, marketers, and product teams use this skill to analyze why customers choose a product and to map functional, emotional, and social jobs. It helps turn customer motivations, alternatives, underserved needs, and priority into product and positioning guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad product strategy questions where the user did not intend a Jobs-To-Be-Done analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that a JTBD framing is useful before applying the framework to ambiguous product strategy requests. <br>
Risk: Customer interviews, product research, and business strategy notes may contain confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting confidential customer research unless the assistant environment is approved for that data. <br>
