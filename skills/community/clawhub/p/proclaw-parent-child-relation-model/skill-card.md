## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese-language parent-child relationship support system for family empowerment, healthier communication, core energy protection, self-drive, exploration, life direction, and confidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thinkbugs](https://clawhub.ai/user/thinkbugs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Parents, caregivers, family-education practitioners, and agents supporting them use this skill to analyze parent-child dynamics, diagnose control, energy, responsibility, and attachment patterns, and produce structured improvement plans, scripts, worksheets, and referral-aware guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Completed worksheets and generated records can contain sensitive child and family information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid full names and unnecessary identifying details, store outputs in private locations, and share them only with appropriate trusted parties. <br>
Risk: Parenting guidance may be inappropriate for urgent safety, medical, legal, child-protection, domestic violence, abuse, self-harm, or severe mental-health situations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as educational support only and seek qualified professional, emergency, legal, medical, or child-protection help when safety or serious symptoms are involved. <br>
