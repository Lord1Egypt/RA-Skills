## Description: <br>
Advisor Finder helps users find and analyze potential academic advisors for a target university, department, or research direction, ranking faculty by research match, recent activity, and public profile evidence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ntsl-ye](https://clawhub.ai/user/ntsl-ye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and academic applicants use this skill to build evidence-based advisor shortlists for master's, PhD, RA, visiting, or exploratory outreach decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Advisor rankings may reflect incomplete, stale, or hard-to-scrape public university pages. <br>
Mitigation: Verify affiliations, faculty pages, recent papers, and evidence quality before relying on the shortlist. <br>
Risk: Public contact details or research interests may be outdated or misattributed. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check official faculty pages, personal homepages, publication profiles, and emails before outreach. <br>
Risk: The skill may output in Chinese unless the user asks otherwise. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the preferred output language when invoking the skill. <br>
