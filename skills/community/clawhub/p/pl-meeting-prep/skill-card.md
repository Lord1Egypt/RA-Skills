## Description: <br>
Automates pre-meeting research, agenda creation, talking points, and post-meeting action tracking for informed and efficient client, board, or vendor meetings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[samledger67-dotcom](https://clawhub.ai/user/samledger67-dotcom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Finance professionals, client advisors, and operations leads use this skill to prepare meeting briefs, agendas, talking points, and follow-up action summaries for client, board, vendor, and stakeholder meetings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read sensitive client, calendar, notes, and financial context. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped accounts and folders, and grant access only to the sources needed for the specific meeting workflow. <br>
Risk: Meeting briefs and logs may retain sensitive client or financial information. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated briefs before keeping or sharing them, and periodically delete or redact stored meeting logs that should not persist. <br>
Risk: Generated research, financial details, and contact context may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before use or external sharing, require citations or source labels, and mark uncertain facts as needing verification. <br>
