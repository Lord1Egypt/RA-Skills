# Marketplace Guide

Taobao is strong for:

- variety and long-tail products
- fashion, accessories, home goods, custom goods, and niche styles
- broad price range and many competing sellers
- cases where the user wants choice more than standardized fulfillment

Taobao is weaker when:

- authenticity, warranty, invoice, or official after-sales matters most
- the product is beauty, baby, health, safety-related, high-value electronics, or branded shoes/bags
- the listing has unclear SKU variants or a suspiciously low headline price
- final price depends on account-only coupons, payment offers, region, or checkout-only rules

Trust cues to capture:

- Tmall flagship / official flagship / Tmall / enterprise / personal store
- description, service, and logistics ratings
- recent review quality and photo evidence
- return policy, shipping insurance, authenticity promise, warranty, invoice
- whether the selected SKU matches the title and main image

Common traps:

- headline price belongs to a low-spec SKU
- coupon-after price requires hidden membership or payment conditions
- similar-looking SKU differs by size, count, model, color, flavor, or bundle
- review volume is high but recent reviews reveal quality drift
- personal store is cheap but after-sales and returns are unclear
