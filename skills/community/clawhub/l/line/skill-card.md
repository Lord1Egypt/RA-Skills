## Description: <br>
Provides organized summaries of LINE's public platform features, content formats, operational rules, and help resources without accessing private data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codenova58](https://clawhub.ai/user/codenova58) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, operators, and researchers use this skill to organize public LINE product pages, help resources, community rules, and commercial or creator-facing information into concise summaries, checklists, or tables. It is scoped to public information and excludes account operations, private messages, group control, permission bypass, and safety-control evasion. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Public LINE help pages, rules, and regional feature descriptions may change frequently or vary by location. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve original source links, access dates, and applicable regions in outputs, and verify current pages before relying on summaries. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misapplied to private data, account actions, group controls, or attempts to bypass platform protections. <br>
Mitigation: Limit use to publicly visible information and do not request or perform account login, private-message access, group administration, permission bypass, or safety-control evasion. <br>
