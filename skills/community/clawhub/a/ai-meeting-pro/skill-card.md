## Description: <br>
AI meeting assistant for agenda generation, minute taking, and action item tracking using Manager-Worker pattern. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[banxian87](https://clawhub.ai/user/banxian87) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, managers, and team leads use this skill to draft meeting agendas, capture minutes, summarize decisions, and track follow-up actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meeting transcripts can contain sensitive, regulated, or private participant information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm participant consent and organizational rules for recording or transcription; avoid sensitive content unless processing and storage are approved. <br>
Risk: Generated minutes, decisions, or action items may misstate what participants agreed to. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated meeting records with the appropriate participants before relying on them for follow-up. <br>
