## Description: <br>
This skill helps users in China estimate retirement timing and social-insurance contributions using the 2025 delayed-retirement policy, city contribution bases, salary, and employment type. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[feversaga](https://clawhub.ai/user/feversaga) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan retirement timing and estimate future personal social-insurance costs for employee or flexible-employment scenarios in mainland China. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Mainland China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks for personal and salary details to estimate retirement and social-insurance costs. <br>
Mitigation: Use approximate values where possible and avoid retaining or sharing unnecessary personal data. <br>
Risk: Retirement and social-insurance calculations may become inaccurate when local rules or contribution bases change. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as estimates and verify them against current local official rules before relying on them. <br>
