This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, technical writers, and documentation authors use this skill to draft Mermaid, Graphviz, data chart, UML, timeline, and mind map syntax for diagrams in technical content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Diagram syntax may be rendered by external Mermaid, Graphviz, or other tools whose behavior is outside this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Evaluate and sandbox any renderer separately, and review generated diagrams before publication. <br>
