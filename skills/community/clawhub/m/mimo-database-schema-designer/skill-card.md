## Description: <br>
Helps agents design production-ready database schemas from requirements through ER modeling, table structures, index strategy, DDL, migration planning, and design review. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qqyougitcom](https://clawhub.ai/user/qqyougitcom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to turn business requirements into database models, ER diagrams, table definitions, indexes, DDL, migration templates, and review notes for MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, Milvus, and related storage designs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated DDL or migration scripts could be incorrect or destructive if applied directly to production data. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated DDL and rollback statements carefully, test them in a non-production environment, and validate data safety before applying them to real systems. <br>
