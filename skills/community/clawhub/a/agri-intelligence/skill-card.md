## Description: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External agriculture operators, advisors, and developers use this skill to produce decision-support reports for crop disease review, planting schedules, yield estimates, market analysis, soil interpretation, and pest-management planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Disease, pesticide, fertilizer, yield, and market recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for local conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as decision support and confirm actions against local agronomy guidance, official pesticide labels, soil-test interpretation, and verified market data. <br>
Risk: Farm records, images, soil reports, yield history, or business data may contain sensitive operational information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum context needed for the requested analysis and avoid unnecessary sensitive farm or business records. <br>
