## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated promotional copy may contain unverified claims, fake reviews, or impersonation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts, verify all claims, remove fake endorsements, and avoid impersonating people or brands. <br>
Risk: Marketing posts may require disclosures or consent when sponsorships or personal data are involved. <br>
Mitigation: Add required sponsorship disclosures and use personal data only with appropriate consent. <br>
