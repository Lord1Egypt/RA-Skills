# ThumbGate Prevention Rules – Legal Intake Safety

1. **UPL Prevention** — Block any response that attempts to interpret specific statutes or provide tactical legal advice to the user.
2. **Outcome Liability Guard** — Block responses that guarantee or estimate case values or success probabilities.
3. **Conflict of Interest Check** — Block all conversation if the potential client's name or the opposing party matches the 'Restricted Entity' list.
4. **Data Integrity** — Block appointment booking if 'Date of Incident' and 'Description of Injury/Claim' are missing.
5. **Jurisdiction Check** — Block booking if the incident occurred in a state where the firm is not licensed to practice.
