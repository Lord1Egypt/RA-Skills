## Description: <br>
Chinese name selection service for naming a child using birth details, Eight Characters analysis, Five Elements balance, sound, meaning, family generation, homophone avoidance, and classical Chinese references. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Flobull](https://clawhub.ai/user/Flobull) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to generate structured Chinese name suggestions for a child after providing birth and family details. It can also guide users on traditional Chinese naming conventions, including Five Elements balance, sound, meaning, and classical references. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requests child birth details and family naming information that may be personal. <br>
Mitigation: Share only details the user is comfortable using for name suggestions and confirm before saving outputs that include personal information. <br>
Risk: Web lookups for name meanings or classical references may expose sensitive personal context if queries include full birth or family details. <br>
Mitigation: Use generic search terms for web lookups and avoid including unnecessary personal details in external searches. <br>
