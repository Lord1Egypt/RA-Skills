## Use Case: <br>
Developers, audio engineers, creators, and podcast teams use this skill to plan and configure AI-assisted audio processing workflows for transcription, speech generation, restoration, music analysis, mastering, podcast publishing, and translated dubbing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers sensitive audio workflows such as voice samples, voice cloning, transcription, translated dubbing, and publishing outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with recordings and voice samples the user is authorized to process, and confirm consent before cloning, translating, dubbing, or publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated transcripts, subtitles, audio edits, RSS feeds, or published audio may contain errors or unsuitable content. <br>
Mitigation: Review transcripts, generated audio, and any RSS or publishing output manually before sharing. <br>
