## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Cold outreach and public posting tactics can affect brand reputation or conflict with platform and email rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review outreach copy and posting plans before use, and confirm they comply with applicable platform policies and email regulations. <br>
Risk: Growth experiments can produce misleading conclusions when run without enough traffic or tracked activation metrics. <br>
Mitigation: Define one target metric, track source and activation data, and wait for sufficient conversions before acting on A/B test results. <br>
