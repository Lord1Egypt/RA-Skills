## Description: <br>
Draft professional follow-up emails to contacts made at conferences - not too pushy, but memorable. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to draft conference follow-up emails or LinkedIn connection messages from a contact name, conference, topic, sender name, and tone. It is intended for bounded networking outreach, with assumptions and limits called out when inputs are missing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The documentation includes broad academic-writing wording that is outside the core email-drafting scope. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for conference follow-up email or LinkedIn drafts and stop when a request needs unsupported assumptions. <br>
Risk: Generated outreach can include sensitive personal or business details when supplied in prompts or command arguments. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive details unless they are appropriate for local generated output, and review drafts before sending. <br>
