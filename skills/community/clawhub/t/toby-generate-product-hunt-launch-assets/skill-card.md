## Description: <br>
Generates complete Product Hunt launch assets tailored to SaaS founders and startup teams supporting product-led growth strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External SaaS founders, product-led growth teams, and startup founders use this skill to draft and refine Product Hunt launch copy, visuals, and supporting launch materials before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated launch copy or images may be inaccurate, misleading, or unsuitable for publication without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit all generated materials before publishing them externally. <br>
Risk: The skill declares broad Bash and file-reading access that may not be needed for every launch asset request. <br>
Mitigation: Approve Bash or file-reading actions only when they are clearly necessary for the requested launch assets. <br>
