## Description: <br>
Create, manage, sync, and share minimalist notes with tags, search, and reminders across multiple devices using simple commands. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hgta23](https://clawhub.ai/user/hgta23) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People who want lightweight note capture use this skill to create, tag, search, sync, share, and set reminders for notes through natural-language requests. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Notes may contain sensitive information, and the release does not document backend privacy, retention, deletion, or access controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for low-sensitivity notes until the publisher documents the backend, privacy policy, retention and deletion controls, and access revocation process. <br>
Risk: Sharing and syncing notes can expose content to other users or devices without clear confirmation and revocation details. <br>
Mitigation: Review sharing targets before use and avoid storing confidential material unless the publisher documents a confirmation flow and revocation controls. <br>
