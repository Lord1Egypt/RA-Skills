## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to generate culturally framed BaZi and four-pillar fortune analysis from birth information or a prepared chart. It is intended for entertainment and cultural-reference guidance, not for medical, financial, legal, safety, relationship, or life-planning decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce advice touching sensitive life areas such as health, finance, legal issues, relationships, and major planning decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Require the agent to frame outputs as entertainment and cultural-reference analysis, include uncertainty, and direct users to qualified professionals for medical, financial, legal, safety, relationship, or life-planning decisions. <br>
Risk: Birth date, birth time, birthplace, sex, and relationship context can be personal or identifying information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the minimum information needed for the requested detail level, avoid collecting third-party birth data without consent, and prefer no-network operation when users share sensitive birth information. <br>
Risk: Fortune-telling outputs may sound deterministic or frightening if not constrained. <br>
Mitigation: Enforce the skill's safety boundaries against fear-based claims, curse-removal claims, guaranteed outcomes, coercive manipulation, or replacing professional judgment. <br>
