## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release is labeled as a DSPy prompt optimizer, but the security summary says its main instructions also define finance, market-data, and backtesting workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if those finance/ZVT workflows are intended; review references/seed.yaml before use and require explicit confirmation before installs, data fetching, backtests, or file creation. <br>
Risk: The release may involve sensitive provider, paid data-source, or broker credentials in finance workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide broker or paid-provider credentials unless the requested workflow and credential use are clear and approved. <br>
