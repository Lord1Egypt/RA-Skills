## Use Case: <br>
Agents and developers use this skill for routine delivery experience work when they need a concise confirmation style for delivery confirmation, export handoff, or workspace notification requests. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill could be used where a delivery workflow needs factual delivery status checks rather than wording guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only to choose confirmation wording, and rely on the surrounding workflow or system of record for delivery status. <br>
Risk: Unexpected requests could be interpreted beyond the skill's narrow confirmation-style purpose. <br>
Mitigation: Keep usage scoped to delivery confirmation, export handoff, or workspace notification requests and verify that no credentials, private files, command execution, or network access are needed. <br>
