## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common legal services questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Legal operations teams, attorneys, and compliance teams use this skill to draft customer-facing FAQ content for legal services workflows. Outputs should be reviewed by qualified legal or compliance staff before client use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft FAQ answers may be inaccurate, jurisdiction-specific, or unsuitable as client-facing legal guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified attorney or compliance reviewer verify accuracy, jurisdiction-specific claims, and client-facing language before publication. <br>
Risk: Prompts or searches could expose sensitive client information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid putting sensitive client information into prompts or searches unless approved by the organization. <br>
