## Description: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trading backtest methodology can be mistaken for financial advice or live trading assurance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the content as educational methodology and independently validate assumptions, data quality, and execution costs before live deployment. <br>
Risk: Backtest results can be misleading due to overfitting, look-ahead bias, survivorship bias, small samples, or unrealistic execution assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Use out-of-sample validation, parameter sensitivity analysis, survivorship-bias-free data, sufficient sample sizes, and pessimistic slippage and commission modeling. <br>
