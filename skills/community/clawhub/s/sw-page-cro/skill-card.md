## Description: <br>
Conducts detailed audits of landing pages to identify conversion blockers and recommends prioritized fixes based on CRO principles and benchmarks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[amdf01-debug](https://clawhub.ai/user/amdf01-debug) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, product teams, and agents use this skill to audit landing or product pages for conversion blockers and produce prioritized fixes, A/B test ideas, and expected impact notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may fetch and analyze private or internal URLs supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Use public or approved pages when possible, and avoid sharing confidential URLs or page content with agents that do not have authorization to process them. <br>
Risk: Conversion recommendations may be inaccurate for a specific audience, market, or funnel. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as review guidance and validate high-impact changes through human review, analytics, and A/B testing. <br>
