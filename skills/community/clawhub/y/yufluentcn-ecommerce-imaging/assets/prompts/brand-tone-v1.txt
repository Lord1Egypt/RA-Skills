# 品牌调性 Prompt 模板 v1
# 两种用法：
#   1. 作为 scene 整帧风格：--scene luxury_minimal
#   2. 作为叠加修饰：--scene white_bg --brand-style eco_natural

## luxury_minimal（极简奢华）
A {product} in ultra-minimal luxury brand style, generous negative space, muted neutral palette, soft diffused studio light, matte surfaces, understated elegance, high-end editorial product photography

## youthful_pop（年轻活力）
A {product} in youthful vibrant brand style, bold saturated colors, playful geometric props, energetic dynamic composition, Gen-Z social commerce aesthetic, bright even lighting, fun and approachable

## eco_natural（环保自然）
A {product} in eco-friendly natural brand style, organic linen and wood props, earth-tone palette, soft daylight, sustainable lifestyle aesthetic, calm authentic atmosphere, green commerce photography

## pro_tech（专业科技）
A {product} in professional tech brand style, precision lighting, dark graphite gradient background, clean lines, subtle reflection on surface, innovative and reliable aesthetic, premium gadget photography

## 叠加修饰（非独立 scene 时自动追加）
luxury_minimal → luxury minimal aesthetic, muted tones, editorial negative space
youthful_pop   → vibrant youthful energy, bold colors, playful social-commerce vibe
eco_natural    → eco natural aesthetic, earth tones, sustainable organic mood
pro_tech       → professional tech aesthetic, precision lighting, innovative reliable mood
