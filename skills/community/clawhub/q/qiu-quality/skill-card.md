## Description: <br>
Assists agents in analyzing whether a company is high quality using a 邱国鹭-style checklist covering ROE, margins, industry position, and pricing power. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to structure company-quality analysis for investment research. It helps evaluate ROE, gross margin, net margin, industry rank, and pricing power while keeping the output as educational analysis rather than complete investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may be mistaken for complete investment advice or a buy/sell recommendation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational analysis, verify financial data independently, and apply separate valuation and investment review before making decisions. <br>
Risk: A simplified quality checklist can miss industry differences, leverage effects, one-time gains, or competitive dynamics. <br>
Mitigation: Review ROE drivers, compare companies within the same industry, check long-term averages, and assess industry concentration and barriers before relying on the score. <br>
