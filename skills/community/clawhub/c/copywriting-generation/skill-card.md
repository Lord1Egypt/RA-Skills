## Description: <br>
Generates copywriting for advertising, marketing, brand, product, and social media use cases using built-in style guidance, templates, and output-format rules. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to plan, draft, format, and save copy for advertisements, marketing campaigns, brand messaging, product descriptions, and social media posts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated copy or recommendations may include inaccurate, misleading, or non-compliant marketing claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual, legal, brand, and advertising claims before publication or customer use. <br>
Risk: The skill may save generated content to local files. <br>
Mitigation: Keep output in a known workspace and confirm filenames, paths, and formats before writing files. <br>
Risk: Generated HTML may contain content or formatting that should not be opened or shared without review. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect generated HTML before opening it in a browser, publishing it, or sending it externally. <br>
