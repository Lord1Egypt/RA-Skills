## Description: <br>
Provides a quantitative strategy testing workflow with regression testing steps, test case templates, and test report templates for testing and release phases. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and quantitative strategy teams use this skill to plan unit, integration, backtest, and small-capital live validation before releasing a trading strategy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Testing guidance may be applied to live-trading or real-money decisions without sufficient independent review. <br>
Mitigation: Review any strategy, backtest, and live-validation decision with qualified project stakeholders before acting on it. <br>
Risk: Generic trigger terms such as testing, launch, or release may invoke the skill in unrelated contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the conversation is about quantitative strategy testing before using the checklist or report template. <br>
