This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to prepare Amazon KDP publishing assets, including Markdown-to-EPUB conversion, low-content interior PDFs, cover prompts, KDP metadata, and batch production plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The artifact includes guidance that can be read as cropping away AI image watermarks. <br>
Mitigation: Use only licensed, watermark-free cover assets and review image rights and KDP policy compliance before upload. <br>
Risk: The activation guidance is broad and may trigger the skill for loosely related publishing requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants KDP asset generation before proposing scripts, files, or publishing workflows. <br>
Risk: Generated activity-book and puzzle outputs may not match their stated answers or promises. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated activity books as drafts and manually review every puzzle, answer page, and print-ready PDF before publication. <br>
Risk: Generated metadata, categories, pricing, and production suggestions may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a specific KDP listing. <br>
Mitigation: Review metadata and pricing against current KDP requirements and the publisher's own rights, market, and quality checks before upload. <br>
