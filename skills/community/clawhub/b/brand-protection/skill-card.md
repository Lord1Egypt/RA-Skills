## Description: <br>
Guides discovery, reporting, and prevention of brand impersonation, fake websites, phishing sites, trademark infringement, and domain squatting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Brand, legal, support, and security teams use this skill to collect evidence, prioritize reports, file abuse or takedown complaints, and add official-site verification measures when a fake site or brand impersonation incident appears. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Evidence packages and abuse reports can expose sensitive customer, payment, or internal brand details. <br>
Mitigation: Review project-context files and redact private customer, payment, and internal data before submitting reports or sharing evidence. <br>
Risk: Incorrect URLs or weak evidence can lead to inaccurate abuse, takedown, or legal complaints. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the official domain, compare the suspected site against official assets, and confirm URLs before filing registrar, hosting, platform, or legal reports. <br>
