## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Loan, tax, insurance, registration, and negotiation guidance may become outdated or differ by locality and contract. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as estimates and verify current local rules, lender terms, dealer contracts, insurance quotes, and vehicle registration requirements before making financial commitments. <br>
Risk: The local calculator uses simplified assumptions for fees, taxes, insurance, fuel or electricity costs, and financing. <br>
Mitigation: Recalculate with actual quotes and official fee schedules before relying on results for purchase decisions. <br>
