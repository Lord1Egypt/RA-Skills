# Customer Success Lifecycle

## 1. First Contact And Onboarding

Create an account profile, map contacts, confirm communication preferences, record approved routing, and set the initial cadence.

## 2. Delivery Relationship Management

Track meetings, open commitments, relationship risks, support issues, success criteria, and stakeholder changes while delivery is active.

## 3. Launch And Adoption

After launch, check adoption, support needs, user feedback, monitoring expectations, and whether the delivered workflow is meeting verified success criteria.

## 4. Retention And Renewal

Review relationship health, support history, outcomes, open risks, renewal timing, approvers, contract dependencies, and unresolved blockers.

## 5. Expansion And Advocacy

Identify expansion only when there is verified need, fit, stakeholder interest, or delivered value. Ask for testimonials or referrals only with approval and supporting evidence.

## 6. At-Risk Recovery Or Offboarding

When trust, fit, delivery, support, billing, or stakeholder alignment breaks down, create a recovery plan or transition checklist with clear ownership and communication review.
