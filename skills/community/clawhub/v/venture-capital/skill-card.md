## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and investment teams use this skill to assess startup pitches, run due diligence, reason about valuation, and review venture financing terms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Venture-capital, valuation, and term-sheet responses may be mistaken for legal, tax, financial, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general information and route investment, legal, tax, and financing decisions through qualified human review. <br>
Risk: Deal memos, investment notes, and portfolio analysis may contain confidential business information. <br>
Mitigation: Share confidential deal materials only in an agent environment the user trusts. <br>
