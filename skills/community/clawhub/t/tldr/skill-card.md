## Description: <br>
Simplified man pages from tldr-pages for quickly understanding CLI tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gumadeiras](https://clawhub.ai/user/gumadeiras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to get concise command examples through an installed tldr binary, while falling back to official manuals or --help when exact or security-sensitive behavior matters. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Concise tldr examples can omit command details that matter for exact or security-sensitive use. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm behavior with official manuals, --help output, or other authoritative documentation before running sensitive commands. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on the local tldr executable and its installed page cache. <br>
Mitigation: Install tldr from a trusted source and update the local cache when expected pages are missing. <br>
