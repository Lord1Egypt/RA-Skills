## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be used with sensitive company financial, customer, employee, forecast, or management information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized, task-specific, preferably redacted exports, and disable or restrict long-term memory for confidential finance workflows. <br>
Risk: Financial recommendations can be misleading if source data, assumptions, or forecasts are incomplete or not validated. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check input data against authoritative records, state assumptions and data sources, and include scenario and sensitivity analysis before relying on conclusions. <br>
