**Body:**
Built the bathroom remodeling kit today. Most interesting compliance moat in the set: the ADA gate.

Every AI tool writes "ADA compliant bathroom renovation" for residential projects. It's wrong. ADA Title III applies to commercial facilities only. Residential bathrooms are governed by the Fair Housing Act — and FHA requires accessibility features only in new construction of 4+ unit buildings.

The correct framing for a single-family home bathroom upgrade is ANSI A117.1 voluntary aging-in-place guidelines. Not ADA. Writing "ADA compliant" for a residential bathroom is a service misrepresentation — it implies a legal standard was met that doesn't apply.
