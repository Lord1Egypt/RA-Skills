## Description: <br>
Xiaozhi Interest Explorer is a Chinese-language coaching skill that helps students explore interests over a 52-week framework using interest-DNA reflection, challenge-response tracking, and critical validation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[qizhitang](https://clawhub.ai/user/qizhitang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External students use this skill to identify, test, and document possible interests through weekly exploration prompts and staged review. Guardians may use its summaries to support exploration when reflection data sharing is clear and consented. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Student reflection data and interest summaries may be sensitive, especially when shared with guardians. <br>
Mitigation: Clarify what is recorded and shared before use, and share summaries only with the student's or guardian's consent. <br>
Risk: Early interest judgments can be misleading when based on too few exploration sessions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat early conclusions as preliminary trends and revisit them as more weekly challenge-response data is collected. <br>
Risk: Cross-skill writes or reminders could affect a student's learning records or notification flow. <br>
Mitigation: Use reminders and cross-skill updates only after explicit consent. <br>
