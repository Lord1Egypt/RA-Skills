## Description: <br>
Streamline approval for non-standard deals by enforcing discount limits, validating deal value, and managing custom terms to protect margin and speed closing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Revenue leaders, sales teams, and finance teams use this skill to review non-standard B2B deals, apply discount guardrails, check margin impact, route approvals, and prepare deal terms before close. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Deal approval or pricing guidance could affect revenue or exceed a user's authority limits. <br>
Mitigation: Keep final pricing, discounting, legal, and approval decisions subject to the organization's normal human review and authority controls. <br>
Risk: The skill uses general deal thresholds and business formulas that may not match every organization's policies or economics. <br>
Mitigation: Validate thresholds, formulas, and approval routes against current internal policy before relying on the recommendations. <br>
