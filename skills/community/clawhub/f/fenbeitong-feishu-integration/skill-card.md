## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language support knowledge base that helps agents answer questions about Fenbeitong and Feishu integration capabilities, version choices, supported features, unsupported cases, installation paths, delivery notes, and escalation handling. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Customer-facing support, sales, and implementation staff use this skill to answer Fenbeitong-Feishu integration questions, compare market and embedded versions, explain capability boundaries, and escalate uncovered customer requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer support conversations may include unnecessary sensitive customer or employee data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid recording unnecessary sensitive details in notes or unanswered-question logs; keep escalation summaries limited to the facts needed for product or delivery confirmation. <br>
Risk: Escalation guidance can trigger Feishu notifications to internal contacts. <br>
Mitigation: Have staff approve customer-related notifications before sending them to internal contacts and route only to the named responsible team when needed. <br>
Risk: Capability boundaries may differ between market and embedded versions or contain inconsistencies across reference files. <br>
Mitigation: Verify version-specific capability claims before using the skill for deployment decisions or customer commitments. <br>
