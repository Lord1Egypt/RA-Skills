## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Novice-facing home repair guidance can be hazardous when work involves electricity, garbage disposals, gas, structural elements, hidden utilities, damaged wiring, local code, rental obligations, or uncertain conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as informal DIY guidance, require safety checks such as shutting off water or breakers, and direct users to qualified professionals or landlords for hazardous, code-regulated, or uncertain repairs. <br>
Risk: General repair steps may not match the user's exact fixture, appliance model, wiring, plumbing, local code, or property condition. <br>
Mitigation: Encourage users to verify model-specific instructions, take photos before disassembly, cross-check trusted repair sources, and stop when the observed condition differs from the expected simple repair. <br>
