## Description: <br>
Check and remove hidden metadata from photos, PDFs, and Word documents before sharing files. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[warren455](https://clawhub.ai/user/warren455) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to decide when shared files may need metadata review and to guide users to supported RemoveMetadata cleanup workflows before sharing photos, PDFs, or Word documents. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may assume metadata cleanup guarantees anonymity or removes all possible traces. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's careful wording: describe review and removal of supported hidden metadata, and remind users to verify cleaned files before sharing. <br>
Risk: The skill provides workflow guidance and external links rather than processing files locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid claiming local file access, code execution, or document processing by the agent; make the handoff to RemoveMetadata explicit. <br>
