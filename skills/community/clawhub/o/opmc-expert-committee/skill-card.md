## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language expert-panel decision-support skill that maps strategy, business, technology, brand, design, and risk questions to eleven named expert-style analytical frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External and internal users can use this skill for Chinese-language decision support when they need structured perspectives on strategy, business models, competition, AI and product architecture, marketing, brand communication, design, or legal and ethical risk. The skill selects one or more expert-style frameworks and returns synthesized analysis, recommendations, and risk notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outputs may be mistaken for legal, financial, compliance, or other professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as advisory decision support and require qualified human review before using them for legal, financial, compliance, or high-stakes business decisions. <br>
Risk: Included case-study reference material may contain concrete business-scenario details that are not appropriate for every deployment context. <br>
Mitigation: Review the reference files before deployment and remove or adapt scenario-specific material that does not fit the intended audience or use case. <br>
