## Description: <br>
Academic Paper Assistant helps users plan, draft, revise, polish, and format academic papers and references across multiple disciplines. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dingwenbin1978](https://clawhub.ai/user/dingwenbin1978) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and academic writers use this skill to plan paper structure, normalize academic language, organize references, and receive revision guidance while keeping research claims and data under user review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Citation details, venue formatting, or style requirements may be inaccurate or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify citations and formatting against original sources, target journal guidance, and institutional requirements before submission. <br>
Risk: Generated writing or revision suggestions may conflict with academic integrity, originality, or authorship expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as writing support, review all output, and follow the user's institution or publisher rules for originality and AI assistance. <br>
Risk: The skill does not provide experimental data, research results, or a formal plagiarism report. <br>
Mitigation: Supply and validate all research evidence independently, and use dedicated plagiarism or originality tools when formal checks are required. <br>
