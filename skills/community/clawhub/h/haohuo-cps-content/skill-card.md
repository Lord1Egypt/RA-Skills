## Description: <br>
电商 CPS 社群文案生成助手，为推广者生成通用推广文案和社群剧本。支持根据商品信息自动生成适合微信群、QQ群等社群场景的带货文案，提升转化率 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce CPS promoters and community operators use this skill to draft short affiliate promotion copy or staged community conversation scripts from product details such as name, price, selling points, and purchase link. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate staged group-chat promotions with planted participants and fake social proof. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for transparent promotional drafting; do not fabricate customer reactions, purchases, testimonials, stock pressure, or undisclosed endorsements. <br>
Risk: Generated promotional copy can contain inaccurate product claims, prices, availability, or affiliate disclosures if the provided inputs are wrong or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify product claims, prices, availability, purchase links, and affiliate or sponsored disclosures before publishing. <br>
