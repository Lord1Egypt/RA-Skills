## Description: <br>
Creates presentation deliverables from a user-provided topic through a staged workflow for requirement confirmation, content search, outline design, slide drafting, presentation generation, visual refinement, and delivery packaging. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[earthwalking](https://clawhub.ai/user/earthwalking) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and presentation authors use this skill to produce academic reports, thesis defenses, conference presentations, seminar talks, and course materials from a topic and optional audience, timing, and content requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow may use web search, multiple model providers, and local generated files, which can expose confidential, private, or regulated input if used with sensitive material. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive inputs unless the data flows and saved outputs are acceptable, and review local generated files before sharing. <br>
Risk: Generated presentation facts, citations, and visual summaries may be inaccurate or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated facts, data, citations, and slides before presenting or distributing the deck. <br>
