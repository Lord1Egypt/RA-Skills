## Use Case: <br>
External creators, freelancers, and teams use this skill to plan a video editing monetization workflow, validate a minimum viable offering, choose channels, standardize delivery, and track commercial metrics. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The monetization guidance may become inaccurate as platform policies, advertising rules, or marketplace terms change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current platform policies and local legal requirements before applying the recommendations. <br>
Risk: Video editing work can create copyright, licensing, contract, invoicing, or delivery disputes. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm source-media permissions, document client deliverables in contracts, and maintain clear invoicing and acceptance records. <br>
