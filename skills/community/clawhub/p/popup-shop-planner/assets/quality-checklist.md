# Pop-Up Shop Planning Quality Checklist

## Concept & Objectives (6 items)
- [ ] Primary objective clearly stated (awareness / revenue / acquisition / market test)
- [ ] Secondary objectives identified with measurable targets
- [ ] One-sentence concept statement crafted
- [ ] Target visitor profile defined with demographics and psychographics
- [ ] Duration recommendation aligned with objectives (not arbitrary)
- [ ] Pop-up concept differentiates from regular retail experience

## Venue Selection (8 items)
- [ ] Minimum 3 venue options evaluated using weighted scorecard
- [ ] Foot traffic data verified (not just estimated)
- [ ] Demographic match confirmed with census or observational data
- [ ] Accessibility assessed (transit, parking, ADA compliance)
- [ ] Lease terms reviewed including setup/teardown access windows
- [ ] Build-out allowances and restrictions documented
- [ ] Infrastructure confirmed (WiFi, electrical, HVAC, restroom)
- [ ] Total venue cost calculated including deposits, utilities, insurance

## Budget Planning (7 items)
- [ ] Total budget defined with line-item breakdown
- [ ] All major categories covered (venue, build-out, marketing, staffing, inventory)
- [ ] Contingency budget allocated (minimum 7-10% of total)
- [ ] Break-even analysis completed
- [ ] ROI projections calculated (direct + LTV + attributed)
- [ ] Payment schedule aligned with cash flow
- [ ] Permits, insurance, and hidden costs accounted for

## Space Design (6 items)
- [ ] Floor plan created with distinct zones (threshold, discovery, engagement, conversion)
- [ ] Product display hierarchy planned (hero products at eye level)
- [ ] Traffic flow designed to guide visitors through the narrative
- [ ] Instagram/social-worthy photo moment designed and placed
- [ ] Checkout area optimized for speed, upsells, and data capture
- [ ] Fixture list finalized with delivery/setup timeline

## Staffing (6 items)
- [ ] Staffing needs calculated based on space size and expected traffic
- [ ] All roles defined with clear responsibilities
- [ ] Staff sourced with appropriate experience and brand alignment
- [ ] Training curriculum created covering brand, product, POS, and engagement
- [ ] Daily schedule built with peak/off-peak staffing levels
- [ ] Backup plan for staff no-shows or illness

## Marketing Plan (8 items)
- [ ] Pre-launch campaign planned (4+ weeks before opening)
- [ ] Local PR outreach list compiled and pitched
- [ ] Social media content calendar created for full duration
- [ ] Influencer partnerships confirmed with visit dates
- [ ] Geo-targeted ad campaigns set up and budgeted
- [ ] Google My Business temporary location listing created
- [ ] During-event daily content plan documented
- [ ] Post-event follow-up sequence designed (email, ads, social)

## Inventory & Operations (5 items)
- [ ] Product assortment curated with hero, exclusive, and impulse items
- [ ] Inventory quantities planned at 2.5-3x expected sales
- [ ] POS system selected, tested, and loaded with products
- [ ] Payment methods confirmed (card, tap, mobile pay, cash with float)
- [ ] Unsold inventory plan documented (return, sell online, donate)

## Data Capture & Measurement (5 items)
- [ ] Email/SMS capture integrated into POS and standalone station
- [ ] Foot traffic counting method established
- [ ] Daily sales and metrics tracking template ready
- [ ] Post-event analysis template prepared with all KPIs
- [ ] 30-day post-event online sales monitoring planned

## Legal & Compliance (4 items)
- [ ] Business license / temporary use permit obtained
- [ ] Sales tax registration completed for location
- [ ] General liability insurance secured (certificate to landlord)
- [ ] Health/safety requirements met (fire exits, capacity limits, ADA)
