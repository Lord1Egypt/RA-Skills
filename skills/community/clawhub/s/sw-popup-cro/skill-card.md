## Description: <br>
Design optimized popups with clear CTAs triggered by exit intent, scroll, time, or click to boost lead capture and conversions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[amdf01-debug](https://clawhub.ai/user/amdf01-debug) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, ecommerce operators, and conversion-focused developers use this skill to draft popup specifications with trigger rules, copy, design guidance, targeting, and frequency caps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Popup recommendations may affect privacy, consent, accessibility, or user experience when collecting emails or offering discounts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated popup specifications against internal privacy, consent, accessibility, and brand requirements before deployment. <br>
