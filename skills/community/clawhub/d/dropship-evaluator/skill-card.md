## Description: <br>
Evaluate dropshipping product opportunities and supplier reliability by scoring margin potential, shipping times, supplier communication quality, and sample quality to reject dud products before sinking ad spend on them. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and ecommerce operators use this skill to screen dropshipping product ideas, compare suppliers, model margins, order and review samples, and document launch decisions before committing advertising spend. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may guide supplier messages, product research, and sample purchases that involve external communications or spending money. <br>
Mitigation: Keep purchases user-approved, avoid sharing account credentials with suppliers, and document supplier communications before relying on a decision. <br>
Risk: Marketplace, ad-library, or supplier research workflows may be subject to platform terms, especially if performed at high volume. <br>
Mitigation: Review applicable marketplace and ad-tool terms before using automated or high-volume research workflows. <br>
Risk: Supplier claims, shipping estimates, margins, and product quality can be incomplete or change after the evaluation. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm costs and shipping terms in writing, order samples before launch, keep a backup supplier, and rerun the margin model when supplier terms change. <br>
