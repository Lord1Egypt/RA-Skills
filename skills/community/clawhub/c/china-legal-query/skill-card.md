## Description: <br>
China Legal Query helps agents search Chinese laws, regulations, judicial interpretations, and related case references across areas such as criminal, civil, labor, contract, and intellectual property law. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobewin](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobewin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and legal researchers use this skill to query Chinese statutes, regulations, judicial interpretations, and case references, then receive informational summaries for follow-up verification against official sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal answers may be incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific matter. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as informational research assistance, verify legal text against official Chinese sources, and consult a qualified lawyer for important or case-specific decisions. <br>
Risk: AI interpretation may vary across models or overstate confidence in legal analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Review the cited or retrieved legal text directly and separate statutory text from AI-generated explanation before relying on the result. <br>
