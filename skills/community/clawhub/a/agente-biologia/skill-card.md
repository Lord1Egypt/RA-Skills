## Description: <br>
Agente Biologia is a Portuguese-language biology tutoring agent for the FUVEST first-phase exam, covering cell biology, histology, human physiology, genetics, evolution, ecology, botany, and zoology with an evolutionary framing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners preparing for FUVEST use this skill for biology tutoring, topic routing, and study explanations across core exam subjects, especially genetics and ecology. The source content is personalized for Antonio, so teams should adapt user-specific references before broader deployment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is personalized around Antonio and may activate on broad biology mentions. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt user-specific wording and activation triggers before deploying it for other learners or alongside many study skills. <br>
Risk: The skill provides educational biology explanations for exam preparation without bundled evaluation artifacts. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer compare explanations and study coverage against the intended FUVEST curriculum before relying on it for high-stakes preparation. <br>
