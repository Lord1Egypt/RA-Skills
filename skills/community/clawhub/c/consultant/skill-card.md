## Description: <br>
Diagnose business problems, scope engagements, and deliver decision-ready recommendations with measurable outcomes and executable plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and business operators use this skill to frame ambiguous business problems, scope consulting engagements, structure workstreams, and produce decision-ready memos, roadmaps, and implementation plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store consulting context and engagement notes locally under ~/consultant/. <br>
Mitigation: Review or delete the local consulting workspace when needed and avoid storing secrets, credentials, or unnecessary confidential details in memory files. <br>
Risk: Consulting recommendations can be wrong or misleading when based on incomplete evidence, unclear decision rights, or unstated assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included discovery flow and quality gates to document assumptions, validate evidence, identify owners, and label unresolved risk before acting on recommendations. <br>
