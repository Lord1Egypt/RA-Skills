## Description: <br>
Automatically parses submitted literature text into a summary, keywords, and key conclusions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lyt0302](https://clawhub.ai/user/lyt0302) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and agents working with Chinese-language academic text use this skill to turn submitted literature into a concise summary, 3-6 keywords, and 1-3 key conclusions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate broadly and apply Chinese literature-analysis formatting to unrelated prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use it in workflows intended for Chinese-language literature or text analysis, and disable it when another language or output format is required. <br>
