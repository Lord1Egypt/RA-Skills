**Samsung Refrigerators:**
- Ice maker freezing over (Twin Cooling Plus models) — ice fan + housing replacement
- Water dispenser not working — inlet valve or control board
- Temperature fluctuation — evaporator fan motor or defrost heater

**Whirlpool/Maytag:**
- Not cooling: start relay failure on compressor (common, affordable fix)
- Water inlet valve failure — ice maker + dispenser
- Door seal replacement — all models
