## Description: <br>
A guided assistant that helps software development teams plan and deliver SDLC work across Agile and Waterfall roles, phases, and artifacts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[pattzaks](https://clawhub.ai/user/pattzaks) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Software development teams use this skill for role-specific SDLC guidance across developers, QA, product owners, project managers, and business analysts. It helps with phase planning, sprint and release activities, requirements work, test planning, bug triage, retrospectives, and practical SDLC artifacts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill has broad SDLC scope and may activate for general software-development questions where a more specialized skill would be better suited. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer a specific coding, debugging, architecture, security review, or document-generation skill when the user request is clearly about one of those tasks. <br>
Risk: Generated SDLC guidance or artifacts may be incomplete or mismatched to a team's actual process, controls, or release criteria. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated plans, templates, and checklists against project-specific requirements, organizational standards, and stakeholder sign-off rules before use. <br>
