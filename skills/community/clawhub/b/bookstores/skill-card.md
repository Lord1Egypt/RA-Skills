## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to find trendy bookstore recommendations, plan visit routes, and prepare for venue-specific details such as reservations, entry rules, transportation, nearby food, and accommodations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ticketing, exhibition, refund, entry, photography, and minimum-spend details may be venue-specific or change before a visit. <br>
Mitigation: Verify these details with the venue or booking channel before traveling, reserving, or spending money. <br>
Risk: The artifact mixes bookstore planning with event-ticketing language, which may confuse the skill's intended scope. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as bookstore visit planning guidance and confirm whether any referenced events or exhibitions are actually part of the venue. <br>
