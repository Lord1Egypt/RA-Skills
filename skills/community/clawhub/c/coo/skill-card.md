## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: COO-style recommendations can affect restructures, hiring, vendor commitments, crisis response, and culture-impacting process changes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as decision support and require accountable human leaders to review and approve consequential business actions. <br>
Risk: Operational frameworks, metrics, and templates may be misapplied if company stage, team size, bottlenecks, dependencies, or baselines are not considered. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt guidance to the organization's current stage, measurement baseline, and cross-functional context before implementation. <br>
