## Description: <br>
Organizes and summarizes public Dongchedi vehicle, buying guide, event, and service-entry information. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mikeclaw007](https://clawhub.ai/user/mikeclaw007) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent builders use this skill to summarize public Dongchedi vehicle pages, buying guides, events, service flows, dealer links, and help content without handling private account or purchase data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may surface region- or time-sensitive vehicle prices, offers, or dealer-service information that can become inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Keep official Dongchedi links with summaries, avoid absolute pricing claims, and note when price or offer details depend on location or date. <br>
Risk: Using the skill beyond public pages could expose private account, purchase lead, dealer-only, or other non-public data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only public Dongchedi pages and do not request, process, or summarize private account details, lead forms, dealer-only data, or user purchase information. <br>
