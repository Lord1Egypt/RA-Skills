## Description: <br>
Check loans and search resources from the National Library Board of Singapore. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kk17](https://clawhub.ai/user/kk17) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Library patrons and assistants use this skill to sign in to an NLB account, check current or overdue loans, view recommendations, and search the NLB catalogue with common availability, collection, location, material type, and language filters. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide an agent through NLB account pages that expose loan, overdue, and recommendation information while the user is logged in. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for intended NLB account assistance, enter credentials only on the official NLB sign-in page, and avoid asking the agent to store or repeat passwords. <br>
