## Description: <br>
Professional China export data analysis and market intelligence for international traders, with actionable insights on export trends, emerging hot products, market growth, trade opportunities, visualization recommendations, and business context. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
International traders, sourcing managers, and market researchers use this skill to turn China export data into structured market intelligence, including trend analysis, hot product discovery, sourcing recommendations, risk factors, and visualization notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Export trends, tariffs, regulations, and trade restrictions can change quickly or lag official reporting. <br>
Mitigation: Cite data sources and dates, disclose the expected 1-2 month data lag, and verify current regulations before making trade decisions. <br>
Risk: Market intelligence may be mistaken for legal, tariff, or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present findings as decision-support analysis and include due diligence reminders for legal, tariff, investment, and supplier decisions. <br>
