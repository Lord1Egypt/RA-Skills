## Description: <br>
Guides air purifier buyers through room size, pollutant type, CADR, filter, and region-specific questions to identify suitable purifier specifications for their space and health situation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arbazex](https://clawhub.ai/user/arbazex) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users shopping for an air purifier use this skill to gather room, pollutant, household, region, and infrastructure details, then receive a structured specification recommendation and limited product starting points. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Product suggestions, prices, certifications, filter availability, and warranties can change after the skill is published. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current product specifications, local availability, filter supply, certifications, pricing, and warranty before buying. <br>
Risk: Incomplete room, health, pollutant, or regional details can lead to unsuitable CADR, filter, noise, voltage, or certification recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Ask targeted follow-up questions and revise the recommendation when the user's room measurements or situation details change. <br>
