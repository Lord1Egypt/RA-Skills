## Description: <br>
Build VKE evidence-led decision workflows and business knowledge products from a request. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wodeking](https://clawhub.ai/user/wodeking) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and business teams use this skill to turn ambiguous VKE knowledge requests into traceable decision packages with problem briefs, research plans, evidence ledgers, analysis notes, red-team reviews, reports, watchlists, and retrospectives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may create or update local case artifacts when the user asks for file changes. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated case folder, state whether external research is allowed, and review proposed artifact changes before relying on them. <br>
