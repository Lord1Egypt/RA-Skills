## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can provide incomplete or incorrect security guidance if treated as a substitute for project-specific review. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as an advisory checklist and verify recommendations against the actual code, security policies, and current dependency state. <br>
Risk: Quality badges, optimization claims, and bundled placeholder tests may overstate validation coverage. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on those claims as proof of audit coverage; run independent tests, scans, and human review before deployment. <br>
