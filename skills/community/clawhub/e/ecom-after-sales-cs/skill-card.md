## Description: <br>
Generates brand-compliant e-commerce after-sales customer service replies for return or exchange requests, logistics exceptions, and compensation negotiations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer service teams use this prompt-only skill to draft standardized after-sales replies for e-commerce customers. It supports return and exchange requests, delayed or damaged shipment inquiries, and negotiation of approved compensation options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill drafts customer-facing policy and compensation language that could exceed brand authorization if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated replies against current brand policies and approve any refund, replacement, or compensation terms before sending. <br>
Risk: Customer messages and order context may contain sensitive customer or order information. <br>
Mitigation: Limit inputs to the minimum necessary case details and follow the organization's data handling rules for customer service workflows. <br>
