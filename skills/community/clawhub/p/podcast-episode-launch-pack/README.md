# Podcast Episode Launch Pack

You recorded the episode. This skill handles everything else.

## What it does

Paste a transcript, episode outline, or topic and get a complete launch package:

- **2 episode title variants** — curiosity-framed (browse traffic) and clarity-framed (search traffic), both under 50 chars for podcast app display
- **Full show notes** — keyword-hooked summary, guest intro, specific key takeaways, timestamped chapters, resources section, and extended guest bio
- **Subscriber email blast** — subject line, preheader, and ~120-word body ready to send
- **Instagram audiogram caption** — quote-led hook, context, CTA, and 5 hashtags
- **Pull quotes** *(optional)* — 2–3 verbatim or drafted quotes formatted for cards and carousels
- **LinkedIn post draft** *(optional)* — standalone and publish-ready; run it through LinkedIn Post Optimizer for a structured polish, or use it as-is
- **3-tweet / X promo thread** *(optional)*

Works from transcript (most detailed output), episode outline (structural placeholders for timestamps), or topic only (full package with shells to fill in post-recording). Handles solo, interview, and narrative episode formats differently.
