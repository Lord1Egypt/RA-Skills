### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Downstream AgentPMT skills may involve bearer tokens, MCP or REST access, x402 payments, or provider-specific image generation usage. <br>
Mitigation: Review each downstream skill separately before installing it or providing credentials, payment authorization, or provider configuration. <br>
Risk: The static index can become stale as AgentPMT skill listings change. <br>
Mitigation: Check the AgentPMT ClawHub publisher page before relying on the index when freshness matters. <br>
