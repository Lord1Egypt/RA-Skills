## Description: <br>
Optimizes Chinese image descriptions into ready-to-use English Midjourney prompts by applying replacement rules for sensitive terms and adding a standard style suffix. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tianheihei002](https://clawhub.ai/user/tianheihei002) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and agents use this skill to turn Chinese image concepts into English Midjourney prompts with style enhancements and predefined wording substitutions before image generation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may silently alter wording to avoid Midjourney sensitivity filters, reducing transparency and potentially conflicting with platform policy expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts before use, and do not rely on this skill for transparent or policy-compliant content moderation guidance. <br>
Risk: Rule-based substitutions may change the user's intended content, tone, or visual details. <br>
Mitigation: Compare the final prompt with the original request before submitting it for image generation. <br>
