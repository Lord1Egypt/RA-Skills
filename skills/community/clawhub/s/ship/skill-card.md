## Description: <br>
Ship helps ecommerce sellers, importers, exporters, and other users compare carriers, estimate costs, handle customs, manage fulfillment, track shipments, file claims, and optimize logistics decisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[agenticio](https://clawhub.ai/user/agenticio) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and business operators use this skill to reason through parcel, international, freight, fulfillment, returns, and claims workflows for physical goods. It is best suited for logistics planning and decision support, with carrier rates, customs duties, restrictions, and claim deadlines verified against official sources before action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Shipping workflows can involve customer addresses, tracking numbers, invoices, customs values, and business data. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum details needed for the task and avoid unnecessary sensitive shipment or customer information. <br>
Risk: Carrier rates, customs duties, import restrictions, and claim deadlines can change or vary by carrier, destination, and shipment details. <br>
Mitigation: Verify rates, duties, restrictions, and deadlines with official carrier or government sources before acting. <br>
