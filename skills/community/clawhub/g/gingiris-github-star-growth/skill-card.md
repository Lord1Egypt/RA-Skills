## Description: <br>
Gingiris Github Star Growth helps agents produce sustained GitHub star growth plans with monthly content cadence, community operations, event-driven growth, contributor systems, and ambassador program guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, maintainers, and developer-marketing teams use this skill to plan sustained post-launch GitHub star growth through content calendars, community operations, events, contributor workflows, and ambassador programs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Outreach, DM automation, paid promotion, Hacker News submissions, and KOL engagement can violate platform rules, consent expectations, anti-spam norms, or privacy requirements if followed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review every campaign with a human before execution, confirm platform rules and disclosure requirements, respect opt-outs, and avoid collecting or using personal data without a clear lawful basis. <br>
Risk: The playbook includes growth benchmarks and case-study outcomes that may not generalize to every project. <br>
Mitigation: Treat benchmarks as planning inputs, validate tactics against the project's own audience and metrics, and avoid presenting expected stars or conversion rates as guaranteed results. <br>
