Risk: The skill can produce forceful stock-trading guidance and market-tactics material without built-in safety limits. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for historical or educational persona simulation, and enforce refusals for personalized trading advice, real-money buy or sell decisions, market coordination, or manipulation-oriented requests. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake persona-style answers for reliable financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present responses with clear educational-only framing and require independent professional review before any financial decision. <br>
