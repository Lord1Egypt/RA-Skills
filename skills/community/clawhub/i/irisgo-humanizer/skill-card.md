## Description: <br>
Remove AI writing patterns based on Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" research. 24 pattern detection and rewriting rules for making AI-generated text sound natural. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lmanchu](https://clawhub.ai/user/lmanchu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, writers, and editors use this skill to revise AI-generated or AI-sounding prose so it reads more naturally while preserving the intended meaning and tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can read and edit files explicitly provided by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the agent to show the rewritten output or a diff before applying changes to important documents. <br>
Risk: Humanizing prose can unintentionally change factual claims, neutrality, or point of view. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims, tone, and point of view before using the rewritten result. <br>
