## Description: <br>
Humanizer Zh helps agents edit Chinese text to reduce common AI-writing patterns and make prose read more naturally. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zachary2024](https://clawhub.ai/user/zachary2024) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, editors, and writers use this skill to review Chinese prose, identify AI-like writing patterns, and rewrite the text while preserving its core meaning and intended tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting may remove or soften required citations, provenance statements, AI-use disclosures, or compliance language. <br>
Mitigation: Before accepting edits to legal, academic, compliance, or disclosure-sensitive text, confirm that required citations, provenance statements, and disclosures remain intact. <br>
Risk: The skill edits style and tone, so it may change emphasis or introduce unsupported specificity if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the rewritten text against the source material and keep factual claims grounded in the original document. <br>
