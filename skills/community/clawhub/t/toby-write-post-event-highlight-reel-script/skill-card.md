## Description: <br>
Turns event notes into a short recap script for social and recap videos. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobeyrebecca](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobeyrebecca) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Event marketers, field teams, and sponsorship managers use this skill to turn event notes into concise highlight-reel narration or recap copy for social and post-event videos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated recap copy could contain inaccurate, misleading, or overclaimed event details. <br>
Mitigation: Review the script against event notes and stakeholder requirements before publishing or recording. <br>
Risk: Event notes may include sensitive attendee, sponsor, or business information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive details unless sharing them with the underlying chat or text-to-speech services is acceptable. <br>
