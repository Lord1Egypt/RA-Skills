## Description: <br>
Multi-platform social sentiment engine for crypto, stocks & macro trading signals. Tracks X/Reddit/Telegram/Discord, whale wallet movements, and momentum indicators. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to review social sentiment, whale movement, and market momentum signals for crypto, equities, forex, and macro assets. Outputs should be treated as informational trading analysis rather than financial advice or instructions to transact. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users to send payment credentials over a plain HTTP endpoint. <br>
Mitigation: Do not send real payment credentials over the listed HTTP endpoint; use only trusted, encrypted payment flows. <br>
Risk: The skill can produce concrete trading signals that may affect real-money decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational analysis only and independently verify any market, portfolio, or transaction decision. <br>
