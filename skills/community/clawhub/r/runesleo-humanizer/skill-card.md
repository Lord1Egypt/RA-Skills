## Description: <br>
Humanizer helps agents rewrite text to sound more natural by identifying and fixing common AI-writing patterns while preserving meaning and voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[runesleo](https://clawhub.ai/user/runesleo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and agents use this skill to review AI-assisted prose, remove common AI-writing tells, and produce a more natural draft while preserving the intended meaning and tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting can shift tone, nuance, or meaning, especially in sensitive or non-English text. <br>
Mitigation: Review the final text before relying on it, with extra care for academic, legal, medical, compliance, or professional writing. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misused to avoid required disclosure of AI-assisted writing. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only where rewriting is permitted and follow any applicable disclosure or authorship rules. <br>
