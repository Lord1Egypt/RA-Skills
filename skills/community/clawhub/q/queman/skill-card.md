## Description: <br>
雀漫将长篇小说或剧情片段转换为面向 Seedance 2.0 的 15 秒动态漫画分镜脚本，包含分集建议、画风与画幅确认、旁白音色、6-8 个静态分镜、镜头运动、配音和音效设计。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators and agent users use this skill to turn supplied Chinese fiction chapters or plot summaries into short dynamic-comic storyboard scripts optimized for Seedance 2.0. It is intended for dialogue-based creative drafting, style and format confirmation, and final script self-checking before video generation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided story text may contain private or copyrighted material. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide text that is appropriate to share in the agent conversation and confirm usage rights before generating or publishing derived storyboard scripts. <br>
Risk: Generated storyboard timing or motion instructions may drift from the skill's dynamic-comic constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the included pre-output checklist and review each shot for static image descriptions, limited micro-motion, audio timing, and 15-second episode structure. <br>
