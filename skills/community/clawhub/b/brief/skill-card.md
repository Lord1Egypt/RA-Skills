## Description: <br>
Condense information into actionable briefings. User specifies sources, skill structures the output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use Brief to turn user-provided or explicitly authorized source material into concise executive, project, meeting, handoff, or decision briefings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may create ~/brief/templates and save briefing format preferences locally. <br>
Mitigation: Review or remove ~/brief/preferences.md and ~/brief/templates if saved formatting preferences are no longer wanted. <br>
Risk: Brief quality depends on the user-provided or explicitly authorized sources. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm source access and review generated briefs before relying on them for decisions. <br>
