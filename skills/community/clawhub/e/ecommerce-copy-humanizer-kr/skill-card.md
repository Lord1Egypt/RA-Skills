## Description: <br>
한국 이커머스 플랫폼 맞춤형, AI 티 없는 자연스러운 문체로 전환 효율 높은 판매용 카피를 재작성합니다. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce marketers, sellers, and content teams use this skill to diagnose AI-like Korean sales copy and rewrite it into natural, platform-appropriate ecommerce copy. It supports product detail pages, ads, banners, TikTok Shop live copy, social ads, email, and notification copy while preserving selling points and reducing high-risk marketing language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ecommerce copy may contain unsupported marketing claims, absolute rankings, medical-effect wording, misleading discount language, or customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy against source product facts and local advertising requirements before publication, and replace high-risk claims with qualified, evidence-backed wording. <br>
