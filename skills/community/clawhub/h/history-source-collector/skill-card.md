## Description: <br>
调用内部秘塔学术检索工具，帮助历史学用户收集正史、档案、方志、古籍和学术文献，并按时间顺序整理带出处的 Markdown 史料摘录。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
History educators, students, and researchers use this skill to gather authoritative Chinese historical sources and academic references for teaching, coursework, and research notes. It organizes primary and secondary sources with traceable citations and GB/T 7714-style references. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on an internal academic search tool that may not be available to all users. <br>
Mitigation: Install and use it only where approved access to that internal tool is available. <br>
Risk: Historical quotations, source metadata, or GB/T 7714 citations may be incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify original sources, quotations, editions, and citations before using outputs in teaching, coursework, or publication. <br>
Risk: Prompts or workflow notes could accidentally expose private credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste private API keys or secrets into prompts; use only approved credential handling for the internal search tool. <br>
