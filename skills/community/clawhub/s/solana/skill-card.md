## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Solana transaction, account-closing, fee, or wallet approval guidance can affect real assets if followed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Independently review wallet prompts, transaction simulations, fee settings, and account-closing actions before approving any asset-moving transaction. <br>
Risk: Users may expose seed phrases or private keys while troubleshooting wallet or transaction issues. <br>
Mitigation: Never share seed phrases or private keys, and use burner wallets for unknown sites, mints, or airdrops. <br>
