## Description: <br>
Dating App Coach helps users improve dating-app profiles, openers, follow-ups, date invitations, and rejection recovery for Tinder, Bumble, Hinge, and similar apps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for concise coaching on dating-app profile presentation, message writing, conversation flow, and moving matches toward respectful real-life dates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may be tempted to share phone numbers, social handles, locations, screenshots of private conversations, or meet someone quickly based on coaching advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill's coaching as optional advice and keep personal safety decisions separate from message optimization. <br>
Risk: Dating-app coaching can be misused to pressure uninterested matches or misrepresent identity. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the skill's stated safety constraints: do not coach deception, respect disinterest, prioritize safety concerns, and avoid body-shaming photo feedback. <br>
