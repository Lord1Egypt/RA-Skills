## Description: <br>
Draft and manage vendor outreach emails for quotes/availability, follow-up cadence, and response summarization into structured decision tables. Use when contacting hotels, transport vendors, or activity providers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[austineyapp](https://clawhub.ai/user/austineyapp) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travel planners and operations staff use this skill to draft concise vendor RFQ emails, maintain follow-up cadence, and summarize vendor replies into comparison tables for hotels, transport vendors, and activity providers. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Vendor emails may be sent to incorrect recipients or include inaccurate travel, pricing, availability, or timing details. <br>
Mitigation: Review the recipient list, final wording, travel and pricing details, and follow-up timing before any email leaves the user's account. <br>
Risk: Outreach tracking may retain vendor response details beyond the active sourcing task. <br>
Mitigation: Keep pending-reply tracking and comparison tables limited to the current outreach task. <br>
