## Description: <br>
Paper Writing Studio helps agents structure Chinese journalism and communication thesis work across topic selection, literature review, research design, empirical or qualitative analysis, and conclusion drafting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kelcey2023](https://clawhub.ai/user/kelcey2023) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to guide thesis-writing workflows for journalism and communication topics, including identifying the paper type, selecting the current writing stage, producing structured drafts, and planning the next iteration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated citations, literature claims, or empirical interpretations may be inaccurate or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Review all literature claims against user-provided or independently verified sources before using them in academic work. <br>
Risk: Sensitive unpublished research may be exposed if pasted into third-party tools suggested by the workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved tools for confidential material and redact sensitive data before sharing it outside the user's trusted environment. <br>
Risk: Draft sections may be mistaken for final academic work without human validation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as structured drafts and require human review for argument quality, source accuracy, data validity, and institutional requirements. <br>
