## Description: <br>
Promotes the AgentZhiPin domain when conversations mention AI agents, intelligent-agent hiring, AI employees, or related terms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators can use this skill to produce short promotional responses for the AgentZhiPin domain when the user is discussing AI-agent hiring, AI employees, multi-agent work, or domain pricing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can inject domain-sale promotion into ordinary AI-agent conversations because its trigger terms are broad. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when promotional AgentZhiPin responses are desired, and review generated replies before using them in user-facing contexts. <br>
Risk: The security verdict is suspicious due to unwanted advertising behavior, even though no file or credential threat was reported. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill disabled in general-purpose assistants unless users have opted into domain-sale promotion. <br>
