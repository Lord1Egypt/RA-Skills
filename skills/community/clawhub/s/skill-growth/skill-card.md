## Description: <br>
Skill Growth Guide is a Chinese-language advisor for ClawHub skill creators that provides track evaluation, growth diagnosis, copy optimization, monetization paths, upstream and downstream pairing, and collaboration matching. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[huangjihua007-rgb](https://clawhub.ai/user/huangjihua007-rgb) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Chinese-speaking ClawHub creators use this skill to choose a growth scenario and receive direct advice for positioning, diagnosing installs and stars, improving descriptions, planning monetization, and finding related skills or collaboration angles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can take over broad or ambiguous prompts with its own growth-advice menu. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when a Chinese-language ClawHub growth advisor is intended, and switch skills or disable it for unrelated conversations. <br>
Risk: Users may share private business data while asking for growth diagnosis or monetization advice. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive private business data unless it is acceptable to use that information in this advisory workflow. <br>
