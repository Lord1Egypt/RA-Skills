## Use Case: <br>
Insurance brokers, MGAs, carriers, and insurtech teams use this skill to guide AI agents through underwriting, claims triage, policy administration, renewals, compliance checks, broker operations, and insurance operations planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Insurance operations can involve sensitive customer data and regulated underwriting, claims, binding, cancellation, payment, credit, telematics, and policyholder workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval, audit logs, jurisdiction-specific compliance review, and least-privilege access before connecting the skill to real insurance systems. <br>
Risk: Automated recommendations for underwriting, claims, fraud, or compliance may be incorrect or incomplete for a specific jurisdiction or policy context. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as decision support and require qualified insurance, legal, or compliance review before taking customer-facing or legally significant action. <br>
