Startup founders, growth marketers, and SaaS operators use this skill to choose marketing tools, prioritize acquisition channels, and track SaaS growth metrics by stage and budget. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing tool recommendations may lead to spending or SaaS integrations that do not fit a specific business context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the playbook as general business guidance, compare recommendations against current CAC, LTV, budget, and data needs, and perform vendor due diligence before purchases or integrations. <br>
