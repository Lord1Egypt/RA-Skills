## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Crypto payment, wallet, token launch, and market-facing guidance can be legally or financially sensitive if treated as execution approval. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for planning only unless a human explicitly approves the action after legal, compliance, and operational review. <br>
Risk: Outreach and Crypto Twitter tactics could be used to send automated DMs, posts, or undisclosed paid promotions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep agents in drafting mode for external communications; require human review and disclose paid promotions where applicable. <br>
Risk: Wallet, social, OAuth, or payment credentials could enable unauthorized purchases, posts, or transactions if exposed to an autonomous agent. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide sensitive credentials or signing authority to the agent; use read-only planning access unless supervised execution is explicitly authorized. <br>
