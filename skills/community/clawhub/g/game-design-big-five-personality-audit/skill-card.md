## Description: <br>
Audit a game, feature, progression system, social system, live-ops loop, onboarding flow, monetization surface, or multiplayer space through the lens of the Big Five personality traits (OCEAN): Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[stanestane](https://clawhub.ai/user/stanestane) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Game designers, product teams, and agent users use this skill to audit games, features, progression systems, social systems, live-ops loops, onboarding flows, monetization surfaces, and multiplayer spaces through an OCEAN personality-fit lens. It helps identify which trait expressions a design rewards, strains, or may accidentally exclude. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: OCEAN framing can be misused as a diagnosis or label for players. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a design-fit lens, not as a psychometric or clinical assessment of people. <br>
Risk: Player examples or research inputs could include identifiable or sensitive data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including identifiable or sensitive player data unless there is a clear purpose, consent, and appropriate handling. <br>
