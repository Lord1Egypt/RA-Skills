## Description: <br>
Categorise UK business expenses and invoices against HMRC Self Assessment categories, generate quarterly profit and loss summaries, and produce VAT-ready and MTD-compatible breakdowns from transaction data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users such as UK sole traders, freelancers, self-employed contractors, small business owners, and accountants use this skill to categorise expenses, separate capital items, calculate mileage or home office allowances, and prepare Self Assessment, VAT, or MTD-ready summaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United Kingdom <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat bookkeeping categorisation as professional tax advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as bookkeeping guidance and verify complex or borderline tax treatment with an accountant before filing. <br>
Risk: Transaction inputs may contain personal identifiers, full account numbers, card details, or customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting unnecessary sensitive data; provide only the transaction details needed for categorisation. <br>
