## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can provide product guidance based on advisory interpretations rather than authoritative current research. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current product data, user research, and business constraints before using them for roadmap decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact references recent WeChat and AI product developments without live sources. <br>
Mitigation: Check current primary sources before relying on time-sensitive claims about WeChat, AI assistants, or platform strategy. <br>
