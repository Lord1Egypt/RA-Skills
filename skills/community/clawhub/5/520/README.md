# Number 520 Skill

> A comprehensive prompt-based skill about the number **520** — the digital expression of **"我爱你" (I Love You)** in Chinese culture, and much more.

## Overview

This skill provides rich, structured knowledge about the number 520, covering its extraordinary cultural significance as the Chinese internet code for "I Love You" (我爱你), along with its mathematical properties, scientific connections, historical events on May 20th, geography, pop culture, the broader Chinese numeric slang system, and philosophical reflections on love and language. It is designed as a **natural-language, prompt-based skill** that activates whenever a user mentions or implies topics related to the number 520.

## Activation

The skill activates when the user:

- Directly asks about the number 520 or its meaning
- Mentions "520" in the context of love, romance, or Chinese culture
- Discusses May 20th (5/20) as a romantic holiday in China
- Asks about Chinese number slang or numeric homophones
- Inquires about 520nm wavelength (green light)
- Mentions related codes like 5201314, 521, 1314
- Talks about WeChat red envelopes with romantic amounts
- Discusses the SR 520 Bridge, Area Code 520, or 520 Park Avenue
