## Description: <br>
Design product packaging and unboxing experiences that reinforce brand identity, encourage social sharing, reduce damage rates, and include insert cards that drive reviews and repeat purchases. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External brand operators, ecommerce teams, and packaging designers use this skill to plan product packaging, vendor briefs, dunnage, insert-card copy, unboxing sequences, and pre-production validation before placing production orders. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Packaging briefs and test plans may include sensitive brand files, die-lines, vendor terms, QR destinations, product details, or live-ship addresses. <br>
Mitigation: Review and redact sensitive details before sharing outputs with vendors or testers, and use approved destinations for QR codes and landing pages. <br>
Risk: Insert-card incentives or review requests can conflict with marketplace review rules. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the applicable marketplace policies before printing cards; request honest feedback and avoid rating-conditioned language. <br>
Risk: Packaging recommendations may not satisfy product-specific legal, logistics, sustainability, or safety requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Validate country-of-origin labels, customs data, hazmat status, recyclability or FSC claims, and transit-test results with the relevant vendor, legal, and logistics reviewers before production. <br>
