## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market claims, factory names, contacts, certifications, and supplier details may be incomplete, outdated, or unverified. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify the information with primary sources, regulators, certification bodies, and suppliers before using it for sourcing or business decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes individual factory contact entries even though the skill documentation describes the data as cluster-level only. <br>
Mitigation: Treat individual contacts as unverified leads and review them for accuracy, consent, and compliance before outreach or operational use. <br>
