## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The bundle includes an undisclosed Cloudflare image-generation helper with embedded credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Remove or fully disclose the helper and rotate the embedded token before trusting or publishing the package. <br>
Risk: The Cloudflare helper invokes curl through shell execution. <br>
Mitigation: Replace shell execution with a structured API client or safe subprocess call that avoids shell interpolation. <br>
Risk: The collage script depends on local image assets that may be absent. <br>
Mitigation: Verify required image assets are packaged or accept that missing assets will be rendered as gray placeholders. <br>
