## Description: <br>
Plans and executes a product launch for a solopreneur business, covering pre-launch strategy, anticipation building, launch day execution, post-launch momentum, and launch success measurement. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External solopreneurs, founders, and small business operators use this skill to plan and run launches for new products, services, features, or major updates. It provides timelines, channel plans, content structures, launch-day checklists, follow-up steps, and metrics for reviewing launch performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Launch guidance may become customer-facing email, social, or community content if used with tools that can send or publish messages. <br>
Mitigation: Approve the exact copy, audience, platform, and timing before anything is sent, scheduled, or published. <br>
Risk: Launch plans and promotional copy can overstate benefits, urgency, availability, or social proof if adapted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify offers, claims, dates, testimonials, and product readiness against the actual launch before using the guidance publicly. <br>
