## Description: <br>
SCI论文三轮滚动精修助手 applies a three-pass academic editing method for SCI papers, core journal manuscripts, and academic reports: reducing rhetorical density, adding evidence labels, and surfacing caveats. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[freak30](https://clawhub.ai/user/freak30) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External researchers and academic authors use this skill to refine SCI or core journal manuscripts while preserving meaning, adding evidence-level labels, and documenting study limitations. It is intended for clarity, structure, and academic writing quality, not for bypassing disclosure requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill could be used to hide AI involvement in scholarly submissions or bypass journal, school, or employer disclosure requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for legitimate clarity, structure, and language editing, and require users to follow all applicable AI-assistance disclosure rules. <br>
Risk: Manuscript edits and evidence labels could introduce or preserve unsupported academic claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review all revised claims, evidence labels, caveats, citations, and reported statistics against the manuscript and source literature before submission. <br>
