## Description: <br>
Provides Traditional Chinese Medicine consultation support for symptom intake, pattern differentiation, reference formula suggestions, and dietary wellness guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for educational TCM-oriented symptom collection, body constitution assessment, pattern differentiation, reference formula suggestions, and diet or wellness guidance. Outputs should be treated as educational reference material and not as medical diagnosis, prescription, or treatment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides health-related diagnosis, formula, and dietary suggestions that users may mistake for professional medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat all outputs as educational only, keep safety disclaimers visible, and direct users to licensed medical care for symptoms, medication decisions, pregnancy, children, chronic illness, worsening symptoms, or emergencies. <br>
Risk: The artifact describes persistent health-consultation memory without clear consent or deletion controls. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive health details unless persistent memory is acceptable, and review deployment memory behavior before use in settings involving personal health information. <br>
