This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to analyze supported currency pairs, review market factors, and generate short-, medium-, and long-term exchange-rate forecast reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Currency forecasts and trading recommendations may be inaccurate or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational analysis and review them before making financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Prompts used for API calls or web search may expose private financial details or account information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including account identifiers, private portfolio details, or other sensitive financial data in prompts. <br>
Risk: The skill may propose commands or API calls while gathering or analyzing exchange-rate data. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed commands before execution. <br>
