## Description: <br>
Pro Color Palette generates professional three-color palette cards, extracts dominant colors from images, and creates poster, phone UI, and cover mockups using local scripts or optional AI image generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ryantaozi](https://clawhub.ai/user/ryantaozi) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, developers, and content creators use this skill to choose a preset or image-derived color palette, generate PNG palette cards, and preview the palette in common design mockups. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-supplied image URLs can point to private, internal, or otherwise sensitive resources. <br>
Mitigation: Use local image files or trusted public image URLs, and avoid localhost, internal network, private, or confidential image sources. <br>
Risk: Optional AI image generation can disclose prompt or image context to the selected provider. <br>
Mitigation: Use the optional AI image mode only with approved, non-confidential prompts and image context. <br>
