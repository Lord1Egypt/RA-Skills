## Description: <br>
Autonomous webinar strategy and content builder for coaches, course creators, community builders, and service providers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[adrianboysel](https://clawhub.ai/user/adrianboysel) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External coaches, course creators, community builders, service providers, and marketing teams use this skill to plan and draft complete webinar campaigns, including positioning, scripts, slide outlines, offers, email sequences, traffic plans, and launch checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated webinar copy may use aggressive sales framing, shame-based language, or false scarcity. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated emails, offer language, scarcity claims, and calls to action for brand safety and factual accuracy before use. <br>
Risk: Real campaign execution may require compliance review for email, SMS, tracking pixels, retargeting, privacy notices, consent, and opt-outs. <br>
Mitigation: Run legal and compliance checks before publishing or sending campaign assets. <br>
Risk: The skill can suggest funnel, traffic, and purchase-related tactics that affect spend and customer-facing commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human operator approve budgets, payment flows, promotion terms, and live campaign changes before execution. <br>
