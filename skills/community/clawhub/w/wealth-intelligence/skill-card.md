## Use Case: <br>
External users and finance-focused agents use this skill to generate educational personal-finance analysis, including portfolio allocation, retirement readiness, debt payoff comparisons, home buying scenarios, FIRE projections, and tax-planning considerations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake generated finance, tax, or legal planning output for professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as educational information and recommend consultation with a qualified fiduciary, tax professional, attorney, or other licensed professional before major decisions. <br>
Risk: Personal financial details can be sensitive even though the skill has no executable code, credential use, persistence, or hidden data handling. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the minimum information needed, avoid unnecessary account-level details, and do not store or transmit personal financial data. <br>
Risk: Tax brackets, contribution limits, investment data, mortgage rates, and local rules can change or vary by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Use current authoritative sources where available, localize assumptions to the user's jurisdiction, and label assumptions clearly in generated scenarios. <br>
