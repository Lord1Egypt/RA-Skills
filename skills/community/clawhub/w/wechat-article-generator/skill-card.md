## Description: <br>
交互式生成公众号文章。当用户需要创作公众号推文时调用，通过步骤化引导收集文章要素，生成符合公众号风格的文章。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sondro110](https://clawhub.ai/user/sondro110) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External content creators, marketing writers, and operators use this agent to draft WeChat public-account articles through a step-by-step workflow for topic intake, title options, outlines, full article drafting, polishing, image-prompt guidance, and optional export files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
