## Description: <br>
Choose the right ecommerce platform for any overseas market in minutes by comparing marketplaces, DTC, social commerce, and wholesale channels by margin, category fit, operational readiness, and policy risk. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce founders, marketplace operators, DTC brand teams, and cross-border expansion managers use this skill to compare overseas sales channels and produce decision matrices, readiness checklists, pilot roadmaps, and validation tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Platform fees, policies, eligibility rules, and market facts may change after the skill produces guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current platform documentation, local laws, and operational requirements before acting on recommendations. <br>
Risk: Channel recommendations can be misleading when user inputs are incomplete or assumptions are untested. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions explicitly, validate them with local evidence, and use limited pilots before scaling inventory or marketing spend. <br>
Risk: The skill may touch legal, tax, financial, compliance, or market-entry questions without being professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as planning guidance and consult qualified local professionals for regulated or high-impact decisions. <br>
