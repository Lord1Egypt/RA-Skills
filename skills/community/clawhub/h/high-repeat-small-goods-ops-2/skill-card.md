## Description: <br>
E-commerce operations workflow for high-repeat small goods stores that turns store operations, repeat purchase, campaigns, content conversion, customer service, and performance metrics into executable playbooks and table templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce operators and growth teams use this skill to diagnose high-repeat small goods stores and produce weekly execution plans, campaign briefs, customer-service SOPs, repeat-purchase rhythms, and review tables. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The broad activation wording may cause the skill to respond in many store-operations conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when structured e-commerce operations planning is intended, and narrow the request when only a small copywriting or creative task is needed. <br>
Risk: Store operations prompts may include sensitive business information such as margins, supplier terms, customer records, or campaign budgets. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the data needed for planning, anonymize private customer or supplier details, and avoid including unnecessary confidential metrics. <br>
Risk: Generated campaign, product-page, coupon, or customer-service guidance could lead to misleading claims or unclear promotion rules if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations for false efficacy, exaggerated material or ingredient claims, asset rights, coupon terms, after-sales rules, and platform compliance before publishing. <br>
