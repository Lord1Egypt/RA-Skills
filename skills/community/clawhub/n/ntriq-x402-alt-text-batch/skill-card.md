## Description: <br>
Batch-generate WCAG-compliant alt text for up to 500 images in one call. Flat $3.00 USDC via x402. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ntriq-gh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ntriq-gh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and content operations teams use this skill to generate accessible alt text and longer image descriptions for batches of image URLs, such as product catalog imagery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can require a paid $3.00 USDC x402 batch request. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm payment authorization, network, and cost before sending an x402 payment header. <br>
Risk: Image URLs and optional context are sent to x402.ntriq.co.kr for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Use only image URLs and context approved for third-party processing; avoid private, signed, internal, personal, or confidential URLs unless sharing is approved. <br>
