## Description: <br>
Provides eldercare planning guidance for chronic medication reminders, ADL/IADL/MMSE health assessment, care facility comparison, long-term care insurance guidance, and silver economy travel, education, and re-employment resource matching. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Family caregivers, eldercare facility operators, and silver economy practitioners use this skill to assess care needs, compare care models and providers, plan medication reminders, prepare long-term care insurance materials, and match elder-focused life planning resources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global; content is oriented toward China, Japan, and Korea eldercare policy contexts. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on health assessment, medication, or diagnosis-related guidance as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat health outputs as informational support only and confirm medication, diagnosis, and care decisions with qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Care facility, insurance, or policy guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or location-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify facility details, application steps, coverage terms, and local policy requirements with official sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Eldercare planning can involve sensitive personal and health information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum necessary details and avoid entering unnecessary personal, medical, or insurance information. <br>
