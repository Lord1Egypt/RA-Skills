# AI Recipe Creator

Culinary creativity engine generating recipes from available ingredients, dietary constraints, and cuisine preferences. Covers global cuisines with technique guidance.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Recipe Generation
- Ingredient-based: input what you have, get complete recipes
- Cuisine exploration: Italian, French, Japanese, Indian, Mexican, Thai, etc.
- Meal type: breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, desserts, beverages
- Dietary: vegetarian, vegan, keto, paleo, gluten-free, dairy-free

### 2. Cooking Techniques
- Dry heat: roasting, baking, grilling, broiling, sauteing, stir-frying
- Moist heat: boiling, steaming, poaching, braising, stewing
- Knife skills: dice, julienne, chiffonade, brunoise
- Sauce making: mother sauces, emulsions, reductions, pan sauces

### 3. Ingredient Science
- Substitution guide: dietary swaps, allergy alternatives
- Flavor pairing science: complementary and contrasting
- Seasonality charts by region
- Storage and shelf-life optimization

### 4. Baking & Pastry
- Bread: sourdough, yeast breads, quick breads, flatbreads
- Cakes, cookies, pastries, pies, tarts
- Understanding ratios: flour-fat-liquid-egg balance
- Troubleshooting: sinking, cracking, density issues

### 5. Meal Planning
- Weekly meal prep strategies, batch cooking guides
- Calorie and macro-balanced meal combinations
- Kid-friendly, picky eater solutions
- Budget-friendly meal planning, reducing food waste
