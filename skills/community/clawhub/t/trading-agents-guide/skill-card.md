## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users could mistake educational trading guidance or AI-generated strategy discussion for investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as learning material only, require independent financial review, and avoid presenting recommendations as guaranteed or personalized investment advice. <br>
Risk: Users could connect trading guidance directly to broker credentials or real-money execution without enough validation. <br>
Mitigation: Do not collect broker credentials; use simulation, backtesting, manual review, and jurisdiction-specific compliance checks before any live trading. <br>
