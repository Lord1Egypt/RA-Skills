# 品牌官方配方库

## Campari Group (Campari Academy)

Campari Academy 是 Campari Group 的官方培训平台，覆盖 Campari、Aperol、Grand Marnier、Cynar、Appleton Estate、Wild Turkey、Skyy Vodka 等品牌。网址：campariacademy.com

### Negroni 家族

**Classic Negroni**
- 1 oz Gin
- 1 oz Campari
- 1 oz Sweet Vermouth (Cinzano 1757 Vermouth di Torino Rosso)
- Stir, strain into rocks glass over ice. Orange peel garnish.

**Boulevardier**
- 1.5 oz Bourbon (Russell's Reserve)
- 0.75 oz Campari
- 0.75 oz Sweet Vermouth (1757 Vermouth di Torino Rosso)
- Stir, strain into chilled coupe. Orange peel garnish.
- 历史：禁酒令时期美国作家 Erskine Gwynne 的签名酒，以其杂志《The Boulevardier》命名

**Americano**
- 1.5 oz Campari
- 1.5 oz Sweet Vermouth
- Soda Water (top)
- Build in rocks glass over ice. Orange slice garnish.
- 1860 年代 Gaspare Campari 在 Caffé Campari 创制，是 Negroni 的前身

**Negroni Sbagliato**
- 1 oz Campari
- 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
- Prosecco (top)
- Build in rocks glass. Orange slice.

**Coffee Negroni**
- 1 oz Gin
- 1 oz Campari
- 1 oz Coffee-Infused Sweet Vermouth
- Stir, strain. 过滤咖啡带出底层温暖风味

### Campari 创新配方

**Don't Blink**
- 1.5 oz Wild Turkey Rye
- 0.75 oz Raspberry-Infused Campari
- 0.5 oz Sweet Vermouth
- 2 dashes Chocolate Bitters
- Stir, strain.

**Clarified Banana Daiquiri**
- 1.5 parts Appleton Estate 15 Year Rum
- 0.5 parts Banana Liqueur
- 0.5 parts Cynar 70
- 0.75 parts Lime Juice
- 0.5 parts Demerara Syrup (1:1)
- 2 dashes Aromatic Bitters
- 奶洗澄清 → 丝滑质感的进阶版 Banana Daiquiri

**Sweet Potato Rye (Vegan Milk Punch)**
- Rye Whiskey + Amaro (Cynar) 基底的奶洗 Punches
- 素食友好（用椰奶/燕麦奶代替牛奶）

**Buyers Club (Manhattan Riff)**
- Grand Marnier + Cynar + Whiskey
- Manhattan 爱好者的进阶选择

**Little Louis**
- Grand Marnier + Sherry 基底
- 复杂但低 ABV，适合节日

**Grand Swizzle**
- Appleton Estate Rum + Grand Marnier
- 简易版 Swizzle

**Fermented Tomato Martini**
- 发酵番茄的咸鲜风味马天尼

## Aperol

### Aperol Spritz (官方配方)
- 3 parts Prosecco D.O.C. (9cl / 90ml)
- 2 parts Aperol (6cl / 60ml)
- 1 splash Soda (3cl / 30ml)
- Build in wine glass over ice. Orange slice garnish.
- 3-2-1 黄金比例

### Aperol Sour
- Aperol + Gin + Lemon Juice + Simple Syrup + Egg White
- Dry shake → wet shake → strain

## Grand Marnier

### Grand Margarita
- 1.5 oz Tequila
- 0.75 oz Grand Marnier
- 0.75 oz Fresh Lime Juice
- Shake, strain into salt-rimmed rocks glass

### Cadillac Margarita
- Margarita 升级版：用 Grand Marnier 代替 Triple Sec
- 2 oz Tequila + 1 oz Grand Marnier + 1 oz Lime Juice

## Cynar

Cynar 是洋蓟（Artichoke）基底的意大利 Amaro，ABV 16.5% (普通版) / 35% (Cynar 70)。

### Cynar Spritz
- 2 oz Cynar
- 3 oz Prosecco
- 1 oz Soda
- Build in wine glass. Orange slice.

### Cynar Negroni
- 1 oz Gin
- 1 oz Cynar
- 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
- 用 Cynar 替换 Campari，苦味更柔和、有蔬菜底

### Bitter Giuseppe
- 2 oz Cynar
- 1 oz Sweet Vermouth (Carpano Antica)
- 0.25 oz Lemon Juice
- 6 dashes Orange Bitters
- Stir, strain into rocks glass. Lemon twist.

---

## Chartreuse

Chartreuse Diffusion 官方网站：chartreuse.fr

### Last Word
- 0.75 oz Gin
- 0.75 oz Green Chartreuse
- 0.75 oz Maraschino Liqueur
- 0.75 oz Fresh Lime Juice
- Shake, strain into coupe
- 底特律 Athletic Club 1920 年代发明，禁酒令时期的经典

### Bijou
- 1 oz Gin
- 1 oz Green Chartreuse
- 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
- 1 dash Orange Bitters
- Stir, strain. Lemon twist.
- "Bijou" 意为宝石：Gin = 钻石、Chartreuse = 祖母绿、Vermouth = 红宝石

### Greenpoint
- 2 oz Rye Whiskey
- 0.5 oz Yellow Chartreuse
- 0.5 oz Sweet Vermouth (Punt e Mes)
- 1 dash Angostura Bitters
- 1 dash Orange Bitters
- Stir, strain. Lemon twist.
- Brooklyn 风味的 Manhattan 变体

### Champs-Élysées
- 2 oz Cognac
- 0.75 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
- 0.5 oz Green Chartreuse
- 0.25 oz Simple Syrup
- 1 dash Angostura Bitters
- Shake, strain into coupe. Lemon twist.

### Alaska
- 2.25 oz London Dry Gin
- 0.75 oz Yellow Chartreuse
- 2 dashes Orange Bitters
- Stir, strain. Lemon twist.

### Yellow Jacket
- 2 oz Reposado Tequila
- 0.75 oz Yellow Chartreuse
- 0.5 oz Honey Syrup
- 0.75 oz Lemon Juice
- Shake, strain. Lemon peel.

## Giffard (法国利口酒)

### 常用产品
- Crème de Mûre (黑莓利口酒) → Bramble
- Crème de Pêche (桃子利口酒) → Bellini
- Crème de Violette → Aviation
- Crème de Pamplemousse Rose (粉红葡萄柚) → Paloma 变体
- Menthe-Pastille (薄荷利口酒) → Stinger/White Spider

### White Lady
- 40ml Gin
- 30ml Giffard Triple Sec
- 20ml Lemon Juice
- Shake, strain into coupe. — 1920 年代 Harry's New York Bar (Paris) 发明

## Tanqueray

### Tanqueray & Tonic
- 50ml Tanqueray London Dry Gin
- 150ml Premium Indian Tonic Water
- Build in Highball over ice. Lime wedge.
- 汤力水选 Fever-Tree 或 Schweppes，不要用廉价汤力水

### Tanqueray No. Ten Martini
- 60ml Tanqueray No. Ten
- 10ml Dry Vermouth
- Stir, strain into frozen Martini glass. Grapefruit twist.
- No. Ten 使用新鲜柑橘蒸馏，葡萄柚皮 Twist 比柠檬更适配

### Negroni (Tanqueray)
- 25ml Tanqueray No. Ten
- 25ml Campari
- 25ml Sweet Vermouth
- Stir, strain into rocks glass over ice. Orange slice.

## Bacardi

### Bacardi Daiquiri
- 50ml Bacardi Carta Blanca
- 25ml Fresh Lime Juice
- 15ml Simple Syrup
- Shake, double strain into coupe.

### Bacardi Mojito
- 50ml Bacardi Carta Blanca
- 25ml Fresh Lime Juice
- 15ml Simple Syrup
- 8-10 Mint Leaves
- Soda Water (top)
- Lightly muddle mint in Highball, add crushed ice, rum, lime, sugar. Churn. Top with soda.

### Bacardi Cuba Libre
- 50ml Bacardi Carta Oro
- 120ml Cola
- 10ml Fresh Lime Juice
- Build in Highball over ice. Lime wedge.

---

## St-Germain (接骨木花利口酒)

### St-Germain Spritz
- 40ml St-Germain
- 60ml Prosecco
- 60ml Soda Water
- Build in wine glass over ice. Lemon twist or mint.

### St-Germain Gin & Tonic
- 50ml Gin
- 20ml St-Germain
- Tonic Water (top)
- Build in Highball. Lemon wheel.
