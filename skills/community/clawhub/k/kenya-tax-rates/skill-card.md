## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill directs users to install an npm package, creating normal package supply-chain exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the npm package provenance and pin a specific package version before operational use. <br>
Risk: Payroll tax rates can change and incorrect rates may affect real payroll decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current Kenya payroll rates against official guidance before relying on calculations for production payroll. <br>
