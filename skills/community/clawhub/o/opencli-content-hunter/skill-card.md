## Description: <br>
Opencli Content Hunter helps agents use opencli to collect trending and keyword-related content across many content, news, social, finance, developer, academic, media, design, audio, and gaming platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and content analysts use this skill to guide platform selection, run opencli collection commands, and summarize cross-platform trends or search results. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users to install external browser tooling and reuse logged-in Chrome sessions across many platforms. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated Chrome profile or test accounts, choose the smallest necessary platform set, avoid sensitive personal, work, finance, education, or private-message accounts, and disable or remove the extension when finished. <br>
Risk: Collected platform content and trend summaries can include incomplete, misleading, or policy-sensitive material. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before reuse, verify important claims against primary sources, and avoid treating scraped platform content as authoritative. <br>
