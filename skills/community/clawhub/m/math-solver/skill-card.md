## Description: <br>
Math Solver helps agents solve math problems step by step, describe function graphs in ASCII, look up formulas, convert units, and generate practice problems. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xueyetianya](https://clawhub.ai/user/xueyetianya) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, educators, and agents use this skill to request worked math solutions, detailed tutoring steps, ASCII graph descriptions, formula references, unit conversions, and practice prompts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release includes an unrelated local data utility that can store user input on disk. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm which script the installed command runs before use, avoid entering private information, and review local persistence under the math-solver data directory. <br>
Risk: Generated math solutions, graph descriptions, formulas, conversions, or practice material may contain errors. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important answers independently before relying on them for grading, instruction, engineering, finance, or other consequential decisions. <br>
