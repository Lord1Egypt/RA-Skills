## Description: <br>
ChatGPT SEO Optimization Guide helps agents provide guidance for getting content cited by ChatGPT Search and Bing Copilot, covering citation behavior, GPTBot crawling, E-E-A-T signals, Bing integration, structured content, and optimization tactics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, SEO practitioners, and content teams use this skill to ask an agent for ChatGPT Search and Bing Copilot optimization guidance, including robots.txt, structured data, E-E-A-T, and indexing recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO guidance and example configuration changes may be incorrect, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific site. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggested robots.txt, schema, IndexNow, and Bing Webmaster changes before applying them, and verify SEO claims against current business requirements. <br>
Risk: An agent following the skill could request or handle private webmaster or analytics credentials during implementation. <br>
Mitigation: Do not share private webmaster credentials with an agent; apply changes through controlled accounts and least-privilege workflows. <br>
