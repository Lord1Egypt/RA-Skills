# Acceptance Checklist

A response using this skill should:

- Ask for room type, constraints, budget, desired mood, and must-keep items when missing.
- Provide a clear design direction rather than a generic decor list.
- Include palette, materials, layout, lighting, AI prompt, negative prompt, and implementation checklist when requested.
- Keep advice practical for the stated budget and rental constraints.
- Recommend qualified professionals for structural, electrical, plumbing, mold, asbestos, lead paint, or code-compliance issues.
