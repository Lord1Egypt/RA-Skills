## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language OPC industry analysis skill that guides 10-dimensional industry research with authoritative data-source selection, citation requirements, and structured report generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and business analysts use this skill to scope a target sector, research market size, competitive landscape, technology trends, customer profiles, unit economics, compliance risk, and recommendations for strategy, investment, or market entry. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow performs substantial external research and may include user-provided business context in the analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential business data unless it is appropriate for use in the analysis context. <br>
Risk: Industry analysis can produce incorrect or outdated conclusions if market data, regulations, or projections are not verified. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the skill's citation requirements, cross-check core market figures with multiple authoritative sources, and review assumptions before relying on the report. <br>
