## Description: <br>
Generates structured AI assistant daily, weekly, and monthly work reports from local memory or diary entries, including completed tasks, discussion themes, decisions, follow-ups, and plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liujw0214](https://clawhub.ai/user/liujw0214) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
OpenClaw users use this skill to turn session history, local memory, or diary notes into structured work summaries for status reporting, retrospectives, and follow-up planning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private memory or diary notes may be summarized into saved local reports. <br>
Mitigation: Enable the skill only when local report generation is intended, review generated reports before sharing them, and avoid storing secrets in memory files that may be summarized. <br>
