## Description: <br>
Terms Of Service generates bilingual Terms of Service and user agreement drafts for SaaS, ecommerce, and app services. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain3](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, personal users, and businesses use this skill to draft editable Terms of Service text for SaaS, ecommerce, and app services. Generated legal text should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated Terms of Service text may be incomplete, jurisdiction-specific, or unsuitable for publication without legal review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a boilerplate drafting aid and review generated terms with qualified legal counsel before publishing. <br>
Risk: Helper commands may store entered text in the local terms-of-service data directory. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets or sensitive business details unless that local storage is acceptable. <br>
