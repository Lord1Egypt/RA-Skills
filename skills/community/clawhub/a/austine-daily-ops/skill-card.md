## Description: <br>
Daily execution and trip-ops command system for Austine. Use when asked to run daily brief/checklist/status workflows, track priorities, or plan and procure trip logistics (transport, lodging, reservations, vendor quotes, recommendation + decision handoff). <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Austine uses this skill to run concise daily status, planning, and priority workflows, and to manage trip logistics from constraint gathering through option comparison and decision handoff. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trip logistics may involve vendor messages, booking, cancellation, payment, or personal travel details. <br>
Mitigation: Review every vendor message, booking, cancellation, and payment step before sending it, and avoid sharing full card numbers, account credentials, or unnecessary personal details. <br>
Risk: Travel recommendations may rely on uncertain provider availability, pricing, timing, or cancellation terms. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current details with the vendor or booking channel before acting, and keep a fallback option in the final recommendation. <br>
