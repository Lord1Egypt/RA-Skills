This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External Taobao sellers use this skill to fill product names and recommendation reasons for public material submissions during 618, Double 11, and similar campaign enrollment workflows. It is intended for already-authenticated Taobao seller-center sessions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, subject to Taobao seller-center availability and local marketplace rules. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can automatically submit live Taobao seller-center material fields without a documented review or confirmation step. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill in a fill-only or preview workflow when available, review generated product names and recommendation reasons, and approve explicitly before any final submit action. <br>
Risk: The skill operates in an authenticated seller-center session and may affect live business campaign submissions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in the intended Taobao seller-center page, with an account authorized for the specific campaign workflow, and monitor the browser during execution. <br>
