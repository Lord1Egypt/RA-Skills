## Description: <br>
将物理问题的运动过程制作成交互式 HTML Canvas 动画，适合力学、电磁学等需要展示物体运动轨迹的场景。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wxdwqy](https://clawhub.ai/user/wxdwqy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, educators, and developers use this skill to turn physics problem statements or diagrams into an interactive HTML Canvas animation workflow, with confirmation steps for vectorization, motion modeling, and final output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated physics animations may contain incorrect calculations or misleading motion. <br>
Mitigation: Independently check physics calculations and trajectories when accuracy matters. <br>
Risk: Generated HTML or source diagrams may expose content that should not be shared. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML before publishing or sharing, and avoid using private diagrams unless input sharing is acceptable. <br>
