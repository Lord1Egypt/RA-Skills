## Description: <br>
Ai Anthropology is a Chinese-language guide that helps users ask about cultural, biological, archaeological, linguistic, economic, political, religious, and applied anthropology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, students, researchers, and product teams use this skill to get introductory anthropology explanations, fieldwork guidance, theory summaries, and examples for cultural research or product design. <br>
