## Description: <br>
A public policy information skill for querying, organizing, and citing official Chinese social security, medical insurance, and housing fund policy information without making individual-case judgments or operational recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ToBeWin](https://clawhub.ai/user/ToBeWin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to look up and summarize public Chinese social security, medical insurance, and housing fund rules from official national and local sources, with dates, regions, citations, and risk notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Social security, medical insurance, and housing fund policies can vary by region and change by year. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important decisions against the latest national or local official agency guidance before relying on an answer. <br>
Risk: Users may treat public policy summaries as individual eligibility, benefit, or compliance determinations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses limited to public policy information, flag uncertainty, and direct individual cases to official service windows, agency hotlines, or qualified advisers. <br>
