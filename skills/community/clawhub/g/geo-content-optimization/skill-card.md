## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External content, marketing, and documentation teams use this skill to audit and rewrite articles, landing pages, docs, FAQs, comparisons, and Chinese-language content for clearer extraction, citation, and recommendation by AI answer engines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-supplied files may contain secrets or unrelated private content. <br>
Mitigation: Run the helper scripts only on articles, pages, or documents intended for content analysis. <br>
Risk: Editorial recommendations may be mistaken for guaranteed AI citations, rankings, or traffic gains. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as editorial advice and review claims before publishing. <br>
