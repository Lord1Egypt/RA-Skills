## Description: <br>
Labor Arbitration helps workers in China assess labor-dispute evidence, prepare arbitration materials, generate application documents, follow online submission steps, and plan mediation strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Workers and supporting agents use this skill to organize labor-arbitration claims in China, evaluate evidence gaps and timing risks, prepare filing materials, and generate Markdown arbitration documents for review before submission. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive identity, employment, wage, address, phone, and employer information. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for intended labor-arbitration help, keep generated files in a secure local folder, and avoid sharing unnecessary personal data. <br>
Risk: Submission links, regional rules, and legal requirements may change over time or differ by locality. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current filing channels and procedural requirements through official government sites, 12333, 12348, or qualified legal support before submitting. <br>
Risk: Generated documents and calculations may contain incorrect facts, assumptions, or amounts if case inputs are incomplete or inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated arbitration applications, evidence lists, and amount tables against source documents before use. <br>
Risk: Platform account credentials could be exposed if shared with an agent during online submission workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide platform passwords or one-time codes to the agent; complete account login and final submission steps directly. <br>
