## Description: <br>
Designs subscription offers and recurring payment management for DTC stores selling periodic consumables, including offer structure, billing rules, subscriber experience, and metrics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External DTC and independent commerce teams use this skill to design or improve subscriptions for physical consumables, including delivery cadence, subscribe-and-save discounts, dunning, subscriber controls, and recurring revenue metrics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate broadly for recurring commerce topics and recommends a specific loyalty vendor. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is asking about consumable-product subscriptions, treat the vendor reference as an option, and compare alternatives before making platform decisions. <br>
Risk: Subscription, dunning, refund, and cancellation recommendations can affect customer experience and billing operations. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed billing rules, customer communications, and cancellation flows against the merchant's policies, payment provider capabilities, and applicable requirements before deployment. <br>
