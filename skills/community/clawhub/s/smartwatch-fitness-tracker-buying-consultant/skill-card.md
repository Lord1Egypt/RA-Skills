## Description: <br>
Guide users buying a smartwatch or fitness tracker through platform, health sensors, battery life, durability, and activity questions to get the exact specs they need - brand-neutral. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arbazex](https://clawhub.ai/user/arbazex) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to evaluate smartwatch or fitness tracker requirements before purchase. It guides a structured consultation and produces prioritized specs and product research starting points based on platform, health, activity, battery, durability, region, and wearability needs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Smartwatch specifications, prices, product availability, and carrier support can change after the advice is generated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current model specifications, regional availability, LTE band and carrier compatibility, and pricing before purchase. <br>
Risk: Consumer health sensors such as ECG, SpO2, and heart rhythm alerts may be region-limited and are not medical diagnosis tools. <br>
Mitigation: Treat health sensor guidance as consumer buying advice only, confirm feature availability for the user's country, and seek medical guidance for health concerns. <br>
