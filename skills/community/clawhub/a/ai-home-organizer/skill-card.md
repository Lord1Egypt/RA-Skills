## Description: <br>
Ai Home Organizer provides Chinese-language home organization guidance for decluttering, whole-home storage planning, space expansion, storage product selection, and moving preparation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Home occupants, renters, movers, and organizing enthusiasts use this skill to get Chinese-language advice for decluttering, closet organization, kitchen and bathroom storage, children's rooms, entryways, storage rooms, and moving workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded room or closet photos may contain personal or sensitive household information. <br>
Mitigation: Treat images as personal data and avoid sharing or retaining them beyond what is needed to provide organization guidance. <br>
Risk: Organization or purchasing advice may be unsuitable for a user's space, budget, accessibility needs, or household constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before acting and adapt them to the user's actual measurements, budget, safety needs, and living situation. <br>
