## Description: <br>
Openclaw Talk Analyzer helps agents analyze business conversations to extract summaries, action items, decisions, sentiment, speaker insights, and follow-up recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ZhenStaff](https://clawhub.ai/user/ZhenStaff) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business, sales, support, product, and leadership teams use this skill to turn meeting notes, sales calls, support transcripts, negotiations, interviews, and other conversations into structured analysis and next-step guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Conversation transcripts may contain sensitive personal, customer, employee, legal, medical, financial, or confidential business information, and cloud AI providers create a privacy boundary. <br>
Mitigation: Redact sensitive content and confirm permission and provider terms before using Claude or OpenAI; prefer local LLM mode for sensitive conversations. <br>
