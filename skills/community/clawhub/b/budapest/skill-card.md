## Use Case: <br>
External users ask about visiting, moving to, studying in, working in, or starting a business in Budapest. The skill helps agents provide practical city guidance across neighborhoods, transport, costs, safety, visas, food, culture, healthcare, education, and lifestyle. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Time-sensitive visa, healthcare, legal, price, salary, transport, and venue information may become outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current requirements, prices, and operating details with official sources, qualified professionals, or current local providers before making decisions. <br>
Risk: Travel, relocation, business, healthcare, and education guidance can affect costly or regulated decisions if treated as professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as planning guidance and consult relevant legal, tax, healthcare, school, housing, or immigration professionals for binding decisions. <br>
Risk: Static neighborhood, safety, lodging, food, and nightlife recommendations may not reflect current local conditions or user-specific constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current conditions, reviews, accessibility, safety advisories, and personal requirements before booking, visiting, renting, or relocating. <br>
