## Description: <br>
Generate and create UGC-style product review and testimonial videos using SJinn Seedance 2.0, with 9-layer prompt architecture for authentic selfie-style content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jax-explorer](https://clawhub.ai/user/jax-explorer) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators, marketers, and agents use this skill to collect product and campaign details, draft structured UGC video prompts, and submit them to SJinn Seedance 2.0 for short product review or testimonial videos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow can send product images, campaign details, likeness preferences, or audio preferences to the SJinn generation service. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential product images, unreleased campaign details, private likenesses, or sensitive audio preferences unless those inputs are intended for SJinn processing. <br>
Risk: Generated video prompts may produce misleading product testimonials or unrealistic advertising claims if user-provided product details are inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review the final prompt and generated result before publication, and verify that claims, audience targeting, and testimonial framing match approved campaign materials. <br>
