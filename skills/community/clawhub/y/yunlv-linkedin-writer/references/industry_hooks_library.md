# 行业钩子库

## 一、制造业钩子

### 采购经理钩子
```
"I've noticed [Company] has been [recent news/action] — this got me thinking about [industry challenge]."

"Supply chain disruptions are top of mind for most procurement leaders lately — how is your team handling [specific challenge]?"

"With [industry trend] accelerating, I'm curious how your procurement strategy is evolving."
```

### 运营总监钩子
```
"I saw [Company]'s expansion into [market/product]. What operational challenges come with that growth?"

"Quality control at scale is something we help companies like yours solve — interested in how?"

"Your competitors seem to be focusing on [trend] — what's your take on that?"
```

---

## 二、零售/批发钩子

### 采购总监钩子
```
"[Company]'s growth in [category] has been impressive. How are you approaching supplier diversification?"

"With consumer demand shifting toward [trend], I imagine [challenge] is top of mind for your team."

"I noticed [Company] just launched [product line]. Any gaps in your current supply chain?"
```

### 创始人/CEO钩子
```
"Building a reliable supply chain is one of the biggest challenges for [industry] brands — what does yours look like?"

"Interested in how companies like [similar brand] have streamlined their sourcing?"

"How are you thinking about [cost/quality/reliability] in your current sourcing strategy?"
```

## 三、科技公司钩子

### CTO/技术负责人钩子
```
"I see [Company] is working on [technology/product]. What infrastructure are you building for that?"

"Tech companies often struggle with [scalability/reliability/cost] — what's your approach?"

"How is [Company] addressing [industry technical challenge]?"
```

## 四、创业公司钩子

### 创始人钩子
```
"As you scale [Company], supplier reliability becomes critical — have you found the right partners?"

"Quick growth often means supply chain growing pains — how are you managing that?"

"I noticed [Company] is in [funding stage/expansion]. Congrats on the momentum!"
```

## 五、通用钩子

### 观察到客户动态的钩子
```
"I noticed [Company] just [hired/released/expanded]. Exciting times!"

"Your recent post about [topic] really resonated. Especially your point about [specific insight]."

"Congrats on [Company]'s [achievement/milestone]!"
```

### 行业趋势钩子
```
"[Industry trend] seems to be accelerating. How is [Company] positioning for that?"

"With [market shift], we're seeing companies like yours focus on [opportunity]."

"[Statistic/fact about industry]. How does that compare to your experience?"
```

### 共同联系钩子
```
"We both seem to know [mutual contact]. Small world!"

"I see [mutual connection] connected us on LinkedIn. Great to see the network effect in action."

"[Mutual contact] mentioned you might be interested in [topic]. Would love to explore."
```
