## Description: <br>
Plan, protect, and execute your day with morning briefings, priority management, calendar blocking, and commitment tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and individual professionals use this skill to generate daily plans, protect focus time, prioritize a Top 3, manage calendar conflicts, and track commitments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The planner may use sensitive calendar, email, notification, contact, and conversation context. <br>
Mitigation: Limit shared context to what is needed for planning and periodically review or delete stored planner data under ~/planner/. <br>
Risk: The planner describes actions that could change calendars, notifications, messages, shared summaries, drafts, meetings, or saved commitments. <br>
Mitigation: Require user confirmation before writing calendar events, pausing notifications, enabling auto-replies, sharing summaries, sending drafts, canceling meetings, or saving commitments. <br>
