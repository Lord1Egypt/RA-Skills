## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can help control a logged-in X browser and prepare public posts. <br>
Mitigation: Review the article, account, media, and final publish dialog before posting; keep the CDP browser local and avoid sensitive primary accounts unless the public-posting risk is accepted. <br>
