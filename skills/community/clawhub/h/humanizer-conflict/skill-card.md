## Description: <br>
Removes signs of AI-generated writing from text and rewrites prose to sound more natural while preserving the original meaning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Beelkic](https://clawhub.ai/user/Beelkic) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and agents use this skill to review AI-sounding prose, identify common AI writing patterns, and produce a more natural rewrite with an optional summary of changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be used to make AI-generated prose appear human-written, which may misrepresent authorship. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs carefully and do not use the rewrite to misrepresent authorship or invent a personal voice that was not in the original text. <br>
Risk: A rewrite may alter nuance, add unsupported specificity, or make factual writing less accurate. <br>
Mitigation: Check the rewritten text against the source material, especially for academic, legal, professional, or factual writing. <br>
