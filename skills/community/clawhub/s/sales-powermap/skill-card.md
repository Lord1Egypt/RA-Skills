## Description: <br>
智能销售助手：从模糊意图出发，发现目标客户、挖掘组织架构、分析决策链路、构建 Power Map 可视化关系图，输出可执行的销售攻单方案。触发词：卖、客户、power map、决策人、组织架构 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales and business-development users use this skill to turn vague B2B sales intent into target-company recommendations, decision-maker research, Power Map relationship diagrams, and actionable account-entry plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce person-level sales prospect profiles and decision-maker maps. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for compliant B2B outreach and limit research to publicly available business contact data. <br>
Risk: Generated leads, roles, relationships, and outreach channels may be inaccurate or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Review and verify all generated leads, contact paths, and messages before acting on them. <br>
Risk: The skill may encourage collection of personal emails, phone numbers, or inferred contact details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid personal emails, personal phone numbers, and speculative email inference unless policy and applicable law explicitly permit their use. <br>
