## Description: <br>
A Chinese fiction-writing reference skill that provides core formulas, templates, examples, and checklists for planning and improving emotionally driven serialized stories. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mumuli2021](https://clawhub.ai/user/mumuli2021) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers and agent users use this skill to plan romance, fantasy, urban, and reincarnation-themed fiction with structured emotional arcs, character guidance, scene formulas, templates, and quality checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
