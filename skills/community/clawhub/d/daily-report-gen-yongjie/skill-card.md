## Description: <br>
根据工作内容描述自动生成结构化日报或周报，支持通用、军工、简洁和详细格式。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, project contributors, and managers use this skill to turn daily or weekly work items into structured Chinese status reports. It is especially useful when teams need consistent report sections, project progress summaries, issue tracking, or military-format draft fields. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Military-format reports may contain generated quality record numbers, review IDs, and standards citations that appear official. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated reports as drafts and verify identifiers, review records, and standards citations before official submission. <br>
Risk: Work-report prompts may include classified, protected, or otherwise sensitive project details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in an approved agent environment and avoid entering sensitive details unless authorized. <br>
