## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-aware marketing content for auto repair, auto body, and collision repair shops, including campaigns, website copy, review workflows, local SEO materials, fleet outreach, and related disclosure guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Shop owners, service department managers, and marketing teams use this skill to draft localized auto repair marketing assets with prompts that account for ASE credential wording, EPA Section 608 disclosures, FTC warranty rules, review practices, and state estimate requirements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SMS or email outreach could be used without the required opt-in, legal basis, unsubscribe language, or suppression handling. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm recipient consent or legal basis, include required opt-out language, and honor suppression requests before sending outreach. <br>
Risk: Generated warranty, estimate, review, or advertising claims may need state-specific or platform-specific review before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review regulated claims against applicable state law, platform policy, and shop-specific credentials before publishing or sending. <br>
