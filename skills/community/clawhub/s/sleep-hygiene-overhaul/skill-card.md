## Description: <br>
Evidence-based sleep improvement protocol for people who have trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, waking unrefreshed, or optimizing sleep quality without medication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and their agents use this skill to run a two-week behavioral sleep-improvement protocol with a sleep audit, wake-time anchor, wind-down ritual, caffeine cutoff, environment checklist, and follow-up check-in. It is intended for common sleep dysfunction and explicitly redirects possible clinical sleep disorders to medical care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may save sleep-habit details, which can include sensitive health-adjacent information. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if the user is comfortable with sleep details being persisted, and avoid sharing health details that should not be stored. <br>
Risk: The skill may create recurring sleep-related reminders that reveal private routines through notifications. <br>
Mitigation: Review reminder text and timing after setup, and disable or edit reminders when notification privacy matters. <br>
Risk: The protocol is not designed to diagnose or treat clinical sleep disorders such as sleep apnea, clinical insomnia disorder, or restless leg syndrome. <br>
Mitigation: Use the built-in red flags to recommend medical evaluation when symptoms suggest a clinical condition or when the two-week protocol does not improve sleep. <br>
