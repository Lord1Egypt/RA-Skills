## Description: <br>
Complete Placed career platform integration for resume building, interview coaching, job tracking, ATS checking, cover letter generation, LinkedIn optimization, and salary tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career-focused users use this skill to manage resumes, practice interviews, track applications, optimize materials for ATS systems, generate cover letters, research companies, and review salary information through the Placed platform. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores a Placed API key in a local credentials file. <br>
Mitigation: Use a dedicated, revocable API key and protect or delete ~/.config/placed/credentials when it is no longer needed. <br>
Risk: Resume, cover letter, interview, and job-tracker content may be sent to Placed's API. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if sharing relevant career data with Placed is acceptable, and review important career-material changes before sending them. <br>
