## Description: <br>
Innovation OS is a Chinese-language brainstorming framework that uses four innovation modes to generate non-obvious plans for a user-specified domain. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to structure ideation sessions, select innovation modes, evaluate candidate ideas, and refine selected proposals for business, technical, policy, education, creative, or personal-development contexts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can generate ideation for investment, legal, policy, and business strategy topics where unsupported advice may be costly or non-compliant. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as brainstorming material and independently verify facts, compliance requirements, market assumptions, and professional implications before use. <br>
Risk: The framework may produce plausible but speculative innovation proposals when domain facts are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's evaluation and validation steps to mark uncertain claims for verification and test selected proposals with domain experts or small experiments. <br>
