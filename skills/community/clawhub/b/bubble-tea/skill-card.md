## Description: <br>
提供关于网红奶茶的推荐清单、打卡路线与指南。用户搜索网红奶茶或规划相关出行时调用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to ask an agent for bubble-tea shop recommendations, route ideas, and visit-planning guidance filtered by city, budget, taste, queue time, and accessibility needs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prices, hours, queue lengths, popularity, and shop rules can change after recommendations are generated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current details from the shop or a trusted local source before visiting or relying on a recommendation. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes some restaurant-style fields that may be off topic for bubble-tea recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Constrain agent responses to bubble-tea-specific fields and omit restaurant-only details unless they are directly relevant. <br>
