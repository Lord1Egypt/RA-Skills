## Description: <br>
Answer questions and generate responses for Finance Claw, a FinChain and FUSD focused finance skill covering FUSD, FUSDLP, FinChain, RWA product facts, stablecoin yield calculations, RWA risk-rating lookups, FUSD and USDT comparisons, and finance-themed humorous replies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial figures such as yields, reserve data, risk ratings, and product availability may be stale or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current figures against the online knowledge index, official FinChain sources, and live data sources before relying on the answer. <br>
Risk: Users may treat informational output as personalized investment advice or a market prediction. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses educational, use framework-based guidance for allocation questions, and avoid hard investment instructions or price predictions. <br>
Risk: Unsupported APRs, reserve ratios, product flows, or compliance claims could mislead users. <br>
Mitigation: Ground claims in the remote structured sources or official references and clearly distinguish documented facts from live or estimated data. <br>
