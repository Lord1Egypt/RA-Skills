## Description: <br>
Guide taxpayers through filing Geneva (Switzerland) tax returns with GeTax, including salary, professional expenses, pension deductions, insurance and medical expenses, securities, bank accounts, debts, crypto, foreign property, and Geneva cantonal tax rules. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users preparing Geneva, Switzerland tax returns use this skill to work through GeTax sections, compare forfaitaire versus effectifs deductions, gather supporting documents, and check Geneva-specific rules before filing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Switzerland (Geneva tax filings) <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may encourage users to misstate work-from-home or meal-deduction facts on a tax return. <br>
Mitigation: Users should report only facts that match their actual working pattern and verify Geneva work-from-home and meal-deduction rules with official guidance or a qualified tax professional. <br>
Risk: Tax guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or mismatched to a user's filing year and personal facts. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as filing assistance, check current official GeTax and Geneva tax sources, and confirm uncertain positions before submitting a return. <br>
