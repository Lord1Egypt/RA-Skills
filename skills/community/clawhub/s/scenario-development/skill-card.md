## Description: <br>
Develop multiple future scenarios to prepare for uncertainty for long-term strategic planning, risk management, and helping organizations think about the future. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[linuszz](https://clawhub.ai/user/linuszz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Strategists, planners, and organizational decision-makers use this skill to frame focal questions, identify critical uncertainties, build scenario matrices, write future narratives, and translate scenarios into strategic implications. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases may activate the skill in unrelated conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow the trigger phrases or invoke the skill explicitly when structured scenario planning is needed. <br>
Risk: Scenario outputs can reflect incomplete assumptions about a domain, market, or organization. <br>
Mitigation: Review the focal question, uncertainties, signposts, and strategic implications with subject-matter experts before using them for decisions. <br>
