Risk: The skill describes use of sensitive banking, customer, credit, transaction, and regulatory-risk data while the manifest claims no tools, network access, storage, or executable behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in a controlled banking environment with approved connectors, role-based access, explicit customer and credit authorization, and documented retention controls. <br>
Risk: The workflow can influence customer strategy, credit preparation, and risk presentation in regulated banking contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Require qualified human review, preserve audit records, keep risk warnings visible, and prevent outputs from being treated as financial, legal, insurance, or credit-approval advice. <br>
