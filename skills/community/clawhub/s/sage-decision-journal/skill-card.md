## Description: <br>
A decision capture and review system that records every significant choice with context, reasoning, and alternatives so users can detect blind spots and decision patterns over time. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[EvanL1](https://clawhub.ai/user/EvanL1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to capture meaningful decisions, classify them by context and reversibility, and review patterns over weekly, monthly, and quarterly retrospectives. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is designed to record and analyze personal or work decisions across conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when decision capture is intentional, and confirm that users can opt in, pause capture, review and edit entries, delete history, and limit which conversations are recorded. <br>
Risk: The skill depends on sage-cognitive and may write decision tendencies, bias signals, and pattern insights back to that profile. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect the sage-cognitive dependency and its retention behavior before relying on this skill in sensitive personal or workplace contexts. <br>
