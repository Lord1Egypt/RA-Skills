## Description: <br>
Build a same-day notary readiness packet with document inventory, ID checklist, signer logistics, and verification prompts while avoiding legal advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to prepare for urgent notarization appointments by organizing documents, signer identity details, witness questions, logistics, and verification prompts before meeting a notary or receiving institution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce jurisdiction-specific notary guidance or drafting-like outputs that go beyond general preparation. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as an organizational checklist only and verify notary rules, legal language, acceptable IDs, and receiving-institution requirements with the notary, institution, or a qualified professional. <br>
Risk: Notary workflows can involve sensitive identity data, signatures, thumbprints, and legal documents. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the minimum needed details with the agent and avoid providing full ID numbers, signatures, thumbprints, or complete legal documents unless the data handling environment is trusted. <br>
