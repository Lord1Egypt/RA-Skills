## Description: <br>
Expert guidance for systematic backtesting of trading strategies. Use when developing, testing, stress-testing, or validating quantitative trading strategies. Covers "beating ideas to death" methodology, parameter robustness testing, slippage modeling, bias prevention, and interpreting backtest results. Applicable when user asks about backtesting, strategy validation, robustness testing, avoiding overfitting, or systematic trading development. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users could treat educational backtesting guidance as financial advice or deploy trading systems without separate review. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as research guidance only; review generated trading code, datasets, and live-execution steps separately before use, and do not share brokerage credentials or allow trades based only on this skill. <br>
Risk: Backtests may be misleading when affected by overfitting, look-ahead bias, survivorship bias, small samples, or unrealistic execution assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the documented stress tests, pessimistic slippage, all-case testing, out-of-sample validation, and sample-size checks before relying on results. <br>
