## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese guidance for a proactive but restrained work companion that gives short reminders, lightweight check-ins, brief summaries, and gentle follow-ups without excessive interruption. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Julia-XD](https://clawhub.ai/user/Julia-XD) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external users who prefer a Chinese-speaking work companion use this skill to guide the assistant toward concise reminders, brief status summaries, low-pressure check-ins, and restrained follow-up during work sessions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can make the assistant more proactive than the user expects. <br>
Mitigation: Keep proactive behavior limited to reminders, summaries, and low-impact follow-ups, and stay quiet when a message adds no clear value. <br>
Risk: A proactive companion style could be over-applied to consequential actions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation before file edits, account actions, public posts, or sensitive decisions. <br>
