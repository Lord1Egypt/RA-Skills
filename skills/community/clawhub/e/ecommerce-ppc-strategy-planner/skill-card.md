## Description: <br>
Cross-platform PPC strategy planner for ecommerce businesses that analyzes product economics, recommends advertising platforms, allocates budget, and generates campaign briefs with ad copy and creative direction. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/phheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators and marketing teams use this skill to plan new PPC campaigns or audit existing spend across Google Ads, Meta Ads, and TikTok Ads. It helps translate product margin, budget, and performance data into ROAS targets, budget allocations, ad copy, and creative briefs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Installation trust depends on the referenced package and source being the intended release. <br>
Mitigation: Before installing, verify that the referenced GitHub repository and npx command are sources you intend to trust. <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive commercial data such as margins, budgets, and ad performance. <br>
Mitigation: Share only business margin, budget, and ad-performance data you are comfortable exposing. <br>
Risk: PPC recommendations can affect real ad spend and may be based on estimates or benchmarks when data is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations carefully, confirm estimates against current account data, and test changes before applying them broadly. <br>
