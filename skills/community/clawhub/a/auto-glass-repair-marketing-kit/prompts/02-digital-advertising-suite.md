## Compliance Gates (All Prompt 1 gates apply, plus:)

**ADAS Claims:**
- "ADAS certified recalibration" → only if shop has documented calibration equipment (Autel ADAS Pro, Launch X-431, Bosch DAS 3000, or OEM equivalent) AND trained technician.
- "ADAS-capable" or "we offer ADAS recalibration" → permissible with equipment + training.
- Do NOT use "guaranteed ADAS accuracy" or "100% calibrated" without documented post-calibration verification process.

**Yelp:**
- Yelp Terms of Service prohibit soliciting reviews in exchange for discounts. No review incentive language on Yelp-specific outreach. FTC 2023 applies: no review gating on any platform.

**Google Ads:**
- Auto glass is not a restricted category on Google, but "insurance" references in ads must not misrepresent coverage ("covered by insurance" requires "check your policy" qualifier).
- Healthcare/safety claims: any claim about driver safety must be substantiated (AGSC certification, ADAS calibration documentation).
