## Description: <br>
Build a meditation practice with guided sessions, streaks, and mindfulness reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jhillin8](https://clawhub.ai/user/jhillin8) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to start guided meditation sessions, take short breathing pauses, track practice streaks, manage reminders, and review local session logs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Meditation notes and exported logs may contain private wellness data. <br>
Mitigation: Keep logs local, review exported content before sharing, and avoid recording sensitive details that are not needed for reflection. <br>
Risk: Mindfulness reminders may become disruptive if scheduled too frequently. <br>
Mitigation: Enable reminders only at a frequency the user actually wants and adjust or disable them when they interrupt normal work. <br>
