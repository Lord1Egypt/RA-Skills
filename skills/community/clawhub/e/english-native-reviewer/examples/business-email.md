# 案例 1：商务邮件审查

## 原文

Dear John,

I am writing to inform you that in today's society, with the rapid development of our business, we need to make a decision to take measures to optimize our process. According to me think, we should discuss about the new proposal in the meeting. It is well known that there are many people who believe this is a very good quality plan. Through this, we can make our best effort to achieve a significant increase in order to promote the development of our company.

Please be advised that we would like to inform you that the deadline is approaching. Please let me know if you have any questions.

Best regards,
Li Ming

---

## 审查报告

### 🔴 必须修改（硬错误）

1. "According to me think" → "In my opinion" | according to 只能引用他人观点，不能用于自己
2. "discuss about" → "discuss" | discuss 是及物动词，不需要 about
3. "there are many people who believe" → "many believe" | 冗余 there-be 结构
4. 中英文标点混用："Li Ming" → 确认无中文标点残留

### 🟡 建议修改（翻译腔/不地道）

1. "in today's society, with the rapid development of our business" → 直接删除 | 中式套话开头，英文商务邮件不需要
2. "make a decision to take measures to" → "decide to" | 三重冗余，中文习惯堆叠动词
3. "It is well known that" → 删除 | 空泛开场白，直接说观点
4. "Through this, we can" → "This will enable us to" | 典型中式直译
5. "make our best effort" → "do our best" | 英语固定搭配
6. "in order to promote the development of" → "to drive" | 冗余 + 中式搭配
7. "Please be advised that we would like to inform you that" → "The deadline is approaching" | 双重委婉开头，直接说事

### 🟢 优化建议

1. "Dear John" → 考虑直接称呼名（已做）| 美式商务邮件偏非正式
2. "very good quality plan" → "well-designed plan" | vague adjective
3. 结尾 "Please let me know if you have any questions" → "Next steps: Please confirm by Friday" | 明确行动项

### 📊 整体评估

- **当前水平**：初级翻译腔
- **主要问题类型**：中式套话开头 / 动词冗余 / there-be 滥用 / 委婉表达过度
- **风格判断**：过于正式且冗长，商务邮件应简洁直接

## 修改后版本

Dear John,

We need to decide how to optimize our process. In my opinion, we should discuss the new proposal in the meeting. Many believe it's a well-designed plan. This will enable us to drive growth for the company.

The deadline is approaching. Please confirm by Friday.

Best regards,
Li Ming

---

## 为什么这样改

原文 93 词，修改后 51 词，减少 45%。核心信息完全保留，但去掉了所有中式套话和冗余表达。商务邮件的原则是"让读者 5 秒内理解你要什么"，修改后的版本开门见山，直接说事，结尾有明确行动项。
