## Description: <br>
China Hot Monitor aggregates public hot-list pages from Douyin, Weibo, Baidu, Toutiao, Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, and Kuaishou to compare trends, flag rising topics, and suggest content ideas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[windy-001-crypto](https://clawhub.ai/user/windy-001-crypto) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and operators use this skill to monitor public Chinese trending-topic lists, compare topic momentum across platforms, receive hot-topic alerts, and generate content-topic recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad Chinese trend or topic-ideation requests may activate the skill and fetch public trend pages. <br>
Mitigation: Review whether the requested topic-monitoring workflow is intended before using fetched trend data. <br>
Risk: Generated topic recommendations may be incomplete, misleading, or unsuitable for business decisions or publication. <br>
Mitigation: Verify trend context and editorial suitability before publishing content or acting on recommendations. <br>
