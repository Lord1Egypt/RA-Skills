## Description: <br>
简历优化助手。帮我写简历，改简历、导出PDF、准备面试问答。适用于：更新简历、补充项目经验、排版调整、导出PDF、准备面试问答。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and candidates use this skill to draft or revise Chinese resumes, format them as HTML for PDF export, and prepare interview question-and-answer guidance from resume content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume and interview-prep files may contain personal information and can be saved in the workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill in a trusted workspace and avoid providing sensitive personal details unless workspace storage is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Generated resume wording or interview guidance may be inaccurate or overstate experience. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated materials before sharing them with employers or using them in interview preparation. <br>
