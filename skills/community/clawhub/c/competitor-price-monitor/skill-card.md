## Description: <br>
Automatically track competitor prices, product updates, and feature changes, sending alerts and weekly reports to benchmark market positioning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business, product, marketing, and competitive intelligence teams use this skill to monitor approved competitor pages for pricing, product, feature, and messaging changes. It helps agents generate alerts and weekly competitive intelligence summaries from configured competitor URLs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Monitoring unapproved competitor pages or scraping too frequently can create legal, contractual, or operational risk. <br>
Mitigation: Configure only approved competitor URLs and use a reasonable check frequency before enabling scheduled monitoring. <br>
Risk: Stored baselines and page snapshots may retain more competitor or market data than necessary. <br>
Mitigation: Set retention limits for stored snapshots and avoid keeping unnecessary page content. <br>
Risk: Alerts and reports can expose internal pricing strategy or competitive analysis through external channels. <br>
Mitigation: Send email or Telegram alerts only to trusted recipients and avoid sharing internal strategy in unapproved channels. <br>
