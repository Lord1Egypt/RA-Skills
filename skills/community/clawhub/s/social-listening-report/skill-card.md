## Description: <br>
Expert system for creating social listening reports, benchmarks, competitive analyses, and brand health reports from digital and social media data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing strategists, insights researchers, brand managers, and agency analysts use this skill to turn social and digital media data into brand health reports, competitive benchmarks, campaign analyses, recurring reports, trend insights, and metric explanations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Real exports or screenshots may contain customer data, private identifiers, credentials, or confidential business details. <br>
Mitigation: Remove unnecessary sensitive data before use and provide only the fields needed for the requested report. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger language may activate the skill for general marketing or analytics requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants a social listening or brand analytics report before applying the skill's templates. <br>
Risk: Reports may overstate conclusions when inputs are incomplete, small, or missing benchmarks. <br>
Mitigation: State data quality limits, compare against prior periods or competitors where possible, and tie recommendations to supplied evidence. <br>
