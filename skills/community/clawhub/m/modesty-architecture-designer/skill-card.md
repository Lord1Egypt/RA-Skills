## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can recommend architecture choices that may be incomplete, unsuitable for a specific operating environment, or too costly if requirements are missing. <br>
Mitigation: Validate requirements, non-functional constraints, failure modes, and trade-offs with engineering and stakeholder reviewers before adopting the design. <br>
Risk: The artifact links to an external setup guide outside the local release evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review the external setup-guide link before following it, and treat requests for OAuth tokens or API keys as outside what the local skill artifacts require. <br>
