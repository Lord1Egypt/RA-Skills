## Use Case: <br>
Operations and product teams use this skill to turn ambiguous operational requests into product-ready requirements, align success metrics, compare priorities, and prepare acceptance and collaboration documents. It is suited for campaign planning, attribution reviews, requirements negotiation, and metric-definition alignment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Common operations and product collaboration phrases can activate the helper in normal planning conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the trigger phrases are intended for the target workspace before installation. <br>
Risk: Generated priorities, attribution models, data definitions, and acceptance criteria may not match local business rules or analytics systems. <br>
Mitigation: Have operations, product, and data owners review generated artifacts before using them for decisions or sign-off. <br>
Risk: Future releases could add scripts, external integrations, credential handling, or automatic actions. <br>
Mitigation: Re-run security review and inspect release changes before upgrading. <br>
