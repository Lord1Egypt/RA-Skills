## Description: <br>
Chinese STEM paper-writing assistant that guides an agent through title selection, outline creation, chapter drafting, results analysis, conclusions, abstracts, and keywords with academic consistency checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dongjiangliu9-tech](https://clawhub.ai/user/dongjiangliu9-tech) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, students, and research writers use this skill to help an agent draft Chinese STEM academic-paper components from provided references, research background, and user-confirmed section plans. It is intended as writing assistance that requires human review for accuracy, citations, originality, and any simulated data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated academic content can contain inaccurate claims, weak citations, originality issues, or simulated data presented too confidently. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review for factual accuracy, citation coverage, originality, and replacement or clear labeling of any simulated data before use. <br>
Risk: The artifact describes local processing and no external API calls, but privacy promises do not guarantee behavior of the broader AI platform or host runtime. <br>
Mitigation: Install from ClawHub or another verified source and grant only the file access needed for templates and drafts. <br>
