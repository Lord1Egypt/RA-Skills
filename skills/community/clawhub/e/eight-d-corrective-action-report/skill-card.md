## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a quality engineer, supplier-quality engineer, or manufacturing engineer needs to draft an 8D corrective action report for a nonconformance, SCAR, customer complaint, or recurring defect. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[archlab-space](https://clawhub.ai/user/archlab-space) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Quality engineers, supplier-quality engineers, and manufacturing engineers use this skill to draft a D0-D8 corrective action report for nonconformances, supplier corrective action requests, customer complaints, or recurring manufacturing defects. The report remains a draft for qualified quality-owner review and sign-off before transmittal. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft 8D content may be incorrect or incomplete for safety, recall, regulatory, or customer-submittal matters. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs as drafts and require review by the quality engineer of record, customer-quality counterpart, and qualified legal or regulatory owners when applicable. <br>
Risk: Manufacturing reports may include confidential customer, supplier, part, drawing, or measurement details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential business details unless the user is comfortable sharing them in the agent session. <br>
