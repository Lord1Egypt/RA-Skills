## Description: <br>
Estimates AI-detection risk for academic text using local heuristics and can suggest humanizing revisions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to analyze academic text for AI-like writing patterns and receive a structured risk report. It can also suggest rewritten text, but the results are heuristic estimates rather than official proof of authorship. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can be used to help academic work avoid AI-detection systems, which may violate school, publisher, or workplace integrity rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use only where the relevant institution or workflow permits this kind of review, and require users to verify that revisions preserve truthful attribution and academic integrity. <br>
Risk: The skill presents Turnitin-like scores that are local heuristic estimates, not official Turnitin results or reliable proof of authorship. <br>
Mitigation: Label scores as estimates, avoid treating them as determinations, and review the underlying text and context before making decisions. <br>
