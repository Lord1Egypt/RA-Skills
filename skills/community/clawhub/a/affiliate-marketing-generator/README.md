# Affiliate Marketing Content Generator

Generate a complete affiliate marketing content system from 4 prompts — review posts, comparison posts, email sequences, and social scripts.

## What's Included

| Prompt | Output | Use Case |
|--------|--------|----------|
| `affiliate-review` | 1,200-1,800 word SEO review | Rank for "[product] review" searches |
| `comparison-post` | 1,500-2,000 word X vs Y vs Z | Capture decision-stage buyers |
| `email-affiliate-sequence` | 5-email promotion sequence | Convert warm list subscribers |
| `social-affiliate-scripts` | IG Reel + TikTok + YouTube pre-roll + X thread | Short-form social promotion |

## Quick Start

1. Pick the affiliate product you want to promote
2. Identify your target audience
3. Run `affiliate-review` first to establish your authoritative source
4. Run `comparison-post` to capture comparison searches
5. Use `email-affiliate-sequence` to promote to your list
6. Use `social-affiliate-scripts` to drive traffic to your review
