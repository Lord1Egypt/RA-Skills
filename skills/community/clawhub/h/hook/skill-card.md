## Description: <br>
Hook helps an agent diagnose and improve Chinese article openings by evaluating titles and first paragraphs across eight attention and trust dimensions, then proposing rewrites and title options. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[shing19](https://clawhub.ai/user/shing19) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and content-focused agents use this skill to critique Chinese titles and openings, identify why readers may not continue, and produce targeted rewrite and title options while preserving the author's voice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Suggested rewrites may shift the author's intended tone or introduce unsupported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated openings and title options for accuracy, factual fit, and consistency with the original article before publishing. <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential or unpublished drafts into the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential drafts unless the user is comfortable providing that text to the agent environment. <br>
