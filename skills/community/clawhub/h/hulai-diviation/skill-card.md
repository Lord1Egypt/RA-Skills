## Description: <br>
Provides light Chinese-language fortune-style entertainment with random Bagua, scenario-based, and custom readings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tiandaox](https://clawhub.ai/user/tiandaox) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users invoke this skill for casual Chinese-language fortune-style entertainment, including daily luck prompts, scenario-specific jokes, and custom question readings. Outputs are intended for amusement and should not be treated as real divination, religious guidance, or life advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake fortune-style results for real advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the entertainment disclaimer visible and treat all readings as jokes, not decisions or guidance. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases such as 今日运势 or 测一测 may activate the skill unexpectedly. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when casual Chinese-language fortune entertainment is desired, and review outputs before relying on them in shared contexts. <br>
