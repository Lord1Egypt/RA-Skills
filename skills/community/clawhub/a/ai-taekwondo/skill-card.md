## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat martial-arts coaching output as a substitute for qualified in-person instruction or medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as supplemental training guidance only; consult a qualified coach or medical professional for technique correction, injury concerns, or readiness decisions. <br>
Risk: Advanced kicks, aerial techniques, breaking practice, and unsupervised training can create injury risk, especially for minors, beginners, or injured users. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid unsupervised advanced drills; use protective equipment, mats, warmups, progressive difficulty, and qualified supervision for high-impact or high-risk practice. <br>
