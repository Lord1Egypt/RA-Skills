## Description: <br>
CLI tool for analyzing, optimizing, and formatting resumes to improve ATS scoring through keyword matching and structured templates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[HiroFumiko](https://clawhub.ai/user/HiroFumiko) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to inspect resumes, compare them with job descriptions, surface keyword gaps, and prepare ATS-oriented resume output. It is intended for local resume and job-description workflows where candidate data may be sensitive. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume and job-description files can contain personal, contact, reference, or employer-confidential information, and the skill may retain data in a configured local database path. <br>
Mitigation: Process only data you are comfortable storing locally, redact sensitive details when possible, and review or clear the configured database path if retained content is not desired. <br>
