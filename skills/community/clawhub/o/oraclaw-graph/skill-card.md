## Description: <br>
Network intelligence for AI agents. PageRank, community detection (Louvain), critical path, and bottleneck analysis for any graph of connected things. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[whatsonyourmind](https://clawhub.ai/user/whatsonyourmind) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and AI agents use this skill to analyze connected data such as task dependencies, org charts, knowledge graphs, and decision networks. It helps identify influential nodes, communities, critical paths, and bottlenecks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires ORACLAW_API_KEY and sends graph data to an external analysis service. <br>
Mitigation: Configure the API key only in trusted environments and review graph contents before submitting sensitive or proprietary data. <br>
Risk: The artifact describes paid usage after the free tier. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm pricing, usage limits, and billing expectations before high-volume analysis. <br>
