## Description: <br>
Generates structured HR-style resume evaluation reports with overall recommendations, candidate profile details, strengths, risks, and follow-up interview questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kongkong827](https://clawhub.ai/user/kongkong827) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and hiring teams use this skill to turn resume files or resume text into concise, evidence-based HR evaluation reports and targeted interview questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for resume review and may process sensitive candidate and hiring-context information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide resumes intentionally, avoid unnecessary personal data, and handle candidate materials according to applicable privacy and retention policies. <br>
Risk: Generated pass, fail, or risk recommendations may be inaccurate, unfair, or legally sensitive if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human verify facts, fairness, job relevance, and legal compliance before acting on any hiring recommendation. <br>
Risk: Resume parsing from PDF, DOCX, or images may miss or misread details that affect the evaluation. <br>
Mitigation: Check extracted facts against the original resume and treat uncertain or missing information as requiring interview verification. <br>
