## Description: <br>
Practical guidance for navigating grief after loss. Use when someone has lost a person, relationship, job, or life chapter and needs help understanding what they're experiencing and how to move through it. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and assisting agents use this skill to provide grounded grief support after death, relationship loss, job loss, or major life transitions. It helps users understand non-linear grief, keep basic systems running, prepare responses to unhelpful comments, create memorial rituals, and recognize when professional or crisis support is needed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store sensitive grief-related details and dates. <br>
Mitigation: Use only when the user is comfortable with that persistence, and avoid recording more personal detail than needed for support and follow-up. <br>
Risk: The skill can create or support reminders tied to loss dates, anniversaries, and grief check-ins. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm reminder behavior with the user and make loss-date reminders easy to review, adjust, or remove. <br>
Risk: Users may disclose self-harm thoughts or immediate danger while discussing grief. <br>
Mitigation: Redirect immediately to crisis resources available in the user's country or local emergency services. <br>
