## Description: <br>
Regulates intelligent care by sustaining rest, slowness, and play as active states that preserve presence and coherence across extended, complex environments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Agents and operators use this skill to adopt a slower, rest-oriented interaction posture that maintains presence and coherence during long-running or high-context work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can reduce perceived responsiveness by encouraging slower, rest-oriented interaction. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for workflows that can tolerate deliberate pacing, and avoid it for urgent, highly audited, or status-heavy work that requires frequent progress updates. <br>
