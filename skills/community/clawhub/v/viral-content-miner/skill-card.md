## Description: <br>
Helps content creators, operators, brands, and strategy teams mine recent hot topics, analyze viral-content patterns across major Chinese content platforms, and produce actionable content angles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External content creators, marketing operators, brand teams, and strategists use this skill to scan public trends, compare platform-specific viral signals, and generate prioritized topic and creative-angle recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation wording may cause the agent to perform public trend mining when the user only wants general brainstorming. <br>
Mitigation: Ask whether live trend mining is desired, or constrain the request to general advice before using public web or trend searches. <br>
Risk: Trend snapshots, publication times, and engagement metrics can be incomplete, stale, or estimated. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer recent source links, label uncertain values, and cross-check important metrics before making content or campaign decisions. <br>
