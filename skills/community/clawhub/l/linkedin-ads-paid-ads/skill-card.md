## Description: <br>
Guides setup, targeting, creative, budget, tracking, and optimization for LinkedIn Ads campaigns, especially B2B paid ads where professional targeting matters. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, growth teams, and operators use this skill to plan and refine LinkedIn Ads campaigns, including campaign naming, ad format selection, professional targeting, creative checks, budget and bidding choices, and tracking setup. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
