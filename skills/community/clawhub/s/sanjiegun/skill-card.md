## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, consultants, teams, and brand operators use this skill to turn structured answers about capability, public image, and values into a personal or team IP positioning report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential client data, financial details, or private business strategy while answering positioning questions. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive business or client information unless the user is comfortable discussing it in the chat. <br>
Risk: Brand positioning and content recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific market or audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review suggested positioning, visual hooks, and content changes before acting on them. <br>
