## Description: <br>
Find high-intent leads on Hacker News via the free Algolia API, score posts, surface Ask HN threads, and identify technical founders actively seeking tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[3rdbrain](https://clawhub.ai/user/3rdbrain) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sales, founder-led growth, and developer-relations teams use this skill to find Hacker News posts where technical buyers are asking for tools, alternatives, or workflow recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms are sent to the public Algolia Hacker News API. <br>
Mitigation: Use generic market or problem keywords and avoid confidential customer names, deal details, internal strategy terms, or sensitive prospecting plans. <br>
