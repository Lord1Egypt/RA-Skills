## Description: <br>
Gingiris Opensource is an open-source marketing playbook that helps developer-tool teams plan README optimization, Show HN, Reddit, Product Hunt, KOL outreach, and a six-month community growth loop. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External OSS maintainers, developer-tool founders, and DevRel teams use this skill to choose launch channels, improve README conversion, plan community outreach, and sustain GitHub star growth. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing recommendations could be applied in ways that violate GitHub, Reddit, Hacker News, Product Hunt, or community rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review platform and community rules before using channel-specific tactics, and avoid spam, artificial star farming, or undisclosed coordinated promotion. <br>
Risk: The playbook is advisory content, so growth claims and timing recommendations may not fit every project or market. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as planning guidance, validate them against the target community, and monitor outcomes before scaling outreach. <br>
