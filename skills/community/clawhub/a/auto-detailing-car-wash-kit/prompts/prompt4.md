# Prompt 4 — Digital Ads + Local SEO + Fleet/Dealer Outreach

## Overview
Generates Google LSA optimization checklist, 5-ad-group RSA structure, Facebook 3-segment campaign, 30-day GBP calendar, local SEO keyword targets, and fleet/dealer cold outreach email sequence.

## Required Inputs
```
Business name: [your business name]
City/state/ZIP: [primary market]
Service area cities: [list 3-5 surrounding cities/neighborhoods]
Services highlighted in ads: [check all]
  - [ ] Interior detail
  - [ ] Exterior detail
  - [ ] Ceramic coating
  - [ ] PPF
  - [ ] Mobile/on-site
  - [ ] Fleet/dealer prep
  - [ ] Express wash
Monthly ad budget: [total Google + Meta combined, e.g., "$500/month"]
Google rating: [X.X stars, XXX reviews — required for "top-rated" claims]
Primary differentiator for ads: [certified installer / mobile / fastest turnaround / compliance-focused / luxury specialty]
Fleet outreach desired: [Yes / No]
Fleet target types: [dealerships / property management / delivery fleets / hotels / all]
Owner name (for outreach emails): [name]
Owner phone/email (for outreach): [contact]
```

## Prompt
```
You are a digital marketing specialist for auto detailing and car wash businesses. Generate a complete digital advertising and local SEO plan for the business described below.

**Business details:**
[PASTE INPUTS HERE]

---

## SECTION 1: Google LSA Optimization Checklist

Generate a prioritized LSA setup checklist for the "Auto Detailing" category:

**Profile completeness requirements:**
- [ ] Business name matches Google Business Profile exactly
- [ ] License verification (varies by state — detailing typically not licensed, but garage keepers insurance required for LSA)
- [ ] Insurance certificate uploaded (garage keepers liability minimum)
- [ ] Background check badge: business owner + all employees who enter vehicles
- [ ] Service area confirmed (radius from primary address)
- [ ] Services selected in LSA dashboard: Interior Detailing, Exterior Detailing, Car Wash, Auto Detailing (all applicable)
- [ ] Profile photo: shop exterior or van wrap (not stock photo)
- [ ] At least 10 Google reviews before going live (LSA penalizes thin review profiles)

**Weekly LSA management tasks (generate as checklist):**
[5 tasks: budget pacing, lead response time, lead dispute protocol, review monitoring, service area adjustments]

**LSA compliance note:** "Google Guaranteed" or "Google Screened" badges are Google program designations — do not use in ads, website, or marketing materials unless actively enrolled in the program. Using these terms without enrollment = trademark misuse.

---

## SECTION 2: Google RSA — 5 Ad Groups

For each selected service, generate one ad group with:
- Ad group name
- 3 RSA headlines (30 char max each) — write 8 total, Google picks best combinations
- 2 RSA descriptions (90 char max each) — write 4 total
- 3 keyword targets (broad match modifier format)

**Ad Groups:**
1. Interior Detailing
2. Exterior Detail / Paint Decontamination
3. Ceramic Coating + PPF (if selected)
4. Mobile / On-Site Detailing (if selected)
5. Fleet / Dealer Prep (if selected) — otherwise: Express Wash / Quick Detail

**Compliance rules for RSA copy:**
- "Best detailer in [city]": only with Google rating citation in description ("rated X.X stars on XXX reviews")
- "Ceramic coating lasts [X] years": only with product name + manufacturer warranty. Otherwise: "long-lasting ceramic protection"
- "Guaranteed results": replace with "100% satisfaction re-service guarantee"
- "Lowest price": do not use — leads to price-only customers and disputes
- "Eco-friendly": only if EPA-compliant water reclaim confirmed. Otherwise: "low-VOC products used"

---

## SECTION 3: Facebook/Instagram 3-Segment Campaign

Generate one ad set per segment:

**Segment 1 — Luxury/New Car Owners**
- Audience: car enthusiasts, new car buyers (vehicle purchase in past 6 months), luxury vehicle owners
- Hook: paint protection / ceramic coating angle
- Primary text (125 char preview): [fear of depreciation / paint damage angle]
- Headline: [protection-focused]
- CTA: Get a Quote

**Segment 2 — Family Vehicle Owners**
- Audience: parents, SUV/minivan owners, homeowners 30-55
- Hook: interior clean + odor elimination angle
- Primary text: [kid mess / pet hair / "feels new again" angle]
- Headline: [interior-focused]
- CTA: Book Now

**Segment 3 — Fleet/Business Owners (if fleet outreach selected)**
- Audience: business owners, fleet managers, property managers, auto dealership employees
- Hook: dealer prep / fleet efficiency angle
- Primary text: [professional image / reduced downtime angle]
- Headline: [fleet-focused]
- CTA: Get Fleet Quote

**Compliance for Facebook ads:**
- Before/after photos: permissible as long as both images are of the same vehicle and conditions are disclosed ("same vehicle, same lighting")
- "Ceramic coating" in ad: permissible — but product name/spec required in landing page (not ad)
- Testimonial quotes: include disclosure if reviewer received incentive ("received referral credit for introduction")

---

## SECTION 4: 30-Day Google Business Profile Calendar

Generate 30 daily GBP post ideas (alternating between: before/after case study, tip, promotional, service spotlight, FAQ answer, community/seasonal hook). Format:
- Day X: [Post type] — [Hook sentence] — [CTA]

**Seasonal timing:** Adjust for month provided in inputs (or use generic rotation if month not specified).

**GBP compliance:**
- Before/after: caption must state vehicle year/make/model and service performed
- "Award-winning": only with specific award citation (year, organization, category)
- Price posts: must include "starting at" and link to booking page

---

## SECTION 5: Local SEO — 15 Keyword Targets

Generate 15 keywords in format: [keyword] | [estimated intent] | [page to target]

Include mix of:
- Service + city (e.g., "ceramic coating Henderson NV")
- Near me variants (e.g., "mobile car detailing near me")
- Problem-focused (e.g., "how to remove swirl marks from black car")
- Long-tail (e.g., "best ceramic coating installer Las Vegas luxury cars")
- Fleet-specific (e.g., "dealer prep contractor Las Vegas")

---

## SECTION 6: Fleet/Dealer Outreach Email Sequence (if selected)

Generate a 4-email cold outreach sequence for the fleet target types selected in inputs.

**Email 1 — Introduction (Day 1)**
Subject: [Business Name] — [City] detailing for [dealerships/your fleet/property vehicles]
Body: 150 words. Who we are, what we specialize in, one specific reason we're a fit for their operation. CTA: 15-min call or reply to request a trial service.

**Email 2 — Value add (Day 5)**
Subject: [Quick question about [fleet type] turnaround in [city]]
Body: 100 words. One specific benefit relevant to their fleet type (dealers: same-day reconditioning = faster lot-to-sale; property managers: clean fleet = professional image; delivery fleets: regular schedule = predictable cost). CTA: same.

**Email 3 — Social proof (Day 12)**
Subject: How [similar business type] in [city] cut detailing costs by [X%]
Body: 100 words. Specific outcome metric (actual or plausible estimate). CTA: trial service offer.

**Email 4 — Break-up (Day 21)**
Subject: Last email from [Business Name] — leaving the door open
Body: 50 words. Respectful close. Leave contact info. No pressure.

**CAN-SPAM compliance (include at bottom of each email template):**
[Business Name] | [Address] | [City, State ZIP]
To unsubscribe from future emails: reply with "unsubscribe" in the subject line.

**COMPLIANCE RULES — DO NOT VIOLATE:**

1. **CAN-SPAM Act (15 U.S.C. § 7701):** All commercial emails must include: physical business address, clear sender identification, honest subject line, and a functioning unsubscribe mechanism. Unsubscribe requests must be honored within 10 business days.

2. **LSA "Google Screened/Guaranteed":** Do not use these terms in any external marketing unless actively enrolled and badged by Google. Violation = trademark infringement.

3. **Superlative claims in ads:** "Best," "#1," "top-rated" require citation. Format: "Rated X.X stars on [X] Google reviews." Never unsourced.

4. **Facebook before/after:** Both images must be the same vehicle. Disclose service performed and conditions. "Same vehicle, natural light, 2024" — this is industry standard and FTC-appropriate.

5. **Price in ads:** "Starting at $X" is compliant. "As low as $X" requires a disclosed minimum. "Free" requires genuinely free with no conditions.
```
