# Commission Rate Benchmarks Guide

Use these benchmarks to set competitive commission rates. Rates that are too low won't attract active partners; rates that are too high erode profitability.

---

## Physical Product Commission Rates

### By Category (affiliate/influencer base rates)

| Category | Low | Mid | High | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Beauty & Skincare | 10% | 15–20% | 25% | High competition for creators |
| Health & Supplements | 10% | 15% | 20% | Regulatory claim risk = fewer creators |
| Fashion & Apparel | 8% | 12–15% | 20% | High return rates reduce net commissions |
| Home & Kitchen | 8% | 10–15% | 18% | Lower competition, easier to attract creators |
| Electronics & Gadgets | 5% | 8–10% | 15% | Lower margins limit rates |
| Pet Products | 10% | 15% | 20% | High engagement category |
| Baby & Kids | 8% | 12% | 18% | Trust-critical; good creators are valuable |
| Sports & Fitness | 8% | 12–15% | 20% | High creator interest in category |
| Food & Beverage | 10% | 15% | 25% | Subscription models can afford higher rates |

## Platform-Specific Norms

### TikTok Shop Affiliate
- **Minimum viable rate:** 10–12% (below this, top Shop affiliates won't promote)
- **Competitive rate:** 15–20%
- **Top-seller rates:** 20–25% for high-performing Shop affiliates
- Note: TikTok Shop shows commission rate on the product listing — low rates are visible to affiliates

### Amazon Associates
- **Standard:** 1–10% based on category (platform-set)
- **Brand Referral Bonus:** Brands can earn 5–10% back on external traffic they send to Amazon
- Note: Amazon rates are partially set by Amazon policy; limited flexibility

### Shopify / DTC Affiliate
- **Content creators:** 10–20% per sale
- **Coupon/deal sites:** 5–10% (lower because of cannibalization risk)
- **Customer referral programs:** $10–$25 flat or 10–15% of first order

## Creator Tier Rate Guide

| Creator Tier | Follower Range | Base Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nano | 1K–10K | 12–15% | High engagement, low reach |
| Micro | 10K–100K | 15–20% | Best ROI for most brands |
| Mid-tier | 100K–500K | 18–25% + flat fee | Expect a base + commission hybrid |
| Macro | 500K–1M | 15–20% + $500–$2K flat | Flat fee more common at this tier |
| Mega/Celebrity | >1M | 10–15% + $2K–$20K flat | Commission often supplemental to flat fee |

## Referral Program Benchmarks

| Program Type | Referrer Incentive | Referee Incentive | Industry Norm |
|---|---|---|---|
| Store credit | $5–$15 credit | 10–15% off | DTC standard |
| Cash back | $5–$20 cash | $5–$10 cash | Works well for repeat-purchase categories |
| Free product | 1 free unit at threshold | Free sample | Good for consumables |
| Percentage | 5–10% of referred order | 10–15% off first order | Simple, scales with AOV |

---

## Cannibalization Risk Guide

| Channel Type | Cannibalization Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Coupon/deal sites | High — capture existing intent | Lower rate, exclude from program, or new-customer only |
| Loyalty/cashback sites | Medium | Attribution window of 24–48h only |
| Content creators (reviews/tutorials) | Low — genuine discovery | Reward with full rate + bonuses |
| Customer referrals | Low if new-customer only | Restrict to new customer first purchase |
| Retargeting affiliates | High — show ads to your own customers | Exclude retargeting partners or set new-customer only |
