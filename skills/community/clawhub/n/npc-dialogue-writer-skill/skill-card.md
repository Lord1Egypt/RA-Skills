Risk: Users may paste secrets or sensitive personal data into prompts while requesting dialogue guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for goals, constraints, and owned notes only; avoid passwords, OTPs, private keys, wallets, seeds, session secrets, and unnecessary personal data. <br>

Mitigation: Treat the donation link as optional and separate from using or evaluating the skill. <br>
