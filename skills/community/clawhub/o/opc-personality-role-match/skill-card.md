## Description: <br>
This Chinese-language skill assesses OPC founders with MBTI, DISC, Enneagram, Holland, and Big Five models, then maps results to role-fit and MVP team-composition guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External OPC founders, independent builders, and team coaches use this skill to explore personality strengths, role fit, and 2- to 4-person MVP team combinations. It produces advisory reports and collaboration guidance for personal planning and team formation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personality and role-fit reports may be misleading if treated as definitive decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the reports as advisory input only; do not rely on them as the sole basis for hiring, exclusion, or other high-impact decisions. <br>
Risk: The workflow may involve personal personality or team information. <br>
Mitigation: Get consent before entering information about other people and avoid unnecessary sensitive personal details. <br>
