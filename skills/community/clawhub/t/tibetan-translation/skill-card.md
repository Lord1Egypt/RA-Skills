## Description: <br>
Expert translation methodology and best practices for English-Tibetan bilingual translation, including Classical and Colloquial Tibetan, honorifics, Buddhist and religious terminology, cultural nuance, and script conventions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TenzDelek](https://clawhub.ai/user/TenzDelek) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, translators, writers, and agents use this skill to translate between English and Tibetan while choosing the right register, script conventions, terminology, and review workflow for the text. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Translations may be inaccurate, use the wrong register, or mishandle culturally sensitive or religious terminology. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for accuracy, register, cultural sensitivity, and religious terminology before use, especially for formal or Buddhist texts. <br>
Risk: The output may not match user expectations for Tibetan Unicode, Wylie transliteration, modern mixed-script usage, or punctuation style. <br>
Mitigation: Specify script, transliteration, mixed-script, and punctuation preferences before translation and verify the rendered output. <br>
