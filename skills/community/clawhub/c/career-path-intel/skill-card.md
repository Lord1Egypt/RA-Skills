## Description: <br>
Career Path & Education Intelligence helps agents produce career plans, skills gap analyses, salary benchmarks, education ROI comparisons, transition roadmaps, and lifelong learning plans using public labor market and education sources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, career advisors, and education planners use this skill to compare career options, plan role transitions, assess skills gaps, benchmark compensation, and evaluate education investments. It is suited to individualized career planning where salary, hiring, and education ROI outputs are treated as planning estimates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad career-related phrases may activate the skill more often than expected. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's career-planning intent and scope before producing detailed recommendations. <br>
Risk: Salary, job-market, and education ROI outputs are planning estimates. <br>
Mitigation: Check outputs against current authoritative sources before making major financial, education, or career decisions. <br>
Risk: Labor market data can become stale quickly. <br>
Mitigation: Flag salary and job-market data older than six months and prefer current source data where available. <br>
