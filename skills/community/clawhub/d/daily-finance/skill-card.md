## Description: <br>
Daily Finance fetches and summarizes daily finance hotlists and investment news from public finance and business sources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[foxxc](https://clawhub.ai/user/foxxc) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and market-news readers use this skill to collect a daily finance digest from public hotlists and news streams. It helps agents identify current investment-related topics without providing real-time chart data or investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Finance news summaries may be mistaken for investment advice or complete market data. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as informational context only and verify decisions against authoritative market data or qualified advice. <br>
Risk: Optional akshare-based extensions introduce separate dependency and data-source trust considerations. <br>
Mitigation: Review akshare and the akshare-stock skill separately before installing or relying on those optional extensions. <br>
