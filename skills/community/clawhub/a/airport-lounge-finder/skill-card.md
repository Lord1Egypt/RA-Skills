## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to look up airport lounge locations, access conditions, facilities, ratings, and credit-card or lounge-program benefits before a trip. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lounge access rules, credit-card benefits, and program allowances may change after the bundled data was published. <br>
Mitigation: Use the results as reference information and confirm current policies with the airport, lounge program, airline, or bank before travel. <br>
Risk: The skill cannot book lounge access, report real-time crowding, or guarantee coverage for every small airport or newly opened lounge. <br>
Mitigation: Treat missing or operational details as prompts for direct verification with the lounge or travel provider. <br>
