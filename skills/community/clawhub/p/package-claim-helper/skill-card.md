## Description: <br>
One-stop AI courier claim assistant for lost, damaged, or disputed packages: legal analysis, compensation calculation, escalation paths, and document generation for all major China express companies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External consumers use this skill to analyze courier disputes in China, estimate compensation, choose escalation paths, and draft claim documents, communication scripts, evidence checklists, and deadline reminders for lost, damaged, or disputed packages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may draft complaint documents using personal package and contact details. <br>
Mitigation: Keep submissions user-controlled and provide only the personal data needed for the user's chosen document. <br>
Risk: Legal references, complaint windows, and deadlines may change or vary by courier, platform, or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current rules, deadlines, and cited legal references before relying on generated guidance. <br>
Risk: High-value, injury-related, or court-filed disputes may require professional legal judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Consult a licensed lawyer before using generated documents for those disputes. <br>
