## Description: <br>
Creates Xiaohongshu-style visual content by reading a material library, generating seven vertical HTML pages, running two rounds of AI review, and delivering final images after user confirmation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators and marketing agents use this skill to turn a requested topic and available source material into Xiaohongshu-ready carousel images with HTML layouts, screenshots, review iterations, and final delivery. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated screenshots may be sent through Feishu to a hard-coded recipient without clear user control. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing, replace or remove the hard-coded recipient and local paths, and require explicit confirmation before any external send. <br>
Risk: The workflow can move generated content outside a local-only process. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer local screenshot review unless the destination is shown to the user and approved for each send. <br>
