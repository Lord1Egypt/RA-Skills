## Description: <br>
Generate structured markdown test reports for online English classes covering review units that span multiple previous units. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[recom273](https://clawhub.ai/user/recom273) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Teachers or education support staff use this skill to turn multi-unit review lesson notes, summary slides, and student performance notes into a structured markdown test report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on a specific local Obsidian/iCloud mission-statement file that may contain personal report-format guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review that file before use, or provide a bundled or user-selected template when personal notes should not be read. <br>
