## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career-focused users use this Chinese-language skill to diagnose resumes, rewrite experience bullets with STAR framing, customize resumes for job descriptions, draft self-introductions and cover letters, and prepare for interviews. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume and interview workflows can involve sensitive personal, employer, or job-search information. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information suitable for the agent or connected service, and remove unnecessary identifiers such as exact addresses, ID numbers, marital status, and confidential company data. <br>
Risk: Resume guidance may overstate achievements if users provide vague or unverifiable details. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated bullets for factual accuracy and keep claims grounded in real responsibilities, outcomes, and evidence. <br>
