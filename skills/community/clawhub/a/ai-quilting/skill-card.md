## Description: <br>
AI Quilting is a Chinese-language quilting guide that helps users learn patchwork styles, tools, materials, classic patterns, quilting techniques, color matching, and quilting history. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Quilting learners, hobbyists, and craft educators use this skill to ask Chinese-language questions about getting started, selecting tools and materials, understanding classic quilt patterns, troubleshooting quilting issues, and planning patchwork projects. Users may also describe their needs or upload fabric photos for personalized craft guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded fabric photos could include private surroundings or identifying details. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only images you are comfortable sharing, and crop or remove personal context before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Quilting guidance could be mistaken for complete safety instruction when using cutters, needles, irons, or sewing machines. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as educational craft guidance and follow tool manuals, manufacturer safety instructions, and local workshop practices. <br>
