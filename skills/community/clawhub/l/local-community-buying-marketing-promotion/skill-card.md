## Description: <br>
This skill helps platform operations and marketing teams plan community group-buying promotions, including campaign calendars, bestseller creation, coupon systems, platform strategy comparisons, and supporting promotion tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External platform operations and marketing managers use this skill to design, compare, and review community group-buying promotion plans, coupons, sales copy, calendars, SOPs, and ROI assumptions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated promotion copy or SOPs may include inaccurate or unauthorized claims about limited stock, sales volume, pricing, discounts, coupon terms, refunds, or account restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated marketing materials and operating procedures against approved campaign terms before use. <br>
Risk: Promotion plans may involve customer data, fraud controls, or account restrictions that are subject to local rules and platform policies. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm data use, anti-fraud handling, and customer-facing restrictions with legal, compliance, and platform operations teams. <br>
