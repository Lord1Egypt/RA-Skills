## Use Case: <br>
Creators, developers, and small business operators use this skill to plan content monetization paths across social posts, newsletters, affiliate links, digital products, and courses. ## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce monetization strategy advice that includes financial claims, platform compliance assumptions, taxes, or business risk. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations critically and verify platform policies, revenue assumptions, tax obligations, and business risks before acting. <br>
Risk: Content monetization platforms and affiliate programs can change rules, fees, eligibility requirements, and commission rates. <br>
Mitigation: Check current terms from each platform or affiliate program before launching or recommending a monetization path. <br>
