## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Checklist guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific live trading system. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against project requirements, market constraints, operational risk controls, and domain expertise before implementation. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases can be ambiguous and may apply the checklist to non-quantitative architecture prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user is asking about quantitative trading system architecture before applying the review. <br>
