## Description: <br>
Provides a five-dimension framework for assessing enterprise OPC transformation potential and planning ontology, skill distillation, and agent deployment work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[golngod](https://clawhub.ai/user/golngod) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
OPC mentors, enterprise transformation consultants, and business engineers use this skill to score an organization's OPC readiness, identify fit boundaries, and design a staged path from enterprise diagnosis to ontology extraction, skill distillation, and agent deployment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Business transformation recommendations could be applied directly to real enterprise workflows without adequate approval. <br>
Mitigation: Define human approval gates for automatic triggers and review recommendations before using them in live enterprise automations. <br>
Risk: Assessments may involve customer, process, or operational data from the evaluated organization. <br>
Mitigation: Minimize collected data, protect customer information, and set retention limits before using the framework with real organizations. <br>
Risk: License metadata and document terms may need reconciliation before redistribution or commercial reliance. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the authoritative license and terms of use before deployment, redistribution, or customer-facing use. <br>
