## Description: <br>
Provides Chan Theory technical-analysis reference guidance for identifying trend structures, central zones, divergences, and buy/sell point categories in A-share chart analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[danpian1](https://clawhub.ai/user/danpian1) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts can use this skill as a reference aid for applying Chan Theory concepts to stock-chart analysis workflows. It helps organize fractals, strokes, segments, central zones, divergence checks, and three classes of buy/sell points. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
