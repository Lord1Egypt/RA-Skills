## Description: <br>
SEO Content Optimizer analyzes content for keyword use, titles, metadata, structure, internal links, readability, and technical SEO, then returns prioritized optimization guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External content creators, marketers, and site owners use this skill to review blogs, product pages, landing pages, knowledge-base articles, news releases, and platform-specific content for SEO improvements. It helps produce a scored report, specific rewrite suggestions, and an action plan while preserving readability and user experience. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations can lead to inaccurate public claims about rankings, click-through rates, conversion lifts, testimonials, or experience if copied without validation. <br>
Mitigation: Review all public-facing suggestions before publishing and keep only claims that are truthful, supportable, and appropriate for the content owner. <br>
Risk: Optimization advice can overemphasize search mechanics and reduce readability or user experience. <br>
Mitigation: Apply recommendations selectively, preserve natural keyword usage, and reject changes that make the content less clear for readers. <br>
