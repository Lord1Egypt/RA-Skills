## Description: <br>
自学教练 helps teachers turn course materials and exam questions into single-file HTML study guides and interactive quiz apps for self-study. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Teachers and education-focused agents use this skill to convert lectures, notes, outlines, and question banks into local HTML study resources and practice tools they can review and share with learners. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated study pages or quizzes may contain incorrect explanations, questions, or answer keys. <br>
Mitigation: Teachers should review generated pages, answers, and explanations before sharing them with students. <br>
Risk: Quiz progress saved in browser localStorage can remain on a student's device or a shared computer. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid putting private student data into shared files, use export/import deliberately, and clear browser data on shared machines when needed. <br>
Risk: Learning-guide pages may rely on CDN assets, which can affect offline use or environments with restricted network access. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML before distribution and bundle or replace external assets when offline or controlled deployment is required. <br>
