### FAQ Section

**Q: When does SnapFlow launch?**  
A: We're launching publicly on May 15, 2026. Join the waitlist now to get early access, a 50% discount for your first year, and priority onboarding with the founding team.

**Q: Is this free? What does it cost?**  
A: SnapFlow offers a free plan with 3 active workflows. The Pro plan (unlimited workflows + priority support) is $29/month for early-access users — locked in for life. Regular pricing will be $59/month after May 15.

**Q: How is this different from Zapier?**  
A: Zapier was built for technical users. SnapFlow was built for operations managers — people who know what they want automated but don't want to debug API connections. Our templates, plain-English workflow builder, and zero-jargon interface make the difference.

**Q: Can I trust you with my business data?**  
A: Yes. SnapFlow uses OAuth for all app connections (we never see your passwords), and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're SOC 2 compliant and have a public security page at snapflow.io/security.

**Q: What apps does SnapFlow connect to?**  
A: We launch with 50 native integrations including Google Workspace, Shopify, Slack, QuickBooks, HubSpot, Notion, Airtable, and Typeform. The full integration list is at snapflow.io/integrations. New integrations ship every two weeks based on user requests.
