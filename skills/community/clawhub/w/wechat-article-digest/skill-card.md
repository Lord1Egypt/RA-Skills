## Description: <br>
Fetches WeChat public account articles, summarizes and categorizes them, and can generate a daily reading digest. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[milesnee](https://clawhub.ai/user/milesnee) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Readers who save WeChat public account articles use this skill to fetch article content, produce short summaries, classify articles by topic, and compile a same-day reading digest. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill fetches WeChat article pages and stores local article links and summaries in dated memory files. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for reading material where local retention is acceptable, and periodically review or delete memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md files. <br>
