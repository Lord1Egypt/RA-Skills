## Core Function of the Conclusion

The conclusion delivers the final take-home message of the study. It is not merely a repetition of the abstract, introduction, results, or discussion.

A strong conclusion should answer:

- What has this study done?
- What has this study established or demonstrated?
- What are the most important findings?
- Why do those findings matter?
- What contribution does the study make to knowledge, practice, or a specific field?
- What limitations remain?
- What future work or application direction follows from the study?

For a thesis, the conclusion should close the entire project. It should make clear what the reader can responsibly take away from the whole paper.

## Evidence Boundaries

Never invent:

- Research findings, statistical results, themes, cases, or experimental outcomes
- Sample size, instruments, reliability, validity, or analysis details
- Applications or practical value unsupported by the findings
- Future directions unrelated to the study
- Citations, author names, publication years, or literature claims
- Effectiveness claims when no intervention or evaluation was implemented

If the user has not supplied findings, use placeholders:

> 本研究的主要结论应根据第四章结果填写。当前尚未提供具体研究结果，因此此处不能直接写出实质性结论。建议补充：研究问题、主要发现、是否支持假设、研究贡献和局限。

English:

> The substantive conclusions should be based on the reported findings. Since the actual findings have not been provided, this section should be completed after adding the research questions, main findings, supported or unsupported hypotheses, contribution, and limitations.

## Conclusion Placement and Length

The conclusion may appear in different formats:

- A separate short "Conclusion" section of one to three paragraphs.
- The last one or two paragraphs of the discussion section.
- A separate conclusion chapter in a thesis or dissertation.
- A broader final chapter that includes conclusion, limitations, implications, and future research.

For article-style papers, conclusions are often brief. For undergraduate theses and dissertations, the conclusion may be longer and may use subsections.

Recommended undergraduate Chinese length:

- Short paper: 400-800 Chinese characters.
- Full undergraduate thesis: 800-1500 Chinese characters, depending on school format.
- If "不足与展望" is included, add a separate concise subsection.

Follow the user's school format when supplied.

## Generic Conclusion Model

Use these components selectively. Not every conclusion needs all of them.

1. What is in the paper
2. What the paper/study has achieved
3. Relevant background information
4. The gap, aim, or need for the study
5. The method or approach
6. Key results with evaluative comments
7. Implications of the results
8. Potential or actual limitations
9. Potential or actual applications
10. How the study advances knowledge
11. Potential future directions for research

For most thesis conclusions, the strongest order is:

1. Briefly restate the aim or problem.
2. State the main conclusions by research question or finding.
3. Explain the contribution or significance.
4. State limitations honestly.
5. Point to future research or application.
6. End with a final take-home message.


### Article-Style Short Conclusion

```markdown
Conclusion

This study has .... The findings show that .... These results suggest .... Although ..., future research should ....
```

### Conclusion with Recommendations

```markdown
结论与建议

二、实践建议
三、研究不足与展望
```

Use "建议" only when the paper's findings support practical recommendations.

## Writing from a Complete Thesis

Do not use the abstract as the primary source for the conclusion. Extract the conclusion from the full thesis:

- Introduction: research problem, aim, gap, significance.
- Literature review: theoretical frame and unresolved issue.
- Methodology: approach used, only briefly.
- Results: main findings and answers to research questions.
- Discussion: interpretation, contribution, implications, limitations.
- Conclusion chapter requirements: headings and expected length.

Use this extraction table internally:

| Thesis Element | Use in Conclusion |
|---|---|
| Research aim | Opening sentence |
| Research questions/hypotheses | Main conclusion order |
| Key findings | Core conclusion claims |
| Theory/literature gap | Contribution statement |
| Method | One brief phrase only |
| Limitations | Limitation subsection |
| Future research | Final outlook |

Avoid:

- Long method descriptions.
- Detailed statistical reporting.
- Repeating literature review paragraphs.
- Copying discussion paragraphs without synthesis.
- Adding new citations unless the user specifically requests a literature-linked conclusion.

## Research-Type Patterns

### Quantitative Study

Conclusion should answer each research question or hypothesis.

Template:

> 本研究结果表明，……与……之间存在/不存在……关系，说明……。同时，……能够/不能显著预测……，表明……。因此，本文认为，在……样本中，……是理解……的重要因素。

Avoid causal wording unless the design supports causality.

### Qualitative Study

Conclusion should synthesize themes and meaning, not claim statistical representativeness.

Template:

> 通过对……资料的分析，本文发现……群体在……方面呈现出……、……和……等主要经验。这些主题共同表明，……。由于本研究采用质性研究方法，结论主要体现为对特定情境下……现象的深入理解，可为相似情境提供参考。

### Mixed Methods Study

Conclusion should integrate numeric and qualitative evidence.

Template:

> 定量结果显示……，质性资料进一步揭示……。两类结果相互补充，说明……。因此，本研究从总体趋势和个体经验两个层面加深了对……的理解。

### Literature Review or Systematic Review

Conclusion should synthesize evidence patterns and gaps.

Template:

> 综合已有研究可以发现，……领域已经形成了关于……的基本认识，但在……方面仍存在不足。本文通过梳理……，归纳出……。未来研究仍需在……方面继续推进。

### Intervention-Scheme Design Without Implementation

Do not claim effectiveness.

Template:

> 本研究基于……理论设计了面向……的心理干预方案。方案围绕……目标设置……环节，具有一定理论依据和实践参考价值。但由于本研究未实际实施干预，也未收集效果评估数据，因此不能得出方案有效性的结论。后续研究可通过……进一步检验方案的可行性与效果。

### Theoretical Analysis Paper

Conclusion should state conceptual clarification, mechanism, framework, or argument contribution.

Template:

> 本文通过对……的理论分析，明确了……的内涵及其与……之间的关系。研究认为，……。这一分析有助于从……角度理解……，并为后续实证研究提供理论基础。

## Contribution Ownership

Make the study's own contribution explicit. Readers may jump directly from the title or abstract to the conclusion, so do not assume they know which claims are yours.

Use explicit sentence openings:

- 本研究发现……
- 本文表明……
- 本研究的主要贡献在于……
- 通过……，本文进一步说明……
- This study shows that ...
- The present thesis demonstrates that ...
- Our findings suggest that ...

Avoid ambiguous sentences that could sound like background information:

Weak:

> 大学生学习倦怠与社会支持密切相关。

Stronger:

> 本研究发现，在本研究样本中，大学生学习倦怠与社会支持呈显著负相关。

Weak:

> 正念训练能够缓解压力。

Stronger when no intervention was implemented:

> 基于文献与理论分析，本文设计的正念训练方案以压力觉察和情绪调节为核心目标；其实际干预效果仍需未来实证研究检验。

## Language and Tense

For Chinese conclusions, use clear academic expressions:

- 本研究围绕……展开
- 研究结果表明……
- 本文主要得出以下结论
- 这一发现说明……
- 总体而言……
- 受……限制，本文结论仍需……
- 未来研究可进一步……

For English conclusions:

- Present Perfect is useful for stating what the study has done: "This study has examined..."
- Present Simple is useful for implications: "The findings suggest..."
- Past Simple can be used for specific methods or results: "Data were collected from..."

Use active and explicit phrasing when possible:

- "This study demonstrates..." is clearer than "It is demonstrated..."
- "本研究发现..." is clearer than "可以发现..."

```markdown

```

### Concise Undergraduate Conclusion

```markdown
综上所述，本研究通过……发现，……。研究结果说明……，并提示……。虽然本研究在……方面仍存在不足，但其结果能够为……提供一定参考。未来可在……基础上进一步深化研究。
```

### Contribution Sentence Bank

- 本研究的价值在于将……与……联系起来，进一步说明了……。
- 本文从……角度补充了已有研究对……的认识。
- 研究结果为……实践提供了经验依据。
- 本研究构建的……方案为后续实证检验提供了基础。
- 本文对……的分析有助于更全面地理解……。

### Limitation and Future Direction Sentence Bank

- 受样本来源和样本规模限制，本文结论的推广仍需谨慎。
- 由于本研究采用横断面设计，尚不能对变量关系作严格因果解释。
- 由于未实施干预，本文不能直接说明方案的实际效果。
- 未来研究可扩大样本范围，并采用纵向或实验设计进一步验证。
- 后续研究可结合访谈、观察或案例资料，进一步揭示……的形成机制。

## Common Problems and Fixes

Problem: The conclusion only repeats the abstract.

Fix: Add explicit conclusion claims, contribution, limitations, and future direction.

Problem: The conclusion restates detailed results.

Fix: Convert detailed results into higher-level answers to research questions.

Problem: The conclusion overstates causality.

Fix: Use "相关", "预测", "关联", "可能", "提示" unless causal design supports stronger wording.

Problem: The conclusion claims broad application from a narrow sample.

Fix: Limit claims to the study population and similar contexts.

Problem: The contribution is vague.

Fix: State exactly what the study adds: population, context, method, theory, application, scheme, or evidence.

Problem: The conclusion introduces new ideas.

Fix: Move new theory, data, or literature to discussion or remove it.

## Revision Checklist

Before finalizing, check:

- Does the conclusion use the same language as the thesis unless otherwise requested?
- Does it state the study's main take-home message?
- Are conclusions derived from actual findings?
- Does it answer the research questions or hypotheses?
- Does it avoid detailed methods and statistics?
- Does it make the study's own contribution explicit?
- Are implications and applications supported by the results?
- Are limitations included when the format requires them?
- Are future directions specific and connected to limitations or findings?
- Does it avoid new data, new citations, and unsupported claims?
- Is the ending clear, concise, and academically appropriate?
