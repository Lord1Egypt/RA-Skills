## Description: <br>
Exec Gilles is a directive executive assistant for a leader with ADHD that manages calendars, tasks, communications, documents, commitments, and career pipeline follow-up. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gillescv](https://clawhub.ai/user/gillescv) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Executives or individual operators use this skill to turn commitments from conversations, Gmail, WhatsApp, Google Drive, Google Calendar, and Google Tasks into concrete next actions, scheduled work blocks, reminders, logs, and follow-ups. It is designed for proactive accountability, ADHD-aware task initiation, transition management, and job-search pipeline tracking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requests broad ongoing access to private communications, calendars, tasks, and files. <br>
Mitigation: Configure the narrowest possible account scopes and restrict scans to selected folders, labels, chats, and file types. <br>
Risk: The skill can direct creation, modification, deletion, and external communication actions. <br>
Mitigation: Require user confirmation for deletes and external messages before execution. <br>
Risk: Operational logs and scans may retain sensitive personal or business information. <br>
Mitigation: Make retained logs inspectable and deletable, and review storage practices before use. <br>
