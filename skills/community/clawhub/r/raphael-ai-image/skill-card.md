## Description: <br>
Guides agents through Raphael AI image generation from text prompts, including style selection, image download, and Discord delivery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[charliedaqin](https://clawhub.ai/user/charliedaqin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and creators use this skill to request Raphael AI image generation from text prompts, choose visual styles or common content templates, and receive the downloaded image in the current Discord channel. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts and generated images are submitted to Raphael AI and shared in the current Discord channel. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential, personal, or proprietary prompts unless the external service and channel are appropriate for that content. <br>
