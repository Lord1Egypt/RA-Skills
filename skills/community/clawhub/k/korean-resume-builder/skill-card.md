## Description: <br>
Builds Korean-style resumes and cover letters tailored to an applicant's target role, work history, skills, education, awards, certifications, and personal statement. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jochoonhwan](https://clawhub.ai/user/jochoonhwan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External job applicants use this skill to draft Korean-market resumes and cover letters from their personal details, target role, experience, skills, education, awards, certifications, and self-introduction material. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process personal resume information, including contact details and employment history. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only information needed for the intended application, avoid unnecessary sensitive identifiers such as national ID numbers, passwords, bank details, or private addresses, and review the final document before sharing it. <br>
Risk: Generated resumes and cover letters may contain inaccurate, outdated, or overstated claims if the supplied information is incomplete or imprecise. <br>
Mitigation: Review all dates, roles, credentials, achievements, and quantitative claims for accuracy before submitting the document. <br>
