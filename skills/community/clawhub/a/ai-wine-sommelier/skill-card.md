## Description: <br>
AI Wine Sommelier provides wine variety, region, tasting, food-pairing, collecting, label-reading, decanting, and service guidance across major wine-producing countries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External wine consumers, WSET students, collectors, and hospitality workers use this skill to choose wines, understand regions and grape varieties, interpret labels, pair food and wine, and plan serving or storage decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Wine collecting, valuation, and investment guidance may be uncertain or time-sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Treat collection or purchase advice as informational and verify pricing, provenance, storage condition, and authenticity with current market sources or qualified professionals before making financial decisions. <br>
Risk: Wine recommendations may be inappropriate for users who avoid alcohol or have health, age, legal, or safety restrictions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only where alcohol guidance is lawful and appropriate, and follow applicable age, health, and responsible-consumption requirements. <br>
Risk: Uploaded wine-label photos may include personal or location information. <br>
Mitigation: Review images before submission and avoid sharing unnecessary personal, address, or purchase information. <br>
