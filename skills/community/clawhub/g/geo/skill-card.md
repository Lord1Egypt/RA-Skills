## Description: <br>
Optimize for AI recommendations so ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity suggest your brand first. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External marketing and growth teams use this skill to audit how AI assistants recommend brands, compare visibility against competitors, and plan content or authority-building work for generative engine optimization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audit prompts may be sent to external AI services during visibility testing. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include secrets, private customer data, confidential strategy, or sensitive prompts when testing external AI services. <br>
Risk: Monthly or sub-agent audit runs can scale external AI queries beyond the user's intent. <br>
Mitigation: Keep recurring and delegated audit runs under explicit user control. <br>
Risk: AI recommendation results can vary by model, access tier, prompt wording, and date. <br>
Mitigation: Record the exact prompt, model, date, response summary, brand position, and competitors for each audit result. <br>
