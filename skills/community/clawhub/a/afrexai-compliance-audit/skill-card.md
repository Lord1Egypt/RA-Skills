## Description: <br>
Generates structured compliance readiness audits with risk-prioritized findings and remediation plans for frameworks including SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOX, and CCPA/CPRA. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, compliance leads, security teams, and developers use this skill to draft internal readiness audits and gap analyses for common security, privacy, healthcare, payment, and financial-control frameworks. Generated reports should support preparation and triage, not replace review by qualified compliance, legal, or security professionals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive business, regulated, personal, payment, healthcare, or security details while requesting compliance analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Provide high-level sanitized descriptions, avoid secrets and regulated records, and have qualified compliance, legal, or security professionals validate final decisions. <br>
Risk: Generated compliance findings may be incomplete or unsuitable for formal audit, certification, or legal determinations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the report as a readiness draft and confirm framework interpretations, evidence requirements, and remediation priorities with appropriate professionals. <br>
