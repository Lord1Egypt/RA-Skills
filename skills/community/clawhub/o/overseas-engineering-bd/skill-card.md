## Use Case: <br>
Business-development, engineering, and sales operations teams use this skill as a domain reference for building overseas engineering BD workflows around East African public tenders, partner directories, project intelligence, outreach preparation, and follow-up tracking. With UTOS available, it provides task catalogs, dependency topology, and output requirements for generating scripts, prompts, CRM configurations, contact lists, briefs, and outreach materials. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The scanner summary reports that the skill enables scraping, contact enrichment, CRM imports, and WhatsApp or email outreach without sufficient consent, privacy, or user-control guardrails. <br>
Mitigation: Use only for authorized business-development work with explicit approval, lawful basis, source review, rate limits, opt-out handling, and platform-policy compliance before any data collection or outreach execution. <br>
Risk: The scanner guidance warns against automatic UTOS installation and automated execution of scraping, enrichment, CRM writes, Google Contacts imports, WhatsApp checks, or outreach. <br>
Mitigation: Require a human approval gate before installing dependencies or running any collection, enrichment, import, contact synchronization, channel verification, or outreach workflow. <br>
Risk: Generated contact lists, partner profiles, and background briefs can contain inaccurate, stale, or sensitive personal and business data. <br>
Mitigation: Review sources, validate extracted fields, minimize stored personal data, document data provenance, and remove or correct records when contacts opt out or data quality is uncertain. <br>
