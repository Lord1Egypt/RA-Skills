## Description: <br>
Generate or search PNG transparent icons for PowerPoint presentations, including Iconify search, SVG-to-PNG conversion, and simple prompt, shape, or text based icon creation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can run local scripts that write generated, downloaded, or converted icon files. <br>
Mitigation: Review output paths and inspect generated assets before using them in presentations or publishing them. <br>
Risk: Icon search and AI-assisted generation workflows can contact external services. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved services for the deployment environment and review downloaded or generated assets before reuse. <br>
