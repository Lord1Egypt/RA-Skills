## Description: <br>
Provides a Chinese-language relationship coaching framework for diagnosing couple relationship patterns, matching communication and action templates, and tracking relationship repair or growth using the TXGA five-step method. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thinkbugs](https://clawhub.ai/user/thinkbugs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to structure couple relationship conversations, identify relationship blockage types and severity, generate dialogue scripts, suggest action plans, and organize follow-up tracking. It should be used as self-reflection and relationship-coaching support, not as professional mental-health, crisis, legal, medical, or domestic-violence care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat relationship coaching, diagnosis-style language, or crisis guidance as a substitute for professional care. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as self-reflection support and direct emergencies, self-harm risk, abuse, coercive control, child safety, sexual trauma or pain, legal issues, and severe mental-health symptoms to emergency services, licensed professionals, or crisis resources. <br>
Risk: Relationship prompts can elicit sensitive personal, sexual, trauma, family, or mental-health details. <br>
Mitigation: Warn users to share only details they are comfortable storing or processing through their agent, and avoid requesting unnecessary identifying information. <br>
Risk: Monitoring, phone-checking, location-reporting, or supervision advice could normalize coercive control if applied without consent. <br>
Mitigation: Require any transparency or accountability practice to be voluntary, mutual, specific, and time-limited, and recommend professional or safety support when control or abuse is present. <br>
