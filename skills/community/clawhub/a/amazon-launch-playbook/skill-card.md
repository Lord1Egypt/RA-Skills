## Description: <br>
Generate a customized 90-day Amazon product launch plan covering category selection, listing optimization, inventory strategy, advertising, and launch KPI tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangm-a3](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangm-a3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Amazon sellers and launch operators use this skill to turn a product category and short product description into a 90-day launch plan with listing, FBA inventory, ad campaign, review, and KPI checkpoints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The launch plan includes business-spending guidance for ads, inventory, promotions, and review programs without checking live Amazon account state or current platform requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Manually verify Amazon policy requirements, Seller Central settings, ad budgets, FBA inventory, Vine enrollment, review-request compliance, and any checklist item marked complete before acting on the output. <br>
