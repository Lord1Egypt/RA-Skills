## Description: <br>
Conduct a structured operational audit to identify friction points, map workflows, quantify waste, and produce a priority-ranked automation blueprint with ROI projections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[adawodu](https://clawhub.ai/user/adawodu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External consultants, operations teams, and pre-sales users use this skill to turn transcripts, workflow descriptions, and operational pain points into a client-ready automation blueprint with friction scoring, waste quantification, ROI modeling, and an implementation roadmap. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided transcripts or process descriptions may contain passwords, tokens, or sensitive operational details. <br>
Mitigation: Use redacted inputs, avoid pasting credentials, and remove unnecessary sensitive data before invoking the skill. <br>
Risk: Generated lists of accounts, access needs, contacts, or automation actions may be mistaken for approval to make operational changes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those lists as planning checklists and require explicit human approval before granting access or implementing automations. <br>
Risk: ROI and waste calculations can be misleading when source data is incomplete or based on rough estimates. <br>
Mitigation: Review assumptions, validate rates and frequencies with stakeholders, and keep estimates conservative before sharing with decision-makers. <br>
