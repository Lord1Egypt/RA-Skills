## Description: <br>
Maintains persistent focus on active work across compactions and session restarts by guiding agents to read and update concise workspace focus files. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[crewhaus](https://clawhub.ai/user/crewhaus) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to preserve active project context, resume after compaction or session restart, and avoid losing track of multi-step work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Workspace focus files may capture sensitive project context in shared or sensitive repositories. <br>
Mitigation: Review FOCUS.md and FOCUS-LOG.md before sharing or committing them, and avoid storing secrets, credentials, or confidential details in those files. <br>
