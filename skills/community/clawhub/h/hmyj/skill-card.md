## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prices, store details, business terms, contact numbers, and QR codes may become stale or differ by location. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the relevant details with an official 华目眼镜 store, current promotion page, or publisher-approved contact before purchasing or entering a cooperation agreement. <br>
Risk: Web-search-based eyewear advice may be general information rather than official brand guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Label internet-derived eyewear information as reference-only and direct users to a qualified optometry professional for personal prescriptions or medical concerns. <br>
