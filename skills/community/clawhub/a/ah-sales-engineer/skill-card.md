## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may guide work involving customer data, environment provisioning, data migration, role changes, or security testing. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user approval before making operational changes, handling sensitive customer data, changing access roles, or running security tests. <br>
Risk: Sales engineering outputs can be inaccurate or overstate product fit if prospect requirements are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Validate requirements, assumptions, security claims, and proof-of-concept results with qualified technical reviewers before customer delivery. <br>
