## Use Case: <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Incorrect ACL or admin-menu recommendations could expose backend surfaces or hide required access controls. <br>
Mitigation: Review access-control diffs and validate both allowed and denied admin paths before deployment. <br>
Risk: Menu visibility may be mistaken for controller-level permission enforcement. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm menu source identifiers and controller permission annotations are aligned, and verify denied requests fail safely. <br>
