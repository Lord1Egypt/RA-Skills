## Description: <br>
A Chinese persona and advice skill that applies the documented Tong Jincheng perspective to relationship questions, social dynamics, and personal growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dongsheng123132](https://clawhub.ai/user/dongsheng123132) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to get stylized Chinese relationship, interpersonal, and self-development guidance from a disclosed simulated Tong Jincheng-inspired viewpoint. It is best suited for perspective-taking and conversational advice, not for professional counseling, commercial strategy, or claims about the real person's current views. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may answer in a strong stylized roleplay voice that users could mistake for the real person's current opinion. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as a disclosed simulation and avoid treating them as statements from the real person. <br>
Risk: Relationship and interpersonal guidance may be over-applied to high-stakes personal, legal, medical, or financial situations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output for perspective-taking only and seek qualified professional advice for high-stakes decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact README includes an external npx install command. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the external npx source before using that install command outside ClawHub. <br>
