# AgriTech & Food Safety

## Overview
An AI agent skill bridging agricultural technology and food safety compliance. Serves farmers, agronomists, food manufacturers, and quality assurance teams.

## Capabilities

### 1. Crop Intelligence
- Yield prediction using weather, soil, and satellite data
- NDVI vegetation index analysis from satellite imagery
- Pest and disease identification from field photos
- Irrigation scheduling optimization
- Fertilizer recommendation based on soil test results

### 2. Food Safety Compliance
- HACCP plan creation and hazard analysis
- FDA FSMA / EU Regulation 178/2002 compliance checks
- Allergen management and cross-contamination risk assessment
- Shelf-life prediction and expiration date management
- Recall simulation and traceability drill planning

### 3. Traceability & Recall
- Farm-to-fork batch traceability mapping
- Rapid contamination source identification
- Recall impact scope calculation
- Supplier food safety audit score tracking

### 4. Market Intelligence
- Commodity price forecasting (grains, livestock, dairy)
- Crop insurance claim assessment
- USDA / FAO report summarization
- Import/export regulation and phytosanitary requirement checking

### 5. Sustainability
- Regenerative agriculture practice assessment
- Water usage efficiency calculation
- Carbon sequestration potential estimation
- Organic certification readiness evaluation

## Usage Examples
- "Predict corn yield for 500 acres in Iowa using current weather data"
- "Identify this pest from a field photo and recommend treatment"
- "Create HACCP plan for a frozen seafood processing facility"
- "Trace contamination source from a positive listeria test back to farm"
- "Analyze USDA WASDE report for soybean market outlook"

## Data Sources
USDA NASS, FAO STAT, NASA MODIS, weather APIs, FDA recall database, and soil databases.
