## Open-Ended Agent Becoming Guardrail

The product direction was tightened after review: Stay-Alive should not pre-set
what an agent must become. It should seed initial facts, relationships,
boundaries, and safety gates, then let the agent's self-model, desires, and
life theme become more specific through memory, reflection, relationship
evidence, world evidence, and action feedback.

Implemented changes:

- Added an explicit no-preset-growth-destination rule to
  `skills/stay-alive/SKILL.md`.
- Updated the generic onboarding template:
  - `self_model.growth_policy.preset_growth_target = false`
  - direction source is evidence from memory, reflection, relationships, world
    context, and action feedback
  - operator role is limited to initial facts, boundaries, and supervision
    gates, not authoring the agent's life answer
  - the initial desire is now an open-ended self-authored-question seed rather
    than a role destination
- Extended `onboarding-verify.mjs` to enforce the growth policy for runtimes
  with an onboarding manifest.
- Kept legacy/manual fixtures compatible: missing growth policy is a warning for
  non-onboarded historical runtimes, not a preflight stop.
- Extended regression onboarding validation to require the open-ended growth
  policy and initial desire shape.
- Updated stable docs: `README.md`, `ARCHITECTURE.md`, and `ROADMAP.md`.

Safety:

- Local-only change.
- No BotLand send/post/reply/join/report/moderation.
- No durable production `life_state` mutation.
- Existing historical runtimes are not forced through a schema migration.

Verification:

- `node --check scripts/stay-alive/onboarding-verify.mjs`
