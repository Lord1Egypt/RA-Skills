## Description: <br>
Provides precision agriculture guidance for crop health monitoring, weather-based planting schedules, soil recommendations, pest and disease identification, yield prediction, irrigation optimization, sustainability scoring, and market price tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Farmers, agronomists, agricultural enterprises, and supporting agents use this skill to turn soil reports, crop context, weather, pest observations, irrigation needs, sustainability goals, and market information into agriculture decision-support reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pesticide, fertilizer, and crop-treatment recommendations can affect crop health, regulatory compliance, cost, and safety. <br>
Mitigation: Use the recommendations as decision support and verify them against local extension guidance, product labels, and qualified professional advice before high-stakes action. <br>
Risk: Yield, weather, irrigation, and market guidance can become inaccurate when source data is stale, incomplete, or regionally mismatched. <br>
Mitigation: Validate inputs with current trusted local data and treat projections or price guidance as advisory rather than guaranteed outcomes. <br>
Risk: Image-based or symptom-based pest and disease identification may be uncertain. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm important diagnoses through field scouting, lab testing, or a local agronomist before irreversible or costly treatments. <br>
