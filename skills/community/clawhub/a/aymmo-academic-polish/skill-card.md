## Description: <br>
学术写作润色 helps agents polish Chinese and English academic writing, review argument logic, and build critical humanities and social-science framing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aymmo](https://clawhub.ai/user/aymmo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, editors, and agents use this skill to polish academic prose, reduce AI-like phrasing, review argument logic, adapt English academic style, and construct critical frameworks for humanities and social-science writing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional checklist script can read a local file path provided by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Only run the checklist on documents the user intends to analyze, and avoid pointing it at unrelated or sensitive files. <br>
Risk: Academic rewriting and critique can change emphasis, nuance, or argument strength. <br>
Mitigation: Review revised passages against the source text before using them in academic submissions. <br>
