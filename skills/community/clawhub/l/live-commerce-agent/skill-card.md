## Description: <br>
Supports end-to-end live commerce operations, including product planning, livestream scripts, host training, data analysis, and fan operations for ecommerce teams and MCNs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce teams, MCNs, livestream sellers, and cross-border commerce teams use this skill to plan livestreams, draft selling scripts, guide host interactions, review funnel metrics, and structure fan-retention workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sales scripts and urgency prompts may become inaccurate, misleading, or platform-noncompliant if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated scripts against product facts, platform rules, advertising requirements, and brand policy before use. <br>
Risk: Performance benchmarks and funnel recommendations may not fit the seller's category, audience, or current platform data. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current commerce dashboards and business data before changing campaign plans. <br>
