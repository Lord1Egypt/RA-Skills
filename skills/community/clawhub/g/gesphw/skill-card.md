## Use Case: <br>
Teachers, tutors, and learners use this skill to generate GESP-style homework from specified knowledge points, including multiple-choice, true/false, programming questions, and an answer file. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad Chinese education prompts such as 出题 or 练习卷 may invoke this skill when the user intended a different worksheet generator. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants a GESP-style homework sheet before relying on the generated output. <br>
Risk: Generated homework questions or answers may be incorrect or not match the requested knowledge points. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated worksheet and answer content before assigning or publishing it. <br>
