## Engagement Rate Benchmarks (Healthy Ranges)

| Platform | Nano (1-10K) | Micro (10-50K) | Mid-tier (50-100K) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram | 4-8% | 2.5-5% | 1.5-3.5% |
| TikTok | 6-12% | 4-9% | 3-7% |
| YouTube | 5-10% | 3-7% | 2-5% |

Below these ranges suggests low audience interest or algorithmic suppression. Above suggests highly engaged niche audience or giveaway inflation.

## Content Usage Rights Pricing

| Usage Type | Typical Add-On |
|---|---|
| Organic social repurpose (brand's channel) | +25-50% of base rate |
| Paid social ads (whitelisting/Spark Ads) | +50-100% of base rate |
| Website and email usage | +25-50% of base rate |
| Full buyout (all channels, unlimited duration) | +100-200% of base rate |

Duration matters: 30-day usage is standard at base add-on. 90 days typically 1.5x the add-on. Perpetual rights 2-3x.

## Negotiation Leverage Points
- Multi-post packages: 15-25% discount vs. individual posts
- Long-term partnerships (3+ months): 20-30% discount
- Product-only compensation: Viable for nano influencers, rarely for micro+
- Affiliate/commission model: 15-25% commission in lieu of or in addition to flat fee
- Performance bonuses: $5-25 per tracked sale above baseline
- Content exclusivity: Brands pay 30-50% more for category exclusivity windows
