## Description: <br>
Smart Leaner is a personal learning assistant that explains concepts, creates diagrams and examples, archives study notes, tracks mastery, quizzes understanding, and schedules reviews. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[HeXavi8](https://clawhub.ai/user/HeXavi8) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, students, and professionals use this skill to learn unfamiliar topics, calibrate explanations to their current knowledge, retain concepts through notes and spaced review, and test understanding with quizzes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores local notes, quiz results, review plans, and learning preferences under smart-learner/. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only where local learning records are acceptable, and avoid sensitive documents unless study notes derived from them are acceptable. <br>
Risk: Learning requests may be interpreted as progress-tracking sessions rather than one-off answers. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording when a one-off answer is desired without saving progress. <br>
