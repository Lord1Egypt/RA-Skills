## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: QR codes can expose sensitive content, including private WiFi, payment, contact, or destination details. <br>
Mitigation: Review encoded data before publishing and avoid including private details unless the use case requires them. <br>
Risk: Short links, dynamic QR destinations, UTM parameters, or scan analytics may track users. <br>
Mitigation: Provide notice when using analytics or tracked links, and verify that destinations are appropriate before print or deployment. <br>
Risk: Poor contrast, undersized codes, over-customization, or dense content can make a QR code unreliable. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the sizing, contrast, error-correction, and multi-device testing guidance before production use. <br>
