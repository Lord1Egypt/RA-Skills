## Use Case: <br>
External users and ceramic artists use this skill to get pottery planning guidance, troubleshoot forming and glazing issues, compare firing approaches, and plan studio or workshop activities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pottery firing guidance can involve open flame, high heat, smoke, fumes, and local fire-code requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Use trained supervision or a community studio where possible, verify local regulations, maintain fire controls, and ensure strong ventilation before attempting firing work. <br>
Risk: Glaze, clay, and firing decisions can affect toxic-contaminant exposure and food safety. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on the skill alone for food-safety or contaminant decisions; confirm materials, glaze chemistry, and firing practices with qualified ceramic safety resources. <br>
