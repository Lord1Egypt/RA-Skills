## Description: <br>
Generates content in a user's brand voice by reading a local brand profile, matching relevant trend reports, and drafting posts, threads, newsletters, articles, and video scripts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ryudi84](https://clawhub.ai/user/ryudi84) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and content teams use this skill to turn brand voice profiles and trend reports into draft social posts, newsletters, articles, and video scripts for review and scheduling. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local brand voice profiles, writing examples, or trend reports may contain private, client-sensitive, or strategic information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep secrets, private client data, and sensitive account information out of those files before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Draft content may be inaccurate, off-brand, or unsuitable for publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated drafts before publishing or scheduling them. <br>
