## Description: <br>
Generates standardized Chinese after-sales customer service replies for return or exchange requests, logistics exceptions, and compensation negotiations in e-commerce contexts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[terrycarter1985](https://clawhub.ai/user/terrycarter1985) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer support teams use this skill to draft policy-aligned after-sales replies for shoppers requesting returns or exchanges, reporting logistics issues, or negotiating compensation. It helps keep empathy, fact confirmation, process steps, benefits reminders, privacy masking, and policy limits consistent across support responses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated replies may make inaccurate commitments if order status, logistics status, or after-sales policy has not been verified. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm order and logistics facts against the merchant's systems and keep commitments within the current approved after-sales policy before sending. <br>
Risk: Customer replies may expose personal information or include overly specific private order details. <br>
Mitigation: Mask personal data such as phone numbers and addresses, and review the reply for unnecessary private details before use. <br>
Risk: Unexpected changes to the installed skill instructions could introduce behavior outside the stated customer-service purpose. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the skill page and source files match the stated purpose, and review the artifact before enabling it. <br>
