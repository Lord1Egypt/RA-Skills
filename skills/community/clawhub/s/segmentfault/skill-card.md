## Description: <br>
Access and summarize public SegmentFault Q&A and articles by keyword, extracting titles, tags, metrics, technology stacks, and key technical points. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CodeKungfu](https://clawhub.ai/user/CodeKungfu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and technical researchers use this skill to summarize public SegmentFault Q&A and articles by keyword, extract technical points and interaction metrics, and compare topic or tag trends. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automated use could exceed SegmentFault rules if used for bulk collection or to bypass human checks. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use to lightweight public-page research, control request frequency, and follow SegmentFault rules. <br>
Risk: Summaries and extracted metrics may be incomplete or inaccurate because content is dynamically rendered and agent-generated. <br>
Mitigation: Open pages manually when needed and review generated summaries against source links before relying on them. <br>
