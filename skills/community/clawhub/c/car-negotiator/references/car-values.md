# Car Values & Formulas Reference

## Key Numbers to Research Before Any Deal

| Data Point         | Source                  | Why It Matters                        |
|--------------------|-------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| MSRP               | Window sticker / OEM    | Starting anchor (never pay this)      |
| Invoice Price      | Edmunds TMV / TrueCar   | What dealer paid; real floor          |
| Dealer Holdback    | ~2-3% of MSRP           | Hidden profit; pushes floor lower     |
| Fair Market Value  | KBB / Edmunds           | What others actually pay              |
| Rebates/Incentives | Manufacturer website    | Deduct from cap cost                  |
| Residual %         | Edmunds lease guide     | Higher = better lease deal            |
| Money Factor (MF)  | Edmunds forums / dealer | Multiply x 2400 = APR equivalent      |
| Acquisition Fee    | Lender (not dealer)     | $595–$995 typically; not negotiable   |
| Doc Fee            | State-capped or dealer  | Negotiate or walk                     |

## Lease Formula
Monthly Payment = Depreciation + Finance Charge (+ tax)
- Depreciation = (Adjusted Cap Cost – Residual) / Term
- Finance Charge = (Adjusted Cap Cost + Residual) × Money Factor
- Adjusted Cap Cost = Selling Price – Down Payment – Rebates

## Buy Formula
Monthly Payment = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n – 1]
- P = Loan principal (price + tax – down)
- r = monthly rate (APR / 12)
- n = term in months

## Buy vs. Lease Rule of Thumb
- Lease wins: Drive <12k mi/yr, want new car every 3yr, business use deductions
- Buy wins: Keep car 6+ years, high mileage, build equity, modify vehicle

## Pennsylvania / Eastern US Notes
- PA Sales Tax: 6% (Philadelphia adds 2% = 8%)
- On leases in PA: tax applies to full vehicle price upfront (not monthly)
- NJ: 6.625% tax on monthly payments only
- Registration/title fees: ~$200–$400 PA

## Red Flags / Dealer Tricks
- "What payment are you looking for?" → Never give a payment; negotiate OTD price
- Four-square worksheet → Deflects attention; always focus on one number at a time
- Market adjustment / ADM → Walk unless rare vehicle
- "We can't go lower on price but can help on payment" → They're extending the term
- Spot delivery / yo-yo financing → Don't drive home until financing is finalized in writing
