## Description: <br>
Confession Coach is an AI guide for telling someone you like them without turning the moment into a disaster. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[imwyvern](https://clawhub.ai/user/imwyvern) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to prepare respectful romantic confessions, assess timing and signals, draft low-pressure messages, and plan graceful responses to yes, maybe, or no outcomes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
