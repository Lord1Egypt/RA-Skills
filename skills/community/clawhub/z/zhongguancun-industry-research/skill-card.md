## Description: <br>
Supports Chinese-language industry research for Zhongguancun partnership discussions by producing company profiles, research-direction matching, cooperation entry points, attendee suggestions, and optional meeting agendas. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhangxiaopeng-zgci](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhangxiaopeng-zgci) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Partnership and research staff use this skill to prepare company cooperation research and meeting materials for industry-academic-research engagement. It helps organize public company information, compare it with Zhongguancun research directions, and draft cooperation modes or agendas. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use web or search results when preparing company research, so generated analysis can reflect incomplete, outdated, or unverified public information. <br>
Mitigation: Review important claims against authoritative and current sources before using the research for outreach, meetings, or decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill can activate on broad company-research prompts and may include user-provided material in the resulting analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential internal material unless it is intended to be used in the report; redact sensitive details before prompting. <br>
