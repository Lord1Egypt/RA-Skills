## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to gather requirements, compare May Day holiday destinations in China, draft itineraries, and provide booking, packing, safety, and crowd-avoidance guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel prices, opening hours, ticket rules, weather, and booking availability may change before or during the May Day holiday. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current conditions with official booking, transport, attraction, and weather sources before relying on the itinerary. <br>
Risk: Trip planning can involve personal details such as departure city, dates, budget, and group composition. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for details needed to plan the trip and avoid collecting unnecessary sensitive personal information. <br>
