## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automated lead scraping and follow-up outreach may run without clear consent, opt-out handling, rate limits, or human approval. <br>
Mitigation: Use only lawful, consented outreach with explicit rate limits, unsubscribe and suppression handling, and human approval before scraping, publishing websites, or sending campaigns. <br>
Risk: API keys and sending accounts used for discovery, website generation, or email delivery could be over-scoped or exposed during automation. <br>
Mitigation: Use dedicated low-privilege API keys and sending accounts, keep credentials out of generated content, and rotate or revoke them if exposure is suspected. <br>
