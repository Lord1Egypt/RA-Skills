## Use Case: <br>
External marketers, founders, and app developers use this skill to plan paid directory and marketplace listing campaigns for platforms such as Taaft, Shopify App Store, G2, and Capterra. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Paid advertising details such as pricing, eligibility, credits, and campaign settings may change by platform. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current platform pricing, eligibility, credits, and campaign settings before approving any spend. <br>
Risk: Campaign recommendations could lead to ineffective or unintended ad spend if used without measurement. <br>
Mitigation: Start with controlled budgets, use UTM tagging and conversion tracking, and scale only after reviewing platform analytics. <br>
