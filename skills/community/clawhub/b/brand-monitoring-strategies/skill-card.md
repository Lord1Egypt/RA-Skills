## Description: <br>
Guides ongoing brand monitoring by helping agents plan monitoring for impersonation, trademark infringement, counterfeit products, and other brand abuse. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and brand operators use this skill to create monitoring plans, manual search queries, alert setup guidance, and escalation paths for suspected brand abuse. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use existing project context files to identify brand names, official domains, and key assets. <br>
Mitigation: Review context content before use and include only brand information needed for monitoring. <br>
Risk: Following the guidance can involve public searches or evaluating third-party monitoring vendors. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved brand terms, document findings, and assess vendor coverage, privacy, and commercial terms before adoption. <br>
