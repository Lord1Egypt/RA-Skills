## Description: <br>
Academic Deep Research guides agents through transparent, rigorous research with mandatory two-cycle investigation per theme, APA 7th citations, evidence hierarchy, and user checkpoints. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[marjoriebroad](https://clawhub.ai/user/marjoriebroad) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, analysts, and developers use this skill to plan and execute rigorous web-based literature reviews, competitive intelligence, trend reports, and other multi-source investigations that require visible methodology and reproducible evidence trails. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad activation language and automated research cycles can produce an expansive investigation that exceeds the user's intended scope. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the research question, depth, source preferences, and geography before execution, and require explicit approval of the research plan. <br>
Risk: Research outputs can contain outdated, low-quality, contradictory, or unsupported source material. <br>
Mitigation: Prioritize primary and authoritative sources, document contradictions, assign confidence levels, and include limitations in the final report. <br>
Risk: Sensitive topics may expose private or regulated information through web research, memory use, or parallel sessions. <br>
Mitigation: For sensitive topics, restrict web use, disable memory or parallel sessions when unnecessary, and pause before each theme or source batch. <br>
