## Description: <br>
Generates tailored agency client reports, QBR narratives, health scorecards, and renewal proposals to prove ROI, retain clients, and grow accounts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agency owners, consultants, managed service providers, and account teams use this skill to turn client performance inputs into monthly reports, quarterly business review narratives, internal client health scorecards, and renewal or upsell proposal drafts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Client reports and proposals may include confidential client identifiers, financial details, campaign results, or other sensitive commercial data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved AI providers for confidential data, confirm authorization to process the information, and redact unnecessary identifiers or sensitive details before use. <br>
Risk: Generated ROI summaries, health scores, urgency statements, or renewal recommendations may be inaccurate or overstated. <br>
Mitigation: Review all outputs against source data and business context before sharing them with clients, especially claims about ROI, churn risk, pricing, and deadlines. <br>
