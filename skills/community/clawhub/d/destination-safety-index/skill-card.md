## Description: <br>
查询25+国家旅行安全评级，提供犯罪、恐怖、自然灾害、健康卫生和交通安全等5个维度的评分，并支持多目的地安全排名与对比。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to compare static destination safety ratings, identify major travel risks, and retrieve local safety tips and emergency numbers. It is best used as general planning guidance before checking current official travel advisories. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses static built-in safety ratings that may not reflect current security, health, weather, or political conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as general planning guidance and verify risks with current government, embassy, or official travel advisories before making travel decisions. <br>
Risk: Destination safety recommendations could be over-relied on for high-stakes travel decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for comparison and orientation only, and consult official sources or local authorities for final decisions. <br>
