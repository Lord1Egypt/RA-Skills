## Description: <br>
深圳中考填报全流程助手，帮助深圳初中生和家长查询中考政策、规划志愿填报、参考高中录取分数线，并理解指标生和自主招生路径。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, especially Shenzhen junior high students and parents, use this skill to understand Zhongkao policies, compare school options, plan volunteer preferences, and generate admissions-planning guidance from public reference data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Admissions policies, school plans, and score references can change year to year. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important admissions details with official Shenzhen education sources before making decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill includes a promotional WeChat consulting callout that may invite sharing a child's personal records or contact details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing personal student records or contact details unless the consultant has been independently trusted by the user. <br>
