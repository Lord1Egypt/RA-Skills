## Description: <br>
Provides Lottie and dotLottie adapter patterns for embedding lottie-web JSON animations, .lottie files, and @lottiefiles/dotlottie-web players in HyperFrames. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lucas-kay8](https://clawhub.ai/user/lucas-kay8) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers use this skill to embed and configure lottie-web JSON and dotLottie animations in HyperFrames projects. It provides adapter patterns for registering players, keeping animation containers stable, and validating HyperFrames pages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sample CDN script tags may introduce supply-chain risk if copied directly into production. <br>
Mitigation: Pin versions, vendor dependencies, or review them under the production supply-chain policy before release. <br>
Risk: Unsupported After Effects features may not survive export to Lottie or dotLottie. <br>
Mitigation: Test each JSON or .lottie asset in a browser and run HyperFrames lint and validation before deployment. <br>
