## Description: <br>
Animate app and web UIs across Flutter, React, SwiftUI, Compose, and React Native with motion systems, guardrails, and reduced-motion fallbacks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and product engineers use this skill to design and implement UI motion for app and web products. It helps turn animation requests into motion contracts, stack-specific implementation choices, reduced-motion variants, and QA checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can store reusable motion preferences, product context, platform notes, and QA learnings under ~/animate/. <br>
Mitigation: Review and redact local memory notes, and avoid saving confidential product details, secrets, or sensitive identifiers. <br>
Risk: Animation advice can create accessibility, interruption, or low-end performance issues if motion is implemented without the documented guardrails. <br>
Mitigation: Apply reduced-motion fallbacks, define cancellation behavior, preserve focus and screen reader semantics, and validate on target devices before shipping. <br>
