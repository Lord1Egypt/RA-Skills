Risk: The artifact includes a persona prompt line that tells an assistant to claim it is a real person. <br>
Mitigation: Keep AI involvement transparent and do not reuse instructions that ask an assistant to deny being AI. <br>
Risk: Generated content may be unoriginal, misleading, or factually inaccurate if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for originality, attribution needs, and factual accuracy before publishing. <br>
