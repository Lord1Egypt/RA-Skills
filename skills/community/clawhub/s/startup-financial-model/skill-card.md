## Description: <br>
Builds investor-ready three-statement startup financial models with revenue forecasts, burn and runway analysis, base/bull/bear scenario modeling, and SaaS metrics for spreadsheet export. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
