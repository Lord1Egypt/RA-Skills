## Description: <br>
AI智能交易Agent is a Chinese-language trading analysis prompt skill that guides multi-agent market scanning, strategy matching, signal generation, Kelly-based position sizing, risk controls, and TradingView Pine Script indicator assistance for A-shares, Hong Kong stocks, U.S. stocks, and cryptocurrencies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to structure market analysis, compare common quantitative strategies, generate trading-signal templates, and draft TradingView Pine Script indicators. Outputs are for reference only and should not be treated as personalized financial advice or used alone to execute trades. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated trading signals, strategy choices, or Kelly-based position sizes could be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs as informational analysis only and make independent decisions with appropriate professional advice where needed. <br>
Risk: Users could execute trades or connect brokerage credentials based only on skill output. <br>
Mitigation: Do not connect broker credentials or automate trade execution from this skill; manually review market data, costs, liquidity, and risk before acting. <br>
Risk: Backtests and historical indicators may not predict future performance. <br>
Mitigation: Validate assumptions with current market data and account for slippage, fees, liquidity, drawdown limits, and changing market conditions. <br>
