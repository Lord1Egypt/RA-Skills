This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Developers and engineers use this skill as a quick reference when designing BMS architecture for lithium-ion packs, comparing balancing approaches, estimating SOC/SOH, setting protection thresholds, and reviewing thermal, communication, topology, and safety considerations. <br>
