## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing content could be published, used in replies, tag people, repost third-party material, use customer examples, or synthesize Arthur-style cloned voice without sufficient review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review before any publishing, replies, tagging, reposting, customer-example use, or cloned-voice generation. <br>
Risk: Third-party AI and media tools may receive content provided during image, video, voice, or research workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those tools as external services and avoid submitting sensitive, private, or customer-confidential content unless approved. <br>
