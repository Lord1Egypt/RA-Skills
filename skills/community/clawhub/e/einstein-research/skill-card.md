A complete quantitative market research toolkit for serious traders and investors, including 11 specialized skills covering backtesting, breadth analysis, bubble detection, follow-through day signals, options strategy, portfolio risk, macro regime detection, scenario analysis, and market theme tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market research outputs may be mistaken for financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational inputs, validate them independently, and do not rely on them as the sole basis for investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Installing the full pack adds 11 child skills, including skills that may read portfolio files, CSV data, or produce trading-related recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review each child skill before installation and limit file access to only the data needed for the intended analysis. <br>
