## Description: <br>
Helps users assess how well they understand a target investment by researching public company information, asking a four-question framework, and returning a diagnosis of cognition gaps and study priorities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xbthh](https://clawhub.ai/user/xbthh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Individual investors use this skill as an educational self-check to test whether their understanding of a named company covers its business model, sustainability, growth potential, and shareholder returns before or during an investment review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat the educational investment self-check as financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: State that conclusions should be independently reviewed before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive brokerage, account, portfolio, or private document details during the assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Ask only for the company being assessed and avoid collecting brokerage credentials, account numbers, confidential portfolio details, or private documents. <br>
Risk: Public web research may be incomplete, outdated, or wrong for the company being assessed. <br>
Mitigation: Use disclosed public sources and encourage users to verify conclusions independently. <br>
