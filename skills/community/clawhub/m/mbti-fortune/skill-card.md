## Description: <br>
MBTI Fortune Teller draws a random MBTI type for any question and answers through that type's cognitive function stack. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leilei926524-tech](https://clawhub.ai/user/leilei926524-tech) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for entertainment-oriented MBTI-themed readings about casual questions. It should not be used for consequential medical, legal, financial, workplace, relationship, or safety decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad fortune-telling triggers can produce confident-sounding answers to questions that users may treat as real advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for playful entertainment and avoid relying on its readings for medical, legal, financial, workplace, relationship, safety, or other consequential decisions. <br>
