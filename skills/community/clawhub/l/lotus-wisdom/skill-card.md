## Description: <br>
Lotus Wisdom guides user-paced, step-by-step contemplative problem solving with Buddhist wisdom domains for complex problems, paradoxes, creative blocks, and strategic reflection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[linxule](https://clawhub.ai/user/linxule) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to slow down complex reasoning tasks into deliberate, user-controlled reflective steps. It is suited for multidimensional problems, paradoxes, creative blocks, strategic decisions, philosophical questions, and holistic system design. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may over-apply its contemplative pacing to factual, urgent, or direct operational tasks. <br>
Mitigation: Use a different mode for factual lookups, urgent requests, or tasks where speed and directness matter more than reflection. <br>
Risk: The framework loses value if the agent answers multiple steps at once. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses to one contemplative step and wait for explicit user continuation before proceeding. <br>
