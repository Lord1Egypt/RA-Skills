## Description: <br>
Assists with R&D expense evaluation, budget estimation, project cost analysis, ROI review, and compliance-oriented reporting for software, hardware, AI algorithm, and process technology projects. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Business, finance, R&D, and project management teams use this skill to estimate R&D project budgets, analyze cost composition, review investment return, and draft structured Chinese R&D expense reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Compliance conclusions about R&D expense treatment or tax deduction eligibility may be incomplete for a specific organization or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a draft and have finance, tax, or legal reviewers confirm final compliance positions before filing or relying on them. <br>
Risk: Budget, salary, staffing, and project data used for estimates may be sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Review and minimize sensitive inputs before sharing them with an agent, and follow the organization's data handling rules. <br>
