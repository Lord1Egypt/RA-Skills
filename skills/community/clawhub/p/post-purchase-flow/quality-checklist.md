# Post-Purchase Flow Quality Checklist

Use this checklist to verify completeness and quality of a post-purchase flow design before implementation.

---

## Order Confirmation (8 items)

- [ ] Sent within 60 seconds of order placement
- [ ] Includes complete order details (items, quantities, prices, shipping method)
- [ ] Shows estimated delivery window (not just "3-5 business days" but actual date range)
- [ ] Contains "what happens next" timeline or expectations section
- [ ] Includes brand-voice thank-you message that reduces buyer's remorse
- [ ] Provides direct links to tracking, FAQ, and support contact
- [ ] Contains conditional logic for first-time vs. repeat buyers
- [ ] Renders correctly on mobile devices (single-column layout, tappable buttons)

## Shipping Communications (7 items)

- [ ] Covers all key milestones: shipped, in transit, out for delivery, delivered
- [ ] Includes proactive delay notification template with updated ETA
- [ ] Provides carrier-specific deep tracking links (not just tracking numbers)
- [ ] Has separate flows for domestic and international shipments
- [ ] SMS configured for time-sensitive updates (out for delivery, delivered)
- [ ] Delivery confirmation transitions naturally into post-delivery content
- [ ] Suppresses marketing emails during active shipping period

## Unboxing Experience (6 items)

- [ ] Branded outer packaging consistent with brand identity
- [ ] Insert card with single clear CTA and QR code to tracked landing page
- [ ] Product-specific care or first-use guide included
- [ ] Surprise element appropriate to brand and order value (sample, note, sticker)
- [ ] Return/exchange information accessible but not prominent
- [ ] Social sharing prompt with branded hashtag and incentive

## Review Collection (7 items)

- [ ] Timing varies by product category (not one-size-fits-all)
- [ ] Initial request uses low-friction format (star rating first, then optional text)
- [ ] Follow-up limited to maximum 2 additional touches for non-responders
- [ ] Photo/video review incentive offered (loyalty points or discount)
- [ ] Negative review interception routes low ratings to support before public posting
- [ ] Review syndication configured for key platforms (Google, product pages)
- [ ] Compliant with FTC guidelines and platform review policies

## Cross-Sell & Replenishment (6 items)

- [ ] Replenishment timing based on product consumption cycle data
- [ ] Recommendations personalized based on purchase history and browsing behavior
- [ ] Easy reorder functionality (one-click or pre-filled cart)
- [ ] Subscription conversion offer included for consumable products
- [ ] Win-back sequence configured for customers who miss replenishment window
- [ ] Incentive ladder escalates for non-converters (discount → stronger discount → last chance)

## Channel Integration (5 items)

- [ ] Email and SMS coordinated to avoid duplicate messaging
- [ ] Frequency caps enforced across all channels (max emails per day/week)
- [ ] Transactional messages exempt from marketing frequency caps
- [ ] SMS opt-in confirmed and compliant with TCPA/carrier requirements
- [ ] Push notifications configured for app users (if applicable)

## Suppression & Logic (5 items)

- [ ] Open support tickets suppress marketing communications
- [ ] Active returns/exchanges suppress review requests and cross-sell
- [ ] Cancelled/refunded orders exit the post-purchase flow entirely
- [ ] New orders restart the journey (don't continue old flow)
- [ ] Engagement-based adjustments reduce frequency for unengaged contacts

## Measurement & Optimization (6 items)

- [ ] KPIs defined: repeat purchase rate, WISMO reduction, review rate, post-purchase revenue
- [ ] Cohort tracking implemented to measure journey effectiveness over time
- [ ] A/B testing plan documented for subject lines, timing, incentives, and content
- [ ] Dashboard connects post-purchase engagement to customer lifetime value
- [ ] Monthly or quarterly review cadence established
- [ ] Learnings documented and playbook updated regularly

---

**Total items: 50**
**Minimum passing score: 40/50 (80%)**
**Target score: 45/50 (90%)**
