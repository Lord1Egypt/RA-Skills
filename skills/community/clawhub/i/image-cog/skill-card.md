## Description: <br>
AI image generation and photo editing powered by CellCog for text-to-image, image-to-image, consistent characters, product photography, reference-based generation, style transfer, image sets, social media visuals, brand assets, stickers, comics, and GIFs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and content creators use this skill to ask an agent to generate or edit images through CellCog, including product photography, brand assets, social visuals, character sets, reference-based images, and style-transfer outputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires CELLCOG_API_KEY, which is a sensitive credential. <br>
Mitigation: Use a revocable API key and keep it out of prompts, logs, screenshots, and shared outputs. <br>
Risk: Image requests are routed through CellCog and external model providers. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if CellCog, the cellcog Python package, provider routing, and data-handling terms are acceptable for the intended use. <br>
Risk: Uploaded private photos, confidential brand assets, or regulated data may be submitted to external services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive or regulated content unless the provider terms and data-handling controls meet the user's requirements. <br>
