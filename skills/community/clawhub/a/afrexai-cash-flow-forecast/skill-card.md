## Description: <br>
Builds a 13-week rolling cash flow forecast from current balance, expected income, and expenses, highlighting weeks below a user-defined cash buffer. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, agencies, and business operators use this skill to turn current balances, income timing, recurring expenses, variable costs, and cash buffer targets into a week-by-week cash forecast with risk alerts and suggested actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial forecasts require sensitive business figures, and unnecessary secrets or account identifiers could be exposed to the agent. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the balances, dates, income, expenses, and buffer amounts needed for forecasting; avoid bank logins, account numbers, customer secrets, tax IDs, and other unrelated sensitive details. <br>
