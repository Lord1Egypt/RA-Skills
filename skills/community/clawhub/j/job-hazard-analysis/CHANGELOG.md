## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-24
Initial release. Drafts an OSHA-aligned Job Hazard Analysis (JHA / JSA) for a single job or task — step-by-step hazard identification by category, inherent and residual risk scoring (Severity × Likelihood), controls in strict hierarchy-of-controls order, permit / training / PPE cross-reference, emergency response and stop-work triggers, worker-participation block, and a competent-person / supervisor / EHS sign-off block for review before the work is authorized.
