## Description: <br>
热点追踪专家基于用户输入的主题搜索最新信源、挖掘近期爆点话题，并生成高质量长文内容，适合追踪热点事件、行业动态、人物资讯等持续性话题。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content, marketing, and research teams use this skill to track timely topics, collect and verify sources, identify high-value angles, and produce Chinese-language long-form articles or investor-education topic recommendations. It is especially oriented toward finance, wealth-management, and new-media workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Tracked topics and research prompts may be sent to web or search tools. <br>
Mitigation: Use non-sensitive topics and avoid including confidential, personal, or restricted information in prompts. <br>
Risk: Generated finance-marketing or investor-education content may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for compliance, add appropriate disclaimers, and avoid presenting generated content as individualized advice. <br>
Risk: Scheduled runs can repeatedly monitor and process topics over time. <br>
Mitigation: Limit scheduled use to topics intentionally approved for ongoing monitoring. <br>
Risk: The workflow may create Markdown or DOCX article files. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated files before sharing or publishing them. <br>
