## Description: <br>
识别伴侣格局类型（丫鬟型/妃子型/皇后型），分析行为信号与误判点，提供关系影响评估与决策建议；当用户需要判断伴侣关系模式、分析伴侣是否拖垮或成就自己、制定关系决策时使用 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[thinkbugs](https://clawhub.ai/user/thinkbugs) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill as a structured journaling and analysis aid for identifying relationship behavior patterns, tracking signals over time, and organizing relationship decision considerations. It should be used with concrete behavior examples and not as a substitute for qualified counseling or safety support. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can encourage manipulative relationship tests or secret partner tracking. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only as a subjective journaling aid; avoid manufactured crises, withheld affection or money, and detailed identifiable records without privacy and safety review. <br>
Risk: Rigid scoring may be overused for high-stakes relationship decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as prompts for reflection, and seek qualified human support for abuse, coercion, self-harm threats, legal issues, or urgent safety concerns. <br>
