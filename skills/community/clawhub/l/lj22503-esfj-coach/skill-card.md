## Description: <br>
Guides ESFJ users through growth, career, study, and focus challenges with a coaching mode for reflective questions and an advisor mode for concrete action plans. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lj22503](https://clawhub.ai/user/lj22503) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for MBTI-style ESFJ growth coaching, including reflective dialogue, practical action planning, and follow-up growth records. The skill is intended for personal development support and explicitly does not replace professional mental health care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The artifact mixes ESFJ packaging with INTP coaching material, which can produce mismatched personality guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review the ESFJ and INTP source materials before use and require the publisher to align the packaged skill, README, keywords, and references to the intended personality type. <br>
Risk: The skill asks the agent to keep ongoing personal growth records without clear consent, review, deletion, or retention controls. <br>
Mitigation: Make memory and growth-record storage opt-in, show users what will be saved, and provide clear review, edit, delete, and retention controls before deployment. <br>
Risk: Users could treat MBTI-style coaching as professional mental health, career, or medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the documented disclaimer visible in deployment guidance and route severe psychological distress or high-stakes decisions to qualified professionals. <br>
