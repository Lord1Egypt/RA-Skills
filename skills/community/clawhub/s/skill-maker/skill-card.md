## Use Case: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated or replaced skills can introduce incorrect guidance, unsafe scripts, or unintended overwrite targets if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated SKILL.md, confirm overwrite and target paths, and inspect or scan generated scripts or executable resources before enabling a created skill. <br>
