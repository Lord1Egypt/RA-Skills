## Description: <br>
Find wholesale buyers, retail buyers, importers, procurement targets, and volume-purchase opportunities for ecommerce and trade products. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External business users and commerce operators use this skill to identify and prioritize wholesale buyers, retail buyers, importers, and procurement targets by product, region, buyer type, and likely volume-purchase fit. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated buyer leads may be incorrect, stale, or weakly supported. <br>
Mitigation: Verify supporting signals and buyer relevance before outreach. <br>
Risk: Search inputs may expose confidential customer lists, pricing, supplier data, or other sensitive commercial information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing confidential business data unless it is intentionally needed for the search. <br>
