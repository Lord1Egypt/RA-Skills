## Description: <br>
Full short-form video content creation workflow for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts: analyze a reference viral video URL, reverse-engineer its viral structure using the 7 psychological triggers framework, generate AI image prompts for each scene, create a detailed shooting script with text overlays, build a posting schedule, and produce a monetization roadmap. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators and content teams use this skill to analyze short-form reference videos, generate new video concepts, write scene-by-scene scripts and prompts, and plan posting and monetization workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow may fetch user-provided video URLs and create temporary local media files. <br>
Mitigation: Review URLs before analysis, use trusted installs of yt-dlp and ffmpeg, and remove temporary media files when work is complete. <br>
Risk: Generated concepts and scripts may imitate unsafe, unlawful, or rights-sensitive themes from a reference video. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated content before posting and adapt concepts to comply with platform rules, rights requirements, and applicable law. <br>
