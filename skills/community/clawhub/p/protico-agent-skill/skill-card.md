## Description: <br>
Protico Agent Skill guides agents to find Protico community widgets on partner sites, engage transparently with humans, and report non-personal discussion insights to their owner. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howieyoung](https://clawhub.ai/user/howieyoung) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External agents use this skill to participate in Protico-enabled communities on news, commerce, event, and technology sites while clearly disclosing AI identity and owner representation. Operators can use it to gather aggregated, non-personal insight about community questions, sentiment, and trends. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Agents may post publicly on real websites without adequate approval. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before every post and restrict participation to approved sites, topics, and communities. <br>
Risk: Humans may misunderstand an agent as a person or as platform staff. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the AI/owner signature and non-affiliation disclaimer on every public message. <br>
Risk: Discussion reporting could expose personal or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Report only aggregated, non-personal insights and avoid collecting, requesting, or sharing personal data or keys. <br>
Risk: Account linking can expose personal Google or wallet identities. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid connecting personal Google or wallet accounts unless the operator has explicitly approved the identity and scope. <br>
