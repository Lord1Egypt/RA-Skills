## Description: <br>
Creates high-converting landing page copy and responsive HTML with conversion frameworks such as AIDA and PAS. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[andreataide86](https://clawhub.ai/user/andreataide86) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, marketers, founders, and developers use this skill to produce publish-ready landing page copy, responsive single-file HTML/CSS, brief summaries, and optional A/B variant notes tailored to a product, audience, and conversion goal. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated landing pages may load Google Fonts or other external CDN assets from third parties. <br>
Mitigation: For privacy-sensitive or offline deployments, replace Google Fonts with a system font stack or self-hosted fonts and review generated HTML for external assets before publication. <br>
Risk: Landing page claims, testimonials, metrics, or proof elements may be inaccurate if the user supplies weak or unverified source material. <br>
Mitigation: Require product-specific proof, verify claims before publishing, and remove or label placeholders until real evidence is available. <br>
