## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generic dating advice may not fit a user's personal situation, local norms, or relationship boundaries. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt recommendations for cultural fit, consent, safety, and the user's actual goals before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Dating scenarios can involve sensitive personal details about users or other people. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing real names, exact income, addresses, private contact details, or unnecessary sensitive personal information in prompts or generated content. <br>
