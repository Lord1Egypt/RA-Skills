## Description: <br>
Guides users through planning a rocket mass heater build by researching local requirements, adapting designs to available materials, and producing material lists, safety checklists, build logs, reminders, and performance tracking while humans perform the physical work. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan an efficient wood-based heating project for unreliable grid power, high heating costs, workshops, off-grid homes, or emergency resilience. The agent provides research, build planning, checklists, logs, sourcing guidance, and performance tracking while the user remains responsible for construction and operation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rocket mass heater planning can lead to fire, carbon monoxide, chimney, clearance, permit, or insurance hazards if instructions are followed without local validation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify local fire and building codes, permits, insurance requirements, chimney suitability, required clearances, and carbon monoxide and smoke detector placement before following build-specific instructions. <br>
Risk: Site-specific design or structural decisions may exceed the user's skills, materials, or installation conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Have a licensed HVAC, masonry, or structural professional review the design when appropriate, and stop operation immediately if smoke, unusual heat, draft failure, or a carbon monoxide alarm occurs. <br>
