# Key Principles — The Economics of Kickbacks

> Source: kickbacks.ai product documentation

## Revenue Model

Kickbacks operates a transparent two-sided marketplace:

### Developer Side

- **50% revenue share** — Half of every ad dollar paid by advertisers goes to the developer who saw the ad.
- **Impression revenue** — Each display of an ad pays the developer a fraction of a cent.
- **Click revenue** — Ads are billed at **50× the impression rate** when clicked. A click earns 50× what a passive view earns.
- **Real-time accumulation** — Every spinner spin with an active ad generates income. No batching, no delays.

> **Case: Earnings Estimate** (Product): A heavy Claude Code user (4+ hours of active thinking time per day) could earn roughly $5-40/month depending on advertiser demand, time zone, and click-through rate. This is not a primary income source — it's "spinner micro-earnings."

### Advertiser Side

- **Bid per block** — Each block = 1,000 impressions (5-second spinner views).
- **Minimum bid** — $1.00 per block.
- **Queue priority** — Highest bidder serves first. Your bid doesn't add views — it moves you up the queue.
- **Clicks charged at 50×** — If the ad gets clicked, the impression rate × 50 is charged instead.

### Factors Affecting Your Earnings

| Factor | Impact |
|---|---|
| Daily AI usage | More thinking time = more impressions |
| Time zone | Peak hours (US business hours) have higher advertiser demand |
| Click-through rate | Clicks earn 50× more; worth optimizing |
| Number of machines | Install on multiple machines to multiply impressions |
| Geographic region | US/EU advertisers typically bid higher |
| Advertiser market health | More advertisers = more bids = higher fill rate |

## Best Practices to Maximize Earnings

1. **Stay signed in** — Preview impressions don't earn. Sign in with Google immediately.
2. **Use it on your primary dev machine** — The machine where you spend most of your Claude Code time will generate the most impressions.
3. **Don't chase clicks** — Clicks earn more, but the best strategy is simply using the tools you'd use anyway. The earnings are a bonus, not a target.
4. **Keep the extension updated** — Auto-updates are enabled by default. Ensure they stay on to maintain compatibility with the latest Claude Code versions.
5. **Check your balance weekly** — Earnings micro-accumulate. A weekly check helps you understand your personal earning pattern.
