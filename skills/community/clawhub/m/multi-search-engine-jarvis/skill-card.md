## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and other users use this skill to choose and formulate searches across Chinese and global search engines, including advanced operators, time filters, privacy-focused search, and WolframAlpha knowledge queries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search queries may be sent to public search providers and can reveal sensitive terms. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering secrets, confidential business terms, private personal data, or sensitive investigative queries unless disclosure to the selected provider is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Advanced search operators can be used against content or systems outside the user's authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Use site, filetype, cache, and similar operators only for content and systems the user is authorized to research. <br>
