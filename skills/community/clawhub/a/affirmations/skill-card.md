This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to receive affirmations, create personalized affirmations, and maintain a daily affirmation practice with favorites, preferences, and practice logs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Affirmation preferences, favorites, custom affirmations, and practice logs may contain personal details stored in a local ~/affirmations/ folder. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid recording sensitive personal details on shared or monitored computers, and periodically review or delete the folder when local retention is not desired. <br>
