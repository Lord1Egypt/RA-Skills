## Description: <br>
Provides Stoic philosophy quotes from Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, Epictetus, Cato, and other Stoic philosophers when users ask for Stoic wisdom, inspiration, or theme-specific guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bshandley](https://clawhub.ai/user/bshandley) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees use this skill to request attributed Stoic quotes for inspiration, reflection, or theme-specific philosophical guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Quote wording or attribution may need confirmation for scholarly or publication use. <br>
Mitigation: Verify exact wording and attribution against authoritative sources before relying on a quote in scholarly, legal, or publication contexts. <br>
Risk: Broad inspiration requests may receive Stoic content even when the user did not explicitly ask for Stoicism. <br>
Mitigation: Review the response for fit and ask for a different tradition, author, or theme when Stoic framing is not desired. <br>
