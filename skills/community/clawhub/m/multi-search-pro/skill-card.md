## Description: <br>
Multi Search Pro helps agents route general, news, and finance queries across multiple public search engines and finance-focused search sources without requiring API keys. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[windy-001-crypto](https://clawhub.ai/user/windy-001-crypto) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to select suitable search providers, compose advanced queries, and compare results for general information, news, finance, stock, and research-report lookups. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms can be sent to public search engines or finance search providers. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid private, confidential, or sensitive keywords, and choose providers appropriate for the query context. <br>
