This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External learners, hobbyists, and practicing magicians use this skill to get magic lesson guidance, troubleshoot sleight-of-hand practice, plan routines, make or select props, and discuss performance theory. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Physical prop construction or practice guidance could be unsafe if applied without judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Use safe materials and avoid hazardous stunts, sharp tools, fire, or electrical modifications unless qualified supervision and appropriate precautions are in place. <br>
Risk: Uploaded performance videos may contain private images, voices, locations, or audience information. <br>
Mitigation: Upload only videos the user is comfortable sharing with the agent environment, and remove unnecessary personal or audience-identifying content when possible. <br>
