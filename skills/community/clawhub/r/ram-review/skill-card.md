## Description: <br>
Summons a Ram-style persona to evaluate Rem's work with concise, critical, and constructive feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can invoke this persona-review skill when they want a Ram-style critique of Rem's completed work, including pointed feedback and occasional constructive suggestions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording could activate the Ram persona unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Use clearer trigger phrases or require confirmation before spawning the persona review session. <br>
