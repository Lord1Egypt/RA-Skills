## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language prompt template pack with 50+ reusable prompts for writing, code, translation, brainstorming, operations copy, business, marketing, learning, and productivity scenarios. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to request ready-to-copy prompt templates and brief explanations for common writing, business, marketing, technical, study, and productivity tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Accidental activation could produce prompt templates when the user intended a different workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly by name or by requesting a specific prompt template category. <br>
Risk: Some advertised template categories appear incomplete in this version. <br>
Mitigation: Check the bundled reference files before relying on a category as complete, and treat missing templates as a content limitation. <br>
