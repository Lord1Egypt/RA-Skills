# Vane Daily Market Report

Generates a comprehensive daily market report covering crypto, US stocks, and Chinese A-shares. Designed for automated publishing to Telegram, Substack, or any messaging platform.

## Report Sections

### 1. Crypto Market
- BTC/ETH/SOL prices with 24h change
- 24h high/low and volume
- RSI(14) on 1H and 4H timeframes
- EMA 7/25 crossover signals
- MACD momentum indicator

### 2. Portfolio
- Real-time position P&L per asset
- Total portfolio P&L
- Account balance snapshot

### 3. US Stocks (via Yahoo Finance)
- NVDA, AVGO, POET, AAOI, MRVL, COIN
- Price and daily change

### 4. Chinese A-Shares (via Sina)
- Key tech stocks with daily performance

### 5. Signal Summary
- Automatic scoring of market conditions
- Buy/sell/hold recommendations

## Requirements
- Node.js 18+
- OKX API (public data)
- Internet access (Yahoo Finance, Sina API)
