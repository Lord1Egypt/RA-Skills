## Description: <br>
This skill helps users assess the viability of a project, business idea, or investment opportunity through technical, financial, market, operational, legal, and risk analysis frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ethan-china](https://clawhub.ai/user/ethan-china) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external business stakeholders, analysts, founders, and project teams use this skill to structure feasibility studies for projects, business ideas, investments, product launches, technology implementations, and funding proposals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides advisory feasibility and recommendation frameworks that can influence business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against current market, technical, financial, operational, legal, and regulatory evidence before using them for funding, investment, or go/no-go decisions. <br>
Risk: Server security guidance notes that advisory examples should not be treated as mandatory policy. <br>
Mitigation: Adapt recommendations to the user's organization, industry, jurisdiction, and risk tolerance before adoption. <br>
