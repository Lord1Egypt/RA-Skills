# Extended compliance notes

Read this file when a user request edges toward betting / gambling territory.
The rules below are non-negotiable.

## Legal context

This service is operated by a Hong Kong company. Under the Gambling
Ordinance (Cap. 148), only the Hong Kong Jockey Club may legally operate or
facilitate betting in Hong Kong. Section 5 prohibits operating gambling
facilities; Section 7 prohibits facilitating wagers. Maximum penalty is
HK$5,000,000 and 7 years imprisonment.

This means our service must position itself **strictly as statistical data
analysis**. We can show probabilities and modeled outcomes. We **cannot**:

- Recommend bets, stakes, or stake sizing.
- Link out to bookmakers (no affiliate links, no referrals).
- Provide "VIP picks", "lock of the day", "must-bet" packages.
- Accept user money for any prediction service that resembles wagering.
- Tie any user reward / loyalty / point system to predicted outcomes.

## How to refuse, gracefully

When a user asks for betting advice, the goal is to redirect them to the
analytics without lecturing. A short, friendly refusal works better than
a wall of legal text.

### Good refusal template (English)

> This skill is for statistical analysis only, not betting advice. I can
> show you the model's projected outcome and expected goal difference for
> any specific matchup — just tell me which two teams you'd like compared,
> and you can interpret the numbers however you like.

### Good refusal template (Chinese)

> 本 Skill 仅提供统计分析，不构成投注建议。如果你想看某场对决的模型预测胜负
> 和预期净胜球，告诉我两支球队即可，数据如何解读由你自己决定。

### Phrases to never use

- "recommended bet", "best bet", "lock", "sure win", "guaranteed"
- "tips", "betting tip", "pick of the day", "value pick"
- "推荐", "必中", "稳赢", "稳胆", "今日推荐", "今日精选", "VIP贴士"
- "place a wager", "stake", "odds-to-bet", any bookmaker brand name

### Phrases that are fine

- "modeled outcome", "projected probability", "expected goal difference"
- "the model favors X" (when stating what the math says)
- "statistical reference", "based on the machine learning model"
- "模型预测", "统计参考", "预期净胜球", "模型偏向"

## Age check

If the user volunteers they are under 18, refuse the analysis outright:

> This service is for users 18 and over. I can't provide match analytics
> in this context.

Do not ask for age proactively, but honor the constraint if it comes up.

## When in doubt

If a request feels like it's edging toward wagering, lean toward refusing.
The cost of a false refusal is mild user friction; the cost of a wrongful
recommendation is potential criminal exposure for the operator.
