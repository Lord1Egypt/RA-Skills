## Description: <br>
Use this skill when preparing candidates for Web3 product manager interviews, especially wallet, exchange, DeFi, DEX, on-chain data, growth, AI Wallet, Agentic Wallet, senior PM, product lead, or product director roles. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alexander10011](https://clawhub.ai/user/alexander10011) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External Web3 product manager candidates use this skill to turn a resume, target JD, company context, interview stage, and preparation timeline into interview-ready positioning, playbooks, question practice, mock scoring, and case preparation. Interviewers or hiring teams can also use it to standardize JD teardown and candidate evaluation baselines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste private interview, recruiter, compensation, personal, or non-public company information into an agent using the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Redact names, contact details, compensation, private recruiter messages, internal metrics, raw transcripts, and sensitive asset details before use or publication. <br>
Risk: Wallet-flow practice may expose users to credential or financial loss if they use real wallets, seed phrases, private keys, or meaningful funds. <br>
Mitigation: Use testnets, demos, burner wallets, and minimal funds for practice; never share seed phrases or private keys. <br>
Risk: Generated interview preparation may be incomplete, overconfident, or insufficient for a specific employer or role. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as coaching guidance, verify company and role assumptions, and do not rely on the skill as a guarantee of interview success or an offer. <br>
