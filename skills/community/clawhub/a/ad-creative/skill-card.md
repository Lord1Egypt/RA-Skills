## Description: <br>
Generates, iterates, and organizes ad creative such as headlines, descriptions, primary text, and full ad variations for paid advertising platforms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Mrhuang09](https://clawhub.ai/user/Mrhuang09) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, growth practitioners, and agents use this skill to draft ad copy at scale, validate it against platform limits, and iterate from performance data. It supports Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Twitter/X copy workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can suggest campaign-management actions that may affect live advertising accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Use generated campaign changes as drafts and require explicit human approval before uploading or modifying campaigns. <br>
Risk: The skill discusses voice cloning and voiceover generation for ads. <br>
Mitigation: Generate cloned voices only with documented commercial rights and consent from the voice owner. <br>
Risk: The skill may use local marketing-context files and approved provider credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Review local context files before use and restrict API keys to approved providers and accounts. <br>
