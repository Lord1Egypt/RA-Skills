Mitigation: Treat recommendations and salary ranges as advisory, compare them with current market sources and employer details, and adjust answers to the user's real experience. <br>
Risk: Guidance about contracts, non-compete terms, labor disputes, or other legal employment issues may require professional review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for preparation only and consult a qualified legal or HR professional before making decisions on legal or contractual matters. <br>
