## Description: <br>
Investor Update helps founders and startup operators draft structured monthly or quarterly investor updates from company metrics, milestones, challenges, cash runway, and specific asks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup operators use this skill to turn investor-facing metrics, cash status, milestones, challenges, team changes, and asks into concise updates for current or prospective investors. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafts may include sensitive company metrics, cash runway, customer names, and fundraising context. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only information suitable for investor-facing drafting and review the final update before sharing. <br>
Risk: Benchmarks or outbound resource links may be stale or unsuitable for a specific company, industry, or fundraising context. <br>
Mitigation: Verify benchmark claims, links, and financial assumptions against current company records and trusted sources before sending. <br>
