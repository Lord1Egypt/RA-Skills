## Description: <br>
Presentation and slide deck generator. Create outlines, full slide decks, speaker notes, pitch decks, training slides, report presentations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers can use this skill to generate Markdown presentation outlines, full slide decks, speaker notes, pitch decks, training slides, report decks, and slide design guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores user inputs in local logs under ~/.local/share/slide-maker and can later search or export those logs. <br>
Mitigation: Use only non-sensitive material unless local logging is acceptable, and review or clear the local data directory before sharing the environment. <br>
Risk: Generated presentation content can include placeholders, sample metrics, or generic claims that may be inaccurate for a real audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review and replace generated facts, metrics, names, and recommendations before relying on the slides. <br>
