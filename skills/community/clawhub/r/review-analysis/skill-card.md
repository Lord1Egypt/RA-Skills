## Description: <br>
Analyze customer reviews, complaints, and feedback to find repeat patterns, likely root causes, and action priorities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, operations, customer experience, and marketing teams use this skill to turn defined sets of customer reviews, complaints, refund notes, or survey feedback into ranked patterns, likely root causes, and next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Customer feedback can contain personal details such as names, emails, order numbers, or other sensitive identifiers. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide feedback data that may be shared with the agent and redact personal details unless they are necessary for the analysis. <br>
Risk: Pattern and root-cause analysis may overstate certainty when the review set is small, noisy, or biased. <br>
Mitigation: Treat conclusions as directional, keep evidence snippets representative, and distinguish repeated patterns from isolated anecdotes. <br>
