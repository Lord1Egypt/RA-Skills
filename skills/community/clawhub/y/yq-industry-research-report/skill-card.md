## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, researchers, and business teams use this skill to generate full industry, market, company, or competitive research reports with sourced findings, visualizations, fact checking, and formatted deliverables. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create report files and formatted deliverables, including DOCX and PDF outputs. <br>
Mitigation: Before running it, specify whether files should be created, where outputs should be written, and whether DOCX/PDF conversion is required. <br>
Risk: Industry reports may contain incorrect, outdated, or insufficiently sourced facts if research inputs are weak. <br>
Mitigation: Review sources, fact-check critical statistics, and verify final claims before relying on the report for business decisions. <br>
