## Description: <br>
Q is a general-purpose utility skill that routes natural-language requests across information query, creative thinking, quick developer tools, and content quality checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, content creators, product managers, students, and researchers use this skill to route everyday assistant tasks through query triage, brainstorming, text and data transformations, and quality review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The invocation wording is broad, so the skill may be selected for many unrelated general-purpose requests. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke it intentionally for query, brainstorming, quick-tool, or quality-review tasks, and review the selected module output before relying on it. <br>
Risk: The skill processes user-provided prompts and content locally, which may include sensitive or private material if the user supplies it. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid passing secrets or private content unless you are comfortable with the agent processing that input. <br>
