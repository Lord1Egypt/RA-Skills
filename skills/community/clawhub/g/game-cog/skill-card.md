## Description: <br>
AI game development skill powered by CellCog that helps generate character-consistent art, sprites, tilesets, music, UI, 3D models, GDDs, level designs, and prototypes from a single prompt. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nitishgargiitd](https://clawhub.ai/user/nitishgargiitd) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and game teams use Game Cog to generate cohesive game concepts, production assets, 3D models, UI elements, level ideas, and prototype-ready guidance through CellCog. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, concepts, referenced assets, and project details may be sent to an external CellCog service. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the publisher and CellCog dependency are trusted, avoid sending secrets or highly sensitive unreleased material, and review generated outputs before use. <br>
Risk: The skill requires the CELLCOG_API_KEY credential. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the credential through the agent environment, keep it out of prompts and source files, and rotate it if exposure is suspected. <br>
