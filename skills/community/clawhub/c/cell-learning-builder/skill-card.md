## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language learning-plan builder that clarifies learner goals, prioritizes authoritative research, and produces actionable learning packs with exercises, milestones, and citations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cellinlab](https://clawhub.ai/user/cellinlab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External learners, creators, and agents use this skill to turn a vague learning goal into a structured Chinese learning pack. It is suited for self-study, course planning, focused topic training, and converting research into writing or project material. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Learning plans may cite outdated, low-quality, or weakly verified sources. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's authority-first research process, record source dates and credibility reasons, and cross-check high-risk claims with at least two independent sources. <br>
Risk: Optional export commands for Word or PDF output may not be appropriate for every environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review any generated export command before running it and keep Markdown as the default source document. <br>
