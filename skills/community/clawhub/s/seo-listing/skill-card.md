## Description: <br>
Optimizes cross-border e-commerce listings by converting basic product selling points or Chinese-style English into localized, SEO-friendly product copy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zzhimin](https://clawhub.ai/user/zzhimin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External sellers, marketers, and e-commerce operators use this skill to generate localized SEO product listing copy for Amazon, Shopify, or independent storefronts from product names, selling points, and target market/language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated listings may include inaccurate product claims, certification statements, trademark references, or platform-policy-sensitive language. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated copy against product evidence, applicable certifications, trademark constraints, target-market requirements, and marketplace rules before publishing. <br>
Risk: Persuasive SEO copy may not match the intended market, language variant, or consumer expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the target market and language before use, and localize final wording with market-specific review when needed. <br>
