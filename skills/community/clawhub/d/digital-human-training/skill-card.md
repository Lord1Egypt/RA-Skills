## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Voice and likeness cloning can misuse recordings or videos of people who have not consented. <br>
Mitigation: Use only source media from yourself or people who gave clear informed consent, and disclose AI-generated avatars when others may interact with them. <br>
Risk: Training assets and generated model files may contain sensitive biometric or identity-linked information. <br>
Mitigation: Keep generated model files private, restrict access to source media, and delete unused recordings or videos. <br>
Risk: Cloud services used in a digital-human pipeline may retain or process voice and face data under provider-specific terms. <br>
Mitigation: Review privacy and retention terms before sending recordings, videos, or generated assets to any cloud provider. <br>
