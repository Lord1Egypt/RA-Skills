## Use Case: <br>
Drivers, vehicle owners, and automotive shoppers use this skill to evaluate purchases, plan maintenance, interpret repair symptoms, and understand driving or ownership decisions. Its guidance should be treated as informational and checked against local laws, manufacturer instructions, and qualified automotive professionals where safety or legality matters. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Driving-technique or recovery guidance could be unsafe if applied without appropriate conditions, training, or legal context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat maneuvers as informational, follow local traffic laws and manufacturer guidance, and use certified instruction for performance, recovery, off-road, or emergency-driving techniques. <br>
Risk: Maintenance or diagnostic advice may not fit a specific vehicle, warranty, or repair situation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations against the vehicle service manual, recall information, and a qualified mechanic before acting on safety-critical repairs. <br>
