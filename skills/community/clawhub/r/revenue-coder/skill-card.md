## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks an agent to autonomously build, test, post, and deploy revenue automation without clear approval limits. <br>
Mitigation: Use only in a sandbox or staging repository, disable autonomous live deployment and external posting unless explicitly approved, and require confirmation before exec commands, GitHub pushes, scraping, public posting, or deployment. <br>
Risk: Example affiliate identifiers or revenue-focused automation could be reused without review. <br>
Mitigation: Replace any example affiliate identifiers with approved identifiers and review generated scripts for platform, legal, and policy compliance before use. <br>
