## Use Case: <br>
External creators, channel operators, and content teams use this skill to get direct, actionable feedback on YouTube video packaging and retention decisions. It is best suited for title, thumbnail, hook, pacing, and concept critiques where a MrBeast-inspired coaching style is desired. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill uses a strong MrBeast-inspired persona that may dominate tone or be mistaken for real attribution. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke it only when that style is desired, keep the first-use disclaimer, and do not treat persona responses as statements from Jimmy Donaldson or MrBeast. <br>
Risk: The skill provides content strategy advice that could be incorrect, misleading, or unsuitable for a specific channel or audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against channel analytics, audience expectations, platform rules, and production constraints before acting on them. <br>
