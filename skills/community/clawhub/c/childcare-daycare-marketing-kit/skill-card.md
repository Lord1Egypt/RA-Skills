## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-aware childcare marketing materials, parent communications, seasonal campaigns, digital ads, local SEO content, and review response templates for licensed childcare programs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Licensed childcare centers, preschools, home daycare providers, and early childhood programs use this skill to draft enrollment campaigns, parent communications, seasonal promotions, local ads, SEO landing pages, and review responses. Outputs are intended as drafting aids that require center-specific legal, licensing, consent, and factual review before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated public marketing, ads, parent communications, license numbers, accreditation claims, meal program claims, testimonials, and opt-out language may be inaccurate or noncompliant if center inputs are wrong or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated item against current center records, state licensing requirements, FTC/COPPA expectations, NAEYC status, CACFP enrollment, testimonial consent, and communications opt-out rules before sending or posting. <br>
Risk: Incident, illness, enrollment, or family communications can create privacy, liability, or licensing exposure if they disclose individual child information or overstate the center's actions. <br>
Mitigation: Keep individual child and health details out of group communications and have an attorney, licensing consultant, or qualified administrator review sensitive incident or illness communications before use. <br>
Risk: Public ads or social posts may misuse child photos, staff photos, parent testimonials, reviews, or claims about staff ratios and background checks. <br>
Mitigation: Use only approved photos and testimonials with documented consent, state staff credentials and screening types exactly, and confirm all ratio, capacity, and availability claims against licensed limits. <br>
Risk: Unexpected requests for credentials, OAuth permissions, payment authority, crypto access, or automatic posting would exceed the scanner-described instruction-only drafting posture. <br>
Mitigation: Deny unexpected permission, credential, purchase, crypto, or autoposting requests and keep publication and account access under explicit human control. <br>
