## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Network-scan guidance could be applied to systems without authorization. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit confirmation before any live scan and use the skill only against systems the operator owns or is authorized to test. <br>
Risk: Granting elevated privileges or broad network access can increase the impact of scan-related mistakes. <br>
Mitigation: Do not grant sudo or broad network access automatically; keep privileges and network scope limited to the approved task. <br>
