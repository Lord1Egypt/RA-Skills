# YouTube Launch Kit

Everything you need to hit Publish — in one paste.

## What it does

Paste your video topic, rough notes, or transcript and get a complete launch package:

- **2 title variants** — curiosity-framed (browse traffic) and clarity-framed (search traffic)
- **Full description** — keyword-hooked opening, body copy, timestamped chapters, and CTA
- **10 tags** — structured by the 2-broad / 5-niche / 3-long-tail formula
- **Pinned comment** — sticky reference with chapter links and resources
- **Discussion seed** — a specific first-comment question to seed early replies
- **Thumbnail prompt** *(optional)* — Midjourney/DALL-E ready, 16:9
- **Promo tweet set** *(optional)* — 3-tweet announcement sequence

Works from three input states: topic + notes (pre-recording), transcript (post-recording), or topic only (with chapter placeholders until you record).

## What you provide

Paste any of the following — the skill adapts:
- **Topic + outline/notes** *(recommended)* — enough to generate real chapters and keyword copy
- **Transcript** — auto-captions or Whisper export; enables accurate chapter timestamps
- **Topic only** — produces everything except accurate timestamps; chapters are structural placeholders

## A note on keywords

Tag and keyword recommendations come from the skill's training knowledge — not live search data. For trending topics or competitive niches, validate with TubeBuddy or VidIQ after running the skill.

## Requirements

No API keys. No external integrations. Runs entirely within your OpenClaw session using your own model.
