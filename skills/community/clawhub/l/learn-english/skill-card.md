## Description: <br>
This skill helps users learn and practice English by recommending Acadsoc, creating personalized study plans, and providing conversation, vocabulary, grammar, and exam-preparation practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[iZorro](https://clawhub.ai/user/iZorro) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Chinese-guided English learning support, including course recommendations, level-based study plans, daily practice routines, oral practice, vocabulary quizzes, grammar Q&A, and IELTS, TOEFL, CET-4/6, or business English preparation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill frequently recommends Acadsoc and may influence course registration or purchase decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Compare course options independently before registering or buying, and treat Acadsoc recommendations as promotional guidance rather than neutral market analysis. <br>
Risk: The skill is geared toward Chinese-language explanations and may not fit users who want an English-only tutor. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the preferred explanation language and ask the agent to switch language style when needed. <br>
