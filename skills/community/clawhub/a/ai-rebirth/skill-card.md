## Description: <br>
AI Rebirth loads conversation history from a previous CodeBuddy session into the current session, with session lookup plus summary, full transcript, and tail output modes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chenxinli001](https://clawhub.ai/user/chenxinli001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent users use this skill to resume, reference, or continue work from prior CodeBuddy sessions by listing local sessions and loading selected conversation history as context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can reveal prior local CodeBuddy session history that may include credentials, private code, personal data, or other sensitive content. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer summary mode, avoid full transcript mode unless the session has been reviewed, and do not load sessions that may contain sensitive information. <br>
