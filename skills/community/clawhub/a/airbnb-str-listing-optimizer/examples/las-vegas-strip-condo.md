## GUEST FAQ

**Q1: What is the check-in process?**
Check-in is fully self-guided — no need to meet anyone. You'll receive a unique smart lock code 48 hours before arrival via Airbnb message. The unit is on the 22nd floor; take the main elevator, turn right, and look for #2207. Your code activates at 4:00 PM on arrival day.

**Q2: Where do I park?**
You have one assigned spot: Underground Garage Level B1, Spot #47. Enter from the building's south parking entrance on Paradise Road. Your building access fob (hanging by the front door) opens the garage gate.

**Q3: What is the WiFi network name and password?**
Network: **TheNeonNest5G**
Password: **VegasView22**
Speed is 500 Mbps fiber — streaming 4K and video calls run without issue.

**Q4: Is early check-in or late checkout available?**
Sometimes, yes — it depends on whether there's a same-day booking. Message me at least 24 hours before arrival/departure and I'll check. Early check-in (noon) and late checkout (1:00 PM) are sometimes available at no charge.

**Q5: Are pets allowed?**
Unfortunately, no — the building HOA does not permit pets. Registered service animals are the only exception per fair housing law.

**Q6: Can I host a party or event?**
No parties or events. This is a residential building; neighbors live on all sides. Small gatherings of the registered 2 guests are fine — just keep noise levels reasonable, especially after 11 PM.

**Q7: What do I do if something is damaged or broken?**
Message me through the Airbnb thread right away. Accidents happen and I won't hold it against you — I just need to know so I can fix it for the next guest (and handle it properly on my end). Don't wait until checkout to tell me.

**Q8: Is there a cleaning fee? What should I clean before checkout?**
Yes, a cleaning fee is included in your booking total — you don't need to deep clean. Before you leave: toss used towels in the bathtub, run the dishwasher if it's full (or wash by hand if just a few items), take the trash to the hall bin outside the door, and make sure the smart lock is engaged when you leave.

**Q9: What local restaurants / experiences do you recommend?**
A few of my favorites within 10 minutes:
- **Lotus of Siam** — best Thai food in Las Vegas, locals' pick, 8 min drive (skip the Strip versions)
- **PublicUs** — walkable coffee shop, great for remote work mornings, 0.4 miles
- **Fremont Street Experience** — free, 15 min drive, completely different vibe from the Strip, best at night
- **Secret Pizza at Harrah's** — no sign, third floor of Harrah's, cash only, locals eat here

**Q10: Who do I contact if I have an issue during my stay?**
Message me through the Airbnb thread — I typically respond within 30 minutes, day or night. For genuine emergencies (water leak, lockout, safety concern) use the phone number in your booking confirmation.
