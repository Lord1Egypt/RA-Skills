Creators, marketers, and commerce teams use this skill to draft TikTok video scripts, hooks, calls to action, storyboards, and shooting checklists for product-focused short-form videos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated sales scripts may include inaccurate prices, scarcity language, product performance claims, health or beauty claims, or professional credentials. <br>
Mitigation: Verify all factual claims, product attributes, pricing, comparisons, and credentials before publishing. <br>
Risk: Promotional scripts may omit sponsorship, affiliate, or advertising disclosures. <br>
Mitigation: Add required disclosures such as #ad or equivalent platform- and jurisdiction-appropriate notices before posting. <br>
