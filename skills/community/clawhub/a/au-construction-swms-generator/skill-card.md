## Description: <br>
Generate compliant Australian construction Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS) for all 19 high-risk construction work categories, applying the WHS Act 2011 hierarchy of controls, hazard libraries, and site-specific control measures for builders, subcontractors, and principal contractors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Construction businesses, builders, subcontractors, and principal contractors use this skill to collect site-specific details and draft Australian Safe Work Method Statements for high-risk construction work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Australia <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SWMS documents may include workplace details, contact information, signatures, or other sensitive site information. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only details needed for the job, use placeholders when exact personal details are not necessary, and handle generated documents as workplace records. <br>
Risk: A generated SWMS may be incorrect or incomplete if site conditions, work steps, hazards, or emergency arrangements are missing. <br>
Mitigation: Complete the intake questions with site-specific information and have a competent WHS professional review the SWMS before site use. <br>
Risk: The skill is not a substitute for specialist licensed advice for high-consequence work such as explosives, radiological work, live high-voltage electrical work, or asbestos clearance decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Engage qualified WHS consultants, licensed contractors, or the relevant regulator for specialist work and use the generated SWMS only as a documentation aid. <br>
