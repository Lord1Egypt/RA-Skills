## Description: <br>
Writes personalized cold emails that actually get replies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, sales teams, founders, and business development teams use this skill to draft short personalized cold emails and follow-up sequences from recipient details, offer context, pain points, personalization notes, and a single call to action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill helps draft unsolicited outreach that could misuse personal information or violate anti-spam, privacy, or consent requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Use only lawful, publicly available, context-appropriate business information and review every email for privacy, consent, and anti-spam compliance before sending. <br>
