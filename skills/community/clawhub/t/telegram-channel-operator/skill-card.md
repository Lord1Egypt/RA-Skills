## Use Case: <br>
External project teams, founders, and agents use this skill to draft and audit Telegram channel posts, announcements, digests, pinned posts, and content calendars for Web3 and AI communities. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Telegram posts, especially token or trading-related content, could publish misleading or overconfident claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review all posts before publishing, include risk notes where relevant, and avoid financial guarantees. <br>
Risk: Identity-specific ArewaOS, Zahra, or Kano framing could be used when the user is not representing that project or voice. <br>
Mitigation: Use identity-specific framing only when representing the project or when permission to use that voice is clear. <br>
