## Description: <br>
Free cold outreach templates for B2B sales messages, including openers, follow-ups, value propositions, and optional Company Brain-assisted personalization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[certainlogicai](https://clawhub.ai/user/certainlogicai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, growth, and founder/operator users use this skill to draft B2B cold outreach emails, follow-ups, and value propositions with placeholders they can personalize. Agents may optionally use local Company Brain context to verify claims and enrich brand positioning before outreach. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The bundled helper can pull local Company Brain content into outreach prompts when invoked, which may expose internal strategy or source material. <br>
Mitigation: Treat Company Brain enhancement as opt-in; review the helper and use it only with data approved for outreach workflows. <br>
Risk: Outreach drafts can contain unverified claims or compliance-sensitive messaging. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims against approved sources and include required unsubscribe and compliance language before sending. <br>
