## Description: <br>
Diagnoses why specific users behave the way they do using a 7-module analytical framework (Needs, Attention, Trust, Decision, Emotion, Spread, Prediction), producing diagnostic analysis and behavioral insights only rather than persuasive copy, manipulation tactics, or growth-hacking instructions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[web-seeker](https://clawhub.ai/user/web-seeker) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to diagnose observed behavior patterns in specific scenarios, including user needs, attention, trust, decision barriers, emotional drivers, spread patterns, and likely next behaviors. It is intended for root-cause analysis and testable behavioral hypotheses, not for campaigns or behavior-change tactics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing-adjacent behavioral analysis could be mistaken for persuasive recommendations or growth tactics. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only to analyze observed behavior and treat outputs about copy, tests, expansion, churn, or experiments as diagnostic hypotheses. <br>
Risk: Behavioral conclusions may overstate certainty when the input scenario has incomplete evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Require evidence checks, stated assumptions, confidence levels, and investigation paths before relying on any diagnosis. <br>
