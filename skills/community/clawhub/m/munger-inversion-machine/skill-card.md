## Description: <br>
Stress-test ideas, businesses, investments, startups, products, strategies, plans, careers, offers, and decisions by inverting them. Use when the user wants to find failure modes, hidden risks, bad incentives, second-order effects, blind spots, stupidity filters, pre-mortems, kill criteria, or a brutally honest downside analysis before committing time, money, reputation, or effort. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to pressure-test decisions before committing time, money, reputation, or effort. It produces inversion-based downside analysis, failure modes, incentive checks, kill criteria, and the smallest high-signal test for ideas, offers, investments, acquisitions, products, strategies, careers, and personal decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on advisory outputs for money, crypto, acquisitions, legal, career, or business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision-support only and obtain qualified professional review before making high-impact commitments. <br>
Risk: The skill can discuss purchases or investment-style decisions but is not an authority to transact. <br>
Mitigation: Require separate human approval and independent evidence before making purchases, buy/sell decisions, or binding commitments. <br>
