## Description: <br>
Design, optimize, and batch-produce video covers and thumbnails for Bilibili, YouTube, Xiaohongshu, Douyin, and other platforms with title strategy, reference analysis, portrait consistency, AI prompt generation, layout specs, and quality scoring. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and design operators use this skill to turn titles, platform targets, portraits, reference covers, and account style into cover strategies, no-text image prompts, layout instructions, batch briefs, and QC reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide portraits, reference covers, logos, or commercial photography they do not have rights to use. <br>
Mitigation: Use only authorized assets and follow the skill's reference-analysis workflow, which emphasizes learning structure while avoiding copied protected expression, logos, IP elements, and third-party likenesses. <br>
Risk: Generated cover concepts can become misleading clickbait or violate platform expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Review each prompt and layout before publishing for accuracy, platform policy fit, and the skill's stated limits against false, vulgar, dangerous, discriminatory, or privacy-invasive covers. <br>
Risk: Portrait prompt workflows can affect likeness and identity consistency. <br>
Mitigation: Use user-authorized portrait inputs, avoid identifying real people from images, and review generated prompts and assets for likeness, age, demographic, and identity changes before use. <br>
