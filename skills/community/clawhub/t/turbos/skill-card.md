## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Wallet seed phrases, private keys, or other secrets may be exposed if users paste them into chats, logs, source control, or untrusted tools. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste real seed phrases or private keys into the agent session, logs, source control, or untrusted tools. <br>
Risk: Swap, liquidity, fee, reward, or position transaction examples can affect funds or NFTs if reviewed or signed with incorrect parameters. <br>
Mitigation: Use testnet first where practical and review every transaction before signing, including network, pool, token, amount, slippage, recipient, and whether liquidity or NFTs may be permanently changed. <br>
