## Use Case: <br>
External roofing contractors, adjusters, inspectors, and sales or operations teams use this skill for mentoring-style guidance on roof measurements, proposals, insurance narratives, workflows, business metrics, and roofing fundamentals. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Roofing guidance may be mistaken for licensed roofing, engineering, legal, insurance, code, permit, or local regulatory judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as professional coaching and verify measurements, pricing, coverage, compliance, and regulatory decisions with qualified sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Insurance-facing narratives may be overstated if users convert observations into causation, approval, denial, or coverage claims. <br>
Mitigation: Keep claim language factual and observational, avoid coverage determinations, and have insurance-sensitive wording reviewed before submission. <br>
