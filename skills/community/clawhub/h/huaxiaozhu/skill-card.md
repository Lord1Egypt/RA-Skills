## Description: <br>
Huaxiaozhu is a ride-hailing guidance assistant for budget travel, trip planning, fare estimates, discounts, and safer ride practices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhangifonly](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhangifonly) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill to compare Huaxiaozhu ride-hailing options, understand fixed-price fares and discounts, plan routine trips, and review safer ride practices. Agents should present fare, coupon, policy, and emergency-feature details as general guidance that must be checked in the official app. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fare estimates, coupons, platform policies, and safety feature availability can change or vary by location. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current prices, coupons, policy terms, and emergency options in the official Huaxiaozhu app before relying on the guidance. <br>
