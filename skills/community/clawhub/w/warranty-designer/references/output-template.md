## [Brand Name] Limited Warranty

### What this warranty covers

[Brand Name] warrants that the [product or product category] purchased through an authorized channel will be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a period of **[length, e.g., 1 year / 2 years / lifetime of the original purchaser]** from the date of original retail purchase.

### Who can make a claim

This warranty is offered to the **original purchaser** and is **[non-transferable / transferable for the first 6 months only]**. Proof of original purchase from an authorized seller is required.

### What we will do

If a defect covered by this warranty arises, [Brand Name] will, at our option:

1. Repair the product, or
2. Replace it with the same or a comparable product, or
3. Refund the original purchase price.

We choose the remedy in that order based on cost and availability. We may use new, refurbished, or B-stock components or units.

### What is not covered

This warranty does **not** cover:

- Normal wear and tear including [list category-specific examples].
- Damage from misuse, accident, modification, unauthorized repair, or use outside the product's intended purpose.
- Consumables (batteries, filters, blades, ear-tips, etc., as applicable).
- Cosmetic damage that does not affect function.
- Loss or theft of the product.
- Damage caused by water, fire, or environmental conditions outside the product's stated rating.
- Products purchased from unauthorized sellers.
- Indirect, incidental, or consequential damages, where law allows such limitation.

### How to make a claim

1. Email **[claims@brand.com]** or use the **claim form at [URL]**.
2. Include: order number, photos of the issue, and a brief description.
3. We will respond within **48 hours** with a decision and next steps.

You **do not need to ship the product back** for items under [threshold value]. For higher-value claims, we will provide a prepaid return label.

### Your statutory rights

This warranty is provided in addition to your statutory rights under applicable consumer protection law and **does not replace them**. In the EU, this includes the 2-year legal guarantee under Directive 2019/771. In the UK, this includes rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015. In the US, this is a "Limited Warranty" under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act.
