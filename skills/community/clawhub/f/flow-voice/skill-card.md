## Description: <br>
FlowVoice clones a voice from a short audio sample and generates speech or video voiceover output with LuxTTS. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[windseeker1111](https://clawhub.ai/user/windseeker1111) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and creators use FlowVoice to generate local voiceovers, speech clips, or video narration from authorized voice samples. It is intended for voices the user owns or has explicit permission to clone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Voice cloning can enable impersonation when used with voices that the user does not own or have permission to clone. <br>
Mitigation: Use only owned or explicitly authorized voice samples, and do not clone people from social media or public recordings without permission. <br>
Risk: Saved voice profiles can be reused to generate speech in the cloned voice. <br>
Mitigation: Delete or protect saved voice profiles after use, and do not load voice profile files received from others. <br>
