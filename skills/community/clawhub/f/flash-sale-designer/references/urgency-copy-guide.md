# Urgency Copy Guide — Flash Sale Designer

Channel-by-channel urgency copy templates. Adapt to brand voice and event specifics.

## Principles

- **Specificity beats vagueness**: "47 left" > "limited stock"
- **Time anchoring**: Always name a specific end time in the local timezone of your primary audience
- **One CTA per touchpoint**: Don't split attention between "Shop" and "Learn More"
- **Honest scarcity**: Real numbers build more trust than manufactured ones
- **Urgency escalates**: Copy should become more direct as the event end approaches

## Product Page Copy

### Banner/Hero Area
- `⏰ Flash Sale — Ends [TIME] [TIMEZONE]`
- `[X]% off — Today Only. [HOURS] hours remaining.`
- `FLASH SALE LIVE: $[price] until [TIME]. [N] units remaining.`

### Below Add-to-Cart Button
- `Only [N] left at this price`
- `[X] people have this in their cart right now`
- `Flash price valid until [TIME] today`
- `[N] units sold in the last hour`

### Countdown Timer Label
- `Sale ends in:`
- `Price increases in:`
- `Event closes in:`

## Email Subject Lines

### Early-Window (Hours 1–3)
- `[X]% off [Product] — Live now until [TIME]`
- `Flash sale is live: $[price] for [N] more hours`
- `Today only: [Product] at [X]% off`
- `🔴 LIVE: [Product Name] flash sale`

### Mid-Window (Hours 3–5)
- `[N] units sold so far today`
- `Halfway through — [X] units left`
- `Don't sleep on this: [N] hours left`

### Final Push (Last 30–60 Minutes)
- `Last [N] minutes. $[price] disappears at [TIME].`
- `Final call: [Product] at $[price] ends soon`
- `⚠️ [N] minutes left in the flash sale`
- `This price ends at [TIME]. Don't miss it.`

### Post-Event
- `You missed the flash sale (here's what's next)`
- `The sale ended — but we saved a spot for you`
- `Join the waitlist for the next drop`

## Avoiding Urgency Fatigue

Signs your urgency copy is backfiring:
- Unsubscribe rate spikes during event
- Open rate on "final call" emails is lower than launch email
- Social comments like "you always say limited" or "this timer always resets"

Fixes:
- Run flash sales less frequently (max once per month per brand)
- Ensure countdowns are real and non-resetting
- Vary the urgency format (time-based vs. stock-based vs. quantity-based)
- Give your best customers early access — they feel rewarded, not pressured
