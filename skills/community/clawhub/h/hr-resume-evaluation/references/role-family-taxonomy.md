# Role Family Taxonomy

Infer role family from resume and optional JD, then allow recruiter overrides from `config/evaluation.yaml`.

## Families

- Technical: implementation depth, architecture, production impact, performance, reliability, code or system evidence.
- Sales and Business: revenue, customer scale, conversion, channels, deal cycle, collection, stakeholder management.
- Operations and Marketing: growth metrics, activity scale, user segmentation, content, campaign conversion, retention.
- Product and Project: requirement judgment, roadmap, cross-team delivery, launch result, resource coordination, risk control.
- Finance, Legal, HR, Administration: qualifications, compliance, accuracy, process reliability, risk control, service scope.
- Management: team size, budget, goal breakdown, organization coordination, performance result, decision scope.

Role-family adaptation changes evidence interpretation. It must not change compliance rules or use protected traits.
