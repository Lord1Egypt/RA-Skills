## Description: <br>
Provides AI company legal and compliance support for contract governance, intellectual property, algorithm audit, AIGC compliance, cross-border data rules, ethics committee structure, and data-protection workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[johnsmithfan](https://clawhub.ai/user/johnsmithfan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Legal, compliance, and governance agents use this skill to draft legal opinions, prepare contract governance artifacts, assess GDPR/CCPA and AIGC compliance status, and route high-risk matters for human approval. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may store personal and work context and includes heartbeat-style background checks. <br>
Mitigation: Review where memory and profile data are stored, document deletion procedures, and narrow triggers before using the skill with legal or business-confidential material. <br>
Risk: The skill can modify files and may communicate through sessions_send or linked messaging accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Require confirmation before file deletion or external sends, restrict permissions to the minimum needed, and disable autonomous commits or pushes unless explicitly reviewed. <br>
