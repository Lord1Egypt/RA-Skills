## Description: <br>
Design structured A/B test hypotheses for ad creatives, hooks, destination pages, and audience segments with clear success metrics and test duration logic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, growth operators, and external agents use this skill to design disciplined A/B tests for ad creatives, offers, destination pages, and audiences. It helps structure hypotheses, isolate variables, choose success metrics, plan sample size and duration, and document test learnings. <br>
