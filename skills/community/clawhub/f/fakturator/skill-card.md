## Description: <br>
Fakturownik helps generate Polish VAT invoices with templates, automatic tax calculations, invoice numbering, and text, CSV, or JSON output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers, small businesses, and agents assisting Polish businesses use this skill to prepare VAT invoice content from seller, buyer, item, payment, and tax-rate details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Invoice workflows can involve customer, tax, and business data that may be stored in generated files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering real customer or tax data until storage behavior is understood, and review generated files before sharing or retaining them. <br>
Risk: The artifact advertises a paid PRO email feature that may involve SMTP credentials and external payment setup. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify the publisher, payment link, and SMTP configuration before sharing credentials or sending invoices. <br>
