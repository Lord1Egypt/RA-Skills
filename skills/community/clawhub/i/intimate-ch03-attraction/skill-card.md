## Description: <br>
A Chinese study assistant for Rowland S. Miller's Intimate Relationships, 6th edition, Chapter 3 on attraction, helping users review concepts, examples, and applications through progressive disclosure. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[junwugit](https://clawhub.ai/user/junwugit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students and learners use this skill to study, review, and apply Chapter 3 attraction concepts from Intimate Relationships in Chinese, with English terms introduced on first use. The skill supports concise Q&A, concept comparisons, textbook example recall, and exam-oriented review within the chapter scope. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat educational discussion about attraction as relationship, psychological, medical, or legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Frame responses as study support for a textbook chapter, avoid prescriptive relationship counseling, and redirect high-stakes personal concerns to appropriate professionals. <br>
Risk: Users may share sensitive personal relationship details while asking for examples or applications. <br>
Mitigation: Encourage minimal disclosure and keep examples focused on textbook concepts rather than collecting personal details. <br>
