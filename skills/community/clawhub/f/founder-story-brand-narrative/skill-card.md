## Description: <br>
Creates and refines founder story and brand narrative content for DTC and independent stores selling founder-driven products, including structured narrative frameworks, placement guidance, and ready-to-use copy aligned with DTC best practices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External brand operators, founders, marketers, and agents use this skill to turn founder-led product stories into About page, PDP, hero, email, packaging, and social narrative assets. It is intended for DTC and independent stores that need structured brand storytelling, placement guidance, and conversion-aware copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on loosely related storytelling prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user is working on founder-led brand, origin-story, About page, PDP, hero, or related DTC narrative work before applying the full framework. <br>
Risk: The skill may prefer English unless the user asks for another language. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for or preserve the requested output language when the user is not clearly requesting English. <br>
Risk: Founder and product claims could become misleading if the agent fills gaps with invented details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's documented clarification questions and brand-safe placeholders for unknown facts, then have the founder or brand owner confirm origin, process, proof, and claims before publishing. <br>
