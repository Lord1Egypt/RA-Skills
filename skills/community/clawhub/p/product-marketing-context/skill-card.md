## Description: <br>
Creates or updates a reusable product marketing context document so marketing tasks can share positioning, audience, messaging, proof point, and goal context. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[MichaelHoughtonDeBox](https://clawhub.ai/user/MichaelHoughtonDeBox) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and agent workflows use this skill to create or update a structured product marketing context file. The context captures product overview, audience, personas, pain points, competitors, differentiation, objections, customer language, brand voice, proof points, and goals for reuse by other marketing skills. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Auto-drafting reads repository documentation and marketing-related files. <br>
Mitigation: Use auto-draft only in repositories where that local reading is acceptable. <br>
Risk: The generated marketing context may contain incomplete or inaccurate positioning. <br>
Mitigation: Review `.claude/product-marketing-context.md` before relying on it or letting other marketing skills reuse it. <br>
