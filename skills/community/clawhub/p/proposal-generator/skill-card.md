## Description: <br>
Generates professional freelancer proposals, scopes of work, and project quotes from a brief description, with deliverables, timelines, pricing, and terms. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jamespatrickthom2003-star](https://clawhub.ai/user/jamespatrickthom2003-star) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External freelancers, consultants, and agencies use this skill to draft client-ready proposals, scopes of work, quotes, pitches, and project briefs from a short client or project description. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals or SOWs may include inaccurate scope, deliverables, prices, currency, payment terms, IP language, cancellation terms, confidentiality language, dates, or jurisdiction-specific legal wording. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adjust all commercial and legal details before sending generated documents to a client. <br>
