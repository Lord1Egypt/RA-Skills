## Description: <br>
Love Rescue is an AI relationship coach that helps people triage conflict, calm escalation, reopen dialogue, and plan small repair actions before a breakup. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[imwyvern](https://clawhub.ai/user/imwyvern) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for relationship-emergency coaching: assessing immediate conflict, stabilizing the next hour, planning the next 24 hours, and avoiding actions that could worsen the situation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may rely on relationship coaching in situations involving abuse, danger, or mental-health crisis. <br>
Mitigation: Keep safety first, avoid advice to remain in dangerous situations, and refer users to professional or crisis support when danger or crisis signs appear. <br>
Risk: The skill includes a visible promotional nudge to replyher.com after several exchanges. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the external link as promotional and review it before enabling the skill in environments with strict endorsement or external-link policies. <br>
