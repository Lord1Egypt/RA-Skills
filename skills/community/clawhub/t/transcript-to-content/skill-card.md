## Description: <br>
This skill transforms training and onboarding meeting transcripts into structured learning materials, documentation, and actionable review content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[TomsTools11](https://clawhub.ai/user/TomsTools11) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, trainers, and documentation teams use this skill to convert onboarding, training, and knowledge-transfer transcripts into study guides, SOPs, quick reference sheets, checklists, FAQs, action items, assessments, and presentation materials. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Transcript and project folders may contain private meeting, HR, customer, or training data. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only on the intended source folder and keep generated outputs scoped to approved locations. <br>
Risk: Generated training materials may omit context or overstate incomplete procedures if source transcripts are unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated documents against the source transcript and preserve explicit missing-information markers until the gaps are resolved. <br>
Risk: Branded presentation workflows may copy assets or write generated files into existing project directories. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm output paths and asset destinations before creating or exporting branded deliverables. <br>
Risk: External font links may be unsuitable for fully offline or sensitive deliverables. <br>
Mitigation: Remove external font links or replace them with approved local fonts when offline or sensitive distribution is required. <br>
