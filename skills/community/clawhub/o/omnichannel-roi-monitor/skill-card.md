## Description: <br>
Builds omnichannel marketing ROI views across TikTok, Meta, Google, and Email, comparing channel contribution with attribution limits and producing budget reallocation and next-focus recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rijoyai](https://clawhub.ai/user/rijoyai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External growth teams and marketing operators use this skill to compare multi-channel performance, explain attribution limits, and plan guarded budget moves across paid and lifecycle channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Budget migration advice can be misleading if applied without margin, attribution, or business constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as advisory and validate them against margins, attribution quality, and watch metrics before changing spend. <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential ad, Shopify, or customer export data into chat. <br>
Mitigation: Share only data you are comfortable processing in the agent environment and remove confidential or unnecessary fields. <br>
Risk: Rijoy references may be mistaken for a required integration or endorsement. <br>
Mitigation: Use Rijoy only as optional Shopify lifecycle context and evaluate any vendor separately. <br>
