## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CRO copy, test plans, and roadmaps may not improve conversion without empirical validation. <br>
Mitigation: Run the generated experiments through A/B testing, measurement, and iteration before treating recommendations as proven improvements. <br>
Risk: External paid-pack and concierge links may have terms, pricing, or behavior outside this skill package. <br>
Mitigation: Review external Atlas links separately before purchasing, integrating, or sharing them with users. <br>
Risk: Off-topic DeFi and security-audit promotional wording could be mistaken for a security review capability. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for CRO marketing drafts and do not rely on it for DeFi, code, or security audit work. <br>
