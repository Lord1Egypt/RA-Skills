## Description: <br>
Reading Squad simulates a structured multi-expert book discussion in a chat-based AI tool, guiding setup, debate, consensus synthesis, and reading advice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jinguo0524](https://clawhub.ai/user/jinguo0524) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Readers, students, educators, and knowledge workers use this skill to organize a multi-perspective discussion about a book, chapter, or reading topic and turn the discussion into consensus points and actionable reading guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI-generated discussion may simulate named perspectives and be mistaken for actual statements from those people. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as synthetic reading guidance and do not attribute simulated viewpoints to real individuals. <br>
