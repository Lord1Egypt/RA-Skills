## Description: <br>
Enterprise ESG and sustainability analysis skill that generates ESG reports with carbon footprint calculations, regulatory compliance checks, supply chain sustainability scoring, and peer benchmarking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sustainability, compliance, and corporate reporting teams use this skill to analyze company ESG data, estimate carbon footprint, check gaps against major disclosure frameworks, benchmark peers, and draft structured sustainability reports. It supports work with public filings or authorized company documents where assumptions and data quality limitations need to be called out clearly. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Company ESG inputs can include sensitive business information or personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Use only data the user is authorized to process, prefer public filings or redacted documents, and avoid unnecessary confidential or personal information. <br>
Risk: Regulatory conclusions can become stale as reporting frameworks and climate disclosure rules change. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify compliance findings against current GRI, CSRD, ISSB, SEC, TCFD, and other applicable requirements before relying on a report. <br>
