## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use web search to gather market, competitor, pricing, trend, and falsification evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Keep confidential details out of prompts when web search is enabled and follow the host environment's network and privacy rules. <br>
Risk: The skill is designed for deliberately harsh product or decision critique and allows implicit invocation in its host configuration. <br>
Mitigation: Disable implicit invocation in the host if the skill should run only after an explicit user request. <br>
