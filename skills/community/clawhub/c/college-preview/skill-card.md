## Description: <br>
Helps college students organize a 20-day final exam sprint with profile setup, study planning, daily tasks, short quizzes, progress feedback, and local plan adjustments for university courses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[yedda1117](https://clawhub.ai/user/yedda1117) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students and learning-support agents use this skill to turn course materials, exam dates, daily availability, weak topics, and completion feedback into a structured 20-day review workflow. It supports tutoring-style explanations, practice questions, progress tracking, and bounded plan adjustments without replacing the student's own coursework or exam work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read and update user-visible exam prep materials in the recommended study workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Keep unrelated private documents, credentials, and sensitive personal files out of the study folder. <br>
Risk: General study prompts may activate the skill when the user did not intend to enter the exam-prep workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants an exam-prep workflow before creating or updating study records. <br>
Risk: Users may ask for direct assignment, lab report, or exam answers instead of study support. <br>
Mitigation: Refuse substitution or fabrication requests and redirect to explanations, hints, practice, and error analysis. <br>
