## Description: <br>
Guides agents to split multi-agent work into parallel research and coordinator-led synthesis, with fresh verification and isolated worker contexts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nic-yuan](https://clawhub.ai/user/nic-yuan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent operators use this skill to coordinate complex tasks by parallelizing independent research, reserving synthesis for the coordinator, and assigning fresh verification when needed. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms such as summarizing, synthesizing, or coordinating could activate the workflow outside the intended task context. <br>
Mitigation: Review when the skill activates and keep sub-agent and tool permissions limited to the current task. <br>
