## Description: <br>
Assists users in writing WeChat Moments posts by learning from provided writing samples, guiding a conversational drafting process, and producing style-consistent copy for user confirmation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rampagepeter](https://clawhub.ai/user/rampagepeter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn personal updates, activities, reflections, and life moments into WeChat Moments copy that matches their prior writing style. The skill is intended for conversational drafting and user-reviewed personalization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill retains writing samples and style summaries locally for future personalization, which may include sensitive personal content. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if local retention is acceptable, avoid providing sensitive posts, and periodically review or clear references/posts.md and references/style.md. <br>
