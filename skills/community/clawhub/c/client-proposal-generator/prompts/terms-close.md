# Prompt: Terms & Close

## Purpose
Generate the closing section of a proposal — payment terms, guarantees, and a confident call to action that makes it easy to say yes.

---

## Prompt

```
You are a senior consultant writing the final section of a client proposal. The goal: make signing feel like the obvious, low-risk next step.

PROJECT DETAILS:
- Total project price: $[TOTAL_PRICE]
- Project duration: [DURATION]
- Payment structure preference: [DEPOSIT_PERCENTAGE or "suggest standard"]
- Your satisfaction guarantee policy: [DESCRIBE or "suggest standard"]
- Proposal expiration: [DATE or "30 days from today"]

Write the Terms & Close section of a professional proposal. Include:

**PAYMENT TERMS**
- Deposit required to start (suggest 50% for projects under $5K, 30% for $5K+)
- Milestone-based payment schedule (at least 2 installments)
- Final payment trigger
- Accepted payment methods (list: bank transfer, PayPal, Stripe, etc. — adjust to what's realistic)
- Late payment policy (brief, professional)

**SATISFACTION GUARANTEE**
- What you stand behind (quality, revisions, communication)
- What happens if the client isn't satisfied after delivery (revision rounds, partial refund, etc.)
- Keep it specific enough to be real, not a blank "100% money-back guarantee"

**NEXT STEPS**
- Step 1: Client reviews and approves proposal
- Step 2: Invoice sent for deposit
- Step 3: Kickoff call scheduled
- Timeline note: when work begins after deposit received

**PROPOSAL VALIDITY**
- This proposal is valid until [DATE]
- After expiration, pricing and availability subject to change

**CLOSING STATEMENT**
- 2–3 sentences. Warm but confident. Expresses enthusiasm for the project.
- Ends with a specific, low-friction CTA: "Reply to this email" or "Click the link below to sign."

Tone: Professional, warm, confident. Not pushy. Make saying yes feel easy and safe.
```

---

## Example Inputs

- Total price: $4,500 (Professional tier)
- Duration: 6 weeks
- Payment: Standard (50% deposit, 50% on delivery)
- Guarantee: 2 revision rounds included; additional revisions at $75/hour
- Expiration: 30 days

---

## Tips

- A deposit protects you from abandonment and signals client commitment
- The "next steps" section reduces decision paralysis — tell them exactly what to do
- Proposal expiration creates gentle urgency without being pushy
- Keep the guarantee specific — vague guarantees feel like marketing; specific ones feel trustworthy
