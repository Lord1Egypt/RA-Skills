## Description: <br>
Generates structured competitive analysis reports with feature comparisons, pricing analysis, SWOT analysis, and strategic recommendations for market research or competitive intelligence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[seanwyngaard](https://clawhub.ai/user/seanwyngaard) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business, marketing, product, and consulting teams use this skill to research public competitor information and create client-ready competitive intelligence reports for a target product or business. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated Markdown, HTML, summary, and CSV files may contain business-sensitive competitive analysis saved locally under output/competitor-analysis/. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated files before sharing and handle the output directory according to the organization's data-handling requirements. <br>
Risk: The skill may propose shell commands during report generation. <br>
Mitigation: Review any proposed shell command before allowing it to run. <br>
Risk: Competitive research based on public web information can become outdated or inaccurate as products, pricing, and positioning change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important facts against current public sources and clearly label assumptions before using the report with stakeholders. <br>
