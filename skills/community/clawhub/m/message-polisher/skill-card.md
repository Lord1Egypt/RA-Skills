## Description: <br>
Polishes Chinese workplace messages by reducing emotional or blunt language, diagnosing communication risks, and producing goal-oriented drafts for different business contexts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sssssHaoa](https://clawhub.ai/user/sssssHaoa) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, managers, and business communicators use this skill to turn emotionally charged workplace drafts into professional Chinese messages. It supports peer coordination, leadership escalation, client responses, and responsibility-boundary clarification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated workplace messages may misstate facts, tone, recipient context, or responsibility boundaries if the user's source draft is incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review the facts, recipient context, tone, deadlines, and responsibility boundaries before sending any generated message. <br>
Risk: The skill is written for Chinese workplace communication and may not fit non-Chinese recipients without adaptation. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for translation or language adaptation when the recipient or workplace context requires another language or cultural register. <br>
