## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Fintech guidance may be insufficient for real payment, banking, trading, KYC/AML, or compliance deployments without domain-specific validation. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval, independent testing, rollback planning, and separate verification of regulatory or certification claims before production use. <br>
Risk: Generated architecture or implementation advice could affect high-impact financial workflows if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs with qualified engineering, security, compliance, and legal stakeholders before relying on them. <br>
