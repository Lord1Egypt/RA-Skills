## Description: <br>
终极版 SPIN 销售法专家系统，提供从开场、挖掘到促成的全周期、可操作的销售对话脚本框架，适用于复杂的 B2B 销售咨询场景。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sales teams, sales consultants, and sales enablement users can use this skill to structure complex B2B discovery conversations with SPIN questions, opening scripts, objection-handling guidance, and follow-up action plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Some customer-facing scripts may obscure or deny commercial intent even though the skill supports sales conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Review and rewrite opening or objection-handling scripts before real prospect use so the commercial purpose is clear and not misleading. <br>
Risk: The skill metadata requests memory and web-search access for customer or business context. <br>
Mitigation: Enable memory_search or tavily_search only when the data being used is appropriate for the workflow, and verify generated market or customer-context claims before use. <br>
