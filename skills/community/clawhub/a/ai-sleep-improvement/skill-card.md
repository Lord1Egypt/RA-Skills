## Description: <br>
Guides agents to provide educational sleep-improvement guidance covering sleep cycles, circadian rhythm, CBT-I techniques, bedtime routines, sleep environment, diet, naps, sleep disorders, jet lag, shift work, and medication caution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to answer sleep wellness questions and structure educational guidance for insomnia, sleep routines, sleep environment, naps, jet lag, shift work, and warning signs that merit medical care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sleep and medication guidance may be mistaken for medical diagnosis or treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as educational guidance, preserve its medical disclaimer, and direct persistent insomnia, suspected sleep apnea, restless legs, parasomnias, medication changes, or severe daytime sleepiness to qualified clinicians. <br>
Risk: General sleep advice may not fit children, older adults, pregnant users, shift workers, or users with medical conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for relevant context, keep recommendations conservative, and escalate special cases to medical care when the user's situation suggests individualized treatment is needed. <br>
