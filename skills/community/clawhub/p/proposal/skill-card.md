## Description: <br>
Turn late-stage opportunities into proposals that are easier to approve by reducing friction, answering likely buyer questions in advance, and clarifying the yes-path. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[depinHQ](https://clawhub.ai/user/depinHQ) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, sales teams, consultants, agencies, and operators use this skill to draft, structure, audit, and tighten proposals for client, partner, project, service, and internal approval decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated proposals may include incorrect pricing, legal terms, commitments, or client-facing claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposal outputs before sending, especially for regulated, high-value, or contract-sensitive deals. <br>
Risk: Proposal outputs can appear decision-ready while still needing jurisdiction, procurement, tax, financial, or contract review. <br>
Mitigation: Validate outputs against the user's formal agreements, internal policies, and applicable review processes. <br>
