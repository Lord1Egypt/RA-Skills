This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for general informational guidance about State Farm coverage options, quotes, claims, policy management, discounts, and contact channels. Quotes, purchases, claims, payments, and policy changes must be completed through verified State Farm channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States, including Washington, D.C. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat general insurance guidance as binding policy, quote, payment, claim, or coverage advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for general information only and complete quotes, claims, payments, policy changes, and coverage decisions through verified State Farm channels. <br>
Risk: Users may share personal, payment, claim, or policy details with the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide sensitive personal, financial, claim, or policy information to the skill; use verified State Farm channels for account-specific actions. <br>
