## Description: <br>
Guide product launches end-to-end: assess readiness, design strategy, build pre-launch buzz, manage launch week, and optimize post-launch growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, product marketers, and launch teams use this skill to assess readiness, choose a launch strategy, build timelines and checklists, coordinate launch week execution, and analyze post-launch performance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated launch emails, public posts, pricing claims, or outreach messages may create legal, regulatory, or brand risk if sent without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve externally facing launch materials before publication or outreach. <br>
Risk: Launch planning recommendations may not account for industry-specific regulatory requirements or market constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Validate launch plans with relevant legal, compliance, product, and marketing stakeholders before execution. <br>
