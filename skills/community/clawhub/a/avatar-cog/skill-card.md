## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, marketers, creators, and agent users use Avatar Cog to create and manage persistent CellCog avatars for brand mascots, digital twins, spokespersons, podcast hosts, stories, social media, and video content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill supports persistent digital-clone and persona behavior using chat logs, voice samples, and optional images. <br>
Mitigation: Use only source material you have permission to use, review where persona data is stored, and install only when persistent avatar functionality is intended. <br>
Risk: Voice cloning and likeness-based avatar workflows can be misused for impersonation or deceptive content. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use the skill for impersonation or deception; keep generated avatar use aligned with consent, disclosure, and applicable policies. <br>
Risk: Changing OpenClaw timeout defaults may alter long-running CellCog behavior in ways the operator does not expect. <br>
Mitigation: Keep default timeout behavior unless the operator understands the operational effect of the change. <br>
