Comprehensive legal applications skill covering civil/criminal/administrative litigation workflows, trial defense strategies, legal document drafting (complaints, answers, appeals, retrial petitions, enforcement applications, representation opinions, defense statements, legal opinions, etc.), arbitration (commercial & labor), mediation, enforcement procedures, evidence rules, and litigation risk assessment. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Legal practitioners and users preparing litigation materials use this skill for civil, criminal, and administrative procedure guidance, arbitration workflows, trial preparation, evidence review, litigation strategy assessment, and drafting legal documents. Outputs are drafting support and must be reviewed by a qualified attorney before formal use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat drafted legal documents or litigation strategy as legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as drafting support and have a qualified attorney verify jurisdiction, deadlines, legal basis, and case-specific strategy before formal use. <br>
Risk: Legal filings and case materials can contain sensitive personal, financial, or asset details. <br>
Mitigation: Use placeholders or redacted values for ID numbers, addresses, phone numbers, bank accounts, and asset details unless working in an approved secure environment for final filing preparation. <br>
Risk: Cited laws, procedural deadlines, and filing requirements may be outdated or jurisdiction-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current law, local court requirements, and all critical deadlines against authoritative sources before relying on the output. <br>
