## Description: <br>
Guides users through structured product-requirements questions and produces a clear requirements document for product planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ecparter](https://clawhub.ai/user/ecparter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product builders, founders, and developers use this skill to turn a vague product idea into a structured requirements document through guided multi-choice questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential business or product details during the requirements interview. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information needed for planning and remove sensitive details from the final document before reuse. <br>
Risk: The generated requirements document may be incomplete, incorrectly prioritized, or too generic for a specific build. <br>
Mitigation: Review the draft with stakeholders and revise priorities, risks, and technical recommendations before treating it as implementation guidance. <br>
