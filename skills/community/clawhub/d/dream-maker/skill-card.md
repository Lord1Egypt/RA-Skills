## Description: <br>
Generate rich, immersive, novel-length dreams with vivid sensory detail, emotional arcs, complex plots, and meaningful reflections. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Alita-real](https://clawhub.ai/user/Alita-real) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, creative agents, and individual users use this skill to generate varied dream narratives as markdown files for creative reflection, storytelling, or imagination exercises. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated dreams are saved to a persistent local archive, which may retain sensitive personal details if the user includes them. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including sensitive personal information in prompts or generated reflections, and review or delete files under memory/dreams when retention is not desired. <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally creates intense, uncomfortable, or chaotic dream content. <br>
Mitigation: Set clear content boundaries before generation and review outputs before sharing or reusing them. <br>
