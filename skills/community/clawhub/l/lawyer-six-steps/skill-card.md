## Description: <br>
中国律师办案六步法 helps Chinese lawyers structure civil and commercial case analysis across facts, law, jurisprudence, case research, adjudication rules, and outcome prediction, then produce a complete case analysis report. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[happylawyer](https://clawhub.ai/user/happylawyer) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External legal professionals use this skill to analyze Chinese civil and commercial litigation matters, review elements and evidence, search laws and cases, assess litigation risk, and generate a Markdown case analysis report for internal discussion or case files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process confidential case materials and save a Markdown report in the workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Use a safe output directory, limit unnecessary client-identifying details, and review saved report handling for confidentiality. <br>
Risk: Legal search results, cited laws, cases, and outcome predictions may be incomplete or incorrect. <br>
Mitigation: Independently verify all citations, legal authorities, case summaries, and conclusions before relying on the report. <br>
