## Description: <br>
Test product names and listing titles for TikTok Shop conversion potential before going live by scoring hook strength, search fit, and compliance risk. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Brand, ecommerce, and marketplace operators use this skill to evaluate product names and listing titles before launch. It scores candidates for hook strength, search fit, character efficiency, brand fit, and compliance risk, then recommends platform-ready naming options. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace limits, compliance checks, and trademark guidance may not reflect the exact target market or current platform policy. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm platform rules, legal requirements, trademark availability, and market-specific language concerns before publishing listings. <br>
Risk: Product-name recommendations can be misleading if input search data, competitor examples, or brand constraints are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations with current keyword data, competitor research, and brand stakeholders before launch. <br>
