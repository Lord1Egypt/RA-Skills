# 90-Day Competitor Promotional Calendar Guide

Anticipating competitor promotions is more valuable than reacting to them. Most ecommerce platforms run predictable promotional events. Map them in advance, watch your competitors in the 2 weeks before each event, and prepare your own response strategy before the event hits.

---

## Pre-Event Watch Protocol

1. **Day -14:** Baseline snapshot
2. **Day -7:** First movement check
3. **Day -3:** Final pre-event check
 4. **Day 0:** Event start - daily monitoring
5. **Day +7 post-event:** Reversion check

## Response Strategy Matrix

| Competitor discount depth | Your strategy |
|---|---|
| <15% | Hold price, emphasize value in ads |
| 15-30% | Matching bundle or light promotion (10%) |
| 30-50% | Event-specific promotion + scarcity messaging |
| >50% (loss leader) | Do not match - emphasize quality |
