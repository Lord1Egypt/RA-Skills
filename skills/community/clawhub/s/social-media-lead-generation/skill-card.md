## Description: <br>
Generates social media posts, ads, follow-up messages, email responses, and sales scripts that help education providers convert course audiences into leads and customers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External training institutes, online course sellers, coaches, mentors, and education companies use this skill to draft course promotion posts, captions, lead responses, follow-up messages, email nurturing copy, targeting suggestions, and sales closing scripts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated ads, captions, sales scripts, pricing, availability claims, or targeting language could be inaccurate or misleading if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review all marketing copy for truthfulness, current pricing, course availability, audience fit, and platform policy compliance before posting or sending. <br>
Risk: Follow-up and lead nurturing messages could be used without appropriate consent or outside applicable marketing rules. <br>
Mitigation: Send outreach only to contacts with appropriate consent and align messages with applicable email, messaging, advertising, and privacy requirements. <br>
