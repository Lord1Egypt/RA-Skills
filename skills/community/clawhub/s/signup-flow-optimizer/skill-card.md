## Description: <br>
Audit and redesign SaaS signup flows to reduce friction and increase activation. Paste or describe your current flow -- get a structured friction analysis, redesigned flow, and directional conversion lift estimate using the Friction Audit Method. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flynndavid](https://clawhub.ai/user/flynndavid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External SaaS founders, growth teams, product managers, designers, and engineers use this skill to audit signup flows, identify likely friction points, and produce an actionable redesign report for improving activation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Signup-flow descriptions may include confidential customer records, unreleased business metrics, secrets, or private internal URLs. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymized flow descriptions and omit secrets, customer records, sensitive metrics, and private internal URLs unless the user is comfortable sharing them in the assistant session. <br>
