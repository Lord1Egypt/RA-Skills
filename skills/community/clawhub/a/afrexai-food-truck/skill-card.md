## Description: <br>
Provides a food truck operations playbook covering menu engineering, pricing, startup costs, permits, daily operations, route planning, financial benchmarks, marketing, and fleet growth. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Food truck owners, aspiring operators, consultants, and agents use this skill to plan mobile food businesses, compare operating assumptions, organize licensing tasks, and prepare day-to-day operating workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Permitting, tax, health department, and licensing requirements vary by jurisdiction and may change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current local regulations, tax requirements, costs, inspections, and licensing rules before relying on the guidance operationally. <br>
Risk: Financial ranges, route revenue estimates, and cost benchmarks are generalized planning assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Validate estimates with current supplier quotes, local market data, event terms, insurance quotes, and actual operating history. <br>
