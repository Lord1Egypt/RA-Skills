## Description: <br>
D&D 5e Toolkit helps players and DMs roll dice, look up SRD spells and monsters, generate characters, create encounters, and create NPCs using the D&D 5e API. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[capt-marbles](https://clawhub.ai/user/capt-marbles) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External players, DMs, and agents use this skill to run tabletop utility commands for dice rolls, SRD spell and monster lookups, random characters, encounters, and NPCs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lookup and generation commands that contact the D&D 5e API may send user-provided lookup terms to that public service. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering private campaign details or sensitive names as lookup terms unless sharing them with the service is acceptable. <br>


## Reference(s): <br>
