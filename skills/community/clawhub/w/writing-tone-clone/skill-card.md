## Description: <br>
Clones a person's writing tone from sample text, analyzes it across eight linguistic dimensions, and produces a documented voice profile plus a deployable voice skill. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[couriard](https://clawhub.ai/user/couriard) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, writers, and agent builders use this skill to analyze authorized writing samples and create a reusable voice profile plus a deployable voice-style skill. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Private or third-party writing samples may contain sensitive personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only writing you own or have explicit permission to process, and redact private details before analysis. <br>
Risk: A generated voice profile or voice skill could be reused to impersonate the person being modeled. <br>
Mitigation: Inspect generated files before reuse, keep them access-controlled, and do not present generated text as if it was written by that person. <br>
Risk: The generated voice skill may preserve quoted sample text or identifying phrases. <br>
Mitigation: Review the voice profile and SKILL.md for retained quotes, names, or unique identifiers before sharing or deploying them. <br>
