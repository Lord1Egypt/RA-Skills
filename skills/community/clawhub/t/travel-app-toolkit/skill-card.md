## Description: <br>
Generates an interactive, browser-openable HTML checklist of destination-specific travel apps with iOS and Android store links, category filters, search, priority labels, and practical tips. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lmercy](https://clawhub.ai/user/lmercy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and travel-planning agents use this skill to build a destination-specific app toolkit before international travel, tailored by destination, travel style, phone platform, and payment preference. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: A generated HTML file could overwrite an existing user file if the filename is not checked. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the output filename before writing the generated HTML page. <br>
Risk: App links or extra destination recommendations may be inaccurate, outdated, or outside the bundled database. <br>
Mitigation: Review app-store links and any agent-added recommendations before opening or sharing the generated page. <br>
