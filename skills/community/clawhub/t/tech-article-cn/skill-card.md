## Description: <br>
Helps agents plan, draft, review, and polish Chinese technical articles from topic selection through publishing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lanyasheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/lanyasheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and technical writers use this skill to turn real technical experience into Chinese technical articles, internal technical shares, blog posts, retrospectives, and talk drafts. It can also review existing drafts and return structured improvement guidance without modifying the source file. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
