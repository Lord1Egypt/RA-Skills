## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to plan France trips with city guidance, regional routing, food context, accommodation strategy, transport planning, family travel notes, safety basics, and practical logistics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local trip memory may retain travel preferences or other personal trip context in ~/france/memory.md. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving sensitive confirmation numbers or unnecessary personal details, and review, edit, or delete the file when persistent trip context is no longer wanted. <br>
Risk: Travel logistics such as rail disruptions, opening hours, weather, and reservation availability can change after the skill content was authored. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm high-value bookings, operating hours, transport disruptions, weather alerts, and safety restrictions directly with current operators or official sources before travel. <br>
