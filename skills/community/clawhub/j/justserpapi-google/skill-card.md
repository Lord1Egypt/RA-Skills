## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and research teams use this skill to request structured Google result data for search research, competitive intelligence, trend monitoring, local business lookup, shopping analysis, and scholar analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms, URLs, and optional location or localization filters are sent to Just Serp API with the user's API key. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when that disclosure is acceptable, and avoid confidential research, private URLs, or precise locations unless approved. <br>
Risk: The skill requires a sensitive API credential. <br>
Mitigation: Keep JUST_SERP_API_KEY private and do not paste it into chat messages, screenshots, or logs. <br>
