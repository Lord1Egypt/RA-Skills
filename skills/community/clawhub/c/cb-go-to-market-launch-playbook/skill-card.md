## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Market sizing, pricing, legal, tax, advertising, logistics, and compliance suggestions may be incomplete or outdated for a specific country or launch context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning starting points and verify them with current local sources and qualified professionals before acting. <br>
Risk: Launch plans may rely on assumptions about local channels, culture, operations, or customer behavior. <br>
Mitigation: State assumptions, validate them locally, avoid stereotyping, and update the plan with market feedback before scaling. <br>
