## Description: <br>
A medical diagnostics skill collection based on PMPH Diagnostics, 10th Edition, covering 183 clinical diagnostic skills for history taking, physical examination, lab interpretation, ECG and imaging analysis, differential diagnosis, procedures, and clinical reasoning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, clinicians, medical students, residents, and health-profession trainees use this skill as an education and reference collection for structured clinical diagnostics learning. It helps organize diagnostic reasoning, clinical examination steps, lab interpretation, ECG and imaging analysis, and procedure-oriented review under qualified clinical oversight. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Medical diagnostic and procedure guidance could be mistaken for autonomous clinical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as clinician-supervised education and reference material; require qualified clinician review before applying guidance to patient care. <br>
Risk: Subskills covering invasive procedures, sedatives or anesthesia, anticoagulation, emergency ECG findings, kidney-failure handling, or tuberculosis interpretation may create higher harm if used without local context. <br>
Mitigation: Require current local protocols, explicit escalation rules, and specialist or attending-clinician oversight for these topics. <br>
