## Use Case: <br>
External users and career-focused professionals use this skill to turn resume bullet goals or draft notes into concise plans, reusable checklists, next actions, and editable draft copy while minimizing unnecessary personal data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste secrets or unnecessary sensitive personal details while asking for resume help. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for only the resume-relevant goal, constraints, and user-owned notes; avoid passwords, private keys, OTPs, wallets, seeds, session secrets, and nonessential personal data. <br>
Risk: Generated resume wording may be inaccurate, overstated, or unsuitable for a target role. <br>
Mitigation: Have the user review all generated resume text for factual accuracy, ownership, tone, and role fit before using it. <br>
