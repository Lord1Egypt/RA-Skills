Mao Zedong's Methodology / 毛泽东方法论 provides investigation research, contradiction analysis, practice theory, protracted-war strategy, united-front tactics, policy-making distinctions, military principles, and organizational methods distilled from Selected Works Volumes 1-4. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

Analysts, strategists, and agent users use this skill to structure investigation, contradiction analysis, stakeholder mapping, policy distinctions, and strategy reviews with bilingual Chinese-English reference material and templates. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The methodology includes political and adversarial strategy framing that may be unsuitable for some contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review the framing for fit with the intended use and adapt it to lawful, nonviolent, and organization-appropriate analysis. <br>
Risk: The optional helper script processes local DOCX files and depends on python-docx. <br>
Mitigation: Run the script only on documents intended for processing and install python-docx from a trusted source. <br>
