## Use Case: <br>
Product makers, hunters, and launch teams use this skill to plan Product Hunt launches, coordinate hunts, draft engagement, and interpret launch research signals while avoiding manipulation patterns. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted public posts, comments, emails, tweets, community shares, or replies could harm reputation or violate Product Hunt norms if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve public copy before posting, and avoid requests for upvotes, fake testimonials, mass DMs, or vote-exchange behavior. <br>
Risk: Product Hunt research signals such as upvotes, rank, and comments can be misleading if treated as business validation. <br>
Mitigation: Use Product Hunt signals as directional research and validate decisions with customer, conversion, retention, and revenue data. <br>
