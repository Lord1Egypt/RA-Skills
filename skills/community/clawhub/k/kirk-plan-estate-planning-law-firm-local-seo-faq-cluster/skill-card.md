## Description: <br>
Researches local questions, groups them into clusters, and drafts launch-ready FAQ copy for an estate planning law firm local SEO workflow. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External SEO practitioners, estate planning attorneys, and firm operators use this skill to research local search questions, organize them into FAQ clusters, and draft client-ready FAQ content. Human review is expected before publishing or treating output as legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ content could be inaccurate, outdated, or mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human reviewer verify legal accuracy, local relevance, and client suitability before publishing or sending to clients. <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive business or client details during SEO content drafting. <br>
Mitigation: Follow the security guidance: review the skill before use and avoid sharing sensitive private data unless it is necessary and the publisher is trusted. <br>
