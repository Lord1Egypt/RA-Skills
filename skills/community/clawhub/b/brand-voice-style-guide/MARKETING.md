**Tagline:** One session. Complete brand style guide. Every writer on the same page.  
**Category:** Marketing / Branding

**Short description (150 chars):**  
Generate a complete brand voice style guide — archetype, style rules, platform adaptation, and 30-piece example bank. 4 prompts, 1 session.

**Full description:**

Every brand has a voice problem. The website sounds corporate. The emails sound casual. The social posts sound like a different company. New writers guess. AI tools produce generic copy. The founder rewrites everything.

A brand voice style guide fixes this — permanently. But most businesses don't have one because the strategists who produce them charge $2,000–$10,000 and take weeks.

This skill changes that.

**Run 4 prompts in one Claude session:**

→ **Prompt 1** identifies your brand archetype, maps your tone across 5 spectrums, and gives you a manifesto that can brief any writer in 2 minutes  
→ **Prompt 2** produces 20 writing rules (with DO/DON'T examples), a 30-word preferred/banned vocabulary list, and headline formulas for 4 formats  
→ **Prompt 3** adapts the voice for every platform you use — website, email, LinkedIn, Instagram, X, support, and more  
→ **Prompt 4** rewrites your existing copy side-by-side (original → on-brand), generates a 30-piece example library, and produces a Quick Reference Card for onboarding new writers

**The output is a complete style guide** most agencies charge $2K+ to produce. You'll finish with a document you can hand to every writer, contractor, and AI tool you use — and everything starts sounding consistent.

**Works as a standalone guide or as the foundation for:**
- Ad Copy Generator — ads sound like the brand, not generic
- Email Sequence Generator — sequences feel like one person wrote them
- Newsletter Writer — consistent voice across issues
- Landing Page Copywriter — page voice matches ad voice matches email voice
- Sales Script Generator — reps sound like the brand

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct ClawHub Competitors
None identified in brand voice / style guide category (verified April 2026).

### Adjacent Tools

**Jasper ($49/month)**
- Has a "Brand Voice" feature — learns from uploaded copy and tries to match it
- Does not produce a structured style guide; no vocabulary rules, no platform adaptation
- Works reactively (matches existing copy) not proactively (builds from scratch)

**Copy.ai ($49/month)**
- Similar tone selector; no comprehensive guide output
- Infer voice from examples; no archetype framework, no example bank

**Brand strategists ($2,000–$10,000)**
- Full guides with workshops, stakeholder interviews, mood boards
- Takes 2–6 weeks
- Most small businesses can't afford it or don't need the full agency version

**Free brand voice templates (HubSpot, Canva, etc.)**
- Give you the framework (fill in the blanks)
- Don't generate the actual content for each section
- Require significant time and writing skill to complete

**This skill:** $29 one-time, complete output in 60–90 minutes. No blank template. Actual sentences, rules, examples — all tailored to the brand.
