## Description: <br>
Checker reviews outputs from other agents before delivery and produces QA verdicts with specific issues, required fixes, and approval status. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[honeybee1130](https://clawhub.ai/user/honeybee1130) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and agent operators use Checker as a pre-delivery QA reviewer for content, research, outreach, and image outputs before they are sent, posted, or made public. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Review notes are saved to a local artifacts folder and may contain sensitive draft content. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending highly sensitive drafts to this skill unless local persistence in the artifacts folder is acceptable. <br>
Risk: QA verdicts may still miss factual errors, stale research, or broken links. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the verdict as a review aid and verify important claims, links, and source material before publishing. <br>
