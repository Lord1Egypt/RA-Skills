## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Persistent memory may store and reuse user conversation details without clear privacy, consent, retention, or deletion controls. <br>
Mitigation: Decide what may be stored before deployment and provide clear controls to inspect, delete, disable, and limit saved memories. <br>
Risk: Remote memory storage may expose remembered context if it is used without deliberate approval and access controls. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer local storage unless remote storage is intentional, documented, access-controlled, and appropriate for the data being remembered. <br>
