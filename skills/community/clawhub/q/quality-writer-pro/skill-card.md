## Description: <br>
A writing and quality-review workflow for creating novels, blog posts, marketing copy, and technical documentation with structured drafting steps and review checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wdsega](https://clawhub.ai/user/wdsega) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Writers, content teams, marketers, and technical authors use this skill to plan, draft, revise, and quality-check long-form and short-form content before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow includes publishing and verification steps, which could affect a blog, CMS, social media account, or other publishing destination. <br>
Mitigation: Require the agent to show the final content, destination, and publish action for approval before any publishing step is performed. <br>
Risk: The skill is written in Chinese and may produce Chinese-language output by default. <br>
Mitigation: Specify the desired output language before drafting or reviewing content. <br>
