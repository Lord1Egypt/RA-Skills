## Description: <br>
Create, embed, and manage AI chatbots for any website in under 2 minutes using HoverBot. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Website owners, developers, and support teams use this skill to create a HoverBot chatbot, embed the widget on a website, customize its behavior, and train it with documents or URLs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill depends on a third-party HoverBot dashboard and CDN for chatbot creation, configuration, and widget delivery. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for websites you control, verify the HoverBot dashboard and CDN domains before deploying, and enable allowed-domain restrictions where available. <br>
Risk: Uploaded documents, website URLs, chat history, or visitor interactions may include confidential, regulated, or customer-sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm HoverBot privacy, retention, and access controls before uploading sensitive content or using the chatbot with customer data. <br>
Risk: A browser-visible widget key may be reused outside the intended website if domain controls are not configured. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the widget key is intended for browser use and restrict allowed domains in the HoverBot dashboard where available. <br>
