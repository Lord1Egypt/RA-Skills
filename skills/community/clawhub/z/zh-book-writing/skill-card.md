## Description: <br>
中文长篇小说创作工作流。用于创建或延续单本书项目、提炼原作/样本文风、规划章节、起草或修订正文、执行章节 preflight、编号检查、质量门、Markdown 交付、更新本书记忆与连续性。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External writers and agent users use this skill to manage Chinese long-form fiction projects, plan and draft chapters, preserve per-book continuity notes, and deliver Markdown chapter files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated memory files may contain sensitive story notes, private writing preferences, or copyrighted source summaries. <br>
Mitigation: Review local project notes before sharing or publishing them, and avoid storing identifiable excerpts or protected source expression. <br>
Risk: Analysis of source books, excerpts, or style samples could drift toward close imitation or retention of protected expression. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's stated boundary of extracting structure, character dynamics, pacing, and other reusable craft signals instead of copying or preserving recognizable text. <br>
