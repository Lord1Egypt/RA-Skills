## Use Case: <br>
HR teams and operators use this skill to draft recruitment, onboarding, offboarding, policy, handbook, and HR document template materials. It provides prompt-style shell commands and markdown templates that users can adapt to their organization and jurisdiction. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The generic helper can retain HR-related inputs and command history in local files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential employee or candidate details into the generic add helper, and inspect or delete the local hr-toolkit data directory if sensitive data was entered. <br>
Risk: Generated HR policies, handbooks, and offboarding materials may not match local labor law or company requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Have HR, legal, or compliance reviewers adapt generated materials before operational use. <br>
