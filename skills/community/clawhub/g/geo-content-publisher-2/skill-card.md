## Description: <br>
End-to-end GEO content publishing and distribution orchestrator for turning optimized content into multi-channel publishing plans and AI crawler signaling packages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[geoly-geo](https://clawhub.ai/user/geoly-geo) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, content teams, marketers, and developers use this skill to plan GEO-focused publishing campaigns, repurpose one core asset across web, social, newsletter, and documentation channels, and prepare sitemap, llms.txt, schema, and internal-linking recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated copy, factual claims, schema JSON-LD, canonical URLs, sitemap or llms.txt changes, CTAs, and social or email drafts could be amplified across public channels if published without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated publishing assets, structured data, links, and channel-specific drafts before publishing or scheduling them. <br>
Risk: The skill may be invoked broadly for publishing-related requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the publishing brief, channels in scope, audience, and GEO goals before using the generated plan as an execution checklist. <br>
