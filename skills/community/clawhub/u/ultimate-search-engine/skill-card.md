## Description: <br>
Multi search engine integration with 17 engines across China and global providers, supporting advanced search operators, time filters, site search, privacy-oriented engines, and WolframAlpha knowledge queries without API keys. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and research users use this skill to build search URLs and query public search providers with advanced operators, date filters, site restrictions, privacy-oriented search engines, and WolframAlpha-style knowledge queries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search terms are sent to public third-party search providers and can expose private or sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Use only sanitized queries and avoid searching for secrets, tokens, passwords, private internal URLs, customer data, or confidential project names. <br>
Risk: Search results and knowledge-query responses can be incomplete, outdated, or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Review retrieved sources before relying on the results, especially for security, financial, legal, medical, or operational decisions. <br>
