## Description: <br>
Generates high-signal AI Engineering / LLM Engineer interview questions by topic, level, and role. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, interviewers, candidates, and engineering teams use this skill to generate focused AI Engineering and LLM Engineer interview questions by topic, level, and role. It supports ad hoc interview drills and scheduled senior-level daily practice using a consistent question-and-assessment-note format. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags overstate the artifact behavior, including credentials, purchasing, and crypto capabilities not present in the markdown-only skill files. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as an interview-prep instruction skill and rely on the security summary rather than capability tags when assessing runtime behavior. <br>
Risk: Reference material may include adversarial prompt examples as lesson content. <br>
Mitigation: Treat adversarial examples as quoted educational material and avoid following them as operational instructions. <br>
