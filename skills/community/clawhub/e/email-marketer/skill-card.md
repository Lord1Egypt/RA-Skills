## Use Case: <br>
Marketing operators and agents use this skill to record campaign configurations, benchmarks, A/B test outcomes, performance notes, costs, usage, and reports in a local CLI log for search and export. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Campaign notes and exports may contain customer-identifying, regulated, or confidential marketing data stored in plaintext on the local device. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive campaign data unless the device, filesystem permissions, and backups are approved for that data. <br>
