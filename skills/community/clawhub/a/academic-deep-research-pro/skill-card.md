## Description: <br>
Academic Deep Research Pro guides agents through transparent, rigorous research with two-cycle investigation, APA 7th citations, evidence hierarchy, and user checkpoints. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[nancliu](https://clawhub.ai/user/nancliu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, analysts, and developers use this skill to run reproducible deep research, literature reviews, competitive intelligence, and other multi-source investigations that require visible methodology, source quality checks, APA citations, and explicit user checkpoints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill searches and fetches web content, so research scope can expose sensitive, confidential, or regulated information if the user includes it in prompts or approves inappropriate source targets. <br>
Mitigation: Review the research plan before execution, set source and scope limits, and avoid confidential or regulated internal information. <br>
Risk: The skill may delegate independent research themes to sub-agents, which can broaden data exposure and produce inconsistent intermediate findings. <br>
Mitigation: Approve only well-scoped parallel research tasks and require final synthesis to reconcile source quality, contradictions, and confidence. <br>
