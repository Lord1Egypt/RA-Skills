## SYSTEM INSTRUCTIONS FOR CLAUDE

Generate a complete digital advertising and local SEO system for a cabinet and kitchen cabinetry contractor. All outputs enforce the 7 compliance moats in SKILL.md. Non-negotiable blocks apply to all ad copy, keywords, and landing page content.

**CHARACTER LIMITS (enforced in all ad copy):**
- Google RSA headlines: 30 characters max (including spaces)
- Google RSA descriptions: 90 characters max (including spaces)
- Google LSA service descriptions: 200 characters max
- Facebook/Instagram primary text: 125 characters for mobile preview (full copy can be longer)
- Facebook/Instagram headline: 40 characters max
- Facebook/Instagram description: 30 characters max

**HARD BLOCKS (all ad copy):**
- "No license needed" for any cabinet or kitchen work
- "Solid wood" without component disclosure in landing page copy
- "Non-toxic," "eco-friendly," "GREENGUARD certified" without documentation (blocked in ad copy; requires landing page disclosure)
- "Safe in older homes" without EPA RRP certification disclosure
- "Lifetime guarantee" on soft-close without Blum Tandem Plus Blumotion residential + written terms
- "#1" or "best" without verifiable third-party source in the ad or its landing page
- "50% cheaper than replacement" without contractor's actual documented average data

## OUTPUT 1 — Google Local Services Ads (LSA) Eligibility Checklist

Before running Google LSA for cabinet/kitchen contractors in Nevada:

**License verification (required):**
- [ ] NSCB [NV_LICENSE] — active status verified at nscb.nv.gov
- [ ] Google LSA requires license verification — upload license certificate to Google LSA portal
- [ ] B-2 or C-3 classification confirmed — LSA category: "Cabinet & Countertop Contractor" or "General Contractor"

**Insurance verification (required):**
- [ ] General liability: minimum $1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate (Google LSA requirement)
- [ ] Workers' compensation: required if any employees (NRS 616B)
- [ ] Upload certificates of insurance to Google LSA portal

**Background check (required):**
- [ ] Owner background check completed via Google's partner (Pinkerton or Accurate Background)
- [ ] No disqualifying criminal history (Google LSA policy)

**EPA RRP (required for pre-1978 service):**
- [ ] If serving pre-1978 homes: EPA RRP Certified Firm number required; upload certification to LSA profile
- [ ] If not serving pre-1978 homes: add service note in LSA profile

**Google Guarantee badge:**
- [ ] After verification complete, Google Guarantee badge shows on LSA ads
- [ ] Customer claims up to $2,000 per job under Google Guarantee — understand scope before enrolling
- [ ] "Google Guaranteed" in marketing must link to Google's guarantee terms
