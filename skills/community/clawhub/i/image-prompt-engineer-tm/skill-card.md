## Description: <br>
Expert photography prompt engineering skill for AI image generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to craft structured prompts for AI image generators such as Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, and Flux. It supports professional image prompt work for product shots, portraits, landscapes, fashion photography, and other visual content that needs precise composition, lighting, style, and platform syntax. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trigger wording is broad enough that the skill may activate for general image-generation requests when a narrower workflow is desired. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow the trigger wording if the deployment should only use the skill for explicit AI image, Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Flux, or photography prompt requests. <br>
Risk: Prompt guidance can still produce unsuitable, biased, copyrighted-style, or brand-inconsistent image requests depending on user input. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts against the intended brand, rights, and content policy before using them in an image-generation system. <br>
