## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to plan Italy travel with city, food, wine, beach, hiking, family, culture, transport, telecom, and emergency guidance tailored to their preferences. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can store travel preferences and trip history in ~/italy/preferences.md. <br>
Mitigation: Do not save passport numbers, payment details, or other sensitive personal data, and review or delete the folder when that memory is no longer wanted. <br>
Risk: Travel details such as prices, hours, train fares, museum tickets, ZTL rules, and entry fees may change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current prices, hours, booking requirements, and local rules before relying on recommendations for travel decisions. <br>
