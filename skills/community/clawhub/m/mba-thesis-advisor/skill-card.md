## Description: <br>
MBA Thesis Advisor helps MBA students and advisors diagnose drafts, strengthen analytical contribution, rewrite key thesis sections, and prepare LaTeX/thuthesis formatting for submission. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chao1208](https://clawhub.ai/user/chao1208) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External MBA students, thesis advisors, and reviewers use this skill to evaluate thesis drafts, develop stronger theoretical framing and original contribution, improve critical analysis, and prepare Chinese MBA thesis formatting with LaTeX/thuthesis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Thesis drafts can contain confidential company information or personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only on documents you are authorized to share and anonymize confidential company or personal details before review. <br>
Risk: Formatting, signature insertion, template changes, or LaTeX cleanup commands could modify thesis files incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed edits and commands before applying them, and keep recoverable source files under version control. <br>
