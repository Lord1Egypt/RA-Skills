## Description: <br>
[Didoo AI] Defines campaign strategy for Meta Ads - sets objective, targeting, structure, budget, and bidding approach before launching a new Meta Ads campaign. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketers, founders, and growth teams use this skill to plan Meta Ads campaigns before launch, including campaign objective, audience, structure, budget, bidding approach, and strategy brief. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Campaign recommendations may be based on incomplete business context or outdated ad benchmarks. <br>
Mitigation: Review the strategy brief before launch and validate benchmark assumptions with current market research when needed. <br>
Risk: Sharing Meta credentials, access tokens, or raw customer lists in chat could expose sensitive account or customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Do not paste credentials, tokens, or raw customer lists; provide only the minimum campaign context needed and use customer-list targeting only with appropriate consent and legal basis. <br>
