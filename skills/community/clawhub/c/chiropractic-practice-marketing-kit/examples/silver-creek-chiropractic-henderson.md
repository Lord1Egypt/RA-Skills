## Sample Output — Prompt 2A: Sports Chiropractic Service Page

**Title tag:** Sports Chiropractor Henderson NV | CCSP® Dr. Marcus Bell

**Meta description:** Silver Creek Chiropractic — Dr. Marcus Bell DC, CCSP® (Am. Chiropractic Board of Sports Physicians). Sports injury care in Henderson, NV. Call (702) 555-0194.

**H1:** Sports Chiropractic in Henderson — Dr. Marcus Bell DC, CCSP®

**Body:**

Sports injuries respond differently than everyday back pain — they involve high-load repetitive movement patterns, training-specific biomechanics, and return-to-sport timelines that general chiropractic care alone doesn't address. Dr. Marcus Bell holds the CCSP® certification from the American Chiropractic Board of Sports Physicians (ACBSP credential #B-2019-04412), a post-doctoral sports injury specialty that requires advanced training and board examination.

**Conditions we see:**
- Muscle strains and soft tissue injuries (acute and overuse)
- Shoulder, elbow, hip, and knee joint dysfunction
- Low back and cervical spine pain in athletes
- Running injuries: IT band syndrome, patellar tendinopathy, plantar fasciitis (adjunct care — within chiropractic scope)
- Post-competition recovery and maintenance

**What sports chiropractic involves at Silver Creek:**
- Spinal adjustment and extremity manipulation (CPT 98940–98942, 97140)
- Therapeutic exercise and movement pattern assessment (CPT 97530)
- Soft tissue techniques as adjuncts to adjustment

**Return to activity:** Most athletes begin returning to modified activity within their care plan — individual timelines vary based on injury type, severity, and the demands of your sport. We coordinate with your coach, athletic trainer, or sports medicine physician when requested.

**Not a substitute for emergency care:** If you've experienced acute trauma (fracture, dislocation, concussion) — go to the ER first. We're the right next step after clearance.

Call (702) 555-0194 or book online for a sports injury evaluation.

*Individual results vary. CCSP® is a registered certification mark of the American Chiropractic Board of Sports Physicians.*
