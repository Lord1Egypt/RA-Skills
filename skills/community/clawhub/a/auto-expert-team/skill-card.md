## Description: <br>
Auto Expert Team is a markdown guidance skill that frames multi-role expert-team orchestration as a cautionary example and provides templates for task analysis, role design, collaboration planning, execution, and quality checks. <br>

This skill is for demonstration purposes and not for production usage. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers can use this skill to study a broad expert-team routing template and generate markdown plans for task analysis, expert role design, collaboration flow, task execution, and quality validation. It is presented by the skill itself as a cautionary example rather than a recommended operating model. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad expert-team routing can steer an agent into high-impact domains without enough safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Keep activation explicit and review proposed plans before using them for real decisions or workflows. <br>
Risk: The skill may produce guidance related to social automation, legal work, investment advice, or invoice handling. <br>
Mitigation: Do not allow autonomous posting or social automation, and do not use outputs as personalized legal, financial, or business-file handling advice. <br>
Risk: Business or invoice files may contain sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide such files when users understand where the files will be read, stored, and shared. <br>
