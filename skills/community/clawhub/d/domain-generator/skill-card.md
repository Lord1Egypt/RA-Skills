## Description: <br>
AI-powered domain naming consultation that helps users turn an early project idea into registrable domain candidates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abtdomain](https://clawhub.ai/user/abtdomain) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, builders, and developers use this skill to clarify naming requirements, explore brand directions, and verify domain availability through DomainKits before choosing a domain. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Domain ideas, project descriptions, and candidate names may be sent to DomainKits for availability checks. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential business details unless needed for the search. <br>
Risk: A DomainKits API key may be used for higher limits. <br>
Mitigation: Store the API key only in the approved secret or environment store. <br>
Risk: Domain monitoring can create ongoing tracking for a domain. <br>
Mitigation: Enable monitoring only when the user intentionally wants ongoing tracking. <br>
