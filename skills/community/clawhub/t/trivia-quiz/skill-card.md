## Description: <br>
Play knowledge quizzes with facts, categories, and daily challenges for learning, flashcard drills, answer review, and progress tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain-lab](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain-lab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners, students, and developers use this skill to structure self-study sessions, run topic quizzes, create flashcards, review notes, and track study progress from a local command-line workflow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores notes and command history locally, which can retain sensitive study or work content. <br>
Mitigation: Use it for ordinary study material only; avoid passwords, API keys, private work notes, or sensitive personal information, and delete or redirect the local trivia-quiz data directory when retained history is not wanted. <br>
