## Description: <br>
Social media copywriting and distillation skill. Transforms any news, dev logs, or external articles into high-engagement, opinionated tech posts with authentic voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[HackSing](https://clawhub.ai/user/HackSing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, and technical creators use this skill to turn source material such as news, dev logs, project summaries, and articles into concise, opinionated social media posts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated social posts may contain inaccurate factual claims, first-person anecdotes, or strong opinions that do not match the publisher's intended public voice. <br>
Mitigation: Review each generated post before publishing and verify factual claims against the source material. <br>
