## Use Case: <br>
Pet owners and caregivers use this skill for dog and cat care guidance across adoption planning, daily health monitoring, training, nutrition, and emergency triage. It provides first-aid support and care recommendations, not a replacement for veterinary diagnosis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pet emergency and first-aid advice is high stakes and may be insufficient for urgent medical conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for first-aid triage support; contact an emergency veterinarian or poison control immediately for emergencies. <br>
Risk: Reminder or state-tracking workflows may retain pet incident or care details for follow-up. <br>
Mitigation: Enable tracking only when the user wants those details retained, and review stored care information before relying on it. <br>
