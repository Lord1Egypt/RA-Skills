## Use Case: <br>
External students, families, and school staff use this skill to understand the Asian pathway curriculum, NCC course structure, school activities, and study-abroad routes for Singapore, Malaysia, Korea, and Japan. Risk: Admissions facts, rankings, costs, and deadlines may become outdated or may vary by institution. <br>
Mitigation: Verify pathway details against current university and admissions sources before using the guidance for decisions. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad Asia study-abroad or curriculum-consulting questions beyond this school's specific program. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as school-specific context and confirm whether the user's question is about the 科德高中融合部 Asian pathway before applying the guidance. <br>
