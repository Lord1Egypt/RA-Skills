## Description: <br>
Jimeng Prompter helps users turn image and video ideas into Jimeng AI prompts for Seedance 2.0 video and Seedream 4.0, 4.5, and 5.0 image generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sallyface0](https://clawhub.ai/user/sallyface0) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and developers use this skill to plan Jimeng image or video generations, choose the appropriate Jimeng model path, and receive structured prompts, parameters, negative prompts, storyboards, and production guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may retain local visual preferences after user consent. <br>
Mitigation: Review or clear references/evolution-memory.md when cross-session personalization is not wanted. <br>
Risk: The artifact references PRIVACY.md, but that file is not present in the supplied artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm privacy terms with the publisher and add the missing privacy document before relying on the documented retention and deletion policy. <br>
Risk: The memory file still says sessions are automatically appended, while the current skill behavior says writes are consent-gated. <br>
Mitigation: Align references/evolution-memory.md with the consent-gated behavior before publication. <br>
