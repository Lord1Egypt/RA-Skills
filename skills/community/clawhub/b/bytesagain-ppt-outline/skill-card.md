## Description: <br>
Generate structured presentation outlines for business decks, TED-style talks, academic conferences, investor pitches, slide plans, and timing guides. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[loutai0307-prog](https://clawhub.ai/user/loutai0307-prog) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, creators, educators, and business users can use this skill to quickly draft slide-by-slide presentation structures, speaker notes, pitch outlines, and timing allocations for different presentation formats. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated academic, business, or pitch content may be mistaken for verified research, financial analysis, or final presentation advice. <br>
Mitigation: Review factual claims, citations, business assumptions, and speaker guidance independently before relying on or presenting the outline. <br>
Risk: The local helper depends on bash and python3 being available in the execution environment. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill only in environments where those tools are installed and inspect generated output before incorporating it into presentation materials. <br>
