## Description: <br>
Production-grade AI coding discipline for AI coding assistants that promotes thinking before coding, simplicity, surgical changes, measurable goals, consistency checks, anti-rationalization, verification loops, and context hygiene. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[christianye](https://clawhub.ai/user/christianye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill as persistent coding-session guidance for AI assistants, especially before production changes, multi-file refactors, and situations where assumptions or scope creep could lead to poor changes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad multilingual activation wording may cause the skill to influence more coding sessions than intended. <br>
Mitigation: Review activation wording before installation and enable it only where persistent coding-discipline guidance is desired. <br>
