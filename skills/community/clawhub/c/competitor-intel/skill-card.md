## Description: <br>
Competitor Intel guides agents through public competitive research, product and pricing comparisons, market gap analysis, and battle-card creation for a company, product, or market. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[v3ads](https://clawhub.ai/user/v3ads) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, sales teams, marketers, and product teams use this skill to research competitors, compare positioning, pricing, and features, identify market gaps, and produce quick briefs, full reports, or battle cards. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide confidential customer lists, internal sales notes, credentials, or non-public strategy while requesting competitive analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for public competitive research and avoid sharing sensitive internal information unless it is intentionally part of the analysis. <br>
Risk: Public web sources can be outdated, promotional, or incomplete, which may lead to misleading battle-card claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review source quality, preserve citations for key claims, and verify high-impact findings before using them in sales or strategy decisions. <br>
