## Description: <br>
Write Danish that sounds human. Not formal, not robotic, not AI-generated. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Writers, translators, and agents use this skill to make Danish text sound more casual, native, and human by applying informal register, common particles, concise phrasing, dry humor, and natural English mixing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can steer Danish output toward casual slang, English mixing, dry humor, and occasional profanity that may be unsuitable for formal or professional contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated Danish before using it in formal, customer-facing, or professional materials. <br>
