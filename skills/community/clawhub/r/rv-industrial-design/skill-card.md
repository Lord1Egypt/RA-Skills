## Description: <br>
房车（RV/Motorhome/Touring Car）工业设计师技能。专注于旅居车、拖挂房车、商务房车、B型/C型房车的空间布局、外观造型、CMF设计、水电系统、人机舒适、智能控制、品牌体验设计。当用户需要房车设计方案、布局规划、设计规范、竞品分析、CMF材料工艺、人机工程、智能控制功能规划时使用此技能。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External designers, product teams, and builders use this skill to draft RV industrial design concepts, layout plans, CMF specifications, water and electrical system guidance, ergonomics standards, smart-control requirements, and competitive analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: RV electrical, gas, structural, and road-safety guidance may be mistaken for build-ready engineering approval. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's output as conceptual design support only and have real builds reviewed against applicable regulations by qualified professionals. <br>
Risk: Incorrect specifications or layout assumptions could affect occupant safety, vehicle compliance, or operating reliability. <br>
Mitigation: Validate dimensions, loads, electrical protection, gas systems, restraints, and emergency access with domain experts before implementation. <br>
