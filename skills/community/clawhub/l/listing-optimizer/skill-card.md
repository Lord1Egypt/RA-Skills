## Description: <br>
Rewrite and optimize ecommerce product listings for maximum search visibility and conversion by improving titles, bullet points, descriptions, and backend keywords across Amazon, Shopify, and TikTok Shop. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators, marketplace sellers, and listing teams use this skill to turn raw product briefs or underperforming listings into platform-aware copy for Amazon, Shopify, TikTok Shop, and Shopee. It helps produce titles, bullets, long descriptions, backend keywords, keyword coverage checks, and compliance notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive customer, account, or private business information while asking for listing rewrites. <br>
Mitigation: Use public product facts, owned listing copy, and summarized review themes; do not paste personal data, account details, or private business data. <br>
Risk: Users may provide large copyrighted review dumps or unauthorized source material for optimization. <br>
Mitigation: Only use material the user has rights to use, and prefer summarized review themes over raw copied review collections. <br>
Risk: Generated listing claims may be inaccurate or non-compliant for a target marketplace. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated copy against product facts, substantiation, and the platform-specific checklist before publishing. <br>
