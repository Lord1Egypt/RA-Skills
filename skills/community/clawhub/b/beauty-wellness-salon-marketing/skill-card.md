## Description: <br>
Generates a full marketing system for beauty salons and wellness businesses, covering client acquisition, retention, and consistent social media content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Salon owners, wellness operators, and marketing agencies use this skill to draft launch campaigns, service-page copy, social proof requests, retention campaigns, referral programs, social calendars, newsletters, and local marketing assets for beauty and wellness businesses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SMS and email outreach may be sent to people without the required consent or unsubscribe handling. <br>
Mitigation: Use the generated copy only with consented contact lists, preserve opt-out records, and confirm unsubscribe language before sending. <br>
Risk: Generated testimonials, before/after captions, med-spa claims, or promotional offers may need jurisdiction-specific legal or platform review. <br>
Mitigation: Review all claims, disclosures, and offer terms against applicable laws, platform rules, and professional requirements before publication. <br>
Risk: Marketing drafts may include sensitive client details if provided in inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering unnecessary personal or health-related client information and anonymize examples before generating public content. <br>
