Watermark is a reference skill for visible and invisible watermarking, steganography, copyright protection, detection, and digital rights management for images, documents, and media. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill covers dual-use topics such as invisible watermarking, forensic tracking, and watermark attack resistance. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the guidance only where there are appropriate rights, consent, and legal basis. <br>
Risk: The script prints educational reference guidance that may not be sufficient for a production content-protection workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Review the selected technique, legal requirements, and security properties before deployment. <br>
