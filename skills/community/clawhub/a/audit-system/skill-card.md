## Description: <br>
Perform structured audits on code, workflows, prompts, and products, producing actionable reports with severity, evidence, and fixes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Mededdahby](https://clawhub.ai/user/Mededdahby) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, product teams, and agent builders use this skill to review code, workflows, prompts, and product experiences, then prioritize fixes with severity, evidence, and recommended actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may activate the skill for ordinary review, feedback, or debugging requests. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit wording when a full structured audit is desired, and choose a more specific skill for specialized review tasks. <br>
Risk: The skill only analyzes provided material and does not perform external verification, legal certification, blockchain auditing, or cryptographic proof generation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as review guidance and obtain appropriate external verification or specialist review for regulated, legal, financial, or cryptographic claims. <br>
