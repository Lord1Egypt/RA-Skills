## Description: <br>
Miaoji Video Script Pro helps ecommerce sellers plan multi-duration video script matrices with platform-specific hooks, A/B testing angles, thumbnail copy, and heuristic performance estimates. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce sellers and marketing teams use this skill to turn product details, target audiences, and platform goals into structured video content plans for Amazon, TikTok, YouTube, and independent storefronts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can provide performance metrics and campaign estimates that may be mistaken for guaranteed outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat estimated views, completion rates, click-through rates, and conversion values as heuristic planning inputs and validate them with real campaign data. <br>
Risk: User-provided product or campaign details may include sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide product, audience, pricing, or campaign details that are appropriate to share with the agent using the skill. <br>
