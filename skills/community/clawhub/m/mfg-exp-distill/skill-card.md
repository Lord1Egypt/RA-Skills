## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad manufacturing-related questions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat responses as general manufacturing reference material and confirm applicability to the specific process, site, and quality system before use. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes auto-update metadata, so future releases may change operational guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review updated releases before relying on the skill for production, quality, supply chain, or maintenance decisions. <br>
Risk: Manufacturing guidance can affect operational quality, safety, delivery, or cost outcomes if applied without context. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified manufacturing, quality, or maintenance personnel validate recommendations against local procedures and regulatory obligations. <br>
