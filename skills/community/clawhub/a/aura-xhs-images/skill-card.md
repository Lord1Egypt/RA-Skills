## Description: <br>
Aura XHS Images helps agents plan Xiaohongshu infographic card series by selecting visual styles, layouts, and prompt templates for astrology, tarot, and lifestyle content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[luoboask](https://clawhub.ai/user/luoboask) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators and content operators use this skill to turn Xiaohongshu post ideas into a consistent card-series plan, including style, layout, copy outline, and image-generation prompts. The skill is especially suited to astrology, tarot, traditional culture, lifestyle, ranking, and knowledge-card content. <br>
