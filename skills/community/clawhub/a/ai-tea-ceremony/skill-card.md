## Description: <br>
Ai Tea Ceremony provides educational guidance on Chinese tea and incense culture, including tea types, brewing methods, tasting, utensils, tea spaces, Pu'er topics, and introductory incense practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External tea learners, tea ceremony enthusiasts, Pu'er collectors, incense beginners, and tea space operators use this skill to ask for educational guidance on tea selection, brewing, tasting, tea wares, tea spaces, and incense practice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Health, fire safety, incense ingredient, or tea collecting claims could be incomplete or inappropriate for a user's specific situation. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as educational cultural guidance and verify important health, safety, ingredient, or collecting decisions with qualified sources. <br>
Risk: Tea investment, authenticity, or valuation guidance could be inaccurate or overly general. <br>
Mitigation: Use the guidance as background only and seek independent expert review before making purchase, investment, or authentication decisions. <br>
