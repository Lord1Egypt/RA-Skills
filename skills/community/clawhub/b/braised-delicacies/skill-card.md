## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Restaurant hours, prices, queue times, and popularity can change quickly or be inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as planning suggestions and confirm current details with official venue sources before traveling or spending money. <br>
Risk: Venue rules such as reservations, filming limits, minimum spend, or peak-time crowding may affect the visit. <br>
Mitigation: Check venue rules in advance, reserve where possible, and plan alternate timing or stops for crowded periods. <br>
