This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to make Chinese writing sound more casual, natural, and regionally consistent instead of overly formal or AI-like. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may make Chinese wording too informal, slangy, or crude for professional, elderly, superior, or customer-service audiences. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for a formal tone when needed and review generated wording for audience, region, and context before use. <br>
Risk: The skill may mix Mainland and Taiwan wording or character sets if the target region is unclear. <br>
Mitigation: Specify Simplified or Traditional Chinese and the target region when regional consistency matters. <br>
