## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language constraint-driven skill that acts as an extreme constraint engine to identify existing constraints, intensify them, add random new constraints, exhaust possibilities within constraint boundaries, and extract boundary solutions across 6 domains and 30 task types. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill as a Chinese-language ideation framework for constraint analysis, constraint intensification, possibility exploration, and boundary-solution generation. It is most useful for structured brainstorming where outputs will be reviewed before operational use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms may steer ordinary planning discussions into this constraint-driven method. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill when constraint-driven ideation is intended, and confirm the method is appropriate before applying its workflow to general planning tasks. <br>
Risk: Generated constraints and boundary solutions may be brainstorming output rather than verified facts or operational instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for factual accuracy, feasibility, and operational risk before acting on them. <br>
