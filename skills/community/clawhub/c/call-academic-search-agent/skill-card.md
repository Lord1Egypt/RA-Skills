## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through academic search tasks with structured research logging, source review, and final report synthesis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[teamolab](https://clawhub.ai/user/teamolab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and developer-operated agents use this skill to plan academic searches, prioritize scholarly sources, keep concise research logs, and produce cited final reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External academic search and URL scraping can expose confidential or unpublished research topics to third-party services. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive topics unless external querying and persisted logs are acceptable for the deployment context. <br>
Risk: Search results and scraped pages can produce incorrect, incomplete, or misleading citations. <br>
Mitigation: Review important citations and quoted literature manually before relying on the final report. <br>
