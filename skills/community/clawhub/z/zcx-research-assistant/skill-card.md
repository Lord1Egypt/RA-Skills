## Description: <br>
Conducts multi-source research with cross-verification to produce structured reports such as research briefs, SWOT analyses, competitor analyses, and industry reports with source citations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhaocaixia888](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhaocaixia888) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and developers use this skill to research companies, industries, or topics from multiple angles, cross-check claims across independent sources, and generate concise or deep structured reports with citations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security review flagged the bundle as suspicious because it includes staff moderation powers and a full-access review helper. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in a trusted ClawHub maintainer environment; review the helper before use; prefer non-yolo operation; and avoid confirmation-only moderation flows until the target, reason, and account or content impact are verified. <br>
Risk: Research outputs may include inaccurate or conflicting claims if sources are weak, stale, or inconsistent. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented cross-verification workflow, prefer primary and authoritative sources, and label major findings with confidence levels. <br>
