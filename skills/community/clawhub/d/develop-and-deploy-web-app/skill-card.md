## Description: <br>
Build, run, and share a simple full-stack web application with a TypeScript Express backend, React frontend, and SQLite database. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kadbbz](https://clawhub.ai/user/kadbbz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to scaffold, run, manage, and share small full-stack web apps from the current workspace for demos, testing, or review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated apps may expose locally hosted content through a public URL. <br>
Mitigation: Do not place sensitive data in generated apps unless authentication and access controls are added. <br>
Risk: The skill intentionally installs npm dependencies and starts Node services. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated app changes, dependency manifests, and running processes before using the app with important data. <br>
Risk: Demo apps can continue running after creation if they are not stopped. <br>
Mitigation: Stop or remove generated apps when review or testing is complete. <br>
