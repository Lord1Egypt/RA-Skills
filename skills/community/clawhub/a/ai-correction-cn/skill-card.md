## Description: <br>
AI幻觉纠偏 / AI纠偏 is a Chinese intent alias that maps AI correction and hallucination-remediation queries to the canonical VeriClaw skill. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sheygoodbai](https://clawhub.ai/user/sheygoodbai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to recognize Chinese AI-correction, AI-hallucination, and model-correction intents and route them to the canonical VeriClaw install or information surfaces. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generic Chinese AI-correction phrases may invoke this alias when the user expected a different correction workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use this alias only for VeriClaw-related Chinese AI-correction or AI-hallucination routing; choose the canonical VeriClaw skill for the actual tool experience. <br>
Risk: Users may treat the alias page as the canonical install, star, or review surface. <br>
Mitigation: Route installs, stars, comments, and review signals to the main VeriClaw ClawHub page. <br>
