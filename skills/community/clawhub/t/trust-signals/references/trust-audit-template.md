## Platform-Specific Trust Norms

### Amazon
- Primary trust signal: review count + verified purchase badge
- Buyers trust: Amazon's A-to-Z guarantee (mention it in listings for high-value items)
- Red flags buyers notice: <25 reviews, all 5-star (no critical reviews = suspicious), seller with no feedback

### TikTok Shop
- Primary trust signal: video reviews + creator endorsement
- Buyers trust: creator they follow + live demo they watched
- Red flags: static listing photos only, no creator content, no live sales history

### Shopify / DTC
- Primary trust signal: press mentions + money-back guarantee
- Buyers trust: recognizable payment logos, SSL, founder story
- Red flags: no About page, no social media links, P.O. box only address

### Shopee (SEA)
- Primary trust signal: "Preferred Seller" badge + chat response rate
- Buyers trust: local warehouse (faster shipping), COD option available
- Red flags: no local address, no COD, <4.5 seller rating

## Common Trust Signal Copy Formulas

### Guarantee (high-converting formula)
"[Timeframe] [refund type] guarantee — [process simplicity] — [risk removal]. [Brand commitment statement]."

Example: "60-day money-back guarantee — email us once, get a full refund within 48 hours, no return shipping needed. We stand behind every order."

### Security badge copy
"Secure checkout — your payment information is encrypted with 256-bit SSL and never stored on our servers."

### Return policy line (product page)
"Free returns within [X] days — [shipping paid by whom]. [Process in one sentence]."
