## Use Case: <br>
External homeowners, especially users planning residential renovation in China, use this skill to estimate budgets, avoid material and contractor pitfalls, prepare inspection checklists, and review renovation contract terms. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Renovation prices, contract comments, and safety suggestions may be too general for a user's city, project scope, or legal terms. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as general planning guidance and verify major spending, contract decisions, and local requirements with qualified local professionals. <br>
Risk: Structural changes and electrical, plumbing, or gas decisions can create safety or compliance issues if acted on without expert review. <br>
Mitigation: Require qualified local professionals to review and perform structural and utility-related work before execution. <br>
