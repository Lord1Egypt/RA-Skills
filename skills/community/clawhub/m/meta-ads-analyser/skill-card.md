## Description: <br>
Analyze extracted Meta ad creatives and generate a professional strategy report with inline videos and images grouped by funnel and landing page. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bluerockerr](https://clawhub.ai/user/bluerockerr) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, growth analysts, and operators use this skill after Meta ad asset extraction to analyze creatives, landing pages, funnels, and strategy patterns, then package the findings into a shareable report. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ad creatives, landing-page screenshots, and generated reports may contain confidential campaign or customer information. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only with material intended for local processing and sharing, and avoid confidential campaign assets unless the recipient and workflow are approved. <br>
Risk: The finished asset bundle may be sent through Telegram to an unintended destination. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the Telegram destination before delivery and review the generated zip contents before sending. <br>
Risk: Untrusted ad or landing-page text could be inserted into generated HTML reports. <br>
Mitigation: Ensure generated HTML reports escape untrusted text before opening or sharing them. <br>
