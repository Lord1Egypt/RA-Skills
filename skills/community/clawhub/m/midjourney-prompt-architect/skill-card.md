## Description: <br>
Generate detailed, creative, and optimized prompts for Midjourney and other AI image generation tools (Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, Flux). Covers style specification, composition, lighting, camera parameters, and negative prompting. Use when creating image generation prompts, refining visual concepts, or building prompt templates for batch generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Creative prompts may include artist or style references that conflict with content, copyright, or platform policies. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated prompts against applicable policy before use, and replace restricted artist or style references with permitted descriptive visual traits. <br>
Risk: Negative prompts related to text, logos, signatures, or watermarks may be inappropriate for some workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Use negative prompts only for legitimate quality control and keep platform rules and rights-holder requirements in scope. <br>
