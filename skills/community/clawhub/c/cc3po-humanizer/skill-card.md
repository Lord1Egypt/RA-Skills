## Description: <br>
Removes common signs of AI-generated writing from text so drafts read more natural and human-written. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[carloscbrls](https://clawhub.ai/user/carloscbrls) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, and developers use this skill to revise drafts, documentation, and correspondence that sound formulaic or AI-generated while preserving the intended meaning and tone. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewrites may change meaning, add unsupported specifics, or create a false personal voice. <br>
Mitigation: Review the rewritten text against the source draft before publishing or sending it. <br>
Risk: Using the skill to mask AI-generated writing could violate a policy, disclosure rule, or authorship expectation. <br>
Mitigation: Check applicable disclosure requirements and use the skill only where editing AI-assisted text is allowed. <br>
