## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The dialect is a simple Caesar cipher and should not be treated as secret or secure. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for stylistic recognition or lore, and do not encode sensitive information with it. <br>
Risk: The linked lore site could be mistaken for higher-priority instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the lore site as reference material and keep normal user requests, agent rules, and safety constraints in force. <br>
