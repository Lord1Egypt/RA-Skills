## Description: <br>
Build 13-week cash flow forecasts, identify liquidity gaps, and design capital allocation plans for ecommerce businesses navigating inventory cycles and seasonal demand. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce operators and finance teams use this skill to build rolling cash-flow forecasts, stress-test liquidity scenarios, and plan capital allocation around inventory and seasonal demand. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive financial records or processor details while using the planning workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Treat real financial inputs as sensitive and share only the minimum data needed to build the forecast. <br>
Risk: Forecast outputs and capital-allocation suggestions may be incorrect if assumptions, settlement timing, or inventory data are inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Validate assumptions against bank, payment processor, supplier, and accounting records before using the forecast for decisions. <br>
Risk: Financing or capital-allocation decisions can affect business solvency. <br>
Mitigation: Review financing and capital-allocation decisions with a qualified finance professional before acting. <br>
