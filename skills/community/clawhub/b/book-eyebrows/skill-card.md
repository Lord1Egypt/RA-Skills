## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to search for eyebrow service providers, check appointment availability, and prepare bookings through Lokuli. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Booking creation sends customer name, email, phone number, provider, service, date, and time to Lokuli. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the provider, service, date, time, and customer contact details with the user before submitting any booking. <br>
