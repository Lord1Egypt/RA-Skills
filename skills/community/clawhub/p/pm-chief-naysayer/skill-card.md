## Description: <br>
Use when a user proposes a product idea, feature concept, or product plan that needs logical stress-testing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[trient](https://clawhub.ai/user/trient) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, and product teams use this skill to stress-test product ideas, feature concepts, and product plans through strict challenge questions before committing resources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intentionally strict and may be unsuitable when the user needs normal product planning, technical help, or an adaptive coaching tone. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for deliberate product-idea stress testing, and choose a different skill when planning, implementation advice, or collaborative product development is needed. <br>
Risk: Its critique questions could be mistaken for complete product validation guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as challenge prompts and validate product decisions with user research, analytics, and stakeholder review. <br>
