## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Football analysts and bettors use this skill to research a specified match, estimate win/draw/loss probabilities, compare tactical scenarios, and understand key uncertainty before making decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Match probabilities and betting-oriented suggestions can be wrong or become stale as injuries, lineups, odds, and tactical information change. <br>
Mitigation: Review the cited sources and timestamps, prefer official or authoritative updates, and treat the output as uncertain analysis rather than a guarantee. <br>
Risk: The skill may ask for or discuss betting context, which could lead users to reveal sensitive account or credential information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not share private betting account access, credentials, tokens, or payment details when using the skill. <br>
Risk: Public football data sources can conflict or be unavailable, reducing confidence in the analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check important claims against multiple reliable sources and clearly downgrade conclusions when key data is missing. <br>
