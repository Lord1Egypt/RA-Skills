## Description: <br>
Generates compliance-gated, conversion-focused marketing content for asphalt sealing and driveway maintenance contractors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Contractors, agencies, and property-service marketers use this skill to draft service pages, Google Business Profile copy, SEO content, ads, email follow-ups, referrals, and SMS campaigns for asphalt driveway sealing services. The prompts focus on Nevada and Clark County contractor-marketing constraints while producing human-reviewable marketing drafts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States, focused on Nevada (Clark County/Las Vegas) and federal advertising, environmental-claim, warranty, and SMS compliance contexts. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing drafts may include legal, warranty, environmental, HOA, storm-water, or contractor-license claims that need jurisdiction-specific review. <br>
Mitigation: Before publishing, verify license disclosures, warranty wording, environmental claims, HOA statements, and storm-water language against applicable law, local requirements, and platform policies. <br>
Risk: SMS follow-up and referral content can create consent, opt-out, or messaging-platform compliance issues. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm recipient consent and include required opt-out language before sending SMS campaigns through any messaging platform. <br>
