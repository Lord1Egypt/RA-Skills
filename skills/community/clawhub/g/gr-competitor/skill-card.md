## Description: <br>
Gr Competitor helps agents scan competitor landing pages, pricing pages, blog indexes, and Wayback history with Actionbook, then synthesize structured competitive intelligence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product, growth, and market research teams use this skill to gather competitor website, pricing, content, and change-history data in bulk and turn it into comparison tables and positioning insights. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bulk competitor scans can collect data from sites where scraping, logged-in access, or restricted content may be subject to terms, permissions, or legal limits. <br>
Mitigation: Provide explicit target URLs, avoid logged-in or restricted sites unless that access is intended and authorized, and keep scans within site terms and applicable law. <br>
Risk: The workflow depends on Actionbook and a local browser or extension to fetch competitor website data. <br>
Mitigation: Review Actionbook and any browser extension before installation, use trusted sources, and run the browser session with only the access needed for the scan. <br>
Risk: Very large scans can be unreliable or excessive for the intended workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Keep batches within the documented Actionbook concurrency pattern and review generated reports before relying on competitive conclusions. <br>
