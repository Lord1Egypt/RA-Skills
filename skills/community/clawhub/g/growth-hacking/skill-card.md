## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Growth tactics such as outreach, community posting, referrals, and re-engagement can be misused as spam or violate platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Apply the playbook only with appropriate consent, platform-compliant posting behavior, and applicable anti-spam law review. <br>
Risk: Growth recommendations may not fit every product, audience, or market context. <br>
Mitigation: Treat recommendations as experiment candidates and validate them with measured A/B tests, cohort analysis, and user feedback before scaling. <br>
