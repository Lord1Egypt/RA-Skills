This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Startup founders, operators, and advisors use this skill to structure practical guidance for startup strategy, product planning, marketing, team design, financial management, and fundraising preparation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad startup and business-advice triggers may activate the skill in general conversations where the user did not intend to receive consulting guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Review whether this advisory behavior fits the deployment context before enabling the skill broadly. <br>
Risk: Market, funding, policy, and competitive information can be time-sensitive or incomplete, which may make recommendations inaccurate if accepted without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify external facts and adapt recommendations to the startup's current situation before relying on the advice for business decisions. <br>
