## Description: <br>
Use this skill when a PLS, title underwriter, lender counsel, or real-estate attorney needs to draft a scope-of-services memo for a 2026 ALTA/NSPS Land Title Survey before fieldwork. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Licensed Professional Land Surveyors, title underwriters, lender counsel, real-estate attorneys, and developer project managers use this skill to draft pre-fieldwork scope memos for 2026 ALTA/NSPS Land Title Surveys. It helps align Table A selections, state-mandatory items, Item 15 written-agreement requirements, deliverables, certification parties, and follow-up questions before mobilization. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include confidential transaction, title, lender, or property details while drafting the memo. <br>
Mitigation: Keep confidential transaction details out of public feedback channels and use appropriate data-handling practices for client materials. <br>
Risk: State-specific surveying requirements or final scope language may be incomplete or incorrect without professional review. <br>
Mitigation: Have a licensed Professional Land Surveyor and appropriate counsel verify state-specific requirements and final language before use. <br>
Risk: Draft scope language could be mistaken for signed or certified survey work. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs labeled as drafts and require licensed PLS review and signature before fieldwork. <br>
