## Description: <br>
image2生图助手 helps marketing, operations, media, and ecommerce teams assess visual readiness and turn image goals, references, and brand constraints into summaries, diagnostic conclusions, action recommendations, and reusable deliverables. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[allinherog-star](https://clawhub.ai/user/allinherog-star) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, operations, media, and ecommerce users use this skill to evaluate whether an image or image-generation request is ready for publication. It accepts image goals, audience context, reference URLs or image files, prompt text, product details, and brand requirements, then produces a concise assessment with recommended next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts, uploaded image files, reference URLs, and related business context are sent to ai-skills.ai for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for data approved for that third-party workflow, and avoid private customer data, confidential brand assets, personal images, regulated data, or unlicensed content unless the organization has approved that data flow. <br>
Risk: Visual quality conclusions and publication recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific brand, channel, audience, or legal review process. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated diagnosis and recommendations before using them for publication, campaign decisions, or client delivery. <br>
