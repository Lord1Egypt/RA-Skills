## Description: <br>
Builds omnichannel e-commerce marketing strategies from product, market, budget, audience, and competitor inputs, including personas, channel priorities, budget allocation, and a 90-day plan. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[phheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/phheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External e-commerce founders, operators, and marketing teams use this skill to turn product, margin, budget, sales-channel, audience, and competitor details into an actionable marketing strategy. It is intended for planning across paid ads, SEO, email/SMS, content, social media, influencer, referral, and retention channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The user may share business-sensitive information such as margins, budgets, target customers, competitors, and growth goals. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in an approved environment for sensitive business planning, and avoid providing confidential details that are not needed for the strategy. <br>
Risk: The generated strategy may rely on estimates, category benchmarks, or competitive research rather than the user's live analytics data. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against first-party analytics, financial constraints, and current channel performance before execution. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes a global npx install command tied to source text while server-resolved GitHub provenance is unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the package source and publisher before running the global install command. <br>
