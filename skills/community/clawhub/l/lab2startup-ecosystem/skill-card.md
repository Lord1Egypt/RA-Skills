## Description: <br>
Guides users through planning university, platform, investment, industry, and government resource connections for teacher-student startup projects. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[earthwalking](https://clawhub.ai/user/earthwalking) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, founders, university entrepreneurship advisors, teachers, and students use this skill to inventory startup support resources, identify gaps, prioritize outreach, and draft a resource connection plan. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Startup policy, tax, intellectual-property, and funding guidance may be incomplete or outdated for a user's institution or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a planning checklist and verify policy details with the relevant university office, technology-transfer office, tax advisor, investor, or government program before acting. <br>
