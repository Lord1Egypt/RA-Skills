## Description: <br>
Official 看得清 Readable Output skill. Turns dense AI replies into a clearer reading layer with overview, key points, warnings, next steps, and folded raw text. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to reformat dense AI responses into a readability-first layer with overview, key points, warnings, next steps, and preserved raw wording for verification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Readable summaries can shift emphasis or hide nuance when exact wording matters. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the preserved raw wording available and check it before acting on the summary. <br>
Risk: Linked plugin or browser companion components may have separate installation risks. <br>
Mitigation: Review those components separately before installing them. <br>
