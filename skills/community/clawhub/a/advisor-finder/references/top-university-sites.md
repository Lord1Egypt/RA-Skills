# Top University Site Hints

Use this file when the target school is a top university and the site structure is messy.

These are not authoritative faculty lists by themselves. They are fallback navigation hints to reach official department, faculty, people, or lab pages faster.

## Global universities

### MIT
- Main: https://www.mit.edu/
- EECS: https://www.eecs.mit.edu/
- CSAIL: https://www.csail.mit.edu/
- People / faculty often live under lab or department pages rather than one clean directory.

### Stanford
- Main: https://www.stanford.edu/
- Computer Science: https://cs.stanford.edu/
- AI Lab: https://ai.stanford.edu/
- Vision Lab pages are often lab-first rather than department-first.

### Carnegie Mellon
- SCS: https://www.cs.cmu.edu/
- Machine Learning Department: https://www.ml.cmu.edu/
- Robotics Institute: https://www.ri.cmu.edu/
- Faculty often appear across school pages, institute pages, and lab pages.

### UC Berkeley
- EECS: https://eecs.berkeley.edu/
- BAIR: https://bair.berkeley.edu/
- RISELab / successor project pages may still carry useful faculty links.

### Oxford
- CS: https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/
- People directories may be split by faculty, staff, and researchers.

### Cambridge
- Computer Laboratory: https://www.cst.cam.ac.uk/
- Lab and group pages are often better than school-level landing pages.

## China universities

### Tsinghua
- Main: https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/
- CS: https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/
- AIR / related institutes may host faculty on separate subdomains.

### Peking University
- Main: https://www.pku.edu.cn/
- CS: https://cs.pku.edu.cn/
- AI-related faculty may sit in CS, EE, Intelligence Science, or institutes.

### Zhejiang University
- Main: https://www.zju.edu.cn/
- Personal pages portal: https://person.zju.edu.cn/
- Software / CS faculty may be split across school pages, personal pages, labs, and project pages.
- The personal page portal may block direct scraping or return incomplete text. Treat it as a partial source, not a complete directory.

### Shanghai Jiao Tong University
- Main: https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/
- CS: https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/

## How to use this file

When the official department faculty page is weak:
1. go to the official department page first,
2. then check official people/faculty pages,
3. then official lab pages,
4. then personal homepages,
5. only then use scholar pages to confirm direction.

Do not start from Google Scholar alone when school affiliation is still unverified.
