## Description: <br>
Helps users plan content strategy, decide what content to create, and identify topics to cover for traffic, authority, and lead generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mariokarras](https://clawhub.ai/user/mariokarras) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and content strategists use this skill to turn business, customer, content, and competitive context into content pillars, topic clusters, prioritized topics, and editorial planning guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate for broad writing or content prompts when the user only wants simple drafting help. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the task needs content strategy; for drafting individual pieces, route to a copywriting-focused workflow. <br>
Risk: The skill can use local product-marketing context and competitor web research, which may involve sensitive business information or external-source uncertainty. <br>
Mitigation: Review local context before use and validate externally researched claims before publishing or making strategic decisions. <br>
