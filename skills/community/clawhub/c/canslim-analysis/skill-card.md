## Description: <br>
Executes a hybrid quantitative and qualitative CANSLIM analysis on US stocks using a fixed schema and a modular Python pipeline, returning a ranked shortlist. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lkmsteven](https://clawhub.ai/user/lkmsteven) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and analysts use this skill to screen U.S. stocks for CANSLIM-style candidates, enrich quantitative results with qualitative checks, and produce a ranked research shortlist. The generated reports support further review and should not be treated as investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill installs and runs local Python dependencies and makes outbound requests to public financial-data sources. <br>
Mitigation: Use the documented virtual environment, review the dependency list before installation, and run only in an environment where outbound market-data requests are acceptable. <br>
Risk: Stock rankings and catalyst notes may be incomplete, delayed, or unsuitable as investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research support, verify findings against authoritative market sources, and perform independent review before making decisions. <br>
Risk: The pipeline writes local JSON, log, and PDF report files containing the generated analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill in an appropriate workspace and review, retain, or remove generated files according to local data-handling requirements. <br>
