## Use Case: <br>
Investors, analysts, and developers use this skill to structure long-term investment analysis around civilization stage, knowledge accumulation, productivity improvement, and China-oriented opportunity themes. It is intended for structured thinking and research support, not personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The framework may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as research structure only, verify conclusions independently, and apply the user's own risk constraints before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: The analysis lens may favor China-oriented, long-term civilization-progress themes. <br>
Mitigation: Compare conclusions against alternative frameworks, current fundamentals, and contrary evidence before relying on the result. <br>
