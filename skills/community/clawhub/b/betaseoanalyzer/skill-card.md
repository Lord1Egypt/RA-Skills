## Description: <br>
Analyzes websites for SEO opportunities. Generates keyword ideas, checks on-page SEO factors, and provides actionable optimization recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, site owners, and marketers use this skill to fetch a public web page and review basic SEO signals such as title, description, H1 count, word count, mobile viewport presence, and target keyword context. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill fetches URLs supplied by the user, which can expose internal, localhost, cloud metadata, file, or other sensitive endpoints if misused. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with public websites you intend to analyze, and do not pass localhost, private network, cloud metadata, file, or sensitive internal URLs. <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations are generated from lightweight page parsing and may be incomplete or misleading for production decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the findings before applying changes, and validate important recommendations with site analytics or search-console data. <br>
