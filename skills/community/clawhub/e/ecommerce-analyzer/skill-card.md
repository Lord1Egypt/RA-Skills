## Use Case: <br>
Business operators, analysts, and ecommerce teams use this skill to ask an agent for product analysis, competitor monitoring, price tracking, review sentiment analysis, keyword research, and exportable reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ecommerce analysis can involve confidential business, product, store, or customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Set clear limits for allowed stores, products, platforms, time ranges, report formats, and alert thresholds before use, and avoid providing confidential data unless the privacy implications are accepted. <br>
Risk: Monitoring, alerts, and exported reports can spread inaccurate or overly broad analysis if the agent is given unclear scope. <br>
Mitigation: Review analysis scope and generated reports before acting on them or sharing them outside the approved audience. <br>
Risk: Manually installed dependencies can change the security posture of the environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve any dependencies such as selenium, requests, or pandas before installation or use. <br>
