# Community Engagement Playbook

## Weekly Content Calendar Template

| Day | Content type | Format | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | New member welcome thread | Pinned weekly post | Onboard joiners, signal active community |
| Tuesday | Topic-specific discussion seed | Open question or poll | Mid-week engagement spike |
| Wednesday | Member spotlight or UGC feature | Post with @mention | Reward contribution, model behavior |
| Thursday | Question of the week | Simple question with easy answer | Low-barrier participation |
| Friday | Brand insider / exclusive news | Announcement with early access | Reward membership, drive excitement |
| Weekend | Let conversations breathe | No forced content | Allow organic discussion |

## Monthly Ritual Calendar

### Month 1 (Launch)
- Week 1: Founding member welcome event (live Q&A with founder)
- Week 2: First "Routine/Story/Haul" challenge
- Week 3: Community guidelines co-creation thread ("what should our rules be?")
- Week 4: 30-day milestone post + teaser of Month 2 plans

### Ongoing Monthly
- Day 1: Monthly theme announcement
- Day 7: Mid-month challenge/contest opens
- Day 21: Member of the month nomination thread
- Day 28: Monthly wrap-up + stats share ("We had X posts this month!")

## Engagement Boosters by Stage

### 0–100 Members (Seeding Stage)
- Reply to every single post personally
- DM every new member with a specific question about their interest
- Create threads that invite specific people: "Hey @member, I think you'd have a take on this"
- Share community activity publicly ("Look what our members are saying...")

### 100–500 Members (Growth Stage)
- Introduce weekly recognition posts
- Launch first referral push: "Invite a friend who loves [interest]"
- Identify and recruit top 5 contributors as volunteer moderators
- Host first live event (AMA, workshop, challenge)

### 500–2,000 Members (Scaling Stage)
- Restructure channels based on which ones are actually used
- Create subgroups or threads for niche interests within the community
- Build moderator training documentation
- Launch ambassador program with formal perks

### 2,000+ Members (Sustainability Stage)
- Shift from admin-driven to member-driven content
- Establish community council or advisory group
- Annual community survey for feedback and direction
- Publish annual "state of the community" transparency report

## Re-engagement Sequences

### Dormant Member Email (30 days inactive)
Subject: You're missed in [Community Name]
Body: Highlight 3 best threads from the past 30 days. Single CTA: "Jump back in."

### Seasonal Re-engagement
Tie community re-engagement campaigns to relevant seasonal moments for your niche (e.g., pre-summer for fitness, back-to-school for education brands).

## Event Formats That Drive Participation

| Format | Time required | Participation barrier | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| Text AMA | 60–90 min admin time | Low — async, any timezone | Founder connection, trust-building |
| Live video Q&A | 60 min | Medium — requires scheduling | High-engagement moments, product launches |
| Community challenge | 2–4 weeks | Low — async entries | UGC generation, habit formation |
| Peer expert spotlight | 30 min prep | Low — member does the work | Community ownership, recognition |
| Product co-creation vote | Ongoing | Very low — single click | Product development feedback |
| Group buy / exclusive | Time-limited | Low — financial upside | Revenue + community reward |

## Signs of a Healthy Community

✅ New members posting without prompting within 48 hours of joining  
✅ Members answering each other's questions before the brand responds  
✅ Organic threads that have nothing to do with the product  
✅ Members defending the brand in threads without being asked  
✅ Community slang or inside references emerging naturally  
✅ Members who met in the community reporting real-world connections  

## Warning Signs Requiring Intervention

⚠️ Admin posts get 10x more engagement than member posts  
⚠️ Same 5 people account for 80%+ of all posts  
⚠️ New member post rate below 20% in first 7 days  
⚠️ More announcements than conversations in the past 2 weeks  
⚠️ Unresolved conflict thread with no mod response after 2+ hours
