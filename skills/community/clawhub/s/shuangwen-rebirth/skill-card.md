## Description: <br>
Shuangwen Rebirth is a Chinese roleplay and storytelling skill that lets users play a reborn shuangwen protagonist, choose preset or custom story actions, and track plot state through an updating narrative panel. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[uccu](https://clawhub.ai/user/uccu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for interactive Chinese web-novel roleplay, selecting a theme such as CEO revenge, academic comeback, urban cultivation, or ancient political intrigue, then advancing the story through generated choices or free-form actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Casual phrases about rebirth or time travel may trigger the roleplay flow unintentionally. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only when intending to start roleplay, and stop or reset the session when the story mode is no longer desired. <br>
Risk: The save/load flow may preserve user-provided story details, including sensitive personal information if the user includes it. <br>
Mitigation: Keep roleplay details fictional and avoid entering sensitive personal data into saved story state. <br>
