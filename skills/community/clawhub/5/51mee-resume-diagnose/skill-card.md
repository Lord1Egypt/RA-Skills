## Description: <br>
Analyzes uploaded resumes and returns a structured diagnosis with scores, key issues, optimization priorities, and rewrite suggestions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[51mee-com](https://clawhub.ai/user/51mee-com) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers, career coaches, and resume-review agents use this skill to evaluate resume quality across completeness, structure, formatting, keyword fit, and language quality. It helps produce prioritized fixes, optimization plans, and rewrite suggestions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Uploaded resumes may contain personal or sensitive information that is analyzed by a model. <br>
Mitigation: Redact unnecessary personal details before use and only submit resumes the user is authorized to share. <br>
Risk: Resume scores and rewrite suggestions may be subjective or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the report as decision support and review recommendations against the user's goals, target role, and actual experience. <br>
