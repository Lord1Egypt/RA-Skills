## Description: <br>
Systematically analyze scientific papers by mapping figures to discussions, identifying logical flow, and tracking evidence sources. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[larry-of-cosmotim](https://clawhub.ai/user/larry-of-cosmotim) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, reviewers, and technical agents use this skill to deconstruct scientific papers by tracing how figures support claims, discussions, and the paper's overall argument. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent's figure-to-claim mapping or evidence-strength judgments may be inaccurate or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Users should verify scientific conclusions, cited evidence, and flagged logical gaps against the source paper. <br>
