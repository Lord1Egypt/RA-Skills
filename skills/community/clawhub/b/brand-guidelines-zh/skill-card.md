## Description: <br>
Chinese-language brand guidelines generator that helps agents draft complete brand manuals covering logo usage, color systems, typography, image style, and brand voice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[olina1ye](https://clawhub.ai/user/olina1ye) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Brand managers, marketing leaders, design leads, and creative agencies use this skill to draft Chinese brand guideline documents for new or existing brands. It helps organize logo rules, brand colors, typography, image style, and voice guidance for team and vendor alignment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated brand guideline documents may contain inaccurate or unsuitable brand claims, usage rules, or style recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated DOCX or PPTX outputs for accuracy before sharing or publishing. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include confidential brand strategy, unreleased assets, or proprietary market research. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid providing sensitive brand materials unless they are appropriate for the configured agent environment. <br>
