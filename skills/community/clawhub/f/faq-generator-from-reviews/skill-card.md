## Description: <br>
Turn ecommerce reviews and objections into practical FAQ drafts for product pages and support surfaces. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce content, product, and support teams use this skill to turn customer reviews, objections, Q&A, and product facts into FAQ drafts for product detail pages, landing pages, and support articles. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: FAQ drafts may include unsupported product-performance claims if reviews or product facts are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify generated answers against product facts and mark claims needing proof before publishing. <br>
Risk: Raw customer reviews may contain unnecessary personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Remove personal customer information from inputs and outputs unless it is required and approved for publication. <br>
