## Description: <br>
Interview preparation system with company research, story building, and mock interview practice. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AGIstack](https://clawhub.ai/user/AGIstack) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and interview coaches use this skill to prepare for interviews by researching companies and roles, building STAR-format story libraries, practicing interview responses, preparing salary discussions, and drafting follow-up messages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Interview targets, role details, compensation notes, and personal story drafts may be stored locally in the OpenClaw workspace. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential employer or compensation details unless needed, and review or delete local interview memory files on shared machines. <br>
Risk: Interview preparation guidance could be used to overstate experience or create misleading interview answers. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill to structure truthful preparation, and keep the documented boundary that it must not provide false information or guarantee job offers. <br>
