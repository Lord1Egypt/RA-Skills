## Description: <br>
A metabolic chronic-disease health-management skill system that routes agents through assessment, nutrition, exercise, analytics, education, safety review, community operations, service coordination, standards, and supply-chain workflows for non-pharmacological lifestyle support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External health-management teams, clinicians, coaches, and service operators use this skill to organize metabolic health assessments, lifestyle intervention plans, safety checks, follow-up workflows, education content, and operational support. It is intended as an auxiliary workflow and guidance system, not as a substitute for medical diagnosis, prescribing, emergency care, or professional clinical review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive health, community, CRM, and business data without making privacy and consent controls mandatory across every workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in controlled settings with explicit consent, privacy notices, data minimization, retention and deletion rules, role-based access, and audit logging before real-user deployment. <br>
Risk: Health, nutrition, exercise, medication, emergency, and special-population guidance could be mistaken for direct medical decision-making. <br>
Mitigation: Require clinician or qualified professional review for medication changes, emergency symptoms, exercise plans, nutrition interventions, pregnancy, children, older adults, and users with multiple conditions. <br>
Risk: Community, service, and CRM workflows could be used for undisclosed profiling or marketing in a health context. <br>
Mitigation: Disclose any service or marketing use, limit profiling to consented purposes, and separate health-support workflows from commercial targeting decisions. <br>
