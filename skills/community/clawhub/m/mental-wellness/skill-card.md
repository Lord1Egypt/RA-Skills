## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this Chinese-language skill for everyday mental wellness self-help, including mood tracking, mindfulness practice, stress management, sleep hygiene, CBT-style reflection, and counseling resource discovery. It is not a medical diagnosis, treatment, or crisis-care tool. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake wellness self-help guidance for medical care or crisis support. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the non-medical limitation visible and direct urgent distress, self-harm thoughts, or worsening symptoms to emergency services or licensed professionals. <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for Chinese-language mental wellness interactions, which may not fit every user's language or regional support needs. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when Chinese-language guidance is appropriate and adapt crisis and professional-resource referrals to the user's local context. <br>
