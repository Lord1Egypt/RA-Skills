## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-22

Initial release. Dental treatment plan presenter that converts clinical findings into a DRAFT phased treatment plan (Phase 1 Emergency/Disease control → Phase 2 Restorative → Phase 3 Definitive → Phase 4 Maintenance) with CDT codes, per-procedure alternatives, risks, benefits, and informed-consent prompts — for licensed dentist review before presentation to the patient.
