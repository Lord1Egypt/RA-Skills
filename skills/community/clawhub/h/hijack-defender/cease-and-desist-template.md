# Cease-and-Desist Letter Templates

## Template A — Amazon Buyer-Seller Messaging (Counterfeit)

**Send via:** Amazon Buyer-Seller Messages (go to seller's storefront → "Ask a question")

---

Subject: Notice of Intellectual Property Infringement — Immediate Action Required

Dear [Seller Name / Amazon Store Name],

It has come to our attention that your Amazon seller account ([Seller ID]) is currently offering products on ASIN [ASIN] under the brand name [Your Brand Name] without authorization from [Your Company Name].

We are the owner of the registered trademark [TRADEMARK NAME], Registration No. [TM NUMBER], registered with the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). Your listing and/or products constitute:

- Trademark infringement (use of our registered mark without authorization)
- Unfair competition
- [If applicable: Counterfeiting of our product and/or packaging]

We have documented evidence of this infringement, including a test purchase conducted on [DATE], which confirmed the product sold under our brand name does not meet our authenticity standards.

**We demand that you:**
1. Immediately remove your offer from ASIN [ASIN] and all related listings
2. Cease all sales of [BRAND NAME] products not purchased from [Your Company Name] or our authorized distributors
3. Provide written confirmation of compliance within 48 hours of this notice

Failure to comply will result in formal trademark infringement claims filed with Amazon Brand Registry, as well as potential legal action including claims for actual damages, statutory damages of up to $2,000,000 per counterfeit mark under 15 U.S.C. § 1117, attorney's fees, and injunctive relief.

This letter is not a complete statement of all rights or claims we may have, all of which are expressly reserved.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Title]
[Company Name]
[Contact Email]
[Date]

## Template B — Amazon Messaging (Unauthorized Reseller / MAP Violation)

Subject: Unauthorized Resale of [Brand Name] Products

Dear [Seller Name],

We have identified that your Amazon seller account is currently listing [Brand Name] products on ASIN [ASIN] at a price of $[PRICE], which is below our Minimum Advertised Price (MAP) of $[MAP PRICE] and/or without authorization to resell [Brand Name] products.

[Brand Name] products are sold exclusively through our authorized retail partners. Your account does not appear on our list of authorized resellers.

We request that you:
1. Immediately remove your offer from ASIN [ASIN]
2. Provide information about the source of your [Brand Name] inventory (invoice or purchase records)

We are actively investigating the source of unauthorized [Brand Name] inventory in the market and will take appropriate steps to address supply chain violations. Your cooperation in identifying your source will be taken into account.

Please respond within 48 hours.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Company Name]
[Contact Email]
[Date]

## Template C — Formal Legal Letter (Attorney-Ready Template)

*Note: Have this reviewed by a licensed trademark attorney before sending. This version is appropriate for serious or repeat infringers, U.S.-based sellers, or when legal action is imminent.*

---

[ATTORNEY LETTERHEAD OR COMPANY LETTERHEAD]
[Date]

VIA CERTIFIED MAIL / EMAIL

[Seller/Company Name]
[Address if known]
[Email if known]

**RE: Trademark Infringement and Demand for Immediate Cessation — [BRAND NAME]**

To Whom It May Concern:

This firm represents [Client Company Name] ("Client"), the owner of the registered trademark [TRADEMARK NAME] (U.S. Trademark Registration No. [NUMBER]) for use in connection with [product category].

It has come to our client's attention that [Infringer Name] ("You") has been selling, offering for sale, and/or distributing products under the [BRAND NAME] trademark via Amazon.com (ASIN: [ASIN]) and/or through [other channels] without authorization from our client.

**Your actions constitute:**
1. Federal trademark infringement under 15 U.S.C. § 1114
2. Unfair competition under 15 U.S.C. § 1125(a)
3. [If applicable] Counterfeiting under 15 U.S.C. § 1116(d)
4. [State law claims as applicable]

Our client has conducted a test purchase and confirmed that the product sold under the [BRAND NAME] trademark is [counterfeit / not authorized / otherwise in violation]. Documentation is maintained.

**Pursuant to this letter, we demand that you:**
1. Immediately cease all sales, offers for sale, advertising, or distribution of any product bearing the [BRAND NAME] mark
2. Destroy or return all [BRAND NAME]-branded inventory in your possession
3. Provide within 10 business days: (a) an accounting of all sales of [BRAND NAME] products, (b) the source of your [BRAND NAME] inventory, and (c) written confirmation of compliance with the above demands
4. Pay all profits derived from your unauthorized use of our client's trademark

Our client reserves all rights, including the right to seek statutory damages of up to $2,000,000 per counterfeit mark, actual damages, disgorgement of profits, attorney's fees, and injunctive relief.

This demand is not exhaustive of our client's rights or claims, all of which are expressly reserved. Please govern yourself accordingly.

Sincerely,

[Attorney Name]
[Law Firm Name]
[State Bar Number]
[Contact Information]

## Notes on Template Selection

| Scenario | Recommended Template |
|---------|---------------------|
| First contact with suspected counterfeiter via Amazon | Template A |
| First contact with unauthorized reseller | Template B |
| Repeat infringer who ignored Template A | Template C (attorney review recommended) |
| Overseas seller | Template A + Amazon Brand Registry IP report (legal letters less effective cross-border) |
| High-volume infringement causing significant revenue loss | Template C + legal action |
