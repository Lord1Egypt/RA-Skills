## Description: <br>
A Chinese short-video scripting workflow that turns benchmark videos or product context into a structured script pack with hook analysis, storyboards, pacing, calls to action, and compliance checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cellinlab](https://clawhub.ai/user/cellinlab) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content marketers, social commerce operators, and creative teams use this skill to analyze Chinese short-video benchmarks or general formulas and produce script packs for Douyin-style seeding, advertising, tutorial, review, and comparison videos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated advertising, efficacy, comparison, or compliance claims may be inaccurate, exaggerated, or unsupported. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated claims against product substantiation and platform policy before publishing, and keep the compliance reminder section in the script pack. <br>
Risk: Benchmark videos, proprietary launch plans, or confidential campaign details may be exposed to the agent runtime when supplied as inputs. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering proprietary or confidential materials unless they are approved for use with the agent. <br>
Risk: Formula-first drafts have lower confidence when no benchmark videos are provided. <br>
Mitigation: Treat formula-first output as a baseline draft and validate it against category-specific examples before reuse. <br>
