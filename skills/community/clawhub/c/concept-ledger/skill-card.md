## Description: <br>
Concept Ledger helps an agent track evolving terms, clarify definitions, and intervene when naming drift or metaphor overreach creates ambiguity during collaborative discussions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and product teams use this skill during brainstorming, vibe coding, and system design sessions to keep concept names, definitions, and implementation boundaries coherent. It is most useful when multiple ad-hoc terms or metaphors are evolving in the same conversation. <br>
