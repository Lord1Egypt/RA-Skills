## Description: <br>
Applies Anthropic's official brand colors and typography to any sort of artifact that may benefit from having Anthropic's look-and-feel. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hsuyungfeng](https://clawhub.ai/user/hsuyungfeng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
Apache 2.0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, designers, and developers use this skill to apply Anthropic-like colors, typography, visual hierarchy, and accent styling to documents, presentations, and other artifacts that need brand-style guidance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill claims Anthropic brand guidance that may be outdated or may require permission for public or commercial materials. <br>
Mitigation: Verify Anthropic's current official brand guidelines and permission to use Anthropic-like branding before relying on this skill for public or commercial work. <br>
Risk: Expected fonts may be unavailable in the user's environment, which can change the final appearance. <br>
Mitigation: Install Poppins and Lora before use when exact typography matters, or review the fallback Arial and Georgia output before publishing. <br>
