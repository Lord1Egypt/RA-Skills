## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may support official correspondence, filings, meeting records, or compliance artifacts that could be used as records of action. <br>
Mitigation: Route official communications, filings, and records through established human review, versioning, and approval controls before release or archiving. <br>
Risk: The skill may assist procurement or purchase-related workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Keep purchases, contract approvals, procurement decisions, and payment actions under authorized human review. <br>
Risk: The skill may be installed near calendars, document stores, procurement records, compliance filings, or official correspondence. <br>
Mitigation: Install with least-privilege access and clear boundaries around sensitive government workflow data. <br>
