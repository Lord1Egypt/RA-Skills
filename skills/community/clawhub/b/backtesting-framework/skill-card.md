## Description: <br>
Strategy backtesting framework for futures and stocks. Supports moving average crossovers, RSI mean reversion, Bollinger Bands breakout, and custom strategies. Generates performance reports with Sharpe, max drawdown, win rate. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Backtest results may be mistaken for reliable forecasts or investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the strategies as educational examples and account for slippage, commissions, liquidity, overfitting, and paper-trading validation before any live trading use. <br>
