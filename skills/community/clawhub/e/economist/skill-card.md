## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, researchers, and agents use this skill to structure economic research, monitor macro data and policy signals, build scenario analyses, and draft research notes. It is intended for economic analysis and explanation, not financial advice or trading recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Economic conclusions can be misleading when based on stale, incomplete, or weakly sourced data. <br>
Mitigation: Use trusted current data sources, verify figures and citations before relying on outputs, and clearly separate facts from forecasts. <br>
Risk: Research output could be mistaken for financial advice or trading recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Keep outputs framed as economic analysis, include assumptions and uncertainty, and avoid buy, sell, hold, or transaction guidance. <br>
Risk: External data integrations could expose sensitive credentials if configured carelessly. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide brokerage, wallet, paid-data, or internal-system credentials unless a separately reviewed, scoped trusted integration is configured. <br>
