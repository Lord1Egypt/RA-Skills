## Description: <br>
Transform AIGC images into a structured visual essay with narrative sequencing, bilingual captions, artist statements, and Instagram-ready packaging. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[MiLab-Bit](https://clawhub.ai/user/MiLab-Bit) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, AI artists, and brand storytellers use this skill to turn sets of AIGC images into structured visual essays with narrative arcs, bilingual captions, artist statements, posting order, and hashtag frameworks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated captions, translations, hashtags, or posting order could be inaccurate, culturally inappropriate, or off-brand. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit all generated text and sequencing before publication. <br>
Risk: Publishing-ready packaging may be mistaken for automatic posting behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts only; the skill does not post content or access social media accounts. <br>
