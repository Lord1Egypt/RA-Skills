## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, revenue operations, and sales teams use this skill to validate ad-generated leads, score qualification fit, route leads to owners, set response SLAs, and define follow-up cadences for advertising-driven funnels. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: CRM lead records may include personal or commercially sensitive details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the fields needed for scoring and routing, and avoid unnecessary personal lead details. <br>
Risk: Routing, SLA, cadence, or spend-related recommendations can affect live CRM operations. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations before applying them in production systems and use rollback or stop-loss conditions for spend-sensitive changes. <br>
