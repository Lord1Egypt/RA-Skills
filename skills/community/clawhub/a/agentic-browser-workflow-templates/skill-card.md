## Description: <br>
Generate repeatable, audit-friendly browser-agent workflow templates for form filling, case routing, portal operations, research capture, and approval-gated web actions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and operations teams use this skill to create reusable browser-agent workflow templates for portal operations, form filling, case routing, research capture, and approval-gated web actions. The generated workflows help teams review assumptions, approval gates, evidence requirements, error handling, and handoff payloads before connecting to live browser tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security scan verdict is suspicious and advises manual review before installation because maintainer workflows can exercise local authority. <br>
Mitigation: Install only if you trust the publisher workflow, follow the documented confirmation steps, and use the scanner guidance for any higher-authority helper workflows. <br>
Risk: Generated browser workflows may include state-changing steps such as submit, execute, apply, send, delete, payment, or approval actions. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated approval gates before connecting to live browser tools, require human approval for irreversible actions, and capture source URLs, before-and-after screenshots, timestamps, and confirmation IDs when available. <br>
