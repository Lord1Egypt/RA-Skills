## Description: <br>
Story Chain Multiverse is a Chinese-language interactive fiction skill that continues player-driven stories across mixed genres such as science fiction, xianxia, fantasy, reincarnation, urban powers, and fan-fiction-inspired settings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[klcintw](https://clawhub.ai/user/klcintw) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to play interactive, Chinese-language story-chain roleplay sessions where the assistant writes the next short scene, preserves established worldbuilding, offers choices, and can summarize the completed story as a novella-style ending. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad story or roleplay prompts and includes optional adult/R18 story paths. <br>
Mitigation: Install it only for Chinese-language interactive fiction use cases, keep adult branches off by default, and use them only with explicit user intent in appropriate contexts. <br>
