## Use Case: <br>
Agents use this skill when users need .xlsx, .xlsm, .csv, or .tsv files created, repaired, analyzed, formatted, charted, or converted. It is especially suited to spreadsheet tasks that need formulas, visual formatting, charts, data cleaning, or preservation of original workbook sheets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Formula recalculation and workbook editing may rewrite cached values in local spreadsheet files. <br>
Mitigation: Use explicit input and output paths, work from a copy for important workbooks, and review generated or modified sheets before relying on them. <br>
