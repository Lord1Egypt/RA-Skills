**SECTION 5: B2B Cold Outreach Letters (4 channels)**

**Letter 1 — Airbnb / VRBO Host**
Subject: Same-day carpet turnover for your [City] rental — IICRC certified

Opening: Reference specific challenge (5-star review pressure, same-day turnover window, guest hair/pet dander accumulation)
Value: Truck-mount extraction + [X–Y hour] drying time (equipment-spec, not guarantee) = carpets guest-ready same day
Credential: IICRC Firm cert # + WRT if applicable + WoolSafe if member
Offer: First turnover cleaning at [X% discount] — "let the results speak before we talk contracts"
Recurring pitch: Monthly/quarterly maintenance program pricing (lower per-visit rate for committed schedule)
CTA: Book one unit; I'll send a before/after photo report you can share with your Airbnb co-host
Footer: Physical address + unsubscribe (CAN-SPAM)

**Letter 2 — Property Management Company**
Subject: IICRC-certified carpet care for [PM Company Name]'s portfolio in [City]

Opening: Acknowledge their core pressure: tenant disputes, make-ready timelines, HOA standards
Value: IICRC service record documentation → defensible security deposit deductions → reduced dispute liability
Credential: IICRC Firm cert #, WRT for water extraction scope, 5-star Google rating
Volume pricing: Tiered pricing for recurring portfolio accounts (3 units/month, 10 units/month, 20+)
Offer: Walk one make-ready unit together — free assessment of carpet condition + replacement vs. clean analysis
CTA: 15-minute call + one complimentary unit cleaning
Footer: Physical address + unsubscribe

**Letter 3 — Boutique Hotel / Motel Facility Manager**
Subject: Scheduled carpet maintenance for [Hotel Name] — IICRC Firm certified

Opening: Reference hotel-specific context (ADA hallway carpet standards, revenue per room pressure, guest review impact of carpet condition)
Value: Off-hours scheduling (3–6 AM corridor cleaning), fast drying in climate-controlled environment, IICRC documentation for franchise inspection compliance
Credential: IICRC Firm cert #, CCT for commercial carpet scope, WRT if water extraction is in scope
Offer: One floor/wing trial at no commitment — compare your current vendor's results to ours
Contract pitch: Annual corridor/room inspection program at [$/sq ft] rate locked for 12 months
CTA: Facility walk-through at your convenience
Footer: Physical address + unsubscribe

**Letter 4 — Commercial Office Building Facility Manager**
Subject: Quarterly carpet maintenance program for [Building Name or Address]

Opening: Office facility manager pain points: appearance standards for tenant retention, after-hours service requirement, recurring vendor reliability
Value: Encapsulation maintenance program for commercial loop pile nylon (low moisture, no disruption, dry in 30–60 min) + annual deep HWE for full fiber reset
Credential: IICRC CCT for commercial carpet scope + Firm cert #
Pricing model: Per sq ft pricing for quarterly encapsulation + annual HWE (provide example calculation for 5,000 sq ft floor)
Compliance note: "We do not use the term 'steam cleaning' in commercial contracts — our service records document hot water extraction temperature, dwell time, and drying conditions for your facilities management records"
CTA: Inspection + proposal for your specific floor plan
Footer: Physical address + unsubscribe
