## Description: <br>
Manage novel data -- chapters, characters, plots -- from the terminal fast. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[bytesagain3](https://clawhub.ai/user/bytesagain3) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External writers and developers use Novel to manage chapter notes, character records, plot timelines, and related writing activity from a local terminal workflow. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The CLI stores user-entered novel data and activity history in plaintext under ~/.local/share/novel, and exported JSON, CSV, or text files are sensitive copies. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter passwords, API keys, confidential manuscript text, or sensitive personal data; review or delete ~/.local/share/novel and exported files when they are no longer needed. <br>
