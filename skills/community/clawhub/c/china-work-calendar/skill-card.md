## Description: <br>
China Work Calendar calculates Chinese statutory holidays, adjusted workdays, workday counts, holiday countdowns, and vacation-planning checks for 2024-2027. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, travelers, HR and operations teams, and developers use this skill to answer China workday and holiday questions, calculate workday ranges, and plan leave or overtime checks with embedded 2024-2027 holiday data. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Embedded holiday and adjusted-workday data may become outdated or contain inaccuracies. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important payroll, HR, legal, or travel decisions against official Chinese government notices before relying on results. <br>
Risk: The included holiday data is limited to 2024-2027. <br>
Mitigation: Update the embedded HOLIDAYS and ADJUSTED_WORKDAYS data before using the skill for later years. <br>
