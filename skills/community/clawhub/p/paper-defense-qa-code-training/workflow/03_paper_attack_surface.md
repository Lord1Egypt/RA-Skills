# Step 3: Paper-Level Attack Surface

## Goal

从审稿人、导师、同领域 peer 和非专业听众视角生成论文层面的追问。

## Axes

1. Problem and motivation
2. Novelty and related work
3. Method mechanism
4. Formula and theory
5. Experiment design
6. Baselines and ablations
7. Figures and tables
8. Limitations and failure modes
9. Ethics / safety / privacy
10. Future work

## Output

```markdown
## 论文层面攻击面

### A. Problem / Motivation
| Question | Why it matters | Evidence needed | Current answer status |
|---|---|---|---|

### B. Novelty / Related Work
...
```

## Special rule

For every question, produce both:

- a safe spoken answer;
- the exact evidence that should be shown if challenged.
