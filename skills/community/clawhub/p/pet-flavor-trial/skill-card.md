## Description: <br>
Designs flavor-variety trial bundles and assortment packs for DTC pet treat stores selling multi-flavor products such as freeze-dried treats, chew sticks, and similar items. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[RIJOYAI](https://clawhub.ai/user/RIJOYAI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External DTC pet treat merchants and ecommerce operators use this skill to design flavor-variety trial bundles, including assortment definitions, naming, product-page and cart copy, placement guidance, and validation metrics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Suggested bundle pricing, discounts, product-page copy, or store setup guidance could be unsuitable for a merchant's live catalog, margins, or brand requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review pricing, discount levels, copy, and implementation details manually before applying the recommendations to a live shop. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on nearby pet-commerce bundle questions even when the user does not use the exact phrase "flavor trial." <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's scenario involves multi-flavor pet treat assortment or trial bundles before applying the skill's full workflow. <br>
