## Description: <br>
跨平台商品比价与返利综合工具，计算各平台真实到手价（售价-券-返利），推荐最优购买渠道。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[fangshan101-coder](https://clawhub.ai/user/fangshan101-coder) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Shoppers use this skill to compare cross-platform prices, coupons, rebates, shipping, and after-sales factors before choosing a purchase channel. It is especially useful for daily shopping, major sales preparation, high-value purchases, and checking whether a discount is credible. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Price, coupon, rebate, shipping, and seller terms may be stale or incomplete when the agent produces shopping guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Verify final prices, coupons, cashback eligibility, shipping, and seller terms directly on the relevant platform before buying. <br>
