## Description: <br>
Data quality checklist skill for quantitative projects that helps identify completeness, accuracy, consistency, and timeliness issues in price, factor, and signal data. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Quantitative developers and analysts use this skill to guide data quality reviews, diagnose common issues in price, factor, and signal datasets, and draft concise quality reports. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad data-quality trigger phrases may activate the skill outside its intended quantitative data review context. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user's dataset and task context before applying the checklist or recommendations. <br>
Risk: Example Python snippets reference project-specific local data paths and may not match the user's environment. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt paths, column names, and thresholds before executing any snippet. <br>
