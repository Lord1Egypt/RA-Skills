## Use Case: <br>
External researchers, students, and advisors use this skill to plan psychology studies before data collection. It helps turn a research idea into a variable list, measurement plan, sample-size estimate, data-collection workflow, analysis mapping, and ethics checklist. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Statistical design and sample-size guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific study. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as planning support and verify important study designs with a qualified advisor or methodologist before collecting data. <br>
Risk: Recommended psychology scales may have licensing, authorization, or population-validity constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm scale licensing, authorization, scoring rules, and fit for the target population before use. <br>
Risk: Human-subjects research can create consent, anonymization, data-protection, or institutional ethics obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Follow institutional ethics review, informed-consent, anonymization, and data-protection requirements before deployment in a study. <br>
