## Description: <br>
Equips an agent to explain, compare, and troubleshoot MIMO wireless architecture concepts including spatial multiplexing, diversity, beamforming, SU-MIMO, MU-MIMO, and Massive MIMO. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, wireless engineers, educators, and technical agents use this skill to produce concise explanations and analysis of MIMO system behavior, performance tradeoffs, deployment challenges, and common misconceptions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: MIMO architecture guidance can become misleading if applied as design advice without checking current standards, hardware constraints, and deployment conditions. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for explanation and drafting, then validate technical conclusions against current wireless standards, vendor documentation, and qualified engineering review before deployment decisions. <br>
