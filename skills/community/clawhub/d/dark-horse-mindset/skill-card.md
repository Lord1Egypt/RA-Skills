## Description: <br>
A conversational coaching skill that applies the Dark Horse personalized success framework to career and life-direction questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[0xcii](https://clawhub.ai/user/0xcii) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to structure reflective coaching conversations about career confusion, personal direction, motivation, fit, and next-step planning through the Dark Horse framework. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad career or life-direction prompts may activate this specific coaching framework even when the user has not explicitly requested it. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user wants Dark Horse-style reflective coaching before applying the framework to ambiguous personal or career prompts. <br>
Risk: Publisher social and referral links are present in the skill metadata and footer. <br>
Mitigation: Treat those links as publisher context only, and avoid presenting them as required resources for using the coaching framework. <br>
