## Description: <br>
Reviews business contracts for risks, missing clauses, unfavorable terms, compliance gaps, and recommended next steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business users, legal operations teams, and contract reviewers use this skill to triage NDAs, MSAs, SaaS agreements, vendor contracts, SOWs, and employment agreements before signing or negotiation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Contracts may contain sensitive business or legal information. <br>
Mitigation: Only provide contract material you are authorized to share and handle outputs according to your organization's confidentiality requirements. <br>
Risk: AI-assisted contract review can miss jurisdiction-specific requirements or regulated-industry obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the report as review support and have qualified legal or compliance counsel review contracts before signing. <br>
Risk: Suggested redlines or recommendations may be incomplete or unsuitable for the parties' negotiation goals. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed changes against the original contract, business context, and applicable law before relying on them. <br>
