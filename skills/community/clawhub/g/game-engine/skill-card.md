This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to create browser-based 2D and 3D games, build game loops, implement rendering and controls, add physics and collision detection, and prepare web games for publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Example games may rely on third-party dependencies, frameworks, or CDN sources that change over time. <br>
Mitigation: Review dependencies and CDN sources before shipping a game, and pin versions where practical. <br>
Risk: Examples may use browser capabilities such as sensors, vibration, microphone, multiplayer networking, storage, or audio. <br>
Mitigation: Make those behaviors clear to players with permission prompts and opt-out controls. <br>
Risk: Game templates and generated code may not match the performance, privacy, or platform requirements of a shipped game. <br>
Mitigation: Review, test, and scan implementations before deployment. <br>
