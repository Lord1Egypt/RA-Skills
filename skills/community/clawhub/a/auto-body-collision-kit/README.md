## The 7 Compliance Moats

| # | Moat | What it blocks | Why competitors miss it |
|---|------|---------------|------------------------|
| 1 | OSHA Isocyanate / Spray Booth [ANCHOR] | "Fume-free refinishing," "safe non-toxic paint" | Occupational safety standard not in marketing databases |
| 2 | EPA NESHAP Subpart 6H / AQMD | "Eco-friendly shop," "zero-emission painting" | 40 CFR Part 63 HHHHHH unknown to all marketing AI |
| 3 | EPA RCRA Hazardous Waste | "EPA-certified disposal" without manifest | RCRA generator categories unknown to marketing tools |
| 4 | I-CAR / ASE / OEM Certification Accuracy | "ASE-certified shop" (shops can't hold ASE certs) | Industry credential misrepresentation is ubiquitous |
| 5 | OEM vs. Aftermarket/LKQ Parts Disclosure | "Genuine OEM parts" without sourcing documentation | Insurance fraud exposure invisible to marketing AI |
| 6 | FTC / NRS 598 Warranty & Estimate Gate | "Lifetime guarantee" without terms; deductible waiver | Magnuson-Moss + NRS 598 unknown to national tools |
| 7 | Nevada NRS 487 Salvage Title Advertising | "Like new" / "Carfax clean" on rebuilt-title vehicles | Nevada-specific title brand law not in training data |
