# Minimum Advertised Price (MAP) Policy — Template

This document is what you hand every wholesale account. Also publish it on the wholesale portion of your website.

## [Brand Name] Minimum Advertised Price Policy

**Effective [YYYY-MM-DD] · Version [X.Y]**

### Purpose

This MAP policy protects the value of the [Brand Name] brand, supports the investment our retail partners make in displaying and promoting our products, and maintains a level playing field across channels.

### What this policy is

MAP is the minimum price at which [Brand Name] products may be **advertised or displayed publicly** — including but not limited to:

- Retailer websites.
- Online marketplaces (Amazon, eBay, Walmart.com, etc.).
- Print and digital advertising.
- Physical signage and posted prices.
- Email promotions and social media posts.

### What this policy is not

MAP does **not** dictate the price at which a retailer sells. A retailer is free to sell below MAP at the point of sale (e.g., via "add to cart to see price," in-cart discounts, or a clerk-applied markdown). MAP only governs the **advertised** price.

### The MAP price

The MAP price is set at **[X]% below MSRP** for each SKU. A table of current MAP prices per SKU is published at [URL] and updated within 15 days of any change.

### Permitted exceptions

MAP does not apply to:

- Closeout of discontinued SKUs (in writing from [Brand Name]).
- Demonstrably damaged or refurbished stock, sold as such.
- Holiday sale windows announced by [Brand Name] (e.g., Black Friday, Memorial Day), with specific dates and prices communicated 30 days in advance.
- "Loyalty program" or "member price" gated behind a login, not publicly visible.

### Enforcement

1. **Warning 1**: we notify the retailer via email, with 72 hours to correct the advertised price.
2. **Warning 2**: a second violation within 90 days triggers a second email notice, with 48 hours to correct.
3. **Suspension**: a third violation within 90 days results in a 30-day account suspension. No new orders shipped during the suspension.
4. **Termination**: repeated or egregious violations may result in termination of the wholesale account.

Enforcement is at our sole discretion and applied uniformly across accounts. We use [MAP monitoring service, e.g., Trackstreet / Price2Spy] to detect violations.

### Not a resale price agreement

This is a unilateral policy adopted by [Brand Name]. We do not require retailers to agree to it. Compliance is voluntary, and our sole remedy for non-compliance is suspension or termination of the wholesale account. Nothing in this policy creates a horizontal or vertical price-fixing arrangement.
