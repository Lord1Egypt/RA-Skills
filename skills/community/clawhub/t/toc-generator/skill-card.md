## Description: <br>
Helps agents add, optimize, or audit table of contents guidance for long-form articles, guides, whitepapers, and other structured content. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kostja94](https://clawhub.ai/user/kostja94) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, content strategists, and SEO practitioners use this skill to plan TOC placement, heading structure, jump-link IDs, semantic HTML, accessibility, mobile behavior, and SEO checks for long-form web content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may read local project context files to tailor TOC recommendations, and those files can contain sensitive project information. <br>
Mitigation: Review `.claude/project-context.md` and `.cursor/project-context.md` before use and remove sensitive details that should not influence generated guidance. <br>
