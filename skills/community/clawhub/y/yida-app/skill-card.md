## Description: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and app builders use this skill to turn a high-level Yida application request into requirements, pages, optional forms, custom page code, and a published application. The workflow emphasizes corpId consistency checks and session-aware publishing before creating or updating real Yida resources. <br>
