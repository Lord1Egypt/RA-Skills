## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Briefings depend on current web research and can become inaccurate if sources are stale, incomplete, or weak. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current developments with reliable sources, use exact calendar dates, and keep source links close to the claims they support. <br>
Risk: Generated Markdown or HTML may still contain placeholders or unreviewed claims before publication. <br>
Mitigation: Review the rendered briefing, replace all placeholders, and confirm every major story before publishing or sharing. <br>
Risk: Unsupported synthesis can make a news briefing misleading even when individual facts are sourced. <br>
Mitigation: Label inferences clearly, avoid grand conclusions not supported by the source set, and revise against the editorial standards rubric. <br>
