# Brand Voice & Style Guide Generator

**The foundation skill that makes every other marketing skill better.**

---

## The Problem

Your brand has a website, email list, social accounts, and a support team. Four different channels. Four different voices. None of them sound like each other. New writers guess. The founder rewrites everything. Contractors produce generic copy. The brand sounds like a committee.

A brand voice style guide fixes this — but most businesses never have one because the human writers who produce them charge $2,000–$10,000 and take weeks.

---

## What This Skill Produces

Run 4 prompts in one Claude session and you have a complete Brand Voice & Style Guide:

**Prompt 1 — Brand Voice Discovery & Personality Profile**
Brand archetype, tone spectrum, 5 personality adjectives with examples, sounds-like / never-sounds-like sentence banks, brand voice manifesto, one-line voice summary.

**Prompt 2 — Writing Style Rules & Grammar Guide**
20 writing rules (with DO/DON'T examples), 30-word preferred/banned vocabulary list, jargon policy, reading level target, headline formulas for 4 formats.

**Prompt 3 — Platform Adaptation Guide**
Per-platform tone adjustment, format rules, content pillars, and example posts for every channel the brand uses (website, email, LinkedIn, Instagram, X, TikTok, YouTube, support, and more).

**Prompt 4 — Brand Voice Example Bank**
Side-by-side rewrites of your existing copy (original → on-brand), 30-piece example library across 6 formats, Quick Reference Card for new writer onboarding, 5-question brand voice quiz with answer key.

## Quick Start

1. Gather: business name, target audience description, 3 adjectives, 3 admired brands, 3 brands that feel wrong
2. Run Prompt 1 → read the output → adjust if anything feels off before continuing
3. Run Prompt 2 using the Prompt 1 output + your style preferences
4. Run Prompt 3 — check the boxes for your active platforms
5. Run Prompt 4 — paste 5–10 samples of your existing copy (any format)
6. Save the output as your brand voice guide. Share with every writer, contractor, and AI tool you use.

**Total time:** 60–90 minutes  
**Output:** A guide most agencies charge $2K+ to produce
