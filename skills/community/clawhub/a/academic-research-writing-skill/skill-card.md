## Description: <br>
Helps users develop clear, logically structured postgraduate academic research writing for proposals, literature reviews, hypotheses, methods, ethics content, and bilingual Chinese-English polishing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[hoffnung131](https://clawhub.ai/user/hoffnung131) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, researchers, and academic writers use this skill to structure, draft, translate, and polish postgraduate research materials such as proposals, literature reviews, study objectives, methodology sections, statistical plans, and ethics application text. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated citations, research claims, clinical language, statistical plans, or ethics text may be incomplete or inaccurate if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Verify claims, references, methods, clinical wording, analysis plans, and ethics content against authoritative sources and institutional requirements before submission. <br>
Risk: Academic drafts may include sensitive unpublished research information or patient-identifying data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive, confidential, unpublished, or patient-identifying data unless processing it with the agent is acceptable under the user's privacy and compliance obligations. <br>
