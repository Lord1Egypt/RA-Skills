## Description: <br>
The Answer Book / 答案之书 lets a user flip to a random or selected page and receive a short philosophical answer in English or Chinese. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[enihsago](https://clawhub.ai/user/enihsago) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill as a novelty oracle-style answer book for light yes/no, guidance, or wisdom-style prompts. It returns a short answer and page number, matching English or Chinese output to the user's language. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat broad oracle-style answers as substantive advice. <br>
Mitigation: Present outputs as novelty guidance and avoid using them for consequential financial, medical, legal, purchasing, or safety decisions. <br>
Risk: The server-listed crypto and purchase capability tags do not appear to match the observed local answer-book behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Review and correct capability metadata before enabling policy-sensitive routing, procurement, or crypto-related workflows. <br>
