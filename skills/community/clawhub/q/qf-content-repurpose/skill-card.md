## Description: <br>
Transforms a single piece of content into platform-optimized drafts for Xiaohongshu, WeChat Official Accounts, Douyin, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and similar channels while preserving the core message. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, marketers, founders, and content teams use this skill to repurpose articles, transcripts, announcements, and research summaries into channel-specific social and long-form drafts. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated posts may contain inaccurate claims, unsuitable tone, or wording that creates business, legal, or reputational risk. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated draft before publishing, with attention to factual claims, target language, audience fit, and sensitive wording. <br>
Risk: Platform adaptations can overfit generic posting conventions and miss the user's brand voice or current platform norms. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as a draft and adjust titles, hashtags, timing, and calls to action against the intended campaign and channel. <br>
