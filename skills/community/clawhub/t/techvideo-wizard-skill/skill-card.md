## Description: <br>
Interactive wizard that gathers product video requirements through a structured step-by-step flow and generates a professional Markdown narration script. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[neshoir](https://clawhub.ai/user/neshoir) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, product, and technical content teams use this skill to turn product video ideas into structured Chinese Markdown narration scripts for SuperMap GIS demonstrations, technical feature introductions, operational walkthroughs, and promotional short videos. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search queries can expose unreleased product names, campaign plans, or internal terminology to public platforms. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential launch details or internal terminology unless public search exposure is acceptable. <br>
Risk: The skill writes the final video script to a local Markdown file, which can persist sensitive product or campaign details. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated script contents and file location before sharing, committing, or uploading the Markdown file. <br>
