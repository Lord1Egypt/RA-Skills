## Description: <br>
Multi-platform content generator for Chinese social media. Produces publish-ready posts for Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, WeChat Official Accounts, and Douyin with platform-native formatting, hooks, and CTAs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content creators, marketers, and agents use this skill to draft platform-native Chinese social media posts, articles, and short-video scripts from a topic, target platform, desired length, tone, and optional image guidance. <br>

Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can activate on broad writing prompts and may produce publishable social content that still needs editorial, factual, policy, and platform-compliance review. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when Chinese social media content is intended, verify cited data and claims before publication, and review outputs against the skill's quality checklist and banned-word guidance. <br>
Risk: The artifact references an external seo-optimizer check that is not included in the evidence bundle. <br>
Mitigation: Do not assume that helper exists or grant additional tool, account, or file access unless it is separately reviewed. <br>
