## Description: <br>
Popeye Analysis is a Chinese-language assistant for data analysis, decision support, and problem diagnosis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiangtaoid](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiangtaoid) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and teams use this skill to structure data reviews, compare decision options, diagnose business or project issues, and produce practical next-step recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide sensitive business data, personal details, or screenshots for analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Review inputs before use, minimize sensitive data, and follow the organization's privacy and data-handling requirements. <br>
Risk: Decision or diagnosis output could be mistaken for professional medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as structured analysis support and require qualified human review for professional or safety-critical decisions. <br>
