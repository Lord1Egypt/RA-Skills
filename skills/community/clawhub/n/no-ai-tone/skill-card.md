## Description: <br>
Checks and rewrites Chinese, English, and Japanese user-facing copy to reduce AI-generated tone and make it sound more natural. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[is-xins-xiaobai](https://clawhub.ai/user/is-xins-xiaobai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and content teams use this skill to audit and rewrite homepage text, product descriptions, marketing copy, emails, social posts, and brand messaging in Chinese, English, or Japanese. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill applies aggressive and subjective style rules that may remove intentional brand voice or culturally specific phrasing. <br>
Mitigation: Review rewritten copy against the intended audience, brand voice, and locale before publishing. <br>
Risk: Optional in-place edits can change documents the user points the agent at. <br>
Mitigation: Use in-place editing only for documents the user is comfortable modifying, and review diffs before accepting changes. <br>
