## Description: <br>
E-E-A-T SEO Optimization Guide helps agents produce guidance for building Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness signals for AI search engines and Google. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External SEO practitioners, content teams, and agents use this skill to audit and improve content for E-E-A-T signals, schema markup, author credibility, citations, and AI-search visibility. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Templates may lead users to publish author details, photos, credentials, profile links, or other personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Publish only author information approved for public use and remove private or sensitive details before release. <br>
Risk: SEO authority guidance can be misused to create unverifiable citations, credentials, or authority claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify citations, credentials, and claims before publishing, and do not fabricate authority signals. <br>
