## Description: <br>
Chinese-language self-reflection and habit-building guidance that combines Wang Yangming's unity of knowledge and action, Adlerian purpose analysis, small-action behavior design, and a one-day life reset protocol. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[chase-zhouchang](https://clawhub.ai/user/chase-zhouchang) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for Chinese-language self-reflection, habit formation, procrastination analysis, and translating goals into small concrete actions. It supports reflective journaling and personal planning, not therapy, medical advice, or addiction treatment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake the self-reflection and habit-building material for therapy, medical advice, or addiction treatment. <br>
Mitigation: Present it as personal reflection and planning support, and direct users to qualified professionals for medical, mental-health, addiction, or crisis concerns. <br>
Risk: Some prompts may ask users to reflect on sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid collecting or sharing highly sensitive personal information unless necessary, and remind users to keep private reflections in a trusted environment. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes physically risky suggestions such as cold exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Advise users to skip practices that are unsafe for their health or environment and to seek professional guidance before physically stressful activities. <br>
