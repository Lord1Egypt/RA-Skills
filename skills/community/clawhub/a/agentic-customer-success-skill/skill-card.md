## Description: <br>
Create customer success and account management artifacts for CompleteTech LLC agentic development clients across contact routing, follow-up tracking, health reviews, renewal planning, expansion briefs, QBRs, escalations, and adoption check-ins. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[completetech](https://clawhub.ai/user/completetech) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Customer success, account management, and delivery teams use this skill to draft relationship artifacts from verified account facts. It helps agents organize contacts, commitments, risk, adoption, renewal, expansion, and approval-sensitive communications without inventing client details. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated customer success drafts may contain unverified client details or unsupported assumptions if source facts are incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Review drafts before sending, keep unknown facts as TBD or open questions, and separate facts from assumptions and recommendations. <br>
Risk: Approval-sensitive communications can affect billing, legal, support, renewal, testimonial, referral, executive, or at-risk account workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Keep these communications under human review before final use and require client approval before publishing testimonials, named references, referrals, public outcomes, or executive summaries. <br>
Risk: The local renderer writes to user-selected output paths. <br>
Mitigation: Choose output paths deliberately and review generated file locations before running renderer commands. <br>
