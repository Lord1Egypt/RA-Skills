## Description: <br>
Create a polished explainer handout with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for a estate planning law firm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Estate planning attorneys and law firm operators use this skill to draft client education handouts with FAQs, visuals, and clear next steps. The output is intended as a drafting aid and should be reviewed before client distribution. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Estate planning information can be jurisdiction-specific or unsuitable for a particular client. <br>
Mitigation: Verify jurisdiction-specific accuracy and have a qualified attorney review the handout before client use. <br>
Risk: Client facts used to personalize a handout may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide sensitive client information unless the operating environment is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Generated education materials may be mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a drafting aid and review it before sharing with clients or relying on it as legal advice. <br>
