## Description: <br>
Comprehensive pathology skill collection based on People's Medical Publishing House "Pathology" 10th Edition, covering inflammation, tumors, infection, metabolism, immunology, and major organ-system pathology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Medical students, pathologists, clinical researchers, and public health workers use this skill as an educational and clinician-reviewed pathology reference for disease mechanisms, diagnostic criteria, molecular subtyping, and case-oriented pathology analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can produce pathology diagnosis, molecular subtyping, and treatment-support guidance that may be misused as standalone medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs only as educational or clinician-reviewed reference material, and verify recommendations against qualified medical professionals, current official guidelines, and local protocols. <br>
Risk: Outputs may be inappropriate for emergency triage or high-stakes care decisions such as cancer therapy selection or anticoagulation and thrombolysis decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on the skill for emergency or high-stakes treatment decisions; escalate to qualified care teams and current institutional guidance. <br>
