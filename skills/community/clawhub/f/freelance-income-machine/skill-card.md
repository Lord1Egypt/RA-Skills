## Description: <br>
Finds crypto-native freelance gigs, scores them for fit, helps build a CryptoGig profile, and can guide proposal submission. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers and operators use this skill to find crypto-native freelance opportunities, score them against user criteria, prepare profile/service content, and manage proposal workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can guide an agent to submit freelance proposals and create platform activity without clear approval controls. <br>
Mitigation: Use dry-run mode first and require human review before profile changes, proposal submissions, or other platform actions. <br>
Risk: Platform credentials and earnings_log.json may expose sensitive business or financial metadata. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing credentials with untrusted runtimes and protect or redact generated earnings logs. <br>
