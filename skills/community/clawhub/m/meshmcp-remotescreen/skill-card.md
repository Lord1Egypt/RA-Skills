## Description: <br>
Plan consent-based screen-support review steps for owned Android devices using only user-provided context, privacy boundaries, safe next steps, and stop conditions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zack-dev-cm](https://clawhub.ai/user/zack-dev-cm) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External support helpers and device owners use this skill to plan supervised, consent-based Android screen-support review checklists from user-provided screenshots or descriptions while preserving privacy boundaries. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Support screenshots or descriptions may expose private notifications, account details, contacts, or payment information. <br>
Mitigation: Ask users to redact private content before sharing and keep the checklist limited to user-provided context. <br>
Risk: A support helper may proceed without clear device-owner consent or supervision. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm ownership, consent boundaries, and stop conditions before continuing, and stop when consent is unclear. <br>
