## Description: <br>
Helps product managers turn early software product ideas into structured PRDs, user stories, workflow details, data rules, and risk notes for design and engineering teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[weslon1987](https://clawhub.ai/user/weslon1987) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product managers, founders, and delivery teams use this skill to clarify goals, break product ideas into modules and workflows, and produce concise requirements documents suitable for handoff to design and engineering teams. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated PRDs and requirement notes may contain business assumptions or gaps that are not valid for the user's actual product context. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated documents with product, design, and engineering stakeholders before using them for delivery planning. <br>
Risk: Requirements can become over-scoped if the user provides broad goals without priority or MVP constraints. <br>
Mitigation: Provide clear goals, target users, priority levels, and MVP boundaries before asking the skill to produce final documentation. <br>
