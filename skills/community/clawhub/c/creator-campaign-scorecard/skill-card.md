## Description: <br>
Evaluate creator campaign plans before launch to identify readiness gaps, performance risks, and recommended next actions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Campaign operators and marketers use this skill to score creator, affiliate, or TikTok Shop campaign plans before launch, compare alternatives, and prioritize blockers or fixes before committing spend. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Campaign plans may include confidential budgets, creator contracts, unreleased strategy, or sensitive compliance details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only information appropriate for the agent session and redact sensitive commercial or compliance details when needed. <br>
Risk: The scorecard can guide launch decisions but is not a substitute for legal, financial, or post-launch analytics review. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as an operational readiness review and route legal, financial, or live-performance questions to the appropriate review process. <br>
