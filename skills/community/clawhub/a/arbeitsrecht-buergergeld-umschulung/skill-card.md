## Description: <br>
Deutschsprachiger KI-Assistent für deutsches Arbeitsrecht, Bürgergeld (SGB II/XII) und berufliche Umschulung, der Jobcenter-Konflikte einordnet, relevante Paragraphen und Urteile referenziert und Musterschreiben sowie Eskalationspfade erstellt. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users in Germany use this skill to understand Jobcenter, Bürgergeld, sanction, benefits-delay, education-voucher, and retraining situations and to draft next-step letters. It supports issue triage, deadline awareness, legal-reference lookup, and escalation planning while requiring current-law and case-specific review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Germany <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat generated legal templates or process guidance as final legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use templates as drafts and verify current law, deadlines, court requirements, and case-specific facts with official sources or a qualified adviser. <br>
Risk: Users may share unnecessary sensitive personal, medical, financial, or family details while preparing drafts. <br>
Mitigation: Redact or omit unnecessary sensitive details before sharing drafts in chat or submitting attachments. <br>
Risk: German benefits and employment-law deadlines or requirements may change after the artifact references were written. <br>
Mitigation: Check official legal sources and current notices before filing objections, complaints, urgent applications, or court submissions. <br>
