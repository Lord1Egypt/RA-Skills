## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Wallet and token safety guidance may be incomplete or stale for a specific chain, protocol, explorer, or contract. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as reference guidance and verify addresses, contracts, networks, and transaction status with current trusted sources before acting. <br>
Risk: Wallet analytics can expose sensitive account relationships when used on real addresses. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for authorized analysis and avoid publishing private or doxxing-oriented conclusions. <br>
