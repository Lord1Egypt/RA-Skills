## Description: <br>
基于目标岗位JD分析简历匹配度，提供关键词优化、STAR法则改写等具体建议；当用户需要优化简历、改进简历、提升简历匹配度时使用 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Job seekers and career-support agents use this skill to compare a resume against a target job description, identify gaps, and produce honest Chinese-language optimization guidance. The skill emphasizes improving expression, keyword alignment, STAR-based rewrites, and truthful handling of missing experience. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume and job-description text can contain personal or private employer details. <br>
Mitigation: Remove unnecessary phone numbers, addresses, IDs, and private employer details before using the skill. <br>
Risk: Resume rewrites or suggested metrics could become misleading if they overstate skills, responsibilities, or outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Verify that every suggested skill, metric, and rewritten claim remains truthful before using it in a resume. <br>
