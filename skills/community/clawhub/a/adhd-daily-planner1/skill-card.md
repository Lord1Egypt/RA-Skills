## Description: <br>
Time-blind friendly planning, executive function support, and daily structure for ADHD brains, with realistic time estimation, dopamine-aware task design, and neurodivergent-friendly systems. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[marjoriebroad](https://clawhub.ai/user/marjoriebroad) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to create ADHD-friendly daily plans, prioritize a small number of tasks, estimate time realistically, add transition buffers, and externalize working memory. It provides productivity support and planning guidance, not medical care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat ADHD productivity guidance as medical care. <br>
Mitigation: Present the skill as productivity support and planning guidance, and direct users to qualified professionals for medical diagnosis or treatment decisions. <br>
Risk: The artifact references supplemental materials that are absent from this package. <br>
Mitigation: Do not rely on missing referenced files; use only the guidance present in the packaged artifact unless supplemental materials are separately provided and reviewed. <br>
