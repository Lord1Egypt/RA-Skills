## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may include confidential workplace, employee, HR, legal, medical, disciplinary, protected-category, or similarly sensitive details in prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Use minimal or anonymized workplace details and follow organizational and platform policies before entering sensitive information. <br>
Risk: Leadership coaching output may be incomplete, context-sensitive, or inappropriate for high-stakes employment decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review guidance before acting and consult appropriate HR, legal, or management reviewers for disciplinary, legal, protected-category, or high-impact decisions. <br>
