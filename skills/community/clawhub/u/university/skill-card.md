## Description: <br>
Replace or complement traditional university with AI-powered degree programs, adaptive learning, exam preparation, and progress tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners, students, career changers, exam candidates, and tutors use this skill to create structured curricula, study sessions, practice assessments, progress tracking, and adaptive review plans. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The local ~/university/ folder can contain study materials, goals, schedules, assessments, and progress history. <br>
Mitigation: Keep unrelated sensitive files out of that folder and review or delete the folder when the learning program is complete. <br>
Risk: Calendar sync, notifications, or parent/tutor sharing can expose study data beyond the learner. <br>
Mitigation: Enable sharing features only after deciding exactly what data should be visible and to whom. <br>
Risk: Generated curricula, study resources, and exam readiness estimates may be incomplete or inaccurate for a user's actual institution, certification, or field. <br>
Mitigation: Cross-check plans and assessments against official syllabi, current exam requirements, and trusted course materials before relying on them. <br>
