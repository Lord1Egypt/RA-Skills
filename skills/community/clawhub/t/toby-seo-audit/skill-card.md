## Description: <br>
SEO specialist agent with site audits, content writing, keyword research, technical fixes, link building, and ranking strategies. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, site owners, and marketing teams use this skill to audit websites, plan SEO fixes, draft optimized content, research keywords, review competitor pages, and organize SEO work across local notes and reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The optional SEO workspace can contain private client, site, keyword, or audit data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing passwords, tokens, or sensitive client data there, and review workspace contents before sharing them. <br>
Risk: Applying SEO recommendations to live site files or Google tooling can affect production pages and accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Grant access to live site files or Google tools only when the user intends the agent to work on those assets, and review proposed changes before deployment. <br>
