## Description: <br>
Automates planning, publishing, reporting, and compliance workflows for Xiaohongshu and WeChat Channels social media operations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and operations teams use this skill to configure social accounts, plan content, publish posts through official APIs or browser automation, and generate daily performance reports. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can publish public social media content and schedule account reports without clear approval or stop controls. <br>
Mitigation: Require human approval before each post, make scheduled posting and reporting opt-in, and provide an easy way to pause automation. <br>
Risk: The workflow may rely on social media account credentials, API tokens, cookies, and report recipients. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer least-privilege official API tokens, encrypt credential and session storage, limit access to stored secrets, and verify Feishu recipients before sending reports. <br>
