## Description: <br>
Get honest design feedback on visual materials such as UI designs, logos, photos, graphics, and PDFs, with prioritized critique backed by design and usability frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[couriard](https://clawhub.ai/user/couriard) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, product teams, developers, and other external users can use this skill to critique visual work and identify prioritized fixes for usability, hierarchy, accessibility, typography, color, and platform-convention issues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-provided screenshots, PDFs, URLs, or design assets may include unreleased products, private client work, or other sensitive visual information. <br>
Mitigation: Only share visual assets that are appropriate for analysis, and remove or redact sensitive details before use. <br>
