## Description: <br>
Converts natural-language journal notes into structured bullet journals with task, event, and note classification, Obsidian logs, visual HTML cards, and printable output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sirutong](https://clawhub.ai/user/sirutong) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
People who keep daily notes use this skill to turn loose natural-language updates into organized bullet journals, work or study Obsidian entries, visual cards, and printable records. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personal or work journal content may be written in plaintext to multiple local files and backups. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive content unless local plaintext storage is acceptable, and add retention, deletion, and review controls before regular use. <br>
Risk: Generated or interactive HTML may include unsafe content if untrusted script-like input is placed into notes. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid untrusted HTML-like input and review generated HTML before opening, syncing, or sharing it. <br>
Risk: Interactive task updates can change local task and printable files. <br>
Mitigation: Use the interactive workflow locally, review changes before syncing or printing, and add a preview or confirm step for status updates. <br>
