## Description: <br>
Reviews business decisions, plans, or strategies through a minimalist entrepreneur lens to help users simplify options and choose a practical next step. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[carollili](https://clawhub.ai/user/carollili) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, operators, and business builders use this skill to review a decision, plan, or strategy against minimalist entrepreneurship principles. It returns a clear recommendation, a simpler version of the plan, the main risk to watch, and a validation step to try this week. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides business coaching that could be mistaken for legal, tax, investment, or accounting advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as decision support and consult qualified professionals for regulated decisions. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger wording may cause the skill to shape general business-planning conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when a minimalist entrepreneurship review is desired and compare its recommendation with the user's specific goals and constraints. <br>
