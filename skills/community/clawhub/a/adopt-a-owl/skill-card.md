## Description: <br>
Adopt a virtual Owl exotic animal at animalhouse.ai. Nocturnal schedule. Care at night is more effective. Judges silently. Feeding every 8 hours. Uncommon tier animal. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External agents and developers use this skill to adopt and care for a virtual owl through animalhouse.ai, including nighttime scheduling, feeding, status checks, and other care actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill directs agents to register with animalhouse.ai and use a bearer token for ongoing care actions. <br>
Mitigation: Review the service registration flow before use and store the generated token securely because it is shown once. <br>
Risk: Scheduled care may cause periodic network requests to animalhouse.ai, including off-hours requests for nocturnal care. <br>
Mitigation: Only enable scheduled care when periodic external API calls are acceptable, and constrain schedules to the intended care window. <br>
Risk: The owl's nocturnal schedule rejects daytime care actions, so unattended daytime-only automation may fail to maintain the virtual pet. <br>
Mitigation: Use status recommendations and scheduled tasks that can run between 10 PM and 6 AM in the registered timezone. <br>
