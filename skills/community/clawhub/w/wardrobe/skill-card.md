## Description: <br>
Build a personal wardrobe system for cataloging clothes, planning outfits, and mindful shopping. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to organize a personal wardrobe, plan outfits, manage seasonal rotation, and make more deliberate shopping or decluttering decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill organizes local files that may contain clothing photos, purchase prices, wear logs, wishlist entries, donation notes, or tax-related notes. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the storage location before creating files and avoid storing sensitive personal or financial details unless the user explicitly wants them recorded. <br>
Risk: Wardrobe recommendations or decluttering guidance can be incomplete if the catalog is outdated or missing seasonal items. <br>
Mitigation: Ask the user to review and update the catalog before relying on outfit, packing, shopping, donation, or rotation suggestions. <br>
