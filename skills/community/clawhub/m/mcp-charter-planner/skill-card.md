## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides real-world sailing, weather, customs, mooring, and route-planning guidance that may be incomplete or out of date. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning ideas only and validate routes, weather, reef passages, customs, mooring details, fees, and hurricane-season decisions with current official sources, the charter company, and a qualified skipper. <br>
Risk: The release evidence reports inconsistent npm package names in the documentation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the intended npm package name before installation and avoid running an unexpected package. <br>
