## Description: <br>
Divide Agent decomposes a user's problem or task into two MECE layers of sub-problems. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[alvinecarn](https://clawhub.ai/user/alvinecarn) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Knowledge workers, strategists, and agents use this skill to break down ambiguous problems into mutually exclusive, collectively exhaustive first- and second-layer sub-problems before deeper analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may browse external reference pages while decomposing a problem. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that web browsing is allowed for the user's task before using the skill. <br>
Risk: The skill may save generated work as a wiki document and submit it as an attachment. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm where generated documents and attachments will be stored or shared before using the skill with confidential plans, client data, or internal strategy. <br>
