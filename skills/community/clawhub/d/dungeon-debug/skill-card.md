## Description: <br>
Transforms debugging sessions into a text-based dungeon crawl where bugs become final bosses, stack frames become rooms, variables become loot, log messages become inscriptions, and breakpoints become save points. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jcools1977](https://clawhub.ai/user/jcools1977) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to reframe stalled or tedious debugging sessions as structured text-adventure investigations that encourage systematic stack-frame, variable, log, and hypothesis review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Debugging prompts and logs may include production secrets, tokens, or sensitive customer data. <br>
Mitigation: Redact secrets and sensitive data before pasting bug reports, stack traces, logs, or variable values into the workflow. <br>
