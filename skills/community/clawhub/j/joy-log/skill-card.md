## Description: <br>
Automatically records warm or joyful conversation moments, supports manual marking, and generates shareable joy cards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ericsma2019](https://clawhub.ai/user/ericsma2019) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Users who want an agent to keep a lightweight joy journal can capture positive chat moments, manually mark memorable exchanges, and turn selected excerpts into shareable image cards. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automatic capture can save sensitive conversation excerpts. <br>
Mitigation: Disable or avoid auto-capture for sensitive conversations and review saved text before generating a card. <br>
Risk: Generated cards may share private or unintended conversation content. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit confirmation before sharing or sending any generated card. <br>
