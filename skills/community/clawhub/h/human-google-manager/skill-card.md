## Description: <br>
Manages Gmail, Calendar, Sheets, Docs, Drive, Contacts, and Tasks with a human-friendly tone, mandatory confirmation, and automatic to-do detection. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
People who rely on Google Workspace use this skill to draft and manage emails, calendar events, documents, files, contacts, spreadsheets, and tasks while reviewing each proposed change before it is applied. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can propose changes across Gmail, Calendar, Sheets, Docs, Drive, Contacts, and Tasks, which may affect sensitive communications, files, schedules, and task records. <br>
Mitigation: Review every proposed email, event, file move, spreadsheet edit, contact update, document action, and task before approving it. <br>
Risk: Automatic to-do detection may capture sensitive or incidental details from user requests. <br>
Mitigation: Approve task creation only after checking the task title, due date, priority, and target task list. <br>
