## Description: <br>
Helps agents organize public Eggy Party website, activity, tournament, announcement, community rule, and help information with dates and source links. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CodeKungfu](https://clawhub.ai/user/CodeKungfu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to summarize public Eggy Party version updates, events, rewards, tournaments, rules, account-safety notices, and help content. Outputs should retain event dates and source links because game information changes frequently. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Event dates, rewards, rules, and game notices may change after a summary is produced. <br>
Mitigation: Verify dates and source links against the official public pages before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Requests may ask for account trading, payment bypasses, cheating, private player data, or rule evasion. <br>
Mitigation: Refuse those requests and keep use limited to public, official, non-sensitive information. <br>
