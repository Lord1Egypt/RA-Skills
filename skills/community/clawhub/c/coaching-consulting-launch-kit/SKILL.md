## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Signature Offer Framework (FREE)
Builds the core offer: methodology name, outcome promise, ideal client profile, program phases and deliverables, pricing tiers (1:1, group, self-study), and a 3-sentence positioning statement. Output: complete offer document coaches can paste to their website About page and use in sales conversations.

### Prompt 2 — Enrollment Conversation Script + Objection Handler
Full discovery call guide: opening rapport questions, diagnostic questions (uncover pain depth), vision questions (amplify urgency), offer presentation script, price reveal sequence, and word-for-word objection handlers for "I need to think about it," "I can't afford it," "My spouse needs to agree," and "I'm not ready yet." Closes with a graceful no-save script.

### Prompt 3 — Content Marketing System
Generates a complete content pillar framework: 3-5 authority topics tied to the offer's transformation, weekly posting schedule across LinkedIn + Instagram + email, 30 content prompts by format (story post, myth-busting, results showcase, how-to, client win), and a Minimum Viable Content Calendar (one piece per day, 5 days/week).

### Prompt 4 — Client Results Case Study + Testimonial Kit
Builds the full proof asset system: before/after story framework (5-step narrative arc), 3-format case study (long-form, one-paragraph social, one-sentence soundbite), pull quotes extraction guide, video testimonial script for clients, and an ROI metrics table (hours saved, revenue gained, outcome achieved). Includes outreach email to ask past clients for testimonials.
