## Description: <br>
Monitors upstream supply-chain volatility for manufacturing companies, including core material shortages, price pressure, disruption events, substitution options, geopolitical exposure, and business impact. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[daviddatamining](https://clawhub.ai/user/daviddatamining) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Business, procurement, strategy, and risk teams use this skill to request supply-chain volatility reports for a target manufacturing company. It produces sourced monitoring, conservative inference labels, and structured assessment of cost, capacity, geopolitical, and substitution risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Public web research and modeled estimates may produce stale, incomplete, or unverified supply-chain and financial-impact conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for current sources, dates, and language adjustments, and treat estimates as analytical support rather than verified investment advice. <br>
