## Description: <br>
商标驳回复审推理引擎（SJ-IRAC）：面向CNIPA驳回通知的要件化论证、证据链工程与A–E风险闸门，输出审查员可读、可直接提交的复审材料结构。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Trademark practitioners and teams use this skill to analyze CNIPA trademark refusal notices, map legal elements to evidence, build SJ-6 evidence chains, and choose proceed, cautious proceed, or stop-loss routes. It supports Quick, Pro, and Document modes for examiner-readable refusal-review work products. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, for CNIPA trademark-refusal review matters. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Submission-ready legal material may be incorrect if case facts, deadlines, or governing law are incomplete or stale. <br>
Mitigation: Verify deadlines, governing law, refusal grounds, and all case materials before relying on the output, and have filing-ready work reviewed by a qualified trademark professional. <br>
Risk: Sensitive trademark, business, or client materials may be included in user prompts. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the materials necessary for the refusal-review task and avoid unnecessary confidential or personal information. <br>
Risk: Unsupported facts or weak evidence could lead to misleading arguments. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's evidence-first workflow: require source, date, proof purpose, target element, weakness, and reinforcement guidance for each exhibit. <br>
