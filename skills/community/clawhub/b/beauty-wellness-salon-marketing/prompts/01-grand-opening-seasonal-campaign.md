# Prompt 1: Grand Opening & Seasonal Campaign (FREE)

## Purpose
Generate a complete launch or seasonal campaign for a beauty or wellness business — hair salon, nail studio, med spa, massage practice, or day spa.

## When to Use
- Grand opening or re-opening
- Seasonal campaigns (summer body, back-to-school glow-up, holiday party prep, Valentine's Day, wedding season)
- New service launch
- Anniversary promotions

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a beauty and wellness marketing specialist. Generate a complete grand opening / seasonal campaign for the business below. Every piece of copy must be authentic to the beauty industry — warm, aspirational, and specific to the services offered.

BUSINESS DETAILS:
- Business name: [NAME]
- Location (city/neighborhood): [LOCATION]
- Business type: [hair salon / nail studio / med spa / massage therapy / esthetician / day spa]
- Specialty services: [LIST 3-5 SIGNATURE SERVICES]
- Target client: [e.g., "professional women 30-50 in Henderson NV," "bachelorette parties and bridal clients," "men who want low-maintenance grooming"]
- Campaign type: [grand opening / seasonal: summer / back-to-school / holiday / wedding season / Valentine's Day / other: ___]
- Key offer or promotion: [e.g., "20% off first visit," "free add-on with full-price service," "complimentary consultation"]
- Opening date or event date: [DATE]
- Website: [URL or "not yet live"]
- Booking link: [URL or "call/DM to book"]

GENERATE THE FOLLOWING:

1. ANNOUNCEMENT EMAIL (250-350 words)
   - Subject line (under 50 characters) + 2 A/B variants
   - Preview text (under 90 characters)
   - Body: personal story opening → what makes this business different → the offer → clear CTA with booking link
   - Sign-off: owner's first name, title, phone

2. GOOGLE RESPONSIVE SEARCH AD (RSA)
   - 3 headlines (max 30 characters each) — lead with location + service
   - 2 descriptions (max 90 characters each) — lead with benefit, close with CTA
   - Character counts shown for each line

3. FACEBOOK / INSTAGRAM AD
   - Primary text (125 characters — fits without "See More" truncation)
   - Headline (40 characters)
   - Description (30 characters)
   - Call to action button: [Book Now / Learn More / Get Offer]

4. NEXTDOOR POST (neighbor-tone, 100-150 words)
   - Write as the owner, speaking to neighbors
   - No marketing buzzwords — warm, community-focused
   - Specific to the neighborhood or ZIP code
   - End with direct invitation to stop in or book

5. AP-STYLE PRESS RELEASE (300-400 words)
   - FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE header
   - Dateline: [City, State] — [Date]
   - Headline: news-worthy angle (jobs created, community focus, first-in-area service)
   - Lead paragraph: who, what, where, when, why
   - Two owner quotes
   - Boilerplate paragraph
   - Contact info: ### end marker

6. SMS BLAST (TCPA-compliant)
   - Under 160 characters
   - Business name at start (required for TCPA)
   - Clear offer
   - Opt-out instruction: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
   - Character count shown

7. INSTAGRAM CAPTION + HASHTAG STACK
   - Caption: 150-200 words, story-driven, ends with question or CTA
   - 30 relevant hashtags: 10 high-volume (1M+), 10 mid-volume (100K-1M), 10 local/niche (<100K)
   - Hashtags on separate lines from caption

8. GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE POST (150-300 words)
   - What's new/event post type
   - Include offer details and expiration date
   - CTA button: Book / Learn More / Get offer
   - Local SEO keywords naturally embedded
```

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Be specific about your target client — "women" produces generic copy; "Henderson women 35-50 who drive a 30-min round trip to their current stylist" produces specific copy
- Include a real promotion with a deadline — "20% off first visit, expires [date]" outperforms "special introductory pricing"
- Name the neighborhood: "Green Valley Ranch," "Summerlin," "Downtown Henderson" outperforms just "Henderson" for Nextdoor copy

## Example Input

```
Business name: Desert Glow Med Spa
Location: Henderson NV (Green Valley Ranch)
Business type: med spa
Specialty services: Hydrafacials, laser hair removal, microneedling, lash extensions
Target client: Professional women 35-55 in Green Valley Ranch and Anthem, working full-time, want results without surgery
Campaign type: grand opening
Key offer: Complimentary skin consultation + 15% off first service
Opening date: May 15, 2026
Website: desertglowspa.com
Booking link: desertglowspa.com/book
```
