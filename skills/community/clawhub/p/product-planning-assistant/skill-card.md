## Description: <br>
Helps product managers complete high-quality product planning from requirements to implementation for annual, quarterly, product iteration, leadership reporting, and team alignment workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Product managers and product teams use this skill to plan annual, quarterly, or product-iteration work, align goals and requirements, screen priorities, break down execution paths, prepare risk plans, and create leadership or team-alignment materials. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Planning guidance may be a poor fit for a team's planning style, language, or operating context. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the workflow and Chinese-language format are appropriate before adopting the generated plan. <br>
Risk: Product planning conversations may include confidential business details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential business information unless the current chat context is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Generated product plans can contain incomplete assumptions or misleading prioritization. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs with relevant stakeholders before using them for execution, leadership reporting, or team alignment. <br>
