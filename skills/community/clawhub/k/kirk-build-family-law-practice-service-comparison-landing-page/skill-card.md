Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated landing page claims, pricing cues, or disclaimers may be inaccurate or unsuitable for jurisdiction-specific legal advertising rules. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified human review all claims, pricing language, disclaimers, and client-facing content before publication. <br>
Risk: Prompts could include confidential case details or client personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Use anonymized business context and avoid entering confidential case details or client PII. <br>
Risk: Marketing copy may be mistaken for legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as marketing drafts only and keep legal interpretation or advice under qualified professional review. <br>
