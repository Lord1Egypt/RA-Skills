## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Voice-agent deployments may trigger AI-disclosure, call-recording, telemarketing, healthcare, payment, and data-retention obligations that vary by jurisdiction and use case. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm applicable requirements before launch, document caller disclosure and recording-consent behavior, and provide human escalation and opt-out paths. <br>
Risk: Sensitive data such as payment card data, protected health information, or unnecessary personal data could be exposed through speech transcription, LLM prompts, logs, or connected CRM/API systems. <br>
Mitigation: Scope connected-system access tightly, redact or minimize logs, avoid sending card data or unnecessary sensitive data through LLMs, and use compliant payment or healthcare workflows. <br>
Risk: The skill is a reference guide and does not constitute production compliance approval for a deployed voice agent. <br>
Mitigation: Have legal, security, and operations reviewers validate generated call flows, prompts, integrations, retention policies, and monitoring plans before production use. <br>
