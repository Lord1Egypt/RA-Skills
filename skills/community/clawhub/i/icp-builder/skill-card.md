## Description: <br>
Builds Ideal Customer Profiles with scoring methodology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Sales, marketing, and go-to-market teams use this skill to define target customer profiles, score prospects, identify buying triggers, and document poor-fit disqualifiers from customer inputs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can prompt users for real customer names, pricing, poor-fit customer examples, and sales strategy details. <br>
Mitigation: Anonymize customer names and omit confidential deal, pricing, and strategy details before using the skill. <br>
