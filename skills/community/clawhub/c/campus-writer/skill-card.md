专属校园文案写作助手，帮助大学生撰写活动策划书、演讲稿、团日活动心得、实习周记、短视频脚本、朋友圈文案、公众号推文、论文大纲等，灵活调整语气风格，每次提供2-3个不同风格初稿供选择。 <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, especially university students, use this skill to draft and refine campus writing such as event plans, speeches, activity reflections, internship journals, social posts, public account articles, and paper outlines. It helps produce multiple tone options so the user can choose and revise the best fit for the assignment or campus scenario. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated campus writing may contain inaccuracies, unoriginal phrasing, or content that does not meet school rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review and edit drafts for factual accuracy, originality, assignment requirements, and institutional policy before submission or publication. <br>
Risk: Prompts for personalized writing may include unnecessary private personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing sensitive personal information unless it is required for the writing task, and remove private details from drafts before reuse. <br>
