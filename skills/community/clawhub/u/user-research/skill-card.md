## Description: <br>
Designs survey questions, interview scripts, and feedback loops for user research. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[c0ldsmi1e](https://clawhub.ai/user/c0ldsmi1e) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
SaaS and startup founders use this skill to plan customer interviews, surveys, in-app feedback, analytics review, and feedback loops. It helps turn research goals into tailored scripts, questions, analysis frameworks, and action checklists. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User-research workflows can involve customer personal data, confidential business metrics, or sensitive interview notes. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the data needed for the research task and review generated plans, scripts, and summaries before using them with customers or internal teams. <br>
