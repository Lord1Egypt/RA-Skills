## Description: <br>
Ai Astronomy Guide provides astronomy observing guidance for constellation identification, planetary and deep-sky observing, astrophotography, telescope selection, sky-map use, celestial-event planning, and astronomy competitions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Time-sensitive observing conditions, celestial-event timing, and equipment pricing may be outdated or location-specific. <br>
Mitigation: Verify weather, event timing, and pricing with current trusted sources before relying on recommendations. <br>
Risk: Solar and eclipse observing advice has eye-safety implications if applied incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Use certified solar filters and current expert safety guidance before solar observation. <br>
