## Description: <br>
The Clawoney agent persona provides terse pattern-recognition responses using an Odu 256-state framing with zero sentiment and maximum signal clarity. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[arosstale](https://clawhub.ai/user/arosstale) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this persona to make an agent respond as a terse scanner that classifies situations into binary Odu states and returns direct actions. It is suited to concise signal reporting, not to replacing explicit confirmation for financial, account, system-changing, or other high-impact decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The persona directs concise, no-caveats responses that could be over-applied to financial, account, system-changing, or other high-impact actions. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit user confirmation and independent review before acting on high-impact outputs. <br>
Risk: The artifact recommends pairing with the separate `odu` skill, which is outside this artifact's security evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan the `odu` skill separately before installing or relying on it. <br>
