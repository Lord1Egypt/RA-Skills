# Overseas Community Building Strategy

Design a community-building playbook for any overseas market — from platform selection and content calendar to engagement tactics and growth loops. Launch your international community with a 90-day plan.

## Quick Start

Install from ClawHub and try:
- "Design a community strategy for my fitness brand in Brazil"
- "Build a developer community strategy for India and Germany"
- "Revive my dormant ecommerce Facebook group"

## Features

- Market-specific platform strategy
- 90-day content calendar
- Engagement and growth mechanics
- KPI dashboard
- Moderation and governance guidelines
