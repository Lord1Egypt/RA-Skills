## Description: <br>
帝王纪年转换 / Chinese imperial year converter for bidirectional conversion between Chinese reign years and CE/BCE dates, with dynasty, emperor, reign-name, and historical-year lookup across 790 reign periods from 878 BCE through 1911 CE. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users, educators, researchers, and developers use this skill to convert Chinese imperial reign years to CE/BCE years, convert CE/BCE years back to matching reign periods, and search or list reign names by dynasty, emperor, or keyword. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The bundled historical dataset may contain inaccuracies or incomplete coverage for specialized historical research. <br>
Mitigation: Treat conversion results as reference assistance and verify important dates against authoritative historical sources. <br>
Risk: The optional batch converter reads and may write local file paths supplied by the user. <br>
Mitigation: Use only intended input and output paths when running the Python script directly. <br>
