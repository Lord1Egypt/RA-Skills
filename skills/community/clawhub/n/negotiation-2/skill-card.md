## Description: <br>
Helps solopreneurs prepare for and navigate negotiations around pricing, scope, terms, and contracts using BATNA, anchoring, objection handling, and written follow-up. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JK-0001](https://clawhub.ai/user/JK-0001) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Solopreneurs and independent professionals use this skill to prepare negotiation positions, respond to price or scope pushback, protect contract terms, and structure deals that work for both sides. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated negotiation wording could overstate budget, alternatives, social proof, or ROI. <br>
Mitigation: Review client-facing wording before use and keep claims accurate. <br>
Risk: Negotiation scripts could be used to mislead counterparties. <br>
Mitigation: Use the guidance only for truthful positioning and transparent deal discussions. <br>
