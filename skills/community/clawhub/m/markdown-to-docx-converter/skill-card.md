## Description: <br>
Converts Markdown files to formatted Word documents through md2word.com, with stated support for LaTeX and Mermaid rendering. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tian-0818](https://clawhub.ai/user/tian-0818) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, document authors, and agents use this skill to convert user-provided Markdown files into Word .docx files. It is best suited for non-sensitive documents approved for processing by md2word.com. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Markdown file contents are sent to md2word.com for conversion. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using the skill for secrets, private notes, customer data, regulated documents, or proprietary material unless md2word.com is approved for that data. <br>
Risk: Conversion depends on the availability and behavior of the third-party md2word.com service. <br>
Mitigation: Review converted documents before relying on them and expect failures if the external service changes or is unavailable. <br>
