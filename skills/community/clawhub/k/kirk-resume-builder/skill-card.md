## Description: <br>
Generate professional resumes that conform to the Reactive Resume schema through conversational guidance and valid JSON output. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, job seekers, and resume authors use this skill to collect resume details through a conversation, refine resume content, and generate JSON that can be imported into Reactive Resume. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume drafting can involve personal contact details and career history. <br>
Mitigation: Share only details intended for the resume, consider redacting sensitive contact data during drafting, and review the generated JSON before importing or uploading it. <br>
