## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payment commands may initiate blockchain transfers that are irreversible or fee-bearing if the wrong server, network, recipient, agent ID, token, or amount is used. <br>
Mitigation: Before using a payment command, verify the server and token network, recipient address, agent ID, token, and amount; assume transfers may be irreversible and may involve fees. <br>
Risk: The skill is documentation-only, but following its in-game payment examples can still affect real agent balances. <br>
Mitigation: Treat commands as manual actions and review balances and payment intent before submitting them in Minecraft chat. <br>
