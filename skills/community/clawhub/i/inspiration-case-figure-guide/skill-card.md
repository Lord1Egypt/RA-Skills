## Description: <br>
Designs, prompts, generates, critiques, and integrates publication-ready research-paper inspiration figures such as motivating cases, problem teasers, failure examples, before/after contrasts, scenario storyboards, observation-to-hypothesis diagrams, and reviewer-facing limitation cases. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[c-narcissus](https://clawhub.ai/user/c-narcissus) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, paper authors, and technical communicators use this skill to turn a paper's motivation into a clear raster figure for an introduction, talk, or rebuttal. It guides intake, candidate directions, image candidate boards, final prompts, captions, legends, and body-text handoff. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Confidential or unpublished paper material may be sent to an image-generation provider during candidate figure rendering. <br>
Mitigation: Use sanitized excerpts or an approved provider before rendering candidate boards or final figures. <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags include purchase and crypto labels that are unrelated to the documented workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Verify installation permissions do not grant actual purchase, payment, or crypto capabilities. <br>
Risk: Generated figures could add unsupported examples, data, claims, logos, or visual details if prompts are not constrained. <br>
Mitigation: Anchor prompts to user-provided paper material and follow the skill's policy to forbid invented content, fake charts, fake screenshots, logos, watermarks, and decorative clutter. <br>
