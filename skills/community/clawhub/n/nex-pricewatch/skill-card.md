## Description: <br>
Nex Pricewatch helps agents configure and operate local price monitoring for competitor, supplier, and market pricing pages using CSS, XPath, regex, or text selectors with history, exports, and optional Telegram alerts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External business users, agencies, resellers, and operators use this skill to track competitor or supplier price changes, review local price history, compare monitored targets, and export pricing data for analysis. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Monitored pricing data and saved page snapshots are stored locally on the user's machine. <br>
Mitigation: Install and run the skill only where local storage of competitor, supplier, or market pricing data is acceptable. <br>
Risk: Enabling Telegram alerts can send price alert details to Telegram. <br>
Mitigation: Keep Telegram disabled for sensitive competitor or supplier monitoring, and enable it only with appropriate bot and chat settings. <br>
Risk: Configured URLs may target private, unauthorized, or inappropriate endpoints. <br>
Mitigation: Review each configured URL before monitoring and use only endpoints the user is authorized to access. <br>
