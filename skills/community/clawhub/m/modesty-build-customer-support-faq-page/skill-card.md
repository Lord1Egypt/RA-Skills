## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common customer support questions. Use when working on FAQ content for support teams, customer success managers. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[modestyrichards](https://clawhub.ai/user/modestyrichards) <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Support teams and customer success managers use this skill to draft and refine customer-facing FAQ answers for common support questions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated FAQ content may include inaccurate claims or outdated links when web search is used. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated FAQ content before publishing and verify factual claims and links against authoritative sources. <br>
Risk: FAQ drafting may expose sensitive customer data if confidential details are entered into the workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive customer data unless workspace policy explicitly permits it. <br>
