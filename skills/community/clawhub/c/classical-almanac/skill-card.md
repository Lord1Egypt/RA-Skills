## Description: <br>
基于清乾隆钦定《协纪辨方书》的古典择日系统，提供结婚、开业、搬家、签约、出行等活动的择吉选日报告。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ikplpeter](https://clawhub.ai/user/ikplpeter) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to generate traditional Chinese almanac-style date-selection guidance for activities such as marriage, business opening, moving, signing contracts, travel, worship, wealth seeking, and taking office. Its recommendations should be treated as cultural reference or entertainment rather than authoritative personal, financial, legal, or business advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can present traditional date-selection recommendations in contexts involving marriage, travel, contracts, investments, or business decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as cultural reference or entertainment and use independent professional judgment for consequential decisions. <br>
Risk: The security evidence says the accuracy claims are stronger than the implementation supports. <br>
Mitigation: Review the generated factors and scoring assumptions before relying on a recommendation, especially when exact almanac calculation fidelity matters. <br>
Risk: The security guidance notes possible command-name issues. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the actual script path and command in the installed artifact before use. <br>
