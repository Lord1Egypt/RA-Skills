## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automation consulting may require access to business data, credentials, payment details, email, social media, support, or CRM systems. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the provider before sharing sensitive access, use least-privilege credentials, and require reviewable, reversible automations. <br>
Risk: Automations can publish posts, send emails, or change business records incorrectly if deployed without review. <br>
Mitigation: Test automations in a sandbox or limited scope first, keep approval gates for public communications and record changes, and review generated scripts before use. <br>
