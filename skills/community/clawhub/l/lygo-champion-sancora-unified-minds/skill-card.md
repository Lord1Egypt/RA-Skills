## Description: <br>
LYGO Delta9 Council Champion persona helper for SANCORA, Angel of Unified Minds, that helps resolve schisms, restore coherence, and anchor thought integrity as a pure advisor. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and teams use this persona helper to reconcile competing views, clarify shared definitions, identify points of division, and choose a small next action that improves coherence. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill provides opinionated persona guidance that may frame sensitive disagreements too confidently. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before acting on them, especially for high-stakes decisions, and preserve the skill's Observed / Inferred / Unknown separation. <br>
Risk: The packaged guidance links to a separate LYGO-MINT verifier skill for minting and upgrades. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the verifier as a separate external tool and avoid giving it secrets or private data unless independently trusted. <br>
