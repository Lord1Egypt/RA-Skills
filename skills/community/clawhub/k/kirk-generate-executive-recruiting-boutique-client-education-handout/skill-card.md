## Description: <br>
Create a polished explainer handout with visuals, FAQs, and clear next steps for an executive recruiting firm. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Executive recruiters and talent partners use this skill to draft client education handouts that explain an executive recruiting process, answer common questions, include visuals, and provide next steps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated recruiting education materials may include inaccurate, biased, or legally unsuitable statements. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for accuracy, bias, and legal suitability before sharing them with clients. <br>
Risk: Client education content could be misused as candidate screening or decision support. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only as a drafting aid for client-facing materials; do not use it to screen, rank, or make decisions about candidates. <br>
