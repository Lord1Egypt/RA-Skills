## Description: <br>
A professional legal advisor assistant that provides professional answers and recommendations for legal issues based on statutes, case law, and best practices, including specific solutions and preventive measures. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and employees can use this skill to structure informational legal issue analysis, identify relevant legal areas and jurisdiction questions, and draft practical next steps for topics such as contracts, labor, corporate law, intellectual property, tort, and criminal law. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Legal guidance may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate for the user's jurisdiction or facts. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current law for the relevant jurisdiction and consult a licensed attorney before relying on outputs for significant legal, financial, criminal, employment, or business decisions. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake generated legal information for formal legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep the skill's disclaimer in user-facing outputs and treat responses as informational starting points rather than a substitute for professional counsel. <br>
