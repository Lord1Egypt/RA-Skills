# Marketing Plan — Chimney Sweep & Fireplace Repair Contractor Marketing Kit v2.0

## Market Opportunity

**Target market:** 25,000+ chimney sweep companies in the US
- Only ~1,800 hold active CSIA Certified Chimney Sweep (CCS) credentials
- ~4,000 NFI-certified gas appliance technicians
- Most operators are 1–3 truck owner-operators with no marketing budget or expertise

**Why they'll pay:**
- NFPA 211 compliance language is not Googleable in ready-to-use format
- Real estate referral revenue is the highest-margin channel; operators know it but can't write the pitch
- EPA Phase 2 rules (2020) created a compliance requirement most small operators ignore — the ones who know it want to advertise it
- CSIA certification has real scarcity value (~1,800 certified sweeps for 140M+ US homes with fireplaces/chimneys)
- JSON-LD schema markup directly improves Google local pack rankings — measurable revenue impact

**Las Vegas / Southwest angle:**
- Most Henderson/Las Vegas homes built 1990–2010 have gas log fireplaces — all need annual service
- Zero wood-burning/creosote market (desert climate, mostly gas) but massive gas fireplace install base
- Clark County has Stage 1/2 No-Burn episodes October–March — wood-burning fireplace operators need compliant seasonal messaging
- High real estate transaction volume (Las Vegas is consistently a top-10 US real estate market) = high Level II inspection demand
- 65,000+ home sales in Clark County annually × average $200/Level II inspection = $13M/year Level II inspection market
