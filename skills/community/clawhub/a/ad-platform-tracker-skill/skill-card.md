## Description: <br>
Use this skill to track, analyze, and respond to advertising platform changes across Meta, Google Ads, privacy compliance, and competitor intelligence. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, paid media operators, and growth consultants use this skill to monitor Meta and Google Ads changes, assess campaign impact, audit compliance risks, and summarize competitor advertising activity. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Optional scraper, API, or token-based workflows may involve third-party services and external platform terms. <br>
Mitigation: Use them only for public sources, review the relevant third-party terms, and provide tokens only when those external calls are intentional. <br>
Risk: Advertising platform and compliance guidance can change quickly or vary by jurisdiction, account type, and regulated vertical. <br>
Mitigation: Verify consequential campaign, policy, or compliance actions against current official sources before making production changes. <br>
Risk: User-provided campaign, competitor, or business data may be sensitive. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending sensitive business data unnecessarily and limit inputs to the minimum needed for the requested analysis. <br>
