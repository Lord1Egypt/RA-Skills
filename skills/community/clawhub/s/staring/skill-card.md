## Description: <br>
YiMa StarEngine helps commerce and livestream teams match influencers, analyze campaign performance, generate FABE livestream scripts, create promotional copy, and plan marketing calendars for Douyin commerce workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External developers, commerce operators, livestream teams, and marketing staff use this skill to find ranked influencers, analyze GMV, ROI, conversion, order, and commission data, draft livestream scripts, and produce channel-specific promotional content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may use a local cache for sensitive campaign, sales, order, and commission data, and the cache behavior is under-documented. <br>
Mitigation: Before installation, confirm the cache location, retention period, local access controls, and cleanup process, and use the skill only in an environment approved for sensitive commerce data. <br>
