## Description: <br>
辅助高校档案人员按事由原则分类归档文件，确定分类号、保管期限和档号，生成规范组卷方案供审阅。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Archive staff, especially in higher-education settings, use this skill to classify completed records, group related materials into case files, assign retention periods, and draft archive numbers and file inventories for human review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
