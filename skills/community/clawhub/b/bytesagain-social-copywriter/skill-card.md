## Description: <br>
Generates platform-optimized social media copy for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, and Weibo, including copy variants, hashtag strategies, and engagement-focused formatting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Social media marketers, founders, creators, and developers can use this skill to draft channel-specific posts, captions, hashtag plans, and hook variants for campaigns or content experiments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Hard-coded statistics, timing advice, hashtag guidance, and platform recommendations may be stale or unsuitable for a specific audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review and update generated advice before publishing, especially campaign-sensitive claims and platform timing recommendations. <br>
Risk: Generated hooks and urgency-oriented copy can overstate claims or create an unsuitable brand voice. <br>
Mitigation: Fact-check claims, adjust tone, and apply editorial or legal review before using copy in public channels. <br>
Risk: The skill runs a local bash helper with embedded Python. <br>
Mitigation: Install only in environments where local script execution is acceptable and inspect the helper before use. <br>
