## Description: <br>
Systematic content quality audit based on 80 CORE-EEAT standards, evaluating content's GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and SEO (Search Engine Optimization) potential. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Content strategists, SEO specialists, editors, and developers use this skill to audit articles, landing pages, reviews, FAQs, and competitor content against CORE-EEAT criteria for GEO and SEO readiness. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Audit output can be mistaken for definitive publishing, compliance, SEO, or quality assurance judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Treat results as advisory, review recommendations before publication, and validate high-impact content decisions with qualified reviewers. <br>
Risk: User-provided content, URLs, or file paths may expose private documents, secret-bearing paths, authenticated pages, or internal URLs. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only material intended for review and avoid sensitive or internal sources unless that access is deliberate. <br>
Risk: Some authority, trust, schema, backlink, or site-level checks may be unavailable from standalone content. <br>
Mitigation: Mark unsupported checks as N/A and exclude them from affected score calculations as described by the skill. <br>
