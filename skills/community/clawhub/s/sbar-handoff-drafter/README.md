# SBAR Handoff Drafter

Turns a nurse's raw shift notes into a structured **Situation / Background / Assessment / Recommendation** handoff packet for shift change, intra-facility transfer, or a provider escalation call.

## When to use

- End-of-shift handoff to the oncoming nurse.
- Intra-facility transfer (ED → floor, floor → ICU, PACU → floor, etc.).
- Calling a provider to escalate a deteriorating patient.
- Charge-nurse rounding briefs.

## What it produces

- SBAR-formatted packet (Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation).
- Priority concerns ranked by acuity.
- Read-back checklist of items the receiver must confirm.
- An unresolved-information list (vitals not taken, labs pending, family contact missing).
- DRAFT label requiring nurse review before delivery.

## What it does **not** do

- It does not give clinical advice or suggest medication doses.
- It does not replace the nurse's clinical judgment or facility handoff policy.
- It does not chart anything; output is for verbal/written handoff use after review.

## Privacy

The user must de-identify the patient (no full name, MRN, date of birth, address) before pasting notes. The skill prompts for this before accepting clinical content.
