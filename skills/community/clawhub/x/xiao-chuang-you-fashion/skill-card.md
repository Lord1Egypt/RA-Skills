### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generic clothing terms may activate the skill in ordinary shopping or outfit conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use it when fashion or styling help is desired, and review its suggestions as advice rather than as requirements. <br>
Risk: Personal styling requests can invite unnecessary sensitive personal details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the context needed for the outfit decision and avoid sensitive personal information unless it is necessary. <br>
