## Description: <br>
Checks pasted English sentences or paragraphs for grammar, spelling, punctuation, sentence structure, and word choice errors, then returns corrections, explanations, and a polished version. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhouzhi-tal](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhouzhi-tal) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Students, educators, and writing learners use this skill to review English writing, identify grammar and usage issues, and receive bilingual explanations suited to the learner's level. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The artifact text includes Xueersi branding, but the security guidance treats that branding as an unverified publisher claim. <br>
Mitigation: Treat zhouzhi-tal as the server-resolved publisher unless ClawHub separately verifies the claimed affiliation. <br>
Risk: Users may paste private or sensitive writing for grammar checking. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid submitting sensitive text unless the user is comfortable having the agent or model process that content. <br>
