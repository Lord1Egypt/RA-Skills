## Description: <br>
Humanize Vietnamese ecommerce copy to reduce robotic AI phrasing while keeping the message persuasive and platform-appropriate. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Leooooooow](https://clawhub.ai/user/Leooooooow) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Ecommerce operators, marketers, and sellers use this skill to rewrite Vietnamese product pages, ads, seller replies, customer replies, captions, and selling scripts so they sound more natural while preserving the original offer and call to action. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewritten ecommerce copy may still contain misleading claims, ad-platform policy issues, or regulated-product wording. <br>
Mitigation: Review final copy for truthfulness, platform rules, and regulated-product claims before publication. <br>
Risk: The skill may weaken compliance boundaries if the input asks for exaggerated benefits or invented claims. <br>
Mitigation: Provide banned wording and compliance boundaries as input, and use the risk-safe wording swaps before publishing. <br>
