## Description: <br>
Thesis Review Pro helps academic reviewers analyze master's and doctoral theses with a structured review framework and produce formatted review comments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[paudyyin](https://clawhub.ai/user/paudyyin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Academic reviewers, thesis committee members, and institutional review staff use this skill to extract thesis details, assess originality, compare relevant literature, identify writing and methodology issues, and draft structured review opinions. It supports individual and batch review workflows for master's and doctoral theses. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Thesis PDFs and generated review documents may contain personal, confidential, or unpublished academic information. <br>
Mitigation: Remove identifying details for blind or confidential reviews and keep generated documents within the approved review process. <br>
Risk: Generated review opinions may be incomplete, inaccurate, or too sensitive to use without expert judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified reviewer verify factual claims, literature comparisons, evaluation conclusions, and final recommendations before sharing them. <br>
