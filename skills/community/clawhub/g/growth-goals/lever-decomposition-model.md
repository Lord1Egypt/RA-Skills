# GMV Lever Decomposition Model

Formula: GMV = Sessions x CVR x AOV x  (1 + Repeat Rate)

---

## Baselines

| Lever | How to Measure | Your Current Value |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Sessions | Platform analytics | |

## Lever Tactics

Traffic: paid ads, organic content, affiliates, email/SMS

CVR: listing optimization, reviews, pricing, live selling

AOV: bundles, upsells, free shipping threshold, premium SKUs

Retention: post-purchase flows, loyalty program, re-engagement
