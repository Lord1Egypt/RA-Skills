## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Sensitive spreadsheets or private business data may be exposed if the skill is used in an untrusted agent environment. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only in trusted environments for sensitive data, redact unnecessary private fields, and follow the security guidance before sharing business data. <br>
Risk: Forecasts, anomaly judgments, or action recommendations may be incorrect or misleading if the source data is incomplete, biased, or misinterpreted. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated analysis against the underlying data, validate assumptions, and treat recommendations as decision support rather than automatic decisions. <br>
