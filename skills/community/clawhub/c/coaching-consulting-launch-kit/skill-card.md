## Description: <br>
Generates a complete launch kit for coaches and consultants, including a signature offer, enrollment script, content marketing system, and case study assets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gitflopez](https://clawhub.ai/user/gitflopez) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Coaches and consultants use this skill to draft launch materials for a paid expertise business, including positioning, sales conversation scripts, content plans, and proof assets. It is intended for business-development drafting and should be reviewed before client-facing publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated testimonials, client quotes, case studies, and ROI claims could be mistaken for verified endorsements. <br>
Mitigation: Publish those assets only when claims are verified and quoted language is verbatim and client-approved, or clearly label outputs as composite or sample material. <br>
Risk: Sales scripts, pricing language, and proof assets may overstate outcomes if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against actual client results, advertising rules, and the user's offer before using them in public marketing or sales conversations. <br>
