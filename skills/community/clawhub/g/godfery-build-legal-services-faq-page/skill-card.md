## Description: <br>
Generate customer-facing answers for common legal services questions. Use when working on FAQ content for legal ops teams, attorneys, compliance teams. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Legal operations teams, attorneys, and compliance teams use this skill to draft customer-facing FAQ answers for common legal services questions before human review and publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Draft FAQ answers could be mistaken for legal advice or published with inaccurate legal claims. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as a drafting aid only, verify web-sourced claims, and have a qualified human review answers before client delivery or publication. <br>
Risk: FAQ drafting may involve sensitive client or matter information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential client information unless the agent environment and tool settings are approved for that data. <br>
