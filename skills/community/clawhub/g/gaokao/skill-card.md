## Description: <br>
Prepare for China's national college entrance exam with progress tracking, weak area analysis, spaced repetition, and university targeting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External students, parents, tutors, retakers, and overseas Chinese learners use this skill to plan Gaokao preparation, track progress, analyze weak areas, manage spaced repetition, review mock exams, and target universities or majors. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill stores detailed exam-prep records and inferred learning patterns locally. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid unnecessary full names, school details, health notes, or multi-student records on shared or synced machines, and periodically review or delete stored files. <br>
Risk: Tutor mode may create records for multiple students under ~/gaokao-tutor/. <br>
Mitigation: Keep multi-student records local, limit personally identifying details, and avoid using shared or synced directories unless appropriate access controls are in place. <br>
