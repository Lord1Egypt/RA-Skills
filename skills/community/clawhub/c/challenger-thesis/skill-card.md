## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through a challenger-style, source-cited research workflow for analyzing one public or pre-IPO company's competitive moat and long-term investment logic. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liufx13](https://clawhub.ai/user/liufx13) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and investors use this skill to structure a single-company deep dive, confirm company identity, gather source-backed financial and industry evidence, and pressure-test moats through simulated new-entrant attacks. It is not intended for buy/sell timing, technical trading, macro commentary, or multi-company ranking. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on the research output as investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as research support, not investment advice, and verify cited sources independently. <br>
Risk: A full deep-dive may be incomplete when market data, filings, or other sources cannot be fetched. <br>
Mitigation: Use a specific prompt, provide missing documents when needed, and keep unavailable facts marked as items to verify. <br>
