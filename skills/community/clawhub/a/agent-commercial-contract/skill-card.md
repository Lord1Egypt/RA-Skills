## Description: <br>
Enables AI agents to negotiate, sign, execute, and enforce commercial agreements with digital identity, escrow, milestone payments, audit trails, and dispute resolution. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agent builders use this skill to add contract, signature, escrow, milestone payment, audit, and dispute-resolution workflows to agent marketplaces, API networks, data exchanges, and multi-agent projects. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Autonomous contract signing, escrow deposits, refunds, dispute rulings, and payment releases can create financial or legal exposure if executed without user control or package provenance review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and pin the external package source before installation; use sandbox or staging credentials; require human approval for signatures and payment actions; enforce strict amount and counterparty limits. <br>
