## Description: <br>
Scrapes YouTube search results by keyword to collect creator channel details, video counts, relevant video views, and public contact email or social links. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[browseract-cli](https://clawhub.ai/user/browseract-cli) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, creator relations, and developer teams use this skill to search YouTube by keyword and compile publicly available creator and channel contact leads for outreach research. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Collecting creator contact data can create privacy, terms-of-service, spam, or profiling concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for legitimate outreach research on publicly available information, respect YouTube and linked-site terms, and avoid spam or profiling. <br>
Risk: The skill operates through browser-act in a browser context. <br>
Mitigation: Run it in a separate browser profile and review browser state and actions before collecting or using results. <br>
