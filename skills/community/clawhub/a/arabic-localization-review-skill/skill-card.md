## Description: <br>
Review Arabic and bilingual localization for apps, websites, course notes, and UI screens with RTL, tone, terminology, and consistency checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[abdullah944](https://clawhub.ai/user/abdullah944) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, content teams, and localization reviewers use this skill to check Arabic and bilingual text before publication or product release. It supports meaning preservation, tone review, RTL/UI fit, terminology consistency, and final readiness classification. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may provide confidential documents, customer data, credentials, or other sensitive source material for localization review. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only the minimum text needed for review and exclude passwords, OTPs, private account data, customer records, keys, tokens, and confidential documents. <br>
Risk: Localization suggestions may accidentally alter meaning, add unsupported claims, or mishandle sensitive religious, legal, medical, financial, or public brand wording. <br>
Mitigation: Preserve the source intent, mark sensitive or unclear wording for human/native review, and verify final copy before publication. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes an optional donation link that is not required to use the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Treat donation activity as optional and unrelated to installation, operation, or review quality. <br>
