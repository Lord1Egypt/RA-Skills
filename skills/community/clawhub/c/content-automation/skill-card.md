## Description: <br>
Content Automation helps creators and operators generate social posts, video scripts, scheduled publishing tasks, and content planning assets. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[antonia-sz](https://clawhub.ai/user/antonia-sz) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, social media operators, and developers use this skill to draft social posts and video scripts, build content calendars, and run content automation workflows with manual review before publishing. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The release asks users to install unpinned third-party code from MoneyPrinterV2 and its dependencies. <br>
Mitigation: Review the repository and dependency set before use, preferably in an isolated environment. <br>
Risk: Publishing workflows may require powerful social-media credentials and can upload or schedule content. <br>
Mitigation: Use test or scoped accounts, keep secrets out of version control, and require manual review and explicit approval before upload or scheduled posting. <br>
