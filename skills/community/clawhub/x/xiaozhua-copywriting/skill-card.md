## Description: <br>
Helps agents polish copy and adjust wording by role, scenario, audience, tone, and Chinese or English language needs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mirowangl-ops](https://clawhub.ai/user/mirowangl-ops) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and external-facing communicators use this skill to draft or polish concise reports, emails, customer-service replies, group messages, and product-context updates. It adapts wording for bosses, peers, teams, customer service, and mixed Chinese or English communication contexts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Drafted communications may be inappropriate or misleading in HR, legal, finance, customer-facing, or cross-cultural contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs before sending and state the intended audience, language, and tone when requesting revisions. <br>
Risk: Ambiguous role or scenario information can produce wording with the wrong level of formality or authority. <br>
Mitigation: Provide the sender role, recipient relationship, communication scenario, and desired style before using the rewritten copy. <br>
