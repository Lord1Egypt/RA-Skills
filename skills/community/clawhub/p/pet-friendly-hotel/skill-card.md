## Use Case: <br>
External travelers and pet owners use this skill to check pet-friendly hotel guidance, domestic airline pet transport policies, train pet restrictions, and document checklists for pet travel in China and selected international routes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Hotel, airline, rail, and destination pet policies can change after the local built-in data was published. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current rules directly with the hotel, airline, railway provider, destination authority, or official channel before booking or travel. <br>
Risk: The skill provides travel and lodging guidance but does not book hotels or replace pet health, veterinary, legal, or border-control advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a planning checklist and involve the relevant provider, veterinarian, or authority for final decisions and required documentation. <br>
Risk: Some pet transport restrictions depend on animal breed, weight, carrier size, temperature, aircraft type, route, and limited per-trip capacity. <br>
Mitigation: Verify trip-specific constraints early, reserve pet transport slots in advance, and avoid relying on generic policy summaries for time-sensitive travel. <br>
