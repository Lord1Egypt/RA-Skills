## Description: <br>
Guides agents through high-fidelity, visual-first website rebuilding from permitted design references using screenshots, targeted DOM/CSS inspection, asset inspection, and React/Tailwind componentization. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and design-oriented agents use this skill to rebuild owned or permitted websites and prototypes by capturing visual references, extracting design tokens, and translating them into clean React/Tailwind components. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The workflow can copy protected implementation details or media, including site assets, DOM structure, shader code, video, and canvas output. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only on sites and assets you own or have explicit permission to reproduce, and avoid shader interception, full outerHTML copying, video extraction, or canvas recording on third-party sites. <br>
Risk: High-fidelity reconstruction can reproduce copyrighted design elements, trademarks, branding, or material covered by site terms. <br>
Mitigation: Review rights before use, remove or replace protected branding and assets, and comply with applicable copyright, trademark, and terms-of-service requirements. <br>
