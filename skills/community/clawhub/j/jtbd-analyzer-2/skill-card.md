## Description: <br>
Uncovers the real jobs customers hire a product to do by analyzing functional, emotional, and social motivations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[marjoriebroad](https://clawhub.ai/user/marjoriebroad) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product teams, founders, marketers, and researchers use this skill to analyze customer motivations, write Jobs-To-Be-Done statements, identify alternatives, and prioritize underserved customer jobs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags include crypto and purchasing even though the security evidence describes a text-only analysis skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review install-time permissions or capability prompts and avoid granting unrelated privileges. <br>
Risk: JTBD outputs can become misleading product-strategy guidance when based on sparse or unvalidated customer input. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated jobs, alternatives, and priorities as hypotheses and validate them with customer research before making product decisions. <br>
