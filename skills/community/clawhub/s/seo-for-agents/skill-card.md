## Description: <br>
SEO for Agents provides guidance for making websites discoverable to AI agents through llms.txt, structured data, agent-facing APIs, GEO content strategy, and monitoring. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, site owners, and content teams use this skill to have an agent plan agent-discoverable web presence, including llms.txt files, structured service facts, Schema.org markup, API endpoint recommendations, and citation-friendly content updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated llms.txt files, facts pages, structured data, or API endpoints could publish sensitive business details or unsupported claims. <br>
Mitigation: Review all generated public content and endpoint descriptions against approved facts before publishing. <br>
Risk: Agent-facing inquiry or service endpoints could be implemented with unsafe write behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Require authentication, validation, rate limits, and human review for write actions; keep discovery endpoints read-only where possible. <br>
