## Description: <br>
Podcast Producer helps agents plan and produce podcast episodes, including research, scripting, guest preparation, audio production guidance, show notes, distribution, analytics, and content repurposing. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mtsatryan](https://clawhub.ai/user/mtsatryan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content teams, podcast hosts, and agents use this skill to structure end-to-end podcast production workflows, from episode planning and guest preparation through show notes, distribution, promotion, and analytics. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated guest outreach, sponsorship copy, show notes, or promotional posts may include claims about real people, sponsors, platforms, or public facts that need verification. <br>
Mitigation: Review and fact-check all externally published or sent content before release. <br>
Risk: Generated production scripts or publishing guidance may not match a team's legal, brand, or platform requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Have a responsible producer or editor approve scripts, metadata, sponsorship language, and distribution settings before use. <br>
