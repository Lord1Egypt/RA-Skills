## Description: <br>
Navigate Zillow for buying, selling, investing, and market research with Zestimate interpretation, pricing strategy, and ROI analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External real estate users use this skill for Zillow-based property search, Zestimate interpretation, buyer and seller pricing decisions, investment calculations, and market research. It emphasizes cross-checking Zillow data against sold comps, tax records, rental comps, flood maps, and other authoritative local sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Zillow estimates, listing status, property taxes, rental estimates, and other real estate data may be stale, incomplete, or locally inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Verify Zillow outputs against sold comps, county assessor records, rental comps, FEMA flood maps, local MLS data where available, and qualified legal, tax, or financial professionals before acting. <br>
