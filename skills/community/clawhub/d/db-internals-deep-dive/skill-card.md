## Description: <br>
Guides an agent through deep technical explanations of PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, RabbitMQ, and Kafka internals, including storage, replication, consistency, performance, and operations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, database engineers, and platform teams use this skill to get structured internals deep dives for PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, RabbitMQ, and Kafka.
