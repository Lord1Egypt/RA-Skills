## Description: <br>
Organize child activity registrations with deadline tracking, document checklists, payments, gear, schedules, transportation, and follow-up reminders. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Parents, caregivers, guardians, and family organizers use this skill to organize child activity signups, compare options, track deadlines and payments, prepare required materials, and create calendar handoffs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security summary flags unsafe advice to store a child's ID and payment-related details in a reusable note. <br>
Mitigation: Keep planning notes to checklist labels and status only; enter sensitive identity or payment information directly into trusted organizer portals or a secure password/document manager. <br>
Risk: Registration plans may be incomplete or stale if organizer policies, fees, supervision rules, medical requirements, deadlines, refunds, or availability change. <br>
Mitigation: Verify official requirements and current availability directly with the activity organizer before acting on the command sheet. <br>
