## Description: <br>
Paper Workflow coordinates academic drafting, reviewer-style validation, and figure planning to turn existing project assets into a submission-ready academic paper. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[lzfxxx](https://clawhub.ai/user/lzfxxx) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, researchers, and technical teams use this skill to convert existing code, documentation, screenshots, pilot notes, and summaries into academic-paper artifacts such as abstracts, outlines, manuscript drafts, figure plans, review checklists, and submission packages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Companion-skill installation commands fetch external skills from LobeHub and GitHub. <br>
Mitigation: Review those installation commands and the installed companion skill files before relying on the full workflow. <br>
Risk: Unpublished project materials, screenshots, or draft paper assets may contain sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Tell the agent which materials are confidential and whether screenshots must preserve their original language or be anonymized. <br>
Risk: Paper drafting can overstate evidence or present planned evaluation as completed results. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's evidence-level and reviewer-quality gates to separate measured results, pilot evidence, case evidence, and future work. <br>
