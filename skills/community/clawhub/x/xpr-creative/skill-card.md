## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent developers use this skill to generate and store creative job deliverables such as Markdown, PDFs, CSVs, generated images, generated videos, web media, and public GitHub repositories. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can publish arbitrary content to public or persistent third-party services. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in workspaces approved for Replicate, Pinata/IPFS, and GitHub publication, and require human review before IPFS uploads or public repository creation. <br>
Risk: Configured service tokens may expose sensitive workspace content through generated deliverables or public repositories. <br>
Mitigation: Do not configure service tokens in workspaces containing secrets, private code, customer data, or unreleased business content unless additional review controls are enforced. <br>
