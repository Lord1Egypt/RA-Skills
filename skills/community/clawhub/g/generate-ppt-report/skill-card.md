## Description: <br>
Generates a daily financial-market PowerPoint brief from collected market data using a local M2.pptx dashboard template. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[szrw1825](https://clawhub.ai/user/szrw1825) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Finance and operations users use this skill to produce a single-slide daily market dashboard covering market performance, policy updates, and company news. It is intended for environments that maintain the expected local E:\daily files and workspace-yoyo configuration. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generic PPT-generation requests may invoke a workflow tailored to local financial-market report files and templates. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user intends to run this specific daily market brief workflow and that the expected E:\daily template, data, and workspace-yoyo configuration are present before execution. <br>
