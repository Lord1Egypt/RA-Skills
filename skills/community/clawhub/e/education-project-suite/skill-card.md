为初中教师提供省级和市级教育课题申报全流程AI辅助，涵盖选题推荐、申报书生成、竞争力优化及实施指导。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
教师、教研人员和学校管理者 use this skill to choose an appropriate provincial or municipal education research project path, draft proposal materials, improve proposal competitiveness, and plan implementation after approval. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may enter student-identifying, private school, or personnel information while drafting proposal materials. <br>
Mitigation: Collect only the minimum necessary context and avoid entering unnecessary personal, student, school, or personnel data. <br>
Risk: Generated proposal content may omit local application requirements or overstate project competitiveness. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated materials against official application notices, local forms, deadlines, and reviewer criteria before submission. <br>
Risk: The release declares package dependencies that may be installed by an OpenClaw setup. <br>
Mitigation: Pin, review, or scan the declared dependencies before installation in managed environments. <br>
