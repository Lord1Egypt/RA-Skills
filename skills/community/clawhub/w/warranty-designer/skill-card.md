## Description: <br>
Design product warranty and guarantee programs that increase buyer confidence and conversion rates while managing liability exposure, including policy language, claim workflows, fulfillment costs, and disclosure requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

External operators, founders, product teams, finance teams, and support teams use this skill to design, revise, cost, disclose, and operationalize product warranty or guarantee programs. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Warranty language may be incomplete for a specific jurisdiction, market, or product category. <br>
Mitigation: Have counsel review jurisdiction-specific warranty terms before publishing customer-facing policy language. <br>
Risk: Example claim-rate and reserve calculations may understate or overstate actual financial exposure. <br>
Mitigation: Have finance validate claim assumptions, remedy costs, and reserve levels against SKU-level operating data. <br>
Risk: Claim or order examples can contain customer personal information. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting unnecessary customer PII into prompts; redact or summarize claim examples when possible. <br>
