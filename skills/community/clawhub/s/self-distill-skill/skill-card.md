## Description: <br>
Provides a Jungian psychology-based framework for structuring personality metadata and producing evidence-based persona analyses. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[roryyu](https://clawhub.ai/user/roryyu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to analyze public figures, fictional characters, personal profiles, and relationships through a structured personality framework. It guides evidence collection, archetype identification, cognitive-function inference, and standardized Markdown persona output. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Personality analysis can be mistaken for a definitive psychological assessment. <br>
Mitigation: Frame outputs as evidence-based inferences, preserve uncertainty, and avoid diagnostic or absolute claims. <br>
Risk: Low-quality or secondhand source material can distort persona conclusions. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer primary materials, cite the evidence level used, and cross-check claims before relying on the analysis. <br>
Risk: Users may over-rely on generated relationship or self-awareness guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Keep recommendations reflective and non-clinical, and route medical, mental-health, legal, or employment decisions to qualified human review. <br>
