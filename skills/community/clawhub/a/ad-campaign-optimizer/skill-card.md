## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill describes automatically changing paid advertising campaigns without enough safety controls or clear permission scoping. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped account permissions, hard budget limits, preview every proposed change, and require manual approval before creating ads, launching tests, changing targeting, or increasing spend. <br>
Risk: Optimization guidance may affect spend, targeting, or campaign performance if applied without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review cost, ROI, audience, and creative recommendations against business constraints before applying them to live campaigns. <br>
