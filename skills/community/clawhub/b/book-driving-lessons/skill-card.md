### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to search for driving-lesson providers, check available lesson times, and create a booking through Lokuli. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can send contact information and booking details to Lokuli's external service. <br>
Mitigation: Before creating a booking, review the provider, service, time slot, name, email, and phone number with the user and confirm they want those details sent to Lokuli. <br>
