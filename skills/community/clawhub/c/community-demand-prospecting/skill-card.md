## Description: <br>
Audit a repo or product, run market research and competitor research, choose positioning, find users, and draft safe Reddit or X outreach. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cuongducle](https://clawhub.ai/user/cuongducle) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, founders, and product teams use this skill to turn a repository or product brief into launch-readiness findings, market and competitor research, positioning, community prospecting queries, scored opportunities, and safe draft outreach. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outreach could become spammy, repetitive, or inconsistent with community rules if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated reply before posting, follow each community or platform's rules, disclose relevant product relationships, and avoid automated or repetitive outreach. <br>
Risk: Drafts may over-promote a product when a thread is a poor fit or when a helpful answer without promotion is safer. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill's intent and risk scoring, skip low-fit or high-risk threads, and default to help-only or soft-mention engagement when appropriate. <br>
