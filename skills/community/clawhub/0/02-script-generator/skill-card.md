## Description: <br>
Automatically converts structured novel or IP information into concise short-drama scripts with controlled pacing, conflict, and episode duration. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ghwyever](https://clawhub.ai/user/ghwyever) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content teams and developers use this skill to turn structured novel or IP details into 90-120 second short-drama episodes for Douyin/Fanqie-style production workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Story or IP details are sent to the configured model API. <br>
Mitigation: Install only with a trusted model provider and API base, use a scoped API key where possible, and avoid confidential unpublished IP, personal data, or proprietary story material unless the provider's data retention and training policies are acceptable. <br>
