## Description: <br>
Automates short-form video mashup planning with MoviePy editing, Edge TTS voiceover, synchronized subtitles, batch generation, and quality checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[dally-bee](https://clawhub.ai/user/dally-bee) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content operators, marketers, and developers use this skill to create batches of short promotional videos for platforms such as Douyin, WeChat Channels, and Xiaohongshu. It guides material selection, copywriting, voice generation, video editing, subtitle timing, and quality control for repeated short-video production. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Script text may be sent to Edge TTS for voice generation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sending confidential scripts, customer data, or unpublished campaign copy unless third-party processing is acceptable for the use case. <br>
Risk: Generated code snippets may need review before use in a local video-production environment. <br>
Mitigation: Verify the publisher and review any Python implementation created from the snippets before installing dependencies or processing media. <br>
