## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers, consultants, agencies, and service operators use this skill to draft tailored cold outreach for AI operations managed services. It helps generate subject lines, opening hooks, short email bodies, CTAs, follow-up framing, and client-specific positioning. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outreach may include unsupported social proof, results claims, or compliance-sensitive messaging. <br>
Mitigation: Review and personalize messages before use, avoid unsupported claims, and ensure any email or CRM automation remains user-controlled and compliant with applicable anti-spam and privacy rules. <br>
Risk: Generated messages may reference credentials or contact workflows that are not appropriate for a specific campaign. <br>
Mitigation: Do not include API keys or tokens in messages, verify recipient addresses before sending, and keep credential handling outside the outreach content. <br>
