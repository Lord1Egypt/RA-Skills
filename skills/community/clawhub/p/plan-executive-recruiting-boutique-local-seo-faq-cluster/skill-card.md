## Description: <br>
Research local questions, group them into clusters, and draft launch-ready FAQ copy for an executive recruiting boutique. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kirkraman](https://clawhub.ai/user/kirkraman) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and SEO practitioners working with executive recruiting firms use this skill to research local search questions, organize them into FAQ clusters, and draft publishable local SEO FAQ copy. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated recruiting marketing content can include inaccurate local SEO claims or compliance-sensitive employment language. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs for recruiting, legal, compliance, and factual accuracy before publication. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misapplied to candidate evaluation, hiring decisions, or other sensitive employment workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only for marketing and SEO copy; keep candidate screening and hiring analysis out of scope. <br>
