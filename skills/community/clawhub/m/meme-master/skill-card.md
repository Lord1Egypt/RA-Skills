## Description: <br>
Use when the user talks with memes, stickers, reaction images, or wants a more natural image-meme chat style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ave-nue](https://clawhub.ai/user/ave-nue) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agent operators use this skill to make agents interpret meme, sticker, and reaction-image exchanges by social meaning while switching to literal image analysis only when requested. It also guides lightweight meme library organization and conservative meme reply behavior. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The agent may retain memes and usage notes in a workspace-local meme folder and index. <br>
Mitigation: Disable passive collection for private channels, avoid saving personal or sensitive images, and periodically review or delete <workspace>/memes/. <br>
Risk: Meme replies can feel dismissive in serious, urgent, vulnerable, or technical conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use text-only responses when the tone is serious or ambiguous, and reserve meme replies for casual situations where they add clear social value. <br>
