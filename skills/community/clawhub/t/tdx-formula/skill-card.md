## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated stock screening or indicator formulas could be mistaken for financial advice or used directly in live trading decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as technical drafting assistance; review and test formulas before use and make independent trading decisions. <br>
Risk: Future functions can repaint signals by moving, changing, or removing historical signals. <br>
Mitigation: Prefer non-future functions for stock selection and clearly flag any use of future functions before relying on a formula. <br>
