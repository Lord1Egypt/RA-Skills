## Description: <br>
Automatically generates detailed project proposals with templates, requirements analysis, cost and timeline estimates, and risk assessments. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Freelancers, agencies, and project teams use this skill to draft Chinese-language client project proposals from short project prompts or requirements documents, including scope, schedule, budget, payment terms, team roles, and risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated cost and schedule estimates may be inaccurate for the actual project scope. <br>
Mitigation: Provide concrete requirements, budget assumptions, and timeline constraints, then verify estimates before sending a proposal to a client. <br>
Risk: Generated proposals may contain generic placeholders or assumptions that are not appropriate for a specific client. <br>
Mitigation: Review the proposal for client-specific facts, contact details, payment terms, and risk statements before delivery. <br>
