## Description: <br>
Analyzes Chinese A-share K-line data with real-time prices, technical indicators, candlestick pattern detection, volume analysis, structured reports, scoring, trend estimates, and optional charts. <br>

This skill is for research and development only. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers, analysts, and agents can use this skill to run informational technical analysis for Chinese A-share stocks, including indicator summaries, structured reports, JSON output, and optional chart generation. It should not be treated as personalized investment advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial analysis output may be mistaken for personalized investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational, verify data independently, and do not rely on buy, hold, reduce, or trend wording as the sole basis for trading. <br>
Risk: Results depend on third-party market data and local Python dependencies. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the required packages are installed, check that data sources are reachable, and review generated reports or charts before relying on them. <br>
