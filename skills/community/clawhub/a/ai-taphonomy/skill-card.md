## Description: <br>
Ai Taphonomy is an educational guide that helps agents explain taphonomy, fossil formation, preservation bias, diagenesis, Lagerstatten, time-averaging, fossil surface traces, and experimental or forensic taphonomy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
