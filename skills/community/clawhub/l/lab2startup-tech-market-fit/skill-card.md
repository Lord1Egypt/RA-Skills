## Description: <br>
Lab2startup Tech Market Fit helps teacher-student startup teams compare technology-led and market-led strategies, explore application scenarios, and plan technology-to-market transitions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[earthwalking](https://clawhub.ai/user/earthwalking) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, students, mentors, and agents use this skill to assess whether a startup project should lead with technical advantage or market demand, then produce a technology-market fit strategy report with scenario selection, transition planning, and validation experiments. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill's examples and frameworks may be mistaken for current market evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as planning guidance and validate real business decisions with fresh market research, customer interviews, and domain experts. <br>
Risk: Broad Chinese-language triggers for technology-market fit could activate the skill in adjacent business-strategy conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Narrow triggers or confirm the user wants technology-market fit guidance before applying the full framework. <br>
