## Description: <br>
Socratic design refinement before coding. Use when user requests feature without clear spec. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Nefas11](https://clawhub.ai/user/Nefas11) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and agents use this skill to clarify vague or complex feature requests before implementation. It guides requirements discovery, compares two to three approaches, and records the selected design in a markdown design document. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Design notes may contain sensitive project or business details. <br>
Mitigation: Keep secrets and unnecessary sensitive details out of docs/design/ notes, and review documents before handing them to follow-on planning workflows. <br>
