## Description: <br>
A conversational assistant for drafting insurance marketing copy, compliance review notes, and image-generation prompts for insurance promotion workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gechengling](https://clawhub.ai/user/gechengling) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Insurance agents, brokers, and marketing teams use this skill to draft social posts, campaign copy, product disclaimers, image prompts, and compliance review notes for insurance promotion across Chinese social and messaging channels. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China-focused <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Compliance review or regulatory summaries may be mistaken for legal approval. <br>
Mitigation: Use generated compliance checks as drafting support and have company compliance or legal reviewers approve materials before publishing. <br>
Risk: Generated marketing copy may include outdated, incomplete, or product-specific claims that do not match current terms. <br>
Mitigation: Verify product terms, disclaimers, and current regulatory requirements against authoritative company and regulator sources before use. <br>
