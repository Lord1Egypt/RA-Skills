## Description: <br>
Guides agents through a circle-of-competence test for consequential decisions in unfamiliar domains, helping users decide whether to act, defer, wait, or size a small learning bet. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[deciqai](https://clawhub.ai/user/deciqai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, employees, founders, investors, and decision-makers use this skill to evaluate whether they have calibrated competence for a consequential decision before entering a market, making a senior hire, investing in a domain, or allocating significant capital. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may apply the skill's coaching output to consequential financial, hiring, or business decisions as if it were professional advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as structured coaching, consult qualified domain experts for high-stakes decisions, and document uncertainty before acting. <br>
Risk: Confidential decision details could be copied into reusable examples or notes while using the skill. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving sensitive company, investment, hiring, or personal details in reusable artifacts; redact or generalize decision context where possible. <br>
