# Platform Playbook

The same idea must be re-cut for each platform. Below are the practical differences that change the script, plus the CTA path each platform rewards.

## TikTok

- **Length:** 15–34s converts best for product videos; under 30s for impulse buys.
- **Hook:** native, slightly raw, "talking to a friend" energy. Trend-aware audio helps reach.
- **Pacing:** fastest of the three — cut every 1.5–3s, text on screen almost constantly.
- **Sell intensity:** high. Direct CTAs and TikTok Shop integration are expected, not resented.
- **CTA path:** "Tap the orange cart" (TikTok Shop), product link sticker, or "comment WORD and I'll send the link." One action only.
- **Sound:** design for sound-on but caption everything; many watch muted at first.

## Instagram Reels

- **Length:** 15–30s; Reels audiences favor polish and aesthetic over rawness.
- **Hook:** strong visual hook; cleaner, more brand-consistent look than TikTok.
- **Pacing:** brisk but smoother; transitions and on-beat cuts perform well.
- **Sell intensity:** softer. Optimize the ending for **saves and shares** (e.g. "save this for your next restock"), which Reels weights heavily.
- **CTA path:** "Link in bio," product tags/shopping stickers, or "DM me [word]." Avoid hard, repeated selling.
- **Sound:** trending audio aids distribution; keep brand voice intact over it.

## YouTube Shorts

- **Length:** 20–45s; Shorts tolerate slightly more setup and explanation.
- **Hook:** problem- or curiosity-led; works for more considered purchases.
- **Pacing:** can breathe a little more; still change the visual every ~3s.
- **Sell intensity:** medium, education-forward. Watch-time and completion are the key signals.
- **CTA path:** "Link in the description"; invite research ("read the reviews") for higher-ticket items.
- **Sound:** clear voiceover matters most; music supports rather than leads.

## Cross-platform rule

Write the master idea once, then produce three variants: re-time the hook, adjust sell intensity, and swap the CTA to each platform's buy path. Never publish one identical cut everywhere — completion rates and the algorithm punish the mismatch.

## Length-to-beat guide

| Length | Hook | Body | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| 15s | 0–1s | 1–12s | 12–15s |
| 30s | 0–2s | 2–24s | 24–30s |
| 45s | 0–2s | 2–38s | 38–45s |
| 60s | 0–3s | 3–50s | 50–60s (only if payoff justifies length) |
