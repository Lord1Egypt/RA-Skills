## Description: <br>
Search products, check prices and stock, create quotes, place orders — multi-tenant B2B/B2C commerce API <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Commerce agents and operators use this skill to search product catalogs, check EUR pricing and stock, create quotes, place orders, and track order status for multi-tenant B2B/B2C product commerce. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can create quotes and place real orders, including high-value orders up to EUR 50,000. <br>
Mitigation: Require manual approval before quote creation or order placement, and confirm products, quantities, total price, delivery address, tenant or account, payment handling, and cancellation or correction process. <br>
