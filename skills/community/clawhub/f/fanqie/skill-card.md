## Description: <br>
Provides summaries and trend analysis for popular novels and short drama adaptation candidates from public Fanqie Novel pages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[codenova58](https://clawhub.ai/user/codenova58) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and content analysts use this skill to summarize visible Fanqie Novel listings, rankings, and book metadata, then screen topics for trend tracking or short drama adaptation potential. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill could be used too broadly against Fanqie Novel pages despite its public-page analysis scope. <br>
Mitigation: Keep use limited to visible public pages, avoid bulk collection, and follow the site's rules. <br>
Risk: Dynamic pages may produce incomplete or stale summaries if content has not finished loading. <br>
Mitigation: Wait for pages to render before extracting visible fields, and include source links or collection time when summarizing rankings. <br>
Risk: Trend and adaptation-potential analysis may overstate confidence when based only on visible summaries, tags, and rankings. <br>
Mitigation: Present screening results as preliminary analysis and keep the underlying visible fields available for review. <br>
