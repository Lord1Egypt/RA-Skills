## Description: <br>
Generates short weekly safety talk scripts for construction crews and supervisors. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[tobeyrebecca](https://clawhub.ai/user/tobeyrebecca) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Construction teams, general contractors, estimators, project managers, and supervisors use this skill to draft concise toolbox talk scripts for recurring jobsite safety briefings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated construction safety talks may omit site-specific hazards, company policy, OSHA or local requirements, or supervisor judgment. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt every generated script against the actual jobsite conditions and applicable safety requirements before presenting it to a crew or using text-to-speech externally. <br>
