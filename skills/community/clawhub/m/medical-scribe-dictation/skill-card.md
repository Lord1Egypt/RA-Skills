## Description: <br>
Converts physician dictation or transcribed clinical text into structured SOAP notes with terminology normalization and clinical quality checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aipoch-ai](https://clawhub.ai/user/aipoch-ai) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Healthcare teams and developers use this agent to convert physician dictation, audio transcription, or pre-transcribed clinical text into reviewable SOAP-note drafts for documentation workflows. Generated notes require clinician review before use in a medical record. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Patient dictation may be sent to external LLM APIs when the OpenAI or Anthropic option is used. <br>
Mitigation: Use those providers with patient data only after organizational approval of the provider, privacy terms, and PHI handling; otherwise run in an isolated environment and use the non-LLM fallback path. <br>
Risk: Generated diagnoses, differential diagnoses, reasoning, and plans may be incomplete or misleading. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as draft documentation and require clinician review before finalizing or entering notes into a medical record. <br>
Risk: The security verdict requires review because clinical-safety and network boundaries are understated. <br>
Mitigation: Pin and review dependencies, scan before deployment, and document approved data-handling boundaries before production use. <br>
