## Description: <br>
Generates structured post outlines from reference materials for wisdom-style social posts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[vincentchan](https://clawhub.ai/user/vincentchan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, marketers, and social media teams use this skill to turn newsletters, scripts, notes, journal entries, URLs, or raw ideas into five structured social post outlines with distinct themes, paradoxes, examples, objections, and closing insights. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outlines can include direct quotes or summaries from user-supplied material. <br>
Mitigation: Provide only source material intended for analysis and review the saved Markdown before publishing or sharing it. <br>
Risk: The requested style favors strong, absolute language that may overstate nuance. <br>
Mitigation: Edit outlines for accuracy, audience fit, and appropriate qualifications before turning them into public posts. <br>
Risk: User-provided URLs may expose private or internal content to the agent workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Use only URLs and source material that are appropriate for the agent to fetch and analyze. <br>
