## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Arduino guidance or generated code could be unsafe for real hardware if power, motor, battery, wiring, or voltage-level details are wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated Arduino code and hardware assumptions before upload, and verify wiring, power supply capacity, grounds, and voltage levels against the target board and components. <br>
