## Description: <br>
Use when using Feishu/Lark Calendar through lark-cli or feishu-cli for agendas, scheduling, freebusy checks, meeting rooms, RSVP, review blocks, release windows, and follow-up planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and team members who use Lark or Feishu calendars use this skill to plan project review, QA, release, retro, and follow-up events through lark-cli or feishu-cli. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Calendar commands can create events, invite attendees, book rooms, or add reminders. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed lark-cli or feishu-cli calendar commands, participants, rooms, reminders, and times before running them. <br>
Risk: Ambiguous participants or times can lead to incorrect meeting creation or updates. <br>
Mitigation: Do not create or update meetings until participant and time details are explicit. <br>
