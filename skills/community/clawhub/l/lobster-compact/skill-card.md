## Description: <br>
Summarize long conversations to preserve context. Automatically triggered when context window approaches limits, or manually with /compact. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangxiaofei860208-source](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangxiaofei860208-source) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Agents and users working in long conversations use this skill to create structured summaries that preserve requests, decisions, technical context, files, errors, feedback, open tasks, and next steps for continued work. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Conversation summaries and memory updates may retain sensitive details from long chats. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid using the skill on chats containing secrets, credentials, private keys, regulated personal data, or confidential project details unless those details are redacted or intentionally retained. <br>
