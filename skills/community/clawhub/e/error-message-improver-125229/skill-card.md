## Description: <br>
Helps users improve software error messages by turning requests into a practical plan, artifact, checklist, analysis, or implementation support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[kyro-ma](https://clawhub.ai/user/kyro-ma) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and maintainers use this skill to improve software error messages by clarifying requirements, producing checklists or implementation guidance, and validating the result against success criteria. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger terms may cause implicit activation on generic requests containing words like better or error. <br>
Mitigation: Invoke the skill explicitly or tighten trigger wording before relying on implicit activation. <br>
Risk: Suggested message wording or implementation guidance may not fit the target product context. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs against the application's requirements, user audience, and validation criteria before adoption. <br>
