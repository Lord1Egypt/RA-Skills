# AI Automation Agency Starter Kit

**Category:** Business / AI / Agency Building  
**Tier:** Premium  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Author:** maxagent0x1

## What This Skill Does

Builds the complete launch system for an AI automation agency in 4 prompts — from positioning and niche selection through client acquisition, service packaging, and delivery playbooks.

AI automation is the fastest-growing service category of 2025-2026. Businesses are spending $5K-$50K to automate workflows they can't build themselves. This skill gives freelancers and consultants the complete agency infrastructure to capture that market in under a week.

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Agency Positioning & Niche Selector (FREE)
Input: your background, current skills, target industry, desired income
Output:
- Top 3 niche recommendations with demand score, competition score, and average project value
- Agency positioning statement (one-sentence, two-sentence, and elevator pitch versions)
- 3 core service descriptions (plain English, no jargon)
- Differentiation hook: why YOUR agency vs. a generalist
- 30-second cold intro script for phone/video

### Prompt 2 — Service Packaging & Pricing Guide
Input: chosen niche, skill level, target client size, local vs. remote
Output:
- 3-tier productized service menu (Starter / Growth / Enterprise) with scope, deliverables, timeline, and price for each
- Proposal template (executive summary, problem statement, solution overview, deliverables, timeline, investment, next steps)
- Pricing psychology guide: anchoring, value framing, ROI projection formula
- Rate justification script for "that's too expensive" objections
- Upsell path: retainer model framing and monthly maintenance pricing
- Contract scope definition (what's in, what's out) to prevent scope creep

### Prompt 3 — Client Acquisition System
Input: chosen niche, agency name, LinkedIn URL, local market
Output:
- LinkedIn profile optimization checklist (headline formula, about section, featured section, banner brief)
- 5-message LinkedIn DM sequence: connection request → value opener → case study → soft pitch → follow-up
- Cold email sequence: 3-email drip (hook → proof → ask) with subject lines, bodies, and CTAs
- Discovery call script: 12 questions, qualification framework (Budget / Authority / Need / Timeline), close script
- 3 case study templates (before/after format) ready to fill with your first client results
- 5 content post templates for LinkedIn to build authority (problem/solution, data insight, mini case study, myth bust, process reveal)
- Outreach tracking system: CRM-free pipeline tracker (Notion table structure + status definitions)

### Prompt 4 — Delivery & Fulfillment Playbook
Input: service type, primary tools used, client name and industry
Output:
- Client onboarding sequence: welcome email, questionnaire (15 questions), kickoff call agenda, shared folder structure
- Project documentation template: scope document, change order form, progress update format
- 3-phase delivery framework: Discovery → Build → Launch, each with owner, deliverables, and sign-off gate
- Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) pack: 5 reusable SOPs for common AI automation tasks (Zapier/Make workflow, data pipeline, chatbot deployment, report automation, email automation)
- Results reporting template: before/after metrics, ROI calculation, testimonial request email
- Offboarding + retention system: project close email, 30-day check-in, retainer pitch script, referral ask
