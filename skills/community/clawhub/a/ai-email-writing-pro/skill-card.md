## Description: <br>
Professional email writing assistant with tone adjustment, template library, grammar checking, and response suggestions for business communication. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and business communicators use this skill to draft, revise, and evaluate professional emails, including outreach, follow-ups, meeting requests, status updates, apologies, and recommendations. It can also suggest subject lines, tone adjustments, reply drafts, templates, and writing-quality improvements. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated outreach may include misleading urgency, spam-like content, or claims that are not appropriate for the sender's context. <br>
Mitigation: Review every generated message before sending, verify factual claims, and avoid deceptive urgency or unsolicited bulk outreach. <br>
Risk: Analytics and tracking examples may create privacy or consent obligations when applied to real recipients. <br>
Mitigation: Use tracking only with appropriate recipient consent, privacy disclosure, and organizational approval. <br>
Risk: Tone, grammar, and subject-line suggestions may not match legal, compliance, cultural, or relationship expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Treat suggestions as drafts and adapt them to the audience, jurisdiction, and communication policy before sending. <br>
