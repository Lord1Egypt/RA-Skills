## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Beekeeping requirements and registration rules vary by locality. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm applicable local regulations, permits, inspections, and neighbor-notification requirements before keeping bees or moving hives. <br>
Risk: Bee-sting allergy and emergency treatment guidance can be high risk if treated as medical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as general reference only and seek qualified medical advice or emergency care for allergic reactions or other health concerns. <br>
Risk: Disease-control and chemical-treatment recommendations may be unsuitable for a specific colony, product, or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify disease diagnosis, approved treatments, withholding periods, and animal-health guidance with qualified veterinary, extension, or local apiculture authorities. <br>
