## Description: <br>
Expert sales proposal and pricing presentation strategist for proposals, executive summaries, ROI business cases, pricing presentations, SOWs, RFP responses, and competitive positioning documents. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales, business development, and proposal teams use this skill to draft and improve buyer-facing proposals, executive summaries, pricing and ROI narratives, SOWs, RFP responses, competitive positioning, and follow-up messaging. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated pricing, ROI, terms, or SOW language may be inaccurate or inappropriate for a specific deal. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated pricing, ROI, terms, and SOW language before sending or relying on it. <br>
Risk: Sample names, figures, or assumptions may remain in generated proposal content. <br>
Mitigation: Replace sample names and numbers with verified customer, project, and commercial facts. <br>
Risk: Client-sensitive data may be exposed if pasted into the model context. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting sensitive client data unless its use in the model context is approved. <br>
