## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad browser automation can interact with live sites and accounts in unintended ways. <br>
Mitigation: Use isolated or test browser sessions where possible and review proposed browser actions before running them against real accounts. <br>
Risk: Saved browser state can contain cookies, localStorage, or other sensitive account data. <br>
Mitigation: Keep state files private, avoid saving real-account state unless necessary, and do not expose cookies or localStorage values in logs or chats. <br>
