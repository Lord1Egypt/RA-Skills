## Description: <br>
Aggregates and critically synthesizes scholarly literature from global academic sources into structured literature reviews, evidence tables, citation analysis, and research-gap summaries. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, PhD candidates, R&D teams, and evidence-based decision-makers use this skill to search, screen, appraise, and synthesize academic literature across disciplines and languages. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated syntheses may include incomplete, unavailable, low-quality, or inaccurate citations and source claims. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important claims against primary papers, academic databases, indexing status, and retraction records before relying on the output. <br>
Risk: Search coverage, language coverage, paywalls, and database availability can bias the included literature. <br>
Mitigation: Review the search strategy, inclusion and exclusion criteria, language choices, and omitted full texts before treating the synthesis as comprehensive. <br>
