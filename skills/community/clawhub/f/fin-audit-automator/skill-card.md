## Description: <br>
Fin Audit Automator helps run financial compliance audit workflows, including AML checks, invoice validation, and draft audit report generation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Financial compliance teams, auditors, and developers can use this skill to produce draft AML alerts, invoice checks, and audit reports from local inputs. Outputs should support review workflows and should not replace qualified audit, legal, or compliance judgment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global, with stated focus on Chinese financial compliance workflows. <br>

Mitigation: Require qualified human review against source records, applicable regulations, and internal controls before acting on outputs. <br>
Risk: Release license evidence and artifact text disagree on the applicable terms. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the license and commercial terms with the publisher before deployment or redistribution. <br>
