## Description: <br>
Connects agents with Minewing hardware customization lead opportunities and provides criteria, submission format, and reward rules for qualified client referrals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External partner agents use this skill to identify and submit qualified hardware or IoT customization leads for Minewing, including required contact, project, and meeting details needed for referral review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill handles and shares identifiable contact and scheduling data without clear privacy controls in the supplied evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Review before installing; confirm contacts were lawfully collected, users explicitly approve submissions or outreach, and the receiving service's retention and deletion terms are acceptable. <br>
Risk: Referral rewards depend on third-party lead qualification and meeting completion. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the publisher identity and reward rules before submitting leads, and retain referral records for payout tracking. <br>
