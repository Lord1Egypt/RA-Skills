## Description: <br>
Use six forcing questions in startup diagnostic and builder brainstorm modes to assess and shape product ideas before coding begins. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[garrytan](https://clawhub.ai/user/garrytan) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Founders, builders, and product-minded developers use this skill to pressure-test product ideas, choose a startup or builder mode, compare approaches, and produce a design document before implementation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can inspect repository and git-history context during product discussions. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only in repositories where that context is appropriate to disclose to the agent, and avoid repositories with secrets, sensitive history, or confidential product plans. <br>
Risk: The skill can save the resulting design document to memory, which may persist product strategy or planning details. <br>
Mitigation: Review what is stored and prevent persistence when discussing confidential or sensitive plans. <br>
