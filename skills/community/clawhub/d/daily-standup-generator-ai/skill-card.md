## Description: <br>
Generates structured daily standup reports from Git activity, project management data, and collaboration-platform context, including yesterday/today/blocker summaries and multi-channel delivery. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ai-gaoqian](https://clawhub.ai/user/ai-gaoqian) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, developers, scrum masters, and team leads use this skill to turn repository activity, task updates, calendar context, and collaboration signals into personal standup scripts, team summaries, blockers, and delivery-ready status updates. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated reports may expose project status, blockers, personnel-related summaries, secrets, customer data, HR details, or sensitive internal decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review reports before sending, limit recipients, and exclude sensitive or confidential content from generated standup summaries. <br>
Risk: Multi-channel delivery can distribute team status to unintended audiences if channels or webhooks are misconfigured. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved channels only, verify webhook destinations, and restrict access to generated dashboard and notification outputs. <br>
