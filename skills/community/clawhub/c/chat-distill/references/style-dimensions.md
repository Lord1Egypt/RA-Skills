# Style Dimensions

Analyze the target speaker across these dimensions. For each, provide concrete examples from the chat.

## 1. 词汇特征 (Vocabulary)

- **高频词/口头禅**: Top repeated words, catchphrases, fillers (e.g. "就是说", "然后就是", "emmm", "笑死")
- **用词层级**: Formal vs casual, slang, internet speak, dialect words
- **专业术语/领域词**: Domain-specific jargon (gaming, tech, finance, etc.)
- **避用词**: Words the speaker never uses (compared to peers)

## 2. 句式特征 (Sentence Patterns)

- **平均句长**: Short punchy vs long flowing
- **句式类型偏好**: Questions, exclamations, statements, fragments
- **连词习惯**: How ideas connect ("然后", "所以", "but also", "话说回来")
- **省略习惯**: Dropping subjects, abbreviating, using shorthand

## 3. 语气与情感 (Tone & Emotion)

- **情绪基调**: Overall emotional range (upbeat, dry, sarcastic, warm, etc.)
- **情绪表达方式**: Direct ("我很开心!") vs understated ("还行吧") vs emoji-mediated
- **幽默风格**: Sarcasm, self-deprecation, absurd, puns, deadpan
- **表达强度**: Uses exclamation marks? Caps? Repetition for emphasis?

## 4. 标点符号习惯 (Punctuation Habits)

- **句末标点**: Period vs no period, tilde (~), ellipsis (...), etc.
- **感叹号使用频率**: !!! vs single ! vs none
- **逗号/句号使用**: Frequent punctuation vs run-on messages
- **中英标点混用**: Chinese vs English punctuation

## 5. 表情与符号 (Emoji & Symbols)

- **常用表情包/emoji**: Top 5-10 most used
- **使用场景**: Emoji replaces words, supplements tone, or used sparingly
- **颜文字**: Kaomoji usage (╯°□°)╯︵ ┻━┻
- **特殊符号**: ★, ♪, →, etc.

## 6. 消息节奏 (Message Rhythm)

- **消息长度分布**: Many short bursts vs fewer long messages
- **回复速度**: Quick-fire vs thoughtful pauses
- **连续发送**: How many messages in a row before waiting for reply
- **时间模式**: Active hours, message frequency

## 7. 互动风格 (Interaction Style)

- **回应模式**: Agreeable, questioning, advice-giving, storytelling
- **称呼习惯**: How they address others (nicknames, full names, pronouns)
- **开场白/结束语**: Common greetings and sign-offs
- **主动性**: Who initiates topics more?

## 8. 主题偏好 (Topic Preferences)

- **常聊话题**: What they talk about most
- **话题展开方式**: Deep dive vs surface level, tangential vs focused
- **知识/观点表达**: Confident assertions vs hedging ("maybe", "我觉得")

## Scoring

For each dimension, assign:
- **Strength**: 1-5 (how distinctive/consistent this trait is)
- **Confidence**: low/medium/high (based on sample size)
