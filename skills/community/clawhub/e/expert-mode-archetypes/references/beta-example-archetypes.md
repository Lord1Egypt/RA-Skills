# Beta Example Archetypes v0.8.0

This beta set demonstrates the range of Expert Mode archetypes. These are not mandatory defaults and should not all be loaded at once. Use them as examples, seeds, or inspiration when selecting project-specific experts.

## Selection principle

Choose for coverage:

- expertise
- stakeholder reality
- risk
- operations
- translation
- quality/taste
- institutional review
- constraints
- adversarial testing
- field wisdom

## Notes on variety

### Professional experts

Software Architect, Prompt Engineering Expert, Customer Researcher, Incident Commander, and Launch Operator show classic role-based expertise.

### Non-job-title archetypes

Confused First-Time User, One-Person Maintenance Team, Bad-Faith User, Old Hand Who Has Seen This Fail Before, and Evidence Purist show why Expert Mode uses “archetype” rather than only “profession”.

### Client-facing wisdom

Expert-Client Relationship Advisor, Plain-Language Translator, Executive Briefer, and Brand Guardian show how expertise must be delivered in a way the client can receive and use.

### Risk and institutional review

Security Paranoiac, Privacy Guardian, Regulator/Auditor, Procurement Committee Member, and Bad-Faith User show defensive and approval-oriented perspectives.
