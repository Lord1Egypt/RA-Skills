## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Operational, legal, regulatory, pricing, and employment guidance may be outdated or vary by jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current DEA, OSHA, pricing, and employment figures for the relevant jurisdiction before relying on the guidance operationally. <br>
Risk: The skill may be mistaken for authoritative clinical, legal, or regulatory advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a reference context pack and have qualified professionals review decisions before implementation. <br>
Risk: Linked external sites may request information outside ClawHub. <br>
Mitigation: Do not enter payment details or sensitive practice data into external sites unless they are independently trusted. <br>
