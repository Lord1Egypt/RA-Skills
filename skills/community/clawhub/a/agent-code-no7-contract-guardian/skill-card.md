## Description: <br>
Agent Code No.7 Contract Guardian reviews Chinese and English contracts, identifies risky clauses, assesses compliance, and suggests revisions with an overall risk score. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[monarchmio](https://clawhub.ai/user/monarchmio) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Legal, procurement, and business users can use this skill to review pasted contract text, surface high-risk clauses, and receive structured modification suggestions. It is intended as reference support for contract review, not a substitute for qualified legal advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Contract text may contain confidential or sensitive business information. <br>
Mitigation: Only submit contracts in an agent environment approved for that information and avoid pasting confidential agreements into unsuitable environments. <br>
Risk: Generated contract analysis may be incomplete, jurisdiction-dependent, or inappropriate for high-value agreements. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as reference guidance and have important contracts reviewed by a qualified lawyer. <br>
