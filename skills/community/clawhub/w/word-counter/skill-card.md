## Description: <br>
A comprehensive text analysis tool that counts words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs. Calculates reading time, speaking time, reading level (Flesch-Kincaid), and keyword density. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[openlark](https://clawhub.ai/user/openlark) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, writers, editors, and content teams use this skill to analyze text length, estimate reading and speaking time, assess readability, and review keyword frequency for SEO-oriented content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Input text may contain private or sensitive content that is printed in derived analysis results. <br>
Mitigation: Only analyze files or text the user intends to process locally, and avoid passing sensitive content unless local handling is acceptable. <br>
