This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can use this skill for empathetic conversational support, active listening, emotional reflection, and non-clinical coping guidance. It is not a substitute for licensed therapy, diagnosis, treatment planning, confidentiality, or emergency intervention. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may mistake emotional support for diagnosis, treatment planning, therapy, or professional clinical advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses framed as non-clinical support, avoid diagnosis, and encourage qualified human professional care for persistent symptoms, trauma, severe depression, or other clinical concerns. <br>
Risk: Users in immediate danger, self-harm, suicide risk, or violence-risk situations may need urgent human intervention. <br>
Mitigation: Treat crisis indicators seriously, provide local emergency or crisis resources, and encourage involving qualified human support. <br>
Risk: Users may assume confidentiality that the agent cannot guarantee. <br>
Mitigation: Be clear that the agent is an AI support aid and should not promise confidentiality beyond the hosting environment's actual guarantees. <br>
