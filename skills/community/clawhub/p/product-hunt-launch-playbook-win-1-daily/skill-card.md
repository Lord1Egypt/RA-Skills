## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is a lightweight launch-resource page and includes external promotional or paid-product links. <br>
Mitigation: Review linked resources and paid offerings separately before relying on them for a launch plan. <br>
Risk: Product Hunt ranking behavior and platform rules can change, making tactical launch guidance stale or noncompliant. <br>
Mitigation: Validate tactics against current Product Hunt policies and use organic engagement practices before executing a launch. <br>
