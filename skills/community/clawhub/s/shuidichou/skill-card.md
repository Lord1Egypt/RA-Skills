## Description: <br>
水滴筹众筹助手 helps users find current ShuiDiChou medical crowdfunding projects for donation and, with explicit consent, submit a phone number for fundraising follow-up by a ShuiDiChou adviser. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[jiwenbing](https://clawhub.ai/user/jiwenbing) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to browse donation opportunities on ShuiDiChou and to request help starting a medical crowdfunding campaign. The skill is intended for charity and fundraising workflows where the user confirms intent before donation links are shown or contact details are submitted. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
China <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share a phone number before they understand how it will be used. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for explicit consent, explain that the number is submitted for ShuiDiChou fundraising follow-up, and provide the official hotline as an alternative. <br>
Risk: Users may donate through a link without confirming the destination. <br>
Mitigation: Show ShuiDiChou links clearly and advise users to verify the official domain and project details before donating. <br>
