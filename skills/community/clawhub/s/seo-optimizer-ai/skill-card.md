## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users to submit webpage content to an external service. <br>
Mitigation: Use only content that is approved for external processing, and avoid unpublished, confidential, regulated, or customer-sensitive material unless appropriate terms are in place. <br>
Risk: The documented service and payment confirmation flow use a plain-HTTP IP address. <br>
Mitigation: Do not send payment credentials or proof over the documented endpoint unless the publisher provides an HTTPS endpoint and clear data-handling terms. <br>
Risk: SEO recommendations can be inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific market. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated recommendations before publication and validate market-specific guidance against current search-engine policies and site requirements. <br>
