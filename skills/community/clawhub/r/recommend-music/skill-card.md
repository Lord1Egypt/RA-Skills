## Description: <br>
Recommends real, popular songs released after 2000 based on the user's described mood, activity, scene, genre preference, or music request. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[flonerze](https://clawhub.ai/user/flonerze) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this Chinese-language skill to get structured music recommendations for situations such as studying, working out, relaxing, driving, parties, or broad song discovery. The agent returns 5-10 candidate songs with artist, album, year, genre, and a recommendation rationale. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Song metadata may be inaccurate because recommendations rely on model knowledge rather than a live music database. <br>
Mitigation: Verify song titles, artists, album names, release years, and popularity claims against a trusted music source when accuracy matters. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad music questions where the user did not ask for a full recommendation list. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm whether the user wants recommendations or a narrower answer when the request is ambiguous. <br>
