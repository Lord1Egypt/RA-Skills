## Description: <br>
Transforms long, unstructured Chinese source text into structured WeChat Official Account article drafts with extracted key points, standardized formatting, and risk notices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ninetyhe-90](https://clawhub.ai/user/ninetyhe-90) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External content creators and publishing teams use this skill to turn chat logs, drafts, meeting notes, reading notes, industry analysis, and stock-review material into WeChat-ready Markdown articles. It is especially useful when the source text needs clearer structure, stronger headline sections, preserved core opinions, and a standard risk notice before publication. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated drafts may contain incomplete, misleading, or publication-ready financial commentary if the source material is inaccurate. <br>
Mitigation: Review, fact-check, and edit drafts before publishing; retain clear investment-risk notices where stock-review content is used. <br>
Risk: Generated articles may be saved locally and could include sensitive chats, meeting notes, or private business material. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sensitive inputs unless local saving is acceptable, and confirm or change the save location before use. <br>
