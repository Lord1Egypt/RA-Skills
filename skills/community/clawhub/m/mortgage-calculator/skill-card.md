## Description: <br>
Calculate mortgage payments with equal-principal and equal-interest comparisons for loan comparison, payment calculation, and prepayment evaluation. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Home buyers, mortgage holders, and financial workflow users use this skill to compare repayment methods, estimate affordability or prepayment effects, and keep local searchable mortgage notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Mortgage notes and command history are stored locally in plaintext files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering account numbers, full identity details, or other highly sensitive financial information; review the local mortgage-calculator data directory when using record-keeping commands. <br>
