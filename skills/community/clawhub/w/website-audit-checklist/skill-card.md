## Description: <br>
Website audit workflow for SaaS, agency, and product sites that finds conversion leaks, trust gaps, mobile UX issues, weak CTAs, pricing-page friction, and SEO/on-page basics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, agencies, marketers, and agent operators use this skill to produce a structured first-pass website audit that identifies conversion, trust, mobile UX, copy, and on-page SEO issues. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may paste confidential analytics, customer data, or unpublished business details into an agent session while requesting an audit. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential data unless the user is comfortable having the agent process it; use public page copy or sanitized context where possible. <br>
Risk: The audit is a first-pass heuristic and may produce recommendations that do not improve conversion. <br>
Mitigation: Validate larger redesign decisions with analytics, session recordings, customer interviews, and controlled A/B tests. <br>
Risk: The skill includes disclosed promotional links to Atlas paid ZIP packs. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the Atlas link as a commercial offer and review whether the paid-pack promotion is appropriate for the intended deployment. <br>
