## Description: <br>
NIH funding trend analysis to identify high-priority research areas. <br>

This skill is for demonstration purposes and not for production usage. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[AIPOCH-AI](https://clawhub.ai/user/AIPOCH-AI) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, grant strategists, and developers use this demonstration skill to explore mock NIH funding trends, suggested institutes, and topic opportunities before validating any grant strategy against authoritative funding databases. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Mock funding recommendations could be mistaken for real grant strategy guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for demonstration or education and verify any conclusions against authoritative funding databases before making grant decisions. <br>
Risk: The optional report output can write a file to a user-selected path. <br>
Mitigation: Choose an intentional workspace-local output path and review generated files before sharing them. <br>
