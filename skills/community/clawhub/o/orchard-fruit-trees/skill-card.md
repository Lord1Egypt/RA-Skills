## Description: <br>
Planting and managing fruit trees and berry bushes for food production. Use when someone wants to grow fruit, is planting trees on a property, needs to maintain existing fruit trees, or wants faster-producing options like berry bushes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[howtousehumans](https://clawhub.ai/user/howtousehumans) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and agents use this skill to plan and manage home orchards, fruit trees, and berry bushes. It helps tailor site assessment, variety selection, planting, pruning, pest management, and seasonal care to the user's location and growing conditions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may ask for city, zip code, climate zone, or site details to tailor fruit-tree guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the location precision and property details needed for the recommendation. <br>
Risk: Fruit-tree recommendations can be climate-specific and may be wrong if local chill hours, disease pressure, or regulations are not checked. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm local zone, chill hours, extension guidance, and applicable plant restrictions before planting or treating trees. <br>
