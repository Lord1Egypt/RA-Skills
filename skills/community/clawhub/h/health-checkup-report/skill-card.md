## Description: <br>
Interpret routine health checkup reports and lab results in plain Chinese, highlighting abnormal items, likely meaning, urgency, follow-up questions, and practical next steps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to turn routine checkup reports, lab values, and imaging summaries into plain Chinese explanations with urgency levels, follow-up questions, and practical next steps. It is intended for informational interpretation and should not replace clinician diagnosis or treatment. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may produce diagnosis-like or treatment-like medical guidance even though it is intended to be informational. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as discussion prompts for a licensed clinician, not as diagnosis, treatment, or supplement instructions. <br>
Risk: Users may rely on the skill for urgent symptoms or serious abnormal lab results. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use it for urgent symptoms or serious abnormal results; seek prompt medical care in those cases. <br>
