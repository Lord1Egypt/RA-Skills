## Description: <br>
Builds and maintains rolling 13-week cash flow forecasts with weekly receipts, disbursements, variance tracking, borrowing base calculations, covenant monitoring, and executive liquidity dashboards. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[samledger67-dotcom](https://clawhub.ai/user/samledger67-dotcom) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Finance teams, CFOs, turnaround advisors, and lenders use this skill to build short-term liquidity forecasts, test covenant headroom, plan revolver activity, and prepare weekly cash reporting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive cash-flow inputs, including bank balances, AR/AP details, customer information, lender identifiers, and covenant data. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm authorization before use and redact account numbers, customer PII, and unnecessary lender identifiers. <br>
Risk: Forecast recommendations about draws, repayments, vendor payment timing, or covenant status could affect finance decisions if assumptions are wrong. <br>
Mitigation: Review outputs with the appropriate finance decision-maker and validate assumptions against current bank, AR/AP, payroll, debt, and covenant records before acting. <br>
