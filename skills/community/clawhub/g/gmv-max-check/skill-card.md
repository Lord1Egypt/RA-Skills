## Description: <br>
Check whether a GMV Max setup is usable, risky, or ineligible based on configuration choices, order volume, ROI handling, and conflicting modes. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ad operators and ecommerce campaign owners use this skill to review GMV Max settings, order volume, ROI handling, and conflicting modes before launch or troubleshooting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: GMV Max settings supplied through screenshots or rough notes may be incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: List assumptions explicitly and request only the settings, screenshots, or exports needed for the review. <br>
Risk: Setup recommendations could be mistaken for guaranteed platform eligibility or performance outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as setup review guidance and confirm proposed ad account changes before applying them. <br>
