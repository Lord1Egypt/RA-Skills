## Description: <br>
Extracts key points, methods, results, conclusions, and simpler explanations from academic paper PDFs in Chinese and English. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[eric060](https://clawhub.ai/user/eric060) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Researchers, students, and developers use this skill to summarize academic paper PDFs, extract methods, results, conclusions, and key data, and produce plain-language explanations in Chinese or English. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Referenced PDF-processing scripts and dependencies are not bundled in the reviewed artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Verify any obtained scripts or dependencies come from the expected source before running them. <br>
Risk: PDF inputs may contain sensitive or untrusted content. <br>
Mitigation: Process only PDFs the user intends the summarizer to read and review generated summaries before relying on them. <br>
