## Description: <br>
Rewrite a rough sentence into 5 better title options. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[meituan-openplatform](https://clawhub.ai/user/meituan-openplatform) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and writers use this skill to turn rough sentences, topics, or draft headlines into concise title options while preserving the intended meaning. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompt text may later be sent to the model or service used to apply the generated prompt. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential or regulated text unless that execution environment is approved for the data. <br>
Risk: Generated title options may shift the intended meaning when the source input is vague. <br>
Mitigation: Review selected titles against the original intent before publishing or reusing them. <br>
