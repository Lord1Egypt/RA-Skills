## Description: <br>
Chinese-language courseware generation skill for creating slide decks, teaching materials, training materials, and online course content with built-in design specifications, templates, and content workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wangjiaocheng](https://clawhub.ai/user/wangjiaocheng) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Educators, trainers, and content creators use this skill to plan, generate, format, and save courseware such as PPT decks, teaching plans, training materials, and online course content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated courseware may contain incorrect, outdated, or unsuitable instructional content. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated files for factual accuracy, audience fit, and teaching quality before sharing or presenting. <br>
Risk: Custom exemplars may contain sensitive or identifying information that could be reused in generated materials. <br>
Mitigation: Remove sensitive information from exemplars and prompts before using them with the skill. <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on common courseware or PPT requests. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm that the user intends to generate courseware before saving files or producing final deliverables. <br>
