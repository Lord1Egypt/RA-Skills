# Marketing Plan: Ad Copy Generator

## ClawHub Marketplace Listing

**Title:** Ad Copy Generator — Google, Facebook, LinkedIn & Retargeting

**Tagline:** Stop paying $300/ad. Generate platform-ready copy in 5 minutes.

**Short Description (150 chars):**
4 prompts. 4 platforms. Google RSA, Facebook/IG, LinkedIn, and retargeting copy — all built on direct response principles.

**Full Description:**

Every business running paid ads faces the same problem: great copy is expensive, slow, or generic.

Freelancers charge $75–$300 per ad. Jasper and Copy.ai cost $49–$82/month and produce filler. ChatGPT without a structured prompt wastes 45 minutes to produce something mediocre.

**Ad Copy Generator** solves this with 4 precision prompts — one for each major ad platform — built around direct response principles, character limits, and buyer psychology.

**What's included:**

**Google Search Ads:**
- 15 RSA headlines with character counts (30 chars max, enforced)
- 4 descriptions (90 chars max, enforced)
- Full ad extension set: sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets
- Pin strategy recommendation
- Quality Score optimization tips

**Facebook & Instagram:**
- 3 complete ad variants: Story, Problem/Solution, Social Proof
- Platform-spec copy: 125-char hooks, 40-char headlines, 30-char descriptions
- Instagram caption adaptation with hashtags
- A/B test recommendation

**LinkedIn Ads:**
- Sponsored Content (intro + headline + description + body)
- Lead Gen Form (all fields + qualifying questions)
- InMail (subject + body + CTA + P.S.)
- Campaign strategy notes (objective, format, targeting, bid)

**Retargeting System:**
- 9 ad units: 3 stages × 3 psychological angles (urgency, social proof, objection removal)
- Stage 1: Abandoned cart/trial
- Stage 2: Past visitor win-back
- Stage 3: Post-purchase upsell/cross-sell
- Campaign structure: audience windows, frequency caps, budget splits
