## Description: <br>
Convert photos of people, pets, objects, or logos into four captioned animated GIF stickers in a Funko Pop / Pop Mart style. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[daidai8910g](https://clawhub.ai/user/daidai8910g) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and creators use this skill to turn photos into a four-item animated sticker pack with custom or language-aware default captions for chats, emoji packs, animated avatars, and expression sets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User photos and generated images may be sent to MiniMax APIs for processing. <br>
Mitigation: Use only photos that the user has permission to process and avoid sensitive personal images. <br>
Risk: External helper scripts and requirements are referenced by the skill but were not included in this artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review helper scripts, dependencies, and required environment setup before running generation or conversion commands. <br>
