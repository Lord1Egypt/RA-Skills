# Agentic Development Email Sequence Blueprints

## Cold Outbound: Operations Leader

Goal: book a short workflow review.

1. `cold-problem-pilot`: problem-aware first touch.
2. `followup-workflow-map`: offer to map one workflow.
3. `followup-risk-controls`: approval gates and logging angle.
4. `followup-example`: concrete workflow example.
5. `breakup-close-loop`: polite close-the-loop.

Recommended spacing: day 1, day 3, day 7, day 14, day 24.

## Cold Outbound: Technical Leader

Goal: discuss architecture and evaluation.

1. `cold-technical-evaluation`: evaluation-first first touch.
2. `followup-architecture`: tool routing, retrieval, approval gates.
3. `followup-monitoring`: logs, tests, and operational controls.
4. `followup-build-vs-buy`: scoped build angle.
5. `breakup-technical`: close or defer.

Recommended spacing: day 1, day 4, day 9, day 16, day 28.

## Warm Intro

Goal: convert trust into a useful first conversation.

1. `warm-intro-context`: acknowledge source and reason.
2. `warm-intro-workflow`: suggest a specific workflow review.
3. `warm-intro-next-step`: offer two meeting windows or a one-page outline.

## Inbound Lead

Goal: qualify and book discovery.

1. `inbound-fast-response`: fast acknowledgement.
2. `inbound-qualification`: ask for workflow, systems, approval constraints.
3. `inbound-booking`: propose discovery agenda.
4. `inbound-no-response`: nudge with smaller ask.

## Discovery to Proposal

Goal: turn conversation into a clear pilot.

1. `discovery-confirm-agenda`: pre-call agenda.
2. `post-discovery-recap`: summarize pain, workflow, risks, next step.
3. `proposal-preview`: preview scope before formal proposal.
4. `proposal-sent`: send proposal and decision points.
5. `proposal-followup-questions`: invite edits and concerns.

## Closing

Goal: resolve friction and start the pilot.

1. `close-decision-summary`: summarize business reason and scope.
2. `close-objection-budget`: budget objection.
3. `close-objection-risk`: risk/control objection.
4. `close-objection-timing`: timing objection.
5. `close-final-nudge`: final decision prompt.

## Contract and Payment

Goal: move from yes to kickoff.

1. `contract-sent`: send agreement.
2. `contract-clarifications`: answer contract questions.
3. `deposit-invoice`: send deposit or first invoice.
4. `signature-reminder`: polite signature reminder.
5. `kickoff-after-signature`: kickoff after signed agreement.

## Post-Sale and Delivery

Goal: keep confidence high and reduce delivery friction.

1. `kickoff-agenda`: kickoff agenda and required inputs.
2. `access-request`: request credentials or sandbox access.
3. `weekly-update`: weekly progress update.
4. `review-ready`: request review of prototype.
5. `acceptance-request`: ask for acceptance or specific defects.
6. `handoff-complete`: handoff and support period.

## Expansion, Referral, Win-Back

Goal: create more opportunities without pressure.

1. `expansion-next-workflow`: propose next workflow.
2. `referral-request`: ask for a relevant introduction.
3. `testimonial-request`: ask for a short testimonial.
4. `quarterly-checkin`: post-project check-in.
5. `winback-new-trigger`: reconnect on a new trigger.
