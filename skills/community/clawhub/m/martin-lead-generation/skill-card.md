## Description: <br>
Lead Generation helps agents find high-intent buyers in live Twitter, Instagram, and Reddit conversations, research a product, generate targeted searches, and draft reviewed outreach using SkillBoss API Hub search. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales, growth, and customer discovery users use this skill to research a product, generate buyer-intent queries, find relevant public social posts, score leads, and prepare outreach drafts for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill requires a SkillBoss API key and sends product and search information to SkillBoss. <br>
Mitigation: Use the API key only in trusted environments and avoid including secrets or private customer data in product descriptions or search queries. <br>
Risk: Lead results and outreach drafts may be inappropriate if used without review or without following platform rules. <br>
Mitigation: Review and approve outreach before sending, follow each platform's rules, and disclose affiliations when contacting prospects. <br>
Risk: Local prospecting files can contain product strategy, search queries, and lead history. <br>
Mitigation: Store data/lead-generation in an appropriate workspace and delete or protect the files when they contain sensitive business information. <br>
