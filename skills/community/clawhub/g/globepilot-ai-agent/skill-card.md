## Description: <br>
Globepilot Ai Agent is a travel assistant that helps with visa requirements, currency conversion, airport status, local events, cultural tips, emergency contacts, lodging ideas, hidden local spots, and food recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[sarqovik](https://clawhub.ai/user/sarqovik) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and travel-planning agents use this skill to request destination-specific travel guidance before or during trips. It supports common travel questions such as visa checks, exchange rates, airport and weather information, local events, cultural norms, emergency contacts, seasonal timing, stays, hidden gems, and food recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel, visa, currency, airport, weather, event, and emergency information can become outdated or vary by traveler circumstances. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm critical travel requirements, emergency contacts, prices, and operational status with authoritative local or official sources before relying on the guidance. <br>
Risk: The artifact includes promotional Teneo and crypto-monetization references. <br>
Mitigation: Treat monetization and network references as promotional context and use the skill only for its listed travel-assistance commands. <br>
