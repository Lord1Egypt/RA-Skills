## Description: <br>
Helps agents answer questions about Caocao ride-hailing services, EV mobility options, trip planning, fare estimates, safety features, and corporate rides. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and support agents use this skill to compare Caocao ride options, estimate costs, plan trips, and understand safety or corporate ride guidance before using the official service. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Ride prices, vehicle availability, service coverage, policies, and safety features may change after the skill content was published. <br>
Mitigation: Verify important trip details in the official Caocao app or with the provider before relying on the guidance. <br>
Risk: Fare estimates and city coverage guidance are informational and may differ from the provider's live pricing or availability. <br>
Mitigation: Treat estimates as planning guidance only and defer to the platform's live quote, city list, and booking flow for final decisions. <br>
