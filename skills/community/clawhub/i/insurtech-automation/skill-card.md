## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may process sensitive insurance, medical, financial, or identity documents. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with files the user intends the agent to inspect, and follow applicable privacy and data-handling requirements. <br>
Risk: Coverage recommendations, policy interpretations, product comparisons, or claim estimates may be incomplete or outdated. <br>
Mitigation: Verify insurance terms, regulatory requirements, product data, and claim outcomes against official insurer or regulator sources before acting. <br>
Risk: The skill is focused on Chinese insurance data and regulatory sources, which may not apply in other jurisdictions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm local laws, regulator guidance, product availability, and professional review requirements before using outputs outside that market context. <br>
