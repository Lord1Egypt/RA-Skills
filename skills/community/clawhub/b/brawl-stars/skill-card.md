Mitigation: Check publication dates and source links before relying on event schedules, rewards, penalties, or support guidance. <br>
Risk: Game-help requests may ask for cheating, account trading, recharge bypass, private data, or other rule-violating assistance. <br>
Mitigation: Limit responses to public information and decline requests for cheating, account trading, payment bypass, private player data, or unpublished test content. <br>
