## Description: <br>
E-commerce Live Streaming Master helps agents create e-commerce livestream plans, scripts, product pacing, objection responses, closing language, and analytics templates for platforms such as Douyin, Kuaishou, Taobao Live, and WeChat Channels. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing and e-commerce teams use this skill to plan livestream selling sessions, generate category-specific scripts, structure product portfolios, and prepare post-stream analytics. It is intended for public-facing commerce workflows where generated sales language should be reviewed before use. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad trigger words may cause the skill to appear in too many related marketing or commerce conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill when e-commerce livestream planning or scripting is the intended task, and confirm that its output is relevant before applying it. <br>
Risk: Generated urgency, scarcity, or closing language may conflict with platform rules, advertising law, or consumer-protection expectations. <br>
Mitigation: Review public-facing scripts and offers against applicable platform policies and legal requirements before publication. <br>
