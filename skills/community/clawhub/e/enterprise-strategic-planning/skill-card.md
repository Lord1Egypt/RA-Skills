## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill asks users for sensitive company strategy inputs such as financials, roadmaps, customer context, competitive position, and internal capabilities. <br>
Mitigation: Use sanitized or aggregated inputs unless disclosure is approved, avoid trade secrets and regulated data, and treat generated Word/PPT outputs as confidential until reviewed. <br>
Risk: The security review notes inconsistent and under-scoped instructions about recording and reusing user input. <br>
Mitigation: Decline optional case contribution or feedback reuse unless explicitly approved, and remove company names, specific figures, customer details, and roadmap information before sharing examples. <br>
Risk: Strategic recommendations can be misleading when based on incomplete user input or broad assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm each step with business owners, validate assumptions and numbers against authoritative internal sources, and review the final plan before using it for decisions or external communication. <br>
