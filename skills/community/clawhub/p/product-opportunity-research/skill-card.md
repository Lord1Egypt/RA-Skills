## Description: <br>
A multi-agent product opportunity research framework that analyzes user needs, workflow fit, technical feasibility, system integration, compliance risk, and business value to produce opportunity maps, prioritization, roadmaps, validation plans, and packaging strategy. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Joe-rq](https://clawhub.ai/user/Joe-rq) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, strategy, and business teams use this skill to run structured product opportunity research and feature prioritization. It is intended for market, opportunity, product strategy, competitive differentiation, and business model analysis where multiple perspectives must be challenged before roadmap decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Broad product or market questions can expand into a long, multi-phase research workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the research scope after Phase 0 before starting the full six-agent analysis. <br>
Risk: The skill may produce strategic recommendations that appear decision-ready without external market validation. <br>
Mitigation: Use the built-in validation plan, Go/No-Go criteria, and risk review before committing roadmap or pricing decisions. <br>
