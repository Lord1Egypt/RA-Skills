## Description: <br>
Deep research skill that decomposes questions into parallel sub-queries, executes searches concurrently, selectively fetches high-value sources, and synthesizes structured answers with confidence ratings. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[PHY041](https://clawhub.ai/user/PHY041) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, developers, and analysts use this skill to perform multi-source web research, competitive analysis, technology comparisons, and fact checking with structured synthesis and confidence ratings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Research prompts can become web search queries and may expose sensitive private details. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid including secrets, credentials, or private personal data in research prompts or generated search queries. <br>
Risk: Research outputs depend on web search results and fetched sources, which can be outdated, incomplete, or conflicting. <br>
Mitigation: Review citations, confidence ratings, and stated gaps before relying on conclusions. <br>
