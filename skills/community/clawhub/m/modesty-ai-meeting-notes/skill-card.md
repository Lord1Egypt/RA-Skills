## Description: <br>
AI Meeting Notes turns pasted meeting notes, transcripts, and other unstructured text into summaries, action items with owners and deadlines, saved notes, and to-do tracking. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[modestyrichards](https://clawhub.ai/user/modestyrichards) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, external users, and team operators use this skill to convert pasted meeting notes, transcripts, email threads, or chat logs into structured summaries, action items, decisions, and follow-up tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Pasted meeting content, including raw notes, may be written into workspace files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential, legal, HR, customer, credential, or strategy discussions unless raw-note retention is disabled and the storage location is confirmed. <br>
Risk: The artifact text says notes are not stored or sent elsewhere, but the skill also auto-saves notes. <br>
Mitigation: Tell users before use that local workspace files may be created and may include the original pasted notes. <br>
