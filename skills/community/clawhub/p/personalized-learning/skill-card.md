## Description: <br>
Personalized Learning turns learning requests into structured lessons, curricula, tutorials, primers, or self-contained HTML learning pages with progressive sections and instructional visuals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[vaintwyt](https://clawhub.ai/user/vaintwyt) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners, educators, and agent users use this skill to convert a topic or study goal into a complete teaching deliverable with scoped sections, explanatory prose, examples, misconceptions, and visuals. By default it packages the result as a self-contained HTML page with navigation and lesson content. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated HTML may include inline JavaScript for navigation or instructional visuals. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated HTML before publishing or sharing, especially when the lesson includes user-provided links, embedded text, or custom scripting. <br>
