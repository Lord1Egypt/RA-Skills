# Response Letter Template

*Use this template after completing a strategy report to draft the formal response.*

---

## Standard Response Structure

```
Subject: Response to [Authority/Employer] Letter dated [Date] – [Subject]

Dear [Name / Sir/Madam],

1. INTRODUCTION / ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
We refer to your letter dated [Date] regarding [brief subject].

2. SCOPE OF RESPONSE
This response addresses only the matters raised in your letter.
We reserve our position on all other matters.

3. PRIMARY POSITION
[Insert your strongest argument here – usually Textual Interpretation
or Factual Distinction]

4. ALTERNATIVE / FALLBACK POSITION (if needed)
Without prejudice to the above, even if [alternative interpretation],
the facts of this case are materially different because...

5. DISCLOSURE
We provide the following information:
- [Precise information – clause references, key facts, dates]
- [General information – high-level context only]

Further detailed quantification and analysis will be provided in due course.

6. CONCLUSION & RESERVATION OF RIGHTS
For the reasons set out above, the Contractor maintains its position
that it is entitled to [relief].

We reserve all our rights and remedies under the Contract and at law.

Yours faithfully,

[Name]
[Position]
```

---

## How to Map Strategy Report → Response Letter

| Strategy Report Section | Letter Section | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Situation Assessment | Introduction | Keep brief |
| Scope Control | Scope of Response | Critical — prevents scope creep |
| Primary Argument | Primary Position | Be clear and structured |
| Fallback Argument | Alternative Position | Use "Without prejudice to the above" |
| Disclosure Control | Disclosure section | Follow precisely / generally / defer |
| Response Architecture | Overall structure | Choose the right pattern |
| Risk Assessment | Internal use only | Helps prepare for counter-arguments |

---

## Closing Patterns

### Standard Reservation of Rights
> Nothing in this response shall be construed as a waiver of, or prejudice to, any of the Contractor's rights, remedies, claims or entitlements, whether under the Contract, at law or otherwise, all of which are expressly reserved.

### Cooperative Closing
> The Contractor remains committed to working cooperatively with the [Authority/Employer] to resolve this matter.

### Deferral of Full Particulars
> Further particulars, supporting records, and detailed quantification will be provided in the [Notified Claim Report / formal submission] once the full effects of the event become ascertainable.
