## Use Case: <br>
Employees, team leads, and Feishu workspace administrators use this skill to get step-by-step operational guidance for messaging, documents, bitables, calendars, file sharing, permissions, automation, and collaboration workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Guidance may involve sharing files, documents, chats, or meeting records that contain sensitive information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm recipients and permissions before sharing, avoid public links for sensitive files, and treat exports as sensitive copies outside Feishu controls. <br>
Risk: Automation and integration guidance may involve webhook URLs or API keys. <br>
Mitigation: Protect webhook URLs and API keys, rotate exposed credentials, and limit access to only the systems and users that need them. <br>
Risk: Meeting recordings, transcripts, exported chats, and location messages may require privacy or consent review. <br>
Mitigation: Get required consent before recording or sharing location data, and review exported or transcribed content before redistribution. <br>
