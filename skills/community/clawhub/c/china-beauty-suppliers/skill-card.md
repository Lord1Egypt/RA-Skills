## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes embedded factory contact emails that may be stale, incorrect, or undisclosed in the public documentation. <br>
Mitigation: Verify supplier identities and contact details independently before acting on them. <br>
Risk: Supplier contact data could be misused for unsolicited bulk outreach. <br>
Mitigation: Use the sourcing guide for legitimate evaluation workflows and avoid unsolicited bulk messaging. <br>
