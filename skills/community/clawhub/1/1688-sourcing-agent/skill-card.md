## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Supplier, payment, certification, or shipping guidance could be incomplete or outdated for a user's specific purchase. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as educational support and independently verify supplier documents, import rules, product compliance, and payment protections before spending money. <br>
Risk: The skill provides procurement guidance but cannot guarantee supplier reliability or product quality. <br>
Mitigation: Use samples, supplier documentation, video checks, and third-party or expert review for high-value, regulated, customized, or first-time import orders. <br>
