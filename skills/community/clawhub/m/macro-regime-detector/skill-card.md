## Description: <br>
Detects current macro market regimes using sentiment, volatility, currency, gold/BTC, Reddit, news, and on-chain signals to support crypto and portfolio positioning analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[JamieRossouw](https://clawhub.ai/user/JamieRossouw) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to ask about current macro conditions, risk-on versus risk-off context, and crypto positioning considerations. It returns a regime classification, confidence, supporting signals, and a positioning recommendation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Long, short, or portfolio-positioning guidance may be mistaken for personalized financial advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as general macro and crypto sentiment analysis; verify market data independently before making investment decisions. <br>
Risk: Macro regime classifications can become stale or inaccurate if the underlying market signals are outdated or unavailable. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm current market conditions with trusted external sources before acting on a recommendation. <br>
