## Description: <br>
Ai Image Team coordinates planner, maker, and reviewer agents to create e-commerce images from Chinese natural-language requirements. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[duzhihai123](https://clawhub.ai/user/duzhihai123) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill to turn e-commerce product requirements into creative briefs, generated image drafts, and quality review reports for marketplace, social, and campaign assets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Image prompts, product details, and project history may be retained locally or handled by configured image-generation tools. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid confidential campaign, customer, or unreleased product data unless local retention and provider-side handling are acceptable. <br>
Risk: The built-in reviewer is a workflow quality check, not a substitute for visual safety, brand, legal, or marketplace compliance review. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated images and QA reports manually before publishing or delivering commercial assets. <br>
