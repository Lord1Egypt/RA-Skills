## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can suggest public SEO content, comparison pages, or paid backlink outreach that may be inaccurate, misleading, or misaligned with brand and compliance requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review all public copy, competitor claims, pricing references, outreach, and citations before publishing or sending. <br>
Risk: The skill can guide robots.txt, analytics, IndexNow, schema, and API-call workflows that affect live websites or accounts. <br>
Mitigation: Apply changes only to websites, accounts, and keys the user is authorized to manage, and test configuration changes before production use. <br>
