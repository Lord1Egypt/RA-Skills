## Description: <br>
Summarizes public js.design project and share pages, including page lists, component annotations, and design resource links. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[CodeKungfu](https://clawhub.ai/user/CodeKungfu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, designers, and product teams use this skill to organize information from public js.design project and share pages, including page updates, component annotations, and resource links. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Summaries of public design pages can include names, links, annotations, and resource URLs that may be sensitive when reshared. <br>
Mitigation: Review extracted names, links, design annotations, and resource URLs before sharing them outside the team. <br>
Risk: Repeated access to dynamic public pages may create unnecessary traffic or stale summaries if pages have not finished loading. <br>
Mitigation: Use rate control, wait for dynamic page content to finish loading, and process only public js.design links or content intended for summarization. <br>
