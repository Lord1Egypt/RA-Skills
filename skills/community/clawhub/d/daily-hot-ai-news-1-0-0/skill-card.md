## Description: <br>
Provides Chinese-language AI news briefings on recent hot topics, industry events, technical developments, and commentary when the user asks for AI news or trends. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and AI practitioners use this skill to request current AI news briefings with source links, neutral explanations, and short analysis across domestic and international sources. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: AI news summaries can become stale or repeat example March 2026 search terms and sample claims from the artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Use the current date for searches and verify important claims against cited current links before relying on the briefing. <br>
Risk: Public web searches may surface incomplete, low-quality, or biased reporting. <br>
Mitigation: Compare multiple cited sources and keep analysis neutral, especially for high-impact claims about releases, funding, regulation, or company statements. <br>
