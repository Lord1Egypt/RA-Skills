## Description: <br>
Analyze resumes and design interview strategies using evidence-based methodology. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[mikonos](https://clawhub.ai/user/mikonos) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Hiring teams, interviewers, and recruiters use this skill to create structured interview guides for a specific role and candidate. It defines a competency scorecard, scans resume evidence against that scorecard, and generates pressure-test and future-simulation questions for review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Resume details and hiring decisions may involve personal data and fairness concerns. <br>
Mitigation: Share only necessary candidate information and review generated questions for job relevance, bias, and compliance with hiring policies. <br>
Risk: Generated interview questions could overemphasize concerns or produce misleading guidance if used without review. <br>
Mitigation: Have a human reviewer check the guide before use and balance concern-driven probes with evidence for strengths. <br>
