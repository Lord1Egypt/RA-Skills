## Description: <br>
Design native macOS apps following Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and Liquid Glass design system. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and designers use this skill to design, review, and modernize SwiftUI/AppKit macOS interfaces against Apple's Human Interface Guidelines, Liquid Glass patterns, and platform conventions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated SwiftUI or AppKit examples may be incorrect or incomplete for a real application context. <br>
Mitigation: Review and test generated UI code before applying it to production projects. <br>
Risk: Design examples may include destructive actions such as Delete buttons. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm destructive workflows use appropriate labels, roles, confirmations, and undo or recovery behavior. <br>
