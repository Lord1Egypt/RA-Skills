## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prompts may include sensitive client, payment, or project details. <br>
Mitigation: Share only the details needed for the draft and avoid unnecessary sensitive information. <br>
Risk: Generated invoices or client updates may contain incorrect amounts, dates, terms, or status details. <br>
Mitigation: Review totals, due dates, payment terms, and project status before sending to a client. <br>
Risk: The skill links to an external paid product. <br>
Mitigation: Review the external product page and purchasing terms separately before buying. <br>
