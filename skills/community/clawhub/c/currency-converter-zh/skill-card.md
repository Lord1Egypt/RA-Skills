## Description: <br>
A Chinese-language tool for currency exchange-rate lookup and currency conversion. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users can ask in Chinese for exchange-rate lookups and conversions across common currencies such as USD, CNY, EUR, JPY, GBP, and HKD. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Currency or exchange-rate responses may be inaccurate or unsuitable for financial decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Verify any financial data with an authoritative market-data source before acting on it. <br>
Risk: Broad currency terms may invoke the skill in finance-adjacent conversations. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants currency conversion or exchange-rate lookup before relying on the response. <br>
