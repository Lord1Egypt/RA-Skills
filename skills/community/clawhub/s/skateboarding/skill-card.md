## Description: <br>
提供关于网红滑板的推荐清单、打卡路线与指南，供用户搜索网红滑板或规划相关出行时调用。 <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill to discover popular skateboarding routes, compare route filters, and plan skateboarding outings with travel, safety, crowding, equipment, and venue-rule notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Route, weather, venue-rule, cost, permit, or crowding information may be stale or incomplete. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current weather, local rules, costs, permits, and site conditions before relying on travel or route suggestions. <br>
Risk: Skateboarding route recommendations can create physical safety risk when difficulty, slope, road surface, signal coverage, or exit options are unsuitable. <br>
Mitigation: Review difficulty, mileage, elevation, road surface, supply points, signal coverage, emergency exits, and protective equipment before travel. <br>
