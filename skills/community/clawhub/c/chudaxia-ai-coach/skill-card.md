## Description: <br>
Supports enterprise AI transformation coaching for B2B training, workshops, digital employee definition, business scenario interviews, scene selection cards, and PoC planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Enterprise trainers, consultants, and business teams use this skill to design and deliver AI transformation programs, identify high-value AI use cases, define digital employees, and plan PoC work for B2B organizations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: External company research may involve client-sensitive or permission-restricted information. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm permission for external company research and use only approved sources and tools before preparing client materials. <br>
Risk: Recorded or transcribed interviews may contain personal data or confidential business information. <br>
Mitigation: Obtain consent before recording or transcribing interviews, minimize collected data, and keep transcripts in organization-approved systems. <br>
Risk: Training outputs and PoC plans may contain business recommendations that need local review. <br>
Mitigation: Have qualified client stakeholders review scene cards, digital employee definitions, and PoC plans before operational use. <br>
