## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Jewelry identification, grading, valuation, or investment guidance may be incomplete or misleading if based on limited descriptions, photos, or certificate excerpts. <br>
Mitigation: Use outputs as preliminary guidance and verify material claims, grading, treatments, value, and authenticity with a qualified gemologist, reputable laboratory report, or trusted jeweler before purchase or sale. <br>
Risk: The server security review found no artifact-backed malicious behavior, but generated recommendations can still affect buying, selling, or collecting decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations critically, compare against independent sources, and avoid treating the skill as the sole basis for high-value transactions. <br>
