## Description: <br>
Audits payroll compliance across worker classification, overtime, tax withholding, recurring checklists, and audit-ready report drafting. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[1kalin](https://clawhub.ai/user/1kalin) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
HR, payroll, and operations teams use this skill to review payroll practices, estimate compliance exposure, and draft audit-ready findings for United States payroll scenarios. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
United States <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Payroll audit output may be mistaken for authoritative legal, tax, or payroll advice. <br>
Mitigation: Have a qualified payroll, tax, or legal professional verify findings before changing payroll practices or responding to regulators. <br>
Risk: Payroll reviews can involve sensitive employee and compensation data. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing unnecessary employee or payroll details when using the skill. <br>
